5.1 Initiation Stage — Overview

The Initiation stage covers the process of determining whether the TT of CMC activities for a pharmaceutical or biopharmaceutical product should be performed. In this stage, the team evaluating the need for the TT often has limited information on the product characteristics, process requirements, and analytical testing from the SU, particularly if the product is still under development. While the Initiation stage is primarily driven by the Business function of the MAH, technical experts also should conduct high-level risk assessments.

A PM should be assigned to facilitate RU site selection and to develop the initial charter. This stage results in a decision on whether to perform the TT, the choice of an RU site, the formation of a steering committee, and the development of an initial TT charter as well as an overall product strategy, including business and regulatory considerations. 啟動階段 — 總覽

核心概念：啟動階段是什麼？

啟動階段是技術轉移（TT）的第一個正式階段。此階段的核心問題只有一個：「我們應該轉移這個產品嗎？轉到哪裡？」

在此階段，資訊通常不完整（特別是產品仍在開發中時），因此決策依賴高層次風險評估與業務判斷，而非詳細技術數據。

比喻：搬家前的評估

啟動階段就像決定要不要搬家、以及搬到哪裡。你不需要知道每一件家具如何搬運，但你需要評估：新家的空間夠嗎？交通方便嗎？費用在預算內嗎？一旦決定了，才開始詳細規劃搬家流程。

重點：啟動階段的四大輸出

  • 決定是否執行 TT
  • 選定接收單位（RU）廠址
  • 成立指導委員會（Steering Committee）
  • 建立初始 TT 章程（Charter）與產品策略

5.1.1 Business

The Business function at the MAH takes the lead role in the Initiation stage, developing the business case for initiating a TT activity. It facilitates obtaining key information from the SU, such as the quality target product profile (QTPP) and/or specifications to identify the product characteristics and high-level process requirements. Using a high-level risk assessment, the Business function develops the product strategy to guide the TT decision. Legal counsel also is engaged for intellectual property review to ensure the freedom to operate, if needed.

At this point, the RU site selection process can begin. If the product will be sourced externally, the Business function secures nondisclosure agreements or confidentiality agreements with potential RUs. The criteria laid out in the QTPP or product specification, the product strategy, the intellectual property review, and the information required for product lifecycle phase (see Section 3.1) are used in the site selection process. Typically, potential RUs are assessed for capability, age of facility, location, capacity, resourcing, costs, tax implications, compliance history, regulatory strategy, and other logistical considerations. Once a RU site is selected, the initial TT charter is approved, and the steering committee is formed. Section 8.1 presents a case study of best practices and benefits of establishing a steering committee for a project.

The stage gate for Initiation results in a decision to proceed with the TT; selection of the RU, often with a signed contract; and approval of the initial charter. Two case studies, Section 8.4 on using a regulatory strategy for decision-making and Section 8.8 on TT of an aseptic drug product, illustrate factors to consider when selecting a RU or supplier.

5.1.1 業務功能

業務功能在啟動階段的三大任務

  1. 建立商業案例：開發 TT 活動的商業論證，包含市場需求分析與財務可行性
  2. 取得產品資訊：從 SU 獲取 QTPP 和/或產品規格，理解產品特性與製程需求
  3. 主導 RU 廠址選擇：帶領評估與選擇接收單位，完成法律文件（NDA/CDA）

比喻：RU 廠址選擇就像選外包商

選 RU 就像企業評估外包廠商：不只看報價，更要評估能力（capability）、合規歷史（compliance history）、稅務考量、地理位置、產能彈性。最終選定後才簽合約，組成指導委員會監督整個專案。

RU 選擇評估標準（典型清單）

  • 製造能力（Capability）
  • 廠房設施年齡（Age of facility）
  • 地理位置（Location）
  • 產能（Capacity）
  • 人力資源（Resourcing）
  • 成本與稅務影響（Costs, tax implications）
  • 合規歷史（Compliance history）
  • 法規策略一致性（Regulatory strategy）
  • 其他後勤考量（Logistical considerations）

階段閘門（Stage Gate）輸出

啟動階段的 Stage Gate 有三個明確輸出：

  • 決定繼續執行 TT
  • 選定 RU（通常已簽合約）
  • 初始章程獲批准

5.1.2 Initiation Stage Project Manager

Involvement of a PM during the Initiation stage of a TT is critical to provide overall leadership and accountability for the project. The PM needs to be knowledgeable across all relevant functional areas and able to communicate across organizational levels. Additionally, this role requires such soft skills as facilitation, negotiation, decision-making, and motivation to effectively lead a cross-functional team to achieve business goals. Once the RU has been selected and the TT moves into the Planning stage, a TT PM will be assigned. In many TTs, the RU PM will serve as TT PM for the remainder of the project.

The Initiation Stage PM coordinates an initial high-level risk assessment using cross-functional expertise and drives site selection by ensuring the completion of proper due diligence of the RUs under consideration. These assessments must be documented to support both the TT decision and site selection. One methodology for these assessments is risk-ranking and filtering.

One key deliverable the Initiation Stage PM must fulfill during the Initiation stage is the establishment of a charter with the steering committee. While the initial charter at this stage may not yet include all the details, it should document the boundaries, major risks, and assumptions used to determine whether the TT will proceed as a part of the product strategy, and it should specify the RU site that is selected. Boundaries and/or assumptions that should be considered during the Initiation stage to determine the scope of the TT include:

  • Required time frame for completion
  • Regulatory strategy options
  • Estimated capital required
  • Resource needs and constraints
  • Scope of process to be transferred
  • Target markets
  • Annual production volume
  • Site selection decision and criteria used

Finally, the PM creates an initial, high-level project plan estimating timing, resource needs, and budget.

5.1.2 啟動階段專案經理

TT PM 的核心職責

啟動階段 PM 是整個 TT 的早期架構師，需具備：

  • 跨功能知識：了解業務、製程、法規、品質各面向
  • 跨層級溝通：能與高管及操作層人員有效溝通
  • 軟技能：促進（facilitation）、談判（negotiation）、決策（decision-making）、激勵（motivation）

比喻：PM 是 TT 的「建設指揮官」

啟動階段的 PM 就像建案的總指揮：他不需要親自砌磚，但必須確保地基穩固（章程正確）、各工班準時到位（跨部門協調）、圖紙與實際需求一致（風險評估文件化）。建案一旦動工（進入規劃階段），通常會移交給 RU 的 PM 接手。

初始章程（Initial Charter）必須涵蓋的邊界條件

  • 完成所需時間框架
  • 法規策略選項
  • 預估所需資本
  • 人力資源需求與限制
  • 待轉移製程範圍
  • 目標市場
  • 年產量
  • 廠址選擇決定及使用的標準

思考練習：誰是你的啟動階段 PM？

在你的組織中，當一個新產品 TT 案開始時，第一個被指派的 PM 是誰？來自 MAH 還是 RU？他在章程建立之前就介入了嗎？這對 TT 決策品質有什麼影響？

5.1.3 Process

The Process function first determines the subject matter expertise required on the TT team for the product under evaluation by modality or stage of manufacture. For instance, a small-molecule drug substance would need a process expert who has worked with organic chemistry processing, while a filled-vial drug product would need an expert in sterile manufacturing. Second, the MAH Process function develops a preliminary process flow and works with the SU to develop or obtain the QTPP and/or specification for the product. Third, using the assumptions provided by the Business function, Process develops batch size options and assesses scale-up or scale-down requirements against the current process at the SU. Finally, working with the Engineering, Manufacturing, and Analytical functions, Process assesses the process fit and evaluates the facilities, equipment, and operations group of the potential RU site to determine its capability to manufacture the product, noting gaps and investments required.

5.1.3 製程功能

製程功能的四個步驟

  1. 確定 SME 需求：依產品類型決定所需專業知識（小分子藥物 vs 無菌製劑 vs 生物製品）
  2. 建立初步製程流程：與 SU 合作取得 QTPP 和/或產品規格
  3. 評估批量與規模需求：比較 SU 現有製程與 RU 目標批量，評估放大或縮小需求
  4. 製程適配性評估（Process Fit Assessment）：確定 RU 是否有能力製造該產品

關鍵交付物

  • Process Flow/Description：初步製程流程圖
  • Process Fit/Gap Assessment：製程適配與差距評估報告

差距評估的輸出將直接影響 RU 廠址選擇決定，並決定啟動後的資本投入需求。

無菌填充場景的應用

對於無菌填充藥品（如我廠的業務），製程 SME 需評估：RU 是否具備適合的隔離系統（isolator/RABS）、凍乾設備（lyophilizer）、自動化視覺檢測（AVI）、以及相應的無菌工藝控制能力，這些差距將形成資本估算的基礎。

5.1.4 Analytical

The Analytical function uses the SU's QTPP and/or specification to determine product testing requirements and to evaluate potential testing of raw materials, single-use and other components, and packaging. Test methods are categorized either as compendial or non-compendial for the target markets to define the complexity of method transfer from the SU. With this information, the Analytical function assesses the potential RU's laboratory capabilities and capacity and provides a high-level estimate of what would be required for the TT. This evaluation covers instrumentation needs, glassware, reference standards, personnel, external laboratory services, and timing. Evaluation of site analytical capabilities of potential RUs is an essential element of due diligence. At this stage, the Analytical function documents the laboratory and method evaluations.

5.1.4 分析功能

分析功能的核心工作

分析功能從 QTPP/產品規格 出發，評估：

  • 產品所需的測試項目（放行測試、原料測試、包裝材測試）
  • 方法類型：法典方法（compendial）vs 非法典方法（non-compendial）
  • RU 實驗室的儀器、人員、外部委外實驗室能力

比喻：Compendial vs Non-compendial 方法

法典方法（如 USP、Ph. Eur. 規定的無菌試驗）就像「公定食譜」，任何廠都可直接使用；非法典方法則是「獨門秘方」，必須從 SU 完整轉移，驗證難度更高、耗時更長。

關鍵交付物

  • Laboratory Evaluation Report：RU 實驗室能力評估報告
  • Methods Evaluation Report：方法評估報告

這兩份文件也是 RU due diligence 的重要組成部分，直接影響廠址選擇。

5.1.5 Engineering

Evaluation of site manufacturing capabilities is another essential element of due diligence in selecting the most appropriate RU and deciding to proceed with the TT. This evaluation, also referred to as a facility fit assessment, includes such items as site utilities and infrastructure, processing equipment, and automation capabilities. The Engineering function, working closely with Process, Analytical, and Manufacturing functions, performs the facility fit assessment to form an initial rough estimate of capital that would be required. The case study in Section 8.6 provides more detail on the facility fit assessment.

5.1.5 工程功能

廠房適配性評估（Facility Fit Assessment）

工程功能執行廠房適配性評估，涵蓋：

  • 廠區公用設施與基礎設施（site utilities and infrastructure）
  • 製造設備（processing equipment）
  • 自動化能力（automation capabilities）

評估結果形成初步資本需求估算（rough order of magnitude capital estimate）。

關鍵交付物

  • Facility Fit Assessment：廠房適配性評估報告
  • Capital Estimate：資本估算
  • Permit Requirements：建照/許可需求清單

比喻：廠房適配就像房屋驗屋

移入新家前，你會請驗屋師評估電路（utilities）、格局空間（equipment fit）、智慧家電相容性（automation capabilities）。如果需要大改裝（資本投入），就會影響你的搬家決策與預算。

5.1.6 Manufacturing

To perform site selection, the Business function needs to understand the potential RU's ability to implement the process. Capability extends beyond capital requirements. The Manufacturing function assesses the potential RU's resource flexibility, operational training program, previous and current manufacturing activities in the facility, organizational structure, current operating shifts, and process data management systems.

Environment, health, and safety (EHS) issues at the potential RU should be evaluated as well. An employee health and safety viability report can be used in evaluating potential issues. The Manufacturing function should assess the environmental and occupational permits or certifications that will be needed, along with the RU's ability to obtain them and their potential impact on the TT timeline.

5.1.6 製造功能

能力不只是設備 — 製造功能的評估範圍

製造功能提醒業務方：能力（capability）遠不止設備。評估項目包含：

  • 人力資源彈性（resource flexibility）
  • 操作培訓計畫（operational training program）
  • 現有及歷史製造活動（previous and current activities）
  • 組織架構（organizational structure）
  • 班次安排（operating shifts）
  • 製程數據管理系統（process data management systems）

EHS 評估不可忽視

環境、健康、安全（EHS）評估是廠址選擇的必要項目。相關交付物：

  • EHS Viability Report：EHS 可行性報告
  • Permit Requirements：環保/職安許可需求

EHS 許可的取得時間可能很長，必須在啟動階段即識別，否則可能成為 TT 時程的瓶頸。

5.1.7 Regulatory

Involving the MAH Regulatory function as soon as possible is critical to a TT project. A regulatory strategy should be developed to align the product strategy with the Business function and to establish boundaries and assumptions for the TT charter. Key elements of the initial regulatory strategy are filing category, regulatory assumptions, likely data requirements and source documents required, potential need for a health authority meeting, projected timing of regulatory submission to each health authority, and regulations applicable to the product in the region(s) in which it is to be manufactured and marketed.

The regulatory strategy is a living document that will evolve throughout the project. It should be reviewed at each stage for required updates and further refinement based on the knowledge gained at that stage. Therefore, the ultimate regulatory strategy may look significantly different than this initial draft as knowledge is transferred and more information becomes available.

The regulatory strategy typically would address the following:

  • List of markets and the planned timing of submission for each market
  • Proposed filing category for the TT and the justification for this determination
  • Specific regulations applicable to the product and region(s) where it will be marketed
  • Specific data requirements and source documents to support the change related to the TT that the governing regulatory agency would expect (e.g., sterility and aseptic processing, stability, qualification and validation considerations, clinical/bioequivalence requirements, and potential required permits)
  • Key regulatory assumptions and risks that could impact the overall TT success and possible mitigation strategies
  • Documents the RU will need to prepare for authoring, review, approval, and submission of the regulatory filing
  • Product approval timeline
  • GMP inspection history (lack of)
  • Current GMP certificates

The Regulatory function also will perform due diligence of potential RUs for outstanding actions taken by regulatory authorities that would make the RU unsuitable for use.

5.1.7 法規功能

法規功能必須盡早介入

TR65 明確指出：「Involving the MAH Regulatory function as soon as possible is critical。」法規策略不是製造完成後再考慮的事，而是啟動階段就要建立的邊界條件。

初始法規策略的核心要素

  • 申請類別（Filing category）：此次 TT 對應的申請類型（如 Prior Approval Supplement、Annual Report 等）
  • 數據需求（Data requirements）：各法規機構預期的支持數據
  • 申請時程（Submission timing）：各市場的預計申請時間
  • 健康主管機關會議（HA meeting）：是否需要事先諮詢
  • GMP 稽查歷史（GMP inspection history）

比喻：法規策略是「活文件」

法規策略就像導航 App 的路線規劃：出發前設好方向（初始策略），但沿途遇到施工（新法規）或改道（技術變更），會即時更新重新規劃。啟動階段的版本與最終提交版本可能大相徑庭，這是正常的。

CDMO 觀點：RU Due Diligence 的法規面

對 CDMO 作為 RU 而言，法規 due diligence 包含：確認 RU 有無未解決的法規行動（483、Warning Letter、進口禁令），以及評估 RU 在目標市場的 GMP 認可狀態。這直接影響客戶的申請策略與時程。

5.1.8 Quality

The Quality function at the MAH performs the quality assessment as part of due diligence on the potential RUs. In preparing the quality assessment for each RU, which includes the RU site assessment, the Quality function typically asks the potential RU to complete a questionnaire and conducts a search for compliance history records. For external RUs, the compliance history records may be public, while the assessment of an internal site can involve records internal to the company. The purpose of the quality due diligence is to determine the maturity of the RU quality system, the RU's organizational mindset regarding GMPs, and its adherence to applicable regulatory requirements. Quality will provide a compliance risk evaluation of the potential RUs.

5.1.8 品質功能

品質 Due Diligence 的三個目的

  1. 評估 QMS 成熟度（maturity of the RU quality system）
  2. 了解 GMP 組織文化（organizational mindset regarding GMPs）
  3. 確認法規符合性（adherence to applicable regulatory requirements）

評估方法

  • 要求 RU 填寫問卷（questionnaire）
  • 查閱公開合規歷史記錄（Warning Letters、483s、EMA 報告）
  • 內部廠址則查閱內部記錄

輸出：Quality Assessment(s) — 合規風險評估報告，作為廠址選擇的重要依據。

比喻：品質評估就像信用調查

選擇 RU 前的品質 due diligence，就像銀行放款前的信用調查：過去有多少不良記錄（Warning Letter）？現在的還款能力（現行 QMS）如何？組織文化上是否重視誠信（GMP mindset）？這決定了這個「借款方」（RU）的風險等級。

5.1.9 Sourcing/Supply Chain

The Sourcing/Supply Chain function develops initial capacity-planning models using estimated yield and cycle times. These models are used to evaluate potential RUs for batch size, manufacturing cycle time, and on-site storage needs as part of the facility fit assessment. The capacity model should incorporate product volumes for development and validation activities as well as commercial scale. The Sourcing/Supply Chain function evaluates sourcing of the required materials, consumables, and components to determine any potential long-lead items and specially regulated materials (e.g., materials subject to national quotas), and it works with the Regulatory or appropriate function to identify special import/export requirements where permits would be needed. The Sourcing/Supply Chain function also prepares a preliminary supply chain strategy to assess the impact of each potential RU on the product value chain.

5.1.9 採購 / 供應鏈功能

供應鏈功能的三大任務

  1. 初步產能規劃模型：利用估算的良率與週期時間，評估 RU 的批量、製造週期、倉儲需求
  2. 原物料來源評估：識別長前置時間（long-lead）物料與特殊管制物料（如國家配額物質）
  3. 初步供應鏈策略：評估各 RU 候選廠對產品價值鏈的影響

關鍵交付物

  • Preliminary Product Supply Chain Strategy, Including Map：初步供應鏈策略與地圖
  • Critical Material List：關鍵物料清單
  • Perform RU Capacity Analysis：RU 產能分析

長前置時間物料的重要性

識別長前置時間物料（如特定濾芯、隔離袋、特殊玻璃容器）對於 TT 時程規劃至關重要。若在規劃階段才發現某物料需要 18 個月採購期，可能導致整個 TT 時程大幅延誤。啟動階段即識別此類風險，才能提前採取行動。

Section Summary — 本節重點總結

What is the Initiation Stage? (啟動階段是什麼？)

The Initiation Stage answers the fundamental question: Should we perform this TT, and where? It runs before detailed planning begins, when information about the product and process is still limited. Decision-making relies on high-level risk assessments rather than detailed technical data.

啟動階段回答一個基本問題：我們應該執行這次 TT 嗎？轉到哪裡？此階段在詳細規劃開始之前進行，產品與製程資訊仍然有限，決策依賴高層次風險評估。

Key Outputs (關鍵輸出)

  • Decision to proceed with TT / 決定執行 TT
  • RU site selected (often with signed contract) / 選定 RU 廠址（通常已簽合約）
  • Steering Committee formed / 指導委員會成立
  • Initial TT Charter approved / 初始 TT 章程獲批
  • Preliminary regulatory strategy / 初步法規策略
  • High-level project plan / 高層次專案計畫

Key Takeaways for CDMO Operations (CDMO 操作重點)

  • 當你的公司（TWi）作為 RU，要通過客戶的 Business、Quality、Regulatory、Engineering due diligence，必須在各面向有充分的文件準備（Facility Fit Assessment、Quality Assessment responses、Regulatory track record）。
  • 法規功能必須在最早期就介入，因為申請策略（PAC vs. Prior Approval）直接影響 TT 時程與資源分配。
  • 長前置時間物料（如隔離袋、特殊濾芯）必須在啟動階段識別，不能等到規劃階段才處理。
