4.2

Single-Use Systems (SUS) — Flexible Bulk Containers

單次使用系統 — 軟性散裝容器 | Pages 17–20

Overview 概覽

The term "flexible bulk container" refers to the application of single-use flexible technologies, commonly referred to as bioprocess containers, bioprocess bags, or single-use systems (SUS), for the manufacturing process of bulk drug substance and bulk drug product.

In general, these bags are available in "2-D" shapes, scaled from roughly 20 mL to 50 L, and in "3-D" shapes scaled from roughly 50 L to 3000 L in size. Historically, the use for bulk containers has been limited to less than 1000 L.

With their broad range of sizes, complexity of design, and polymer material characteristics, flexible bulk containers present unique challenges for establishing inherent SUS integrity.

中文解析 — SUS 是什麼？

Single-Use System (SUS) 即「單次使用系統」，是生物製藥製程中用於儲存、混合、傳輸原料藥或藥品的一次性軟性袋體，使用後即丟棄，無需清洗驗證。

2-D 平板袋
  • 容量：20 mL – 50 L
  • 外觀：扁平如枕頭
  • 適用：小批量、實驗室規模
  • 代表：Mobius® (Merck)
3-D 立體袋
  • 容量：50 L – 3000 L
  • 外觀：圓柱形桶狀
  • 適用：大批量生產
  • 代表：Flexsafe® (Sartorius)

CDMO 重要性

SUS 的最大優點是省去清洗驗證（CIP/SIP），但代價是必須對每個批次的完整性負責。在 CDMO 環境下，SUS 常用於細胞培養基、原料藥儲存與灌裝前的緩衝液準備。

4.2.1

Design 設計

4.2.1 DESIGN

For products not terminally sterilized, the sterility of the final drug product depends on the sterility of the bulk; therefore, the integrity of the bulk container system is crucial. An integrity assessment may even be required to support a sterile claim on the bulk container for terminally sterilized products.

The critical leak size of concern for flexible containers typically relates to microbial ingress, although liquid leaks can be a critical risk to some products, for example, environmental and operator safety related to toxicity. Gas permeation could also be a factor for product quality inside the SUS.

Barrier properties to gas exchange are established during the development of the laminate material, and the protection offered may need to be assessed during the selection stage. For establishing the integrity of the SUS, the MALL must be established based on the use conditions.

While SUS are out of the scope of USP <1207>, a lifecycle approach can be applied to CCI of these systems as well. How to evaluate the lifecycle and apply quality risk management (QRM) principles related to the SUS is described in ASTM WK64337.

設計重點解析

MALL 定義

MALL（Maximum Allowable Leakage Limit）= 最大允許洩漏限值。對 SUS 而言，MALL 必須根據實際使用條件來確立，例如：灌裝時的靜壓力、運輸振動、高度變化等。

USP <1207> 不適用 SUS

USP <1207> 的範圍僅涵蓋「有限制性密封元件」的容器（如瓶/塞/蓋組合），SUS 的軟性結構不符合此定義。但 ASTM WK64337 正在制訂專屬 SUS 的完整性指引，補充這個監管空白。

比喻：提袋 vs. 硬盒

SUS 就像軟質購物袋：你可以擠壓它、折疊它，但每一個縫線交接點都是潛在的破洞。相比之下，玻璃瓶就像硬質盒子，形變有限，更容易評估。

三大風險因子：

  • 微生物侵入 — 最常見的 MALL 驅動因素
  • 液體洩漏 — 對高毒性產品（如腫瘤科藥物）有操作員安全疑慮
  • 氣體滲透 — 影響對 O₂ 敏感的生物製品穩定性
4.2.2

Challenges 挑戰

4.2.2 CHALLENGES

During routine production, transportation, and handling, various scenarios and events may arise that can affect the integrity of the SUS. Qualification and a holistic approach should be used to ensure product quality as well as ensure patient and operator safety.

A good understanding of the failure mode along the product lifecycle and the result of a risk analysis may lead to implementation of nondestructive integrity testing. This testing could be performed at the supplier's manufacturing site or at the user's site, depending on the risks to be mitigated.

One key challenge for integrity testing on bulk containers is defining the critical leak size, or MALL, that the system must satisfy. Suppliers of bulk containers own the design of the whole system and therefore should be expected to qualify the containers against such a requirement.

Existing literature does not support a universal maximum defect size for flexible bulk containers to determine the MALL for any specified test method. However, a maximum defect size for common use conditions, like the transport of filled bulk containers, must be determined in order to select a corresponding test method. The key step to determining the MALL is to measure it during the intended use condition.

挑戰核心：接合點 = 失效風險

70+
Large SUS assemblies may have more than 70 joining points
大型 SUS 組件可能有超過 70 個接合點

關鍵風險思維

每個接合點（luer 接頭、軟管倒刺、焊接薄膜、歧管連接）都是一個潛在洩漏點。對於 70 個接合點的系統，如果每個接合點的失效概率是 0.1%，整個組件的完整失效概率接近 7%——遠高於單個玻璃瓶的風險。

SUS 複雜性來源

  • 膜對膜密封（film-to-film seals）— 熱封質量難以 100% 驗證
  • 軟性材料形變 — 填充後形狀改變，使測試表面積難以評估
  • 輻射滅菌效應 — gamma 輻射可能使聚合物材料脆化，增加微裂縫
  • 連接器多樣性 — luer、hose barb、tri-clamp 等各類接頭

供應商 vs. 用戶責任劃分

SUS 供應商負責：設計驗證、MALL 確立、製造放行測試。
CDMO 用戶負責：使用前檢測、運輸驗證、操作相容性確認。
兩者缺一不可，責任不能單方轉移。

4.2.3

Test Method Considerations 測試方法考量

4.2.3 TEST METHODS

Microbial challenge testing is a well-known approach for identifying the MALL if prevention of microbial ingress is the critical container quality attribute. Two types of microbial integrity test methods are commonly used: testing by immersion and testing by aerosol.

Recent studies have demonstrated aerosol testing for microbial ingress to be equally as stringent as the immersion method, showing a MALL of 2 µm no matter which method is used. With aerosol testing, the ability to easily test a greater number of samples is an advantage in reducing the impact of the probabilistic nature of the test.

Once the MALL is determined, both probabilistic and deterministic physical methods are available for testing bulk containers; the industry is encouraged to evaluate deterministic methods. Helium leak detection applied to bulk containers is the most sensitive technology available. Its complex nature and relatively high investment cost suggest this technology would be used more appropriately by the supplier for release testing.

Pressure drop technology is less complex, less expensive, easier to operate, and sensitive to detecting leaks of concern to check for final integrity directly before use. Larger defects than the MALL are more likely to occur during normal operations.

Table 4.2.3-1: Comparison of Physical SUS Integrity Testing Methods

Four major obstacles for developing a reliable 100% release-testing system:

  • Size of leaks that occur in real life.
  • Maximum leak size that poses no risk to microbial ingress (MALL).
  • Minimum leak size that can be rapidly and reliably detected by the inspection system (LoD).
  • Selection and creation of model defects for qualification of the test method.

測試方法決策框架

微生物挑戰測試：2 種方法比較

浸沒法（Immersion）

將 SUS 浸入含菌液中，施加壓力後檢查內部汙染。傳統上認為較嚴苛、統計上較可靠。

氣溶膠法（Aerosol）

將 SUS 暴露於含菌氣溶膠環境，更能代表實際運輸/使用條件，可測試更多樣品。

新發現：兩種方法靈敏度相當，MALL 均為 2 µm。

氦氣 vs. 壓力降：如何選擇？

氦氣洩漏檢測 → 供應商放行測試首選（靈敏度高、成本可分攤）

壓力降技術 → 使用前現場測試首選（操作簡單、設備成本低）

兩者不互斥，在生命週期不同節點各有角色。

比喻：MALL vs. LoD 的三角關係

想像 MALL 是「允許通過的最大汙染物尺寸」，LoD 是「儀器能偵測到的最小洩漏尺寸」。理想情況：

LoD ≤ MALL

若 LoD 大於 MALL，則測試法無法保護產品——這是大型複雜 SUS 常見的困境。

CDMO 實務：驗收測試計畫

作為 SUS 用戶，CDMO 應要求供應商提供：

  • 每批 SUS 的 100% 放行測試記錄（helium 或壓力降）
  • MALL 確立的科學依據文件
  • 運輸驗證報告（振動、衝擊測試）
  • 輻射滅菌後完整性驗證數據

思考練習

你的 CDMO 從供應商採購 200 L SUS 袋用於 mRNA 原料液儲存。供應商聲稱已進行 100% 壓力降測試。你需要向他們索取哪些額外資訊才能接受這份放行測試報告？

提示：考慮 MALL、LoD、使用條件相符性

4.2.4

Additional Considerations 其他考量

4.2.4 ADDITIONAL

Flexible bulk packaging systems are increasingly being used for drug substance, drug product, and fill-finish applications in the biotech industry. Sound, scientific test methods are needed to ensure their quality throughout the product lifecycle.

The current model to ensure integrity is based on a quality by design (QbD) and QRM approach that should be applied by suppliers and users. Suppliers must possess a strong understanding of the design and qualification of a product for its intended use. Likewise, users must ensure their handling and other processes are compatible with the design of the SUS. Both suppliers and users should consider the method and frequency of testing as part of risk mitigation.

New technologies will continue to develop to meet the market needs, such as faster inspection times and greater sensitivity. Regardless of the capability of any single test method, it must always fit into an overall approach to quality and risk mitigation through each step of the supply chain.

Ongoing research on MALLs and the comparison of real-world to model defects is required to enhance understanding and increase integrity assurance for flexible bulk container systems.

整合框架：QbD + QRM for SUS

生命週期完整性管理模型

SUS 的 CCI 不是單一測試點，而是貫穿整個生命週期的管理框架：

  1. 設計開發階段 — 材料選擇、接合點設計、MALL 初步確立
  2. 供應商放行階段 — 100% 氦氣/壓力降測試
  3. 運輸驗證階段 — 振動、衝擊、高度模擬
  4. 使用前確認階段 — 現場壓力降測試或視覺檢查
  5. 使用後評估階段 — 端對端完整性（end-of-use testing）

SUS 的清洗驗證 vs. 完整性測試

SUS 最大優點是不需要清洗驗證（一次性使用，避免交叉汙染）。但這不等於不需要完整性測試——完整性測試反而更加重要，因為一旦袋體失效，你無法重新清洗再用。

CDMO 採購決策重點

選擇 SUS 供應商時，除價格外，應重點評估：

  • 是否提供 ASTM WK64337 合規的完整性文件
  • 是否有專用的完整性測試設備（非目視檢查替代）
  • 是否能支援用戶現場的使用前測試
  • 客訴與調查回應速度（Field Alert 歷史）
4.3

IV Bags — Flexible Finished Pharmaceutical Containers

IV 袋 — 軟性成品藥容器 | Pages 21–22

Overview 概覽

An IV bag is a pharmaceutical product container made of flexible material, closed at the bottom and along the sides by sealing; the top of the container may be closed by fusion to IV tubing or a medication port, depending on the intended use.

Because it comes in direct contact with the pharmaceutical product or formulation, IV bags are made of materials that will not alter the quality (efficacy or stability) of the product or formulation. Some of the information in this section may be relevant to considerations for bulk containers as well.

IV 袋 vs. SUS 袋的關鍵差異

4.3.1

Design 設計

4.3.1 DESIGN

Flexible packages often undergo temporary shape changes caused by forces within the environment, such as damages incurred during distribution and storage. The magnitude of forces sufficient to cause loss of package sterility by converting a defect into a leak must be considered when trying to determine an appropriate CCIT method that can reliably detect the leak of concern.

Imposed pressures capable of initiating liquid or gas flow through a defect provide the necessary conditions for ingress or egress to occur and, subsequently, result in a loss of sterility or quality of the product.

For a specific product package system, an understanding of product's liquid properties, such as surface tension and viscosity, is useful to determine the critical leak for that system.

IV 袋設計要素

關鍵設計組件

  • 端口設計（Port Design） — 輸液口、加藥口與薄膜的密封介面是最脆弱的部位
  • 外包裝（Overwrap） — 保護主袋免受機械損傷與氣體滲透
  • 薄膜材料 — PVC、非 PVC（PP/PE 多層膜）等不同材質影響滲透性

液體特性影響 CCI 偵測

表面張力高的液體（如純水）能在微小缺陷處形成液橋，需要更大壓力才會洩漏——導致洩漏「難以發生」但若發生則難以偵測。

黏度高的液體（如蛋白質製劑）流動更慢，可能造成微孔堵塞，干擾測試結果。

4.3.2

Challenges 挑戰

4.3.2 CHALLENGES

When liquid-filled IV bags are tested for CCI, any leak from the container can pose a challenge if the product leaks and contaminates the test system (equipment and/or test chamber). The residual liquid or vapor that leaks into a test chamber from a defect in the container must be removed and the test system must be cleaned before testing can resume. This activity can affect the throughput of the test method.

A defect in direct contact with air may be detected at a faster rate than a defect in contact with a liquid or product solution, and the location of the defect can have a significant impact. The product solution of some IV bags can also dry and crystallize, potentially clogging micron-size defects and making leak detection a challenge.

The escape of residual volatile gases and vapors from the outside surface of IV bags when they are exposed to vacuum conditions may affect leak test results by giving false positives during testing (e.g., with vacuum decay or mass extraction test methods). With such methods, the use of special fixtures or the inclusion of equilibration time before the actual test time may be necessary.

Positive controls (or engineered defects) for IV bags can pose a challenge when considering leak detection through micron-sized holes. No one method can detect every different type of defect. Hence, the CCIT tool kit should encompass a variety of methods.

IV 袋五大 CCI 挑戰

挑戰 1：液體汙染測試設備

若 IV 袋有真實洩漏，藥液進入測試腔後必須清洗，直接降低測試吞吐量（throughput）。對生產線而言，停線清洗的代價極高。

挑戰 2：缺陷位置影響偵測速率

接觸空氣的缺陷比接觸液體的缺陷更容易被快速偵測——這意味著液面以下的缺陷可能被低估。

挑戰 3：藥液結晶堵塞微孔

部分 IV 袋藥液（如某些注射用抗生素、電解質液）在乾燥後會結晶，可能暫時堵塞微米級缺陷，造成「假陰性」——缺陷存在但測試通過。

挑戰 4：真空測試假陽性問題

IV 袋表面的殘留揮發氣體（如滅菌後的水蒸氣）在抽真空時會大量釋出，導致真空衰減法（Vacuum Decay）或質量萃取法（Mass Extraction）出現假陽性。解決方案：在測試前加入足夠的「平衡時間」（equilibration time）或使用特殊夾具限制測試區域。

挑戰 5：正對照的工程難度

為軟性 IV 袋製作可重複的微米級人工缺陷（正對照）非常困難——每次鑽孔或雷射打孔的結果差異很大，且薄膜材料的彈性可能讓孔洞自動閉合。

4.3.3

Test Method Considerations 測試方法考量

4.3.3 TEST METHODS

The condition of the test specimens can have a significant impact on the test results; for example, the temperature of flex bag, time between sterilization and testing, humidity, and many other factors can influence test results. Moisture may be present on the surface of liquid-filled IV bags, especially after moist heat sterilization, which may make it difficult to detect leaks with certain technologies.

Certain IV bags may not be suitable for leak detection methods that employ high vacuum or high pressure due to the potential for bursting or dislodging the container closure components.

Deterministic method options are HVLD, vacuum decay, mass extraction, force decay, and laser headspace analysis (tunable laser diode spectroscopy). Probabilistic method options are dye or microbial ingress, and internal pressure.

Certain CCIT methods, like HVLD, detect leaks in the walls of nonporous, rigid, or flexible packaging containing liquid product. Hence, this method is not compatible with dry pharmaceutical drug products or product packages with liquid on the exterior of the package (e.g., liquid loads immediately removed from a sterilizer).

Some films that are used in the manufacture of IV bags are readily permeable to inert gases like helium, which makes application of a helium leak test method a challenge for that specific type of CCS.

Natural or real-world CCI defects are generally larger in size than engineered defects and represent all possible CCI failure modes. Natural CCI defects may not be pristine holes but rather long, complex, irregular channels and tortuous pathways.

IV 袋可用測試方法彙整

重點：IV 袋薄膜可能對氦氣有滲透性

部分 IV 袋使用的多層薄膜（如非 PVC PP/PE 膜）對氦氣具有固有滲透性，這使氦氣洩漏測試出現假陽性而不適用。這是選擇 CCI 方法時必須提前確認的材料特性。

4.3.4

Additional Considerations 其他考量

4.3.4 ADDITIONAL

Drug products can interact with defects in IV bags in ways that can interfere with the leak detection capability of the specific CCIT method. The possible impact to product stability (e.g., HVLD or short-wave infrared imaging) should be considered in the application of certain CCIT methods.

Inherent package integrity is typically established during product-package development and manufacturing qualification. The permeation rate of gaseous molecules through the material of an IV bag must be defined and differentiated from a true leak through a micron-size defect that is critical to product quality. Some gaseous permeation may be acceptable; leakage is not.

滲透 vs. 洩漏：關鍵區分

Permeation 滲透 vs. Leakage 洩漏

滲透（Permeation）是氣體分子通過完整薄膜材料的擴散——這是材料的固有特性，在一定範圍內是可接受的。

洩漏（Leakage）是通過缺陷（孔洞、裂縫、未密封的接口）發生的液體或氣體流動——這是不可接受的，直接代表 CCI 失效。

在 IV 袋 CCI 研究中，必須先量化材料固有滲透率，才能設定 CCI 方法的判定閾值（threshold）。

CDMO 稽查準備

FDA/EMA 稽查員在審查 IV 袋 CCI 驗證時會問：

  • 如何區分滲透率訊號與洩漏訊號？
  • 使用前 vs. 使用後測試如何安排？
  • HVLD 對產品是否有光降解風險已評估？
  • 正對照缺陷是否能在測試條件下被可靠偵測？
4.4

Cryogenic Conditions

低溫條件下的容器完整性 | Pages 22–26

Overview 概覽

Many sterile products are stored at cold (2 °C to 8 °C), freezing (-20 °C), and ultra-cold (-80 °C to -196 °C) temperatures to limit formulation degradation and extend product shelf life. Several considerations should be addressed regarding cryogenic storage and package integrity:

  • Risks to package after fill and freeze.
  • Risks to large-scale bags after fill and freeze.
  • Loss of integrity in frozen state due to material differences at low temperatures that are restored at high temperatures.
  • Risk to package integrity in frozen state during transport and handling.

儲存溫度分布

2–8 °C 冷藏（Refrigeration）— 蛋白質製劑、疫苗
-20 °C 冷凍（Freezing）— 次常用溫度，部分小分子注射劑
-80 °C 超低溫（Ultra-cold）— 臨床樣品、活病毒疫苗、mRNA LNP
-196 °C 液態氮（Liquid Nitrogen）— 細胞治療、CAR-T

為何低溫 CCI 特別困難？

低溫下的 CCI 失效具有「自我修復」特性——在冷凍狀態下失效，升溫後橡膠塞回彈，外觀看起來完整。傳統目視檢查完全無法偵測這類問題。

4.4.1

Design — Cryogenic Storage Observations 設計：低溫儲存觀察

4.4.1 DESIGN

Refrigeration storage temperatures in the 2 °C to 8 °C range are common for many products. Freezer storage at -20 °C is the next most common temperature. Ultra-cold storage, beginning at -80 °C, is most commonly used for clinical trial samples where complete stability data may not yet be available (e.g., live viral vaccines); it is also the temperature of dry ice, commonly used for the shipment of product.

Observations of overpressure have been reported in a significant number of vials after storage at -80 °C. When syringes were inserted into these vials, the plungers pushed outwards and, when the syringe was removed, product sprayed from needle puncture holes. Syringe plunger movement after being inserted into a vial, or product spraying out of a needle hole, indicates a substantial overpressure inside the vial.

When vials exhibiting the overpressure phenomena were tested using laser headspace gas analysis methods, the results clearly showed changes in headspace conditions different from the specified manufacturing process parameters. The vials should have, at room temperature, a headspace gas composition of air (20.9% oxygen / 79.1% nitrogen / 0.04% CO₂) and a pressure of 1 atmosphere.

Figures 4.4.1-1, 4.4.1-2, and 4.4.1-3 show examples of changes in headspace gas composition and pressure after storage at -80 °C on dry ice. The measurements show excessive levels of CO₂ (atmospheric air has <0.4 mbar), reduced levels of oxygen (atmospheric air has 20.9% oxygen), and elevated levels of total pressure (atmospheric air is 1000 mbar). These headspace changes have been attributed to a CCI failure while the product was stored at -80 °C.

The root cause of CCI loss at cold temperatures is believed to be a result of physical changes to packaging components at low temperatures. Elastomers and glass have markedly different thermal expansion coefficients, and stopper elastomers have glass transition temperatures similar to storage temperatures. The greater thermal contraction of elastomer materials, compared to glass, and the material transition below the glass transition temperature to more plastic-like properties, increases the possibility of a CCI failure.

When CCI is compromised, nonsterile reactive gases from the surrounding environment enter the package and are trapped; once the vial warms to room temperature, the elastomer regains its elastic properties and returns to its room-temperature dimensions and fit.

案例分析：超壓現象與頭空間指標

3 / 20
在 20 支於 -80 °C（乾冰）儲存的樣品中
3 支顯示 CO₂ 超標 / O₂ 降低 / 總壓升高

超壓的臨床風險

當注射器插入已回溫的失效瓶時，瓶內超壓（>1 atm）會推出注射器活塞，並在取出針頭時噴出藥液。這不只是一個「完整性問題」——這是一個直接的操作員安全風險（尤其對高活性腫瘤學或生物製劑）。

物理機制比喻

想像一個充氣氣球（橡膠塞）放在冰箱裡——它會收縮，讓瓶口露出縫隙。外界的 CO₂（乾冰環境）趁機滲入。當氣球（橡膠塞）回到室溫，它又膨脹封住縫隙，把 CO₂ 鎖在裡面，造成超壓。

4.4.2

Challenges — Thermal Properties of Packaging Components 挑戰：包材熱學特性

4.4.2 CHALLENGES

Glass vials used to package finished sterile products are commonly made of borosilicate materials. Stoppers are primarily butyl elastomers. Each of these components have coefficients of linear thermal expansion and may also have glass transition temperatures.

The change in temperature of a sterile product package is significant, from room temperature of 20 °C to ultra-cold temperature of -80 °C and, possibly, to liquid nitrogen temperatures of -196 °C.

Key quantitative data: The volume of a butyl elastomer stopper will decrease by 6% when the temperature drops from 20 °C to -20 °C, and decrease by 11% when the temperature drops from 20 °C to -70 °C (the glass transition temperature). Below the glass transition temperature, the thermal expansion-contraction rate of materials decreases, but a further decrease in temperature, to -196 °C, could result in a total stopper volume decrease of up to 20% compared to room temperature volume.

In contrast, over the same temperature range (room temperature to -196 °C), borosilicate glass undergoes a volume change of less than 1%.

When the temperature drops from 20 °C to -80 °C, the stopper will contract to 88% of its original volume while the glass vial contracts to 99.5%. Below the glass transition temperature, the stopper loses its elasticity and becomes rigid and more plastic-like.

As the temperature drops to -80 °C, the headspace gas pressure in the vial drops to 66% of its initial room temperature value (1000 mbar drops to 660 mbar). When a stopper–vial seal loses integrity at -80 °C, the pressure difference between the 1 atmosphere storage environment and the 0.66 atmosphere inside the vial will drive the outside gas into the vial headspace.

If a vial is stored at -80 °C on dry ice and loses CCI below the glass transition temperature of -70 °C, the vial will fill with 0.35 atmosphere of CO₂. Over a longer storage period, the oxygen in the vial will diffuse out and be replaced with CO₂. If the vial is then removed from dry ice storage and warmed, the stopper will regain elasticity at the glass transition temperature and the vial will reseal. The headspace pressure will increase from 1 atmosphere at -70 °C to 1.4 atmosphere at 20 °C.

定量分析：橡膠塞收縮的物理學

體積收縮率對比

溫度變化: 20°C → -80°C

橡膠塞（丁基橡膠）:
  20°C → -20°C:  體積減少  6%
  20°C → -70°C:  體積減少 11%（玻璃化轉變溫度）
  20°C → -196°C: 體積減少 20%

硼矽玻璃瓶:
  20°C → -196°C: 體積減少 <1%

差異: 橡膠塞收縮 = 玻璃瓶收縮的 20倍

這個熱收縮差異是低溫 CCI 失效的根本原因。

頭空間壓力計算（Gay-Lussac 定律）

P₁/T₁ = P₂/T₂  （體積固定）

室溫: T₁ = 293 K, P₁ = 1000 mbar
-80°C: T₂ = 193 K
P₂ = 1000 × (193/293) = 659 mbar ≈ 660 mbar

乾冰環境洩漏後回溫:
-70°C (重新密封) → 20°C 回溫:
壓力 = 1000 × (293/203) ≈ 1443 mbar
= 1.4 atm 超壓！

玻璃化轉變溫度（Tg）的意義

丁基橡膠的 Tg 約為 -70 °C。在此溫度以下：

  • 橡膠塞從「彈性體」變為「剛性塑料」
  • 失去主動密封力，無法補償收縮造成的間隙
  • 材料更脆，承受低溫震動時更易破裂

-80 °C 儲存剛好超過 Tg，是最危險的儲存溫度。

4.4.3

Test Method Considerations 測試方法考量

4.4.3 TEST METHODS

Measuring changes in headspace gas composition and pressure after cryogenic storage can provide insight into whether a package has maintained CCI. A key point is that the CCI failure is temporary. As the vial warms up, the elastomer regains its elastic properties, expands, and reseals the vial.

Some methods are well suited for measuring CCI failure. Laser headspace analysis is uniquely suited to study changes nondestructively in headspace gas composition and pressure to determine CCI failures. Helium leak detection and residual seal force measurement can also be used to indicate changes from cold conditions.

When CCI is lost in a sterile pharmaceutical vial stored at cryogenic temperatures, the gas composition and pressure in the vial headspace will change, and headspace analysis can measure those changes. Depending on the cold storage atmosphere (air in a freezer or CO₂ on dry ice), the increases in CO₂ or total pressure and/or the decreases in oxygen concentration can be measured.

低溫 CCI 偵測的核心方法

雷射頭空間分析（Laser Headspace Analysis）

使用TDLS（可調諧雷射二極體光譜法），非破壞性地測量瓶內 CO₂、O₂ 濃度及總壓。

為何是低溫 CCI 首選？

  • CCI 失效是「暫時性的」——回溫後外觀完整，只有頭空間組成改變
  • 雷射可在不破壞樣品的情況下讀取頭空間
  • 可同時測量 CO₂↑、O₂↓、P↑ 三個指標
  • 適合 100% 批次放行測試

測試時機：原位（In-Situ）vs. 回溫後

原位測試（In-Situ）：在 -80 °C 下進行測試，可直接偵測洩漏狀態，但設備需低溫相容，技術難度高。

回溫後測試：回到室溫後，雖然瓶口已重新密封，但頭空間組成的「痕跡」仍可被雷射分析偵測——這是目前最可行的方法。

殘餘密封力（Residual Seal Force, RSF）的應用

RSF 測量橡膠塞對玻璃瓶口的壓縮力。低溫後的 RSF 下降可預測性地指示 CCI 風險——即使外觀尚未失效，RSF 降低就是早期預警信號。在 CAR-T 細胞治療產品的 CCI 驗證中，RSF 測試是重要的補充工具。

4.4.4

Additional Considerations for Cryogenic Conditions 低溫條件的其他考量

4.4.4 ADDITIONAL

Several studies have been published in the PDA Journal of Science and Technology or presented at PDA meetings on the study of CCI failures of sterile product packages stored at cryogenic temperatures and other established sources.

Some of these thermal properties of parenteral packaging components, found in the common glass vial, stopper, and crimp assembly, should be given consideration in the context of cryogenic storage. The lifecycle approach advocated by USP <1207> means that CCI should be assessed as part of the package development phase for all products destined for cryogenic storage.

The coldest storage temperatures are used for storing cellular materials, which use liquid nitrogen as the refrigerant at a temperature of -196 °C. Package integrity must be maintained at the lowest temperature a package will experience over its intended shelf life. The loss of CCI at any temperature leads to sterility and stability risks to the product and patient as well as to the clinicians administering the products.

CDMO 前沿應用：細胞治療與 mRNA

CAR-T 細胞治療（-196 °C 液態氮）

CAR-T 產品在液態氮中儲存，每個患者樣本的不可替代性使 CCI 失效的風險遠高於一般製劑。目前這類容器（通常為冷凍袋或特殊冷凍管）缺乏成熟的 CCIT 標準，是業界正在努力解決的空白。

關鍵考量：液態氮溫度下，任何非金屬材料的脆化風險都需要特別評估。

mRNA 疫苗（-80 °C）

COVID-19 mRNA 疫苗的超低溫需求將 -80 °C CCI 問題推上業界議程。多劑量小瓶在反復取用（多次穿刺橡膠塞）後的 CCI 維持是新的驗證挑戰。每次穿刺都在橡膠塞上留下累積損傷，與低溫收縮共同作用可能加速失效。

USP <1207> 生命週期方法的應用

對超低溫儲存產品，生命週期 CCI 評估應包括：

  • 研發階段：包材選擇時的低溫 CCI 評估（材料 Tg 分析）
  • 製造階段：凍融驗證（freeze-thaw cycling）中的 CCI 監測
  • 貨架期：定期抽樣的雷射頭空間分析
  • 運輸驗證：乾冰/液態氮運輸過程中的 CCI 模擬

Section 2b Key Messages Summary

核心訊息總結 — 三大包裝挑戰

Key Messages

SUS: Complexity = Exponential Risk

  • 70+ joining points in large SUS assemblies create multiplicative failure risk
  • No single test method covers all lifecycle stages
  • Helium leak (supplier release) + pressure drop (point-of-use) is the recommended dual approach
  • MALL must be established under actual use conditions, not theoretical models
  • SUS eliminates cleaning validation but makes integrity testing MORE critical, not less

IV Bags: Liquid = Test Interference

  • Liquid content contaminates test chambers, reducing throughput
  • Crystallizing drug solutions can clog micro-defects → false negatives
  • Film permeability to helium makes helium leak test unsuitable for some IV bags
  • Vacuum/mass extraction methods require equilibration time to avoid false positives
  • Permeation (acceptable) vs. leakage (not acceptable) must be quantitatively differentiated

Cryogenic: Temporary Failure, Permanent Consequences

  • Butyl stopper contracts 11–20% vs. <1% for borosilicate glass at cryogenic temps
  • Glass transition temperature (-70 °C) = stopper loses elasticity = highest risk point
  • Headspace CO₂↑, O₂↓, P↑ = fingerprint of CCI failure during cryo storage
  • CCI failure is self-repairing on warming — invisible to visual inspection
  • Laser headspace analysis is the method of choice for post-cryo CCI verification

CDMO 應用要點

SUS 管理：供應商審計優先

在 CDMO，SUS 通常是由客戶指定供應商，但 QA 責任仍在工廠。應在技術協議中明確要求供應商提供：ASTM WK64337 合規文件、100% 放行測試數據、MALL 科學依據。

IV 袋：方法選擇必須考慮薄膜材質

不同薄膜材料的氦氣滲透性差異很大，方法開發前必須先進行材料相容性評估。HVLD 是充液袋的最常見選擇，但需確認測試前已移除外部水分。

低溫產品：從包材選擇開始

最有效的低溫 CCI 保障從包材選擇開始，而不是從測試方法開始。選用 Tg 遠低於儲存溫度的橡膠塞（如特殊配方丁基橡膠）、選用適當的壓蓋壓力——這些設計決策的影響遠大於任何測試方法。
