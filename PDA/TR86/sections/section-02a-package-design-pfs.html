4.0

Challenges with Package Design — Overview

包裝設計挑戰 — 概述 • Pages p10

Original Text (English)

Testing for CCI is intended to provide evidence of the use of appropriately matched components and an adequate closing process. Assurance of integrity is developed from consistency of the latter design parameters. This is simple with legacy CCS such as glass vials with stoppers or glass ampoules; however, the industry has progressed to using newer product delivery designs and different materials.

These newer designs are more complex and introduce a new learning curve for developing leak detection methods. Section 4 will offer current knowledge and perspectives regarding the impact of current package designs on package integrity and the impact on the performance and selection of CCIT.

中文解說 & 實務觀點

核心概念：CCI 的根本目的

CCI 測試的目的是提供證據，證明使用了相互匹配的元件，且密封過程充分。密封保證的基礎在於設計參數的一致性。

簡單說：CCI 不只是「測漏」，更是驗證整個密封系統設計和製程是否合理。

比喻說明：傳統 vs. 新型包裝

傳統 CCS（玻璃瓶 + 膠塞）就像標準門鎖——業界用了幾十年，密封原理清晰、驗證方法成熟。

新型包裝（PFS、SUS、IV 袋）就像智能鎖——功能更強，但結構更複雜，需要重新建立「怎麼測它是否真的密封好了」的方法論。

重點提示：新型包裝帶來的三大挑戰

  • 更多密封界面：每個界面都是潛在失效點
  • 新材料風險：聚合物、彈性體對環境應力敏感
  • 方法適用性問題：傳統 CCIT 方法未必適用於新型容器

4.1

Prefilled Syringes — Introduction

預充填注射器 — 概述 • Pages p10–p11

Original Text (English)

Prefilled syringes and cartridges are now a common dosage form for injectables. Design has progressed from standard glass prefilled syringes to plastic alternatives as well as assembled auto-injection systems. All of these advances add complexity to better understanding integrity issues and add more specific challenges to developing an approach to CCI that fits into the lifecycle assessment.

中文解說 & 實務觀點

PFS 發展歷程與複雜度遞增

預充填注射器（PFS）已成為注射劑的常見劑型，其發展脈絡：

  • 第一代：標準玻璃 PFS（硼矽酸玻璃筒）
  • 第二代：COP/COC 聚合物 PFS（更輕、更抗碎）
  • 第三代：組裝式自動注射系統（auto-injectors、pen-injectors）

每一代都增加了新的密封界面，使 CCI 驗證策略必須隨之演進。

生命週期評估觀點

TR86 強調 CCI 必須納入生命週期評估（lifecycle assessment），這意味著：

  • 開發階段：建立 MALL（最大允許洩漏量），選擇方法
  • 驗證階段：確認方法性能，建立接受標準
  • 常規生產：持續監控，確認製程穩定
  • 穩定性研究：確認整個貨架期內 CCI 維持

  3. PFS 的 auto-injector 組裝為何使 CCIT 更困難？請列舉至少兩個具體原因。

Original Text (English)

Three Seal Interfaces

Syringes are sealed at three key locations, called interfaces. The plunger stopper-barrel interface is the most common to all syringe (Fig. 4.1.1-1). Other interfaces are the needle-needle shield interface and the barrel-needle shield interface. For more detailed information on syringe design see PDA Technical Report No. 73: Prefilled Syringe User Requirements for Biotechnology Applications.

The seal interface (or closures) for each configuration presents challenges for package integrity and CCI testing. In addition, the syringe design, materials of construction, components, manufacturing processes, secondary packaging, and auto-injectors may also impact the integrity and testing of a prefilled syringe.

Since each type of syringe has its own type of closure system, unique considerations must be made for CCI of the barrel-closure interface. To maintain CCI, proper packaging and handling procedures should be in place to ensure that syringe closures remain properly positioned throughout the manufacture, fill-finish, and transportation of syringes.

Three Common PFS Designs

  • Staked needle (barrel-closure and the needle shield-needle interfaces)
  • Luer lock
  • Luer slip (or luer cone)
中文解說 & 實務觀點

三個密封界面的 CCI 重要性

PFS 的三個密封界面各有不同的 CCI 挑戰：

  • 界面 1 活塞塞－筒身界面（Plunger Stopper-Barrel）：最關鍵的主要屏障，存在於所有注射器；活塞壓縮配合形成密封
  • 界面 2 針頭－針頭護蓋界面（Needle-Needle Shield）：固定針專有；針尖嵌入彈性體中形成密封；護蓋必須完全包覆針尖但不刺穿
  • 界面 3 筒身－針頭護蓋界面（Barrel-Needle Shield）：確保整體針頭區域封閉性

CDMO 實務注意：矽油風險

TR86 明確指出，在魯爾鎖系統中，矽油污染魯爾鎖適配器可能影響密封完整性。這在實際生產中意味著：

  • 需要額外的產線清潔程序（line clearance）
  • 確保殘留矽油不在魯爾鎖適配器上聚集
  • 同樣適用於魯爾錐系統的尖頭蓋

Original Text (English) — Stopper & Lubricity

Elastomers and plunger stoppers provide excellent sealing properties when paired with glass and plastic containers. As with other pharmaceutical parenteral closures, halobutyl elastomers are most widely used for prefilled syringe plungers due to good drug compatibility. The elastomeric formulation must be carefully chosen and must be hard enough to provide sufficient rigidity for consistent functionality, while allowing the viscoelastic properties of the plunger material to provide closure properties.

The design of the plunger provides CCI and functionality of the system. To take advantage of the compressibility of the elastomer material, the plunger is designed to be slightly larger in circumference than the inner diameter of the chosen barrel.

ISO 11040-5:2012 recommends that plungers be designed to use all three ribs. In general, the rib closest to the drug is considered the primary sealing rib; the second and third ribs are meant to stabilize the seal and provide secondary CCI assurance.

The use of lubricity coatings on the syringe barrel will influence the ability of the container system to create CCI. Silicone oil and vapor-deposited coatings are the most commonly used. When silicone oil is sprayed on the inside of the barrel, the coating may or may not be heat-treated. Excess lubricity coating may result in the plunger moving too freely during transit, causing a breach in CCI.

中文解說 & 活塞設計重點

活塞塞設計：三肋原理

活塞塞（Plunger Stopper）的密封原理基於壓縮配合（compression fit）：

  • 活塞外徑 > 筒身內徑 → 形成壓縮密封
  • 第一肋（最靠近藥液）：主要密封肋 — 主要 CCI 屏障
  • 第二肋：穩定密封，提供次要 CCI 保證
  • 第三肋：第三 CCI 保證層，通常也連接活塞桿

ISO 11040-5:2012 規定應利用所有三個肋來最大化密封效果。

比喻說明：矽油的雙面角色

矽油（Silicone oil）就像汽車引擎中的機油——它讓活動部件順暢運作，但用量必須精確控制：

  • 矽油太少：活塞起始力（break-loose force）過大 → 難以注射，功能不佳
  • 矽油適量：功能正常，活塞壓緊筒身壁維持 CCI
  • 矽油太多：活塞在運輸中可能自由滑動 → 藥液外洩，CCI 失效

此外，未經熱固定的矽油在活塞推出時，壓力會將矽油推離界面——這反而有利於 CCI（活塞被「鎖住」），但代價是起始推力增大。

氟聚合物塗層的額外考量

部分活塞塞表面有氟聚合物積層薄膜（fluoropolymer laminate film），可改善藥物相容性。但需注意：

  • 機械安裝（推管法）時，壓縮和推送可能在薄膜上產生皺褶
  • 皺褶可能影響 CCI 性能，需評估
  • 真空塞封法較不易產生皺褶，但真空設定不正確可能導致藥液沸騰

Original Text (English) — Cartridge Systems

For cartridge containers, CCI is maintained by a crimped combination seal (also known as a lined seal or rubber septum) on one end of the cartridge and an elastomeric plunger on the other end. The pen-injector may be a single-dose cartridge that uses a monolayer combination seal, or it may be a multidose cartridge that uses a bilayer combination seal. Cartridges may also consist of a single or dual chamber. Various options exist for dual-chambered cartridges including liquid-liquid, liquid-lyophilized powder, or liquid-powder chambers.

The cartridge container must form a secure seal at the combination seal, cartridge, and cartridge plunger to ensure the system maintains CCI. For dual-chambered cartridges, specific consideration should be given to compatibility of the cartridge bypass feature (internal or external) and the cartridge plunger.

Combination seals perform a vital sealing function in a cartridge system. The combination seal must be adequately crimped to the cartridge with the appropriate compression to maintain CCI. During multiple use, the combination seal will be punctured multiple times and must maintain integrity throughout use and product shelf life. For multidose applications, an appropriate combination seal should be selected, including a bilayer elastomer with robust resealing properties.

中文解說 & 卡匣系統

多劑型卡匣的特殊 CCI 挑戰

多劑型卡匣（multidose cartridge）面臨特殊的 CCI 挑戰：組合封需要在每次穿刺後重新自封（resealing）。因此：

  • 需選用雙層彈性體（bilayer elastomer），具有強韌的重封性能
  • CCI 測試必須包括最大穿刺次數的情境
  • ISO 13926-3 中的重封性測試與 CCI 測試是不同的概念，不可混淆

4.1.2

Prefilled Syringe Challenges

預充填注射器挑戰 • Pages p14–p16

Original Text (English) — Polymer PFS

Alternate materials of construction provide additional challenges with design. Prefilled syringes made of glass have long been available. Polymeric (or plastic) syringes in single-use or combination formats are available as an alternate material and have some advantages over glass. Prefilled plastic or polymer syringes contain a barrel typically made from cyclic olefin polymers or copolymers (COP/COC). Polymer barrel materials are stronger, tougher, and more flexible, allowing for improved design and dimension control; consequently, they are used more often and, in some cases, seen as a potential replacement for glass syringes.

Syringes composed of polymers or copolymers present additional CCI risks. Polymeric materials are prone to undergoing physicochemical changes upon exposure to heat, oxygen, irradiation, mechanical stresses, or various combinations of such stress conditions throughout manufacturing, sterilization, and storage. These risks are related not only to the syringe barrel design, but to its molding and sterilization processes, downstream handling, packaging, and distribution processes, all of which must be identified, assessed, evaluated, and mitigated.

The supplier of plastic syringe components is responsible for establishing and validating design requirements as well as critical process parameters (CPPs) for ensuring CCI. Appropriate CCIT methods should be applied to verify that the inherent leak rate of the syringe design conforms, at a minimum, to the MALL specified in USP <1207> for preserving product sterility and drug formulation content.

中文解說 & 聚合物 PFS 挑戰

責任分工：供應商 vs. MAH

TR86 明確了聚合物 PFS 的責任分工：

  • 塑膠注射器元件供應商：負責建立和驗證設計要求及 CPPs
  • 上市許可持有人（MAH）：負責定義產品特定的 CCI 要求（如 MALL），並確保塑膠筒身和彈性體活塞形成一個符合要求的整合系統

在 CDMO 環境中，這意味著需要明確合約規範，確定每方的 CCI 相關責任。

比喻說明：聚合物的「壓力疲勞」

聚合物 PFS 就像塑膠水瓶——新的時候很牢固，但長期暴露在高溫、UV、機械應力下，材料會逐漸劣化（老化、變脆）。對 PFS 來說，這意味著：

  • 滅菌條件（熱、輻照）→ 材料理化變化 → 密封性能下降
  • 長期儲存 → 尺寸蠕變（creep）→ 活塞壓縮力降低
  • 這些都必須在穩定性研究中追蹤和驗證

Original Text (English) — Filling, Stoppering & Fill Volume

Filling and stoppering a prefilled syringe may impact CCI. When using mechanical means of placing the plunger stopper, the stopper may be compressed into a tube, the tube inserted into the syringe, and a metal rod used to push the stopper out of the tube where it expands to form a compression fit inside the syringe barrel. If the plunger is laminated with a fluoropolymer coating, the compression and pushing may create wrinkles on the plunger which should be evaluated for any impact to CCI.

Vacuum stoppering is another means of stoppering the syringe; it involves creating a vacuum in the syringe, thus pulling the plunger into the syringe. Vacuum methods are less likely to create wrinkles on a fluoropolymer coating, but an incorrect vacuum setting may cause the filled liquid in the syringe to boil, creating other concerns.

The fill volume of samples should be sufficient to conduct the testing method. For example, if dye ingress testing will include spectrophotometric testing, the volume of fill must be sufficient to fill the cuvette used by the instrument. In the case of HVLD, the fill volume must be sufficient for the conducting liquid to cover any defects.

In laser-based headspace oxygen analysis, the sample fill level should allow a minimum headspace volume. The laser light should be able to pass through the headspace region of the sample without being scattered by the liquid product or by the syringe shoulder.

A filled syringe without secondary items, such as plunger rod, backstop, label, or anti-needle stick device, is referred to as a semi-finished syringe. Once all the desired components are added, the syringe is referred to as a finished syringe. Since the addition of these secondary items may impact different seal interface points, the CCI of the finished syringe should be assessed.

中文解說 & 灌裝塞封影響

半成品與成品注射器的 CCI 策略

TR86 明確區分兩個關鍵狀態：

  • 半成品注射器（Semi-finished Syringe）：已灌裝密封，但尚未添加活塞桿、背封、標籤、防針刺裝置等
  • 成品注射器（Finished Syringe）：所有元件均已安裝

由於二次包裝步驟可能影響密封界面，成品注射器的 CCI 必須單獨評估，不能僅依賴半成品的測試結果。

CDMO 實務：灌裝塞封工藝選擇對 CCI 的影響

在 CDMO 的 Fill-Finish 操作中，塞封工藝選擇影響 CCI：

  • 機械推管法：活塞先壓縮進導管，再推入筒身膨脹密封。FPT 積層活塞容易產生皺褶，需在工藝開發中評估
  • 真空塞封法：較溫和，不易產生皺褶，但真空設定必須精確。真空不足 → 活塞無法正確定位；真空過強 → 藥液沸騰
  • 工藝驗證（PV）應包括 CCI 測試，確認塞封工藝的 CPPs 範圍

4.1.3

Test Method Considerations for Prefilled Syringes

預充填注射器的測試方法考量 • Pages p16–p17

Original Text (English)

Decisions regarding which CCIT method to select for prefilled syringes should be based on packaged product physical properties such as density, viscosity, electrical conductivity, molecule size, and evaporation capability.

Although many methods are available for CCIT of prefilled syringe or cartridge systems, CCIT after the assembly of prefilled syringes with auto-injectors or pen-injectors creates a new set of challenges. Disposable auto-injectors are designed to avoid tampering with the primary packaging container. Consequently, using the standard battery of CCIT methods like dye ingress, HVLD, and vacuum decay may not be suitable for this purpose; instead, they must be adapted. An adaptation may involve disassembly of the device before testing, which should verify that the disassembly method does not impact integrity or accept the decreased method sensitivity that testing the intact device would allow.

Risk assessment of a fully assembled syringe and device and its impact on CCIT should be considered. The following factors could compromise the CCIT and may need to be evaluated as part of the design verification:

  • Forces applied onto the primary container closure during final assembly, storage, and use of the device that may cause breakage or defects.
  • Critical forces on the needle shield (rigid or flexible needle shield), cartridge septum, or cartridge cap.
  • Critical forces on the plunger stopper (in cartridges or prefilled syringes).

Testing the actual packaged product for CCI is preferred; however, some solution properties inhibit leak detection, preventing direct testing. When the physical properties of a packaged product inhibit the use of otherwise suitable technologies, surrogate solutions may be used. For example, protein-based drug products are known for their propensity to dry in created defects; this inhibits the ability to detect those created defects by otherwise sensitive, deterministic technologies.

Stopper movement should be deliberately managed during CCIT. If the test methods exploit a pressure differential between the container interior and the test chamber (e.g., vacuum decay and mass extraction), stopper movement should be minimized. Stopper movement, and the resulting change in headspace volume, will reduce the pressure differential, possibly reducing the sensitivity of the method.

The application of a pressure differential to the prefilled syringe during CCIT may cause the stopper to move outward and then move back to its ordinary position once the pressure differential is removed. Stopper movement might allow entry of unwanted foreign matter; in addition, microbial contamination can be caused that could compromise the product. The adoption of technical solutions to prevent stopper movement during testing is recommended. For instance, in a vacuum decay system, one way to overcome this risk is to balance the outward stopper movement with a force equal in magnitude and opposite in direction to that of the stopper itself; this can be achieved by using a contrast pin in the test chamber.

中文解說 & 測試方法選擇

活塞移動問題：CCI 測試的特有難題

這是 PFS 有別於其他容器（如瓶子）的最大 CCIT 挑戰。當真空衰減或質量萃取法施加壓差時：

  • 外部真空 → 筒內相對正壓 → 活塞向外（朝針頭端）移動
  • 活塞移動 → 頭空間體積增大 → 壓差降低 → 方法靈敏度下降
  • 更危險的是：活塞移動可能引入異物或微生物污染
  • 壓差移除後，活塞可能移回，掩蓋污染事件

對位針（Contrast Pin）：緩解活塞移動的工程解決方案

在真空衰減測試系統中，可以使用對位針（contrast pin）來抵抗活塞移動：

  • 對位針施加一個等大反向的力，抵消真空造成的活塞外移趨勢
  • 這樣既可施加足夠的測試壓差，又能保持活塞位置穩定
  • 這是 PFS 真空衰減 CCIT 的重要設備設計考量

在 CDMO 評估 CCIT 設備時，應詢問供應商是否有針對 PFS 的對位針設計。

比喻說明：蛋白質「堵塞缺陷」問題

蛋白質藥物（如抗體）就像蛋液——缺陷越小，蛋白質越容易在缺陷開口處乾燥形成「膜」，把缺陷堵住。

這就是為什麼即使靈敏的確定性方法（如真空衰減）也可能在蛋白質藥物中漏檢真實缺陷。解決方案：使用注射用水（WFI）作為代用液進行方法性能驗證，再結合真實產品的橋接研究。

4.1.4

Additional Considerations for Secondary Packaging

二次包裝的額外考量 • Pages p17

Original Text (English)

A risk-based analysis should be performed to determine if any of the secondary packaging operations could impact CCI, and if CCIT should be performed to confirm that the integrity of the primary container system is maintained. If such a unit operation is identified, a CCI study should be performed on worst-case samples to demonstrate that the unit operation does not impact container integrity.

With the increasing use of self-administered prefilled syringes, secondary packaging of the filled syringe following completion of primary packaging takes place prior to distribution. Secondary packaging typically consists of (in order) plunger rod placement, finger flange placement, labeling, tray placement, and carton insertion.

Secondary packaging of a prefilled syringe can be performed manually by a technician, by a fully automated packaging line, or by a combination of manual and automated packaging.

Plunger Rod Placement: The most commonly used method is screwing the plunger rod into the back of the threaded stopper. The stopper serves as the primary barrier that maintains container integrity. Consequently, the process of inserting the plunger rod should be evaluated to determine if CCIT should be performed on the syringe after the plunger rod has been inserted to confirm that integrity has been maintained.

Finger Flange Placement: The main risk this operation poses to CCI is fracture of the syringe. To ensure that no broken or cracked syringes enter the supply chain, a 100% visual inspection must be performed. Given that the glass syringe is the primary container, an evaluation should be performed to determine if CCIT is required.

Tray Placement and Carton Insertion: The tray may contain one syringe or multiple syringes. The tray is then placed inside a carton for distribution. The operation of placing the syringe in the tray should be evaluated to determine if CCIT is required on the packaged syringe.

風險評估驅動的 CCIT 決策原則

TR86 的核心訊息是：並非每個二次包裝步驟都需要 CCIT，但每個步驟都必須經過風險評估。評估框架：

  1. 該作業是否對主要密封界面施加力或應力？
  2. 如果施加了力，力的大小和方向是否足以擾動密封？
  3. 如果風險存在，是否有其他控制措施（如 100% 目檢）足以緩解，還是需要額外的 CCIT？
  4. 如果確定需要 CCIT，應在最差情況樣本上執行，以確認作業不影響容器完整性。

比喻說明：二次包裝的「最後一里路」風險

想像你精心包裝了一個易碎的玻璃藝術品（= 灌裝密封的 PFS）。主要包裝（填充物和外盒）已經做好，但最後的「二次包裝」——在上面貼標籤、裝進展示架、放入快遞箱——每個步驟都可能在不注意中讓它破裂或移位。

PFS 的情況更複雜：它的「破裂」可能不可見（微裂縫）、或密封界面的移位不影響外觀，但已經損害了 CCI。這就是為什麼每個二次包裝步驟都需要系統性的 CCI 風險評估。

自動化 vs. 人工二次包裝的 CCI 考量

二次包裝可以是全自動、全人工或混合模式，各有不同的 CCI 風險特徵：

  • 全自動產線：力量和速度可精確控制 → 一致性高，但設備調試和驗證要求嚴格；需進行設備性能確認（OQ/PQ）
  • 人工操作：力量變異性大 → 需要詳細的 SOP 和操作人員培訓；「最差情況」難以精確定義
  • 混合模式：需識別人工操作步驟作為 CCI 風險的關鍵控制點

Key Messages Summary — Section 2a

本節關鍵訊息總結 • For rapid review

Key Messages (English)

KM-1: Stopper as Primary Barrier

The plunger stopper-barrel interface is the primary and most critical CCI barrier in any prefilled syringe design. Its three-rib compression structure provides layered sealing assurance. Any process step that disturbs this interface — filling, stoppering, rod insertion, transport — must be evaluated for CCI impact.

KM-2: Multiple Interfaces = Multiple Failure Modes

Unlike a simple glass vial (one primary closure), a PFS has up to three active seal interfaces (stopper-barrel, needle-shield, barrel-shield for staked needle designs). Each interface is a potential failure point. CCI strategy must address all interfaces throughout the product lifecycle.

KM-3: Silicone Oil — Dual Role and Blocking Risk

Silicone oil is essential for PFS functionality but creates two CCI-related risks: (1) excess oil allows the stopper to move freely during transport, breaching CCI; (2) silicone oil can physically block small defects, causing false negatives in probabilistic methods like dye ingress testing. Deterministic methods (vacuum decay, HVLD) are preferred for PFS CCIT.

KM-4: Stopper Movement in Vacuum-Based CCIT

Applying a pressure differential to a PFS during vacuum decay testing causes the stopper to move outward. This (a) reduces the sensitivity of the test by reducing the pressure differential, and (b) risks introducing foreign matter or microbial contamination. A contrast pin in the test chamber is a validated engineering solution to counteract stopper movement.

KM-5: Risk-Based Approach to Secondary Packaging CCIT

Not every secondary packaging operation requires CCIT, but every operation must be assessed for CCI risk. When a risk is identified, a worst-case CCI study must be performed. Semi-finished syringe CCI data does not substitute for finished syringe CCI assessment.

關鍵訊息（中文）

KM-1：活塞塞是主要屏障

活塞塞－筒身界面是任何預充填注射器設計中最主要、最關鍵的 CCI 屏障。三肋壓縮結構提供分層密封保障。任何可能擾動此界面的工藝步驟——灌裝、塞封、活塞桿安裝、運輸——都必須評估對 CCI 的影響。

KM-2：多個界面 = 多種失效模式

與簡單的玻璃瓶（一個主要密封）不同，PFS 有多達三個活躍的密封界面（活塞-筒身、針頭-護蓋、筒身-護蓋）。每個界面都是潛在失效點。CCI 策略必須在整個產品生命週期中覆蓋所有界面。

KM-3：矽油的雙重角色與堵塞風險

矽油對 PFS 功能至關重要，但帶來兩個 CCI 風險：(1) 過量矽油使活塞在運輸中自由移動，破壞 CCI；(2) 矽油可能物理性堵塞微小缺陷，導致染液滲入法等概率性方法出現假陰性。確定性方法（真空衰減、HVLD）是 PFS 的首選 CCIT 方法。

KM-4：真空式 CCIT 中的活塞移動問題

真空衰減測試對 PFS 施加壓差時，活塞會向外移動，這會 (a) 降低測試靈敏度，(b) 可能引入異物或微生物污染。測試腔中的對位針是一種經驗證的工程解決方案，可抵消活塞移動。

KM-5：二次包裝 CCIT 的風險評估方法

並非每個二次包裝作業都需要 CCIT，但每個作業都必須評估 CCI 風險。一旦識別到風險，必須對最差情況樣本進行 CCI 研究。半成品注射器的 CCI 數據不能替代成品注射器的 CCI 評估。
