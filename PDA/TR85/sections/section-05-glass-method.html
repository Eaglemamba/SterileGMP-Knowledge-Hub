5.0 Glass Container Inspection Method

原文內容 Original Text

Today, no alignment exists within the pharmaceutical industry on a method to inspect empty glass containers. This is of special importance for RTU containers since they will not undergo any further cleaning procedure(s). Inspection of empty glass containers may be performed by the glass manufacturer/converter or by the pharmaceutical company prior to use. It may be performed on all units or a statistically valid sample collected from a larger batch.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

RTU Container（即用型容器）：指已經過清洗、滅菌處理，可直接用於充填的容器。由於不會再經過額外的清洗步驟，容器上任何殘留的可見微粒都可能直接進入最終產品，因此進料前的檢查至關重要。

統計有效樣本（Statistically Valid Sample）：從大批次中抽取具有統計代表性的樣本數量，確保檢查結果能合理推論整批品質。這與 AQL（Acceptable Quality Limit）抽樣概念相關。

比喻說明 Analogy

想像你在高級餐廳用餐前，服務生端上的餐具如果是「免洗型」（用完即丟），你會期望它開封即乾淨，不需要再擦拭。RTU 容器就像這樣的免洗餐具 -- 既然下游不再清洗，上游的品質把關就必須更嚴格。而目前業界對「怎麼檢查這些空容器」還沒有統一標準，這正是 TR85 要解決的問題。

重點提示 Key Notes

本節開宗明義指出產業現狀的關鍵缺口：沒有統一的空玻璃容器檢查方法。這意味著不同藥廠、不同玻璃供應商的檢查標準可能差異很大，導致品質基準不一致。TR85 的核心價值就是提出一套經過跨產業驗證的標準化方法。

檢查可以由玻璃製造商/轉換商或藥廠執行 -- 這是一個供應鏈責任分工的議題，需要在品質協議中明確定義。

5.1 Inspection of Ready-to-Use Empty Glass Containers

原文內容 Original Text

The proposed visual inspection method which follows was based largely on the method and inspection conditions defined in the EP (26), USP (6), and JP (28) for filled containers. Table 5.1-1 summarizes the key requirements established to improve the performance of the method employed in the study.

The procedure is a manual inspection method where the quality decision to either accept or reject the container is made by a trained person. MVI is a probabilistic process where detection rates less than 100% are to be expected (3,29,30). To understand the probabilistic nature of this type of inspection a comprehensive study was performed using the defined method to inspect defect kits of empty glass containers seeded with different types of particles of varying size and location on and in the container.

This was necessary to better understand the capability of the method for empty containers and to establish criteria for inspector qualification. Section 5.3 outlines the results of this cross-industry study.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Manual Visual Inspection, MVI（人工目視檢查）：由受過訓練的人員以肉眼判斷容器是否合格的檢查方法。這是一個「機率性」過程 -- 偵測率不可能達到 100%，因為人眼有其生理極限。

藥典方法基礎：本方法以三大藥典（EP、USP、JP）的已充填容器檢查條件為基礎，調整應用於空容器。這確保了方法的法規認可性。

Seeded Defect Kit（植入缺陷測試套組）：在已知乾淨的容器中人為放入特定大小和類型的粒子，用於評估檢查方法的偵測能力和檢查員的資格確認。

比喻說明 Analogy

MVI 就像機場安檢的 X 光檢查員 -- 即使是訓練有素的專業人員，面對大量行李時也不可能 100% 抓到每一件違禁品。疲勞、注意力分散、物品隱藏位置等因素都會影響偵測率。這就是為什麼要設定最長連續檢查時間（1小時）、要求定期休息、控制環境光線等條件 -- 所有措施都是為了將人為變異降到最低。

重點提示 Key Notes

Table 5.1-1 是本節最重要的參考表格，定義了五大關鍵屬性：

  • 檢查員：視力 20/20 或 6/6 以上，每兩年重新測試
  • 訓練與資格：使用含缺陷樣品的測試套組進行資格確認
  • 環境：安靜、乾淨、降低環境光線
  • 設備：2,000-3,750 lux 光源 + 黑白背景
  • 方法：無放大、每面約 5 秒、距眼 30 cm、距光源 10-20 cm、旋轉檢視

這些數字是實務操作時的硬性要求，務必牢記。

實務應用 Practical Application

在 CDMO 環境中，你可能同時服務多個客戶、使用多種容器規格。建立標準化的檢查 SOP 時，Table 5.1-1 的參數是最佳起點。特別注意：

  • 檢查員每兩年重新測試視力和資格，這需要納入人員訓練管理系統
  • 每小時不超過的連續檢查限制，需反映在排班計畫中
  • 光源強度需要定期校驗並記錄 -- 這是稽核常見查核點

Attribute Requirement
Inspectors
  • Pass a medical eye examination before beginning visual inspection activities. Near vision acuity should be equivalent or better than 20/20 ft (or 6/6 meters) or equivalent. Inspectors should be retested and meet this criterion at least every two years.
  • Use of optical correction such as prescription glasses is optional if needed to achieve normal vision.
  • Avoid direct viewing of the light source.
  • Take regular breaks to avoid fatigue and eye strain. Do not exceed one hour of continuous inspection.
Training and Qualification
  • Train inspectors on the specific method and materials used.
  • Qualify inspectors with test sets with representative defect examples and good containers.
  • Define and document qualification requirements.
  • Evaluate inspector performance at least every two years.
Inspection Environment
  • Perform inspection in a clean and quiet area.
  • Reduce ambient lighting.
  • The use of gloves and gowning are recommended.
Equipment
  • An inspection workstation which provides a light intensity between 2,000 and 3,750 lux.
  • Black and white backgrounds.
Method
  • Perform inspection without magnification.
  • Inspect containers for approximately 5 sec against each background.
  • Hold containers approximately 30 cm from the eye.
  • Hold containers 10-20 cm from the light source.
  • Rotate container to examine all visible areas.
5.1.1 Operator Training for the Enhanced Container Inspection Method

原文內容 Original Text

To achieve reliable and consistent results, inspectors must be trained on the inspection method before performing routine inspection and their inspection performance verified by testing both initially and then every two years (minimum) thereafter. This requalification interval is less frequent than that for inspectors assessing filled units but is considered a practical interval since empty containers are reassessed when filled both by 100% and a subsequent acceptance sampling (AQL) inspection. The qualification testing should be performed using seeded test kits containing both good (blank) and bad (defective) samples. Clear acceptance requirements should be defined against which the inspectors are evaluated. Limits on the number of missed defects and good containers incorrectly rejected (false rejects) are necessary when setting the acceptance criteria.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Requalification Interval（再確認間隔）：空容器檢查員的再確認頻率為至少每兩年一次，比已充填容器的檢查員頻率低。原因是空容器後續還會在充填後再經過 100% 檢查和 AQL 抽樣，形成多重防線。

False Reject（偽拒絕）：將合格品誤判為不合格品。這看似「保守」，但過高的偽拒絕率會造成不必要的產品損耗和調查成本。資格確認時需同時設定漏檢率和偽拒絕率的上限。

比喻說明 Analogy

資格確認就像駕照考試 -- 你不只要通過初次測驗，還需要定期換照（重新確認）。而且考試不只看你能不能「找到問題」（漏檢率），還要看你會不會「誤判好品」（偽拒絕率）。一個過於嚴格、把好品都退掉的檢查員，和一個漏掉真正缺陷的檢查員一樣需要再訓練。

重點提示 Key Notes

文中特別解釋了為什麼空容器檢查員的再確認間隔可以比已充填容器寬鬆：因為有後續的雙重保護機制（100% 全檢 + AQL 抽樣）。這是一個基於風險的實務考量 -- 不是降低標準，而是因為整體流程中有足夠的補充檢查點。

  2. 為什麼設定漏檢率上限和偽拒絕率上限同等重要？試從品質和成本兩個角度分析。
5.1.2 Equipment and Setup

原文內容 Original Text

The results of manual inspection are highly influenced by the light intensity in the inspection zone. Light intensity between 2,000-3,750 lux at 10 cm from the light source is recommended based on current finished product inspection conditions found in the USP, EP, and JP. The light intensity should be confirmed prior to use and the intensity recorded at the point of inspection.

Container samples should be examined against both black and white backgrounds to maximize the contrast between the glass and the defect of interest. A matte, nonglossy background is recommended to avoid interference from glare or reflection.

Figure 5.1.2-1 shows a typical visual inspection workstation with diffuse top lighting and black and white backgrounds. A light table may also be used to provide diffuse light upwards to and through the object under inspection to supplement the diffuse illumination from above. Eight of the eleven companies engaged in the study used the light table in addition to the standard inspection workstation.

Figure 5.1.2-2 shows a container being inspected in the workstation using the recommended method and conditions. Figure 5.1.2-3 provides additional detail on the location of the container under inspection relative to the light source and viewing position of the inspector.

Trays should always be clearly labeled and preferably color coded. Discard all samples good and bad after the inspection and any follow-up investigation completed.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Light Intensity（光源強度）：2,000-3,750 lux 是依據 USP、EP、JP 三大藥典的成品檢查條件設定的。光源強度直接影響檢查員能否看到微小粒子 -- 太暗看不清，太亮則會造成眼睛疲勞和反光干擾。

Black and White Background（黑白背景）：黑色背景有利於偵測淺色/透明粒子（如玻璃碎片），白色背景有利於偵測深色粒子（如黑色橡膠碎片）。兩者配合使用才能最大化偵測覆蓋率。

Diffuse Lighting（漫射光源）：使用漫射光而非直射光，能減少容器表面的反光和眩光，讓粒子更容易被觀察到。Light table 從下方提供的透射光對於檢查容器內部的粒子尤其有效。

比喻說明 Analogy

想像你在家裡找掉在地上的透明玻璃碎片。如果房間燈光很暗，幾乎看不到；如果光源太強且直射，地板反光反而看不清。最好的方法是用柔和的漫射光，配合不同角度觀察。而黑白背景的作用就像用不同顏色的布來襯托 -- 淺色碎片放在黑布上一目了然，深色碎片放在白布上立刻現形。

重點提示 Key Notes

  • 光源強度需在使用前確認並記錄 -- 這是 GMP 文件要求，也是稽核必查項目
  • 背景材質需為霧面（matte, nonglossy），避免反光干擾
  • 研究中 11 家公司有 8 家使用了 light table 作為補充光源，顯示透射光在實務上的重要性
  • 工作區域的整理也是關鍵：合格品與不合格品的分流托盤必須清楚標示、最好用顏色區分，防止混淆
  • 所有檢查過的樣品（無論合格與否）在檢查和後續調查完成後都應丟棄，避免污染或重複使用

5.1.3 Magnification Requirements To Inspect Glass Containers

原文內容 Original Text

Magnification, other than that for optical correction to normal vision, is prohibited for routine release inspection. This is recommended to keep alignment with the pharmacopeial inspection conditions. USP General Chapter <790> (6)] states that inspection should be performed without magnification. However, USP Information Chapter <1790> (1) adds that while "not recommended for use during routine inspections, magnification can be helpful for critical examination of a small number of units, as may be needed during an investigation". If magnification is used for investigational purposes, strict guidance should be provided to avoid it becoming an uncontrolled part of the routine inspection process.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

USP <790>（一般章節）：規定例行檢查必須在無放大條件下進行。這是具有法規強制力的章節。

USP <1790>（資訊章節）：補充說明放大鏡可用於調查時的少量樣品「關鍵檢查」，但不適用於例行放行檢查。資訊章節屬於指導性質，不具強制力。

關鍵區分：「例行放行檢查」vs.「調查性檢查」的使用場景不同，放大工具的使用權限也不同。

比喻說明 Analogy

這就像體檢中的視力檢查標準 -- 正常體檢時用標準視力表（裸眼或矯正後），不允許用望遠鏡。但如果醫生發現異常需要進一步調查，可以用特殊儀器做精密檢查。放大鏡在這裡就是那個「特殊儀器」-- 只在調查時使用，而且必須有明確的管控規範。

重點提示 Key Notes

這一小節的重點是防止範圍蔓延（scope creep）。如果允許在調查中使用放大鏡，但沒有嚴格的管控指引，很容易逐漸演變成例行檢查的一部分，這會：

  • 偏離藥典要求，造成法規合規風險
  • 改變偵測基準線，導致偽拒絕率上升
  • 讓不同批次的檢查標準不一致

建議在 SOP 中明確定義放大鏡的使用場景、審批流程和記錄要求。

5.1.4 Glass Container Inspection Procedure

原文內容 Original Text

Detailed instructions for empty glass container inspection can be found in 8.4 Appendix D. Information recorded during or after inspection may include details such as the type and location of any particles observed, and any other defects observed. On completion of the inspection, the data should be evaluated against the established acceptance criteria to determine the disposition of the batch. During inspection, the operator should avoid direct viewing of the light source and take regular eye breaks to avoid eye strain which may adversely influence the results of the inspection. Inspection time should be based on the parameters defined in the qualification.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Disposition（批次處置）：根據檢查結果和預設的允收標準，決定該批次是放行、拒絕還是需要進一步調查。這是檢查流程的最終輸出。

記錄內容：不只是「合格/不合格」的結論，還包括觀察到的粒子類型、位置和其他缺陷的詳細資訊，為後續趨勢分析和供應商管理提供數據基礎。

比喻說明 Analogy

檢查記錄就像醫生的病歷 -- 不只記錄「健康/生病」的結論，還詳細記錄症狀、位置和嚴重程度。這些資料累積下來，才能做出趨勢分析（某供應商的容器是否特定位置常出現缺陷？）和根本原因調查。

重點提示 Key Notes

本小節再次強調了兩個操作安全要點：

  • 避免直視光源：高強度光源（2,000-3,750 lux）長期直視會造成眼睛損傷
  • 定期休息：眼睛疲勞會直接降低偵測率，這不是「舒適性」問題而是「品質」問題

詳細的檢查程序見 Appendix D（8.4 節），本節僅為概述。

5.2 Method Qualification Strategy and Detection Capability

原文內容 Original Text

To set realistic targets for human inspection performance, the detection capability of the method being used for inspection must be understood. A significant portion of the work reported here centered on performing inspections on seeded containers assembled into test kits to better assess the detection capability of the method described.

For all observations reported in this study, the light intensity was confirmed as ≥3,000 lux at the point of verification which is at the upper end of the inspection conditions defined in the EP (26), USP (6), and JP (28), of 2,000-3,750 lux.

Three equivalent test kits were created representing a wide range of visible particles types and sizes commonly found in glass containers. Specific examples were based on survey results (31) and the experience of glass team members. Eight different particulate types (glass, metal, cellulose, hair, plastic, and rubber [black, grey, and orange]), were defined and used when preparing the kits. The kits were prepared without any visible adhesive which might bias the measured detection rate. Each container was checked for particles prior to seeding, each unit was seeded with a single particle and then sealed to prevent further contamination. For these studies, each kit contained 60 each of vials, syringes, and cartridges, making a total of 180 pieces. The kits were compared and checked against each other to ensure that they were of similar composition. The locations of seeded particles are shown in Figure 5.2-1 and the type and nominal size of particles are detailed in Table 5.2-1. Only seeded containers were used in these kits to assess differences in ability to detect particles when compared with others of varying type and size.

[Figure 5.2-1] Locations Where Particles Were Seeded Into Or Onto Containers (Vials, Cartridges, And Syringes) In Prepared Test Kits

The sample kits were inspected by five pharmaceutical companies and six tubular glass convertors. Each of the organizations taking part in the study had the kits examined by five inspectors, six times each for a total of thirty inspections per company. A total of 59,400 independent observations were made. However, some data points were excluded from the study because they did not meet the stated inspection requirements (e.g., the inspection time was out of the specified range). Ultimately, 49,360 valid data points were used to analyze the performance of the inspectors and the inspection method.

Particle size measurement. Because of the unique optical properties and geometric configuration of both containers and particles, different methods were used to determine the nominal size of seeded particles. Table 5.2-2 shows the methods used for the various materials incorporated into the test kits. These were chosen to best represent how these particles appear to the human eye during inspection.

Length is simply a measure of the distance along the longest axis. Feret Max is the maximum distance between parallel tangents at opposing particle borders and Rect A, the longer side of a rectangle constructed around the particle which is in contact with the two points used to define Feret Max as shown in Figure 5.2-2.

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Detection Capability（偵測能力）：指在特定檢查條件下，方法能夠偵測到特定大小和類型粒子的能力。這是建立務實的檢查員績效標準的前提 -- 你不能要求人超越方法本身的極限。

Rect A：包圍粒子的矩形較長邊的長度，適用於橡膠等較規則形狀的粒子。

比喻說明 Analogy

建立測試套組就像準備一場標準化考試 -- 你需要設計一套涵蓋不同難度（粒子大小）和不同題型（粒子材質）的試卷，然後讓不同考場（公司）的考生（檢查員）都用相同條件作答。只有這樣，你才能客觀比較不同人、不同公司的表現，並建立有統計意義的基準線。

原文內容 Original Text

A total of eleven companies participated in the study: five from the pharmaceutical industry and six manufactures of the primary packaging materials. Each company used five inspectors for the study, each inspector performed the inspection six times, producing a total of 59,400 data points. Based on the inspection results, non-fibrous particles of 125 μm in size had an average PoD of approximately 70%. The PoD increases to 95% for a particle of 300 μm. 導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Probability of Detection, PoD（偵測機率）：在給定檢查條件下，特定大小和類型粒子被成功偵測到的機率。這是評估檢查方法性能的核心指標。PoD 隨粒子增大而升高，但不同材質/形狀的粒子在相同尺寸下的 PoD 不同。

Aspect Ratio（長寬比）：纖維的長度與寬度之比。高長寬比的纖維（如毛髮、塑膠條）雖然長度大，但由於寬度很小，在人眼中呈現的「面積」較小，因此比同長度的粒子更難偵測。

比喻說明 Analogy

想像在白色桌布上找東西 -- 一顆 300 μm 的黑色圓珠（粒子）比一根 300 μm 長的細線（纖維）容易看到得多，因為圓珠佔的「視覺面積」更大。這就是為什麼同樣 300 μm 的粒子有 95% PoD，但同樣 300 μm 的纖維只有 70% PoD -- 差異在於長寬比。

圖表解讀 Figure Analysis

  • 兩條曲線之間的差距反映了長寬比的影響

  • 在供應商品質協議中引用這些 PoD 數據，可以設定務實的允收標準

5.3.1 Normalizing the Particle Size

原文內容 Original Text

To better understand the difference in detectability between fibers and particles the impact of the aspect ratio on detectability (which is the relationship between the width and length of the fiber) must be considered. To address the impact of the aspect ratio the data was normalized by multiplying the Key Dimension of each particle by its SF. This conversion was applied to both particles and fibers to create the approximate area that would be seen by the inspector's unaided eye.

Normalized Particle Size = Key Dimension x Shape Factor (SF)

Using this transformation, the PoD for fibers and particles is similar to the PoD seen in Figure 5.3.1-1. The combined PoD then becomes 80% for 100 μm particulate and 95% for particulates of 200 μm. The diameter of the circles at specific x-y locations is proportional to the number of data points which occurred at that location.

In summary, a core element of the study was the creation of sample kits containing realistic defect examples where the PoD was not compromised or biased by the sample preparation (e.g., if the glue holding the particle is visible). By preparing these kits with defects that appear as those found during routine inspection the performance of the inspector using this method could be verified in a controlled way.

The study produced an extensive database with 49,360 validated data points, thereby providing a high degree of statistical confidence in the conclusions drawn on inspection performance. This study found that using the compendial inspection conditions the PoD for visible particles ranged from 70% for 125 μm particles to 95% for particles >300 μm. Due to their aspect ratios, fibers are more difficult to detect and their corresponding PoD are 80% for 300 μm fibers to 90% for fibers >800 μm.

These results align well with detectability studies performed on filled containers following the compendial methods (6,26,28). These earlier studies demonstrated that under controlled conditions, the smallest size standard sphere that could be reliably detected (i.e., average PoD >70%) was 150 μm.

In conclusion, from this study with empty containers and the previous work on filled containers, this method can be used successfully for the inspection of unfilled RTU containers by both the pharmaceutical manufacturing companies and the glass component suppliers. Inspectors using this method should be trained and qualified using test kits with well characterized and representative samples that accurately simulate routine defects and defect free samples. Additional review and discussion of the data analysis on inspection performance can be found in Section 8.3 Appendix C.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Shape Factor, SF（形狀因子）：用來將不同形狀粒子的「關鍵尺寸」轉換為「視覺等效面積」的係數。圓形粒子的 SF 接近 1，而細長纖維的 SF 遠小於 1（因為寬度遠小於長度）。

Normalized Particle Size（歸一化粒子尺寸）：= Key Dimension x Shape Factor。這個轉換使得粒子和纖維能在同一基準線上比較偵測率，消除了形狀差異的影響。

Apparent Area（視覺表觀面積）：檢查員肉眼看到的粒子「有效面積」。這才是決定能否被看到的真正因素，而非單一維度的長度或直徑。

公式與計算 Formula

歸一化粒子尺寸公式：

Normalized Particle Size = Key Dimension x Shape Factor (SF)

舉例：
- 圓形粒子（SF ≈ 1.0）：300 μm x 1.0 = 300 μm（歸一化後不變）
- 細長纖維（SF ≈ 0.1）：300 μm x 0.1 = 30 μm（歸一化後大幅縮小）

歸一化後的 PoD 基準：
- 100 μm（歸一化）→ 80% PoD
- 200 μm（歸一化）→ 95% PoD

對比未歸一化的數據：
- 粒子：125 μm → 70% PoD；300 μm → 95% PoD
- 纖維：300 μm → 70% PoD；800-1,000 μm → 90% PoD

比喻說明 Analogy

歸一化就像貨幣匯率換算 -- 一根 300 μm 的纖維和一顆 300 μm 的粒子雖然「標價」相同，但在人眼中的「購買力」（可見度）完全不同。通過形狀因子的換算，我們把它們都換成同一種「貨幣」（視覺表觀面積），這樣才能公平比較。換算後發現，纖維和粒子在相同的「視覺面積」下，偵測率其實差不多 -- 問題只是出在纖維的「匯率」低（面積小）。

重點提示 Key Notes

本節是整個 Section 5 的總結性結論，核心要點：

  • 方法有效性：經 49,360 個數據點驗證，此方法適用於空 RTU 容器的檢查
  • 適用對象：藥廠和玻璃元件供應商均可使用
  • 與已充填容器一致：結果與過往的已充填容器偵測率研究吻合，最小可靠偵測球徑為 150 μm（PoD >70%）
  • 歸一化的價值：消除形狀差異後，粒子和纖維的 PoD 匯聚到同一條曲線上，這驗證了「視覺面積才是決定偵測率的關鍵因素」
