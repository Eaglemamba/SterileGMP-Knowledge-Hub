4.0 System Functionality and Design Choice
There are a variety of aspects to filling system functionality that influence the selection of the liquid
filling technology and design. As always, the foremost requirement is the ability for both the engineering
(Parts 1, 2 and 4) and the required interventions (Part 3) to deliver a sterile product. Table 4.0-1
highlights several of the functionality considerations for liquid filling systems. Additional details for each
topic are provided in the corresponding Sections 4.1-4.10.
