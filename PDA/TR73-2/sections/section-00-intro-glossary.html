1.0 Introduction

原文內容 Original Text

The European Union regulation (EU) 2017/745 on medical devices, referred to as MDR, introduces new requirements for manufacturers of medical devices. These new requirements, according to MDR Articles 1(8) and 1(9), subparagraphs 2, also apply to pharmaceutical companies developing and manufacturing combinations of medicinal products and medical devices, including prefilled syringes (PFS), both integral and single integral products. The device part of these PFS must comply with the rules outlined in MDR Annex I: General Safety and Performance Requirements (GSPRs). Evidence of compliance, as described in Article 117, must be provided by requesting an opinion from a Notified Body (NB) appropriately accredited for the issuance of such an opinion (1) (See Section 3.0).

PDA Technical Report No. 73-2: Application of Medical Device Regulation Annex I Requirements for Staked Needle Syringes represents the current state of knowledge as to its application. As this is a new process based on a new regulatory requirement, expectations of the NBs and competent authorities may diverge as the process evolves. Ultimately, the responsibility for the entire content of the submission file that is reviewed by the NB lies with the applicant. Accordingly, the intended users of TR-73-2 are applicants, i.e., pharmaceutical companies, their suppliers, and other interested parties.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

MDR (Medical Device Regulation, 醫療器材法規)：這是歐盟於 2017 年發布、2021 年 5 月 26 日正式施行的醫療器材新法規，取代了舊有的 Directive 93/42/EEC。MDR 對預充填注射器 (PFS) 的「器材部分 (device part)」提出了更嚴格的安全與性能要求。

GSPRs (General Safety and Performance Requirements, 一般安全與性能要求)：MDR Annex I 所列出的全面性要求清單，涵蓋設計、材料、生物相容性、化學穩定性等多個面向。PFS 的器材部分必須逐項滿足相關 GSPRs。

Notified Body (NB, 認證機構)：經歐盟認可的第三方評估機構，負責審查 PFS 器材部分是否符合 MDR 要求，並出具正式意見 (NB Opinion)。

比喻說明 Analogy

可以把 MDR 的 GSPRs 想像成一張「建築安全檢查清單」。就像蓋房子需要通過結構、消防、水電等各項安全檢查一樣，預充填注射器的器材部分也需要逐一通過 GSPRs 的各項安全與性能要求。而 Notified Body 就像是「建築檢查員」，負責審核你的設計文件和測試報告，確認一切合規後才能核發許可。

重點提示 Key Notes

TR-73-2 代表的是「當前的知識狀態 (current state of knowledge)」。由於 MDR 仍是相對新的法規框架，不同的 NB 和各國主管機關 (Competent Authorities) 的期望可能存在差異。這意味著：

  • 申請者不能完全依賴單一指引，需要保持對法規演變的關注
  • 提交文件的最終責任在申請者 (applicant) 本身
  • 藥廠、供應商和其他相關方都是本報告的目標讀者

1.1 Purpose

原文內容 Original Text

As an addendum to PDA Technical Report No. 73: Prefilled Syringe User Requirements for Biotechnology Applications, this technical report provides recommendations on preparing the required documentation of the device part of the PFS to facilitate obtaining an NB opinion.

Table 5.1-1 in Section 5.0 provides GSPR guidance on the syringe components, syringe subassembly ready for filling, and the device part (see Figure 4.2-1). It systematically recommends the amount and depth of information needed for each relevant GSPR to support pharmaceutical companies in assembling the submission file to the NBs.

Section 4.0 provides information on documents that provide guidance on completing the NB submission file. Following MDR Annex II: Technical Documentation to complete the submission file for the NBs is recommended; individual sections can be written in more or less detail, at the discretion of the submitting applicant (2). Additional guidance can be found in the Team-NB position papers on documentation requirements and on best practices for the submission of technical documentation (2,3). TR-73-2 also follows the same structure as the submission file.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

TR-73 與 TR-73-2 的關係：TR-73 是原始的預充填注射器使用者需求技術報告（針對生物技術應用），而 TR-73-2 是其增補版 (addendum)，專門聚焦於如何準備 MDR 所要求的器材部分文件，以順利取得 NB Opinion。

MDR Annex II (Technical Documentation, 技術文件)：這是 MDR 規範的技術文件結構框架。TR-73-2 建議遵循此框架來組織提交文件，但各章節的詳細程度可由申請者自行決定。

比喻說明 Analogy

如果把 TR-73 比作一本「如何設計好注射器」的教科書，那麼 TR-73-2 就是一本「如何準備考試申請資料」的指南。兩者缺一不可 -- 你不僅需要設計出好產品，還需要知道如何把合規證據有條理地呈現給審查員 (NB)。

重點提示 Key Notes

TR-73-2 的核心價值在 Table 5.1-1：它逐項列出每個 GSPR 所需的資訊深度與廣度。這就像一張「通關清單」，幫助藥廠確認每項要求是否已充分準備支持證據。Team-NB 的立場文件也提供了額外的實務指引，建議一併參考。

實務應用 Practical Application

對於 CDMO 或藥廠而言，準備 NB 提交文件是一項跨部門的系統工程。建議在專案初期就依照 MDR Annex II 的架構建立文件管理系統，並指派專人負責追蹤每項 GSPR 的證據蒐集進度。提早規劃可避免後期「補考」式的匆忙補件。

1.2 Scope

原文內容 Original Text

While the scope of PDA TR-73 applies only to 1 ml long glass PFS for biotechnology application, TR-73-2 applies to PFS of glass and polymer of different volumes within the definition of MDR Article 1(9), subparagraph 2. The scope of TR-73-2 is not exclusive for biotechnology applications. The device part, including its corresponding components and materials, are in scope as they will be evaluated by the NBs, but the medicinal product (drug) is not.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Staked Needle Syringe (膠針注射器)：針頭直接固定於針筒上的注射器，針頭無法拆卸。這是本報告的核心對象。相對地，Luer lock（鎖定式接頭）和 Luer cone（圓錐式接頭）型注射器不在範圍內。

Device Part vs. Drug (器材部分 vs. 藥品)：NB 只評估器材部分（針筒、針頭、針頭護套、活塞桿等），藥品本身不在 NB 的審查範圍內，藥品由藥品主管機關（如 EMA）負責。

Fixed Dose (固定劑量)：本報告僅涵蓋固定劑量的 PFS，不包括需要量測功能的可變劑量設計。

重點提示 Key Notes

TR-73-2 的範圍比 TR-73 更廣：

  • 材料：不限於玻璃，也涵蓋高分子 (polymer) 針筒
  • 容量：不限於 1 mL，適用於不同容量
  • 應用：不僅限於生物技術產品，涵蓋所有注射型藥品

但請注意：包裝 (Packaging) 雖不在範圍內，若包裝相關證據（如運送研究、使用者標示）會影響器材部分，仍應納入 NB 提交文件中。

比喻說明 Analogy

可以把 Table 1.2-1 想像成一個「考試範圍公告」。就像學生需要知道哪些章節會考、哪些不考一樣，藥廠需要清楚知道哪些元件會被 NB 評估、哪些不會。不在範圍內的項目（如 autoinjector、針頭安全裝置）可能有其他法規要求，但不屬於本報告的「考試範圍」。

表格解讀 Table Analysis

Table 1.2-1 的關鍵要點：

  • Autoinjector (自動注射器)：不在範圍內，因為它屬於更複雜的器材系統，有專門的法規要求
  • ATMP (先進治療藥品)：不在 MDR 適用範圍內，因此也不在本報告範圍內
  • Intravitreal (玻璃體內注射)：排除在外，因為此類 PFS 通常不採用膠針設計
  • Non-EU regulatory frameworks：本報告僅聚焦歐盟法規，其他地區（如 FDA、PMDA）的要求不在討論範圍內

2.0 Glossary and Abbreviations
2.1 Glossary

原文內容 Original Text

Definitions have been provided to help clarify the concepts discussed in this technical report. While some of the definitions vary among companies, the definitions described below are consistent for use within this technical report. Where a definition is based on another published source, the source is cited. Only definitions that are either not mentioned in TR-73 or not taken from the TR-73 glossary are mentioned here.

As there is no agreed-upon EU-wide term for a product combining a medicinal product and a medical device part, such as "prefillable syringes ready for filling," this document uses the term "drug-device combination (DDC)" to refer to the final PFS system.

Applicant
The commercial entity responsible for the marketing authorisation application or post-approval application in the EU (5).
Competent Authorities (National)
A medicines regulatory authority in a European Union Member State (6).
Container Closure Integrity
Container closure integrity (CCI) is the ability of a container closure system to maintain the sterility and product quality of sterile final pharmaceutical, biological, and vaccine products throughout their shelf life.
Device Part
A device that fulfills the definition of a medical device, as defined in the relevant EU legal framework, and is intended to be placed on the EU market or made available on the EU market. This also applies to part of a device that fulfills the definition of Article 2(1). In the context of this document, the device part comprises the syringe subassembly ready for filling plus the plunger rod, needle shield, and finger flange extension.
Direct Contact
Medical device or medical device component that comes into physical contact with body tissue (7).
Drug-Device Combination (DDC) Product
Medicinal products where the medical device and/or device part and the medicinal product form an integral product that is not reusable (hereafter called integral) and where the action of the medicinal product is principal (1). There are two types of DDCs according to MDR Articles 1(8) and 1(9):
  • Any device which, when placed on the market or put into service, incorporates, as an integral part, a substance which, if used separately, would be considered to be a medicinal product... [and] if the action of that substance is principal and not ancillary to that of the device, the integral product will be regulated as a medicinal product (Article 1(8)).
  • If the [medical] device intended to administer a medicinal product and the medicinal product is placed on the market in such a way that they form a single integral product which is intended exclusively for use in the given combination and which is not reusable, that single integral product shall be governed by Directive 2001/83/EC or Regulation (EC) No 726/2004, as applicable (Article 1(9), subparagraph 2) (8).
Indirect Contact
Medical device or medical device component through which a fluid or gas passes, prior to the fluid or gas coming into physical contact with body tissue (in this case the medical device or medical device component itself does not physically contact body tissue) (7).
Market Authorization Holder
The company or other legal entity that has the authorization or aims to receive it, to market a medicine in one, several, or all European Union Member States (adapted from EMA Glossary of Regulatory Terms) (6).
Nanomaterial
A natural, incidental or manufactured material containing particles in an unbound state or as an aggregate or as an agglomerate and where, for 50 % or more of the particles in the number size distribution, one or more external dimensions is in the size range 1-100 nm; Fullerenes, graphene flakes and single-wall carbon nanotubes with one or more external dimensions below 1 nm shall also be deemed to be nanomaterials (Article 2(18)) (1).
Needle Safety Device
A safety feature or mechanism that effectively reduces the risk of an exposure incident (e.g., accidental needlestick) (9); sharps injuries protection system.
Needle Shield, Rigid Needle Shield
Elastomeric syringe closure, which seals the front end of staked needle syringe, designed to protect the needle tip/bevel from damage and allows sterilization of the syringe. The elastomeric syringe closure covered by a rigid housing is called rigid needle shield (10).
Notified Body
Conformity assessment body designated in accordance with Regulation (EU) 2017/745 (Article 2(42)) (1).
Processing Aids
A substance (or mixture of substances) that is used to facilitate manufacturability, processing, transport, or storage of a material or component. It may assist in, for example, reducing energy/time/resources required to aid the process, increasing production, and cleaning efficiency and machinability. Processing aids do not contribute to the intended attributes of the finished component and do not belong to the intentionally added ingredients of the formulation (e.g., silicone oil, mold release, and anti-tack agents). Processing aids may be removed during the manufacturing process or, depending on the process clearance capability, may remain as low-level residuals in the finished component.
Processing Residues
Residues of processing aids in or on the final drug-device combination.
Quality Certificate
Certificate of Analysis, Certificate of Compliance, or equivalent document providing evidence of compliance with product specifications.
Risk Evaluation
Process of comparing the estimated risk against given risk criteria to determine the acceptability of the risk (11).
Risk Management
Systematic application of management policies, procedures and practices to the tasks of analyzing, evaluating, controlling and monitoring risks (11).
Single-Dose Container
A container that holds a quantity of the preparation intended for total or partial use on one occasion only (Ph. Eur. 3.2) (12).
Staked Needle Syringe
Syringe with a needle integrated into the barrel; the fixation can be done by insert molding, gluing or other bonding methods (13).
State of the Art (SOTA), Generally Acknowledged (GA)
Developed stage of current technical capability and/or accepted clinical practice in regard to products, processes and patient management, based on the relevant consolidated findings of science, technology and experience (14).
Surgically Invasive
Surgically invasive device means (a) an invasive device which penetrates inside the body through the surface of the body, including through mucous membranes of body orifices with the aid or in the context of a surgical operation; and (b) a device which produces penetration other than through a body orifice (Annex VIII, Chapt. 1, 2.2) (1).
Syringe Barrel
Cylindrical glass or polymer body with front end and finger flange (10, 13).
Syringe Components
Can be a glass or polymer syringe barrel with or without lubrication, a needle with lubrication and a needle shield (soft or rigid), plunger (also plunger stopper) with lubrication, coating and/or lamination, plunger rod and, if needed, a backstop.
Syringe Subassembly Ready for Filling (also Prefillable Subassembled Syringe)
Subassembly consisting of a glass or polymer syringe barrel, a needle and a needle shield (soft or rigid) that has been washed and sterilized; considered as starting material for the filling and assembling process of a prefilled syringe (10).

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Drug-Device Combination, DDC (藥品-器材組合產品)：這是本報告最核心的概念之一。DDC 指的是藥品與醫療器材組合成不可重複使用的一體化產品，且藥品作用為主要 (principal) 作用。PFS 就是一個典型的 DDC：針筒是器材部分，藥液是藥品部分，兩者組合成一體化產品。

Article 1(8) vs. Article 1(9) 的區別：

  • Article 1(8)：器材「內含」藥物性質的物質，若該物質的作用是主要的，則整個產品按藥品法規管理
  • Article 1(9), subparagraph 2：器材與藥品以單一整合產品上市、不可重複使用，則由 Directive 2001/83/EC 管理（即藥品法規為主導）

PFS 主要適用 Article 1(9), subparagraph 2 的情境。

關鍵術語對照 Key Term Reference

Direct Contact (直接接觸)：元件直接接觸人體組織。例如：針頭刺入皮膚、針筒內壁接觸藥液後注入體內。

Indirect Contact (間接接觸)：元件不直接接觸組織，但液體或氣體通過它後才接觸人體。例如：針筒內壁（藥液通過後注入體內）。

Container Closure Integrity, CCI (容器密封完整性)：容器密封系統在整個保存期限內維持無菌性和產品品質的能力。這是 PFS 的關鍵品質屬性。

Processing Aids (加工助劑)：如矽油 (silicone oil)、脫模劑等，不屬於配方的一部分，但可能以低量殘留存在於成品中。這些殘留 (Processing Residues) 需要被評估和管控。

比喻說明 Analogy

DDC 的概念可以用「即食便當」來理解。便當盒（器材）和食物（藥品）組合成一體化產品，消費者吃完就丟棄（不可重複使用）。食物（藥品）的功能是主要的，但便當盒（器材）必須符合食品安全容器的標準。在法規上，這個「即食便當」主要由食品法規管理，但便當盒部分仍需符合容器安全要求 -- 這就像 PFS 主要由藥品法規管理，但器材部分需符合 MDR GSPRs。

重點提示 Key Notes

詞彙表中有幾個容易混淆的概念需要特別注意：

  • Syringe Components (注射器元件)：包括所有零件（針筒、針頭、護套、活塞、活塞桿、止退器）
  • Syringe Subassembly Ready for Filling (待充填注射器組件)：已清洗和滅菌的針筒+針頭+護套組件，是充填製程的起始材料
  • Device Part (器材部分)：= Syringe Subassembly + 活塞桿 + 護套 + 指法蘭延伸件

這三個層級的區分對於理解後續 GSPR 的適用範圍至關重要。

State of the Art, SOTA (技術現狀) -- 特別說明

SOTA 在 MDR 中具有特殊的法律意義。它不僅指「最新技術」，更指「基於科學、技術和經驗的綜合成果所達到的當前技術水準和/或公認的臨床實踐」。在準備 GSPR 證據時，必須證明你的設計和製造符合 SOTA。這意味著需要引用相關標準 (harmonised standards)、指引文件和已發表的科學文獻。

重點提示 Key Notes

CMR 和 ED 是 MDR 中特別關注的材料安全議題。MDR 要求製造商評估器材中是否含有 CMR 物質（致癌、致突變、生殖毒性物質）或 ED 物質（內分泌干擾物質），並在技術文件中提供相關證據。這將在後續 GSPR 評估中詳細討論。

TSE/BSE 涉及動物源性材料的安全性評估。如果 PFS 元件（如彈性體護套）使用了動物源性原料，必須提供 TSE/BSE 風險評估。

本節重點回顧 Section Summary

  • 法規背景：MDR (EU) 2017/745 取代了舊有的醫療器材指令，對 PFS 的器材部分提出新的 GSPRs 合規要求，需取得 NB Opinion
  • 報告定位：TR-73-2 是 TR-73 的增補版，專注於指引藥廠如何準備 NB 提交文件，Table 5.1-1 是核心參考工具
  • 範圍界定：適用於玻璃和高分子材質、不同容量的膠針型 PFS（固定劑量），不包括 autoinjector、針頭安全裝置、ATMP 和非歐盟法規
  • DDC 分類：PFS 屬於 MDR Article 1(9) subparagraph 2 定義的單一整合產品，主要由藥品法規管理，但器材部分需符合 MDR Annex I
  • 關鍵層級：Syringe Components（零件）→ Syringe Subassembly（待充填組件）→ Device Part（器材部分）→ DDC（最終組合產品），每個層級有不同的 GSPR 適用範圍
  • 材料安全：CMR、ED 物質和 TSE/BSE 評估是 MDR 特別關注的材料安全議題，將在後續 GSPR 章節中深入探討
