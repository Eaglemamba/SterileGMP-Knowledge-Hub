4.1 風險管理概述 — Risk Management Overview

4.0 Risk Assessments

An effective risk management process can reduce glass handling hazards during initial process development and design and also when evaluating process changes. Further, risk management can be used to discover the root cause of glass breakage events and make informed decisions during investigations. A risk management program can support regulatory discussions and inquiries, ultimately providing regulators with higher confidence in the pharmaceutical manufacturer's capabilities to address these types of events.

The scope of the risk management program, specific to glass handling, should include the full lifecycle of the glass container including the manufacture and shipment from the supplier to filling, packaging, and distribution to the customer. There are several helpful documents available to support risk assessment activities, such as ISO documents, ICH guidelines, and PDA technical reports.

Risk assessment comprises identification of potential risks, analysis of the probability of occurrence, evaluation of potential impact of the risk, and prioritization for action. Risk assessments can be quantitative or qualitative in nature. A quantitative assessment, in which a numerical value or range of values describing the level of risk, may be used. Or, a qualitative assessment, using relative categories such as "high," "medium," and "low," may be used to characterize the risks. In either case, the definition and rationale for each category should be clearly defined.

A risk assessment is performed by a cross-functional team that includes the appropriate SMEs and/or responsible persons to assure accurate analysis. Multiple data sources are used to support the assessment including, among other material, process maps, procedures, manufacturing floor experience, frequency data, deviation and complaint history, and detection capability. These inputs ensure the accuracy of the risk analysis, so they must be robust.

解說與應用 Commentary

核心概念：為什麼需要風險管理？

TR87 將風險管理定位為三大功能的工具：

  • 預防性：在製程設計初期識別玻璃破損的潛在風險
  • 調查性：協助找出玻璃破裂事件的根本原因（root cause）
  • 法規溝通性：提升主管機關對廠商能力的信心

這三個功能在 CDMO 的日常運作中高度相關——客戶審核、FDA 查廠、以及 CAPA 執行都需要這套思維框架。

全生命週期範圍（Full Lifecycle Scope）

風險管理的範圍不只是你的填充生產線，而是從供應商製造玻璃瓶 → 運輸 → 你的廠房接收 → 填充包裝 → 出廠配送，每個環節都在 scope 內。

這對 CDMO 特別重要：供應商交貨品質的波動直接影響你的製程風險，必須納入評估。

比喻：定量 vs. 定性評估

定量（Quantitative）：像體溫計，給出 38.5°C 的具體數字——適用於有可靠數據、能精確量化的場景。

定性（Qualitative）：像醫生說「你發燒了，屬於中度」——用「高/中/低」分類，適用於數據不足或情境複雜的場景。

TR87 允許兩種方式並用，但強調無論選哪種，各類別的定義與依據必須清楚記錄。

跨職能團隊（Cross-functional Team）

風險評估不是 QA 部門自己關門做的文件！TR87 明確要求納入相關 SME（主題專家）。在 CDMO 環境中，這通常包含：製程工程師、QA、設備維護、生產線領班，甚至客戶代表。

數據來源越多元、越紮實，分析結果的可信度就越高——這是法規審查時最容易被挑戰的地方。

4.2 風險控制與溝通 — Risk Controls and Communication

Risk Controls and Communication

Subsequent to risk assessment, risk controls are considered and applied where appropriate to eliminate or reduce risks identified by the risk assessment. The amount of risk control (i.e., risk reduction or elimination) should be proportional to the significance of the risk and should be assessed based on a benefit-to-risk analysis.

The resulting risks, mitigating activities, and level of risk acceptance must be effectively communicated. Risk communication and review requires appropriate documentation as well as communication of the risk decisions to the persons authorized to accept the risk and direct response strategies for risk mitigation and follow-up (e.g., functional experts and area or operations heads). Risk response activities need to be reviewed periodically to confirm ongoing compliance effectiveness and proactively reevaluated when new process, equipment, or industry changes influence or alter the underlying risk profile.

Many tools are available that can be used to employ effective risk management. Table 4.0-1 provides a list of tools typically used with a brief description and potential applications for each.

解說與應用 Commentary

比例原則：風險控制量 ∝ 風險重要性

TR87 明確說明：風險控制的力道要與風險的嚴重程度相稱。這避免了兩個極端——

  • 對低風險過度管控（浪費資源、增加操作負擔）
  • 對高風險輕描淡寫（法規合規缺口、產品安全疑慮）

決策依據是 效益對風險分析（benefit-to-risk analysis），這與 ICH Q9 的精神完全一致。

風險溝通容易被忽略的細節

很多廠商做完 FMEA 就「存檔了事」，但 TR87 強調：

  • 風險決策必須傳達給有權接受風險的授權人員（不只是 QA 記錄）
  • 風險應對措施需定期審查，確認持續有效
  • 當製程、設備或行業有新變化時，需主動重新評估風險輪廓

在 CDMO 中，客戶的配方變更、設備保養後的參數偏移，都可能觸發這個重評機制。

實務應用：風險溝通清單

  • FMEA 結果是否已向製程主管和 QA 主管正式簡報？
  • 高風險項目是否有指定負責人和追蹤截止日期？
  • 製程變更時，是否有觸發現有 FMEA 的更新流程？
  • 定期審查（periodic review）的頻率是否已明文規定？

4.3 FMEA 方法論 — Failure Mode and Effects Analysis

FMEA — Overview and Method

One effective method used to assess the risk inherent in a process specific to glass handling is the Failure Mode and Effects Analysis (FMEA). This method is widely used by many industries, including the pharmaceutical industry. Although its original and intended use had been to proactively identify unacceptable risks prior to failures occurring, the FMEA has also been used successfully in a reactive fashion to evaluate processes for most likely root causes of failures after they occur.

The FMEA method is based on the theory that the risk of any particular failure is a composite of the severity, frequency, and probability of the detection of that failure. Cross-functional team participants, with a diverse knowledge of the process, compile an exhaustive list of potential failures and, taking into account the known attributes of the process, thoroughly analyze each one. This output is then evaluated to ascertain if the risk is acceptable or unacceptable. Mitigation activities are identified and completed for those risks deemed unacceptable.

解說與應用 Commentary

FMEA（失效模式與效應分析）的雙重用途

TR87 特別強調 FMEA 的兩種應用模式——這點常被忽略：

  • 前瞻性（Proactive）：製程設計時主動識別潛在失效，在問題發生前預防
  • 反應性（Reactive）：玻璃破損事件發生後，用 FMEA 框架系統性評估最可能的根本原因

這使 FMEA 成為無論在什麼階段都有價值的工具——它不只是一份「開廠前要做的文件」。

RPN（風險優先數）核心公式

RPN（風險優先數） = Severity × Occurrence × Detection

RPN = S × O × D

S（嚴重度 Severity）：
  失效對產品的影響有多嚴重？
  範圍通常 1–10，10 = 最嚴重

O（發生頻率 Occurrence）：
  這個失效原因發生的機率有多高？
  範圍通常 1–10，10 = 最常發生

D（偵測度 Detection）：
  現有控制措施能偵測/預防的機率？
  範圍通常 1–10，10 = 完全無法偵測

→ RPN 越高 = 風險越大 = 越優先處理

注意：D 的計分邏輯是反向的——偵測能力越差，分數越高（代表風險越大）。這是 FMEA 初學者最容易搞混的地方。

比喻：FMEA 就像飛行前安全檢查

航空業的 preflight checklist 就是 FMEA 的精神——飛機還沒起飛，機師就系統性地確認每個可能失效的環節：引擎、液壓、通訊。製藥的玻璃瓶填充線也一樣，不能等到產品出了問題才來找原因。

4.4 FMEA 執行時機與範圍 — When and What to Assess

FMEA — Timing, Scope, and Data Sources

An FMEA should be performed during the initial design and development of the product. Thus, critical elements of the container design can be identified to ensure product quality including, but not limited to, considerations for processing steps such as filling, blast freezing, lyophilization, and final storage conditions. The glass breakage FMEA may be performed in conjunction with a FMEA for other types of potential failures. This can confirm the original FMEA and provide a benchmark risk assessment for production. An FMEA should be performed during evaluations of changes to the process as a specific step of process change control. The existing FMEA should be modified and used to ascertain if risk will change due to the planned process change. Any increase in risk due to the planned change should be mitigated prior to implementation. A periodic review should be completed to confirm ongoing effectiveness of controlling risk levels and identify opportunities for further mitigation through development of new technology or advanced procedures.

The specific glass-handling hazards to address as part of an FMEA should include glass particles or fragments that could enter a container from a glass breakage event, and/or events or risks resulting in breakage or a cracked container. The scope of the FMEA should cover as much of the lifecycle of the glass container as possible, including supplier manufacturing and shipment as well as receipt, filling and packaging, and final distribution.

During the risk analysis, potential failure modes specific to the glass-handling hazards are identified and the potential causes and current controls are captured. The risk associated with specific failure scenarios is estimated using a risk priority number (RPN). An RPN is determined for each scenario based on the severity of the failure effect, the frequency of occurrence of the potential cause, and the detection and mitigation (prevention) capability that exists based on the controls currently in place. The definition and rationale for rankings used to complete each element of the RPN should be clearly outlined and agreed upon by the FMEA team.

Multiple sources of information are employed to determine the RPN. Process maps and procedures that outline the relevant process steps, and the equipment associated with them, are used to ensure that all relevant areas are captured in the evaluation. In addition, and importantly, observations on the manufacturing line are conducted as part of a complete process review of the manufacturing floor or location of the process and equipment. Pictures and videos gathered, either during the process review or from previous efforts, are useful for evaluating the severity, frequency, and detection of each failure mode. Specific glass-breakage frequencies, locations, and root causes, as well as investigation history, are used to further inform the FMEA. Visual inspection capabilities throughout the process should be outlined to provide information relative to detection.

Finally, the definition and rationale for rankings used to assess each criterion of the RPN should be clearly outlined and documented.

解說與應用 Commentary

FMEA 三大執行時機

TR87 明確列出 FMEA 應在三個關鍵時點執行：

  • 1. 初始設計開發階段：在問題發生前，系統性識別風險點。特別涉及：填充、急速冷凍（blast freezing）、凍乾（lyophilization）、最終儲存條件等步驟。
  • 2. 製程變更評估時：變更控制（change control）的一部分——任何計畫中的變更都應先評估是否增加 RPN，若是，需在實施前完成緩解措施。
  • 3. 定期審查：確認風險控制的持續有效性，並識別新技術或程序帶來的進一步緩解機會。

玻璃特定危害範圍

FMEA 的玻璃危害範圍應涵蓋兩大類型：

  • 玻璃碎片/顆粒進入容器（contamination）：直接影響產品安全性
  • 導致破裂或裂縫的事件（breakage/crack）：影響容器完整性（container integrity）

生命週期涵蓋：供應商製造與運輸 → 廠房接收 → 填充包裝 → 最終配送。

RPN 數據來源：多元輸入確保準確性

TR87 強調 RPN 評分不能憑空想像，必須有實證基礎：

  • 製程流程圖與 SOP（確保所有步驟都被納入）
  • 現場生產線實際觀察（最重要！文件看不到的細節在現場才看得到）
  • 照片與影片記錄（評估嚴重度、頻率、偵測能力）
  • 玻璃破損頻率、位置、根本原因的歷史數據
  • 偏差（deviation）與客訴（complaint）歷史
  • 目視檢查能力分析（visual inspection capability）

實務應用：CDMO 環境中的 FMEA 執行要點

  • 在現場觀察前，先預備好流程圖——讓觀察更有系統方向
  • 用手機或工業攝影機記錄填充線瓶子移動的關鍵段落（尤其是瓶子與設備接觸的轉換點）
  • 歷史偏差記錄中涉及玻璃的，全部納入 FMEA 的 occurrence 評分依據
  • 客戶提出製程變更要求時，立即觸發既有 FMEA 的差異評估（gap assessment）

三個維度的評分邏輯

  • Severity（嚴重度）：關注的是「影響後果」——玻璃碎片進入無菌產品是最高嚴重度（10），完全不會造成破損則為 1
  • Occurrence（發生頻率）：基於歷史數據與製程知識估計——每日發生 = 10，極罕見 = 1
  • Detection（偵測度）：評估現有控制措施的偵測能力——分數越高代表越難偵測（風險越高）。這是反向邏輯，請特別注意。

重要：Detection 的反向邏輯

Detection 評分與一般直覺相反：

  • 偵測能力 100%（確定能抓到）→ D = 1（低風險）
  • 偵測能力 <50%（幾乎抓不到）→ D = 10（高風險）

這反映了一個核心思想：即使一個失效很嚴重且頻繁，只要你的系統能 100% 偵測並阻止它流出到客戶，RPN 就不會太高。目視檢查（visual inspection）的能力確認因此成為關鍵輸入。

比喻：RPN 就像汽車保險費率

保險公司計算費率時，考慮三個因素：事故的嚴重程度（車毀人傷 vs. 擦傷）、你發生事故的頻率（過去記錄）、以及你有沒有 ABS/安全氣囊等防護（偵測/控制能力）。

保護措施越好，保費越低——RPN 越低的邏輯相同：好的偵測與控制降低整體風險。

4.5 RPN 後續行動與文件化 — Post-Assessment Actions

Risk Acceptance, Mitigation, and Documentation

After all risks are identified and assigned an RPN, each risk is evaluated to determine the appropriate level of mitigation or acceptance of risk and prioritized as to which potential failure modes should be addressed first. The level of mitigation should be proportionate to the level of risk. The rationale for the level of mitigation and/or acceptance of risk should be clearly documented and agreed upon by all FMEA participants and stakeholders.

The FMEA results, participants, ranking rationale, and supporting data should be appropriately documented to support future investigations, process change or periodic review, and regulatory discussions. In addition, the results of the FMEA should be communicated to the appropriate stakeholders to ensure mitigation actions are taken and tracked to closure.

解說與應用 Commentary

RPN 之後：優先排序與緩解措施

完成 RPN 評分只是 FMEA 的中途站，後續步驟同等重要：

  • 優先排序：RPN 最高的失效模式優先處理（有限資源下的決策工具）
  • 比例原則：緩解力道要與風險水平相稱，過度緩解低風險是資源浪費
  • 風險接受：若決定接受某個風險（不採取進一步措施），理由必須明確記錄且獲所有 FMEA 參與者同意

文件化：FMEA 的法規價值

TR87 強調 FMEA 文件的重要性，因為它在三個場景中直接被使用：

  • 未來偏差調查：可快速回溯當初是否預見此風險、如何評分
  • 製程變更審查：用既有 FMEA 作為基準，評估變更的影響
  • 法規討論（FDA 查廠、客戶審核）：展示你有系統性的風險管理能力

文件必須包含：FMEA 結果、參與人員名單、評分依據、支持性數據。

實務應用：FMEA 結案清單

  • 每個高 RPN 項目是否都有指定負責人（owner）和目標完成日期？
  • 緩解措施完成後，是否重新評估 RPN（post-mitigation RPN）？
  • FMEA 文件是否存入品質系統（QMS），並連結到相關 SOP 版本號？
  • 是否有機制在下次定期審查時拉出此 FMEA 重新確認？
  • FMEA 結果是否已正式傳達給製程主管、QA 主管及客戶（如適用）？

比喻：FMEA 文件是你的「賽前策略書」

就像球隊的賽前分析報告——你花了時間研究對手的弱點，制定了應對策略。如果你沒有記錄下來，下次遇到類似比賽，前人的智慧就消失了。在製藥業，這份「策略書」在法規查廠時就是你最有力的證明文件。

第四章重點摘要 Section Summary

  • 風險管理三大功能：預防（製程設計時識別風險）、調查（根本原因分析）、法規溝通（提升稽核信心）。範圍涵蓋玻璃容器全生命週期——從供應商到客戶端。
  • 評估方法二分法：定量評估（數值）vs. 定性評估（高/中/低分類）；兩者均可用，但各類別定義與依據必須清楚記錄。
  • 跨職能團隊必要性：風險評估不能單打獨鬥，需 SME 與相關責任人共同參與，數據來源必須多元且紮實。
  • 六大風險工具：基礎工具、Risk Ranking、統計工具、FTA、HAZOP、FMEA——各有適用場景，FMEA 為玻璃瓶處理風險評估的首選工具。
  • RPN 核心公式：RPN = Severity（嚴重度）× Occurrence（發生頻率）× Detection（偵測度）。Detection 為反向邏輯——偵測能力越強，D 分數越低，整體 RPN 越低。
  • FMEA 三大執行時機：① 初始設計開發 ② 製程變更評估 ③ 定期審查。玻璃瓶特定危害範圍涵蓋碎片污染與容器破裂兩大類。
  • 後 RPN 行動：依 RPN 高低優先排序緩解措施，力道與風險成比例；決策理由須文件化，結果須傳達至授權人員並追蹤至結案。
