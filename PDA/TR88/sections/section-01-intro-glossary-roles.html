1.0 Introduction 1.0 導論

1.0 Introduction

Microbial data deviations are microbial test results that fall outside the product specification or acceptance criterion established in a drug application, drug master file, official compendia, good manufacturing practice regulations, or internally by the manufacturer. This includes, but is not limited to, results generated during environmental and water monitoring as well as pharmaceutical ingredient, in-process, and finished product testing. The validity of the test results is supported by a documented laboratory and manufacturing investigation, where applicable.

The information presented in this technical report was compiled by a team of experts representing both industry microbiologists and regulators and reflects current industry best practices. The membership of personnel from the U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA) on the task force does not imply Agency endorsement. Feedback from industry and regulatory professionals was evaluated and incorporated into the technical report to ensure that the suggested methods, terminology, and practices for conducting microbial data deviation investigations reflect sound science and current technological approaches. This technical report is not intended to establish any mandatory or implied standards. Published references supporting the recommendations presented are cited in the text and listed in Section 7.0 References.

Definitions for the terms used throughout this report are provided in the Glossary. When available, the same or similar technical definitions have been used as those included in the FDA or International Conference on Harmonization (ICH) guidelines. To avoid ambiguity, the task force members believe that use and definitions of these terms should also be consistent and standardized when incorporated into company-specific standard operating procedures, validation protocols, and regulatory submissions.

導讀解析

核心概念：什麼是微生物數據偏差？

凡微生物測試結果超出以下任一基準，即構成偏差：

  • 藥品申請文件（Drug Application）或藥品主文件（DMF）中的規格
  • 官方藥典（USP、EP 等）
  • GMP 法規要求
  • 製造商內部制定的標準

涵蓋範圍：環境監控（EM）、水系統、原料成分、中間體及成品測試——所有微生物數據都在管轄之內。

比喻：偏差就像汽車儀表板的警示燈

警示燈亮起不代表車子壞了，但你必須調查原因——是真的引擎故障（真實製程問題），還是感應器誤報（實驗室誤差）？TR88 給的就是這份「診斷手冊」，告訴你每種警示燈的診斷流程。

監管重點：FDA 參與不等於背書

FDA 人員參與 TR88 撰寫任務小組，但本報告不構成強制標準，也不代表 FDA 正式背書。這是業界 best practice 指引，非法規命令。

CDMO 實務意義：可援引 TR88 作為內部 SOP 的科學依據，但客戶合約中若有更嚴格要求，以合約為準。

思考練習

你的廠區上週發生一筆環境監控（EM）數值超過 Action Limit 的結果。這是「microbial data deviation」嗎？測試結果的效力（validity）如何確認？

提示：看偏差定義中「環境及水監控」是否明確被納入範圍，以及「documented laboratory and manufacturing investigation」如何支撐結果效力。

1.1 Purpose 1.1 目的

1.1 Purpose

The FDA issued a Guidance for Industry: Investigating Out of Specification (OOS) Test Results for Pharmaceutical Production in 2006 in response to the U.S. versus Barr Laboratories decision, which is applicable to laboratory testing results for products regulated by the Center for Drug Evaluation and Research (CDER) (1, 2). The FDA guidance does not extend to microbiological failure investigations, however. Consequently, PDA developed this technical report to provide approaches, specific to the pharmaceutical industry, for investigating various types of microbial data deviations based on current scientific knowledge and modern testing technologies.

The term "microbial data deviation" is preferred to "out-of-specification" or "out-of-limit result" in this technical report, as most microbiological tests, especially in a sterile product manufacturing facility, are in-process tests, not finished-product tests that must meet a regulatory-approved specification. For simplicity and consistency, the term "deviation" will be used throughout this document.

Technical Report No. 88 presents a holistic approach for performing a microbiological investigation. It provides a framework to assist with focusing on the investigational areas that may contain or contribute to the root cause of data deviations, such as sampling, test methodology, and suitability of the test, as opposed to providing methods for analysis of batch acceptability. (Batch acceptability determinations and release decisions are made by the quality control unit as required by FDA good manufacturing practice (GMP) regulations, not by deviation investigation teams.)

導讀解析

為什麼不叫 OOS，要叫「Deviation」？

FDA 2006 年的 OOS 指引源自 U.S. v. Barr Laboratories 判決，只適用於 CDER 監管的成品測試，明確不適用於微生物學失敗調查。

微生物學的特殊性：大多數微生物測試（尤其無菌充填環境中）是過程中測試（in-process tests），而非有法規規格的成品測試。用「OOS」這個詞會造成監管上的混淆，因此 TR88 統一用「deviation（偏差）」。

比喻：OOS 是出口海關，偏差是生產線品管站

OOS 像海關——只在最終出口（成品）把關，有明確的法規規格線。微生物偏差更像生產線上的品管站——每個環節（EM、水系統、中間體）都可能需要介入，但沒有統一的「合格放行線」。TR88 是為這些中間站制定的診斷框架。

重要邊界：調查 ≠ 批次放行決策

TR88 明確劃定：偏差調查團隊負責找根本原因（root cause），但批次放行（batch disposition）決策屬於 QCU 的職責，依 FDA GMP 法規執行。兩者不可混淆。

CDMO 紅線：即便調查結論是「實驗室誤差，結果無效」，批次是否放行仍需 QCU 正式核准——不能由調查團隊自行決定。

思考練習

客戶要求在偏差調查報告完成前先放行批次。你會如何回應？引用 TR88 中哪個原則？合約品質協議（Quality Agreement）應如何預先規範這個情境？

1.2 Scope 1.2 範圍

1.2 Scope

Each of the three sections of this document focuses on distinct areas where microbial data is collected and inspected for deviation investigations and with the following designated responsibilities:

Roles and Responsibilities—Focuses on personnel, especially microbiologists, in the laboratory investigations and cross-functional teams responsible for manufacturing investigations.

Conducting Laboratory Investigations (Phase I)—Provides additional details on how an investigation may be conducted to determine that the microbial test was valid and what is the root cause of a failure when the result is deemed invalid. Subsections focus on different microbiological tests conducted, with points for consideration in the final determination of the investigation.

Conducting Manufacturing Investigations (Phase II)—Illustrates where a microbiologist, acting as a subject matter expert (SME), can contribute to the evaluation of personnel activities, environmental changes, utilities, materials, and process-related events and can assist in determining the probable cause of the deviation and the most appropriate corrective and preventive actions (CAPA).

If needed and when appropriate, Phase I and Phase II investigation activities can be conducted in parallel.

The roles of other contributors to deviation investigations required for effective root cause analysis, such as representatives from manufacturing, process engineering, and quality assurance, will also be briefly described. The composition of the investigation team will depend on the nature of the investigation.

This technical report promotes a lifecycle approach, but greater emphasis is placed on laboratory and manufacturing investigations of marketed drug products. The approaches described may be suitable during research and product development and the manufacture of clinical supplies, as well as the manufacture and testing of regulatory-approved products. The microbial tests discussed in this technical report include microbial attribute testing of nonsterile products, components, sterility testing, bacterial endotoxin assay, mycoplasma testing, environmental and personnel monitoring, and microbial monitoring of utilities including pharmaceutical-grade water and compressed gases.

Microbial assays of antibiotics and vitamins and the detection and enumeration of protozoans, viruses, and prions are not included in the scope of this report. Additionally, batch disposition is separate from the outcome of a microbial investigation report. This document is directed specifically to pharmaceutical manufacturing; however, the industry practices may be suitable for deviation investigations in the biological, consumer health, medical devices, and cosmetic industries. In addition, these can be extended to contract manufacturing and testing organizations supporting pharmaceutical companies.

While this document provides a framework illustrating how deviations and the resulting investigations might be conducted, each company is responsible for developing their internal policies and procedures suitable for its specific needs that also meets current GMPs.

導讀解析

核心架構：Phase I + Phase II 雙階段調查

Phase I — 實驗室調查

  • 核心問題：這次測試結果有效嗎？
  • 調查主體：微生物實驗室（分析員 + 管理層）
  • 目標：確認結果有效性；若無效，確定實驗室誤差的根因

Phase II — 製造調查

  • 核心問題：若測試有效，製造端出了什麼問題？
  • 調查主體：跨職能團隊（製造、工程、QA + 微生物 SME）
  • 目標：確定最可能根因（probable cause），提出 CAPA

關鍵：兩個 Phase 可以並行——不必等 Phase I 完全結束才啟動 Phase II。時間敏感性要求如此。

比喻：醫院的雙軌急診診斷

病患送急診，醫生同步進行：(1) 確認檢驗數據是否正確（Phase I）；(2) 調查可能病因（Phase II：感染源、用藥史、環境接觸）。兩條線並行才能快速找到真相。

CDMO 關鍵洞察：品質協議必須劃分 Phase I vs. Phase II 責任

TR88 明確指出框架可延伸至「contract manufacturing and testing organizations」。對無菌充填 CDMO：

  • Phase I（實驗室）：通常由 CDMO 的微生物實驗室主導
  • Phase II（製造）：CDMO 主導，但客戶可能要求參與或索取報告
  • 合約品質協議（Quality Agreement）必須清楚規定：誰負責哪個 Phase、時間線要求、報告格式與核准機制

未明確規定的後果：客訴糾紛、查廠發現、批次被客戶拒絕放行。

思考練習

你的廠發生一筆無菌測試（Sterility Test）陽性結果。請畫出 Phase I 和 Phase II 的調查啟動時序：哪些事項必須在 Phase I 結論前完成？哪些可以並行？誰有權核准並行啟動？

2.0 Glossary of Terms 2.0 術語彙編

2.0 Glossary of Terms

術語解析

三層警戒結構：Alert Level → Action Limit → Adverse Trend

理解這三個術語的層次關係是 EM 管理的基礎：

  • Alert Level（警示水準）：早期預警，超標不一定要矯正行動，但必須加強監控與追蹤。
  • Action Limit（行動限度）：超標即表示製程已偏離正常操作範圍，必須啟動書面調查並採取矯正行動。
  • Adverse Trend（不利趨勢）：不是單一超標事件，而是超標頻率上升、或連續低濃度微生物回收的模式——代表製程控制正在流失，需要系統性評估。

比喻：三色交通燈

Alert Level = 黃燈（放慢、觀察）；Action Limit = 紅燈（停車、採取行動）；Adverse Trend = 連續黃燈後的模式警示（這個路口設計有問題，需要根本改善）。

Invalid Result vs. Invalid Test：微妙但重要的區別

  • Invalid Result（無效結果）：測試做完了，但經過 Phase I 調查確認結果無效。
  • Invalid Test（無效測試）：測試本身未能符合方法要求；包括測試被中止的情況（如隔離器在無菌測試途中故障）。

這個區別影響調查記錄的方式和後續動作的觸發條件——兩者在 Phase I 報告中須清楚區分。

CAPA 的核心地位：不只是一張表格

TR88 將 CAPA 定義為「收集分析資訊、識別並調查品質問題、採取有效措施防止再發」的子系統（subsystem）——它是一套完整的問責機制，不是填個表就結束。

CAPA 的有效性（Effectiveness）是任何偏差調查的最終考驗。若 CAPA 執行後同樣偏差再次發生，監管機構會追問為什麼根因找錯了。

Correction vs. Corrective Action：治標 vs. 治本

  • Correction（矯正）：處理已發生的偏差（修補、重工）——治標。
  • Corrective Action（矯正措施）：消除偏差的原因，防止再發——治本。

稽查常見問題：企業只做了 Correction（例如重測）卻沒有真正的 Corrective Action（消除根因），導致偏差反覆發生。

術語識別練習

請分類以下情境屬於哪個術語：

  1. A 無菌室連續三個月 Alert Level 超標率從 2% 升至 8%。
  2. 無菌測試途中 RABS 故障，測試中止。
  3. 水系統微生物超過 Action Limit，啟動書面調查。
  4. 發現測試員用錯培養基，結果不可信。

答案：1. Adverse Trend；2. Invalid Test；3. Environmental Monitoring Excursion（+ 觸發 OOL 調查）；4. Invalid Result（涉及 Laboratory Error）

2.1 Abbreviations/Acronyms 2.1 縮寫 / 首字母縮寫

2.1 Abbreviations/Acronyms

縮寫速記

TR88 核心縮寫群組

這些縮寫在後續章節會密集出現，建立肌肉記憶非常重要：

  • EM（環境監控）：無菌充填廠最核心的日常微生物監控活動。
  • BET（細菌內毒素測試）：注射劑品質的核心測試，俗稱 LAL test。
  • OOS vs. OOL：OOS 適用於有法規規格的成品；OOL 適用於 EM 和公用設施的限度——這個區別在 TR88 中是刻意強調的，不能混用。
  • QCU vs. QA vs. QC：在 21 CFR 框架下，QCU 是兼含 QA 與 QC 職能的法定授權單位。稽查報告中只說「QA 部門核准」可能不夠精確。
  • SME（主題專家）：Phase II 中的技術領路人，可以是內部人員也可以是外部顧問。

比喻：縮寫是偵探的工具袋標籤

BET 陽性 → 內毒素問題 → 觸發調查；EM excursion → OOL 調查；CAPA 關閉 → 案件「結案」。搞清楚每個縮寫對應的調查路徑，你就掌握了整份 TR88 的使用邏輯。

縮寫應用練習

以下句子中哪個縮寫用錯了？

「因本次 EM 結果超過 Action Limit，依 SOP 啟動 OOS 調查，由 QA 核准調查計劃後，SME 進行 BET 方法評估。」

提示：EM 超過 Action Limit 應啟動 OOL（而非 OOS）調查；核准方應為 QCU（而非僅 QA）。

3.0 Microbiologist Roles and Responsibilities — 3.1 Laboratory Microbiologist Responsibilities 3.0 微生物學家角色與職責 — 3.1 實驗室微生物學家職責

3.1 Laboratory Microbiologist Responsibilities

The principal responsibility for achieving accurate laboratory testing results resides with the analyst performing the microbiological test. Recognition of potential practices that could create invalid results should be an integral part of the laboratory analyst's training. The analyst is responsible for performing all testing according to standard operating procedures (SOP) and in-house documentation practices. The analyst should only use qualified, calibrated, and well-maintained laboratory instruments and equipment and approved growth media, diluents, and reagents.

The analyst should check the product test results for compliance with their specifications before discarding test preparations, whenever practical. If any potential or suspected laboratory errors occurred during testing, the analyst must discontinue testing immediately and notify their laboratory management. The analyst, with guidance and instruction from the laboratory management, must collect and retain all test samples, reagents, consumables, paperwork, and any other relevant materials related to the affected assay, even if the test is deemed invalid, to aid the laboratory investigation.

If errors are clear and indisputable, such as atypical appearance of a test sample (e.g., unexpected particulate matter present), damaged test container, test sample spillage, use of wrong pipetting volumes, missing dilution steps, aseptic handling missteps, or use of the wrong testing medium, the analyst should immediately document the event. When in doubt, the analyst may consult with the laboratory management to confirm that the test should be discontinued.

The analyst should not knowingly continue a test that they expect to invalidate at a later time for an assignable cause (i.e., analyses should not be completed for the sole purpose of seeing that a passing result can be obtained when obvious errors are known). In addition, the analyst is responsible for assisting management with the laboratory investigation.

導讀解析

分析員的核心職責：數據品質的第一道防線

實驗室分析員（Laboratory Analyst）的職責包含：

  • 嚴格按 SOP 執行所有測試
  • 只使用合格、校正過的儀器與核准的培養基 / 稀釋液 / 試劑
  • 棄置測試製備前先確認結果是否符合規格
  • 察覺潛在誤差時立即停止測試並通報管理層
  • 保留所有相關樣品、試劑、耗材和紀錄以協助調查
  • 協助管理層進行實驗室調查

比喻：分析員是實驗室的「誠實目擊者」

外科醫生在手術中發現器械污染，第一個責任不是繼續手術期待不出事，而是立即暫停、通報、保留證據。實驗室分析員的角色完全相同。發現問題就停下來，是專業素養，不是失職。

監管紅線：不得為了得到合格結果而繼續有問題的測試

TR88 明確聲明："analyses should not be completed for the sole purpose of seeing that a passing result can be obtained when obvious errors are known."

這不只是品質要求，也是 FDA Data Integrity 的監管底線。違反此原則可能導致 Warning Letter、Form 483 觀察，甚至刑事指控。

CDMO 管理意義：分析員需要心理安全感（Psychological Safety）——在發現問題時能立即停止並通報，而不是擔心影響生產進度而硬撐。這是管理層和組織文化的責任。

情境判斷練習

分析員在無菌測試第 3 天觀察到某一培養基外觀顏色偏差，但尚未出現明顯渾濁。請問：

  1. 應立即停止測試，還是先觀察至第 7 天？
  2. 測試器材應保留還是棄置？
  3. 應記錄什麼？何時記錄？

提示：「when in doubt」原則和「即使測試被宣告無效，相關材料仍須保留」的規定如何共同指導行動？

3.2 Microbiologist Role in a Manufacturing Investigation 3.2 微生物學家在製造調查中之角色

3.2 Microbiologist Role in a Manufacturing Investigation

The key to successful manufacturing investigations of microbial testing failures with pharmaceutical ingredients and finished drug products is the inclusion of an experienced microbiologist as an SME on the manufacturing investigation team. This microbiologist may work in the quality control (QC) microbiology testing laboratory at the manufacturing site, the quality control unit (QCU), product development, or a corporate microbiology support group. Ideally, the microbiologist should have a comprehensive knowledge of product formulation and specific manufacturing operations, obtained from frequent shop-floor visits or surveillance of the overall process. For smaller organizations, without internal microbiological expertise, the microbiologist may be an outside consultant. To be truly effective, the SME must be a trained microbiologist with broad experience in microbiological investigations. Furthermore, it would be advantageous if the SME is familiar with the actual product implicated in the failure. Without this expertise on the investigation team, it is less likely that the root cause of the failure can be determined, and the investigation may be hampered or severely limited.

The microbiologist should be engaged in the investigation as soon as is practical, as microbiological investigations are always time-sensitive.

The microbiologist should be part of the first step in an investigation, such as performing a walk-through of the manufacturing area, to become oriented and identify situations and events that are nonroutine and may be directly linked to the deviation. To be successful, the microbiologist must have access to all related testing results, have the cooperation of manufacturing managers, supervisors, operators, and support personnel, and have the authority to interview staff, inspect processing equipment, take investigatory microbial samples, and review change control and batch records. In some instances, access to customer complaint records may be necessary. For failures related to purchased pharmaceutical ingredients, it is highly desirable that a microbiologist join any audit team visiting the ingredient supplier to conduct an investigational review.

Systematic, proactive risk assessments for assessing the overall aseptic manufacturing process and controls have been proven helpful in identifying potential weaknesses in advance, and may mitigate the occurrence of a deviation, for example, sterility failures and endotoxin or environmental monitoring (EM) excursions (12).

導讀解析

製造調查中的微生物學家：技術橋樑，缺不可

TR88 強調的理想 SME 微生物學家特質：

  • 受過正式微生物學訓練
  • 有廣泛的微生物調查實戰經驗
  • 熟悉產品配方和具體製造操作（最好是現場走訪所累積的知識）
  • 理想情況下熟悉該次失敗涉及的具體產品

TR88 直接說：若調查團隊中沒有這樣的人，「the investigation may be hampered or severely limited」。

比喻：微生物學家是犯罪現場的法醫

警察（製造/工程人員）可以描述現場狀況，但要判斷「這個污染源在 24 小時內留下的嗎」，你需要法醫（微生物 SME）。法醫必須在現場及早介入，因為微生物證據（菌株、環境條件）會隨時間消失——「time-sensitive」不是比喻，是微生物學的物理現實。

實務關鍵：微生物學家必須有正式調查授權

TR88 列出微生物 SME 在調查中必須具備的授權：

  • 取得所有相關測試結果
  • 訪談製造部門員工（操作員、主管、支援人員）
  • 檢查加工設備
  • 採集調查性微生物樣品（Investigatory Microbial Samples）
  • 審閱變更控制紀錄和批次紀錄
  • （必要時）查閱客訴記錄

若微生物學家在調查中被製造部門「擋門」，或沒有採集額外樣品的正式授權，調查品質將嚴重受損。這是管理層需要在制度層面解決的問題。

CDMO 應用：主動預防勝於被動調查

TR88 指出：系統性、主動式風險評估有助於提前識別製程弱點，可降低偏差（包括無菌測試失敗、內毒素 / EM 超標）發生率。

對 Operations Director 的啟示：不要只在偏差發生後才找微生物 SME。讓微生物學家定期參與製程風險評估（FMEA、PHA），才是真正的主動品質管理。

情境練習

週五下午 4 點，你的 CDMO 收到通知：客戶產品的無菌測試呈陽性。請問：

  1. 誰是第一個應被通知的人？
  2. 微生物 SME 應在何時介入？
  3. 若內部沒有足夠專業，你的應急計劃是什麼？（外部 SME 名單是否已存在？）
3.3 Microbiology Laboratory Management Responsibilities 3.3 微生物學實驗室管理職責

3.3 Microbiology Laboratory Management Responsibilities

Upon notification by a laboratory analyst of a suspected laboratory error, the laboratory management must conduct an objective assessment to determine if an error has occurred. Data should be reviewed promptly to ensure the contemporaneous memory of events and to determine if the results might be attributed to an isolated or systematic laboratory error. In the absence of laboratory error, a confirmed valid test could indicate problems in the manufacturing operations; however, all facts need to be evaluated before predetermining a cause. The correct compliance assumption is that the results are valid until proven otherwise.

Laboratory management should ensure that the probable cause is supported by evidence, using root cause analysis tools when appropriate (e.g., 5 Whys, Ishikawa/fishbone diagram). Laboratory management should promptly notify the QCU and any impacted departments of the suspected deviation and inform them that a laboratory investigation has been initiated and when it might be completed.

If testing is conducted by a contract testing laboratory, the quality agreement between the contract testing laboratory and the manufacturer should clearly assign responsibilities, include a timeline, and specify the requirements of the laboratory investigation.

導讀解析

實驗室管理層的行動框架

收到分析員通報後，管理層的標準反應流程：

  1. 客觀評估：是否確實發生誤差？避免「先入為主」
  2. 分類誤差：孤立性（Isolated）vs. 系統性（Systematic）實驗室誤差
  3. 前提假設：「結果有效，直到被證明為無效」——不能因為結果不好看就預設是測試問題
  4. 使用根因工具：5 Whys、魚骨圖（Ishikawa / Fishbone Diagram）
  5. 主動通報 QCU 和受影響部門：及時、附上預估調查完成時間

比喻：法官的「無罪推定」原則

「結果有效直到被證明無效」就是微生物學版本的「無罪推定」。如果默認所有不好看的結果都是「實驗室誤差」，整個品質體系就失去意義——這也是 FDA 在 Data Integrity 方面最在乎的核心原則之一。

「最可能原因」原則：偵查需要證據，不需要確定性

TR88 用的是 probable cause（最可能原因），不是 definitive root cause（確定根因）。現實中，微生物調查很難達到 100% 確定——菌株可能無法溯源，環境條件可能已改變。

監管要求的是：結論有證據支持、根因工具被適當使用、CAPA 針對 probable cause 設計。「找不到確定根因」不是可接受的結論，但「最可能原因是 X，依據是 Y 和 Z，CAPA 是 A」是可接受的。

CDMO 合約實驗室的特殊規定

若由合約測試實驗室執行測試，品質協議（Quality Agreement）必須：

  • 清楚分配調查職責（誰負責哪個部分）
  • 包含調查時間線（Timeline）
  • 規定實驗室調查的具體要求

對 CDMO 的雙重意涵：你既可能是「受委方」（對客戶負責），也可能是「委託方」（對分包實驗室有要求）。兩個方向的品質協議都必須覆蓋偏差調查責任分工。

管理層決策練習

分析員通報：無菌測試第 7 天觀察到疑似污染，但記錄顯示測試程序無明顯偏差。管理層的第一個動作應該是什麼？

  1. 立即宣告 Invalid Test，要求重測
  2. 通知 QCU，同步啟動 Phase I 客觀調查
  3. 先等 14 天觀察期結束再決定
  4. 請分析員回憶是否有操作失誤

提示：「valid until proven otherwise」和「time-sensitive」兩個原則如何共同指導行動？

3.4 Subject Matter Expert Responsibilities 3.4 主題專家職責

3.4 Subject Matter Expert Responsibilities

SMEs can support the investigation by providing technical assistance related to the assay methods and technologies utilized, designing any investigational testing, and providing general scientific guidance, as needed. This expert could have greater experience and scientific background related to the assay or manufacturing process from within the company. Hiring a qualified outside consultant may also be an option.

導讀解析

SME 的角色定位：技術顧問，非調查主導者

SME（Subject Matter Expert，主題專家）在 TR88 中的定位是支援性的：

  • 提供測試方法和技術的專業協助
  • 設計調查性測試（Investigational Testing）
  • 提供一般科學指引

注意：SME 的定義比「製造調查微生物學家（3.2 節）」更廣——可以是任何領域的專家（設備工程師、統計學家等），且可以來自公司外部。

比喻：SME 是調查的「外腦」或「會診醫師」

重大醫療案例會邀請院外專家會診（Second Opinion）。偏差調查邀請 SME 是為了引入超出日常運營知識的深度專業——在 CDMO 特別有用，因為不常見的測試方法或設備問題可能超出內部能力。

何時需要外部 SME？

  • 涉及少見生物體（如孢子形成菌的無菌測試失敗）
  • 需要特殊鑑定技術（如全基因組定序 WGS）
  • 重大客訴或監管調查，需要獨立第三方意見
  • 內部調查陷入瓶頸，根因遲遲無法確定

最佳實踐：建立外部 SME 名單，包含聯絡方式和資格資訊，在偏差發生前就準備好。

思考練習

你的廠發生未知菌種的 EM Action Limit 超標，內部實驗室無法鑑定菌株來源。你會如何識別並動員適合的 SME？需要哪些資訊才能決定誰是合適的人選？

3.5 Quality Control Unit Responsibilities 3.5 品質管制單位職責

3.5 Quality Control Unit Responsibilities

The QCU oversees all aspects of the microbial deviation investigation. A QCU representative or other approved personnel would be responsible for coordinating with the cross-functional team involved in supporting the investigation, including personnel from manufacturing, facilities, calibration, validation; approving any laboratory retesting plans; and approving the laboratory and manufacturing investigations. The QCU also would be responsible for overseeing quality agreements made with contract testing laboratories and for ensuring that microbial deviation investigations are adequately addressed.

導讀解析

QCU：調查的最終監管者與核准機構

QCU 在偏差調查中扮演監督（Oversee）與核准（Approve）角色，而非直接執行：

  • 協調跨職能調查團隊（製造、設施、校正、驗證等）
  • 核准實驗室重測計劃（Laboratory Retesting Plans）
  • 核准 Phase I（實驗室）和 Phase II（製造）調查報告及結論
  • 監督對合約測試實驗室的品質協議執行
  • 確保微生物偏差調查被充分且妥善處理

比喻：QCU 是法院的法官，不是偵探

偵探（實驗室管理層、微生物 SME、跨職能團隊）蒐集證據和提出結論。法官（QCU）審核程序是否合規、判決是否有依據，並正式核准最終結論。沒有法官的核准，任何結論都不具效力。

CDMO Operations Director 的制度建置清單

確保以下制度已到位，才能在偏差發生時有效運作：

  • 分析員層面：訓練計劃包含「何時停止測試」的判斷準則；建立心理安全文化，讓人敢於通報問題而不是隱瞞。
  • 實驗室管理層：書面程序規定接到通報後的標準反應時序（例如：1小時內完成初步評估）；根因分析工具使用培訓。
  • 微生物 SME：SOP 中明確列出 SME 的調查通行授權；建立外部 SME 名單（含緊急聯絡）。
  • QCU：核准流程有明確 SLA；對客戶的品質協議已覆蓋 Phase I vs. Phase II 的責任分工。

綜合練習：CDMO 合約場景角色分配

你的 CDMO 為客戶 A 生產注射劑，無菌測試由分包實驗室 B 執行。分包實驗室通知你本批次無菌測試陽性。請回答：

  • 誰立即啟動 Phase I 調查？（分包實驗室 B 的哪個角色負責？）
  • 你的 CDMO 的 QCU 需要在何時介入，做什麼？
  • 客戶 A 的角色是什麼？品質協議應如何預先規範？
  • 若 Phase I 結論是「測試有效，結果真實陽性」，Phase II 由誰主導？客戶 A 需要知道什麼、何時知道？

本節重點回顧

  1. 偏差定義：微生物數據偏差涵蓋 EM、水系統、原料、中間體、成品等所有微生物測試，超出任何已建立的基準（法規、藥典、內部標準）即構成偏差。測試結果的效力需有書面調查支撐。
  2. 術語精確性：用「deviation」而非「OOS」，因為大多數微生物測試是 in-process tests；Alert Level（警示）、Action Limit（行動限度）、Adverse Trend（不利趨勢）三層結構必須清楚區分；Invalid Result 與 Invalid Test 有不同的調查含義。
  3. 雙階段框架：Phase I（實驗室，確認測試有效性）和 Phase II（製造，確認製程根因）可並行；批次放行決策始終屬於 QCU，不是調查團隊的工作。
  4. 調查的黃金原則：結果有效直到被證明無效；找 probable cause 而非要求 definitive root cause；絕不為了合格結果繼續有問題的測試；微生物調查 time-sensitive，越早介入越好。
  5. CDMO 核心行動項目：品質協議必須明確 Phase I vs. Phase II 的 CDMO / 客戶責任分工；微生物 SME 必須有正式調查授權；分析員文化須支持「發現問題立即停止通報」；CAPA 有效性是最終考驗。
