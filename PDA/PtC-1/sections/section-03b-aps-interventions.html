I

Fill Volume — 充填體積

原文內容 Original Text

Problem Statement
What fill volume should be used for APS in order to assess potential contamination?

Recommendation
Fill volume should be sufficient to assess potential microbial contamination and to ensure complete contact with all sterile surfaces inside the container when inverted. The volume used should provide adequate headspace to support potential contaminant growth and enough volume should be present to visually determine growth. Where the headspace is found to be insufficient to support growth or the volume is found to be insufficient to detect contamination, adjustments to the headspace or the volume in the production scale container should be made.

Rationale
APS should simulate as closely as possible the actual production in order to capture any potential inherent risks or variables that could negatively impact the process (e.g., foaming, splash-up). The adequacy of headspace to support potential contaminant growth should be verified during growth-promotion studies. Where production-fill volumes are too small to be visually inspected for contaminant growth, fill volumes should be increased to the level where visual inspection is effective.

References
PDA Technical Report No. 22 (Revised 2011): Process Simulation for Aseptically Filled Products.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

充填體積（Fill Volume）的核心要求有兩個面向：

  • 微生物生長空間：頂部空間（headspace）需含足夠氧氣，使潛在污染菌得以生長繁殖，才能在培養後以肉眼或濁度計偵測到陽性結果。
  • 全面接觸無菌表面：翻轉容器時，培養液必須能接觸容器內所有可能的污染位點（如瓶蓋內壁、膠塞底面）。

這兩個要求同時代表了「最低充填體積（minimum fill volume）」的設計目的：不是模擬真實產品體積，而是確保偵測靈敏度。

公式與計算 Fill Volume Rule of Thumb

一般業界經驗法則（非 PtC 明文規定）：

最低充填體積建議值：
  ≥ 1/10 × 容器標稱容積（nominal container volume）

範例：
  10 mL 小瓶 → 最低充填約 1 mL
  50 mL 小瓶 → 最低充填約 5 mL

但需注意：
  若容器小至 1 mL 以下（例如預充式注射器），
  上述 1/10 規則可能使充填量過少而無法偵測，
  此時需將充填量提高至「可目視判讀濁度」的最小值，
  並以生長促進試驗（growth-promotion study）驗證。

判斷基準優先順序：
  1. 目視可偵測到濁度 ← 最重要
  2. 翻轉後培養液接觸全部表面
  3. 頂部空間足夠（headspace ≥ 容器 1/3 體積）

比喻說明 Analogy

充填體積的選擇，就像選擇培養皿裡的培養液量——太少，細菌長不出來看不見；太多，頂部空間不足，氧氣供應不夠，厭氧菌以外的微生物同樣長不起來。

APS 的培養液不是在「模擬產品」，而是在「創造一個最佳微生物偵測環境」。這是一個常見的概念混淆點，請務必釐清。

重點提示 Key Notes

小容器（small containers）的例外處理：對於充填量本身已足夠小（例如眼藥水用 1 mL 瓶），若生產充填量太小而無法目視偵測生長，PtC No. 1 明確允許增加 APS 充填量超過生產量，只要能說明此調整的必要性即可。

這代表 APS 充填量可以且應該不同於實際生產充填量，只要能支持科學上的偵測能力。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 一個 2 mL 的預充式注射器（PFS），生產充填量為 1 mL，頂部空間約 0.5 mL。請問此 APS 充填量是否足夠？需要驗證什麼？
  2. 若生長促進試驗（growth-promotion test）顯示某容器頂部空間不足以讓金黃色葡萄球菌生長，公司應如何應對？

Interventions — Type and Frequency — 干預操作的種類與頻率

原文內容 Original Text

Problem Statement
What types of interventions should be included in an APS and with what frequency?

Recommendation
The priority for an APS is designing the process to minimize the requirements for interventions and, that any interventions that are intended are designed to be as low a risk as possible. Any risks deemed requiring remediation activities to reduce their inherent risk should require those activities to be completed in order not to validate bad practices. The technique or method used to perform interventions in the aseptic fill should be captured or listed in a written procedure that may be enhanced by pictorial or videographic means. Aseptic-fill personnel should be trained in the performance of these interventions in such a way that the sterility of the product is not compromised. A list of approved qualified interventions should be maintained and should be reviewed on a regular basis to ensure the risk assessments remain current.

A risk-based assessment of interventions should be performed to plan for their inclusion in APS. This may include a grouping of interventions of a similar nature, provided that their complexity, risk, and execution are comparable. The rationale for any grouping should be documented.

The frequency of interventions to be included in the APS should be justified based on the risk assessment. The type of interventions and frequency in routine production may be identified from process design, production logs, records, and observation of the aseptic-filling process.

Interventions in routine manufacturing should be periodically compared to those interventions included in periodic process simulations to update the APS program.

A new corrective intervention (e.g., one not included in the firm's APS program) performed during production must be evaluated as a deviation. The intervention may be determined acceptable if it is similar to a previously simulated intervention and has been performed with proper aseptic technique. The evaluation of such an intervention may include an APS subsequent to the fill in which that intervention occurred. Evaluation of such a corrective intervention should be supported by a risk assessment. The assessment should conclude with either an acceptance or rejection of this intervention relative to the current and future manufacturing processes. If the new intervention is accepted, then it should be reviewed for inclusion into the list of identified interventions simulated during a scheduled APS.

Where the presence of operators increases the risk of contamination, worst-case conditions should be simulated by requiring each operator to participate in the process simulation for the maximum duration for which the operator may be present and operating in a cleanroom according to the procedure used in commercial manufacturing.

Rationale
Interventions should be designed and performed using proper aseptic techniques in order to minimize the risk of product contamination.

The principle of an APS test is to assess the capability to perform a process (including interventions) under defined conditions to achieve units free from microbial contamination. The level of confidence that the same acceptable result (sterile production) can be achieved when performing the same process for actual (not simulated) production conditions is thereby supported. This confidence is supported by the firm's ability to monitor and control conditions during the APS test, and to verify the same/similar conditions every time the process is performed. The design of the aseptic process, training of aseptic-processing personnel, and use of aseptic technique in the performance of the intervention are essential. This simulation test must be performed periodically to reconfirm this capability.

Including the presence of operators for the maximum permitted time during normal routine production in the simulation allows assessment of those operators' capability to work and behave properly in the cleanroom, despite the increased fatigue over time.

Similarly, within an APS test, the inclusion of certain activities (e.g., interventions) is directed at the demonstration of the capability to perform those activities and to achieve an acceptable result. It is not based on demonstration that the number of repetitions of these actions is directly connected to the capability to perform those actions. It should be understood that an APS in and of itself is not sufficient to qualify interventions. Qualified interventions should first undergo a risk assessment to understand the suitability of the intervention and, depending on the risk-level of the intervention, be confirmed by smoke studies prior to being simulated in an APS.

References
EU GMP Annex 1 (2022); PDA TR22 (Revised 2011).

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Inherent Intervention（計畫性干預）：製程設計中本就預期會發生的操作，例如換班、更換零件、補充物料等。這些應事先列入 APS 計畫。

Corrective Intervention（矯正性干預）：因突發狀況而臨時執行的操作，例如機台卡瓶後的手動清除、容器掉落後的處理。這些屬偏差事件，未在 APS 中模擬的新型矯正干預必須啟動偏差程序。

Qualified Intervention List（合格干預清單）：公司應維護一份經正式評估並記錄的干預操作清單，定期審查以確保仍反映現行製程。

比喻說明 Analogy — 手術室突發狀況模擬

APS 中的干預操作，就像醫院手術前的「情境演練（simulation drill）」。外科醫師在正式手術前，必須事先練習所有已知的風險處置動作（計畫性干預），例如換手術工具、補充耗材。但若手術中突然發生從未演練過的狀況（矯正性干預），就必須在手術後回報、分析，並決定是否將其納入未來的演練劇本中。

沒有練習過的動作，就是潛在的污染風險點。

重點提示 Key Notes

APS 本身不能「資格認定」干預操作：這是非常重要的邏輯！在 APS 中執行一個干預，只是證明「在 APS 條件下可以執行並維持無菌」，並不等同於該干預操作已正式被資格認定（qualified）。正式資格認定應先進行：

  1. 風險評估（risk assessment）
  2. 必要時進行煙霧試驗（smoke study）驗證氣流不受干擾
  3. 才能納入 APS 中模擬

最差情況操作員疲勞（Operator Fatigue）：每位操作員必須在 APS 中參與其在正式生產中允許的最長工作時間，以評估疲勞對無菌操作技術的影響。

實務應用 Practical Application — CDMO 情境

作為 CDMO，當客戶帶入一個新產品，其充填製程有一個非標準的「雙人協作換針操作」（客戶自定義干預）。這個干預：

  • 若在你的 APS 合格干預清單中 → 可以在例行 APS 中模擬，毋須額外處理
  • 若不在清單中 → 必須先做風險評估 + 煙霧試驗 + 技術評估，才能將其納入 APS
  • 若在正式生產中臨時執行了一個未在清單中的干預 → 必須啟動偏差，並評估對該批次的影響

不遵守此邏輯，是 FDA 483 警告信中最常見的 APS 缺失之一。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 某批次充填過程中，操作員因機台異常，執行了一個「未列於干預清單」的手動補料操作，且沒有中止充填。請問後續應採取哪些步驟？
  2. APS 中干預操作的「頻率」應如何決定？僅模擬一次是否足夠？請從文件來源角度說明如何找到適當頻率。
  3. 若兩種性質類似的干預操作（例如：更換 A 型工具 vs. 更換 B 型工具）可以「分組模擬」，需要滿足哪些條件才能分組？

Video Recording — 影片錄影

原文內容 Original Text

Problem Statement
Should APS be video recorded and how long should recordings be kept?

Recommendation
Video recording of APS is not a mandated requirement and should be optional based on the manufacturing organization's internal policies and procedures.

Video recordings of process simulations, in addition to human observation, can be extremely useful, not only as a training tool but as an investigative tool in the evaluation of positive units and for the evaluation of aseptic training efficacy.

If used as part of an investigation, the applicable sections of the video records should be retained as any other GMP documentation. The video record may then be referenced in any deviation raised. Each manufacturer should have a procedure that defines the purpose of the video record, how it is to be used, and its retention period.

NOTE: The internal privacy policy of the company should be considered in the use of video recording of personnel operations.

Rationale
Video records allow for multiple reviews of the execution of the actual simulation process, which facilitates the investigation in case of product contamination. They can also be used for training new associates. There are inherent limitations to using video (e.g., limitation of camera angle view(s)) as the sole means to evaluate the aseptic process and these should be taken into consideration. Video recording equipment should be suitable for entry into an aseptic area, and its placement should not interfere with routine operations. Several countries have strict privacy and retention requirements related to video record retention; these should be taken into consideration when determining the timing for video record destruction.

References
EU GMP Annex 1 (2022); GDPR; PDA TR22 (Revised 2011).

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

影片錄影（Video Recording）的法規地位：

  • FDA 立場：目前並未強制要求 APS 影片錄影，屬建議性工具。
  • EU GMP Annex 1（2022）：明確建議對 APS 進行影片錄影，視為良好實踐（good practice），雖非強制，但歐盟查廠官員對此高度關注。
  • 實務建議：對 CDMO 而言，建議至少對初始驗證（initial qualification）的 APS 進行錄影，作為文件佐證與培訓素材。

重點提示 Key Notes

若影片用於調查（investigation），則自動成為 GMP 文件，必須依照公司的 GDocP（良好文件管理規範）規定保存，不得隨意刪除。保存期限應在 SOP 中明確定義。

GDPR（歐盟通用資料保護法）的挑戰：在歐洲設廠的 CDMO，若錄影內容含有個人臉部或身分識別資訊，必須考慮 GDPR 要求。影片保存期限可能與 GMP 保存要求衝突，需法務與品保協同制定程序。

相機角度的限制（camera angle limitations）：PtC 提醒，影片不能作為評估無菌操作的唯一手段，因為相機有視角盲點。現場人員觀察（human observation）仍是必要的補充。

比喻說明 Analogy

APS 影片錄影就像足球比賽的 VAR（Video Assistant Referee，視頻助理裁判）系統——它不是比賽本身，但當有爭議時（陽性單元出現），可以重播關鍵片段、找到問題根源。沒有 VAR 仍然可以踢球，但爭議發生時就只能靠人的記憶和主觀描述。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. APS 影片一旦啟用調查用途，必須依 GMP 文件管理。請問在你的公司中，GMP 文件通常需保存多久？影片檔案大小可能帶來哪些儲存挑戰？
  2. 若公司決定對所有 APS 進行例行錄影（非調查用），但 SOP 未說明其目的與保存期限，可能面臨什麼法規風險？

Invalidated or Aborted APS — 無效化或中止的 APS

原文內容 Original Text

Problem Statement
Under what conditions can an APS run be invalidated or aborted?

NOTE: For purposes of this document, an invalidated run is one that was filled to completion and then invalidated. An aborted run is one that begins and is then stopped and not filled to completion.

Recommendation — Invalidation
Invalidation of an APS run should be a rare occurrence and should be allowed only under circumstances in which the process is found to have been compromised by conditions external to the processes being simulated. An invalidated APS needs to be repeated.

The following may be reasons to invalidate an APS:

  • Failure of growth promotion of media providing there are no positive units in the APS
  • Definitive evidence that the media used for the APS was not sterile (e.g., sterile filter integrity-test failure).

Recommendation — Abortion
An APS run may be aborted (discontinued) if conditions are present that would normally result in the stoppage of the production run. Any units that have been filled prior to discontinuation should be incubated, regardless of the number filled. These conditions should be described in a production procedure.

Documentation of the event(s) or conditions that caused discontinuation, and the disposition of any media-filled units from the APS should be collected, approved by the quality function, and maintained. Depending on the number of units ultimately filled, an additional APS may be necessary to supplement the aborted one.

Rationale
Media that does not support the growth of challenge organisms or is already contaminated at the start of the APS are not suitable for the detection of potential process contaminants.

Unexpected events that would pose an unacceptable risk to the aseptic process and would result in the stoppage and discontinuation of a production fill do not represent actual production conditions; therefore, they do not need to be simulated. Units filled with media prior to the stoppage of the run may, under normal production conditions, be considered acceptable product and therefore should be evaluated as part of the media fill for the APS run.

References
EU GMP Annex 1 (2022); PDA TR22 (Revised 2011).

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

PtC No. 1 明確區分兩種 APS 失效情境：

類型 定義 條件 後續動作
Invalidation（無效化） 已充填完成，事後宣告無效 培養基本身問題（生長促進失敗、培養基非無菌） 必須重做整個 APS
Abortion（中止） 充填進行中被迫停止，未完成 符合正式生產中會停止生產的條件 已充填單元仍需培養；視充填數量決定是否補做

重點提示 Key Notes

「稀少發生」（rare occurrence）是 PtC 的明確期待：若一家公司 APS 無效化頻率偏高，查廠時必然引起關注——這代表生長促進試驗計畫不完善，或培養基管理有缺失。

無效化的兩個唯一理由（per PtC）：

  1. 培養基生長促進試驗失敗（且無陽性單元）
  2. 確認培養基在 APS 開始時即非無菌（如過濾器完整性測試失敗）

注意：「出現了陽性單元但不想處理」絕對不能作為無效化理由——這是法規的紅線。

FDA vs EU 立場差異：EU GMP Annex 1 在無效化標準上要求更嚴格的書面決策樹（decision tree）和 CAPA。FDA 亦反對任意無效化，但 EU 對文件要求更細緻。

比喻說明 Analogy — 決策樹

APS 無效化的判斷，就像一場考試的閱卷機器故障：
— 若閱卷機器在考試結束後才發現故障（培養基問題事後才知）→ 考試成績作廢（invalidation），需重考。
— 若考試中途發生停電（生產條件不符）→ 停止考試（abortion），但已作答的題目仍按規則處理。
無論哪種情況，都不能以「學生答得不好」為由廢除考試——這等同於以「APS 出現陽性」為由無效化，是法規明確不允許的。

  1. 一個 APS 結果出現 2 個陽性單元，品保主管希望以「充填機台當天有短暫異常，已維修」為由宣告無效化，重新執行 APS。請問此做法是否符合 PtC No. 1？為什麼？

Number of APS Tests Required — 所需 APS 次數

原文內容 Original Text

Problem Statement
How does a manufacturer determine the appropriate number of APS tests to be performed and when to perform them?

Recommendation
For new processes or lines or when there have been changes to the processes, equipment, or manufacturing conditions, or when there have been extensive process disruptions, multiple APS runs should be performed.

NOTE: Most health authorities expect a minimum of three consecutive successful APS runs.

The risk assessment may be used to determine if the changes represent an increased risk to the sterility of the product and therefore should result in multiple APS runs.

When companies are deciding on the minimum number of APS runs, they should take into consideration the following circumstances:

  • In the absence of changes that could increase the risk of product contamination requalification, a single APS run, at a minimum, should be conducted semiannually.
  • In circumstances where aseptic processing runs do not meet criteria, refer to recommendations in Section III, Topic A: Acceptance Criteria.
  • In circumstances where the novelty, duration, and/or complexity of a new routine process exceeds APS experience of the site and operators, a greater number of APS runs can be considered under risk assessment and management.
  • If multiple processes and/or product configurations are being validated, a greater number of APS are required to adequately bracket the operations. The bracketing of processes should only occur when the processes share significant similarities and when the "worst case" can be simulated in both processes (e.g., a lyophilization line being bypassed to be liquid filling only).
  • Executing a media fill before shutdown should not prevent a full investigation and impact assessment of the batches manufactured, should the post-APS shutdown fail.

Rationale
Multiple APS runs afford a better opportunity to uncover process variables that may adversely affect the performance of the aseptic process and therefore pose a higher risk of product contamination. Changes to the aseptic process should be evaluated to determine if they result in a greater risk of product contamination. Where these changes do result in increased risk, the process is considered as changed, and multiple process simulation runs should be considered to determine the potential impact these changes have on the process.

Changes should be addressed using change control procedures, and a rationale for the number of runs must be documented. If there is a media-fill failure after a shutdown, prior batches must be investigated, unless the failure can be correlated to the changes that occurred during the shutdown.

References
EU GMP Annex 1 (2022); ISO 13408-1:2008; PDA TR22 (Revised 2011); PIC/S PI 007-6; FDA Aseptic Guidance (2004).

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

APS 次數要求可以用三個情境框架理解：

情境 最低 APS 次數 法規來源
新產線 / 初始驗證 3 次連續成功 FDA、EU Annex 1、PIC/S
例行再確認（無重大變更） 每 6 個月至少 1 次 PtC No. 1、TR22
重大變更後再驗證 風險評估決定，通常 ≥ 3 次 視變更幅度而定

公式與計算 APS 次數決策框架

新產線啟動（Initial Qualification）：
  最低要求 = 3 次連續成功 APS
  建議：同一天不同班次，或連續 3 天各 1 次
  EU Annex 1 特別強調「連續（consecutive）」

例行再確認（Routine Requalification）：
  頻率 = 每 6 個月 ≥ 1 次
  （= 每年至少 2 次）

重大變更後（Post-Change Requalification）：
  步驟 1 → 執行風險評估（QRM）
  步驟 2 → 若風險評估認定有增加污染風險
          → 需多次 APS（通常 ≥ 3 次）
  步驟 3 → 若風險評估認定風險未增加
          → 可能僅需 1 次 APS
          → 但決策須文件化

APS 失敗後（Post-Failure Response）：
  → 參閱 Topic A（Acceptance Criteria）
  → 通常需要 3 次連續成功 APS 才能重新上市

重點提示 Key Notes

Bracketing（括限法）：當兩條產線高度相似時，可用「最差情況（worst-case）」概念，以一個 APS 覆蓋多個配置，但前提是必須能在同一個模擬中真正模擬最差情境。括限必須有完整文件說明其合理性，不能隨意宣稱適用。

停線前媒體充填的陷阱：即使在計畫停線前執行了 APS 並通過，若停線後的 APS 失敗，仍必須對停線前生產的批次進行調查——除非失敗可明確歸因於停線期間的變更。這是常見的邏輯盲點。

比喻說明 Analogy

APS 次數就像新駕駛考試：

第一次開車上路（新產線），需要通過 3 次連續考試才能拿到正式執照。已有執照後，每半年要做一次定期審驗（例行 APS）。如果你換了一輛不同的車（重大變更），視改變幅度，可能需要重新考試。但若你只換了雨刷（小變更，風險評估認定無增加風險），可能只需 1 次確認即可。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 一條凍乾產線進行年度維護保養後，更換了所有充填針頭（filling needles）和蠕動泵管（peristaltic pump tubing）。請問這是否需要執行多次 APS？依據什麼決定次數？
  2. 公司有兩條充填線（Line A 和 Line B），設備型號相同，但一條有凍乾機連線，一條沒有。可以用括限法讓一條線的 APS 結果覆蓋另一條嗎？需要哪些條件？

Infrequently Used Lines — 低使用頻率產線

原文內容 Original Text

Problem Statement
How many APS runs should be performed for infrequently used lines or processing areas?

Recommendation
If changes to the qualified aseptic process, equipment, personnel, or manufacturing area are not made, and if the equipment and area were maintained under demonstrated control during the time period that the line was not in use, then the procedures for semiannual requalification should be sufficient, with due consideration of ensuring that operator qualification requirements can be assured.

If changes were made or if the company is not able to demonstrate that control is maintained, then those changes or potential changes need to be addressed under the company's change-control procedure, the outcome of which may involve additional APS runs prior to recommencement of GMP production. Additional personnel retraining and requalification may also be necessary, as per organizational policies and procedures.

Rationale
If the aseptic process equipment and manufacturing area have been maintained under demonstrated control and there is no evidence of additional process variables, then the process can be considered as having remained in a qualified state. Since APS is not the sole or primary means to demonstrate ongoing aseptic process validation, when infrequent usage applies, companies should apply equal weight to ongoing process controls, results from EM, and maintenance of training. Therefore, the rationale for reinitiating GMP production for a line in a qualified state should be the same as for a line that is in more frequent use.

References
ISO 13408-1:2008; PIC/S PI 007-6; PDA TR22 (Revised 2011).

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Infrequently Used Line（低使用頻率產線）的定義：PtC No. 1 並未給出明確的使用頻率閾值，但實務上通常指「每年使用少於 2 次」或「超過 6 個月未使用」的充填線。

關鍵判斷依據是：在停用期間，設備與環境是否維持在「可示範控制（demonstrated control）」狀態。這需要以下証據支撐：

  • 定期環境監測（EM）記錄顯示維持在控
  • 設備維護記錄完整（calibration、PM 未中斷）
  • 無重大變更、無人員大幅更替

重點提示 Key Notes

APS 不是唯一的驗證工具：PtC 在此明確說明，APS 並非持續製程驗證（ongoing process validation）的主要或唯一工具。對低頻率使用的產線，應同等重視：

  1. 持續製程管控（ongoing process controls）
  2. 環境監測（Environmental Monitoring, EM）結果
  3. 培訓維持（maintenance of training）

人員資格的特別挑戰：低使用頻率產線最容易忽略的是操作員的無菌技術是否維持。若操作員長期未接觸此產線，其無菌技術可能退化，必須在重新投產前確認操作員資格仍有效。

比喻說明 Analogy

低使用頻率產線就像一輛長期停放的車：只要你定期發動引擎（EM）、保持保養（PM），就不需要重新考駕照（3 次 APS）。但若車子停放超過 1 年沒任何檢查，甚至換了駕駛員（新人員），就必須重新確認所有項目才能上路。

實務應用 Practical Application — CDMO 情境

CDMO 最常見的情境是：某條產線專為一個客戶的季節性產品服務，每年只用 2 個月，其餘 10 個月閒置。此時：

  • 若閒置期間有完整的 EM 記錄、定期設備維保、且無人員大幅更替 → 每半年一次的例行 APS 即可，無需額外增加次數
  • 若閒置期間有任何設備維修（如更換過濾器）、環境異常或人員更替 → 必須啟動變更管理，可能需要 3 次 APS 才能重新投產

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 一條產線已有 9 個月未使用（超過半年）。這是否代表必須重新執行 3 次 APS？請依 PtC No. 1 邏輯說明判斷流程。
  2. 低使用頻率產線的主要查廠風險點是什麼？你會如何設計 SOP 以應對？

Special Considerations for APS in Isolators and Other Advanced Aseptic Processes — 隔離器及先進無菌製程的 APS 特殊考量

原文內容 Original Text

Problem Statement
What are the special considerations for APS in isolators and other advanced aseptic processes (e.g., closed-vial container filling)?

Recommendation
Following the same risk-based analysis and approach taken for APS of all current or advanced processing technologies is recommended.

Rationale for Recommendation
The highest risk for contamination of an aseptic process is based upon the events that occur. The variability of the process (i.e., the level of human versus automated interventions) and the protection of the process (e.g., closed versus open, etc.) are risk factors that should also be addressed. Many isolator applications still involve a number of interventions that may represent a risk if performed poorly, and therefore it is not possible to generalize on the relative risk of a process in an isolator.

Isolators and closed-vial filling systems afford isolation of the aseptic process from microbiological contamination risks (e.g., operators and surrounding room environment) throughout processing. For such closed systems, robust design of the processing equipment and minimized manual manipulations in the manufacturing process might justify simulation of a lower number of interventions (e.g., shift changes) based on a comprehensive risk analysis.

References
EU GMP Annex 1 (2022); FDA Aseptic Guidance (2004); PIC/S PI 007-6.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

隔離器（Isolator）APS vs. 傳統 RABS APS 的核心差異：

面向 傳統 RABS 隔離器（Isolator）
污染來源 操作員風險較高 操作員物理隔離，污染來自手套端口
主要干預風險點 直接手部接觸操作 手套完整性、RTP（快速傳遞口）操作
APS 干預次數 依製程設計，通常較多 若高度自動化，可基於風險評估減少
APS 前置處理 消毒（disinfection） VHP（汽化過氧化氫）去污，需確認殘留

重點提示 Key Notes

VHP（汽化過氧化氫）殘留的重要性：隔離器 APS 前必須進行 VHP 去污。但 VHP 殘留（residual H₂O₂）可能抑制培養基中微生物的生長，導致：

  • 假陰性（false-negative）結果——即便有污染也長不出來
  • 生長促進試驗可能提示此問題

因此，隔離器 APS 必須確認：在充填媒體前，VHP 殘留濃度已降至不影響培養基生長的水平（通常透過殘留 H₂O₂ 監測或定期再驗證建立的充氣排除時間來確認）。

「無法一概而論（not possible to generalize）」：PtC No. 1 明確指出，即使是隔離器，也不能因為「用了隔離器就比較乾淨」而自動減少 APS 次數或干預。每個製程都必須做具體的風險評估。

比喻說明 Analogy

隔離器就像太空站（space station）——幾乎完全與外部環境隔絕，但並非零風險。太空站內仍有需要太空人執行的手動操作（相當於隔離器的手套端口干預），這些操作的品質，決定了安全性，而非「因為在太空站，所以就自動安全」。

而 RABS 則更像無塵室（clean room）——有屏障，但操作員與製程的物理距離更近，污染風險模式不同。

實務應用 Practical Application — 隔離器 APS 設計要點

在為隔離器設計 APS 計畫時，應特別關注：

  • VHP 殘留驗證：確認 APS 執行時的殘留 H₂O₂ 濃度不影響培養基
  • 手套端口干預：即使在隔離器中，手套破裂偵測、手套更換操作仍是高風險干預，必須納入 APS 模擬
  • RTP（快速傳遞口）操作：物料傳入傳出的 RTP 操作必須模擬，這是隔離器的「邊界污染」風險點
  • 自動化程度評估：若充填完全自動化且無需手動干預，可基於風險評估合理減少干預模擬次數，但仍需文件支持

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 一台隔離器的 VHP 去污循環後，若殘留 H₂O₂ 仍為 1 ppm，是否可以立即開始 APS？需要哪些數據支持這個決定？
  2. 一條完全自動化的隔離器充填線（無人員進入），在 APS 設計上可以省略哪些傳統 APS 要素？需要補充哪些額外驗證？

Hold Times for Sterile Bulk — 無菌散裝保存時間

原文內容 Original Text

Problem Statement
Should maximum hold times for sterile bulk be included in the APS for aseptic filling?

Recommendation
Maximum hold times may be included in the APS; however, the APS should not be used as the primary means to validate or qualify the maximum hold time for sterile bulk product/material. Nor should the APS be used to establish maximum production hold times.

The maximum hold time should be based on:

  • Production output requirements
  • Stability of material/product and conditions required during the holding period
  • Ability of the container, assemblies, and procedure to maintain the sterility of bulk material/product, as assessed in the CCS

Maintenance of the sterility of material/product should be qualified as part of a separate sterile hold study that includes an evaluation of the design of the holding vessels, assemblies, and procedures to maintain aseptic conditions for that period. The qualification should include an evaluation of the integrity of the holding container and assembly, and it may comprise studies that include holding sterilized media for the maximum hold time and then evaluating that media for microbial growth.

If a single-use-system (SUS) container is used, separate container–closure integrity studies should be performed that demonstrate the ability of the SUS to maintain integrity over the maximum hold time. Containers, assemblies, and procedures used for holding sterilized materials should be qualified initially and on a periodic frequency as determined through a risk-based assessment and CCS.

Once qualified, the maximum holding time should be considered in the design and performance of the APS. The hold time conditions assessed to be impacted by duration should be used as the rationale for determining the hold time required to be simulated in the APS study.

Rationale
Sterilized material/product must be held under conditions that maintain its sterility for the period of time prior to sealing.

Any bulk that is held as sterile bulk for an extended period of time should be held under conditions that are demonstrated to be:

  • Compatible with product stability
  • Able to maintain sterility

This also further supports the container–closure integrity of the holding unit.

References
EU GMP Annex 1 (2022); PDA TR28 (Revised 2006): Process Simulation Testing for Sterile Bulk Pharmaceutical Chemicals; PDA TR29 (Revised 2012): Points to Consider for Cleaning Validation.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

無菌散裝保存時間（Hold Time for Sterile Bulk）：指無菌原液/半製品在充填前，在無菌容器中等待的最長允許時間。

PtC No. 1 的核心立場是：APS 不是驗證保存時間的主要工具，原因如下：

  • APS 是測試「充填操作的無菌技術」，不是測試「容器在靜置下維持無菌的能力」
  • 最大保存時間必須透過獨立的「無菌保存研究（sterile hold study）」來驗證

APS 中可以（且應該）包含最大保存時間，作為整體 APS 設計的一部分（模擬最差情況），但這與「用 APS 驗證保存時間」是完全不同的邏輯。

公式與計算 無菌散裝保存時間決策模型

最大保存時間（Maximum Hold Time）的決定因素：

因素 1：生產需求（Production Requirements）
  → 批次大小 ÷ 充填速度 = 最短需要的保存時間
  → 這是保存時間的實際業務需求下限

因素 2：產品穩定性（Stability）
  → 在保存條件下（溫度、光照、混合等）
     API 是否維持在規格內
  → 這是上限的科學限制

因素 3：容器無菌完整性（Sterile Hold Capacity）
  → 需獨立的 Sterile Hold Study 驗證：
     (a) 容器 / 組件 / 程序 CCI（容器密封完整性）
     (b) 媒體保存模擬（hold sterilized media for max time → check growth）
     (c) SUS 使用時：需額外 SUS 完整性研究（integrity study）

在 APS 中的應用：
  → 把已驗證的最大保存時間，
    納入 APS 模擬的「最差情境時間線」
  → 不是用 APS 決定保存時間，而是
    在 APS 中「使用」已知的最大保存時間

比喻說明 Analogy — 食物保鮮期決策模型

無菌散裝保存時間就像生鮮食品的保鮮期管理：

你不會在「餐廳服務演練（APS）」中才決定食材的保鮮期——食材保鮮期是事先由食品科學家在獨立的保鮮實驗中確定的（sterile hold study）。演練時，你只是依照已知的保鮮期來安排服務流程，確保食材在保鮮期內被使用（在 APS 中模擬最大保存時間）。

兩件事分開做，各自負責不同的品質保證目的。

重點提示 Key Notes

SUS（一次性使用系統）的額外要求：若使用一次性使用袋或容器儲存無菌散裝，必須：

  • 執行 SUS 專屬的容器密封完整性（CCI）研究
  • 驗證 SUS 在最大保存時間下仍維持密封完整性（不能假設 SUS 天生可以長時間無菌保存）

CCS（污染控制策略）的連結：保存時間的資格認定必須整合進整體 CCS（污染控制策略）框架中，而不是獨立的個別研究。這符合 EU GMP Annex 1（2022）對 CCS 全面整合的要求。

定期再驗證（Periodic Requalification）：保存容器、組件與程序的無菌保存資格認定，需定期透過風險評估決定再驗證頻率，不是一次性活動。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 一個生物製劑批次的充填需要 12 小時，但穩定性研究顯示無菌散裝最多只能在 2–8°C 下保存 8 小時。公司應如何處理這個矛盾？
  2. 若 APS 執行時，設計的最差情況包含了「充填前靜置 10 小時」（模擬最大保存時間），但 APS 培養結果通過，這是否代表 10 小時的保存時間已被驗證？為什麼？
  3. 使用 SUS 無菌袋儲存無菌散裝，除了 APS 之外，還需要哪些額外研究才算完整驗證？

本節重點回顧 Section Summary — Topics I through P

  • Topic I（充填體積）：APS 充填體積以「可目視偵測濁度、翻轉後接觸全部內表面」為原則，而非複製生產充填量；小容器可合理增加充填量。
  • Topic J（干預操作）：計畫性干預事先納入 APS；矯正性干預屬偏差事件。干預資格認定先於 APS，包含風險評估和煙霧試驗。操作員必須在 APS 中參與其最長允許工作時間。
  • Topic K（影片錄影）：錄影非強制要求，但 EU Annex 1 高度建議。若用於調查，自動升格為 GMP 文件，須符合 GDocP 保存要求，並需考量 GDPR 限制。
  • Topic L（無效化與中止）：無效化條件僅限培養基本身問題（生長促進失敗、培養基非無菌）；出現陽性結果絕不可作為無效化理由。中止的已充填單元仍須培養。
  • Topic M（APS 次數）：初始驗證需 3 次連續成功；例行再確認每 6 個月至少 1 次；重大變更後依風險評估決定，通常需重新 3 次。括限法需有完整科學依據。
  • Topic N（低使用頻率產線）：若停用期間維持示範控制（有 EM 記錄、維保正常、無重大變更），半年例行 APS 即可。但須特別確認操作員資格仍有效。
  • Topic O（隔離器 APS）：隔離器不能自動假設風險低；VHP 殘留可能抑制培養基生長，需驗證其不影響靈敏度。手套端口干預和 RTP 操作是隔離器特有的關鍵風險點。
  • Topic P（無菌散裝保存時間）：APS 可包含最大保存時間的模擬，但不能用 APS 驗證保存時間。最大保存時間必須透過獨立的 Sterile Hold Study + CCI 研究來驗證，SUS 需額外的完整性研究。
