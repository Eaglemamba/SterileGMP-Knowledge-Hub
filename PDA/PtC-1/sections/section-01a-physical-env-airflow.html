Topic A.1: Airflow Velocity — 氣流速度量測位置

原文內容 Original Text

Problem Statement
Where should airflow velocity measurements be taken with respect to a filling line or other aseptic processing areas?

Recommendation
Airflow velocity measurements should be taken at locations where critical surfaces or products are exposed, and should be protected by "first air," that is, at working height or elsewhere that would yield valuable information (e.g., at a distance of 15–30 cm from filter face). Measurement location may need to be modified due to equipment configuration that results in interference of airflow velocity as identified by risk assessment and rationalized and documented by the contamination control strategy (CCS). Readings should be reproducible.

Rationale
The primary reason for airflow velocity measurements in unidirectional airflow areas (e.g., areas where products, product-contact packaging components, and product-contact surfaces are exposed) is to ensure adequate airflow to protect the materials from external airborne contamination and to verify continued compliance with current smoke studies. The adequacy of the environment can be determined, in part, from airflow velocity and airflow visualization studies, and from particulate matter monitoring (at the working position).

Accurate measurements can be taken and changes over time can be detected when airflow velocities are evaluated at a predetermined distance from the filter surface that is sufficiently close to the filter surface to be reproducible to detect changes in the performance of the filter.

These data must be paired with an airflow visualization study to provide evidence of adequate protection of the aseptic process when the air velocity is in a specific range (and no change in the design of the area has occurred).

The airflow velocity depends on the design of the filling line, room design, and air-handling system. Once velocity is determined, it is important to ensure that the velocity stays within the specified parameters. Routine air velocity measurements should be taken at the same locations used during the initial airflow studies to ensure consistency.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

First Air（第一道空氣）：指直接來自 HEPA 過濾器、尚未經過任何設備或人員的潔淨空氣。暴露中的藥液、容器及產品接觸表面必須始終沐浴在「第一道空氣」之中，確保沒有任何上游污染源的二次風流。

量測位置邏輯：距濾網面 15–30 cm 是「足夠靠近以偵測濾網性能變化、又不受設備干擾」的最佳平衡點。工作高度量測則驗證產品實際暴露位置的保護效果。兩者目的不同，通常需要同時執行。

CCS（Contamination Control Strategy，污染控制策略）：若設備外形干擾氣流，必須在 CCS 中合理化並記錄修改後的量測位置——這是 2022 EU Annex 1 的明確要求。

比喻說明 Analogy

手術室通風標準：想像手術室的無菌罩頂（surgical canopy）從天花板向下吹送過濾後的空氣，確保手術切口上方永遠是「從天花板直達傷口」的乾淨風流，不被手術人員的動作打亂。氣流速度量測就好比護理師定期用風速計確認罩頂風速符合規格——太慢則保護不足，太快則造成渦流反而引入污染。無菌充填（Aseptic Filling）的 Grade A 保護邏輯與此完全相同。

重點提示 Key Notes

速度量測必須與煙霧試驗配對：速度數值本身不能單獨作為「環境足夠保護」的證明。PDA PtC-1 明確指出，必須將速度數據與氣流可視化研究（smoke study）結合，才能提供完整的無菌保護證據。許多公司只有速度記錄而缺乏同期的可視化驗證，這是查廠常見的缺失。

量測位置一致性：例行監測必須使用與初始資格驗證（OQ/PQ）完全相同的量測點，才能偵測出濾網性能的長期退化趨勢。

  2. 若充填機設備外形（如注射針頭載台）干擾了 Grade A 罩頂的氣流量測，依據 PtC-1 應如何處理？需要記錄在哪份文件中？
Topic A.2: Airflow Velocity Measurements — 0.45 m/s 是否為強制要求？

原文內容 Original Text

Problem Statement
Is an airflow velocity of 0.45 meters/sec ± 20% a requirement at the working surface in a critical zone?

Recommendation
Airflow patterns should be sufficient to protect exposed products, product-contact packaging components, and product-contact surfaces from the ingress of potential environmental contaminants outside the critical zone. Unidirectional flow is an important factor in providing this protection.

Although a linear air velocity of 0.45 meters/sec ± 20% when measured 15 cm to 30 cm from the filter face is a commonly recommended range to establish unidirectional airflow, this should not be considered a requirement at the working level. The velocity ranges at the working level and at the filter face should be established and justified in the CCS. Performing this test in conjunction with air visualization studies can also demonstrate the maintenance of unidirectionality at the working height, and this should be done at both at-rest and in-operation situations.

Rationale
Airflow velocity and pattern are dependent upon obstacles encountered within the critical zone, including the filling equipment/line, interventions by personnel, and configuration of the barrier that separates the zone from the outer environment. Unidirectional flow is intended to allow the air to flow smoothly past and around these obstacles with minimal turbulence and no induction of potential contamination from outside the zone. The supply air velocity should be correlated to airflow visualization studies and optimized to produce airflow patterns that protect exposed products, product-contact packaging components, and product-contact surfaces from airborne contamination at the working level. This may be lower (or even higher) than the recommended accepted range.

NOTE (EU Annex 1, 2022): "Unidirectional airflow systems should provide a homogeneous air speed in a range of 0.36–0.54 m/s (guidance value) at the working position, unless otherwise scientifically justified in the CCS. Airflow visualization studies should correlate with the air speed measurement." The air velocity range is specifically indicated as a guidance.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

0.45 m/s ±20% 的由來：這個數值源自 1960–70 年代美國航太工業潔淨室規範（美國 Federal Standard 209），後被製藥業沿用。它的意義是「足以維持單向流動性的經驗值」，而非嚴格的物理必要條件。

PDA vs EU Annex 1 立場比較：

  • PDA PtC-1：0.45 m/s ±20%「不應視為工作高度的強制要求」，應由 CCS 建立並論證。
  • EU Annex 1 (2022)：提供 0.36–0.54 m/s 作為指引值（guidance value），並要求與氣流可視化研究相關聯。
  • 共同底線：兩者均強調「目標是保護產品的單向流動性」，而非死守一個數字。

公式與計算 Formula

EU Annex 1 (2022) 指引範圍（工作位置）：
  下限：0.36 m/s（= 0.45 × 0.80）
  建議：0.45 m/s
  上限：0.54 m/s（= 0.45 × 1.20）

PDA 建議（距濾網面 15–30 cm）：
  0.45 m/s ± 20% → 0.36 – 0.54 m/s

注意：工作高度速度通常低於濾網面速度
因為設備障礙物會分散氣流。
CCS 須分別建立兩個位置的驗收標準。

比喻說明 Analogy

馬路限速標誌 vs 實際行車安全：0.45 m/s 就像高速公路的「建議速度 90 km/h」標誌，並非硬性限速。在山路彎道（充填機設備密集的 Grade A 區）可能需要降速（更低氣流速度），在筆直寬敞路段（開放式罩頂）可能需要稍快才能維持穩定的單向流動性。重點不是速度計上的數字，而是「氣流是否真的保護了產品」——這需要煙霧試驗來確認。

重點提示 Key Notes — FDA vs EU 差異

FDA 2004 Aseptic Processing Guidance 仍常被引用 0.45 m/s，但措辭為「推薦」而非「要求」。
EU Annex 1 (2022) 明確標注 0.36–0.54 m/s 為「guidance value」，允許科學論證偏離。
實務上：若您的設施速度在 0.3 m/s 但煙霧試驗顯示完美的單向保護，並有充分的科學論據記錄於 CCS，FDA/EMA 查廠均可接受。關鍵是「論據」與「文件」，而非硬套數字。

實務應用 Practical Application

CDMO 情境：您的客戶要求在標書（RFP）中保證 Grade A 氣流速度為 0.45 m/s ±20%。但您的充填機因設備外形限制，工作高度實際速度為 0.38 m/s，但煙霧試驗顯示完美保護。

建議做法：在 CCS 中記錄工作高度的驗收標準（如 0.35–0.55 m/s），附上煙霧試驗報告作為科學論據，並向客戶解釋 PDA 與 EU Annex 1 均不要求死守 0.45 m/s。這樣既符合法規，也能讓客戶理解。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. EU Annex 1 (2022) 與 PDA PtC-1 在 0.45 m/s 的立場是否一致？各自的措辭關鍵字是什麼？
  2. 若工作高度速度低於 0.36 m/s，但煙霧試驗結果良好，法規上可以接受嗎？需要哪些文件支撐？
Topic A.3: Airflow Velocity Measurement Frequency — 量測頻率

原文內容 Original Text

Problem Statement
When do airflow velocity measurements have to be taken?

Recommendation
Airflow velocity measurements should be taken during operational and performance qualification studies. High-efficiency particulate air (HEPA) filters in critical zones (i.e., Grade A and surrounding Grade B areas) should then be tested every six months, and the frequency of testing HEPA filters in other areas should be at least annually. More frequent measurements may be appropriate if other measures of cleanroom quality indicate a significant deviation (e.g., increased airborne particulates).

The impact of air velocity levels that are outside validated acceptance criteria should be evaluated by performing airflow visualization studies as part of applicable change-management procedures.

Rationale
Airflow velocity is measured to ensure adequate airflow to protect exposed products, product-contact packaging components, and product-contact surfaces. It is also measured to ensure that there have been no significant changes to the performance of the heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC) system. Airflow criteria are established during qualification studies.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

頻率要求一覽：

  • OQ/PQ 期間：必須執行（建立基準值）
  • Grade A + 周邊 Grade B：每 6 個月（半年一次）
  • 其他區域（Grade C、D）：至少每 12 個月（每年一次）
  • 例外觸發（ad-hoc）：顆粒數異常升高、HVAC 系統重大變更、查廠前

值得注意：「氣流速度量測頻率」與「HEPA 完整性測試頻率」在 PtC-1 中是同步的（見 Topic E），二者通常一起執行。

公式與計算 Formula — 年度測試行事曆

Grade A / Grade B 測試週期：
  1月 → 7月 → 1月 → ...（每6個月）

Grade C / Grade D 測試週期：
  1月 → 1月（每12個月）

若有顆粒數 OOS 事件：
  → 立即執行氣流可視化研究
  → 評估速度是否偏離驗收標準
  → 啟動變更管理程序

比喻說明 Analogy

汽車定期保養：Grade A 每 6 個月測一次氣流速度，就像賽車每場比賽前必須檢查煞車性能，因為風險最高；Grade C/D 就像一般家用車每年一次年度保養。重點是：「風險越高，保養頻率越高」。HVAC 異常就是「行駛中警示燈亮起」，需要立即停下檢查，不能等到下一個排程保養。

重點提示 Key Notes

速度超出驗收標準時的處理邏輯：不能僅記錄偏差並調整設定值就了事。PDA PtC-1 明確要求必須以「氣流可視化研究」評估影響，並作為適用的「變更管理程序（change-management procedures）」的一部分。這意味著速度 OOS 事件必須觸發一次完整的煙霧試驗重新評估。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  2. HEPA 過濾器在 10 月完成了 PQ 驗證，下一次半年度測試應安排在何時？
Topic B.1: Airflow Visualization — 氣流可視化研究

原文內容 Original Text

Problem Statement
What is the purpose of airflow visualization studies and how often should they be performed?

Recommendation
Airflow visualization studies should be used to evaluate airflow patterns in critical zones including Grade A and Grade B to ensure there is no ingress at interfaces from the surrounding environment. Such studies are recommended for the qualification of new or renovated facilities or of changes in facilities. In particular, airflow visualization studies should be done during qualification studies, and the studies should be repeated when any significant changes are made that might have an impact on study results (e.g., changes to air-handling systems, aseptic processing equipment, HEPA filters, air return risers/outlets, effectiveness of barrier systems, such as restricted access barrier system (RABS) doors) and after periodic intervals that are consistent with the capabilities and level of process monitoring of the air-handling system.

For Grade A airflow, visualization studies should also be performed to assess if new and current interventions are in compliance with good aseptic working principles. Evaluation of these interventions should show that they are appropriate to meet first-air principles. These interventions should then be simulated and assessed in aseptic process simulation (APS) runs, and finally be included in the list of authorized interventions.

Airflow visualization studies can also be used for investigative purposes (e.g., in cases of contamination) and can be very effective training tools.

Visualized airflow patterns should be executed in operational (dynamic) conditions to understand the impact of moving parts and personnel interventions on the actual airflow. In situ air-pattern analysis should be conducted at the critical zones to demonstrate unidirectional airflow and sweeping action over and away from the product under dynamic conditions.

The decision and frequency of repeated air visualization studies should be based on a risk assessment of the process and capabilities of the air system. Even where no changes are believed to have occurred and where monitoring indicates that the airflow remains in a state of control, it is prudent to consider periodic air visualization studies. Where that period of time exceeds 5 years, additional justification for that length of time may be necessary.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

氣流可視化研究（Airflow Visualization Study / Smoke Study）：使用乾冰煙霧、油質煙霧或液態氮等煙霧介質，在潔淨室動態（in-operation）條件下，拍攝氣流流動型態，確認：

  • 產品暴露區始終在「第一道空氣」保護下
  • 操作人員介入不破壞 Grade A 保護
  • 氣流從較潔淨區域向較不潔淨區域流動（無反轉）
  • RABS 門開關不造成外部污染入侵

觸發時機（必須執行）：新廠建立、重大設備變更、HEPA 更換、RABS/隔離器門設計變更、APS 前評估。

比喻說明 Analogy

游泳池排水試驗：想像在游泳池中滴入彩色染料，觀察水流走向——哪裡形成死角、哪裡水流回流。氣流可視化就是同樣的概念：煙霧是「染料」，HEPA 罩頂是「注水口」，充填機設備是「障礙物」。死角（渦流）等於微生物污染的高風險點，設計師必須重新調整設備布局或氣流方向來消除死角。

重點提示 Key Notes — 超過 5 年未重做需額外論證

PDA PtC-1 明確指出：若距上次氣流可視化研究超過 5 年，需要「額外的時間論證」。這在查廠中是高頻缺失點——許多設施在 PQ 驗證時執行一次煙霧試驗後，即使設備多次小幅調整也未重新執行。

EU Annex 1 (2022) Section 4.15 則要求在較不重要區域也需驗證氣流從潔淨到不潔淨方向的流動，這比舊版要求更嚴格。

實務應用 Practical Application

授權介入清單（Authorized Interventions List）的建立流程：
1. 首先在動態條件下執行煙霧試驗，確認各介入動作（如更換充填針、取樣）不破壞 Grade A 氣流。
2. 將通過的介入動作納入 APS（無菌製程模擬）中驗證，確認實際操作不引入污染。
3. 最後將完成驗證的介入列入「授權介入清單」，每次作業只允許執行清單內的動作。
這是 EU Annex 1 (2022) 對介入管理的完整閉環要求。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 為什麼氣流可視化研究必須在「動態（in-operation）」條件下執行？靜態（at-rest）條件的結果有何侷限性？
  2. 某 CDMO 客戶更換了充填機型號，但 Grade A 罩頂 HEPA 系統沒有改變，是否需要重新執行煙霧試驗？依據是什麼？
  3. 煙霧試驗結果顯示某操作員的介入動作造成輕微渦流，但沒有直接打到開口容器，應如何處理？
Topic B.2: Airflow Visualization Recording — 影像記錄要求

原文內容 Original Text

Problem Statement
Should airflow visualization studies be recorded?

Recommendation
Airflow visualization studies should be recorded (typically by video), and the review and approval of the video recording should be documented and retained. Written conclusions should be reported to describe the outcomes of these tests. Preferably, the source of smoke generation should cause minimal contamination of the area. The quantity of smoke generated should be appropriate for the size of the area being evaluated and sufficient to clearly discern the airflow pattern. The impact of operator intervention and equipment operation during aseptic processing should be investigated during airflow visualization studies.

Multiple angles may be needed to provide an accurate representation of airflow.

Video recordings are electronic data and must be retained and maintained accordingly. Records of airflow visualization studies should also be used as training material for personnel operating in cleanrooms.

Rationale
Due to the fact that video recordings provide a two-dimensional visualization, multiple angles may be needed to provide an accurate representation of three-dimensional airflow visualization.

Recordings allow for additional evaluation of the study at any successive time, by employees who did not participate in their execution, and for training purposes.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

影像記錄的法規屬性：PDA PtC-1 明確指出影像（video）屬於「電子數據（electronic data）」，必須依照電子數據管理規範（GDocP/Annex 11/21 CFR Part 11）進行保存。這意味著：

  • 原始影像不得刪除或篡改
  • 需建立審閱與批准的電子簽名記錄
  • 保存期限依批次記錄規範（通常產品上市後至少 1 年或產品有效期 +1 年）
  • 影像必須清晰到可以辨識氣流型態——模糊或低解析度的記錄不可接受

重點提示 Key Notes — 多角度拍攝

三維空間問題：Grade A 罩頂的氣流是三維的（X、Y、Z 軸），但單一攝影機只能捕捉二維平面。若只從正面拍攝，可能遺漏側面渦流；只從上方拍攝，可能看不到底部回流。

實務建議：至少從前方、側面各至少 2 個角度拍攝，關鍵操作動作（如開 RABS 門、換充填針）需要近距離特寫。所有角度的影像均需包含在最終報告中。

比喻說明 Analogy

法庭監視器規範：銀行保險庫的監視錄影有嚴格的保存與防篡改規定，因為它是犯罪調查的關鍵證據。氣流可視化影像的地位完全類似——它是「無菌製程保護有效性」的電子證據。FDA 或 EMA 查廠人員有權要求調閱原始影像，確認與報告結論一致。若影像遺失或品質不佳，等同於「監視器壞掉了」，是嚴重的 GMP 缺失。

實務應用 Practical Application

煙霧試驗的清潔程序：PtC-1 提醒使用的煙霧介質應「盡量少污染潔淨室」。實務上，油質煙霧會殘留在 HEPA 濾網和設備表面，試驗後需要依照已驗證的清潔 SOP（包含表面清潔、EM 採樣確認）恢復到潔淨室規格。這個清潔驗證本身也需要記錄並列入試驗方案。

練習思考 Practice Questions

Topic C: Grade A Environment Over Cappers — 軋蓋區域的環境要求

原文內容 Original Text

Problem Statement
What are the recommended environmental conditions for capping aseptically processed, stoppered vials when the capping takes place outside of a Grade A environment?

Recommendation
When capping is undertaken as a clean process outside a Grade A environment, a Grade A air supply over the capping machine is sufficient, provided validated automatic checks for all the vials (e.g., using a missing/raised stopper detector) are in place immediately prior to, or in front of, the capping station to reject any vials with stoppers that do not remain properly seated before they enter the capping and sealing station. The HEPA filters used should be requalified periodically as described in Section I, Topic E.

Monitoring requirements for total particulate and microbiological contamination at the capping machine outside the aseptic core should be defined by the company following a risk assessment.

NOTE (EU Annex 1): When vial capping is undertaken as a clean process outside the aseptic core, stoppered vials should be protected with a Grade A air supply until the cap has been crimped. It also states that RABS and isolators may be beneficial in assuring the required conditions.

Rationale
A properly seated stopper represents an adequately sealed container and a microbiological barrier. Therefore, efforts should be taken to ensure that the container is maintained under conditions that will not add contamination until it is properly stoppered prior to capping. When there is a risk that the stopper might be raised prior to capping, steps should be taken to reject those vials.

A qualified aseptic filling process includes assurance of the proper routine placement of stoppers, as aligned with container closure integrity requirements. A missing or raised stopper-detector must be qualified and challenged on a routine basis and units with compromised or non-integral stoppers should be rejected before capping.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

軋蓋（Capping/Crimping）的位置選擇：軋蓋在工業上通常有兩種配置：

  • 配置 1：在 Grade A 區域內軋蓋（最高保護）
  • 配置 2：在 Grade A 氣流供應下、但位於 Grade A 區域外（本 Topic 討論的情境）

配置 2 的核心前提是：「正確就座的瓶塞本身即是微生物屏障」。因此，只要確保瓶塞完整（透過自動檢測剔除），在乾淨的 Grade A 氣流供應下軋蓋即可接受。

關鍵控制點：軋蓋前必須有已驗證的自動化瓶塞偵測系統（missing/raised stopper detector），且需定期挑戰（challenge testing）。

重點提示 Key Notes — PDA vs EU Annex 1 差異

PDA PtC-1：軋蓋機上方有 Grade A 氣流供應即足夠，監測要求由公司依風險評估定義。
EU Annex 1 (2022)：要求在整個軋蓋完成前，瓶子必須持續受到 Grade A 氣流供應的保護（not just at the machine, but until the cap has been crimped），並建議考慮 RABS 或隔離器（Isolator）以確保條件。
結論：EU 標準比 PDA 要求更嚴格，CDMO 若以歐洲市場為目標，應遵循 EU Annex 1 的解讀。

比喻說明 Analogy

快遞包裝密封檢查：想像一件精密儀器，在包裝車間（相當於無菌充填區）放入泡棉保護後，送到另一個房間（相當於軋蓋區）進行外盒封裝。如果外盒封裝前確認泡棉沒有脫落（相當於瓶塞自動偵測），並在乾淨的環境下完成封裝，整體保護效果是可接受的。但如果泡棉可能鬆動，就必須在與包裝車間相同等級的環境中完成封裝（在 Grade A 區內軋蓋）。

Topic D: Differential Pressure — 差壓管理

原文內容 Original Text

Problem Statement
What should be the air-pressure differential between zones of differing cleanliness classification (e.g., between Grade B and Grade C zones)?

Recommendation
Positive pressure between zones with different air cleanliness classifications should ensure airflow direction from the "cleaner" toward the "less clean" zones.

A prolonged zero-pressure differential between such zones, or a pressure reversal to the extent that the cleanliness of the environment is compromised, must be avoided.

A pressure differential between different classified zones of not less than 10 pascals (with doors closed) is commonly expected. However, because the principal purpose is to ensure that a minimum measurable positive pressure is maintained, other differential pressure levels may be required based on facility complexity, HVAC system design, and operational characteristics.

An appropriate pressure differential is principally maintained by interlocked and alarmed air locks and doors between the zones of different cleanliness levels and should be continuously monitored by differential pressure devices. Although brief periods of low- or zero-differential pressure may exist between an area and an air lock (of the same grade) with a door open, a measurable pressure differential should be maintained between areas (of different grades) separated by the air lock.

An alarm strategy with appropriately qualified alarm delay limits should be defined for changes to differential pressure and/or pressure drops and should be underpinned by quality risk management. Transient low- or zero-differential pressure occurrences represent critical alarm functions, and these alarms must indicate airflow inversions that are assessed to pose a risk to cleanliness of the area.

Inversion of airflow may be rectified by such steps as:

  • Room balancing (e.g., volume of air and differential pressure adjustments in adjacent rooms) and air-exchange rate of the room
  • Changes in sequence and/or timing of door openings
  • Process and cleanroom design (use of interlocks)
  • Clearing obstructions from air returns

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

差壓（Differential Pressure, dP）的物理目的：確保空氣永遠從「較潔淨（更高等級）」的區域流向「較不潔淨（較低等級）」的區域，阻止微生物、顆粒從外部環境侵入潔淨核心。

基本要求：

  • 最低差壓：10 Pa（關門狀態）——這是業界通行的期望值
  • 必須連續監測（非間歇式）
  • 必須有警報系統（帶有適當的延遲設定，避免開門時的短暫低壓觸發誤報）
  • 警報策略必須有 QRM（品質風險管理）支撐

注意：10 Pa 是「通常期望值（commonly expected）」，非硬性法規數值。設施可依複雜度設定不同值，但必須有論據。

比喻說明 Analogy

差壓如同水壩：想像一個水壩系統——Grade A 是最高水位水庫，Grade B 次之，Grade C、D 依序遞減。水（相當於空氣）永遠從高處流向低處，不會逆流。差壓就是這個「高度落差」的量度。若某段水位差歸零（差壓為零），代表兩個水庫水位相同，一旦有擾動（開門、設備震動），就可能發生雙向流動（逆流），骯髒的水就會流入潔淨的水庫。10 Pa 的差壓就是確保有「足夠高度落差」的最低安全閾值。

公式與計算 Formula — 典型差壓梯度設計

典型梯度設計（由內而外）：
  Grade A（核心）：最高壓力（如 +60 Pa 相對大氣）
  Grade B          ：次高（如 +50 Pa）  ΔP = 10 Pa
  Grade C          ：中等（如 +40 Pa）  ΔP = 10 Pa
  Grade D          ：較低（如 +30 Pa）  ΔP = 10 Pa
  一般環境          ：大氣壓（0 Pa 基準）

注意：含致敏性或高毒性物質的設施
可能需要負壓（negative pressure）
以防物質外洩，此時梯度設計相反。

重點提示 Key Notes — 警報策略設計

延遲警報（Alarm Delay）的必要性：門開啟時差壓必然短暫下降，若沒有適當的延遲設定（如 5–30 秒），HVAC 系統的警報將不斷誤觸發，增加操作員的「警報疲勞（alarm fatigue）」，反而掩蓋了真正的危險信號。

EU Annex 1 (2022) 要求差壓系統必須記錄並報告，且異常必須被評估。美國 FDA 對持續低差壓事件通常會追問根本原因及批次影響評估。

Topic E: Testing of HEPA Filters — HEPA 過濾器測試

原文內容 Original Text

Problem Statement
What is the frequency with which HEPA filters should be tested?

Recommendation
HEPA-filter testing should comprise integrity testing, airflow volume measurement, and airflow velocity testing. HEPA filters should be tested minimally upon initial installation and replacement. Requalification frequency should be based on a risk assessment using historical performance, available data, cleanroom design, and usage.

HEPA-filter performance characteristics for Grade A (and surrounding Grade B) areas should be requalified at a maximum interval of six months. For lower-grade areas (Grades C, Grade D, and remaining Grade B areas not required to be supplied with unidirectional airflow), a maximum interval of 12 months applies. The airflow velocity testing may be replaced with recovery testing. The frequency of recovery testing in these lower grade areas should be determined based on risk assessment.

In lower-grade areas where airflow is unidirectional the air velocity testing should be based on risk assessment.

Production campaigns should be scheduled to allow for requalification. Where exceptions occur that result in a delay in a periodic requalification, the requalification should be performed as soon as possible after the delay and the risk of such a delay on product quality should be evaluated.

Where testing indicates a HEPA filter integrity or performance failure, steps should be taken to repair or replace the filter, or otherwise address the failure, and steps should also be taken to address product impact from the failure.

Rationale
Because routine monitoring is not sensitive enough to assure the performance of the HEPA filter system or individual HEPA filters, periodic retesting of the HEPA-filter system and individual HEPAs is beneficial, as reflected in EU Annex 1 and other health authority guidances.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

HEPA（High-Efficiency Particulate Air）過濾器測試的三要素：

  • 完整性測試（Integrity Test）：即 DOP/PAO 測試（Dispersed Oil Particulate / Poly-alpha Olefin），以氣溶膠挑戰 HEPA 濾網，確認無針孔或密封缺陷。測試儀器需定期校正。
  • 氣流量測（Airflow Volume Measurement）：確認通過濾網的總風量符合設計規格。
  • 氣流速度測試（Airflow Velocity Testing）：與 Topic A.3 的要求一致（Grade A/B 每 6 個月）。

恢復測試（Recovery Testing）：在低等級區域（Grade C/D），可以用「潔淨室恢復時間測試」替代氣流速度測試，量測潔淨室在人工污染後恢復到規格內顆粒數的時間，間接確認 HVAC 系統效能。

  修補（見 Topic F）或更換 → 完整性 + 速度重測

重點提示 Key Notes

排程延誤的處理：PtC-1 提醒，若因生產排程延誤了例行資格重驗（requalification），應盡快補測，並評估延誤期間對產品品質的風險。這是一個重要的 CAPA 觸發點——不能僅以「生產繁忙」作為理由而無正式評估記錄。

實務應用 Practical Application

CDMO 資格維護行事曆設計：建議將 HEPA 測試與氣流速度量測整合進同一次「半年度設施再資格驗證（semi-annual facility requalification）」，與 APS（無菌製程模擬）的排程協調，避免出現連續停產。一般而言，設施再資格占滿 3–5 個工作日（視設施規模），應提前 2–3 個月向客戶告知停產計畫。

Topic F: HEPA Filter Patching — HEPA 過濾器修補

原文內容 Original Text

Problem Statement
Can HEPA filters be patched? If so, what is the maximum allowable patch size?

Recommendation
HEPA-filter patching for Grade A should be avoided. HEPA-filter patching for Grade B–D should proceed only after the repair is justified by careful risk assessment, the repair is performed to industry standards to ensure patch durability, and aseptic process production should only commence after the performance of the repaired HEPA-filter has been verified to show that it has returned to a qualified state. Testing should include leak and velocity testing. If the testing does not verify that the repaired HEPA has returned to a qualified state, then the HEPA should be repaired if possible or replaced.

HEPA filters can be repaired or patched, but the company should develop a rationale for allowable filter repair shape and size fit for the different installation and area classifications based on a thorough risk assessment.

If this rationale supports repair, HEPA filters may be patched with room-temperature vulcanization silicone or other suitable material as long as filter integrity is restored and the airflow volume and velocity through the filter are not significantly affected (i.e., room pressure differentials and airflow patterns remain as validated).

Industry standards for patch size:

  • IEST-RP-CC001.5: Not to exceed an area of 13 cm² (2 in²) in any one patch, or a total of 1% of the area being patched.
  • EN ISO 29463-4: All repairs together shall not block or restrict more than 0.5% of the filter face area and the maximum length of each single repair shall not exceed 3.0 cm.
  • IEST RP-CC034.5: Field repair should not block or restrict more than an additional 3.0% of the filter face area, and no single repair should have a lesser dimension exceeding 3.8 cm (1.5 in.).
  • ISO 14644-3, Section B.6.6: Allows for repairs "by agreement between the customer and supplier."
NOTE (EU Annex 1): Requires precautions when carrying out this type of intervention, such as restricting access to the work area, clearly defining work protocols, and cleaning, disinfecting, and environmental monitoring being considered.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Grade A 的立場：不建議修補（avoid）
Grade A 是最高風險區域，其 HEPA 過濾器任何修補的結果均難以與新濾網的完整性相比擬。PDA 的立場是「避免（avoid）」，而不是「禁止（prohibit）」——雖然有此區別，但實務上查廠人員對 Grade A 修補持極嚴格態度。

Grade B–D 的立場：可修補，但有條件
必須要有：(1) 風險評估論據、(2) 行業標準支撐的修補規格、(3) 修補後的完整性與速度再測試通過。不同標準（IEST、ISO、EN ISO）對「最大修補面積」的規定略有差異，公司需選定一個並記錄在 SOP 中。

比喻說明 Analogy

安全帽破損是否繼續戴：假設你的安全帽（相當於 HEPA 過濾器）在意外中出現小裂縫，你有幾個選擇：(A) 直接扔掉換新的（Grade A 建議做法）；(B) 用特殊膠帶補好，然後請專業機構重新測試強度（Grade B–D 修補流程）；(C) 不管裂縫繼續戴（絕對不可接受）。關鍵在於：你用來騎摩托車（Grade B）還是賽車頭盔（Grade A）——後者的標準更高，裂縫寧可換新，不冒任何風險。

實務應用 Practical Application

修補 SOP 建立要點（Grade B–D）：
1. 風險評估：評估破損位置（中央 vs 邊緣）、大小、可能受影響的製程。
2. 修補規格：選定適用的行業標準（通常採 EN ISO 29463-4 因歐洲市場），明確最大修補面積與長度。
3. 修補材料：記錄使用的材料（如室溫硫化矽膠，RTV silicone）及批號。
4. 修補後測試：完整性測試（PAO）+ 速度測試，兩者均需通過才可恢復生產。
5. EU Annex 1 要求：修補作業區隔離 + 事後清潔消毒 + EM 採樣。

Topic G: Laminar vs Unidirectional Airflow — 術語澄清

原文內容 Original Text

Problem Statement
Should airflow in Grade A critical zones be laminar or unidirectional?

Recommendation
Airflow in critical Grade A zones (i.e., where products or sterile components are exposed) should be unidirectional. In aseptic processing, unidirectional airflow means that the air mass flows from the source (i.e., the HEPA filter) in one direction into or over the critical zone and its contents in such a way as to ensure that the exposed products and sterile components are always in air that has not passed any other component, operator, or equipment that has not been sterilized. This is the concept of first air.

"NOTE: In closed isolators, turbulent flow may be acceptable for Grade A zones when it is supported by operational qualification demonstrating the maintenance of acceptable particulate levels."

Rationale
The term "laminar," although used historically in this context, is problematic. True laminar airflow is virtually impossible to achieve in physical environments. Moreover, true laminar airflow is not necessary to achieve appropriate airflow that maintains the first-air concept.

ISO 14644-3:2019 defines unidirectional airflow as "controlled airflow through the entire cross-section of a clean zone with a steady velocity and approximately parallel streamlines."

The first-air approach applies to all airflow situations and aseptic processing methods where contamination through airborne means might be a risk.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

術語演變的歷史背景：

  • Laminar Flow（層流）：物理學術語，指雷諾數（Reynolds number）極低時流體中每個粒子完全平行流動，不發生任何混合。在真實潔淨室環境中（有設備、人員、溫度差），這幾乎不可能實現。
  • Unidirectional Airflow（單向流）：業界改用的實用術語（ISO 14644-3 定義），重點在「穩定速度」和「大致平行的流線」，允許小程度的渦流，但總體方向一致。

EU Annex 1 (2022) 的對齊：EU Annex 1 全文改用「unidirectional airflow（單向氣流）」取代舊版的「laminar flow（層流）」，與 ISO 14644 和本 PDA PtC-1 的建議完全對齊。

比喻說明 Analogy

河流 vs 水道：「層流」就像理想狀態下靜止水槽中的完全平行流動，現實中幾乎不存在。「單向流」就像設計良好的人工水道——雖然水流過障礙物時會有小渦流，但整體上從上游流向下游，不會倒流。充填線 Grade A 區域就是這樣的「人工水道」：設備是障礙物，但只要主體氣流方向是從 HEPA 向下（或向前），就達到了保護產品的目的。

重點提示 Key Notes — 隔離器的例外

PDA PtC-1 提出一個重要例外：密閉隔離器（Closed Isolators）中，湍流（turbulent flow）在 Grade A 區域可以被接受——前提是 OQ 必須證明能維持可接受的顆粒水平。

這個例外的邏輯是：密閉隔離器本身提供了極高的物理屏障保護，即使氣流非單向，外部污染也無法進入。因此，保護機制是「密封屏障」而非「氣流方向」。這與開放式或半開放式 RABS 系統完全不同。

實務應用 Practical Application

文件中的術語一致性：許多舊版 SOP、驗證方案和批次記錄仍使用「laminar flow」。建議在下一次文件審閱周期中統一更換為「unidirectional airflow（單向氣流）」，以與 EU Annex 1 (2022)、ISO 14644 及 PDA PtC-1 保持一致，避免查廠時引發監管質疑。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 為什麼「真正的層流（true laminar flow）」在製藥潔淨室中幾乎不可能實現？哪些物理因素使其難以達成？
  2. 密閉隔離器（Closed Isolator）中允許湍流的理由是什麼？這與開放式 RABS 系統有何根本差異？
  3. 如果公司的 URS（用戶需求規格）中要求「laminar flow for Grade A」，是否需要更新？為什麼？
Topic H: Length and Radii of Airborne Sampling Tubing — 取樣管路設計

原文內容 Original Text

Problem Statement
How should tubing for total particulate sampling be configured?

Recommendation
The sample tubing length and number of bends should be minimized. Bend radii should be maximized in accordance with the recommended practices from the equipment manufacturer and should be technically justified. Tubing with bends should be sized so that the bend radii do not change.

Rationale
Each unique particle size will have a different deposition profile in tubing based on, for example, airflow velocity and sample volume, tubing diameter, sampling port arrangement, temperature, bend radius, tube length, and tubing material. Because it is a range of sizes (e.g., 0.5 microns and greater in optical diameter) that particulate monitoring attempts to assess, no single set of radii or length conditions can describe best practices for each of these particle sizes. Minimizing the number of tubing bends, maximizing radii, and minimizing overall tubing length should be the goal and should be done in accordance with the particle-counter equipment manufacturer's recommended best practices.

The particle-counter equipment manufacturer should provide a technical justification for its recommendations. Instrument calibration should address sampling configuration.

NOTE (EU Annex 1, 2022): Tube length should typically be no longer than 1 m.
NOTE (ISO 14644-1:2015): Tube length should be no longer than 1 m.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

為什麼管路長度和彎曲半徑很重要？

顆粒計數器是通過管路將潔淨室中的空氣樣品吸引到感測器。管路過長或彎曲過多，會造成顆粒在管壁上的靜電沉積（electrostatic deposition）或慣性損失（inertial loss），使到達感測器的顆粒數少於實際環境中的顆粒數，導致監測數據失真（低估污染）。

關鍵設計原則：

  • 管路長度：盡量短，EU Annex 1 和 ISO 14644-1 均建議不超過 1 m
  • 彎曲次數：盡量少（每個彎曲都造成顆粒損失）
  • 彎曲半徑：盡量大（半徑越小，慣性衝擊越嚴重）
  • 等速取樣（Isokinetic sampling）：取樣入口速度應與環境氣流速度匹配，避免過採或漏採

公式與計算 Formula — 顆粒損失機制

主要顆粒損失機制：
1. 慣性撞擊（Inertial Impaction）
   → 顆粒在彎曲處無法跟隨氣流轉向
   → 解決：最大化彎曲半徑 R/D ≥ 5

2. 重力沉降（Gravitational Settling）
   → 大顆粒（>5 μm）在水平管段沉降
   → 解決：最小化水平管路長度

3. 靜電沉積（Electrostatic Deposition）
   → 帶電顆粒吸附管壁
   → 解決：使用靜電消散材料（ESD tubing）

EU Annex 1 / ISO 14644-1 要求：
   管路總長度 ≤ 1 m

比喻說明 Analogy

吸管喝珍珠奶茶：想像用一根很長、彎曲很多的細吸管喝珍珠奶茶——很多珍珠（相當於顆粒）會被卡在彎曲處或沉在管底，真正吸到嘴裡的比杯子裡的少很多。顆粒計數器的取樣管路面臨完全一樣的問題。如果吸管短而直（管路短、少彎曲），幾乎每個珍珠都能順利通過；如果吸管長且彎曲複雜，監測結果就會嚴重低估環境中的實際顆粒數。

重點提示 Key Notes — PDA vs EU Annex 1 差異

PDA PtC-1：管路長度和彎曲應「最小化」，但沒有規定具體的長度數值——強調依據廠商建議和技術論證。
EU Annex 1 (2022) 和 ISO 14644-1：明確建議管路長度「通常不超過 1 m」。
實務影響：若 Grade A 遠端監測（如 RABS 內部取樣點）因設備配置必須使用較長管路，必須有技術論證（例如：廠商提供的顆粒回收率 (particle recovery efficiency) 測試報告），並在 CCS 中記錄。

本節重點回顧 Section Summary — Topics A–H

  • A.1 量測位置：氣流速度量測應在產品暴露的關鍵表面附近進行，距濾網面 15–30 cm 或工作高度，並需與煙霧試驗數據配對使用。量測位置的任何修改均需在 CCS 中論證。
  • A.2 速度數值：0.45 m/s ±20%（PDA）或 0.36–0.54 m/s（EU Annex 1 指引值）均為「建議範圍」而非硬性要求。目標是在科學論據支撐下，達到保護產品的單向流動性。
  • A.3 量測頻率：Grade A/B 每 6 個月，其他區域至少每年一次；出現顆粒數異常或 HVAC 系統變更時需追加量測。
  • B.1 煙霧試驗：必須在動態（in-operation）條件下執行，驗證操作介入與設備運作對氣流的影響；超過 5 年未重做需提供額外論證；結果應連結 APS 設計和 EM 計畫。
  • B.2 影像記錄：影像屬電子數據，須依 GDocP 保存；需多角度拍攝以完整呈現三維氣流；可作為人員訓練素材。
  • C 軋蓋環境：Grade A 氣流供應 + 已驗證的自動瓶塞偵測系統 = 可接受的配置（PDA）。EU Annex 1 更進一步要求在整個軋蓋完成前持續提供 Grade A 保護。
  • D 差壓：不同等級間需維持 ≥10 Pa（通行期望值），需連續監測並設置帶 QRM 支撐的警報策略；差壓反轉必須即時調查並評估產品影響。
  • E HEPA 測試：Grade A/B 每 6 個月、Grade C/D 每 12 個月完整性+速度測試；失效必須評估批次影響並啟動修補或更換程序。
  • F HEPA 修補：Grade A 避免修補（首選更換）；Grade B–D 可修補，但需風險評估 + 符合業界標準規格 + 修補後再測試通過。EU Annex 1 要求修補作業有隔離管制與事後環境監測。
  • G 術語：應使用「單向流（unidirectional airflow）」而非「層流（laminar flow）」，與 EU Annex 1 (2022) 和 ISO 14644 對齊。密閉隔離器中湍流可被接受（需 OQ 支撐）。
  • H 取樣管路：管路長度最小化（EU Annex 1 / ISO 14644-1 建議 ≤1 m）、彎曲最少且半徑最大化；偏離需技術論證，並在儀器校正中涵蓋取樣配置。
