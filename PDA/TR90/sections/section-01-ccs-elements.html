3.0 Elements of a Contamination Control Strategy

原文內容 Original Text

The CCS is a combination of interwoven and successively linked elements, which includes many elements of a pharmaceutical quality system and GMP measures. Figure 3.0-1 illustrates this concept, the elements of which are described in this section.

The foundational elements—scientific knowledge, quality risk management, and personnel awareness/quality culture (dark blue)—inform and influence every other aspect of the CCS. These elements are described in more detail in Section 3.1.1. These foundations of CCS align well with the enablers described in ICH Quality Guideline Q10: Pharmaceutical Quality System.

The individual control elements (red) are designed using the foundational elements, validated to show they can reasonably achieve the appropriate level of control (green), and then monitored to verify they achieve ongoing control (purple). The individual controls in the red row do not comprise an exhaustive list.

The quality systems, such as investigation, change control, and corrective and preventive actions (CAPA)—feedback loops—provide the mechanisms to continuously refine and improve controls and respond to unexpected events.

The governance (light blue) assesses the output of each CCS element (e.g., monitoring data, validation results, investigations, change controls) to ensure the overall CCS remains holistic and effective. A weakness in any of these elements or a mismatch between elements can lead to an ineffective CCS and therefore, contamination.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Contamination Control Strategy, CCS (污染控制策略)：這不是一份單獨的文件，而是一套系統性的整合架構。CCS 將藥品品質系統 (PQS) 與 GMP 措施中與污染控制相關的所有元素串聯起來，形成一個多層次、環環相扣的防護網。

ICH Q10 的連結：TR90 明確指出 CCS 的基礎要素與 ICH Q10 中的「促進因子 (Enablers)」——即知識管理與品質風險管理——完全一致。這表示 CCS 並非獨立於現有品質體系之外的新系統，而是將既有框架聚焦於「污染控制」這個核心目標。

圖表解讀 Figure 3.0-1 Analysis

Figure 3.0-1 是理解整個 TR90 的關鍵圖。它以色彩分層展示 CCS 的五個層級：

  • 深藍色（底層）= 基礎要素：科學知識、QRM、人員意識/品質文化 — 這是一切的根基
  • 紅色 = 個別控制元素：製程設計、設施設計、設備、清潔消毒、環境監控等具體措施
  • 綠色 = 確效 (Validation)：證明每個控制元素確實能達到預期的控制水準
  • 紫色 = 監控 (Monitoring)：持續驗證控制措施在日常運作中仍然有效
  • 淺藍色（頂層）= 治理 (Governance)：從全局角度審視所有 CCS 元素的整體效果

請特別注意：圖中還有回饋迴路 (Feedback Loops)，包含偏差調查、變更管制、CAPA，這些機制確保 CCS 能持續改進而不是一成不變。

比喻說明 Analogy

把 CCS 想像成醫院的感染管控計畫。醫院不是只靠一項措施來防止院內感染——它需要同時具備：醫學知識（基礎）、洗手消毒程序（個別控制）、定期稽核確認程序有效（確效）、持續監測感染率（監控）、以及院長層級的定期檢討會議（治理）。任何一環薄弱，整個防感染體系就會出現漏洞。

重點提示 Key Notes

原文特別強調："A weakness in any of these elements or a mismatch between elements can lead to an ineffective CCS."

這句話有兩層含義：

  • 單一要素的弱點就足以讓整個策略失效（木桶原理——最短的板決定水位）
  • 要素之間的不匹配同樣危險。例如設施設計先進但人員訓練不足，或者監控系統完善但治理審查機制鬆散，都是典型的「mismatch」

3.1 Foundations of a Contamination Control Strategy

原文內容 Original Text

Understanding the foundational elements of scientific knowledge, quality risk management, and personnel awareness/quality culture is crucial to the development of an effective CCS.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

3.1 節明確指出 CCS 的三大基礎支柱。這三項不是「可選的加分項」，而是開發有效 CCS 的前提條件 (prerequisites)。如果缺乏科學知識，你無法識別風險；缺乏 QRM，你無法評估和排序風險；缺乏品質文化，你無法確保措施被確實執行。

3.1.1 Scientific Knowledge Foundational Element

原文內容 Original Text

Both scientific knowledge of the process and technical knowledge of preventing contamination are the foundations of the CCS. They are used to inform the quality risk management (QRM) foundational element of the CCS, including the elements outlined below. These elements should be summarized and/or referenced in the CCS document. When establishing the CCS, knowledge of how to prevent contamination for each step of a manufacturing process should also be considered (references to helpful technical knowledge guidances are included here as well as in Section 14.0):

Process Knowledge

  • Potential ingress points for contaminants to enter the process stream including particulates, microorganisms, viruses/bacteriophages, spores, endotoxins, and other microbial by-products (e.g., exotoxins, proteases, and other metabolites)
  • Potential proliferation points for microorganisms and viruses, including bacteriophages, to grow in the raw materials, solutions, process equipment, and process stream, thereby, allowing formation of undesirable microbial by-products
  • Microbial growth potential identified for each process step by assessing attributes such as pH, temperature, nutrients, water activity, and duration of exposure and/or by performing a profiling study of process matrices such as the antimicrobial effectiveness test
  • Viral proliferation potential identified for each process step considering the presence of viral/bacteriophage cell hosts
  • Process removal or reduction capability for potential contaminants (e.g., filtration, heating, and chromatography)
  • History of contamination events and trends (from microbial, viral, or foreign particulate matter), microbiological and endotoxin concentration, and microbial flora profile for existing processes

Note: The viral aspects noted above are only applicable for processes that use living cell systems.

Technical Knowledge

  • Microbial attributes, behavior, and biofilm development (PDA Technical Report No. 69: Bioburden and Biofilm Management in Pharmaceutical Manufacturing Operations)
  • Aseptic processing (PDA Points to Consider for Aseptic Processing, Parts 1 and 2)
  • Viral attributes, behavior, and host infection (PDA Technical Report No. 83: Virus Contamination in Biomanufacturing: Risk Mitigation, Preparedness and Response)
  • Process design principles (PDA Technical Report No. 41 (rev. 2008): Virus Filtration; Technical Report No. 42: Process Validation of Protein Manufacturing; Technical Report No. 44: Quality Risk Management for Aseptic Processes; Technical Report No. 45: Filtration of Liquids Using Cellulose-Based Depth Filters; Technical Report No. 60: Process Validation: A Lifecycle Approach; Technical Report No. 81: Cell-Based Therapy Control Strategy; PDA Points to Consider for Aseptic Processing, Parts 1 and 2)
  • Facility and utility design principles (ISPE Baseline Guide: Volume 3 – Sterile Product Manufacturing Facilities; PDA Points to Consider for Aging Facilities)
  • Equipment design principles (PDA Technical Report No. 34: Design and Validation of Isolator Systems for the Manufacturing and Testing of Health Care Products; PDA Points to Consider for the Aseptic Processing of Pharmaceutical Products in Isolators)
  • Cleaning, disinfection, decontamination, sanitization, sterilization principles (PDA Technical Report No. 1 (Rev. 2007): Validation of Moist Heat Sterilization Processes: Cycle Design, Development, Qualification and Ongoing Control; Technical Report No. 3 (Rev. 2013): Validation of Dry Heat Processes Used for Depyrogenation and Sterilization; Technical Report No. 26 (Rev. 2008): Sterilizing Filtration of Liquids; Technical Report No. 29 (Rev. 2012): Points to Consider for Cleaning Validation; Technical Report No. 48: Moist Heat Sterilizer Systems: Design, Commissioning, Operation, Qualification and Maintenance; Technical Report No. 49: Points to Consider for Biotechnology Cleaning Validation; Technical Report No. 61: Steam In Place; Technical Report No. 70: Fundamentals of Cleaning and Disinfection Programs for Aseptic Manufacturing Facilities; U.S. Pharmacopeia (USP) General Chapter <1072> Disinfectants and Antiseptics)
  • Raw material quality (ICH Quality Guideline Q7A: Good Manufacturing Practice Guidance for Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients)
  • Quality Risk Management principles (PDA Technical Report No. 54: Implementation of Quality Risk Management for Pharmaceutical and Biotechnology Manufacturing Operations; ICH Quality Guideline Q9: Quality Risk Management)

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Scientific Knowledge (科學知識)：TR90 將科學知識分為兩大類：

  • Process Knowledge（製程知識）：了解你的具體製程在哪裡、如何可能受到污染
  • Technical Knowledge（技術知識）：了解防止污染的通用原理與最佳實務

這兩者缺一不可。只有製程知識而不懂防污染技術，你知道問題在哪但不知道怎麼解決；只有技術知識而不了解製程，你有工具但不知道用在哪裡。

重點提示 Key Notes

Process Knowledge 清單中的六大面向，本質上是在回答三個關鍵問題：

  1. 污染從哪裡進來？（Ingress points — 進入點）
  2. 污染會不會在製程中增殖？（Proliferation points — 增殖點；Microbial growth potential — 微生物生長潛力）
  3. 製程能移除多少污染？（Removal/reduction capability — 移除/降低能力）

    4. 加上歷史數據（contamination events and trends），這就構成了完整的風險圖譜。注意：病毒相關面向僅適用於使用活細胞系統的製程（如生物製劑）。

比喻說明 Analogy

想像你是一位城市防洪工程師。你需要兩類知識：

  • 製程知識 = 了解你的城市：哪些地勢低窪容易積水（ingress points）？哪些區域排水不良會讓水位持續上升（proliferation）？現有的堤防和排水系統能承受多大雨量（removal capability）？過去哪裡曾經淹過水（history）？
  • 技術知識 = 了解防洪工程原理：如何設計堤防、怎麼建造排水系統、泵站的規格該怎麼選——這些是通用的工程知識，不管放到哪個城市都適用。

重點提示 Technical Knowledge 參考文獻整理

TR90 在 Technical Knowledge 部分引用了大量 PDA 技術報告作為支撐。這反映出 CCS 是一個跨領域的綜合策略。以下是按主題分類的參考文獻地圖：

  • 微生物學基礎：TR69（生物負荷與生物膜管理）
  • 無菌製程：PDA Points to Consider（無菌充填 Parts 1 & 2）
  • 病毒控制：TR83（病毒污染）、TR41（病毒過濾）
  • 製程設計與確效：TR42, TR44, TR60, TR81
  • 設施/設備設計：ISPE Baseline Guide Vol.3、TR34
  • 清潔與滅菌：TR1, TR3, TR26, TR29, TR48, TR49, TR61, TR70, USP <1072>
  • 原物料品質：ICH Q7A
  • 風險管理：TR54, ICH Q9

對 CDMO 而言，這意味著建立 CCS 團隊時，需要包含微生物學、工程、品保、製程開發等多領域專家。

實務應用 Practical Application

在 CDMO 環境中，你可能同時服務多個客戶的不同產品。每個產品的 Process Knowledge 都不同——有的是小分子無菌充填、有的是細胞治療產品、有的是生物製劑。但 Technical Knowledge 有很大部分是共通的。

實務建議：建立一個跨產品的「技術知識資料庫」（引用 TR90 列出的所有參考文獻），然後針對每個產品/客戶開發專屬的 Process Knowledge 評估。這樣既能確保知識的完整性，又能提高效率。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 針對你最熟悉的一個製程，試著列出至少三個 ingress points 和兩個 proliferation points。
  2. 為什麼 TR90 要求將 contamination events history 納入科學知識？這對一個全新產品（沒有歷史數據）的 CCS 有什麼影響？
  3. 在生物製劑 (biologics) 與小分子藥物 (small molecules) 的 CCS 中，Process Knowledge 的評估重點有何不同？
3.1.2 Quality Risk Management Foundational Element

原文內容 Original Text

To achieve contamination control, the design of new and existing manufacturing processes and facilities should employ QRM principles to identify and assess risk with the intent to prevent and control contamination.

The CCS should be designed with redundant individual control elements so that no single failure in one element will result in contamination. If any contamination risks are not well-controlled through process design or engineered into the facility design, this will result in other CCS elements (e.g., procedural controls) being built up to compensate, thereby increasing the overall cost and complexity of manufacturing throughout the life of the product.

For example, a process step that is designed to be manual and open (exposed directly to the environment) requires all the surrounding CCS elements to be more robust than if the process were closed, including additional technical and procedural measures (e.g., local unidirectional flow protection, personnel and material movement controls, environmental controls such as cleaning, disinfection, environmental monitoring, in-process testing of that step, and validation).

For new processes, existing contamination control technologies and procedures that have proven effective in processes with similar risk profiles can be used as a model (e.g., connections, sanitization or sterilization practices, sampling methods). New contamination control technologies and procedures that are not yet proven effective in a relevant CCS, should be designed and assessed using QRM principles to reduce the risk of contamination.

New construction or renovation of production areas are ideal opportunities for innovation. This should involve an evaluation of the existing CCS for any ways to improve contamination control through innovative technologies, such as closed or barrier systems, automation, and advanced monitoring and detection methods.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Quality Risk Management, QRM (品質風險管理)：QRM 在 CCS 中的角色是作為「決策引擎」。所有的控制措施設計都應該基於風險評估的結果來決定——在哪裡投入更多資源、在哪裡可以適度簡化。

Redundant Control Elements（冗餘控制元素）：這是一個關鍵原則——CCS 必須設計成沒有任何單一失效點 (single point of failure)。當一個控制元素失效時，其他元素仍能防止污染發生。這就像飛機有多套獨立的液壓系統一樣。

比喻說明 Analogy

把「開放式 vs. 封閉式製程」的控制需求想像成在不同環境中保護一盤壽司：

  • 封閉式製程 = 壽司放在密封的保鮮盒裡：只需要基本的溫度控制就能安全保存
  • 開放式製程 = 壽司放在露天攤位上：你需要防蟲網、防塵罩、溫度監控、定時更換、人員衛生管理、周圍環境清潔......每一項額外措施都是成本

TR90 的訊息很明確：在設計階段就選擇較高的工程控制水準（如封閉系統），可以大幅降低整個產品生命週期的營運複雜度和成本。

重點提示 Key Notes

原文中有一段非常重要的經濟學邏輯：

"If any contamination risks are not well-controlled through process design or engineered into the facility design, this will result in other CCS elements (e.g., procedural controls) being built up to compensate, thereby increasing the overall cost and complexity of manufacturing throughout the life of the product."

這段話的意涵是：前期在設計上省的錢，後期會以數倍的營運成本還回來。一個設計不良的設施，終其一生都需要更多的人員、更複雜的程序、更頻繁的監控來彌補設計缺陷。這對 CDMO 的設施投資決策有重大影響。

重點提示 Innovation Opportunity

TR90 特別指出新建或翻新是導入創新的最佳時機。具體包括：

  • Closed or barrier systems（封閉或屏障系統）：如隔離器 (Isolator)、RABS
  • Automation（自動化）：減少人員介入，降低最大的污染源——人
  • Advanced monitoring and detection（先進監控與偵測技術）：如即時微生物偵測、連續粒子監測

如果你的工廠正在規劃擴建或翻修，這正是重新審視 CCS 並進行升級的黃金機會。

實務應用 Practical Application

CDMO 情境：假設一位客戶要求你們代工一個需要在開放環境下進行手動操作的無菌充填步驟。根據 TR90 的 QRM 原則，你需要：

  1. 評估該開放步驟帶來的額外風險
  2. 設計冗餘控制措施（單向氣流保護、人員動線管控、更嚴格的環境監控）
  3. 計算這些額外措施的長期成本
  4. 向客戶提議是否可改用封閉系統設計來降低整體成本

這是 CDMO 展現技術領導力並為客戶創造價值的機會。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 在你的製造環境中，有哪些步驟目前是「開放式」的？如果改為「封閉式」，需要哪些投資？可以省去哪些日常營運成本？
  2. 什麼是「冗餘控制」？試舉一個你工作中的實際例子，說明當某一控制失效時，哪個備援控制會接手。
  3. 為什麼 TR90 建議新的污染控制技術需要經過 QRM 評估才能導入？直接採用最新技術不好嗎？
3.1.3 Personnel Awareness and Quality Culture Foundational Element

原文內容 Original Text

The health of the quality culture and level of personnel awareness around contamination controls have a direct and significant impact on the success of the CCS.

Contamination control should be a priority that is reflected in the goals of the firm. A dedicated champion or team should oversee the performance of the CCS (see Section 11.7 Governance and Oversight).

The hallmarks of a robust quality culture that promotes contamination control are:

  • Focus on preventing contamination and other quality hazards
  • Willingness to proactively invest time and resources to improve quality and prevent contamination
  • Strong response to contamination events that corrects true root cause(s) and prevents reoccurrence
  • Management that appreciates and values the contributions and skills of employees
  • Strong, supportive community of practices and network of subject matter experts (SMEs) who focus on continuous improvement—good cross-department and cross-site communication and application of best practices and lessons learned
  • Employees who feel ownership over the quality of the product and process, including contamination prevention
  • Employees who understand their responsibility to highlight quality risks and work toward solutions
  • Non-blaming culture

Firms should maintain an ongoing campaign to promote contamination control awareness among all employees engaged in GMP activities, including shop floor and management staff. One-time or annual training does not ensure ongoing awareness.

Shop floor personnel are the true guardians of contamination control because they directly perform processes and operate equipment according to procedures to deliver contaminant-free process intermediates and products.

Personnel awareness is achieved through a combination of training, education, and ongoing diligence. Training addresses what a task is and how it is done; education addresses why it is done.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Quality Culture (品質文化)：品質文化不是掛在牆上的標語，而是組織中每個人在面對壓力、衝突、截止日期時，仍然會優先考慮品質和安全的集體行為模式。TR90 將其列為 CCS 的基礎要素，表明再好的技術控制措施，如果沒有正確的文化支撐，都可能流於形式。

Training vs. Education (訓練 vs. 教育)：這是一個經常被忽略的重要區分：

  • Training（訓練）：教人「做什麼」和「怎麼做」— 操作程序、SOP 執行
  • Education（教育）：教人「為什麼這樣做」— 背後的科學原理和風險邏輯

只有訓練沒有教育的人，在遇到 SOP 未涵蓋的異常狀況時，無法做出正確判斷。

比喻說明 Analogy

品質文化就像一個家庭的「家風」。你可以制定再多的家規（SOP），但如果家庭成員的核心價值觀不一致，規則就不會被認真遵守。

TR90 列出的品質文化特徵中，「Non-blaming culture（非責怪文化）」特別值得關注。這就像航空業的安全報告文化——如果飛行員因為報告險情而受到懲罰，以後就沒有人敢報告了，而問題會在暗處累積直到發生重大事故。

同理，如果操作人員因為主動報告異常而被責備，他們下次就會選擇沉默，而污染風險就此被隱藏起來。

重點提示 Key Notes

TR90 有兩個特別強烈的聲明值得銘記：

  1. "One-time or annual training does not ensure ongoing awareness." — 一次性或年度訓練不足以維持持續的意識。這對許多公司的訓練體系是一個挑戰，因為大多數訓練計畫都是按年度週期設計的。
  2. "Shop floor personnel are the true guardians of contamination control." — 第一線操作人員才是污染控制的真正守護者。這句話翻轉了許多組織中「品質是品保部門的責任」的錯誤觀念。

圖表解讀 Table 3.1.3-1 Analysis

Table 3.1.3-1 定義了三個層級的 CCS 角色：

  • Leadership（領導層）：負責「定方向」— 將污染預防列為組織優先事項，推動非責怪文化，確保人員配置得當
  • Expert（專家層）：負責「看全局」— 監督控制措施的表現，提升污染危害，提供技術領導以持續改進
  • Shop Floor（現場操作層）：負責「做實事」— 實際執行污染控制、報告問題、提供對個別控制元素的技術專業意見

注意：所有三個層級都有「Escalates contamination hazards（提升污染危害的通報）」的職責。這表示通報責任不分層級——從操作員到專家都有義務提出警示。

實務應用 Practical Application

CDMO 挑戰：CDMO 的品質文化面臨獨特的挑戰——人員流動率可能較高、同一設施可能同時服務多個客戶的不同產品、操作人員需要頻繁切換製程。

建議做法：

  • 建立一個跨產品的「污染控制意識推廣計畫」，而非僅依賴產品別的訓練
  • 每季而非每年進行污染控制的持續教育（mini-education sessions），搭配實際案例分享
  • 設立「CCS Champion」或專責團隊，如 TR90 建議的，定期審視整體 CCS 表現
  • 建立匿名的「Near-miss」報告系統，強化非責怪文化

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 你的組織中，品質文化的八項特徵中，最強和最弱的各是哪一項？為什麼？
  2. 「Training」和「Education」在你的現有訓練計畫中各佔多少比重？如何增加「Education」的成分？
  3. 如果你是一位現場操作主管，你會如何在不增加太多額外時間負擔的情況下，維持團隊的持續污染控制意識？
  4. Table 3.1.3-1 中，為什麼所有三個角色都包含「Escalates contamination hazards」？這反映了什麼樣的組織設計理念？

本節重點回顧 Section Summary

  • CCS 是多層架構：由基礎要素（科學知識、QRM、品質文化）、個別控制元素、確效、監控、治理五個層級組成，並透過回饋迴路（調查、變更管制、CAPA）持續改進
  • 科學知識是雙面的：既需要了解你自己的製程特性（Process Knowledge），也需要掌握防污染的通用技術原理（Technical Knowledge）
  • QRM 驅動設計決策：風險管理原則指導控制措施的設計，核心概念是「冗餘控制」——不允許單一失效點。在設計階段的工程投資可大幅降低長期營運成本
  • 人是最關鍵的因素：品質文化決定了所有技術措施能否被確實執行。非責怪文化、持續教育（而非僅訓練）、以及賦予現場人員「守護者」角色，是 CCS 成功的關鍵軟實力
  • CCS 的三大基礎要素與 ICH Q10 的促進因子一致：這表明 CCS 不是獨立的新體系，而是聚焦於污染控制的品質管理系統應用
