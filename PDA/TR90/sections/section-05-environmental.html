7.0 Environmental Control, Validation, and Monitoring 7.0 環境管制、驗證與環境監測

原文內容 Original Text

Environmental control is a fundamental part of the CCS because contaminants from the environment can enter the utilities, raw materials, equipment, and the process stream from the air and through surface transfer. A spike in environmental contaminants (e.g., seasonal variations) can challenge the other elements of the CCS. For instance, molds and spore-forming bacteria are in high concentrations outdoors, and a spike in these organisms in the cleanroom can challenge the facility cleaning/disinfection and equipment sanitization/sterilization programs (spore-forming bacteria are still the most common bioreactor contaminants). Increased skin bacteria from the operators in the environment can challenge the aseptic processing controls; skin bacteria are still the most common contaminants found in products intended to be sterile.

Environmental monitoring (EM) programs do not prevent or control contamination, but they provide an indicator of environmental quality through multiple discrete snapshots in time. The main purpose of the EM program is to measure the performance of the contamination controls related to the environment and to highlight the potential loss of control due to a variety of causes, such as personnel behavior, disinfection practices, HVAC equipment malfunction, and facility age. An ongoing generation of EM trends can be used for remediation to ensure that the facility continues to operate in a state of control and to prevent extrinsic contamination of the product. The effectiveness of an EM program is dependent on the proper identification of critical control points (CCPs) as part of a risk assessment (e.g., HACCP).

"An EM program with acceptable trends should not be used to support poor aseptic or disinfection practices, for example, to defend a decrease in disinfection frequencies or the elimination of sporicidal agents."

Section 15.0, Table 9 and Table 10 provide additional points to consider regarding EM and testing controls. PDA Technical Report No. 13 (Revised 2022): Fundamentals of an Environmental Monitoring Program also provides extensive guidance on establishing an effective EM program for facilities, critical utilities, and personnel.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Environmental Control (環境控制)：CCS 的根基之一。環境中的污染物可透過空氣傳播 (airborne) 或表面轉移 (surface transfer) 進入公用設施、原料、設備及製程中。季節性變化（如春夏黴菌高峰）會直接挑戰其他 CCS 要素。

Environmental Monitoring, EM (環境監測)：EM 本身不具備「預防」或「控制」污染的功能，它只是提供環境品質的離散快照 (discrete snapshots)。其核心價值在於量測污染控制措施的「績效」，並在失控前發出預警。

Critical Control Points, CCPs (關鍵管控點)：EM 計畫的有效性取決於 CCPs 的正確辨識。CCPs 通常透過 HACCP 風險評估來確定。

比喻說明 Analogy

環境監測就像醫院的「感染管控巡查」——巡查本身不會殺死病菌，但能告訴你哪裡的消毒不夠徹底、哪個病房的空氣清淨度下降了。如果巡查結果一直合格，也不代表可以減少打掃次數或不再使用漂白水。同樣地，EM 趨勢良好絕不能作為降低消毒頻率或取消殺孢子劑的藉口。

重點提示 Key Notes

兩大常見污染源：

  • 產孢菌 (Spore-forming bacteria)：仍是生物反應器 (bioreactor) 最常見的污染物。戶外高濃度的黴菌與產孢菌在季節高峰期可滲入潔淨室，挑戰清潔/消毒與設備滅菌計畫。
  • 皮膚菌 (Skin bacteria)：仍是無菌產品中最常見的污染物。操作人員是潔淨室中最大的活性污染源。

這兩個事實強調了 CCS 必須同時關注「環境面」（空氣、表面）與「人員面」（著裝、行為）的控制。

實務應用 Practical Application

在 CDMO 的實務中，客戶稽核員常會檢視 EM 趨勢數據，並詢問：「當 EM 數據連續多月合格後，你們是否有調降消毒頻率？」正確的回答是：良好的 EM 趨勢是控制有效的證明，不應被用來辯護降低控制標準。反之，應利用趨勢數據來持續優化取樣策略和識別新的風險區域。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 為什麼 EM 計畫被定義為「不預防也不控制污染」？它在 CCS 中的真正角色是什麼？
  2. 季節性變化如何影響潔淨室的 CCS？請舉出至少兩個具體的風險情境。
  3. 如果某潔淨室的 EM 數據連續12個月零偵測 (zero counts)，這代表什麼？是否可以減少監測頻率？

7.1 Environmental Performance Qualification 7.1 環境效能確效

原文內容 Original Text

Environmental monitoring performance qualification (EMPQ) is performed to provide documented evidence that the facility design and environmental controls can act together to consistently achieve the desired level of contamination control for the intended manufacturing use. The EMPQ evaluates the CCS environmental control elements including facility and HVAC design, access and flow, cleaning/disinfection, gowning, material transfer, and pest control.

EMPQ is conducted in newly constructed controlled classified areas or after significant changes in existing facilities, that is, changes that could affect the contamination control performance of the environment. The EMPQ sample locations (including CCPs) should be selected based on a risk assessment using guidance from ISO 14644-1:2015 Cleanrooms and Associated Controlled Environments – Part 1: Classification of Air Cleanliness by Particle Concentration. Prior to the EMPQ, the new area should be fully functional with equipment installed and all relevant CCS programs established and underway.

The cleanroom installation and operation qualification are outlined in ISO 14644 and include airflow visualization (smoke) studies, which should be performed under static and dynamic (worst-case or process-relevant) conditions. The data generated from the execution of the EMPQ supports the establishment of the ongoing EM program, which includes the selection of routine sampling locations. For additional information on EMPQ design and site selection, refer to PDA Technical Report No. 13 (Revised 2022): Fundamentals of an Environmental Monitoring Program.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

EMPQ (環境監測性能確效)：用文件化證據證明設施設計與環境控制措施能共同且持續地達成目標污染控制水準。它不是單一測試，而是對整個 CCS 環境控制要素的綜合評估，涵蓋：

  • 設施與 HVAC 設計
  • 人員與物料動線 (Access and flow)
  • 清潔/消毒程序
  • 著裝程序 (Gowning)
  • 物料轉移 (Material transfer)
  • 害蟲防治 (Pest control)

ISO 14644-1:2015：潔淨室分級的國際標準，用於指導 EMPQ 取樣點的選擇。

比喻說明 Analogy

EMPQ 就像新餐廳開業前的衛生檢查。你不能等到客人已經用餐後才來驗證廚房是否乾淨——必須在開業前（也就是 GMP 生產啟動前），確認所有設備就位、通風系統運作正常、清潔流程已執行且有效、員工已受過衛生訓練。只有通過這個「開業前大檢」，才能獲得營業許可（進入常規生產階段）。

重點提示 Key Notes

執行時機要點：

  • 新建區域：必須在所有設備安裝完成、CCS 程序全部建立並啟動後才執行 EMPQ
  • 既有設施變更：僅在「可能影響環境污染控制性能」的重大變更後才需要
  • 氣流可視化 (Smoke Study)：必須在靜態 (static) 和動態 (dynamic/worst-case) 兩種條件下執行

EMPQ 產出的數據直接支撐後續常規 EM 計畫的取樣點選擇，因此 EMPQ 的品質決定了日常監測的有效性。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. EMPQ 與日常 EM 計畫之間的關係是什麼？為什麼說 EMPQ 的品質決定了 EM 的有效性？
  2. 為什麼氣流可視化研究必須在靜態和動態兩種條件下都執行？只做靜態有什麼風險？

7.1.1 Environmental Requalification 7.1.1 環境再確效

原文內容 Original Text

Requalification is performed after controlled classified areas have been modified in ways that involve changes to structure, process flow, or materials of construction. In addition, the area may be requalified after a significant change has occurred, such as a change in the operational use of the area or other contamination control programs (e.g., EM program, sanitization program, gowning). Periodic requalification studies may be performed based on risk assessments, site validation procedures, or changes in the ongoing trend data or as required by local regulations (e.g., current version of Annex 1 recommends requalification of Grade A & B every 6 months and Grade C & D every 12 months.)

The change control for the affected rooms should assess cumulative changes in the rooms that may warrant requalification. An EMPQ study protocol should include the scope and requirements of the requalification, sampling plan, and acceptance criteria.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Requalification (再確效)：不是「重做一次 EMPQ」，而是針對特定變更所做的確認性研究。觸發條件包括：

  • 結構性變更（牆壁、天花板、地板更換）
  • 製程流程改變
  • 建材變更 (materials of construction)
  • 區域用途改變（如從 Grade C 包裝區改為 Grade B 配液區）
  • CCS 程序變更（消毒計畫、著裝程序等）
  • 趨勢數據出現變化

比喻說明 Analogy

再確效就像汽車的定期保養檢測。即使你的車開起來好好的，每半年或一萬公里還是要回廠檢查。如果你換了引擎零件（重大變更），就要立刻回廠驗車；如果只是換了雨刷（小變更），可能不用，但如果你同時換了雨刷、胎壓感測器、又調了方向盤角度，這些累積起來就該整體檢查一次了。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 如果一個 Grade B 區域在過去6個月內進行了三次小型維修（更換一塊天花板板材、調整回風口位置、更換一盞 UV 燈），是否需要再確效？你會如何評估？
  2. 為什麼 Annex 1 對 Grade A/B 的再確效週期（6個月）比 Grade C/D（12個月）更頻繁？風險的差異在哪裡？

重點提示 Key Notes

EU GMP Annex 1 的週期性再確效要求：

潔淨等級再確效週期
Grade A & B每 6 個月
Grade C & D每 12 個月

這是 Annex 1 的明確建議。值得注意的是，Change Control 程序需要評估「累積性變更」——也就是說，單獨看每一個小變更可能不需要再確效，但多個小變更累積起來的效應可能需要。

7.1.2 Environmental Monitoring Critical Parameters 7.1.2 環境監測關鍵參數

原文內容 Original Text

The HVAC system and associated air handling units control the cleanliness of the air and provide an environment that is suitable for operators to work safely and comfortably. Procedures should be in place to monitor and trend critical parameters to assess whether the cleanroom is performing as intended. The procedures should also include instructions on how to handle excursions and alarms. Critical parameters are outlined in Sections 7.1.2.1–7.1.2.3.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

HVAC System (暖通空調系統)：潔淨室的「生命維持系統」。它不只控制空氣清潔度，還要同時為操作人員提供安全、舒適的工作環境。三大關鍵參數（即後續小節說明的氣流模式、壓差、溫濕度）都由 HVAC 系統管控。

Excursions (偏移)：當關鍵參數超出設定限度時的事件。程序中必須包含偏移處理與警報應對的明確指引。

7.1.2.1 Air Patterns, Air Movement, and Air Changes 7.1.2.1 氣流型態、氣流流動與換氣次數

原文內容 Original Text

Airflow patterns in critical zones and the background environment need to be studied during qualification to show that air turbulence does not interfere with aseptic processes during static or dynamic conditions. There are two types of air flow patterns: turbulent flow and unidirectional flow.

Turbulent airflow is used in most classified areas other than Grade A environments; it is designed to dilute particles and microorganisms in cleanrooms and keep them suspended in the air until they are removed with new "clean" air. The number of air changes per hour of a cleanroom is related to the extent to which potential contaminants can be removed from the room. Each grade of cleanroom should meet a preset number of air changes per hour based on a risk assessment performed as part of the EMPQ. Airflow visualization studies may be conducted in areas of turbulent airflow such as cleanrooms to optimize the layout, use and monitoring locations.

Unidirectional airflow is applied in higher classified areas (i.e., Grade A) and is designed to sweep particles and microorganisms away from critical operations, ensuring that the "first air" is never contaminated before it comes in contact with the product (the critical zone). Airflow visualization studies are designed to demonstrate that unidirectional airflow is delivered to the working position by the BSC or barrier system, and that the product is only exposed to "first air" during operations. Airflow visualization studies are also performed in areas that adjacent to the critical zone to ensure air is not moving into the aseptic area, e.g., air entering through the mouseholes or depyrogenation tunnel into a barrier system, or air passing through the air curtain of a BSC. PDA Technical Report No. 62: Recommended Practices for Manual Aseptic Processes and EU GMP Annex 1: Manufacture of Sterile Medicinal Products also discuss airflow in aseptic manufacturing areas.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Turbulent Airflow (亂流氣流)：用於 Grade B/C/D 等大部分潔淨區域。原理是透過大量換氣「稀釋」空氣中的微粒和微生物，使其懸浮後隨新鮮空氣排出。每小時換氣次數 (ACH, Air Changes per Hour) 越高，稀釋能力越強。

Unidirectional Airflow (單向氣流)：用於 Grade A 關鍵區域。原理完全不同——不是稀釋，而是「掃除」。空氣以均勻、平行的方式流動，將微粒和微生物從關鍵操作區域「吹走」，確保產品只接觸到「第一氣流」(First Air)。

First Air (第一氣流)：指從 HEPA 過濾器出來後、尚未接觸任何物體或人員的潔淨空氣。產品在關鍵操作期間只能暴露於 first air，這是無菌保證的核心原則之一。

比喻說明 Analogy

想像兩種清潔地板的方式：

  • 亂流 = 用水沖洗浴室：大量的水沖進去，髒東西被水流帶動、稀釋，最後從排水孔流走。水量越大，沖得越乾淨。
  • 單向流 = 用掃把從一端掃到另一端：所有灰塵都被有方向性地推到一邊去，不會來回擾動。你站在掃把前方（first air 的位置），永遠不會碰到灰塵。

Mouseholes（傳送窗口）就像門縫——如果沒有正確的氣流方向，外面走廊的灰塵就會從門縫鑽進手術室。

重點提示 Key Notes

氣流可視化 (Smoke Study) 的關鍵檢查點：

  • Grade A 區域：確認單向氣流確實送達工作位置，產品只暴露於 first air
  • Grade A 鄰接區域：確認無外部空氣侵入（如 mouseholes、去熱原隧道、BSC 的氣簾）
  • 亂流區域：優化佈局與監測點位置
  • 必須在靜態與動態兩種條件下執行

練習思考 Practice Questions

  2. 如果 smoke study 顯示充填區有一個小區域存在渦流 (vortex)，這代表什麼風險？應如何處理？

7.1.2.2 Differential Air Pressure 7.1.2.2 壓差

原文內容 Original Text

Air pressurization will ensure airflow from a higher to a lower cleanliness level by maintaining positive pressure in the areas of higher classification. Air visualization performed in both static and dynamic conditions will demonstrate appropriate pressure differential. In viral-vector manufacturing, guidance on differential pressure between rooms of different classification can be found in U.S. FDA Guidance for Industry: Sterile Drug Products Produced by Aseptic Processing — Current Good Manufacturing Practice, EU GMP Annex 1 Manufacture of Sterile Medicinal Products, and PDA Technical Report No. 13 (Revised 2021): Fundamentals of an Environmental Monitoring Program.

In the case of high-potency products, both active egress from the area and contamination of the product should be prevented. This can be achieved by applying air locks with different pressures between areas of different classification. Pressure differentials should be monitored with an alarm mechanism for minimum and maximum pressure limits.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Pressure Differential (壓差)：透過維持較高等級區域的正壓，確保空氣永遠從較潔淨的區域流向較不潔淨的區域。這是防止外部污染物侵入的物理屏障。

High-Potency Products (高效能/高活性產品)：面臨雙重挑戰——既要防止外部污染進入產品（正壓保護），又要防止高效能物質擴散到外部環境（負壓圍堵）。解決方案是使用具有不同壓力的 Air Locks (氣閘) 在不同分類區域之間建立壓力梯度。

比喻說明 Analogy

壓差就像「風向」的人為控制。想像一棟大樓的每一層都有不同氣壓：頂樓氣壓最高，每往下一層就低一點。這樣空氣永遠是從上往下流，灰塵不可能逆流上去。潔淨室的壓差原理完全一樣——最乾淨的房間壓力最高，「空氣門」永遠朝外推。

高效能產品的情況更像生物安全實驗室 (BSL)：你既不想讓外面的細菌進來污染你的實驗，也不想讓裡面的危險病原跑出去。這時就需要「三明治」結構的氣閘——中間是緩衝區，分別用不同壓力來控制兩個方向的氣流。

重點提示 Key Notes

壓差監測必須配備警報機制：需設定最低和最高壓力限值，當壓差偏離時立即觸發警報。這不是建議，而是基本要求。壓差的喪失意味著污染屏障的瓦解，可能導致整批產品報廢。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 如果你負責設計一個同時生產高效能抗癌藥物的無菌充填區，壓差策略應該如何規劃？畫出各區域的壓力梯度圖。
  2. 為什麼壓差監測需要同時設定「最低」和「最高」限值？過高的壓差會造成什麼問題？

7.1.2.3 Temperature and Relative Humidity 7.1.2.3 溫度與相對濕度

原文內容 Original Text

Products may be affected by temperature and humidity, and these parameters are important for operator comfort as well. Shivering or excess perspiration on the part of an operator may result in the release of a higher number of particles into the cleanroom than an operator's garments can retain, which may contaminate the environment. Temperature and humidity levels also have an impact on microbial proliferation. Both parameters should be monitored against their set limits.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

溫濕度的雙重角色：溫度和相對濕度影響兩個面向：

  • 產品面：某些產品（如生物製劑、蛋白質藥物）對溫濕度敏感
  • 人員面：操作人員太冷會發抖、太熱會流汗，兩者都會導致釋放更多微粒到潔淨室中，超出無塵衣的攔截能力
  • 微生物面：高溫高濕有利於微生物增殖

比喻說明 Analogy

溫濕度控制就像手術室的溫度設定——外科醫生穿著厚重的手術衣和手套，如果室溫太高會大量出汗，手可能打滑；如果太冷會手指僵硬，影響精細操作。無菌操作的道理完全一樣：操作人員的舒適度直接影響他們作為「污染源」的風險等級。一個流汗的操作員產生的微粒量可能是正常狀態的數倍。

重點提示 Key Notes

溫濕度往往被視為「次要參數」而被忽略，但本節明確指出它對污染控制有直接影響。特別注意：操作人員著裝後（穿上無塵衣、戴手套、護目鏡），體感溫度會比環境溫度高出許多。因此潔淨室的溫度設定不能只考慮產品需求，還必須考慮穿著完整無塵衣後的人體舒適範圍。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  2. 在撰寫 CCS 文件時，溫濕度監測應歸類在哪個控制要素下？應如何設定行動限值 (action limits)？

7.2 Environment and Utility Disruption and Recovery Plan 7.2 環境與公用系統中斷及復原計畫

原文內容 Original Text

When a disruption occurs in a controlled, classified environment or to a critical utility, the risk of contamination is inherently higher than during daily operations. For this reason, a containment strategy for disruptions and a recovery plan should be established with the aim of reducing the contamination risk.

A disruption to the controlled, classified environment or critical utility may be planned or unplanned:

  • Planned disruptions are deliberate, scheduled events, for example, construction projects, annual shutdown, preventive maintenance, equipment installation
  • Unplanned disruptions are unexpected events, such as leaks in critical utilities, loss of power, or equipment repair, and should be addressed immediately

The level of containment and recovery should be commensurate with the risk. It is useful to define tiers of risk and outline the appropriate containment strategy and response plan for each tier. An example is presented in Section 17.0–Appendix III.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Disruption (中斷事件)：指潔淨環境或關鍵公用設施（如注射用水系統 WFI、純蒸汽、壓縮空氣、HVAC）發生的任何干擾，可分為：

  • Planned (計畫性)：建築工程、年度停機保養、預防性維護、設備安裝等已排程的事件
  • Unplanned (非計畫性)：公用設施洩漏、斷電、緊急設備維修等突發事件，必須立即處理

Recovery Plan (復原計畫)：定義從中斷到恢復「受控狀態」所需的步驟。圍堵 (Containment) 與復原的力度應與風險相稱 (commensurate with risk)。

Tiers of Risk (風險分級)：將不同類型和嚴重程度的中斷事件分級，每一級對應不同的圍堵策略與應變計畫。詳見 Section 17.0 Appendix III 的範例。

比喻說明 Analogy

中斷與復原計畫就像醫院的緊急應變分級制度：

  • 計畫性中斷 = 排程手術：提前準備好所有資源、排好時程、通知所有相關人員，事後有計畫地清潔和復原。
  • 非計畫性中斷 = 緊急送醫：沒有預警，必須立即啟動應急程序，邊處理邊評估風險。

風險分級就像醫院的「檢傷分類」(triage)——不是每個中斷事件都需要全面重新確效，輕微的可能只需加強監測，嚴重的可能需要停線、清潔、再確效後才能復產。

重點提示 Key Notes

「與風險相稱」(Commensurate with Risk) 是關鍵原則：

  • 過度反應（每次小中斷都全面停線重新確效）= 浪費資源、降低產能
  • 反應不足（嚴重中斷只做最低限度處理）= 污染風險，可能導致產品召回
  • 正確做法是預先建立風險分級表 (risk tier matrix)，讓所有人員在事件發生時能快速、一致地做出判斷

Annex 1 特別強調，計畫性中斷也必須有正式的控制程序——不能因為「是排程好的」就放鬆管控。

實務應用 Practical Application

作為 CDMO，你的設施可能同時服務多個客戶。當發生 HVAC 異常（如 AHU 故障導致 Grade B 區域壓差喪失 30 分鐘）時，你需要：

  1. 依據預定的風險分級表判斷此事件屬於哪一級
  2. 啟動對應級別的圍堵策略（如隔離受影響區域、暫停操作）
  3. 執行復原程序（如額外清潔消毒、加強 EM 取樣）
  4. 評估對所有在製批次的影響，並通知各客戶
  5. 完成偏差調查、記錄根因並更新 CAPA

沒有預建風險分級表的工廠，每次都會陷入「臨時判斷」的混亂，不同班次的處理方式可能完全不一致。

  2. 計畫性中斷（如年度停機保養）和非計畫性中斷（如突然斷電），在 CCS 文件中應分別如何記載？復原要求有何不同？

本節重點回顧 Section Summary

  • 環境控制是 CCS 的基礎：環境污染物（季節性黴菌、產孢菌、人員皮膚菌）可透過空氣和表面傳播，直接挑戰設施清潔、設備滅菌和無菌操作控制
  • EM 是「測量」而非「控制」：環境監測計畫提供環境品質的離散快照，用於量測控制措施績效並預警失控，但絕不能用良好的 EM 趨勢來辯護降低控制標準
  • EMPQ 是 EM 的基石：在新建或重大變更後的潔淨區域執行 EMPQ，其結果直接決定日常 EM 計畫的取樣策略和監測點位
  • 再確效有明確觸發條件：結構/製程/建材變更、用途變更、CCS 程序變更、趨勢變化，以及 Annex 1 要求的週期性再確效（A/B 級每6個月、C/D 級每12個月）
  • 三大 HVAC 關鍵參數：氣流模式（亂流稀釋 vs. 單向流掃除）、壓差（正壓防護+高效能產品的雙向控制）、溫濕度（產品穩定性+操作人員舒適度+微生物增殖）
  • 中斷與復原必須預先規劃：區分計畫性與非計畫性中斷，建立風險分級表，圍堵與復原力度與風險相稱，避免過度反應或反應不足
