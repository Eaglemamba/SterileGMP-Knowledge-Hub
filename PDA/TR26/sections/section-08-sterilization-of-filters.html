8.0 Sterilization of Filters 濾膜滅菌（總論）

原文內容 Original Text

A key element of a successful sterilizing filtration process is the sterility of the filtration assembly, especially on the filtrate side. Proper sterilization of filters involves applying the right physicochemical conditions, and with careful attention, this process can be highly effective while preserving the integrity of the filter membranes and components. By following the manufacturer's recommendations for sterilization, the risk of thermal, mechanical, or chemical damage to the filters can be minimized. The sterilization process should be appropriately qualified to ensure that there is no variation during the cycle and that it achieves the respective sterility goal and does not cause harm to the filtration device. In instances when the sterilization process is not automated, a routine integrity test of the filter after sterilization may be performed. An integrity test after the sterilization process can confirm that the filter was not damaged during sterilization.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Filtration Assembly Sterility（過濾組件無菌性）：除菌過濾不僅要求濾膜本身具備截留細菌的能力，整個過濾組件（包括殼體、管路、接頭）都必須處於無菌狀態。特別強調的是 filtrate side（濾液側），因為這是產品已通過除菌濾膜後的「乾淨面」，任何污染都直接影響最終產品的無菌保證。

Sterility Goal（滅菌目標）：指滅菌製程需要達成的微生物殺滅水準。對於蒸汽滅菌，通常要達到 10-6 SAL (Sterility Assurance Level)，即一百萬個滅菌單元中僅容許一個不合格。

比喻說明 Analogy

想像你在做手術前，不只要消毒手術刀，還要消毒整個手術台面、器械盤、和所有接觸到傷口的器具。除菌過濾也一樣 — 光是濾膜無菌不夠，整個「濾液側管路」都要滅菌乾淨，就像手術室的無菌區域概念。如果你只消毒了手術刀卻忘了消毒器械盤，病人依然會感染。

重點提示 Key Notes

本節開宗明義點出了一個核心矛盾：滅菌必須徹底有效，但又不能損傷濾膜。這就是為什麼必須遵循製造商的建議 — 溫度、壓力、時間的組合都需要在「殺滅微生物」和「保護濾膜材料」之間取得平衡。

此外，當滅菌製程非自動化時，TR26 建議在滅菌後進行完整性測試 (Integrity Test)。這呼應了 Section 7.0 的 PUPSIT（使用前滅菌後完整性測試）概念 — 滅菌過程本身可能導致濾膜損傷，完整性測試就是確認損傷是否發生的最後一道防線。

8.1 Steam Sterilization 蒸汽滅菌

原文內容 Original Text

Steam under pressure is widely recognized as one of the most effective sterilization methods, typically carried out in an autoclave or in situ as SIP. Steam sterilization cycles are required to be properly validated and performed to ensure the entire filter and membrane matrix is sterilized. The steam sterilization cycles are often automated and well controlled within the end-user site to assure maintaining the validated state. Validation studies typically include integrity-testing the filters post-sterilization and the use of biological indicators (BI). BI's may be placed in the filter core or embedded into the pleat packs. These studies play a critical role in confirming that the cycle achieves a 10-6 sterility assurance level and that the filters are integral.

Steam sterilization parameters should be considered carefully when designing a sterilization process for any filter. Refer to the filter manufacturer's literature for additional information regarding temperature and pressure limitations together with the maximum allowable sterilization cycles.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Steam Sterilization（蒸汽滅菌）：利用高壓飽和蒸汽（通常 121°C 以上）殺滅微生物的方法，是製藥業最廣泛使用的滅菌方式。分為兩種執行方式：

  • Autoclave（高壓滅菌鍋）：將物品放入封閉腔體中滅菌，適用於可拆卸的過濾組件
  • SIP - Sterilize-in-Place（就地滅菌）：蒸汽直接通入已安裝的管路系統進行滅菌，適用於固定式不鏽鋼殼體系統

Biological Indicator, BI（生物指示劑）：含有已知數量高耐熱芽孢的載體，用來驗證滅菌條件是否足以殺滅微生物。BI 可放置在濾膜核心或摺疊濾材內部，確認蒸汽是否真正穿透到最難達到的位置。

10-6 SAL（無菌保證水準）：表示每一百萬個滅菌單元中，允許不超過一個單元殘存活微生物的機率。這是藥典和法規對最終滅菌產品的標準要求。

比喻說明 Analogy

蒸汽滅菌就像用高壓蒸鍋消毒嬰兒奶瓶 — 單純的熱水不夠，必須是加壓的飽和蒸汽才能確保每個角落都達到殺菌溫度。而 BI 就像在蒸鍋最裡面、最難被蒸汽觸及的角落放一張「溫度試紙」— 如果那裡的微生物都被殺死了，就能確信其他地方也沒問題。

重點提示 Key Notes

TR26 強調驗證研究必須同時包含兩項確認：

  • 滅菌有效性：透過 BI 確認達到 10-6 SAL
  • 濾膜完整性：透過滅菌後的完整性測試確認濾膜未損傷

這兩項缺一不可。即使 BI 證明滅菌成功，如果濾膜被蒸汽壓力或溫度損傷而失去完整性，整個除菌過濾製程仍然無效。

8.1.1 Autoclave Sterilization 高壓滅菌鍋滅菌

原文內容 Original Text

Most sterilizing filters are qualified for steam sterilization by their manufacturers to at least 121°C and, in some cases, up to 130 – 134°C. Temperatures higher than this may make many of the plastics used for filter construction unstable and may affect the physical integrity of the filter or increase the level of extractables.

Filter assembly preparation for autoclave sterilization is critical. Sterilized filter components should be protected with a suitable microbial barrier to avoid recontamination after sterilization; however, the barrier must allow air to pass from the filter and steam enter the filter during sterilization. It is critical to ensure that filter inlets and outlets are left open to avoid excessive differential pressures during sterilization. Wrapped articles should be covered with only enough wrapping material to protect critical surfaces while allowing free transfer of saturated steam and air through the material. Use of sterilization tape should be minimal. The weight of any attached fittings and hoses should be supported to avoid potentially deforming of capsule filter inlet and/or outlet at high temperatures. Hoses should not be kinked or closed to allow the free flow of steam and air. The filter user should refer to the manufacturer's specifications when developing an autoclave sterilization cycle for the filter assembly. Slow exhaust (e.g., liquid) cycles may be considered for filter sterilization to avoid excessive pressure differentials across the filter during the cool-down phase of the cycle. Filters should be removed promptly after the sterilization cycle to avoid potential oxidative damage from heated air in the chamber.

One of the major difficulties encountered in autoclave sterilization is air removal. Air can be effectively removed at the initiation of the sterilization cycle by applying a series of vacuum and steam purge cycles. Large filter assemblies may require adjustments to the sterilization parameters, especially the prevacuum phase, to optimize air removal and steam penetration (57). During the sterilization cycle, the filter assembly should be oriented to allow condensates to drain. If the filter is integrity-tested immediately post-sterilization, it does not have to be dry.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Extractables（可萃取物）：在極端條件下（如高溫滅菌）從濾膜材料中萃取出的化學物質。溫度越高、時間越長，可萃取物越多。這些物質可能影響產品品質，因此滅菌溫度不宜過高。

Microbial Barrier（微生物屏障）：滅菌後用來包裹濾膜的保護層，必須達到「擋住微生物但允許空氣和蒸汽通過」的雙重要求。常見的材料包括滅菌用包裝紙或透氣型滅菌袋。

Differential Pressure（壓差）：濾膜上下游的壓力差。在高溫滅菌期間，如果進出口被封閉，內部壓力無法平衡，可能導致濾膜結構性損傷甚至破裂。

Air Removal（排氣）：Autoclave 滅菌的核心挑戰。空氣是蒸汽穿透的最大障礙 — 如果殘留空氣形成「氣穴」，該區域將無法達到滅菌溫度，造成「冷點」(cold spot)。

比喻說明 Analogy

Autoclave 滅菌中的排氣問題，就像用電鍋蒸包子 — 如果蒸籠裡的冷空氣沒有排乾淨，包子表面看起來「蒸好了」，但內部其實還沒熟。蒸汽必須完全取代空氣，才能均勻傳熱。所以 Autoclave 會用「真空-蒸汽交替循環」(vacuum and steam purge cycles) 來反覆抽真空再灌蒸汽，就像反覆打開蒸籠蓋讓冷空氣排出一樣。

重點提示 Key Notes

這一小節列出了多項 Autoclave 操作的實務細節，每一項都是常見偏差的來源：

  • 進出口必須保持開放：封閉的管路會產生壓差，可能在降溫階段撕裂濾膜
  • 包裝材料要適量：太多層包裝會阻礙蒸汽穿透，但太少又無法在滅菌後維持無菌狀態
  • 接頭和軟管需要支撐：高溫下塑料軟化，重力可能使膠囊濾器的進出口變形
  • 滅菌後立即取出：長時間暴露在高溫乾空氣中會造成氧化損傷
  • 考慮慢排氣循環：避免降溫階段的快速壓力變化對濾膜的衝擊

實務應用 Practical Application

CDMO 場景：你的工廠同時使用 10" 和 30" 膠囊濾器，兩種尺寸可以放在同一個 Autoclave 批次中滅菌嗎？

答案是要非常謹慎。30" 大型濾器的排氣和蒸汽穿透需求與 10" 小型濾器不同。如果使用相同的 prevacuum 參數，大型濾器內部可能殘留冷空氣，導致滅菌不完全。建議依據濾器尺寸分別驗證滅菌參數，或至少以「最大裝載量 / 最難滅菌組合」作為最差情境進行驗證。

8.1.2 Sterilize-in-Place 就地滅菌 (SIP)

原文內容 Original Text

The SIP method is limited to filters in housings that can withstand steam pressures of 15–30 psig (1–2 bar). Disposable filter capsules with polypropylene or polyester housings should not be subjected to in-situ steam under pressure, except for high-temperature polymers, such as polyetherimide.

To ensure optimal performance during the sterilization process, it is important to monitor pressure differentials by installing pressure gauges both upstream and downstream of the filter (58). Adhering to the differential pressure limits set by the filter manufacturer for each specific housing or assembly is critical, especially at elevated temperatures. High differential pressure may occur due to restricted drainage or blocked steam flow but, with proper practices, this can be effectively avoided.

To protect wetted filters, steam should be introduced gently in forward flow at low pressure. This approach allows the filters to heat up gradually, ensuring that any liquid in the membrane matrix is evaporated before steam flow begins. An effective way to facilitate this is by opening the upstream vent and housing drain before the steaming process. This allows steam to efficiently heat the filter, ensuring a smooth transition to sterilization. Once downstream pressure increase is observed, upstream steam pressure can be increased to target the sterilizing pressure by partially closing the vent and drain valves. Alternatively, the system can be designed to introduce steam from both sides of the filter to avoid excessive steam-pressure differentials.

During the cool-down phase of an SIP process, it is critical that air or other suitable gas maintain a positive pressure across the filter. Excessive reverse pressure differential can arise if the upstream side of the filter is cooled more rapidly than the filter core. Gas-assisted cooling is preferred over liquid cooling, because the latter may generate thermal shock and damage the filter (58, 59).

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

SIP - Sterilize-in-Place（就地滅菌）：蒸汽直接通入已安裝在製程管路上的過濾殼體，無需拆卸即可進行滅菌。通常用於不鏽鋼殼體的固定式過濾系統。

Forward Flow（正向流）：蒸汽沿著正常製程方向（從上游到下游）通入。這是保護濕潤濾膜的關鍵 — 讓濾膜上的殘留液體在蒸汽壓力升高前先蒸發掉。

Reverse Pressure Differential（反向壓差）：當下游壓力高於上游壓力時發生。在冷卻階段，如果上游先冷卻（蒸汽冷凝產生真空），而濾膜核心仍保持較高壓力，就會形成反向壓差，可能反向撕裂濾膜。

Thermal Shock（熱衝擊）：溫度的急劇變化（例如液態冷卻水接觸高溫濾膜）可能導致材料結構性損傷。因此建議使用氣體輔助冷卻 (gas-assisted cooling)，讓溫度緩慢下降。

比喻說明 Analogy

SIP 的操作就像在家裡幫暖氣管路做維護 — 你不能直接把滾燙的蒸汽灌進冷管路（會像把冷玻璃杯放進滾水一樣碎裂），而是要慢慢加溫。同樣地，冷卻時也不能用冰水去沖 — 必須讓它像關火後的爐子一樣自然降溫（或用風扇吹涼，即氣體輔助冷卻），而不是潑水降溫。

重點提示 Key Notes

SIP 有幾個容易被忽視的關鍵操作要點：

  • 材料限制：聚丙烯 (PP) 和聚酯 (PET) 殼體的一次性膠囊濾器不能進行 SIP，除非是耐高溫的特殊聚合物如聚醚亞胺 (PEI)
  • 壓力監控：上下游都必須安裝壓力錶，因為壓差是 SIP 過程中濾膜損傷的首要風險
  • 緩慢升壓：蒸汽必須以低壓正向緩慢引入，先排除濾膜中殘留的液體
  • 雙側進蒸汽：可以考慮從濾膜兩側同時引入蒸汽，消除跨膜壓差
  • 冷卻階段維持正壓：必須用空氣或惰性氣體維持正壓，避免形成反向壓差

實務應用 Practical Application

CDMO 場景：你的客戶要求使用 SIP 除菌過濾系統。你需要評估以下問題：

  • 殼體材質是否能承受 1-2 bar 蒸汽壓力？（不鏽鋼殼體通常沒問題，但高分子殼體需要確認）
  • 管路設計是否允許冷凝水排放？（如果冷凝水無法排出，會造成積水阻礙蒸汽穿透）
  • 冷卻系統是否配置了氣體輔助冷卻？（如果只有水冷卻，需要評估熱衝擊風險）
  • PLC 控制系統是否能監控上下游壓差並在超限時自動中止？

這些設計考量在專案初期就要確定，因為它們影響管路佈局、儀表配置和自動化程式設計。

8.2 Irradiation Sterilization 輻射滅菌

原文內容 Original Text

Common irradiation sterilization processes are gamma radiation and X-ray. Gamma and X-ray irradiation sterilization can be validated using a bioburden and dosimetry approach. Follow current International Organization for Standardization (ISO) standards such as ISO-11137: Sterilization of Health Care Products — Radiation (60).

Some polymers used in filter manufacturing have limited resistance to sterilizing irradiation. Filters may be constructed of radiation-resistant materials or polymers formulated with antioxidant additives designed to protect the polymer from excessive radiation-induced degradation. Irradiation sterilization, as with all sterilization methods, requires validation to establish sterility and stability.

Irradiation sterilization has several advantages, such as a high sterility-assurance level, no residual gas- or chemical-sterilization components, dry filters, and minimal interference by packaging components with the sterilization process. Disadvantages include potential lack of filter compatibility to high-dose and limited filter shelf life (most irradiated filters are not validated to withstand a second sterilization cycle involving irradiation or steam). Steam sterilization of previously irradiated filters may increase extractables and leachables and could lead to loss of filter integrity, which could be confirmed by an integrity test.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Irradiation Sterilization（輻射滅菌）：利用高能量輻射（伽瑪射線或 X 射線）破壞微生物的 DNA 來達到滅菌效果。這是一種「穿透式」滅菌，輻射可以直接穿過包裝材料，因此濾膜可以在最終包裝狀態下進行滅菌。

Bioburden and Dosimetry Approach（生物負荷與劑量測定法）：先測量產品上的初始微生物量（bioburden），再計算需要多少輻射劑量（dosimetry）才能達到所需的無菌保證水準。這與蒸汽滅菌使用 BI 的方法不同。

ISO-11137：輻射滅菌的國際標準，規範了劑量建立、劑量審核和常規滅菌的要求。

Shelf Life（保存期限）：輻射滅菌的濾膜通常有較短的保存期限，因為輻射會持續引起聚合物降解，即使在滅菌完成後。

比喻說明 Analogy

輻射滅菌就像把食物送進機場的 X 光機 — 射線直接穿透包裝殺死病菌，食物本身不需要被拆封或加熱。好處是「乾式」滅菌（不需要蒸汽或水），壞處是某些材料會被輻射「曬傷」— 就像皮膚被紫外線曬傷後會變脆弱，某些塑膠材料被伽瑪射線照射後也會劣化，而且這種劣化隨時間累積，所以保存期限有限。

重點提示 Key Notes

TR26 點出了一個極其重要的交叉滅菌風險：

Steam sterilization of previously irradiated filters may increase extractables and leachables and could lead to loss of filter integrity.

這表示已經過輻射滅菌的濾膜，不應該再進行蒸汽滅菌。輻射已經使聚合物結構弱化，蒸汽的高溫高壓會進一步加速降解，導致：

  • 可萃取物和可溶出物 (E&L) 大幅增加 — 影響產品品質
  • 濾膜物理完整性喪失 — 影響無菌保證

在 CDMO 環境中，這意味著如果你購買的是「已輻射滅菌」的即用型濾器，千萬不要在使用前再 Autoclave 一次。

8.3 Gas Sterilization 氣體滅菌

原文內容 Original Text

The most common sterilization gas is ethylene oxide, which may be used at 100% or diluted in a carrier gas. In addition to the increased environmental impact from using ethylene oxide as a sterilant, there may be safety factors such as the formation of ethylene chlorohydrin and ethylene glycol reaction products and ethylene oxide that may remain in the filter matrix. For successful filter sterilization by ethylene oxide, the filters should be dry and wrapped in a manner that allows for the free entry of water vapor and gas into the filter matrix. Following sterilization, the package also should permit the removal of residual gas and other undesirable byproducts. Due to the difficulty of ensuring gas penetration into and out of the filter membrane, this method of sterilization is typically not used for pleated filters but only for very small, encapsulated disc-style filters.

Parameters influencing gas sterilization are preconditioning factors, gas concentration, relative humidity, temperature, and time. Since there is no established correlation between lethality and the interrelationship between the sterilization parameters, destruction of a suitable biological indicator is the preferred method for validation and process monitoring. Spores of Bacillus atrophaeus have been established as the indicator of choice for this sterilization method.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Ethylene Oxide, EO / EtO（環氧乙烷）：一種具有強烈殺菌能力的氣體，可在較低溫度下殺滅微生物，適用於不耐高溫的材料。但它是一種已知的致癌物，且會在材料中殘留，產生有害副產物。

Ethylene Chlorohydrin（氯乙醇）和 Ethylene Glycol（乙二醇）：EO 與濾膜材料或包裝中的氯化物和水分反應產生的副產物。這些殘留物具有毒性，必須在滅菌後通過充分的除氣 (aeration) 過程去除。

Bacillus atrophaeus（萎縮芽孢桿菌）：EO 滅菌驗證專用的生物指示劑菌種（過去也稱為 B. subtilis var. niger）。之所以不用數學模型建立殺滅動力學，是因為 EO 滅菌的多參數交互作用過於複雜，尚無可靠的預測公式。

比喻說明 Analogy

EO 氣體滅菌就像家裡用蟑螂燻蒸劑 — 密封空間裡釋放有毒氣體，殺死所有蟲子（微生物），但之後必須徹底通風（除氣），否則殘留的毒氣會傷害住在裡面的人（患者）。而且，燻蒸劑很難穿透到折疊的衣服深處 — 這就是為什麼 EO 不適合用在摺疊式濾器 (pleated filters) 上，因為氣體無法有效穿透多層摺疊的濾膜結構。

重點提示 Key Notes

TR26 對 EO 滅菌的態度相當謹慎，基本上將其限制在「非常小的圓盤式濾器」。主要原因：

  • 穿透性問題：EO 氣體難以穿透摺疊式濾膜的複雜結構
  • 殘留物風險：毒性副產物必須被徹底去除，但濾膜內部的去除效率難以驗證
  • 環境與安全：EO 是致癌物，全球監管趨勢正在限制其使用
  • 缺乏數學模型：與蒸汽滅菌不同，EO 的五個參數之間的殺菌交互作用無法用公式預測，只能依賴 BI 來驗證

在現代製藥生產中，EO 滅菌的濾器已經非常罕見。大多數應用已被輻射預滅菌或蒸汽滅菌取代。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 為什麼 EO 滅菌只適用於小型圓盤式濾器而不適用於大型摺疊式濾器？請從「氣體穿透」和「殘留物去除」兩個角度分析。
  2. 蒸汽滅菌可以用 D 值和 Z 值來計算所需的滅菌時間，但 EO 滅菌卻無法這樣做。為什麼？這對驗證策略有什麼影響？
  3. 如果法規要求完全禁止 EO 的使用，需要 EO 滅菌的小型濾器可以改用什麼替代滅菌方法？
8.4 Resterilization of Filters 濾膜再滅菌

原文內容 Original Text

Many sterilizing filters are designed to withstand multiple autoclave or in situ SIP cycles, either to demonstrate robustness or to meet filter-user resterilization requirements. Multiple gamma-irradiation sterilization cycles are generally not applicable due to cumulative dosage effects. Autoclave resterilization of previously radiation-sterilized filters may also not be possible. Suitability of resterilization processes should be confirmed with the filter manufacturer.

Where the resterilization of sterilizing filters is elected, the filter user should ensure that the resterilization process and the number of resterilization cycles does not adversely affect the filter or the product. Resterilization, including sterilization conditions, number of sterilization cycles and cumulative sterilization times should be validated.

Sterilizing filters should be routinely discarded after the processing of a single lot. In instances where repeated use can be justified, however, the sterilizing-filter validation including integrity-testing, bacterial retention studies, and cleaning should incorporate the maximum number of lots to be processed.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Resterilization（再滅菌）：在同一個濾膜上進行多次滅菌循環。這可能發生在兩種情境下：

  • 製程需求：例如 SIP 滅菌後因故未能立即開始過濾，需要再次 SIP
  • 多批次使用：一支濾器在一批次使用完畢後，清洗並再次滅菌後用於下一批次

Cumulative Dosage Effects（累積劑量效應）：多次輻射滅菌的劑量會累加，即使每次劑量在材料耐受範圍內，累積後的總劑量可能超出材料承受極限。

Single-Lot Use（單批次使用原則）：TR26 的預設立場是除菌濾膜在處理完一個批次後即應丟棄。重複使用需要額外的驗證。

比喻說明 Analogy

再滅菌就像把同一件衣服反覆用高溫烘乾機烘乾 — 每烘一次，衣服都會稍微縮水、褪色、變脆。蒸汽滅菌對濾膜的影響也是累積性的：第一次可能看不出差別，但第 10 次、第 20 次之後，濾膜的機械強度、化學穩定性和可萃取物特性都可能發生變化。所以製造商會標明「最大允許滅菌次數」，就像衣服洗標上的「最高洗滌溫度」。

重點提示 Key Notes

本小節涉及三個層次的風險管理：

  1. 滅菌方法的累積效應：
    • 蒸汽可多次使用（有上限）
    • 輻射不建議多次使用（累積劑量）
    • 交叉滅菌（輻射+蒸汽）通常不可行
  2. 再滅菌驗證要求：必須驗證最大允許循環次數下的完整性、細菌截留能力和可萃取物水準
  3. 重複使用的額外驗證：如果濾膜在多個批次間重複使用，驗證範圍必須擴大到包含清洗驗證、最大批次數下的完整性測試和細菌截留研究

在決策層級中，再滅菌和重複使用的經濟效益屬於最低層級（商業與效率）。如果再滅菌有任何可能影響無菌濾液保證（最高層級），則不應進行。

實務應用 Practical Application

CDMO 場景：客戶要求將一支 30" 不鏽鋼殼體除菌濾器在 5 個連續批次中重複使用，以降低過濾耗材成本。作為製程工程師，你需要怎麼評估？

  • 確認濾膜製造商標示的最大 SIP 循環次數（例如 50 次），確保 5 次使用加上必要的 SIP 在此範圍內
  • 驗證計畫必須使用「最大使用次數」（5 批次 + 相應次數的 SIP 和清洗）作為驗證條件
  • 每次使用後必須進行完整性測試，且須通過才能進入下一批次
  • 清洗驗證必須證明產品殘留不會影響下一批次的品質
  • 細菌截留研究必須在最大使用次數後進行

重要提醒：對於 CDMO 多產品廠房，濾器重複使用還涉及交叉污染風險 — 不同產品之間絕對不應共用除菌濾器。

  2. 為什麼多次輻射滅菌通常不可行，但多次蒸汽滅菌在一定範圍內是可以接受的？兩者對材料的影響機制有何不同？

  4. 從決策層級的角度分析：重複使用濾器帶來的成本節約，是否足以合理化額外的驗證負擔和潛在的品質風險？

本節重點回顧 Section Summary

  • 濾膜滅菌的核心目標：在確保達到無菌保證水準（10-6 SAL）的同時，不損傷濾膜的物理完整性和化學穩定性。這是一個需要平衡的「雙重要求」。
  • 蒸汽滅菌（Section 8.1）是最廣泛使用的方法，分為 Autoclave（適用於可拆卸組件）和 SIP（適用於固定式系統）。關鍵控制點包括：排氣（Autoclave）、壓差控制（SIP）、緩慢升溫降溫、以及冷卻階段維持正壓。
  • 輻射滅菌（Section 8.2）提供乾式無殘留的滅菌方式，但受限於材料相容性和保存期限。最關鍵的警告：已輻射滅菌的濾膜不應再進行蒸汽滅菌，否則可萃取物增加且可能喪失完整性。
  • 氣體滅菌（Section 8.3）以 EO 為主，但因氣體穿透困難和毒性殘留問題，僅限用於小型圓盤式濾器。驗證必須依賴生物指示劑（B. atrophaeus），因為缺乏可靠的數學殺菌模型。
  • 再滅菌（Section 8.4）需注意累積效應 — 蒸汽滅菌可在製造商限制內多次執行，但輻射和交叉滅菌通常不可行。濾膜的預設使用原則是「單批次即丟棄」，重複使用需要全面的額外驗證。
  • 決策層級提醒：無論選擇哪種滅菌方法，無菌濾液保證 (Sterile Filtrate Assurance) 永遠是第一優先。任何可能危及無菌保證的成本節約措施都應該被拒絕。
