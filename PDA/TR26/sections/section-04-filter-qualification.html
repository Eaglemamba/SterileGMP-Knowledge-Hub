4.0 Filter Qualification, Design, and Characterization

原文內容 Original Text

Filter manufacturers typically provide the information discussed in this section, which should be considered by the end user for their risk assessment when selecting a sterilizing-grade filter. Since bacterial retention studies and filter integrity testing are important components of manufacturer-supplied information, those topics are covered separately in Section 6.0 and Section 7.0, respectively.

Sterilizing-grade filters are available in a variety of sizes, configurations (e.g., flat sheets, capsules, cartridges) and membrane chemistries so that the filter user can select the most appropriate filter for a particular application. Commonly used membrane chemistries include polyvinylidene fluoride (PVDF), polysulfone (PS), polyethersulfone (PES), nylon, cellulose esters, polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE), polyester and polypropylene. Not only do different chemistries impart differences in flow characteristics and filtration performance, but they also affect the levels of filter extractables and leachables, the thermal and physical properties of the filter, and the interactions with the process stream (as determined via compatibility and adsorption-testing). Once the membrane is manufactured into its final format, it is evaluated by the filter manufacturer with respect to flow rate and pressure drop, operating limits of temperature and pressure, extractables, compatibility, cleanliness, sterilization conditions, integrity, and bacterial retention applying defined test conditions.

Since sterilizing-grade filters are a critical part of any aseptic process, a significant amount of testing and documentation is conducted by filter manufacturers to qualify their filters. Filter support documentation may include a validation or qualification guide, FDA Drug Master File (DMF), product literature, specification sheets, technical bulletins and application notes. Pharmaceutical-grade filters are also often accompanied by an individual certificate listing the filter part, lot numbers and release criteria. The supporting documentation from filter manufacturers can help the end users in their assessment for qualifying filters and supporting regulatory inquiries.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Sterilizing-Grade Filter (除菌級濾膜)：指經過驗證能夠完全攔截微生物（特別是 Brevundimonas diminuta，挑戰密度至少 107 CFU/cm2）的 0.2 微米（或更小）孔徑濾膜。這是無菌製程中最關鍵的最後一道防線。

Filter Qualification (濾膜確效)：由濾膜製造商執行的一系列標準化測試，證明濾膜適合藥品應用。包含細菌攔截、完整性測試、可萃取物、生物相容性等。

Membrane Chemistry (濾膜化學材質)：不同高分子材料（PVDF、PES、Nylon 等）各有不同的流速特性、化學相容性和萃取物特性。選擇時須綜合考量製程需求。

比喻說明 Analogy

選擇濾膜就像選擇淨水器的濾芯。市面上有活性碳、RO 逆滲透、中空纖維等不同類型，每種都有不同的過濾效果、流量和壽命。你不會只看價格就買 -- 你要考慮水質（製程液特性）、用水量（批量大小）、過濾後是否會釋放異味（可萃取物）等。同樣地，濾膜製造商提供的技術文件就像淨水器的檢驗報告，但你還是要在自家水管系統中實際測試才能確認效果。

重點提示 Key Notes

責任劃分很重要：濾膜製造商負責「確效」(Qualification)，證明產品本身的品質與性能；濾膜使用者則負責「驗證」(Validation)，證明在自己的實際製程條件下，濾膜仍能達到預期的除菌效能。製造商的文件是基礎，但不能取代使用者自己的驗證工作。

FDA DMF (Drug Master File)：這是濾膜製造商向 FDA 提交的機密文件，包含完整的製造與品質資訊。藥廠在提交 NDA/BLA 時可以引用此 DMF，無需自行提供這些細節。

4.1 Filter Qualification and Validation

原文內容 Original Text

Filter manufacturers typically publish results of tests performed according to applicable compendial methods to qualify the filter as suitable for pharmaceutical applications. This qualification documentation supports, but does not replace, performance qualification as a part of process validation conducted by the filter user. Table 4.1-1 lists qualification and lot-release tests on membranes and devices commonly performed by manufacturers and validated by filter users.

Table 4.1-1 Qualification and Validation Recommendations

L = Filter Manufacturer's Lot Release Criteria | Q = Filter Manufacturer's Qualification | V = Process Validation Testing (Rationale can be provided for scale-down testing) | E = Evaluate the Need for Additional Testing, Document Technical Assessment | * = Typically Using Water

Further details of the tests listed in Table 4.1-1 are provided below:

  • Bacterial retention of a filter is initially qualified by the filter manufacturer in saline lactose broth (SLB), water or another suitable carrier fluid and then again by the filter user under process conditions for critical sterilizing-filtration applications. In each case, 100% retention is correlated to an integrity test using a standard reference fluid (e.g., water or alcohol). During the membrane development process, filter manufacturers define the correlation between integrity testing and bacterial retention following ASTM F838 methodology (6).
  • The chemical compatibility between the filter and product can be qualified by the filter manufacturer. Filter-user testing is often necessary to confirm compatibility of the process fluids with the filter and operation conditions. Tests such as product-based integrity and bacterial retention may be used to assess chemical compatibility.
  • Extractables may be qualified by the filter manufacturer using model solvents and specific laboratory conditions and may also be determined by the filter user for product-specific fluids under simulated processing conditions (e.g., temperature, duration). Based on the results obtained from the extractables study, the filter user should evaluate the need for testing of leachables. The leachables study should build upon the identified extractables and be tested with the product in scope or a representative surrogate.
  • Filter safety testing (i.e., biological reactivity) is conducted by the filter manufacturer and does not need to be repeated by the filter user (16).
  • The capability of a filter to be sterilized and the maximum sterilization conditions recommended for the filter are qualified by the filter manufacturer. The filter user is responsible for validating the sterilization method used in the process.*
  • Particulate matter, nonfiber release, oxidizable substances, total organic carbon (TOC), conductivity testing and flow rates may be qualified by the filter manufacturer. The filter user should determine if additional testing is required due to differences in processing conditions.
  • Filter selection done by the end user will look at filter sizing, filter type based on process scale and design, and impact on product critical quality attributes (CQAs) by evaluating compatibility (e.g., adsorption, reaction, degradation) through contact with the filter unit.
  • Since solvents could be used as wetting agents to determine if the integrity test failed due to wetting problems, it would be advisable to determine the appropriate amount of flush volumes needed.

*Note: Sterilization of filter capsules by gamma or x-ray irradiation is generally validated by the filter manufacturer.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Q、L、V、E 四層責任架構：

  • Q (Qualification，確效)：濾膜製造商執行的標準化測試，建立產品的基本品質數據
  • L (Lot Release，批次放行)：製造商對每批出廠產品的放行檢驗，確保批間一致性
  • V (Validation，驗證)：使用者必須執行的製程驗證，證明在實際製程條件下的效能
  • E (Evaluate，評估)：使用者需評估是否需要額外測試，並記錄技術評估理由

ASTM F838：這是細菌攔截測試的標準方法，使用 Brevundimonas diminuta 作為挑戰菌株，建立完整性測試與細菌攔截之間的關聯性。

比喻說明 Analogy

Table 4.1-1 的責任分工就像買車：汽車製造商（濾膜廠）負責出廠前的碰撞測試、排放檢驗、品質管控（Q, L）。但車主（濾膜使用者）買車後還是要做：實際路況試駕（V = 製程驗證）、評估是否需要加裝行車記錄器或改裝排氣管（E = 評估額外測試需求）。製造商說車子「可以開」不代表「在你的路線上可以安全開」-- 那是車主的責任。

重點提示 Key Notes

Extractables vs. Leachables 的關鍵差異：

  • 可萃取物 (Extractables)：在極端實驗條件（強溶劑、高溫、長時間）下從濾膜中「強制」萃取出來的化學物質 -- 代表「最壞情況」的完整清單
  • 可溶出物 (Leachables)：在正常製程條件下實際遷移到產品中的化學物質 -- 通常是可萃取物的一個子集 (subset)

Leachables 研究應以 extractables 研究結果為基礎來設計，而非從零開始。這是一個科學驅動的漸進式方法。

實務應用 Practical Application

CDMO 實務情境：當你的客戶要求更換濾膜供應商時，Table 4.1-1 就是你的檢查清單。你需要：

  1. 向新供應商索取所有標示 Q 和 L 的測試數據
  2. 重新執行所有標示 V 的製程驗證（不能沿用舊濾膜的數據）
  3. 對所有標示 E 的項目進行風險評估，決定哪些需要重新測試

這個變更可能需要 3-6 個月和數十萬的費用 -- 因此供應商選擇應該在製程開發早期就慎重考量。

4.2 Filter Design

原文內容 Original Text

Sterilizing-grade membranes are employed in a wide variety of sizes and filter formats, including cartridges, capsules and disc styles to suit many scales and types of applications. Membranes can also be pleated to increase the surface area, and this can be placed in either a cartridge or a capsule filter. Figure 4.2-1 shows examples of filter cartridges, capsules and components.

The design of filter elements is important because design features like membrane pleat shape and density determine the EFA (Effective Filtration Area) which, in turn, influences the flow rate and total throughput (i.e., the larger the EFA, the higher the flow and/or throughput). If the pleat density is too high, the free flow path is restricted, and the flow is reduced; if the pleat density is too low, the EFA might be too low and the flow restricted. There may be a difference in the pleating geometry used in a scale-up study in process development (PD) and the pleating geometry of the process-scale device, since high-EFA pleating geometries are typically used in process-scale devices only. Potential differences in performance should be evaluated during PD (see Section 5.4.1 for additional information).

Depending on the filtration needs (e.g., product, process, equipment or facility constraints), different setups and filter types can be employed. Figure 4.2-2 shows different filter types and configurations.

Figure 4.2-3 and Figure 4.2-4 are cutaways that reveal the internal components of a typical cartridge and capsule. Filter capsules, composed of filter elements preinstalled and sealed in plastic housings, are compact and relatively lightweight. Depending on the orientation and configuration of the capsule or filter-housing, the fluid may enter from the bottom or from the top of the capsule or housing and flow around the outside of the internal filter module, pass through the pleated filter membrane to the interior core, and exit the outlet.

Stainless-steel filter housings and disposable filter capsules are available in two configurations: T-style or in-line style (Figure 4.2-5A and Figure 4.2-6A, respectively). The T-style housings have the inlet and outlet pipe adjacent to each other at the bottom plate of the housing (Figure 4.2-5B). The inlet pipe in the in-line style housing is positioned at the dome of the housing and the outlet pipe on the bottom plate (Figure 4.2-6B). Most housings and capsules also have vent ports for removing air.

In addition to housing styles, the filter elements themselves have multiple adapter options (Figure 4.2-7) for fitting the filter into the housing. The most common filter-cartridge adapter is a double O-ring, double-bayonet adapter. This adapter type is twisted into place on the base plate to ensure a tight seal between the filter element and the housing base. As housing-base tolerances may vary, it is imperative that the specific filter and housing provide the proper seal throughout the filtration operation (e.g., from steam-in-place (SIP) through post-use integrity-testing).

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

EFA (Effective Filtration Area，有效過濾面積)：這是濾膜中實際參與過濾的表面積。透過褶皺 (pleating) 設計，可在有限的濾器外殼空間內大幅增加有效面積。EFA 直接決定了流速和總處理量。

濾器三大型式：

  • Cartridge (濾芯)：安裝在不鏽鋼外殼中，可更換內芯，適合大規模生產
  • Capsule (囊式濾器)：濾膜預裝在塑膠外殼中的一體式設計，使用後整體丟棄，屬於一次性使用 (Single-Use)
  • Disc (平片式)：用於實驗室或小規模測試

T-Style vs. In-Line Style：兩種外殼進出口配置。T-style 的進出口在底部同側（便於排液），In-line style 的進口在頂部、出口在底部（利用重力排空）。

比喻說明 Analogy

褶皺密度的平衡就像摺扇：把扇面摺得太密（pleat density 太高），扇子打不開、扇不到風（流路受限）；摺得太疏（pleat density 太低），扇面面積太小，扇風效果也不好。最佳設計是在有限的空間裡找到摺皺密度的甜蜜點，讓空氣（液體）能自由流過，同時最大化有效面積。

而 adapter（轉接頭）就像電器插頭：即使電器再好，如果插頭和插座不匹配、接觸不良，輕則漏電（洩漏），重則起火（無菌保證失敗）。雙 O-ring 雙卡榫設計就是「防呆+雙保險」的概念。

重點提示 Key Notes

Scale-Up 的陷阱：製程開發階段使用的小型濾器與量產階段的大型濾器可能有不同的褶皺幾何設計。高 EFA 褶皺通常只用在量產設備上。這意味著 PD 階段的流速和處理量數據可能無法直接外推到量產規模 -- 需要在量產設備上重新評估。

密封的全程完整性：注意原文強調密封必須在整個操作過程中維持 -- 從 SIP（就地滅菌，高溫高壓條件）一直到使用後完整性測試。這涵蓋了極端的溫度和壓力變化，對密封材料和設計是很大的挑戰。

4.3 Filters in Single-Use Disposable Systems

原文內容 Original Text

It is common to build transfer sets of filters integrated with tubing, sensors, and connectors that are presterilized (e.g., gamma, x-ray) typically used together with other single-use technology or in hybrid systems together with stainless steel equipment. Furthermore, the assembling of multiple filters in ready-to-use assemblies has also become common. These systems offer some advantages over conventional stainless-steel equipment including the:

  • Ability to obtain presterilized
  • Reduction in setup time
  • Elimination of cleaning
  • Elimination of cleaning validation
  • Elimination of risk of cross-contamination
  • Elimination of filter housing maintenance
  • Reduction in operator exposure due to closed systems
  • Reduction of drug product contamination from the external environment
  • Reduction of the operator's exposure to the drug substances

Implementation of SUS should follow a risk-based approach that considers product and process compatibility and demonstrates robustness, suitability, and consistent performance under intended use conditions. (Refer to PDA's Technical Report No. 66: Application of Single-Use Systems in Pharmaceutical Manufacturing for additional information.)

Depending on the processing considerations and setup requirements, the filter user chooses the best configuration (capsule, cartridge in stainless-steel housing, or hybrid) for the filtration operation. Use of disposable capsules obviates the need for cleaning validation and equipment amortization, which may reduce operating costs. Another advantage is that operator contact with potentially hazardous products is minimized. The use of stainless steel must be balanced against the use of capsule filters, which can be single use and can reduce utilities and validation efforts. Different optimization can be performed based on environment (waste), energy use, or financial analysis.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Single-Use System, SUS (一次性使用系統)：預先組裝、預先滅菌（通常用 gamma 或 x-ray 輻射）的濾器與管路組件。使用一次後即丟棄，不需清洗、不需 CIP/SIP。

Hybrid System (混合系統)：結合不鏽鋼設備與一次性組件的系統。例如使用不鏽鋼配液桶搭配一次性管路與囊式濾器。這是許多 CDMO 目前最常見的配置。

Ready-to-Use Assembly (即用型組件)：由供應商預先組裝好的完整過濾系統，包含濾器、管路、接頭、感測器等，經 gamma 輻射滅菌後出貨，現場拆封即可使用。

比喻說明 Analogy

不鏽鋼濾器系統就像家裡的瓷器餐具：品質好、可重複使用，但每次用完要洗、要消毒、要檢查有沒有破損，而且洗碗（清洗驗證）本身就是一項大工程。一次性使用系統就像免洗餐具：開封即用、用完即丟，不用洗碗，也不用擔心上一批客人的菜汁殘留（交叉污染）。但免洗餐具有環保問題（廢棄物），而且你需要確認免洗餐具本身沒有釋放有害物質（可萃取物/可溶出物）。

重點提示 Key Notes

SUS 的隱藏成本：雖然 SUS 省去了清洗驗證和設備折舊，但有其他成本需考量：

  • 廢棄物處理成本：大量塑膠廢棄物的處理和環保合規
  • 供應鏈風險：對特定供應商的依賴性增加，如遇供應中斷可能影響生產
  • 可萃取物/可溶出物評估：塑膠組件需要額外的 E&L 研究
  • 儲存空間：預滅菌組件需要乾淨、溫控的倉儲空間

PDA TR-66 提供了 SUS 實施的完整指引，在引進 SUS 前應詳細參考。

實務應用 Practical Application

CDMO 多產品生產的決策：CDMO 同時生產多種藥品，交叉污染風險是最大挑戰之一。使用 SUS 可以從根本上消除交叉污染風險（每批使用全新組件），同時大幅縮短批次間的轉換時間（不需 CIP/SIP）。對於高效價藥物（如 ADC、高活性 API），SUS 幾乎是必要的選擇。但對於大批量、低價值產品，不鏽鋼系統的長期經濟性可能更佳。

4.4 Filter Toxicity and Biocompatibility

原文內容 Original Text

One important aspect of selecting a filter is assessing the safety of the filter medium and/or device. Filter manufacturers should provide information about the origin and toxicity of the filter components, including their origin of animal-derived components.

4.4.1 Toxicity

Filters must not impart toxic materials to the fluid stream. Filter manufacturers generally qualify filters through performance of standardized testing in compliance with compendial methods (16). These tests involve the introduction of filter extracts or actual filter samples into cell-culture systems. Reactivity of the cell culture to the test article is assessed visually, scored and compared against predefined acceptance criteria for toxicological safety. The results of these tests are commonly included as part of the filter validation guide.

Over the years there has been a shift away from animal-based testing, to better align with animal-welfare principles.

4.4.2 Animal-Derived Materials

The filter manufacturer typically includes information on biocompatibility of animal-derived materials used in filter manufacture. The use of bovine tallow-derived stearates poses essentially no risk for transmission of transmissible spongiform encephalopathies (TSE) and other diseases. Typically, the manufacturing processes used to produce bovine tallow and tallow derivatives are very rigorous and meet or exceed safety requirements (17, 18).

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Biocompatibility (生物相容性)：濾膜材料與生物體接觸時不產生有害反應的能力。這是藥品安全的基本要求 -- 濾膜本身不能成為毒性物質的來源。

Toxicity Testing (毒性測試)：依據藥典方法（如 USP <87> 體外細胞毒性試驗、USP <88> 體內生物反應性試驗），將濾膜萃取液或樣品暴露於細胞培養系統，觀察細胞反應來評估毒性。

TSE (Transmissible Spongiform Encephalopathies，可傳播性海綿狀腦病變)：如牛海綿狀腦病（BSE，俗稱狂牛症）。任何使用動物來源材料的產品都需評估 TSE 傳播風險。

比喻說明 Analogy

生物相容性測試就像食品容器的安全檢測：你家微波爐用的塑膠容器必須通過食品級安全測試，確認加熱後不會釋放有害物質到食物中。同樣地，濾膜在接觸高價值藥品液流時，不能釋放任何對患者有毒的物質。而動物來源成分的追溯就像食品的產銷履歷 -- 你需要知道原料來源，確保沒有傳染性風險。

重點提示 Key Notes

動物來源材料的法規趨勢：

  • EU GMP Annex 1 和 ICH Q5A 對動物來源材料有嚴格的追溯和風險評估要求
  • 業界正在從動物實驗轉向細胞培養等替代方法，這是全球 3Rs（Replacement, Reduction, Refinement）原則的體現
  • 牛脂來源的硬脂酸鹽 (bovine tallow-derived stearates) 雖然 TSE 風險極低，但仍需在文件中記錄風險評估

對 CDMO 的實際影響：某些客戶（特別是疫苗或生物製劑廠商）可能要求完全無動物來源成分 (Animal-Free) 的濾膜。確認供應商能提供相關聲明文件。

4.5 Filter Cleanliness

原文內容 Original Text

Particulate contamination from the filter, filter hardware, filter installation and the process should be evaluated and considered since each source may contribute to particle burden in the product. Filter flush effluent samples are examined for particles greater than 10 microns and greater than 25 microns. Filter flush effluent is considered compliant upon meeting compendial guidelines for particulate matter in injections. Using these criteria, filters are also qualified as nonfiber-releasing (10-12).

In addition to particles, filters may be a source of other contaminants, such as endotoxin, organic carbon, or oxidizable substances. Potential sources may include surfactants, wetting agents, additives in the plastic component manufacture, manufacturing debris and materials of construction (MoC). Preflushing filters may reduce levels of particulates and contaminants and may be performed as part of the wetting process prior to integrity-testing. A filter can be preflushed based on the extractable studies, taking into account the sterilization method and process step.

Note: Filter flushing and preflushing are performed by the filter user.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Particulate Matter in Injections (注射劑微粒物質)：依 USP <788> / Ph. Eur. 2.9.19 標準，注射劑中 ≥10 μm 微粒不得超過 6000 個/容器（大容量注射劑），≥25 μm 不得超過 600 個/容器。濾膜沖洗液必須符合這些限度。

Nonfiber-Releasing (非纖維釋放型)：確認濾膜在過濾操作中不會釋放纖維到濾液中。這是藥典的基本要求。

Preflushing (預沖洗)：在正式過濾前用水或適當溶劑沖洗濾膜，以去除殘留微粒、製造殘留物和表面活性劑等。沖洗量應基於可萃取物研究來決定。

比喻說明 Analogy

預沖洗就像新買的水壺第一次使用前要先煮幾壺水倒掉：新水壺內壁可能有製造過程中的殘留物質（塑膠味、金屬粉末等），前幾壺水會把這些東西沖掉。同樣地，新濾膜上可能殘留製造過程中的添加劑、表面活性劑或微粒，預沖洗就是把這些「出廠殘留」清除乾淨，確保正式過濾時產品不會被污染。沖多少量（flush volume）要根據實驗數據決定，就像水壺要煮幾壺水才沒味道，要自己測試才知道。

重點提示 Key Notes

汙染源的全面考量：原文特別指出微粒汙染不只來自濾膜本身，還包括：

  • Filter hardware：不鏽鋼外殼、O-ring、adapter 等硬體組件
  • Filter installation：安裝過程中引入的微粒（操作人員、工具、環境）
  • Process：製程本身產生的微粒

這提醒我們在微粒調查時不能只聚焦在濾膜上 -- 整個系統都要評估。內毒素 (endotoxin) 和有機碳 (TOC) 也是需要關注的清潔度指標。

  2. 如果濾膜經 gamma 輻射滅菌後的可萃取物比蒸汽滅菌後更高，這對預沖洗量的設定有什麼影響？
4.6 Filter Extractables

原文內容 Original Text

Since sterilizing-filters are often used in the final manufacturing steps of a pharmaceutical production process, the effect of the filter on the final product should be evaluated. An extractable is any chemical component that is removed from a material by the application of an artificial or exaggerated condition (e.g., solvent, temperature, time). A leachable is a chemical component that migrates from a contact surface into a drug product or process fluid during storage or normal use conditions. These are typically a subset of extractables (19, 20).

Potential sources of extractables and leachables include, but are not limited to, membrane components (e.g., plasticizers, surfactants, antioxidants, residual solvents, device support layers), and device components (e.g., end caps, housings, cages, O-rings). Factors affecting leachables may include the chemical properties of the process stream, sterilization process, contact time, temperature, and volume-to-area ratios (21). Filtration of organic solutions may lead to higher levels of leachables than aqueous solutions.

Extractable data may be obtained from the filter manufacturer or may be generated by the filter user (see Section 6.5 for filter user-generated data). Filter manufacturers can use model streams to determine the extractable profiles under worst case conditions (22). Although an important component of filter validation, the level of extractables and leachables from most sterilizing-grade filters that can potentially enter the process stream is relatively low. Flushing the filters prior to use can further reduce these levels as demonstrated by flush curves measured by TOC or other tests for specific leachables (21).

Data should ideally include a comprehensive list of the compounds that could be leachables when the filter is used in the actual formulation under process conditions. The necessity to perform leachable study should be evaluated based on risk determined from extractable studies and risk to patient safety.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Extractables (可萃取物)：在人為加強條件（強溶劑、高溫、長時間）下從材料中被萃取出來的化學物質。這是「最壞情況」的全面清單。

Leachables (可溶出物)：在正常使用或儲存條件下，從接觸表面遷移到藥品中的化學物質。通常是可萃取物的子集 -- 也就是說，在正常條件下實際會跑出來的量比極端條件下少很多。

Volume-to-Area Ratio (體積面積比)：製程液體體積與濾膜接觸面積的比值。比值越小（少量液體接觸大面積濾膜），單位體積中的可溶出物濃度可能越高。

Flush Curve (沖洗曲線)：以 TOC 或特定分析方法監測沖洗液中萃取物濃度隨沖洗量變化的趨勢。當曲線降至可接受水平時，即可停止預沖洗。

比喻說明 Analogy

Extractables 和 Leachables 的關係就像洗車：如果你用高壓水槍加清潔劑猛沖（極端條件 = extractables），車身上所有能被沖下來的東西都會下來 -- 灰塵、鳥糞、蠟層、甚至一點點漆。但如果只是下一場普通小雨（正常使用條件 = leachables），可能只會沖掉表面的灰塵。所以 leachables 通常是 extractables 的子集。但你做 extractables 測試的目的就是要先知道「最壞情況」，再來評估「正常情況」的風險。

重點提示 Key Notes

有機溶劑的特殊風險：原文指出過濾有機溶液可能導致更高水平的可溶出物。這對使用有機溶劑的製程（如某些小分子藥物的最終過濾步驟）尤為重要。濾膜材質的選擇和 E&L 研究設計都需要特別考量。

患者安全是最終考量：是否需要執行可溶出物研究，最終取決於對患者安全的風險評估。即使可萃取物水平看起來很低，如果其中包含已知的致癌物或基因毒性物質，仍需進行詳細的可溶出物研究。這再次印證了決策層級中「無菌保證/患者安全」永遠排在第一位。

公式與計算 Formula

E&L 風險評估的關鍵變數：

可溶出物風險 = f(萃取物種類, 接觸時間, 溫度,
                溶劑強度, 體積面積比, 滅菌方法)

實際可溶出物濃度 (估算) =
  萃取物總量 x 萃取效率因子 x (接觸時間/萃取時間)
  ÷ 藥品體積

其中：有機溶劑的萃取效率因子 > 水溶液的萃取效率因子

  2. 你正在設計一個小批量生物製劑的除菌過濾步驟，批量只有 5L，但使用了標準 10 吋濾芯。從 volume-to-area ratio 的角度，你應該擔心什麼？如何緩解這個風險？
4.6.1 Chemical Compatibility

原文內容 Original Text

The chemical compatibility of the filter device must be evaluated to avoid potential filter damage or alteration and to avoid fluid contamination by either leachables or particulates. Filter manufacturers will have general chemical-compatibility guides based on the MoC and a range of standard chemicals. Since there may be numerous chemical interactions between filter device components and the process fluid or solvents, the typical chemical compatibility table provided by the filter manufacturer is commonly used as a starting point for further testing. More extensive testing should be performed by the end user to determine chemical compatibility of the filter device and process fluid under process conditions (see Section 6.0 for more details).

Chemical-compatibility testing should encompass the entire device and depends on the fluid, filtration temperature, and contact time. Additional studies should be performed by the filter user. Additional product-specific studies may be conducted by the filter vendor on request from the filter user to ensure there is no impact to product CQAs. Common chemical-compatibility tests include integrity test, tensile strength, nonvolatile residue (NVR), extractables, particulates, flow rate, optical or scanning electron microscopy (SEM), burst pressure and membrane/O-ring thickness (23). Use of a combination of tests is recommended since a single test might not be able to detect subtle incompatibilities.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Chemical Compatibility (化學相容性)：濾膜及其所有組件（膜材、外殼、O-ring、支撐層等）與製程液體在實際操作條件下不發生有害化學反應的能力。不相容可能導致：

  • 濾膜結構受損 → 細菌攔截失敗
  • 萃取物/微粒增加 → 產品污染
  • 完整性測試失敗 → 批次報廢

Entire Device（整個裝置）：強調不能只測試濾膜本身，還要包括 O-ring、外殼、端蓋等所有接觸零件。往往是非膜材組件（如 O-ring）最容易出現相容性問題。

比喻說明 Analogy

化學相容性測試就像在買新的保鮮盒裝酸性食物（如番茄醬、醋）之前：你不只需要知道盒體材質耐不耐酸，還要確認蓋子的矽膠密封圈不會被腐蝕、卡扣不會變脆斷裂。濾膜製造商的相容性表就像保鮮盒包裝上的「適用食材」列表 -- 它只是起點。如果你要裝的是一種特殊的辣椒醬（專屬藥品配方），你可能需要自己做實際測試才能放心。

重點提示 Key Notes

多測試方法組合的必要性：原文明確建議使用「多種測試的組合」，因為單一測試可能無法檢測到微妙的不相容性。例如：

  • 完整性測試可能通過，但 SEM 顯示膜表面已有微觀損傷
  • 流速沒有變化，但 NVR（不揮發性殘留物）顯著增加，代表有物質溶出
  • 拉伸強度下降表示材料正在降解，即使其他指標看起來正常

這就是為什麼化學相容性評估是一個綜合性的、多維度的評估過程。

  2. 某客戶的藥品配方含有 20% 乙醇。從化學相容性的角度，你會特別關注濾器的哪些組件？為什麼？
4.7 Operational Ranges

原文內容 Original Text

Manufacturers typically specify the maximum operational temperature, pressure, and sterilization temperature limits and provide water flow-rate data for their filters. Manufacturers provide maximum forward and reverse differential pressure limits at specific temperature conditions, incorporating adequate safety margins. This information may also reference limits at different temperatures, facilitating the selection of process flow rates, temperatures, and sterilization options compatible with the filter. Section 8.0 addresses selection and validation of sterilization options by the filter user.

Filter-system flow rate at a given differential pressure is a function of membrane polymer and structure type, pore size, housing inlet and outlet diameter, effective filter area, fluid temperature and viscosity. During the design of a process filtration system, these variables should be evaluated for compatibility with the operating limits of the filters and the filter area selected. Filter suppliers also test pressure-pulsation cycles to determine the mechanical stability of the filter device. However, care should be taken to avoid pressures exceeding the manufacturer's stated specifications of maximum allowable differential pressure at specified temperature ranges.

Note: There may be a distinction between the operational ranges with which a filter is generally suitable, and the "validated filtration parameters" for a sterilizing filtration unit operation, as defined in process-specific bacterial retention study validation in order to cover scale-down worst-case process conditions (see Section 6.0).

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Differential Pressure (差壓)：濾膜上游與下游之間的壓力差。差壓太高可能損壞濾膜結構，導致完整性失敗；差壓太低則流速不足。製造商會提供在不同溫度下的最大允許差壓。

Operational Range vs. Validated Parameters（操作範圍 vs. 驗證參數）：這是一個關鍵區分 --

  • 操作範圍：製造商說「這個濾膜可以在 X 溫度、Y 壓力下使用」-- 這是通用的硬體極限
  • 驗證參數：你在自己的製程中，經過細菌攔截驗證後確認的實際可用參數範圍 -- 通常比操作範圍更窄

Pressure-Pulsation Cycles (壓力脈動循環)：模擬實際操作中壓力的波動變化（如泵浦造成的脈動），測試濾膜在反覆壓力變化下的機械穩定性。

比喻說明 Analogy

操作範圍和驗證參數的區別就像汽車的設計時速和你在特定道路上的安全駕駛速度：車廠說這輛車最高時速可達 250 km/h（操作範圍），但在你家附近的山路上，經過實際測試後，安全速度可能只有 80 km/h（驗證參數）。超過這個速度不一定會翻車，但你無法保證安全 -- 因為你只驗證到 80 km/h。同理，濾膜製造商的操作範圍是「硬體不會壞」的極限，但你的「除菌保證」只在你驗證過的參數範圍內有效。

重點提示 Key Notes

溫度與壓力的交互作用：這是一個常被忽略的重點。最大允許差壓通常會隨溫度升高而降低。例如，某濾膜在 25°C 時最大差壓是 5 bar，但在 80°C 時可能只有 2.5 bar。如果你在高溫條件下過濾卻使用了常溫的壓力限制，可能會損壞濾膜。

SIP（就地滅菌）的極端條件：SIP 過程中的高溫（通常 121-134°C）和蒸汽壓力對濾膜是最嚴苛的考驗。滅菌溫度限制和操作溫度限制可能不同 -- 務必區分。

公式與計算 Formula

流速影響因素：

Flow Rate = f(ΔP, A_eff, μ, T, d_pore, d_inlet/outlet)

其中：
  ΔP    = 差壓 (Differential Pressure)
  A_eff = 有效過濾面積 (EFA)
  μ     = 液體黏度 (隨溫度變化)
  T     = 溫度 (影響黏度和最大差壓)
  d_pore = 孔徑大小
  d_inlet/outlet = 外殼進出口口徑

注意：溫度升高 → 黏度降低 → 流速增加
      但同時 → 最大允許差壓降低 → 操作窗口縮小

  1. 你的製程在 60°C 下過濾高黏度蛋白質溶液。在選擇操作壓力時，你需要考量哪些互相矛盾的因素？如何找到最佳操作點？
  2. 為什麼原文特別提到「forward and reverse differential pressure limits」？在什麼情況下會出現反向差壓？這對濾膜完整性有什麼風險？
4.8 Shelf Life and Shipping Studies

原文內容 Original Text

Filter manufacturers will validate packaging and shipping of the filters according to industry standards to ensure that the products can withstand common transport hazards (24). In addition, filter manufacturers will establish shelf-life recommendations based on product stability under defined conditions, which will be valid if the filter is stored and handled properly. These recommendations can be used by the filter user to establish expiration dates or use-by dates.

Filters that are sterilized via gamma or x-ray irradiation will have expiration dates due to the sterility claim associated with the sterilization method.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Shelf Life (保存期限)：濾膜在製造商建議的儲存條件下，能維持其品質特性和性能規格的期間。超過保存期限的濾膜不應使用於藥品製造。

Sterility Claim (無菌聲明)：經 gamma 或 x-ray 輻射滅菌的濾器具有「預滅菌」聲明，此聲明有時效性 -- 因為包裝材料會隨時間老化，可能喪失無菌屏障功能。因此這類濾器一定有明確的有效期。

Shipping Validation (運輸驗證)：依據 ISTA 或 ASTM D4169 等標準，模擬運輸過程中的振動、跌落、壓縮等風險，確保濾器在送達使用者手中時仍完好無損。

比喻說明 Analogy

濾器的保存期限管理就像超市食品的有效期：牛奶（經輻射滅菌的囊式濾器）有明確的有效日期，過期就不能喝；罐頭（未預滅菌的不鏽鋼濾芯）保存期限較長，但仍然有限制。而運輸驗證就像網購易碎品的包裝測試 -- 供應商必須確保產品能承受快遞運輸過程中的各種碰撞和擠壓，安全送到你手上。

重點提示 Key Notes

CDMO 庫存管理的注意事項：

  • 先進先出 (FIFO)：嚴格執行物料輪替，避免過期報廢
  • 儲存條件監控：溫度、濕度超出範圍可能加速材料老化，縮短實際保存期限
  • 收貨檢驗：進貨時應檢查包裝完整性，確認運輸過程中未受損
  • 輻射滅菌濾器的特殊管理：這些濾器的有效期通常比未滅菌濾器短，需要更積極的庫存管理

在多產品 CDMO 中，不同客戶可能指定不同品牌/型號的濾器，庫存品項繁多 -- 過期報廢是實際且常見的成本浪費。

實務應用 Practical Application

供應鏈策略：COVID-19 疫情期間，全球單次使用組件供應鏈嚴重中斷，許多藥廠面臨濾器缺貨危機。建議 CDMO 採取：

  1. 建立安全庫存（但要平衡過期風險）
  2. 驗證至少兩個供應商的替代濾器
  3. 與供應商建立長期合約和優先供貨協議
  4. 考慮 consignment stock（寄售庫存）模式降低資金占壓

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 如果一批 gamma 滅菌的囊式濾器在倉庫中被發現儲存溫度曾超過建議上限（但仍在保存期限內），你會如何處理？可以直接使用嗎？
  2. 從供應鏈韌性 (supply chain resilience) 的角度，使用 SUS 濾器系統相比傳統不鏽鋼系統有什麼額外的風險？如何緩解？

本節重點回顧 Section Summary

  • 責任劃分明確：濾膜製造商負責確效 (Q) 和批次放行 (L)，使用者負責製程驗證 (V) 和額外評估 (E)。Table 4.1-1 是最重要的責任分工參考表。
  • 濾膜設計影響性能：褶皺密度決定 EFA（有效過濾面積），進而影響流速和處理量。Scale-up 時須注意 PD 階段與量產階段濾器幾何設計的差異。
  • SUS 的雙面性：一次性使用系統消除了清洗驗證和交叉污染風險，但帶來了供應鏈依賴、廢棄物管理和 E&L 評估的新挑戰。
  • E&L 是層層遞進的：先做可萃取物 (Extractables) 研究建立最壞情況基線，再根據風險評估決定是否需要可溶出物 (Leachables) 研究。預沖洗可有效降低兩者的水平。
  • 化學相容性需全面評估：必須測試整個裝置（不只是膜材），使用多種測試方法的組合，在實際製程條件下進行。
  • 操作範圍 ≠ 驗證參數：製造商的操作範圍是硬體極限，使用者的驗證參數（來自細菌攔截驗證）才是除菌保證的依據。
  • 保存期限管理不可忽視：尤其是 gamma/x-ray 滅菌的濾器有明確有效期，需嚴格執行 FIFO 和儲存條件監控。
  • 決策層級再次確認：所有選擇（材質、型式、供應商）最終都回歸到：無菌保證 > 產品 CQA > 商業彈性的優先順序。
