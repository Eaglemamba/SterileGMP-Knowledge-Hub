重點提示 Key Notes — 最關鍵術語

Reprocessing vs. Reworking — 法規路徑的分水嶺

Reprocessing（再加工）：將中間體或 API 重新引入已建立的製程中，重複相同的步驟（如再過濾、再濃縮）。在生物製劑中，通常限於設備技術性失敗（如層析管柱機械故障）所導致的少數情況。

Reworking（返工）：對不符規格的中間體或 API 施以不同於原既定製程的步驟，以達到可接受品質（如以不同溶劑再結晶，或額外的層析純化步驟）。風險更高，通常需要預先核准的協議（pre-approved protocol）。

→ 決策原則：能用 Reprocessing 解決的，避免走 Reworking；走 Reworking 必須有完整的可比較性（Comparability）數據支撐。

Design Space（設計空間）：在批准的範圍內操作不構成變更；超出設計空間才啟動法規變更流程（post-approval change process）。

Prior Approval（事前核准）：

  • 臨床期產品：EMA 要求實質性修改事先核准；FDA 要求提交 information amendment。
  • 商業上市產品：EMA 要求 Type II variation；FDA 要求 Prior Approval Supplement (PAS) to BLA。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 一批生物製劑的病毒過濾步驟因過濾器洩漏中斷，品保決定以同規格新過濾器重新過濾該批次。這屬於 Reprocessing 還是 Reworking？依據本節定義，應啟動什麼法規動作？
  2. 某 mAb 批次宿主細胞蛋白（HCP）略超規格，擬增加一步預先驗證的額外層析純化。這屬於哪一類操作？你需要準備哪些文件？

Acceptance Criteria 驗收標準 Numerical limits, ranges, or other suitable measures for acceptance of the results of analytical procedures which the drug substance or drug product or materials at other stages of their manufacture should meet. (Source 2)
Action Limit 行動標準 An internal (in-house) value used to assess the consistency of the process at less critical steps. (Source 2)
Active Pharmaceutical Ingredient (API) / Drug Substance 活性藥物成分 / 原料藥 Any substance or mixture of substances intended to be used in the manufacture of a drug product and that, when used in the production of a drug, becomes an active ingredient of the drug product. Such substances are intended to furnish pharmacological activity or other direct effect in the diagnosis, cure, mitigation, treatment or prevention of disease, or to affect the structure and function of the body. (Source 3)
Contaminants 污染物 Any adventitiously introduced materials (e.g., chemical, biochemical, or microbial species) not intended to be part of the manufacturing process of the drug substance or drug product. (Source 2)
Note: Contaminants are distinct from Impurities — see below.
Critical Process Parameter (CPP) 關鍵製程參數 A process parameter whose variability has an impact on a critical quality attribute and therefore should be monitored or controlled to ensure the process produces the desired quality. (Source 4)
Critical Quality Attribute (CQA) 關鍵品質屬性 A physical, chemical, biological, or microbiological property or characteristic that should be within an appropriate limit, range, or distribution to ensure the desired product quality. CQAs are generally associated with the drug substance, intermediates, excipients, in-process materials and drug product. (Source 4)
Design of Experiments (DOE) 實驗設計 An applied statistical method that deals with planning, conducting, analyzing, and interpreting controlled tests to evaluate the factors that control a process parameter output.
Design Space 設計空間 The multidimensional combination and interaction of input variables (e.g., material attributes) and process parameters that have been demonstrated to provide assurance of quality. Working within the design space is not considered as a change. Movement out of the design space is considered to be a change and would normally initiate a regulatory post-approval change process. Design space is proposed by the applicant and is subject to regulatory assessment and approval. (Source 4)
Drug Product (Dosage Form; Finished Product) 藥品製劑（劑型；成品） A pharmaceutical product type that contains a drug substance, generally, in association with excipients. (Source 2)
Drug Substance 原料藥 The material which is subsequently formulated with excipients to produce the drug product. It can be composed of the desired product, product-related substances, and product- and process-related impurities. It may also contain excipients including other components such as buffers. (Source 2)
Failure Mode and Effects Analysis (FMEA) 失效模式與影響分析 Provides for an evaluation of potential failure modes for processes and their likely effect on outcomes and/or product performance. Once failure modes are established, risk reduction can be used to eliminate, contain, reduce or control the potential failures. FMEA relies on product and process understanding and methodically breaks down complex processes into manageable steps. (Sources 5, 6; see IEC 60812)
Impurity 雜質 Any component present in the drug substance or drug product which is not the desired product, a product-related substance, or excipient including buffer components. It may be either process- or product-related. (Source 2)
Note: Impurities are to be distinguished from Contaminants (defined above).
In-process Control (or Process Control) 製程中管控 Checks performed during production to monitor and, if appropriate, to adjust the process and/or to ensure that the intermediate or API conforms to its specifications. (Source 3)
Intermediate Drug Substance 中間體原料藥 A material produced during steps of the processing of an API that undergoes further molecular change or purification before it becomes an API. (Source 3)
Note: "Intermediate" in this document applies to both intermediates of the drug substance (API) and intermediates of the drug product, unless otherwise specified.
Drug Product (Intermediate context) 藥品製劑中間體 A material that is the result of a drug substance having undergone at least one processing step in the presence of any other substance(s) which must undergo further processing step(s) to become the finished dosage form. (Source 7)
Prior Approval 事前核准 Regulatory requirement to approve a change that has a substantial potential to have an adverse effect on the identity, strength, quality, purity or potency of the drug product. (Sources 6, 8, 9)
Clinical/Investigational: EMA requires prior approval of substantial modifications; FDA requires information amendments.
Commercial/Marketed: EMA requires Type II variations; FDA requires a Prior Approval Supplement (PAS) to the BLA.
Process Validation 製程驗證 The collection and evaluation of data, from the process design stage through commercial production, which establishes scientific evidence that a process is capable of consistently delivering quality products; or the documented evidence that the process, operated within established parameters, can perform effectively and reproducibly to produce a medicinal product meeting its predetermined specifications and quality attributes. (Sources 10, 11)
Prospective Process Validation 前瞻性製程驗證 Validation conducted prior to the distribution of either a new product or a product made under a revised manufacturing process where the revisions may affect the product's characteristics. (Source 3)
Reprocessing 再加工 Introducing an intermediate or API, including one that does not conform to standards or specifications, back into the process and repeating a crystallization step or other appropriate chemical or physical manipulation steps (e.g., distillation, filtration, chromatography, milling) that are part of the established manufacturing process. (Source 3)
Note: In biological manufacturing, usually restricted to some refiltration or re-concentration steps upon technical failure of equipment or mechanical breakdown of a chromatography column.
Reworking 返工 Subjecting an intermediate or API that does not conform to standards or specifications to one or more processing steps that are different from the established manufacturing process to obtain acceptable quality intermediate or API (e.g., recrystallizing with a different solvent). (Source 3)
Note: Under some extraordinary situations, a biological bulk drug substance with host cell protein levels slightly above specification limits may be reworked via an additional, validated chromatography step under a pre-approved protocol.
Specification 規格 A list of tests, references to analytical procedures, and appropriate acceptance criteria which establish the set of criteria to which a drug substance, drug product, or materials at other stages of its manufacture should conform to be considered acceptable for its intended use. Specifications are critical quality standards proposed and justified by the manufacturer and approved by regulatory authorities as conditions of approval. (Source 2)
Unit Operation (or Process Step) 單元操作（製程步驟） A discrete step or manipulation in a manufacturing process where process and operating parameters are defined to achieve a specific process objective. (e.g., chromatography, depth filtration, virus filtration, ultra-filtration/diafiltration, bioburden reduction filtration, freeze/thaw formulation and filling)
Validation 驗證 A documented program that provides a high degree of assurance that a specific process, method, or system will consistently produce a result meeting predetermined acceptance criteria. (Source 3)

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

本縮寫表有幾個值得特別標記的項目：

  • EC（Established Conditions，既定條件）：這是 ICH Q12 框架的核心概念，代表被核准的製程條件。任何 EC 的變更都需要啟動法規通知或申請流程，是 TR74 中反覆出現的關鍵詞。
  • CP（Comparability Protocol，可比較性協議）：在 Reworking 或製程變更後，用來證明產品品質未受不利影響的預先建立框架。
  • PACMP（Post-Approval Change Management Protocol）：上市後變更管理協議，允許在事先核准的框架下實施低風險變更，是提升 CDMO 製程靈活性的重要工具。
  • MAH（Manufacturing Authorization Holder，製造授權持有人）：在 CDMO 情境下，MAH 通常是客戶（品牌廠），而非 CDMO 本身。法規責任歸屬的釐清至關重要。

本節重點回顧 Section Summary

  • Reprocessing vs. Reworking：前者在既定製程內重複步驟（如再過濾），後者引入不同步驟（如額外層析）。兩者的法規影響截然不同，Reworking 需要更嚴格的事前核准與可比較性評估。
  • Design Space 的邊界：在批准的設計空間內操作不算製程變更；一旦超出即觸發 post-approval change process。這是 TR74 中「re-」系列操作合規性判斷的基礎框架。
  • Prior Approval 的雙軌制：臨床期與商業上市產品面對不同的 EMA/FDA 要求，CDMO 在支援客戶 MAH 進行任何再加工或返工前，必須確認客戶所處的法規軌道。
  • 決策層級提醒：無論是 Reprocessing 或 Reworking，產品 CQA（無菌保證、效價、純度）始終優先於商業與時間壓力，不得妥協。
