4.0 Introduction to the Regulatory Framework 法規架構導論

原文內容 Original Text

Guidelines published by ICH contain harmonized guidance specifically for to-be-marketed and marketed pharmaceuticals, including biologicals, specifically for submissions involving reprocessing of biologicals. Some of the common characteristics harmonized to enable efficient reprocessing submissions include:

  • To-Be-Marketed: Initial market application to contain details of any anticipated future reprocessing in the relevant sections of the Common Technical Document (CTD) referred to as a Post-Approval Change Management Protocol (PACMP). Allowing this inclusion of reprocessing protocols for relevant steps provides reprocessing flexibility and minimizes post-approval reporting.
  • Marketed: Post-market clear guidance and well-defined timeframes for review and approval of Post-Approval Changes (PAC), as well as PACMPs.
  • Acceptance of science- and risk-based approaches for developing data to support PACs and PACMPs.
  • Relatively fewer requests for additional documentation not included in CTD Module 3.

Table 4.2-1 provides guidance on reprocessing/reworking in the ICH guidelines.

"Allowing this inclusion of reprocessing protocols for relevant steps provides reprocessing flexibility and minimizes post-approval reporting."

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

PACMP (Post-Approval Change Management Protocol，上市後變更管理計畫)： 在初次申請上市許可（NDA/BLA/MAA）時，主動在 CTD 中預先聲明未來可能執行的 reprocessing 步驟及其可接受標準。審查機關預先審核後，日後若實際發生，僅需 Annual Report（AR）或 CBE 層級即可，而非 PAS。這是主動規劃、降低未來監管負擔的核心策略。

CTD（Common Technical Document，通用技術文件）：ICH M4 規範的全球統一申請格式，Module 3 為 CMC 資料，是 reprocessing 協議主要存放位置。

比喻說明 Analogy

PACMP 就像建築師在設計圖審查時，就先取得「未來改建預審許可」。房子蓋好後若按圖改建，不用再重新申請建照，只需備案。若未事先審查就擅自改建，則需完整重新審查（= PAS）。在生物製劑 reprocessing 上，這個時間差可能意味著數月甚至一年的延誤。

重點提示 Key Notes

ICH 協調框架的核心效益：同一套 PACMP 可同時支援 FDA BLA 及 EMA MAA 申請，大幅降低 CDMO 在多市場服務 Sponsor 時的文件重工量。台灣 TFDA 接受 PIC/S 標準，而 PIC/S 主要參照 EMA 架構——因此 PACMP 設計時應以 EMA 要求為基準，確認 FDA 兼容性。

4.2 Regulatory Guidance 法規指引（ICH 指引彙整）

原文內容 Original Text

See Table 4.2-1 below for a comprehensive overview of guidance on reprocessing/reworking across relevant ICH guidelines. Key ICH guidelines covered include ICH Q7 (GMP for APIs), ICH Q9(R1) (Quality Risk Management), and ICH Q13 (Continuous Manufacturing).

Under ICH Q7, the following principles apply:

  • Reprocessing of APIs: Repeating a crystallization step or other appropriate chemical or physical manipulation steps is generally considered acceptable. However, if such reprocessing is used for a majority of batches, it should be included as part of the standard manufacturing process.
  • Continuation after in-process control: Continuation of a process step after an in-process control test has shown that the step is incomplete is considered to be part of the normal process — NOT reprocessing.
  • Unreacted material: Introducing unreacted material back into a process and repeating a chemical reaction is considered reprocessing unless it is part of the established process. Such reprocessing should be preceded by careful evaluation to ensure product quality is not adversely impacted.

Under ICH Q7 Reworking:

  • Before a decision is taken to rework batches, an investigation into the reason for non-conformance should be performed.
  • Reworked batches should be subjected to appropriate evaluation, testing, stability testing if warranted, and documentation.
  • Concurrent validation is often the appropriate validation approach for rework procedures.
  • Procedures should provide for comparing the impurity profile of each reworked batch against batches manufactured by the established process.

Under ICH Q7 Q&A: It may be acceptable to reprocess or rework expired API where the API manufacturer has all related historical GMP documentation and additional stability data. There may be registration/filing considerations beyond the scope of ICH Q7.

Under ICH Q9(R1): Quality Risk Management applies to materials management — assessment and evaluation of suppliers and contract manufacturers, use of materials, and to determine appropriateness of reprocessing, reworking, use of returned goods.

Under ICH Q13 (Continuous Manufacturing): In the event of a filter failure, a clear strategy for material diversion and refiltration (reprocessing) should be defined. Filtration steps in CM may be subject to longer filtration periods — control strategy must allow for filter changes and pre/post-use integrity testing without interrupting the process.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

ICH Q7 的重要區分：「製程步驟尚未完成時繼續執行」≠ reprocessing。例如：過濾尚未達到目標流量，繼續過濾屬正常製程；但若過濾失敗後重新過濾（refiltration），則屬 reprocessing。此區分對無菌充填步驟的 CDMO 至關重要。

Concurrent Validation（同步驗證）：不等待三批完整驗證批次，而是在 reworking 批次生產同時執行驗證。適用於一次性或極少數批次的 rework 情境，但需要預先定義接受標準。

比喻說明 Analogy

ICH Q7 對 reprocessing vs. 正常製程的區分，就像餐廳廚師：
• 牛排還沒熟，繼續烤 → 正常製程（non-reprocessing）
• 牛排煎焦了，切除焦皮重新調味 → reworking
• 牛排快焦時取出冷卻後再烤 → reprocessing（若事先定義在 SOP 中則可接受）

重點提示 Key Notes

過期 API 的 reprocessing（ICH Q7 Q&A）：技術上可行，但必須同時滿足：(1) 完整 GMP 歷史紀錄、(2) 額外穩定性數據、(3) 確認是否需要補充申報。台灣 CDMO 若為 Sponsor 提供此類服務，務必在 QTA 中明確責任歸屬——申報義務仍在 MAH（上市許可持有人），不在 CMO。

ICH Q13 與連續製造：隨著 CM 技術導入生物製劑，過濾失敗後的 refiltration 策略需在 CPMP（Continuous Process Monitoring Plan）中預先定義，不可事後補救。

實務應用 Practical Application

台灣某 CDMO 在批量配製後的無菌過濾（sterile filtration）發生 integrity test 失敗。此情況下：
(1) 若 BLA 或 PACMP 中已有預先核准的 refiltration 協議 → 屬 reprocessing（approved protocol），申報層級較低（AR 或 CBE）
(2) 若無預先核准協議 → 屬 reprocessing without approved protocol → PAS 層級（需 FDA 批准後才能放行）
決策原則：Sterility（無菌保證）優先 → 不確定時必須向 Regulatory Authority 確認

4.3 Specific Guidance from the United States (FDA) 美國 FDA 特定指引

原文內容 Original Text

In addition to implementation of the ICH Guidelines, the FDA provides general guidance for biologicals during the clinical investigation period and specific guidance for biologicals during the post-market approval period.

4.3.1 During Clinical Investigation Period

In 2008, FDA published Guidance for Industry: CGMP for Phase 1 Investigational Drugs that stressed the importance of documenting how the manufacturing process was carried out during the clinical investigational period:

  • A record of manufacturing data that details the materials, equipment, procedures used, and any problems encountered during manufacturing. Manufacturers should retain records sufficient to replicate the manufacturing process.
  • A record of changes in procedures and processes used for subsequent batches along with the rationale for any changes.

Since reprocessing and/or reworking are significant changes, they are required to be reported to the FDA under an information amendment to the Investigational New Drug (IND) Application on file to carry out the human clinical trials (21 CFR 312.31). Although there is no required time period of delay of implementation for these information amendments, manufacturers typically wait for a period of time to allow the FDA reviewers opportunity to review the amendment.

Note: "Consider the impact of changes to be discussed with regulatory authorities in order to determine the need for additional toxicology studies."

FDA in 2020 published Guidance for Industry: CMC Information for Human Gene Therapy Investigational New Drug Applications (INDs). While there is no mention of reworking, the following guidance for reprocessing applies:

  • Drug Substance: For early stage INDs, there may be differences between manufacturing of toxicology lots and clinical study material. For later stage INDs, process changes or reprocessing step(s) may exist. Describe how manufacturing differences are expected to impact product safety and activity; provide batch analysis in CTD Section 3.2.S.4.4.
  • Drug Product: Under certain conditions, repeating sterile filtration (if applicable) may be critical. Clearly describe the conditions and justifications for each reprocessing procedure and demonstrate product consistency between reprocessed lots and normal production lots.

4.3.2 Post-Market Approval Period

Under 21 CFR 601.12, each post-approval change in the production process established in the approved BLA must be reported using the submission type associated with one of the three tier-based reporting categories. This depends on the potential (i.e., risk) of the change to have an adverse effect on the identity, strength, quality, purity, or potency of the product. The submission types are:

  • Prior Approval Supplement (PAS): Change that has a substantial potential to have an adverse effect on product quality (major changes). A PAS must be approved by FDA prior to distribution of the product manufactured using the change (21 CFR 601.12(b)).
  • Changes Being Effected in 30 Days / CBE (CBE-30/CBE): Changes that have a moderate potential to have an adverse effect on product quality. Report at least 30 days prior to distribution (21 CFR 601.12(c)).
  • Annual Report (AR): Changes that have a minimal potential to have an adverse effect. Documented by the applicant in an annual report (21 CFR 601.12(d)).

In 1997, FDA published Guidance for Industry: Changes to an Approved Application for Specified Biotechnology and Specified Synthetic Biological Products. Key provision: reprocessing without a previously approved reprocessing protocol is considered a change with substantial potential to adversely affect product quality, safety, or effectiveness — requiring a PAS.

In 2021, FDA published Guidance for Industry: Chemistry, Manufacturing, and Controls Changes to an Approved Application: Certain Biological Products (scope includes vaccines, plasma-derived protein products, cellular and gene therapy products). Table 4.3.2-1 lists the guidance provided for both reprocessing and reworking. See Table 4.3.2-1 below.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

IND Information Amendment（IND 資訊修正案）：在臨床試驗期間，若製造過程發生 reprocessing 或 reworking，需向 FDA 提交 21 CFR 312.31 資訊修正案。重點：無強制等待期，但業界慣例是等待 FDA 審閱機會再繼續。這不同於上市後 PAS 的強制等待。

21 CFR 601.12 三層申報架構：

  • PAS（Prior Approval Supplement）= 大幅變更，需等待 FDA 批准（通常 6–12 個月）
  • CBE-30 = 中度變更，提交後等待 30 天即可放行
  • AR（Annual Report）= 輕微變更，年度報告時一併揭露

重點提示 Key Notes

關鍵裁定：無預先核准協議 = PAS
依 1997 年 FDA 指引，reprocessing without a previously approved protocol 直接歸類為 PAS。這是實務上最常見的陷阱：CDMO 在緊急情況下執行 refiltration，若 BLA 未包含此協議，即觸發 PAS——批次必須扣留至 FDA 批准。
對策：PACMP 預先涵蓋所有預期 reprocessing 步驟。

實務應用 Practical Application — 台灣 CDMO 服務美國 Sponsor

場景：台灣 CDMO 執行 BLA 商業批生產，無菌過濾後 integrity test 失敗，執行 refiltration。

情境 FDA 申報類別 批次放行時機
BLA 含已核准 refiltration 協議 CBE 或 AR 30 天後或年度揭露
BLA 無協議，首次發生 PAS 等待 FDA 批准（≥6 個月）

決策優先順序：Sterility（無菌保證）→ CQA → Business。若過濾失敗批次無菌性存疑，廢棄優先於申請 PAS 放行。

比喻說明 Analogy

PAS / CBE-30 / AR 就像建築改建許可：
• PAS = 大型結構變更，需完整重新審查建照（等待核准才能動工）
• CBE-30 = 中型內部裝修，30 天後若無異議即可動工
• AR = 小型修繕，年底統一申報備案即可
CDMO 的工作就是在設計階段（PACMP）就把所有「可能要做的裝修」預先納入建照，避免緊急時面對 PAS 的漫長等待。

TFDA 對應說明

台灣 TFDA 法規對於製造場址位於台灣的 CDMO，主要參照 PIC/S GMP 規範（EMA 架構），但若 Sponsor 為美國廠商且申請 FDA BLA，則批次申報義務依 21 CFR 601.12 執行，由 MAH（BLA holder，通常為 Sponsor）承擔。CDMO 需在 QTA（品質技術協議）中明確規定：發生 reprocessing 時，CDMO 需在多少工作天內通知 Sponsor，以便 Sponsor 決定申報類別。

4.4 Specific Guidance from the European Union (EMA) 歐盟 EMA 特定指引

原文內容 Original Text

In addition to implementation of the ICH Guidelines, EMA provides specific guidance for biologicals both during the clinical investigation period and the post-market approval period.

4.4.1 During Clinical Investigation Period

EMA in 2022 published the Guideline on the Requirements for Quality Documentation Concerning Biological Investigational Medicinal Products in Clinical Trials – Revision 2. Scope included proteins and polypeptides (produced from recombinant and nonrecombinant cell-culture expression systems), their derivatives and products of which they are components (e.g., conjugates), and auxiliary medicinal products. There is no mention of reworking; however, the following guidance is provided for reprocessing:

  • Drug Substance: Any reprocessing during manufacture of the active substance (e.g. filter integrity test failure) should be described and justified. Reprocessing could be considered in exceptional circumstances. For biological products, these situations are usually restricted to certain refiltration and re-concentration steps upon technical failure of equipment or mechanical breakdown of a chromatography column.
  • Drug Product: Reprocessing may be acceptable for particular manufacturing steps (e.g. refiltration) only if the steps are adequately described and appropriately justified.

A change to the production process submitted in the investigational medicinal product dossier (IMPD), if it is a substantial modification, must be reported. Substantial modification means any change that is likely to have a substantial impact on the safety and rights of the subjects or on the reliability and robustness of the data generated in the clinical trial. See Table 4.4.1-1 below.

In 2025, EMA published the Guideline on Quality, Non-Clinical and Clinical Requirements for Investigational Advanced Therapy Medicinal Products in Clinical Trials (scope: gene therapy medicinal products, somatic cell therapy medicinal products, tissue-engineered products, combined ATMPs). No mention of reworking; guidance for reprocessing:

  • Drug Substance: Reprocessing could only be considered in exceptional circumstances. Usually restricted to refiltration and re-concentration steps upon technical failure.
  • Drug Product: Reprocessing may be acceptable for particular manufacturing steps (e.g. refiltration) only if adequately described and appropriately justified.

4.4.2 Post-Market Approval Period

Under the EMA Variation Regulation, each post-approval change in the production process established in the approved Marketing Authorisation Application (MAA) must be reported using the submission type associated with one of the three tier-based reporting categories. The submission types are:

  • Type II Major Variation: Change must be approved by EMA prior to distribution of the product manufactured using the change.
  • Type IB Minor Variation: Change must wait 30 days after submission to EMA.
  • Type IA Minor Variation: Change must be submitted within 12 months from the date of implementation.

There is no mention of reworking in the Variation Regulation. Table 4.4.2-1 provides guidance for reprocessing biological manufacturing processes. See Table 4.4.2-1 below.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

IMPD（Investigational Medicinal Product Dossier，試驗性醫療產品檔案）：等同於 FDA IND 的歐盟對應文件。臨床試驗期間，若製造過程發生 reprocessing，需評估是否構成 "substantial modification"；若是，需向成員國主管機關申報。

EMA Variation Regulation 三層分類：

  • Type II（重大變更）≈ FDA PAS
  • Type IB（次要變更）≈ FDA CBE-30
  • Type IA（微小變更）≈ FDA AR（但時間規定不同：12 個月內申報）

重點提示 Key Notes — EMA 對 Reworking 的態度

EMA 指引（2022 臨床期 + Variation Regulation 商業期）均完全不提及 reworking。這並非疏漏，而是 EMA 立場：生物製劑的 reworking 被視為不可接受或極端例外，預設要求 case-by-case 洽詢 EMA，無標準化申報路徑。

相較之下，FDA 2021 年指引雖也對 reworking 列出 N/A，但至少在表格中明確呈現，暗示需個案評估。EMA 連提都不提，代表其態度更為保守。

比喻說明 Analogy — EMA vs. FDA 文化差異

FDA 對 reprocessing/reworking 的態度較為「工程導向」：只要有充分數據支持，就可以找到一條申報路徑。EMA 較為「預防原則導向」：生物製劑的 reworking 幾乎等同於重新生產，preferably avoided。

台灣 CDMO 同時服務 FDA 和 EMA 市場時，應以 EMA 較嚴格的立場為設計基準——若能通過 EMA 的 "exceptional circumstances" 要求，FDA 端通常也可接受。反之則不然。

實務應用 Practical Application — FDA vs. EMA 申報對照

台灣 CDMO 同時為美國和歐洲市場生產同一生物製劑 Drug Product，發生 refiltration（有預先核准協議）：

TFDA (Taiwan) 依 PIC/S 對應 參照 EMA Type IA/IB TFDA 依 PIC/S，偏 EMA 架構

TFDA 說明：台灣 TFDA 藥品 CMC 申報主要依 PIC/S Annex 1 及相關 EMA 指引，對 reprocessing 申報採 EMA 類比框架。若 CDMO 同時持有 Taiwan 藥品許可，應另行確認 TFDA 個案要求。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. EMA 2022 指引將 Drug Substance reprocessing 限定於 "exceptional circumstances"，而 FDA 允許以協議預先核准。這對 CDMO 的 reprocessing 開發策略有何影響？
  2. 若某批次在臨床期間（IMPD 已提交）需要 reprocessing，如何判斷是否構成 EMA 定義的 "substantial modification"？

4.5 Other Guidance 其他法規指引（WHO / PIC/S / BioPhorum / PDA）

原文內容 Original Text

Biological manufacturers wish to operate across many geographical areas, yet not all countries participate in ICH (e.g., Russia). Keeping track of each country's or region's CMC regulatory compliance review procedures and oversight for reprocessing and reworking of biologicals has proven challenging.

Various specialized government agencies and industry associations provide recommendations on effective control and safety when considering reprocessing and/or reworking of biological manufacturing processes:

  • World Health Organization (WHO): A United Nations agency to support health around the world, including the safety of manufactured drugs.
  • Pharmaceutical Inspection Co-Operation Scheme (PIC/S): An informal, nonbinding cooperative arrangement between regulatory authorities in the field of GMPs for medicinal products.
  • BioPhorum: A collaboration of industry leaders and subject matter experts working together to pool knowledge, best practices, and ideas on global biopharmaceutical industry practices.
  • Parenteral Drug Association (PDA): The leading global provider of science, technology, and regulatory information detailed in technical reports and points-to-consider documents.

Overall, carrying out reprocessing or reworking according to the principles indicated in the ICH guidelines and the FDA and EMA guidances is generally a safe approach. However, if there is any doubt about proceeding with reprocessing or reworking a biological manufacturing process, contact the respective regulatory authority for specific guidance.

Note: "If reprocessing or reworking has not been explicitly mentioned in the manufacturer's submitted documentation on the manufacturing process, correctly assume that it has not yet been approved."

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

PIC/S（Pharmaceutical Inspection Co-Operation Scheme）：目前有 57 個成員監管機構，包括 TFDA（台灣）、TGA（澳洲）、Health Canada 等。PIC/S GMP Guide 主要跟隨 EMA GMP 指引架構，並定期與 EMA Annex 同步更新。因此，TFDA GMP 要求本質上與 EMA 高度一致。

PIC/S 非法規機構，無法律約束力，但其成員機構彼此承認 GMP 查廠結論（Mutual Recognition），大幅降低重複稽查負擔。

重點提示 Key Notes — 黃金準則

本節結尾的 Note 是全章最重要的操作原則：

「若 reprocessing 或 reworking 未在已提交的製造過程文件中明確提及，應正確假設其尚未獲批准。」

這意味著：沉默 ≠ 允許。在不確定時，必須聯繫主管機關確認，而非逕行執行並事後報告。CDMO 在接受 Sponsor 授權前，應確認 BLA/MAA/IND 中是否已有明確的 reprocessing 條款。

實務應用 Practical Application — TFDA 與全球 Sponsor 的交集

台灣 CDMO 的監管三角：

  • TFDA（台灣藥品申請）→ PIC/S GMP，參照 EMA 架構
  • FDA（美國 Sponsor BLA）→ 21 CFR 601.12，三層申報制
  • EMA（歐洲 Sponsor MAA）→ Variation Regulation，Type II/IB/IA

實務建議： 1. QTA（品質技術協議）中應明確規定：哪些事件構成 reprocessing，CDMO 的通知義務時限（建議 3–5 工作天），以及由哪方承擔申報責任（通常為 MAH/Sponsor）。
2. CDMO 的 Site Master File（SMF）應包含 reprocessing 的一般框架政策，避免逐一 Sponsor 重複協商。
3. BioPhorum 發布的 reprocessing 最佳實務文件可作為 SOP 撰寫的技術參考。

比喻說明 Analogy

WHO / PIC/S / BioPhorum 就像國際外送平台（Uber Eats）的評分與規則：
• FDA = 美國市場的法律（強制性）
• EMA = 歐盟市場的法律（強制性）
• PIC/S = 國際 GMP 標準（準強制性，因為成員機構相互承認）
• WHO / BioPhorum = 業界最佳實踐建議（非強制，但具重要參考價值）
台灣 CDMO 同時在 FDA 和 EMA 這兩個「市場」服務客戶，必須同時遵守兩套法律，並以 PIC/S 標準確保自身 GMP 狀態。

本節重點回顧 Section Summary

  • ICH 協調框架：PACMP 是預先核准 reprocessing 的黃金工具，可將事後申報從 PAS 降至 AR/Type IA，是 CDMO 設計階段最重要的策略。
  • FDA 三層架構：無協議 = PAS（批次扣留至核准）；有協議 = AR；Reworking = N/A（個案洽詢）。關鍵依據：21 CFR 601.12 及 FDA 1997/2021 年指引。
  • EMA 三層架構：無協議重大 reprocessing = Type II（需核准）；有協議低風險 = Type IA（12 個月內申報）；Reworking 完全未提及（比 FDA 更保守）。
  • TFDA 定位：PIC/S 成員，主要跟隨 EMA 架構。台灣 CDMO 在 QTA 中應明確 reprocessing 通知義務時限與申報責任歸屬。
  • 黃金準則：「文件未提及 = 未核准」。在不確定時，聯繫主管機關優先於任何製造決策。
  • 決策優先順序：無菌保證 → CQA → Business。若無菌性存疑，廢棄優先於申報放行。
