Introduction — 導論

原文內容 Original Text

The COVID-19 pandemic has driven the world to find new technological tools to do business safely without sacrificing quality, efficiency, and effectiveness. This is especially true for the biopharmaceutical industry. While many consider on-site inspections to be the optimal way of assessing compliance, remote inspection tools increasingly are seen as useful ways of obtaining meaningful insights into a site's operations. But what factors should companies and health authorities consider when adjusting to the inspection format selected? How do you determine, address, and plan for the technological hurdles and considerations?

This document examines advantages and best practices for planning and implementing all types of remote inspections, including desktop, virtual, and hybrid inspections. It describes best practices that will help all participants engage efficiently and avoid unnecessary or unexpected delays, stressors, or complications in the remote inspection process. While good planning and preparation by both the inspectors and the inspected entity are important for any inspection, they are vital to an efficient and effective remote inspection. This document therefore presents the topics in order, from planning and notification through closing and response, as illustrated in Figure 1. The suggestions in this document may be useful to sites that are the target of inspections and to health authorities.

If inspections conducted using remote tools can provide thorough and reliable information, health authorities may continue to use them in certain situations in the future. Remote tools present advantages, as they allow health authorities to make optimal use of resources based on risk, allow for the involvement of specialized experts in a way that has not traditionally been possible on-site, and are more environmentally friendly. Issues currently seen as constraints may become less problematic as sites and health authorities gain experience.

In addition, Appendix 2 presents the complete results of a PDA global survey on biopharmaceutical manufacturing experiences related to remote inspections. These results may provide insights about industry's needs and concerns.

"Because health authorities around the world may take different approaches or be governed by specific legal requirements, sites should always consider local laws and practices... This document does not represent a standard or regulatory guidance."

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析：三種遠端稽查類型的光譜 Key Concepts

本文件定義的三種遠端稽查形式，可視為一個複雜度由低至高的連續光譜：

  • Desktop Inspection（桌面稽查）：純文件審閱，書面問答，無實地接觸。複雜度最低，準備工作以書面資料為主。
  • Virtual Inspection（虛擬稽查）：桌面稽查 + 即時視訊串流、螢幕分享或虛擬廠區導覽。稽查員「眼睛進得去，身體進不去」。
  • Hybrid Inspection（混合稽查）：部分遠端 + 部分現場到訪。靈活組合，可同步或分階段執行，並可出具單一或分開的稽查報告。

理解這個光譜非常重要：稽查形式決定了你的準備策略、人員部署與技術基礎設施投入的等級。

比喻說明 Analogy

遠端稽查就像視訊看診 vs. 親自到診所的選擇：

  • 桌面稽查 = 先傳病歷和檢驗報告給醫生，醫生書面回覆問題——醫生完全沒見到你本人。
  • 虛擬稽查 = 視訊看診，醫生透過鏡頭看你的喉嚨、聽你描述症狀——比書面更多資訊，但仍無法觸診。
  • 混合稽查 = 先視訊初診，確認需要後再到診所做精密檢查——兩者互補。
  • 到廠稽查 = 親赴診所，最全面，但也最耗時、耗資源。

重點：目標相同（評估合規性 / 診斷病況），但工具和資訊深度不同。同樣地，遠端稽查不是放棄合規標準，而是用不同工具達到同等目的。

重點提示：本文件的關鍵限制 Key Notes

請特別注意文件中的這句話：「This document does not represent a standard or regulatory guidance.」

這意味著：

  • 本文件屬於 Points to Consider（值得考慮的觀點），是 PDA 的最佳實務建議，並非法規要求。
  • 各國衛生主管機關仍依其本國法律框架行事；本文件的建議不能取代當地法規要求。

Scope — 適用範圍

原文內容 Original Text

This document applies only to regulatory inspections concerning good manufacturing practices (GMP) and good distribution practices (GDP) and provides information relevant to both health authorities and the industry. The recommendations described in this document may be applied to other types of GxP inspections, although those were not specifically considered in the development of this document.

This document is not intended to address remote auditing programs, such as remote supplier audits, vendor audits, internal audits, or self-inspections, although similar challenges apply.

Glossary — 詞彙定義

原文內容 Original Text

This document uses the following definitions. Because of the diversity of terminology used by inspectorates around the world, however, sites should be aware that inspectors may use a different term or definition than stated here.

Desktop Inspection

A type of remote inspection in which documentation (e.g., filing commitments, site description, and key procedures) is requested by the inspecting body that is not physically present at the site(s). Requests and responses to questions are shared in written form (e.g., via email or on a document-sharing platform). There can be several iterations of the requests and review of documentation, questions, and answers. Phone calls and videoconferences take place only rarely to provide clarification about the documents requested or provided.

Note: Some regulatory authorities may use documentation requests and review in advance of an inspection or to update a company's risk profile.

Document-Sharing Platform

A secure software application that facilitates file transfer. Document-sharing platforms allow end users to upload files to a shared storage space and designate who may access the files.

GMP/GDP Inspection

Assessment of the compliance of a site with the applicable GMP/GDP regulations and filing commitments performed by officials of a competent (local/international) health authority.

Hybrid Inspection

An inspection that is conducted remotely in part and also includes an in-person presence and on-site visit by the inspecting body. The remote and on-site aspects may be concurrent or at different times (e.g., a portion of the inspection is performed virtually or desktop, followed by a second portion on-site). The combination of remote and on-site components may be captured in a single inspection report or in separate reports.

Remote Inspection

An inspection in which the inspector is not physically present at the site(s) where the activities subject to the assessment take or have taken place. Remote inspections may take several forms, including desktop, virtual, and hybrid.

Video Communication Platform

Web-conferencing software that allows participants to conduct or attend meetings via the internet. This is also known as online meeting software or videoconferencing. The video communication platform enables remote meetings based on voice over internet protocol (VoIP), online video, instant messaging, file-sharing, and screen-sharing.

Virtual Inspection

In addition to sharing documentation as in any other remote inspection, virtual inspections utilize technologies such as live or real-time streaming video, screen-sharing, or other means of real-time communication. Virtual inspections may include virtual tours of aspects of the inspected site. These inspections also may be referred to as distant assessments or remote interactive evaluations.

重點提示：術語差異——Virtual = Distant Assessment = Remote Interactive Evaluation

文件特別指出，全球稽查機關使用的術語存在差異，這對實務應用非常重要：

  • Virtual Inspection：PDA 本文件使用的標準術語，強調即時通訊技術的運用。
  • Distant Assessment：某些主管機關（如英國 MHRA）偏好使用此詞，強調「距離上的評估」。
  • Remote Interactive Evaluation：另一種表述，強調「互動性」，與 Desktop 的單向書面問答形成對比。

實務建議：在收到主管機關稽查通知時，務必確認對方使用的術語所對應的實際稽查形式，避免依據錯誤的假設進行準備。例如，某 EU 國家主管機關說「Distant Assessment」，你必須確認這是否等同於本文件的 Virtual Inspection（需要視訊設備），還是僅限於桌面文件審閱。

實務應用：CDMO 稽查動員策略 Practical Application

作為 CDMO，收到不同類型的遠端稽查通知時，應採取不同程度的動員策略：

稽查類型 動員等級 主要行動
Desktop Inspection 文件團隊動員 QA / RA 主導文件準備；無需生產線待命；設置文件分享平台；預留書面問答回覆時間（通常數輪迭代）
Virtual Inspection 全廠核心團隊動員 除文件外，需準備視訊設備、網路頻寬、虛擬廠區導覽路線；各部門 SME 待命即時回應；IT 支援視訊平台穩定性；QA 協調即時問題回覆流程
Hybrid Inspection 全廠最高等級動員 同時具備 Desktop + Virtual 準備，加上到廠稽查的全套現場準備；生產線清潔整頓、更衣程序確認、現場接待流程、法務與保安配合

關鍵原則：稽查形式的確認越早越好。一旦確認為 Virtual 或 Hybrid，立即啟動跨部門稽查準備委員會（Inspection Readiness Committee），並按本文件後續章節的建議逐步推進。

本節重點回顧 Section Summary

  • COVID-19 的催化作用：疫情加速了遠端稽查工具的採用，但到廠稽查仍被視為合規評估的最佳方式；遠端工具是「有意義的補充」，而非等量替代。
  • 三種遠端稽查類型：Desktop（純文件書面）→ Virtual（文件 + 即時視訊）→ Hybrid（遠端 + 現場部分到訪），複雜度與準備需求依序遞增。
  • 範圍限定：本文件僅適用於 GMP/GDP 法規稽查，不適用於供應商稽核、內部稽查或自查；其他 GxP 稽查可非正式參考。
  • 術語一致性警示：全球稽查機關術語不統一——Virtual Inspection = Distant Assessment = Remote Interactive Evaluation；收到通知時務必確認對方所指的稽查形式。
  • 文件定位：本文件為 PDA Points to Consider，屬於最佳實務建議，非法規標準；需以當地法律法規要求為最終依據。
  • CDMO 動員策略：Desktop 稽查以文件團隊為主；Virtual 稽查需全廠核心團隊含 IT 和視訊設備待命；Hybrid 稽查需最高等級全廠動員，整合遠端與現場準備。
