5.5 Rapid Microbiological Methods (RMM) in Environmental Monitoring

The industry should look toward utilizing innovative approaches for the detection and quantification of microorganisms as part of a holistic EM program. From a QRM perspective, the benefits of implementing rapid microbiological testing strategies can help to:

  • Design robust processes that prevent contamination
  • Ensure that a state of microbial control is maintained
  • Evaluate the impact of human interventions during aseptic filling
  • Establish recovery time after power shortages
  • Develop more effective strategies to correct a contamination problem
  • Continually improve processes and products
  • Quickly and efficiently assess the potential impact of failing results on the patient

Key applications of RMM within EM programs include:

  • Confirmation of microbial control in controlled areas prior to use (e.g., manufacturing suites, gowning rooms)
  • Rapid assessment of controlled areas during facility qualification activities (e.g., EM PQ)
  • Evaluation of contamination remediation efforts and root cause analysis

Additional detailed guidance may be found in PDA Technical Report No. 33: Evaluation, Validation and Implementation of Alternative and Rapid Microbiological Methods.

中文解說：RMM 導入的戰略意義

TR13 第 5.5 節正式將快速微生物方法（RMM, Rapid Microbiological Methods）納入環境監測（EM）程序的核心討論，這標誌著業界從「傳統培養皿計數」向「即時感測」的典範轉移。

為什麼 RMM 如此重要？傳統培養基法的最大弱點是結果滯後：一般培養基需 3–7 天才出結果，等到發現超標，批次早已前處理甚至出貨。RMM 的核心價值在於縮短「問題發生→偵測→干預」的時間窗口。

RMM 適用的四大 EM 情境

  • 使用前確認：生產前快速確認潔淨室微生物負荷符合要求
  • 廠房確效：EM PQ 期間的快速評估，加速確效時程
  • 汙染溯源：根本原因調查（RCA）中，快速鎖定汙染熱點
  • 消毒效果驗證：清潔/消毒後立即評估處理效果

從品質風險管理（QRM）視角，RMM 提升了微生物控制的即時性，直接保護病患安全，這也是監管機構積極鼓勵創新方法的原因。

5.5.1 Rapid Microbiological Method Scientific Principles Applied to Environmental Monitoring

Rapid methods appropriate for EM are based on a wide variety of scientific principles. Some methods still require growth of microorganisms using conventional microbiological media; however, detection and enumeration can be faster using novel detection techniques:

Growth-dependent RMM (faster detection via novel readout):

  • Digital imaging and autofluorescence of microcolonies
  • Fluorescent staining and laser excitation of microcolonies
  • Rapid detection of carbon dioxide (CO2)
  • Detection of adenosine triphosphate (ATP) after a brief microbial enrichment period

Growth-independent RMM (instantaneous or near real-time):

  • Flow and solid-phase cytometry using viability stains and laser detection systems
  • ATP bioluminescence (when enrichment is not required due to concentration of microorganisms in the sample)
  • Intrinsic fluorescence and optical spectroscopy detection of microorganisms in air or water
  • RAMAN spectroscopy coupled with viability staining
  • Nucleic acid amplification techniques (when enrichment is not required)

Technology selection factors:

  • Sample type and size required for analysis
  • Sample compatibility with RMM workflow and instrumentation
  • Desired time to result
  • Level of sensitivity required
  • Need to neutralize antimicrobial activity (for growth-based methods)
  • Ability to allow identification of recoveries (destructive vs. non-destructive assay)
  • Potential for false positives or negatives due to cleaning or disinfectant residues

Automated and high-throughput methods using conventional media are also available for faster results with better automation, data management, and data integrity.

中文解說：RMM 技術分類與選擇邏輯

TR13 將 RMM 分為兩大類，理解這個架構是選擇正確工具的基礎：

類型一：仍需微生物生長（Growth-Dependent）

這類方法使用傳統培養基，但以更快速的偵測手段縮短等待時間。

  • 數位影像法：偵測微菌落（microcolony），數小時出結果（傳統需 3 天）
  • 自動螢光：利用微生物自發螢光，免去染色步驟
  • CO2 偵測：微生物代謝釋放 CO2，間接定量
  • ATP（富集後）：微生物 ATP 作為活性指標，需短暫富集

類型二：不需生長（Growth-Independent）

直接偵測樣品中的微生物，結果可接近即時。

  • 流式細胞術（Flow Cytometry）：逐一計數活性細胞，速度極快
  • ATP 生物發光：無需富集時可直接偵測，常用於水系統
  • 內源螢光光譜：利用微生物固有螢光特性辨識
  • RAMAN 光譜：結合活性染色，提供分子指紋
  • 核酸擴增（NAT）：PCR 等方法偵測特定基因序列

實務選擇關鍵：沒有萬能的 RMM。選擇時需考慮樣品基質（空氣、表面、水）、是否需要後續菌種鑑定（破壞性 vs. 非破壞性）、以及消毒劑殘留可能造成的假陽性/假陰性干擾。

5.5.2 Benefits of Using Rapid Microbiological Methods

The implementation of rapid methods for EM may present operational enhancements related to product quality, process knowledge, and regulatory acceptance, including:

  • Potential for reduced in-process microbiology testing and finished product release cycle times
  • Reduction in risks associated with forward processing
  • Elimination or reduction of off-line microbiology assays
  • Increases in laboratory automation and reductions in manual testing, sample handling, and data management
  • Reduced overhead and/or headcount for sampling and/or testing
  • Ability to make immediate quality decisions on the state of microbial control
  • Faster response to contamination events and initiation of investigations
  • Reduced repeat testing, lot rejection, reprocessing, and rework
  • Reduction in room or facility downtime and investigations
  • Faster confirmation of contamination remediation efforts

中文解說：RMM 十大效益解析

TR13 列舉的效益，可從三個維度理解：

維度一：時間效益（Speed）

  • 縮短批次放行週期（Release Cycle Time）
  • 加快汙染調查啟動速度
  • 更快確認消毒補救措施效果

維度二：風險效益（Risk Reduction）

  • 降低「前處理風險」（Forward Processing Risk）：傳統法等待結果期間，後續批次可能已進入無菌灌裝，若超標則連帶受影響
  • 減少重複測試、批次拒收、再加工
  • 縮短設施停機時間

維度三：營運效益（Operational）

  • 提升實驗室自動化程度
  • 降低人力成本（採樣與測試人員）
  • 改善數據完整性（Data Integrity）

稽核提示：若公司尚未導入 RMM，稽核員可能詢問是否評估過 RMM 的可行性，以及不導入的科學理由。TR13 的這份效益清單也是業務案例（Business Case）的有力依據。

5.5.3 Validation of Rapid Microbiology Methods and Equipment

Rapid microbiology methods vary in complexity and in the associated equipment required:

  • Equipment/software validation follows the model found in USP <1058> (Analytical Instrument Qualification)
  • Method validation is discussed in compendial chapters: USP <1116> and Ph. Eur. 5.1.6
  • PDA TR No. 33 also provides industry-proven validation strategies

The most recent EU Annex 1 recommends that when a technology presents a result in a manner different from the CFU, the manufacturer should:

  • Scientifically justify the limits applied
  • Where possible, correlate them to the CFU
  • This applies to Grade A areas where "no growth" is expected

Key challenge — non-CFU signal correlation:

If the data signal is a CFU (growth-based RMM), results can be compared with existing alert levels and action limits directly. If the data signal is not a CFU, the quantitative result may not align with existing limits, even though a CFU correlation can be made. In this case, the end-user may:

  • Generate sufficient data to modify existing alert and action levels based on historical data
  • Rationalize and apply new levels using a risk-based analysis

Alternatively, trending EM values may be more appropriate than looking at individual counts — especially in well-controlled environments (e.g., Grade A / ISO 5 areas) where zero cell counts are expected.

Results using growth-independent RMM may be numerically higher than traditional methods, as viable but non-culturable (VBNC), physically stressed, damaged, or dormant organisms may be detected that would not grow on conventional media.

中文解說：RMM 驗證的三層框架與最大挑戰

RMM 驗證的複雜性遠超傳統方法驗證，TR13 清楚指出了三個層次：

驗證層次架構

  • 儀器/設備確效（USP <1058>）：AIQ (IQ/OQ/PQ)
  • 方法驗證（USP <1116>, Ph. Eur. 5.1.6）：靈敏度、專一性、線性、精確度
  • 業界策略指引（PDA TR33）：完整的替代方法評估/驗證/實施框架

最大挑戰：非 CFU 訊號的限值設定問題

傳統 EM 的警戒限（Alert Level）與行動限（Action Limit）都是以 CFU 為單位制定的（參照 EU Annex 1 Table 1）。當 RMM 產生的訊號不是 CFU（例如流式細胞術輸出的「事件數/mL」、ATP 輸出的「RLU」），就無法直接套用現行限值。

兩種解決路徑：

  • 建立 CFU 相關性曲線 → 轉換為等效 CFU 後比較（數學上可行，但統計挑戰大）
  • 用足夠的歷史數據重新制定該 RMM 的專屬警戒/行動限（較實際，需風險評估支持）

VBNC 議題：不需培養的 RMM 可偵測到「有活性但不可培養」的微生物，因此計數往往高於傳統法，這不代表環境變差，而是偵測靈敏度提升的結果。

Grade A 的特殊考量：在預期「零生長」的環境中，趨勢分析（Trending）比單一計數更有意義，因為任何正值都應觸發調查，而不必等到超過 action limit。

5.6 Identification of Isolates

Identifying and strain-typing of microorganisms is not only important as part of cleanroom qualification or when related to EM excursions, but should also form an integral part of routine surveillance monitoring programs.

The identification program selected by the laboratory should be defined in writing, including:

  • The frequency of identification
  • Standard procedures for the methods used
  • Consistency with regulatory expectations for identifications

Companies relying on contract laboratories for microbial identification should qualify them per supplier qualification procedures and have a quality agreement in place.

The identification of microorganisms forms an important element to assess microbial state of control, allowing:

  • Detection of any microbial changes and potential seasonal variations of the flora
  • Definition of potential sources of isolates related to a detected contamination (e.g., as part of an investigation)
  • Assessment of the level of contamination control
  • Response upon recovery of undesirable organisms that might pose particular risks

Grade A/B considerations: The Grade A environment is expected to be completely free of viable organisms. In the surrounding Grade B environment, spore-forming organisms (mold, Bacillus spp.) and water-borne Gram-negative organisms are of particular concern. Gram-negative organisms may indicate water intrusion, increasing the risk of mold proliferation.

Grade C/D: Levels of undesirable organisms should be controlled to limit microbial stress on surrounding aseptic cleanrooms.

Table 5.6-1: Typical Scheme for Microbial Identification

Extent of Identification Isolate and Origin
Identification to species
(or genus where speciation is not possible)
  • All recoveries in Grades A & B
  • Grades C & D, for alert level or action limit excursions
  • Periodically as part of the ongoing surveillance program
Strain typing or molecular fingerprinting Significant product failure (e.g.):
  • Media fills
  • Sterility test failure
  • Significant adverse trends

Initially, many isolates may be identified to establish a database of representative microorganisms found in the area. Periodic identifications should be performed even if no excursions have occurred in Grades C and D. A change in the microflora might signify a change in environmental controls that should be investigated.

Common sources by organism type:

  • Staphylococcus spp. — commonly found on skin
  • Pseudomonas spp. — usually associated with water sources
  • Bacillus spp. and mold — generally connected to soil, dust, and air

中文解說：微生物鑑定的分層策略與溯源應用

微生物鑑定是 EM 程序中最具科學深度的環節之一。Table 5.6-1 提供了一個清晰的三層策略框架：

三層鑑定策略

  • 第一層：全面鑑定至種（Species Level）
    適用：Grade A/B 所有回收菌；Grade C/D 超標時；定期監測採樣
    方法：MALDI-TOF MS、16S rRNA 定序、生化鑑定
  • 第二層：週期性鑑定
    適用：Grade C/D 例行監測（即使未超標也需定期執行）
    目的：建立環境微菌相資料庫，偵測微菌相漂移（Microflora Shift）
  • 第三層：菌株分型（Strain Typing）
    適用：重大產品失敗（Media Fill、無菌測試失敗、重大不良趨勢）
    方法：MLST（多位點序列分型）、PFGE（脈衝場凝膠電泳）、全基因組定序（WGS）

比喻：鑑定就是微生物的「身分證+家族DNA」

鑑定到種（species）就像確認一個人的「身分」；菌株分型（strain typing）則像做「家族DNA比對」，可以確定兩個地方分離的菌是否為同一株（即同一汙染源）。當 Media Fill 失敗，我們需要的不只是「這是葡萄球菌」，而是「它與操作員手套上的菌是同一株嗎？」

汙染來源溯源（Source Attribution）的實際應用：

  • 分離到 Pseudomonas spp. → 立即懷疑水系統（WFI、純水）或清潔用水
  • 分離到 Staphylococcus epidermidis → 聚焦人員更衣、手套、口罩
  • 分離到 Aspergillus spp. (黴菌) → 檢查空氣過濾、外包材料帶入
  • 微菌相季節性變化 → 可能反映戶外空氣品質、植物花粉季節影響

委外鑑定的監管要求：若使用合約實驗室，必須完成供應商資格評估（Supplier Qualification）並簽訂品質協議（Quality Agreement），確保鑑定方法與精確度符合自身 SOP 要求。

5.7 Investigations and Corrective Actions

Investigations are needed in response to an action-limit excursion and to address an adverse trend in order to determine a cause-and-effect relationship or most probable cause (i.e., sources of contamination). Investigative and corrective action steps should be specified in a written procedure.

The procedure should define the level of investigation required if there are multiple or sequential excursions. The investigation should include:

  • A product impact assessment
  • Evaluation of the risk to other batches of the same or related products manufactured in the same time frame
  • Critical investigations should be closed prior to product release

Table 5.7-1: Examples of Investigation Elements for Different Systems

System Investigation Elements
Compressed Gas System
  • Repeat test immediately. Perform filter integrity testing.
  • Replace filter if excursion is confirmed on retest.
  • Evaluate impact on processed component and/or product.
Room Air / HVAC
  • Review level of personnel activity.
  • Review/perform airflow patterns and HEPA integrity tests.
  • Review aseptic technique of personnel and training records.
  • Review gowning procedures and requirements for area.
  • Review trends and any incidents of HVAC outages.
  • Inspect incoming air filters for leaks and pressure differential across filter.
  • Review room cleaning and disinfection procedures; review training records.
  • Check area pressure differentials, particularly with respect to the last sanitization.
  • Evaluate mechanical equipment in the area as possible contamination source.
  • Review prior and subsequent monitoring results.
  • Review sterilization cycle records.
Facility Surfaces
  • Perform investigation for possible sources of contamination.
  • Evaluate cleaning and disinfection practices; review preparation of disinfectants, cleaning records, and training records.
  • Review possible unusual events during manufacturing operation.
  • Examine areas during operation.
  • Review closed-circuit video (if applicable).
  • Verify that environmental controls were not circumvented.
  • Review risk of direct or indirect product contact with impacted surfaces.
  • Review isolates for occurrence in other types of EM tests.
  • Evaluate condition of the room (e.g., peeling paint or cracks in ceiling, walls, and floor).
High Purity Water System
(WFI, clean steam, purified water)
  • Review upstream water treatment systems (e.g., carbon beds used to remove chlorine from municipal water systems).
  • Examine bioburden data for other samples or sites in system — port contamination vs. system contamination.
  • Examine endotoxin and water chemistry data for system.
  • Review efficacy of sanitization procedure and schedule.
  • Inspect system preventive maintenance records; evaluate impact on product.
  • Verify integrity of sample collection and use procedures.
  • Inspect system for dead-legs, proper sloping, hose management, and sample-port design and location.
  • Review data for flow rate, pressure, and vent filter integrity wherever used.
Personnel Gowning
(gowning and gloves)
  • Evaluate possible operator impact on product.
  • Review EM data and sterility test data.
  • Review preparation and expiry dates for disinfectants used on gloves.
  • Identify all morphologically unique isolates (human vs. environmental).
  • Interview operator for potential cause and retrain or requalify operator; check integrity of isolators and RABS gloves.
  • Evaluate training of operator; review sanitization and disinfection records.
  • Review closed-circuit video (if available).
  • Review previous gowning data for the operator and other operators on the same day.

The points listed in the table are not all-inclusive; these recommendations are intended only to suggest investigative activities. Corrective actions based on process knowledge can be implemented when sampling and laboratory failures have been ruled out. Appropriate corrective actions arise holistically from evaluation of the investigation elements, leading to the root cause.

The reviewer may employ scientific judgment to postpone any corrective action until the result is confirmed and/or an investigation has been completed. It may also be appropriate to provide management with a routine summary of action-level excursions for review.

中文解說：調查流程與根本原因分析策略

Section 5.7 是 EM 程序中最具實務操作性的部分。調查（Investigation）不是「找人負責」，而是「找出系統問題」。

比喻：EM 調查就像刑事偵查

偵探（QA 調查員）發現案發現場（Action Limit 超標），必須系統性地排查所有嫌疑人（汙染來源）：是空調系統（HVAC）、是水管、是人員操作疏失、還是設施本身的老化問題？不能靠直覺，必須有書面的調查 SOP，按步驟執行，最終找到「最可能的根本原因（Most Probable Cause）」。

五大系統的調查重點解析：

壓縮氣體系統（Compressed Gas）

調查最簡單直接：立即重測確認真偽 → 做過濾器完整性測試 → 若確認超標即更換過濾器。重點在「迅速行動」防止產品受影響。

空調/HVAC 系統

調查範圍最廣，需多面向檢視：人員活動（最大微生物負荷來源）、HEPA 完整性、氣流型態（靜/動態可視化測試）、壓差記錄、清潔/消毒記錄。HVAC 超標往往反映多因素交互作用。

設施表面（Surface）

需現場勘查：目視檢查牆壁/天花板/地板是否有裂縫、脫漆（可能是黴菌隱藏處）。CCTV 影片往往是還原「操作疏失」的關鍵證據。

高純水系統（WFI/PW）

需區分「點位汙染」（Port Contamination）vs「系統汙染」（System Contamination）。同步查驗內毒素數據（Gram-negative 菌的代謝產物），死角（Dead-legs）、斜度不足、軟管管理是常見的根本原因。

人員更衣（Personnel Gowning）

涉及人員個資與訓練記錄，需要訪談操作員、調閱更衣監控記錄、核對同日其他操作員的更衣數據，判斷是個人技能問題還是系統性訓練缺失。

產品影響評估（Product Impact Assessment）必須在調查中完成，且關鍵調查應在批次放行前關閉。這是監管機構稽核時的高頻查核點。

5.8 Documentation

The types of records that should be maintained as part of a routine EM program include:

  1. Date and time of sampling
  2. Product identification
  3. Identification of the individual performing test
  4. Test method or procedure reference
  5. Activity level at site during test
  6. Sampling equipment identification
  7. Physical parameters like temperature, relative humidity, and differential pressurization
  8. Sample site
  9. Area classification
  10. Schematics of areas showing sample site locations
  11. Sample site criticality
  12. Sampling frequency
  13. Test results with units (e.g., CFU/plate/hour)
  14. Identification of the analyst recording results
  15. Date results read
  16. Alert level and/or action limit
  17. Temperature and duration of incubation
  18. Traceability of the certification date, release date, lot number, and expiration date of media used
  19. Microbial characterization of contaminants
  20. Name of reviewer
  21. Approval of data
  22. Review of historical data
  23. Calibration due date for sampling instrumentation and equipment
  24. Methodology and analysis used to specify action and/or alert levels
  25. System for documenting investigative and corrective action:
    • Description of deficiency
    • Possible causes of problem
    • Identification of persons responsible for relevant corrective action
    • Description of action steps and their schedule for implementation
    • Evaluation of effectiveness of corrective and preventative action steps

中文解說：25 項文件記錄要求的分組解析

TR13 Section 5.8 的 25 項文件清單看似繁瑣，但邏輯分明，可分為五大組別：

組一：採樣事件識別（第 1–6 項）

回答「誰、何時、何處、用什麼採樣」。日期時間（#1）、產品識別（#2）、採樣者（#3）、方法參照（#4）、採樣時活動程度（#5）、設備識別（#6）。這六項確保採樣事件可完整重現與追溯。

組二：環境條件（第 7–12 項）

記錄採樣當下的環境狀態：溫溼度、壓差（#7）、採樣點位置（#8）、潔淨等級（#9）、採樣點示意圖（#10）、點位關鍵性（#11）、採樣頻率（#12）。這些數據支持趨勢分析與異常解讀。

組三：分析/檢測記錄（第 13–19 項）

記錄實驗室端的關鍵資訊：含單位的測試結果（#13，e.g., CFU/plate）、分析員（#14）、讀取日期（#15）、警戒/行動限值（#16）、培養溫度與時間（#17）、培養基批次追溯（#18）、微生物特徵鑑定（#19）。培養基批號追溯（#18）特別重要，因為失效培養基是 EM 假陰性的常見原因。

組四：審核與核准（第 20–24 項）

審查者（#20）、數據核准（#21）、歷史數據回顧（#22）、儀器校正到期日（#23）、限值設定方法（#24）。這些記錄支持數據完整性（Data Integrity）和 GMP 合規性審計。

組五：調查與 CAPA（第 25 項）

最後一項「調查與矯正行動系統」是最複雜的，包含五個子要素：缺失描述、可能原因、負責人、行動步驟與時程、CAPA 有效性評估。這直接對應 FDA 21 CFR Part 211 及 EU GMP Annex 11 的要求。

稽核提示：稽核員常要求「拿一份 EM 記錄，現場核對是否包含這 25 項要素」。建立勾選式記錄表（Checklist-based Logbook）是最有效的合規工具。

  2. 鑑定策略應用（Table 5.6-1）：以下三個情境，各應執行何種層級的微生物鑑定？請說明理由：(a) Grade B 被動沉降碟回收到 2 CFU，計數低於 Alert Level；(b) Grade D 表面接觸碟回收到 30 CFU，超過 Action Limit（25 CFU/plate）；(c) Media Fill 中有一支安瓿檢出陽性，初步鑑定為 Bacillus spp.。
