8.10

Residual Gas Ionization | 殘留氣體離子化

High-voltage field ionizes headspace gas in vacuum-sealed packages — qualitative, non-destructive

Description

A high voltage, high frequency field is applied to packages sealed under vacuum. The field causes residual headspace gas to ionize. A conducting plate located near the package detects the level of ionization current. Ionization above an established limit triggers rejection of the package.

Validation

The residual gas ionization method can be validated for the detection of insufficient vacuum in packages designed to maintain a minimal vacuum level. Validation studies should examine the ability of the ionization sensors to differentiate between packages containing various known levels of vacuum as a function of voltage, frequency, or package headspace.

Sensitivity

The sensitivity is based on the ability to accurately detect pressure exceeding a desired acceptance limit in evacuated packages. Instrument sensitivity can be determined using control packages containing known pressure levels. It is possible to detect smaller leaks and permeation rates as the time interval from when the package was originally evacuated to the test time is lengthened.

Quantitative / Qualitative

Qualitative

Critical Test Parameters

  • Voltage and frequency
  • Conducting plate and voltage field positioning
  • Package component ionization potential
  • Package headspace volume
  • Gaseous content of the residual headspace
  • Package component geometry

Time to Complete

Less than a second.

Equipment Required

  • Gas ionization instruments
  • Package handling systems

Stress on Package

High frequency high voltage spark. Impact of the voltage spark on product stability should be considered.

Product / Package Types

Applicable to pharmaceutical packages designed to maintain a partial vacuum in the headspace. The package tested must be relatively non-ionizing.

Literature References

Wu, 1989 • Crist, 1994

中文解說 | Commentary

原理說明 — 殘留氣體離子化 (Residual Gas Ionization)

對封裝在真空條件下的容器施加高壓高頻電場。若包裝內有殘餘氣體（代表存在洩漏或真空不足），這些氣體會被電離，附近的導電板即可偵測到電離電流。電流超出允許限值時，該包裝即被拒絕。

類比：就像螢光燈管在低壓時發光一樣，氣體在高電場下會被激發發光/導電。真空越高（氣體越少），電離越弱；若有洩漏（氣體增加），電離增強。

重點提示 | Key Points

  • 定性方法（Qualitative）：只能判斷通過/拒絕，無法量化洩漏速率
  • 非破壞性（Non-destructive）：包裝完好無損，可回收
  • 僅適用於真空密封包裝，不適用正壓包裝
  • 測試時間極短（<1秒），適合生產線上的100%在線檢查
  • 包裝材料本身不能有太高的電離電位（即材料不能太導電）

時間效應 | Time-Dependent Sensitivity

靈敏度會隨時間延長而提高：若包裝在密封後存放較久再測，即使只有微小洩漏，積累的氣體也較多，更容易被偵測。這意味著可以在包裝完成後延遲一段時間再測，以提高靈敏度。

實際應用 | Practical Application

常見於凍乾注射劑（lyophilized injectables）的玻璃瓶封裝完整性檢查。凍乾後瓶內為真空，殘留氣體離子化法可快速篩選出真空不足的瓶子。

8.11

Tracer Gas Leak Detection | 示蹤氣體洩漏偵測

Analytical detection of tracer gas permeating through package — quantitative, non-destructive

Description

A tracer gas is placed on one side of the package seal. An inert carrier gas is passed across the opposite seal side. Tracer gas is detected by appropriate analytical methods. Examples of analytical methods include coulometric detectors for oxygen detection and photoelectric sensors for carbon dioxide and water vapor detection.

This test is useful for the measurement of leakage and permeation from packages that require a specific gas headspace to ensure long term product stability, or for products that are susceptible to degradation upon exposure to environmental gases or vapors. While oxygen, carbon dioxide, and water vapor are typically selected for testing pharmaceutical packages, a wide variety of other tracer gases can be used as well.

Validation

This method can be validated for the detection, location, and measurement of gaseous leaks and permeation large enough to result in an unacceptable shift in headspace content over time or in product degradation. Validation studies should examine the ability of the analytical method to accurately measure gas leakage/permeation rate in the package system.

Sensitivity

The sensitivity of tracer gas detection methods is based on the ability to accurately measure tracer gas leakage/permeation rate in the package system. Instrument sensitivity can be established using reference gas mixtures of known tracer concentration. Test method sensitivity is largely dependent on the design of the test fixture.

Quantitative / Qualitative

Quantitative

Critical Test Parameters

  • Gas analytical instrument sensitivity and accuracy
  • Package test fixture design and dimensions
  • Temperature and humidity of the testing environment
  • Inert carrier gas temperature and humidity
  • Concentration of the tracer gas in the package
  • Concentration of tracer gas in the effluent atmosphere

Time to Complete

Several hours to a few days.

Equipment Required

  • Analytical gas measurement instrumentation with package test fixture
  • Carrier gas source
  • Tracer gas

Stress on Package

None.

Product / Package Types

Applicable to pharmaceutical packages that are required to maintain a specified leakage/permeation rate of gas through the package to ensure product stability.

Literature References

Amini, 1981 • ASNT, 1982 • ASTM E 427 • ASTM E 498 • ASTM E 1603 • Demorest, 1988, 1992, 1996 • Elder, 1995 • Gnanasekharan, 1994 • Guazzo, 1996

中文解說 | Commentary

原理說明 — 示蹤氣體洩漏偵測 (Tracer Gas Leak Detection)

將示蹤氣體（tracer gas）置於密封的一側，另一側用惰性載體氣體沖洗，再用分析儀器偵測載體中是否含有示蹤氣體。示蹤氣體的選擇通常為：

  • 氧氣（O₂）：庫侖計偵測器（coulometric detector）
  • 二氧化碳（CO₂）/ 水蒸氣：光電感測器
  • 其他惰性氣體（氦氣 He 等）：質譜儀

重點提示 | Key Points

  • 定量方法（Quantitative）：可測量實際洩漏/滲透速率
  • 非破壞性（Non-destructive）：對包裝無應力
  • 測試時間長（數小時至數天）——不適合高通量 100% 在線檢查
  • 測試夾具（test fixture）的設計是靈敏度的關鍵變數
  • 適合評估需要特定氣體頭頂空間的包裝（如充 N₂ 的凍乾劑、充 CO₂ 的液體）

何時使用示蹤氣體法？ | When to Use?

當您需要確認包裝在整個保質期內的氣體屏障性能時，示蹤氣體法最合適。例如：某凍乾注射劑要求氧氣含量 <1%，需驗證包裝在 2 年貨架期內的滲透速率是否符合。

與其他方法對比 | vs. Other Methods

示蹤氣體法同時測量洩漏（leakage）與滲透（permeation），而壓力衰減法只測洩漏。因此，若要完整評估包裝屏障性能，示蹤氣體法更全面，但耗時更長。

8.12

Ultrasonic Detection of Gas Flow | 超聲波氣流偵測

Detects turbulent gas escaping from pressurized packages — qualitative, non-destructive

Description

Containers under pressure are placed near an ultrasonic sensor capable of detecting acoustic energy in the ultrasonic range of 20–100 kHz. Ultrasonic sensors will detect movement of air or gasses out of package leaks large enough to create turbulent gas flow.

Smaller leaks may be detected by the application of a special surfactant to the seal. Bursting of surfactant bubbles is detected ultrasonically. There must be enough package headspace to permit detectable gas flow out of the package.

Validation

Ultrasonic gas flow detection methods can be validated for the detection and location of leaks in package systems sealed under pressure or in packages which can be "squeezed" or otherwise manipulated to create pressure inside the package. Validation studies should examine the ability of the ultrasonic sensor to detect control leaks as a function of instrument sensitivity settings, internal package pressure, package-sensor positioning, background noise, and line speeds.

Sensitivity

Sensitivity of the method is limited by the presence of turbulent flow gas leakage and the ability to detect such flow using positive control package leaks or standard gaseous reference leaks.

Quantitative / Qualitative

Qualitative

Critical Test Parameters

  • Sensitivity settings
  • Background noise
  • Internal package pressure
  • Line speeds
  • Package-sensor positioning

Time to Complete

Test time is almost instantaneous.

Equipment Required

  • Ultrasonic detection instrumentation
  • Package handling system for squeezing package and/or positioning near the sensor
  • Surfactant for ultrasonic detection of bubble breakage

Stress on Package

Possible squeezing of flexible packages. Possible surfactant wetting of seal surface.

Product / Package Types

Applicable to pharmaceutical packages that do not rely on a tortuous path or barrier seal, that contain a headspace volume, that are sealed under pressure or that are flexible enough to permit pressurization by squeezing.

Literature References

ASNT, 1982 • ASTM E 1002 • Bandes, 1988 • Elder, 1995 • Guazzo, 1997

中文解說 | Commentary

原理說明 — 超聲波氣流偵測 (Ultrasonic Detection of Gas Flow)

當加壓包裝有洩漏時，氣體從洩漏處逸出，形成紊流（turbulent flow），產生 20–100 kHz 的超聲波聲能。超聲波感測器偵測此聲能即可判斷是否有洩漏。

對於較小洩漏，可在密封處塗抹界面活性劑（surfactant），洩漏氣泡破裂時也會產生超聲波訊號。

重點提示 | Key Points

  • 定性方法（Qualitative）：判斷通過/拒絕
  • 非破壞性（Non-destructive）
  • 測試幾乎瞬間完成，適合生產線在線檢查
  • 限制：僅能偵測到能產生紊流的較大洩漏；微小洩漏可能被漏掉
  • 背景噪音是干擾主要來源，需在驗證中評估
  • 需要包裝有足夠的頭頂空間（headspace），或可被擠壓加壓

比較：8.12 vs. 8.13 超聲波法 | 8.12 vs. 8.13

8.12（氣流偵測）：偵測氣體從洩漏處逸出的聲音，需要有壓差，適合壓力密封包裝。

8.13（超聲波成像）：用脈衝超聲波掃描密封界面，偵測密封缺陷的影像，不需要氣體流動，適合評估密封品質。

適用包裝範例 | Applicable Packages

軟袋（IV bags）、充氣泡殼包裝（blister packs）、含正壓頭空間的安瓿（ampoules under pressure）。不適合依靠曲折路徑密封（tortuous path seal）的容器。

8.13

Ultrasonic Imaging | 超聲波成像

High-frequency pulse imaging of seal integrity — qualitative, non-destructive

Description

Very short ultrasound pulses are generated at frequencies up to several hundred MHz. These ultrasound frequencies are focused on the container seal by acoustic lenses. Once inside the package the ultrasonic pulses may be absorbed, scattered or reflected depending on the change in the material. The reflected ultrasound echoes are used to create an image that can be analyzed for the presence of improper sealing, delamination, gaps, or product-contaminated seals.

Alternate ultrasonic inspection methods work by contact transmission of ultrasonic energy through the package seal using an array of transmitters and receivers in direct contact with the seal region.

Validation

Ultrasonic imaging methods can be validated for the detection and location of seal imperfections in flat thermal or adhesive bonded seals, for instance a pouch. Validation studies should examine the ability of the ultrasonic sensor to detect control leaks as a function of ultrasound pulse generation and transmission variables, instrument sensor sensitivity settings, transmission and detection sensor positioning, package materials of construction, and package geometry. Validation studies should reflect the range of acceptable package sealing parameters.

Sensitivity

Sensitivity is based on the ability to detect positive control package defects such as gaps, seal contaminants, package material defects, and other seal inconsistencies.

Quantitative / Qualitative

Qualitative

Critical Test Parameters

  • Sensor sensitivity settings
  • Quantity of transmitters and sensors per seal surface area
  • Package-sensor and transmission positionings
  • Ultrasound frequency
  • Ultrasound pulse amplitude
  • Package materials of construction
  • Package geometry
  • Dry package testing vs. water immersion testing

Time to Complete

Test time is less than one second.

Equipment Required

  • Ultrasonic detection and imaging instrumentation
  • Package handling system for positioning packages in the sensor

Stress on Package

None.

Product / Package Types

Applicable to pharmaceutical packages that do not rely on a tortuous path or barrier seal, that contain a headspace volume, that are sealed under pressure or that are flexible enough to permit pressurization by squeezing.

Literature References

Adams, 1995

中文解說 | Commentary

原理說明 — 超聲波成像 (Ultrasonic Imaging)

以高頻（數百 MHz）超短脈衝超聲波掃描密封界面。超聲波在材料界面（如密封面與空氣間）發生反射、散射、吸收，反射回波形成影像。缺陷（如分層、污染物、間隙）會顯示出異常的反射特徵。

可分為兩種模式：

  • 反射模式（Reflection mode）：分析反射回波影像
  • 透射模式（Transmission mode）：發射器與接收器夾住密封區域，檢測穿透強度

重點提示 | Key Points

  • 定性方法（Qualitative）：影像判讀通過/拒絕
  • 非破壞性（Non-destructive）：無應力施加於包裝
  • 測試時間極短（<1秒），適合生產線
  • 特別適合偵測密封面污染（product-contaminated seals），這是其他方法難以偵測的
  • 驗證需涵蓋乾式測試 vs. 水浸測試兩種模式
  • 頻率越高，解析度越好，但穿透深度越淺

實務優勢 | Practical Advantage

超聲波成像是少數能夠可視化密封缺陷位置的方法。不僅能說「有缺陷」，還能精確指出缺陷在密封區域的哪個部位，特別適合密封工藝開發與故障分析（root cause investigation）。

適用包裝範例 | Applicable Packages

熱封袋（pouches with thermal seals）、黏合密封袋（adhesive bonded seals）。常用於評估熱封工藝參數範圍的驗證（如溫度、壓力、時間的邊界條件測試）。

8.14

Vacuum/Pressure Decay (Differential) | 真空/壓力衰減（差壓法）

Measures pressure change vs. reference — qualitative or quantitative, non-destructive

Description

A pressure differential is applied to a package and the change in pressure or vacuum is measured either inside the package itself using a test fixture, or outside in a sealed package test chamber. The change in test chamber pressure or vacuum can be determined by absolute pressure measurement, or it can be determined using a differential pressure manometer in which the test chamber pressure change is compared to that of a control package.

Validation

Vacuum/pressure decay methods can be validated for the detection or measurement of gaseous leaks large enough to allow a measurable change in pressure in the package or the test chamber over the duration of the test. Validation studies should examine the ability of the instrument to detect control leaks as a function of test chamber geometry, instrument sensor capabilities, differential pressure testing conditions, test time, and environmental temperature conditions.

Sensitivity

Sensitivity is based on the ability to detect and/or measure a change in pressure or vacuum resulting from gaseous leaks. Generally, pressure decay is considered a more sensitive test than vacuum rise, and differential pressure is potentially more sensitive than absolute pressure sensors.

Quantitative / Qualitative

Qualitative or quantitative, depending on the instruments and test conditions used.

Critical Test Parameters

  • Pressure/vacuum sensor mechanism of operation and capabilities
  • Volume of space being tested (test chamber or package headspace)
  • Test chamber geometry
  • Time allowed for measured pressure change to occur
  • Stabilization time
  • Differential pressure applied to package
  • Water vapor pressure inside package
  • Atmospheric pressure and temperature
  • Package creep effects when exposed to differential pressure

Time to Complete

Test time may range from less than a second to a few minutes.

Equipment Required

  • Leak test instrumentation including vacuum or pressure sensors in absolute or differential pressure sensing mode
  • Differential pressure source, monitors and controls
  • Temperature and humidity controls and monitors

Stress on Package

Differential pressure applied to package. Consider: headspace volume impact, liquids plugging leaks under vacuum, and package tolerance to differential pressure (vacuum rise tests limited to 1 atm; pressure decay may be much greater).

Product / Package Types

Applicable to pharmaceutical packages that do not rely on a tortuous path or barrier seal and that can tolerate a differential pressure applied to the seal area.

Literature References

ASNT, 1982 • Blakistone, 1995 • Chrai et al., 1994 • Elder, 1995 • Gnanasekharan et al., 1992, 1994 • Guazzo, 1994, 1996 • Morton, 1987, 1989 • Snow, 1992 • The Parenteral Society, 1985

中文解說 | Commentary

原理說明 — 真空/壓力衰減差壓法

對包裝施加壓差，測量壓力/真空度隨時間的變化。有兩種測量模式：

  • 絕對壓力模式（Absolute）：直接測量測試腔的壓力變化
  • 差壓模式（Differential）：將測試包裝的壓力變化與參考包裝（control）比較，消除環境溫度波動影響

有四種測試構型：

  • Vacuum rise（真空上升）：外腔抽真空，包裝內部有氣壓；若有洩漏，氣體進入外腔，壓力上升
  • Pressure decay（壓力衰減）：對包裝施加正壓；若有洩漏，壓力下降

重點提示 | Key Points

  • 可為定性或定量（Qualitative or Quantitative），取決於儀器與條件
  • 非破壞性（Non-destructive）
  • 靈敏度排序：差壓 > 絕對壓力；壓力衰減 > 真空上升
  • 真空上升最大壓差限制為 1 atm；壓力衰減可施加更大壓差
  • 液體（或半固體）可能在真空下堵塞洩漏點，造成偽陰性
  • 包裝的潛變（creep）效應（特別是軟包裝）會干擾壓力讀數

8.14 vs. 8.15 區別 | Key Distinction

8.14（差壓/衰減法）：從外部施加壓差，測量腔室或包裝的壓力/真空變化。較靈敏，適合一般密封完整性檢查。

8.15（直接量測/保留法）：用穿刺裝置直接進入包裝頭空間，讀取絕對壓力值。適合需維持真空或正壓的包裝（如凍乾劑）。

選擇考量 | Selection Considerations

含液體的注射劑瓶在真空測試時，液體可能被吸入密封處堵住洩漏，宜改用壓力衰減法。粉末或凍乾劑則兩者均可適用。測試腔容積越小，靈敏度越高。

8.15

Vacuum/Pressure Retention (Absolute) | 真空/壓力保留（絕對量測法）

Direct headspace pressure measurement via piercing — qualitative or quantitative, destructive

Description

Vacuum/pressure retention tests consist of directly measuring the vacuum or pressure within a package headspace by typically using a package piercing device and an absolute pressure gauge. Vacuum/pressure retention tests are used for packages that are designed to maintain either a vacuum or positive pressure within the package as a function of product stability.

Validation

Vacuum/pressure retention methods can be validated for the detection or measurement of gaseous leaks large enough to allow a measurable change in pressure in the test chamber over the duration of the test. Validation studies should examine the ability to access the package headspace and accurately measure headspace pressure.

Sensitivity

Sensitivity is based on the ability to detect and/or measure a change in pressure or vacuum resulting from gaseous leaks. Test method sensitivity is dependent on the ability to access the package headspace without creating a loss of pressure or vacuum.

Quantitative / Qualitative

Qualitative or quantitative

Critical Test Parameters

  • Pressure/vacuum sensor mechanism of operation and capabilities
  • Volume of space being tested (test chamber or package headspace)
  • Time allowed for measured pressure change to occur
  • Vacuum or pressure initially introduced to the package
  • Water or other vapor pressure inside package
  • Temperature of package and test system
  • Technique for piercing or accessing package headspace

Time to Complete

Test time is a few seconds.

Equipment Required

  • Absolute pressure gauge
  • Temperature controls and monitors

Stress on Package

Pressure or vacuum applied to access headspace.

Product / Package Types

Applicable to pharmaceutical packages that are designed to maintain a positive pressure or a vacuum in the headspace over product shelf-life.

Literature References

ASNT, 1982 • Morton, 1987 • Chrai et al., 1994 • PDA, 1981 • Crist, 1994 • Rhea et al., 1984 • Gnanasekharan et al., 1992 • Wang et al., 1997

中文解說 | Commentary

原理說明 — 真空/壓力保留絕對量測法

使用穿刺裝置（piercing device）插入包裝頭空間，連接絕對壓力計直接讀取包裝內部壓力值。若包裝設計為真空密封（如凍乾劑），測得值應接近 0（真空）；若為充氮密封（如正壓包裝），應讀到預定壓力值。

比較 8.14 與 8.15 的核心差異：

  • 8.14：從外部施加壓差，測量腔室變化（不穿刺）
  • 8.15：穿刺進入包裝頭空間，直接讀絕對壓力

重點提示 | Key Points

  • 由於需要穿刺包裝，通常視為破壞性（Destructive）測試（部分設計可重新密封，但一般屬破壞性）
  • 可為定性或定量（Qualitative or Quantitative）
  • 測試速度快（數秒）
  • 關鍵挑戰：穿刺過程不能改變包裝內的壓力——穿刺工具的設計至關重要
  • 包裝內的水蒸氣壓（water vapor pressure）會影響讀數，需在解讀時校正

典型應用場景 | Typical Application

凍乾注射劑（lyophilized injectables）的真空品質確認。例如：凍乾結束後，抽取樣品，以穿刺法確認瓶內真空值是否在規格內（如 <200 mTorr），以此評估凍乾工藝的一致性。

注意事項 | Caution

此方法因涉及穿刺，不適用於生產線 100% 在線檢查，通常作為抽樣測試（sampling test）或工藝開發中使用。

8.16

Visual Inspection | 目視檢查

Human or electronic detection of product leakage — qualitative, non-destructive

Description

The package is examined visually by human or by electronic inspection devices for evidence of product leakage. The test may be accompanied by additional package challenges of temperature or pressure extremes to increase the sensitivity of the test.

Validation

Visual inspection for leakage determines the presence or location of leaks penetrated by the contained product. This method can be validated for the detection of larger leaks or package imperfections. Validation will depend on the reliability and sensitivity of the mode of leakage detection.

Sensitivity

The sensitivity is based on the incidence of product migration through positive control package defects and the ability to detect such migration. There must be sufficient product or contents leakage to permit visible or electronic detection.

Quantitative / Qualitative

Qualitative test

Critical Test Parameters

  • Package cleanliness
  • Differential pressure and temperature exposure (optional)
  • Light intensity, magnification, time, background, angle of inspection
  • Visual inspector training and experience
  • Electronic inspection parameters dependent on mode of inspection

Time to Complete

From less than a second for electronic inspection to several minutes by visual inspection.

Equipment Required

  • Temperature and/or pressure controls for optional stress testing
  • Appropriate lighting, light meters, magnification, and color background
  • Electronic inspection equipment and instrumentation

Stress on Package

Pressure or temperature differential (optional).

Product / Package Types

Applicable to all pharmaceutical packages and closure/seal types.

Literature References

ASNT, 1982 • ASTM E 165 • Chrai et al., 1994 • Gnanasekharan et al., 1992 • Hansen et al., 1995 • Jones et al., 1995 • Lin et al., 1982 • Shaw, 1995

中文解說 | Commentary

原理說明 — 目視檢查 (Visual Inspection)

最傳統的包裝完整性方法。透過人工目視或電子/機器視覺系統，觀察包裝外部是否有產品滲漏的跡象（如液體痕跡、顏色變化、包裝變形等）。

可搭配溫度或壓力應力（stress test），迫使潛在洩漏點顯現，提高靈敏度。

重點提示 | Key Points

  • 定性方法（Qualitative）、非破壞性（Non-destructive）
  • 唯一適用於所有包裝類型的方法（無限制）
  • 靈敏度低：僅能偵測到已發生可見產品外滲的較大洩漏
  • 人工目視受操作員訓練、燈光、角度等主觀因素影響，可重複性低
  • 電子視覺系統（AVI）速度快但仍依賴影像對比，對微小洩漏不靈敏
  • 是法規（FDA、EMA）要求的最低基線方法，幾乎所有產品都需進行

目視檢查的局限性 | Limitations

目視檢查只能看到「已發生的洩漏痕跡」，無法預測或偵測尚未顯現的微小洩漏。就像汽車的機油警告燈——它告訴你機油已經不夠了，而不是告訴你某個密封件有微小裂縫即將失效。

法規要求 | Regulatory Context

FDA 無菌工藝指南（2004）要求所有注射劑進行 100% 目視檢查（21 CFR 211.167）。但同時也建議補充更靈敏的確定性方法（deterministic methods），如壓力衰減法，以確保微小洩漏也能被偵測。

8.17

Volumetric Gas Flow | 體積氣流法

Measures outward gas flow rate under differential pressure — quantitative, non-destructive

Description

A pressure differential is applied to a package and the outward flow rate of gas is measured.

Validation

Volumetric gas flow methods can be validated for the measurement of gaseous leaks large enough to allow a measurable flow of gas out of the package over the duration of the test. Validation studies should examine the ability of the volumetric flow rate instrument to measure control leaks as a function of instrument sensor capabilities, differential pressure testing conditions, and test time.

Sensitivity

The sensitivity is based on the ability to accurately measure gaseous flow out of a package under positive pressure. Instrument sensitivity can be verified using gaseous reference leaks or positive control package defects. Test method sensitivity is influenced primarily by flow meter accuracy, test time, and the differential pressure applied to the package. Generally, volumetric gas flow is not as sensitive as vacuum/pressure decay methods.

Quantitative / Qualitative

Quantitative

Critical Test Parameters

  • Flow meter accuracy
  • Time of test
  • Differential pressure applied to package

Time to Complete

A few seconds.

Equipment Required

  • Leak test instrumentation equipped with flow meters
  • Differential pressure source, monitors and controls

Stress on Package

Differential pressure.

Product / Package Types

Applicable to pharmaceutical packages that do not rely on a tortuous path or barrier seal and that can tolerate a differential pressure applied to the seal area. This test is particularly useful for checking critical orifice sizes of specialty packages or devices. The test can often be used instead of vacuum/pressure decay for packages that tend to deform under pressure.

Literature References

ASNT, 1982 • Guazzo, 1996 • Gnanasekharan et al., 1992, 1994 • Hoffmann, 1996 • Snow, 1992

中文解說 | Commentary

原理說明 — 體積氣流法 (Volumetric Gas Flow)

對包裝施加正壓，用流量計（flow meter）測量從包裝洩漏逸出的氣體流量（單位：mL/min 或 sccm）。流量越大，代表洩漏越嚴重。

與壓力衰減法的差異：

  • 體積氣流法：直接測量流量（流速）
  • 壓力衰減法（8.14）：測量壓力下降速率（間接計算流量）

重點提示 | Key Points

  • 定量方法（Quantitative）：可測量實際氣體流量
  • 非破壞性（Non-destructive）
  • 靈敏度低於壓力衰減法（flow meter 精度限制）
  • 測試時間短（數秒）
  • 特別適合易在壓力下變形（deform）的包裝——變形包裝用壓力衰減法會因包裝膨脹/收縮造成偽陽性，而流量法不受此影響
  • 適合測試特定孔徑（critical orifice）規格，如吸入器（inhaler）的噴嘴

何時選擇體積法 vs. 壓力衰減法？

如果包裝是軟袋（如輸液袋）或其他在加壓時容易形變的容器，壓力衰減法讀數會受形變影響而失真。此時選擇體積氣流法更可靠，因為它直接測量逸出氣體量，不受容器形變影響。

8.18

Weight Loss / Gain | 重量損失/增加法

Gravimetric measurement of volatile loss or hygroscopic gain — quantitative, non-destructive

Description

The container is filled with either a volatile liquid or solid, or a hygroscopic material such as a desiccant. The container is stored in an environment intended to promote migration of the substances out of the package (volatile materials), or to promote migration of water vapor into the package (hygroscopic materials). Leakage plus permeation are measured in units of package weight loss or weight gain.

For greater accuracy, it is recommended that control packages filled with inert materials be tested at the same time. Each time the test units are weighed, the control packages' weight change should be subtracted from the change in test unit weights.

Validation

Weight loss or weight gain methods can be validated for the measurement of leaks of a wide variety of sizes, as well as for permeation measurement. Validation will depend on the test method parameters and the sensitivity of the balance used to monitor weight change. Acceptance limits are generally established as a function of the product-package system.

Sensitivity

The sensitivity is dependent on the extent of weight loss or gain. The weight change of the test units must vary significantly from the control units, and should be above the limit of balance sensitivity.

Quantitative / Qualitative

Quantitative

Critical Test Parameters

  • Volatility (weight loss) or hygroscopicity (weight gain) of the packaged material
  • Time allowed for weight change
  • Challenge environment: temperature, and either pressure (weight loss) or relative humidity (weight loss or gain)
  • Handling of samples and weighing techniques

Time to Complete

A few minutes may be required for highly volatile materials (e.g., dry ice). Otherwise, several days or weeks may be required.

Equipment Required

  • Balance
  • For weight gain: relative humidity storage chamber; temperature and humidity monitors
  • For weight loss: appropriate package storage environment; optional elevated temperature, differential pressure with monitors

Stress on Package

Environmental extremes of temperature, pressure, humidity.

Product / Package Types

Applicable to all pharmaceutical packages and seal types intended to contain products susceptible to weight change over shelf-life.

Literature References

Amini, 1981 • Cilento, 1977 • Guazzo, 1996 • Morton, 1987 • USP <661> • USP <671>

中文解說 | Commentary

原理說明 — 重量損失/增加法 (Weight Loss/Gain)

利用天平稱重監測包裝在特定儲存條件下的重量變化：

  • 重量損失（Weight Loss）：包裝內含揮發性物質（如有機溶劑、乾冰），在壓力/溫度條件下，物質蒸發逸出，重量降低
  • 重量增加（Weight Gain）：包裝內含乾燥劑等吸濕性物質，在高濕環境下，水蒸氣滲入，重量升高

使用對照包裝（control packages，填充惰性材料）同步測試，以扣除環境因素影響。

重點提示 | Key Points

  • 定量方法（Quantitative）：可測量洩漏 + 滲透的總量
  • 非破壞性（Non-destructive）：稱重後包裝可繼續使用/觀察
  • 適用範圍廣：適合所有包裝類型與密封型式
  • 測試時間很長（數天至數週）——不適合在線檢查
  • 靈敏度取決於天平精度與重量變化量
  • 是 USP <661> 和 <671> 的參考方法，法規認可度高
  • 同時測量洩漏與滲透（leakage + permeation），無法區分兩者貢獻

最適應用 | Best Use Case

包裝保質期驗證與貨架壽命研究（shelf-life study）中，用於確認包裝在整個保質期內的阻隔性能是否符合規格。例如：確認含乾燥劑的膠囊瓶在 25°C/60%RH 條件下，2 年內水分增重不超過限值。

USP 關聯 | USP Reference

USP <671>（容器滲透性）規定了水蒸氣滲透的測試方法，重量法是其核心方法之一。USP <661>（注射用容器）也引用重量法作為容器密封性的評估工具。

Vacuum/Pressure Decay Types Explained | 真空/壓力衰減類型詳解

Method 8.14 can be implemented in four distinct configurations. Understanding the differences is critical for method selection and validation:

"Generally, pressure decay is considered a more sensitive test than vacuum rise, and differential pressure is potentially more sensitive than absolute pressure sensors." — PDA TR27, Section 8.14

四種構型中文解析 | Four Configurations Explained

記憶口訣：壓差 > 絕對；衰減 > 上升

靈敏度排序（由高至低）：

  1. 壓力衰減 + 差壓感測（Pressure Decay Differential）—— 最高
  2. 壓力衰減 + 絕對感測（Pressure Decay Absolute）
  3. 真空上升 + 差壓感測（Vacuum Rise Differential）
  4. 真空上升 + 絕對感測（Vacuum Rise Absolute）—— 最低

為何差壓更靈敏？

差壓模式中，測試包裝與參考包裝（無洩漏）同時加壓/抽真空。環境溫度波動對兩個腔室的影響幾乎相同，因此在差壓讀數中被抵消。這大幅減少了溫度漂移造成的假訊號，讓儀器可以偵測更微小的真實壓力變化。

液體包裝的特殊考量

含液體的包裝（如注射液）在真空上升測試中，液體可能被吸入密封點，暫時封堵洩漏路徑，導致偽陰性（false negative）。此情況宜改用壓力衰減法，從包裝內部向外加壓，液體不會被吸向密封處。

Practice Questions | 練習思考題

Reinforce understanding of TR27 Section 8.10–8.18 | 強化對 8.10–8.18 方法的理解

Question 1 | 題目一

A lyophilized injectable product is packaged in glass vials sealed under vacuum. You need to perform 100% in-line container closure integrity testing (CCIT) on the production line. The test must be completed in under one second per vial. Which method(s) from TR27 Section 8 would be most appropriate, and why?

一種凍乾注射劑在玻璃瓶中真空密封。您需要在生產線上對每個瓶子進行 100% 在線容器密封完整性測試（CCIT），每瓶測試時間不超過一秒。哪種方法最合適？請說明理由。

Show Answer | 顯示答案

Best answer: 8.10 Residual Gas Ionization (殘留氣體離子化)

Rationale: (1) Speed <1 second — meets the in-line requirement. (2) Specifically designed for vacuum-sealed packages. (3) Non-destructive — allows 100% testing without sample loss. (4) The high-voltage field ionizes residual headspace gas; a vial that has lost vacuum will show higher ionization current, triggering automatic rejection.

Also consider: 8.16 Visual Inspection (required by regulation but insufficient alone), and 8.5 Laser-Based Headspace Analysis (if available, highly sensitive and also <1 second).

理由：殘留氣體離子化法（8.10）：速度極快（<1秒）、非破壞性、專為真空密封設計，完全符合在線 100% 檢查的要求。另需補充 8.16 目視檢查（法規要求）。雷射頭空間分析（8.5）若有條件也可考慮，靈敏度更高。

Question 2 | 題目二

You are validating a container closure integrity test for a pharmaceutical product filled in flexible plastic pouches. During method development, you find that the pouches deform significantly when subjected to differential pressure in the vacuum/pressure decay test, giving inconsistent results. What alternative method would you recommend, and what are its key validation parameters?

您正在驗證軟性塑膠袋裝藥品的容器密封完整性測試。在方法開發中發現，施加差壓時袋子明顯形變，導致壓力衰減測試結果不穩定。您會建議哪種替代方法？關鍵驗證參數有哪些？

Show Answer | 顯示答案

Recommended: 8.17 Volumetric Gas Flow (體積氣流法)

TR27 explicitly states that volumetric gas flow "can often be used instead of vacuum/pressure decay for packages that tend to deform under pressure." Key validation parameters: (1) Flow meter accuracy, (2) Test time, (3) Differential pressure applied. Also consider: 8.12 Ultrasonic Detection of Gas Flow (for larger leaks, qualitative), 8.13 Ultrasonic Imaging (to characterize seal quality).

答案：推薦 8.17 體積氣流法。TR27 明確指出此法「常用於替代易形變包裝的壓力衰減法」。因為流量計直接測量逸出氣體量，不受容器形變影響。關鍵驗證參數：流量計精度、測試時間、施加壓差。輔助方案：8.13 超聲波成像，可評估熱封品質。

Question 3 | 題目三

A product team is developing a moisture-sensitive oral solid dosage form in a high-density polyethylene (HDPE) bottle with a desiccant. They want to establish the shelf-life specification for moisture ingress. Which leak test method from TR27 would best characterize the moisture barrier performance over a 24-month shelf life? What are the critical limitations of this method?

一個產品團隊正在開發裝有乾燥劑的高密度聚乙烯（HDPE）瓶裝口服固體製劑，需要建立水分滲入的保質期規格。哪種 TR27 方法最適合評估 24 個月保質期的防潮性能？此方法的關鍵限制是什麼？

Show Answer | 顯示答案

Best answer: 8.18 Weight Gain (重量增加法)

Rationale: (1) Directly measures moisture ingress as weight gain — the most relevant metric. (2) Applicable to all package types including HDPE bottles. (3) Quantitative — provides actual mg/day permeation rate. (4) Referenced in USP <671> for container moisture permeation. (5) Desiccant inside the bottle serves as the hygroscopic material for the test.

Critical limitations: (1) Very long test time (weeks to months for HDPE). (2) Measures leakage + permeation combined — cannot distinguish between defects and material permeation. (3) Requires precise balance and controlled humidity chamber. (4) Control packages (filled with inert material) must be tested simultaneously to subtract environmental effects.

答案：8.18 重量增加法最合適。理由：(1) 直接測量水分滲入量（重量增加），最符合保質期評估需求。(2) 瓶中乾燥劑本身即為吸濕材料，無需另行填充。(3) 定量，可獲得 mg/day 滲透速率。(4) USP <671> 引用此法。限制：測試時間長（數週至數月）；同時測量洩漏與滲透，無法區分；需同步測試對照包裝。
