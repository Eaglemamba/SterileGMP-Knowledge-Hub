5.7 Bovine Spongiform Encephalopathy (BSE)/Transmissible Spongiform Encephalopathy (TSE) Concerns

Original Text

SUSs have low-risk BSE/TSE concerns and they do not contain high-risk animal-derived components. Certain mold release and slip agents and plastics resins (e.g., polypropylene or polycarbonate) may contain trace levels of additives (e.g., calcium stearate), which are manufactured from bovine tallow and have low BSE/TSE risk. Tallow is a lubricant sometimes used in pelletizing operations for bulk resin or as a mold release agent for the manufacture of SU components. Tallow is produced by treating beef fat with steam to create a mixture of fats. The fat (e.g., glyceryl tristearate) is then boiled in a sodium hydroxide solution, resulting in a mixture of sodium stearate and glycerin.

Tallow-derived additives are not considered as specified BSE risk materials according to the current revision of the U.S. Code of Federal Regulations, Title 21 Part 189.5 (47). Furthermore, the CPMP's "Note for Minimizing the Risk of Transmitting Animal Spongiform Encephalopathies via Human and Veterinary Medicinal Products" (EMA 410/01 rev 3), gives specific consideration to tallow derivatives (48). It states that they are unlikely to be infectious due to the rigorous processing steps (e.g., transesterification, or hydrolysis, at not less than 200°C under pressure for not less than twenty minutes) used during their manufacture. A BSE/TSE risk assessment should be completed and periodically updated to reflect whether an SUS supplier is using components in the fluid pathway that were fabricated from plastic resins containing bovine-derived additives at trace levels.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

BSE/TSE（牛海綿狀腦病/傳播性海綿狀腦病）：這是一類由 Prion（乃變性蛋白）引起的致命性神經退化疾病。在製藥領域，任何含有動物來源成分的材料都必須評估是否有傳播此類疾病的風險。

Calcium Stearate（硬脂酸鈣）：從牛脂（Tallow）衍生的添加劑，常用作塑膠的脫模劑或滑劑。雖然只有微量存在，但仍需要進行風險評估。

Tallow（牛脂）：將牛脂肪以蒸氣處理後，再經氫氧化鈉皂化反應而成。經過高溫（≥200°C）高壓至少20分鐘的嚴格加工程序，已大幅降低 BSE/TSE 感染風險。

比喻說明 Analogy

想像你在買有機食品。食品本身是有機的（SUS 本身不含高風險成分），但包裝上可能用了微量的膠水，而膠水原料可能來自動物。雖然你實際接觸到這些膠水的機率極低，但作為負責任的製造商，你仍然需要追溯每一層供應鏈，確認風險是否可接受——這就是 BSE/TSE 風險評估的精神。

重點提示 Key Notes

美國 21 CFR 189.5 與 EMA 410/01 rev 3 均認定：經過嚴格加工的牛脂衍生物不屬於高風險 BSE 材料。但這不代表可以忽略——終端使用者仍須完成並定期更新 BSE/TSE 風險評估文件，特別是在更換供應商或材料時。

這也是 GMP 精神的核心：不是「零風險」，而是「已評估、已記錄、已管控的可接受風險」。

  2. 如果 SUS 供應商更換了塑膠樹脂來源，終端使用者應如何更新其 BSE/TSE 風險評估？

5.8 The Quality Systems of the End User and Supplier

Original Text

It is important for the end user to have instituted a robust quality system to receive materials, quarantine unreleased and rejected materials, inspect those materials, and release them to production. Since the testing and release of an SUS can vary widely from end user to end user, a complete and careful documentation in the form of a purchase specification and inspection plan should accompany any off-the-shelf or customized SUS requisition. A supplier quality agreement is another useful way to manage the requirements and expectations of both supplier and end user.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Purchase Specification（採購規格書）：詳細列出終端使用者對 SUS 的品質要求，包括材質、尺寸、微粒限值、滅菌方式等。這是品質管控的第一道防線。

Inspection Plan（進料檢驗計畫）：規定收貨後需執行哪些檢查——外觀、包裝完整性、證書核對等。

Supplier Quality Agreement, SQA（供應商品質協議）：正式合約文件，明確雙方在品質、變更管理、偏差處理等方面的權責。這在 SUS 領域尤其重要，因為供應商的任何材料或製程變更都可能影響到最終藥品。

比喻說明 Analogy

就像開餐廳需要跟食材供應商簽訂合約一樣——你要規定食材的品質標準（採購規格書），到貨時要檢查新鮮度和包裝（檢驗計畫），還要跟供應商約定：如果換了牧場來源必須提前通知（供應商品質協議）。SUS 的品質管控邏輯完全一樣。

重點提示 Key Notes

SUS 的測試與放行標準因終端使用者而異，這意味著沒有「一體適用」的標準。每家 CDMO 必須根據自身的製程風險，制定專屬的進料管控程序。

特別注意：「現成品」（off-the-shelf）和「客製品」（customized）SUS 都需要有採購規格書和檢驗計畫，不能因為是標準品就省略品質文件。

實務應用 Practical Application

在 CDMO 環境中，你可能同時為多個客戶代工不同藥品，每個藥品對 SUS 的品質要求可能不同。因此，建立分級的進料管控體系非常重要——例如：用於最終充填的 SUS 需要最嚴格的檢驗（包括微粒、內毒素等），而用於緩衝液配製的 SUS 可以採用簡化的檢驗流程。

  2. 為什麼即使是「現成品」SUS 也需要制定採購規格書？

5.9 Quality Management for Single-Use System Implementation

5.9.1 Determination of Expiration Date and Shelf Life

Original Text

The SUS supplier is expected to provide estimates for expiration dates and the shelf life of the SUS according to validation of maintenance of sterility tests and key functional properties. The expiration date is normally set post-sterilization. Any extension of the shelf life of the SUS must be supported by pertinent data. Real-time aging studies provide the best data to ensure that both the materials and the functional integrity of a sterile barrier system do not degrade with time. Accelerated studies may be a suitable substitute if real-time aging studies cannot be performed. The ASTM F1980–07(2007) Standard Guide for Accelerated Aging of Sterile Barrier Systems for Medical Devices is a widely used test for accelerated aging of the plastic components of SUSs (49).

The term Ready-To-Use is a marketing term often used to describe the benefits of single-use technology or SUS. This designation has no regulatory or scientific basis supporting suitability for use, and end users are responsible for evaluating and determining whether appropriate quality requirements have been met for their application.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Shelf Life（保存期限）：SUS 滅菌後到失效之間的有效時間。這個期限必須有數據支持，確保材料不會隨時間劣化、無菌屏障不會失效。

Real-time Aging（即時老化試驗）：在實際儲存條件下長期觀察材料性能變化，提供最可靠的保存期限數據。

Accelerated Aging（加速老化試驗）：透過提高溫度等方式加速材料老化，以較短時間預測長期性能。ASTM F1980 是業界廣泛採用的加速老化標準。

重點提示 Key Notes

「Ready-To-Use」只是行銷術語！這個名稱沒有法規或科學依據來證明其適用性。終端使用者必須自行評估 SUS 是否符合其特定應用的品質要求。切勿因供應商標示「即用型」就省略必要的評估與驗證。

比喻說明 Analogy

想像超市裡的「即食沙拉」——包裝上寫著「即食」（Ready-To-Eat），但如果你經營的是醫院餐廳，你仍然需要檢驗食材來源、微生物指標、保存溫度等，不能單純相信包裝上的標示。SUS 的「Ready-To-Use」也是同樣道理。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 為什麼即時老化試驗（real-time aging）比加速老化試驗提供更可靠的數據？
  2. 如果一個 SUS 供應商聲稱其產品「Ready-To-Use」，終端使用者還需要執行哪些評估？

5.9.2 Dealing with Particulates

Original Text

The level of particulates found in the SUS depends on the application (fluid path contents) of the system. Particles embedded in the bulk plastic as well as the presence of loose particles or fibers in an SUS are undesirable and their limits should be clearly specified in purchase agreements. If the presence of particulates in the fluid path would not have adverse effects on the product or process, then components used upstream of final filters may not require stipulated particulate levels. However, SUS components employed downstream of final filters or without particulate filtration may require stringent limits for particulates to ensure that the final product meets pharmacopeia standards. An acceptable solution is to qualify fluid path rinse volumes to meet USP <788> and USP <790> or equivalent pharmacopeia standards for large-volume injectables (10,50). In addition, the frequency of sampling and analysis may be extended from lot samples to periodic audits of model SUSs—this would be akin to the bioburden audits that are conducted for the validation of sterilization by irradiation. Applicable SUS model system design and particulate audit practices should be established between the supplier and the end user.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Particulates（微粒）：包括嵌入塑膠本體的顆粒、鬆散的粒子或纖維。在藥品生產中，微粒可能對患者安全構成風險，尤其是注射劑。

USP <788>（注射劑微粒檢測）：美國藥典標準，規定注射劑中不可見微粒的限值。

USP <790>（眼用製劑微粒檢測）：針對眼科藥品的更嚴格微粒標準。

重點提示 Key Notes

微粒管控的嚴格程度取決於 SUS 在製程中的位置：

  • 最終過濾器上游：微粒要求可以較寬鬆，因為下游還有過濾步驟
  • 最終過濾器下游：必須執行嚴格的微粒限值管控，因為已無後續過濾保護

這就是「風險分級管控」的具體體現——在最關鍵的位置投入最多的管控資源。

比喻說明 Analogy

就像家裡的淨水系統：廚房水龍頭前還有濾芯（最終過濾器），所以進水管（上游 SUS）的水質要求可以稍寬；但如果你直接從水龍頭喝水而沒有濾芯（下游 SUS，無後續過濾），那進水管的水質標準就必須達到飲用水等級。

5.9.3 Sanitation and Sterilization

Original Text

The use of an SUS in biopharmaceutical manufacturing can vary so widely that in one instance there may be no need for a specific bioburden control (e.g., as in the mixing of nonsterile buffer formulations or other raw materials) whereas in another, stringent restrictions on the incidence of a bioburden are called for (e.g., validated sterility for final formulation and filling of sterile drug product). It is critical for the end user to review processes, fluids, contact, and storage conditions for microbial quality to determine the appropriate measures to be taken.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Bioburden Control（生物負荷控制）：指控制微生物污染的程度。不同應用場景對生物負荷的要求差異極大——從非無菌緩衝液配製（允許一定生物負荷）到最終無菌充填（需經驗證的無菌保證）。

重點提示 Key Notes

決定清潔和滅菌策略的關鍵因素包括：製程類型、接觸流體的性質、接觸時間、以及儲存條件。終端使用者必須根據這些因素的綜合評估來決定適當的微生物品質管控措施——這體現了 GMP 中「基於風險的方法」（Risk-Based Approach）。

5.9.4 Bioburden Control

Original Text

The molding and extrusion of SUS components at high temperatures plus their assembly into a functional SUS in a clean and controlled environment confers advantages such as intrinsically low incidence of bioburden on the fluid paths of the SUS. Although this inherently low degree of bioburden may be adequate for nonsterile processes, many SU components or systems may be autoclaved or subjected to gamma irradiation for additional control of bioburden. Doses of irradiation as low as 8 to 10 kGy are enough to reduce intrinsic bioburden to zero; however, in practice, doses >25 kGy are used to achieve a sterility assurance level (SAL) of 10-6. The increased irradiation dosage has the advantage of attaining a bioburden level comparable to that of validated sterility without conducting the necessary validation protocols or periodic sterilization audits that would otherwise be required for a statistical claim of sterility of 10-6 (51). This may be suitable for bacterial fermentation applications and aseptic processes such as the preparation and storage of downstream chromatography and ultrafiltration buffers and intermediates.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

SAL（Sterility Assurance Level, 無菌保證水準）：表示滅菌後存在活微生物的概率。SAL 10-6 表示每一百萬個產品中最多只有一個可能是非無菌的。

Gamma Irradiation（伽瑪射線照射）：利用放射性同位素（通常是鈷-60）發出的伽瑪射線穿透塑膠材料，破壞微生物 DNA 以達到滅菌效果。

劑量與 SAL 的關係

低劑量照射：8-10 kGy → 可將固有生物負荷降至零
                        （但不足以聲稱 SAL 10⁻⁶）

高劑量照射：≥25 kGy  → 可達到 SAL 10⁻⁶
                        （等同於經驗證的無菌水準）
                        （免除定期滅菌稽核的需求）

重點提示 Key Notes

使用 ≥25 kGy 照射劑量的策略優勢在於：不需要執行完整的滅菌驗證方案和定期無菌稽核，就能達到統計上等同於 SAL 10-6 的無菌保證水準。這對下游緩衝液和中間體儲存等應用特別實用。

但要注意：更高的照射劑量也會增加材料劣化和可萃取物（Extractables）生成的風險，這需要在風險評估中一併考量。

5.10 Sterilization

5.10.1 Irradiation Sterilization

Original Text

SUSs are especially suitable to sterilization by irradiation because of the relative transparency of plastics to ionizing radiation, which allows complex systems to be sterilized without the risk of spreading the sterilizing agent throughout the fluid path. Irradiation may be used to sterilize plastics or SUS assemblies that may be unsuitable for steam sterilization. The practice of sterilizing via irradiation is well established in the medical device industry with transferable standards that can be applied to SU process equipment (51). Although no specific industry standard has been developed for the sterilization of SUSs, the industry and regulatory communities have adopted the ANSI/AAMI/ISO 11137 standard, Sterilization of Health Care Products—Radiation, and the closely related AAMI Technical Information Report TIR 33:2005, Substantiation of a Selected Sterilization Dose—Method VD max (51,52). Adaptation and implementation of these standards to the implementation of SUS have been discussed in the literature (53). The most common alternative methods mentioned in the standards are the dose substantiation methods (i.e., the verification dose max, or "VD max," methods).

In practice, a typical audit involves the selection of a single-use product (SUP) as representative of the SUS. A supplier quality audit must consider how the product family and the representative product design were derived. In cases where testing the representative product would be impractical (e.g., due to size and complexity), a portion of the sample item may be used with mathematical scaling between the actual product and the sample item. A supplier quality audit must include an understanding of the mathematical scaling technique. Since the standards require that a supplier must perform quarterly dose audits for each product family, the end-user quality audit of the SU supplier must ascertain that there is a procedure in place for the supplier to conduct quarterly dose auditing. Validation normally involves the determination of an average bioburden level in a representative master system, assessment of radiation resistance by sterility testing of the fluid path of the master system after a low "verification" dose (about 8 to 10 kGy), and calculation of a suitable dose to provide a sterility assurance level or a probability of nonsterility of 10-6 for the model product and its variants. The supplier and the end user should review the applicability of validation via master system sterilization to the end user's SUS configuration.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

ISO 11137（醫療產品輻射滅菌標準）：雖然最初是為醫療器材制定的，但已被 SUS 業界廣泛採用。這是目前 SUS 照射滅菌最主要的參考標準。

VD max（驗證劑量最大值法）：一種劑量確認方法。先以低劑量（8-10 kGy）照射代表性產品並進行無菌測試，再根據結果計算達到 SAL 10-6 所需的滅菌劑量。

Quarterly Dose Audit（每季劑量稽核）：供應商必須每季對每個產品家族執行劑量驗證，確保生物負荷水準未發生顯著變化。

比喻說明 Analogy

照射滅菌就像用 X 光機掃描行李：X 光可以穿透行李箱（塑膠外殼），不需要打開箱子就能達到「檢查」效果。同樣地，伽瑪射線可以穿透 SUS 的塑膠材料，在不打開密封系統的情況下完成滅菌——這正是照射滅菌對複雜 SUS 系統最大的優勢。

重點提示 Key Notes

照射滅菌驗證的核心流程：

  1. 選擇代表性產品（或可行的樣品部分）
  2. 測定平均生物負荷水準
  3. 以低驗證劑量（8-10 kGy）照射後進行無菌測試
  4. 計算達到 SAL 10-6 所需的正式滅菌劑量
  5. 每季執行劑量稽核以確認持續合規

終端使用者必須確認供應商的驗證方法適用於其特定的 SUS 配置。

  2. 當代表性產品因尺寸過大而無法直接測試時，可以使用什麼替代方法？

5.10.2 Sterilization with Moist Heat

Original Text

Several methods of sterilization are acceptable for use with SUS. Each is appropriate for different applications and should be evaluated for suitability.

  • Autoclaving — This technique is often used to sterilize an SUS or SU components assembled by end users (54). After that, most end users may find it more convenient to source preassembled SUSs that are sterilized by gamma irradiation.
  • Sterilize-in-place (SIP) — Steam-to-connector configuration is one example of SIP. The physical limitations of plastics limit the ability of an SUS to be sterilized by steam pressure in situ. Some SU connectors are designed to be physically attached to a stainless multiple-use port so that the tank connection can be sterilized with steam. Steam-to or steam-through connectors tend to be preconnected to tubing with an SUS at one end and are presterilized by autoclaving or irradiation. A valve or barrier mechanism is used to isolate the sterile system. The steam-to or steam-through connector is then connected to a tank, and the interface is sterilized with steam (55).
  • Gas sterilization — Although it is not a common practice, an SUS may be sterilized using a gas such as ethylene oxide. Gas sterilization is not as popular as other methods of sterilization for two reasons: 1) ethylene chlorohydrin and ethylene glycol reaction by-products can form; and 2) some ethylene oxide may be retained in the filter matrix, when a filter is present. Some of the factors that affect the use of gas sterilization are preconditioning, concentration, relative humidity, temperature, and time. The monitoring and validation of gas sterilization is conducted by destroying a suitable biological indicator such as the spores of Bacillus subtilis. To achieve successful sterilization using ethylene oxide, the SUS should be dry and wrapped in a way that allows for the influx of water vapor and gas into the fluid path. The appropriate concentration of gas and other parameters must be established during development and validation processes. It is important to rid the SUS of residual gas and other undesirable by-products after gas sterilization.
  • Resterilization — SU components are not amenable to multiple autoclave cycles. Gamma irradiation is a cumulative process and most SU components cannot be re-irradiated without causing significant degradation of physical integrity, creation of extractables, and reduction of shelf life. In addition, irradiation may reduce the resistance of the SUS to subsequent steam exposure, which may increase the formation of extractables and leachables. In general, an SUS is not designed to be resterilized.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

SIP（就地滅菌）：利用蒸氣對連接介面進行滅菌。由於塑膠材料耐溫有限，SUS 無法像不鏽鋼設備那樣全面進行 SIP，但可透過特殊設計的 Steam-to 或 Steam-through 連接器，對 SUS 與不鏽鋼槽體的接合處進行蒸氣滅菌。

EtO（環氧乙烷）滅菌：較少用於 SUS，主要因為會產生有害副產物（氯乙醇、乙二醇），且氣體可能殘留在過濾器基質中。

Resterilization（再滅菌）：SUS 設計上不支援再滅菌。多次高壓滅菌或照射會導致材料劣化、可萃取物增加、保存期縮短。

重點提示 Key Notes

SUS 滅菌方法的選擇決策樹：

  • 優先選擇：伽瑪射線照射（最適合預組裝 SUS）
  • 終端使用者自組裝：高壓滅菌（Autoclaving）
  • 不鏽鋼介面連接：SIP（蒸氣到連接器）
  • 不建議：EtO 氣體滅菌（副產物風險）
  • 禁止：再滅菌（材料劣化嚴重）

比喻說明 Analogy

SUS 就像免洗餐具——設計上就是一次性使用的。你不會把用過的免洗筷放進洗碗機再用一次，因為材質無法承受反覆清洗和高溫。同理，SUS 不設計為可再滅菌——強行再滅菌就像把紙杯放進烤箱，只會造成材料變質和安全風險。

實務應用 Practical Application

在 CDMO 環境中，如果 SUS 在使用前發現滅菌包裝破損或過期，正確的做法是整套報廢而非嘗試再滅菌。這也是為什麼庫存管理（先進先出 FIFO）和保存期限監控在 SUS 管理中如此重要。一套過期的客製化 SUS 可能需要數週才能重新訂購，直接影響生產排程。

  2. Steam-to-connector 的設計如何解決塑膠材料無法耐受高壓蒸氣的限制？
  3. 如果一個 SUS 已經過伽瑪照射滅菌但包裝破損，可以用高壓滅菌重新滅菌嗎？為什麼？

5.10.3 Sensor Technology

Original Text

The pace of technological evolution of SU sensors is so rapid that the end user may decide to use several strategies at the same time. The end user may:

  • Choose a novel method
  • Adapt an existing technology for SU
  • Apply traditional multiple-use sensors in an SUS

The end user must evaluate the technology for suitability according to the accuracy, repeatability, range, and calibration requirements. Other questions to consider are:

  • How compatible is the SU sensor with the sterilization technique?
  • What is the shelf life of the sensor?
  • What is the in-service life of the sensor?
  • How appropriate is the sensor for the SUS in question?

Early examples of SU sensors for pH and dissolved oxygen in an SU bioreactor (SUB) were based on optical differences observed on a chemical patch immersed in the process fluid. Optical sensors are calibrated with a batch-specific calibration standard instead of a zero-and-span calibration technique. The end user must be aware that optical sensors may be sensitive to photo bleaching and drift. In general, steam sterilization cycles may be more damaging to a reusable sensor than a single gamma sterilization cycle. Some SU sensors may be calibrated using a traditional two-point calibration technique prior to use without any adverse effects on sterility. Thus, SU sensors may be inherently more reliable than reusable sensors. The end user must pay attention to the advantages and limitations of a chosen SU sensor, including the specific requirements and circumstances of application.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

SU Sensor（一次性感測器）：專為一次性使用設計的製程感測器，常見於 pH 和溶氧（DO）的測量。相比傳統可重複使用的感測器，SU 感測器只經歷一次滅菌循環，可能具有更高的可靠性。

Optical Sensor（光學感測器）：利用浸入製程流體中的化學貼片（Chemical Patch）上的光學變化來測量參數。使用批次專用校準標準品而非傳統的零點/跨距校準。

Photo Bleaching（光漂白）：光學感測器的化學貼片在長時間光照下可能失去螢光強度，導致測量漂移。

比喻說明 Analogy

傳統可重複使用的感測器就像一把反覆經過高溫洗碗機清洗的菜刀——每次清洗都會造成些許磨損。而 SU 感測器就像一把全新的手術刀——只用一次，所以性能最佳。這也是為什麼 SU 感測器在某些方面可能比可重複使用的感測器更可靠。

重點提示 Key Notes

選擇 SU 感測器時必須評估的四大面向：

  • 準確度與重複性：是否符合製程要求？
  • 滅菌相容性：與 SUS 的滅菌方法是否相容？
  • 保存期限：感測器本身（尤其是化學貼片）的有效期是多久？
  • 使用壽命：在製程條件下能穩定運作多長時間？

  1. 為什麼 SU 感測器可能比傳統可重複使用的感測器更可靠？
  2. 光學式 pH 感測器的校準方式與傳統電極式有何不同？各有什麼優缺點？

5.10.4 Qualification and Validation

Original Text

The end user is responsible for process validation, and the method for validating an SUS must be determined using a risk-based approach, which considers the effective integration of the SUS design into the end user's quality systems (8). Although purchasing a preassembled, sterilized SUS allows the end user to take advantage of the qualification data generated by the SUS supplier, it is incumbent on the end user to understand the supplier's qualification approach and how it applies to the end user's specific application. The scope of process validation at the manufacturing site must encompass the materials for construction, relevant testing procedures (e.g., for heavy metals, particulates, pyrogenicity, cytotoxicity, leachables, and extractables), and information on any sterilization method used. Any deficiencies discovered during the validation process may be mitigated by applying additional targeted checks or other controls. Additional information may include pressure limitations, correct procedures for using aseptic connectors and tubing fusers, and how many times pump tubing can be reused. The validation study should ensure and document process-specific fitness for use of the SUS.

Pertinent validation concerns for an SUS include the following:

  • SU products, and qualified alternative designs, used in bulk drug substance and sterile filling should be incorporated into the media fill program.
  • SU filtration systems may need to be designed with alternative receiving containers to accommodate pre-use wetting and integrity testing.
  • Since it may not be feasible to test the SUS for leaks, the manufacturing, shipping, handling, and operation procedures should be assessed for their assurance of avoiding issues (punctures, tears, stresses) that may lead to leaks.
  • While extractables data may be suitable for predicting worst-case in-process leachables, actual testing of leachables may be necessary for final drug formulation in bulk storage containers.
  • When proprietary sterile connections are incorporated into the SUS, the end user must review supplier validation data to determine whether any additional process-specific validation may be required.
  • Proper handling and manipulation of the SUS by trained operators should be qualified.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Process Validation（製程驗證）：終端使用者的責任，必須證明 SUS 在其特定製程中能持續且可靠地運作。供應商的確認數據是起點，但不是終點。

Risk-Based Approach（基於風險的方法）：驗證的深度和廣度應與風險成正比——高風險應用（如最終充填）需要最全面的驗證。

Media Fill（培養基充填模擬）：使用培養基代替藥品進行模擬充填，以驗證無菌製程的有效性。SUS 組件必須納入培養基充填計畫中。

重點提示 Key Notes

SUS 驗證的六大關注重點：

  1. 培養基充填：SUS 和替代設計須納入 Media Fill 計畫
  2. 過濾系統設計：需考慮使用前潤濕和完整性測試的需求
  3. 洩漏風險：因無法直接測試，需從製造、運輸、操作流程保證
  4. 浸出物測試：最終藥品配方可能需要實際浸出物測試
  5. 專利連接：需審查供應商驗證數據的適用性
  6. 操作人員培訓：SUS 的正確處理和操作須經確認

比喻說明 Analogy

買一台預組裝的烤箱（預組裝 SUS），廠商會提供性能測試報告（供應商確認數據）。但這不代表你不需要在自己的廚房試烤——你仍然要確認在你的電壓、你的擺放位置、你的食譜下，這台烤箱能正常運作（製程驗證）。供應商的數據是起點，而非終點。

實務應用 Practical Application

在 CDMO 環境中，驗證 SUS 的成本和時間投入不容忽視。建議建立「SUS 驗證平台」——將常用的 SUS 配置進行一次性全面驗證（包括 E&L、微粒、無菌性、培養基充填），之後新專案如使用相同配置，可引用平台驗證數據，大幅縮短專案啟動時間。

  2. SUS 為什麼必須納入培養基充填計畫？如果不納入會有什麼風險？
  3. 「驗證過程中發現的缺陷可透過額外的針對性檢查來緩解」——請舉一個具體例子。

5.11 Integrity

5.11.1 Testing an SUS for Leaks

Original Text

The requirements for testing an SUS will depend on its intended use. The retention of structural integrity (i.e., containers do not leak) is the main concern and is a key concern in process-contact applications. For low-risk applications (e.g., sample and buffer bags), an understanding of supplier testing, performance, and training in appropriate handling procedures may be sufficient.

SUSs are increasingly used for the production of vaccines, and the egress of a live virus could have serious consequences; therefore, specific testing of individual systems is recommended.

When an SUS is used for holding media, the potential for microbial ingress that may compromise sterility becomes a significant concern. Attempts have been made to assess the maximum size of a defect that will compromise the effectiveness of a flexible bag (56). This is a complex undertaking because it depends on factors such as the surface tension of the liquid in the bag, time, temperature, and the method of measurement. In addition, the permeability of individual components (e.g., tubing and films) may differ according to the test agent (e.g., gas or dye) used.

The structural integrity of the SUS should be carefully evaluated in a collaborative manner by the end user and the supplier. Post-shipment/transport testing to evaluate the method of packaging the SUS will need to be incorporated. To test for the integrity of the fluid path during qualification—including tubing manifolds and connectors—a suggested method is to internally pressurize tubing with air and hold it under water. Provided that testing will not compromise the sterility or cleanliness of the equipment, manifolds can be inspected by manipulation to check for air bubbles at both tubing connector engagements and along the entire length of tubing. For upstream applications, the user should confirm the supplier's claims during verification and accept materials based on their documentation thereafter. A special case may need to be made for customized SUS. Demands are generally stricter for product containers; therefore, end users may wish to ask the suppliers what testing is feasible for larger containers at their sites versus the end user's site (after installation).

To test a bag container or a SUS for leakage, a suggested method (depending on the size of the SUS) is to fill the plastic bag or SUS with water inside the bin, drum, or tank and check for leaks at maximum volume. If the location of the leak is not apparent, fill the plastic container with water outside of the bin, drum, or tank on a flat, absorbent surface such as cardboard or paper. Locate the leak by identifying wet spots on the absorbent surface corresponding to a position on the container. Using the mapped location of the leak on the bag, examine the bin, drum, or tank for sharp points that may have caused the leak. Examine the leak under magnification to determine whether the leakage was caused by puncture, tear, creasing, pinhole, or another means. Track leak locations to help with root cause analysis of failure modes (e.g. handling, structural/design considerations, and material failure).

If the end user determines that a leak was caused by a manufacturing defect, the failed sample should be returned to the supplier for quality investigation and corrective action. The returned sample should include full information regarding the conditions of use and location of the leak. Suppliers should maintain records of system failures and their root causes for further analysis.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Structural Integrity（結構完整性）：SUS 的容器不會洩漏——這是最基本的品質要求。完整性測試的嚴格程度取決於應用風險。

Microbial Ingress（微生物侵入）：當 SUS 用於儲存培養基或無菌製品時，任何缺陷都可能導致微生物進入，破壞無菌性。

重點提示 Key Notes

完整性測試的風險分級策略：

  • 低風險（樣品袋、緩衝液袋）：依賴供應商測試數據和適當的操作訓練即可
  • 中風險（上游製程）：驗證時確認供應商聲明，後續依據文件接受
  • 高風險（疫苗生產、活病毒）：建議對每個系統進行個別測試
  • 最高風險（最終藥品容器）：最嚴格的測試要求

比喻說明 Analogy

洩漏偵測就像偵探辦案：先在容器中注水（犯罪現場保全），然後在吸水性表面上找到濕點（找到證據），再對照袋子上的位置找到漏點（鎖定嫌疑人），最後用放大鏡檢查是穿刺、撕裂還是材料缺陷（確認動機和手法）。追蹤每次洩漏的位置，就像建立犯罪熱點地圖——幫助你找出系統性的根本原因。

實務應用 Practical Application

洩漏測試的實務流程：

  1. 在容器/桶內以水填滿 SUS 袋至最大容量
  2. 如漏點不明顯，將裝水的袋子移到吸水表面（紙板或紙張）上
  3. 辨識吸水表面上的濕點位置，對照袋子上的相應位置
  4. 檢查容器/桶內是否有尖銳物可能刺穿袋子
  5. 用放大鏡檢查漏點類型（穿刺、撕裂、折痕、針孔）
  6. 記錄並追蹤所有洩漏位置，進行失效模式分析

  2. 運輸後測試（Post-shipment testing）為什麼需要納入完整性評估？
  3. 如果發現洩漏是由製造缺陷造成的，終端使用者應該採取哪些步驟？

5.11.2 Leak Prevention in an SUS

Original Text

Leaks in fluid management systems can lead to contamination, personnel safety risk, and product loss. A thorough investigation must be conducted to determine the cause, identify the source of the leak, and monitor corrective and preventive actions. The investigation strategy should include a leak-investigation procedure for generating standard reports. Such a report must include the location of the leak on the assembly, the surrounding temperature conditions, composition of the fluids, any adverse mechanical events, and an analysis of the failure mode. This information is particularly useful for trend analysis. Consider the example depicted in Figure 5.11.2-1 in which a bioprocess container (BC) was investigated for leaks. Individual reports for each leak were analyzed and the location of each leak was mapped and identified according to type and cause (57).

Figure 5.11.2-1: Investigation of a Bioprocess Container for Leaks

The leaks were mapped onto a deconstructed view of the BC (Figure 5.11.2-2). The map indicated the leaks were more likely to occur at the bottom and top of the bag (i.e., where the BC was handled by operators). Subsequently, the operators were trained to avoid damage due to mishandling, and this led to a significant decline in leak rates (57).

Distribution of Bioprocess Container Leak Locations: Bag Chamber, Tubing, Connector, Sampling Manifold

Figure 5.11.2-2: Identifying the Location of Leaks on a Bioprocessing Container

This, in turn, led to the following recommendations.

For end users:

  • Train operators to be aware of the root causes of leaks.
  • Remove sharp objects from the vicinity of a BC.
  • Improve lifting equipment for handling the BC.
  • Make sure that the BC fits properly into the container in order to minimize handling.

Partnering with suppliers:

  • Share leak investigation reports when returning defective products.
  • Use supplier-developed methods to improve reliability.
  • Identify and implement a more robust BC.

The summation of activities involving the hands-on steps required to successfully assemble and make a system ready for use for a specific SUS application is called deployment. This includes a defined setup and deployment stage, with standardized activities for each part, and is a recognized success factor for SUS implementation. Deployment should be a routine part of the workflow and planning for each SUS application.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Leak-Investigation Procedure（洩漏調查程序）：標準化的報告程序，記錄洩漏位置、環境條件、流體組成、機械事件和失效模式。這些數據是趨勢分析的基礎。

Trend Analysis（趨勢分析）：透過系統性收集和分析洩漏數據，找出重複出現的模式和根本原因——例如案例中發現洩漏多發生在袋子頂部和底部（操作人員拿取的位置）。

Deployment（部署）：指將 SUS 組裝並準備就緒以供特定應用使用的所有動手操作步驟的總和。標準化的部署流程是 SUS 實施成功的關鍵因素。

比喻說明 Analogy

想像你經營一家物流公司，運送液體產品。如果某條路線的包裹經常破損，你不會只是每次賠償了事——你會建立破損報告系統，追蹤每個破損的位置、原因和時間，最終發現是搬運工在卸貨時總是抓同一個角落導致破損。解決方案：培訓搬運工正確的搬運方式 + 改善包裝設計。SUS 洩漏預防的邏輯完全一樣。

重點提示 Key Notes

案例研究的關鍵發現：將洩漏位置「地圖化」後，發現洩漏集中在袋子頂部和底部——正是操作人員拿取的地方。這個發現直接指向根本原因（操作不當）而非材料缺陷。

改善措施雙管齊下：

  • 終端使用者端：操作人員培訓、移除尖銳物、改善搬運設備、確保袋子正確放入容器
  • 供應商合作端：分享洩漏調查報告、採用供應商改善方法、導入更耐用的設計

實務應用 Practical Application

在 CDMO 環境中，建立 SUS 洩漏追蹤系統的具體做法：

  1. 建立標準化的洩漏調查表單（含位置圖示、照片、環境數據）
  2. 每月彙整洩漏數據進行趨勢分析
  3. 設定洩漏率 KPI 和告警閾值
  4. 定期與供應商召開品質改善會議，分享洩漏分析結果
  5. 將洩漏預防納入操作人員的年度複訓計畫

本節重點回顧 Section Summary

  • BSE/TSE 風險：SUS 整體屬於低風險，但含牛脂衍生添加劑（如硬脂酸鈣）的塑膠樹脂仍需完成風險評估。美國 21 CFR 189.5 和 EMA 410/01 均認定經嚴格加工的牛脂衍生物不屬於高風險 BSE 材料。
  • 品質系統：終端使用者須建立完整的進料管控體系，包括採購規格書、檢驗計畫和供應商品質協議（SQA）。不同 SUS 的測試與放行標準因應用而異。
  • 有效期限與保存期限：須有即時或加速老化試驗數據支持。「Ready-To-Use」僅為行銷術語，不具法規或科學效力。
  • 微粒管控：依據 SUS 在製程中的位置（最終過濾器上游 vs 下游）實施風險分級管控，下游組件須符合 USP <788>/<790> 標準。
  • 生物負荷控制：低劑量照射（8-10 kGy）可消除固有生物負荷，但 ≥25 kGy 才能達到 SAL 10-6 的無菌保證水準。
  • 滅菌方法：伽瑪照射是 SUS 首選滅菌方式；SIP 適用於不鏽鋼介面連接；EtO 因副產物風險不建議使用；SUS 不設計為可再滅菌。
  • 感測器技術：SU 感測器只經歷一次滅菌循環，可能比可重複使用的感測器更可靠，但須注意光漂白和漂移等限制。
  • 確認與驗證：終端使用者負責製程驗證，須涵蓋材料建構、測試程序、滅菌方法，且 SUS 須納入培養基充填計畫。
  • 完整性與洩漏：完整性測試需依風險分級——從低風險（依賴供應商數據）到高風險（個別系統測試）。建立標準化洩漏調查程序和趨勢分析是預防洩漏的關鍵。
  • 洩漏預防：案例研究顯示操作不當是洩漏的主要根本原因。標準化的部署（Deployment）流程和操作人員培訓是 SUS 實施成功的關鍵因素。
