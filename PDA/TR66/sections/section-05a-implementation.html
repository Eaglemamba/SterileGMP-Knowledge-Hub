7.0 Implementation of a Single-Use System

Original Text

This section provides a framework for the implementation of an SUS. A successful implementation of an SUS takes into account the unique situation and requirements of the project and then communicates these needs effectively to stakeholders, suppliers, and sponsors. Although good project management skills are essential for any successful project implementation, SUS implementation tends to be enhanced by a stage-gate approach in which the details of the application are evaluated and the results communicated to the broader team. The approach presented in this section allows for important decisions to be taken at appropriate times to facilitate the success of the overall project.

Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Stage-Gate Approach (階段-閘門法)：這是一種結構化的專案管理方法，將整個實施過程分為多個「階段」(Stage)，每個階段之間設有「閘門」(Gateway)。只有當某一階段的目標全部達成，並通過評審後，專案才能進入下一個階段。

這種方法的核心價值在於：在每個閘門處提供「暫停、評估、決策」的機會，避免在錯誤的方向上投入過多資源。

比喻說明 Analogy

想像你正在蓋一棟房子。你不會在地基還沒打好時就開始裝潢。Stage-Gate 就像建築工程的各個檢查點：地基驗收通過 (G1) 才進入結構施工 (S2)；結構通過檢驗 (G2) 才進入水電配管 (S3)。每個閘門就是「監工來驗收」的時刻，確保前一步做對了再往下走。

重點提示 Key Notes

TR66 在此強調 SUS 實施不是簡單的設備採購，而是一個需要跨部門協調、多階段驗證的系統工程。即使是經驗豐富的工程師，忽略結構化流程也可能導致關鍵步驟遺漏。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 為什麼 SUS 實施特別適合使用 Stage-Gate 方法，而不是傳統的線性專案管理？
  2. 如果你的公司是一家小型 CDMO，你認為哪些階段可以合併？哪些絕對不能省略？
7.1 Implementation Road Map

Original Text

Figure 7.1-1 shows an implementation road map based on a natural progression of workflows. These workflows use a manufacturing strategy that provides the foundation for the main SUS implementation process described in this section (see also Figures 3.0-1 and 3.0-2). Table 7.1-1 provides a more detailed overview of each of the implementation stages together with key documentary outputs for the entire SUS implementation process.

Stages 3 to 7, as outlined in the figure below, can also be aligned with ICH Q8 and Q9 (Quality-by-Design and Quality Risk Management) processes/tools: stakeholder management, risk management, and process validation and verification.

The SUS implementation road map consists of a series of gateways (G1 to G8) and stages (S1 to S7). The stages (shown in yellow, blue, and green boxes) describe the natural progression of workflows in which information is gathered and documented. Specific goals (summarized in the drop-down boxes) must be attained before progressing to the next gateway. The process starts with a manufacturing strategy (S1 and S2); described in detail in Section 3.5) and proceeds into the main project-implementation, stage-gateway process via G3.

The implementation process is not necessarily linear: a more in-depth study of elements during S1 and S2 may negate the need for some of these activities at the later stages. For example, if a more elaborate technical assessment and definition of general principles in the SUS scope document are performed in S1 and S2, entry can occur at G4. A parallel approach from G1 to G3 and from G4 to G8 may facilitate or accelerate effective project completion. S3 and S4 may also be pursued in parallel. However, if one or more of these modifications are applied to the road map, care should be exercised that the implementation process does not lose structure and that the comprehensive treatment of all of the elements necessary remains in place.

Sections 4, 5, and 6 of this technical report provide additional information for reviewing the scope and activities of various workflows.

Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Implementation Road Map (實施路線圖)：這是 TR66 的核心框架，由 8 個閘門 (G1-G8) 和 7 個階段 (S1-S7) 組成：

  • S1：技術調查與商業目標對齊 (Manufacturing Strategy Part 1)
  • S2：影響評估與實施方法 (Manufacturing Strategy Part 2)
  • S3：範疇界定 (Scoping) - 制定 SUS 策略文件
  • S4：商業論證 (Business Case) - 啟動專案執行計畫 (PEP)
  • S5：開發 (Development) - 使用者需求與採購
  • S6：測試與確效 (Testing & Validation) - C&Q、PV、人員訓練
  • S7：啟動 (Launch) - 物料管理與日常運營

重點提示 Key Notes

非線性彈性：TR66 特別指出這個路線圖不一定是線性的。如果在 S1/S2 做了更深入的技術評估，可以直接從 G4 切入，跳過 S3 的部分工作。S3 和 S4 也可以平行推進。

但 TR66 也警告：如果修改了路線圖的順序，必須確保不會遺失結構性，所有必要元素仍然需要被完整處理。

比喻說明 Analogy

這個路線圖就像是導航 GPS 的「建議路線」。GPS 會建議你走高速公路 (標準線性流程)，但如果你對當地路況很熟悉 (S1/S2 做得很深入)，你可以走捷徑 (直接從 G4 進入)。但無論你走哪條路，目的地 (成功實施 SUS) 和必經的關鍵路口 (閘門檢查) 都不能跳過。

實務應用 Practical Application

CDMO 場景：假設你的 CDMO 已經有多年使用 SU bioreactor 的經驗，現在要導入新的 SU 混合系統。由於你已經有成熟的供應商審核流程和品質系統，S1/S2 的許多工作已經在組織層面完成，你可以直接從 G4 進入，專注於這個特定設備的商業論證和技術開發。

  1. 在什麼情況下，S3 和 S4 的平行推進是合理的？在什麼情況下應該嚴格按順序執行？
  2. 對於一個從未使用過 SUS 的新設廠專案，你認為哪個閘門 (G1-G8) 是最關鍵的「不可回頭」決策點？為什麼？

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

三大實施主題 (Implementation Themes)：這三個主題不是階段性的活動，而是貫穿整個實施過程的橫向支柱：

7.2.1 Stakeholder Management

Original Text

Stakeholder management is important throughout the SUS implementation process. It helps an organization to achieve strategic objectives by 1) interpreting and influencing both the external and internal environments, and 2) creating positive relationships with stakeholders by appropriately managing their expectations and agreed objectives.

A structured approach is important and beneficial to every stage of implementation (Table 7.1-1). Identifying stakeholders who are involved across multiple functional areas would encourage teamwork and facilitate effective communication. Deficient stakeholder management may lead to adverse conditions such as diverging implementation and missed opportunities for standardization and process streamlining (e.g., the parallel pursuit of several different types of sterile connectors or storage bags, when multiple SUS technologies or suppliers are engaged). It may be helpful to create a "power" grid that classifies stakeholders according to their influence and their interest in the project. Figure 7.2.1-1 shows an example for preparing user requirements and managing stakeholders.

Figure 7.2.1-1: An Example of a Stakeholder Power Grid

The power grid allows the project team to identify, gather input from, and manage internal or external stakeholders. It is also useful for preparing SUS user requirements, and it fosters effective communication among project team members.

Low Interest / Motivation High Interest / Motivation
High Influence / Power Manage Satisfaction: Management Board, Quality Department Manage Carefully: Shareholders, Parent Company, Regulators, HSE Staff
Low Influence / Power Be Informed: Internal memos & positions, Industry initiatives (external), Websites/Blogs, Conferences Keep Informed: Project Team, Suppliers, Customer

Effective stakeholder management may be achieved by 1) evaluating company governance and management processes in order to identify functional areas, and 2) using a responsible, accountable, consulted, and informed (RACI) template to clearly define roles and responsibilities within functional areas and between stakeholders (71).

Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Stakeholder Power Grid (利害關係人權力格)：這是一個 2x2 矩陣，根據「影響力/權力」和「興趣/動機」兩個維度來分類利害關係人：

  • 高權力 + 高興趣 = Manage Carefully (謹慎管理)：股東、母公司、法規單位、HSE 人員。這些人既有決策權又很關心結果，是最需要主動溝通和管理的群體。
  • 高權力 + 低興趣 = Manage Satisfaction (管理滿意度)：管理層、品質部門。他們有權否決決策但可能不深入參與日常細節，所以要確保他們對整體方向滿意。
  • 低權力 + 高興趣 = Keep Informed (保持知情)：專案團隊、供應商、客戶。他們很關心但影響力有限，要定期更新資訊。
  • 低權力 + 低興趣 = Be Informed (被動知會)：透過內部備忘錄、產業活動等管道即可。

重點提示 Key Notes

缺乏利害關係人管理的後果：TR66 特別提到一個常見陷阱 - 當多個 SUS 技術或供應商同時被導入時，如果缺乏協調，不同部門可能會各自選擇不同類型的無菌連接器或儲存袋，導致標準化失敗和流程碎片化。

這在 CDMO 尤其常見，因為不同客戶的產品可能推動不同的技術選擇。

比喻說明 Analogy

利害關係人管理就像籌備一場大型婚禮。新人的父母 (高權力 + 高興趣) 需要「謹慎管理」，充分溝通每一個決定；公司老闆 (高權力 + 低興趣) 只需要知道你什麼時候要請假就好；朋友們 (低權力 + 高興趣) 需要被告知日期和地點；而其他同事 (低權力 + 低興趣) 只需要收到一封通知信即可。

RACI 矩陣解析

RACI Template (RACI 矩陣)：是一個定義角色與責任的工具：

  • R (Responsible / 負責執行)：實際做這件事的人
  • A (Accountable / 當責)：最終為結果負責的人（每個任務只能有一個 A）
  • C (Consulted / 諮詢)：提供意見的專家
  • I (Informed / 知會)：需要被告知結果的人

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 在你的組織中，SUS 導入專案的「Manage Carefully」象限中會有哪些利害關係人？法規部門是否應該在這個象限？
  2. 如果供應商（如 Sartorius、Pall）在你的 Power Grid 中從「Keep Informed」移到「Manage Carefully」，這意味著什麼？
7.2.2 Risk Management

Original Text

As shown in Figure 7.1-1, risk management is important throughout the lifecycle of a project (specifically from Sections 7.4 to 7.7). Application of a quality risk management (QRM) program during SUS project implementation confers benefits on the manufacturer, patients, quality auditors, and regulators. These benefits come from an understanding of product and process risks, individual assessments, mitigation or acceptance, communication, and review as outlined in Section 3.3.1 (4,7,72).

It is critical for an organization to use a QRM program to underpin product quality. An effective QRM program must be organized as a team effort with regular review meetings involving stakeholders. A company charter should provide policies and guidelines for when and how to apply risk management, along with the necessary implementation level and control. The risk assessment and risk mitigation tools used should be commensurate with the objectives of the project and the quality impact of the SUS being addressed.

Risk Assessment Tools

  • Information gathering (data mining) and data evaluation
  • Ad hoc applications (e.g., using simple tables that delineate some risks associated with predefined implementation scenarios)
  • Directional-risk profiles
  • Fishbone analysis
  • More robust applications such as failure mode and effects analysis (FMEA), in which events are assessed in terms of impact, severity, probability, and detectability

Risk Mitigation Tools

  • Identification and ranking of risks, their impact, and acceptance or mitigation (i.e., applying and comparing current and future states)
  • At a minor level, mitigation can range from the requirement for further data mining to simple process adjustments
  • For more elaborate subjects, quality audits that employ an efficient document management system are appropriate
  • A specified risk-review period commensurate with the risk in question

In-depth reviews of common risk-assessment techniques and their applications in risk management are available in specialized literature (4,7,72,73). A more detailed evaluation of the risks associated with SUS implementation and the necessary qualification and validation studies is reviewed in Section 5.2. Some examples of QRM application in SUS implementation are shown in Tables 7.2.3-1 to 7.2.3-3.

Once the severity of the risk and the likelihood of its detection have been assessed, the most suitable detection tool can be selected.

Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Quality Risk Management, QRM (品質風險管理)：根據 ICH Q9 的框架，QRM 是一個系統化的過程，包含：

  • 風險識別 (Risk Identification)：找出可能出錯的事情
  • 風險分析 (Risk Analysis)：評估發生機率和影響程度
  • 風險評價 (Risk Evaluation)：決定是否可接受
  • 風險控制 (Risk Control)：減緩或接受
  • 風險溝通 (Risk Communication)：與利害關係人分享結果
  • 風險審查 (Risk Review)：定期回顧和更新

風險評估工具層級

TR66 列出的工具從簡單到複雜，形成一個漸進式工具包：

  • 最簡單：資料蒐集 (Data Mining) - 收集歷史數據和供應商資訊
  • 簡易：Ad Hoc 風險表 - 用簡單表格列出預定情境的風險
  • 中等：方向性風險概況 (Directional Risk Profile) - 用矩陣排列風險優先順序
  • 中等：魚骨圖 (Fishbone / Ishikawa) - 找出根本原因
  • 最嚴謹：FMEA - 系統性評估影響、嚴重度、發生機率和可偵測性

選擇哪個工具取決於風險的嚴重程度和專案的複雜度 - 這就是「與目標相稱 (commensurate)」的原則。

比喻說明 Analogy

風險評估工具的選擇就像看病就醫。如果只是小感冒 (低風險)，你量個體溫吃個藥就好 (data mining + ad hoc)；如果是反覆頭痛 (中等風險)，你需要做 X 光或 CT (fishbone analysis)；如果懷疑是嚴重疾病 (高風險、影響產品品質)，你需要做完整的健康檢查包括 MRI、血液分析等全套檢查 (FMEA)。

重點提示 Key Notes

團隊協作是關鍵：TR66 特別強調 QRM 必須是「team effort」，需要定期召開利害關係人參與的審查會議。在實務上，許多 SUS 風險評估失敗的原因不是工具選錯，而是沒有納入正確的人。例如，如果風險評估會議沒有邀請供應商技術代表，可能會遺漏重要的材料相容性風險。

7.2.3 Process Validation and Verification (PVV)

Original Text

In pharmaceutical manufacturing, process validation (PV) is the collection and evaluation of data from the process design stage through commercial production that establishes scientific evidence that a process is capable of consistently delivering quality product (8). The U.S FDA and EMA consider PV a requirement in both general and specific terms in Good Manufacturing Practice (74,75). Verification is a systematic approach for ensuring that manufacturing systems (acting either singly or in combination) are suitable for their intended use, have been properly installed, and are operating correctly. This is an umbrella term that encompasses all types of activities to assuring systems are fit for use, such as qualification, commissioning and qualification, verification, system validation, and others (18). Verification (or process verification) is an important concept for SUSs and is a primary control strategy for sourcing from suppliers. This remains active throughout the lifecycle of the project.

PVV is a tool that requires an integrated project management approach that involves all pertinent stakeholders (e.g., a cross-company project team) and full support from management. A science- and risk-based approach to PVV tends to generate a thorough understanding of the manufacturing process, which in turn enhances product quality and patient safety (8,74). ICH guidance documents, such as ICH Q8, Q9, and Q10, provide useful guidelines for development, risk analysis, and product control (3-5). A draft guideline on process validation for the manufacture of biotechnology-derived active substances and a guideline on process validation for finished products has been published by EMA (75,76).

SUS process validation and verification is normally performed using a lifecycle approach. The FDA 2011 process validation guidance and PDA Technical Report 60: Process Validation - A Lifecycle Approach provide useful models (8,74). Within that model, SUS implementation becomes part of process validation stages 1 and 2 (S1 and S2). Productive use of SUS is part of process validation S3. Additional technical guidance is available for this and for a phased approach to quality systems and the application of GMP as well (63,77).

Continuous quality verification can also be used. The knowledge obtained as the product lifecycle progresses will allow a framework to be established in order to attain mitigation of risks, reduction of process variability, enhancement of process capability, definition of enhancement of process design space, and improvements in product quality. These may lead to other benefits that address both compliance and operational goals (e.g., continuous process improvement) (78). Figure 7.2.3-1 illustrates two component elements—validation prerequisites and conformance batches—for process validation S2 that confer greater clarity on SUS implementation.

"SUS process validation and verification is normally performed using a lifecycle approach. Within that model, SUS implementation becomes part of process validation stages 1 and 2 (S1 and S2). Productive use of SUS is part of process validation S3."

Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Process Validation (製程確效) vs Verification (驗證)：理解這兩個術語的差異至關重要：

  • Process Validation (PV)：收集和評估從製程設計到商業生產的數據，建立科學證據證明製程能夠「持續」(consistently) 交付品質合格的產品。這是 FDA/EMA 的 GMP 要求。
  • Verification (驗證)：一個更廣泛的傘式術語 (umbrella term)，涵蓋所有確保製造系統適合其預期用途的活動。包括：qualification、commissioning、system validation 等。

在 SUS 的脈絡下，Verification 特別重要，因為它是控制供應商品質的主要策略 (primary control strategy)。你無法在供應商工廠進行完整的 PV，但你可以透過驗證活動確認供應商交付的 SUS 符合要求。

FDA 製程確效生命週期模型

FDA 2011 年製程確效指引將 PV 分為三個階段，TR66 將 SUS 實施對應到這個模型中：

  • Stage 1 - Process Design (製程設計)：對應 SUS 實施的前期工作 - 確定製程參數、CQA、設計空間
  • Stage 2 - Process Qualification (製程資格確認)：對應 SUS 的 validation prerequisites + conformance batches - 證明製程在商業規模下能夠重現
  • Stage 3 - Continued Process Verification (持續製程驗證)：對應 SUS 的日常生產運營 - 持續監控確保製程維持在驗證過的狀態

比喻說明 Analogy

PV 和 Verification 的關係就像「考駕照」和「日常車檢」的關係。Process Validation 就像通過駕照考試 - 你需要證明你有能力安全駕駛 (consistently deliver quality)。Verification 則像每次上路前檢查輪胎氣壓、機油和剎車 - 確保車子 (系統) 仍然適合使用。對於 SUS，因為每次使用的都是「新車零件」(一次性組件)，所以每次使用前的 Verification 格外重要。

重點提示 Key Notes

Continuous Quality Verification (持續品質驗證)：TR66 提到這是一個進階概念，隨著產品生命週期的推進，累積的知識可以用來：

  • 降低風險 (mitigation of risks)
  • 減少製程變異 (reduction of process variability)
  • 提升製程能力 (enhancement of process capability)
  • 定義/擴展設計空間 (design space)
  • 提升產品品質

這與 ICH Q10 的「持續改善」(continual improvement) 理念完全一致。

法規對接 Regulatory Alignment

ICH Q8/Q9/Q10 與 SUS 實施的關係：

  • ICH Q8 (Pharmaceutical Development)：提供 QbD 框架，定義設計空間和 CQA
  • ICH Q9 (Quality Risk Management)：提供風險管理方法論
  • ICH Q10 (Pharmaceutical Quality System)：提供品質系統和持續改善的框架

TR66 的 S3-S7 階段可以直接對應到這三個 ICH 指引的要求，這使得 SUS 實施與法規期望之間有了清晰的橋梁。

實務應用 Practical Application

CDMO 場景：當你在準備向客戶交付的 PV 文件包 (validation package) 時，需要清楚區分 SUS 相關的驗證活動屬於 FDA PV 的哪個階段。例如：

  • PV Stage 1：SU bioreactor 的材料相容性研究、extractables 評估、製程參數範圍確認
  • PV Stage 2：使用 SU bioreactor 進行的 conformance batches (通常 3 批)，包括 leachables 分析
  • PV Stage 3：日常生產中的 SUS 來料檢驗、供應商審核、持續監控趨勢
