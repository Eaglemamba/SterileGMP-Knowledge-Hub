4.9 Storage 儲存

原文內容 Original Text

Appropriate storage is necessary in order to maintain the quality of process solutions (e.g., buffers and culture media), intermediates, and bulk drug substance until they are ready for further processing. Long-term storage of process intermediates often requires rapid, controlled freezing or cryogenic preservation. The storage bag must be qualified for the specific use, with a focus on maintaining bag integrity at the lower of the storage or freezing temperatures.

4.9.1 Technological Examples

A storage bag with a polyethylene fluid contact layer is normally used to store aqueous-based liquid intermediates. Polyethylene polymers (particularly low-density grades) have broad chemical compatibility and very low reactivity to most intermediates and process fluids. Other materials like fluoropolymers are used to address specific process compatibility concerns.

Typical plastics used for freezing applications include poly(tetrafluoroethylene) (PTFE) and poly(ethylene vinyl acetate) (EVA). These materials are particularly good at retaining their mechanical strength at very low freezing temperatures. Specific bag systems have been integrated into commercially available controlled freeze-thaw systems. A gas barrier such as ethylene vinyl alcohol (EVOH) is typically used with EVA to create a multilayered composite film that offers mechanical strength and resistance to gas permeation.

For short-term storage, process intermediates in standard bags are normally kept at ambient temperature or in a refrigerator. Long-term storage of process intermediates often requires rapid, controlled freezing or cryogenic preservation (refer to Section 4.10, Freezing). Factors that affect the storage of process intermediates are summarized in Table 4.9.1-1.

Table 4.9.1-1 Specific Considerations for the Storage of Process Intermediates
FactorConsideration
Potential for loss of quality (e.g., chemical, physical, or functional) of the process intermediate In some cases, the process intermediates may have adverse interactions with the polymeric bag containers of the SUS. A special protective film may have to be applied to a bag containing oxygen-sensitive or photolabile process intermediates. It is vital to make sure that chemicals leaching from the polymer in the course of storage do not have adverse effects on the intermediates, buffer, or culture media that might get carried to unit operations.
Maintenance of system integrity Ensure the integrity of the SUS by the careful choice and qualification of the bag, use of a supporting structure, and adherence to good laboratory practices.
Microbial quality Ensure that sterility and bioburden control are maintained.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Storage (儲存)：在生物製藥製程中，儲存是連接各單元操作的「緩衝帶」。無論是緩衝液、培養基還是製程中間體，都需要在適當條件下保存，以維持其品質屬性直到下一步處理。

Polyethylene Fluid Contact Layer (聚乙烯流體接觸層)：PE（特別是 LDPE 低密度聚乙烯）是最常見的 SUS 袋體接液面材料，因其化學惰性高、反應性極低，適用於大多數水溶液中間體。

EVOH Gas Barrier (EVOH 氣體阻隔層)：EVA 袋與 EVOH 結合形成多層複合膜，可同時提供低溫機械強度和氣體滲透阻隔，這對於氧氣敏感的蛋白質藥物特別重要。

比喻說明 Analogy

想像你去超市買了生鮮食品 — 有些可以放在室溫（短期儲存），有些必須冰在冰箱（冷藏），更脆弱的需要放冷凍庫（冷凍保存）。而你用來裝食物的容器材質也很重要：塑膠保鮮盒、真空袋或鋁箔袋各有其阻隔效果。SUS 的儲存袋選擇也是同樣邏輯 — 要根據中間體的敏感度選擇材料和溫度條件。

重點提示 Key Notes

Leachables 風險：長期儲存時，聚合物材料中的化學物質可能會浸出（leach）到製程液體中。這些 leachables 不僅影響中間體品質，還可能被「帶」到下游單元操作中，對最終產品造成影響。因此必須做儲存相容性研究。

光敏性與氧敏性保護：對於含光敏或氧敏成分的中間體，需使用特殊保護膜（如遮光膜或氧氣阻隔膜），這是標準 PE 袋可能無法滿足的地方。

  2. 如果你的製程中間體是一種氧氣敏感的單株抗體，你會如何選擇儲存袋的膜層結構？

4.10 Freezing 冷凍

原文內容 Original Text

The end user may use freezing techniques to separate process pools or intermediates and hold them for a short time at the process temperature and conditions or for a longer duration before another production step. For long durations, the end user may freeze the intermediate directly in an SUS that is designed for freezing. Uncontrolled freezing is performed in standard laboratory freezers, purpose-designed blast cells, or nitrogen freezing chambers. Controlled freeze-and-thaw equipment (e.g., a plate freezer) is used to regulate the rate of freezing and thawing of the contents of each SU bag. To maintain the integrity of the bag containing the frozen process intermediates or bulk drug substances, careful attention must be paid to the reusable storage container design and logistics of shipping and handling.

4.10.1 Technological Examples

  • Unprotected small-scale bag: It is common practice to freeze an unprotected bag (less than 20 L) that is not specifically designed for the application. Compared to other technologies, the failure rates of frozen unprotected bags tend to be higher (Table 4.10.1-1).
  • Separate bag shell — The purpose of a bag shell is to maintain the structural integrity of a frozen bag by protecting it from bumps and drops during handling at low temperatures. Bag shells are made of either rigid plastics or rigid foam construction.
  • Integrated shell-protected bag — The shell design includes a tubing and fitting compartment for security and storage at the most common point of failure. Off-the-shelf International Safe Transit Association (ISTA) and ASTM-qualified shipping containers can also be used.
  • Controlled freeze-thaw system, bag, frame, and logistics equipment — A controlled freeze-thaw system uses contact freezing technology to allow for the reproducible freezing of the contents of the bag. Contact freezing plates maintain freezing distances by maintaining the filled bag between two freezing contact plates. In addition, the rate of heat transfer from the bags is increased in a controlled freeze-thaw system, and this decreases the time required for freezing. Thus, a controlled freeze-thaw system is part of an integrated refrigeration system for maintaining the integrity of the bag and the quality of its contents during fill, transport, freezing, and thawing.
Table 4.10.1-1 Specific Considerations for Freezing
FactorConsideration
Storage temperature At the typical storage temperature range of -40°C to -70°C, most polymer films, tubing, and fittings become brittle due to being below their glass transition temperature. Therefore, it is important to perform a structural integrity test on each freezing system.
Handling Effective staff training is critical to the successful implementation of a freeze-thaw step in an SUS.
Shipping Cold-chain logistics manufacturers are making robust, off-the-shelf solutions commercially available to the biopharmaceutical industry.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Controlled Freeze-Thaw (控制凍融)：與在一般冰箱中隨意冷凍（uncontrolled freezing）不同，控制凍融系統使用接觸式冷凍板來精確控制冷凍速率。這對於生物藥品（如蛋白質、抗體）特別重要，因為不均勻的冷凍會導致局部濃縮效應（cryoconcentration），可能使蛋白質聚集或降解。

Glass Transition Temperature (玻璃轉化溫度, Tg)：在 -40°C 至 -70°C 的典型儲存溫度下，大多數高分子材料會低於其 Tg 而變脆。這就是為什麼冷凍用 SUS 需要選擇 PTFE 或 EVA 等在低溫下仍保持機械強度的特殊材料。

比喻說明 Analogy

這就像搬運冰雕一樣 — 你不會用普通紙箱搬運冰雕，而需要專用的保護外殼和緩衝材料。冷凍袋也是如此：未受保護的小袋在低溫下變脆，稍有碰撞就可能破裂（就像冰雕碎裂）。帶有硬殼保護的整合式袋體（integrated shell-protected bag）就是為冰雕量身訂做的運輸箱。

重點提示 Key Notes

人員培訓至關重要：文件特別強調「有效的員工培訓對於成功實施 SUS 凍融步驟至關重要」。這是因為冷凍袋在低溫下非常脆弱，操作人員的不當處理（如摔落、急速搬動）是袋體破損的主要原因之一。

管路接頭是最常見的失效點：整合式外殼設計特別包含管路和接頭的保護腔室，因為這些連接點在低溫下最容易發生洩漏或斷裂。

實務應用 Practical Application

假設你是一家 CDMO，正在為客戶的單株抗體原液（bulk drug substance）建立冷凍保存流程。你需要考慮：

  • 選擇 EVA/EVOH 多層膜冷凍袋，確保在 -70°C 下的機械強度
  • 投資控制凍融設備（如 Sartorius 或 CryoFin 系統）以確保可重現的冷凍曲線
  • 建立 SOP 訓練操作人員正確處理冷凍袋
  • 規劃冷鏈運輸方案，使用 ISTA/ASTM 合格的運輸容器

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 為什麼「非控制凍融」在未受保護的小袋中失敗率較高？從材料科學和操作因素兩方面解釋。
  2. 控制凍融系統的「接觸式冷凍板」如何改善冷凍均勻性？這對蛋白質品質有何影響？

4.11 Filtration 過濾

原文內容 Original Text

Normal flow filtration (NFF) or direct flow filtration (DFF) devices are used in biopharmaceutical manufacturing to remove contaminants such as particulates, microbial cells, and virus particles. Cross flow filtration (CFF) or tangential flow filtration (TFF) devices are used to reduce the volume of a buffer and/or perform buffer exchange as part of the downstream process.

4.11.1 Technological Examples

CFF devices are commercially available in the following structural formats: flat-sheet cassettes, and hollow fiber cartridges. Currently, only a hollow-fiber cartridge can be classified as an SU filter device. Structurally, there is little difference between SU and MU CFF hollow fiber devices. Functionally, an SU CFF hollow fiber device provides a higher degree of cleanliness and sterility compared to that of a SU flat sheet device. Current flat sheet devices marketed as single-use contain a disposable flow path (e.g., ports, gaskets, and the filter device), which is then assembled into a stainless steel holder that provides the compression and support required for filtration. NFF devices have been commercially available in an SU format for many years, and structurally, they contain a depth filter media or membrane for separation via size exclusion.

Table 4.11.1-1 Specific Considerations for Filtration
FactorConsideration
Device design Versions of most NFF and hollow fiber CFF devices can be easily accommodated in a self-enclosed sterilized assembly (e.g., tubing, bags, and filters).
Microbial control In general, flat-sheet devices are not supplied presterilized; however, they can be steam sterilized on-site. As an SU device, they allow a way to integrate a known separations technology into an SU unit operation.
Pre- and post-use integrity testing From a technical perspective, it is less important to conduct a pre-use integrity test of a sterilizing filter that has been sterilized with gamma radiation. The pre-use test has a primary purpose of detecting a damaged filter, a purpose that has roots in steam sterilization, which has known mechanisms by which filters can be damaged. These mechanisms do not exist with gamma radiation sterilization.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

NFF vs CFF（正向過濾 vs 交叉流過濾）：

  • NFF (Normal Flow Filtration / 正向過濾)：液體垂直通過濾膜，用於去除微粒、微生物和病毒顆粒。深層過濾（depth filter）和除菌過濾（sterilizing filter）都屬於此類。
  • CFF (Cross Flow Filtration / 交叉流過濾)：液體平行於濾膜表面流動，用於濃縮和緩衝液交換。在下游純化中常稱為 TFF (Tangential Flow Filtration)。

Disposable Flow Path (拋棄式流路)：目前的 SU 平板式 CFF 設備其實是「半一次性」的 — 產品接觸的流路（接口、墊片、膜匣）是一次性的，但壓緊和支撐的不鏽鋼支架是重複使用的。

比喻說明 Analogy

NFF 就像咖啡濾紙 — 水從上方直直穿過濾紙，渣滓被攔截在上面。CFF 則像洗腎機 — 血液沿著膜管橫向流動，小分子通過膜壁被濾出，大分子繼續流動回體內。這兩種完全不同的過濾原理決定了它們在製程中的不同角色。

重點提示 Key Notes

Gamma 滅菌 vs 蒸汽滅菌對完整性測試的影響：這是一個非常重要但容易被忽略的觀點。傳統的 PUPSIT（使用前滅菌後完整性測試）源於蒸汽滅菌可能損壞濾器的風險（如高溫導致膜收縮、密封失效等）。然而，gamma 射線滅菌並不存在這些損壞機制，因此從「技術角度」來看，對 gamma 滅菌的除菌過濾器進行 PUPSIT 的必要性較低。

但請注意：法規層面上，許多監管機構仍要求進行 PUPSIT，不論滅菌方式為何。這是一個技術觀點與法規要求可能不完全一致的領域。

  2. 如果你要將一個傳統不鏽鋼過濾系統轉換為 SUS，最重要的評估項目是什麼？

4.12 Centrifugation 離心

原文內容 Original Text

Centrifugation is often used for cell harvest as an alternative to filtration, and SU formats of this technology are emerging for low shear separations, including harvest clarification; cell concentration, wash, and harvest; and cell separation. These systems utilize a gamma-irradiated single-use module or polymeric liner for product contact and, as such, eliminate the need for CIP or manual cleaning and SIP or hot water for injection (WFI) for bioburden sterilization and control of traditional centrifuges. Flow rates vary depending on the manufacture and design of the equipment, ranging from <10 to 100 L per minute at <100X to 8000X G force. Continuous and batch centrifugation technologies are available.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

SU Centrifugation（一次性離心）：傳統離心機需要繁複的 CIP/SIP 程序來清洗和滅菌產品接觸面。SU 離心系統使用 gamma 射線預滅菌的一次性模組或高分子內襯，直接消除了清洗和滅菌的需求。

應用範圍：主要用於低剪切力的分離操作，包括收穫澄清（harvest clarification）、細胞濃縮/洗滌/收穫，以及細胞分離。流速範圍從 <10 L/min 到 100 L/min，G 力從 <100X 到 8000X。

比喻說明 Analogy

傳統離心機就像餐廳的大型洗碗機 — 每次使用後都要徹底清洗和消毒。而 SU 離心模組就像免洗餐具 — 使用後直接丟棄，省去了清洗的時間和人力成本。對於 CDMO 來說，這意味著換批（changeover）時間大幅縮短。

重點提示 Key Notes

新興技術：文件使用「emerging」一詞描述 SU 離心技術，表示在 2014 年撰寫時這仍是相對較新的領域。此後已有更多商業化產品問世（如 kSep、Carr Centritech 等），但在選用時仍需仔細評估其處理能力、剪切力對產品的影響，以及一次性模組的成本效益。

4.13 Chromatography 層析

原文內容 Original Text

Biomolecules are purified using chromatographic techniques that separate them according to differences in their specific physical or chemical properties. SU formats for various chromatography steps are available.

4.13.1 Technological Examples

Chromatographic media can be divided into two main categories: resin- or bead-based and membrane-based. Membrane-based chromatographic devices are also known as membrane adsorbers, and because they are membrane-based, they share similar physical and structural features with traditional filter devices. In order to produce membrane-based chromatographic devices with specific properties, the membrane is chemically derivatized with ligands.

Disposable bead-based chromatographic devices tend to contain the same resins and have the same general physical structure as traditional packed-bed columns that are made of glass and stainless steel. Bead-based chromatographic devices are commercially available as prepacked units made of plastic structural parts. They are useful for product campaigning because they can be used more than once. As with traditional packed-bed columns, sterilization methods such as gamma irradiation or moist heat cannot be used with disposable bead-based chromatographic devices; instead, rigorous chemical sanitization is used. As with multiple-use columns, the preservative solution in the SU device must be flushed before the unit is used. Conventional chromatography and SU chromatography both require large amounts of process fluids like buffers (refer to Section 4.8.1, Fluid Management, and Section 4.17, Transportation).

Table 4.13.1-1 Specific Considerations for Chromatography
FactorConsideration
Scaling Make sure that the method of choice can be scaled to a larger SUS or to an MUS.
Flow rate and capacity Membrane adsorbers are used in polishing steps because they have a relatively high flow rate and low binding capacity.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Resin-based vs Membrane-based Chromatography（樹脂基 vs 膜基層析）：

  • 樹脂基（Bead-based）：與傳統層析柱使用相同的填充樹脂，只是管柱材料從玻璃/不鏽鋼改為塑膠。預充填管柱可重複使用（campaign mode），適合需要高結合容量的捕獲步驟（capture step）。
  • 膜基（Membrane adsorbers）：以膜為載體，經化學修飾接上配體（ligand）。流速高但結合容量低，因此主要用於精製步驟（polishing），如去除殘留的 DNA、HCP 或病毒。

Chemical Sanitization（化學消毒）：與其他 SUS 不同，預充填樹脂管柱無法用 gamma 射線或濕熱滅菌（會破壞樹脂），必須使用 NaOH 等化學消毒劑進行嚴格的清洗消毒。

比喻說明 Analogy

樹脂基層析柱就像是用即棄塑膠杯裝的高級濾掛咖啡 — 裡面的咖啡粉（樹脂）品質跟傳統陶瓷壺用的一樣好，只是外面的容器變成了塑膠。而膜基層析則像是快速濾水壺的濾芯 — 水流通過快，適合最後的精製處理，但不適合大量的初步分離。

重點提示 Key Notes

放大性（Scalability）很關鍵：文件特別提醒要確保所選方法能夠從 SU 規模放大到更大的 SUS 或轉回 MUS。這對於 CDMO 而言尤其重要 — 客戶的產品可能需要從小批量生產放大到商業規模。

緩衝液用量：無論是傳統層析還是 SU 層析，都需要大量緩衝液，這牽涉到流體管理（4.8.1）和運輸（4.17）的問題。對 CDMO 來說，緩衝液的配製和供應鏈管理是一個重要的營運考量。

  2. 使用預充填 SU 層析柱進行 campaign 製造時，需要注意哪些與清潔消毒相關的風險？
  3. 從 CDMO 的角度，使用 SU 層析柱相較於傳統不鏽鋼柱，在供應鏈和庫存管理方面有什麼挑戰？

4.14 Drug Product Final Filling 藥品最終充填

原文內容 Original Text

The impact of any failures in final fill operations is high, since further processing cannot reduce quality failures at this stage. Common strategies for reducing the risk of failure include performing filling operations in aseptic environments, use of physical barrier systems to separate operators from the process, and validating the sterility of process components (refer to Section 4.15, Isolators).

SU components are widely used in product final filling operations. For example, flexible plastic tubing is used between the filling pump head and needle. Another example is a peristaltic filling system, which employs disposable peristaltic tubing inside the pump head. Both types of tubing are normally replaced between batches.

4.14.1 Technological Examples

An SU dosing system comprises a product hold bag, supply tubing, and a filling needle. These components are assembled in a cleanroom and sterilized using gamma irradiation. The complete assembly is removed from its outer pouch and connected to the filling machine immediately before use. The filling machine may be as simple as a single pump or may contain multiple pumps to achieve commercial production speeds.

SU filling systems are controlled volumetrically or gravimetrically.

  • Volumetric dosing system — This type of system involves a volumetric system that consists of a peristaltic pump and specialized tubing, within which flows the product to be dispensed. Peristaltic pumps work by mechanically compressing the tubing within the pump head to push a specified volume of fluid through the dosing path. Accurate dosing depends on factors such as the mechanical life of the pump tubing. A positive displacement pump-based system relies on the use of either a rotary-piston or rolling-diaphragm pump design. In both cases, fluid is forced out of the pump by the action of the piston. Accurate dosing depends on the precision of the piston stroke length.
  • Gravimetric dosing system — A gravimetric system is normally used in low-throughput applications. It uses an optical sensor to dose a specified weight of the product based on the calculation of the interior volume of a given length of tubing or glass. The entire product path comprises SU components. The accuracy and precision of the system with aqueous solutions is comparable to that of other dosing systems. The dosing speed and accuracy are directly related to the fluid temperature and viscosity. Dosing time is the speed at which a liquid will flow through the tubing based on gravity; thus, viscosity influences dosing time. Relatively small temperature changes over the course of a filling event can affect product viscosity enough to have a significant effect on the dose volume.

Alternatively, determine that the filling system can be aseptically transferred into a presanitized isolation system.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Drug Product Final Filling（藥品最終充填）：這是整個生產流程中風險最高的環節之一。文件開宗明義指出：「任何最終充填操作的失敗影響都很大，因為後續處理無法減少此階段的品質缺陷。」換言之，充填後無法再「補救」。

Volumetric vs Gravimetric（容積式 vs 重量式）：

  • 容積式：蠕動泵或正位移泵（旋轉活塞/滾動膜片），通過控制泵管壓縮量來控制體積。速度較快但精度受管路老化影響。
  • 重量式：使用光學感測器和重力，根據管路內容積計算充填重量。精度高但產速較慢，適合低產量應用。

比喻說明 Analogy

容積式充填就像用量杯倒水 — 你依據容器的標線來量取固定體積，但量杯本身可能因使用而變形（類似泵管老化導致的系統性偏移）。重量式充填則像用電子秤精確稱量 — 不受容器形狀影響，但每次都要等秤穩定讀數，所以速度較慢。

重點提示 Key Notes

泵管壽命與劑量偏移：文件特別提到「polymeric parts (such as pump tubing) that can lead to a systematic drift in dosing volume」。這是 SUS 充填系統的一個關鍵挑戰 — 蠕動泵管在反覆壓縮後會逐漸變形（疲勞），導致每次壓縮推出的體積緩慢漂移。必須建立 in-line weight check 來即時監控。

VHP 相容性：如果 SUS 要放入使用 VPHP（vapor-phase hydrogen peroxide，氣相過氧化氫）消毒的隔離器或 RABS 中，必須確認 SUS 材料與 VPHP 的相容性，包括遮蔽效應（shadowing）和滲透性。

內毒素與微粒控制：充填系統必須符合 USP <788>（微粒）和 USP <85>（內毒素）等多國藥典要求。

實務應用 Practical Application

作為 CDMO，在評估 SU 充填系統時的決策矩陣：

  • 高產量產品（>100,000 瓶/批）：可能仍需傳統不鏽鋼系統 + SU 管路，蠕動泵多通道並聯提高產速
  • 低產量/高價值產品：全 SU 重量式系統可最大化精度、最小化交叉污染風險
  • Campaign 製造：需要確認 SU 部件的使用壽命是否能覆蓋整個 campaign 週期

  2. 蠕動泵管的「系統性劑量偏移」如何影響充填精度？你會建議什麼樣的監控策略？

4.15 Isolators 隔離器

原文內容 Original Text

If the canister of a traditional alpha-beta (A-B) port is replaced with an SU bag or connector, SU technology can be used for the aseptic transfer of liquids, powders, stoppers, plungers, tools, materials, and waste into and out of isolators or restricted-access barrier systems (RABS). To avoid compromising the sterility of the isolator, SU bags are designed to prevent reuse. Categories of an SU product format include:

  • Gamma-sterilizable bag with mating A-B connector — Manufacturers of stoppers use this as packaging for prewashed, siliconized, and sterile ready-to-use stoppers or plungers.
  • Gamma-sterilizable bag with mating A-B connector and tubing set — This bag design includes one or more tubing sets for the transfer of liquids into the isolator or RABS. These are normally available as stand-alone components or as part of a presterilized SUS.
  • Autoclavable bag with mating A-B connector — Manufacturers of stoppers use autoclavable bags as packaging for prewashed, siliconized, and ready-to-sterilize stoppers or plungers. In addition, autoclavable bags are used for in-house stopper processing, tool, and component transfer.
  • A-B connector — A connector is used for liquid transfer. Connectors can be used with either an SUS or an MUS because they can be sterilized by gamma irradiation or autoclaving.
Table 4.15-1 Specific Considerations for Isolators
FactorConsideration
Sterilization method The SU transfer bag must withstand the chosen method of sterilization (e.g., gamma irradiation or autoclaving).

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Alpha-Beta (A-B) Port（阿法-貝塔接口）：這是隔離器上的無菌傳遞接口。「Alpha」端裝在隔離器壁上，「Beta」端裝在轉運容器（傳統上是不鏽鋼罐）上。兩個端口對接後打開，讓物料在不破壞隔離器無菌環境的情況下進出。SUS 的創新是用一次性袋體取代傳統的不鏽鋼 Beta 罐，大幅簡化了清洗和滅菌流程。

Aseptic Transfer（無菌傳遞）：在隔離器或 RABS 環境中，任何物料的進出都是無菌屏障的潛在破口。SU 袋體設計為不可重複使用，避免了重複使用可能帶來的交叉污染風險。

比喻說明 Analogy

A-B 接口就像太空站的對接口 — 太空船（Beta 端/SU 袋）必須與太空站（Alpha 端/隔離器）精確對接，才能在不讓外太空（非無菌環境）侵入的情況下傳遞物資。SU 袋取代不鏽鋼罐，就像從可重複使用的太空貨運艙改為一次性太空包裹 — 更簡便，但必須確保每個包裹與對接口完全相容。

重點提示 Key Notes

供應商鎖定風險：文件明確指出「commercially available aseptic transfer ports have complementary bags that must be obtained from the same suppliers」。這意味著選擇了某一品牌的 A-B 接口後，就必須使用該品牌的配套袋體，形成供應商鎖定（vendor lock-in）。這對 CDMO 的供應鏈多元化策略是一個挑戰。

抗靜電膜：傳遞粉末或小零件時可能需要使用抗靜電膜，以防止靜電吸附造成的損失或污染。

  2. Gamma 滅菌和高壓蒸汽滅菌各適用於哪些類型的 SU 傳遞袋？各有什麼優缺點？
  3. 在選擇 A-B 接口系統時，如何平衡「供應商鎖定」風險與「系統相容性」需求？

4.16 Sampling and Laboratory Analysis 取樣與實驗室分析

原文內容 Original Text

Sampling is the process of removing a volume of process fluid from an SU assembly. This sample fluid is then usually submitted for laboratory analysis.

4.16.1 Technological Examples

Techniques for obtaining a sample fluid include:

  • Use of a sampling port with or without the aid of a needle to collect the sample
  • Use of a Luer-containing sampling bag or via the connection of a Luer-style syringe to a Luer adapter on the bag
  • Use of a preassembled manifold of sampling bags that may be filled and detached from the assembly one at a time using crimping tools or a tube sealer
  • Use of an SU sample valve integrated with one or more sample bags
Table 4.16.1-1 Specific Considerations for Sampling and Laboratory Analysis
FactorConsideration
Sterility Determine the degree to which maintenance of the sterility of the contents of the bioprocess bag or that of the sample is required on a case-by-case basis. Although using alcohol to sanitize a port before entry does not ensure sterility, it tends to reduce bioburden load. One should be cautious of the possibility of contaminating the sampled fluid with alcohol.

The competence of the laboratory staff at sample handling is critical to the success of the process.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Sampling in SUS（SUS 取樣）：取樣是品質控制的基礎操作，但在 SUS 環境中面臨獨特的挑戰。每次取樣都是對無菌屏障的一次「侵入」，因此取樣設計必須在分析需求和系統完整性之間取得平衡。

Representative Sample（代表性樣品）：文件特別強調要注意管路中的「滯留區」（stagnant parts），在取樣前必須先沖洗排出滯留液體，以確保取得的樣品能代表實際製程流體的狀態。

比喻說明 Analogy

SUS 取樣就像從密封的無塵室中取出物品 — 每開一次門就增加一次污染風險。預組裝的取樣歧管（manifold）設計就像設置了一系列「安全抽屜」：每次只拉出一個抽屜取樣，用完後密封切斷，不影響其他抽屜和主系統的無菌狀態。

重點提示 Key Notes

酒精消毒的局限性：文件明確指出「using alcohol to sanitize a port before entry does not ensure sterility」— 酒精擦拭只能降低生物負載，不能保證無菌。同時還要注意酒精可能污染取樣液體，影響分析結果。

樣品品質驗證：SU 取樣容器（袋、管路）本身可能對分析方法產生正向或負向干擾（interference），或導致樣品降解。在建立取樣方法前，必須評估這些因素。

4.17 Transportation 運輸

原文內容 Original Text

Transportation is the movement of process solutions such as buffers, culture media, WFI, and process intermediates either within a facility or between facilities.

4.17.1 Technological Examples

The supply of process solutions in large-volumes bags (≤1000 L) from the point of manufacture to the point of use is a well-established practice involving the following components:

  • A bag designed to fit a rigid-wall outer container
  • A rigid-wall outer container such as a plastic drum or tote, or a stainless steel bin
  • Secondary packaging materials (e.g., dunnage) and lids or other mechanical devices to suppress the fluid wave action in the bioprocess bag

Transportation of process solutions in bags in small volumes (nominally ≤20 L) is an equally well-established process that requires less complex packaging solutions. The exception is the transportation of frozen material, which requires special temperature-resistant materials and processes (e.g., bags, shells and secondary packaging materials, operating procedures, and cold chain logistics; refer to Section 4.11, Freezing).

Shipping systems must be qualified for their intended use through proper design and testing in consultation with a packaging engineer. The ISTA and ASTM D4169 are good references for testing standards.

Table 4.17.1-1 Specific Considerations for Transportation
FactorConsideration
Mode of transport Compare the use of ground and air shipping over long distances versus wheeled dollies that are pushed from suite to suite.
Environmental controls This involves temperature and relative humidity regulation and documentation. Check for material compatibility and protection from excessive cold or heat.
Functionality Ensure forklift access plus the ability to stack outer containers. Make sure that process fluids can be transferred in and out of the bioprocess bag.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Transportation in SUS（SUS 運輸）：運輸不僅指廠際之間的長距離運輸，也包括廠區內部不同房間或潔淨區之間的物料移動。SUS 的運輸需要考慮袋體保護、流體波動抑制、環境控制和潔淨度等級維護等多重因素。

Progressive Removal of Packaging（逐層拆除包裝）：將 SUS 袋體從非潔淨區移入潔淨區時，需要逐層移除外包裝，並在每個等級轉換點進行擦拭/消毒，以確保進入更高潔淨等級的物品不會帶入污染物。

比喻說明 Analogy

這就像醫院的「層層消毒」概念 — 手術器械從外部運入手術室時，要經過層層拆包和消毒：最外層包裝在走廊拆除，次外層在緩衝區拆除並擦拭，最後在手術室內才打開無菌包裝。SUS 的運輸與跨潔淨等級轉移遵循同樣的邏輯。

重點提示 Key Notes

Wave Action（液體波動）：大容量袋（可達 1000 L）在運輸過程中，液體的劇烈晃動可能對袋體接縫和管路連接處造成應力，增加洩漏風險。因此需要使用蓋子或機械裝置來抑制波動。

舊設施 vs 新設施：文件特別指出「material management capability may vary significantly between an existing facility and a new facility」。舊設施可能缺乏足夠的暫存區、走廊空間和廢料處理能力來支持 SUS 的大量包裝和廢料流。這是許多工廠在導入 SUS 時容易低估的問題。

實務應用 Practical Application

作為 CDMO 設施規劃者，導入 SUS 前你需要評估：

  • 暫存空間：SUS 元件體積大但輕，需要比傳統不鏽鋼系統更多的倉儲面積
  • 廢料處理：使用後的大量塑膠廢料需要專門的處理和移除通道
  • 人流物流動線：大型外容器（tote、drum）的搬運路線是否與現有走廊和門寬相容
  • 堆高機通道：確保堆高機可以到達所需區域，且外容器可堆疊存放

4.18 Sensors 感測器

原文內容 Original Text

An SU sensor enhances the functionality of an SUS by providing parametric data that allows for manual or automated process control or monitoring. Modern sensors can be directly integrated into SU systems, and traditional sensors equipped with a fluid-path barrier can be used to measure factors such as temperature and pressure. A hybrid sensor uses a combination of a sensor and a transducer. Although sensor and transducer technologies have been available in many industries for a considerable time, the application of SU sensor technology in bioprocessing is relatively recent.

4.18.1 Technological Examples

SU sensor technology is routinely used to measure factors such as temperature, pressure, and conductivity. Recently, advanced sensors for measuring pH, dissolved oxygen, and CO2, as well as other sensors that employ chemical and/or optical technologies, have become available in an SU format. As the use of SU sensors becomes more widespread, challenges such as their compatibility with sterilization methods and the physical design of the SUS are beginning to emerge.

The dynamic range, accuracy, and robustness of a particular sensor must be taken into account during sensor evaluation for a particular SUS application. Other factors to consider are the need for a common sensing platform across several unit operations (i.e., from small- to large-scale operations); the impact of employing multiple sensing technologies, each with different quantification attributes (such as range, accuracy, and precision); and assessment of sensor calibration requirements and the ease of performing a post-use verification.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

SU Sensor（一次性感測器）：感測器是 SUS 的「眼睛和耳朵」，提供製程參數數據以實現手動或自動化的製程控制與監控。SU 感測器的三種形式：

  • 直接整合式：感測器直接嵌入 SU 袋體或管路中，隨系統一起丟棄
  • 流路屏障式：傳統感測器搭配流路屏障（如膜片），探頭不直接接觸產品
  • 混合式（Hybrid）：結合感測器和轉換器，部分一次性、部分重複使用

常規測量參數：溫度、壓力、電導度已成熟；pH、溶氧（DO）和 CO2 的 SU 感測器則較新，採用化學或光學技術。

比喻說明 Analogy

傳統不鏽鋼系統中的感測器就像家裡的固定式溫度計和濕度計 — 安裝好後反覆使用，定期校準。SU 感測器則像即棄式醫療體溫計 — 用完就丟，免去了交叉污染的風險和重新校準的麻煩，但成本較高且需要確保每支都準確。

重點提示 Key Notes

校準挑戰：傳統感測器可以在使用前進行完整的多點校準。SU 感測器由於是一次性的，校準策略不同 — 有些依賴出廠預校準，有些支持簡化的使用前校準。使用後驗證（post-use verification）的便利性也是評估的重要因素。

跨操作平台的一致性：文件強調需要考慮是否能在多個單元操作中使用相同的感測平台。如果不同步驟使用不同的感測技術（每種都有不同的量測範圍、精度和準確度），數據的可比性和製程控制策略的一致性都會受到影響。

滅菌相容性：SU 感測器必須能承受所選的滅菌方法（通常是 gamma 射線），同時在滅菌後仍保持測量精度，這是技術上的一大挑戰。

  • 儲存 (4.9)：PE 接液層是最常見的選擇；對於氧敏性或光敏性中間體需要特殊保護膜（如 EVOH 氣體阻隔層）。Leachables 評估在長期儲存中至關重要。

  • 過濾 (4.11)：NFF 和中空纖維 CFF 最適合 SU 格式。Gamma 射線滅菌的濾器在技術上對 PUPSIT 的需求較低（不同於蒸汽滅菌），但法規可能仍有要求。
  • 離心 (4.12)：SU 離心為新興技術，使用 gamma 預滅菌的一次性模組/內襯，消除了 CIP/SIP 需求，適用於低剪切力的收穫和澄清操作。
  • 層析 (4.13)：分為樹脂基（預充填管柱，可 campaign 使用，需化學消毒）和膜基（膜吸附器，高流速低容量，用於精製步驟）。放大性和緩衝液需求是關鍵考量。
  • 最終充填 (4.14)：風險最高的環節 — 充填後無法補救品質缺陷。容積式和重量式兩種劑量控制方式各有優缺；蠕動泵管壽命導致的系統性劑量偏移是 SUS 特有的挑戰。
  • 隔離器 (4.15)：SU 袋體取代傳統不鏽鋼 Beta 罐用於 A-B 接口的無菌傳遞。供應商鎖定（vendor lock-in）是重要的供應鏈風險。
  • 取樣 (4.16)：每次取樣都是對無菌屏障的侵入。預組裝取樣歧管可降低風險；需注意管路滯留區對樣品代表性的影響，以及 SU 容器對分析方法的潛在干擾。
  • 運輸 (4.17)：涵蓋廠內和廠際運輸；大容量袋的液體波動抑制、跨潔淨等級的逐層拆包、以及舊設施的物料管理能力（暫存空間、廢料處理）是常被低估的挑戰。
  • 感測器 (4.18)：SU 感測器提供製程控制所需的參數數據；溫度/壓力/電導度已成熟，pH/DO/CO2 為較新技術。校準策略、滅菌相容性、以及跨操作平台的一致性是評估的關鍵要素。
