8.0 Appendix I: Overall User Requirement Specification Example

8.1 Description of Document

This appendix contains an example of user requirements (UR) written for an SUS facility. This material is referenced in Section 7.5.1, User Requirements, and should be used as an example for how this type of document is structured. The complexity and size of the document will change according to the type of SUS and complexity of the project. These types of documents should be considered as living (i.e., they can be updated as appropriate should the project or UR change). The document can be prepared by the end user or a sponsor of the end user such as an engineering company or supplier.

This document describes the UR concerning the new production suite, GMP, SUS (shown in figure below). This is an overall UR specification and detailed UR will be written. The requirements stated in this document may not be qualified but will form the basis for design.

8.1.1 Abbreviations

Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

User Requirement Specification, URS (使用者需求規格書): URS 是製藥工程專案中最重要的基礎文件之一。它由使用者（製藥公司）撰寫，明確定義「我需要什麼」，而非「如何做到」。URS 是後續所有設計（概念設計、基本設計、細節設計）的起點，也是 Design Qualification (DQ) 的驗證依據。

Living Document (活文件): URS 不是一次定稿就永不更改的文件。隨著專案進展、法規變化或製程需求調整，URS 可以且應該被更新——但必須透過正式的變更管控 (Change Control) 程序。

比喻說明 Analogy

URS 就像蓋房子時的「需求清單」：你告訴建築師「我需要三間臥室、兩間浴室、一個開放式廚房」，但不會告訴他用什麼品牌的水泥或什麼尺寸的鋼筋。URS 描述的是「What」（需要什麼），而非「How」（如何實現）。建築師根據你的需求出設計圖，就像工程公司根據 URS 產出設計規格 (Design Specification)。

重點提示 Key Notes

本附錄提供的是一個整體性 (Overall) 的 URS 範例，實際專案中還需要針對個別系統（如 HVAC 系統、WFI 系統、自動化系統等）撰寫更詳細的 URS。整體 URS 定義大框架，細節 URS 則深入每個子系統。

注意：本 URS 範例中的需求「may not be qualified」——意思是這些需求不一定全部需要正式確認 (Qualification)，但它們構成設計的基礎。哪些需求需要確認，會在後續風險評估中決定。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 為什麼 URS 被稱為「活文件」？在什麼情況下需要更新 URS？
  2. URS 可以由「end user 的 sponsor（如工程公司或供應商）」撰寫，這在 CDMO 情境中意味著什麼？
  3. 「Overall URS」與「Detailed URS」之間有何差異？請舉例說明。

8.2 Introduction 8.2 導論

8.2.1 Description of Facility 8.2.1 廠房說明

Original Text

Company X needs to equip a new facility GMP, SUS for the manufacture of injectable products for viral particle gene therapy. This document outlines the user requirements of the facility. In this project and UR document, abbreviation T4 stands for the main building site and GMP, SUS for the cleanroom area. The new production suite should be designed for biosafety level 2 (BSL2) and comply with EU GMP and FDA cGMP.

The products of this facility shall be based on modified live viral particle technologies (gene therapy). The manufacturing process shall cover production of biological bulk and final filled products for sterile, as parenteral applications. The initial adenovirus production portfolio covers in the first instance two bulk and two final products, which are known as Product 1 and Product 2.

Product 1 is a novel gene-based product that is given as a suicide therapy for tumors. Product 2 is a novel product being developed to deliver a growth factor for use in surgery.

Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

BSL2 (生物安全等級 2): BSL2 適用於處理對人員有中等潛在危害的微生物。此案例使用活病毒載體（腺病毒），因此需要 BSL2 等級的生物安全防護。這直接影響設施的 HVAC 設計（需要負壓或定向氣流）、廢棄物處理（需要滅活）、及人員防護。

Gene Therapy (基因治療): 利用病毒載體將治療性基因送入患者細胞。此處使用腺病毒 (Adenovirus) 作為載體，是一種常見的基因治療技術平台。

雙重法規合規: 設施需同時符合 EU GMP 和 FDA cGMP，這表明產品將在歐盟和美國市場上市。雙重合規會增加設計複雜度，因為兩套法規在潔淨室分級、環境監控等方面有些許差異。

比喻說明 Analogy

BSL2 設施就像一個「有門禁的實驗室」——與一般辦公室相比，它有嚴格的出入管控、特殊的通風系統（確保空氣只往內流，不往外洩）、以及專用的廢棄物處理通道。想像一個處理感染性物質的醫院病房：空氣是負壓控制的，廢棄物要先滅菌才能丟棄，人員進出要換裝。SUS 設施在此基礎上，再加上 GMP 的潔淨度要求。

重點提示 Key Notes

這個 URS 範例特別有價值，因為它展示了 SUS 技術在基因治療這類先進治療藥品 (ATMP) 生產中的應用。由於基因治療產品批量通常較小、產品種類變化快，SUS 的靈活性和免清洗特性特別適合此類應用。

注意 URS 中使用的語言：「shall」表示強制性需求；「should」表示建議性需求。在 URS 撰寫中，用詞的選擇直接影響後續驗證的範圍。

8.2.2 Environment / 8.2.3 Capacity 8.2.2 環境 / 8.2.3 產能

Original Text

8.2.2 Environment

As a design basis for the HVAC in Company X, an upper limit of +30°C at 50% RH (63 kJ/kg) in summertime and lower limit of −37°C in wintertime for the outside climate will be used.

8.2.3 Capacity

The target capacities to fulfill the expected need of the market for the facility are summarized in the following list. Total number of vials will be about 130,000.

The process capabilities are limited by the drug substance manufacture. The drug substance manufacture will need 24-hour, 7-day-week control. However, the filling operation will be run on an 8-hour, 5-day-week basis.

Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

HVAC 設計基準 (Design Basis): HVAC 系統的設計需考慮當地最極端的氣候條件。此案例設定夏季最高 +30°C/50% RH、冬季最低 −37°C，暗示設施可能位於北歐（如芬蘭）。這些數據決定了 HVAC 系統的冷卻和加熱能力設計。

產能規劃: 年產約 130,000 瓶——在基因治療領域屬於中等規模。藥物原料 (Drug Substance) 製造需要 24/7 監控（因為細胞培養不能中斷），但充填 (Filling) 只需 8 小時/天、5 天/週，反映了兩個工段不同的運營節奏。

重點提示 Key Notes

在 URS 中明確定義環境設計基準至關重要——如果 HVAC 設計基準設定錯誤（例如低估了夏季最高溫度），可能導致潔淨室在極端天氣下無法維持所需的溫濕度條件，進而影響產品品質和 GMP 合規性。

8.2.4 Process 8.2.4 製程

Original Text

8.2.4.1 Production Process

The planned process for large-scale manufacturing of Product 1 and Product 2 vectors for the commercial scale process is shown below.

Thawing and expansion of master cell banks (API clean process)

  • T-flask, roller bottles, and rocker bags

Infection of expanded cells with working virus seed stock (WVSS) (API clean process)

  • Infection of the cell suspension with WVSS
  • Full CPE monitored with analytical and process tools

Cell lysis and clarification of crude viral lysate (cCVL) (API clean process)

  • Benzonase® and surfactant treatments to lyse the cells
  • Depth filtration to remove cell debris

Buffer conditioning by crossflow ultrafiltration (API clean process)

  • Diafiltration to reduce the amount of host cell– and growth medium–originated impurities
  • Buffer conditioning suitable for chromatography

Chromatography (API clean process)

  • Capture and polishing of recombinant adenoviruses

Crossflow ultrafiltration (API clean process)

  • Concentration
  • Diafiltration to remove the chromatography elution components (salts)
  • Change to final formulation buffer standardization of concentration
  • Freezing and holding in −70°C

Fill and finish (aseptic process)

  • Thawing final sterilization filtration prior to filling
  • Washing and sterilization of vials
  • Filling, stoppering, and crimping

Inspection, packaging, and storage

  • Checking of container seals and visual inspection
  • Labeling of primary containers and packaging
  • Freezing and storage of the drug product

8.2.4.2 Assumptions for planning

  • Disposable equipment is used during processing of the product.
  • The production suite should be equipped with its own WFI facility and solution manufacture.
  • Glass vials for sterile filling are brought into the facility ready to be washed and depyrogenated. Stoppers and aluminum crimps for filling are bought from suppliers presterilized.

8.2.4.3 Storage in GMP, SUS

There shall be storage areas for incoming raw materials and packaging material within T4. The GMP, SUS area shall provide storage of a batch material between process steps in the production.

8.2.4.4 Inactivation

All floor and process drains, such as product-containing waste waters, elution buffers, and so on, from the manufacturing areas shall be led to the collection tanks in the inactivation system. The connection to the collection tank shall be a closed system. Inactivation shall be carried out chemically or thermally in the balance tanks. After inactivation and neutralization, the drain liquid is transferred to the common drainage for transportation to the municipal wastewater treatment plant.

Floor and process drains from other areas shall be led directly to the common drainage or, when appropriate, to existing T4 balancing tank.

8.2.4.5 Waste Handling

Used equipment from the aseptic area and reusable equipment from the API areas are placed and sealed into plastic bags and decontaminated in a decontamination area (BSL2). The risk assessment will show the need for decontamination in the autoclave.

8.2.4.6 CIP

No CIP station is necessary due to the use of disposables. Elution solutions are used in order to clean columns and filter systems. A washing machine is used for washing filling-machine parts and filter holders.

8.2.4.7 Washing and Sterilization

Sterilization of reusable equipment shall be performed in an autoclave. Stoppers and aluminum crimps are bought from suppliers presterilized. Vials are to be washed and depyrogenized locally on entering the filling area.

8.2.4.8 SIP

No sterilization-in-place (SIP) is necessary due to the use of disposables and the supply of media, buffer, and elution solutions.

Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

製程流程概觀: 這是一個典型的腺病毒載體生產製程，包含以下關鍵階段：

  • 上游 (Upstream): 細胞解凍 → 細胞擴增 → 病毒感染 → 細胞裂解
  • 下游 (Downstream): 深層過濾 → 超過濾/透析過濾 → 層析純化 → 濃縮/換液
  • 充填 (Fill & Finish): 無菌過濾 → 充填 → 加塞 → 壓蓋

注意所有上游和下游步驟都標註為「API clean process」（原料藥潔淨製程），而充填則標註為「aseptic process」（無菌製程），反映不同工段的無菌要求等級。

比喻說明 Analogy

SUS 在這個製程中的角色就像「免洗餐具」用於外燴活動——每次使用全新的拋棄式設備（搖擺袋、管路、過濾器、層析管柱等），用完即丟，不需要清洗和滅菌。這不僅省去了 CIP/SIP 系統的建置成本，更重要的是消除了交叉污染的風險——就像用免洗餐具永遠不會有前一位客人的殘留。

重點提示 Key Notes

SUS 對設施設計的簡化效果:

  • 無需 CIP 站 — 大幅減少公用設施需求和設備佔地面積
  • 無需 SIP 系統 — 不需要潔淨蒸氣系統的複雜管線配置
  • 但仍需高壓滅菌釜 (Autoclave) — 用於可重複使用設備的滅菌和 BSL2 廢棄物的滅活

這是 SUS 的核心價值主張：簡化設施、加速建設、降低資本支出，但不是完全消除所有傳統設備。

生物安全滅活要求 Biosafety Inactivation

由於使用活病毒載體 (BSL2)，所有接觸過製程物料的廢水和排放物必須經過化學或熱滅活才能排入公共下水道。這是生物安全法規的硬性要求，在 URS 中必須明確定義滅活系統的規格。

8.2.5 Material Flows 8.2.5 物料流

Original Text

The risk of cross-contamination shall be minimized in the layout of the plant area and in the manufacturing areas. Special attention shall be put on straight flows so that possibility for mix-ups between products from different process phases or different products is minimized. Line clearance shall be performed between production runs of different products. The facility shall be designed for sequential batches of cells to be expanded in cell culture at the same time. Attention shall also be put on keeping the BSL2 requirements in the layout.

8.2.5.1 Traffic to and from T4

The incoming raw materials shall be transported to the goods-receiving area of T4. In the same way, the final products are removed for transportation from the same T4 building. Rejected material is transported to incineration.

The following shall be delivered to or brought out of T4:

Incoming:

  • Raw material for the process
  • Utensils, disposables, and components for production
  • Clothing for cleanrooms
  • Packaging materials
  • Material for cleaning of cleanrooms

Outgoing:

  • Final products in cardboard boxes
  • Clothing from cleanrooms
  • Production waste
  • Additional waste

Material flows for the production shall be separated from the other material flows.

8.2.5.2 Storage areas in GMP, SUS and T4

The main function of the storage areas in T4 is to receive and store the raw material needed in production and daily work and, when approved, to distribute it for use. Released final products shall be stored and dispatched to the customer in pallets or smaller packages. The storage areas also work as storage for hazardous and medical material and collection points for waste. The storage areas shall be designed for storage of at least two months' needs in production and consumption. The storage for finished products shall be dimensioned for six months' production.

There shall be storage areas for the following materials and products:

  • Incoming raw material, packaging material, and pallets (existing)
  • Raw material in cold storages (MCB in liquid nitrogen and MVSS in freezer)
  • Intermediates during process (cold storages)
  • Quarantined material and packaging material (existing)
  • Approved bulk material and packaging material (existing)
  • Rejected raw material and packaging material (existing)
  • Finished products (freezers)
  • Quarantined finished products (existing freezers)
  • Approved finished products (existing freezers)
  • Rejected finished products
  • Reference samples (existing)
  • Spare parts (existing)
  • Samples for stability studies (existing)
  • Lining materials and used boxes (existing)

8.2.5.3 Material Airlocks

Transport of material or products between unclassified and classified rooms and rooms with different classifications shall be done through extracted airlocks or pass-throughs. The airlocks shall be of the one-way type because personnel and the material flow shall be unidirectional. The material or product shall be unpacked and sprayed with ethanol inside the airlock when transporting to a room with class C classification. Filling components passed into the class B area shall be washed and depyrogenated, or packages of presterilized components shall be subject to a vaporized hydrogen peroxide (VHP) cycle.

Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Unidirectional Flow (單向流動): 物料和人員只能沿一個方向移動，避免乾淨物料與廢棄物料交叉。這是 GMP 設施設計的基本原則之一，也是防止交叉污染 (Cross-Contamination) 的關鍵。

Line Clearance (生產線清場): 在不同產品批次之間進行的清潔和檢查程序，確保上一批次的物料不會殘留在生產線上。在 SUS 設施中，由於使用拋棄式設備，line clearance 的範圍和複雜度會大幅降低。

Material Airlock (物料氣鎖): 連接不同潔淨等級區域的過渡空間，用於物料傳遞。氣鎖內維持壓差，防止低潔淨區的空氣進入高潔淨區。

比喻說明 Analogy

物料流管理就像機場的行李處理系統——出境行李和入境行李走不同的通道，不會交叉。「Airlock」就像機場的安檢區——所有進入管制區的物品必須經過檢查和處理（拆包、噴酒精消毒或 VHP 滅菌），才能進入更乾淨的區域。

重點提示 Key Notes

儲存需求設計: URS 要求原物料儲存量為至少兩個月的生產需求，成品儲存為六個月的產量。成品儲存需求較大，因為基因治療產品需要在 −70°C 冷凍儲存，且放行檢測需要較長時間。這些數字直接決定了冷凍櫃和液態氮儲存設施的規模。

SUS 對儲存的特殊影響: 使用拋棄式設備意味著需要大量儲存空間來存放一次性耗材（袋子、管路、過濾器等），這是傳統不鏽鋼設施不需要考慮的。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 為什麼 URS 要求物料氣鎖必須是「one-way type」？如果允許雙向通行會有什麼風險？

8.2.6 Personnel Flows / 8.2.7 Training 8.2.6 人員流動 / 8.2.7 教育訓練

Original Text

8.2.6 Personnel Flows

The risk of cross-contamination shall also be minimized in the personnel flows. The main office building area has existing entrance, lunchroom, offices, and other social rooms.

There shall be emergency exits from the T4 and GMP, SUS buildings. If possible, the use of emergency exits should not compromise contained operation.

Personnel lockers and changing rooms for factory suit shall also be situated in T4. There shall be authorization control at the entrance to the manufacturing area, allowing only authorized personnel to enter. The working areas should be visible by inspectors and QA personnel for observation of the activities.

8.2.6.1 Gowning

Personnel working in the production areas and in laboratories shall wear suitable protective clothes for classified rooms and working in a BSL2 containment area. Personnel working in office areas, such as the central facility control room, may wear common clothes.

8.2.6.2 Personnel Airlocks

The first main objective with airlocks is to maintain a positive pressure in the classified area and, by that, ensure a proper separation of the individual rooms or areas when only cleanliness grades are to be considered. The second main objective with airlocks is to prevent contaminants from BSL2 active rooms from spreading to nonactive cleaner areas. The personnel entering or leaving the cleanrooms shall go through airlocks where gowning is performed to suit the classified area gowns. The airlocks can be of the one-way type. There should be unidirectional personnel flow in the active areas.

8.2.6.3 Estimated Number of Personnel

About 8–11: cell culture rooms, 2–3 persons; downstream, 3–4 persons; filling, 3–4.

8.2.7 Training

In order to assure that the personnel have updated information of their working area, all personnel shall be trained in GMP procedures and in using SOPs and instructions concerning them.

Sufficient training for the personnel shall be provided by the supplier of every process equipment and utility and automation system. The education level shall be maintained with additional training during the whole lifecycle of the plant.

Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Personnel Airlock 的雙重目的:

  • GMP 目的: 維持潔淨區的正壓差，防止外界空氣中的微粒和微生物進入
  • BSL2 目的: 防止活性區 (Active Areas，即處理活病毒的區域) 的生物性污染物擴散到非活性區

這兩個目的有時會產生衝突——GMP 要求潔淨區正壓（空氣從乾淨區往髒區流），而 BSL2 要求活性區負壓（空氣從外往內流，防止生物危害物外洩）。設施設計必須巧妙平衡這兩個需求。

比喻說明 Analogy

人員氣鎖就像「太空站的氣密艙」——太空人從外太空（非管控區）進入太空站（潔淨區）前，必須在氣密艙中換裝、加壓。同樣，操作人員從辦公區進入潔淨室前，必須在更衣區逐層穿上無塵衣。差別在於，這裡還要考慮 BSL2 的反向需求——有些區域的空氣流向要「向內」而非「向外」。

重點提示 Key Notes

緊急出口與生物安全的平衡: URS 中特別提到「emergency exits should not compromise contained operation」——這意味著即使在緊急疏散時，也要盡可能維持生物安全的圍阻。這在實務設計中是一個挑戰，需要特殊的氣鎖設計或連鎖控制系統。

人力規劃: 僅需 8-11 人即可運行整個設施，反映了 SUS 設施的人力效率優勢——不需要大量人力進行設備清洗、組裝和滅菌驗證等工作。

Original Text

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

多重法規合規框架:

  • ISO 14698: 潔淨室的生物污染控制標準

Original Text

8.4.3.1 Room Classification

The rooms are classified according to the needs of the process and BSL2 containment requirements. The cleanrooms are designed according to ISO 14644-1 but validated, operated, and monitored according to EU GMP and PIC/S. The protection classes of electrical equipment in the cleanrooms shall be IP54 and in other areas according to room activity.

Table 8.4.3.1-1🔍 Sample Room Classifications

ISO classes are, unless separately mentioned, defined as during operation.

Airlocks shall be clean-room classified according to cleaner classification when at rest.

8.4.3.2 Ventilation Equipment

The ventilation system is divided into separate systems, according to use (one for cleanrooms and one for technical basement). Space will be reserved for future expansion of ventilation systems, which will serve cleanrooms on the first floor.

The cleanroom ventilation systems consist of fresh-air-handling units to obtain the air changes specified for each room or classified area. The ventilation system shall be designed as a "full fresh air" system in order to ensure the BSL2 and prevent cross-contamination in the production suite. The cleanrooms and technical spaces with high heat generation shall be equipped with separate circulation air-handling units to maintain room temperature within acceptable limits. Fan filter units shall be used in nonactive (BSL2) cleanrooms. There shall be a redundancy in the HVAC for the primary fan motors at a minimum. Due to the fact that operators will be in the cleanroom only eight hours a day, there shall be a possibility to have a lower airflow at night.

8.4.3.3 Air Filtration

The air for basement ventilation is filtered in three steps: G4, F7, and F9 filter, according to EN 779, within the air-handling unit.

Cleanroom air is prefiltered in four steps: G4, F7, F9, and H12, according to EN 779 and EN 1822, within the air-handling units. The terminal filter is minimum H13.

Exhaust air from the area where live virus is processed is filtered in two steps: F7 and H14 filter. Exhaust air from the basement is F7 filtered.

Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

ISO vs EU GMP 潔淨室分級對照:

  • ISO 5 / Grade A: 無菌操作核心區（UDAF 層流罩下），微粒數最嚴格
  • ISO 7 / Grade B: 無菌操作背景環境
  • ISO 8 / Grade C: 無菌製造的一般操作區
  • ISO 8 at rest / Grade D: 較低風險操作區（靜態時達到 ISO 8）

Full Fresh Air System (全新風系統): 所有送入潔淨室的空氣都是經過過濾的新鮮空氣，不回收再利用。這是 BSL2 設施的要求——如果回收空氣，可能將活病毒從一個房間帶到另一個房間，造成交叉污染。

H14 排氣過濾: 處理活病毒區域的排氣必須經過 HEPA H14 過濾（效率 ≥ 99.995%），防止活病毒顆粒排入大氣。

比喻說明 Analogy

HVAC 的「全新風系統」就像醫院負壓隔離病房的通風——每一口送進去的空氣都是新鮮的，每一口排出來的空氣都經過 HEPA 過濾。不像一般辦公室的空調會回收室內空氣。這確保了「進去的空氣是乾淨的，出來的空氣也是安全的」。

重點提示 Key Notes

夜間降低風量: URS 允許在非操作時段降低送風量——這是一個重要的節能設計考量。全新風系統的能耗極高（因為所有空氣都需要加熱/冷卻和過濾），夜間降低風量可以顯著降低運營成本。但必須確保降低風量後仍能維持必要的壓差和潔淨度（at rest 狀態）。

風機冗餘 (Redundancy): 主風機必須有備用——如果潔淨室 HVAC 風機故障，將導致壓差喪失，可能危及產品品質和 BSL2 安全性。

  2. 「Full fresh air system」相比「回風系統」的主要缺點是什麼？在哪些情況下可以考慮部分回風？

8.4.4 Control and Automation System 8.4.4 管制與自動化系統

Original Text

8.4.4.1 Process Control System

The process equipment is of the stand-alone type, which means that every unit has its own control system. The system shall consist of a local PLC/PC and an HMI and it shall be possible to handle at least three user levels. Every user shall have an individualized user name and password to restrict the access and maintain the audit trail. 8.4.4.2 Facility Monitoring System

The cleanrooms shall have a facility monitoring system (FMS) consisting of a PLC and an HMI. The FMS shall monitor the critical parameters for room conditions [e.g. temperature, pressure, viable and nonviable particles, microbial presence, and moisture (some areas)] in every classified room. The critical refrigeration devices also have to be connected to the FMS. The whole GMP, SUS suite is controlled by the building monitoring system (BMS). Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Stand-alone Control (獨立控制): 每台設備有自己的 PLC/PC 和 HMI，而非由中央系統統一控制。這在 SUS 設施中很常見，因為拋棄式設備供應商通常提供整合好的設備控制系統。

三級使用者權限: 通常為 Operator（操作員）、Supervisor（主管）、Administrator（管理員），每級有不同的操作權限。這是 21 CFR Part 11 / EU GMP Annex 11 對電子記錄的基本要求。

Audit Trail (稽核追蹤): 系統必須記錄所有操作、修改、和數據存取的時間戳和操作者身份。這是數據完整性 (Data Integrity) 的核心要素。

FMS vs BMS: FMS 專注於 GMP 關鍵參數（潔淨室壓差、微粒、溫度等），BMS 負責整體建築的設施控制（照明、電力、一般 HVAC 等）。兩者通常是獨立系統，但可以交換數據。

重點提示 Key Notes

連續線上粒子監控: A 級和 B 級區域必須配備連續線上粒子監控——這是 EU GMP Annex 1 的要求。C 級區域使用多歧管系統 (Manifold System)，由一台粒子計數器輪流採樣多個採樣點，是一種成本效益較高的方案。

URS 中指出「electronic records but no electronic batch records」——表示雖然有電子數據記錄，但批次記錄仍以紙本為主。這在 2014 年是常見做法，但現今趨勢已朝向電子批次記錄 (EBR) 發展。

Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

HSE (健康、安全、環境) 風險分析: 這是設施設計中與 GMP 同等重要但常被忽視的面向。URS 要求進行系統性的 HSE 風險評估，涵蓋：

  • 健康 (Health): 人員暴露於生物危害物質的風險
  • 安全 (Safety): 液態氮窒息風險、高壓蒸氣燙傷風險等
  • 環境 (Environment): 排氣過濾、廢水滅活、廢棄物分類處理

廢棄物處理優先順序: 再利用 → 能源回收 → 最後才是掩埋。這反映了歐盟的永續發展法規要求，對 SUS 設施尤為重要，因為拋棄式設備會產生大量塑膠廢棄物。

重點提示 Key Notes

緊急系統的層次化設計:

  • UPS (不斷電系統): 提供短時間（通常 15-30 分鐘）的備用電力，讓關鍵設備（UDAF 罩、HVAC、監控系統）有序關機或切換到柴油發電機
  • 柴油發電機: 提供長時間備用電力，覆蓋範圍更廣（包括冷凍櫃和冷藏庫）
  • 兩者配合形成「無縫供電」：UPS 負責瞬間切換，柴油發電機負責長期供電

注意：不需要灑水系統 (Sprinkler = No)——這可能是因為潔淨室中灑水會造成嚴重的微粒和微生物污染，以及 BSL2 區域的水可能被生物污染。替代方案可能是惰性氣體滅火系統。

比喻說明 Analogy

緊急供電系統就像醫院的電力備援——手術室的電力不能中斷，即使外面停電了，UPS 立刻接手供電，同時柴油發電機在幾十秒內啟動提供長期電力。SUS 設施的 UDAF 罩和 HVAC 就像手術室的無影燈和層流系統——一旦斷電，無菌環境就會被破壞。

  • URS 的定位: User Requirement Specification 是 SUS 設施專案的基礎文件，定義「需要什麼」而非「如何做到」，是後續所有設計和驗證活動的起點
  • 範例場景: 本 URS 範例描述的是一個用於基因治療腺病毒載體生產的 BSL2 級 SUS 設施，需同時符合 EU GMP 和 FDA cGMP
  • SUS 對設施設計的影響: 免去 CIP/SIP 系統，但仍需高壓滅菌釜、WFI 系統、潔淨蒸氣等基礎公用設施；同時增加了拋棄式耗材的儲存需求
  • BSL2 的設計挑戰: 需要全新風 HVAC 系統、排氣 HEPA H14 過濾、廢水/廢棄物滅活系統、以及 GMP 正壓與 BSL2 負壓之間的壓差平衡
  • 潔淨室分級: 從 Grade A (ISO 5) 到 Unclassified，每個功能區域都需要明確定義其潔淨等級，且需同時參照 ISO 14644 和 EU GMP 標準
  • 控制與監控: 獨立設備控制 + SCADA 整合能力 + FMS/BMS 環境監控，構成完整的自動化架構
  • HSE 與緊急系統: UPS + 柴油發電機的分層供電保障、廢棄物分類與滅活處理、氧氣偵測器等安全措施是 BSL2 SUS 設施的必備要素
  • 活文件概念: URS 是隨專案演進持續更新的文件，初版可以有佔位符式的章節，後續逐步充實
