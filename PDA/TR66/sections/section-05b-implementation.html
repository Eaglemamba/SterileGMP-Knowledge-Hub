7.4 Implementation S4: Business Case 7.4 實施階段 S4：商業案例

Original Text

This stage of implementation concerns the different business and technical scenarios that can influence user requirements and the implementation process. Examples of key business drivers include:

  • Whether the project concerns drug substance or drug product
  • Whether the project concerns the manufacture of preclinical material, investigational medicinal products (IMPs), or licensed product
  • Whether the product is a biological or chemical entity
  • Containment requirements

The main documentary output from S4 is the project execution plan (PEP). Important parameters for preparing the PEP (such as facility and process integration, technological survey, supplier selection, and supply chain) are discussed in the paragraphs that follow. Further details on the evaluation of business drivers can be found in Section 5.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Implementation S4: Business Case（商業案例階段）— 在完成 S3 的範圍界定之後，S4 階段聚焦於將 SUS 策略轉化為具體的商業與技術方案。此階段的核心產出是 Project Execution Plan (PEP，專案執行計畫)，它是整個實施專案的主要路線圖文件。

Business Drivers（商業驅動因素）— 影響 SUS 實施方向的關鍵商業因素，包括產品類型（藥物活性成分 vs 藥品）、產品生命週期階段（臨床前 vs GMP 生產）、產品性質（生物製劑 vs 化學實體）以及圍阻需求。

比喻說明 Analogy

S4 階段就像建造新工廠之前的「商業企劃書」。想像你要開一家餐廳 — 在裝潢施工之前，你必須先決定：賣什麼菜（產品類型）、目標客群是誰（臨床階段 vs 商業生產）、是西餐還是中餐（生物製劑 vs 化學實體）、以及是否需要特殊通風設備（圍阻需求）。PEP 就是這份完整的開店企劃書。

重點提示 Key Notes

PEP 是 S4 的核心產出。它不僅定義預算、時程和品質目標，還整合了設施、製程和供應鏈等所有面向。一個結構不完整的 PEP 會直接導致後續實施階段的混亂與延誤。對 CDMO 而言，每個客戶專案都應有對應的 PEP 來確保 SUS 實施的系統性。

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

PEP (Project Execution Plan，專案執行計畫)— 這是 SUS 實施的「總綱文件」。它涵蓋 12 大主題，從專案簡介、範圍界定、合約文件、品質策略、風險管理，一路延伸到 HSE（健康安全環境）、採購安裝、教育訓練和移交支援。

重點提示 Key Notes

  • 第 8 章 Risk Management：不只是風險評估，還要有具體的緩解策略和計畫

PEP 的 12 個章節就像蓋房子時的「建築設計說明書」。第 1-4 章是「我們要蓋什麼」；第 5-6 章是「誰來蓋、怎麼蓋」；第 7-8 章是「品質和風險怎麼管」；第 9 章是「安全和環保」；第 10-11 章是「實際施工到驗收移交」；第 12 章是「附件和參考資料」。少了任何一個部分，房子都可能出問題。

7.4.2 Process and Facility Requirements: Basis of Approach 7.4.2 製程與廠房需求：方法基礎

Original Text

Process and facility requirements are key business drivers, and their consideration forms an important part of the PEP preparation. SUSs can be integrated and deployed within existing processes and manufacturing facilities. However, when a high level of SUS integration is necessary, new facilities may be required because of the radical structural differences between SU and MU systems. For PEP preparation, SUS integration must be considered from the point of view of the engineering service, suppliers, and the end user. The outputs are three relatively simple documents which outline the implementation strategy: facility-, implementation-, and operational-level plans.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

SUS 整合需要從三個不同的視角來規劃，產出三份文件：

  • Facility-Level Plan（設施層級計畫）— SUS 如何融入設施設計空間
  • Implementation-Level Plan（實施層級計畫）— 供應商和終端使用者在生產、行銷和供應 SUS 時的參數
  • Operational-Level Plan（營運層級計畫）— SUS 組裝、連接和部署的參數

重點提示 Key Notes

當 SUS 整合程度很高時，可能需要全新的設施設計。這是因為 SU 和 MU 系統在結構上有根本差異：SU 不需要 CIP/SIP 管路、不需要大量公用設施連接，但需要更多的倉儲空間、廢棄物處理區域和人員操作動線。這個決策點對 CDMO 的資本支出影響巨大。

This plan shows how the SUS fits into the facility design space together with other process equipment, and it summarizes different functional situations.

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

三個層級的整合計畫形成一個從宏觀到微觀的規劃架構：

重點提示 Key Notes

Implementation-Level Plan 中提到兩種 SUS 來源：off-the-shelf（現成品）和 self-made（自製品）。這個選擇對風險管理有重大影響 — 自製 SUS 雖然靈活度高，但需要自行承擔規格制定、批次紀錄、適格性確認和穩定性研究等所有品質責任。

7.4.6 Facility-Level Integration Plan 7.4.6 廠房層次整合計畫

Original Text

This is normally a concise document that describes the integration of the SUS into the facility design space (87). Robust process knowledge is a prerequisite for defining the various operational situations of the facility. It is critical to adopt a holistic approach because several factors (e.g., compliance, environment, operational requirements, and the compatibility of processes and equipment) may affect the successful operation of the SUS. These factors depend on the nature and properties of the product (e.g., biological or chemical entity) and the requirements for containment. Figure 7.4.6-1🔍 provides a conceptual representation of how the successive steps in facility-level integration may be achieved. Some of the most relevant parameters to consider include:

  • Product type
  • Sterile or nonsterile processing
  • Product volume
  • Lifecycle
  • Available surface footprint
  • Dependence on utilities
  • Flexibility of facility design
  • Avoiding cross-contamination
  • Level of segregation and dedication
  • Environment (e.g., room air grade, containment)
  • Personnel interactions
  • Process validation (may be necessary when the equipment used in scale-up changes in terms of volume, aspect ratio, or between MUS and SUS)

[Figure 7.4.6-1🔍: Process and Facility Considerations for SUS Implementation]

The chart on the left side of the figure depicts a holistic vision of a facility from the top-level processes that created it (outside rings) to the process design space (inside ring). The key points to address during implementation are illustrated as a stakeholder pathway. The triangle and the down-facing arrow on the right show how the risk from biological or chemical potency may affect the amount of dedication/segregation and containment necessary at all levels within the manufacturing site.

A structured risk-based approach should be used to prepare the facility plan, starting with the manufacturing strategy prepared in S1 and S2, and the SUS implementation strategy prepared in S3.

Process mapping and regulatory assessment tables (RATs) are additional tools for defining facility requirements. Process mapping identifies operating scenarios and potential CQAs, while a RAT enables cross-referencing to key GMP factors and other regulatory requirements where it is appropriate for the user to document their policy regarding those regulations (i.e., how they intend to practically address those requirements) (87). The use of good engineering practice (GEP) also complements this strategy by providing a framework for the application of key concepts (e.g., project engineering, common practices, and operations and maintenance) (90). Examples include non-GxP compliance, design reviews, change management, equipment maintenance, and lifecycle management.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Facility Design Space（設施設計空間）— 指設施中可供 SUS 整合的物理和功能空間。這不僅是地板面積的問題，還包括潔淨度等級、公用設施配置、人員動線和物流動線的整體規劃。

RAT (Regulatory Assessment Table，法規評估表)— 一種實用工具，用於將法規要求與使用者的具體應對策略進行交叉對照。例如，針對 ICH Q7A 的某一條款，RAT 會記錄該條款的原文以及使用者計畫如何滿足該要求。

GEP (Good Engineering Practice，良好工程實務)— 為工程專案提供系統性框架的方法論，涵蓋專案工程、共通實務、營運和維護等面向。

重點提示 Key Notes

設施層級整合的 12 個參數清單非常重要，特別注意以下幾點：

  • Cross-contamination avoidance（避免交叉污染）— SUS 本身就是降低交叉污染風險的有效手段，但設施設計仍需考慮人員流動和物料流動的隔離
  • Process validation — 從 MUS 轉換到 SUS 時，即使製程相同，因設備型態改變（如體積比、攪拌方式）可能需要重新進行製程確效
  • Dependence on utilities — SUS 設施通常較不依賴 WFI、純蒸汽等公用設施，但對倉儲和廢棄物處理的需求增加

比喻說明 Analogy

設施層級整合就像在既有的社區中規劃一棟新建築。你不能只看建築本身的設計（SUS 設備），還必須考慮周圍的道路（物流動線）、供水電力（公用設施）、停車位（倉儲空間）、消防通道（安全規範）、鄰居的影響（交叉污染風險）以及市政法規（GMP 要求）。RAT 就是你用來逐條檢查建築法規是否符合的核對表。

ICH Q7A - Basic Requirements for Active Substances Used as Starting Materials: Specific Guidance for APIs Manufactured by Cell Culture/Fermentation

This table is an example based on Chapter 18 of ICH Q7A (EU-GMP Part II) (2). Other regulatory texts can also be exploited in this manner. 導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

RAT（法規評估表）的實際運作方式：

  • 左欄：列出法規原文的相關條款編號和內容
  • 右欄：記錄使用者（公司）針對每一條款的具體因應政策

這種「法規原文 vs 公司政策」的對照格式，讓查核員和內部團隊都能清楚看到每一項法規要求是如何被滿足的。

重點提示 Key Notes

注意 Table 7.4.6-1🔍 中幾個重要的政策聲明：

  • 18.30 對應政策：所有無菌添加都在封閉系統中進行 — 這正是 SUS 的核心優勢
  • 18.31 對應政策：開放式連接必須在 EU-GMP Grade A + 背景 Grade B 環境下進行 — 這是典型的 SUS-MUS 混合操作的風險控制
  • 18.34 對應政策：上游製程設備使用 SUS，只使用一次 — 直接消除清潔確效的需求
  • 18.38 對應政策：混合系統中的 MUS 部件（如鋼製取樣閥）需要經過確效的 CIP 程序

實務應用 Practical Application

建議 CDMO 為每個新的 SUS 實施專案建立自己的 RAT。除了 ICH Q7A 之外，也可以針對以下法規建立 RAT：

  • EU GMP Annex 1（無菌藥品製造）
  • FDA 21 CFR 211（cGMP）
  • PIC/S GMP Guidelines
  • ICH Q8-Q12（品質設計與生命週期管理）

RAT 可以作為法規查核的「預防性準備工具」，也是跨部門溝通法規要求的統一語言。

7.4.7 Equipment-Level Integration Plan 7.4.7 設備層次整合計畫

Original Text

This brief document describes factors influencing the supplier or end user during the construction, marketing, and supply of the SUS as an off-the-shelf, customized, or self-made system. Some of the parameters for verification are:

  • Position in the product lifecycle and making sure that it is compatible with future GMP and quality systems
  • Working volumes
  • Transport conditions
  • Handling or moving the system, and protecting it during this process
  • Physical support (e.g., whether the system can support itself or requires a supporting structure)
  • Physical parameters
  • Flow rates for gases and liquids
  • Temperature range (e.g., cold storage to process conditions)
  • Heating and cooling requirements
  • Agitation and mixing
  • Pressure
  • Sterilization or chemical sanitation cycles
  • Chemical compatibilities (e.g., organic solvents, gas permeability)
  • Direct-impact phenomena (e.g., cellular growth kinetics)
  • Biocontainment capabilities
  • Standardization
  • Control systems and their validation
  • Autonomy [i.e., does the system require an uninterrupted power supply (UPS) in case of power failure?]

At S4, it is generally assumed that 1) sufficient process knowledge exists to enable a coherent review of the design factors that must be built into the SU, and 2) this would be performed from a risk-based perspective. Some of the most relevant design factors are:

  • Construction materials
  • Physical properties
  • Microbial control methods
  • Size and volume
  • Physical parameters
  • Direct-impact phenomena (e.g., contact time, E&L, biocompatibility)

For many end users, establishing a close partnership with major SUS suppliers allows them to leverage their extensive experience in the process field. Adopting customized SUS or using inexperienced suppliers would increase risk, although this may be mitigated by on-site SUS testing. The onus is on the end user to ascertain the compatibility of process, equipment, and facility design space from a risk-based perspective (Sections 7.4.10 and 7.7).

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Equipment-Level Integration Plan（設備層級整合計畫）涵蓋 18 個驗證參數，這些參數可以分為幾大類：

  • 物理與機械：工作體積、壓力、溫度範圍、攪拌混合、物理支撐
  • 化學相容性：有機溶劑、氣體滲透性、化學消毒循環
  • 生物學影響：細胞生長動力學、生物圍阻能力
  • 物流與後勤：運輸條件、搬運保護
  • 系統整合：控制系統確效、不斷電系統 (UPS)、標準化

重點提示 Key Notes

S4 階段有兩個關鍵假設：(1) 已有足夠的製程知識，(2) 設計審查基於風險方法。這意味著如果製程知識不足就貿然進入 S4，可能導致設備規格不正確，後續的修改代價極高。

另一個重要觀點：與大型 SUS 供應商建立夥伴關係可以降低風險，因為他們有豐富的應用經驗。使用客製化 SUS 或經驗不足的供應商會增加風險，但可以透過現場測試來緩解。這對 CDMO 的供應商管理策略有直接影響。

比喻說明 Analogy

設備層級整合就像選購汽車引擎。你不能只看馬力（工作體積）這一個指標，還要考慮油耗（公用設施依賴）、排氣量（廢棄物產生）、散熱效率（加熱/冷卻需求）、耐用性（化學相容性）、安全氣囊（生物圍阻）、自動駕駛功能（控制系統）以及備用電池（UPS）。任何一個參數的疏忽都可能導致整台車無法正常運行。

7.4.8 Operational-Level Integration Plan 7.4.8 操作層次整合計畫

Original Text

This describes the parameters for SUS integration (i.e., assembly, connection, and deployment) in readiness for manufacturing. A risk-based approach should be used because of the structural and functional diversity of SUSs (91). Some of the important parameters to consider during deployment include:

  • Packaging and container integrity — It must be determined whether preuse leak testing of SUS is feasible. Manufacturing, shipping, handling, and operational procedures should be qualified to maintain SUS integrity. This can be achieved by implementing control strategies within the end user's organization and between supplier and end user.
  • SUS testing and release — Testing and release vary with use. The same off-the-shelf system may have different test and release criteria depending on process-, product-, regulatory-, and user-imposed requirements.
  • Reuse of components — In general, reuse of SUSs is not recommended. The policy for this should have been established in the SUS strategy (Section 7.2.1.7). However, there are circumstances in which the reuse of SUS may be acceptable (e.g., use of a container as a mixing vessel or reservoir for buffer concentrates in which there are no broken connections). A risk analysis should be performed to determine if the risks are acceptable.
  • Training — Appropriate personnel training is essential because the collapse of a bag may lead to stress cracks that could result in a loss of structural and functional integrity. Training topics might include the use of different sterile connection and disconnection devices or techniques for avoiding bag creases or holes during deployment.
  • Shelf life — A coherent expiration and shelf-life policy should be implemented, especially in situations in which the end user has a large number of different SUSs from multiple suppliers or local, self-made assemblies.
  • Quality assurance — Ensuring quality during deployment should be addressed at an early stage of implementation. Technical diligence may enhance this goal (Section 7.4.10 and 7.7.3).

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Operational-Level Integration（營運層級整合）是 SUS 從「靜態設備」變成「可用系統」的關鍵轉換點。六個重點參數涵蓋了 SUS 從到貨到上線使用的完整流程：

  • Preuse leak testing（使用前洩漏測試）— 確認 SUS 在運輸和儲存過程中未受損
  • Reuse policy（重複使用政策）— 原則上不建議重複使用，但在特定條件下（如未斷開連接的緩衝液容器）可接受
  • Technical diligence（技術盡職調查）— 對 SUS 產品、供應商和製程相容性的深入了解

重點提示 Key Notes

營運層級最容易被忽略但影響最大的是人員訓練。袋體的塌陷可能導致應力裂紋，進而破壞結構和功能完整性 — 這是一個非常實際的操作風險。培訓主題應包括：

  • 不同類型的無菌連接和斷開裝置的使用
  • 部署過程中避免袋體褶皺或孔洞的技巧
  • 正確的搬運姿勢和方法

對 CDMO 而言，操作人員的 SUS 專業訓練品質直接決定了產品品質和批次成功率。

比喻說明 Analogy

營運層級整合就像飛行員的起飛前檢查清單。不管飛機設計得多好（設備層級）、機場跑道多完美（設施層級），如果起飛前的儀器檢查（洩漏測試）、機組人員訓練（Training）、油料有效期（Shelf life）沒有到位，飛行安全就無法保障。每一項看似瑣碎的檢查項目，都可能是決定任務成敗的關鍵。

The aim of this document is to complement the information gathered as part of the manufacturing strategy (Sections 3.0, 7.0, and 7.1).   • In-house know-how
  • Industry networks and associations
  • Journals
  • Conferences
  • Industry guidelines

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Technological Survey（技術調查）是對市場上可用的 SUS 技術解決方案進行系統性調研。其目的是確保選擇最佳可用技術 (Best Available Technology, BAT)。

比喻說明 Analogy

技術調查就像買房前的市場調研。你會查看不同建商的口碑（Suppliers）、問住過的朋友的經驗（In-house know-how）、參加房展會（Conferences）、閱讀建築雜誌（Journals）、加入購屋社群（Industry networks）。SWOT 分析則是你在幾個候選物件之間做比較時的決策工具。

7.4.10 Supplier Selection and Supply-Chain Review 7.4.10 供應商選擇與供應鏈審查

Original Text

The aim of this review is to determine a policy for the identification and selection of suppliers in order to complete the PEP and project planning.

The selection of appropriate SUS suppliers can often be an arduous task that can be made less cumbersome with a risk-management approach. For a successful SUS implementation, it is essential to have a detailed and transparent relationship between suppliers, vendors, and the end user. This requires good communication and understanding of stakeholder management in order to garner the expectations of both the suppliers and the end users. Being able to share pertinent information with suppliers at the early stages of the project may speed up the design process. Note that in order to obtain full benefits from this approach, the exchange of uni- or bilateral confidential disclosure agreements (CDA) may be required. The implementation and management of an end user–supplier relationship via technical diligence is described in Section 7.7.3. Suppliers provide material data, advice on applying typical designs, and the following documents:

  • Process flow diagrams (PFDs)
  • Piping and Instrumentation Diagrams (P&IDs)
  • Component lists
  • Physical layout proposals
  • Operation manuals
  • Pricing
  • Qualification packages, including guidelines on E&L, for example

For both large companies and SMEs, it is critical to prevent the selection of equipment and suppliers from becoming an ad hoc process because that can lead to unnecessary and costly redundancies. In addition, working with too many suppliers may divert limited resources that could otherwise be gainfully employed in other parts of the SUS implementation process. Since the degree of business integration of a particular SUS supplier may depend on the size, location, and SUS assembly concerned, it is important to adopt a risk-mitigation approach to SUS supplier qualification and validation (Section 7.7.3).

Starting materials such as resins and films should be treated as critical raw materials as part of the verification process due to the complexity of SUS implementation (18). It is important to begin the supplier technical diligence (i.e., obtaining a thorough understanding of the SUS product, supplier, and compatibility of the SUS with the end user's process and facility) at this stage. These principles are based on established procedures for critical raw materials as recommended by the Association for Professionals in Infection Control and Epidemiology (APIC) due diligence process, ASTM E2500-07 vendor technical capability as part of the PVV process (Figure 7.2.7.1-1), and quality by design (QbD)-inspired raw materials qualification processes (18,92–94). Full details on how to implement the technical diligence process are described in Section 7.7.3. Note that this task may require the exchange of uni- or bilateral CDAs or nondisclosure agreements (NDA).

The quality of the entire supply chain should be assessed, qualified, and implemented via a structured risk-management process (72). All parties in the supply chain should make sure that their activities maintain business continuity and support the health of patients. SUS equipment that is purchased occasionally may be done on a contractual basis, but a quality and supply agreement must be used to buy routine SUS equipment (Sections 7.5.7 and 7.7.12).

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Technical Diligence（技術盡職調查）— 這是 TR66 中的核心概念之一。它不僅僅是供應商稽核，而是對 SUS 產品本身、供應商的技術能力以及 SUS 與使用者製程和設施的相容性進行全面深入的了解。基礎概念源自 APIC 盡職調查流程和 ASTM E2500-07。

CDA/NDA（保密協議）— 為了實現供應商與使用者之間的資訊透明交流，可能需要簽署單向或雙向保密協議。這在早期專案階段特別重要，可以加速設計流程。

Quality and Supply Agreement（品質與供應協議）— 用於常規 SUS 設備採購的正式合約，涵蓋品質標準、供應條款和變更通知等。臨時性採購可以用一般合約，但常規採購必須有品質供應協議。

重點提示 Key Notes

供應商選擇的兩個核心風險：

  • Ad hoc selection（隨意選擇）— 設備和供應商的選擇不能是臨時起意的過程。這會導致不必要的重複採購和成本浪費，不論公司規模大小都可能發生。
  • Too many suppliers（過多供應商）— 與太多供應商合作會分散有限的資源。供應商管理本身就需要投入大量時間和人力，過多的供應商會壓縮用於 SUS 實施其他環節的資源。

TR66 強調「起始物料（如樹脂和薄膜）應被視為關鍵原材料」— 這意味著它們需要與 API 起始物料同等級的管控和審查。

比喻說明 Analogy

供應商選擇與管理就像組建一支 F1 賽車團隊。你需要精心挑選引擎供應商（核心 SUS 供應商）、輪胎供應商（薄膜和配件供應商）和燃料供應商（樹脂等原材料）。與太多供應商合作就像賽車有太多不同品牌的零件 — 整合成本暴增、故障排除困難。而 Technical Diligence 就是你在簽約前對每個供應商進行的徹底試車和技術評估。CDA 則是賽車隊之間保護技術機密的協議。

Implementation S5: Development（開發階段）— 在 S4 的商業案例建立之後，S5 聚焦於將策略轉化為具體的使用者需求文件。此階段的關鍵輸出包括：使用者需求 (User Requirements)、佈局與設計、規格書 (Specifications) 和標準化政策。重要的是，完整的使用者需求必須在採購流程之前完成。

7.5.1 User Requirements for Implementation 7.5.1 實施之使用者需求

Original Text

User requirements should be prepared at the start of the development process. The principal user and stakeholders must be identified and their requirements obtained. The user requirements are based on product and process knowledge, critical quality attributes, and any relevant regulatory requirements. The principal user should also provide the justifications for the resources required to design, purchase, qualify, and implement any new process, system, or equipment. These justifications can be developed prior to selecting an SUS or supplier, or they may be developed for a particular system. Some or all of the requirements can be based on a QbD approach. In this case, the product and process-control strategy performed earlier in the QbD process will become a prerequisite for defining the SUS requirements.

User requirements and functional specifications may be recorded together in a single document for low-risk, small-scale SUS. However, multiple user requirements may be needed for complex systems or processes. In this case, it would be critical to avoid conflicts across multiple documents. Any necessary conflicts must be identified and documented. The user requirements are created by the end user or on the end user's behalf; however, some supplier documentation may be necessary to supplement this. Supplier documentation is acceptable if the supplier follows GEP (90), has an appropriate quality system, and is technically capable according to ASTM E2500-07 and PDA Technical Report No. 60: Process Validation — A Lifecycle Approach (8,18). In some cases, the supplier documentation could replace the company document. The user requirements are kept current throughout the system's lifecycle and should be reevaluated after any change in intended use, user needs, or altered materials specifications (e.g., the films used, functional specifications, regulatory assessments or supplier quality audits, technical diligence). An example of a user requirements document is included in Section 8, Appendix 1.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

User Requirements（使用者需求）— SUS 實施的基礎文件，基於三大支柱：

  • 產品與製程知識— 了解產品特性和製程參數
  • CQA（關鍵品質屬性）— 影響產品安全性和有效性的品質特性
  • 法規要求— 適用的 GMP 和法規規範

使用者需求可以採用 QbD（品質源於設計）方法，將產品和製程控制策略作為定義 SUS 需求的前提條件。

重點提示 Key Notes

使用者需求文件的管理有幾個重要原則：

  • 文件複雜度應匹配風險等級：低風險小規模 SUS 可以將需求和功能規格合併在一份文件中；複雜系統則需要多份文件，但必須避免文件之間的衝突
  • 供應商文件可以補充甚至替代公司文件：前提是供應商遵循 GEP、有適當的品質系統、並符合 ASTM E2500-07 的技術能力要求
  • 使用者需求是活文件：必須在系統生命週期中保持更新，任何用途變更、使用者需求變更或材料規格變更都需要重新評估

比喻說明 Analogy

使用者需求文件就像訂製西裝前的量身記錄。你必須先測量身材數據（產品和製程知識）、了解穿著場合（CQA）、確認禮儀規範（法規要求），然後才能讓裁縫（供應商）開始製作。如果你讓三個裁縫分別做上衣、褲子和背心，必須確保尺寸規格不會互相矛盾（避免多文件衝突）。而且，如果你增重或減重了（製程變更），量身數據就需要更新。

7.5.2 Preparing User Requirements 7.5.2 準備使用者需求

Original Text

Brainstorming sessions, workshops, interviews, assessments, and ad hoc discussions may be used to gather pertinent information about the needs of the end user. Other useful information may be obtained from suppliers, case histories, and workflow analyses. These requirements may not be fully defined initially but may be developed further during subsequent phases of the project. It is crucial to determine the level of interest of each stakeholder has in the project and ensure that stakeholders receive adequate and regular communications throughout the SUS process lifecycle. User requirements statements must be written to a level of specificity that:

  • Describes the SUS function
  • Allows end users to verify that the system meets their functional requirements
  • Addresses the compatibility of processes and materials (e.g., polymer films)

However, they must be general enough to avoid constraining the system design. If a supplier will provide specific functionality, then the user requirements must be specific enough to determine that functionality.

Any particular safety concerns and training needs resulting from the requirements should be noted for later implementation. Finally, make sure that a change-control process is in place for this process.

A functional risk-assessment approach may be used for further evaluation of critical requirements for the quality and function of the SUS. For instance, if an integrated SUS is composed of components from multiple sources, it may be necessary to identify the CQA of each component. An example would be a bioreactor system in which the hardware and SUS bags are sourced from different suppliers. Requirements that are not deemed to satisfy CQA may be further evaluated to determine whether they express a regulatory requirement or not. The project team may apply other criteria such as priority, clinical development stage, importance to the business, and other factors that may influence company decisions.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

使用者需求的撰寫是一門平衡的藝術，需要在兩個極端之間取得平衡：

  • 足夠具體：能描述 SUS 功能、可驗證是否滿足需求、涵蓋製程與材料相容性
  • 足夠概括：不會限制系統設計的彈性空間

Functional Risk Assessment（功能性風險評估）可用於進一步評估關鍵需求。例如，一個整合型 SUS 由多個來源的組件組成時（如生物反應器硬體和 SUS 袋來自不同供應商），需要識別每個組件的 CQA。

重點提示 Key Notes

需求收集的方法多元：腦力激盪、工作坊、訪談、評估和非正式討論。TR66 特別指出需求可能「最初並非完全定義」而在後續階段進一步開發 — 這反映了 SUS 實施的迭代本質。

同時注意兩個關鍵提醒：

  • 必須有變更管控流程來管理使用者需求的變更
  • 必須確保利害關係人在整個 SUS 生命週期中獲得充分且定期的溝通

比喻說明 Analogy

撰寫使用者需求就像訂披薩：你需要說「我要一個 12 吋的瑪格麗特披薩，不要洋蔥」（具體到可以驗證），但不需要說「麵團必須用 00 號義大利麵粉，水溫 38 度，發酵時間 24 小時」（太具體會限制廚師的專業發揮）。如果你對特定供應商有特殊的功能需求（例如無麩質），那就必須明確寫出來。

重點提示 Key Notes

Layout and Design 的「Typical Considerations」清單是 SUS 設計的實用檢查表。特別注意：

  • Ergonomics（人因工程）— SUS 操作通常比 MUS 需要更多的手動操作（如袋體安裝、管路連接），人因工程設計直接影響操作效率和錯誤率
  • Staging processes（分階段流程）— 從 Setting up 到 Dismantling 的七個步驟定義了 SUS 的完整使用週期
  • SU parts 中的 Sensors（感測器）— 一次性感測器（如 pH、DO 探頭）是 SUS 中技術挑戰較大的部分

比喻說明 Analogy

SUS 的佈局設計就像設計一個組合式行動廚房（用於大型戶外宴會）。Operations 是整個烹飪流程（從食材搬運到上菜撤盤）；Equipment 是可以反覆使用的設備（瓦斯爐、工作台、冰箱）；Materials 是每次使用後丟棄的物品（鋁箔紙、保鮮膜、烘焙紙）。S88 標準的方法論確保你不會只設計了廚房但忘了規劃從停車場到廚房的搬運動線。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 為什麼 TR66 建議使用 S88 批次自動化標準的框架來進行 SUS 設計？這個標準如何確保「設計完整性」？
  2. 在 SUS 的 Layout and Design 中，人因工程 (Ergonomics) 為什麼特別重要？請舉一個因為人因設計不佳導致操作失敗的潛在情境。
7.5.4 Specification 7.5.4 規格

Original Text

The design process produces a series of specifications for the proposed SUS. The user requirements cover both MU and SU parts and can be communicated through the following means:

  • Descriptive text
  • Calculation
  • Diagrams, drawings, and PFDs
  • Reference to existing equipment

Implementation of a periodic design review procedure is critical for success. The delivery and installation process must be examined carefully to ensure that the SUS will be delivered in a form that will integrate into the facility.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Specifications（規格書）是設計流程的具體產出，將使用者需求轉化為可測量、可驗證的技術要求。規格的表達方式包括：描述性文字、計算結果、圖表和流程圖、以及與現有設備的參照比較。

Periodic Design Review（定期設計審查）是確保設計持續符合需求的關鍵機制。特別要注意 SUS 的交付和安裝過程 — 必須確保 SUS 以能整合進設施的形式交付。

重點提示 Key Notes

規格書涵蓋 MU 和 SU 兩個部分 — 這很重要，因為 SUS 系統通常是混合系統（MU 硬體 + SU 組件），規格必須同時考慮兩者。定期設計審查的目的是在發現問題時能及早糾正，避免在製造完成後才發現設計不符合需求，導致昂貴的返工。

7.5.5 Standardization Policy 7.5.5 標準化政策

Original Text

Although the inherent flexibility at the core of SUS design is an advantage, it could degenerate into a disadvantage by introducing unwelcome variations in the supply chain if left unchecked. Therefore, introducing a standard design policy across the project may reduce the incidence of variations, especially as the scale of operations becomes more complex. Implement a standardization policy as part of the user requirements according to the following guidelines:

  • Working volumes may be chosen so that bag ranges cover a series of application ranges.
  • Connections and material on bags and tubing may be chosen so that several possibilities for connecting are maintained (e.g., providing weldable tubing and a typical connector together).
  • Setting up an SU toolbox consisting of Y pieces, sample manifolds, and lengths of tubing for special or infrequent applications will allow a more specific design element to be attached to a more standardized bag design.
  • An active standardization policy may have been defined as part of the SUS strategy (Section 7.2.1). If so, it may not be necessary to implement it at this stage.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Standardization Policy（標準化政策）是控制 SUS 設計彈性所帶來的供應鏈變異的關鍵策略。TR66 提出三種實用的標準化方法：

  • Bag ranges（袋體範圍系列化）— 選擇標準化的工作體積，讓一系列的袋體規格能涵蓋多種應用需求
  • Connection standardization（連接標準化）— 統一袋體和管路的連接方式，同時保留多種連接可能性
  • SU Toolbox（一次性工具箱）— 建立標準化的附件庫（Y 型接頭、取樣歧管、管路段），讓特殊需求可以透過附件附加到標準化袋體設計上

比喻說明 Analogy

標準化政策就像 LEGO 積木的設計理念。LEGO 的偉大之處在於用有限數量的標準化零件（標準的凸點間距和尺寸）來實現無限的組合可能。SU Toolbox 就是你的「特殊零件盒」— 當標準零件無法滿足需求時，你可以從中取出 Y 型接頭或取樣歧管來擴展功能。這比為每個應用訂製全新的設計要高效得多。

重點提示 Key Notes

SUS 的靈活性是雙面刃：優點是可以快速客製化，缺點是如果不加控制，每個操作者或每個專案都可能創建不同的設計，導致供應鏈變異激增、庫存管理困難、人員訓練成本上升。標準化政策的核心目標是「在保持必要彈性的同時，最大化可重複性和可預測性」。

  2. 「SU Toolbox」的概念如何在保持標準化的同時滿足特殊應用需求？
7.5.6 Extractables and Leachables 7.5.6 可萃取物與溶出物

Original Text

In some cases, an E&L database collection may have begun in S4 (Sections 5.6 and 7.4.11). If not, E&L data should be compiled now. Although there is no specific regulatory guidance document for E&L in SU systems, the USP, FDA, and EMA have extensive E&L guidance documents for SU technology (96–100). In addition, a number of cross-industry initiatives are in place to standardize the approach for SUS and achieve industry and regulatory consensus or provide a cross-industry database of current knowledge (100–102).

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

E&L 的法規環境在 2014 年（TR66 出版時）尚無 SUS 專屬的法規指引文件，但 USP、FDA 和 EMA 有針對一次性技術的廣泛 E&L 指引。業界也有多項跨產業標準化倡議，致力於：

  • 統一 SUS 的 E&L 評估方法
  • 達成產業和法規共識
  • 建立跨產業的知識資料庫

重點提示 Key Notes

如果 E&L 資料庫在 S4 尚未開始建立，S5 是最後的建立時機。E&L 數據的收集需要時間，且可能影響材料選擇和設計決策。越早開始收集，越能減少後期發現材料不適用而需要重新設計的風險。

7.5.7 Procurement 7.5.7 採購

Original Text

Although procurement procedures will differ from company to company, it is critical to make sure that the end user requirements and other relevant specifications are correctly used and that the SUS-based equipment adheres to those specifications. It is important to maintain a close partnership between suppliers and end users in order to facilitate the procurement process. Some of the key points to consider are as follows:

  • The main procurement process will be outlined and planned in the PEP.
  • Key stakeholders must be identified and their roles and responsibilities must be clearly defined. This is extremely important in cases where procurement precedes the implementation of a quality and supply agreement.
  • Contractual issues must be clearly defined. The end user's legal team should have reviewed and, if necessary, negotiated any modification(s) to the supplier's general terms and conditions of sale to ensure that the user's business, financial, and operational expectations are met (e.g., compliance with Incoterms 2011) (103).
  • Good project planning and management principles must be carefully followed so that the project remains on track throughout its life.
  • A change-notification process should be in place between the equipment supplier and the end user in order to monitor any design changes that take place at this point.
  • Appropriate verifications must be conducted and the acceptance criteria met on a timely basis in order to avoid unnecessary delays.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Procurement（採購）是將使用者需求和規格書轉化為實際訂單的關鍵步驟。TR66 列出六個關鍵考量：

  • PEP 中的採購規劃— 採購流程應在 PEP 中預先規劃
  • 利害關係人的角色與責任— 特別是在品質供應協議尚未建立時
  • 合約條款— 包括 Incoterms（國際貿易條件）的遵守
  • 專案管理— 確保專案時程不脫軌
  • Change-Notification Process（變更通知流程）— 監控供應商端的設計變更
  • 驗收標準的及時驗證— 避免不必要的延誤

重點提示 Key Notes

採購流程中最關鍵的風險控制點是變更通知流程。SUS 供應商可能在採購期間變更材料、設計或製程，如果沒有正式的變更通知機制，終端使用者可能在不知情的情況下收到與原始規格不同的產品。這對產品品質和法規合規性有重大影響。

另一個容易被忽視的重點：合約條款中的 Incoterms（國際貿易條件）定義了運輸過程中風險和費用的分擔方式。SUS 在運輸過程中特別脆弱（薄膜材料容易受損），選擇正確的 Incoterms 條款可以明確責任歸屬。

比喻說明 Analogy

SUS 採購就像訂製精密的醫療器材。你不能只簽一張訂單然後等貨到 — 你需要持續監控製造商是否按照你的規格生產（驗收驗證）、是否偷偷換了原材料（變更通知流程）、運輸過程中是否妥善保護（Incoterms）、以及如果有爭議該怎麼處理（合約條款）。

實務應用 Practical Application

CDMO 的採購部門應建立 SUS 專屬的採購 SOP，涵蓋：

  • 採購前的需求確認和規格審查流程
  • 與供應商的變更通知協議模板
  • 到貨驗收的標準檢查清單
  • 不符合規格時的處理程序（退貨、讓步接受等）
  • 緊急採購的授權和簡化流程
