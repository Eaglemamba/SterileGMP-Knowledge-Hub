中文解析：監測頻率設定原則

風險等級與頻率的對應邏輯

此表將風險評級直接轉換為可執行的監測頻率，是 EM 計畫的「頻率設定依據」。關鍵要點如下：

  • 低風險：每兩週或每週一次——適用於支援區域（走廊、更衣室）等汙染潛力低的環境
  • 中等風險：每週一次——定期提供足夠的趨勢數據，又不過度消耗資源
  • 高風險：每次執行關鍵操作時——確保每一次可能影響產品的活動都有監測記錄

歷史數據的調整彈性

TR13-2 明確允許依歷史數據調整頻率。這體現了「數據驅動」的 EM 哲學：若長期數據顯示某高風險區域持續達標，可藉由 Change Control 下調監測頻率；反之，若中等風險區出現趨勢惡化，應上調頻率。對於新建設施，原則是先採用較高頻率，累積數據後再行最佳化。

4.3.4 Conclusion

EM is an important element in bioburden control strategies for environments used for manufacturing processes that require bioburden control. Data derived from the environment and the process provide assurance that processes are being exposed to consistent and suitable environments commensurate with process requirements. Current guidance recommends using a risk-based approach to develop an EM program suitable to support manufacturing steps. The assessment of the critical factors identified in this technical report and presented methodology for executing a structured and robust risk assessment provide a scientifically sound approach to developing a risk-based EM program.

中文解析：風險評估設計框架總結

"a scientifically sound approach to developing a risk-based EM program"

這句話是 Section 4 整個框架的核心主張。TR13-2 強調，雖然法規對低生物負荷環境的 EM 並無強制性的採樣位置或頻率要求，但透過結構化的風險評估，仍可建立一套具科學依據的 EM 計畫，能通過 GMP 稽核的檢視。

EM 的驗證性角色

EM 在此框架中的角色是「污染控制措施的驗證工具」（verification tool for contamination controls），而非污染防治的第一道防線。真正的防線是 HVAC 設計、消毒程序和人員行為管控；EM 則提供定期的「狀態確認」，確保這些防線持續有效運作。

中文解析：實例一背景——哺乳動物細胞培養暖室

什麼是「暖室」(Warm Room)?

暖室（Warm Room）是維持高於室溫（通常 >25°C，此例為 36°C ± 1°C）的受控環境房間，專門用於哺乳動物細胞培養的擴增（scale-up）作業，如滾瓶（roller bottle）培養、T型培養瓶培養等。

為何溫度增加風險？微生物的增殖速率在 25°C 以上顯著加快，許多環境微生物（如葡萄球菌屬、芽孢桿菌屬）在接近人體體溫（37°C）時達到最快生長速率。因此，暖室本身的溫度條件就構成了額外的汙染風險因子。

跨部門風險評估團隊

TR13-2 明確列出參與者來自：製造（Manufacturing）、品保（QA）、驗證工程（Validation Engineering）、品管（QC），以及微生物學主題專家（Microbiology SME）。這體現了 ICH Q9 風險管理的跨職能（cross-functional）精神：風險評估的品質來自多元觀點的整合，而非單一部門的獨斷。

4.4.2 Discussion

A risk analysis team used a risk tool to evaluate contamination risks in all ISO-graded areas, choosing the EM sample site locations by the nature of operations to be carried out in the rooms. Sample site selection considered the various sanitized or sterilized surfaces, personnel, equipment, and material flow, as well as points of human activity that may represent potential risks to the state of control to the product and in the manufacturing area.

The risk analysis included the evaluation of each EM sample location for hazards. The hazards included the various physical conditions, operational activities, and EM-specific indicators that could contribute to or detect contamination. The identified hazards, factor descriptions, rationale, and weighting scores established for this analysis are provided in Table 4.4.2-1.

Each factor was assigned a weighting score of 0, 1, 3, or 5, based on its perceived contribution to contamination risk. Zero (0) equates to extremely low or no impact on contamination and 5 equates to the highest potential impact on contamination. The values were added to allow for a means to generate a risk priority.

中文解析：Table 4.4.2-1 評分矩陣詳解

評分制度：0 / 1 / 3 / 5 分

0 分 = 極低或無汙染影響
1 分 = 低風險
3 分 = 中等風險
5 分 = 最高潛在汙染影響

各因子分數相加 → 總危害分數（Hazard Score）

注意：此量表故意設計為非連續值（0、1、3、5），而非等距的 1-5。這樣的設計使高風險因子（5分）對總分的影響遠大於低風險因子（1分），自然地拉開高低風險場所之間的差距。

Group 1 vs Group 2 的意義

Group 1（製程步驟）評估的是「在這個空間執行什麼操作」。最終調配（Final Formulation）得最高分，因為產品在無保護狀態下暴露於環境中；走廊和前室得最低分，因為僅為支援功能。

Group 2（潛在汙染貢獻者）評估的是「這個空間的物理條件和系統設計」。開放系統最危險（5分），全封閉系統最安全（1分）；暖室溫度 >25°C 得 5分，因為微生物在此溫度下快速繁殖。

暖室在此矩陣中的得分特徵

Warm Room X（見 4.4.3）符合以下高分因子：

  • 溫度：>25°C → 5分
  • 製程步驟：上游操作（滾瓶培養）→ 3分
  • 人員活動：多項操作共存 → 5分
  • 製程持續時間：多天 → 5分

溫度因子在低生物負荷環境評估中往往是被忽略的項目，TR13-2 將其明確列為評分因子，提醒評估者不應只關注「開放/封閉系統」而忽略環境條件。

The total hazard score for each of the EM sample locations was ranked and then filtered by the severity score for the room (Table 4.4.2-2). Severity is the measure of the possible consequences of a hazard potentially resulting in product contamination. Low severity was assigned if product contamination was unlikely as the product was never present in the room. Medium severity was assigned if the product was present but was never exposed to the room environment. High severity was assigned if the product was present and could be exposed to the environment.

Hazard Probability Severity: Low (1) Severity: Medium (2) Severity: High (3)
High Risk Class 2 Risk Class 1 Risk Class 1
Medium Risk Class 3 Risk Class 2 Risk Class 2
Low Risk Class 3 Risk Class 3 Risk Class 3

The resulting risk class was then filtered by the detection controls (Table 4.4.2-3). Low detection is the use of the building monitoring system (e.g., HVAC, temperature monitoring systems) with no other means of detection. Medium detection includes the building monitoring system plus EM. High detection includes in-process product monitoring for contamination in addition to the building monitoring system and EM.

Table 4.4.2-3 — Sampling Priority Designation

Risk Class Low Detection Medium Detection High Detection
Class 1 (High) High Priority High Priority Medium Priority
Class 2 (Medium) High Priority Medium Priority Low Priority
Class 3 (Low) Medium Priority Low Priority Low Priority

The output of the EM risk assessment is the level of risk at the EM sampling location expressed as a high, medium, or low EM sampling priority. The EM sampling priority provides information used to assess the EM sampling plan for each room by EM sample location. This ensures the identification of EM sample locations within the rooms that provide meaningful EM data and those sample locations that may be redundant or do not significantly contribute to the overall picture of environmental control.

High EM sampling priority locations indicate room locations with the highest contamination risk or low detection capability. These EM sampling locations were assessed to determine if additional remediation should be implemented to increase their environmental control. Medium EM sampling priority locations presented an acceptable level of risk and provided meaningful EM data. Low EM sampling priority locations were assigned in areas that either posed a very low risk of product contamination or sites that may have had higher risk classes, but the risk was mitigated by high-detection capabilities.

三步驟確定採樣優先級

TR13-2 實例一採用了一個三層過濾系統，每一層都在降低不確定性：

第1層：危害概率（Hazard Probability）
由 Table 4.4.2-1 各因子加總得出
↓
第2層：嚴重性（Severity）
產品是否在場、是否暴露於環境 → Risk Class 1/2/3
↓
第3層：偵測能力（Detection）
現有監測系統是否足夠 → 最終採樣優先級

類比：ICH Q9 的 RPN 計算

這個三層框架本質上是 FMEA 中 RPN（Risk Priority Number）計算的改編：

  • Severity（嚴重性）：產品暴露程度
  • Occurrence（發生率）：危害概率（由各因子加總）
  • Detection（可偵測性）：現有監測系統的覆蓋程度

不同之處在於，此框架採用矩陣分類（Risk Class 1/2/3）而非乘積計算，避免了 RPN 乘積法的已知缺陷（如高O×低S×低D 與 低O×高S×低D 可能得到相同 RPN 但實際風險完全不同）。

偵測控制的保護性作用

Table 4.4.2-3 展示了一個重要觀念：高偵測能力可以降低採樣優先級。例如，一個 Risk Class 1（高危害、高嚴重性）的採樣點，若有「製程中產品污染監測」作為額外偵測手段，其採樣優先級可從「高優先」降至「中優先」。這意味著，投資在線監測系統（如快速微生物方法 RMM）可以合理降低傳統培養基 EM 的頻率。

4.4.3 Conclusion — Warm Room X

Warm Room X (Figure 4.4.3-1) is used to incubate the roller bottles. The room is maintained at 36°C ± 1°C. Activities in this room include introducing roller bottle racks, rotating roller bottles, removing roller bottles from the room, and inspecting daily for gross contamination or leaks of roller bottles; roller bottles will not be touched by humans. Due to the high traffic in the room and the higher than ambient temperature, sites in this room will be sampled weekly. Protecting the cleanliness of this room is key to the strategy of not contaminating roller bottles.

The locations noted represent current routine sampling locations that are assessed annually to determine if they are appropriate, if there have been changes in the activities in the room, and if adverse bioburden events have occurred that may have resulted in targeted monitoring to determine root cause.

中文解析：暖室 X 採樣設計的實務意義

為何選擇「每週」監測？

根據 Table 4.3.3-1，中等風險環境的監測頻率為每週一次。暖室 X 的綜合風險評估結果落在「中等至高等風險」，加上 36°C 的高溫環境（高危害分數），因此採用每週監測。

此外，監測的策略性目標是「保護滾瓶清潔度」——滾瓶雖為封閉系統，但頻繁的人員進出（搬移瓶架、每日目視檢查）使房間成為一個潛在的污染引入途徑。

年度採樣計畫審查（Annual Review）

TR13-2 特別強調採樣計畫應「每年評估一次」，觸發條件包括：

  • 房間活動內容發生變化
  • 出現不良生物負荷事件（Adverse Bioburden Events）
  • 歷史數據顯示趨勢改變

這與 PDA TR13 正本（Ref 1）的持續改進精神一致：EM 計畫不是靜態文件，而是隨製程知識累積而演化的動態工具。

4.5 Risk Assessment Design: Example 2

This report summarizes results of the risk analysis conducted to evaluate the EM sample plan for a media supplementation (via aliquoting) process for a cell therapy manufacturing process. Knowledgeable personnel from the microbiology, manufacturing, quality assurance, compliance, and manufacturing science and technology departments participated in the risk analysis.

In cell therapy manufacturing, media supplementation is performed by adding sterile supplements into sterile media using a sterile pipette or other sterile transfer method. The process occurs inside an ISO 5-classified UDF hood. Routine EM is performed based on the results of a risk assessment. Personnel are appropriately trained on the process and on the proper aseptic techniques. Personnel also wear sterile gloves and sterile sleeves while performing the process.

The media, supplements, and containers used during the process are sterile. In addition, an absence-of-contamination test is performed post-process using a rapid microbiology method.

4.5.1 Executive Summary

The EM program is designed to monitor manufacturing areas and ensure that the levels of environmental controls are maintained as specified in site SOPs and in the laboratory information management system. Using FMEA and following the risk assessment guidance methodology, an EM risk analysis was conducted to identify and quantify risks. The effort was intended to optimize the EM sample plans for media supplementation as well as to determine the appropriate ISO/Grade level needed. (See Table 4.3.1-1 for recommended classifications and types of activities.) Other risk tool methodology may be used depending on the specific process.

中文解析：實例二背景——培養基補充製程

什麼是「培養基補充」(Media Supplementation)?

在細胞治療製造中，細胞培養所需的培養基（media）通常需要在使用前或使用中加入特定補充劑（supplements），如細胞激素（cytokines）、生長因子（growth factors）、抗生素或血清替代品。這個加料過程稱為培養基補充（Media Supplementation），通常以分注（aliquoting）方式進行——使用無菌吸管將補充劑從容器中吸取後加入培養基容器。

為何是特定風險場景？雖然操作在 ISO 5 UDF 罩中進行，但吸管操作本身是開放系統的瞬間，人員的手套和袖子直接接觸吸管，每次吸管進出容器都是污染引入的機會窗口。

FMEA 方法的選用

與實例一（使用通用風險工具）不同，實例二明確使用 FMEA（Failure Mode and Effects Analysis，失效模式與影響分析）。FMEA 的優勢在於：

  • 系統性地列舉每個製程步驟可能的失效模式
  • 評估每種失效模式的影響（Effect）和可偵測性
  • 特別適合操作步驟多、人員接觸頻繁的手動製程

TR13-2 指出「其他風險工具方法亦可使用」，展現了 ICH Q9 強調方法靈活性的精神。

後製程快速微生物測試的角色

「缺乏污染測試（absence-of-contamination test）」在製程後使用快速微生物方法（RMM）執行，這提供了第三層偵測手段，直接對應 Table 4.4.2-3 中的「High Detection」。這意味著，即使某個 EM 採樣點的風險等級較高，但因為有製程後直接產品測試，其採樣優先級可以相應調降。

4.5.2 Discussion

A risk tool was executed using appropriate methodology to evaluate contamination risks during media supplementation. EM sample site locations, including personnel monitoring, and the types of monitoring were determined based on the nature of the process. The nature of the materials (i.e., sterile), duration of the process, personnel, material flow, and post-processing testing were considered during the analysis. The risk analysis evaluated each EM sample location for hazards including the various physical conditions, operational activities, and EM-specific indicators that could contribute to or detect contamination.

中文解析：培養基補充製程的風險特徵

Table 4.5.2-1：製程步驟分類的關鍵判斷

本表最值得注意的是：吸管加料操作被歸類為「中等風險（3分）」而非高風險（5分）。原因是：

  • 操作在 ISO 5 UDF 罩下進行，提供了局部防護
  • 所有材料均為無菌（起始點已高度受控）
  • 容器外壁已在進入 UDF 前消毒

相反，製程後的快速測試被歸為「低風險（1分）」，因為這是一種偵測/驗證活動，本身不引入污染。

Table 4.5.2-2：人員是最主要的風險來源

仔細觀察 Table 4.5.2-2，得「高風險（5分）」的因子幾乎全部與人員有關：

  • 吸管操作（人員手部動作）
  • 意外觸碰吸管或容器蓋（人員接觸）
  • 身體前傾遮蔽第一氣流（人員姿勢）
  • 整個操作為全手動流程（無自動化緩衝）
  • 手套消毒不充分（人員行為）

這解釋了為何 Table 4.5.3-1 將人員監測（Personnel Monitoring）列為最重要的監測項目，勝過環境表面或空氣監測。

低風險因子的保護性貢獻

本例中大量因子落在「低風險（1分）」欄位，如：只使用無菌一次性器材、周邊區域活動極少、無水源等。這些因子共同構成了製程的「風險緩解層」，使得整體EM計畫可以更精準聚焦在真正的高風險操作（吸管操作）上，而不需要在低風險區域鋪設大量監測點。

Based on the risk assessment, a routine EM was defined as seen in Table 4.5.3-1.

Personnel monitoring was determined to be the best indicator in detecting contamination based on the criticality factors and would provide the most information on contamination sources in the process. The process relies upon aseptic technique and manual manipulation and transfer of media into containers, making personnel monitoring the best detection tool for the introduction of contamination. In addition, contamination is readily detected in the media bottles by turbidity and, typically, by a color change of the media from red to yellow, indicating acid production.

「步驟特定」vs「時間特定」監測

此 EM 計畫的一個重要特點是：監測頻率以「製程步驟」為單位，而非「時間」為單位。例如，人員監測在「培養基補充製程中的定義時間點」執行，而非「每週一次」。

這種步驟特定（step-specific）的設計更適合批次相關操作（batch-related operations），因為批次的執行頻率本身就決定了監測頻率，避免了「批次頻率低但強制每週監測」或「批次頻率高但每週監測不足」的困境。

人員監測的顏色變化偵測

TR13-2 提到培養基污染可通過濁度（turbidity）和顏色從紅色變黃色直接偵測，原因是：大多數細胞培養基含有酚紅（Phenol Red）作為 pH 指示劑。當微生物生長並代謝培養基時產生酸性物質，pH 下降，培養基從紅色變為黃色。這是一種即時、肉眼可見的污染指標，是細胞培養製程的天然「快速污染偵測」機制，補充了傳統 EM 培養等待期間的監測空缺。

法規空白區域的因應

值得注意的是，這 13 篇參考文獻中沒有一篇是專門針對「低生物負荷環境 EM」的法規文件——因為截至 2020 年，此領域尚無專屬的全球性法規指引。這正是 PDA TR13-2 存在的原因：填補法規空白，提供行業最佳實踐指南。引用 ICH Q9、ISO 14644、USP <1115> 等文件是為了借助既有架構，在沒有特定法規命令的情況下，建構具科學說服力的 EM 計畫。

實例一：哺乳動物細胞培養暖室

  • 三層過濾系統：危害概率 × 嚴重性 × 偵測能力 → 採樣優先級
  • Table 4.4.2-1 的 0/1/3/5 評分矩陣覆蓋製程步驟和潛在污染貢獻者
  • 暖室 36°C 高溫使溫度因子直接評為 5分（最高風險）
  • 主動採氣採樣點（Sites 1/2/3）取得最高優先級，每週監測
  • 採樣計畫每年審查，由製造、QA、驗證、QC 和微生物 SME 共同維護

實例二：培養基補充製程

  • FMEA 方法識別手動吸管操作為最高風險步驟
  • 人員手套/袖子監測為首要 EM 項目（Human-as-Vector 概念）
  • 步驟特定（step-specific）監測頻率取代固定時間表
  • 後製程快速微生物測試作為第三層偵測，整合至風險評級
  • 酚紅顏色變化（紅→黃）提供天然即時污染指標
