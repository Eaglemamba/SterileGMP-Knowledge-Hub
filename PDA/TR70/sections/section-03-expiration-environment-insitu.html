6.0 In-Use Expiration Dating（使用中有效期標示）

6.0 In-Use Expiration Dating

Sanitizers, disinfectants, and sporicides should be assessed to ensure their performance throughout their assigned in-use period. They should be stored for use no longer than the predefined period as specified by written procedures.

The expiration dating provided by the manufacturer relates to the expiration of a closed or "primary" container. Once the container (ready-to-use or concentrate) has been opened, the manufacturer's expiration date is no longer valid for active ingredient potency and sterility.

The two important points surrounding in-use expiration are:

  • Antimicrobial effectiveness: The length of time the solution retains its ability to destroy microorganisms, as evaluated during efficacy testing.
  • Container bioburden: For controlled areas, how long the container and its contents maintain an appropriate bioburden level.

This is determined by performing bioburden testing on samples taken from containers (spray bottles, squeeze bottles, etc.) used in the disinfection process at the end of their in-use period.

Key Regulatory Implication

Failure to label prepared disinfectant containers with preparation date and in-use expiry is a common FDA 483 observation. Written SOPs must define the maximum in-use period for each type of container and agent.

6.0 使用中有效期標示

消毒劑（Sanitizer）、殺菌劑（Disinfectant）與殺孢子劑（Sporicide）在使用期間必須持續評估其效能，且不得超過書面程序所規定的預設使用期限。

製造商提供的有效期限，僅適用於未開封的原裝容器（Primary Container）。一旦容器被開封（無論是即用型還是濃縮液），原有效期即不再適用於活性成分效力與無菌性保證。

使用中有效期（In-Use Expiration Dating）的兩個核心考量：

  • 消毒效力保持期：溶液在多長時間內仍能達到效力測試所驗證的殺滅能力。
  • 容器生物負荷控制：在受控區域中，容器及其內容物能在多長時間內維持適當的微生物負荷水準。

判定方法：在使用期末，對噴霧瓶、擠壓瓶等實際使用中的容器取樣，進行生物負荷測試（Bioburden Testing）。

CDMO 稽核實務提醒

未在配製好的消毒劑容器上標示配製日期與使用期限，是 FDA 483 最常見的觀察項目之一。書面 SOP 必須明確規定每種容器與藥劑的最大使用期限。TWi 所有配製消毒劑容器均須依此標示。

7.0 Control of the Environment（環境管控）

7.0 Overview — Contamination Control Framework

An effective cleaning and disinfection system starts by limiting the introduction of contamination into the facility. Stopping both viable and nonviable contamination from entering controlled areas is critical to achieving the desired environmental conditions.

On the scale of importance, the control over the introduction of contamination is the most critical concern in the entire cleaning and disinfection process. This begins with the cleanliness of all items transferred into the facility — components, personnel, carts, tanks, tools, and instruments.

A comprehensive list of every item entering the controlled area should be constructed, and each item evaluated to determine whether it can be effectively cleaned and disinfected (or sterilized). Items that cannot be appropriately cleaned must be replaced.

Three high-risk time periods when contamination entry can impact operations:

  1. After cleaning/disinfection, before manufacturing begins: Personnel and components enter the area and potentially shed particulates and microbes during setup.
  2. During manufacturing interventions: Inappropriate clean room behavior and poor aseptic technique can contaminate disinfected surfaces.
  3. After manufacturing, before cleaning: Personnel must maintain aseptic awareness to preserve environmental cleanliness.

Control must also address proliferation of viable contaminants already present — molds can penetrate surfaces and equipment attachment points, making elimination difficult. Gram-negative organisms can generate endotoxins from product-contact surfaces if not cleaned appropriately.

7.0 概述 — 污染管控架構

有效的清潔消毒系統，始於限制污染物進入設施。阻止有生命（viable）與非生命（nonviable）污染物進入受控區域，是達到預期環境條件的關鍵。

在整個清潔消毒流程中，污染物進入的管控是最重要的環節。這從進入設施的每一項物品的清潔度開始：物料、人員、推車、槽桶、工具、儀器，全部納入管控。

應建立進入受控區域的所有物品清單，逐一評估是否能被有效清潔、消毒或滅菌。無法適當清潔的物品，必須以可清潔的替代品取代。

三個污染風險最高的時間節點：

  1. 清潔消毒完成後、生產開始前：人員與物料進入區域時，可能在設置期間散落微粒與微生物。
  2. 生產干預期間：不當的潔淨室行為與差劣的無菌操作技術，可能污染已消毒表面。
  3. 生產結束後、清潔前：人員仍需保持無菌意識，防止環境受到污染。

管控還必須涵蓋對已存在污染物的增殖進行防制。黴菌可滲入設備接縫等難以觸及處，造成清除困難。革蘭陰性菌若未適當清潔，可在產品接觸表面產生內毒素（Endotoxins）。

7.1 Introduction of Clean Room Manufacturing Supplies

The design of the facility and procedures in place must assure prevention of contamination from the flow of components, drug products, containers, closures, labeling, and in-process materials through the building.

"Materials should be disinfected according to appropriate procedures or, when used in critical areas, rendered sterile by a suitable method." — U.S. FDA Guidance for Industry: Sterile Drug Products Produced by Aseptic Processing (CGMPs)

Items entering the Aseptic Processing Area (APA) should be exposed to the highest level of decontamination the material can withstand. If an item can be physically sterilized, it must be.

Sterilization methods for items entering APA:

  • Autoclaving — most widely used; multiple outer wraps allow progressive de-layering as items move to cleaner grades.
  • Gamma irradiation — multiple wraps/barriers used, removed stepwise when passing to more stringent grades.
  • VPHP (Vaporized Hydrogen Peroxide) decontamination chambers — for items that cannot tolerate heat or radiation.
  • Manual sporicidal disinfection — spray or wipe with sporicidal agent as the minimum acceptable method for non-sterilizable items.
  • UV pass-through systems — can reduce bioburden but are NOT considered sterilization.

If an item cannot be disinfected before entering the clean room, it must not be introduced and a suitable alternative must be found. Personnel should not carry items through the gowning room.

Common clean room supplies requiring introduction procedures: mechanic's tools, carts, production supplies, handling implements, EM supplies, wipers, antimicrobial agents, filling equipment components, markers, pens, electronic equipment (meters, particle counters).

7.1 潔淨室製造耗材的引入管控

設施設計與程序必須確保，物料、藥品、容器、封蓋、標籤及中間品在廠房流轉過程中不引入污染。

「物料應依照適當程序消毒，或在關鍵區域使用時以適合的方法滅菌。」— 美國 FDA 工業指南：無菌加工藥品生產（cGMP）

進入無菌加工區（APA, Aseptic Processing Area）的物品，應接受該材質所能承受的最高等級去污處理。若物品能被實體滅菌，則必須滅菌。

進入 APA 物品的滅菌方法：

  • 高壓滅菌（Autoclaving）：最廣泛使用；多層外包裝可在物品進入更嚴格等級時逐層移除。
  • Gamma 輻射滅菌：多層包裝/屏障，逐步移除以進入更高潔淨等級。
  • VPHP（汽化過氧化氫）去污室：適用於無法承受高溫或輻射的物品。
  • 人工殺孢子消毒：以殺孢子劑噴灑或擦拭，為無法滅菌物品的最低可接受方式。
  • 紫外線（UV）穿越系統：可降低生物負荷，但不視為滅菌方法。

若物品進入潔淨室前無法被消毒，則不得引入，必須尋找合適替代品。人員不得攜帶物品通過更衣室。

常見需執行引入程序的潔淨室耗材：維修工具、推車、生產耗材、操作器具、EM 耗材、抹布、消毒藥劑、充填設備組件、筆記用具、電子設備（量測儀、粒子計數器）。

7.1.1 Types of Clean Room Disinfecting Agents

Three categories of antimicrobial chemical agents are used in clean room item disinfection:

Category Examples Key Properties Limitations
Sanitizers IPA 70%, EtOH 70% Quick-drying, minimal residue — preferred for item disinfection in APA. IPA slightly more bactericidal than EtOH. No effect on spores; rapid evaporation reduces contact time effectiveness.
Disinfectants Phenols, Quaternary Ammonium Compounds (QACs) Broad-spectrum kill of vegetative cells. Leave surface residues — must be followed by IPA wipe-down.
Sporicides Sodium hypochlorite (bleach), H₂O₂/peracetic acid, H₂O₂ 6% Required for spore-forming organisms. H₂O₂ breaks down to water + oxygen (no residue). Also active against molds. Most leave some residue (except H₂O₂).

IPA Selection Note

IPA 70% is preferred over EtOH 70% for item disinfection in the APA due to slightly greater bactericidal action, unless material compatibility prohibits its use.

7.1.1 潔淨室消毒藥劑類型

潔淨室物品消毒使用三大類抗微生物化學藥劑：

類別 典型藥劑 特性 限制
殺菌劑 Sanitizer 70% 異丙醇 IPA、70% 乙醇 EtOH 快速揮發、殘留極少；APA 物品消毒首選。IPA 殺菌效果略優於 EtOH。 對孢子無效；揮發過快降低有效接觸時間。
消毒劑 Disinfectant 苯酚類、季銨鹽（QACs） 廣效殺滅營養細胞。 會在表面留下殘留物，使用後需以 IPA 二次擦拭清除。
殺孢子劑 Sporicide 次氯酸鈉（漂白水）、H₂O₂ /過氧乙酸、6% H₂O₂ 針對孢子形成菌的必備藥劑。H₂O₂ 分解為水與氧氣（無殘留）；對黴菌亦有效。 多數會留下殘留物（H₂O₂ 除外）。

IPA 選用原則

除非材質相容性問題禁止，APA 物品消毒應優先選用 70% IPA，而非 70% EtOH，因 IPA 殺菌效果略勝一籌。

7.1.2 Introduction of Tanks, Vessels, Carts, and Equipment into the APA

The transfer of equipment into an aseptic system can introduce contamination and must be addressed accordingly. Ideally via autoclave or VPHP chamber, but commonly performed manually.

Special attention to cart wheels:

  • Wheels are high-risk — they contact floors outside the APA and carry significant contamination.
  • Manual process: First wipe with a particulate-free, non-shedding wipe to clean the wheel surface, then apply disinfectant to assure validated contact (dry) time.
  • VPHP chamber process: Wipe wheels with cleaning agent, then spray with antimicrobial agent before entering the chamber.

Why Wheels Matter

Cart wheels roll across non-classified corridors before entering the APA. Each rotation deposits floor contamination that is then carried into the clean room. Overlooking wheel disinfection is a frequent root cause identified in environmental excursion investigations.

7.1.2 槽桶、容器、推車與設備的引入管控

設備移入無菌加工系統的過程可能引入污染，必須妥善處理。理想方式為高壓滅菌或 VPHP 腔室處理，但實際上多以人工方式執行。

特別注意：推車輪子

  • 輪子是高風險部位，在進入 APA 前滾過非潔淨走廊，攜帶大量污染物。
  • 人工程序：先以無微粒、不脫落的抹布擦拭輪面清潔，再施用消毒劑確保達到驗證的接觸（乾燥）時間。
  • VPHP 腔室程序：先用清潔劑擦拭輪子，再噴灑抗微生物藥劑後進入腔室。

為何輪子格外重要

推車輪子在進入 APA 前，已滾過非潔淨等級走廊。每一圈轉動都在沉積地板污染物，隨後帶入潔淨室。忽視輪子消毒，是環境超標調查中頻繁發現的根本原因之一。

7.1.3 Introduction of Cleaning Supplies and Equipment into the APA

Cleaning supplies and equipment represent potential bioburden sources. Prior sterilization or disinfection is standard practice.

For Grade A (ISO 5) and adjacent Grade B (ISO 5/6) areas: Cleaning equipment (buckets, mops, mop handles, mop heads, sprayers, wipes, extensions) must be thoroughly cleaned and rendered sterile prior to use. Methods: steam sterilization or sterile single-use disposable systems.

Sterility of disinfectant solutions: Sanitizers, disinfectants, and sporicides can harbor resistant microorganisms. For Grade A/B (ISO 5/6) areas, solutions must be sterile-filtered or sterilized before use. Approaches:

  • Aseptic filtration at 0.2 µm of final-use dilution into pre-sterilized containers in the Grade A/B area.
  • Mixing with sterile unit-dose containers certified as sterile, using sterile WFI.
  • Purchasing ready-to-use or ready-to-mix sterile solutions from a vendor with appropriate sterility documentation.
  • Autoclaving the mixed solution (if formulation permits).

Container type matters: Aspirating containers (squeeze bottles, trigger sprayers, bulk containers opened and closed) introduce environmental contamination into the solution — 70% IPA bottles that aspirate have been found to harbor mold. Non-aspirating containers prevent contamination ingress and may be used until their validated expiration period.

Grade cascade rule: A solution used in a Grade A area may subsequently be used in Grade B, then Grade C, in that descending order. A solution used in Grade A cannot return to Grade A use unless kept under positive pressure. Discard open bucket contents after each cleaning operation.

7.1.3 清潔耗材與設備的引入管控

清潔耗材與設備本身即為潛在生物負荷來源，使用前滅菌或消毒應視為標準作業。

Grade A（ISO 5）與鄰近 Grade B（ISO 5/6）區域：桶子、拖把、拖把桿、拖把頭、噴霧器、抹布、延伸桿等清潔設備，使用前必須徹底清潔並滅菌。方法：蒸汽滅菌或無菌一次性拋棄式系統。

消毒溶液的無菌性要求：消毒劑本身可能含有耐藥微生物。用於 Grade A/B 區域的溶液，必須在使用前0.2 µm 過濾滅菌或滅菌處理。方法包括：

  • 在 Grade A/B 區域外，將最終使用濃度進行 0.2 µm 無菌過濾，注入置於 Grade A/B 區域的預滅菌容器。
  • 使用已驗證無菌的單劑量容器，以無菌注射用水（WFI）配製。
  • 向供應商採購有無菌性文件佐證的即用型或即配型無菌溶液。
  • 若配方允許，高壓滅菌已配製好的溶液。

容器類型的選擇：會吸入空氣的容器（擠壓瓶、扳機噴霧器、反覆開蓋的大容器）會將環境污染帶入溶液中——已有 70% IPA 瓶子因吸氣而含有黴菌的案例。不吸氣容器（Non-Aspirating Containers）可避免此問題，可使用至驗證的有效期限。

潔淨等級遞降使用原則：Grade A 使用後的溶液可降至 Grade B、再降至 Grade C 使用，但不得反向回到 Grade A，除非容器持續保持正壓防止污染回流。開放式桶系統的溶液，每次清潔操作完畢後應丟棄。

7.1.4 Introduction of Components into the APA

"Components" refers to items used directly in the manufacturing process — stoppers, plungers, vials, cartridges, and similar product-contact items.

Depyrogenation and sterilization are required for components that come into direct contact with the sterile product. A validated sterilization process must be used, and sterility must be maintained after entry into the APA through integration into the final product.

Methods: dry heat (for depyrogenation and sterilization) or validated washing and sterilization processes. For components sterilized outside the APA, multiple outer wraps, layers, or barriers allow appropriate disinfection before entering the Grade A (ISO 5) environment.

Refer to PDA Technical Report No. 3 (Revised 2013): Validation of Dry Heat Processes Used for Depyrogenation and Sterilization for additional guidance.

7.1.4 物料組件（Components）的引入管控

「組件（Components）」指直接用於生產製程的物品，包括膠塞（Stoppers）、柱塞（Plungers）、西林瓶（Vials）、卡匣（Cartridges）等產品接觸物品。

與無菌產品直接接觸的組件，須完成去熱原（Depyrogenation）與滅菌（Sterilization）。必須使用經過驗證的滅菌製程，且在進入 APA 後直至整合入最終產品的整個過程，均須維持無菌性。

方法：乾熱法（同時完成去熱原與滅菌）或驗證過的清洗滅菌程序。在 APA 外完成滅菌的組件，應使用多層外包裝，以便在進入 Grade A（ISO 5）環境前，一層一層消毒後移除。

詳細指引參閱：PDA Technical Report No. 3（2013 修訂版）：Validation of Dry Heat Processes Used for Depyrogenation and Sterilization。

7.2 Environmental Monitoring Data Analysis

Environmental monitoring (EM) data demonstrates the effectiveness of the microbial contamination control system, including the cleaning and disinfection program. Analysis considers:

  • Genus and species of organisms recovered
  • Number of isolates and their distribution within the facility
  • Trending history — comparison against historical performance

Normal pattern: When a facility is in control, data follows a predictable profile. Most common isolates are from personnel; fewer from air, soil, water, or liquid sources.

Analysis frequency: Formal documented trend analysis should be performed periodically. Evaluation of cleaning/disinfection program effectiveness and alert/action level adequacy should be performed at least annually. Results should be reported to site management and reviewed/documented by quality management.

Adverse trends and excursions: Any deviation from normal conditions triggers a signal. Actions are proportional to risk:

  • Alert level reached or atypical organism: heightened awareness notification.
  • Recurring action level exceedances: special cleaning/disinfection of the area.
  • Multiple sites or multiple occasions: full investigation.

Organism-specific responses: Vegetative organisms above action levels may require increased disinfection. Detection of spore-forming organisms above action level signals immediate response with sporicidal agents.

7.2 環境監測數據分析

環境監測（EM）數據用來證明微生物污染控制系統（包括清潔消毒程序）的有效性。分析重點包括：

  • 回收微生物的屬種（Genus & Species）鑑定
  • 分離株數量及其在設施內的分布
  • 趨勢追蹤——與歷史數據比較

正常狀態特徵：設施處於管控狀態時，數據呈現可預測的分布型態。最常見的分離株來自人員；來自空氣、土壤、水或液體來源的較少。

分析頻率：應定期進行正式、文件化的趨勢分析。清潔消毒程序有效性及警戒/行動限界適用性的評估，應至少每年執行一次。分析結果應呈報場址管理層，並由品質管理人員審查與文件化。

異常趨勢與偏差（Excursion）：任何偏離正常狀態的情況均需採取行動，行動力度與風險相稱：

  • 達到警戒限（Alert Level）或出現非典型菌：提高警覺通知。
  • 多次超過行動限（Action Level）：對該區域進行特別清潔消毒。
  • 多個監測點或多次發生：展開完整調查。

菌種特異性回應：超過行動限的營養細胞，可能需要加強消毒。偵測到超過行動限的孢子形成菌，則需立即以殺孢子劑進行處理。

7.3 Attaining and Selecting Environmental Isolates

Recoveries of microorganisms from environmental monitoring samples should be identified to genus and species level when exceeding alert or action levels, and periodically even when limits are not exceeded.

Organism identifications should be evaluated to determine the most frequently occurring organisms. Representative organisms should be preserved and included in the panel of organisms used in efficacy testing of antimicrobial chemical agents used in the facility.

Closing the Loop: EM to Efficacy Testing

This section connects environmental monitoring back to cleaning validation. The organisms you find in your facility should drive which test organisms are used to qualify your disinfectants — ensuring efficacy data reflects real-world contamination profiles rather than just standard challenge organisms.

7.3 環境分離株的取得與選用

超過警戒限或行動限時，必須將 EM 回收的微生物鑑定至屬種層級（Genus & Species）；即使未超限，也應定期進行鑑定。

應評估鑑定結果，確認最常出現的菌種，並保存代表性菌株，納入設施所使用消毒藥劑效力測試（Efficacy Testing）的測試菌株組合。

閉迴路管控：EM 與效力驗證的連結

本節點將環境監測與清潔驗證緊密連結。你在設施中找到的菌種，應成為消毒劑資格測試的測試菌株——確保效力數據反映真實的污染輪廓，而非僅依賴標準挑戰菌株。這是清潔驗證資料包（Cleaning Validation Package）的重要組成部分。

8.0 In-Situ Field Studies（現場實地研究）

8.0 In-Situ Field Studies — Overview

The true test of the effectiveness of a cleaning and disinfection program is the monitoring data collected from the manufacturing area. Evaluation of in-situ data from a robust environmental monitoring program will verify that the program is capable of attaining and maintaining a level of cleanliness that minimizes the probability of contamination.

In-situ data types include:

  • Nonviable (total particulate) data
  • Viable data for surfaces and ambient air
  • Personnel monitoring data
  • Microbial identification of representative environmental isolates
  • Residual testing of surfaces
  • Product quality data (in-process bioburden and sterility testing)

Two approaches to conducting in-situ monitoring are commonly used:

  1. Environmental monitoring before and after start-up of a facility or area (Section 8.1)
  2. Environmental monitoring before and after cleaning during routine operation (Section 8.2)

Laboratory vs. Real-World Performance

Coupon studies in the lab use controlled inoculums on polished stainless steel — clean, flat, and reproducible. Real clean rooms have epoxy floors, acrylic windows, irregular equipment surfaces, and personnel interactions. In-situ studies bridge this gap, confirming that the program works under actual operating conditions with real surfaces and real operators.

8.0 現場實地研究 — 概述

清潔消毒程序有效性的真正考驗，是從生產區域收集的監測數據。評估來自健全環境監測計劃的現場（In-Situ）數據，能驗證程序是否能達到並維持所需的清潔度，將污染風險降至最低。

In-situ 數據類型包含：

  • 非活性微粒（Non-viable Particulate）數據
  • 表面與環境空氣的活菌（Viable）數據
  • 人員監測數據
  • 代表性環境分離株的微生物鑑定結果
  • 表面殘留測試（Residual Testing）
  • 產品品質數據（中間品生物負荷與無菌測試）

現場監測常用兩種方法：

  1. 設施或區域啟用前後的環境監測（第 8.1 節）
  2. 例行生產期間清潔前後的環境監測（第 8.2 節）

實驗室 vs. 真實生產環境

實驗室的測試片（Coupon）研究使用受控菌液、拋光不鏽鋼材質——表面光滑、條件穩定、結果可重現。但真實潔淨室有環氧樹脂地板、壓克力窗、不規則設備表面，還有人員干預。In-situ 研究彌合這個落差，確認程序在真實作業條件、真實表面材質、真實操作人員下同樣有效。這是清潔驗證資料包的核心支撐。

8.1 Environmental Monitoring Before and After Start-up of a Facility or Area

Applicable scenarios include: opening a new area, area shutdown due to adverse events, areas that have undergone significant modifications, or areas left idle for a significant period.

Facilities should have special start-up cleaning and disinfection programs for shutdowns or significant construction activities.

Standard approach — step-by-step EM after each stage:

  1. Initial cleaning: Remove gross soil (broom, vacuum) — e.g., after construction, to prepare the area for formal cleaning.
  2. Baseline monitoring: Increased viable monitoring of air and surfaces. Nonviable air monitoring. This establishes the pre-cleaning baseline for comparison.
  3. Cleaning and disinfection: Execute the full cleaning and disinfection program.
  4. Post-cleaning monitoring: After surfaces are dry, repeat increased viable surface monitoring and nonviable air monitoring.

Analysis: Compare before/after data. A robust program should demonstrate reduction of viable and nonviable counts and minimization of spore-forming or mold contaminants. If results do not demonstrate acceptable reduction, the program must be reviewed and modified.

8.1 設施或區域啟用前後的環境監測

適用情境：新區域開啟、因異常事件關閉的區域、已進行重大改造的區域，或閒置較長時間的區域。

針對停工或重大施工活動，設施應備有專屬的啟動清潔消毒程序（Start-up C&D Program）。

標準方法——每個階段後進行 EM：

  1. 初步清潔：移除粗大污垢（掃帚、吸塵器）——例如施工完成後，為後續正式清潔程序做準備。
  2. 基線監測：加強空氣與表面的活菌監測；同時進行非活性微粒監測。此步驟建立清潔前基線數據作為比較基準。
  3. 執行清潔消毒：執行完整的清潔消毒程序。
  4. 清潔後監測：表面乾燥後，重複加強的表面活菌監測與非活性微粒監測。

數據分析：比對清潔前後數據。健全的清潔消毒程序應能證明活菌與非活性微粒數量顯著下降，並最小化孢子形成菌或黴菌的存在。若結果未達可接受的降低水準，必須檢討並修改程序。

8.2 Environmental Monitoring Before and After Cleaning During Routine Operation

In this approach, a facility in routine manufacturing operation evaluates its ongoing cleaning and disinfection program effectiveness:

  1. Increased viable surface and air monitoring is performed after operations have occurred and just before cleaning and disinfection take place. Nonviable air monitoring is also performed.
  2. Cleaning and disinfection are performed.
  3. Increased monitoring is performed again after cleaning and disinfection are complete.

The data gathered before and after is analyzed. Expectations: reduction of viable and nonviable counts, minimization of spore-forming or mold contaminants. If results are unsatisfactory, the program must be reviewed and modified.

Additional guidance: PDA Technical Report No. 13 (Revised 2014): Fundamentals of an Environmental Monitoring Program.

CDMO Application — Cleaning Validation Package

In-situ studies (8.1 and 8.2) generate the critical field data that supports the cleaning validation package. Regulators expect documented before/after EM data that demonstrates cleaning effectiveness under actual production conditions, not just laboratory coupon studies.

8.2 例行生產期間清潔前後的環境監測

此方法針對正常生產運行中的設施，用於評估清潔消毒程序的持續有效性：

  1. 在生產操作完成後、清潔消毒執行前，加強表面與空氣的活菌監測，並進行非活性微粒監測。
  2. 執行清潔消毒程序。
  3. 清潔消毒完成後，再次進行加強監測。

分析清潔前後的數據。預期：活菌與非活性微粒數量下降，孢子形成菌或黴菌最小化。若結果未達要求，必須檢討並修改程序。

延伸參考：PDA Technical Report No. 13（2014 修訂版）：Fundamentals of an Environmental Monitoring Program。

CDMO 應用 — 清潔驗證資料包

8.1 與 8.2 的 in-situ 研究，產生支撐清潔驗證資料包（Cleaning Validation Package）所需的現場關鍵數據。法規機關期望看到文件化的清潔前後 EM 數據，以證明清潔程序在真實生產條件下的有效性，而不僅是實驗室測試片研究結果。

Section Summary — 本節總結

Key Takeaways (English)

  • In-Use Expiration Dating (Sec. 6.0): Once a disinfectant container is opened, the manufacturer's expiration date is void. In-use periods must be defined, and containers labeled with preparation date and expiry — a common 483 observation.
  • Environmental Control (Sec. 7.0): Contamination prevention begins before cleaning starts — at the point of item introduction. Every item entering the APA requires a documented decontamination procedure. The highest achievable level of decontamination is required.
  • Disinfectant solution sterility: Sanitizers and disinfectants for Grade A/B must be sterile-filtered (0.2 µm) or otherwise sterilized before use. Non-aspirating containers prevent contamination ingress and are preferred.
  • EM Data Analysis (Sec. 7.2–7.3): EM data must be trended periodically. Environmental isolates should be identified and used to update efficacy test panels, closing the loop between monitoring and disinfection qualification.
  • In-Situ Field Studies (Sec. 8.0): Before/after EM around cleaning events provides the field-level evidence that laboratory efficacy data alone cannot supply. Both start-up scenarios and routine operations must be covered. This data is central to cleaning validation packages.

重點摘要（繁體中文）

  • 使用中有效期（第 6.0 節）：消毒劑容器一旦開封，製造商有效期即失效。必須定義使用期限並在容器上標示配製日期與到期日——此為最常見的 FDA 483 觀察項目。
  • 環境管控（第 7.0 節）：污染防制從清潔啟動前就開始——從物品進入的那一刻。每件進入 APA 的物品都需要書面去污程序，且應使用材質所能承受的最高去污等級。
  • 消毒溶液的無菌性：用於 Grade A/B 的消毒劑必須先行 0.2 µm 過濾滅菌或其他滅菌處理。不吸氣容器可防止環境污染進入，為優先選擇。
  • EM 數據分析（第 7.2–7.3 節）：EM 數據必須定期進行趨勢分析。環境分離株應鑑定並用於更新效力測試菌株組合，形成監測與消毒資格認證之間的閉迴路管控。
  • 現場實地研究（第 8.0 節）：清潔事件前後的 EM 數據，提供實驗室效力數據所無法取代的現場驗證證據。啟用情境與例行生產均須涵蓋。此數據為清潔驗證資料包的核心。
