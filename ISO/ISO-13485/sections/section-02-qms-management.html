品質管理系統要求與管理責任 — Clauses 4 & 5

本章學習目標

Clause 4.1 — 品質管理系統一般要求

原文內容 Original Text

4 Quality management system

4.1 General requirements

4.1.1

The organization shall document a quality management system and maintain its effectiveness in accordance with the requirements of this International Standard and applicable regulatory requirements.

The organization shall establish, implement and maintain any requirement, procedure, activity or arrangement required to be documented by this International Standard or applicable regulatory requirements.

The organization shall document the role(s) undertaken by the organization under the applicable regulatory requirements.

NOTE: Roles can include manufacturer, authorized representative, importer or distributor.

4.1.2

The organization shall:

  • determine the processes needed for the QMS and their application throughout the organization taking into account the roles undertaken by the organization;
  • apply a risk based approach to the control of the appropriate processes needed for the QMS;
  • determine the sequence and interaction of these processes.
4.1.3

For each QMS process, the organization shall:

  • determine criteria and methods to ensure operation and control are effective;
  • ensure the availability of resources and information;
  • implement actions necessary to achieve planned results;
  • monitor, measure as appropriate, and analyse these processes;
  • establish and maintain records needed to demonstrate conformance (see 4.2.5).
4.1.4

Changes to QMS processes shall be:

  • evaluated for their impact on the quality management system;
  • evaluated for their impact on the medical devices produced;
  • controlled in accordance with the requirements of this International Standard and applicable regulatory requirements.
4.1.5

When the organization chooses to outsource any process that affects product conformity to requirements, it shall monitor and ensure control over such processes. The organization shall retain responsibility of conformity to this International Standard and to customer and applicable regulatory requirements for outsourced processes. The controls shall be proportionate to the risk involved and the ability of the external party to meet the requirements in accordance with 7.4. The controls shall include written quality agreements.

4.1.6

The organization shall document procedures for the validation of the application of computer software used in the quality management system. Such software applications shall be validated prior to initial use and, as appropriate, after changes to such software or its application. The specific approach and activities associated with software validation and revalidation shall be proportionate to the risk associated with the use of the software.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

概念解析：4.1 的三層邏輯

Clause 4.1 的結構是：

  • 4.1.1：建立 QMS 的基本義務 + 識別組織角色
  • 4.1.2–4.1.3：流程識別與控制方法（PDCA 循環的架構）
  • 4.1.4：變更控制（任何對 QMS 流程的修改都需評估影響）
  • 4.1.5：外包流程控制（責任不可外包）
  • 4.1.6：QMS 軟體驗證（2016 版新增的重要要求）

對多國市場 CDMO 而言，4.1.1 要求「文件化在適用法規下的角色」意味著：在不同客戶/市場的 QMS 範疇聲明中，需要明確列出適用的法規清單（FDA 21 CFR Part 820、EU MDR、PMDA 等）。

重點提示：4.1.5 外包控制 — 最容易被忽視的要求

4.1.5 的核心邏輯：外包執行，但不能外包責任。

稽核員針對外包控制最常問的問題：

  • 是否有書面品質協議（Written Quality Agreement）？
  • 品質協議中是否規定了績效指標和稽核權利？
  • 控制措施是否與風險成比例（高風險外包 = 更嚴格控制）？
  • 外包商是否依照 7.4（採購）進行資格確認？

常見失誤：CDMO 將滅菌外包後僅依賴滅菌廠的 CoC（Certificate of Compliance），未對滅菌驗證資料進行獨立審查 → 此為 4.1.5 的 Major NC。

概念解析：4.1.6 QMS 軟體驗證 — 2016 版新增要求

這是 2016 版相對於 2003 版最重要的新增要求之一。涵蓋範圍：

  • 電子批次記錄系統（eBMR/EBR）
  • LIMS（實驗室資訊管理系統）
  • ERP 中的品質模組（SAP QM 等）
  • 統計分析軟體（Minitab、JMP 等）用於 QC 放行決策
  • 文件管理系統（DMS/EDMS）

驗證範圍需與軟體的使用風險成比例——影響放行決策的系統需要最嚴格的 IQ/OQ/PQ 驗證。

類比說明：風險導向流程控制

4.1.2 要求對 QMS 流程應用「風險導向方法」。這就像航空公司的安全管理：

起飛前的引擎檢查（高風險流程）需要最嚴格的確認程序；座椅靠背調整（低風險流程）則不需要同等級別的控制。

在 CDMO 的 QMS 中，這意味著：無菌灌裝流程（直接影響患者安全）的流程控制遠比行政費用報銷流程嚴格，文件要求、監控頻率、人員資格要求也隨風險等級遞增。

Clause 4.2 — 文件要求：從手冊到記錄的完整架構

原文內容 Original Text

4.2 Documentation requirements

4.2.1 General

The QMS documentation shall include:

  • documented statements of a quality policy and quality objectives;
  • a quality manual;
  • documented procedures and records required by this International Standard;
  • documents, including records, determined by the organization to be necessary to ensure the effective planning, operation, and control of its processes;
  • other documentation specified by applicable regulatory requirements.

4.2.2 Quality manual

The organization shall document a quality manual that includes:

  • the scope of the quality management system, including details of and justification for any exclusion or non-application;
  • the documented procedures for the quality management system, or reference to them;
  • a description of the interaction between the processes of the quality management system.

The quality manual shall outline the structure of the documentation used in the quality management system.

4.2.3 Medical device file

For each medical device type or medical device family, the organization shall establish and maintain one or more files either containing or referencing documents generated to demonstrate conformity to the requirements of this International Standard and compliance with applicable regulatory requirements.

The content of the file(s) shall include, but is not limited to:

  • general description of the medical device, intended use/purpose, and labelling, including any instructions for use;
  • specifications for product;
  • specifications or procedures for manufacturing, packaging, storage, handling and distribution;
  • procedures for measuring and monitoring;
  • as appropriate, requirements for installation;
  • as appropriate, procedures for servicing.

4.2.4 Control of documents

Documents required by the QMS shall be controlled. A documented procedure shall define the controls needed to:

  • a) review and approve documents for adequacy prior to issue;
  • b) review, update as necessary and re-approve documents;
  • c) ensure that the current revision status of and changes to documents are identified;
  • d) ensure that relevant versions of applicable documents are available at points of use;
  • e) ensure that documents remain legible and readily identifiable;
  • f) ensure that documents of external origin are identified and their distribution controlled;
  • g) prevent deterioration or loss of documents;
  • h) prevent the unintended use of obsolete documents and apply suitable identification to them.

The organization shall define the period for which at least one copy of obsolete documents shall be retained. This period shall ensure that documents to which medical devices have been manufactured and tested are available for at least the lifetime of the medical device, but not less than the retention period of any resulting record, or as specified by applicable regulatory requirements.

4.2.5 Control of records

Records shall be maintained to provide evidence of conformity to requirements and of the effective operation of the QMS. The organization shall document procedures to define controls needed for identification, storage, security and integrity, retrieval, retention time and disposition of records.

The organization shall define and implement methods for protecting confidential health information contained in records in accordance with applicable regulatory requirements.

Records shall remain legible, readily identifiable and retrievable. Changes to a record shall remain identifiable.

The organization shall retain records for at least the lifetime of the medical device as defined by the organization, or as specified by applicable regulatory requirements, but not less than two years from the medical device release by the organization.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

概念解析：Medical Device File（4.2.3）— ISO 13485 的核心創新

MDF 是 ISO 13485 相對於 ISO 9001 最具特色的獨特要求。它不是一份單一文件，而是一套文件集合：

  • 器械描述 + 預期用途 + 標籤（含 IFU）
  • 產品規格書（材料、尺寸、性能）
  • 製造/包裝/儲存/配送程序
  • 量測和監控程序
  • 安裝要求（如適用）
  • 服務程序（如適用）

MDF 的概念與 FDA QSR 的 Device History Record (DHR) + Device Master Record (DMR) 體系和歐盟 MDR 的 Technical Documentation 高度對應。

對 CDMO 的含義：CDMO 通常不擁有 MDF（客戶擁有），但需要向 MDF 貢獻製造相關文件（第 c 點），並確保自己的生產程序能對應到客戶 MDF 的要求。

重點提示：文件管制的 8 項控制 — 稽核熱點

4.2.4 列出 8 項（a–h）文件管制要求，每一項都是稽核觀察點：

  • a) 發行前審批：新版 SOP 未批准即使用 → Nonconformity
  • b) 審查與再批准：SOP 上次修訂已超過規定週期未審查 → Observation
  • c) 識別現行版本：文件缺少版次標識或修訂履歷 → Nonconformity
  • d) 使用點可得性：生產現場使用過期版本 SOP → Major NC
  • e) 清晰可識別：字跡模糊的作業指導書 → Observation
  • f) 外來文件管制：客戶規格書未登錄文件清單 → Nonconformity
  • g) 防止損壞/遺失：電子文件無備份機制 → Observation
  • h) 防止誤用廢止文件：舊版 SOP 仍在現場未標記「已廢止」→ Major NC

概念解析：記錄保存期限的計算邏輯

4.2.5 的記錄保存要求是三個條件取最長：

  1. 組織定義的器械生命週期
  2. 最少自器械放行起 2 年
  3. 適用法規要求的保存期限

實務參考：

  • FDA 21 CFR 820.180：記錄保存至少 2 年或器械預期壽命（取較長）
  • EU MDR：植入式器械記錄需保存至少 15 年
  • 一般消耗性器械（PFS/注射器）：通常 5–10 年

CDMO 必須在品質協議中與客戶確認保存期限，確保各方一致。

類比說明：文件 vs. 記錄的本質差異

ISO 13485 明確區分「文件（document）」和「記錄（record）」：

文件：告訴你「應該怎麼做」的規定性文件（SOP、規格書、手冊）——可以被修訂更新。

記錄：證明你「已經做了什麼」的客觀證據（批次記錄、測試報告）——不能被修改，只能更正（需留稽核軌跡）。

類比：食譜（文件）可以改版；你昨天實際做了什麼料理的記錄（記錄）不能回頭修改。稽核員最關注：記錄是否真實反映了實際發生的事情。

實務思考 Practice Questions

  1. CDMO 接受一個新客戶，客戶提供了一份詳細的器械規格書（Product Specification）和一份 MDF 摘要。根據 4.2.3 和 4.2.4，CDMO 應該如何將這些外來文件整合進自己的 QMS 文件管制系統？
  2. 生產現場的操作員使用了一份已廢止的灌裝參數設定 SOP（Revision A 而非最新的 Revision C）完成了一批生產。根據 4.2.4(h)，這是哪個級別的缺失（Observation / Minor NC / Major NC）？CAPA 應如何制定？
  3. 某植入式醫療器械（心臟支架）的批次記錄，器械生命週期客戶定義為 10 年，器械於 2026 年放行。考慮 EU MDR 的 15 年要求，這批記錄最少應保存到哪一年？

概念解析：QMS 文件層次架構

ISO 13485 的文件體系構成一個金字塔：

ISO 13485 比 ISO 9001 更嚴格的地方在於：Legal 4 適用法規文件（如 FDA 法規、CE 技術文件）也是文件體系的一部分，需納入管制。

Clause 5.1–5.4 — 管理責任：承諾、客戶焦點、方針與規劃

原文內容 Original Text

5 Management responsibility

5.1 Management commitment

Top management shall provide evidence of its commitment to the development and implementation of the quality management system and maintenance of its effectiveness by:

  • communicating to the organization the importance of meeting customer as well as applicable regulatory requirements;
  • establishing the quality policy;
  • ensuring that quality objectives are established;
  • conducting management reviews;
  • ensuring the availability of resources.

5.2 Customer focus

Top management shall ensure that customer requirements and applicable regulatory requirements are determined and met.

5.3 Quality policy

Top management shall ensure that the quality policy:

  • is applicable to the purpose of the organization;
  • includes a commitment to comply with requirements and to maintain the effectiveness of the quality management system;
  • provides a framework for establishing and reviewing quality objectives;
  • is communicated and understood within the organization;
  • is reviewed for continuing suitability.

5.4.1 Quality objectives

Top management shall ensure that quality objectives, including those needed to meet applicable regulatory requirements and requirements for product, are established at relevant functions and levels within the organization. The quality objectives shall be measurable and consistent with the quality policy.

5.4.2 Quality management system planning

Top management shall ensure that:

  • the planning of the QMS is carried out in order to meet the requirements given in 4.1, as well as the quality objectives;
  • the integrity of the QMS is maintained when changes to the quality management system are planned and implemented.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

概念解析：Management Commitment（5.1）的「evidence」要求

注意 5.1 的措辭：「provide evidence of its commitment」。Top Management 的承諾不是空話，稽核員會尋找具體的客觀證據：

  • 管理審查會議記錄（是否有 Top Management 親自參與？）
  • 品質目標的分配（是否落實到各職能部門？）
  • 資源分配決定（預算、人員配置）
  • 法規溝通記錄（Top Management 是否了解適用法規？）

若稽核員發現管理審查由 QA 副理代替 CEO 主持，這可能構成 5.1 的 Observation——「Top Management 未直接提供承諾的證據」。

重點提示：5.4.1 品質目標必須包含法規要求

ISO 13485 在 5.4.1 明確要求品質目標需包含「適用法規要求所需的目標」。這意味著：

  • 若適用法規要求投訴回應時限為 30 天，品質目標可能需要設定為「100% 投訴在 30 天內初步回應」
  • 若 FDA 483 觀察事項要求改善特定流程，相關改善目標應納入品質目標
  • 品質目標必須「可測量（measurable）」——非「提升客戶滿意度」，而是「客戶滿意度調查分數 ≥ 4.2/5」

類比說明：5.4.2 QMS 完整性在變更時的保護

5.4.2(b) 要求在 QMS 變更時「維護 QMS 的完整性」。這就像醫院在裝修手術室時——施工期間不能停止手術，需要有臨時措施確保無菌操作的連續性。

在 CDMO 的場景中：當組織遷廠、更換 ERP 系統或擴增新產品線時，需要事先規劃：哪些 QMS 流程會受影響？如何確保在過渡期間品質控制不中斷？這些規劃需要文件化。

概念解析：5.3 品質方針 — 與 ISO 9001 的關鍵差異

這是一個微妙但重要的差異：

實務意義：ISO 13485 的品質方針不必承諾「持續改進」——這反映了監管邏輯：法規要的是一致性合規，而非開放式的改善承諾。但若組織的品質方針同時聲稱符合 ISO 9001，則必須加回持續改進承諾。

Clause 5.5–5.6 — 責任與授權、內部溝通、管理審查

原文內容 Original Text

5.5.1 Responsibility and authority

Top management shall ensure that responsibilities and authorities are defined, documented and communicated within the organization.

Top management shall document the interrelation of all personnel who manage, perform and verify work affecting quality and shall ensure the independence and authority necessary to perform these tasks.

5.5.2 Management representative

Top management shall appoint a member of management who, irrespective of other responsibilities, has responsibility and authority that includes:

  • ensuring that processes needed for the QMS are documented;
  • reporting to top management on the effectiveness of the QMS and any need for improvement;
  • ensuring the promotion of awareness of applicable regulatory requirements and QMS requirements throughout the organization.

5.5.3 Internal communication

Top management shall ensure that appropriate communication processes are established within the organization and that communication takes place regarding the effectiveness of the quality management system.

5.6.1 Management review — General

The organization shall document procedures for management review. Top management shall review the organization's QMS at documented planned intervals to ensure its continuing suitability, adequacy and effectiveness. The review shall include assessing opportunities for improvement and the need for changes to the QMS, including the quality policy and quality objectives.

Records from management reviews shall be maintained (see 4.2.5).

5.6.2 Review input

The input to management review shall include, but is not limited to, information arising from:

  • a) feedback;
  • b) complaint handling;
  • c) reporting to regulatory authorities;
  • d) audits;
  • e) monitoring and measurement of processes;
  • f) monitoring and measurement of product;
  • g) corrective action;
  • h) preventive action;
  • i) follow-up actions from previous management reviews;
  • j) changes that could affect the QMS;
  • k) recommendations for improvement;
  • l) applicable new or revised regulatory requirements.

5.6.3 Review output

The output from management review shall be recorded and include the input reviewed and any decisions and actions related to:

  • improvement needed to maintain the suitability, adequacy, and effectiveness of the QMS and its processes;
  • improvement of product related to customer requirements;
  • changes needed to respond to applicable new or revised regulatory requirements;
  • resource needs.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

概念解析：5.5.1 責任矩陣 — 「獨立性」的法規要求

5.5.1 特別要求確保影響品質人員的「獨立性和授權」。這在醫療器械行業有特定意涵：

  • QA/QC 人員不能向生產主管直接匯報（避免商業壓力影響品質決定）
  • 負責最終放行決策的 QP（Qualified Person）必須有拒絕放行的獨立授權
  • 稽核員（Internal Auditor）不能稽核自己負責的流程

文件要求：需有組織架構圖（Org Chart）+ 職責說明書（Job Description）+ 職責矩陣（RACI Matrix），三者需一致。

概念解析：Management Representative（5.5.2）的特殊職責

5.5.2 的 Management Representative（管理代表）在 ISO 13485 中有一個在 ISO 9001:2015 中已被取消的特定要求：這個職位必須是「management 的成員」（不能只是普通員工）。

關鍵職責 c）要求管理代表確保「適用法規要求的認知在整個組織內推廣」——這意味著：

  • 當 FDA 或 EU 更新法規時，管理代表有責任確保組織知曉
  • 法規培訓計劃應由管理代表督導
  • 稽核員可能要求面談管理代表，確認其對適用法規的掌握程度

重點提示：5.6.2 管理審查輸入 — 醫療器械特定項目

與 ISO 9001 相比，ISO 13485 的管理審查輸入增加了醫療器械特定項目：

  • b) 投訴處理：需有統計摘要，含投訴率趨勢、嚴重投訴案例分析
  • c) 向法規機構的通報：MDR（Medical Device Reporting）、不良事件回報的統計
  • l) 適用的新/修訂法規要求：必須告知 Top Management 法規變化及其對 QMS 的影響

常見缺失：管理審查記錄缺乏對「投訴趨勢」和「法規更新」的討論 → Major NC in FDA/notified body audits。

類比說明：5.6 管理審查的本質

管理審查就像企業的「年度董事會健康體檢」——不是要處理個別問題（那是 CAPA 的工作），而是從系統層面評估整個 QMS 是否仍然適合、充分、有效。

5.6.3 的輸出要求有四類決定：QMS 改善、產品改善、法規變更回應、資源需求。注意：沒有「持續改進」這個措辭——只有「維持適合性、充分性和有效性所需的改善」，這與 ISO 9001 的語言有細微但重要的差別。

若管理審查的結論每年都是「一切良好，無需改變」，稽核員會質疑：這個審查是否真的被認真執行了？

實務思考 Practice Questions

  1. 某 CDMO 的管理審查每年由品保主任（QA Manager，非 Top Management）主持，記錄顯示從未有 CEO 或 COO 出席。根據 5.1 和 5.6 的要求，這種做法是否符合 ISO 13485？稽核員可能提出什麼觀察？
  2. EU MDR（EU 2017/745）於 2024 年更新了若干技術文件要求。根據 5.6.2(l) 和 5.6.3(c)，CDMO 的管理審查應如何回應這個法規變化？需要產生什麼類型的 output？
  3. 5.5.2 要求 Management Representative 促進全組織對法規要求的認知。設計一個簡單的機制，確保生產操作員（不直接閱讀法規原文的人）也能了解與其工作相關的法規要求更新。

  • 4.1 外包不外包責任：CDMO 外包任何影響產品符合性的流程（如滅菌、分析測試）時，必須有書面品質協議，控制程度與風險成比例；QMS 軟體（ERP、LIMS、eBMR）需驗證。
  • 4.2.3 Medical Device File 是 ISO 13485 的核心創新：每個器械型號/系列需有獨立的文件集，包含從描述到製造到監控的完整技術文件；CDMO 需向客戶的 MDF 貢獻製造程序文件。
  • 4.2.4 文件管制 8 項控制：發行前審批、審查再批准、版次識別、使用點可得性、清晰識別、外來文件管制、防損/遺失、防廢止文件誤用——每一項都是稽核熱點。
  • 5.3 品質方針無需承諾「持續改進」：ISO 13485 只要求「維護 QMS 有效性」和「遵從要求」，這是與 ISO 9001 的重要差異，體現了監管邏輯對一致性合規的優先。
  • 5.6 管理審查有醫療器械特定輸入：投訴處理狀況、法規機構通報記錄、適用新/修訂法規要求——這三項輸入是 ISO 9001 沒有的，確保 Top Management 持續了解法規動態並作出決策。
