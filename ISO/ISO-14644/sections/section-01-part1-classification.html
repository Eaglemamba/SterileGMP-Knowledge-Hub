Introduction — 前言

Introduction

Cleanrooms and associated controlled environments provide for the control of contamination of air and, if appropriate, surfaces, to levels appropriate for accomplishing contamination-sensitive activities. Contamination control can be beneficial for protection of product or process integrity in applications in industries such as aerospace, microelectronics, pharmaceuticals, medical devices, healthcare and food.

This part of ISO 14644 specifies classes of air cleanliness in terms of the number of particles expressed as a concentration in air volume. It also specifies the standard method of testing to determine cleanliness class, including selection of sampling locations.

This edition is the result of a response to an ISO Systematic Review and includes changes in response to user and expert feedback validated by international enquiry. The nine ISO cleanliness classes are retained with minor revisions. Table 1 defines the particle concentration at various particle sizes for the nine integer classes.

The most significant change is the adoption of a more consistent statistical approach to the selection and the number of sampling locations; and the evaluation of the data collected. The statistical model is based on adaptation of the hypergeometric sampling model technique, where samples are drawn randomly without replacement from a finite population. The new approach allows each location to be treated independently with at least a 95% level of confidence that at least 90% of the cleanroom or clean zone areas will comply with the maximum particle concentration limit for the target class of air cleanliness.

The revised version of this part of ISO 14644 addresses the ≥ 5 µm particle limits for ISO Class 5 in the sterile products annexes of the EU, PIC/S and WHO GMPs by way of an adaptation of the macroparticle concept.

導讀解析 Commentary

為什麼需要 ISO 14644-1？

想像一下：如果每家藥廠、每個國家都用不同的方式定義「多乾淨才算乾淨」，國際間的合規驗證將一片混亂。ISO 14644-1 正是為了解決這個問題而生的——它提供了一套通用的語言，讓全球任何地方的潔淨室都能用相同的標準溝通。

本標準適用於：航空航天、微電子、製藥、醫療器材、食品等多個行業，但在製藥領域，它更與 EU GMP Annex 1、FDA 無菌指引、PIC/S 等法規密切連結。

2015 版 vs. 1999 版：關鍵變化

2015 年第二版（現行版）的最重要改變：

  • 統計方法革新：捨棄 1999 版假設粒子計數呈常態分佈的前提，改採超幾何抽樣模型（Hypergeometric Sampling Model），每個採樣點獨立評估
  • 信心水準明確化：95% 信心水準 + 90% 區域符合率，取代 1999 版不一致的 95% UCL 應用
  • 採樣點數量改變：Table A.1 重新設計，最小採樣點數量有所調整
  • ≥5 µm 粒子：透過 M descriptor 概念，明確處理 EU GMP Grade A 的 ≥5 µm 限值問題

比喻：ISO Class 就像飯店星級評定

就像五星飯店和三星飯店都有明確的服務標準一樣，ISO Class 1（最潔淨）到 ISO Class 9（最不嚴格）也有明確的粒子濃度標準。數字越小，代表空氣越乾淨，要求越嚴苛——就像五星飯店比三星飯店要求更高一樣。

Section 1 — Scope 適用範圍

1 Scope

This part of ISO 14644 specifies the classification of air cleanliness in terms of concentration of airborne particles in cleanrooms and clean zones; and separative devices as defined in ISO 14644-7.

Only particle populations having cumulative distributions based on threshold (lower limit) particle sizes ranging from 0.1 µm to 5 µm are considered for classification purposes.

The use of light scattering (discrete) airborne particle counters (LSAPC) is the basis for determination of the concentration of airborne particles, equal to and greater than the specified sizes, at designated sampling locations.

This part of ISO 14644 does not provide for classification of particle populations that are outside the specified lower threshold particle-size range, 0.1 µm to 5 µm. Concentrations of ultrafine particles (particles smaller than 0.1 µm) will be addressed in a separate standard to specify air cleanliness by nano-scale particles. An M descriptor (see Annex C) may be used to quantify populations of macroparticles (particles larger than 5 µm).

This part of ISO 14644 cannot be used to characterize the physical, chemical, radiological, viable or other nature of airborne particles.

2 Normative References

  • ISO 14644-2:2015 — Monitoring of air cleanliness by particle concentration
  • ISO 14644-7 — Separative devices (clean air hoods, gloveboxes, isolators and mini-environments)

重點解析 Commentary

適用範圍三要素

本標準管轄的邊界非常明確，可以用三個問題來判斷是否適用：

  1. 場所：是潔淨室（Cleanroom）、潔淨區（Clean Zone），還是隔離裝置（Separative Device）？→ 是
  2. 粒徑範圍：粒子大小在 0.1 µm 到 5 µm 之間？→ 適用本標準；<0.1 µm 或 >5 µm → 分別使用其他方法
  3. 測量方法：使用 LSAPC（光散射離散式空氣粒子計數器）？→ 是

比喻：LSAPC 就像一台超精密「粒子照相機」

LSAPC 的工作原理：讓空氣通過一道雷射光束，每當有粒子通過，就會散射出光信號。根據散射光的強度，計算粒子的大小與數量。就像交通攝影機會記錄每輛車的尺寸一樣，LSAPC 也會記錄每個「粒子車輛」的大小。

CDMO 實務場景

在 CDMO 廠的潔淨室驗收（IQ/OQ/PQ）過程中，確認使用的粒子計數儀為 LSAPC，且具備有效校正證書（依 ISO 21501-4），是第一個合規查核點。所有分級報告必須明確聲明依據 ISO 14644-1:2015（注意年份，不是 1999 版）。

Section 3 — Terms and Definitions 術語定義

3.1 General Terms

3.1.1 cleanroom（潔淨室）: Room within which the number concentration of airborne particles is controlled and classified, and which is designed, constructed and operated in a manner to control the introduction, generation and retention of particles inside the room.

Note 1: The class of airborne particle concentration is specified.
Note 2: Levels of other cleanliness attributes such as chemical, viable or nanoscale concentrations in the air, and also surface cleanliness might also be specified and controlled.
Note 3: Other relevant physical parameters might also be controlled as required, e.g. temperature, humidity, pressure, vibration and electrostatic.

3.1.2 clean zone（潔淨區）: Defined space within which the number concentration of airborne particles is controlled and classified, and which is constructed and operated in a manner to control the introduction, generation and retention of contaminants inside the space.

Note 3: A clean zone(s) can be a defined space within a cleanroom or might be achieved by a separative device.

3.1.3 installation（安裝設施）: Cleanroom or one or more clean zones, together with all associated structures, air-treatment systems, services and utilities.

3.1.4 classification（分級）: Method of assessing level of cleanliness against a specification for a cleanroom or clean zone.

3.2 Airborne Particle Terms

3.2.1 particle（微粒）: Minute piece of matter with defined physical boundaries.

3.2.2 particle size（粒徑）: Diameter of a sphere that produces a response, by a given particle-sizing instrument, that is equivalent to the response produced by the particle being measured. Note: For discrete-particle light-scattering instruments, the equivalent optical diameter is used.

3.2.3 particle concentration（粒子濃度）: Number of individual particles per unit volume of air.

3.2.4 particle size distribution（粒徑分佈）: Cumulative distribution of particle concentration as a function of particle size.

3.2.5 macroparticle（大粒子）: Particle with an equivalent diameter greater than 5 µm.

3.2.6 M descriptor: Designation for measured or specified concentration of macroparticles per cubic metre of air, expressed in terms of the equivalent diameter that is characteristic of the measurement method used. Note: M descriptors cannot be used to define ISO Classes, but the M descriptor may be quoted independently or in conjunction with ISO Classes.

3.2.7 unidirectional airflow（單向流）: Controlled airflow through the entire cross-section of a cleanroom or a clean zone with a steady velocity and airstreams that are considered to be parallel.

3.2.8 non-unidirectional airflow（非單向流）: Air distribution where the supply air entering the cleanroom or clean zone mixes with the internal air by means of induction.

3.3 Occupancy States

3.3.1 as-built（竣工狀態）: Condition where the cleanroom or clean zone is complete with all services connected and functioning but with no equipment, furniture, materials or personnel present.

3.3.2 at-rest（靜止狀態）: Condition where the cleanroom or clean zone is complete with equipment installed and operating in a manner agreed upon, but with no personnel present.

3.3.3 operational（運行狀態）: Agreed condition where the cleanroom or clean zone is functioning in the specified manner, with equipment operating and with the specified number of personnel present.

3.4–3.5 Instrument Terms

3.5.1 LSAPC: Light scattering discrete airborne particle counter — instrument capable of counting and sizing single airborne particles and reporting size data in terms of equivalent optical diameter. Note: Specifications given in ISO 21501-4:2007.

3.5.2 discrete-macroparticle counter: Instrument capable of counting and sizing single airborne macroparticles.

3.5.3 time-of-flight particle sizing apparatus: Discrete-particle counting and sizing apparatus that defines the aerodynamic diameter of particles by measuring the time for a particle to accommodate to a change in air velocity.

術語深度解析 Commentary

潔淨室 vs. 潔淨區：有什麼差別？

Cleanroom（潔淨室）是一個獨立的房間，有實體牆壁和空調系統。

Clean Zone（潔淨區）的範圍更廣——它可以是：

  • 潔淨室內的特定區域（例如灌裝線附近的 Grade A 區）
  • 隔離器（Isolator）或 RABS 所形成的保護空間
  • 層流工作台（Laminar Flow Workstation）內部

在 EU GMP Annex 1 的無菌製造環境中，Grade A 通常指的是「Clean Zone」，而不是整個房間。

比喻：三種狀態就像餐廳的三種情況

As-built = 餐廳剛裝修完，還沒放桌椅，廚房設備也沒安裝，只有空調通風在運行。

At-rest = 餐廳備餐完成，桌椅齊全、廚房設備全開，但還沒開門，廚師和服務員都還沒進來。

Operational = 餐廳正常營業，廚師在廚房炒菜，服務員穿梭其間——這才是真實生產環境！

LSAPC 校正的合規要求

LSAPC（光散射離散式空氣粒子計數儀）是本標準唯一認可的基準測量儀器。其校正要求：

  • 必須依 ISO 21501-4:2007 進行校正
  • 必須具備有效的校正證書
  • 每次測試前須進行零計數檢查（Zero Count Check）

在 FDA 查廠或 EU GMP 審查中，LSAPC 校正記錄是常見的查核項目。

思考題 1

某 CDMO 廠的灌裝室在 at-rest 狀態量測，≥0.5 µm 粒子濃度為 2,800 粒/m³，符合 ISO Class 5 限值（3,520 粒/m³）。但在 operational 狀態下測量為 4,100 粒/m³。請問：

  1. 此灌裝室的 ISO 分級狀況如何？
  2. 若此灌裝室申報為 EU GMP Grade A，是否符合要求？
  3. 若申報為 EU GMP Grade B，其 at-rest 狀態是否達標？

（提示：Grade A 的 operational 狀態 ≥0.5 µm 限值為 3,520 粒/m³；Grade B 的 at-rest 限值也是 3,520 粒/m³）

三種佔用狀態：製藥廠最重要的概念之一

這三種狀態不是學術概念，而是直接關係到驗證測試的執行時機：

EU GMP Annex 1 的特殊規定：Grade A 必須在 at-rest 和 operational 兩種狀態下均符合 ISO Class 5 標準。這是比 ISO 14644-1 更嚴格的要求。

Section 4 — Classification 空氣潔淨度分級

4 Classification

4.1 Occupancy State(s): The air cleanliness class by particle concentration of air in a cleanroom or clean zone shall be defined in one or more of three occupancy states, viz. "as-built," "at-rest" or "operational" (see 3.3).

4.2 Particle Size(s): One, or more than one, threshold (lower limit) particle sizes situated within the range from ≥0.1 µm to ≥5 µm are to be used to determine air cleanliness particle concentration for classification.

4.3 ISO Class Number: Air cleanliness class by particle concentration shall be designated by an ISO Class number, N. The maximum permitted concentration of particles for each considered particle size is determined from Table 1.

Particle number concentrations for different threshold sizes in Table 1 do not reflect actual particle size and number distribution in the air and serve as criteria for classification only.

分級核心概念

分級公式 Classification Formula

Table 1 中的所有數值均來自以下公式（公式 E.1）：

Cn = 10^N × (K/D)^2.08

其中：
Cn = 最大允許濃度（粒子/m³），四捨五入至三位有效數字
N  = ISO 等級編號（1 至 9）
D  = 考慮的粒徑（µm）
K  = 0.1 µm（常數）

驗證範例：ISO Class 5，D = 0.5 µm

Cn = 10^5 × (0.1/0.5)^2.08
   = 100,000 × (0.2)^2.08
   = 100,000 × 0.03520
   = 3,520 粒子/m³  ✓（與 Table 1 吻合）

這個公式的物理意義：粒徑越大的粒子，在空氣中的允許濃度越低（因為大粒子更難被過濾，對產品的污染風險更直接）。

如何正確「指定」一個 ISO Class

依據 4.4 Designation，完整的 ISO 分級指定必須包含三個要素：

  1. ISO Class 編號：例如「ISO Class 5」
  2. 佔用狀態：at-rest、operational 或 as-built
  3. 考慮的粒徑：例如 ≥0.5 µm

完整範例："ISO Class 5; at-rest; ≥0.5 µm"

若同時測量多個粒徑，較大粒徑必須是較小粒徑的至少 1.5 倍：D2 ≥ 1.5 × D1。例如可以選擇 0.3 µm 和 0.5 µm（0.5/0.3 = 1.67，符合），但不能選擇 0.3 µm 和 0.4 µm（0.4/0.3 = 1.33，不符合）。

Section 5 — Demonstration of Compliance 合規性驗證

5 Demonstration of Compliance

5.1 Principle

Compliance with air cleanliness (ISO Class) requirements specified by the customer is verified by performing specified testing procedures and by providing documentation of the results and conditions of testing.

At-rest or operational classification may be performed periodically based upon risk assessment of the application, typically on an annual basis.

For monitoring cleanrooms, clean zones and separative devices, ISO 14644-2:2015 shall be used.

NOTE: Where the installation is equipped with instrumentation for continuous or frequent monitoring, the time intervals between classification may be extended provided that the results of the monitoring remain within the specified limits.

5.2 Testing

The reference test method for demonstrating compliance is given in Annex A (normative). Alternative methods or instrumentation, having at least comparable performance, may be specified. If no alternative is specified or agreed upon, the reference method shall be used.

Tests performed to demonstrate compliance shall be conducted using instruments which are in compliance with calibration requirements at the time of testing.

5.3 Airborne Particle Concentration Evaluation

Upon completion of testing in accordance with Annex A, the concentration of particles (expressed as number of particles per cubic metre) in a single sample volume at each sampling location shall not exceed the concentration limit(s) given in Table 1 or Table E.1 for intermediate decimal classes for the considered size(s).

If multiple single sample volumes are taken at a sampling location, the concentrations shall be averaged and the average concentration must not exceed the concentration limits.

Particle concentrations used for determination of compliance with ISO Classes shall be measured by the same method for all considered particle sizes.

5.4 Test Report

The results from testing each cleanroom or clean zone shall be recorded and submitted as a comprehensive report, along with a statement of compliance or non-compliance with the specified designation of air cleanliness class by particle concentration.

The test report shall include:

  1. the name and address of the testing organization, and the date on which the test was performed,
  2. the number and year of publication of this part of ISO 14644, i.e. ISO 14644-1:2015,
  3. a clear identification of the physical location of the cleanroom or clean zone tested (including reference to adjacent areas if necessary), and specific designations for coordinates of all sampling locations,
  4. the specified designation criteria, including the ISO Class number, the relevant occupancy state(s), and the considered particle size(s),
  5. details of the test method used, with any special conditions, departures from the test method, and identification of the test instrument and its current calibration certificate, and
  6. the test results, including particle concentration data for all sampling locations.

合規驗證解析 Commentary

分級 vs. 監測：兩個不同的活動

很多人容易混淆「分級」和「監測」：

  • 分級（Classification）：依據 ISO 14644-1，通常每年一次，使用 Annex A 規定的參考方法，是正式的「確認潔淨室等級」行為。
  • 監測（Monitoring）：依據 ISO 14644-2，是日常或定期的「維持潔淨度確認」，頻率更高（可以是連續、每班、每批次等），不一定需要 Annex A 的完整程序。

分級是「取得資格」，監測是「維持資格」。兩者都不可缺少。

5.3 評估準則的核心：每個採樣點都必須達標

這是一個非常重要的合規原則：

  • 每個採樣點的（平均）濃度不得超過 Table 1 的限值
  • 若多個樣本在同一採樣點採集，取平均值，但平均值仍不得超標
  • 不得以「所有採樣點的平均值」來判斷合規（必須逐點評估）

換句話說：10 個採樣點中有 1 個超標，整個潔淨室就判定不符合該 ISO Class，即使其他 9 個點的平均濃度遠低於限值。

這與 1999 版使用 95% UCL 的方法完全不同，是 2015 版最實質性的改變之一。

測試報告的六大要素（5.4）

在 FDA 查廠或 EMA GMP 審查中，測試報告是首要審查文件。缺少任何一項都可能導致觀察事項：

  1. 測試機構名稱、地址、日期
  2. 標準依據：明確標明「ISO 14644-1:2015」（年份不能遺漏）
  3. 潔淨室位置及所有採樣點座標圖
  4. 指定等級：ISO Class N + 佔用狀態 + 粒徑
  5. 測試方法、特殊條件、儀器編號及校正證書編號
  6. 所有採樣點的粒子濃度數據

CDMO 實務：年度分級計畫

典型的 CDMO 廠年度分級計畫包含：

  • 時機選擇：通常在年度維護停機後重新啟動時進行，確保在生產排程最少干擾的情況下完成
  • 文件準備：SOP 需預先定義採樣計劃（Sampling Plan），包含採樣點位置圖（Floor Plan with Grid）
  • 雙重驗證：若同一潔淨室同時申報多個客戶的產品，可能需要在不同佔用狀態下分別記錄
  • 偏差管理：任何超標點必須立即啟動偏差調查（Deviation Investigation），並在報告中記錄根本原因和糾正措施

Annex A 解析

Annex A 的地位：為什麼是「Normative」？

ISO 標準的附件分兩種：

  • Normative（規範性）：必須遵守，與正文具有同等法律效力
  • Informative（資訊性）：提供指引，但非強制

Annex A 是「Normative」，意味著除非雙方同意使用替代方法（且替代方法性能不低於 Annex A），否則必須嚴格遵照 Annex A 的程序進行分級測試。在 FDA 和 EMA 的審查中，使用非規範性替代方法必須有充分的科學依據。

95% 信心水準 + 90% 區域符合率：什麼意思？

這是 2015 版的核心統計概念，比 1999 版更嚴謹：

  • 95% 信心水準（Confidence Level）：測試結果的可靠性——如果反覆測試多次，95% 的情況下結論是正確的
  • 90% 區域符合率（Area Compliance）：整個潔淨室區域中，至少 90% 的面積符合等級要求

這個統計模型基於「超幾何分佈」，不假設粒子在空間中均勻分佈——更接近真實情況。

A.5 Sampling Procedure

A.5.1 Set up the particle counter in accordance with the manufacturer's instructions including performing a zero count check.

A.5.2 The sampling probe shall be positioned pointing into the airflow. If the direction of the airflow being sampled is not controlled or predictable (e.g. non-unidirectional airflow), the inlet of the sampling probe shall be directed vertically upward.

A.5.3 Ensure normal conditions for the selected occupancy state are established before sampling.

A.5.4 Sample the volume of air determined in A.4.4, as a minimum, for each sample at each sampling location.

A.5.5 If an out-of-specification count is found at a location due to an identified abnormal occurrence, then that count can be discarded and noted as such on the test report and a new sample taken.

A.5.6 If an out-of-specification count found at a location is attributed to a technical failure of the cleanroom or equipment, then the cause should be identified, remedial action taken and retesting performed of the failed sampling location, the immediate surrounding locations and any other locations affected.

A.6 Processing of Results

A.6.1.1 Average concentration at each sampling location (when ≥2 samples taken):

x̄i = (xi,1 + xi,2 + ... + xi,n) / n

A.6.1.2 Concentration per cubic metre:

Ci = (x̄i / Vt) × 1000

where Vt is the selected single sample volume in litres.

採樣程序重點

零計數檢查（Zero Count Check）：為什麼重要？

在每次採樣前，必須先進行零計數檢查——將儀器抽取經 HEPA 過濾的清潔空氣，確認讀數接近於零。這個步驟驗證儀器本身沒有誤報粒子（如光學系統的電子雜訊或管路殘留）。若零計數檢查失敗，所有後續測量數據均無效。

OOS（Out-of-Specification）的兩種情況處理

情況 A：已識別的異常事件（A.5.5）

  • 例如：採樣期間有人打噴嚏、門被意外打開
  • 可以：捨棄該數據，在報告中記錄，重新採樣
  • 前提：異常原因必須明確記錄，不能「選擇性」捨棄不利數據

情況 B：設備或潔淨室故障（A.5.6）

  • 例如：HVAC 系統異常、HEPA 過濾器破損
  • 必須：識別根本原因、採取糾正措施、重新測試超標點及周邊點
  • 不得：在未採取糾正措施的情況下繼續使用該潔淨室

C_i = (x̄_i / V_t) × 1000

  計數：245 粒（於 28.3 公升中）

B.1 Example 1 — ISO Class 5, 18 m², Two Particle Sizes

B.4 Example 4 — ISO Class 5, FAIL Case (Critical Learning)

Cleanroom: 25 m², ISO Class 5, ≥0.5 µm. NL = 7 minimum, customer agreed to add 3 additional = 10 total. Vs = 5.68 L → 28.3 L per sample.

Conclusion: Location 4 average = 4,165 > 3,520. Cleanroom FAILS ISO Class 5 — even though all other locations pass.

B.5 Example 5 — ISO Class 7.5 (Intermediate Class)

計算範例解析

掌握範例 B.1 的計算流程

Annex B 的範例是考試和實務中最常用到的參考。以 B.1 為例，流程是：

  1. 查 Table A.1：18 m² → NL = 6 個採樣點
  2. 確認限值：ISO Class 5，≥0.5 µm → 3,520 粒/m³
  3. 計算 Vs：(20/3,520) × 1,000 = 5.68 L
  4. 決定實際採樣量：儀器流量 28.3 L/min × 1 min = 28.3 L（≥ Vs 且 ≥ 2 L，符合要求）
  5. 計算濃度：計數 × (1,000/28.3) = 計數 × 35.3
  6. 逐點比較：每個點的濃度不得超過限值

Example 4 的重要教訓：平均值不能掩蓋超標點

Example 4 中，採樣點 4 的平均濃度（4,165 粒/m³）超過了 ISO Class 5 的限值（3,520 粒/m³），儘管其他 9 個點都通過了。結果是整個潔淨室不符合要求。

更值得注意的是採樣點 9：三次採樣分別為 54、159、78 粒，其中第二次 159 × 35.3 = 5,612 粒/m³，明顯超標。但三次的平均值 97 × 35.3 = 3,424 粒/m³，低於限值，所以通過。

結論：判斷以「採樣點的平均值」為準，而不是「每個單次樣品」。這與某些人的直覺相反。

中間等級計算（Example 5）驗算

ISO Class 7.5，D = 0.5 µm

Cn = 10^7.5 × (0.1/0.5)^2.08

Cn = 31,622,777 × 0.03517

Annex C — Macroparticle Descriptor (M Descriptor) 大粒子描述符

Annex C — Counting and Sizing of Airborne Macroparticles

C.1 Principle

In some situations, typically those related to specific process requirements, alternative levels of air cleanliness may be specified on the basis of particle populations that are not within the size range applicable to classification. The maximum permitted concentration of such particles and the choice of test method to verify compliance are matters for agreement between the customer and the supplier.

C.2 M Descriptor Format

The M descriptor is expressed in the format:

"ISO M (a; b); c"

  • a = maximum permitted concentration of macroparticles (macroparticles/m³)
  • b = equivalent diameter (µm) associated with the specified method
  • c = specified measurement method

Example 1: "ISO M (29; ≥5 µm); LSAPC" — 29 particles/m³ at ≥5 µm, measured by LSAPC.

Example 2: "ISO M (2,500; ≥10 µm); time-of-flight aerosol particle counter"

Example 3: "ISO M (1,000; 10 to 20 µm); cascade impactor followed by microscopic sizing and counting"

Note: If the population contains fibres, supplement with "Mfibre (a; b); c".

C.3 Macroparticle Measurement Methods

In situ measurement (C.4.1):

  • LSAPC — reports equivalent optical diameter
  • Time-of-flight apparatus — reports aerodynamic diameter

Collection methods (C.4.2):

  • Filter collection + microscopic measurement
  • Cascade impactor collection + microscopic measurement

C.7 ISO Class 5 and ≥5 µm Particles

In order to express an airborne concentration of 29 particles/m³ in the particle size range ≥5 µm based on the use of an LSAPC, the designation would be "ISO M (29; ≥5 µm); LSAPC" and for 20 particle/m³ the designation would be "ISO M (20; ≥5 µm); LSAPC" (see Table 1, Note f).

M Descriptor 解析

為什麼 ≥5 µm 粒子需要 M Descriptor？

回顧 Table 1 的腳注 e：

"Sample collection limitations for both particles in low concentrations and sizes greater than 1 µm make classification inappropriate, due to potential particle losses in the sampling system."

大粒子（>1 µm，尤其是 >5 µm）在採樣管路中容易因重力、慣性而沉積（撞擊採樣管壁），導致實際到達感測器的粒子數量偏低，測量結果不可靠。

因此，ISO 14644-1 規定 ≥5 µm 的粒子不納入 ISO Class 的正式分級，而是以「M descriptor」另行補充說明。

Annex D — Sequential Sampling Procedure 順序採樣程序

Annex D — Sequential Sampling

D.1 Background and Limitations

Sequential sampling is useful when classifying a clean controlled environment with a very low particle concentration at the class limit. It allows reduction of the sample volume and sampling time by measuring the rate of counting and predicting the likelihood of passing or failing.

Sequential sampling is most appropriate for air cleanliness of ISO Class 4 or cleaner. It may also be used for other classes when the limit for the chosen particle size is low.

Limitations:

  • Only applicable when expected counts from a single sample are <20
  • Each sample measurement requires supplementary monitoring and data analysis
  • Particle concentrations are not determined as precisely as with conventional sampling

D.2 Basis for the Procedure

Reference values are derived from formulae for upper- and lower-limit boundaries:

Upper limit (FAIL): Cfail = 3.96 + 1.03E    (D.1)

Lower limit (PASS): Cpass = −3.96 + 1.03E    (D.2)

where E = expected count = Q × t × Cn,m / 1,000    (D.5)

D.3 Procedure

  1. Record the total number of particles counted as a function of time
  2. Calculate the expected count E (Formula D.5)
  3. Plot the total count versus E (Figure D.1)
  4. Compare with upper and lower limit lines
  5. If cumulative count crosses upper line → FAIL
  6. If cumulative count crosses lower line → PASS
  7. If between the lines → continue sampling until E reaches 20 or count reaches 20

If the total count is 20 or fewer at the end of the prescribed sampling period and has not crossed the upper line, the air is judged to have complied with the class limit.

順序採樣解析

比喻：順序採樣就像「中途計票」

想像你在選舉開票中——如果某候選人在計了 30% 的票後就遠遠領先，你可以有信心預測結果，不需要等到 100% 開完。順序採樣的邏輯相似：

  • 空氣非常潔淨 → 粒子計數增長非常緩慢，很快觸及「通過線」，可以提前停止採樣
  • 空氣明顯污染 → 粒子計數快速增加，很快觸及「失敗線」，可以提前判定不合格
  • 空氣接近臨界值 → 需要繼續採樣直到完整樣本量

順序採樣公式詳解

通過/失敗邊界（縱軸：累積觀測計數 C，橫軸：期望計數 E）：

上限（FAIL）: C_fail = 3.96 + 1.03 × E
下限（PASS）: C_pass = −3.96 + 1.03 × E

期望計數（expected count）:
E = Q × t × C_n,m / 1000

其中：
Q   = 採樣流速（公升/秒）
t   = 採樣時間（秒）
C_n,m = ISO Class 等級限值（粒/m³）

例（D.4.1，ISO Class 3，0.1 µm，1,000 粒/m³）：
Q = 28.3 L/min = 0.472 L/s
t = 5 s → E = 0.472 × 5 × 1000 / 1000 = 2.4

上限：C_fail = 3.96 + 1.03 × 2.4 = 3.96 + 2.47 = 6.43 → 7（上取整）
下限：C_pass = −3.96 + 1.03 × 2.4 = −3.96 + 2.47 = −1.49 → N.A.（負數）

何時應使用順序採樣？

當常規採樣體積過大（採樣時間過長）時，順序採樣特別有用：

對於 ISO Class 3 的 ≥0.5 µm 或更嚴格等級的測量，使用順序採樣可以大幅縮短驗證時間——在實際測試中，如果空氣遠比限值潔淨，可能只需幾分鐘就能判定通過。

Annex E — Intermediate Decimal Cleanliness Classes

E.1 Intermediate Decimal Classes

If intermediate decimal cleanliness classes are required, Table E.1 should be used. Uncertainties associated with particle measurement make increments of less than 0.5 inappropriate.

E.2 Intermediate Particle Sizes

For any intermediate particle size not listed in Table 1, use Formula (E.1):

Cn = 10N × (K/D)2.08

where:

  • Cn = maximum permitted concentration (particles/m³), rounded to 3 significant figures
  • N = ISO Class number (1 to 9)
  • D = considered particle size (µm), NOT listed in Table 1
  • K = constant, 0.1 µm

中間等級解析

何時需要中間等級？

九個整數 ISO Class（1–9）覆蓋了大多數應用場景。但在某些特殊情況下，客戶或法規可能要求介於兩個整數等級之間的清潔度，此時可使用中間等級（Intermediate Classes）。

常見案例：

  • 某些半導體製程需要比 ISO Class 7（352,000 粒/m³）嚴格，但不需要 ISO Class 6（35,200 粒/m³）的條件
  • Annex B Example 5 就以 ISO Class 7.5（限值 1,110,000 粒/m³ at ≥0.5 µm）為示例

注意：增量不得小於 0.5（如 ISO Class 7.3 是不允許的），因為粒子測量的不確定性使更小增量失去意義。

公式 E.1 的完整應用範例

問題：計算 ISO Class 5 在 ≥0.4 µm（非 Table 1 標準粒徑）的限值

Cn = 10^5 × (0.1/0.4)^2.08
   = 100,000 × (0.25)^2.08

(0.25)^2.08：
  = exp(2.08 × ln(0.25))
  = exp(2.08 × (-1.3863))
  = exp(-2.8834)
  = 0.05612

Cn = 100,000 × 0.05612
   = 5,612 粒/m³
   ≈ 5,610 粒/m³（三位有效數字）

注意：D2(0.4)/D1(0.3) = 1.33 < 1.5，因此 0.3 和 0.4 µm
不能同時被選為考慮粒徑（違反 4.4 節的要求）。
但若只考慮 ≥0.4 µm 這單一粒徑，則使用此公式計算是合法的。

中間等級在製藥領域的實際意義

在 EU GMP Annex 1 和 ISO 14644-1 的對應關係中，製藥廠通常只使用 ISO Class 5、7、8 這三個整數等級，中間等級在製藥環境中並不常見。

但理解中間等級的公式原理很重要——它揭示了 Table 1 的所有數值都來自同一個數學公式，理解公式比死記數值更有價值。

Annex F — Test Instruments 測試儀器

Annex F — Test Instruments

F.1 Introduction

This annex describes the measuring apparatus that should be used for the recommended tests given in Annexes A, C and D. Data given in Tables F.1 and F.2 indicate the minimum necessary requirements for each item of apparatus. Measuring apparatus should be chosen subject to agreement between the customer and supplier.

F.2 Instrument Specifications

The following instruments should be used for the recommended tests:

  1. Light scattering (discrete) airborne particle counter (LSAPC) — specifications given in ISO 21501-4:2007
  2. Discrete-macroparticle counter
  3. Time-of-flight particle sizing apparatus
  4. Microscopic measurement of particles collected on filter paper (see ASTM F312-8)

儀器規範解析

LSAPC 的核心標準：ISO 21501-4:2007

LSAPC 的詳細技術規範（感測器靈敏度、計數效率、流量精確度、取樣速率等）在 ISO 21501-4:2007 中規定。ISO 14644-1 直接引用此標準，意味著：

  • 儀器必須符合 ISO 21501-4 的所有技術要求
  • 校正必須依據 ISO 21501-4 的方法進行
  • 校正憑證必須明確說明依據此標準

在製藥廠的 GMP 審查中，查核員常會要求查看 LSAPC 的校正憑證，確認其包含 ISO 21501-4 的相關測試項目。

為什麼 Annex F 是「Informative」而非「Normative」？

儀器技術進步很快——今天最先進的 LSAPC 可能比 2015 年的規範更好。若將 Annex F 設為 Normative，可能限制使用性能更佳的新儀器。

因此 Annex F 只提供「最低要求參考」，允許使用者選擇性能更優的儀器（須經客戶/供應商同意），這體現了 ISO 標準靈活性的立法思路。

CDMO 儀器管理實務

典型 CDMO 廠對 LSAPC 的管理要點：

  • 校正頻率：通常每 12 個月一次，依 ISO 21501-4 要求
  • 使用前確認：每次分級測試前進行零計數檢查（Zero Count Check）
  • 流量驗證：定期驗證儀器實際流量符合規格（如 28.3 L/min ± 5%）
  • 管路長度限制：採樣管路越長，大粒子損失越多；一般 >1 µm 的粒子採樣管路不超過廠商建議長度（通常 ≤ 1 m）
  • 校正追溯性：校正必須追溯至國家計量標準（NIST 或等效）

本節重點回顧 Section Summary

  • ISO 14644-1:2015 核心：以微粒濃度為基準，定義 ISO Class 1–9 九個等級，使用 LSAPC 測量 0.1–5 µm 範圍的粒子，>5 µm 粒子用 M descriptor 補充。
  • 分級公式：Cn = 10N × (0.1/D)2.08，所有 Table 1 數值均來自此公式；每升一個等級，限值約乘以 10。
  • 三種佔用狀態：as-built（無人無設備）→ at-rest（有設備無人）→ operational（有人有設備生產中）；製藥廠驗證必須分別記錄相應狀態。
  • EU GMP 對應關係：Grade A = ISO Class 5（at-rest & operational）；Grade B = ISO Class 5（at-rest），ISO Class 7（operational）；Grade C = ISO Class 7（at-rest），ISO Class 8（operational）；Grade D = ISO Class 8（at-rest）。
  • 採樣設計（Annex A）：採樣點數從 Table A.1 確定；Vs = (20/Cn,m) × 1,000 L；每點最少採樣 2 L 或 1 分鐘，取較大值；每個採樣點的平均濃度均不得超標。
  • M Descriptor：格式為 "ISO M (a; b); c"，用於 >5 µm 大粒子；ISO Class 5 搭配 EU GMP Grade A/B 的 ≥5 µm 限值（20 或 29 粒/m³）。
  • 順序採樣（Annex D）：適用於 ISO Class 4 或更嚴格的低濃度環境；利用計數速率即時預測通過/失敗，節省採樣時間；邊界公式：Cfail = 3.96 + 1.03E，Cpass = −3.96 + 1.03E。
  • 2015 版 vs. 1999 版關鍵差異：統計模型從假設常態分佈改為超幾何採樣模型；信心水準明確為 95% × 90%；取消 95% UCL；採樣點數量更新；M descriptor 正式納入標準。
