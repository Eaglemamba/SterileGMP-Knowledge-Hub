Foreword — 前言

ISO 15378:2017 is the fourth edition, prepared by ISO/TC 76 (Transfusion, infusion and injection, and blood processing equipment for medical and pharmaceutical use). It cancels and replaces ISO 15378:2015 (third edition).

Key technical changes from the 2015 edition:

  • Integration of sector-specific GMP requirements directly into the ISO 9001:2015 framework
  • Deletion of the requirement for a quality manual
  • Inclusion of all annexes from ISO 9001:2015
  • Terminology aligned with ISO 9000:2015
  • Addition of an alphabetical index of defined terms

The document uses a visual convention: clauses quoted directly and unchanged from ISO 9001:2015 appear in boxes. GMP-specific additions appear outside boxes.

前言解析

第四版的重大意義：2017 版將 GMP 要求完全整合進 ISO 9001:2015 架構，不再是附加文件，而是真正的「雙軌標準」。

排版識別規則 — 如何閱讀這份標準

框框內的文字：直接引自 ISO 9001:2015 的原文，未修改。

框框外的文字：ISO 15378 新增的 GMP 特定要求或建議，這是初級包裝材料廠商需要額外滿足的部分。

讀標準時，先看框框外的文字，因為那才是 ISO 15378「超出 ISO 9001」的部分，也是供應商稽核時要特別檢查的重點。

刪除「品質手冊」要求

2015 版以前要求企業要有一份品質手冊 (quality manual)。2017 版配合 ISO 9001:2015，不再強制要求品質手冊的形式，但品質管理系統的文件化要求仍然存在，只是形式更彈性。

Introduction 0.1 — General 總則

This document identifies Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP) principles and specifies requirements for a quality management system applicable to primary packaging materials for medicinal products.

The realization of GMP principles in production and control of primary packaging materials is of great importance for patient safety, because of their direct product contact. The application of GMP for pharmaceutical packaging materials helps ensure that these materials meet the needs and requirements of the pharmaceutical industry.

"This document is an application standard for primary packaging materials, which contains the text of ISO 9001:2015."

Verbal forms used:

  • "shall" — a requirement (強制要求)
  • "should" — a recommendation (建議)
  • "may" — a permission (許可)
  • "can" — a possibility or capability (可能性)

The phrase "if appropriate" appears throughout the document. When a requirement is qualified by this phrase, it is deemed appropriate unless the organization can document a justification otherwise.

0.1 解析 — 標準的本質

ISO 15378 = ISO 9001 + GMP 的雙軌架構

可以把 ISO 15378 想成一棟建築：

  • 地基 = ISO 9001:2015（品質管理系統通用框架）
  • 上層建築 = GMP 特定要求（為藥品包裝材料量身訂製）

供應商若取得 ISO 15378 認證，代表他們的 QMS 同時滿足兩套要求，達到製藥等級的品質管理水準。

CDMO 稽核實務應用

當你在做橡膠塞或玻璃瓶供應商的資格審查 (supplier qualification) 時：

情境 A：供應商持有 ISO 15378 認證 → 可以縮短稽核範圍，重點確認認證有效性和近期監察報告，不必從零審查 QMS 框架。

情境 B：供應商只有 ISO 9001 認證 → 需要額外稽核 GMP 相關要求（生產控制、批次紀錄、留樣等），工作量大幅增加。

ISO 15378 認證就是供應商的「GMP 資格預審證明」。

"if appropriate" 的法律意涵

這個詞組很重要：ISO 15378 預設所有標有 "if appropriate" 的要求都是適用的，除非企業能提出書面理由說明為何不適用。這意味著「不適用」的舉證責任在企業身上，不能輕易略過。

Introduction 0.2 — Quality Management Principles 品質管理原則

ISO 9001:2015 is based on seven quality management principles described in ISO 9000. These principles form the philosophical foundation of the standard:

  1. Customer focus — 以顧客為核心
  2. Leadership — 領導力
  3. Engagement of people — 人員參與
  4. Process approach — 流程方法
  5. Improvement — 持續改進
  6. Evidence-based decision making — 基於證據的決策
  7. Relationship management — 關係管理

A key objective of this document is to specify GMP for primary packaging materials.

0.2 解析 — 七大原則在包裝材料廠的體現

Introduction 0.3 — Process Approach 流程方法

0.3.1 General

This standard promotes the adoption of a process approach — understanding and managing interrelated processes as a system. This enables the organization to control interrelationships and interdependencies among processes, enhancing overall performance.

The application of the process approach enables:

  • Understanding and consistency in meeting requirements
  • Consideration of processes in terms of added value
  • Achievement of effective process performance
  • Improvement of processes based on evaluation of data and information

0.3.2 Plan-Do-Check-Act (PDCA) Cycle

The PDCA cycle can be applied to all processes and to the QMS as a whole:

  • Plan: Establish objectives and processes, identify risks/opportunities
  • Do: Implement what was planned
  • Check: Monitor and measure processes against policies and objectives
  • Act: Take actions to improve performance

0.3.3 Risk-Based Thinking

Risk-based thinking is essential for achieving an effective QMS. It enables an organization to determine factors that could cause deviation from planned results and put in place preventive controls.

"Because of the nature of primary packaging materials the risk-based approach is applied throughout all processes of the organization."

0.3 解析 — PDCA 與風險思維

PDCA 在玻璃瓶製造的實際循環

  • Plan：設定每批玻璃瓶的尺寸規格 (dimensional spec) 和微粒標準
  • Do：按 SOP 執行生產，進行線上控制 (IPC)
  • Check：最終檢驗 (final inspection)，比對規格，統計製程能力 (Cpk)
  • Act：發現不合格趨勢，調整成型參數或設備，更新 SOP

風險思維的具體化

ISO 15378 特別強調：由於初級包裝材料直接接觸藥品，風險思維必須貫穿所有流程，不只是設計或驗證階段。

舉例：橡膠塞供應商必須評估矽化處理 (siliconization) 對相容性的風險；鋁蓋廠商必須評估壓合力 (crimp force) 對封閉完整性的影響。

這就是為什麼 3.11 特別設有「風險管理相關術語」這個子章節。

CDMO 稽核查核點

  1. 請供應商展示其 PDCA 改進紀錄（CAPA 清單 + 完成率）
  2. 查看是否有書面的風險評估 (risk assessment) 報告，涵蓋關鍵製程步驟
  3. 確認風險評估工具是否適當（FMEA、FTA 或 HACCP 均可接受）

Introduction 0.4 — Relationship with Other Standards 與其他標準的關係

This document incorporates the requirements of ISO 9001:2015 and, additionally, particular requirements for primary packaging materials derived and adapted from GMP for the production and control of medicinal products.

ISO 15378 relates to other ISO standards as follows:

  • ISO 9000:2015 — provides essential background for understanding ISO 9001
  • ISO 9004 — provides guidance for organizations progressing beyond ISO 9001 requirements

This standard does not include requirements specific to environmental management, occupational health and safety, or financial management. It is a sector-specific QMS standard for primary packaging materials manufacturers.

0.4 解析 — 標準生態系統定位

Section 1 — Scope 適用範圍

ISO 9001:2015 Scope (incorporated)

This International Standard specifies requirements for a quality management system when an organization:

  • (a) needs to demonstrate its ability to consistently provide products and services that meet customer and applicable statutory and regulatory requirements, and
  • (b) aims to enhance customer satisfaction through the effective application of the system, including processes for improvement and assurance of conformity to requirements.

All requirements are generic and intended to be applicable to any organization, regardless of type, size, or the products and services it provides.

ISO 15378 Additional Scope

In addition to ISO 9001, this document specifies GMP requirements applicable to primary packaging materials for a quality management system where an organization needs to demonstrate its ability to provide primary packaging materials for medicinal products, which consistently meet:

  • Customer requirements
  • Regulatory requirements
  • International Standards

This document is an application standard for the design, manufacture and supply of primary packaging materials for medicinal products.

Section 1 解析 — 誰需要遵循？

適用對象：初級包裝材料製造商

ISO 15378 適用於設計、製造和供應初級包裝材料的組織。以下是常見的初級包裝材料：

  • 玻璃小瓶 (glass vials)、安瓶 (ampoules)
  • 橡膠塞 (rubber stoppers / closures)
  • 鋁蓋 / 壓蓋 (aluminum crimp caps / seals)
  • 塑料容器 (HDPE/PP/PET bottles)
  • 預填充注射器部件 (prefilled syringe components)：筒體 (barrel)、活塞塞 (plunger stopper)、針頭護罩 (needle shield)、端蓋 (tip cap)
  • 泡殼包裝材料 (blister foils)：鋁箔 (aluminum foil)、PVC/PVDC 底材
  • IV 袋 (intravenous bags)

不適用範圍

  • 次級包裝（紙箱、說明書、標籤）→ 不直接接觸藥品
  • 合約實驗室（只做檢測，不製造包裝材料）
  • 經銷商（只轉售，不涉及設計或製造）
  • 藥品製造商本身（應遵循 EU GMP、ICH Q10 等）

CDMO QA 實務：供應商稽核的起點

  1. 確認供應商是否持有有效的 ISO 15378 認證書（Certificate of Registration）
  2. 核對認證範圍是否涵蓋你採購的具體產品類別（例如：橡膠塞 vs 玻璃瓶是不同範疇）
  3. 確認認證機構 (CB) 是否為 IAF 認可的 accredited body
  4. 查詢認證到期日及最近一次監督稽核 (surveillance audit) 的結果

Section 2 解析 — 規範性引用的意義

規範性 vs 資訊性引用

規範性引用 (normative reference)：引用文件構成 ISO 15378 的一部分，必須遵守。

資訊性引用 (informative reference)：提供補充背景，不強制要求。

ISO 14698-1 和 -2 被列為規範性引用，代表潔淨室生物污染控制方法是 ISO 15378 的強制要求組成部分。

為何 ISO 14698 被納入？

初級包裝材料（特別是橡膠塞和注射器部件）的生產環境需要控制微生物污染。ISO 14698 定義了生物污染控制的系統方法，包括環境監控 (EM) 程序、樣品採集、數據解釋。

這意味著 ISO 15378 認證工廠的潔淨室生物污染控制必須符合 ISO 14698 的方法論，而不是任意設計的 EM 程序。

Section 3 概覽 — 為什麼術語定義很重要

術語統一的實務意義

在供應商協議 (Quality Agreement, Technical Agreement) 中，雙方必須使用一致的術語，否則容易產生歧義和糾紛。ISO 15378 的術語定義提供了客觀的法律依據。

例如："batch"（批次）和 "lot"（批）在此標準中是同義詞，但如果藥廠和供應商對此有不同理解，可能導致可追溯性記錄不完整。

ISO 15378 新增術語 vs ISO 9000:2015 原有術語

ISO 15378 只重複定義 ISO 9000:2015 中已修改的術語（例如 organization、validation）。其他未列出的術語，直接引用 ISO 9000:2015 的原始定義。

因此閱讀 ISO 15378 Section 3 時，應同時參照 ISO 9000:2015。

3.1 Terms Related to Organization — 與組織相關的術語

3.1.1 organization

Person or group of people that has its own functions with responsibilities, authorities and relationships to achieve its objectives.

Note 2 (ISO 15378 specific): In this document the organization is the company manufacturing the primary packaging material (3.6.4).

[Modified from ISO 9000:2015, 3.2.1]

3.1.2 quality unit

Organizational unit which fulfils both quality assurance (QA) and quality control (QC) responsibilities.

Note: The quality unit(s) can consist of separate QA and QC units or of a single individual (or group), depending upon the size and structure of the organization.

3.1 解析

organization — 在本標準中的特定含義

ISO 9001 的「組織」泛指任何類型的公司；ISO 15378 特別說明，在本標準語境中，組織是指製造初級包裝材料的公司。這個澄清很重要，確保標準的適用主體不被誤解為藥品製造商或其他角色。

quality unit — GMP 的核心組織架構

QU（品質單位）是 GMP 環境中的核心概念，相當於藥廠的「QA 部門」，但在小型包裝材料廠可能是一個人兼任。

關鍵職能：

  • 批次放行 (batch release) 決定
  • 偏差 (deviation) 調查和核准
  • 供應商稽核
  • 文件控制和 SOP 審核

稽核時要確認 QU 在組織架構中的獨立性，不能直接向生產部門報告。

3.2 Terms Related to Activity — 與活動相關的術語

3.2.1 assembly

Fitting together of primary packaging materials and/or components.

Example: Pipette assemblies for filling, prepared components of injection systems, positioning of needle shields on prefillable syringes.

3.2.2 change control

Documented control of changes with an appropriate risk management.

Note: Changes can include changes in raw materials, specifications, facilities, equipment, production processes and test methods.

3.2.3 Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP)

Manufacturing, quality control and quality assurance applied in quality control.

Note 3: GMP for primary packaging materials requires, in addition to suitable provision of personnel, premises and equipment, a QMS that includes controls for incoming starting materials, manufacture, documentation, factory hygiene, final inspection, distribution records, processing of complaints and self-inspection.

Note 4: GMP and cGMP are equivalent.

3.2.4 installational qualification (IQ)

Process of obtaining and documenting evidence that equipment has been provided and installed in accordance with its specification.

[SOURCE: ISO/TS 11139:2006, 2.22]

3.2.5 operational qualification (OQ)

Process of obtaining and documenting evidence that installed equipment operates within predetermined limits when used in accordance with its operational procedures.

[SOURCE: ISO/TS 11139:2006, 2.27]

3.2.6 origination / artwork

All preparative activities prior to print.

Note: These include concept, design, graphics, reprographics, film, plate making, silk screens and digital files and masters.

3.2.7 performance qualification (PQ)

Verification that the proposed specification for the facility, equipment or system is suitable for the intended use.

[SOURCE: ISO/TS 11139:2006, 2.30]

3.2.8 qualification

Process to demonstrate the ability to fulfil specified requirements.

Note 2: Qualification comprises design qualification (DQ), IQ (3.2.4), OQ (3.2.5) and could include PQ (3.2.7) as well as re-qualification.

Note 3: Qualification can be applied to facilities, equipment and utilities.

3.2.9 quality control

Part of quality management focused on fulfilling quality requirements.

Note: Quality control includes checking or testing that specifications are met.

[Modified from ISO 9000:2015, 3.3.7]

3.2 解析 — 活動術語

change control — 變更控制的 GMP 意義

變更控制是 GMP 最核心的管理工具之一。ISO 15378 特別強調變更控制必須「文件化」，且需進行適當的風險管理。

為何如此重要？初級包裝材料的任何變更（哪怕只是原料供應商改變）都可能影響藥品的安全性和穩定性。藥廠可能需要重新進行相容性測試 (compatibility testing)，甚至向監管機構申報。

從 CDMO QA 角度：供應商必須在變更前及時通知你，並提供充分的技術資料，讓你評估是否需要重新驗證 (revalidation)。

qualification vs validation — 常見混淆澄清

Qualification（確效/鑑定）：用於設備、設施、公用系統（utility）——證明它們能滿足規格要求。

Validation（驗證）：用於製造流程、清潔程序、分析方法——證明流程能一致地產出預期結果。

簡單說：確效你的洗瓶機 (IQ/OQ/PQ)，驗證洗瓶製程（清潔驗證）。

IQ / OQ / PQ — 確效三部曲

這是 GMP 環境中設備確效的標準步驟：

ISO 15378 在 3.2.7 特別說明：PQ 是一種 verification（確認），即提供客觀證據證明規格的適用性。

3.3 Terms Related to System — 與系統相關的術語

3.3.1 airlock

Enclosed space to control air-flow.

Note: Typically has at least two interlocked doors between two or more rooms, used either by people or for goods, to control different conditions (cleanliness, air-flow).

3.3.2 calibration

Process of checking or adjusting (by comparison with a reference standard) the accuracy of a measuring instrument.

Note: Calibration establishes the relationship between values indicated by a measuring instrument and corresponding known values of a reference standard.

3.3.3 cleanroom

Room within which the number concentration of airborne particles is controlled and classified, and which is designed, constructed and operated in a manner to control the introduction, generation and retention of particles inside the room.

[SOURCE: ISO 14644-1:2015, 3.1.1]

3.3.4 clean zone

Defined space within which the number concentration of airborne particles is controlled and classified, and which is constructed and operated in a manner to control the introduction, generation and retention of contaminants inside the space.

Note 3: A clean zone can be a defined space within a cleanroom or achieved by a separative device (isolator, RABS).

[SOURCE: ISO 14644-1:2015, 3.1.2]

3.3.5 controlled area / controlled environment

Area or environment constructed and operated to control the possible introduction of potential contaminants.

3.3.6 working area

Defined area where origination, production, packaging, test or inspection operations are carried out and where such activity will usually be subject to a line clearance.

3.3 解析 — 系統術語

airlock — 氣閘的功能

氣閘是防止污染跨越潔淨等級的關鍵設施。ISO 15378 定義氣閘的核心功能是「控制氣流」，而非僅是一個過渡空間。

在初級包裝材料廠，氣閘用於：

  • 人員進入潔淨區前的更衣/消毒
  • 物料從非潔淨區進入潔淨區的傳遞
  • 防止不同潔淨等級之間的壓差失控

cleanroom vs clean zone — 兩者的區別

Cleanroom（潔淨室）：一個完整的房間，結構設計本身就為了控制微粒。

Clean zone（潔淨區）：潔淨室內的特定空間，或由分離裝置（如隔離器 isolator、RABS）形成的區域。

例如：整個灌裝室是潔淨室（ISO Class 8），其中的 RABS 填充區是潔淨區（ISO Class 5）。

注意：ISO 15378 引用 ISO 14644-1 的定義，確保術語與潔淨室標準一致。

calibration — 校準與 GMP 的連結

在 GMP 環境中，所有用於產品放行和製程控制的量測儀器都必須定期校準。ISO 15378 把校準定義為與參考標準比對的過程，強調可追溯性 (traceability to national/international standards)。

稽核時要確認：校準計畫是否完整？是否有逾期未校準的儀器？校準結果是否在接受標準內？

3.4 Terms Related to Requirement — 與要求相關的術語

3.4.1 expiration date

Expected suitable use limit; shelf-life.

Note 2: This is typically the period during which a primary packaging material is expected to remain suitable for use if stored under defined conditions, and after which it should not be used.

3.4.2 shelf-life

Period during which a primary packaging material is expected to comply with the requirements (specifications).

3.4 解析

expiration date vs shelf-life — 初級包裝材料的效期

藥品有效期大家都熟悉，但初級包裝材料本身也有效期，這是很多人忽略的重點。

以橡膠塞為例：矽化 (siliconization) 效果可能隨時間降低，或溶出物 (extractables) 可能在長期存放後超標。因此供應商必須定義：

  • 在何種儲存條件下（溫度、濕度、光照）
  • 材料可以保持規格符合性多久（shelf-life）
  • 逾期後不得使用（expiration date）

CDMO 的收料程序 (receiving procedure) 必須核查包裝材料的效期，避免使用過期材料。

3.5 Terms Related to Process — 與過程相關的術語

3.5.1 batch / lot

Defined quantity of primary packaging material manufactured in one process or series of processes intended to have uniform characteristics with consistent, homogeneous quality.

Note 1: A batch can be divided into sub-batches that are later combined to form a single consistent batch.

Note 2: In continuous production, the batch is a fraction of production defined as a fixed quantity or fixed time interval.

3.5.2 contamination

Introduction of any unwanted material into the primary packaging material.

Note 1: A finished product can be contaminated by physical (particulate), chemical or biological (bioburden and endotoxin) action.

3.5.3 cross-contamination

Contamination of a material or of a product with another material or product.

Note 1: Cross-contamination can also be referred to as mix-up or admixture.

3.5.4 line clearance

Removal (line purge) of everything associated with the prior production run.

Note: Line clearance is done prior to a production run to prevent any error and cross-contamination. The production facility (line) and its associated working area must be completely clear of all materials, waste, products, samples, documents from the previous run.

3.5.5 manufacturing

All operations including purchasing and receipt of materials to production, packaging, labelling, quality control, release, storage, distribution of products and the related controls.

3.5.6 process aids

Material used to facilitate process realization. The material is not included in the product specification and can be removed at or before the final processing stage.

Example: Mould release agents, compressed air, rolling lubricants.

3.5.7 production

Processes resulting in primary packaging material, from receipt of starting materials through processing and packaging, to completion as a finished product.

3.5.8 quarantine

Status of materials or products isolated pending a decision on their subsequent approval or rejection.

Note: Quarantined material is typically isolated by physical or other effective means.

3.5.9 realization

Result of applying all processes required to achieve the desired output from design to product delivery.

3.5.10 reconditioning

Processing or reprocessing primary packaging material to meet specification requirements.

3.5.11 reprocessing

Repeating part of a production process.

Note: Continuation of part of a process after an in-process control test has shown that the part is incomplete is considered normal process, not reprocessing.

3.5.12 retained samples

Materials or finished products stored for future reference.

Note: Samples are taken in a sufficient amount and stored under recommended conditions for reference during a defined period of time.

3.5.13 starting material

Raw material, components and substances used to produce primary packaging materials.

3.5.14 surface treatment

Process to improve primary packaging material surface.

Example: Siliconization or other treatment of internal glass surfaces, coating of internal or external surfaces of glass containers or rubber parts.

3.5 解析 — 過程術語

batch/lot — 批次的核心地位

批次是 GMP 可追溯性的基本單位。所有的品質紀錄、測試結果、放行決定都與特定批次掛鉤。

ISO 15378 的定義強調兩個關鍵特性：

  • 均一性 (uniform characteristics)：同一批次的所有產品應具有一致的品質屬性
  • 均質性 (homogeneous quality)：批次內不允許有系統性的品質差異

批次定義對於抽樣計畫 (sampling plan) 的設計至關重要——必須清楚知道批次的邊界在哪裡。

contamination 的三種形式

ISO 15378 特別列出三種污染類型，這對於包裝材料廠特別重要：

  • 物理污染 (physical/particulate)：玻璃碎片、金屬微粒、纖維
  • 化學污染 (chemical)：溶出物 (leachables)、殘留清潔劑、塑化劑
  • 生物污染 (biological)：微生物 (bioburden)、內毒素 (endotoxin)

這三類污染都可能危害藥品安全，因此包裝材料廠必須針對每一類建立控制措施。

reprocessing vs reconditioning — 重要區別

Reprocessing（再加工）：重複部分生產步驟。例如橡膠塞第一次矽化效果不符標準，重新矽化。需要有書面的核准程序。

Reconditioning（重新整備）：對初級包裝材料進行加工或再加工以達到規格要求。概念更廣，包含外部重新處理。

兩者都需要品質單位的評估和核准，並在批次紀錄中記錄。

surface treatment — 表面處理對相容性的影響

表面處理是初級包裝材料特有的概念。以矽化 (siliconization) 為例：

  • 目的：減少橡膠塞與注射器筒之間的摩擦，確保順暢滑動
  • 風險：矽油可能遷移到藥品中 (leaching)，影響蛋白質藥物 (biologics) 的穩定性
  • GMP 要求：矽化程度必須在規格範圍內，並有驗證數據支持

藥廠在進行 extractables & leachables (E&L) 研究時，必須了解所使用包裝材料的表面處理情況。

quarantine — 隔離狀態的實務管理

隔離狀態是 GMP 物料管理的關鍵概念。物料從收料到放行前，必須處於隔離狀態，防止未經放行的物料被誤用。

ISO 15378 說明隔離可以是「物理隔離」（獨立區域）或「其他有效方式」（如電腦系統中的隔離標記）。現代倉庫管理系統 (WMS) 通常以電子方式實現隔離管理，但必須有適當的系統驗證 (IT system validation)。

3.6 Terms Related to Results — 與結果相關的術語

3.6.1 finished product

Primary packaging material which has completed all stages of production.

3.6.2 intermediate product

Primary packaging material which has completed some but not all production stages.

Note: An intermediate product needs further processing before it becomes a finished product.

3.6.3 medicinal product

Substance or combination of substances presented for treating or preventing disease in human beings or animals.

Note 3: Medicinal products can also be referred to as pharmaceutical or drug products, including clinical trial products.

3.6.4 primary packaging material

Material used in pharmaceutical packaging which will contain, seal or be used for dose application of a medicinal product and which will have direct contact with the medicinal product.

Secondary packaging examples (NOT primary): Printed or unprinted cartons, labels, leaflets or inserts (or outserts), over-wraps, and transit containers such as folding boxes.

3.6.5 secondary packaging materials

Non-contact packaging materials.

Example: Printed or unprinted cartons, labels, leaflets, inserts, over-wraps, transit containers (folding boxes).

3.6 解析 — 結果術語

primary packaging material — 標準的核心概念

這是整份標準最重要的定義。直接接觸藥品是初級包裝材料的決定性特徵。

典型的初級包裝材料：

  • 玻璃容器：小瓶 (vials)、安瓶 (ampoules)、卡匣 (cartridges)
  • 橡膠製品：瓶塞 (stoppers)、活塞 (plungers)、O 型環
  • 金屬製品：鋁壓蓋 (aluminum seals/crimp caps)
  • 塑料容器：HDPE/PP 藥瓶、PET 容器、IV 袋
  • 泡殼材料：PVC/PVDC 底材、鋁蓋箔 (lidding foil)
  • 注射器組件：筒體、活塞塞、針頭護罩

次級包裝材料不在 ISO 15378 範圍內

紙箱、標籤、說明書等不直接接觸藥品的包裝材料，不屬於 ISO 15378 的管轄範疇。這些材料的供應商只需符合 ISO 9001 或其他適用標準。

但注意：印有藥品資訊的標籤雖然是次級包裝，但因為攸關藥品安全資訊，仍受 EU GMP Annex C (Annex 15378 Annex C) 和 EU GMP Part II 的相關規定約束。

intermediate product — 在過程中的品質控制

以橡膠塞製造為例：

  • 混煉後的橡膠 → starting material
  • 成型後、未矽化的橡膠塞 → intermediate product
  • 矽化、清洗、包裝後的橡膠塞 → finished product

每個階段都需要有 in-process controls 和適當的隔離管理。

3.7 Terms Related to Data, Information and Document — 與資料、資訊及文件相關的術語

3.7.1 approved

Confirmed conformity status for any stage of the process [starting materials, process aids, packaging material or finished product].

3.7.2 batch document / batch record

Documents and records that provide a history of the batch, including information relating to its production and control, and which facilitate its traceability.

3.7.3 batch number / lot number

Unique identifier to identify a batch or lot. Can be a combination of numbers, letters and/or symbols; from this the production and distribution history can be determined.

3.7.4 date of manufacture

Date on which one of the first stages in the process of manufacture of the primary packaging material, or the packaging, or the final release, occurs. Can be subject to customer agreement.

3.7.5 deviation

Departure from an approved standard operating procedure (SOP) or established standard.

3.7.6 documented procedure

Procedure that is established, documented, authorized, implemented and maintained.

Note: Documentation can be in any form or type of medium. See SOP (3.7.10).

3.7.7 double-check

Documented verification of an activity, result or record by a second person or system.

Note: Typically, double-checks are signed by a second person. Includes in-process control check signatures and quality records signed by a second person.

3.7.8 out of specification (OOS)

Test results that do not comply with the specification.

3.7.9 rejected

Status of starting materials, quality (process aids), intermediate products or finished products whose test results do not comply with one or more specification requirements, and which have been deemed — usually by the quality unit — as not suitable for use.

3.7.10 standard operating procedure (SOP)

Authorized documented procedure, or set of procedures, work instructions and test instructions.

3.7.11 user requirement specification (URS)

Approved document that states the product specifications of the material produced on this equipment as well as functional, operational and/or technical aspects of the equipment or process required to produce the desired product.

3.7.12 validation

Confirmation, through the provision of objective evidence, that the requirements for a specific intended use or application have been fulfilled.

Note 4 (ISO 15378 specific): Validation can be applied to processes, products and software.

[Modified from ISO 9000:2015, 3.8.13]

3.7.13 verification

Confirmation, through the provision of objective evidence, that specified requirements have been fulfilled.

Note 4 (ISO 15378 specific): In this document the term verification is used to ensure manufacturing systems are properly installed and operating correctly; alternatively this could be done by IQ and OQ.

[Modified from ISO 9000:2015, 3.8.12]

3.7 解析 — 文件術語

batch record — GMP 的核心文件

批次紀錄是 GMP 可追溯性的根本。ISO 15378 定義批次紀錄必須：

  • 提供批次的完整歷史（生產 + 控制）
  • 能夠追溯 (facilitate traceability)

一份完整的批次紀錄應包含：原料資訊、設備編號、操作步驟、IPC 結果、偏差記錄、最終放行簽核。

CDMO 在評估包裝材料供應商時，應要求查閱批次紀錄的樣本，確認記錄完整性和清晰度。

deviation — 偏差管理的法規要求

偏差的定義是「偏離核准的 SOP 或建立的標準」。這意味著即使最終產品符合規格，偏差本身就需要調查和記錄。

GMP 偏差管理的步驟：

  1. 發現偏差 → 立即紀錄
  2. 隔離相關批次（如需要）
  3. 根本原因分析 (RCA)
  4. 評估對產品品質的影響
  5. 制定 CAPA
  6. 品質單位核准後才能放行

OOS — 超出規格結果的處理

OOS 定義清晰：測試結果不符合規格。這觸發正式的 OOS 調查程序，包括：

  • Phase 1：實驗室調查（排除分析誤差）
  • Phase 2：製造調查（是否有製程問題）
  • 最終決定：重新測試（如有合理理由）或拒收/報廢

ISO 15378 的 OOS 管理框架直接對應 FDA OOS 指引（21 CFR context）和 EU GMP Chapter 6 的要求。

URS — 設備採購的起點文件

URS（使用者需求規格）是確效生命週期的第一步。它定義了：

  • 設備需要生產什麼產品
  • 功能性要求（產能、速度）
  • 操作要求（環境條件、清潔方式）
  • 技術要求（材質、電氣規格）

URS 必須在採購前核准，並作為後續 IQ/OQ/PQ 的基準文件。

validation vs verification — 最常見的混淆

ISO 15378 在 3.7.13 特別補充：verification 也可以透過 IQ 和 OQ 來執行，說明這兩個概念在 GMP 設備確效框架中的連結。

3.8 Terms Related to Action — 與行動相關的術語

3.8.1 batch release

Decision to release the batch for sale or supply, following a formal review of the batch document performed by the quality unit or a person authorized by the quality unit.

3.8.2 rejection

Process whereby starting materials, process aids, intermediate products or finished products which have been deemed — usually by the quality unit — as not suitable for use.

3.8.3 return

Process for sending back primary packaging material(s) to the organization.

3.8.4 rework

Action on a nonconforming product or service to make it conform to the requirements.

Note 1: Rework can affect or change parts of the nonconforming product.

Note 2 (ISO 15378 specific): Sorting can be considered to be rework.

[Modified from ISO 9000:2015, 3.12.8]

3.8 解析 — 行動術語

batch release — 批次放行的決策架構

批次放行是品質系統中最重要的決策節點。ISO 15378 明確要求：

  • 必須進行正式審查（不是非正式的口頭確認）
  • 必須審查批次紀錄（不只是測試結果）
  • 必須由品質單位或其授權人員執行（確保獨立性）

從藥廠 CDMO 的角度：你收到的包裝材料 CoA（合格證書）背後，代表供應商的品質單位已審核並簽核了所有批次紀錄。稽核時應查閱放行 SOP 和授權人員清單。

rework — 返工的特殊規定

ISO 15378 特別補充：分選 (sorting) 可以視為返工。這在初級包裝材料廠很常見：

例如：一批玻璃瓶中發現部分有氣泡缺陷，需要逐瓶目視檢查並剔除不良品。這個分選過程按照 ISO 15378 的定義屬於 rework，必須有核准的 SOP 和記錄。

任何返工都需要品質單位評估對產品品質的影響，並決定是否需要重新測試。

3.9 Terms Related to Characteristic — 與特性相關的術語

3.9.1 homogeneity

Uniformity of characteristics and their values throughout a defined quantity of material.

Note: Homogeneity can include uniformity of materials or certain characteristics of materials of special significance.

3.9.2 sterile

Free from viable microorganisms.

[SOURCE: ISO/TS 11139:2006, 2.43]

3.9 解析

homogeneity — 與 batch 定義的連結

均質性是批次定義的核心特徵。橡膠配方的均質性確保批次內所有橡膠塞的機械性能（硬度、彈性、穿刺力）一致；玻璃成分的均質性確保熱膨脹係數一致，防止熱應力破裂。

sterile — 初級包裝材料的無菌要求

不是所有初級包裝材料都需要無菌狀態，但用於無菌製劑的材料通常需要。ISO 15378 採用 ISO/TS 11139 的定義：無菌 = 不含存活微生物。

橡膠塞和玻璃瓶通常由藥廠在使用前進行最終滅菌（高溫蒸汽、乾熱、EO 等），或以輻射滅菌後的形式供應。無論哪種方式，供應商必須提供符合規格的生物負荷 (bioburden) 數據。

3.10 Terms Related to Determination — 與確定相關的術語

3.10.1 automated inspection

Conformity evaluation performed by inspection equipment without manual intervention.

Note: Equipment can include optoelectronics (cameras), laser systems, ultrasonics and their associated data processing functions.

3.10.2 final inspection

Tests carried out on the finished product to determine compliance with the specification.

3.10.3 in-process control

Actions taken during the production process to test product conformity to its specification.

Note 1: Monitoring processes and adjusting means of production can be necessary to meet product requirements.

Note 2: The control of the environment or equipment can also be regarded as part of in-process control.

3.10.4 reconciliation

Comparison between the amount of finished product theoretically and actually produced or used, making allowance for normal variation.

Note: The comparison considers waste, samples or other losses inherent in the process.

3.10 解析

automated inspection — 現代品質控制的核心

自動化檢驗在初級包裝材料廠已是標配。ISO 15378 特別定義此術語，並提供具體技術例子（光電、雷射、超音波），說明自動化檢驗系統的多樣性。

對 CDMO QA 而言，稽核自動化檢驗系統時需確認：

  • 設備已完成確效（IQ/OQ/PQ）
  • 拒絕邏輯和閾值設定有書面依據
  • 假拒率和假接率定期評估
  • 系統校準紀錄完整

in-process control (IPC) — 製程中的品質關卡

IPC 是在生產過程中（而非最終產品）進行的品質確認。ISO 15378 特別補充：環境控制和設備控制也屬於 IPC 的一部分。

玻璃瓶製造的典型 IPC 項目：

  • 尺寸檢查（容積、壁厚、瓶口尺寸）
  • 外觀目視或自動化檢查（氣泡、結石、裂紋）
  • 模式重量監控
  • 潔淨室溫濕度和壓差記錄（環境 IPC）

reconciliation — 物料平衡的重要性

物料平衡是 GMP 防止混淆和計算錯誤的重要工具。公式：理論產量 = 實際產量 + 廢料 + 樣品 + 其他可解釋的損失。

如果物料平衡超出可接受範圍，必須調查是否有產品丟失或混批的可能性。這也是防止藥品包裝材料流入非預期通路的控制手段之一。

3.11 Terms Relating to Risk Management — 與風險管理相關的術語

3.11.1 quality critical

Parameter affecting primary packaging material quality.

Note: A material, process step or process condition, test requirement or any other relevant parameter can be considered quality critical if nonconformity to its requirements could have significant detrimental consequences.

3.11.2 risk analysis

Process to comprehend the nature of risk and to determine the level of risk.

Note 1: Risk analysis provides the basis for risk evaluation and decisions about risk treatment.

Note 2: Risk analysis includes risk estimation.

[SOURCE: ISO Guide 73:2009, 3.6.1]

3.11.3 risk assessment

Overall process of risk identification, risk analysis and risk evaluation.

[SOURCE: ISO Guide 73:2009, 3.4.1]

3.11.4 risk evaluation

Process of comparing the results of risk analysis with risk criteria to determine whether the risk and/or its magnitude is acceptable or tolerable.

Note: Risk evaluation assists in the decision about risk treatment.

[SOURCE: ISO Guide 73:2009, 3.7.1]

3.11.5 risk identification

Process of finding, recognizing and describing risks.

Note 1: Risk identification involves the identification of risk sources, events, their causes and their potential consequences.

Note 2: Risk identification can involve historical data, theoretical analysis, informed and expert opinions, and stakeholder's needs.

[SOURCE: ISO Guide 73:2009, 3.5.1]

3.11.6 risk management

Coordinated activities to direct and control an organization with regard to risk.

[SOURCE: ISO Guide 73:2009, 2.1]

3.11 解析 — 風險管理術語（ISO 15378 的特色）

質量關鍵 (quality critical) — 最重要的術語之一

"Quality critical" 是 ISO 15378 獨有的術語，在其他 ISO 標準中不常見。這個概念直接對應製藥產業的 Critical Quality Attributes (CQA) 和 Critical Process Parameters (CPP) 概念。

判斷標準：若某個參數不符合要求，可能對患者安全產生重大不利後果，則為 quality critical。

包裝材料的 quality critical 參數範例：

  • 橡膠塞的穿刺粒子 (coring) 傾向 → 直接影響注射安全
  • 玻璃瓶的封閉完整性 → 關乎無菌屏障
  • 鋁蓋的壓合力 (crimp force) → 決定瓶蓋密封性
  • 泡殼鋁箔的密封強度 → 防止藥品暴露於濕氣

與 ICH Q9 的對應關係

ISO 15378 的風險管理術語與 ICH Q9 (Quality Risk Management) 高度一致，因為兩者都引用 ISO Guide 73。這意味著已熟悉 ICH Q9 的製藥人員，在理解 ISO 15378 的風險管理要求時幾乎不需要重新學習術語。

CDMO 常用的風險工具（FMEA、Fishbone、FTA）在 ISO 15378 的風險管理框架中都是可接受的方法。

供應商稽核重點：風險管理成熟度評估

  1. 要求查閱最近一次對製程的正式風險評估報告（特別是 quality critical 參數的識別）
  2. 確認 change control 流程中是否有系統性的風險評估步驟
  3. 查核是否對新原料或新設備進行了風險評估
  4. 評估供應商是否能清晰說明哪些製程步驟是 quality critical，以及控制策略

風險管理的完整流程

ISO 15378 按照 ISO Guide 73 定義了風險管理的完整術語鏈：

Key Terms Summary — 核心術語快速參考

ISO 15378 雙軌架構總結

ISO 15378 = ISO 9001 + GMP：哪裡不同？

相較於純粹的 ISO 9001，ISO 15378 在以下領域增加了 GMP 特定要求：

  • 術語層面：新增 batch, GMP, IQ/OQ/PQ, validation, quality critical, line clearance 等 GMP 特有術語
  • 組織層面：強制要求設立 quality unit，確保 QA/QC 職能的獨立性
  • 文件層面：增加 batch record, SOP, URS 等 GMP 文件要求
  • 製程層面：增加 IPC、change control、deviation management、reconciliation
  • 風險層面：增加 quality critical 概念和 risk management 術語體系

如何向非技術人員解釋 ISO 15378

想像建造一棟安全的建築：

  • ISO 9001 = 一般建築法規（結構完整、防火、電氣安全）
  • ISO 15378 = 醫院建築法規（一般建築法規 + 醫療等級的額外要求：無菌區、手術室標準、醫療氣體系統等）

ISO 15378 認證就是告訴藥廠：這家供應商的「工廠」已按醫療等級標準建造和運作，不需要你從頭審查。

Clause 4 — Context of the Organization｜組織背景

4.1–4.4 Standard Requirements

4.1 Understanding the Organization and Its Context

The organization shall determine external and internal issues that are relevant to its purpose and strategic direction and that affect its ability to achieve the intended results of its quality management system. The organization shall monitor and review information about these external and internal issues.

GMP Addition: The organization's overall policy, intentions and approach to risk management, validation and change control shall be documented in order to fulfil the GMP requirements.

4.2 Understanding the Needs and Expectations of Interested Parties

The organization shall determine: (a) the interested parties that are relevant to the quality management system; (b) the requirements of these interested parties that are relevant to the quality management system. The organization shall monitor and review information about these interested parties and their relevant requirements.

4.3 Determining the Scope of the QMS

The organization shall determine the boundaries and applicability of the quality management system to establish its scope. When determining this scope, the organization shall consider: (a) external and internal issues (4.1); (b) requirements of relevant interested parties (4.2); (c) the products and services of the organization.

The scope shall state the types of products and services covered, and provide justification for any requirement determined not applicable.

4.4 Quality Management System and Its Processes

4.4.1 — The organization shall establish, implement, maintain and continually improve a QMS, including the processes needed and their interactions. The organization shall:

  • Determine inputs/outputs and sequence of processes
  • Determine and apply criteria, monitoring, measurement and performance indicators
  • Ensure resources and assign responsibilities for each process
  • Address risks and opportunities (Clause 6.1)
  • Describe how the quality policy and objectives are implemented, cascaded and monitored to provide assurance of product quality and meet agreed customer specifications
  • Document the structure of the quality management system (e.g. quality manual, master SOP)

4.4.2 — The organization shall maintain documented information to support operations and retain documented information to provide confidence that processes are being carried out as planned.

解讀與教學 Commentary

Clause 4.1 — 脈絡分析實務：包材供應商的 PESTLE

ISO 15378 要求供應商系統性分析外部與內部環境。對初包材製造商而言，關鍵外部議題包括：

  • 法規趨勢：EU MDR/IVDR 升級、中國 NMPA 新規、FDA 21 CFR Part 211 修訂
  • 原材料可得性：硼矽玻璃、鹵化丁基橡膠的供應鏈集中風險
  • 市場集中度：客戶基礎過於依賴單一藥廠的商業脆弱性
  • 競爭環境：替代包材（COP/COC 注射筒）對傳統玻璃的衝擊

GMP 強化要求（標準中特有）：風險管理、驗證和變更控制的整體方針必須文件化——這已超出 ISO 9001 原本的要求。

Clause 4.2 — 利害相關方識別

初包材供應商的利害相關方三層架構：

  • 直接客戶（藥廠/CDMO）：提出規格、進行稽核、要求 CoA/CoC
  • 監管機構（FDA/EMA/NMPA）：設定法規底線，間接影響供應商要求
  • 終端患者：最終受益者，任何包材缺陷都可能影響藥品安全性

重要洞察：患者雖不是合約當事人，卻是 ISO 15378 整個標準保護的核心對象。

Clause 4.4 — QMS 範圍：ISO 15378 與 ISO 9001 的核心差異

這是 ISO 15378 最關鍵的差異點：QMS 範圍必須明確涵蓋 GMP 要求。

純 ISO 9001 認證的包材供應商，其 QMS 可能未涵蓋：驗證、批次記錄、ALCOA 文件原則、潔淨室分類。而 ISO 15378 明確要求這些都必須在 QMS 範圍內。

CDMO 稽核視角

當稽核一家塞子或玻璃瓶供應商時，Clause 4.4 合規的具體問法：

「請展示您的品質手冊或主 SOP——其中是否明確列出 GMP 要求屬於 QMS 範圍？」

若供應商的 QMS 文件只提到品質目標和客戶滿意度，卻沒有提到 GMP、驗證或批次放行——這是 Clause 4.4 的重大缺口。

Clause 5 — Leadership｜領導統御

5.1–5.3 Standard Requirements

5.1.1 General — Leadership and Commitment

Top management shall demonstrate leadership and commitment with respect to the quality management system by:

  • Taking accountability for the effectiveness of the QMS
  • Ensuring quality policy and objectives are established and compatible with context and strategic direction
  • Ensuring integration of QMS requirements into business processes
  • Promoting the use of the process approach and risk-based thinking
  • Ensuring resources needed for the QMS are available
  • Communicating the importance of effective quality management
  • Ensuring the QMS achieves its intended results
  • Promoting improvement

5.1.2 Customer Focus

Top management shall ensure that customer and applicable statutory and regulatory requirements are determined, understood and consistently met; and that risks affecting conformity of products are addressed.

NOTE (GMP): Key customer requirements include suitable facilities, competent and trained personnel, processes designed to ensure product security and avoidance of cross-contamination, and the ability to consistently produce product conforming to customer specifications.

5.1.3 Customer Audits

Top management shall approve access by mutual agreement for existing/prospective customers or their nominated representatives to conduct audits to review and assess the organization's QMS.

5.2 Policy

5.2.1 Establishing the quality policy: Top management shall establish, implement and maintain a quality policy that is appropriate to the purpose and context of the organization, provides a framework for quality objectives, includes a commitment to satisfy applicable requirements, and includes a commitment to continual improvement.

5.2.2 Communicating the quality policy: The quality policy shall be available as documented information, communicated, understood and applied within the organization, and available to relevant interested parties.

5.3 Organizational Roles, Responsibilities and Authorities

Top management shall ensure that responsibilities and authorities for relevant roles are assigned, communicated and understood. Top management shall assign responsibility and authority for: (a) ensuring QMS conforms to requirements; (b) ensuring processes deliver intended outputs; (c) reporting QMS performance to top management; (d) ensuring customer focus throughout the organization; (e) maintaining QMS integrity when changes are planned.

GMP Addition: The organization shall maintain a current record of signatures of responsible persons. Signature/user identification lists of all personnel checking or double-checking process steps and in-process controls are recommended.

GMP Addition: The quality unit(s) with responsibility for quality critical decisions shall have the authority to make those decisions independently.

解讀與教學 Commentary

Clause 5.1 — 領導承諾：超越形式的 GMP 精神

ISO 15378 的領導承諾不只是簽署品質政策。標準隱含的要求是：當生產目標與產品品質發生衝突時，產品安全必須優先於產量目標。

可量化的管理承諾指標：

  • 管理階層是否親自出席品質審查會議？
  • 品質部門的人員編制比例是否足夠？
  • 偏差調查是否有時限要求並由管理層追蹤？
  • CAPA 完成率是否納入管理層 KPI？

資料來看：管理承諾與品質結果的關聯

業界研究顯示，當供應商管理層能見度高（定期 gemba walk、品質議題進入董事會報告）時，年度偏差率平均比管理被動的供應商低 30–40%，客訴率低 50%。

這說明 Clause 5.1 不只是文件要求，而是預測供應商長期績效的領先指標。

Clause 5.1.3 — 客戶稽核：主動開放 vs. 被動接受

標準要求最高管理層批准客戶稽核的准入，這代表供應商的高層必須認可稽核文化，而不是由品質部門單獨應付。

從 CDMO 角度：稽核申請被拖延或推辭，往往是重大風險信號。優質供應商通常有標準的稽核接待程序，最高管理層在開幕會議中出席。

Clause 5.3 — 品質單位的獨立決策權

這是 ISO 15378 最接近藥廠 Qualified Person (QP) 概念的條款。

標準明確規定：品質部門對品質關鍵決策擁有獨立決策權，不受生產或商業部門的壓力。這在實務上意味著：

  • 品質部門可以拒絕放行批次，即使生產部門要求出貨
  • 品質負責人的職位不應向生產總監匯報
  • 偏差調查結論不應被業務壓力改變

稽核時的問法：「請描述一個品質部門否決出貨決定的案例。」

簽名記錄要求 — Signature List

標準要求維護現行有效的責任人簽名記錄，並建議為所有執行查核或雙重查核的人員建立簽名/用戶識別清單。

這與 GMP 的 ALCOA 原則（可歸屬性 Attributable）直接連結——每筆記錄都必須能追溯到具名的個人。

Clause 6 — Planning｜策劃

6.1–6.3 Standard Requirements

6.1 Actions to Address Risks and Opportunities

6.1.1 — When planning for the QMS, the organization shall consider the issues (4.1) and requirements (4.2) and determine the risks and opportunities that need to be addressed to: (a) give assurance that the QMS can achieve its intended results; (b) enhance desirable effects; (c) prevent or reduce undesired effects; (d) achieve improvement.

6.1.2 — The organization shall plan actions to address risks and opportunities and evaluate their effectiveness. Actions shall be proportionate to the potential impact on conformity of products and services.

6.1.3 — GMP Addition: The organization shall ensure that risk management is included in all processes, associated with the design/development, manufacturing and delivery of primary packaging materials. Records shall be maintained.

Risk management applies to, for example:

  • Change control & cleaning
  • Complaints & contamination
  • Design control (new products/processes)
  • Health and hygiene
  • Labelling & maintenance
  • Manufacturing planning & materials management
  • Nonconformities and quality defects
  • Pest control & purchasing/supply chain
  • Rework & traceability
  • Validation, verification and qualification

NOTE: Risk management principles can be found in ISO 31000, ISO 14971, ICH Q9, and GAMP5. Methods for hazard identification and assessment: IEC 31010.

6.2 Quality Objectives and Planning to Achieve Them

6.2.1 — The organization shall establish quality objectives at relevant functions, levels and processes. Quality objectives shall:

  • Be consistent with the quality policy
  • Be measurable
  • Take into account applicable requirements
  • Be relevant to conformity of products/services and to customer satisfaction enhancement
  • Be monitored, communicated and updated as appropriate

6.2.2 — When planning how to achieve quality objectives, the organization shall determine: what will be done; what resources are required; who is responsible; when it will be completed; how results will be evaluated.

6.3 Planning of Changes

When the organization determines the need for changes to the QMS, changes shall be carried out in a planned manner. The organization shall consider:

  • The purpose of the changes and their potential consequences
  • The integrity of the quality management system
  • The availability of resources
  • The allocation or reallocation of responsibilities and authorities

For change control, see Clause 8.5.6.2.

解讀與教學 Commentary

Clause 6.1.3 — 包材製造的全流程風險管理

ISO 15378 的 GMP 強化要求明確將 ISO 14971（醫療器材風險管理）的思維帶入包材領域。對初包材製造商，這意味著風險管理不能只是一份 FMEA，而必須貫穿所有流程。

實務對照（以注射用玻璃瓶為例）：

  • 設計控制：新玻璃配方對 pH 值或脫層風險的 FMEA
  • 製造規劃：熔爐溫度偏離對缺陷率的風險評估
  • 驗證：清洗機 / 去熱原流程的風險導向驗證策略
  • 追蹤：批號系統確保缺陷批次可被完整追回

風險選項的現實考量

標準 NOTE 1 列出的風險應對選項包括：避免風險、接受風險（換取機會）、消除風險源、改變可能性/後果、共擔風險或保留風險。

對 CDMO 採購決策的意義：若一家供應商對高嚴重性風險採用「保留風險（informed decision）」策略，要求其提供書面的風險評估依據與控制措施——不能只有口頭說明。

Clause 6.3 — 變更控制：QMS 層面 vs. 製程層面

Clause 6.3 處理的是 QMS 系統層面的變更（如組織重組、新流程引入），而 Clause 8.5.6.2 處理的是製程/產品層面的變更控制。

兩者都要求「有計畫地」執行——不允許臨時或非正式的系統性變更。這與製藥 CDMO 的變更控制程序（包含影響評估、客戶通知、驗證觸發評估）精神一致。

稽核時的風險旗標

若供應商的品質目標只有業務指標（準時交貨率、產能利用率），卻沒有任何產品安全或 GMP 合規指標——這是 Clause 6.2 的缺口，也預示著品質文化問題。

Clause 6.2 — SMART 品質目標：GMP 層面的加碼

ISO 9001 的品質目標要求是可量測、可溝通。ISO 15378 在精神上進一步要求：品質目標必須包含產品安全與合規指標，而非僅限商業 KPI。

Clause 7 — Support (Part 1): Resources, Infrastructure & Environment｜資源與基礎設施

7.1 Resources — General to Environment

7.1.1–7.1.2 General Resources & People

The organization shall determine and provide the resources needed for the establishment, implementation, maintenance and continual improvement of the QMS. The organization shall consider: (a) capabilities and constraints on existing internal resources; (b) what needs to be obtained from external providers.

The organization shall determine and provide the persons necessary for effective implementation of its QMS and for the operation and control of its processes.

7.1.3 Infrastructure

7.1.3.1 General: The organization shall determine, provide and maintain the infrastructure necessary for the operation of its processes and to achieve conformity of products and services.

7.1.3.2 GMP-related requirements on infrastructure:

  • Infrastructure shall be managed, operated and maintained to avoid product contamination
  • Facilities shall be protected against entry of unauthorized personnel
  • Personnel entering defined production/storage/QC areas shall only access wearing appropriate clothing
  • Layout, design and operation shall minimize the risk of errors and permit effective cleaning and maintenance to avoid cross-contamination, based on a risk assessment
  • Changing facilities, toilets and hand-washing facilities shall be provided; where product quality is affected, these shall be separated from manufacturing areas and not ventilated directly to them

Storage areas shall be of adequate capacity to allow orderly storage of starting materials and products, and appropriate with regard to material and product quality.

7.1.4 Environment for the Operation of Processes

7.1.4.2 Work environment:

  • Documented requirements for health, cleanliness, clothing and access control shall be established
  • If work environment conditions can adversely affect primary packaging material quality, appropriate conditions shall be defined and effectively monitored and controlled
  • Special conditions shall be established for control of contaminated or potentially contaminated material
  • Where primary packaging materials are exposed, covers shall be used unless justified by documented risk assessment

7.1.4.3 Classification of clean zones/cleanrooms: Clean zones/cleanrooms shall be classified and monitored/operated. See ISO 14644-1, -2, -3, -5. For biocontamination monitoring: ISO 14698-1 and -2.

7.1.4.4 Risk control of contamination: The organization shall determine and control risks that can result in contamination, including: personal hygiene and health; personal clothing, jewellery and make-up; smoking, eating, chewing, drinking and personal medication; handling and disposal of waste; microbiological contamination; protective clothing appropriate to the process area classification.

7.1.4.5 Pest control: An effective, documented pest control programme shall be implemented and maintained.

7.1.4.6 Materials and utilities: All utilities (air, gases, steam, water) shall be assessed for their potential impact on primary packaging material quality. If water comes into direct contact with the primary packaging material or is used for cleaning contact equipment, its quality shall be determined and controlled. Processing aids shall be subject to a documented risk assessment.

7.1.4.7 Maintenance and cleaning: Documented requirements for maintenance activities shall be established. Maintenance operations shall not introduce contamination and, on completion, shall include a documented cleanliness check. A cleaning schedule that takes into account contamination risk shall be defined and documented. Defective quality critical equipment shall be removed from service and/or clearly labelled; prior to reintroduction it shall be verified as suitable for use.

解讀與教學 Commentary

Clause 7.1.3.2 — GMP 廠房設計原則

標準要求廠房佈局必須基於風險評估，以最小化錯誤風險並允許有效清潔。這是製藥 GMP 的核心設計原則。

對初包材製造商的具體意涵：

  • 不同材質（橡膠 vs. 玻璃）的生產線需要物理隔離，防止交叉污染
  • 人流/物流路徑設計必須防止污染品與合格品的混淆
  • 更衣室/洗手間的位置與通風設計，必須不直接通向生產區

Clause 7.1.4.3 — 潔淨室分類與 ISO 14644

ISO 15378 明確引用 ISO 14644 系列作為潔淨室管理的技術依據，建立了一個標準內引用標準的整合架構：

  • ISO 14644-1：潔淨室分類（ISO 5–9）
  • ISO 14644-2：監控規範
  • ISO 14644-3：測試方法
  • ISO 14644-5：作業
  • ISO 14698-1/2：生物污染監測

對無菌包材製造商（如無菌注射筒預裝廠），潔淨室分類通常為 ISO 5 或 ISO 7，需定期懸浮粒子計數和微生物監測。

水質管理：從 WFI 到工藝用水

Clause 7.1.4.6.3 要求：若水直接接觸初包材或用於清洗接觸產品的設備，必須確定並控制水質。

實務場景（玻璃瓶洗瓶機）：最終沖洗水若為注射用水（WFI），需要定期微生物、內毒素和電導率測試，記錄保存。若降級使用純化水，必須有文件化的風險評估支持。

維護後潔淨度確認 — 常被忽略的要求

Clause 7.1.4.7.3 明確要求：維修保養完成後必須進行書面的潔淨度查核。這是稽核中常見的缺口。

常見問題：工程部門完成維修後，未通知品質部門進行潔淨度確認就恢復生產，導致潤滑油、工具遺留等污染風險未被管控。

Clause 7 — Support (Part 2): Measurement, Competence & Documented Information｜量測、能力與文件化資訊

7.1.5–7.5 Standard Requirements

7.1.5 Monitoring and Measuring Resources

7.1.5.1 General: The organization shall determine and provide resources to ensure valid and reliable results when monitoring or measuring is used to verify conformity of products.

7.1.5.2 Measurement traceability: When measurement traceability is required, measuring equipment shall be:

  • Calibrated or verified at specified intervals or prior to use, against measurement standards traceable to international or national measurement standards
  • Identified in order to determine their status
  • Safeguarded from adjustments, damage or deterioration that would invalidate calibration status

The organization shall determine if the validity of previous measurement results has been adversely affected when measuring equipment is found unfit for purpose, and shall take appropriate action.

7.1.6 Organizational Knowledge

The organization shall determine the knowledge necessary for the operation of its processes and to achieve conformity of products and services. This knowledge shall be maintained and made available. When addressing changing needs, the organization shall consider current knowledge and determine how to acquire necessary additional knowledge.

7.2 Competence

7.2.1 General: The organization shall: (a) determine the necessary competence of persons doing work that affects QMS performance; (b) ensure persons are competent based on appropriate education, training, or experience; (c) take actions to acquire necessary competence and evaluate effectiveness; (d) retain appropriate documented information as evidence of competence.

7.2.2 GMP Training: Additional training shall be conducted regularly and shall include:

  • Awareness of applicable GMP and all procedures/policies affecting product quality
  • The risk of contamination and cross-contamination
  • The potential hazard to end user/patient if product is contaminated
  • The impact of deviations from specified procedures, processes or specifications on customer's product quality or the end user

Particular attention shall be given to training of personnel involved with manufacture of sterile components or components to be subsequently sterilized. Specific training on microbiological and particulate contamination and the patient risk shall be provided.

Additional refresher training shall be carried out at defined intervals. Temporary personnel shall be trained or supervised by a trained person. Contractors and visitors shall receive appropriate instructions prior to entering manufacturing/production facilities.

7.3 Awareness

The organization shall ensure that persons doing work are aware of: (a) the quality policy; (b) relevant quality objectives; (c) their contribution to QMS effectiveness; (d) the implications of not conforming with QMS requirements.

7.4 Communication

The organization shall determine internal and external communications relevant to the QMS. GMP Addition: GMP and regulatory requirements shall be communicated, as appropriate, to each level of the organization. Top management shall be notified of quality critical situations in a timely manner.

7.5 Documented Information

7.5.1–7.5.2: The QMS shall include documented information required by this Standard and as determined necessary by the organization. When creating and updating documented information, the organization shall ensure appropriate identification, description, format, review and approval. Changes to documents shall be reviewed and approved by the original or another designated function. If electronic signatures are used, they shall be controlled to provide equivalent security to a hand-written signature.

7.5.3 Control of documented information:

  • 7.5.3.1: Documented information shall be available and suitable for use, and adequately protected
  • 7.5.3.2: The organization shall address distribution, access, retrieval and use; storage and preservation including legibility; version control; retention and disposition
  • 7.5.3.3: The organization shall define the period for retaining at least one copy of obsolete controlled documents; obsolete documents shall be stored and marked to prevent unintended use
  • 7.5.3.4: Records shall be controlled to provide evidence of conformity. Records shall remain legible, readily identifiable and retrievable
  • 7.5.3.5: Electronic records shall be subject to the same controls as other records
  • 7.5.3.6 (GMP): Entries in records shall be clear, indelible, made directly after performing the activity, dated, and initialled or signed. Corrections shall be dated, initialled or signed and explained, leaving the original entry still legible
  • 7.5.3.7: The organization shall define quality critical processes/parameters where a double-check is necessary for batch release
  • 7.5.3.8: For each batch, a record shall be established providing traceability and identifying quantity manufactured and quantity approved for distribution
  • 7.5.3.11: All manufacturing, control, testing, distribution and investigation records shall be retained for at least five years after the date of manufacture, or as agreed with the customer

7.5.4 Administration of IT systems and data: There shall be documented procedures for: responsibility assignment for IT and data security; network/file access control; file integrity on shared systems; password management and sleep mode; back-up and recovery of electronic product-related data (frequency, method, media, physical process for safe storage). The organization shall have a documented IT recovery plan. Access to legacy systems shall be defined as part of change control when IT systems are changed.

解讀與教學 Commentary

Clause 7.1.5 — 量測設備校準：GMP 的具體實踐

所有用於品質關鍵量測的設備都必須校準且保持記錄。這在包材製造中覆蓋範圍很廣：

  • 玻璃瓶生產：壁厚量具、容量量規、破裂強度測試機
  • 橡膠塞生產：硬度計、穿刺力測試儀、尺寸量測儀
  • 環境監測：粒子計數器、培養箱溫度計、壓差計
  • 分析實驗室：HPLC、天平、pH 計

校準狀態的識別（標籤）和量測設備清單是稽核查核的基本項目。

Clause 7.2.2 — GMP 訓練：超越 SOP 的患者安全意識

ISO 15378 的 GMP 訓練要求特別之處：不只要求員工知道「怎麼做」，還要理解「為什麼」——包括對患者的潛在危害。

這三個意識層次對應訓練設計：

  • 層次 1（知識）：污染的定義、類型、來源
  • 層次 2（理解）：污染如何經由包材傳遞到藥品並危害患者
  • 層次 3（行動）：偏差時應立即通報而非隱瞞，因為後果是患者安全

對無菌組件的人員，標準要求特別加強微生物和微粒污染的培訓——這是最高風險等級的要求。

臨時人員與承包商的 GMP 管控

Clause 7.2.2 明確要求：臨時工必須接受訓練或在受訓人員監督下工作；承包商和訪客在進入生產設施前必須接受適當指導。

實務風險：旺季大量聘用臨時工，卻未建立有效的快速訓練程序——是常見的 GMP 合規缺口，也是稽核發現的高頻觀察項。

記錄保存期限：五年最低要求

Clause 7.5.3.11 要求所有製造、管制、測試、配送和調查記錄保存至少五年，或依客戶協議（可能更長）。

實務注意：若藥廠客戶要求保存至藥品有效期後兩年（這可能是 10–15 年），供應商必須在品質協議中反映這個要求，並確保儲存系統（含 IT 備份）能支持長期保存。

Clause 7.5.4 — IT 系統管理：數據完整性的現代挑戰

ISO 15378:2017 明確要求建立 IT 系統管理程序，涵蓋：

  • 網路和檔案的存取控制（僅授權人員）
  • 共享伺服器的文件完整性保護
  • 密碼管理和螢幕鎖定（人員離開時）
  • 電子數據備份：頻率、方法、媒體、安全儲存
  • IT 系統災難復原計畫，並定期驗證

這些要求與 FDA 數據完整性指南（2018）和 MHRA 數據完整性指南（2021）的精神高度一致，反映了監管機構對電子化 GMP 環境的日益關注。

ISO 15378 的記錄要求完整呼應 ALCOA 原則，這是 FDA/EMA 評估數據可靠性的基礎框架：

8.1 Operational Planning and Control / 運作規劃與控制

原文內容 Original Text

ISO 9001:2015 base text:

The organization shall plan, implement and control the processes needed to meet the requirements for the provision of products and services, and to implement the actions determined in Clause 6, by:

  1. determining the requirements for the products and services;
  2. establishing criteria for:
    1. the processes;
    2. the acceptance of products and services;
  3. determining the resources needed to achieve conformity to the product and service requirements;
  4. implementing control of the processes in accordance with the criteria;
  5. determining, maintaining and retaining documented information to the extent necessary:
    1. to have confidence that the processes have been carried out as planned;
    2. to demonstrate the conformity of products and services to their requirements.

The output of this planning shall be suitable for the organization's operations.

The organization shall control planned changes and review the consequences of unintended changes, taking action to mitigate any adverse effects, as necessary.

The organization shall ensure that outsourced processes are controlled (see 8.4).

Additional GMP-Related Requirements

Product realization planning shall consider the requirement for consistent processing of primary packaging materials. Planning shall also take account of the need for taking and retaining samples in appropriate conditions.

The organization shall ensure that risk management processes are included in the planning and implemented throughout product realization; records shall be maintained (see 7.5.3.4).

教學解說 Educational Commentary

核心概念：雙層架構 (Layered Structure)

ISO 15378 的結構設計非常獨特：每個條款先引用 ISO 9001:2015 的原文，然後追加額外 GMP 要求（Additional GMP-related requirements）。

這意味著製造藥用主包材的企業，必須同時滿足：

  • ISO 9001 的品質管理系統基礎 — 通用製造要求
  • ISO 15378 的 GMP 附加條款 — 因藥品安全而增加的特殊要求

就像「駕照 + 職業司機執照」— 後者涵蓋前者，但有更嚴格的標準。

運作規劃的五個關鍵動作

8.1 要求組織對每個產品實現製程做到：

  • 確認要求：產品規格、法規要求是什麼？
  • 建立標準：製程接受準則 + 產品驗收標準
  • 確保資源：人員、設備、環境
  • 執行控制：按標準操作
  • 保存記錄：證明製程依計畫執行且產品符合要求

比喻：如何製造一批玻璃瓶？

想像你接到一張訂單，要生產 10 mL 的注射用玻璃瓶。8.1 要求你在動手前就規劃好：玻璃熔融溫度範圍、成型尺寸允差、退火曲線、清洗條件，以及何時取樣、如何保存留樣。

如果中途發現烤箱溫度偏高（非計畫變更），必須評估對產品的影響，採取緩解行動，並留下記錄。

GMP 附加要求的重點：樣品保留 & 風險管理

樣品保留（Sample Retention）：主包材的留樣不只是品質記錄，更是當市場出現問題時的調查依據。ISO 15378 要求在「適當條件」下保存，意味著需要考慮儲存溫度、濕度、光線等因素。

風險管理貫穿全程：不是只在設計階段做一次風險評估就結束；整個產品實現過程（從原料進廠到成品出廠）都需要持續的風險管理，且記錄必須保存（7.5.3.4）。

Communication with customers shall include:

  1. providing information relating to products and services;
  2. handling enquiries, contracts or orders, including changes;
  3. obtaining customer feedback relating to products and services, including customer complaints;
  4. handling or controlling customer property;

教學解說 Educational Commentary

為何藥用包材需要更嚴格的客戶溝通機制？

一般製造業的客戶溝通多半是商業往來（訂單、報價、投訴處理）。但藥用主包材的客戶（製藥廠）把這些包材直接用在藥品上，若包材品質出問題，最終影響的是患者安全。

因此 ISO 15378 在 ISO 9001 基礎上新增三項 GMP 要求：回饋系統、變更確認機制、法規申請文件支援。

8.2.1.2.1 — 文件化回饋系統

這不只是「收客訴就處理」的被動做法。ISO 15378 要求的是主動式早期預警系統，要能：

  • 及早發現潛在品質問題（不只是已發生的）
  • 將客戶的意見回饋納入 CAPA（糾正和預防措施）系統
  • 形成閉環：問題 → 分析 → 改善 → 確認效果

比喻：DMF 和 CoS 是什麼？

想像製藥廠要申請一款新藥上市許可，其中需要說明所用玻璃瓶的詳細製造資訊（原料成分、測試方法、製程條件）。

DMF（Drug Master File）：包材商直接向美國 FDA 提交技術檔案，藥廠申請時引用此 DMF 號碼。包材商的機密資訊不對藥廠公開，FDA 可直接審查。

CoS（Certificate of Suitability）：歐洲藥典（Ph.Eur.）頒發的適用性證書，證明某材料符合 Ph.Eur. 專論標準，可在歐洲上市申請中引用。

這兩種機制讓包材商在保護商業機密的同時，仍能支援客戶的法規申請。

變更通知的分類管理

8.2.1.2.2 要求雙方事先協議哪些變更需要「書面確認後才能實施」，哪些只需「通知即可」。這應寫入技術/品保協議（Quality Agreement）中。

實務常見分類：

  • 需書面批准：原料供應商替換、製程參數重大變更、設備型號更換
  • 通知即可：輕微的製程優化、非關鍵輔助材料的替換

印刷主包材 — Annex C 適用

8.2.1.2.4 特別指出：有印刷文字的主包材（如印刷瓶身標籤、直接印刷在包材上的劑量資訊）需遵循 Annex C 的 GMP 規定，包括版本控制、防止過期版本使用、印刷品核對等。這是因為印刷錯誤可能直接導致用藥錯誤（wrong dose, wrong drug）。

8.2.2 Determining the Requirements for Products and Services / 確認產品與服務要求

原文內容 Original Text

When determining the requirements for the products and services to be offered to customers, the organization shall ensure that:

  1. the requirements for the products and services are defined, including:
    1. any applicable statutory and regulatory requirements;
    2. those considered necessary by the organization;
  2. the organization can meet the claims for the products and services it offers.

Additional GMP-Related Requirements

Requirements related to the product, including changes requiring notification, shall be determined and documented.

Customer requirements to avoid unauthorized use of waste primary packaging material (including samples, print media, labels) shall be determined and documented.

教學解說 Educational Commentary

廢棄包材的安全銷毀要求

這個 GMP 附加要求乍看不起眼，卻非常重要。「禁止未授權使用廢棄主包材」的典型情境：

  • 不合格品（Rejects）的玻璃瓶流入市場，被非法填充藥品
  • 廢棄的印刷標籤被回收用於偽造藥品
  • 試作樣品（Samples）在未授權情況下被轉售

因此，組織需要與客戶明確界定廢棄包材的處理方式（例如：銷毀記錄、第三方認證銷毀），並形成文件要求。

法規要求的識別

「適用的法規要求」對藥用包材而言可能包括：

  • 各國藥典（USP、Ph.Eur.、JP）中的包材專論
  • FDA 21 CFR（例如用於製藥的塑料容器相關規定）
  • EU GMP 附錄中對包材的要求
  • 客戶所在市場的特定法規
8.2.3 Review of Requirements / 要求審查

原文內容 Original Text

The organization shall ensure that it has the ability to meet the requirements for products and services to be offered to customers. The organization shall conduct a review before committing to supply products and services to a customer, to include:

  1. requirements specified by the customer, including the requirements for delivery and post-delivery activities;
  2. requirements not stated by the customer, but necessary for the specified or intended use, when known;
  3. requirements specified by the organization;
  4. statutory and regulatory requirements applicable to the products and services;
  5. contract or order requirements differing from those previously expressed.

The organization shall ensure that contract or order requirements differing from those previously defined are resolved.

The customer's requirements shall be confirmed by the organization before acceptance, when the customer does not provide a documented statement of their requirements.

The organization shall retain documented information:

  1. on the results of the review;
  2. on any new requirements for the products and services.

教學解說 Educational Commentary

承接訂單前的可行性審查

這條款的核心精神是：在承諾供應前，先確認自己做得到。對藥用包材廠商而言，審查的關鍵問題包括：

  • 我們的製程能達到客戶要求的粒子規格嗎？
  • 潔淨室等級符合要求嗎？
  • 我們已取得生產此規格產品所需的驗證嗎？
  • 客戶要求的測試項目，我們有能力執行嗎？

「未明示但必要」的要求

客戶說「我要一批 2 mL 安瓶，需符合 Ph.Eur. 3.2.1」，但沒提「耐熱冲擊測試」。然而玻璃安瓶用於最終滅菌製程時，耐熱冲擊是必要特性。

依 8.2.3(b)，即使客戶沒提，組織應主動識別並確認此要求。這正是包材商作為「專業夥伴」的價值所在。

8.2.4 Changes to Requirements / 要求變更

原文內容 Original Text

The organization shall ensure that relevant documented information is amended, and that relevant persons are made aware of the changed requirements, when the requirements for products and services are changed.

教學解說 Educational Commentary

變更傳達的實務重點

這條款看似簡單，但在實務中常出現「修改了規格書，但現場操作人員不知道」的問題。組織需要建立有效的變更傳達機制，確保：

藥用包材的設計開發 — 為何需要正式流程？

  • 滅菌方式的耐受性
  • 臨床使用時的操作性（患者/護士能正確使用嗎？）
  • 生產製程的可行性和一致性
  • 法規申請所需的文件支持

缺乏正式 D&D 流程，很容易出現「設計出來的產品生產不了」或「上市後發現相容性問題」。

GMP 附加要求的核心：患者影響評估

ISO 9001 的設計規劃只要求考慮客戶要求和資源。ISO 15378 額外要求將「對患者的潛在影響」納入設計流程的文件化程序。

實例：開發一款新型橡膠塞。ISO 9001 視角只需確保尺寸符合規格、機械性能合格。ISO 15378 還要求評估：橡膠塞的可萃取物（Extractables）會不會遷移到藥液中？對患者是否有毒性風險？

這個「從患者角度出發」的思維，是 GMP 相較於一般品質管理的核心差異。

設計責任的客戶-供應商協議

條款建議雙方事先協議設計和風險評估的責任分工。實務上，這通常寫入 Quality Agreement 或 Technical Agreement 中。

典型分工案例：藥廠提供功能需求和相容性要求，包材廠負責材料設計和製程驗證，共同負責最終的風險評估文件。

8.3.3 Design and Development Inputs / 設計與開發輸入

原文內容 Original Text

The organization shall determine the requirements essential for the specific types of products and services to be designed and developed. The organization shall consider:

  1. functional and performance requirements;
  2. information derived from previous similar design and development activities;
  3. statutory and regulatory requirements;
  4. standards or codes of practice that the organization has committed to implement;
  5. potential consequences of failure due to the nature of the products and services.

Inputs shall be adequate for design and development purposes, complete and unambiguous.

Conflicting design and development inputs shall be resolved.

The organization shall retain documented information on design and development inputs.

教學解說 Educational Commentary

設計輸入的品質決定設計成敗

「垃圾進，垃圾出（Garbage in, garbage out）」在設計開發中同樣適用。設計輸入的關鍵要求是：

  • 完整（Complete）：不能缺項，特別是安全相關要求
  • 明確（Unambiguous）：不能含糊，例如「足夠強度」要轉化為具體數值
  • 無矛盾（Conflict-resolved）：若客戶要求「儘量輕薄」但法規要求最低厚度，必須解決這個矛盾

(e) 失效後果評估 — GMP 的核心思維

要求在設計輸入階段就考慮「若失效，後果是什麼？」這是 FMEA（失效模式與效應分析）的起點。

以藥瓶密封為例：若密封失效，可能導致無菌性失效 → 藥品污染 → 患者感染。這個嚴重性評估會直接影響設計的安全係數和驗收標準。

8.3.4 Design and Development Controls / 設計與開發控制

原文內容 Original Text

The organization shall apply controls to the design and development process to ensure that:

  1. the results to be achieved are defined;
  2. reviews are conducted to evaluate the ability of the results to meet requirements;
  3. verification activities are conducted to ensure that outputs meet the input requirements;
  4. validation activities are conducted to ensure that the resulting products meet the requirements for the specified application or intended use;
  5. any necessary actions are taken on problems determined during reviews or V&V activities;
  6. documented information of these activities is retained.
NOTE: Design and development reviews, verification and validation have distinct purposes. They can be conducted separately or in any combination, as is suitable for the products and services of the organization. Verification and validation in this context is limited to design and development. For verification and validation of production and service provision, see 8.5.

教學解說 Educational Commentary

Review / Verification / Validation 的區別

這三個活動常被混淆，但目的不同：

  • Review（設計審查）：評估設計結果能否滿足要求。可在設計各階段進行，通常為跨部門會議評審。問：「設計方向對嗎？」
  • Verification（驗證）：確認輸出符合輸入規格。通常是文件/計算/測試層面的核對。問：「做出來的東西符合設計規格嗎？」
  • Validation（確效）：確認產品在實際應用條件下能滿足預期用途。問：「這個包材在實際使用條件下能保護藥品嗎？」

以藥用橡膠塞開發為例

設計一款用於凍乾粉針的橡膠塞：

  • Review：設計審查會議確認配方、尺寸設計符合客戶需求規格
  • Verification：測試橡膠塞尺寸是否符合設計圖面公差（例如內徑 ±0.1mm）
  • Validation：將橡膠塞裝入實際藥瓶並通過凍乾循環，確認密封性、抽真空後恢復性、與藥品的相容性符合預期用途
8.3.5 Design and Development Outputs / 設計與開發輸出

原文內容 Original Text

The organization shall ensure that design and development outputs:

  1. meet the input requirements;
  2. are adequate for the subsequent processes for the provision of products and services;
  3. include or reference monitoring and measuring requirements, as appropriate, and acceptance criteria;
  4. specify the characteristics of the products and services that are essential for their intended purpose and their safe and proper provision.
  5. are verified as suitable before finalizing product specifications.

The organization shall retain documented information on design and development outputs.

NOTE 1: The organization and customer are encouraged to work together to verify the appropriateness of the primary packaging materials for their intended use.

NOTE 2: Design and development outputs can include records (specifications, manufacturing procedures, engineering drawings, engineering or research logbooks) and samples.

教學解說 Educational Commentary

設計輸出是什麼？

設計輸出是設計過程的「交付物」，通常包括：

  • 產品規格書（Specification）
  • 製造程序（Manufacturing Procedure / SOP）
  • 工程圖面（Engineering Drawings）
  • 品管計畫（Control Plan / Inspection Plan）
  • 驗收標準（Acceptance Criteria）
  • 確認用樣品（Validation Samples）

(e) 規格確定前的適宜性驗證 — GMP 增加的要求

這是 ISO 15378 相較 ISO 9001 的額外要求。產品規格不能僅憑設計計算確定，必須在「最終確定」前，通過實際驗證（測試或試產）確認其適宜性。

實務意義：不能直接把設計圖紙變成正式規格書；必須先有試作樣品驗證的結果支持。

NOTE 1 的實踐：供應商-客戶協同驗證

包材廠開發出新的 PFS 筒管（Cartridge），客戶需在自己的灌裝線上進行相容性測試（填充、冷凍、運輸振動）。這種「聯合驗證」是業界的最佳實踐，可提前發現生產應用中的問題，避免上市後才出現系統性不符合。

8.3.6 Design and Development Changes / 設計與開發變更

原文內容 Original Text

The organization shall identify, review and control changes made during, or subsequent to, the design and development of products and services, to the extent necessary to ensure that there is no adverse impact on conformity to requirements.

The organization shall retain documented information on:

  1. design and development changes;
  2. the results of reviews;
  3. the authorization of the changes;
  4. the actions taken to prevent adverse impacts.

Additional GMP-Related Requirements

Changes that affect any of the data supplied shall be reported to the customer and, if a technical dossier/master file has been supplied by the organization, directly to the regulatory authorities.

When implementing change, the existing validation and documents affected by the change shall be reviewed and revised; personnel shall be retrained as appropriate.

NOTE: Confidential scientific and technical information can be supplied as a dossier directly to the regulatory authorities (e.g. technical dossier and/or master file).

教學解說 Educational Commentary

設計變更控制的四大要素

任何設計或開發後期的變更，必須透過正式的變更控制（Change Control）流程處理：

  1. 識別（Identify）：誰、何時、改了什麼
  2. 審查（Review）：評估對產品品質、法規合規性的影響
  3. 授權（Authorize）：有權限的人員批准
  4. 行動（Act）：更新文件、重新訓練、必要時重新驗證

GMP 關鍵要求：法規通報義務

這是 ISO 15378 最重要的 GMP 附加要求之一。若你向客戶提供了 DMF 或技術檔案，任何影響這些資料的變更，必須直接通報監管機構。

典型違規案例：原料供應商悄悄更換，包材廠沒有通知客戶也沒有更新 DMF，導致藥廠的上市許可技術要求與實際情況不符，引發 FDA Warning Letter。

「驗證影響評估」是實施變更的前提

當變更可能影響已驗證的製程或設備時，必須：

  • 評估現有驗證文件是否仍然有效
  • 決定是否需要重新驗證（全部或部分）
  • 更新受影響的所有相關文件（SOP、規格書、驗證報告）
  • 對相關人員進行再培訓並記錄

這就是為什麼變更控制需要跨職能（設計、生產、QA、RA）共同評估。

8.4.1 Control of Externally Provided Processes — General / 外部提供製程的控制 — 總則

原文內容 Original Text

ISO 9001:2015 base text:

The organization shall ensure that externally provided processes, products and services conform to requirements.

The organization shall determine the controls to be applied when:

  1. products and services from external providers are intended for incorporation into the organization's own products and services;
  2. products and services are provided directly to the customer(s) by external providers on behalf of the organization;
  3. a process, or part of a process, is provided by an external provider as a result of a decision by the organization.

The organization shall determine and apply criteria for the evaluation, selection, monitoring of performance, and re-evaluation of external providers, based on their ability to provide processes or products and services in accordance with requirements. The organization shall retain documented information of these activities and any necessary actions arising from the evaluations.

The organization shall maintain relevant purchasing information to the extent required for traceability.

Additional GMP-Related Requirements

If any quality critical process is outsourced, the organization shall ensure that the process complies with the requirements of this document.

教學解說 Educational Commentary

外部提供者的三種類型

8.4 涵蓋所有形式的「向外採購或委外」：

  • 原材料供應商：玻璃砂、橡膠配方、聚合物原料
  • 服務供應商：滅菌服務、檢測實驗室、校驗服務
  • 委外製造：某製程步驟由另一廠商代工（例如表面處理）

供應商管理的四步驟循環

  • 評估（Evaluation）：新供應商進入前的資格審查（稽核、樣品測試）
  • 選擇（Selection）：依評估結果決定是否核准
  • 績效監控（Performance Monitoring）：持續追蹤 CoA 合格率、交期、異常記錄
  • 再評估（Re-evaluation）：定期重新審查（例如每年一次稽核或表現評分）

GMP 附加要求：品質關鍵製程外包的管控

「品質關鍵製程（Quality Critical Process）」若外包，整個流程仍須符合 ISO 15378 的要求，責任不因外包而轉移。

例如：將玻璃瓶的最終清洗外包，但清洗是影響微粒污染的關鍵步驟，外包商的清洗程序、設備、環境、記錄，都必須符合 ISO 15378 的標準。

8.4.2 Type and Extent of Control / 控制類型與範圍

原文內容 Original Text

8.4.2.1 General (ISO 9001:2015 base):

The organization shall ensure that externally provided processes, products and services do not adversely affect the organization's ability to consistently deliver conforming products and services. The organization shall:

  1. ensure that externally provided processes remain within the control of its quality management system;
  2. define both the controls that it intends to apply to an external provider and those it intends to apply to the resulting output;
  3. take into consideration:
    1. the potential impact on the organization's ability to consistently meet requirements;
    2. the effectiveness of the controls applied by the external provider;
  4. determine the verification, or other activities, necessary to ensure requirements are met.

8.4.2.2 Additional GMP-Related Requirements

8.4.2.2.1 — The organization shall approve suppliers of (a) starting materials, (b) quality critical process aids, and (c) packaging materials for use in cleanrooms.

8.4.2.2.2 — The organization shall notify the customer prior to outsourcing any part of the production process.

8.4.2.2.3 — All outsourced services that can affect product quality shall be controlled, including origination (artwork), laboratory services, sterilization, calibration services and qualification services, maintenance, cleaning, haulage, pest control and waste contractors, depending on the risks involved.

8.4.2.2.4 — Consultants advising on the production and control of primary packaging materials shall be considered as suppliers.

8.4.2.2.5 — Suppliers of quality critical materials and services shall be approved by the quality unit(s) or a person assigned by the quality unit(s).

8.4.2.2.6 — The organization shall evaluate and record the competence of laboratories to perform quality critical activities. The organization shall only use laboratories that it has accepted as being competent.

8.4.2.2.7 — If the sterilization process is outsourced, the organization shall ensure that the process complies with the requirements of 8.5.1.2, 8.5.1.3 and 8.5.1.4.

8.4.2.2.8 — Changing the source of quality critical raw materials shall be subject to change control.

8.4.2.2.9 — Incoming materials shall be physically or administratively quarantined until they have been approved and released for use.

NOTE: In exceptional circumstances, material under test can be used, provided there are fail-safe procedures in place to prevent the release of primary packaging material, until the status of those materials has been confirmed.

8.4.2.2.10 — For quality critical materials, the organization shall periodically verify the relevant and/or critical information received from their suppliers on a certificate of analysis (CoA), certificate of conformity (CoC), or certificate of testing (CoT).

NOTE 1: This can mean performing similar testing on site, by an independent contractor, or routine audit of the facilities.

NOTE 2: In lieu of such testing, a report of analysis (CoA/CoC/CoT) can be accepted from the supplier, provided that at least one specific identity test is conducted on the material by the organization.

8.4.2.2.11 — Records of the verification shall be maintained.

8.4.2.2.12 — Sampling activities shall be conducted using procedures, facilities and equipment designed to avoid contamination.

教學解說 Educational Commentary

8.4.2.2.1 — 三類必須核准的供應商

這條款明確列出三類必須通過正式核准程序的供應商：

  • 起始材料（Starting Materials）：構成最終包材的原料，如玻璃原料、橡膠配方原料、聚合物顆粒
  • 品質關鍵製程輔助品（Quality Critical Process Aids）：例如模具潤滑劑、清潔劑、矽油（若用於注射器塗層）
  • 潔淨室用包裝材料：在潔淨室中使用的包材本身，例如包裝袋、托盤、運輸容器

「核准」通常包含：供應商稽核、樣品測試、風險評估、核准委員會批准，並列入合格供應商清單（AVL）。

8.4.2.2.2 — 外包前必須通知客戶

這是許多包材廠容易忽略的要求。在開始外包任何生產製程的任何部分之前，必須事先通知客戶。

典型場景：為提升產能，決定將某個加工步驟分包給另一家廠商。此舉需要先知會客戶，因為客戶的藥品上市許可可能指定了生產地點或製程描述。

8.4.2.2.4 — 顧問也是供應商！

這是一個常被忽視的要求。若你聘請外部顧問協助生產或品質管控，這位顧問在 ISO 15378 框架下屬於「供應商」，需要被評估和管理。

實務上，顧問的資格（學歷、專業執照、相關經驗）應被評估和記錄，其提供的建議和報告也應納入品質管理體系。

8.4.2.2.6 — 實驗室能力評估

委外實驗室（如 CRO、第三方測試機構）必須通過能力評估，常見方法包括：

  • 認證資質：ISO/IEC 17025 認可（最佳方式）
  • 能力比對（Proficiency Testing）：與已知結果的樣品比對
  • 稽核：現場審查實驗室的 SOP、設備、人員資格
  • 方法確認（Method Validation）：確認其使用的測試方法適合目的

8.4.2.2.9 — 進料隔離（Quarantine）的實務

「物理或行政隔離」意味著：

  • 物理隔離：設置隔離倉庫區域，貼上「隔離中 — 待驗」標籤，未放行前無法進入生產區
  • 行政隔離：在 ERP/倉儲系統中鎖定庫存狀態，庫管人員無法撿貨除非系統放行

NOTE 中的「例外情況使用」必須有嚴格的防錯機制，例如批次追溯系統確保若測試不合格時，使用了此材料的所有半成品可被快速識別和處置。

8.4.2.2.10 — CoA/CoC 的定期驗證

單純接受供應商的 CoA 不夠，還需要「定期驗證」。兩種可接受方式：

  • 方式一（完整測試）：定期自行或委託第三方對供應商材料進行完整測試，與 CoA 比對
  • 方式二（最低要求）：接受 CoA，但每批必須自行做至少一項專屬鑑別試驗（Identity Test）。例如橡膠配方的 FTIR 鑑別，確認材料是宣稱的那個。

「定期」的頻率應基於風險評估：新供應商或有問題記錄的供應商需要更高頻率驗證。

8.4.2.2.8 — 原料供應商變更 = 觸發變更控制

更換品質關鍵原材料的供應商，必須走完整的變更控制流程，包括：重新核准新供應商、比對新舊材料規格、評估對下游製程和產品品質的影響、必要時重新驗證，以及通知客戶和監管機構（若適用）。

這個要求的背景是歷史上多起因供應商悄悄換料導致的品質問題，例如配方微小調整改變了可萃取物（Extractables）特性。

8.4.2.2.3 — 廣泛的服務供應商管控範圍

8.4.2.2.3 列出的服務涵蓋範圍相當廣，強調「任何可能影響產品品質的外包服務」都需要管控：

8.4.3 Information for External Providers / 對外部提供者的資訊

原文內容 Original Text

The organization shall ensure the adequacy of requirements prior to their communication to the external provider.

The organization shall communicate to external providers its requirements for:

  1. the processes, products and services to be provided;
  2. the approval of:
    1. products and services;
    2. methods, processes and equipment;
    3. the release of products and services;
  3. competence, including any required qualification of persons;
  4. the external providers' interactions with the organization;
  5. control and monitoring of the external providers' performance to be applied by the organization;
  6. verification or validation activities that the organization, or its customer, intends to perform at the external providers' premises.

教學解說 Educational Commentary

採購資訊的完整性要求

「先確認要求的充分性，再傳達給供應商」— 這條款要求先做內部評估，確保採購規格是正確的和完整的，然後才發給供應商。常見缺失：

  • 規格書版本不是最新版
  • 遺漏了特定測試方法的說明
  • 沒有說明人員資格的最低要求
  • 沒有明確稽核權（客戶是否可到供應商廠稽核）

(f) 在供應商廠進行的驗證/確效

這條款特別重要：若計畫到供應商廠進行驗收測試、工廠驗收測試（FAT）或其他確效活動，必須事先在採購要求中明確告知供應商。

例如：委外實驗室的方法轉移確認（Method Transfer Validation），組織自己的 QC 人員到供應商廠進行現場確認。這個安排必須在合約/採購規格中事先說明。

採購資訊就像「施工說明書」

好的採購規格應讓供應商清楚知道：做什麼（產品/服務規格）、用什麼方法（核准的方法或設備）、誰來做（人員資格要求）、如何證明做好了（驗收標準和測試要求）、我會怎麼監督（績效監控方式）。模糊的採購規格是供應商品質問題的源頭之一。

  • 雙層架構理解：ISO 15378 第8節每個條款都建立在 ISO 9001:2015 基礎上，再加入藥用包材專屬的 GMP 要求。理解「為何需要這些額外要求」（因為最終影響患者安全）是學習本標準的核心思維。
  • 風險管理貫穿全程（8.1）：從規劃階段開始，風險管理必須融入整個產品實現過程，且須保存記錄。樣品保留需在適當條件下進行。
  • 文件化回饋系統（8.2.1）：要建立主動式早期預警機制，而非被動等待投訴。DMF 和 CoS 是保護商業機密同時支持法規申請的重要工具。廢棄包材的管控是防止藥品偽造的重要環節。
  • 設計開發需評估患者影響（8.3）：Review / Verification / Validation 三者目的不同但相互補充。設計變更必須評估對法規申請文件的影響，必要時需直接通報監管機構。
  • 供應商管控的 GMP 強化要求（8.4）：品質關鍵材料/服務的供應商必須由品質單位核准；委外任何生產製程需事先通知客戶；顧問也屬於供應商；委外實驗室需評估能力；進料需隔離至放行；CoA 接受時至少需做一項鑑別試驗；更換原料供應商需走變更控制。
  • 供應鏈責任不因外包而轉移：無論外包哪個環節，最終品質責任仍在組織。外包的品質關鍵製程必須符合 ISO 15378 全部要求。
核心洞察：ISO 15378 將藥品 GMP 的供應鏈管控思維前移到包材廠。包材廠不再只是「做好產品就好」，而是需要像藥廠一樣管理整個供應鏈、建立完整的文件系統、主動評估對患者安全的影響。這種思維轉變，是理解和實施 ISO 15378 的關鍵。

教育解說 / Commentary

Line Clearance 是什麼？為什麼重要？

換線清場（Line Clearance）是指在一個批次生產結束、下一個批次開始之前，對生產線進行全面確認，確保前一批次的任何材料、標籤、文件都已完全清除。

在包材廠，這包含：確認所有模具/工具已清除；確認標籤已移除；確認前批材料無殘留；完成清場記錄並由 QC 確認。

比喻：餐廳換桌

換線清場就像餐廳服務生在客人離開後必須重新整理桌子——確認前一桌的碗碟、餐具、剩食全部清除，才能讓下一桌入座。若只做一半（半清場），就必須有明確規則保證「不會把前一桌的東西遺留給下一桌」。

在製藥包材中，「前批次殘留」可能導致藥品被錯誤包裝、標籤混淆，是嚴重的 GMP 違規。

GMP 重點：自動換線不能免除風險評估

某些現代生產線採用自動化換線系統，號稱可縮短換線時間且不需全清場——但 ISO 15378 明確要求這類系統仍需經過書面風險評估，並建立控制措施。「自動化」不等於「不需驗證」。

木棧板的隱藏風險

8.5.1.3.14 特別提醒木棧板的污染風險：木材可能吸附化學品（例如防蟲劑、防腐劑），並在儲存或運輸過程中遷移至包材表面。許多高規格包材廠已改用塑膠棧板，或對木棧板實施嚴格的來源管控與檢驗。

Why Pharma Packaging Manufacturers Face Higher GMP Standards

為什麼藥品包材廠要承擔比一般包裝廠更高的 GMP 要求

The Unique Position of Primary Packaging Manufacturers

What Sets Pharma Packaging Apart

ISO 15378 exists because primary packaging materials come into direct contact with the medicinal product — they are the final barrier between the drug and the patient. Unlike secondary or tertiary packaging, primary packaging can directly affect:

  • Drug purity (contamination migration)
  • Drug stability (moisture, oxygen permeation)
  • Drug identity (label mix-ups)
  • Drug sterility (particulate contamination)

The ISO 15378 Layered Structure

Throughout this standard, every clause follows a predictable architecture:

  1. ISO 9001:2015 base text — the generic quality management requirement applicable to any manufacturing organization
  2. Additional GMP-related requirements — pharmaceutical-specific overlays that go beyond ISO 9001

Reading only the ISO 9001 base text gives an incomplete picture. The GMP additions are where the pharmaceutical regulatory requirements live.

教育解說 / Commentary

ISO 15378 的雙層架構邏輯

ISO 15378 刻意採用「ISO 9001 基礎條文 + GMP 附加要求」的結構，原因在於：

  • 讓已持有 ISO 9001 認證的製造商能清楚看到「升級差距」
  • 方便稽核員比對 ISO 9001 基礎合規 vs. 藥品特定要求
  • 使標準可隨 ISO 9001 改版同步更新（2017 版即對應 ISO 9001:2015）

實務上，一般包裝廠只需符合 ISO 9001；而藥品包材廠則必須同時滿足 ISO 9001 基礎條文 加上 所有附加 GMP 要求。

比喻：一般廚師 vs. 手術室備餐人員

一般包裝廠就像普通餐廳廚師——遵守食品衛生法規即可。但藥品包材廠更像為手術室備餐的人員——除了食品衛生，還必須符合無菌操作規範、穿著潔淨服、在受控環境中工作。

產品的最終用途（接觸患者的藥品）決定了製造環境必須達到的標準。

教育解說 / Commentary

哪些包材需要滅菌？

需要滅菌的一次包材主要包括：用於無菌注射劑的橡膠塞（Rubber Stoppers）、用於無菌粉末的管制玻璃瓶（Vials）組件、預充填注射器（Pre-filled Syringes）用注射器筒、以及某些醫療級袋子（IV Bags）等。

關鍵點：這些包材在出廠後會直接接觸無菌藥品，所以包材廠自身也必須確保包材達到規定的無菌保障水準（SAL, Sterility Assurance Level）。

滅菌驗證的三大參考標準

  • ISO 11135：環氧乙烷（EO）滅菌的驗證與常規控制
  • ISO 11137-1/-2：放射線滅菌（Gamma、E-beam）的驗證要求
  • ISO 14937：其他滅菌方法（例如蒸汽、乾熱）的驗證通用要求

每種滅菌方法都需要獨立的 IQ/OQ/PQ 驗證，並根據滅菌劑量、暴露時間、溫度/濕度等關鍵參數建立操作範圍。

比喻：醫院滅菌鍋的使用記錄

就像醫院高壓滅菌鍋（Autoclave）每次使用都必須記錄溫度、壓力、時間，並將記錄與特定批次的手術器械掛勾——ISO 15378 要求包材廠的滅菌過程記錄也必須與每批包材「一對一」可追溯。若日後發現某批藥品有污染問題，稽查人員可以回溯至對應的滅菌批次記錄。

外包滅菌：責任不能外包

8.5.1.4.3 特別規定：即使組織將滅菌程序外包給第三方滅菌服務商，組織仍需確保外包商的程序符合 ISO 15378 要求。這意味著包材廠必須：

  • 審核外包滅菌商的資格與驗證文件
  • 在合約中明確規定合規要求
  • 定期稽核外包商
  • 確保獲得每批次的滅菌記錄

「外包」不等於「免責」——這是 GMP 的基本原則。

教育解說 / Commentary

追溯性（Traceability）在包材廠的實踐

ISO 15378 要求從「原料來源」到「產品實現」的全鏈追溯。對包材廠而言，這表示：

  • 向上追溯：每批包材使用的原材料（樹脂、玻璃料、橡膠配方）批號
  • 生產追溯：使用的設備（含校正狀態）、操作員、時間戳
  • 向下追溯：該批包材出貨至哪家製藥廠、哪個批次

這種雙向追溯系統讓藥品召回時，可在數小時內確定受影響的包材批次範圍。

唯一批號（Unique Batch Reference）的重要性

8.5.2.2.2 要求每批生產記錄必須有唯一識別碼。這不僅是文件管理的便利性問題，而是法規要求。當製藥廠客戶對某批包材進行調查（例如懷疑是包材導致藥品不合格），包材廠必須能快速調出完整的批次生產記錄，包括：原料批號、設備狀態、操作員、環境監控數據等。

8.5.2.2.3：設備使用記錄的「時序性」要求

這個條款特別要求設備使用記錄必須包含「清潔和維護活動的時序」——即按照實際發生的時間順序記錄。這是因為：

若設備在某次生產後進行了緊急維修，然後立即投入下一批次生產，這個時序必須被記錄清楚。維修過程中是否可能引入污染物？維修後是否需要額外的清潔驗證？這些都需要追溯。

退回材料管理（8.5.2.2.4）

客戶退回的包材（例如品質問題退貨、調貨退回）必須與正常生產材料在物理上或系統上完全隔離，且必須有明確標識。這是防止「退回的不合格品被誤用於正常生產」的關鍵控制點。常見做法：設置專用退回材料暫存區（貼紅色「HOLD」標籤）+ ERP 系統隔離封存。

比喻：超市的 RFID 追蹤系統

現代超市用條碼/RFID 追蹤每個商品從供應商到貨架到收銀台的全過程——一旦發現問題食品，可在幾分鐘內確定哪些門店、哪些貨架有問題商品。ISO 15378 對包材的追溯要求原理相同，差別在於後果更嚴重：追蹤不到的包材批次可能導致藥品召回，甚至傷害患者。

8.5.3 客戶財產：包材廠常見的應用場景

在藥品包材業務中，「客戶財產」最常見的形式包括：

  • 客供模具：製藥廠提供的專屬包材模具，包材廠使用後必須妥善保管
  • 客供原材料：製藥廠提供特定配方橡膠或樹脂讓包材廠加工
  • 知識產權：客戶的包材設計圖紙、技術規格（保密義務）
  • 測試樣品：客戶送來用於相容性測試的藥品或藥液

任何客戶財產若發生損壞或遺失，必須立即通知客戶並保留書面記錄。

保存系統（8.5.4.2.1）：有效期管理

許多包材（尤其是橡膠密封件、預滅菌包材）有明確的保質期——例如滅菌有效期（Shelf Life of Sterile Products）通常為 12–24 個月。ISO 15378 要求：

  • 必須有書面程序追蹤有效期
  • 超期材料必須隔離並處置
  • 保質期限必須有數據支持（穩定性研究）

交貨文件必須批次對應（8.5.4.2.2）

「交貨文件必須批次特定（batch-specific）」是 GMP 中的關鍵要求。每次出貨的送貨單必須對應到具體批號，而非僅列出產品名稱和數量。這確保制藥廠在進料收貨時能將實物與批次記錄匹配，是端到端追溯鏈的一環。

重複使用包裝容器（8.5.4.2.3）

某些包材在配送時使用的週轉箱/容器可能被重複使用。ISO 15378 要求：若容器重複使用，舊標籤必須完全移除或塗銷（防止識別混淆），並按書面程序確認容器清潔。這看似微小的要求，實際上是防止「容器殘餘污染物」或「舊標籤引起識別錯誤」的重要控制。

教育解說 / Commentary

8.5.5 交付後活動：包材廠的義務範圍

「交付後活動」在包材廠情境中主要指：處理客戶投訴（交貨後發現品質問題）、提供技術支援（例如包材在客戶灌裝線的相容性問題）、保留樣品的長期管理、以及配合客戶進行已交付包材的法規調查或藥品召回。

ISO 15378 要求在設計這些活動範圍時，必須考慮「潛在的不良後果」——也就是包材問題可能對患者造成的影響，這是風險導向思維的體現。

變更控制（Change Control）：GMP 中最重要的系統之一

8.5.6.2 的四項 GMP 附加要求勾勒出一個完整的變更控制系統架構：

  • 觸發：任何可能影響產品品質的變更（原料供應商、設備、製程參數、廠房）
  • 評估：風險評估 + 判斷是否需要驗證/再驗證
  • 數據支持：需要生成支持性數據（如相容性測試、工程批次）
  • 批准：需要有授權職能（通常是 QA 部門）正式批准
  • 實施：在受控方式下執行，並通知客戶（若適用）

比喻：藥品製造商角度看包材變更

想像你是製藥廠的 QA 主管。你的橡膠塞供應商突然通知說他們更換了橡膠配方。這個變更會影響：橡膠的提取物/浸出物（Extractables/Leachables）、密封力、與藥液的相容性。

ISO 15378 要求包材廠在做這個變更前，必須先完成風險評估和必要的支持性數據，並通知客戶（製藥廠）。這就是為什麼許多藥品製造合同中要求「供應商未經批准不得擅自變更」。

8.5.6.2.4：誰有權批准變更？

「Defined functions（指定職能）」通常指 Quality Assurance（QA）部門。這確保了變更批准不會僅由工程或生產部門主導——品質部門必須參與並有最終批准權。實務上，許多公司建立了「變更控制委員會（Change Control Board）」跨部門審查重大變更。

8.6 Release of Products and Services

產品放行 — 批次文件審查與放行授權

ISO 15378:2017 — Original Text (p43)

8.6 Release of Products and Services (ISO 9001:2015 base)

The organization shall implement planned arrangements, at appropriate stages, to verify that the product and service requirements have been met.

The release of products and services to the customer shall not proceed until the planned arrangements have been satisfactorily completed, unless otherwise approved by a relevant authority and, as applicable, by the customer.

教育解說 / Commentary

批次放行（Batch Release）：包材廠的 QP 等效功能

在製藥廠，批次放行由合格人（Qualified Person, QP）授權。ISO 15378 為包材廠建立了類似機制——批次必須通過文件審查後才能放行。這個審查通常包括：

  • 批次生產記錄（Batch Production Record）完整性確認
  • 所有進料材料已通過進料檢驗
  • 製程中控制（In-Process Controls）數據在規格內
  • 最終檢驗（Final Inspection）結果符合規格
  • 偏差（Deviations）已調查並關閉或已評估影響

「放行前出貨」的允許條件

ISO 15378（透過 9.1.3.5）允許在「客戶協議」下進行批次放行前出貨（Shipment Prior to Batch Release）。這種安排在業界常稱為 Preliminary Release 或 Ship-to-Test，常用於緊急供貨場景。但這需要書面的客戶合約條款支持，且最終批次放行結論必須補完。

比喻：銀行的雙簽制度

批次放行就像銀行的大額轉帳需要雙重授權——即使所有文件都準備好了，也必須有授權人員的簽名確認，才能讓貨物「出閘」進入客戶手中。這個授權記錄（誰在何時批准了哪個批次）是事後稽查的關鍵證據。

8.7 Control of Nonconforming Outputs

不合格品管制 — 隔離、特許放行、處置三條路徑

ISO 15378:2017 — Original Text (p44)

8.7.1 (ISO 9001:2015 base)

The organization shall ensure that outputs that do not conform to their requirements are identified and controlled to prevent their unintended use or delivery.

The organization shall take appropriate action based on the nature of the nonconformity and its effect on the conformity of products and services. This shall also apply to nonconforming products and services detected after delivery of products, during or after the provision of services.

The organization shall deal with nonconforming outputs in one or more of the following ways:

  • a) correction;
  • b) segregation, containment, return or suspension of provision of products and services;
  • c) informing the customer;
  • d) obtaining authorization for acceptance under concession.

Conformity to the requirements shall be verified when nonconforming outputs are corrected.

8.7.2 (Documented Information)

The organization shall retain documented information that:

  • a) describes the nonconformity;
  • b) describes the actions taken;
  • c) describes any concessions obtained;
  • d) identifies the authority deciding the action in respect of the nonconformity.
教育解說 / Commentary

不合格品的三條路徑

發現不合格品後，ISO 15378 提供三種處理選項：

  • 矯正/返工（Correction/Rework）：修復後重新驗證是否符合規格（最常見）
  • 特許放行（Concession/Waiver）：在客戶書面授權下，接受不完全符合規格的材料（需記錄）
  • 銷毀/處置（Disposal/Destruction）：當無法矯正且客戶不接受特許時，按書面程序銷毀

特許放行（Concession）：必須客戶授權

特許放行是 GMP 中一個受嚴格管制的概念。ISO 15378 明確要求：任何不合格品的特許放行，必須由客戶書面授權，不能由包材廠單方面決定。

這反映了一個重要的責任分配原則：不合格品能否被接受使用，最終使用者（製藥廠）必須知情並同意，因為使用不合格包材的風險由製藥廠的最終產品承擔。

交付後發現的不合格品

8.7.1 特別涵蓋「交付後發現的不合格品」——即包材已到達客戶手中，甚至已用於藥品生產後，才發現問題。這種情況下，包材廠必須：迅速通知客戶、保留調查記錄、配合客戶評估影響範圍，並採取 CAPA。這直接連接到 Section 10 的客戶投訴處理程序。

比喻：汽車零件的召回流程

汽車零件供應商發現已交付的零件有缺陷時，不能自行決定「沒事」繼續出貨——必須立即通知汽車廠（客戶），並由汽車廠決定如何處置已安裝到車輛上的零件。ISO 15378 對包材的要求遵循相同邏輯。

Section 9 的三大支柱

ISO 15378 第 9 節建立了績效評估的完整架構，分三個主要層面：

  • 9.1 監測與評估：日常數據收集、OOS 調查、進料/製程/批次控制
  • 9.2 內部稽核：系統性定期稽核 QMS 合規性
  • 9.3 管理審查：高層定期評估 QMS 整體有效性

這三個層面形成一個由下而上的品質監控體系：日常數據 → 稽核發現 → 管理層決策。

客戶滿意度監測（9.1.2）在包材業務的實踐

對包材廠而言，客戶滿意度的關鍵指標通常包括：批次拒收率（Batch Rejection Rate）、客戶投訴數量與嚴重性、交期達成率（On-Time Delivery Rate）、品質偏差通知（Quality Notifications）的回應時間。

許多大型製藥廠客戶對供應商有定期的「Supplier Scorecard」評分系統，這些分數直接回饋到包材廠的客戶滿意度監測數據。

9.1

9.1.3 Analysis and Evaluation — OOS, Incoming Inspection & In-Process Controls

分析與評估 — 超規結果調查（OOS）、進料檢驗、製程中控制

ISO 15378:2017 — Original Text (p45–46)

9.1.3.1 General

The organization shall analyse and evaluate appropriate data and information arising from monitoring and measurement. The results of analysis shall be used to evaluate: conformity of products and services; degree of customer satisfaction; performance and effectiveness of the QMS; whether planning has been implemented effectively; effectiveness of actions taken to address risks and opportunities; performance of external providers; the need for improvements.

The quality unit(s) shall ensure that quality critical deviations are investigated, resolved and documented. The organization shall establish and maintain documented procedures, including requirements for the analysis of data, to identify existing or potential causes of nonconforming product or other quality problems.

9.1.3.2 Investigation of OOS Results

Any out-of-specification (OOS) result shall be investigated according to a documented procedure and the outcome recorded (see 7.5.3).

9.1.3.3 Incoming Inspection and Testing

Requirements shall be established and maintained for all materials used. Incoming materials shall be inspected or otherwise verified as conforming to specified requirements.

9.1.3.4 In-Process Controls

9.1.3.4.1 — The organization shall, as required by documented procedures, inspect and test the product during processing.

9.1.3.4.2 — Sampling procedures shall be defined to ensure that samples are representative of the process being assessed. Samples shall not be returned to the production area if removed to a separate testing location.

9.1.3.4.3 — Additional in-process controls shall be carried out after an equipment breakdown or an unscheduled interruption which stops the process.

教育解說 / Commentary

OOS 調查（9.1.3.2）：GMP 的強制要求

「超規結果（Out-of-Specification, OOS）調查」是美國 FDA 21 CFR Part 211 和 EU GMP 中的核心要求，ISO 15378 將其納入包材廠的義務範圍。OOS 調查的基本程序：

  • 第一步（實驗室調查）：確認是否為計算錯誤、儀器故障、操作員失誤等可辨識的實驗室錯誤
  • 第二步（製造調查）：若無法排除實驗室原因，擴大至生產過程調查
  • 結論：確認 OOS 有效性，採取 CAPA，決定批次處置

關鍵：OOS 調查必須有書面程序，且每次調查結果必須記錄歸檔。

進料檢驗（9.1.3.3）：包材廠的兩種方式

ISO 15378 要求對「所有使用材料」建立進料要求，並進行「檢驗或其他方式的符合性確認」。「其他方式」通常指：

  • 供應商釋放憑證（Certificate of Analysis, CoA）：對經批准的合格供應商，接受 CoA 代替每批檢驗（需有定期稽核做後盾）
  • 跳批抽樣（Skip-Lot Testing）：對歷史記錄良好的供應商，降低抽樣頻率（需書面驗證）

製程中控制的三個關鍵點

代表性樣品（9.1.3.4.2）：抽樣必須代表整個製程——例如在注塑成型中，每個模腔都可能有不同的製程特性，抽樣計劃必須覆蓋所有模腔。

樣品不得返回（9.1.3.4.2）：移出生產區的樣品不得送回——因為樣品可能在測試過程中受到污染或破損。

設備故障後的額外控制（9.1.3.4.3）：設備故障或非計劃停機後，必須進行額外的製程中控制，確認恢復生產後的品質穩定性。

比喻：品質管控層次金字塔

進料檢驗（9.1.3.3）是第一道防線；製程中控制（9.1.3.4）是製造過程中的即時監測；最終檢驗（9.1.3.5）是出貨前的最後確認。OOS 調查貫穿所有層次——任何一個層次發現超規，都必須觸發系統性調查，而不僅僅是「重測一次」。

9.1

9.1.3.5–9.1.3.7: Batch Release, Retained Samples & Process Data

批次放行、保留樣品與製程數據的開放原則

ISO 15378:2017 — Original Text (p47)

9.1.3.5 Batch Release

The organization shall implement an approval process for the batch release of products from the organization.

NOTE 1: Shipment prior to batch release can occur in accordance with a customer agreement.

If final inspection is a requirement, it shall be completed prior to batch release. Sampling procedures shall be defined to ensure that samples are representative of the batch being assessed. Samples shall not be returned to the production area if removed to a separate testing location.

A review of batch documentation shall be performed in order to release the batch.

NOTE 2: Final inspection might not include all specification parameters on basis of the control system and control strategy.

9.1.3.6 Retained Samples

Retained samples shall be taken in accordance with the organization's and/or customer's requirements.

9.1.3.7 Process Data

When required by the customer or their representative, production and control data related to the product (excluding the organization's confidential intellectual property) shall be made available for verification that the production process, in-process and final control and test equipment meet the requirements.

教育解說 / Commentary

批次放行：文件審查的核心

ISO 15378 強調批次放行必須包含批次文件審查（Review of Batch Documentation）。這不僅僅是確認最終檢驗通過，而是全面審查整個批次的生產歷史：

  • 原料進料記錄 + CoA
  • 生產批次記錄（設備、參數、操作員）
  • 製程中控制數據
  • 偏差記錄（若有）及其評估
  • 最終檢驗報告
  • 環境監控數據（若適用）

Note 2 的重要性：風險導向放行（RTRT）

9.1.3.5 的 NOTE 2 暗示了現代 GMP 中「即時放行測試（Real-Time Release Testing, RTRT）」的概念——最終檢驗不必測試所有規格參數，只要控制系統和控制策略可以保證品質。這與 ICH Q8/Q10 的製程分析技術（PAT）和 QbD 方法論一致。

保留樣品（Retained Samples）的用途

保留樣品是出貨批次的「時間膠囊」。在客戶日後提出投訴或出現問題時，保留樣品可用於：

  • 重新測試原批次品質（確認出廠時是否合格）
  • 進行客戶報告中描述問題的還原試驗
  • 藥品召回調查的參考依據

保留樣品的數量、儲存條件、保存期限通常由客戶合約或內部規程規定（通常為產品有效期限後至少 1 年）。

9.1.3.7 製程數據開放原則

客戶有權要求查閱生產和控制數據以驗證製程合規性——但「不包括組織的機密知識產權」。這在實務上需要仔細界定：哪些數據可以共享（如生產記錄、檢驗數據），哪些屬於商業機密（如配方配比、特殊製程技術）。供應商協議（Quality Technical Agreement）通常會事先約定數據共享範圍。

9.2 Internal Audit

內部稽核 — 頻率、方法、客觀性與後續行動

ISO 15378:2017 — Original Text (p47–48)

9.2.1 (ISO 9001:2015 base)

The organization shall conduct internal audits at planned intervals to provide information on whether the quality management system:

  • a) conforms to:
    • 1) the organization's own requirements for its quality management system;
    • 2) the requirements of this International Standard;
  • b) is effectively implemented and maintained.

9.2.2 (ISO 9001:2015 base)

The organization shall:

  • a) plan, establish, implement and maintain an audit programme(s) including the frequency, methods, responsibilities, planning requirements and reporting, which shall take into consideration the importance of the processes concerned, changes affecting the organization, and the results of previous audits;
  • b) define the audit criteria and scope for each audit;
  • c) select auditors and conduct audits to ensure objectivity and impartiality of the audit process;

教育解說 / Commentary

ISO 15378 內部稽核的雙重目的

9.2.1 清楚列出內部稽核的兩個目的：確認符合 ISO 15378 要求（合規確認），以及確認 QMS 是否有效實施和維持（有效性評估）。

許多組織的內部稽核只關注「有沒有這個程序」，而忽略「這個程序是否真正有效運作」。ISO 15378 要求兩者並重。

稽核程序計劃的四個考量維度（9.2.2.a）

制定年度內部稽核計劃時，必須考量：

  • 製程重要性：高風險製程（如滅菌、潔淨室生產）稽核頻率應更高
  • 組織變化：新廠房、新設備、人員異動後需要優先稽核
  • 歷史稽核結果：上次發現重大不合格的領域需要加強追蹤
  • 客戶稽核/法規檢查結果：外部稽核發現應納入內部稽核重點

客觀性與公正性（9.2.2.c）

稽核員不能稽核自己負責的工作領域——這是最基本的獨立性要求。在人數較少的組織中，這往往是一個挑戰，常見解決方法包括：交叉稽核（不同部門互相稽核）、聘請外部稽核員補充內部稽核、建立稽核員資格要求確保專業能力。

比喻：財務年度審計

內部稽核就像企業的內部財務審計——不是等到外部稽查才發現問題，而是主動、定期地自我檢查。差別在於：財務審計關注金流合規，GMP 內部稽核關注品質系統合規。兩者都需要獨立性，結果都需要報告給管理層，發現問題都需要在合理時間內糾正。

9.3 Management Review

管理審查 — 輸入項目、輸出決策與高層責任

ISO 15378:2017 — Original Text (p48–49)

9.3.1 General (ISO 9001:2015 base)

Top management shall review the organization's quality management system, at planned intervals, to ensure its continuing suitability, adequacy, effectiveness and alignment with the strategic direction of the organization.

9.3.2 Management Review Inputs

The management review shall be planned and carried out taking into consideration:

  • a) the status of actions from previous management reviews;
  • b) changes in external and internal issues relevant to the QMS;
  • c) information on the performance and effectiveness of the QMS, including trends in:
    • 1) customer satisfaction and feedback;
    • 2) the extent to which quality objectives have been met;
    • 3) process performance and conformity;

教育解說 / Commentary

管理審查：高層的 QMS 年度健康檢查

管理審查（Management Review）是高層管理者定期評估整個 QMS 是否仍然適合、充分且有效的正式機制。注意：這不是品質部門的事——「Top management（最高管理層）」必須親自參與。

對包材廠而言，管理審查通常每年至少進行一次（大型或複雜組織可能半年一次），並產生書面的管理審查記錄和行動計劃。

9.3.2(g) 訓練有效性：ISO 15378 的附加項目

值得注意的是，ISO 15378 在管理審查輸入中加入了 g) 「訓練有效性（effectiveness of training）」——這是比 ISO 9001:2015 基礎版本多的一個輸入項目，反映了 GMP 對人員能力和訓練的高度重視。管理層需要評估：訓練計劃是否真的提升了員工能力，而不只是紙面上的訓練時數達標。

輸入 vs. 輸出：管理審查的邏輯閉環

ISO 15378 對管理審查採用 PDCA 邏輯：

  • 輸入（9.3.2）：收集過去一年的品質績效數據
  • 審查（9.3.1）：最高管理層評估數據、識別問題和機會
  • 輸出（9.3.3）：做出決策——需要哪些改善？需要什麼資源？需要哪些 QMS 變更？

輸出必須包含具體決策和行動，不能只是「討論了一下，沒什麼大問題」。下次管理審查的第一個輸入項目（9.3.2.a）就是追蹤上次決議的執行狀況。

比喻：董事會年度報告

管理審查的結構與企業董事會年度報告類似——回顧上一年的表現（財務指標 → 品質指標），評估內外部環境變化，決定來年的資源投入和戰略調整。差別在於：管理審查的「指標」是品質績效數據（不合格率、客訴數量、稽核發現），而「戰略調整」是 QMS 改善計劃。

Section 10: Improvement — Nonconformity, CAPA & Continual Improvement

第10節：改善 — 不符合項、矯正預防行動（CAPA）與持續改善

ISO 15378:2017 — Original Text (p50–51)

10.1 General (ISO 9001:2015 base)

The organization shall determine and select opportunities for improvement and implement any necessary actions to meet customer requirements and enhance customer satisfaction. These shall include:

  • a) improving products and services to meet requirements as well as to address future needs and expectations;
  • b) correcting, preventing or reducing undesired effects;
  • c) improving the performance and effectiveness of the quality management system.

10.2 Nonconformity and Corrective Action

10.2.1 (ISO 9001:2015 base) — When a nonconformity occurs, including any arising from complaints, the organization shall:

  • a) react to the nonconformity: 1) take action to control and correct it; 2) deal with the consequences;
  • b) evaluate the need for action to eliminate the cause(s): 1) reviewing and analysing the nonconformity; 2) determining the causes; 3) determining if similar nonconformities exist, or could potentially occur;
  • c) implement any action needed;
  • d) review the effectiveness of any corrective action taken;
  • e) update risks and opportunities determined during planning, if necessary;
  • f) make changes to the quality management system, if necessary.

Corrective actions shall be appropriate to the effects of the nonconformities encountered.

10.2.2 — The organization shall retain documented information as evidence of: the nature of the nonconformities and any subsequent actions taken; the results of any corrective action.

教育解說 / Commentary

Section 10 的三個層次

ISO 15378 第 10 節的改善框架有三個遞進層次：

  • 矯正（Correction）：針對已發生問題的即時修復（治標）
  • 矯正行動（Corrective Action）：消除不符合的根本原因，防止再發（治本）
  • 持續改善（Continual Improvement）：主動識別並實施提升機會，即使目前沒有問題（超越合規）

GMP 要求的是三個層次都必須有，而不是只有矯正。

CAPA 的六步驟閉環（10.2.1）

10.2.1 實際上描述了完整的 CAPA 閉環流程：

  1. 即時反應：控制並矯正不符合，處理後果
  2. 根本原因分析：不只是「發生了什麼」，要找到「為什麼發生」
  3. 橫向延伸：確認類似問題是否在其他地方存在或可能發生
  4. 實施行動：執行根本原因消除措施
  5. 有效性確認：驗證矯正行動真的有效（最常被忽略的步驟）
  6. 更新 QMS：若必要，更新風險評估、程序文件或 QMS
10.2.3 — Additional GMP-Related Requirements (p51) 10.2.3 — 額外的 GMP 相關要求（p51）
ISO 15378:2017 — Original Text

The following additional GMP-related requirements apply to nonconformity and corrective action:

  • a) Nonconforming material or products shall be quarantined pending determination of corrective or other actions. When considering correction via rework or reconditioning, a risk assessment of any adverse effect of the reworking on the products shall be performed and recorded.
  • b) Rework and/or reconditioning shall be in accordance with a documented procedure that has been approved by the quality unit(s). The rework procedure shall be agreed with the customer, where this is a specified requirement.
  • c) If primary packaging material has been produced under cleanroom conditions, any rework shall be carried out under the same conditions.
  • d) Any proposal to release nonconforming product shall be via a documented concession, authorized by the customer.
  • e) Following rejection, primary packaging materials shall be disposed of or destroyed in accordance with a documented procedure.
  • f) The organization shall establish and maintain documented procedures for the analysis of data, to identify existing or potential causes of nonconforming product or other quality problems.
  • g) The organization shall investigate all customer complaints in a timely manner and communicate identified corrective action to all production and production-related sites. Action(s) shall be implemented as soon as practical and to an agreed timetable; records of investigation shall be maintained.
  • h) Customer complaints not followed by corrective and/or preventive action shall be justified and also recorded.
教育解說 / Commentary

10.2.3(c)：潔淨室返工的強制要求

這是 ISO 15378 中一個非常具體且嚴格的要求：如果包材是在潔淨室環境下生產的，返工也必須在相同的潔淨室條件下進行。

這是因為：潔淨室生產的包材（例如預滅菌器具）的品質保證依賴於潔淨環境。若在普通環境下返工，即使包材外觀修復，潔淨度可能已受損，不能作為替代。

10.2.3(d)：特許放行必須客戶書面授權

此條款與 8.7.1(d) 呼應，再次強調：不合格品的特許放行（Concession）必須由客戶書面授權。「我們內部評估認為可以接受」不夠——客戶必須知情並授權。

這在供應鏈中具有重要意義：客戶（製藥廠）有義務在接受特許放行後，將此資訊反映在其自身的批次記錄中，並評估對最終藥品品質的影響。

10.2.3(g)：客戶投訴處理的時效要求

「及時（timely manner）」和「盡快可行（as soon as practical）」是 ISO 15378 的措辭。雖然沒有硬性規定具體天數，但 GMP 行業慣例通常是：初步確認回覆 24–48 小時內；初步調查報告 5–10 個工作日；最終 CAPA 報告 30 天內。這些時限通常會在客戶與供應商的品質技術協議（QTA）中明確規定。

10.2.3(h)：「不行動」也需要理由

這是一個常被忽略的要求：即使組織判斷某個客戶投訴不需要採取矯正或預防行動，這個判斷本身也必須書面記錄並說明理由。不能簡單地「關閉投訴記錄」而不留下處置決定的依據。

持續改善 — 超越合規，建立品質文化

ISO 15378:2017 — Original Text (p51)

10.3 Continual Improvement (ISO 9001:2015 base)

The organization shall continually improve the suitability, adequacy and effectiveness of the quality management system.

教育解說 / Commentary

持續改善 vs. 矯正行動的本質差異

許多組織混淆了「矯正行動（Corrective Action）」和「持續改善（Continual Improvement）」：

  • 矯正行動：由已發生的不符合觸發，目的是消除根本原因，防止再發——這是被動的
  • 持續改善：主動識別提升機會，即使目前沒有問題——這是主動的

ISO 15378 要求組織建立的是持續改善文化，而不只是「有問題才修」。

持續改善的變更也需要風險管理

10.3 最後一句非常重要：「持續改善所提出的變更必須接受風險管理（risk management）。」

這意味著：即使是「出於好意的改善」，也可能引入未預見的風險。例如，為了提高生產效率而修改生產流程，可能在無意中影響包材品質。每一個改善提案都必須經過風險評估，並連接到 8.5.6 的變更控制程序。

比喻：精益生產（Lean）+ 品質管理的結合

持續改善的概念源自日本的「改善（Kaizen）」文化——每天都在尋找小的改進機會。在製藥包材業，這意味著：不只等到客戶投訴才改變，而是主動分析製程數據（SPC 統計製程管制）、找出正在漂移但仍在規格內的趨勢，在問題爆發前就採取預防行動。

這就是 CAPA 中的 PA（Preventive Action） 的本質——預防，而非僅矯正。

本節核心洞見 / Core Insights

洞見 1：責任不可外包

貫穿本節的一個主題是：無論滅菌驗證、特許放行、還是返工——責任永遠在組織本身，不會因為外包或客戶授權而消失。包材廠是品質鏈上的一個關鍵節點，其品質決策對下游製藥廠和最終患者都有直接影響。

洞見 2：ISO 15378 的 GMP 附加要求都指向同一件事

仔細審視 Section 8–10 的所有 GMP 附加要求，可以發現它們都指向同一目標：確保每一批交到製藥廠手中的包材，都有完整、可追溯的品質保證記錄，讓製藥廠可以相信這批材料不會危害患者。

這就是為什麼 ISO 15378 比普通的 ISO 9001 難以通過認證——它要求的不只是「有程序」，而是「有效執行的、有記錄的、可稽查的品質保證系統」。

洞見 3：Section 9 和 10 形成閉環

Section 9（績效評估）收集數據 → Section 10（改善）利用數據驅動行動。這個閉環是 ISO 管理體系 PDCA 邏輯的體現：

  • P（計劃）：Sections 6–8 建立策略和流程
  • D（執行）：Section 8 的操作要求
  • C（檢查）：Section 9 的績效評估
  • A（行動）：Section 10 的改善措施

Annex A (Informative) — Clarification of New Structure, Terminology and Concepts

Original Text (ISO 15378:2017)

This Annex is reproduced from ISO 9001:2015, Annex A. It provides informative clarification of the new structure, terminology and concepts introduced in the 2015 revision.

The International Standards described in this annex explain why the clause structure and some of the terminology of ISO 9001:2015 — and by extension ISO 15378:2017 — have been changed to improve alignment with other management systems standards.

There is no requirement in this International Standard for its structure and terminology to be applied to the documented information of an organization's quality management system. 為什麼 ISO 15378 包含 ISO 9001 的附錄？

ISO 15378 的正文是「建立在 ISO 9001:2015 之上，加入製藥包裝的特殊要求」。因此 Annex A 和 B 直接來自 ISO 9001:2015，幫助 ISO 15378 使用者理解底層框架的邏輯。

換句話說：讀懂 Annex A = 讀懂 ISO 9001:2015 的設計哲學。

標準明確說明：組織不需要把內部文件全部改成 ISO 術語。例如製藥廠習慣用的 "SOP"、"batch record"、"specification" 都可以保留，只要其功能符合標準要求即可。

解說 Commentary

術語改變背後的邏輯

ISO 2015 版的術語變動不只是換名字，而是反映了 QMS 哲學的轉移：

  • Products → Products and services：服務型組織（如 CRO、CDMO）被明確納入範圍
  • Supplier → External provider：更廣義，涵蓋合約研究機構、外包服務商
  • Documented information：統合了四個概念（文件 / 程序 / 記錄 / 品質手冊），減少文件管理的行政負擔
  • Management representative 取消：責任分散到各職能，而非指定一人

製藥稽核員的關鍵對照

EU GMP Annex 15 和 FDA 21 CFR Part 211 仍使用舊術語（如 "procedures"、"records"、"supplier"）。稽核時需理解對照關係：

  • FDA "records" = ISO "retain documented information"
  • FDA/EU "procedures" = ISO "maintain documented information"
  • GMP "supplier qualification" = ISO "control of external providers"

理解這個對照表，可以避免在跨稽核時產生不必要的混淆。

比喻：從「分類夾」到「知識庫」

2008 版像是有五個不同的文件夾（SOP 夾、記錄夾、品質手冊、計劃書...），每個夾子有不同的管理規則。2015 版說：全部都叫「文件化資訊」，你自己決定要「維持（maintain）」還是「保留（retain）」哪些，重點是符合目的。

A.2 Products and Services

為何明確加入「服務」概念 | p52

Original Text

ISO 9001:2008 used the term "product" to include all output categories. This edition of this International Standard uses "products and services". "Products and services" include all output categories (hardware, services, software and processed materials).

The specific inclusion of "services" is intended to highlight the differences between products and services in the application of some requirements. The characteristic of services is that at least part of the output is realized at the interface with the customer. This means, for example, that conformity to requirements cannot necessarily be confirmed before service delivery.

In most cases, products and services are used together. Most outputs that organizations provide to customers, or are supplied to them by external providers, include both products and services. For example, a tangible or intangible product can have some associated service or a service can have some associated tangible or intangible product.

解說 Commentary

對初級包裝材料廠的影響

ISO 15378 的對象是製藥初級包裝材料製造商（如玻璃瓶、注射器、塑膠瓶蓋）。這些廠商除了供應實體產品，往往也提供：

  • 技術支援（材料相容性測試建議）
  • 包材設計服務
  • 客製化開發協議

2015 版明確將這些「伴隨服務」納入 QMS 管轄範圍。

服務特性 vs 產品特性

服務的關鍵差異：交付前無法完全驗證合規性（因為服務在「交付當下」才完成）。這與 GMP 的出廠放行邏輯不同，要求在設計階段就確保服務品質，而非僅靠事後檢查。

A.4 Risk-Based Thinking

風險思維 — 取代「預防措施」條款的設計邏輯 | p53–p54

Original Text

The concept of risk-based thinking has been implicit in previous editions of this International Standard, e.g. through requirements for planning, review and improvement. This International Standard specifies requirements for the organization to understand its context (see 4.1) and determine risks as a basis for planning (see 6.1). This represents the application of risk-based thinking to planning and implementing quality management system processes (see 4.4) and will assist in determining the extent of documented information.

One of the key purposes of a quality management system is to act as a preventive tool. Consequently, this International Standard does not have a separate clause or subclause on preventive action. The concept of preventive action is expressed through the use of risk-based thinking in formulating quality management system requirements.

The risk-based thinking applied in this International Standard has enabled some reduction in prescriptive requirements and their replacement by performance-based requirements. There is greater flexibility than in ISO 9001:2008 in the requirements for processes, documented information and organizational responsibilities.

Although 6.1 specifies that the organization shall plan actions to address risks, there is no requirement for formal methods for risk management or a documented risk management process. Organizations can decide whether or not to develop a more extensive risk management methodology than is required by this International Standard, e.g. through the application of other guidance or standards.

Not all the processes of a quality management system represent the same level of risk in terms of the organization's ability to meet its objectives, and the effects of uncertainty are not the same for all organizations. Under the requirements of 6.1, the organization is responsible for its application of risk-based thinking and the actions it takes to address risk, including whether or not to retain documented information as evidence of its determination of risks.

解說 Commentary

為什麼 2015 版取消「預防措施」條款？

ISO 9001:2008 的 Clause 8.5.3 是「預防措施（Preventive Action）」，要求識別潛在不符合並採取措施。但在實踐中，這個條款常被機械式執行（填表、開 PA，但沒有真正的風險分析）。

2015 版的做法更根本：把風險思維內建到整個 QMS 框架，特別是：

  • 4.1：理解組織情境
  • 4.2：識別相關方需求
  • 6.1：規劃時識別風險與機會

結果：QMS 本身就是一個持續性的「預防機制」，而非有一個獨立的預防措施表單系統。

比喻：從「滅火器」到「防火設計」

舊版的預防措施像是在每個房間放滅火器（事後補救思維）。新版的風險思維像是從建築設計階段就規劃逃生動線、防火材料、灑水系統——把安全內建到結構裡，而不是靠工具補充。

與 ICH Q9 風險管理的關係

標準明確說：不要求正式的風險管理方法論或文件化的風險管理流程。這與 ICH Q9 的正式 FMEA/HACCP 方法不同——ISO 15378 的風險思維是「態度與判斷」，ICH Q9 是「工具與方法」。兩者互補，ISO 15378 不取代 ICH Q9。

對製藥包裝廠的實際影響

風險思維帶來的彈性包括：

  • 製程風險低的包材（如紙箱）文件化要求可較簡單
  • 直接接觸藥品的包材（如注射器柱塞）需要更嚴格的文件化
  • 組織可自主決定哪些風險評估需要保留記錄，哪些口頭確認即可

A.5 Applicability

適用性 — 取代舊版「豁免（Exclusions）」概念 | p54

Original Text

This International Standard does not refer to "exclusions" in relation to the applicability of its requirements to the organization's quality management system. However, an organization can review the applicability of requirements due to:

  • the size or complexity of the organization
  • the management model it adopts
  • the range of the organization's activities
  • the nature of the risks and opportunities it encounters

The requirements for applicability are addressed in 4.3, which defines conditions under which an organization can decide that a requirement cannot be applied to any of the processes within the scope of its quality management system.

解說 Commentary

「豁免」vs「不適用」的本質差異

2008 版允許在品質手冊中聲明「豁免（exclusion）」特定條款——但這導致一些組織直接略過與其業務相關的要求。

2015 版改為：沒有「豁免」這個詞。你只能說某條款「不適用於你的 QMS 範疇」，且前提是這個決定不會影響產品/服務的合規性。

稽核實務關鍵點

當被稽核方聲稱某條款「不適用」時，稽核員應確認：

  1. 是否有書面理由說明為何不適用
  2. 該決定是否影響產品合規性
  3. 是否在 QMS 範疇聲明（scope statement）中有所說明

這與舊版「品質手冊列豁免章節」的做法在形式和精神上都有所不同。

A.6 Documented Information

文件化資訊 — 統合文件、程序、記錄、品質手冊 | p54

Original Text

As part of the alignment with other management system standards, a common clause on "documented information" has been adopted without significant change or addition (see 7.5). Consequently, "documented information" is used for all document requirements.

Where ISO 9001:2008 used specific terminology such as "document" or "documented procedures", "quality manual" or "quality plan", this edition of this International Standard defines requirements to "maintain documented information".

Where ISO 9001:2008 used the term "records" to denote documents needed to provide evidence of conformity with requirements, this is now expressed as a requirement to "retain documented information". The organization is responsible for determining what documented information needs to be retained, the period of time for which it is to be retained and the media to be used for its retention.

A requirement to "maintain" documented information does not exclude the possibility that the organization might also need to "retain" that same documented information for a particular purpose, e.g. to retain previous versions of it.

Where this International Standard refers to "information" rather than "documented information" (e.g. in 4.1), there is no requirement that this information is to be documented. In such situations, the organization can decide whether or not it is necessary or appropriate to maintain documented information.

解說 Commentary

品質手冊去哪了？

ISO 9001:2015 正式取消了對「品質手冊」的強制要求。組織仍可選擇維持品質手冊，但這不再是標準的必要要求。對於已取得 ISO 15378 認證並有品質手冊的組織，只需確保其涵蓋範疇與標準要求的 documented information 一致即可。

比喻：食譜 vs 烹飪記錄

Maintain = 廚師手冊（食譜）：要保持最新版本，有人用時取出，修改後更新，舊版可以丟棄。Retain = 每日餐廳出菜記錄：每天的記錄都要保留，作為食安稽查的證明，不能任意處置。

組織知識 — 2015 版新增要求 (7.1.6) | p54–p55

Original Text

In 7.1.6, this International Standard addresses the need to determine and manage the knowledge maintained by the organization, to ensure the operation of its processes and that it can achieve conformity of products and services.

Requirements regarding organizational knowledge were introduced for the purpose of:

a) safeguarding the organization from loss of knowledge, e.g.

  • through staff turnover;
  • failure to capture and share information;

b) encouraging the organization to acquire knowledge, e.g.

  • learning from experience;
  • mentoring;
  • benchmarking.
解說 Commentary

組織知識 — 2015 版全新要求

這是 ISO 9001:2015 相比 2008 版最具創新性的新增要求之一。Clause 7.1.6 要求組織：

  1. 確定所需的組織知識
  2. 維持這些知識（並提供給需要的人）
  3. 當面對新需求時，考慮如何獲取知識

製藥廠常見的知識流失風險

對初級包裝材料製造商，以下情境需要特別注意：

  • 資深製程工程師離職 → 特殊材料加工技術失傳
  • 客戶規格歷史沿革未記錄 → 下次認證無法溯源決策依據
  • 稽核 finding 的根因分析結果未系統化 → 同類問題重複發生
  • 供應商變更評估的歷史資料散落各部門

比喻：組織記憶 vs 個人記憶

如果關鍵知識只存在某位員工的腦袋裡，那不叫「組織知識」，那叫「人質風險」。7.1.6 的本質是要求把「人的知識」轉化為「組織的知識」。

A.8 Control of Externally Provided Processes, Products and Services

外部提供之製程、產品與服務的管控 (8.4) | p55

Original Text

All forms of externally provided processes, products and services are addressed in 8.4, e.g. whether through:

  • a) purchasing from a supplier;
  • b) an arrangement with an associate company;
  • c) outsourcing processes to an external provider.

Outsourcing always has the essential characteristic of a service, since it will have at least one activity necessarily performed at the interface between the provider and the organization.

The controls required for external provision can vary widely depending on the nature of the processes, products and services. The organization can apply risk-based thinking to determine the type and extent of controls appropriate to particular external providers and externally provided processes, products and services.

解說 Commentary

外部提供的三種形式

ISO 15378 在此擴展了管控對象，從「採購」延伸到更廣義的「所有形式的外部提供」：

  • 採購（Purchasing）：向供應商購買原料、零件（傳統供應商管理）
  • 關聯公司安排：集團內跨廠區提供製程或服務
  • 外包（Outsourcing）：將某一製程委託外部機構執行

風險思維決定管控程度

並非所有外部提供都需要相同強度的管控。風險導向評估：

  • 直接接觸藥品的包材（如注射用玻璃瓶）→ 嚴格的供應商稽核、材料認證
  • 辦公室清潔服務外包 → 合約約定即可，無需 GMP 稽核
  • 外包滅菌製程 → 接近 GMP 等級管控，需稽核能力

這種比例原則的管控邏輯，與 ICH Q10 的 Quality Systems 精神一致。

Annex A Key Takeaways

  • Terminology mapping: "Supplier" = "External provider"; "Records" = "Retain documented information"; "Documents/Procedures" = "Maintain documented information"

  • Risk-based thinking: Replaces the standalone preventive action clause; embedded throughout planning (4.1, 4.2, 6.1) — the entire QMS becomes the preventive mechanism
  • Documented information: "Maintain" = living documents (SOPs, specs); "Retain" = evidence records (batch records, test reports)
  • Organizational knowledge (7.1.6): New requirement to capture and protect knowledge from staff turnover and to acquire new knowledge through mentoring, benchmarking, experience
  • External provision (8.4): Risk-proportionate controls covering purchasing, associate arrangements, and outsourcing

Annex B Key Takeaways

  • ISO/TC 176 developed 17+ supporting standards that complement ISO 9001/15378 — none of them add mandatory requirements
  • Core three: ISO 9000 (vocabulary), ISO 9001 (requirements), ISO 9004 (excellence beyond compliance)
  • ISO 19011: The go-to guide for audit programme management — critical for supplier audits in pharmaceutical supply chains
  • ISO 10002: Customer complaint handling guidance — aligns with FDA and EU GMP complaint requirements
  • ISO 10015: Training guidelines — directly supports Clause 7.2 competence requirements
  • Table B.1 provides a navigation map: use it to find the right supporting standard when improving a specific QMS clause area

Annex C (Normative) — GMP Requirements for Printed Primary Packaging Materials

附錄C（規範性）：印刷初級包材的GMP要求 | p60-p63

Annex C — Introduction & Status

This annex sets out GMP requirements for printed primary packaging materials. It is normative — meaning its requirements are mandatory and binding on organizations that manufacture printed packaging materials covered by this standard.

The annex covers three main areas:

  • C.1 — Artwork/origination and print impression media controls
  • C.2 — Print and conversion processes (including digital printing)
  • C.3 — Security code systems and reel material controls

解說：為什麼附錄C特別重要？

規範性（Normative）= 強制性

ISO標準中，附錄分為兩類：

  • Normative（規範性）：與正文條款同等效力，必須遵守
  • Informative（資訊性）：提供指導，非強制

附錄C是規範性的 — 所有製造印刷初級包材的組織均必須遵守。這與附錄D（資訊性的驗證指引）形成對比。

患者安全核心

印刷包材上的文字直接影響患者用藥安全：錯誤的劑量說明、過期日期格式錯誤、或與其他產品混裝，都可能造成嚴重後果。附錄C的每項要求都指向同一個目標：確保「正確的標籤裝在正確的產品上」。

C.1

C.1 Artwork/Origination and Print Impression Media

藝術稿件／原版與印刷壓印媒介的管控

C.1.1 General — Artwork File Control

C.1.1.1 Artwork/origination files shall be named and stored according to a documented procedure that enables them to be readily identified, issue controlled and traceable.

C.1.1.2 All print impression media shall be:

  • a) clearly and uniquely identified such that it is traceable to the origination material,
  • b) produced from, and traceable to, the master origination material held by the customer,
  • c) verified against the customer approved hard copy or electronic data and recorded (see 7.5.3), and
  • d) stored in a secure area within a defined system for authorized issue and return to store.

解說：印版追溯鏈

追溯鏈示意

ISO 15378要求從客戶主版到生產印版的完整追溯鏈：

客戶核准原版 (Master Origination)
↓ 複製並驗證
印版/印筒 (Print Plate/Cylinder)
↓ 唯一識別碼
生產用印版（授權發出/回收）

每一層都必須文件化記錄，確保生產中使用的印版與客戶核准設計完全一致。

類比：銀行的簽章樣本管理

就像銀行管理印章一樣：有「主印章」（客戶原版），開行後複製「作業印章」（生產印版）。每次借出/歸還都要登記，廢棄舊印章時要銷毀並記錄。印刷包材的印版管理邏輯完全相同。

C.1.2 Multiple Plates/Cylinders

Where more than one printing plate/cylinder is required, there shall be a documented system for ensuring that all plates/cylinders within the set are used.

Where a set of plates/cylinders contains the generic design for several jobs, each individual plate/cylinder within the set shall be clearly, uniquely identified and documented.

解說：多色印刷的套版控制

多色印刷的風險

藥品包材通常需要多色印刷（例如：黑色文字版、藍色色塊版、紅色警示版）。若某一色版遺失或使用錯誤版本，可能造成：

  • 資訊缺失（如缺少劑量說明）
  • 顏色辨識錯誤（顏色是藥品識別的重要元素）
  • 不同批次版本混用

因此，每套印版必須整組管理，確保「全套齊全」才能上機生產。

C.1.3 Copy/Design Change

Where a design requires several plates/cylinders and some of them are to be replaced because of a copy/design change, there shall be a documented procedure to allow for the replacement of the affected plate(s)/cylinder(s) and the retention of the other media within the set.

The original plates/cylinders shall be subject to a procedure that allows re-identification.

解說：版本變更的複雜性

部分變更的挑戰

設計變更時，不一定所有色版都需要更換。例如：只更新有效期格式（文字版變更），色塊版保持不變。此時需要：

  • 明確記錄哪些版更換、哪些保留
  • 對舊版重新標識（避免誤用於新設計）
  • 新舊版本不得在同一套版中混用

C.1.4 Verification, Quarantine and Destruction

C.1.4.1 General — Verification of the design on print impression media shall be carried out during the printing machine make-ready and before the approval to run the product is given.

C.1.4.2 Quarantine and destruction — The organization shall have:

  • a) a documented procedure which ensures that origination and print media, for a design undergoing revision, are subject to formal quarantine, and
  • b) a documented system detailing the method for disposal of the unwanted origination and print impression media; such items shall be rendered unusable and disposed of in a controlled and secure manner.

解說：預防「舊版重出江湖」

廢棄印版的風險

如果廢棄的印版未被銷毀，可能被意外拿出使用，生產出帶有過期設計的包材。「渲染為不可用」（rendered unusable）是關鍵要求：

  • 金屬版：物理毀形（彎折、穿孔）
  • 電子檔：正式刪除並記錄
  • 感光底片：剪斷或化學處理

隔離期間的版材必須有清楚的隔離標識，防止誤用。

開機驗證（Make-Ready Verification）

每次開機前必須驗證印版設計是否正確。這不是一次性的事件 — 換版後、換班後、生產中斷復工後都需要重新確認。驗證結果必須記錄（7.5.3條款）。

C.2

C.2 Print and Conversion Processes

印刷及轉換製程的GMP要求 | 開機準備、換版、留樣、幫派印刷

C.2.1 Print Machine Set-Up (Make-Ready)

C.2.1.1 Initial print make-ready shall be performed using unprinted components or material.

C.2.1.2 Make-ready for subsequent processes may use material from the initial print process of the same batch.

C.2.1.3 Initial make-ready material may be reused during the make-ready process in order to achieve correct colour.

C.2.1.4 Material used for make-ready shall be segregated and then disposed of as production waste.

解說：開機準備廢料管理

Make-Ready（試印）的目的

每次印刷機開機時，需要「試印」若干數量才能達到顏色穩定、套版精準。這些試印品（make-ready material）不能混入正式批次，必須：

  • 明確標識為「廢料」
  • 物理隔離存放
  • 作為生產廢棄物處置

前道工序（如印刷）的試印品可用於後道工序（如燙金、裁切）的試機，但同樣必須在後道工序結束後作廢料處置。

C.2.2 Changeover Systems

Changeover systems that are designed to reduce make-ready time (e.g. automated plate changing), and which do not permit a total line clearance, shall be subject to a documented risk assessment and operated with controls to ensure product security.

All print media from the previous job shall be removed from the line prior to formal approval of the print run being given.

All controls shall be recorded (see 7.5.3).

解說：自動換版的風險

自動化 ≠ 免於GMP風險

自動換版系統可以大幅縮短換版時間，但如果系統設計不允許進行「全線清場」（total line clearance），就必須：

  • 進行文件化風險評估（documented risk assessment）
  • 建立專項控制措施確保不發生產品混淆
  • 確認前一工單的所有印版已從生產線移除
  • 記錄所有控制措施的執行結果

關鍵原則：技術便利性不能以犧牲產品安全性為代價。風險評估是強制性的。

C.2.3 Retained Samples

C.2.3.1 All in-process printed samples which are to be retained shall be clearly identified and securely stored.

C.2.3.2 Samples used for other purposes (e.g. administration/sales) shall be voided if they leave the control of the organization.

解說：樣品的雙重管理

兩類留樣，兩種處理

「作廢」通常意味著在樣品上印上/蓋上「SAMPLE」或「VOID」字樣，使其無法被誤用為正式包材。這防止了樣品流入市場或被誤裝藥品。

C.2.4 Replacement Print Media

C.2.4.1 During a production run, if replacement plates are made from an existing fixed approved source (e.g. negatives, or using computer-to-plate technology from an existing stepped image), the job may be continued after a new "first off" check has been carried out.

C.2.4.2 During a production run, if plates are created from a new source (e.g. re-stepping a "one-up" image), the existing job shall be lifted; the replacement plates shall be treated as new origination. Subsequent production shall be treated as a new batch. The introduction of all replacement print media shall be recorded (see 7.5.3).

解說：生產中換版的批次判定

兩種換版情境對比

邏輯：來源的可追溯性決定了批次的連續性。新來源意味著新的設計來源，必須重新驗證，因此必須開新批次。

C.2.5 Gang Printing

Gang printing (the process of printing more than one design on a substrate at one production run) is recognized and classified as an acute contributory risk relating to admixtures.

Therefore this shall only be permitted:

  • in agreement with the customer, and
  • on completion of a documented risk assessment to evaluate and mitigate the risk of cross-contamination (see 8.2.1).

解說：幫派印刷 — 效率與患者安全的衝突

Gang Printing（幫派印刷）的嚴重風險

「幫派印刷」是指在一次印刷作業中，在同一基材上同時印製多種不同設計。這在商業印刷中很常見（節省成本），但在藥品包材中是一個急性（acute）的混淆風險：

  • 不同設計之間的物理分隔不足
  • 裁切誤差可能導致設計A的部分進入設計B的批次
  • 「Admixture」（混入）= 一種產品的標籤混入另一種產品

類比：製藥中的「交叉污染」

就像無菌製藥中，不同產品不能在同一條線上生產（除非有嚴格隔離和清場），幫派印刷把「不同藥品的識別信息」印在同一張紙上，其本質風險就是製藥中的「交叉污染（cross-contamination）」。標準明確稱之為「acute contributory risk」，措辭非常嚴厲。

合規路徑：兩個必要條件缺一不可

  • 客戶書面同意：不可只是口頭協議
  • 文件化風險評估：必須評估具體的混淆風險並記錄緩解措施

C.2.6 Batch Production and Stock Holding

C.2.6.1 Batched production and holding of product in stock shall only be practised if contractually agreed.

C.2.6.2 The organization shall control the storage to ensure security and integrity of the product and maintain its traceability back to manufacture and the materials used.

解說：庫存型生產的管控

Batched Production（批量生產備貨）

某些包材製造商會「預先生產」並在倉庫持有庫存，等待客戶下單後出貨。這種模式必須：

  • 有合約明確授權（contractually agreed）
  • 庫存管理系統確保追溯性（可追溯至製造批次和原料）
  • 防止未授權的設計更改期間放行舊版庫存

庫存型模式的最大風險是：設計更改後，舊版庫存未被攔截就出貨給客戶。

C.2.7 Digital Printing — Emerging Control Challenges

數位印刷：新技術帶來的新管控要求 | p62

C.2.7 Digital Printing Requirements

C.2.7.1 The flexible capabilities of digital printing introduce new activities, which shall be controlled and documented to ensure the accuracy and security of the printed products.

C.2.7.2 The use of digital printing and any special requirements for the product shall be agreed with the customer.

C.2.7.3 The organization shall establish a secure file access system, which is designed to prevent unintentional use of incorrect origination files.

C.2.7.4 Unless alternative security is designed, the controlling computer within the digital printing machine shall have only the specific origination file in its memory for the current print run; removal of this file shall form part of documented line clearance.

C.2.7.5 Operational settings to achieve acceptable colours shall be established through a formal process and recorded (see 7.5.3).

C.2.7.6 For reel-fed production on a continuous basis there shall be a suitable method to ensure product separation; the subsequent processes shall be verified to ensure correct separation of products and removal of defined gap material.

EXAMPLES: Defined change-over or gap between each printed job e.g. printed dummy text or blank material, code reading and code verification.

解說：數位印刷 — 傳統印版管控的邊界失效

數位印刷的獨特風險

傳統印刷靠「物理印版」作為防錯機制：錯誤的印版根本安不上機。但數位印刷完全依靠電腦文件，一個按鍵錯誤就可能把A產品的設計印在B產品的包材上，且外觀完全正常。

四大新增控制要求

  • 安全文件訪問系統：防止操作人員意外選到錯誤的設計文件（如設置嚴格的文件權限、作業切換需要雙重確認）
  • 單一文件在記憶體：印刷機電腦在印刷過程中只能存有當前作業的文件，切換前必須清除
  • 顏色設定正式化：數位印刷的顏色管理（色彩配置文件、ICC設定）需要通過正式流程建立並記錄，不能依賴操作人員的經驗調整
  • 產品分隔驗證：連續式卷材生產時，必須確認不同設計之間的間隔材料被正確識別並移除

類比：電子病歷的訪問控制

就像醫院的電子病歷系統必須確保醫生不會意外查看或修改錯誤患者的記錄，數位印刷系統必須確保操作人員不會意外載入錯誤產品的設計文件。兩者都是「信息系統的合規訪問控制」問題。

C.3 Security Code Systems & Reel Material Controls

安全碼系統、線上掃描驗證與卷材控制 | p62-p63

C.3.1 General & C.3.2 Verification Methods

C.3.1 To ensure the security of the product and prevent cross-contamination, security code systems may be included in the design of printed packaging materials for verification either by the organization during manufacture and/or by the customer during the packaging operation.

NOTE: Common security code systems include barcode systems, data matrix codes, and tag technology.

C.3.2.1 Where practical, every security coded item shall be verified by online scanning equipment to ensure that the codes are readable and that the correct product is being produced. Scanning should be carried out during the last feasible production process.

C.3.2.2 The scanning equipment software/control configuration shall be controlled to prevent unauthorized tampering. Codes shall be loaded from an independent source, e.g. specification or approved proof.

C.3.2.3 There shall be an effective system for rejecting any product that fails the scanning process. Any product rejected by the online scanning system shall be inspected, subsequently scrapped, and findings recorded and reviewed prior to product release.

C.3.2.4 The online scanning equipment and its associated reject mechanism shall be subjected to a challenge test during production to verify whether its operation is effective in detecting and removing incorrectly coded material. Such monitoring shall take place at the start of the process, at regular intervals, and be recorded.

C.3.2.5 Any product produced where electronic scanning is specified, but has not been performed, shall be properly authorized and recorded. The customer shall be notified and documented approval obtained before product release.

C.3.2.6 For reel-fed multi-lane production, all lanes should be subject to security code verification. Where this cannot be performed, one lane only may be verified, in agreement with the customer.

C.3.2.7 / C.3.3 "Point of measure" codes and/or verification of sample codes from all lanes shall be carried out.

Where "point of sale" codes (GS1/EAN, Code 39, PZN, etc.) are incorporated into the design, a documented sample verification shall be carried out during the production process.

解說：線上掃描的核心要求

線上掃描 = 100%驗證

「每一個安全碼件均應由線上掃描設備驗證」—— 這是100%的線上確認，不是抽樣。對藥品包材的安全碼（如條碼、Data Matrix）來說，漏掉任何一個都可能意味著錯誤產品流出。

挑戰測試（Challenge Test）的重要性

掃描設備不只需要確認「能掃到碼」，還需要定期進行挑戰測試，確認：

  • 能偵測到錯誤的碼（不只是缺失的碼）
  • 剔除機構確實動作，將不合格品排出
  • 每次生產開始和定期間隔均執行，並記錄

類比：消防演習不只是確認警報響了，還要確認員工真的能撤離到安全地點。

掃描未執行的例外處理

若規定要掃描但未執行，不能自動放行：

  • 必須獲得正式授權（documented approval）
  • 必須通知客戶並獲得書面同意
  • 這是一個正式的偏差事件，需要記錄在品質記錄中

C.3.4 Reel Materials and Procedures

C.3.4.1 Unless otherwise specified by the customer, splices shall be:

  • a) made using a brightly coloured adhesive tape on both sides of the web, and
  • b) checked either side of the splice to ensure that identical materials are joined and in register.

A limit on the maximum number of splices may be specified.

C.3.4.2 The quantity of material (length, weight or numerical) produced on each reel shall be determined within accuracy limits agreed with the customer and recorded on the reel.

C.3.4.3 The batch identity, reel number and production date shall be recorded on the inner face of the core for each individual reel.

C.3.4.4 To prevent cross-contamination, the web shall be run to plain material at the end of the run to ensure that no printed material remains in the printing equipment.

C.3.4.5 Where it is necessary to leave printed material in the converting equipment due to the difficulty in carrying out re-webbing (e.g. slitters), there shall be a formal documented procedure for removal and disposal of the material used to pull the new design through the machine.

C.3.4.6 If material with missing print can be produced as a consequence of the design or operation of the printing equipment, the organization shall have a secure system for the detection, removal and segregation of product produced with missing colours or text.

解說：卷材生產的特殊管控

為什麼接縫（Splice）需要彩色膠帶？

卷材生產中，當一卷原材料用完需要接上新卷時，接縫處的包材可能存在印刷偏移、錯位等問題。使用鮮豔顏色的膠帶標記接縫，使客戶在後續灌裝操作中能快速辨識並排除這些位置的包材。

卷芯追溯信息（C.3.4.3）

批號、卷號、生產日期要記錄在卷芯內面（而非外面），原因是：

  • 外面的標籤容易在運輸中損壞或脫落
  • 卷芯是整卷用完後仍保留的實物，追溯信息必須與其一起保留
  • 直到最後一圈材料用完，追溯鏈都保持完整

類比：「清場到素材」的邏輯

C.3.4.4要求生產結束時要「跑到素材（plain material）」才停機，就像廚房改換菜系前必須先清理鍋具一樣。印刷設備中殘留的印刷品如果進入下一個工單，就是「admixture（混入）」事件的源頭。

缺印（Missing Print）的偵測系統

部分印刷設備在特定條件下可能產生缺字、缺色的包材。如果這類包材流出，後果是患者拿到信息不完整的標籤。組織必須有系統性的偵測和剔除機制——不能依賴視覺檢查。

Annex D (Informative) — Verification, Qualification & Validation Guidance

D.1 General — Scope of Annex D

This annex describes the approach when verification or qualification and validation are required. The guidance relates to the requirements given in 8.5.1.2 (additional GMP-related requirements on verification, qualification and validation).

Items which can require verification/qualification include:

  • equipment used for production of primary packaging materials, utilities and facilities, and
  • test equipment used for determining the acceptance of quality critical starting materials, intermediate/in-process or finished product.

Items which can require validation include:

  • validation of processes, and
  • validation of individual product, where appropriate or as agreed with the customer.

The aim of verification/qualification/validation is to confirm through documented evidence that pre-determined specifications are consistently fulfilled.

與EU GMP Annex 15的連結

  • 變更控制維持確認狀態

D.2 Prerequisites and Considerations

先決條件與考量因素：設備確認和製程驗證的前置要求

D.2.1 General

A documented risk assessment is used to determine which equipment and processes need to be verified/qualified/validated. Product validation is optional, either after an internal decision of the organization or at the request of the customer.

Verification/qualification of equipment and validation of processes/product can be performed independently of each other, e.g. validation of a new process/product does not require re-verification/re-qualification of existing equipment.

D.2.2 Prerequisites for Verification/Qualification/Validation

Prerequisites to verification/qualification of the equipment:

  • approved/agreed upon requirement specifications,
  • identification of roles and agreed responsibilities (organization and supplier),
  • definition of critical process parameters,
  • training such as GMP and verification/qualification.

Prerequisites to validation of the process/product:

  • approved/agreed upon process/product specifications,
  • verified/qualified equipment,
  • identification of roles and agreed responsibilities (organization and customer),
  • definition of critical process parameters,
  • training (operators, quality personnel, engineers, etc.),
  • standard operating procedures to provide detail on how to produce/operate and clean the equipment.

D.2.3 Software Validation

Software can be validated/verified by functional tests. Software which is part of the equipment can be included in the equipment verification/qualification.

NOTE: The GAMP guideline might give guidance on how the functional test can be executed.

D.3 Validation Master Plan — Planning, Types & Decision Criteria

驗證主計劃：類型、角色與關鍵設備/製程判斷準則 | p65-p68

D.3.1 VMP — General Elements

The Validation Master Plan (VMP) describes the validation activities and the order of execution in accordance with the overall validation approach. It is recommended to revise the plan on a regular basis, for example yearly.

It should usually include the following elements:

  • validation planning (see D.3.2) and scheduling;
  • organizational structure of validation activities — roles and responsibilities (see D.3.3);
  • summary of quality critical equipment, process (see D.3.4 and D.3.5) and product;
  • references to existing documents (e.g. reference to an existing validation report), if applicable;
  • references to applicable procedures (e.g. overall validation approach, documentation format).

解說：VMP是確認確效活動的「地圖」

VMP的功能定位

VMP不是確認本身，而是確認活動的總體規劃文件。它回答三個問題：

  • 做什麼？（哪些設備和製程需要確認）
  • 怎麼做？（採用什麼方法和標準）
  • 誰來做？（角色和責任分配）

年度更新確保VMP反映最新的設備狀態、新引入的製程，以及任何已完成或計劃中的確認活動。

D.3.2 Validation Planning — Three Approaches

D.3.2.1 General — Prospective validation is recommended as the preferred approach but under certain circumstances a concurrent or retrospective validation can be accepted (this statement is not valid for sterilization processes).

D.3.2.2 Prospective validation — Prospective validation occurs prior to commercial production.

D.3.2.3 Concurrent validation — Concurrent validation should only apply to performance qualification and process validation. Concurrent validation consists of activities conducted in parallel with the manufacture of commercial product where the commercial product is released before the conclusion of validation activities.

Concurrent validation should follow the principles and procedures associated with prospective validation. Validation that takes place in campaigns represents a speciality in the manufacture of primary packaging material — concurrent validations may be interrupted and resumed with the next production campaign.

D.3.2.4 Retrospective validation — This approach includes establishing documented evidence that installed and operating equipment produces a consistent product by performing a historic review of data generated over the range of operating parameters and raw materials. This review can include maintenance and engineering records, quality records, and customer complaints.

解說：三種驗證確效方式的選擇邏輯

包材製造的特殊性：戰役性生產

許多包材製造商採用「戰役性生產（campaign production）」模式——某個產品每季度或每半年生產一次大批量。在此情況下，並行驗證可能因為戰役結束而中斷，並在下次戰役開始時繼續。這是包材行業特有的靈活性，標準明確允許。

例外：滅菌製程必須使用前瞻性驗證

標準明確指出，這個「三種方式都可接受」的原則不適用於滅菌製程（見8.5.1.4.2和8.5.1.2.5）。滅菌製程必須使用前瞻性驗證——這也與FDA和EU GMP的立場一致。

D.3.2.5 Bracketing/Matrix Approach

In general a bracketing/matrix approach can be appropriate based on risk analysis. Where the equivalence of equipment has been proven, a bracketing/matrix approach can be used to:

  • reduce the number of installation and operational testing during equipment verification/qualification,
  • reduce the number of PQ batches during equipment qualification,
  • reduce the number of PV batches during product or process validation, and
  • increase the re-validation period for individual equipment and performing the re-validation on equivalent equipment alternately.

解說：括號法/矩陣法的實務應用

這是製造商最有價值的工具之一

對於擁有多台相同型號設備的包材製造商，矩陣法可以大幅減少驗證工作量，同時保持合規性。關鍵前提是：必須先證明設備的等效性（equivalence）。

實際計算：矩陣法節省的批次數

以Table D.1的3台等效機器為例：

無矩陣法（傳統方式）：

  • 初次驗證：3台 × 3批 = 9批
  • 年度重驗：3台 × 1批 × 每年 = 3批/年

有矩陣法（Table D.1）：

  • 初次驗證：1台3批 + 2台1批 = 5批（節省44%）
  • 年度重驗：每年1台 × 1批 = 1批/年（節省67%）

4年總計：無矩陣 = 9+12 = 21批；有矩陣 = 5+4 = 9批。節省57%的驗證批次。

類比：藥廠的括號法驗證

在製藥行業，括號法（bracketing）常用於穩定性研究——只測試最強和最弱規格的劑型，中間規格假設在範圍內。ISO 15378把相同邏輯應用到包材製程驗證，這是「基於科學和風險的法規思維」的典型應用。

D.3.4 Quality Critical Equipment — Decision Criteria

Risk assessment is an inherent part of the decision-making process. Equipment is critical in case one of the following questions is answered with "yes":

  • Would malfunction of the equipment impact directly on product quality as defined for the type of products it is producing?
  • Is the equipment used to guarantee sterility of the products?
  • Does the equipment control or measure quality critical processing steps or parameters?
  • Does the equipment produce data/records for acceptance or rejection?
  • Is equipment in direct contact with the product?
  • Is the equipment used to prevent contamination, to remove contamination or to clean?

When equipment is considered critical, verification or qualification should be performed and it is included in the VMP. When equipment is not critical, good engineering practices apply and it is not included in the VMP.

D.4.1.2 Risk assessment — The risk assessment should underpin the specification and verification/qualification process, and be appropriately applied at each stage. Documentation should be based on the risk to product quality.

D.4.1.3 Requirement Specification

D.4.1.3.1 General — The requirement specifications can be combined in one or more specification documents depending on the criticality of the equipment, whether it concerns standardized or customized equipment, or depending on responsibilities. Every specification should contain well defined, specific, testable, achievable acceptance criteria.

D.4.1.3.2 User Requirement Specification (URS) — A URS (as defined in 3.7.11) is an approved document that states the product specifications of the material produced on this equipment as well as functional, operational and/or technical aspects of the equipment or process required to produce the desired product. The URS can include the consideration for equipment lay-out with sufficient space for material flow and operators, availability of spare parts, and ease of access for cleaning and line clearance.

D.4.1.3.3 Equipment specification — Critical aspects of manufacturing systems are typically functions, features, abilities and performance or characteristics necessary for the manufacturing process and system to ensure consistent product quality.

D.4.1.3.4 Equipment verification/qualification — A systematic approach should be defined to verify that the equipment is fit for intended use, has been properly installed and is operating correctly. Verification/qualification can be split in two sub-stages: installation qualification and operational qualification, but it is also possible to combine them in one stage (typically done during testing at the supplier and testing on site). Leveraging of data are allowed — data obtained during testing at the supplier does not have to be obtained again on site unless the transfer of the equipment influences the testing.

解說：IQ/OQ/PQ三階段確認

URS — 確認的起點

URS（使用者需求規格書）必須在採購設備前完成，它定義了：

  • 設備必須生產的產品規格
  • 功能性要求（速度、精度等）
  • GMP相關要求（可清潔性、材質相容性）
  • 操作人員接觸/安全性要求
  • 空間佈局考量

URS是之後所有確認活動的基準文件。

「數據借用（Leveraging）」原則

若供應商工廠測試（FAT）已收集了充分數據，這些數據可以「搬運」到現場確認中使用，不需要重複收集。前提是：設備在運輸/安裝過程中沒有發生可能影響測試結果的事件。這是基於風險的務實做法，避免不必要的重複工作。

D.4.1.4 Performance Qualification (PQ)

PQ testing uses production materials to verify that the equipment is robust and the primary packaging material can be consistently produced under routine operating conditions. PQ challenges the overall line performance to ensure that it can produce consistently at the quality standard required.

The test process and results of an appropriate number of consecutive production batches (typically three) are formally documented and approved.

Based on risk management, performance qualification can be skipped or can also be combined with process or product validation.

An exception to using three subsequent batches may be made where the production process is lengthy — for example a single batch may require several weeks of continuous production. In this case PQ may be conducted on sub-batches of a minimum of one day's duration each.

解說：PQ的靈活性

「通常3批」不是鐵律

「Typically three」（通常3批）在GMP語境中是常見的基準，但ISO 15378明確提供了例外情況。對於需要數週才能完成一批的製程（例如大卷材的長時間印刷），可以用「最少一天的sub-batch」替代完整的3批。

這是科學性和務實性的平衡——確認原則不因生產週期長就被跳過，而是以等效的方式執行。

PQ可以與製程驗證合併

基於風險管理，PQ可以：

  • 跳過：若設備標準化程度高，OQ已足夠確認性能
  • 與PV合併：PQ批次同時作為製程驗證批次，一舉兩得

決定跳過或合併需要有風險評估支持和品質部門批准。

D.4.2 Process and Product Validation

D.4.2.1 Process validation — A process validation consists of a number of consecutive production batches (typically three) produced under normal conditions with a higher level of sampling and additional testing compared to routine production. Process validation can be combined with PQ.

Process validation has to be based on a firm base of knowledge of the process and its variables/variations. Process validation is independent of the product. Bracketing or matrix approach may be used (see D.3.2.5).

D.4.2.2 Product validation — The approach is in line with process validation; however specific customer requirements can be added.

解說：製程驗證 vs. 產品驗證

關鍵區別：製程驗證獨立於產品

製程驗證（Process Validation）是驗證「製程本身」的能力，與具體產品無關。例如：

  • 驗證「輪轉凹版印刷製程」能穩定達到特定色彩精度和套印誤差
  • 一旦製程驗證完成，新產品在此製程上生產時，通常只需要產品確認，而不需要重新驗證製程

產品驗證（Product Validation）是在製程驗證基礎上，加入特定客戶或產品的要求（如特定油墨相容性、特殊基材）。

D.5 Documentation Structure for V/Q/V

D.5 Documentation Hierarchy

D.5.1 General — All documents should be reviewed and approved prior to the commencement of verification/qualification/validation/release. Any release decisions should be the responsibility of the quality unit. Any revision of a document should be tracked by version control.

D.5.2 Plan (optional) — One or more plans can be created to detail the steps to be taken in the different stages. These plans can include:

  • description of the project/scope;
  • roles and responsibilities;
  • planning;
  • risk analysis;
  • overall acceptance criteria;
  • validation approach;
  • documents to be created;
  • reference to procedures and change control;
  • definition of conditions for quarantine status of batches subject to PQ or validation.

Plan and protocol may be combined in one document.

D.5.3 Protocol — A written protocol should be established that specifies how verification/qualification/validation will be conducted. The protocol can include:

  • description of the project/scope;
  • roles and responsibilities;
  • justification of the approach taken;
  • tests to be performed with test methods and test conditions;
  • detailed acceptance criteria for each test;
  • sampling plan;
  • critical process parameters;
  • reference to procedures, change control and requirement specifications;
  • prerequisites.

D.5.4 Report — A report that cross-references the protocol should be prepared. The report can include:

  • summary of test results;
  • raw data;
  • deviations observed and corrective actions taken or to be taken;
  • conclusion;
  • changes to the plan as defined in the protocol with appropriate justification.

After completion of satisfactory testing, a formal release for the next step should be made as a written authorization.

D.5.5 Validation Summary Report — VSR (optional) — The VSR follows the completion of all stages of V/Q/V and concludes the project. The VSR can include:

  • cross-reference to protocols and reports;
  • changes to plans and justification for any change;
  • formal approval and closure of the project.

D.5.6 Archiving — Verification or qualification and validation records should be maintained according to 7.5.3.11.

Protocol vs. Report 的核心差異

協議（Protocol）必須在執行前核准——它定義「我們計劃怎麼做」。
報告（Report）在執行後撰寫——它記錄「我們實際做了什麼以及結果如何」。

這個「先核准協議，再執行，再報告」的順序是GMP的基本原則——確保測試前已明確定義驗收標準，防止「事後更改標準」的行為。

類比：醫療試驗的預先申報

臨床試驗也要求在試驗前預先申報方案（研究方案），結果的統計方法和成功標準必須事先定義。確認確效的協議先行原則出自同樣的科學誠信邏輯。

偏差的處理原則

報告中記錄的偏差不一定意味著確認失敗。關鍵是：

  • 每個偏差必須有書面的根本原因分析
  • 對產品質量影響必須評估
  • 糾正措施必須文件化並追蹤完成
  • 品質部門必須批准「帶偏差放行」

D.6 Validation Maintenance — Change Control & Re-qualification

確認確效的維護：變更控制、預防性維護與重新確認 | p74

D.6 Validation Maintenance

D.6.1 General — The verification/qualification of equipment and validation of process(es)/product should be maintained. Any changes should be risk assessed for their impact on the validation status. If necessary, appropriate verification/qualification/validation or re-qualification/re-validation should be performed.

Changes/improvements to validation procedures should also be risk assessed to determine if re-validation is required.

D.6.2 Change control — Specific controls should be implemented to cover changes during verification/qualification/validation. Formal, documented change control should be implemented after release of the equipment/process/product and kept throughout the lifetime of the equipment/process/product.

D.6.3 Follow-up — Action should be taken to complete any outstanding actions, e.g. deficiencies following verification/qualification/validation. All actions taken should be documented.

D.6.4 Preventive maintenance — After release of equipment a preventive maintenance plan (including calibration) should be established to guarantee equipment performance.

D.6.5 Re-qualification/re-validation — As long as the equipment/process operates in a state of control and no changes have been made to the equipment/process or output product, the equipment need not be re-qualified and process need not be re-validated.

Whether the equipment/process is operating in a state of control is determined by analysing day-to-day process control data and any product testing data for conformance with specifications and for variability.

When changes or deviations occur, the equipment/process should be reviewed and evaluated, and re-qualification/re-validation should be performed where appropriate. Periodic qualifications/validations can be required e.g. clean rooms, sterilization processes.

解說：確認確效不是一次性活動

「控制狀態」= 不需要重新確認的前提

ISO 15378 Annex D的立場與ICH Q10一致：若設備/製程在控制狀態下穩定運行，不需要週期性重新確認（除了潔淨室、滅菌製程等特殊情況）。

如何判斷「控制狀態」：

  • 日常製程控制數據符合規格
  • 產品測試數據無統計趨勢
  • 無重大偏差或客訴
  • 無設備或製程變更

變更控制 = 確認狀態的守門員

確認確效完成後，變更控制系統成為保護確認狀態的核心機制：

  • 任何對已確認設備/製程的變更必須通過正式變更控制
  • 變更風險評估需評估對確認狀態的影響
  • 重大變更（如設備大修、製程參數調整）可能觸發重新確認
  • 變更控制需要貫穿設備/製程的整個生命週期

類比：汽車的定期保養

預防性維護計劃（D.6.4）就像汽車的定期保養——不是等車壞了才修，而是按照製造商的建議定期維護，確保設備始終在設計性能範圍內運作。校準也是其中的一部分：測量儀器必須定期校準，確保「設備說合格，確實是合格」。
