Clause 4 — Context of the Organization｜組織背景

4.1–4.4 Standard Requirements

4.1 Understanding the Organization and Its Context

The organization shall determine external and internal issues that are relevant to its purpose and strategic direction and that affect its ability to achieve the intended results of its quality management system. The organization shall monitor and review information about these external and internal issues.

GMP Addition: The organization's overall policy, intentions and approach to risk management, validation and change control shall be documented in order to fulfil the GMP requirements.

4.2 Understanding the Needs and Expectations of Interested Parties

The organization shall determine: (a) the interested parties that are relevant to the quality management system; (b) the requirements of these interested parties that are relevant to the quality management system. The organization shall monitor and review information about these interested parties and their relevant requirements.

4.3 Determining the Scope of the QMS

The organization shall determine the boundaries and applicability of the quality management system to establish its scope. When determining this scope, the organization shall consider: (a) external and internal issues (4.1); (b) requirements of relevant interested parties (4.2); (c) the products and services of the organization.

The scope shall state the types of products and services covered, and provide justification for any requirement determined not applicable.

4.4 Quality Management System and Its Processes

4.4.1 — The organization shall establish, implement, maintain and continually improve a QMS, including the processes needed and their interactions. The organization shall:

  • Determine inputs/outputs and sequence of processes
  • Determine and apply criteria, monitoring, measurement and performance indicators
  • Ensure resources and assign responsibilities for each process
  • Address risks and opportunities (Clause 6.1)
  • Describe how the quality policy and objectives are implemented, cascaded and monitored to provide assurance of product quality and meet agreed customer specifications
  • Document the structure of the quality management system (e.g. quality manual, master SOP)

4.4.2 — The organization shall maintain documented information to support operations and retain documented information to provide confidence that processes are being carried out as planned.

解讀與教學 Commentary

Clause 4.1 — 脈絡分析實務：包材供應商的 PESTLE

ISO 15378 要求供應商系統性分析外部與內部環境。對初包材製造商而言，關鍵外部議題包括：

  • 法規趨勢：EU MDR/IVDR 升級、中國 NMPA 新規、FDA 21 CFR Part 211 修訂
  • 原材料可得性：硼矽玻璃、鹵化丁基橡膠的供應鏈集中風險
  • 市場集中度：客戶基礎過於依賴單一藥廠的商業脆弱性
  • 競爭環境：替代包材（COP/COC 注射筒）對傳統玻璃的衝擊

GMP 強化要求（標準中特有）：風險管理、驗證和變更控制的整體方針必須文件化——這已超出 ISO 9001 原本的要求。

Clause 4.2 — 利害相關方識別

初包材供應商的利害相關方三層架構：

  • 直接客戶（藥廠/CDMO）：提出規格、進行稽核、要求 CoA/CoC
  • 監管機構（FDA/EMA/NMPA）：設定法規底線，間接影響供應商要求
  • 終端患者：最終受益者，任何包材缺陷都可能影響藥品安全性

重要洞察：患者雖不是合約當事人，卻是 ISO 15378 整個標準保護的核心對象。

Clause 4.4 — QMS 範圍：ISO 15378 與 ISO 9001 的核心差異

這是 ISO 15378 最關鍵的差異點：QMS 範圍必須明確涵蓋 GMP 要求。

純 ISO 9001 認證的包材供應商，其 QMS 可能未涵蓋：驗證、批次記錄、ALCOA 文件原則、潔淨室分類。而 ISO 15378 明確要求這些都必須在 QMS 範圍內。

CDMO 稽核視角

當稽核一家塞子或玻璃瓶供應商時，Clause 4.4 合規的具體問法：

「請展示您的品質手冊或主 SOP——其中是否明確列出 GMP 要求屬於 QMS 範圍？」

若供應商的 QMS 文件只提到品質目標和客戶滿意度，卻沒有提到 GMP、驗證或批次放行——這是 Clause 4.4 的重大缺口。

Clause 5 — Leadership｜領導統御

5.1–5.3 Standard Requirements

5.1.1 General — Leadership and Commitment

Top management shall demonstrate leadership and commitment with respect to the quality management system by:

  • Taking accountability for the effectiveness of the QMS
  • Ensuring quality policy and objectives are established and compatible with context and strategic direction
  • Ensuring integration of QMS requirements into business processes
  • Promoting the use of the process approach and risk-based thinking
  • Ensuring resources needed for the QMS are available
  • Communicating the importance of effective quality management
  • Ensuring the QMS achieves its intended results
  • Promoting improvement

5.1.2 Customer Focus

Top management shall ensure that customer and applicable statutory and regulatory requirements are determined, understood and consistently met; and that risks affecting conformity of products are addressed.

NOTE (GMP): Key customer requirements include suitable facilities, competent and trained personnel, processes designed to ensure product security and avoidance of cross-contamination, and the ability to consistently produce product conforming to customer specifications.

5.1.3 Customer Audits

Top management shall approve access by mutual agreement for existing/prospective customers or their nominated representatives to conduct audits to review and assess the organization's QMS.

5.2 Policy

5.2.1 Establishing the quality policy: Top management shall establish, implement and maintain a quality policy that is appropriate to the purpose and context of the organization, provides a framework for quality objectives, includes a commitment to satisfy applicable requirements, and includes a commitment to continual improvement.

5.2.2 Communicating the quality policy: The quality policy shall be available as documented information, communicated, understood and applied within the organization, and available to relevant interested parties.

5.3 Organizational Roles, Responsibilities and Authorities

Top management shall ensure that responsibilities and authorities for relevant roles are assigned, communicated and understood. Top management shall assign responsibility and authority for: (a) ensuring QMS conforms to requirements; (b) ensuring processes deliver intended outputs; (c) reporting QMS performance to top management; (d) ensuring customer focus throughout the organization; (e) maintaining QMS integrity when changes are planned.

GMP Addition: The organization shall maintain a current record of signatures of responsible persons. Signature/user identification lists of all personnel checking or double-checking process steps and in-process controls are recommended.

GMP Addition: The quality unit(s) with responsibility for quality critical decisions shall have the authority to make those decisions independently.

解讀與教學 Commentary

Clause 5.1 — 領導承諾：超越形式的 GMP 精神

ISO 15378 的領導承諾不只是簽署品質政策。標準隱含的要求是：當生產目標與產品品質發生衝突時，產品安全必須優先於產量目標。

可量化的管理承諾指標：

  • 管理階層是否親自出席品質審查會議？
  • 品質部門的人員編制比例是否足夠？
  • 偏差調查是否有時限要求並由管理層追蹤？
  • CAPA 完成率是否納入管理層 KPI？

資料來看：管理承諾與品質結果的關聯

業界研究顯示，當供應商管理層能見度高（定期 gemba walk、品質議題進入董事會報告）時，年度偏差率平均比管理被動的供應商低 30–40%，客訴率低 50%。

這說明 Clause 5.1 不只是文件要求，而是預測供應商長期績效的領先指標。

Clause 5.1.3 — 客戶稽核：主動開放 vs. 被動接受

標準要求最高管理層批准客戶稽核的准入，這代表供應商的高層必須認可稽核文化，而不是由品質部門單獨應付。

從 CDMO 角度：稽核申請被拖延或推辭，往往是重大風險信號。優質供應商通常有標準的稽核接待程序，最高管理層在開幕會議中出席。

Clause 5.3 — 品質單位的獨立決策權

這是 ISO 15378 最接近藥廠 Qualified Person (QP) 概念的條款。

標準明確規定：品質部門對品質關鍵決策擁有獨立決策權，不受生產或商業部門的壓力。這在實務上意味著：

  • 品質部門可以拒絕放行批次，即使生產部門要求出貨
  • 品質負責人的職位不應向生產總監匯報
  • 偏差調查結論不應被業務壓力改變

稽核時的問法：「請描述一個品質部門否決出貨決定的案例。」

簽名記錄要求 — Signature List

標準要求維護現行有效的責任人簽名記錄，並建議為所有執行查核或雙重查核的人員建立簽名/用戶識別清單。

這與 GMP 的 ALCOA 原則（可歸屬性 Attributable）直接連結——每筆記錄都必須能追溯到具名的個人。

Clause 6 — Planning｜策劃

6.1–6.3 Standard Requirements

6.1 Actions to Address Risks and Opportunities

6.1.1 — When planning for the QMS, the organization shall consider the issues (4.1) and requirements (4.2) and determine the risks and opportunities that need to be addressed to: (a) give assurance that the QMS can achieve its intended results; (b) enhance desirable effects; (c) prevent or reduce undesired effects; (d) achieve improvement.

6.1.2 — The organization shall plan actions to address risks and opportunities and evaluate their effectiveness. Actions shall be proportionate to the potential impact on conformity of products and services.

6.1.3 — GMP Addition: The organization shall ensure that risk management is included in all processes, associated with the design/development, manufacturing and delivery of primary packaging materials. Records shall be maintained.

Risk management applies to, for example:

  • Change control & cleaning
  • Complaints & contamination
  • Design control (new products/processes)
  • Health and hygiene
  • Labelling & maintenance
  • Manufacturing planning & materials management
  • Nonconformities and quality defects
  • Pest control & purchasing/supply chain
  • Rework & traceability
  • Validation, verification and qualification

NOTE: Risk management principles can be found in ISO 31000, ISO 14971, ICH Q9, and GAMP5. Methods for hazard identification and assessment: IEC 31010.

6.2 Quality Objectives and Planning to Achieve Them

6.2.1 — The organization shall establish quality objectives at relevant functions, levels and processes. Quality objectives shall:

  • Be consistent with the quality policy
  • Be measurable
  • Take into account applicable requirements
  • Be relevant to conformity of products/services and to customer satisfaction enhancement
  • Be monitored, communicated and updated as appropriate

6.2.2 — When planning how to achieve quality objectives, the organization shall determine: what will be done; what resources are required; who is responsible; when it will be completed; how results will be evaluated.

6.3 Planning of Changes

When the organization determines the need for changes to the QMS, changes shall be carried out in a planned manner. The organization shall consider:

  • The purpose of the changes and their potential consequences
  • The integrity of the quality management system
  • The availability of resources
  • The allocation or reallocation of responsibilities and authorities

For change control, see Clause 8.5.6.2.

解讀與教學 Commentary

Clause 6.1.3 — 包材製造的全流程風險管理

ISO 15378 的 GMP 強化要求明確將 ISO 14971（醫療器材風險管理）的思維帶入包材領域。對初包材製造商，這意味著風險管理不能只是一份 FMEA，而必須貫穿所有流程。

實務對照（以注射用玻璃瓶為例）：

  • 設計控制：新玻璃配方對 pH 值或脫層風險的 FMEA
  • 製造規劃：熔爐溫度偏離對缺陷率的風險評估
  • 驗證：清洗機 / 去熱原流程的風險導向驗證策略
  • 追蹤：批號系統確保缺陷批次可被完整追回

風險選項的現實考量

標準 NOTE 1 列出的風險應對選項包括：避免風險、接受風險（換取機會）、消除風險源、改變可能性/後果、共擔風險或保留風險。

對 CDMO 採購決策的意義：若一家供應商對高嚴重性風險採用「保留風險（informed decision）」策略，要求其提供書面的風險評估依據與控制措施——不能只有口頭說明。

Clause 6.3 — 變更控制：QMS 層面 vs. 製程層面

Clause 6.3 處理的是 QMS 系統層面的變更（如組織重組、新流程引入），而 Clause 8.5.6.2 處理的是製程/產品層面的變更控制。

兩者都要求「有計畫地」執行——不允許臨時或非正式的系統性變更。這與製藥 CDMO 的變更控制程序（包含影響評估、客戶通知、驗證觸發評估）精神一致。

稽核時的風險旗標

若供應商的品質目標只有業務指標（準時交貨率、產能利用率），卻沒有任何產品安全或 GMP 合規指標——這是 Clause 6.2 的缺口，也預示著品質文化問題。

Clause 6.2 — SMART 品質目標：GMP 層面的加碼

ISO 9001 的品質目標要求是可量測、可溝通。ISO 15378 在精神上進一步要求：品質目標必須包含產品安全與合規指標，而非僅限商業 KPI。

目標類型不足的例子符合 ISO 15378 的例子
客訴率「降低客訴」「每百萬件客訴率 <5 ppm，年底前」
偏差管理「減少偏差」「重複偏差率 <10%，CAPA 30天內關閉率 >90%」
產品安全「保持品質」「零外來物污染事件；年度無菌包材客戶投訴零件」

Clause 7 — Support (Part 1): Resources, Infrastructure & Environment｜資源與基礎設施

7.1 Resources — General to Environment

7.1.1–7.1.2 General Resources & People

The organization shall determine and provide the resources needed for the establishment, implementation, maintenance and continual improvement of the QMS. The organization shall consider: (a) capabilities and constraints on existing internal resources; (b) what needs to be obtained from external providers.

The organization shall determine and provide the persons necessary for effective implementation of its QMS and for the operation and control of its processes.

7.1.3 Infrastructure

7.1.3.1 General: The organization shall determine, provide and maintain the infrastructure necessary for the operation of its processes and to achieve conformity of products and services.

7.1.3.2 GMP-related requirements on infrastructure:

  • Infrastructure shall be managed, operated and maintained to avoid product contamination
  • Facilities shall be protected against entry of unauthorized personnel
  • Personnel entering defined production/storage/QC areas shall only access wearing appropriate clothing
  • Layout, design and operation shall minimize the risk of errors and permit effective cleaning and maintenance to avoid cross-contamination, based on a risk assessment
  • Changing facilities, toilets and hand-washing facilities shall be provided; where product quality is affected, these shall be separated from manufacturing areas and not ventilated directly to them

Storage areas shall be of adequate capacity to allow orderly storage of starting materials and products, and appropriate with regard to material and product quality.

7.1.4 Environment for the Operation of Processes

7.1.4.2 Work environment:

  • Documented requirements for health, cleanliness, clothing and access control shall be established
  • If work environment conditions can adversely affect primary packaging material quality, appropriate conditions shall be defined and effectively monitored and controlled
  • Special conditions shall be established for control of contaminated or potentially contaminated material
  • Where primary packaging materials are exposed, covers shall be used unless justified by documented risk assessment

7.1.4.3 Classification of clean zones/cleanrooms: Clean zones/cleanrooms shall be classified and monitored/operated. See ISO 14644-1, -2, -3, -5. For biocontamination monitoring: ISO 14698-1 and -2.

7.1.4.4 Risk control of contamination: The organization shall determine and control risks that can result in contamination, including: personal hygiene and health; personal clothing, jewellery and make-up; smoking, eating, chewing, drinking and personal medication; handling and disposal of waste; microbiological contamination; protective clothing appropriate to the process area classification.

7.1.4.5 Pest control: An effective, documented pest control programme shall be implemented and maintained.

7.1.4.6 Materials and utilities: All utilities (air, gases, steam, water) shall be assessed for their potential impact on primary packaging material quality. If water comes into direct contact with the primary packaging material or is used for cleaning contact equipment, its quality shall be determined and controlled. Processing aids shall be subject to a documented risk assessment.

7.1.4.7 Maintenance and cleaning: Documented requirements for maintenance activities shall be established. Maintenance operations shall not introduce contamination and, on completion, shall include a documented cleanliness check. A cleaning schedule that takes into account contamination risk shall be defined and documented. Defective quality critical equipment shall be removed from service and/or clearly labelled; prior to reintroduction it shall be verified as suitable for use.

解讀與教學 Commentary

Clause 7.1.3.2 — GMP 廠房設計原則

標準要求廠房佈局必須基於風險評估，以最小化錯誤風險並允許有效清潔。這是製藥 GMP 的核心設計原則。

對初包材製造商的具體意涵：

  • 不同材質（橡膠 vs. 玻璃）的生產線需要物理隔離，防止交叉污染
  • 人流/物流路徑設計必須防止污染品與合格品的混淆
  • 更衣室/洗手間的位置與通風設計，必須不直接通向生產區

Clause 7.1.4.3 — 潔淨室分類與 ISO 14644

ISO 15378 明確引用 ISO 14644 系列作為潔淨室管理的技術依據，建立了一個標準內引用標準的整合架構：

  • ISO 14644-1：潔淨室分類（ISO 5–9）
  • ISO 14644-2：監控規範
  • ISO 14644-3：測試方法
  • ISO 14644-5：作業
  • ISO 14698-1/2：生物污染監測

對無菌包材製造商（如無菌注射筒預裝廠），潔淨室分類通常為 ISO 5 或 ISO 7，需定期懸浮粒子計數和微生物監測。

水質管理：從 WFI 到工藝用水

Clause 7.1.4.6.3 要求：若水直接接觸初包材或用於清洗接觸產品的設備，必須確定並控制水質。

實務場景（玻璃瓶洗瓶機）：最終沖洗水若為注射用水（WFI），需要定期微生物、內毒素和電導率測試，記錄保存。若降級使用純化水，必須有文件化的風險評估支持。

維護後潔淨度確認 — 常被忽略的要求

Clause 7.1.4.7.3 明確要求：維修保養完成後必須進行書面的潔淨度查核。這是稽核中常見的缺口。

常見問題：工程部門完成維修後，未通知品質部門進行潔淨度確認就恢復生產，導致潤滑油、工具遺留等污染風險未被管控。

Clause 7 — Support (Part 2): Measurement, Competence & Documented Information｜量測、能力與文件化資訊

7.1.5–7.5 Standard Requirements

7.1.5 Monitoring and Measuring Resources

7.1.5.1 General: The organization shall determine and provide resources to ensure valid and reliable results when monitoring or measuring is used to verify conformity of products.

7.1.5.2 Measurement traceability: When measurement traceability is required, measuring equipment shall be:

  • Calibrated or verified at specified intervals or prior to use, against measurement standards traceable to international or national measurement standards
  • Identified in order to determine their status
  • Safeguarded from adjustments, damage or deterioration that would invalidate calibration status

The organization shall determine if the validity of previous measurement results has been adversely affected when measuring equipment is found unfit for purpose, and shall take appropriate action.

7.1.6 Organizational Knowledge

The organization shall determine the knowledge necessary for the operation of its processes and to achieve conformity of products and services. This knowledge shall be maintained and made available. When addressing changing needs, the organization shall consider current knowledge and determine how to acquire necessary additional knowledge.

7.2 Competence

7.2.1 General: The organization shall: (a) determine the necessary competence of persons doing work that affects QMS performance; (b) ensure persons are competent based on appropriate education, training, or experience; (c) take actions to acquire necessary competence and evaluate effectiveness; (d) retain appropriate documented information as evidence of competence.

7.2.2 GMP Training: Additional training shall be conducted regularly and shall include:

  • Awareness of applicable GMP and all procedures/policies affecting product quality
  • The risk of contamination and cross-contamination
  • The potential hazard to end user/patient if product is contaminated
  • The impact of deviations from specified procedures, processes or specifications on customer's product quality or the end user

Particular attention shall be given to training of personnel involved with manufacture of sterile components or components to be subsequently sterilized. Specific training on microbiological and particulate contamination and the patient risk shall be provided.

Additional refresher training shall be carried out at defined intervals. Temporary personnel shall be trained or supervised by a trained person. Contractors and visitors shall receive appropriate instructions prior to entering manufacturing/production facilities.

7.3 Awareness

The organization shall ensure that persons doing work are aware of: (a) the quality policy; (b) relevant quality objectives; (c) their contribution to QMS effectiveness; (d) the implications of not conforming with QMS requirements.

7.4 Communication

The organization shall determine internal and external communications relevant to the QMS. GMP Addition: GMP and regulatory requirements shall be communicated, as appropriate, to each level of the organization. Top management shall be notified of quality critical situations in a timely manner.

7.5 Documented Information

7.5.1–7.5.2: The QMS shall include documented information required by this Standard and as determined necessary by the organization. When creating and updating documented information, the organization shall ensure appropriate identification, description, format, review and approval. Changes to documents shall be reviewed and approved by the original or another designated function. If electronic signatures are used, they shall be controlled to provide equivalent security to a hand-written signature.

7.5.3 Control of documented information:

  • 7.5.3.1: Documented information shall be available and suitable for use, and adequately protected
  • 7.5.3.2: The organization shall address distribution, access, retrieval and use; storage and preservation including legibility; version control; retention and disposition
  • 7.5.3.3: The organization shall define the period for retaining at least one copy of obsolete controlled documents; obsolete documents shall be stored and marked to prevent unintended use
  • 7.5.3.4: Records shall be controlled to provide evidence of conformity. Records shall remain legible, readily identifiable and retrievable
  • 7.5.3.5: Electronic records shall be subject to the same controls as other records
  • 7.5.3.6 (GMP): Entries in records shall be clear, indelible, made directly after performing the activity, dated, and initialled or signed. Corrections shall be dated, initialled or signed and explained, leaving the original entry still legible
  • 7.5.3.7: The organization shall define quality critical processes/parameters where a double-check is necessary for batch release
  • 7.5.3.8: For each batch, a record shall be established providing traceability and identifying quantity manufactured and quantity approved for distribution
  • 7.5.3.11: All manufacturing, control, testing, distribution and investigation records shall be retained for at least five years after the date of manufacture, or as agreed with the customer

7.5.4 Administration of IT systems and data: There shall be documented procedures for: responsibility assignment for IT and data security; network/file access control; file integrity on shared systems; password management and sleep mode; back-up and recovery of electronic product-related data (frequency, method, media, physical process for safe storage). The organization shall have a documented IT recovery plan. Access to legacy systems shall be defined as part of change control when IT systems are changed.

解讀與教學 Commentary

Clause 7.1.5 — 量測設備校準：GMP 的具體實踐

所有用於品質關鍵量測的設備都必須校準且保持記錄。這在包材製造中覆蓋範圍很廣：

  • 玻璃瓶生產：壁厚量具、容量量規、破裂強度測試機
  • 橡膠塞生產：硬度計、穿刺力測試儀、尺寸量測儀
  • 環境監測：粒子計數器、培養箱溫度計、壓差計
  • 分析實驗室：HPLC、天平、pH 計

校準狀態的識別（標籤）和量測設備清單是稽核查核的基本項目。

Clause 7.2.2 — GMP 訓練：超越 SOP 的患者安全意識

ISO 15378 的 GMP 訓練要求特別之處：不只要求員工知道「怎麼做」，還要理解「為什麼」——包括對患者的潛在危害。

這三個意識層次對應訓練設計：

  • 層次 1（知識）：污染的定義、類型、來源
  • 層次 2（理解）：污染如何經由包材傳遞到藥品並危害患者
  • 層次 3（行動）：偏差時應立即通報而非隱瞞，因為後果是患者安全

對無菌組件的人員，標準要求特別加強微生物和微粒污染的培訓——這是最高風險等級的要求。

臨時人員與承包商的 GMP 管控

Clause 7.2.2 明確要求：臨時工必須接受訓練或在受訓人員監督下工作；承包商和訪客在進入生產設施前必須接受適當指導。

實務風險：旺季大量聘用臨時工，卻未建立有效的快速訓練程序——是常見的 GMP 合規缺口，也是稽核發現的高頻觀察項。

記錄保存期限：五年最低要求

Clause 7.5.3.11 要求所有製造、管制、測試、配送和調查記錄保存至少五年，或依客戶協議（可能更長）。

實務注意：若藥廠客戶要求保存至藥品有效期後兩年（這可能是 10–15 年），供應商必須在品質協議中反映這個要求，並確保儲存系統（含 IT 備份）能支持長期保存。

Clause 7.5.4 — IT 系統管理：數據完整性的現代挑戰

ISO 15378:2017 明確要求建立 IT 系統管理程序，涵蓋：

  • 網路和檔案的存取控制（僅授權人員）
  • 共享伺服器的文件完整性保護
  • 密碼管理和螢幕鎖定（人員離開時）
  • 電子數據備份：頻率、方法、媒體、安全儲存
  • IT 系統災難復原計畫，並定期驗證

這些要求與 FDA 數據完整性指南（2018）和 MHRA 數據完整性指南（2021）的精神高度一致，反映了監管機構對電子化 GMP 環境的日益關注。

供應商稽核查核清單 — Clauses 4–7

  1. Cl. 4.4：品質手冊或主 SOP 是否明確將 GMP 納入 QMS 範圍？
  2. Cl. 5.1.3：最高管理層是否出席客戶稽核開幕會議？
  3. Cl. 5.3：品質部門是否有批次拒絕/暫停出貨的獨立決策記錄？
  4. Cl. 6.1.3：是否有涵蓋所有製造流程的風險評估（不只是 FMEA 一份）？
  5. Cl. 6.2：品質目標是否包含 GMP 合規指標（如偏差率、CAPA 完成率）？
  6. Cl. 7.1.5：校準設備清單是否完整，且設備上有效期標籤是否在效期內？
  7. Cl. 7.2.2：訓練記錄是否包含 GMP 意識、患者安全教育和refresher紀錄？
  8. Cl. 7.5.3.6：抽查批次記錄：是否有塗改未保留原值的情況？
  9. Cl. 7.5.3.11：記錄保存政策是否符合五年最低要求及客戶協議？
  10. Cl. 7.5.4：是否有書面的 IT 災難復原計畫，並有最近一次驗證記錄？

Clause 7.5 — 文件層級架構

GMP 文件管理的標準三層架構：

層級文件類型說明
第一層品質手冊 / 品質政策系統性描述 QMS 架構，引用 ISO 15378 條款
第二層標準作業程序（SOP）跨部門流程描述，說明「誰做什麼」
第三層工作指示（WI）/ 批次記錄具體操作步驟、記錄格式

版本控制是核心：每份受控文件必須有版本號、生效日期，並維護變更歷程。廢止文件必須標識並妥善保存，防止誤用。

Clause 7.5.3.6 — ALCOA 原則：GMP 記錄的黃金標準

ISO 15378 的記錄要求完整呼應 ALCOA 原則，這是 FDA/EMA 評估數據可靠性的基礎框架：

ALCOA 字母英文中文ISO 15378 對應要求
AAttributable可歸屬必須由執行活動的人員簽名/初始
LLegible易讀記錄必須清晰（clear, indelible）
CContemporaneous同步記錄必須在執行活動後立即記錄
OOriginal原始修改後原始記錄仍必須可讀
AAccurate準確更正必須有說明並保留原始值

電子記錄同等適用：Clause 7.5.3.5 明確規定電子記錄須受相同控制，連結 21 CFR Part 11 和 EU Annex 11 的精神。
