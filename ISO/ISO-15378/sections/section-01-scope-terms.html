Foreword — 前言

ISO 15378:2017 is the fourth edition, prepared by ISO/TC 76 (Transfusion, infusion and injection, and blood processing equipment for medical and pharmaceutical use). It cancels and replaces ISO 15378:2015 (third edition).

Key technical changes from the 2015 edition:

  • Integration of sector-specific GMP requirements directly into the ISO 9001:2015 framework
  • Deletion of the requirement for a quality manual
  • Inclusion of all annexes from ISO 9001:2015
  • Terminology aligned with ISO 9000:2015
  • Addition of an alphabetical index of defined terms

The document uses a visual convention: clauses quoted directly and unchanged from ISO 9001:2015 appear in boxes. GMP-specific additions appear outside boxes.

前言解析

第四版的重大意義：2017 版將 GMP 要求完全整合進 ISO 9001:2015 架構，不再是附加文件，而是真正的「雙軌標準」。

排版識別規則 — 如何閱讀這份標準

框框內的文字：直接引自 ISO 9001:2015 的原文，未修改。

框框外的文字：ISO 15378 新增的 GMP 特定要求或建議，這是初級包裝材料廠商需要額外滿足的部分。

讀標準時，先看框框外的文字，因為那才是 ISO 15378「超出 ISO 9001」的部分，也是供應商稽核時要特別檢查的重點。

刪除「品質手冊」要求

2015 版以前要求企業要有一份品質手冊 (quality manual)。2017 版配合 ISO 9001:2015，不再強制要求品質手冊的形式，但品質管理系統的文件化要求仍然存在，只是形式更彈性。

Introduction 0.1 — General 總則

This document identifies Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP) principles and specifies requirements for a quality management system applicable to primary packaging materials for medicinal products.

The realization of GMP principles in production and control of primary packaging materials is of great importance for patient safety, because of their direct product contact. The application of GMP for pharmaceutical packaging materials helps ensure that these materials meet the needs and requirements of the pharmaceutical industry.

"This document is an application standard for primary packaging materials, which contains the text of ISO 9001:2015."

Verbal forms used:

  • "shall" — a requirement (強制要求)
  • "should" — a recommendation (建議)
  • "may" — a permission (許可)
  • "can" — a possibility or capability (可能性)

The phrase "if appropriate" appears throughout the document. When a requirement is qualified by this phrase, it is deemed appropriate unless the organization can document a justification otherwise.

0.1 解析 — 標準的本質

ISO 15378 = ISO 9001 + GMP 的雙軌架構

可以把 ISO 15378 想成一棟建築：

  • 地基 = ISO 9001:2015（品質管理系統通用框架）
  • 上層建築 = GMP 特定要求（為藥品包裝材料量身訂製）

供應商若取得 ISO 15378 認證，代表他們的 QMS 同時滿足兩套要求，達到製藥等級的品質管理水準。

CDMO 稽核實務應用

當你在做橡膠塞或玻璃瓶供應商的資格審查 (supplier qualification) 時：

情境 A：供應商持有 ISO 15378 認證 → 可以縮短稽核範圍，重點確認認證有效性和近期監察報告，不必從零審查 QMS 框架。

情境 B：供應商只有 ISO 9001 認證 → 需要額外稽核 GMP 相關要求（生產控制、批次紀錄、留樣等），工作量大幅增加。

ISO 15378 認證就是供應商的「GMP 資格預審證明」。

"if appropriate" 的法律意涵

這個詞組很重要：ISO 15378 預設所有標有 "if appropriate" 的要求都是適用的，除非企業能提出書面理由說明為何不適用。這意味著「不適用」的舉證責任在企業身上，不能輕易略過。

Introduction 0.2 — Quality Management Principles 品質管理原則

ISO 9001:2015 is based on seven quality management principles described in ISO 9000. These principles form the philosophical foundation of the standard:

  1. Customer focus — 以顧客為核心
  2. Leadership — 領導力
  3. Engagement of people — 人員參與
  4. Process approach — 流程方法
  5. Improvement — 持續改進
  6. Evidence-based decision making — 基於證據的決策
  7. Relationship management — 關係管理

A key objective of this document is to specify GMP for primary packaging materials.

Introduction 0.3 — Process Approach 流程方法

0.3.1 General

This standard promotes the adoption of a process approach — understanding and managing interrelated processes as a system. This enables the organization to control interrelationships and interdependencies among processes, enhancing overall performance.

The application of the process approach enables:

  • Understanding and consistency in meeting requirements
  • Consideration of processes in terms of added value
  • Achievement of effective process performance
  • Improvement of processes based on evaluation of data and information

0.3.2 Plan-Do-Check-Act (PDCA) Cycle

The PDCA cycle can be applied to all processes and to the QMS as a whole:

  • Plan: Establish objectives and processes, identify risks/opportunities
  • Do: Implement what was planned
  • Check: Monitor and measure processes against policies and objectives
  • Act: Take actions to improve performance

0.3.3 Risk-Based Thinking

Risk-based thinking is essential for achieving an effective QMS. It enables an organization to determine factors that could cause deviation from planned results and put in place preventive controls.

"Because of the nature of primary packaging materials the risk-based approach is applied throughout all processes of the organization."

0.3 解析 — PDCA 與風險思維

PDCA 在玻璃瓶製造的實際循環

  • Plan：設定每批玻璃瓶的尺寸規格 (dimensional spec) 和微粒標準
  • Do：按 SOP 執行生產，進行線上控制 (IPC)
  • Check：最終檢驗 (final inspection)，比對規格，統計製程能力 (Cpk)
  • Act：發現不合格趨勢，調整成型參數或設備，更新 SOP

風險思維的具體化

ISO 15378 特別強調：由於初級包裝材料直接接觸藥品，風險思維必須貫穿所有流程，不只是設計或驗證階段。

舉例：橡膠塞供應商必須評估矽化處理 (siliconization) 對相容性的風險；鋁蓋廠商必須評估壓合力 (crimp force) 對封閉完整性的影響。

這就是為什麼 3.11 特別設有「風險管理相關術語」這個子章節。

CDMO 稽核查核點

  1. 請供應商展示其 PDCA 改進紀錄（CAPA 清單 + 完成率）
  2. 查看是否有書面的風險評估 (risk assessment) 報告，涵蓋關鍵製程步驟
  3. 確認風險評估工具是否適當（FMEA、FTA 或 HACCP 均可接受）

Introduction 0.4 — Relationship with Other Standards 與其他標準的關係

This document incorporates the requirements of ISO 9001:2015 and, additionally, particular requirements for primary packaging materials derived and adapted from GMP for the production and control of medicinal products.

ISO 15378 relates to other ISO standards as follows:

  • ISO 9000:2015 — provides essential background for understanding ISO 9001
  • ISO 9004 — provides guidance for organizations progressing beyond ISO 9001 requirements

This standard does not include requirements specific to environmental management, occupational health and safety, or financial management. It is a sector-specific QMS standard for primary packaging materials manufacturers.

Section 1 — Scope 適用範圍

ISO 9001:2015 Scope (incorporated)

This International Standard specifies requirements for a quality management system when an organization:

  • (a) needs to demonstrate its ability to consistently provide products and services that meet customer and applicable statutory and regulatory requirements, and
  • (b) aims to enhance customer satisfaction through the effective application of the system, including processes for improvement and assurance of conformity to requirements.

All requirements are generic and intended to be applicable to any organization, regardless of type, size, or the products and services it provides.

ISO 15378 Additional Scope

In addition to ISO 9001, this document specifies GMP requirements applicable to primary packaging materials for a quality management system where an organization needs to demonstrate its ability to provide primary packaging materials for medicinal products, which consistently meet:

  • Customer requirements
  • Regulatory requirements
  • International Standards

This document is an application standard for the design, manufacture and supply of primary packaging materials for medicinal products.

Section 1 解析 — 誰需要遵循？

適用對象：初級包裝材料製造商

ISO 15378 適用於設計、製造和供應初級包裝材料的組織。以下是常見的初級包裝材料：

  • 玻璃小瓶 (glass vials)、安瓶 (ampoules)
  • 橡膠塞 (rubber stoppers / closures)
  • 鋁蓋 / 壓蓋 (aluminum crimp caps / seals)
  • 塑料容器 (HDPE/PP/PET bottles)
  • 預填充注射器部件 (prefilled syringe components)：筒體 (barrel)、活塞塞 (plunger stopper)、針頭護罩 (needle shield)、端蓋 (tip cap)
  • 泡殼包裝材料 (blister foils)：鋁箔 (aluminum foil)、PVC/PVDC 底材
  • IV 袋 (intravenous bags)

不適用範圍

  • 次級包裝（紙箱、說明書、標籤）→ 不直接接觸藥品
  • 合約實驗室（只做檢測，不製造包裝材料）
  • 經銷商（只轉售，不涉及設計或製造）
  • 藥品製造商本身（應遵循 EU GMP、ICH Q10 等）

CDMO QA 實務：供應商稽核的起點

  1. 確認供應商是否持有有效的 ISO 15378 認證書（Certificate of Registration）
  2. 核對認證範圍是否涵蓋你採購的具體產品類別（例如：橡膠塞 vs 玻璃瓶是不同範疇）
  3. 確認認證機構 (CB) 是否為 IAF 認可的 accredited body
  4. 查詢認證到期日及最近一次監督稽核 (surveillance audit) 的結果

Section 2 解析 — 規範性引用的意義

規範性 vs 資訊性引用

規範性引用 (normative reference)：引用文件構成 ISO 15378 的一部分，必須遵守。

資訊性引用 (informative reference)：提供補充背景，不強制要求。

ISO 14698-1 和 -2 被列為規範性引用，代表潔淨室生物污染控制方法是 ISO 15378 的強制要求組成部分。

為何 ISO 14698 被納入？

初級包裝材料（特別是橡膠塞和注射器部件）的生產環境需要控制微生物污染。ISO 14698 定義了生物污染控制的系統方法，包括環境監控 (EM) 程序、樣品採集、數據解釋。

這意味著 ISO 15378 認證工廠的潔淨室生物污染控制必須符合 ISO 14698 的方法論，而不是任意設計的 EM 程序。

Section 3 概覽 — 為什麼術語定義很重要

術語統一的實務意義

在供應商協議 (Quality Agreement, Technical Agreement) 中，雙方必須使用一致的術語，否則容易產生歧義和糾紛。ISO 15378 的術語定義提供了客觀的法律依據。

例如："batch"（批次）和 "lot"（批）在此標準中是同義詞，但如果藥廠和供應商對此有不同理解，可能導致可追溯性記錄不完整。

ISO 15378 新增術語 vs ISO 9000:2015 原有術語

ISO 15378 只重複定義 ISO 9000:2015 中已修改的術語（例如 organization、validation）。其他未列出的術語，直接引用 ISO 9000:2015 的原始定義。

因此閱讀 ISO 15378 Section 3 時，應同時參照 ISO 9000:2015。

3.1 Terms Related to Organization — 與組織相關的術語

3.1.1 organization

Person or group of people that has its own functions with responsibilities, authorities and relationships to achieve its objectives.

Note 2 (ISO 15378 specific): In this document the organization is the company manufacturing the primary packaging material (3.6.4).

[Modified from ISO 9000:2015, 3.2.1]

3.1.2 quality unit

Organizational unit which fulfils both quality assurance (QA) and quality control (QC) responsibilities.

Note: The quality unit(s) can consist of separate QA and QC units or of a single individual (or group), depending upon the size and structure of the organization.

3.1 解析

organization — 在本標準中的特定含義

ISO 9001 的「組織」泛指任何類型的公司；ISO 15378 特別說明，在本標準語境中，組織是指製造初級包裝材料的公司。這個澄清很重要，確保標準的適用主體不被誤解為藥品製造商或其他角色。

quality unit — GMP 的核心組織架構

QU（品質單位）是 GMP 環境中的核心概念，相當於藥廠的「QA 部門」，但在小型包裝材料廠可能是一個人兼任。

關鍵職能：

  • 批次放行 (batch release) 決定
  • 偏差 (deviation) 調查和核准
  • 供應商稽核
  • 文件控制和 SOP 審核

稽核時要確認 QU 在組織架構中的獨立性，不能直接向生產部門報告。

3.2 Terms Related to Activity — 與活動相關的術語

3.2.1 assembly

Fitting together of primary packaging materials and/or components.

Example: Pipette assemblies for filling, prepared components of injection systems, positioning of needle shields on prefillable syringes.

3.2.2 change control

Documented control of changes with an appropriate risk management.

Note: Changes can include changes in raw materials, specifications, facilities, equipment, production processes and test methods.

3.2.3 Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP)

Manufacturing, quality control and quality assurance applied in quality control.

Note 3: GMP for primary packaging materials requires, in addition to suitable provision of personnel, premises and equipment, a QMS that includes controls for incoming starting materials, manufacture, documentation, factory hygiene, final inspection, distribution records, processing of complaints and self-inspection.

Note 4: GMP and cGMP are equivalent.

3.2.4 installational qualification (IQ)

Process of obtaining and documenting evidence that equipment has been provided and installed in accordance with its specification.

[SOURCE: ISO/TS 11139:2006, 2.22]

3.2.5 operational qualification (OQ)

Process of obtaining and documenting evidence that installed equipment operates within predetermined limits when used in accordance with its operational procedures.

[SOURCE: ISO/TS 11139:2006, 2.27]

3.2.6 origination / artwork

All preparative activities prior to print.

Note: These include concept, design, graphics, reprographics, film, plate making, silk screens and digital files and masters.

3.2.7 performance qualification (PQ)

Verification that the proposed specification for the facility, equipment or system is suitable for the intended use.

[SOURCE: ISO/TS 11139:2006, 2.30]

3.2.8 qualification

Process to demonstrate the ability to fulfil specified requirements.

Note 2: Qualification comprises design qualification (DQ), IQ (3.2.4), OQ (3.2.5) and could include PQ (3.2.7) as well as re-qualification.

Note 3: Qualification can be applied to facilities, equipment and utilities.

3.2.9 quality control

Part of quality management focused on fulfilling quality requirements.

Note: Quality control includes checking or testing that specifications are met.

[Modified from ISO 9000:2015, 3.3.7]

3.2 解析 — 活動術語

change control — 變更控制的 GMP 意義

變更控制是 GMP 最核心的管理工具之一。ISO 15378 特別強調變更控制必須「文件化」，且需進行適當的風險管理。

為何如此重要？初級包裝材料的任何變更（哪怕只是原料供應商改變）都可能影響藥品的安全性和穩定性。藥廠可能需要重新進行相容性測試 (compatibility testing)，甚至向監管機構申報。

從 CDMO QA 角度：供應商必須在變更前及時通知你，並提供充分的技術資料，讓你評估是否需要重新驗證 (revalidation)。

IQ / OQ / PQ — 確效三部曲

這是 GMP 環境中設備確效的標準步驟：

確效類型 問的問題 典型文件
IQ 安裝確效 設備是否按規格安裝？ 安裝檢核清單、儀器清單
OQ 操作確效 設備在操作範圍內是否正常運作？ 操作範圍測試報告
PQ 性能確效 設備是否能持續生產符合規格的產品？ 製程驗證報告

ISO 15378 在 3.2.7 特別說明：PQ 是一種 verification（確認），即提供客觀證據證明規格的適用性。

qualification vs validation — 常見混淆澄清

Qualification（確效/鑑定）：用於設備、設施、公用系統（utility）——證明它們能滿足規格要求。

Validation（驗證）：用於製造流程、清潔程序、分析方法——證明流程能一致地產出預期結果。

簡單說：確效你的洗瓶機 (IQ/OQ/PQ)，驗證洗瓶製程（清潔驗證）。

3.3 Terms Related to System — 與系統相關的術語

3.3.1 airlock

Enclosed space to control air-flow.

Note: Typically has at least two interlocked doors between two or more rooms, used either by people or for goods, to control different conditions (cleanliness, air-flow).

3.3.2 calibration

Process of checking or adjusting (by comparison with a reference standard) the accuracy of a measuring instrument.

Note: Calibration establishes the relationship between values indicated by a measuring instrument and corresponding known values of a reference standard.

3.3.3 cleanroom

Room within which the number concentration of airborne particles is controlled and classified, and which is designed, constructed and operated in a manner to control the introduction, generation and retention of particles inside the room.

[SOURCE: ISO 14644-1:2015, 3.1.1]

3.3.4 clean zone

Defined space within which the number concentration of airborne particles is controlled and classified, and which is constructed and operated in a manner to control the introduction, generation and retention of contaminants inside the space.

Note 3: A clean zone can be a defined space within a cleanroom or achieved by a separative device (isolator, RABS).

[SOURCE: ISO 14644-1:2015, 3.1.2]

3.3.5 controlled area / controlled environment

Area or environment constructed and operated to control the possible introduction of potential contaminants.

3.3.6 working area

Defined area where origination, production, packaging, test or inspection operations are carried out and where such activity will usually be subject to a line clearance.

3.3 解析 — 系統術語

airlock — 氣閘的功能

氣閘是防止污染跨越潔淨等級的關鍵設施。ISO 15378 定義氣閘的核心功能是「控制氣流」，而非僅是一個過渡空間。

在初級包裝材料廠，氣閘用於：

  • 人員進入潔淨區前的更衣/消毒
  • 物料從非潔淨區進入潔淨區的傳遞
  • 防止不同潔淨等級之間的壓差失控

cleanroom vs clean zone — 兩者的區別

Cleanroom（潔淨室）：一個完整的房間，結構設計本身就為了控制微粒。

Clean zone（潔淨區）：潔淨室內的特定空間，或由分離裝置（如隔離器 isolator、RABS）形成的區域。

例如：整個灌裝室是潔淨室（ISO Class 8），其中的 RABS 填充區是潔淨區（ISO Class 5）。

注意：ISO 15378 引用 ISO 14644-1 的定義，確保術語與潔淨室標準一致。

calibration — 校準與 GMP 的連結

在 GMP 環境中，所有用於產品放行和製程控制的量測儀器都必須定期校準。ISO 15378 把校準定義為與參考標準比對的過程，強調可追溯性 (traceability to national/international standards)。

稽核時要確認：校準計畫是否完整？是否有逾期未校準的儀器？校準結果是否在接受標準內？

3.4 Terms Related to Requirement — 與要求相關的術語

3.4.1 expiration date

Expected suitable use limit; shelf-life.

Note 2: This is typically the period during which a primary packaging material is expected to remain suitable for use if stored under defined conditions, and after which it should not be used.

3.4.2 shelf-life

Period during which a primary packaging material is expected to comply with the requirements (specifications).

3.4 解析

expiration date vs shelf-life — 初級包裝材料的效期

藥品有效期大家都熟悉，但初級包裝材料本身也有效期，這是很多人忽略的重點。

以橡膠塞為例：矽化 (siliconization) 效果可能隨時間降低，或溶出物 (extractables) 可能在長期存放後超標。因此供應商必須定義：

  • 在何種儲存條件下（溫度、濕度、光照）
  • 材料可以保持規格符合性多久（shelf-life）
  • 逾期後不得使用（expiration date）

CDMO 的收料程序 (receiving procedure) 必須核查包裝材料的效期，避免使用過期材料。

3.5 Terms Related to Process — 與過程相關的術語

3.5.1 batch / lot

Defined quantity of primary packaging material manufactured in one process or series of processes intended to have uniform characteristics with consistent, homogeneous quality.

Note 1: A batch can be divided into sub-batches that are later combined to form a single consistent batch.

Note 2: In continuous production, the batch is a fraction of production defined as a fixed quantity or fixed time interval.

3.5.2 contamination

Introduction of any unwanted material into the primary packaging material.

Note 1: A finished product can be contaminated by physical (particulate), chemical or biological (bioburden and endotoxin) action.

3.5.3 cross-contamination

Contamination of a material or of a product with another material or product.

Note 1: Cross-contamination can also be referred to as mix-up or admixture.

3.5.4 line clearance

Removal (line purge) of everything associated with the prior production run.

Note: Line clearance is done prior to a production run to prevent any error and cross-contamination. The production facility (line) and its associated working area must be completely clear of all materials, waste, products, samples, documents from the previous run.

3.5.5 manufacturing

All operations including purchasing and receipt of materials to production, packaging, labelling, quality control, release, storage, distribution of products and the related controls.

3.5.6 process aids

Material used to facilitate process realization. The material is not included in the product specification and can be removed at or before the final processing stage.

Example: Mould release agents, compressed air, rolling lubricants.

3.5.7 production

Processes resulting in primary packaging material, from receipt of starting materials through processing and packaging, to completion as a finished product.

3.5.8 quarantine

Status of materials or products isolated pending a decision on their subsequent approval or rejection.

Note: Quarantined material is typically isolated by physical or other effective means.

3.5.9 realization

Result of applying all processes required to achieve the desired output from design to product delivery.

3.5.10 reconditioning

Processing or reprocessing primary packaging material to meet specification requirements.

3.5.11 reprocessing

Repeating part of a production process.

Note: Continuation of part of a process after an in-process control test has shown that the part is incomplete is considered normal process, not reprocessing.

3.5.12 retained samples

Materials or finished products stored for future reference.

Note: Samples are taken in a sufficient amount and stored under recommended conditions for reference during a defined period of time.

3.5.13 starting material

Raw material, components and substances used to produce primary packaging materials.

3.5.14 surface treatment

Process to improve primary packaging material surface.

Example: Siliconization or other treatment of internal glass surfaces, coating of internal or external surfaces of glass containers or rubber parts.

3.5 解析 — 過程術語

batch/lot — 批次的核心地位

批次是 GMP 可追溯性的基本單位。所有的品質紀錄、測試結果、放行決定都與特定批次掛鉤。

ISO 15378 的定義強調兩個關鍵特性：

  • 均一性 (uniform characteristics)：同一批次的所有產品應具有一致的品質屬性
  • 均質性 (homogeneous quality)：批次內不允許有系統性的品質差異

批次定義對於抽樣計畫 (sampling plan) 的設計至關重要——必須清楚知道批次的邊界在哪裡。

contamination 的三種形式

ISO 15378 特別列出三種污染類型，這對於包裝材料廠特別重要：

  • 物理污染 (physical/particulate)：玻璃碎片、金屬微粒、纖維
  • 化學污染 (chemical)：溶出物 (leachables)、殘留清潔劑、塑化劑
  • 生物污染 (biological)：微生物 (bioburden)、內毒素 (endotoxin)

這三類污染都可能危害藥品安全，因此包裝材料廠必須針對每一類建立控制措施。

reprocessing vs reconditioning — 重要區別

Reprocessing（再加工）：重複部分生產步驟。例如橡膠塞第一次矽化效果不符標準，重新矽化。需要有書面的核准程序。

Reconditioning（重新整備）：對初級包裝材料進行加工或再加工以達到規格要求。概念更廣，包含外部重新處理。

兩者都需要品質單位的評估和核准，並在批次紀錄中記錄。

surface treatment — 表面處理對相容性的影響

表面處理是初級包裝材料特有的概念。以矽化 (siliconization) 為例：

  • 目的：減少橡膠塞與注射器筒之間的摩擦，確保順暢滑動
  • 風險：矽油可能遷移到藥品中 (leaching)，影響蛋白質藥物 (biologics) 的穩定性
  • GMP 要求：矽化程度必須在規格範圍內，並有驗證數據支持

藥廠在進行 extractables & leachables (E&L) 研究時，必須了解所使用包裝材料的表面處理情況。

quarantine — 隔離狀態的實務管理

隔離狀態是 GMP 物料管理的關鍵概念。物料從收料到放行前，必須處於隔離狀態，防止未經放行的物料被誤用。

ISO 15378 說明隔離可以是「物理隔離」（獨立區域）或「其他有效方式」（如電腦系統中的隔離標記）。現代倉庫管理系統 (WMS) 通常以電子方式實現隔離管理，但必須有適當的系統驗證 (IT system validation)。

3.6 Terms Related to Results — 與結果相關的術語

3.6.1 finished product

Primary packaging material which has completed all stages of production.

3.6.2 intermediate product

Primary packaging material which has completed some but not all production stages.

Note: An intermediate product needs further processing before it becomes a finished product.

3.6.3 medicinal product

Substance or combination of substances presented for treating or preventing disease in human beings or animals.

Note 3: Medicinal products can also be referred to as pharmaceutical or drug products, including clinical trial products.

3.6.4 primary packaging material

Material used in pharmaceutical packaging which will contain, seal or be used for dose application of a medicinal product and which will have direct contact with the medicinal product.

Secondary packaging examples (NOT primary): Printed or unprinted cartons, labels, leaflets or inserts (or outserts), over-wraps, and transit containers such as folding boxes.

3.6.5 secondary packaging materials

Non-contact packaging materials.

Example: Printed or unprinted cartons, labels, leaflets, inserts, over-wraps, transit containers (folding boxes).

3.6 解析 — 結果術語

primary packaging material — 標準的核心概念

這是整份標準最重要的定義。直接接觸藥品是初級包裝材料的決定性特徵。

典型的初級包裝材料：

  • 玻璃容器：小瓶 (vials)、安瓶 (ampoules)、卡匣 (cartridges)
  • 橡膠製品：瓶塞 (stoppers)、活塞 (plungers)、O 型環
  • 金屬製品：鋁壓蓋 (aluminum seals/crimp caps)
  • 塑料容器：HDPE/PP 藥瓶、PET 容器、IV 袋
  • 泡殼材料：PVC/PVDC 底材、鋁蓋箔 (lidding foil)
  • 注射器組件：筒體、活塞塞、針頭護罩

次級包裝材料不在 ISO 15378 範圍內

紙箱、標籤、說明書等不直接接觸藥品的包裝材料，不屬於 ISO 15378 的管轄範疇。這些材料的供應商只需符合 ISO 9001 或其他適用標準。

但注意：印有藥品資訊的標籤雖然是次級包裝，但因為攸關藥品安全資訊，仍受 EU GMP Annex C (Annex 15378 Annex C) 和 EU GMP Part II 的相關規定約束。

intermediate product — 在過程中的品質控制

以橡膠塞製造為例：

  • 混煉後的橡膠 → starting material
  • 成型後、未矽化的橡膠塞 → intermediate product
  • 矽化、清洗、包裝後的橡膠塞 → finished product

每個階段都需要有 in-process controls 和適當的隔離管理。

3.7 Terms Related to Data, Information and Document — 與資料、資訊及文件相關的術語

3.7.1 approved

Confirmed conformity status for any stage of the process [starting materials, process aids, packaging material or finished product].

3.7.2 batch document / batch record

Documents and records that provide a history of the batch, including information relating to its production and control, and which facilitate its traceability.

3.7.3 batch number / lot number

Unique identifier to identify a batch or lot. Can be a combination of numbers, letters and/or symbols; from this the production and distribution history can be determined.

3.7.4 date of manufacture

Date on which one of the first stages in the process of manufacture of the primary packaging material, or the packaging, or the final release, occurs. Can be subject to customer agreement.

3.7.5 deviation

Departure from an approved standard operating procedure (SOP) or established standard.

3.7.6 documented procedure

Procedure that is established, documented, authorized, implemented and maintained.

Note: Documentation can be in any form or type of medium. See SOP (3.7.10).

3.7.7 double-check

Documented verification of an activity, result or record by a second person or system.

Note: Typically, double-checks are signed by a second person. Includes in-process control check signatures and quality records signed by a second person.

3.7.8 out of specification (OOS)

Test results that do not comply with the specification.

3.7.9 rejected

Status of starting materials, quality (process aids), intermediate products or finished products whose test results do not comply with one or more specification requirements, and which have been deemed — usually by the quality unit — as not suitable for use.

3.7.10 standard operating procedure (SOP)

Authorized documented procedure, or set of procedures, work instructions and test instructions.

3.7.11 user requirement specification (URS)

Approved document that states the product specifications of the material produced on this equipment as well as functional, operational and/or technical aspects of the equipment or process required to produce the desired product.

3.7.12 validation

Confirmation, through the provision of objective evidence, that the requirements for a specific intended use or application have been fulfilled.

Note 4 (ISO 15378 specific): Validation can be applied to processes, products and software.

[Modified from ISO 9000:2015, 3.8.13]

3.7.13 verification

Confirmation, through the provision of objective evidence, that specified requirements have been fulfilled.

Note 4 (ISO 15378 specific): In this document the term verification is used to ensure manufacturing systems are properly installed and operating correctly; alternatively this could be done by IQ and OQ.

[Modified from ISO 9000:2015, 3.8.12]

3.7 解析 — 文件術語

batch record — GMP 的核心文件

批次紀錄是 GMP 可追溯性的根本。ISO 15378 定義批次紀錄必須：

  • 提供批次的完整歷史（生產 + 控制）
  • 能夠追溯 (facilitate traceability)

一份完整的批次紀錄應包含：原料資訊、設備編號、操作步驟、IPC 結果、偏差記錄、最終放行簽核。

CDMO 在評估包裝材料供應商時，應要求查閱批次紀錄的樣本，確認記錄完整性和清晰度。

validation vs verification — 最常見的混淆

術語 核心問題 應用對象
Validation（驗證） 這個流程能否一致地達到預期用途？ 製程、清潔程序、分析方法、軟體
Verification（確認） 這個特定要求是否已被滿足？ 設備安裝 (IQ/OQ)、設計輸出審核

ISO 15378 在 3.7.13 特別補充：verification 也可以透過 IQ 和 OQ 來執行，說明這兩個概念在 GMP 設備確效框架中的連結。

deviation — 偏差管理的法規要求

偏差的定義是「偏離核准的 SOP 或建立的標準」。這意味著即使最終產品符合規格，偏差本身就需要調查和記錄。

GMP 偏差管理的步驟：

  1. 發現偏差 → 立即紀錄
  2. 隔離相關批次（如需要）
  3. 根本原因分析 (RCA)
  4. 評估對產品品質的影響
  5. 制定 CAPA
  6. 品質單位核准後才能放行

OOS — 超出規格結果的處理

OOS 定義清晰：測試結果不符合規格。這觸發正式的 OOS 調查程序，包括：

  • Phase 1：實驗室調查（排除分析誤差）
  • Phase 2：製造調查（是否有製程問題）
  • 最終決定：重新測試（如有合理理由）或拒收/報廢

ISO 15378 的 OOS 管理框架直接對應 FDA OOS 指引（21 CFR context）和 EU GMP Chapter 6 的要求。

URS — 設備採購的起點文件

URS（使用者需求規格）是確效生命週期的第一步。它定義了：

  • 設備需要生產什麼產品
  • 功能性要求（產能、速度）
  • 操作要求（環境條件、清潔方式）
  • 技術要求（材質、電氣規格）

URS 必須在採購前核准，並作為後續 IQ/OQ/PQ 的基準文件。

3.8 Terms Related to Action — 與行動相關的術語

3.8.1 batch release

Decision to release the batch for sale or supply, following a formal review of the batch document performed by the quality unit or a person authorized by the quality unit.

3.8.2 rejection

Process whereby starting materials, process aids, intermediate products or finished products which have been deemed — usually by the quality unit — as not suitable for use.

3.8.3 return

Process for sending back primary packaging material(s) to the organization.

3.8.4 rework

Action on a nonconforming product or service to make it conform to the requirements.

Note 1: Rework can affect or change parts of the nonconforming product.

Note 2 (ISO 15378 specific): Sorting can be considered to be rework.

[Modified from ISO 9000:2015, 3.12.8]

3.8 解析 — 行動術語

batch release — 批次放行的決策架構

批次放行是品質系統中最重要的決策節點。ISO 15378 明確要求：

  • 必須進行正式審查（不是非正式的口頭確認）
  • 必須審查批次紀錄（不只是測試結果）
  • 必須由品質單位或其授權人員執行（確保獨立性）

從藥廠 CDMO 的角度：你收到的包裝材料 CoA（合格證書）背後，代表供應商的品質單位已審核並簽核了所有批次紀錄。稽核時應查閱放行 SOP 和授權人員清單。

rework — 返工的特殊規定

ISO 15378 特別補充：分選 (sorting) 可以視為返工。這在初級包裝材料廠很常見：

例如：一批玻璃瓶中發現部分有氣泡缺陷，需要逐瓶目視檢查並剔除不良品。這個分選過程按照 ISO 15378 的定義屬於 rework，必須有核准的 SOP 和記錄。

任何返工都需要品質單位評估對產品品質的影響，並決定是否需要重新測試。

3.9 Terms Related to Characteristic — 與特性相關的術語

3.9.1 homogeneity

Uniformity of characteristics and their values throughout a defined quantity of material.

Note: Homogeneity can include uniformity of materials or certain characteristics of materials of special significance.

3.9.2 sterile

Free from viable microorganisms.

[SOURCE: ISO/TS 11139:2006, 2.43]

3.9 解析

homogeneity — 與 batch 定義的連結

均質性是批次定義的核心特徵。橡膠配方的均質性確保批次內所有橡膠塞的機械性能（硬度、彈性、穿刺力）一致；玻璃成分的均質性確保熱膨脹係數一致，防止熱應力破裂。

sterile — 初級包裝材料的無菌要求

不是所有初級包裝材料都需要無菌狀態，但用於無菌製劑的材料通常需要。ISO 15378 採用 ISO/TS 11139 的定義：無菌 = 不含存活微生物。

橡膠塞和玻璃瓶通常由藥廠在使用前進行最終滅菌（高溫蒸汽、乾熱、EO 等），或以輻射滅菌後的形式供應。無論哪種方式，供應商必須提供符合規格的生物負荷 (bioburden) 數據。

3.10 Terms Related to Determination — 與確定相關的術語

3.10.1 automated inspection

Conformity evaluation performed by inspection equipment without manual intervention.

Note: Equipment can include optoelectronics (cameras), laser systems, ultrasonics and their associated data processing functions.

3.10.2 final inspection

Tests carried out on the finished product to determine compliance with the specification.

3.10.3 in-process control

Actions taken during the production process to test product conformity to its specification.

Note 1: Monitoring processes and adjusting means of production can be necessary to meet product requirements.

Note 2: The control of the environment or equipment can also be regarded as part of in-process control.

3.10.4 reconciliation

Comparison between the amount of finished product theoretically and actually produced or used, making allowance for normal variation.

Note: The comparison considers waste, samples or other losses inherent in the process.

3.10 解析

automated inspection — 現代品質控制的核心

自動化檢驗在初級包裝材料廠已是標配。ISO 15378 特別定義此術語，並提供具體技術例子（光電、雷射、超音波），說明自動化檢驗系統的多樣性。

對 CDMO QA 而言，稽核自動化檢驗系統時需確認：

  • 設備已完成確效（IQ/OQ/PQ）
  • 拒絕邏輯和閾值設定有書面依據
  • 假拒率和假接率定期評估
  • 系統校準紀錄完整

in-process control (IPC) — 製程中的品質關卡

IPC 是在生產過程中（而非最終產品）進行的品質確認。ISO 15378 特別補充：環境控制和設備控制也屬於 IPC 的一部分。

玻璃瓶製造的典型 IPC 項目：

  • 尺寸檢查（容積、壁厚、瓶口尺寸）
  • 外觀目視或自動化檢查（氣泡、結石、裂紋）
  • 模式重量監控
  • 潔淨室溫濕度和壓差記錄（環境 IPC）

reconciliation — 物料平衡的重要性

物料平衡是 GMP 防止混淆和計算錯誤的重要工具。公式：理論產量 = 實際產量 + 廢料 + 樣品 + 其他可解釋的損失。

如果物料平衡超出可接受範圍，必須調查是否有產品丟失或混批的可能性。這也是防止藥品包裝材料流入非預期通路的控制手段之一。

3.11 Terms Relating to Risk Management — 與風險管理相關的術語

3.11.1 quality critical

Parameter affecting primary packaging material quality.

Note: A material, process step or process condition, test requirement or any other relevant parameter can be considered quality critical if nonconformity to its requirements could have significant detrimental consequences.

3.11.2 risk analysis

Process to comprehend the nature of risk and to determine the level of risk.

Note 1: Risk analysis provides the basis for risk evaluation and decisions about risk treatment.

Note 2: Risk analysis includes risk estimation.

[SOURCE: ISO Guide 73:2009, 3.6.1]

3.11.3 risk assessment

Overall process of risk identification, risk analysis and risk evaluation.

[SOURCE: ISO Guide 73:2009, 3.4.1]

3.11.4 risk evaluation

Process of comparing the results of risk analysis with risk criteria to determine whether the risk and/or its magnitude is acceptable or tolerable.

Note: Risk evaluation assists in the decision about risk treatment.

[SOURCE: ISO Guide 73:2009, 3.7.1]

3.11.5 risk identification

Process of finding, recognizing and describing risks.

Note 1: Risk identification involves the identification of risk sources, events, their causes and their potential consequences.

Note 2: Risk identification can involve historical data, theoretical analysis, informed and expert opinions, and stakeholder's needs.

[SOURCE: ISO Guide 73:2009, 3.5.1]

3.11.6 risk management

Coordinated activities to direct and control an organization with regard to risk.

[SOURCE: ISO Guide 73:2009, 2.1]

3.11 解析 — 風險管理術語（ISO 15378 的特色）

質量關鍵 (quality critical) — 最重要的術語之一

"Quality critical" 是 ISO 15378 獨有的術語，在其他 ISO 標準中不常見。這個概念直接對應製藥產業的 Critical Quality Attributes (CQA) 和 Critical Process Parameters (CPP) 概念。

判斷標準：若某個參數不符合要求，可能對患者安全產生重大不利後果，則為 quality critical。

包裝材料的 quality critical 參數範例：

  • 橡膠塞的穿刺粒子 (coring) 傾向 → 直接影響注射安全
  • 玻璃瓶的封閉完整性 → 關乎無菌屏障
  • 鋁蓋的壓合力 (crimp force) → 決定瓶蓋密封性
  • 泡殼鋁箔的密封強度 → 防止藥品暴露於濕氣

風險管理的完整流程

ISO 15378 按照 ISO Guide 73 定義了風險管理的完整術語鏈：

步驟 術語 核心問題
1 Risk identification (3.11.5) 有哪些風險？
2 Risk analysis (3.11.2) 風險的性質和程度？
3 Risk evaluation (3.11.4) 風險是否可接受？
1+2+3 Risk assessment (3.11.3) 完整的評估過程
全程 Risk management (3.11.6) 協調指揮控制所有風險活動

與 ICH Q9 的對應關係

ISO 15378 的風險管理術語與 ICH Q9 (Quality Risk Management) 高度一致，因為兩者都引用 ISO Guide 73。這意味著已熟悉 ICH Q9 的製藥人員，在理解 ISO 15378 的風險管理要求時幾乎不需要重新學習術語。

CDMO 常用的風險工具（FMEA、Fishbone、FTA）在 ISO 15378 的風險管理框架中都是可接受的方法。

供應商稽核重點：風險管理成熟度評估

  1. 要求查閱最近一次對製程的正式風險評估報告（特別是 quality critical 參數的識別）
  2. 確認 change control 流程中是否有系統性的風險評估步驟
  3. 查核是否對新原料或新設備進行了風險評估
  4. 評估供應商是否能清晰說明哪些製程步驟是 quality critical，以及控制策略

ISO 15378 雙軌架構總結

ISO 15378 = ISO 9001 + GMP：哪裡不同？

相較於純粹的 ISO 9001，ISO 15378 在以下領域增加了 GMP 特定要求：

  • 術語層面：新增 batch, GMP, IQ/OQ/PQ, validation, quality critical, line clearance 等 GMP 特有術語
  • 組織層面：強制要求設立 quality unit，確保 QA/QC 職能的獨立性
  • 文件層面：增加 batch record, SOP, URS 等 GMP 文件要求
  • 製程層面：增加 IPC、change control、deviation management、reconciliation
  • 風險層面：增加 quality critical 概念和 risk management 術語體系

如何向非技術人員解釋 ISO 15378

想像建造一棟安全的建築：

  • ISO 9001 = 一般建築法規（結構完整、防火、電氣安全）
  • ISO 15378 = 醫院建築法規（一般建築法規 + 醫療等級的額外要求：無菌區、手術室標準、醫療氣體系統等）

ISO 15378 認證就是告訴藥廠：這家供應商的「工廠」已按醫療等級標準建造和運作，不需要你從頭審查。
