Annex C (Normative) — GMP Requirements for Printed Primary Packaging Materials

附錄C（規範性）：印刷初級包材的GMP要求 | p60-p63

Annex C — Introduction & Status

This annex sets out GMP requirements for printed primary packaging materials. It is normative — meaning its requirements are mandatory and binding on organizations that manufacture printed packaging materials covered by this standard.

The annex covers three main areas:

  • C.1 — Artwork/origination and print impression media controls
  • C.2 — Print and conversion processes (including digital printing)
  • C.3 — Security code systems and reel material controls

解說：為什麼附錄C特別重要？

規範性（Normative）= 強制性

ISO標準中，附錄分為兩類：

  • Normative（規範性）：與正文條款同等效力，必須遵守
  • Informative（資訊性）：提供指導，非強制

附錄C是規範性的 — 所有製造印刷初級包材的組織均必須遵守。這與附錄D（資訊性的驗證指引）形成對比。

患者安全核心

印刷包材上的文字直接影響患者用藥安全：錯誤的劑量說明、過期日期格式錯誤、或與其他產品混裝，都可能造成嚴重後果。附錄C的每項要求都指向同一個目標：確保「正確的標籤裝在正確的產品上」。

C.1

C.1 Artwork/Origination and Print Impression Media

藝術稿件／原版與印刷壓印媒介的管控

C.1.1 General — Artwork File Control

C.1.1.1 Artwork/origination files shall be named and stored according to a documented procedure that enables them to be readily identified, issue controlled and traceable.

C.1.1.2 All print impression media shall be:

  • a) clearly and uniquely identified such that it is traceable to the origination material,
  • b) produced from, and traceable to, the master origination material held by the customer,
  • c) verified against the customer approved hard copy or electronic data and recorded (see 7.5.3), and
  • d) stored in a secure area within a defined system for authorized issue and return to store.

解說：印版追溯鏈

追溯鏈示意

ISO 15378要求從客戶主版到生產印版的完整追溯鏈：

客戶核准原版 (Master Origination)
↓ 複製並驗證
印版/印筒 (Print Plate/Cylinder)
↓ 唯一識別碼
生產用印版（授權發出/回收）

每一層都必須文件化記錄，確保生產中使用的印版與客戶核准設計完全一致。

類比：銀行的簽章樣本管理

就像銀行管理印章一樣：有「主印章」（客戶原版），開行後複製「作業印章」（生產印版）。每次借出/歸還都要登記，廢棄舊印章時要銷毀並記錄。印刷包材的印版管理邏輯完全相同。

C.1.2 Multiple Plates/Cylinders

Where more than one printing plate/cylinder is required, there shall be a documented system for ensuring that all plates/cylinders within the set are used.

Where a set of plates/cylinders contains the generic design for several jobs, each individual plate/cylinder within the set shall be clearly, uniquely identified and documented.

解說：多色印刷的套版控制

多色印刷的風險

藥品包材通常需要多色印刷（例如：黑色文字版、藍色色塊版、紅色警示版）。若某一色版遺失或使用錯誤版本，可能造成：

  • 資訊缺失（如缺少劑量說明）
  • 顏色辨識錯誤（顏色是藥品識別的重要元素）
  • 不同批次版本混用

因此，每套印版必須整組管理，確保「全套齊全」才能上機生產。

C.1.3 Copy/Design Change

Where a design requires several plates/cylinders and some of them are to be replaced because of a copy/design change, there shall be a documented procedure to allow for the replacement of the affected plate(s)/cylinder(s) and the retention of the other media within the set.

The original plates/cylinders shall be subject to a procedure that allows re-identification.

解說：版本變更的複雜性

部分變更的挑戰

設計變更時，不一定所有色版都需要更換。例如：只更新有效期格式（文字版變更），色塊版保持不變。此時需要：

  • 明確記錄哪些版更換、哪些保留
  • 對舊版重新標識（避免誤用於新設計）
  • 新舊版本不得在同一套版中混用

C.1.4 Verification, Quarantine and Destruction

C.1.4.1 General — Verification of the design on print impression media shall be carried out during the printing machine make-ready and before the approval to run the product is given.

C.1.4.2 Quarantine and destruction — The organization shall have:

  • a) a documented procedure which ensures that origination and print media, for a design undergoing revision, are subject to formal quarantine, and
  • b) a documented system detailing the method for disposal of the unwanted origination and print impression media; such items shall be rendered unusable and disposed of in a controlled and secure manner.

解說：預防「舊版重出江湖」

廢棄印版的風險

如果廢棄的印版未被銷毀，可能被意外拿出使用，生產出帶有過期設計的包材。「渲染為不可用」（rendered unusable）是關鍵要求：

  • 金屬版：物理毀形（彎折、穿孔）
  • 電子檔：正式刪除並記錄
  • 感光底片：剪斷或化學處理

隔離期間的版材必須有清楚的隔離標識，防止誤用。

開機驗證（Make-Ready Verification）

每次開機前必須驗證印版設計是否正確。這不是一次性的事件 — 換版後、換班後、生產中斷復工後都需要重新確認。驗證結果必須記錄（7.5.3條款）。

C.2

C.2 Print and Conversion Processes

印刷及轉換製程的GMP要求 | 開機準備、換版、留樣、幫派印刷

C.2.1 Print Machine Set-Up (Make-Ready)

C.2.1.1 Initial print make-ready shall be performed using unprinted components or material.

C.2.1.2 Make-ready for subsequent processes may use material from the initial print process of the same batch.

C.2.1.3 Initial make-ready material may be reused during the make-ready process in order to achieve correct colour.

C.2.1.4 Material used for make-ready shall be segregated and then disposed of as production waste.

解說：開機準備廢料管理

Make-Ready（試印）的目的

每次印刷機開機時，需要「試印」若干數量才能達到顏色穩定、套版精準。這些試印品（make-ready material）不能混入正式批次，必須：

  • 明確標識為「廢料」
  • 物理隔離存放
  • 作為生產廢棄物處置

前道工序（如印刷）的試印品可用於後道工序（如燙金、裁切）的試機，但同樣必須在後道工序結束後作廢料處置。

C.2.2 Changeover Systems

Changeover systems that are designed to reduce make-ready time (e.g. automated plate changing), and which do not permit a total line clearance, shall be subject to a documented risk assessment and operated with controls to ensure product security.

All print media from the previous job shall be removed from the line prior to formal approval of the print run being given.

All controls shall be recorded (see 7.5.3).

解說：自動換版的風險

自動化 ≠ 免於GMP風險

自動換版系統可以大幅縮短換版時間，但如果系統設計不允許進行「全線清場」（total line clearance），就必須：

  • 進行文件化風險評估（documented risk assessment）
  • 建立專項控制措施確保不發生產品混淆
  • 確認前一工單的所有印版已從生產線移除
  • 記錄所有控制措施的執行結果

關鍵原則：技術便利性不能以犧牲產品安全性為代價。風險評估是強制性的。

C.2.3 Retained Samples

C.2.3.1 All in-process printed samples which are to be retained shall be clearly identified and securely stored.

C.2.3.2 Samples used for other purposes (e.g. administration/sales) shall be voided if they leave the control of the organization.

解說：樣品的雙重管理

兩類留樣，兩種處理

「作廢」通常意味著在樣品上印上/蓋上「SAMPLE」或「VOID」字樣，使其無法被誤用為正式包材。這防止了樣品流入市場或被誤裝藥品。

C.2.4 Replacement Print Media

C.2.4.1 During a production run, if replacement plates are made from an existing fixed approved source (e.g. negatives, or using computer-to-plate technology from an existing stepped image), the job may be continued after a new "first off" check has been carried out.

C.2.4.2 During a production run, if plates are created from a new source (e.g. re-stepping a "one-up" image), the existing job shall be lifted; the replacement plates shall be treated as new origination. Subsequent production shall be treated as a new batch. The introduction of all replacement print media shall be recorded (see 7.5.3).

解說：生產中換版的批次判定

兩種換版情境對比

邏輯：來源的可追溯性決定了批次的連續性。新來源意味著新的設計來源，必須重新驗證，因此必須開新批次。

C.2.5 Gang Printing

Gang printing (the process of printing more than one design on a substrate at one production run) is recognized and classified as an acute contributory risk relating to admixtures.

Therefore this shall only be permitted:

  • in agreement with the customer, and
  • on completion of a documented risk assessment to evaluate and mitigate the risk of cross-contamination (see 8.2.1).

解說：幫派印刷 — 效率與患者安全的衝突

Gang Printing（幫派印刷）的嚴重風險

「幫派印刷」是指在一次印刷作業中，在同一基材上同時印製多種不同設計。這在商業印刷中很常見（節省成本），但在藥品包材中是一個急性（acute）的混淆風險：

  • 不同設計之間的物理分隔不足
  • 裁切誤差可能導致設計A的部分進入設計B的批次
  • 「Admixture」（混入）= 一種產品的標籤混入另一種產品

類比：製藥中的「交叉污染」

就像無菌製藥中，不同產品不能在同一條線上生產（除非有嚴格隔離和清場），幫派印刷把「不同藥品的識別信息」印在同一張紙上，其本質風險就是製藥中的「交叉污染（cross-contamination）」。標準明確稱之為「acute contributory risk」，措辭非常嚴厲。

合規路徑：兩個必要條件缺一不可

  • 客戶書面同意：不可只是口頭協議
  • 文件化風險評估：必須評估具體的混淆風險並記錄緩解措施

C.2.6 Batch Production and Stock Holding

C.2.6.1 Batched production and holding of product in stock shall only be practised if contractually agreed.

C.2.6.2 The organization shall control the storage to ensure security and integrity of the product and maintain its traceability back to manufacture and the materials used.

解說：庫存型生產的管控

Batched Production（批量生產備貨）

某些包材製造商會「預先生產」並在倉庫持有庫存，等待客戶下單後出貨。這種模式必須：

  • 有合約明確授權（contractually agreed）
  • 庫存管理系統確保追溯性（可追溯至製造批次和原料）
  • 防止未授權的設計更改期間放行舊版庫存

庫存型模式的最大風險是：設計更改後，舊版庫存未被攔截就出貨給客戶。

C.2.7

C.2.7 Digital Printing — Emerging Control Challenges

數位印刷：新技術帶來的新管控要求 | p62

C.2.7 Digital Printing Requirements

C.2.7.1 The flexible capabilities of digital printing introduce new activities, which shall be controlled and documented to ensure the accuracy and security of the printed products.

C.2.7.2 The use of digital printing and any special requirements for the product shall be agreed with the customer.

C.2.7.3 The organization shall establish a secure file access system, which is designed to prevent unintentional use of incorrect origination files.

C.2.7.4 Unless alternative security is designed, the controlling computer within the digital printing machine shall have only the specific origination file in its memory for the current print run; removal of this file shall form part of documented line clearance.

C.2.7.5 Operational settings to achieve acceptable colours shall be established through a formal process and recorded (see 7.5.3).

C.2.7.6 For reel-fed production on a continuous basis there shall be a suitable method to ensure product separation; the subsequent processes shall be verified to ensure correct separation of products and removal of defined gap material.

EXAMPLES: Defined change-over or gap between each printed job e.g. printed dummy text or blank material, code reading and code verification.

解說：數位印刷 — 傳統印版管控的邊界失效

數位印刷的獨特風險

傳統印刷靠「物理印版」作為防錯機制：錯誤的印版根本安不上機。但數位印刷完全依靠電腦文件，一個按鍵錯誤就可能把A產品的設計印在B產品的包材上，且外觀完全正常。

四大新增控制要求

  • 安全文件訪問系統：防止操作人員意外選到錯誤的設計文件（如設置嚴格的文件權限、作業切換需要雙重確認）
  • 單一文件在記憶體：印刷機電腦在印刷過程中只能存有當前作業的文件，切換前必須清除
  • 顏色設定正式化：數位印刷的顏色管理（色彩配置文件、ICC設定）需要通過正式流程建立並記錄，不能依賴操作人員的經驗調整
  • 產品分隔驗證：連續式卷材生產時，必須確認不同設計之間的間隔材料被正確識別並移除

類比：電子病歷的訪問控制

就像醫院的電子病歷系統必須確保醫生不會意外查看或修改錯誤患者的記錄，數位印刷系統必須確保操作人員不會意外載入錯誤產品的設計文件。兩者都是「信息系統的合規訪問控制」問題。

C.3

C.3 Security Code Systems & Reel Material Controls

安全碼系統、線上掃描驗證與卷材控制 | p62-p63

C.3.1 General & C.3.2 Verification Methods

C.3.1 To ensure the security of the product and prevent cross-contamination, security code systems may be included in the design of printed packaging materials for verification either by the organization during manufacture and/or by the customer during the packaging operation.

NOTE: Common security code systems include barcode systems, data matrix codes, and tag technology.

C.3.2.1 Where practical, every security coded item shall be verified by online scanning equipment to ensure that the codes are readable and that the correct product is being produced. Scanning should be carried out during the last feasible production process.

C.3.2.2 The scanning equipment software/control configuration shall be controlled to prevent unauthorized tampering. Codes shall be loaded from an independent source, e.g. specification or approved proof.

C.3.2.3 There shall be an effective system for rejecting any product that fails the scanning process. Any product rejected by the online scanning system shall be inspected, subsequently scrapped, and findings recorded and reviewed prior to product release.

C.3.2.4 The online scanning equipment and its associated reject mechanism shall be subjected to a challenge test during production to verify whether its operation is effective in detecting and removing incorrectly coded material. Such monitoring shall take place at the start of the process, at regular intervals, and be recorded.

C.3.2.5 Any product produced where electronic scanning is specified, but has not been performed, shall be properly authorized and recorded. The customer shall be notified and documented approval obtained before product release.

C.3.2.6 For reel-fed multi-lane production, all lanes should be subject to security code verification. Where this cannot be performed, one lane only may be verified, in agreement with the customer.

C.3.2.7 / C.3.3 "Point of measure" codes and/or verification of sample codes from all lanes shall be carried out.

Where "point of sale" codes (GS1/EAN, Code 39, PZN, etc.) are incorporated into the design, a documented sample verification shall be carried out during the production process.

解說：線上掃描的核心要求

線上掃描 = 100%驗證

「每一個安全碼件均應由線上掃描設備驗證」—— 這是100%的線上確認，不是抽樣。對藥品包材的安全碼（如條碼、Data Matrix）來說，漏掉任何一個都可能意味著錯誤產品流出。

挑戰測試（Challenge Test）的重要性

掃描設備不只需要確認「能掃到碼」，還需要定期進行挑戰測試，確認：

  • 能偵測到錯誤的碼（不只是缺失的碼）
  • 剔除機構確實動作，將不合格品排出
  • 每次生產開始和定期間隔均執行，並記錄

類比：消防演習不只是確認警報響了，還要確認員工真的能撤離到安全地點。

掃描未執行的例外處理

若規定要掃描但未執行，不能自動放行：

  • 必須獲得正式授權（documented approval）
  • 必須通知客戶並獲得書面同意
  • 這是一個正式的偏差事件，需要記錄在品質記錄中

C.3.4 Reel Materials and Procedures

C.3.4.1 Unless otherwise specified by the customer, splices shall be:

  • a) made using a brightly coloured adhesive tape on both sides of the web, and
  • b) checked either side of the splice to ensure that identical materials are joined and in register.

A limit on the maximum number of splices may be specified.

C.3.4.2 The quantity of material (length, weight or numerical) produced on each reel shall be determined within accuracy limits agreed with the customer and recorded on the reel.

C.3.4.3 The batch identity, reel number and production date shall be recorded on the inner face of the core for each individual reel.

C.3.4.4 To prevent cross-contamination, the web shall be run to plain material at the end of the run to ensure that no printed material remains in the printing equipment.

C.3.4.5 Where it is necessary to leave printed material in the converting equipment due to the difficulty in carrying out re-webbing (e.g. slitters), there shall be a formal documented procedure for removal and disposal of the material used to pull the new design through the machine.

C.3.4.6 If material with missing print can be produced as a consequence of the design or operation of the printing equipment, the organization shall have a secure system for the detection, removal and segregation of product produced with missing colours or text.

解說：卷材生產的特殊管控

為什麼接縫（Splice）需要彩色膠帶？

卷材生產中，當一卷原材料用完需要接上新卷時，接縫處的包材可能存在印刷偏移、錯位等問題。使用鮮豔顏色的膠帶標記接縫，使客戶在後續灌裝操作中能快速辨識並排除這些位置的包材。

卷芯追溯信息（C.3.4.3）

批號、卷號、生產日期要記錄在卷芯內面（而非外面），原因是：

  • 外面的標籤容易在運輸中損壞或脫落
  • 卷芯是整卷用完後仍保留的實物，追溯信息必須與其一起保留
  • 直到最後一圈材料用完，追溯鏈都保持完整

類比：「清場到素材」的邏輯

C.3.4.4要求生產結束時要「跑到素材（plain material）」才停機，就像廚房改換菜系前必須先清理鍋具一樣。印刷設備中殘留的印刷品如果進入下一個工單，就是「admixture（混入）」事件的源頭。

缺印（Missing Print）的偵測系統

部分印刷設備在特定條件下可能產生缺字、缺色的包材。如果這類包材流出，後果是患者拿到信息不完整的標籤。組織必須有系統性的偵測和剔除機制——不能依賴視覺檢查。

D

Annex D (Informative) — Verification, Qualification & Validation Guidance

附錄D（資訊性）：驗證、確認與驗證確效指引 | p64-p74

D.1 General — Scope of Annex D

This annex describes the approach when verification or qualification and validation are required. The guidance relates to the requirements given in 8.5.1.2 (additional GMP-related requirements on verification, qualification and validation).

Items which can require verification/qualification include:

  • equipment used for production of primary packaging materials, utilities and facilities, and
  • test equipment used for determining the acceptance of quality critical starting materials, intermediate/in-process or finished product.

Items which can require validation include:

  • validation of processes, and
  • validation of individual product, where appropriate or as agreed with the customer.

The aim of verification/qualification/validation is to confirm through documented evidence that pre-determined specifications are consistently fulfilled.

解說：V/Q/V框架在包材製造中的意義

與EU GMP Annex 15的連結

ISO 15378 Annex D的V/Q/V框架與EU GMP Annex 15（Qualification and Validation）高度平行。兩者都強調：

  • 風險評估驅動決策
  • IQ → OQ → PQ的順序確認
  • 文件化的計劃、協議、報告結構
  • 變更控制維持確認狀態

熟悉EU GMP Annex 15的讀者在ISO 15378 Annex D中會找到相同的邏輯框架。

D.2

D.2 Prerequisites and Considerations

先決條件與考量因素：設備確認和製程驗證的前置要求

D.2.1 General

A documented risk assessment is used to determine which equipment and processes need to be verified/qualified/validated. Product validation is optional, either after an internal decision of the organization or at the request of the customer.

Verification/qualification of equipment and validation of processes/product can be performed independently of each other, e.g. validation of a new process/product does not require re-verification/re-qualification of existing equipment.

D.2.2 Prerequisites for Verification/Qualification/Validation

Prerequisites to verification/qualification of the equipment:

  • approved/agreed upon requirement specifications,
  • identification of roles and agreed responsibilities (organization and supplier),
  • definition of critical process parameters,
  • training such as GMP and verification/qualification.

Prerequisites to validation of the process/product:

  • approved/agreed upon process/product specifications,
  • verified/qualified equipment,
  • identification of roles and agreed responsibilities (organization and customer),
  • definition of critical process parameters,
  • training (operators, quality personnel, engineers, etc.),
  • standard operating procedures to provide detail on how to produce/operate and clean the equipment.

D.2.3 Software Validation

Software can be validated/verified by functional tests. Software which is part of the equipment can be included in the equipment verification/qualification.

NOTE: The GAMP guideline might give guidance on how the functional test can be executed.

D.3

D.3 Validation Master Plan — Planning, Types & Decision Criteria

驗證主計劃：類型、角色與關鍵設備/製程判斷準則 | p65-p68

D.3.1 VMP — General Elements

The Validation Master Plan (VMP) describes the validation activities and the order of execution in accordance with the overall validation approach. It is recommended to revise the plan on a regular basis, for example yearly.

It should usually include the following elements:

  • validation planning (see D.3.2) and scheduling;
  • organizational structure of validation activities — roles and responsibilities (see D.3.3);
  • summary of quality critical equipment, process (see D.3.4 and D.3.5) and product;
  • references to existing documents (e.g. reference to an existing validation report), if applicable;
  • references to applicable procedures (e.g. overall validation approach, documentation format).

解說：VMP是確認確效活動的「地圖」

VMP的功能定位

VMP不是確認本身，而是確認活動的總體規劃文件。它回答三個問題：

  • 做什麼？（哪些設備和製程需要確認）
  • 怎麼做？（採用什麼方法和標準）
  • 誰來做？（角色和責任分配）

年度更新確保VMP反映最新的設備狀態、新引入的製程，以及任何已完成或計劃中的確認活動。

D.3.2 Validation Planning — Three Approaches

D.3.2.1 General — Prospective validation is recommended as the preferred approach but under certain circumstances a concurrent or retrospective validation can be accepted (this statement is not valid for sterilization processes).

D.3.2.2 Prospective validation — Prospective validation occurs prior to commercial production.

D.3.2.3 Concurrent validation — Concurrent validation should only apply to performance qualification and process validation. Concurrent validation consists of activities conducted in parallel with the manufacture of commercial product where the commercial product is released before the conclusion of validation activities.

Concurrent validation should follow the principles and procedures associated with prospective validation. Validation that takes place in campaigns represents a speciality in the manufacture of primary packaging material — concurrent validations may be interrupted and resumed with the next production campaign.

D.3.2.4 Retrospective validation — This approach includes establishing documented evidence that installed and operating equipment produces a consistent product by performing a historic review of data generated over the range of operating parameters and raw materials. This review can include maintenance and engineering records, quality records, and customer complaints.

解說：三種驗證確效方式的選擇邏輯

包材製造的特殊性：戰役性生產

許多包材製造商採用「戰役性生產（campaign production）」模式——某個產品每季度或每半年生產一次大批量。在此情況下，並行驗證可能因為戰役結束而中斷，並在下次戰役開始時繼續。這是包材行業特有的靈活性，標準明確允許。

例外：滅菌製程必須使用前瞻性驗證

標準明確指出，這個「三種方式都可接受」的原則不適用於滅菌製程（見8.5.1.4.2和8.5.1.2.5）。滅菌製程必須使用前瞻性驗證——這也與FDA和EU GMP的立場一致。

D.3.2.5 Bracketing/Matrix Approach

In general a bracketing/matrix approach can be appropriate based on risk analysis. Where the equivalence of equipment has been proven, a bracketing/matrix approach can be used to:

  • reduce the number of installation and operational testing during equipment verification/qualification,
  • reduce the number of PQ batches during equipment qualification,
  • reduce the number of PV batches during product or process validation, and
  • increase the re-validation period for individual equipment and performing the re-validation on equivalent equipment alternately.

Where a range of products are produced in accordance with a same process, an experimental design to test only the extremes (smallest and largest product) may be used. In case a worst case can be defined, it is sufficient to test the worst case only.

In all cases, there should be sufficient process and product knowledge on which to base a sound rationale. The equivalence of equipment, the technical rationale and justification for decision have to be documented.

解說：括號法/矩陣法的實務應用

這是製造商最有價值的工具之一

對於擁有多台相同型號設備的包材製造商，矩陣法可以大幅減少驗證工作量，同時保持合規性。關鍵前提是：必須先證明設備的等效性（equivalence）。

實際計算：矩陣法節省的批次數

以Table D.1的3台等效機器為例：

無矩陣法（傳統方式）：

  • 初次驗證：3台 × 3批 = 9批
  • 年度重驗：3台 × 1批 × 每年 = 3批/年

有矩陣法（Table D.1）：

  • 初次驗證：1台3批 + 2台1批 = 5批（節省44%）
  • 年度重驗：每年1台 × 1批 = 1批/年（節省67%）

4年總計：無矩陣 = 9+12 = 21批；有矩陣 = 5+4 = 9批。節省57%的驗證批次。

類比：藥廠的括號法驗證

在製藥行業，括號法（bracketing）常用於穩定性研究——只測試最強和最弱規格的劑型，中間規格假設在範圍內。ISO 15378把相同邏輯應用到包材製程驗證，這是「基於科學和風險的法規思維」的典型應用。

D.3.3 Roles and Responsibilities

Detailed information concerning roles and responsibilities (naming the individuals with responsibility for conducting the validation steps and authorizing the results) should be part of the respective validation documents and should be specified prior to the commencement of work unless it is defined in other documents or procedures.

解說：確認前必須定義角色

D.3.4 Quality Critical Equipment — Decision Criteria

Risk assessment is an inherent part of the decision-making process. Equipment is critical in case one of the following questions is answered with "yes":

  • Would malfunction of the equipment impact directly on product quality as defined for the type of products it is producing?
  • Is the equipment used to guarantee sterility of the products?
  • Does the equipment control or measure quality critical processing steps or parameters?
  • Does the equipment produce data/records for acceptance or rejection?
  • Is equipment in direct contact with the product?
  • Is the equipment used to prevent contamination, to remove contamination or to clean?

When equipment is considered critical, verification or qualification should be performed and it is included in the VMP. When equipment is not critical, good engineering practices apply and it is not included in the VMP.

GEP vs. 正式確認

非關鍵設備不需要正式的IQ/OQ/PQ，但仍應遵循良好工程實踐（GEP）：

  • 定期預防性維護
  • 校準（如適用）
  • 不需要書面確認方案/報告

D.4.1 Verification or Qualification Stages

D.4.1.1 General — The verification or qualification should be based on a risk assessment. Equipment verification/qualification consists of the following stages: risk assessment, requirement specification setting and verification/qualification.

D.4.1.2 Risk assessment — The risk assessment should underpin the specification and verification/qualification process, and be appropriately applied at each stage. Documentation should be based on the risk to product quality.

D.4.1.3 Requirement Specification

D.4.1.3.1 General — The requirement specifications can be combined in one or more specification documents depending on the criticality of the equipment, whether it concerns standardized or customized equipment, or depending on responsibilities. Every specification should contain well defined, specific, testable, achievable acceptance criteria.

D.4.1.3.2 User Requirement Specification (URS) — A URS (as defined in 3.7.11) is an approved document that states the product specifications of the material produced on this equipment as well as functional, operational and/or technical aspects of the equipment or process required to produce the desired product. The URS can include the consideration for equipment lay-out with sufficient space for material flow and operators, availability of spare parts, and ease of access for cleaning and line clearance.

D.4.1.3.3 Equipment specification — Critical aspects of manufacturing systems are typically functions, features, abilities and performance or characteristics necessary for the manufacturing process and system to ensure consistent product quality.

D.4.1.3.4 Equipment verification/qualification — A systematic approach should be defined to verify that the equipment is fit for intended use, has been properly installed and is operating correctly. Verification/qualification can be split in two sub-stages: installation qualification and operational qualification, but it is also possible to combine them in one stage (typically done during testing at the supplier and testing on site). Leveraging of data are allowed — data obtained during testing at the supplier does not have to be obtained again on site unless the transfer of the equipment influences the testing.

解說：IQ/OQ/PQ三階段確認

URS — 確認的起點

URS（使用者需求規格書）必須在採購設備前完成，它定義了：

  • 設備必須生產的產品規格
  • 功能性要求（速度、精度等）
  • GMP相關要求（可清潔性、材質相容性）
  • 操作人員接觸/安全性要求
  • 空間佈局考量

URS是之後所有確認活動的基準文件。

「數據借用（Leveraging）」原則

若供應商工廠測試（FAT）已收集了充分數據，這些數據可以「搬運」到現場確認中使用，不需要重複收集。前提是：設備在運輸/安裝過程中沒有發生可能影響測試結果的事件。這是基於風險的務實做法，避免不必要的重複工作。

D.4.1.4 Performance Qualification (PQ)

PQ testing uses production materials to verify that the equipment is robust and the primary packaging material can be consistently produced under routine operating conditions. PQ challenges the overall line performance to ensure that it can produce consistently at the quality standard required.

The test process and results of an appropriate number of consecutive production batches (typically three) are formally documented and approved.

Based on risk management, performance qualification can be skipped or can also be combined with process or product validation.

An exception to using three subsequent batches may be made where the production process is lengthy — for example a single batch may require several weeks of continuous production. In this case PQ may be conducted on sub-batches of a minimum of one day's duration each.

解說：PQ的靈活性

「通常3批」不是鐵律

「Typically three」（通常3批）在GMP語境中是常見的基準，但ISO 15378明確提供了例外情況。對於需要數週才能完成一批的製程（例如大卷材的長時間印刷），可以用「最少一天的sub-batch」替代完整的3批。

這是科學性和務實性的平衡——確認原則不因生產週期長就被跳過，而是以等效的方式執行。

PQ可以與製程驗證合併

基於風險管理，PQ可以：

  • 跳過：若設備標準化程度高，OQ已足夠確認性能
  • 與PV合併：PQ批次同時作為製程驗證批次，一舉兩得

決定跳過或合併需要有風險評估支持和品質部門批准。

D.4.2 Process and Product Validation

D.4.2.1 Process validation — A process validation consists of a number of consecutive production batches (typically three) produced under normal conditions with a higher level of sampling and additional testing compared to routine production. Process validation can be combined with PQ.

Process validation has to be based on a firm base of knowledge of the process and its variables/variations. Process validation is independent of the product. Bracketing or matrix approach may be used (see D.3.2.5).

D.4.2.2 Product validation — The approach is in line with process validation; however specific customer requirements can be added.

解說：製程驗證 vs. 產品驗證

關鍵區別：製程驗證獨立於產品

製程驗證（Process Validation）是驗證「製程本身」的能力，與具體產品無關。例如：

  • 驗證「輪轉凹版印刷製程」能穩定達到特定色彩精度和套印誤差
  • 一旦製程驗證完成，新產品在此製程上生產時，通常只需要產品確認，而不需要重新驗證製程

產品驗證（Product Validation）是在製程驗證基礎上，加入特定客戶或產品的要求（如特定油墨相容性、特殊基材）。

D.5 Documentation Structure for V/Q/V

確認確效的文件層次結構：計劃→協議→報告→VSR | p71-p74

D.5 Documentation Hierarchy

D.5.1 General — All documents should be reviewed and approved prior to the commencement of verification/qualification/validation/release. Any release decisions should be the responsibility of the quality unit. Any revision of a document should be tracked by version control.

D.5.2 Plan (optional) — One or more plans can be created to detail the steps to be taken in the different stages. These plans can include:

  • description of the project/scope;
  • roles and responsibilities;
  • planning;
  • risk analysis;
  • overall acceptance criteria;
  • validation approach;
  • documents to be created;
  • reference to procedures and change control;
  • definition of conditions for quarantine status of batches subject to PQ or validation.

Plan and protocol may be combined in one document.

D.5.3 Protocol — A written protocol should be established that specifies how verification/qualification/validation will be conducted. The protocol can include:

  • description of the project/scope;
  • roles and responsibilities;
  • justification of the approach taken;
  • tests to be performed with test methods and test conditions;
  • detailed acceptance criteria for each test;
  • sampling plan;
  • critical process parameters;
  • reference to procedures, change control and requirement specifications;
  • prerequisites.

D.5.4 Report — A report that cross-references the protocol should be prepared. The report can include:

  • summary of test results;
  • raw data;
  • deviations observed and corrective actions taken or to be taken;
  • conclusion;
  • changes to the plan as defined in the protocol with appropriate justification.

After completion of satisfactory testing, a formal release for the next step should be made as a written authorization.

D.5.5 Validation Summary Report — VSR (optional) — The VSR follows the completion of all stages of V/Q/V and concludes the project. The VSR can include:

  • cross-reference to protocols and reports;
  • changes to plans and justification for any change;
  • formal approval and closure of the project.

D.5.6 Archiving — Verification or qualification and validation records should be maintained according to 7.5.3.11.

Protocol vs. Report 的核心差異

協議（Protocol）必須在執行前核准——它定義「我們計劃怎麼做」。
報告（Report）在執行後撰寫——它記錄「我們實際做了什麼以及結果如何」。

這個「先核准協議，再執行，再報告」的順序是GMP的基本原則——確保測試前已明確定義驗收標準，防止「事後更改標準」的行為。

類比：醫療試驗的預先申報

臨床試驗也要求在試驗前預先申報方案（研究方案），結果的統計方法和成功標準必須事先定義。確認確效的協議先行原則出自同樣的科學誠信邏輯。

偏差的處理原則

報告中記錄的偏差不一定意味著確認失敗。關鍵是：

  • 每個偏差必須有書面的根本原因分析
  • 對產品質量影響必須評估
  • 糾正措施必須文件化並追蹤完成
  • 品質部門必須批准「帶偏差放行」

D.6 Validation Maintenance — Change Control & Re-qualification

確認確效的維護：變更控制、預防性維護與重新確認 | p74

D.6 Validation Maintenance

D.6.1 General — The verification/qualification of equipment and validation of process(es)/product should be maintained. Any changes should be risk assessed for their impact on the validation status. If necessary, appropriate verification/qualification/validation or re-qualification/re-validation should be performed.

Changes/improvements to validation procedures should also be risk assessed to determine if re-validation is required.

D.6.2 Change control — Specific controls should be implemented to cover changes during verification/qualification/validation. Formal, documented change control should be implemented after release of the equipment/process/product and kept throughout the lifetime of the equipment/process/product.

D.6.3 Follow-up — Action should be taken to complete any outstanding actions, e.g. deficiencies following verification/qualification/validation. All actions taken should be documented.

D.6.4 Preventive maintenance — After release of equipment a preventive maintenance plan (including calibration) should be established to guarantee equipment performance.

D.6.5 Re-qualification/re-validation — As long as the equipment/process operates in a state of control and no changes have been made to the equipment/process or output product, the equipment need not be re-qualified and process need not be re-validated.

Whether the equipment/process is operating in a state of control is determined by analysing day-to-day process control data and any product testing data for conformance with specifications and for variability.

When changes or deviations occur, the equipment/process should be reviewed and evaluated, and re-qualification/re-validation should be performed where appropriate. Periodic qualifications/validations can be required e.g. clean rooms, sterilization processes.

解說：確認確效不是一次性活動

「控制狀態」= 不需要重新確認的前提

ISO 15378 Annex D的立場與ICH Q10一致：若設備/製程在控制狀態下穩定運行，不需要週期性重新確認（除了潔淨室、滅菌製程等特殊情況）。

如何判斷「控制狀態」：

  • 日常製程控制數據符合規格
  • 產品測試數據無統計趨勢
  • 無重大偏差或客訴
  • 無設備或製程變更

變更控制 = 確認狀態的守門員

確認確效完成後，變更控制系統成為保護確認狀態的核心機制：

  • 任何對已確認設備/製程的變更必須通過正式變更控制
  • 變更風險評估需評估對確認狀態的影響
  • 重大變更（如設備大修、製程參數調整）可能觸發重新確認
  • 變更控制需要貫穿設備/製程的整個生命週期

類比：汽車的定期保養

預防性維護計劃（D.6.4）就像汽車的定期保養——不是等車壞了才修，而是按照製造商的建議定期維護，確保設備始終在設計性能範圍內運作。校準也是其中的一部分：測量儀器必須定期校準，確保「設備說合格，確實是合格」。

ICH Q9 + ICH Q10的重要性

ISO 15378在Annex D中多次隱含地引用ICH Q9（風險管理）和ICH Q10（藥品質量體系）的原則。明確引用[54][55]表明標準作者有意將包材製造商的GMP要求與藥品製造商的最佳實踐對齊。

這對包材製造商的含義是：理解ICH Q9的風險評估工具（FMEA、HACCP、FTA等）能幫助更好地執行ISO 15378的要求。

EU GMP Annex 15 vs. ISO 15378 Annex D

EU GMP Annex 15主要針對藥品製造商（成品廠）。ISO 15378 Annex D把同樣的V/Q/V框架應用到包材製造商，並加入了包材行業特有的考量（如戰役性生產、矩陣法）。兩者可以相互參照——理解一個有助於理解另一個。

解說：術語索引的使用指南

術語索引的實務用途

ISO 15378的術語章節（第3條款）定義了本標準所有特定術語的確切含義。在審計、合規評估或合約談判中，當術語有爭議時，應回到第3條款的定義確認含義。

需特別關注的術語組

確認確效術語組：

  • qualification (3.2.8) vs. validation (3.7.12) vs. verification (3.7.13)
  • IQ (3.2.4) → OQ (3.2.5) → PQ (3.2.7)

批次管理術語組：

  • batch/lot (3.5.1) — 注意：batch和lot在本標準中同義
  • batch record (3.7.2) vs. batch document (3.7.2) — 同一條款下的同義詞
  • batch release (3.8.1) vs. rejection (3.8.2)

環境控制術語組：

  • cleanroom (3.3.3) vs. clean zone (3.3.4) vs. controlled area (3.3.5)
  • airlock (3.3.1) vs. working area (3.3.6)

「Quality Critical」(3.11.1) 的核心地位

「quality critical（品質關鍵）」是整個ISO 15378風險管理框架的核心判斷點。設備（D.3.4）、製程（D.3.5）、參數，都需要先判斷是否「quality critical」，再決定需要什麼等級的確認確效控制。這個術語在整個標準中出現的頻率反映了它的重要性。

Section Summary 本節要點總結

Annex C Key Takeaways

  • C is Normative — all printed packaging manufacturers must comply
  • Gang printing = acute admixture risk — customer agreement + risk assessment mandatory
  • Digital printing requires secure file access + single file in memory
  • Online scanning requires periodic challenge tests to verify reject mechanism
  • Obsolete plates/cylinders must be rendered unusable

Annex D Key Takeaways

  • D is Informative — but reflects industry best practice aligned with EU GMP Annex 15
  • Risk assessment determines what needs V/Q/V and at what level
  • Prospective validation preferred; concurrent valid for campaign production
  • Bracketing/matrix approach can reduce validation batches by 50%+ for equivalent machines
  • Document hierarchy: VMP → Plan → Protocol → Report → VSR
