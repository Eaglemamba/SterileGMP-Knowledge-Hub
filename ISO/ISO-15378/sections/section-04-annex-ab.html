Annex A (Informative) — Clarification of New Structure, Terminology and Concepts

資訊性附錄A：新結構、術語與概念說明 | p52–p55

Original Text (ISO 15378:2017)

This Annex is reproduced from ISO 9001:2015, Annex A. It provides informative clarification of the new structure, terminology and concepts introduced in the 2015 revision.

The International Standards described in this annex explain why the clause structure and some of the terminology of ISO 9001:2015 — and by extension ISO 15378:2017 — have been changed to improve alignment with other management systems standards.

There is no requirement in this International Standard for its structure and terminology to be applied to the documented information of an organization's quality management system. Organizations can choose to use terms which suit their operations, e.g. using "records", "documentation" or "protocols" rather than "documented information"; or "supplier", "partner" or "vendor" rather than "external provider".

解說 Commentary

為什麼 ISO 15378 包含 ISO 9001 的附錄？

ISO 15378 的正文是「建立在 ISO 9001:2015 之上，加入製藥包裝的特殊要求」。因此 Annex A 和 B 直接來自 ISO 9001:2015，幫助 ISO 15378 使用者理解底層框架的邏輯。

換句話說：讀懂 Annex A = 讀懂 ISO 9001:2015 的設計哲學。

重要提醒：術語自由度

標準明確說明：組織不需要把內部文件全部改成 ISO 術語。例如製藥廠習慣用的 "SOP"、"batch record"、"specification" 都可以保留，只要其功能符合標準要求即可。

解說 Commentary

術語改變背後的邏輯

ISO 2015 版的術語變動不只是換名字，而是反映了 QMS 哲學的轉移：

  • Products → Products and services：服務型組織（如 CRO、CDMO）被明確納入範圍
  • Supplier → External provider：更廣義，涵蓋合約研究機構、外包服務商
  • Documented information：統合了四個概念（文件 / 程序 / 記錄 / 品質手冊），減少文件管理的行政負擔
  • Management representative 取消：責任分散到各職能，而非指定一人

製藥稽核員的關鍵對照

EU GMP Annex 15 和 FDA 21 CFR Part 211 仍使用舊術語（如 "procedures"、"records"、"supplier"）。稽核時需理解對照關係：

  • FDA "records" = ISO "retain documented information"
  • FDA/EU "procedures" = ISO "maintain documented information"
  • GMP "supplier qualification" = ISO "control of external providers"

理解這個對照表，可以避免在跨稽核時產生不必要的混淆。

比喻：從「分類夾」到「知識庫」

2008 版像是有五個不同的文件夾（SOP 夾、記錄夾、品質手冊、計劃書...），每個夾子有不同的管理規則。2015 版說：全部都叫「文件化資訊」，你自己決定要「維持（maintain）」還是「保留（retain）」哪些，重點是符合目的。

A.2 Products and Services

為何明確加入「服務」概念 | p52

Original Text

ISO 9001:2008 used the term "product" to include all output categories. This edition of this International Standard uses "products and services". "Products and services" include all output categories (hardware, services, software and processed materials).

The specific inclusion of "services" is intended to highlight the differences between products and services in the application of some requirements. The characteristic of services is that at least part of the output is realized at the interface with the customer. This means, for example, that conformity to requirements cannot necessarily be confirmed before service delivery.

In most cases, products and services are used together. Most outputs that organizations provide to customers, or are supplied to them by external providers, include both products and services. For example, a tangible or intangible product can have some associated service or a service can have some associated tangible or intangible product.

解說 Commentary

對初級包裝材料廠的影響

ISO 15378 的對象是製藥初級包裝材料製造商（如玻璃瓶、注射器、塑膠瓶蓋）。這些廠商除了供應實體產品，往往也提供：

  • 技術支援（材料相容性測試建議）
  • 包材設計服務
  • 客製化開發協議

2015 版明確將這些「伴隨服務」納入 QMS 管轄範圍。

服務特性 vs 產品特性

服務的關鍵差異：交付前無法完全驗證合規性（因為服務在「交付當下」才完成）。這與 GMP 的出廠放行邏輯不同，要求在設計階段就確保服務品質，而非僅靠事後檢查。

A.3 Understanding the Needs and Expectations of Interested Parties

相關方需求與期望 (Clause 4.2) | p53

Original Text

Subclause 4.2 specifies requirements for the organization to determine the interested parties that are relevant to the quality management system and the requirements of those interested parties. However, 4.2 does not imply extension of quality management system requirements beyond the scope of this International Standard.

As stated in the scope, this International Standard is applicable where an organization needs to demonstrate its ability to consistently provide products and services that meet customer and applicable statutory and regulatory requirements, and aims to enhance customer satisfaction.

There is no requirement in this International Standard for the organization to consider interested parties where it has decided that those parties are not relevant to its quality management system. It is for the organization to decide if a particular requirement of a relevant interested party is relevant to its quality management system.

解說 Commentary

「相關方」在製藥包裝供應鏈的範圍

對 ISO 15378 適用的包裝材料廠商，相關方（stakeholders）可包括：

  • 直接客戶：製藥廠、CDMO
  • 監管機構：FDA、EMA、PMDA
  • 最終使用者：患者（透過藥品安全）
  • 供應商：原料玻璃廠、塑膠粒料供應商
  • 認證機構：ISO / GMP 稽核單位

重要邊界：不等於無限擴大範圍

標準明確說明：識別相關方不代表 QMS 需要無限擴張以滿足所有相關方的每一項需求。組織有自主判斷哪些要求與 QMS 相關的權利。這個邊界說明對稽核時的 scope 討論非常重要。

A.4 Risk-Based Thinking

風險思維 — 取代「預防措施」條款的設計邏輯 | p53–p54

Original Text

The concept of risk-based thinking has been implicit in previous editions of this International Standard, e.g. through requirements for planning, review and improvement. This International Standard specifies requirements for the organization to understand its context (see 4.1) and determine risks as a basis for planning (see 6.1). This represents the application of risk-based thinking to planning and implementing quality management system processes (see 4.4) and will assist in determining the extent of documented information.

One of the key purposes of a quality management system is to act as a preventive tool. Consequently, this International Standard does not have a separate clause or subclause on preventive action. The concept of preventive action is expressed through the use of risk-based thinking in formulating quality management system requirements.

The risk-based thinking applied in this International Standard has enabled some reduction in prescriptive requirements and their replacement by performance-based requirements. There is greater flexibility than in ISO 9001:2008 in the requirements for processes, documented information and organizational responsibilities.

Although 6.1 specifies that the organization shall plan actions to address risks, there is no requirement for formal methods for risk management or a documented risk management process. Organizations can decide whether or not to develop a more extensive risk management methodology than is required by this International Standard, e.g. through the application of other guidance or standards.

Not all the processes of a quality management system represent the same level of risk in terms of the organization's ability to meet its objectives, and the effects of uncertainty are not the same for all organizations. Under the requirements of 6.1, the organization is responsible for its application of risk-based thinking and the actions it takes to address risk, including whether or not to retain documented information as evidence of its determination of risks.

解說 Commentary

為什麼 2015 版取消「預防措施」條款？

ISO 9001:2008 的 Clause 8.5.3 是「預防措施（Preventive Action）」，要求識別潛在不符合並採取措施。但在實踐中，這個條款常被機械式執行（填表、開 PA，但沒有真正的風險分析）。

2015 版的做法更根本：把風險思維內建到整個 QMS 框架，特別是：

  • 4.1：理解組織情境
  • 4.2：識別相關方需求
  • 6.1：規劃時識別風險與機會

結果：QMS 本身就是一個持續性的「預防機制」，而非有一個獨立的預防措施表單系統。

比喻：從「滅火器」到「防火設計」

舊版的預防措施像是在每個房間放滅火器（事後補救思維）。新版的風險思維像是從建築設計階段就規劃逃生動線、防火材料、灑水系統——把安全內建到結構裡，而不是靠工具補充。

與 ICH Q9 風險管理的關係

標準明確說：不要求正式的風險管理方法論或文件化的風險管理流程。這與 ICH Q9 的正式 FMEA/HACCP 方法不同——ISO 15378 的風險思維是「態度與判斷」，ICH Q9 是「工具與方法」。兩者互補，ISO 15378 不取代 ICH Q9。

對製藥包裝廠的實際影響

風險思維帶來的彈性包括：

  • 製程風險低的包材（如紙箱）文件化要求可較簡單
  • 直接接觸藥品的包材（如注射器柱塞）需要更嚴格的文件化
  • 組織可自主決定哪些風險評估需要保留記錄，哪些口頭確認即可

A.5 Applicability

適用性 — 取代舊版「豁免（Exclusions）」概念 | p54

Original Text

This International Standard does not refer to "exclusions" in relation to the applicability of its requirements to the organization's quality management system. However, an organization can review the applicability of requirements due to:

  • the size or complexity of the organization
  • the management model it adopts
  • the range of the organization's activities
  • the nature of the risks and opportunities it encounters

The requirements for applicability are addressed in 4.3, which defines conditions under which an organization can decide that a requirement cannot be applied to any of the processes within the scope of its quality management system.

The organization can only decide that a requirement is not applicable if its decision will not result in failure to achieve conformity of products and services.

解說 Commentary

「豁免」vs「不適用」的本質差異

2008 版允許在品質手冊中聲明「豁免（exclusion）」特定條款——但這導致一些組織直接略過與其業務相關的要求。

2015 版改為：沒有「豁免」這個詞。你只能說某條款「不適用於你的 QMS 範疇」，且前提是這個決定不會影響產品/服務的合規性。

稽核實務關鍵點

當被稽核方聲稱某條款「不適用」時，稽核員應確認：

  1. 是否有書面理由說明為何不適用
  2. 該決定是否影響產品合規性
  3. 是否在 QMS 範疇聲明（scope statement）中有所說明

這與舊版「品質手冊列豁免章節」的做法在形式和精神上都有所不同。

A.6 Documented Information

文件化資訊 — 統合文件、程序、記錄、品質手冊 | p54

Original Text

As part of the alignment with other management system standards, a common clause on "documented information" has been adopted without significant change or addition (see 7.5). Consequently, "documented information" is used for all document requirements.

Where ISO 9001:2008 used specific terminology such as "document" or "documented procedures", "quality manual" or "quality plan", this edition of this International Standard defines requirements to "maintain documented information".

Where ISO 9001:2008 used the term "records" to denote documents needed to provide evidence of conformity with requirements, this is now expressed as a requirement to "retain documented information". The organization is responsible for determining what documented information needs to be retained, the period of time for which it is to be retained and the media to be used for its retention.

A requirement to "maintain" documented information does not exclude the possibility that the organization might also need to "retain" that same documented information for a particular purpose, e.g. to retain previous versions of it.

Where this International Standard refers to "information" rather than "documented information" (e.g. in 4.1), there is no requirement that this information is to be documented. In such situations, the organization can decide whether or not it is necessary or appropriate to maintain documented information.

解說 Commentary

品質手冊去哪了？

ISO 9001:2015 正式取消了對「品質手冊」的強制要求。組織仍可選擇維持品質手冊，但這不再是標準的必要要求。對於已取得 ISO 15378 認證並有品質手冊的組織，只需確保其涵蓋範疇與標準要求的 documented information 一致即可。

比喻：食譜 vs 烹飪記錄

Maintain = 廚師手冊（食譜）：要保持最新版本，有人用時取出，修改後更新，舊版可以丟棄。Retain = 每日餐廳出菜記錄：每天的記錄都要保留，作為食安稽查的證明，不能任意處置。

A.7 Organizational Knowledge

組織知識 — 2015 版新增要求 (7.1.6) | p54–p55

Original Text

In 7.1.6, this International Standard addresses the need to determine and manage the knowledge maintained by the organization, to ensure the operation of its processes and that it can achieve conformity of products and services.

Requirements regarding organizational knowledge were introduced for the purpose of:

a) safeguarding the organization from loss of knowledge, e.g.

  • through staff turnover;
  • failure to capture and share information;

b) encouraging the organization to acquire knowledge, e.g.

  • learning from experience;
  • mentoring;
  • benchmarking.
解說 Commentary

組織知識 — 2015 版全新要求

這是 ISO 9001:2015 相比 2008 版最具創新性的新增要求之一。Clause 7.1.6 要求組織：

  1. 確定所需的組織知識
  2. 維持這些知識（並提供給需要的人）
  3. 當面對新需求時，考慮如何獲取知識

製藥廠常見的知識流失風險

對初級包裝材料製造商，以下情境需要特別注意：

  • 資深製程工程師離職 → 特殊材料加工技術失傳
  • 客戶規格歷史沿革未記錄 → 下次認證無法溯源決策依據
  • 稽核 finding 的根因分析結果未系統化 → 同類問題重複發生
  • 供應商變更評估的歷史資料散落各部門

比喻：組織記憶 vs 個人記憶

如果關鍵知識只存在某位員工的腦袋裡，那不叫「組織知識」，那叫「人質風險」。7.1.6 的本質是要求把「人的知識」轉化為「組織的知識」。

A.8 Control of Externally Provided Processes, Products and Services

外部提供之製程、產品與服務的管控 (8.4) | p55

Original Text

All forms of externally provided processes, products and services are addressed in 8.4, e.g. whether through:

  • a) purchasing from a supplier;
  • b) an arrangement with an associate company;
  • c) outsourcing processes to an external provider.

Outsourcing always has the essential characteristic of a service, since it will have at least one activity necessarily performed at the interface between the provider and the organization.

The controls required for external provision can vary widely depending on the nature of the processes, products and services. The organization can apply risk-based thinking to determine the type and extent of controls appropriate to particular external providers and externally provided processes, products and services.

解說 Commentary

外部提供的三種形式

ISO 15378 在此擴展了管控對象，從「採購」延伸到更廣義的「所有形式的外部提供」：

  • 採購（Purchasing）：向供應商購買原料、零件（傳統供應商管理）
  • 關聯公司安排：集團內跨廠區提供製程或服務
  • 外包（Outsourcing）：將某一製程委託外部機構執行

風險思維決定管控程度

並非所有外部提供都需要相同強度的管控。風險導向評估：

  • 直接接觸藥品的包材（如注射用玻璃瓶）→ 嚴格的供應商稽核、材料認證
  • 辦公室清潔服務外包 → 合約約定即可，無需 GMP 稽核
  • 外包滅菌製程 → 接近 GMP 等級管控，需稽核能力

這種比例原則的管控邏輯，與 ICH Q10 的 Quality Systems 精神一致。

Annex B (Informative) — Other International Standards on Quality Management

資訊性附錄B：ISO/TC 176 發展的其他品質管理國際標準 | p56–p59

Original Text — Annex B Introduction

The International Standards described in this annex have been developed by ISO/TC 176 to provide supporting information for organizations that apply this International Standard, and to provide guidance for organizations that choose to progress beyond its requirements.

Guidance or requirements contained in the documents listed in this annex do not add to, or modify, the requirements of this International Standard.

This annex does not include reference to the sector-specific quality management system standards developed by ISO/TC 176.

The three core standards developed by ISO/TC 176:

  • ISO 9000 — provides an essential background for the proper understanding and implementation. Defines terms, definitions and concepts used in ISO 15378. The quality management principles are described in detail and form the foundation of the requirements.
  • ISO 9001 (this International Standard) — specifies requirements aimed primarily at giving confidence in the products and services provided by an organization and thereby enhancing customer satisfaction.
  • ISO 9004 — provides guidance for organizations that choose to progress beyond the requirements of this International Standard. Includes a self-assessment methodology to evaluate the maturity of a quality management system.
解說 Commentary

ISO/TC 176 是什麼？

ISO Technical Committee 176 是負責制定所有 ISO 9000 系列標準的委員會。Annex B 列出的所有標準都出自這個委員會，形成一個「ISO QMS 生態系」。

三個核心標準的定位：

  • ISO 9000：字典（vocabulary 和 principles）
  • ISO 9001：法規（requirements，認證依據）
  • ISO 9004：進階指引（超越合規，追求卓越）

Annex B 的重要聲明

這些附加標準不增加、也不修改 ISO 15378 的要求。它們是選用工具，而非強制要求。對稽核員而言：Annex B 的標準不可作為稽核 finding 的直接依據。

解說 Commentary

ISO 9000 系列生態系地圖

  • 基礎框架：ISO 9000（詞彙）、ISO 9001（要求）、ISO 9004（卓越）
  • 客戶導向：ISO 10001-10004（客戶滿意系列）
  • 文件管理：ISO 10005（品質計劃）、ISO/TR 10013（文件指引）
  • 量測系統：ISO 10012（量測管理）、ISO/TR 10017（統計技術）
  • 人員發展：ISO 10015（訓練）、ISO 10018（人員能力）
  • 稽核：ISO 19011（稽核指引）
  • 顧問：ISO 10019（顧問選擇）

製藥 QMS 最實用的三個

對 ISO 15378 的使用者而言，最具直接應用價值的附加標準：

  1. ISO 19011：供應商稽核計劃的設計指引——如何規劃稽核頻率、稽核員資格、稽核報告
  2. ISO 10002：客訴處理流程——與 21 CFR Part 820 的 complaint handling 要求高度呼應
  3. ISO 10015：人員訓練管理——支持 Clause 7.2 的能力管理要求

比喻：本標準是主機，附加標準是 App

ISO 15378 是手機作業系統（強制規定基本功能）。ISO 9000 系列的其他標準是 App Store 的應用程式——你可以根據需求選擇安裝，但不安裝也不違規，且它們不能修改作業系統的核心規則。

如何使用 Table B.1？

當你在改善 QMS 的某個領域時，Table B.1 告訴你哪些 ISO 標準有相關指引：

  • 改善 Clause 7.5 文件管理 → 參考 ISO/TR 10013
  • 加強 Clause 9.2 內部稽核 → 參考 ISO 19011
  • 優化 Clause 9.1.2 客戶滿意度 → 參考 ISO 10001, 10002, 10003, 10004
  • 強化 Clause 8.4 外部提供管控 → 參考 ISO 10019（顧問/服務商選擇）
  • 提升 Clause 7.2 人員能力 → 參考 ISO 10015

Section Summary — 本節重點總結

Annex A Key Takeaways

  • Terminology mapping: "Supplier" = "External provider"; "Records" = "Retain documented information"; "Documents/Procedures" = "Maintain documented information"
  • No exclusions: The concept was replaced by scope-based applicability (Clause 4.3) — organizations must justify why a requirement is not applicable, not simply declare an exclusion
  • Risk-based thinking: Replaces the standalone preventive action clause; embedded throughout planning (4.1, 4.2, 6.1) — the entire QMS becomes the preventive mechanism
  • Documented information: "Maintain" = living documents (SOPs, specs); "Retain" = evidence records (batch records, test reports)
  • Organizational knowledge (7.1.6): New requirement to capture and protect knowledge from staff turnover and to acquire new knowledge through mentoring, benchmarking, experience
  • External provision (8.4): Risk-proportionate controls covering purchasing, associate arrangements, and outsourcing

Annex B Key Takeaways

  • ISO/TC 176 developed 17+ supporting standards that complement ISO 9001/15378 — none of them add mandatory requirements
  • Core three: ISO 9000 (vocabulary), ISO 9001 (requirements), ISO 9004 (excellence beyond compliance)
  • ISO 19011: The go-to guide for audit programme management — critical for supplier audits in pharmaceutical supply chains
  • ISO 10002: Customer complaint handling guidance — aligns with FDA and EU GMP complaint requirements
  • ISO 10015: Training guidelines — directly supports Clause 7.2 competence requirements
  • Table B.1 provides a navigation map: use it to find the right supporting standard when improving a specific QMS clause area

稽核員提醒

Annex A 的術語對照表是跨標準稽核的「翻譯字典」。EU GMP / FDA 使用的術語 (procedures, records, supplier) 可對應到 ISO 15378 的術語。理解這個對照可避免在混合稽核時的溝通誤解。
