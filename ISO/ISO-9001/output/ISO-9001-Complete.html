0.1

Introduction 0.1 — General

QMS 作為策略決策

0.1 General

The adoption of a quality management system is a strategic decision for an organization that can help to improve its overall performance and provide a sound basis for sustainable development initiatives.

The potential benefits to an organization of implementing a quality management system based on this International Standard are:

  1. the ability to consistently provide products and services that meet customer and applicable statutory and regulatory requirements;
  2. facilitating opportunities to enhance customer satisfaction;
  3. addressing risks and opportunities associated with its context and objectives;
  4. the ability to demonstrate conformity to specified quality management system requirements.

This International Standard employs the process approach, which incorporates the Plan-Do-Check-Act (PDCA) cycle and risk-based thinking.

In this International Standard, the following verbal forms are used:

  • "shall" — indicates a requirement
  • "should" — indicates a recommendation
  • "may" — indicates a permission
  • "can" — indicates a possibility or a capability

重點解析

QMS = 策略工具，不是行政負擔

標準開宗明義：導入 QMS 是策略決策（strategic decision）。這意味著 QMS 不只是用來通過客戶審核的紙本作業，而是協助組織達成業務目標的管理架構。

對製藥 CDMO 來說，這等同於說：ISO 9001 認證是你向潛在客戶展示「我們有能力持續穩定供貨」的市場信號。

「shall」vs「should」— 查核重點

稽核時最重要的用字辨別：

  • shall = 強制要求，審核員必查
  • should = 建議，可選擇性實施

許多組織誤將 should 當 shall，或忽略 shall 的要求，都是不符合項的常見原因。

比喻：ISO 9001 在製藥業的定位

ISO 9001 是品質管理的「基礎建設」。ISO 13485（醫療器材）和 ISO 15378（藥品包材）都是在 9001 的地基上蓋起的專業建築。掌握 9001，等於掌握整個 ISO 品質家族的共同語言。

Introduction 0.2 — Quality Management Principles

八大品質管理原則

0.2 Quality Management Principles

This International Standard is based on the quality management principles described in ISO 9000. The descriptions include a statement of each principle, a rationale of why the principle is important for the organization, some examples of benefits associated with the principle and examples of typical actions to improve the organization's performance when applying the principle.

The quality management principles are:

  • Customer focus
  • Leadership
  • Engagement of people
  • Process approach
  • Improvement
  • Evidence-based decision making
  • Relationship management

Note: The standard lists 7 principles here; ISO 9000:2015 details all 7. An eighth principle — risk-based thinking — is embedded throughout but is treated as a concept rather than a standalone principle in 9001:2015.

八大原則在製藥 CDMO 的對應

原則詳解與製藥應用

原則製藥 / CDMO 應用場景
1. 顧客導向客戶的 CQA（關鍵品質屬性）= 你的品質目標
2. 領導力品質文化由最高管理者示範，不只簽核文件
3. 人員投入GMP 訓練不只是合規，而是人員真正理解為何重要
4. 流程方法製程驗證、IQ/OQ/PQ 皆為流程導向思維
5. 持續改善CAPA 系統、管理審查輸出的改善行動
6. 基於證據決策OOS/OOT 調查基於數據，不基於直覺
7. 關係管理供應商資格審核、技術轉移夥伴管理

比喻：原則是指南針，條款是地圖

七大原則是 ISO 9001 的「哲學基礎」，而標準的具體條款（Clauses 4–10）是落地的地圖。理解原則，才能在無明確規定的情況下做出符合精神的判斷。

0.3

Introduction 0.3 — Process Approach & PDCA Cycle

流程方法與 PDCA 循環 — 風險導向思維

0.3.1 General — Process Approach

This International Standard promotes the adoption of a process approach when developing, implementing and improving the effectiveness of a quality management system, to enhance customer satisfaction by meeting customer requirements.

Understanding and managing interrelated processes as a system contributes to the organization's effectiveness and efficiency in achieving its intended results. The process approach involves the systematic definition and management of processes, and their interactions, so as to achieve the intended results in accordance with the quality policy and strategic direction of the organization.

The application of the process approach in a quality management system enables:

  1. understanding and consistency in meeting requirements;
  2. the consideration of processes in terms of added value;
  3. the achievement of effective process performance;
  4. improvement of processes based on evaluation of data and information.

0.3.2 Plan-Do-Check-Act (PDCA) Cycle

The PDCA cycle can be applied to all processes and to the quality management system as a whole. Clauses 4 to 10 can be grouped in relation to the PDCA cycle:

  • Plan: establish objectives and processes (Clauses 4, 5, 6, 7)
  • Do: implement what was planned (Clause 8)
  • Check: monitor and measure processes against policies, objectives, requirements (Clause 9)
  • Act: take actions to improve performance (Clause 10)

0.3.3 Risk-Based Thinking

Risk-based thinking is essential for achieving an effective quality management system. The concept of risk-based thinking has been implicit in previous editions of this International Standard including, for example, carrying out preventive action to eliminate potential nonconformities.

To conform to the requirements of this International Standard, an organization needs to plan and implement actions to address risks and opportunities. Addressing both risks and opportunities establishes a basis for increasing the effectiveness of the quality management system, achieving improved results and preventing negative effects.

Opportunities can arise as a result of a situation favourable to achieving an intended result, for example, a set of circumstances that allow the organization to attract customers, develop new products and services, reduce waste or improve productivity.

PDCA 在製藥的實踐對應

P — Plan 策劃 (Cl. 4-7)
ICH Q8 設計空間、製程開發、品質目標設定、SOP 建立、資源規劃
D — Do 執行 (Cl. 8)
商業製造批次、無菌灌裝作業、供應商管理、客戶需求確認
C — Check 查核 (Cl. 9)
QC 放行測試、OOS/OOT 調查、環境監測、內部稽核
A — Act 行動 (Cl. 10)
CAPA 執行、管理審查決議、製程改善、偏差根因消除

流程單元（Turtle Diagram）概念

ISO 9001 的流程方法要求每個核心流程都能回答：

  • 輸入 (Inputs)：這個流程需要什麼？
  • 輸出 (Outputs)：這個流程產出什麼？
  • 資源 (Resources)：人員、設備、環境
  • 方法 (Methods)：SOP、指引、技術
  • KPI：如何衡量這個流程是否有效？

這正是「海龜圖（Turtle Diagram）」的框架，常用於製藥 QMS 流程描述。

風險導向思維 vs 舊版預防措施

2008 版：8.5.3 要求組織訂定「預防措施」程序，通常淪為形式。

2015 版：廢除了獨立的「預防措施」條款，改為將風險思維貫穿整個標準。組織必須在規劃階段（6.1）就識別風險並決定如何應對。

製藥應用：這與 ICH Q9 品質風險管理（FMEA、HACCP 等工具）的理念完全一致。

比喻：從「出事再補救」到「先行設防」

2008 版預防措施像是在十字路口撞車後才裝護欄。2015 版風險思維像是在設計道路時就做風險評估——哪些路口容易出事？應裝號誌還是圓環？先設計好，再開放通行。

Introduction 0.4 — Relationship with Other Management System Standards

與其他標準的關係 — Annex SL

0.4 Relationship with Other Standards

This International Standard applies the framework developed by ISO to improve alignment among its International Standards for management systems (sometimes referred to as Annex SL — the High Level Structure).

This International Standard enables an organization to use the process approach, coupled with the PDCA cycle and risk-based thinking, to align or integrate its quality management system with the requirements of other management system standards.

This International Standard relates to ISO 9000 and ISO 9004 as follows:

  • ISO 9000:2015 provides essential background for the proper understanding and implementation of this International Standard — vocabulary and fundamental concepts
  • ISO 9004 provides guidance for organizations that choose to progress beyond the requirements of this International Standard — Managing for the sustained success of an organization

Sector-specific quality management system standards based on the requirements of this International Standard have been developed for a number of sectors. Some of these standards specify additional quality management system requirements, while others are limited to providing guidance.

Annex SL 的意義與標準整合

Annex SL（高階結構）是什麼？

ISO 為避免不同管理體系標準「各說各話」，設計了 Annex SL 通用框架：10 個相同的高階條款編號，讓各標準共享：

  • 第 4 節：組織背景
  • 第 5 節：領導力
  • 第 6 節：策劃
  • 第 7 節：支援
  • 第 8 節：運作
  • 第 9 節：績效評估
  • 第 10 節：改善

採用 Annex SL 的標準包括：ISO 9001、ISO 14001（環境）、ISO 45001（職安衛）、ISO 27001（資安）等。

製藥業整合 QMS 的機會

因為 Annex SL 共同架構，若工廠同時持有 ISO 9001 + ISO 14001 + ISO 45001，可整合為統一管理體系（IMS），降低文件重複與稽核負擔。

CDMO 場景：客戶審核 + ISO 9001 + cGMP 可在相似的文件框架下協同管理，節省維護成本。

比喻：Annex SL = USB-C 接口標準

過去各管理標準像各廠商的充電接口——形狀不同、無法共用。Annex SL 就是 ISO 推出的「USB-C 標準」，讓所有管理體系標準用同一個架構插孔，整合更容易。

Clauses 1–3: Scope, Normative References & Terms

範圍、參考文件、術語定義

Clause 1 — Scope

This International Standard specifies requirements for a quality management system when an organization:

  1. needs to demonstrate its ability to consistently provide products and services that meet customer and applicable statutory and regulatory requirements, and
  2. aims to enhance customer satisfaction through the effective application of the system, including processes for improvement of the system and the assurance of conformity to customer and applicable statutory and regulatory requirements.
All the requirements of this International Standard are generic and are intended to be applicable to any organization, regardless of its type or size, or the products and services it provides.

NOTE 1: The terms "product" or "service" only apply to products and services intended for, or required by, a customer.
NOTE 2: Statutory and regulatory requirements can be expressed as legal requirements.

Clause 2 — Normative References

The following documents are normatively referenced in this document and are indispensable for its application:

  • ISO 9000:2015, Quality management systems — Fundamentals and vocabulary

This is the only normative reference. All other related standards (ISO 9004, etc.) are informative only.

Clause 3 — Terms and Definitions

For the purposes of this document, the terms and definitions given in ISO 9000:2015 apply.

範圍與適用性解析

「All requirements applicable」— 適用性原則

ISO 9001 要求組織適用所有條款，但允許在 Clause 4.3（QMS 範圍）中記錄哪些條款不適用及理由。

常見不適用場景：

  • 純粹服務型組織 → 8.3 設計開發可能不適用
  • 不從事設計的製造廠 → 8.3 不適用，但必須記錄理由

重要：不適用必須不影響組織「確保產品符合性和客戶滿意度」的能力。

製藥 CDMO 的適用範圍思考

對 CDMO 而言，通常所有條款都適用，因為：

  • 有設計開發（客製化製程 / 方法開發）→ 8.3 適用
  • 有外包供應商（原料 / 分析服務）→ 8.4 適用
  • 直接面對品牌藥廠客戶 → 顧客滿意度條款全部適用

ISO 9000 vs ISO 9001 — 兩份文件的關係

文件性質功能
ISO 9000:2015詞彙標準定義術語、基本概念，Clause 2 唯一規範性參考
ISO 9001:2015要求標準規定 QMS 的強制要求，用於認證
ISO 9004:2018指引標準超越 9001 要求的卓越管理，非認證用

比喻：9000 是字典，9001 是法規

ISO 9000 像是醫學字典（告訴你每個詞的定義）。ISO 9001 像是《藥事法》（告訴你必須做什麼）。兩者必須配合使用，但只有 9001 能用於認證。

Clause 4 — Context of the Organization

組織背景 — 內外部議題、利害關係人、QMS 範圍與流程

4.1 Understanding the Organization and Its Context

The organization shall determine external and internal issues that are relevant to its purpose and its strategic direction and that affect its ability to achieve the intended result(s) of its quality management system.

The organization shall monitor and review information about these external and internal issues.

NOTE 2: Understanding the external context can be facilitated by considering issues arising from legal, technological, competitive, market, cultural, social and economic environments, whether international, national, regional or local.
NOTE 3: Understanding the internal context can be facilitated by considering issues related to values, culture, knowledge and performance of the organization.

4.2 Understanding the Needs and Expectations of Interested Parties

The organization shall determine:

  1. the interested parties that are relevant to the quality management system;
  2. the requirements of these interested parties that are relevant to the quality management system.

The organization shall monitor and review information about these interested parties and their relevant requirements.

4.3 Determining the Scope of the QMS

The organization shall determine the boundaries and applicability of the quality management system to establish its scope. When determining this scope, the organization shall consider:

  1. the external and internal issues referred to in 4.1;
  2. the requirements of relevant interested parties referred to in 4.2;
  3. the products and services of the organization.
The scope of the organization's quality management system shall be available and be maintained as documented information. The scope shall state the types of products and services covered, and provide justification for any requirement of this International Standard that the organization determines is not applicable.

4.4 Quality Management System and Its Processes

The organization shall establish, implement, maintain and continually improve a quality management system, including the processes needed and their interactions.

The organization shall determine the processes needed and shall:

  1. determine the inputs required and the outputs expected from these processes;
  2. determine the sequence and interaction of these processes;
  3. determine and apply the criteria and methods (including monitoring, measurements and related performance indicators) needed to ensure the effective operation and control of these processes;
  4. determine the resources needed for these processes and ensure their availability;
  5. assign the responsibilities and authorities for these processes;
  6. address the risks and opportunities as determined in accordance with the requirements of 6.1;
  7. evaluate these processes and implement any changes needed to ensure that these processes achieve their intended results;
  8. improve the processes and the quality management system.

4.4.2: To the extent necessary, the organization shall (a) maintain documented information to support the operation of its processes; (b) retain documented information to have confidence that the processes are being carried out as planned.

4.2 — 利害關係人識別

製藥 CDMO 的主要利害關係人：

  • 客戶（品牌藥廠）：產品規格、技術轉移要求、稽核期望
  • 主管機關（FDA / EMA / TFDA）：GMP 合規要求
  • 員工：安全環境、公平待遇、職涯發展
  • 供應商：長期合作條件、品質協議
  • 投資者 / 股東：財務績效、法規風險管理
  • 患者（最終使用者）：產品安全性、有效性

4.4 — 流程清單 (Process Inventory) 的重要性

4.4.1 要求組織確定所有 QMS 需要的流程。實務建議：

  1. 列出核心業務流程（如：接單 → 技術審查 → 製造 → QC → 放行 → 出貨）
  2. 加入支援流程（HR、採購、設備維護、文件管理）
  3. 加入管理流程（策略規劃、管理審查、內部稽核）
  4. 為每個流程指定流程擁有者（Process Owner）

這份流程清單是 QMS 架構的骨幹，也是稽核員必查的文件化資訊。

比喻：QMS 範圍 = 保險的承保範圍

4.3 的 QMS 範圍就像保險單的「承保範圍」：你必須清楚說明涵蓋了哪些地址、哪些產品、哪些活動。範圍外的事項，保險不負責——ISO 認證也不涵蓋。CDMO 若有多個廠址、多條產品線，每個範圍決定都需要書面依據。

Clause 5 — Leadership

領導力 — 最高管理者的 10 項承諾、品質政策、組織角色

5.1.1 General — Leadership and Commitment

Top management shall demonstrate leadership and commitment with respect to the quality management system by:

  1. taking accountability for the effectiveness of the quality management system;
  2. ensuring that the quality policy and quality objectives are established for the quality management system and are compatible with the context and strategic direction of the organization;
  3. ensuring the integration of the quality management system requirements into the organization's business processes;
  4. promoting the use of the process approach and risk-based thinking;
  5. ensuring that the resources needed for the quality management system are available;
  6. communicating the importance of effective quality management and of conforming to the quality management system requirements;
  7. ensuring that the quality management system achieves its intended results;
  8. engaging, directing and supporting persons to contribute to the effectiveness of the quality management system;
  9. promoting improvement;
  10. supporting other relevant management roles to demonstrate their leadership as it applies to their areas of responsibility.

5.1.2 Customer Focus

Top management shall demonstrate leadership and commitment with respect to customer focus by ensuring that:

  1. customer and applicable statutory and regulatory requirements are determined, understood and consistently met;
  2. the risks and opportunities that can affect conformity of products and services and the ability to enhance customer satisfaction are determined and addressed;
  3. the focus on enhancing customer satisfaction is maintained.

5.2 Policy

5.2.1 Establishing the Quality Policy

Top management shall establish, implement and maintain a quality policy that:

  1. is appropriate to the purpose and context of the organization and supports its strategic direction;
  2. provides a framework for setting quality objectives;
  3. includes a commitment to satisfy applicable requirements;
  4. includes a commitment to continual improvement of the quality management system.

5.2.2 Communicating the Quality Policy

The quality policy shall:

  1. be available and be maintained as documented information;
  2. be communicated, understood and applied within the organization;
  3. be available to relevant interested parties, as appropriate.

5.3 Organizational Roles, Responsibilities and Authorities

Top management shall ensure that the responsibilities and authorities for relevant roles are assigned, communicated and understood within the organization.

Top management shall assign the responsibility and authority for:

  1. ensuring that the quality management system conforms to the requirements of this International Standard;
  2. ensuring that the processes are delivering their intended outputs;
  3. reporting on the performance of the quality management system and on opportunities for improvement, in particular to top management;
  4. ensuring the promotion of customer focus throughout the organization;
  5. ensuring that the integrity of the quality management system is maintained when changes to the quality management system are planned and implemented.

領導力：不只是簽名

5.1 — 最高管理者的 10 項行動（稽核重點）

稽核員看的不是「最高管理者有沒有簽批品質政策」，而是有沒有實質行動的證據：

  • 管理審查會議記錄（親自主持？還是委派？）
  • 品質目標與公司策略的連結文件
  • 品質議題在董事會 / 高層會議的討論紀錄
  • 資源投入決策（人力、設備）的核准記錄
  • 對員工的溝通記錄（全體大會、Email、內部公告）

5.1.2 顧客導向 — 製藥的 CQA 連結

對製藥 CDMO：「客戶需求」包括：

  • 產品的 CQA（關鍵品質屬性），如純度、含量、無菌性
  • 法規要求：FDA / EMA / ICH 要求
  • 合約要求：交期、包裝規格、穩定性數據
  • 隱性需求：客戶未明說但期待的服務水準

最高管理者必須確保組織有機制持續追蹤並滿足上述需求，而非僅靠業務部門口頭溝通。

5.3 — 2015 版重大改變：無管理代表（MR）要求

ISO 9001:2008 要求指定一名管理代表（Management Representative）。

ISO 9001:2015 刪除了此要求。原因：避免品質被「外包給一個 QA 主管負責」，強調品質是整個組織 / 最高管理者的責任。

實務上許多組織仍指定品質主管執行這些功能，但標準不再強制指定單一「管理代表」頭銜。

品質政策的六項要求（5.2 總結）

  1. 適合組織目的與背景
  2. 與策略方向一致
  3. 提供品質目標的框架
  4. 包含滿足要求的承諾
  5. 包含持續改善的承諾
  6. 以文件化資訊形式存在並傳達

稽核常見問題：品質政策貼在牆上，但員工問起不知道內容——這違反「被溝通、理解和應用」的要求。

比喻：最高管理者是「品質文化的體溫計」

組織的品質文化溫度，由最高管理者的行為決定。如果 CEO 在會議上說「這個出貨不符合規格，但客戶急，先出」——這一句話讓整個品質體系失效。反之，若最高管理者主動參與 CAPA 討論，品質意識自然向下擴散。

Clause 6 — Planning

策劃 — 風險與機會、品質目標、變更規劃

6.1 Actions to Address Risks and Opportunities

6.1.1

When planning for the quality management system, the organization shall consider the issues referred to in 4.1 and the requirements referred to in 4.2 and determine the risks and opportunities that need to be addressed to:

  1. give assurance that the quality management system can achieve its intended result(s);
  2. enhance desirable effects;
  3. prevent, or reduce, undesired effects;
  4. achieve improvement.

6.1.2

The organization shall plan:

  1. actions to address these risks and opportunities;
  2. how to: (1) integrate and implement the actions into its quality management system processes; (2) evaluate the effectiveness of these actions.
Actions taken to address risks and opportunities shall be proportionate to the potential impact on the conformity of products and services.

NOTE 1: Options to address risks can include avoiding risk, taking risk in order to pursue an opportunity, eliminating the risk source, changing the likelihood or consequences, sharing the risk, or retaining risk by informed decision.

NOTE 2: Opportunities can lead to the adoption of new practices, launching new products, opening new markets, addressing new customers, building partnerships, using new technology and other desirable possibilities.

6.2 Quality Objectives and Planning to Achieve Them

6.2.1

The organization shall establish quality objectives at relevant functions, levels and processes. The quality objectives shall:

  1. be consistent with the quality policy;
  2. be measurable;
  3. take into account applicable requirements;
  4. be relevant to conformity of products and services and to enhancement of customer satisfaction;
  5. be monitored;
  6. be communicated;
  7. be updated as appropriate.

The organization shall maintain documented information on the quality objectives.

6.2.2 — Planning to Achieve Quality Objectives

When planning how to achieve its quality objectives, the organization shall determine:

  1. what will be done;
  2. what resources will be required;
  3. who will be responsible;
  4. when it will be completed;
  5. how the results will be evaluated.

6.3 Planning of Changes

When the organization determines the need for changes to the quality management system, the changes shall be carried out in a planned manner. The organization shall consider:

  1. the purpose of the changes and their potential consequences;
  2. the integrity of the quality management system;
  3. the availability of resources;
  4. the allocation or reallocation of responsibilities and authorities.

策劃解析：從 PESTLE 到行動

6.1 — 風險管理工具（非強制，但推薦）

標準不要求特定工具，但常用方法：

工具適合場景
FMEA（失效模式分析）製程風險、設備故障
SWOT 分析組織層級策略風險
HACCP食品安全 / 製藥污染風險
風險矩陣快速的可能性 × 影響評估
ICH Q9 方法藥品品質風險（與 GMP 整合）

風險應對行動必須與潛在影響成比例——高風險投入更多資源，低風險可接受或監控即可。

6.2 — 品質目標 = SMART 目標

標準要求品質目標必須「可量測（measurable）」——這直接對應 SMART 原則：

  • Specific：明確說明要達成什麼
  • Measurable：有量化指標（如批次良率 ≥ 98%）
  • Achievable：可實現，有資源支持
  • Relevant：與品質政策和客戶需求相關
  • Time-bound：有明確完成時限

製藥 CDMO 常見品質目標：OOS 率、客訴次數、CAPA 準時關閉率、稽核不符合項數。

6.3 — 變更管理 (Change Control)

6.3 要求 QMS 的變更要「有計畫地進行」。這直接對應製藥業的變更管制（Change Control）系統：

  • 任何對 SOP、設備、製程、廠址的變更
  • 需評估對產品品質、客戶要求、法規合規的影響
  • 必須有核准流程和影響評估記錄
  • 對重大變更：可能需要通知主管機關或客戶

比喻：風險管理 = 製藥業已在做的事

對製藥人來說，6.1「風險與機會」並不陌生——這就是 ICH Q9 在做的事。ISO 9001 的風險管理比 ICH Q9 更寬，延伸到商業風險（供應商中斷、市場需求改變），但核心邏輯完全一致：識別 → 評估 → 應對 → 監控。

Clause 7 — Support

支援 — 資源、人員能力、意識、溝通、文件化資訊

7.1 Resources

7.1.1 General

The organization shall determine and provide the resources needed for the establishment, implementation, maintenance and continual improvement of the quality management system. The organization shall consider:

  1. the capabilities of, and constraints on, existing internal resources;
  2. what needs to be obtained from external providers.

7.1.2 People

The organization shall determine and provide the persons necessary for the effective implementation of its quality management system and for the operation and control of its processes.

7.1.3 Infrastructure

The organization shall determine, provide and maintain the infrastructure necessary for the operation of its processes. NOTE: Infrastructure can include: (a) buildings and associated utilities; (b) equipment, including hardware and software; (c) transportation resources; (d) information and communication technology.

7.1.4 Environment for the Operation of Processes

The organization shall determine, provide and maintain the environment necessary for the operation of its processes. NOTE: A suitable environment can be a combination of human and physical factors, such as: (a) social (e.g. non-discriminatory, calm, non-confrontational); (b) psychological (e.g. stress-reducing, burnout prevention, emotionally protective); (c) physical (e.g. temperature, heat, humidity, light, airflow, hygiene, noise).

7.1.5 Monitoring and Measuring Resources

7.1.5.1 General: The organization shall determine and provide the resources needed to ensure valid and reliable results when monitoring or measuring is used to verify the conformity of products and services. The resources provided shall: (a) be suitable for the specific type of monitoring and measurement activities being undertaken; (b) be maintained to ensure their continuing fitness for their purpose.

7.1.5.2 Measurement Traceability: When measurement traceability is a requirement, measuring equipment shall be:

  1. calibrated or verified, or both, at specified intervals, or prior to use, against measurement standards traceable to international or national measurement standards;
  2. identified in order to determine their status;
  3. safeguarded from adjustments, damage or deterioration that would invalidate the calibration status and subsequent measurement results.

7.1.6 Organizational Knowledge

The organization shall determine the knowledge necessary for the operation of its processes and to achieve conformity of products and services. This knowledge shall be maintained and made available to the extent necessary.

When addressing changing needs and trends, the organization shall consider its current knowledge and determine how to acquire or access any necessary additional knowledge and required updates.

NOTE 1: Organizational knowledge is knowledge specific to the organization; it is generally gained by experience. It is information that is used and shared to achieve the organization's objectives.

NOTE 2: Organizational knowledge can be based on: (a) internal sources (e.g. intellectual property; knowledge gained from experience; lessons learned from failures and successful projects; capturing and sharing undocumented knowledge and experience; the results of improvements in processes, products and services); (b) external sources (e.g. standards; academia; conferences; gathering knowledge from customers or external providers).

7.2 Competence

The organization shall:

  1. determine the necessary competence of person(s) doing work under its control that affects the performance and effectiveness of the quality management system;
  2. ensure that these persons are competent on the basis of appropriate education, training, or experience;
  3. where applicable, take actions to acquire the necessary competence, and evaluate the effectiveness of the actions taken;
  4. retain appropriate documented information as evidence of competence.

7.3 Awareness

The organization shall ensure that persons doing work under the organization's control are aware of:

  1. the quality policy;
  2. relevant quality objectives;
  3. their contribution to the effectiveness of the quality management system, including the benefits of improved performance;
  4. the implications of not conforming with the quality management system requirements.

7.4 Communication

The organization shall determine the internal and external communications relevant to the quality management system, including:

  1. on what it will communicate;
  2. when to communicate;
  3. with whom to communicate;
  4. how to communicate;
  5. who communicates.

7.5 Documented Information

7.5.1 General

The organization's quality management system shall include: (a) documented information required by this International Standard; (b) documented information determined by the organization as being necessary for the effectiveness of the quality management system.

7.5.2 Creating and Updating

When creating and updating documented information, the organization shall ensure appropriate:

  1. identification and description (e.g. a title, date, author, or reference number);
  2. format (e.g. language, software version, graphics) and media (e.g. paper, electronic);
  3. review and approval for suitability and adequacy.

7.5.3 Control of Documented Information

7.5.3.1: Documented information required by the quality management system shall be controlled to ensure: (a) it is available and suitable for use, where and when it is needed; (b) it is adequately protected (e.g. from loss of confidentiality, improper use, or loss of integrity).

7.5.3.2: For the control of documented information, the organization shall address the following activities, as applicable:

  1. distribution, access, retrieval and use;
  2. storage and preservation, including preservation of legibility;
  3. control of changes (e.g. version control);
  4. retention and disposition.
Documented information retained as evidence of conformity shall be protected from unintended alterations.

支援體系解析

7.1.5 — 量測溯源 (Metrological Traceability)

製藥廠的量測管理是 GMP 核心之一：

  • 每台量測設備必須定期校正（校正週期 + 標準品溯源至 NIST/BIPM）
  • 校正記錄必須保存（文件化資訊）
  • 發現設備失效 → 必須評估過去量測結果的有效性
  • 設備管理清單 + 校正標籤 = 基本合規要求

7.1.6 — 組織知識：製藥業最被低估的條款

這是 ISO 9001:2015 新增的條款，也是製藥業最容易忽視的要求。

常見的知識流失場景：

  • 資深 QP（授權人）離職 → 帶走多年法規判斷經驗
  • 製程研發人員跳槽 → 配方優化的隱性知識無處找
  • 舊設備報廢 → 操作技巧和異常排除方法沒有文件記錄
  • 客戶技術轉移完成後 → 原研究的脈絡和決策依據遺失

應對策略：知識管理計畫、技術文件化、師徒制、離職移交規程、經驗教訓資料庫（Lessons Learned）

7.2 — 四步驟能力管理流程

  1. 確定：哪些崗位需要什麼能力？（工作說明書、能力矩陣）
  2. 確保：透過訓練、招募、外包獲得所需能力
  3. 保持：持續教育、年度複訓、能力評估
  4. 保留證據：訓練記錄、測驗成績、OJT 表單（文件化資訊）

注意：學歷/資格 ≠ 能力。能力需要通過評估證明，而非只看證書。

7.5 — 「文件化資訊」統一術語的意義

ISO 9001:2008 使用三個不同術語：

  • 文件（Documents）— SOP、標準、程序
  • 品質手冊（Quality Manual）— 強制要求
  • 記錄（Records）— 活動的證據

ISO 9001:2015 統一為 「文件化資訊（Documented Information）」：

  • 「維持文件化資訊」= 過去的「文件」（隨時更新的文件）
  • 「保留文件化資訊」= 過去的「記錄」（不可更改的證據）

製藥 QMS 文件體系需要據此更新術語，但實質要求並未重大改變。品質手冊不再強制要求，但仍推薦作為 QMS 架構說明。

7.5.2 — ALCOA 精神的 ISO 對應

7.5.2 的文件建立要求對應製藥業的 ALCOA+ 原則：

ISO 7.5.2 要求ALCOA+ 對應
識別與描述（標題、日期、作者）Attributable 可歸屬、Contemporaneous 即時
格式（語言、版本、媒介）Legible 可讀性
審核與批准Accurate 準確、Original 原始

製藥 CDMO 實作思考

你的工廠如何回答以下 Clause 7 稽核問題？

  • Cl.7.1.6：當資深員工離職，你如何確保關鍵技術知識不流失？有 Lessons Learned 資料庫嗎？
  • Cl.7.2：訓練記錄只有出席簽到表，還是有能力評估紀錄？
  • Cl.7.3：操作人員知道他們的工作如何影響客戶和患者嗎？
  • Cl.7.5.3：電子文件系統有版本控制嗎？紙本記錄防止未授權修改嗎？

比喻：組織知識 = 企業的「隱性資產」

財務報表列不出「20 年製程優化知識」的價值，但這往往是 CDMO 最核心的競爭優勢。7.1.6 要求組織對這類隱性資產進行管理——就像你會定期備份電腦資料一樣，知識也需要「備份」到文件和系統中，而不是只留在幾個人的腦袋裡。

8.1

Operational Planning and Control — 運作規劃與控制

Clause 8.1 — The execution layer: translating risk/opportunity plans into controlled processes

8.1 Operational Planning and Control

The organization shall plan, implement and control the processes needed to meet the requirements for the provision of products and services, and to implement the actions determined in Clause 6, by:

  1. determining the requirements for the products and services;
  2. establishing criteria for:
    1. the processes;
    2. the acceptance of products and services;
  3. determining the resources needed to achieve conformity to the product and service requirements;
  4. implementing control of the processes in accordance with the criteria;
  5. determining, maintaining and retaining documented information to the extent necessary:
    1. to have confidence that the processes have been carried out as planned;
    2. to demonstrate the conformity of products and services to their requirements.

The output of this planning shall be suitable for the organization's operations.

The organization shall control planned changes and review the consequences of unintended changes, taking action to mitigate any adverse effects, as necessary.

The organization shall ensure that outsourced processes are controlled (see 8.4).

解說：運作規劃是 Clause 6 計畫的執行層

核心邏輯：計畫 → 執行 → 文件化

8.1 是 ISO 9001 的「執行引擎」。Clause 6 決定了要做什麼（風險/機會計畫），8.1 規定了如何把這些計畫落地成受控的製程操作。

  • 準則（Criteria）：製程接受準則，等同藥廠的製程規格與 in-process control 限值
  • 文件化資訊（Documented information）：批次紀錄、製程規程——藥廠已遠超此要求
  • 計畫性變更控制：正式的 Change Control 系統——GMP 必備，ISO 9001 僅要求「控制並審查後果」

類比：蓋房子的施工圖

Clause 6 是建築師的設計方案（決定要建什麼），8.1 是施工圖加上品質管理計畫（告訴工人每道工序的標準和驗收條件）。沒有施工圖，工人再努力也可能蓋錯。

藥廠視角：外包製程的控制

「外包製程應受控制」（見 8.4）——在製藥 CDMO 中，每一個委外的檢驗、滅菌、包裝步驟都屬此範疇。ISO 9001 要求「控制」，GMP 則進一步要求正式的技術協議（Technical Agreement）與供應商稽核。

8.2

Requirements for Products and Services — 產品與服務要求

Clauses 8.2.1–8.2.4 — Customer communication, requirement determination, review, and change management

8.2.1 Customer Communication

Communication with customers shall include:

  1. providing information relating to products and services;
  2. handling enquiries, contracts or orders, including changes;
  3. obtaining customer feedback relating to products and services, including customer complaints;
  4. handling or controlling customer property;
  5. establishing specific requirements for contingency actions, when relevant.

8.2.2 Determining Requirements

The organization shall ensure that:

  1. the requirements for the products and services are defined, including:
    1. any applicable statutory and regulatory requirements;
    2. those considered necessary by the organization;
  2. the organization can meet the claims for the products and services it offers.

8.2.3 Review of Requirements

The organization shall ensure that it has the ability to meet the requirements before committing to supply, to include:

  1. requirements specified by the customer, including delivery and post-delivery activities;
  2. requirements not stated by the customer, but necessary for the specified or intended use;
  3. requirements specified by the organization;
  4. statutory and regulatory requirements;
  5. contract or order requirements differing from those previously expressed.

The organization shall retain documented information on the results of the review and on any new requirements.

8.2.4 Changes to Requirements

The organization shall ensure that relevant documented information is amended, and that relevant persons are made aware of the changed requirements, when requirements are changed.

解說：合約審查——CDMO 的核心能力

8.2.1 客戶溝通的 5 個面向

  • ① 產品資訊提供：規格書、CoA、SDS
  • ② 詢價/合約/訂單處理：含變更管理
  • ③ 客戶回饋與投訴：ISO 要求收集，GMP 要求 CAPA
  • ④ 客戶財產管理：客戶提供的原料、模具、IP
  • ⑤ 應急措施要求：供應鏈中斷應對計畫

類比：餐廳點餐的確認流程

8.2.3 合約審查就像廚師接到特殊菜單前先確認：食材有沒有（資源）、廚師會不會做（能力）、客人有沒有過敏（法規/隱含需求）。先確認，再承諾，才不會在出菜時出狀況。

藥廠 CDMO 的合約審查（8.2.3）

CDMO 在接受新客戶委託前，合約審查要涵蓋：

  • 法規市場要求（FDA/EMA/PMDA 哪個市場？）
  • 技術可行性（劑型、製程能力、無菌等級）
  • 隱含要求（例：無菌產品自帶 Annex 1 要求，即使客戶未明確指定）
  • 技術協議（Quality Agreement）內容

這正是 8.2.2b 所說的「組織需確認自己能兌現承諾」。

8.3

Design and Development — 設計與開發

Clauses 8.3.1–8.3.6 — Planning, inputs, controls (review/verification/validation), outputs, changes

8.3.1–8.3.2 General & Planning

The organization shall establish, implement and maintain a design and development process appropriate to ensure the subsequent provision of products and services.

In determining the stages and controls, the organization shall consider:

  • nature, duration and complexity of activities;
  • required process stages, reviews, verification and validation activities;
  • responsibilities and authorities;
  • internal and external resource needs;
  • involvement of customers and users;
  • documented information needed to demonstrate requirements have been met.

8.3.3 Design and Development Inputs

The organization shall determine requirements essential for the specific types of products and services, considering:

  1. functional and performance requirements;
  2. information derived from previous similar activities;
  3. statutory and regulatory requirements;
  4. standards or codes of practice;
  5. potential consequences of failure.

Inputs shall be adequate, complete and unambiguous. Conflicting inputs shall be resolved.

8.3.4 Design and Development Controls

Controls shall ensure that:

  1. the results to be achieved are defined;
  2. reviews are conducted to evaluate the ability to meet requirements;
  3. verification activities ensure outputs meet input requirements;
  4. validation activities ensure products/services meet requirements for specified application or intended use;
  5. necessary actions are taken on problems determined;
  6. documented information of these activities is retained.
NOTE: Design and development reviews, verification and validation have distinct purposes. They can be conducted separately or in any combination.

8.3.5 Design and Development Outputs

Outputs shall:

  1. meet the input requirements;
  2. be adequate for subsequent provision processes;
  3. include or reference monitoring/measuring requirements and acceptance criteria;
  4. specify characteristics essential for intended purpose and safe provision.

8.3.6 Design and Development Changes

The organization shall identify, review and control changes to ensure no adverse impact on conformity. Retained documented information shall include:

  1. design and development changes;
  2. results of reviews;
  3. authorization of changes;
  4. actions taken to prevent adverse impacts.

解說：設計開發在製藥的對應

Review / Verification / Validation 三者的根本區別

ISO 9001 的 NOTE 特別強調三者目的不同，這在藥廠環境中尤為重要：

  • Review（審查）：評估設計結果「能否達到要求」——設計審查會議（Design Review Meeting），藥廠比照 ICH Q8 中的配方設計審查
  • Verification（驗證）：確認「輸出符合輸入規格」——例如確認處方設計符合 TPP（Target Product Profile）中的規格要求
  • Validation（確效）：確認產品在「實際使用條件下」能達到預期用途——藥廠的製程確效（Process Validation）、清潔確效、滅菌確效

類比：設計一架飛機

Review：工程師開會審查設計圖，確認翼展計算沒有錯誤。
Verification：在風洞中測試模型，確認氣動數據符合設計規格。
Validation：實機飛行測試，確認飛機在真實天空中能安全飛行。

三步缺一不可——風洞通過了，不代表真機飛得起來。

藥廠的設計開發對應關係

  • 8.3 設計開發 → ICH Q8 藥品開發（pharmaceutical development）
  • 設計輸入（8.3.3） → Target Product Profile（TPP）、法規要求、前期研究數據
  • 設計輸出（8.3.5） → 批次製造記錄（BMR）、品管方法、規格書
  • 設計變更（8.3.6） → 技術轉移後的 Post-Approval Change（變更管理）
  • 設計開發 → 生產：技術轉移（Technology Transfer）= 從 R&D 到商業生產

ICH Q8 Quality by Design（QbD）與 8.3 的連結

ICH Q8 提倡從設計初期就系統性地理解產品和製程，建立 Design Space。這正是 ISO 9001 8.3 設計控制的進階版：

  • CQA（關鍵品質屬性）= 設計輸入
  • CPP（關鍵製程參數）= 設計輸出的一部分
  • Design Space = 驗證過的操作範圍
8.4

Control of Externally Provided Processes, Products and Services — 外部供應管控

Clauses 8.4.1–8.4.3 — Three scenarios, risk-proportionate controls, six communication categories

8.4.1 General

The organization shall ensure that externally provided processes, products and services conform to requirements.

Controls shall be determined when:

  1. products and services from external providers are intended for incorporation into the organization's own products and services;
  2. products and services are provided directly to the customer by external providers on behalf of the organization;
  3. a process, or part of a process, is provided by an external provider as a result of a decision by the organization (outsourcing).

The organization shall determine and apply criteria for the evaluation, selection, monitoring of performance, and re-evaluation of external providers, based on their ability to provide in accordance with requirements. Documented information of these activities and any necessary actions shall be retained.

8.4.2 Type and Extent of Control

The organization shall ensure externally provided processes, products and services do not adversely affect ability to consistently deliver conforming products/services. The organization shall:

  1. ensure externally provided processes remain within the control of its QMS;
  2. define both the controls for the external provider and those for the resulting output;
  3. take into consideration:
    1. the potential impact on the organization's ability to consistently meet requirements;
    2. the effectiveness of the controls applied by the external provider;
  4. determine verification or other activities necessary to ensure requirements are met.

8.4.3 Information for External Providers

The organization shall communicate to external providers its requirements for:

  1. the processes, products and services to be provided;
  2. the approval of: products and services; methods, processes and equipment; the release of products and services;
  3. competence, including any required qualification of persons;
  4. the external providers' interactions with the organization;
  5. control and monitoring of the external providers' performance to be applied by the organization;
  6. verification or validation activities that the organization, or its customer, intends to perform at the external providers' premises.

解說：供應商管控——ISO 9001 vs. GMP

8.4.1 的三種情境

  • 情境 A（納入自有產品）：採購原料 API、輔料、包材——典型的藥廠採購場景
  • 情境 B（代表你直接交貨給客戶）：分銷商代替你出貨——藥廠較少但合約研究中存在
  • 情境 C（委外製程）：委託滅菌、委託檢驗、委託包裝——CDMO 的核心業務模式

ISO 9001 與 GMP 的供應商管控深度差異

ISO 9001 要求「評估、選擇、監測、再評估」，但沒有規定如何做。GMP 標準走得更遠：

  • ICH Q10：要求書面的供應商品質協議（Technical/Quality Agreement）
  • ISO 15378（藥品包材）：供應商稽核計畫、定期評估報告
  • FDA QS Regulation（21 CFR 820）：要求正式的 Approved Supplier List 與稽核紀錄
  • EU GMP Annex 8：原料採樣與抽驗——到廠前的確認

類比：餐廳的食材供應商管理

米其林餐廳不會只看供應商的報價單，他們會親自去農場確認種植方式（現場稽核），要求農藥檢測報告（CoA），並建立長期合作關係（Approved Supplier）。ISO 9001 說「你要管好供應商」，GMP 告訴你「具體怎麼管」。

8.4.3 對外部供應商的六類溝通要求

  1. 要提供的製程/產品/服務規格
  2. 核准事項（產品、方法、設備、放行）
  3. 人員資格能力要求
  4. 與組織的互動界面
  5. 組織對供應商績效的監控方式
  6. 組織或客戶將在供應商場所執行的驗證/確效活動

第 6 點特別重要：客戶到 CDMO 現場執行品質稽核，或 CDMO 到原料供應商執行 Vendor Audit——都在此條款範疇。

8.5

Production and Service Provision — 生產與服務提供

Clauses 8.5.1–8.5.6 — Controlled conditions, traceability, customer property, preservation, post-delivery, change control

8.5.1 Control of Production and Service Provision

The organization shall implement production and service provision under controlled conditions, including as applicable:

  1. the availability of documented information defining: characteristics of products, services, or activities; and results to be achieved;
  2. availability and use of suitable monitoring and measuring resources;
  3. implementation of monitoring and measurement at appropriate stages to verify criteria have been met;
  4. use of suitable infrastructure and environment;
  5. appointment of competent persons, including any required qualification;
  6. validation, and periodic revalidation, of processes where the resulting output cannot be verified by subsequent monitoring or measurement;
  7. implementation of actions to prevent human error;
  8. implementation of release, delivery and post-delivery activities.

8.5.2 Identification and Traceability

The organization shall use suitable means to identify outputs when necessary to ensure conformity. The organization shall identify the status of outputs with respect to monitoring and measurement throughout production. The organization shall control the unique identification of outputs when traceability is a requirement, and retain documented information to enable traceability.

8.5.3 Property Belonging to Customers or External Providers

The organization shall exercise care with property belonging to customers or external providers while under the organization's control. The organization shall identify, verify, protect and safeguard such property. When property is lost, damaged or found unsuitable, the organization shall report to the customer or external provider and retain documented information.

NOTE: Property can include materials, components, tools and equipment, premises, intellectual property and personal data.

8.5.4 Preservation

The organization shall preserve the outputs during production and service provision to ensure conformity to requirements. Preservation can include identification, handling, contamination control, packaging, storage, transmission or transportation, and protection.

8.5.5 Post-Delivery Activities

The organization shall meet requirements for post-delivery activities, considering:

  1. statutory and regulatory requirements;
  2. potential undesired consequences;
  3. nature, use and intended lifetime of products and services;
  4. customer requirements;
  5. customer feedback.

8.5.6 Control of Changes

The organization shall review and control changes for production to ensure continuing conformity with requirements. Retained documented information shall describe: results of the review of changes; person(s) authorizing the change; and any necessary actions arising from the review.

解說：生產控制與藥廠 GMP 的深度對應

8.5.1f — 「特殊製程」確效：ISO 9001 的核心概念

最重要的條款：當製程結果「無法靠後續監測或量測驗證」時，需要對該製程進行確效（Validation）並定期再確效。

這正是藥廠確效的理論基礎——你無法對每支注射劑做無菌測試（破壞性），所以必須提前確效整個無菌製程：

  • 滅菌確效（Sterilization Validation）：高壓蒸汽、乾熱、過濾除菌
  • 培地灌裝（Media Fill / Process Simulation）：無菌灌裝製程模擬
  • 清潔確效（Cleaning Validation）：共線生產的殘留交叉污染控制
  • 電腦化系統確效（CSV）：無法靠肉眼確認的自動化製程

類比：烘焙蛋糕 vs. 罐頭殺菌

烘焙蛋糕可以切開確認熟了（後續量測可驗證）。但罐頭殺菌後無法每罐都打開測試內容物還在不在，所以必須事先確效：在特定溫度、壓力、時間組合下，確認所有位置都能達到足夠的致死率（F₀值）。這就是「特殊製程」確效的本質。

8.5.2 可追溯性（Traceability）在藥廠的意義

ISO 9001 要求「需要時」建立唯一識別和追溯。藥廠 GMP 則是強制要求完整的批次追溯：

  • 批號系統（Batch/Lot Number）
  • 原料追溯到 CoA 和供應商
  • 生產設備、人員、環境數據
  • 發生召回時能快速追溯所有影響批次（Batch Record Review）

21 CFR Part 211 要求批次記錄保存至效期後 1 年，或至少 3 年——遠超 ISO 9001 的最低要求。

8.5.5 交貨後活動——藥廠的藥物警戒對應

ISO 9001 要求考量「潛在不良後果、使用壽命、法規要求」。藥廠對應項目：

  • 藥物警戒（Pharmacovigilance）：上市後不良反應監測
  • Field Safety Corrective Action（FSCA）：召回與安全通知
  • 穩定性計畫（Ongoing Stability）：確認效期內品質
  • 年度產品品質審查（APR/PQR）：EU GMP Ch.1 要求

8.5.6 變更控制——藥廠的 Change Control 系統

ISO 9001 要求記錄變更審查結果、授權人及後續行動。藥廠 GMP 更進一步：

  • 分類（微小/重大/重要 change）
  • 風險評估
  • 跨部門審查（QA、生產、法規、工程）
  • 法規申報評估（是否需 Variation/PAS/CBE）
  • 有效性確認（Effectiveness Check）
8.6–7

Release of Products & Control of Nonconforming Outputs — 放行與不合格品管控

Clauses 8.6–8.7 — Product release criteria, four disposal actions, documented information requirements

8.6 Release of Products and Services

The organization shall implement planned arrangements, at appropriate stages, to verify that the product and service requirements have been met. The release of products and services shall not proceed until the planned arrangements have been satisfactorily completed, unless otherwise approved by a relevant authority and, as applicable, by the customer.

The organization shall retain documented information on the release of products and services, including:

  1. evidence of conformity with the acceptance criteria;
  2. traceability to the person(s) authorizing the release.

8.7.1 Control of Nonconforming Outputs

The organization shall ensure that outputs that do not conform to their requirements are identified and controlled to prevent their unintended use or delivery. The organization shall deal with nonconforming outputs in one or more of the following ways:

  1. correction;
  2. segregation, containment, return or suspension of provision of products and services;
  3. informing the customer;
  4. obtaining authorization for acceptance under concession.

Conformity to the requirements shall be verified when nonconforming outputs are corrected.

8.7.2 Documented Information

The organization shall retain documented information that:

  1. describes the nonconformity;
  2. describes the actions taken;
  3. describes any concessions obtained;
  4. identifies the authority deciding the action.

解說：放行管控與不合格品——藥廠的嚴格要求

8.6 產品放行：QP（Qualified Person）的責任

ISO 9001 要求放行前通過計畫性安排，並記錄授權人。藥廠有更嚴格的制度：

  • EU GMP：每批次必須由 QP（授權人）簽署放行，具法律責任
  • FDA：Quality Unit 負責批次審查和放行（21 CFR 211.192）
  • 放行前要求：完整批次記錄審查、偏差評估、OOS 調查結果、穩定性數據

ISO 9001 說「授權人追溯性」——藥廠是 QP 對政府當局的個人法律責任，層級完全不同。

8.7.1 不合格品四種處置方式

ISO 9001 處置藥廠對應
Correction（修正）重加工（Rework）——需要經過額外確效
Segregation/Return/Suspension物理隔離，貼上「拒用」標籤，退回供應商
Informing the customerField Alert、Recall Notice、Customer Complaint
Concession（讓步接受）Technical Justification（技術評估）+ Deviation Report，需 QA 批准

藥廠的不合格品管理更嚴格

  • OOS（Out-of-Specification）：需按 FDA OOS 指引進行調查，不能簡單讓步接受
  • 重加工（Rework）：需要正式的 Rework Protocol，並重新通過完整的 QC 測試
  • 銷毀記錄：不合格品的銷毀需要雙人見證並記錄
  • 偏差系統：ISO 9001 僅要求文件記錄，藥廠要求偏差調查、根本原因分析（RCA）、CAPA
9.1

Monitoring, Measurement, Analysis and Evaluation — 監測、量測、分析與評估

Clauses 9.1.1–9.1.3 — What to measure, customer satisfaction, seven analysis outcomes

9.1.1 General

The organization shall determine:

  1. what needs to be monitored and measured;
  2. the methods for monitoring, measurement, analysis and evaluation needed to ensure valid results;
  3. when the monitoring and measuring shall be performed;
  4. when the results shall be analysed and evaluated.

The organization shall evaluate the performance and effectiveness of the QMS. The organization shall retain appropriate documented information as evidence of the results.

9.1.2 Customer Satisfaction

The organization shall monitor customers' perceptions of the degree to which their needs and expectations have been fulfilled. The organization shall determine the methods for obtaining, monitoring and reviewing this information.

NOTE: Examples of monitoring customer perceptions can include customer surveys, customer feedback on delivered products and services, meetings with customers, market-share analysis, compliments, warranty claims and dealer reports.

9.1.3 Analysis and Evaluation

The organization shall analyse and evaluate appropriate data and information. The results of analysis shall be used to evaluate:

  1. conformity of products and services;
  2. the degree of customer satisfaction;
  3. the performance and effectiveness of the QMS;
  4. if planning has been implemented effectively;
  5. the effectiveness of actions taken to address risks and opportunities;
  6. the performance of external providers;
  7. the need for improvements to the QMS.

解說：績效量測——從 KPI 到患者安全

9.1.1 量測什麼？何時量測？

ISO 9001 沒有規定具體量測什麼，只要求組織自行決定。藥廠的典型量測矩陣：

量測面向典型指標
產品品質批次合格率、OOS 率、偏差數
生產效率OEE、Right First Time（RFT）率
供應商CoA 合格率、準時交貨率
客戶滿意投訴數、NPS、定期回饋問卷
法規合規稽核發現數、CAPA 關閉率

類比：飛機的儀表板

飛行員不是靠感覺飛，是靠儀表板：高度計、速度計、燃油量。ISO 9001 的 9.1 就是要求組織建立自己的「品質儀表板」——確定哪些指標是飛行安全的關鍵，並定時讀取和評估。

9.1.2 CDMO 的「客戶滿意度」：誰是真正的客戶？

這是藥廠 CDMO 的獨特挑戰：

  • 直接客戶：委託方（製藥公司）——他們對交貨期、品質、溝通的滿意度
  • 終端客戶（Ultimate Customer）：患者——他們的安全性和療效

患者不會直接給 CDMO 填問卷，但患者的安全就是 CDMO 品質工作的最終目的。ICH Q10 提倡的Patient-centric quality culture（以患者為中心的品質文化）正是這個理念。

監測方法：客戶投訴分析、藥物警戒訊號、市場回收數據——這些間接反映了對患者的影響。

9.1.3 的七個分析輸出——整合到 APR/PQR

藥廠的年度產品品質審查（Annual Product Review / Product Quality Review）正是 9.1.3 的實踐：整合全年產品、製程、供應商、偏差、CAPA、穩定性數據，評估 QMS 有效性，識別改善機會。

9.2

Internal Audit — 內部稽核

Clauses 9.2.1–9.2.2 — Audit programme, objectivity, reporting, corrective action, documented information

9.2.1 Internal Audit Requirements

The organization shall conduct internal audits at planned intervals to provide information on whether the QMS:

  1. conforms to:
    1. the organization's own requirements for its QMS;
    2. the requirements of this International Standard;
  2. is effectively implemented and maintained.

9.2.2 Audit Programme

The organization shall:

  1. plan, establish, implement and maintain an audit programme including the frequency, methods, responsibilities, planning requirements and reporting, taking into consideration the importance of the processes, changes affecting the organization, and the results of previous audits;
  2. define the audit criteria and scope for each audit;
  3. select auditors and conduct audits to ensure objectivity and impartiality;
  4. ensure that the results are reported to relevant management;
  5. take appropriate correction and corrective actions without undue delay;
  6. retain documented information as evidence of the audit programme and audit results.

NOTE: See ISO 19011 for guidance on auditing management systems.

解說：內部稽核——查廠準備的日常訓練

稽核計畫的風險導向設計

9.2.2a 要求稽核頻率考量「製程重要性、組織變更、前次稽核結果」——這是風險導向稽核計畫（Risk-based audit programme）的基本邏輯。

藥廠實務：無菌灌裝、滅菌、OSD 壓錠等高風險製程的稽核頻率高於倉儲、行政部門。新引入製程或設備變更後應安排額外稽核。

客觀性與公正性（Objectivity and Impartiality）

ISO 9001 要求稽核員對被稽核區域保持獨立性——稽核員不應稽核自己負責的工作。

藥廠實務：通常採用交叉稽核（Cross-audit）——A 線人員稽核 B 線，或由 QA 部門主導，生產人員參與技術面稽核。外部稽核（Third-party audit）提供更高的客觀性。

類比：飛機的定期維護檢查

航空公司不等到飛機出問題才檢查，而是按照飛行小時定期進行 A-check、C-check、D-check。內部稽核就是 QMS 的「定期維護」——不是在出問題後才亡羊補牢，而是系統性地找出問題苗頭。

內部稽核 = 法規查廠的日常訓練

對藥廠而言，內部稽核有雙重價值：

  • QMS 維護：找出合規缺口、識別改善機會
  • 查廠準備（Inspection Readiness）：以 FDA/EMA/衛福部查廠的標準來稽核自己，讓員工熟悉查廠情境，找出文件缺口

優秀的藥廠 QA 部門會把內部稽核報告當作模擬查廠觀察，針對性地在真正查廠前解決問題。

9.3

Management Review — 管理審查

Clauses 9.3.1–9.3.3 — General, eight input categories, three output decisions

9.3.1 General

Top management shall review the organization's QMS, at planned intervals, to ensure its continuing suitability, adequacy, effectiveness and alignment with the strategic direction of the organization.

9.3.2 Management Review Inputs

The management review shall be planned and carried out taking into consideration:

  1. the status of actions from previous management reviews;
  2. changes in external and internal issues relevant to the QMS;
  3. information on the performance and effectiveness of the QMS, including trends in:
    1. customer satisfaction and feedback from relevant interested parties;
    2. the extent to which quality objectives have been met;
    3. process performance and conformity of products and services;
    4. nonconformities and corrective actions;
    5. monitoring and measurement results;
    6. audit results;
    7. the performance of external providers;
  4. the adequacy of resources;
  5. the effectiveness of actions taken to address risks and opportunities;
  6. opportunities for improvement.

9.3.3 Management Review Outputs

The outputs shall include decisions and actions related to:

  1. opportunities for improvement;
  2. any need for changes to the QMS;
  3. resource needs.

The organization shall retain documented information as evidence of the results of management reviews.

解說：管理審查——高層責任的具體體現

管理審查的三個關鍵字

  • Suitability（適切性）：QMS 是否仍適合組織的目的與背景？（隨著業務規模變化）
  • Adequacy（充分性）：QMS 的資源和架構是否足夠？（人員、設備、系統）
  • Effectiveness（有效性）：QMS 是否真的在運作並達到結果？（不只是有文件，而是有效果）

類比：年度董事會報告

管理審查就像公司的年度董事會：高層主管不看日常細節，而是看整體趨勢——去年達成了哪些目標、有什麼重大問題、明年需要投入什麼資源。9.3.2 的輸入清單就是「品質版董事會議程」。

「前次管理審查的後續行動狀態」——追蹤閉環

9.3.2a 要求審查前次決議的執行狀態，這是品質管理的「閉環」原則：做出決定（輸出）→ 執行 → 追蹤結果 → 下次管理審查確認關閉。

沒有這一步，管理審查就會淪為「每年開一次的例行報告會」，決議從未真正執行。

藥廠的管理審查輸入矩陣（GMP 強化版）

10.1

Improvement: General — 改善：一般要求

Clause 10.1 — Three categories of improvement opportunities

10.1 General

The organization shall determine and select opportunities for improvement and implement any necessary actions to meet customer requirements and enhance customer satisfaction.

These shall include:

  1. improving products and services to meet requirements as well as to address future needs and expectations;
  2. correcting, preventing or reducing undesired effects;
  3. improving the performance and effectiveness of the QMS.

NOTE: Examples of improvement can include correction, corrective action, continual improvement, breakthrough change, innovation and re-organization.

解說：改善的全光譜

改善的三個層次

  • 產品/服務改善：提升配方、工藝、包裝——通常對應技術開發項目
  • 問題預防與糾正：這是 10.2 CAPA 的核心
  • QMS 效能提升：流程優化、數位化、培訓計畫改善

NOTE 提到的改善光譜

從漸進到突破：矯正（Correction）→ 矯正措施（Corrective Action）→ 持續改善（Continual improvement）→ 突破性變革（Breakthrough change）→ 創新（Innovation）→ 組織重整（Re-organization）

藥廠的改善活動通常聚焦在前三項，「突破性變革」需要監管申報。

10.2

Nonconformity and Corrective Action — 不符合事項與矯正措施

Clauses 10.2.1–10.2.2 — Six-step CAPA process, documented information requirements

10.2.1 Nonconformity and Corrective Action

When a nonconformity occurs, including any arising from complaints, the organization shall:

  1. react to the nonconformity and, as applicable:
    1. take action to control and correct it;
    2. deal with the consequences;
  2. evaluate the need for action to eliminate the cause(s) of the nonconformity, in order that it does not recur or occur elsewhere, by:
    1. reviewing and analysing the nonconformity;
    2. determining the causes of the nonconformity;
    3. determining if similar nonconformities exist, or could potentially occur;
  3. implement any action needed;
  4. review the effectiveness of any corrective action taken;
  5. update risks and opportunities determined during planning, if necessary;
  6. make changes to the QMS, if necessary.

Corrective actions shall be appropriate to the effects of the nonconformities encountered.

10.2.2 Documented Information

The organization shall retain documented information as evidence of:

  1. the nature of the nonconformities and any subsequent actions taken;
  2. the results of any corrective action.

解說：CAPA——FDA/EMA 查廠最關鍵的系統

Correction vs. Corrective Action——最重要的區別

概念定義目的例子
Correction（矯正） 對已發生的不合格品或情況的即時處置 消除問題本身 隔離不合格批次、重測 OOS 樣品、修復設備故障
Corrective Action（矯正措施） 消除不合格的根本原因，防止再發 消除問題的根源 修改 SOP、增加培訓、改善設備設計、修正製程參數

ISO 9001 的 10.2.1a 是 Correction，10.2.1b-f 是 Corrective Action。兩者都需要，缺一不可。

類比：醫生看病

Correction：開退燒藥，讓體溫下降（處理症狀）。
Corrective Action：抽血找感染源、開抗生素根治感染（消除根因）。

只退燒不找病因，下週又會燒。只 Correction 不做 Corrective Action，下次同樣問題還是會發生。

CAPA 在 FDA/EMA 查廠中的核心地位

對藥廠而言，CAPA 系統是最受監管機構關注的系統之一：

  • FDA Warning Letter 常見缺失：CAPA 根本原因調查不足、有效性確認未執行、CAPA 逾期未關閉
  • EMA GMP 查廠重點：CAPA 是否針對反覆發生的偏差？高層管理是否接收 CAPA 趨勢報告？
  • ICH Q10 要求：CAPA 系統是製藥品質系統的四大要素之一

10.2.1 的六步驟對應藥廠 CAPA 流程

  1. 反應（10.2.1a）：即時控制——隔離、停線、通知客戶
  2. 評估根因（10.2.1b）：根本原因分析（RCA）——5 Whys、Fishbone、FMEA
  3. 實施行動（10.2.1c）：執行 CAPA——修改 SOP、培訓、設備改善
  4. 確認有效性（10.2.1d）：Effectiveness Check——30/60/90天後確認問題未再發
  5. 更新風險評估（10.2.1e）：回頭修訂 FMEA 或風險登記冊
  6. 更新 QMS（10.2.1f）：若需修改文件系統或程序
10.3

Continual Improvement — 持續改善

Clause 10.3 — Suitability, adequacy, effectiveness; feeds from analysis and management review

10.3 Continual Improvement

The organization shall continually improve the suitability, adequacy and effectiveness of the quality management system.

The organization shall consider the results of analysis and evaluation, and the outputs from management review, to determine if there are needs or opportunities that shall be addressed as part of continual improvement.

解說：持續改善——ISO 9001 的精神核心

「持續」的含義——不只是「改善過一次」

Continual improvement 中的 "continual"（持續的、循環進行的）而非 "continuous"（不間斷的），意思是改善是一個反覆循環的過程，而非一次性活動。

驅動來源：9.1.3 分析評估結果（數據驅動）+ 9.3.3 管理審查輸出（高層決策）→ 識別改善機會 → 實施改善 → 再次評估。

類比：PDCA 螺旋

持續改善不是走一個圈回到原點，而是像螺旋梯——每轉一圈都比上一圈高一層。每個 PDCA 循環結束後，標準提升了，下一個循環從更高的起點開始。

藥廠的持續改善機制

  • APR/PQR（年度產品品質審查）：系統性識別產品和製程改善機會
  • Lean / Six Sigma：製程效率改善（減少浪費、降低變異）
  • 知識管理（ICH Q10）：累積製程理解，持續縮小設計空間的不確定性
  • Benchmarking：對標行業最佳實踐，設定改善目標

Key Takeaways — 核心要點總結

Clauses 8–10 in context: the operational heart of ISO 9001

Structure Summary: Clauses 8–10

Clause 8 (Operation) is the largest and most operationally detailed clause — it covers the complete production value chain from customer requirements through to nonconforming output control. Key themes:

  • 8.3: Review / Verification / Validation are distinct — each has a unique purpose and cannot substitute for each other
  • 8.4: Supplier control must be risk-proportionate — the higher the impact, the deeper the control
  • 8.5.1f: "Special processes" (where output cannot be verified by subsequent monitoring) must be validated — this is the theoretical basis for sterilization validation and media fill in pharma
  • 8.7: Four disposal actions for nonconforming outputs — correction, segregation/suspension, informing customer, concession

Clause 9 (Performance Evaluation) closes the loop: measure → analyze → audit → management review. All feeds into the improvement cycle.

Clause 10 (Improvement): The critical distinction is Correction (fix the symptom immediately) vs. Corrective Action (eliminate the root cause permanently). Both are needed. CAPA effectiveness verification (10.2.1d) is what separates a real CAPA system from paperwork.

藥廠人員的核心學習點

ISO 9001 是地板，GMP 是天花板

對製藥從業者而言，ISO 9001 的要求在多數情況下是你們已經在做的事的最低版本。理解 ISO 9001 的邏輯框架，有助於：

  • 向非藥廠人員解釋你們的品質系統
  • 評估新供應商的品質成熟度（他們可能只有 ISO 9001 認證）
  • 準備 ISO 9001 審查，理解 GMP 的額外要求從何而來

三個「必記」的關鍵概念

  1. Review ≠ Verification ≠ Validation：三個不同目的，不能互相取代（8.3.4）
  2. Correction ≠ Corrective Action：一個消除症狀，一個消除根因（10.2.1）
  3. 特殊製程需要確效：因為無法靠後續量測驗證——滅菌、無菌灌裝的法源（8.5.1f）

給 CDMO Operations Director 的視角

Clauses 8–10 描述的整個循環——計畫執行 → 評估績效 → 持續改善——正是營運卓越（Operational Excellence）的 DNA。ISO 9001 提供了框架，GMP 填入了藥廠的具體要求。作為 COO 軌道的領導者，要把這個框架從「合規工具」提升到「競爭優勢」：一個運作良好的 QMS 不只是通過查廠，而是真正減少偏差、提升 OEE、縮短放行週期——這些都直接影響 CDMO 的競爭力和客戶信心。

A.1

Structure and Terminology — 結構與術語

Annex A (Informative) — Clarification of new structure, terminology and concepts

A.1 Structure and Terminology

The clause structure (i.e. clause sequence) and some of the terminology of this edition of this International Standard, in comparison with the previous edition (ISO 9001:2008), have been changed to improve alignment with other management systems standards.

There is no requirement in this International Standard for its structure and terminology to be applied to the documented information of an organization's quality management system.

The structure of clauses is intended to provide a coherent presentation of requirements, rather than a model for documenting an organization's policies, objectives and processes.

There is no requirement for the terms used by an organization to be replaced by the terms used in this International Standard to specify quality management system requirements. Organizations can choose to use terms which suit their operations (e.g. using "records", "documentation" or "protocols" rather than "documented information"; or "supplier", "partner" or "vendor" rather than "external provider").

解讀：術語變革的意義

為什麼 ISO 9001 要改術語？

2015 版最大的幕後動機是「高階結構（High Level Structure, HLS）」的採用。ISO 決策：所有管理系統標準（QMS、EMS、OHSMS 等）共用相同的條款骨架，讓企業可以輕鬆整合多個管理系統。術語統一是第一步。

比喻：電源插頭的標準化

以前各國插頭規格不同，每次出國要帶轉接頭。HLS 就是 ISO 管理系統的「通用插頭」——不管你同時跑 ISO 9001（品質）、ISO 14001（環境）還是 ISO 45001（安全），條款編號和術語對齊，讓你只需維護一套系統。

製藥業實務：術語映射

ISO 9001:2015 明確指出，組織不必把內部文件裡的「SOP」改叫「documented information」，也不必把「supplier」改叫「external provider」。這對已建立龐大文件系統的製藥廠是一大解脫。

實務做法：在 QMS 手冊的術語對照表中說明公司用語與 ISO 2015 用語的對應即可。審計員接受這種做法。

「Quality Manual 不再必要」的影響

2008 版要求必須有品質手冊（Quality Manual）。2015 版取消此要求，改為彈性的「documented information」。但在 GMP 管制產業（製藥、醫療器材），監管機構（FDA、EMA）通常仍期望看到某種形式的品質手冊或等效文件作為系統概覽。

A.2

Products and Services — 產品與服務

Why "services" is now explicitly included alongside "products"

A.2 Products and Services

ISO 9001:2008 used the term "product" to include all output categories. This edition uses "products and services". "Products and services" include all output categories (hardware, services, software and processed materials).

The specific inclusion of "services" is intended to highlight the differences between products and services in the application of some requirements. The characteristic of services is that at least part of the output is realized at the interface with the customer. This means, for example, that conformity to requirements cannot necessarily be confirmed before service delivery.

In most cases, products and services are used together. Most outputs that organizations provide to customers, or are supplied to them by external providers, include both products and services. For example, a tangible or intangible product can have some associated service or a service can have some associated tangible or intangible product.

解讀：服務被明確納入的含義

核心差異：服務的「現場性」

製造業生產完成品，可以在交付前進行檢驗確認。服務業（或服務成分）的特點是：至少有部分輸出是在與客戶互動的當下才完成的，無法事先完全檢驗。

例如：CRO（合約研究組織）提供臨床試驗管理服務——你無法在「交付前」完整驗證所有服務品質，因為服務本身就是過程。

製藥 CDMO 的視角

你的公司（CDMO）同時提供「產品」（製成的藥品）和「服務」（製程開發、技術移轉、法規支援）。ISO 9001:2015 的框架更清楚地涵蓋這兩種性質，幫助 CDMO 建立更完整的 QMS。

對製藥廠的實際影響

  • 委外測試服務（外部實驗室）= 服務採購，納入 8.4 管控
  • 委外滅菌（EO 滅菌廠）= 服務 + 製程外包，需供應商審核
  • CDMO 本身的製造服務 = 品質協議（Quality Agreement）是關鍵文件
A.3

Understanding Needs & Expectations of Interested Parties — 理解相關方需求

Scope limitation — not all interested parties require QMS coverage

A.3 Understanding the Needs and Expectations of Interested Parties

Subclause 4.2 specifies requirements for the organization to determine the interested parties that are relevant to the quality management system and the requirements of those interested parties.

However, 4.2 does not imply extension of quality management system requirements beyond the scope of this International Standard. As stated in the scope, this International Standard is applicable where an organization needs to demonstrate its ability to consistently provide products and services that meet customer and applicable statutory and regulatory requirements, and aims to enhance customer satisfaction.

There is no requirement in this International Standard for the organization to consider interested parties where it has decided that those parties are not relevant to its quality management system. It is for the organization to decide if a particular requirement of a relevant interested party is relevant to its quality management system.

解讀：相關方的範圍界定

什麼是「相關方（Interested Parties）」？

對組織有影響、或受組織影響的個人或群體。在製藥 CDMO 中，典型的相關方包括：

  • 客戶（品牌藥廠、仿製藥廠）
  • 監管機構（FDA、EMA、PMDA、TFDA）
  • 員工
  • 原料供應商
  • 股東 / 投資人
  • 社區（環境影響、噪音等）

重要限制：不是所有相關方都需納入 QMS

標準明確指出，組織自行判斷哪些相關方與 QMS 相關。例如：社區居民對噪音的關切，可能屬於 EMS（環境管理）範疇，而非 QMS 的範疇。不要過度擴張 QMS 的邊界，否則系統會變得臃腫且難以維護。

實務原則

識別相關方後，只需將「對產品/服務品質有直接影響，或對 QMS 有法規/合約要求」的需求納入管控。投資人關心的是財務回報，這不是 QMS 要處理的事——但監管機構的 cGMP 要求，絕對要納入。

A.4

Risk-Based Thinking — 風險思維

The most significant philosophical shift in ISO 9001:2015 — replaces the preventive action clause

A.4 Risk-Based Thinking

The concept of risk-based thinking has been implicit in previous editions of this International Standard, e.g. through requirements for planning, review and improvement. This International Standard specifies requirements for the organization to understand its context (see 4.1) and determine risks as a basis for planning (see 6.1). This represents the application of risk-based thinking to planning and implementing quality management system processes (see 4.4).

One of the key purposes of a quality management system is to act as a preventive tool. Consequently, this International Standard does not have a separate clause or subclause on preventive action. The concept of preventive action is expressed through the use of risk-based thinking in formulating quality management system requirements.

The risk-based thinking applied in this International Standard has enabled some reduction in prescriptive requirements and their replacement by performance-based requirements. There is greater flexibility than in ISO 9001:2008 in the requirements for processes, documented information and organizational responsibilities.

Although 6.1 specifies that the organization shall plan actions to address risks, there is no requirement for formal methods for risk management or a documented risk management process. Organizations can decide whether or not to develop a more extensive risk management methodology than is required by this International Standard, e.g. through the application of other guidance or standards.

Not all the processes of a quality management system represent the same level of risk in terms of the organization's ability to meet its objectives, and the effects of uncertainty are not the same for all organizations. Under the requirements of 6.1, the organization is responsible for its application of risk-based thinking and the actions it takes to address risk, including whether or not to retain documented information as evidence of its determination of risks.

解讀：最關鍵的哲學轉變

核心轉變：從「有沒有程序」到「有沒有思考過風險」

ISO 9001:2008 有一個獨立的「預防措施（Preventive Action）」條款（8.5.3），要求組織建立預防措施程序。2015 版取消了這個條款，改為要求風險思維貫穿整個 QMS。

這意味著：不再是「我們有一份預防措施 SOP」就過關，而是「我們在規劃、執行每個流程時，都有系統地考慮過風險」。

ISO 9001 vs. ICH Q9 / ISO 14971 的關鍵區別

標準 風險管理要求程度 適用場景
ISO 9001:2015 不要求正式風險管理方法論，無需文件化的風險管理流程 一般商業 QMS
ICH Q9 建立完整的品質風險管理（QRM）框架，鼓勵使用 FMEA、HACCP 等工具 製藥 GMP
ISO 14971 強制要求系統性風險管理流程，含風險評估、控制、殘餘風險評估、全生命週期監控 醫療器材

結論：ISO 9001 的「風險思維」是最低門檻，製藥廠實務上需依循 ICH Q9 執行更嚴格的 QRM。

比喻：開車的風險意識 vs. 賽車的風險管理

ISO 9001 要求的是「開車時有基本的風險意識」——看到彎道減速、雨天小心。ICH Q9 要求的是「賽車手等級的系統性風險評估」——每個彎道的曲率、輪胎磨耗率、剎車距離都要量化分析。

製藥廠同時承擔兩套要求：ISO 9001 是底線，ICH Q9 是實際要做到的高度。

在 CDMO 填充生產的實際應用

  • 計畫階段：評估新客戶產品的複雜度風險（毒性、低劑量、高活性）→ 決定設施需求
  • 製程設計：FMEA 評估無菌填充關鍵步驟（依 ICH Q9）
  • 供應商管理：依風險等級分類供應商，決定審核頻率
  • 文件化：ISO 9001 不要求你把所有風險評估都保留記錄，但 FDA 的 cGMP 通常會期望看到
A.5

Applicability — 適用性

"Exclusions" is gone — replaced by scope-based applicability determination

A.5 Applicability

This International Standard does not refer to "exclusions" in relation to the applicability of its requirements to the organization's quality management system. However, an organization can review the applicability of requirements due to the size or complexity of the organization, the management model it adopts, the range of the organization's activities and the nature of the risks and opportunities it encounters.

The requirements for applicability are addressed in 4.3, which defines conditions under which an organization can decide that a requirement cannot be applied to any of the processes within the scope of its quality management system. The organization can only decide that a requirement is not applicable if its decision will not result in failure to achieve conformity of products and services.

Only Clause 8 requirements can be determined not applicable when they do not apply to the organization's operations.

解讀：「排除」條款的廢除

舊版「排除（Exclusions）」的問題

ISO 9001:2008 允許組織宣告某些 Clause 7（Product Realization）條款「不適用」，並在品質手冊中說明理由。這在實務上被許多組織濫用——把不方便執行的條款都「排除」掉。

新版的邏輯：只有 Clause 8 可以不適用

2015 版改為：只有當某個 Clause 8 的要求，在你的業務範圍內根本不存在對應活動時，才能宣告不適用。條件：不適用的決定不能導致無法達到產品/服務的符合性。

例如：一家純軟體公司，若沒有任何實體「設計與開發輸出」需要物理驗證，可宣告 8.3 的部分子條款不適用。但製藥廠幾乎不可能合理排除任何 Clause 8 要求。

製藥廠典型可排除 / 不可排除情境

條款 一般製藥廠能排除？ 理由

A.6–8

Documented Information / Organizational Knowledge / External Provision

文件化資訊 · 組織知識 · 外部供應管控

A.6 Documented Information

As part of the alignment with other management system standards, a common clause on "documented information" has been adopted without significant change or addition (see 7.5).

Where ISO 9001:2008 used specific terminology such as "document" or "documented procedures", "quality manual" or "quality plan", this edition defines requirements to "maintain documented information".

Where ISO 9001:2008 used the term "records" to denote documents needed to provide evidence of conformity with requirements, this is now expressed as a requirement to "retain documented information". The organization is responsible for determining what documented information needs to be retained, the period of time for which it is to be retained and the media to be used for its retention.

A requirement to "maintain" documented information does not exclude the possibility that the organization might also need to "retain" that same documented information for a particular purpose, e.g. to retain previous versions of it.

Where this International Standard refers to "information" rather than "documented information" (e.g. in 4.1), there is no requirement that this information is to be documented.

A.7 Organizational Knowledge

In 7.1.6, this International Standard addresses the need to determine and manage the knowledge maintained by the organization, to ensure the operation of its processes and that it can achieve conformity of products and services.

Requirements regarding organizational knowledge were introduced for the purpose of:

  1. Safeguarding the organization from loss of knowledge, e.g.:
    • through staff turnover
    • failure to capture and share information
  2. Encouraging the organization to acquire knowledge, e.g.:
    • learning from experience
    • mentoring
    • benchmarking

A.8 Control of Externally Provided Processes, Products and Services

All forms of externally provided processes, products and services are addressed in 8.4, e.g. whether through:

  1. purchasing from a supplier;
  2. an arrangement with an associate company;
  3. outsourcing processes to an external provider.

Outsourcing always has the essential characteristic of a service, since it will have at least one activity necessarily performed at the interface between the provider and the organization.

The controls required for external provision can vary widely depending on the nature of the processes, products and services. The organization can apply risk-based thinking to determine the type and extent of controls appropriate to particular external providers and externally provided processes, products and services.

解讀：三個新要求的製藥意涵

A.6：「維持」vs.「保留」文件化資訊

這是 2015 版最容易混淆的術語對。理解方式：

  • Maintain（維持）：持續有效的、活的文件——如 SOP、品質手冊、過程描述。對應舊版的「文件（Documents）」。
  • Retain（保留）：已完成的、作為證據的記錄——如批次記錄、測試報告、稽核記錄。對應舊版的「記錄（Records）」。

一份文件可以同時要求「維持」（保持當前版本有效）和「保留」（歸檔舊版本）。

A.7：組織知識——製藥廠的致命弱點

組織知識（Organizational Knowledge）是 2015 版全新的要求（無對應舊條款）。對製藥 CDMO 尤其關鍵：

  • 資深工程師、QA 退休或離職 → 關鍵製程知識流失
  • 設備供應商停止支援 → 維護知識斷鏈
  • 客戶產品技術移轉 → 知識轉移需系統化記錄

實務工具：製程知識庫、技術移轉文件包（Technology Transfer Package）、交接 SOP、師徒制度、Lessons Learned 資料庫。

A.8：外部供應的風險分級管控

ISO 9001:2015 明確要求以風險思維決定對外部供應商的管控強度，而非一刀切。典型的分級方式：

外部供應類型 風險等級 典型管控措施
API 原料藥供應商 高 稽核、品質協議、CoA 驗收、定期再評估
輔料供應商 中 問卷評估、CoA 驗收
辦公室文具採購 低 正常採購流程
委外滅菌服務 高 現場稽核、品質協議、製程驗證確認

三個 A 條款的共同主題

A.6（文件彈性化）、A.7（知識管理）、A.8（供應風險管控）都體現了 2015 版的核心精神：減少強制規定，增加組織自主判斷的空間——但要求你有能力說明判斷的依據。這對成熟的製藥 QMS 是鬆綁，對薄弱的 QMS 卻是更大的挑戰。

解讀：ISO/TC 176 標準生態圈

ISO/TC 176 是誰？

ISO Technical Committee 176（品質管理與品質保證技術委員會），負責制訂所有 ISO 9000 系列及相關配套標準。本附錄列出的所有標準都是同一個委員會的產品，相互一致、互相補強。

製藥廠最常用的配套標準

  • ISO 10012 — 量測管理系統：與 21 CFR Part 11 校正要求、計量可追溯性高度相關。量測設備管控是製藥 QMS 的核心，這份標準提供了實作指引。
  • ISO/TR 10013 — QMS 文件化指引：協助設計 SOP 體系、文件架構，對文件管理系統建置很有幫助。
  • ISO 10015 — 培訓指引：GMP 培訓體系設計、培訓有效性評估，對應 ISO 9001 Clause 7.2（能力）。
  • ISO 19011 — 稽核管理：內部稽核計畫設計、稽核員能力評估，是 ISO 9001 Clause 9.2 的最佳實踐指引。

比喻：ISO 9001 是作業系統，配套標準是 App

ISO 9001 定義了 QMS 的「作業系統」——它說你需要「管理量測設備」（7.1.5），但不告訴你怎麼設計校正程序。ISO 10012 就是那個「App」，告訴你量測管理系統的具體做法。根據你的業務需求，選擇性安裝對你有用的 App 即可。

ISO 15378（初級包材 GMP 品質管理系統）是以 ISO 9001 為基礎、加上 GMP 層疊加的專業標準。了解三者的定位差異，有助於製藥 CDMO 判斷：在哪些場景使用哪個標準框架，以及多標準整合的策略。

ISO 13485 vs. ISO 9001：為什麼沒有以 2015 版為基礎？

ISO 13485:2016 仍以 ISO 9001:2008 為框架基礎，而非 2015 版。原因：醫療器材產業的監管機構（FDA、BSI 等）認為 ISO 9001:2015 的彈性化方向（移除強制性持續改善、取消品質手冊要求）對高風險的醫療器材行業不合適。因此 ISO 13485 維持更嚴格的強制要求，並可能在未來版本才考慮融合 HLS 架構。

三標準整合策略

對於同時涉及多個領域的製造商（如：既做初級包材也供應醫療器材），整合三個標準的關鍵是：

  • 以 ISO 9001 的 HLS 架構為骨幹
  • 在適用範圍內疊加 ISO 13485 的強制要求
  • 在包材業務範圍內疊加 ISO 15378 的 GMP 層
  • 避免為不同客戶群維護三套獨立文件體系——盡量整合

ISO 9001:2015 Annex A & B 核心要點回顧

Key Takeaways — Annex A

  • A.1 術語變革：不強制組織改變內部術語，但需理解術語映射（尤其 documented information 的 maintain vs. retain 區別）
  • A.2 產品與服務：服務的「現場性」要求前置品質管控，無法事後完全檢驗
  • A.3 相關方：組織自主判斷哪些相關方與 QMS 相關，不需無限擴張範圍
  • A.4 風險思維：取代獨立的預防措施條款；ISO 9001 不要求正式風險管理方法論（與 ICH Q9 / ISO 14971 形成重要對比）
  • A.5 適用性：「排除」一詞廢除；只有 Clause 8 可宣告不適用，且不能影響產品符合性
  • A.6 文件化資訊：maintain（維持活文件）vs. retain（保留記錄）是核心概念
  • A.7 組織知識：2015 版新增要求，保護關鍵製程知識不因人員流失而消失
  • A.8 外部供應管控：以風險為基礎決定管控強度，涵蓋採購、合作、外包三種形式

Annex B 核心訊息

  • ISO/TC 176 維護一個完整的支援標準生態系，ISO 9001 是核心，其他標準提供具體實踐指引

三標準比較的核心結論

  • ISO 9001：彈性、風險思維驅動、適合任何組織的通用框架
  • ISO 13485：更嚴格、持續改善非強制、ISO 14971 風險管理為強制要求、醫療器材導向
  • ISO 15378：ISO 9001 + GMP 疊加、填充用初級包材專用、製藥客戶最直接相關

最後的思維框架

ISO 9001 的 Annex A 最核心的訊息是：2015 版從「合規導向」轉向「績效導向」。不再問「你有沒有這個程序？」，而是問「你有沒有識別風險並採取有效行動？」。這正是 ICH Q10 的精神——一個活的、有效的品質系統，而非一堆漂亮的文件。
