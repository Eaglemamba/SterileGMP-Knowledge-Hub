A.1

Structure and Terminology — 結構與術語

Annex A (Informative) — Clarification of new structure, terminology and concepts

A.1 Structure and Terminology

The clause structure (i.e. clause sequence) and some of the terminology of this edition of this International Standard, in comparison with the previous edition (ISO 9001:2008), have been changed to improve alignment with other management systems standards.

There is no requirement in this International Standard for its structure and terminology to be applied to the documented information of an organization's quality management system.

The structure of clauses is intended to provide a coherent presentation of requirements, rather than a model for documenting an organization's policies, objectives and processes.

There is no requirement for the terms used by an organization to be replaced by the terms used in this International Standard to specify quality management system requirements. Organizations can choose to use terms which suit their operations (e.g. using "records", "documentation" or "protocols" rather than "documented information"; or "supplier", "partner" or "vendor" rather than "external provider").

解讀：術語變革的意義

為什麼 ISO 9001 要改術語？

2015 版最大的幕後動機是「高階結構（High Level Structure, HLS）」的採用。ISO 決策：所有管理系統標準（QMS、EMS、OHSMS 等）共用相同的條款骨架，讓企業可以輕鬆整合多個管理系統。術語統一是第一步。

比喻：電源插頭的標準化

以前各國插頭規格不同，每次出國要帶轉接頭。HLS 就是 ISO 管理系統的「通用插頭」——不管你同時跑 ISO 9001（品質）、ISO 14001（環境）還是 ISO 45001（安全），條款編號和術語對齊，讓你只需維護一套系統。

製藥業實務：術語映射

ISO 9001:2015 明確指出，組織不必把內部文件裡的「SOP」改叫「documented information」，也不必把「supplier」改叫「external provider」。這對已建立龐大文件系統的製藥廠是一大解脫。

實務做法：在 QMS 手冊的術語對照表中說明公司用語與 ISO 2015 用語的對應即可。審計員接受這種做法。

「Quality Manual 不再必要」的影響

2008 版要求必須有品質手冊（Quality Manual）。2015 版取消此要求，改為彈性的「documented information」。但在 GMP 管制產業（製藥、醫療器材），監管機構（FDA、EMA）通常仍期望看到某種形式的品質手冊或等效文件作為系統概覽。

A.2

Products and Services — 產品與服務

Why "services" is now explicitly included alongside "products"

A.2 Products and Services

ISO 9001:2008 used the term "product" to include all output categories. This edition uses "products and services". "Products and services" include all output categories (hardware, services, software and processed materials).

The specific inclusion of "services" is intended to highlight the differences between products and services in the application of some requirements. The characteristic of services is that at least part of the output is realized at the interface with the customer. This means, for example, that conformity to requirements cannot necessarily be confirmed before service delivery.

In most cases, products and services are used together. Most outputs that organizations provide to customers, or are supplied to them by external providers, include both products and services. For example, a tangible or intangible product can have some associated service or a service can have some associated tangible or intangible product.

解讀：服務被明確納入的含義

核心差異：服務的「現場性」

製造業生產完成品，可以在交付前進行檢驗確認。服務業（或服務成分）的特點是：至少有部分輸出是在與客戶互動的當下才完成的，無法事先完全檢驗。

例如：CRO（合約研究組織）提供臨床試驗管理服務——你無法在「交付前」完整驗證所有服務品質，因為服務本身就是過程。

製藥 CDMO 的視角

你的公司（CDMO）同時提供「產品」（製成的藥品）和「服務」（製程開發、技術移轉、法規支援）。ISO 9001:2015 的框架更清楚地涵蓋這兩種性質，幫助 CDMO 建立更完整的 QMS。

對製藥廠的實際影響

  • 委外測試服務（外部實驗室）= 服務採購，納入 8.4 管控
  • 委外滅菌（EO 滅菌廠）= 服務 + 製程外包，需供應商審核
  • CDMO 本身的製造服務 = 品質協議（Quality Agreement）是關鍵文件
A.3

Understanding Needs & Expectations of Interested Parties — 理解相關方需求

Scope limitation — not all interested parties require QMS coverage

A.3 Understanding the Needs and Expectations of Interested Parties

Subclause 4.2 specifies requirements for the organization to determine the interested parties that are relevant to the quality management system and the requirements of those interested parties.

However, 4.2 does not imply extension of quality management system requirements beyond the scope of this International Standard. As stated in the scope, this International Standard is applicable where an organization needs to demonstrate its ability to consistently provide products and services that meet customer and applicable statutory and regulatory requirements, and aims to enhance customer satisfaction.

There is no requirement in this International Standard for the organization to consider interested parties where it has decided that those parties are not relevant to its quality management system. It is for the organization to decide if a particular requirement of a relevant interested party is relevant to its quality management system.

解讀：相關方的範圍界定

什麼是「相關方（Interested Parties）」？

對組織有影響、或受組織影響的個人或群體。在製藥 CDMO 中，典型的相關方包括：

  • 客戶（品牌藥廠、仿製藥廠）
  • 監管機構（FDA、EMA、PMDA、TFDA）
  • 員工
  • 原料供應商
  • 股東 / 投資人
  • 社區（環境影響、噪音等）

重要限制：不是所有相關方都需納入 QMS

標準明確指出，組織自行判斷哪些相關方與 QMS 相關。例如：社區居民對噪音的關切，可能屬於 EMS（環境管理）範疇，而非 QMS 的範疇。不要過度擴張 QMS 的邊界，否則系統會變得臃腫且難以維護。

實務原則

識別相關方後，只需將「對產品/服務品質有直接影響，或對 QMS 有法規/合約要求」的需求納入管控。投資人關心的是財務回報，這不是 QMS 要處理的事——但監管機構的 cGMP 要求，絕對要納入。

A.4

Risk-Based Thinking — 風險思維

The most significant philosophical shift in ISO 9001:2015 — replaces the preventive action clause

A.4 Risk-Based Thinking

The concept of risk-based thinking has been implicit in previous editions of this International Standard, e.g. through requirements for planning, review and improvement. This International Standard specifies requirements for the organization to understand its context (see 4.1) and determine risks as a basis for planning (see 6.1). This represents the application of risk-based thinking to planning and implementing quality management system processes (see 4.4).

One of the key purposes of a quality management system is to act as a preventive tool. Consequently, this International Standard does not have a separate clause or subclause on preventive action. The concept of preventive action is expressed through the use of risk-based thinking in formulating quality management system requirements.

The risk-based thinking applied in this International Standard has enabled some reduction in prescriptive requirements and their replacement by performance-based requirements. There is greater flexibility than in ISO 9001:2008 in the requirements for processes, documented information and organizational responsibilities.

Although 6.1 specifies that the organization shall plan actions to address risks, there is no requirement for formal methods for risk management or a documented risk management process. Organizations can decide whether or not to develop a more extensive risk management methodology than is required by this International Standard, e.g. through the application of other guidance or standards.

Not all the processes of a quality management system represent the same level of risk in terms of the organization's ability to meet its objectives, and the effects of uncertainty are not the same for all organizations. Under the requirements of 6.1, the organization is responsible for its application of risk-based thinking and the actions it takes to address risk, including whether or not to retain documented information as evidence of its determination of risks.

解讀：最關鍵的哲學轉變

核心轉變：從「有沒有程序」到「有沒有思考過風險」

ISO 9001:2008 有一個獨立的「預防措施（Preventive Action）」條款（8.5.3），要求組織建立預防措施程序。2015 版取消了這個條款，改為要求風險思維貫穿整個 QMS。

這意味著：不再是「我們有一份預防措施 SOP」就過關，而是「我們在規劃、執行每個流程時，都有系統地考慮過風險」。

比喻：開車的風險意識 vs. 賽車的風險管理

ISO 9001 要求的是「開車時有基本的風險意識」——看到彎道減速、雨天小心。ICH Q9 要求的是「賽車手等級的系統性風險評估」——每個彎道的曲率、輪胎磨耗率、剎車距離都要量化分析。

製藥廠同時承擔兩套要求：ISO 9001 是底線，ICH Q9 是實際要做到的高度。

在 CDMO 填充生產的實際應用

  • 計畫階段：評估新客戶產品的複雜度風險（毒性、低劑量、高活性）→ 決定設施需求
  • 製程設計：FMEA 評估無菌填充關鍵步驟（依 ICH Q9）
  • 供應商管理：依風險等級分類供應商，決定審核頻率
  • 文件化：ISO 9001 不要求你把所有風險評估都保留記錄，但 FDA 的 cGMP 通常會期望看到

ISO 9001 vs. ICH Q9 / ISO 14971 的關鍵區別

結論：ISO 9001 的「風險思維」是最低門檻，製藥廠實務上需依循 ICH Q9 執行更嚴格的 QRM。

A.5

Applicability — 適用性

"Exclusions" is gone — replaced by scope-based applicability determination

A.5 Applicability

This International Standard does not refer to "exclusions" in relation to the applicability of its requirements to the organization's quality management system. However, an organization can review the applicability of requirements due to the size or complexity of the organization, the management model it adopts, the range of the organization's activities and the nature of the risks and opportunities it encounters.

The requirements for applicability are addressed in 4.3, which defines conditions under which an organization can decide that a requirement cannot be applied to any of the processes within the scope of its quality management system. The organization can only decide that a requirement is not applicable if its decision will not result in failure to achieve conformity of products and services.

Only Clause 8 requirements can be determined not applicable when they do not apply to the organization's operations.

解讀：「排除」條款的廢除

舊版「排除（Exclusions）」的問題

ISO 9001:2008 允許組織宣告某些 Clause 7（Product Realization）條款「不適用」，並在品質手冊中說明理由。這在實務上被許多組織濫用——把不方便執行的條款都「排除」掉。

新版的邏輯：只有 Clause 8 可以不適用

2015 版改為：只有當某個 Clause 8 的要求，在你的業務範圍內根本不存在對應活動時，才能宣告不適用。條件：不適用的決定不能導致無法達到產品/服務的符合性。

例如：一家純軟體公司，若沒有任何實體「設計與開發輸出」需要物理驗證，可宣告 8.3 的部分子條款不適用。但製藥廠幾乎不可能合理排除任何 Clause 8 要求。

A.6–8

Documented Information / Organizational Knowledge / External Provision

文件化資訊 · 組織知識 · 外部供應管控

A.6 Documented Information

As part of the alignment with other management system standards, a common clause on "documented information" has been adopted without significant change or addition (see 7.5).

Where ISO 9001:2008 used specific terminology such as "document" or "documented procedures", "quality manual" or "quality plan", this edition defines requirements to "maintain documented information".

Where ISO 9001:2008 used the term "records" to denote documents needed to provide evidence of conformity with requirements, this is now expressed as a requirement to "retain documented information". The organization is responsible for determining what documented information needs to be retained, the period of time for which it is to be retained and the media to be used for its retention.

A requirement to "maintain" documented information does not exclude the possibility that the organization might also need to "retain" that same documented information for a particular purpose, e.g. to retain previous versions of it.

Where this International Standard refers to "information" rather than "documented information" (e.g. in 4.1), there is no requirement that this information is to be documented.

A.7 Organizational Knowledge

In 7.1.6, this International Standard addresses the need to determine and manage the knowledge maintained by the organization, to ensure the operation of its processes and that it can achieve conformity of products and services.

Requirements regarding organizational knowledge were introduced for the purpose of:

  1. Safeguarding the organization from loss of knowledge, e.g.:
    • through staff turnover
    • failure to capture and share information
  2. Encouraging the organization to acquire knowledge, e.g.:
    • learning from experience
    • mentoring
    • benchmarking

A.8 Control of Externally Provided Processes, Products and Services

All forms of externally provided processes, products and services are addressed in 8.4, e.g. whether through:

  1. purchasing from a supplier;
  2. an arrangement with an associate company;
  3. outsourcing processes to an external provider.

Outsourcing always has the essential characteristic of a service, since it will have at least one activity necessarily performed at the interface between the provider and the organization.

The controls required for external provision can vary widely depending on the nature of the processes, products and services. The organization can apply risk-based thinking to determine the type and extent of controls appropriate to particular external providers and externally provided processes, products and services.

解讀：三個新要求的製藥意涵

A.6：「維持」vs.「保留」文件化資訊

這是 2015 版最容易混淆的術語對。理解方式：

  • Maintain（維持）：持續有效的、活的文件——如 SOP、品質手冊、過程描述。對應舊版的「文件（Documents）」。
  • Retain（保留）：已完成的、作為證據的記錄——如批次記錄、測試報告、稽核記錄。對應舊版的「記錄（Records）」。

一份文件可以同時要求「維持」（保持當前版本有效）和「保留」（歸檔舊版本）。

A.7：組織知識——製藥廠的致命弱點

組織知識（Organizational Knowledge）是 2015 版全新的要求（無對應舊條款）。對製藥 CDMO 尤其關鍵：

  • 資深工程師、QA 退休或離職 → 關鍵製程知識流失
  • 設備供應商停止支援 → 維護知識斷鏈
  • 客戶產品技術移轉 → 知識轉移需系統化記錄

實務工具：製程知識庫、技術移轉文件包（Technology Transfer Package）、交接 SOP、師徒制度、Lessons Learned 資料庫。

三個 A 條款的共同主題

A.6（文件彈性化）、A.7（知識管理）、A.8（供應風險管控）都體現了 2015 版的核心精神：減少強制規定，增加組織自主判斷的空間——但要求你有能力說明判斷的依據。這對成熟的製藥 QMS 是鬆綁，對薄弱的 QMS 卻是更大的挑戰。

The International Standards described in this annex have been developed by ISO/TC 176 to provide supporting information for organizations that apply this International Standard. Guidance or requirements contained in the documents listed in this annex do not add to, or modify, the requirements of this International Standard.

Three core standards developed by ISO/TC 176:

解讀：ISO/TC 176 標準生態圈

ISO/TC 176 是誰？

ISO Technical Committee 176（品質管理與品質保證技術委員會），負責制訂所有 ISO 9000 系列及相關配套標準。本附錄列出的所有標準都是同一個委員會的產品，相互一致、互相補強。

製藥廠最常用的配套標準

  • ISO 10012 — 量測管理系統：與 21 CFR Part 11 校正要求、計量可追溯性高度相關。量測設備管控是製藥 QMS 的核心，這份標準提供了實作指引。
  • ISO/TR 10013 — QMS 文件化指引：協助設計 SOP 體系、文件架構，對文件管理系統建置很有幫助。
  • ISO 10015 — 培訓指引：GMP 培訓體系設計、培訓有效性評估，對應 ISO 9001 Clause 7.2（能力）。
  • ISO 19011 — 稽核管理：內部稽核計畫設計、稽核員能力評估，是 ISO 9001 Clause 9.2 的最佳實踐指引。

比喻：ISO 9001 是作業系統，配套標準是 App

ISO 9001 定義了 QMS 的「作業系統」——它說你需要「管理量測設備」（7.1.5），但不告訴你怎麼設計校正程序。ISO 10012 就是那個「App」，告訴你量測管理系統的具體做法。根據你的業務需求，選擇性安裝對你有用的 App 即可。

比較

QMS Standards Comparison: ISO 9001 vs. ISO 13485 vs. ISO 15378

三大 QMS 標準定位對比——製藥包材產業必備視角

為什麼要比較這三個標準？

ISO 15378（初級包材 GMP 品質管理系統）是以 ISO 9001 為基礎、加上 GMP 層疊加的專業標準。了解三者的定位差異，有助於製藥 CDMO 判斷：在哪些場景使用哪個標準框架，以及多標準整合的策略。

ISO 13485 vs. ISO 9001：為什麼沒有以 2015 版為基礎？

ISO 13485:2016 仍以 ISO 9001:2008 為框架基礎，而非 2015 版。原因：醫療器材產業的監管機構（FDA、BSI 等）認為 ISO 9001:2015 的彈性化方向（移除強制性持續改善、取消品質手冊要求）對高風險的醫療器材行業不合適。因此 ISO 13485 維持更嚴格的強制要求，並可能在未來版本才考慮融合 HLS 架構。

三標準整合策略

對於同時涉及多個領域的製造商（如：既做初級包材也供應醫療器材），整合三個標準的關鍵是：

  • 以 ISO 9001 的 HLS 架構為骨幹
  • 在適用範圍內疊加 ISO 13485 的強制要求
  • 在包材業務範圍內疊加 ISO 15378 的 GMP 層
  • 避免為不同客戶群維護三套獨立文件體系——盡量整合

製藥 QMS 建置的推薦閱讀順序

如果你是製藥廠 QA，從這份書目中，建議的優先順序：

  1. ISO 9000（雖不在書目中，但是基礎）— 先建立術語概念
  2. ISO 9004（[1]）— 了解更成熟的 QMS 樣貌，適合系統優化階段
  3. ISO 19011（[18]）— 建立內部稽核能力的實用指引
  4. ISO 10012（[10]）— 量測管理，GMP 校正系統的補充
  5. ISO 10015（[13]）— GMP 培訓體系設計
  6. ISO 31000（[19]）— 若要建立正式風險管理框架

ISO 31000 — 正式風險管理的框架

雖然 ISO 9001 本身不要求正式風險管理方法論，但附錄書目中列入了 ISO 31000（風險管理原則與指引）。這代表 ISO 9001 認可：有需要的組織可以採用 ISO 31000 作為建立更完整風險管理系統的基礎。

在製藥業，ISO 31000 + ICH Q9 + 具體的 FMEA 工具，構成了完整的 QRM 體系。

ISO 9001 Auditing Practices Group

書目最後兩個網址之一是 ISO 9001 稽核實踐小組（APG）的網站。這個小組由 ISO/TC 176 和 IAF（國際認可論壇）共同運作，提供免費的稽核技術文件和常見問題解答——是準備 ISO 9001 認證稽核的寶貴免費資源。

Section Summary — 本節重點總結

ISO 9001:2015 Annex A & B 核心要點回顧

Key Takeaways — Annex A

  • A.1 術語變革：不強制組織改變內部術語，但需理解術語映射（尤其 documented information 的 maintain vs. retain 區別）
  • A.2 產品與服務：服務的「現場性」要求前置品質管控，無法事後完全檢驗
  • A.3 相關方：組織自主判斷哪些相關方與 QMS 相關，不需無限擴張範圍
  • A.4 風險思維：取代獨立的預防措施條款；ISO 9001 不要求正式風險管理方法論（與 ICH Q9 / ISO 14971 形成重要對比）
  • A.5 適用性：「排除」一詞廢除；只有 Clause 8 可宣告不適用，且不能影響產品符合性
  • A.6 文件化資訊：maintain（維持活文件）vs. retain（保留記錄）是核心概念
  • A.7 組織知識：2015 版新增要求，保護關鍵製程知識不因人員流失而消失
  • A.8 外部供應管控：以風險為基礎決定管控強度，涵蓋採購、合作、外包三種形式

Key Takeaways — Annex B & Bibliography

Annex B 核心訊息

  • ISO/TC 176 維護一個完整的支援標準生態系，ISO 9001 是核心，其他標準提供具體實踐指引
  • Table B.1 是快速定位配套資源的導覽工具
  • 本附錄不包含各行業的專業 QMS 標準（如 ISO 13485、ISO 15378）——這些不在 TC 176 的直接管轄下

三標準比較的核心結論

  • ISO 9001：彈性、風險思維驅動、適合任何組織的通用框架
  • ISO 13485：更嚴格、持續改善非強制、ISO 14971 風險管理為強制要求、醫療器材導向
  • ISO 15378：ISO 9001 + GMP 疊加、填充用初級包材專用、製藥客戶最直接相關

最後的思維框架

ISO 9001 的 Annex A 最核心的訊息是：2015 版從「合規導向」轉向「績效導向」。不再問「你有沒有這個程序？」，而是問「你有沒有識別風險並採取有效行動？」。這正是 ICH Q10 的精神——一個活的、有效的品質系統，而非一堆漂亮的文件。
