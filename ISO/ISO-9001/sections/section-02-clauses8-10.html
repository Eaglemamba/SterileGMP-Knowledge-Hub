Clauses 8–10: Operation, Performance Evaluation & Improvement

第8-10節：運作、績效評估與改善
ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management Systems — Requirements | p25 – p36

學習目標 Learning Objectives

  • 理解 Clause 8 運作控制的完整結構：從客戶需求確認、設計開發、外部供應商管控，到生產放行與不合格品處置
  • 區分設計審查（Review）、驗證（Verification）與確效（Validation）的不同目的，並對應藥廠實務
  • 掌握 Clause 9 績效評估三個工具：監測量測、內部稽核、管理審查，及其在法規查廠準備中的意義
  • 理解 Clause 10 改善機制中，矯正（Correction）與矯正措施（Corrective Action）的根本區別——CAPA 在 FDA/EMA 查廠中的核心地位
  • 比較 ISO 9001 要求與藥廠 GMP（ICH Q10、ISO 15378、QS Reg）在供應商管控、特殊製程確效、CAPA 等面向的深度差異
8.1

Operational Planning and Control — 運作規劃與控制

Clause 8.1 — The execution layer: translating risk/opportunity plans into controlled processes

8.1 Operational Planning and Control

The organization shall plan, implement and control the processes needed to meet the requirements for the provision of products and services, and to implement the actions determined in Clause 6, by:

  1. determining the requirements for the products and services;
  2. establishing criteria for:
    1. the processes;
    2. the acceptance of products and services;
  3. determining the resources needed to achieve conformity to the product and service requirements;
  4. implementing control of the processes in accordance with the criteria;
  5. determining, maintaining and retaining documented information to the extent necessary:
    1. to have confidence that the processes have been carried out as planned;
    2. to demonstrate the conformity of products and services to their requirements.

The output of this planning shall be suitable for the organization's operations.

The organization shall control planned changes and review the consequences of unintended changes, taking action to mitigate any adverse effects, as necessary.

The organization shall ensure that outsourced processes are controlled (see 8.4).

解說：運作規劃是 Clause 6 計畫的執行層

核心邏輯：計畫 → 執行 → 文件化

8.1 是 ISO 9001 的「執行引擎」。Clause 6 決定了要做什麼（風險/機會計畫），8.1 規定了如何把這些計畫落地成受控的製程操作。

  • 準則（Criteria）：製程接受準則，等同藥廠的製程規格與 in-process control 限值
  • 文件化資訊（Documented information）：批次紀錄、製程規程——藥廠已遠超此要求
  • 計畫性變更控制：正式的 Change Control 系統——GMP 必備，ISO 9001 僅要求「控制並審查後果」

類比：蓋房子的施工圖

Clause 6 是建築師的設計方案（決定要建什麼），8.1 是施工圖加上品質管理計畫（告訴工人每道工序的標準和驗收條件）。沒有施工圖，工人再努力也可能蓋錯。

藥廠視角：外包製程的控制

「外包製程應受控制」（見 8.4）——在製藥 CDMO 中，每一個委外的檢驗、滅菌、包裝步驟都屬此範疇。ISO 9001 要求「控制」，GMP 則進一步要求正式的技術協議（Technical Agreement）與供應商稽核。

8.2

Requirements for Products and Services — 產品與服務要求

Clauses 8.2.1–8.2.4 — Customer communication, requirement determination, review, and change management

8.2.1 Customer Communication

Communication with customers shall include:

  1. providing information relating to products and services;
  2. handling enquiries, contracts or orders, including changes;
  3. obtaining customer feedback relating to products and services, including customer complaints;
  4. handling or controlling customer property;
  5. establishing specific requirements for contingency actions, when relevant.

8.2.2 Determining Requirements

The organization shall ensure that:

  1. the requirements for the products and services are defined, including:
    1. any applicable statutory and regulatory requirements;
    2. those considered necessary by the organization;
  2. the organization can meet the claims for the products and services it offers.

8.2.3 Review of Requirements

The organization shall ensure that it has the ability to meet the requirements before committing to supply, to include:

  1. requirements specified by the customer, including delivery and post-delivery activities;
  2. requirements not stated by the customer, but necessary for the specified or intended use;
  3. requirements specified by the organization;
  4. statutory and regulatory requirements;
  5. contract or order requirements differing from those previously expressed.

The organization shall retain documented information on the results of the review and on any new requirements.

8.2.4 Changes to Requirements

The organization shall ensure that relevant documented information is amended, and that relevant persons are made aware of the changed requirements, when requirements are changed.

解說：合約審查——CDMO 的核心能力

8.2.1 客戶溝通的 5 個面向

  • ① 產品資訊提供：規格書、CoA、SDS
  • ② 詢價/合約/訂單處理：含變更管理
  • ③ 客戶回饋與投訴：ISO 要求收集，GMP 要求 CAPA
  • ④ 客戶財產管理：客戶提供的原料、模具、IP
  • ⑤ 應急措施要求：供應鏈中斷應對計畫

類比：餐廳點餐的確認流程

8.2.3 合約審查就像廚師接到特殊菜單前先確認：食材有沒有（資源）、廚師會不會做（能力）、客人有沒有過敏（法規/隱含需求）。先確認，再承諾，才不會在出菜時出狀況。

藥廠 CDMO 的合約審查（8.2.3）

CDMO 在接受新客戶委託前，合約審查要涵蓋：

  • 法規市場要求（FDA/EMA/PMDA 哪個市場？）
  • 技術可行性（劑型、製程能力、無菌等級）
  • 隱含要求（例：無菌產品自帶 Annex 1 要求，即使客戶未明確指定）
  • 技術協議（Quality Agreement）內容

這正是 8.2.2b 所說的「組織需確認自己能兌現承諾」。

8.3

Design and Development — 設計與開發

Clauses 8.3.1–8.3.6 — Planning, inputs, controls (review/verification/validation), outputs, changes

8.3.1–8.3.2 General & Planning

The organization shall establish, implement and maintain a design and development process appropriate to ensure the subsequent provision of products and services.

In determining the stages and controls, the organization shall consider:

  • nature, duration and complexity of activities;
  • required process stages, reviews, verification and validation activities;
  • responsibilities and authorities;
  • internal and external resource needs;
  • involvement of customers and users;
  • documented information needed to demonstrate requirements have been met.

8.3.3 Design and Development Inputs

The organization shall determine requirements essential for the specific types of products and services, considering:

  1. functional and performance requirements;
  2. information derived from previous similar activities;
  3. statutory and regulatory requirements;
  4. standards or codes of practice;
  5. potential consequences of failure.

Inputs shall be adequate, complete and unambiguous. Conflicting inputs shall be resolved.

8.3.4 Design and Development Controls

Controls shall ensure that:

  1. the results to be achieved are defined;
  2. reviews are conducted to evaluate the ability to meet requirements;
  3. verification activities ensure outputs meet input requirements;
  4. validation activities ensure products/services meet requirements for specified application or intended use;
  5. necessary actions are taken on problems determined;
  6. documented information of these activities is retained.
NOTE: Design and development reviews, verification and validation have distinct purposes. They can be conducted separately or in any combination.

8.3.5 Design and Development Outputs

Outputs shall:

  1. meet the input requirements;
  2. be adequate for subsequent provision processes;
  3. include or reference monitoring/measuring requirements and acceptance criteria;
  4. specify characteristics essential for intended purpose and safe provision.

8.3.6 Design and Development Changes

The organization shall identify, review and control changes to ensure no adverse impact on conformity. Retained documented information shall include:

  1. design and development changes;
  2. results of reviews;
  3. authorization of changes;
  4. actions taken to prevent adverse impacts.

解說：設計開發在製藥的對應

Review / Verification / Validation 三者的根本區別

ISO 9001 的 NOTE 特別強調三者目的不同，這在藥廠環境中尤為重要：

  • Review（審查）：評估設計結果「能否達到要求」——設計審查會議（Design Review Meeting），藥廠比照 ICH Q8 中的配方設計審查
  • Verification（驗證）：確認「輸出符合輸入規格」——例如確認處方設計符合 TPP（Target Product Profile）中的規格要求
  • Validation（確效）：確認產品在「實際使用條件下」能達到預期用途——藥廠的製程確效（Process Validation）、清潔確效、滅菌確效

類比：設計一架飛機

Review：工程師開會審查設計圖，確認翼展計算沒有錯誤。
Verification：在風洞中測試模型，確認氣動數據符合設計規格。
Validation：實機飛行測試，確認飛機在真實天空中能安全飛行。

三步缺一不可——風洞通過了，不代表真機飛得起來。

藥廠的設計開發對應關係

  • 8.3 設計開發 → ICH Q8 藥品開發（pharmaceutical development）
  • 設計輸入（8.3.3） → Target Product Profile（TPP）、法規要求、前期研究數據
  • 設計輸出（8.3.5） → 批次製造記錄（BMR）、品管方法、規格書
  • 設計變更（8.3.6） → 技術轉移後的 Post-Approval Change（變更管理）
  • 設計開發 → 生產：技術轉移（Technology Transfer）= 從 R&D 到商業生產

ICH Q8 Quality by Design（QbD）與 8.3 的連結

ICH Q8 提倡從設計初期就系統性地理解產品和製程，建立 Design Space。這正是 ISO 9001 8.3 設計控制的進階版：

  • CQA（關鍵品質屬性）= 設計輸入
  • CPP（關鍵製程參數）= 設計輸出的一部分
  • Design Space = 驗證過的操作範圍
8.4

Control of Externally Provided Processes, Products and Services — 外部供應管控

Clauses 8.4.1–8.4.3 — Three scenarios, risk-proportionate controls, six communication categories

8.4.1 General

The organization shall ensure that externally provided processes, products and services conform to requirements.

Controls shall be determined when:

  1. products and services from external providers are intended for incorporation into the organization's own products and services;
  2. products and services are provided directly to the customer by external providers on behalf of the organization;
  3. a process, or part of a process, is provided by an external provider as a result of a decision by the organization (outsourcing).

The organization shall determine and apply criteria for the evaluation, selection, monitoring of performance, and re-evaluation of external providers, based on their ability to provide in accordance with requirements. Documented information of these activities and any necessary actions shall be retained.

8.4.2 Type and Extent of Control

The organization shall ensure externally provided processes, products and services do not adversely affect ability to consistently deliver conforming products/services. The organization shall:

  1. ensure externally provided processes remain within the control of its QMS;
  2. define both the controls for the external provider and those for the resulting output;
  3. take into consideration:
    1. the potential impact on the organization's ability to consistently meet requirements;
    2. the effectiveness of the controls applied by the external provider;
  4. determine verification or other activities necessary to ensure requirements are met.

8.4.3 Information for External Providers

The organization shall communicate to external providers its requirements for:

  1. the processes, products and services to be provided;
  2. the approval of: products and services; methods, processes and equipment; the release of products and services;
  3. competence, including any required qualification of persons;
  4. the external providers' interactions with the organization;
  5. control and monitoring of the external providers' performance to be applied by the organization;
  6. verification or validation activities that the organization, or its customer, intends to perform at the external providers' premises.

解說：供應商管控——ISO 9001 vs. GMP

8.4.1 的三種情境

  • 情境 A（納入自有產品）：採購原料 API、輔料、包材——典型的藥廠採購場景
  • 情境 B（代表你直接交貨給客戶）：分銷商代替你出貨——藥廠較少但合約研究中存在
  • 情境 C（委外製程）：委託滅菌、委託檢驗、委託包裝——CDMO 的核心業務模式

ISO 9001 與 GMP 的供應商管控深度差異

ISO 9001 要求「評估、選擇、監測、再評估」，但沒有規定如何做。GMP 標準走得更遠：

  • ICH Q10：要求書面的供應商品質協議（Technical/Quality Agreement）
  • ISO 15378（藥品包材）：供應商稽核計畫、定期評估報告
  • FDA QS Regulation（21 CFR 820）：要求正式的 Approved Supplier List 與稽核紀錄
  • EU GMP Annex 8：原料採樣與抽驗——到廠前的確認

類比：餐廳的食材供應商管理

米其林餐廳不會只看供應商的報價單，他們會親自去農場確認種植方式（現場稽核），要求農藥檢測報告（CoA），並建立長期合作關係（Approved Supplier）。ISO 9001 說「你要管好供應商」，GMP 告訴你「具體怎麼管」。

8.4.3 對外部供應商的六類溝通要求

  1. 要提供的製程/產品/服務規格
  2. 核准事項（產品、方法、設備、放行）
  3. 人員資格能力要求
  4. 與組織的互動界面
  5. 組織對供應商績效的監控方式
  6. 組織或客戶將在供應商場所執行的驗證/確效活動

第 6 點特別重要：客戶到 CDMO 現場執行品質稽核，或 CDMO 到原料供應商執行 Vendor Audit——都在此條款範疇。

8.5

Production and Service Provision — 生產與服務提供

Clauses 8.5.1–8.5.6 — Controlled conditions, traceability, customer property, preservation, post-delivery, change control

8.5.1 Control of Production and Service Provision

The organization shall implement production and service provision under controlled conditions, including as applicable:

  1. the availability of documented information defining: characteristics of products, services, or activities; and results to be achieved;
  2. availability and use of suitable monitoring and measuring resources;
  3. implementation of monitoring and measurement at appropriate stages to verify criteria have been met;
  4. use of suitable infrastructure and environment;
  5. appointment of competent persons, including any required qualification;
  6. validation, and periodic revalidation, of processes where the resulting output cannot be verified by subsequent monitoring or measurement;
  7. implementation of actions to prevent human error;
  8. implementation of release, delivery and post-delivery activities.

8.5.2 Identification and Traceability

The organization shall use suitable means to identify outputs when necessary to ensure conformity. The organization shall identify the status of outputs with respect to monitoring and measurement throughout production. The organization shall control the unique identification of outputs when traceability is a requirement, and retain documented information to enable traceability.

8.5.3 Property Belonging to Customers or External Providers

The organization shall exercise care with property belonging to customers or external providers while under the organization's control. The organization shall identify, verify, protect and safeguard such property. When property is lost, damaged or found unsuitable, the organization shall report to the customer or external provider and retain documented information.

NOTE: Property can include materials, components, tools and equipment, premises, intellectual property and personal data.

8.5.4 Preservation

The organization shall preserve the outputs during production and service provision to ensure conformity to requirements. Preservation can include identification, handling, contamination control, packaging, storage, transmission or transportation, and protection.

8.5.5 Post-Delivery Activities

The organization shall meet requirements for post-delivery activities, considering:

  1. statutory and regulatory requirements;
  2. potential undesired consequences;
  3. nature, use and intended lifetime of products and services;
  4. customer requirements;
  5. customer feedback.

8.5.6 Control of Changes

The organization shall review and control changes for production to ensure continuing conformity with requirements. Retained documented information shall describe: results of the review of changes; person(s) authorizing the change; and any necessary actions arising from the review.

解說：生產控制與藥廠 GMP 的深度對應

8.5.1f — 「特殊製程」確效：ISO 9001 的核心概念

最重要的條款：當製程結果「無法靠後續監測或量測驗證」時，需要對該製程進行確效（Validation）並定期再確效。

這正是藥廠確效的理論基礎——你無法對每支注射劑做無菌測試（破壞性），所以必須提前確效整個無菌製程：

  • 滅菌確效（Sterilization Validation）：高壓蒸汽、乾熱、過濾除菌
  • 培地灌裝（Media Fill / Process Simulation）：無菌灌裝製程模擬
  • 清潔確效（Cleaning Validation）：共線生產的殘留交叉污染控制
  • 電腦化系統確效（CSV）：無法靠肉眼確認的自動化製程

類比：烘焙蛋糕 vs. 罐頭殺菌

烘焙蛋糕可以切開確認熟了（後續量測可驗證）。但罐頭殺菌後無法每罐都打開測試內容物還在不在，所以必須事先確效：在特定溫度、壓力、時間組合下，確認所有位置都能達到足夠的致死率（F₀值）。這就是「特殊製程」確效的本質。

8.5.2 可追溯性（Traceability）在藥廠的意義

ISO 9001 要求「需要時」建立唯一識別和追溯。藥廠 GMP 則是強制要求完整的批次追溯：

  • 批號系統（Batch/Lot Number）
  • 原料追溯到 CoA 和供應商
  • 生產設備、人員、環境數據
  • 發生召回時能快速追溯所有影響批次（Batch Record Review）

21 CFR Part 211 要求批次記錄保存至效期後 1 年，或至少 3 年——遠超 ISO 9001 的最低要求。

8.5.5 交貨後活動——藥廠的藥物警戒對應

ISO 9001 要求考量「潛在不良後果、使用壽命、法規要求」。藥廠對應項目：

  • 藥物警戒（Pharmacovigilance）：上市後不良反應監測
  • Field Safety Corrective Action（FSCA）：召回與安全通知
  • 穩定性計畫（Ongoing Stability）：確認效期內品質
  • 年度產品品質審查（APR/PQR）：EU GMP Ch.1 要求

8.5.6 變更控制——藥廠的 Change Control 系統

ISO 9001 要求記錄變更審查結果、授權人及後續行動。藥廠 GMP 更進一步：

  • 分類（微小/重大/重要 change）
  • 風險評估
  • 跨部門審查（QA、生產、法規、工程）
  • 法規申報評估（是否需 Variation/PAS/CBE）
  • 有效性確認（Effectiveness Check）
8.6–7

Release of Products & Control of Nonconforming Outputs — 放行與不合格品管控

Clauses 8.6–8.7 — Product release criteria, four disposal actions, documented information requirements

8.6 Release of Products and Services

The organization shall implement planned arrangements, at appropriate stages, to verify that the product and service requirements have been met. The release of products and services shall not proceed until the planned arrangements have been satisfactorily completed, unless otherwise approved by a relevant authority and, as applicable, by the customer.

The organization shall retain documented information on the release of products and services, including:

  1. evidence of conformity with the acceptance criteria;
  2. traceability to the person(s) authorizing the release.

8.7.1 Control of Nonconforming Outputs

The organization shall ensure that outputs that do not conform to their requirements are identified and controlled to prevent their unintended use or delivery. The organization shall deal with nonconforming outputs in one or more of the following ways:

  1. correction;
  2. segregation, containment, return or suspension of provision of products and services;
  3. informing the customer;
  4. obtaining authorization for acceptance under concession.

Conformity to the requirements shall be verified when nonconforming outputs are corrected.

8.7.2 Documented Information

The organization shall retain documented information that:

  1. describes the nonconformity;
  2. describes the actions taken;
  3. describes any concessions obtained;
  4. identifies the authority deciding the action.

解說：放行管控與不合格品——藥廠的嚴格要求

8.6 產品放行：QP（Qualified Person）的責任

ISO 9001 要求放行前通過計畫性安排，並記錄授權人。藥廠有更嚴格的制度：

  • EU GMP：每批次必須由 QP（授權人）簽署放行，具法律責任
  • FDA：Quality Unit 負責批次審查和放行（21 CFR 211.192）
  • 放行前要求：完整批次記錄審查、偏差評估、OOS 調查結果、穩定性數據

ISO 9001 說「授權人追溯性」——藥廠是 QP 對政府當局的個人法律責任，層級完全不同。

藥廠的不合格品管理更嚴格

  • OOS（Out-of-Specification）：需按 FDA OOS 指引進行調查，不能簡單讓步接受
  • 重加工（Rework）：需要正式的 Rework Protocol，並重新通過完整的 QC 測試
  • 銷毀記錄：不合格品的銷毀需要雙人見證並記錄
  • 偏差系統：ISO 9001 僅要求文件記錄，藥廠要求偏差調查、根本原因分析（RCA）、CAPA
9.1

Monitoring, Measurement, Analysis and Evaluation — 監測、量測、分析與評估

Clauses 9.1.1–9.1.3 — What to measure, customer satisfaction, seven analysis outcomes

9.1.1 General

The organization shall determine:

  1. what needs to be monitored and measured;
  2. the methods for monitoring, measurement, analysis and evaluation needed to ensure valid results;
  3. when the monitoring and measuring shall be performed;
  4. when the results shall be analysed and evaluated.

The organization shall evaluate the performance and effectiveness of the QMS. The organization shall retain appropriate documented information as evidence of the results.

9.1.2 Customer Satisfaction

The organization shall monitor customers' perceptions of the degree to which their needs and expectations have been fulfilled. The organization shall determine the methods for obtaining, monitoring and reviewing this information.

NOTE: Examples of monitoring customer perceptions can include customer surveys, customer feedback on delivered products and services, meetings with customers, market-share analysis, compliments, warranty claims and dealer reports.

9.1.3 Analysis and Evaluation

The organization shall analyse and evaluate appropriate data and information. The results of analysis shall be used to evaluate:

  1. conformity of products and services;
  2. the degree of customer satisfaction;
  3. the performance and effectiveness of the QMS;
  4. if planning has been implemented effectively;
  5. the effectiveness of actions taken to address risks and opportunities;
  6. the performance of external providers;
  7. the need for improvements to the QMS.

解說：績效量測——從 KPI 到患者安全

類比：飛機的儀表板

飛行員不是靠感覺飛，是靠儀表板：高度計、速度計、燃油量。ISO 9001 的 9.1 就是要求組織建立自己的「品質儀表板」——確定哪些指標是飛行安全的關鍵，並定時讀取和評估。

9.1.2 CDMO 的「客戶滿意度」：誰是真正的客戶？

這是藥廠 CDMO 的獨特挑戰：

  • 直接客戶：委託方（製藥公司）——他們對交貨期、品質、溝通的滿意度
  • 終端客戶（Ultimate Customer）：患者——他們的安全性和療效

患者不會直接給 CDMO 填問卷，但患者的安全就是 CDMO 品質工作的最終目的。ICH Q10 提倡的Patient-centric quality culture（以患者為中心的品質文化）正是這個理念。

監測方法：客戶投訴分析、藥物警戒訊號、市場回收數據——這些間接反映了對患者的影響。

9.1.3 的七個分析輸出——整合到 APR/PQR

藥廠的年度產品品質審查（Annual Product Review / Product Quality Review）正是 9.1.3 的實踐：整合全年產品、製程、供應商、偏差、CAPA、穩定性數據，評估 QMS 有效性，識別改善機會。

9.2

Internal Audit — 內部稽核

Clauses 9.2.1–9.2.2 — Audit programme, objectivity, reporting, corrective action, documented information

9.2.1 Internal Audit Requirements

The organization shall conduct internal audits at planned intervals to provide information on whether the QMS:

  1. conforms to:
    1. the organization's own requirements for its QMS;
    2. the requirements of this International Standard;
  2. is effectively implemented and maintained.

9.2.2 Audit Programme

The organization shall:

  1. plan, establish, implement and maintain an audit programme including the frequency, methods, responsibilities, planning requirements and reporting, taking into consideration the importance of the processes, changes affecting the organization, and the results of previous audits;
  2. define the audit criteria and scope for each audit;
  3. select auditors and conduct audits to ensure objectivity and impartiality;
  4. ensure that the results are reported to relevant management;
  5. take appropriate correction and corrective actions without undue delay;
  6. retain documented information as evidence of the audit programme and audit results.

NOTE: See ISO 19011 for guidance on auditing management systems.

解說：內部稽核——查廠準備的日常訓練

稽核計畫的風險導向設計

9.2.2a 要求稽核頻率考量「製程重要性、組織變更、前次稽核結果」——這是風險導向稽核計畫（Risk-based audit programme）的基本邏輯。

藥廠實務：無菌灌裝、滅菌、OSD 壓錠等高風險製程的稽核頻率高於倉儲、行政部門。新引入製程或設備變更後應安排額外稽核。

客觀性與公正性（Objectivity and Impartiality）

ISO 9001 要求稽核員對被稽核區域保持獨立性——稽核員不應稽核自己負責的工作。

藥廠實務：通常採用交叉稽核（Cross-audit）——A 線人員稽核 B 線，或由 QA 部門主導，生產人員參與技術面稽核。外部稽核（Third-party audit）提供更高的客觀性。

類比：飛機的定期維護檢查

航空公司不等到飛機出問題才檢查，而是按照飛行小時定期進行 A-check、C-check、D-check。內部稽核就是 QMS 的「定期維護」——不是在出問題後才亡羊補牢，而是系統性地找出問題苗頭。

內部稽核 = 法規查廠的日常訓練

對藥廠而言，內部稽核有雙重價值：

  • QMS 維護：找出合規缺口、識別改善機會
  • 查廠準備（Inspection Readiness）：以 FDA/EMA/衛福部查廠的標準來稽核自己，讓員工熟悉查廠情境，找出文件缺口

優秀的藥廠 QA 部門會把內部稽核報告當作模擬查廠觀察，針對性地在真正查廠前解決問題。

9.3

Management Review — 管理審查

Clauses 9.3.1–9.3.3 — General, eight input categories, three output decisions

9.3.1 General

Top management shall review the organization's QMS, at planned intervals, to ensure its continuing suitability, adequacy, effectiveness and alignment with the strategic direction of the organization.

9.3.2 Management Review Inputs

The management review shall be planned and carried out taking into consideration:

  1. the status of actions from previous management reviews;
  2. changes in external and internal issues relevant to the QMS;
  3. information on the performance and effectiveness of the QMS, including trends in:
    1. customer satisfaction and feedback from relevant interested parties;
    2. the extent to which quality objectives have been met;
    3. process performance and conformity of products and services;
    4. nonconformities and corrective actions;
    5. monitoring and measurement results;
    6. audit results;
    7. the performance of external providers;
  4. the adequacy of resources;
  5. the effectiveness of actions taken to address risks and opportunities;
  6. opportunities for improvement.

9.3.3 Management Review Outputs

The outputs shall include decisions and actions related to:

  1. opportunities for improvement;
  2. any need for changes to the QMS;
  3. resource needs.

The organization shall retain documented information as evidence of the results of management reviews.

解說：管理審查——高層責任的具體體現

管理審查的三個關鍵字

  • Suitability（適切性）：QMS 是否仍適合組織的目的與背景？（隨著業務規模變化）
  • Adequacy（充分性）：QMS 的資源和架構是否足夠？（人員、設備、系統）
  • Effectiveness（有效性）：QMS 是否真的在運作並達到結果？（不只是有文件，而是有效果）

類比：年度董事會報告

管理審查就像公司的年度董事會：高層主管不看日常細節，而是看整體趨勢——去年達成了哪些目標、有什麼重大問題、明年需要投入什麼資源。9.3.2 的輸入清單就是「品質版董事會議程」。

「前次管理審查的後續行動狀態」——追蹤閉環

9.3.2a 要求審查前次決議的執行狀態，這是品質管理的「閉環」原則：做出決定（輸出）→ 執行 → 追蹤結果 → 下次管理審查確認關閉。

沒有這一步，管理審查就會淪為「每年開一次的例行報告會」，決議從未真正執行。

10.1

Improvement: General — 改善：一般要求

Clause 10.1 — Three categories of improvement opportunities

10.1 General

The organization shall determine and select opportunities for improvement and implement any necessary actions to meet customer requirements and enhance customer satisfaction.

These shall include:

  1. improving products and services to meet requirements as well as to address future needs and expectations;
  2. correcting, preventing or reducing undesired effects;
  3. improving the performance and effectiveness of the QMS.

NOTE: Examples of improvement can include correction, corrective action, continual improvement, breakthrough change, innovation and re-organization.

解說：改善的全光譜

改善的三個層次

  • 產品/服務改善：提升配方、工藝、包裝——通常對應技術開發項目
  • 問題預防與糾正：這是 10.2 CAPA 的核心
  • QMS 效能提升：流程優化、數位化、培訓計畫改善

NOTE 提到的改善光譜

從漸進到突破：矯正（Correction）→ 矯正措施（Corrective Action）→ 持續改善（Continual improvement）→ 突破性變革（Breakthrough change）→ 創新（Innovation）→ 組織重整（Re-organization）

藥廠的改善活動通常聚焦在前三項，「突破性變革」需要監管申報。

10.2

Nonconformity and Corrective Action — 不符合事項與矯正措施

Clauses 10.2.1–10.2.2 — Six-step CAPA process, documented information requirements

10.2.1 Nonconformity and Corrective Action

When a nonconformity occurs, including any arising from complaints, the organization shall:

  1. react to the nonconformity and, as applicable:
    1. take action to control and correct it;
    2. deal with the consequences;
  2. evaluate the need for action to eliminate the cause(s) of the nonconformity, in order that it does not recur or occur elsewhere, by:
    1. reviewing and analysing the nonconformity;
    2. determining the causes of the nonconformity;
    3. determining if similar nonconformities exist, or could potentially occur;
  3. implement any action needed;
  4. review the effectiveness of any corrective action taken;
  5. update risks and opportunities determined during planning, if necessary;
  6. make changes to the QMS, if necessary.

Corrective actions shall be appropriate to the effects of the nonconformities encountered.

10.2.2 Documented Information

The organization shall retain documented information as evidence of:

  1. the nature of the nonconformities and any subsequent actions taken;
  2. the results of any corrective action.

解說：CAPA——FDA/EMA 查廠最關鍵的系統

類比：醫生看病

Correction：開退燒藥，讓體溫下降（處理症狀）。
Corrective Action：抽血找感染源、開抗生素根治感染（消除根因）。

只退燒不找病因，下週又會燒。只 Correction 不做 Corrective Action，下次同樣問題還是會發生。

CAPA 在 FDA/EMA 查廠中的核心地位

對藥廠而言，CAPA 系統是最受監管機構關注的系統之一：

  • FDA Warning Letter 常見缺失：CAPA 根本原因調查不足、有效性確認未執行、CAPA 逾期未關閉
  • EMA GMP 查廠重點：CAPA 是否針對反覆發生的偏差？高層管理是否接收 CAPA 趨勢報告？
  • ICH Q10 要求：CAPA 系統是製藥品質系統的四大要素之一

10.2.1 的六步驟對應藥廠 CAPA 流程

  1. 反應（10.2.1a）：即時控制——隔離、停線、通知客戶
  2. 評估根因（10.2.1b）：根本原因分析（RCA）——5 Whys、Fishbone、FMEA
  3. 實施行動（10.2.1c）：執行 CAPA——修改 SOP、培訓、設備改善
  4. 確認有效性（10.2.1d）：Effectiveness Check——30/60/90天後確認問題未再發
  5. 更新風險評估（10.2.1e）：回頭修訂 FMEA 或風險登記冊
  6. 更新 QMS（10.2.1f）：若需修改文件系統或程序

Correction vs. Corrective Action——最重要的區別

ISO 9001 的 10.2.1a 是 Correction，10.2.1b-f 是 Corrective Action。兩者都需要，缺一不可。

10.3

Continual Improvement — 持續改善

Clause 10.3 — Suitability, adequacy, effectiveness; feeds from analysis and management review

10.3 Continual Improvement

The organization shall continually improve the suitability, adequacy and effectiveness of the quality management system.

The organization shall consider the results of analysis and evaluation, and the outputs from management review, to determine if there are needs or opportunities that shall be addressed as part of continual improvement.

解說：持續改善——ISO 9001 的精神核心

「持續」的含義——不只是「改善過一次」

Continual improvement 中的 "continual"（持續的、循環進行的）而非 "continuous"（不間斷的），意思是改善是一個反覆循環的過程，而非一次性活動。

驅動來源：9.1.3 分析評估結果（數據驅動）+ 9.3.3 管理審查輸出（高層決策）→ 識別改善機會 → 實施改善 → 再次評估。

類比：PDCA 螺旋

持續改善不是走一個圈回到原點，而是像螺旋梯——每轉一圈都比上一圈高一層。每個 PDCA 循環結束後，標準提升了，下一個循環從更高的起點開始。

藥廠的持續改善機制

  • APR/PQR（年度產品品質審查）：系統性識別產品和製程改善機會
  • Lean / Six Sigma：製程效率改善（減少浪費、降低變異）
  • 知識管理（ICH Q10）：累積製程理解，持續縮小設計空間的不確定性
  • Benchmarking：對標行業最佳實踐，設定改善目標

ISO 9001 vs. Pharma GMP: Requirements Comparison — 標準要求深度比較

Where ISO 9001 sets the floor and GMP raises the bar

理解對照表的用途

ISO 9001 是品質管理的通用基礎框架——適用於任何行業、任何規模的組織。製藥 GMP 在這個基礎上疊加了行業特有的強制要求，反映藥品對患者安全的特殊責任。理解這兩個層次有助於藥廠人員知道「我們做的，遠超 ISO 9001 要求」。

★

Key Takeaways — 核心要點總結

Clauses 8–10 in context: the operational heart of ISO 9001

Structure Summary: Clauses 8–10

Clause 8 (Operation) is the largest and most operationally detailed clause — it covers the complete production value chain from customer requirements through to nonconforming output control. Key themes:

  • 8.3: Review / Verification / Validation are distinct — each has a unique purpose and cannot substitute for each other
  • 8.4: Supplier control must be risk-proportionate — the higher the impact, the deeper the control
  • 8.5.1f: "Special processes" (where output cannot be verified by subsequent monitoring) must be validated — this is the theoretical basis for sterilization validation and media fill in pharma
  • 8.7: Four disposal actions for nonconforming outputs — correction, segregation/suspension, informing customer, concession

Clause 9 (Performance Evaluation) closes the loop: measure → analyze → audit → management review. All feeds into the improvement cycle.

Clause 10 (Improvement): The critical distinction is Correction (fix the symptom immediately) vs. Corrective Action (eliminate the root cause permanently). Both are needed. CAPA effectiveness verification (10.2.1d) is what separates a real CAPA system from paperwork.

藥廠人員的核心學習點

ISO 9001 是地板，GMP 是天花板

對製藥從業者而言，ISO 9001 的要求在多數情況下是你們已經在做的事的最低版本。理解 ISO 9001 的邏輯框架，有助於：

  • 向非藥廠人員解釋你們的品質系統
  • 評估新供應商的品質成熟度（他們可能只有 ISO 9001 認證）
  • 準備 ISO 9001 審查，理解 GMP 的額外要求從何而來

三個「必記」的關鍵概念

  1. Review ≠ Verification ≠ Validation：三個不同目的，不能互相取代（8.3.4）
  2. Correction ≠ Corrective Action：一個消除症狀，一個消除根因（10.2.1）
  3. 特殊製程需要確效：因為無法靠後續量測驗證——滅菌、無菌灌裝的法源（8.5.1f）

給 CDMO Operations Director 的視角

Clauses 8–10 描述的整個循環——計畫執行 → 評估績效 → 持續改善——正是營運卓越（Operational Excellence）的 DNA。ISO 9001 提供了框架，GMP 填入了藥廠的具體要求。作為 COO 軌道的領導者，要把這個框架從「合規工具」提升到「競爭優勢」：一個運作良好的 QMS 不只是通過查廠，而是真正減少偏差、提升 OEE、縮短放行週期——這些都直接影響 CDMO 的競爭力和客戶信心。
