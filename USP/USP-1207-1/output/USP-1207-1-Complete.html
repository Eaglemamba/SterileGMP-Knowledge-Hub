Section 1 — Introduction 導論

原文內容 Original Text

Package Integrity Evaluation—Sterile Products 〈1207〉 provides guidance in the integrity assurance of product packages. This chapter describes package integrity verification during three product life cycle phases: 1) package development, and package processing and assembly validation; 2) product manufacturing; and 3) commercial product shelf-life stability assessments. Further, this chapter provides information on how to select, develop, and validate leak test methods.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

USP〈1207〉系列包含三個子章節：〈1207.1〉方法選擇、〈1207.2〉洩漏技術、〈1207.3〉密封品質技術。這三章合在一起構成 USP 對 CCI（Container Closure Integrity）的完整指引。

本章的核心訴求：包裝完整性驗證必須貫穿產品整個生命週期，而非僅在上市前完成一次。

比喻說明 Analogy

想像你買了一輛新車。CCI 測試就像汽車的防水測試：不只是出廠時測一次，而是在設計階段（研發）、生產線上（製造）、以及長時間存放後（穩定性）都要確認「密封無虞」。藥品包裝也是同樣邏輯。

Section 2 — Package Integrity Testing in the Product Life Cycle 產品生命週期中的包裝完整性測試

2.1 Development and Validation — Package Development

Package development begins with the preparation of a product–package profile (e.g., user requirements specification), which considers the product end use, stability requirements, and method of manufacture, as well as the anticipated storage, shipment, and distribution environments. This profile also defines the proper product–package quality requirements and the package's maximum allowable leakage limit.

Assurance of package integrity originates from the use of appropriate materials, accurate and optimum closing properties, matching dimensional fit, appropriate multi-component stack heights and tolerances, and consistent control of processes used to assemble the closed package.

A battery of integrity and seal quality test methods may be employed during product–package development, starting with techniques able to measure the product's inherent package integrity. Inherent package integrity of a viable package system conforms to the product's maximum allowable leakage limit.

Test package quantities for inherent package integrity verification may vary on the basis of: 1) the complexity of the product–package, 2) the specifics of the user specification requirements, and 3) the prior experience of the producer.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析：產品-包裝概況 (Product-Package Profile)

開發階段的關鍵輸出是「使用者需求規格書 (URS)」，必須涵蓋：

  • 產品用途與穩定性需求
  • 預期的儲存、運輸、配送環境
  • 最大允許洩漏限度 (MALL)——這是整個 CCI 策略的核心數字
  • 關鍵物理屬性與尺寸公差

重點提示 Key Notes

固有包裝完整性 (Inherent Package Integrity) 的概念非常重要：它指的是在沒有任何缺陷的情況下，包裝系統本身就能達到的完整性水準。這是設定 MALL 的基礎。

USP 建議使用空瓶或安慰劑填充的容器進行大樣本量的固有完整性測試，以避免產品配方干擾測試方法。

2.1.2 Package Processing and Assembly Validation

Final confirmation of acceptable inherent package integrity is generally part of a larger process validation activity for the overall production process. The scope depends upon the product type and whether the organization has previous experience with the container–closure system.

All processes germane to the sterilization and formation of a package having integrity are to be evaluated against the user requirement specifications, including likely process extremes. For example, inherent package integrity verification may consider extremes of package assembly variables such as line speed, heat-sealing temperature, screw-cap application torque, and vial-capping forces.

Successful validation will result in a package that meets its user requirement specifications. The primary objective is to arrive at a quality product–package prepared using processes that reliably and consistently run within specified operating parameters, yielding critical package defects at a satisfactorily low rate. When performed, in-process and end-product package integrity testing should complement, not replace, thorough package development efforts.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析：組裝驗證的關鍵參數

包裝組裝驗證必須覆蓋製程極端值，不能只測最佳條件。例如玻璃瓶軋蓋驗證必須包含：

  • 最低/最高軋蓋力 (vial-capping forces)
  • 最低/最高線速 (line speed)
  • 重新滅菌後的完整性
  • 二次與三次包裝製程的影響

比喻說明 Analogy

包裝組裝驗證就像汽車的碰撞測試：不只測試正常行駛，而是要在最惡劣的撞擊條件下（製程極端值）確認安全性（包裝完整性）。通過極端值測試，才能對日常生產的包裝有信心。

重點提示 Key Notes

USP 明確指出：製程內與成品的包裝完整性測試是「補充」而非「取代」充分的包裝開發工作。這與 FDA 的觀點一致——品質不是靠測試測出來的，而是設計進去的。

2.2 Product Manufacturing

To ensure the quality of the manufactured product–package, it is critical to specify components of sufficient quality and to select vendors carefully. The factors to consider include:

  • Acceptable results of the initial vendor or supplier evaluation
  • Appropriate vendor acceptance quality limits and statistical sampling plan(s), or relevant certification
  • Incoming component quality verification, including statistical assessment of quality against purchase specifications
  • Appropriate procedure(s) for establishing corrective and preventive action when a vendor falls short of quality expectations

Package integrity re-evaluation is considered when changes occur in the product, package design, package materials, or manufacturing/processing conditions. The extent of change control efforts are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

The proper combination of package leak tests, complementary package seal quality tests, and visual inspection is largely based on package failure results observed in earlier production validation and package development studies.

For example, glass or plastic ampules closed by heat fusion are customarily subjected to 100% nondestructive leak testing.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析：製造階段的品質策略

製造階段的 CCI 控制策略包含三個層次：

  • 供應商管理：進料品質的統計抽樣與驗收
  • 製程監控：結合洩漏測試、密封品質測試、目視檢查
  • 變更控制：任何產品、包裝設計或製程的變更都需重新評估 CCI

重點提示：100% 非破壞性測試

玻璃安瓿（熔封）是 100% 非破壞性洩漏測試的典型要求。這在法規上有依據——FDA 的無菌製造指引要求安瓿進行 100% 完整性測試。在 fill-finish 設施設計時，這是產線設備配置的重要考量。

實務應用 Practical Application

在 CDMO 的 aseptic fill-finish 生產中，當客戶提出包裝材料的供應商替代時，必須評估 CCI 影響。USP〈1207.1〉第 2.2 節提供了清單：不只是供應商評估，還需要對新組件的進料品質進行統計評估，並更新 CAPA 程序。

2.3 Commercial Product Stability

Container–closure integrity tests have been recommended as alternatives to sterility testing as part of commercial product stability programs. The goal is to ensure package integrity as a function of long-term product storage. [Note—Package integrity tests do not replace product release sterility tests.]

Testing product for sterility is a poor measure of product–package integrity and also will not ensure product–package integrity over the shelf life of the product when performed as part of the stability program. Validated package integrity test methods are more sensitive and reliable than product lot sterility testing for detecting a breach in package integrity.

Ideally, package integrity tests selected to support marketed-product stability studies are able to verify the absence of the smallest leaks of concern for a given product–package system. In other words, the product's maximum allowable leakage limit falls within the chosen test method leak detection range.

Stability test samples intended for package integrity evaluation are kept at labeled storage conditions for the marketed product. If an integrity test is nondestructive to the product or package, samples that pass package integrity testing may be further used in the stability testing for that specific test period or interval.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析：CCI 取代穩定性無菌試驗

FDA 2008 年發布的指引允許以 CCI 測試替代穩定性方案中的無菌試驗。理由是：

  • 無菌試驗樣本量太小，統計代表性不足
  • 無菌試驗只能偵測已經污染的樣本，無法預防性確保整個貨架期的完整性
  • CCI 測試（如真空衰減、雷射氣體分析）對微小洩漏更靈敏

比喻說明 Analogy

穩定性無菌試驗就像每年只做一次血液檢查來確認自己有沒有生病——樣本量小、時間點有限。CCI 測試則像是持續監測體溫——更能反映整個儲存期間容器是否保持「密封無虞」的狀態。

重點提示 Key Notes

非破壞性 CCI 測試通過的樣品可繼續用於當期穩定性測試，但不可在一個時間點（如 12 個月）測試後，再存放到下一個時間點（如 24 個月）使用。每個穩定性時間點必須有獨立的樣本組。

Section 3 — Test Method Selection Criteria 測試方法選擇標準

3.1–3.4 Package Contents, Design, Materials & Leakage Limit

No single package leak test or package seal quality test method is applicable to all product–package systems. Test method selection is made on a product–package on a case-by-case basis.

3.1 Package Contents: Whether the package contains a liquid or solid formulation, and whether it contains a headspace of inert gas, air, vacuum, or even no headspace at all, will influence leak test method choice. For example, if testing liquid-filled packages by vacuum decay or mass extraction, test vacuum conditions may trigger some formulations to solidify inside leak paths.

3.2 Package Design:

  • Rigid or Flexible: Rigid packages can tolerate pressure/vacuum-challenge conditions. Flexible packages may need special tooling to restrict expansion.
  • Moveable Components: Syringe stoppers may require restraint to prevent dislocation during differential pressure tests.
  • Polymeric Materials: Plastics and elastomers may outgas volatiles under vacuum, falsely implicating leakage.
  • Metallic Materials: Foil laminates may be incompatible with electrical conductivity tests.
  • Transparent/Opaque: Transparent materials allow laser-based headspace analysis; opaque packages are incompatible with visual inspection-based methods.

3.4 Maximum Allowable Leakage Limit (MALL):

  • 3.4.1: Sterility & formulation preserved; no headspace requirement → tracer gas (vacuum mode), laser-based headspace analysis
  • 3.4.2: Headspace content must also be preserved → laser-based gas headspace analysis
  • 3.4.3: Multiple-dose packages with reseal requirement → physicochemical + microbiological methods

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析：MALL 是方法選擇的核心

最大允許洩漏限度 (MALL) 決定了你需要的測試靈敏度。簡單邏輯：

  • MALL 越小（越嚴格）→ 需要更靈敏的測試方法（偵測限要更低）
  • 產品類別不同 → MALL 設定不同 → 方法選擇不同
  • 液態注射劑（需防菌入侵）vs. 凍乾劑（需保護真空頭空間）有根本上不同的需求

重點提示：材料相容性是第一道篩選

在選方法之前，先問「這個方法與包裝材料相容嗎？」

  • 塑料/彈性體 + 真空衰減 = 可能有 outgassing 干擾
  • 鋁箔層壓 + HVLD = 不相容（鋁導電）
  • 不透明容器 + 雷射分析 = 不相容
  • 含溶液產品 + 壓力衰減 = 需注意液體覆蓋洩漏點

比喻說明 Analogy

選擇 CCI 測試方法就像選擇醫學影像技術：X 光（快速便宜但限制多）、超音波（適合軟組織）、MRI（精細但昂貴慢）。沒有一種方法適用所有情況——必須根據「要找什麼、在哪裡找」來選擇。

實務應用 Practical Application

CDMO 接到新產品時的 CCI 方法選擇流程：(1) 確認產品類別（液態/凍乾/固態）→ (2) 確認容器材質（玻璃/塑料/鋁箔）→ (3) 確認頭空間需求 → (4) 查 MALL → (5) 比對各技術的偵測限（見〈1207.2〉Table 1）→ (6) 確認方法是否可上線或只能離線。

3.5–3.8 Deterministic vs. Probabilistic; Outcome; Quantitative vs. Qualitative

3.5 Deterministic Methods: A "deterministic leak test method" is one in which the leakage event is based on phenomena that follow a predictable chain of events, and leakage is measured using physicochemical technologies that are readily controlled and monitored, yielding objective quantitative data. Examples: tracer gas (vacuum mode), laser-based gas headspace analysis, pressure decay, vacuum decay, mass extraction.

A "probabilistic leak test method" relies on a series of sequential and/or simultaneous events each associated with uncertainties, yielding random outcomes described by probability distributions. Thus, the findings are associated with uncertainties that necessitate larger sample sizes. Examples: microbial challenge tests, bubble emission tests, tracer liquid tests, tracer gas sniffer probe.

A deterministic leak test method having the ability to detect leaks at the product's maximum allowable leakage limit is preferred when establishing the inherent integrity of a container–closure system.

3.7 Method Outcome — desired outcomes may include:

  • Leak path presence detection
  • Leak path location determination
  • Leakage rate measurement
  • Liquid egress/ingress potential
  • Microbial ingress potential

3.8: When method considerations permit, leak test methods that yield quantitative measurements are preferred over qualitative ones.

核心概念解析：確定性 vs. 機率性

這是 USP〈1207〉最重要的分類概念：

重點提示：FDA/USP 的監管立場

USP 明確表明：能偵測到 MALL 的確定性方法為首選。這與 FDA 2016 年後對 CCI 的監管趨勢一致——越來越多的 FDA 警告信點名企業使用機率性方法（如染料侵入法）而未進行充分驗證。

比喻說明 Analogy

確定性方法像是數位體重計——每次測量值都一樣，可以精確記錄是否超重（超過 MALL）。機率性方法像是問路人「這個人看起來超重嗎？」——答案依賴觀察者判斷，樣本越多才越準確。

3.10 Leak Test Method Range: All leak test methods have an optimum detection range. Therefore, it is possible that additional tests may be needed to catch those leaks of concern that fall outside the chosen leak test method's detection range.

3.11 Nondestructive or Destructive: Only nondestructive test methods are appropriate for leak testing product–package units earmarked for commercial or clinical study distribution.

3.12 Off-Line or On-Line: On-line testing can potentially provide greater assurance that all packages have integrity and can yield instant feedback in the event of package misassembly or breakage, enabling real-time line corrections.

核心概念解析：偵測限 vs. 偵測範圍

偵測限（Detection Limit）：方法能可靠偵測的最小洩漏。

偵測範圍（Detection Range）：方法的最小到最大偵測能力。注意：能偵測小洩漏的方法，不一定能偵測非常大的漏洞（如缺失組件）——可能需要視覺系統補充。

重點提示：On-line vs. Off-line 的取捨

On-line 測試的優點：100% 批次覆蓋、即時回饋。缺點：高線速→測試周期短→偵測靈敏度下降。Off-line 測試：只能抽樣，但靈敏度更高。這不是「哪個更好」，而是互補——填充線越複雜，越需要組合策略。

實務應用 Practical Application

在無菌灌裝線設計時，常見的組合策略：On-line HVLD（快速，Row 3 偵測）用於 100% 篩查 + Off-line 真空衰減（慢速，更靈敏）用於穩定性研究樣本。兩個方法互補，覆蓋從粗大缺陷到微小洩漏的完整範圍。

Section 4 — Test Instrument Qualification, Method Development, and Method Validation 測試儀器資格確認、方法開發與方法驗證

4.1–4.2 Instrumentation Qualification and Method Validation Parameters

4.1 Instrumentation and Equipment Qualification: includes: 1) evaluation of instrument/equipment functionality, and 2) determination of test system detection capabilities using appropriate calibration tools or reference standards to simulate with-leak test conditions.

4.2 Method Development and Validation — The following properties of a valid test method are defined:

4.2.1 Accuracy: a measure of the method's ability to correctly differentiate packages that leak above the claimed detection limit from those that leak below this limit (do not leak). Accuracy provides a measure of false positive and false negative occurrence.

4.2.2 Precision: the ability of the method to yield reliable, repeatable data. Includes repeatability (same instrument, same day), ruggedness/intermediate precision (multiple operators, multiple days, multiple instruments), and reproducibility (among laboratories).

4.2.3 Specificity: the ability to accurately differentiate between leaking and nonleaking packages despite interfering factors that may cause false detection. For example, helium permeation through the package wall may mask small leaks or be falsely interpreted as leakage.

4.2.4 Detection Limit: demonstrated by challenging packages with and without known defects by the leak test method for multiple test days by multiple operators.

Example of detection limit expression: "The detection limit for method X was determined to be 5 ± 2 µm. Validation studies found defects of this nominal size were detected 95% of the time; all larger defects were detected 100% of the time. Studies included three replicate test series performed on multiple days by multiple operators in a single laboratory using one instrument."

4.2.8 Robustness: the method's ability to accurately identify leaking versus nonleaking packages despite small but deliberate variations in procedural parameters.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析：CCI 方法驗證的八要素

對應 ICH Q2(R1) 分析方法驗證，CCI 方法驗證的八個要素：

  • 準確性 (Accuracy)：正確區分洩漏/不洩漏的能力 → 衡量假陽性與假陰性率
  • 精密性 (Precision)：重複測試的一致性（含重複性、中間精密性、再現性）
  • 專一性 (Specificity)：排除干擾因素的能力
  • 偵測限 (Detection Limit)：最小可偵測洩漏
  • 定量限 (Quantitation Limit)：最小可定量洩漏速率
  • 線性 (Linearity)：結果與洩漏大小成正比的能力
  • 範圍 (Range)：偵測範圍的上下邊界
  • 穩健性 (Robustness)：方法在小偏差下仍可靠的能力

比喻說明 Analogy

CCI 方法驗證就像校準一把尺：準確性 = 這把尺量出來的 10cm 是真的 10cm；精密性 = 同一個人量三次都一樣；專一性 = 溫度變化不會讓尺變長；偵測限 = 這把尺能分辨到 0.1mm 還是 1mm？每個要素都是確保測量可靠性的一塊拼圖。

重點提示：假陰性比假陽性更危險

在 CCI 驗證中，假陰性（漏掉洩漏）的風險遠高於假陽性（錯判洩漏）。FDA 的 CCI 相關警告信中，最常見的批評是方法無法可靠地偵測到接近 MALL 的小洩漏。驗證時應重點確保：在 MALL 附近的缺陷被偵測的概率達到可接受水準（通常 ≥ 95%）。

4.3–4.5 System Suitability, Microbial Ingress Risk, Negative and Positive Controls

4.3 System Suitability: a manner of ensuring that the leak test method, including all factors that may be subject to variability, is adequately controlled. System suitability is important for all leak test methods. Extra care may be required for probabilistic methods more prone to variability.

4.4 Microbial Ingress Risk Comparison: An experimental comparison of physicochemical leak test to microbiological challenge data may NOT be needed when:

  • The validated physicochemical method has a proven detection limit at or below the product-package MALL.
  • The method is used to find leaks notably larger than the MALL (e.g., for damaged package rejection).

Comparison study MAY be useful when the method's limit of detection is notably greater than the MALL, or when the MALL is lacking or not well defined.

4.5 Negative and Positive Controls: "Negative controls" are packages with no known leak, and "positive controls" are packages with intentional or known leaks. A "master" is a type of negative control — a no-leaking mock package used in system suitability verification.

4.5.1 Defect Creation Methods:

  • Laser drilling: creates defects as small as 2–3 µm in rigid glass or plastic; not ideal (non-cylindrical, asymmetric), but simulates actual defects
  • Glass micropipettes: simulate single-orifice defects as small as ~0.1 µm
  • Microtubes (microcapillaries): can be as narrow as 2 µm; good for channel defects
  • Needle insertion, wire/microfilament between sealing surfaces, holed metal plate: useful for larger defects in feasibility studies

4.5.2 Type Defects — realistic package flaws:

  • Heat sealed bag: weak seal, wrinkled seal, seal gap, seal channel, product entrapment in seal
  • Stoppered vial: vial finish channel defect, loosely capped stopper, product trapped between stopper and vial
  • Prefilled syringe: needle shield punctured, defective plunger
  • Ophthalmic dropper bottle: loose cap, missing/poorly inserted dropper tip

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析：陽性對照品設計的層次

USP 要求陽性對照品必須覆蓋完整的偵測範圍：

  • 第一層：接近偵測限的小缺陷（最關鍵）→ 確認方法在邊界能否偵測
  • 第二層：括弧式缺陷（稍大和稍小於偵測限）→ 建立偵測機率曲線
  • 第三層：大缺陷（確認方法上限）→ 確保大缺陷不會「miss」
  • 類型缺陷 (Type Defects)：模擬真實生產中可能出現的缺陷型態

重點提示：激光鑽孔的局限性

激光鑽出的「孔洞」並非完美的圓形小孔，而是非對稱的微裂紋矩陣。因此：

  • 不能直接用幾何公式計算洩漏率
  • 需要通過「與標準孔板的氣體洩漏率比較」來定名直徑
  • 優點是接近真實缺陷形態（如裂縫）

比喻說明 Analogy

系統適用性 (System Suitability) 就像每天早上上班前校準辦公室的體重計。在開始一批測試之前，先用已知的陰性/陽性對照品確認儀器今天是否正常運作——這是保障每批測試數據可信度的例行動作。

實務應用 Practical Application

在 aseptic vial filling 的 CCI 驗證中，類型缺陷 (Type Defects) 的設計應包含：(1) 瓶口通道缺陷（模擬瓶口缺角）→ 最常見的真實生產缺陷；(2) 膠塞未完全插入（loosely capped stopper）→ 模擬軋蓋失敗；(3) 產品夾在膠塞與瓶口之間（product trapped）→ 模擬灌裝過量。這三類缺陷若無法被選定的 CCI 方法偵測，需要補充其他監控措施。
