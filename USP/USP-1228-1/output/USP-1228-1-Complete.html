Introduction 簡介

原文內容 Original Text

〈1228.1〉 DRY HEAT DEPYROGENATION

INTRODUCTION

Dry heat is the method most frequently used for the depyrogenation of heat stable materials. Dry heat depyrogenation is dependent upon two parameters: time and temperature, which equates to a thermal input. As a result, dry heat depyrogenation processes can be easily monitored/controlled and are highly reproducible. Depyrogenation processes typically operate at a range of temperatures from approximately 170° up to about 400°.

The most prevalent pyrogenic agents in parenteral manufacturing that are of concern relative to patient safety are bacterial endotoxins, found in the outer cell walls of Gram-negative bacteria. The destruction of bacterial endotoxins (depyrogenation) by dry heat has been studied extensively and has been shown to follow first order kinetics. The well-defined kinetics of inactivation makes it possible to predict the efficacy of dry heat processes operating at different times and temperatures by understanding the total thermal input (FH).

The range of temperatures used for dry heat depyrogenation overlaps the upper range of temperatures used for dry heat sterilization (see Dry Heat Sterilization 〈1229.8〉). This is because bacterial endotoxins are more resistant to the effects of dry heat than the most heat-resistant bacterial spores. This chapter provides an overview of the process of dry heat depyrogenation, its control, and validation.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 — 去熱原 vs 滅菌

Depyrogenation (去熱原)：從物品表面移除或破壞熱原 (Pyrogens)，特別是細菌內毒素 (Bacterial Endotoxins)，使其降至對病人安全的水準。

Sterilization (滅菌)：殺滅或去除所有微生物（包括孢子）的製程。

關鍵差異：細菌內毒素比最耐熱的細菌孢子更能抵抗乾熱，因此去熱原製程需要更高的溫度，或更長的時間，或兩者兼具。

比喻說明 — 為什麼內毒素更難對付？

想像一下，細菌孢子像是穿了防護衣的士兵，用高溫就能消滅；而細菌內毒素就像是士兵戰死後留下的「防護盔甲碎片」——即使士兵已死，這些碎片（內毒素）仍然可以造成人體的發燒反應。去熱原就是要把這些「殘留盔甲碎片」也一併破壞掉，需要更猛烈的熱能攻勢。

重點提示 — 乾熱去熱原的兩個控制參數

USP 明確指出乾熱去熱原只依賴兩個參數：時間 (Time) 與溫度 (Temperature)。這樣簡單的製程控制邏輯，使其具有：

  • 高度可重現性 (Reproducibility)
  • 易於監控與管控
  • 透過數學模型預測效能

這也是為何乾熱去熱原是最普遍使用的方法。

原文內容 Original Text

TECHNOLOGIES USED FOR DEPYROGENATION BY DRY HEAT

Although all dry heat depyrogenation processes rely strictly on time of exposure and temperature to assure effectiveness, the equipment used typically falls into two categories: the dry heat "batch" oven and continuous tunnel systems. Batch ovens are routinely used for the depyrogenation of product containers, most often glass, but also other heat stable product contact parts or laboratory equipment. Continuous tunnels, on the other hand, are used primarily to depyrogenate glass product containers.

Batch Ovens

Circulating heated air is used to heat the load items, which may be individually covered or wrapped in a material that is unaffected by the temperature used, or placed in a lidded container for protection during pre- and post-process handling. When depyrogenation and sterilization are to be achieved in the same process, air supplied to the oven is passed through one or more high efficiency particulate air (HEPA) filter(s) to maintain sterility within the oven after completion of the dwell period. These forced air ovens typically operate at a positive air pressure differential relative to the surrounding room. This design results in particulate air quality that can meet ISO 5 requirements to reduce particulate matter and microbial contamination risk throughout processing.

In order to ensure sufficient lethality and process control, oven control probe(s) must maintain a predefined temperature for a predefined time period prior to cooling. The limited heat transfer capacity of air requires that items in the oven be placed in fixed locations confirmed acceptable during the cycle development/validation effort. Caution should be exercised in defining variable load patterns as minimum load sizes may result in inadvertent slower heating of the load and greater temperature variability. Smaller facilities may use a single door oven, but the principles of operation and validation are the same as with larger double door production units. The important batch oven process variables are set-point temperature, duration of dwell period, load type and configuration, airflow characteristics, and container size.

Continuous Tunnels

The use of tunnels for dry heat depyrogenation of glass containers on a moving conveyor allows for substantially higher throughput and packing densities than the batch process, reduces handling, and is ordinarily integrated with a washing and filling system. Tunnels typically use forced heated air systems or radiant IR systems that recirculate air through a battery of HEPA filters. Load items in tunnels are typically fed directly from an integrated container washing system.

Depyrogenation tunnels have separate heating zones and cooling zones, allowing for continuous in-feed and discharge at temperatures appropriate for production purposes. The tunnel is maintained at constant airflow and temperature conditions during use, and as glass passes through the tunnel it is heated to depyrogenating temperatures and cooled before exiting. Although the conditions within the tunnel are essentially constant and well controlled, the temperature of the glass as it passes through the tunnel on the conveyor will change with its location. Dwell time is controlled by adjusting the conveyor speed, which in the depyrogenation tunnel is the process parameter that governs exposure time.

The air in the tunnel is most commonly heated using electrical coils but other heat sources, such as infrared or high-pressure steam, have been used. For energy conservation, heated air in depyrogenation tunnels is often recirculated. The important continuous tunnel process variables are heating zone temperature, cooling zone temperature, belt speed, and container mass per unit.

Note: Not all tunnels have a variable speed capacity.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 — 兩種設備類型比較

USP 將乾熱去熱原設備分為兩大類：

  • 批次烘箱 (Batch Oven)：適用於多種物品（玻璃容器、不鏽鋼零件、實驗室器具等），彈性高但產量低。
  • 連續隧道 (Continuous Tunnel)：專門針對玻璃容器的高速去熱原，通常與清洗及充填系統整合，是現代無菌充填生產線的核心設備。

比喻說明 — 批次烘箱 vs 連續隧道

批次烘箱就像家庭用烤箱：放入食物、設定溫度和時間、等待完成、取出。彈性高，但一次只能處理一批次，效率有限。

連續隧道則像工廠的輸送帶烤爐（例如麵包工廠的隧道烤箱）：麵包連續進入、加熱、冷卻、出來，產量極大，且與前後製程無縫整合。對 CDMO 而言，高速充填線必然採用連續隧道。

重點提示 — 批次烘箱的五大製程變數

USP 明確列出批次烘箱的關鍵製程參數 (Critical Process Parameters, CPPs)，這五項都必須在驗證中受控：

  • 設定溫度 (Set-point temperature)
  • 保溫時間 (Duration of dwell period)
  • 負載類型與配置 (Load type and configuration)
  • 氣流特性 (Airflow characteristics)
  • 容器尺寸 (Container size)

特別注意：最小負載量可能導致加熱速率不均，造成更大的溫度變異性。

核心概念解析 — 連續隧道的分區設計

連續隧道有三個功能區：

  • 加熱區 (Heating Zone)：玻璃升溫至去熱原溫度
  • 去熱原區 (Depyrogenation Zone)：維持足夠溫度與時間
  • 冷卻區 (Cooling Zone)：玻璃在出口前降溫至適合充填的溫度

連續隧道中，輸送帶速度是控制曝露時間（停留時間）的關鍵製程參數，相當於批次烘箱中的保溫時間設定。

重點提示 — HEPA 過濾與 ISO 5 等級

當烘箱同時進行去熱原與滅菌時，需透過 HEPA 過濾器維持烘箱內的無菌狀態，並維持正壓差 (Positive Pressure Differential)。這樣的設計讓烘箱內的空氣品質可達 ISO 5（相當於 Class 100）等級，大幅降低微粒與微生物污染風險。

這是滅菌與去熱原同步執行的設備設計要點，在法規審查中必須明確說明。

實務應用 — CDMO 設備選型考量

對於 CDMO 而言，去熱原設備選型需考量：

  • 產品多樣性：若承接多種容器類型（小瓶、安瓿、注射器），批次烘箱彈性更高
  • 產能需求：大批量商業化生產線，連續隧道是標準配置
  • 整合程度：連續隧道可與洗瓶機、充填線整合，減少人員介入降低污染風險
  • 驗證資源：連續隧道的驗證通常更複雜，需要更多的感溫器和溫度分佈研究

原文內容 Original Text

DRY HEAT DEPYROGENATION FUNDAMENTALS

Dry heat depyrogenation uses air first to heat and then to cool the items. The limited heat capacity of dry air results in relatively slow heating and cooling of the load items. Variability in temperature distribution in dry heat ovens and tunnels is typically much higher than that observed in moist heat systems. The limited heat capacity of air requires that items in ovens be placed in the same locations as confirmed acceptable in the cycle development/validation effort. Packing and thermal mass will also play critical roles in temperature management.

Caution must also be exercised with varying load mass and distribution as in some instances (resulting from oven design, airflow characteristics, and control probe position) minimum load sizes may result in process variability.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 — 乾熱 vs 濕熱的溫度分佈差異

乾熱與濕熱（蒸汽滅菌）的最大差異在於熱傳導介質的熱容量：

  • 飽和蒸汽：潛熱 (Latent Heat) 大，凝結時釋放大量熱量，溫度均勻性極佳（通常 ±0.5°C 以內）
  • 乾熱空氣：熱容量低，對流傳熱效率差，溫度分佈變異可達 ±15°C 或更大

這就是為什麼乾熱製程對裝載位置、熱質量 (Thermal Mass) 和氣流特性的要求特別嚴格。

比喻說明 — 熱容量差異的生活比喻

把手放進 100°C 的烤箱（乾熱），你可以維持幾秒不燙傷；但把手放進 100°C 的蒸汽，瞬間就嚴重燙傷。這說明蒸汽的熱傳導能力遠遠優於乾熱空氣——蒸汽凝結時釋放的潛熱讓熱能快速、均勻地傳遞到物品。乾熱空氣的熱傳導就像吹熱風，效果慢且不均勻。

重點提示 — 最小負載量的矛盾

USP 特別警告：最小負載量反而可能造成製程變異。這初看起來有點違反直覺，原因是：

  • 負載量少時，熱質量小，烘箱的氣流和溫控探針的位置影響更顯著
  • 空腔（無物品的空間）氣流特性與滿載時不同，可能造成局部過熱或冷點
  • 因此驗證時必須針對最大與最小負載量分別進行

Depyrogenation Process Control 去熱原製程控制

原文內容 Original Text

DEPYROGENATION PROCESS CONTROL

Process temperatures in dry heat depyrogenation are controlled by calibrated temperature sensors placed at specific locations within the equipment. The exposure portion of the process is designed to attain a minimum dwell time at a predefined minimum temperature ensuring that depyrogenation conditions are adequately uniform. The defined dwell time is determined by using measurement devices (e.g., thermocouples) directly in contact with the items to be depyrogenated during development. The inactivation of bacterial endotoxins by dry heat involves the control of only two parameters: time and temperature.

The simplicity of process control for these parameters provides a predictable depyrogenation effect. Once validated, a clear understanding of the inputs to the system, meaning resident endotoxin load on incoming materials, and outputs of the system, meaning reduction of resident levels of endotoxin to safe levels, is more meaningful than spiking with unnaturally high levels of challenge material and looking for a prescriptive requirement for log reduction. The dosimetric measurement for dry heat depyrogenation processes is the FH unit. An FH = 1 is defined as the depyrogenation effect achieved by 1 min of heating at 250°. The FH-value enables the integration of temperature over the process duration (time). By convention, the rate at which depyrogenation destruction rate (D-value) varies as a function of temperature change is defined as the z-value. The z-value for dry heat depyrogenation has been shown to be in the range of 45°–55°. For the purposes of this chapter, 50° is used as a standard z-value. Other values may be used. (1,2)

The FH approach is used as a means to compare dry heat depyrogenation effects produced by processes that operate at varying temperatures. Basic mathematics can be used to calculate the depyrogenation effect produced at temperatures other than 250° to determine equivalence to that provided at 250°.

Using a reference temperature of 250° and an assumed z-value of 50°, the FH calculation can be determined:

  • FH = accumulated destruction
  • t0 = process start time
  • tf = process end time
  • T = temperature at each time increment
  • Δt = time interval between temperature measurements

Summing the instantaneous temperature contributions over the entire depyrogenation process allows for the calculation of the overall process efficacy or FH delivered over the course of the process. Many commercial data loggers are equipped with software that enables them to make this calculation and integrate the total FH accumulated during a process. The FH calculation is used during initial validation, validation maintenance, and change control. The mathematical principles of the FH calculation are essentially the same as those used to calculate lethality (F0) values in moist heat sterilization. FH values are used to confirm process consistency over time as correlation to endotoxin destruction is rarely possible.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 — FH 值定義

FH 值 (Depyrogenation Value)：乾熱去熱原的劑量計量單位，表示製程累積的去熱原效果。

  • 參考溫度：250°C
  • 定義：FH = 1 相當於在 250°C 加熱 1 分鐘的去熱原效果
  • z 值：50°C（標準值，代表 D 值改變 10 倍所需的溫度變化量）

這與濕熱滅菌的 F0 值（參考溫度 121°C，z = 10°C）概念完全平行，只是應用對象從細菌孢子改為細菌內毒素。

公式與計算 — FH 計算公式

F_H = Σ [ 10^((T - 250) / 50) × Δt ]

其中：
  T   = 每個時間點的測量溫度（°C）
  Δt  = 相鄰溫度量測間的時間間隔（分鐘）
  250 = 參考溫度（°C）
  50  = 標準 z 值（°C）

範例計算：
  若在 300°C 加熱 1 分鐘：
  F_H = 10^((300-250)/50) × 1 = 10^1 = 10

  即在 300°C 加熱 1 分鐘 ≡ 在 250°C 加熱 10 分鐘
  （F_H = 10）

比喻說明 — FH vs F0

FH 和 F0 就像是兩種不同的「熱能貨幣」：

  • F0（濕熱滅菌）：以 121°C 為面額基準，z = 10°C，消滅細菌孢子
  • FH（乾熱去熱原）：以 250°C 為面額基準，z = 50°C，破壞細菌內毒素

兩者都是累積「熱能貢獻」的工具，讓你能比較不同溫度-時間組合的等效效果。z 值越大，表示溫度變化對效果影響較小（內毒素對溫度變化的敏感度比孢子低很多）。

重點提示 — USP 對「挑戰試驗」的重新定位

這一段是 USP 〈1228.1〉 的重要觀點轉變：傳統上驗證去熱原製程需要進行加標挑戰試驗 (Spike Challenge Test)，要求達到特定對數降低值（如 3-log reduction）。

但 USP 指出：一旦製程已驗證，了解來料的內毒素殘留量（輸入）並確認降至安全水準（輸出）比人工加標非自然高濃度的挑戰材料更有意義。

這種以「劑量學」(Dosimetric) 為基礎的方法，強調製程理解而非單純的挑戰合格，符合現代基於風險的驗證思維。

核心概念解析 — z 值的意義

z 值 (z-value)：使 D 值（滅活速率）改變 10 倍所需的溫度變化量（°C）。

  • 乾熱去熱原 z 值範圍：45°–55°C，標準使用 50°C
  • 濕熱滅菌（孢子）z 值：約 10°C

z 值越大，表示需要更大的溫度變化才能顯著影響破壞速率。細菌內毒素（z=50°C）對溫度的敏感度遠低於細菌孢子（z=10°C），因此在乾熱製程中，溫度略有波動對 FH 的影響相對較小。

  3. 若 z 值改為 45°C（比標準 50°C 更小），對同樣的溫度-時間組合，FH 計算結果會更大還是更小？請解釋原因及其驗證意涵。

實務應用 — FH 在 CDMO 驗證策略中的角色

在 CDMO 的乾熱去熱原驗證中，FH 扮演以下關鍵角色：

  • 初始驗證：建立製程的最低可接受 FH 值，作為驗證通過標準
  • 驗證維護：定期量測確認 FH 值在驗證範圍內，確保製程持續受控
  • 變更管控：設備維修、更換元件或製程參數調整後，重新計算並確認 FH 是否符合要求
  • 日常放行：每批次確認溫度與時間參數均符合驗證範圍，等同確認 FH 達標

本節重點回顧 Section Summary

  • 乾熱空氣熱容量有限，導致溫度分佈變異遠大於濕熱製程（可達 ±15°C），因此裝載位置固定、熱質量管理至關重要。
  • FH 值是乾熱去熱原的劑量計量單位，定義為 250°C 下加熱 1 分鐘的效果（FH=1），標準 z 值為 50°C。
  • FH 計算公式與濕熱滅菌的 F0 平行：透過積分溫度對時間的貢獻，比較不同溫度-時間組合的等效去熱原效果。
  • USP 強調製程理解（來料內毒素輸入與輸出安全水準）比人工加標挑戰試驗更具意義，體現現代劑量學驗證思維。

Validation 驗證

原文內容 Original Text

VALIDATION

Because dry heat depyrogenation is appropriate only for heat stable materials, a high margin for safety is always attainable. Times and temperatures used for the purpose of destroying challenge materials can result in extreme challenges to material integrity and stability. Attention to depyrogenation processes, including an understanding of the resident endotoxin load on incoming materials and reduction to levels needed to assure patient safety, should take place during drug product development, prior to validation.

Equipment Qualification

Equipment Qualification (EQ) is a predefined program that focuses on the processing equipment to confirm that it has been properly installed and operates as intended prior to evaluation of the process. In some companies, EQ may be separated into installation qualification (IQ) and operational qualification (OQ), or combined together under a joint terminology of installation/operational qualification (I/OQ). Equipment qualification provides a baseline for preventive maintenance and change control assuring reproducibility of equipment operation over time.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 — 設備確效 (EQ) 架構

乾熱去熱原設備確效通常分為以下階段：

  • IQ（安裝確效）：確認設備按設計規格安裝，文件記錄完整（型號、序號、公用設施連接、校準狀態）
  • OQ（運作確效）：確認設備在空載條件下按預期運作（溫控系統功能、警報響應、HEPA 完整性）
  • PQ（效能確效）：在實際生產條件下（含負載）確認設備能一致達到去熱原效果

EQ 為後續的預防性維護計畫和變更管控建立基準，是維持「驗證狀態」的根基。

比喻說明 — EQ 就像新車交車檢查

設備確效就像買新車的「交車檢查」流程：

  • IQ = 確認車輛規格與訂單相符（引擎排氣量、配備清單、VIN 碼）
  • OQ = 在停車場測試所有功能（冷氣、喇叭、雨刷、車窗）
  • PQ = 實際上路行駛，確認在各種路況下均達到預期效能

EQ 為維護保養（如定期換油 = 定期校準）和重大改裝（變更管控）建立基準。

重點提示 — 驗證前就要了解內毒素來源

USP 特別強調：對去熱原製程的關注（包括了解來料內毒素殘留量），應在藥品開發階段就開始，而非等到驗證才處理。這是「製程理解先於驗證」的現代 GMP 思維，與 ICH Q8 的「設計空間」概念一致。

若開發期間就建立清楚的內毒素輸入/輸出模型，驗證設計將更具科學依據，且更有機會採用「基於劑量學」的驗證策略。

Temperature Distribution & Component/Load Mapping 溫度分佈與元件/負載映射

原文內容 Original Text

Empty Chamber Temperature Distribution for Ovens

The oven should be evaluated for empty chamber temperature distribution. This is assessed by measurement of temperature at each corner of oven, near the controlling probe(s) and other locations as justified. Differences in the cycle dwell period can be discounted in this evaluation, as only the shortest dwell period need be evaluated. The evaluation is best performed over the last few minutes of the dwell period once the system has fully stabilized. The acceptance criteria for this test vary with the oven's design and operating conditions; however, temperature distribution is typically substantially less uniform than observed in autoclaves and may be ±15° or more. Depyrogenation ovens that are located at floor level may have even greater ranges in temperature. The temperature distribution measurement may be of value in the evaluation of changes to the oven.

Empty Temperature Distribution in Tunnels

While these studies are often done, they are actually of limited value. Unloaded depyrogenation tunnels will always produce far more variability in temperature distribution than will a fully loaded tunnel. Therefore, for depyrogenation tunnels, temperature studies under fully loaded conditions only are indicated. Important to the proper operation of the dry heat tunnel is the establishment of the required air flow balance between the tunnel and the adjoining areas. Improper air flow can cause uneven heating across the load being processed.

Component Mapping

The ability of dry heat to penetrate load items and to bring them to the required temperature should be determined. Load items that are complex, of significant mass, with enclosed volumes and product contact surfaces that must be depyrogenated, should be subjected to component mapping to determine internal cold spots. All load items should be prepared, wrapped (if that is the practice), and oriented in a manner consistent with how they will be processed. Glass typically enters the depyrogenation process (whether tunnel or oven) wet and must be evaluated wet to properly determine the effect on overall thermal input. Mapping of glass components to be processed in tunnels is not necessary; all monitoring of temperature in tunnels is accomplished with probes in contact with the bottom of the container.

Load Mapping in Ovens

Fixed loading patterns are necessary in oven depyrogenation because of the limited heat capacity of the air; fully packed conditions because of their greater mass ordinarily result in the best process temperature uniformity. Load mapping assures that items placed throughout the load attain the desired depyrogenation conditions. Identification of cold zones within the oven should be established during depyrogenation cycle development. Information from the load mapping is used to adjust cycle timing to assure appropriate efficacy across the entire load. It may be possible to validate maximum and minimum loads (as determined by either the number of items or their mass). FH is calculated from the temperature data at all monitored locations within the load pattern.

Load Mapping in Tunnels

Load mapping can be assessed using sets of calibrated sensors (i.e., trailing or wireless temperature sensors) positioned within the glass pack as it moves through the tunnel. Temperature sensors should be placed into direct contact with the glass item at the bottom of the container. Temperature measurements should be made on the leading edge, the middle (highest density), and the trailing edge of the glass pack across the width of the conveyor belt. There should be NLT 5 temperature sensors positioned across the belt in each section of the load. FH is calculated from the temperature data at all monitored locations. Studies should be performed using all container sizes to determine the lowest FH locations.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 — 三層溫度研究架構

USP 設計了清晰的三層溫度研究架構：

  • 空腔溫度分佈 (Empty Chamber)：了解設備本身的加熱均勻性（烘箱有意義，隧道價值有限）
  • 元件映射 (Component Mapping)：確認熱穿透到複雜物品的最冷點（冷點定位）
  • 負載映射 (Load Mapping)：在實際生產負載條件下，找出整個裝載中 FH 最低的位置

這三層研究層層遞進，從設備到元件到生產條件，確保每個環節都受到充分理解。

重點提示 — 隧道空載研究的「有限價值」

USP 罕見地直接說明某種研究「價值有限」（limited value）。對於連續隧道，空載研究的問題在於：

  • 空載時氣流模式與有載時完全不同
  • 玻璃容器本身會影響隧道內的熱傳特性
  • 因此空載資料無法代表實際生產條件

隧道溫度研究必須在滿載條件下進行，這是與烘箱驗證的重要差異。在法規審查中，若僅提供空載隧道研究，可能被質疑驗證數據的代表性。

比喻說明 — 冷點映射如同找建築最冷的房間

元件映射就像在寒冬中測試一棟建築物的保溫效果：你不能只測門廊的溫度就說整棟建築都夠暖，而是要找到最難加熱的那個角落（北面地下室）——這就是「冷點」。對乾熱去熱原而言，去熱原效果只取決於最冷點的 FH 值，因為如果最難加熱的地方都達標，其他地方必然也達標。

核心概念解析 — 烘箱負載映射關鍵原則

烘箱負載映射有兩個看似矛盾的原則需要理解：

  • 滿載優於空載：更大的熱質量通常使溫度分佈更均勻（大量玻璃吸收和分散熱量）
  • 最大/最小負載均需驗證：因為最小負載可能造成更大的溫度變異（如前述）

因此實務上需要驗證兩個邊界條件：最大負載和最小負載，兩者都必須符合 FH 要求。

公式與計算 — 隧道負載映射感溫器配置要求

隧道負載映射溫度感測器配置：

位置要求：
  - 前緣 (Leading edge)：玻璃進入加熱區的第一排
  - 中間 (Middle/Highest density)：密度最高區域
  - 尾緣 (Trailing edge)：玻璃離開加熱區的最後排

橫向要求：
  - 每段至少 NLT 5 個感測器（跨輸送帶寬度均勻分佈）

感測器接觸要求：
  - 必須直接接觸玻璃容器底部
  - 使用可隨動的拖曳型 (Trailing) 或無線 (Wireless) 感溫器

F_H 計算：
  - 從所有監測位置的溫度資料計算
  - 找出最低 F_H 位置 → 作為驗證確認研究的最壞情況

  1. 為什麼玻璃容器進入去熱原製程時通常是「濕的」？這對元件映射研究有何影響？若未以「濕玻璃」進行映射，驗證結論可能有何偏差？
  2. 隧道負載映射為何必須在「前緣、中間、尾緣」三個位置都放置感溫器？每個位置各代表哪種最壞情況？
Confirmation of Depyrogenation 去熱原確認

原文內容 Original Text

Confirmation of Depyrogenation

The materials/glass components to be depyrogenated should be assessed for their incoming endotoxin content prior to the validation study. This would include glass as received and immediately after washing. All tested materials should be handled and prepared using defined procedures. Materials and glass prepared in the same manner are used in the depyrogenation validation studies. The addition of challenge material to the load items, including a requirement to demonstrate a 3-log reduction, may not be required if time and temperature studies consistently indicate that depyrogenation conditions are met. Temperature monitoring as described above must be done simultaneously with the depyrogenation confirmation studies. The confirmation studies should be performed at reduced time-temperature conditions from those utilized in routine processing and deliver lower FH results when compared to those determined in the mapping studies and are considered "worst case" confirmation of depyrogenation process efficacy. There is no requirement to perform temperature heat distribution measurements during these studies.

ovens

A minimum of five (5) samples should be taken in proximity at NLT 10 temperature-monitored locations (including those determined to be the coldest from the oven load mapping study) in the oven and tested for endotoxin content post-processing. The process is considered acceptable if the amount of endotoxin per sample is NMT 0.1 EU.

tunnels

A minimum of five (5) samples should be taken in proximity to each monitored position within the tunnel load (including those determined to be the coldest from the tunnel load mapping study) and tested for endotoxin content post-processing. The process is considered acceptable if the amount of endotoxin per sample is NMT 0.1 EU.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 — NMT 0.1 EU 的由來

可接受標準：NMT 0.1 EU/樣品（Not More Than 0.1 EU）

這個數值來自於注射用藥品的安全限值邏輯：

  • FDA 規定靜脈注射藥品的內毒素限值通常為 5 EU/kg/hr
  • 以 70 kg 體重的患者，每小時最大用量計算，容器的內毒素殘留應遠低於這個值
  • 0.1 EU/容器是一個保守且實務可達的目標，確保即使最壞情況也對病患安全

重點提示 — 確認研究在「減量條件」下執行

確認研究的關鍵設計邏輯：在比常規生產更嚴苛（更低 FH）的條件下執行，仍能通過 NMT 0.1 EU 的標準。

這種「縮短時間/降低溫度」的最壞情況設計，確保即使製程在驗證邊界的下限運作，去熱原效果仍然充分。通過最壞情況後，日常生產條件（FH 更高）自然被涵蓋在內。

這也說明為什麼確認研究不是在「正常條件」下進行——那樣只能證明正常條件有效，不能證明邊界條件的安全性。

比喻說明 — 最壞情況測試如同安全測試

汽車安全碰撞測試不會只在最輕微的碰撞中測試，而是模擬最嚴苛的撞擊情境（如 64 km/h 正面碰撞）。若車輛在最壞條件下乘客仍安全，代表日常的輕微碰撞必然也安全。去熱原確認研究的邏輯完全相同：在最壞的 FH 條件下仍能通過 NMT 0.1 EU，日常生產的更高 FH 自然也通過。

Routine Process Control 常規製程控制

原文內容 Original Text

ROUTINE PROCESS CONTROL

As with all processes, after the dry heat depyrogenation process has been validated, it must be subject to ongoing controls that maintain it within the validated state at all times. Temperature and exposure time, which are the important dry heat depyrogenation parameters, can be used to confirm performance on a routine basis. Where direct assessment of FH is not possible, assuring that the temperature and exposure time conditions were met results in an equivalent confidence that the depyrogenation system operated in a validated state of control.

Depyrogenation 〈1228〉 details the general practices that are appropriate for all depyrogenation systems. This is accomplished by a number of related practices that are essential for the continued use of the process over an extended period of time. The essential practices to maintain validated status include calibration, physical measurements, periodic endotoxin assessment on incoming materials, ongoing process control, change control, preventive maintenance, and periodic reassessment and training.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 — 維持驗證狀態的七大要素

USP 列出維持乾熱去熱原驗證狀態的七大必要實務：

  • 校準 (Calibration)：溫度感測器定期校準，確保量測準確性
  • 物理量測 (Physical Measurements)：定期確認設備物理狀態（如 HEPA 完整性、氣流）
  • 定期來料內毒素評估 (Periodic Endotoxin Assessment)：確認輸入端內毒素殘留在已知範圍內
  • 持續製程控制 (Ongoing Process Control)：每批次確認溫度與時間參數
  • 變更管控 (Change Control)：任何設備或製程變更都需評估影響
  • 預防性維護 (Preventive Maintenance)：依計畫維護設備，防範失效
  • 定期重新評估與訓練 (Periodic Reassessment and Training)：確保人員能力與製程知識持續更新

比喻說明 — 維持驗證狀態如同飛機適航維護

一架飛機通過初次適航認證後，不代表永遠可以安全飛行。每次飛行前需要例行檢查（持續製程控制），定期需要大檢修（定期重新評估），使用授權零件更換（變更管控），飛行員需要定期複訓（訓練）。去熱原製程的「維持驗證狀態」邏輯完全相同——初始驗證只是取得「適航證書」，之後的持續控制才能確保每批次「安全起飛」。

重點提示 — 溫度與時間的替代確認邏輯

USP 指出：在無法直接計算 FH 的情況下，確認溫度與時間條件均已達到，就等同確認製程在驗證受控狀態下運作。

這是很重要的實務邏輯：大多數商業生產中，每批次並不實際計算 FH 值，而是透過批次記錄確認：

  • 最低溫度 ≥ 驗證最低溫度設定值
  • 保溫時間 ≥ 驗證最短保溫時間

若兩個條件均滿足，根據 FH 數學模型，去熱原效果必然 ≥ 驗證時確認的最低效果。

核心概念解析 — PDA TR3 的重要性

Appendix 引用的 PDA Technical Report 3 (2013)「Validation of dry heat processes used for depyrogenation and sterilization」是業界最重要的乾熱去熱原指引文件之一。

USP 〈1228.1〉 與 PDA TR3 互相補充：

  • USP 提供法定要求（藥典標準）
  • PDA TR3 提供更詳細的技術實施指引和最佳實務

在 CDMO 驗證計畫的設計中，同時參考兩份文件是標準做法。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 某乾熱隧道的驗證溫度設定為 320°C，保溫時間 12 分鐘。一批生產記錄顯示最低溫度 322°C，保溫時間 13 分鐘。這批次的去熱原狀態是否可接受？請用 FH 概念解釋。
  2. 「定期來料內毒素評估」是七大要素之一，為什麼需要持續監控原料的內毒素輸入？如果來料內毒素突然顯著上升，對驗證狀態有何影響？
  3. 某工廠的去熱原烘箱因加熱元件老化，修復後更換了新元件。請說明需要執行哪些後續驗證活動，依據 USP 常規製程控制原則。

實務應用 — CDMO 驗證主計畫整合

對 CDMO 而言，去熱原驗證應整合到驗證主計畫 (Validation Master Plan, VMP) 中，確保：

  • 跨產品一致性：相同設備用於不同客戶產品時，驗證方法論一致
  • 共用設備管理：同一台烘箱/隧道用於多個產品的驗證範圍清晰界定
  • 年度回顧：定期審查 FH 趨勢、異常事件和校準歷史，評估製程持續適宜性
