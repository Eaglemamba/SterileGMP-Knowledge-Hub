Introduction — 導論

Original Text 原文內容

Sterilization-in-place (SIP) can be defined as the sterilization of a system or piece of process equipment in situ. The purpose of SIP is to eliminate, or greatly reduce, the need for post-sterilization handling, including that necessary to make aseptic connections. Mobile process equipment (e.g., portable tanks, storage vessels, and other equipment), once sterilized in this manner, may be relocated. The SIP process can be carried out by using any of the following physical methods: moist heat, dry heat, gas, liquid, or vapor (described below) according to the approaches described in Sterilization of Compendial Articles 〈1229〉, as adapted for use with the specific equipment or system.

"The purpose of SIP is to eliminate, or greatly reduce, the need for post-sterilization handling, including that necessary to make aseptic connections."

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Sterilization-In-Place（就地滅菌 / SIP）：指在設備或系統的原始安裝位置直接進行滅菌，不需要拆卸後送到高壓滅菌釜處理。這是無菌製造中最關鍵的基礎設施能力之一。

  • In situ — 拉丁文「在原位」，意味著設備在安裝位置直接接受滅菌處理
  • 消除滅菌後處理 — SIP 的核心價值：減少人為介入，降低二次汙染風險
  • 五種方法 — 濕熱（蒸汽）、乾熱、氣體、液體、蒸氣 — 不同設備適用不同方法

CDMO 實務觀點 CDMO Perspective

充填產線的 SIP 關鍵性：在無菌充填作業中，SIP 是確保充填機內部（產品接觸路徑）、配液罐、輸送管線達到無菌狀態的核心手段。每一批生產前的 SIP 製程成功與否，直接決定了該批產品是否能無菌放行。

可移動設備：原文特別提到可移動設備（如移動式儲罐）經 SIP 後可以重新定位。這在 CDMO 多產線共用設備的場景中很常見 — 但移動過程的無菌維護是一個額外的風險控制點。

Common Elements of SIP Processes — SIP 製程的共通要素

Original Text 原文內容

There are a number of considerations appropriate for the design and use of SIP procedures that apply to all of the sterilization methods:

  • The use of an SIP method is normally associated with a “closed system.” Closed systems are almost always sterilized in situ, and the design elements of the typical closed system are consistent with many SIP process needs.
  • For large systems, it may be necessary to sterilize in portions. The individual sterilization processes should overlap to ensure treatment of all internal surfaces.
  • The focus of the SIP procedure is sterilization of the product contact surfaces (the interior of the system). Demonstration of process lethality relies upon physical measurements and biological indicators. This confirmation should extend to the “sterile boundary” of the system, including vessel headspace, connections to other vessels/equipment, and other parts of the system. The interior surfaces of the process equipment, irrespective of their materials of construction, should be exposed to lethal conditions sufficient to sterilize the system and confirmed as lethal with an appropriate biological challenge.
  • SIP is accomplished almost exclusively using the overkill approach to sterilization. The components of the physical equipment should be chosen based upon their ability to withstand the sterilizing conditions to be used. Filters in the process system, whether membrane or high-efficiency particulate air (HEPA), are typically susceptible to damage during SIP, and care must be taken to preserve their integrity. Filter manufacturers can provide guidance on acceptable sterilization methods and parameters.
  • The absence of specifically designed equipment in which the sterilization process is performed places the bulk of responsibility for design onto the user. SIP systems ordinarily cannot be purchased directly in the way one purchases a steam sterilizer or a dry heat oven. Instead, the system, which was designed for the operating process, may require modification to accommodate the SIP process to be used. The user must assume the role of designer for the process, equipment, and control system.
  • The system design and operating procedures must provide for an efficient means of introducing and removing the sterilization agent. Sterilization agent removal must consider the potential effects of residual sterilant on the materials to be processed. Establishment of a reliable process sequence is a critical part of the cycle development exercise. The sterilizing agent is normally introduced through a filter on the system that may also serve as a process, purge gas, or vent filter.
  • At the conclusion of the sterilization process sequence and until ready for use, the system should be pressurized with a purge gas (sterile air or nitrogen are the most common) to prevent the introduction of contaminants to the sterilized system.
  • The critical process parameters for the SIP process should be recorded as the process is executed. The important parameters may include temperature, pressure, concentration, flow rate, humidity, and time, among others.

重點提示 Key Notes

Sterile Boundary（無菌邊界）：這是 SIP 驗證中最容易被忽略的概念。無菌邊界不只是產品接觸的管路內壁，還包括：

  • 容器頂部空間（Headspace） — 蒸汽可能無法有效到達的死角
  • 設備連接點 — 閥門、接頭、取樣口
  • 排氣/進氣濾芯 — 濾芯本身也需要被滅菌覆蓋

濾芯保護：原文特別警告濾膜和 HEPA 濾芯在 SIP 過程中容易受損。CDMO 實務中，蒸汽 SIP 後的濾芯完整性測試（Integrity Test）是必做項目 — 過度的蒸汽壓力或溫度會導致濾膜變形或穿孔。

比喻說明 Analogy

消毒你的整棟房子：

想像你要對整棟房子進行消毒。你不能只消毒客廳的地板 — 門把、窗框、通風口、甚至閣樓都必須涵蓋在內。如果房子太大，你可能需要一個房間一個房間地做，但相鄰房間的門口區域必須重複處理，確保沒有遺漏。消毒完成後，你要把所有門窗關好（正壓維持），防止外面的髒空氣再次進入。

SIP 的邏輯完全相同 — 只是「房子」是你的充填機管路系統，「消毒劑」是飽和蒸汽。

CDMO 充填線 SIP 實務 Filling Line SIP Practice

分段滅菌的實務應用：一條典型的無菌充填產線可能包含配液罐、輸送管線、過濾組件、充填泵浦、充填針頭等多個區段。整條產線一次性 SIP 在技術上往往不可行（蒸汽供應量不足、冷凝水排除困難），因此通常分為 2-4 個 SIP 區段（Zone），每個區段的滅菌邊界必須與相鄰區段重疊。

使用者設計責任：這一點對 CDMO 特別重要。充填機廠商（如 IMA、Bausch+Stroebel）提供的 SIP 程序只是基礎框架，CDMO 必須根據自身產品特性、管路配置、蒸汽系統能力來開發和驗證最終的 SIP 週期。

Closed Systems — 封閉系統

Original Text 原文內容

In pharmaceutical manufacturing operations, closed systems are used for various applications including maintenance of large quantities of materials (liquids or powders) in a sterile state; manufacture of biological and synthetic organic active ingredients (especially where microbial absence is essential); and preparation of process equipment for use in sterile drug product manufacturing and filling. The use of closed systems provides superior separation of sterile materials from the surrounding environment. Typically, closed systems are maintained under positive pressure at all times. The characteristics of a closed system that establish its designation as “closed” include the following:

  • It maintains integrity during all operating periods and under all conditions.
  • It is sterilized-in-place or sterilized while closed before use.
  • It can be adapted for materials transfer in and/or out while maintaining its sterile state.
  • It can be connected to other closed systems while maintaining the integrity of all systems.
  • It is subject to scheduled preventive maintenance.
  • It uses sterilizing-grade filters for sterilization of liquid and gas process streams.

重點提示 Key Notes

正壓維護：封閉系統在所有時間都維持正壓（通常使用無菌壓縮空氣或氮氣）。這確保即使有微小洩漏，氣流方向也是從系統內部向外，防止外部微生物進入。這與潔淨室的正壓概念完全相同 — 壓力差是無菌製造的第一道防線。

封閉系統 vs 開放製程：USP 〈1229.13〉 強調封閉系統的重要性，因為 SIP 的前提就是系統必須能夠維持滅菌後的無菌狀態。如果系統無法「封閉」，SIP 的效果就無法被維持。

SIP Methods — SIP 方法

Original Text 原文內容

Moist Heat 濕熱

The use of saturated steam is the most prevalent method for SIP of large systems. The majority of installations use gravity displacement cycles adapted from those originally used in steam sterilizers (the size and complexity of many systems preclude the use of prevacuum cycles). Important considerations include the provision for air removal, condensate discharge, and steam removal post-dwell. This method is commonly used for bioreactors, sterile bulk production, holding tank and delivery lines, and other large systems.

Superheated Water 過熱水

Systems used for Water for Injection and Purified Water can be sterilized by using superheated water (water that is heated above its boiling point and pressurized to maintain it as a liquid) circulating through the system. This method has the ability to sterilize vessels, filters, and other wetted components at the same time. Removal of residual water subsequent to the sterilization phase is recommended.

Dry Heat 乾熱

Dry heat has been used for SIP of spray dryers and their associated material collection systems. The air supply for sterilization in these systems is provided through HEPA filters.

Gas 氣體

Gas-phase SIP has been used for non- and low-pressure-rated process equipment, such as freeze dryers, prefreezers, process vessels, and other equipment.

Liquid 液體

Liquid chemical sterilization is best suited for liquid-handling systems and can be used only for fully wetted surfaces. This process is similar to those using superheated water except the lethal modality is chemical rather than thermal.

Vapor 蒸氣

Sterilizing vapors have been used for the in situ sterilization of the same types of process equipment as those treated with sterilizing gases. The precautions associated with vapor sterilization described in Vapor Phase Sterilization 〈1229.11〉 are required.

CDMO 充填線重點 Filling Line Focus

飽和蒸汽 SIP — 充填產線的標準方法：在 CDMO 的無菌充填作業中，飽和蒸汽 SIP 是最主要的滅菌方式。三個關鍵控制點：

  • 排氣（Air Removal）：管路系統中的殘留空氣會形成「氣穴」（Air Pocket），阻擋蒸汽到達該區域，導致局部溫度不足。這就是「冷點」（Cold Spot）的主要成因。蒸汽陷阱（Steam Trap）和排氣閥的正確設置是解決方案
  • 凝結水排除（Condensate Discharge）：蒸汽在管路中冷凝形成的水如果不能及時排出，會在管路低點積聚，同樣阻擋蒸汽穿透。管路設計必須有適當的坡度和排放點
  • 滅菌後蒸汽移除（Steam Removal Post-Dwell）：滅菌完成後，必須以無菌空氣或氮氣置換蒸汽，並維持正壓直到使用

冷凍乾燥機 SIP Lyophilizer SIP

氣體法 SIP — 冷凍乾燥機的選擇：冷凍乾燥機（Lyophilizer）通常無法承受蒸汽 SIP 的壓力和溫度（腔體設計壓力較低），因此常使用氣體法或蒸氣法（如 VHP — 汽化過氧化氫）進行 SIP。這在 CDMO 的凍乾產品製造中是常見配置。

濾芯 SIP 注意事項：無論使用哪種方法，過濾組件的 SIP 都需要特別關注。過熱水法的優勢在於可以同時滅菌容器和濾芯，但蒸汽法中濾芯可能因壓差過大而受損 — 必須控制壓力差在濾芯廠商建議的範圍內。

比喻說明 Analogy

六把不同的「消毒鑰匙」：

每種 SIP 方法就像不同型號的鑰匙 — 不是每把鑰匙都能開每把鎖。飽和蒸汽是最萬能的「萬能鑰匙」（適用範圍最廣），但遇到冷凍乾燥機這把「精密鎖」，你需要氣體法或蒸氣法這把更精細的鑰匙。選錯鑰匙不只打不開鎖，還可能把鎖弄壞（設備損傷）。

Routine Process Control — 例行製程控制

Original Text 原文內容

SIP processes are subject to formal controls that maintain a validated state over time. The practices outlined in 〈1229〉 include the general requirements appropriate for all sterilization systems as well as those specific to an individual sterilization method. Sterilization is accomplished by a number of related practices that are essential for continued use of the process over an extended period of time. The essential practices to maintain validated status include calibration, physical measurements, physical integrators or indicators, ongoing process control, change control, preventive maintenance, periodic reassessment, and training.

重點提示 Key Notes

維持驗證狀態（Validated State）：SIP 驗證不是「做一次就結束」的事情。USP 〈1229.13〉 明確要求透過八項持續性實務來維持驗證狀態。在 CDMO 的實際操作中，最常見的失效模式包括：

  • 蒸汽疏水閥（Steam Trap）故障 — 導致凝結水排除不良，產生冷點
  • 溫度感測器漂移 — 顯示合格但實際溫度不足
  • 管路改造後未重新驗證 — 新增的管路段可能產生新的冷點
  • 操作人員未按 SOP 執行 — 閥門開關順序錯誤

變更管制在 SIP 中尤其關鍵 — 即使是看似微小的管路修改（如增加一個取樣閥），都可能改變蒸汽流動路徑，產生新的冷點。
