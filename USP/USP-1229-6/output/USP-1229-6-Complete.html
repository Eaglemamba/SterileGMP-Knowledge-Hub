Introduction — 導論

Original Text

Microorganisms are subject to destruction in a variety of ways. Aside from the classical methods of steam, dry heat, and radiation, destructive sterilization may also occur by immersion in a chemical solution. This is termed liquid-phase sterilization. A number of chemical agents, such as aldehydes, acids, bases, and strong oxidants in solution, under the appropriate conditions, are capable of destroying bacteria and fungi, including both vegetative cells and spores in a quantitative fashion. Objects to be sterilized are immersed in the solution of the chemical agent, after which the agent must be removed in a manner that preserves the sterilized object from recontamination.

Removal of the chemical sterilant from the exposed surfaces that have been sterilized must be accomplished in a manner that maintains the sterility of the item postprocessing. Recontamination falls outside the scope of usual consideration for sterilization processes. However, in liquid chemical sterilization it is customary to include the agent’s removal (whether this is accomplished by physical or chemical means) in the overall process, together with any needed additional steps to avoid recontamination.

A substantial number of liquids in aqueous solution are capable of sterilizing articles during immersion. Examples include the following:

  • Aldehydes — glutaraldehyde, formaldehyde
  • Acids — peracetic acid, nitric acid, sulfuric acid
  • Bases — sodium hydroxide, potassium hydroxide
  • Oxygenating compounds — hydrogen peroxide, ozone, chlorine dioxide
  • Halides — sodium hypochlorite, chlorine

As is the case for gas sterilization, the effectiveness of chemical sterilants varies with concentration and temperature. Other factors that affect antimicrobial activity include pH, extent of mixing (if present), and presence of cellular or other debris. Because of the limited number of variables, process control for sterilization by liquids is relatively simple.

Because there are no widely accepted biological indicators for sterilization by liquids, the use of a common mesophilic sporeformer such as Bacillus atrophaeus or B. subtilis is commonplace because these are the likely worst-case bioburden isolates.

The agents used for sterilization by liquids vary with respect to sterilant stability, effective pH range, concentration, temperature, contact time required, and potential interaction with the materials. When selecting the most appropriate sterilant, manufacturers must consider its effect on the materials, package components, and equipment, as is the case with all other sterilizing processes. The variety of agents, process diversity, and potential applications preclude a material-by-material review of these agents in this chapter. Manufacturers should note that these agents are highly toxic, and appropriate safety measures should be practiced at all times during cycle development, validation, and routine operation.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Liquid-Phase Sterilization（液相滅菌）：將物品浸泡在化學滅菌劑溶液中，利用化學方式破壞微生物（包括孢子）的滅菌方法。與蒸氣或乾熱等物理方式不同，這裡的殺滅機制是化學反應。

與其他滅菌方式的定位差異：

  • Steam / 蒸氣（⟨1229.1⟩） — 高溫高壓，最通用
  • Dry Heat / 乾熱（⟨1229.8⟩） — 除熱原（depyrogenation）
  • Radiation / 輻射（⟨1229.10⟩） — 最終包裝滅菌
  • Liquid-Phase / 液相（本章） — 表面浸泡滅菌，適用於無法耐受高溫的物品

重點提示 Key Notes

化學滅菌劑五大類：

類別代表藥劑CDMO 常見應用
Aldehydes / 醛類Glutaraldehyde, Formaldehyde設備表面滅菌（較少用於 SUS）
Acids / 酸類Peracetic Acid (PAA)SUS 流路滅菌、isolator 表面
Bases / 鹼類NaOH, KOHCIP 清洗（高濃度可滅菌）
Oxygenating / 含氧化合物H<sub>2</sub>O<sub>2</sub>, Ozone, ClO<sub>2</sub>水系統消毒、表面滅菌
Halides / 鹵化物NaOCl, Chlorine水系統消毒、環境清潔

CDMO 首選：Peracetic Acid (PAA / 過氧乙酸) — 它分解產物為醋酸和水，對環境友善，且對一次性系統材料相容性好。這是製藥業液相滅菌最常用的化學劑。

重點提示 Safety & Toxicity

原文最後特別警告：這些化學劑具高度毒性（highly toxic）。在 CDMO 環境中，操作過氧乙酸或次氯酸鈉等試劑時，必須配備：

  • 適當的個人防護裝備（PPE）— 護目鏡、手套、面罩
  • 通風設施 — 避免蒸氣吸入
  • 洩漏應變計畫（Spill Response）
  • 職業安全衛生（EHS）培訓紀錄
Validation of Sterilization by Liquids — 液相滅菌驗證

Original Text

Experimental evidence has shown that first-order kinetics is appropriate for microbial destruction, which makes validation a simple exercise. The validation of sterilization by liquids can be accomplished using either the half-cycle approach or the bracketing method.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 First-Order Kinetics

一級反應動力學（First-Order Kinetics）：微生物在化學滅菌劑作用下的死亡遵循一級動力學 — 意味著在對數座標上，存活微生物數量與暴露時間呈線性關係。這與蒸氣滅菌中的 D 值概念相同。

這使得驗證相對簡單：只要確認在特定條件下的殺滅速率（death rate），就能計算出達到目標 SAL（Sterility Assurance Level, 10−6）所需的暴露時間。

Half-Cycle Approach — 半週期法

Original Text

The half-cycle approach described below is a modification of the method described in Gaseous Sterilization ⟨1229.7⟩ (see Figure 1). It is a method that requires the destruction of a resistant microorganism at defined lethal conditions. In routine operation, the process dwell period is arbitrarily doubled and supports a theoretical reduction of the biological indicator (and thus the bioburden) to a probability of a nonsterile unit of at least 10−6 for a full cycle.

The half-cycle method originally was used for ethylene oxide sterilization when the relationship between the microorganisms and the delivered process parameters was less certain.

"In routine operation, the process dwell period is arbitrarily doubled and supports a theoretical reduction of the biological indicator to a probability of a nonsterile unit of at least 10−6."

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Half-Cycle Method

半週期法的運作邏輯：

  1. 驗證階段：在一半的暴露時間（half cycle）內，證明能完全殺滅所有生物指示劑（total kill）
  2. 常規操作：將暴露時間加倍（full cycle），提供額外的安全餘量
  3. 結果：理論上達到至少 10−6 的非無菌概率（PNSU）

這種方法的優勢在於不需要精確測定 D 值 — 只要在半週期內確認 total kill，加倍後的安全餘量就足以保證無菌性。

比喻說明 Analogy

游泳池消毒的例子：假設你測試發現，用特定濃度的消毒劑浸泡 30 分鐘就能殺死所有測試微生物。半週期法的邏輯就是：常規操作時浸泡 60 分鐘（加倍），確保即使有變異因素，也能達到足夠的滅菌保證。

公式邏輯 Validation Logic

Half-cycle (validation):
  Expose BI at time T/2 → Total kill confirmed

Full cycle (routine):
  Expose at time T (= 2 × T/2)
  → Theoretical PNSU ≤ 10−6
Bracketing Approach — 包圍法

Original Text

In this method (see Figure 2), analysts evaluate conditions of concentration and temperature that bracket the defined process condition to support both over- and under-treatment of the materials and bioburden, respectively. Users can establish the death rate for the microbial population for each of the conditions that bracket the routine process.

Regardless of the approach used, in order to complete the cycle development and validation manufacturers must identify a rapid neutralization method that inactivates the chemical agent to allow microbial quantification after fractional kill exposure. The exposure periods may need to be brief because many of these agents have rapid kill rates.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Bracketing Method

包圍法（Bracketing）的運作邏輯：

  • High bracket（上限條件）：高於常規的濃度/溫度 — 確認不會對物料造成損害（over-treatment）
  • Low bracket（下限條件）：低於常規的濃度/溫度 — 確認仍然能夠殺滅微生物（under-treatment worst case）
  • Routine（常規條件）：落在上下限之間，兼顧滅菌效力與材料相容性

這種方法比半週期法更加精確，因為它建立了完整的殺滅動力學數據（D 值），但也需要更多的實驗工作。

重點提示 Neutralization Challenge

中和方法的關鍵挑戰：原文特別強調必須找到一種快速中和方法來在暴露後立即停止化學劑的殺滅作用。這是因為：

  • 許多化學滅菌劑（如 PAA）殺菌速率非常快
  • 如果中和太慢，fractional kill 數據會被扭曲 — 實際暴露時間比設計的更長
  • 常見的中和劑：硫代硫酸鈉（sodium thiosulfate）用於氧化性滅菌劑；蛋白（catalase）用於 H2O2
Equipment Qualification — 設備確效

Original Text

Equipment qualification is a predefined program that examines the equipment to confirm that it has been properly installed and operates as intended before the sterilization process. This activity for sterilization by liquid chemicals is simple, because the equipment used is rarely complex. Temperature control and agitation/recirculation rates are the essential considerations.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 EQ for LPS

設備確效在液相滅菌中相對簡單，因為所需設備通常不複雜。核心需確認的參數：

  • IQ（安裝確效）：浸泡槽/容器的安裝符合設計規格
  • OQ（操作確效）：溫度控制系統能維持設定溫度；攪拌/循環系統運作正常
  • PQ（性能確效）：在裝載條件下能均勻分布滅菌劑

CDMO 實務：對於 SUS 流路的液相滅菌，「設備」可能只是一個泵循環系統加上溫度感測器。但仍需完成完整的 IQ/OQ/PQ 文件。

Component & Load Definition — 組件與裝載定義

Original Text

Sterilization by liquids is a surface phenomenon, and all surfaces of the materials must be immersed in the sterilant. Treatment uniformity can be ensured by recirculation or mixing of the sterilant during the process. Penetration into needle lumens, closely fitted parts, and porous materials should be confirmed. The use of a maximum load per defined vessel or container represents the worst case because it provides the maximum surface area to be sterilized.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Surface Contact

液相滅菌是表面現象（surface phenomenon） — 這是本章最重要的原則之一。化學滅菌劑必須與所有待滅菌表面直接接觸才能產生效果。

關鍵挑戰：

  • Needle lumens / 針頭管腔：內徑極小，滅菌劑可能無法完全滲入
  • Closely fitted parts / 緊密配合部件：接觸面之間可能形成死角
  • Porous materials / 多孔材料：內部孔隙需要滅菌劑滲透

重點提示 CDMO SUS Application

SUS 流路滅菌的裝載定義：在 CDMO 無菌充填操作中，使用過氧乙酸滅菌 SUS 流路時，「maximum load」指的是最長的管路配置、最多的連接點、最複雜的歧管（manifold）。驗證時必須使用此最劣情境（worst case）。

循環/攪拌的重要性：對 SUS 流路而言，泵送循環（recirculation）確保滅菌劑能到達所有內表面，包括 T 型接頭、閥門死角和管路彎曲處。靜態浸泡在複雜流路中是不夠的。

Biological Indicators — 生物指示劑

Original Text

The common indicator organisms for chemical sterilization are B. atrophaeus ATCC 9372 or B. subtilis ATCC 6633. The spore challenge is inoculated directly onto the items. End users should determine the populations of inoculated items. Manufacturers should place indicators within loads at locations believed to be hardest for the agent to reach, on the basis of visual examination.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 BI Selection

為什麼選用 B. atrophaeus / B. subtilis？

  • 這兩種都是嗜溫性（mesophilic）芽孢形成菌
  • 它們代表了實際生產環境中最可能遇到的最劣情境生物負載
  • 注意：這與蒸氣滅菌使用耐熱的 Geobacillus stearothermophilus 不同 — 因為液相滅菌通常在較低溫度下進行

直接接種法（Direct Inoculation）：孢子挑戰直接接種在待滅菌物品上，而非使用市售 BI 載體。這更能反映實際滅菌條件。

重點提示 Placement Strategy

原文明確指出：BI 應放置在化學劑最難到達的位置（hardest to reach）。這些位置通常包括：

  • 管路最遠端（furthest point from inlet）
  • T 型接頭的死腿（dead legs）
  • 閥門密封面附近
  • 組件的凹槽或螺紋內
Process Confirmation / Microbiological Challenge — 製程確認 / 微生物挑戰

Original Text

The core of the validation activity is confirmation of acceptable process parameters and inactivation of the microbial challenge. The end user should expect a linear death curve for the spore challenge and require total death of the challenge. The end user can consider adjustment in chemical sterilant concentration, process time, agitation, and other factors. Proof of cycle efficacy is provided in replicate studies in which the biological indicators are killed, and physical measurements are taken as documentation.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Validation Core

驗證活動的核心要素：

  1. Linear death curve / 線性死亡曲線：在半對數圖上，存活菌數 vs. 時間應呈直線 — 這確認了一級動力學假設的正確性
  2. Total kill / 完全殺滅：所有 BI 必須被殺死 — 不接受部分存活
  3. Replicate studies / 重複研究：業界慣例至少 3 次連續成功運行
  4. Physical measurements / 物理量測：溫度、濃度、時間等參數的紀錄作為輔助文件

重點提示 Adjustable Parameters

當滅菌效果不理想時，可調整的製程參數包括：

  • 濃度（Concentration） — 增加化學劑濃度
  • 時間（Process Time） — 延長暴露時間
  • 攪拌（Agitation） — 增加混合以確保接觸
  • 溫度（Temperature） — 提高溫度以加速反應

但要注意：每個參數的調整都可能影響材料相容性，需要同步評估對 SUS 材料或設備的影響。

Agent Neutralization / Removal — 化學劑中和 / 去除

Original Text

After exposure, the sterilizing agent must be adequately removed from the items or neutralized before further processing. This segment of the process uses chemical neutralization or physical removal and must be executed in a manner that preserves the sterility of the items. Aseptic processing with appropriate capability demonstration should be provided. Process simulation beginning with the completion of sterilization through placement into a sealed sterile container is expected. See Sterility Assurance ⟨1211⟩ for additional information.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Post-Sterilization Handling

滅菌後處理是液相滅菌最獨特也最具挑戰性的環節。兩種去除方式：

  • Chemical neutralization / 化學中和：使用中和劑（如硫代硫酸鈉）使滅菌劑失活。適用於難以沖洗的複雜結構
  • Physical removal / 物理去除：使用無菌水或 WFI 沖洗去除滅菌劑殘留。更常用於 SUS 流路

重點提示 Sterility Maintenance

維持無菌狀態的關鍵要求：

  • 去除/中和過程本身必須在無菌條件下進行 — 否則剛滅菌的物品會被重新污染
  • 需要無菌製程模擬（Process Simulation / Media Fill）來驗證從滅菌完成到放入密封容器的整個過程
  • 沖洗用的水必須是無菌水或 WFI
  • 所有接觸已滅菌物品的設備和容器都必須是預先滅菌的

CDMO 實務提示：對於 SUS 流路的 PAA 滅菌後沖洗，通常使用無菌 WFI 經由同一泵系統進行循環沖洗，並透過 TOC（Total Organic Carbon）或 pH 測試確認殘留去除的完整性。沖洗後的排放物需要進行殘留測試以確認符合限值。

Routine Process Control — 日常製程管控

Original Text

Liquid-phase sterilization must be subject to controls that maintain the validated state. The practices outlined in Sterilization of Compendial Articles ⟨1229⟩ address the general requirements for all sterilization systems. Sterilization is accomplished by means of a number of related practices that are essential for continued use of the process over an extended period of time, including calibration, chemical and physical measurements, ongoing process control, change control, preventive maintenance, periodic reassessment, and training.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Maintaining Validated State

維持驗證狀態（Validated State）的七大要素：

要素說明頻率（典型）
Calibration / 校正溫度計、濃度計、pH 計等依 SOP，通常每 6-12 個月
Chemical measurements / 化學量測滅菌劑濃度確認每批次
Physical measurements / 物理量測溫度、時間、流速紀錄每批次
Ongoing process control / 持續製程管控製程參數趨勢分析持續/定期審查
Change control / 變更管控任何製程變更的評估與核准依需求
Preventive maintenance / 預防性維護設備維護計畫依設備類型
Training / 培訓操作人員持續教育初始 + 年度

重點提示 Cross-Reference

本節提到的日常管控原則直接引用 USP ⟨1229⟩（母章）的通用要求。這表示液相滅菌與其他滅菌方式（蒸氣、乾熱、輻射等）共享相同的 GMP 管控框架 — 差異只在於具體的製程參數和監測項目。

  • 滅菌後處理：化學劑的中和或去除是液相滅菌最獨特的挑戰 — 必須在無菌條件下進行，並通過製程模擬驗證
  • 日常管控：與所有滅菌方式共享 USP ⟨1229⟩ 的通用要求：校正、量測、製程管控、變更管控、維護、定期評估、培訓
