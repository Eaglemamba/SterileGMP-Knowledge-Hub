Introduction — 導論

Original Text 原文內容

A biological indicator (BI) is a well-characterized preparation of a specific microorganism that has known resistance to a specific sterilization process. BIs are used to demonstrate the effectiveness of processes that render a product sterile in its final package or container, as well as the effectiveness of the sterilization of equipment, product contact materials, and packaging components as required.

BIs may also be used to monitor established sterilization cycles and are used for periodic reassessment of sterilization process effectiveness. BIs are process aids and can support the correlation of physical parameters to microbiological destruction. Microorganisms recognized as suitable for BIs are spore-forming bacteria; the spores of these microorganisms are significantly more resistant than the vegetative cells that comprise the majority of bioburden in or on materials.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Biological Indicator（生物指示劑, BI）：一種經過充分特性鑑定的微生物製備品，對特定滅菌製程具有已知的抗性。關鍵詞是「well-characterized」和「known resistance」— BI 不是隨便找一株菌，而是經過嚴格量化的標準化工具。

  • Well-characterized — 菌種鑑定明確、族群數量已知、抗性參數（D 值、z 值）已測定
  • Known resistance — 對目標滅菌製程的抗性高於一般生物負載（bioburden），提供「最壞情況」挑戰

為什麼用芽孢（Spore）而非營養細胞？芽孢對極端環境（高溫、化學品、輻射）的抗性遠高於營養態細胞。如果滅菌製程能殺滅芽孢，幾乎可以確定也能殺滅所有營養態微生物。

重點提示 Key Notes

BI 的三大用途：

  • 製程開發（Cycle Development） — 確認滅菌製程的有效性
  • 驗證（Validation） — 展示製程在最壞條件下仍能達到預期的微生物殺滅效果
  • 日常監控（Routine Monitoring） — 週期性再確認已驗證製程的持續有效性

BI 是「製程輔助工具」（Process Aid） — 原文用詞精確。BI 不是產品的一部分，而是用來「見證」滅菌條件是否達標的哨兵（sentinel）。它幫助建立物理參數（溫度、時間、壓力）與微生物殺滅之間的關聯性。

Proper Use of Biological Indicators — 生物指示劑的正確使用

Original Text 原文內容

BIs provide microbiological evidence of process effectiveness that should be correlated to physical measurements (see Sterilization of Compendial Articles &langle;1229&rangle;). Microbiological resistance to sterilization varies with the physical conditions; however, there are no established means for accurately predicting microbial destruction based solely on physical measurements, with the exception of radiation sterilization.

BI placement locations within or on materials to be sterilized are chosen to confirm that the desired sterilizing conditions have been attained. BIs are typically spore-forming bacteria of the genera Geobacillus, Bacillus, and Clostridium. Preference in BI selection should be given to well-characterized strains.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

物理參數 vs 微生物學證據：原文強調一個關鍵觀點 — 除了輻射滅菌之外，不能僅靠物理測量來準確預測微生物殺滅效果。這就是為什麼 BI 不可替代：

  • 溫度探針告訴你滅菌器達到了 121°C，但無法告訴你負載中心是否也達到了
  • BI 放在負載最難穿透的位置（cold spot），提供直接的微生物學「是否存活」的答案

輻射滅菌的例外：輻射的殺菌效果完全取決於吸收劑量（absorbed dose），且可通過劑量計精確測量。因此 ISO 11137 系列標準使用劑量設定（dose setting）方法，不需要常規使用 BI。

重點提示 BI 放置策略

CDMO 實務：BI 的放置位置（placement location）是滅菌驗證的關鍵決策之一。放置原則：

  • 選擇最難達到滅菌條件的位置（worst-case location）— 通常是負載最密集處或蒸汽最難穿透處
  • 在滅菌器進行溫度分佈研究（Temperature Distribution Study）時，結合溫度探針數據來確定 cold spot
  • 每次驗證批次至少在 cold spot 放置 BI，加上分散在負載各處的額外位置
Responsibilities — 責任

Original Text 原文內容

BI Manufacturer's Responsibility

The responsibility for determining the performance characteristics of each BI lot resides with the BI manufacturer. The manufacturer should provide, with each lot of BIs, a certificate of analysis that attests to the validity of BI performance claims. The manufacturer should provide information concerning the microbial population and resistance (D- and z-values, respectively, where appropriate) as well as storage and expiry information.

The BI manufacturer should include survival and kill times for the BI in their documentation and this should be verified by the end user. The resistance of the BI should be determined by the manufacturer under defined conditions. The manufacturer should provide directions for use, including the medium and conditions used for the recovery of microorganisms after exposure to the sterilization process. Disposal instructions also should be provided by the manufacturer of the BI.

BI User's Responsibility

When BIs are purchased, their suitability for use in a specific sterilization process must be established. The BI user should obtain a certificate of analysis for each lot of BIs and verify the manufacturer's label claims for spore population (see Biological Indicators — Resistance Performance Tests &langle;55&rangle;). When a BI is used in accordance with the BI manufacturer's directions, the resistance of the BI need not be reconfirmed.

User-Prepared Biological Indicators

A user of BIs may elect to propagate spore crops of a single species for use as a suspension. Alternatively, these spore suspensions may be purchased from a BI manufacturer. When liquid suspensions are applied to a substrate, it is the user's responsibility to determine the population and resistance of the microorganism used. The resistance determined for liquid suspensions relates only to other lots of the same suspension and is not representative of how that microorganism will perform on a substrate or in a different suspending medium. In these circumstances, the BI resistance and population should be re-established (see &langle;55&rangle;).

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 D 值與 z 值

D-value（D 值, 十進制減少時間）：在特定條件下，將微生物族群數量減少 90%（即一個 log）所需的暴露時間。例如：D121°C = 1.5 min 代表在 121°C 下，每 1.5 分鐘殺滅 90% 的芽孢。

z-value（z 值, 溫度敏感度）：使 D 值改變一個 log（10 倍）所需的溫度變化。例如：z = 10°C 意味著溫度每升高 10°C，D 值降低 10 倍。

Survival Time（存活時間）：BI 在特定滅菌條件下全數存活的最長暴露時間。

Kill Time（殺滅時間）：BI 在特定滅菌條件下全數被殺滅的最短暴露時間。

比喻說明 Analogy

BI 批號管理就像試劑管理：把 BI 想成實驗室的標準試劑 — 供應商提供出廠 CoA，但你作為使用者必須在進貨時進行「接收檢驗」確認族群數量符合標示。這就是 USP &langle;55&rangle; 所描述的接收測試。如果你自己製備 BI（例如將芽孢懸浮液接種到產品容器上），那麼你就同時承擔了「製造商」的責任 — 必須自行測定族群數量和抗性。

重點提示 CDMO 實務考量

BI 批號資格確認（Lot Qualification）：在 CDMO 環境中，BI 是經常使用的耗材。建議建立以下管理制度：

  • 每個新批號進貨時，執行 USP &langle;55&rangle; 所要求的族群數量驗證
  • 建立批號接收記錄，與 CoA 一同歸檔
  • 監控不同批號之間的變異性 — 同一供應商的不同批號 D 值差異過大時應發起偏差調查
  • 確保 BI 儲存條件符合標示要求（通常 2–8°C），並納入溫度監控程式
Characterization of Biological Indicators — 生物指示劑的特性鑑定

Original Text 原文內容

The use of BIs should include procedures for their acceptance and control. The following elements outline the major considerations. Resistance performance is addressed separately in &langle;55&rangle;, which provides methods for evaluating BI resistance.

Packaging and Storage: Store under the conditions recommended on the label or under validated conditions, and protect from light, toxic substances, excessive heat, and moisture.

Expiration Date: Use within the BI's labeled or determined expiration date.

Identification: Where identification of the BI species is deemed necessary, as in the course of an investigation into unusual results, use either a phenotypic or genotypic identification method (see Microbial Characterization, Identification, and Strain Typing &langle;1113&rangle; for additional information).

Purity: By examination of the colonies derived from the spores on a suitable plate culture medium, determine that there is no evidence of contamination with other microorganisms.

Disposal: Prior to discarding used spores, sterilize using a method recommended by the BI manufacturer or other equivalent means.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 BI 接收與控管要素

USP &langle;1229.5&rangle; 將 BI 特性鑑定分為五個管控面向。在 CDMO 品質系統中，每一項都應有對應的 SOP：

  • 包裝與儲存（Packaging & Storage） — 按標示條件儲存，避光、避熱、避溼。納入倉儲管理的溫度監控
  • 有效期（Expiration Date） — 嚴格遵守，過期 BI 的抗性可能降低導致假陰性結果
  • 鑑定（Identification） — 通常不需要每批鑑定，但在偏差調查時（如 BI 結果異常），可能需要透過表型或基因型方法確認菌種
  • 純度（Purity） — 平板培養確認無雜菌汙染 — 如果 BI 本身不純，結果判讀將不可靠
  • 廢棄（Disposal） — 使用過的 BI（尤其是「未殺滅」的陽性對照）含有活的高抗性芽孢，必須先滅菌再丟棄

重點提示 陽性對照管理

Positive Control（陽性對照）：每次使用 BI 時，必須保留至少一支未暴露於滅菌製程的 BI 作為陽性對照。陽性對照必須在培養後出現生長，以確認：

  • 該批 BI 在使用時仍具有活性（viable）
  • 培養基和培養條件適合微生物生長
  • 整個測試流程沒有系統性錯誤

如果陽性對照未生長 — 整批結果無效，必須調查。

Types of Biological Indicators — 生物指示劑的類型

Original Text 原文內容

A BI is a well-characterized preparation of a specific bacterial spore of known resistance to a specific sterilization process. Some BIs may contain two different species and concentrations of bacterial spores for use in the evaluation of two different sterilization processes.

One form of BI preparation includes spores that are placed on a carrier (e.g., a disk or strip of paper, glass, plastic, metal, or other material) and may be packaged to maintain the integrity and viability of the spores inoculated onto the carrier. The carrier and primary packaging should not be damaged or degraded by the specific sterilization process.

Another preparation of BIs is a spore suspension that is inoculated on or into representative units of the article to be sterilized. A surrogate article may be used if it is not practical to inoculate the actual article. A surrogate article is a preparation that differs in one or more ways from the actual article but performs as the actual article during cycle development, validation, and routine use. The physical design of actual or surrogate articles can affect the resistance of spore suspensions that are inoculated on or into an article (see &langle;55&rangle;). In the case of liquid inoculated products, it is essential to determine the population, D-value (and, in terminal sterilization applications, z-value) of the relevant BI spore in the liquid product, and any simulated product substrate (if utilized).

A third form of BI is a sealed system that includes the growth medium (either in direct contact with the BI during the sterilization or placed in contact with the BI after sterilization) for recovery of process-exposed BI microorganisms. Some BI systems may contain a growth indicator or sensor in addition to growth media.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

重點提示 替代品（Surrogate Article）

何時使用替代品？當無法直接在實際產品上接種芽孢時（例如產品本身具有抑菌性、產品價值極高、或產品結構不允許接種），可以使用替代品。但替代品必須在滅菌製程中的行為（如熱穿透特性）與實際產品一致。

CDMO 常見場景：在含有防腐劑（preservative）或抗生素的注射劑產品進行最終滅菌驗證時，產品本身會殺滅 BI 芽孢，導致無法區分是「滅菌製程殺滅」還是「產品成分殺滅」。此時需要使用模擬產品基質（simulated product substrate），並在此基質中重新測定 BI 的 D 值和族群數量。

比喻說明 Analogy

三種 BI 就像三種溫度計：

  • 載體型 = 把溫度計放在固定支架上測量 — 標準化、方便比較
  • 懸浮液接種型 = 直接把溫度計插進食物中心測量 — 最能反映實際情況
  • 密封含培養基型 = 一次性數位溫度計，即測即讀 — 操作簡便但成本較高
Selection for Specific Sterilization Processes — 各滅菌製程的 BI 選擇

Original Text 原文內容

The selection of a BI requires knowledge of the resistance of the BI system to the specific sterilization process. It must be established that the BI system provides a challenge to the sterilization process greater than the resistance of the native bioburden. The recommendations for BI with each sterilization process are not exclusive; they represent only the more common choices.

Steam Sterilization by Direct Contact

For steam sterilization by direct contact, the commonly used BI contains spores of G. stearothermophilus (ATCC 12980 or ATCC 7953), a thermophilic microorganism with a moist heat resistance substantially greater than that of most vegetative microorganisms (see Steam Sterilization by Direct Contact &langle;1229.1&rangle;).

Moist Heat Sterilization of Aqueous Liquids

Heat-resistant spore-forming microorganisms such as C. sporogenes (ATCC 7955), B. subtilis (ATCC 35021), or B. atrophaeus (ATCC 9372) are used. B. subtilis, B. atrophaeus, and C. sporogenes are preferred for use in sterilization of aqueous solutions or where their lower thermal resistance is more appropriate.

Dry Heat Sterilization

For dry heat sterilization, spores of B. atrophaeus (ATCC 9372) are typically used (see Dry Heat Sterilization &langle;1229.8&rangle;). Where dry heat depyrogenation has been demonstrated (Dry Heat Depyrogenation &langle;1228.1&rangle;), sterilization by dry heat need not be confirmed, and a BI is not required. The elevated temperatures required to depyrogenate materials are more than sufficient to sterilize the materials at the same time.

Ionizing Radiation

The use of a resistant BI is unnecessary for the evaluation of radiation sterilization processes. Dose setting involves the evaluation of preirradiation bioburden as well as dosimetric evaluation and allied tests as defined in ISO 11137-1, ISO 11137-2, and ISO 11137-3, as well as in Radiation Sterilization &langle;1229.10&rangle;.

Gas Sterilization

For ethylene oxide sterilization, spores of B. atrophaeus are commonly used. For other gaseous agents, spores of G. stearothermophilus or B. atrophaeus are commonly used (see Gaseous Sterilization &langle;1229.7&rangle;).

Chemical Sterilization

The sterilization of items using a liquid sterilant is accomplished using spores of an appropriate strain such as B. atrophaeus, B. subtilis, or other appropriate spore-forming species, as determined by the user. Whichever strain is chosen for this purpose should have greater resistance than does the bioburden.

Vapor Phase Sterilization

The biphasic nature of these materials precludes the accurate determination of specific lethal conditions (for establishment of D-values, see Vapor Phase Sterilization &langle;1229.11&rangle;). BIs using either G. stearothermophilus or B. atrophaeus have been utilized in the evaluation of these processes.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

重點提示 CDMO 最常見的三組搭配

在無菌充填 CDMO 環境中，最常遇到的滅菌/指示菌組合：

  • 高壓蒸汽滅菌（Autoclave 121°C） + G. stearothermophilus — 用於設備、容器關閉系統、培養基充填
  • 乾熱滅菌/去熱原（250°C+） + B. atrophaeus（或不需 BI，如為去熱原製程） — 用於玻璃瓶、不鏽鋼零件
  • VHP 氣相過氧化氫 + G. stearothermophilus — 用於隔離器（isolator）、RABS 的表面除汙

重點提示 為什麼液體滅菌不用 G. stearothermophilus？

G. stearothermophilus 對濕熱的 D 值極高（D121°C 通常 1.5–3.0 min），如果用在液體製品的最終滅菌驗證中，所需的暴露時間會過長，可能導致產品過度降解。相較之下，B. subtilis 或 C. sporogenes 的 D 值較低，能在與產品品質相容的暴露時間內提供合理的微生物學挑戰。

這也是 USP &langle;1229.5&rangle; 所說的「where their lower thermal resistance is more appropriate」的含義 — BI 的選擇不是越「難殺」越好，而是要與滅菌製程和產品特性匹配。
