Introduction — 簡介

原文內容 Original Text

INTRODUCTION

Lipid injectable emulsions for intravenous administration are sterile oil-in-water emulsions of soybean oil, used to provide an ample supply of essential fatty acids, linoleic and linolenic, dispersed with the aid of an emulsifying agent in Water for Injection. Alternatively, soybean oil can be mixed with other suitable oils (neutral triglycerides), such as safflower oil, medium-chain triglycerides (MCT) derived from coconut or palm kernel oils, olive oil, or a marine oil, such as menhaden oil.

The size of the lipid droplets is critical: because of mechanical filtration, larger-size fat globules (>5 µm) can be trapped in the lungs. The essential size characteristics of a lipid injectable emulsion for intravenous use include the mean diameter of the lipid droplets and the range of the various droplet diameters distributed around the mean diameter, expressed as the standard deviation. In particular, the amounts of fat globules comprising the large-diameter tail of the globule size distribution are especially important with respect to infusion safety.

These two regions of the globule size distribution (mean droplet size and large-diameter tail) must be controlled within specified limits.

"The size of the lipid droplets is critical: because of mechanical filtration, larger-size fat globules (>5 µm) can be trapped in the lungs."

The two methods described below are used for determination of the mean lipid droplet diameter and the distribution of large-diameter globule sizes in lipid injectable emulsions. Method I and Method II must be validated. The methods described below to assess the quality of lipid injectable emulsions are to be performed in two stages.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Lipid Injectable Emulsion（脂質注射型乳劑）：這是一種靜脈輸注用的油水乳劑，主要成分為大豆油（soybean oil），透過乳化劑均勻分散於注射用水中。臨床上用於無法口服或腸道進食的病患，提供必需脂肪酸與熱量來源。

Large-Diameter Tail（大直徑尾端）：在粒徑分佈曲線中，偏離平均值右側的少數超大顆粒——這些「尾端」顆粒雖然數量少，卻是最大的安全隱患。

PFAT5：Percentage of Fat Residing in Globules Larger Than 5 µm（大於 5 µm 脂肪球所佔分散相的體積百分比）。這是 USP 〈729〉 最核心的品質指標。

比喻說明 Analogy

想像靜脈注射乳劑就像把果汁（油脂）打進血管。正常的脂肪球大小約 200–500 nm，可以輕鬆通過肺部微血管（直徑約 5–8 µm）。但如果乳劑中出現超過 5 µm 的大顆粒，就好像把西瓜籽混進果汁——這些「西瓜籽」會卡在肺微血管中，引起肺栓塞（pulmonary embolism）。PFAT5 就是在測量這杯果汁裡有多少「西瓜籽」。

重點提示 Key Notes

為什麼需要兩種方法？這是 〈729〉 設計的精妙之處：

  • Method I（DLS/光散射）：測量平均粒徑，靈敏度高，適合 1–1000 nm 範圍的小粒子，但無法準確計數或定量大顆粒。
  • Method II（光遮蔽法）：專門計數和定量 >5 µm 的大粒子，可直接計算 PFAT5 值。

兩者互補——DLS 告訴你平均品質，光遮蔽法告訴你安全風險。

實務應用 Practical Application

對於 CDMO 製造脂質注射乳劑的場景：PFAT5 不只是放行指標，更是製程穩定性的預警信號。如果在批次製造過程中發現 PFAT5 上升趨勢，可能意味著均質化（homogenization）製程參數飄移、乳化劑比例失衡或儲存溫度異常——越早發現，越能避免整批報廢的損失。

Method I — Dynamic Light Scattering (DLS) / Classical Light Scattering | 動態光散射法

原文內容 Original Text

METHOD I — MEASUREMENT OF MEAN DROPLET DIAMETER BY DYNAMIC LIGHT SCATTERING OR CLASSICAL LIGHT SCATTERING

For the determination of the mean droplet size of lipid injectable emulsions, either of two common light-scattering techniques may be employed: (1) dynamic light scattering (DLS), also known as photon correlation spectroscopy (PCS), or (2) classical light scattering [laser diffraction (LD)], based on Mie scattering theory.

The DLS, or PCS, technique is based on analyzing the rapid temporal fluctuations in the scattered light intensity that occur due to the random Brownian motion, or diffusion, of any particles, including lipid droplets, suspended in liquid. The intensity is measured at a given angle (usually 90°) by a suitable detector (e.g., photomultiplier tube) able to measure the rapidly fluctuating scattered light intensity produced by the suspended, diffusing droplets.

These scattered intensity data are typically used to calculate the intensity autocorrelation function, which is a simple decaying exponential function in time for droplets of uniform size. A distribution of droplet sizes expresses itself by the superposition, or summation, of exponential functions of different decay times, each representative of a given droplet size. The autocorrelation function can be "inverted" by means of an appropriate deconvolution algorithm in order to obtain the approximate distribution of intensity-weighted diffusion coefficients. From the latter, the distribution of small-diameter droplets is calculated, using the Stokes–Einstein equation and the rules of classical (Mie) light scattering.

By contrast, classical light scattering based on Mie theory analyzes the spatial (angular), rather than temporal, variation of the scattered light intensity by measuring the latter as a function of the scattering angle, typically over a large range of detected angles. The extent of the angular variation is significant whenever the droplet diameter is not small compared with the wavelength of the laser light (typically 633–635 nm).

Thus, light scattering, using either DLS (varying temporal fluctuations due to droplet diffusion) or classical light scattering/Mie theory (varying, average intensity versus angle), can provide acceptable results for both the mean diameter and, to a lesser degree, the standard deviation of the droplet size distribution.

For guidance regarding instruments employing classical Mie-theory light scattering, see Light Diffraction Measurement of Particle Size 〈429〉.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Dynamic Light Scattering, DLS（動態光散射）：又稱光子相關光譜法（Photon Correlation Spectroscopy, PCS）。原理是：懸浮的粒子因布朗運動（Brownian motion）不斷隨機移動，導致其散射的光強度隨時間快速波動。粒子越小，擴散越快，強度波動越頻繁。DLS 分析這些時間域的強度波動，計算粒子的擴散係數，再透過 Stokes–Einstein 方程式換算成粒徑。

Stokes–Einstein Equation（史托克斯-愛因斯坦方程式）：D = kT / (3πηd)，其中 D 為擴散係數、k 為波茲曼常數、T 為溫度、η 為黏度、d 為粒徑。DLS 測量 D，計算 d。

公式與計算 Formula

Stokes–Einstein Equation:
  D = kT / (3πηd)

Where:
  D = diffusion coefficient (擴散係數)
  k = Boltzmann constant (波茲曼常數) = 1.38×10⁻²³ J/K
  T = absolute temperature (絕對溫度, K)
  η = dynamic viscosity of solvent (溶劑動態黏度)
  d = hydrodynamic diameter (流體動力學直徑)

DLS measures D → calculates d
MDD must be < 500 nm (0.5 µm) per USP 〈729〉

比喻說明 Analogy

DLS 就像在黑暗中用手電筒觀察池塘中漂浮的浮球。小浮球（小粒子）被水流（熱運動）推動得很快，光的閃爍頻率高；大浮球移動緩慢，閃爍頻率低。透過分析閃爍的快慢，就能推算浮球的大小——這就是 DLS 的基本邏輯。古典光散射（Laser Diffraction）則是改看浮球在不同角度反射光的強度圖案，就像不同大小的石頭丟入水中產生不同的波紋圖案一樣。

重點提示 Key Notes

DLS 的局限性（為何需要 Method II）：

  • DLS 是「強度加權」方法——大粒子的散射光強度遠高於小粒子（與粒徑六次方成正比），會掩蓋少量大粒子的存在。
  • DLS 主要適用於 1–1000 nm 範圍，對 >5 µm 的大顆粒只提供半定量資訊。
  • DLS 不計數粒子，無法直接計算 PFAT5。
  • USP 明文指出：「DLS is only a sizing method and provides only semi-quantitative information on the relative droplet size distribution.」

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. DLS 與 Laser Diffraction（LD）都屬於光散射技術，但原理截然不同——前者分析時間域波動，後者分析角度域強度。為什麼 USP 允許兩者都可用於 Method I？它們的結果有何差異？
  2. DLS 測量的是「強度加權平均粒徑」而非數量加權平均粒徑，這對 PFAT5 的評估意味著什麼風險？

本節重點回顧 Section Summary

  • 脂質注射乳劑中 >5 µm 的大脂肪球是肺栓塞的潛在風險，PFAT5 是衡量此風險的關鍵指標
  • USP 〈729〉 採用兩階段方法：Method I（DLS/光散射）測量平均粒徑，Method II（光遮蔽法）定量大顆粒並計算 PFAT5
  • DLS 基於布朗運動引起的光強度時間波動，利用 Stokes–Einstein 方程式換算粒徑；限制在於無法計數粒子或精確定量大粒子
  • 兩種方法均需經過驗證（validated），系統適用性（System Suitability）為每次分析的前提
