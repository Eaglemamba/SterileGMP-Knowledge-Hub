Method I — Apparatus, Standard Preparation & Procedure | 儀器設備、標準製劑與程序

原文內容 Original Text

Apparatus

A suitable DLS/PCS instrument with or without the capability of automatic sample dilution is controlled by validated software and is used to perform the measurement, with the scattering angle typically set at 90°. The intensity-weighted results (mean diameter and standard deviation) are reported, provided it is clearly stated which values are given and that the necessary parameter values required for all requisite calculations are also given.

Water

Pass distilled water through a filter of 0.2-µm pore size, and degas by sonication, or use Sterile Water for Injection stored in a glass container.

Standard Preparation

To a pre-established volume of Water add an appropriate amount of concentrated suspension, containing National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST)-traceable polystyrene latex standard particles or other suitable nanospheres. Gently mix the fluids to achieve a homogeneous suspension. This should be performed separately for three different size standards of approximately 100, 250, and 400 nm (triplicate analyses per size), and the corresponding results of intensity-weighted mean diameter and standard deviation should coincide with the expected values within acceptable errors.

Test Preparation

To a pre-established volume of Water add an appropriate volume of sample from the lipid injectable emulsion. Gently mix the fluids to achieve a homogeneous suspension. The concentration of lipid injectable emulsion in the final sample must be optimized for the DLS/PCS instrument used. Testing should be done in triplicate or, as appropriate, based on method validation data.

System Suitability

Using the Standard Preparation, measure the intensity-weighted mean particle diameter and the corresponding standard deviation. The system is suitable once the sample temperature has reached equilibration and the results have stabilized and triplicate mean droplet diameter measurements are obtained. The coefficient of variation (CV) should not exceed 15% of the NIST-traceable mean droplet diameter. A larger CV value indicates that the latex microspheres are not suitable as a standard because they either inherently lack uniformity or have become agglomerated to an unacceptable extent.

Procedure and Interpretation

Allow the sample to equilibrate to a preset controlled temperature close to ambient (between 20° and 25°). Set the instrument scattering angle to 90°, and carry out the measurements. As long as the chi-square (χ²) goodness-of-fit parameter remains acceptably low (per instrument specifications), the results for the Test preparation are acceptable. Excessive values of the χ² parameter suggest that the droplet distribution is not normal and may indicate an unstable emulsion.

"The intensity-weighted mean droplet diameter (MDD) for lipid injectable emulsions must be less than 500 nm or 0.5 µm, irrespective of the concentration of the dispersed lipid phase."

Submicron results from the DLS method and classical (Mie) light scattering method may vary due to the different physical principles of the mechanism of testing and the different mathematical procedures used to analyze the underlying data.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

NIST-Traceable Standard（NIST 可追溯標準）：美國國家標準與技術研究院（NIST）認證的聚苯乙烯乳膠球，具有已知且可追溯的粒徑。USP 要求使用 100、250、400 nm 三個尺寸標準，覆蓋脂肪乳劑正常粒徑範圍，確保儀器校正在分析前已通過驗證。

Intensity-Weighted Mean Diameter（強度加權平均粒徑）：DLS 報告的粒徑是以散射光強度為權重計算的平均值，而非數量加權。由於大粒子的散射強度遠大於小粒子，強度加權均值通常大於數量加權均值——使用者必須清楚報告的是哪種加權方式。

Chi-Square (χ²) Goodness-of-Fit（卡方適合度）：DLS 儀器用來評估測量數據與假設的粒徑分佈模型（通常為對數常態分佈）的吻合程度。χ² 過高代表乳劑粒徑分佈異常，可能是乳化不穩定或已發生破乳（coalescence）的訊號。

重點提示 Key Notes

MDD < 500 nm 是硬性標準，不依賴油脂濃度：無論乳劑中脂肪含量是 10%、20% 還是 30%，平均粒徑都必須小於 500 nm。這是 USP 〈729〉 Method I 的單一核心接受標準。

CV ≤ 15% 的系統適用性：這個 15% 的限度比一般分析方法寬鬆，是因為 DLS 與 LD（Laser Diffraction）計算均值的方式不同——USP 特別在附注中說明了這一點。如果 CV 超標，不能進行樣品分析，必須更換標準品並重新評估。

公式與計算 Formula

Method I Acceptance Criterion:
  MDD (Mean Droplet Diameter) < 500 nm (0.5 µm)
  — Irrespective of lipid concentration —

System Suitability:
  CV of triplicate MDD measurements ≤ 15%
  CV = (Standard Deviation / Mean) × 100%

Temperature Control:
  Sample equilibration: 20°C – 25°C
  (per USP 〈659〉 ambient definition)

比喻說明 Analogy

System Suitability 就像每天開車前先確認煞車功能正常。你不會直接上路就踩煞車測試——你先在停車場低速確認系統運作正常，才敢上高速公路。DLS 的系統適用性（用 NIST 標準品確認儀器讀值準確）就是這道「上路前的煞車測試」，通過了才能分析真實樣品。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 如果某批脂肪乳劑的 MDD = 480 nm，但 χ² 值明顯偏高，您作為品質主管應如何決策？光是 MDD < 500 nm 是否足以放行？
  2. 系統適用性 CV ≤ 15% 的要求為何比一般 CV ≤ 2% 的分析方法寬鬆得多？這說明 DLS 作為定量工具的哪些局限性？
Method II — Light Obscuration (LE/SPOS) | 光遮蔽法 / 單粒子光學定粒法

原文內容 Original Text

METHOD II — MEASUREMENT OF LARGE GLOBULE CONTENT BY LIGHT OBSCURATION OR EXTINCTION METHOD

For determination of the extent of the large-diameter droplet tail (>5 µm) of lipid injectable emulsions, a light obscuration (LO) or light extinction (LE) method that employs a single-particle (globule) optical sizing (SPOS) technique is used.

DLS methodology is effective only for characterizing smaller particle sizes in the nominal size range of about 1–1000 nm; therefore, DLS is complementary to LE and is particularly effective in sizing droplets smaller than 500 nm. Importantly, DLS is only a sizing method and provides only semi-quantitative information on the relative droplet size distribution. DLS does not count particles or droplets; thus, it is mainly a qualitative method. In contrast, LE both sizes (1.3–400 µm) and counts fat globules providing "reliable" count values at a size threshold of 5 µm.

During application of the LE/SPOS technique, passage of a droplet through a thin optical sensing zone results in effective blockage of a portion of the incident light beam, causing a momentary decrease in the light intensity reaching the "extinction" detector. The magnitude of this decrease in the signal is related to the cross-sectional area of the droplet, i.e., to the square of the droplet diameter. The larger the droplet diameter, the larger the height (voltage) of the signal pulse produced.

As long as the fat globule concentration is below the "coincidence limit" of the sensor, only one globule at most will pass through the sensing zone at any given time, allowing it to be counted and accurately sized (with less than 1% particle or globule coincidence events).

The resulting single pulse heights are converted to droplet diameters using a standard calibration curve previously constructed from NIST-traceable monosized polystyrene microspheres of known diameters. For additional guidance in the use of the light obscuration methodology, see Particulate Matter in Injections 〈788〉.

Apparatus

A suitable light obscuration instrument with or without the capability of automatic sample dilution and controlled by a personal computer (PC) is used for the measurement. The number- and volume-weighted particle size distribution data are reported, provided that it is clearly stated which values are given and that the necessary parameter values required for all necessary calculations are also given.

Standard Preparation (Method II)

The sizing and counting accuracy of the light obscuration instrument should be obtained using two different size standards of approximately 5 and 10 µm (triplicate analyses per size). Set the instrument threshold of detection at 1.8 µm, extended to an upper limit of 50 µm. The corresponding results for the mean diameter should coincide with the expected values, within 10% of the relative standard deviation and 90%–110% size accuracy. The number of particle counts obtained per milliliter should also agree within ±10% with the concentration values certified in the documentation provided with each NIST-traceable size standard.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Light Obscuration / Light Extinction (LE)（光遮蔽法/光消光法）：粒子通過細光束時，遮擋部分光線，導致偵測器接收到的光強度瞬間下降。脈衝高度（voltage pulse height）與粒子截面積（即粒徑平方）成正比。與 〈788〉 中用於注射劑微粒監控的技術是同一原理。

Single-Particle Optical Sizing (SPOS)（單粒子光學定粒法）：一次只讓一個粒子通過感測區（sensing zone），確保每個脈衝信號對應一個粒子，實現精確計數與定粒。這是 LE 方法能夠可靠計算 PFAT5 的關鍵——它是一個真正的「計數」方法。

Coincidence Limit（符合限值）：當粒子濃度過高時，兩個粒子可能同時通過感測區，被誤計為一個大粒子，造成「重合誤差」（coincidence error）。必須通過適當稀釋確保濃度低於此限值（符合事件 <1%）。

比喻說明 Analogy

LE/SPOS 就像收費站的電子感應系統（ETC）：每輛車（每個粒子）依序通過感測區，系統記錄每輛車的「大小」（脈衝高度）並計數。如果同時來了兩輛摩托車，系統可能誤判為一輛轎車——這就是「符合誤差」。因此稀釋樣品的目的不只是讓儀器可以量測，更是確保粒子排隊一個一個通過，計數準確。

重點提示 Key Notes

LE 標準品選用 5 µm 和 10 µm（非 DLS 的 100/250/400 nm）：這清楚說明兩種方法覆蓋截然不同的粒徑範圍。Method I 驗證儀器在奈米尺度（<500 nm）的準確性，Method II 驗證儀器在微米尺度（>5 µm）的準確性。

偵測範圍設定：下限 1.8 µm，上限 50 µm：下限 1.8 µm 比目標閾值 5 µm 更低，是為了確保 5 µm 附近的粒子也能被可靠偵測；上限 50 µm 則覆蓋臨床上可能有害的最大脂肪球尺寸。

計數精度要求 ±10%：與 〈788〉 對注射劑微粒計數的精度要求一致——這是光遮蔽法在計數應用上的標準驗收準則。

實務應用 Practical Application

CDMO 執行 〈729〉 Method II 時，稀釋方案（dilution scheme）的開發是方法驗證中最關鍵的步驟之一。不同脂肪濃度（10%、20%、30% TPN 乳劑）需要不同的稀釋倍數才能達到符合限值以下。在常規放行測試中，稀釋方案必須標準化並記錄在 SOP 中——方法驗證報告中必須包含稀釋線性性的驗證資料（dilution linearity study）。

Method II — Procedure, Interpretation & PFAT5 Acceptance Criteria | 程序、解讀與 PFAT5 接受標準

原文內容 Original Text

System Suitability (Method II)

Perform prior to the test procedure, using the Standard preparation of a 5- and 10-µm NIST-traceable particle. Measure in triplicate the number-weighted particle diameter and the counts/mL of the standard. The system is suitable when the triplicate mean number-weighted particle diameter measurements are within 10% of the target value, both in terms of repeatability (CV) and closeness to the certified size on the label of the NIST-traceable standard.

Procedure and Interpretation

Set the instrument threshold of detection at 1.8 µm, extended to an upper size limit of 50 µm, and vary the concentration and/or data collection times such that there is at least a factor of two in the difference of the total number of globules that measure >5 µm between at least two sample runs. In any case, the number of globules that measure >5 µm should be large enough so that it represents an adequate number of globules that are statistically representative of the large-diameter tail population of the native emulsion.

"The volume-weighted, large-diameter fat globule limits of the dispersed phase, expressed as the percentage of fat residing in globules larger than 5 µm (PFAT5) for a given lipid injectable emulsion, must not exceed 0.05%."

Most commonly used instruments provide calculation in software (HIAC Royco, PSS AccuSizer, Klotz). However, the general equation applied is based on the sum of the number of droplets per milliliter of sample per channel multiplied by the volume of a sphere of the geometric mean of each channel:

PFAT5 = Σ(#/mL/channel) × (π D³/6) for D > 5 µm

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

PFAT5 ≤ 0.05% 是什麼概念？換算成直覺的數字：在 100 mL 的脂肪乳劑中，所有直徑大於 5 µm 的脂肪球體積總和，不能超過 0.05 mL（50 µL）。這個限度由美國靜脈輸注護理學會（ASHP/ASPEN）根據靜脈輸注安全研究確立，已被 USP 正式採納。

Volume-Weighted vs. Number-Weighted（體積加權 vs. 數量加權）：選擇體積加權計算 PFAT5，是因為肺部損傷的機制與大脂肪球的體積（即實際脂肪量）直接相關，而不僅僅是個數。一個 20 µm 的大球，其體積相當於 64 個 5 µm 球（體積 ∝ 直徑³）——用數量加權會嚴重低估風險。

重點提示 Key Notes

「至少兩次採樣間 >5 µm 球數差兩倍」的要求：這是一個統計充分性（statistical adequacy）要求，確保計數到足夠數量的大粒子，使 PFAT5 計算結果具有統計代表性。如果採樣次數不足，即使每次結果都符合 ≤ 0.05%，也可能因計數不足而缺乏可靠性。

PVC/DEHP 容器禁忌：USP 在腳注中特別提醒，PVC 含 DEHP（鄰苯二甲酸二乙基己酯）的管路已被證實會破壞脂肪乳劑的乳化穩定性——實驗室樣品準備時必須使用 Type I 玻璃容器，避免 DEHP 造成假陽性（PFAT5 偏高）。

實務應用 Practical Application

在 CDMO 製造 TPN（全靜脈營養液）或脂質奈米粒（Lipid Nanoparticles, LNP）的場景中，PFAT5 的監測策略應整合到製程驗證（Process Validation）與持續製程確認（Continued Process Verification, CPV）中。監控趨勢圖（trending chart）可以在 PFAT5 尚未超出 ≤ 0.05% 標準時，就提前偵測到製程漂移（process drift），為 CAPA 提供更多提前量。LNP 疫苗製造（如 mRNA 脂質奈米粒）雖然原始粒徑更小（約 80–120 nm），但若乳化失控也可能產生超大聚集體，〈729〉的監控原則同樣具有參考價值。

本節重點回顧 Section Summary

  • Method I（DLS）接受標準：MDD < 500 nm；系統適用性 CV ≤ 15%；χ² 值須在儀器規格內
  • Method II（LE/SPOS）接受標準：PFAT5 ≤ 0.05%（體積加權，>5 µm 脂肪球佔總分散相比例）
  • PFAT5 計算公式為體積加權（∝ Di³）的原因：反映大粒子的臨床安全風險比數量加權更準確
  • 兩種方法均需通過 NIST 可追溯標準品的系統適用性驗證，方可進行樣品分析
  • 交叉參考：〈429〉（LD）、〈788〉（注射劑微粒）；樣品製備避免 PVC/DEHP 容器
