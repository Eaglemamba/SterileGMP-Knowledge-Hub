Introduction — 概論

Original Text 原文內容

Dry heat sterilization is a process utilized for heat-stable items (glass, stainless steel, nonaqueous liquids, powders, etc.) that are unsuited for steam sterilization because of either an absence of water (nonaqueous liquids and powders) or requirements for absolute dryness following processing (product contact parts for nonaqueous products). Because dry heat relies on air for the transfer of heat to and from the load items, the process takes longer than a steam process for a comparable size item or load. Lengthy heating and cooling periods require that the load items be unaffected by heat over a long period of time and also require the use of the overkill method for cycle development and validation.

Dry heat sterilization is typically performed in the range of 160°–190° where the objective is sterilization rather than depyrogenation. (Depyrogenation will be covered separately in Dry Heat Depyrogenation &langle;1228.1&rangle;). In dry heat sterilization, hot air is in direct contact with the load items (whether wrapped or unwrapped) and transfers some of its thermal energy. Unlike steam sterilization, in dry heat sterilization there is no phase change of the heating medium, and thus heat transfer is less efficient. The items can be stainless steel, glass, ceramic, or other heat-stable materials and may be wrapped or covered with aluminum foil to protect them during pre- and postprocess handling. Dry heat sterilization is commonly used for heat-stable materials (e.g., petrolatum or powders).

The limited heat transfer capacity of air requires that items in the oven be placed in locations that were confirmed to be acceptable during the validation effort. Manufacturers should exercise caution with varying load sizes because in some instances (resulting from system design and control probe positioning) minimum load sizes may present a worst case.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Dry Heat Sterilization（乾熱滅菌）：利用高溫空氣（而非蒸氣）殺死微生物的滅菌方法。適用對象包括：

  • 玻璃容器 — 西林瓶（Vials）、安瓿、注射筒
  • 不銹鋼部件 — 充填針頭、漏斗、管路接頭
  • 非水性液體 — 凡士林（Petrolatum）、礦物油
  • 粉體 — 原料藥粉末（需在乾燥狀態下滅菌）

為什麼不用蒸氣？蒸氣滅菌（121°C, 15 min）效率更高，但會在物品表面留下水分。對於無水製劑的產品接觸面，即使微量殘留水分也可能破壞產品穩定性。

重點提示 Key Notes

乾熱 vs 蒸氣 — 效率差異的物理根源：

蒸氣滅菌利用水蒸氣的相變潛熱（Latent Heat）— 每克水蒸氣凝結時釋放約 2,260 焦耳的熱能，直接傳遞到物品表面。乾熱僅靠空氣的對流傳熱（Convection），空氣的比熱容（Specific Heat Capacity）遠低於水蒸氣，因此需要更高的溫度和更長的時間才能達到相同的致死效果。

最小負載 = 最差情境：這是一個容易被忽略的重點。在烘箱中，當負載量太少時，控制探頭可能偵測不到真實的腔內溫度變化（因為負載吸熱量不足），導致系統提前判定達標。驗證時必須同時測試最大和最小負載。

CDMO 實務觀點

Depyrogenation Tunnel 的雙重功能：在 CDMO 的無菌充填線上，除熱原隧道（Depyrogenation Tunnel）操作在 ≥250°C，遠超乾熱滅菌的溫度範圍（160–190°C）。這意味著除熱原隧道同時達成了滅菌 + 除熱原兩個目標。但本章 &langle;1229.8&rangle; 聚焦於「乾熱滅菌」，而非除熱原 — 除熱原的驗證要求（≥3 log 內毒素降低）另見 &langle;1228.1&rangle;。

Sterilization Cycle Control — 滅菌週期控制

Original Text 原文內容

Process equipment for dry heat sterilization is controlled by calibrated temperature sensors. During the exposure portions of the cycle, attainment of a minimum dwell time at a predefined temperature is used to document process lethality. Cycle efficacy for dry heat sterilization customarily is measured using FH, which typically is defined as the amount of time the load receives the equivalent of exposure at 170°. The FH approach is used to compare sterilization processes that operate at varying temperature conditions to a single standard. The process lethality at temperatures other than 170° can be calculated to determine lethality equivalent to that provided at 170°.

Sterilizer control systems must deliver conditions within a predefined time–temperature or FH range. Simple mathematics can be used to calculate the total lethality over the course of the process.

Accumulation of the lethality (FH) for the sterilization process across the entire cycle (heat-up and cool-down segments included) includes the contribution of those segments and allows the cycle to be defined by a targeted lethality rather than by a time at a defined minimum temperature.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 FH Value

FH（Dry Heat Lethality）：乾熱滅菌的致死率度量，概念上類似蒸氣滅菌的 F0，但參考條件不同：

z 值 = 20°C 的意義：溫度每升高 20°C，滅菌速率增加 10 倍。例如：在 190°C 操作 1 分鐘，等效於 170°C 操作 10 分鐘。

FH Calculation — 致死率計算公式

Original Text 原文內容

For the specific reference temperature of 170° and a z-value (for definitions see Steam Sterilization by Direct Contact &langle;1229.1&rangle;) of 20°, the FH calculation can be determined by the following equation:

FH Equation (Integral Form)

         t2
F_H  =  ∫   10^((T - 170) / 20) dt
         t1

Discrete summation form:

         t2
F_H  =  ∑   10^((T - 170) / 20) × Δt
         t1

Where:

  • FH = accumulated lethality (equivalent minutes at 170°C)
  • t1 = start time
  • t2 = end time
  • T = temperature (°C) at each time interval
  • Δt = time interval (typically 1 minute)

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

公式拆解 Formula Breakdown

核心邏輯：將整個乾熱滅菌週期（包含升溫和降溫階段）的每一分鐘溫度讀數，換算成等效的「170°C 分鐘數」，然後加總。

計算範例：

假設某一分鐘的溫度 T = 180°C：
  L = 10^((180 - 170) / 20) = 10^(0.5) = 3.16

意義：在 180°C 的 1 分鐘
     = 在 170°C 的 3.16 分鐘

假設另一分鐘的溫度 T = 160°C：
  L = 10^((160 - 170) / 20) = 10^(-0.5) = 0.316

意義：在 160°C 的 1 分鐘
     = 僅等於 170°C 的 0.316 分鐘

重點提示 Key Notes

升溫 + 降溫段的貢獻：原文明確指出，FH 的計算涵蓋整個週期（heat-up and cool-down segments included）。這代表升溫和降溫階段也提供致死效果，可以計入總致死率。這對烘箱操作尤其重要，因為烘箱的升降溫時間可能占總週期的 40–60%。

與除熱原 FH 的區別：&langle;1228.1&rangle; 除熱原使用相同的公式形式，但參考溫度為 250°C，z 值為 46.4°C，且驗收標準不同（≥3 log 內毒素降低）。不要混淆。

Validation of Dry Heat Sterilization — 乾熱滅菌驗證

Original Text 原文內容

Because dry air has limited heat capacity and dry heat conditions are more variable than those encountered with other thermal sterilization methods, analysts routinely validate their dry heat sterilization procedures using the overkill method as defined in Sterilization of Compendial Articles &langle;1229&rangle;.

Overkill sterilization can be defined as a method in which the destruction of a high concentration of a resistant microorganism supports the destruction of reasonably anticipated bioburden that could be present during routine processing. That objective can be demonstrated by attaining any of the following: a defined minimum lethality; a defined set of method conditions; or confirmation of minimum log reduction of a resistant biological indicator.

The validation requirements for the overkill method are less onerous than those of the other sterilization approaches. Because the load items can withstand substantial amounts of heat without adverse consequence, the greater lethality provided by the overkill method clearly is justifiable.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Overkill Method

Overkill Method（過殺法）：乾熱滅菌幾乎都使用過殺法驗證，原因很簡單 — 被滅菌的物品（玻璃、金屬）耐熱性極高，完全能承受遠超最低需求的熱量。這與需要保護熱敏感產品的蒸氣滅菌形成對比。

過殺法的三種證明方式：

  • 定義最低致死率 — 例如 FH ≥ 30 分鐘（在 170°C 等效）
  • 定義製程條件 — 例如 170°C / 120 分鐘
  • 確認最低 log 降低 — 例如 ≥12 log 生物指示劑殺滅（6 log BI population + 6 log safety margin）

比喻說明 Analogy

用大砲打蚊子：過殺法的邏輯就像用大砲打蚊子 — 你可能只需要一把蒼蠅拍就夠了，但因為大砲不會傷到你的家具（耐熱物品），所以何不用大砲來確保萬無一失？這種「過度保障」的策略讓驗證文件大幅簡化，因為你不需要精確測定自然菌（Bioburden）的數量和抗性。

Equipment Qualification — 設備確效

Original Text 原文內容

Equipment qualification is a predefined program that focuses on the sterilizing equipment to confirm that it has been properly installed and operates as intended before evaluation of the sterilization process. In some companies, equipment qualification is separated into installation qualification and operational qualification or is lumped together under a joint terminology of installation/operational qualification. The major use of qualification of the sterilizing equipment is to provide a baseline for preventive maintenance and change control, ensuring reproducibility of operation over time and assurance that the sterilization process is constantly and accurately performed.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 IQ/OQ Framework

設備確效的目的：不是證明「滅菌有效」（那是 PQ 的工作），而是建立設備的操作基線（Baseline），確保設備安裝正確、運作符合設計規格。

IQ（Installation Qualification）：確認設備按照規格安裝 — 電源、管路連接、安全裝置、控制系統軟體版本等。

OQ（Operational Qualification）：確認設備在操作範圍內正常運作 — 溫度控制精度、加熱速率、風速均勻性、警報功能等。

為什麼要建立基線？設備確效產生的數據成為日後預防保養（PM）和變更控制的參考基準。例如，如果原始 OQ 記錄顯示加熱速率為 5°C/min，日後 PM 測試發現降到 3°C/min，這就觸發調查。

Empty Chamber Temperature Distribution — 空腔溫度分布

Original Text 原文內容

The equipment should be evaluated for empty chamber temperature distribution. The oven or tunnel should be evaluated to determine the range of temperatures within the system, and the cycle parameters should be determined to ensure adequate lethality across the expected load. The acceptance criteria for empty chambers can vary with the equipment capabilities and customary use, but it is typically less uniform than observed in steam sterilizers. Biological indicators are not required during the evaluation of empty chamber temperature distribution.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

重點提示 Key Notes

「比蒸氣滅菌器更不均勻」：這是原文直接承認的現實。蒸氣滅菌器中，飽和蒸氣在腔內快速擴散，溫度均勻性通常在 ±0.5°C 以內。乾熱烘箱依靠空氣對流，均勻性可能在 ±5°C 甚至更大範圍。這也是為什麼乾熱滅菌需要使用過殺法 — 較大的溫度變異需要更大的安全裕度來補償。

不需要 BI：空腔測試階段僅測物理參數（溫度分布），不需要放入生物指示劑。BI 在後續的熱穿透階段才使用。

CDMO 實務 — Tunnel vs Oven：

  • 隧道式：空腔測試關注各加熱區（Zone）的溫度梯度、輸送帶速度與溫度的對應關係
  • 烘箱式：關注腔內不同位置（角落、中心、上下層架）的溫度差異
Component Mapping — 組件佈局

Original Text 原文內容

Load items that are complex and feature enclosed volumes and product contact surfaces should be subjected to component mapping to determine internal cold spots. This is particularly important in powder sterilization. For each load item, manufacturers should establish the ability of heat to penetrate the items or containers and to bring them to the required temperature. These studies can be performed in a laboratory setting and need not be repeated when the same item is sterilized in other equipment. Thermocouples should be placed into direct contact with the item(s) being evaluated. During component mapping load items should be prepared and oriented in a manner that is consistent with how they will be processed.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Component Mapping

Component Mapping（組件佈局）：針對單一物品的熱穿透研究，目的是找出物品內部的冷點（Cold Spot）— 即最慢達到目標溫度的位置。

何時需要？當物品結構複雜、有封閉空間或產品接觸面時。例如：

  • 粉體容器 — 粉體的導熱性差，容器中心溫度可能遠低於表面
  • 多層包裝的金屬部件 — 鋁箔包裹的充填針頭組件
  • 複雜幾何形狀 — 有內腔的不銹鋼部件

可轉移性：原文指出組件佈局的結果可以跨設備使用 — 因為它測的是物品本身的熱穿透特性，與特定設備無關。這對 CDMO 很有價值：一次測試即可適用於多條產線。

Load Mapping — 負載佈局

Original Text 原文內容

Fixed loading patterns for dry heat sterilization in batch ovens are preferable because the limited heat capacity of the air allows substantial temperature differences across the load. It may be possible to validate maximum and minimum loads as determined by either the number of items or their mass within the oven. Loading in a continuous tunnel process is typically well defined by the limitations of the conveying system. Load and component mapping ensures that all load items attain the required temperature. Information from the load mapping is used to adjust cycle timing to ensure appropriate lethality. System control must consider the relationship between load position and size relative to temperature control locations.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

重點提示 Key Notes

Fixed Loading Pattern（固定擺放模式）：原文強調烘箱應使用固定的負載擺放模式。在 CDMO 實務中，這表示必須建立Loading SOP，配合照片或圖示明確定義每批次的擺放位置和數量。如果操作人員隨意擺放，驗證結果就沒有意義。

核心概念解析 Load Mapping

Load Mapping（負載佈局）：測試整個負載在設備中的溫度分布，找出負載整體的最差條件。

Biological Indicators — 生物指示劑

Original Text 原文內容

The biological indicator (BI) for dry heat sterilization is Bacillus atrophaeus (ATCC 9372), a thermophilic spore-former with high resistance to dry heat. The spore challenge is placed on a substrate positioned within the load or on a load item. If spores are used as intended by the BI manufacturer, the population and resistance information provided by the vendor can be used. End users should determine the population and resistance of their biological indicator used when inoculating their own items.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

重點提示 Key Notes

供應商 BI vs 自製接種：原文區分了兩種使用方式：

  • 使用供應商提供的 BI — 可直接使用供應商提供的菌數和抗性數據（D 值、菌數），無需自行測定
  • 自行接種到物品上 — 必須自行測定接種後的實際菌數和抗性，因為基質（Substrate）的改變可能影響芽孢的乾熱抗性

CDMO 實務建議：除非有特殊需求，建議使用供應商的商品化 BI（如 Spore Strip 或 Spore Disc），可以大幅減少測試工作量和文件負擔。

核心概念解析 BI Selection

Bacillus atrophaeus（ATCC 9372）：乾熱滅菌專用的生物指示劑菌株。選擇它的原因是其芽孢對乾熱的抗性極高，是自然環境中可能遇到的微生物中最難殺死的之一。

Heat Penetration & Microbiological Challenge — 熱穿透與微生物挑戰

Original Text 原文內容

The core of the validation activity is the confirmation of acceptable heat penetration using temperature measurements and microbial challenges. Thermocouples and BIs are placed within the load items at the locations determined during the component and load mapping to present the worst case. Thermocouples should be placed into direct contact with the item(s) being monitored. Proof of cycle efficacy is provided by replicate studies in which the BIs are killed and the physical measurements correspond to the expected values of time–temperature or FH.

If the microbial and physical measurements do not correlate, manufacturers should conduct an investigation and should take corrective action to rectify the discrepancy. This study customarily is performed slightly subminimal to the lower specification limits for time, temperature, and/or cumulative lethality.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Validation Core

熱穿透 + 微生物挑戰 = 驗證的核心：這是 PQ（Performance Qualification）的本質。兩組數據必須同時收集、交叉驗證：

  • 物理數據（Thermocouples）— 證明每個測試點都達到目標溫度和 FH
  • 微生物數據（BIs）— 證明在最差條件下，所有 BI 都被殺滅

如果物理數據顯示「達標」但 BI 未被殺滅，或物理數據「未達標」但 BI 全部死亡，都需要啟動調查。這種不一致可能暗示熱電偶位置不正確、BI 放置不當、或製程存在未知問題。

重點提示 Subminimal Approach

「略低於下限規格」（Slightly Subminimal）：這是驗證的關鍵策略。不是在正常條件下測試，而是刻意縮短時間或降低溫度到接近（但略低於）操作規格下限。如果在這種「半劑量」條件下 BI 仍然全部被殺死，就證明正常操作條件下有充分的安全裕度。

為什麼不直接用正常條件測？因為正常條件會提供過多的致死率，可能掩蓋負載中的冷點問題。半週期測試能更敏感地檢測出溫度分布不均的問題。

Routine Process Control — 常規製程控制

Original Text 原文內容

As with all sterilization processes, after the dry heat sterilization process has been validated, it must be subject to formalized controls that keep it in a validated state over time. General chapter Sterilization of Compendial Articles &langle;1229&rangle; details the general practices that are appropriate for all sterilization systems. This is accomplished by a number of related practices that are essential for the continued use of the process over an extended period of time. The essential practices to maintain validated status include calibration, physical measurements, physical integrators and indicators, ongoing process control, change control, preventive maintenance, and periodic reassessment and training.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Maintaining Validated State

驗證不是一次性事件：通過 PQ 只是起點。原文列出了維持驗證狀態的七大要素：

  1. Calibration（校準） — 溫度感測器的定期校準，確保讀數準確
  2. Physical Measurements（物理量測） — 每次運行記錄的時間–溫度數據
  3. Physical Integrators/Indicators（物理整合器/指示器） — 化學指示帶、溫度指示標籤等
  4. Ongoing Process Control（持續製程控制） — 監控趨勢、偵測偏移
  5. Change Control（變更控制） — 任何設備或製程變更都需評估對驗證狀態的影響
  6. Preventive Maintenance（預防保養） — 按計劃執行，不能等設備故障再修
  7. Periodic Reassessment & Training（定期再評估與訓練） — 週期性回顧製程表現，持續培訓操作人員

CDMO 實務觀點

Change Control 的陷阱：在 CDMO 環境中，變更控制特別容易被忽略的情境包括：

  • 更換西林瓶供應商 — 不同玻璃材質可能有不同的導熱特性
  • 更換 BI 批號或供應商 — 新批號的 D 值可能不同
  • 更換加熱元件或風扇 — 影響溫度分布和空氣循環模式
  • 修改負載模式 — 即使只是改變物品間距

以上任何變更都應觸發變更控制流程，評估是否需要重新驗證。

  • 乾熱滅菌的適用範圍：耐熱且不適合蒸氣滅菌的物品（玻璃、金屬、粉體、非水性液體），操作溫度 160–190°C
  • FH 計算：參考溫度 170°C、z 值 20°C，計算涵蓋升溫 + 保溫 + 降溫全週期的累積致死率
  • 驗證策略：因物品耐熱性高，幾乎一律採用 Overkill Method，簡化驗證需求
  • 驗證四步驟：設備確效（IQ/OQ）→ 空腔溫度分布 → 組件佈局 + 負載佈局 → 熱穿透 + 微生物挑戰（PQ）
  • BI 選擇：Bacillus atrophaeus（ATCC 9372）— 乾熱滅菌專用指示菌，高度耐乾熱
  • Subminimal 測試：在略低於規格下限的條件下進行驗證，以確保正常操作有充分安全裕度
  • 與 &langle;1228.1&rangle; 的關係：除熱原隧道（≥250°C）同時達成滅菌 + 除熱原；本章僅涵蓋 160–190°C 的滅菌目的
  • 維持驗證狀態：校準、製程監控、變更控制、預防保養、定期再評估 — 七大要素缺一不可
