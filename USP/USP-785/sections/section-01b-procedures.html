Operation and Calibration — Standard Solutions 標準液配製與校正

原文內容 Original Text

Standard Solutions

Solutions for instrument calibration may be purchased or may be prepared as specified in Table 1. Other concentrations not listed in Table 1 are acceptable. They may be stored at 2°–30°, but should not be frozen. When using purchased solutions, follow the manufacturer's expiry date and use instructions. Purchased solutions are typically provided in single-use ampules and should be discarded after use.

Solutions prepared as specified in Table 1 should be stored in nonpermeable (e.g., glass) containers to prevent evaporation. Fresh solutions should be prepared at least monthly as they are often nonsterile unpreserved solutions.

Calibration

The osmometer should be calibrated as per the manufacturer's instructions using either purchased or prepared Standard Solutions (as described in Table 1).

Procedure for Calibration Check

The calibration check should be performed in the same manner that measurements will be performed:

  • If individual measurements will be reported → calibration check with a single measurement
  • If mean of three measurements will be reported → calibration check with the mean of three measurements

Unless a different solution is specified, select at least one solution from Table 1 that lies near the center of the expected range of osmolality for the test solution. Two Standard Solutions bracketing the test sample within 300 mOsmol/kg are also acceptable.

Acceptance criteria:

  • Solutions ≤ 400 mOsmol/kg: within ±4 mOsmol/kg from stated value
  • Solutions > 400 mOsmol/kg: within ±1% from stated value

If the instrument fails the calibration check, recalibrate per manufacturer's instructions, then repeat the calibration check. After a successful check, the instrument is ready for use.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

校正 vs. 校正確認（Calibration vs. Calibration Check）的重要區別：

  • Calibration（完整校正）：至少兩點（低 + 高標準液），調整儀器讀數使其符合標準值。通常在開始使用新標準液時或 PQ 定期重新校正時執行。
  • Calibration Check（校正確認）：每日使用前的快速確認，不調整儀器，只判斷「當前是否仍在接受標準內」。若不通過，才執行完整校正再確認。

重點提示 Key Notes

校正確認標準液選擇邏輯：

  • 首選：在預期測量範圍中心點的標準液（最佳代表性）
  • 備選：兩個「夾住」測量值且各自在 ±300 mOsmol/kg 內的標準液
  • 目的：確保校正在最相關的濃度範圍內是準確的，而非只驗證了遠離實際使用點的範圍

標準液冷凍禁止：冷凍會破壞溶液均勻性（部分水先結冰，改變剩餘液體濃度），解凍後即使外觀正常，滲透量也可能偏移。

比喻說明 Analogy

校正確認就像每天早上上磅秤前先用已知重量的砝碼確認一下——你不是重新校準磅秤，而是確認它今天仍然準確。若砝碼顯示偏差，才需要重新校準。這樣的日常確認既省時間，又保證了每次測量都有當日的品質保證。

實務應用 Practical Application

在 CDMO 批次放行 SOP 中，校正確認結果應記入批次記錄（Batch Record）或儀器使用記錄。若校正確認失敗，需：(1) 執行完整校正；(2) 重新確認；(3) 評估失敗期間已測的樣品是否需要重新測試；(4) 記錄偏差報告（Deviation Report）。步驟 (3) 是最容易被忽略的，但也是最重要的患者安全保障措施。

Test Solutions & Method 1: Freezing-Point Osmometry 樣品處理與凍結點法程序

原文內容 Original Text

Test Solutions

For a solid for injection, reconstitute with the appropriate diluent as specified in the instructions on the labeling. For solutions, use the sample as is.

[Note—A solution can be diluted to bring it within the range of measurement of the osmometer, if necessary, but the results must be expressed as those of the diluted solution and must NOT be multiplied by a dilution factor to calculate the osmolality of the original solution unless otherwise indicated in the monograph. The molal osmotic coefficient is a function of concentration and changes nonlinearly with dilution.]

Unless otherwise directed in the monograph, use Method 1.

Method 1: Freezing-Point Osmometry — Procedure

Verify that the instrument has been turned on for an appropriate amount of time (NLT 30 min unless the instrument contains an internal sensor) to enable the cooling bath to equilibrate to its normal operating temperature. Verify that the calibration of the instrument brackets the expected osmolality of the test solution. If not, perform a new calibration. At least each day of use, confirm the instrument calibration as described in Procedure for Calibration Check.

Before each measurement, rinse the measurement cell with the solution to be tested, and verify that the thermocouple is clean and dry before immersing it into the test solution.

Method 1: Troubleshooting

Samples may sometimes fail to freeze if the sample viscosity is too high, if the sample is precooled too much or not enough, or if the mixing speed or the amplitude of the instrument is not optimally set. If test solutions occasionally fail to freeze, the osmolality measurements may not be accurate. The settings should be optimized to reliably freeze the test solution, and then the instrument should be recalibrated with the optimized settings prior to re-evaluation.

Insoluble particles, emulsified droplets that are insoluble and entrained air, will not significantly affect the osmolality of the aqueous vehicle; however, these may affect the ability of the sample to freeze. If accurate measurement of the osmolality is affected by the presence of the insoluble phase, the insoluble phase should be removed by centrifugation and the osmolality of the clarified aqueous phase should be evaluated and reported.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

稀釋後不可乘以稀釋因子——這是最重要的操作限制！

原因在於 osmolality coefficient（Φm）是濃度的非線性函數。假設你將 600 mOsmol/kg 的樣品稀釋一半，若 osmolality 也正好是原來的一半（300 mOsmol/kg），這只是巧合，不是規律。實際上：

  • 在低濃度下，電解質解離程度改變
  • 溶質-溶劑相互作用改變
  • Φm 本身也隨濃度改變

因此稀釋後的讀數只代表稀釋後溶液的滲透量，不能外推回原始濃度。

重點提示 Key Notes

Method 1 操作清單（SOP 關鍵步驟）：

  1. 開機 ≥ 30 分鐘（冷卻浴平衡至工作溫度）
  2. 確認校正「夾住」預期滲透量（如預期 290，則需 200 和 400 兩個校正點）
  3. 每日使用前執行校正確認
  4. 每次測量前：測量池用待測液沖洗，確認熱電偶清潔乾燥
  5. 執行測量，讀取數據

沖洗步驟的邏輯：消除前一個樣品的殘留，確保當前樣品的基質一致性。

重點提示 Troubleshooting Guide

凍結失敗的常見原因與對策：

原因對策
樣品黏度過高（如高濃度蛋白）增加 buzz amplitude；考慮蒸氣壓法
預冷不足（過冷不夠深）降低 buzz point 溫度
預冷過度（超過凍結點太多）提高 buzz point 溫度
攪拌速度不當按廠商建議調整 stirring amplitude
不溶性粒子影響凍結離心去除不溶相；報告澄清液相結果

比喻說明 Analogy

「稀釋不可乘以稀釋因子」就像問「一杯濃縮咖啡加水稀釋後，咖啡因含量可以直接乘以2嗎？」——不行，因為稀釋後的分子行為（如咖啡因與水的相互作用、溶液密度）都已改變。滲透量也是如此，它不是一個可以線性外推的理想稀釋量。

Method 2: Vapor Pressure Osmometry 蒸氣壓法程序

原文內容 Original Text

Standard Solutions

The osmolality reference standards in Table 1 are based on freezing-point depression measurements and represent the osmolality of the solution at the freezing point. However, manufacturers of dew point osmometers often typically design their instruments to be calibrated with the same standard solutions used for freezing-point depression instruments.

Unless otherwise directed by the instrument manufacturer, it is permitted to use the same Standard Solutions. When performing an instrument calibration and calibration check with these Standard Solutions, the stated value for the standard solution is considered to be the same as indicated for the freezing-point depression instruments.

Procedure

Ensure that the sample and instrument are located in a laboratory with good temperature control and that the sample is equilibrated to the temperature of the laboratory before performing the measurement. Verify that the instrument has been turned on for NLT 30 min to completely equilibrate to the lab temperature while operating.

Verify that the calibration of the instrument brackets the expected osmolality of the test solution. If not, perform a new calibration such that the lower and upper calibration standards bracket the expected osmolality. At least each day of use, confirm the instrument calibration as described in Procedure for Calibration Check.

If the sample is dispensed by a pipette, use a calibrated pipette to deliver the specified volume of liquid to the sample chamber. Consistent timing during sample loading is important for consistent repeatability. The procedure and timing for sample loading need to be equivalent to those used for the instrument calibration.

Troubleshooting

Volatile ingredients (e.g., ethanol) may evaporate rapidly during the sample loading and measurement resulting in inaccurate and variable results.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

蒸氣壓法用凍結點法的標準液——為何可行？

這是 USP 的實用性妥協。理論上，蒸氣壓法在室溫測量，應使用室溫下驗證過 osmolality 的標準液。但實際上：

  • 廠商已將儀器設計為用凍結點法標準液校正
  • 對大多數水性非揮發性溶液，兩種溫度下的 osmolality 差異在儀器誤差範圍內
  • 實務上統一標準液供應，降低了實驗室管理複雜度

但這個「可行」是有條件的，不適用於含揮發性溶質或溶質解離常數對溫度高度敏感的樣品。

重點提示 Key Notes

Consistent timing 一致性的重要性：蒸氣壓法測量時，樣品在密封腔體中的蒸發量隨時間增加。若校正時加樣後等待 20 秒再讀數，測試樣品也必須等待 20 秒——否則測到的是不同揮發程度下的露點，無法直接比對。這是蒸氣壓法常見但容易忽略的操作一致性要求。

樣品溫度平衡：冷藏保存的樣品必須回溫至室溫後才能測量。未平衡的樣品會讓密封腔體內的溫度分布不均，直接影響露點測量結果。

比喻說明 Analogy

蒸氣壓法的「一致計時」就像咖啡師每次萃取濃縮咖啡都用碼表計時——不是因為機器不夠好，而是因為接觸時間直接影響結果。校正和測試必須用完全相同的步驟和時間，才能確保比較有意義。

Reporting of Results 結果報告

原文內容 Original Text

Units

The units of osmolality are osmole per kilogram of water (Osmol/kg) or milliosmole per kilogram of water (mOsmol/kg). The units of osmolarity are osmole per liter of solution (Osmol/L) or milliosmole per liter of solution (mOsmol/L). It is not necessary to state "of water" or "of solution" when using these units. The use of "Osmol" alone must be avoided because it is not clear whether it refers to the number (osmoles of solute) or the osmolality or the osmolarity.

Measurement Result

If a single measurement result is reported, it should be reported with the precision given by the repeatability of the instrument. Preferably, the mean of three or more measurements on the same solution should be reported with a precision represented by the standard deviation of the averaged measurements. Most osmometers report results to the nearest whole number, therefore, the mean result should also be rounded to the nearest whole number and reported in units of mOsmol/kg.

Conversion to Osmolarity

Freezing-point depression and vapor pressure measurements directly result in an assessment of the osmolality. However, it may be desirable to report the results in units of osmolarity. The measured osmolality ξm can be converted to osmolarity ξc using:

ξc = ξm × (ρ − C)

where ρ is the density of the solution and C is the total solute concentration, both expressed in grams per milliliter.

Example: A 3% (w/v) solution of sodium bicarbonate contains 0.030 g/mL of anhydrous sodium bicarbonate. The density of this solution was found to be 1.0192 g/mL at 20°, and its measured osmolality ξm was 615 mOsmol/kg.

(ρ − C) = (1.0192 − 0.030) = 0.9892 kg water / L solution
Osmolarity = 615 mOsmol/kg × 0.9892 kg/L = 608 mOsmol/L

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

為何報告平均值而非單次值？

滲透量計每次測量有隨機誤差（儀器聲稱 repeatability 通常為 ±1–3 mOsmol/kg）。單次測量結果不確定度大；三次平均可將不確定度縮小約 √3 ≈ 1.73 倍。對於規格窗口窄的產品（如 280–320 mOsmol/kg，窗口只有 40 mOsmol/kg），三次平均是確保報告結果可靠的基本要求。

單位嚴格性："Osmol" 單獨使用時語意不明，必須指定 /kg（osmolality）或 /L（osmolarity）。這在 CMC 文件和標籤上是法規合規的基本要求。

Osmolality → Osmolarity 換算公式

公式: ξc = ξm × (ρ − C)

說明:
  ξm = 測量的 osmolality (mOsmol/kg)
  ρ  = 溶液密度 (g/mL = kg/L)
  C  = 總溶質濃度 (g/mL)
  (ρ−C) = 每升溶液中水的質量 (kg water/L solution)

範例計算 — 3% NaHCO₃ 溶液:
  ξm  = 615 mOsmol/kg
  ρ   = 1.0192 g/mL
  C   = 0.030 g/mL
  (ρ−C) = 1.0192 − 0.030 = 0.9892 kg/L
  ξc  = 615 × 0.9892 = 608 mOsmol/L

結論: osmolarity 略低於 osmolality
（因為含溶質的溶液，1 L 溶液中的水略少於 1 kg）

補充公式（另一種等效表達）:
  ξc = 1000 × ξm / (1000/ρ + Σ wi × νi)
  其中 νi = 溶質i的偏比容 (mL/g)
  鹽類 νi ≈ 0.1 mL/g; 胺基酸 νi ≈ 0.6–0.9 mL/g

重點提示 Key Notes

Osmolarity 永遠略低於 osmolality（對水性溶液而言）：因為含溶質的溶液，1 公升溶液中的水少於 1 公斤（被溶質佔去了一部分體積）。差距通常只有 0.5–3%，對於低滲透量溶液影響不大，但對高滲透量（>1000 mOsmol/kg）的溶液差距較明顯。

換算需要密度測量：必須實際測量溶液密度，不能使用純水密度代替。注射劑中的輔料（甘露醇、氯化鈉等）會顯著改變溶液密度。

Tonicity 張力

原文內容 Original Text

Tonicity is a medical term that relates to the osmotic pressure difference between the internal and external sides of a cell membrane. In many cases tonicity and osmolality are not the same because some solutes are able to rapidly pass through the cell membrane.

Red blood cells (erythrocytes) are typically considered the standard for defining what is considered an isotonic solution:

  • Hypotonic: red blood cells swell and become turgid or burst
  • Hypertonic: red blood cells shrink and desiccate
  • Isotonic: no change in the size of the red blood cells, cell wall remains flaccid

Measurements of osmolality or osmolarity alone are not sufficient to characterize the tonicity of a liquid. It is preferable to use the terms hypoosmotic, hyperosmotic, and isoosmotic when discussing the relative osmolality of solutions.

The standard for an isoosmotic solution is 0.9% sodium chloride (NaCl) solution, which has an osmolality of 285 mOsmol/kg and an osmolarity of 284 mOsmol/L.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Tonicity vs. Osmolality — 為何不同？

關鍵在於溶質是否能穿越細胞膜：

  • 不能穿越（如 NaCl、甘露醇）：完全貢獻 tonicity → osmolality ≈ tonicity effect
  • 能快速穿越（如尿素、乙醇、乙二醇）：不影響 tonicity → 高滲透量但不導致細胞脫水

例：一個含高濃度尿素的溶液，osmolality 可能是 400 mOsmol/kg（高滲），但對紅血球而言是「等張」的，因為尿素可自由穿越細胞膜，不會造成滲透壓差。

術語對照：

  • Hypoosmotic（低滲透量）≈ 對NaCl等有效溶質而言的 hypotonic
  • Hyperosmotic（高滲透量）≈ 對NaCl等有效溶質而言的 hypertonic
  • Isoosmotic（等滲透量）= osmolality 接近 285–310 mOsmol/kg

比喻說明 Analogy

把細胞膜想像成一扇門：有些溶質是「有鑰匙的」（能自由進出，如尿素），有些是「沒有鑰匙的」（被擋在門外，如 NaCl）。只有被擋在門外的溶質才真正產生滲透壓差（tonicity 效果）。測量 osmolality 是量「門外所有人」的總數，而 tonicity 只量「沒有鑰匙、真的被擋住」的那部分人。所以 osmolality 是 tonicity 的上限，實際 tonicity 通常更低。

重點提示 Key Notes

臨床重要性：注射劑標籤上標示的「等張」（isotonic）是基於 tonicity（對細胞膜有效溶質），而非單純 osmolality。將「等張」等同於 285 mOsmol/kg 是常見的簡化，在大多數情況下成立，但含高濃度通透性溶質（如甘油、尿素）的製劑需要特別說明。

0.9% NaCl 的標準地位：285 mOsmol/kg（osmolality）/ 284 mOsmol/L（osmolarity）是業界公認的等滲參考點。這也是為何注射劑通常以此為設計目標。

[Note: The following content was part of the "OSMOLARITY" section (now deleted in the current USP 〈785〉 version). It is included here for educational context and historical reference, as it illustrates the important distinction between theoretical osmolarity calculation and experimentally determined osmolality.]

Osmolarity of a solution is a theoretical quantity expressed in osmoles/L (Osmol/L) and is widely used in clinical practice because it expresses osmoles as a function of volume. Osmolarity cannot be measured but is calculated theoretically from the experimentally measured value of osmolality.

Sometimes, osmolarity (ξc) is calculated theoretically from the molar concentrations:

ξc = Σ νi × ci
where νi = number of particles formed by dissociation of one molecule of the ith solute
ci = molar concentration of the ith solute

Example: Osmolarity of a solution prepared by dissolving 1 g of vancomycin in 100 mL of 0.9% NaCl:

[3 × 10 g/L / 1449.25 (MW vancomycin)] + [2 × 9 g/L / 58.44 (MW NaCl)] × 1000
= 329 mOsmol/L (calculated)

However, the solution was found to be hypoosmotic with an experimentally determined osmolality of 255 mOsmol/kg.

This example illustrates that osmolarity values calculated theoretically should be interpreted cautiously and may not represent the osmotic properties of infusion solutions. The discrepancy is due to interactions between solute molecules or between solute and solvent molecules, related to the molal osmotic coefficient (Φm).

SLS Example Calculation (from deleted section):

An aqueous sodium lauryl sulfate (SLS) solution at 30% (w/v), specific gravity 1.08, containing 0.5% sodium sulfate:

  • SLS (MW 288.37, ν=2): 300 g/L × 2 mol/mol ÷ 288.37 = 2080.6 mOsmol/L
  • Na₂SO₄ (MW 142.04, ν=3): 5 g/L × 3 mol/mol ÷ 142.04 = 105.6 mOsmol/L
  • Total osmolarity = 2186.2 mOsmol/L

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

「計算的 osmolarity」永遠只是估算！

Vancomycin 案例是教科書級的示範：計算值（329 mOsmol/L）比實測值（255 mOsmol/kg）高出 29%。原因包括：

  • Vancomycin 是大分子糖肽，在水中有強的自締合（self-association）傾向，有效溶質粒子數遠少於理論值
  • 計算用 ν = 3（一個分子解離成 3 個粒子），但實際上不完全解離
  • Φm << 1，大幅降低了有效滲透壓

這就是為何 USP 〈785〉 強調：「osmolarity values calculated theoretically should be interpreted cautiously」，以及為何現行版本的章節架構把「測量 osmolality」擺在核心位置。

Theoretical Osmolarity Calculation

理論計算公式（適用於理想溶液）:
  ξc,theoretical = Σ (νi × ci)
  νi = 解離係數（離子數）
  ci = 各溶質莫耳濃度

Vancomycin 案例:
  Vanco 10 g/L ÷ 1449.25 g/mol × 3 = 20.7 mOsmol/L
  NaCl  9 g/L ÷ 58.44 g/mol × 2 = 308.0 mOsmol/L
  計算總計 = 329 mOsmol/L
  實測滲透量 = 255 mOsmol/kg
  差異 = -23%（計算值高估了 osmolality）

結論: 含大分子或有自締合的溶質時，
      計算值誤差可達 20–50%，必須實驗測定！

重點提示 Key Notes

現行 USP 〈785〉 版本（2020年以來）已刪除舊版 "OSMOLARITY" 和 "MEASUREMENT OF OSMOLALITY" 獨立章節，將所有內容整合重組。這個版本更新反映了：

  • 理論計算 osmolarity 不可靠，不應作為製藥測試的替代方案
  • 所有 osmolality/osmolarity 結果應以實驗測定為基礎
  • 換算公式 ξc = ξm × (ρ−C) 才是正確的 osmolarity 計算方式

比喻說明 Analogy

理論計算 osmolarity 就像只看食譜上的材料清單估算一道菜的鹹度——理論上你知道放了多少鹽，但鹽分在烹飪過程中與其他食材的交互作用是無法預測的。實際嘗味（實驗測定）才是唯一可靠的方式。Vancomycin 案例就是一個「理論上很鹹，實際上不鹹」的典型例子。

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心參考文獻解析

References 8 & 9（Scatchard 1933, Gibbard 1974）：這是 USP Table 1 NaCl 校正液配方的實驗數據基礎。100年前（Scatchard）和50年前（Gibbard）的精確凍結點下降測量數據，至今仍是 USP 的計算依據——足見高品質基礎科學數據的持久價值。

Reference 1（Pitzer 1984）：Pitzer–Debye–Hückel 方程的原始來源，支撐了 NaCl 校正液的理論計算框架（β₀, β₁, C⁰ 參數的實驗擬合）。

Reference 10（NIST traceable solutions）：NIST（美國國家標準與技術研究院）可追溯性是 GMP 環境中校正液的最高品質標準，確保量測的國際可比性。

重點提示 Key Notes

NIST 可追溯性對製藥 GMP 環境至關重要。使用市售校正安瓿時，其憑證必須聲明可追溯至 NIST（或等效國際標準機構）。這是 FDA 483 和 Warning Letter 中常見的稽查要點——若無法提供可追溯性文件，整個滲透量測量系統的可信度都受到質疑。

Advanced Instruments（USP 提及的供應商）是製藥業最廣泛使用的滲透量計品牌之一，其校正安瓿是業界標準品。但 USP 也明確指出「其他符合 NIST 可追溯性的供應商亦可使用」——給了廠家靈活性。

全章最終重點回顧 Chapter Summary

  • 校正必須「夾住」（bracket）預期測量範圍，每日使用前確認；確認失敗需重新校正後再次確認，並評估期間所有已測樣品
  • 固體注射劑按標籤指示重配；樣品稀釋後結果只代表稀釋液，不可外推至原始濃度（Φm 非線性）
  • 高黏度樣品可能需要優化 buzz 參數；不溶性相干擾凍結時應離心後測澄清液相
  • Osmolarity 換算必須基於測量的 osmolality，搭配溶液密度和溶質濃度的實測值（公式：ξc = ξm × (ρ−C)）
  • Tonicity ≠ Osmolality——能穿越細胞膜的溶質（尿素、乙醇等）不貢獻 tonicity；0.9% NaCl（285 mOsmol/kg）是等滲參考標準
  • 理論計算 osmolarity 誤差可達 20–50%（Vancomycin 案例）；實驗測定永遠是製藥環境的最終依據
