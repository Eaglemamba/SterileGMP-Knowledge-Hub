Introduction 簡介

原文內容 Original Text

Osmotic pressure plays a critical role in all biological processes that involve diffusion of solutes or transfer of fluids through membranes.

Osmosis occurs when the osmotic pressure drives the solvent, but not solute, to cross a semipermeable membrane from regions of lower to higher solute concentrations until equilibrium is reached. Osmotic pressure is a colligative property that depends on the number, but not the identity, of moles of solutes present in solution. Quantitative measurement of the number of moles of solutes in solution is necessary for predicting and controlling the potential damage to biological tissues due to fluid transfer across biological tissue membranes.

The terms osmolality, osmolarity, and tonicity are often used to describe the number of solutes in the solution, however, it is incorrect to use these terms interchangeably. The measurement of the number of solutes in the solution, using colligative properties, allows the direct determination of the osmolality of the solution with units of moles of solutes per kilogram of solvent. This result can be converted into a value for osmolarity with units of moles of solutes per liter of solvent, typically water. Tonicity is a medical term that relates to the osmotic pressure difference between the internal and external sides of a cell membrane.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Osmolality (滲透量)：直接測量值，單位為 mOsmol/kg（溶質莫耳數 ÷ 溶劑公斤數）。這是實驗室實際量到的數字。

Osmolarity (滲透濃度)：計算值，單位為 mOsmol/L（溶質莫耳數 ÷ 溶液公升數）。臨床常用，但必須由 osmolality 換算而來。

Tonicity (張力)：醫學術語，描述溶液對細胞膜造成的淨滲透壓差。並非所有 osmolality 貢獻都能影響 tonicity（例如尿素可自由穿越細胞膜，對 tonicity 無貢獻）。

比喻說明 Analogy

把細胞膜想像成一扇只讓水分子通過、但擋住鹽分子的半透膜。當細胞外液太鹹（高滲），水會從細胞內流出，細胞就像葡萄乾一樣萎縮；若外液太淡（低滲），水則湧入細胞直到細胞脹破。這就是為何靜脈注射劑的滲透量必須嚴格管控。

重點提示 Key Notes

三個術語「不可互換使用」是 USP 的明確警示。在 CMC 文件、標籤及檢驗報告中，必須清楚標明使用的是 osmolality（測量值）還是 osmolarity（計算值）。混用術語可能導致臨床劑量計算錯誤。

Theoretical Background 理論背景

原文內容 Original Text

The osmotic pressure of a solution depends on the number of solutes in a solution, and is therefore referred to as a colligative property. For an ideal solution with water as the solvent, the osmotic pressure, Π, is a function of the water activity, aw:

Π = −(RT / Vm) · ln aw, where aw = γw · xw

where Vm is the molar volume of water at temperature T, Vm = 18.018 cm³ mol⁻¹ at 273.15 K; R is the gas constant, R = 8.3144598 J mol⁻¹ K⁻¹; T is the temperature in Kelvin; γw is the activity coefficient of water; and xw is the mole fraction of water, xw = (1 − xs), where xs is the mole fraction of solute.

At low solute concentrations, the equation can be simplified to the van't Hoff equation for osmotic pressure:

Π = (RT·MW / Vm) · ξm,ideal

where ξm,ideal is the osmolality of the solution in moles per kilogram of water; ν is the stoichiometric coefficient for the solute; and m is the molal concentration of the solute in moles of solute per kilogram of water.

For an ideal, non-dissociating solute, ν = 1 and the osmolality, ξ, is equal to the molal concentration of solute. For a dissociating solute, ν is equal to the stoichiometric number of moles into which one mole of solute will dissociate.

For non-ideal solutes, an osmolality coefficient, Φm, is introduced to correct for the difference between predicted ideal behavior and observed real behavior:

ξm,real = ν · m · Φm

When multiple solutes are present, the osmolality may be approximated by summing the contributions from all individual solutes:

ξm,real = Σi νi · mi · Φm,i

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Water activity (水活度) aw：描述溶液中水分子「有效可用程度」的無量綱值。純水的 aw = 1；加入溶質後 aw 下降，導致水從高 aw 端流向低 aw 端（即從低溶質端流向高溶質端）。

Osmolality coefficient Φm（滲透量係數）：真實溶液的修正因子。理想溶液 Φm = 1；實際溶液（尤其是電解質）Φm ≠ 1，需要實驗測定。NaCl 在生理濃度下 Φm ≈ 0.93。

公式與計算 Key Formulas

Van't Hoff (ideal dilute):
  Π = CRT  (C = molar concentration)

Osmolality (ideal):
  ξ_ideal = ν × m

Osmolality (real):
  ξ_real = ν × m × Φm

Multi-solute mixture:
  ξ_total = Σ (νi × mi × Φm,i)

Example — 0.9% NaCl:
  NaCl → Na⁺ + Cl⁻  (ν = 2)
  m ≈ 0.154 mol/kg
  Φm ≈ 0.926
  ξ ≈ 2 × 0.154 × 0.926 ≈ 285 mOsmol/kg

比喻說明 Analogy

把 ν（解離係數）想像成一個家庭分家後的成員數。NaCl 這個「家庭」分成 Na⁺ 和 Cl⁻ 兩個成員，因此它對滲透量的貢獻是同樣濃度不解離溶質（如葡萄糖）的兩倍。這就解釋了為何 0.9% NaCl 的滲透量比同重量的葡萄糖溶液高得多。

重點提示 Key Notes

多溶質疊加法（additive approach）假設每種溶質的 Φm 在混合物中與單獨存在時相同。這在低濃度下是合理近似，但在高濃度或有溶質-溶質相互作用時可能產生誤差。USP 明確提示此為簡化方法。

Freezing-Point Depression & Vapor Pressure Methods 凍結點下降與蒸氣壓法

原文內容 Original Text

In addition to osmotic pressure, other colligative properties can be used to measure the osmolality of solutions including dew-point depression, boiling-point elevation, and freezing-point depression. The most commonly used method for analysis of the osmolality of solutions is freezing-point depression.

The freezing-point depression, ΔTf, of an aqueous solution relative to pure water is proportional to the change in the water activity, aw:

ΔTf = (RT²f / ΔHfus) · Δln aw

At low solute concentrations, this can be simplified to express freezing-point depression in terms of osmolality as:

ΔTf = Kf · ξm

where Kf = RT²f · MW / ΔHfus. For water, the value of ΔHfus at 273.15 K is 6006.78 J mol⁻¹, which gives a value for Kf of 1.86056 K kg mol⁻¹.

Vapor pressure osmometers measure the relative humidity of the air above the sample that is in equilibrium with the sample. The dew point is the temperature below which the water vapor in the air is above saturation and condensation occurs. The change in the dew point of the air, ΔTD, is given by the Clausius–Clapeyron equation:

ΔTD = KD · ξm

where KD = MWRT²D / ΔHvap. At 25°C (298.15 K), ΔHvap = 43.990 kJ mol⁻¹, giving KD = 0.3027 K kg mol⁻¹. At the boiling point (373.15 K), KD = 0.5130 K kg mol⁻¹, equivalent to the ebullioscopic constant, Kb, for water.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Freezing-point depression (凍結點下降)：溶液凍結點低於純水（0°C），下降幅度正比於滲透量。是最常用的 osmolality 測量原理。每 1 mOsmol/kg ≈ 1.86 × 10⁻³ °C 的凍結點下降。

Vapor pressure osmometry（蒸氣壓滲透量測定）：利用露點 (dew point) 下降原理，在室溫下測量。測量值代表環境溫度下的 osmolality，與凍結點法（在凍結點測量）結果可能不同——因為某些溶質的解離程度隨溫度改變。

公式速查 Formula Reference

凍結點下降法 (Freezing-Point):
  ΔTf = Kf × ξm
  Kf (water) = 1.86056 K·kg/mol
  例：290 mOsmol/kg → ΔTf = 1.86056 × 0.290
                            = 0.5396°C 下降

蒸氣壓法 (Vapor Pressure, at 25°C):
  ΔTD = KD × ξm
  KD (25°C) = 0.3027 K·kg/mol

兩法靈敏度比:
  Kf / KD = 1.86 / 0.30 ≈ 6.2×
  凍結點法靈敏度約為蒸氣壓法的6倍

比喻說明 Analogy

想像你在山頂（低氣壓）煮水，水的沸點比海平面低。依數性質的道理是一樣的——溶液中加入溶質，就像改變了水分子的「逃逸意願」：沸點升高（難以逃逸成蒸氣）、凍結點下降（難以結成冰晶）。這些微小的溫度變化就是滲透量計的量測原理。

重點提示 Key Notes

兩種方法對相同樣品可能給出不同結果。這不是錯誤，而是物理現象：凍結點法在 0°C 測量；蒸氣壓法在室溫（~25°C）測量。含揮發性成分（如乙醇）的樣品，蒸氣壓法會產生誤差，因為揮發性溶質會在測量過程中蒸發。此情況必須改用凍結點法。

實務應用 Practical Application

在 CDMO 製劑開發時，若產品含乙醇（如某些注射用溶媒）或其他揮發性輔料，必須選用凍結點滲透量計。如果誤用蒸氣壓法，乙醇在量測過程中揮發，會低估實際滲透量，導致產品被誤判為等滲而進入臨床。

Calibration Standards — NaCl Solutions 氯化鈉校正標準液

原文內容 Original Text

To calibrate instruments for measurements of water activity and osmolality, it is necessary to have well-characterized solutions that are easy to prepare. The nonlinear relationship between the osmolality coefficient, Φm, and the molality of the solute has been studied for many solutions of single salts and is most often represented by the Pitzer–Debye–Hückel equation as a virial expansion in terms of molality:

Φm − 1 = fΦ + BΦm + CΦm²

where fΦ = −AΦ·(√I / (1 + b√I)), BΦ = β0 + β1·e−α√I, I = ½Σ mizi², and AΦ is the Debye–Hückel slope; b = 1.2 (empirical ion size parameter); α = 2.0 (empirical ionic strength dependence parameter).

For a simple 1:1 electrolyte like sodium chloride (NaCl), the ionic strength I equals the molality. For NaCl at 273.15 K and standard pressure, AΦ = 0.37642. With best-fit parameters β0 = 0.040933, β1 = 0.26955, CΦ = 0.004506, the resulting recipes for Standard Solutions are listed in Table 1, spanning the experimental range from 2–7300 mOsmol/kg.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Pitzer–Debye–Hückel 方程：這是電解質溶液非理想行為的最佳半理論模型，結合了長程靜電作用（Debye–Hückel 項）與短程離子-離子相互作用（virial 展開項）。對於滲透量校正液的精確配製至關重要。

為什麼用 NaCl？NaCl 是生物體液的主要電解質、溶解度高、純度可精確控制、與人體滲透量範圍完美匹配（生理值約 285–310 mOsmol/kg），且其 Φm-molality 關係已有大量實驗數據。

比喻說明 Analogy

校正用 NaCl 溶液就像音叉之於調音師——它的滲透量已由第一原理推算並經實驗驗證，可信度高。USP Table 1 所列的配方精度（±1 mOsmol/kg）遠高於市售校正液（±2 mOsmol/kg 或 ±0.5%），自配時必須嚴格遵守秤量精度要求。

重點提示 Key Notes

  • NaCl 必須預先在 110°C 乾燥 2 小時，去除吸附水分
  • 稱量精度：NaCl ±0.03 g；水 ±0.2 g——這不是建議，是保證 ±1 mOsmol/kg 精度的必要條件
  • 儲存在玻璃容器（非透氣容器），防止水分蒸發改變濃度
  • 非無菌非防腐劑溶液，每月至少新鮮配製一次
  • 市售安瓿裝校正液：一次性使用，開封後丟棄

本節重點回顧 Section Summary

  • 滲透壓是依數性質，由溶質粒子的數量決定，不取決於種類——這是 osmolality 測量的根本原理
  • Osmolality（mOsmol/kg）是測量值；osmolarity（mOsmol/L）是計算值；tonicity 是醫學術語，三者不可混用
  • 凍結點下降法（Kf = 1.86 K·kg/mol）是最靈敏、最常用的測量方法；蒸氣壓法在室溫運作，但不適用於含揮發性溶質的樣品
  • 真實溶液需引入 osmolality coefficient（Φm）修正；NaCl 的 Φm 由 Pitzer–Debye–Hückel 方程精確推算，支撐 USP Table 1 校正液配方
  • 校正標準液的配製精度（秤量、乾燥、儲存）直接決定滲透量測量的可追溯性，是品質體系的關鍵控制點
