Introduction 簡介

原文內容 Original Text

Osmotic pressure plays a critical role in all biological processes that involve diffusion of solutes or transfer of fluids through membranes.

Osmosis occurs when the osmotic pressure drives the solvent, but not solute, to cross a semipermeable membrane from regions of lower to higher solute concentrations until equilibrium is reached. Osmotic pressure is a colligative property that depends on the number, but not the identity, of moles of solutes present in solution. Quantitative measurement of the number of moles of solutes in solution is necessary for predicting and controlling the potential damage to biological tissues due to fluid transfer across biological tissue membranes.

The terms osmolality, osmolarity, and tonicity are often used to describe the number of solutes in the solution, however, it is incorrect to use these terms interchangeably. The measurement of the number of solutes in the solution, using colligative properties, allows the direct determination of the osmolality of the solution with units of moles of solutes per kilogram of solvent. This result can be converted into a value for osmolarity with units of moles of solutes per liter of solvent, typically water. Tonicity is a medical term that relates to the osmotic pressure difference between the internal and external sides of a cell membrane.

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Osmolality (滲透量)：直接測量值，單位為 mOsmol/kg（溶質莫耳數 ÷ 溶劑公斤數）。這是實驗室實際量到的數字。

Osmolarity (滲透濃度)：計算值，單位為 mOsmol/L（溶質莫耳數 ÷ 溶液公升數）。臨床常用，但必須由 osmolality 換算而來。

Tonicity (張力)：醫學術語，描述溶液對細胞膜造成的淨滲透壓差。並非所有 osmolality 貢獻都能影響 tonicity（例如尿素可自由穿越細胞膜，對 tonicity 無貢獻）。

比喻說明 Analogy

把細胞膜想像成一扇只讓水分子通過、但擋住鹽分子的半透膜。當細胞外液太鹹（高滲），水會從細胞內流出，細胞就像葡萄乾一樣萎縮；若外液太淡（低滲），水則湧入細胞直到細胞脹破。這就是為何靜脈注射劑的滲透量必須嚴格管控。

重點提示 Key Notes

三個術語「不可互換使用」是 USP 的明確警示。在 CMC 文件、標籤及檢驗報告中，必須清楚標明使用的是 osmolality（測量值）還是 osmolarity（計算值）。混用術語可能導致臨床劑量計算錯誤。

Theoretical Background 理論背景

原文內容 Original Text

The osmotic pressure of a solution depends on the number of solutes in a solution, and is therefore referred to as a colligative property. For an ideal solution with water as the solvent, the osmotic pressure, Π, is a function of the water activity, aw:

Π = −(RT / Vm) · ln aw, where aw = γw · xw

where Vm is the molar volume of water at temperature T, Vm = 18.018 cm³ mol⁻¹ at 273.15 K; R is the gas constant, R = 8.3144598 J mol⁻¹ K⁻¹; T is the temperature in Kelvin; γw is the activity coefficient of water; and xw is the mole fraction of water, xw = (1 − xs), where xs is the mole fraction of solute.

At low solute concentrations, the equation can be simplified to the van't Hoff equation for osmotic pressure:

Π = (RT·MW / Vm) · ξm,ideal

where ξm,ideal is the osmolality of the solution in moles per kilogram of water; ν is the stoichiometric coefficient for the solute; and m is the molal concentration of the solute in moles of solute per kilogram of water.

For an ideal, non-dissociating solute, ν = 1 and the osmolality, ξ, is equal to the molal concentration of solute. For a dissociating solute, ν is equal to the stoichiometric number of moles into which one mole of solute will dissociate.

For non-ideal solutes, an osmolality coefficient, Φm, is introduced to correct for the difference between predicted ideal behavior and observed real behavior:

ξm,real = ν · m · Φm

When multiple solutes are present, the osmolality may be approximated by summing the contributions from all individual solutes:

ξm,real = Σi νi · mi · Φm,i

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Water activity (水活度) aw：描述溶液中水分子「有效可用程度」的無量綱值。純水的 aw = 1；加入溶質後 aw 下降，導致水從高 aw 端流向低 aw 端（即從低溶質端流向高溶質端）。

Osmolality coefficient Φm（滲透量係數）：真實溶液的修正因子。理想溶液 Φm = 1；實際溶液（尤其是電解質）Φm ≠ 1，需要實驗測定。NaCl 在生理濃度下 Φm ≈ 0.93。

公式與計算 Key Formulas

Van't Hoff (ideal dilute):
  Π = CRT  (C = molar concentration)

Osmolality (ideal):
  ξ_ideal = ν × m

Osmolality (real):
  ξ_real = ν × m × Φm

Multi-solute mixture:
  ξ_total = Σ (νi × mi × Φm,i)

Example — 0.9% NaCl:
  NaCl → Na⁺ + Cl⁻  (ν = 2)
  m ≈ 0.154 mol/kg
  Φm ≈ 0.926
  ξ ≈ 2 × 0.154 × 0.926 ≈ 285 mOsmol/kg

比喻說明 Analogy

把 ν（解離係數）想像成一個家庭分家後的成員數。NaCl 這個「家庭」分成 Na⁺ 和 Cl⁻ 兩個成員，因此它對滲透量的貢獻是同樣濃度不解離溶質（如葡萄糖）的兩倍。這就解釋了為何 0.9% NaCl 的滲透量比同重量的葡萄糖溶液高得多。

重點提示 Key Notes

多溶質疊加法（additive approach）假設每種溶質的 Φm 在混合物中與單獨存在時相同。這在低濃度下是合理近似，但在高濃度或有溶質-溶質相互作用時可能產生誤差。USP 明確提示此為簡化方法。

Freezing-Point Depression & Vapor Pressure Methods 凍結點下降與蒸氣壓法

原文內容 Original Text

In addition to osmotic pressure, other colligative properties can be used to measure the osmolality of solutions including dew-point depression, boiling-point elevation, and freezing-point depression. The most commonly used method for analysis of the osmolality of solutions is freezing-point depression.

The freezing-point depression, ΔTf, of an aqueous solution relative to pure water is proportional to the change in the water activity, aw:

ΔTf = (RT²f / ΔHfus) · Δln aw

At low solute concentrations, this can be simplified to express freezing-point depression in terms of osmolality as:

ΔTf = Kf · ξm

where Kf = RT²f · MW / ΔHfus. For water, the value of ΔHfus at 273.15 K is 6006.78 J mol⁻¹, which gives a value for Kf of 1.86056 K kg mol⁻¹.

Vapor pressure osmometers measure the relative humidity of the air above the sample that is in equilibrium with the sample. The dew point is the temperature below which the water vapor in the air is above saturation and condensation occurs. The change in the dew point of the air, ΔTD, is given by the Clausius–Clapeyron equation:

ΔTD = KD · ξm

where KD = MWRT²D / ΔHvap. At 25°C (298.15 K), ΔHvap = 43.990 kJ mol⁻¹, giving KD = 0.3027 K kg mol⁻¹. At the boiling point (373.15 K), KD = 0.5130 K kg mol⁻¹, equivalent to the ebullioscopic constant, Kb, for water.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Freezing-point depression (凍結點下降)：溶液凍結點低於純水（0°C），下降幅度正比於滲透量。是最常用的 osmolality 測量原理。每 1 mOsmol/kg ≈ 1.86 × 10⁻³ °C 的凍結點下降。

Vapor pressure osmometry（蒸氣壓滲透量測定）：利用露點 (dew point) 下降原理，在室溫下測量。測量值代表環境溫度下的 osmolality，與凍結點法（在凍結點測量）結果可能不同——因為某些溶質的解離程度隨溫度改變。

公式速查 Formula Reference

凍結點下降法 (Freezing-Point):
  ΔTf = Kf × ξm
  Kf (water) = 1.86056 K·kg/mol
  例：290 mOsmol/kg → ΔTf = 1.86056 × 0.290
                            = 0.5396°C 下降

蒸氣壓法 (Vapor Pressure, at 25°C):
  ΔTD = KD × ξm
  KD (25°C) = 0.3027 K·kg/mol

兩法靈敏度比:
  Kf / KD = 1.86 / 0.30 ≈ 6.2×
  凍結點法靈敏度約為蒸氣壓法的6倍

比喻說明 Analogy

想像你在山頂（低氣壓）煮水，水的沸點比海平面低。依數性質的道理是一樣的——溶液中加入溶質，就像改變了水分子的「逃逸意願」：沸點升高（難以逃逸成蒸氣）、凍結點下降（難以結成冰晶）。這些微小的溫度變化就是滲透量計的量測原理。

重點提示 Key Notes

兩種方法對相同樣品可能給出不同結果。這不是錯誤，而是物理現象：凍結點法在 0°C 測量；蒸氣壓法在室溫（~25°C）測量。含揮發性成分（如乙醇）的樣品，蒸氣壓法會產生誤差，因為揮發性溶質會在測量過程中蒸發。此情況必須改用凍結點法。

實務應用 Practical Application

在 CDMO 製劑開發時，若產品含乙醇（如某些注射用溶媒）或其他揮發性輔料，必須選用凍結點滲透量計。如果誤用蒸氣壓法，乙醇在量測過程中揮發，會低估實際滲透量，導致產品被誤判為等滲而進入臨床。

Calibration Standards — NaCl Solutions 氯化鈉校正標準液

原文內容 Original Text

To calibrate instruments for measurements of water activity and osmolality, it is necessary to have well-characterized solutions that are easy to prepare. The nonlinear relationship between the osmolality coefficient, Φm, and the molality of the solute has been studied for many solutions of single salts and is most often represented by the Pitzer–Debye–Hückel equation as a virial expansion in terms of molality:

Φm − 1 = fΦ + BΦm + CΦm²

where fΦ = −AΦ·(√I / (1 + b√I)), BΦ = β0 + β1·e−α√I, I = ½Σ mizi², and AΦ is the Debye–Hückel slope; b = 1.2 (empirical ion size parameter); α = 2.0 (empirical ionic strength dependence parameter).

For a simple 1:1 electrolyte like sodium chloride (NaCl), the ionic strength I equals the molality. For NaCl at 273.15 K and standard pressure, AΦ = 0.37642. 核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Pitzer–Debye–Hückel 方程：這是電解質溶液非理想行為的最佳半理論模型，結合了長程靜電作用（Debye–Hückel 項）與短程離子-離子相互作用（virial 展開項）。對於滲透量校正液的精確配製至關重要。

為什麼用 NaCl？NaCl 是生物體液的主要電解質、溶解度高、純度可精確控制、與人體滲透量範圍完美匹配（生理值約 285–310 mOsmol/kg），且其 Φm-molality 關係已有大量實驗數據。

Types of Osmolality Instruments — Overview 儀器類型概述

原文內容 Original Text

The osmolality of a solution is commonly determined by measurement of the freezing-point depression or dew-point depression. A freezing-point depression instrument measures the osmolality of a solution at the freezing-point of the solution, whereas a dew-point depression instrument measures the osmolality of a solution at ambient temperatures.

Significant changes in the solubility, solvation, and ionization of solutes may occur as temperature changes, resulting in a temperature dependence of activity and osmotic coefficients. These factors may lead to significant differences in the osmolality results obtained by different types of osmometers on the same test solutions.

Volatile solutes will evaporate during a measurement with vapor pressure osmometers and result in erroneous values. Other techniques need to be used when measuring osmolality in solutions containing volatile solutes.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

兩種測量時機的本質差異：

  • 凍結點法：在樣品的凍結點（約 -0.5°C 附近）測量 → 代表凍結點溫度下的 osmolality
  • 蒸氣壓法（露點法）：在室溫（約 25°C）測量 → 代表室溫下的 osmolality

對含溫度敏感解離常數的溶質（如緩衝液、弱電解質），兩法結果可能有顯著差異，這不是誤差，是物理現象。

重點提示 Key Notes

含揮發性成分的樣品（乙醇、乙腈、某些香精）禁止使用蒸氣壓法。揮發性溶質在密封腔體中快速蒸發，改變了氣相組成，導致露點讀數偏高（低估 osmolality）。這是 CDMO 製劑開發的常見誤操作陷阱。

比喻說明 Analogy

同一個人在冰天雪地（凍結點法）與夏天（蒸氣壓法）的生理狀態是不同的。人體的代謝率、血液黏度、電解質解離程度都隨溫度改變。滲透量計也一樣——「在什麼溫度測」決定「量到什麼」。所以比較兩台不同原理的滲透量計時，必須理解它們在本質上測的是不同條件下的滲透量。

Freezing-Point Depression Osmometer 凍結點下降滲透量計

原文內容 Original Text

A freezing-point depression osmometer typically consists of the following: a means of cooling the container used for the measurement, a thermocouple for measuring the temperature of the sample, and a means of initiating crystallization. The liquid sample (generally about 0.02−2 mL) is typically placed into a cooling bath or a cooling pit while being mixed to facilitate uniform cooling.

In small volume freezing-point osmometers (with sample sizes smaller than 100 µL), mixing the liquid is not required for uniform cooling. The sample is supercooled to below the expected freezing point and then a short "buzz" (a brief pulse or shock to initiate crystallization of the water) is applied to the sample.

As the sample freezes, the temperature of the thermocouple rises asymptotically toward the freezing point until the sample is completely frozen and the temperature stabilizes. The freezing-point depression is the difference between the freezing point of pure water and the freezing point of the sample.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

測量流程三階段：

  1. 冷卻（Supercooling）：樣品被冷卻至預期凍結點以下 1–3°C，使其進入過冷狀態（液態卻低於凍結點）
  2. 觸發結晶（Buzz）：施加短暫震動或電脈衝，觸發水分子開始結晶
  3. 相變平衡（Plateau）：結晶放出的潛熱使溫度回升，直到達到真實凍結點（溫度-時間曲線的平坦段）→ 讀取此溫度即為凍結點

比喻說明 Analogy

想像一瓶超市賣的純淨水，冰箱冷藏到 -2°C 卻不結冰（過冷現象）。當你輕輕搖晃它，瞬間結成冰——這就是 "buzz" 的作用。結冰過程中釋放的「凝固潛熱」讓溫度回升到真正的凍結點，儀器就是在這個平台期讀取溫度。溶液比純水多了溶質，凍結點更低，偏移量就是 osmolality 的訊號。

重點提示 Key Notes

樣品量範圍：標準型 0.02–2 mL；微量型 <100 µL（無需攪拌）。微量型特別適合珍貴候選藥物的早期開發階段，當樣品量極為有限時。

溫度-時間曲線判讀：凍結點是曲線上升段的漸近線（asymptote），不是尖峰值。若曲線沒有明確平台，表示樣品未完全凍結，讀數不可靠。

實務應用 Practical Application

在 CDMO 的 QC 實驗室中，凍結點滲透量計是注射劑批次放行的標配儀器。對於高黏度樣品（如生物製劑配方含高濃度蛋白質或 PEG），樣品可能難以凍結，需要調整 buzz 參數或攪拌速度。此類 troubleshooting 必須在 OQ 階段提前驗證，並記入標準操作程序。

  2. 為什麼微量型凍結點滲透量計（<100 µL）不需要攪拌？

Vapor Pressure Osmometer 蒸氣壓滲透量計

原文內容 Original Text

Vapor pressure osmometers use hygrometric (dew-point temperature depression) measurements for determining water activity. They require a smaller volume of specimen (generally about 5–20 µL). The hygrometric technique is based on measuring the temperature of a wet thermocouple junction in a sealed chamber containing the sample.

A current is applied to cool the thermocouple, by means of the Peltier effect, below the dew-point temperature, resulting in condensation of water from the air onto that junction. As water condenses on the thermocouple, the temperature of the thermocouple rises asymptotically toward the dew point. When the temperature of the thermocouple reaches the dew point, condensation ceases, causing the thermocouple temperature to stabilize.

The dew-point temperature depression is the difference between the dew-point temperature for air in equilibrium with pure water and the dew-point temperature for air in equilibrium with the sample.

In some instruments, the solution to be tested is pipetted onto a small, solute-free paper disk, which is then inserted into the sample chamber containing the thermocouple hygrometer. In other instruments, the solution is tested as a drop at the end of a micropipette inserted into the chamber.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Peltier effect（帕爾帖效應）：通電後在兩種不同導體的接合面產生吸熱或放熱的熱電現象。蒸氣壓法用此原理精確將熱電偶冷卻到露點以下。

Dew-point temperature depression（露點下降）：溶液上方的空氣比純水上方的空氣需要更低的溫度才能達到飽和（因為溶質降低了水的蒸氣壓）。這個溫度差就是蒸氣壓法量 osmolality 的訊號。

樣品量優勢：蒸氣壓法只需 5–20 µL，遠少於凍結點法（20–2000 µL）。

比喻說明 Analogy

夏天早晨草地上的露珠，形成於草的溫度低於環境露點時。含溶質的水比純水更難形成露水（蒸氣壓更低），就像「更不願意蒸發的水」——需要冷卻到更低溫度才會凝結。蒸氣壓滲透量計就是精確量測這個「凝結點」的差異來反推溶液的 osmolality。

重點提示 Key Notes

溫度控制至關重要：蒸氣壓法在室溫操作，但室溫波動會直接影響測量結果。USP 要求確保樣品與儀器都完全平衡至實驗室溫度後再測量（儀器開機至少 30 分鐘）。

加樣一致性：使用校正過的移液器，樣品加入時機（timing）必須一致，與校正時保持相同操作程序——這是保證 repeatability 的關鍵。

揮發性成分禁用：乙醇等揮發性溶質會在密封腔體中快速蒸發至氣相，干擾露點測量，導致系統性偏低的 osmolality 讀數。

  2. 若使用紙片式加樣的蒸氣壓法儀器，為何紙片必須是「無溶質」的？若紙片殘留雜質會有什麼影響？

Qualification of Osmolality Instruments — Overview 資格確認概述

原文內容 Original Text

Osmometers are classified as Group B instruments as described in Analytical Instrument Qualification 〈1058〉. The qualification of an osmometer instrument should cover the intended operational range of the instrument, the required accuracy of the instrument, and the required precision of the instrument.

Acceptance criteria for fitness for purpose are based on the uncertainty of the reference material and the performance specification of the instrument. Any osmometer that is shown to be fit for the intended purpose, by meeting the qualification criteria, may be used to provide osmolality results according to the guidelines of this chapter.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Group B instruments（B 類儀器）：USP 〈1058〉 依儀器複雜度分類。Group A（簡單工具，如容量瓶）無需正式 OQ；Group B（有測量功能的儀器，如 osmometer、天平、pH 計）需 IQ + OQ + PQ；Group C（系統級儀器，如 HPLC）需完整電腦化系統驗證。

資格確認三層架構：

  • IQ（安裝資格確認）：硬體與軟體已正確安裝在指定位置
  • OQ（操作資格確認）：儀器在規格範圍內正常運作（精確度 + 準確度）
  • PQ（效能資格確認）：在常規使用條件下持續符合性能要求

比喻說明 Analogy

儀器資格確認就像新車驗收：IQ = 確認車型、配備、VIN 號都符合訂單；OQ = 試車道測試剎車、轉向、儀表板都正常運作；PQ = 正式上路後定期保養記錄，確認長期可靠性。三個階段缺一不可，共同確保「這台車在我的道路條件下安全可靠」。

重點提示 Key Notes

「fit for the intended purpose」是 USP 的核心判斷標準。不是所有滲透量計都需要驗證到同一水準——關鍵是「在你的測量範圍和精度要求下」是否合格。一台用於測量 50–500 mOsmol/kg 的儀器，不需要驗證到 4000 mOsmol/kg。這給了 CDMO 根據產品需求制定合適資格確認方案的彈性。

重點提示 Key Notes

Operational Qualification (OQ) 操作資格確認

原文內容 Original Text

The operational qualification (OQ) for the osmolality instrument should characterize the precision and accuracy of the osmolality measurements. Prior to characterizing the performance of the osmolality instrument, perform a full calibration of the instrument, as instructed by the instrument operation manual, and confirm the proper calibration with a calibration check.

Optimization of Adjustable Freezing Parameters

Freezing-point depression osmometers may have user-adjustable parameters that affect the freezing of the Test Solutions. These parameters may include:

  • Stirring amplitude (during cooling step)
  • "Buzz" point (amount of supercooling that precedes initiation of freezing)
  • "Buzz" amplitude (amplitude used to initiate freezing)

These test parameters may be optimized, following the manufacturer's recommendations, to reliably freeze the Test Solutions.

Precision / Repeatability

The precision should be characterized by performing six or more repeated measurements on the mid-range osmolality standard solution. Calculate the sample standard deviation using (n−1) degrees of freedom, σ, and express repeatability as:

R% = 100σ / X̄₀
Rosm = R% × X̄ / 100

The acceptance criterion is that the relative standard deviation (RSD) should be NMT 2%. If the result is significantly greater than the claimed repeatability (e.g., more than twice), the performance should be more fully investigated.

Accuracy

Verify the accuracy by measurement of at least three replicates of the highest and lowest osmolality standards used during calibration. Acceptance criteria:

  • Standards ≤ 400 mOsmol/kg: within ±4 mOsmol/kg of stated value
  • Standards > 400 mOsmol/kg: within ±1% of stated value

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

OQ 兩大驗證指標：

  • Precision（精確度）：多次重複測量同一標準液的一致性，以 RSD 表示。RSD ≤ 2% 是 USP 的最低標準。反映儀器的「穩定性」。
  • Accuracy（準確度）：測量值與標準值的接近程度。反映儀器的「正確性」。低濃度用絕對值（±4 mOsmol/kg），高濃度用相對值（±1%），因為高濃度的絕對誤差本身就更大。

OQ 計算示例

精確度計算 (Precision):
  測量 290 mOsmol/kg 標準液 n=6 次:
  結果: 288, 291, 290, 289, 292, 290
  平均值 X̄₀ = 290.0
  標準差 σ = 1.41 (用 n-1 自由度)
  R% = 100 × 1.41 / 290.0 = 0.49%
  → 通過 (≤ 2%)

  精確度換算到 400 mOsmol/kg 樣品:
  R_osm = 0.49% × 400 / 100 = 1.96 mOsmol/kg

準確度判定 (Accuracy):
  標準值 = 290 mOsmol/kg (≤400 mOsmol/kg)
  接受標準: ±4 mOsmol/kg
  → 儀器讀數必須在 286–294 mOsmol/kg

  標準值 = 1000 mOsmol/kg (>400 mOsmol/kg)
  接受標準: ±1% = ±10 mOsmol/kg
  → 儀器讀數必須在 990–1010 mOsmol/kg

重點提示 Key Notes

Buzz 參數優化的重要性：凍結點法中，buzz point 和 buzz amplitude 直接影響能否可靠凍結不同類型的樣品。高黏度樣品、含表面活性劑的樣品、含蛋白質的樣品都可能需要不同的 buzz 設定。OQ 階段必須用有代表性的矩陣（基質）測試，而非只用純水或 NaCl 溶液。

「n−1 自由度」：這是樣本標準差（非母體標準差）的計算方式，適用於有限樣本的不確定度評估，是統計學正確做法。

實務應用 Practical Application

在 CDMO 的儀器資格確認實踐中，OQ 的精確度測試應選擇「接近日常使用範圍中心點」的標準液，而非任意濃度。若你的注射劑產品規格是 280–320 mOsmol/kg，則應選用 290 mOsmol/kg 標準液進行 OQ 精確度測試，以確保在最相關的測量範圍內已充分驗證。

Performance Qualification (PQ) 效能資格確認

原文內容 Original Text

The performance qualification (PQ) should be evaluated by periodic recalibration of the instrument and a day-of-use calibration check. It is recommended that the result for the day-of-use calibration check be monitored over time to track performance of the instrument.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

PQ 的兩個核心機制：

  • Periodic recalibration（定期重新校正）：依廠商建議或內部 SOP 設定頻率（通常每季或每半年），使用 Table 1 標準液完整校正，並用更高/更低標準液確認準確度
  • Day-of-use calibration check（每日使用前校正確認）：每次使用前執行簡短確認，確保儀器在當日狀態下仍符合準確度標準

重點提示 Key Notes

趨勢監測（Trending）是 PQ 的精髓。每日校正確認結果應記入趨勢圖，用統計製程管控（SPC）工具觀察是否有系統性漂移（drift）或突然偏移（shift）。這能在儀器性能惡化導致產品放行失敗前提前預警，是符合 ICH Q10 製藥品質體系持續改進精神的最佳實踐。

比喻說明 Analogy

PQ 就像汽車行駛里程追蹤：定期保養（periodic recalibration）確保大系統正常；每次出發前的快速檢查（day-of-use check）確保當天狀況良好；把每次油耗記錄下來看趨勢（trending），就能提前發現引擎問題，而不是在高速公路上拋錨才知道出事了。

Operation and Calibration — Standard Solutions 標準液配製與校正

原文內容 Original Text

Standard Solutions

Solutions for instrument calibration may be purchased or may be prepared as specified in Table 1. Other concentrations not listed in Table 1 are acceptable. They may be stored at 2°–30°, but should not be frozen. When using purchased solutions, follow the manufacturer's expiry date and use instructions. Purchased solutions are typically provided in single-use ampules and should be discarded after use.

Solutions prepared as specified in Table 1 should be stored in nonpermeable (e.g., glass) containers to prevent evaporation. Fresh solutions should be prepared at least monthly as they are often nonsterile unpreserved solutions.

Calibration

The osmometer should be calibrated as per the manufacturer's instructions using either purchased or prepared Standard Solutions (as described in Table 1).

Procedure for Calibration Check

The calibration check should be performed in the same manner that measurements will be performed:

  • If individual measurements will be reported → calibration check with a single measurement
  • If mean of three measurements will be reported → calibration check with the mean of three measurements

Unless a different solution is specified, select at least one solution from Table 1 that lies near the center of the expected range of osmolality for the test solution. Two Standard Solutions bracketing the test sample within 300 mOsmol/kg are also acceptable.

Acceptance criteria:

  • Solutions ≤ 400 mOsmol/kg: within ±4 mOsmol/kg from stated value
  • Solutions > 400 mOsmol/kg: within ±1% from stated value

If the instrument fails the calibration check, recalibrate per manufacturer's instructions, then repeat the calibration check. After a successful check, the instrument is ready for use.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

校正 vs. 校正確認（Calibration vs. Calibration Check）的重要區別：

  • Calibration（完整校正）：至少兩點（低 + 高標準液），調整儀器讀數使其符合標準值。通常在開始使用新標準液時或 PQ 定期重新校正時執行。
  • Calibration Check（校正確認）：每日使用前的快速確認，不調整儀器，只判斷「當前是否仍在接受標準內」。若不通過，才執行完整校正再確認。

重點提示 Key Notes

校正確認標準液選擇邏輯：

  • 首選：在預期測量範圍中心點的標準液（最佳代表性）
  • 備選：兩個「夾住」測量值且各自在 ±300 mOsmol/kg 內的標準液
  • 目的：確保校正在最相關的濃度範圍內是準確的，而非只驗證了遠離實際使用點的範圍

標準液冷凍禁止：冷凍會破壞溶液均勻性（部分水先結冰，改變剩餘液體濃度），解凍後即使外觀正常，滲透量也可能偏移。

比喻說明 Analogy

Method 1: Freezing-Point Osmometry — Procedure

Method 1: Troubleshooting

Samples may sometimes fail to freeze if the sample viscosity is too high, if the sample is precooled too much or not enough, or if the mixing speed or the amplitude of the instrument is not optimally set. If test solutions occasionally fail to freeze, the osmolality measurements may not be accurate. The settings should be optimized to reliably freeze the test solution, and then the instrument should be recalibrated with the optimized settings prior to re-evaluation.

Insoluble particles, emulsified droplets that are insoluble and entrained air, will not significantly affect the osmolality of the aqueous vehicle; however, these may affect the ability of the sample to freeze. If accurate measurement of the osmolality is affected by the presence of the insoluble phase, the insoluble phase should be removed by centrifugation and the osmolality of the clarified aqueous phase should be evaluated and reported.

Reporting of Results 結果報告

原文內容 Original Text

Units

The units of osmolality are osmole per kilogram of water (Osmol/kg) or milliosmole per kilogram of water (mOsmol/kg). The units of osmolarity are osmole per liter of solution (Osmol/L) or milliosmole per liter of solution (mOsmol/L). It is not necessary to state "of water" or "of solution" when using these units. The use of "Osmol" alone must be avoided because it is not clear whether it refers to the number (osmoles of solute) or the osmolality or the osmolarity.

Measurement Result

If a single measurement result is reported, it should be reported with the precision given by the repeatability of the instrument. Preferably, the mean of three or more measurements on the same solution should be reported with a precision represented by the standard deviation of the averaged measurements. Most osmometers report results to the nearest whole number, therefore, the mean result should also be rounded to the nearest whole number and reported in units of mOsmol/kg.

Conversion to Osmolarity

Freezing-point depression and vapor pressure measurements directly result in an assessment of the osmolality. However, it may be desirable to report the results in units of osmolarity. The measured osmolality ξm can be converted to osmolarity ξc using:

ξc = ξm × (ρ − C)

where ρ is the density of the solution and C is the total solute concentration, both expressed in grams per milliliter.

Example: A 3% (w/v) solution of sodium bicarbonate contains 0.030 g/mL of anhydrous sodium bicarbonate. The density of this solution was found to be 1.0192 g/mL at 20°, and its measured osmolality ξm was 615 mOsmol/kg.

(ρ − C) = (1.0192 − 0.030) = 0.9892 kg water / L solution
Osmolarity = 615 mOsmol/kg × 0.9892 kg/L = 608 mOsmol/L

為何報告平均值而非單次值？

滲透量計每次測量有隨機誤差（儀器聲稱 repeatability 通常為 ±1–3 mOsmol/kg）。單次測量結果不確定度大；三次平均可將不確定度縮小約 √3 ≈ 1.73 倍。對於規格窗口窄的產品（如 280–320 mOsmol/kg，窗口只有 40 mOsmol/kg），三次平均是確保報告結果可靠的基本要求。

Osmolality → Osmolarity 換算公式

公式: ξc = ξm × (ρ − C)

說明:
  ξm = 測量的 osmolality (mOsmol/kg)
  ρ  = 溶液密度 (g/mL = kg/L)
  C  = 總溶質濃度 (g/mL)
  (ρ−C) = 每升溶液中水的質量 (kg water/L solution)

範例計算 — 3% NaHCO₃ 溶液:
  ξm  = 615 mOsmol/kg
  ρ   = 1.0192 g/mL
  C   = 0.030 g/mL
  (ρ−C) = 1.0192 − 0.030 = 0.9892 kg/L
  ξc  = 615 × 0.9892 = 608 mOsmol/L

結論: osmolarity 略低於 osmolality
（因為含溶質的溶液，1 L 溶液中的水略少於 1 kg）

補充公式（另一種等效表達）:
  ξc = 1000 × ξm / (1000/ρ + Σ wi × νi)
  其中 νi = 溶質i的偏比容 (mL/g)
  鹽類 νi ≈ 0.1 mL/g; 胺基酸 νi ≈ 0.6–0.9 mL/g

重點提示 Key Notes

Osmolarity 永遠略低於 osmolality（對水性溶液而言）：因為含溶質的溶液，1 公升溶液中的水少於 1 公斤（被溶質佔去了一部分體積）。差距通常只有 0.5–3%，對於低滲透量溶液影響不大，但對高滲透量（>1000 mOsmol/kg）的溶液差距較明顯。

換算需要密度測量：必須實際測量溶液密度，不能使用純水密度代替。注射劑中的輔料（甘露醇、氯化鈉等）會顯著改變溶液密度。

Tonicity 張力

原文內容 Original Text

Tonicity is a medical term that relates to the osmotic pressure difference between the internal and external sides of a cell membrane. In many cases tonicity and osmolality are not the same because some solutes are able to rapidly pass through the cell membrane.

Red blood cells (erythrocytes) are typically considered the standard for defining what is considered an isotonic solution:

  • Hypotonic: red blood cells swell and become turgid or burst
  • Hypertonic: red blood cells shrink and desiccate
  • Isotonic: no change in the size of the red blood cells, cell wall remains flaccid

Measurements of osmolality or osmolarity alone are not sufficient to characterize the tonicity of a liquid. It is preferable to use the terms hypoosmotic, hyperosmotic, and isoosmotic when discussing the relative osmolality of solutions.

The standard for an isoosmotic solution is 0.9% sodium chloride (NaCl) solution, which has an osmolality of 285 mOsmol/kg and an osmolarity of 284 mOsmol/L.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Tonicity vs. Osmolality — 為何不同？

關鍵在於溶質是否能穿越細胞膜：

  • 不能穿越（如 NaCl、甘露醇）：完全貢獻 tonicity → osmolality ≈ tonicity effect
  • 能快速穿越（如尿素、乙醇、乙二醇）：不影響 tonicity → 高滲透量但不導致細胞脫水

例：一個含高濃度尿素的溶液，osmolality 可能是 400 mOsmol/kg（高滲），但對紅血球而言是「等張」的，因為尿素可自由穿越細胞膜，不會造成滲透壓差。

術語對照：

  • Hypoosmotic（低滲透量）≈ 對NaCl等有效溶質而言的 hypotonic
  • Hyperosmotic（高滲透量）≈ 對NaCl等有效溶質而言的 hypertonic
  • Isoosmotic（等滲透量）= osmolality 接近 285–310 mOsmol/kg

比喻說明 Analogy

把細胞膜想像成一扇門：有些溶質是「有鑰匙的」（能自由進出，如尿素），有些是「沒有鑰匙的」（被擋在門外，如 NaCl）。只有被擋在門外的溶質才真正產生滲透壓差（tonicity 效果）。測量 osmolality 是量「門外所有人」的總數，而 tonicity 只量「沒有鑰匙、真的被擋住」的那部分人。所以 osmolality 是 tonicity 的上限，實際 tonicity 通常更低。

重點提示 Key Notes

臨床重要性：注射劑標籤上標示的「等張」（isotonic）是基於 tonicity（對細胞膜有效溶質），而非單純 osmolality。將「等張」等同於 285 mOsmol/kg 是常見的簡化，在大多數情況下成立，但含高濃度通透性溶質（如甘油、尿素）的製劑需要特別說明。

0.9% NaCl 的標準地位：285 mOsmol/kg（osmolality）/ 284 mOsmol/L（osmolarity）是業界公認的等滲參考點。這也是為何注射劑通常以此為設計目標。

Osmolarity — Calculated vs. Measured 滲透濃度：計算值與測量值

原文內容 Original Text

[Note: The following content was part of the "OSMOLARITY" section (now deleted in the current USP 〈785〉 version). It is included here for educational context and historical reference, as it illustrates the important distinction between theoretical osmolarity calculation and experimentally determined osmolality.]

Osmolarity of a solution is a theoretical quantity expressed in osmoles/L (Osmol/L) and is widely used in clinical practice because it expresses osmoles as a function of volume. Osmolarity cannot be measured but is calculated theoretically from the experimentally measured value of osmolality.

Sometimes, osmolarity (ξc) is calculated theoretically from the molar concentrations:

ξc = Σ νi × ci
where νi = number of particles formed by dissociation of one molecule of the ith solute
ci = molar concentration of the ith solute

Example: Osmolarity of a solution prepared by dissolving 1 g of vancomycin in 100 mL of 0.9% NaCl:

[3 × 10 g/L / 1449.25 (MW vancomycin)] + [2 × 9 g/L / 58.44 (MW NaCl)] × 1000
= 329 mOsmol/L (calculated)

However, the solution was found to be hypoosmotic with an experimentally determined osmolality of 255 mOsmol/kg.

This example illustrates that osmolarity values calculated theoretically should be interpreted cautiously and may not represent the osmotic properties of infusion solutions. The discrepancy is due to interactions between solute molecules or between solute and solvent molecules, related to the molal osmotic coefficient (Φm).

SLS Example Calculation (from deleted section):

An aqueous sodium lauryl sulfate (SLS) solution at 30% (w/v), specific gravity 1.08, containing 0.5% sodium sulfate:

  • SLS (MW 288.37, ν=2): 300 g/L × 2 mol/mol ÷ 288.37 = 2080.6 mOsmol/L
  • Na₂SO₄ (MW 142.04, ν=3): 5 g/L × 3 mol/mol ÷ 142.04 = 105.6 mOsmol/L
  • Total osmolarity = 2186.2 mOsmol/L

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

「計算的 osmolarity」永遠只是估算！

Vancomycin 案例是教科書級的示範：計算值（329 mOsmol/L）比實測值（255 mOsmol/kg）高出 29%。原因包括：

  • Vancomycin 是大分子糖肽，在水中有強的自締合（self-association）傾向，有效溶質粒子數遠少於理論值
  • 計算用 ν = 3（一個分子解離成 3 個粒子），但實際上不完全解離
  • Φm << 1，大幅降低了有效滲透壓

這就是為何 USP 〈785〉 強調：「osmolarity values calculated theoretically should be interpreted cautiously」，以及為何現行版本的章節架構把「測量 osmolality」擺在核心位置。

Theoretical Osmolarity Calculation

理論計算公式（適用於理想溶液）:
  ξc,theoretical = Σ (νi × ci)
  νi = 解離係數（離子數）
  ci = 各溶質莫耳濃度

Vancomycin 案例:
  Vanco 10 g/L ÷ 1449.25 g/mol × 3 = 20.7 mOsmol/L
  NaCl  9 g/L ÷ 58.44 g/mol × 2 = 308.0 mOsmol/L
  計算總計 = 329 mOsmol/L
  實測滲透量 = 255 mOsmol/kg
  差異 = -23%（計算值高估了 osmolality）

結論: 含大分子或有自締合的溶質時，
      計算值誤差可達 20–50%，必須實驗測定！

  • 理論計算 osmolarity 不可靠，不應作為製藥測試的替代方案
  • 所有 osmolality/osmolarity 結果應以實驗測定為基礎
  • 換算公式 ξc = ξm × (ρ−C) 才是正確的 osmolarity 計算方式

比喻說明 Analogy

理論計算 osmolarity 就像只看食譜上的材料清單估算一道菜的鹹度——理論上你知道放了多少鹽，但鹽分在烹飪過程中與其他食材的交互作用是無法預測的。實際嘗味（實驗測定）才是唯一可靠的方式。Vancomycin 案例就是一個「理論上很鹹，實際上不鹹」的典型例子。
