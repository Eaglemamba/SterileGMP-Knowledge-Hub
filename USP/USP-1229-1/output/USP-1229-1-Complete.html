Scope and Background — 範圍與背景

Original Text

Steam sterilization is perhaps the most common of all sterilization processes. It is used in settings ranging from practitioner offices to large-scale manufacturing facilities. The diversity of practices that use steam sterilization is reflected in the range and sophistication of the equipment used.

This general information chapter addresses sterilization in which saturated steam comes into direct contact with the load items (whether wrapped or unwrapped) and provides an overview of the basic concepts of this mode of sterilization, including its validation.

The load items in this sterilization process are variously termed parts, components, hard goods, wrapped goods, or porous goods. These items may be metallic, glass, ceramic, elastomeric, or polymeric materials that have little or no sensitivity to thermal degradation at the sterilizing temperatures. For steam sterilization by direct contact, it is customary to sterilize items using an overkill method.

Sterilization of liquid-filled containers may be substantially different. Moist Heat Sterilization of Aqueous Liquids 〈1229.2〉 provides information about applications in which steam is a heating medium but is not in contact with the sterilization target, the liquid in the container.

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

〈1229.1〉 的適用範圍：本章專門討論蒸汽與被滅菌物品直接接觸的情境。這包括：

  • 設備零組件 — 充填機零件、濾殼（filter housings）、管路（tubing）、軟管（hoses）
  • 容器/封蓋 — 西林瓶塞（stoppers）、鋁蓋（seals）、玻璃瓶
  • 工具/器具 — 刮刀、燒杯、量筒等簡單幾何形狀的器具

重點提示 CDMO Context

為什麼這是「最常用」的滅菌方法？在無菌製造的 CDMO 環境中，每一批產品生產前，所有與產品接觸的設備零件、容器封蓋都必須經過滅菌。高壓蒸汽滅菌（Autoclave）是首選方法，因為：

  • 殺滅力最強 — 飽和蒸汽的相變釋能機制遠勝乾熱
  • 可重複驗證 — 溫度-壓力的唯一對應關係使監控簡單可靠
  • 適用 Overkill — 對於耐熱物品，可以採用最寬裕的滅菌條件
Saturated Steam — 飽和蒸汽

Original Text

Saturated steam is a biphasic mixture of H2O in gas and liquid phases in thermal equilibrium. Saturated steam has a singular temperature–pressure relationship in which both phases are present, and at a given temperature only one pressure is possible for saturation.

The importance of using saturated steam for sterilization arises primarily from two attributes. First, saturated steam rapidly kills microorganisms because of the presence of liquid water. Steam heated above saturation, also termed superheated steam, lacks liquid water, and although it is higher in temperature than saturated steam it is substantially less lethal to microbes.

Second, when steam changes phase from gas to liquid, it releases thermal energy (2202 kJ/kg at 121°C) that is transferred to the load items, facilitating sterilization of their exposed surfaces.

The initial objective for saturated steam sterilization is that the air in the sterilizing chamber must be replaced by saturated steam. Residual air within the sterilizer chamber and load items acts as both an insulator and an obstacle to steam penetration to all surfaces of the load items, and its removal is essential for effective sterilization. The presence of residual air in the chamber negates the singular temperature–pressure relationship of saturated steam. In the absence of saturation, physical measurements may not provide assurance of lethality.

"The presence of residual air in the chamber negates the singular temperature–pressure relationship of saturated steam. In the absence of saturation, physical measurements may not provide assurance of lethality."

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Saturated Steam Properties

飽和蒸汽的兩大殺菌優勢：

  • 液態水的存在 — 水分子能穿透微生物的細胞壁，導致蛋白質水解變性（hydrolysis），這比乾熱的氧化機制快得多
  • 相變釋能 — 蒸汽凝結為水時釋放巨大潛熱（2202 kJ/kg），迅速將熱能傳遞給負載表面

溫度-壓力唯一對應：在飽和狀態下，121°C 對應約 1.06 bar（表壓）/ 2.06 bar（絕對壓力）。這個對應關係是我們能用壓力間接確認溫度的基礎。

比喻說明 Analogy

為什麼過熱蒸汽反而殺菌力弱？

想像用蒸氣熨斗燙衣服：噴出「濕潤蒸汽」的熨斗能迅速把衣服燙平，因為水蒸汽碰到布料時凝結釋放熱量，直接穿透纖維。但如果你用「乾燥的熱風」，即使溫度更高，效果反而差 — 因為缺少液態水的直接接觸和凝結釋能。

過熱蒸汽就像「乾燥熱風」— 溫度高但缺少液態水，殺菌效率大幅下降。

重點提示 Air Removal Is Critical

殘餘空氣的致命影響：

  • 絕熱效應 — 空氣是熱的不良導體，形成隔熱層阻擋蒸汽接觸物品表面
  • 破壞溫度-壓力關係 — 空氣+蒸汽的混合氣體，其壓力 = 蒸汽分壓 + 空氣分壓（Dalton’s Law）。此時壓力表讀數不再反映真實蒸汽溫度
  • 實際後果 — 壓力表顯示達到滅菌壓力，但實際溫度可能不足，產生「假陽性」的滅菌結果

這就是為什麼空氣排除測試（如 Bowie-Dick Test）在預真空滅菌器中是每日必做的確認項目。

Gravity Displacement Cycles — 重力排氣循環

Original Text

In the simplest autoclave cycles, air removal is accomplished by gravity displacement. Because steam is hotter and less dense than air, it rises to the top of the autoclave, and the colder air exits at the bottom of the chamber.

Saturated steam entering the chamber changes into liquid condensate as it contacts the colder surfaces of the autoclave chamber and load items. Retention of condensate within the load reduces cycle effectiveness because it is a barrier to steam contact, and additional steam is needed to maintain the saturated steam at the sterilizing temperature.

The load items, wrapping materials, and load arrangement should be designed to facilitate air removal and condensate drainage. In gravity displacement cycles, the load slowly reaches the desired sterilizing temperature because air removal is relatively slow compared to cycles in which its removal is mechanically assisted.

During the exposure segment of the cycle, a thermostatic trap at the bottom of the chamber drain allows the removal of condensate (and any residual air) from the sterilizer while maintaining sterilizing conditions. At the conclusion of the dwell period, the chamber is returned to atmospheric pressure.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Gravity Displacement

工作原理：利用蒸汽與空氣的密度差異進行自然排氣。蒸汽密度低（輕），自然上升；空氣密度高（重），自然下沉並從底部排出。

  • 蒸汽入口 — 通常在腔室頂部或上方
  • 排氣口 — 位於腔室底部，經過恆溫疏水閥（Thermostatic Trap）
  • 恆溫疏水閥 — 感測流體溫度：冷凝水和空氣（溫度低）通過排出；蒸汽（溫度高）被阻擋留在腔內

重點提示 Practical Limitations

CDMO 環境中的考量：

  • 升溫慢 — 重力排氣依賴自然對流，對於複雜或密集的負載，空氣排除不完全的風險較高
  • 冷凝水滯留 — 如果包裝材料或負載排列不當，冷凝水無法順利排出，形成「水窪」阻礙蒸汽接觸
  • 適用場景 — 簡單金屬工具、不包裝的玻璃器皿等。複雜充填零件通常使用預真空循環
Prevacuum Cycles — 預真空循環

Original Text

To remove air more effectively from the chamber and the load items, sterilizers may employ multiple evacuation/pressure pulses in which air is replaced by steam. The number and depth of these pulses may vary.

Because the alternating vacuum and pressure pulses may stress wrapping materials, the latter must be chosen carefully. The operation of the sterilizer during the exposure segment is similar to that of the gravity displacement cycle previously described.

The vacuum system can be used at the end of the process to remove residual steam and condensate from the load items. The selection of a specific cycle and its associated sterilization parameters for a given item depends on a number of factors, including the heat lability of the material, heat penetration into the article, the item’s mass, difficulties with air/condensate removal, and other factors described in the validation program.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Prevacuum Mechanism

多脈衝抽真空/注蒸汽循環：

  • 第一脈衝 — 抽真空移除大部分空氣，注入蒸汽
  • 第二脈衝 — 再次抽真空，移除殘餘空氣+蒸汽混合物，再注入新鮮蒸汽
  • 第三脈衝（或更多） — 持續稀釋殘餘空氣，達到接近 100% 蒸汽的環境

每次脈衝後，腔內空氣濃度呈指數下降。典型的預真空循環使用 3-4 次脈衝。

重點提示 Cycle Selection Factors

比喻說明 Analogy

洗衣機的脫水原理：預真空循環就像洗衣機的多次「注水–排水」循環 — 每次排水（抽真空）後再注入新的清潔水（蒸汽），反覆幾次就能把衣物中的髒水（空氣）徹底稀釋排出。單靠自然滲透（重力排氣）是不夠的。

Sterilization Cycle Control & F0 Calculation — 滅菌循環控制與 F0 計算

Original Text

Sterilizers are controlled by computerized/automated systems that manage the overall process execution and data reporting. The systems for steam sterilization may be controlled by calibrated temperature and/or pressure sensors on the equipment.

During the exposure portions of the cycle, a minimum dwell time at a predefined temperature is required to ensure the method lethality target (minimum time–temperature or F0) is met.

Cycle efficacy for steam sterilization often is measured using F0, which is defined as the equivalent exposure time at 121°C. F0 is a means for quantifying steam sterilization effectiveness by determining the equivalent sterilization time in minutes relative to a base temperature of 121°C and a z-value of 10°C; z-value is defined as the number of degrees of temperature change necessary to change the D-value by a factor of 10.

The process lethality at temperatures other than 121°C can be calculated to determine lethality equivalent to that provided at 121°C. Moist heat sterilization process efficacy is not intrinsically linked to a target temperature of 121°C, which is simply the Celsius conversion of 250°F, and other temperatures can be used.

Sterilizer control systems for direct sterilization typically provide a minimum time at a defined set point temperature after the initial air/condensate removal. Steam sterilizers are controlled using temperature sensors located in the drain line before the thermostatic trap, although other control schemes may be used. The temperature at this location typically is recorded for permanent documentation of sterilizing conditions.

In sterilization by direct contact, exceeding the minimum time–temperature requirements or F0 is acceptable because of minimal adverse consequences to the materials being sterilized.

"In sterilization by direct contact, exceeding the minimum time–temperature requirements or F0 is acceptable because of minimal adverse consequences to the materials being sterilized."

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

F0 Formula — F0 公式

F₀ = ∫ 10^[(T - 121) / 10] dt

     t1 → t2

Discrete approximation:

F₀ = Σ 10^[(T - 121) / 10] × Δt

Where:

  • T = measured temperature (°C) at each time point
  • 121 = reference temperature (°C)
  • 10 = z-value (°C) — temperature change for 10× D-value change
  • Δt = time interval (minutes), typically 1 min or 0.5 min
  • t1, t2 = start time (≥100°C) and end of dwell period

核心概念解析 F0 Interpretation

F0 的本質：將不同溫度下的滅菌效果，統一換算為「在 121°C 下的等效暴露分鐘數」。

  • F0 = 15 min — 等效於在 121°C 暴露 15 分鐘的滅菌效果
  • Overkill 標準 — 常見要求 F0 ≥ 15 min（半循環法基礎）或更高
  • 121°C 不是魔法數字 — 它只是 250°F 的攝氏換算值，USP 明確指出可以使用其他溫度

重點提示 Practical Calculation Example

關鍵觀察：溫度每升高 10°C，滅菌速率增加 10 倍。從 121°C 到 131°C，F0 累積速度快 10 倍。

F0 計算起點：USP 規定應從 100°C 開始計算，並持續到保溫期結束（前提是維持飽和蒸汽條件）。

重點提示 Control Sensor Location

為什麼控制探頭在排水管線（Drain Line）？排水管位於腔室底部，是最後被蒸汽到達的位置（因為冷空氣和冷凝水積聚在此）。當排水管溫度達標時，代表整個腔室都已達到滅菌溫度 — 這是最保守、最安全的控制策略。

Validation of Sterilization by Direct Contact — 直接接觸滅菌驗證

Original Text

The predominant approach for steam sterilization by direct contact is the overkill method defined in Sterilization of Compendial Articles 〈1229〉. Overkill sterilization is a method in which the destruction of a high concentration of a resistant microorganism is correlated with the destruction of reasonably anticipated bioburden present during routine processing.

That objective can be demonstrated by attaining any of the following:

  • A defined minimum lethality (F0)
  • A defined set of physical conditions
  • Confirmation of a minimum log reduction of a resistant biological indicator

The validation requirements for the overkill method are less onerous than those for other methods such as those based on bioburden or bioburden/biological indicators. When the load items can withstand substantial heat without adverse consequence, overkill is the method of choice for steam sterilization because of its ease of execution, reduced considerations for bioburden control, and overall simplicity.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Overkill Method

Overkill（過度殺滅）的三種證明途徑：

  1. 最低 F0 法 — 所有監測點的 F0 值達到預設最低標準（常見 ≥ 15 min 或 ≥ 12 min）
  2. 物理條件法 — 達到預設的最低溫度持續預設的最低時間（如 121°C × 15 min）
  3. BI 對數降低法 — 確認高抗性生物指示劑（如 106 spores of G. stearothermophilus）在半循環條件下全部被殺滅

比喻說明 Analogy

「如果能殺死最強的敵人，普通敵人更不在話下」：

Overkill 的邏輯就像軍事訓練 — 如果你的防禦系統能擊退最精銳的特種部隊（高抗性 BI 芽孢），那對付一般的入侵者（日常生物負載）自然綽綽有餘。這樣你就不需要花大量資源去了解每批產品的具體敵情（bioburden characterization），大大簡化了驗證工作。

重點提示 Why Overkill Is Preferred

CDMO 為什麼幾乎都用 Overkill？

  • 驗證簡單 — 不需要進行生物負載的深入鑑定和抗性測試
  • 換產品風險低 — CDMO 經常切換不同客戶的產品，Overkill 不依賴特定產品的 bioburden 數據
  • 法規審查容易 — Overkill 是最保守的方法，稽查員對此接受度最高
  • 可以超標 — 直接接觸滅菌中，超過 F0 最低值不會損害耐熱負載
Equipment Qualification — 設備確認

Original Text

Equipment qualification is a predefined program that examines the equipment to confirm that it has been properly installed and operates as intended before the sterilization process. Equipment qualification can be separated into installation qualification and operational qualification, or can be considered joint installation and operational qualification. The qualification effort provides a baseline for the sterilizer’s preventive maintenance and change control.

Empty Chamber Temperature Distribution

A common procedure to evaluate steam sterilizer installation is the evaluation of the empty chamber’s performance. Each air removal method used in the sterilizer is evaluated by temperature measurement near the corners of the sterilizer chamber, near the controlling probe, and other locations as appropriate.

The distribution of temperatures in the empty chamber should be determined only by sensors located in the chamber, and the temperatures of the chamber drain or outside the chamber proper are not directly relevant in this validation activity.

Differences in the cycle dwell period can be ignored because only the shortest dwell period for each air removal method must be evaluated. The acceptance criteria for this test vary with the sterilizer’s capabilities and customary use. Biological indicators are not required in the evaluation of empty chamber temperature distribution.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Equipment Qualification

IQ/OQ 的目的：確認滅菌器在未放入任何負載的情況下，本身的基本功能是否正常。

  • IQ（Installation Qualification） — 確認設備按設計安裝：管路連接、公用設施（蒸汽、壓縮空氣、排水）、控制系統、校準
  • OQ（Operational Qualification） — 確認設備在空腔狀態下運行正常：溫度分佈均勻、真空洩漏率、控制邏輯

重點提示 Empty Chamber Study

空腔溫度分佈研究要點：

  • 探頭位置 — 腔室各角落附近 + 控制探頭附近 + 其他適當位置（通常 10-15 支熱電偶）
  • 僅用腔內探頭 — 排水管或腔外的溫度不計入此測試（它們有其他用途）
  • 最短保溫時間 — 只需測試該排氣方式的最短循環即可（最嚴格條件）
  • 不需要 BI — 空腔研究是純物理測量，不使用生物指示劑

常見接受標準：所有探頭在保溫期間的溫度偏差通常 ≤ ±1.0°C（依設備規格和使用目的而定）。

Component Mapping — 組件映射

Original Text

Items that are steam sterilized can be quite complex and may have interior void volumes, obscured surfaces, crevices, and difficult-to-reach product contact surfaces that must be sterilized. The ability of saturated steam to penetrate the wrapping materials or containers and to reach the surfaces should be established for each item.

Although this is relatively easy for simple items such as spatulas, beakers, and other simple geometric shapes, it can be substantially more difficult for filling assemblies, filter housings, tubing, and hoses.

Analysts should conduct studies to determine cold spots in items to ensure that heat penetration takes place throughout the load items using thermocouples in contact with the item’s surface. These studies can be performed in a laboratory setting and need not be repeated when the same item is sterilized in multiple autoclaves.

During this evaluation, all load items should be wrapped and oriented in a manner that facilitates steam ingress and air and condensate removal. Items must be wrapped and oriented in an essentially identical manner for reproducible sterilization.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Component Mapping

組件映射的目的：找出每個被滅菌物品的「最冷點」（Cold Spot）— 即蒸汽最難到達、溫度最後升到的位置。

為什麼重要？如果最冷點都能達到滅菌條件，其他位置一定沒問題。這是「最差條件驗證」的核心邏輯。

典型 CDMO 組件的冷點分析：

關鍵規則：組件映射研究可以在實驗室做，不需要在每台滅菌器上重複。但包裝方式和放置方向必須與實際一致。

Load Mapping — 負載映射

Original Text

The determination of loading patterns is an essential practice for terminal sterilization of aqueous liquids by moist heat (see Moist Heat Sterilization of Aqueous Liquids 〈1229.2〉), but this practice is not a critical concern regarding direct sterilization of items because differences between components play a greater role than location within the load.

Loads for direct steam sterilization can be validated using a maximum and minimum load as determined by either the number of each item or their mass. Best practices include placing larger items on the lower shelves, allowing condensate from these items to exit the sterilizer with minimal contact with other load items.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Load Mapping vs Component Mapping

直接接觸滅菌的關鍵差異：與液體滅菌不同，負載在腔室中的位置不是主要影響因素。更重要的是組件本身的特性（幾何形狀、質量、包裝方式）。

驗證策略：最大/最小負載

  • 最大負載 — 代表最多物品、最大蒸汽需求、最多冷凝水的情境
  • 最小負載 — 代表最少物品的情境（某些設備在低負載時可能有不同的表現）

重點提示 Best Practice

大件物品放下層的原因：大件物品產生更多冷凝水。將它們放在下層，冷凝水直接流入底部排水口，不會滴落到下方其他物品上造成濕潤阻礙。

CDMO 實務：通常會定義若干標準負載配置（Standard Load Configurations），並以 SOP 和照片記錄每種配置的擺放方式。操作人員依照 SOP 擺放，不允許隨意變更。

Biological Indicators — 生物指示劑

Original Text

The commonly used biological indicator for steam sterilization by direct contact contains spores of Geobacillus stearothermophilus (ATCC 12980 or ATCC 7953), a thermophilic microorganism with a moist heat resistance substantially greater than that of most vegetative microorganisms.

The spore challenge can be placed on a substrate within or on a load item, or the challenge can be a load item that is inoculated with a spore suspension.

When biological indicators are used according to the manufacturer’s directions, the resistance information provided by the vendor can be used. End users must determine the population and resistance of inoculated items they prepare.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Biological Indicators

Geobacillus stearothermophilus 被選為蒸汽滅菌 BI 的原因：

  • 嗜熱性 — 最適生長溫度 55-60°C，芽孢在濕熱條件下的抗性遠高於常見微生物
  • 已知 D-value — 在 121°C 下，D-value 通常為 1.5-3.0 分鐘（依菌株和製備條件而定）
  • 標準化菌株 — ATCC 12980 和 ATCC 7953 是全球公認的標準菌株

重點提示 BI Placement

兩種 BI 使用方式：

  • 商業 BI — 放置在負載物品內/上。可直接使用供應商提供的族群數（Population）和 D-value 數據
  • 自製接種 BI — 將芽孢懸浮液接種在實際物品上。使用者必須自行測定族群數和抗性，不能使用供應商數據

CDMO 常見做法：使用商業 BI（如 Mesa Labs、3M 產品），放置在組件映射研究中確定的冷點位置。

Heat Penetration and Microbiological Challenge — 熱穿透與微生物挑戰

Original Text

The goal of the validation activity is the confirmation of acceptable heat penetration using temperature measurements and biological indicator challenges. Customarily this study is performed under conditions where the exposure time and/or temperature are reduced slightly from the routine set points.

Thermocouples and biological indicators should be placed with load items at the locations determined to be most difficult to heat during component mapping. Thermocouples should be in contact with the item’s surface.

Analysts must take care in the insertion of thermocouples and biological indicators so they do not alter the ability of the steam to enter the objects being challenged. This difficulty can be overcome with special fittings for thermocouple entry or by placement of temperature probes in units placed near the units that contain biological indicators.

In the latter case, replicate studies provide proof of cycle efficacy when both the biological indicators are killed and the physical measurements correspond to the expected time–temperature values or F0. If the microbial and physical measurements do not meet predefined acceptance criteria, an investigation is required and corrective action is necessary to rectify the discrepancy.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Half-Cycle Approach

減少暴露條件（Reduced Conditions）：這就是著名的半循環法（Half-Cycle Method）的基礎。例如：

  • 常規循環：121°C × 30 min
  • 驗證循環：121°C × 15 min（半循環）

邏輯：如果在半循環條件下，所有 BI（通常 106 spores）都被殺滅，那麼常規的全循環提供的殺滅力至少是驗證循環的兩倍，安全裕度極大。

重點提示 TC/BI Placement Rules

熱電偶（TC）和 BI 的放置規則：

  • 位置 — 放在組件映射研究中確定的最冷點
  • TC 必須接觸物品表面 — 不是懸掛在空氣中，而是貼附在物品表面
  • 不能改變蒸汽穿透路徑 — 插入 TC 和 BI 時不能影響蒸汽進入物品的方式（如不能打開原本密封的蓋子）
  • 解決方案 — 使用專用的 TC 穿入接頭（thermocouple port fittings），或將 TC 放在相鄰的對照組件中

重點提示 Acceptance Criteria

物理與微生物結果必須一致：

  • 物理測量 — 所有 TC 的 F0 ≥ 預設最低值
  • 微生物測量 — 所有 BI 無生長（No Growth）
  • 兩者不一致 = 調查 — 例如 TC 顯示 F0 達標但 BI 陽性，可能表示蒸汽品質問題（非凝結性氣體、過熱）

本章重點回顧 Chapter Summary

  • 飽和蒸汽（Saturated Steam）是直接接觸滅菌的關鍵：液態水的殺菌力 + 相變釋能（2202 kJ/kg）+ 溫度-壓力唯一對應關係
  • 空氣排除是成功滅菌的前提 — 殘餘空氣破壞飽和狀態，導致物理測量無法保證殺滅力。重力排氣適用簡單負載，預真空循環適用複雜組件
  • F0 公式將不同溫度的殺菌效果統一換算為 121°C 等效分鐘數。計算從 100°C 開始，z-value = 10°C
  • Overkill 方法是直接接觸滅菌的首選驗證策略，可通過三種途徑證明：最低 F0、物理條件、BI 對數降低
  • 驗證流程：設備確認（空腔溫度分佈）→ 組件映射（找冷點）→ 負載映射（最大/最小）→ 熱穿透/微生物挑戰（半循環法）
  • BI：Geobacillus stearothermophilus（ATCC 12980 / 7953），放置在冷點位置
