USP &langle;1&rangle; Injections and Implanted Drug Products (Parenterals) — Product Quality Tests

Section 0: Introduction, Definitions & General Requirements
Source: USP-NF General Chapter &langle;1&rangle; — Pages 1–3 | MANDATORY Chapter (below 1000)

Introduction — 緒論

原文內容 Original Text

Parenteral drug products include both injections and implanted drug products that are injected through the skin or other external boundary tissue, or implanted within the body to allow the direct administration of the active drug substance(s) into blood vessels, organs, tissues, or lesions. Injections may exist as either immediate- or extended-release dosage forms. Implanted parenteral drug products are long-acting dosage forms that provide continuous release of the active drug substance(s), often for periods of months to years.

Routes of administration for parenteral drug products include intravenous, intraventricular, intra-arterial, intra-articular, subcutaneous, intramuscular, intrathecal, intracisternal, and intraocular.

Parenteral dosage forms include solutions, suspensions, emulsions, sterile powders for solutions and suspensions (including liposomes), implants (including microparticles), and products that consist of both a drug and a device such as drug-eluting stents. The definitions and descriptions of these dosage forms, and brief information about their composition and manufacturing processes, are found in Pharmaceutical Dosage Forms &langle;1151&rangle;.

[Note — All references to chapters above 1000 are for informational purposes only, for use as a helpful resource. These chapters are not mandatory unless explicitly called out for application.]

This chapter is divided into three main sections: (1) universal product quality tests that are applicable to parenteral dosage forms; (2) specific product quality tests, which are tests that should be considered in addition to Universal Tests; and (3) product quality tests for specific dosage forms, which list applicable tests (universal and specific) for the specific dosage form.

This chapter applies, in whole or in part, when referenced in a drug product monograph (see General Notices, 3.10 Applicability of Standards).

The pharmacopeial definitions for sterile preparations for parenteral use may not apply to some biologics because of their special nature and licensing requirements (see Biologics &langle;1041&rangle;). However, some biological finished drug products containing “Injection” in the monograph title must meet the requirements of &langle;1&rangle; or indicated chapter subparts, where it is specified in the monograph.

"This chapter applies, in whole or in part, when referenced in a drug product monograph."

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析

USP &langle;1&rangle; 的「觸發器」角色：這是整個 USP 無菌製劑品質測試體系的「總目錄」。當個別藥品的 USP 專論（Monograph）引用 &langle;1&rangle; 時，就等於啟動了一整串的品質測試要求。

  • 章節 <1000 = 強制性（Mandatory）— 必須遵守
  • 章節 ≥1000 = 資訊性（Informational）— 除非專論明確引用，否則僅供參考

三層架構：

  • Universal Tests — 所有注射劑都必須做的基本測試
  • Specific Tests — 根據劑型需額外考慮的測試
  • Dosage Form-Specific Tests — 各劑型的完整測試清單（Universal + Specific 的組合）

比喻說明

駕照考試的分級制度：

把 USP &langle;1&rangle; 想像成交通部的「駕照考試總則」。不管你考的是汽車、機車還是大客車，都有一些「通用科目」（筆試、路考基本項目）— 這就是 Universal Tests。但如果你考大客車，還需要額外的特殊科目（倒車入庫、S 彎）— 這就是 Specific Tests。而每種車型的「完整考試清單」— 就是 Dosage Form-Specific Tests。

重點提示 — CDMO 實務

強制 vs 資訊的實務影響：在 CDMO 場景下，這個區分至關重要。當客戶說「我的產品需要符合 USP &langle;1&rangle;」，你的 QC 團隊必須執行所有 Universal Tests，並根據劑型決定 Specific Tests。這不是建議，是法規要求。

生物製劑的特殊性：原文特別提到生物製劑可能不完全適用 &langle;1&rangle; 的定義，但如果專論標題包含「Injection」，仍然必須遵守。CDMO 承接生物製劑客戶時，需要仔細確認專論的具體引用範圍。

練習思考

  1. 你的 CDMO 工廠目前生產哪些注射劑型？請對照 &langle;1&rangle; 的分類，確認你的產品屬於哪個類別（Solution / Lyophilized / Suspension 等）。
  2. 為什麼 USP 把章節分為 <1000（強制）和 ≥1000（資訊）？這種設計對 CDMO 的品質系統建構有什麼影響？
  3. 如果一個新客戶帶來一個「注射用凍乾微粒」產品，它同時涉及 &langle;1&rangle; 中的哪幾個劑型分類？
Universal Tests — 全品項通用測試

原文內容 Original Text

Universal tests are listed below and are applicable to parenteral dosage forms.

Identification

Identification tests are discussed in General Notices, 5.40. Identification should establish the identity of the drug or drugs present in the article and should discriminate between compounds of closely related structure that are likely to be present.

Assay

A specific and stability-indicating test should be used to determine the strength (content) of the drug product. In cases where the use of a nonspecific assay is justified, other supporting analytical procedures should be used to achieve overall specificity. A specific procedure should be used when there is evidence of excipient interference with the nonspecific assay.

Impurities

Tests for Impurities are discussed in General Notices, 5.60 Impurities and Foreign Substances. All articles should be tested to ensure that they meet the requirements.

Foreign and Particulate Matter

Articles intended for parenteral administration should be prepared in a manner designed to exclude particulate matter as defined in Subvisible Particulate Matter in Therapeutic Protein Injections &langle;787&rangle;, Particulate Matter in Injections &langle;788&rangle;, or Subvisible Particulate Matter in Intraocular Solutions &langle;789&rangle;, as well as excluding other foreign matter as appropriate for the dosage form.

Each final container of all parenteral preparations should be inspected to the extent possible for the presence of observable foreign and particulate matter (hereafter termed visible particulates) in its contents. The inspection process should be designed and qualified to ensure that every lot of all parenteral preparations is essentially free from visible particulates, as defined in Visible Particulates in Injections &langle;790&rangle;.

Every container in which the contents show evidence of visible particulates must be rejected.

Large-volume injections for single-dose infusion, small-volume injections, and pharmacy bulk packages (PBPs) are subject to the light obscuration or microscopic procedures and limits for subvisible particulate matter set forth in &langle;788&rangle;, unless otherwise specified.

Sterility

The sterility of all drug products intended for parenteral administration should be confirmed by the use of methods described in Sterility Tests &langle;71&rangle; or by an approved alternative method.

Bacterial Endotoxins

All articles intended for parenteral administration should be prepared in a manner designed to limit bacterial endotoxins as defined in Bacterial Endotoxins Test &langle;85&rangle; or Pyrogen Test &langle;151&rangle;.

Container Content

Container contents should be determined when appropriate (see Container Content for Injections &langle;697&rangle;).

Packaging Systems

The packaging system should not interact physically or chemically with the preparation to alter its strength, quality, or purity beyond the official or established requirements. The packaging system should meet the requirements in Elastomeric Components &langle;381&rangle;, Packaging and Storage Requirements &langle;659&rangle;, Containers — Glass &langle;660&rangle;, Plastic Packaging Systems &langle;661&rangle;, Plastic Materials of Construction &langle;661.1&rangle;, and Plastic Packaging Systems for Pharmaceutical Use &langle;661.2&rangle;.

Container–Closure Integrity

The packaging system should be closed or sealed in such a manner as to prevent contamination or loss of contents. Validation of container integrity must demonstrate no penetration of microbial contamination or gain or loss of any chemical or physical parameter deemed necessary to protect the product (see Package Integrity Evaluation &langle;1207&rangle;, &langle;1207.1&rangle;, &langle;1207.2&rangle;, and &langle;1207.3&rangle;).

Labeling

All articles intended for parenteral administration should meet the labeling requirements defined in Labeling &langle;7&rangle;.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

比喻說明

出國前的安全檢查清單：

Universal Tests 就像飛機起飛前的「標準檢查清單」。不管是波音 737 還是空中巴士 A380，都必須完成的基本項目：引擎正常（Assay）、機身辨識正確（Identification）、燃油沒有雜質（Impurities）、座艙密封（CCI）、乘客安全（Sterility）。少做任何一項，飛機就不能起飛 — 同理，少做任何一項 Universal Test，批次就不能放行。

重點提示 — 微粒物質的四層防線

&langle;1&rangle; 對微粒物質的要求特別嚴格，建立了四層檢測體系：

  • &langle;787&rangle; — 蛋白質注射劑的不可見微粒（Therapeutic Proteins 專用）
  • &langle;788&rangle; — 一般注射劑的不可見微粒（Light Obscuration / Microscopy）
  • &langle;789&rangle; — 眼內溶液的不可見微粒
  • &langle;790&rangle; — 可見微粒的目視檢查標準

CDMO 實務：你的 100% 目視檢查（Visual Inspection）流程必須經過確效（Qualification），而且「任何含可見微粒的容器必須被剔除」— 這是零容忍標準。

重點提示 — 容器密封完整性（CCI）

&langle;1207&rangle; 系列的重要性：CCI 是近年法規關注的焦點。原文要求驗證必須證明「無微生物穿透」且「無化學或物理參數的增減」。

這意味著你不能只做微生物挑戰測試 — 還需要證明容器不會有氣體洩漏（影響凍乾產品的真空度）或水分侵入（影響凍乾餅的穩定性）。

練習思考

  1. 在 CDMO 場景中，Universal Tests 的 9 大項目中，哪些是在充填線上「即時」執行的，哪些是送 QC 實驗室「離線」測試的？
  2. 原文要求 Assay 必須使用「specific and stability-indicating」方法。為什麼穩定性指示（Stability-indicating）這個要求如此重要？
  3. &langle;85&rangle; 和 &langle;151&rangle; 都能用於內毒素/熱原檢測，但實務上 CDMO 幾乎只用 &langle;85&rangle;。為什麼？

核心概念 — Universal Tests 的全貌

Universal Tests 就是你的 QC 實驗室對每一批注射劑產品都必須執行的「基本檢驗清單」。共有 9 大類：

測試項目觸發的 USP 章節核心目的
Identification（鑑別）General Notices 5.40確認藥品身分
Assay（含量測定）Stability-indicating確認含量/效價
Impurities（不純物）General Notices 5.60雜質控制

Sterility（無菌）&langle;71&rangle;確認無菌

Specific Tests — 劑型特定測試

原文內容 Original Text

In addition to the Universal Tests listed above, the following specific tests may be necessary depending on the dosage form.

Uniformity of Dosage Units

This test is applicable for parenteral drug products and dosage forms packaged in single-unit containers. It includes both the mass of the dosage form and the content of the active substance in the dosage form (see Uniformity of Dosage Units &langle;905&rangle;).

Vehicles and Added Substances

Aqueous vehicles: Aqueous vehicles must meet the requirements of &langle;151&rangle; or &langle;85&rangle;, whichever is specified in the monograph. Water for Injection is generally used as the vehicle. Sodium chloride or dextrose may be added to render the resulting solution isotonic.

Nonaqueous vehicles: Fixed oils are classified under nonaqueous vehicles. Fixed oils used as vehicles are of vegetable origin and are odorless. They must meet specific requirements:

  • Saponification Value: Between 185 and 200
  • Iodine Value: Between 79 and 141
  • Unsaponifiable Matter: NMT 1.5%
  • Acid Value: NMT 0.2
  • Peroxide Value: NMT 5.0
  • Water content (Method Ic): NMT 0.1%
  • Limits for Copper, Iron, Lead, and Nickel per &langle;232&rangle;/&langle;233&rangle;

Added substances: Suitable substances may be added to increase stability or usefulness unless proscribed in the monograph. No coloring agent may be added solely for the purpose of coloring the finished preparation. Special care should be observed for preparations exceeding 5 mL:

  • Mercury and cationic surface-active agents: NMT 0.01%
  • Chlorobutanol, cresol, phenol: NMT 0.5%
  • Sulfur dioxide or equivalent sulfite/bisulfite: NMT 0.2%

Antimicrobial Preservatives

Antimicrobial agents must be added to preparations intended for injection that are packaged in multiple-dose containers unless: (1) the monograph directs otherwise; (2) the substance contains a radionuclide with a physical half-life of less than 24 h; or (3) the active ingredients are themselves antimicrobial. Substances must meet the requirements of &langle;51&rangle; and &langle;341&rangle;.

Water Content

The water content of freeze-dried (lyophilized) products should be determined when appropriate (see &langle;921&rangle;).

Aluminum Content

See Labeling &langle;7&rangle; for information related to specific labeling requirements associated with aluminum content in LVIs, SVIs, and PBPs used in Parenteral Nutrition (PN) Therapy.

Completeness and Clarity of Solutions

The following tests are performed to demonstrate suitability of constituted solutions prepared before administration:

  • The solid dissolves completely, leaving no undissolved matter.
  • The constituted solution is not significantly less clear than an equal volume of the diluent or of purified water.
  • The constituted solution is free from particulate matter that can be observed on visual inspection (see &langle;790&rangle;).

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念 — Specific Tests 的「按需觸發」邏輯

Specific Tests 不像 Universal Tests 那樣「一律適用」。它們是根據產品特性「按需啟動」的附加測試。關鍵觸發條件：

  • 單劑量容器 → 需要做 Uniformity of Dosage Units &langle;905&rangle;
  • 多劑量容器 → 必須添加防腐劑並通過 &langle;51&rangle; 測試
  • 凍乾產品 → 需要測 Water Content &langle;921&rangle;
  • 非水性溶媒 → 固定油必須通過 7 項品質指標
  • 復溶產品 → 必須測 Completeness and Clarity

比喻說明

添加物的「5 mL 分水嶺」：

原文對超過 5 mL 的注射劑設定了更嚴格的添加物限量。為什麼是 5 mL？因為注射量越大，患者接觸到添加物的總量就越多。一個 0.5% 防腐劑在 1 mL 注射劑中只有 5 mg，但在 100 mL 大容量注射液中就是 500 mg — 毒性風險完全不同。

這就像藥物的劑量概念：少量的酒精是調味，大量就是毒。

重點提示 — 多劑量容器的防腐劑要求

「必須加」而非「可以加」：原文用的是 “must be added” — 多劑量注射劑必須含有抗微生物防腐劑。這是因為每次抽取都會引入潛在的微生物污染，防腐劑是防止使用中污染（In-Use Contamination）的最後一道防線。

三個例外：專論另有規定、放射性藥物（半衰期 <24h）、活性成分本身具有抗菌性。

CDMO 影響：如果客戶要求生產多劑量注射劑，你的配方開發團隊必須進行 &langle;51&rangle; 抗微生物效力試驗來確認防腐劑的有效性。

重點提示 — 「不得加色素」規則

USP &langle;1&rangle; 明確禁止「僅為了著色目的」在注射劑中添加色素。這個規則在 CDMO 場景中偶爾會引起爭議 — 某些客戶可能希望透過顏色來區分不同濃度的產品。但 USP 的立場很清楚：注射劑不需要也不應該靠顏色來辨識。

練習思考

  1. 你的 CDMO 是否生產多劑量注射劑？如果是，防腐劑效力測試（&langle;51&rangle;）是在什麼階段進行的？開發階段還是每批放行？
  2. 非水性注射劑（如使用固定油的肌肉注射劑）在 CDMO 場景中越來越常見嗎？這些產品的特殊品質要求對 QC 實驗室提出了哪些額外挑戰？
Container & Labeling Specifications — 容器與標籤規格

原文內容 Original Text

Packaging Systems

The packaging system should not interact physically or chemically with the preparation to alter its strength, quality, or purity. The packaging system should meet the requirements in:

  • Elastomeric Components &langle;381&rangle; — Rubber closures and plungers
  • Packaging and Storage Requirements &langle;659&rangle;
  • Containers — Glass &langle;660&rangle;
  • Plastic Packaging Systems &langle;661&rangle;
  • Plastic Materials of Construction &langle;661.1&rangle;
  • Plastic Packaging Systems for Pharmaceutical Use &langle;661.2&rangle;

Further information regarding packaging systems testing may be found in Assessment of Extractables &langle;1663&rangle; and Assessment of Drug Product Leachables &langle;1664&rangle;.

Container–Closure Integrity

Validation of container integrity must demonstrate no penetration of microbial contamination or gain or loss of any chemical or physical parameter deemed necessary to protect the product. See Package Integrity Evaluation &langle;1207&rangle;, &langle;1207.1&rangle;, &langle;1207.2&rangle;, and &langle;1207.3&rangle;.

Labeling

All articles intended for parenteral administration should meet the labeling requirements defined in Labeling &langle;7&rangle;.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念 — 包裝相容性的 USP 章節地圖

USP &langle;1&rangle; 觸發了一整套包裝測試的章節連鎖。這是 CDMO 經常遇到的複雜領域：

  • &langle;381&rangle; — 橡膠部件（瓶塞、注射器活塞）
  • &langle;660&rangle; — 玻璃容器（Type I borosilicate 等）
  • &langle;661&rangle;/&langle;661.1&rangle;/&langle;661.2&rangle; — 塑膠包裝系統與材料
  • &langle;1663&rangle;/&langle;1664&rangle; — 可溶出物（Extractables）和浸出物（Leachables）評估

關鍵原則：包裝不能改變藥品的 strength、quality 或 purity。這意味著你的容器密封系統需要經過完整的 E&L（Extractables & Leachables）研究。

重點提示 — CCI 是近年稽查焦點

FDA 與 EU Annex 1 的新要求：容器密封完整性（CCI）檢測技術在過去 10 年發生了重大變革。傳統的藍色染料浸入測試（Blue Dye Ingress Test）正被確定性方法取代：

  • 真空衰減（Vacuum Decay）
  • 高壓放電（High Voltage Leak Detection, HVLD）
  • 氦氣洩漏（Helium Leak Detection）
  • 雷射頭空間分析（Laser-based Headspace Analysis）

&langle;1207&rangle; 系列提供了選擇和驗證這些方法的框架。CDMO 在選擇 CCI 技術時，需要考慮產品形態（液體/凍乾）和容器類型（玻璃瓶/預填充注射器/安瓶）。

練習思考

  1. 你的 CDMO 使用哪種 CCI 測試方法？是否已從概率性方法（Probabilistic，如染料浸入）轉換到確定性方法（Deterministic，如真空衰減）？
  2. 如果客戶帶來一個預填充注射器產品，&langle;1&rangle; 要求你對哪些包裝組件進行相容性評估？

本節重點回顧 Section Summary

  • USP &langle;1&rangle; 是「觸發器」：當藥品專論引用 &langle;1&rangle;，就啟動了一整串的品質測試要求 — 這是每個 CDMO QC 實驗室的必做清單
  • 章節編號決定法律效力：<1000 = 強制（Mandatory），≥1000 = 資訊（Informational，除非專論明確引用）
  • 三層測試架構：Universal Tests（9 大類，全品項適用）→ Specific Tests（按劑型/包裝觸發）→ Dosage Form-Specific Tests（詳見 Section 1）
  • 微粒控制四層防線：&langle;787&rangle;（蛋白質）+ &langle;788&rangle;（一般注射劑）+ &langle;789&rangle;（眼科）+ &langle;790&rangle;（可見微粒）
  • 包裝系統章節連鎖：&langle;381&rangle; → &langle;659&rangle;–&langle;661.2&rangle; → &langle;1663&rangle;/&langle;1664&rangle; → &langle;1207&rangle; 系列
  • 多劑量容器：必須（must）添加防腐劑並通過 &langle;51&rangle; 測試，僅三個例外
  • 2024 年 12 月重大修訂生效：確認你的品質系統已更新至最新版本要求
