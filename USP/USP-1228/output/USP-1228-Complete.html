0.1 Introduction | 簡介

原文內容 Original Text

〈1228〉 DEPYROGENATION — INTRODUCTION

The production of parenteral products requires not only that products be sterile, but that they are also free from harmful levels of pyrogens. Depyrogenation is defined as the direct and validated destruction or removal of pyrogens. For the purposes of this and subsequent chapters of the 〈1228〉 series, the term "depyrogenation" refers to the destruction or removal of bacterial endotoxins, the most prevalent and quantifiable pyrogen in parenteral preparations. The chapters in this series discuss depyrogenation procedures that are applicable to product streams, equipment, and drug product containers and closures.

The 〈1228〉 series builds on the tenets described in Sterility Assurance 〈1211〉, first published in USP 20–NF 15. In the time since that publication, the science of depyrogenation has advanced, as has control over manufacturing processes. Manufacturers are very aware of the impact that facility, equipment, and process design has on endotoxin levels and depyrogenation, and they are mindful of the effects of elevated endotoxin levels in raw materials and water systems.

Risk management tools, including process evaluation tools such as Hazard Analysis Critical Control Point (HACCP), are helping companies to identify critical control points (CCP) for the control of endotoxin and other impurities or contaminants in the manufacturing process. As a result of these advances in science, technology, and philosophy, the 〈1228〉 series will also offer alternatives to historical or traditional thinking.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 — Depyrogenation (除熱原)

Pyrogen（熱原）：任何能引起人體發熱反應（pyrexia）的物質。在注射製劑中，最重要的熱原是革蘭氏陰性菌的外膜成分——細菌內毒素（Bacterial Endotoxin）。

Depyrogenation（除熱原）：USP 的定義非常精確——「直接且經驗證的熱原破壞或去除」。注意這裡有兩個關鍵詞：(1) 直接（direct）——指針對內毒素本身的措施，而非間接預防；(2) 驗證（validated）——必須有書面驗證證明有效。

〈1228〉 系列架構：本章是母章，後續子章包括 〈1228.1〉乾熱除熱原、〈1228.2〉過濾、〈1228.3〉液態製劑除熱原等，各自針對不同製程。

比喻說明 Analogy

想像注射製劑的生產就像製作靜脈點滴的生理食鹽水。即使你用最嚴格的方式過濾、滅菌，確保瓶內沒有任何活菌，但如果原料水曾經被革蘭氏陰性菌污染過，菌體死後留下的細胞壁碎片（內毒素）仍然存在——它們非常耐熱，普通高壓滅菌殺不死它們。病人輸液後可能立即發高燒，甚至休克。除熱原就是要把這些「死菌的毒素殘骸」清除乾淨。

重點提示 — 無菌 ≠ 無熱原

這是初學者最常犯的認知錯誤！無菌（Sterile）代表沒有活的微生物；無熱原（Pyrogen-free）代表沒有能引起發熱反應的物質。

一個製劑可以無菌但仍含有大量內毒素（因為死菌的細胞壁殘留）；反之，在某些特殊場景下也可能無內毒素但有其他熱原。這也是為什麼注射製劑必須同時符合兩個標準：Sterility Test〈71〉 AND Bacterial Endotoxins Test〈85〉。

法規視角 — HACCP 在製藥中的應用

HACCP（危害分析與關鍵控制點）原本是食品安全工具，現已被製藥業廣泛採用。USP〈1228〉明確提到用 HACCP 來識別製程中的 CCP（Critical Control Points，關鍵控制點）——例如原料水系統、設備清洗步驟、原料驗收等。這與 ICH Q9 品質風險管理（Quality Risk Management）的理念完全一致。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 一個注射製劑通過了無菌試驗（Sterility Test〈71〉），是否可以確保它對病人是安全的？請從熱原角度說明。
  2. USP 為什麼在 〈1228〉 的定義中強調「validated（驗證的）」這個詞？沒有驗證的除熱原步驟有什麼風險？
  3. 你的 CDMO 廠正在開發一個新的注射用抗體藥物，請舉出至少 3 個可能引入內毒素的「關鍵控制點（CCP）」。
0.2 Bacterial Endotoxin and Lipopolysaccharide | 細菌內毒素與脂多糖

原文內容 Original Text

BACTERIAL ENDOTOXIN AND LIPOPOLYSACCHARIDE

Bacterial endotoxin is a component of the outer cell membrane of Gram-negative bacteria. The natural endotoxin complex contains many cell wall components including phospholipids, lipoproteins, and lipopolysaccharide (LPS), which is the biologically active component of endotoxin. Purified endotoxin is chemically defined as a LPS. LPS consists of three distinct regions:

  1. The hydrophobic lipid A portion of the molecule is highly conserved among Gram-negative bacteria, and is largely responsible for most, if not all, of the biological activity of endotoxin.
  2. A core oligosaccharide links the lipid A to the hydrophilic O-specific side chain or O-antigen.
  3. The hydrophilic O-antigen is a highly variable region that confers serological specificity to the organism and is often used to distinguish strains of Gram-negative bacteria.

Due to the amphipathic nature of the LPS molecule [i.e., having both a polar (hydrophilic) end and a nonpolar (hydrophobic) end], purified LPS preparations, such as reference standard endotoxin (RSE) and control standard endotoxin (CSE) tend to form bilayers, micelles, ribbons, and other conformations when in solution, and they may adsorb, or "stick", to surfaces, making them difficult to extract and detect. The degree of adsorption of LPS to solid surfaces is affected by the composition and finish of the material to be depyrogenated. The extent of aggregation of LPS in solution is affected by a host of formulation matrix attributes to which it is exposed, such as temperature, pH, salt concentration, divalent cation concentration, detergents or emulsifiers, and chelating agents.

When parenteral products are contaminated with endotoxin, the contaminant is not purified LPS, but rather whole cells or cell wall fragments generated during the normal growth cycle of the bacteria or disruption of bacteria, where the LPS remains embedded in or associated with other cell wall components. Purified LPS and native endotoxin are dissimilar in many respects, and the two terms should not be used interchangeably.

Depending on the materials of construction or the formulation of the article to be depyrogenated, the use of native endotoxin as a challenge material in depyrogenation studies may be a consideration because a native endotoxin preparation better reflects operational reality, particularly for the depyrogenation of product streams, and because LPS molecules in natural endotoxin are embedded in cell wall complexes, they may be much less prone to the aggregation and adsorption issues seen with purified LPS.

"For the purposes of the 〈1228〉 series, the term 'challenge material' will be used to generically describe material (endotoxin or LPS) used as a spiking analyte for depyrogenation studies. 'Endotoxin' will refer to the moiety in its natural state, meaning pieces of Gram-negative cell wall from a well-characterized source. LPS will refer to the purified material."

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念 — LPS 的三區段結構

這是理解除熱原科學的基礎。LPS 由三個功能不同的區段組成：

  • Lipid A（脂質 A）：藏在分子深處的疏水性錨點。這是內毒素毒性的核心——幾乎所有革蘭氏陰性菌的 Lipid A 結構都高度相似（conserved），這也是 BET（鱟試劑法）能廣泛檢測各種內毒素的原因。
  • Core Oligosaccharide（核心寡糖）：連接 Lipid A 和外層 O-抗原的橋樑，結構相對保守。
  • O-Antigen（O 抗原）：外層的親水性多糖鏈，結構因菌株而異（variable）。它讓不同菌株有不同的「血清型（serotype）」，是細菌分類鑑定的依據。

比喻說明 — LPS 的兩親性

LPS 分子就像清潔劑（洗碗精）分子：一頭親水（O-抗原）、一頭親油（Lipid A）。這種兩親性（Amphipathic）使 LPS 在水溶液中會自動聚集，形成膠束（micelles）、雙層膜（bilayers）等各種結構——就像洗碗精在水中形成泡泡一樣。這種聚集行為使得 LPS 的有效濃度和生物活性難以預測，也讓除熱原驗證更複雜。

更麻煩的是，LPS 的疏水端（Lipid A）會「黏」在玻璃瓶、塑膠管路的表面——就像油脂沾在鍋底很難洗掉。這直接影響驗證研究中挑戰材料的回收率（recovery）。

關鍵術語比較 — Native Endotoxin vs. Purified LPS

特性比較：
┌─────────────────┬──────────────────┬──────────────────┐
│ 特性            │ Purified LPS     │ Native Endotoxin │
├─────────────────┼──────────────────┼──────────────────┤
│ 組成            │ 純化脂多糖       │ 細菌細胞壁碎片   │
│ 聚集/吸附問題   │ 嚴重（高）       │ 較少（低）       │
│ 反映現實狀況    │ 較差             │ 較好             │
│ 標準化程度      │ 高（RSE/CSE）    │ 低（批次差異大） │
│ 適用場景        │ 常規 BET 校正    │ 複雜基質驗證研究 │
└─────────────────┴──────────────────┴──────────────────┘

RSE = Reference Standard Endotoxin（參考標準內毒素）
CSE = Control Standard Endotoxin（控制標準內毒素）

重點提示 — 挑戰材料選擇對驗證結果的影響

USP 在這裡提出一個重要的科學觀點：對於複雜基質（如發酵液、含蛋白質的製劑）或特殊表面材料，使用純化 LPS 做驗證挑戰材料可能不能代表真實情況。

純化 LPS 在複雜環境下的聚集和吸附行為與天然內毒素截然不同，可能導致：
(1) 挑戰材料回收率偏低，讓除熱原看起來比實際更有效（假陰性風險）
(2) 結果重現性差，批次間差異大

因此，USP 允許使用 native endotoxin 作為挑戰材料，特別是在產品流（product stream）的除熱原驗證中。

實務應用 — CDMO 場景

在 CDMO 承接新客戶的除熱原驗證時，你必須問客戶：「你的製劑基質是什麼？pH？是否含有螯合劑（chelating agents）如 EDTA？是否含有界面活性劑（surfactants）？」——因為這些因素都會影響 LPS 的聚集狀態，進而影響 BET〈85〉的結果和除熱原驗證的設計。不同的製劑基質，可能需要不同的方法驗證（method suitability）。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 為什麼 Lipid A 的結構在各種革蘭氏陰性菌之間高度保守（conserved）？這個特性對 BET 試驗有什麼實際意義？
  2. 你的玻璃瓶除熱原驗證使用純化 LPS 做挑戰材料，結果顯示 4-log reduction。但你知道玻璃瓶表面對 LPS 有很強的吸附。請問這個 4-log reduction 的數字是否可信？可能偏高還是偏低？為什麼？
  3. 某個含有 EDTA 螯合劑的眼用注射製劑，在做 BET〈85〉時遇到干擾（interference）。請問可能的原因是什麼？

1.1 Measuring Endotoxin Pre- and Post-Processing | 製程前後的內毒素量測

原文內容 Original Text

MEASURING ENDOTOXIN PRE- AND POST-PROCESSING

The primary procedure used for the measurement of bacterial endotoxin is the Bacterial Endotoxins Test 〈85〉 (BET). A well-controlled BET assay can provide assurance of accurate readings for the calculation of the reduction in challenge material activity pre- and post-processing, as well as provide consistent quantitation of levels of native endotoxin in raw materials, at CCP in the manufacturing process, and in finished products.

There are many variables in study structure and test method that can affect the outcome of a depyrogenation study. Development of a test method depends on the material under test, the identification of an appropriate challenge preparation, and the method used to extract recoverable activity prior to processing and residual activity after processing. Once a test system is developed that includes the identification of a source for challenge material preparations, inoculation of the articles to be depyrogenated (including drying procedures), extraction or recovery methods, and appropriate BET test methodology and sensitivity, it is recommended that subsequent tests should use the same conditions to ensure the comparability of test results.

Points to consider when constructing a depyrogenation study include the following:

  1. The challenge material: Consider the source of the endotoxin (purified or natural). When using purified LPS, choose a preparation with no fillers, because the presence of these fillers can add to the variability and therefore decrease the accuracy of the assay. Once challenge material preparation is chosen, it is recommended to use material from the same source in subsequent studies to reduce variability.
  2. The characteristics of the material being depyrogenated: It is important to understand the characteristics of the material being tested. For example, LPS may adsorb to plastics, and although two objects may be made of the same plastic, surface finish, surface area, and conformation differences may affect extraction efficiency and LPS recovery. For solutions, formulation matrices may affect aggregation of purified LPS in that pH, salt concentration, chelating agents, surfactants, and the presence of divalent cations may all have an impact on the recovery of the challenge material. The use of natural endotoxin may mitigate some of these recovery issues.
  3. The level of activity needed for the study: How much pre-processing activity do you need to execute the study? The current industry standard is to add enough endotoxin to the system so that at least 1000 EU can be recovered prior to depyrogenation. However, depending on the test system, 1000 EU may be either excessive or insufficient.
  4. Preparation of test samples: The method used to affix challenge materials to the surface of materials to be depyrogenated may affect its removal or recovery. Air-drying is the most convenient method of affixing challenge material to hard surfaces, but freeze drying and vacuum drying also have been used.
  5. Recovery methods: Although there are standard methods for the recovery of endotoxin from medical devices, there is no standard method for the recovery of endotoxin dried onto solid surfaces that are used as indicators in depyrogenation studies. A laboratory may choose to develop and validate a method that better suits the material under test. Recoveries of less than 100% of the challenge material nominal spike in positive controls are not uncommon. Perhaps more important than the percent recovery in positive controls is consistent recovery across lots of the same material and across depyrogenation studies.
  6. Test method: Lysate formulations differ, and they may be subject to different interferences such as leachables, chelators, and salts. If interferences are encountered during method development that are difficult to overcome, consider trying another test method (e.g., gel clot, kinetic chromogenic, kinetic turbidimetric, endpoint chromogenic) or a different source of reagent.
  7. Depyrogenation method: Variability in the depyrogenation treatment may impact recovery. Treatments vary in efficiency, both within and between treatment types. For example, a large difference in efficiency will be seen when comparing the depyrogenation of glass vials using dry heat versus rinsing in water for injection (WFI), and differences may be seen in the reduction of challenge material by the filtration of a solution depending on the type of filter chosen.

The efficiency of a depyrogenation process has been historically measured in terms of a logarithmic (log) reduction of a large "spike" or bolus of purified endotoxin that is added to or dried onto a material prior to treatment. Although log reduction is a convenient measurement benchmark, the more relevant and pragmatic indicator of depyrogenation efficiency is one based on process capability and patient safety, which is the reduction of the measured or anticipated worst case natural levels of contaminating endotoxin to safe levels as defined by reference to calculated endotoxin limits for the material.

With the implementation of the principles of quality by design (QbD) and risk management, the 3-log reduction that was first introduced in 1984 may be inappropriate as a universal benchmark for modern depyrogenation processes. For example, materials with a high level of native endotoxin, such as fermentation broths, could require more than a 3-log reduction to reach a safe level, whereas dry heat depyrogenation of materials with normally low or undetected levels of native endotoxin, such as washed glass vials, will require substantially less than a 3-log reduction. The appropriate endotoxin log reduction for the process should be determined by the user based on a full understanding of the product and process capability including input sources, levels of endotoxin, efficiency of depyrogenation methods, and output (product- or process-specific) endotoxin requirements.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念 — BET〈85〉：除熱原研究的量測基礎

Bacterial Endotoxins Test（細菌內毒素試驗，BET）〈85〉 是目前主流的內毒素定量方法，基於鱟（Horseshoe Crab）的血液凝固反應。常見的四種 BET 方法：

  • Gel Clot（凝膠凝固法）：定性/半定量，最傳統，終點為膠體凝固
  • Kinetic Chromogenic（動態顯色法）：定量，利用顏色變化動態追蹤，精度高
  • Kinetic Turbidimetric（動態濁度法）：定量，利用濁度變化，適合某些基質
  • Endpoint Chromogenic（終點顯色法）：定量，在固定時間點讀取顏色

選擇哪種方法取決於製劑基質——如果基質顏色深、含有干擾物質，就需要換方法或做稀釋。

公式 — Log Reduction 的計算

Log Reduction（對數降低）計算：

Log Reduction = log₁₀(Pre-process activity)
                - log₁₀(Post-process activity)

例：
  Pre-process:  10,000 EU/mL
  Post-process:    10 EU/mL

  Log Reduction = log₁₀(10,000) - log₁₀(10)
                = 4 - 1
                = 3-log reduction ✓

USP 重要觀點：
  3-log reduction ≠ 萬能標準

  適當的 log reduction 目標應基於：
  1. 實際輸入端內毒素水平（input level）
  2. 製程能力（process capability）
  3. 成品的內毒素限度要求（output limit）

  公式：需要的 Log Reduction ≥
    log₁₀(Worst case input) - log₁₀(Endotoxin limit)

重點提示 — 3-Log Reduction 的歷史與現代觀

3-log reduction（1000 倍降低）是 1984 年引入的標準，當時科學理解有限。USP〈1228〉明確指出這個標準在 QbD 時代可能不再適用：

  • 情境 A — 發酵液（高內毒素）：初始污染 10⁶ EU/mL → 目標 <0.5 EU/mL → 需要 >6-log reduction，3-log 遠遠不夠！
  • 情境 B — 已清洗玻璃瓶（低內毒素）：初始 <0.1 EU/vial → 本來就很低，3-log reduction 要求反而過於嚴苛，難以驗證。

現代科學方法：先確認最壞情況輸入值 → 再推算需要多少倍降低 → 設定合理的 log reduction 目標。

比喻說明 — 1000 EU 挑戰量的選擇

USP 建議在除熱原驗證前至少能回收 1000 EU，這就像壓力測試（stress test）：你要讓系統在「最糟的狀況下」証明有效。

但 1000 EU 並不是神聖不可侵犯的數字。就像測試防彈玻璃——如果現實中子彈最大能量只有 300 焦耳，你不需要用 10,000 焦耳去測試。但如果你的產品在極端情況下可能遇到 5,000 EU，那 1000 EU 的挑戰量反而不夠。挑戰量應該根據「最壞情況下的實際污染水平」來設定，並有文件支撐。

實務應用 — 方法一致性是驗證的核心

USP 特別強調：一旦確定了挑戰材料來源、接種方式、回收方法和 BET 方法，後續研究都應該使用「相同條件」——這樣才能確保不同批次、不同時間點的結果具有可比性（comparability）。

在 CDMO 實務中，這意味著：(1) 挑戰材料批次要留存備份；(2) BET 試劑供應商不能隨意更換；(3) 每次換原料或製程調整後，需評估是否影響方法的一致性。這些都要有完整的 SOP 和變更管理（Change Control）。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 你正在驗證一個新型聚合物（polymer）容器的除熱原製程。你發現挑戰材料（純化 LPS）在這種材料表面的回收率只有 40%（即使在未除熱原的正控中也如此）。這個低回收率問題如何處理？是否需要達到 100% 回收才能驗證？
  2. 某工廠的乾熱隧道對玻璃瓶進行 3-log reduction 驗證，但你的新客戶要求以重組蛋白發酵液充填到這些瓶子中，且發酵液本身內毒素水平約為 5 × 10⁴ EU/mL。請問 3-log reduction 夠嗎？如何計算需要的最低降低倍數？（假設成品內毒素限度為 5 EU/mL）
  3. BET〈85〉試驗遇到干擾（inhibition/enhancement）時，USP 建議可嘗試更換 BET 方法類型。請解釋為什麼不同的 BET 方法對同一樣品可能有不同的干擾敏感性。
1.2 Control of Endotoxin in Parenteral Products | 注射製劑中的內毒素控制

原文內容 Original Text

CONTROL OF ENDOTOXIN IN PARENTERAL PRODUCTS

The best control of endotoxin levels in parenteral products is the control of Gram-negative bioburden in raw materials, equipment, process streams, and manufacturing environment and operators. Parenteral manufacturers may exercise three categories of control to keep endotoxin content in drug products at safe levels.

The first category is "indirect control", which is comprised of a series of preventive measures that control bioburden, the potential endotoxin contribution by formulation components (e.g., raw materials, APIs, excipients), water, primary packaging components, equipment, and the manufacturing environment, including personnel.

The second category is "process control", in which endotoxin is monitored at CCP during processing to ensure that there is no increase in endotoxin. These process control elements are subject to validation or qualification.

The third category is "direct control", or the direct destruction or removal of endotoxins from product streams, equipment, and primary packaging materials. As with controls on processing, direct measures of endotoxin destruction or removal must be validated.

INDIRECT CONTROL

Reducing opportunities for Gram-negative microbial proliferation at any stage of manufacturing will reduce the likelihood of endotoxin contamination in the following ways:

  • Exercising control over the endotoxin content of incoming materials, particularly materials derived from natural sources or those with high water activity, will reduce the opportunity for Gram-negative microbial proliferation and therefore reduce or eliminate the need for endotoxin removal downstream. Because of their manufacturing processes, glass and plastic containers as well as elastomeric closures are often received with very low or undetectable levels of endotoxin. Qualification of primary packaging suppliers should include an audit that examines and confirms the supplier's consistent and documented control over applicable manufacturing processes.
  • Bioburden and endotoxin control should be a component of a vendor audit and supplier qualification program for formulation materials that could potentially contribute endotoxin to parenteral products.
  • Water is the most ubiquitous raw material in the manufacturing of parenteral products, but unless the generation and distribution of high-quality water is properly validated and controlled, the system will be prone to contamination by Gram-negative bacteria and the establishment of biofilms that can contribute significantly to the endotoxin load of the product (see Water for Pharmaceutical Purposes 〈1231〉).

PROCESS CONTROL

Product-specific process control requires the identification of CCP for the introduction or removal of endotoxin. Process control requires good process and equipment design consistent with QbD using a risk management tool such as HACCP. Process control measures include but may not be limited to the following:

  • Control of manufacturing practices is essential to endotoxin control. Endotoxin control should be a part of validated cleaning procedures. The use of product contact materials for which endotoxin control cannot be established should be avoided. Clean product-contact equipment should be stored dry to avoid bioburden and Gram-negative bacterial proliferation.
  • Hold times during manufacturing, particularly for nonsterile bulk in-process materials and drug product, should be validated to ensure that the hold conditions do not support microbial proliferation and therefore potential endotoxin production by Gram-negative bacteria.
  • Environmental control, including good manufacturing practices (aseptic, as appropriate), is essential to process management of endotoxin. Operators should be properly garbed and trained. Housekeeping and disinfecting practices should be established to reduce the possibility of microbial proliferation in critical areas. Cleaning regimens should emphasize that standing water be removed at the end of the cleaning process.
  • Endotoxin control and monitoring will be covered in a forthcoming chapter.

DIRECT CONTROL

These processes require validation to ensure that endotoxins are removed or reduced to safe levels. Direct control may be accomplished by a variety of methods and processes that may be combined to ensure endotoxin reduction to a safe level. The most commonly used depyrogenation processes and the associated control measures are the subject of the 〈1228〉 series.

比喻說明 — 三層次控制的餐廳比喻

把注射劑生產想像成一家高級餐廳的廚房：

  • 間接控制= 嚴格篩選食材供應商、確保食材新鮮（控制進貨品質）——不讓污染物進門
  • 製程控制= 廚房保持清潔、廚師洗手、食材在冷藏環境中保存、不讓食物在室溫放太久（監控製程中每個環節）——防止污染在廚房內滋生
  • 直接控制= 上菜前高溫殺菌或使用除菌過濾器（在關鍵步驟主動去除）——最後防線

一家好廚房不會只依賴最後一步，而是三層都要做好。同樣，一個好的注射劑生產系統也應該三層俱全。

重點提示 — 水系統是內毒素的頭號風險

USP 在 Indirect Control 中特別點名「水」是注射製劑最普遍的原料，也是最大的內毒素風險來源。原因：

  • 水無處不在——用於配製、清洗設備、製備注射用水（WFI）
  • 革蘭氏陰性菌（如 Pseudomonas）在水管系統中極易滋生，形成生物膜（Biofilm）
  • 生物膜一旦形成，極難徹底清除，會持續釋放內毒素
  • WFI 系統需要定期消毒，並有完善的監控計畫

法規要求：USP〈1231〉《水的藥用》提供了詳細指導。EU GMP Annex 1 也要求對水系統進行嚴格的驗證和監控。

重點提示 — 設備存放「乾燥」的關鍵性

Process Control 中一個容易被忽視的要點：已清洗的設備必須存放於乾燥狀態（stored dry）。

原因：任何殘留水分都可能成為革蘭氏陰性菌的「孳生地」。即使清洗後設備看起來很乾淨，只要有水的存在，幾個小時內就可能有細菌增殖，產生新的內毒素污染。這與後續滅菌是兩回事——滅菌可以殺死細菌，但無法去除已產生的內毒素！

實務應用 — CDMO 供應商管理

對於 CDMO 來說，Indirect Control 中的「供應商資格確認（Supplier Qualification）」是一個高 ROI 的投資：一旦確認供應商的玻璃瓶或橡皮塞在出廠時內毒素水平極低（如 <0.1 EU/piece），你的乾熱隧道設定可以更合理，不需要超過設計能力。

反過來說，如果供應商控制不穩定，你可能會面臨批次失敗、返工或廢棄的高成本。定期稽核供應商的 endotoxin control 記錄是保護 CDMO 商業利益的核心措施。

本節重點回顧 Section Summary

  • BET〈85〉是除熱原研究的量測基礎，但有七大影響因素（挑戰材料、材料特性、活性水平、樣品製備、回收方法、試驗方法、除熱原方法），每個因素都可能影響結果的準確性和重現性。
  • 3-Log Reduction 不是萬能標準：現代 QbD 思維要求根據實際輸入/輸出內毒素水平科學計算所需的降低倍數，高內毒素來源可能需要 >3-log，低污染材料可能不需要 3-log。
  • 三層次控制：Indirect（間接）→ Process（製程）→ Direct（直接），優先從源頭預防，最後才依賴主動去除（Direct Control）。
  • 水系統管理是 Indirect Control 的最高優先項目，生物膜（Biofilm）一旦形成，對整個生產系統的內毒素控制構成持續威脅。
  • 所有 Process Control 和 Direct Control 措施均需驗證（Validation）或資格確認（Qualification），文件記錄是 GMP 合規的核心要求。

2.1 Selection of an Appropriate Depyrogenation Method | 適當除熱原方法的選擇

原文內容 Original Text

SELECTION OF AN APPROPRIATE DEPYROGENATION METHOD

The basic principles for the control and validation of a depyrogenation process using a life cycle approach include:

  • Assessment of manufacturing processes to identify materials and process components that are essential to the control of endotoxin
  • Focused depyrogenation process development that is consistent with the material to be depyrogenated, the resident endotoxin level of the material to be depyrogenated, and the limit of endotoxin for the finished article
  • Adequate validation studies
  • Ongoing monitoring of process controls to ensure continued efficacy of the depyrogenation process
  • Identifying and documenting changes (a change control program) to the depyrogenation processes over time

Known or anticipated levels of endotoxin can be determined so that appropriate indirect or direct measures of control, consistent with the product or materials of construction, will ensure that endotoxin is eliminated or reduced to levels that ensure product and patient safety.

Should direct methods of depyrogenation be required for the control of endotoxin in or on the article, an important consideration during manufacturing process development is the selection of an appropriate method from the possible alternatives: dry heat, chemical, filtration, or physical removal. In some instances this selection is limited by the potential effects of the depyrogenation treatment may have on the materials themselves. The choice of the appropriate process for a given item requires knowledge of depyrogenation techniques and information concerning effects of the process on the material being processed. The selection of a particular treatment (and the details of its execution) often represents a compromise between those conditions required to destroy the endotoxin or remove it to the desired level and the effect of the process on the materials.

"Depyrogenation processes should be no more aggressive than required for effective process control to avoid adverse consequences to material quality attributes."

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念 — 除熱原方法的生命週期（Life Cycle Approach）

USP 明確採用「生命週期方法」來管理除熱原製程，這與 ICH Q8/Q9/Q10 的理念完全一致，包含四個連續階段：

  • 1. 製程評估（Assessment）：識別哪些材料和步驟對內毒素控制至關重要
  • 2. 方法開發（Development）：依據材料特性、居住內毒素水平（resident endotoxin level）和成品限度，選擇並優化除熱原方法
  • 3. 驗證（Validation）：正式的書面驗證，確認方法在規定參數內能持續有效
  • 4. 持續監控（Ongoing Monitoring）+ 變更管控（Change Control）：確保驗證狀態不因時間或細微改變而失效

比喻說明 — 「折衷（Compromise）」的概念

選擇除熱原方法就像選擇清潔衣物的方式：

  • 棉質內衣 → 可以用沸水煮（最有效，等同乾熱除熱原對玻璃）
  • 羊毛毛衣 → 只能冷水手洗（熱敏感，等同蛋白質製劑只能過濾除熱原）
  • 羽絨外套 → 需要專業乾洗（特殊材料需要特殊方法）

關鍵是：除熱原條件不能只考慮「殺死內毒素」的效果，也要考慮材料能不能承受。正如 USP 說的：「不要比有效控制所需的更激進（no more aggressive than required）」。

重點提示 — 方法選擇是一個科學決策，需要文件支撐

USP 要求除熱原方法的選擇必須基於：(1) 對材料特性的了解，(2) 對各種除熱原技術的掌握。這個選擇過程本身需要文件記錄和科學正當化（scientific justification）。

在法規稽核（inspection）中，稽查員可能會問：「為什麼選擇這種方法而不是另一種？」你需要能提供有根據的答案，而不只是「因為行業慣例」或「因為我們以前都這樣做」。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 你的客戶要生產含有重組蛋白（recombinant protein）的注射製劑，API 溶液本身可能含有痕量內毒素。為什麼乾熱除熱原不適用？你會推薦哪種方法？需要什麼樣的驗證？
  2. 對於橡皮塞（elastomeric closures），既不能用乾熱高溫，化學法又有殘留問題。在現實生產中，橡皮塞通常如何進行內毒素控制？（提示：結合 Indirect Control 思考）
2.2 Validation of a Depyrogenation Method | 除熱原方法驗證

原文內容 Original Text

VALIDATION OF A DEPYROGENATION METHOD

The validation program comprises several formally documented stages to establish that the depyrogenation process is capable of operating within prescribed parameters for process equipment, that independent measurements of critical parameters are possible and accurate, and that acceptance criteria for challenge material removal or destruction are met.

  • The development stage investigates and establishes the operating parameters that define the controls to be used for the depyrogenation process.
  • The installation qualification (IQ) stage establishes that equipment controls and other instrumentation needed to execute depyrogenation processes and measure the results of the depyrogenation process are properly designed and calibrated. Documentation should be available to demonstrate the acceptability of any required utilities such as steam, water, and air.
  • The operational qualification (OQ) stage confirms that the equipment and other processes components function within the defined depyrogenation parameters.
  • The performance qualification (PQ) stage of the validation program directly evaluates the depyrogenation of materials or articles. Wherever possible, these studies should employ or simulate the actual conditions of use, including the use of real or simulated product material. Worst case conditions, for example, might include the bracketing of critical parameters such as time/temperature and belt speed for dry heat depyrogenation of glass vials, flow rate for depyrogenation by filtration of solutions, and maximum measured, anticipated, or defined endotoxin loads for any material. "Worst case" should be defined and justified in the validation protocol. Endotoxin indicators may be utilized to support physical measurements in the validation of the depyrogenation process.

Although the "rule of three" suggests that three consecutive successful validation runs be executed, perform sufficient replicate studies to demonstrate the capability and efficiency of the depyrogenation process, including the validation of operational ranges of any equipment used in such processes. The number of replicate studies chosen should be scientifically based and justified. At the end of the PQ, a report is written to establish operating parameters.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念 — 驗證的四個正式階段

USP 使用標準的 IQ/OQ/PQ 框架來驗證除熱原製程，這與 EU GMP Annex 15（Qualification and Validation）完全一致：

  • Development（開發）：非正式的探索性工作——找出操作參數範圍、了解關鍵變數。這是科學工作，不一定需要 GMP 環境，但要有記錄。
  • IQ（安裝確認）：確認設備按設計規格安裝——溫度感測器在正確位置、校正有效、公用系統（蒸汽、水、空氣）符合要求。
  • OQ（操作確認）：確認設備在預定參數範圍內正常運作——例如乾熱隧道在設定溫度下能維持均一的溫度分布（temperature uniformity）。
  • PQ（性能確認）：最關鍵的一步——在實際使用條件下，用挑戰材料（帶有已知量內毒素的樣品）直接測試除熱原效果。

PQ 最壞情況設定範例 — 乾熱隧道玻璃瓶除熱原

最壞情況參數（Worst Case Parameters for Dry Heat Tunnel）：

時間/溫度（Time/Temperature Bracketing）：
  ✓ 最低溫度（Lower Temp Limit）：設定 250°C 下限
  ✓ 最短時間（Minimum Exposure Time）：例如 30 分鐘
  → 在這些最低條件下仍能達到驗證目標

輸送帶速度（Belt Speed）：
  ✓ 最高速度（Maximum Belt Speed）
  → 代表最短暴露時間，最難達到除熱原目標

最大內毒素負荷（Maximum Endotoxin Load）：
  ✓ 基於供應商歷史數據設定最壞情況污染量
  ✓ 加上安全係數

驗收標準（Acceptance Criteria）：
  ≥ 目標 Log Reduction
  + 0 不合格（0/3 fails）
  + 所有物理量測在規格內（溫度記錄、Belt speed）

重點提示 — Rule of Three 的正確理解

「三次連續成功驗證（Rule of Three）」是行業慣例，但 USP 明確指出這不是固定規則。

USP 的立場：「執行足夠的重複研究以證明製程能力和效率，次數應有科學依據和正當化。」

這意味著：

  • 對於高風險或高複雜度的製程，可能需要超過 3 次
  • 如果製程有明確的統計基礎（如設備均一性已充分表徵），3 次可能已足夠
  • 重要的是科學論據，而不是機械地遵從「3」這個數字

在稽核中，能夠解釋「為什麼選擇 N 次」比機械地說「因為法規要求 3 次」更有說服力。

比喻說明 — 驗證就像取得駕照

除熱原驗證就像考駕照的過程：

  • Development = 學駕駛：摸索車的特性、了解自己的能力邊界
  • IQ = 驗車：確認車輛各部件（方向盤、煞車、燈光）均已正確安裝和校正
  • OQ = 場地考試：在受控環境下測試基本操作能力（倒車、停車、轉彎）
  • PQ = 路考：在實際道路（最壞情況）下，測試你在真實情境中能否安全駕駛

每一步都不可跳過，因為通過前一步並不等同於能通過後一步。

2.3 Routine Process Control | 常規製程控制

原文內容 Original Text

ROUTINE PROCESS CONTROL

Once a depyrogenation process has been validated it must be maintained in that state to ensure the continued acceptability of its operation. This is accomplished through a number of related practices essential for continued use of the process.

  • Physical measurements: Data reported by the equipment sensors and recorders must be verified after the completion of each depyrogenation cycle.
  • Calibration: Any equipment used in the control or quantitative assessment of parameters required for a depyrogenation process must have its measurement accuracy verified against a traceable standard on a periodic basis.
  • Preventive maintenance: There should be a defined maintenance schedule for each piece of process or testing equipment required for depyrogenation that is consistent with the manufacturer's written recommendation.
  • Ongoing process control verification: Depending on the specifics of the particular depyrogenation process, there may be additional requirements for ongoing confirmation of process efficacy. These can include the testing of raw materials, water supplies, and in-process sampling. These performance parameters are monitored against assigned limits designed to ensure that finished products meet acceptable endotoxin levels. Monitoring of operating parameters and controls plays an important role in maintaining the depyrogenation process in a validated state.
  • Periodic reassessment: It is expected that the effectiveness of depyrogenation processes be reconfirmed on a periodic basis. A reassessment schedule should be formalized to assess the potential impact of de minimis or undetected changes to maintain the process in a validated state.
  • Change control: In order to remain in a validated state, the various material, procedure, and equipment elements impacting the depyrogenation process should be carefully monitored to ensure that changes are properly evaluated for their potential impact on the process. The scope of the change control program must include materials being processed, process equipment, processing parameters, and process holding time limits. The extent of the effort required to support a change will vary with the potential impact of the change on the process outcome.

TRAINING

Depyrogenation processes rely heavily on scientific principles for the effective destruction or removal of endotoxins. Scientists and engineers well-grounded in the principles of endotoxin removal and testing develop processes to ensure effective depyrogenation. Individuals involved in the development of depyrogenation processes require a background in microbiology, physics, chemistry, and engineering, and they must be familiar with good manufacturing principles and regulations. Depyrogenation is an interdisciplinary activity where the combined knowledge of a group of individuals is generally required for the establishment of a reliable process.

In addition to the depyrogenation process development team, individuals responsible for the maintenance and operation of depyrogenation processes must also be trained appropriately to ensure that their actions contribute to success. The operators are often the first to identify changes in process performance because of their intimate involvement with it. Effective training programs should be established and documented. Training programs should emphasize depyrogenation principles, adherence to established processes and procedures, and the importance of documenting deviations from normal operations.

ROUTINE TESTING

Testing is not a control mechanism but rather a tool to assess the effectiveness of control measures. Depending on the specifics of the particular depyrogenation process, there may be additional requirements for ongoing confirmation of process efficacy.

These requirements can include the testing of raw materials, water supplies, and in-process sampling. These performance parameters are monitored against assigned limits based on historical data, and are designed to ensure that finished products meet acceptable endotoxin levels. Monitoring of operating parameters and controls plays an important role in maintaining the depyrogenation process in a validated state.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念 — 維持驗證狀態（Maintaining Validated State）

驗證不是一次性的活動——它是一個需要持續維護的「狀態」。一旦驗證完成，就需要通過六個系統性措施來確保這個狀態不被破壞：

  1. 物理量測驗證（Physical Measurements）
  2. 校正（Calibration）
  3. 預防性維護（Preventive Maintenance）
  4. 持續製程控制驗證（Ongoing Process Control Verification）
  5. 定期再評估（Periodic Reassessment）
  6. 變更管控（Change Control）

這六項措施缺一不可，共同構成除熱原製程的「保護罩」。

重點提示 — 「測試不是控制機制」的監管哲學

這是 USP〈1228〉最重要的哲學聲明之一，也是現代 GMP 的核心思想：

傳統思維（錯誤）：「只要最終產品測試通過，就代表安全。」

現代監管思維（正確）：「測試只是評估控制措施有效性的工具，無法替代真正的製程控制。」

為什麼？因為測試（尤其是破壞性測試或抽樣測試）有統計局限性——你無法對每一個成品都做測試。真正的保護來自於整個製程的設計、控制和驗證。這與 ICH Q10 藥品品質系統的理念完全一致。

法規對應：FDA 21 CFR Part 211 和 EU GMP 都強調「Quality cannot be tested into a product; it must be built in.」

六項常規製程控制措施詳解

六項維持驗證狀態的措施：

1. 物理量測（Physical Measurements）
   → 每次除熱原週期完成後驗證感測器數據
   → 保留記錄作為 batch record

2. 校正（Calibration）
   → 定期校正溫度感測器、流量計等
   → 需 traceable standard（可追溯標準）
   → 超出校正期限 = 製程不在驗證狀態

3. 預防性維護（Preventive Maintenance, PM）
   → 依設備製造商建議制定 PM 排程
   → PM 記錄是 GMP 稽核的重點文件

4. 持續製程控制驗證（Ongoing Verification）
   → 原料、水系統、中間品內毒素監控
   → 數據對照歷史設定限度

5. 定期再評估（Periodic Reassessment）
   → 正式的年度/定期回顧
   → 評估微小或未察覺的累積變化（de minimis changes）

6. 變更管控（Change Control）
   → 任何材料、設備、參數、保持時間的變更
   → 必須評估對除熱原製程的潛在影響
   → 重大變更可能需要重新驗證（Revalidation）

比喻說明 — 維持驗證狀態就像保養汽車

通過 PQ 驗證就像汽車出廠「通過品管測試」。但出廠後你需要持續：

  • 每次開車後檢查儀表板（物理量測）
  • 定期送廠保養（預防性維護）
  • 定期更換機油/煞車油（校正/更換耗材）
  • 記錄每次修理（變更管控）
  • 每年做年度保養檢查（定期再評估）

如果你跳過任何一項，車子（製程）就可能在某天「出問題」——而那時你手上沒有足夠的記錄來說明發生了什麼。

重點提示 — 操作員是製程變化的第一偵測者

USP 特別提到：「操作員往往是第一個發現製程性能變化的人，因為他們直接參與其中。」

這強調了訓練的雙重重要性：

  • 技術訓練：了解除熱原原理，知道為什麼要遵守程序
  • 偏差報告文化：鼓勵操作員記錄和報告任何異常，即使是微小的觀察

在 CDMO 環境中，這尤其重要——客戶產品可能非常多樣化，操作員需要了解不同製程的不同關鍵點，以便及時發現異常。

實務應用 — Change Control 的觸發點識別

在實際工作中，哪些變更必須觸發除熱原製程的 Change Control 評估？

  • 乾熱隧道更換加熱元件或更換傳送帶材質
  • WFI 水系統更換管路材質或增加新的分支
  • 玻璃瓶供應商改變（即使規格相同，清洗工藝可能不同）
  • NaOH 濃度或接觸時間的調整（化學除熱原法）
  • 過濾除熱原的濾膜品牌或批次供應商變更
  • 保持時間延長（hold time extension）

每一個變更都需要問：「這是否影響我的除熱原製程能力（process capability）？」如果影響，必須有正式評估和可能的重新驗證。

本節重點回顧 Section Summary

  • 生命週期方法（Life Cycle Approach）：除熱原管理不是一次性驗證，而是從方法開發、驗證、到持續監控和變更管控的完整生命週期。
  • 驗證四階段（Development → IQ → OQ → PQ）：每個階段有明確目的，PQ 必須在最壞情況（Worst Case）下進行，並以文件正當化挑戰條件和驗收標準。
  • 「Rule of Three」只是起點，不是終點：重複次數應由科學依據決定，並需正當化。驗證失敗不能忽視，必須調查根本原因。
  • 六項常規製程控制措施是維持驗證狀態的核心——物理量測、校正、預防性維護、持續製程驗證、定期再評估、變更管控缺一不可。
  • 「測試不是控制機制」：常規測試（Routine Testing）是評估工具，真正的保護來自設計、驗證和持續控制的整體系統。操作員是製程異常的第一偵測者，訓練和偏差報告文化至關重要。
