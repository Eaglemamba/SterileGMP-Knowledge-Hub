Section 1 — Introduction 導論：密封品質測試 vs. 洩漏測試

原文內容 Original Text

"Package seal quality tests" are checks used to characterize and monitor the quality and consistency of a parameter related to the package seal, providing some assurance of the package's ability to maintain integrity. Seal quality tests ensure that seal attributes, package materials, package components, and/or the assembly process are consistently kept within established limits, thus further supporting package integrity.

Seal quality tests differ from leak tests in that they provide no information relative to actual package integrity; thus, a package that meets the requirements of a seal quality test may still be defective and leak. For example, a flexible pouch package that passes a seal strength test may leak through a puncture in the pouch face. A bottle that meets closure application and removal torque tests may have a scratch on the bottle finish surface that allows product leakage.

In contrast, a pouch or bottle that is poorly assembled could pass leak tests at the time of product manufacture, yet develop leaks later, before reaching the end user.

Therefore, seal quality tests and leak tests work together to ensure package integrity.

Unlike package leak tests, seal quality tests are qualified for use rather than being fully validated. Qualification includes a demonstration of instrument performance and in some cases proof of appropriate instrument set-up specific for the package to be tested.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析：密封品質測試的本質

密封品質測試是「製程一致性的代理指標」，而非直接的完整性証明。邏輯鏈：

  • 密封品質一致 → 製程可控 → 產品-包裝系統大概率完整
  • 但「密封品質好」≠「無洩漏」（例如：瓶身穿刺缺陷不在密封區域）
  • 「密封品質差」也可能通過洩漏測試（早期剛剛密封，洩漏尚未明顯）

因此，兩類測試在時間和空間上互補，缺一不可。

重點提示：資格確認 vs. 完整驗証

USP 對密封品質測試的要求是「資格確認 (Qualification)」，而非洩漏測試的「完整驗証 (Full Validation)」。

  • 資格確認：証明儀器工作正常、對該包裝系統設定適當 → 相對輕量
  • 完整驗証：証明方法能可靠偵測到 MALL 附近的洩漏 → 需要大量陽性/陰性對照品數據

這一區別對文件要求有直接影響——密封品質測試的 SOP 不需要達到 USP〈1225〉方法驗証的完整要求。

比喻說明 Analogy

密封品質測試就像汽車生產線上的「扭力扳手」——確保每顆螺絲都按規定扭矩鎖緊。扭矩正確通常意味著連接穩固，但不能保証螺紋本身沒有缺陷。真正的完整性確認（洩漏測試）就像事後用水壓測試整個系統。兩者結合才能確保品質。

Sections 2 & 3 — Closure Torque & Package Burst 閉合扭矩與包裝爆破

2. Closure Application and Removal Torque

The closure application torque test measures the force exerted during the application of a screw-thread cap onto a threaded container. The closure removal torque test measures the force required to initiate screw-cap removal.

Application torque is kept within an optimum range to prevent leakage from loose caps and to preclude component distortion and compromised seals from over-torqued caps.

Removal torque is less than application torque due to stress relaxation and closure "back off" that may occur as a function of time and other environmental variables. Caps that back off excessively during shipping, storage, or distribution increase the risk of product leakage.

For multiple-dose packages, cap design and application forces should be such that the end-user population may be able to open and reclose the package.

Reproducibility is improved when tests are performed using automatic instrumentation that applies and removes caps at uniform speeds. Referenced standards: ASTM D2063, D3198, D3469, D3470, D3472, D3810, D3968.

3. Package Burst

The package burst test involves inserting a pressure source into a test package and applying pressure until the package seal(s) burst open. Applicable to flexible bags and pouches formed by heat-sealing processes.

The burst strength result (in pressure units) provides an indication of relative seal strength and evaluates the most likely location and mode of package failure. However, this test cannot provide a measure of package seal uniformity or overall package integrity.

ASTM F2054 (with restraining plate): stress uniformly applied to all seal areas → identifies weakest point. ASTM F1140 (without restraining plate): simulates real-world pressure events (sterilization cycle, distribution); highest stress at package middle, may not find weakest seal.

重點提示：爆破測試無法証明完整性

USP 明確指出：爆破測試無法提供密封均勻性或整體包裝完整性的資訊。一個能承受高爆破壓力的包裝，仍可能在密封區域之外（如包裝面破損）洩漏。爆破測試主要用於：

  • 確認密封製程的最低強度要求
  • 比較不同密封參數下的密封強度
  • 模擬運輸或滅菌過程中的壓差挑戰

比喻說明 Analogy

包裝爆破測試就像測試氣球能充多少氣才爆——知道了「爆破壓力」，但不知道氣球表面是否有微小針孔。高爆破壓力說明密封接頭夠牢固，但針孔在任何壓力下都會慢慢洩氣。這就是為什麼爆破測試必須與洩漏測試搭配使用。

Sections 4 & 5 — Package Seal Strength & Residual Seal Force 包裝密封強度與殘余密封力

4. Package Seal Strength (Peel Test)

The package seal strength test (peel test) measures the force required to peel apart two bonded surfaces. Applicable for: flexible pouch/bag seals (flexible-to-flexible), or flexible-to-rigid material seals (e.g., lidded tray).

Force results provide a measure of seal strength between bonded surfaces. Maximum seal force, as well as average force to open the seal, may be determined. Results are also useful for monitoring the consistency of package assembly.

The test is performed using a universal stress-strain instrument in extension force mode. The seal strength test is described in ASTM F88. Test results are affected by:

  • Peel test fixture design
  • Peel angle
  • Pull direction
  • Pull speed
  • Properties of the test sample itself

5. Residual Seal Force (RSF)

The residual seal force (RSF) test provides an indirect measure of the compressive force exerted by an elastomeric closure onto a parenteral vial finish after package assembly (capping). RSF tests can be performed on glass or plastic vial packages of all dimensions.

RSF is linearly related to closure compression: more tightly capped vials yield higher RSF values. A consistent and sufficiently significant RSF value provides a useful indicator of capping process consistency.

The RSF test is performed using a universal stress-strain instrument in compression force mode, with a metal tool called a cap anvil. A constant rate of compressive strain is exerted onto the anvil. The point in the stress-response curve immediately before the terminal slope change corresponds to the RSF value (in newtons or pound-force units).

RSF tests are nondestructive in terms of package integrity. Test results are influenced by:

  • Cap anvil tooling design
  • Test compression rate
  • Closure viscoelastic properties
  • Closure dimensions
  • Aluminum seal plastic top (remove for more reproducible results)

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析：剝離測試的兩個數值

剝離測試提供兩個有用的數值：

  • 最大密封力 (Maximum Seal Force)：密封接頭開始破裂時的峰值力 → 反映密封強度
  • 平均開封力 (Average Peel Force)：整個剝離過程的平均力 → 反映密封均勻性

一致的平均剝離力分布（沒有突然下降）表明密封均勻，製程穩定。突然下降可能提示局部通道缺陷 (channel defect)。

核心概念解析：RSF 的物理意義

RSF 衡量的是膠塞被壓入瓶口後，膠塞對瓶口施加的殘余壓縮力。這個力是維持密封的來源：

  • RSF 太低 → 膠塞對瓶口施力不足 → 密封力下降 → 洩漏風險增加
  • RSF 太高 → 可能膠塞過度壓縮 → 長期材料疲勞 → 密封失效

RSF 是軋蓋製程的直接監控指標，與軋蓋力 (capping force) 設定密切相關，常用於製程驗証中的過程能力分析 (Cpk)。

重點提示：RSF 的監管應用

RSF 在無菌小瓶灌裝的法規文件中具有重要地位：

  • 軋蓋製程驗証：需建立 RSF 的合格範圍（通常以 N 或 lbf 表示）
  • 軋蓋機 IQ/OQ/PQ：RSF 作為關鍵輸出參數被監控
  • 日常製造監控：每批可抽樣測量 RSF 以確認製程一致性
  • FDA 483 觀察中有涉及「未對膠塞壓縮力進行充分控制」的案例

實務應用 Practical Application

在 CDMO 承接新客戶的瓶裝凍乾產品時，軋蓋製程驗証通常包括：(1) 不同軋蓋力設定下的 RSF 數值 → 建立 RSF 與軋蓋力的相關性曲線；(2) 同時進行 CCI 測試（如真空衰減），確認 RSF 值在什麼範圍時包裝 CCI 合格；(3) 以此設定生產中的 RSF 規格（上下限），作為軋蓋製程的 IPT 指標。

Section 6 — Airborne Ultrasound 空氣耦合超音波

6. Airborne Ultrasound

The airborne ultrasound test checks seal quality by passing an ultrasound signal through the sealed area of a package or item. The signal strength transmitted through the package seal is compared to that previously established for "good" package seals. Poorly sealed areas will not transmit as much ultrasonic energy as properly sealed areas.

Detectable seal defects include:

  • Incomplete seal
  • Channel defect (seal channel)
  • Wrinkle in the seal
  • Extraneous material inclusion in seal
  • Package seal bond weakness

The method may be used:

  • Off-line: x–y coordinate scan of an entire seal area (detailed map of seal quality)
  • On-line: linear scan of seals during manufacturing

Airborne ultrasound is a noncontact testing technology that requires no package preparation and no coupling media (liquid or gel). Therefore, it is nondestructive to the package under test.

The method is described in ASTM F3004. The test system consists of: transmitter transducer → air gap → package seal → receiver transducer → signal intensity comparison.

Detection capability varies based on: package material of construction, seal structure (smooth vs. textured surface), scanning speed, and scanning signal strength.

Applicable to: flexible pouch seals and rigid tray seals made of metallic or plastic laminates.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析：超音波密封檢測的物理原理

空氣耦合超音波的工作原理：

  • 好的密封：兩層材料緊密熔合，超音波可以從發射端穿越密封區域到達接收端 → 信號強
  • 有缺陷的密封：通道、皺摺、異物夾入 → 密封區域有空隙 → 超音波被空氣衰減或反射 → 信號弱

「空氣耦合 (Airborne)」是指超音波在空氣中傳播，不需要液體或凝膠作為耦合介質（不同於傳統接觸式超音波探傷）。

重點提示：超音波密封測試的應用邊界

超音波密封測試的重要限制：

  • 不能偵測：密封區域以外的包裝完整性問題（如袋面穿刺）
  • 不能定量：洩漏速率或缺陷大小無法量化
  • 影響因素多：材料紋理、厚度、掃描速度都會影響信號基準值的建立
  • 在凍乾托盤密封 (tray sealing) 的品質監控中有良好應用——可快速掃描大面積密封

比喻說明 Analogy

空氣耦合超音波就像在牆的一側對著密封處說話，另一側的人聽聲音大小。如果牆壁（密封）完整，聲音清晰；如果牆上有縫隙（通道缺陷），聲音就會被吸收或改變。這項技術特別適合發現「通道缺陷」——剝離測試可能會錯過極細微的通道，但超音波可以精確掃描到。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 空氣耦合超音波密封測試被描述為「非破壞性」，但它是密封品質測試，不是洩漏測試。如果超音波掃描顯示密封區域完好，能否據此確認包裝不洩漏？請說明原因。
  2. 對於一個含鋁箔層的軟袋包裝（如 IV 袋），超音波密封測試適用嗎？和 HVLD 相比，各有什麼優勢？

實務應用 Practical Application

在無菌軟袋灌裝（如大容量輸液袋）的品質體系中，超音波密封測試是理想的在線製程監控工具：可在灌裝線上即時掃描每個袋子的密封區域，立即發現熱封不均勻、通道缺陷等製程異常，並觸發線上剔除或停線警報。配合定期抽樣的 CCI 洩漏測試（如真空衰減），形成完整的包裝完整性保障體系。

本章重點回顧 Chapter Summary

  • 密封品質測試 ≠ 洩漏測試：前者是製程控制的代理指標，後者直接測量包裝完整性；兩者互補，缺一不可
  • 資格確認即可：密封品質測試只需資格確認（儀器性能 + 設定適當性），不需完整的分析方法驗証
  • 五種技術各有定位：扭矩（螺旋蓋）→ 爆破（軟袋強度）→ 剝離（密封均勻性）→ RSF（玻璃瓶軋蓋）→ 超音波（密封區域缺陷定位）
  • RSF 是無菌小瓶的核心：與軋蓋力線性相關，是軋蓋製程驗証和日常監控的標準工具，在 PDA TR27 和 USP〈1207.3〉中均有依據
  • 超音波的獨特優勢：非接觸、非破壞、可在線、可定位缺陷——是軟袋灌裝線在線品質監控的理想選擇
  • 密封品質測試無法証明完整性：通過任何密封品質測試的包裝，仍可能因為密封區域以外的缺陷（穿刺、裂縫）而洩漏，這是密封品質測試的根本局限性
  • USP〈1207〉三章整體：〈1207.1〉選方法 + 〈1207.2〉洩漏技術 + 〈1207.3〉密封品質技術 = CCI 完整知識體系，三者結合才能構建科學合理的包裝完整性保障計劃
