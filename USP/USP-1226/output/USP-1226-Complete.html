Introduction & Scope 簡介與適用範圍

Original Text 原文內容

INTRODUCTION

The intent of this chapter is to provide general information on the verification of compendial procedures that are being performed for the first time to yield acceptable results utilizing the personnel, equipment, and reagents available. This chapter is not intended for retroactive application to already successfully established laboratory procedures.

Validation of Compendial Procedures 〈1225〉 provides general information on characteristics that should be considered for various test categories and on the documentation that should accompany analytical procedures submitted for inclusion in USP–NF. Verification consists of assessing selected analytical performance characteristics, such as those that are described in 〈1225〉, to generate appropriate, relevant data rather than repeating the validation process.

Users of compendial analytical procedures are not required to validate these procedures when first used in their laboratories, but documented evidence of suitability should be established under actual conditions of use. In the United States, this requirement is established in 21 CFR 211.194(a)(2) of the current Good Manufacturing Practice regulations, which states that the "suitability of all testing methods used shall be verified under actual conditions of use."

Verification of microbiological procedures is not covered in this chapter because it is covered in Antimicrobial Effectiveness Testing 〈51〉, Microbial Enumeration Tests 〈61〉, Tests for Specified Microorganisms 〈62〉, Sterility Tests 〈71〉, and Validation of Microbial Recovery from Pharmacopeial Articles 〈1227〉.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

比喻說明 Analogy

把USP藥典方法想像成一輛「通過國家安全認證的汽車」：確效（Validation）是汽車製造商在出廠前做的完整安全測試（碰撞測試、煞車性能、排放測試）；驗證（Verification）是你買回來後，在你家的車道、你常跑的路況上開個幾趟，確認這輛車在你的使用場景下表現正常。你不需要重複工廠的全套碰撞測試，但要確認它在你的條件下能正常運作。

微生物方法的例外 Microbiological Procedures Exception

本章明確排除微生物方法的驗證，這些由各自的藥典章節規定：

  • <51>：抗菌效力試驗
  • <61>：微生物計數試驗
  • <62>：特定微生物試驗
  • <71>：無菌試驗
  • <1227>：藥典品項微生物回收率驗證

這些方法的「適用性確認」叫做「方法適用性試驗（Method Suitability Test）」，不是本章的驗證。

Verification Process 驗證過程

Original Text 原文內容

VERIFICATION PROCESS

The verification process for compendial test procedures is the assessment of whether the procedure can be used for its intended purpose, under the actual conditions of use for a specified drug substance and/or drug product matrix.

Users should have the appropriate experience, knowledge, and training to understand and be able to perform the compendial procedures as written. Verification should be conducted by the user such that the results will provide confidence that the compendial procedure will perform suitably as intended.

If the verification of the compendial procedure is not successful, and assistance from USP staff has not resolved the problem, it may be concluded that the procedure may not be suitable for use with the article being tested in that laboratory. It may then be necessary to develop and validate an alternative procedure as allowed in the General Notices, 6.30 Alternative and Harmonized Methods and Procedures. The alternative procedure may be submitted to USP, along with the appropriate data, to support a proposal for inclusion or replacement of the current compendial procedure.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

驗證過程的三個核心問題：

  • 問題一：方法能否用於預期目的？ — 在我的產品基質和我的實驗室條件下，方法結果是否可靠？
  • 問題二：操作人員是否具備能力？ — 分析師必須有適當的培訓和經驗
  • 問題三：驗證失敗怎麼辦？ — 先尋求USP協助 → 若仍無法解決 → 開發替代方法 → 可提交USP修訂

驗證失敗的處理路徑比喻 Failed Verification Pathway

驗證失敗就像發現「政府推薦的食譜」在你的廚房裡做不出來：先找食譜的原作者（USP）協助排疑；如果問題在於你的食材（基質干擾）確實讓這個食譜無法使用，你有權開發自己的食譜（替代方法）；如果你的新食譜更好，還可以投稿給政府更新食譜庫（提交USP）。

「替代方法」的法規路徑 Alternative Method Regulatory Pathway

依USP General Notices 6.30：

  • 藥廠可以使用替代方法，但必須能證明等效性
  • 若替代方法可以取代藥典方法，可向USP提交建議修訂
  • 對FDA審查而言：替代方法需要在申請文件中申報，並提供驗證資料支持等效性

Verification Requirements 驗證需求

Original Text 原文內容

VERIFICATION REQUIREMENTS

Verification requirements should be based on an assessment of the complexity of both the procedure and the material to which the procedure is applied. Although complete revalidation of a compendial method is not required to verify the suitability of a procedure under actual conditions of use, some of the analytical performance characteristics listed in Validation of Compendial Procedures 〈1225〉, Table 2, may be used for the verification process. Only those characteristics that are considered to be appropriate for the verification of the particular procedure need to be evaluated.

The process of assessing the suitability of a compendial analytical test procedure under the conditions of actual use may or may not require actual laboratory performance of each analytical performance characteristic. The degree and extent of the verification process may depend on the level of training and experience of the user, on the type of procedure and its associated equipment or instrumentation, on the specific procedural steps, and on which article(s) are being tested.

Verification should assess whether the compendial procedure is suitable for the drug substance and/or the drug product matrix, taking into account the drug substance's synthetic route, the method of manufacture for the drug product, or both, if applicable. Verification should include an assessment of elements such as the effect of the matrix on the recovery of impurities and drug substances from the drug product matrix, as well as the suitability of chromatographic conditions and column, the appropriateness of detector signal response, etc.

As an example, an assessment of specificity is a key parameter in verifying that a compendial procedure is suitable for use in assaying drug substances and drug products. For instance, acceptable specificity for a chromatographic method may be verified by conformance with system suitability resolution requirements (if specified in the procedure). However, drug substances from different suppliers may have different impurity profiles that are not addressed by the compendial test procedure. Similarly, the excipients in a drug product can vary widely among manufacturers and may have the potential to directly interfere with the procedure or cause the formation of impurities that are not addressed by the compendial procedure. In addition, drug products containing different excipients, antioxidants, buffers, or container extractives may affect the recovery of the drug substance from the matrix.

Other analytical performance characteristics such as an assessment of the limit of detection or quantitation and precision for impurities procedures may be useful to demonstrate the suitability of the compendial procedure under actual conditions of use. It is the user's responsibility to demonstrate the long term (more than 24 h) stability and storage conditions of Standard and sample preparations throughout the duration of the procedure during the verification of compendial procedures.

Verification is not required for basic compendial test procedures that are routinely performed unless there is an indication that the compendial procedure is not appropriate for the article under test. Examples of basic compendial procedures include, but are not limited to, loss on drying, residue on ignition, various wet chemical procedures such as acid value, and simple instrumental determinations such as pH measurements. However, for the application of already established routine procedures to compendial articles tested for the first time, it is recommended that consideration be given to any new or different sample handling or solution preparation requirements.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

驗證需求的決策框架 Verification Decision Framework

影響驗證範疇的關鍵因素：

  • 程序複雜度：HPLC含量測定 > 滴定法 > 燒失法；複雜的程序需要更全面的驗證
  • 基質複雜度：多成分製劑 > 單一成分原料藥；賦形劑可能干擾
  • 使用者經驗：熟練分析師 + 常規方法 → 簡化驗證；新分析師 + 複雜方法 → 更完整評估
  • 儀器類型：HPLC/LCMS等需要更多評估；pH計相對簡單

特異性驗證的重要性比喻 Specificity as Priority

章節中以特異性為例，強調其重要性：USP方法是為「普通藥典品項」設計的，但你的產品可能有：(1) 供應商特有的雜質謙 profile；(2) 獨特的賦形劑組合；(3) 容器萃取物。就像「通用型安全帶」設計是為普通體型，但對特殊體型的人可能需要額外確認。

驗證範疇的分級思路 Tiered Verification Approach

基礎藥典試驗（通常不需驗證）：
  - 乾燥失重（Loss on Drying）
  - 灼燒殘渣（Residue on Ignition）
  - 酸值（Acid Value）
  - pH測定
  例外：首次用於新品項時，考慮樣品前處理差異

需要驗證的方向（按風險排序）：
  1. 特異性（Specificity）← 最優先
     → 系統適用性（分離度符合要求？）
     → 強制降解干擾確認
  2. 基質效應（Matrix Effects）
     → 加標回收率（Spike Recovery）
  3. 精確性（Precision）
     → 在實際基質下的重現性
  4. 樣品/標準品穩定性（>24小時）
     → 用戶責任，<1226>明確要求

>24小時穩定性的特別要求 Key Note: >24h Stability

USP <1226> 明確規定（2019年修訂新增）：使用者的責任包括在驗證過程中展示標準品和樣品製備液在整個分析程序期間（超過24小時）的長期穩定性和儲存條件。

這在實務上意味著：若你的HPLC分析序列跨越多天（如大批量樣品），必須證明樣品溶液在此期間穩定，並設定明確的儲存條件（溫度、避光等）。

驗證計畫設計練習 Verification Plan Design Exercise

場景：你的CDMO首次對一個含5種賦形劑的口服固體製劑，使用USP HPLC方法進行含量測定。請規劃驗證方案：

  1. 哪些分析性能特性需要評估？（至少列出3項並說明理由）
  2. 特異性如何評估？（至少列出2種方法）
  3. 基質效應如何評估？需要什麼對照實驗？
  4. 樣品溶液穩定性如何設計實驗？設定什麼時間點？

實務應用：驗證 vs. 確效文件化差異 Practical Application

驗證報告（Verification Report）的典型要素：

  • 目的和範疇（針對特定產品和USP方法）
  • 評估的分析性能特性及其選擇理由
  • 各特性的接受標準（可引用<1225> Category要求）
  • 實驗數據（原始數據 + 計算結果）
  • 系統適用性結果
  • 特異性評估結果（系統適用性分離度、加標干擾）
  • 樣品/標準品穩定性數據
  • 結論（方法是否適合在本實驗室用於本產品）

在CDMO環境中，驗證報告通常作為方法轉移報告（Method Transfer Report）的一部分，或作為獨立的「Methods Verification Report」提交客戶和監管部門。

關係整合 Relationship: <1225> Validation vs. <1226> Verification

Original Text 原文內容

The text of 〈1226〉 directly references 〈1225〉 throughout, using its analytical performance characteristics framework as the basis for selecting what to assess during verification. The key regulatory citations are:

  • 21 CFR 211.194(a): Test methods used for assessing compliance must meet proper standards of accuracy and reliability.
  • 21 CFR 211.194(a)(2): Users of analytical methods described in USP–NF are not required to validate the accuracy and reliability of these methods, but merely verify their suitability under actual conditions of use.
  • USP General Notices, 6.30: Allows for the use of alternative and harmonized methods and procedures under specified conditions.

The relationship between chapters can be summarized: 〈1225〉 defines the complete set of performance characteristics and what data is required to establish a new or revised procedure. 〈1226〉 uses the same characteristics framework but applies only a risk-based subset, focusing on the specific drug substance/product matrix and the actual conditions of use in a given laboratory.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

兩章節的協同作用比喻

<1225>是「汽車設計規範」——告訴你一輛好車應該滿足哪些標準（剎車距離、油耗、碰撞安全）；<1226>是「車主手冊」——告訴你這輛車在你的使用場景下（你的路況、你的駕駛習慣）是否正常工作。兩者用同樣的評估語言，但目的和深度不同。

常見混淆點澄清 Common Confusions Clarified

  • 「驗證」不是「確效」的縮減版——驗證是針對特定基質的適用性確認，不是草率做幾個確效實驗
  • 驗證不等於方法轉移——方法轉移（Method Transfer）可能包含驗證，但也可能包含更多（如設備比較、分析師培訓）
  • 微生物方法不適用本章——請參考各自的方法適用性試驗（Method Suitability Test）
  • 基礎試驗通常豁免驗證——但首次用於新品項時要考慮樣品前處理的差異

本章重點回顧 Chapter Summary

  • 核心定位：<1226>為藥廠使用USP方法提供指引——不是要重複確效，而是要確認「適用性」
  • 法規依據：21 CFR 211.194(a)(2)——「所有測試方法的適用性應在實際使用條件下驗證」
  • 範疇限制：不適用於微生物方法（另有各章規定）；不適用於回顧性應用
  • 基本原則：只評估適當的分析性能特性，不需要全套<1225>確效；由複雜度和風險決定範疇
  • 最重要的特性：特異性——必須確認方法在你的基質條件下無干擾
  • 基質效應評估：賦形劑、合成路線相關雜質、容器萃取物都可能干擾
  • 2019新增要求：使用者負責展示>24小時的樣品和標準品穩定性
  • 豁免項目：基礎試驗（乾燥失重、pH等）通常不需驗證；首次用於新品項除外
  • 驗證失敗處理：諮詢USP → 若仍不適用 → 開發替代方法 → 可提交USP修訂
  • 與<1225>關係：使用相同的分析性能特性框架，但驗證是風險導向的子集應用
