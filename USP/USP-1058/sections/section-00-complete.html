Section 1: Introduction & Instrument Classification (Groups A/B/C)

原文內容 Original Text

A large variety of analytical instruments, ranging from a simple apparatus to complex computerized systems, is used in the pharmaceutical industry to acquire data that will help ensure that products meet their specifications. In order to ensure "fitness for purpose", an integrated approach, based upon a risk assessment, is recommended.

The risk assessment for an AIQ enables the classification of the instrument to determine the extent of qualification and actions needed to demonstrate fitness for purpose. Generally, the more complex the instrument, or the higher the criticality of the measurement, the greater the amount of work that is required.

Instruments can generally be classified as belonging to Groups A, B, or C:

  • Group A: The least complex, standard instruments used without measurement capability or user requirement for calibration, such as a magnetic stirrer or vortex mixer. Proper function is ensured by observation, and no further qualification activities are needed.
  • Group B: Instruments that may provide a measurement or an experimental condition that can affect a measurement. Examples include a pH meter or an oven. May require only routine calibration, maintenance, or performance checks. Generally have firmware but not user-updated software.
  • Group C: Analytical instruments with a significant degree of computerization and complexity, such as high-pressure liquid chromatographs and mass spectrometers. All elements of qualification, including software validation, must be considered.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts — 儀器分類的實用判斷

分類的核心問題是：「這台儀器的測量結果，直接影響到放行決定嗎？」

  • Group A（觀察即可）：攪拌器、離心機（一般用途）、超音波清洗機 — 只要確認它「在動」就夠了
  • Group B（校正即可）：pH計、天平、水浴槽、烤箱、溫度計 — 需要定期校正、記錄校正證書
  • Group C（完整AIQ）：HPLC、GC、質譜儀、UV分光光度計、卡氏水分儀、粒度儀 — 需DQ/IQ/OQ/PQ全套

注意：同一型號儀器在不同用途下可能分屬不同Group。例如天平用於一般稱重 = Group B；用於稱取基準物質進行標準品配製 = 可能需要Group C的管理。

比喻說明 Analogy — 儀器確效的「駕照制度」

把AIQ想成汽車的駕照制度：摩托車（Group A，簡單）→ 普通駕照就夠了；轎車（Group B，中等）→ 需要定期年檢；大型拖車（Group C，複雜）→ 需要特殊駕照 + 定期檢驗 + 行車記錄器。儀器越複雜、用途越關鍵，所需的「資格認證」就越完整。

重點提示 Key Notes — 同類儀器可跨Group

USP明確指出「同類型儀器可能落在不同Group」。一台離心機若只用來分離固液（Group A），若加裝溫控系統且溫度影響測試結果，可能需要Group B管理。在新儀器採購/引進時，AIQ團隊必須基於實際預期用途進行分類，並形成書面文件說明分類依據。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 你的實驗室有哪些儀器？請嘗試將它們分類為Group A/B/C，並說明理由。
  2. 如果一台原本用於Group B的pH計，開始用於校準標準曲線的配製，其分類應如何調整？
Section 2: Components of Data Quality — The Quality Triangle

原文內容 Original Text

There are four critical components involved in the generation of reliable and consistent data (quality data). AIQ forms the base for generating quality data. The other components essential for generating quality data are analytical method validation, system suitability tests, and quality control check samples.

Analytical Instrument Qualification (AIQ): The collection of documented evidence that an instrument performs suitably for its intended purpose. Use of a qualified instrument in analyses contributes to confidence in the validity of generated data.

Analytical Method Validation: The collection of documented evidence that an analytical procedure is suitable for its intended use. Use of a validated procedure with qualified analytical instruments provides confidence that the procedure will generate test data of acceptable quality.

System Suitability Tests: Verify that the system will perform in accordance with the criteria set forth in the procedure. These tests are performed along with the sample analyses to ensure that the system's performance is acceptable at the time of the test.

Quality Control Check Samples: Provide an in-process or ongoing assurance of the test's suitable performance. System suitability tests and quality control checks help ensure the quality of analytical results immediately before or during sample analysis.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts — 品質金字塔的層次邏輯

USP描述的「品質三角形」從底層到頂層：

  • 底層（AIQ）：儀器本身要合格 — 「工具可靠」
  • 第二層（方法驗證）：分析方法要經過驗證 — 「方法可靠」
  • 第三層（系統適用性）：每次分析前的系統確認 — 「當下可靠」
  • 頂層（QC樣品）：分析過程中的持續監控 — 「過程可靠」

任何一層出問題，最終的分析結果都不可信。AIQ是整個品質架構的地基，地基不穩，上面建什麼都白費。

比喻說明 Analogy — 航空安全的多重防護

這四個層次就像飛機的多重安全系統：AIQ = 飛機出廠前的適航認證；方法驗證 = 飛行員的飛行執照；系統適用性 = 起飛前的飛行前檢查；QC樣品 = 飛行途中的儀表監控。缺少任何一層，雖然飛機可能仍然飛行，但系統性風險大幅增加。

重點提示 Key Notes — AIQ與方法驗證的時序

AIQ必須在方法驗證之前完成。邏輯是：你不能用一台沒有確效的儀器去驗證一個分析方法，因為你不確定得到的數據是代表方法本身的表現，還是儀器的問題。在CDMO的技術移轉項目中，這個順序尤其重要：儀器IQ/OQ/PQ完成 → 才能啟動方法驗證或方法轉移驗證。

實務應用 Practical Application

在CDMO接受客戶技術移轉時，建立「儀器確效狀態矩陣」：列出所有相關儀器 × 確效狀態（DQ/IQ/OQ/PQ完成/未完成）× 最後確效日期 × 下次PQ到期日。這個矩陣是決定「哪些儀器可以立即用於移轉方法驗證」的依據，也是客戶稽查時的重要展示資料。

Section 3: AIQ Process — Design Qualification (DQ) & Installation Qualification (IQ)

原文內容 Original Text

Qualification Phases Overview

AIQ is not a single, continuous process but instead results from interconnected activities over the lifetime of the instrument. The first activity is the generation of a user requirements specification (URS), which defines the laboratory's particular needs and technical and operational requirements. The subsequent qualification activities may be grouped into four phases: design qualification (DQ), installation qualification (IQ), operational qualification (OQ), and performance qualification (PQ).

This framework may be extended for complex systems to include functional specifications (FS) and factory acceptance testing (FAT). Some activities may also be satisfied during instrument installation and start-up, referred to as site acceptance testing (SAT). All AIQ activities should be predefined and contemporaneously documented.

Design Qualification (DQ)

DQ is the documented collection of activities that define the functional and operational specifications and intended purpose of the instrument. DQ states what the laboratory wants the instrument to do and shows that the selected instrument is suitable. DQ may be performed by the instrument manufacturer or the user. For commercial, off-the-shelf instruments, DQ requirements will be minimal.

The supplier is generally responsible for robust design and maintaining documentation. The user should ensure instruments are suitable for their intended application and may evaluate whether the supplier has adopted a quality system. Users should also determine the supplier's capability to support installation, services, and training.

Installation Qualification (IQ)

IQ is the documented collection of activities necessary to establish that an instrument is delivered as designed and specified, is properly installed in the selected environment, and that this environment is suitable for the instrument. The activities and documentation typically associated with IQ are:

  • Instrument delivery: Ensure the instrument, software, manuals, supplies arrive as specified and are undamaged.
  • Description: Document supplier(s), model(s), serial number(s), software version(s), and location.
  • Utilities/Facility/Environment: Verify installation site meets supplier-specified environmental requirements.
  • Assembly and installation: Assemble and install the instrument; perform preliminary diagnostics. Document any abnormal events.
  • Software installation, network, and data storage: Connect to network for communications and data storage.
  • Installation verification: Perform initial diagnostics and testing after installation.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts — URS是AIQ的起點

用戶需求規格（URS）是整個AIQ的「設計藍圖」。寫好URS，後續所有確效測試才有依據。URS應包含：

  • 預期用途（例如：用於原料藥含量測定的HPLC）
  • 技術要求（波長範圍、流量精確度、偵測靈敏度等）
  • 環境要求（溫濕度、電源規格、空間）
  • 法規合規要求（21 CFR Part 11、GxP等）
  • 供應商支援要求（技術服務、備品可及性）

比喻說明 Analogy — DQ/IQ就像購屋與驗屋

DQ = 購屋前的需求評估與規格書（幾房幾廳、格局要求、地段條件）；IQ = 交屋時的驗屋（門窗是否安裝正確、水電是否接通、設備是否如規格書所示）。OQ/PQ則是入住後的使用測試（空調是否正常運作、噪音是否在可接受範圍）。跳過DQ就像沒有需求規格就簽約，最後拿到的房子可能完全不符合需要。

公式與計算 Formula — IQ必要文件清單

IQ文件包套（IQ Documentation Package）：

1. 到貨確認單（Delivery Receipt）
   - 儀器型號 / 序號 ✓
   - 軟體版本號 ✓
   - 附件完整性 ✓
   - 外觀無損壞 ✓

2. 安裝記錄（Installation Record）
   - 安裝地點 / 環境條件（T, RH, 電壓）
   - 安裝人員（含資格）
   - 初始診斷結果

3. 軟體/網路設置
   - 軟體安裝日誌
   - 網路連接確認
   - 稽核軌跡功能確認

4. IQ執行報告（含結論和核准簽名）

重點提示 Key Notes — 供應商文件的審核責任

供應商提供的IQ文件包套（如FAT報告、出廠測試報告）可以「滿足部分IQ要求」，但使用者仍有責任審核這些文件是否符合自己的要求。不能直接把供應商文件歸檔就算完成IQ。審核重點：測試參數是否涵蓋URS要求？測試環境是否代表實際使用環境？

  1. 如果你正在引進一台新的HPLC系統，你的URS應該包含哪些技術規格？
  2. 儀器到貨時發現外箱輕微凹陷但儀器外觀完好，你應如何記錄和處理？
Section 4: Operational Qualification (OQ) & Performance Qualification (PQ)

原文內容 Original Text

Operational Qualification (OQ)

OQ is the documented collection of activities necessary to demonstrate that an instrument will function according to its operational specification testing in the selected environment. OQ demonstrates fitness for the selected use, and should reflect URS. Testing activities in the OQ phase may consist of:

  • Fixed parameters: Non-changing parameters such as length, height, weight, voltage inputs, acceptable pressures, and loads. Fixed parameters do not change over the life of the instrument, and therefore never need to be retested.
  • Software functions: Critical elements of the configured application software—data capture, analysis, reporting, security, access control, and audit trail.
  • Secure data storage, backup, and archiving: Test secure data handling at the user's site.
  • Instrument function tests: Verify that the instrument operates as intended by the manufacturer.

OQ tests can be modular or holistic. Modular testing of individual components may facilitate interchanging components without requalification but requires a risk assessment. Holistic tests, which involve the entire system, demonstrate that the whole system complies with URS.

Performance Qualification (PQ)

PQ is the documented collection of activities necessary to demonstrate that an instrument consistently performs according to the specifications defined by the user, and is appropriate for the intended use. After IQ and OQ have been performed, the instrument's continued suitability for its intended use is demonstrated through continued PQ.

PQ may include:

  • Performance checks: Usually based on the instrument's typical on-site applications, may consist of analyzing known components or standards. Testing frequency depends on the ruggedness of the instrument and the criticality of the analytic method.
  • Preventive maintenance and repairs: Periodic preventive maintenance activities. When an instrument fails to meet PQ criteria or malfunctions, the cause must be investigated and documented. The relevant OQ or PQ test(s) should be repeated after maintenance or repair.
  • Practices for PQ, change control, and periodic review: Each PQ, maintenance, and calibration activity should be documented. Change control for instrument configuration, including firmware and software. Critical instruments should have a periodic review.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts — OQ vs. PQ的本質區別

這是最常混淆的概念之一：

  • OQ：測試儀器「能做什麼」— 符合製造商規格，通常用標準品或模擬條件測試，一次性（但重大改變後需重複）
  • PQ：測試儀器「在我們的用途下是否持續做得好」— 符合用戶需求，用實際樣品或接近實際的條件，持續進行
  • 比喻：OQ = 新車出廠的性能測試（0~100km/h加速幾秒）；PQ = 你每次開車前的例行檢查（油表、胎壓、機油）

比喻說明 Analogy — PQ就像定期健康檢查

儀器的PQ就像人的定期健康檢查：不是因為儀器「生病了」才做，而是定期確認它「仍然健康」。頻率依據「風險」決定：高齡（老儀器）、使用頻繁（高負擔）、檢測關鍵參數（高風險用途）→ 健檢頻率更高。每次PQ都要建立記錄，累積的PQ歷史就是儀器的「健康履歷」，可用於趨勢分析和預測性維護。

重點提示 Key Notes — 定期審查（Periodic Review）的必要性

USP特別提到「關鍵儀器應有定期審查（periodic review）」。審查內容：確效/驗證狀態是否仍有效？用戶程序是否最新？變更控制記錄是否完整？系統產生記錄是否正確完整？電子記錄備份與恢復是否正常？這個審查通常每年一次，是品質系統的一部分，而非只是儀器部門的任務。

實務應用 Practical Application

在CDMO無菌產品的QC實驗室，HPLC用於原料藥含量測定（關鍵放行測試）。建議PQ頻率：每季度一次性能核查（使用標準品測試系統精確度、重現性）；每次使用前進行系統適用性測試（SST）作為日常確認。這種分層式確效策略（PQ + SST）既滿足法規要求，又有效控制確效工作量。

Section 5: Roles, Responsibilities, Software Validation & Change Control

原文內容 Original Text

Roles and Responsibilities

Users: Ultimately responsible for specifying their needs and ensuring that a selected instrument meets them, and that data quality and integrity are maintained. Users should be adequately trained and their training records maintained. Users should be responsible for qualifying their instruments—their training and expertise make them the best-qualified group to design instrument tests and specifications.

Quality Unit: Responsible for ensuring that the AIQ process meets compliance requirements, that processes are being followed, and that the intended use of the instrument is supported by complete, valid, and documented data.

Manufacturers, Suppliers, Service Agents, and Consultants: Manufacturers are responsible for designing and manufacturing the instrument. There should be a quality or technical agreement between the user organization and manufacturers/suppliers/service agents/consultants who supply calibration, maintenance, qualification, or validation services.

Software Validation

Software used for analytical instruments can be classified into four groups: firmware, instrument control software, data acquisition software, and processing software.

Firmware: Considered a component of the instrument itself. When the hardware is qualified, the firmware is also essentially qualified. The firmware version should be recorded as part of the IQ activities.

Instrument Control, Data Acquisition, and Processing Software: Three types: 1) non-configurable software; 2) configurable software; and 3) configurable software with custom additions. Integrated qualification of instrument plus software validation involves the entire system.

Change Control

A change control process should be in place to guide the assessment, execution, documentation, and approval of any changes to instrumentation. Users should assess the effects of changes to determine what, if any, requalification activities are required. After implementation, perform any required testing. Document all details of the change, including description, rationale, and identification of new components.

Documentation

Documents obtained during qualification activities should be retained in an accessible manner. During change control, additional documents may supplement those obtained during the qualification process.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts — 使用者才是AIQ的責任主體

USP明確指出：儀器的確效責任在於用戶（User），而非製造商或供應商。這與很多人的認知不同——不是說「設備商做好IQ/OQ就算了」。即便聘用外部服務商執行確效，用戶仍需：審核確效計劃是否符合URS → 參與或監督執行過程 → 審核並批准確效報告 → 對確效結論負責。品質部門的角色是確保合規性，而非替代用戶執行確效。

比喻說明 Analogy — 軟體確效的「三種廚師」

軟體分類就像餐廳的三種廚師模式：Non-configurable = 買便當（菜單固定，廠商負責品質）；Configurable = 半成品料理包（框架固定，但你可以調整鹹淡）；Custom-coded = 從頭自己料理（完全自主，但品質完全自負）。客製化程度越高，驗證工作量越大，自己對品質負的責任也越多。

重點提示 Key Notes — 變更控制的觸發條件

以下情況必須啟動儀器變更控制：更換零件（尤其是關鍵部件如HPLC柱溫箱、偵測器）；軟體/韌體版本升級；儀器搬遷至新地點；重大維修後；使用用途改變（如增加新的測試方法類型）。有些看似「例行維護」的工作，如果替換的零件型號改變，也可能需要啟動變更控制，不能因為是小事就繞過流程。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 你的工廠HPLC的OQ/PQ文件是由供應商提供的嗎？你的QA部門有審核這些文件嗎？
  2. 如果HPLC的資料處理軟體進行了版本更新，你應該採取哪些確效步驟？
  3. 一台儀器從一個實驗室搬到另一個實驗室，需要重新進行哪些確效活動？

本章重點回顧 Chapter Summary

  • 儀器分類（Group A/B/C）：依據複雜度和測量重要性分類，決定確效深度。同一儀器可能因用途不同而落在不同分組。
  • 品質資料四要素：AIQ（地基）→ 方法驗證 → 系統適用性測試 → QC樣品 — 缺一不可。
  • AIQ四階段：URS → DQ → IQ → OQ → PQ — 順序不可顛倒，各有明確文件要求。
  • OQ vs. PQ：OQ是「能做什麼」的功能確認（一次性為主）；PQ是「持續做得好」的性能確認（持續進行）。
  • 責任主體：用戶（User）對AIQ結果負最終責任；品質單位確保合規性；供應商提供支援但不替代用戶責任。
  • 軟體確效：韌體隨硬體確效；控制/採集/處理軟體依客製化程度決定驗證深度。
  • 變更控制：任何對已確效儀器的改動必須經過評估，決定是否需要重新確效及其範圍。
