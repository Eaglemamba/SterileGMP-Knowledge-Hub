Introduction — 概論

Original Text 原文內容

The use of sterilizing gases for the preparation of materials and equipment is commonly used for items that are susceptible to damage by heat or radiation processes. Many polymeric materials, especially medical devices, are surface sterilized in this manner, as is nonpressure-rated process equipment. The sterilization of dry powders using gases is inappropriate due to the inability of gases to penetrate solid materials. The majority of gas sterilization processes employ ethylene oxide (EO), and procedures for use with other gases generally are patterned after EO practices. Ozone, mixed oxides of nitrogen, and chlorine dioxide are some of the other gaseous sterilants used. [Systems that can exist in liquid and gas phase at the operating temperatures (e.g., hydrogen peroxide, peracetic acid, and paraformaldehyde) are excluded from consideration in this chapter.] EO's ability to penetrate through polymers, cellulosics, and other materials allows it to be used for the terminal sterilization of medical devices in their final packaging. The other sterilizing gases may be suitable for similar applications.

Process control for gas sterilization equipment is accomplished by control of sterilant gas concentration, relative humidity, temperature, and system pressure. Mixing of the gas in the sterilization chamber may be beneficial. EO sterilization may be used for parametric release as described in Terminally Sterilized Pharmaceutical Products—Parametric Release ⟨1222⟩.

Gas sterilization differs markedly from processes during which the agent used can condense during the operation. Vapor sterilization processes will be addressed separately in Vapor-Phase Sterilization ⟨1229.11⟩.

As outlined in Sterilization of Compendial Articles ⟨1229⟩, analysts must take care in ensuring sterility and demonstrating that the essential quality attributes of the materials are not adversely affected by the process. With respect to gas processes, key considerations include the immediate effects of sterilizing gas on the materials or equipment being sterilized, residual sterilant, sterilant byproducts, and potential chemical reactions. The common gas processes differ slightly with respect to process execution and material concerns and thus are described individually.

"The sterilization of dry powders using gases is inappropriate due to the inability of gases to penetrate solid materials."

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Gaseous Sterilization（氣體滅菌）：利用具有殺微生物活性的氣體分子，透過化學反應（主要是烷基化作用或氧化作用）殺死微生物。與蒸氣滅菌（利用熱能）和輻照滅菌（利用輻射能量）的殺菌機制根本不同。

適用範圍：

  • 不耐熱的高分子材料 — 塑膠管路、矽膠墊片、一次性充填組件
  • 醫療器材的最終包裝滅菌 — EO 可穿透包裝材料，達成 Terminal Sterilization
  • 非耐壓設備 — 不適合高壓蒸氣滅菌的製程設備

不適用：乾粉（氣體無法穿透固體）、含液體物品（氣體在液體中擴散不均）

重點提示 Gas vs Vapor

氣體（Gas）vs 蒸氣（Vapor）的區別：本章明確排除了在操作溫度下可能以液態和氣態共存的系統（如 H2O2、過乙酸、多聚甲醛）。這些屬於蒸氣滅菌，另見 ⟨1229.11⟩。判斷標準是：如果滅菌劑在操作過程中可能發生凝結（Condensation），就屬於蒸氣製程而非氣體製程。

CDMO 實務觀點

Device Constituent Sterilization：在 CDMO 的無菌充填環境中，氣體滅菌最常見的應用是對一次性元件（Single-Use Components）和預滅菌組件（Pre-sterilized Assemblies）的處理。這些元件由供應商以 EO 滅菌後交付，CDMO 需要驗證供應商的滅菌條件是否充分，並管理 EO 殘留風險。

Ethylene Oxide — 環氧乙烷

Original Text 原文內容

EO is a powerful alkylating agent that destroys microorganisms by chemical reaction, primarily with cell DNA. The destructive mechanism largely follows first-order kinetics and depends on concentration, humidity, and temperature. The use of EO for medical devices in their final packaging has, to a large extent, shaped EO sterilization processes (and, to a lesser extent, all gas sterilization) for other applications.

The usual EO process follows a sequence of prehumidification, air removal, rehumidification in the chamber, gas exposure, gas removal from the chamber, and postexposure aeration. The preexposure steps ensure that adequate moisture is present on and within the items being sterilized. The postexposure steps provide time for the diffusion of EO and its byproducts out of the materials and packaging. When EO is used for nonporous equipment the process can be streamlined, which eliminates many of the pre- and postexposure steps because of the need only for surface sterilization.

During EO sterilization the gas is introduced at the beginning, and only minimal additions are necessary later to maintain pressure as the gas is absorbed into the material/sterilization load within the vessel. Humidity adjustment during the process also may be required. In some instances, EO reacts with materials in the load to form ethylene chlorohydrin (ECH) and ethylene glycol (EG). These compounds, including EO, must be reduced to safe levels before the items can be used by patients.

EO processing requires strict worker safety and environmental controls because it is associated with carcinogenicity, mutagenicity, and neurotoxicity. In addition, EO is explosive in concentrations of greater than 2.6% by volume in air, therefore, inert gases are often used to minimize flammability. The commonly accepted biological indicator (BI) strain is Bacillus atrophaeus (formerly B. subtilis var. niger).

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

EO 滅菌完整製程流程

  1. Prehumidification（預增濕） — 在滅菌腔外或腔內將物品暴露於高濕度環境，確保微生物含有足夠水分以對 EO 敏感
  2. Air Removal（抽真空排氣） — 移除腔內空氣，防止形成爆炸性混合氣體，並促進 EO 穿透
  3. Rehumidification（再增濕） — 在腔內補充濕度，確保滅菌條件最佳化
  4. Gas Exposure（氣體暴露） — 注入 EO，維持設定的濃度、溫度和時間
  5. Gas Removal（排氣） — 抽出 EO，進行多次真空/氮氣沖洗循環
  6. Aeration（曝氣） — 長時間通風，讓殘留 EO 和副產物從材料中擴散出來

重點提示 EO 殘留物控制

三種殘留物及其毒性：

殘留物來源毒性等級
Ethylene Oxide (EO)未完全排除的滅菌劑致癌性、致突變性、神經毒性
Ethylene Chlorohydrin (ECH)EO + 含氯材料反應產物急性毒性
Ethylene Glycol (EG)EO + 水反應產物較低毒性

ISO 10993-7 規定了醫療器材上 EO 和 ECH 的最大允許殘留量。曝氣時間是控制殘留的關鍵 — 對厚壁高分子材料，曝氣可能需要數天甚至數週。

重點提示 安全與環保

爆炸風險：EO 在空氣中濃度超過 2.6%（v/v）即具爆炸性。實務上常用兩種策略降低風險：

  • 100% EO + 負壓操作 — 在負壓下操作，即使洩漏也不會達到爆炸濃度
  • EO/惰性氣體混合物 — 使用 EO/CO2 或 EO/HCFC 混合物，將 EO 濃度稀釋到爆炸下限以下

環保法規趨勢：多國正收緊 EO 排放標準，推動產業尋找替代氣體或替代滅菌技術。

Ozone — 臭氧

Original Text 原文內容

Ozone is a potent oxidizing agent produced by passing a stream of oxygen or air through a high-voltage electrical field. Ozone is an effective biocidal agent for treatment of water supplies and has demonstrated lethality at concentrations from 2%–10% in air. Optimal microbial destruction is accomplished when the relative humidity is above 80% at room temperature. Ozone degrades to oxygen in the presence of moisture and metals and therefore usually is generated in situ.

Ozone does not penetrate porous materials to the same extent as EO does. Process systems that use ozone for gas sterilization have the advantage of simplicity. Its generation and destruction (using a catalytic converter) are accomplished without moving parts or consumables other than the supplied oxygen. The sterilization process uses a sequence of humidification, injection, exposure, and ventilation to remove the ozone from the chamber at the end of the cycle. The common BIs identified for ozone are Geobacillus stearothermophilus and Bacillus atrophaeus.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

臭氧滅菌特性摘要

  • 殺菌機制：強氧化作用，破壞微生物細胞膜和 DNA
  • 優勢：現場生成（In Situ Generation），無需儲存危險化學品；殘留物為氧氣，環保友好；設備簡單，無消耗品
  • 限制：穿透力不如 EO，不適合厚壁或多層包裝物品；在金屬和水分存在下快速降解，穩定性差
  • 最佳條件：室溫、RH >80%、濃度 2–10%
  • BI：G. stearothermophilus 和 B. atrophaeus
Chlorine Dioxide — 二氧化氯

Original Text 原文內容

Chlorine dioxide is an effective sterilizing gas. Pure chlorine dioxide is metastable and therefore is generated as needed. Chlorine dioxide is noncarcinogenic, nonflammable, and effective at ambient temperatures. Its ability to penetrate materials may be less than that of EO.

A typical chlorine dioxide sterilization process uses a sequence of preconditioning, conditioning dwell period, charge, and exposure, followed by aeration.

The BI most commonly used is Bacillus atrophaeus.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

二氧化氯滅菌特性摘要

  • 非致癌性 — 這是相對於 EO 的重要安全優勢
  • 非易燃性 — 降低操作風險
  • 室溫有效 — 適用於熱敏感材料
  • 現場生成 — 因為純 ClO2 化學性質不穩定（Metastable）
  • 穿透力有限 — 可能不如 EO 適合多層包裝
  • BI：B. atrophaeus
Nitrogen Dioxide — 二氧化氮

Original Text 原文內容

Nitrogen dioxide is a sterilizing gas effective at ambient temperature. Liquid nitrogen dioxide is converted to a gas on introduction to the target chamber. Nitrogen dioxide is nonexplosive and its residues are noncarcinogenic, noncytotoxic, and nonteratogenic. It has a limited ability to penetrate polymeric materials in comparison to EO, which makes postcycle aeration more rapid. It is incompatible with cellulosic materials such as paper and cardboard. The suggested BIs for nitrogen dioxide are G. stearothermophilus and B. atrophaeus.

Validation of Gas Sterilization — 氣體滅菌驗證

Original Text 原文內容

The validation of gaseous sterilization generally begins with the establishing of a "minimum lethal process dwell time" through the use of fractional exposure studies. These fractional studies establish that exposure time, under standard process conditions, where the biological indicator is fully inactivated. This minimum exposure time then becomes the basis for the application of the half-cycle approach for validating the sterilization cycle.

The absence of information relating the effect of varying gas concentration, humidity, and temperature on microorganisms resulted in a conservative assumption that the bioburden is equal in antimicrobial resistance and population to that of the biological indicator.

The half-cycle validation method requires the destruction of a high concentration (NLT 106 spores) of a resistant microorganism under defined, minimum conditions for complete kill. This establishes the minimum lethal process dwell time. In routine operation, the process dwell period is arbitrarily doubled and supports a theoretical reduction of the biological indicator (and thus the bioburden) to a probability of a nonsterile unit (PNSU) of 10−6.

Alternative approaches to cycle validation are available. Gillis and Mosley developed a means for parametric evaluation of EO sterilizing conditions that may result in greater use of other validation methods. A bracketing approach that better supports the process operating ranges for the critical parameters relative to the half-cycle method has also been used. In the bracketing method, one evaluates conditions that bracket the defined process condition in order to establish parameters for the minimum and maximum effects on the materials and bioburden. The minimum lethal process dwell time establishes the worst case for microbial kill. Incremental increases in process dwell time beyond the minimum lethal process dwell time are used to establish the routine and maximum exposure periods, the latter of which imparts the greatest effect on materials. In addition, adjustments to agent concentration and relative humidity are utilized to further enhance the bracketing approach. By this method, the routine process conditions may be established between the minimum and maximum process conditions to assure complete microbial kill while maintaining the integrity of the materials.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：半週期法 Half-Cycle Method

邏輯步驟：

  1. Fractional Exposure Studies（分段暴露研究） — 以遞增的暴露時間測試 BI 存活率，找到「恰好完全殺滅 BI」的最短時間
  2. Minimum Lethal Process Dwell Time（最短致死停留時間） — 上述「恰好完全殺滅」的時間
  3. Routine Dwell Time = 2 × Minimum（常規停留時間 = 2 倍最短致死時間） — 提供額外的安全裕度

保守假設：由於缺乏氣體濃度/濕度/溫度對微生物影響的完整數據，半週期法假設製品的自然生物負載與 BI 具有相同的抗性和數量。這是一個非常保守的假設，但正因如此，半週期法被認為是最穩健的驗證方法。

進階概念：包圍法 Bracketing Method

比半週期法更精細的驗證策略：

條件最小值常規值最大值
Dwell Time最短致死時間中間值最長（材料影響最大）
Gas Concentration下限標稱值上限
Relative Humidity下限標稱值上限

優勢：包圍法同時驗證了微生物殺滅效果（最小條件）和材料相容性（最大條件），建立了一個完整的操作窗口（Operating Window），而非僅依賴單一的「2倍安全係數」。

比喻說明 Half-Cycle vs Bracketing

烤麵包的驗證：

半週期法：你發現 180°C 烤 15 分鐘剛好把麵包烤熟。於是你規定每次都烤 30 分鐘（2倍）。簡單、安全，但可能烤過頭。

包圍法：你測試了 170–190°C、12–20 分鐘的各種組合，找到「剛好熟」到「開始焦」的完整範圍。日常操作設定在中間值，既確保熟透又不會烤焦。更精細，但需要更多實驗。

Equipment Qualification — 設備確效

Original Text 原文內容

The equipment qualification for gas sterilization should include both pre- and postcycle systems to confirm that the equipment has been properly installed and operates as intended.

設備確效要點

氣體滅菌設備確效涵蓋標準的 IQ/OQ/PQ 三階段，但需特別關注：

Empty Chamber Parameter Distribution — 空腔參數分布

Original Text 原文內容

Despite the use of true gases, evaluation of parameter uniformity across the chamber is a common activity. This ensures that the gas and humidity introduction methods provide consistency throughout the chamber and can be correlated to the routine monitoring location(s), when present. Biological indicators are not required in the evaluation of the empty chamber uniformity.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

空腔分布測試要點

測試目的：確認氣體和濕度在空腔各處的均勻性，並建立常規監測點與腔內實際條件的相關性。

關鍵發現：原文明確指出空腔均勻性測試不需要使用生物指示劑 — 這與蒸氣滅菌的空腔溫度分布測試邏輯一致。空腔測試僅需物理/化學量測（溫度、濕度、氣體濃度分布），BI 留到負載測試階段使用。

Component & Load Mapping — 組件與負載佈局

Original Text 原文內容

Component and load mapping using invasive sampling are not a part of gas sterilization because sampling systems placed within the load items would alter gas and humidity penetration. Evaluation of lethal conditions with individual items and across loading patterns is best provided by biological indicators or process challenge devices placed within the load items and distributed within the load. Indicators or process control devices are placed within the items and load at locations believed to be hardest for the gas and humidity to penetrate.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

重點提示 氣體滅菌 vs 蒸氣/乾熱滅菌的佈局差異

為什麼不能使用侵入式取樣？在蒸氣或乾熱滅菌中，可以在負載中插入溫度探針（Thermocouple）來測量熱穿透。但在氣體滅菌中，插入任何取樣裝置都會破壞負載的原始結構，改變氣體和濕度的穿透路徑，導致測量結果不代表實際條件。

替代方案：使用 BI 或 PCD（Process Challenge Device）放置在最難穿透的位置（Worst-Case Locations），這些位置通常是：

  • 多層包裝的最內層
  • 負載中心（氣體最後到達的區域）
  • 厚壁高分子部件的內部
  • 負載密度最高的區域
Biological Indicators — 生物指示劑

Original Text 原文內容

The biological indicator of choice for gas sterilization varies, as noted above. B. atrophaeus (ATCC 9372) is used with EO and chlorine dioxide, and ozone sterilization is monitored with G. stearothermophilus (ATCC 12980 or 7953). D-values for the biological indicator can be used to establish exposure periods for the sterilization process to ensure adequate process efficacy. When positioning biological indicators within items it is important to ensure that the placement of the BI does not occlude gas passage or otherwise interfere with the distribution/penetration of the sterilant within the item.

Process Confirmation & Microbiological Challenge — 製程確認與微生物挑戰

Original Text 原文內容

The core of the validation activity is the confirmation of acceptable process parameters with simultaneous physical and chemical measurement and microbial challenge. Sensors are placed in the chamber, or biological indicators are positioned within the load items. Proof of cycle efficacy is provided in replicate studies in which the biological indicators are killed and the physical measurements correspond to the expected values.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

製程確認的核心邏輯

雙重證據原則：

  • 物理/化學量測 — 腔內感測器確認溫度、壓力、濕度、氣體濃度達到設定值
  • 微生物挑戰 — BI 全部被殺滅，證明致死條件確實在負載最差位置實現

Replicate Studies（重複研究）：驗證不是做一次就夠 — 必須以重複批次（通常至少 3 批）證明製程的一致性和可重複性。每一批次都必須同時滿足物理量測和 BI 殺滅兩個條件。

Routine Process Control — 常規製程控制

Original Text 原文內容

Gas sterilization is subject to formal controls that maintain a validated state over time. The practices outlined in ⟨1229⟩ include the general requirements appropriate for all sterilization systems. Sterilization is accomplished by a number of related practices that are essential for continued use of the process over an extended period of time. The essential practices to maintain validated status include calibration, physical measurements, ongoing process control, change control, preventive maintenance, periodic reassessment, and training.

When parametric release has not been established, biological indicators positioned within the load are used for routine release of each sterilization load, along with a review of documentation from the sterilizer control system.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

七大常規控制實務

  1. Calibration（校驗） — 溫度、壓力、濕度感測器的定期校驗
  2. Physical Measurements（物理量測） — 每批次紀錄關鍵參數
  3. Ongoing Process Control（持續製程控制） — 趨勢分析，偵測製程偏移
  4. Change Control（變更控制） — 任何製程、設備、材料變更都需評估對驗證狀態的影響
  5. Preventive Maintenance（預防保養） — 按計畫執行設備保養
  6. Periodic Reassessment（定期再評估） — 週期性重新確認驗證狀態
  7. Training（教育訓練） — 操作人員的持續培訓

重點提示 Parametric Release vs BI Release

兩種放行模式：

  • Parametric Release（參數放行） — 僅依據物理/化學參數放行，不需要等待 BI 培養結果。前提是已經建立了充分的參數與滅菌效果的相關性（見 ⟨1222⟩）
  • BI Release（BI 放行） — 每批滅菌負載都放置 BI，必須等到 BI 培養結果確認為陰性（通常 7 天）才能放行。這是大多數 EO 滅菌操作的現行做法

CDMO 影響：如果使用 BI 放行，滅菌後需要 7 天的等待期才能放行使用，這會顯著影響生產排程和庫存管理。

本章重點回顧 Chapter Summary

  • 氣體滅菌的定位：適用於不耐熱、不耐輻照的物品（尤其是高分子醫療器材），以化學反應殺死微生物
  • EO 是主流：穿透力最強，可用於最終包裝滅菌，但有致癌性、爆炸性，且殘留物需嚴格控制
  • 替代氣體（O3、ClO2、NO2）：安全性較高但穿透力有限，各有材料相容性限制
  • 驗證核心：半週期法（保守穩健）或包圍法（精細但需更多數據），均以 BI 殺滅 + 物理量測雙重證據為基礎
  • 負載佈局特殊性：不能使用侵入式取樣，僅能透過 BI/PCD 放置於最差位置來評估
  • 常規控制七要素：校驗、物理量測、持續製程控制、變更控制、預防保養、定期再評估、教育訓練
  • CDMO 實務影響：供應商交付的 EO 滅菌元件需要驗證殘留物合規（ISO 10993-7），BI 放行的 7 天等待期影響生產排程
