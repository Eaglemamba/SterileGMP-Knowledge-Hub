Description — 說明

原文內容 Original Text

Glass containers for pharmaceutical use are intended to come into direct contact with pharmaceutical products.

The following recommendations can be made as to the suitability of a glass container for pharmaceutical products, based on the tests for hydrolytic resistance.

Glass containers meeting Type I performance are suitable for most products for parenteral and nonparenteral uses (e.g., borosilicate). Glass containers meeting Type II performance are suitable for most acidic and neutral aqueous products for parenteral and nonparenteral uses (e.g., treated soda-lime silica). Glass containers meeting Type II performance may be used for alkaline parenteral products where stability data demonstrate their suitability. Glass containers meeting Type III performance (e.g., soda-lime silica) usually are not used for parenteral products or for powders for parenteral use, except where suitable stability test data indicate that containers meeting Type III performance are satisfactory.

The inner surface of glass containers may be treated to improve hydrolytic resistance. The outer surface of glass containers may be treated to reduce friction or for protection against abrasion or breakage. The outer surface treatment is such that it does not contaminate the inner surface of the container.

Information on chemical composition of glass types, the formation of glass containers, and factors that influence inner surface durability of glass containers is provided in Evaluation of the Inner Surface Durability of Glass Containers 〈1660〉. This chapter also contains recommended approaches to evaluate the potential of a drug product to cause the formation of glass particles and delamination.

Glass may be colored to provide protection from light by the addition of small amounts of metal oxides and is tested as described in Spectral Transmission for Colored Glass Containers. A clear and colorless container that is made light resistant by means of an opaque enclosure (see Packaging and Storage Requirements 〈659〉) is exempt from the requirements for spectral transmission.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：玻璃性能分類 Glass Type Classification

Type I（硼矽酸玻璃 borosilicate glass）：最高水解耐性，適用幾乎所有注射劑與非注射劑。含 B₂O₃，化學穩定性強，是注射劑的首選材料。

Type II（表面處理蘇打石灰矽玻璃 treated soda-lime silica）：內表面經去鹼處理，適合酸性或中性水溶液注射劑。鹼性注射劑需有穩定性數據支持。

Type III（蘇打石灰矽玻璃 soda-lime silica）：一般不用於注射劑，除非有充分穩定性數據。多用於口服液或外用製劑。

NP（非注射劑用，Not for Parenteral use）：一般用途玻璃，不適用於任何注射劑。

比喻說明 Analogy

想像玻璃容器就像廚房用的鍋具：Type I 是高品質的鑄鐵鍋（耐高溫、不反應）；Type II 是不沾鍋（表面有保護塗層，適合特定用途）；Type III 是普通鋼鍋（家常使用可以，但不適合細緻料理）。把藥物放進鍋裡「煮」，就是在模擬藥品與玻璃的接觸反應——選錯鍋，藥品就會被「污染」。

重點提示 Key Notes

內表面處理（inner surface treatment）可提升 Type II 玻璃的水解耐性。但外表面處理（例如塗佈二氧化矽以防碎）不得污染內表面，這是 GMP 容器相容性的核心要求。

Type II 用於鹼性注射劑時，必須有穩定性數據（stability data）支持，這意味著 CDMO 客戶的 CTD 文件中，容器相容性章節必須有此依據。

實務應用 Practical Application

在 CDMO 接受新客戶的製劑時，選擇玻璃容器的第一個問題是：這個產品是注射劑嗎？pH 值是多少？若為 pH 7–9 的鹼性蛋白藥物（biologics），Type I 幾乎是唯一選項；Type II 需要額外的穩定性評估，會增加開發時程與成本。

Specific Tests — Overview & Test Selection Tables | 特定試驗概述與試驗選擇表

原文內容 Original Text

The Glass Grains Test combined with the Surface Glass Test for hydrolytic resistance determines glass performance. The hydrolytic resistance is determined by the quantity of alkali released from the glass under the conditions specified. This quantity of alkali is extremely small in the case of the more resistant glasses, thus calling for particular attention to all details of the tests and the use of apparatus of high quality and precision.

Conducting these tests in conjunction with a glass standard reference material (SRM) on a routine basis will help to ensure the accuracy of the method. Reference materials are available for both borosilicate glass (SRM 623) and soda-lime-silica glass (SRM 622) from the National Institute of Standards and Technology. The tests should be conducted in an area relatively free from fumes and excessive dust.

Test selection is shown in Table 1 and Table 2.

Table 1. Determination of Glass Performance

Performance TypeTestReason
I, II, IIIGlass Grains TestDistinguishes Type I from Type II and III

Table 2. Determination of Inner Surface Hydrolytic Resistance

Performance TypeTestReason
I, II, IIISurface Glass TestDetermines hydrolytic resistance of inner surface; distinguishes between Type I and Type II containers with high hydrolytic resistance and Type III containers with moderate hydrolytic resistance
I, IISurface Etching Test or comparison of Glass Grains Test and Surface Glass Test dataWhere it is necessary, determines whether high hydrolytic resistance is due to inner surface treatment or to the chemical composition of the glass containers

Glass containers must comply with their respective specifications for surface hydrolytic resistance as noted in Table 1 and Table 2. Type I or Type II containers for aqueous parenteral products are tested for extractable arsenic.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：兩大水解耐性試驗

Glass Grains Test（玻璃粉末試驗）：將玻璃研磨成特定粒徑的粉末，以高壓釜加熱後測定釋出的鹼量（alkali release）。由於是「整體玻璃組成」的測試，可區分 Type I 與 Type II/III。

Surface Glass Test（玻璃表面試驗）：直接對完整容器進行高壓釜萃取，測定內表面釋出的鹼量。反映「實際接觸藥品的表面」狀況，更貼近臨床使用情境。

兩者的差異在於：Glass Grains Test 代表「玻璃本體」，Surface Glass Test 代表「容器內表面」——對於 Type II 玻璃（表面處理型），兩者結果會有顯著差異。

比喻說明 Analogy

Glass Grains Test 像是把一顆蘋果打成果汁來測試它的成分；Surface Glass Test 則是直接舔蘋果皮來感受表面特性。對於塗蠟的蘋果（Type II），打成果汁時蠟占比很低，但表面測試會顯示蠟的存在。這正是 Surface Etching Test 的意義——剝除表面的「蠟層」（表面處理），再重新測試，確認耐性來源。

重點提示 Key Notes

SRM（標準參考物質）的重要性：水解耐性測試中釋出的鹼量極微，測試誤差會嚴重影響結果。USP 要求在日常測試中同時使用 NIST 提供的 SRM 622（蘇打石灰矽）或 SRM 623（硼矽酸）作為方法確效工具。這是確保實驗室間（inter-lab）結果一致性的關鍵。

可萃取砷（extractable arsenic）：僅針對 Type I 或 Type II 的水溶液注射劑容器，這是重金屬安全性的底線要求（限度：0.1 µg/g）。

試驗選擇邏輯 Test Selection Logic

目標：判斷容器性能類型

步驟 1：Glass Grains Test
  → 結果低（≤0.1 mL 0.02M HCl/g）→ 可能是 Type I

步驟 2：Surface Glass Test
  → Type I / II 共同判定耐性

步驟 3（如需區分 I vs II）：
  Surface Etching Test 或
  Glass Grains + Surface Glass 數據比對
  → Type I：蝕刻前後數值相近
  → Type II：蝕刻後數值大幅升高

注意：砷測試僅適用 Type I / II 水溶液注射劑容器

Hydrolytic Resistance Apparatus & Reagents | 水解耐性試驗裝置與試劑

原文內容 Original Text

Autoclave: For these tests, use an autoclave capable of maintaining a temperature of 121 ± 1°, equipped with a thermometer, or a calibrated thermocouple device, allowing a temperature measurement independent of the autoclave system; a suitable recorder; a pressure gauge; a vent cock; and a tray of sufficient capacity to accommodate the number of containers needed to carry out the test above the water level. Clean the autoclave and other apparatus thoroughly with purified water before use.

Mortar and pestle: Use a hardened-steel mortar and pestle, made according to the specifications in Figure 1.

Other apparatus: Also required are a set of three square-mesh stainless steel sieves mounted on frames consisting of US Sieve Nos. 25, 40, and 50 (see Particle Size Distribution Estimation by Analytical Sieving 〈786〉); a mechanical sieve-shaker or a sieving machine; a tempered, magnetic steel hammer; a permanent magnet; weighing bottles; stoppers; metal foil (e.g., aluminum, stainless steel); a hot air oven, capable of maintaining 140 ± 5°; a balance, capable of weighing up to 500 g with an accuracy of 0.005 g; a desiccator; and an ultrasonic bath.

Carbon dioxide-free water: This is purified water that has been boiled vigorously for 5 min or more and allowed to cool while protected from absorption of carbon dioxide from the atmosphere, or purified water that has a resistivity of not less than 18 Mohm-cm.

Methyl red solution: Dissolve 50 mg of methyl red in 1.86 mL of 0.1 M sodium hydroxide and 50 mL of ethanol (96%), and dilute with purified water to 100 mL. To test for sensitivity, add 100 mL of carbon dioxide-free water and 0.05 mL of 0.02 M hydrochloric acid to 0.1 mL of the methyl red solution. The resulting solution should be red. NMT 0.1 mL of 0.02 M sodium hydroxide is required to change the color to yellow. A color change from red to yellow corresponds to a change in pH from pH 4.4 (red) to pH 6.0 (yellow).

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：為何使用高壓釜（Autoclave）

高壓釜在 121°C 下維持精確溫度是關鍵。這個溫度源自藥品滅菌的標準條件，也代表玻璃在「最惡劣使用條件」下的萃取情況。121°C 是玻璃/水界面反應的加速條件，可在短時間（30–60分鐘）內模擬玻璃容器整個使用週期的化學反應。

Carbon dioxide-free water（無二氧化碳水）的使用至關重要：CO₂ 溶於水會產生碳酸（H₂CO₃），影響 pH 值與滴定終點，造成假性「酸化」，干擾鹼釋出量的測定。

比喻說明 Analogy

製備無二氧化碳水就像咖啡師使用去礦物質水來沖咖啡——水的「純淨度」決定了風味的純正性。測試中任何外來離子（包括 CO₂）都是「雜訊」，必須去除，才能精確量測玻璃本身釋出的那極微量的鹼。

重點提示 Key Notes

甲基紅指示劑（Methyl red）的 pH 變色範圍是 4.4（紅色）到 6.0（黃色）。這個微酸性的滴定終點選擇是有意的：能偵測極微量的酸（0.02M HCl），意味著可以反向計算出被中和的極微量鹼（來自玻璃）。靈敏度驗證（sensitivity test）確保試劑配製正確，是 SOP 中必要的 QC 步驟。

永久磁鐵（permanent magnet）的使用：研磨玻璃時，鋼製研磨設備可能混入鐵粉。鐵粉（強酸性氧化物）會干擾滴定結果，因此必須在測試前用磁鐵去除。這個細節提醒我們：分析化學中每個步驟都有其科學依據。

本節重點回顧 Section Summary

  • USP 〈660〉 依水解耐性將玻璃分為 Type I（硼矽酸）、Type II（表面處理蘇打石灰矽）、Type III（蘇打石灰矽）三種性能類型，NP 為非注射劑用。
  • 性能判定採兩步驟測試：Glass Grains Test 判斷整體組成；Surface Glass Test 測定內表面耐性；Surface Etching Test 區分 Type I 與 Type II。
  • 測試使用無二氧化碳水與甲基紅指示劑進行酸鹼滴定，靈敏偵測極微量釋出鹼；搭配 NIST SRM 確保方法準確性。
  • Type I 或 Type II 水溶液注射劑容器另需進行可萃取砷測試（限度 0.1 µg/g）。
  • 玻璃容器的選擇直接影響藥品穩定性與患者安全，是 CDMO 容器相容性評估的核心起點。

1.1 Glass Grains Test — Sample Preparation | 玻璃粉末試驗：樣品製備

原文內容 Original Text

The Glass Grains Test may be performed either on the canes used for the manufacture of tubing glass containers or on the containers.

Sample Preparation

Rinse the containers to be tested with purified water, and dry in the oven. Wrap at least three of the glass articles in clean paper, and crush to produce two samples of about 100 g each in pieces NMT 30 mm across. Place in the mortar 30–40 g of the pieces between 10 and 30 mm across taken from one of the samples, insert the pestle, and strike it heavily with the hammer once only. Alternatively, transfer samples into a ball mill-breaker, add the balls, and crush the glass.

Transfer the contents of the mortar or ball mill to the coarsest sieve (No. 25) of the set. Repeat the operation until all fragments have been transferred to the sieve. Shake the set of sieves for a short time by hand, and remove the glass that remains on sieves No. 25 and No. 40. Submit these portions to further fracture, repeating the operation until about 10 g of glass remains on sieve No. 25. Reject this portion and the portion that passes through sieve No. 50. Reassemble the set of sieves, and shake for 5 min. Transfer to a weighing bottle the glass grains that passed through sieve No. 40 and are retained on sieve No. 50. Repeat the crushing and sieving procedure with the second glass sample until two samples of grains are obtained, each of which weighs more than 10 g.

Spread each sample on a piece of clean glazed paper, and remove any iron particles by passing the magnet over them. Transfer each sample into a beaker for cleaning. Add 30 mL of acetone to the grains in each beaker, and scour the grains, using suitable means such as a rubber-tipped or plastic-coated glass rod. After scouring the grains, allow to settle, and decant as much acetone as possible. Add another 30 mL of acetone, swirl, decant, and add a new portion of acetone. Fill the bath of the ultrasonic vessel with water at room temperature, then place the beaker in the rack, and immerse it until the level of the acetone is at the level of the water; apply the ultrasound for 1 min. Swirl the beaker, allow to settle, and decant the acetone as completely as possible; then repeat the ultrasonic cleaning operation. If a fine turbidity persists, repeat the ultrasonic cleaning and acetone washing until the solution remains clear. Swirl, and decant the acetone. Dry the grains, first by putting the beaker on a warm plate, then by heating at 140° for 20 min in a drying oven. Transfer the dried grains from each beaker into separate weighing bottles, insert the stoppers, and cool in a desiccator.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：粒徑篩選的科學依據

Glass Grains Test 使用 US Sieve No. 40（孔徑約 420 µm）與 No. 50（孔徑約 300 µm）之間的玻璃粉末。這個粒徑範圍的選定是為了：

  • 標準化比表面積（specific surface area）：粒徑相同，接觸水的面積就相同，才能讓不同批次的測試結果具有可比性。
  • 避免極端值：太細的粉末（通過 No. 50）比表面積過大，會過度萃取；太粗的顆粒（留在 No. 25）則萃取不足。

兩個獨立樣品（duplicate）必須各自超過 10 g，這是統計學最低要求，也是 Table 3 允許範圍計算的基礎。

比喻說明 Analogy

製備玻璃粉末的過程就像磨咖啡豆：你需要特定粗細的咖啡粉（不能太粗也不能太細）才能萃取出標準量的咖啡因。篩網就是「分級器」，確保每次「萃取」的接觸面積一致。用超音波清洗去除殘餘粉塵，就像咖啡師用清水沖洗濾網，確保上一批的殘留不污染本次測試。

重點提示 Key Notes

磁鐵去除鐵粉：鋼製研磨設備產生的鐵粉是強氧化物（Fe₂O₃），溶於水後呈酸性，會中和玻璃釋出的鹼，使結果偏低（假陰性）。這一步看似微小，卻是確保數據準確性的關鍵。

丙酮清洗（acetone washing）＋超音波：目的是去除玻璃表面的有機污染物（如研磨過程中的潤滑劑殘留）。有機物可能干擾甲基紅指示劑的顏色判斷，影響滴定終點。

140°C 乾燥 20 分鐘：去除殘餘水分。若樣品含水，10.00 g 的稱量就不準確，直接影響計算結果。

1.2 Glass Grains Test — Method, Titration & Limits | 玻璃粉末試驗：方法、滴定與限度

原文內容 Original Text

Method — Filling and Heating

Weigh 10.00 g of the cleaned and dried grains into two separate conical flasks. Pipet 50 mL of carbon dioxide-free purified water into each of the conical flasks (test solutions). Pipet 50 mL of carbon dioxide-free purified water into a third conical flask that will serve as a blank. Distribute the grains evenly over the flat bases of the flasks by shaking gently. Close the flasks with neutral glass dishes or aluminum foil rinsed with purified water or with inverted beakers so that the inner surfaces of the beakers fit snugly down onto the top rims of the flasks.

Place all three flasks in the autoclave containing the water at ambient temperature, and ensure that they are held above the level of the water in the vessel. Carry out the following operations:

  1. Insert the end of a calibrated thermometric device in a filled container through a hole of approximately the diameter of the thermocouple and connect it to an external measuring device.
  2. Close the autoclave door or lid securely but leave the vent-cock open.
  3. Start automatic recording of the temperature versus time, and heat the autoclave at a regular rate such that steam issues vigorously from the vent-cock after 20–30 min, and maintain a vigorous evolution of steam for a further 10 min.
  4. Close the vent-cock, and raise the temperature from 100° to 121° at a rate of 1°/min within 20–22 min.
  5. Maintain the temperature at 121 ± 1° for 30 ± 1 min from the time when the holding temperature is reached.
  6. Cool down to 100° at a rate of 0.5°/min, venting to prevent formation of a vacuum, within 40–44 min.
  7. Do not open the autoclave until it has cooled to 95°.
  8. Remove the hot samples from the autoclave using appropriate safety precautions, and cool the samples cautiously down to room temperature within 30 min, avoiding thermal shock.

Titration

To each of the three flasks add 0.05 mL of Methyl red solution. Titrate the blank solution immediately with 0.02 M hydrochloric acid, then titrate the test solutions until the color matches that obtained with the blank solution. Subtract the titration volume for the blank solution from that for the test solutions. Calculate the mean value of the results in mL of 0.02 M hydrochloric acid per g of the sample. Repeat the test if the highest and lowest observed values differ by more than the permissible range given in Table 3.

核心概念：高壓釜程序的精確控制

高壓釜的升溫、保溫、降溫速率都有嚴格規定，原因如下：

  • 排氣步驟（vent-cock）：先讓蒸汽大量排出 10 分鐘，是為了排除釜內的空氣（air pocket）。殘留空氣會導致蒸汽壓分布不均，造成溫度不一致。
  • 升溫速率 1°C/min（100→121°C）：避免樣品受到熱衝擊，確保萃取過程均勻。
  • 降溫速率 0.5°C/min：更慢的降溫防止玻璃因熱梯度而破裂，同時避免水溶液因快速降壓而沸騰（防止「假陽性」噴出）。
  • 95°C 才開門：保護操作人員安全，防止熱蒸汽傷害。

比喻說明 Analogy

高壓釜升降溫程序就像飛機起降：起飛（升溫）和降落（降溫）都需要穩定的爬升率，不能突然拉起或俯衝，否則乘客（樣品）會不適。「排氣」步驟就像打開飛機艙門前先平衡氣壓——不這樣做，門就會被氣壓「彈開」（真空或過壓）。

重點提示 Key Notes — Table 3 允許範圍的意義

Table 3 的允許範圍設計是相對值（非絕對值）：對於高耐性玻璃（Type I，結果≤0.1 mL/g），允許 25% 的變異，因為這些值本身極小，測量精度有限；對於低耐性玻璃（結果≥0.2 mL/g），只允許 10% 變異，因為數值較大，精確性要求也更高。

這是統計學「比例精度（proportional precision）」的體現——USP 並非要求所有情境相同的絕對誤差，而是根據數值大小調整可接受範圍。

實務應用 Practical Application

在 CDMO 的容器進料品管（incoming material control）中，每批玻璃容器入庫前都應進行 Glass Grains Test（或依 CoA 及廠商驗證記錄判定是否可跳過）。若 Type I 玻璃供應商提供的 CoA 顯示結果為 0.08 mL/g，但 CDMO 自測為 0.12 mL/g，兩者差異（50%）遠超 Table 3 的允許範圍（25%），應觸發調查（investigation）並通知供應商。

Determination of the Filling Volume

The filling volume is the volume of purified water to be added to the container for the purpose of the test. For vials, bottles, cartridges, and syringes, the filling volume is 90% of the brimful capacity. For ampules, it is the volume up to the height of the shoulder.

Vials and bottles: Select six dry vials or bottles from the sample lot, or three if their capacity exceeds 100 mL, and remove any dirt or debris. Weigh the empty containers with an accuracy of 0.1 g. Place the containers on a horizontal surface, and fill them with purified water to about the rim edge, avoiding overflow and the introduction of air bubbles. Adjust the liquid levels to the brimful line. Weigh the filled containers to obtain the mass of the water expressed to two decimal places, for containers having a nominal volume less than or equal to 30 mL, and expressed to one decimal place, for containers having a nominal volume greater than 30 mL. Calculate the mean value of the brimful capacity in mL, and multiply it by 0.9. This volume, expressed to one decimal place, is the filling volume for the particular container lot.

Cartridges and syringes: Select six dry syringes or cartridges, and seal the small opening (mouth of cartridges, Luer cone, or staked needle of syringes), using an inert material. Determine the mean brimful capacity and filling volume according to Vials and Bottles.

Ampules: Place at least six dry ampules on a flat, horizontal surface, and fill them with purified water from a buret until the water reaches point A, where the body of the ampule starts to decrease to the shoulder of the ampule (see Figure 2). Read the capacities, expressed to two decimal places, and calculate the mean value. This volume, expressed to one decimal place, is the filling volume for the particular ampule lot.

Titrate the test solution with the same acid until the color of the resulting solution is the same as that obtained for the blank. Subtract the value found for the blank titration from that found for the test solution, and express the results in mL of 0.01 M hydrochloric acid per 100 mL of test solution.

核心概念：填充體積 = 90% 滿充量

填充體積（filling volume）設定為滿充量（brimful capacity）的 90%，而非 100%，理由是：

  • 模擬實際藥品充填量（regulatory headspace requirement）：注射劑通常不充填至全滿，需留有頭頂空間（headspace）用於熱膨脹與凍乾。
  • 防止溢出：高壓釜加熱時水體積膨脹，90% 填充確保不會溢出而改變萃取液組成。

安瓿瓶（ampule）則以「肩部（shoulder）以下的容積」為填充體積，因其幾何形狀特殊，與瓶裝容器的充填邏輯不同。

重點提示 Key Notes

保溫時間差異：Glass Grains Test 保溫 30 分鐘，Surface Glass Test 保溫 60 分鐘。這是因為完整容器的萃取面是玻璃內表面（intact surface），其面積/質量比遠低於粉末，需要更長時間才能達到類似的萃取程度。

Table 5 的滴定次數設計：較大容器使用更多取樣量（100 mL）且需要 2–3 次滴定，是為了提高統計代表性，因為大容器的個體差異（manufacturing variability）較大。

容器合並（combine the liquids）：測試時將所有容器的萃取液合并混合，再取分液量（aliquot）進行滴定。這等於對該批次取平均值，而非依賴單個容器的結果，更準確地反映批次水平的水解耐性。

Surface Etching Test

The Surface Etching Test is used in addition to the Surface Glass Test when it is necessary to determine whether a container has been surface treated and/or to distinguish between Type I and Type II glass containers. Alternatively, the Glass Grains Test and Surface Glass Test may be used. The Surface Etching Test may be carried out either on unused samples or on samples used in the Surface Glass Test.

Method

Vials and bottles: The volumes of test solution required are shown in Table 5. Rinse the containers twice with purified water, fill to the brimful point with a mixture of one volume of hydrofluoric acid and nine volumes of hydrochloric acid, and allow to stand for 10 min. Empty the containers, and rinse carefully five times with purified water. Immediately before the test, rinse once again with purified water. Submit these containers to the same autoclaving and determination procedure as described in the Surface Glass Test. If the results are considerably higher than those obtained from the original surfaces (by a factor of about 5–10), the samples have been surface treated.

[Caution—Hydrofluoric acid is extremely aggressive. Even small quantities can cause life threatening injuries.]

Ampules, cartridges, and syringes: Apply the test method as described in Vials and Bottles. If the ampules, cartridges, and syringes are not surface treated, the values obtained are slightly lower than those obtained in the previous tests.

[Note—Ampules, cartridges, and syringes made from Type I glass tubing are not normally subjected to internal surface treatment.]

Distinction between Type I and Type II Glass Containers

The results obtained from the Surface Etching Test are compared to those obtained from the Surface Glass Test. For Type I glass containers, the values obtained are close to those found in the Surface Glass Test. For Type II glass containers, the values obtained greatly exceed those found in the Surface Glass Test; and they are similar to, but not greater than, those obtained for Type III containers of the same filling volume.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：Table 6 的設計邏輯

Table 6 顯示一個重要趨勢：容器越大，限度越嚴格（允許的 mL/100mL 越小）。乍看違反直覺，但原理是：

  • 小容器（如 1 mL 安瓿）的表面積/體積比（S/V ratio）遠高於大容器（500 mL 瓶）。
  • 相同的內表面品質，小容器萃取出的鹼相對於體積更多，因此限度「更寬鬆（以mL/100mL計）」。
  • 大容器表面積/體積比小，若每 100 mL 仍有很多鹼釋出，說明表面品質很差——因此限度更嚴。

Type III 的限度約是 Type I/II 的 10 倍，清楚顯示兩者水解耐性的量級差異。

比喻說明 Analogy — Surface Etching Test

Surface Etching Test 就像「剝洋蔥皮」：先用氫氟酸/鹽酸混合液「剝除」玻璃的表面處理層（就像剝去洋蔥外皮），然後再測試裡面的「真實組成」。若剝皮後結果大幅升高（5–10 倍），說明「洋蔥皮」（表面處理）貢獻了大量耐性——這就是 Type II 玻璃的特徵；若剝皮前後差異不大，說明耐性來自「洋蔥本體」（玻璃組成本身）——這是 Type I 的特徵。

氫氟酸（HF）警告：HF 是極危險的化學品，穿透皮膚後會在骨骼中持續造成傷害。任何使用 HF 的操作都必須在通風櫥（fume hood）中進行，並配戴特殊防護手套（非一般橡膠手套）。

重點提示 Key Notes

「5–10 倍」判斷標準：Surface Etching Test 後，若結果比原始 Surface Glass Test 高出 5–10 倍，代表表面處理（脫鹼處理）確實存在。這不是精確的計算限度，而是一個量級判斷（order of magnitude），允許實驗室一定的操作彈性。

安瓿/卡匣/預充針管通常不做內表面處理：這類容器由 Type I 玻璃管製成，本身化學穩定性已足夠，不需要二次表面處理。這說明 Surface Etching Test 主要用於瓶裝（vials/bottles）容器的 Type I vs. Type II 鑑別。

實務應用 Practical Application

在 CDMO 接受新供應商的玻璃容器時，若供應商 CoA 標示為「Type II treated soda-lime」，QC 應進行 Surface Etching Test 確認表面處理的存在。若表面處理在生產過程中因某種原因缺失（例如：去鹼爐溫度異常），Surface Glass Test 仍可能通過（因為測試本身靈敏度有限），但 Surface Etching Test 會揭露真相。這是供應商稽核（supplier audit）與年度複驗（annual requalification）的重點項目。

2.1 Impurities — Extractable Arsenic | 雜質：可萃取砷

原文內容 Original Text

IMPURITIES — Arsenic 〈211〉

Use as the Test Preparation 35 mL of the water from one Type I or Type II glass container, or, in the case of smaller containers, 35 mL of the combined contents of several Type I or Type II glass containers, prepared as directed in the Surface Glass Test. The limit does not exceed 0.1 µg/g.

This test applies only to Type I and Type II containers intended for aqueous parenteral products.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：為何只測砷（Arsenic）

玻璃容器的重金屬雜質關注清單中，砷（As）被 USP 〈660〉 單獨列出，主要理由：

  • 歷史原因：傳統玻璃製造工藝（特別是舊式蘇打石灰矽玻璃）曾使用含砷化合物作為澄清劑（fining agent），使玻璃更均勻透明。現代製程雖已大幅減少，但殘留風險仍存在。
  • 毒性：砷是確認的人類致癌物（IARC Group 1），即使微量也有累積毒性，對注射劑患者而言是不可接受的風險。
  • 釋出途徑：砷溶於水溶液的能力（特別在鹼性環境下），使其可從玻璃內表面萃取進入藥品——恰好就是 Surface Glass Test 的萃取液。

0.1 µg/g 限度以「萃取液重量（g）」而非體積（mL）計算，這是因為不同密度溶液的萃取液質量不同，以重量為基準更準確。

比喻說明 Analogy

砷測試就像飲用水的重金屬檢測——自來水管（玻璃容器）老化後，鉛或砷可能溶入水中。對於注射藥品，這個「水管」的品質要求比自來水管更嚴格，因為藥品直接進入血液，沒有腸胃道的過濾保護。0.1 µg/g 的限度反映了在無菌注射劑使用情境下的最嚴格安全標準。

重點提示 Key Notes

測試樣品來源：直接使用 Surface Glass Test 的萃取液（water from the Surface Glass Test），而非另行製備。這意味著砷測試是 Surface Glass Test 的「附加測試」，不需要額外的高壓釜程序，非常節省時間與資源。

適用對象：僅限 Type I 或 Type II 水溶液注射劑容器。Type III 容器由於不適用於注射劑，不需要此測試。非注射劑用途的 Type I/II 容器也不強制要求。

引用章節 〈211〉：USP 〈211〉 Arsenic 是通則（General Test）中的砷測定法，〈660〉 直接引用其方法，體現 USP 體系的模組化設計——專用測試方法不重複撰寫，而是交叉引用。

實務應用 Practical Application

FUNCTIONALITY — Spectral Transmission for Colored Glass Containers

Apparatus

A UV-Vis spectrophotometer, equipped with either a photodiode detector or a photomultiplier tube coupled with an integrating sphere

Sample Preparation

Break the glass container or cut it with a circular saw fitted with a wet abrasive wheel, such as a carborundum or a bonded diamond wheel. Select sections representative of the wall thickness, and trim them as suitable for mounting in a spectrophotometer. After cutting, wash and dry each specimen, taking care to avoid scratching the surfaces. If the specimen is too small to cover the opening in the specimen holder, mask the uncovered portion of the opening with opaque paper or tape, provided that the length of the specimen is greater than that of the slit. Before placing in the holder, wash, dry, and wipe the specimen with lens tissue. Mount the specimen with the aid of wax, or by other convenient means, taking care to avoid leaving fingerprints or other marks.

Method

Place the specimen in the spectrophotometer with its cylindrical axis parallel to the slit and in such a way that the light beam is perpendicular to the surface of the section and the losses due to reflection are at a minimum. Measure the transmission of the specimen with reference to air in the spectral region of 290–450 nm, continuously or at intervals of 20 nm.

Limits

The observed spectral transmission for colored glass containers for products for nonparenteral use does not exceed 10% at any wavelength in the range of 290–450 nm, irrespective of the type and capacity of the glass container.

The observed spectral transmission in colored glass containers for parenteral products does not exceed the limits given in Table 7.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：為何需要光譜透射率測試

許多藥物對紫外線（UV）敏感，特別是在 290–450 nm（UV-A 至近可見光）範圍內：

  • 維生素（如核黃素、葉酸）：UV 照射導致降解。
  • 蛋白質生物製劑：光照可誘發聚集（aggregation）與氧化。
  • 某些抗生素、抗腫瘤藥物：光化學降解產生有毒副產品。

有色玻璃（amber glass，琥珀色）透過添加氧化鐵（Fe₂O₃）、氧化錳（MnO₂）等金屬氧化物呈現棕/琥珀色，可吸收 UV 光，但其保護效果因容器壁厚、顏色深淺而異，因此需要量化測試——這就是光譜透射率測試的目的。

比喻說明 Analogy

光譜透射率測試就像太陽眼鏡的 UV 防護測試：不是所有深色鏡片都能有效擋 UV——顏色深淺是視覺判斷，但真正的防護能力需要儀器測量（透射率≤10%）。藥廠的琥珀色玻璃瓶就是藥品的「太陽眼鏡」，USP 〈660〉 確保這副眼鏡真的能擋住傷害藥品的「UV 光線」。

積分球（integrating sphere）的作用：曲面玻璃（容器壁切片）會散射光線，普通分光光度計無法完整收集所有透射光。積分球將散射光全部收集後送入探測器，確保測量結果代表「實際透射率」而非只有直射光分量。

（因為藥品有腸胃屏障，安全餘裕較大）

重點提示 Key Notes

測量範圍 290–450 nm：低於 290 nm 的 UV 在大氣層被臭氧層吸收，實際上不會到達藥品；450 nm 以上進入可見光範圍（藍光），對多數藥物無顯著光降解作用。290–450 nm 是最危險的「光化學活性窗口」。

「不超過 10% 在任何波長（at any wavelength）」：這是最嚴格的表述方式。測試時若在 320 nm 處透射率為 8%，但在 380 nm 處為 12%，則不合格，因為任一波長超過限度就判為失敗。不允許「平均值合格」的解釋。

使用不透明外包裝豁免：透明玻璃容器若放入不透明外包裝（如紙盒），依 〈659〉 的「光保護容器」定義，可豁免光譜透射率測試要求。這在選材決策上給了 CDMO 一個替代路徑。

實務應用 Practical Application

在 CDMO 開發光敏性生物製劑（如 ADC 抗體藥物偶聯物）的 2 mL 注射劑時，選擇有色玻璃瓶（蓋封型）時，Table 7 要求透射率 ≤ 15%（2–5 mL，有蓋容器）。若供應商 CoA 上的測試結果為「在 350 nm 達到 18%」，則不符合規格，必須更換供應商或要求供應商改善配方（增加金屬氧化物含量）。

此外，在穩定性研究（ICH Q1B，光穩定性）中，使用不符合規格的琥珀色瓶可能導致光穩定性試驗失敗，進而影響上市申請（NDA/BLA）的時程——這是 CDMO 容器選材風險管理的真實場景。

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：USP 〈660〉 在整個 GMP 體系中的定位

USP 〈660〉 是藥用玻璃容器的「基礎合規工具」。理解其與其他文件的關係非常重要：

  • 〈660〉 vs 〈1660〉：〈660〉 是強制性規格測試（compulsory testing）；〈1660〉 是指引性評估方法（informational/guidance），包含脫層（delamination）風險評估——後者對現代生物製劑的容器相容性研究尤為重要。
  • 〈660〉 vs ICH Q3E：ICH Q3E（可萃取物與浸出物指引）範疇更廣，涵蓋有機可萃取物；〈660〉 的化學測試主要針對無機鹼與重金屬。兩者互補。
  • 〈660〉 vs FDA Container Guidance：FDA 的「Container Closure Systems for Packaging Human Drugs and Biologics」引導企業如何用 〈660〉 的數據支持 NDA/ANDA/BLA 申請。

比喻說明 — 玻璃測試的完整架構

USP 〈660〉 的測試架構像一個三層篩選系統：第一層是「材料鑑定」（Glass Grains Test——你用的是什麼材料？）；第二層是「成品表面品質」（Surface Glass Test——你的容器實際接觸面怎麼樣？）；第三層是「功能性驗證」（Spectral Transmission——你的容器能保護藥品不受光線破壞嗎？）。砷測試則是「安全底線」——確認容器不會主動傷害患者。

重點提示 — IRA 修訂版本說明

本章文字中反覆出現的「▲ (IRA 1-Oct-2023)」標記，代表 Interim Revision Announcement（臨時修訂公告），自 2023 年 10 月 1 日起正式生效。這些修訂主要是文字澄清（clarification），將「glass type（玻璃類型）」的說法統一為「glass performance（玻璃性能）」——強調測試結果是基於「性能表現」的分類，而非純粹的「化學成分」分類。這個細微差異對 Type II 玻璃特別重要：Type II 的「高性能」來自表面處理，而非玻璃本體組成。
