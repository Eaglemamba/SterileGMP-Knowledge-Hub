Introduction — 導論

原文內容 Original Text

Good practices in a microbiology laboratory consist of activities that depend on several principles: aseptic technique, control of media, control of test strains, operation and control of equipment, diligent recording and evaluation of data, and training of the laboratory staff in related competencies.

Because of the inherent risk of variability in microbiology data, reliability and reproducibility are dependent on the use of accepted methods and adherence to good practices in the laboratory. This chapter does not cover the unique and specific laboratory activities for virology and mycoplasma recovery and detection.

"Good practices in a microbiology laboratory consist of activities that depend on several principles: aseptic technique, control of media, control of test strains, operation and control of equipment, diligent recording and evaluation of data, and training of the laboratory staff."

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：微生物實驗室的六大支柱

USP ⟨1117⟩ 開宗明義列出微生物實驗室良好規範的六大基礎：

  • 無菌技術（Aseptic Technique） — 避免外源性污染進入測試系統
  • 培養基管控 — 從製備到儲存到品管的全生命週期管理
  • 測試菌株管控 — 確保菌株身份、純度與活性一致
  • 設備操作與管控 — 確認（Qualification）、校準（Calibration）、維護
  • 數據記錄與評估 — 完整、準確、可追溯
  • 人員訓練 — 專業能力（Competency）為基礎的培訓體系

這六大支柱就是本章的骨架，後續所有內容都是在這些原則上的展開。

比喻說明

廚房管理的比喻：微生物實驗室就像一間高級餐廳的廚房。你需要乾淨的食材（培養基）、經過驗證的標準菜譜（菌株）、校準過的烤箱和爐具（設備）、嚴謹的出菜記錄（數據）、以及訓練有素的廚師（人員）。少了任何一環，端出來的菜（測試結果）就不可靠。

重點提示

適用範圍限制：本章不涵蓋病毒學和支原體（Mycoplasma）檢測的特殊操作 — 這些領域有更專門的指導文件。

Media Preparation — 培養基製備

原文內容 Original Text

Culture media are the basis for most microbiological tests. Culture media may be either prepared in-house or obtained commercially. The term “prepared media” used in this chapter covers both these types of media. Safeguarding the quality of the media is therefore critical to the success of the microbiology laboratory.

It is important to choose the correct media or components in making media based on the use of accepted sources or references for formulas. The manufacturer’s formula and instructions for preparation routinely accompany dehydrated and ready-made media.

Water is the universal diluent for microbiological media. Purified Water is most often used for media preparation, but in certain cases the use of deionized or distilled water may be appropriate. Water of lesser quality should not be used.

Consistent preparation of media requires accurate weighing of dehydrated media or media constituents. A calibrated balance with the appropriate weight range for the ingredients should be used. Clean weighing containers and tools (such as spatulas) should be used to prevent foreign substances from entering the formulation.

Dehydrated media should be thoroughly dissolved in water before dispensing and sterilization. If heating is necessary to help dissolve the media, care should be taken not to overheat the media because all culture media, to a greater or lesser extent, are heat-sensitive. Darkening of media (Maillard-type reaction or nonenzymatic browning) is a general indication of overheating.

Sterilization of media should be performed within the parameters provided by the manufacturer or validated by the user. Autoclaving by moist heat is the preferred sterilization technique. Typically, manufacturers recommend an autoclave cycle of 121° for 15 min using a validated autoclave. These conditions apply to time at temperature of the media.

The pH of each batch of medium should be confirmed after it has cooled to room temperature (20°–25°). The pH of media should be in a range of ±0.2 of the value indicated by the manufacturer unless a wider range is acceptable by the validated method.

Prepared media should be checked by appropriate inspection for the following quality and integrity parameters:

  • Cracked containers or lids
  • Unequal filling of containers
  • Dehydration resulting in cracks or dimpled surfaces
  • Hemolysis
  • Excessive darkening or color change
  • Crystal formation from possible freezing
  • Excessive number of bubbles
  • Status of redox indicators (if appropriate)
  • Lot number and expiration date
  • Sterility
  • Cleanliness of plates (lid should not stick to dish)

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：培養基製備的五大關鍵步驟

培養基是微生物測試的根基。品質不合格的培養基會導致假陰性（漏掉微生物）或假陽性（外源污染），兩者都是災難。

  1. 選擇正確配方 — 依據公認來源或製造商說明書
  2. 精確秤量 — 使用校準天平，記錄用水量和成分重量
  3. 正確溶解 — 充分溶解但避免過度加熱（Maillard 反應 = 培養基變深色 = 品質受損）
  4. 適當滅菌 — 121°C / 15 min 是標準條件，但需根據容器大小和裝載量調整
  5. 確認 pH — 冷卻至室溫後測量，允許 ±0.2 的偏差範圍

比喻說明

烘焙蛋糕的比喻：製備培養基就像烘焙 — 材料比例要精確（秤量），麵糊要完全混合（溶解），烤箱溫度和時間要對（滅菌），出爐後要確認成品狀態（pH 和外觀檢查）。烤過頭蛋糕會焦（Maillard 反應），烤不夠則不熟（未滅菌）。兩種情況下蛋糕（培養基）都不能用。

重點提示：CDMO 實務考量

水質要求：USP 明確指出應使用純化水（Purified Water），不得使用品質較差的水。在 CDMO 環境中，培養基製備用水通常來自與製造用水相同的純水系統，需要有持續的水質監測數據支持。

滅菌週期驗證：「121°C / 15 min」是培養基本身的溫度時間，而非滅菌鍋的設定時間。大容量或大裝載量需要更長的升溫時間，因此實際滅菌週期可能比 15 分鐘長得多。這是稽查中常被檢查的重點。

外觀檢查清單：USP 列出的 11 項外觀檢查項目應轉化為你們實驗室的標準檢查 SOP。每一批製備培養基都應有記錄證明完成了這些檢查。

練習思考

  1. 假設你發現一批 TSA 培養基的顏色明顯比平常深，pH 仍在規格內。你會放行使用嗎？為什麼？你需要執行哪些額外測試？
  2. 為什麼 USP 要求 pH 在冷卻到室溫後才測量？如果在培養基還是 50°C 時測量 pH，結果會有什麼偏差？
Media Storage — 培養基儲存

原文內容 Original Text

It is prudent to understand how the manufacturer or supplier transports and stores media before distribution to the end user. Manufacturers of media should use transport and storage conditions that minimize the loss of moisture, control the temperature, prevent microbial contamination, and provide mechanical protection to the prepared media.

Media should be labeled properly with batch or lot numbers, preparation and expiration dates, and media identification. Media should be stored according to the manufacturer’s instructions. Media prepared in-house should be stored under validated conditions.

Do not store media plates containing agar at or below 0°, as freezing could damage the gel structure. Protect stored media from exposure to light, excessive temperature, and temperature changes, as this may cause condensation.

Remelting of an original container of solid media, if not containing heat-labile components, should be performed only once to avoid media whose quality is compromised by overheating or potential contamination.

It is recommended that molten agar medium be held in a monitored water bath until it has been tempered to 45°–50°, and not further held for more than 8 h, or as determined appropriate for the medium composition.

Disposal of used cultured media (as well as expired media) should follow local biological hazard safety procedures.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：培養基儲存的三個關鍵限制

  • 溫度下限：含瓊脂培養基不得儲存在 0°C 以下 — 結凍會破壞凝膠結構，影響微生物回收率
  • 再融化次數：固態培養基最多只能再融化一次。每次加熱都會降解營養成分和選擇性試劑
  • 熔融保持時間：在 45°–50°C 水浴中保持的熔融瓊脂不應超過 8 小時 — 超過此時間培養基品質會劣化

比喻說明

冰淇淋的比喻：你有沒有把冰淇淋拿出冷凍庫放到融化，然後又放回去再凍？再拿出來的時候口感完全不一樣了 — 冰晶變大、質地粗糙。培養基也是一樣：凍結再融化會改變瓊脂的微結構，影響表面平整度和微生物附著能力。所以 USP 說「再融化最多一次」，跟你不應該反覆凍融冰淇淋是同一個道理。

重點提示

供應商運輸條件：USP 特別提到要了解供應商如何運輸和儲存培養基 — 這在 CDMO 的供應商管理（Supplier Oversight）中是一個常被忽略但很重要的稽查點。如果夏天高溫運輸導致培養基品質下降，再好的實驗室操作也無法補救。

倒培養基的水浴風險：從水浴中取出容器倒培養基時，外壁的水可能會混入無菌培養基。USP 建議在倒之前先將容器外部擦乾 — 這是一個簡單但經常被忽略的細節。

練習思考

  1. 你的實驗室技術員在週五下午將培養基融化後放入水浴，然後週一早上才來倒盤。這超過了 8 小時的限制。你會如何處理這批培養基？需要啟動偏差調查嗎？
  2. 如何建立培養基的有效期驗證方案？你會測試哪些參數、在哪些時間點取樣？
Quality Control Testing — 品質管制測試

原文內容 Original Text

Many laboratories, for convenience, use dehydrated media or purchase commercially prepared media. Manufacturers of media attempt to standardize raw materials from biological sources but must constantly deal with unavoidable differences in raw materials obtained from natural sources, and therefore lot-to-lot variability of media must be considered.

Quality control tests should be performed on all prepared media, including media associated with swabs or media in strips. Tests routinely performed on in-house prepared media should include pH, growth promotion, inhibition, and indicative properties (as appropriate).

A satisfactory growth-promotion outcome includes growth that is visibly comparable to that obtained with a previously tested and approved batch of medium. This is for a qualitative comparison. If quantitative comparison is desired, follow guidance in Validation of Microbial Recovery from Pharmacopeial Articles ⟨1227⟩; the medium should be of the same formula, consistency, and volume.

When a batch of media does not meet the requirements of growth-promotion testing, an investigation should be initiated to identify the cause. Any batch of media that fails growth-promotion testing is unsuitable for use. [Note—Failed growth-promotion test results may not be used to negate positive test results.]

Special care should be taken with media that are used in sterility tests (see ⟨71⟩) and in environmental monitoring studies. Media used for environmental monitoring of critical areas should preferably be double wrapped and terminally sterilized. If terminal sterilization is not performed, media should be subjected to 100% pre-incubation and inspection before use within a critical area.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：培養基品管的四大測試

  1. pH 測試 — 確認在製造商規定的 ±0.2 範圍內
  2. 生長促進試驗（Growth Promotion） — 證明培養基能支持目標微生物的生長
  3. 抑制性測試（Inhibition） — 選擇性培養基應能抑制非目標微生物
  4. 指示性測試（Indicative Properties） — 鑑別培養基應能正確顯示鑑別反應

關鍵原則：生長促進試驗失敗的培養基絕對不得使用。而且，失敗的生長促進結果不能用來否定已經用該批培養基得到的陽性結果。這句話的含義很深 — 即使培養基「有問題」，如果已經長出微生物，那個結果仍然有效。

比喻說明

體溫計的比喻：想像你用一支溫度計量體溫，結果顯示發燒 39°C。後來你發現這支溫度計的品管不合格。你能因此就說「我沒有發燒」嗎？當然不行 — 溫度計可能量不準，但量到的高溫仍然是一個警訊。同理，生長促進失敗的培養基可能回收率低（漏掉微生物），但如果它還是長出了東西，那個陽性結果更不能忽略。

重點提示：環境監測用培養基的特殊要求

用於關鍵區域環境監測的培養基有更嚴格的要求：

  • 首選：雙層包裝 + 終端滅菌（Double wrapped & terminally sterilized）
  • 如無終端滅菌：必須 100% 預培養（Pre-incubation）並檢查無菌後才能進入關鍵區域
  • 額外要求：需驗證預培養不影響回收率（Growth-promotion testing after pre-incubation）

在 CDMO 環境中，環境監測培養基的管控是 FDA 稽查的高頻檢查項目。確保你的 SOP 明確規定了進入潔淨區前的培養基處理流程。

練習思考

  1. 你的實驗室使用商購預製培養基。供應商提供了 CoA 證明已通過生長促進試驗。你是否仍需在進料時執行生長促進試驗？為什麼？
  2. 一批用於環境監測的 TSA 接觸碟在預培養後發現 2 個平板有汙染。你會如何處理這批培養基？需要通報哪些部門？
Microbiological Media Incubation Times — 微生物培養基培養時間

原文內容 Original Text

Incubation times for microbiological tests with a duration of less than 3 days should be expressed in hours: e.g., “Incubate at 30°–35° for 18–72 h”. Tests with a duration longer than 72 h should be expressed in days: e.g., “Incubate at 30°–35° for 3–5 days”.

For tests with incubation times expressed in hours, incubate for the minimum specified time and exercise good microbiological judgment when exceeding that time. For tests with incubation times expressed in days, incubations started in the morning or afternoon should generally be concluded at that same time of day in the morning or afternoon, respectively.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：培養時間的兩套規則

情境單位規則範例
測試 < 3 天小時培養至少達到最短指定時間“Incubate 18–72 h”
測試 > 72 h天上午開始就上午結束，下午開始就下午結束“Incubate 3–5 days”

比喻說明

烹飪計時的比喻：就像烹飪一樣 — 煮水煮蛋你會用分鐘計時（6 分鐘溏心、10 分鐘全熟），但滷肉你會說「滷一天」而不會精確到幾小時幾分鐘。微生物培養也是這樣：短期測試需要精確到小時，長期測試則以天為單位，但要保持「同一時段開始、同一時段結束」的一致性。

重點提示

為什麼要區分小時和天？這個規定看似簡單，但解決了一個很實際的問題：如果 SOP 寫「培養 3–5 天」，而分析員在週一上午 9 點開始培養，那麼「第 3 天」是到週三上午 9 點還是到週三結束？USP 的答案是：上午開始就在上午結束（第 3–5 天的上午讀取結果）。這消除了不同分析員之間的解讀差異。

練習思考

  1. 你的 SOP 規定環境監測培養基的培養時間為「3–5 天，30°–35°C」。一位分析員在週三下午 3 點開始培養。最早可以在什麼時候讀取結果？最遲應該在什麼時候讀取？
  2. 如果一位分析員在以小時表示的測試中，在最短培養時間之前就讀取了結果（例如 18–72 h 的測試在 16 小時就讀取），這構成偏差嗎？需要做什麼處理？

  • 培養基製備：正確選配方、精確秤量、充分溶解（避免過熱）、驗證滅菌週期（121°C/15 min 是培養基溫度）、冷卻後確認 pH（±0.2 範圍）

  • 環境監測培養基：雙層包裝終端滅菌優先；否則 100% 預培養 + 驗證預培養不影響回收率
  • 培養時間：<3 天用小時表示（至少培養最短時間）；>72 h 用天表示（同一時段開始結束）

Maintenance of Microbiological Cultures — 微生物菌株維護

原文內容 Original Text

Biological specimens can be the most delicate standards to manage because their viability and characteristics are dependent on adequate handling and storage. Standardizing the handling and storage of cultures by the user laboratory should be done in a way that will minimize the opportunity for contamination or alteration of growth characteristics.

Cultures for use in compendial tests should be acquired from a national culture collection or a qualified secondary supplier and have documented equivalency to relevant ATCC strains. They can be acquired frozen, freeze-dried, on slants, or in ready-to-use forms. Confirmation of the purity of the culture and the identity of the culture should be performed before its use in quality control testing. The confirmation of identity for commonly used laboratory strains ideally should be done at the level of genus and species.

Preparation and resuscitation of cultures should follow the instructions of the supplier or a validated, established method. The “seed-lot technique” is recommended for storage of stock cultures.

The original sample from the national culture collection or a qualified secondary supplier is resuscitated and grown in an appropriate medium. Aliquots of this stock culture (the first transfer or passage) are suspended in a cryoprotective medium, transferred to vials, and frozen at −30° or below until use. If stored at −70° or below, or in lyophilized form, strains may be kept indefinitely.

Once opened, do not refreeze unused cell suspensions after culturing a working suspension. The unused portion should be discarded to minimize the risk of loss of viability and contamination of the stock.

The number of transfers of working control cultures should be tracked to prevent excessive subculturing that increases the risk of phenotypic alteration or mutation. One passage is defined as the transfer of organisms from a viable culture to a fresh medium with growth of the microorganisms. Any form of subculturing is considered to be a transfer/passage.

核心概念：種子批系統（Seed-Lot Technique）

這是管理微生物標準菌株的黃金標準方法，與疫苗生產中的細胞庫系統（Cell Bank System）原理相同：

國家菌種中心（ATCC 等）
↓ 復甦培養
主庫 Stock Culture（第 1 代 / P1）
↓ 分裝冷凍（−30°C 以下）
工作庫 Working Culture（日常使用）

關鍵規則：

  • −30°C 以下可保存數月至數年
  • −70°C 以下或冷凍乾燥可「無限期」保存
  • 解凍後的菌株不得再凍回去 — 用不完就丟棄
  • 每次從主庫取出一管，復甦後作為工作庫使用

比喻說明

家族傳承的比喻：想像你有一份祖傳食譜的原始手稿（ATCC 原始菌株）。你先影印了幾份放在保險箱裡（主庫），然後從保險箱取出一份再影印來日常使用（工作庫）。你絕對不會拿原始手稿來日常使用（會磨損），也不會用已經影印了很多代的模糊副本來做重要的事（表型變異風險）。每次需要時，都回到保險箱取一份清晰的影本來使用。

重點提示

ATCC 等效性要求：2022 年修訂版新增了一個重要要求 — 菌株必須有「documented equivalency to relevant ATCC strains」。這意味著如果你從非 ATCC 的供應商購買菌株，你需要有文件證明該菌株與 ATCC 參考菌株的等效性。

身份確認層級：USP 建議對常用菌株的身份確認至少要做到屬（Genus）和種（Species）的水平。在 CDMO 環境中，這通常意味著需要使用分子生物學方法（如 16S rRNA 定序）或質譜方法（如 MALDI-TOF）來確認菌株身份，而不僅僅依賴生化鑑定。

繼代次數追蹤：過度繼代培養會導致菌株的表型改變（例如抗藥性降低、生長速度改變），使其不再適合作為品管測試的標準菌株。具體的允許繼代次數通常在各個測試的 USP 章節中有規定（通常不超過 5 代）。

練習思考

  1. 你的實驗室從一家歐洲供應商購買了 Staphylococcus aureus ATCC 6538 的即用型菌株。進料時你需要做哪些確認測試？最低要求是什麼？
  2. 一位技術員不小心將已解凍使用過的菌株又放回了 −70°C 冰箱。你發現後應該如何處理？這需要啟動偏差調查嗎？
  3. 為什麼 USP 強調「任何形式的繼代培養都算一次 passage」？如果你將一管菌液接種到瓊脂斜面上，然後從斜面上刮取菌落接種到液體培養基，這算幾次 passage？
Laboratory Equipment — 實驗室設備

原文內容 Original Text

Most equipment (incubators, water baths, and autoclaves) are subject to standard validation practices of installation qualification, operational qualification, and performance qualification. Some useful guidance can be found in Analytical Instrument Qualification ⟨1058⟩. Additionally, periodic calibration (generally annually or on a frequency based on a risk assessment) is commonly required.

New equipment, critical to the operation of the laboratory, should be qualified according to a protocol approved by the quality assurance unit (QAU). In addition, regular cleaning and sanitization of equipment such as incubators, refrigerators, and water baths should be performed to minimize the potential for contamination in the laboratory. Door seals of incubators and refrigerators should be cleaned and checked for state of repair. These activities should be incorporated into the equipment preventative maintenance program.

Instruments (pH meters and spectrophotometers) used in a microbiology laboratory should be calibrated on a regular schedule and tested to verify performance on a routine basis. Laboratory equipment with software should be evaluated for maintaining data integrity (21 CFR, Part 11).

Equipment that is difficult to sanitize (such as refrigerators and incubators) should be segregated from aseptic operations (such as storage of media for testing and incubation of sterility test samples) and live culture operations to minimize the potential for inadvertent contamination of the tests.

Autoclaves are central to the operation of the laboratory and must have proper validation in place to demonstrate adequate sterilization for a variety of operations. Autoclave resources must be available (and validated) to sterilize waste media (if performed in that laboratory) as well as the media prepared in that laboratory. The choice of one or several autoclaves is not driven by a need to separate aseptic and live operations (everything in the properly maintained autoclave is sterile after the cycle), but rather driven by resource considerations.

核心概念：設備管理的三層架構

層級活動頻率目的
確認（Qualification）IQ / OQ / PQ新設備安裝或重大變更時證明設備適合其預期用途

比喻說明

汽車保養的比喻：買新車時需要驗車（IQ/OQ/PQ），之後每年要做定期保養和年檢（校準），平時還要洗車和檢查輪胎氣壓（日常維護）。微生物實驗室的設備管理也是同樣的三層架構。少了任何一層，設備的可靠性就無法保證，產出的數據也就不可信。

重點提示：設備隔離原則

USP ⟨1117⟩ 提出了一個重要的設備使用原則：

  • 難以消毒的設備（冰箱、培養箱）：必須將「無菌操作用」和「活菌操作用」的設備分開
  • 滅菌鍋：不需要分開 — 因為經過正確驗證的滅菌週期後，所有內容物都是無菌的

這個區分很重要：培養箱和冰箱內部的表面可能被之前放入的活菌樣品汙染，而這些汙染物可能轉移到後續放入的無菌測試培養基上。但滅菌鍋不存在這個問題，因為每次使用都會徹底滅菌。

21 CFR Part 11：2022 年修訂版特別新增了對設備軟體數據完整性的要求。在 CDMO 環境中，這意味著培養箱的溫度記錄器、滅菌鍋的控制軟體等都需要符合電子記錄法規要求（審計追蹤、電子簽名、存取控制等）。

練習思考

  1. 你的實驗室只有一台培養箱，無法將無菌測試樣品和環境監測樣品分開培養。你能想到什麼替代措施來降低交叉汙染風險嗎？
  2. USP 說滅菌鍋不需要區分「乾淨」和「活菌」操作。但有些公司仍然使用專門的滅菌鍋來處理生物廢棄物。你認為這是過度管控還是有合理的理由？
  3. 一台培養箱的校準頻率目前設定為每年一次。基於風險評估，什麼情況下你會建議增加校準頻率？
Using Modern Technologies — 現代技術應用

原文內容 Original Text

The use of new technologies in a microbiology laboratory requires new learning and training to ensure a proper implementation in the laboratory (for examples, see Validation of Alternative Microbiological Methods ⟨1223⟩, Microbial Characterization, Identification, and Strain Typing ⟨1113⟩, and Rapid Microbial Tests for Release of Sterile Short-Life Products ⟨1071⟩).

For each new technology, it is valuable to develop rules and principles that will build consistency and accuracy when laboratory personnel are using that technology to perform the relevant procedure. The rules and principles should be included in the written procedure.

核心概念：新技術導入的三大要素

重點提示

SOP 中的規則與原則：USP 特別強調每項新技術都應制定專屬的「規則與原則」並納入 SOP。例如：MALDI-TOF 鑑定的 SOP 應包含：最低可接受評分閾值是多少？評分不足時的處理流程？樣品製備的標準化步驟？這些都是確保不同操作人員得到一致結果的關鍵。

Laboratory Layout and Operations — 實驗室佈局與操作

原文內容 Original Text

Laboratory layout and design should carefully consider the requirements of good practices in a microbiology laboratory and laboratory safety. It is essential that cross-contamination of microbial cultures or DNA/RNA samples for PCR testing be minimized to the greatest extent possible.

In general, a laboratory should be divided into clean or aseptic areas and live culture areas. Areas in which environmental and other microbiological test samples are handled and incubated should be maintained completely free of live cultures, if possible. If complete separation of live and clean culture zones cannot be accomplished, then other barriers and aseptic practices should be employed to reduce the likelihood of accidental contamination.

When growth is detected, the sample should be taken from the clean section of the laboratory to the live culture section without undue delay. Subculturing, staining, microbial identification, or other investigational operations should be undertaken in the live culture section.

Staff engaged in sampling activities should not enter or work in the live culture handling section unless special precautions are taken, including wearing dedicated protective clothing that should not be worn outside the laboratory and gloves and carefully washing and sanitizing hands upon exiting.

All testing in laboratories used for critical testing procedures—such as sterility testing of final dosage forms, bulk product, seed cultures for biological production or PCR testing of cell cultures—should be performed under controlled conditions. Sterility tests should preferably be carried out in an isolator with ISO 5 classification.

For less-critical microbiological testing, such as with nonsterile product or intermediate bioburden samples, testing under a unidirectional airflow (UDAF) cabinet, or as applicable is preferable. During qualification, the UDAF air must meet the air quality grade for which it is being qualified.

核心概念：實驗室兩區分離原則

微生物實驗室的佈局核心是將操作區域分為兩個截然不同的區域：

區域用途典型操作汙染風險
乾淨區 / 無菌區處理未知樣品環境監測讀盤、生物負載測試、無菌試驗假陽性（外源汙染）
活菌區處理已知/活菌繼代培養、鑑定、革蘭氏染色、生化試驗交叉汙染到乾淨區

單向流動原則：樣品一旦在乾淨區發現有生長，就「單向」移到活菌區做後續分析，不再回到乾淨區。含有生長菌落的樣品絕不應在乾淨區打開。

比喻說明

醫院感染管控的比喻：就像醫院把感染病房和普通病房分開一樣。微生物實驗室也需要這種「區域管控」。如果你剛在感染病房（活菌區）照顧過有傳染性的病人，不能穿著同一套衣服直接走進手術室（乾淨區）去做無菌手術。同樣的，實驗室人員從活菌區出來必須更換防護衣、洗手消毒後才能進入乾淨區。

重點提示

PCR 區域隔離（2022 新增）：2022 年修訂版特別新增了 DNA/RNA 樣品和 PCR 測試的隔離要求。PCR 對 DNA 汙染極度敏感 — 即使極微量的 DNA 也會被擴增產生假陽性。在 CDMO 環境中，如果你使用支原體 PCR 檢測法，PCR 操作區域必須與常規微生物實驗室完全隔離。

無菌試驗的環境要求：USP 明確建議無菌試驗優先使用隔離器（Isolator），因為隔離器的環境汙染水平低於有人操作的潔淨室。對 CDMO 而言，投資隔離器不僅降低假陽性風險，也在稽查時展示了對無菌保證的承諾。

練習思考

  1. 你的實驗室空間有限，無法實現乾淨區和活菌區的完全物理隔離。你會設計哪些「替代屏障措施」來降低交叉汙染風險？
  2. 一位環境監測取樣員在取完無菌製程區的培養碟後，需要將碟子送回實驗室培養。途中他必須經過活菌操作區的走廊。你認為這有風險嗎？如何改善？
Sample Handling — 樣品處理

原文內容 Original Text

Viable microorganisms in most microbiology samples—particularly water, environmental monitoring, and bioburden samples—are sensitive to handling and storage conditions. Critical parameters in these conditions include product (or sample) composition, container composition, time of storage, and temperature of storage.

It is important to minimize the amount of time between the sampling event and the initiation of testing and to control, as much as possible, the conditions of storage. Microbiological samples should never be stored in a frozen state before testing because this practice leads to loss of cell viability.

When monitoring water or bioburden prior to bioburden-reducing steps or sterilizing steps, samples may be held at 2°–8° for up to 24 h from the time of sample collection until the start of the analysis. When testing within 24 h is not possible (e.g., when contract laboratories are used), the actual maximum hold time between collection and testing should be supported by experimental studies (e.g., challenge tests).

If environmental monitoring samples cannot be incubated within a reasonable time frame (e.g., due to the use of an off-site testing laboratory), the time from sample collection to the start of incubation should be supported with experimental data.

All microbiological samples should be taken using aseptic techniques, including those taken in support of nonsterile products.

Samples submitted to the microbiology laboratory should be accompanied by documentation detailing source of the sample, date the sample was taken, date of sample submission, person or department responsible, storage conditions, and any potentially hazardous materials associated with the sample.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：樣品處理的時間-溫度管控

微生物樣品中的活菌數量會隨著時間和儲存條件而變化。延遲測試或不當儲存會導致：

  • 菌數減少 — 某些微生物在不利條件下會死亡，導致計數偏低
  • 菌數增加 — 在營養豐富的樣品中，室溫保存會促進微生物繁殖
  • 菌相改變 — 不同微生物對儲存條件的耐受性不同，長時間儲存會改變菌群組成

標準做法：水樣和生物負載樣品在 2°–8°C 保存，24 小時內開始測試。超過 24 小時需要有挑戰試驗數據支持。

絕對禁止：冷凍微生物樣品 — 結凍/解凍過程會殺死部分微生物，嚴重影響計數準確性。

比喻說明

新鮮食材的比喻：微生物樣品就像新鮮食材 — 買回來的魚放在冰箱（2°–8°C）可以保存一天左右，但不能無限期放下去。放在室溫下幾小時就開始變質。而冷凍魚雖然可以長期保存，但解凍後口感和質地完全不同了（細胞結構被破壞）。同理，冷凍微生物樣品會導致細胞死亡，讓你的計數結果嚴重偏低。

重點提示：CDMO 外包測試的挑戰

委外實驗室的樣品運輸：2022 年修訂版特別提到了使用委外實驗室時的挑戰 — 當測試無法在 24 小時內開始時，必須有挑戰試驗數據來驗證延長保存時間的可行性。對 CDMO 而言，如果你將環境監測樣品或水樣送到外部實驗室測試，運輸過程的時間和溫度控制就變得至關重要。

樣品伴隨文件：USP 要求每個送到實驗室的樣品都應附有完整的文件記錄。這在 CDMO 環境中尤其重要 — 多條產品線、多個客戶的樣品混在一起時，完善的標識和文件系統是防止混淆的唯一保障。

練習思考

  1. 你的 CDMO 計畫將環境監測樣品的培養工作外包給一家距離 2 小時車程的委外實驗室。你需要建立什麼樣的驗證方案來確保運輸過程不影響測試結果？
  2. 一位取樣員在週五下午 5 點取了一批水樣，但實驗室已經下班。樣品被放在冰箱（2°–8°C）直到週一早上才開始測試（約 63 小時）。這符合 USP 的建議嗎？你需要什麼數據來支持這個做法？
Incubation Temperature Excursions — 培養溫度偏移

原文內容 Original Text

Incubation equipment qualification and ongoing calibration provide the acceptable range for temperature conditions relative to the expected procedural incubation temperatures or range.

When an excursion occurs outside of the procedural incubation temperature(s), an assessment should be performed to determine the potential impact on the test samples and what, if any, action should be taken with the test samples.

The effect of a higher or lower temperature on microbial recovery from the test samples for the recorded period of time should be evaluated.

An investigation of the excursion should be documented along with the disposition of the test samples. For instance, a slightly lower temperature excursion (e.g., 1°–2°) for a brief time could be compensated with an increase in incubation time; or, for a higher temperature excursion (e.g., greater than 40° for a total aerobic microbial count or greater than 30° for a total yeasts and molds count) consider any negative impact on recovery.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：溫度偏移評估框架

培養溫度偏移是微生物實驗室最常見的偏差類型之一。USP 提供了一個實用的評估框架：

偏移方向範例影響可能的補救措施
低溫偏移（小幅）低 1°–2°C，短時間生長速度減慢延長培養時間補償
高溫偏移（嚴重）TAMC >40°C某些微生物可能死亡評估回收率影響，可能需重新測試
高溫偏移（嚴重）TYMC >30°C黴菌和酵母菌受抑制考慮回收率的負面影響

比喻說明

烤箱溫度偏差的比喻：如果你在烤蛋糕時烤箱溫度偏低了一點（比如設定 180°C 但實際只有 178°C），多烤幾分鐘通常就能補救。但如果溫度飆到 250°C，蛋糕表面就燒焦了，這時候延長時間沒有用 — 傷害已經造成。微生物培養也是同理：低溫偏移可以用延長時間補救，但高溫偏移可能已經殺死了部分微生物，無法逆轉。

重點提示

TAMC vs TYMC 的溫度敏感性差異：USP 特別舉出了兩個不同的高溫閾值 — TAMC（總需氧微生物計數）的關注溫度是 >40°C，而 TYMC（總酵母菌和黴菌計數）的關注溫度是 >30°C。這是因為黴菌和酵母菌通常在 20°–25°C 培養，對高溫更加敏感。

文件化要求：每次溫度偏移都必須有書面調查記錄，包括偏移的持續時間、幅度、對樣品的影響評估，以及樣品的最終處置決定。這些文件在 FDA 稽查中經常被檢查。

練習思考

  1. 你的 30°–35°C 培養箱在週末因為壓縮機短暫故障，溫度一度升到 38°C 持續了 4 小時，然後恢復正常。箱內有正在培養中的環境監測樣品和無菌試驗樣品。你會如何分別評估這兩類樣品的影響？
  2. 你的實驗室使用持續溫度記錄器監控培養箱溫度。如果記錄器顯示了一次短暫的低溫偏移（降到 28°C，持續 30 分鐘），但事後校準發現記錄器本身有 ±1°C 的誤差，你需要如何處理這個情況？

Competencies and Training of Personnel — 人員能力與訓練

原文內容 Original Text

Each person engaged in each phase of pharmaceutical manufacture should have the education, training, and experience to do his or her job. The demands of microbiological testing require that the core educational background of the staff, supervisors, and managers be in microbiology or a closely related biological science.

A coherent system of standard operating procedures (SOPs) is necessary to run the microbiology laboratory. These procedures serve two purposes in a training program. Firstly, these SOPs describe the methodology that the microbiologist will follow to obtain accurate and reproducible results, and so serve as the basis for training. Secondly, by tracking the procedures in which a particular microbiologist has demonstrated proficiency, the procedure number or title also serves to identify what training the microbiologist has received.

Training records should be current, documenting the microbiologist’s training in the current revision to the particular SOP.

Periodic performance assessment is a wise investment in data quality. This performance testing should provide evidence of competency in core activities of the microbiology laboratory such as hygiene, plating, aseptic technique, documentation, and others as suggested by the microbiologist’s job function.

Microbiologists with supervisory or managerial responsibilities should have appropriate education and in-house training in supervisory skills, laboratory safety, scheduling, budgeting, investigational skills, technical report writing, relevant SOPs, and other critical aspects of the company’s processes.

It is expected that laboratory supervisors and managers have a demonstrated level of competence in microbiology at least as high as those they supervise.

It should be noted that microbiology is a scientifically based discipline that deals with biological principles substantially different from those of analytical chemistry and engineering disciplines. Many times it is difficult for individuals without specific microbiological training to make the transition.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：微生物人員能力模型

USP 定義了一個清晰的能力模型，分為三個層次：

角色核心能力要求額外要求
分析員 / 技術員微生物學或相關生物科學背景、SOP 培訓、操作能力展示定期績效評估
主管 / 組長上述全部 + 管理技能、安全、排程、預算調查能力、技術報告撰寫
經理 / 主任上述全部 + 微生物學能力至少等於其下屬專業認證（如 ASM、CIC）為加分

SOP 的雙重功能：SOP 既是操作指導（「怎麼做」），也是培訓記錄（「這個人會做什麼」）。追蹤每位微生物學家已展示能力的 SOP 清單，就等於追蹤了他/她的培訓記錄。

比喻說明

飛行員執照的比喻：就像飛行員需要有航空學校的學歷（教育）、累積飛行時數（經驗）、定期通過體檢和模擬考核（績效評估），微生物實驗室人員也需要完整的三位一體能力證明。而且就像航空公司不會讓一個只會開汽車的人來開飛機（化學分析師直接轉微生物），USP 也明確指出從化學或工程背景轉到微生物學的人常常遇到困難。

重點提示

主管能力要求的嚴肅性：USP 明確要求「主管和經理的微生物學能力至少要與其下屬相當」。這意味著你不能指派一位化學背景的主管來管理微生物實驗室，除非他/她已經透過充分的培訓和經驗證明了微生物學能力。

SOP 版本培訓：培訓記錄必須反映員工已接受「當前版本」SOP 的培訓。當 SOP 修訂後，所有相關人員都需要重新接受培訓 — 這在 CDMO 環境中是一個常見的稽查發現（Finding）。

微生物學 ≠ 化學分析：USP 特別強調微生物學和化學分析是「本質不同」的學科。這句話的背景是很多公司試圖讓化學 QC 分析員「兼做」微生物測試，但微生物學涉及活的生物系統、統計變異性、對數思維等獨特概念，並非簡單的跨領域轉換。

練習思考

  1. 你的 CDMO 計畫擴編微生物實驗室，但市場上微生物學專業人才很難招到。你會如何設計一個「化學 QC 分析員轉微生物」的培訓計畫，讓他們能在合理時間內勝任基本的微生物測試工作？
  2. 一位微生物組長的績效評估顯示她在無菌操作技術方面表現優秀，但調查報告的品質一直不佳。根據 USP 的能力要求，你會如何幫助她提升？
Qualification of Analysts — 分析員資格確認

原文內容 Original Text

The hiring of experienced personnel combined with periodic training and qualification of analysts, including regular vision checks, should limit to an acceptable level the variability of microbiological test results produced by each analyst’s handling and reading of test results, as well as ensure that the defined good documentation practices are understood and followed.

These also apply to personnel who may be involved in visual inspection of media fill units.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：分析員資格確認的三大要素

  • 招聘有經驗的人員 — 起點是對的人才
  • 定期培訓與資格確認 — 持續維持能力水平
  • 定期視力檢查 — 確保能準確讀取培養基上的菌落

這個章節雖然短，但涵蓋了一個關鍵洞察：微生物測試結果的變異性很大程度上來自於分析員個人的操作手法和讀取結果的方式。

比喻說明

寶石鑑定師的比喻：微生物分析員就像寶石鑑定師 — 需要靠肉眼辨別培養基上微小菌落的數量、形態、顏色。如果鑑定師的視力下降，可能會漏看小鑽石（遺漏菌落）或把灰塵看成鑽石（顆粒物誤判為菌落）。所以定期視力檢查不是走形式，而是確保測試結果準確性的基本保障。

重點提示

培養基模擬灌裝（Media Fill）的連結：USP 特別提到分析員資格確認也適用於「培養基模擬灌裝單元目視檢查」的人員。在 CDMO 環境中，培養基模擬灌裝的陽性結果判讀是極為關鍵的操作 — 如果檢查人員漏看了一個渾濁的瓶子，就可能導致錯誤的結論，進而影響無菌製程的確效結果。

文件操作規範（GDP）：確保分析員理解並遵循良好文件操作規範也是資格確認的一部分。微生物數據的完整性直接影響批次放行決策。

練習思考

  1. 你如何設計一個微生物分析員的年度資格確認方案？應該包含哪些評估項目？
  2. 如果一位資深分析員在年度視力檢查中發現近視度數增加了 100 度，你需要採取什麼行動？
Considerations for Microbiological Risk Assessments — 微生物風險評估

原文內容 Original Text

A microbiological risk assessment can be beneficial and provide information to assist in decision-making about the impact of microbiological quality concerns.

Some examples of when a microbiological risk assessment might be used are: 1) elevated bioburden counts (but within specification); 2) growth of atypical colonies after enrichment on selective agar plates; 3) exceeded alert/action levels; and 4) identification of a recovered species of concern.

The following is a list of example topics that could be included as part of the risk assessment:

  • Scope of the Event — number of product lots directly or indirectly impacted
  • Microorganism Identification — identifying to the species level whenever possible
  • Characterization and Source — natural habitat, physiological states (spore vs. vegetative, planktonic vs. biofilm), potential pathogenicity, and number recovered
  • Manufacturing Process — evaluating microbial ingress/growth-control points or reducing steps
  • Target Patient Base — potential health effects to probable user groups
  • Route of Administration — oral, topical, inhalant, etc.
  • Intrinsic Product Characteristics — water activity, pH, antimicrobial activity
  • Assessment Conclusion — scientifically-based determination of objectionable or not objectionable

Writing and approval of a microbiological risk assessment should be performed by personnel with specialized education and experience in microbiology.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：微生物風險評估的七大面向

USP 提供了一個結構化的風險評估框架，這在 CDMO 環境中特別有用，因為你需要為不同客戶、不同產品的微生物偏差做出科學決策：

  1. 事件範圍 — 影響多少批次？共用的原料/設備有哪些？
  2. 微生物鑑定 — 盡可能鑑定到種的水平
  3. 微生物特性與來源 — 自然棲息地、孢子/營養體狀態、致病性
  4. 製程評估 — 後續有無微生物控制/去除步驟
  5. 目標患者群 — 免疫缺陷患者？嬰幼兒？
  6. 給藥途徑 — 注射劑風險遠高於口服固體
  7. 產品內在特性 — 水活性、pH、抗菌活性能否抑制微生物

比喻說明

偵探破案的比喻：微生物風險評估就像偵探辦案 — 你需要回答「誰」（什麼微生物）、「從哪來」（來源調查）、「影響範圍多大」（事件範圍）、「造成什麼傷害」（對患者的風險）、「有沒有防護機制」（製程中的控制步驟）。把這些線索拼在一起，你才能做出科學的結論：這個微生物是「有害嫌疑犯」還是「無害路人」。

重點提示

四大觸發情境：USP 列出的四種啟動風險評估的情境在 CDMO 日常運作中非常常見：

  • 生物負載偏高但仍在規格內 — 不需要 OOS 調查，但值得做風險評估
  • 選擇性培養基上長出非典型菌落 — 需要進一步鑑定和評估
  • 超過警戒/行動限度 — 環境監測中最常見的觸發情境
  • 鑑定出值得關注的菌種 — 如 Burkholderia cepacia、Pseudomonas aeruginosa

專業人員要求：USP 明確要求風險評估必須由「具有微生物學專業教育和經驗的人員」來撰寫和批准。這不是 QA 或生產部門可以自行完成的 — 必須有微生物學 SME 的參與。

練習思考

  1. 你的 CDMO 在一批注射用水（WFI）中檢出 Burkholderia cepacia 5 CFU/100 mL（規格 <10 CFU/100 mL）。規格內但種類值得關注。請用 USP 的七大面向框架草擬一份風險評估的要點。
  2. 一家客戶的口服液態產品在終產品生物負載測試中發現 E. coli（USP ⟨62⟩ 特定微生物）。你需要做風險評估嗎？還是直接判定不合格？為什麼？
Laboratory Resources & Method Transfer — 實驗室資源與方法轉移

原文內容 Original Text

The laboratory management is responsible for ensuring that the laboratory has sufficient resources to meet the existing testing requirements. This requires some proficiency in budget management and in determining appropriate measures of laboratory performance.

A measure of laboratory performance is the number of investigations performed on tests conducted by the laboratory, but this measure alone is not sufficient. In addition to tracking investigations, the period of time between sample submission and initiation of testing should be tracked, as well as the period of time between end of test and report release.

Oversight of Contract Laboratories: It is the company’s responsibility to thoroughly investigate the reputation and quality control performance of any new or unfamiliar contract laboratory. Before signing an agreement, a manufacturer should confirm that the contract laboratory complies with current GMP regulations. Reports written by the contract laboratory should include complete analytical worksheets from the analysis performed. A certificate of analysis with a summary of analytical results does not provide all the information needed to verify sample test data.

Oversight of Suppliers: The impact of the microbiological quality of materials from a supplier can be significant for the laboratory (media, reagents, cultures) or for manufacturing (excipients, components). An experienced microbiologist should initiate ongoing communications in this type of customer–supplier relationship.

Method Transfer: Performance of validated methods can change from one laboratory to another. Differences in media, reagents, equipment, procedures, and in levels of experience of personnel can all affect the outcome. For method transfers, comparative testing of the same sample may not be relevant for microbiological methods due to the non-homogeneity of microbial cells in the sample. Carrying out a method suitability verification would provide greater assurance that the transfer-receiving unit may adequately perform the tests.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：實驗室資源管理的三大指標

USP 提供了三個實用的實驗室績效指標：

  1. 調查數量 — 調查（偏差/OOS）越多，可能代表實驗室操作品質不佳或資源不足
  2. 樣品提交到開始測試的時間 — 延遲可能導致微生物樣品結果不準確
  3. 測試結束到報告發布的時間 — 延遲影響批次放行速度

關鍵洞察：USP 明確指出「資源不足」是導致測試延遲和調查數量增加的重要原因。這為實驗室管理者提供了向高層爭取預算的科學依據。

比喻說明

委外餐廳的品管：把微生物測試委外給合約實驗室，就像你的餐廳把甜點委外給一家糕餅店。你不能只看最後端上來的蛋糕好不好看（CoA 摘要），你必須要能看到完整的製作過程記錄 — 用了什麼麵粉（培養基批號）、烤箱溫度設了多少（設備校準記錄）、師傅有沒有洗手（分析員培訓記錄）。USP 說得很明白：光有一張 CoA 是不夠的。

重點提示：方法轉移的微生物學特殊性

為什麼微生物方法轉移與化學方法不同：

  • 微生物在樣品中的分布不均勻 — 兩個實驗室測試同一批樣品，即使方法完全相同，結果也可能不同
  • 很多微生物測試的結果是「零計數」— 比較兩個零沒有意義
  • 因此 USP 建議用「方法適用性驗證」（Method Suitability Verification）取代傳統的「同樣品比較測試」

在 CDMO 環境中，當你將測試方法轉移給新的客戶指定實驗室，或接受客戶從其他 CMO 轉來的方法時，這一點特別重要。不要簡單套用化學方法轉移的模板 — 微生物方法轉移需要不同的驗收策略。

重點提示：委外實驗室監督

USP 對委外實驗室的監督要求非常具體：

  • 簽約前必須確認其 GMP 合規性
  • 報告必須包含完整的分析工作表（Complete Analytical Worksheets），不僅僅是 CoA
  • 原始數據（Raw Data）應定期由客戶方的合格微生物學家審查
  • 正負控制結果都必須包含在最終報告中
  • 實驗室管理人員的微生物學資格必須有文件記錄

練習思考

  1. 你的 CDMO 微生物實驗室最近調查數量增加了 40%，同時樣品等待測試的時間也明顯延長。你會如何向管理層報告這個情況並爭取更多資源？USP ⟨1117⟩ 的哪些條文可以支持你的論點？
  2. 你正在評估一家新的委外實驗室。除了 USP 列出的要求外，你還會檢查哪些方面？設計一份簡要的稽核清單。
