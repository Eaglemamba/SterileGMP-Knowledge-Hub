Chapter Scope — 章節範圍與背景

原文內容 Original Text

〈787〉 SUBVISIBLE PARTICULATE MATTER IN THERAPEUTIC PROTEIN INJECTIONS

This chapter can be used as an alternative to USP general chapter Particulate Matter in Injections 〈788〉. It specifically addresses therapeutic protein injections and related preparations, allowing use of smaller test product volumes and smaller test aliquots to determine particulate matter content, with sample-handling instructions that take into account the issues associated with the analysis of these materials. Although the methodology and limits presented in this chapter are preferred for therapeutic protein injections, the use of regulatory-acceptable alternative analytical methods with adequately developed subvisible particulate limits is acceptable.

Therapeutic protein injections are biotechnology-derived products of protein or peptides and other therapeutic protein injections such as naturally sourced therapeutic proteins, including their final infusion preparations. Certain preparations that are not amenable to these tests, such as prophylactic vaccines, are not included in this guidance.

Particulate matter in therapeutic protein injections consists of mobile undissolved substances that may originate from various sources. The particles may be:

  • (a) truly foreign, or "extrinsic" — e.g., unexpected foreign material, such as cellulose
  • (b) "intrinsic" — resulting from addition or by insufficient cleaning during manufacturing, such as tank metals or gaskets, lubricants, filling hardware, or resulting from instability
  • (c) "inherent" — such as particles of the protein or formulation components

All of these particle types may be detected and counted in the test method described in this chapter. In addition, for light obscuration (LO) methods, gas bubbles are commonly counted during particulate matter testing and are counting artifacts.

For the determination of subvisible particles in therapeutic protein injections, the Light Obscuration Particle Count Test is the preferred method. The Microscopic Particle Count Test similar to that described in 〈788〉 may be useful in cases where the particles being analyzed are primarily nonproteinaceous, or in cases where examination of particle characteristics may be important. The results from this test are not equivalent to those from the LO test.

Not all therapeutic protein injections can be examined directly for subvisible particles by the LO method. When the test method is not directly applicable to specific test samples, as indicated by issues with method verification (reproducibility, linearity), a quantitative dilution with an appropriate diluent can be made to allow analysis by light obscuration. Method verification may be required to ensure the appropriateness of the sample handling procedures and the performance of the method for each drug product.

"In the tests described below, the results obtained by examining a discrete unit or group of units for particulate matter cannot be extrapolated with certainty to other units that remain untested. Thus, sampling plans based on known operational factors must be developed to support valid inferences from observed data in order to characterize the level of particulate matter in a large group of units."

Sampling plans should be based on considerations of product volume, particle numbers historically found in comparison to limits, particle size distribution, and variability of particle counts between units. Products that are used with a final filter during administration (in-line) are exempt from these requirements, providing that scientific data are available to justify the exemption.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 — 為何蛋白質需要獨立章節？

Subvisible Particulate Matter (次可見微粒)：指粒徑介於 1–100 µm 之間，肉眼無法直接辨識、但可被儀器計數的顆粒。

〈787〉 從 〈788〉 分支出來，有三個核心理由：

  • 體積限制：生物製劑通常劑量小（0.5–2 mL），〈788〉 要求的測試體積在許多蛋白質產品上根本不夠用
  • 蛋白質聚集體 (protein aggregates)：蛋白質本身就會形成「固有性」微粒，這是藥品本身的特性，不應被當作污染物，但仍需監控
  • 矽油干擾：預填充注射器常使用矽油潤滑劑，這會在 LO 方法中被計數為假陽性微粒

比喻說明 — 三類微粒就像廚房污染的三種來源

想像你在廚房準備一道湯：

  • 外源性 (Extrinsic)：窗外飛進來的沙塵 — 完全不應該存在，代表環境或容器的污染失控
  • 內源性 (Intrinsic)：鍋子鐵鏽脫落、矽膠墊片碎片 — 製程或設備帶入，可以通過製程改善來消除
  • 固有性 (Inherent)：湯汁本身的蛋白質在加熱後凝結成小塊 — 這是食材（藥品分子）的物理化學特性，完全避免很難，但可以透過配方設計最小化

對於蛋白質注射劑，固有性微粒是最難處理的挑戰，因為它介於「可接受的藥品特性」和「潛在安全風險」之間。

重點提示 — 免疫原性風險

為什麼生物製劑的微粒控制比小分子藥物嚴格？答案是免疫原性 (immunogenicity)。

蛋白質聚集體可能觸發患者的免疫系統產生抗體，輕則降低藥效（anti-drug antibodies），重則引發嚴重的過敏反應或自體免疫反應。FDA 和 EMA 已有多起因蛋白質聚集導致的臨床安全事件。

這就是為什麼 〈787〉 對樣品的處理（避免震盪、溫和混合）有特別嚴格的要求 — 每一個操作步驟都可能製造出更多聚集體。

Light Obscuration Particle Count Test — 光遮蔽微粒計數法

原文內容 Original Text

LIGHT OBSCURATION PARTICLE COUNT TEST

Analysts should use a suitable instrument that is based on the principle of light blockage and that allows an automatic determination of particle size and number.

The sensor selected must be appropriate for the intended particle size range and anticipated particle count. Several standardization steps such as sample volume accuracy, sample flow rate, sensor resolution, calibration, and particle count accuracy are important for the proper operation of the apparatus and are further reviewed in general information chapter 〈1788〉.

For equipment calibration purposes, use of NIST-traceable particle size standards or equivalent traceable standards. These standards generally are between 2 and 100 µm. Analysts should verify the performance of the apparatus using the USP Particle Count RS dispersed in particle-free water at an appropriate volume. Care must be taken to avoid agglomeration of particles during dispersion in the calibration process.

General Precautions

Carry out the test under conditions that limit ingress of particulate matter, preferably in a laminar-flow cabinet. The glassware and equipment used should be properly cleaned and handled to remain particle free. Care should be taken to minimize shaking and other stresses to the preparations (for liquids or reconstituted powders) and to prevent introduction of air bubbles into the preparation under examination.

Preparatory Steps

Glassware selected for the method should accommodate the volume of blank and sample aliquots, allow easy mixing, and be amenable to repeated cleaning used to prevent any significant contribution to particle counts. Typically, separate vessels are utilized and retained specifically for this test only.

Two types of system controls are recommended: a Blank Test and System Suitability Verification.

  1. The Blank Test involves the following:
    • A. Run at the start of the day or analysis to ensure that proper care has been used in preparing the site and apparatus for use (Environmental Blank).
    • B. Any additional blanks run throughout the day (such as between product families) should also comply with the Blank Test requirements (Procedural Blank).
  2. The System Suitability Verification is run to verify proper count/mL and ratio for the current USP Particle Count RS or equivalent commercial standard. See general information chapter 〈1788〉.

Blank Test

In order to check that (a) the environment is suitable for the test, (b) the glassware is properly cleaned, and (c) the water or suitable solvent that will be used is particle free (b and c constitute the "blank"), the following test is carried out. Determine the count in five aliquots of degassed particle-free water or suitable solvent, representative of the product test volume (but NLT 1 mL). If the number of particles 10 µm or greater in size exceeds 1 particle per mL for the combined aliquot volume, the precautions taken for the test are not sufficient.

System Suitability Verification

To verify system suitability, Particle-Free Water and the USP Particle Count RS or equivalent commercial standard aqueous suspension are tested from the same or similar type of container as product samples, are degassed in the same manner, and generally are handled like the test article. Ensure that the system can meet the Blank Test requirement. Results from the Standard preparation should fall within the stated specifications of the Reference Standard.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 — 光遮蔽法工作原理

Light Obscuration (LO，光遮蔽法)：樣品液體流過一個光束感測器。當微粒通過光束時，會遮擋部分光線，感測器根據光強度下降的程度計算微粒的等效球形直徑，並累計每個尺寸範圍的粒子數量。

關鍵校準參數：

  • NIST 溯源標準品：確保粒徑測量可追溯至國家標準，是 GMP 校準的基本要求
  • USP Particle Count RS：用於驗證儀器計數準確性的標準品，必須在每次使用前通過
  • 流速準確性：流速直接影響每毫升計數值的計算，必須定期驗證

比喻說明 — Blank Test 就像手術前的無菌驗證

在手術開始前，外科醫師不會直接動刀 — 他們需要先確認手術室的環境、器械都已達到無菌標準。

Blank Test 的邏輯完全相同：

  • 環境空白 (Environmental Blank)：確認空氣環境（層流罩）沒有污染源
  • 程序空白 (Procedural Blank)：確認玻璃器皿、稀釋用水、操作過程沒有引入外來微粒

如果空白測試不通過（>1 粒/mL ≥10 µm），任何後續的測試結果都無法信賴，必須重新清潔環境才能繼續。

重點提示 — 氣泡是 LO 法的「鬼影」

光遮蔽儀器無法區分固體微粒和氣泡 — 氣泡同樣會遮蔽光線，被儀器誤計為微粒。

這對蛋白質製劑特別重要，因為：

  • 蛋白質溶液張力較低，搖晃或移液時容易夾帶氣泡
  • 凍乾品重溶後常含有大量微小氣泡
  • 需要在測試前進行脫氣 (degassing) — 常壓靜置或輕柔真空（約 75 Torr）
  • 嚴禁超聲波處理，因為會破壞蛋白質結構

脫氣後必須重新輕柔混合（手動緩慢旋轉），禁止倒置混合。

實務應用 — CDMO 場景

在承接一個新客戶的 mAb（monoclonal antibody，單株抗體）產品時，CDMO QC 實驗室需要為該產品開發 〈787〉 方法：

  • 首先確認產品體積是否足夠直接測試，或需要合併多個單位
  • 評估產品的光學特性（高濃度蛋白可能影響折射率，導致計數偏低）
  • 執行方法驗證：再現性、線性稀釋回收率（如需稀釋）
  • 建立系統適用性接受標準，納入批次放行規格
Method Detail — 試驗操作方法

原文內容 Original Text

Method

Product samples are tested in the manner that most suitably represents delivery (e.g., expelled syringe contents). For parenterals that have a sufficient volume (i.e., volume large enough to facilitate testing), testing of individual units is often diagnostic. For parenteral products that do not have a sufficient volume, carefully and thoroughly mix each unit, then combine the contents of a suitable number of units in a separate container to obtain the volume required for a single test (generally 0.2–5.0 mL). If a dilution is performed, ensure that the blank container has sufficient volume for product and diluents and that few, if any, particles are introduced in the process.

Open each unit cautiously, remove the sealing closure, and avoid contamination of the contents before sampling, diluting, or, if necessary, pooling.

When a product solvent is specified, e.g., for lyophilized solids or powders for parenteral use, the reconstitution or dilution must be performed with the appropriate amount of specified solvent. In this case, the solvent itself may be tested to ensure that it is not a significant source of particles. Subtraction of the solvent particle count from the total count is not allowed.

Eliminating gas bubbles is a key step, especially for proteinaceous products that readily entrain gas. Two methods are recommended: either allowing the product fluid to stand under ambient pressure or applying a gentle (e.g., 75 Torr) vacuum. Other methods may be used when demonstrated to be suitable. Sonication should be avoided.

Once the samples have been degassed, they must be remixed gently to suspend all particles by mixing the contents of the sample gently but thoroughly by an appropriate means, e.g., slow swirling of the container by hand. Inversion to mix the product fluid is not recommended at any time.

Immediately after mixing, withdraw NLT four aliquots, each of a volume appropriate for the instrument's capacity (generally 0.2–5.0 mL). Count the number of particles over the selected size range, including particles equal to or greater than 10 and 25 µm. Disregard the result obtained for the first aliquot, and calculate the mean number of particles at each size range for the remaining aliquots of the preparation being tested.

General Considerations — Dilution

Dilution is allowed as long as the diluent and methods are demonstrated to be appropriate and the smallest level of dilution that allows for reproducible testing is used. Under certain circumstances dilution of samples may be required:

  • High-concentration products and/or high-viscosity products that cannot be drawn into the instrument properly
  • Products that cause saturation of the instrument sensor due to high counts (especially in smaller than 10-µm size ranges)
  • Cases where the high concentration results in very small differences in refractive index between the solution and the protein aggregates

In situations where sample dilution is conducted, the underlying rationale for the dilution as well as the suitability of the selected dilution scheme including choice of diluent must be demonstrated. Bridging studies with and without dilution at size ranges intended for particle counting should be considered. An orthogonal technique may be used to justify the suitability of the method by verifying the impact of dilution.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 — 為什麼捨棄第一管？

「捨棄第一份等分樣品的結果」是 〈787〉/〈788〉 的特殊要求，原因是：

  • 第一管抽取時，管路尚未完全充滿樣品，可能混入管路死角中殘留的清洗水或前一個樣品的殘餘
  • 流速在第一管抽取時尚未穩定，體積計量可能有偏差

這是一個保守但合理的設計，確保後續三管以上的結果具有代表性。NLT 4 管意味著最少取 4 管，捨棄第 1 管後，以至少 3 管計算平均值。

重點提示 — 溶媒粒子計數不可扣除

USP 明文規定：「Subtraction of the solvent particle count from the total count is not allowed.」

這對凍乾品 (lyophilized products) 尤其重要。重溶使用的注射用水或緩衝液，其本身的背景微粒計數不得從總計數中扣除。

實務意義：如果配方設計者希望使用某種重溶液，必須確保該重溶液本身的微粒水準極低，否則整體測試結果會偏高，導致不必要的 OOS。

公式與計算 — 稀釋後結果的換算

稀釋後計數換算：
  實際粒子數/mL = 儀器讀數/mL × 稀釋因子(DF)

例：
  樣品 1:10 稀釋後讀數 = 500 粒/mL (≥10 µm)
  原液估算 = 500 × 10 = 5,000 粒/mL

注意事項：
  - 必須先確認稀釋不改變粒子大小分布
  - 需執行稀釋線性驗證（至少 3 個稀釋度）
  - Bridging study 必須包含橋接研究對比
    稀釋 vs. 未稀釋（在允許的體積範圍內）

比喻說明 — 高折射率問題就像霧中拍照

當蛋白質濃度很高時（如 100–200 mg/mL 的高濃度抗體），蛋白質溶液的折射率與蛋白質聚集體非常接近。這就像在霧中拍照 — 相機（光遮蔽感測器）很難區分「空氣」（背景溶液）和「霧滴」（蛋白質聚集體），導致計數偏低，遺漏了真正的聚集體。

解決方案是稀釋降低背景濃度，或改用正交技術（如動態光散射 DLS 或奈米追蹤分析 NTA）來驗證稀釋方案的合理性。

本節重點回顧 Section Summary

  • 〈787〉 是針對治療性蛋白質注射劑的次可見微粒專用章節，允許較小測試體積，並考量了蛋白質製劑特有的技術挑戰（聚集、矽油、高黏度、高濃度）
  • 微粒分三類：外源性（污染）、內源性（製程帶入）、固有性（蛋白質本身），三類都需計數和控制，固有性微粒是蛋白質產品的核心挑戰
  • LO 法是首選方法，但蛋白質製劑需特別注意：脫氣（消除氣泡假陽性）、避免倒置、禁用超聲波、捨棄第一管
  • 空白測試（≤1 粒/mL ≥10 µm）是每天測試開始的必要前置步驟，失敗則需重新整備環境
  • 需要稀釋時，必須執行充分的稀釋方案驗證，包含橋接研究和正交技術確認，且溶媒背景微粒不得從結果中扣除
