Introduction — 緒論

Original Text 原文內容

Sterilization can be accomplished using sporicidal agents suspended in air (i.e., vapor). Sterilizing agents that operate in this fashion include hydrogen peroxide (H2O2), peracetic acid (CH3CO3H), formaldehyde (CH2O), and glutaraldehyde [CH2(CH2CHO)2] in aqueous solution. At room temperature these are liquids or solids that can be vaporized for introduction into a vessel or chamber.

They differ from sterilizing gases and liquids in that there are multiple phases within the vessel during sterilization. Vapor sterilization systems are well suited for heat-sensitive materials and surface sterilization. Items exposed to the process should have their surfaces exposed to the greatest extent possible.

Vapor sterilization processes require appropriate sterilant concentration, temperature, and relative humidity, all of which may be variable during the exposure period. Because the agent is ordinarily supplied as an aqueous solution, moisture is introduced with the agent. The consequences of variation in these parameters may be localized differences in relative humidity, agent concentration, and condensation rates on the surfaces to be treated, resulting in variations in process lethality.

The parameters to be established include sterilant amount (usually derived from injection quantities), relative humidity, and temperature. There is no demonstrated correlation between gas phase conditions, surface conditions, and microbial kill. For this reason, online monitoring of vapor phase concentration is not widely utilized as a control parameter.

Efforts to develop a standardized biological indicator for vapor systems have been hampered by the multiphasic nature of these sterilants. Selection of the appropriate biological indicator (BI) and resistance should be based on experimentation within the user’s system. Only under well-defined, specific conditions (e.g., agent concentration, humidity level, temperature, substrate, and phase) can a reliable D-value be established (for a definition of D-value, see Sterilization of Compendial Articles ⟨1229⟩).

"There is no demonstrated correlation between gas phase conditions, surface conditions, and microbial kill. For this reason, online monitoring of vapor phase concentration is not widely utilized as a control parameter."

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

重點提示 Key Notes

無法標準化 D 值 — 這是汽相滅菌最獨特之處：

蒸汽滅菌（121°C）和乾熱滅菌（250°C）都有明確的 D 值，因為製程條件單一且可控。但汽相滅菌由於多相特性：

  • 氣相和液相的殺菌速率不同（液相殺菌力通常高於氣相）
  • 不同表面上的凝結量、濕度、濃度各不相同
  • 線上監測氣相濃度與實際表面殺菌效果之間無已證實的相關性

因此，汽相滅菌驗證必須採用經驗性方法（empirical approach）— 透過 BI 全面挑戰來證明製程效果，而非依賴理論計算。

比喻說明 Analogy

噴霧清潔劑 vs 蒸氣清潔：

想像你用噴霧瓶對房間噴灑消毒劑。噴出的霧滴有些在空中飄浮（氣相），有些直接落在桌面凝結成水珠（液相），有些角落可能噴不到（死角）。你無法單靠測量空氣中的消毒劑濃度來判斷每個表面是否都被充分消毒 — 你必須實際去每個角落貼試紙來確認。這就是為什麼汽相滅菌需要大量的 BI 分布測試，而不是依賴線上氣體濃度監測。

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

汽相滅菌（Vapor Phase Sterilization）：利用殺孢子劑的蒸氣狀態，對容器或腔室內的表面進行滅菌。關鍵特徵：

  • 多相系統（Multiphase） — 滅菌過程中，腔室內同時存在氣相和液相（凝結液滴），這是與氣體滅菌（如 EtO）最根本的區別
  • 表面滅菌 — 汽相滅菌主要作用於物件表面，不適合穿透性滅菌。所有表面必須充分暴露（fully exposed）
  • 適合熱敏感材料 — 在室溫或近室溫條件下操作，是隔離器（isolator）和 RABS 內部去污（bio-decontamination）的首選方法

四大汽相滅菌劑：

Hydrogen Peroxide — 過氧化氫

Original Text 原文內容

The efficacy of hydrogen peroxide as a liquid sterilant has been long established.1 There are several effective approaches to hydrogen peroxide (H2O2) injection, including continuous, intermittent, or all at once.

Some of the systems utilize an evacuation or drying step prior to introduction of the hydrogen peroxide (H2O2) to allow for increased concentration without excess condensation. Alternatively, hydrogen peroxide (H2O2) can be introduced as a liquid, followed by target heating.

Following the exposure period, the chamber or target is aerated to an acceptable level for further processing of materials and/or personnel exposure (whichever is lowest) prior to opening and removing the sterilized article.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

重點提示 CDMO Practical Insights

H2O2 注射策略：

  • 連續注射（Continuous）— H2O2 持續供給，維持穩定氣相濃度。優點：均勻性好。缺點：可能過度凝結
  • 間歇注射（Intermittent / Pulsed）— 分多次脈衝注入，中間有乾燥間歇。許多現代隔離器系統採用此方式
  • 一次注射（All at once / Flash）— 一次性注入全部 H2O2。常見於較小型系統

曝氣優化是 CDMO 效率的關鍵：隔離器去污週期中，曝氣（aeration）往往是最耗時的階段。典型去污週期可能需要 2-4 小時，其中曝氣可佔 1.5-3 小時。透過優化進氣流量、HEPA 過濾器配置、催化分解等手段，可以顯著縮短曝氣時間，直接提升產線利用率。

比喻說明 Analogy

烤箱的預熱與散熱：

VPHP 週期就像使用烤箱：先預熱（conditioning），然後維持烘烤溫度（decontamination），烤完後必須等烤箱降溫才能安全打開門取出食物（aeration）。就像你不會在烤箱還是 200°C 時就打開門一樣，你也不能在 H2O2 殘留量還很高時就打開隔離器。而且降溫（曝氣）通常比烘烤本身還花時間 — 這正是 CDMO 想要優化的痛點。

Peracetic Acid — 過氧乙酸

Original Text 原文內容

Peracetic acid (CH3CO3H), alone or mixed with hydrogen peroxide, is a sporicidal agent that has been proven effective.1 Peracetic acid is introduced as a liquid through an atomizer, resulting in the presence of liquid and vapor phases within the chamber. After the process dwell period, it is removed by evaporation.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 PAA vs VPHP

過氧乙酸（PAA）的特點：

  • 引入方式 — 以液態通過霧化器（atomizer）引入，而非像 H2O2 那樣通過蒸發器汽化。因此液相成分更多
  • 可單獨使用或與 H2O2 混合使用 — 混合配方可提供協同殺菌效果
  • 去除方式 — 透過蒸發去除，不需要像 H2O2 那樣的專門曝氣步驟，但殘留物（醋酸）可能需要額外考量

重點提示 CDMO Context

VPHP vs PAA 選擇考量：

在 CDMO 無菌充填環境中，VPHP 幾乎是隔離器去污的唯一選擇。PAA 更常用於非隔離器環境的大面積去污。

Validation of Vapor Sterilization — 汽相滅菌驗證

Original Text 原文內容

Standard sterilizing conditions have not been defined due to the varying phase and multiphase nature of the sterilant during sterilization processes. Therefore, no standardized BIs with D-values that may be used for conventional predictive analysis of kill rates exist.

In the absence of BI D-values, and due to the variation in vapor sterilization cycle parameters, an empirical approach must be used. The kill rates in the gas and liquid phases that constitute the vapor may be substantially different (liquid kill rates are considered greater than gaseous kill rates). Destruction of BIs distributed across the system or load demonstrates lethality regardless of which phase effects the kill.

Sterilization process parameters (usually time) that do not kill the BIs may be adjusted until a complete kill is achieved. This establishes the minimum conditions necessary for a complete kill. Vapor sterilization may be validated using a half-cycle, bracketing, or other suitable approach as defined in ⟨1229⟩ and Gaseous Sterilization ⟨1229.7⟩.

The half-cycle validation method requires the destruction of a suitable concentration of a resistant microorganism under defined, minimum conditions for a complete kill. Then, in routine operation, the minimum lethal time period is arbitrarily doubled, which supports a doubling of the spore log reduction of the BI, and is more than sufficient to inactivate the bioburden.

A bracketing approach, which better supports process operating ranges for the critical parameters than does the half-cycle method, also can be employed. In the bracketing method, one evaluates conditions that bracket the defined process condition in order to establish parameters for the minimum and maximum effects on the materials and bioburden. The minimum lethal process dwell time establishes the worst case for microbial kill. Incremental increases in process dwell time beyond the minimum lethal time period are used to establish the maximum exposure periods, which impart the greatest effect on materials. In addition, adjustments to the quantity of agent introduced, operating temperature, and relative humidity are utilized to further enhance the bracketing approach. By this method, the routine process conditions may be established between the minimum and maximum process conditions to ensure complete microbial kill while maintaining the integrity of the materials.

"Destruction of BIs distributed across the system or load demonstrates lethality regardless of which phase effects the kill."

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

重點提示 Empirical Approach

經驗性方法的核心邏輯：

  1. 起始：在系統各處分布 BI（含已知數量的抗性芽孢）
  2. 尋找最短殺菌時間：從短週期開始，逐步增加暴露時間，直到所有 BI 均被滅殺 — 這就是 minimum lethal time
  3. 建立安全裕度：半週期法直接將此時間加倍；括弧法則另外測試更長時間以評估材料影響上限
  4. 常規使用：在安全裕度範圍內的參數進行日常操作

這種方法之所以必要，是因為無法像蒸汽滅菌那樣用 D 值和 F0 值進行理論計算。你必須用實際實驗來「證明」你的週期有效。

比喻說明 Analogy

微波爐加熱食物：

半週期法就像用微波爐加熱便當：你先試 2 分鐘發現中心還是冷的，試 3 分鐘還是不夠，試 4 分鐘終於全部熱透了（minimum lethal time = 4 分鐘）。為了安全，你每次都設定 8 分鐘（2 倍）。括弧法則是你還會額外試 10 分鐘、12 分鐘，看看便當會不會過熱變乾（材料損傷上限），最後決定日常設定 8-10 分鐘。

Validation Activities — 驗證活動

Original Text 原文內容

The following activities are defined for a batch process, and therefore appropriate adaptation is necessary when they are applied to intermittent or continuous sterilization processes.

Equipment qualification — The equipment qualification for vapor sterilization mimics that of other sterilization processes in order to confirm that the equipment has been properly installed and operates as intended.

Empty chamber parameter distribution — Although multipoint measurement is possible, it lacks correlation to surface microbial kill. Humidity and temperature measurements, along with chemical indicators, can provide a limited indication of sterilant distribution. BIs are not required in the evaluation of the empty chamber.

Component mapping — For surface sterilization, internal mapping of load items is not required. Although vapors are primarily used as surface sterilants, when they are used for packaged articles with internal surfaces and volumes that need to be sterilized, internal mapping should be performed, with BIs placed in difficult-to-penetrate locations to confirm process lethality.

Load mapping — Humidity and temperature measurements, along with chemical indicators, can provide a limited indication of sterilant distribution on component surfaces. BIs are not required. Effects of load size and patterns should be assessed.

Biological indicators — The use of multiple BIs at each test location is recommended to more adequately support the process lethality.

Process confirmation and microbiological challenge — The core of the validation activity is the confirmation of acceptable process parameters and inactivation of the microbial challenge. Proof of cycle efficacy is provided in replicate studies in which the BIs are killed and chemical or physical measurements are utilized.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

重點提示 CDMO Isolator Validation

隔離器 VPHP 去污驗證的實務要點：

  • BI 選擇：常用 Geobacillus stearothermophilus 芽孢，以不鏽鋼或玻璃載體承載。數量通常 106 CFU/carrier（注意：D 值無法標準化，應在自身系統中驗證）
  • BI 分布位置：至少覆蓋最難到達（worst-case）位置 — 如門密封條附近、角落、管路接頭、手套口等
  • 多個 BI 原則：USP 建議每個位置放置多個 BI — 這提高了統計可信度，避免單一 BI 的假陽性/假陰性影響結論
  • 重複性：製程確認需要重複研究（replicate studies），通常至少三次連續成功的 BI 全滅殺

實務應用 Material Compatibility

材料相容性測試 — USP 未明說但 CDMO 必須考慮：

  • 手套材質：Hypalon®（CSM）是最常見的隔離器手套材料，對 H2O2 耐受性佳，但仍需定期更換（通常每 200-500 週期或按 SOP 規定）
  • 密封墊圈：EPDM 橡膠通常兼容；天然橡膠和某些矽膠可能加速老化
  • 不鏽鋼 316L：與 H2O2 兼容，但長期暴露於 PAA 可能出現腐蝕
  • 塑膠組件：PC（聚碳酸酯）觀察窗可能出現霧化或龜裂 — 需追蹤週期數

Routine Process Control — 常規製程控制

Original Text 原文內容

Vapor sterilization is subject to controls that maintain a validated state over time. The practices outlined in ⟨1229⟩ describe the general requirements appropriate for all sterilization systems. The essential practices required to maintain validated status include calibration, physical measurements, use of BIs, physical or chemical integrators and indicators, ongoing process control, change control, preventive maintenance, periodic reassessment, and training.

"The essential practices required to maintain validated status include calibration, physical measurements, use of BIs, physical or chemical integrators and indicators, ongoing process control, change control, preventive maintenance, periodic reassessment, and training."

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

重點提示 Ongoing Control

CDMO 稽查常見問題：

  • BI 使用頻率 — FDA 和 EU Annex 1 對隔離器去污常規 BI 使用有不同期望。保守做法：每次生產批次使用 BI；部分公司根據風險評估降低頻率，但需有科學依據和數據支持
  • 趨勢分析 — 監控週期參數（注射量、曝氣時間、殘留 H2O2 水平）的長期趨勢，及早偵測設備性能劣化
  • 變更管制觸發再驗證 — 更換手套、更換 HEPA、更換 H2O2 供應商或濃度，均需評估是否觸發再驗證

核心概念解析 Maintaining Validated State

九大維持驗證狀態的基本要素：

本章重點回顧 Chapter Summary

  • 汽相滅菌（Vapor Phase Sterilization）利用殺孢子劑蒸氣對表面進行滅菌，是隔離器和 RABS 去污的標準方法
  • 多相特性（Multiphase Nature）是汽相滅菌的核心挑戰 — 氣相和液相共存，導致無法建立標準化 D 值，必須採用經驗性方法驗證
  • H2O2（VPHP）是製藥隔離器去污的首選滅菌劑；過氧乙酸（PAA）更適合大面積或房間級去污
  • 半週期法（Half-Cycle）是最廣泛使用的驗證策略 — 找到最短全滅殺時間後加倍使用，簡單有效
  • 括弧法（Bracketing）提供更寬的操作範圍驗證，同時評估材料相容性的上限
  • 驗證活動包括設備確認、空腔分布、負載圖譜、BI 挑戰和製程確認 — 每個位置建議多個 BI
  • 常規維護需涵蓋九大要素（校準、BI、CI、變更管制、預防保養等），確保持續維持已驗證狀態
  • CDMO 效率重點：曝氣時間優化是提升隔離器產線利用率的最大機會
