Introduction — 緒論

原文內容 Original Text

Gases, such as compressed air, nitrogen, and atmospheric gases that contact sterile components, containers and closures, and product contact surfaces of processing equipment, such as tanks and piping, must be sterile to prevent contamination of pharmaceuticals and biopharmaceuticals.

The sterilization of gases is typically accomplished by passing the gas through one or more sterilizing-grade membrane filters. The filtration process must be designed to ensure that the filtered gas has been sterilized.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

製程氣體為何需要除菌？在無菌製藥生產中，任何接觸無菌產品、容器、封蓋或設備表面的氣體都必須為無菌。這包括：

  • 壓縮空氣（Compressed Air） — 用於驅動氣動閥、吹建容器、乾燥等
  • 氮氣（Nitrogen） — 用於槽體覆蓋（Nitrogen Overlay）保護對氧氣敏感的產品
  • 大氣（Atmospheric Gas） — 進入無菌區域的空氣

這些氣體如果未經過濾，可能攜帶微生物或微粒，直接污染產品或打破無菌狀態。

重點提示 CDMO 實務關聯

在無菌灌裝（Aseptic Fill-Finish）環境中，氣體過濾涉及多個關鍵點：

  • 槽體呼吸器（Tank Vent Filter） — 當配藥槽進行液位變化時，呼吸器防止外界微生物進入
  • 氮氣覆蓋過濾器 — 覆蓋氮氣直接接觸產品液面，必須經除菌級過濾器
  • Grade A 空氣供應 — 灌裝區的壓縮空氣過濾

  • 完整性測試方法有額外選擇（如水侵入測試）
Membranes for Gas Sterilization — 氣體除菌用濾膜

原文內容 Original Text

Filters used for sterilization of gases are commonly hydrophobic membranes, although hydrophilic membranes can be employed where membrane wetting during use is not an issue.

Wetting may be a factor with filters used for autoclave venting and where vent filters are required to maintain the sterility of tank headspace.

Microporous filter membranes that have a pore-size rating of NMT 0.2 µm, which meets the ASTM International standard (see F838-15a Standard Test Method for Determining Bacterial Retention of Membrane Filters Utilized for Liquid Filtration), are suitable for the sterilization of gases; in fact, these filters are considerably more retentive for gases than for liquids (1).

Additionally, the filters must be chemically compatible with the gas to be filtered and physically compatible with the filtration conditions (e.g., pressure differentials and flow rates).

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

疰水性 vs 親水性濾膜：

  • 疰水性濾膜（Hydrophobic）：排斥水分，不會被水濕潤。這是氣體過濾的首選，因為氣體管路中可能含有凝結水，疰水性濾膜不會因水分堵塞而影響通氣量
  • 親水性濾膜（Hydrophilic）：容易被水濕潤。一旦濾膜被水濕潤，氣體就無法通過，導致過濾器失效

NMT 0.2 µm 孔徑評級：與液體過濾相同的標準。但關鍵差別在於：同一支濾膜對氣體中微生物的截留能力遠高於對液體中微生物的截留能力。這是因為氣相中的擴散截留和慣性撞擊機制更為明顯。

重點提示 CDMO 場景

濕潤（Wetting）問題在 CDMO 中的常見情境：

  • 槽體呼吸器：配藥槽進行液位變化時，蒸汽可能凝結在呼吸器濾膜上。如果濾膜被濕潤，槽體將無法正常呼吸，可能導致槽體變形或真空狀態
  • 滅菌釜排氣（Autoclave Venting）：滅菌過程中的蒸汽會濕潤濾膜，需要特別注意濾膜選擇

比喻說明 Analogy

Gore-Tex 外套的原理：

疰水性濾膜就像戶外運動的 Gore-Tex 外套 — 它能讓空氣（氣體）通過，但擦住雨水（液體）。這就是為何氣體過濾優先選擇疰水性濾膜 — 即使氣體中含有水分，濾膜也不會被堵塞。

Retention Mechanisms — 截留機制

原文內容 Original Text

The primary retention mechanisms of membrane filters used in gas sterilization are size exclusion, impaction, diffusional interception, and electrostatic attraction.

Size exclusion is effective for particles (microorganisms) that are too large to pass through the pore structure of the filter membrane and is essentially independent of the velocity of gas flowing through the filter.

Retention mechanisms other than size exclusion can be effective for particles smaller than the pores they encounter.

Retention due to impaction, diffusional interception, and electrostatic attraction is dependent on particle size, flow rate, and relative humidity.

  • Impaction: Particle retention efficiency is proportional to the mass of the particle; heavier particles have a greater momentum than lighter ones and are therefore more likely to contact pore walls and subsequently be retained by the filter during tortuous flow.
  • Diffusional interception: Capture efficiency is inversely proportional to particle size and velocity. Smaller particles influenced by Brownian motion can exit the streamlines, contact the filter matrix, and be retained. Lower velocity increases potential contact time and improves capture efficiency.
  • Electrostatic attraction: Attraction of particles is reduced by a high relative humidity in the filtered gas.

Adsorption, which can be important in liquid filtration, is not a retention mechanism for gases.

重點提示 Key Notes

氣體 vs 液體截留的關鍵差異：

  • 吸附（Adsorption）不適用於氣體：在液體過濾中，微生物可能被吸附在濾膜表面。但在氣相中，這種機制不存在
  • 擴散截留在氣相中更強：相較於液體，氣體中微粒的布朗運動更顯著，因此擴散截留效果更好 — 這就是為何同一支濾膜對氣體的截留能力更強

濕度的影響：高濕度會降低靜電吸附效果。在 CDMO 環境中，蒸汽滅菌後的管路或潮濕環境中的通氣過濾器，需特別注意這點。

Validation — 驗證

原文內容 Original Text

Validation of the processes used in sterilizing filtration for gases, in general, may be divided into two parts:

  1. Sterilization of the filter and its housing
  2. The ability of the filter to remove microorganisms from the filtered gas

Sterilization of the filter and its housing has been addressed specifically in ⟨1229.4⟩ and Sterilization-in-Place ⟨1229.13⟩, and generally in Sterilization of Compendial Articles ⟨1229⟩.

Membrane filters meeting the requirements of ASTM F838-15a are suitable for sterilizing filtration of gases in terms of microbial retention.

Liquid bacterial challenge testing represents a worst-case condition for sterilizing gas filters because the retention efficiency in liquids is lower than in gases (2).

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

驗證的兩個面向：

  • 濾器本體的滅菌：過濾器和濾殼在使用前必須滅菌（通常透過蒸汽滅菌或 SIP）。這確保過濾系統本身不會成為污染源
  • 微生物截留能力：濾膜必須能夠從氣體中有效去除微生物

Worst-Case 原則：這是 USP 這章最重要的驗證概念之一。因為液體中的細菌截留測試（ASTM F838）已經是氣體過濾的最壞情況，所以通過液體挑戰測試的濾膜不需要另行進行氣體細菌挑戰測試。

重點提示 CDMO 驗證策略

氣體過濾器驗證的簡化邏輯：

  1. 選擇符合 ASTM F838 標準的 0.2 µm 濾膜（供應商提供證明）
  2. 驗證濾器和濾殼的滅菌製程（SIP 或蒸汽滅菌）
  3. 每次使用前後執行完整性測試（Integrity Test）

不需要單獨進行氣體細菌挑戰測試 — 液體測試已經涵蓋了最壞情況。

Integrity Testing — 完整性測試

原文內容 Original Text

Integrity testing requirements and techniques are discussed in ⟨1229.4⟩.

The hydrophobic filters typically used for gas filtration can be integrity tested using:

  • Bubble-point test
  • Diffusive-flow test
  • Pressure-hold test

These tests use a liquid of low surface tension to completely wet the pore structure of the filter membrane.

Additionally, integrity testing can be performed by measuring the membrane’s resistance to wetting with water as a function of pressure. Water intrusion testing is an example of this type of integrity testing.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

疰水性濾膜完整性測試的挑戰：

一般的完整性測試（泡點、擴散流）需要先用液體濕潤濾膜。但疰水性濾膜不會被水濕潤，因此有兩種策略：

  • 低表面張力液體濕潤：使用酒精/水混合液或專用測試液（如 IPA/水）來濕潤疰水性濾膜，然後執行泡點或擴散流測試
  • 水侵入測試（Water Intrusion Test, WIT）：不需要濕潤濾膜。而是利用疰水性濾膜抗拒水滲透的特性，在特定壓力下測量水的侵入量來判斷濾膜完整性

重點提示 CDMO 實務要點

測試方法 濕潤液體 優點 注意事項

擴散流 Diffusive Flow 低表面張力液體 自動化容易 需完全濕潤濾膜

WIT 的實務優勢：在 CDMO 現場，水侵入測試最為便利，因為不需要使用酒精或其他有機溶劑濕潤疰水性濾膜，直接使用 WFI 即可執行測試。但需確認儀器供應商支援這種測試模式。
