Buffer Solutions for Calibration — 校準用緩衝液

原文內容 Original Text

BUFFER SOLUTIONS FOR CALIBRATION OF THE PH MEASUREMENT SYSTEM

Buffer solutions for calibration are prepared as directed in Table 2. Buffer salts of requisite purity can be obtained from the National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST), other national authorities, or other suppliers.

Buffer solutions should be stored in appropriate containers that ensure stability of the pH through the expiry date, and fitted with a tight closure.

  • For buffer solutions with a pH value greater than 11, the storage should be in containers that are resistant to or reduce carbon dioxide intrusion, which would lower the pH of the buffer.
  • For buffer solutions with a pH value lower than 11, they should typically be prepared at intervals not to exceed 3 months.
  • For buffer solutions with a pH value greater than 11, they should typically be prepared and used fresh unless carbon dioxide ingress is restricted.

All buffer solutions should be prepared using Purified Water. Table 2 indicates the pH of the buffer solutions as a function of temperature. The instructions are given in molarity; the difference in concentration between molality and molarity preparations for these buffer solutions is less than 1%, and the pH difference is negligible.

Calibration using buffer solutions shall be done in the temperature range of the buffers listed in Table 2.

[Note — The Nernst temperature compensation corrects only for the electrode millivolt output change with temperature, not the actual pH change of the buffer solution with temperature, which is unique for each buffer.] Features such as automatic buffer recognition or buffer pH–temperature correction are available for convenience in accommodating the temperature influence on buffer solutions.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 標準緩衝液體系

為何需要標準緩衝液？pH 計的玻璃電極老化、污染、或參考電位漂移時，絕對電位讀數會偏移。校準緩衝液提供已知 pH 的「錨點」，讓儀器能修正自身的偏移（offset）和斜率（slope）。

NIST 溯源性（Traceability）：USP 要求緩衝鹽來自 NIST 或其他國家計量機構，確保 pH 值的溯源性。這對製藥廠的計量管理（Metrology）和校準系統符合 GMP 要求至關重要。

pH–溫度關係的重要性（ΔpH/Δ°）：表格最後一行顯示各緩衝液的 pH 隨溫度的變化率。Ca(OH)₂ 飽和液的 ΔpH/Δ° = −0.0310，是所有緩衝液中最大的——溫度每升高 1°C，pH 下降 0.031，在 37°C 下誤差累積顯著。

比喻說明 Analogy

標準緩衝液就像廚師的「試吃標準品」：在開始炒菜前，先用標準品確認自己的味覺（儀器）是否正常。如果嚐到的酸度和預期不符，就知道今天狀態不對，需要調整。pH 校準緩衝液的功能完全相同——它不是「真正的樣品」，而是讓儀器「確認自己狀態正常」的工具。

重點提示 CO₂ 對高 pH 緩衝液的影響

這是實驗室最常被忽略的細節之一。空氣中的 CO₂（約 400 ppm）溶入水中後形成碳酸，會顯著降低 pH >11 的鹼性緩衝液的 pH 值。

對於 Na₂B₄O₇（四硼酸鈉）緩衝液（pH 9.18 at 25°C），雖然 pH <11，但仍需「保護免受 CO₂ 吸收」——因為它對 CO₂ 特別敏感。

實驗室 SOP 要點：高 pH 緩衝液應使用剛煮沸冷卻的純化水配製，並在氮氣保護下儲存，或每日新鮮配製。

公式與計算 緩衝液溫度內插法

Table 2 未列出的溫度可用線性內插：

pH(T) = pH(T₁) + [pH(T₂) − pH(T₁)] × (T − T₁) / (T₂ − T₁)

例：磷酸鹽等摩緩衝液（Equimolal Phosphate）在 22°C：
  pH(20°) = 6.88, pH(25°) = 6.87
  pH(22°) = 6.88 + (6.87 − 6.88) × (22 − 20) / (25 − 20)
           = 6.88 + (−0.01) × 0.4
           = 6.876 ≈ 6.88

實際上 Equimolal Phosphate 非常穩定，
從 20–55°C 的總 pH 變化僅約 0.01
（ΔpH/Δ° = −0.0016）。

Buffer Preparation — 緩衝液配製

原文內容 Original Text

Preparation of alternative volumes at the same concentrations to those indicated below is acceptable.

  • Potassium tetraoxalate, 0.05 m: Dissolve 12.61 g of KH₃(C₂O₄)₂ · 2H₂O, and dilute with water to make 1000.0 mL.
  • Potassium hydrogen tartrate, saturated at 25°: Add C₄H₅KO₆ to water until saturation is exceeded at 25°. Then filter or decant.
  • Potassium dihydrogen citrate, 0.05 M: Dissolve 11.41 g of C₆H₇KO₇, and dilute with water to make 1000.0 mL.
  • Potassium biphthalate, 0.05 m: Dissolve 10.12 g of KHC₈H₄O₄, previously dried at 110° for 1 h, and dilute with water to make 1000.0 mL.
  • Equimolal phosphate, 0.05 m: Dissolve 3.53 g of disodium hydrogen phosphate (Na₂HPO₄) and 3.39 g of potassium dihydrogen phosphate (KH₂PO₄), each previously dried at 120° for 2 h, and dilute with water to make 1000.0 mL.
  • Potassium dihydrogen phosphate, 0.0087 M and disodium hydrogen phosphate, 0.0303 M: Dissolve 1.18 g of KH₂PO₄ and 4.30 g of Na₂HPO₄, both dried for 2 h at 120 ± 2°, and dilute with water to make 1000.0 mL.
  • Sodium tetraborate, 0.01 m: Dissolve 3.80 g of Na₂B₄O₇ · 10H₂O, and dilute with water to make 1000.0 mL. Protect from absorption of carbon dioxide.
  • Sodium carbonate, 0.025 M and sodium bicarbonate, 0.025 M: Dissolve 2.64 g of sodium carbonate (Na₂CO₃) and 2.09 g of sodium bicarbonate (NaHCO₃), and dilute with water to make 1000.0 mL.
  • Calcium hydroxide, saturated at 25°: Add Ca(OH)₂ to water until saturation is exceeded at 25°. Use water that has been recently boiled and protected from the atmosphere to limit carbon dioxide absorption. Then filter or decant.

Commercially available buffer solutions for pH measurement system, calibrated by methods traceable to NIST or other national authorities, labeled with a pH value accurate to 0.02 pH units may be used. Solutions prepared from ACS reagent-grade materials or other suitable materials may be used provided the pH of the resultant solution is the same as that of the solution prepared from the NIST (or other national authorities) certified material.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 緩衝液配製的關鍵細節

預先乾燥（Pre-drying）的重要性：磷酸鹽緩衝液的鹽類需在 120°C 乾燥 2 小時，以確保計算重量時已排除水分（結晶水或吸附水），使溶液濃度精確。若不預乾燥，吸濕性鹽類的實際含量可能偏低，導致緩衝液 pH 偏移。

市售緩衝液的使用條件：市售緩衝液需標示 pH 值準確至 0.02 pH units（對應 USP 校準驗證的 ±0.05 要求，留有足夠餘裕）。溯源至 NIST 的聲明必須在 CoA 上明確記載。

比喻說明 Analogy

配製緩衝液就像咖啡師精準秤量咖啡粉：少 0.5g 和多 0.5g 泡出來的風味完全不同，但如果咖啡豆受潮（吸水），實際磨出的乾物質就比秤重顯示的少。預先乾燥鹽類就是確保「原料是乾燥的」，避免這種系統性誤差。

重點提示 實驗室品質管理要點

GMP 環境中的緩衝液管理：

  • 每批緩衝液應記錄配製日期、操作員、批號（鹽類來源）
  • pH <11 者：使用期 ≤3 個月（USP 規定）
  • pH >11 者：每次使用前新鮮配製，或確認有 CO₂ 防護措施
  • 若使用市售緩衝液：保存 CoA，確認 NIST 溯源聲明和有效期
  • 所有緩衝液應有唯一識別標籤，並在儀器使用記錄中引用

實務應用 Practical Application

在製藥 CDMO 中，QC 實驗室通常使用市售預製緩衝液以節省人力並降低配製誤差風險。採購標準應包含：NIST 溯源（或 PTB/IRMM 等同等機構）、pH 標示準確至 0.02、附有溫度-pH 換算表。每批緩衝液進廠時應核對 CoA，記錄批號並納入儀器校準記錄。

Calibration — 校準程序

原文內容 Original Text

CALIBRATION

Because of variations in the nature and operation of the available pH measurement systems, it is not practical to provide universal directions for the calibration of the measurement system. However, the general principles to be followed are set forth in the following paragraphs. Examine the electrodes, especially the reference electrode and electrolyte level, if a liquid electrolyte is used. If necessary, replenish electrolyte supply, and observe other precautions indicated by the instrument and electrode manufacturers.

The calibration or verification of the pH measurement system should be periodically executed. The historical performance of the measurement system, the criticality of the pH measurement, the maintenance of the pH sensor, and the frequency of measurement operation are used to determine the frequency of the calibration/verification. The procedure below allows for several calibration methodologies (two-point calibration, multiple-point calibration, and multiple-segment calibration).

If the pH of the buffer is sensitive to ambient carbon dioxide, then use Purified Water that has been recently boiled, and subsequently stored in a container designed to minimize ingress of carbon dioxide.

Two-Point Calibration Procedure (Steps 1–14)

  1. Select three buffer solutions for calibration, preferably from Table 2, such that the expected pH of the material under test falls within their range. Two buffers are used for calibration; the third for verification. The verification buffer value shall be between the two calibration buffers.
  2. Rinse the pH sensor several times with water, then with the first buffer solution.
  3. Immerse the pH sensor in the first buffer solution at a temperature within the range of Table 2.
  4. If automatic temperature measurement and compensation are not included, manually enter the temperature of the buffer and pH value at that temperature into the instrument. For temperatures not listed in Table 2, use linear interpolation.
  5. Initiate the two-point calibration sequence with the first buffer according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  6. Remove the pH sensor from the first buffer and rinse the electrode(s) with water, then with the second buffer solution.
  7. Immerse the pH sensor in the second buffer at a temperature within the range of Table 2.
  8. If automatic temperature measurement and compensation are not included, manually enter the temperature and pH value.
  9. Continue the two-point calibration sequence with the second buffer according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  10. After completion, verify that the pH slope and offset are within acceptable parameters:
    • Slope: 90%–105%
    • Offset: 0 ± 30 mV (0.5 pH units at 25°)
    If not within acceptable parameters, the sensor should be properly cleaned, replenished, serviced, or replaced, and the calibration process shall be repeated.
  11. Remove from second buffer, rinse thoroughly with water, then with the verification buffer.
  12. Immerse the pH sensor in the verification buffer at a temperature within the range of Table 2.
  13. If automatic temperature measurement and compensation are not included, manually enter the temperature and pH value.
  14. The pH reading shall be within ±0.05 pH units of the value in Table 2 at the buffer solution temperature.
[Note — If a multiple-point calibration process (three or more calibration buffers) plus at least one verification buffer are used, then repeat steps 9–14, assuring that the pH sensor slope and offset criteria (see step 10) and the calibration accuracy (see step 14) of this range are met. The value of the verification buffers shall be between the highest and lowest calibration buffers of the range.]
[Note — If a multiple-segment calibration process (three or more calibration buffers with at least two slopes and offsets) plus at least one verification buffer for each segment are used, then repeat steps 9–14 for each segment, assuring that the pH sensor slope and offset criteria (see step 10) and the calibration accuracy (see step 14) of each segment are met. The value of each verification buffer shall be between the highest and lowest calibration buffers for each segment.]

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 校準的兩個關鍵參數

斜率（Slope，90–105%）：理想斜率為 100%，代表電極對每個 pH 單位的響應等於 Nernst 理論值（25°C 時為 59.16 mV/pH）。老化或污染的玻璃電極斜率會下降。斜率 <90% 通常提示電極需要更換或再生。

偏移（Offset，0 ± 30 mV）：偏移反映電極的「零點漂移」——當溶液 pH = 7 時，理想情況下兩極電位差應為 0 mV。偏移過大通常由參考電極電位漂移或液接電位（liquid junction potential）不穩定引起。

驗證緩衝液（Verification Buffer）：校準後的獨立驗證，確認整個系統在測試範圍內的準確度。驗證要求 ±0.05 pH，這比大多數製劑規格（通常 ±0.1–0.5）嚴格許多，確保方法有足夠的量測餘裕（measurement margin）。

比喻說明 兩點校準如同設定直線方程式

pH 計的電位響應是線性的（至少在中性 pH 範圍內）：E = 偏移 + 斜率 × pH。要確定一條直線，你需要兩個點——就像數學課上的兩點確定一條直線。第一緩衝液定義「第一個點」，第二緩衝液定義「第二個點」，這兩點確定斜率和截距（偏移）。第三個驗證緩衝液則是「用第三個點檢查這條直線是否畫得準確」。

重點提示 校準頻率的風險基礎決策

USP 沒有規定固定的校準頻率，而是要求「依風險決定」——考量：歷史性能、量測批判性（criticality）、感測器維護狀況、量測頻率。

GMP 建議實務：

  • 每次使用前：至少執行校準驗證（verification），確認前次校準仍有效
  • 每日或每班次：執行完整兩點校準
  • 若儀器閒置 >24 小時、電極更換、或任何異常跡象後：強制重新校準
  • 所有校準結果（斜率、偏移、驗證偏差）均應記錄於儀器使用記錄

公式與計算 斜率百分比計算

斜率百分比 = 實測斜率 / 理論 Nernst 斜率 × 100%

理論 Nernst 斜率（25°C）：
  k = 0.05916 V/pH = 59.16 mV/pH

例：若 pH 4.01 和 pH 7.41 兩緩衝液校準時，
儀器讀得電位差 = 196.2 mV：

實測斜率 = 196.2 mV / (7.41 − 4.01) pH
         = 196.2 / 3.40 = 57.7 mV/pH

斜率% = 57.7 / 59.16 × 100% = 97.5%  ✓ (符合 90–105%)

若斜率% < 90%：電極老化，需更換
若斜率% > 105%：電極異常，需檢查

本節重點回顧 Section Summary

  • USP 提供 9 種標準校準緩衝液（pH 1.68–12.81），涵蓋 10–60°C 溫度範圍，每種緩衝液的 pH 都隨溫度而變化
  • pH >11 的緩衝液需防 CO₂ 入侵，應新鮮配製；pH <11 者使用期不超過 3 個月
  • 兩點校準需選三個緩衝液：兩個校準緩衝液＋一個驗證緩衝液（落在兩個校準值之間）
  • 校準後需驗證：斜率 90–105%、偏移 0 ± 30 mV；驗證緩衝液讀數需在 ±0.05 pH 內
  • 校準頻率由風險決定，但 GMP 環境建議每次使用前至少驗證，每日重新校準
