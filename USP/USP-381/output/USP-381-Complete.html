1. Introduction — 簡介

原文內容 Original Text

Packaging systems, also referred to as container–closure systems, are defined in Packaging and Storage Requirements 〈659〉; these systems are the sum of components that together contain and protect the drug product. Elastomeric components are formulated with elastomeric substances and can be either thermoset or thermoplastic in nature.

Every elastomeric component used in a pharmaceutical packaging/delivery system should be proven suitable for its intended use. The purpose of this chapter is to provide baseline chemical and biological reactivity requirements for the selection of elastomeric injectable packaging/delivery system components.

The establishment of the potential suitability of an elastomeric component does not rely on a single testing strategy. No single strategy can cover all elastomeric component attributes that have the potential to impact suitability. The chemical testing prescribed includes physicochemical tests. Extractable elements may also be relevant to the selection of a packaging system's materials of construction.

It is the material user's responsibility to evaluate the need for extractable elements testing and, if such testing is necessary, to establish and justify the means by which testing is accomplished, taking into account extraction conditions, target elements, and reporting requirements. The physicochemical tests are also augmented with biological reactivity tests.

If elastomeric components comply with the requirements outlined in this chapter, studies should follow to determine their suitability as recommended in Assessment of Extractables Associated with Pharmaceutical Packaging/Delivery Systems 〈1663〉 and Assessment of Drug Product Leachables 〈1664〉.

In summary, establishing chemical suitability involves multiple tests including:

  • Elastomeric component screening—baseline requirements for biological reactivity and physicochemical tests described in this chapter.
  • Controlled extraction studies—studies as described in 〈1663〉 to create extractables profile(s) of particular pharmaceutical packaging/delivery systems.
  • Pharmaceutical product assessment—Actual-case measurement of confirmed leachables in the pharmaceutical product in the packaging/delivery system intended for the commercial market. (For additional information, see 〈1664〉.)

Additional information about elastomeric components is found in 〈1381〉.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Elastomeric Components（彈性體組件）：俗稱「橡膠零件」，包括瓶塞、活塞（plunger）、針頭護套（needle shield）等，是注射用藥品包裝系統中最關鍵的材料之一。

Thermoset（熱固性）vs Thermoplastic（熱塑性）：

  • 熱固性（如傳統丁基橡膠瓶塞）：成型後無法再加熱軟化，化學交聯網絡固定，相對穩定但含有硫化劑、促進劑等可萃取物
  • 熱塑性（如 TPE）：可重新加熱成型，通常添加劑較少，被認為萃取物風險較低，但機械性能可能不同

比喻說明 Analogy

〈381〉 的三層測試架構就像蓋房子的地基、結構、裝修三個階段：

  • 〈381〉基線篩查 = 地基測試（這塊土地能蓋房子嗎？最基本的安全確認）
  • 〈1663〉可萃取物研究 = 結構設計（這個材料在特定條件下會釋放什麼？量化風險）
  • 〈1664〉滲出物評估 = 真實居住測試（在實際產品中測量真實滲出量，最終確認）

三個階段缺一不可，且必須按順序進行。

重點提示 Key Notes

「使用者的責任」是 〈381〉 的重要哲學——章節明確指出，可萃取元素測試的需求評估及測試方法的建立，是使用者（user）的責任，而非 USP 提供統一方法。這反映了監管機構認為藥廠（或 CDMO）必須根據自身產品特性做出科學判斷，而非機械套用標準。

2. Scope — 適用範圍

原文內容 Original Text

Elastomeric components within scope are those used in the packaging systems of products described in Injections and Implanted Drug Products 〈1〉.

Elastomeric components utilized for injectable products within chapter scope include, but are not limited to, those used for:

  • vials and bottles (stoppers and cap liners)
  • prefilled syringes (plungers, needle shields, and tip caps)
  • cartridges (plungers and seal liners)
  • flexible bags (injection ports)
  • blow-fill-seal containers (cap liners)

The specified procedures and requirements outlined in this chapter are not applicable to elastomeric components used in products regulated by the Center for Devices and Radiological Health.

All elastomeric components in direct or indirect contact with the pharmaceutical product are within scope. An example of indirect contact is an elastomeric layer of a multilayer cap liner that does not directly contact the product but may leach into the product via migration through the product contact layer.

Also within scope are elastomeric components of systems or packages that are intended for transient product storage and/or product delivery intended for specific pharmaceutical products.

Elastomeric components outside of scope include those components of containers and closures that do not have direct or indirect contact with the pharmaceutical product or hold intermediate compounds, APIs, and excipients. Also outside chapter scope are elastomeric components of containment and/or transport systems used in product, intermediate compound, API, or excipient manufacturing. Although outside of the chapter scope, chapter tests and requirements may be applied.

Elastomeric component functional suitability tests described in this chapter are within scope until Elastomeric Component Functional Suitability in Parenteral Product Packaging/Delivery Systems 〈382〉 is fully implemented.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

直接接觸 vs 間接接觸 (Direct vs Indirect Contact)：

  • 直接接觸：組件表面直接與藥品接觸，例如西林瓶的瓶塞底面、預充式注射器的活塞
  • 間接接觸：組件本身不接觸藥品，但其滲出物可透過其他材料遷移至藥品中。例如多層瓶蓋內層橡膠（不接觸藥品），但化學物質可透過外層 LDPE 薄膜遷移至瓶內液體

間接接觸的概念是 〈381〉 的關鍵擴展——不能因為「沒有直接碰到藥品」就免除測試義務。

比喻說明 Analogy

直接/間接接觸的區別就像裝食物的包裝：直接接觸是塑膠袋直接裝食物；間接接觸是外層紙箱的墨水透過塑膠袋滲入食物。雖然墨水沒有「直接碰到」食物，但它仍然可能汙染食物——這就是為什麼間接接觸也要測試。

重點提示 Key Notes

Chapter 〈382〉 過渡條款：功能適用性測試（Functionality Tests，即穿刺力、碎屑等）目前在 〈381〉 中，但未來將移交給新章節 〈382〉 管理。在 〈382〉 完全實施前，仍需按 〈381〉 要求執行。CDMO 廠的 SOP 需要追蹤這個過渡時間表。

範圍外但「可以適用」：API 製造用容器的橡膠組件雖在章節範圍外，但「may be applied」——意味著監管機構或客戶可能要求你自願適用這些標準。這是灰色地帶，需要在品質協議中明確約定。

實務應用 Practical Application

CDMO 廠收到客戶提供的多層鋁塑瓶蓋（flip-off cap with multilayer liner），其中彈性體層不直接接觸藥品但可能有遷移風險。按照 〈381〉 的間接接觸定義，這個彈性體層在章節範圍內，需要執行相應的理化和生物反應性測試，即使它不是「藥瓶塞」。在包材審核時（packaging material qualification）需要主動識別此類情況。

3. Test Samples — 測試樣品

原文內容 Original Text

Test samples should mimic finished elastomeric components after the completion of all manufacturing and processing steps (e.g., molding conditions, sterilization, etc.), and surface modifications (such as siliconization, chlorinated surface treatments, fluoropolymer coatings and films).

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

表面處理（Surface Modifications）的種類與影響：

  • Siliconization（矽化處理）：塗覆矽油，降低摩擦係數，廣泛用於預充式注射器活塞。矽油本身需要評估對蛋白藥物的影響（可能引起蛋白聚集）
  • Chlorinated surface（氯化表面處理）：以氯氣處理表面，減少藥品萃取物，但改變表面化學性質
  • Fluoropolymer coating（氟聚合物塗層）：如 Flurotec®，在橡膠表面形成惰性薄膜，大幅降低萃取物，常用於高風險藥品（如蛋白藥、脂質製劑）

重點提示 Key Notes

測試樣品必須代表「最終完成品」，包括所有製造步驟和表面處理後的狀態。這意味著你不能用未經矽化的橡膠塞來代替矽化橡膠塞進行測試——表面處理會改變萃取物輪廓。在實驗室研究階段使用的樣品規格，必須與最終商業產品一致，否則驗證結果無效。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 如果一個橡膠塞在開發階段是「未塗層」的，但商業產品將使用「氟聚合物塗層」版本，開發階段的測試結果能否用於商業申報？
  2. 矽化處理對蛋白藥物（biologic）的影響是什麼？為什麼預充式注射器的矽化方式（噴塗 vs 烘烤）對產品穩定性很重要？

4.2 Physicochemical Tests — Sample Solution & Classification

原文內容 Original Text

Sample Solution

Place whole, uncut elastomeric components corresponding to a surface area of 100 ± 10 cm² into a borosilicate glass, wide-necked flask (see Containers—Glass 〈660〉). If it is not possible to achieve the prescribed closure surface area (100 ± 10 cm²) using uncut elastomeric components, select the number of components that will most closely approximate 100 cm² and adjust the volume of water used to the equivalent of 2 mL/1 cm² of the actual elastomeric component's surface area.

Add 200 mL of Purified Water or Water for Injection to the elastomeric components, and weigh. Cover the mouth of the flask with a borosilicate glass beaker, or similar non-interacting container. Heat in an autoclave so that a temperature of 121 ± 2° is reached within 20–30 min, and maintain this temperature for 30 min. Cool to room temperature over a period of about 30 min. Add Purified Water or Water for Injection to bring it up to the original mass. Shake, and immediately decant and collect the solution.

[Note—This solution must be shaken before being used in each of the tests.]

Blank

Prepare a blank solution similarly, using 200 mL of Purified Water or Water for Injection, omitting the elastomeric components.

Elastomeric component categories, Type I and II

Elastomeric components may be classified in two types: Type I elastomeric closures meet the strictest requirements and are preferred; Type II elastomeric closures have mechanical properties suitable for special uses (e.g., multiple piercing) but cannot meet the Type I acceptance criteria for 4.2.1 Appearance, 4.2.4 Absorbance, and 4.2.5 Reducing Substances. For these tests, Type II elastomeric closures have alternative acceptance criteria that must be met. The intended final product application will determine whether a Type I or Type II elastomeric component is more appropriate.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

樣品溶液制備邏輯（Surface Area Method）：

使用 100 cm² 表面積作為標準化基礎，而非重量，原因是萃取物主要從表面滲出，表面積更能代表實際接觸條件。若無法剛好達到 100 cm²，用實際面積乘以 2 mL/cm² 調整水量，保持萃取濃度的一致性。

高壓蒸汽萃取（121°C/30 min）：模擬最嚴苛的蒸汽滅菌條件，確保萃取出潛在可遷移的化學物質。這是「最壞情況萃取（worst-case extraction）」的概念。

公式與計算 Formula — 表面積調整

標準情況：
表面積 = 100 cm²
萃取水量 = 200 mL（即 2 mL/cm²）

調整情況（無法達到 100 cm²）：
假設實際表面積 = 85 cm²
→ 調整水量 = 85 × 2 = 170 mL

Type I vs Type II 差異化測試：
僅有 3 項指標有不同標準：
  • 4.2.1 外觀（渾濁度）
  • 4.2.4 吸光度
  • 4.2.5 還原性物質
其他 4 項（顏色/酸鹼/揮發性硫化物/銨）
對 Type I 和 Type II 使用相同標準

比喻說明 Analogy

Type I vs Type II 就像廚房砧板的等級：Type I 是玻璃砧板（最乾淨，幾乎不吸附細菌或化學物質）；Type II 是木頭砧板（有更好的耐用性和特殊功能，但可能有更多的物質釋出）。對於高風險食材（如生肉），你一定用玻璃砧板（Type I）；但對於切麵包，木頭砧板（Type II）的功能性優勢可能更重要。選擇哪種取決於最終用途。

4.2.2 Color

Fill one tube to a depth of 40 mm with Sample solution, and fill the second tube with the Color standard (3.0 mL Matching Fluid O diluted with 97.0 mL diluted hydrochloric acid). Compare the liquids in diffuse daylight, viewing vertically against a white background.

Acceptance criteria: Sample solution is not more intensely colored than the Color standard.

4.2.3 Acidity or Alkalinity

Add 0.1 mL of Bromothymol blue solution to 20 mL of Sample solution. If the solution is yellow, titrate with 0.01 N sodium hydroxide until a blue endpoint is reached. If the solution is blue, titrate with 0.01 N hydrochloric acid until a yellow endpoint is reached.

Acceptance criteria: NMT 0.3 mL of 0.01 N sodium hydroxide (to produce blue) or NMT 0.8 mL of 0.01 N hydrochloric acid (to produce yellow), or no titration required.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Turbidity / Opalescence（渾濁度 / 乳光）：衡量萃取液中懸浮顆粒含量的指標。渾濁度高代表橡膠材料可能釋出較多顆粒或膠體物質，增加藥品的可見異物風險。

NTU（Nephelometric Turbidity Unit，比濁度單位）：數值越高代表越渾濁。Type I 要求 ≤6 NTU，Type II 允許到 18 NTU，差距 3 倍，反映兩者在化學純度上的顯著差異。

Formazin（甲臜懸浮液）：一種化學標準，通過不同稀釋比例製成 A–D 四級標準比較液，提供可重現的渾濁度基準。

重點提示 Key Notes

酸鹼試驗的不對稱標準：NaOH 的限量（0.3 mL）遠低於 HCl（0.8 mL），代表 USP 對鹼性萃取物更為嚴格。原因是鹼性物質（如未反應的促進劑）對注射部位的刺激性通常比輕微酸性更高。

颜色試驗使用「Matching Fluid O」——這是 USP 官方顏色參考液，O 代表橙黃色（orangish），並非隨機選擇；它反映了典型橡膠萃取液可能出現的黃棕色調。

比喻說明 Analogy

渾濁度測試就像判斷白開水是否乾淨——你用一個已知「有多髒」的參考標準（Formazin 懸浮液）來比較你的水樣。NTU 越高，代表水越「像洗碗水」。Type I 是「礦泉水級別」，Type II 是「自來水級別」——兩者都能用，但用途不同。

4.2.4–4.2.7 Absorbance, Reducing Substances, Volatile Sulfides, Ammonium

原文內容 Original Text

4.2.4 Absorbance

[Note—Perform this test within 5 h of preparing Sample solution.]

Pass Sample solution through an inert filter of 0.45-µm pore size, discarding the first few milliliters of filtrate. Measure the absorbance of the filtrate at wavelengths between 220 and 360 nm in a 1-cm cell using the Blank in a matched cell in the reference beam.

Acceptance criteria: Type I—NMT 0.2; Type II—NMT 4.0.

4.2.5 Reducing Substances

[Note—Perform this test within 4 h of preparing Sample solution.]

To 20.0 mL of Sample solution add 1 mL of diluted sulfuric acid and 20.0 mL of 0.002 M potassium permanganate. Boil for 3 min. Cool, add 1 g of potassium iodide, and titrate immediately with 0.01 M sodium thiosulfate using 0.25 mL of starch solution TS as the indicator. Perform a titration using 20.0 mL of Blank and note the difference in volume of 0.01 M sodium thiosulfate required.

Acceptance criteria: Type I—NMT 3.0 mL of 0.01 M sodium thiosulfate. Type II—NMT 7.0 mL.

4.2.6 Volatile Sulfides

Place elastomeric components with a total surface area of 20 ± 2 cm² in a 100-mL flask, and add 50 mL of a 20-g/L citric acid solution. Place a piece of lead acetate paper over the mouth of each flask. Also prepare a control solution containing 0.154 mg of sodium sulfide. Heat in an autoclave at 121 ± 2° for 30 min.

Acceptance criteria: Any black stain on the paper produced by the test solution is not more intense than that produced by the control solution.

4.2.7 Ammonium

Dilute 5 mL of Sample solution with water to 14 mL. Add 0.3 mL of Alkaline potassium tetraiodomercurate solution and close the container. Prepare Ammonium standard solution [1 ppm of ammonium (NH₄⁺)].

Acceptance criteria: After 5 min, any yellow color in the Test solution is no darker than the Ammonium standard solution [NMT 2 ppm of ammonium (NH₄⁺) in Sample solution].

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

吸光度（Absorbance）220–360 nm：UV 範圍的吸光度偵測芳香族化合物、共軛系統等有機萃取物。Type I 限量 0.2（極嚴格），Type II 允許到 4.0（20 倍寬鬆），直接反映橡膠配方中有機添加劑含量的差異。

Reducing Substances（還原性物質）：用高錳酸鉀 (KMnO₄) 氧化還原性物質，間接測量橡膠中的抗氧化劑（antioxidants）、硫化助劑等可萃取物。這些物質可能影響藥品的氧化穩定性。

Volatile Sulfides（揮發性硫化物）：偵測硫化橡膠中未反應的硫（elemental sulfur）或硫化物，這是傳統丁基橡膠配方中的典型萃取物，可能影響某些金屬螯合藥物的穩定性。

Ammonium（銨離子）：某些橡膠製造過程使用氨作為製程化學品，殘留的 NH₄⁺ 可能干擾特定藥品或影響 pH，限量 2 ppm 確保安全。

重點提示 Key Notes

時間限制測試：吸光度試驗必須在制備樣品溶液後 5 小時內完成；還原性物質試驗必須在 4 小時內完成。這是因為萃取液中的化學物質可能隨時間氧化或降解，導致結果偏低（假陰性）。SOP 中必須明確記錄樣品制備時間。

揮發性硫化物試驗使用醋酸鉛試紙（lead acetate paper）——H₂S 遇到醋酸鉛會形成黑色硫化鉛（PbS），顏色深淺直接反映硫化物濃度。這是一個簡單但有效的半定量比色試驗。

實務應用 Practical Application

在 CDMO 廠進行包材供應商資格認證 (supplier qualification) 時，需要要求供應商提供符合 〈381〉 所有 7 項理化試驗的 Certificate of Analysis。關鍵注意點：要求供應商說明所使用的是 Type I 還是 Type II 標準，因為同一款橡膠在不同客戶的 spec 中可能有不同分類。如果 CDMO 代理客戶購買橡膠塞，必須確認其分類與最終藥品包裝的監管申請（如 NDA/BLA）保持一致。

4.1 Biological Reactivity — 生物反應性

原文內容 Original Text

Elastomeric components (Type I and Type II) meet the requirements of Biological Reactivity Tests, In Vitro 〈87〉. If components do not meet the requirements of 〈87〉, they can be subjected to in vivo testing set forth in Biological Reactivity Tests, In Vivo 〈88〉, Systemic Injection Test and 〈88〉, Intracutaneous Test. Components that meet the requirements of 〈87〉 are not required to undergo 〈88〉 testing.

Type I and Type II closures must both conform to the requirements of either the in vitro or the in vivo biological reactivity tests.

Acceptance criteria: Test selection and results are consistent with 〈87〉 and/or 〈88〉.

Footnote 1: "Biological reactivity testing in support of elastomeric components used for final pharmaceutical product packaging/delivery systems (drugs and drug/device combination products) provides baseline information and will often not be sufficient to assess the final suitability for use expectations of regulatory authorities. Thus, it is important to work with the appropriate regulatory authority for guidance regarding a product-specific application."

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

〈87〉 體外生物反應性試驗 (In Vitro Biological Reactivity)：使用細胞培養方法（如 MEM Elution, Agar Diffusion, Direct Contact）測試萃取液的細胞毒性（cytotoxicity）。體外試驗快速、成本低，是首選方法。

〈88〉 體內生物反應性試驗 (In Vivo Biological Reactivity)：當體外試驗不符合要求時，可改用小鼠（全身注射試驗）或兔子（皮內注射試驗）的體內試驗作為補充。符合 〈87〉 則不需要 〈88〉。

這個「先體外，不過再體內」的邏輯符合 3Rs 原則（Replace, Reduce, Refine）——盡量減少動物試驗。

重點提示 Key Notes

腳注 1 是一個非常重要的監管聲明：〈381〉 的生物反應性測試只提供「基線信息（baseline information）」，通常不足以滿足監管機構的最終適用性要求。這意味著：

  • 通過 〈87〉/〈88〉 測試 ≠ 監管批准
  • 特別是對於新型生物製劑、高敏感性製劑，還需要進行完整的 〈1663〉/〈1664〉 研究
  • 必須主動與監管機構（FDA、EMA）溝通，確認特定產品的要求

比喻說明 Analogy

生物反應性測試就像餐廳的食材衛生基礎檢查——通過衛生局基本檢查（〈87〉/〈88〉），只說明食材沒有明顯毒素，但這不代表它適合供應給有特殊飲食需求的客人（如過敏、特殊藥物）。針對特定患者（高風險藥品），還需要額外的深度評估（〈1663〉/〈1664〉）。基線就是最低門檻，不是終點。

實務應用 Practical Application

CDMO 廠承接一個 mRNA 疫苗客戶，要使用含氟聚合物塗層的橡膠塞。即使供應商提供了符合 〈87〉 的測試報告，你仍需要：（1）要求供應商提供特定批次的 CoA；（2）評估 〈1663〉 可萃取物研究是否已針對 mRNA 製劑的特殊條件（如低溫儲存、pH 範圍）執行；（3）在 CMC 模塊中向 FDA 說明包材選擇的完整科學依據。

4.3 Functionality Tests — 功能適用性試驗

原文內容 Original Text

[Note—Samples treated as described for preparation of Sample solution and air-dried should be used for the functionality tests of 4.3.1 Penetrability, 4.3.2 Fragmentation, and 4.3.3 Self-Sealing Capacity. Functionality tests are performed on closures intended to be pierced by a hypodermic needle. The 4.3.3 Self-Sealing Capacity test is required only for closures intended for multiple-dose containers. The needle specified for each test is a lubricated, long-bevel (bevel angle 12 ± 2°) hypodermic needle with an external diameter of 0.8 mm (21 gauge).]

4.3.1 Penetrability（穿刺性）

Fill 10 suitable vials to the nominal volume with water, fit the closures to be examined, and secure with a cap. Using a new hypodermic needle as described above for each closure, pierce the closure with the needle perpendicular to the surface.

Acceptance criteria: The force for piercing is no greater than 10 N (1 kilogram-force) for each closure, determined with an accuracy of ±0.25 N (25 gram-force).

4.3.2 Fragmentation（碎屑測試）

Closures for liquid preparations: Fill 12 clean vials with water to 4 mL less than the nominal capacity. Fit the closures to be examined, secure with a cap, and allow to stand for 16 h.

Closures for dry preparations: Fit closures to be examined into 12 clean vials, and secure each with a cap.

Procedure: Using a hypodermic needle as described above fitted to a clean syringe, inject into each vial 1 mL of water while removing 1 mL of air. Repeat this procedure 4 times for each closure, piercing each time at a different site. Use a new needle for each closure. Filter the total volume of liquid in all the vials through a single filter with a nominal pore size NMT 0.5 µm. Count the rubber fragments on the surface of the filter visible to the naked eye.

Acceptance criteria: There are no more than 5 fragments visible. This limit is based on the assumption that fragments with a diameter >50 µm are visible to the naked eye. In case of doubt or dispute, the particles are examined microscopically to verify their nature and size.

4.3.3 Self-Sealing Capacity（自密封性）

[Note—Required only for closures intended for multiple-dose containers.]

Fill 10 suitable vials with water to the nominal volume. Fit the closures that are to be examined, and cap. Using a new hypodermic needle as described above for each closure, pierce each closure 10 times, piercing each time at a different site. Immerse the 10 vials in a solution of 0.1% methylene blue, and reduce the external pressure by 27 kPa for 10 min. Restore to atmospheric pressure, and leave the vials immersed for 30 min. Rinse the outside of the vials.

Acceptance criteria: None of the vials contain any trace of blue solution.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

三項功能試驗的目的：

  • Penetrability（穿刺性）：確保護士/藥師能夠輕鬆穿刺橡膠塞，過緊的塞子會導致操作困難、針頭偏斜，甚至增加針刺傷風險。≤10 N（約 1 kg）的力度確保安全操作性。
  • Fragmentation（碎屑測試）：每次穿刺都可能切削下橡膠碎屑，這些碎屑進入藥液是嚴重的可見/不可見異物污染問題。≤5 碎屑的標準是保護患者的底線。
  • Self-Sealing Capacity（自密封性）：多劑量容器（multi-dose containers）被多次穿刺後，橡膠必須能「自動閉合」防止細菌進入。亞甲藍（methylene blue）的藍色是非常靈敏的洩漏指示劑。

公式與計算 Formula — 碎屑試驗設計邏輯

12 個西林瓶 × 5 次穿刺 = 60 次穿刺總計

每次穿刺在不同位置（different site）
→ 確保測試完整的瓶塞表面，
  而非集中在同一弱點

所有 12 瓶液體合併過濾（single filter）
→ 最大化碎屑收集效率

接受標準：≤5 碎屑（可見，>50 µm）
→ 若有疑義，顯微鏡確認材質和尺寸

注意：液體製劑需浸泡 16 小時
（模擬儲存期間的液體接觸效果）

重點提示 Key Notes

針頭規格的標準化意義：21 gauge、bevel angle 12±2° 是標準化試驗條件，確保不同實驗室的結果可比較。臨床上使用的針頭規格千變萬化（18G 到 27G），但測試用 21G 是通過科學研究選定的「代表性最壞情況」規格。在實際產品設計中，如果預期使用更大直徑的針頭（如 18G 用於黏稠藥品），可能需要額外測試。

自密封試驗的 27 kPa 負壓：模擬臨床取藥時注射器抽取藥液造成的瓶內負壓，這是最容易導致外部液體（包含細菌）被吸入的時機。如果橡膠在負壓條件下密封失效，外部的亞甲藍水溶液就會被吸入瓶內——這就是試驗的模擬邏輯。

比喻說明 Analogy

自密封試驗就像測試游泳圈（橡膠）被刺破多次後是否還能防水——你用針刺穿 10 個洞，然後把游泳圈（西林瓶）浸入有顏色的水（亞甲藍），並且製造負壓（像在拉游泳圈上的開口），看看有色水會不會滲入。如果沒有藍色，代表橡膠的自癒合（self-sealing）能力夠強，能在多次穿刺後依然保持屏障。

實務應用 Practical Application

在 CDMO 廠接受預充式注射器客戶時，活塞（plunger）的穿刺性測試通常不適用（活塞不被針頭穿刺），但碎屑問題仍然存在於組裝和推注過程。應重點評估：（1）活塞在注射力 (glide force) 下的矽油膜穩定性；（2）針頭護套 (needle shield) 的穿刺力；（3）多劑量藥瓶 (multi-dose vial) 的橡膠塞則需完整執行全部三項功能試驗。
