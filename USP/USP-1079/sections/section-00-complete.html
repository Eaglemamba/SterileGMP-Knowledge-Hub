Section 1: Introduction & Scope Section 1: 簡介與範圍

原文內容 Original Text

Proper storage and transportation of finished drug products are critical activities in an integrated supply chain. These finished drug products include but are not limited to temperature-sensitive small molecules, vaccines, biologics, biotechnological products, radiopharmaceuticals, and combination products.

Environmental controls play a key role in maintaining drug safety, quality, and efficacy. Temperature is one of the most important parameters to control. Drugs must be stored and transported according to predetermined conditions (e.g., temperature) as supported by stability data. Temperature excursions outside of their respective labeled storage conditions, for brief periods, may be acceptable provided that stability data and scientific/technical justification exist, demonstrating that product safety, quality, and efficacy is not affected.

To maintain the original quality, every party involved in the storage and transportation of a finished product should have an in-depth understanding of the storage and transportation risks and have the appropriate mitigation strategies in place to control these risks.

Scope

This chapter applies to organizations and individuals involved in the storage and transportation of drug products, including:

  • Manufacturers of drug products, radiopharmaceuticals, biological products, and biotechnological products
  • Repackaging operations
  • Healthcare providers and institutions (hospitals, clinics, etc.)
  • Pharmacies (retail, compounding, specialty, mail order, hospital, nursing home, hospice)
  • Importers and exporters
  • Wholesale distributors
  • Third-party logistics providers, brokers, freight forwarders, consolidators

Manufacturers of active pharmaceutical ingredients, excipients, packaging materials, medical devices, and dietary supplements are not within the scope of this chapter.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts — 為什麼儲運風險如此重要

製藥供應鏈的複雜性在於：一個藥品從工廠出發到患者用藥，可能經過數十個「手」。每一個節點都是潛在的風險點。USP 〈1079〉 存在的意義就是讓每個節點都有共同的風險語言和緩解框架。

  • 溫度敏感藥品（冷藏2-8°C、冷凍、超冷凍）數量越來越多（生物製劑、mRNA疫苗）
  • 全球化供應鏈 = 更多跨越氣候帶、跨越時區的運輸
  • 一次溫度超標可能導致整批產品報廢，損失可達數百萬美元
  • 更重要的是：影響患者能否獲得所需藥品

比喻說明 Analogy — 供應鏈就像接力賽

把藥品供應鏈想成一場馬拉松接力賽：製造商是第一棒，批發商是第二棒，物流是第三棒，藥局是第四棒，患者是終點。每個跑者都必須在規定的溫度「跑道」上完成自己的那一段，並且在交接棒時確認藥品狀態完好。任何一棒「掉棒」（溫度超標、文件缺失），不只是那一棒的失敗，而是整個接力的失敗。

重點提示 Key Notes — CDMO的範圍認知

CDMO（委託開發製造機構）在本章的範圍內屬於「製造商」角色。生產完成後的成品在交給客戶/批發商之前，所有儲存和運輸活動都是CDMO的責任範圍。此外，如果CDMO提供一站式服務包含物流安排，則須符合本章對物流服務商的要求。建議在客戶合約中明確界定各方的儲運責任邊界。

Section 2: Risk-Based Approach — Product Knowledge, Risk Identification & Mitigation Categories Section 2: 風險導向方法 — 產品知識、風險識別與緩解分類

原文內容 Original Text

Figure 1 illustrates the risk-based approach of a quality management system (QMS). Product and process knowledge facilitate the identification of risk. Mitigation strategies that are planned to reduce the identified risks, categorized in clusters, form the pillars of a QMS.

Product knowledge includes: intended use; storage conditions; potential hazards to environment and personnel (e.g., hormones, cytotoxic drug products, and radiopharmaceuticals); and inherent vulnerability (e.g., high potential for abuse, high-value drugs, counterfeiting, and diversion).

Process knowledge includes: knowledge of supply chain partners; physical modes of transportation (air, sea, rail, road, or combination); transportation routes; and national and international regulation.

Risk identification: The systematic use of information to identify potential sources of harm (hazards). Addresses the question: "What might go wrong?"

Mitigation strategies (risk reduction): Addresses the question: "What can be done to reduce or eliminate risks?" Generally fall within four categories:

  1. Documentation — providing instructions for a specific operation or process to standardize it and establish consistency
  2. Training — ensuring competence
  3. Resources — providing capability through infrastructure and human resources
  4. Qualification and Validation — assurance that the resources and processes are reliable, reproducible, and robust

Risk assessment tools: flow charts, process mapping, trends, historical data records, FMEA, FTA, HACCP, HAZOP, and PHA.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts — 四大緩解類別的邏輯

USP 〈1079〉 的四大緩解策略不是隨機的，而是構成一個完整的風險控制閉環：

  • 文件（Documentation）：「我知道該怎麼做」— SOP、品質手冊、標籤
  • 培訓（Training）：「我有能力做到」— 確保人員理解並能執行文件
  • 資源（Resources）：「我有工具可以做」— 設施、設備、人員配置
  • 確效/驗證（Q&V）：「已證明能做得好」— 溫度圖測繪、包裝確效、軟體驗證

缺少任何一類，其他三類的效果都會打折扣。例如：有SOP（文件）但沒有培訓，等於白寫；有設備（資源）但沒確效，不知道能不能用。

比喻說明 Analogy — FMEA思考法

在儲運風險分析中，最實用的工具是FMEA（失效模式效應分析）。把它想成「最壞劇本」腦力激盪：「如果冷藏車空調壞了，會發生什麼？嚴重度？發生頻率？能否提早偵測到？」三個問題的評分乘積（RPN）決定風險優先級。RPN越高，緩解措施越要優先到位。在夏天台灣的高溫環境下，「空調失效」的嚴重度和發生頻率都更高，所以RPN更高，應對措施應更嚴格。

重點提示 Key Notes — 恐懼舉報是隱性風險

Table 1中有一個容易被忽略的風險：「害怕舉報不合規事件（Fear of reporting nonconformance）」。這是組織文化問題，也是最危險的風險之一——因為它是無形的。解決方法：建立獨立的品質舉報機制（不直接向生產主管匯報）；教育員工「舉報問題是保護患者，不是找麻煩」；高層以身作則，對誠實報告給予正面回應而非懲罰。

Section 3: QMS Elements — Documentation (Quality Manual, SOPs, Labels, Quality Agreements, Excursion Handling) Section 3: 品質管理系統（QMS）要素 — 文件（品質手冊、SOPs、標籤、品質協議、偏差處理）

原文內容 Original Text

4.1 Documentation and Procedures

Documentation provides written information that allows for consistency and traceability of actions. Some examples of documentation include quality manuals; standard operating procedures (SOPs); labeling; records pertaining to procurement, receiving, storage, and transportation; supplier qualification records; quality agreements; recalls; and excursion-handling records.

4.1.1 Quality Manual

A quality manual is a top-level quality document for all areas of the business affected by the quality system. Contains the quality policy, quality objectives, quality system structure, and management information. May include inspection management, customer complaints, recalls, withdrawals and holds, corrective and preventive actions, nonconformance and change control, regulatory issues, and performance evaluation through quality indicators.

4.1.2 Standard Operating Procedures

SOPs are controlled documents with document owners and approvers, effective dates, revision management, and scheduled reviews. Key SOP areas include: CAPA; Documentation; Record keeping; Inventory management; Licensing; Management reviews; Nonconforming product; Order processing; Purchasing; Picking; Packing and shipping; Receiving; Returns; Storage; Training.

Storage and transportation: Two approaches to keep product within labeled specifications: 1) Controlling environmental conditions (HVAC, refrigerators); 2) Using packaging materials (passive/thermal packaging, thermal blankets, temperature stabilizers, desiccants, light-resistant material).

4.1.3 Labels

Labels are fundamental to material identification. Labels applied, even to small containers, should be clear, indelible, unambiguous, and permanently fixed. The shipping label should include wording or icons to emphasize storage and transportation conditions, handling requirements, and hazards.

4.1.4 Quality Agreements

Written agreements should be in place between applicable organizations involved in the supply chain. Each supply chain partner should ensure that its respective service level agreements cover delivery and receiving responsibilities.

4.1.5 Excursion Handling

Short-term temperature excursions can occur during distribution, storage, and transportation. Each excursion should be documented and handled with a deviation or appropriate risk assessment. Product disposition should be established on the basis of:

  • Assessment of the excursion (temperature to which exposed, and for how long)
  • Stability data from traditional stability studies (accelerated, intermediate, long-term)
  • Distribution stability studies (thermal cycling, freeze-thaw studies)
  • Mean Kinetic Temperature (MKT)

MKT should be calculated for the period of time that a drug is in residence at a warehouse and/or in transit on a truck to avoid the problem of diluting the impact of excursions by calculating annual MKT values.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts — 溫度超標（Excursion）的評估框架

溫度超標不是自動報廢，而是需要科學評估：

  • 第一步：確認超標詳情（最高溫度、持續時間、超標幅度）
  • 第二步：查閱穩定性數據（加速穩定性試驗數據，確認在該溫度條件下可接受的暴露時間）
  • 第三步：計算MKT（若為累積多次小幅超標）
  • 第四步：品質評估 → 放行/拒絕/進一步測試
  • 第五步：開立偏差報告，記錄評估依據和結論

不能憑感覺決定，一切必須有文件依據。

比喻說明 Analogy — MKT就像「累積熱傷害指數」

平均動力學溫度（MKT）的概念類似運動員的「累積疲勞指數」。一個跑者跑了10次馬拉松，每次稍微超出心率上限，雖然每次都「勉強可以」，但累積下來的損傷是真實的。同樣，藥品在倉庫存放半年，每天溫度略高1-2度，用年度MKT計算可能「看起來還好」，但如果用在倉期間的MKT計算，可能已經超出安全範圍。USP特別強調應計算實際存放期間的MKT，而非稀釋到全年度。

公式與計算 Formula — MKT基本概念

MKT（平均動力學溫度）公式：

MKT = -ΔH/R ÷ ln[Σexp(-ΔH/RTi)/n]

其中：
- ΔH = 活化能（通常使用83.144 kJ/mol，即ICH默認值）
- R  = 氣體常數（8.314 × 10⁻³ kJ/mol·K）
- Ti = 各時間點的絕對溫度（Kelvin = °C + 273.15）
- n  = 測量次數

簡單理解：MKT是一個「等效恆溫」的概念。
如果藥品在MKT條件下恆溫儲存，其化學降解
速率與實際變溫儲存的降解效果相同。

MKT 通常高於算術平均溫度（因為高溫的
影響是指數級的，算術平均低估了熱損傷）。

重點提示 Key Notes — 品質協議是CDMO的保護盾

USP強調品質協議（Quality Agreement）在供應鏈中的重要性。對CDMO而言，這意味著：在成品交付前，必須與客戶/批發商/物流商簽訂品質協議，明確規定：交付溫度要求、交付時的溫度記錄提供方式、溫度超標的通報時限、責任歸屬（CDMO責任到哪裡，物流商責任從哪裡開始）。沒有品質協議，發生糾紛時責任難以釐清。

Section 4: Training, Resources (Storage & Transportation) & Qualification and Validation Section 4: 訓練、資源（儲存與運輸）及資格確認與驗證

原文內容 Original Text

4.2 Training

Training is a teaching-learning process used in the workplace to provide knowledge and develop skills and behaviors. The objective of training is to reduce the gap between the existing competencies and those required for performing the work.

A written training procedure is necessary to establish who can be the trainer, how training needs are identified, and how training effectiveness will be evaluated. SOPs are the foundation of a quality system, and training must be provided for each SOP. Training records should be maintained either as hard copies or electronically.

As a risk mitigation strategy, training should be performed before the SOP becomes effective. All employees whose actions have an impact on product quality and security should have initial and ongoing training.

4.3 Resources — Storage

The facility should be designed to maintain the quality and integrity of the stored drug product. Buildings should accommodate: Security; Product characteristics (narcotics, radiopharmaceuticals); Product status (approved, recalled, returned, quarantined); Required storage temperature; Ease of cleaning; Logical flow; Mix-up prevention; Ergonomics; Product demand; Environmental controls.

Drug product storage areas are required to maintain temperature between the limits defined on the product label. Product storage areas/units should utilize recording systems to log and track temperatures. Alarm systems should be an integral part of the monitoring system.

4.3.2 Transportation

All vehicles used in supply chain activities should be suitable for the intended purpose because they are providing in-transit storage and should prevent exposure to conditions that affect stability and package integrity.

4.4 Qualification and Validation

4.4.2 QUALIFICATION: Temperature mapping is performed to provide information relative to temperature consistency throughout a product area. Factors affecting temperature variability: size of space; location of HVAC; sun-facing walls; low ceilings; geographic location; airflow; external temperature variability; workflow variation; loading patterns; equipment capabilities; SOPs.

When developing a thermal package qualification protocol, consider: Transportation temperature profile for the shipping lane(s); Delivery cycle time, accounting for reasonable delays; Testing beyond qualification time for worst-case data; Seasonal changes; Use of seasonal (warm/summer and cool/winter) configurations; Testing with actual payload; Variable order sizes; Probe placement inside or directly attached to the product; At least 3 replicate tests on a representative package per season.

4.4.3 Validation of Information Systems

Before an information system that can impact the quality of the product in storage or in transportation is brought into use, it should be demonstrated through appropriate validation or verification studies that the system is capable of achieving the desired results accurately, consistently, and reproducibly.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts — 溫度圖測繪（Temperature Mapping）

溫度圖測繪是冷鏈管理的核心技術活動。其目的不只是「通過確效」，而是真正了解你的倉儲/運輸環境：

  • 識別熱點（hot spots）和冷點（cold spots）
  • 確認HVAC系統能否維持均勻溫度
  • 建立「安全儲存區域」的空間範圍
  • 設定溫度感測器的最佳安裝位置
  • 為SOP提供科學依據（如：禁止在門口附近存放溫度敏感產品）

USP特別強調11個可能影響倉儲溫度均勻性的因素，每一個都可能是你倉庫裡的「溫度陷阱」。

比喻說明 Analogy — 包裝確效就像「快遞壓力測試」

熱包裝確效（Thermal Package Qualification）就像快遞公司在出新款包裝盒前做的壓力測試：把裝了模擬商品的包裝盒放在各種惡劣環境下（高溫夏季、低溫冬季、延誤24小時）→ 全程記錄內部溫度 → 確認藥品溫度始終在規格範圍內 → 記錄成確效報告 → 這個包裝方案才可以用於實際運輸。USP要求每個季節至少3次重複測試，因為夏天和冬天的挑戰完全不同。

重點提示 Key Notes — 培訓的「時效性」要求

USP明確要求：培訓必須在SOP生效前完成。這個要求聽起來簡單，但在多班制工廠（三班輪替）中執行起來很有挑戰性。最佳實踐：SOP發布後設置生效緩衝期（如7-14天），在此期間完成所有相關人員的培訓，確認培訓記錄後才讓SOP正式生效。另外，「再培訓（Re-training）」同樣重要——SOP修訂後，所有相關人員必須重新受訓，這個流程應由培訓管理系統（LMS）自動追蹤和提醒。

公式與計算 Formula — 熱包裝確效測試矩陣

熱包裝確效測試設計最低要求：

測試條件 × 季節配置（至少2季）：
  夏季配置（Warm/Summer）× 3次重複
  冬季配置（Cool/Winter）× 3次重複

若使用環境控制箱（Qualified Environmental Chamber）：
  每季可減為 1次重複

每次測試須記錄：
  - 測試開始的預調溫條件（Product + Coolant conditioning）
  - 全程溫度曲線（探針在產品內部或最脆弱位置）
  - 總運輸時間 + 額外延遲時間（worst case）
  - 超標事件（如有）及處理記錄
  - 最終合格/不合格結論

注意：測試時間應超過標稱確效時間（qualification time），
以獲取「超出期限」的數據用於超標偏差評估。

實務應用 Practical Application

CDMO成品倉庫冷藏區溫度圖測繪計劃要點：①安裝資料記錄儀（每15分鐘記錄一次）在20個以上測量點 ②連續記錄至少7天（涵蓋工作日和假日，因工作日開門次數多）③分析結果識別熱點/冷點 ④根據結果調整貨品排放規則（如：最靠門的貨架不放最敏感產品）⑤年度重新進行溫度圖測繪（或倉庫格局重大改變後）。這是ISO 13485和GMP共同要求的基礎活動。
