13. Instrument Standardization Tests | 儀器標準化測試

原文內容 Original Text

ISTs are used to verify that the instrument performs in accordance with the manufacturer's specifications upon calibration and continues to provide accurate and consistent results between calibrations. ISTs, as described in this section, are equivalent to the performance qualification (PQ) in 〈1058〉 and are intended to verify the acceptable performance of the instrument for its intended use. See 〈1058〉 for an in-depth description of PQ, operational qualification (OQ), and system suitability. These tests are also applicable for MM but in a much-simplified approach (see Membrane Microscope Method for the Determination of Subvisible Particulate Matter 〈1788.2〉).

ISTs for LO and FI include several tiers. Instrument calibration is the most comprehensive set of tests, and PQ tests provide assurance that the instrument meets all key performance requirements. Other verification tests provide daily assurance that the instrument performance is acceptable.

13.1 Diameter Calibration

Perform instrument calibration according to the instrument manufacturer's instructions. The count accuracy of an instrument can be significantly affected by the size accuracy of the instrumental particle counter. Count accuracy is also dependent upon the sensor used and may vary with individual sensors of the same model, as described previously.

Perform a system suitability test upon completion of the instrument calibration to ensure that the particle sizes of interest for the application (i.e., 10 and 25 µm) and other sizes (e.g., 5 and 15 µm), are accurately reported and that the count accuracy is verified. Count accuracy should be performed during or after the diameter accuracy is confirmed. Particle count accuracy may be assessed using commercial microsphere count standards. For LO and FI, corrections are incorporated into instrument software to give accurate particle diameters of the calibration microspheres.

13.2 System Suitability Tests

The key attributes of a particle counting system are that it counts particles accurately and that the level of background counts is acceptably low. Count accuracy may be assessed by routine measurement of microsphere count standards. Background counts may be assessed by routine measurement of blanks or filtered formulation. These two types of tests are the core system suitability tests.

A few factors influence how often a system suitability test should be run:

  • Do the test samples tend to clog flow cells in the LO and FI methods?
  • Do the particles in the test sample tend to adhere to wetted surfaces, thereby causing increased counts in subsequent blanks or test sample runs?
  • What are the consequences if assay failure is detected at a date later than when the assay was run?

13.2.1 System Suitability Failure

Instruments that fail system suitability tests can often be remediated by thorough cleaning or by evaluating the particle standard used (these can aggregate over time). If failure of the standard is ruled out, the manufacturer's directions should be followed for thoroughly cleaning all fluid lines and the sensor head. Back-flushing to remove partial clogs may also be beneficial. The instrument is deemed to be suitable for use if it passes the system suitability tests after cleaning. Flush the system to remove traces of the particle standard prior to routine use.

Depending upon the dosage form being tested, proceed as directed under the appropriate category in 14. Calculations.

核心概念 — IST vs SST 的區別

IST（儀器標準化測試）：相當於 PQ，是較全面的性能確認，包括：

  • 樣品體積準確性
  • 樣品流速確認
  • 直徑校準
  • 感測器尺寸解析度
  • 資格考量

通常在新儀器安裝、重大維修、重新校準後執行，頻率較低（月度/季度）。

SST（系統適用性測試）：每次測試前/後的例行確認，核心是「計數準確性」和「背景計數水準」兩個要素。

重點提示 — SST 執行頻率的決策因素

USP 不規定 SST 的具體頻率，而是要求根據風險評估決定。關鍵問題是：

  1. Flow cell 堵塞風險：高黏度或高微粒樣品 → 每個樣品前後都測
  2. 粒子沾附殘留風險：蛋白質類等易沾附樣品 → 樣品間增加空白測試
  3. 後發現失敗的後果：若在日後才發現儀器失效，被影響的批次可能已放行 → 高風險產品需更頻繁 SST

比喻說明 — SST 失敗的處理順序

就像你的溫度計顯示異常：先懷疑溫度計本身（換新的微球標準品），再懷疑測量環境（清洗儀器），最後聯繫製造商。從最簡單、最可能的原因開始排查，而不是立刻宣布儀器故障。

USP 給出的排查順序：檢查微球標準品是否聚集 → 清洗流路和感測器頭 → 反向沖洗去除局部堵塞 → 聯繫製造商。

14. Calculations | 計算方法

原文內容 Original Text

14.1 Pooled Samples

Average the counts from the aliquot portions analyzed. Calculate the number of particles in each container using the following formula:

PΣ≥10 × VT / VA / n   (for ≥10 µm threshold)
PΣ≥25 × VT / VA / n   (for ≥25 µm threshold)
PΣ≥50 × VT / VA / n   (additionally for 〈789〉)

where P is the average particle count per threshold obtained from all portions analyzed; VT is the volume (mL) of pooled sample; VA is the volume (mL) of each portion analyzed; and n is the number of containers pooled.

14.2 Individual Samples

Average the counts obtained for the aliquot portions from each separate unit analyzed and calculate the number of particles in each container:

PΣ≥10 × V / VA   (for ≥10 µm threshold)
PΣ≥25 × V / VA   (for ≥25 µm threshold)
PΣ≥50 × V / VA   (for 〈789〉)

where P is the average particle count per threshold from all portions analyzed; V is the volume (mL) of the tested unit; VA is the volume (mL) of each portion analyzed. For individual unit samples measured directly, the results are reported as P per unit volume (counts per milliliter).

14.3 Individual Unit Samples

Average the counts obtained for the aliquot portions taken from the solution unit. Calculate the number of particles in each milliliter of product solution taken:

PΣ≥10 / VA   (for ≥10 µm threshold)
PΣ≥25 / VA   (for ≥25 µm threshold)
PΣ≥50 / VA   (for 〈789〉)

where P is the average particle count per threshold from all portions analyzed; VA is the volume (mL) of the portion taken.

For all types of product, if the tested material has been diluted to decrease the viscosity, the dilution factor must be accounted for in the calculation of the final test result.

[Note—For all methods, when reporting particle load the particle counts must be reported in cumulative mode. If the instrument has been configured to count in differential bins (e.g., ≥10–24 µm, >25–50 µm, >50 µm), all bins ≥10 µm must be added to yield a total ≥10-µm count and all bins ≥25 µm need to be added to yield a total 25-µm count. Reports should clearly state the diameter limits, the units of particle counts (e.g., counts per container or counts per milliliter), and that the particle loads are for all particles greater than or equal to the stated diameter limits.]

公式解析 — 三種計算模式的情境對應

14.1 合併樣品（Pooled Samples）：
  適用：多個小體積容器合併後測試
  結果表示：每容器的粒子數
  計算：平均計數 × 總體積 / 每次取樣量 / 容器數

14.2 個別樣品（Individual Samples）：
  適用：每個容器分別測試（對應 〈787〉 策略）
  結果表示：每容器的粒子數
  計算：平均計數 × 容器體積 / 每次取樣量

14.3 個別單位樣品（Individual Unit Samples）：
  適用：直接測量容器內容物，結果以濃度表示
  結果表示：每毫升粒子數（counts/mL）
  計算：平均計數 / 每次取樣量

注意：所有方法若有稀釋 → 乘以稀釋因子

核心概念 — 累積計數 vs 差分計數

Cumulative Mode（累積模式）：≥10 µm 的計數 = 所有尺寸 ≥10 µm 的粒子總和

Differential Mode（差分模式）：儀器可能設定為 10–24 µm, 25–49 µm, ≥50 µm 分段計數

USP 要求報告必須是累積模式。若儀器以差分模式記錄，在計算時必須將各段相加：

  • ≥10 µm 報告值 = (10–24 µm) + (25–49 µm) + (≥50 µm)
  • ≥25 µm 報告值 = (25–49 µm) + (≥50 µm)

重點提示 — 個別取樣 vs 合併取樣的戰略選擇

〈788〉 傳統上採用合併取樣（pooled sampling），而 〈787〉 建議個別取樣：

  • 合併取樣優點：適用小體積容器，統計簡單，取樣量充足
  • 個別取樣優點：可評估批內異質性（inter-vial variability），避免合併操作引入微粒

現代趨勢（尤其對蛋白質藥品）是盡量採用個別取樣，以獲得更真實的批次品質圖像。

實務應用 — 計算報告的格式要求

USP 明確要求報告應包含：

  • 計數直徑門檻（e.g., ≥10 µm, ≥25 µm）
  • 計數單位（每容器 或 每毫升）
  • 說明是累積計數（all particles ≥ stated diameter）

在 CDMO 實務中，CoA（出廠檢驗報告）應清楚標示所有上述資訊，避免客戶對結果單位（per container vs per mL）的誤解，尤其在 SVI 和 LVI 混合產品線中。

Glossary | 術語詞彙表

原文內容 Original Text

Diameter bias:
Deviation of a derived particle diameter from the actual diameter due to technique parameters and limitations.
Diascopic illumination:
Transmitted light illumination for transparent to translucent samples.
Episcopic illumination:
An internal illumination path coincident with the optical path, providing top-reflected light for the detection of thin, flat particles and films.
Equivalent circular diameter:
The diameter of a standard sphere that produces an equivalent electronic pulse or other signal to an analyte particle.
Feret's diameter:
The dimension of an object along a specified direction, also known as caliper diameter.
Flow imaging:
An instrumental system configured to collect digital images of solids in sample fluid flowing between a light source and a camera.
Fouling:
Plugging or coating of a particle counting system sensor or flow cell; may be a full blockage, partial blockage, or an extraneous substance affecting the analysis.
Image analysis, dynamic:
A software program used to collect images from a flowing display, store the images, and retrospectively analyze the collected data.
Image analysis, static:
A software application used to collect images and discriminate the images from a live, unchanging image.
Light obscuration:
An optical particle-sensing technique that records impulses on a photodetector from obscuration of a source illumination by analyte particles. Particle size correlates with the response curve of a known spherical standard.
Longest chord:
The longest dimension that can be measured across the perimeter of an object.
Membrane microscopy:
A particle determination method that isolates solids from product samples by filtration and then examines them with a light microscope to count the collected material in size thresholds.
Operational qualification (OQ) / Performance qualification (PQ):
Essential elements of method validation: OQ specifies the principal operational elements of the device in both hardware and method controls; PQ verifies the acceptable performance characteristics for a specified instrument OQ.
Optical particle counter:
An instrument that provides detection, sizing, and enumeration of substances in a fluid medium using visible spectrum illumination and an electronic detector.
Particle concentration:
Number of particles (count) per unit volume.
Particle count accuracy:
An evaluation of the capability of an instrumental system to collect particle counts for evaluation against a standard.
Particle size:
Visible: In the context of pharmaceutical products, the size at which one can visualize a single particle within a final product package. USP has set a conservative threshold at 100 µm. Subvisible: 2–100 µm. Nanoparticles: <2 µm.
Refractive index (RI):
The numerical ratio of the speed of light through a vacuum over the speed of light through a substance. The RI of water is 1.33, with fluid oils ranging to 1.50 and solids spanning a wide range upwards from 1.34. RI is wavelength specific.
Relative standard deviation / Coefficient of variation:
A measure of dispersion of a probability distribution. It is the ratio of the standard deviation to the mean and is expressed as percentage.
Schlieren effects:
These are regions of varying refraction in a transparent medium that are often caused by differences in temperature or pressure and are detectable by optical means, generally by the passage of a beam of light through the medium.
Sheath flow:
A technique using a "wall" of higher-viscosity fluid encasing a lower-viscosity analyte sample flowing through it, thereby producing a limited focal plane due to hydrodynamic channels.
System suitability:
A measurement of the system output with a known standard that demonstrates acceptable performance of the system, often the result of several operational parameters.
Validation:
Validation of an analytical procedure is the process by which it is established, by laboratory studies, that the performance characteristics of the procedure meet the requirements for the intended analytical applications. This typically includes measurement of method accuracy, precision, specificity, detection limit, linearity, range, and robustness. Described in 〈1225〉.

核心概念 — 等效圓徑 vs 費雷特徑的差異

不同的測量方法報告不同的「尺寸」，理解其定義對解讀結果至關重要：

  • Equivalent Circular Diameter（等效圓徑）：LO 和 FI 使用此定義——產生相同信號強度的標準球體直徑。對非球形粒子有系統性偏差。
  • Feret's Diameter（費雷特徑）：MM 使用此定義——從任一方向測量到的粒子最大線性尺寸。更接近粒子的「實際」尺寸。

因此，同一個纖維狀粒子，LO 和 MM 給出的「尺寸」可能差異懸殊，這並非誤差，而是方法定義不同。

重點提示 — Schlieren 效應的實際影響

Schlieren Effect（史里倫效應）：溫度或組成梯度造成局部折射率差異，使 LO 儀器看到「假粒子」（實際上是光學干涉）。

常見觸發條件：

  • 冷凍後剛解凍的樣品（溫度梯度）
  • 高濃度蛋白溶液（濃度梯度）
  • 碳酸氫鹽緩衝液（氣泡產生局部折射率差異）

處理方法：讓樣品充分平衡至室溫，充分混勻後再測試。

比喻說明 — 折射率對偵測的影響

想像在游泳池水面下拍照：透明的果凍（折射率接近水）幾乎看不見，但不透明的石頭（折射率差異大）清晰可辨。LO 偵測的原理類似——粒子與溶液的折射率差異越大，信號越強，偵測越準確。蛋白聚集體的折射率（~1.35）非常接近水（1.33），因此 LO 對它們的偵測靈敏度天生較低。

  2. 一個分析師發現 LO 儀器計數異常高，懷疑是 Schlieren 效應。如何確認並排除此干擾？

Appendix 1: Historical Development of USP Chapters on Subvisible Particulate Matter

The determination of subvisible particulate matter in injections was initiated in 1975 as an MM method for large-volume injections (LVI) in USP XV. This one method was quite effective at revealing the content of unwanted solid particles ≥10 µm in the product, because filtration could accommodate a wide range of product volumes and the microscopist could evaluate the retained solids. However, the microscope method was much slower per sample than corollary instrumental techniques and required high-efficiency particulate air (HEPA) environmental hoods for isolation operations, with special microscope count training for the analysts.

Higher throughput with a robust electronic method was sought. Historically, the British Pharmacopoeia had utilized electrical sensing zone (Coulter counter) counting for all particles ≥2 µm; however, the method required dispersion of the product in an electrolyte solution, which did not work for certain products and formulations.

The current USP subvisible particle determination methods were established in 1986 for determination of particulate matter in sterile injections, and in 1995, an improved version of this methodology was adopted following extensive development by an expert volunteer group. The MM method was improved, and the combination of LO and MM was proposed for use as a two-stage test for both LVI and small-volume injection (SVI) products. In 〈788〉 and 〈789〉, "small-volume parenteral" is synonymous with SVI, and "large-volume parenteral" is synonymous with LVI.

Appendix 2: Cleaning Procedures to Limit Particle Contamination

Cleanse glassware, closures, and other required equipment, preferably by immersing and cleaning the items using warm, nonionic detergent solution. Rinse in flowing tap water, and then rinse again in flowing filtered water. Organic solvents may also be used to facilitate cleaning. [Note—These steps describe one way to clean equipment; alternatively, particle-free equipment may be obtained from a suitable vendor.] Preferably, the test specimen, glassware, closures, and other required equipment are then given a final rinse with filtered water using a hand-held pressure nozzle with final filter or other appropriate filtered water source within an environment protected by HEPA cabinets. While conducting the assay, non-shedding garments and powder-free gloves are worn within the HEPA environment.

For example, prepare the test specimens in the following sequence. Outside of the unidirectional airflow cabinet (UAFC) or HEPA filters to be used for the test, remove outer hardware or sealing bands. Rinse the exteriors of the containers with filtered water. Protect the containers from environmental contamination until analyzed.

Within the UAFC, open the containers and withdraw, pour, or otherwise sample the contents of the containers for testing in a manner least likely to generate particles that could enter the test specimen. Contents of containers with removable stoppers may be withdrawn directly by removing the closures. A sampling device with a needle to penetrate the unit closure may also be used. Products packaged in flexible plastic containers may be sampled by cutting the medication or administration port tube or a corner from the unit with a suitably cleaned razor blade or scissors.

核心概念 — USP 微粒章節的歷史脈絡

理解歷史可以幫助理解為什麼現行標準是這樣設計的：

  • 1975：僅有 MM 方法，針對 LVI——因為當時的大包裝點滴是主要產品
  • 1985：LO 方法納入——自動化革命，大幅提升了 QC 效率
  • 1986：LO+MM 正式成為 USP 標準
  • 1995：現行 〈788〉 框架確立，LO+MM 兩步驟測試適用於 LVI 和 SVI
  • 2024（〈1788〉 現行版）：正式將 FI 方法納入資訊性章節

實務應用 — 玻璃器皿清潔 SOP 要點

Appendix 2 的清潔流程對於實驗室 SOP 建立至關重要。關鍵步驟：

  1. 預清潔：非離子清潔劑溫水浸泡清洗
  2. 沖洗 1：流動自來水
  3. 沖洗 2：流動過濾水
  4. 最終沖洗：HEPA 保護環境內，壓力噴嘴 + 終端過濾水
  5. 個人防護：不掉屑的無塵服 + 無粉手套

清潔效果以空白測試驗證——這是品質系統的閉環。

比喻說明 — 歷史發展的方法論教訓

LO 方法在 1985 年取代 MM 成為首選，就像銀行從人工點鈔換成點鈔機：速度快、一致性高，但機器本身的誤差（例如把外幣當本幣計數）需要周期性用人工抽查來驗證。這就是為什麼 MM 仍保留為 「裁判方法（referee method）」——當 LO 結果異常時，用 MM 確認真相。

USP 〈1788〉 完整章節總結 Complete Chapter Summary

  • 方法體系：LO（首選、自動化）、MM（裁判方法、保留微粒）、FI（形態學資訊、正交方法）各有優勢和局限，正交使用是最佳實踐

  • 計算要點：三種計算模式（合併/個別/個別單位）對應不同取樣策略；稀釋後必須乘以稀釋因子
  • 量測品質：IST（PQ 等效）→ SST（每次確認）→ 空白測試（環境和操作確認）三層次保證結果可信
  • 術語精確：等效圓徑 vs 費雷特徑、折射率對偵測靈敏度的影響、Schlieren 效應的識別——這些概念直接影響結果解讀的正確性
  • 歷史脈絡：從 1975 年 MM 到 1985 年 LO 到 2024 年 FI，每次演進都是科技能力與臨床需求的交匯
  • CDMO 實踐：建立內部微粒資料庫、設定比 USP 更嚴格的內部目標值、在開發早期即導入多種分析方法——這是高品質製造的必要投資
