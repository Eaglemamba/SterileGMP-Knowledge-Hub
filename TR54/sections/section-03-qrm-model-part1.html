Section 6.0 — Complete Generic Risk Model (Figure 6.0-1)

PDA TR54-6 presents a complete generic risk model for excipient risk management that integrates two primary dimensions: intrinsic excipient risks (properties inherent to the excipient itself) and supply chain risks (risks arising from how the excipient is sourced, manufactured, and delivered). Together these feed a structured, iterative QRM process.

中文解說

核心概念：兩大風險維度

TR54-6 的 QRM 模型從兩個方向同時評估藥用輔料風險：

  • 內在風險（Intrinsic Risk）：輔料本身的理化特性、來源、污染潛力、功能複雜度
  • 供應鏈風險（Supply Chain Risk）：製造商品質體系、物流管控、包裝完整性、多來源問題

這兩條線最終匯入同一套風險評分（ERPN），形成整體風險輪廓。

比喻：QRM 模型就像「體檢流程」

整個模型就像醫院的年度體檢：先確定受檢項目（Initiate），再測量各項指標（Identify）， 然後判讀報告（Analyse）、與正常值對比（Evaluate）、開立改善處方（Reduce）， 最後由醫生簽字放行（Accept）。每年還要複檢（Review）。

供應商稽核就像為供應鏈把脈——確認「外部環境」是否健康。

重點：模型循環性

QRM 不是一次性作業。任何變更事件（Change Event）都是重新觸發模型的起點， 包括：更換供應商、產品新適應症、法規要求變更。 這與 ICH Q9 的「風險管理是持續過程」原則完全一致。

Section 6.1 — Initiate Risk Management Activities and Identify Assessment Team

The risk assessment process begins with the identification of a need to qualify an excipient or to review an existing excipient risk assessment (e.g., at new product introduction, as a result of obtaining a second source, being notified by the supplier of major changes, or one of the numerous other drivers discussed below in Section 7.1).

A successful excipient risk assessment program requires a holistic approach encompassing knowledge of the excipient's use in the pharmaceutical dosage form as well as knowledge of the excipient's manufacturer and supply chain. The assessment team should include individuals from various functional areas, including subject matter experts (SME) from the drug product manufacturer who understand the excipient's use in the drug product, as well as individuals from the functions involved in oversight of the supply chain.

The risk assessment process might include participants with defined roles and responsibilities in the functional QRM areas and others, such as:

  • QRM — risk assessment facilitator knowledgeable about the methodology and tools
  • QRM experts — area or function leads for QRM or the QRM decision-makers
  • Quality Assurance — focused on use and function
  • Quality — focused on oversight activities related to supply chain (e.g., excipient manufacturer, supplier, transport)
  • Qualified / Responsible Person
  • Material Sciences
  • Quality Control
  • Regulatory Affairs
  • Technical / Operations
  • Sterility Assurance
  • Manufacturer or Supplier Auditing
  • Procurement
  • Outsourcing Operations — oversight activities
  • R&D
  • Engineering
  • Nonclinical Statistics
  • Legal
  • Manufacturing
  • Medical / Clinical
  • Safety and Pharmacology / Toxicology

Having a multidisciplinary team of individuals working collaboratively to gather and review data and assess the potential for risk is critical to ensuring that all risks are considered holistically. The excipient risk management process should be built into a company's existing processes and quality management system (QMS).

Section 6.2 — Identify Intrinsic Risk Factors

In identifying the excipient's intrinsic risk factors, numerous factors should be considered. One primary source is input from product and process development. The Quality Target Product Profile (QTPP) of the drug product(s) in which the excipient is to be used provides requirements for the drug product, and this forms the basis of information about the use and function of the excipient.

The manufacturing authorization holder assesses the drug product requirements to develop the drug product critical quality attributes (CQA) and to establish the foundation for drug product design and development. During the drug product's design and development, scientific studies are conducted and information is collected concerning the excipient, particularly about how it may interact with the product's other components.

Drug product development and process design studies, commercial manufacturing, and associated risk assessments for this product and any other products in which the excipient has been used, provide historical information about the excipient that can be used to assess its impact on the targeted drug product.

In addition, examples of parameters that can be considered as excipient attributes include:

  • Characteristics of excipient needed for use in the drug product
  • Manufacturer's excipient specifications
  • Factors learned from the manufacture of the excipients
  • Existence of multiple and/or global manufacturers
  • Lot-to-lot variability
  • Risk factors listed in the EC Guidelines of March 19, 2015, such as:
    • Transmissible spongiform encephalopathy (TSE)
    • Potential for viral contamination
    • Potential for microbiological or endotoxin/pyrogen contamination
    • Potential for any impurity originating from raw materials (e.g., aflatoxins, pesticides) or generated as part of the process (e.g., residual solvents and catalysts)
    • Sterility assurance for excipients claimed to be sterile
    • Potential for any impurities carried over from other processes, in the absence of dedicated equipment and/or facilities
    • Environmental control and storage/transportation conditions, including cold chain management if appropriate
    • Excipient stability
    • Packaging integrity evidence

中文解說

重點：給藥途徑是最關鍵的差異化因子

在所有內在風險因素中，給藥途徑（Route of Administration）是風險等級的主要分水嶺：

高風險：注射、眼用、開放性傷口 中風險：吸入 / 肺部 低風險：口服、局部、陰道、直腸

無菌注射劑工廠的輔料，幾乎全部落在高風險區間（RFE3 = 5）。 這代表即使是「惰性」輔料如氯化鈉，其微生物 / 內毒素風險也必須嚴格控管。

核心概念：QTPP → CQA → 輔料屬性鏈

Section 6.2 建立了一條清晰的邏輯鏈：

  1. 藥品需求（QTPP）定義了產品應有的品質標準
  2. 從 QTPP 推導出關鍵品質屬性（CQA）
  3. 逐一分析哪些輔料屬性可能影響 CQA
  4. 有影響的屬性，進入下一步的風險分析

這是 ICH Q8 Product Development 與 QRM 的橋接點。

比喻：食材採購的溯源邏輯

識別內在風險因素就像主廚在採購食材前問：「這個食材是動物源還是植物源？ 加工過程有沒有用到過敏原？批次間穩定性如何？」 確認每個屬性後，才決定要多嚴格地驗收。

Section 6.3 — Excipient Risk Analysis

The excipient risk analysis can be carried out in two steps. First, an initial excipient impact assessment can be conducted to provide input to the internal specification of the excipients, to identify intrinsic risk factors relating to the excipient's impact on the drug product. This assessment is used in the second step — the subsequent risk-scoring of the excipients.

Figure 6.3-1 (Steps in Excipient Risk Identification and Analysis) illustrates the sequential flow from an identified need for an excipient through risk identification and analysis to a final excipient risk score (ERPN), categorized as High / Medium / Low.

Section 6.3.1 — Impact of Excipient on Each Type of Drug Product

After identification as described in Section 6.2, the excipient attributes are assessed for correlation to the CQAs of the drug product in which the excipient is to be used. The rationale of the impact must be explained. This step must be taken for each drug product or drug product family. Then, the results can be consolidated across products.

Initially, all potential risk factors (hazards) are identified and documented as excipient attributes. Then, CQAs are correlated to the pharmaceutical dosage form using the following notation:

+ Known Impact (documented effect) − No Impact (no indication of impact) ? Impact cannot be ruled out; more information may be needed

The information can be compiled in a table such as that shown in Figure 6.3.1-1. A column marked “Compliance” is included near the CQAs so that compliance with, for instance, pharmacopeial specifications, can be ensured as well. This column allows the assessors to force such excipient attributes further into the assessment and into the excipient specification.

Figure 6.3.1-1 (illustrative) — Excipient attributes mapped against drug product CQAs. Columns reflect typical categories for a parenteral dosage form.
+ = Known Impact  |  − = No Impact  |  ? = Cannot be ruled out

Only excipient attributes with a correlation marked “+” are brought forward as needed into the specification for further analysis. The attributes marked “?” should be further investigated to obtain data necessary to determine whether there is potential impact. If further data cannot be gathered, “?” will be treated as “+”, taking a worst-case approach.

From the failure analysis, the excipient user gains understanding of potential and allowed variation in the attributes. With this knowledge, specification limits for each material attribute are proposed to mitigate the risk of impact. In this way, the excipient impact assessment is used to justify the excipient specification.

中文解說

核心概念：風險排名與篩選方法

6.3.1 的影響評估，本質上是一個「篩選器」：只有被標記為 + 或 ? 的輔料屬性，才需要進入後續規格制定與風險評分。

這個邏輯稱為「Risk Ranking and Filtering」——先排序重要性，再集中資源處理高風險項目。

+ → 進入規格 & ERPN ? → 調查；無法確認則視為 + − → 本次不列管

這種「worst-case 優先」的處理方式，確保了風險不被低估。

重點：Compliance 欄的特殊作用

表格中的 Compliance 欄（法規合規性）是一個強制性過濾門： 即使某個輔料屬性對 CQA 沒有直接影響，只要藥典規格或法規要求必須檢驗， 就必須強制納入輔料規格中。

實務例子：某輔料的含水量對產品效能沒有影響（CQA 欄全為 −）， 但若 EP/USP 單論中有含水量規格，仍需保留在 CoA 上。

注射劑 CDMO 的實際應用

在無菌充填工廠，以下輔料屬性幾乎必然被標記為 +：

  • 微生物限度 / 生物負荷（Bioburden）
  • 內毒素 / 熱原（Endotoxin / Pyrogen）
  • 可見異物 / 微粒（Particulate Matter）
  • TSE 風險聲明（適用動物源輔料）
  • 殘留溶劑（Residual Solvents）

這些屬性直接關係到注射劑的安全性 CQA，因此幾乎所有注射劑輔料的影響評估結果 都會驅動嚴格的供應商規格要求與入廠檢驗計畫。

針對一個用於靜脈注射劑的聚山梨酸酯 80（Polysorbate 80, PS80）：

  • 哪些輔料屬性你預期會被標記為 +？
  • PS80 的過氧化物含量（Peroxide Value）對蛋白質穩定性有潛在影響，你會標記 + 還是 ?？
  • 如果製造商無法提供過氧化物含量的歷史數據，你應該如何處置？

Section 6.3.2 — Excipient Risk Profile Based on Use and Intrinsic Factors

The second part of the excipient analysis is creating a risk profile (or ranking) using a question-based risk ranking and filtering system.

At a minimum, the following factors related to the excipient and its use should be considered in developing the risk score, as explained further in Table 6.3.2-1:

  • Excipient attributes and relevant specification (RFE1)
  • Functionality of the excipient in formulation (RFE1)
  • Intended patient intake — consider dose regimen, frequency, strength (RFE2)
  • Route of administration (RFE3)
  • Excipient origin and potential for contamination (RFE4)
  • Excipient complexity (composite) (RFE5)
  • Prior knowledge and experience with excipient (RFE6)
  • Standard packaging size of the excipient (RFE7)

Table 6.3.2-1 — Excipient Risk Calculation Tool. Each RFE scores 1 (Low), 3 (Medium), or 5 (High).

Each selection will result in a value of 1 (low), 3 (medium), or 5 (high) for risk. The risk score for the excipient (ERPN) is calculated as the sum of the risk scores for each of the seven identified risk factors (RFE), divided by the number of risk factors. The resulting ERPN will be in the range of 1 (Low) to 5 (High).

Overall Excipient Risk Score (ERPN)
ERPN = (RFE1 + RFE2 + RFE3 + RFE4 + RFE5 + RFE6 + RFE7) ÷ 7

2 Users would adjust the calculation as necessary to reflect additional risk factors identified for consideration or to weight the individual risk factors based on the company's analysis and risk tolerance.

中文解說

ERPN 公式解析

ERPN（Excipient Risk Priority Number）是 TR54-6 的核心輸出指標：

ERPN = f(Impact × Probability)

ERPN = (RF_E1 + RF_E2 + RF_E3 + RF_E4 + RF_E5 + RF_E6 + RF_E7) / n

n = 風險因子數量（預設 7；可擴充）
每個 RF_E：1（低）、3（中）、5（高）
ERPN 範圍：1（最低風險）～ 5（最高風險）

注意：ERPN 採平均值而非加總，避免因增加因子數量而自動拉高分數。 若公司加入額外 RFEx，分母也相應增加。

公司也可為各 RFE 加入加權係數（Weight），以反映企業特定風險容忍度。 例如，注射劑工廠可給 RFE3（給藥途徑）更高的權重。

核心概念：七個風險因子的業務邏輯

  • RFE1 功能性：防腐劑 / 溶劑化劑是高風險，因為若品質不達標，藥效或安全性直接受損
  • RFE2 劑量攝入：只有「是否超過最大允許日劑量」的二元判斷（無中等）
  • RFE3 給藥途徑：注射 = 5，口服 = 1，這是最大的單一差異化因子
  • RFE4 污染潛力：動物源原料自動為高風險（TSE / 病毒）
  • RFE5 複雜性：複合輔料（如某些植物萃取物）若成分未被追蹤，視為高風險
  • RFE6 使用經驗：新輔料或有缺陷歷史的輔料 = 高風險
  • RFE7 包裝規格：需要再分包或精密稱量的輔料，增加操作失誤風險

注射劑 CDMO 的 ERPN 應用案例

以一個來自豬源的明膠（Gelatin）用於靜脈注射劑為例：

RFE 評分 理由
RFE1 功能3黏合劑 / 增稠劑
RFE2 劑量1在 FDA IID 允許範圍內
RFE3 途徑5靜脈注射
RFE4 污染5豬源，TSE / 病毒風險
RFE5 複雜性1非複合型輔料

RFE7 包裝3需再分包，但環境受控
ERPN (3+1+5+5+1+3+3) ÷ 7 = 3.0 中等風險 → 需強化供應商管理

Section 3 Summary — 本節重點總結

English Key Takeaways

  • The TR54-6 QRM model integrates two primary risk dimensions — intrinsic excipient properties and supply chain risks — into a single iterative process aligned with ICH Q9.
  • Section 6.1 emphasizes the need for a multidisciplinary team and clear trigger events before risk assessment can begin.
  • Section 6.2 establishes the QTPP → CQA → excipient attribute chain, with EC 2015 guidelines providing the base list of intrinsic risk factors.
  • Section 6.3.1 uses a three-symbol notation (+, −, ?) to filter which attributes proceed into specification development. Uncertain attributes are treated as worst-case (+).
  • Section 6.3.2 produces the ERPN score by averaging seven risk factors (RFE1–RFE7), each rated 1/3/5, with a final score range of 1 (lowest) to 5 (highest).
  • Route of administration (RFE3) is the single most impactful differentiator — parenteral/ophthalmic/open wound always scores 5.

中文核心要點

  • QRM 模型是雙軌整合（內在風險 + 供應鏈風險），並非單一線性評估。
  • 風險評估啟動有明確觸發條件，需要跨職能團隊共同完成。
  • 影響評估採用 +/−/? 三符號篩選，「?」在無法進一步確認時，必須以最壞情況處理。
  • ERPN 是 7 個風險因子的平均值（1–5），代表輔料整體風險輪廓。
  • 給藥途徑（RFE3）是最重要的單一決定因素：注射劑 = 5 分，自動進入高風險管理框架。
  • ERPN 結果（High / Medium / Low）決定了後續供應商審計頻率、入廠檢驗計畫及持續監控強度。
