10.0

Annex I: Real World Examples of Risk Assessments

附錄一：實際風險評估案例總覽

10.0 Annex I: Real World Examples of Risk Assessments

The generic model proposed in Section 6.0 was developed based on real-world risk assessment processes donated by several marketing authorization holders. Many risk assessment approaches used some form of risk ranking and filtering approach—conducting assessments in two separate streams that can then be combined in an overall risk score.

One stream would provide a risk profile for the excipient itself, based on its use in the finished product and any intrinsic risk factors. The second stream would generate a risk profile for the supply chain, including the excipient manufacturer, suppliers, brokers, and any other players, as well as the strength of their quality management systems (QMS). The two streams would then be combined into one risk evaluation to determine whether the overall risk is at an acceptable level or additional controls are needed.

"The generic model proposed in Section 6.0 was developed based on real-world risk assessment processes donated by several marketing authorization holders."

解說與應用 Commentary

核心概念：兩流評估架構

PDA TR54-6 的附錄一揭示了一個貫穿所有案例的共同模式：雙流評估（Two-Stream Assessment）。

  • 第一流 — 賦形劑風險：基於製劑功能、給藥途徑、原料來源（動物性/植物性/礦物性/合成）評估內在風險
  • 第二流 — 供應鏈風險：評估製造商、供應商、中間商的品質管理系統（QMS）強度
  • 合流決策：兩個分數合併，決定整體風險等級與所需控制措施

這個框架是後續六個案例理解的基礎。

比喻：飛機安全審查

想像飛機的安全審查：第一流就像評估飛機本身的設計風險（機型、用途、載重），第二流是評估航空公司的維護體系（公司規模、認證、維修記錄）。兩者合起來才能決定這趟航班的整體風險等級。

賦形劑風險評估也是如此：即使是同一種賦形劑，來自不同供應鏈的風險可能截然不同。

重點：本節的學習價值

這六個案例（U~Z）並非學術範例，而是真實的全球製藥公司在符合 EC Guidelines（2015 年歐盟指引）要求下所建立的系統。每個案例都代表一種不同的實施路徑，沒有絕對的「最佳做法」，只有最符合公司規模與複雜度的選擇。

10.1

Example 1: Company U

案例一：公司 U — 加權 RPN 矩陣分析法

10.1 Example 1: Company U

Company U is a global manufacturer of various pharmaceutical dosage forms. The company established a Microsoft® Excel® tool to be used as a corporate standard.

When multiple manufacturing sites use the same excipient provided by the same excipient manufacturing site, the risk assessments can be shared across all sites (with appropriate site-specific modifications to accommodate excipient usage, supply chain complexity, and other factors).

First, basic information about the excipient and the manufacturer is collected. Then, the collected information is consolidated to produce an overview.

For each section of the model, the parameters to evaluate are defined (see Figure 10.1-1), each parameter is described in detail, and possible scenarios are developed. The severity of each parameter is assigned a standard value. In conducting the evaluation, Company U determines the actual probability of each parameter.

The analysis is conducted as follows:

  • Excipient and excipient usage risk score — Each of the identified excipient and excipient use risk factors is evaluated based on the actual origin or usage of the excipient and the actual supply chain to determine the probability of risk (P). The predefined weight, or severity (S), is used to calculate the risk score for each parameter (P × S). Then, the overall excipient risk score is calculated as the average across the parameters.
  • Combined material risk score — A combined material risk score is calculated by adding the excipient risk score and the excipient usage risk score.
  • Manufacturer risk score — The list of risk factors to be considered is evaluated based on the actual performance and QMS of the manufacturer. The predefined weight (S) is used to calculate the risk score of each parameter (P × S). Then, the overall manufacturer risk score is calculated as the average across the parameters.
  • Gap analysis and actions — The combined material risk score and the manufacturer risk score are evaluated together using a risk matrix to identify the "gap level," as illustrated in Table 10.1-1. Where a gap exists between the manufacturer risk score and the combined material risk score, the gap must be evaluated to determine the appropriate remediation.

解說與應用 Commentary

此案例展示的核心方法：加權 RPN 矩陣

公司 U 採用機率 (P) × 嚴重性 (S) = 風險分數的加權計算方式，這是來自 ICH Q9 的 RPN（Risk Priority Number）概念。

  • P（Probability）：實際評估時，依賦形劑的真實來源與使用情境判定
  • S（Severity）：預先定義的標準加權值，確保跨批次評估的一致性
  • Gap Analysis Matrix：2×2 風險矩陣，將賦形劑組合風險與製造商風險交叉比對，得出需補救的落差等級

特別之處：多個製造廠可以共享同一份評估，再加入廠點特定修改，節省大量重複工作。

比喻：供應商評鑑+原料風險的交叉比對

想像公司採購部門評估一個零件供應商：

  • 第一欄：這個零件本身有多重要（高功能性 = 高賦形劑風險）
  • 第二欄：這家供應商的品質有多穩定（工廠認證差 = 高製造商風險）

重要零件 + 不穩定供應商 = 一定要採取行動。公司 U 的矩陣正是把這個直觀判斷系統化。

關鍵觀察：Excel 工具的優缺點

公司 U 使用 Excel 作為企業標準工具。這在中型公司很常見，優點是靈活易用、成本低，缺點是版本管控困難、難以自動觸發複審。後面的案例（公司 V、Z）都在討論如何用電子系統解決這個問題。

思考練習

在你的 CDMO 環境中，如果有五個製造廠都使用同一種乳糖（lactose）作為賦形劑，且都來自同一供應商——你會為每個廠分別做評估，還是採用公司 U 的「共享評估＋廠點修改」模式？哪個方式的 GMP 文件管控風險更低？

CDMO 應用建議

對於同一賦形劑跨多廠使用的情形，建議建立母版評估（Master Assessment）加廠點附件（Site Annex）的架構。母版由中央品質部門維護，廠點附件負責記錄廠點特定的使用量、給藥途徑差異與供應鏈路徑。

10.2

Example 2: Company V

案例二：公司 V — 全球 SOP + 加權 RPN 三分法

10.2 Example 2: Company V

Company V is a global manufacturer of pharmaceutical and biopharmaceutical solid oral dosage forms and lyophilized injectables. To a large extent, its products are manufactured through contract manufacturing organizations (CMOs).

The risk model (Figure 10.2-1) was created to meet the European Commission Guidelines on The Formalised Risk Assessment for Ascertaining the Appropriate GMP For Excipients of Medicinal Products For Human Use (EC Guidelines), and the risk factors to be considered reflect those in the guidelines (1). The model was implemented in a global SOP that applies for all sites manufacturing commercial products and, going forward, for new products, including biologics and other type of products.

This model was extended for products during their transfer into commercial production. The quality risk assessment tool is applied based on risk ranking and filtering, evaluating the risk factors in two groups:

  • Multiple criteria analysis
    • Function and use of excipient (→ Score Excipient)
    • Manufacturer (→ Score Manufacturer)
    • Suppliers (→ Score Supplier)
  • Risk score for each excipient (low, medium, or high)
    • Independent risk factors
    • Risk ranking and weighing
    • Overall weighing

The three single scores—Function and Use, Manufacturer, and Suppliers—were combined by multiplication into a weighted RPN score. Acceptability was evaluated based on the RPN value, and mitigation actions were considered according to the overall risk score (low, medium, or high).

Risk Assessment Model for Company V
(Three-score model: Excipient Score × Manufacturer Score × Supplier Score = Weighted RPN)
Figure 10.2-1: Risk Assessment Model for Company V (p31)

Outcomes and Lessons Learned:

  • No major issues were found for existing products, though some minor changes in audit strategy and other controls were instituted as a result.
  • One benefit of performing the risk assessment was obtaining the collected knowledge and an overview of the supply chains.
  • Company V has found that responsibilities for information-gathering need to be changed in the global SOP. Currently, the global support function gathers the needed information from different stakeholders. Using one point of contact for each product is under discussion as an alternative.
  • While the model has been well accepted, it has not yet been used as a living risk assessment as much as would be liked.
  • The risk assessment is stored in the GMP documentation system. Software automatically triggers formal review and approval cycles globally. More flexibility to revise and update the risk assessment would be appreciated, especially in cases where the risk assessment is completed before the drug product is commercially available. The company will introduce a new electronic tool to serve this purpose.

解說與應用 Commentary

此案例展示的核心方法：三維乘法 RPN

公司 V 比公司 U 多增加了一個維度——獨立評分供應商（Supplier），形成三分法：

  • Score Excipient（賦形劑功能與使用分數）
  • Score Manufacturer（製造商分數）
  • Score Supplier（供應商/中間商分數）

三者相乘（乘法 RPN），而非相加，能放大任何一個高風險維度的影響力——若任何一項分數極高，整體 RPN 會顯著上升，避免被其他低分項目平均掉。

此外，全球 SOP 標準化確保了跨 CMO 廠的一致執行。

比喻：供應鏈三角審查

如同評估一家餐廳的食品安全：

  • 食材本身的風險（Score Excipient）：生食 vs. 熟食
  • 農場/製造商的品質（Score Manufacturer）：認證有機農場 vs. 非認證
  • 物流/配銷商（Score Supplier）：冷鏈是否完整

三個環節任何一個出問題，食品安全風險都會倍增。公司 V 的乘法模型正是體現了這個道理。

重點觀察：Living Risk Assessment 的挑戰

公司 V 坦承「尚未如期望中那樣做到 Living Risk Assessment」——這是許多公司共同面對的痛點。初次建立評估不難，難在建立觸發複審的機制（製造商更換、品質事件、新供應商加入時自動提示），以及讓文件系統足夠靈活、不需要走完整的 GMP 審批流程才能更新。

思考練習

公司 V 採用「乘法 RPN」（三分相乘），公司 U 採用「加法再矩陣」。哪個方法對於 CDMO 的賦形劑評估更適合？想想你公司裡哪些賦形劑可能有「製造商分數很低但賦形劑本身風險很高」的組合——在加法模型和乘法模型中，這個組合會得出不同的優先順序嗎？

CDMO 應用建議

對於大量委外的 CDMO 環境，建議在全球 SOP 中明確指定單一聯絡窗口（One Point of Contact）負責每項賦形劑的資訊彙整——公司 V 正在討論這個改善方向。同時，評估系統應設計自動觸發條件（change control、supplier audit 結果），避免評估停留在靜態文件。

Example 3: Company W

案例三：公司 W — 科學導向定性評估（CQA 關聯分析）

10.3 Example 3: Company W

Company W is a global manufacturer of pharmaceuticals and devices, primarily parenteral products.

As part of the material assessment, the company examines the correlation of impact between parameters (attributes) describing the excipient and the CQAs of the product in which it is an ingredient. An excipient assessment provides input to material specifications, as illustrated in Figure 10.3-1. In this way, the company evaluates the impact of the excipient on the finished drug products.

Attributes are derived by ideation from a defined list of potential risk sources. Attributes with impact are included on the specification, and limits or other controls are defined after analyzing the possible impacts of variation in the attribute. This method uses a qualitative assessment with a simple +/– score for impact combined with text rationales and references describing the underlying science. The impact is evaluated considering the amount of material to be used and its function in the formulation.

The result of the assessment is used to define the excipient's specification based on the specific use in a specific drug product. An assessment is made for each use in a drug product.

Figure 10.3-1
Risk Assessment Model for Company W
(Attribute-CQA Impact Model: Excipient attributes → Material specification → Drug product CQAs)
Figure 10.3-1: Risk Assessment Model for Company W (p32)

Supply Chain Assessment (in parallel):

An assessment of the supply chain is carried out in parallel with the material assessment. The supply chain—including manufacturer, retailers, brokers, and others—is evaluated using a predefined list of risk elements, brainstorming possible relevant issues in the specific supply chain for the particular excipient. The identified risks are then evaluated qualitatively for impact, likelihood, and controls in place.

If a risk becomes evident, the company identifies any additional controls that would reduce the risk to an acceptable level.

解說與應用 Commentary

此案例展示的核心方法：CQA 連結的科學評估

公司 W 是六個案例中最以科學品質屬性（CQA）為核心的方法。其邏輯是：

  • 從潛在風險來源清單，藉由腦力激盪（Ideation）衍生出賦形劑的可評估屬性（Attributes）
  • 每個屬性以+/− 定性評分判斷是否影響成品 CQA
  • 有影響的屬性自動納入賦形劑規格（Specification），並設定允許限度
  • 每個藥品用途都做獨立評估（product-specific + use-specific）

這個方法直接與 ICH Q8/Q9 的品質源於設計（QbD）思維對接。

比喻：食品成分對健康指標的影響分析

想像評估一個食品配方的健康風險：

  • 鈉含量 → 影響血壓（CQA）？+（有影響）
  • 添加劑顏色 → 影響血壓？−（無影響）
  • 鈉含量 → 影響腎臟負荷？+（有影響）

有影響的項目（+）才需要設規格限制。公司 W 的 +/− 評估法讓科學理由透明化，比純數字評分更容易被監管機構理解。

重點觀察：最接近法規期望的方法

FDA 和 EMA 在審查賦形劑風險評估時，最希望看到的是「科學理由（scientific rationale）」而非單純的數字評分。公司 W 的定性 +/− 加文字依據（text rationale and references）的方法，在法規審查中往往最能說服審查員。缺點是工作量大——每個藥品用途都需獨立評估，難以規模化。

思考練習

你公司目前的賦形劑規格是如何制定的？有沒有明確的文件記錄「為什麼這個規格限度設在這裡」——也就是公司 W 所說的 text rationale？如果沒有，這在法規審查（如 EMA MAA）中可能是哪類缺失（observation）？

CDMO 應用建議

對於注射劑（parenteral）賦形劑，建議採用類似公司 W 的CQA 連結評估。實作上，可在賦形劑規格設定文件中增加「CQA Impact Table」，列出每個賦形劑屬性、對應的成品 CQA、影響判斷（+/−）及科學依據來源（ICH、USP、文獻）。這個表格可直接作為 dossier 的支持文件。

10.4

Example 4: Company X

案例四：公司 X — 產品品質經理主導的跨部門協作模式

10.4 Example 4: Company X

Company X expects its suppliers to comply with the IPEC-PQG Joint Good Manufacturing Practices Guide (2), EXCiPACT™ Standard (3), or NSF/IPEC/ANSI 363 (4). The excipient risk management process considered input from the manufacturer qualification process, audits, relationships, testing, and outside intelligence. The company also worked with its CMOs to align expectations between them and the manufacturing authorization holder and rationalized scores. The actual process is owned by the company's organizational product quality management unit and follows the model illustrated in Figure 10.4-1.

The product quality manager (PQM) is responsible for pulling together the information and subject matter experts from across Company X to provide the data needed to complete the spreadsheet. Much of the information comes from the company's audit team. The model allows for rationalization across materials and suppliers to ensure that scoring is consistent and can be readily justified.

Company X maintains an SOP that describes the overall process, including triggers for revisiting a risk assessment. Because the PQMs also are involved in the change control process for company materials, they have firsthand knowledge of any changes that would require a review of the excipient risk assessment. As part of this process, Company X has already made several revisions to some excipient risk assessments.

Initially, there was some resistance to the concept and process. Now that the system is in place, however, staff have noticed that it is easier to find information without going into several systems or speaking to several departments.

解說與應用 Commentary

此案例展示的核心方法：PQM 主導的跨部門整合

公司 X 的特色不在於評估工具本身，而在於組織架構設計：

  • 由產品品質經理（PQM）擔任資訊整合者，召集各領域 SME（稽核、測試、法規、CMO 關係管理）
  • PQM 同時參與變更管控（Change Control），因此能第一時間識別哪些變更需要重新評估
  • 評估結果允許跨物料、跨供應商的合理化比對（Rationalization），確保評分一致性
  • 明確的 SOP 包含觸發複審的條件（Triggers for Revisiting）

公司 X 的方法解決了「誰負責更新」這個最常見的組織痛點。

比喻：案件負責人制度（Case Manager）

公司 X 的 PQM 就像法律事務所的案件負責人——不是所有工作都自己做，而是負責匯集所有相關人員的資訊、做出最終判斷，並追蹤後續行動。這種「一個窗口」的設計，比各部門各自維護分散資訊的方式更有效率，也更不容易遺漏。

重點觀察：變革管理的成功故事

公司 X 坦承「初期遭遇抵抗（resistance）」，但系統建立後員工開始體會到益處——資訊更易查找。這是推行任何新 GMP 系統時典型的採用曲線（adoption curve）。關鍵成功因素：PQM 的角色設計讓評估系統與既有的 change control 流程有機結合，而非額外負擔。

另一個值得注意的點：公司 X 與其 CMO 協作，對齊期望並合理化評分——這對 CDMO 環境尤其重要。

思考練習

在你的組織中，誰應該扮演 PQM 的角色——品質部門？採購部門？法規部門？當供應商提出重大變更（賦形劑製造廠搬遷）時，現有的 change control 流程有沒有自動觸發賦形劑風險評估複審的機制？如果沒有，這個缺口如何填補？

CDMO 應用建議

建議在 CDMO 的賦形劑管理 SOP 中，明確定義觸發複審的條件清單，例如：供應商更換、製造廠遷址、稽核發現重大缺失、法規要求變更、品質事件（QE/CAPA）、產品配方修改等。並將這些觸發條件與 change control 或 supplier change notification 流程直接連結，確保評估是動態的（Living Document）。

10.5

Example 5: Company Y

案例五：公司 Y — 六準則加權工具與稽核頻率決策

10.5 Example 5: Company Y

The model illustrated in Figure 10.5-1 applies to finished products for human use that Company Y manufactures for European markets.

Company Y manufacturing sites completed risk ranking of each excipient from each manufacturer used at their sites.

A risk ranking tool is used to evaluate each excipient from each manufacturer. A weight of 1 (low), 3 (medium), or 10 (high) is assigned for each of six criteria and, from that, an overall risk ranking of the excipient (low, medium, or high) is calculated. Where the GMPs used to manufacture excipients are found to be inappropriate according to risk ranking, mitigation measures are implemented. A risk analysis tool for manufacturer audit frequency is then used to assess the risk and determine audit frequency.

Based on the outcome, a second risk assessment could be performed to determine if (1) additional mitigation measures should be implemented, or (2) use of the excipient from that specific manufacturer should be discontinued. If Company Y decides to continue to use the excipient after implementing additional mitigation measures to reduce the remaining risk to an acceptable level, the company documents this decision in a "statement to maintain business."

Company Y periodically reevaluates risk profiles during the review of a manufacturer's performance. A risk assessment is reevaluated in cases of a significant quality event, e.g., significant change by manufacturer, serious GMP deficiencies, or loss of GMP certification.

Figure 10.5-1
Risk Assessment Model for Company Y
(Six-criteria weighted ranking → Audit frequency tool → Second RA → Continue/Discontinue decision)
Figure 10.5-1: Risk Assessment Model for Company Y (p35)

Outcomes and Lessons Learned:

  • The model is simple to implement.
  • The result of a risk assessment sometimes varies between sites. However, after implementing a control strategy, the sites could reach the same decision to continue use of the excipient from that manufacturer.
  • Use of this model aids Company Y in implementation and modification of control measures to reduce risk to an acceptable level.

解說與應用 Commentary

此案例展示的核心方法：六準則加權 + 稽核頻率決策樹

公司 Y 的方法有兩個顯著特點：

1. 六準則加權工具（1/3/10 Scoring）：

  • 每個準則賦予 1（低）、3（中）、10（高）三個加權值
  • 六個準則的加總或加權平均決定整體風險等級
  • 此 1-3-10 標度比 1-5-10 或 1-2-3 更能放大高風險項目的影響

2. 稽核頻率決策工具（Audit Frequency Risk Tool）：

  • 風險等級直接連結稽核頻率，使資源分配有依據
  • 若第一次評估後仍有疑慮，進行第二次評估決定「繼續使用」或「停止使用」
  • 繼續使用高風險賦形劑時，須出具「維持業務聲明（Statement to Maintain Business）」

比喻：信用評分系統

公司 Y 的 1-3-10 評分類似銀行信用評分：

  • 大多數因素是低風險（1分），只有少數關鍵因素是高風險（10分）
  • 一個 10 分的負面因素（例如：失去 GMP 認證）會大幅拉高整體分數，讓風險等級跳升為「高」

「Statement to Maintain Business」則像銀行在向信用有疑慮的客戶放貸時附上的風險備忘錄——決策有理有據，且有文件記錄。

重點觀察：跨廠評分差異的處理

公司 Y 坦承不同廠點有時對同一賦形劑的評估結果不同。這反映了真實環境中的挑戰：不同廠點的採購路線、測試記錄、稽核經驗不同，導致主觀判斷有差異。公司 Y 的解決方案是：即使分數不同，在執行管控措施後，各廠最終達到一致的使用決策。這是務實的處理方式，也說明了評估結果「一致性」的要求應落在決策層面，而非數字層面。

思考練習

公司 Y 的模型「簡單易實施」，但允許廠點間評分有差異。在你的 CDMO 環境中，你更偏向「評分一致性（統一工具、統一標準）」還是「決策一致性（各廠可自行評分，但最終決策要一致）」？兩者在 GMP 合規性上有什麼不同的含義？

CDMO 應用建議

公司 Y 的稽核頻率決策工具是非常實用的資源分配工具，強烈建議 CDMO 採用。具體實施建議：

  • 高風險賦形劑製造商：每年稽核或書面問卷
  • 中風險：每兩年稽核
  • 低風險：每三至五年稽核或認證審查

並在 SOP 中規定：失去 GMP 認證、重大品質事件發生時，不論排程如何，立即啟動臨時稽核。

Example 6: Company Z

案例六：公司 Z — 中央化兩階段端到端評估體系

10.6 Example 6: Company Z

Company Z's model (Figure 10.6-1) was designed as a centralized process focused on highlighting the inherent risks and control strategies associated with an excipient, regardless of the manufacturer. The process is also designed to provide the user with the minimum quality standard expected for the manufacture of the excipient and its associated key risks. These risk and control strategies provide input for the gap analysis and, subsequently, to the quality risk assessment performed on supplier sites.

Company Z established a corporate procedure that outlines the requirements for the excipient risk assessment process and its linkages to the overarching supplier quality management process.

Figure 10.6-1
Risk Assessment Model for Company Z
(End-to-end two-phase model: Phase 1 — Central team determines inherent risk + control strategy; Phase 2 — Global supplier QM performs gap analysis per supplier site)
Figure 10.6-1: Risk Assessment Model for Company Z (p35–36)

The first part of the "end-to-end process excipient risk assessment" determines the control strategy and standards that are established by the centralized excipient risk assessment team. In the second part, the quality management group of the global supplier uses the output of the risk assessment to conduct individual supplier site quality risk assessments. The information from the individual supplier site quality risk assessments is shared with the formulation sites through a vendor assurance database system, highlighting any changes in supplier risk ranking via change control. The excipient risk assessments are reviewed periodically and any changes in inherent risk are brought to the attention of the global supplier's quality management team. In addition, any quality event that impacts the excipient quality, whether internal or external, results in a review of the initial excipient risk assessment.

After the need for an excipient risk assessment is identified, the centralized, cross-functional excipient risk assessment team performs the assessment, which focuses entirely on inherent risk. Supplier risks are not considered at this time. An electronic tool is used to perform the assessment, and the output is used to determine the appropriate quality level for the excipient and any relevant excipient control strategy.

Each supplier quality manager from Company Z's global supplier quality team then performs a gap analysis of the excipient supplier quality standard against an assessment of defined standards and excipient control strategies for supplied excipients. This identifies any gaps for the supplier. All gaps are challenged to ensure their validity and valid gaps are used as input, along with other supplier quality management intelligence, in performing the supplier site quality risk assessment. This final assessment generates a supplier risk ranking (low, medium, or high) which, in turn, determines the quality oversight to be applied.

The maintenance of the excipient risk assessment is managed under the periodic review process outlined in the corporate standard on excipient risk management. Quality events that impact excipient quality trigger an ad hoc excipient risk assessment review.

Company Z implemented this process in March 2016 in line with the EC Guidelines.

Key Benefits:

  • A key benefit of conducting the assessment is consistency in managing excipients and suppliers. The centralized approach provided a concise framework for consistent input into the initial steps of the process. The implementation formalized the process and improved the documentation of information, supporting existing supplier risk assessment processes.
  • Grouping excipient grades into categories aligned with the route of administration, origin, and function has reduced the number of assessments required.
  • Conducting a formalized assessment process provides clarity to sourcing partners, both internal and external.
  • The risk assessment acts as an effective standard for comparing and assessing supply chain risks.

解說與應用 Commentary

此案例展示的核心方法：兩階段端到端中央化體系

公司 Z 是六個案例中系統化程度最高的，其設計邏輯是：

第一階段（中央化）：固有風險評估

  • 中央跨職能團隊評估賦形劑固有風險（Inherent Risk）——不考慮任何特定供應商
  • 輸出：賦形劑品質標準（Quality Standard）+ 控制策略（Control Strategy）
  • 使用電子工具執行，確保一致性

第二階段（供應商層級）：差距分析

  • 各地供應商品質經理將固有風險控制策略對照各供應商實際情況做差距分析（Gap Analysis）
  • 有效差距作為供應商現場品質風險評估的輸入
  • 最終輸出：供應商風險排名（Low/Medium/High）→ 決定品質監督強度

資訊透過供應商保證資料庫系統（Vendor Assurance Database）傳遞給配方廠，change control 觸發排名更新。

比喻：中央廚房與分店品質管理

公司 Z 的模型像連鎖餐廳集團：

  • 中央廚房（總部）：定義食材標準與最低品質要求（固有風險評估）
  • 各分店（供應商品質經理）：對照中央標準評估各自的在地供應商是否符合（差距分析）
  • 總部資料庫：彙整各分店的供應商評估結果，總部即時掌握全局供應鏈風險

這種架構讓「標準一致」和「在地執行」兩者兼顧——中央制定遊戲規則，各地執行並回報。

重點觀察：分組策略降低評估數量

公司 Z 提到「依給藥途徑、來源與功能將賦形劑分組（Grouping into Categories）」，顯著減少了所需評估的數量。這是非常務實的做法——例如，所有「注射劑用緩衝鹽」可以共用一份固有風險評估框架，差別只在各自的供應商差距分析。這個分組策略對擁有大量賦形劑品項的 CDMO 尤其有價值。

思考練習

公司 Z 的中央化模型適合大型跨國集團，但對於中型 CDMO，「中央跨職能團隊」的人力是否支撐得起？如果你要設計一個縮小版的公司 Z 模型，哪些元素是不可省略的（中央化固有風險評估？電子工具？差距分析程序），哪些可以簡化？

CDMO 應用建議

公司 Z 的賦形劑分組策略是 CDMO 立即可用的成本效益工具。建議步驟：

  • 第一步：清點所有在用賦形劑，依給藥途徑（注射/口服/外用）+ 化學來源（動物性/植物性/合成）建立分組矩陣
  • 第二步：每個分組做一份通用固有風險評估框架（Generic Inherent Risk Assessment）
  • 第三步：各賦形劑在框架上只需填入供應商特定差距分析，大幅降低總評估工作量

此外，建立Vendor Assurance Database（可以是簡單的 SharePoint 清單或 ERP 模組）作為賦形劑風險排名的單一資料來源，確保生產廠點能即時查到最新的供應商風險狀態。

共同模式：所有案例的共通點

六個案例，六種實施路徑，但都呈現以下共同特徵：

  1. 雙流結構：賦形劑固有風險 + 供應鏈/製造商風險，兩流合併判斷
  2. 三級結論：Low / Medium / High 風險等級，對應不同控制措施
  3. EC Guidelines 合規：所有公司都直接或間接對齊 2015 年歐盟指引
  4. Living Document 意圖：所有公司都希望評估能動態更新，但執行程度不一
  5. 觸發複審機制：重大品質事件、製造商重大變更、GMP 認證變化等

哪些方法最健全？

綜合法規期望與實用性，以下組合被認為最健全：

  • 固有風險評估：Company W 的 CQA 連結 +/− 方法（最符合 ICH Q8 精神，法規審查說服力強）
  • 供應商風險評估：Company Z 的差距分析 + Vendor Assurance Database（系統化、可擴展）
  • 組織架構：Company X 的 PQM 主導 + change control 整合（確保 Living Document）
  • 稽核資源分配：Company Y 的稽核頻率決策工具（實用、直接連結行動）

整體比喻：不同的路，同一個目的地

這六個案例就像六種不同的登山路線，登的是同一座山——符合 EC Guidelines 的賦形劑風險評估要求。

  • Company U：基礎路線，Excel 工具，適合起步
  • Company V：標準路線，全球 SOP，成熟穩健
  • Company W：科學路線，CQA 連結，技術深度最強
  • Company X：組織路線，PQM 整合，人員責任最清晰
  • Company Y：務實路線，簡單工具，易推廣易執行
  • Company Z：系統路線，中央化資料庫，規模化能力最強

選擇哪條路，取決於公司的規模、資源、產品組合複雜度，以及目標市場的法規環境。

給 CDMO 的綜合建議：建構你的評估框架

基於六個案例的學習，建議 CDMO 分三階段建立賦形劑風險評估系統：

Phase 1（立即可做）：

  • 建立賦形劑清單，依 Company Z 的分組策略（給藥途徑×來源×功能）分類
  • 為每個分組建立 Company W 式的 CQA Impact Template
  • 採用 Company Y 的 1/3/10 六準則工具快速完成初始評分

Phase 2（3-6 個月）：

  • 建立 Company X 式的 PQM 負責制，明確觸發複審清單
  • 將評估觸發條件納入現有 Change Control SOP
  • 建立簡版 Vendor Assurance 清單（可從 Excel 開始）

Phase 3（長期）：

  • 引入電子工具（Company V、Z 的方向），自動觸發複審週期
  • 將稽核頻率與風險等級正式連結（Company Y 模式）
  • 定期對標 EC Guidelines 及 IPEC-PQG Guide 更新評估框架
核心啟示：賦形劑風險評估沒有唯一的正確答案——重要的是建立一個系統化、文件化、可觸發複審的程序，讓組織能夠持續、一致地判斷每種賦形劑所需的 GMP 監督等級，並在供應鏈或品質條件發生變化時及時響應。這六個真實案例共同證明：形式化的評估不只是法規要求的負擔，更是提升供應鏈透明度、優化稽核資源分配的有效管理工具。
