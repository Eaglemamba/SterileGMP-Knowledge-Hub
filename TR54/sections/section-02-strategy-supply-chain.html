3.0 Overall Strategy

Based on the results of the risk assessment, a pharmaceutical manufacturer can determine what level of GMP will be required. The guides and standards most widely accepted for excipient GMPs are:

  • EXCiPACT™: International Pharmaceutical Excipients Certification Standards for Pharmaceutical Excipient Suppliers
  • NSF/IPEC/ANSI 363: Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP) for Pharmaceutical Excipients
  • IPEC/PQG: The Joint Good Manufacturing Practices Guide for Pharmaceutical Excipients
  • USP: USP <1078> Good Manufacturing Practices for Bulk Pharmaceutical Excipients and <1059> Excipient Performance
  • Japan: Self-imposed Standards of Good Manufacturing Practices for Pharmaceutical Excipients

The pharmaceutical manufacturer may determine that some additional technical considerations beyond those in established GMP guides and standards may be needed. As part of the risk assessment process, the manufacturer or manufacturing authorization holder of the drug product should engage the excipient manufacturer and suppliers to obtain any specifics from the excipient manufacturer's own risk assessment process that the excipient manufacturer is willing to share. Additionally, the complete supply chain profile must be considered for each excipient.

Should the risk assessment uncover an issue requiring additional action, a control strategy should be put in place and the affected parties should be notified.

Before being implemented, any actions recommended as a result of the risk assessment must be discussed and agreed upon by the excipient manufacturer and the manufacturer of the drug product or manufacturing authorization holder, in case those actions may impact the supplier. The results of the discussion should be documented in a quality agreement or other document.

A global risk assessment of all excipients used by the drug product manufacturer or manufacturing authorization holder will ensure a standardized assessment of all excipients in all drug products and provide a holistic view of the supply chain. This enables the excipient user to provide the excipient manufacturer, with one voice, a single (worst case) risk assessment.

That approach is preferable to having multiple users within a single drug product manufacturer each communicating different assessment results to the excipient manufacturer.

In a global risk assessment, risk review and improvements of the risk assessment might be more difficult than they would be if separate risk assessments were completed for each drug product or by each site using the excipient. The review and approval workflows for a single global risk assessment may require more time and effort. However, this process could be shortened by instituting a global risk assessment that involves local ownership. In this scenario, the owner of each product or manufacturing site would conduct a separate risk assessment, but all risk assessments would be collected in a single document repository. The local owner would maintain responsibility for the data collection, review, and approval workflows, and for ensuring that updated information is included in the global risk assessment repository. The repository would serve as a knowledge base for all excipients, allowing the drug product manufacturer or manufacturing authorization holder to communicate its requirements to the excipient manufacturer with a single voice representing all local owners.

賦形劑 GMP 標準體系 — 五大規範

這五個標準並非互斥，而是各有適用範圍與地區偏好。理解其層次有助於製藥廠決定對供應商要求什麼等級的認證：

  • EXCiPACT™：國際第三方認證制度，有實地稽核，是目前業界最具公信力的賦形劑 GMP 認證標誌。
  • NSF/IPEC/ANSI 363：美國標準，與 IPEC/PQG 聯合指南互相對應，著重製造管控。
  • IPEC/PQG 聯合指南：IPEC（國際賦形劑委員會）與 PQG（製藥品質集團）合作制定，歐美通用。
  • USP <1078> / <1059>：美國藥典，<1078> 針對製造，<1059> 針對功能表現。在美國 NDA/ANDA 申請中常被引用。
  • 日本自主標準：由日本賦形劑行業協會制定的自律標準，適用於日本本地供應商。

從 CDMO 角度：客戶合約中應明確要求供應商符合哪一標準，避免模糊空間。EXCiPACT 認證是最容易量化稽核的依據。

比喻：全球評估 vs 本地所有權

想像一間跨國連鎖餐廳集團：

  • 純全球評估 = 總公司統一決定每間分店的食材供應商標準 → 一致性高，但更新緩慢，各地特殊需求難以反映。
  • 本地所有權模式 = 各分店自己評估當地供應商，但結果集中上報總部知識庫 → 彈性與效率兼顧，總公司仍能以「一個聲音」對外溝通。

TR54-6 推薦的是後者：本地執行、全局匯整、對外統一。這也是大型製藥集團（如 Roche、Pfizer）實際運作方式。

重點：行動前必須共識，結果必須文件化

TR54-6 特別強調：風險評估發現問題後，任何矯正行動必須先與賦形劑製造商討論並達成共識，才能執行。原因是：某些要求（如改變包裝方式、增加測試）會影響供應商成本與作業流程。

共識結果應寫入品質協議（Quality Agreement），作為法律與稽核依據。

3.1 Risk Areas Beyond Quality

While the focus of the excipient risk assessment is quality, a company also should consider other aspects that can impact the excipient and, with this, product availability for the patient. These include excipient availability; business continuity risk; environmental, socio-economic, environment, health, safety, and geographic risks; and general business data. While consideration of these risks is useful as part of an overall strategy, it is out of scope of this document and is not discussed further.

4.0 Supply Chain — End to End

Section 4.0 provides examples of supply chains, helps identify the various actors in the supply chain, and describes their typical roles. The subsections herein offer examples of the types of information and documentation that the various actors could provide. Additionally, it discusses some aspects of the potential risks that could be introduced by actors in the supply chain.

Supply chains have become more and more complex over the years. In the past, a manufacturer of pharmaceutical excipients often supplied directly to a drug product manufacturer. Today, in addition to the manufacturer of the excipient, the distributor, broker, and contract manufacturing organization (CMO) each plays a role in the supply chain, and each contributes to the risks to quality in one way or another. Each of these companies also could provide information on the excipient's manufacture, testing, and distribution that might be relevant for the risk assessment.

4.1 Types of Supply Chains and Risks Presented

Many materials used as excipients are not manufactured specifically for pharmaceutical use, but are intended for other applications such as food, cosmetics, or home care. As such, the direct supply of an excipient from the manufacturer to the end user may be unusual. In addition, the quantities of excipients used by the pharmaceutical end user may be only a fraction of the excipient manufacturer's overall volume; in fact, the original manufacturer may not have a business interest in supplying the pharmaceutical industry. In such cases, intermediaries often become involved, procuring larger quantities of the excipient from the original manufacturer, and then repackaging and relabeling it into quantities more suitable for pharmaceutical use. Thus, the supply chain between the excipient manufacturer and the pharmaceutical end user often is complex. The complexity is magnified for excipients that move internationally.

In both international and domestic supply chains, the point at which the legal ownership of the excipient transfers from one organization to another is a critical juncture. Each organization needs to have defined responsibilities for maintaining records and securing the quality and security of the excipient. In complex supply chains, legal ownership may transfer several times, for example from original manufacturer to distributor to repackager to end user.

In all cases, the end user needs to identify the intermediaries in the supply chain in order to properly assess the risks. This may be difficult, as some organizations in the supply chain may not want to disclose their sources, even including the original manufacturer of the excipient.

比喻：為什麼賦形劑供應鏈如此複雜？

想像你要買「食品級白砂糖」（類似於常見的賦形劑蔗糖）。台糖一年生產幾十萬噸，但你的藥廠只需要幾噸。台糖不會為了你這幾噸開一條生產線——他們寧可透過中盤商賣給你。

跨國採購時（如從印度、中國進口微晶纖維素），中間還涉及：出口代理、海運公司、進口報關、在地倉儲、再分裝……每個環節都可能引入汙染、混淆或文件不完整的風險。

法律所有權移轉 = 風險評估的關鍵節點

TR54-6 明確指出：法律所有權移轉的時刻，是品質責任轉移的分水嶺。

每次所有權轉移，都應對應：

  • 書面的品質協議（Quality Agreement）
  • 交接點的取樣/放行記錄
  • 儲存條件的確認文件

如果在所有權轉移後才發現品質問題，而又沒有完整的交接記錄，將很難確定責任方，也難以向監管機構解釋。

在 CDMO 情境中，如果你代客戶採購賦形劑，你接收貨物的當下即取得法律所有權，相關 GMP 責任也隨之而來。

供應鏈風險的定性模型

每增加一個中間商，風險即疊加。定性估算：

供應鏈風險 ∝ 中間環節數量 × 各環節透明度倒數

風險指數（定性）：
  直接供應（0個中間商）     → ★☆☆☆☆ 最低
  經銷商（1個中間商）       → ★★☆☆☆ 低
  再包裝商（加工環節）      → ★★★☆☆ 中
  經紀商（透明度低）        → ★★★★☆ 中高
  國際多段供應鏈            → ★★★★★ 最高（視透明度而定）

每個「不透明」或「難以稽核」的環節
都是風險乘數，而非風險加數。

這個定性框架說明為何 TR54-6 後續會用 SCRPN（Supply Chain Risk Priority Number）來量化這些因素。

直接供應：最理想但不常見

直接供應是風險最低的模式，因為：

  • 供應鏈環節最少，資訊傳遞失真機率低

因此，品質協議應涵蓋物流服務商的管控要求（如 GDP 合規、溫度記錄器使用等）。

4.1.2 Supply via a Distributor

When an excipient moves from manufacturer to distributor, the distributor becomes the legal owner. The distributor then transfers ownership to the end user, possibly after repackaging or relabeling the excipient. The distributor may have an agreement with the original manufacturer to market the excipient using the distributor's tradename and literature; in this instance, the distributor and manufacturer likely will have an agreement to ensure the quality of the excipient. Usually in these cases the entire supply chain can be mapped with some certainty. This type of supply chain, depicted in Figure 4.1.2-1, presents few more risks than the simple manufacturer-to-end user scenario; however, those risks may be magnified if the distributor engages in repacking or relabeling, as discussed in Section 4.1.3.

If the original manufacturer has not established agreements with distributors, it will be much harder to fully trace all parties in the supply chain, increasing the risks. Other parties may repack and/or relabel the excipient. The original manufacturer may change its distributor, hence the process for notification of such changes should be agreed between the parties. These supply chains are more complex to assess, and the attendant risks are higher than in the previous scenarios.

經銷商模式的關鍵：有無授權協議

風險差異在於製造商與經銷商之間是否存在正式協議：

  • 有協議（Authorized Distributor）：製造商對經銷商進行管控，可追溯整條供應鏈，風險僅略高於直接供應。
  • 無協議（Unofficial Distributor）：製造商無從控制下游流向，任何人都可能在市場上分裝、改標後轉售，追溯性大幅降低，風險顯著上升。

實務建議：在供應商稽核問卷（SAQ）中，應明確要求經銷商提供其與原廠的授權協議副本。若無法提供，應視為重大風險訊號。

通知義務：製造商換了經銷商怎麼辦？

TR54-6 特別提到，若原廠更換了授權經銷商，應有正式的通知流程。這一點常被忽略，但對製藥廠非常重要——因為你的品質協議是與特定經銷商簽訂的。

建議在品質協議中加入條款："Supplier shall notify Buyer within [30] days of any change in the authorized distributor relationship with the original manufacturer."

再包裝：最容易引入實體污染的環節

再包裝是賦形劑供應鏈中風險最集中的作業之一，因為它涉及對物料的直接物理處理：

  • 汙染風險：開包、秤量、再包裝環境若未達 GMP 潔淨等級，易引入微粒或微生物汙染

中文解析

國際供應鏈的核心風險：當事方每批可能都不同

TR54-6 點出了國際供應鏈最難管控的特點：每一批貨的運輸公司、船運代理、倉儲設施都可能不同。

這意味著：

  • 你無法對「一個固定的」物流夥伴建立品質協議，因為根本不確定下一批貨是誰執行
  • 溫度記錄器的讀取值可能因不同倉儲設施的設備校準狀況而異
  • 文件追蹤（如原廠封緘記錄）可能在多個換手過程中遺失

TR54-6 的建議解法：若能固定中間方（Fixed Intermediaries），就能在那些固定點設立管控（如固定倉儲的溫度稽核、固定進口代理的文件標準）。

對 CDMO 而言，選擇「能固定物流鏈的供應商」是降低國際採購風險的第一步。

4.1.6 Manufacturer and Contract Manufacturing Organizations

When one or more CMOs are involved in the supply chain, it is critically important to clearly identify and understand which party or parties gather information and who owns the risk assessment.

The manufacturing authorization holder is ultimately responsible for the risk assessment. Depending on the quality agreement and relationship between the CMO and manufacturing authorization holder, there generally are three options for conducting the risk assessment:

  • The CMO might carry out the entire risk assessment, collecting the information about the use and function of the excipient in the final drug product. That risk assessment would be reviewed and approved by the manufacturing authorization holder.
  • The risk assessment might be carried out by the manufacturing authorization holder, incorporating information from the CMO.
  • The risk assessment might be carried out by the manufacturing authorization holder based on the risk assessment delivered by the CMO.

In any case, the roles and responsibilities for the CMO and manufacturing authorization holder must be clearly defined, including what actions will be taken if the risk assessments completed by the CMO and manufacturing authorization holder result in different outcomes.

適用於：MAH 有強大的品質部門，希望掌控評估流程，CDMO 只提供配方用途、用量等資訊。

模式 3：MAH 主導，基於 CMO 先期評估
適用於：混合模式，CDMO 先做初步評估交 MAH，MAH 再整合自身資訊完成最終評估。這是目前業界最常見的合作方式。

無論哪種模式，品質協議（Quality Agreement）中必須明確寫明評估分工，以及若雙方結論不一致時的解決機制。這是稽核時監管機構必看的內容。

CMO 採購賦形劑的雙重風險

CDMO 替客戶採購賦形劑時，面臨雙重責任：

  • 作為「End User」的責任：你需要評估賦形劑的供應鏈風險，確保品質符合要求
  • 作為「CMO」的責任：你需要與 MAH 協調，確保你的風險評估結論與 MAH 的監管申報檔案一致

若使用一個 MAH 申報檔案中未列入的賦形劑供應商，可能構成未申報的供應鏈變更（Unreported Supply Chain Change），這在 FDA 或 EMA 稽核中是重大缺失。

中文解析 Commentary

供應鏈各角色的責任分層：關鍵解讀

Table 5.0-1 揭示了一個重要的責任金字塔：

  • 原廠分包商：提供最基礎的製造與品質記錄，是品質起點。
  • 賦形劑製造商：責任最完整，須提供 EIP（賦形劑資訊包）、DMF（藥物主檔）等完整文件，是供應鏈品質的核心保證人。
  • 經紀商：文件責任最輕（僅 CoA 和供應鏈地圖），但也是透明度最差的環節。
  • 經銷商/供應商：責任居中，需轉遞製造商的文件，並對倉儲/運輸條件負責。
  • CMO/藥品製造商：需執行風險評估（但最終責任在 MAH），提供配方、規格、毒理資料。
  • MAH（上市許可持有人）：最終責任人，負責審批所有評估結果，確保與法規申報一致。

這個層次結構說明：即使 CMO 執行了評估，MAH 仍有不可推卸的監督義務。

CDMO 角色定位 — 文件清單對照

作為 CDMO，你同時扮演「CMO/藥品製造商」角色。根據 Table 5.0-1，你應能提供或產出：

  • 賦形劑風險評估報告（Excipient Risk Assessment）
  • 供應鏈地圖（Supply Chain Map）— 包含你向下採購的供應商鏈條
  • 供應商稽核記錄（Supplier/Manufacturer Audit Documents）
  • 毒理資料（Toxicological Data）— 可來自藥典或 MAH 提供
  • 賦形劑規格書（Specification of Excipient）
  • 管控策略（Control Strategy）

如果你目前尚未為每一個賦形劑建立完整的供應鏈地圖，這是最值得優先補充的文件。它是所有後續風險評估的基礎。

思考練習：供應鏈風險評估的落地問題

  • 你目前的賦形劑供應商清單中，有多少已建立完整的供應鏈地圖（到原廠層級）？
  • 對於使用經紀商採購的賦形劑，你是否有要求提供供應鏈地圖？若對方拒絕，你採取了什麼替代措施？
  • 你的品質協議是否涵蓋供應商通知義務（如更換分包商、更換倉儲等）？
  • 你與客戶（MAH）的品質協議中，賦形劑風險評估的分工是否已明確定義？

Table 5.0-2: Common Scenarios in Information-Gathering

Information to support the excipient risk assessment process can be obtained using a variety of avenues:

  • Meetings with manufacturers and suppliers
  • Obligations placed in quality agreements or equivalent documents
  • Chemistry, manufacturing and controls (CMC) documentation
  • Excipient manufacturer customer portals
  • Internet
  • Manufacturing authorization holder supply chain management and quality oversight functions
  • Health Authority portals
  • Quality and technical collaborations between excipient manufacturer and end user

That said, when collaboration between the manufacturing authorization holder and other parties in the supply chain is limited, the manufacturing authorization holder may need to consider alternative means for obtaining information. Table 5.0-2 lists some common scenarios and potential resolutions.

Potential Issues Resolutions / Approach
Unable to determine all participants in the supply chain for commercial reasons, e.g., a distributor enforces supply chain opaqueness for competitive reasons When distributors are part of the supply chain, this is a common scenario. The manufacturing authorization holder should identify the supply chain risks and, where appropriate, transfer responsibility for supply chain oversight to the distributor.
  • Allow the distributor to redact information in responses to protect their business advantage
  • Use the quality agreement with suppliers/distributors to assign accountability and supply chain oversight
  • Use onsite audit program to challenge and confirm information
Unable to obtain appropriate regulatory declaration statements (e.g., residual solvent statement) for an excipient due to lack of technical understanding or expertise of a participant in the supply chain
  • Support the excipient manufacturer/supplier with in-house technical support, if available
  • Provide the manufacturer/supplier with declaration templates, if available, for manufacturer/supplier to complete
  • Use the manufacturing authorization holder's internal technical team to assess and/or test material in lieu of supplier regulatory declaration
Unable to obtain all information required to support the risk assessment because the excipient manufacturer's quality standards do not meet the excipient user's requirements
  • Determine if the excipient manufacturer is performing additional activities to facilitate alignment of quality standards
  • Reassess to identify gaps that exist and mitigate through internal or external action, e.g., increase quality oversight, change specifications
  • If the residual risks identified are unacceptable, change the excipient supply chain
Insufficient provisions in place (e.g., lack quality agreements or unable to agree on a confidentiality agreement) to support information exchange with manufacturing authorization holder
  • Review obligations agreed with parties in the supply chain
  • Put in place an appropriate agreement to support this process
  • Review manufacturer audit reports and supply management files to determine whether the assessor for the manufacturing authorization holder already possesses required information
  • Explore other avenues of obtaining information, e.g., manufacturer's standard customer information portals, public domain information
Insufficient or no collaboration between key participants and manufacturing authorization holder in the supply chain (e.g., no leverage due to low volume purchase, stressed or non-existent commercial relationships)
  • Use distributor relationship to leverage information transfer
  • Identify alternative sources for obtaining support and information
  • Use already-established agreements or put in place appropriate agreement to obligate supply chain party support
  • Review manufacturer/supplier audit reports/questionnaire/excipient file (e.g., EIP) and supply management files to determine whether the assessor for the manufacturing authorization holder already possesses required information
Unable to support the excipient risk assessment process due to limited oversight of outsourced activity (e.g., unable to secure an audit)
  • Ensure adequate resources are allocated to ensure appropriate prioritization of manufacturer/supplier management activities
  • Use third-party audit program (e.g., EXCiPACT™)

中文解析

資訊蒐集的九種管道

TR54-6 列舉了取得賦形劑評估資訊的九種方式，在實務中，這些管道應優先排序使用：

  • 最直接：與製造商/供應商面對面會議、品質協議義務條款
  • 次之：CMC 文件、廠商客戶入口網站、技術合作
  • 補充：MAH 內部供應鏈管理功能、法規機構公開資料（如 FDA 警告信資料庫）、網路公開資訊

當主要管道受阻時（如供應商拒絕合作），才使用替代方案（Table 5.0-2 的情境）。這不是「偷懶找藉口」，而是有系統地處理資訊缺口。

比喻：Table 5.0-2 是「供應鏈外交手冊」

這張表格本質上是一本「外交談判指南」：當供應鏈對方不合作、不透明、不稱職時，你有哪些應對策略？

六個情境可以歸納為三類問題：

  • 資訊不透明（情境 1, 4, 5）→ 策略：合約義務化、稽核驗證、轉移責任給供應商
  • 技術能力不足（情境 2, 3）→ 策略：提供模板與技術支持、自行測試驗證、換供應鏈
  • 資源/管控不足（情境 6）→ 策略：第三方稽核（EXCiPACT）、重新分配資源

這個框架也提醒我們：沒有萬能的解決方案，每種情境需要個別判斷。但「換供應鏈」永遠是最後一招，不是第一個動作。

CDMO 最常遇到的情境：低採購量的困境

情境 5（低採購量導致無談判籌碼）是 CDMO 最常遇到的現實困境：

當你的某個賦形劑用量只有每年幾百公斤，而原廠年產量是幾萬噸，原廠根本不在乎你。他們不會回覆你的問卷，不會接受你的稽核，不會提供 EIP。

TR54-6 的建議解法：

  • 利用經銷商關係施壓（你買的量雖少，但你的經銷商買的量可能很大）
  • 加入行業協會或共同採購聯盟，以集體力量施壓
  • 查閱已公開的廠商稽核報告（如 EXCiPACT 認證資料庫）
  • 以你自己的技術團隊進行物料評估，彌補文件缺口

Key Takeaways (English)

  • Section 3.0 — Five major GMP standards exist for excipients (EXCiPACT, NSF/IPEC/ANSI 363, IPEC/PQG, USP <1078>, Japan). Risk assessment findings must be agreed with the excipient manufacturer before action. A global assessment with local ownership is the recommended approach.
  • Section 3.1 — Business continuity, environmental, and geographic risks are acknowledged but are out of scope for this TR. They should be addressed in separate supply chain management frameworks.
  • Section 4.0 — Supply chains have grown complex due to intermediaries (distributors, brokers, CMOs). The legal ownership transfer point is a critical quality juncture. Tracing the full supply chain may be difficult due to commercial confidentiality.
  • 4.1.1–4.1.5 — Six supply chain types range from direct supply (lowest risk) to international multi-party chains (highest risk). Broker-mediated chains have variable risk entirely dependent on transparency. Fixed intermediaries allow for better control points.
  • 4.1.6 — When CMOs are involved, the manufacturing authorization holder retains ultimate responsibility. Three models exist for dividing the risk assessment work; the division must be clearly documented in the quality agreement.
  • Table 5.0-1 — Each supply chain role (subcontractor, manufacturer, broker, distributor, CMO, MAH) has defined accountability areas and expected documents. The MAH always holds final responsibility.
  • Table 5.0-2 — Six common information-gathering failure scenarios and their resolutions: use contractual obligations, provide technical support, conduct independent testing, leverage third-party audits (EXCiPACT), or change the supply chain if residual risks are unacceptable.

核心學習重點（中文）

  • 3.0 整體策略：賦形劑 GMP 五大標準。風險評估發現問題後，行動前必須與賦形劑製造商達成共識並文件化。推薦採用「本地所有權的全球評估」模式。
  • 3.1 超越品質的風險：業務連續性、地理環境等風險超出本文件範疇，但在供應商管理實務中仍應考量。
  • 4.0 端到端供應鏈：供應鏈複雜化的根源在於賦形劑多非藥用專屬製造，中間商的介入不可避免。法律所有權轉移點是風險管控的關鍵節點。
  • 4.1.1–4.1.5 六種供應鏈類型：從直接供應（最低風險）到國際多段供應鏈（最高風險）。經紀商模式風險高度依賴透明度。固定中間方可建立管控點。
  • 4.1.6 CMO 情境：最終責任在 MAH。CMO 與 MAH 的評估分工必須在品質協議中明確寫明，包括結論不一致時的解決機制。
  • Table 5.0-1：供應鏈六大角色（分包商、製造商、經紀商、經銷商、CMO、MAH）各有其責任範疇與文件清單。MAH 為最終責任人。
  • Table 5.0-2：六種資訊蒐集困境與應對策略——合約義務化、提供技術支援、獨立檢驗、使用第三方稽核（EXCiPACT），或必要時更換供應鏈。
