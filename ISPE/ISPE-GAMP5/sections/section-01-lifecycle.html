Chapter 3: Life Cycle Approach  |  第3章：生命週期方法

3 Life Cycle Approach

Compliance with regulatory requirements and fitness for intended use may be achieved by adopting a life cycle approach following good practice as defined in this Guide.

A life cycle approach entails defining and performing activities in a systematic way from conception, understanding the requirements, through development, release, and operational use, to system retirement. Figure 3.1 shows a general specification, design, and verification process described in ASTM E2500.

This section of the Guide introduces the computerized system life cycle, a general approach to specification and verification, a framework for computerized system validation, and the application of critical thinking.

3.1 Computerized System Life Cycle

The computerized system life cycle encompasses all activities from initial concept to retirement. The life cycle for any system consists of four major phases:

  • Concept
  • Project
  • Operation
  • Retirement

During the concept phase, the regulated company should consider opportunities to automate one or more business processes based upon business need and benefits. Typically at this phase, initial requirements will be developed and potential solutions considered. An initial risk assessment or GxP assessment should be performed, so that GxP regulated systems are identified before the project phase commences.

The project phase involves planning, supplier assessment and selection, various levels of specification, configuration (or coding for custom applications), and verification leading to acceptance and release for operation. Risk management is applied to identify risks and to remove or reduce them to an acceptable level.

System operation is often the longest phase and is managed using defined, up-to-date, operational processes and procedures applied by personnel who have appropriate training, education, and experience. Maintaining control (including security), fitness for intended use, and compliance are key aspects. The management of changes of different impact, scope, and complexity is an important activity during this phase.

The final phase is the ultimate retirement of the system. It involves decisions about data retention, migration, or destruction, and the management of these processes.

Suppliers of products and services should be involved as appropriate throughout the life cycle. An inventory of computerized systems should be maintained. A GxP assessment should be performed by the beginning of the project stage to determine whether a system is GxP regulated, and if so, which specific regulations apply.

第3章 解析：生命週期思維

核心觀念：為什麼需要「生命週期方法」？

傳統上，許多藥廠把電腦化系統驗證（CSV）視為「上線前的一次性活動」——做完驗證文件、放行就結束了。GAMP 5 第2版明確反對這種心態。

生命週期方法（Life Cycle Approach）的核心思想是：合規不是一個時間點，而是整個系統存在期間的持續狀態。從最初的概念發想，到系統退役，每個階段都有相應的活動和控制。

CDMO 情境類比：新 LIMS 的生命旅程

假設你的 CDMO 決定導入一套新的 LIMS（實驗室資訊管理系統）：

  • 概念階段：業務需求分析、評估是否值得導入、初步 GxP 評估（LIMS 屬於 GxP 系統嗎？答：是的，因為它管理檢測結果）
  • 專案階段：選商、撰寫需求規格書（URS）、設定系統（Category 4）、執行測試、放行上線
  • 操作階段：日常使用、變更控制（例如新增一個分析方法的工作流程）、定期審查（每年確認系統仍符合法規）
  • 退役階段：當更新的 LIMS 取代舊系統時，舊系統的資料要保留多久？如何遷移？

重要實務提醒：GxP 評估要提早做

GxP 評估必須在專案階段開始前完成。這意味著在批准專案預算之前，就要確認這個系統是否受 GMP/GLP/GCP 等法規管轄，以及適用哪些條款。

若等到系統快上線才做 GxP 評估，往往會遇到需要補做大量文件的困境，甚至導致上線延遲。

3.2 Specification and Verification

The GAMP specification and verification approach is not inherently linear; it also fully supports iterative and incremental (Agile) methods.

A linear approach is particularly suitable when system requirements are fully understood and defined upfront (Figure 3.3). The specification activities have equivalent verification steps to determine whether the specification has been met. A hierarchy of specifications may be required for larger systems, while specifications may be combined for smaller, simpler, or standard systems.

As shown in Figure 3.4, the life cycle and specification and verification approach described in the Guide is not inherently linear. This Guide supports the use of Agile approaches for product development, the development of custom applications, and incremental product configuration.

Factors for the successful adoption of Agile include:

  • A robust QMS within an appropriate organizational culture
  • Well-trained and highly disciplined teams following a well-defined process supported by effective tools and automation
  • Proper customer or product owner involvement

This Guide describes the overall GxP system life cycle from the perspective of the regulated company and does not define the software development process in detail. While this section provides a suggested approach, it is recognized that other models and approaches are equally acceptable.

3.2 規格說明與查驗方法

線性 vs. 迭代：GAMP 5 的重要進化

GAMP 5 第1版主要以 V-model（V型模型） 為框架——左側從上到下寫規格（URS → FRS → CS → 程式碼），右側由下到上做測試（單元測試 → 整合測試 → OQ → PQ）。

第2版明確承認：V-model 只是眾多可接受方法之一。Agile 方法——短週期迭代、持續交付、隨時調整需求——同樣完全符合 GAMP 5 框架。

關鍵認知：查驗不等於測試

「Verification（查驗）」是一個廣義概念，包含：

  • 設計審查（Design Review）
  • 程式碼審查（Code Review）
  • 組態設定審查（Configuration Review）
  • 各層次的測試執行

很多工程師習慣把「驗證」等同於「跑測試腳本」，但 GAMP 5 強調：在測試之前的設計審查本身就是查驗活動的一部分，甚至可以取代某些測試。

實務判斷：什麼時候用線性，什麼時候用 Agile？

情境建議方法
新 SCADA 系統（需求明確、供應商固定）線性（接近 V-model）
內部開發的資料分析工具（需求會演進）Agile（迭代+增量）
標準 ERP 模組上線（Category 4）彈性線性（可合併規格文件）
SaaS 系統（持續更新版本）Agile（CI/CD 概念）

3.3 Computerized System Validation Framework

GAMP advocates a computerized system validation framework to achieve and maintain GxP compliance throughout the computerized system life cycle.

The framework is based on system-specific validation plans and reports and the application of appropriate operational controls. Validation plans and reports provide a disciplined and consistent approach to meeting regulatory requirements, leading to appropriate documentation at the right level. Such documents are valuable both in preparing for, and during, regulatory inspections.

Where a computer system is regarded as one component of a wider manufacturing process or system, particularly in an integrated QbD environment, specific and separate computerized system validation may not be necessary. This environment requires both complete product and process understanding and that the CPPs can be accurately and reliably predicted and controlled over the design space. In such a case, the fitness for intended use of the computer system within the process may be adequately demonstrated by documented engineering or project activities together with subsequent process validation or continuous quality verification of the overall process or system. The same principle applies to the adoption of Process Analytical Technology (PAT).

For automated manufacturing equipment, separate computer system validation should be avoided. Computer system specification and verification should be part of an integrated engineering approach to ensure compliance and fitness for intended use of the complete automated equipment.

3.3 CSV 框架解析

CSV 框架的核心文件：驗證計畫與報告

每個 GxP 電腦化系統都應有：

  • Validation Plan（驗證計畫）：描述「我們打算怎麼驗證」——範圍、方法、責任分工、可接受標準
  • Validation Report（驗證報告）：記錄「我們實際上做了什麼」——活動摘要、偏差、未解決事項、適用聲明（fitness for intended use statement）

這兩份文件是查驗活動的書面骨架，查驗計畫訂好游戲規則，報告確認規則有被遵守。

進階概念：何時不需要獨立的 CSV？

GAMP 5 提出一個重要例外：當電腦化系統只是更大製程系統中的一個組件（例如整合 QbD 環境），如果整體製程驗證已能涵蓋電腦系統的適用性，就不需要單獨進行 CSV。

CDMO 情境：一台凍乾機（Lyophilizer）的 PLC 控制器，如果其關鍵製程參數（CPPs）的控制能力已在製程驗證（PPQ）中充分驗證，那麼對這台 PLC 單獨做 IQ/OQ/PQ 可能是重複且不必要的。這正是 GAMP 5 與 ISPE C&Q 指引（Volume 5）整合思維的體現。

3.4 Critical Thinking Through the Life Cycle

Critical thinking promotes informed decision-making and good judgment on where and how to apply and scale quality and compliance activities for computerized systems. It relies on knowledge of the business process and on the detailed comprehension and analysis of where the business process can potentially impact patient safety, product quality, and data integrity.

The extent and depth, formality, and level of documentation formality may, and should, vary to a considerable degree between different business processes, types of systems, functions within a system, and applications. Significant efficiency improvements can be achieved by focusing on what is essential in individual situations and avoiding unnecessary work.

Critical thinking is not a one-time activity and should be applied throughout the computerized system life cycle. As such, critical thinking should become a habitual mindset based on an intellectual commitment to continual improvement.

Figure 3.5 illustrates critical thinking for computerized systems whereby proactive adoption of a risk-based approach takes into account the multiple layers of assurance provided elsewhere within the business process — combining technical, procedural, and behavioral controls applied throughout the business process.

This holistic approach requires an initial investment of time and effort to analyze the overall business process that the computerized system will support, and the associated regulated data. Business process mapping and data flow diagrams capture this knowledge and facilitate the identification and understanding of the potential risks to patient safety, product quality, and data integrity.

Regulatory authorities are adopting critical thinking to help determine whether controls are fit for intended use. Practitioners should not consider that the level of regulatory compliance achieved is directly proportionate to the amount of paperwork produced. Too much paperwork can confuse and make it harder to maintain and inspect computerized systems. Regulators look for scaled and targeted activities with well-organized information and records that have an appropriate level of detail, supported by clear and unambiguous rationales explaining the critical thinking applied.

3.4 批判性思維的實際應用

什麼是「批判性思維」在 GMP 情境中的意義？

「Critical Thinking」在 GAMP 5 中不是哲學概念，而是一個實際的決策工具：

  • 分析業務流程在哪裡可能影響病患安全、產品品質或數據完整性
  • 根據這個分析，判斷需要多嚴格的控制
  • 將控制力度與風險成比例——高風險處嚴格，低風險處精簡

多層保證（Layers of Assurance）的概念

假設你的 MES 系統有一個功能：記錄無菌灌裝操作的環境監測數據。批判性思維的問法是：

  • 如果 MES 這個功能出錯，還有什麼其他控制層可以接住？（例如：操作員人工確認、QC 實驗室複核、SCADA 系統備份數據）
  • 如果這些其他控制層很強，那麼這個 MES 功能的驗證深度就可以相應降低
  • 如果 MES 是唯一來源（single point of truth），沒有備援，那麼驗證就必須更嚴格

這就是「多層保證」思維——不孤立評估系統，而是評估整個業務流程的防護網。

監管機構的新期待：品質勝於數量

GAMP 5 明確寫道：合規程度與文件數量不成正比。這是對藥廠普遍「文件越厚越安全」心態的直接反駁。

FDA、EMA 查廠官員現在更傾向於問：

  • 「你為什麼這樣設計驗證方案？」（要看你的風險評估邏輯）
  • 「這份規格書的這個要求，你是怎麼確認它被滿足的？」（看可追溯性）

而不是：「你的 IQ/OQ/PQ 文件在哪裡？」

Chapter 4: Life Cycle Phases  |  第4章：生命週期各階段詳解

Phase 1: CONCEPT

4 Life Cycle Phases

The life cycle approach described is not inherently linear, and is designed to be compatible with a wide range of models and methods, including iterative and incremental (Agile) approaches. References to documentation and deliverables should not be interpreted as always requiring traditional documents. The maintenance of records and information in appropriate and effective software tools is encouraged.

4.1 Concept

Detailed activities in this phase will depend on company approaches to initiating and justifying project commencement. Gaining management commitment to provide appropriate resources is an important pre-project activity.

An initial risk assessment or GxP assessment should be performed so that GxP regulated systems are identified before the project phase commences.

階段一：概念期

4.1 概念階段解析

概念階段的核心任務

概念階段是整個生命週期的起點，在這個階段投入的思考品質，直接決定後續專案的順暢度。主要任務包括：

  • 定義業務需求（不是系統需求）——「我們需要什麼功能」而非「我們要買什麼軟體」
  • 初步評估可能的解決方案（外購、客製化、SaaS？）
  • 進行 GxP 評估：這個系統是否直接或間接影響 GxP 活動？
  • 取得管理層承諾與資源支持

CDMO 實例：新 SCADA 系統的概念評估

CDMO 計畫為新建無菌灌裝線安裝 SCADA 系統。概念階段要做的事：

  • GxP 評估：SCADA 控制灌裝參數（灌裝量、充氮速度、溫度）→ 直接影響產品品質 → GxP 系統，適用 GMP Annex 11
  • 評估方案：整合型工控 SCADA vs. 獨立 PLC + HMI？
  • 初步成本效益分析：是否值得進入專案階段？
Phase 2: PROJECT

4.2 Project

This section describes the following project stages in more detail:

  • Planning
  • Specification, configuration, and coding
  • Verification
  • Reporting and release

The key supporting processes of risk management, change and configuration management, design review, traceability, and document management also are described in this section. Each project stage typically involves multiple activities.

4.2.1 Planning

Planning should cover all required activities, responsibilities, procedures, and timelines. As described in Section 2.1.3, life cycle activities should be scaled according to:

  • System impact on patient safety, product quality, and data integrity (risk assessment)
  • System complexity and novelty (architecture and nature of system components, including maturity and level of configuration or customization)
  • Outcome of supplier assessment (supplier capability)

A clear and complete understanding of the process to be supported and initial user requirements facilitates effective planning. Initial requirements are often gathered during the concept phase and refined and enhanced during the planning stage.

The extent and detail of requirements gathering and specification should be sufficient to support risk assessment, further specification, development of the system, and verification. Where appropriate, e.g., for process control systems, requirements should be traceable to relevant CPPs and CQAs.

Requirements are the responsibility of the user community and should be maintained and controlled.

階段二：專案期

4.2 專案階段解析

專案階段的流程架構

專案階段是系統生命週期中活動最密集的時期，可以理解為四個主要階段的串聯：

規劃 → 規格／組態設定 → 查驗 → 報告與放行

這四個階段不是嚴格線性的——規劃期間可能已開始部分規格制定，查驗活動從規格審查就已開始。

4.2.1 規劃階段：三個決策維度

規劃階段最重要的決策是「這個系統的驗證要做到什麼程度」，GAMP 5 要求從三個維度評估：

  1. 風險程度：系統對病患安全、產品品質、數據完整性的影響有多大？影響越大，驗證越嚴格。
  2. 複雜度與新穎性：是成熟的標準軟體（低新穎性）還是全新客製化開發（高新穎性）？越新穎，越需要嚴格測試。
  3. 供應商能力：供應商有沒有通過藥廠稽核？供應商的測試文件能不能被採用？供應商能力越強，受監管公司的工作量越可以降低。

需求的歸屬：用戶社群的責任

GAMP 5 明確指出：需求的制定、維護與控制是用戶社群（user community）的責任——也就是實際使用這個系統的業務部門（生產、QC、QA 等），而不是 IT 部門或驗證部門。

實務提醒：URS（用戶需求規格書）必須由業務端主導撰寫，IT 或供應商只是協助澄清技術可行性。許多 CDMO 的問題在於讓 IT 單獨寫 URS，最後系統做出來不符合 GMP 業務邏輯。

4.2.2 Specification, Configuration, and Coding

The role of specification is to enable systems to be developed, verified, and maintained. The number and level of detail of the specifications will vary depending upon the type of system and its intended use. For example, software design specifications are not expected from the regulated company for non-custom products.

Specifications should be adequate to support subsequent activities, including risk assessment, further specification and development of the system, verification as appropriate, and system maintenance and update.

Any required configuration should be performed in accordance with a controlled and repeatable process. Any required software coding should be performed in accordance with defined standards. The need for code reviews within the organization producing the software should be considered.

Configuration management and version control are intrinsic and vital aspects of controlled configuration and coding. Specifications should be maintained and controlled.

4.2.2 規格說明、組態設定與程式撰寫

規格文件的目的：三個用途

規格文件存在的理由不是「監管機構要看」，而是：

  • 開發依據：讓供應商知道要做什麼
  • 查驗依據：讓測試人員知道要確認什麼
  • 維護依據：未來變更時，讓人知道原本的設計意圖

如果規格文件不能服務這三個目的，它就是沒有價值的文件。

URS → FRS → CS 的實務流程

以 CDMO 導入 LIMS 為例，規格文件的層次關係：

  • URS（用戶需求規格）：「QC 需要記錄每批原料的效期，並自動在效期前30天發出警告」
  • FRS（功能需求規格）：「系統應在效期 - 30 天時觸發警告通知，發送至指定的收件群組」（供應商層級）
  • CS（組態規格）：「Alert_Rule_01: Expiry_Date - 30 days, Recipient_Group: QC_Supervisors」（具體設定值）

對於 Category 3 系統（標準產品），通常只需要 URS + 測試記錄，不需要 FRS 和 CS。

版本控制是核心控制措施

GAMP 5 特別強調：組態管理與版本控制是受控組態設定的內在必要條件。在 GMP 環境中，這意味著：

  • 每一次系統設定的變更都必須有記錄可循
  • 在任何時間點都能回溯系統當時的狀態
  • 沒有經過版本控制的系統設定，在查廠時無法證明「受控狀態」

4.2.3 Verification

Verification confirms that specifications have been met. This may involve multiple stages of reviews and testing depending on the type of system, the development method applied, and its use.

While verification is shown as a single box on Figure 4.1, verification activities occur throughout the project stages. For example, design reviews should verify specifications during the specification stage.

Testing computerized systems is a fundamental verification activity. Testing is concerned with identifying defects so that they can be corrected, as well as demonstrating that the system meets requirements.

The use of effective and appropriate testing tools is encouraged. Such tools should have documented assessments for their adequacy (refer to EU Annex 11 Clause 4.7) and be controlled in use; however, validation is not required.

Testing is often performed at several levels depending on the risk, complexity, and novelty. Tests may be defined in one or more test specifications to cover hardware, software, configuration, and acceptance.

An appropriate test strategy should be developed based on the risk, complexity, and novelty. Supplier documentation should be assessed and used if suitable. The strategy should define which types of testing are required and the level and purpose of test specifications.

4.2.3 查驗活動解析

查驗貫穿整個專案期

這是 GAMP 5 一個重要的視角修正：查驗不只是最後的測試執行，而是貫穿專案的持續活動：

  • 規格階段：設計審查（Design Review）確認規格合理且可實現
  • 組態設定階段：組態審查確認設定符合規格
  • 測試階段：各層次測試確認功能符合需求
  • 放行階段：驗證報告確認所有查驗活動已完成

測試工具的使用：不需要驗證，但需要評估

很多藥廠不敢使用自動化測試工具（如 Selenium、UFT），因為擔心「這些工具本身要不要驗證」。

GAMP 5 的答案明確：測試工具不需要驗證（validation is not required），但需要：

  • 記錄評估文件，確認工具適合於預定用途（adequacy assessment）
  • 在使用中保持受控狀態（controlled in use）

這大幅降低了引入自動化測試工具的合規門檻，CDMO 應積極利用。

4.2.4 Reporting and Release

The system should be accepted for use in the operating environment and released into that environment in accordance with a controlled and documented process. Acceptance and release of the system for use in GxP regulated activities should follow a defined process and involve oversight and input of the process owner, system owner, and the appropriate quality function as necessary and applicable.

This may be a continuously managed process rather than a one-off event, for example Continuous Integration and Continuous Deployment (CI/CD), and Software as a Service (SaaS).

A computerized system validation report should be produced summarizing the activities performed, any deviations from the plan, any outstanding and corrective actions, and provide a statement of fitness for intended use of the system.

A well-managed system handover from the project team to the process owner, system owner, and operational users is a prerequisite for effectively maintaining compliance of the system during operation.

4.2.4 報告與放行

驗證報告的必要內容

一份完整的 Validation Report（驗證報告）應包含：

  • 已執行的查驗活動摘要
  • 相對於計畫的偏差（Deviations from plan）
  • 未解決事項及其矯正行動
  • 適用聲明（Fitness for Intended Use Statement）——明確聲明系統適合在 GxP 環境中使用

移交（Handover）：被忽視的關鍵環節

GAMP 5 特別強調移交管理的重要性。在很多 CDMO 中，系統驗證完成後，專案團隊就解散了，但運維團隊對系統的了解遠不足夠。

良好的移交應確保：

  • 運維程序（SOPs）已制定並培訓
  • 隱性知識（implicit knowledge）已轉移——不只是文件，還有「為什麼這樣設計」的背景理解
  • 支援服務（如供應商合約、維護計畫）已就位

4.2.5 Supporting Processes

4.2.5.1 Risk Management
An appropriate risk-management process should be established throughout the project phase.

4.2.5.2 Change and Configuration Management
Appropriate configuration management processes should be established such that a computerized system and all its constituent components can be identified and defined at any point during its life cycle. Change management procedures covering software, hardware, and documentation should be established. Appropriate change processes should be applied to both project and operational phases.

4.2.5.3 Design Review
At suitable stages during the life cycle, planned and systematic design reviews of specifications, design, and development should be performed. This design review process should evaluate deliverables to ensure that they satisfy the specified requirements. The rigor of the design review process and the extent of documentation should be based on risk, complexity, and novelty. Design reviews are mainly applicable for custom applications and within the supplier product development processes.

4.2.5.4 Traceability
Traceability is a process for ensuring that:

  • Requirements are traceable back to business process needs
  • Requirements are addressed and traceable to the appropriate functional and design elements in the specifications
  • Requirements can be traced to the appropriate verification

Traceability should be focused on aspects critical to patient safety, product quality, and data integrity. The use of traceability tools and automated traceability methods is encouraged.

4.2.5.5 Documentation and Knowledge Management
Management of documentation includes preparation, review, approval, issue, change, withdrawal, and storage. Documentation, tools, and systems contain explicit knowledge that is captured and codified, whereas implied knowledge is context-specific knowledge acquired through personal experience. Both need to be considered for successful initial and subsequent system deployments.

4.2.5 支援流程解析

可追溯性矩陣（RTM）的實務價值

可追溯性（Traceability）的核心工具是需求可追溯性矩陣（Requirement Traceability Matrix, RTM），它確保：

  • 每個 URS 需求都有對應的規格條款
  • 每個規格條款都有對應的測試案例
  • 每個測試案例的結果都有記錄

RTM 在查廠時讓查廠官員可以快速確認：「這個需求，你是怎麼驗證它被滿足的？」

設計審查：什麼情況下需要正式進行？

GAMP 5 說設計審查「主要適用於客製化應用和供應商內部產品開發」。轉譯到實務：

  • Category 3 標準產品：通常不需要設計審查，規格審查即可
  • Category 4 組態設定產品：對複雜的組態設定可考慮進行組態設計審查
  • Category 5 客製化應用：必須進行設計審查，因為沒有現成的產品功能規格可以依賴

隱性知識管理：最常被忽視的合規風險

GAMP 5 提到「implied knowledge（隱性知識）」——這是指存在於人腦中、未被文件化的系統知識。

典型風險場景：唯一熟悉某 SCADA 系統設定邏輯的工程師離職，而文件中只記錄了「做了什麼」，沒有記錄「為什麼這樣做」。一旦系統出現問題或需要變更，繼任工程師根本不知道設計意圖，容易引入新的錯誤。

4.2.6 Practical Examples — GAMP Categories

This section shows how the general approach may be applied in a scalable manner to three common types of systems, using Categorization of software.

Computerized systems are generally made up of a combination of components from different GAMP Categories. Categories 3 to 5 should be viewed as a continuum with no absolute boundaries. Table-driven approaches purely based on categorization decisions should be avoided.

4.2.6.1 Category 3 — Standard Product: Software products used off-the-shelf (not configurable for a specific business process). Approach: one level of specification and verification is typically applicable. Testing typically covers correct installation, tests demonstrating fitness for intended use, and any further tests from risk/supplier assessments.

4.2.6.2 Category 4 — Configured Product: Commercial software products configured for a specific business process. A flexible approach to specification and verification may be applied. Testing covers correct installation, configuration of the system, functionality supporting the specific business process, and acceptance against requirements. Testing should be focused on the configuration.

4.2.6.3 Category 5 — Custom Application: Developed to meet individual user requirements where no commercially available solution is suitable. May use linear (waterfall) or Agile methods. Testing covers correct installation, functionality and design, fitness for intended use, and any further tests from risk assessments. Rigorous testing should be performed at both functional and design levels.

4.2.6 GAMP 分類實務應用

分類只是起點，不是終點

GAMP 5 非常清楚地警告：不要純粹依賴分類表格來決定驗證活動（Table-driven approaches should be avoided）。

分類（Category 3/4/5）只是一個起點，實際的驗證深度還要考慮：

  • 這個系統功能對 GxP 的影響有多大？
  • 供應商的品質系統可靠度如何？
  • 系統的複雜度和新穎性是什麼水準？

同樣是 Category 4 系統，一個管理批次記錄的 LIMS 和一個管理員工訓練記錄的系統，驗證深度可以差距很大。

4.2.6.4 Terminology — IQ/OQ/PQ vs. GAMP 5

The specific terminology used to describe life cycle activities and deliverables varies from company to company. This Guide does not use traditional pharmaceutical process validation terminology such as IQ/OQ/PQ to describe system life cycle activities. This aligns with the US FDA, who also chose not to use the terminology in General Principles of Software Validation because the terminology may not be well understood among many software professionals. Such terminology implies an inherently linear approach.

Whatever terminology is used for verification activity, the overriding requirement is that the regulated company can demonstrate that the system is compliant and fit for intended use.

4.2.6.4 術語對應解析

為什麼 GAMP 5 要放棄 IQ/OQ/PQ 術語？

傳統的 IQ/OQ/PQ 術語有幾個根本問題：

  • 隱含線性假設：IQ → OQ → PQ 的順序暗示驗證只能按照這個固定順序進行，與 Agile 方法不相容
  • 跨界溝通障礙：IT 工程師和軟體供應商不理解這些術語，溝通摩擦大
  • 誤導重點：許多藥廠把重點放在「完成 IQ/OQ/PQ 文件」，而非「確認系統適合用途」

實務轉換：如何在現有 GMP 體系中使用這個觀念？

很多監管機構和公司的 SOP 仍然使用 IQ/OQ/PQ 術語。GAMP 5 不是要你立刻廢除這些術語，而是要你理解：

  • IQ 的本質 = 確認安裝和組態設定正確（不一定需要一份叫做「IQ Protocol」的文件）
  • OQ 的本質 = 確認系統在規定操作範圍內運行符合規格
  • PQ 的本質 = 確認系統在實際業務環境中適合預定用途

如果你的公司的 SOP 要求做 IQ/OQ/PQ，可以繼續用這個框架，但在規劃時用 GAMP 5 的批判性思維決定每個部分要做多深。

FAT / SAT 的角色

GAMP 5 提到 Factory Acceptance Testing (FAT) 和 Site Acceptance Testing (SAT) 是受監管公司在正式放行前的驗收活動。重點原則：查驗活動不應不必要地重複。

如果供應商已在工廠做了充分的 FAT，受監管公司到現場後的 SAT 可以聚焦在安裝確認和現場特定條件的測試，而不是把 FAT 的所有測試案例在現場重跑一遍。

Phase 3: OPERATION

4.3 Operation

This section provides guidance on system operation. Not all the activities will be directly relevant to all systems. The approach and required activities should be selected and scaled according to the nature, risk, and complexity of the system in question through the application of critical thinking.

Once the system has been accepted and released for use, there is a need to maintain compliance and fitness for intended use throughout its operational life. This is achieved by the use of established and up-to-date procedures and training that cover use, maintenance, and management.

The operational phase of a system may last many years, and may include changes to services, software, hardware, the business process, and regulatory requirements. The integrity of the system and its data should be maintained at all times and verified as part of periodic review.

Change management should provide a dependable mechanism for prompt implementation of technically sound improvements following the approach to specification, design, and verification described in this Guide. The rigor of the approach, including the extent of documentation and verification, should be based on the risk and complexity of the change through the application of critical thinking.

Key operational process groups (Table 4.2):

  • Handover
  • Service Management and System Monitoring
  • Incident and Problem Management, Deviation, and CAPA
  • Configuration Management
  • Audits and Review (Periodic Review)
  • Continuity Management (Backup, BCP, Disaster Recovery)
  • Security and System Administration
  • Records Management
階段三：操作期

4.3 操作階段解析

操作階段：最長且最容易被忽視的階段

操作階段通常是整個系統生命週期中持續時間最長的——一個 SCADA 或 LIMS 系統的操作期可長達10-15年。然而在許多藥廠，驗證工作完成後，合規關注度就大幅下降。

GAMP 5 的立場是：操作階段的合規維護與初始驗證同等重要。維持合規的核心機制是有效的操作程序、培訓、變更管理和定期審查。

CDMO 情境：SCADA 系統的操作階段管理

你的 CDMO 已有一套 SCADA 系統控制無菌灌裝線三年了。操作階段的典型事件：

  • 供應商推送軟體更新（需要走變更控制，評估衝擊）
  • 新增一台灌裝頭（需要重新評估並驗證相關功能）
  • 電腦崩潰，需要重裝（備份與還原程序是否驗證過？）
  • 年度定期審查（確認系統仍符合現行法規要求）

操作階段的八大流程群組

GAMP 5 Table 4.2 列出了操作階段的主要流程群組，每個都有對應的附錄（O1-O13）。這不是說每個系統都要建立所有這些流程，而是根據系統的風險和複雜度選擇適用的項目。

4.3.1 Handover

Handover is the process for transfer of responsibility of a computerized system from a project team or a service group to the operations team or a new service group. The support group should be involved at the earliest opportunity to ensure effective knowledge transfer and establishment of operational procedures. A period of elevated support and maintenance, often referred to as Hypercare Services, may be arranged to facilitate the transfer.

4.3.3 Incident and Problem Management and CAPA

The incident management process aims to categorize incidents to direct them to the most appropriate resource or complementary process to achieve a timely resolution; whereas problem management involves analyzing root causes and preventing incidents from happening in the future.

There should be a procedure defining how problems related to software, hardware, and procedures should be captured, reviewed, prioritized, progressed, escalated, and closed.

CAPA is a process for investigating, understanding, and correcting discrepancies based on root-cause analysis, while attempting to prevent their recurrence. In the operational environment the CAPA process should feed into the overall CAPA system used for GxP operations.

4.3.4 Change Management

Change management is a critical activity that is fundamental to maintaining the proper functioning and controlled status of systems and processes. All changes that are proposed during the operational phase of a computerized system, whether related to software (including middleware), hardware, infrastructure, or use of the system, should be subject to a formal change-control process.

This process should ensure that proposed changes are appropriately reviewed to assess impact and risk. Regression analysis and regression testing may be required. The process should ensure that changes are suitably evaluated, authorized, documented, tested, and approved before implementation, and subsequently closed.

Configuration management includes those activities necessary to precisely define a computerized system at any point during its life cycle. Configuration items should only be modified by application of a change management process.

4.3 關鍵操作流程解析

Incident vs. Problem：兩個不同的管理流程

這組概念來自 ITIL（IT 服務管理框架）：

  • Incident Management（事件管理）：目標是「最快速度恢復服務」。例如 LIMS 突然當機，IT 重啟服務，系統恢復——這是 Incident 管理，重點是速度。
  • Problem Management（問題管理）：目標是「找到根因，防止再發」。同一台 LIMS 每個月都當機一次，這是 Problem，需要深入調查根因（資料庫索引效率？記憶體洩漏？）並解決。

在 GMP 環境中，Incident 需要記錄（偏差記錄），Problem 需要 CAPA。兩者要連動。

變更控制：與 GMP 設施變更控制的連結

GAMP 5 要求：所有電腦化系統的軟硬體、基礎設施、使用方式的變更，都必須走正式的變更控制流程（change-control process）。

這與 GMP 廠房的變更控制 SOP 是同一個邏輯框架。在 CDMO 中，電腦化系統的變更控制應整合到廠區統一的變更控制體系中，而非孤立運作。

關鍵要求：變更在實施前必須完成評估、授權、文件記錄、測試和審核。這意味著緊急變更也要有後補記錄機制，不能以「緊急」為由跳過文件要求。

組態項目（Configuration Item）概念

Configuration Item（CI）是系統中在正常操作下不應改變的組件。例如：

  • LIMS 的版本號和安裝包
  • 資料庫的 schema 設計
  • 系統設定參數檔
  • 整合介面的 API 定義

任何 CI 的變更都必須通過變更管理流程——不能直接在生產環境手動修改設定，然後事後補記錄。

4.3.5 Periodic Review

Periodic reviews are used throughout the operational life of systems to verify that they remain compliant with regulatory requirements, fit for intended use, and meet company policies and procedures, including those related to data integrity. Periodic reviews should be:

  • Scheduled at an interval appropriate to the impact and operational history of the system. Risk and other assessments should be used to determine which systems are in scope and the frequency of periodic review.
  • Performed in accordance with a predefined process
  • Documented with corrective actions tracked to satisfactory completion

4.3.6 Continuity Management

4.3.6.1 Backup and Restore: Processes and procedures should be established to ensure that backup copies of software, records, and data are made, maintained, and retained for a defined period within safe and secure areas. Restore procedures should be established, tested, and the results of that testing documented.

4.3.6.2 Business Continuity Planning (BCP): A series of related activities ensuring that an organization is fully prepared to respond effectively in the event of failures and disruptions. Critical business processes and systems should be identified, and the risks to each assessed. Plans should be established and exercised to ensure timely and effective resumption. Plans should be prioritized based on patient and business risk, in case of disruption to multiple systems.

4.3.6.3 Disaster Recovery Planning: A subset of BCP, plans should be specified, approved, and rehearsed for the recovery of specific systems in the event of a disaster. Disaster Recovery (DR) plans often require a shared responsibility model between internal organizations and external service providers (e.g., cloud service providers).

4.3.7 Security Management

Computerized systems and data should be adequately protected against willful or accidental loss, damage, or unauthorized change. Role-based security should be implemented. Security provisions should ensure that data is protected against unauthorized intrusions including cyber security attacks. Intrusion prevention and detection processes should be in place.

4.3.5–4.3.8 定期審查、持續運作與安全管理

定期審查的頻率：沒有固定答案

GAMP 5 沒有規定「每年一次」或「每兩年一次」，而是要求根據以下因素決定頻率：

  • 系統對患者安全和產品品質的影響程度
  • 系統的操作歷史（有沒有頻繁出現問題？）
  • 法規環境是否有變化

高風險系統（如直接控制無菌製程的 SCADA）可能需要每年審查；低風險的 HR 系統可能三年一次就足夠。

備份與還原：最容易被忽略的 GMP 要求

幾乎所有 IT 系統都有備份排程，但在 GMP 環境中，備份還原程序必須被測試，且測試結果要記錄。

常見問題：「我們有每天備份，但從來沒有試過從備份還原。」這在 GMP 環境中是不合規的——因為沒有測試過的程序，不能確保它能在緊急情況下正常運作。

BCP 的優先順序邏輯

當多個系統同時發生故障時，BCP 需要明確的優先順序。GAMP 5 要求基於患者風險和業務風險來排序。

CDMO 的典型優先順序：

  1. 批次記錄系統（直接影響產品放行決策）
  2. SCADA/製程控制系統（影響生產連續性）
  3. LIMS（影響放行測試）
  4. ERP/採購系統（影響業務，但可短期手動作業）
Phase 4: RETIREMENT

4.4 Retirement

This activity covers system withdrawal, system decommissioning, system disposal, and migration of required data.

4.4.1 Withdrawal

Withdrawal is the removal of the system from active operation, i.e., users are deactivated, interfaces disabled. No data should be added to the system from this point forward. Special access should be retained for data reporting, results analysis, and support.

4.4.2 Decommissioning

Decommissioning is a controlled process by which an application or system is removed from use in an organization once it has been retired and/or has become obsolete.

4.4.3 Disposal

Data, documentation, software, or hardware may be permanently destroyed. Each may reach this stage at a different time. Data and documentation may not be disposed of until they have reached the end of the record-retention period, as specified in the company's record-retention policy, following an authorized and documented process.

Consideration should be given to:

  • Establishing procedures covering system retirement, including withdrawal, decommissioning, and disposal as appropriate
  • Documentary evidence to be retained of actions taken during retirement of the system
  • GxP records to be maintained, their required retention periods, and which records can be destroyed
  • The need to migrate records to a new system or archive, and the method of verifying and documenting this process
  • Ability to retrieve these migrated records on the new system

4.4.4 Data Migration

Data migration may be required when an existing system is replaced by a new system, when an operational system experiences a significant change, or when the scope of use of a system is changed. The migration process should be accurate, complete, and verified.

階段四：退役期

4.4 退役階段解析

退役的三個步驟

GAMP 5 將退役拆解為三個概念上不同的步驟：

  • Withdrawal（撤除）：停止使用——用戶帳號停用、介面切斷、不再寫入新資料。但系統可能還在線上，供查閱歷史資料。
  • Decommissioning（除役）：受控地移除系統——從基礎設施中卸除，包含硬體和軟體層面的移除。
  • Disposal（銷毀）：永久刪除資料或銷毀硬體。注意：GxP 記錄必須等到記錄保留期限到期後才能銷毀。

CDMO 情境：舊 MES 退役

假設你的 CDMO 要用新的 MES 取代已用10年的舊 MES：

  • 撤除：切換至新 MES 後，舊 MES 禁止新批次建立，但保留查閱舊批次記錄的能力
  • 資料遷移：決定哪些歷史批次記錄要遷移到新系統？哪些保留在歸檔系統？遷移的準確性和完整性需要被驗證
  • 除役：確認記錄保留期限要求（通常批次記錄保留期為產品有效期 + 1 年或最低 5 年），滿足後才能進行伺服器下線
  • 銷毀：依資料安全要求，確保敏感資料的安全銷毀並留存記錄

資料遷移的三個驗證要求

GAMP 5 要求資料遷移必須是：

  • Accurate（準確）：遷移後的資料與原資料一致
  • Complete（完整）：所有應遷移的記錄都已遷移，無遺漏
  • Verified（已查驗）：上述兩點需要有文件化的確認活動

資料遷移是數據完整性（Data Integrity）的重大風險點。一旦資料在遷移中損壞或遺失，且未被發現，可能影響未來的批次回顧、趨勢分析或法規查詢。

Chapter Summary — Key Takeaways

  • Life cycle = 4 phases: Concept → Project → Operation → Retirement. Each phase has defined activities; the operation phase is typically the longest.
  • Specification & verification is not linear: GAMP 5 explicitly supports Agile, iterative, and incremental approaches alongside the traditional V-model.
  • CSV framework is built on system-specific validation plans and reports, with scalable rigor based on risk, complexity, and novelty — not on document count.
  • Critical thinking is a continuous mindset across all phases: consider multiple layers of assurance, avoid unnecessary paperwork, and document the rationale for your decisions.
  • IQ/OQ/PQ → GAMP 5 activities: DQ ≈ Design Review; IQ ≈ Installation/configuration verification; OQ ≈ Functional/operational testing; PQ ≈ Fitness-for-use acceptance testing.
  • Operation phase requires formal change control for all changes, periodic reviews, incident/problem management with CAPA integration, backup/restore testing, and security management.
  • Retirement is a controlled process: withdrawal → decommissioning → disposal, with data migration verified for accuracy and completeness.

章節摘要 — 核心要點

  • 生命週期 = 4個階段：概念→專案→操作→退役。操作階段通常持續最長，但合規管理要求最易被忽視。
  • 查驗方法多元化：V-model 只是眾多可接受方法之一；Agile 迭代方法在 GAMP 5 框架下完全合規。
  • 批判性思維貫穿全生命週期：合規程度與文件數量無關，與控制的針對性和有效性有關。多層保證思維是評估風險的核心工具。
  • 分類（Cat. 3/4/5）是起點，不是終點：實際驗證深度還要考慮 GxP 影響程度、供應商能力和系統新穎性。
  • 操作階段四大支柱：變更管理（所有變更走變更控制）、定期審查（依風險決定頻率）、CAPA 整合、業務持續計畫（BCP + DR + 備份還原測試）。
  • 退役不是簡單地關機：需要規劃記錄保留期、資料遷移驗證、安全銷毀，並留存退役活動的文件證據。
