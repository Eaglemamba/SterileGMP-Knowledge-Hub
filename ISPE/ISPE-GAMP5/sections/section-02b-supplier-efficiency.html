Chapter 7 — Supplier Activities  |  第 7 章：供應商活動

7.1 Supplier Products, Applications, and Services

Although the responsibility for compliance with GxP regulations lies with the regulated company, the supplier may have considerable involvement in the process. Regulated companies may be able to leverage supplier knowledge and documentation, subject to suitability following formal assessment. This may involve an audit, depending on risk, complexity, and novelty.

Suppliers provide a range of products, applications, and services for hardware, software, and related technologies including cloud-computing services. The relationship between supplier and regulated company will vary significantly depending upon the product, application, or scope of service being provided. Consultants, for instance those acting as implementation partners, should have sufficient education, training, and experience to advise on the subject for which they are retained.

7.1.1 Standard Product (GAMP Category 3)

If the product is purchased off-the-shelf and does not require configuration to support business processes, or only offers defined ranges of factory-provided values or ranges (parameterization), supplier involvement with the regulated company is typically limited to the provision of documentation, training, support, and maintenance. The product should be developed and maintained by the supplier in accordance with good practices.

7.1.2 Configured Product (GAMP Category 4)

If the product requires configuration to support specific business processes, supplier involvement will typically include support with specification, configuration, verification, and operation of the system. Procedures to follow should be agreed between the regulated company and the supplier and be documented in the appropriate plan. Procedures adopted may be those of the regulated company or from the supplier QMS.

7.1.3 Custom Application (GAMP Category 5)

For a custom application, the regulated company typically contracts a supplier to develop the application based on defined requirements. Therefore, the supplier will be involved during the full project life cycle of the system, and also to provide support during system operation. Procedures to follow should be agreed between the regulated company and the supplier and be documented in the appropriate plan.

7.1.4 Service Provision

Suppliers that provide IT/IS services (including cloud-computing services) should operate within a QMS. Quality planning should define the activities, procedures, deliverables, and responsibilities for establishing delivery and monitoring of the service. Such a plan is a contractual document, and as such, should be approved for use by both the supplier and the regulated company.

The required information may be satisfactorily covered by other contractual documents such as a service level agreement (SLA), in which case a separate plan would not be required.

GxP 法規責任永遠在受規管公司（即 CDMO），供應商只是協助者。即使供應商做了所有測試，CDMO 仍需評估並正式接受那些測試結果。這一點不因 Cat 3/4/5 而改變。

雲端服務供應商必須在其 QMS 內運作，且質量計劃須由雙方共同核准。若 SLA 已涵蓋必要資訊，則可免除另立品質計劃。建議參考 ISPE GAMP GPG: Enabling Innovation Chapter 4 (IT Service Management)。

7.3 Quality Management System

It is recommended that suppliers follow a QMS, preferably based on recognized standards. The QMS should define:

  • The process being followed to deliver and support the product, application, or service
  • The process to understand customer business process needs and capture requirements (including business, quality, and regulatory requirements)
  • Responsibilities, including clear separation of authority between QA and other groups such as product development, product support, finance, or marketing
  • Deliverables
  • Management of documentation, records, and information
  • Planned reviews of the QMS and internal audits
  • Management of incidents/problems/non-conformities
  • Approach to continual improvement, including the adoption of current software development models, methods and good practices, and associated selection of supporting tools and automation

The QMS should be based on a life cycle concept. There are many equally valid life cycle approaches that may be used by suppliers, including Agile. This Guide does not recommend any particular approach, but rather highlights those activities expected of suppliers to support the regulated company in achieving and maintaining compliance.

The QMS should include procedures covering:

  • Software management, control, and release
  • Development change control
  • Configuration management
  • Traceability
  • Training of supplier staff
  • Document, records, and information management
  • Backup and restore

The QMS should cover the approach to continual improvement. CMMI provides an approach based on a framework for assessing and improving organizational capability and maturity. The use of ITIL for IT service delivery is encouraged. Industry guidance such as ISPE GAMP should be treated as supporting information and should not override the supplier's established QMS.

中文解說：供應商 QMS 要求

Agile 與傳統瀑布式開發都可接受

GAMP 5 第二版明確表示不偏好特定生命週期模型。無論是 Waterfall、Agile (Scrum/Kanban) 或混合方法，只要供應商能提供符合法規要求的可追溯文件，都是可接受的。

對 CDMO 的實務意義：當供應商說「我們用 Agile 所以沒有傳統 FS/DS」時，你應要求等效的 Agile 文件（User Stories、Sprint Review Records、Acceptance Criteria），而非直接拒絕。

QMS 中的 QA 獨立性

供應商 QMS 要求 QA 與開發、行銷、財務等部門有明確的職責分離。這與製藥 GMP 的 QA 獨立性概念相同。在評估供應商時，可詢問：「誰有權批准產品發布？QA 是否能夠獨立阻止不合規的發布？」

CMMI 與 ITIL 是加分項

供應商若持有 CMMI Level 3+ 認證，表示其軟體開發成熟度較高，可作為減少現場審計的依據。持有 ITIL 認證的 IT 服務供應商在事件管理、變更管理、問題管理方面有規範化流程，對 SaaS 平台特別重要。

7.4 Requirements

Requirements are captured and defined internally by the supplier (in the case of product development), and/or may be provided by the customer (for a configured product, custom application, or a service). The requirements should define clearly and precisely what the system should do and state any constraints.

Requirements should be developed, reviewed, and approved in accordance with the development method implemented by the supplier whether it be linear or iterative. Changes to requirements should be controlled.

Regulated companies wish to maximize the use of supplier testing to support their compliance activities. Therefore, requirements should be written such that they can be tested. Individual requirements should be traceable through the life cycle.

For configured products and custom applications, the regulated company should describe the business processes to be automated. In the case of configured products, these processes should be aligned with the functionality of the product to be used. This may require significant process reengineering.

The use of appropriate and effective software tools should be considered by suppliers to manage information/records that may otherwise have been managed via paper documentation.

中文解說：需求規格的可測試性

核心原則：需求必須可被測試

GAMP 5 強調供應商的需求必須「可測試」(testable)。這不只是對供應商的要求，也是受規管公司在審查 URS/RS 時的核心標準。不可測試的需求（如「系統應易於使用」）在驗證中毫無意義。

配置型產品可能需要流程再造

若採購 Cat 4 系統（如 SAP、Oracle），業務流程需要「配合系統標準功能」，而非強迫系統配合所有現有流程。GAMP 5 明確指出這「可能需要顯著的流程再造 (process reengineering)」。CDMO 在引入大型 LIMS 或 ERP 時，應提前評估這個代價。

7.5 Supplier Quality Planning

The supplier should define how the QMS will be implemented for a particular product, application, or service. This should include defining the life cycle model, project organization, activities, procedures, deliverables, and responsibilities for establishing fitness for intended use. The approach may include prototyping or other software development techniques. The role of supplier QA should be clearly defined.

These supplier quality requirements may be captured in a separate Quality Plan or other supplier documentation. Quality requirements should be clearly documented, reviewed, approved, accessible, and followed.

7.5.1 Prototyping

Prototyping methods may be used to clarify user requirements or to evaluate areas of risk. Typically, a prototype is used to evaluate the acceptability of a user interface, the performance of critical algorithms, suitability of the overall solution, or aspects of system performance such as capacity and speed.

The aims and objectives of the prototype should be clearly defined, and the prototype evaluated against these to ensure the objectives are met. Prototyping is often used as part of Agile software development where control is achieved through Agile ceremonies (e.g., daily Scrum), control of the sprint backlog, use of tools, and sprint reviews and retrospectives.

中文解說：供應商品質計劃

品質計劃 (Quality Plan) 的核心內容

對受規管公司而言，供應商品質計劃是非常重要的評估文件。它應涵蓋：

  • 採用的生命週期模型（Waterfall、Agile、混合）
  • QA 角色與職責分工
  • 關鍵交付物清單（Deliverables List）
  • 測試策略概述
  • 變更控制程序

若供應商無法提供品質計劃，這是一個警示信號（Red Flag），應在供應商評估報告中記錄。

原型法 (Prototyping) 對 CDMO 的意義

當你委託開發客製化系統（如 BPR 自動化、設備介面系統）時，建議要求供應商先提供原型。這讓你在大量開發投入前，能驗證關鍵功能是否符合需求，並識別潛在風險，降低後期變更的成本。Agile 的 Sprint Demo 即是原型法的一種形式。

7.6 Sub-Supplier Assessments

Suppliers should formally assess their sub-suppliers as part of quality planning. They also should be periodically reassessed in accordance with the QMS.

The decision whether to perform an audit of their sub-suppliers should be documented and based on a risk assessment.

In some cases, such as those sub-suppliers related to the location and hosting of GxP regulated data, sub-supplier management may have a direct impact on regulated company compliance, and should be made transparent.

See Appendix M2 for further details on supplier assessments.

中文解說：次供應商評估

雲端時代的次供應商風險

這是現代 CSV 最重要的課題之一。當你使用 SaaS LIMS（如 Veeva Vault、Benchling），而 LIMS 廠商的伺服器在 AWS 或 Azure 上，那麼 AWS/Azure 就是你的次供應商。

GAMP 5 明確指出：若次供應商涉及 GxP 數據的存儲與託管，次供應商管理對受規管公司的合規性有直接影響，必須透明化。

實務場景：如何處理雲端次供應商

  • 要求主供應商提供：其次供應商評估報告的摘要（或 ISO 27001、SOC 2 Type II 認證）
  • 在技術協議 (Technical Agreement) 中載明：數據存儲地點、備份策略、DR 計劃
  • 定期再評估：若次供應商（如雲端平台）發生重大安全事件，主供應商應通知你
  • 供應商切換條款：若主供應商更換雲端平台，應事先通知並允許你進行影響評估

第三方函式庫也是次供應商

當軟體供應商在其產品中使用開源或商業第三方函式庫（如 log4j、OpenSSL），這些函式庫的供應商也屬於次供應商範疇。近年來 log4shell 漏洞事件就是典型例子。優良供應商應維護軟體物料清單 (SBOM, Software Bill of Materials) 並進行依賴項安全掃描。

7.7 Specifications

For product development, the supplier should document the functionality and design of the system to meet the defined requirements. This should cover software, hardware, and configuration.

References to documentation and specifications should not be interpreted as requiring traditional documents, and the maintenance of records in appropriate and effective software tools is encouraged.

Functional specifications should clearly and completely describe what the product will do, produced such that objective testing can be subsequently performed. Design specifications should be based on the functional specifications and should be sufficiently detailed so that the product can be developed.

Specifications should be reviewed and approved with traceability established between related documents. They should be managed under change control.

If the supplier is involved in configuring a product, service, or developing a custom application, the number and level of specifications can vary considerably and should be agreed with the regulated company.

7.8 Design Reviews

Design reviews evaluate deliverables against standards and requirements, identify issues, and propose required corrective actions. They should be planned and systematic reviews of specifications, design, and development, and should be planned to occur at suitable stages during the life cycle, based on risk, complexity, and novelty. Design reviews aim to identify and eliminate issues that would otherwise lead to changes at a later stage.

7.9 Software Production / Configuration

The supplier should establish and maintain a formal system for controlling software production. Appropriate methods and tools should be used and defined. Rules and conventions, such as acceptable languages, coding standards, version control, and naming conventions should be established. The use of code reviews should be considered.

Existing software should be used in accordance with documented build processes and taking into account any changes in the system hardware, interfaces, and peripherals.

If the supplier is involved in configuring a product, it should be performed in accordance with the controlling configuration specification and follow appropriate guidelines and recommendations.

中文解說：規格、設計審查與軟體開發

規格文件的靈活性

GAMP 5 明確指出，規格文件不一定要是傳統的 Word 文件格式。供應商可以使用：

  • Jira 中的 Epic/Story（Agile 方法）
  • 需求管理工具（如 DOORS、Polarion）
  • 設計圖 + 技術規格表

重點是：這些記錄必須可追溯、受變更控制管理，且能支持後續測試。

設計審查 (Design Review) 的 CDMO 實踐

對於 Cat 5 客製化系統，建議你作為受規管公司參與供應商的設計審查，而非僅接收最終報告。這相當於在施工途中進行品質檢查，比交房後發現問題便宜得多。

參與設計審查的記錄可作為你的 DQ（設計確認）證據，減少後續需要進行的正式 DQ 工作量。

程式碼審查 (Code Review) 的重要性

GAMP 5 建議（非強制）供應商實施程式碼審查。對 Cat 5 系統，你應在供應商評估時詢問程式碼審查流程：是人工審查、自動靜態分析工具 (SonarQube)，還是兩者兼用？這直接影響你對系統可靠性的信心，也影響你後期需要做多深入的測試。

7.10 Testing

For product development, the supplier should test the product in accordance with approved test plans and test specifications. The test specifications, when executed, should demonstrate that all requirements, functionality, and design have been met.

This may involve one or many stages of testing depending on the nature of the product:

  • Module (unit) testing
  • Integration testing
  • System testing

Test records for each stage should be reviewed and approved, and retained for a period defined in the QMS (not to be shorter than the supported lifetime of the current software version). Test failures should be managed in accordance with a formal documented process.

If the supplier is involved in configuring a product or developing a custom application, the number and level of test specifications can vary considerably and should be agreed with the regulated company.

7.11 Commercial Release

System release to customers should be performed in accordance with a formal process that describes the criteria for release, responsibilities, records to be retained, and items to be released, including software, hardware, and documentation. Clear policies and criteria should be established for the acceptability or otherwise of product release, based on the number and severity of known defects. Release notes defining fixes, changes, known problems, and new features should accompany each release, including minor releases and patches.

Note: Commercial release by a supplier is not a release into the GxP environment, which is a regulated company activity.

7.12 User Documentation and Training

The supplier should provide adequate system management documentation, operational documentation, and training for both maintenance and operation in accordance with agreed contracts.

7.13 System Support and Maintenance During Operation

The supplier should support and maintain the system in accordance with agreed contracts. Formal procedures should be followed, typically covering:

  • Operational change control
  • Configuration management
  • Patch management
  • Incident management
  • Documentation management
  • Backup and restore
  • Business continuity
  • Disaster Recovery (DR)
  • Managing software product releases
  • Training of supplier staff
  • System maintenance
  • Security management

7.14 System Replacement and Retirement

The supplier should manage the replacement or withdrawal of products or service in accordance with a documented process and plans. Sufficient notice of the retirement of a system or version should be given to regulated companies to allow them to plan for their required activities. The supplier also may support the regulated company with the retirement of computerized systems.

Release Notes 是重要的供應商文件

GAMP 5 要求每次發布（包含小版本更新和補丁）都必須附帶 Release Notes，列出修復項目、變更、已知問題和新功能。

對 CDMO 的實務意義：在處理供應商版本更新時，Release Notes 是你評估影響範圍、決定是否需要重新驗證的第一手資料，也是變更控制記錄的支持文件。

退役通知 — 不能被遺忘的條款

GAMP 5 要求供應商在系統退役前給予「充足通知 (sufficient notice)」。在技術協議中，建議明確約定：最短通知期（建議至少 12 個月），以及供應商是否協助資料遷移。遺漏此條款的後果：供應商突然宣布停止支援某版本，你的驗證系統瞬間成為 unsupported software，面臨重大稽查風險。

維護期 13 項覆蓋範圍

Section 7.13 列出的 12 個維護領域（變更控制、配置管理、補丁、事件管理等）是評估供應商服務能力的完整框架。在起草技術協議 (Technical Agreement) 時，這 12 項應逐一確認責任歸屬，並在 SLA 中具體化（如：事件響應時間、備份頻率、DR 演練週期）。

Chapter 8 Introduction

This Guide provides a flexible framework for achieving compliant computerized systems that are fit for their intended use. The benefits will be obtained only if the framework is applied effectively in the context of a particular organization.

In this Second Edition of the Guide many examples of efficiency improvements are also discussed in the context of critical thinking.

Aspects that can assist efficiency include:

  • Establishing verifiable and appropriate user requirements
  • Making risk-based decisions
  • Leveraging supplier input
  • Leveraging existing records and information
  • Using efficient testing practices
  • Employing a well-managed handover process
  • Managing changes efficiently
  • Anticipating data archiving and migration needs
  • Using tools and automation

CDMO 資源有限的現實

許多中小型 CDMO 的 CSV 團隊人手有限，無法對每個系統做同等深度的驗證。GAMP 5 Chapter 8 提供的 9 個策略，正是幫助有限資源做出最高效合規投入的工具。核心邏輯：把精力投在真正影響病人安全、產品品質和數據完整性的地方。

8.1 Establishing Verifiable and Objective User Requirements

Requirements should be analyzed to ensure that they are fully defined and are verifiable and objective. For example:

Incomplete Requirement: Room shall be controlled at 20°C.

Complete Requirement: Room shall be controlled at 20°C ± 2°C. Excursions of no greater than 7°C are permitted for <10 minutes.

The level of detail is dependent on the novelty and complexity of the processes and system being implemented. Table 8.1 lists aspects to consider when developing verifiable and objective requirements.

中文解說：可驗證的 URS

為什麼模糊的 URS 會浪費時間？

模糊的需求在 CSV 專案後期造成的損失遠超過前期釐清的時間成本：

  • 測試人員無法判斷測試是否通過（PASS/FAIL 標準不清楚）
  • 需要額外的審批循環來解釋需求含義
  • 稽查時監管機構可能質疑驗證的有效性
  • 變更控制時難以判斷是否為「同類替換 (like-for-like)」

Table 8.1 的七個面向：URS 審查會的角色分工

建議在 URS 審查會議中確保以下知識都有人代表：

  • 製程知識：製程工程師或批次生產人員
  • 業務知識：業務流程負責人（Process Owner）
  • 分析能力：品質工程師或系統驗證人員
  • 技術/產品：IT 或設備工程師
  • 製程/產品影響：品質保證人員（QA）
  • 技術寫作：文件管理人員或有寫作經驗的驗證工程師

與關鍵品質屬性 (CQA) 或關鍵製程參數 (CPP) 直接相關的需求，應在 URS 中明確標注（如加上「GxP Critical」標籤）。這些需求在後續風險評估和測試深度決策時有更高優先級，也是稽查重點。

8.2 Making Risk-Based Decisions

Risk management provides an opportunity to scale life cycle activities. Benefit may also be achieved if organizations use risk assessments as input to decisions to omit or include an activity. Examples of areas where risk assessments may help with scaling include:

  • Number and depth of design reviews required
  • Need for, and extent of, source-code review
  • Rigor of supplier assessment
  • Depth and rigor of testing

Similar opportunities exist during system operation:

  • Extent and level of specification and verification of changes
  • Rigor of the backup and restore process
  • Level of business continuity required
  • Frequency and level of DR
  • Degree to which identity checks are completed prior to providing access rights
  • Scope and frequency of periodic reviews

The benefits of risk-based decision-making can be maximized only if the conclusions and decisions can be leveraged. Therefore, there should be a practical, searchable means of access to conclusions and decisions available to those involved in decision-making — e.g., a risk register.

The risk-based approach should be focused and resourced for maximum effectiveness and efficiency. Organizations should not invest more effort and time into the risk-management process than is commensurate with the potential impact on patient safety, product quality, and data integrity.

中文解說：風險決策的務實應用

風險分層決定驗證深度

GAMP 5 的核心邏輯：不是所有系統和功能都需要相同深度的驗證。風險評估應能回答：

  • 此系統若失效，對病人安全、產品品質、數據完整性的影響為何？
  • 失效的可能性是否因供應商的良好實踐而降低？
  • 現有的其他控制措施（SOP、環境監控、人工覆核）是否降低了殘餘風險？

風險決策必須可查詢、可重用

GAMP 5 特別指出：風險評估的結論必須以可搜尋的方式存取。使用一個「風險登記冊 (Risk Register)」是最佳做法。每次做類似系統的驗證或變更評估，都能引用之前的風險結論，不必重頭開始。這是效率提升的關鍵工具。

警告：過度的風險評估也是浪費

GAMP 5 有一句關鍵警句：「組織不應在風險管理過程中投入超過其潛在影響所值的時間和精力。」換言之，為一台簡單的桌上型 pH meter 寫一份 20 頁的風險評估報告，本身就是一種資源浪費。根據比例原則，應用時間在高風險系統上。

8.3 Leveraging Supplier Input

Supplier activities, including testing, may be used by the regulated company as part of verification, provided the regulated company has assessed the supplier processes as suitable. This assessment may include a supplier audit, depending on the risk, complexity, and novelty of the system.

The regulated company should assess the supplier for evidence of:

  • An acceptable supplier quality system
  • Supplier technical capability
  • Supplier application of good practice such that activities performed by and information obtained from the supplier will be complete, accurate, and suitable to meet the purpose of verification

Supplier documentation, information, and records should be assessed for content and quality. Regulated company procedures and processes should be flexible regarding acceptable format and structures so that supplier documentation may be leveraged.

If inadequacies are found in the supplier quality system, technical capability, or documentation, then the regulated company may choose to manage potential risks by applying specific, targeted additional verification checks or other controls, rather than repeating supplier activities and replicating supplier documentation.

The decision and justification to use supplier activities to support verification should be based on the intended use of the system and should be documented and approved by SMEs, which may include the quality unit.

Suppliers also may have tools or techniques unique to the specification and testing of their product which may be leveraged by the regulated company during specification and testing.

中文解說：借用供應商文件的策略

CDMO 可節省最多時間的策略

對資源有限的 CDMO 而言，Section 8.3 是整個 Chapter 8 中最具實用價值的部分。正確應用可節省 30-60% 的驗證測試工作量。

借用供應商文件的三個前提

借用必須有書面依據，三個前提缺一不可：

  1. 供應商 QMS 評估通過：你必須對供應商的品質系統進行評估（書面審計或現場審計）並有書面結論
  2. 技術能力確認：供應商具備足夠的技術能力執行測試，測試方法是可靠的
  3. 文件格式可接受：你的 SOP 允許使用非標準格式的供應商文件（需要預先審視公司的驗證 SOP）

實務建議：如何讓供應商文件更容易借用

在採購合約或技術協議的談判階段，主動要求：

  • 供應商提供符合 GAMP 5 格式的測試報告（非其內部格式）
  • FAT 測試腳本可依你的 URS 客製化
  • 測試記錄保留期不短於你的法規要求
  • 允許你查閱（不必全部持有）供應商的詳細測試記錄

8.4 Leveraging Existing Information

In addition to the use of supplier activities and information, regulated companies should also leverage their own activities and information related to existing systems when introducing new, similar systems. Examples include:

  • Laboratory equipment
  • Secondary manufacturing equipment
  • Packaging equipment

Relevant information to reuse may include risk assessments, RS, various plans, test specifications, test results, and design reviews.

The new system should be assessed and any differences with the existing system identified and managed by the introduction of appropriate specification and verification as required. A review of existing information should determine which may be used as is or updated as required. These conclusions should be documented.

The new system should be subject to installation and verification based on user requirements. The validation report should explain the rationale for reuse of documentation.

中文解說：重用既有文件

「類似系統」的文件重用策略

當公司已有某廠牌系統的驗證包，採購同廠牌相同型號（或相近型號）的新設備時，可大幅重用既有文件。重用範圍：

  • 風險評估 (RA)：更新差異分析，補充新設備特有風險
  • 需求規格 (RS/URS)：複製並修訂為適合新設備的版本
  • 測試腳本：直接複製，執行前確認版本差異
  • 驗證計劃/報告模板：複製並更新系統描述與資訊

重用必須有差異分析文件

GAMP 5 要求：若重用既有文件，必須正式評估新舊系統的差異，並在驗證報告中說明重用的理由。「因為一樣所以直接用」是不夠的——你需要書面記錄「評估後確認差異為 X，對原測試腳本的影響為 Y，因此決定重用/更新 Z 項目」。

CDMO 的典型場景

填充線從四頭擴充到六頭，新增兩台相同型號的灌裝泵。既有四台泵的 IQ/OQ/PQ 文件可作為起點：

  • IQ：直接複製安裝確認腳本，執行新機台的現場安裝確認
  • OQ：借用功能測試腳本，確認新機台的參數範圍一致
  • PQ：需在新配置下重新執行，因為整條線的流量分配已改變

8.5 Using Efficient Testing Practices

Testing is a major, time-consuming exercise. It perhaps offers the greatest opportunity for efficiency savings.

8.5.1 Reuse of Test Results

Many systems have large amounts of test results available due to suppliers following a QMS independently of a regulated company. On a project, there may be pre-delivery testing, which may include factory acceptance testing (FAT). Wherever possible, regulated companies should clearly communicate to suppliers the testing and document requirements in advance such that supplier test documentation is of the required standard to support compliance activities.

Testing also may take place to meet other business or legal requirements, such as HSE, and finance such as SOX. Unnecessary duplication of testing should be avoided.

8.5.2 Extent of Required Testing

The level and type of testing should be risk-based and based on the nature of the component involved (standard, configurable, or custom). Types of testing include:

  • Normal case (positive)
  • Invalid case (negative)
  • Repeatability
  • Performance
  • Volume/load
  • Regression
  • Structural testing

While user requirements should be verified by the regulated company through installation and acceptance tests demonstrating fitness for intended use, other required tests should be identified based on risk, complexity, and novelty. The regulated company's procedures should allow for the use of such existing test evidence subject to a documented and justified review and approval by an SME.

8.5.3 Secondary Test Evidence

The generation and retention of secondary supporting hard copy or image evidence such as screenshots, in addition to the primary test result or output, is unnecessary in most cases and does not add value. It often adds significant cost without associated benefit. Such additional evidence should be generated and maintained only where value-added and necessary for effective testing.

Examples where additional test evidence may be useful:

  • Complex results, which may be difficult or time consuming to record manually
  • When the result is essentially visual or pictorial
  • Where there is a need for before and after comparisons

Test evidence may be retained electronically provided adequate security and retention mechanisms are established. Systems may also have data audit trails or other system logs that capture much of the information traditionally captured by screen prints.

8.5.4 Use of Test Witnesses

The use of witnesses during testing is not a requirement and involves a significant overhead without added benefit. A regulated company may choose to witness some tests (e.g., site or factory acceptance testing) for commercial, contractual, communication, education, or training reasons. It is disproportionate and unnecessary to require test witnessing or requiring the tester to initial every test step to affirm they followed the instructions.

Decisions to use witnesses should consider knowledge and experience of testers, practical issues (e.g., two-person processes), level and degree of review by an SME, and degree of automation of tests and resulting test evidence.

中文解說：高效測試實踐

測試是最大的效率節省機會

GAMP 5 原文指出「測試可能提供最大的效率節省機會」。以下是 CDMO 可立即應用的策略：

8.5.1：FAT 前就應告知供應商你的要求

常見錯誤：等供應商做完 FAT 才去審查文件，發現格式不符，浪費雙方時間。

正確做法：在採購前或 Kick-off Meeting 時，提供供應商你的「可接受測試文件標準」清單，包含：測試腳本格式、Pass/Fail 標準記錄方式、測試員和審查員簽名要求、電子記錄保存格式。

8.5.2：測試取樣策略

不必測試所有可能的組合，基於風險決定測試覆蓋範圍：

  • 高風險功能：正常案例 + 邊界值 + 異常案例 + 重複性 = 完整測試
  • 中風險功能：正常案例 + 一個異常案例即可
  • 低風險功能：正常案例 + 引用供應商測試結果

例如：LIMS 中的「計算分析結果」是高風險功能（影響放行決策），必須完整測試。「修改個人密碼」是低風險功能，測試一次即可。

8.5.3：截圖不是必須的！

這是許多公司長期浪費大量時間的地方。GAMP 5 明確指出：截圖在大多數情況下不必要且不增加價值。

截圖有用的情況（只有這三種）：

  1. 結果複雜，難以手動記錄
  2. 結果本質上是視覺的（如圖表、介面外觀）
  3. 需要前後對比

大部分的數值型測試結果（數字是否在範圍內、計算是否正確），直接記錄實際值就夠了。系統的稽查軌跡本身就是記錄，不需要再截圖一遍。

8.5.4：見證測試不是必要的！

要求測試員在每個步驟旁首字母確認是「不必要且不合比例的」——GAMP 5 的原文措辭非常直接。見證測試的唯一合理理由是：

  • 商業或合約原因（客戶要求）
  • 溝通、教育或培訓目的
  • 系統性質需要兩人操作（如操作員在現場、工程師在控制室）

對 CDMO 的實務影響：可以廢除「每個測試步驟都要品質人員在場見證」的做法，改為 SME 審查測試記錄。這能釋放大量 QA 人員時間。

8.6 Employing a Well-Managed Handover Process

System handover (from the project team to the process owner, system owner, and operational users) should be well-managed. It is a prerequisite for the effective maintenance of system compliance during operation. Handover should be planned in accordance with agreed criteria and should consider:

  • Support requirements for maintenance, as defined by IT or engineering
  • Outstanding problems or deficiencies
  • How long business processes can be stopped to enable handover
  • Ability and steps required to roll back to a previous operational state
  • Situations where updates are not optional (e.g., updates pushed to SaaS applications)
  • Information required at handover (specification and verification documentation, user and maintenance manuals or guides), or new or updated user procedures
  • Training needs (user, support, and maintenance)
  • Clear communication between groups, e.g., with application support and client service groups
  • The impact on the change-control process to apply during the handover period, when handover is to be phased
  • Responsibilities during handover, e.g., for accepting the system and for assessing the severity of outstanding problems or deficiencies
  • Potential need of a period of elevated support and maintenance, often referred to as hypercare
  • Preservation of implied as well as explicit knowledge, e.g., through SME lists and recording of lessons learned

中文解說：良好的系統移交

移交是合規維持的先決條件

許多 CDMO 在驗證完成後急於上線，缺乏正式的移交計劃，導致：

  • 操作人員未接受足夠培訓
  • IT 不知道如何支持系統（備份程序、存取管理）
  • 未解決的問題在生產中暴露
  • 隱性知識只在專案團隊中，移交後流失

Hypercare 期：上線後的加強支援

GAMP 5 特別提到「Hypercare」——這是指系統正式上線後的一段時間（通常 2-4 週），在此期間供應商和/或專案團隊提供加強支援。對 CDMO 的建議：在技術協議和專案計劃中明確定義 Hypercare 的時間長度、支援管道和響應時間。

SaaS 系統的強制更新問題

GAMP 5 明確列出「SaaS 應用的強制推送更新」是移交計劃需要考量的因素。對 CDMO 而言：若使用雲端 LIMS 或 ELN，供應商可能在未徵詢你的情況下推送功能更新。你的移交計劃和持續變更控制程序必須包含「如何處理非自願更新」的機制，否則系統可能在未經驗證的狀態下持續運作。

移交檢查清單（CDMO 版）

  • ☐ 移交前所有 Outstanding Issues 已分級（Critical/Major/Minor）
  • ☐ Rollback 程序已測試並文件化
  • ☐ 所有使用者培訓已完成並有記錄
  • ☐ IT 已接收系統架構文件、備份程序、存取管理 SOP
  • ☐ QA 已核准上線（包含接受 Open OOS/Deviation 的決定）
  • ☐ Hypercare 期間的支援管道和聯絡人已確認
  • ☐ 專案經驗教訓已記錄（Lessons Learned）

8.7 Managing Changes Efficiently

Efficient change management should be executed in conjunction with configuration management. Key elements include:

  • Documented description and business benefit of the change
  • Confirmation of availability of resource
  • Assessment of the impact of the change on the application, the underlying infrastructure, the people (users and engineering support staff), and the documentation
  • Leveraging the risk assessment information from the original project and assessing any new risks introduced by the change to define the strategy for maintaining compliance — including the need for any regression testing
  • Evaluation of the change from the financial, technical (IT or engineering), and compliance perspectives at the lowest technically competent level prior to management approval
  • Establishing and maintaining the distinction between changes at the pharmaceutical quality system level (impacting the medicinal product life cycle) versus changes at the IT delivery and service management level
  • Minimizing the number of approval points in the process
  • Documentation and communication of the decision
  • Execution and verification of the change, using traceability to identify existing applicable tests
  • Closing the change record in a timely manner

Weaknesses in change management systems that may lead to inefficiencies include:

  • Lack of scalability for minor changes or standard infrastructure components
  • Failure to execute change management steps in the appropriate sequence
  • Failure in scheduling or identifying dependencies
  • Abuse or misapplication of emergency change processes
  • An inability to prevent unnecessary changes
  • Failure to keep specifications current
  • Failure to leverage existing documentation for risk assessment, traceability matrices, or protocols
  • Lack of follow-up processes to close a change record
  • Independent change processes leading to duplication of effort
  • Inappropriate application of like-for-like principles in change management
  • Inadequate management of changes conducted by a supplier, leading to life cycle documents and configuration management records that are out-of-date
  • Lack of adequate follow-up after emergency changes

中文解說：高效變更管理

藥品品質系統層級 vs IT 服務管理層級

GAMP 5 特別強調要區分兩個層次的變更：

  • 藥品品質系統層級：影響藥品生命週期的變更（如 BPR 格式變更、放行標準修改）— 須走 GMP 變更控制，QA 核准，可能需要重新驗證
  • IT 服務管理層級：基礎設施的技術變更（如伺服器更新、防毒軟體升級）— 可走 IT 變更管理流程，風險低時無需完整 GMP 驗證流程

混淆這兩個層次是效率低下的主要原因。

「緊急變更」濫用是常見問題

GAMP 5 列出「濫用或誤用緊急變更流程」是效率低下的警示項目。緊急變更應有明確定義（如系統停機影響生產），且每次緊急變更後必須有後續的補充評估和文件補全。若緊急變更佔總變更比例過高（如超過 20%），應重新評估變更管理流程本身。

實務改善：減少不必要的審批層次

GAMP 5 明確建議「最小化審批節點」。可考慮的改善措施：

  • 低風險基礎設施變更（如補丁、防毒更新）設立快速審批路徑
  • 使用「同類替換 (like-for-like)」快速通道，但注意界定標準，並確保不被濫用
  • 建立預批准的標準變更清單 (Standard Change Catalogue)
  • 變更評估在「最低技術能力層級」完成，只有涉及合規決策時才升級至管理層

供應商更新管理：文件同步的挑戰

當供應商進行系統更新，你的驗證文件（FS、測試腳本、驗證報告）必須同步更新。GAMP 5 將「供應商更新導致生命週期文件過時」列為效率低下警示項目。對 SaaS 系統尤為重要——若供應商每季推送功能更新，你的文件管理和再驗證評估流程必須能夠承載這個頻率。

8.8 Anticipating Data Archiving and Migration Needs

Data archiving and migration requirements should be considered to ensure that data structures and formats are efficient.

8.8.1 Different Retention Periods

It may be difficult to archive data with different retention periods that share the same data structures. It may also be difficult to destroy retained data that is no longer required (to reduce risk exposure and retention costs) where data with different retention periods share the same data structures. A data structure that separates data by retention period can address the requirements of archiving and data destruction, but may involve a complex database design.

8.8.2 Data Formats

The migration of custom data formats to a replacement system requires special attention and may cause difficulties. The use of standard data formats should assist subsequent data extraction and migration.

8.8.3 Static and Dynamic Data

Data migration may be complicated where static and dynamic data is combined in a form that is difficult to separate.

8.8.4 Data Ownership

Lack of clear data ownership can lead to failure to appropriately dispose of data at the end of its retention period or make such processes and decisions unnecessarily difficult.

中文解說：資料封存與遷移規劃

在實施前就規劃資料出口策略

許多公司在系統上線 5-10 年後更換系統時，才發現資料遷移是噩夢。GAMP 5 建議在系統實施階段就預先考慮：

  • 不同類型數據的保留期是否一致？（批次記錄 vs 系統日誌 vs 使用者活動記錄）
  • 資料是否使用標準格式儲存（CSV、XML）？還是私有格式？
  • 靜態數據（配置、主資料）與動態數據（批次結果、分析數據）是否可分離？

CDMO 的實際風險場景

場景 1：批次記錄保留期 30 年，系統日誌保留期 2 年，但兩者在同一資料庫表中混合儲存。2 年後你必須刪除系統日誌，但技術上不可能只刪除部分記錄，結果要麼全保留（增加成本），要麼面臨誤刪批次記錄的風險。

場景 2：LIMS 廠商停業或被收購，你的分析數據以私有格式存儲在系統中，無法導出至新系統，被迫繼續支付昂貴的舊系統維護費。

資料所有權條款必須在合約中明確

技術協議應明確規定：你對儲存在供應商（特別是 SaaS 供應商）系統中的 GxP 數據的所有權；服務終止後的數據導出格式和時間框架；及數據保留期結束後的銷毀確認義務。這是資料完整性 (Data Integrity) 合規的基本要求。

Section Summary  |  章節總結

Chapter 7 establishes that while GxP compliance responsibility remains with the regulated company, supplier activities can be formally leveraged to reduce the regulated company's validation burden — provided the supplier is properly assessed. The 12-step good practices framework (Table 7.1) defines what a qualified supplier should deliver, from QMS establishment through retirement management.

第 7 章確立了：GxP 合規責任雖始終在受規管公司，但在完成正式評估後，供應商的活動可被正式借用以減少驗證負擔。12 步驟良好實踐框架定義了合格供應商應提供的完整服務範圍。

Chapter 8 provides 9 actionable efficiency strategies. The most impactful for resource-constrained CDMOs are: (1) leveraging supplier test evidence with documented justification; (2) eliminating unnecessary screenshots and test witnesses; (3) reusing existing validation documentation for similar systems; and (4) applying risk-based test depth rather than uniform comprehensive testing.

第 8 章提供 9 項可操作的效率策略。對資源有限的 CDMO 最具價值的四項是：借用供應商測試證據（有書面依據）、取消不必要的截圖和見證、重用既有類似系統的驗證文件、以及基於風險決定測試深度（而非統一測試所有項目）。

  • GAMP Category 3/4/5 determines the scope and depth of supplier involvement and regulated company verification obligations.
  • Sub-supplier management, especially for cloud-hosted GxP data, directly impacts regulated company compliance and must be transparent.
  • Risk registers should be searchable and reused across projects and change events — not created anew each time.
  • Commercial release (supplier activity) is entirely separate from GxP release to production (regulated company activity).
  • Data archiving and migration strategy should be designed at system implementation, not at retirement.
