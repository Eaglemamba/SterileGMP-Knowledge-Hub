Chapter 1: Introduction — 第一章：導論

1. Introduction

GAMP guidance aims to safeguard patient safety, product quality, and data integrity in the use of GxP computerized systems. It aims to achieve computerized systems that are fit for intended use and meet current regulatory requirements by building upon existing industry good practice in an efficient and effective manner.

GAMP provides practical guidance that:

  • Facilitates the interpretation of regulatory requirements
  • Establishes a common language and terminology
  • Promotes a system life cycle approach based on good practice
  • Clarifies roles and responsibilities

It is not a prescriptive method or a standard, but rather provides pragmatic guidance, approaches, and tools for the practitioner. When applied with expertise and good judgment, this Guide offers a robust, cost-effective approach.

The approach described in this document is designed to be compatible with a wide range of other models, methods, and schemes including:

  • Quality systems standards and certification schemes, such as the ISO 9000 Series
  • ISO 14971: Medical devices – Application of risk management to medical devices
  • Schemes for assessing and improving organization capability and maturity, such as Capability Maturity Model Integration® (CMMI)
  • Software process models such as ISO 12207
  • Iterative and incremental (Agile) software development methods and models
  • Approaches to IT service management, such as ITIL

Where possible, terminology is harmonized with standard international sources such as ICH and ISO. This Guide aims to be fully compatible with the approach described in the ASTM E2500 Standard Guide for Specification, Design, and Verification of Pharmaceutical and Biopharmaceutical Manufacturing Systems and Equipment.

GAMP is an ISPE Community of Practice.

導論解析

核心使命：三重保護

GAMP 5 的存在理由是一個三角形使命：

  • 病患安全（Patient Safety）：數據決策影響治療結果
  • 產品品質（Product Quality）：系統控制製程的每個步驟
  • 數據完整性（Data Integrity）：記錄的真實性與可追溯性

這三者互相依存，缺一不可。

比喻：交通法規還是路況指南？

GAMP 5 更像「駕駛最佳實踐手冊」，而非強制性的《道路交通法》。它告訴你如何開車更安全、更有效率，但各國交通法規（21 CFR Part 11、EU Annex 11）才是你必須遵守的法律。GAMP 5 幫你詮釋這些法規，但不能取代它們。

重要：GAMP 不是標準，不能聲稱「GAMP 認證」

原文明確指出：GAMP 是指引（guides），不是標準（standards）。因此，任何公司、供應商或產品都不適合聲稱自己是「GAMP 認證（GAMP certified）」或「GAMP 合規（GAMP compliant）」。

CDMO 合規聲明應依據 21 CFR Part 11、EU GMP Annex 11 等法規要求。

CDMO 應用場景

在無菌充填 CDMO 中，GAMP 5 所說的「電腦化系統」包括：

  • 充填機的 PLC/SCADA 控制系統
  • LIMS（實驗室資訊管理系統）
  • MES（製造執行系統）
  • ERP（企業資源規劃）
  • BMS（樓宇管理系統，含溫濕度監控）
  • 電子批次記錄（eBPR）

1.1 Rationale for GAMP 5 Second Edition

This Second Edition Guide aims to protect patient safety, product quality, and data integrity by facilitating and encouraging the achievement of computerized systems that are effective, reliable, and of high quality.

While the overall approach, framework, and key concepts remain unchanged, technical content of the Guide has been updated to reflect the increased importance of IT service providers including cloud service providers, evolving approaches to software development including incremental and iterative models and methods, and increased use of software tools and automation to achieve greater control, higher quality, and lower risks throughout the life cycle.

Associated with this is the reinforcement of the message that the GAMP specification and verification approach is not inherently linear but also fully supports iterative and incremental (Agile) methods.

Guidance on the application of new and developing technological areas such as Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AI/ML), blockchain, cloud computing, and Open-Source Software (OSS) has been included or updated.

The importance of critical thinking and the application of patient-centric, risk-based approaches (aimed at quality and safety) versus primarily compliance-driven approaches is further underlined. Concepts of computerized systems assurance as discussed as part of the US FDA Center for Devices and Radiological Health (CDRH) Case for Quality program are also explored and applied.

Links and references to GAMP guidance on the topic area of record and data integrity have been added.

The following ISPE initiatives and topic areas are supported:

  • Knowledge Management – focusing on how organizations create, manage, and use knowledge throughout the life cycle of a product.
  • APQ (Advancing Pharmaceutical Quality) – building industry-for-industry tools and programs to help companies assess and improve their quality operations.
  • Pharma 4.0™ – providing guidance to accelerate Pharma 4.0 transformations, enabling organizations to leverage the full potential of digitalization to provide faster innovations for the benefit of patients.

GAMP guidance adopts a patient-centric risk-based approach that enables innovation while demonstrating compliance with regulatory requirements.

1.3 Purpose

The purpose of this Guide is to provide a cost-effective framework of good practice to ensure that computerized systems are effective and of high quality, fit for intended use, and compliant with applicable regulations. The framework aims to safeguard patient safety, product quality, and data integrity while also delivering business benefit.

This Guide also provides suppliers to the life-science industry with guidance on the development and maintenance of systems by following good practice.

Patient safety is affected by the integrity of critical records, data, and decisions, as well as those aspects affecting physical attributes of the product. The phrase "patient safety, product quality, and data integrity" is used throughout this Guide to underline this point.

This Guide is intended for use by regulated companies, suppliers, and regulators. Suppliers include providers of software, hardware, equipment, system integration services, IT service providers, and IT support services, both internal and external to the regulated company.

The Guide has been designed for use by a wide range of disciplines and responsibilities, including:

  • Management
  • Quality Unit
  • Research
  • Development
  • Manufacture
  • Laboratory
  • Engineering
  • IT
  • Support Staff
  • All associated suppliers

GAMP documents are guides and not standards. It is the responsibility of regulated companies to establish policies and procedures to meet applicable regulatory requirements. Consequently, it is inappropriate for regulated companies, suppliers, or products to claim that they are GAMP certified, approved, or compliant.

1.3 目的解析

目的的三個層次

  • 合規層：幫助解讀法規要求（21 CFR Part 11、EU Annex 11、ICH Q9 等）
  • 品質層：確保系統 fit for intended use（符合預期用途）
  • 業務層：在合規的同時帶來商業利益（減少停機、降低維護成本）

再次強調：不能聲稱 GAMP 認證

原文："it is inappropriate for regulated companies, suppliers, or products to claim that they are GAMP certified, approved, or compliant."

這是很多 CDMO 供應鏈稽核時常見的誤解。供應商說「我們是 GAMP 合規」是不正確的說法，正確的說法應是「我們的系統驗證遵循 GAMP 5 指引，並符合 EU GMP Annex 11 要求。」

比喻：廚師的食譜書 vs 食品安全法規

GAMP 5 像米其林廚師的最佳實踐食譜書，告訴你怎麼做才能出好菜。FDA 21 CFR Part 11 和 EU Annex 11 是食品安全法規，不遵守就違法。食譜書幫你符合法規，但法規才是強制要求。

適用對象對 CDMO 的意義

GAMP 5 明確指出適用對象包含：受監管公司、供應商、監管機構，以及管理層、QA、研發、製造、實驗室、工程、IT 等所有職能。

這意味著在 CDMO 中，CSV 活動不是 IT 一個部門的責任，而是跨部門協作：製程負責人（Process Owner）定義需求，IT 負責技術實施，QA 負責驗證策略，供應商提供文件支持。

1.4 Scope

This Guide applies to computerized systems used in regulated activities covered by:

  • Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP) (pharmaceutical, including Active Pharmaceutical Ingredient (API), veterinary, and blood)
  • Good Clinical Practice (GCP)
  • Good Laboratory Practice (GLP)
  • Good Distribution Practice (GDP)
  • Good Pharmacovigilance Practices (GVP)
  • Medical Device Regulations (where applicable and appropriate, e.g., for systems used as part of production or the quality system, and for some examples of Software as a Medical Device (SaMD))

These are collectively known as GxP regulations.

This Guide provides an approach that is suitable for all types of computerized systems, focusing on those based on standard and configurable products, but equally applicable to custom (bespoke) applications.

The principles described can be applied to a wide range of computerized systems. Detailed application of these principles to specific system types (e.g., IT, infrastructure, process control systems, and analytical laboratory systems) is described in supporting ISPE GAMP Good Practice Guides.

Not all the activities defined in this Guide will apply to every system. The scalable approach, with application of critical thinking, enables regulated companies to select the appropriate system life cycle activities.

This Guide is also consistent with other regulatory demands such as Sarbanes-Oxley (SOX) and those associated with data privacy. The use of this Guide, however, does not guarantee compliance with, or replace, these regulatory demands.

It is recognized that there are acceptable methods other than those described in this Guide. This Guide is not intended to place any constraints on innovation and development of new concepts and technologies.

1.4.1 Supplier Aspects

The computerized system life cycle described in this Guide for a regulated company should not be confused with the need for a defined approach or method for software development, which is the responsibility of the supplier.

This Guide defines activities and responsibilities expected of the supplier in the provision of products and services. These activities perform an important role in supporting regulated company activities. The supplier may be a third party or an internal group of the regulated company. Such internal groups should follow processes consistent with the regulated company Quality Management System (QMS).

This Guide uses various diagrams to represent the system life cycle. These diagrams often present relationships in a linear representation. This is not intended to constrain the choice of development methods and models. Suppliers should use the most appropriate methods and models, which may include iterative and incremental (Agile), evolutionary, exploratory, and prototyping techniques, or the use of DevOps approaches.

Modern systems may have a complex supply chain involving multiple suppliers. This Guide aims to meet the needs of each group.

GAMP 5 明確要求受監管公司評估供應商能力。在 CDMO 中，這意味著：

  • 設備供應商（如充填機製造商）提供的 IQ/OQ 文件，可以作為 CSV 證據使用，但前提是通過了供應商評估（Supplier Assessment）
  • 雲端服務提供商（AWS、Azure）的安全認證可納入系統驗證策略
  • 內部 IT 部門開發的系統，同樣需要遵循 QMS 規定

1.5 Business Benefits

Effective, reliable, and high-quality computerized systems assist in achieving the primary objectives of patient safety, product quality, and data integrity, but there are major business benefits in having a defined process that delivers systems fit for intended use, on time, and within budget. Systems that are well defined and specified are easier to support and maintain, resulting in less downtime and lower maintenance costs. Adoption of the approaches described in this Guide will assist regulated companies in managing business risk as well as quality risks.

Specific benefits to both regulated companies and suppliers include:

  • Reduction of cost and time taken to achieve and maintain compliance
  • Early defect identification and resolution leading to reduced impact on cost and schedule
  • Cost-effective operation and maintenance
  • Effective change management and continual improvement
  • Enabling of innovation and adoption of new technology
  • Providing frameworks for user/supplier cooperation
  • Assisting suppliers to produce required documentation
  • Promotion of common system life cycle, language, and terminology
  • Providing practical guidelines and examples
  • Promoting pragmatic interpretation of regulations

1.5 商業利益解析

CSV 不只是合規成本，也是競爭優勢

GAMP 5 明確指出，良好的 CSV 帶來商業價值：

  • 減少停機時間：充填線每小時停機成本極高，好的系統維護策略直接影響 OEE
  • 降低維護成本：規格清楚的系統，變更管理更容易
  • 加速新技術採用：有框架的公司可以安全地引入 AI/ML、雲端等新技術
  • 共同語言：與客戶、供應商、監管機構建立共通術語，減少溝通成本

比喻：提早發現缺陷的價值

「Early defect identification」就像工程界的「右移測試成本曲線」：在需求階段發現一個問題修改的成本是 1，在驗證階段是 10，在上線後是 100。GAMP 5 的生命週期方法就是要把問題提前發現。

對 CDMO 來說：一個在 FAT（工廠驗收測試）階段發現的 SCADA 問題，遠比在 PQ 後才發現便宜得多。

對管理層的說服邏輯

當你需要向管理層說明為什麼要投資 CSV 時，這裡提供了完整的商業案例：

  • 減少合規成本 → 降低稽查風險罰款
  • 系統可靠性提升 → OEE 提升
  • 變更管理標準化 → 上市速度加快
  • 供應商協作框架 → 採購談判有據可依

1.6 Structure

1.6.1 Overview of GAMP Documentation Structure

This Guide forms part of a family of documents that together provide a powerful and comprehensive body of knowledge covering all aspects of computerized systems' good practice and compliance (Figure 1.2: GAMP Documentation Structure).

This Guide comprises a main body and a set of supporting appendices. The main body provides principles and a life cycle framework applicable to GxP regulated computerized systems. Practical guidance on a wide range of topics is provided in the supporting appendices.

Separate ISPE GAMP Good Practice Guides cover the application of these general principles and framework to specific types of systems and platforms.

1.6.2 GAMP 5 Main Body Structure

The main body introduction covers the purpose, scope, benefits, and structure of this Guide. Subsequent sections of the main body cover the topics:

  • Key concepts
  • Life cycle approach
  • Life cycle phases:
    • Concept
    • Project
    • Operation
    • Retirement
  • QRM
  • Regulated company activities:
    • Governance for achieving compliance
    • System-specific activities
  • Supplier activities
  • Efficiency improvements

The key concepts, described in Chapter 2, are the five concepts that underpin the rest of the document and should be applied using critical thinking.

QRM is a systematic approach for the identification, assessment, control, communication, and review of risks to patient safety, product quality, and data integrity. It is an iterative process applied throughout the system life cycle. Chapter 5 describes this approach.

Chapter 4 describes the project life cycle phase in more detail, including: Planning; Specification, configuration, and coding; Verification; Reporting and release. The key supporting processes of risk management, change and configuration management, design review, traceability, and document management are also introduced.

Ensuring compliance and fitness for purpose is the responsibility of the regulated company. Governance and system-specific regulated company activities are covered in Chapter 6. An overview of typical supplier activities is given in Chapter 7. Chapter 8 covers key topics leading to efficiency improvements.

2 Key Concepts

2.1 Overview

Five key concepts are applied throughout this Guide:

  1. Product and process understanding
  2. Life cycle approach within a QMS
  3. Scalable life cycle activities
  4. Science-based QRM
  5. Leveraging supplier involvement

The relationship between these concepts is shown in Figure 2.1: Key Concepts of this Guide. These five concepts underpin the entire framework and should be applied using critical thinking.

2.1 五大核心概念總覽

五大核心概念記憶框架

  • 1. 了解製程（Product & Process Understanding）：你的系統要服務什麼製程？
  • 2. 全生命週期（Life Cycle Approach）：從搖籃到墳墓，都在 QMS 框架內管理
  • 3. 可縮放活動（Scalable Activities）：高風險系統多做，低風險系統少做
  • 4. 科學化 QRM（Science-Based QRM）：用 ICH Q9 方法識別、評估、控制風險
  • 5. 善用供應商（Leveraging Supplier）：供應商的文件、測試、經驗都是你的資源

比喻：蓋房子的五個基本原則

1=了解地基和建築用途；2=從設計到拆除都有計畫；3=豪宅和倉庫規格不同；4=用科學計算結構風險；5=充分利用建築師和承包商的專業。五個概念環環相扣，缺任何一個都會出問題。

2.1.1 Product and Process Understanding

An understanding of the supported process is fundamental to determining system requirements. Product and process understanding is the basis for making science- and risk-based decisions to ensure that the system is fit for its intended use. An understanding of the intended use of data within the process is also fundamental. Data integrity cannot be achieved without a complete understanding of the data flow.

"Product and process knowledge should be managed from development through the commercial life of the product up to and including product discontinuation… Knowledge management is a systematic approach to acquiring, analysing, storing, and disseminating information related to products, manufacturing processes and components." — ICH Q10

Efforts to ensure fitness for intended use should focus on those aspects that are critical to patient safety, product quality, and data integrity. These critical aspects should be identified, specified, and verified.

Systems within the scope of this Guide support a wide range of processes, including but not limited to clinical trials, toxicological studies, API production, formulated product production, warehousing, distribution, and pharmacovigilance.

For some manufacturing systems, process requirements depend on a thorough understanding of product characteristics. For these systems, identification of Critical Quality Attributes (CQAs) and related Critical Process Parameters (CPPs) enable process control requirements to be defined.

Specification of requirements should be focused on critical aspects. The extent and detail of requirement specification should be commensurate with associated risk, complexity, and novelty of the system. Requirements may be developed iteratively or incrementally, based upon an initial set of base requirements.

Incomplete process understanding hinders effective and efficient compliance and achievement of business benefit.

概念一：符合預期用途（Fit for Intended Use）

「符合預期用途」是整個 GAMP 5 的核心邏輯

「Fit for intended use（符合預期用途）」是 GAMP 5 最重要的一個短語。它的意思是：系統要能完成它被設計來完成的任務，同時滿足相關的法規要求。

關鍵推論：你必須先清楚知道預期用途，才能驗證系統是否符合。這就是為什麼「產品與製程理解」排在五大概念第一位。

CQA → CPP → 系統需求的鏈結

QTPP（品質目標產品檔案）
    │
    ▼
CQA（關鍵品質屬性）：如無菌性、效價、純度
    │
    ▼
CPP（關鍵製程參數）：如滅菌溫度、充填重量、環境微粒
    │
    ▼
系統控制需求（System Requirements）：SCADA 需監控 CPP
    │
    ▼
驗證重點（Verification Focus）：只驗 CPP 相關功能

這條鏈結確保 CSV 工作聚焦於真正影響品質的地方。

比喻：廚房設備的「預期用途」

一台設計用來揉麵的攪拌機（預期用途），你驗證它能不能正確揉麵。你不需要驗證它能不能榨果汁——那不是它的預期用途。GAMP 5 告訴你：把驗證精力集中在預期用途中的關鍵功能上。

數據完整性從製程理解開始

原文重點："Data integrity cannot be achieved without a complete understanding of the data flow."

在 CDMO 中，這意味著：在設計 eBPR 或 LIMS 之前，你必須先畫出數據流圖（Data Flow Diagram）——數據從哪裡產生、如何傳遞、由誰核准、保存在哪裡、保存多久。沒有這張圖，數據完整性只是空話。

2.1.2 Life Cycle Approach within a QMS

Adopting a complete computerized system life cycle entails defining activities in a systematic way from system conception to retirement. This enables management control and a consistent approach across systems.

The life cycle should form an intrinsic part of the company's QMS, which should be maintained and kept up-to-date as new ways of working are developed.

As experience is gained in system use, the QMS should enable continual process and system improvements based on periodic review and evaluation, operational and performance data, and root-cause analysis of failures. Identified improvements and corrective actions should follow change management.

A suitable life cycle, properly applied, enables the assurance of quality and fitness for intended use, and achieving and maintaining compliance with regulatory requirements. A well-managed and understood life cycle facilitates adoption of a Quality by Design (QbD) approach.

The life cycle approach is fundamental to this Guide and embodies each of the other key concepts. The life cycle is structured into phases and activities, as described in Chapter 3.

概念二：生命週期方法

系統生命週期四個階段

  • 概念階段（Concept）：確定需求、進行初步風險評估
  • 專案階段（Project）：規格、開發/採購、驗證、發布
  • 運營階段（Operation）：日常使用、變更管理、定期審查
  • 退場階段（Retirement）：數據遷移、系統停用

每個階段都在 QMS 框架內運行，並與持續改善（CAPA）連結。

比喻：車輛全生命週期管理

就像一輛公司車：採購前評估需求（概念）→ 驗車交車（專案）→ 定期保養、事故記錄（運營）→ 報廢/轉售（退場）。每個階段都有記錄，都在公司流程（QMS）內。臨時買的車、不維護、報廢也不留記錄——這就是沒有生命週期方法的系統。

QbD（品質設計）與 CSV 的連結

原文提到「well-managed life cycle facilitates QbD approach」。QbD 的核心是在設計階段就建入品質，而不是靠測試找問題。應用在 CSV：在系統規格階段就定義好 CQA/CPP 相關的系統功能，而不是系統上線後才想要測什麼。

2.1.3 Scalable Life Cycle Activities

Life cycle activities should be scaled according to:

  • System impact on patient safety, product quality, and data integrity (risk assessment)
  • System complexity and novelty (architecture and nature of system components including maturity and level of configuration or customization)
  • Outcome of supplier assessment (supplier capability)

Business impact also may influence the scaling of life cycle activities.

The strategy should be clearly defined in a plan and follow established and approved policies and procedures.

概念三：可縮放生命週期活動

可縮放（Scalable）的三個維度

  • 系統影響程度：影響 CQA/CPP 的系統 → 高度驗證；只管非 GxP 流程 → 最低驗證
  • 系統複雜度與新穎性：現成商品軟體（COTS）→ 較少驗證；客製化系統 → 更多驗證
  • 供應商能力：通過評估的成熟供應商 → 可槓桿其文件；新供應商 → 需更多自行驗證

GAMP 系統分類（Category）

Category 1：基礎設施軟體（操作系統、虛擬化）
    → 最少驗證文件

Category 3：非配置商品軟體（Word, Excel 非用於 GxP 用途）
    → 最少驗證

Category 4：可配置商品軟體（LIMS, MES, ERP）
    → 中度驗證，重點在配置驗證

Category 5：客製化軟體（內部開發 eBPR）
    → 最高驗證，需完整開發文件

（GAMP 類別在附錄中詳細定義，但此概念貫穿全書）

比喻：滅火器和核電廠的安全驗證不同

你不會用驗證核電廠的標準去驗證辦公室滅火器。同樣，一個只管行政費用報銷的 Excel 表，和一個控制無菌充填壓力的 SCADA 系統，驗證程度應該完全不同。可縮放原則就是讓你把驗證資源用在刀口上。

CDMO 系統分級實例

系統類別驗證程度
充填機 SCADACat 4高（CPP 監控）
LIMSCat 4高（GLP/GMP）
MESCat 4高（批次記錄）
ERP（庫存）Cat 4中（間接影響）
BMS（溫濕度）Cat 4高（環境監控）
HR 系統Cat 1/3低（非 GxP）

2.1.4 Science-Based Quality Risk Management

QRM is a systematic process for the identification, assessment, control, communication, mitigation, and review of risks. Application of QRM enables effort to be focused on critical aspects of a computerized system in a controlled and justified manner.

QRM should be based on clear process understanding and potential impact on patient safety, product quality, and data integrity. Combining knowledge management and QRM will facilitate achievement of QMS objectives by providing the means for science- and risk-based decisions related to product quality. For systems controlling or monitoring CPPs, these should be traceable to CQAs, and ultimately back to the Quality Target Product Profile (QTPP) and relevant regulatory submissions.

Qualitative or quantitative techniques may be used to identify and manage risks. Controls are developed to reduce risks to an acceptable level. Implemented controls are monitored during operation to ensure ongoing effectiveness.

A practical risk-management process is described in Chapter 5.

概念四：科學化品質風險管理

QRM 與 ICH Q9 的關聯

GAMP 5 的 QRM 方法直接對應 ICH Q9（品質風險管理）。ICH Q9 是製藥 QRM 的聖經，GAMP 5 將其應用到電腦化系統領域。

QRM 的六個步驟：識別（Identify）→ 評估（Assess）→ 控制（Control）→ 溝通（Communicate）→ 緩解（Mitigate）→ 審查（Review）

科學化 QRM 的鏈結

QTPP（目標產品品質檔案）
    │
    ▼
CQA（關鍵品質屬性）
    │
    ▼
CPP（關鍵製程參數）← 系統需控制/監控
    │
    ▼
系統風險評估（Risk Assessment）
    │
    ├── 高風險功能 → 深度驗證 + 持續監控
    └── 低風險功能 → 簡化驗證

關鍵：風險評估必須可追溯（traceable）回 CQA 和 QTPP，符合法規要求。

科學化 vs 合規驅動的差異

合規驅動（舊思維）：「我要驗證這個功能，因為 SOP 說要。」
科學化 QRM（新思維）：「這個功能影響無菌充填的溫度控制（CPP），進而影響產品效價（CQA），所以我要深度驗證它。」

前者是盲目填表，後者是有科學依據的決策。第二版的批判性思維就是要推動這種轉變。

定性 vs 定量風險評估

GAMP 5 允許兩種方式：

  • 定性：高/中/低風險矩陣（FMEA 式）——最常用，適合大多數 CDMO 系統
  • 定量：概率 × 後果的數值計算——較少見，適合高度複雜系統

選擇方法本身也需要批判性思維：不要為了做定量評估而做，如果定性評估已足夠支持決策，就用定性。

2.1.5 Leveraging Supplier Involvement

Regulated companies should seek to maximize supplier involvement throughout the system life cycle in order to leverage knowledge, experience, and documentation, subject to satisfactory supplier assessment.

For example, the supplier may assist with requirements gathering, risk assessments, the creation of functional and other specifications, system configuration, testing, support, and maintenance.

Planning should determine how best to use supplier documentation, including existing test documentation, to avoid wasted effort and duplication. Justification for the use of supplier documentation should be provided by the satisfactory outcome of supplier assessments, which may include supplier audits.

Documentation should be assessed for suitability, accuracy, and completeness. There should be flexibility regarding acceptable format, structure, and documentation practices.

Supplier assessment is described in Chapter 6.

概念五：善用供應商參與

供應商文件可以作為 CSV 證據

這是 GAMP 5 最具實用價值的概念之一：如果供應商通過評估，其文件（IQ/OQ 協議、測試報告、FAT 記錄）可以直接用作你的驗證包一部分，避免重複工作。

前提：必須先完成供應商評估（Supplier Assessment），確認其能力和可信度。

比喻：信任但需驗證（Trust but Verify）

就像買車時，你信任原廠的品質測試報告，但你仍然要試駕和檢查重要功能。GAMP 5 的供應商槓桿就是：相信有能力的供應商的工作，但你仍需評估其適用性、準確性和完整性，並在自己的文件中引用和認可。

CDMO 供應商槓桿實例

  • 充填機製造商：利用其 FAT 測試記錄作為 IQ 基礎，不需全部重測
  • LIMS 供應商（如 LABVANTAGE）：使用其標準安裝驗證協議，只補充自己的配置驗證
  • 雲端服務商（AWS/Azure）：使用其 ISO 27001、SOC 2 報告支持基礎設施驗證
  • ERP 供應商（SAP）：利用其標準化流程文件，聚焦於自己的客製化驗證

每種情況都需先完成供應商評估並記錄，才能合理使用其文件。

文件評估三個標準

GAMP 5 明確指出，使用供應商文件前需評估：

  • 適用性（Suitability）：文件是否適用於你的系統配置？
  • 準確性（Accuracy）：內容是否正確反映系統實際狀態？
  • 完整性（Completeness）：是否覆蓋所有你需要驗證的功能？

2.2 Key Terms

Computerized System

A computerized system consists of the hardware and software components, together with the controlled function or process (including procedures, people, and equipment and associated documentation).

This term covers a broad range of systems, including, but not limited to:

  • Clinical trials data management
  • Manufacturing resource planning
  • Laboratory information management
  • Automated manufacturing equipment
  • Automated laboratory equipment
  • Process control and process analysis
  • Manufacturing execution
  • Building management
  • Warehousing and distribution
  • Blood processing management
  • Adverse event reporting (vigilance)
  • Document management
  • SaMD and digital health applications

Computerized System Validation (CSV)

Achieving and maintaining compliance with applicable GxP regulations and fitness for intended use by:

  • The adoption of principles, approaches, and life cycle activities within the framework of validation plans and reports
  • The application of appropriate operational controls throughout the life of the system

GxP Compliance

Meeting all applicable pharmaceutical and associated life-science regulatory requirements.

GxP Regulated Computerized System

Computerized systems that are subject to GxP regulations. The regulated company must ensure that such systems comply with the appropriate regulations.

GxP Regulation

The underlying international pharmaceutical requirements, such as those set forth in the US FD&C Act, FDA regulations, EU Directives, UK MHRA regulations, Japanese regulations, or other applicable national legislation or regulations. These include but are not limited to: GMP, GCP, GLP, GDP, Good Quality Practice (GQP), GVP, Medical Device Regulations, Prescription Drug Marketing Act (PDMA).

Process Owner

The owner of the business process or processes being managed. The process owner is ultimately responsible for ensuring that the computerized system and its operation is in compliance and fit for intended use in accordance with applicable company SOPs. The process owner may also be the system owner. The process owner may be the de facto owner of the data residing on the system (data owner) and therefore, ultimately responsible for the integrity of the data. Process owners are typically the head of the functional unit using the system.

Quality Management System (QMS)

Management system to direct and control an organization with regard to quality. (ISO). This is equivalent to Quality System as defined in ICH Q9.

Subject Matter Expert (SME)

Those individuals with specific expertise in a particular area or field. SMEs should take the lead role in the verification of computerized systems. SME responsibilities include planning and defining verification strategies, defining acceptance criteria, selection of appropriate test methods, execution of verification tests, and reviewing results. (ASTM E2500)

System Owner

The system owner is responsible for the availability and support and maintenance of a system, and for the security of the data residing on that system. The system owner is responsible for ensuring that the computerized system is supported and maintained in accordance with applicable company SOPs. For systems supporting regulated processes and maintaining regulated data and records, the ownership of the data resides with the GxP process owner, not the system owner. The system owner acts on behalf of the users. The system owner for larger systems will typically be from IT or engineering functions. Global IT systems may have a global system owner and a local system owner to manage local implementation.

2.2 關鍵術語解析

電腦化系統（Computerized System）的廣義定義

注意 GAMP 5 的定義包含「硬體 + 軟體 + 受控功能或製程（包括程序、人員、設備和相關文件）」。

這意味著：驗證的對象不只是軟體本身，還包括 SOP、操作人員的訓練、相關設備的整合。這是為什麼 CSV 是跨部門工作的根本原因。

比喻：系統 = 硬體 + 軟體 + 人 + 流程

就像「廚房系統」不只是烤箱（硬體）和定時器控制程式（軟體），還包括廚師（人）和食譜 SOP（流程）。一個環節出問題，整個系統就出問題。

CSV 的正式定義重點

CSV 是「達成並維持合規性與符合預期用途」，關鍵詞是「達成 AND 維持」——不是一次性驗證，而是整個生命週期的持續活動。這也是為什麼「定期審查（Periodic Review）」和「變更管理」是 CSV 的核心組成部分。

製程負責人 vs 系統負責人的責任區分

製程負責人（Process Owner）
├── 業務製程合規的最終責任
├── 確保系統符合預期用途
├── 通常是使用該系統的功能單位主管
└── 數據完整性的最終責任（數據擁有者）

系統負責人（System Owner）
├── 系統可用性、支援與維護
├── 數據安全（存取控制）
├── 通常來自 IT 或工程部門
└── 代表使用者管理系統

重要：受監管製程的數據所有權 → 製程負責人
      （不是系統負責人）

CDMO 中誰是誰

系統製程負責人系統負責人
充填機 SCADA生產部門主管工程部門
LIMSQC 主管IT 部門
MES/eBPR生產部門主管IT 部門
BMS廠務/工程主管工程部門
ERP多個（財務/採購）IT 部門

SME（主題專家）的關鍵角色

GAMP 5 明確說 SME 應在系統驗證中扮演主導角色——負責規劃驗證策略、定義接受標準、選擇測試方法、執行測試、審查結果。

對 CDMO 的意義：驗證工作不應只由 QA 或 IT 執行，必須由真正懂製程的 SME（如 QC 分析師、生產工程師）主導。QA 是框架提供者和監督者，而非所有 CSV 活動的唯一執行者。

GAMP 5 與主要法規的關聯

法規適用市場GAMP 5 的角色
21 CFR Part 11美國 FDA提供實施指引
EU GMP Annex 11歐盟 EMA提供實施指引
ICH Q9國際通用QRM 方法來源
ICH Q10國際通用QMS 框架對齊

GAMP 5 不是法規，但它的框架設計確保遵循 GAMP 5 的公司更容易滿足上述所有法規。

章節總結 Section Summary — Chapters 1–2

  • GAMP 5 是什麼：ISPE 的實務指引，不是標準，不是認證體系。目標是幫助製藥業確保 GxP 電腦化系統符合預期用途，同時保護病患安全、產品品質與數據完整性。
  • 第二版最大亮點：新增批判性思維（M12）、AI/ML（D11）、區塊鏈（D10）、Agile（D8）、IT 基礎設施（M11）指引；強調從合規驅動走向以病患為中心的科學化風險決策。
  • 五大核心概念：①了解製程→定義需求；②全生命週期在 QMS 內管理；③根據風險/複雜度/供應商能力縮放驗證；④用 ICH Q9 科學化 QRM 聚焦關鍵功能；⑤善用供應商知識與文件（需先評估）。
  • 可縮放原則的實踐意義：LIMS 和充填 SCADA 的 CSV 策略應顯著不同；把驗證精力集中在 CPP/CQA 相關的關鍵功能，而非平均分配。
  • 關鍵術語釐清：電腦化系統 = 硬體+軟體+製程+人員；CSV 是持續活動而非一次性驗證；製程負責人擁有數據，系統負責人管理系統；SME 主導驗證工作。
  • CDMO 核心應用：建立系統清單並分類（Category 1-5），為每個 GxP 系統指定製程負責人，利用供應商 IQ/OQ 文件（通過評估後），以及數據流分析作為數據完整性的起點。
