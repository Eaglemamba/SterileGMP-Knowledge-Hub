2.3

Developing Sampling Plans  |  取樣計畫的制定

Introduction — life cycle approach, documentation requirements

Original Text (English)

The frequencies and durations of sampling plans have historically coincided with the phases of the validation program for the water system. Using this rationale, the final phase of validation forms the basis for ongoing sampling frequencies with the goal of ensuring that the system is maintained in a validated state.

However, the use of risk analysis tools coupled with periodic data review may be used to alter the frequency of sampling when the data provides overwhelming support for a change (increase or decrease) in sampling frequency. Regular data review should be documented as part of the planning process.

A sampling plan should define and document:

  • Potential sampling points and the purpose for each
  • Sample locations intended for continuous, regular, routine, periodic, or as-needed monitoring
  • Attributes to be tested at each sampling location
  • The frequency at which samples are required for any given attribute
  • The purpose and use of the resulting data for those attributes
  • When sample valves and use point valves may be used as the same valve

Sampling plans are required for both new and renovated systems and may involve sampling locations throughout the water purification and distribution systems, including source water.

In specific circumstances, adequate scientific and statistical rationale may be required to not sample at an installed sampling point.

Sample locations installed for technical, investigative, or diagnostic purposes may not be included in a routine sampling plan. The purpose and use of these sample locations should be defined, and they should be used on an as-needed basis, be suitably maintained, and not pose a contamination risk to the system.

Note: Designating sampling locations "For Information Only (FIO)" or "for future use" should be avoided.

中文解析與CDMO應用

核心邏輯：取樣計畫的演進

ISPE的取樣計畫哲學是動態的，不是固定的。驗證末期形成例行監控的基準，但之後可以用風險分析與歷史數據來調整頻率。關鍵點是調整必須有科學與統計的依據。

查核重點：取樣計畫文件必備要素

法規查核時，稽查員會要求看到以下六項都在SOP或取樣計畫文件中明確定義：

  • 取樣點清單 + 各點目的
  • 監控頻率分類（連續/例行/定期/按需）
  • 各點測試屬性
  • 各屬性取樣頻率
  • 數據用途（放行？趨勢？調查？）
  • 取樣閥與使用點閥共用的條件

比喻：「FIO」標記為何有風險？

把一個取樣點標為「僅供資訊」或「未來使用」，等於告訴稽查員：這個點的存在沒有明確目的。這會引發「為什麼裝了卻不用？」或「這是不是迴避取樣的藉口？」的疑問。在CDMO環境中，每個安裝的取樣閥都應有明確的書面理由。

練習思考

  1. 你的工廠水系統取樣計畫是否明確區分「例行監控點」與「診斷用取樣點」？兩者的管理要求有何不同？
  2. 如果風險分析支持降低某個取樣點的頻率，需要準備哪些文件才能讓稽查員接受？
  3. 取樣計畫何時需要修訂？請列出至少三種觸發修訂的情況。
2.3.1

Background Philosophy  |  背景哲學

Life cycle approach to water system sampling — from design through PSE

Original Text (English)

Sample plan development should be based on the expected sampling required during the life cycle of the system.

A typical life cycle scenario for water sampling activities may include:

  • Conceptual and detailed design
  • Construction and installation
  • Commissioning
  • Performance Qualification (including worst case test and special scenario testing)
  • Ongoing testing during production
  • System changes and modifications (major and minor)
  • Periodic System Evaluation (PSE)

Sampling guidance is provided in Tables 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 regarding specific sampling locations, testing frequencies, and the type of monitoring or testing for various activities including commissioning and qualification/verification.

Automated electronic monitoring of conductivity and TOC can provide a comprehensive database from which chemical water quality trends may be observed over time. When analyzed, this information may allow for minimization or elimination of manual chemical testing of the water during routine operations.

However, during the Performance Qualification, confirmation that the on-line or in-line readings are representative of water quality used should be verified if this data is going to be used for product release purposes.

Water may be delivered at several different temperatures for use at a single POU. The water sample collected from this point should be collected at the worst case temperature for microbiological control. For example, if water is delivered at both 65°C and 20°C from the same POU, the sample should be collected at 20°C.

中文解析與CDMO應用

生命週期取樣思維

ISPE強調取樣計畫不是一次性文件，而是隨系統生命週期演進的動態框架。這與ICH Q10的產品生命週期管理哲學一致。

七個生命週期階段各有不同的取樣目的：設計階段確立基準、試車獲取工程知識、PQ確認合規性、例行監控維持驗證狀態、PSE決定是否調整。

在線儀器與手動取樣的關係

這是許多工廠容易誤解的地方：在線電導率和TOC可以取代手動化學測試，但有前提——必須在PQ期間確認在線讀值代表實際使用的水質。如果有局部污染風險（例如POU熱交換器），仍需要下游取樣或抓取樣品用於放行。

最壞情況溫度取樣：為什麼選20°C？

微生物在較低溫度下比在65°C時更活躍且更易存活。如果同一個使用點可輸送65°C熱水和20°C冷水，在熱水溫度取樣對微生物來說是「最佳條件」（對微生物不好），但冷水才是微生物最有機會繁殖的真實風險點。最壞情況 = 微生物污染風險最高的條件。

練習思考

  1. 你的工廠在PSE（定期系統評估）時，是否評估取樣頻率的合理性？依據什麼標準決定是否調整？
  2. 如果在線電導率數據用於產品放行，PQ報告中需要哪些驗證內容來支持這個決定？
  3. 一個系統剛完成大型改造，生命週期中哪個階段的取樣要求最接近全新系統？
2.3.2

Regulatory Requirements Related to Monitoring Frequency  |  監控頻率的法規要求

USP, EP, JP, ICH Q7, FDA — comparative regulatory landscape

Original Text (English)

No well-defined regulatory requirements exist regarding routine sampling frequency and duration. Various pharmacopoeias provide specifications for microbiological and chemical contaminants for the various grades of water, but do not define the frequency or duration of sampling.

General expectations from compendia and consensus standards:

USP <1231> — "Water systems should be monitored at a frequency that is sufficient to ensure that the system is in control, and continues to produce water of acceptable quality"; and "The sampling plan should take into consideration the desired attributes of the water being sampled."
EP GMP Vol. 4 Annex 1 — "Water sources, water treatment equipment and treated water should be monitored regularly for chemical and biological contamination and, as appropriate, for endotoxins."
JP16 General Information G8 (Section 4.2) — "Sampling frequency should be established based on the data from validation studies on the system."
ICH Q7 Section 4.20 — "All utilities that could impact on quality (e.g., steam, gases, compressed air) should be qualified and appropriately monitored and action should be taken when limits are exceeded."
1993 US FDA Guide to Inspection of High Purity Water Systems — "The results of the Commissioning & Qualification are designed to demonstrate that all of the process steps and components within a Purified Water or Water For Injection system are capable of consistently providing water meeting the necessary water quality requirements."

The FDA guide provides the most specific guidelines for sampling frequency during system validation, but does not stipulate frequency beyond validation and recognizes that more than one approach may be acceptable.

Summary: Routine sampling should always be adequate for the intended purpose. Frequency and duration decisions should be based on risk assessment with documented rationales.

中文解析與法規對比

法規真相：沒有人規定你要多久取一次樣

這是許多QA人員感到困惑的地方：沒有任何主要法規文件明確規定例行取樣的頻率。法規只說「要足夠」、「要定期」、「要基於驗證數據」。這給了工廠彈性，但也意味著你必須能自己解釋和捍衛你選擇的頻率。

法規/標準 對取樣頻率的要求 特點
USP <1231> 「足夠確保系統受控」+ 考量水質屬性 原則性，彈性最大
EP Annex 1 定期監控化學、微生物、內毒素 強調化學+微生物+內毒素三者並列
JP16 G8 基於驗證研究數據建立頻率 明確要求以驗證數據為依據
ICH Q7 需確認 + 適當監控 + 超標時採取行動 API製造適用；強調超標行動
FDA 1993 Guide 驗證期間最具體；驗證後未規定 歷史文件但仍被引用；三階段PQ概念源自此

FDA vs. EMA稽查重點差異

FDA稽查員傾向問：「你的取樣頻率的科學依據是什麼？」他們接受多種方法，但要求有書面依據。

EMA/EP稽查員更關注Annex 1的要求，特別是無菌產品用水的內毒素監控是否完整，以及是否有alert/action兩層級。

CDMO注意：服務不同客戶時，可能需要同時滿足FDA和EMA的期望。建議建立一個稍嚴格的統一標準。

練習思考

  1. 你的工廠水系統取樣SOP的頻率是如何決定的？是否有書面的風險評估支持？
  2. EP Annex 1提到「適當時」（as appropriate）監測內毒素——什麼情況下「適當」，什麼情況下「不需要」？
  3. ICH Q7適用於API製造。如果你的客戶是API製造商，他們的水系統要求和製劑廠有何不同？
2.3.3

Commissioning and Pre-validation  |  試車與驗證前取樣

GEP-based testing to establish baseline before formal PQ

Original Text (English)

Water testing of the pretreatment, generation and distribution systems for PW and WFI systems during commissioning is an exercise in Good Engineering Practice (GEP). This activity may contribute to developing operational and maintenance practices and procedures that will be confirmed at a later time during qualification and may provide baseline information for the system that may be useful in the future.

The testing activities may include chemical testing, or monitoring as appropriate, for specific unit operations and microbial testing to identify the indigenous microbial bioburden of the system. The duration of commissioning activities may vary depending upon the complexity and size of the system (e.g., 1 to 5 days).

Once the entire system is operating, the effluent of each unit operation should be monitored or tested at least once during the commissioning phase in order to provide baseline values for the system.

It may be necessary to perform extended pre-validation microbiological sampling activities in order to achieve process knowledge and assure system robustness.

中文解析與CDMO應用

試車取樣：GEP行為，非GMP要求

試車階段的取樣是工程實踐（GEP），不是正式的GMP法規要求。但這個區分很重要：試車數據雖然不能直接用作PQ數據，卻能幫助你理解系統行為、建立SOP，並識別潛在問題。

試車取樣的三個目的

  • 建立基準值：每個單元操作出口至少測一次，了解系統「正常」是什麼樣子
  • 識別固有菌群：系統的原生微生物種群，為後續監控提供參考
  • 開發操作規程：試車取樣的結果直接影響SOP的制定（清洗、消毒頻率等）

比喻：試車 = 新車磨合期

就像新車需要磨合期一樣，新的水系統在正式「上路」（PQ）前需要一段時間運行，讓工程師了解系統特性。這段時間的數據不算正式里程，但對判斷系統是否「準備好」接受正式評估非常關鍵。

練習思考

  1. 試車取樣結果發現某個RO單元出口的微生物計數偏高，這是否需要在PQ開始前解決？依據什麼？
  2. 試車持續1-5天——你如何決定對一個新建大型WFI系統應該試車幾天？
  3. 延長的驗證前微生物取樣（extended pre-validation microbiological sampling）通常在什麼情況下需要？

Source Water  |  來源水監控

Potable water requirements, responsibility boundaries, risk assessment

Original Text (English)

USP <1231> states: "To ensure adherence to certain minimal chemical and microbiological quality standards, water used in the production of drug substances or as source or feed water for the preparation of the various types of purified waters must meet the requirements of the National Primary Drinking Water Regulations (NPDWR) (40 CFR 141) issued by the U.S. EPA, or the drinking water regulations of the European Union or Japan, or the WHO drinking water guidelines."

Various nomenclature may be in use across the globe for drinking water including: potable water, tap water, city water, and feed water, however, the quality intent remains consistently the same.

WHO Technical Report Series No. 929, Annex 3 recommends daily sampling of incoming potable water during the early validation phase to verify its quality, but this may not be required in all areas of the world.

If there is little or no knowledge of the feed water quality at the system inlet, it is recommended to take daily samples for analysis. If the potable water quality is already well known and historical data shows good, stable water quality, a rationale can be made to perform less source water testing during the PQ period.

Typically, the potable/drinking water supplier is responsible for the potable water quality up to the property boundary; thereafter the responsibility is transferred to the property owner/site operator. Pharmaceutical product manufacturers are ultimately responsible for end product quality.

An impact assessment should be performed on potable water depending on the attributes, the treatment technology employed, and the end use of the water.

中文解析與CDMO應用

責任邊界：進廠邊界是關鍵分水嶺

供水公司只負責到廠界，廠界之後的水質責任歸工廠。這意味著：即使供水公司的水符合飲用水標準，工廠也需要自行確認進廠水質。這在有季節性水質波動（例如雨季、農業用水影響）的地區尤其重要。

來源水取樣頻率的決策樹

  • 新系統 / 不熟悉水源 → 初始PQ期間每日取樣（WHO建議）
  • 已有歷史數據且水質穩定 → 可以降低頻率，但需要書面依據
  • 高風險地區（季節變化大） → 維持較高頻率，特別是雨季

CDMO實務：多客戶、多地區的挑戰

CDMO可能為歐美日不同地區的客戶生產產品。來源水標準需要同時符合40 CFR 141（美國）、EU飲用水指令、WHO標準中最嚴格的那個。建議在來源水風險評估中明確列出所有適用標準的比較。

練習思考

  1. 你的工廠是否每年定期取得供水公司的水質報告？這是否足以免除自行取樣的責任？
  2. 如果工廠附近有農業活動，雨季時農藥殘留可能進入水源，你的來源水取樣計畫應如何回應？
  3. 中國藥典要求WFI蒸餾器的進水必須符合PW規格，但USP/EP/JP不要求。如果你的客戶包括在中國銷售的產品，這對你的前處理取樣計畫有什麼影響？
2.3.4.1

Pretreatment Sampling  |  前處理取樣

Unit-operation specific sampling — filters, softeners, carbon beds, RO, CEDI

Original Text (English)

Pretreatment represents designated treatment steps typically prior to primary/final treatment. When used as feed water for WFI or Pure Steam, pretreated water does not necessarily need to comply with PW requirements to ensure WFI/PS quality. Note: the Chinese Pharmacopoeia requires that supply water to a WFI still must comply with PW specification. USP, EP, and JP do not require PW quality as feed for production of WFI.

In order to ensure that the pretreatment fulfills its purpose, sampling is carried out specifically for each purification step:

  • Sand/mixed media filter: SDI measured before and after. Worst case: just before automatic back flush.
  • Water softener: Hardness measured before and after. Worst case: immediately before regeneration.
  • Carbon filter: Chlorine concentration before and after. Worst case: immediately before back flush.
  • RO system: Conductivity before and after the RO unit.
  • CEDI: Conductivity before and after the CEDI.

For all purification steps, microbiological testing may be required, including before and after the RO if applicable. Acceptable microbiological levels should be defined for each purification step. There should at least be an Action Level with consideration for adding an Alert Level if appropriate.

If, during risk assessment, the pretreatment is determined to be a no-impact system, samples should be taken for record purposes during PQ. If determined to be indirect impact, a rationale should be developed for the sampling frequency during PQ of the pretreatment.

中文解析與CDMO應用

每個單元操作都有自己的「最壞情況」

ISPE特別指出每種前處理設備的最壞情況取樣時間點，這是非常實用的設計指導：

設備測試屬性最壞情況時機
砂濾器SDI自動反沖洗前
軟化器硬度再生前
活性碳濾器殘氯反沖洗前
RO電導率、微生物正常操作範圍內
CEDI電導率正常操作範圍內

Alert Level vs. Action Level：必須有一個，最好有兩個

警戒水準（Alert Level）

早期預警信號。超過時需要調查和加強監控，但不一定停止使用水。通常設在法規限度的某個百分比（例如50-70%）。

行動水準（Action Level）

必須採取矯正措施。超過時通常需要隔離水批、調查根因、並可能啟動偏差報告。必須設定，且應低於藥典規格。

ISPE說「至少要有Action Level」——在CDMO環境中，建議兩個都設，因為Alert Level給你提前介入的機會，避免超出Action Level。

練習思考

  1. 你的工廠活性碳濾器的最壞情況取樣是否在反沖洗前執行？如果沒有，PQ報告如何記錄這個缺口？
  2. ChP要求WFI蒸餾器進水符合PW規格，但USP/EP不要求。如果同一個系統要同時服務中國市場和歐美市場的產品，你如何設計前處理取樣計畫？
  3. 前處理被評估為「無直接影響」（no-impact）——這是否意味著PQ期間可以完全不取樣？
2.3.4.2

Purified Water (PW) Sampling  |  純化水取樣

Rotational sampling strategies, alert/action levels for PW systems

Original Text (English)

Depending on the number of POUs and sample points in the distribution system, consideration could be given to rotational sampling for chemical tests since chemical contaminants are uniformly distributed in the water.

It is generally recommended that several samples be taken from each use point during the PQ period. It should be noted that worst case testing or special testing may influence the results and should be taken into account. The number of worst case tests chosen may influence the length of the PQ.

Define the microbiological levels as required by intended use of the water. There should be an Alert Level and an Action Level. Testing frequency should be determined based on a suitable risk assessment.

中文解析與CDMO應用

輪換取樣（Rotational Sampling）的邏輯

化學污染物（TOC、電導率）在水中均勻分布——如果系統出口達標，所有使用點也應達標。因此化學測試可以輪換取樣，不必每個使用點每次都測。

但微生物污染是局部的——某個使用點的生物膜不會影響其他點。所以微生物取樣不能過度依賴輪換策略，特別是高風險使用點。

練習思考

  1. 你的PW系統有20個使用點，你如何設計一個合理的輪換取樣計畫？哪些使用點不應該輪換（即需要每次都測）？
  2. PQ期間「從每個使用點取多個樣品」——你認為最少應該取幾個？依據是什麼？
  3. 如果PQ期間的最壞情況測試使某些數據升高，這些數據是否應該排除在設定Alert/Action Level的統計計算之外？
2.3.4.3

Water for Injection (WFI) Sampling  |  注射用水取樣

POU rotation, daily sampling expectations, PSE considerations, frequency reduction risks

Original Text (English)

POU sampling plans should rotate through all use points on the system, with the expectation that samples are collected on a daily basis from various use points, and that all use points are sampled on a routine basis based on risk analysis. System representative locations (e.g., supply and/or return) may be sampled on a daily basis for process control purposes and to aid in the investigation of OOS results from individual use points.

During the PSE of a WFI (or PW) system, sampling frequency may be evaluated. Increases or decreases in sampling frequency should be based on historical data and should only occur when data indicates that more or less frequent sampling is justified based on performance of the use point or system.

Sample frequency should not be arbitrarily adjusted without adequate scientific and statistical justification. A decrease in sampling frequency may result in increased regulatory scrutiny.

Decreased sampling frequency also puts more product at risk during investigations because of longer periods between acceptable test results surrounding a deviant test result. In short, careful consideration should be given before decreasing sampling frequency relative to the increased number of product batches that will be put at risk.

中文解析與CDMO應用

WFI取樣的高標準期望

ISPE對WFI的取樣期望明顯高於PW：

  • 各使用點每日輪換取樣（非每週）
  • 供水端和回水端每日取樣作為系統代表點
  • 所有使用點在例行監控中都需被涵蓋

這反映了WFI用途（注射劑）的高風險性。

降低取樣頻率的三重風險

1. 法規風險：可能引起稽查員的特別關注，被視為降低品質管控力道的信號。

2. 調查風險：若發現OOS，兩次合格結果之間的間隔越長，需要評估風險的產品批次就越多，可能導致更大規模的回溯調查和潛在召回。

3. 舉證責任：必須有充足的歷史數據和統計分析才能支持降頻決定。

比喻：取樣頻率降低 = 安全網縮小

想像你的WFI取樣是一張安全網。取樣頻率越高，網格越密，能捕捉到的問題越早、範圍越小。降低頻率等於把網格放大——偶爾的小問題可能漏網，一旦發現問題，網格之間的批次都可能有風險。在CDMO環境中，這些批次可能是不同客戶的產品，法律責任更加複雜。

練習思考

  1. 你的工廠WFI系統有10個使用點，但週末沒有生產。週一第一批取樣代表什麼最壞情況？
  2. PSE評估建議將WFI某使用點的微生物取樣從每週3次降到每週1次，需要準備哪些書面依據？
  3. 發現WFI系統一個使用點的微生物計數超過Action Level，需要回溯多少批次的產品？取樣頻率如何影響這個數字？
2.3.5

Sampling for Performance Qualification/Verification  |  性能確認取樣

PQ framework introduction — new vs. modified systems

Original Text (English)

The PQ/Verification sampling plan covers three scenarios:

  1. Typical (nominal) use conditions
  2. Worst case use conditions (system limits)
  3. Special scenarios

The goal is to document that the stated water quality is delivered from the water system under all of the above conditions and that the water complies with the requirements for the grade of pharmaceutical water type specified.

The period for the PQ depends on whether it applies to a completely new system or to modifications made to an existing system.

PQ Sampling Phases — FDA Three-Phase Approach:

Phase 1 — Initial Sampling: Most intensive, typically 2–4 weeks (10–20 consecutive working days). All sample and use points in the distribution system tested daily. Worst case: first day after weekend if no water consumption occurred.

Phase 2 — Intermediary Sampling: Less intensive, 10–20 working days. Reduced sampling frequency. Demonstrates repeatability and consistent quality using SOPs. May provide additional worst case data since use points are not as frequently active.

Phase 3 — Extended Sampling: Not less than the remaining balance of one year (minus Phase 1 and Phase 2 time). Captures seasonal variations in potable water feed, development of the system's natural flora, and any other annual variations. Typically reduced frequency. Forms the basis for the routine monitoring and testing program once validation is complete.

中文解析：PQ三階段完整框架

三階段PQ：FDA 1993指南的核心概念

FDA 1993年指南確立的三階段PQ方法至今仍是業界黃金標準。每個階段有不同的目的、強度和持續時間：

Phase 3的關鍵重要性：季節性驗證

為什麼要一整年？因為進水水質（微生物種類、溫度、化學成分）隨季節變化。夏天的水和冬天的水不一樣。只有完整一年的數據才能證明系統在所有季節條件下都能可靠運作。

這也是為什麼Phase 3的頻率會形成例行監控計畫的基礎——它代表了系統在現實條件下的真實表現。

  1. 一個新WFI系統的PQ從1月1日開始，Phase 1花了3週，Phase 2花了3週。Phase 3最晚什麼時候可以結束並簽核PQ完成？
  2. 如果Phase 1期間某個使用點持續出現微生物計數偏高，是否可以在問題解決後繼續Phase 1，還是需要重新開始計時？
  3. 「系統改造後的PQ」和「全新系統的PQ」在三階段要求上有何不同？
2.3.5.1

Purpose of PQ Sampling  |  PQ取樣的目的

Documenting water quality delivery under all operating conditions

Original Text (English)

The purpose of PQ/verification is to document that the stated water quality is delivered from the water system during:

  1. Typical (nominal) use conditions
  2. Worst case use conditions (system limits)
  3. Special scenarios

...and that the water complies with the requirements for the grade of pharmaceutical water type specified.

The period for the PQ depends on whether it applies to a completely new system or to modifications made to an existing system. When minor changes are made to a qualified system, reduced sampling requirements may be considered.

The PQ sampling protocol should describe the purpose, scope, and strategy (including justification). The sampling plan and the expected data from in-line/on-line measuring equipment should be described in the protocol. The prerequisites and the acceptance criteria for the performance testing should also be stated in the protocol.

All stakeholders involved in the PQ (production, engineering, quality control, and quality assurance) should have an opportunity to review and comment on the protocol. Communication and information sharing between the different participants before, during, and after the PQ are essential for efficient and successful qualification.

中文解析與CDMO應用

PQ ≠ 「確認系統沒問題」

PQ的目的是記錄（document）水質在各種條件下都符合規格，不只是「測試」。這個區分很重要：PQ是一個正式的書面確認過程，必須有預先批准的方案（Protocol）、明確的接受標準，以及完整的報告。

PQ方案必備要素清單

  • 目的和範圍（Purpose and Scope）
  • 取樣策略和依據（Strategy with Justification）
  • 在線/行程儀器的預期數據描述
  • 前提條件（Prerequisites）：IQ/OQ完成、SOP批准
  • 接受標準（Acceptance Criteria）
  • 最壞情況和特殊情境的說明
  • 生產活動的考量（例如週末無生產）

多部門協作的重要性

PQ不是QC或工程的獨角戲。生產部門知道什麼是真實的「正常使用」和「最大使用量」；工程部門了解系統設計限制；QC執行測試；QA確保合規性。四個部門各有不可替代的角色。

在CDMO中，還需要考慮客戶的特定要求是否需要被納入PQ設計。

練習思考

  1. 你的PQ方案中「接受標準」是否明確區分：化學屬性標準、微生物標準、內毒素標準？各自的標準來源是什麼？
  2. PQ執行期間發現缺失樣品（missing samples），ISPE說要立即溝通並在偏差中記錄——你的工廠有這個流程嗎？
  3. 「較小的系統變更可以考慮減少PQ取樣要求」——你如何在文件中定義「較小的變更」？
2.3.5.2

Typical Use Conditions (Nominal)  |  典型使用條件（正常）

Normal operational range definition with documented rationales

Original Text (English)

All PQs include a period of normal operation or simulated normal operation to demonstrate that the water quality during normal operation complies with the requirements for the actual specified pharmaceutical water quality.

Normal operational usage ranges are defined with rationales.

This forms the baseline against which worst case conditions and special scenarios are compared. The normal operating range encompasses the typical day-to-day variations in production demand, usage patterns, temperature fluctuations within design parameters, and normal maintenance cycles.

中文解析與CDMO應用

什麼是「正常操作範圍」？需要書面定義

「正常」不是假設，是需要明確定義並有依據的。典型的正常操作範圍定義包括：

  • 每日用水量範圍（最小到最大）
  • 系統循環溫度範圍
  • 使用點數量和同時使用情況
  • 每週操作天數（例如5天工作制 vs 7天）
  • 消毒頻率和時間窗口

「模擬正常操作」的應用場景

當新系統安裝完成但商業生產尚未開始時，可以進行模擬正常操作（例如用清水模擬用水需求）。但模擬必須真實反映預期的操作範圍，並在PQ方案中有明確依據。

練習思考

  1. 你的工廠WFI系統的正常操作範圍是否在URS（用戶需求規格）或PQ方案中有書面定義？包括哪些參數？
  2. 如果工廠計劃在PQ期間從5天生產制轉為7天生產制，這是否需要重新進行PQ？
  3. 「正常操作範圍」如何為後續的最壞情況定義提供依據？
2.3.5.3

Worst Case Use Conditions  |  最壞情況使用條件

System limit testing — stagnation, maximum flow, sanitization boundaries, maintenance events

Original Text (English)

A worst case scenario tests the limits of the system and is defined as the most extreme situations during the operation of the water system.

Examples of worst case conditions:

  • No water consumption (e.g., during a weekend or shutdown): Can result in a rise in the level of bioburden and conductivity. The first day of the week after no weekend consumption may represent a worst case scenario for sampling.
  • Maximum use of water: Confirms the system or unit operation is capable of delivering suitable water quality during a period of maximum delivery.
  • Just before sanitization: If routine sanitization occurs, sampling just before the start of sanitization captures the maximum time of accumulation of bioburden.
  • Just after sanitization: Confirms that sanitization has been effective and that chemical sanitant residuals are within acceptable limits.
  • After a maintenance procedure: After a periodic event such as derouging and/or passivation, confirming water quality restoration.
中文解析與CDMO應用

最壞情況的定義邏輯

最壞情況不是「隨機挑一個壞的情況」，而是系統性地識別所有可能對水質造成最大壓力的操作條件，然後在這些條件下取樣並確認水質仍符合規格。

CDMO通常有多種產品、多種客戶，生產排程變化大。以下是CDMO環境特有的最壞情況場景：

  • 長假期後（春節、聖誕）：系統可能靜置1-2週，是最嚴峻的滯留測試
  • 客戶審計週：生產暫停期間系統靜置
  • 設備共用客戶切換：大量沖洗後的水質確認

練習思考

  1. 你的工廠PQ報告是否包含所有五類最壞情況的取樣數據？如果遺漏了某類，事後如何補充？
  2. 「消毒後取樣」的接受標準與「正常操作取樣」的接受標準相同嗎？化學消毒劑殘留如何設定接受標準？
  3. 去鏽（derouging）操作後需要多久的清洗沖洗才能恢復水質？你的SOP是否規定了恢復取樣的要求？
2.3.5.4

Special Scenarios  |  特殊情境

Power failure, emergency maintenance, seasonal qualification — pre-validation to minimize future disruptions

Original Text (English)

Special scenarios are situations which may occur during normal use either as unplanned or as planned scenarios. Often these special scenarios occur at inconvenient times, and it would be advantageous to validate these scenarios to avoid delays when they occur.

Validation of special scenarios can be part of the ordinary PQ of the pharmaceutical water system, or it can be performed as a specific PQ for only this purpose.

The validation of a special scenario may need to be performed in a worst case scenario and be documented by water quality sampling data. The outcome and conclusion of the validation, based on the data, can decrease time spent on the procedure during daily operation, when going from the special scenario to normal condition, due to less sampling, sanitization, etc.

Examples of special scenarios:

  • Power failure: To document that the pharmaceutical water quality is unchanged after restarting after a defined period of time without any sanitization. A prerequisite for this test is no breach of integrity of the water system. If water retains quality for three hours after power failure, then up to a three-hour power failure can be followed by normal operation without additional sanitization (provided system integrity is not otherwise compromised).
  • Emergency or unplanned maintenance procedures: Including unusual or unexpected component failure, system damage from internal or external action. Validation of all emergency or unplanned maintenance procedures is not practical.

Acceptance criteria should be set up properly for the special scenario. A special scenario test may be performed for a longer period than the period used as the limit, to incorporate a safety factor (e.g., testing four hours to validate a three-hour limit).

中文解析與CDMO應用

特殊情境驗證的核心價值

特殊情境驗證的邏輯是：提前在受控條件下測試，比事後在緊急情況下處理要有效得多。預先驗證可以：

  • 確立明確的「可接受時間窗口」（例如：停電3小時內可不消毒）
  • 減少事件發生時的緊急取樣需求
  • 縮短重新投入使用的等待時間
  • 避免不必要的生產延誤
特殊情境 典型驗證內容 接受標準設定要點 安全係數建議
停電/電力故障 停電X小時後，不消毒直接重啟，確認水質 水質須符合藥典規格；系統完整性未受損 測試4小時以驗證3小時限度
夏季高溫期（季節驗證） 進水溫度最高時的水質和微生物表現 在最高進水溫度條件下仍符合規格 選擇歷史最高溫月份執行
冬季低溫期（季節驗證） 進水溫度最低時的設備效能和結冰風險 系統效能在低溫條件下維持 選擇歷史最低溫月份執行
計畫外維護（非緊急） 組件更換後的水質恢復 維護後的沖洗取樣標準 依組件影響範圍決定取樣點數

季節性驗證：夏季與冬季的不同挑戰

夏季挑戰：

  • 進水溫度升高 → 微生物生長速率加快
  • 冷卻水溫度限制 → 某些冷凍乾燥或熱交換效率下降
  • 消毒頻率可能需要增加

冬季挑戰：

  • 露天管路結凍風險
  • 進水電導率可能因除冰鹽影響而升高（北方地區）
  • 某些微生物在低溫下生長更慢，但特定嗜冷菌反而活躍

比喻：消防演習的邏輯

特殊情境驗證就像消防演習：你不會等真的火災發生才第一次測試撤離流程。預先演練的目的是確認標準作業程序（SOP）在緊急情況下確實有效，並且所有人知道自己的角色。停電3小時的特殊情境驗證，就是在告訴你的操作人員：「面對這種情況，按這個SOP做，不需要消毒，水質是安全的。」

練習思考

  1. 你的工廠是否有停電特殊情境驗證？驗證的時間窗口是多少小時？接受標準是什麼？
  2. 如果停電期間系統完整性受到損害（例如閥門洩漏），是否可以使用停電特殊情境驗證的結論？為什麼？
  3. ISPE說「對所有緊急或計畫外維護程序進行驗證是不切實際的」——那麼工廠如何在沒有預先驗證的情況下處理緊急維護後的水質恢復？需要什麼文件？

Risk-Based Approach to C&Q/Verification  |  確認驗證的風險基礎方法

CQA definition, impact classification, non-critical equipment testing strategy

Original Text (English)

A risk-based approach to C&Q/verification may be utilized to develop the monitoring and testing strategy. Risk analyses or impact assessments may be utilized to determine what equipment or functions of the water system are considered critical.

There may be unit operations or process steps within the generation or distribution system that require testing during initial commissioning as well as during ongoing routine operation (e.g., UV treatment, pumps, heat exchangers) that may not be considered critical.

If determined to be non-critical, such equipment operation (e.g., multimedia filters, softeners) may be tested only during commissioning as part of the C&Q/Verification process. Testing relating to the continued operation of a unit process may become part of a routine monitoring program based upon the impact to the system and the finished quality of the water.

Frequency of routine testing could be adjusted based on the consistency of equipment performance. These tests assure proper maintenance and operation of the water system (e.g., regeneration of resin beds, sanitization of equipment).

A Critical Quality Attribute (CQA) is a test defined as critical, based on the need to meet a specific compendia specification or other defined non-compendial requirements. For example, conductivity may be a CQA of water when measured at the outlet of a PW generation system.

A risk-based approach may also be utilized for determining the sampling approach to qualify changes made to an existing validated water system.

中文解析與CDMO應用

風險分類決定取樣策略

ISPE的風險分類架構：

  • 直接影響（Direct Impact）：直接影響水質的設備 → 需要PQ
  • 間接影響（Indirect Impact）：支援性設備 → 需要試車測試，PQ期間取樣頻率可基於RA
  • 無影響（No Impact）：對水質無影響 → 試車記錄即可，PQ期間僅需記錄用途

CQA的定義應用範例

在PW系統出口測量電導率：這是一個CQA（關鍵品質屬性），因為：

  • 有藥典規格（USP溫度表格）
  • 代表最終產品（PW）的品質
  • 可以在線監測
  • 超標直接影響產品放行

相比之下，砂濾器進出口的SDI不是CQA（是前處理效能指標），但是系統表現的重要指標。

CDMO系統變更後的PQ風險評估

CDMO的水系統可能因為不同客戶需求或新產品線而進行改造。風險評估方法用於決定改造後的PQ範圍：

  • 主要變更（例如新增分配環路）→ 接近完整PQ
  • 次要變更（例如更換一個閥門）→ 限定範圍的確認取樣
  • 相似設備更換（同型號同規格）→ 書面評估 + 有限驗證取樣

練習思考

  2. WFI系統的UV殺菌燈更換後需要做什麼確認取樣？依據是什麼？
