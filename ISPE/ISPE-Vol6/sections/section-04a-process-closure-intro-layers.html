4.1 Introduction — 導論：製程密閉的根本邏輯

原文內容 Original Text

The goal of this chapter is to provide a means to evaluate bioprocess unit operations and to discuss opportunities for closing these operations; therefore, decoupling them from the environment.

Isolating a process from its environment mitigates a major risk of contamination by adventitious agents, thereby yielding a process that is safer, more robust, more consistent, and where the risk of product adulteration is reduced significantly. Isolating a process from its environment also can mitigate risk when the release of the process fluid or aerosols can pose a risk to the operator, the environment or other open operations.

Technology improvements have resulted in more robust systems that can isolate bioprocess unit operations from the immediate environment. This is important, as control of the physicochemical conditions within a process micro-environment is more achievable than control of the macro-environment surrounding that process. As an example, it is easier to maintain an aseptic environment within a bioreactor than it is to maintain a low bioburden Grade 5 condition within a processing suite housing bioreactors and people.

Performing aseptic bioprocessing operations in an open system represents high risk. Where feasible, aseptic operations should be performed in closed aseptic systems. Closed processing represents the lowest risk option in terms of mitigating the risk of contamination from the environment for both aseptic and low bioburden operations. Operations that cannot be practically closed should be processed in a monitored clean classified environment that is consistent with the bioburden and particulate requirements of the process unit operation, whether aseptic or low bioburden.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

製程密閉 (Process Closure): 將製程的產品接觸面與外部環境完全隔離，使環境成為對製程品質無關緊要的參數（non-critical parameter）。這是 ISPE Vol.6 的核心主張，也是現代生物製藥設施設計的根本邏輯。

解耦（Decoupling）: 密閉製程的最大意義在於將製程與環境的風險「解耦」——即使周遭環境並非潔淨室，密閉系統的產品依然受到保護。

外來因子 (Adventitious Agent): 在製程中非預期引入的微生物污染物，包含細菌、病毒、黴菌等。生物製藥製程因其水性介質特性，極易成為這類污染物的繁殖溫床。

比喻說明 Analogy

密閉系統 = 密封保溫瓶： 想像你正在山上健行，隨身帶了一個密封保溫瓶裝了熱咖啡。無論外面氣溫多低、風多大、空氣多髒，保溫瓶裡的咖啡都不受影響。這就是「密閉製程」的核心概念——裝著寶貴生物藥液的系統，就像那個密封保溫瓶，與惡劣的外部環境完全隔絕。

相比之下，如果你用的是一個開口紙杯，那你就得在一個極度乾淨的空間（Grade 5 潔淨室）裡才能保護咖啡不受污染。開放式系統的代價，就是永遠需要昂貴的環境保護。

重點提示 Key Note

微環境比宏環境更可控： Vol.6 的一個關鍵洞見是——控制一個生物反應器內部的無菌環境，遠比控制整個製程廠房的潔淨度容易且經濟。這個觀念顛覆了傳統無菌製造「全廠高等級潔淨室」的思維，導向「只需保護接觸面」的精準設計。

4.2 Sources of Product and Process Contamination — 產品與製程的污染來源

原文內容 Original Text

Particulates in the environment can provide vehicles for adventitious agents, representing a biological risk to a biopharmaceutical process and product. A risk management program should be established to evaluate and address any potential impact of particulate contamination to process unit operations.

Bioprocessing is generally performed in an aqueous media that can support the growth of bacteria, viruses, and other adventitious agents; therefore, bioprocesses are prone to contamination by propagation of these agents. Facility designs and designs of process closures should account for potential sources of biological contamination.

Bioburden contamination (such as bacteria, yeasts, or mold contamination) can disrupt the physicochemical properties of a bioprocess solution, yielding chemicals and conditions that can be potentially harmful to the patient.

Bioprocesses should be monitored for bioburden and particulates, which should be controlled. Most bioprocesses typically include multiple clarification steps that would effectively remove small levels of particulate contamination (in the form of suspended solids), mitigating the risk of physical damage to equipment, disruption of a bioprocess unit operation, or adulteration of the drug substance.

Figure 4.1 depicts the sources of product and process contamination.

[Figure 4.1: Sources of Product and Process Contamination — 圖示四大污染來源：原料/耗材、環境因素（設備/空氣/人員/公用系統）、交叉污染、殘留攜帶]

A. The ingredients used in media, buffers, and other bioprocess solutions can represent a significant potential source of microbial contamination. Consumables such as chromatography resins, filter media, and tubing also represent potential sources of contamination.

B. Potential sources of contamination from the environment include:

  • Equipment
  • Airborne particulates and bioburden
  • Contamination on personnel
  • Contamination from utility supply services
  • Contaminating waste systems

C. Sources of crossover contamination include contamination from concurrent multi-product, multi-batch, and multi-unit operations (including concurrent CIP and SIP process operations).

D. Carryover is typically a result of inadequate system preparation, including cleaning and sanitization operations between manufacturing batches.

Centralized CIP operations can be a source of contamination from the environment, carryover contamination, crossover contamination, and contamination from cleaning solvents and chemicals.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

四大污染來源分類：

  • A — 原料與耗材 (Ingredients & Consumables): 培養基成分、緩衝液原料、層析樹脂、過濾膜、管路等——這些「進入製程的東西」本身可能帶菌。
  • B — 環境因素 (Environment): 空氣中的微粒、設備表面、操作人員脫落的皮屑與微生物、公用系統（純水、潔淨蒸氣）的污染。
  • C — 交叉污染 (Cross-Contamination): 相鄰製程、多產品或多批次同時操作時，一個系統的污染物進入另一個系統。CIP/SIP 管路若設計不當，可能成為交叉污染的載體。
  • D — 殘留攜帶 (Carryover): 前一批次或前一步驟的殘留物（包括清潔劑殘留）未被有效清除而帶入下一批。這是 CIP 驗證的核心課題。

比喻說明 Analogy

交叉污染 = 餐廳的砧板管理： 好的餐廳廚師用不同顏色的砧板分別處理生肉、蔬菜、魚類，就是在防止「交叉污染」。生物製藥廠的多產品環境中，若沒有嚴格的隔離設計（硬體隔離或製程密閉），就像所有食材都用同一張砧板——風險極高。

殘留攜帶 = 杯子沒洗乾淨： 你用同一個杯子先裝橙汁，沒洗乾淨再裝牛奶——殘留的酸會讓牛奶結塊。生物製藥製程中，前批次殘留的蛋白質或清潔劑，可能對下一批產品的純化或效價造成嚴重干擾。

重點提示 Key Note

水性介質的特殊脆弱性： 生物製藥製程幾乎全程在水溶液環境中進行（培養基、緩衝液、純化層析）。水性介質是細菌和酵母菌的天然繁殖溫床。一旦有少量污染引入，短時間內（37°C 培養條件下，大腸桿菌每 20 分鐘倍增一次）污染就會呈指數型擴散，整批廢棄損失極為慘重。

CIP 的雙重風險： CIP 本是清潔工具，但集中式 CIP 系統（Centralized CIP）本身也可能成為污染的媒介——同一套 CIP 管路若清洗多個設備，管路殘留可能造成交叉污染。這也是近年來 CIP 設計傾向「單用途」（dedicated CIP）的原因之一。

  2. 如果生物反應器內的培養基受到酵母菌污染，可能的污染入口有哪幾個？請根據 4.2.1 的清單推論。
4.2.1 Sources of Bioburden in Aseptic/Axenic Operations — 無菌/純粹培養操作中的生物負荷來源

原文內容 Original Text

Cell culture and fermentation operations are by necessity aseptic/axenic operations. Contamination of these processes can result in loss or rejection of batches. Prior to inoculation, the medium within a bioreactor should be free of measurable bioburden (effectively sterile). Single use bioreactors and systems for cell culture operations should be discarded and replaced. Stainless steel systems should be decontaminated, cleaned, and sterilized prior to subsequent use. The reason for contamination should be investigated and addressed to minimize risk of another contamination.

Potential sources of contamination in cell culture operations include:

  • Breach of integrity of the Grade 5 environment in the BSC used in open operations
  • Failure in the bioreactor sterilization
  • Failure in media sterilization
  • Failure in bioreactor cleaning, resulting in soil carryover
  • Compromise/breach of sterile air filter integrity
  • Contamination of the cell bank or inoculums
  • Compromise/breach of the vent filter integrity and system pressurization failure
  • Compromise of the sampling system integrity
  • Compromise of the addition port(s) integrity
  • Contamination of the clean steam system
  • Contamination of process water system
  • Presence of prions or adventitious agents in animal derived ingredients
  • Failure of mixer system or mixer mechanical seals
  • Failure of aseptic transfer system sterilization, breach of transfer system integrity
  • Failure/breach of vessel integrity
  • Contamination of feed solution
  • Compromise/breach of perfusion system integrity
  • Leachables/extractables from polymeric components, glass, etc.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

無菌操作 (Aseptic Operation) vs 純粹培養 (Axenic Operation):

  • Aseptic（無菌）: 指整個操作環境與系統排除所有微生物——適用於細胞培養的接種前準備、培養基製備等。
  • Axenic（純粹培養）: 特指培養物中只含有特定的目標生物（如 CHO 細胞），不含任何其他微生物。細胞培養批次必須在全程 axenic 條件下維持。

接種前滅菌（Pre-inoculation Sterility）: 生物反應器在接種前，其中的培養基必須達到實質上的無菌狀態（effectively sterile）。這通常透過 SIP（就地蒸氣滅菌）達成。一旦 SIP 失效，後續接種的細胞將立即暴露於污染風險。

重點提示 Key Note

密閉系統 18 個潛在污染入口的警示： 這份清單看似嚇人，但其實是一份「系統密閉設計查核表」。每一個列出的入口都代表一個需要被保護的介面（interface）。優秀的系統設計師在評估生物反應器密閉性時，必須逐一審視每個入口是否具備足夠的保護措施。

動物源成分的特殊風險： 動物源原料（血清、水解物）可能含有病毒或 prion（普里昂蛋白，如 BSE 相關）。雖然現今無血清培養基已大幅降低此風險，但歷史上動物源成分的使用至今仍是 FDA 與 EMA 審查的重點。

比喻說明 Analogy

18 個污染入口 = 城堡的 18 道門： 想像一座中世紀城堡（生物反應器），它的價值在於城堡內部的安全。但如果城堡有 18 道門（取樣口、通氣口、加料口、攪拌器密封等），每一道門都可能成為敵人（污染）的入口。城堡的安全不取決於最強的那道門，而取決於最弱的那道門。這就是密閉性驗證必須全面覆蓋每個介面的原因。

實務應用 Practical Application

作為一個 CDMO 的製程工程師，當你接收到一台全新的 1,000L 不鏽鋼生物反應器並進行首次 SIP 驗證時，你需要：

  1. 識別所有探針插入口（pH、DO、溫度）是否完整安裝並經過灼燒滅菌
  2. 確認通氣過濾器（vent filter）完整性測試通過
  3. 驗證取樣系統的蒸汽消毒步驟有效
  4. 確認所有加料口均在 SIP 程序覆蓋範圍內

遺漏任何一項，都可能導致接種後數天内因污染而廢批，損失數十萬至百萬美元的原料物料成本。

4.2.2 Mitigation of Risks from Sources of Potential Product and Process Contamination — 污染風險緩解策略

原文內容 Original Text

The risk of contamination from raw materials can be mitigated and monitored with established quality control strategies. Ingredient specifications including bioburden, impurities, properties, and recommended storage conditions, should be understood. Dry solids should be stored in environments with controlled humidity and temperature. Solutes used in media and buffer formulation are usually not sterile; the solutions using these ingredients should be:

  • Sterilized
  • Stored under bacteriostatic conditions
  • Used within specified hold periods (with limited shelf life — before microbial contaminants proliferate)

Media are typically at higher risk than buffer solutions because of their growth promoting properties. The composition of media may affect their susceptibility to contamination: defined chemical media may be considered lower risk. The use of sera can affect the susceptibility to contamination. Consumables also represent potential sources of contamination.

Contamination from the environment and crossover contamination from neighboring systems can occur for open processes when these systems are in close proximity. Protecting an open system from potential contamination by operators requires effective sanitary gowning and/or other layers of system protection, including a clean environment. Enhanced gowning may help reduce bioburden levels in a suite.

Closing a system is considered an effective approach to mitigating the risk of crossover contamination or contamination from the environment. Closing a system also effectively mitigates the risk of contamination by personnel. Contamination from the environment of a closed system would require a breach of that system, typically as a result of multiple failures of mechanical systems (e.g., valve and/or filter failures) representing the layers of protection to that system. Crossover contamination of two adjacent closed systems would require the breach of integrity of both systems.

Carryover is typically a result of inadequate system preparation including cleaning and sanitization operations between manufacturing batches. Validation of the cleaning and sanitization program should adequately mitigate the risk of carryover. An effective cleaning and sanitization program should include adequate monitoring to ensure critical parameters are maintained, including:

  • Temperature
  • Time
  • Action (impingement and/or turbulent flow; pressure)
  • Chemical concentration and components
  • Sequence of cleaning steps

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Hold Period（保存期限）的科學基礎： 培養基配製後並非立即使用，必須設定「最長保存時限」（hold period）。這個時限不是任意訂定的，而是基於微生物生長動力學研究——在特定溫度和 pH 條件下，從「零污染」到「污染超出可接受範圍」需要多少時間。Hold period 到期後，培養基必須廢棄或重新滅菌。

培養基 vs 緩衝液的風險差異： 培養基（Media）含有碳源、氮源、維生素等豐富營養成分，是微生物的「五星級餐廳」，污染風險顯著高於緩衝液（Buffer）。化學限定培養基（defined chemical media）因組成明確、不含不確定的動物源成分，比複雜培養基（complex media）風險更低。

重點提示 Key Note

密閉系統的「多重失效」保護邏輯： Vol.6 明確指出，一個真正密閉的系統，要使環境污染進入，必須同時發生「多重機械失效」（multiple failures）——例如同時失效的閥門和過濾器。這種「多重失效才能污染」的設計，正是密閉系統比開放系統更安全的根本原因，也是為何密閉系統可以放置在較低等級環境中的監管依據。

CIP 的 TACT 原則： 有效清洗需要同時滿足四個要素——Temperature（溫度）、Action（機械衝擊力/湍流）、Chemical（清潔劑濃度）、Time（接觸時間）。任何一個要素不足，整體清洗效果就會大打折扣。這也是 CIP 驗證的核心評估架構。

公式與框架 Framework

CIP 有效性 = f(溫度 × 作用力 × 化學濃度 × 時間 × 步驟順序)

關鍵概念：
- 提高溫度 → 可降低化學濃度需求
- 增加機械衝擊力 → 可縮短清洗時間
- 步驟順序錯誤（如鹼洗後未充分沖洗就進行酸洗）
  → 中和反應產物可能堵塞管路或損傷設備

CIP 驗證三要素：
1. 視覺清潔（visually clean）
2. 微生物指標（bioburden limits）
3. 殘留限度（residue limits, e.g., TOC, conductivity）

  2. CDMO 承接兩個相鄰批次的不同客戶產品時，CIP 驗證需要涵蓋哪些額外的考量？
4.3 Providing Appropriate and Effective Layers of Protection to a Bioprocess Unit Operation — 保護層次：Vol.6 的知識核心

原文內容 Original Text

Different levels of bioburden and contamination control are needed for different unit operations. Different approaches and layers of protection are required to mitigate the risk of contamination from the environment for these different operations. The impact (or severity) of bioburden or particulate contamination will differ in different situations. The mitigation of risk from contamination present in the environment requires the implementation of sufficient layers of appropriate protection to a process system. The purpose of this section is to discuss possible options for closing an operation by providing appropriate layers of protection to that operation.

The use of highly classified cleanrooms for open operations can reduce the level of potential environmental contaminants. For aseptic operations, classified cleanrooms provide an environment with lower bioburden. For other bioprocesses, classified cleanrooms can lower the particulate burden and bioburden within the environment.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts — 洋蔥式保護層次模型

這是整本 Vol.6 最重要的概念。ISPE 的核心主張是：

每一層有效的保護 = 可降低一個等級的潔淨室要求

就像洋蔥一樣，越多層皮（保護層），核心（產品）越安全，外層環境的重要性也就越低。

比喻說明 Analogy

洋蔥式防護（每一層洋蔥皮 = 一層保護）：

想像產品是洋蔥的核心。每剝一層洋蔥皮（保護層），外界的「污染」都要先突破那一層才能到達核心。當洋蔥有足夠多的層（密閉設備 + 密閉傳輸管路 + 受控房間），即使最外層的環境（建築空間）並非嚴格潔淨室，產品依然安全。

洋蔥式保護層次模型（由內至外）

核心：Product (藥液)
Layer 1 — Primary Containment (初級圍堵)
密閉設備本體：焊接接頭、SIP 接口、密閉生物反應器
Layer 2 — Secondary Containment (二級圍堵)
密閉房間 / RABS / 隔離器
Layer 3 — Building Segregation (建築隔離)
不同製程區域的物理分隔，獨立 HVAC
Layer 4+ — Environmental Controls (環境管控)
潔淨室等級、更衣程序、環境監控

保護層次越多 → 外部環境等級要求越低 → 設施建設成本越低

4.3.1 Providing Appropriate Environments within Biosafety Cabinets — 生物安全櫃（BSC）的環境提供

原文內容 Original Text

Open aseptic operations require environments of extremely low bioburden, where the probability of detecting bioburden within those environments during the open operation is essentially zero/probability less than 1. Grade 5 environments, such as that found within properly managed Biosafety Cabinets (BSCs), are typically considered acceptable for open aseptic operations, as they meet this criterion. Even within BSCs, proper cleaning, operations, technique, preparation, gowning, staging, etc., and surrounding environment are required to maintain the Grade 5 condition.

Independent of the strategy used, the overall BSC management plan should include:

  • A well designed cleanroom housing the BSC. This design will consider:
    • The BSC
    • The HVAC (location of HEPA and air return) that mitigates risk of "rogue" unfiltered air currents in environment immediately surrounding the open face of the BSC
    • Adequate air change per hour rate
    • Location and design of airlock accesses
  • Restriction of the number of personnel occupying the room
  • Restriction of the amount of equipment located in room
  • Appropriate gowning of personnel
  • Proper aseptic technique in the BSC
  • Effective cleaning and sanitization of the BSC

For further information, see the ISPE Good Practice Guide: HVAC [58].

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

生物安全櫃 (Biosafety Cabinet, BSC): BSC 是細胞培養實驗室和種子準備的核心設備。它利用垂直層流（vertical laminar airflow）和 HEPA 過濾，在操作台面創造一個 Grade 5（ISO 5，等同 EU GMP A 級）的微環境。

「Rogue」空氣流的危險： 如果 HVAC 設計不當，房間內存在未受控的渦流或亂流，可能破壞 BSC 操作面附近的層流，將外部環境的微粒/微生物帶入 BSC 的開口面——這就是所謂「流氓空氣流（rogue air currents）」的危害。

重點提示 Key Note

BSC ≠ 無限等級 5 保護： 即使在 BSC 內操作，Grade 5 環境也並非自動達成——需要搭配正確的操作技術（aseptic technique）、適當更衣、定期清潔消毒，以及良好的周邊環境設計（HVAC、氣密門道）。BSC 只是提供了潛力，人員操作行為決定了實際效果。

人員數量的重要性： 每增加一個人在 BSC 所在房間內，就增加額外的微粒和微生物負荷。這就是為什麼 BSC 操作房間通常嚴格限制人員進入數量，並且在使用中盡量排除非必要人員。

  2. 在 CDMO 中，種子培養（seed train）通常在 BSC 中進行。若要從 BSC 升級到封閉式搖瓶培養系統，需要考慮哪些設計改變？
4.3.2 Strategies for Closing Bioprocess Operations — 製程密閉策略

原文內容 Original Text

The goal for closing systems is to isolate the system product contact surfaces from the surrounding environment. Available technology allows systems to be closed or isolated from the environment and by default, from each other. Effective isolation can be achieved without the need for walls or segregation of the HVAC controlling the environment housing these operations. This strategy has previously been supported by regulatory agencies (e.g., process and utility piping have traditionally and typically been housed in uncontrolled interstitial spaces).

"Transfers between unit operations are considered critical to a successful bioprocess. Figures 4.2 and 4.3 show mixed piping systems that are routinely routed through uncontrolled interstitial (or 'gray') space because these critical systems can be shown to be isolated from the environment and from each other. The environment in these uncontrolled spaces represents a non-critical aspect to the transfer operation, because the critical transfer piping is closed."

The process transfer piping systems, routed through the uncontrolled interstitial spaces, represent extensions of the process equipment to which they are attached.

[Figure 4.2: Closed Piping through Non-classified Mechanical Spaces]
[Figure 4.3: Closed Piping through Non-classified Mechanical Spaces (3D Diagram)]

This strategy has been extended to closed buffer hold vessels that may be housed in Controlled Not Classified (CNC) or uncontrolled spaces. Although housed in an uncontrolled environment, buffer hold vessels are rendered closed prior to use by effective cleaning and sanitization.

Organizations should demonstrate that their process equipment is appropriately closed (and validated as such) or the agencies will continue to expect it to be housed in classified cleanrooms.

Advances in bioprocessing include technologies that provide effective isolation of process from the environment. For closed operations, the environment becomes a property or parameter with no critical aspect according to Quality Risk Management (QRM). For a process or system to be considered closed, the product contact surfaces and process environment needs to be completely isolated from the external environment during all manipulations, including setup, transfers, sampling and other process operations.

Open systems can be rendered closed with appropriate preparation, including cleaning and sanitization operations. Risk assessments would show that isolation of the product and process from the environment represents lower risk to product quality by eliminating a significant source of hazards. The elimination of the environment as a parameter having a critical aspect can have significant impact on the process design, on the process equipment layout, and on the overall facility design.

[Figure 4.4: Open versus Closed (Ease of Contamination Figure)]

This is independent of the nature of the process or classification of the environment. As an example, bioreactors and fermenters that have been properly steamed-in-place and are appropriately closed and controlled have been shown to be capable of maintaining an axenic culture for months.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Controlled Not Classified (CNC) 空間： 這是一種介於「完全潔淨室」和「未管控空間」之間的環境——有基本的環境監控和維護要求，但不需要符合特定的 ISO 潔淨室等級。密閉緩衝液儲存槽可以放置在 CNC 空間，就是因為其密閉性讓環境分類變得不關鍵。

間隙空間（Interstitial Space）的監管接受： 生物製藥廠的管路系統穿越非分類的機械夾層（灰色空間）已是長期被監管機構接受的做法。理由是：這些管路是密閉的，環境對管路內的產品沒有影響。這個「灰色空間合理性」的原則，正是 Vol.6 的密閉策略之一。

比喻說明 Analogy

密閉管路穿越灰色空間 = 自來水管穿越牆壁： 家裡的自來水管穿過牆壁、天花板，你不會要求整面牆達到食品衛生標準，因為水是在密閉管道內流動的。生物製藥廠的製程管路穿越非潔淨的機械夾層，道理完全相同——關鍵的是管道本身的密閉性，而不是周遭牆壁的潔淨程度。

設備的「延伸」概念： 每段連接在生物反應器上的密閉管路，法規上視為生物反應器本身的延伸部分——享有與生物反應器相同的密閉保護。只要管路本身密閉，外部環境分類就無關緊要。

重點提示 Key Note

「環境成為非關鍵參數」的革命性意義： Vol.6 最重要的一句話是：對於密閉操作，「環境成為品質風險管理中無關鍵性的參數」。這句話的監管意涵是：

  • 密閉設備不需要放在潔淨室 → 建設成本大幅降低
  • 環境監控需求降低 → 運營成本降低
  • 設施設計靈活性提高 → 可在更小的潔淨室中容納更多設備

但前提是： 必須有充分的文件和驗證來「證明」密閉性——否則監管機構仍會要求潔淨室環境。

實務應用 Practical Application

CDMO 設施設計的商業意義： 如果一個 CDMO 計劃建造一個 5,000L 不鏽鋼生物反應器廠房，選擇是：

  • 方案 A（傳統思維）: 把整個生物反應器廠房設計為 Grade 8（C 級）潔淨室。建設成本極高，HVAC 系統複雜，維運成本高。
  • 方案 B（Vol.6 策略）: 生物反應器系統經過完整的密閉驗證（SIP、壓力保持測試、過濾器完整性測試），放置在 CNC 空間。只有真正需要開放操作的區域（如取樣站、種子準備室）建設 Grade 8 潔淨室。

方案 B 可節省數百萬至千萬美元的設施建設成本，這正是 Vol.6 的核心商業價值所在。

4.4 Demonstrating that Processes are Closed — 密閉性的驗證與證明

原文內容 Original Text

4.4.1 Performing a Closure Analysis

This section provides an example of how risk assessments can be used to demonstrate that a process has been successfully isolated from the surrounding environment and that the environment no longer represents a critical aspect of this process.

Changes to a process or processing strategy should be systematically evaluated, e.g., by using a risk management tool and pre-established criteria. The tool should mitigate bias influencing the risk ranking of a hazard. The primary goal of a closure analysis is to confirm that a process unit operation can and/or should be removed from a cleanroom environment of higher classification to a controlled non classified suite. Closure analysis is a type of risk assessment that should focus on the probability and severity of exposing a process system to the surrounding environment before, during, and after the process operation. The scope of the risk assessment should be restricted to potential impact by the environment and should omit other process improvement initiatives.

Closure analysis can provide an opportunity to:

  • Evaluate and improve manufacturing processes
  • Reduce risk
  • Evaluate new and improved technologies
  • Reduce failures
  • Increase productivity

These improvements can lower the cost of manufacturing, improve consistency, increase reliability and provide a more predictable level of product quality.

For a process unit operation, all penetrations and operations that can influence the integrity of the system should be considered. Figure 4.5 shows penetrations and operations that should be considered for a stainless steel bioreactor used in a cell culture operation.

[Figure 4.5: Stainless Steel Bioreactor Used in a Cell Culture Operation — 圖示所有可能影響密閉性的穿透點與操作點]

The steps listed in Figure 4.5 represent opportunities for breach of the closed integrity of the bioreactor system. The closure analysis should consider these steps and determine whether the methodology used in the step or subsequent steps adequately protects the integrity of the closed system. Stainless steel bioreactor systems require significant manual intervention during setup. The insertion of instrumentation into lower probe belts and assembly of vent and sterile gas filters can result in particulate and bioburden contamination of the vessel and system. The risk may be adequately mitigated by performing SIP on the system prior to use.

"This risk cannot be mitigated by housing an aseptic system in a Grade 8 or Grade 7 environment. Only Grade 5 should be considered acceptable for an open aseptic system."

A hot WFI rinsing and sanitizing rinse after manual insertions, but prior to SIP, may be needed to mitigate the risk of particulate contamination within the vessel chamber. Bioburden present in the system after human intervention is usually removed using thermal sanitization and particulates should be rinsed away with condensate in a self-draining system. Following SIP, the system should be maintained under constant positive pressure by supplying sterile filtered gas. Dissolved Oxygen (DO) and pH monitoring prior to inoculation can be used to confirm success of the SIP process in rendering the bioreactor closed.

This evaluation should be repeated for all unit operations in a process.

[Figure 4.7: Closure Analysis Risk Assessment Workflow – Phase 1 (Process understanding, PFD/P&ID assembly, system boundary definition)]
[Figure 4.8: Closure Analysis Risk Assessment Workflow – Phase 2 (Criteria listing, closure risk ranking vs. pre-established criteria)]
[Figure 4.9: Closure Analysis Risk Assessment Workflow – Phase 3 (Mitigation options for unacceptable risk)]

The workflow should be used only for process contact operations. In the program depicted:

  • Open aseptic operations would represent the operations or steps of highest risk
  • Performing controlled bioburden operations using sterile gamma irradiated single use systems would represent the lowest risk of contamination from the environment; however, other higher value options are available that also represent acceptable low risk

Phase 3 of the closure analysis evaluates options for mitigation of unacceptable risk due to insufficient product protection or process closure.

[Figure 4.10: Fault Tree Flow Diagram — 用於最終評估密閉完整性的故障樹]

At the end of this workflow, only those elements, steps, or operations representing a moderate or high risk of contamination from the environment should be further evaluated in the final assessment. Should contamination from the environment be a possibility, the fault tree approach shown in Figure 4.6 could be used for final assessment of adequate closure.

The final assessment addresses the needs of the process in a qualitative manner. The purpose is to determine whether it is more appropriate to close a process by upgrading the process system or to maintain status quo by leaving the operation open and housing it in an appropriately classified environment. A closure analysis risk assessment should show that most aseptic processes used in the manufacture of drug substances are closed. Exceptions may include inoculum preparation processes and seed cell expansion when shaker flasks are used. When closing the process is not possible, the operation should be housed in an appropriate environment.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

密閉分析（Closure Analysis）的本質： 密閉分析是一種聚焦於「環境與製程系統互動」的專項風險評估。它的獨特性在於：

  • 範疇刻意限縮為「環境影響」——不涉及其他製程改善
  • 目標是「移除環境的關鍵性」——降低潔淨室等級要求
  • 結果是「是否可以從 Grade X 降至 CNC」的決策依據

密閉分析三階段工作流程（Phase 1–3）：

  • Phase 1 — 製程理解: 收集 PFD（製程流程圖）和 P&ID（管道儀錶圖），定義系統邊界，識別所有穿透點。
  • Phase 2 — 風險排序: 建立密閉評估標準，對每個穿透點/操作進行風險排序，與預設可接受標準比較。
  • Phase 3 — 風險緩解: 針對「不可接受」的風險項目，評估升級選項（如改用密閉傳輸系統、增加 SIP 步驟等）。

比喻說明 Analogy

SIP 驗證 = 用蒸汽消毒管路接頭——無須拆解，現場驗證密閉性：

想像你家的天然氣管道接頭——你不會每次使用前都把接頭拆下來送去滅菌。你會在原位（in-place）用特殊方法（如壓力測試或洩漏偵測）確認接頭密封完好。SIP（就地蒸氣滅菌）的邏輯類似：生物反應器的整套管路系統，包含各個閥門、過濾器、取樣口，不需要拆解，直接在原位通入高壓蒸汽（121°C 以上，至少 15 分鐘），達到滅菌效果，同時也驗證了系統能承受蒸汽壓而不洩漏——這等於同時完成了密閉性確認。

重點提示 Key Note

SIP 後正壓維持的邏輯： SIP 完成後，系統立即切換為「無菌過濾氣體正壓維持」。這一步驟的目的是：即使系統有微小洩漏，也是氣體從內向外洩漏（正壓外洩），而不是外部環境的微粒從外向內進入。這是密閉系統的最後一道防線，也是密閉性持續保持的機制。

DO 和 pH 監測作為密閉確認的指標： 接種前的 DO（溶氧）和 pH 穩定性可以間接確認 SIP 的成功——如果培養基被微生物污染，DO 和 pH 的異常變化會在幾小時內出現。這是一個巧妙的「密閉性功能測試」，利用培養基的化學特性作為密閉成功的生物指示劑。

例外情況：搖瓶和種子準備： Vol.6 坦承，有些操作天生無法完全密閉——搖瓶（shaker flask）用於種子培養擴增時，本質上是半開放系統，仍需要 Grade 5（BSC）環境的保護。這是密閉策略的例外，不是規則。

密閉分析風險矩陣框架 Risk Matrix Framework

密閉分析風險評估邏輯：

每個穿透點 / 操作步驟 → 評估兩個維度：

1. 暴露機率 (Probability of Exposure)
   - 操作頻率
   - 操作持續時間
   - 人員介入程度

2. 暴露嚴重性 (Severity of Exposure)
   - 污染對製程的影響
   - 是否有後續去除步驟？
   - 對患者安全的影響

風險排序：
   高機率 × 高嚴重性 → 必須升級（密閉化）
   低機率 × 低嚴重性 → 可接受（維持現狀）
   中等風險 → Phase 3 評估緩解選項

最終決策：
   密閉升級 OR 保留開放 + 潔淨室環境

實務應用 Practical Application

CDMO 密閉分析的實際步驟（以 2,000L SS 生物反應器為例）：

  1. Phase 1: 從工程部取得 P&ID，標記所有穿透點（探針口 × 5、取樣口 × 3、加料口 × 4、通氣口 × 2、傳輸接口 × 6）共 20 個潛在暴露點。
  2. Phase 2: 對每個穿透點評估：是否有 SIP 覆蓋？是否有緊急切斷閥？是否有完整性測試記錄？
  3. Phase 3: 發現取樣口在取樣操作時（開放式取樣）為高風險 → 升級為密閉取樣系統（如 SureSampler® 或蒸汽阻障取樣閥）。
  4. 文件輸出: 產出密閉分析報告，支持將生物反應器移至 CNC 空間的監管申請（如 CMC 模組更新）。

本節重點回顧 Section Summary — Chapter 4 Part A (4.1–4.4)

  • 製程密閉（Process Closure）是 ISPE Vol.6 的核心策略：將產品接觸面與環境完全解耦，使環境分類成為非關鍵參數，從而降低設施建設和維運成本。
  • 四大污染來源為：原料/耗材、環境因素（設備/空氣/人員/公用系統）、交叉污染（多產品/多批次/CIP 管路）、殘留攜帶（清洗不完全）——每一類均有對應的風險緩解策略。
  • 洋蔥式保護層次模型是本章的知識核心：每增加一層有效保護（初級圍堵 → 二級圍堵 → 建築隔離 → 環境管控），就可以降低一個等級的潔淨室要求，最終實現在 CNC 或非分類空間中安全操作密閉製程。
  • 密閉分析（Closure Analysis）是一種三階段（製程理解 → 風險排序 → 緩解選項評估）的專項風險評估工具，其最終產出是「是否可將設備移至低等級環境」的科學決策依據——必須有完整文件和驗證支撐，監管機構才會接受。
  • SIP + 正壓維持 + DO/pH 監測是確認密閉生物反應器密閉狀態的標準方法組合；密閉性驗證不能僅靠環境等級提升替代。
  • 例外情況：搖瓶種子培養等天生難以密閉的操作，仍需在 Grade 5（BSC）環境中進行——「密閉優先」是策略，但不是可以忽視科學現實的口號。
