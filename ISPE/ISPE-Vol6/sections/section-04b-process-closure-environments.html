4.5 Appropriate Environments Based on Risk — Overview

原文內容 Original Text

Where equipment technology limits the ability to close operations, those operations will need to remain open. Personnel represent one of the highest risks as sources of bioburden and particulates. Open aseptic operations involving human intervention or manipulation, represent high risk conditions.

Closed processing can help mitigate the risk of contamination from the environment and from the "human risk factor."

When open aseptic operations are used, an appropriately controlled and maintained environment with sufficient layers of protection (e.g., Grade A/5) should be used to house the operation.

"Closed processing can help mitigate the risk of contamination from the environment and from the 'human risk factor.'"

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：製程密閉性的最終目的 Core Concept

Section 4.5 是第四章的「收割」章節——在前面幾節建立了保護層（Layers of Protection）框架之後，這裡正式回答一個關鍵問題：不同密閉程度的製程，需要什麼等級的環境？

最核心的邏輯是：製程越密閉 → 環境要求越低 → 設施成本越低。這不是取巧，而是有科學依據的風險管理決策。

人員（Personnel）被明確點名為最高風險污染源。這也解釋了為何 Grade A 環境的隔離器（Isolator）設計要把操作員完全排除在產品接觸區域之外。

比喻說明 Analogy

依照食物類型決定廚房等級：

你不會用同一個廚房標準來處理真空包裝罐頭與生魚片。罐頭食品（完全密封）可以在普通環境加工；生魚片（完全開放）需要在最嚴格的衛生環境處理。

生物製劑製程也是同樣邏輯——全密閉系統可以放在 CNC（非管制區），開放無菌操作才需要 Grade A/B 的環境保護。環境等級應該匹配製程暴露風險，而不是統一最高標準。

重點提示 Key Note

「人員風險因子」（Human Risk Factor）的重要性：即使是在 Grade B 背景下的 Grade A 層流罩，只要有人員在場操作，就引入了生物負荷（Bioburden）的潛在來源。因此，減少人員介入本身就是一種污染管控策略（CCS）的重要組成。

這也是現代設施設計趨向自動化、密閉化的根本驅動力之一。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  2. 「人員是最高風險污染源」這個論述，在設施設計上帶來哪些具體的設計決策？（提示：想想 RABS vs. Isolator 的比較）
4.5.1 Example Open Aseptic Operations — 典型開放無菌操作範例

原文內容 Original Text

Examples of aseptic operations in biopharmaceutical manufacturing that are typically open include:

  • Cell bank preparation
  • Inoculum preparation

Cell Bank Preparation

The preparation of Master Cell Banks (MCBs) and Working Cell Banks (WCBs) are usually open operations. The process of selecting a clone and culturing it in preparation of the MCB, requires open manipulations that are normally performed in a Grade A/5 environment.

Inoculum Preparation

The initial stages in culture propagation may be performed in spinner flasks, shake flasks, roller bottles, or wave bags, etc. Depending on the cell bank configuration, the transfer of cryo-stored cells may be open or closed.

Following an incubation period, the contents of a seed culture container are transferred into a larger container for subsequent incubation and expansion of the culture. The opening of containers and transfer of materials are open operations and considered high risk.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：MCB/WCB 為何必須開放操作 Key Concepts

主細胞庫（Master Cell Bank, MCB）和工作細胞庫（Working Cell Bank, WCB）的製備，在技術上目前難以完全密閉，原因包括：

  • 克隆選擇（Clone Selection）需要個別培養皿的目視觀察與挑選，本質上是開放操作
  • 冷凍保存管（Cryovial）的分裝與標記需要人工操作
  • 初始接種量通常很小（µL 至 mL 級），難以用管路系統傳輸

因此，這類操作通常在 Grade A/5（Class 100）的生物安全操作台（BSC）或層流工作台（Laminar Flow Hood）下進行。

接種物製備（Inoculum Preparation）的風險分析

為什麼接種物製備是高風險操作？

  • Wave bags（搖擺式生物反應袋）雖然本身是密閉的，但接種時需要開口（除非使用無菌接頭）
  • 種子培養物（Seed Culture）從小容器移至大容器的「傳代」步驟，每次傳代都是一個潛在污染窗口
  • 傳代次數越多（Scale-up 步驟越多），累積的開放操作風險越高

行業趨勢：現代 CDMO 傾向於採用預先驗證的無菌連接件（Sterile Connectors）和一次性使用系統來「功能性密閉」這些操作，以降低環境等級要求。

比喻說明 Analogy

接種物製備就像是在實驗室培養珍貴菌種的研究員：你必須小心翼翼地打開培養皿、轉移菌落，每一次打開都是一次暴露機會。這就是為什麼這類操作需要「最乾淨的廚房」（Grade A）——因為食材本身非常脆弱且珍貴，而且操作過程不可避免地要暴露在外。

重點提示 Key Note — 監管視角

EU GMP Annex 1（2022 年版）明確要求，細胞庫製備等級需要適當的環境控制。FDA 和 EMA 在審查生物製劑 BLA/MAA 申請時，會特別審視細胞庫製備和接種物製備的環境等級選擇是否有充分的科學依據。

文件要點：你的 Site Master File（SMF）或設施設計依據（URS/FDS）應清楚說明這些操作的暴露風險評估，作為選擇 Grade A 的文件依據。

實務應用 Practical Application

CDMO 業務情境：一家 CDMO 正在為客戶的 mAb（單株抗體）產品建立細胞庫。若採用傳統的 BSC（生物安全操作台）方式，需要 Grade B 背景 + Grade A 操作台，設施建置與維護成本較高。

替代方案：評估採用預先無菌化的一次性接種物製備系統（如 Cytiva ReadyToProcess 或 Sartorius Biostat 系列），搭配驗證的無菌接頭，可能使背景環境從 Grade B 降為 Grade D，大幅降低 HVAC 成本與環境監測頻率。

4.5.2 Examples of Closed Aseptic Operations — 典型密閉無菌操作範例

原文內容 Original Text

Set up and preparation of closed systems should be managed, validated, and monitored to confirm that system integrity is established and maintained throughout a bioprocess.

Examples of aseptic operations in biologics manufacturing that are typically closed include:

  • Cell culture and fermentation operations
  • Bulk fill
  • Harvest operations
  • Filtration steps
  • Sterile hold operations for media and buffer

Preferably bulk fill is closed, but may be open based on the final container closure needs (such as recapping).

Cell Culture and Fermentation Operations

Single use systems used in aseptic operations are received sterile (typically polyethylene based systems sterilized by gamma irradiation) at the manufacturing site. A sterilization certificate should accompany each system certifying the conditions used for sterilization. Setup and preparation of the system for use in cell culture operations should ensure that system integrity and continued isolation from the environment is maintained.

The closing of a traditional stainless steel bioreactor includes cleaning and thermal sterilization and maintenance of a positive pressure on the system post-SIP to mitigate the risk of contamination from the environment. CIP and SIP operations should be validated. Following pre-manufacturing validation, CIP and SIP operations should be monitored to ensure that the system is maintained in a validated state.

The connections of all transfer systems also should be demonstrated to be aseptic or closed. The introduction of particulates into an open bioreactor in any environment whether Grade 7, 8, or CNC is a probability; however, the risk to the manufacturing process is considered to be small following a properly executed SIP of the system.

The initial phase in an SIP sequence during heat up of the vessel includes the condensation of clean steam (equivalent to purified water) along the cool surfaces of the vessel, resulting in a rinsing of those walls. However, if this initial "rinse" of the vessel is insufficient, a WFI (or other purified water) burst prior to SIP could be performed to ensure removal of any particulates that could have settled on the product contact surfaces during the opening of the vessel. The SIP would subsequently sterilize the vessel rendering it functionally closed.

Bulk Fill

Fully integrated bulk fill – filtration systems are available. With the exception of materials that cannot be filter sterilized, it is recommended that all bulk fill operations are prepared using an integrated filter – container system. The product at this phase of the bulk drug substance manufacturing sequence normally has high value. Performing a bulk fill operation open in a Biosafety Cabinet (BSC) should be considered bad practice.

If sterile filtration is not an option for the bulk fill operation, the use of an isolator to protect the required zero bioburden (Grade A; Grade 5) processing environment is recommended.

Harvest Operations

In some processes, such as continuously harvested perfusion systems, the harvest process should be aseptic. Filtration and sedimentation technology is typically used as the cell barrier in a perfusion system. Filtration systems, centrifuges and cell settlers should be steamed in place (or single use) to functionally close the fermentation/cell culture – harvest integrated system. The integrated system should maintain its aseptic state throughout the perfusion process until final harvest at end of batch.

Sterile Buffer Hold Operations

Solutions that are not used within a short time period after formulation should be stored sterile, even if used in low bioburden non sterile operations. Sterile solutions (media and/or buffers) should be stored in sterile single use systems or in traditional stainless steel vessels that have been sterilized prior to use. Exposing solutions that are stored long term to the environment should be considered moderate to high risk, unless these solutions are bacteriostatic. Buffer hold systems should be closed and either single use or sterilizable to allow for long term storage of buffers. These systems should be closed to prevent contamination from the environment.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：密閉操作的三種實現方式

Section 4.5.2 列舉的密閉操作可以透過以下三種方式實現：

  • 完全密閉（Fully Closed）：一次性使用袋（Single-Use Bag），系統在出廠前已經 Gamma 滅菌，現場只需確認完整性後即可使用
  • 功能性密閉（Functionally Closed）：傳統不銹鋼生物反應器，透過 SIP（就地滅菌）+ 正壓維持，使系統在使用過程中「功能上等同密閉」
  • 驗證無菌連接（Validated Aseptic Connections）：使用已驗證的無菌連接器（如 ReadyMate、Lynx）在系統間傳遞物料，保持密閉狀態

比喻說明 Analogy — 功能性密閉

功能性密閉就像「每次都有滅菌保護的接口」：

想像一個高規格的水族箱系統——雖然偶爾需要打開維護（換水、清潔），但每次「打開」之後都要經過嚴格的消毒程序（SIP），讓系統重新回到無菌狀態。這種「雖有接口、但接口有保護」的設計，就是功能性密閉的精髓。

不銹鋼生物反應器的 SIP 就是這個「每次連接都有滅菌保護」的機制——SIP 完成後系統渲染為密閉，因此可以容納在非管制環境中。

重點提示：SIP 前 WFI 衝洗的重要性

原文提到一個常被忽略的細節：SIP 升溫階段，蒸汽冷凝水確實有「沖洗器壁」的作用，但不一定足夠。若生物反應器開口期間（如接種、取樣）已累積顆粒物，應考慮在 SIP 前先進行 WFI 衝洗。

這個細節在設施 SOP 中常被省略，但在 FDA 工廠視察中可能成為觀察項目，特別是對於 Grade 8 或 CNC 環境中的反應器。

重點提示：Bulk Fill 在 BSC 中操作 = Bad Practice

這是書中少見的強烈措辭。背景是：批量原液（Bulk Drug Substance, BDS）在此階段通常已是產品最終精製狀態，具有極高的商業價值（每批可能價值數百萬美元）。

在 BSC 中開放操作意味著：操作員的微生物污染風險直接暴露給最珍貴的產品，且 BSC 的保護程度遠不及密閉過濾填充系統。ISPE 的明確立場：使用整合的過濾-容器系統是業界最佳實踐，不可取巧。

灌流系統（Perfusion System）的特殊考量

灌流（Perfusion）是一種連續培養技術，特點是不斷補充培養基、同時持續收穫產品（通常是 mAb）。與批次（Batch）或饋批（Fed-Batch）不同，灌流系統的收穫過程是持續的，因此：

  • 收穫設備（離心機、過濾器）必須與培養系統完全密閉整合
  • 任何收穫步驟的密閉失效，都可能危及整個長時間運行的批次（有時長達數月）
  • Cell settler 和 ATF 過濾器的 SIP 驗證是灌流系統的關鍵要素

實務應用 Practical Application

CDMO 建廠決策情境：一家 CDMO 正在設計一個新的上游生物製程（Upstream Bioprocessing）廠房，包含 2000L 不銹鋼生物反應器。

如果能充分驗證 SIP 程序並維持正壓，這台生物反應器可以放在 Grade 8（ISO Class 8）甚至 CNC 環境，而不必建置在 Grade D（ISO Class 8）的潔淨室中，兩者的差異主要在 HVAC 系統的規格與維護要求。

這一個決定可以節省的成本：HVAC 系統成本差距可達 30-50%，加上後續的環境監測（EM）人力成本大幅降低。對於 5-10 台反應器的設施，累計節省可達數百萬美元。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 一個 2000L 不銹鋼生物反應器，其 SIP 程序已驗證、正壓維持系統已安裝，但環境監測紀錄顯示偶爾出現細菌計數偏高。這個觀察如何影響你對其環境等級需求的判斷？

4.5.2 Filtration Steps — 過濾步驟的密閉性分析

原文內容 Original Text

The assembly and setup of a filtration skid is normally an open operation. The system may be rendered closed by effective conditioning, such as flush and/or SIP as appropriate for the filtration step. The use of a 0.2 µm (absolute) or better filter may not in all instances result in the effective closing of a process.

The assembly, preparation, conditioning, and configuration of the filtration system can determine whether a filtration system is effectively isolated from the environment housing it. The aseptic integrity of a gamma irradiated single use filter can be compromised with improper setup. All process contact surfaces of a sterilizing grade filtration system should be sterile on the post filtration side of a filter element for effective closure. This also applies to steamable filtration systems. Upstream configuration of an absolute 0.2 µm filter can have little effect on the integrity of a sterile barrier (or system closure).

The value of redundant absolute 0.2 µm filters in series in a sterile solution filtration process as depicted below should be evaluated from a risk mitigation standpoint. Manufacturers will use redundant filters in the event of failure or breach of integrity of a system. Possible reasons for failure in sterile filtration systems include:

  • Temperature or pressure excursions during SIP
  • Fouling of the filter element during the product filtration process
  • Improper installation of the filter

Post integrity testing should confirm that failure of the filter element has occurred. Re-filtration of the media or buffer or product solution may be possible. The solution may need to be discarded. Where fouling occurred in the upstream filter, removal or replacement of upstream filter may be allowable, dependent upon an organization's protocols. Where the error occurred during SIP, both absolute filter elements in the filtration system could be compromised; therefore, redundancy provides little benefit in terms of mitigation of risk.

If the filtration system fouls during operation, it is probable that the lead (upstream) filter fouled. Typically, protocol calls for replacement or removal of the lead filter. If these are positioned in series as depicted in Figure 4.12, the lead filter no longer represents the sterile barrier and is no more than a very expensive pre-filter. In addition, the SIP of the lead filter actually increases the risk of the overall operation. A more cost effective and lower risk option is to use a non-absolute 0.2 µm filter that is not steamed in place during the setup of the system.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：無菌屏障的「方向性」

Figure 4.11 揭示了一個關鍵的思維框架：0.2 µm 過濾器是一道「單向屏障」——它只保護下游（Downstream），對上游（Upstream）的無菌狀態毫無幫助。

這個概念可以直接轉化為設施設計決策：

  • 過濾器上游的連接管路、閥門、感測器 → 不需要在無菌環境中安裝
  • 過濾器下游的所有組件 → 必須維持無菌完整性，任何一個接口的洩漏都等於打開了系統
  • 因此，提升上游區域的環境等級（如從 CNC 到 Grade 8）幾乎不帶來任何無菌保證的提升

重點提示：冗餘過濾器的邏輯陷阱

許多工廠在串聯（Series）配置中安裝兩個 0.2 µm 過濾器，認為這樣更安全。ISPE 明確指出這種邏輯的缺陷：

  • SIP 失效情境：如果 SIP 溫度/壓力超標，兩個過濾器同時受到影響 → 冗餘無效
  • 前置過濾器堵塞情境：堵塞的 Filter 1 已失去無菌屏障功能，成為「昂貴的預過濾器」，而 Filter 2 才是真正的無菌屏障 → 問題是你不知道 Filter 2 是否也有損傷
  • 安裝失誤情境：兩個過濾器都可能在同一次安裝中發生錯誤 → 冗餘同樣無效

結論：真正的冗餘需要並聯（Parallel）配置，讓兩個過濾器在不同的操作條件下承受壓力，才能提供獨立的保護。

比喻說明 Analogy

串聯 vs. 並聯冗餘過濾器 = 消防系統設計：

如果你有兩個「連在一起」（串聯）的煙霧偵測器，但它們共用同一條電源線——斷電時兩個同時失效，冗餘是假的。

真正的冗餘系統需要兩個偵測器使用獨立的電源（並聯），讓一個失效時另一個仍能正常運作。過濾器的並聯配置正是這個道理——兩條獨立的過濾路徑，分別進行 SIP，互不干擾。

4.5.3 Low Bioburden Operations/Systems — 低生物負荷操作系統

原文內容 Original Text

The risk of contamination from the environment of a bioprocess should be mitigated for systems qualified as low bioburden. Low bioburden systems that are closed do not need to be sterilized prior to use and it may not be feasible to sterilize some systems. For example, WFI distribution systems are not sterile, but are effectively closed. There is little concern of contamination from the environment of the WFI systems except at the Points Of Use (POUs) where WFI is collected or sampled. The risk of contamination from the environment at the POU can be mitigated by maintaining a constant flow and pressure on the distribution loop.

Examples of bioprocess unit operations that are usually open and typically are not (or cannot be) sterilized include:

  • Chromatography operations
  • Ultrafiltration TFF Operations
  • Virus nanofiltration operations
  • Buffer preparation
  • Media preparation
  • Centrifugation operations

Open Briefly Exposed Operations

Some open operations represent very low risk as these are transitional operations that are subsequently closed, e.g.:

  • Media preparation operations
  • Buffer preparation operations
  • Centrifugation operations

核心概念：低生物負荷 vs. 無菌的區別

低生物負荷（Low Bioburden）和無菌（Sterile）是截然不同的概念：

  • 無菌（Sterile）：理論上無任何活微生物（SAL ≤ 10⁻⁶），需要通過滅菌程序達成
  • 低生物負荷（Low Bioburden）：微生物數量受到控制（如 <10 CFU/mL），但不要求完全無菌，透過密閉+控制來維持

WFI 分配系統是最典型的例子：WFI 在藥典規格下最多允許 <10 CFU/100 mL，系統透過維持高溫循環（70°C+）或持續流動來維持低生物負荷，而非滅菌狀態。

「短暫暴露操作」（Briefly Exposed Operations）的判定標準

Figure 4.14 揭示了一個非常實用的概念框架：如果一個操作是「短暫開放，隨後重新密閉，且下游有 0.2 µm 過濾步驟」，這個操作就可以被定義為「短暫暴露操作」，其設備可以放在 CNC（非管制區）環境中。

判定三要素：

  • 暴露時間「短暫且有界定」（Brief and bounded）
  • 操作後系統重新密閉
  • 下游有滅菌級過濾步驟（0.2 µm 絕對值）作為最終屏障

比喻說明 Analogy

短暫暴露 = 隧道中的短暫開放路段：

想像一條水管，大部分是密閉的（隧道），只有一小段是開放的（橋上）——但橋的盡頭有一個「超級過濾閘門」（0.2 µm 過濾器）。任何在開放路段進入的「乘客」（微生物）都會在閘門被攔截。

因此，開放的橋段雖然有風險，但因為有後段的過濾保護，這段橋不需要建在最嚴格的環境中——用 CNC 就夠了。

重點提示：下游的哪些操作不能用這個框架？

「短暫暴露」邏輯的適用限制：

  • 如果製程串流（Process Stream）在暴露後不接受 0.2 µm 過濾（例如含有不能過濾的大分子或細胞），這個邏輯就不適用
  • 病毒奈米過濾（Virus Nanofiltration）雖然是過濾步驟，但其目的是病毒去除，而非降低生物負荷——需要分開評估
  • 色層分離（Chromatography）操作本身不是無菌的，其樹脂通常以低生物負荷狀態維護，不需要滅菌條件

4.5.3 Centrifugation — Briefly Exposed Operation in Practice

原文內容 Original Text

Buffer Preparation — Risk Analysis

Most buffer salts used in bioprocessing are not sterile when processed during buffer preparation. The sources of bioburden and contaminants in a buffer preparation include contamination from the environment, including contamination from operators and the contaminants present in the buffer salt package.

Risk of contamination from personnel can be mitigated by protecting the buffer salts in protected containers, such as gamma irradiated sterile bag containers. An in-line 0.2 µm process filter for the removal of bioburden and suspended contaminants from the process stream is likely to be more effective than addition of terminal HEPA filtration and over gowning of personnel in the buffer preparation suite. Upgrading the buffer preparation suite to a Grade 7 or 8 may reduce a small portion of the bioburden load in a briefly exposed buffer solution; however, the overall impact may be insignificant when compared to that of adding an in line 0.2 µm filter.

Exposure time also should be considered in a buffer preparation risk mitigation strategy. If buffers are not processed and/or stored at temperatures below 10°C (50°F) or above 50°C (122°F), or processed under other bacteriostatic conditions, exposure time can be significant. Microbial contamination can double as quickly as every 20 minutes under ideal conditions; therefore, buffers that are not consumed soon after formulation should be 0.2 µm filtered. It may be necessary to 0.2 µm filter any buffer not fully consumed within 24 hours and store the buffer in sterile containers.

Centrifugation as Briefly Exposed

Centrifugation for cell culture harvest clarification represents another example of an open operation that can be closed or briefly exposed operation. It is possible to configure a centrifuge, whether disk stack or tubular bowl for full CIP and SIP; however, centrifuges are not regularly steamed in place. A preferred approach is to combine the best of available technologies to make centrifugation a briefly exposed operation.

In the briefly exposed option, an aseptic cell culture is clarified rapidly by centrifugation. The goal of the centrifugation is to remove 95% to 98% of the turbidity (i.e., cells, cell debris, and other suspended solids) in the harvested culture. The remaining turbidity is removed by depth filtration followed by 0.2 µm filtration. If properly executed, the filtration steps render the process cell-free (thereby closed) thereby effectively isolating the process stream from the environment and from any potential bioburden that may have been present in the transition equipment.

In this scenario, the product or process stream is very briefly exposed to the transition equipment. From a process risk standpoint, the centrifuge and depth filter are analogous to a transfer pump and representing extremely low risk of contamination from the environment to the process; therefore, this equipment can be housed in a non-classified environment.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：緩衝液配製的風險緩解優先順序

ISPE 提供了一個反直覺但極其重要的結論：

加裝在線 0.2 µm 過濾器 > 升級房間等級至 Grade 7/8

為什麼？因為緩衝液中的生物負荷主要來源有兩個：

  1. 緩衝鹽本身帶入的污染（佔主要部分）
  2. 操作環境的空氣污染（暴露時間短，影響相對小）

升級房間等級只能緩解第 2 項，對第 1 項幾乎無效。但一個 0.2 µm 在線過濾器可以同時消除兩個來源的污染。

Cost-benefit 結論：把房間從 CNC 升級到 Grade 8 的成本，遠不如安裝一個 0.2 µm 過濾器。這是資源配置的最優決策。

重點提示：微生物倍增時間的實際意義

原文提到「在理想條件下，微生物每 20 分鐘可以翻倍」——這個數字有重要的實務意義：

  • 若緩衝液在配製後 2 小時才過濾，初始 1 CFU/mL 可能增長至 64 CFU/mL（2⁶）
  • 若在 4 小時後過濾，可能已達 4096 CFU/mL（2¹²）
  • 儲存在 2-8°C 可以顯著延緩倍增，但不能完全阻止生長

SOP 設計建議：緩衝液配製後應設定「最長未使用時限」（如 24 小時），超過時限必須重新過濾或廢棄。這個限制應該有微生物挑戰研究作為依據。

比喻說明 Analogy — 離心機的「短暫暴露」地位

離心機就像水管中的「彎頭」：

水從一個密閉管道（密閉培養系統）→ 流過一個彎頭（離心機）→ 進入另一個密閉管道（過濾系統）。彎頭雖然不是密閉的，但流過的時間極短，而且後段有「終端過濾閘門」（0.2 µm）。

因此，彎頭（離心機）放在哪個環境中（CNC vs. Grade D），對最終產品的無菌性幾乎沒有影響——這就是為什麼 ISPE 說這些設備可以放在非管制區。

4.5.3 Weigh Dispense Operations — 稱量分裝操作

原文內容 Original Text

Weigh Dispense

When materials arrive from suppliers, the material outer packaging and transportation materials (e.g., pallets, cardboard boxes) should be removed prior to introduction of the material into the manufacturing area to mitigate risk of contamination of the process area environment. Materials received are usually not sterile, having been manufactured in controlled or uncontrolled and unclassified environments. Powders and other non-liquid ingredients should be kept out of the manufacturing areas. These materials should be introduced into the manufacturing process via closed transfer systems. Where closed transfers are not possible, minimizing open operations is preferred.

Weigh dispensing operations should be performed in clean and controlled environments. The use of a well-designed weigh booth will both protect the ingredient during open manipulations and can protect the environment from dust or other particulates that can spread into the facility. The weigh booth should provide a Unidirectional Airflow (UDAF) HEPA filtered air supply over the weighing area and can collect stray particulates in a dust collection system. These particulates could include viable organisms and could serve as carriers for viral contaminants. The risk of these manipulations increases with increased volume and number of manipulations.

The kitting of pre-weighed materials into closed transfer systems can further mitigate the risk of particulate contamination by removing the open manipulations from the primary process areas and by providing a vehicle for the closed transfer of materials into a process system.

Centralizing the weigh dispense area is typically more cost effective and can minimize the number of areas where dust formation can occur. Locating this function in a cGMP area near the warehouse area may provide better control of the raw material inventory as partially open containers can now be stored and controlled by the warehouse specialists. The centralized weigh dispense area can serve as the warehouse – manufacturing transfer point enhancing control of incoming materials.

Additional Considerations for Solids Handling Operations

Handling large volumes in a central location can be beneficial, as risks spills and dust generation are higher. Kitting of material in closed containers for delivery to the manufacturing area is usually preferred.

A central weigh dispense next to a warehouse can provide a better method for controlling partial lots. When a raw material container is opened, it typically requires special handling and storage to prevent adulteration of product.

If raw material additions are batch dependent, there is likely a minimum threshold volume that will be required for each batch. A secondary staging area may be used to address the smaller remaining volumes that may be required in the specific batch. Options include a two stage weigh dispense function or standardization of batch sizes (possibly with discard of product) to streamline all additions.

The classification of weigh dispense areas should be consistent with the quality of the materials weighed and the quality requirements of materials prepared. Buffer salts are typically manufactured in CNC or uncontrolled unclassified environments. Quality of the buffer can be dependent on the:

  • Quality of the water (e.g., WFI ≤10 cfu/100 mL)
  • Quality of the salt
  • Time of incubation, prior to sterile filtration or prior to use

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：稱量分裝設計的三個目的

設計稱量分裝（Weigh Dispense）區域需要同時達到三個目的，這三個目的有時相互矛盾：

  1. 保護原料：防止稱量過程中環境對原料的污染
  2. 保護環境：防止粉塵（包括具有生物活性或病毒載體的微粒）擴散到生產區域
  3. 保護操作員：防止原料粉塵（特別是高效能 API）對員工健康的影響

因此，稱量台（Weigh Booth）的設計不僅是一個 GMP 問題，也是職業安全（OEL）問題。HEPA 過濾+單向氣流（UDAF）+ 粉塵收集系統是應對三個目的的核心設計。

「Kitting（套件化）」的策略價值

Kitting 是指將一批製程所需的所有原料預先稱量、分裝到密閉袋/容器中，再以密閉方式轉入生產區域。其好處包括：

  • 將所有開放稱量操作集中在一個有控制的「稱量間」，而非在多個生產區域各自稱量
  • 一次稱量、密閉包裝，在生產區域只需開袋加入系統 → 最小化生產區的開放操作
  • 批次記錄（Batch Record）更清晰，每個批次的 Kit 對應到一個批號，可追溯性更強

比喻說明 Analogy

集中稱量站 = 超市的預先包裝區：

超市不會讓顧客自己去倉庫切牛肉——而是在有控制的後台預先包裝好，顧客只需取走密封包裝。

集中稱量站對 CDMO 的意義也一樣：原料在有 HEPA 防護的稱量間完成「預先包裝」，生產操作員只需取走密封的 Kit，最大限度減少生產區的粉塵暴露與開放操作。

重點提示：稱量區分級依據材料的最終用途

原文強調：稱量區的環境等級應與被稱量材料的品質需求一致，而不是統一最高標準。

實務上的分級原則：

  • 緩衝鹽（Buffer Salts）：通常在 CNC 或非管制環境稱量即可，因為後段有 0.2 µm 過濾
  • 培養基（Media）成分：需要考慮後段是否有滅菌步驟，若有則 CNC 可以接受
  • 直接添加到無菌系統的原料：需要更高等級的保護（Grade D 或以上）

關鍵因素：水的品質 + 鹽的品質 + 配製後存放時間共同決定最終緩衝液的品質，環境等級只是其中一個（且通常不是最重要的）因素。

集中稱量站的成本-效益框架

分散式稱量（每個生產區域各有稱量台）：
  - 多個區域都有粉塵風險 → 多處環境監測需求
  - 部分用量的原料需要多點存放 → 庫存管理複雜
  - 每個稱量台需要各自維護和驗證
  - 生產員工需要接受稱量操作訓練

集中式稱量站（Centralized Weigh Dispense）：
  - 僅一個高規格稱量間需要建置和維護
  - 庫存集中在倉庫旁 → 部分用量管理更容易
  - 只需培訓少數專職稱量員工
  - 生產區域只接收密封 Kit → 降低生產區複雜性

位置建議：緊鄰倉庫（Warehouse）
理由：
  1. 原料不必穿越生產區才能到稱量站
  2. 未用完的開封容器可以立即回到倉庫控制
  3. 作為「倉庫-生產」的物料轉移控制點

本節重點回顧 Section 4.5 Summary

  • 人員是最高風險：開放無菌操作中，人員（Personnel）是主要的生物負荷和微粒污染來源；製程密閉性是緩解「人員風險因子」的核心策略
  • 開放 vs. 密閉的典型例子：MCB/WCB 製備、接種物製備屬於必要的開放操作，需要 Grade A/B 環境；細胞培養、收穫、過濾、緩衝液儲存屬於可密閉操作，可放置在較低等級甚至 CNC 環境
  • 0.2 µm 過濾器是「方向性屏障」：它只保護下游（Downstream），升級上游環境等級對無菌保證幾乎沒有貢獻——這是設施設計中常見的資源浪費點
  • 冗餘過濾器需要並聯（不是串聯）：串聯冗餘在 SIP 失效情境下提供的保護很有限；並聯（Lead-Lag）配置才能提供真正的冗餘保護
  • 「短暫暴露操作」（Briefly Exposed）可放在 CNC：離心機、緩衝液配製等操作，只要暴露時間短且下游有 0.2 µm 過濾，可以放置在非管制區，顯著節省設施成本
  • 緩衝液配製：在線過濾 > 升級房間等級：安裝 0.2 µm 在線過濾器的效益遠大於將緩衝液配製間升級為 Grade 7/8——這是資源配置的最優決策
  • 集中稱量站設計：將稱量操作集中、靠近倉庫，提供 UDAF+HEPA+粉塵收集，配合 Kitting 策略，可以最小化生產區的開放操作和環境管制需求
  • 文件化是關鍵：所有環境等級的降低決策，都需要有書面的風險評估和科學依據，以備 FDA/EMA 審查——這是將技術邏輯轉化為監管認可的必要步驟
