導師解析 — 核心法規文件的重要性

ICH Q7/Q7A — API GMP 準則

本指引規範了活性醫藥成分（API）的 GMP 要求，是全球藥廠共同遵守的基準。對生物製藥而言，雖然 API 多為大分子蛋白質，但此指引確立了生產環境控制、設備確效、批次記錄等核心概念，為後續生物製藥特定指引（如 ICH Q11）奠定基礎。

ICH Q11 — 原料藥開發與製造

Q11 是 Q7 的延伸，專門針對「生技／生物製品」的原料藥（drug substance）開發，包括細胞培養、發酵、純化等程序。此指引要求廠商建立設計空間（Design Space）並說明工藝開發的科學依據，與 ISPE Vol.6 的設施設計邏輯緊密連動。

21 CFR Part 211 — FDA 完成劑型 GMP

21 CFR Part 211 是 FDA 對完成劑型藥品（finished pharmaceuticals）的核心法規要求，涵蓋廠房設計、設備、人員、生產與製程控制、品管等全面要求。生物製藥廠若打算在美國銷售產品，必須確保所有設施設計與操作程序符合 Part 211 的最低要求。重點：Part 211 是法規底線，而 ISPE 指引是最佳實務；在合規上，法規永遠優先。

ICH Q9 — 品質風險管理（QRM）

Q9 建立了製藥業通用的風險管理框架，包括 FMEA（失效模式效應分析）、HACCP（危害分析重要管制點）等工具。ISPE Vol.6 全書的設施設計決策（密閉系統、環境分級、HVAC 設計等）均以 QRM 為核心依據，若不理解 Q9，就無法理解為何某些空間需要更嚴格的環境控制。

比喻：法規文件的層次關係

把這些文件想像成建築法規的體系：ICH Q7/Q11 是「建築法規總則」（適用所有建築），21 CFR Part 211 是「消防安全強制規範」（必須遵守），ICH Q9 是「風險評估工具手冊」，而 ISPE Vol.6 則是「高端實驗室建築設計最佳實務指南」。後者告訴你怎麼蓋得最好，但前者告訴你最低必須做到什麼。

NIH Guidelines 和 CDC/NIH 聯合出版的《Biosafety in Microbiological and Biomedical Laboratories (BMBL)》是定義 BSL 1-4 生物安全等級的權威文件。ISPE Vol.6 的圍堵策略（Containment Strategy）和設施設計均依賴這些指引來確定微生物的危害等級，以決定所需的物理圍堵措施。若處理 BSL-2 以上的微生物，廠房設計必須符合相應圍堵要求，這不只是 GMP，更是生物安全法律義務。

ISO 14644-1 — 潔淨室空氣分級標準

ISO 14644-1 以科學化的粒子計數方式定義 ISO Class 1-9，是歐美日藥廠環境監測的通用語言。FDA 採用 ISO 5/7/8 對應其傳統的 Class 100/10,000/100,000；EU GMP 則採用 Grade A/B/C/D 但同樣參照 ISO 標準測試方法。ISPE Vol.6 的環境章節以 ISO 14644 為基礎，橋接兩套體系。

Appendix 3 — Glossary（附錄三：詞彙表）
12.1 Acronyms and Abbreviations（縮寫對照表）

原文 — Acronyms & Abbreviations

導師解析 — 關鍵縮寫深入說明

BSL（生物安全等級）— 圍堵設計的起點

BSL 1-4 是依據微生物對人體的危害程度分級，決定廠房圍堵等級的關鍵輸入：

  • BSL-1：低危害，標準微生物操作（一般實驗室）
  • BSL-2：中度危害（多數生物製藥細胞株，如 CHO、大腸桿菌），需負壓、高壓滅菌器、BSC
  • BSL-3：嚴重危害，可致命但有治療方法（如結核桿菌），需雙重門、專用排氣
  • BSL-4：極高危害，無治療方法（如伊波拉病毒），需正壓太空衣或隔離式設施

大多數生物製藥廠運作在 BSL-1/2 級別；了解 BSL 等級是設施設計圍堵章節的先決條件。

CNC / CNC+ — 生物製藥特有的環境分類

CNC（Controlled Not Classified，受控但未分級）是 ISPE Vol.6 特有的環境類型，介於一般空間（UC）與 ISO 分級潔淨室之間：

  • 有 HVAC 控制（溫度、濕度、換氣次數），但未達 ISO 14644 的正式分級標準
  • 適用於封閉系統操作（如密閉管路、密閉生物反應器），因製程本身提供足夠的污染屏障
  • CNC+ 則額外加裝本地監測設備（如粒子計數器），提升環境數據可見度

CHO（中國倉鼠卵巢細胞株）

CHO 是生物製藥業最廣泛使用的哺乳動物表達系統，約 70% 的治療性蛋白質（包括多數 MAb）由 CHO 細胞生產。了解 CHO 細胞特性（對 pH、溶氧、溫度敏感）有助於理解生物反應器設計要求。

MAb（單株抗體）— 生物製藥的旗艦產品

MAb（Monoclonal Antibody，單株抗體）是目前全球銷售額最高的生物製藥產品類別（包括抗腫瘤、自體免疫疾病治療藥物）。MAb 生產流程（細胞培養 → 收穫 → 蛋白A純化 → 病毒去除過濾 → 製劑）是本 ISPE Vol.6 設施設計的主要應用場景。

SIP vs CIP — 兩個必須區分的概念

CIP（就地清洗，Clean-In-Place）：使用清洗液（NaOH、酸液等）在不拆卸設備的情況下清除污垢和殘留。目標是減少生物負荷（bioburden reduction）。

SIP（就地滅菌，Steam-In-Place）：使用飽和蒸汽（通常 121°C / 15 分鐘）在不拆卸設備的情況下達到滅菌（sterile = 零生物負荷）。目標是消滅所有活微生物。

順序：CIP 先行，SIP 其後。就像洗碗（CIP）後才能送去高壓消毒（SIP）。

比喻：MCB vs WCB

把 MCB（Master Cell Bank，主細胞庫）想像成細胞的「原版母帶」，WCB（Working Cell Bank，工作細胞庫）是「複製品」。每次生產批次使用 WCB 的一小管，MCB 永遠作為備份保存。若 WCB 受污染，可從 MCB 重新展開（re-derive）。MCB 的保存條件和特性確認是生物製藥廠最重要的資產保護措施之一。

12.2 Definitions — Part 1: A through C（定義：A 至 C）

原文 — Definitions: A–C

Adventitious Agents（外源性污染微生物）

Adventitious agents are microorganisms that have been unintentionally introduced into the manufacturing process of a biological product. Adventitious agents include bacteria, fungi, mycoplasmas, rickettsia, protozoa, parasites, Transmissible Spongiform Encephalopathy (TSE) agents, and viruses.

Airborne Total Particulate（空浮總粒子）

A measurement of airborne particles in a specified size range (typically determined by mass mean diameter) as recorded by a Discrete Particle Counter (DPC). Since the vast majority of particle counters are incapable of distinguishing between viable and non-viable particulate, this measurement is erroneously called "non-viable".

Air Change Rate / Ventilation Rate（換氣次數）

The volume of air supplied to a room (per hour) divided by the volume of the room. This is a purely numerical evaluation used as a rule of thumb in cleanroom design.

Aseptic Operations（無菌操作）

Processes that are devoid of measurable (detectable) bioburden. Aseptic operations generally require sterilization of the environment, equipment and process solutions to achieve the sterile state prior to use.

Aseptic Transfer（無菌移送）

Material transfer where the risk of contamination from the environment has been mitigated.

Axenic State（純淨狀態）

A culture that includes the intended living organism but is entirely free of all other contaminating organisms.

Bioburden-Free（無微生物負荷）

A condition where bioburden is not detectable. Bioreactors used in cell culture or fermentation operations should be sterile prior to use for consistent and reproducible performance. Biosafety Cabinets (BSCs), RABs and isolators should provide an environment free of bioburden for consistent and reproducible aseptic operations. Sterile product manufacturing testing and qualification guidelines are by necessity more stringent and prescriptive than that of active pharmaceutical ingredient manufacturing and therefore are not required.

Bioprocessing（生物製造加工）

Biologics manufacturing. Includes all operations used in the manufacture of biologics including upstream processes such as inoculum preparation, cell culture/fermentation processes, harvest and clarification processes such as sedimentation and filtration processes, downstream processing such as chromatography and filtration processes.

Briefly Exposed（短暫暴露）

Open processes containing process and/or product components that are rendered closed by means of an appropriate closing process. Examples of briefly exposed processes include open buffer or media preparations where the solution is "briefly" exposed to the environment prior to closing by sterile filtration and/or thermal sanitization. Briefly exposed operations may be performed in Controlled Not Classified (CNC) or low bioburden environments if measures used to close the process are appropriate to mitigate risk of contamination from the environment.

Carryover（殘留攜帶）

Contaminants detected in process streams arising from insufficient removal of contaminating components from previous manufacturing steps or batches. Maximum allowable carryover residues should be estimated and meet defined criteria. Carryover is typically a result of insufficient cleaning, sanitization, rinsing and/or conditioning.

導師解析 — A至C 關鍵術語

Adventitious Agents — 生物製藥的頭號風險

外源性污染微生物（尤其是病毒，包括 TSE 朊病毒）是生物製藥安全性的核心挑戰，因為：

  • 原料（動物源血清、原料細胞）可能攜帶潛伏病毒
  • TSE 朊病毒具有異常的抗滅菌性（一般 121°C 蒸汽滅菌無法完全消滅）
  • ICH Q5A 專門規範生物製品的病毒安全測試要求

設施設計需包含「病毒去除/不活化步驟」（如低 pH 不活化、奈米過濾）並設置病毒隔離區域。

Airborne Total Particulate — 「非活性」粒子的迷思

本定義特別提醒：粒子計數器無法區分活性（有菌）和非活性（無菌）粒子，因此常見的「非活性粒子計數」其實是「總粒子計數」。活性微生物監測需要另外的方法（如沉降碟、接觸碟）。這是設計環境監測計畫時必須理解的基本限制。

Axenic State（純淨狀態）— 與 Sterile 的差異

Axenic（純淨）強調「只有目標生物，沒有任何污染生物」，常用於描述細胞培養或發酵過程中的理想狀態。與 Sterile（無菌）的差異：

  • Sterile（無菌）：完全無任何活的微生物，包括目標生物
  • Axenic（純淨）：只有目標生物存在，無雜菌，但目標生物本身是活的

發酵槽在接種後的理想狀態是 Axenic（純培養），而不是 Sterile。

Bioburden-Free（無微生物負荷）— 不等於無菌

Bioburden-Free 的技術定義是「以現有檢測方法，生物負荷無法被偵測到」。這意味著：

  • 它是一個偵測限度的概念，而非絕對的零
  • 用於描述環境（BSC 內部、RABs 內部）應達到的狀態
  • 比較保守的「功能性」說法，相對於更嚴格的「Sterile（無菌）」

這也是為何 ISPE Vol.6 不要求所有生物製程空間都達到無菌等級，而是依照製程開放程度設計相應的環境。

比喻：Briefly Exposed 就像開蓋的鍋子

「短暫暴露」就像燉湯時短暫掀蓋加料，隨後立刻蓋回（無菌過濾或熱滅菌「關閉」製程）。關鍵是：暴露時間要短、後續的密閉步驟必須充分驗證。若暴露時間太長或滅菌步驟不足以消滅潛在污染，就需要升級環境等級。

Carryover（殘留攜帶）— 多產品廠的核心風險

對於同時生產多種生物製品的 CDMO 而言，Carryover 是最重要的跨批次污染風險。ISPE Baseline Guide Vol.7（Risk-MaPP）提供了評估最大允許殘留量（MACO）的方法學。設計良好的 CIP 程序、適當的沖洗確效和殘留測試是管控 Carryover 的關鍵。

12.2 Definitions — Part 2: Closed / Open System & Process Closure（密閉與開放系統）

原文 — Closed System, Open Process & Related Terms

Classified Space（分級潔淨空間）

Areas where HVAC systems are specifically designed to reduce airborne contaminants below a specified level as defined in ISO 14644-1 (tested per ISO 14644-2 and ISO 14644-3) and both temperature and Relative Humidity (RH) are controlled more tightly than in the ambient environment. These areas must be performance verified / qualified. These areas may be tested to meet ISO requirements for airborne 0.5 µm particulate and viable organisms in the "in-operation" state to meet US FDA requirements or they may be tested to meet ISO requirements for both airborne 0.5 µm and 5.0 µm particulate as well as viable organisms in both the "in-operation" state as well as the "at-rest" state to meet EMA and PIC/S requirements. Where EMA and PIC/S requirements are to be satisfied the transition between these two states should take place in 15 to 20 minutes.

Closed System（密閉系統）

A process system that is designed and operated such that the product is never exposed to the surrounding environment. Additions to and draws from closed systems must be performed in a completely closed fashion. Sterile filters may be used to provide effective barriers from contaminants in the environment. A system is closed (or isolated from the environment) when the risk of contamination to the product or process cannot be mitigated by housing the operation in a bioburden-free or particulate-free environment. In Quality Risk Management (QRM) Verification, the environment does not represent a Critical Aspect of a closed process. A closed process is one that has been validated to show that there are sufficient layers of protection to mitigate the risk of contamination from the environment. The detection of a contaminant from the environment in an otherwise closed system indicates a breach of the closed system and therefore constitutes a system failure. Examples of closed systems include sterile single use bags supplied with integrated aseptic connection devices.

Functionally Closed（功能性密閉）

Process systems that may be opened but are "rendered closed" by a cleaning, sanitization and/or sterilization process that is appropriate or consistent with the process requirements. These systems shall remain closed during production within the system. A functionally closed process must be validated to show that sufficient layers of protection have been implemented to mitigate the risk of contamination from the environment. Transfers into or from these systems must also be validated as closed. Examples include process vessels that may be cleaned in place and steamed in place between uses.

Open Process（開放式製程）

A process that is not closed and therefore requires appropriate environmental controls to mitigate the risk of contamination from the environment. Under QRM verification, the environment within the RABs and/or BSCs represents a critical aspect of the open process. Open sterile and aseptic operations must be performed in an environment where the probability of contamination is acceptably low. Open bioburden-controlled processing may be performed in a Grade 7 or 8 environment as appropriate for the unit operation.

Critical Control Point — CCP（關鍵管制點）

A step where control is required to prevent or eliminate a product safety or quality hazard or reduce it to an acceptable level.

Critical Process Parameter — CPP（關鍵製程參數）

A measurable input (input material attribute or operating parameter) or output (process state variable or output material attribute) of a process step that must be controlled to achieve the desired product quality and process consistency.

Critical Quality Attribute — CQA（關鍵品質屬性）

Physical, chemical, biological or microbiological properties or characteristics that need to be controlled (directly or indirectly) to ensure product quality (ISPE PQLI Guide). Physical, chemical, biological or microbiological properties or characteristics that should be within an appropriate limit, range, or distribution to ensure the desired product quality (ICH Q8(R2)).

Cross-Contamination / Crossover（交叉污染）

Contamination of the product or processing area by components or contaminants found in a neighboring product or processing area. Cross contamination typically occurs between open processes. Recirculating HVAC can be a source of cross contamination if improperly designed.

導師解析 — 密閉/開放系統與 CQA/CPP

密閉系統的三個關鍵判定條件

ISPE Vol.6 的「密閉系統」定義比直覺更嚴格，必須同時滿足：

  1. 設計密閉：產品永不暴露於周圍環境
  2. 操作密閉：所有進出（取樣、添加、排放）均以密閉方式進行
  3. 確效密閉：已驗證（validated）系統能提供足夠的保護層

符合這三個條件，環境才「不是」製程的 Critical Aspect——也就是說，該空間的環境等級可以降低（如使用 CNC 而非 ISO 7/8）。

密閉系統違規的後果

「若在原本密閉的系統中偵測到來自環境的污染物，即構成系統失效（system failure）。」這個定義說明了密閉系統違規（breach）的嚴重性——這不只是偏差（deviation），而是整個密閉系統設計假設的崩潰，需要徹底的根本原因調查和設計評審。

比喻：密閉系統 vs 開放系統如同保溫瓶 vs 敞口杯

密閉系統就像保溫瓶：無論外界環境多髒，裡面的飲料都不受影響（只要蓋子確實蓋緊且已清潔滅菌）。開放系統就像敞口的水杯：一旦置於不乾淨的環境中，就必然受到污染風險。因此開放系統操作就必須在潔淨室（像把水杯放在極度潔淨的無塵房間）中進行。

CQA vs CPP — 生物製藥 QbD 的核心語言

這兩個概念是 ICH Q8/Q10 品質體系的核心，也是 ISPE Vol.6 設施設計的驅動力：

  • CQA（關鍵品質屬性）：定義「我需要保護什麼」，例如蛋白質的糖型分佈、生物活性、純度。CQA 直接關係到患者安全。
  • CPP（關鍵製程參數）：定義「我需要控制什麼」，例如細胞培養的 pH、溶氧、溫度。CPP 是控制 CQA 的手段。

設施設計邏輯：CQA → 找出影響 CQA 的 CPP → 設計能有效監控和控制 CPP 的設施和系統。

  2. 單株抗體的 CQA 可能包括哪些屬性？對應的設施設計要求是什麼？

12.2 Definitions — Part 3: Environmental Monitoring, Classification & Remaining Terms 12.2 定義 — 第三部分：環境監測、分級與其餘術語

原文 — Environmental & Remaining Definitions

ISO 14644 — Classification（環境分級）

This standard classifies spaces following a decimal system according to the concentration of particulate in a series of size ranges, specifically: 0.1, 0.2, 0.3, 0.5, 1.0, and 5.0 µm. An ISO class designation indicates that particulate within the space are within limits as defined by the formula Cn = 10N × (0.1/D)2.08 where Cn is the particle count, N is the ISO class, and D is the particle mean diameter in µm.

ISO 14644-1 Air Quality Classes Table

Note: 1 cubic meter = 35.3 cubic feet. FDA measures only 0.5 µm (in-operation). EMA/PIC/S measures both 0.5 µm and 5.0 µm (at-rest and in-operation).

FDA Application of ISO Classifications
  • ISO 5: 3,520 particles/m³ (0.5 µm), in-operation. Normally with unidirectional flow (0.20–0.45 m/s)
  • ISO 6: 35,200 particles/m³, in-operation
  • ISO 7: 352,000 particles/m³, in-operation
  • ISO 8: 3,520,000 particles/m³, in-operation
  • ISO 9: 35,200,000 particles/m³ (not in FDA guidance but found in FDA-regulated facilities)
Local Protected Environments（本地保護環境）

Local protected environments may be used for open processes. Examples include Biosafety Cabinets (BSCs), Unidirectional Down Flow Hoods (UDFHs), isolators and Restricted Access Barriers (RABs). When used, it is critical that the local environment be protected from unexpected breach. Only a bioburden-free environment should be used for open aseptic operations.

Low Bioburden (operation)（低微生物負荷操作）

Process where a limited and controlled level of measurable bioburden is acceptable under specific conditions. (e.g. WFI, chromatography operations).

MERV (Multiple Efficiency Reporting Value)（過濾效率值）

The ASHRAE Standard 52.2 method of testing filter efficiency by challenging filters with particles of multiple sizes and integrating the efficiency into a single number rating. The higher the MERV number the more efficient the filter. MERV 14/15 is approximately equivalent to a 95% (ASHRAE) efficient filter.

Risk Assessment（風險評估）

Systematic process of organizing information to support risk decisions. This includes identification of risk(s) and evaluation of the nature, severity and likelihood of those risk(s).

Sanitization（衛生消毒）

Operation that reduces the level of bioburden.

Sterile（無菌）/ Sterilization（滅菌）

Sterile: Devoid of life. Zero bioburden. Free of bioburden.

Sterilization: Form of sanitization that results in a bioburden-free (sterile) condition. Methods include thermal sterilization (121°C/15 minutes or higher), sterile filtration (0.2 µm or better), chemical sterilization (e.g., VHP, chlorine dioxide, ozone) or irradiation (e.g. gamma, UV).

Uncontrolled — UC（非受控區域）

Areas where the HVAC systems may be present, but no claim is made or qualified for the specific control of particulate, temperature or humidity. These areas are sometimes referred to as "general" or "comfort controlled" areas within pharmaceutical facilities such as office and technical space.

Ventilation Efficiency (Effectiveness)（換氣效率）

The ability of an HVAC system to dilute contaminants when compared to ideal dilution. A Ventilation Effectiveness of 1.0 indicates ideal dilution. Displacement ventilation may have an effectiveness as high as 3.0 to 4.0.

導師解析 — 環境監測與分級深度解析

ISO 分級公式

C_n = 10^N × (0.1 / D)^2.08

其中：
  C_n = 最大允許粒子濃度 (粒子/m³)
  N   = ISO 等級數 (1-9)
  D   = 粒子平均直徑 (µm)

例：ISO 5 等級，0.5 µm 粒子：
  C_n = 10^5 × (0.1/0.5)^2.08
      = 100,000 × (0.2)^2.08
      ≈ 3,520 粒子/m³  ✓（與表格一致）

15-20 分鐘恢復時間 — EMA 獨有要求

EU GMP 要求從「動態」到「靜態」的環境恢復（recovery）時間必須在 15-20 分鐘內完成，這可透過 ISO 14644-3 的「恢復測試（recovery test）」驗證。此要求直接影響 HVAC 設計的換氣次數（ACH）和氣流速度。台灣 CDMO 若申請歐盟市場必須在確效報告中包含此測試數據。

Sterilization 方法選擇原則

不同滅菌方法各有適用場景：

  • 熱滅菌（SIP, 121°C/15min）：最可靠，用於不鏽鋼設備、管路；需確效 F₀ 值
  • 無菌過濾（0.2µm）：用於不耐熱溶液（培養基、蛋白質溶液）
  • VHP（過氧化氫蒸汽）：用於環境表面、隔離器去污；需確效殺孢效果（6-log D-value)
  • UV 輻射：用於空氣、表面；穿透力弱，僅限暴露面
  • γ 射線：用於一次性使用物品（如 SU 袋）；無法現場使用

比喻：MERV 就像口罩的過濾等級

MERV 值就像口罩的 N 數（N95、N99）：數字越高，過濾效率越強。MERV 14/15 ≈ 95% 過濾效率，約等於一般潔淨室的 bag filter；HEPA 則是 99.97% 以上（超過 MERV 評分上限）。選擇 MERV 等級需在過濾效能和系統壓降（energy cost）之間取得平衡。

ISO 分級 vs EU GMP 等級 — 雙軌體系對應

全球生物製藥廠必須同時理解兩套環境分級語言：

FDA vs EMA 差異：FDA 只要求在「動態（in-operation）」下測試 0.5µm 粒子；EMA/PIC/S 另外要求「靜態（at-rest）」以及 5µm 粒子的數據，且兩個狀態的轉換應在 15-20 分鐘內完成。
