11.10 Design of a WFI/Purified Water Distribution System

The layout and general design of a high purity water system (WFI or purified water) should be consistent and follow good manufacturing guidelines in respect to installation, support, natural drainage, flow rates, dead or stagnation areas (dead legs), and minimization of areas that may promote micro-organism growth.

11.10.1 Fittings and Equipment

All equipment that, when installed, comes in direct contact with the high purity water should use a suitable stainless steel or non-metallic material for all contact areas, except for valve diaphragms and tri-clamp gaskets.

This includes all valves and monitoring sensors.

Valve design should be sanitary diaphragm type, installed at approximately 60° to the vertical to optimize drainability. Hash marks or drain dots are provided on many designs to allow precise orientation of the valves in horizontal installations.

PTFE Teflon faced diaphragms are preferred for hot water applications, covering a temperature range from -30 to 350°F (0 to 177°C).

Connecting flanges for all valves and fittings should be tri-clamp design.

11.10.2 Natural Drainage

Well designed distribution loops should be installed overhead and point of use valves should be located at a convenient location within the process or user area, below the distribution loop.

Slopes should be verified externally after hanging and before insulation is installed.

Hangers should use steel clamps/fittings with Viton or similar inserts designed for use with the particular tubing, installed for SS at least on 10 ft (3 m) centers. Support for PVDF piping should be appropriate for the operating temperatures. Continuous support of plastic pipe should be considered using angle iron or equivalent to prevent slope changes due to expansion and contraction and point stresses from hangers.

Piping distribution from an unavoidable low point in the system, such as the storage tank discharge elbow, should be designed with a clear low point and a sanitary drain valve installed with a maximum of 6 diameter dead leg (no less than 6 diameters from the centerline of the main line to the valve center line based on the main line diameter).

Slope of distribution system process pipe or tubing should be a minimum of 1/16 in./ft (0.52 cm/m).

Location of all drain valves should be totally and easily accessible.

Tutorial Commentary

配送系統設計的核心原則

高純水配送系統設計必須同時滿足三大要求：

  • 可排水性（Drainability）：所有管線必須能完全排空，避免死水區域
  • 防微生物增長：消除死角（dead legs）、確保持續流動
  • 材質相容性：所有接觸面使用不鏽鋼或合格非金屬材料

關鍵數值記憶

  • 閥門安裝角度：60° to vertical（非水平安裝），優化排水
  • Dead leg 最大值：6 倍管徑（從主管中心線到閥門中心線）
  • 最小坡度：1/16 in./ft = 0.52 cm/m
  • SS 支撐間距：每 10 ft (3 m) 一個支架
  • PVDF 管件：需要連續支撐（因高溫軟化）

比喻說明

想像一條山間溪流：設計師希望水流在每一段管路中都能像溪水一樣「自然流動、自然排空」。如果管路有凹陷或水平段，就像溪流中的水潭 -- 水會停滯、微生物就會開始滋生。所以每段管路都必須有明確的坡度，讓水在停機時能靠重力自然排出。

實務重點：Tri-clamp 設計

Tri-clamp（三片式卡箍）是製藥級管件連接的標準。所有閥門、感測器都應使用 tri-clamp 連接，因為它：

  • 提供平整的內壁接觸面（無螺紋死角）
  • 可快速拆裝進行清潔和維護
  • 使用 EPDM 或 PTFE 墊片確保密封

11.11 Fabrication of a WFI/Purified Water Distribution System

High purity water distribution systems using the material and finishes specified above must be joined using acceptable welding or other sanitary techniques.

11.11.1 Assurance of Quality Distribution Piping

The distribution piping and storage systems should be installed in accordance with cGMPs and should be fabricated, manufactured, procured, and installed in strict accordance with explicit operating procedures.

11.11.2 Operating Procedures Should Include the Following:

Purchase of components for the distribution and storage system from a list of selected/preferred vendors. Procurement of specific components based on preferred part numbers, listed for each vendor where applicable. Procurement of other components, such as valves, pumps, filters, instruments, and tanks etc. using approved specifications.

Inspection of all components of the system on receipt for compliance and/or damage. Verification of the bill of lading using a detailed list of purchased parts.

The qualification of welding procedures in accordance with a detailed list of set-up parameters for:

  1. Receiving, fabrication, cutting, end facing, and welding to be performed in a clean area devoid of any equipment previously used on “carbon” steels.
  2. Tubing specification, heat lot, diameter to be used for the specific application:
    • Check tubing for crushing and confirm the nominal diameter.
    • Examine interior and exterior of tubing for physical damage, scratches (interior) dirt or grease.
    • Clean tubing of grease and dirt using an approved cleaning solvent isopropyl alcohol (IPA) and lint free cloth. Repeat cleaning until lint free cloth is visually free of grease or dirt.
  3. Length of tubing and facing specifications. Cut tubing to length and finish with a square deburred butt end or as recommended by the fusion welding equipment supplier.

11.11.2.1 Cleaning

  • Place a lint free cloth in tubing prior to facing and/or deburring.
  • Use only Isopropyl alcohol as a cleaner solvent for SS and other appropriate solvent for PVDF. Use sparingly and ensure that all traces have evaporated prior to bagging.
  • Use only lint free cloths such as “Texwipe.”
  • Clean both inside and outside of tube ends and do not touch the end area with bare hands or soiled material.

11.11.2.2 Protection

  1. Bag the prepared tubing or the ends of the tubing with clear plastic bags and tape the bag to the tubing if the tubing is not to be welded within two hours or if the tube is to be welded outside the clean fabrication area.
  2. Weld fittings, elbows, tees, etc. should be examined similar to the tubing and cleaned of all dirt or grease prior to welding. All weld fittings should be received, sealed in a plastic or other lint free, non-metallic sealed bag.
  3. The fitting should remain in the bag until just prior to welding or for inspection following receipt.

Valves and instruments, etc. should be procured with tri-clamp or suitable sanitary type fittings and mating fittings should be welded onto the tubing in accordance with the system design.

Welding of tubing, weld fittings, tri-clamp, or suitable sanitary type fittings should be performed inside the fabrication cleanroom, using TIG orbital welding for SS throughout and PVDF fusion machine for PVDF.

Purging of each assembly with Argon Purge gas should be completed for all SS prior to welding. The gas should be 99.999% pure and contain less than 1 ppm of moisture or 2 ppm of oxygen.

The complete removal of all air should be accomplished prior to commencing welding.

Welder qualifications should be performed for all welders and re-qualification at the commencement of each work-day and/or the change to a different diameter.

Tutorial Commentary

施工品質控管的完整鏈條

ISPE 把配管施工視為一個完整的品質鏈條，每一環都不能斷裂：

  1. 採購控管：使用核准供應商清單（AVL）與指定料號
  2. 到貨檢驗：逐件比對 Bill of Lading
  3. 清潔準備：IPA 清洗 + 無塵布擦拭
  4. 保護措施：2 小時內未焊接必須封袋
  5. 焊接環境：潔淨室內操作，禁止碳鋼設備
  6. 保護氣體：99.999% 純度氬氣
  7. 人員資格：每日開工前須重新認證

為什麼不能混用碳鋼設備？

碳鋼微粒如果殘留在不鏽鋼管內壁，會形成電偶腐蝕（Galvanic Corrosion）的起始點。即使是肉眼不可見的微量碳鋼碎屑，也能在高純水環境中引發 rouge（紅鏽）形成，進而污染整個水系統。

這就是為什麼 ISPE 要求施工區域完全隔離碳鋼作業。

比喻：就像手術室的無菌操作

高純水配管施工就像外科手術：管件是「器官」，焊接區是「手術室」，氬氣是「無菌環境」，IPA 清潔是「消毒擦拭」。外科醫師不會用碰過地面的手術刀，同理，施工人員也不能用接觸過碳鋼的工具。

2 小時的封袋時限就像手術器械的有效無菌期 -- 超過時間就必須重新準備。

氬氣純度要求 -- 99.999%

這個「五個 9」的純度要求代表：

  • 水分 < 1 ppm
  • 氧氣 < 2 ppm

使用液態氬氣（Dewar 瓶）比高壓鋼瓶更佳，因為液態氬氣在蒸發過程中自然達到極高純度。搭配 Nanochem 等氣體純化器可將雜質降至 ppb 等級。

11.11.3 Clean Preparation Area

A cleanroom/trailer should be used to ensure that welding, bending, and fabrication of high purity water piping are not contaminated with metal or non-metallic particulates.

The area should be filtered to control particulates with limited controlled access. The following procedures should be followed:

  • All cutting and welding equipment should be cleaned of any impurities or particulates.
  • The room should be maintained clean at all times and access should be across sticky floor mats.
  • All piping and fittings should be cleaned prior to and following welding.
  • Assemblies should be bagged following welding and the bags removed just prior to making field welds.
  • Welding and fitting technicians should use overshoes and cleanroom gloves. The gloves are worn to prevent finger print oils from getting onto the pipe.

The above procedures are currently typical for a high purity piping system for the semi-conductor industry and is considered advisable for pharmaceutical installations with similar requirements.

"Inspection of completed welds is sometimes not possible or practical. Remote borescope inspection is limiting and often not precise. Common practice and the practice recommended is to provide a validated process for all welds, a precise instrument to make the welds, an inert environment around the weld and a clean contact surface."

Skin oils and metal particulates will contaminate the surfaces of the mating tubing. The mating surfaces must be cleaned and maintained clean through the welding process to ensure a contamination free weld and therefore a validated weld.

The use of a clean area and clean area procedures will protect the welds and reduce the defect incidents associated with contaminates in the welding areas. (This approach has been shown to be cost effective by the cost sensitive semi-conductor industry.)

Spot checks of the outside and inside surfaces on a percentage or time basis will provide assurance of the system efficiency.

Tutorial Commentary

「驗證焊接」的概念（Validated Weld）

ISPE 的核心理念是：與其事後逐一檢查每條焊縫，不如驗證整個焊接製程。如果以下條件都受到控制：

  • 焊接機台參數已驗證
  • 焊接環境潔淨受控
  • 材料表面清潔
  • 保護氣體純度達標
  • 焊工通過每日資格認證

則可合理推定產出的焊縫品質一致且合格。這與製藥業的 Process Validation 思維完全一致。

半導體業的啟示

ISPE 特別引用半導體業的做法並非偶然。半導體製程對潔淨度的要求甚至比製藥更嚴格，而他們已經證明潔淨施工區是降低成本的有效方法 -- 因為減少了返工、報廢和品質問題。

製藥業可以直接借鏡這套成熟做法，不需要自己摸索。

為什麼手指油脂是大敵？

人體皮膚油脂（主要是脂肪酸和角鯊烯）在焊接高溫下會分解產生碳化物。這些碳化物會：

  • 嵌入焊縫金屬中形成夾渣
  • 破壞鈍化膜的形成
  • 成為微生物附著的起點

戴手套不只是「習慣」，而是焊縫品質的基本保障。

11.11.4 Joints Using Fusion Butt Welding

Orbital welding is the recommended industry standard for high purity water systems in biotech, pharmaceutical and semi-conductor industries. This is due to the smooth inner weld bead that is characteristic of this joining process.

11.11.4.1 Orbital Welding

Orbital welding is a welding method used for joining stainless steel piping and tubing with electric arc in a welding machine not using any filler (welding rod) under inert atmosphere argon (Ar). Electric power characteristics are displayed on the control panel instruments and, if required, a printout of the welding parameters can be obtained.

11.11.4.2 Borescoping

A borescope is a fiber optic instrument with a monitor (TV screen and optional video tape recorder) used for inspecting the weld inside the pipe or tubing. The fiber-glass cable is a maximum 20 ft. long with a light and lens at the end. Borescoping is done for verification of the weld quality.

With the control of the welding process and the processing area, borescope inspection of the inside of the weld is only considered necessary as a means of control and is recommended as part of the qualifying process for the weld size and daily restart checks.

"Video recording each weld is considered un-necessary and difficult to justify."

11.11.5 High Tech. Orbital Tube and Pipe Welding for Stainless Steel

Semi-automatic, programmable, orbital, inert gas purged welding equipment for both tube and pipe is available from many manufacturers. The basic principals and techniques are the same for all commercial machines.

The system is an automatic “Heliarc” or “TIG” (Tungsten Inert Gas) process where Inert Argon gas from a cryogenic or low temperature source is used to protect the molten steel from oxidation during the metal fusion.

Liquid argon in Dewers is preferred due to its high purity at 99.999%. A gas purifier such as the Nanochem should be used during purging to bring the contaminants down to the ppb levels which is preferred.

The above equipment will weld stainless steels, nickel based alloys, titanium, and aluminum.

11.11.5.1 Accessories

Welding accessories are available to assist in set-up, alignment of pipe butts, purging, fitting alignment, pre-tacking fixtures, and finishing of the pipe ends in preparation for welding.

Tutorial Commentary

為什麼軌道焊接是業界標準？

軌道焊接（Orbital Welding）之所以成為高純水系統的首選，有三個關鍵原因：

  • 無填料焊接（Autogenous）：不使用焊條，避免引入外來材料
  • 機器控制：焊接參數（電流、速度、脈衝）由程式精確控制，排除人為變異
  • 內壁品質：特徵性的平滑焊道（smooth inner weld bead），不會形成微生物聚集的凹凸面

TIG 焊接原理簡述

TIG = Tungsten Inert Gas，也叫 GTAW（Gas Tungsten Arc Welding）：

  • 鎢電極產生電弧，熔化管壁母材
  • 氬氣覆蓋焊接區域，隔絕空氣中的氧氣和水分
  • 管內同步通入背面保護氣（Purge Gas）
  • 焊接頭圍繞管件360° 旋轉（這就是 “Orbital” 的含義）

Borescope 檢查的定位

注意 ISPE 的立場：在製程驗證完備的前提下，borescope 檢查不是每條焊縫必須做的，而是用於：

  • 每日開工前的首件確認
  • 更換管徑時的資格認證
  • 製程問題的抽檢追蹤

錄影記錄每條焊縫被認為「不必要且難以正當化」-- 這是一個風險導向的務實立場。

11.11.6 Weld Criteria & Welder Qualification 11.11.6 焊接標準與焊工資格

Original Text

11.11.6 Weld Criteria

  • Weld quality must meet the strictest standards.
  • All welds must be fully penetrated around the entire weld perimeter with no crevices or entrapment sites. These areas are particularly vulnerable to crevice corrosion.
  • All welds should be smooth, uniform, complete and flat, not concave, on the outside.
  • The weld should have a uniform and complete weld bead width on the inside with little or no convexity.
  • The inner weld bead should contain no concavity.
  • There should be no visible signs of oxidation/discoloration of the inner weld.
  • The joints should be square facing and properly aligned. Tubing surfaces should not be offset in any plane or direction by more than +/-0.003 in.
  • The weld width should be nominal 1/8 in. wide.

11.11.6.1 Welder Qualification

Each welder performing welding on certain metals must be qualified for this type of metal and welding. The American Society of Mechanical Engineers (ASME) and the American Welding Society (AWS) have welding procedures for welder qualifications. Each qualified welder should be formally qualified with a certificate.

As a general rule, welders and welding procedures are qualified to ASME B 31.3 (chemical plant and petroleum refinery piping), that incorporates ASME Section IX by reference.

11.11.6.2 Common Test Methods for Installed Piping

Test methods on installed piping are: pressure testing, verification of slope and the last step of passivation requiring the testing of the final rinse water.

Tutorial Commentary

為什麼內壁凹陷不可接受？

焊道內壁凹陷（suck-back / concavity）會造成：

  • 管壁厚度在焊縫處減薄，降低結構強度
  • 凹陷處形成死水區域，微生物容易附著
  • CIP 清洗液無法有效接觸凹陷底部

理想的焊縫內壁應該是微微凸起或完全平齊。

焊工認證體系

ASME B31.3 是化工和石油精煉廠配管標準，引用 ASME Section IX（焊接和釬焊資格標準）。焊工認證的重點：

  • 每位焊工需持有特定金屬種類和焊接方式的證書
  • 每日開工前需進行試焊和 borescope 確認
  • 更換管徑需重新資格認證
  • 這套體系確保的是人員 + 程序 + 設備三位一體的品質保證

焊接品質八大標準 -- 背記重點

11.11.7 Weld Defect Catalog 11.11.7 焊接缺陷目錄

Original Text

11.11.7 Weld Defects Examples

11.11.7.1 Joint Misalignment

Poor alignment of joint caused by equipment or procedure failure.

Remedy: Follow GMP welding procedures / use tube alignment gauge.

11.11.7.2 Lack of Penetration

Weld bead does not completely penetrate the ID of tube caused by poor setting of weld program parameters, material thickness and/or composition did not allow for complete penetration or improper welding amperage caused by power fluctuations.

Remedy: Develop proper welding parameters / adjust program to compensate for thickness or material / adjust for amperage fluctuation.

11.11.7.3 Excessive Penetration

Weld bead over penetrates causing excessive concavity or spikes caused by incorrect weld program (too hot), excessive welding amperage caused by power fluctuations or material thickness and/or composition which caused excessive penetration.

Remedy: Develop proper welding parameters / adjust program / use dedicated circuit.

11.11.7.4 Lack of I.D. Purge

Discoloration/oxidization in the heat effected zone caused by a lack of ID purge or the use of an impure purge gas. Sugaring of the weld bead caused by a lack of ID purge.

Remedy: Monitor the purge-flow at the weld site and ensure the hose is not pinched.

11.11.7.5 Interrupted Purge

A short segment of discoloration/oxidation and excessive penetration caused by a momentary purge gas pressure drop commonly caused by a pinched gas line.

Remedy: Monitor purge-flow and ensure the gas hose is not pinched.

11.11.7.6 Purge Gas Purity Problems

Coloration/oxidation along the weld area caused by impurities in the weld purge gas or in or on the metal surface.

Remedy: Eliminate purge and surface impurities and check for excessive concentrations of oxygen and moisture.

11.11.7.7 Heat Tint/Haze

Light coloration/haze along weld area caused by minor impurities in weld purge gas or metal surface.

Remedy: Eliminate impurities in weld zone.

11.11.7.8 Oxidation/Discoloration

Discoloration ranging from blue/brown haze to dark blue with black edges caused by oxygen contaminated purge gas, incomplete sealing, or insufficient pre-purge.

Remedy: Ensure the complete sealing of the purged line and monitor the purge flow and purity.

11.11.7.9 Insufficient Tie-In

Weld on OD is good, but the ID is incomplete and does not overlap weld start caused by incorrect speed or time in the weld program. (Uncalibrated weld head.)

Remedy: Use high quality welding equipment and check for appropriate procedures.

11.11.7.10 Electrical Current Fluctuations

Significant narrowing of ID weld bead width and/or lack of penetration of tube ID in affected areas caused by electrical power fluctuations (not using a dedicated circuit).

Remedy: Ensure that a dedicated circuit is used.

11.11.7.11 Inclusions/Dross (non-metallic substance)

Non-metallic formation on ID weld caused by tungsten inclusion.

Remedy: Use high quality base material for welding rod.

11.11.7.12 Pinholing

Small holes in the weld bead caused by the weld puddle being cooled too rapidly.

Remedy: Ramp (feather) the welding amperage properly and provide post purge.

11.11.7.13 Porosity

Metallic or non-metallic impurities trapped in the weld bead (tungsten or slag) caused by the introduction of impurities from outside or from the weld material.

Remedy: Eliminate impurities from outside or from the metal.

11.11.7.14 Cracking

Cracks in either the surface of the weld caused by brittle metal or use of a very hot weld program with rapid cooling.

Remedy: Use only good quality materials and acceptable welding programs. Do not quench or cool the weld.

Tutorial Commentary

最常見的問題：保護氣體

14 種缺陷中有 5 種（36%）直接與保護氣體相關。這告訴我們：

  • 氬氣的供應、純度、流量監控是焊接品質的第一道防線
  • 氣管折彎是現場最常見的低級錯誤 -- 簡單但致命
  • 投資氣體純化器（如 Nanochem）的投資報酬率很高

「Sugaring」-- 最嚴重的缺陷之一

Sugaring（糖化）是指焊縫內壁出現粗糙、多孔的氧化物堆積，外觀像砂糖。它代表焊接時完全沒有有效的內部保護氣體。Sugaring 焊縫是完全不可接受的，因為：

  • 粗糙表面提供大量微生物附著點
  • 氧化物會持續剝落污染水質
  • CIP 無法清洗乾淨

變色判斷法則

焊縫變色是保護氣問題的「溫度計」：

  • 無色/金色：保護良好（合格）
  • 淡藍色/霧化：微量雜質，邊界情況
  • 深藍色 + 黑邊：嚴重氧化，不合格
  • 白色/灰色砂糖狀：完全無保護，報廢

現場排查練習

情境：你發現連續三條焊縫的內壁都出現不規則的變色帶。根據缺陷目錄，請列出你的排查順序和每一步要檢查什麼？（提示：從 11.11.7.4 到 11.11.7.8 依序分析）

11.11.8 High Tech. Orbital Tube and Pipe Welding for PVDF Piping

Semi automatic fusion welding machines are available for orbital welding of PVDF piping.

An elastic pressure element backs up the inside of the fusion zone, totally avoiding an inner bead.

Tutorial Commentary

PVDF 的優勢場景

PVDF 管件不會有 rouge（紅鏽）問題，且耐化學腐蝕。特別適合：

  • 常溫或中溫（≤90°C）PW 系統
  • 使用臭氧消毒的系統

11.11.9 Remedial Action for Defective Welds 11.11.9 缺陷焊接的矯正措施

Original Text

11.11.9 Remedial Action for Defective Welds – SS or PVDF Piping

Rewelding or weld repairs are not acceptable.

Weld Replacements

The following is a procedure for replacing defective welds:

  1. Cut out rejected weld using a band or cut-off saw.
  2. Where possible, retain section for further inspection.
  3. Prepare faces of tubing and check for signs of discoloration.
  4. Ensure that all traces of any heat effected areas are removed.
  5. Cut and shape pipe ends using proprietary facing equipment.
  6. Debur and reweld.
  7. Assign a “R” suffix to weld number.

Tutorial Commentary

絕對原則：不修補，只替換

這是高純水焊接的鐵律：不合格的焊縫必須整段切除重焊，不允許在原焊縫上修補。原因：

  • 修補焊接會在同一區域形成多重熱影響區（HAZ）
  • 重複加熱改變金屬微觀結構，降低耐蝕性
  • 修補焊縫的內壁品質幾乎不可能達到標準

替換焊縫的 7 步驟流程

ISPE 規定的標準流程是一個完整的品質循環：

  • 步驟 a-b：切除 + 保留樣品（用於根因分析）
  • 步驟 c-d：確認 HAZ 完全移除（變色區域必須全部切掉）
  • 步驟 e-f：重新準備管端 + 按標準程序重焊
  • 步驟 g：追蹤標記 -- 替換焊縫加「R」後綴，確保完整的焊縫履歷

為什麼要保留被切除的焊縫？

就像醫院保留切除的組織做病理分析一樣，保留不合格焊縫可以：

  • 進行根因分析（是參數問題？氣體問題？材料問題？）
  • 作為焊工再培訓的教學樣品
  • 累積缺陷數據，優化焊接程式
11.11.10 Joints Using Sanitary Clamps 11.11.10 衛生夾管接頭

Original Text

11.11.10 Joints Using Sanitary Clamps

The sanitary clamp system of quick disconnect fittings are designed to provide a smooth non-contacting or non-corrosive environment.

The sanitary joints may be cleaned in place, provide leak tight connections and may be adapted to other forms of piping.

These joints should be used for all distribution pipe connections to valves, sensor housings, and fittings not adaptable to orbital fusion welding.

Tutorial Commentary

Sanitary Clamp 的適用場景

Sanitary clamp（衛生卡箍）用於需要定期拆卸維護的連接點：

  • 閥門連接（需定期更換膜片）
  • 感測器外殼（需定期校準/更換）
  • 取樣點（需定期消毒）
  • 無法進行軌道焊接的管件連接

設計原則：能焊接的地方用焊接（永久、無死角），不能焊接的地方才用 clamp。

Clamp 接頭的維護要點

  • 墊片（gasket）是唯一消耗件，需定期檢查和更換
  • EPDM 墊片適合熱水，PTFE 適合臭氧環境
  • 安裝時確保墊片居中，避免突入管內形成死角
  • clamp 扭矩要適當 -- 過鬆漏水，過緊壓壞墊片

Section Summary 章節摘要

Key Takeaways

  • Distribution Design: Natural drainage (min 1/16 in./ft slope), dead leg control (≤6 diameters), sanitary diaphragm valves at 60° to vertical, tri-clamp connections throughout.
  • Fabrication Quality Chain: Approved vendors → incoming inspection → clean area fabrication → IPA cleaning → 2-hour bagging rule → orbital welding in cleanroom → 99.999% Ar purge.
  • Orbital Welding: TIG/autogenous process, no filler metal, programmable parameters, the industry standard for high purity water systems.
  • Weld Criteria: Full penetration, no concavity, no oxidation, ≤±0.003 in. misalignment, 1/8 in. bead width. Qualified per ASME B31.3 / Section IX.
  • 14 Weld Defects: 36% are purge-gas related. No repair allowed — cut out and replace with “R” suffix tracking.
  • PVDF Welding: Thermal fusion with elastic backup element, no inner bead, controlled pressure melt.
  • Glossary: 60+ definitions and 70+ abbreviations covering water treatment, microbiology, instrumentation, and regulatory terminology.

本章總結

三大核心收穫

  1. 施工即驗證：ISPE 的理念是透過控制整個施工製程（環境、材料、人員、設備、參數）來確保品質，而非依賴事後逐一檢查每條焊縫。
  2. 零容忍修補：不合格焊縫只能切除重焊，不允許修補。這個看似嚴苛的要求，實際上是最經濟的做法 -- 因為修補焊縫永遠無法達到原始品質。
  3. 術語是溝通基石：掌握這些標準術語和縮寫，才能與設備供應商、工程顧問、法規審查員順暢溝通。中英對照的掌握尤其重要。
