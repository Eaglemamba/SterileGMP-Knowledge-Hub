5.1 Introduction

This chapter discusses the final treatment technologies and basic system configurations related to the manufacturing process of USP Purified Water and non-compendial water.

Various system configurations are presented, and reflect a significant shift from ion exchange based systems to membrane based systems. Equipment and system materials, surface finish and other design factors are discussed to promote the use of Good Engineering Practice for proper selection of components, piping, instrumentation, and controls.

USP Purified Water and non-compendial water can be produced by an almost unlimited combination of unit processes in various configurations. The most common pretreatment and final treatment technologies used in purified water production are shown in Figure 5-1, Figure 5-2, and Table 5-4 at the end of the chapter.

Ion exchange based systems were the dominant systems for decades in purified water production and are still successfully utilized in facilities today. The last decade has seen the growth of reverse osmosis membrane based systems increase to the point where over 90% of new systems employ primary reverse osmosis, with final polishing by continuous electrodeionization, ion exchange, or a second reverse osmosis stage.

Membrane based systems usage has increased due to chemical consumption reduction, contaminant rejection (ionized solids, organics, colloids, microbes, endotoxins, and suspended solids), reduced maintenance, consistent operation, and effective lifecycle cost.

Many material selections are made erroneously to conform to cGMP requirements that do not actually dictate the details of construction for most final treatment components. Good Engineering Practice should be employed to optimize the system for consistent operation to specifications and lifecycle cost optimization.

Commentary / 教學解析

導論重點

產業趨勢轉變

這一章最重要的資訊之一：超過 90% 的新系統已採用逆滲透（RO）為主要處理技術，搭配 CEDI、IX 或第二段 RO 進行拋光。離子交換不再是主流首選。

膜基系統為何崛起？

  • 減少化學品使用 — 不需酸鹼再生
  • 多元汙染物去除 — 離子、有機物、膠體、微生物、內毒素
  • 維護成本降低 — 操作更穩定
  • 生命週期成本更佳

比喻：GEP vs. 過度設計

就像買車不需要每個零件都用賽車規格一樣，水系統也不是每個元件都要用到最高等級的表面處理（如電拋光）。ISPE 強調 Good Engineering Practice：根據每個元件的功能、位置、微生物控制需求來選擇「足夠好」的規格，而不是盲目追求最高標準。

Compendial vs. Non-Compendial

本章不刻意區分藥典水（如 USP PW）與非藥典水（製程用水等）。實務上，非藥典水的製造與驗證方式通常與藥典水一致。

5.2 Ion Exchange (IX)

5.2.1 Description

Cation and anion exchange resins are regenerated with acid and caustic solutions, respectively. As water passes through the ion exchange bed, the exchange of ions in the water stream for the hydrogen and hydroxide ions, held by the resin, occurs readily and is driven by concentration. Thus, the regeneration process is driven by excess chemical concentrations.

A two-bed ion exchange system includes both cation and anion resin tanks. Two-bed ion exchange systems often function as the workhorse of a strictly deionization (DI) water system in terms of salt removal. Mixed-bed ion exchange systems are typically used as a secondary or "polishing" system.

Ion exchange resins are available in on-site and off-site regenerable systems. On-site regeneration requires chemical handling and disposal, but allows for internal process control and microbial control. Off-site regeneration can be accomplished through new resin to be used one time, or through repeated regeneration of the existing resin.

5.2.2 Application

The major purpose of ion exchange equipment in USP purified water systems is to satisfy the conductivity requirements of the USP. Deionization (DI) systems are often used alone or in conjunction with reverse osmosis to produce USP Purified Water. Typical ion exchange systems do not effectively remove other contaminants noted in the USP purified water specification.

5.2.3 Pretreatment Requirements

Ion exchange systems require pretreatment to remove undissolved solids from the water stream and to avoid resin fouling or degradation. Although dechlorination is also recommended to avoid resin degradation by oxidation, the low levels of chlorine commonly found in most potable water supplies normally demonstrate only long-term effects on most ion exchange resins.

5.2.5 Advantages and Disadvantages

Advantages:

  • Simple design and maintenance
  • Flexible in water flow production
  • Good upset recovery
  • Low capital cost for single train DI systems
  • Removes ionizable substances (ammonia, carbon dioxide, and some organics)

Disadvantages:

  • High cost of operations on high TDS in-feed-water
  • Requires chemical handling for on-site regenerable DI (safety and environmental issues)
  • Full on-site DI system can take significantly more floor space
  • Off-site DI systems will require outside service and significant costs for regeneration services
  • Off-site regeneration involves loss of control over the use, handling, and care of DI vessels
  • DI vessels are excellent places for microbial growth to occur between regenerations

5.2.6 Sanitization

All ion exchange resins can be sanitized chemically with various agents. Chemical cleaners include peracetic acid, sodium hypochlorite, and others. Some resins are capable of hot water sanitizations at temperatures between 65°C to 85°C. Ion exchange resins suitable for limited thermal sanitizations include: strong acid cation resin, and standard polystyrene cross-linked with divinylbenzene Type 1 strong base resin.

離子交換（IX）解析

IX 核心原理

離子交換的基本概念很簡單：

  • 陽離子樹脂：用酸（H+）再生 → 將水中的 Na+、Ca2+ 等替換為 H+
  • 陰離子樹脂：用鹼（OH-）再生 → 將水中的 Cl-、SO42- 等替換為 OH-
  • H+ + OH- = H2O → 產出純水

Two-Bed vs. Mixed-Bed

  • Two-Bed（雙床式）：陽、陰樹脂分別在不同槽中，為系統「主力」
  • Mixed-Bed（混床式）：陽、陰樹脂混合在同一槽中，做為「拋光」步驟，可達到更高純度

On-Site vs. Off-Site 再生

項目廠內再生（On-Site）廠外再生（Off-Site）
化學品需酸鹼儲存與處理不需
微生物控制可自行管控由外部服務商處理
資本成本高（需再生設備）低
操作成本較低較高（服務費）
品質控制完全掌控依賴供應商

IX 最大隱患：微生物滋長

DI 樹脂槽在再生之間是微生物生長的絕佳場所。這是離子交換系統最關鍵的劣勢之一，也是膜系統逐漸取代 IX 的重要原因。

比喻：離子交換像自助洗衣機

樹脂就像洗衣機的濾網，會「吸附」水中的離子雜質。用了一段時間需要「再生」（就像清洗濾網），可以自己洗（on-site）或送外面洗（off-site）。但濾網放太久不清洗，就會長黴菌 — 這就是 IX 的微生物風險。

消毒方式選擇

化學消毒（過氧乙酸、次氯酸鈉）適用所有樹脂；熱水消毒（65-85°C）僅適用於特定類型。選購樹脂時需確認耐熱規格。

5.3 Continuous Electrodeionization (CEDI)

5.3.1 Description

Electrodeionization removes ionized or ionizable species from water using electrically active media and an electrical potential to effect ion transport. It is distinguished from electrodialysis by the use of electrically active media, and from other ion exchange processes by the use of an electrical potential.

The continuous electrodeionization (CEDI) process is continuous rather than batch or intermittent. CEDI devices typically comprise semi-permeable ion exchange membranes, permanently charged media, and a DC electrical field.

A CEDI cell has alternating ion depleting (purifying) and ion concentrating cells. Water is purified through ion transfer: ionized species are drawn from the purifying cells into the concentrate stream.

The water purified in CEDI units passes only through the electrically charged ion exchange media, and not through the ion exchange membranes. The membranes are permeable to ionized species, but not to water.

As the ionic strength of the purified water decreases, high voltage gradient at the water-ion exchange media interfaces causes water decomposition to H+ and OH-. These ions continuously regenerate the ion exchange media — allowing production of 1 to 18 MΩ-cm quality water.

5.3.2 Application

CEDI units may be utilized downstream of RO units for USP Purified Water or non-compendial water production. For USP WFI water, CEDI units are utilized upstream of RO units.

5.3.3 Limitations

CEDI cannot remove all contaminants; the principal mechanism is for ionized or ionizable species. A concentrate stream is always required. Most CEDI units operate between 10-40°C (50-104°F).

5.3.5 Performance

Ionized solids reduction is generally greater than 99%, allowing 1-18 MΩ-cm quality water from RO feed. Organic rejection varies from 50% to 95%. UV light (185 nm) upstream can substantially increase organic rejection. CO2 is converted to bicarbonate and removed. Silica removal: 80-95%.

5.3.7 Advantages and Disadvantages

Advantages:

  • Attainment of Stage 1 conductivity
  • Elimination of chemical handling
  • Elimination of outside service (off-site regenerated resin)
  • Electric field provides some bacterial control
  • Removal of ionizable substances (CO2, ammonia, some organics)

Disadvantages:

  • Does not remove non-ionic contaminants
  • Unique designs for each manufacturer (modules not interchangeable)
  • May require UV, sub-micron filtration, or RO for further bacterial reduction
  • May require RO pretreatment
  • Rinse up after chemical sanitization may take hours to reach peak resistivity and TOC

5.3.8 Sanitization

CEDI units are typically chemically sanitized with agents including: peracetic acid, sodium percarbonate, sodium hydroxide, hydrogen peroxide, and others.

Commentary / 教學解析

連續電去離子（CEDI）解析

CEDI 運作原理（簡化版）

  1. 水通過充填離子交換樹脂的「淨化室」
  2. 直流電場將離子從淨化室拉向「濃縮室」
  3. 離子穿過離子交換膜進入濃縮室，隨廢水排出
  4. 水純度越高，電壓梯度越大，水分解產生 H+ 和 OH-
  5. H+ / OH- 持續再生樹脂 — 所以叫「連續」電去離子

比喻：CEDI 像自動充電的淨水器

傳統 IX 樹脂用完需要「充電」（酸鹼再生），但 CEDI 透過電場讓水自己分解產生 H+/OH-，相當於「自動充電」，永遠不需要停機再生。這是 CEDI 最大的優勢。

關鍵數據

  • 離子去除率 > 99% — 可產出 1-18 MΩ-cm 純水
  • 有機物去除 50-95% — 變異很大，需看有機物種類
  • SiO2 去除 80-95%
  • 操作溫度 10-40°C — 無法熱水消毒

CEDI 的位置取決於水的等級

  • PW 系統：RO → CEDI（CEDI 做拋光）
  • WFI 系統：CEDI → RO（CEDI 做前處理，RO 做最終屏障）

這個位置差異反映了對微生物控制的不同要求。WFI 需要更嚴格的微生物屏障，RO 膜比 CEDI 更能有效阻隔微生物和內毒素。

模組不可互換

各家 CEDI 製造商（如 Ionpure、E-Cell）的模組設計專屬，不像 RO 膜那樣有統一尺寸規格。這意味著一旦選定供應商，就存在一定程度的「綁定」，在採購策略上需要考慮。

思考題

如果你的進水 TOC 偏高（> 2 ppm），CEDI 的有機物去除率只有 50-95%，你會怎麼強化系統設計？

提示：原文提到 185 nm UV 放在 CEDI 上游可大幅提升有機物去除。

5.4 Reverse Osmosis (RO)

5.4.1 Description

Reverse osmosis (RO) is a pressure driven process utilizing a semi-permeable membrane capable of removing dissolved organic and inorganic contaminants from water. The membrane is permeable to water while being impermeable to many salts, acids, bases, colloids, bacteria, and endotoxins.

RO membranes are produced commercially in a spiral wound configuration for pharmaceutical water production. Membranes are available in two basic materials: cellulose acetate and thin film composite (polyamide).

5.4.2 Application

RO can be utilized upstream of regenerable or off-site regenerated deionizers to reduce chemical consumption. Two-pass RO units (product staged) with proper pH control are generally capable of producing water that meets USP requirements for TOC and conductivity.

5.4.3 Limitations

RO cannot remove 100% of contaminants and has very low to no removal capacity for extremely low molecular weight dissolved organics. Carbon dioxide passes directly through the RO membrane and may increase product conductivity beyond USP Stage 1 limit.

A concentrate flow is always necessary. Many users reuse the RO waste stream for cooling tower make-up, compressor cooling water, etc.

5.4.5 Performance

A single stage of RO typically reduces salts, colloids, organics, bacteria, and endotoxin by 90 to 99%. Single stage RO does not normally meet USP without further purification. Some two-pass units produce water meeting USP 24 Stage 1 conductivity requirements.

5.4.6 Advantages and Disadvantages

Advantages:

  • Eliminates or significantly reduces chemical handling relative to IX
  • More effective microbial control than IX
  • Integrity testing by salt challenge and differential conductivity
  • Removes wide variety of contaminants: ionized solids, non-ionic materials (colloids, bacteria, endotoxin, some organics)

Disadvantages:

  • Higher water consumption than IX (unless wastewater is reused)
  • Energy consumption higher than IX, less than distillation
  • No removal of dissolved gases (CO2 and ammonia)

5.4.8 Waste Water Reuse

RO wastewater is frequently used as cooling tower make-up, non-contact cooling, or re-purified in a wastewater RO unit. The wastewater from the second pass of a two-pass RO is normally returned to the feed of the first pass.

5.4.9 Sanitization

All RO membranes can be sanitized with chemical agents that vary by membrane type. Specially constructed membranes are available for hot water sanitization at 60° to 80°C.

逆滲透（RO）解析

RO 核心原理

利用高壓將水推過半透膜。膜允許水分子通過，但阻擋鹽類、有機物、膠體、細菌、內毒素等。RO 是最「全面」的單一處理技術，可同時去除多種汙染物。

兩種膜材比較重點

特性醋酸纖維素（CA）聚醯胺/薄膜複合（TFC）
去除率90-98%97-99%（更高）
氯耐受性1.0 mg/L0.05 mg/L（極低）
抗菌性差好
熱消毒最高 30°C50-80°C
pH 範圍4-7（窄）2-11（寬）

實務上 TFC 膜已成為主流選擇。CA 膜唯一優勢是耐氯，但 TFC 在幾乎所有其他性能上都更優。

CO2 是 RO 的盲點

二氧化碳是溶解氣體，RO 膜完全無法阻擋。CO2 會影響產水電導度，可能超過 USP Stage 1 限值。解決方式：

  • 前處理降低 pH → CO2 脫氣
  • RO 後接 CEDI 或 IX（CO2 轉化為碳酸氫根後可被去除）
  • 脫氣膜（membrane degasifier）

比喻：RO 像一面精密篩網

想像一面極細的篩網，大部分雜質都過不去，只有水分子能通過。但像 CO2 這種「氣態小分子」就像空氣一樣能穿過篩網。這就是為什麼 RO 什麼都能擋，唯獨擋不住溶解氣體。

廢水回收策略

RO 廢水（concentrate）仍含有價值：

  • 冷卻塔補水 — 最常見的回收方式
  • 壓縮機冷卻水
  • 濾料反洗水
  • 二次 RO 處理 — 再回收一部分純水
  • 雙通 RO：第二段廢水回到第一段進水 — 標準設計

善用廢水回收可大幅改善 RO 系統最大的劣勢 — 高耗水量。

熱水消毒 RO 膜

特殊的高溫耐受 RO 膜（60-80°C）已經商業化，使 RO 系統也能執行熱水消毒，大幅簡化消毒流程。這是系統設計的重要考量 — 化學消毒後 rinse up 耗時且麻煩。

5.5 Polishing Components — Non-Ionic Contaminants Reduction

5.5.1 Ultrafiltration (UF)

5.5.1.1 Description

Ultrafiltration (UF) is a cross-flow process similar to RO. A pressurized feed stream flows parallel to a porous membrane. The membrane rejects particulates, organics, microbes, pyrogens, and other contaminants too large to pass through. UF does not reject low molecular weight ionic contaminants, as does RO.

Membranes are available in both polymeric and ceramic materials. Polymeric elements come in spiral wound and hollow fiber configurations. Ceramic modules are in single/multiple channel configurations.

5.5.1.2 Application

UF is frequently used downstream of ion exchange for organic, colloidal, microbial, and endotoxin reduction. Purified Water with low endotoxin levels (<0.25 EU/ml) is utilized for ophthalmic solutions, topicals, and bulk pharmaceutical chemicals for parenteral manufacturing.

UF is also frequently used in still feed water systems, in combination with IX, to limit endotoxin and colloidal silica feed levels to the still.

5.5.1.5 Performance

Organic molecular weight cutoffs range from 1,000 to 100,000. Endotoxin reduction varies from 2 log10 to 4 log10. UF can consistently meet USP WFI endotoxin limit of 0.25 EU/ml. Microbial reduction: 3 log10 to 4 log10. Some UF membranes operate continuously at temperatures up to 90°C.

5.5.1.6 Advantages and Disadvantages

Advantages:

  • Better endotoxin and organic removal than microfiltration
  • More effective operating costs than MF in high particle loading
  • Some UF elements tolerate steam or ozone sanitization
  • Generally less waste stream than RO
  • Less energy intensive than RO

Disadvantages:

  • Cannot remove ionic contaminants (unlike RO)
  • Generally requires a waste stream (2-10%)
  • Sometimes more difficult to integrity test than MF cartridges

5.5.1.8 Sanitization

Most polymeric membranes tolerate various chemical agents (NaOCl, H2O2, peracetic acid, NaOH). Some polymeric membranes can be hot water or even steam sanitized. Ceramic UF elements can tolerate all common agents, hot water, steam, and ozone.

5.5.1.9 Waste Water Recovery

Most pharmaceutical UF units are fed deionized water. The wastewater is still low conductivity water that can be recycled upstream to RO units or fed to boilers, cooling towers, or other uses.

超濾（UF）解析

UF 的角色定位

UF 不是用來去除離子的（那是 RO 和 IX 的工作）。UF 的專長是：

  • 內毒素（Endotoxin） — 2-4 log 去除
  • 微生物 — 3-4 log 去除
  • 有機物 — 依 MWCO 而定
  • 膠體、微粒

UF 是 IX 系統後的理想拋光步驟，彌補 IX 在微生物和內毒素方面的不足。

MWCO（分子量截留值）

UF 膜以分子量截留值（Molecular Weight Cut-Off）分類，範圍 1,000 到 100,000 Dalton。數值越低，能擋住越小的有機分子，但通量也越低。選擇時需在去除效率與系統產量之間取得平衡。

許多設施在蒸餾器前加裝 UF，目的是：

  • 降低進入蒸餾器的內毒素負荷
  • 去除膠體二氧化矽（避免結垢）
  • 延長蒸餾器維護週期

5.5.2 Microfiltration (MF)

5.5.2.1 Description

Microfiltration is a membrane process for the removal of fine particles and microorganisms. No waste stream is generally employed. Virtually all microfiltration cartridges are disposable and available in a wide range of materials and pore sizes, generally from 0.45 microns down to 0.04 microns.

Microfilters are generally employed for microbial retention downstream of components where some microbial growth may exist. Microfilters are most appropriately employed in central purified water production systems and their use is discouraged in distribution systems. The filters should not be the only microbial control unit operation — they need to be part of a comprehensive microbial control plan.

5.5.2.2 Advantages and Disadvantages

Advantages:

  • Simple design and maintenance
  • Flexible in water flow production
  • No waste stream
  • Cartridges are integrity testable
  • Heat and chemical sanitization possible

Disadvantages:

  • Can only be used as a safety net for microbial production
  • No ion or endotoxin removal
  • Shorter life due to dead head design; replacement required
  • Not recommended for use in distribution piping

5.5.2.3 Performance

Microfiltration can be as effective as UF in microbial reduction and minimizes water consumption (no waste stream). However, MF cannot reduce dissolved organic levels as UF can, and cannot remove particles as small as UF due to pore size differences.

Commentary / 教學解析

微濾（MF）解析

MF 的定位：最後一道安全網

MF 不是系統的「主力」處理，而是「安全網」（safety net）。它的功能很明確：

  • 擋住微生物和微粒
  • 不能去除離子、內毒素、溶解有機物
  • 拋棄式濾芯 — 用完就換

重要限制：不建議用於配送系統

ISPE 明確指出 MF 不應用於配送管路。原因是：

  • MF 容易成為微生物的「溫床」（dead-end 設計）
  • 配送管路應靠循環、溫度、臭氧等方式控制微生物
  • MF 只適合放在製水系統的中央產水端

這一點在實際稽核中經常被提問。

比喻：MF 像門口的紗窗

紗窗可以擋住蚊蟲（微生物），但擋不住灰塵和氣味（離子、有機物、內毒素）。它不是空調系統（主力處理），只是多一層防護。而且如果紗窗放在房子深處（配送管路），反而會變成蟲卵孵化的地方。

完整性測試（Integrity Test）

MF 濾芯的一大優勢是可以做完整性測試（如泡點測試、擴散流測試）。這讓 MF 在 GMP 環境中更容易驗證。相比之下，UF 的完整性測試通常更複雜。

5.5.3 Ultraviolet Light Treatment

5.5.3.1 Description

Ultraviolet light rays strike microorganisms (bacteria, virus, yeast, mold, or algae) and break through their outer membrane to modify the DNA, bringing about the destruction of the organism. UV radiation is a point of use application with no residual radiation characteristics. Proper prefiltration should be implemented to keep particulate from shielding organisms from UV light.

5.5.3.2 Advantages and Disadvantages

Advantages:

  • Simple design and maintenance
  • 254 nm design for microbial reduction
  • 185 nm design for TOC reduction
  • No waste stream
  • Heat, ozone, and chemical sanitization are possible

Disadvantages:

  • Can only be used as a safety net for microbial production
  • No ion or endotoxin removal
  • No disinfection residual
  • Particulate can shield organisms from UV light

5.5.3.3 Performance

UV light is used as a final treatment step to address microbial control and TOC reduction (where necessary), after deionization processes.

紫外線（UV）處理解析

兩種 UV 波長，兩種功能

波長功能機制
254 nm殺菌破壞微生物 DNA，使其無法繁殖
185 nmTOC 降解產生臭氧和自由基，氧化有機物為 CO2

系統設計時需根據目的選擇正確波長。有些設備同時提供兩種波長。

UV 的致命弱點：無殘留效果

UV 只在照射當下有效，水一離開 UV 燈就不再有消毒保護。這代表：

  • UV 下游的管路仍可能有微生物生長
  • UV 不能替代系統性的微生物控制策略
  • UV 必須搭配其他控制手段（如循環、溫度維持）

顆粒遮蔽效應

水中如果有懸浮微粒，微粒會遮擋 UV 光，使被遮蔽的微生物存活。因此 UV 前必須有適當的前過濾（prefiltration）。這也是為什麼 UV 通常放在系統最末段、其他處理之後。

思考題：UV 的最佳安裝位置

在一個典型的 RO + CEDI + UV 系統中，185 nm UV 和 254 nm UV 各自應該放在什麼位置？為什麼？

提示：回顧 CEDI 段落 — 185 nm UV 放在 CEDI 上游可提升有機物去除；254 nm UV 放在最末端做微生物控制。

Commentary / 教學解析

系統比較總覽解讀

如何閱讀這張表

這是選擇純化水系統最重要的決策參考表。關鍵看法：

  • 微生物性能：只有 Two-Pass RO 和蒸餾評為 H（高），其他都是 M 或 L
  • 化學品處理：On-Site IX 最高，膜系統最低
  • 耗水量：所有含 RO 的系統都是 H（除非回收廢水）
  • 資本成本：蒸餾最高，Off-Site IX 最低

主流組態推薦

  • RO + CEDI：現今最主流的 PW 系統組態。化學品需求低、無須外部服務、電導度可達 Stage 1
  • Two-Pass RO：微生物性能最佳（膜系統中），適合對微生物要求更嚴格的場景
  • 蒸餾：微生物性能最好，但資本和能源成本最高，通常用於 WFI

TOC 的隱藏風險

註(4)指出：純 IX 系統（無 RO）的 TOC 不一定能穩定達標，尤其當進水 TOC > 2 ppm 時。這是因為離子交換樹脂可能釋出有機物，反而增加產水 TOC。含 RO 的系統 TOC 表現則穩定在 <500 ppb。

決策演練

場景：你需要為一個新藥廠設計 PW 系統。原水 TDS 350 ppm，TOC 3 ppm。預算有限但希望長期營運成本合理。你會選哪個組態？為什麼？

考慮因素：TDS 太高不適合純 IX；TOC 高需要 RO 前處理；RO + CEDI 或 RO + IX 都是合理選擇，但 RO + CEDI 免化學品、維護更簡單。

關鍵要點

  • 產業趨勢：超過 90% 的新純化水系統以 RO 為核心，搭配 CEDI/IX/第二段 RO 做拋光
  • GEP 原則：材料規格應根據功能需求選擇，不是所有元件都需要最高等級表面處理
  • CO2 問題：RO 無法去除溶解氣體，需要後段處理（CEDI 或 IX）
  • 微生物控制：IX 系統微生物風險最高；膜系統和蒸餾表現較好
  • 廢水回收：善用 RO 廢水可顯著降低系統最大劣勢（高耗水量）
  • 消毒策略：熱水消毒比化學消毒更簡便，但需確認設備耐溫規格
  • MF 定位：僅作為安全網，不應用於配送管路，不能作為唯一微生物控制手段
  • UV 雙波長：254 nm 殺菌、185 nm 降 TOC，各有不同最佳安裝位置
