6.1 Introduction

This chapter addresses the USP approved final treatment methods for the production of compendial WFI. WFI is the purest grade of bulk water monographed by the USP and would be expected to be used for the manufacture of parenteral, some ophthalmic and inhalation products, and for finishing steps of parenteral grade active pharmaceutical ingredients (API's).

Recommended systems include either distillation or RO as the final processing step, but may also include ultrafiltration (UF), deionization (DI) and/or ion exchange (IX), to compliment the RO or distillation unit operation.

The technology, operation, maintenance, and relative cost issues for the approved process methods are discussed. This chapter includes USP monograph information, regulatory issues, and subsections that cover the unit operations:

  • Single effect (SE) distillation
  • Multi-effect (ME) distillation
  • Vapor compression (VC) distillation
  • Reverse osmosis (RO)

Feed water pretreatment is covered along with economic factors such as construction materials, surface finishes, and instrumentation and controls. A comparison table on USP-WFI final treatment options and relative attributes is provided.

導論重點

WFI 的地位

WFI（Water for Injection，注射用水）是 USP 規範中最高等級的製藥用水。用途包括：

  • 注射劑（Parenteral）製造
  • 部分眼科產品與吸入劑
  • 注射級原料藥（API）的最終步驟

簡言之：任何會直接進入人體血液或繞過人體天然防線的產品，都需要使用 WFI。

比喻：水的等級制度

如果把製藥用水比喻成醫院的空間等級：

  • 飲用水 = 醫院大廳（一般公共區域）
  • Purified Water = 一般病房（有基本管控）
  • WFI = 手術室（最嚴格的無菌管控）

WFI 必須將內毒素（Endotoxin）控制到極低水平，因為內毒素進入血液會引發致命的熱原反應。

本章四大技術

本章涵蓋四種 WFI 最終處理技術，可分為兩大類：

  • 蒸餾類：SE（單效）、ME（多效）、VC（蒸汽壓縮）
  • 膜處理類：RO（逆滲透）

蒸餾是傳統的「金標準」，RO 則是近年逐漸被接受的替代方案。

6.2 US Pharmacopoeia Issues

The United States Pharmacopoeia (USP) allows WFI to be "purified by distillation or by RO". This statement does not imply that the regulated process step is the only process step, but the USP advisory section does imply that it is the final step in the process.

  • Only distillation may be used to produce WFI under current European regulations
  • Distillation, RO and UF are allowable methods to produce WFI under the Japanese regulations

There are few regulations, which govern the design and construction of pharmaceutical water purification systems. There are no existing regulations governing materials of construction, type, or level of instrumentation, surface finish, or operating temperatures.

Among U.S. government publications, including the Code of Federal Regulations (CFR) and the FDA Guide to Inspection of High Purity Water Systems, there are few stipulations related to design and construction of WFI processing equipment. Two notable stipulations are:

  • "Heat exchangers, other than the double concentric tube type or double tube sheet type, must employ a pressure differential and a means for monitoring the differential."
  • "All stills and tanks holding liquid requiring microbial control shall have air vents with non-fiber releasing sterilizable filters capable of preventing microbial contamination of the contents."

法規要求解析

USP vs. EP vs. JP 法規差異（出版時）

重要法規更新（2017 年 EP 修訂）

本指引出版於 2001 年，當時 EP 僅允許蒸餾製備 WFI。2017 年 4 月，EP 正式修訂，也接受「蒸餾以外的等效純化方法」（包括 RO）來生產 WFI。

這是製藥水系統領域的里程碑事件，意味著全球三大藥典現在都接受膜技術作為 WFI 生產方式。

「最終步驟」的含義

USP 強調蒸餾或 RO 必須是最終步驟（final step）。這意味著：

  • 你可以在蒸餾前使用 RO 做前處理
  • 你可以在 RO 前使用 DI 做前處理
  • 但蒸餾或 RO 之後，不能再加其他處理步驟（否則該步驟就變成了「最終步驟」）

CFR 兩項硬性規定

雖然法規對設計細節很少規定，但有兩項明確要求必須遵守：

  1. 熱交換器壓差監控 — 防止冷卻水側滲入純水側（除雙管板型外）
  2. 儲罐通氣過濾 — 防止微生物從空氣進入儲水系統

這兩條看似簡單，卻是查廠時的高頻檢查點。

6.3 Distillation

The pharmaceutical still chemically and microbiologically purifies water by phase changes and entrainment separation. In this process water is evaporated, producing steam. The steam disengages from the water leaving behind dissolved solids, non-volatiles, and high molecular weight impurities. However, low molecular weight impurities are carried with the water mist/droplets, which are entrained in the steam. A separator removes fine mist and entrained impurities, including endotoxins. The purified steam is condensed into WFI.

Distillation systems are available to provide a minimum of 3 log10 (99.99%) reduction in endotoxin concentration. Specific endotoxin loading limits should be reviewed with the manufacturer.

A variety of different designs are available including single effect (SE), multi-effect (ME), and vapor compression (VC). The distilled water quality expected from an SE still is equivalent to an ME design, by virtue of the fact that water is distilled only once in both systems. The benefit to the user of ME versus SE distillation are the significantly lower operating costs associated with utilities.

In an ME system, purified steam produced by each effect is utilized to heat water and generate more steam in the subsequent effect. Due to this staged evaporation and condensation process, only the first effect requires heat from an external source, and only the purified steam produced by the final effect is condensed, using an external cooling medium.

VC stills can produce similar quality water using a different technique. Energy imparted to the generated steam, by a mechanical compressor, results in compressed steam with increased pressure and temperature. The higher energy steam is then discharged back into the evaporator/condenser vessel to generate more steam in a continuous cycle.

Areas of concern are carry over of impurities, evaporator flooding, stagnant water, and pump and compressor seal design. These concerns may be addressed using mist eliminators, high water level indicators, use of sanitary pumps and compressors, proper drainage, adequate blow down control, and conductivity sensing to divert unacceptable water to drain.

蒸餾原理解析

蒸餾純化的三步驟

  1. 蒸發（Evaporation） — 水加熱變成蒸汽，固體雜質與大分子留在液相
  2. 分離（Separation） — 蒸汽通過分離器（Separator），去除被夾帶的水霧與內毒素
  3. 冷凝（Condensation） — 純淨蒸汽冷凝回液態，即為 WFI

關鍵：蒸餾能達到 3 log（99.99%）以上的內毒素去除率，這是蒸餾在 WFI 領域長期被視為金標準的核心原因。

比喻：蒸餾 = 燒開水的升級版

家裡燒開水只是殺菌，但蒸餾更像是「只取蒸汽再冷凝」。想像一個鍋蓋上有收集裝置：

  • 鍋裡的水含有鹽、內毒素等雜質
  • 蒸汽上升時，雜質留在鍋底
  • 但有些微小水滴會被蒸汽「帶走」（entrainment） — 這就需要分離器來攔截

SE vs. ME vs. VC 快速區分

  • SE（單效）：最簡單，一次蒸發＋冷凝，能耗最高
  • ME（多效）：多段串聯，每段的蒸汽為下一段加熱，大幅節省蒸汽
  • VC（蒸汽壓縮）：用壓縮機提升蒸汽能量再循環，只需電力，不需外部蒸汽

三者產出的水質基本相同（都只蒸餾一次），差異在於能效和成本結構。

蒸餾系統的風險點

  • Carry over（夾帶） — 蒸汽帶走雜質水滴 → 需 mist eliminator
  • Flooding（溢流） — 液位過高 → 需高液位警報
  • Stagnant water（死水） — 未排盡的水滋生微生物 → 需良好排水設計
  • Seal failure（密封洩漏） — 泵浦或壓縮機密封 → 需衛生級設計

6.5.1 Single Effect Distillation (SE)

SE systems incorporate a single evaporator heat exchanger, separator mechanism, and a condenser.

SE systems are available in electrically or steam powered versions, although electrical units are limited to very small production rates (<30 gallons per hour).

Steam powered units typically require 30-60 psig plant steam. Cooling fluid is required for both steam and electric powered versions. When water is the coolant, the rate is approximately 8-10 gallons per gallon of WFI produced, based on a supply temperature of 4 - 16°C, and temperature rise of 67°C.

SE systems typically operate at atmospheric pressure and 100°C, and incorporate non-ASME code vessels. WFI is delivered at atmospheric pressure and 80 - 100°C; thus a distillate transfer pump is required, unless the WFI tank is at a lower elevation than the still.

Commentary / 教學解析

單效蒸餾解析

SE 系統特點

  • 結構最簡單：蒸發器 + 分離器 + 冷凝器，三件套
  • 常壓操作（100°C），容器不需 ASME 壓力容器認證
  • 冷卻水消耗大：每產出 1 加侖 WFI 需要 8-10 加侖冷卻水
  • 出水無壓力：需要轉送泵浦或利用位差（蒸餾器高於儲罐）

比喻：SE 像微型蒸餾壺

SE 蒸餾器就像實驗室的蒸餾燒瓶放大版 — 一次蒸發、一次冷凝、產出純水。簡單有效，但能耗高、產量小，適合 R&D 或小型製劑廠。

冷卻水消耗計算

以 SE 產出 50 GPH WFI 為例：

冷卻水需求 = 50 × 8~10 = 400~500 GPH

這意味著冷卻水成本是 SE 運行的主要費用之一。

6.5.2 Multi-Effect Distillation (ME)

ME systems incorporate two or more evaporator heat exchangers, separator mechanisms, and a condenser into a staged evaporation and condensation process. Typical systems have 3-8 effects. Each effect includes an evaporator and a separator.

ME systems typically require plant steam at 80 - 120 psig, and cooling fluid at a supply temperature of 4°C - 16°C, based on a temperature rise of 65°C - 70°C. The quantity of steam and cooling fluid required varies significantly based upon the WFI production rate and the number of effects. Capital costs increase while steam and cooling fluid consumption decrease, as the number of effects to produce a given quantity of WFI increases. ME systems operate under pressure, and typically deliver WFI at 80°C - 100°C.

Normally, water used for cooling is not the same as the feed water, and does not require special pretreatment for the purpose of scale prevention. However, corrosion prevention measures, such as chlorine and chloramine removal, are necessary.

Some designs deliver the water at atmospheric pressure and require a transfer pump unless the WFI storage tank is at lower elevation than the still. Other designs which may operate at 5-10 psig condenser pressure, do feature a distillate transfer pump for higher pressure deliveries.

Commentary / 教學解析

多效蒸餾解析

ME 核心原理：能量重複利用

ME 系統的精髓在於「級聯熱回收」：

  1. 第 1 效（Effect）使用外部蒸汽加熱進水
  2. 第 1 效產出的純蒸汽成為第 2 效的加熱源
  3. 第 2 效的蒸汽再為第 3 效加熱…以此類推
  4. 只有最後一效的蒸汽需要外部冷卻水冷凝

效數越多 → 蒸汽消耗越少 → 但設備成本越高。典型選擇 3-8 效。

效數與蒸汽經濟性

近似關係：蒸汽經濟比 ≈ 效數 × 0.8~0.9

例：6 效蒸餾器的蒸汽經濟比約 4.8~5.4，代表每投入 1 kg 蒸汽可產出約 5 kg WFI。

相較之下，SE 的蒸汽經濟比僅約 0.9~1.0。

注意：ME 需要較高壓力蒸汽

ME 需要 80-120 psig 的廠務蒸汽，遠高於 SE 的 30-60 psig。建廠前需確認鍋爐系統能否提供足夠壓力的蒸汽。

ME 的權衡取捨

增加效數優點缺點
更多效蒸汽/冷卻水消耗更低設備成本更高
更少效設備成本較低運行成本較高

最佳效數取決於蒸汽成本、產能需求與投資預算的綜合分析。

6.5.3 Vapor Compression Distillation (VC)

VC is a distillation method where water is evaporated inside, or outside, a bank of tubes arranged in a horizontal or vertical configuration. The horizontal design is normally of the forced circulation type with recirculation pump and spray nozzles, while the vertical design is of the natural circulation type.

Major system components are the evaporator, compressor, heat exchangers, deaerator, pumps, motors, valves, instruments, and controls.

The VC process operates on the same principle as the mechanical refrigeration cycle.

In a VC still, feed water is evaporated on one side of the tubes. The generated steam passes through the disengagement space, through the separator, and into the compressor.

The energy imparted by the compressor results in compressed steam with increased pressure and temperature. The higher energy steam is then discharged back into the evaporator/condenser vessel. There, the steam condenses and gives up its latent heat, which is transferred through the tube wall to the water. More water is boiled off, generating more vapor, and the process is repeated. The outgoing distillate and blow down streams preheat the incoming feed water, thus saving energy. Since the latent heat is recycled, there is no need for a stand-alone condenser as in the SE or ME systems.

Commentary / 教學解析

蒸汽壓縮蒸餾解析

VC 原理：潛熱回收循環

VC 的核心概念是壓縮機驅動的能量循環：

  1. 進水在管束上蒸發為蒸汽
  2. 蒸汽通過分離器後進入壓縮機
  3. 壓縮機提升蒸汽的壓力與溫度
  4. 高能蒸汽送回蒸發器/冷凝器，在管壁另一側冷凝
  5. 冷凝時釋放的潛熱，加熱管壁另一側的水，產生更多蒸汽
  6. 循環往復，不需要獨立冷凝器

比喻：VC 像熱泵（冷暖空調）

VC 蒸餾和家用冷暖空調的原理完全相同 — 都是「機械壓縮循環」：

  • 空調壓縮機壓縮冷媒 → VC 壓縮機壓縮水蒸汽
  • 空調的冷凝器放熱 → VC 中高壓蒸汽冷凝放熱來加熱進水
  • 能量不斷循環利用，只需投入壓縮機的電力

因此 VC 的主要能源成本是電力，而非蒸汽。

VC 獨特優勢

  • 不需獨立冷凝器 — 潛熱完全回收
  • 不需外部蒸汽（常溫出水模式）— 只靠電力
  • 可產出常溫 WFI — SE 和 ME 都只能產出熱水
  • 最大產能範圍 — 最高 6,000 GPH

VC 的挑戰

壓縮機是 VC 系統最關鍵也最脆弱的元件。壓縮機密封設計、振動管理、以及與製藥衛生要求的兼容性，是 VC 系統設計的重點關注項。

6.5.4 Distillation Pretreatment Requirements — General

All distillation units are susceptible to scaling and corrosion, if the appropriate feed water pretreatment is not provided. VC and some SE stills operate slightly above atmospheric pressure, and the removal of calcium and magnesium, by way of water softening, is normally required as a minimum. ME stills operate at a much higher pressure and temperature, and require higher quality feed water in order to prevent scaling and corrosion. Normally, ion exchange beds are employed as feed water pretreatment to a multiple effect still. RO is also used as feed water pretreatment for either the VC or multiple effect stills.

All distillation units will invariably experience some form of scale build-up and must therefore include routine visual inspections plus cleaning of the still during shutdown periods when appropriate. Both types of stills are susceptible to attack by chlorine. Chlorine removal is essential if damage is to be avoided. Activated carbon filters and sodium bisulfite injection are effective and common methods for chlorine removal.

From a microbiological perspective, the bacterial and endotoxin load should be consistently controlled to a level that does not overload the still.

6.5.5 Pretreatment Requirements — Specific

6.5.5.2 ME Still: The baseline pretreatment for ME must provide very low TDS feed water, preferably less than 10 mg/l, and less than 1 mg/l silica. Some manufacturers offer ME stills to operate on softened water. Others allow higher levels of silica, up to 5 mg/l. The pretreatment must also remove chlorine and objectionable volatiles, such as ammonia.

6.5.5.3 VC Still: The baseline pretreatment for VC stills is softening, the removal of chlorine, and other objectionable volatiles such as ammonia, if present.

6.5.5.4 Economics

a) SE Economics: Commercially available SE systems are inherently simple in design. Operating costs are associated mainly with plant steam and cooling fluid.

b) ME Economics: Opportunities for cost savings exist in the areas of construction materials, surface finishes, and instrumentation. Operating costs are associated mainly with plant steam and cooling fluid. Utilities consumption rates vary among ME manufacturers.

c) VC Economics: Significant opportunities exist to reduce capital cost associated with selection of construction materials, surface finishes, and instrumentation. Operating costs of VC systems are associated mainly with electrical power.

6.5.6 Construction Materials, Surface Finish, and Instrumentation

Recommended Construction Materials (Table 6-2)

6.5.6.1 Surface Finish

Mechanical polishing (MP), electropolishing (EP), and passivation processes are implemented in stainless steel distillation systems in order to improve corrosion resistance. These processes are neither necessary, nor applicable, to other alloys such as tin-coated copper, titanium, and Inconel.

MP and EP/passivation processes affect the microscopic amplitude and chemical composition, respectively, of the stainless surface. These processes are not considered necessary to control microbial growth due to the relatively high operating temperatures. MP is advocated for final finishing of mechanical welds and EP/passivation for all stainless steel surfaces to optimize the formation of the corrosion resistant chromium oxide barrier.

The impact of progressive mechanical polishing on the capital cost of stills and other equipment is considerable, and often can account for 25% to 30% of a ME or VC still cost. MP processes, except when used to smooth out a mechanical weld or misalignment, may be removed from the applicable specification without fear of compromising the water quality.

6.5.6.2 Instrumentation and Controls

For WFI applications, the level of instrumentation should be sufficient to monitor parameters considered critical because they relate to ensuring proper hydraulic/thermodynamic functionality and the production of the appropriate quality of WFI. Instrumentation for critical operating parameters should be calibratable using National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) traceable equipment.

Commentary / 教學解析

材質與表面處理

材質選擇原則

  • SE：可用錫鍍銅（Tin-coated copper），成本最低
  • ME：全面採用 316/316L 不鏽鋼，管材可選鈦合金（耐腐蝕）
  • VC：外殼 304 SS，接液部位 316 SS，壓縮機用 316 SS 或 Inconel（鎳基合金，耐高溫高壓）
  • 骨架/底座：三者均可用碳鋼（不接觸純水）

重大成本啟示：MP 佔成本 25-30%

ISPE 明確指出：在蒸餾系統中，機械拋光（MP）不是微生物控制的必要條件，因為系統在高溫下運行。

過度指定 MP 可能佔設備成本的 25-30%，卻不會提升水質。這是典型的「過度設計」（Over-specification）案例，是 Good Engineering Practice 強調需要避免的。

比喻：鍍金門把手

在一個 100°C 運行的蒸餾系統上做精密拋光，就像在消防站門口裝鍍金門把手 — 看起來高級，但對功能（滅火）毫無幫助。高溫本身就是最好的微生物控制手段。

表面處理的正確用途

  • MP（機械拋光） — 用於平滑焊接接縫，僅此而已
  • EP（電拋光）+ 鈍化 — 用於所有不鏽鋼表面，建立氧化鉻保護層以防腐蝕（不是防微生物）
  • 非不鏽鋼合金（錫鍍銅、鈦、Inconel）— 不需要也不適用 MP/EP

儀表校正要求

關鍵操作參數的儀表必須使用 NIST 可追溯設備進行校正。這確保了量測數據的準確性和法規合規性。在台灣，對應的標準追溯體系為國家度量衡標準實驗室（NML）。

6.6 Reverse Osmosis (RO) for WFI

RO employs a semi-permeable membrane and a relatively high pressure differential to force water through the membrane to achieve chemical, microbial, and endotoxin reduction, critical in USP WFI applications. The feed water is converted into two streams, permeate and reject. The permeate water flows through the membrane and is produced cold and as such does not have the temperature protection for microbial growth afforded by the alternate distillation processes.

6.6.1 Application

RO systems are used as USP WFI pretreatment for distillation processes, or as final treatment for USP Purified water systems. RO is also an accepted means of producing WFI, and may provide a low capital and operational cost alternative to distillation.

Membranes that are hot water sanitizable at 80°C are now available for pretreatment and final treatment, thus eliminating the need for chemical sanitization and simplifying the validation process.

Membranes, which may allow for continuous operation at 80°C, are under development. Failure of a membrane or seal will result in permeate contamination. These problems may be controlled by:

  • Pretreatment of the feed water
  • Appropriate membrane material selection
  • Latest technology membrane design
  • Integrity challenges
  • Periodic sanitization
  • Monitoring of microbial levels, conductivity, total organic carbon and differential pressures

6.6.2 Description

Semi-permeable RO membranes are produced commercially in spiral wound and hollow fiber configurations. Membranes are available in four basic materials: cellulose acetate, polyamide, thin film composite, and polysulfone.

Bacteria and endotoxin removal, required for WFI applications, can be performed at ambient temperatures. This significantly reduces utility costs compared to alternative elevated temperature processes (distillation). By operating at ambient temperatures, distribution piping may not require insulation and may not need to be constructed of stainless steel.

For WFI applications, opportunities exist for enhanced control of the single pass unit, by utilizing multi-pass-product-staged or other combination designs.

Commentary / 教學解析

RO 製備 WFI 解析

RO 作為 WFI 生產手段

RO（Reverse Osmosis，逆滲透）利用半透膜在高壓下將水與雜質分離：

  • Permeate（透過液） — 通過膜的純水，即產品水
  • Reject（濃縮液） — 被膜阻擋的雜質濃縮水，排放

RO 最大特點：產出常溫水。這既是優勢（低能耗），也是挑戰（沒有溫度抑菌保護）。

RO-WFI 的核心挑戰：微生物控制

蒸餾系統在 80-100°C 下運行，天然抑制微生物。RO 在常溫下運行，必須靠其他手段控制微生物：

  • 80°C 熱水消毒膜（Hot water sanitizable membrane）
  • 定期化學消毒/清洗
  • 完整性測試（Integrity challenge）
  • 連續監測電導度、TOC、微生物與壓差

比喻：RO vs. 蒸餾 = 冷水洗手 vs. 熱水洗手

蒸餾就像用熱水洗手 — 高溫本身就能殺菌。RO 就像用冷水加肥皂（膜）洗手 — 需要額外的清潔劑（消毒程序）來確保衛生，但更省能源。

6.6.3 Pretreatment Requirements

RO, as the final processing step, may require pretreatment using ion exchange, deionization, RO, and/or ultrafiltration to improve operability and quality attributes.

Pretreatment requirements normally include gross particle filtration, scale prevention, and chlorine removal. Carbon dioxide and ammonia gas are not removed by the RO process, and may be removed by degasification, caustic addition, ion exchange, or electrodeionization, prior to the final RO process step.

Due to the stringent microbial and endotoxin control required, the pretreatment prior to the RO should incorporate additional provisions for control and monitoring of microorganisms. Disinfectants, such as chlorine or chloramine, should be maintained at appropriate levels throughout the pretreatment chain. Stagnant water should be avoided by design, or by the inclusion of recirculation systems with in-line UV sterilizers.

Regular and appropriate sanitization and cleaning of all unit operations subsequent to the disinfectant removal should be scheduled.

6.6.4 Economics

Operating costs of RO systems are associated mainly with replacement membranes, water concentrate discharge, electrical power, cleaning and sanitizing chemicals, replacement filters, and pretreatment cost.

6.6.5 Construction Materials

Construction material selection for RO systems are driven by structural integrity (high pressure), chemical compatibility, and the need to control micro-organism growth.

The low operating temperature of the RO system allows the use of non-metallic construction materials. Sanitary piping and valves are generally optional features. For the final purification step, it may be very cost effective to utilize mill finish 304 stainless steel for the feed and concentrate waste piping, maintaining 316L stainless steel or PVDF and sanitary design for the product piping only.

6.6.6-7 Surface Finish & Instrumentation

MP and EP processes are not applicable to non-metallic systems. RO control systems usually use local control and indication and do not typically require PLCs as a standard feature. The typically monitored parameters are feed pH, feed conductivity, and product quality (TOC and conductivity), using calibratable, NIST traceable instruments.

Commentary / 教學解析

RO 前處理與經濟性

RO-WFI 前處理策略

RO 作為 WFI 最終步驟時，前處理鏈需要比一般 PW 系統更嚴格：

  • 粗過濾 — 保護膜不受顆粒損壞
  • 防垢處理 — 防止鈣鎂結垢
  • 除氯 — 保護聚醯胺/TFC 膜
  • 微生物控制 — 維持消毒劑（氯/氯胺）直到除氯點
  • 死水預防 — 循環系統 + UV 殺菌

RO 的成本優勢與隱藏成本

顯而易見的優勢：

  • 不需蒸汽 — 僅用電力驅動高壓泵浦
  • 常溫運行 — 管線不需保溫，材質選擇更靈活

容易被忽視的成本：

  • 膜的更換費用（定期消耗品）
  • 清洗與消毒化學品
  • 濃縮水排放（水資源浪費）
  • 前處理系統的維護

材質選擇的 GEP 原則

RO 系統的一個重大成本優勢：因為常溫運行，可以使用非金屬材料（如 PVDF、PVC）。只有產品水管線需要 316L SS 或 PVDF 衛生設計。進水和廢水管線甚至可以用普通 304 SS 就行。

這與蒸餾系統幾乎全面使用 316L SS 形成鮮明對比，是 RO 資本成本較低的重要原因之一。

6.6.8 RO Advantages and Disadvantages

Multi-pass can, in most cases, produce water quality consistent with the minimum requirements of USP WFI. In cases where the feed water quality is such that this is not possible, the use of some type of deionization as a pretreatment may be required.

Advantages:

  1. RO systems designed for production of USP WFI may provide for significantly reduced capital costs when compared with distillation processes, while maintaining the appropriate USP WFI quality.
  2. The utility requirements are significantly lower for RO systems (electricity for pump horsepower) than for distillation, resulting in lower operating costs, which may be a very significant factor over the lifetime of the system.

Disadvantages:

  1. Membrane fouling and integrity:
    • Bacteria grow-through
    • Seal leakage or by-passing
    • Seal failure or damage caused by chemical attack
    • Membrane damage during installation
    • Membrane damage due to chemical or high temperature attack
  2. Membrane material sensitivity to bacteria and sanitizing agents
  3. Inherent sanitization limitations:
    • Periodic chemical or hot water sanitization may be required
    • Periodic chemical cleaning may be required
  4. Pretreatment cost may be high

RO systems provide a method for consistently producing ambient water in accordance with the USP WFI requirements. This not only reduces utility requirements, but also may reduce installation costs, since thermal insulation may not be required for storage and distribution.

Commentary / 教學解析

RO 優缺點分析

最大風險：Bacteria Grow-Through

「細菌穿透」是 RO-WFI 系統最受關注的風險。與蒸餾的相變分離（100°C 蒸發）不同，RO 膜在常溫下運行，長期使用後膜的微觀結構可能讓細菌逐漸穿透。這要求：

  • 定期完整性測試
  • 嚴格的消毒週期
  • 持續監測產品水微生物

思考練習：選 RO 還是蒸餾？

考慮以下場景，你會推薦 RO-WFI 還是蒸餾-WFI？

  1. 新建小型生物製劑工廠，預算有限，東南亞廠區，電費便宜
  2. 歐洲大型充填工廠（2017 年以前），需同時供應 WFI 和 Clean Steam
  3. 已有完善冷卻水和高壓蒸汽系統的老廠擴建

提示：考慮法規、公用設施條件、Clean Steam 需求、以及生命週期成本。

Chapter Summary / 章節總結

  • WFI 是最高等級製藥用水，用於注射劑、部分眼科/吸入劑產品及注射級 API。內毒素控制是核心要求。
  • 四種最終處理技術：SE 蒸餾（小量/簡單）、ME 蒸餾（中大量/可兼產 Clean Steam）、VC 蒸餾（大量/電力驅動/可產常溫水）、RO（低成本替代方案）。
  • 蒸餾的金標準地位：相變分離（100°C）提供天然的微生物屏障，內毒素去除 ≥ 3 log。所有藥典均接受。
  • RO 的崛起：USP 允許、JP 允許、EP 於 2017 年也接受。能耗與資本成本低，但需嚴格的微生物控制與膜完整性管理。
  • 前處理是系統成敗的關鍵：ME 需要最高品質的進水（TDS < 10, SiO2 < 1），所有蒸餾器都需要除氯，RO 需要防垢和微生物控制。
  • GEP 原則貫穿全章：不要過度指定表面處理（MP 佔 25-30% 成本），材質選擇應基於功能需求而非盲目追求高規格。
  • 選型決策：沒有單一「最佳」方案。需綜合考量法規環境、公用設施條件、Clean Steam 需求、微生物風險管理能力與生命週期成本。
