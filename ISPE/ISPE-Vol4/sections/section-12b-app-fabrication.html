11.9.1 Introduction

Original Text

Fabrication of the distribution system must be made with extreme care and precision to ensure a smooth internal finish that will not allow any crevices that will support or promote bacterial growth, metal corrosion, or particulate generation.

The chapter provides an understanding of the requirements of fabrication, materials of construction, and specialized equipment required to fabricate a piping distribution system.

Decisions concerning the material of construction, the orbital welding, the special handling, and the special environment required must be made and understood by the pharmaceutical manager responsible for the high purity water distribution piping fabrication and installation.

Unlike the equipment selection, the piping fabrication will require selections of material with specific compositions, welding using inert gas envelopes, and a cleanroom environment.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念 Key Concepts

配水系統製造 (Distribution System Fabrication)：這不是普通的配管工程。ISPE 在開頭就強調「extreme care and precision」——製藥用高純度水的管路，每一個內部表面、每一條焊縫都必須達到近乎完美的光滑度。任何微小的縫隙 (crevice) 都可能成為微生物的溫床、腐蝕的起點、或微粒的來源。

三大威脅：bacterial growth（微生物生長）、metal corrosion（金屬腐蝕）、particulate generation（微粒產生）。所有的材料選擇、焊接技術、環境控制，最終都是為了消滅這三個敵人。

比喻說明 Analogy

想像你在做心臟繞道手術——你植入的血管必須內壁完美光滑、不能有任何毛刺或接縫不良，否則血栓就會在那裡形成。高純度水管路就是製藥廠的「血管系統」，標準同樣嚴苛。而且，這個手術（安裝）還必須在無塵室裡進行，不能讓灰塵和油脂汙染「血管」內壁。

重點提示 Key Notes

注意 ISPE 特別指出「unlike the equipment selection」——管路製造比設備選型更講究。設備通常是整機採購，但管路是現場施工的，施工品質完全取決於：(1) 材料的化學組成是否一致 (2) 焊接是否在惰性氣體保護下進行 (3) 整個施工環境是否潔淨受控。這三個條件缺一不可。

11.9.2 Materials of Construction

Original Text

Material selection should be consistent throughout the distribution, storage, and processing systems. The material should be rigid, capable of withstanding steam sterilizing temperatures (as required), cleaning solutions, passivation solutions (as required), and capable of maintaining a durable and corrosion resistant surface finish.

The material in common use in pharmaceutical water systems is a stainless metal. Based on cost and ease of fabrication, this material is nickel chromium steel.

Inert non-metallic materials also are available that can withstand steam sterilization (as required). These materials are used extensively in the electronics industry for ultra-pure water systems where they have proved themselves capable of containing and preserving the high level of purity used in the semi-conductor industry.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念 Key Concepts

材料一致性原則：整個系統從配水管路、儲存槽到製程端，材料必須「consistent」。這不只是美觀問題——混用不同材質會產生電偶腐蝕 (Galvanic Corrosion)，就像把銅和鐵放在鹽水裡一樣會加速腐蝕。

材料需承受的條件：蒸汽滅菌溫度 (121°C+)、清洗液 (CIP chemicals)、鈍化液 (passivation solutions)。材料必須在這些嚴苛條件下仍能維持耐腐蝕的表面品質。

重點提示 Key Notes

ISPE 在這裡埋了一個重要伏筆：非金屬材料（即後文的 PVDF）在半導體業已經大量使用，且證明可以維持超高純度水的品質。這暗示製藥業可以向半導體業借鏡——事實上，半導體的水質要求比製藥還嚴格，所以半導體驗證過的材料在製藥業應該也適用。

11.9.3 Types of Stainless Steel & Corrosive Resistance

Original Text

Type 316L is the preferred steel for a high purity water generation and distribution system. The "L" designation indicates a low level of carbon compared to the non "L" grade.

Alternates to 316L grades are 317L with its higher chromium and molybdenum contents and 304L with its higher chromium content but lower nickel and molybdenum levels.

11.9.3.1 Corrosive Resistance

Chromium content is the most important alloying element in stainless steel followed by nickel and molybdenum.

Steels with chromium in excess of 11.5% will form a protective film of chrome oxide on the metal surface. The presence of nickel in amounts more than 7% enhances the corrosion resistance over the straight chromium grade and improves its ductility.

The performance of stainless steels is governed by the oxidizing characteristics of the environment, similarly to the oxidization of ordinary steels. Strong oxidizing conditions generate a superior protective coating for the stainless steel and a powdery rust on ordinary steels which eventually, if left unchecked, will consume all of the metal.

耐蝕性三大元素

鉻 (Cr) > 11.5%：形成鉻氧化物保護膜——這就是「不鏽鋼」之所以「不鏽」的根本原因。沒有足夠的鉻，就沒有這層保護膜。

鎳 (Ni) > 7%：增強耐蝕性並改善延展性 (ductility)，使鋼材更容易成型和焊接。

鉬 (Mo)：提高抗點蝕 (pitting) 能力，特別是在含氯環境中。316L 比 304L 多了 Mo，這就是為什麼 316L 是高純度水系統的首選。

比喻說明 Analogy

不鏽鋼的鉻氧化物保護膜就像人體皮膚——健康的皮膚能抵擋外來感染。鉻含量不夠（低於 11.5%），就像皮膚有破口，細菌和腐蝕因子就能入侵。而「L」代表 Low Carbon，低碳含量減少了「碳化鉻」析出的風險，就像避免皮膚產生疤痕組織，讓保護層更完整。

11.9.4 Weldability and Polishability

Original Text

Austenitic stainless steels such as AISI type 316L contain impurities in addition to the major elements described. These include: Sulfur (S), Aluminum (Al), Oxygen (O), Silicon (Si), Manganese (Mn), Phosphorus (P), Titanium (Ti), Calcium (Ca).

The effects of some of these elements are cumulative inasmuch as the oxygen can offset some of the welding characteristics of low sulfur steels and the weld penetrations experienced with sulfur may be offset by high levels of aluminum. In addition, the ratio of the trace elements of aluminum to silicon has been shown to effect slag formation during welding which in turn produces inclusions.

Manganese has been shown to combine with the sulfur and form manganese sulfide (MnS) inclusions on or in the surface of the metal.

11.9.4.1 The Impact of Sulfur on Welding and Internal Finishing

Small amounts of sulfur improves machinability as well as weldability; however, with the advent of modern techniques in the steel refining process, the mills are able to produce steels at a cost with very specific chemical compositions.

Low sulfur AISI 316L steels when based on the specification limit of 0.03% have some advantages to obtaining an unpitted polish, but other impurities such as manganese, silicon, oxygen, aluminum, calcium, titanium, and chromium can contribute to the oxide inclusions.

A mid-range sulfur content would be ideal for welding but any mismatch in the sulfur content of the mating parts will easily outweigh the advantages of low or lower sulfur levels.

An ideal compromise would be a level between 0.005% and 0.02% or a modified maximum limit of 0.02% since the lower limit will probably not be attained.

11.9.4.2 Heat Number and its Impact on Weldability

Weld parameters will remain consistent and orbital welding processors will reproduce similar welds if the material remains the same. Since the melting point of steel varies over a range depending upon the concentration of each element present in the steel, the amount of heat required to melt and thus attain the liquefaction of the steel will vary from concentration to concentration.

Each "melt" of steel is assigned a "Heat Number" to identify the mix or exact composition of the steel.

316L stainless steel has a wide tolerance of "active" elements such as chromium, molybdenum, nickel, and carbon which vary as much as 100% and as little as 12%. These variations will change the melting point and the electrical current input of the welding machine considerably.

Where possible, all fittings, valves, tubing, and weldable pieces of the same nominal size (diameter) should be purchased and manufactured from AISI 316L steel with the same Heat Number in order to standardize the weld quality for each tubing size.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

硫含量的兩難困境

硫 (Sulfur) 在不鏽鋼焊接中扮演矛盾角色：

  • 有利面：適量硫可改善加工性和焊接滲透性
  • 不利面：硫會與錳結合形成 MnS 夾雜物，留在金屬表面成為腐蝕起點
  • 最大風險：兩個配合件的硫含量不匹配 (mismatch) 時，焊接品質會嚴重劣化

理想範圍：0.005% ~ 0.02%——這是 ISPE 給出的「黃金區間」，兼顧焊接品質與表面光潔度。

Heat Number 管理的實務重要性

這是許多新進工程師容易忽略的關鍵點。316L 的規格範圍其實很寬——Cr 可以從 16% 到 18%，Mo 從 2% 到 3%——這些差異會顯著改變熔點。如果同一條管路上，管子是 Heat #A、彎頭是 Heat #B、閥門是 Heat #C，每換一個接頭就要重新調整焊接參數，品質一致性就很難保證。

實務建議：採購時要求同一管徑的所有零件（管材、管件、閥門）來自同一爐號 (Heat Number)。雖然閥門和管件的爐號較難控制，但至少管材應該統一。

比喻說明 Analogy

Heat Number 就像烘焙時的麵粉批號。同一品牌的麵粉，不同批次的蛋白質含量可能略有不同。如果你做蛋糕時中途換了一批麵粉，烤出來的口感就會不一樣。焊接也是如此——同樣標稱 316L，不同爐號的鋼材在同樣的焊接參數下，熔融行為可能完全不同。所以要用「同一批麵粉」做完整個蛋糕。

11.9.5 Corrosion Hazards of Stainless Steels

Original Text

While being protected by the chrome oxide film, which will guard against most corrosive attacks, there remains five corrosive hazards associated with the successful use of stainless steels.

a) Intergranular Corrosion — Austenitic 300 series stainless steels which do not contain any of the stabilizing elements, titanium, or columbium are susceptible to intergranular corrosion which can cause early failure or reduced life.

b) Galvanic Corrosion — Occurs when an assembly of dissimilar metals is immersed in any solution which acts as an electrolyte. Therefore, any transition of drain or condensate piping to a dissimilar metal should use a dielectric union to prevent electrolysis.

c) Contact Corrosion — Occurs when small pieces of carbon steel, scale, copper or other foreign material is lodged on the surface of stainless steel.

d) Pitting or Pinhole Corrosion — Solutions containing chlorides may attack stainless steel in a pitting action. This is usually due to high concentrations of the chloride ion due to evaporation.

e) Stress Corrosion Cracking — Chloride solutions are the worst offenders in promoting stress cracking. Cracking is most likely to occur in hot rather than cold solutions. High and low stresses in the same member produce a condition likely to result in stress corrosion if chlorides are present.

Corrective action is to keep the internal strain in stainless steels as low as possible by fully annealing or utilizing stress relieved versions of stainless steels at 1200°F.

Select tubing with good concentricity and close wall thickness tolerance to avoid high and uneven stresses when tubes are rolled into tube bundle headers etc. Avoid joining dissimilar metals and avoid cyclic bending.

接觸腐蝕是最容易在施工現場犯的錯誤。如果施工團隊之前在碳鋼管路上工作過，然後直接用同一套工具來處理不鏽鋼——磨輪上殘留的碳鋼微粒會嵌入不鏽鋼表面，形成腐蝕核。這就是為什麼 ISPE 後文要求「clean area devoid of any equipment previously used on carbon steels」。

11.9.6 Corrosion Protection, Passivation & Rouging

Original Text

11.9.6 Corrosion Protection of Stainless Steel

Surface protection of chromium nickel stainless steel is inherent when chromium levels are in excess of 11.5%.

A passive or inert surface is established naturally with these steels due to exposure to air, aerated water, and other oxidizing atmospheres.

Airborne impurities, heating, and other direct contact materials can damage this protective film causing the metal to be compromised in its ability to ward off the corrosive process.

11.9.6.1 Passivation of Stainless Steels to Restore Protective Film

Passivation is the process that establishes a surface or film on nickel-chrome steels and maximizes the corrosion resistance.

The passivation process cleans the exposed surfaces of the contaminates, soils, and surface impurities that cause film damage. The process forms a new strong protective film in areas that have become "active" or sensitized due to welding.

11.9.6.2 Rouging or Ferric Oxide Discoloration of SS Systems

Rouging is seen in many water systems, but is more prominent in hot, distilled, and clean steam systems.

Rouge can be in the form of dust or a light film that can be wiped off. Rouge also can be in the form of a bonded multi-layer that requires scraping with a sharp tool to remove.

Rouge is found in many forms:

  • Orange — found in high purity/high temperature systems
  • Light Red — found in high purity/high temperature systems
  • Red — found in high purity/high temperature systems
  • Reddish Brown — found in high purity/high temperature systems
  • Purple — found in clean steam and high temperature water systems
  • Blue — found in clean steam systems
  • Gray — found in clean steam systems
  • Black — found in clean steam systems

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

鈍化 (Passivation) 原理

鈍化不是「鍍」一層東西上去，而是利用化學方式（通常是硝酸或檸檬酸）清除表面的鐵、污染物和夾雜物，讓鉻原子暴露出來，在空氣中自然形成一層緻密的鉻氧化物 (Cr2O3) 保護膜。

何時需要鈍化：

  • 新系統安裝完成後（管路焊接會破壞原有保護膜）
  • 系統維修或改造後
  • 發現紅鏽 (Rouge) 後
  • 定期預防性維護

紅鏽 (Rouge) 的分級與對策

紅鏽的顏色是系統健康的「體溫計」：

  • 橙色/淺紅：早期 rouge，通常可以用酸洗擦拭去除，再做鈍化即可
  • 紅褐色：中期 rouge，需要化學清洗（CIP derouging）+ 重新鈍化
  • 紫/藍/灰/黑：出現在蒸汽系統中的高溫 rouge，這些是 Fe3O4 (磁鐵礦) 形態，非常難以去除，可能需要刮除並重新電拋光

實務經驗：在 CDMO 工廠中，WFI 高溫循環系統（80°C+）幾乎都會出現某種程度的 rouge。關鍵是建立定期監測和 derouging 計劃，而不是等到嚴重時才處理。

比喻說明 Analogy

鈍化就像給鋼琴定期調音——鋼琴弦（不鏽鋼表面）在使用後會偏離最佳狀態，調音師（鈍化程序）把它恢復到最佳音準（最大耐蝕狀態）。而紅鏽就像鋼琴弦上的鏽斑，顏色從淺到深代表問題從輕到重。淺色的擦擦就好，深色的可能要換弦（重新處理表面）。

11.9.7 Castings/Forgings & 11.9.8 Surface Finish of Stainless Steels

Original Text

11.9.7 Castings and Forgings

Most castings and forgings are used for components attached to vessels, such as agitator impeller hubs and instrument housings. Castings typically contain higher levels of ferrite that can cause potential problems with components in contact with high purity water (rouging). In addition, castings are usually more porous or grainier, and will not typically take a polish higher than a 4 at best. Forgings are less susceptible to rouging and can be polished up to a 8 mirror finish.

11.9.8.1 Cold Rolled Stainless Steel Finishes

300 series stainless steel "sheet" may be produced with a very fine grain, slightly milky appearance through to a bright highly reflective finish produced on mirror polished rolls.

Mills also relate to the ASTM or AISI finishes which range from 1 to 8, where 8 is a "mirror finish." This method also is subjective and relates to the method used to obtain the finish.

11.9.8.2 Stainless Steel Polishes and Improved Finishes

A third identification method commonly used to define surface finishes is the "Grit Finish" which is the number of scratch lines per inch of surface produced by an abrasive wheel or belt.

Grit finishes come in grades related to the standard abrasive tools or surfaces:

  • 20 grit = 100+ RMS and 90+ RA
  • 180 = same as a 2B finish = 80 RMS and 70 RA approximately
  • 240 = between a 2B and a 3A finish = 15 to 63 RMS and 14 to 57 RA
  • 320 = same as an 8 finish = 10 to 32 RMS and 9 to 29 RA
  • 500 = same as a 9 finish = 4 to 16 RMS and 4 to 14 RA

11.9.8.3 Electro-Polishing of Stainless Steel

Mechanical finishing has certain inherent deficiencies, one of which is the tendency to enlarge the exposed surface area.

Electro-polishing is able to improve the mechanical finish by rounding off the sharp peaks of the "scratch lines."

The advantages of electro-polishing are:

  • reduces the surface areas
  • provides a sanitary acceptable surface finish
  • cleans the surface
  • passivates the surface of stainless steel with a chromium layer
  • removes impurities trapped below folded layers of mechanically formed ridges
  • reveals defects that have been hidden by mechanical polishing through smearing effect

Electro-polishing requires a mechanical polish preparation developed with a uniform progressive grit polishing application.

電拋光 (Electro-Polishing) 的六大優勢

電拋光不只是「讓表面更光亮」——它有六個關鍵功能：

  1. 縮小表面積：平滑的山峰→減少微生物可附著的面積
  2. 達到衛生級表面：滿足 cGMP 清洗要求
  3. 清潔表面：移除機械拋光殘留物
  4. 同時鈍化：形成富鉻保護層——一舉兩得
  5. 揭露隱藏缺陷：機械拋光有「塗抹效應」(smearing effect)，會把缺陷蓋住，EP 反而能揭露
  6. 移除深層雜質：機械拋光形成的褶層下方可能藏有雜質

鑄件 vs 鍛件的關鍵差異

在選擇容器零件時，鍛件 (Forgings) 優於鑄件 (Castings)：

  • 鑄件含較多鐵素體 (ferrite)→更容易產生紅鏽
  • 鑄件多孔、組織粗糙→最多只能拋光到 #4
  • 鍛件組織緻密→可拋光到 #8 鏡面

結論：高純度水系統中，與水接觸的零件應優先使用鍛件。

11.9.9 Stainless Steel Distribution Piping & Bends

Original Text

11.9.9.1 Solid Drawn Thin Wall Tubing

The selected materials are available in solid drawn tube suitable for the type of service expected from a high purity water distribution system. This is an acceptable material for a high purity water loop and should conform to ASTM A632.

Tubing should be 16 gauge (.065 in./0.165 cm) for tubing diameters of between 1 and 3 inches inclusive and 14 gauge for 4 in. diameter.

11.9.9.2 Welded Seam Thin Wall Tubing

Acceptable thin wall tubing is available at a reduced cost with a welded seam produced in an oxygen free environment (TIG).

The tubing is available with a uniform smooth surface in accordance with ASTM A269 and may be polished mechanically and electrically to a suitable surface finish.

The metal is cut and cold rolled to form a round tube. The tube is clamped so that the two edges make contact. The inside and outside of the tube is blanketed with Argon gas to expel all oxygen and the seam is welded without filler, producing a consistent smooth and oxidation free surface inside and out.

11.9.10 Elbows/Bends In Tubing

Current practice is to use tight welded elbows for each directional change in the distribution piping. Considering the extent of high purity water distribution piping, the total number of elbows can range from 130 to 500 and from 60 to 1000 welds to install them.

The purchase of longer lengths than the standard 20 ft (6 m) lengths (say 40 ft (12 m) lengths) and the utilization of long sweeping bends, instead of the traditional tight welded elbows can reduce the total number of welds by up to 60% and the total pipe used by 10%.

Large sweeping bends are preferred in order to reduce the potential of wrinkling and/or damage to the internal pipe finish.

11.9.11 Types of Non-Metallic Materials (PVDF)

Original Text

Few compatible non-metallic piping materials are available that will withstand the rigors of a pharmaceutical high purity water system, such as: 80 to 90°C operation or periodic sanitization, 121°C steam sterilizing temperatures, ozone and/or chlorine contact.

One such material that will support the above requirements and limit extractable to a minimum level compatible with stainless steel is polyvinylidene fluoride (PVDF).

This material is available in a compatible range of pipe diameters, surface finishes, and automated orbital welding capability.

11.9.11.1 Corrosive Resistance of PVDF

PVDF is inert and will not exhibit any surface corrosion when in contact with 90°C high purity water or commonly used oxidizers.

11.9.11.2 Weldability and Polishability of PVDF

The surface finish of PVDF is equivalent to electro-polished stainless steel and the fusion welding equipment and capabilities are similar to stainless steel orbital welders.

11.9.11.3 PVDF Distribution Piping and Fittings

Weldable fittings, elbows, tees, reducers, adapters, diaphragm valves, zero static valves, flow meters, regulators, etc. are available for PVDF pipe.

11.9.11.4 Pressure Rating of PVDF Piping Systems

Rated pressures for PVDF piping ranges from 230 psi at 68°C to 50 psi at 149°C.

Due to softening at elevated temperatures, continuous support is recommended for systems that are operated at 65°C or above.

  • 149°C → 50 psi（蒸汽滅菌時，強度大幅下降）

比喻說明 Analogy

PVDF 就像高級矽膠——化學上幾乎什麼都不怕，但物理上「軟」。不鏽鋼像盔甲——硬度夠但有化學弱點（氯離子、rouge）。選材就像選裝備：如果你的水系統溫度不太高、不需要蒸汽滅菌，PVDF 可能是更省心的選擇（不用鈍化、不會 rouge）；但如果系統需要承受高溫高壓，不鏽鋼的「硬度」優勢就無可取代。

11.10 Design of a WFI/Purified Water Distribution System

Original Text

The layout and general design of a high purity water system (WFI or purified water) should be consistent and follow good manufacturing guidelines in respect to installation, support, natural drainage, flow rates, dead or stagnation areas (dead legs), and minimization of areas that may promote micro-organism growth.

11.10.1 Fittings and Equipment

All equipment that, when installed, comes in direct contact with the high purity water should use a suitable stainless steel or non-metallic material for all contact areas, except for valve diaphragms and tri-clamp gaskets.

Valve design should be sanitary diaphragm type, installed at approximately 60° to the vertical to optimize drainability. Hash marks or drain dots are provided on many designs to allow precise orientation of the valves in horizontal installations.

PTFE Teflon faced diaphragms are preferred for hot water applications, covering a temperature range from -30 to 350°F (0 to 177°C).

Connecting flanges for all valves and fittings should be tri-clamp design.

11.10.2 Natural Drainage

Well designed distribution loops should be installed overhead and point of use valves should be located at a convenient location within the process or user area, below the distribution loop.

Slopes should be verified externally after hanging and before insulation is installed.

Piping distribution from an unavoidable low point should be designed with a clear low point and a sanitary drain valve installed with a maximum of 6 diameter dead leg (no less than 6 diameters from the centerline of the main line to the valve center line based on the main line diameter).

Slope of distribution system process pipe or tubing should be a minimum of 1/16 in./ft (0.52 cm/m).

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

配水系統設計關鍵參數

  • 管路坡度：最小 1/16 in./ft (0.52 cm/m)——確保自然排空
  • 閥門安裝角度：與垂直方向成 60°——優化排水性
  • Dead Leg 限制：最大 6 倍主管管徑——超過此值水會停滯
  • 法蘭型式：Tri-clamp 衛生連接——易拆卸清洗
  • 隔膜材質：PTFE Teflon——適用高溫至 177°C

Dead Leg 的 6D 法則

「6 diameter dead leg」是高純度水系統中最常被稽核員檢查的設計準則之一。舉例：如果主管管徑是 2 英寸，則從主管中心到支管閥門中心的距離不得超過 12 英寸 (6 x 2")。

為什麼重要：Dead leg 中的水是靜止的，無法被主管路的湍流帶走微生物和生物膜。FDA 查廠時會拿捲尺量——這不是理論問題，是實際執行問題。

坡度驗證時機：ISPE 特別提醒要在「after hanging and before insulation」時驗證坡度。一旦包了保溫層就看不到了，而且保溫層的重量可能改變管路的坡度。

11.11 Fabrication of a WFI/PW Distribution System

Original Text

11.11.1 Assurance of Quality Distribution Piping

The distribution piping and storage systems should be installed in accordance with cGMPs and should be fabricated, manufactured, procured, and installed in strict accordance with explicit operating procedures.

11.11.2 Operating Procedures Should Include:

Purchase of components from a list of selected/preferred vendors. Procurement based on preferred part numbers. Procurement of other components using approved specifications.

Inspection of all components on receipt for compliance and/or damage. Verification of the bill of lading.

Fabrication requirements:

  • Receiving, fabrication, cutting, end facing, and welding to be performed in a clean area devoid of any equipment previously used on "carbon" steels
  • Tubing specification, heat lot, diameter to be used for the specific application
  • Check tubing for crushing and confirm the nominal diameter
  • Examine interior and exterior for physical damage, scratches (interior) dirt or grease
  • Clean tubing using approved cleaning solvent isopropyl alcohol (IPA) and lint free cloth
  • Cut tubing to length and finish with a square deburred butt end

11.11.2.1 Cleaning

  • Place a lint free cloth in tubing prior to facing and/or deburring
  • Use only Isopropyl alcohol for SS and other appropriate solvent for PVDF
  • Use only lint free cloths such as "Texwipe"
  • Clean both inside and outside of tube ends and do not touch the end area with bare hands or soiled material

11.11.2.2 Protection

  • Bag the prepared tubing with clear plastic bags if not to be welded within two hours
  • Weld fittings should be received sealed in a plastic or other lint free, non-metallic sealed bag
  • The fitting should remain in the bag until just prior to welding or for inspection

Welding should be performed inside the fabrication cleanroom, using TIG orbital welding for SS and PVDF fusion machine for PVDF.

Purging with Argon gas 99.999% pure containing less than 1 ppm of moisture or 2 ppm of oxygen. The complete removal of all air should be accomplished prior to commencing welding.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

施工品質保證程序總覽

ISPE 在這裡建立了一個從採購到安裝的完整品質鏈：

  1. 採購控制：AVL (Approved Vendor List) + 指定料號
  2. 進料檢驗：核對提貨單、檢查損傷
  3. 準備程序：切割、端面加工、清潔
  4. 保護程序：2 小時規則——未焊接的管端必須封袋
  5. 焊接程序：在潔淨室中、氬氣保護下進行

「2 小時規則」與碳鋼隔離

兩個容易被忽略但極其重要的規定：

1. 兩小時封袋規則：準備好的管端如果不在 2 小時內焊接，必須用透明塑膠袋封住。這是因為暴露在空氣中的潔淨管端會累積灰塵、油氣和水氣，汙染焊接區域。

2. 碳鋼零容忍：施工區域不得有任何曾用於碳鋼的設備。碳鋼微粒即使是微量的，嵌入不鏽鋼表面後就成為腐蝕起點。很多承包商不理解這一點，會用同一台砂輪機「先磨碳鋼再磨不鏽鋼」——這是絕對不可以的。

氬氣純度要求

99.999% 純度（5N），水分 <1 ppm，氧氣 <2 ppm。

這個要求比一般工業焊接的氬氣純度（99.95%~99.99%）高一個等級。原因是：即使微量的氧氣或水分，在焊接高溫下都會與不鏽鋼表面反應，造成氧化變色——這正是後文所列多種焊接缺陷的根本原因之一。

液態氬氣 (Dewar) 優先：因為液態氬氣的純度天生就高（雜質在液化過程中已被分離）。再加上 Nanochem 等純化器，可以把汙染物降到 ppb 等級。

Original Text

11.11.3 Clean Preparation Area

A cleanroom/trailer should be used to ensure that welding, bending, and fabrication of high purity water piping are not contaminated with metal or non-metallic particulates.

The area should be filtered to control particulates with limited controlled access. The following procedures should be followed:

  • All cutting and welding equipment should be cleaned of any impurities or particulates
  • The room should be maintained clean at all times and access should be across sticky floor mats
  • All piping and fittings should be cleaned prior to and following welding
  • Assemblies should be bagged following welding and the bags removed just prior to making field welds
  • Welding and fitting technicians should use overshoes and cleanroom gloves. The gloves are worn to prevent finger print oils from getting onto the pipe

The above procedures are currently typical for a high purity piping system for the semi-conductor industry and is considered advisable for pharmaceutical installations with similar requirements.

Inspection of completed welds is sometimes not possible or practical. Common practice is to provide a validated process for all welds, a precise instrument to make the welds, an inert environment around the weld and a clean contact surface.

Spot checks of the outside and inside surfaces on a percentage or time basis will provide assurance of the system efficiency.

潔淨施工區域管控要點

ISPE 推薦的潔淨施工規格，其實借鏡自半導體業的做法：

  • 過濾通風：控制空氣中的微粒
  • 黏性地墊 (Sticky Mats)：入口處除塵
  • 受限進入：只有經過培訓的人員可進入
  • 個人防護：鞋套 + 潔淨室手套（防指紋油脂）
  • 焊後封袋：組裝件焊完立刻封袋，到現場才拆

「Validated Process」的重要概念

由於管路內部焊縫的檢查往往不可能或不實際（管子太長、位置難以到達），ISPE 的策略是：與其逐條檢查結果，不如驗證整個製程。

這是典型的「Process Validation」思維：

  1. 精確的焊接設備（validated instrument）
  2. 惰性氣體環境（inert environment）
  3. 潔淨的接觸面（clean contact surface）
  4. 經資格認證的焊工（qualified welder）

當這四個條件都被驗證和控制時，就可以合理地相信每一條焊縫都符合品質要求——無需百分之百檢查。

11.11.4-11.11.5 Orbital Welding & Borescoping

Original Text

11.11.4 Joints Using Fusion Butt Welding

Orbital welding is the recommended industry standard for high purity water systems in biotech, pharmaceutical and semi-conductor industries. This is due to the smooth inner weld bead that is characteristic of this joining process.

11.11.4.1 Orbital Welding

Orbital welding is a welding method used for joining stainless steel piping and tubing with electric arc in a welding machine not using any filler (welding rod) under inert atmosphere argon (Ar). Electric power characteristics are displayed on the control panel instruments and, if required, a printout of the welding parameters can be obtained.

11.11.4.2 Borescoping

A borescope is a fiber optic instrument with a monitor (TV screen and optional video tape recorder) used for inspecting the weld inside the pipe or tubing. The fiber-glass cable is a maximum 20 ft. long with a light and lens at the end.

With the control of the welding process and the processing area, borescope inspection of the inside of the weld is only considered necessary as a means of control and is recommended as part of the qualifying process for the weld size and daily restart checks.

Video recording each weld is considered un-necessary and difficult to justify.

11.11.5 High Tech. Orbital Tube and Pipe Welding for SS

Semi-automatic, programmable, orbital, inert gas purged welding equipment for both tube and pipe is available from many manufacturers. The system is an automatic "Heliarc" or "TIG" (Tungsten Inert Gas) process where Inert Argon gas from a cryogenic or low temperature source is used to protect the molten steel from oxidation during the metal fusion.

Liquid argon in Dewars is preferred due to its high purity at 99.999%. A gas purifier such as the Nanochem should be used during purging to bring the contaminants down to the ppb levels which is preferred.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

軌道焊接 (Orbital Welding) 原理

為什麼是「軌道」：焊頭沿著管外圓周自動旋轉（orbits around the tube），不需要人工操控焊槍方向。

關鍵特徵：

  • 無填料 (Autogenous)：不加焊條，靠母材本身熔融結合
  • TIG/GTAW 製程：鎢極惰性氣體保護焊
  • 可程式化：電流、速度、脈衝等參數可精確設定並記錄
  • 可重現：同樣參數 + 同樣材料 = 同樣品質的焊縫

內視鏡 (Borescope) 檢查策略

ISPE 對內視鏡檢查的態度很務實：

  • 不是每條焊縫都要做——因為製程已經被驗證
  • 建議用於：焊接資格認證 (qualification) 和每日開工檢查 (daily restart)
  • 錄影每條焊縫不必要——ISPE 明確說「difficult to justify」

實務建議：制定一個抽檢計劃，例如每個焊工每天前 3 條焊縫做 borescope，加上隨機抽檢 5-10%。這比「全檢」更有成本效益，同時提供足夠的品質保證。

比喻說明 Analogy

軌道焊接就像精密的 3D 列印機在管子接口上「印」出一圈完美的金屬融合線。人工焊接像手寫書法——高手可以寫得很美，但品質不穩定；軌道焊接像印表機——每次輸出幾乎一模一樣。這也是為什麼可以用「製程驗證」取代「逐一檢查」：既然機器每次做的都一樣，你只要驗證設定是對的，就可以信任產出。

11.11.6 Weld Criteria & Welder Qualification

Original Text

11.11.6 Weld Criteria

  • Weld quality must meet the strictest standards
  • All welds must be fully penetrated around the entire weld perimeter with no crevices or entrapment sites. These areas are particularly vulnerable to crevice corrosion
  • All welds should be smooth, uniform, complete and flat, not concave, on the outside
  • The weld should have a uniform and complete weld bead width on the inside with little or no convexity
  • The inner weld bead should contain no concavity
  • There should be no visible signs of oxidation/discoloration of the inner weld
  • The joints should be square facing and properly aligned. Tubing surfaces should not be offset in any plane or direction by more than +/-0.003 in.
  • The weld width should be nominal 1/8 in. wide

11.11.6.1 Welder Qualification

Each welder performing welding on certain metals must be qualified for this type of metal and welding. The American Society of Mechanical Engineers (ASME) and the American Welding Society (AWS) have welding procedures for welder qualifications.

As a general rule, welders and welding procedures are qualified to ASME B 31.3 (chemical plant and petroleum refinery piping), that incorporates ASME Section IX by reference.

11.11.6.2 Common Test Methods

Test methods on installed piping are: pressure testing, verification of slope, and the last step of passivation requiring the testing of the final rinse water.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

焊縫品質八大準則

  1. 完全熔透 (Full Penetration)：焊縫必須貫穿整個管壁厚度，360°無死角
  2. 無縫隙 (No Crevices)：任何縫隙都會成為縫隙腐蝕和微生物棲息地
  3. 外部平坦：不可凹陷 (concave)
  4. 內部焊道均勻：盡量無凸起
  5. 內部不可凹陷：凹陷處會積水成為死角
  6. 無氧化變色：內部焊縫必須銀白色
  7. 對齊精度：管壁偏移 ≤ 0.003 in. (0.076 mm)——比一根頭髮還細
  8. 焊道寬度：標稱 1/8 in. (3.2 mm)

焊工資格認證

ASME B31.3 + Section IX 是製藥管路焊接資格的標準依據。每位焊工必須：

  • 通過特定金屬類型和焊接方法的認證
  • 持有正式的焊工資格證書
  • 每個工作日開始前重新資格驗證
  • 更換管徑時重新資格驗證

實務提醒：「每日開工檢查」(daily restart check) 不是走形式——它確保焊接設備經過一夜停機後仍在最佳狀態，同時也驗證焊工的手感沒有因為休息而偏差。第一條焊縫用 borescope 檢查確認品質後，才能繼續正式焊接。

0.003 in. 偏移容差的意義

0.003 英寸 = 0.076 mm，大約是一張影印紙的厚度。這個容差之所以如此嚴格，是因為管壁錯位會在焊縫內部形成一個「階梯」(step)，而這個階梯就是一個潛在的微生物附著點和縫隙腐蝕起始點。要達到這種精度，除了高品質的軌道焊接設備外，管材本身的同心度 (concentricity) 和壁厚均勻度也必須非常好。

11.11.7 Weld Defects Examples

Original Text

The following are common weld defects with their causes and corrective actions:

11.11.7.1 Joint Misalignment — Poor alignment of joint caused by equipment or procedure failure. Follow GMP welding procedures/use tube alignment gauge.

11.11.7.2 Lack of Penetration — Weld bead does not completely penetrate the ID of tube. Caused by poor setting of weld program parameters, material thickness and/or composition issues, or improper welding amperage from power fluctuations.

11.11.7.3 Excessive Penetration — Weld bead over penetrates causing excessive concavity or spikes. Caused by incorrect weld program (too hot), excessive amperage, or material variations.

11.11.7.4 Lack of I.D. Purge — Discoloration/oxidization in the heat affected zone caused by lack of ID purge or impure purge gas. Sugaring of the weld bead caused by a lack of ID purge.

11.11.7.5 Interrupted Purge — Short segment of discoloration/oxidation and excessive penetration caused by momentary purge gas pressure drop, commonly from a pinched gas line.

11.11.7.6 Purge Gas Purity Problems — Coloration/oxidation along weld area caused by impurities in weld purge gas or in/on the metal surface.

11.11.7.7 Heat Tint/Haze — Light coloration/haze along weld area caused by minor impurities in weld purge gas or metal surface.

11.11.7.8 Oxidation/Discoloration — Discoloration ranging from blue/brown haze to dark blue with black edges. Caused by oxygen contaminated purge gas, incomplete sealing, or insufficient pre-purge.

11.11.7.9 Insufficient Tie-In — Weld on OD is good, but ID is incomplete and does not overlap weld start. Caused by incorrect speed or time in weld program (uncalibrated weld head).

11.11.7.10 Electrical Current Fluctuations — Significant narrowing of ID weld bead and/or lack of penetration caused by power fluctuations (not using a dedicated circuit).

11.11.7.11 Inclusions/Dross — Non-metallic formation on ID weld caused by tungsten inclusion.

11.11.7.12 Pinholing — Small holes in weld bead caused by weld puddle being cooled too rapidly. Ramp (feather) the welding amperage properly and provide post purge.

11.11.7.13 Porosity — Metallic or non-metallic impurities trapped in the weld bead (tungsten or slag). Caused by introduction of impurities from outside or from the weld material.

11.11.7.14 Cracking — Cracks in the surface of the weld caused by brittle metal or very hot weld program with rapid cooling. Use only good quality materials and acceptable welding programs. Do not quench or cool the weld.

氣體保護問題佔 5/14 = 36%

統計 14 種缺陷，會發現有 5 種（超過三分之一）都與氬氣保護有關。這告訴我們：管好保護氣體就能預防三分之一以上的焊接問題。

氣體管理三要件：

  1. 純度：99.999%，使用液態氬氣 + 純化器
  2. 流量：監控焊接現場的實際流量，而非只看調壓器設定
  3. 密封：確保氣管無洩漏、無被壓扁，管路兩端完全密封

「Sugaring」——最嚴重的氣體保護失敗

「Sugaring」是焊縫內部因完全缺乏保護氣而產生的粗糙、顆粒狀氧化物，外觀像結晶砂糖。這是最嚴重的焊接缺陷之一，因為：

  • 粗糙的表面是微生物生物膜的理想棲息地
  • 無法透過清洗或鈍化修復
  • 唯一的解決方案是切除焊縫重新焊接

11.11.8 PVDF Welding

Original Text

Semi automatic fusion welding machines are available for orbital welding of PVDF piping.

An elastic pressure element backs up the inside of the fusion zone, totally avoiding an inner bead.

A controlled pressure thermoplastic melt optimizes the homogeneous joint between the plastic parts, producing a consistent joint quality.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

PVDF 焊接 vs SS 焊接

PVDF 焊接與不鏽鋼焊接有本質區別：

  • 原理不同：SS 是電弧熔融→PVDF 是熱板/紅外線熱熔融
  • 無需保護氣：塑膠不會氧化，省去氬氣系統
  • 無內焊道：彈性壓力元件支撐內壁，避免形成突出的焊道
  • 均質結合：熱塑性材料在分子層級融合，接合品質非常一致

PVDF 焊接的最大優勢是「完全沒有內焊道」(no inner bead)——不鏽鋼焊接即使做到最好，內部仍會有一個微凸的焊道，而 PVDF 的內壁可以做到幾乎完全平滑。

11.11.9 Remedial Action for Defective Welds

Original Text

Rewelding or weld repairs are not acceptable.

Weld Replacements — The following is a procedure for replacing defective welds:

  1. Cut out rejected weld using a band or cut-off saw
  2. Where possible, retain section for further inspection
  3. Prepare faces of tubing and check for signs of discoloration
  4. Ensure that all traces of any heat affected areas are removed
  5. Cut and shape pipe ends using proprietary facing equipment
  6. Debur and reweld
  7. Assign a "R" suffix to weld number

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

「零修補」原則 — 最嚴格的焊接品管

「Rewelding or weld repairs are not acceptable」——這句話的意思是：如果一條焊縫不合格，你不能在原位重新焊接或補焊。必須把整個焊縫切除，重新準備管端，然後重新焊接。

原因：

  • 重焊或補焊會在同一位置造成多次熱影響，改變金屬微觀結構
  • 多次加熱可能導致碳化鉻析出（敏化），增加晶間腐蝕風險
  • 補焊無法保證與原焊道的完美融合，可能形成隱藏的縫隙

焊縫替換 7 步驟

注意第 (g) 步——替換焊縫必須在焊縫編號後加「R」字尾。例如原始焊縫 W-047 被替換後，新焊縫編號為 W-047R。這確保了完整的追溯性 (traceability)：任何人看到「R」就知道這是替換焊縫，可以追查原始缺陷報告。

第 (b) 步「retain section for further inspection」也很重要——保留被切除的焊縫段可以做根本原因分析 (RCA)，避免同類缺陷再次發生。

11.11.10 Joints Using Sanitary Clamps

Original Text

The sanitary clamp system of quick disconnect fittings are designed to provide a smooth non-contacting or non-corrosive environment.

The sanitary joints may be cleaned in place, provide leak tight connections and may be adapted to other forms of piping.

These joints should be used for all distribution pipe connections to valves, sensor housings, and fittings not adaptable to orbital fusion welding.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

衛生夾具 (Tri-Clamp) 的角色

衛生夾具是高純度水系統中唯一可接受的「非焊接」連接方式。它的設計特點：

  • 快速拆裝：不需要特殊工具，方便維護和檢查
  • 平滑內壁：兩個對接面 + EPDM/PTFE 墊片，形成連續平滑的內表面
  • 可 CIP 清洗：不必拆開就能用線上清洗系統處理
  • 防洩漏：適當的夾具力矩可確保密封

使用場景

Tri-clamp 連接用於所有「需要可拆卸」的位置：

  • 閥門連接：閥門可能需要定期更換隔膜
  • 感測器安裝：溫度、壓力、電導度感測器需要校準和更換
  • 無法軌道焊接的位置：空間受限或最終連接點

注意：墊片是最弱的環節——必須定期檢查和更換，特別是在高溫 WFI 系統中，墊片老化速度較快。

本章重點摘要 Chapter Summary

  • 材料選擇：316L 是首選，因為低碳含量降低晶間腐蝕風險，Mo 增強抗點蝕能力。所有零件盡量使用同一 Heat Number 以確保焊接一致性
  • 硫含量控制：理想範圍 0.005%-0.02%。配合件硫含量不匹配是焊接品質的最大威脅之一
  • 五大腐蝕類型：晶間、電偶、接觸、點蝕、應力開裂——每種都有特定的預防策略
  • 鈍化與紅鏽：鈍化是恢復保護膜的關鍵程序；Rouge 顏色從淺到深代表問題嚴重程度遞增
  • 表面處理：電拋光 (EP) 優於純機械拋光，能同時清潔、平滑化、鈍化，並揭露隱藏缺陷
  • PVDF 替代方案：化學惰性、無 rouge 風險、表面等同 EP，但高溫下強度下降需連續支撐
  • 施工環境：必須在潔淨室中進行，碳鋼工具零容忍，2 小時封袋規則，人員穿戴鞋套和手套
  • 軌道焊接：業界標準，無填料 TIG 製程。Borescope 用於資格認證和每日抽檢，非全檢
  • 焊縫品質：八大準則，偏移容差 0.003 in.。14 種常見缺陷中 36% 與氣體保護有關
  • 缺陷處理：零修補原則——不合格焊縫必須切除重做，不可在原位修補。替換焊縫編號加「R」字尾
  • 衛生夾具：用於所有需要可拆卸的連接位置（閥門、感測器、最終接口）
