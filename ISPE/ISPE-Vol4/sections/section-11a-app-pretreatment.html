11.4.1 Barrier Filtration Details（阻隔過濾詳述）

Original Text

Barrier filtration includes cartridge filtration, pre-coat filtration, ultrafiltration, and nanofiltration. This type of filtration has a "barrier" through which the water must flow. The barrier retains particulates that are removed by changing the barrier (cartridge and pre-coat) or by a purge stream (ultra and nanofiltration).

Barrier filtration is typically not used as the primary method for removal of particulates because of cost of the barrier, labor, and the frequent need for replacement due to the relatively high particulate loading in the water entering pretreatment. It is frequently used as a "final clean-up" after other pretreatment processes such as ion exchange or carbon bed filtration, and before going to a final treatment step such as reverse osmosis.

As a "final clean-up," the barrier is often cartridges with a nominal particle size retention of 1-10 μm for removal of particulate carry-over. If the objective is to remove insoluble forms of silica and iron and achieve a SDI (silt density index) of less than 5 for feed to an RO system, the absolute particle size retention might be <1 μm -- at the lower end of microfiltration or upper end of ultrafiltration.

Ultrafiltration (0.1-0.001 μm) and nanofiltration (0.005-0.0005 μm) are methods of barrier filtration. They are not used for primary removal of particulates due to cost and rapid blinding of the membrane.

Advantages: Low initial cost; can vary particle size retention by cartridge selection.

Disadvantages: Cartridge filtration is expensive for high solids concentration; potential for microbial growth in a non-chlorinated environment.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：阻隔過濾的定位

阻隔過濾在前處理中扮演的是「收尾清潔」角色，而非「主力去除」。原因很簡單：進水微粒量大，頻繁更換濾芯的成本太高。

典型配置是放在軟化器或活性碳之後、RO 之前，保護下游膜系統不被顆粒堵塞。

重點提示：SDI < 5 的意義

SDI（Silt Density Index，淤泥密度指數）是 RO 進水品質的關鍵指標。SDI < 5 是大多數 RO 膜製造商的最低要求，SDI < 3 為理想值。

如果水中有不溶性矽或鐵，可能需要用絕對過濾精度 <1 μm 的濾芯或超過濾（UF）來達到此目標。

比喻說明：過濾如同門禁系統

把水系統想成一棟大樓：
介質過濾（Media Filtration）= 大門口的保全，擋掉大部分訪客（粗顆粒）
阻隔過濾（Barrier Filtration）= 電梯前的門禁卡，只讓通過安檢的人進入（細顆粒把關）
RO 膜= 總裁辦公室的指紋鎖，最高等級過濾

每一層都有成本，所以先用便宜的保全擋掉大部分，再用昂貴的門禁做精細把關。

11.4.2 Hardness and Metals — Ion Exchange（硬度與金屬離子交換）

Original Text

11.4.2.1 Water Softening

An ion exchange system consists of a tank containing small beads of synthetic resin treated to selectively absorb either cations or anions and exchange these ions based upon their relative activity. The process will continue until all available exchange sites are filled, at which point the resin is exhausted and must be regenerated.

The presence of calcium (Ca) and magnesium (Mg) in a water supply is commonly known as "hardness," usually expressed in grains per gallon (gpg). Ion exchange is the principal method of removing hardness. This is required to prevent scale formation in final treatment operations such as RO and distillation.

A standard water softener has four major components: a resin tank, resin, a brine tank, and a valve or controller. The resin beads initially absorb sodium ions during brine regeneration. The resin has a greater affinity for multi-valence ions such as calcium and magnesium than for sodium. As hard water passes through the resin, Ca, Mg, and other multivalent ions adhere to the resin, releasing sodium ions until equilibrium is reached.

Regeneration is achieved by passing a sodium chloride (NaCl) solution through the resin, exchanging the hardness ions for sodium ions. The resin's affinity for hardness ions is overcome by using a highly concentrated brine solution.

Advantages: Industry standard, low cost and effective. Works well in a chlorinated environment. Inherent flushing of some microbial growth during regeneration.

Disadvantages: Salt handling for brine regeneration and disposal of spent brine solution.

11.4.2.2 Demineralization/De-ionization

Three multivalent ions present unusual removal problems:

a) Iron — May change valence states from water-soluble ferrous to insoluble ferric state. Organisms such as Crenothrix, Sphaerotilus, and Gallionella use iron as an energy source, forming a rusty, gelatinous sludge that plugs piping and membranes.

b) Silica — May be soluble ionized species or insoluble material, sometimes as colloidal mixture. Insoluble silica removed by barrier filtration <0.5 μm (UF) or by nanofiltration/RO.

c) Aluminum — Can exist in multiple valences; its solubility is a function of pH. Between pH 6-8, hydrated aluminum oxides are only partially (~80%) rejected by RO, often leading to fouling.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：軟化的原理與必要性

軟化（Softening）是前處理中最基礎的步驟之一。目的很明確：去除鈣和鎂，防止下游 RO 膜或蒸餾器結垢（scaling）。

原理是離子交換：樹脂上的鈉離子（Na+）與水中的鈣（Ca2+）和鎂（Mg2+）交換。因為樹脂對二價離子的親和力大於單價離子，所以交換自然發生。

當所有交換位點都被佔滿，就需要用高濃度鹽水（brine）再生，把鈣鎂沖下來、重新裝上鈉離子。

比喻說明：軟化器如同停車場

想像樹脂是一個停車場，每個車位上停著一台小車（Na+）。大型車輛（Ca2+, Mg2+）進來時，因為體積大（多價），會自動擠走小車佔據車位。

當車位全被大車佔滿（樹脂耗竭），管理員就用一大批小車（高濃度 NaCl）湧入，用數量優勢把大車逼走。這就是「再生」。

重點提示：三大「問題離子」

  • 鐵（Fe）：會變價！溶解態（Fe2+）遇氧氣變成不溶態（Fe3+）沉澱，堵塞膜系統。某些細菌還會以鐵為能源，產生鐵鏽狀膠體。
  • 矽（Si）：同時有離子態和膠體態。離子態軟化器可減少，膠體態需要 UF（<0.5 μm）或 NF/RO 去除。
  • 鋁（Al）：pH 6-8 之間最棘手，RO 只能去除約 80%。如果鋁是主要問題，可能需要「軟化 + pH 調整 + RO」的三步驟組合。

  2. 你的水源分析報告顯示鐵含量偏高且 pH 為 7.5，你會建議哪種前處理組合？為什麼？
11.4.3 Hardness and Metals — Other Processes（其他硬度去除程序）

Original Text

11.4.3.1 Acidification and Degasification

Use of a pH adjustment step is common to favor or inhibit the formation of CO2. Injecting acid to a pH of approximately 5.5 will maximize the concentration of CO2, whereas addition of a base to pH 8.3 will minimize CO2, converting it to carbonate ion.

If high levels of CO2 are present, it can be removed down to about 5-10 ppm with an atmospheric degasifier. An atmospheric degasifier has the potential of increasing bacterial burden and should be located where bacterial control measures are available.

The incoming water is adjusted to a pH in the range of 3.8-4.2 with sulfuric acid. The acidified water is sent to a packed column degasifier for removal of free CO2 by air. Removal efficiency is better than 98%. The pH after removal of CO2 will be in the range of 6.5-7.0. Just prior to feeding the RO unit, the pH should be adjusted to approximately 8.0-8.5.

Advantages: Replaces softener and handling of large amounts of salt; CO2 released to atmosphere; the added sulfate ion from acidification is easier to remove in RO than added sodium ion from softening.

Disadvantages: Handling acid for acidification; instrumentation and chemical handling for two pH adjustments.

11.4.3.2 Nanofiltration

Nanofiltration is a pressure driven membrane process with performance characteristics between RO and ultrafiltration. The theoretical pore size is one nanometer (10-9 meter). These membranes are sometimes referred to as "softening membranes".

The nanofiltration membrane offers high rejection of salts of divalent anions as well as organics with molecular weights above 200. Operating at 70-150 psig (4.76-10.2 bar) vs. 200-350 psig for RO.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：酸化脫氣法的邏輯

這個程序的核心思路是：用酸把碳酸鹽/重碳酸鹽全部轉化為 CO2 氣體，然後用脫氣塔吹掉。

步驟：加酸（pH 降到 3.8-4.2）→ 脫氣塔去除 CO2（>98%）→ 加鹼（pH 升到 8.0-8.5）→ 進 RO

這個方法可以取代軟化器，特別適合大流量（>50 gpm）或高硬度（>50 ppm）的場景。

重點提示：脫氣塔的微生物風險

脫氣塔會引入空氣，空氣中的細菌會進入水中。解決方案：

  • 在進氣管加裝 HEPA 過濾器
  • 將脫氣塔設置在有微生物控制措施的位置（例如兩段式 RO 之間）
  • 脫氣後的水通常收集在儲水槽中，可在此進行 TOC 去除和微生物控制

實務應用：奈米過濾 vs. 軟化器 vs. 酸化脫氣

奈米過濾典型去除率

規律：分子量越大、價數越高，去除率越高。單價離子如 NaCl 只有 60%。

11.4.4 Organics and Microbiological Impurities（有機物與微生物雜質）

Original Text

11.4.4.1 Introduction

Organic and microbiological contaminants need to be addressed in water treatment systems. The concerns are twofold: contaminants entering the system and contaminants created/growing in the system.

The first issue is water source: well water typically has low organic loading; surface water (lake, river, reservoir) will probably contain relatively high organics with seasonal variation. Municipal water is usually chlorinated, keeping microbiological content low until chlorine/chloramine is removed.

11.4.4.2 Organic Contaminants

a) Bacterial Contamination — Usually expressed as "total viable microbial counts per ml" or Colony Forming Units (CFU) per unit volume.

b) Pyrogenic Contamination — Pyrogens are substances that can produce fever in mammals. Often endotoxins (lipopolysaccharides) shed by bacterial cells. Molecular weight generally 12,000 to 320,000. Quantified in Endotoxin Units (EU) per milliliter. Chemically and physically stable — not necessarily destroyed by conditions that kill bacteria.

c) Total Organic Carbon (TOC) — A measure of organic materials in mg/l or μg/l. A direct measure of oxidizable organic material. Not a good measure of microbial contamination.

d) Dissolved Organic Compounds — Naturally occurring organics include Tannin, Humic acid, and Fulvic acids. Under conditions of free halogen compounds, they form trihalomethanes (THMs), suspected carcinogens.

11.4.4.3 Removal of Organics

Technologies available include:

  • Ozone — Twice as powerful an oxidant as chlorine. Not frequently used in pretreatment due to material compatibility issues.
  • Strong Base Ion Exchange — Organic scavengers that absorb organics with slightly negative charge. Regenerated with NaCl brine.
  • Carbon — Activated carbon adsorbs organics. Periodically replaced when capacity diminishes.
  • Microfiltration — Depth cartridge, pleated, and cross-flow membrane filters; 100 μm down to 0.1 μm. Captures bacteria, giardia cyst, large MW organics.
  • Ultrafiltration — 0.1 to 0.001 μm; removes organics, bacteria, viruses, pyrogens. Cross-flow design is self-cleaning.
  • RO — Also removes organics and microbiological impurities through membrane rejection and purge stream.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：有機物與微生物的雙重來源

ISPE 強調水系統的有機/微生物污染有兩個來源：

  1. 外部進入：隨原水帶入的有機物、細菌、熱原
  2. 內部生長：系統內部滋生的微生物（特別是去除氯之後）

這個「雙重來源」概念是水系統設計的基本思維框架。每個設備都要同時考慮「能去除多少外部污染」和「自身會製造多少內部污染」。

11.4.4.4 Control of Microbiological Growth & Chlorine Management（微生物控制與氯管理）

Original Text

Periodic Sanitization

Methods include heat, chemical sanitization, regeneration or replacement of media, flushing, or drainage. With heat, USP indicator organisms are killed above 60°C; temperatures above 80°C result in complete kill. Sanitization times might be 1-2 hours at temperature, with total cycle time of 4-8 hours.

Chemical sanitization agents include hydrogen peroxide, iodine, ammonium compounds, and per-oxygen compounds. Temperatures below 15°C slow microbial growth.

Pressurized Carbon Dioxide

During shutdown periods, cartridge filters and RO are deadheaded and pressurized with CO2 to 2-4 bar, lowering pH to <5.5 to inhibit microbial growth while also dissolving scaling chemicals. This has the potential to eliminate the need for a softener before the RO.

Ultraviolet Light (UV)

UV light de-activates DNA in microbes preventing duplication. Feed water must be free of suspended solids which can "shadow" bacteria. UV does not leave a residual in treated water. Typically used for controlling feed water to an RO unit that cannot accept chlorine or heat.

Chlorine

Municipalities use chlorine at dosage levels of 0.2 to 2.0 ppm. Chlorine can adversely affect: polyamide membranes (oxidative deterioration), DI resins (degradation), and stainless steel (corrosion at elevated temperatures). Two principal methods of chlorine removal:

  • Activated Carbon — Removes chlorine by adsorption; bed depths of 2-3 feet at 2-4 gpm/ft3. Creates perfect conditions for microbial growth: slow flow in warm media with nutrients. Heat sanitization (190°F hot water) required daily to weekly.
  • Reduction — Sodium bisulfite reduces chlorine to chloride: SO3- + Cl2 + H2O → 2Cl- + 2H+ + SO4=

Chloramines

Chloramines (formed by chlorine + ammonia) are longer-acting disinfectants. Removal by carbon requires hydraulic rates 3-6X less than for chlorine. Removal results in dissociation to ammonium ion and ammonia. It may be desirable to locate the carbon bed before the water softener if chloramines are present.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：微生物控制的多層策略

前處理系統的微生物控制不是靠單一方法，而是多層防禦：

  • 持續性控制：氯/氯胺（進水端）、UV（無氯環境）
  • 週期性控制：定期消毒（熱、化學、再生）
  • 設計控制：避免死角、維持循環、溫度管理（<15°C 或 >80°C）

重點提示：活性碳 -- 最大的矛盾

活性碳是前處理中最矛盾的設備：它去除氯（保護下游膜），但同時也去除了抑制微生物的武器。結果就是：

  • 碳床本身成為微生物的溫床（慢流速 + 暖溫 + 營養物質 = 完美培養基）
  • 必須定期熱消毒：每日到每週 1-2 次，用 190°F（88°C）熱水或蒸汽
  • 消毒後需要沖洗去除碳粉（fines）

這就是為什麼活性碳的位置和消毒計畫是水系統設計的關鍵決策之一。

氯胺 vs. 氯的去除差異

氯胺比氯難去除 3-6 倍！如果你的市政水源使用氯胺消毒：

  • 碳床需要 3-6 倍的接觸時間（床體更大或流速更低）
  • 去除後會產生銨離子（NH4+）和氨（NH3），需要後續離子交換去除
  • 碳床可能需要放在軟化器之前（而非通常的之後），讓軟化器順便去除銨離子

設計時務必先確認水源是使用氯還是氯胺消毒。

CO2 加壓停機法 -- 創新做法

這是一個聰明的節能方案：在停機期間用 CO2 加壓（2-4 bar），同時達成三個目標：

  1. 降低 pH 到 <5.5 -- 抑制微生物生長
  2. 溶解碳酸鈣等結垢物質 -- 清洗 RO 膜
  3. 節省停機期間的水和電力消耗

啟動前只需將酸性水和溶解的鹽類沖掉即可。如果停機頻率與 RO 結垢頻率匹配，甚至可能省掉軟化器。

11.4.5-11.4.6 pH, Carbon Dioxide & Feed Water pH（pH、二氧化碳與進水 pH 管理）

Original Text

11.4.5 pH and Carbon Dioxide

pH, the negative log of the hydrogen ion concentration, affects the chemistry of the water: it can tell us if the water will corrode piping or if contaminants (carbonates) are likely to precipitate and cause scaling.

Free carbon dioxide in water is produced by decay of organic matter, dissolution from underground sources, and solution from the atmosphere. In high purity water, low levels of CO2 from the atmosphere can cause pH to drop from 7.0 to 5.5 and conductivity to increase from 0.1 μS to 1 μS.

The equilibrium between carbonate, bicarbonate, and carbon dioxide is affected by pH. As pH is lowered, equilibrium shifts toward CO2. As pH is increased, equilibrium shifts toward bicarbonate and then carbonate.

11.4.6 Importance of Feed Water pH

EPA drinking water standards require pH within 6.5-8.5. Key considerations:

  • If feed pH < 8.3, analyze for CO2 — the lower the pH from 8.3, the higher the dissolved CO2 potential.
  • CO2 passes directly through pretreatment and RO membrane, depressing conductivity and pH, making it difficult to meet USP requirements.
  • Feed pH 6-10: RO has potential for hardness scaling. Adding acid controls scale but creates CO2; adding base converts bicarbonate to carbonate, removed by RO but may cause scaling.
  • Most pharmaceutical companies use ion exchange softening to prevent scaling in RO.
  • Chloramine in feed water can result in ammonia, which affects conductivity and pH in final product.

Common pH adjustment chemicals: sulfuric acid (readily available, less corrosive) and sodium hydroxide (readily available, sodium removed by final treatment).

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：pH-碳酸鹽平衡系統

這是水化學中最關鍵的平衡之一，直接影響水系統設計：

  • pH < 5.5：幾乎全部是 CO2（中性氣體），可以用脫氣塔去除
  • pH 5.5-8.3：CO2 和 HCO3- 共存
  • pH 8.3-10：幾乎全部是 HCO3-（離子），可以被 RO 或離子交換去除
  • pH > 10：主要是 CO32-

設計前處理系統時，要根據 pH 決定用什麼方法處理碳酸鹽系統。

重點提示：CO2 對製藥用水的致命影響

CO2 是一個「隱形殺手」：

  • 它可以直接穿透 RO 膜（因為是中性分子，不帶電荷）
  • 微量 CO2 就能讓純水 pH 從 7.0 降到 5.5，電導率從 0.1 μS 升到 1 μS
  • 這可能導致水質無法通過 USP 電導率測試
  • 對兩段式 RO 系統，即使微量 CO2 也可能無法產出 <1 μS 的水

因此，了解進水 pH 和 CO2 含量是系統設計的第一步。

比喻說明：pH 調整如同交通疏導

想像碳酸鹽系統中的三種形態（CO2、HCO3-、CO32-）是三條道路上的車流。

加酸 = 把所有車都引導到「CO2 高速公路」上，然後用脫氣塔這個「出口匝道」把車送走。缺點：出口擁擠時（高 CO2），部分車輛會「溜進」RO 系統。

加鹼 = 把車都引導到「碳酸鹽大道」上，RO 膜可以攔截離子態的車輛。缺點：太多車聚集可能「堵車」（結垢）。

因此多數藥廠選擇「軟化器」先去除容易結垢的鈣鎂，再讓 RO 處理剩餘離子。

11.5 Final Treatment: Ion Exchange for Purified Water（最終處理：離子交換）

Original Text

11.5.1.1 Ion Exchange Use in USP Systems

The primary purpose of ion exchange equipment in USP high purity water systems is to satisfy the ionic quality portion of the specification. Ion exchange can effectively reduce organics with proper resin selection, but may not meet USP TOC requirements without additional membrane processes where high feed water TOC levels exist.

11.5.1.2 Functionality

Cation Exchange: Exchange of cations (Ca, Mg, Na, etc.) for hydrogen ion (H+). Regenerated with dilute acid (H2SO4 or HCl). All salts converted to corresponding acids.

Anion Exchange: Exchange of anions (SO4=, HCO3-, Cl-, etc.) for hydroxide ions (OH-). Regenerated with NaOH. Following cation exchange, this completely demineralizes water.

Strongly ionized resins: Greater affinity for all ionized constituents, capable of removing weakly ionized species (acetates, silica). Capacities: 18 and 14 kgs as CaCO3 per ft3 for cation and anion respectively.

Weakly ionized resins: Ineffective at removing weakly ionized constituents but have 2-3X the exchange capacity and can be regenerated more efficiently.

Two-bed deionizers: Separate columns for cation and anion resin. Simple regeneration. Types: co-current (regeneration same direction as process water) and counter-current (opposite direction; produces higher quality water with ~50% less chemicals).

Mixed-bed deionizers: One column with both resins intimately mixed. Functions as infinite series of two-bed exchangers. Superior removal of monovalent ions. More complex regeneration requiring resin separation and mixing.

11.5.1.3 Operating Parameters

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：離子交換的完整去離子化

前處理中的「軟化」只用陽離子交換（Na 型），而最終處理中的離子交換是完整的去離子化：

  1. 陽離子交換（H+ 型）：Ca2+, Mg2+, Na+ → H+（水變成含酸的溶液）
  2. 陰離子交換（OH- 型）：SO42-, Cl-, HCO3- → OH-
  3. H+ + OH- → H2O（純水）

這就是「去離子」（Deionization / DI）的基本原理。

重點提示：Two-Bed vs. Mixed-Bed 的選擇

  • Two-bed（雙床）：再生簡單，適合大量處理。逆流再生型可產出 0.2 μS/cm 的水，化學品用量減半。
  • Mixed-bed（混床）：產出水質最好（可達 0.055 μS/cm，接近純水理論值 0.055），但再生複雜（需要分離、混合樹脂）。
  • 常見配置是 RO + Mixed-bed polisher：RO 去除 90-99% 離子，混床做最後把關。

Counter-Current vs. Co-Current 再生

逆流再生（Counter-Current）是現代離子交換的優選：

  • 化學品用量減少約 50%
  • 產出水質更高
  • 原理：再生液從出水端進入，最後接觸最乾淨的樹脂層，確保出水端永遠是最新鮮的樹脂

比喻說明：Mixed-bed 如同咖啡混合豆

Two-bed 像是先喝一杯酸咖啡（陽離子交換後的酸性水），再喝一杯鹼咖啡（陰離子交換後的鹼性水），最後在嘴裡中和。

Mixed-bed 像是把兩種咖啡豆磨在一起沖泡：每一口都同時中和酸鹼，所以每一口的品質都極高。這就是為什麼 Mixed-bed 的產出水質遠優於 Two-bed。

11.5.1.4-11.5.1.10 Ion Exchange Components, Regeneration & Sanitization（離子交換組件、再生與消毒）

Original Text

11.5.1.4 Component Description

An ion exchange system comprises: tank(s), ion exchange resin, piping and valve system, water/chemical distributors internal to tank(s), regeneration system, and conductivity or resistivity meter. Available in on-site regenerable and off-site regenerable (rechargeable) versions.

Tanks may be constructed from fiberglass, stainless steel, or carbon steel with inert interior lining (vulcanized rubber or PVC). Off-site units: fiberglass or light gauge SS, sizes <1 ft3 to 50 ft3 per tank.

11.5.1.5 Tanks

For pharmaceutical applications, tanks rarely need to be more than 3-4 feet (0.91-1.22 m) in diameter with straight shells of 6-8 feet (1.82-2.44 m). Steel tanks welded per ASME Code for operating pressures between 100-150 psig (7-10.5 kgs/cm2).

11.5.1.6 Distributors

Each tank includes distributors at all pipe-to-tank interfaces. Required to prevent resin escape and provide adequate flow distribution. Materials: stainless steel, PVC, CPVC, polypropylene, or PVDF. Structural integrity is a key design element — a ruptured distributor can cause significant resin loss.

11.5.1.7 Piping and Valves

Most systems use Schedule 80 PVC or CPVC. Specialty plastics (polypropylene, PVDF) offer lower organic leachables. Stainless steel offers structural integrity and hot water/steam sanitization capability, but is more vulnerable to corrosion from acid/caustic.

11.5.1.8 Regeneration System

Requires chemical pumps/eductors, chemical tanks, piping, and instrumentation. Positive displacement pumps driven by electric motors or compressed air are ideally suited.

11.5.1.9 Microbiological Concerns

Regeneration of resin beds effectively sanitizes the system; however, as resin exhausts, pH approaches neutral, and organic matter fosters bacteria growth. Regeneration frequency is critical for systems without auxiliary microbiological control (UV lights). Polishing systems positioned with sub-micron filters and UV may operate several weeks without regeneration.

11.5.1.10 Advantages and Disadvantages

Advantages: Flexibility in flow rate; consistent Stage 1 conductivity; regeneration doubles as microbial control; extensive installed base.

Disadvantages: Acid/caustic storage and handling; waste neutralization; reduced ability to reduce dissolved organics vs. membrane systems.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

重點提示：分配器是隱藏的關鍵

Distributor（分配器）是離子交換槽中最容易被忽略但最關鍵的零件：

  • 功能：防止樹脂洩漏 + 均勻分配水流
  • 材質選擇：SS、PVC、CPVC、PP、PVDF
  • 破裂的分配器 = 災難：大量樹脂洩漏、修復耗時、可能汙染下游系統

設計時要特別注意結構強度，定期檢查是維護重點。

管材選擇的取捨

  • PVC/CPVC：低成本、耐腐蝕，但有機物溶出較多
  • PP/PVDF：有機物溶出低，管路設計可接近衛生級不鏽鋼焊接，但成本更高
  • 不鏽鋼：結構強、可熱消毒，但接觸酸鹼有腐蝕風險

選擇取決於預算、產品水質要求、以及消毒方式。如果需要熱消毒 → 不鏽鋼；如果重視低溶出 → PVDF。

微生物控制與再生頻率

離子交換系統的微生物控制策略是「以再生代替消毒」：酸鹼再生液（極端 pH）本身就能殺菌。

但隨著樹脂耗竭，pH 趨近中性，加上有機物沉積，微生物就會開始增長。因此：

  • 沒有 UV 輔助的系統：再生頻率必須考量微生物因素，不能只看離子耗竭程度
  • 有 UV + 微過濾的系統（polishing）：可以延長再生週期到數週

11.5.2 Reverse Osmosis (RO) for High Purity Water Applications（高純水用逆滲透系統）

Original Text

11.5.2.1 Description

RO is a pressure driven process utilizing a semi-permeable membrane capable of removing dissolved organic and inorganic contaminants. Commercial membranes available in spiral wound and hollow fiber configurations. Two basic materials:

  • Cellulose acetate (CA) — Oldest commercially; lowest initial cost; chlorine tolerant; disadvantages include fastest loss of rejection, requires pH 5-6, and free chlorine in feed stream. Higher pressure (300-500 psig) and temperature (60-80°F).
  • Thin film composite (TFC/polyamide) — Highest rejection; works at low temperatures (40°F+) and low pressure (150 psig+); intolerant of chlorine. Majority of pharmaceutical systems use TFC membranes.

Most spiral wound membranes incorporate a brine seal at the leading end. The seal can cause bacterial problems due to stagnant water conditions. Some membranes are produced without a brine seal ("loose wrap" or "full fit") — superior for pharmaceutical RO units as bacterial contamination is minimized.

11.5.2.2 Application

RO can be utilized upstream of deionizers to reduce chemical consumption, or as product staged (two-pass) RO to produce water meeting USP TOC and conductivity requirements. Some installations produce Stage 1 conductivity (on-line measurement); others pass Stage 2 or 3 (laboratory testing).

11.5.2.3 Instrumentation and Control

Even small changes in feed pH can have dramatic effects on final conductivity. Monitoring parameters: feed/permeate conductivity, permeate flow, waste flow, feed temperature, pressures (feed, concentrate, permeate). Instruments must be calibrated, NIST traceable. Protective devices for pumps (low suction) and membranes (high pressure, high temperature).

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

重點提示：Brine Seal 的微生物風險

傳統 RO 膜元件前端的 brine seal（鹽水密封圈）會在膜與壓力容器之間創造滯留水區，成為微生物溫床。

製藥級 RO 系統應優先選用無 brine seal 的膜（loose wrap / full fit），允許少量受控水流通過膜與容器之間，消除滯留區。

兩段式 RO（Product Staged）的架構

單段 RO 通常無法達到 USP 純水標準。兩段式 RO 的水路：

原水 → 前處理 → 第一段 RO（去除 90-99%）→ 第二段 RO（再去除 90-99%）→ 產品水

兩段式 RO 的產出水電阻率通常可達 0.5 M&ohm;/cm 以上，足以符合 USP Purified Water。部分系統甚至能通過 Stage 1 電導率測試（可線上量測）。

RO 系統的關鍵監測參數

  • 進水/產水電導率 → 計算即時去除率
  • 產水流量 + 廢水流量 → 回收率
  • 進水溫度 → 影響膜通量
  • 壓力（進水/濃水/產水） → 膜狀態指標
  • 膜間壓力 + 電導率 → 結垢和劣化指標
  • ORP / 殘氯 → 保護 TFC 膜

所有儀表必須校正且可追溯至 NIST。

核心概念：RO 膜材質的選擇

這是 RO 系統設計的第一個也是最重要的決策：

結論：TFC 膜是製藥的主流選擇，但前提是必須先去除氯。

11.5.2.4-11.5.2.8 RO Limitations, Scaling, Cleaning & Performance（RO 限制、結垢、清洗與效能）

Original Text

11.5.2.4 Limitations

RO cannot remove 100% of contaminants and has very low removal capability for some extremely low molecular weight dissolved organics. A concentrate flow is always necessary. Typical recovery: 75%; small units may be lower; large systems up to 85%.

Carbon dioxide passes directly through the RO membrane — for design purposes, CO2 will be in the product at the same level as in the feed. Excess CO2 in product may cause quality problems or increase anion resin loading.

Present RO technology requires ambient operation with occasional sanitization. Operating at ambient temperatures can result in biological growth. The inability to continuously operate at high temperature (80°C) is the most significant factor limiting RO use in WFI applications.

Performance is dependent on numerous O-rings between membrane elements and housings. O-ring slippage may result in poor water quality, detected by high conductivity.

11.5.2.5 Scaling

Scale formation is predicted by calculation using concentrate stream levels of calcium, alkalinity, TDS, and temperature. Proper pH to operate without antiscalant is minimum 0.5 pH units below the scaling pH.

Antiscalant chemicals increase the time necessary for crystal formation of precipitate. Various membranes have different maximum operating temperatures. Many systems utilize feed water preheating to optimize membrane area and minimize pumping energy.

11.5.2.6 Cleaning and Sanitization

Virtually all RO units need periodic cleaning. Acid cleaners for metals/salts; alkaline detergent for silt/organics. Cleaning frequency should not exceed four times per year if pretreatment is working properly. Chlorine tolerant membranes: chlorine solutions. Non-chlorine tolerant: peracetic acid/hydrogen peroxide. Some membranes allow thermal sanitization at 80°C.

11.5.2.7 Purification Capability

Single stage RO reduces raw water salts by 90-99%; colloids, bacteria, endotoxin reduced by 1-3 logs. Single pass may not meet USP Purified Water. Product staged (two-pass) RO can produce water exceeding USP PW or WFI in most applications, typically ≥0.5 M&ohm;/cm.

11.5.2.8 Performance

Sufficient membrane area is required. SDI reading of 3 or less is recommended. Higher membrane area for fixed flow = lower fouling rate (though recovery is a factor). Design must optimize membrane area vs. maintenance and cleaning requirements.

Advantages: Reduces/eliminates chemical handling; better TOC reduction than IX alone; membrane barrier to bacteria, endotoxin, organics, salts.

Disadvantages: Water consumption (high waste flow); maintenance can be expensive if pretreatment does not properly handle raw water changes.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：RO 的四大限制

  1. CO2 穿透：中性分子直接通過膜，影響產水電導率和 pH
  2. 無法 100% 去除：始終需要濃水排放（通常 25%），且極低分子量有機物難去除
  3. 不能持續高溫運行：常溫運行有微生物生長風險，這是 RO 在 WFI 應用中最大的限制
  4. O-ring 洩漏風險：膜元件之間的密封圈鬆脫會導致水質急劇下降

重點提示：清洗頻率是前處理品質的指標

ISPE 明確指出：如果前處理系統設計和運作正常，RO 清洗頻率不應超過每年 4 次。

如果你的 RO 清洗頻率明顯超過這個標準，問題不在 RO 本身，而在前處理系統。需要回頭檢查：

  • 軟化器是否正常再生？（硬度結垢）
  • 碳床是否有效去除有機物？（有機物汙堵）
  • 微粒過濾是否到位？（SDI > 3？）
  • 進水 pH 是否在正確範圍？

RO 效能關鍵計算

Recovery (回收率) = 產水流量 / 進水流量 x 100%
  典型值：75%（小型可能更低，大型可達 85%）

Salt Rejection (鹽去除率) = (1 - 產水電導率/進水電導率) x 100%
  單段：90-99%
  兩段串聯：99-99.9%

SDI (淤泥密度指數) 目標值：
  理想值 < 3
  最大可接受值 < 5

比喻說明：RO 清洗如同汽車保養

酸洗 = 用除鏽劑清除鈣鎂結垢（如同去除車底盤鏽蝕）

鹼洗 = 用清潔劑去除有機物和淤泥（如同清洗引擎油垢）

通常兩者依序執行確保徹底清洗。如果你的車需要每個月進廠大修，不是車的問題，而是路的問題（前處理不佳）。

Section Summary（本節總結）

Key Takeaways

Pretreatment Process Summary

  • Barrier filtration: Used as "final clean-up" before RO; target SDI < 5
  • Water softening: Ion exchange (Na-form) removes Ca/Mg hardness to prevent scaling
  • Problem ions: Iron (valence change), Silica (multiple forms), Aluminum (pH-dependent solubility)
  • Acidification/degasification: Alternative to softener for high-flow/high-hardness applications
  • Nanofiltration: "Softening membrane" at lower pressure than RO
  • Organics control: Dual concern — incoming contamination AND internal growth
  • Microbial control: Multi-layer approach (chlorine, UV, heat, design)
  • pH/CO2 management: Critical for downstream RO/IX performance

Final Treatment Summary

  • Ion exchange: Two-bed (simple) vs. Mixed-bed (highest quality); co-current vs. counter-current regeneration
  • RO: TFC membranes preferred; two-pass for USP PW; recovery ~75%
  • RO limitations: CO2 pass-through; cannot run continuously at 80°C; O-ring risks
  • Cleaning indicator: >4 cleanings/year = pretreatment problem

學習重點回顧

本節核心心得

  1. 前處理決定最終處理的壽命：前處理做得好，RO 膜用 3-5 年；做不好，每季就要清洗甚至更換。
  2. 水源分析是起點：井水 vs. 地表水、硬度、鐵/矽/鋁含量、氯 vs. 氯胺——這些決定了整個前處理的架構。
  3. 氯的矛盾：要靠氯控制微生物，但氯又會破壞下游膜和樹脂。去除氯的時機和方法是關鍵設計決策。
  4. CO2 不可忽視：它穿透 RO 膜、影響電導率、增加離子交換負擔。pH 管理直接影響 CO2 行為。
  5. IX vs. RO 的選擇：IX 化學品處理複雜但流量彈性大；RO 減少化學品但有廢水和微生物問題。多數現代系統採用 RO + IX polisher 組合。
