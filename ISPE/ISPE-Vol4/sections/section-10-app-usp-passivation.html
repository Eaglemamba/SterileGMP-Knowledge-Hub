11.1 USP Regulated Water Quality / USP 法規水質

Original Text

11.1.1 USP Three-Stage Conductivity Levels

In order to set the maximum allowable conductivity, USP determined the limit concentration of the least conductive water attribute species used in USP 22 to be chloride at 0.47 ppm and 1.01 µS/cm at the standard temperature. When actual measurements of conductivity are evaluated at temperatures above the standard 25°C, the least conductive water attributes between pH 5 and 7, changes to favor ammonia at the maximum acceptable concentration of 0.3 ppm, using chloride as the electro-neutrality-balancing counter ion.

Stage 1 — In-Line Test

Primarily intended as an in-line test. Measure water conductivity and temperature using a non-compensated conductivity sensor using a suitable container or an in-line measurement.

Stage 2 — Off-Line Grab Sample

If the sample fails the Stage 1 test, adjust the temperature to 25°C and stir. If the sample stabilizes to the minimum conductivity value listed in the Stage 3 table, the water meets the requirements.

Stage 3 — Laboratory Test

Measure pH within 5 minutes of the Stage 2 conductivity reading after increasing its ionic strength to allow a pH reading, using saturated potassium chloride solution at 3%.

Commentary / 教學解說

三階段電導率測試架構

核心概念：為什麼要三階段？

USP 設計三階段測試是為了在效率與準確性之間取得平衡：

  • Stage 1（線上）：快速篩檢，90%以上的樣品在此階段通過
  • Stage 2（離線）：溫度標準化到 25°C，排除溫度影響
  • Stage 3（實驗室）：加入 pH 測量，考慮鹼度對電導率的影響

生活比喻

就像機場安檢的三道關卡：第一道是金屬探測門（快速通過），第二道是手持掃描器（進一步確認），第三道是開箱檢查（完整檢驗）。大多數旅客在第一道就通過了。

實務重點

  • Stage 1 使用非溫度補償的感測器 — 這是刻意設計，因為限值已經按溫度區間設定
  • Stage 2 失敗才需要做 Stage 3 — 不是每個樣品都需要做到底
  • Stage 3 的 pH 測量必須在電導率讀數後 5 分鐘內完成，避免 CO2 吸收影響

11.1.3 In-Line (Stage 1) Conductivity Limits

Based on the chloride-ammonia model at the least conductive water attributes between pH 5 and 7.

電導率限值表解讀

溫度與電導率的關係

水溫越高，離子活動度越大，電導率自然越高。USP 的限值隨溫度上升而放寬：

  • 0°C 時限值僅 0.6 µS/cm
  • 25°C 時限值為 1.3 µS/cm（標準參考點）
  • 100°C 時限值放寬到 3.1 µS/cm

常見陷阱

  • 熱水循環系統（80°C）的線上電導率 2.5 µS/cm 是合格的 — 不要用 25°C 的標準來判斷
  • 75-94.9°C 合併為一個區間（2.7 µS/cm），這是因為在此高溫範圍內變化較小
  • 實際操作中，良好系統的電導率通常遠低於限值

Stage 2 & 3 補充

Stage 2：將樣品調到 25°C 測量，限值為 2.1 µS/cm。

Stage 3：在 25°C 下測量 pH，再對照 pH-電導率對照表判定。此階段考慮了大氣 CO2 溶入的影響。

11.1.5-11.1.6 Total Organic Carbon (TOC) / 總有機碳

Original Text

11.1.5 TOC Requirements

TOC is an indirect measure, as carbon, of organic molecules present in high purity water. A TOC limit was determined by USP to be 0.5 ppm or 500 ppb, based on the results of studies and an industry wide survey of pharmaceutical water systems.

Instruments are available for measuring TOC in-line from slip streams and from grab samples. USP have not prevented acceptable technologies from being used, but limit the methods to instruments that are capable of:

  • Completely oxidizing the organic molecules to carbon dioxide (CO2)
  • Measuring the CO2 levels as carbon
  • Discriminating between Inorganic Carbon (IC) and CO2 from organic oxidation
  • Maintaining an equipment limit of detection of 0.05 mg/l or lower
  • Periodically demonstrating equipment "suitability"
Commentary / 教學解說

TOC 測量基礎

什麼是 TOC？

Total Organic Carbon（總有機碳）是水中有機物含量的間接指標。USP 限值為 500 ppb。

基本原理：將水中有機物完全氧化為 CO2，再量測 CO2 的濃度換算回碳含量。

TC = TOC + IC（Total Carbon = 有機碳 + 無機碳）

生活比喻

TOC 測量就像燃燒垃圾來秤重：你把所有有機物燒成 CO2，量測 CO2 的量，就知道原本有多少有機物。但要先扣除水中原本就有的 CO2（無機碳），才能得到真正的有機碳含量。

偵測極限的重要性

USP 要求儀器的偵測極限 ≤0.05 mg/L（50 ppb），這是限值 500 ppb 的 1/10。在製藥用水中，IC 通常很低，不需要特別去除。

11.1.6 Types of TOC Analyzers

Oxidation Methods

CO2 Detection Methods

Four CO2 detection methods are currently used:

  • NDIR (Non-Dispersive Infrared) — measures IR absorption of CO2 in gas phase; dynamic range ~1.5-2 orders of magnitude; LOD 2-10 ppb
  • CO2 Selective Conductivity — uses special membrane to diffuse CO2 into DI water collector; calibration stability 6-12 months; LOD 0.05 ppb
  • Direct Conductivity — measures conductivity of sample directly; requires water to be CO2/DI water equilibrium only; LOD ~0.05 ppb
  • Differential Conductivity — pre/post oxidation difference; partially oxidizes organics to organic acids stage

TOC 分析儀技術比較

四種氧化方法

  • 高溫燃燒（>500°C）：最暴力、最徹底，適合未知樣品，但需要壓縮氧氣
  • 熱活化過硫酸鹽：用熱能產生強氧化性自由基，可處理大分子
  • UV 活化過硫酸鹽：用紫外線激活，可量測 50-100 ppm 的高濃度 TOC
  • 純 UV 光（<195 nm）：不需外加化學品！最適合線上監測，但上限較低（~250 ppb 無氧，~2000 ppb 含氧）

選擇指南

  • 線上持續監測：純 UV 光法最簡便（不需化學品），適合製藥用水
  • 實驗室分析：高溫燃燒或過硫酸鹽法，量測範圍更廣
  • 過硫酸鹽法對 >30 µm 的大顆粒氧化困難

11.1.7 Limit Response & System Suitability Testing

Apparatus Requirements:

  • Reagent Water (rW): complying with USP definition, TOC ≤ 0.25 mg/L
  • Standard Solution (rS): containing 1.19 mg of sucrose
  • System Suitability Solution (rSS): containing 1,4-Benzoquinone RS (0.5 mg/L carbon)

Instrument Standardization (Limit Response Test):
The test solution meets the requirement if:

rt < (rS − rW)

Instrument Suitability (Response Efficiency):
An instrument is "suitable" if its response efficiency is within ±15% of theoretical:

100 × [(rSS − rW) / (rS − rW)] = 100% ± 15%

For in-line testing, standardization once per operating day may be appropriate. Suitability tests may range from each sample test (off-line) to once per month (in-line).

限度回應與系統適用性測試

兩個關鍵公式

限度回應測試（Limit Response）：

r_t < (r_S - r_W)
測試樣品回應 < (蔗糖標準液回應 - 試劑水回應)

適用性測試（Suitability）：

100 x [(r_SS - r_W) / (r_S - r_W)] = 100% +/- 15%
苯醌回應效率必須在 85%-115% 之間

為什麼用蔗糖和苯醌？

蔗糖（sucrose）：非常容易被氧化，作為「最佳情況」的標準品。

1,4-苯醌（benzoquinone）：較難被氧化，用來測試儀器能否處理「困難」的有機物。如果儀器對苯醌的回應效率在 ±15% 以內，表示它能有效氧化各類有機物。

線上 vs 離線測試頻率

  • 線上儀器做標準化測試需要斷開水系統，成本高
  • 每日一次標準化可接受；適用性測試可每月一次
  • 有些 TOC 儀器內建自動化測試功能，可節省大量時間和費用

11.1.8 USP Microbial and Endotoxin Testing

Microbial and Endotoxins are traditionally sampled at the points of use in a water system.

11.1.9 USP pH Testing

pH testing to support in-line conductivity testing was eliminated in May 1998.

Calibration of pH instruments: See Chapter 2, Section 2.3 USP Regulated Water Quality.

Dual Purpose TOC/Conductivity Meters

A number of TOC meters designed for in-line applications include conductivity measurements. To use this dual purpose instrument, the conductivity cell must be separately calibrated with a NIST-traceable precision resistor accurate to ±0.1%.

微生物、內毒素與 pH 測試

微生物與內毒素取樣

必須在使用點（POU）取樣，因為微生物可能在管路末端繁殖。不能只在產水點取樣就代表整個系統合格。

pH 測試的演變

1998 年 5 月起，USP 取消了用 pH 測試來輔助線上電導率測試的要求。原因：

  • 高純水的 pH 極難準確測量
  • 三階段電導率測試已涵蓋 pH 的影響
  • EP 仍保留獨立的 pH 測試要求

雙用途 TOC/電導率儀器

越來越多線上 TOC 儀器兼具電導率測量功能。要符合 USP 要求，電導率池必須獨立校正，使用 NIST 可追溯的精密電阻器（精度 ±0.1%）。

11.2 European Perspective / 歐洲觀點

Original Text

11.2.1 Regulatory Structure

The pharmaceutical industry within the EU is regulated by the European Agency for the Evaluation of Medicinal Products (EMEA), based in London. EMEA has three major functions:

  • Centralized drug approval — granting marketing authorization across Europe; mandatory for biotech products, optional for others
  • Supervision — continued use of medicinal products, including site inspections and pharmacovigilance
  • Coordination — European and international harmonization (ICH framework)

Within Member States, each nation has a national agency (e.g., MCA in UK, BfArM in Germany). The Pharmaceutical Inspection Convention (PIC) has been superseded by the Pharmaceutical Inspection Cooperation Scheme (PIC/S).

11.2.2 Regulations Governing Water and Steam

The EC recognizes the European Pharmacopoeia (EP) as the source of compendial water standards. The EP does not regulate Clean Steam. In Europe, chemical quality of steam for sterilization is governed by European Standard EN 285. UK guidance on steam quality is found in HTM-2010 and HTM-2031.

Commentary / 教學解說

歐洲法規架構

歐洲藥品監管體系

歐洲的架構是中央（EMEA）+ 國家機構雙層制：

  • EMEA 負責跨歐洲的集中審批（生技產品強制）
  • 各國機構負責國內註冊和工廠檢查
  • PIC/S 提供非約束性的檢查指南，促進協調

水和蒸汽的法規來源

  • 水質標準：EP（歐洲藥典）
  • 潔淨蒸汽品質：EN 285 + HTM-2010/2031
  • EP 不規範潔淨蒸汽 — 這是歐洲與美國的重要差異
  • EU cGMP 附錄僅要求蒸汽品質「適當」且不含汙染物

11.2.3 Water Issues

  • While USP requires starting point must meet national drinking water standard, EP requires only that the history of the starting water be documented.
  • EP allows production of WFI by distillation only. Some pharmacopoeias allow WFI by reverse osmosis.
  • EP requires sample sizes of 100 ml for purified water and 500 ml for WFI. USP is not definitive on sample sizes.
  • EP has not accepted substitution of conductivity/resistivity for traditional wet chemistry analyses.
  • EP has not added the test for TOC.
  • EP continues to require the test for pH as an independent test.
  • European inspectors are sensitive to the drainability of systems through points of use.

11.2.4 Steam Issues

  • HTM-2010 emphasizes dryness requirements. EN-285 requires dryness value ≥ 0.9 (0.95 for metal loads).
  • Superheat concern: superheated steam is less effective for sterilization; may result from adiabatic expansion.
  • Non-Condensable Gases (NCGs): air and CO2 from failure to degas feed water; can create pockets preventing steam penetration.

EP vs USP 關鍵差異

蒸汽品質三大關注點

  1. 乾燥度（Dryness）：≥0.9（金屬負載 ≥0.95）。過濕會造成多孔負載潮濕；「過乾」可能過熱
  2. 過熱度（Superheat）：過熱蒸汽殺菌效果差。控制夾套溫度和閥門壓降可預防
  3. 不凝結氣體（NCGs）：空氣和 CO2 會形成「氣穴」，阻止蒸汽穿透。預處理進水加熱至 80°C 以上脫氣

實務影響

如果你的產品同時銷售歐美市場，水系統設計必須同時滿足 USP 和 EP 要求。最安全的做法是採用最嚴格的標準組合：USP 的電導率/TOC 測試 + EP 的蒸餾法 WFI + EP 的取樣量。

11.3 Passivation / 鈍化

Original Text

11.3.1 Introduction

Pharmaceutical equipment and high purity water systems are designed so that product contact surfaces are not reactive, additive, or absorptive. High purity water systems are primarily composed of austenitic stainless steel (SS) due to their corrosion resistant and contaminant free properties.

Passivation is performed to maximize the metal's corrosion resistance. The stainless steel is sulfuric/nitric/hydrofluoric acid pickled at the mill to remove manganese sulfide inclusions, scale, and other impurities. As the steel is removed from the pickling bath, a thin oxide layer forms immediately over the surface. This oxide layer is what renders the stainless steel passive and non-reactive to corrosion.

Any 300 series chromium steels containing 17% or more chromium that has been handled, welded, or worked should be passivated prior to service.

11.3.3 The Chemical Process

Establishing a passive surface or film on austenitic SS is essential to maximize corrosion resistance. Passive surfaces occur naturally when exposed to an oxidizing environment. Nitric acid solution (HNO3) is an oxidizing acid which erodes the metal, and this initial reaction resists further chemical reaction on the surface. The chromium oxide film thickness typically ranges from 0.5-5.0 nm, averaging 2.0-3.0 nm. The chrome to iron ratio should be at least 1:1, with ratios of 2:1 or more being optimal.

Commentary / 教學解說

鈍化基礎原理

什麼是鈍化？

鈍化是在不鏽鋼表面形成一層氧化鉻保護膜的過程。這層膜：

  • 厚度僅 0.5-5.0 nm（奈米級，肉眼不可見）
  • 平均 2-3 nm
  • 使金屬表面「鈍性」— 不再與環境發生化學反應

生活比喻

鈍化就像給不鏽鋼「打蠟」。新車出廠時有保護漆，但如果刮傷了（像焊接破壞表面），就需要重新上漆（鈍化）。這層「蠟」是氧化鉻，只有幾奈米厚，但能有效阻擋腐蝕。

關鍵數據

  • 鉻鐵比（Cr/Fe ratio）：至少 1:1，最佳 ≥2:1
  • 鉻含量 ≥17% 的 300 系列不鏽鋼都需要鈍化
  • 任何經過焊接、加工、搬運的表面都應鈍化
  • 焊接會破壞現有的被動膜，特別是熱影響區

11.3.4 Passivation Procedures

All passivation procedures consist of four main steps:

  1. Wash (Solvent Degreasing) — Remove dirt, dust, oil, and grease using a water-soluble detergent or solvent
  2. Water Rinse — Remove dissolved soils and detergent
  3. Acid Wash (Passivation Step) — Remove free iron, metallic residues, oxides, and corrosion products. Inorganic acids are typically used.
  4. Final Water Rinse — Flush until effluent quality equals influent quality

Reference: ASTM A 380-96, "Standard Recommended Practice for Cleaning and Descaling Stainless Steel Parts, Equipment and Systems."

Guidelines for establishing effective procedures:

  • Start with an accepted or specified procedure
  • Obtain weld coupons from the system for testing
  • Perform specified and alternate procedures for comparison
  • Confirm effectiveness with field and/or laboratory testing
  • Include procedure confirmation in validation documentation

11.3.5 Passivation Chemical Alternatives

Nitric acid (HNO3) is the most common acid specified for passivation. Reference: Federal Specification QQ-P-35C (1988).

The trend is to reduce chemical aggressiveness and consider safety, cost, and environmental impact.

Citric acid and ammonium citrate are gaining popularity as alternatives. ASTM A 380 refers to these as cleaning acids, not passivating acids (they are not oxidizers). To achieve true oxidation, chelating agents in conjunction with citric acid have been introduced.

Phosphoric acid is a weak oxidizing acid sometimes specified, but with no formal documentation as a passivating acid.

Chelants (sequestering agents) such as STPP, NTA, and EDTA may be compounded into acid passivation solutions to enhance metal ion extraction.

Orbital welding with electropolished tubing decreases the aggressiveness required of passivating acids during initial passivation.

鈍化化學品選擇

產業趨勢

從硝酸轉向檸檬酸 + 螯合劑的組合：

  • 安全性：檸檬酸對人員和環境更安全
  • 成本：廢液處理更簡單便宜
  • 有效性：配合 EDTA 等螯合劑可達到類似效果
  • 電拋光管材 + 軌道焊接 = 可使用更溫和的鈍化條件

ASTM 的區分

ASTM A 380 將檸檬酸歸類為清洗酸（cleaning acid）而非鈍化酸（passivating acid），因為檸檬酸本身不是氧化劑。要實現真正的氧化鈍化，需要額外加入螯合劑或氧化劑。

11.3.7 Tests for Cleanliness and Passivity

Cleanliness tests: Water break free surface test, wipe test, UV light testing (per ASTM A 380).

Indirect passivity tests (free iron detection):

  • Ferroxyl Test — test solution applied; blue stain within 15 seconds indicates free iron
  • Water Immersion/Wetting and Drying Test
  • High Humidity Test
  • Copper Sulfate Test
  • Ferroxyl/Potassium Ferricyanide-Nitric Acid Test

Direct passivity tests:

  • XPS (X-ray Photoelectron Spectroscopy) — measures oxidation states of surface elements
  • AES (Auger Electron Spectroscopy) — measures elemental Cr/Fe ratio with depth profiling (destructive)

應用方法與測試

應用方法選擇指南

  • 循環法（首選）：適合管路系統，流速 ≥1.5 m/s
  • 注意：高流速不等於顆粒去除！1000 英尺的管路需要約 25 小時的過濾循環才能完全去除顆粒
  • 噴塗法：適合槽體內部
  • 浸泡法：適合小零件和預製管件
  • 擦拭法：最後手段，用於無法噴塗或循環的區域

實務建議

  • Ferroxyl 測試最常用：便宜、快速（15 秒出結果）
  • XPS/AES 較昂貴，最適合用於焊接試片以驗證程序有效性
  • 假設邏輯：無游離鐵 → 表面夠乾淨 → 氧化膜可均勻形成

11.3.13 Rouging

Rouging is seen in many water systems, usually high temperature (80°C) distilled water and clean steam systems. Rouge is not limited to storage and distribution; it also can be found in distillation and clean steam generating equipment.

The main constituent of the rouge film is ferric oxide, but it can contain iron, chromium, and nickel of different forms. From AES analysis, the outer layer is carbon rich, and the underlying region is iron and oxygen rich.

Rouge can be observed in a variety of colors: orange, light-red, red, reddish-brown, purple, blue, gray, and black. It can range from a loose dust-like film to a tight pertinacious film requiring scraping.

"Traditionally the red rouges are most common in high purity high temperature water systems, while the blue/black rouges are typically found in clean steam systems."

Key places to look: still and clean steam generator discharge lines, tank water/vapor interface, pump heads, Teflon® diaphragms (rouge has affinity for Teflon®), and heat affected area of welds.

The presence of rouge has not been proven to affect water quality. The FDA has no written position addressing rouging. Their criterion remains meeting established USP standards.

Removal: phosphoric, citric, oxalic acids, and ammonium citrates depending on severity. Oxalic acid for worst cases. Passivation with nitric acid required after oxalic acid rinse.

紅銹（Rouging）現象

什麼是紅銹？

紅銹是在不鏽鋼水系統中自然形成的氧化鐵薄膜。主要成分是 Fe2O3（三氧化二鐵），也含鉻和鎳。

常見於：

  • 80°C 高溫純水系統
  • 潔淨蒸汽系統
  • 蒸餾設備

顏色代表什麼？

  • 紅色系（橙、紅、紅棕）：高溫純水系統最常見
  • 藍黑色系（紫、藍、灰、黑）：潔淨蒸汽系統常見
  • 可以是鬆散灰塵狀（容易擦掉）到頑固薄膜（需要刮除）
  • 紅銹有時是多層的，不同層有不同顏色和質地

FDA 的立場

FDA 沒有針對紅銹發布任何書面立場。唯一的標準是水質是否符合 USP 要求。紅銹本身未被證明會影響水質。

但業界的擔憂是：隨時間推移，薄膜可能脫落並分散到系統中 — 這在過濾使用點的濾器變色中可以觀察到。

去銹（Derouging）策略

案例：某冷 WFI 系統去銹鈍化三次，每次一週內又完全紅銹。原因始終未確定 — 紅銹具有高度場址特異性。

11.3.14 Preparing Systems for Passivation

  • Hydrostatic pressure testing first — all new or modified systems
  • Confirm compatibility of all components with passivating solutions (instrumentation, flow meters, valves, UV lights, pumps, seals, filter membranes, gaskets)
  • Remove incompatible items and replace with blanks, valves, or temporary jumper hoses
  • Isolate the system from existing systems, process equipment, utility tie-ins
  • Eliminate all dead legs — critical for chemical contact and complete rinsing
  • Ensure high point vents and low point drains for complete filling and draining
  • Review automated controls: valve alignments, temperature sensors, diverter valves

11.3.15-11.3.16 Disposal & Documentation

Waste solutions can be neutralized; the primary concern is heavy metals (chromium, nickel) in the acid wash effluent. Three disposal options: chemical drains, contractor neutralization, or off-site disposal.

Documentation must include: passivation procedure, development data, testing procedures, calibration records, passivation log sheets, chemical batch records, and marked-up system drawings.

系統準備、化學配方與文件

鈍化前準備清單

  1. 完成水壓測試 — 確保系統無洩漏
  2. 確認所有組件與鈍化液相容性 — 不相容的移除
  3. 將系統與其他系統隔離
  4. 消除所有死角（dead legs）
  5. 確保高點排氣和低點排水
  6. 檢查自動控制 — 避免異常溫度觸發轉向閥

六種化學配方速查

  • C1 硝酸：標準鈍化，室溫 30-60 分鐘
  • C2 鹼性脫脂：去除有機膜和顆粒
  • C3 檸檬酸/螯合劑：可去除鐵、錳、鋁、銅等金屬
  • C4 礦酸：磷酸/硫酸/氨基磺酸，3-10%
  • C5 強化酸/螯合劑：對付高溫氧化膜和矽垢
  • C6 連二亞硫酸鈉：強還原劑，用於去銹

廢液處理三選項

  1. 排入化學排水系統（需有相容處理設施）
  2. 在承包商設備中中和後排放
  3. 場外處置（最貴，需驗證運輸商和處理廠資質）

關鍵：廢液中的鉻和鎳是優先污染物金屬，不可直接排入雨水排水系統。

文件記錄要求

鈍化文件應納入最終驗證文件包，包含：

  • 鈍化程序書
  • 程序開發資料（試片測試結果）
  • 測試程序與設備
  • 校正文件
  • 鈍化日誌表
  • 化學品批次記錄
  • 標記的系統圖紙（point check list）

Section Summary / 本節摘要

  • USP Three-Stage Conductivity: A tiered testing approach (in-line → off-line → lab) with temperature-dependent limits from 0.6 to 3.1 µS/cm / USP 三階段電導率：分層測試架構，溫度依賴限值 0.6-3.1 µS/cm
  • TOC Testing: Limit 500 ppb; four oxidation methods and four CO2 detection methods; suitability testing with sucrose and benzoquinone / TOC 測試：限值 500 ppb；四種氧化法與四種 CO2 偵測法
  • EP vs USP Differences: EP requires distillation-only WFI, retains pH testing, has not adopted conductivity or TOC testing / EP 與 USP 差異：EP 要求蒸餾法 WFI，保留 pH 測試，未採用電導率或 TOC 測試
  • Passivation: Four-step process (degrease, rinse, acid wash, rinse) to form protective chromium oxide film (0.5-5 nm); Cr/Fe ratio ≥1:1 / 鈍化四步驟建立氧化鉻保護膜
  • Chemical Alternatives: Industry trending from nitric acid to citric acid + chelants for safety and environmental reasons / 化學品趨勢從硝酸轉向檸檬酸 + 螯合劑
  • Rouging: Common but unregulated phenomenon; ferric oxide film in hot water/steam systems; site-specific; does not necessarily affect water quality / 紅銹：常見但無法規要求，場址特異性高，不一定影響水質
  • Documentation: Comprehensive records (procedures, logs, batch records, system drawings) required for validation / 文件記錄：完整的鈍化記錄需納入驗證文件
