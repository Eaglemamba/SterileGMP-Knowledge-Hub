7.1 Introduction

This chapter aims to simplify and standardize the process of selection, programming, and design of pharmaceutical steam systems. Guidelines, information, and options are provided, along with advantages and disadvantages, based on the best and most cost effective of current and proven practices and technologies.

The absence of regulations governing the use of steam in pharmaceutical processes has resulted in the proliferation of differing practices and interpretations. Most interpretations are made on the side of conservatism. Unfortunately, in addition to increasing cost without an associated increase in benefits, excessive conservatism can result in system complexity, and possibly reduced reliability.

One example is the use of clean steam (non-utility boiler produced steam) where a form of utility steam (utility boiler produced steam) would be adequate to maintain product quality. The installation of a clean steam generator when a simple steam reducing station would suffice results in added equipment and the associated impact on cost, complexity, and reliability.

In some instances, interpretations are based on inaccurate assumptions of what is important or critical. An example is the over specifying of pretreatment or using WFI as feed to solve the perceived problem.

導讀：為何蒸汽是「灰色地帶」？

核心訊息

製藥蒸汽是少數「缺乏明確法規」的關鍵公用系統之一。FDA 和 USP 對水系統有詳盡規範，但對蒸汽卻幾乎沒有最低標準。這導致業界普遍「寧可做過頭」，反而造成：

  • 成本增加 — 不必要的潔淨蒸汽產生器
  • 系統複雜化 — 更多設備 = 更多故障點
  • 可靠性降低 — 過度設計反而增加維護負擔

比喻：用大砲打蚊子

想像你的工廠只需要「乾淨的自來水」就能洗手，但因為不確定法規要求，你決定用「注射用水（WFI）」來洗。水質當然合格，但成本高出10倍，而且 WFI 系統更難維護。蒸汽也一樣 — 很多工廠在只需要 Utility Steam 的地方用了 Clean Steam。

實務重點

本章的核心價值：提供一套基於風險的決策框架，讓你不再「猜測」該用什麼等級的蒸汽，而是根據產品接觸風險、製程需求來做出合理選擇。

7.2 cGMP Issues

The user has the ultimate responsibility for system design and performance, and for ensuring that the proper type of steam is used for a given process.

There is no FDA or USP minimum standard for clean steam. However, cGMPs for large volume parenterals (LVPs) issued in 1976 indicated that feed water for boilers supplying steam that contact components, drug products, and drug product contact surfaces shall not contain volatile additives such as amines or hydrazines.

Few regulations govern the design and construction of clean steam generators. There are also no regulations governing materials of construction, type or level of instrumentation, surface finishes, or operating temperatures.

Among US Government publications, the FDA's Code of Federal Regulations (CFR) provides culinary steam recommendations and stipulations related to heat exchanger and tank air vents design and construction. The Culinary steam recommendations apply to food applications only.

US Public Health Service/Dairy Industry Committee, 3A Sanitary Standards, Number 609-02, adds additional limitations to Culinary steam feed water additives for food applications. It should be noted that boiler feed water additives permitted in food for human consumption may not be acceptable in drinking water or orally ingested drug products.

法規環境解析

關鍵發現：法規近乎空白

這是本章最重要的觀念之一：Clean Steam 幾乎沒有法規標準。具體來說：

  • FDA 沒有 Clean Steam 最低標準
  • USP 沒有 Clean Steam 專論（monograph）
  • 沒有規範建材、表面粗糙度、儀控等級
  • 唯一明確的要求：1976年 LVP cGMP — 禁止胺類/聯氨類揮發性添加劑

誰負責？— 使用者自負

「The user has the ultimate responsibility」— 這句話在 GMP 世界裡份量很重。意思是：

  • 法規不會告訴你「用哪種蒸汽」
  • 你必須自己評估風險、選擇合適等級
  • 稽核時，你要能說明你的選擇邏輯（rationale）

這就是為什麼後面的 Decision Tree（7.6節）如此重要。

比喻：Culinary Steam 的陷阱

Culinary Steam 是「食品級蒸汽」— 聽起來很安全？但注意：食品容許的鍋爐添加劑（如某些胺類），在藥品製造中可能完全不可接受。這就像食品級的防腐劑不能直接加入注射液一樣。製藥標準永遠高於食品標準。

7.2.1 Steam Attributes

7.2.1.1 Quality

The term "Quality" when referring to steam indicates the level of steam saturation. There are no FDA or USP regulations relating to minimum "steam quality" or the level of non-condensable gasses present in pharmaceutical steam.

European regulators have defined specific criteria for pharmaceutical steam used for equipment sterilization. (European Standard EN 285 - Steam Sterilizers - reference section 13.3) These cover acceptable levels of saturation or dryness, the level of superheat, and the volume of non-condensable gases present.

7.2.1.2 Purity

Purity requirements for steam used in pharmaceutical manufacturing and product development are driven by the product characteristics, manufacturing process, and the intended use of the product. The product manufacturer is responsible for ensuring that steam used to process the product is appropriate.

Though steam purity requirements are product specific, it may be impractical to reliably produce special steam for each situation. Manufacturing operations typically generate and distribute only one or two steam purity grades, commonly grouped.

Commentary | 教學解說

Quality vs. Purity — 最容易混淆的概念

比喻：瓶裝水 vs. 自來水

Quality 就像水的「溫度和壓力」— 你需要的是熱水，但水龍頭流出來的是溫的（不夠飽和）或太燙（過熱），都不好用。

Purity 就像水裡「含什麼成分」— 即使溫度壓力都對，但水裡有鐵鏽或化學藥劑，就不能用在產品上。

兩者獨立但都重要：高 Quality 低 Purity 的蒸汽（乾但含雜質）不適合產品接觸；低 Quality 高 Purity 的蒸汽（濕但乾淨）滅菌效率差。

EN 285 — 歐盟的領先標準

美國 FDA/USP 沒有蒸汽品質標準，但歐盟 EN 285 規定了：

  • 乾度（Dryness）：≥ 0.95
  • 過熱度（Superheat）：≤ 25°C
  • 不凝結氣體（NCG）：≤ 3.5%（體積）

實務上，即使在美國工廠，EN 285 常被作為內部蒸汽品質標準使用。

兩個完全不同的概念

屬性 Quality（品質） Purity（純度）
定義 蒸汽的飽和程度（乾度） 蒸汽中化學物質/內毒素含量
關注重點 含水量、過熱度、不凝結氣體 胺類、雜質、微生物副產物
影響 滅菌效率（濕包、冷點） 產品污染風險
法規 歐盟 EN 285 有明確標準 FDA/USP 無最低標準

7.3 Types of Steam

Pharmaceutical steam is classified into two (2) types based on their respective sources:

  1. Utility-Boiler produced steam, hereafter called Utility Steam.
  2. Non-Utility Boiler produced steam, hereafter called Clean Steam.

7.3.1 Utility Steam

Utility steam is characterized with usually having:

  • Chemical additives to control scale and corrosion
  • Relatively high pressure with the potential of generating superheat during expansion
  • Relatively high pH

Chemical additives: Utility steam is produced, in most cases, using conventional fire-tube steam boilers, normally of steel construction. Such boilers are almost always provided with systems that inject additives in the feed water to protect the boiler and steam distribution piping from scale and corrosion. Some of these scale and corrosion inhibitors may, and often do, include amines and other substances that may not be acceptable in steam being used in pharmaceutical processes.

Utility steam can be filtered to remove particulate matter, but filtration does not remove dissolved substances and volatiles such as amines.

Superheat: Superheated steam is produced in water tube boilers by reheating the steam or by generating the steam at a higher pressure in a fire tube boiler and then reducing the pressure through a regulating valve. When the pressure is reduced, the energy in the higher temperature steam is dissipated to generate steam at the lower pressure and produce superheated steam above the corresponding saturation temperature.

pH control: In order to protect carbon steel from corrosion by the steam, it is necessary to use additives to raise the pH to between 9.5 - 10.5.

Commentary | 教學解說

兩種蒸汽的比較

分類邏輯：看「來源」而非「品質」

蒸汽分類很簡單 — 只看它從哪裡來：

  • Utility Steam = 從工廠公用鍋爐來（有添加劑）
  • Clean Steam = 從專用產生器來（無添加劑）

這不是「好壞」之分，而是「用途」之分。很多場景下 Utility Steam 完全合格。

Utility Steam 的三大特徵

  1. 化學添加劑 — 防垢防蝕，但含胺類/聯氨類
  2. 過熱（Superheat） — 高壓減壓後產生，對滅菌不利
  3. 高 pH — 9.5-10.5，保護碳鋼管路

過濾不等於純化

這是非常重要的觀念：Utility Steam 可以過濾去除顆粒物（particulate），但無法去除溶解物和揮發物（如胺類）。就像水的活性碳濾芯能擋住沙子，但擋不住溶解的氯。

所以如果應用需要「無添加劑」的蒸汽，過濾 Utility Steam 是不夠的 — 必須使用 Clean Steam。

過熱蒸汽 — 為何滅菌時是壞事？

過熱蒸汽（Superheated Steam）溫度高於飽和溫度，行為更像「熱空氣」而非蒸汽。滅菌需要的是飽和蒸汽的潛熱（latent heat）釋放 — 蒸汽凝結在物體表面時放出大量熱能。過熱蒸汽不凝結，傳熱效率大幅下降。

就像用吹風機烘乾和用蒸氣熨斗燙衣服的差別 — 蒸氣熨斗效果更好，因為凝結釋放的熱量更集中。

7.3.2 Clean Steam (CS)

Pharmaceutical clean steam is generated from treated water free of volatile additives, such as amines or hydrazines, and is used for thermal disinfection or sterilization processes. It is considered especially important to preclude such contamination from injectable drug products.

Clean steam is characterized as having:

  • No additives
  • No generated superheat except when the generated pressure is significantly higher than the use pressure of the steam
  • Relatively low pH

There are many terms used in the pharmaceutical industry to describe Clean Steam. These include Clean Steam, Pure Steam, Pyrogen Free Steam, WFI Steam, and USP Purified Water steam. There is no standard or accepted definition for any of these terms. However, the most commonly used terms are "Pure Steam" and "Clean Steam". In this Guide, the term "Clean Steam" is used in lieu of all others.

The condensate of Clean Steam has no buffer, and may have a relatively low pH compared to that of utility steam.

Commentary | 教學解說

Clean Steam 深入解析

名稱混亂 — 業界痛點

Clean Steam、Pure Steam、Pyrogen Free Steam、WFI Steam... 同一件事至少有5種叫法，而且沒有任何一個有標準定義。這在跨國稽核或設備採購時經常造成誤解。

ISPE 的建議：統一使用 "Clean Steam" 一詞。在你的 SOP 和 URS 中也應該明確定義你用的術語。

低 pH 的隱藏風險

Clean Steam 凝結水沒有緩衝劑，pH 可能偏低（接近純水的 ~5.5-6.5）。這代表：

  • 管路必須用耐蝕材質（316L 不鏽鋼）
  • 碳鋼管路會被快速腐蝕
  • 這也是 Clean Steam 系統成本較高的原因之一

Clean Steam 三大特徵

特徵 Utility Steam Clean Steam
添加劑 有（防垢/防蝕劑） 無
過熱 常見（減壓產生） 極少（除非壓差很大）
pH 高（9.5-10.5） 低（無緩衝劑）
管路材質 碳鋼 316L 不鏽鋼
應用 加熱、HVAC、非關鍵 滅菌、產品接觸

7.4 Background and Industry Practices

7.4.1 Purity of Sterilizing Steam

When steam or the resulting condensed water comes in direct or indirect contact with the drug product, the purity should be equivalent to the water purity acceptable for final rinsing of the drug contact surfaces.

Note: A continuous supply of Dry Saturated Steam at the point of use is considered necessary for efficient steam sterilization. Water carried by the steam in suspension may cause damp loads and superheated steam is considerably less effective than saturated steam when used for sterilization. Non-condensable gases if contained in the steam may prevent the attainment of sterilization conditions in parts of the sterilizer load.

7.4.2 Steam Used for Humidification

When steam is used for indirect humidification, such as injection into HVAC air streams prior to final air filtration, the steam does not need to be purer than the air that it is being mixed with.

However, when humidifying process areas, the potential level of impurities, including amines and hydrazines should be evaluated in order to ascertain the impact on the final drug product. This is particularly important in areas where open processing takes place, such as aseptic filling suites and formulation areas.

7.4.3 Common Practices

It is common practice to generate pharmaceutical steam from compendial waters and test the steam condensate for equivalency to the compendial standard. This practice ignores the ability of the pharmaceutical steam generator to remove impurities. This overprocessing is wasteful and unnecessary.

An exception is when the steam quantity is small and the cost and maintenance of a dedicated feed water pretreatment system exceeds the cost of using compendial water.

Commentary | 教學解說

業界實務 — 過度保守的代價

滅菌蒸汽純度原則

核心規則很簡單：蒸汽（或其凝結水）接觸產品時，純度應等同於該產品可接受的最終淋洗水純度。

例如：滅菌注射劑設備 → 凝結水應等同 WFI 品質。非無菌 API 中間體 → 可能 PW 品質就足夠。

滅菌蒸汽的三大敵人

  1. 含水（Wet Steam） → 造成「濕包（damp loads）」→ 滅菌失敗
  2. 過熱（Superheat） → 傳熱效率差 → 溫度到了但殺不死菌
  3. 不凝結氣體（NCG） → 形成氣囊（air pockets）→ 蒸汽無法到達死角

這三者影響的是蒸汽「Quality」而非「Purity」，但都會導致滅菌失敗。

加濕蒸汽：風險取決於「開放性」

想像一下：

  • 辦公室空調加濕 → Utility Steam 完全OK（蒸汽不接觸產品）
  • 一般潔淨室加濕 → 需要評估（蒸汽進入空氣，但有 HEPA 過濾）
  • 無菌充填室加濕 → 必須謹慎評估（開放製程，蒸汽可能接觸產品）

關鍵判斷：蒸汽中的雜質經稀釋後，是否會顯著影響產品品質？

常見浪費：用 WFI 產 Clean Steam

ISPE 明確指出這是「overprocessing, wasteful and unnecessary」。Clean Steam Generator 本身就是蒸餾器 — 它能去除雜質。用 WFI 作為進水等於「蒸餾兩次」，毫無必要。

唯一例外：蒸汽需求量極小時，專用前處理系統的成本可能超過直接使用 WFI。

7.5 System Planning

Pharmaceutical Steam System Planning, shown in Figure 7-1, is a graphic representation of the system boundaries, limitations, and restrictions. Initial system planning reveals primary boundaries that establish the cornerstone for design criteria. These system boundaries are:

  1. Steam Requirements
  2. System Design
  3. Use Point Criteria
  4. Distribution System requirements

The arrows encircling each boundary represent limitations that establish more specific operating strategies and ranges. To allow more flexibility in final planning and detailed design the designer should always indicate ranges of acceptability, rather than a specific value or position.

7.5.1 Steam Requirements

The planning process starts with the listing of all steam requirements and applications that include:

  • Company standards including QA/QC requirements and published SOPs
  • The categorization of use-point by:
    • Type of application (Humidification, critical or non-critical, API, and Dosage form applications)
    • Purity selection (based primarily on the application and the endotoxin and chemical purity criteria set for the product)
    • Steam quality (dryness, non-condensable limits, and maximum superheat)

7.5.2 System Design

Pharmaceutical steam is generated using different methods. The most appropriate method for each application must be selected. (See the Pharmaceutical Steam Purity Decision Tree, Section 7.6.)

The process continues with an evaluation of the steam system requirements (generation) that includes:

  • The types of generation systems available (if both pyrogen free clean steam and clean steam without endotoxin limits is required, the practicality and economy of producing only the higher grade should be raised)
  • The source of utility steam or electrical power
  • The type and number of systems required based on feedback from the "Distribution System" evaluation
  • The condensate sampling needs
  • Safety considerations

系統規劃方法論

四大規劃邊界

ISPE 提出的系統規劃是一個迭代循環，包含四個相互關聯的邊界：

  1. Steam Requirements（蒸汽需求） — 列出所有使用點和應用
  2. System Design（系統設計） — 選擇產生方式
  3. Use Point Criteria（使用點標準） — 定義交付規格
  4. Distribution System（輸送系統） — 管路設計

這四個步驟不是線性的 — 每一步的結果都會回饋影響其他步驟，需要反覆迭代。

比喻：規劃蒸汽系統就像規劃自來水系統

想像你要為一棟新大樓設計供水系統：

  • 需求：哪些樓層需要飲用水？哪些只需要沖馬桶？
  • 設計：需要幾個水塔？要不要裝淨水器？
  • 使用點：每個水龍頭需要多少流量和壓力？
  • 輸送：管路怎麼走？要不要保溫？

蒸汽系統規劃邏輯完全相同，只是介質從水變成蒸汽。

設計智慧：用「範圍」而非「定值」

ISPE 特別強調：「indicate ranges of acceptability, rather than a specific value」。

例如：不要寫「壓力 3.0 bar」，要寫「壓力 2.5-3.5 bar」。範圍給設計留有餘裕，避免因微小偏差就「不合規」。

經濟性考量

7.5.2 提到一個務實建議：如果工廠同時需要「有內毒素限制的 Clean Steam」和「無內毒素限制的 Clean Steam」，評估是否乾脆只產高等級。有時一套高等級系統比兩套不同等級系統更經濟。

7.5.3 Use Point Criteria

The third step defines the specific delivery requirement ranges for clean steam at the point of use including:

  • Utilization — determined from each overall system peak demand(s), average demand, and the relationships between peak demand time periods and their flow rates
  • Pressures and flow levels
  • Use periods and histogram analysis, if available
  • Quality
  • Purity

7.5.4 Distribution System

The fourth step includes the distribution system evaluation, which includes:

  • Condensate, non-condensable and moisture removal
  • Pipe size and Insulation requirements including:
    • Materials of construction, sanitary design requirements and surface finish
    • Physical location of each use point
    • Heat and temperature losses
    • Natural drainage
Note: Since the steam quality will decline, due to heat losses, with time, the efficiency of the insulation and the length of the distribution system, the quality at the use point will not be expected to reflect the generation quality level.

7.5.5 Re-evaluation of System Boundaries and Constraints

These sequential steps are repeated and re-evaluated as information in the design process iterates, and further criteria about the overall system boundaries are identified.

In operations with a requirement for only one grade of steam, the steam system is designed to meet the most stringent requirements of the most demanding product or process. With more than one purity grade of steam, products and processes are often categorized and fed by the most appropriate system.

使用點與輸送系統

Use Point Criteria — 需求分析三要素

  • 需求量分析：尖峰需求 vs. 平均需求。蒸汽系統必須能應對尖峰，但不能過度設計。用 histogram（直方圖）分析用量分布最有效。
  • 壓力與流量：不同使用點需求不同（Autoclave 需要較高壓力，加濕需要較低壓力）。
  • 品質與純度：使用點的 Quality 和 Purity 要求。

輸送系統的隱藏挑戰

蒸汽管路不是「裝了就好」。管路越長：

  • 熱損失越大 → 蒸汽凝結 → Quality 下降
  • 凝結水積累 → 需要足夠的疏水閥（steam trap）
  • 不凝結氣體累積 → 需要排氣點
  • 管路傾斜 → 必須確保自然排水（natural drainage）

因此 ISPE 提醒：使用點的蒸汽品質不等於產生器出口的品質。

單等級 vs. 多等級策略

就像工廠用水策略：

  • 小工廠（使用點少）→ 只產一種最高等級的 Clean Steam，全廠共用。簡單、好管理。
  • 大工廠（使用點多、需求差異大）→ 分兩級：Clean Steam 給關鍵點，Utility Steam 給非關鍵點。省錢但系統更複雜。

決策關鍵：蒸汽消耗量和純度需求的差異程度。

設計思考題

你的工廠蒸汽管路從 Clean Steam Generator 到最遠的 Autoclave 有 150 公尺。你觀察到 Autoclave 偶爾出現「濕包」問題。可能的原因和解決方案是什麼？

提示：考慮管路長度、保溫效果、疏水閥配置、管路傾斜度。

Commentary | 教學解說

決策樹詳解 — 蒸汽選擇的核心工具

最常見的誤區

  • 誤區1：「所有滅菌蒸汽都要 Clean Steam」→ 錯！發酵槽滅菌用 Utility Steam 就是 baseline。
  • 誤區2：「Clean Steam 一定要用 WFI 產」→ 錯！Steam Generator 本身就能去除雜質，處理水（Treated Water）通常就足夠。
  • 誤區3：「所有潔淨室加濕都要 Clean Steam」→ 錯！只有「開放製程」且雜質影響產品時才需要。

實務應用：快速判斷法

當有人問「這個點需要 Clean Steam 嗎？」，按以下順序回答：

  1. 蒸汽碰到產品嗎？→ YES = Clean Steam
  2. 蒸汽碰到「會碰產品的設備」嗎？→ YES = Clean Steam
  3. 蒸汽進入「開放製程的空氣」嗎？→ 要評估
  4. 以上都不是？→ Utility Steam 通常就夠

情境練習：Decision Tree 應用

判斷以下場景該用哪種蒸汽：

  1. SIP 滅菌無菌充填線的 holding tank
  2. API 合成反應中，蒸汽直接注入反應液
  3. 配方區 HVAC 加濕（封閉製程）
  4. WFI 儲槽的週期性 SIP
  5. 包裝區加熱用蒸汽（夾套加熱）

參考答案：1. Clean Steam, 2. Clean Steam, 3. 需評估（封閉製程可能 Utility 即可）, 4. Clean Steam 或 Utility+WFI沖洗, 5. Utility Steam

決策邏輯總結

Decision Tree 的邏輯可以簡化為一個核心問題：蒸汽（或其凝結水）是否接觸產品？

判斷條件 結論
直接接觸藥品/API Clean Steam（無爭議）
滅菌產品接觸設備 Clean Steam（凝結水等同最終淋洗水）
加濕開放製程區域 視雜質影響評估（可能是 Clean 或 Utility）
熱交換器/非關鍵應用 Utility Steam（基準做法）

Section Summary | 本節摘要

  • 法規空白：FDA/USP 對 Clean Steam 沒有最低標準，使用者自負選擇責任。唯一明確禁令：LVP 相關蒸汽不得含揮發性添加劑（胺類/聯氨類）。
  • Quality vs. Purity：Quality = 飽和度/乾度（影響滅菌效率）；Purity = 化學/內毒素含量（影響產品安全）。兩者獨立且都重要。
  • 兩類蒸汽：Utility Steam（有添加劑、高 pH、可能過熱）vs. Clean Steam（無添加劑、低 pH、專用產生器）。分類依據是「來源」而非品質高低。
  • 業界過度保守：常見做法包括「用 WFI 產 Clean Steam」和「非關鍵應用也用 SCSG」，造成不必要的成本和複雜性。
  • 系統規劃四步驟：Steam Requirements → System Design → Use Point Criteria → Distribution System，迭代循環執行。
  • Decision Tree 核心邏輯：蒸汽是否接觸產品？→ 是否滅菌產品設備？→ 是否加濕開放製程？→ 依序判斷蒸汽等級。
  • 實務建議：用「範圍」而非「定值」規劃；小工廠可用單等級策略；輸送管路會降低蒸汽品質，需考慮保溫和疏水。
