Preface | 序言

Purpose and legal context of the GAMP IT Infrastructure Guide · 指南目的與法律聲明

Original Text (English)

This document, the GAMP® Good Practice Guide: IT Infrastructure Control and Compliance, is intended as a supplement to the Guide for Validation of Automated Systems (GAMP® 4). It provides an approach to meeting current regulatory expectations for compliant IT Infrastructure platforms, including the need to identify, qualify, and control those aspects impacted by GxP.

This document has been designed so that it may be used in conjunction with guidance provided in GAMP® 4 and other ISPE publications, such as the ISPE Baseline® Guides.

Disclaimer

This Guide is meant to assist pharmaceutical companies in managing the validation of IT Infrastructure platforms. The GAMP Forum cannot ensure and does not warrant that a system managed in accordance with this Guide will be acceptable to regulatory authorities. This Guide does not replace the need for hiring professional engineers or technicians.

CDMO 實務解讀

這份指南是什麼？

GAMP IT基礎設施指南是GAMP 4的補充文件，專門聚焦於支撐GxP應用程式的底層IT環境——包含伺服器、網路、作業系統、資料庫平台等。

在製藥CDMO中，IT基礎設施是所有數位化製造系統的地基。MES出問題了？先查OS patch。LIMS資料遺失？先看backup策略是否合規。這本指南就是管控這個「地基層」的標準。

建築比喻

應用程式（MES、LIMS、QMS）是建築物，IT基礎設施是地基和鋼筋。即使建築物本身設計完美，若地基不穩定，整棟建築都有崩塌風險。這份指南確保「地基」本身符合GxP要求。

1.1

Overview | 概述

Why IT Infrastructure control matters for GxP compliance · 為何IT基礎設施管控對GxP合規至關重要

Original Text (English)

GxP regulated companies have an ever increasing dependency on computerized systems when conducting their day-to-day businesses.

The validated status of GxP applications that are dependent upon an underlying IT Infrastructure is compromised if that IT Infrastructure is not maintained in a demonstrable state of control and regulatory compliance.

The consequences of the IT Infrastructure being out of effective control can be significant. Depending on the nature of a failure, an entire site or geographic region of operations could be brought to a standstill while the problem is resolved.

The infrastructure should be brought into initial conformance with the company's established standards through a planned qualification process building upon acknowledged good IT practices. Once in conformance, this state should be maintained by documented standard processes and quality assurance activities, the effectiveness of which should be periodically verified.

Key aspects to consider include:

  • Installation and operational qualification of infrastructure components
  • Configuration management and change control in a highly dynamic environment
  • Management of risks to IT Infrastructure
  • Involvement of service providers in critical infrastructure processes
  • Security management — access controls, availability of services and data integrity
  • Backup, restore, and disaster recovery
  • Archiving
"IT Infrastructure" and "infrastructure" are used synonymously to indicate an aggregation of platforms and services including their associated processes, procedures, and personnel. — GAMP IT Infrastructure Guide, Footnote 1
CDMO 實務解讀

核心警示：連鎖失效風險

指南明確說明：若IT基礎設施失控，整個廠區或地區的作業可能停擺。在CDMO環境中，這意味著：

  • MES停機 → 批次記錄無法建立 → 生產暫停
  • LIMS無法存取 → 放行檢驗無法完成 → 批次卡關
  • DCS/SCADA失效 → 製程監控中斷 → FDA 21 CFR Part 11 數據完整性疑慮

CDMO的IT基礎設施涵蓋範圍

  • Server infrastructure：MES、LIMS、QMS的主機伺服器
  • Network：廠區內部網路、隔離的GxP網段
  • OS/Database：Windows Server、Oracle DB等
  • Virtualization：VMware環境（若使用）
  • Storage/Backup：批次記錄的長期保存系統

關鍵定義：IT Infrastructure

IT Infrastructure（IT基礎設施） = 平台（Platforms）+ 服務（Services）+ 相關流程、程序、人員的集合體。

這個定義很重要：IT基礎設施不只是硬體，還包含圍繞它運作的流程和負責的人員。

思考練習

  1. 列出你廠區中三個依賴IT基礎設施的GxP應用程式，並描述若底層伺服器發生故障的影響。
  2. 「可示範的控制狀態（demonstrable state of control）」在法規稽查時意味著需要準備哪些文件？
  3. 為何備份（Backup）和災難復原（Disaster Recovery）被列為IT基礎設施的關鍵考量？

Purpose | 目的

What this Guide aims to achieve · 本指南的目標

Original Text (English)

This Guide provides comprehensive guidance on meeting current regulatory expectations for compliant IT Infrastructure platforms, including the need to identify, qualify, and control those aspects impacted by GxP.

This Guide intends to satisfy the growing need for guidance on key IT Infrastructure subjects in relation to current international GxP regulations, and to align terminology and language with other GAMP® Good Practice Guides.

The Guide is intended primarily for regulated life science industries, including pharmaceutical, biological, and medical devices, but also provides valuable information for suppliers of systems, products, or services.

This Guide includes the description of a scaleable qualification framework which has been derived from key principles and practices. It describes how this framework can be applied to different platform types in order to determine the extent and scope of qualification efforts.

In addition, this Guide provides an overview of current best practices for the design, qualification, and operation of an IT Infrastructure with emphasis on the qualification requirements of the major components.

CDMO 實務解讀

三個核心動作：Identify → Qualify → Control

這三個動詞定義了整個IT基礎設施合規管理的生命週期：

  • Identify（識別）：哪些IT元件支撐GxP應用程式？哪些在GxP範圍內？
  • Qualify（資格確認）：執行IQ/OQ，確認元件能滿足規格要求
  • Control（管控）：持續的變更管理、組態管理、定期審查

可縮放資格確認框架（Scaleable Qualification Framework）

框架可依據以下因素調整規模：

  • 平台的GxP衝擊程度（風險驅動）
  • 公司規模（小型CDMO vs 跨國大廠）
  • 平台類型（獨立伺服器 vs 虛擬化環境 vs 雲端）

這表示框架不是一刀切的——你可以根據風險評估調整資格確認的深度和廣度。

Scope | 適用範圍

What is included and excluded from this Guide · 本指南的涵蓋範圍與排除項目

Original Text (English)

This Guide addresses compliance with international GxP regulations and considers:

  • The establishment of new platforms and extensions to existing ones
  • Existing platforms already in support of GxP applications

GxP regulations considered:

  • Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP)
  • Good Clinical Practice (GCP)
  • Good Laboratory Practice (GLP)
  • Good Distribution Practice (GDP)

Specific regulations and guidelines considered:

  • US FDA regulations and Compliance Policy Guides
  • Relevant parts of EU GMPs, e.g., Annexes 11, 15, and 18
  • PIC/S Guidance

This Guide covers a range of IT Infrastructures, from those found in companies operating globally to isolated or semi-isolated GxP regulated infrastructures.

Out of Scope

Business Continuity Planning is led by business users and application owners — it is outside the scope of this Guide. This Guide covers IT disaster recovery and contingency planning, but not alternative operating procedures for business processes in case of failure.

涵蓋（In Scope）：

  • GxP資料庫平台（Oracle、SQL Server）

1.4

Benefits | 效益

Advantages of the horizontal platform-based approach · 水平式平台導向方法的優勢

Original Text (English)

This Guide applies a structured approach, including Risk Management, to the qualification, management, and control of IT Infrastructure platforms supporting GxP applications.

A vertical, or system based, approach to qualification and audit of IT Infrastructure would be inefficient and impractical, as overlapping and often identical platform elements would be dealt with repeatedly. To avoid unnecessary effort, this Guide describes a horizontal, or platform based, approach.

Benefits of a horizontal approach include:

  • Higher level of standardization throughout the entire life cycle
  • Minimal overlap in documentation
  • Minimal overlap in qualification
  • Minimal overlap in audits, inspections, and assessments

The approach also builds upon:

  • The relatively low residual risk to GxP applications attributable to IT Infrastructure platforms (in contrast to application software designed to create and manipulate data)
  • Industry standard components with built-in error detection and self-correction features
  • Current good IT practices and international standards for reliable network performance
  • Efficient, automatic, standardized IT monitoring and management software tools
  • Many similar platform components used in similar configurations across a company
CDMO 實務解讀

垂直 vs 水平方法：超市收銀機比喻

垂直方法（低效）：每台收銀機都獨立稽查一次POS系統的伺服器、網路、OS——但同一台伺服器可能服務20台收銀機，結果做了20次重複的工作。

水平方法（高效）：先資格確認「收銀機共用平台」一次，後續每台收銀機的應用程式驗證只需確認「平台已合規」即可。

在CDMO中，若一台伺服器同時支援MES和LIMS，水平方法讓伺服器只需資格確認一次。

為何平台的殘留風險較低？

平台（OS、資料庫）設計目的是保護資料完整性，而應用程式設計目的是建立和操作資料。應用程式更可能引入業務邏輯錯誤，導致數據問題；平台的風險通常是可用性（availability）而非數據正確性。

水平方法的CDMO實踐

  • Standard server build：建立標準化伺服器配置包，資格確認一次後可複製部署
  • Platform qualification package：一份平台資格確認文件，多個應用程式共用
  • One audit for the platform：稽查人員只需審查一次平台文件，不需重複查每個應用程式的底層基礎設施

思考練習

  1. 若你的廠區有5個GxP應用程式都運行在同一台虛擬化伺服器上，水平方法能節省多少資格確認工作量？
  2. 「標準化元件有自我錯誤偵測功能」這個特性如何支持較輕量的資格確認策略？
  3. 水平方法在哪些情況下可能不適用（例如：特殊配置或高度客製化環境）？

CDMO 實務解讀

GxP IT管控的根本目的

指南開宗明義：IT管控的目標是保護產品品質和公眾健康，而非為了技術本身。這個框架讓你在回答稽查員問題時有明確的論述基礎：「我們的每一項IT控制措施都是為了確保GxP記錄的完整性和系統的可靠性，最終保護患者安全。」

Change Control中的特殊挑戰：Patch Management

指南特別提到病毒簽名更新和安全性補丁的變更控制——這在GxP環境中是高頻率的實際挑戰：

  • 安全補丁需要快速部署（資安要求）
  • 但每次OS patch都可能影響已驗證的應用程式（GxP要求）
  • 解決方案：建立patch管理SOP，包含影響評估和快速回歸測試程序

ISO17799（現為ISO 27001）在GxP中的應用

資訊安全標準ISO17799（現已演進為ISO 27001）與GxP的IT安全要求高度重疊，尤其在：訪問控制、審計日誌、事件管理、備份程序等方面。許多CDMO同時追求ISO 27001認證和GxP合規，兩者可以共用大量管控措施。

  3. 「實用且高效（practical and efficient）」的目標如何影響你在小型CDMO中設計合規程序的方式？

Key Concepts | 核心概念

Horizontal approach, key terminology, qualification vs validation · 水平方法、關鍵術語、資格確認vs驗證

Original Text (English)

1.7.1 Horizontal Platform Based Approach

The IT Infrastructure exists to support the primary business, by providing:

  • Platforms to run business applications (e.g., CDMS, IVRS, LIMS, or ERP)
  • IT Infrastructure processes that facilitate a capable and controlled IT environment
  • General IT services (e.g., email, office tools, intranet, file storage)

Where platform components support multiple applications, these components should be qualified separately from the applications to avoid unnecessary duplication of activities and effort.

Separate qualification of the IT Infrastructure means that adding or changing a business application would require only the validation of the application. Similarly, changing components in the IT Infrastructure may not require further validation of the business application; however, the risk to GxP applications of each controlled change should be assessed.

Standard, re-usable, qualified platform components are referred to as building blocks throughout this Guide.

1.7.2 Key Terminology

Requirements: Often specified in documents such as Service Level Agreements (SLAs). For infrastructures supporting real-time applications (such as process control), requirement specifications may be more specific.

Commissioning: A well planned, documented, managed engineering approach to start-up and handover to end-user, resulting in a safe and functional environment meeting established design requirements. In the context of IT Infrastructures, useful for aspects that do not support GxP activities directly.

Qualification: A process of demonstrating the ability of an entity to fulfill specified requirements. Demonstrates the ability of servers, clients, and peripherals to fulfill specified requirements. Basic requirements include: QA involvement, formally verified design solutions, test results compliant with GxP documentation requirements, traceability of actions.

Validation: In the context of an IT Infrastructure, validation applies to the GxP applications that run on the infrastructure, rather than the infrastructure platforms themselves. The focus for platforms is on qualification of components.

Building Blocks（積木）概念

標準化、可重用的已資格確認平台元件稱為「building blocks」。

實務範例：若你建立了一套標準Windows Server配置並完成資格確認，下次部署相同配置的伺服器時，只需確認配置一致，不需重新從頭做完整IQ/OQ——大幅降低重複工作。

Qualification vs Validation：關鍵區別

Qualification（資格確認）適用於IT基礎設施平台（伺服器、網路、OS、資料庫）。

Validation（驗證）適用於運行在基礎設施上的GxP應用程式（MES、LIMS）。

稽查員問你的LIMS伺服器有沒有「驗證」時，正確答案是：「伺服器已完成資格確認（qualified），LIMS應用程式已完成驗證（validated）。」

Regulatory Context | 法規背景比較

21 CFR Part 11 vs EU Annex 11 — key requirements for IT Infrastructure · 美歐法規對IT基礎設施的要求比較

Regulatory Requirements Comparison

Table: 21 CFR Part 11 vs EU Annex 11 — IT Infrastructure Relevance

法規比較解讀

兩大法規的核心差異

  • 21 CFR Part 11：美國法規，重點在電子記錄和電子簽名的技術控制（以IT功能要求為主）
  • EU Annex 11：歐盟GMP附件，涵蓋整個電腦化系統生命週期，對IT基礎設施的明確要求更多

EU Annex 11 在2011年更新後，對供應商管理、雲端/外包、以及基礎設施資格確認都有更明確的規定，比Part 11更全面地涵蓋IT基礎設施。

CDMO合規策略：同時滿足兩套法規

服務美歐市場的CDMO必須同時符合兩套要求。實務上：

  • Audit trail：系統層級的時間戳記審計日誌（滿足Part 11 §11.10(e)和Annex 11第9條）
  • Backup & restore：定期備份並驗證還原能力（滿足Annex 11第7.2條）
  • Access control：角色型存取控制，最小權限原則（滿足兩者）
  • Change control SOP：涵蓋IT基礎設施變更（滿足Annex 11第10條）

EU Annex 11 第4.7條的重要性

EU Annex 11明確要求「IT基礎設施資格確認」，是法規史上少數直接點名IT基礎設施（而非只談應用程式）需要資格確認的法規條文。這直接呼應本GAMP指南的核心主張。

21 CFR Part 11 vs Annex 11 適用情境

  • FDA PAI（上市前稽查）：重點查Part 11合規
  • EMA GMP稽查：重點查Annex 11合規
  • PICS稽查（日本PMDA、澳洲TGA等）：通常遵循Annex 11框架
  • 雙重申報（美歐）：兩套要求都需滿足

思考練習

  1. 若你的DCS系統只向FDA申報（不申報歐盟），你是否還需要滿足EU Annex 11的要求？
  2. 21 CFR Part 11第11.10(e)的「電腦生成時間戳記審計日誌」——時間同步（NTP伺服器）是IT基礎設施還是應用程式的責任？
  3. EU Annex 11明確提到「外包服務」，若你的LIMS資料庫外包給第三方雲端，供應商協議需要包含哪些GxP要求？

On-going quality management of the IT Infrastructure includes assurance that all processes and procedures are in place to control the life cycle activities of the IT Infrastructure platforms, and that qualified personnel are available to complete assigned tasks.

CDMO 實務解讀

GAMP類別速查（IT基礎設施版）

IT基礎設施平台主要落在 HW Cat 1, SW Cat 1-2；應用程式為 SW Cat 3-5。

為何大多數IT基礎設施是Cat 1？

Cat 1（基礎設施軟體）的假設是：這類軟體由大廠商（Microsoft、Cisco、VMware）廣泛測試和部署，行業已積累大量使用經驗，自我錯誤偵測能力強。因此，只需確認安裝正確、配置符合規格，不需要像應用程式一樣進行完整的業務功能測試。

實務提醒：韌體的特殊處理

韌體（Firmware）通常被視為硬體的一部分。但當韌體可以獨立更新時（如伺服器BIOS/UEFI、網路設備韌體），就需要像軟體一樣管理：

  • 納入變更管理流程
  • 分類為SW Category 2
  • 記錄版本和更新紀錄

在GxP環境中，韌體更新需要評估對上層GxP應用程式的潛在影響。

思考練習

  1. 你廠區的Cisco防火牆屬於哪個GAMP類別？其配置文件需要如何管理？
  2. 若VMware vSphere被用作GxP應用程式的虛擬化層，它應被分類為哪個GAMP類別？資格確認策略是什麼？
  3. 「thin client vs thick client」的選擇對GxP合規有哪些影響？本地儲存的資料如何管理？

Processes | 流程

ITIL-based IT Infrastructure processes for GxP compliance · 基於ITIL的IT基礎設施流程

Original Text (English)

The number of IT Infrastructure processes a company decides to implement and the method of implementation depend mainly on the criticality of the business processes being supported and on the size of the company.

The processes listed cover typical aspects required for good business practice, as well as compliance. This list, which takes into account ITIL (IT Infrastructure Library), is indicative only, and organizations may view different processes as being sub-processes to other processes.

Key IT Infrastructure Processes:

  • Change Management
  • Configuration Management
  • Security Management
  • Server Management
  • Client Management
  • Network Management
  • Problem Management
  • Help Desk (also known as Service Desk in ITIL)
  • Backup, Restore, and Archiving
  • Disaster Recovery
  • Performance Monitoring
  • Supplier Management

Key processes are considered further in Section 6 of this Guide.

CDMO 實務解讀

ITIL框架與GxP的整合

ITIL（IT基礎設施資源庫）是全球IT服務管理的最佳實踐框架。許多IT部門已遵循ITIL，而GAMP指南承認ITIL的有效性，意味著：

若你的IT部門已實施ITIL：大部分流程框架可直接用於GxP合規，只需在現有流程上增加GxP相關要求（如更嚴格的文件記錄和QA審查）。

若尚未實施ITIL：可以從GxP要求最高的流程開始建立，再逐步完善。

Change Management的特殊GxP挑戰

在GxP環境中，IT的「變更管理」比一般企業複雜：每一次OS patch、網路設備韌體更新、伺服器配置調整都需要評估是否影響已驗證的應用程式。這需要IT和QA緊密協作，而不是IT單獨決定。

思考練習

  1. 你廠區目前有哪些ITIL流程已實施？哪些尚未建立但GxP上是必要的？
  2. 「Configuration Management（組態管理）」在GxP環境中的「組態漂移（configuration drift）」是什麼意思？如何預防？
  3. 若廠區的IT Help Desk是外包的，如何確保Help Desk記錄的問題和解決方案符合GxP的文件追溯要求？

Personnel | 人員

Roles, responsibilities, training, and qualifications · 角色、責任、訓練與資格

Original Text (English)

Management should define roles and responsibilities (job descriptions) in terms of tasks to be undertaken, and the qualifications and experience needed. Individual job descriptions should be assigned to named individuals fulfilling those roles, permanently or on an ad-hoc basis.

Key roles that may be identified include:

  • Executive Management (Project Sponsorship)
  • Project Manager
  • Application (system/data) owner / administrator / application-oriented SMEs
  • Data owners (if not coinciding with application owner)
  • IT Infrastructure process owner / administrators / SMEs (e.g., IT support engineers)
  • Platform owner / administrator / SMEs (e.g., network engineers)
  • Independent QA in relation to information technology
  • IT Quality and Compliance

Technical and managerial staff with appropriate educational background and experience should be available. Provision of training should be planned to ensure the required skills are developed and maintained.

Staff should be made aware of any regulatory requirements that apply to their duties, trained in those procedures that are applicable to them, and re-trained as changes occur. Records of training should be maintained.

Appendix 1 of this Guide provides more detailed guidance on the various roles.

CDMO 實務解讀

IT人員的GxP訓練要求

IT人員通常具備技術背景但缺乏GxP法規意識。必要的訓練項目包括：

  • 法規認知：21 CFR Part 11、EU Annex 11的基本要求
  • 流程訓練：GxP變更管理SOP、偏差處理程序
  • 文件要求：ALCOA+原則（可歸屬、清晰、同步、原始、正確、完整、一致、持久、可用）
  • 資格確認：IQ/OQ文件撰寫和執行

「獨立QA」的重要性

指南特別強調「independent QA in relation to information technology」——QA對IT活動的審查必須是獨立的，不能由IT部門自己審查自己的資格確認文件。

在小型CDMO中，QA可能沒有IT專業背景，這時可考慮：

  • 聘請兼具IT和GxP背景的IT Quality Compliance專員
  • 外部顧問提供獨立審查
  • 建立交叉審查機制

Application Owner vs Data Owner：不一定是同一人

指南明確區分兩個角色：

  • Application Owner：對LIMS系統（軟體）負責的人，通常是Lab Manager
  • Data Owner：對特定資料集負責的人，例如品保副總可能是「批次放行資料」的Owner，即使她不擁有LIMS系統

在GxP數據完整性調查中，釐清誰是Data Owner非常重要。

思考練習

  1. 你廠區是否有明確定義的「IT Quality and Compliance」角色？這個角色在組織架構中向誰匯報最合適？
  2. 若IT系統管理員執行了一個未記錄的伺服器配置變更，這在GxP稽查中會引發哪些問題？如何從人員/流程角度預防？
  3. 「訓練記錄應被維護」——你廠區的IT人員訓練記錄是在哪個系統中管理的？這個系統本身是否也需要GxP合規？

Chapter 1–2 Summary | 第1–2章總結

Key takeaways and self-assessment · 重點整理與自我評估

Key Takeaways

The Core Logic of IT Infrastructure GxP Compliance

  • GxP applications depend on IT Infrastructure → Infrastructure must be qualified
  • Horizontal (platform-based) approach avoids duplication — qualify the platform once, validate applications separately
  • IT Infrastructure = Platforms + Processes + Personnel (all three must be managed)
  • Platform components are mostly GAMP HW Cat 1, SW Cat 1–2 (lower risk than applications)
  • ITIL provides a solid foundation — add GxP requirements on top
  • Key regulatory hooks: 21 CFR Part 11 (US) and EU Annex 11 (EU GMP)

Common Inspection Findings — IT Infrastructure

  • Lack of documented IT infrastructure qualification (IQ/OQ) for GxP-supporting systems
  • Undocumented changes to servers/OS/database configurations
  • No formal change control for security patches in GxP environments
  • Backup not tested — no verified restore capability
  • Access control gaps — shared accounts, no audit trail
  • IT staff with no GxP training records
核心重點整理

Ch 1–2 的五條核心訊息

  1. IT基礎設施是GxP系統的地基：地基失控，上面的應用程式驗證就失效。
  2. 水平方法是核心策略：一次資格確認平台，多個應用程式受益，避免重複工作。
  3. 三元素缺一不可：平台資格確認了、但沒有受過訓練的人員和文件化流程，合規依然不完整。
  4. 風險驅動，規模可調：小公司不需要和大型跨國藥廠做同樣深度的資格確認，但框架邏輯相同。
  5. IT和QA必須協同：沒有獨立的QA監督，IT基礎設施的GxP合規就是空架子。

最終自我評估 — 你準備好了嗎？

  1. 你能向FDA稽查員解釋為什麼你們採用水平（而非垂直）的IT資格確認方法嗎？
  2. 你的廠區能立即提供所有GxP伺服器的IQ/OQ文件嗎？
  3. 你能說出每個GxP應用程式的Application Owner和Data Owner分別是誰嗎？
  4. 若明天伺服器發生故障，你能在RTO（恢復時間目標）內恢復GxP系統並提供完整的事件記錄嗎？
  5. 你的IT人員上次接受GxP相關訓練是什麼時候？訓練記錄在哪裡？
