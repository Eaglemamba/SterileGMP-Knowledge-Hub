2.3 Process Equipment Design （製程設備設計）— 概述

原文內容 Original Text

This section focuses on the design aspects of process equipment design and the operational characteristics of single-use components for implementation of SUT. The topics that are addressed in this section include: sensors, single-use valves, pumps, single-use assemblies, bioreactors, environmental requirements, and considerations for control systems, utility and process support, maintenance and calibration, safety, and cleaning.

Choosing the appropriate components can be one of the most challenging aspects of implementing SUT, especially for transitions from traditional clean-and-reuse systems. Creating detailed user requirements at the beginning of the project is essential in determining which single-use components are suitable for the application. In selecting equipment, primary consideration should be given to suitability with the process. Considerations should also be given to equipment flexibility, customization options, future scale-up requirements, and supplier support.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：製程設備設計的挑戰

從傳統不鏽鋼設備轉向 SUT，最難的地方不是技術，而是選擇適合的組件。傳統系統幾十年來已有標準規格，但 SUT 市場仍在快速演進，沒有「一套通吃」的標準。

這一節的邏輯是：先有 User Requirements（使用者需求），再根據需求評估哪些 SU 組件「適配」製程。適配性 (Process Suitability) 是第一優先，而非品牌或成本。

比喻說明 Analogy

就像裝修新廚房——你不是直接去建材行買最貴的設備，而是先想清楚「我每天要煮幾個人的飯、用什麼烹飪方式、廚房有多大空間」。SUT 選型也一樣：先寫 URS（使用者需求規格書），再去找能滿足需求的供應商。

CDMO 應用情境

在 CDMO 環境下，同一設施可能服務不同客戶的製程。選擇 SUT 組件時要特別考慮彈性——能否適用於不同製程流體？能否支援不同批量規模？這直接影響設備投資的回收效率。

2.3.1 Sensors （感測器）

原文內容 Original Text

The determination of the level of accuracy needed (as compared to what the traditional system can measure) is important, as sensors may vary in accuracy and cost. For all single-use sensors, it is highly advised to test the accuracy and precision of the device in-house before implementation in the manufacturing facility.

Considerations for the selection of single-use sensors include:

  • Fundamental technology behind the sensor (e.g., a rotary flow meter measuring rotations by reflection of infrared light from rotor blades will not be reliable when used with colored solutions such as cell culture media)
  • Materials of construction (sensors exposed to process solutions and solvents should be assessed for chemical compatibility)
  • Compatibility with the desired sterilization method (e.g., chemical, radiation, thermal) to ensure that calibration is not affected
  • Calibration strategy (availability of pre-calibrated probes, if required)
  • Robustness of the measurement and drift level under actual conditions of use
  • Standardized connections (selection of components with specialized connections may result in being locked in with one supplier)
  • Allowance for the end-user to perform pre-use steps without breaking sterility

2.3.1.1 Level Sensors

Options for level measurements in SUSs include:

  • For hanging bags (with understanding of solution density): load cell strain gauge
  • For mixers and larger tanks: guided-wave radar, floor scale, and load cells
  • For bubble traps: gas/liquid detector

2.3.1.2 Flow Sensors

Options for flow measurements vary greatly in fixed cost, variable cost, and accuracy:

  • Rotary meters: typically less accurate (± 5%) and more affordable
  • Coriolis meters: more accurate (± 1%) and more expensive
  • Ultrasonic flow meters: easily moved but tend to be more expensive
  • Magnetic flow meters: good accuracy for mid-range expense but not usable with deionized liquids

2.3.1.3 pH Sensors

Options for pH measurements include:

  • Traditional pH sensors: Cleaned while connected to an aseptic connector half, then autoclaved. Accurate across the range used in biological operations.
  • Single-use pH sensors: For physiological range (pH 5.5–8.5), can be inserted into the flow path and gamma irradiated. Provides similar accuracy to traditional probes in this range.
  • Sensor spots: Chemical optical spots placed inside a clear bioprocess container before sterilization, read by a fiber optical cable. Limited pH range; certain cell culture media components can affect accuracy. The end-user should verify accuracy on their specific media, feeds, and buffers before implementation.

2.3.1.4 Dissolved Oxygen

The most straightforward way to bridge DO sensing from stainless steel to SUSs is to use traditional DO sensors (cleaned, then autoclaved). Single-use sensors using plastic sheath and optical sensing technology are available, with narrower range and higher detectability limit but similar accuracy in typical cell culture ranges.

2.3.1.5 Pressure

Several types of single-use pressure sensors are available. Main differences are accuracy, connection type (in-line vs. T), inner diameter, maximum pressure rating, and cost. Multiple-use gauges added onto single-use instrument Ts in a tubing set are also common. Calibration concerns may drive the need for a pre-use calibration depending on the criticality of the measurement.

2.3.1.6 Temperature / Conductivity

Most available sensors have accuracy of approximately 3%. The major differences between suppliers are operating conductivity ranges and available hose barb connection sizes. An alternative is a single-use conductivity probe pre-connected to a bioprocess bag, pre-calibrated, irradiated, providing 2% accuracy (100 mS/cm) via an integrated four-electrode system and temperature sensor.

紅外線旋轉流量計用有色培養基就會失準——就像用可見光測量深色玻璃後面的物體，顏色本身就會干擾讀值。這個例子提醒我們：感測器的物理原理決定了應用限制，不能只看規格表，要理解背後的量測機制。

重點提示：pH 感測器的實務陷阱

Sensor Spots（光學感測貼片）操作方便，但特定細胞培養基成分會影響讀值準確性。ISPE 特別提醒：正式導入前，終端使用者必須用自己的培養基、饋料液和緩衝液進行驗證，不能光靠供應商規格書。

在 GMP 生產環境，感測器的校正策略必須在驗證計畫中記載清楚——包括使用前校正、使用後確認，以及可接受的漂移範圍。

CDMO 應用情境：生物製程感測

在無菌充填的 CDMO 製程中，pH 和 DO 是上游生物反應器的關鍵參數。選擇 Single-use pH probe（可 gamma 照射）搭配 Single-use DO probe，可在不破壞無菌屏障的前提下完成 pre-use 安裝——對縮短批次切換時間（Changeover Time）非常關鍵。

2.3.2 Single-Use Valves （單次使用閥門）

原文內容 Original Text

Opening and closing of flow paths on SUSs is typically performed using manual or automatic pinch valves. The two main types of automated pinch valves are:

  • Pneumatic pinch valves: Typically used for larger inner diameter (ID) tubing and can hold back greater pressures
  • Solenoid valves: Smaller and do not require the system to be supplied with instrument air (usually 90–100 psi)

Pressure control valves are simply pinch valves controlled by a stepper motor, such that they have thousands of positions rather than just open and closed. Due to the stepper motor function, solenoid pinch valves have greater versatility than pneumatic valves.

Single-use diaphragm-style valves are also available.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：流路控制的設計邏輯

SUT 的閥門設計圍繞一個核心原則：管路就是閥門。透過夾壓柔性管路來實現流路開關，無需任何接觸製程流體的金屬零件。

  • 氣動夾管閥 (Pneumatic pinch valve)：適合大管徑、高壓差，需要廠務提供壓縮空氣（90–100 psi）
  • 電磁夾管閥 (Solenoid valve)：體積小，不需壓縮空氣，但承壓能力較低
  • 步進馬達壓力控制閥：千段位置控制，可做精細流量調節，比氣動閥更靈活

重點提示

SUT 系統通常沒有真正的壓力額定值（與不鏽鋼管路不同），這意味著管路及閥門的壓力承受能力必須在設計階段仔細評估。若壓力超過管路承受極限，可能導致破袋或管路爆裂，是 SUT 實施的重要安全考量。

CDMO 應用情境

在 aseptic fill-finish 前處理（如配製、過濾）流路中，電磁夾管閥因不需壓縮空氣管路配置，可讓整個 SU 配製系統更容易「模組化移動」，搭配輪式支架可靈活配置在不同生產套房內。

2.3.3 Pumps （泵浦）

原文內容 Original Text

2.3.3.1 Pump Types

Positive displacement pumps are typically used in situations where accurate flow control is required while handling fluids sensitive to shear. The main pump types commonly used in single-use applications are peristaltic pumps, diaphragm pumps, and centrifugal pumps.

Peristaltic pumps operate with flexible tubing. The wear of the tubing will cause a slight drift in accuracy; if better flow control over time is needed, a flowmeter can be installed to control pump speed. The peristaltic pump is more flexible and requires less labor.

Diaphragm pumps can provide higher operating pressures and higher flow rates than a typical peristaltic pump. They can also operate for longer continuous periods.

In certain perfusion systems, single-use centrifugal pumps may be used. These will not build up pressure to the point of mechanical failure of the SUS, but will vary flow with pressure changes (e.g., filter loading).

2.3.3.2 Typical Materials of Construction

There is a large variety of tubing materials for peristaltic pumps; selection should be made with the fluid product as a primary consideration. For diaphragm pumps, construction materials are similar to soft goods in traditional stainless steel systems (e.g., housing PP, O-rings EPDM, diaphragms TPE).

2.3.3.3 Selection of Tubing/Diaphragms

Due to operating principles of positive displacement pumps, there are pulsations in pressure and flow that can cause stress on shear sensitive fluids.

For peristaltic pumps, tubing selection is a delicate balance:

  • Larger diameter tubing at a given flow rate operates at lower RPM — yields less frequent pulsations and lower shear; however, lower velocity can cause air to be trapped
  • Selecting pumps with multiple channels also mitigates pulsation

Additional considerations for tubing/diaphragm selection:

  • Dimensional consistency: Pump tubing must maintain its shape under repeated squeezing to ensure consistent flow rate vs. RPM correlation
  • E&L: Leachables testing should be done under operating conditions since tubing is squeezed, stretched, and stressed
  • Spallation: Fragmentation of material during use must be confirmed under process conditions. Recycling fluid to the pump concentrates any spalled material, making it more detectable
  • Compatibility with sterilization conditions: Autoclaving or gamma irradiation can stiffen material and modify the flow rate–pump speed correlation. Characterization after sterilization is strongly recommended

2.3.3.4 Overall Selection Criteria for Pumps

Recommendations for single-use pump package selection include:

  • Material compatibility with fluid
  • Accuracy of flow rate: A balance must be struck for each process
  • Minimum pulsation and stable flow rate: Pulsations challenge stability and strain components
  • Ease of replacement: After every run, quick and error-free reassembly is key
  • Synergy with other SUS components: E.g., different size tubing provides flexibility with pinch valves at higher flow rates

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：泵浦類型選擇邏輯

生物技術流體（特別是含活細胞或蛋白質的流體）對剪切力 (Shear Stress) 高度敏感。正排量泵（Positive Displacement Pump）以容積推動流體，對流體的剪切傷害遠低於離心泵。

  • 蠕動泵 (Peristaltic)：最常用、最靈活，管路是唯一接觸流體的部分，更換簡單
  • 隔膜泵 (Diaphragm)：高流量、長時間連續操作，適合大批量轉移
  • 離心泵 (Centrifugal)：適合灌流系統，不會累積壓力至導致破袋

比喻說明：Spallation（材料碎裂）

就像橡皮筋用久了表面開始掉橡膠屑——蠕動泵管路長時間被滾輪反覆擠壓，材料疲勞後可能產生微小碎片進入製程流體。在生物製藥無菌製造中，這是嚴重的污染風險，必須用製程條件（而非純水）來評估 spallation 特性。

重點提示：Gamma 照射對泵管的影響

Gamma 照射滅菌會讓多種塑料（特別是 PVC、EPDM 等彈性體）硬化 (Stiffening)。硬化後的管路在相同 RPM 下流量與原先不同——出廠前建立的流量–轉速校正曲線可能失效。ISPE 建議在照射後重新評估流量特性，並在工藝確認中納入此考量。

泵浦脈衝控制框架

蠕動泵脈衝控制策略：
  ↑ 管徑 → ↓ RPM → ↓ 脈衝頻率 → ↓ 剪切力
              但 → ↓ 流速 → 空氣滯留風險↑
  多通道泵頭 → 脈衝互相抵消 → 更平滑流量

隔膜泵脈衝控制策略：
  多個小隔膜交替動作 → 脈衝相位分散 → 流量更平穩

CDMO 應用情境：批次切換

SUT 蠕動泵最大優勢：管路更換不需要 CIP/SIP——直接換新管路即完成批次切換。但要注意每批更換管路後，必須重新確認泵速–流量關聯性（新管路的尺寸有批間差異）。在 GMP 環境中，這個確認步驟應記錄於 BPR 中。

2.3.4 Bioreactors （生物反應器）

原文內容 Original Text

2.3.4.1 Design

The primary application of single-use bioreactors is mammalian cell culture. Single-use bioreactors have had limited applicability with microbial cell lines due to limitations in vessel pressure, mass transfer, and venting. Microbial cell lines also present a challenge when the culture becomes heat generating, requiring a jacketed vessel with cooling capabilities.

Bioreactors are equipped with vent filters and filter heaters to promote evaporation of condensate. Even with heaters, filters may be blocked with condensate from exhaust gases, potentially resulting in overpressure, process stops, and potential breach of the bioreactor bag.

For inoculation or harvesting, all transfers should be performed by pumping since overpressure is not possible. This is a critical point for cells sensitive to shear stress.

Ease of use is an important consideration. As the bags are sensitive and complex setup/operations are required, end-users should be sufficiently trained. Factors to consider include: setup and removal time, number of personnel required, and the weight of the new and emptied bag.

Bioreactor designs range from mirroring traditional stainless steel bioreactors to novel agitation mechanisms. Options are still relatively limited with regard to impeller design, impeller size, and sparger type.

2.3.4.2 Process Transfer/Scale-Up

Single-use bioreactors should be characterized prior to use; a robust scaling factor should be selected for the scale-up strategy. Parameters may need to be adjusted since configuration options are more limited compared to stainless steel bioreactors. The recommended practice is to use an equivalent characterized small-scale model.

Considerations for applying conventional scale-up approaches include matching:

  • Geometric ratio (volume/surface) to retain equivalence between scales
  • Oxygen Transfer Rate (OTR) coefficient (kLa) — kept constant if possible
  • Power input per unit volume (P/V, in kW/m³)
  • Volumetric gas flow rate per unit volume (VVM)
  • Mixing time — an important consideration for pH adjustment

Other approaches: constant volumetric scale-up to maintain shear stress; constant tip speed; constant VVM correlated to theoretical kLa.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：為何 SU 生物反應器主要用於哺乳類細胞

哺乳類細胞培養（如 CHO 細胞生產單株抗體）對壓力容忍度低、剪切敏感，需要低速攪拌和溫和的氣液傳質——這正是 SU 生物反應器的設計「甜蜜點」。

微生物發酵（大腸桿菌、酵母菌）則相反：需要高溶氧傳遞效率（OTR）、高攪拌速度、大量排熱，這些都需要堅固的金屬容器，SU 袋體很難滿足。

比喻說明：Vent Filter 冷凝水堵塞

Vent filter 排氣過濾器被冷凝水堵住，就像排水管被油脂堵塞——系統內壓力無處宣洩，最終可能導致袋體破裂。Filter heater 的作用就像給排水管加熱保溫，讓水蒸氣保持氣態而不凝結成液態。這是 SU 生物反應器設計中容易忽略但非常重要的細節。

放大關鍵參數（Scale-Up Parameters）

kLa（體積氧傳遞係數）= 最核心的放大基準
  → 保持 kLa 不變，確保相同的溶氧供給能力

P/V（單位體積功率輸入，kW/m³）
  → 代表攪拌強度，影響混合均勻性與剪切力

VVM（每分鐘氣體體積/液體體積）
  → 控制通氣量，影響 kLa 和 CO₂ 移除

Mixing Time（混合時間）
  → pH 調整時的鹼液混合效率，
     避免局部 pH 過高損傷細胞

重點提示：SU 生物反應器的放大限制

相比傳統不鏽鋼反應器，SU 生物反應器的攪拌器設計、葉輪尺寸和分氣器類型選項非常有限。這意味著放大時可能需要接受工藝參數的妥協（例如無法完全維持相同的 kLa），而不是把傳統系統的參數直接移植過來。

CDMO 應用情境

在 CDMO 接受新客戶工藝時，如果客戶原本使用不鏽鋼生物反應器，轉換到 SU 生物反應器需要進行小規模等效性研究。ISPE 建議的方法是：先用特性化的小規模 SU 模型驗證放大規則，再在商業規模執行。這個研究結果必須記錄並納入工藝驗證計畫。

2.3.5–2.3.6 Chromatography & Ultrafiltration （色層分析與超過濾）

原文內容 Original Text

2.3.5 Chromatography

Chromatography skids with single-use flow paths generally use peristaltic pumps and thus are not suitable for gradient elution, limiting use to binding/elution or flow-through chromatography operations.

This area of SUT is rapidly changing with advancements in continuous processing, in-line dilution, and in-line conditioning. These technologies often lower flow rates to within SUT limits. However, single-use components may not be compatible with concentrated solutions; end-users should consult with suppliers prior to using single-use assemblies with highly concentrated solutions.

2.3.6 Ultrafiltration

Single-use ultrafiltration membranes/systems are available; however, the vast majority of medium to large scale ultrafiltration systems remain multiple-use. This is mainly due to the cost of the membranes, flow limitations through single-use tubing, and the need to spread cost over multiple batches. As a result, single-use ultrafiltration tends to be restricted to smaller scale and niche applications.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：色層分析 SUT 化的限制

色層分析是目前 SUT 化程度最低的下游純化單元操作之一。原因：

  • 梯度洗脫 (Gradient Elution) 需要精準的多路緩衝液混合，蠕動泵的脈衝特性難以勝任
  • 大規模操作（管柱直徑 > 80 cm）所需流量遠超 SU 管路承壓和流量限制
  • 重複使用 vs 拋棄：色層管柱填料非常昂貴，一次性使用的經濟賬算不過來

重點提示：高濃度溶液的相容性

連續製程和 in-line 稀釋技術降低了流量要求，讓更多步驟有機會 SUT 化。但 ISPE 特別提醒：高濃度溶液（高濃度鹽、有機溶劑）可能與 SU 材料不相容，終端使用者在採用前必須先與供應商確認相容性。

超過濾 SUT 化的困境

超過濾膜很昂貴，每次用完就丟——就像每次做義大利麵都換一個新的義大利麵機，完全不符合經濟邏輯。大規模 UF 系統仍以多次使用為主。SU 超過濾只有在小規模或特殊場景（如高活性物質防交叉污染）才具有經濟合理性。

2.3.7 Environmental Requirements （環境需求）

原文內容 Original Text

The ISPE Baseline® Guide: Biopharmaceutical Manufacturing Facilities defines three categories of processing:

  • Open process: A process exposed to the environment, requiring environmental conditions to mitigate contamination risk.
  • Closed process: A process system designed and operated such that the product is never exposed to the surrounding environment. Additions to and draws from closed systems need to be performed in a completely closed fashion.
  • Functionally closed process: Process systems that may be opened but are rendered closed by a cleaning, sanitization, and/or sterilization process appropriate to the process requirements (sterile, aseptic, or low bioburden). These systems remain closed during production.

SUSs that use ready-to-use flow paths with aseptic connections are compatible with the reduced environmental requirements of closed or functionally closed operations. A process closure risk assessment should be performed to identify the required classification and segregation needed for different process steps.

Hybrid systems, such as a stainless steel vessel connected to a single-use flow path, are a typical application. A system that has any clean-and-reuse portion should be cleaned, closed, and returned to its sterile state. If the system can be returned to its closed sterile state, it could be reconnected to single-use components and operated in an area with lower area classification as a functionally closed system. The stainless steel vessel could be connected via a steam-through connector, cleaned, and steamed in place (SIP). Once through a SIP cycle, the connection is engaged and the hybrid system is both sterile (or bioburden controlled) and closed.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：製程封閉性決定潔淨室等級需求

這是 SUT 最重要的成本節約機制之一：封閉製程可以在較低等級的潔淨室中操作。

  • 開放製程：產品暴露於環境 → 需要高等級潔淨室（ISO 5 / Grade A）
  • 封閉製程：完全不暴露 → 可降至 ISO 8 甚至 CNC（Controlled Not Classified）
  • 功能性封閉：中間狀態 → 視製程性質，通常 ISO 7 或 ISO 8

關鍵工具：製程封閉性風險評估 (Process Closure Risk Assessment) 必須為每個製程步驟分別評估，不能一刀切。

比喻說明 Analogy

想像水在管道中傳輸（封閉製程）vs. 在開放容器中傳遞（開放製程）。管道裡的水不在乎外面的空氣多髒；但一旦要倒入開放容器，就需要乾淨的操作環境。SUT 的「封閉性」讓你把很多步驟的操作環境從「手術室」降級到「一般醫院走廊」——省下大量設施成本。

重點提示：Hybrid System 的管理原則

不鏽鋼容器 + 單次使用流路的混合系統很常見，但要特別注意：不鏽鋼部分需要按照傳統多次使用設備管理（CIP/SIP），完成 SIP 後才能連接到 SU 流路，整個系統才構成「功能性封閉」。兩個世界的管理規則不能混用，必須在 SOP 中明確區分。

CDMO 應用情境：廠房設計節省

封閉製程讓相當多的上游步驟（培養基配製、緩衝液製備）可在較低等級區域完成，大幅降低潔淨室建設和維護成本。這是新建生物製藥 CDMO 幾乎都以 SUT 為核心的原因——不是因為 SU 耗材便宜，而是因為它讓整個廠房設計成本更低。

2.3.8–2.3.10 Control Systems, Utilities, Maintenance, Safety & Cleaning （控制系統、廠務、維護、安全與清潔）

原文內容 Original Text

2.3.8 Control Systems

Skid manufacturers should follow Good Automated Manufacturing Practice (GAMP®), use modern Open Platform Communications (OPC), and allow open standards to end-users — since closed systems prohibit inclusion into a GMP area.

Additional recommendations:

  • Automation should enable end-users to handle multiple scales
  • The system should display process and system operational parameters with a feedback loop for every control parameter (e.g., agitation should verify motor speed to set point)
  • The control system should include a network interface card and a batch manager
  • Connectivity to the network should be considered since the lighter weight of SUSs allows for more mobility (e.g., a single-use mixer wheeled between locations requires data ports at both locations or a wireless radio)

2.3.9 Utility and Process Support

Electrical: Main electrical load contributions are motors and temperature control. Unlike stainless steel skidded systems, electrical connections on SUSs are typically cord and plug, enabling reconfiguration within a suite.

Process Gases: The lack of a true pressure rating on SUSs may require different gassing/venting control schemes from equivalent stainless steel systems.

Water: For smaller scale operations, bagged water prepared off-site may eliminate purified water lines. For larger scale, water systems typically have fewer use points. Due to temperature sensitivity of SUSs, ambient vs. hot distribution loops with coolers should be evaluated.

Drains: Leveraging the closed system abilities of SUSs could allow systems in a classification that allows an open drain. Decrease in CIP activities may reduce pH neutralization demands.

2.3.10 Maintenance and Calibration

Maintenance requirements are generally reduced for SUSs. Skid manufacturers should: include automated pinch valve cycle counts in automation; allow easy access to single-use components; arrange tubing/connectors/filters for accessibility with sufficient support.

Supplier calibration can be used for single-use components, possibly coupled with on-site post-use calibration. Suppliers should be compliant with ISO/IEC 17025 or equivalent and provide transmitter calibration procedures as part of the turnover package.

2.3.10.1 Safety

Additional safety aspects for SUSs beyond standard E-stops include:

  • Secondary containment to capture leaks during system disassembly
  • Biosafety considerations and containment/inactivation procedures
  • Pressure sensors providing feedback to pump motors to prevent leakage due to bag/plastic rupture
  • Guards surrounding tubing for processes with toxic buffers/drugs
  • Guards/shields around exposed drive shafts
  • Electrical wiring designed per governing codes (e.g., UL listed)

2.3.10.2 Cleaning

While process line cleaning is eliminated for fully SUSs, non-product surfaces should still be wiped/washed per facility procedures for the designated area classification.

Single-use components are packaged in sealed containers. Some assemblies are packaged in multiple bags that should be cleaned/wiped as they move into stricter area classifications. Care should be taken in choosing sanitization methods since certain solvents may adversely impact plastics. The intended use of sanitizing agents (including chlorine dioxide and vaporized hydrogen peroxide) should be communicated between end-user and supplier to assess impact on hardware and plastic selection.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：控制系統的開放性原則

ISPE 明確要求 SUT 設備的控制系統必須遵循開放標準 (Open Standards)——使用 OPC 通訊協定，允許整合到廠房的 DCS/SCADA 系統。封閉式的專屬系統會阻礙 GMP 合規（無法整合到廠房的批次記錄系統）。

在 CDMO 中尤為重要：不同客戶可能要求將批次數據整合到不同的工廠資訊系統，封閉式控制系統會成為合規和商業上的阻礙。

重點提示：移動式設備的數據管理

SUT 設備因重量輕可在廠區內移動——但批次數據必須完整記錄（21 CFR Part 11 要求）。在設備移動路徑上的每個操作地點都需要有數據接口（有線或無線），不能因「設備移位」就有數據斷點。廠房設計階段就要規劃好數據網絡覆蓋。

重點提示：清潔驗證的轉變

傳統系統：CIP/SIP 驗證是合規核心工作（大量文件、取樣、分析）。

SUT：製程管路清潔驗證幾乎歸零——但不代表沒有清潔工作。非接觸產品表面仍需清潔消毒。同時，SU 組件進入潔淨區的脫包程序（逐層去除外包裝）本身就是一種污染管控措施，必須有書面程序。

比喻說明：VHP 對塑料的影響

VHP（汽化過氧化氫）是無菌充填廠房常用的環境去污方法，但對某些塑料（特別是矽膠和聚碳酸酯）有氧化攻擊作用——就像用強力漂白水清洗衣物，棉布沒問題但彩色化纖可能褪色。使用 VHP 環境消毒前，必須確認所有 SU 組件材質的相容性，並通知供應商。

CDMO 應用情境：廠務設計優化

SUT 對廠務的最大改變：純水系統 (WFI/PW) 的使用點大幅減少。傳統不鏽鋼廠每個 CIP 站都需要純水接點，SUT 廠可能只在幾個固定位置保留接點，其餘改用預填充袋裝水溶液。這讓廠房設計更簡潔，也降低了純水系統的維護成本——是 CDMO 新廠規劃的重要 ROI 計算項目。

核心概念：濕面 vs 非濕面的分類管理

濕面組件 (Wetted Components)：任何直接接觸製程流體或產品的部件。這是品質控制的重心——Extractables、生物相容性、生物負荷、內毒素、微粒，一樣都不能少。

比喻說明 Analogy

就像飯店房間清潔標準：直接接觸客人的物品（床單、浴巾、餐具）需要最高清潔標準（類比濕面組件），而不直接接觸客人的物品（地板、牆壁、家具）採用較低但仍足夠的標準（類比非濕面組件）。你不會要求地板用餐具消毒等級的方法清洗，但也不能不管它。

重點提示：包裝材料的製造環境要求

Table 2.5 中容易忽略的點：泡棉、外包裝盒等非濕面包材，其製造環境仍被列為 A（Required）。原因是：包材本身在惡劣環境中製造，可能污染組件的外表面，進而在安裝過程中污染潔淨環境。這是 SU 供應商稽核時必查的項目。

CDMO 應用情境：進料品質管理

採購 SU 組件時，常見問題：「供應商的 CoC 說 bioburden < 1 CFU/100 cm²，這是否足夠？」答案取決於你的製程性質（生物負荷控制 vs 無菌）和後續是否滅菌（gamma 照射後 bioburden 要求降為零）。進料檢驗的取樣計畫和接受標準必須基於整個製程的風險評估，而不是照抄供應商規格。

2.4.1.2 Quality Requirements for Single-Use Assemblies

In addition to requirements for single-use components, considerations for single-use assembly quality requirements include:

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：三層品質要求架構

ISPE 把 SUT 品質要求分成三個層次，每個層次都有獨特的附加要求：

  • 組件層 (Component)：基礎品質——材料相容性、生物指標、微粒
  • 組合件層 (Assembly)：製造控制——潔淨室環境、組裝完整性、無菌屏障、滅菌確認、運輸
  • 系統層 (System)：整合確認——機械強度、軟體合規（21 CFR Part 11）、微粒產生潛力

每個層次繼承上一層的要求，再疊加額外考量。

重點提示：Packaging Integrity 的重要性

無菌屏障 (Sterile Barrier) 的完整性是 SU 組合件品質最關鍵的指標之一。組合件通常以真空密封袋出貨——收貨時檢查真空是否保持完好，是終端使用者的第一道品質關卡。破真空或包裝破損的組合件，無論其他指標多好，都應立即隔離並通知供應商。

重點提示：移動部件的微粒產生

SUT 系統中的馬達、風扇、泵浦都是微粒產生源 (Particulate Generation Sources)。在高等級潔淨區（ISO 5 / Grade A）使用時，這些設備的微粒散發特性必須被確認，供應商應提供測試數據，確效中應包含靜態和動態微粒監測。

比喻說明：運輸確認的重要性

ASTM D4169 運輸測試就像模擬 SU 組合件從工廠到你手上的「最差情況旅程」——顛簸的卡車、機場貨艙的振動、倉庫的疊壓。如果組合件通過這個測試後完整性仍良好，才能說明包裝設計足夠可靠。

CDMO 應用情境：21 CFR Part 11 合規

SUT 設備的軟體（批次管理、過程歷史記錄）必須符合 21 CFR Part 11 對電子記錄和電子簽名的要求。在 CDMO 接受美國 FDA 監管的客戶項目時，供應商評估階段就應確認其軟體是否經過 Part 11 審查，並取得相關驗證文件。

核心概念：三層供應商矩陣的管理邏輯

2.4.2 Checking Single-Use Product Quality （單次使用產品品質查核）

原文內容 Original Text

The performance of a single-use product is often dependent on many factors during the manufacturing process and subsequent handling throughout the product's life. It is important that the complete histories of single-use components and assemblies are tracked and documented.

Quality Aspects to be Checked by Suppliers:

At receipt (of components): Package conditions; visual check of components (by trained inspectors); compliance with specifications; presence of certificates; inspection of components (quantity and labeling)

核心概念：供應鏈品質責任分工

這種分層查核結構意味著：問題在供應鏈越早被發現，損失越小。

收貨時不能只看有沒有證書，還要看證書內容：

  • Certificate of Compliance (CoC)：供應商聲明產品符合特定規格——應核對規格版本是否與採購訂單一致
  • Certificate of Quality (CoQ)：附帶實際測試數據（如 bioburden CFU 數值、endotoxin EU/mL 數值）——數值是否在規格範圍內，不能只靠供應商自稱「合格」

在 GMP 環境，CoC/CoQ 副本應保存於批次記錄中。

比喻說明：真空確認 = 無菌屏障的第一道關卡

收到 SU 組合件時檢查真空是否完好，就像打開真空包裝食品前先按壓確認是否「嘭嘭作響」。食品漏氣了你不會吃；SU 組合件真空破了，也不應在無菌製造中使用——無菌屏障已遭破壞，組合件可能已被環境污染。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 你目前的 SU 組合件進料檢驗 SOP 是否涵蓋「使用前確認（At Use）」的所有項目（assembly drawings、connection types、inlet/outlet 確認）？誰負責執行？
  2. 如果發現一批 SU 組合件的真空包裝已破損，你的不符合規定品 (Non-Conformance) 處理流程是什麼？需要通知哪些部門？
2.4.3 Methods Applied to Meet Single-Use Product Quality Requirements （品質要求實現方法）

原文內容 Original Text

2.4.3.1 Quality Management System

Quality by Design (QbD): A QbD approach, as described in ICH Q8, should be applied as part of SUT implementation.

Communication Flow: Successful SUT implementation depends on streamlined communication flow between the supplier and end-user, ensuring the supplier follows the User Requirement Specification (URS) written by the end-user. Assigning a key project contact from both parties is recommended.

Typical interactive activities:

  • The end-user: Writes the URS; reviews and approves drawings
  • The supplier: Confirms understanding of the URS; provides material data, advice on typical designs, component lists, and operation manuals; submits drawings; meets agreed manufacturing/delivery schedules

Quality Risk Management (QRM): QRM methods, as described in ICH Q9, should be applied for defining quality requirements for single-use products. Understanding and assessing product and process risks — with communication to upstream suppliers and downstream end-users — are key factors.

Acceptance/Rejection Inspections: Documented methods for acceptance/rejection of materials should be used throughout the supply chain. Any non-conforming material should be identified and placed in quarantine or returned to the supplier.

2.4.3.2 Development of Design Specifications

Functional and technical design specifications should be defined by end-users, with support from suppliers. Critical aspects (in terms of ensuring product quality and patient safety, per ASTM E2500) should be considered.

2.4.3.3 Qualification of Single-Use Components and Assemblies

Qualification practices should include defined and acceptable methods to qualify single-use components for their use in the manufacture of single-use assemblies and systems. Qualification criteria are often geared towards producing material for specific application requirements. For details of testing performed to meet these criteria, refer to Chapter 8 (Appendix 4) of the ISPE SUT Guide.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：URS 是一切的起點

使用者需求規格書 (User Requirement Specification, URS) 在 SUT 實施中就像建築的藍圖——所有後續的設計、採購、確認活動都以它為基礎。一份寫得不清楚的 URS 會導致：

  • 供應商誤解需求 → 交付不符合要求的產品
  • 確效時沒有清晰的驗收標準 → 稽核時說不清楚
  • 設計變更頻繁 → 延誤進度、增加成本

ISPE 強調：溝通流 (Communication Flow) 必須雙向——終端使用者寫 URS，供應商確認理解並提供設計反饋，終端使用者審核批准圖面。

核心概念：QbD 在 SUT 中的應用

Quality by Design (QbD) 不只用於藥品開發——ISPE 把它延伸應用於 SUT 選型和實施。核心思想是：在設計階段就把品質要求「建入」系統，而不是靠事後測試「檢出」。

對 SUT 的實際意義：在寫 URS 時就明確定義 Critical Quality Attributes (CQAs) 和 Critical Process Parameters (CPPs)，並在 SU 組件選型中確保這些要求被滿足。

比喻說明：QRM 的實際應用

ICH Q9 的風險管理工具（FMEA、HACCP、風險矩陣）在 SUT 中的典型應用：評估「如果這個 SU 袋子破了」的風險——發生機率有多高？對產品品質影響有多嚴重？目前的控制措施（壓力感測器、次要圍堵）能將風險降低到可接受水準嗎？這個思考框架幫助你決定哪些地方需要額外的控制措施。

重點提示：確效測試方法參考 Chapter 8

本節在確效方法上只提供框架，具體的測試方法（integrity testing、extractables testing、bioburden testing 的詳細程序）在 ISPE SUT 指引的 Chapter 8 (Appendix 4) 中有詳細規定。建立 SU 組件確效計畫時，應一併參閱該附錄，確保測試方法符合 ISPE 推薦的最佳實踐。

CDMO 應用情境：客戶稽核準備

當客戶或 FDA 稽核員來稽核你的 SUT 系統時，最常問的問題是：「你的 URS 在哪裡？」「你如何確認供應商的品質系統符合 GMP？」「你的進料檢驗標準是如何制定的？」如果這三個問題都有清楚的書面答案，且與本節描述的方法一致，稽核就能順利通過。SUT 的品質管理不是「多做一堆文件」，而是把現有的 GMP 框架正確地應用到 SU 特有的情境。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 你的 SUT 系統目前有完整的 URS 文件嗎？URS 是否明確定義了 Critical Quality Attributes 和驗收標準？
  2. 在你最近一次 SU 供應商選型中，你使用了哪些品質風險管理工具？結果如何影響了你的最終選擇？
  3. 你的 SU 組件確效計畫是否已更新為參考 ISPE SUT Chapter 8 (Appendix 4) 的測試方法要求？

本節重點回顧 Section Summary

  • 感測器選型原則：理解量測技術的物理機制，優先選擇標準化接頭（避免供應商鎖定）。所有 SU 感測器在正式使用前必須進行廠內準確度和精密度驗證。pH 5.5–8.5 可用 Single-use probe 或 Sensor spots，但需以實際培養基驗證。
  • 泵浦選型邏輯：生物技術流體優先使用正排量泵（蠕動泵最靈活、隔膜泵高流量）。Gamma 照射會讓管路硬化，影響流量–轉速關係，必須在照射後重新評估。Spallation（材料碎裂）須在製程條件下評估，不能只用純水。
  • 生物反應器限制：SU 生物反應器主要適用於哺乳類細胞培養；微生物發酵因高 OTR、高散熱需求而受限。放大時需以 kLa、P/V、VVM、Mixing Time 為關鍵參數。Vent filter 冷凝水管理是易被忽略的設計細節。
  • 製程封閉性決定環境等級：封閉製程可在較低潔淨室等級操作，是 SUT 的核心成本優勢。必須為每個製程步驟進行封閉性風險評估。Hybrid 系統（不鏽鋼 + SU 流路）需明確區分兩套管理規則。
  • 濕面 vs 非濕面品質要求：濕面組件 (Wetted) 需要 Extractables、生物相容性、Bioburden、Endotoxin、Particulate 等全套品質要求；非濕面組件 (Non-wetted) 要求較少，但製造環境仍為 A（Required）。
  • 三層品質要求架構：組件層（基礎材料品質）→ 組合件層（製造控制 + 無菌屏障 + 滅菌 + 運輸確認）→ 系統層（機械強度 + 軟體 21 CFR Part 11 + 微粒產生控制）。每層繼承並疊加上一層的要求。
  • 供應鏈品質分工：組件供應商（進料→製造中→出貨前）、組合件供應商（製造控制 + CoC/CoQ）、終端使用者（收貨查核：真空完整性、標籤、證書；使用前查核：圖面符合性、連接點確認）。缺一不可。
  • 品質管理系統方法：URS 是一切的起點（端到端溝通）；QbD（ICH Q8）將品質建入設計；QRM（ICH Q9）識別和控制風險；進料檢驗的接受/拒絕標準必須書面化，不符合規定品需隔離處理。
