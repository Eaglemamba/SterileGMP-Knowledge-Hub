5.1 Introduction — 導論

原文內容 Original Text ISPE TechTransfer Ch.5

This chapter provides specific guidance for technology transfer of drug substance processes for large molecules (e.g., recombinant proteins, monoclonal antibodies) or small synthetic molecules. This guidance is intended to be relevant to all technology transfers between sending and receiving units.

For this reason, some aspects included in this chapter may not be applicable to specific transfer programs that are usually dependent on the stage in the product lifecycle. For example, early stage transfers (from one R&D group to another), transfers of supporting laboratory scale processes, and transfers of limited process scope may not require all the elements presented.

Technology transfer teams should review the guidance provided and determine which are appropriate for their situation. This chapter can be read as a stand-alone guide for drug substance technology transfer. Further relevant information is included in the complete Guide; references to other chapters in the Guide are therefore included.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 — 小分子 vs. 大分子 DS

小分子 API (Small Molecule API)：化學合成定義明確的化合物，分子量小，結構可完整表徵。可比性較易建立，主要透過 ICH Q3A 雜質控制與 ICH Q6A 規格確認。

大分子生物藥 (Large Molecule / Biologic)：包括重組蛋白、單株抗體 (mAb)。分子量大、結構複雜，生物活性受製程條件（細胞株、上游/下游製程）深度影響。可比性需廣泛分析與功能性表徵，依據 ICH Q5E。

核心差異：小分子的「化學等同」較易判定；大分子的「生物等效」必須透過多層次測試才能確認。

比喻說明

小分子 API 技術轉移就像把一道有固定食譜的料理搬到新廚房——換了爐子和炒鍋，食材不變，只要確認火候和比例正確，成品幾乎相同。大分子生物藥的轉移則像移植一座活的酒莊——光是複製設備不夠，葡萄藤（細胞株）、土壤（培養基）、氣候（環境條件）都會影響最終的酒體風味（蛋白質摺疊與糖基化）。

重點提示

本章明確指出：並非所有元素都適用於每次轉移。早期臨床轉移、實驗室規模轉移的要求低於商業規模轉移。團隊必須根據產品生命週期階段決定哪些步驟是必要的，避免過度設計導致進度延誤。

5.2 Technology Transfer Process — 技術轉移流程

原文內容 Original Text

The focus of this chapter is on the product and process specific requirements of the technology transfer (from the initial high level technology transfer proposal to operational readiness and process/procedure qualification) as analytical method requirements are covered in Chapter 4. Where relevant, aspects that are specific to only large molecule or small molecule drug substance will be indicated.

Information on forming a technology transfer team and developing a charter is presented within Chapters 2 and 3.

5.2.1 Initial Risk/Feasibility Assessment

An initial risk assessment will be required to determine whether the technology transfer of the process and product to the receiving unit is feasible (see also Section 2.2). Prior to obtaining a detailed technology transfer package from the sending unit, sufficient information to perform an initial, high level assessment of the process at the receiving unit should be obtained. At this stage, especially if the process is being transferred to an external company, it may not be possible to share very much information due to confidentiality restrictions and documents may be redacted.

During this feasibility assessment, a skeleton cross-functional team at the potential receiving unit (representing process, analytical, operations, HSE, materials management, regulatory, and quality) should perform an initial risk assessment based on the targeted markets and desired volumes against the product specification, mass balance, raw materials, and the block/process flow diagram.

For example, potential safety hazards, toxicity of the materials, corrosive nature, unique analytical requirements, raw material supply, and plant capacity are factors that should be assessed as part of this early stage risk assessment.

The rationale for evaluating product specification, mass balance, raw materials, and block/process flow diagram in the initial risk assessment are as follows:

  • Depending on the stage in the product lifecycle, the regulatory strategy required to meet the business strategy should be part of the initial focus of the technology transfer. If a product specification has been filed at the sending unit, this will form the basis of the technology transfer to ensure the product at the receiving unit meets the targeted market requirements, with justification of differences from existing pharmacopoeia requirements. The CQAs in the product specification are not the only items needing to be successfully produced as other quality attributes may be difficult to achieve in a new manufacturing facility.
  • A mass balance defines the plant capacity highlighting where equipment changes may be required. For large molecules, equipment changes are likely to be required if the product titer is low at harvest and the drug substance final product concentration is high. For small molecule API manufacture, the mass balance serves to provide process stream composition based on both analytical data and computational analysis. Balanced chemical equations identifying all side- and by-products of the process chemistry need to be part of the initial risk assessment.
  • Assessment of the raw materials establishes critical supply chain, analytical complexity, and any safety constraints.
  • The block/process flow diagram provides an overview of the process defined at the sending unit to gain better understanding of the plant requirements.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念 — 初步風險/可行性評估的四大維度

  • 產品規格 (Product Specification)：已申報的規格就是轉移的底線。接收單位必須確認能達到相同或等效的 CQA 水準，差異需要有科學依據說明。
  • 質量平衡 (Mass Balance)：大分子決定設備規模（例如低效價原液需要更大的純化柱）；小分子則揭示副反應產物與廢棄物流。
  • 原料 (Raw Materials)：供應商認可、分析複雜度、安全限制（如 ICH M7 基因毒性雜質）。
  • 製程流程圖 (BFD/PFD)：讓接收單位在拿到完整資料前先理解製程全貌。

比喻說明 — 評估前的 due diligence

初步可行性評估就像買房前的房屋鑑定——不需要拆開每一面牆，但要確認基礎結構、電路容量、地基是否能承載你的需求。CDMO 接收評估也一樣：在簽約前先確認廠房、設備、分析能力是否能「承載」這個製程。

重點提示 — 保密協定 (NDA/CDA) 的現實限制

初始評估階段，送出單位往往因保密限制只能提供有限資訊（甚至需要遮蓋關鍵部分）。這正是為什麼 BFD 和質量平衡如此重要——它們是在不揭露完整製程細節的情況下仍能進行評估的最小資訊集。CDMO 業務開發團隊應建立標準化的「接收可行性評估問卷」，明確列出最低需求資訊清單。

實務應用 — CDMO 接收就緒性清單（初步評估）

  • 廠房是否有符合製程規模的生物反應器 / 反應釜？
  • 是否具備相關 API 的處理資質（如高活性化合物、生物製品）？
  • 分析平台是否支援所需的測試項目（HPLC、生物活性測試、細胞活性試驗）？
  • 現有原料供應商名單是否覆蓋關鍵起始物料？
  • 法規歷史（藥政審查記錄）是否符合目標市場要求？

5.2.2 Consolidate Knowledge for Technology Transfer Package — 整合知識形成技術轉移套件

原文內容 Original Text

Once the initial risk assessment has been performed and the receiving unit has been identified, the formal technology transfer can proceed. Confidentiality agreements may be required before the technology transfer package can be sent to an external company. A structured approach to sharing process and product information should be agreed upon upfront. Use of a controlled/defined file sharing system helps to limit large email traffic and allows for secure transfer of information and organization of information type (e.g., development reports, batch records, analytical information) between the sending and receiving units.

A checklist of information/documents for large and small molecule processes is provided in Chapter 8 (Appendix 1).

5.2.2.1 Process Information

  • Block Flow Diagrams (BFDs) are a Lean tool used to improve processes. For technology transfers, they ensure that the process steps are understood, in the correct order, and identify all process streams including waste and by-products. They can also serve the same function as the Process Flow Diagram and since they are typically easier to develop, can be used to quickly compare processing options. An example BFD is shown in Figure 5.1.
  • Process Flow Diagrams (PFDs) aid visualization of key data, e.g., processing times, in-process testing, etc. PFDs from all previous installations of the process (different sites or scales) can be helpful, especially for supporting updates to regulatory filings.
  • Process overview descriptions written in a site and scale-independent way so that the particulars of the existing implementation(s) are not confused with the generic requirements or validated ranges. Examples include: tabular data for process setpoints, acceptable ranges for unit operations as well as media and buffer preparations, descriptions of operating procedures and strategies, critical and other important process parameters, CQAs, and KPIs.
  • Manufacturing batch records (blank and/or examples of executed documents where relevant) can be the starting point for the receiving unit to generate their internal batch records.
  • SOPs from the sending unit provide information about detailed operation procedures (e.g., particle sizing for small molecules), although these may be related to site specific equipment and procedures.
  • Drug substance/process control strategy and the link between drug product CQAs, CPPs, potential CPPs, and drug substance manufacturing requirements.
  • CPV data generated to support process parameter trending and any statistical process control analysis.
  • Process observation for transfer of the often tacit process knowledge that arises from hands-on operating experience — e.g., addition of component #4 always causes a color change, pictures of typical cell morphology.
  • PAT (Process Analytical Technology), as relevant.
  • Storage and shipping conditions for final drug substance, including container type, packaging requirements, and any specific transportation requirements or validation.
  • Process intermediate and final drug substance stability information to identify stability constraints that affect hold times between steps or timing of product release for shipping.
  • Process specific deviations, investigations, and CAPAs from GMP batches.
  • Extractables/leachables/elemental impurities knowledge and to clarify which disposable equipment is suitable for use in the process.
  • Spreadsheet-based process models to facilitate process fit at the receiving unit — e.g., predicting column sizes depending on a range of harvest titers.
  • Product and reagent safety documentation detailing any potency concerns and cleanability issues.

Historical data is valuable for product comparability and includes: historical process manufacturing reports, historical process and analytical data (SPC trend charts, tabulated means/ranges), process development reports, virus clearance data (for mammalian-origin molecules), small scale model qualification, process characterization reports (DoE studies, design space, CPPs, Proven Acceptable Ranges), process validation reports, reports from prior transfers, annual product reviews, regulatory license documents, and change control summaries.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念 — 技術轉移套件的四大類資料

完整的技術轉移套件包含四個層次，由粗到細：

  • 製程資訊 (Process Information)：BFD、PFD、批次記錄、SOP、控制策略、CPV 數據
  • 物料資訊 (Materials Information)：原料規格、細胞庫（大分子）、供應商清單
  • 分析方法資訊 (Analytical Method Information)：藥典法、in-house 方法、驗證報告
  • 廠房設備資訊 (Facility/Equipment Information)：P&ID、URS/FRS、自動化控制需求、清潔驗證

比喻說明 — 默會知識的轉移

文字 SOP 傳遞的是「顯性知識」，就像樂譜；真正讓音樂活起來的是演奏家的「默會知識 (tacit knowledge)」——手腕的力道、呼吸的節奏。製程觀察 (Process Observation) 和 SME 駐廠支援，就是讓接收單位的操作員親眼看到「加入第四種原料時液體會變色」這類無法寫進 SOP 的經驗。這也是為什麼現場學習比閱讀文件更有價值。

重點提示 — BFD vs. PFD 的功能差異

BFD (Block Flow Diagram，方塊流程圖)：快速、高層次，適合早期評估和跨廠比較。重點是製程步驟順序、物料流和廢棄物流。

PFD (Process Flow Diagram，製程流程圖)：詳細的工程圖，包含處理時間、在製程測試點、設備容積等數據。適合後續工程評估與法規申報更新。

兩者皆需要，不可相互替代。

5.2.2.2 Materials Information

Early alignment of raw materials or consumables used in the process will facilitate transfer. Materials information include:

  • Specifications of raw materials
  • Critical material attributes
  • CQAs for raw materials that can impact the final API (for small molecules)
  • Bill of materials for the process
  • Media and buffer formulations, including titrants used for pH adjustments
  • Other materials/consumables (e.g., filters, bags, gaskets/O-rings, tubing, sieves for sifters, screens for milling) — including lead times
  • Identification of new materials such as disposables/consumables and understanding of lead times (see Table 5.4)
  • Current suppliers and catalog numbers — primary and secondary sources, including audit findings
  • Identification of acceptable alternative materials (see Table 5.5)
  • Identification of receiving unit restrictions on suppliers or materials
  • Cell bank, origin of cell bank, cell bank testing (large molecule only)
  • Appropriate general safety documentation
  • Excipient risk assessments

5.2.2.3 Analytical Method Information

To support in-process and drug substance testing at the receiving unit, early establishment of analytical methods is a critical activity (see Chapter 4). At a minimum, the following information is required:

  • Compendial (e.g., USP) or other qualified methods used for all raw materials and final drug substance
  • In-house methods used for all raw materials and final drug substance
  • Method qualification and validation protocols/reports at sending unit
  • In-process sample control specifications and methods
  • Setup and qualification of raw material and reagent testing at the receiving unit — this can be a long lead item

5.2.2.4 Facility/Equipment Specifications and Capabilities Information

Information on specific process details that affect the facility/equipment for the manufacturing process should be highlighted early on in the technology transfer to allow any facility modifications at the receiving unit to be priced out, planned, and implemented. This information includes:

  • Piping and Instrumentation Diagrams (P&IDs) for equipment that may be specific to the process
  • Equipment User Requirements Specification (URS) and/or Functional Requirements Specification (FRS)
  • Automation/process control requirements (e.g., pH control strategy, nutrient feed control strategy, controlled rate freezing, UF/DF, chromatography)
  • System size and capacity — suitability of available vessels, piping diameters, pumps, etc.
  • Capability for temperature control if needed
  • Requirements for particular sensors or analytical equipment where differences in types (sensitivity, methodology) or suppliers may result in skewed measurements
  • Cleaning procedures, development studies, validation reports
  • Safety assessments and/or information on hazardous waste or other waste streams specific to the process
  • Instrumentation requirements (pH meters, control systems, PAT technology used, etc.)

核心概念 — 細胞庫轉移 (Cell Bank Transfer) — 大分子獨有挑戰

大分子的製程核心是「細胞株 (Cell Line)」。轉移時必須同步轉移：

  • 主細胞庫 (Master Cell Bank, MCB)：WHO/ICH Q5B 要求完整的表徵套件（身份、純度、穩定性）隨技術轉移一併提交
  • 工作細胞庫 (Working Cell Bank, WCB)：接收單位可能需要建立自己的 WCB，需要再次表徵
  • 法規複雜性：細胞庫轉移本身可能觸發 CMC 申報更新，需要提前與監管機構溝通策略

這是大分子轉移時間線最容易被低估的環節之一。

重點提示 — 等效物料的評估原則

Table 5.4 的例子顯示真實轉移中的物料替換決策。評估原則：

  • 過濾器：相同膜孔徑、相同膜材料 → 可接受等效
  • 緩衝液鹽類水合物差異：需在小規模重新評估配方（pH、滲透壓）
  • 容器材料（Teflon vs. HDPE）：需要 E&L 相容性評估

原則：「等效可接受」需有科學依據文件，不能僅憑外觀相似就判定。

比喻說明 — 分析方法是「翻譯器」

分析方法就像兩個廠房之間的「翻譯器」——同一個製程，用不同廠商的 HPLC 測出的數字可能有系統性偏差 (bias)。如果翻譯器不準確，即使製程做得完全一樣，數據對話也會「雞同鴨講」。這就是為什麼分析方法的轉移需要在 PPQ 批次之前完成——因為你需要確定數據是可信的才能做比較。

5.2.3 Technology Transfer Operational Readiness — 技術轉移作業就緒

原文內容 Original Text

Gap Analysis / Risk Mitigation

Once the technology transfer package has been obtained, the receiving unit can then initiate a process gap analysis in order to compare how the process was operated at the sending unit to how the process will be operated at the receiving unit. This will identify:

  • Where there are gaps in process knowledge
  • Where process changes may be required to fit the new facility
  • Whether any engineering modifications are required for the new facility

Section 3.3.2 describes approaches to risk analysis and evaluation and the tools that can be used. By considering the severity (impact), occurrence (probability) and detection, a risk priority number (RPN) can be derived. This can be used as a basis for determining the level of risk and whether mitigations are required. Examples of process gap analyses are provided in Section 5.4. The gap analysis is often the first opportunity for the transfer team to intensively review the process together, and thus can serve as a good learning exercise for the receiving unit.

Transfer and Implementation of Laboratory Scale Process

If possible, the laboratory scale version of the process should be transferred prior to (or concurrently with) the full scale process transfer. This provides the receiving technical unit the opportunity to work with the process hands-on and catch any details that may have been overlooked in the technology transfer package. Prior to implementation of an engineering batch at the receiving unit, a full laboratory scale run of the process is advisable as soon as possible to help in scaling up the process, to minimize risks and establish acceptance criteria.

The knowledge gained from this exercise can be used for drafting preliminary process descriptions and as a basis for scale-up. Technical SMEs from the sending and receiving units can align on process details. The laboratory scale process may also be used to qualify raw material suppliers or to evaluate revised buffer formulations. For small molecule APIs, laboratory scale/pilot runs can be conducted outside the GMP facility for evaluation/consistency in order to demonstrate reproducibility under GMP conditions.

Low Risk Transfers

If there is sufficient knowledge of the operating procedure of the receiving unit, based on previous transfers, a transfer straight into the manufacturing facility is possible. However, it remains advisable to implement a laboratory scale process that can be operated in satellite mode (using the manufacturing process intermediates, materials, and solutions at each step) during initial full scale operations.

"In a large molecule purification process, buffer pH and conductivity are important measurements for ensuring the required product quality and impurity clearance on chromatography steps. However, the use of different meters between the sending and receiving units may result in small differences in measurements. One way to mitigate this is to send buffer samples between sites and compare measurements."

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念 — 差距分析的三層輸出

  • 知識缺口 (Knowledge Gaps)：哪些製程參數的科學依據不足？哪些默會知識尚未文件化？
  • 製程適配變更 (Process Changes)：為配合新廠設備而需要調整的操作條件（如流速、溫度設定值）
  • 工程改造需求 (Engineering Modifications)：廠房硬體需要投資改造的項目（如蒸汽壓力升級、防塵爆設計）

差距分析的輸出直接決定轉移計畫的投資規模和時間線。

比喻說明 — 實驗室規模轉移是「排練」

正式演出前先進行排練，才能發現演員不熟悉的走位。實驗室規模轉移就是這個排練機會——在小規模條件下，操作人員可以親手執行製程，發現文件裡沒有寫清楚的細節（例如過濾速度太慢、某步驟攪拌需要特定手法）。正式工程批次之前的排練，比工程批次失敗後的除錯便宜得多。

重點提示 — pH/導電度計的系統性偏差問題

引文中的例子說明了一個常被忽略的細節：不同廠商的 pH 計和導電度計可能存在系統性偏差 (systematic bias)。對色層製程而言，緩衝液 pH 偏差 0.1 就可能影響產品的純化效果和雜質清除率。

緩解方案：在兩廠之間交叉比對同一批緩衝液的測量值，若有偏差則在接收單位的文件中建立修正偏差量 (offset) 的依據。此舉必須完整記錄，避免日後看起來像是未申報的規格變更。

實務應用 — Satellite Mode 的 CDMO 價值

「衛星模式 (Satellite Mode)」是一種風險緩解策略：在全規模 GMP 批次執行期間，同步在實驗室以全規模的中間體進行小規模平行操作。當全規模批次出現異常，可立即以衛星模式的中間體進行快速根本原因分析，而不必等到下一個 GMP 批次。對 CDMO 而言，這是在轉移早期維持時間線的重要工具。

  2. 在低風險轉移（直接進入全規模）的情況下，仍建議實施衛星模式的原因是什麼？
5.2.4 At Scale Process Evaluation and Qualification — 全規模製程評估與確效

原文內容 Original Text

This is the principle of demonstrating the process at full scale at the receiving unit. Proceeding to full scale runs may require approval via the stage gate reviews to demonstrate that the required preparation has been completed. When these runs are intended to be fully GMP and released for use (clinical or commercial), it is good practice to document the decision making leading to the conclusion of readiness.

The initial run(s) at full scale may be designated as engineering runs or commissioning runs. These may be fully GMP runs intended for release, depending on the perceived risk and mitigation strategies. Engineering runs are defined here as trial full scale runs performed in the same equipment to be used for GMP manufacturing using the intended procedures and without the intent to be released for use.

  • Engineering runs/testing:
    • If an engineering run is processed, the data should be reviewed prior to the first GMP batch to identify any potential issues. An engineering run provides opportunity to ensure that the data generated would meet acceptance criteria (if performing process validation) or is comparable to previous campaign data.
    • Developed manufacturing instructions should be used during the engineering run and updated with necessary changes if applicable.
    • In-process sampling can be used for testing, including evaluating the status of assay procedures to be used. Samples may also be used for any further studies identified which may be run concurrent with the first GMP batch (e.g., resin reuse).
  • Qualification/validation:
    • The main objectives of validation include ensuring that all facilities, processes, systems, procedures, and equipment that may affect the quality of the product operate reliably and reproducibly and that the process is run under a state of control.
    • A validation master plan should be generated by the receiving unit prior to validation activities.
    • Equipment qualification should be completed in advance of the process validation. Typically, cleaning validation may be performed concurrently with process validation.
    • Process validation protocols should be approved prior to initiation of validation runs, summarizing specific parameters and tests to be monitored and compared to acceptance criteria.

核心概念 — 工程批次 vs. PPQ 批次的定位

工程批次 (Engineering Run)：全規模但非 GMP 放行目的。目的是發現問題、驗證製程說明的可執行性、建立批次記錄的初稿。數據可用於評估是否達到接受標準，但批次本身不用於放行。

PPQ 批次 (Process Performance Qualification)：正式 GMP 確效批次，需依預先核准的協議執行，數據直接支持商業化放行和法規申報。

關鍵原則：工程批次是「試跑」，PPQ 是「正式比賽」。工程批次的數據必須在第一個 GMP 批次前審查完畢。

重點提示 — 確效主計畫 (Validation Master Plan)

VMP 是確效活動的「地圖」，應在確效前完成，涵蓋：

  • 設備確效計畫（IQ/OQ/PQ）
  • 製程確效範圍與批次數
  • 清潔確效策略（通常與製程確效同步）
  • 分析方法確效時間線
  • 成功標準定義

VMP 的缺失或不完整是 FDA/EMA 查廠常見的觀察項目，對 CDMO 承接合約的信譽影響很大。

比喻說明 — 工程批次是「彩排」

工程批次就像正式演出前的最後總彩排——全套佈景、全套服裝、全套流程都用上，但沒有觀眾買票。這讓導演（製程負責人）有機會發現燈光時序的問題、演員走位不順，在正式場合 (PPQ) 前做最後的修正。

實務應用 — CDMO PPQ 批次時間線規劃

典型的商業規模 DS 轉移時間線（以大分子為例）：

  • 技術轉移套件接收 → 差距分析：4-8 週
  • 設備採購/改造：3-12 個月（視缺口而定）
  • 設備確效 (IQ/OQ)：2-4 個月
  • 實驗室規模轉移：2-3 個月
  • 工程批次（1-2 批）：2-4 個月
  • PPQ 批次（通常 3 批）：3-6 個月
  • 總計：12-24 個月（依複雜度）

5.2.5 Finalize Transfer and Perform Review — 完成轉移與審查

原文內容 Original Text

5.2.5.1 Evaluate Transfer Performance Against Goals

In order for the technology transfer to be considered successful, the project manager needs to establish clear boundaries and goals for the outcome of the project. Typically, a commercial-commercial technology transfer is considered to be complete upon successful validation at the commercial scale of the receiving unit with the required regulatory filing. For clinical products, the technology transfer may be considered complete after the manufacture of the clinical batch supply and filing of the investigational new drug.

The goals and boundaries should be defined and agreed upon with management using a formal process such as a charter (see Section 3.1.2). Upon completion of the planned full scale runs, the data needs to be carefully reviewed against the performance goals. Key elements may include:

  • Process data which demonstrates successful execution of the control strategy
  • Satisfactory in-process and drug substance product quality
  • Successful operation of the specified process over an agreed number of batches
  • Material usage within the predicted ranges
  • Successful operation of the plant and equipment with regard to operability, labor, and plant occupancy times
  • Successful manufacture of drug product from the drug substance manufactured at the receiving unit
  • A remediation plan, if necessary, should be agreed to prior to wrapping up the technology transfer activities

5.2.5.2 Project Close-Out

There are likely to be unique goals for each technology transfer. However, a process and product comparability report should be written to demonstrate that the transfer was successful, with justification of any differences which may have occurred due to use of different equipment, different analytical methods etc. Any major changes or deviations should be documented and justified. This report may form the basis of the next stage in the product lifecycle (e.g., from clinical to commercial batch) or may be required for a filing update.

A lessons learned activity between the sending and receiving units, preferably via face-to-face meetings, is useful to identify what went well and what could be improved (covering e.g., program management, technical aspects, analytical/QC, GMP documentation, QA, raw materials). The output should be documented as a summary report to provide a basis for subsequent technology transfer between units and to identify future improvements.

核心概念 — 轉移成功標準的六個維度

技術轉移不是「批次生產出來就算成功」——成功標準包括：

  • 技術層面：控制策略成功執行、製程數據落在目標範圍
  • 品質層面：在製程和原液的品質屬性符合規格
  • 製程一致性：指定批次數內均成功
  • 物料使用率：實際耗用量在預測範圍內（影響成本）
  • 作業效率：人力、設備佔用時間符合預期
  • 下游生產：以新廠原液成功完成製劑批次

重點提示 — 可比性報告 (Comparability Report) 的法規功能

可比性報告不只是「結案文件」，它可能直接作為：

  • 臨床 → 商業規模移轉：支持 BLA/NDA 申報的製程可比性章節
  • CMC 文件更新：若轉移涉及法規申報範圍內的製程變更，此報告是申報附件
  • 後續轉移的起點：作為未來轉移的歷史數據基準

因此，報告中對「差異」的科學合理性說明 (scientific justification) 至關重要，不能含糊帶過。

比喻說明 — 檢討會是「賽後分析」

頂級運動隊每場比賽後都有影片回顧和戰術檢討。技術轉移的經驗教訓活動 (Lessons Learned) 就是這個賽後分析——面對面、跨組織地回顧哪裡做得好、哪裡需要改進。唯有文件化的檢討報告，才能讓下一次轉移不重蹈同樣的覆轍。

5.3 Points to Consider — 需考量的重點

原文內容 Original Text

For large molecules, development of a platform process (for use with multiple products where possible) facilitates process transfer, whether between internal sites or to a CMO. After transfer of the first platform process, the receiving unit will then have some knowledge of process operating conditions. In addition, as a process transfers into a CMO from a process development sending unit, the sending unit will gain more knowledge of the CMO capabilities, which facilitates future process development.

A technology transfer policy and SOP are recommended at the sending unit and receiving unit to define the framework of the transfer, to include, e.g., where decision points should be made and which functional groups should be involved at each stage of the transfer.

During process development for large or small molecules at a sending unit, with the intent of transferring to a CMO selected for partnering, it is recommended to work with the CMO during process development to ensure the process will fit the manufacturing facility in order to reduce risks. The CMO will have standard operating conditions for equipment (such as flow rates on chromatography steps) or there may be equipment constraints in volume handling and there may be a defined timeframe for the manufacturing slot.

To ensure more rapid large molecule process transfers, the use of animal component free, chemically defined media is preferred. The use of media such as hydrolysates or that contain animal components can introduce more variability into a process, which can affect process comparability between sites. If animal components are present, these must be of suitable quality for use in a GMP facility, with good traceability of supply.

Any raw materials or consumables new to the receiving unit may cause delays due to establishment of the supply chain (e.g., new supplier agreements may be required) and availability of appropriate paperwork (demonstrating appropriate quality).

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念 — 平台製程 (Platform Process) 的戰略價值

大分子生物藥的平台製程是指一套標準化的上游（細胞培養）和下游（純化）製程框架，可適用於多個相似的分子（如同類 mAb）。

對 CDMO 的意義：建立一個平台製程後，後續接收同類分子的轉移時間可大幅縮短（因為廠房、設備、分析平台已具備），這是 CDMO 差異化競爭力的重要來源。

典型範例：Lonza 的 Ibex 平台、Samsung Biologics 的 mAb 生產平台——都是透過第一個客戶建立能力後，以更低成本服務後續客戶。

重點提示 — 早期讓 CMO 參與製程開發

這是本章最重要的實務建議之一：若計畫將製程轉至 CMO，應在製程開發階段就讓 CMO 參與，而非開發完成後才「丟」過去。

原因：CMO 的設備有固定的標準操作條件（如色層流速）和容積限制。若開發時沒有考慮這些限制，轉移後必須修改製程，重新做研究，浪費時間和金錢。

這也是 White Raven 等前瞻性 CDMO 建立「早期夥伴關係」模式的原因——在客戶的製程開發期就進場，降低日後轉移風險。

比喻說明 — 化學定義培養基 vs. 水解物

化學定義培養基 (Chemically Defined Media, CDM) 就像標準化的即溶咖啡包——每次沖出來的成分幾乎完全一致。水解物培養基（含動物源成分）則像用天然咖啡豆研磨——批次間口味會受豆子產地和採收季節影響。對跨廠比較而言，培養基的批次間變異是最難控制的「噪音來源」，因此 CDM 大幅降低了跨廠可比性的複雜度。

實務應用 — 建立技術轉移 SOP 的 CDMO 框架

一個完整的 CDMO 技術轉移 SOP 應定義：

  • 各階段的決策點 (Stage Gates) 與審批矩陣
  • 各功能組（製程、QA、QC、法規、物料管理）的責任劃分
  • 技術轉移套件的接收清單與確認程序
  • 差距分析的模板與 RPN 評分矩陣
  • 知識轉移活動的記錄要求

重點提示 — 從 Table 5.6 讀出的關鍵風險模式

高風險項目的共同特徵：

  • pH 量測差異：不同地區的儀器可用性限制 + 取樣位置設計 → 影響關鍵製程參數控制
  • 粉塵爆炸防護：小分子 API 粉末處理的特殊安全要求，資本投入大且可能觸發法規申報

比喻說明 — 差距分析是「體檢報告」

差距分析就像把新廠房送去做健康檢查——不是為了找出誰不好，而是要找出需要治療的地方和治療方案的優先順序。「High」風險是需要立即手術的項目，「Medium」是要追蹤觀察的慢性問題，「Low」是日常調整就能處理的輕微狀況。有了體檢報告，才能制定合理的治療計畫（緩解計畫）和預期康復時間（里程碑）。

RPN 評分方法論

RPN = 嚴重性 (S) × 發生機率 (O) × 可偵測性 (D)
S: 1–10 (10 = 最嚴重：患者傷害/批次報廢)
O: 1–10 (10 = 最高：幾乎確定發生)
D: 1–10 (10 = 最難偵測)

範例 — pH 計差異風險：
S = 8 (影響關鍵製程參數，可能導致批次不合格)
O = 6 (跨廠儀器差異常見)
D = 4 (可透過方法比對發現)
RPN = 8 × 6 × 4 = 192 → 高優先緩解

RPN > 100：高優先，必須緩解後才能繼續
RPN 50-100：中優先，需計畫性管理
RPN < 50：低優先，監控即可

  • 設備等效性：反應釜/生物反應器規格、攪拌功率、溫控精度是否匹配？
  • HSE 合規：API 毒性分類下的密閉性需求是否滿足（含防塵爆設計）？
  • 分析平台：HPLC、生物活性、細胞活力測試等分析能力是否到位？
  • 原料供應商：關鍵原料的供應商是否已被接收廠核可或有快速核可計畫？
  • 公用設施：高壓蒸汽、冷卻水、氮氣等的容量和壓力是否符合製程需求？
  • 文件體系：批次記錄系統是否支援電子化移轉或需要截圖/紙本轉換？
  • 法規歷史：接收廠的 GMP 合規記錄是否滿足目標市場的監管要求？

比喻說明 — 在製程控制是「中途檢查站」

馬拉松比賽中有多個補給站，工作人員會在那裡確認選手的狀態。但並非每個補給站的數據都會記錄在官方成績單上（申報 IPC）——只有最後幾個關鍵站點的通過記錄才算數。製程中每個色層步驟後的 HCP 和聚集體測試，就是這些「關鍵檢查站」——因為後面沒有機會再補救，所以必須在這裡確認合格。
