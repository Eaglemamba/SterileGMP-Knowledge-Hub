Chapter 6 — Technology Transfer of Drug Product（製劑技術轉移）
6.1 Introduction

原文內容 Original Text

ISPE Tech Transfer 3rd Ed.

As a drug product transitions through each stage of the clinical program and a higher degree of confidence in the clinical profile of the product materializes, the strategy should shift to focus upon commercialization of the dosage form. This should include defining process scale-up and transfer plans to the intended commercial manufacturing site.

This activity traditionally occurs between the phase 2 and phase 3 clinical stages, in order to ensure phase 3 clinical and pivotal stability supplies are produced within a commercial facility. This also can be advantageous in supporting lifecycle validation activities for the product and helping to build manufacturing experience in a commercial facility.

In addition, there may be a need to transfer a drug product to a new commercial manufacturing site post-registration and launch. Although more information may be available while transferring a commercial product (filed release specifications, CPPs, etc.), the general knowledge needed and steps to successfully transfer a product remains the same.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 — DP 技術轉移的兩種情境

情境一：開發期轉移（Phase 2 → Phase 3）
在二期臨床確認療效後，公司需要將製劑工藝轉移至商業廠，讓三期臨床樣品與關鍵穩定性批次在商業設施內生產。這是法規要求的重要一步——三期批次必須代表未來商業生產的狀態。

情境二：商業後轉移（Post-registration）
產品上市後因產能、成本或策略因素，將生產移至新廠。此時雖然已有完整的 CPP、規格等資料，但轉移流程的嚴謹度不得降低——法規主管機關同樣會要求申報。

比喻說明 Analogy

開發期的技術轉移就像一家餐廳的試菜廚師（研發廚房）把食譜交給連鎖店中央廚房（商業廠）——不只是交出食譜，還要親自到場示範、驗證大量生產時口味是否一致。商業後轉移則像連鎖店把某一個店面的出餐作業完整移轉給另一個廠址，所有步驟都要重新確認。

重點提示 — CDMO 視角

對無菌 CDMO 而言，最常見的情境是接受 Phase 2→3 轉移，或承接商業後轉移。兩者的法規風險不同：Phase 3 批次若失敗，影響臨床時程；商業後轉移若申報分類錯誤，可能觸發補充申請（PAS）而非 CBE-30，直接影響產品上市時程。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 為何 Phase 2→3 的技術轉移時機對藥廠的臨床試驗供應計畫如此關鍵？
  2. 商業後轉移與開發期轉移在文件完整性上最大的差異是什麼？
6.2 Technology Transfer Process

原文內容 Original Text

This chapter highlights those aspects of a technology transfer process specific to drug product. Analytical requirements are covered in Chapter 4. Note that many of the principles of drug substance transfer, covered in Chapter 5, are applicable to drug products.

In general, a technology transfer can be described by answering the following questions:

  • What is the current process/product?
  • What is the desired final product/process, based on any constraints/criteria related to the new site?
  • What needs to be done to prove that the final product is correct and the process is robust?

The technology transfer is defined by how these questions are answered.

6.2.1 Identify Risks, Conduct Risk Assessments, and Develop Technology Transfer Plan

An initial risk assessment should be conducted to determine whether the technology transfer of the process and product to the receiving unit is feasible. This high level risk assessment may be challenging to complete if the product is being transferred to or from an external company and information is difficult to share due to confidentiality agreements.

A cross-functional team consisting of, at minimum, process, analytical, supply chain, operations, HSE, and quality should evaluate the data available to determine feasibility. Items for consideration include:

  • Projected volume and plant capacity
  • Target markets
  • Safety hazards
  • Special equipment, PAT, and analytical needs

For aseptic processes, additional consideration should be given to facility capabilities to maintain an aseptic area. The assumption is that the receiving facility will have the appropriate controls in place to produce aseptic products (e.g., HEPA filtration, air classification, gowning procedures, etc.).

Based on a positive outcome from this analysis, conducting an in depth risk assessment with technical experts from both the sending and receiving units is highly recommended. It is highly advisable, if possible, to conduct this assessment in a face-to-face environment, to better facilitate the transfer of knowledge. The risk assessment should also capture gaps in product knowledge/process understanding, or highlight the additional information needed from the external site to guide the technology transfer plan.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 — TT 三大基本問題

技術轉移的本質可以用三個問題框架：

  • 現況是什麼？（送方工藝的完整理解）
  • 目標是什麼？（受方設施限制下的最終工藝樣貌）
  • 如何證明成功？（比較性、驗證、PPQ）

這三個問題的答案定義了整個轉移計畫的範疇與工作量。

重點提示 — 無菌製程的可行性評估

ISPE 特別指出，無菌製程的可行性評估不能只看設備清單，必須確認受方設施具備：

  • HEPA 過濾系統與空氣分級（ISO 5/7/8）
  • 更衣規程與人員訓練體系
  • 環境監測（EM）基線數據
  • 滅菌驗證能力（高壓滅菌、乾熱滅菌、過濾滅菌）

這些不是「加分項」，而是無菌生產的最低門檻。CDMO 若尚未建立 EM 基線，技術轉移前必須先完成此工作。

比喻說明 — 跨廠轉移的保密困境

保密協議（NDA/CDA）讓早期可行性評估像是「蒙眼猜物」——送方不能完全揭露配方，受方又無法充分評估設備適配性。實務做法是先簽署 CDA，再以層次揭露的方式：先分享製劑類型（液態無菌）→ 再分享關鍵參數範圍 → 確認接案後才揭露完整工藝。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 當跨廠轉移涉及保密協議時，CDMO 如何進行初步可行性評估而不違反保密義務？
  2. 面對面風險評估會議（Face-to-face risk assessment）為何比書面審查更有效？
6.2.2 Consolidate Knowledge for Technology Transfer Package

原文內容 Original Text

Block Flow Diagrams (BFDs) are a Lean tool used to improve processes. For technology transfers, they ensure that the process steps are understood, in the correct order, and identify all process streams including waste and by-products. They can also serve the same function as the PFDs and since they are typically easier to develop, can be used to quickly compare processing options.

Process Flow Diagrams (PFDs) aid visualization of key data, e.g., processing times, in-process testing, etc. PFDs from all previous installations of the process (different sites or scales) can be helpful, especially for supporting updates to regulatory filings.

The following list indicates useful information about the current process being transferred:

  • Manufacturing batch records (blank and/or examples of executed documents) can be the starting point for the receiving unit to generate their internal batch records.
  • Drug product/process control strategy and the link between drug product CQAs, CPPs, potential CPPs, critical aspects, and drug product manufacturing requirements.
  • Risk Assessments that may have been conducted, including any excipient analysis work or packaging analysis.
  • Process observation for transfer of the often tacit process knowledge. It can be a worthwhile exercise to observe the process operations with the existing manufacturing instruction documents and look for any additional elements that may not be well documented.
  • Storage and shipping conditions for drug product to include container type, packaging requirements, freeze/thaw cycle data, and any specific transportation requirements or validation.
  • Process intermediate stability information to identify stability constraints that affect hold times between steps (e.g., blend hold time prior to tableting, suspension hold time prior to filling).
  • Extractables/leachables/elemental impurities/materials compatibility knowledge (risk assessments or testing data relevant to the product/process).
  • Spreadsheet-based process models, if available, to facilitate process fit at the receiving unit.
  • Product safety documentation detailing any hazard concerns and cleanability issues.
  • Equipment cleaning procedures or cleaning validation information.
  • Historical process and analytical data including statistical process control trend charts, tabulated means and ranges of KPIs, as well as in-process trends for continuous data.
  • QA documentation — deviation reports, laboratory investigations, and incident reports.
  • Process development reports — especially for transfers from process development to manufacturing.
  • Process characterization reports — results from Design of Experiment studies or other studies evaluating the design space around process parameters and resulting CQAs. This informs the process control strategy, guides the establishment of Normal Operating Ranges for the process.
  • Product characterization reports — results evaluating the product characteristics and how they are impacted by processing parameters.
  • Process validation reports — demonstrating the acceptable ranges and robustness of the process at the sending unit.
  • Regulatory license documents (relevant sections) and existing commitments for the process.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 — DP 技術包的關鍵分類

ISPE 的技術包清單可分為四類，幫助受方系統性地整理所需文件：

  • 工藝理解類：BFD、PFD、製程特性化報告、開發報告
  • 管控策略類：批次記錄、CQA/CPP 連結、製程中管控規格
  • 材料相容性類：E&L、元素雜質、容器相容性
  • 歷史數據類：SPC 趨勢圖、偏差報告、驗證報告

重點提示 — 隱性知識的轉移最難

ISPE 特別強調「製程觀摩（Process Observation）」的價值——許多關鍵的操作細節不會出現在批次記錄裡，例如：「加入賦形劑 #3 時溫度會驟降，需立即攪拌」。這類隱性知識（Tacit Knowledge）只能透過親自觀察製程、或送方 SME 到受方現場輔導才能有效轉移。

對無菌 CDMO 尤其重要的隱性知識包含：充填速度微調時機、軋蓋扭力的手感判斷、目視檢查的標準樣品庫。

重點提示 — 無菌 DP 的特殊技術包項目

針對無菌製劑，技術包還需包含：

  • 容器密封系統（Container-Closure System）的規格與 CCI 測試方法
  • 過濾器驗證資料（相容性、完整性、效率）
  • 凍乾曲線（若為凍乾製劑）：各設施設備間可能有差異
  • 無菌工藝模擬（APS）歷史數據
  • 滅菌驗證報告（含生物負荷基線）

實務應用 Practical Application

身為 CDMO 的 TT 負責人，在收到送方技術包後，建議建立一份「缺口清單（Gap Register）」——對照 ISPE 清單逐項確認是否已收到。缺少的項目立即要求補件，並評估是否需要在工程批（Engineering Run）中自行產生這些數據。不要等到 PPQ 前才發現缺少製程特性化報告。

6.2.3 Technology Transfer Execution / Pilot Scale Batches

原文內容 Original Text

Scale-up or pilot batches are often used to gather initial information using the new equipment train while minimizing use of raw materials and API. It is important to design these batches with the final scale/equipment in mind.

For example, for a direct compression tablet process, it would be advisable to collect stratified tablet core samples across the run to determine if there is a tendency toward segregation or a lack of uniformity. For a liquid product, it may be desirable to collect multiple samples during API dissolution to gain an understanding of the dissolution profile.

Pilot batches are also useful in addressing gaps that were identified in the transfer risk assessments. For example, process understanding Design of Experiments that may not be practical on a large scale may yield product understanding across a range of conditions.

For aseptic processes, consider using these batches to aid in filter validation, container/closure integrity testing, and developing protocols for inspection processes.

Depending on the phase of the product being transferred, it may be worthwhile to conduct another risk assessment or gap evaluation once the pilot lots are complete, in preparation for moving to the full scale work.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 — 試驗批次的目的層次

試驗批次（Pilot Batch）有三個層次的目的：

  • 學習：了解新設備訓練下的製程行為
  • 驗證假設：確認風險評估中識別的高風險項目
  • 建立基線：為 PPQ 的比較性提供受方的製程數據

試驗批次不應等同「熟悉操作」，應有明確的取樣計畫與接受標準。

重點提示 — 無菌製程試驗批的特殊用途

ISPE 明確指出，無菌製程的試驗批應同時用於：

  • 過濾器驗證（Filter Validation）：確認過濾器與該配方相容、不吸附 API
  • CCI 測試方法建立：確認密封完整性測試參數適用於受方設備
  • 目視檢查（Visual Inspection）規程制訂：建立受方的檢查標準與樣品庫

這些在試驗批階段不做，等到 PPQ 才發現問題，成本將大幅上升。

比喻說明

試驗批就像廚師到新廚房的第一次備餐演練——不是為了供餐，而是測試動線、了解設備特性、找出隱藏的問題（例如新烤箱的加熱不均勻）。在演練中發現問題，比正式開幕時出錯代價小得多。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 在無菌製劑的試驗批中，為何「過濾器驗證」的時機不能等到 PPQ 前才進行？
  2. 若試驗批結果顯示充填重量的 CV 偏高，應如何決定是否繼續推進到工程批？
6.2.4 Engineering Runs / Pre-PPQ Runs

原文內容 Original Text

Where an engineering run is processed, the data should be reviewed prior to proceeding to batches to be released to identify any potential issues. An engineering run provides opportunity to ensure the data generated will meet acceptance criteria if performing process validation (or is comparable to previous campaign data.)

The initial run(s) at full scale may be designated as engineering runs or may be full GMP runs intended for release, depending on the perceived risk and mitigation strategies. Engineering runs are defined here as full scale trial runs performed in the same equipment to be used for GMP manufacturing using the intended procedures and without the intent to be released for use.

Developed manufacturing instructions should be used during the engineering run and updated with necessary changes if applicable.

In-process sampling can be used for testing, including evaluating the status of assay procedures to be used. Samples may also be used for any further studies identified, i.e., prototype stability studies.

A development report should be compiled to document the process, parameters, findings from development work (edge of failure, sensitive processes), control strategy, etc. prior to moving into qualification runs.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 — Engineering Run 的法律地位

工程批（Engineering Run）的定義非常精確：在 GMP 設備上、使用正式程序、全規模生產，但不以放行為目的。這意味著：

  • 它不是 GMP 批次，但必須用 GMP 設備和文件
  • 數據可用於製程理解，不可直接計入 PPQ 批次
  • 若工程批表現良好，可作為 PPQ 接受標準設定的參考

重點提示 — PPQ 前必須完成的開發報告

ISPE 要求在進入 PPQ 前，必須完成一份開發報告（Development Report），涵蓋：

  • 製程參數與設定值
  • 失敗邊緣（Edge of Failure）研究結果
  • 敏感製程步驟識別
  • 管控策略的科學依據

這份報告是 FDA 和 EMA 審查 PPQ 報告時的背景文件，缺少會被要求補件。

實務應用 Practical Application

在 CDMO 環境中，工程批的資源分配是常見衝突點——客戶希望節省成本跳過工程批，直接進入 PPQ。CDMO QA 的職責是清楚說明：若無工程批就進行 PPQ，一旦 PPQ 失敗，重批成本遠高於工程批成本。特別是無菌製劑，一批 PPQ 失敗的直接成本（原料、人工、設施時間）可能超過 100 萬美元。

6.2.5 Process Qualification (PPQ)

原文內容 Original Text

Pre-requisites for qualification activities in terms of facility, equipment, skill capability, and documentation should be established. The timing of qualification activities may be dependent on the completion of these pre-requisites.

Qualification of Technology Transfer / Process Qualification Runs

Prior to validation activities, a plan should be generated by the receiving unit to determine the validation strategy. Process validation protocols should be approved prior to initiation of validation/qualification runs. The protocols should summarize specific parameters and tests that need to be monitored and compared to the acceptance criteria.

The following points should be addressed by the completion of validation:

  • Control strategy
  • Master and executed batch records
  • Cleaning validation
  • Training and SOPs
  • Sampling requirements to build product and process understanding
  • Stability protocol
  • Analytical methods
  • Acceptance criteria for in-process controls and finished product testing
  • Storage and packaging conditions

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 — PPQ 的法規框架

製程績效確認（Process Performance Qualification, PPQ）是技術轉移的最終驗證活動，其法規依據包含：

  • FDA 2011 Process Validation Guidance：三階段驗證框架（設計→PPQ→持續製程驗證）；無菌製劑通常需要至少 3 批 PPQ，且要求加強取樣
  • EU GMP Annex 15：歐盟對於 PPQ 的要求，需在正式商業生產前完成
  • EMA Guideline on Process Validation：特別強調生物製劑的比較性數據要求

PPQ 前置條件查核清單

設施 / Facility:
  [x] 廠房資格認定完成（IQ/OQ/PQ）
  [x] 環境監測基線建立（至少 6 個月）
  [x] 空調系統認定完成

設備 / Equipment:
  [x] 充填機 IQ/OQ 完成
  [x] 滅菌設備驗證完成
  [x] 量測設備校正完成

文件 / Documentation:
  [x] Master Batch Record 核准
  [x] PPQ Protocol 核准
  [x] SOP 訓練完成記錄

分析 / Analytical:
  [x] 放行方法轉移確認完成
  [x] 穩定性研究方案核准

重點提示 — 無菌 PPQ 的特殊要求

根據 FDA 2011 指南，無菌製劑 PPQ 除一般要求外，還需：

  • 加強取樣（Enhanced Sampling）：統計上有意義的批次內取樣，不能只取規格表規定的最低取樣數
  • APS（無菌工藝模擬）：PPQ 前必須完成培養基充填（Media Fill），且每條充填線需個別執行
  • 同步驗證（Concurrent Validation）：在某些情況下，FDA 允許同步驗證，但需事先向主管機關說明理由並取得認可

Chapter 7 — Quality Aspects of Technology Transfer（技術轉移的品質面向）
7.1 Introduction

原文內容 Original Text

ISPE Tech Transfer 3rd Ed. | p89

This chapter provides guidance on quality aspects to consider for technology transfer, including:

  • Quality representation on the technology transfer team
  • QbD and control strategy
  • QRM
  • Analytical method comparability and stability strategies
  • Process validation strategies
  • Change management
  • Execution

This chapter is intended to be relevant to all technology transfers; therefore, some aspects may not be applicable to specific transfer programs. For example, early stage transfers (clinical versus commercial), may not require all the elements presented. Technology transfer teams should review the information provided and determine which are appropriate for their situation.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 — 第七章的架構邏輯

第七章從「品質系統」的角度審視技術轉移，涵蓋七個面向：

  • QA 的角色定位（不只是審核，要主動參與）
  • QbD 文件的轉移（QTPP、CQA、設計空間如何跨廠）
  • QRM 的應用（ICH Q9 工具在 TT 的具體應用）
  • 分析比較性（送方與受方產品是否等同）
  • PPQ 策略（法規對齊）
  • 變更管理（TT 如何分類為法規變更）
  • 執行品質監督（TT 期間的品質文化）

重點提示 — 階段適用性

ISPE 提醒：這七個面向不一定全部適用於每一個轉移案。臨床早期（Phase 1-2）的轉移，設計空間可能尚未建立，就不需要「設計空間轉移」；商業後轉移則每個面向都必須完整執行。團隊需根據產品生命週期階段，判斷哪些是必要、哪些是建議執行。

7.2 Quality Representation on the Technology Transfer Team

原文內容 Original Text

Quality needs to be considered throughout the technology transfer process; therefore, it is essential to ensure quality is represented in the core technology transfer team. The core team should include representation from QA and analytical/QC as well as SMEs to address technical aspects of product quality, analytical comparability, and stability requirements with sufficient product knowledge to participate in quality risk assessments.

It is important to ensure close communication with the quality unit throughout planning and execution phases to ensure alignment on strategies, timelines, and resource allocations.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 — QA 在 TT 團隊中的雙重角色

ISPE 強調 QA 必須是 TT 核心團隊的成員，而不是後期審核者。QA 的兩個角色：

  • 主動參與（Active Participation）：在風險評估、技術包審查、變更分類等決策中提供品質視角
  • 獨立審查（Independent Review）：在 PPQ 數據審核、批次放行中維持獨立性

CDMO 的 QA 代表需要對送方的品質系統有足夠理解，才能識別差距。

比喻說明

QA 在技術轉移中的角色就像飛機上的副駕駛——不是只在起飛前和落地後出現，而是全程參與飛行決策。若只在 PPQ 報告送到桌上才讓 QA 介入，就像飛行途中才叫副駕駛進駕駛艙，錯誤無法即時糾正。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 若 CDMO 的 QA 代表在技術轉移初期缺席風險評估會議，可能帶來哪些後果？
  2. 在資源有限的中小型 CDMO，如何確保 QA 的「主動參與」不被排擠？
7.3 Quality by Design and Control Strategy

原文內容 Original Text

QbD principles should be considered when the control strategy is developed; these include QTPP, CQAs, CPPs and design space (as applicable). For more information on these principles, refer to the following ICH quality guidelines:

ICH Q8(R2): "demonstration of greater understanding of pharmaceutical and manufacturing sciences can create a basis for flexible regulatory approaches"
ICH Q10: "The goal of technology transfer activities is to transfer product and process knowledge… This knowledge forms the basis for the manufacturing process, control strategy, process validation approach and ongoing continual improvement."

CQAs are the basis of the control strategy and should be available to the receiving unit to provide the scientific rationale for manufacturing process controls and supporting analytical methods. In combination with product quality risk assessments, CQAs ensure adequate controls are in place to produce consistent product quality.

To increase the likelihood of successful technology transfer, changes should be minimized as follows:

  • Materials (including raw materials, starting materials, intermediates, reagents, primary packaging materials, resins, disposables, etc.) should be well understood and, ideally, as similar to the materials used at the sending unit as possible.
  • Differences in the design of the manufacturing process should be limited to differences in scale to minimize any potential product quality impact.
  • Any changes to in-process controls (including in-process tests and process parameters) and release testing should be limited to enable comparison of data to the sending unit.
  • Analytical methods should be consistent between sites. If analytical methods need to change, then ensure equivalency studies are performed so results can be compared.
  • Consider the various levels of controls within the manufacturing process, specifications, in-process controls, batch records, and automation controls. Any differences need to be clearly documented and justified.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 — QbD 文件的轉移挑戰

QTPP（目標產品品質概要）：定義最終產品應有的品質特性，通常隨藥品一起轉移，受方需確認其設備能生產出符合 QTPP 的產品。

設計空間（Design Space）的問題：設計空間是在送方的設備上建立的。受方的設備不同，同樣的參數範圍不一定適用。ISPE 的立場是：受方需要「証明」其設備能操作在相同的設計空間內，而非直接繼承設計空間。

CQA 的轉移：這是相對直接的部分——CQA 是基於產品科學，不依賴特定設備，可以完整轉移。

重點提示 — 最小化變更的原則

ISPE 明確建議：技術轉移期間，變更越少、成功率越高。這對 CDMO 有直接的商業含義：

  • 不要因為受方的設備習慣而主動修改製程
  • 原料商變更必須進行相容性評估，不能直接替換
  • 若分析方法必須變更，等同性研究（Equivalency Study）是強制要求，不是選項

比喻說明 — 設計空間轉移

設計空間就像一份菜單上「鹹度範圍 6-8 分」的標準。這個標準是在特定廚房的爐具上測試出來的。換了新廚房，即使用同樣的食材，也需要重新測試新爐具在同樣的參數設定下是否能做出 6-8 分鹹的料理。直接套用舊標準而不驗證，是重大的品質風險。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 若送方已在 CMC 文件中登記了設計空間，受方 CDMO 能否直接沿用此設計空間？需要做什麼才能合法使用？
  2. 分析方法從送方轉移到受方時，需要進行哪些確認才算完成「方法轉移」？
7.4 Quality Risk Management

原文內容 Original Text

As described in Chapter 1, QRM per ICH Q9 is an extremely important part of a technology transfer project. Successful technology transfer should ensure all high risk areas have been considered, appropriate mitigation has been put in place, and that any residual risks are assessed and agreed as acceptable between sites. The QRM process should be documented.

  • A detailed risk assessment (e.g., using tools such as cause effect matrices, Ishikawa (fishbone) diagrams, FMEA, gap analysis, etc.) should be put in place so that risks are reduced to an acceptable level and controlled through a defined risk mitigation plan.
  • Most technology transfer projects require various risk assessments. Existing risk assessments may need to be adapted to acknowledge changes in risk during the technology transfer.
  • Introduction of new technology to replace obsolete systems or equipment is a common challenge during technology transfers. A risk assessment evaluating new risks caused by new technologies is recommended.
  • Transfers frequently need to be made into existing equipment and facilities. The fit-for-use assessment when the receiving unit equipment is not identical to the sending unit is required to ensure all significant risks are mitigated.
  • Segregation risk assessments are needed to confirm that proper segregation measures have been incorporated into the facility design or via procedural controls. In the case of a multi-product facility, a cross-contamination risk assessment is recommended.
  • Contamination risk assessment (in alignment with EU GMP Annex 1) should be conducted.
  • Consider use of a consolidated risk register to track mitigation of each high risk item.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 — TT 中的 QRM 工具選擇

ISPE 列出多種 QRM 工具，各有適用場景：

  • Fishbone（魚骨圖）：適合識別導致 CQA 偏差的根本原因；適用於轉移初期的問題識別
  • FMEA（失效模式影響分析）：適合製程步驟逐一分析；提供嚴重性/發生率/檢出率的量化風險評分
  • Cause-Effect Matrix（因果矩陣）：連接製程參數與 CQA；識別哪些參數對哪些品質屬性影響最大
  • Gap Analysis（缺口分析）：比較送方與受方的差異；適合 Section 5 的表格格式

重點提示 — 風險登記冊（Risk Register）

ISPE 建議使用統一的風險登記冊（Risk Register）追蹤所有高風險項目的緩解狀態。這是 GMP 稽查中最常被要求查看的文件之一。風險登記冊應包含：

  • 風險描述與分類（技術/法規/商業）
  • 初始風險評分（severity × occurrence × detectability）
  • 緩解措施與責任人
  • 緩解後剩餘風險評分
  • 關閉狀態與日期

重點提示 — 無菌 CDMO 的特殊風險項目

針對無菌多品種設施（Multi-Product Facility），以下風險需要獨立的評估文件：

  • 交叉污染風險：同一設施同時生產多個產品時，需確認設備清潔驗證覆蓋新產品
  • 隔離風險：無菌區的人流/物流設計是否支持新產品的生產動線
  • EU GMP Annex 1 對齊：污染控制策略（CCS）需要對應新產品的特定風險

7.5 Analytical Comparability / Similarity and Stability Strategy

原文內容 Original Text

Analytical comparability is a consideration at any point after the first toxicology lot, when there is a change in the manufacturing process which may potentially impact product quality. During technology transfer, it is advantageous to minimize process changes as much as possible to ensure analytical comparability is maintained.

Analytical comparability strategies are tailored to each product and each change. The comparability strategy incorporates the existing knowledge of the product and process as well as the risks associated with the change itself. In general, products in the early lifecycle stage have limited non-clinical and clinical data associated with the pre-change product and the safety or efficacy profile of the product have not yet been defined. Therefore, the expectations and requirements for comparability increase as the product matures throughout the lifecycle stages.

The analytical comparability strategy depends on the significance of the process changes and their potential to impact product quality, safety, and efficacy. Considerations for potential impact at the following manufacturing stages include:

  • Cell culture changes – consider attributes that may be influenced by cell bank, cell growth, media composition, etc. (e.g., primary sequence, glycans, size, and charge heterogeneity)
  • Purification changes – consider attributes that may be influenced by the performance of the unit operation and processing conditions (e.g., impurity clearance, shear forces, light exposure, temperature changes)
  • Drug product changes – consider attributes that may be influenced by process conditions and product handling, including primary and secondary containers, shear forces, light exposure, or temperature changes (e.g., aggregates, oxidation, deamidation, leachables, extractables)
  • Combination products (devices) – consider changes that may impact device functionality and the interface with the drug product
  • Synthetics – consider process changes that may impact lot release testing

Stability studies at the recommended storage condition confirm the integrity of the product through expiry. Studies at accelerated stability conditions and/or stress stability conditions are often conducted to satisfy regional filing requirements as well as to support product impact outside of the recommended storage condition.

The comparability assessment criteria should be established prospectively. The product quality risk assessment and the QTPP should be utilized to provide additional information for establishing a comparability strategy based on the product CQAs.

In the event a comparability test result yields an abnormal result, teams will need to determine if there is an impact of the aberrant product quality attribute on patient safety and/or product efficacy. A lot selected for comparability may be a lot rejected for clinical or human use.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 — 比較性（Comparability）的法規定義

分析比較性（Analytical Comparability）是指：在製程變更前後，透過分析方法証明產品品質屬性保持相似或等同，以確保患者的安全性與療效不受影響。

法規依據：ICH Q5E（生物製劑）是最明確的指南，要求比較：

  • 理化特性（分子量、等電點、糖型分析）
  • 生物活性（效力、受體親和力）
  • 免疫化學特性（抗原性）
  • 雜質 profile（製程相關雜質和產品相關雜質）

對小分子製劑，比較性要求相對簡單，但同樣需要制訂明確的接受標準。

比較性策略設計原則

生命週期階段 vs. 比較性要求強度：

Phase 1-2 TT:
  → 有限的比較性數據
  → 重點：少量批次確認理化等同
  → 不要求臨床橋接

Phase 3 TT (Pre-pivotal):
  → 完整的比較性數據組
  → 要求放行測試 + 特性化測試
  → 加速穩定性試驗支持

Commercial TT (Post-approval):
  → 最嚴格要求
  → 可能觸發法規申報（CBE-30 / PAS）
  → 多批次數據確認一致性
  → 穩定性橋接研究

重點提示 — 接受標準必須前瞻性設定

ISPE 強調：比較性的接受標準必須在實驗開始之前就設定好（Prospective），不能等到看到數據後才決定標準。這是最常見的 GMP 違規之一——「看到數據不好才調整接受標準」是不可接受的做法，在 FDA 483 觀察項和 EMA 缺陷中屢見不鮮。

重點提示 — 異常比較性結果的處理

若比較性數據出現異常結果，調查邏輯應為：

  1. 確認是否為分析誤差（重測、方法確認）
  2. 若確認是真實差異，評估對患者安全性和療效的影響
  3. 判斷是否需要額外非臨床或臨床研究
  4. 決定該批次是否仍可用於比較性目的（即使不能放行）

不合格的比較性批次並不自動等於廢棄——其分析數據可能仍具有科學意義，需逐案判斷。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 為何比較性接受標準必須前瞻性設定？若在看到數據後才設定，有什麼法規後果？
  2. 一個被拒絕放行的比較性批次，其分析數據是否仍可用於比較性評估？如何判斷？
7.6 Process Validation / Process Performance Qualification Strategy

原文內容 Original Text

Process validation for technology transfer should align with the process validation concepts and lifecycle approach as described in, e.g., ICH Q7, FDA Guidance on process validation, EU GMP Annex 15, EMA Guideline on process validation for finished products, and EMA Guideline on process validation for biologics.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 — 多法規框架的對齊

ISPE 要求技術轉移的 PPQ 策略必須對齊多個法規指南，因為同一個 CDMO 可能同時生產面向美國（FDA）和歐洲（EMA）市場的產品：

  • FDA 2011 Process Validation Guidance：三階段框架，強調持續製程驗證（CPV）；無菌製劑要求加強取樣
  • EU GMP Annex 15（2015）：明確要求 PPQ 報告，規定同步驗證（Concurrent Validation）的條件
  • EMA 生物製劑驗證指南：對比較性數據要求更嚴，特別是糖型分析
  • ICH Q7：API 廠的驗證要求，適用於藥物活性成分的轉移

無菌製劑 PPQ 策略摘要

標準 PPQ 要求（FDA + EU 對齊）：
  批次數量：最少 3 批（FDA 建議，不是強制）
  批次規模：商業批規模（不得縮小）
  取樣要求：統計上有意義的加強取樣

無菌製劑額外要求：
  APS（培養基充填）：PPQ 前完成
  CCI 確認：每批 PPQ 均需執行
  無菌測試：依 Ph. Eur. 2.6.1 / USP <71>

同步驗證（Concurrent Validation）條件：
  → 製程已有充足歷史數據
  → 已向主管機關說明並取得認可
  → 需有加強的即時監測計畫
  → 完整 PPQ 必須在商業批次數量
    達到設定值後回顧確認

重點提示 — CDMO 的商業壓力 vs. 法規要求

技術轉移中最常見的衝突：客戶要求「第一批就商業放行」，但法規要求完整的 PPQ 流程。CDMO QA 的責任是：在接案合約中明確約定 PPQ 批次數量與策略，避免後期在製程驗證策略上產生爭議。若客戶堅持同步驗證，必須確保有法規依據支持，並在 NDA/MAA 中事先申報。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 「同步驗證（Concurrent Validation）」在何種條件下才是可接受的？對無菌製劑是否有特殊限制？
  2. 若 FDA 和 EMA 對 PPQ 批次數量的要求不同，CDMO 應如何制訂滿足兩個市場的 PPQ 策略？
7.7 Change Management（變更管理）

原文內容 Original Text

Technology transfer inherently involves changes to the manufacturing process. These changes need to be managed through the change management system at both the sending and receiving sites. The classification of the technology transfer as a regulatory change (e.g., CBE-30, Prior Approval Supplement (PAS), or equivalent in EU) must be determined before execution, not after. QA must classify the TT change, assess the regulatory filing impact, and ensure appropriate notifications are made to health authorities.

The change management process for technology transfer should ensure:

  • All changes are documented and justified with scientific rationale
  • Impact on the approved regulatory dossier is assessed before implementation
  • Risk-based approach determines the level of change control documentation required
  • The transition point from technology transfer to routine commercial manufacturing is clearly defined, with quality responsibility formally transferred

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

重點提示 — 分類必須在執行前完成

ISPE 的明確立場：法規分類必須在技術轉移執行前完成，不是執行後。若在 PPQ 完成後才發現需要 PAS（需要 FDA 批准才能生產），而生產計畫已排定，將面臨重大的商業損失。

QA 的責任清單：

  • 在接案時就啟動法規分類評估
  • 確認是否需要 prior approval，並與法規事務（RA）部門協同確認
  • 在合約中明確告知客戶申報時程和限制

重點提示 — TT 完成點的明確定義

技術轉移何時算「完成」？CDMO 與送方必須在合約中明確約定：

  • PPQ 報告核准後？
  • 第一個商業批次放行後？
  • 第一次法規稽查通過後？

轉移完成點定義了技術責任的移交時刻——完成點之前，送方 SME 仍有技術支援義務；完成點之後，CDMO 承擔全部生產責任。

實務應用 — CDMO 接案風險管理

CDMO 商務開發（BD）在接受技術轉移報價時，必須要求 QA 和 RA 同步評估：

  1. 這個 TT 屬於哪一類法規變更？
  2. 申報時程多長？對商業化時程有何影響？
  3. 需要哪些 CDMO 端的資料才能支持客戶的申報文件？

這些問題在簽約前回答，遠比簽約後發現問題的代價低。

核心概念解析 — TT 的法規變更分類

技術轉移幾乎不可避免地會觸發法規申報，分類正確至關重要：

美國 FDA（21 CFR 314/601）說明
Prior Approval Supplement (PAS)重大變更，需批准後才能實施，典型：製造廠遷移
CBE-30（Changes Being Effected in 30 days）中等重大變更，通知後 30 天可實施
CBE-0（Annual Report）輕微變更，年報中申報

歐盟 EMA（Variations Regulation）說明
Type II Variation重大變更，需 EMA 審查批准
Type IB Variation中等重大，通知後可實施
Type IA Variation輕微變更，年報中申報
7.8 Execution — Quality Oversight During TT

原文內容 Original Text

Quality oversight during technology transfer execution is essential. The quality unit should ensure that:

  • All deviations during the transfer are documented, investigated, and resolved in a timely manner
  • Critical decisions during execution are made with quality representation
  • The transition from technology transfer activities to routine commercial manufacturing is formally completed with documented quality sign-off
  • The accumulated knowledge from the technology transfer is captured and available to support the commercial manufacturing process

Good documentation during technology transfer execution builds the foundation for the commercial quality system at the receiving site. Every finding, decision, and deviation captured during the transfer contributes to the process knowledge base that will support ongoing manufacture and continuous improvement.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 — 執行期品質監督的四個面向

TT 執行期間的品質監督不僅是「審核文件」，而是主動確保：

  • 即時偏差管理：TT 期間的偏差若未即時記錄，後期可能無法還原事件序列，影響 PPQ 數據的完整性
  • 決策品質：「是否繼續下一批次」等關鍵決策需要 QA 參與和簽核
  • 知識捕獲：TT 產生的製程知識（包括「什麼不行」）必須被文件化，成為常規製造的知識庫
  • 正式移交：從 TT 到商業製造的移交必須有正式的品質簽核文件，不能只是「PPQ 通過就繼續」

比喻說明 — 知識捕獲的重要性

技術轉移就像建設一棟大樓——過程中遇到的地基問題、工法調整、材料替換，如果都記錄在施工日誌裡，未來維修或改建時就有完整依據。如果只記「大樓已竣工」，將來出現問題時就無從追溯。TT 的文件就是這棟大樓的施工日誌。

重點提示 — CDMO 技術責任的移交時刻

ISPE 強調「TT 完成」的正式文件化是 CDMO 與客戶之間最重要的里程碑之一。完成點之後：

  • CDMO 承擔全部的 GMP 合規責任
  • 送方的技術支援義務轉為「諮詢」性質
  • CDMO 的 QA 成為主要品質主管

建議在 TT 合約中明確定義「移交條件（Handover Criteria）」，並要求雙方 QA 主管簽署移交確認書。

  • QRM 工具選擇：FMEA 適合製程步驟分析、Fishbone 適合根因識別、Risk Register 是追蹤緩解措施的主要工具，稽查時必查
  • 比較性接受標準：必須前瞻性設定（Prospective），不得在看到數據後再調整；生物製劑需對齊 ICH Q5E 要求
  • 變更管理先行：法規分類（PAS/CBE-30/Type II）必須在 TT 執行前完成，由 QA 和 RA 協同判斷，避免 PPQ 完成後才發現申報障礙
  • CDMO 責任移交：TT 完成點需在合約中明確定義，並以雙方 QA 主管簽署的移交確認書作為正式里程碑
