7.1 Introduction 7.1 簡介

Original Text

This chapter focuses on electrical services that may have GMP implications. It outlines the critical characteristics of systems appropriate to the manufacturing environment.

導師解析

核心概念

電氣服務的 GMP 角色：在無菌製造設施中，電氣系統通常不直接影響產品品質或患者安全。然而，電氣設備的設計與安裝方式會影響潔淨室的可清潔性、密封完整性以及壓差維持能力。

本章的核心問題是：電氣設備如何在不破壞潔淨室完整性的前提下提供必要功能？

比喻說明

手術室的電源插座：想像醫院的手術室 — 你不能隨意在牆上打洞來拉電線，每個插座和開關都必須嵌入式安裝、可清潔消毒、不能有任何藏污納垢的縫隙。潔淨室的電氣設計邏輯完全相同：功能要有，但不能犧牲環境品質。

7.2 General Requirements 7.2 一般要求

Original Text

When designing, selecting, and installing electrical equipment within aseptic processing areas, GMP considerations are limited to ensuring that equipment is cleanable, ledge and crevice free, non-shedding, sealed, and compatible with sanitization agents.

The selection and installation of all electrical equipment and wiring should, at a minimum, be in accordance with applicable local codes. All electrical components and materials should be compatible with the manufacturing process and operations.

Although the criteria for equipment appear to be identical from ISO 5/Grade A to ISO 8/Grade C environments, the degree of these aspects may differ (e.g., equipment in ISO 5/Grade A environments will require a higher standard). Recessed electrical devices may help achieve the standard required in each of these areas.

Sealed components are specified, not only to alleviate the risk of contamination, but also to cope with the different pressure regimes of adjacent rooms. In ISO 5/Grade A environments, the term “sealed” refers to being hermetically sealed, whereas in ISO 8/Grade C environments, the term “sealed” refers to a high degree of protection against the ingress of water and dust.

Electrical equipment within ISO 5/Grade A environments should be kept to an absolute minimum. Any services that can achieve their function by being located in an adjacent room or area should be so located. For example, a light switch for the room could be located outside the access door in the corridor.

Regarding the manufacturing process, electrical services normally do not affect patient safety and product quality; however, for business continuity and ensuring uninterrupted product supply, these systems should be designed in accordance with GEP (Good Engineering Practice).

導師解析

核心概念：電氣設備的 GMP 五大要求

所有安裝在無菌區域的電氣設備必須滿足五個基本要求：

  • 可清潔（Cleanable） — 表面平滑，可使用標準清潔劑擦拭
  • 無凸緣與縫隙（Ledge & Crevice Free） — 不能有微生物或微粒積聚的死角
  • 不脫落（Non-shedding） — 材質不會釋放微粒到環境中
  • 密封（Sealed） — 防止空氣洩漏影響壓差，並阻止外部污染進入
  • 與消毒劑相容（Compatible with Sanitization Agents） — 能承受 VHP、過氧化氫、酒精等消毒劑

重點提示：「密封」的不同等級

原文特別區分了不同等級的「密封」含義：

  • ISO 5/Grade A — 氣密封（Hermetically Sealed）：完全不透氣，確保單向氣流不受干擾
  • ISO 8/Grade C — 高度防護（High Protection against Ingress）：防水防塵，相當於 IP65 等級以上

這個區別在實務上非常重要 — ISO 5 區域內的每個電氣元件都必須視為潔淨室外殼的一部分。

比喻說明

潛水艇 vs 雨衣：ISO 5 的電氣密封要求就像潛水艇 — 絕對氣密，任何微小的洩漏都不能接受。ISO 8 的密封要求則像一件好的雨衣 — 能擋住水和灰塵，但不需要像潛水艇一樣承受壓力差。

重點提示：最小化原則

ISO 5 區域的「減法思維」：在最高等級的潔淨室中，設計原則不是「如何安裝電氣設備」，而是「如何避免安裝電氣設備」。能放在外面的就放在外面 — 燈的開關放走廊、配電盤放機房、感測器盡可能遠端安裝。

這是 GMP 電氣設計中最常被忽略的原則。許多工程師習慣在需要的地方安裝設備，而不是先問「能不能把這個東西搬到外面去？」

實務應用

GEP 原則：電氣系統雖然不直接影響產品品質，但影響業務連續性。根據 GEP（Good Engineering Practice），設計時需考慮：冗餘供電、緊急發電機、UPS 保護等。停電造成的生產批次損失可能高達數百萬元。

7.3 Control System Power 7.3 控制系統電源

Original Text

The prevalence of digital controls in HVAC and process equipment requires strong consideration of reliable and continuous power to the control units. Considerations should be made with regard to:

  • How multiple power sources affect equipment (e.g., equipment may have multiple power sources, such as line voltage motors, or the control system may operate on Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) while the fan motor operates on three phase line voltage)
  • Providing for orderly shutdown or planned restarts on loss of main equipment power
  • Programming of the systems to prevent tripping of main breakers on simultaneous restart of all equipment
  • Power needs for associated instruments

導師解析

核心概念：控制系統電力

UPS（不斷電系統）：現代無菌設施大量依賴數位控制系統（BMS、SCADA、PLC）。這些控制器對電力中斷極為敏感 — 即使短暫的電力閃斷也可能導致：

  • 控制程式遺失或重置
  • HVAC 系統失去控制，房間壓差崩潰
  • 製程參數記錄中斷，造成 GMP 數據完整性問題

因此，關鍵控制系統必須由 UPS 供電，確保斷電時仍能執行有序關機（Orderly Shutdown）或維持監控。

比喻說明

電腦突然關機 vs 正常關機：想像你正在電腦上做一份重要的報告，突然停電 — 所有未存檔的工作全部遺失。UPS 就是給你的電腦裝一個大電池，讓你在停電後有足夠時間儲存檔案並正常關機。在無菌設施中，這個「未存檔的工作」可能是一個價值百萬的生產批次。

重點提示：同時重啟的風險

跳閘保護（Breaker Trip Prevention）：停電恢復後，如果所有設備同時啟動，瞬間湧入電流（Inrush Current）可能超過主斷路器容量，導致再次跳閘。系統必須程式化為依序啟動（Staggered Restart），避免這種「二次災難」。

這在 CDMO 工廠尤其重要 — 多條生產線的 HVAC、冷水機組、充填設備同時啟動的衝擊電流可能是正常運行的 5-8 倍。

7.4 Power Distribution 7.4 配電

Original Text

Both reliability and stability of the power supply are important.

The impact of surges, dips, or total power loss on the overall manufacturing process, HVAC/mechanical services, or individual equipment items, should be studied to determine risk and effects. Generally, the impact is economic (loss of production capacity). If these impacts are considerable, then a standby generator or UPS should be considered.

For HVAC systems, momentary power losses may be significant; impact could be mediated if there are provisions for fan rotation to continue and room pressures are maintained within acceptance criteria for short periods. The impact of any power loss potentially affecting the sterility of the product should be evaluated.

Power for monitoring of DP (differential pressure) is a critical issue, as loss of differential or the ability to monitor differential pressure during outages may result in significant product quality impact.

Consideration for UPS or emergency generators can be considered for critical processes, equipment, and systems that may provide, maintain, or record critical parameters as part of GMP operations.

Power quality is equally important as precision equipment has a narrow bandwidth to operate within. Special consideration should be given to:

  • Power factor correction
  • Phase balance
  • Harmonics from digital equipment

導師解析

核心概念：配電系統的雙重要求

配電系統必須同時滿足兩個面向：

  • 可靠性（Reliability） — 電力不中斷，保證生產連續性
  • 穩定性（Stability） — 電壓、頻率穩定，不出現突波或驟降

壓差監控（DP Monitoring）的特殊地位：原文特別強調差壓監控的電力供應是「關鍵議題」。因為潔淨室的壓差級聯是防止交叉污染的核心屏障 — 如果停電後既失去壓差、又無法監控壓差，就無法證明產品在停電期間未受污染。

重點提示：HVAC 風扇慣性

風扇旋轉續行（Fan Rotation Continue）：短暫停電後，HVAC 風扇的慣性可以讓氣流繼續運行數秒。如果設計得當，這段時間足以維持房間壓差在可接受範圍內。這就是為什麼原文說「impact could be mediated」— 不是所有短暫停電都會導致壓差崩潰。

但這需要經過風險評估來確認：停電多長時間壓差仍可接受？這通常是確效（Qualification）過程中需要測試的項目。

練習思考

  1. If differential pressure monitoring loses power during a production batch, what investigation steps would you initiate? How would you determine if the batch is still acceptable?
  2. Your facility experiences a 3-second power dip. HVAC fans coast for 5 seconds. Is this sufficient to maintain cleanroom integrity? What data would you need to make this determination?
7.5 Lighting 7.5 照明

Original Text

There should be good uniform lighting levels in all manufacturing areas. Minimum levels in the personnel work areas should be no less than 500 lux, one meter from the floor.

Light fixtures in manufacturing areas should:

  • Be designed and selected to be cleanable, non-shedding, ledge free, or sealed, as appropriate for the different classifications of areas
  • Be arranged to prevent accumulation of dust, and be air tight and sealed to ensure no foreign matter is released into the manufacturing environment
  • Be located so they are not directly above the work area when the manufacturing process is open to the room
  • Have sealing properties which can withstand water jet pressure in wash down areas
  • Be constructed of materials that are compatible with room cleaning agents, which may be corrosive (stainless steel or aluminum fixtures may be considered appropriate because they are non-shedding and resistive to corrosive environments)

In ISO 5/Grade A environments, recess mounted or teardrop fixtures may be appropriate. The installation of surface mounted lighting in unidirectional ISO 5/Grade A airflow zones may interfere with airflow patterns and should be avoided.

In ISO 7/Grade B environments, recess mounted fixtures are beneficial because they can be installed through the ceiling, with maintenance access provided from a walkable ceiling or floor (facility room) above.

Lamps or fixtures, maintained from within the room, may be changed on an annual basis to reduce the effects of unplanned disturbances to production due to occasional lamp failures. Considerations for lighting include:

  • Redundant lighting units
  • Upgraded light types (e.g., T-5 fluorescent or Light Emitting Diode (LED)) which allow for longer life and reduced maintenance intervention
  • Adjustable lighting (adjusted in lux output) for night time operation, as increased light levels have demonstrated to improve night time work performance — if increasing light output, consideration for product impact (photo stability) should be evaluated

If color rendition and intensity of lighting equipment used for inspection, cleaning, etc., is considered critical, appropriate provisions should be made.

Emergency lighting should be provided in accordance with applicable local codes. Combining emergency fixtures with normal fixtures helps to limit the amount of electrical equipment on ceilings or walls.

Since priority needs to be given to the HVAC systems (e.g., air supply diffusers) and services to process equipment, lighting fixtures cannot always be positioned to achieve ideal lighting distribution. Therefore, careful coordination of ceiling services should be considered at the design stage.

導師解析

核心概念：潔淨室照明設計原則

潔淨室照明設計必須平衡三個目標：

  • 足夠亮度 — 最低 500 lux（工作區域），確保人員操作安全與準確
  • 不破壞潔淨度 — 燈具本身不能成為微粒或微生物的污染源
  • 不干擾氣流 — 特別在 ISO 5 單向氣流區域，表面安裝燈具會擾亂層流

核心概念：嵌入式 vs 表面式燈具

嵌入式燈具（Recess Mounted / Flush-Mount Fixture）：

  • 燈具嵌入天花板，表面與天花板齊平
  • 不產生凸出面，不干擾氣流
  • 維護可從天花板上方的技術夾層進行，不進入潔淨室
  • ISO 5 和 ISO 7 區域的首選方案

淚滴式燈具（Teardrop Fixture）：

  • 流線型設計，減少對氣流的干擾
  • 適用於 ISO 5 區域中無法嵌入安裝的位置

比喻說明

天花板是寸土寸金的「不動產」：潔淨室的天花板空間就像市中心的房地產 — HVAC 出風口、回風口、HEPA 過濾器、製程設備接口、燈具、消防噴頭全部都要搶這塊空間。原文提醒我們，HVAC 優先權最高，照明只能「見縫插針」。因此，設計階段就必須做好天花板服務的協調規劃。

重點提示：光穩定性考量

Photo Stability（光穩定性）：增加照明亮度以改善夜班工作表現是好主意，但必須評估光線對產品的影響。某些藥品（特別是生物製劑和某些小分子藥物）對光敏感，過強的照明可能導致降解。

這是一個典型的 GMP 交叉考量：改善一個面向（人員效能）時，不能忽略另一個面向（產品品質）。

重點提示：預防性維護策略

年度定期更換（Annual Replacement）：原文建議燈具可以每年定期更換，而不是等到故障再換。這是因為在潔淨室內更換燈具需要：

  • 停機或暫停生產
  • 可能需要維修人員更衣進入潔淨室
  • 更換後需要清潔和環境恢復

計劃性維護比非計劃性維護的干擾小得多。使用 LED 燈具可以將更換週期延長到數年，大幅減少維護介入次數。

練習思考

  1. Why must surface-mounted lighting be avoided in ISO 5 unidirectional airflow zones? Describe the specific mechanism by which a protruding fixture disrupts laminar flow.
  2. Your facility has a product that is light-sensitive (ICH Q1B photostability testing required). How would you design the lighting in the filling room to balance operator visibility with product protection?
7.6 Hazardous Environments 7.6 危險環境

Original Text

The selection and installation of electrical equipment within hazardous environments (due to dust or solvent vapor) should comply with applicable local codes. Classification of an area to require explosion proof electrical equipment is likely to be very rare, because of the high air change requirement and monitoring of the area; however, design should also take into consideration loss of HVAC fan rotation and reduction of room air changes (e.g., failure mode operations with regards to volatiles).

Hazardous area classification is not a GMP issue, but the class of room may affect the location of production equipment and it also may affect the selection of the electrical equipment. Some electrical equipment may suit a higher class of area in terms of cleanliness.

Electrical equipment within these areas should be kept to an absolute minimum. Any devices that can achieve their function while located in an adjacent room should be located externally and do not need to be classified.

Provisions should be made in these areas to dissipate possible static build-up on personnel, equipment, and materials. Conductive floors should be installed, if necessary.

導師解析

核心概念：防爆環境

ATEX（防爆分類）：當潔淨室中使用含溶劑的製程（如某些注射劑的配液步驟使用乙醇），或處理可燃粉塵時，該區域可能需要進行防爆分類。

但原文指出，在無菌設施中這種情況非常罕見，原因是：

  • 潔淨室的高換氣次數（每小時 20-60 次以上）快速稀釋可燃蒸氣
  • 環境監控系統持續偵測異常

然而，設計時必須考慮「如果 HVAC 風扇停止旋轉會怎樣？」— 這就是失效模式分析（Failure Mode Analysis）的範疇。

比喻說明

瓦斯爐 vs 通風廚房：在通風良好的廚房使用瓦斯爐，瓦斯洩漏的風險很低，因為空氣流通會稀釋任何洩漏。但如果抽油煙機壞了（相當於 HVAC 故障），瓦斯就可能累積到危險濃度。潔淨室的高換氣次數就是那台「超級抽油煙機」— 但你仍然需要考慮它故障時的情境。

重點提示：靜電防護

靜電累積（Static Build-up）在使用可燃溶劑或處理粉體的區域是嚴重的安全隱患。原文要求：

  • 安裝導電地板（Conductive Floors），將靜電導入地面
  • 為人員、設備、物料提供靜電消散措施

在 CDMO 環境中，如果承接含有有機溶劑的注射劑代工專案，這些防爆要求就必須納入設施設計。

7.7 Wiring 7.7 配線

Original Text

If possible, wiring and wiring accessories should be hidden within the building fabric to improve cleanliness, particularly in higher classification areas. Recessed boxes also would be appropriate in these instances. The number of penetrations through walls, ceilings, or floors for services to equipment should be minimized.

Where wiring is installed on the surface, installation should minimize the accumulation of foreign matter and allow easy and effective cleaning.

Enclosing wiring in fully sealed conduit or trunking may improve the level of cleanliness and prevent air leakage from a classified room. Trunking, which is not sealed, may appear clean but act as a reservoir for contaminants. Sealing of conduits and trunking may be necessary to reduce the risk of contamination from outside and loss of air from pressurized rooms.

Lengths of wiring to mobile equipment should be kept to a minimum and should be kept off the floor.

Where necessary, as part of the process operations, wiring and glands should withstand washing.

導師解析

核心概念：配線穿牆管理

穿牆（Wiring Penetration）是潔淨室完整性的重大威脅。每一個穿過牆壁、天花板或地板的電線通道都是潛在的：

  • 空氣洩漏點 — 破壞房間壓差
  • 污染通道 — 外部微粒或微生物可能沿著管線進入
  • 清潔死角 — 穿牆處難以徹底清潔

因此，原文強調必須最小化穿牆數量，且所有穿牆處必須密封。

重點提示：看不見的污染庫

未密封線槽的隱藏風險：原文提出一個深刻的觀察 — 未密封的線槽（Trunking）表面看起來可能很乾淨，但內部實際上是污染物的儲存庫（Reservoir for Contaminants）。灰塵、微粒甚至微生物可以在線槽內部累積，然後隨著氣流緩慢釋放到潔淨室中。

這就是為什麼 ISPE 指南要求線槽必須完全密封 — 不只是「看起來密封」，而是真正能阻止空氣和微粒流通。

比喻說明

水管漏水的比喻：想像你家的牆壁裡有一根生鏽的水管 — 表面看起來牆壁完好無損，但水管內部一直在漏水，慢慢侵蝕牆體。未密封的電線管道就是潔淨室裡的「隱形漏水管」— 外表完好，但持續讓不受控的空氣（和污染物）進出潔淨室。

實務應用

CDMO 配線最佳實務：

  • 在設計階段就規劃所有穿牆位置，預留適當的管線通道
  • 使用合格的穿牆密封系統（如 Roxtec 或同等品），每個穿牆點都要有壓力測試記錄
  • 移動設備的電纜使用天花板垂吊或捲線器，避免拖地造成清潔困難
  • 所有密封點納入定期檢查計畫，確保長期維持完整性
7.8 Door Interlocks 7.8 門互鎖

Original Text

Electrical interlocks for the doors of airlocks or changing rooms assist in maintaining pressure regimes and GMP practices. Alternatively, an audible local alarm could be generated to indicate if more than one airlock door is open at the same time. If interlocks are provided, override features should be included in case of emergency.

Additionally, provisions should include electronic access control for rooms that require restricted access for aseptic operations (e.g., ISO 5/Grade A). It is recommended that door interlocks are verified as part of facility commissioning/qualification per GEP.

導師解析

核心概念：門連鎖系統

門連鎖 / 互鎖（Door Interlock）：確保氣閘（Airlock）的兩扇門不會同時打開的電氣控制機制。這是維持潔淨室壓差級聯的關鍵防線。

運作原理：

  • 當一扇門打開時，另一扇門被電氣鎖定，無法開啟
  • 只有當第一扇門完全關閉後，第二扇門才能解鎖
  • 如果兩扇門同時打開（故障情況），系統觸發警報

比喻說明

銀行金庫的雙門設計：就像銀行金庫的入口 — 你必須先進入第一扇門，等它關閉後，第二扇門才會打開。這確保金庫不會直接暴露在外界環境中。潔淨室的氣閘互鎖設計邏輯完全相同 — 防止高潔淨度區域直接暴露在低潔淨度區域的空氣中。

重點提示：緊急覆寫功能

Override（覆寫 / 強制開啟）：原文特別強調，門互鎖系統必須包含緊急覆寫功能。這是人命安全（Life Safety）的基本要求 — 在火災、化學洩漏或其他緊急情況下，人員必須能夠快速撤離，不能被互鎖系統困住。

這是一個典型的 GMP vs 安全 的權衡：正常情況下互鎖保護產品品質，緊急情況下人命安全優先。

重點提示：電子門禁

電子門禁控制（Electronic Access Control）在 ISO 5 無菌操作區域是必要的。目的是：

  • 限制只有經過培訓和更衣認證的人員才能進入
  • 記錄進出人員和時間，提供可追溯性
  • 防止未授權人員進入可能污染無菌環境

門互鎖系統應在設施試車 / 確效（Commissioning / Qualification）階段進行驗證，確認其功能符合設計要求。

7.9 Outlets and Miscellaneous Equipment 7.9 插座與其他設備

Original Text

Electrical components should be designed and selected to be:

  • Cleanable
  • Non-shedding
  • Ledge and crevice-free, or sealed
  • Appropriate to the classification (HVAC grade) of area

Fittings in process areas should be arranged to prevent accumulation of dust and be air tight and sealed, to ensure no foreign matter is released into the manufacturing environment. Recess mounting of fittings in these areas provides distinct benefits.

Aspirated fire detection, or systems able to detect fire within the HVAC extract systems for ISO 5/Grade A environments, may avoid installing conventional fire/smoke detection equipment within the room. Flashing lights, in place of conventional sounders, also may be beneficial. Additionally, source specific cleanroom appropriate sprinkler heads may be used for fire suppression systems.

Sealing membranes on loudspeaker systems located within ISO 5/Grade A and ISO 7/Grade B environments should be considered. A membrane in the wall between two adjoining rooms may provide acceptable voice communication between those rooms. Consideration for use of appropriate cleanable/sealed wireless communication should be given.

Insect light traps should be placed strategically outside of cleanroom areas to reduce contamination risks from flying insects.

Since sinks and drains are not permitted in aseptic processing areas (ISO 5/Grade A and ISO 7/Grade B), an electronically heated (bio-control) trap beneath sinks in ISO 8/Grade C environments could serve as an alternative to routine chemical sanitization.

導師解析

核心概念：雜項設備的 GMP 設計

潔淨室內除了照明和配電之外，還有許多「雜項」電氣設備需要符合 GMP 要求。原文列舉了幾個重要類別：

  • 消防偵測 — 吸入式偵煙（Aspirated Fire Detection）可以避免在潔淨室內安裝傳統煙霧偵測器
  • 通訊系統 — 喇叭需要加裝密封膜，或改用無線通訊
  • 蟲害防治 — 捕蟲燈（Insect Light Traps）放在潔淨室外圍
  • 排水控制 — ISO 8 區域水槽下方使用電加熱生物控制陷阱

重點提示：吸入式偵煙系統

Aspirated Fire Detection（吸入式偵煙）：這是一種將空氣從被監控區域持續吸入到遠端偵測器的系統。它的優點在於：

  • 潔淨室內無設備 — 偵測器主體安裝在技術區域，只有小型取樣管在潔淨室內
  • 高靈敏度 — 可以在極早期偵測到煙霧
  • 避免誤報 — 傳統煙霧偵測器可能被 VHP 消毒的霧氣觸發

在 ISO 5 區域，這幾乎是唯一可行的偵煙方案。

重點提示：無菌區禁止排水

ISO 5 和 ISO 7 區域不允許有水槽和排水口 — 這是 GMP 的基本原則。排水系統是微生物生長的溫床，直接威脅無菌環境。

但在 ISO 8/Grade C 區域，水槽是必要的（如手部清潔）。原文建議在水槽下方使用電加熱生物控制陷阱（Bio-control Trap） — 通過加熱來抑制排水管中的微生物生長，作為化學消毒的替代方案。

比喻說明

潔淨室是「無聲手術室」：在手術室裡，你不會安裝普通的家用對講機 — 所有通訊設備都必須防水、可消毒、不釋放微粒。潔淨室的喇叭系統也是如此 — 加裝密封膜後，聲音可以穿透，但空氣和微粒不能。現代趨勢是使用可清潔的密封式無線通訊設備，完全消除牆壁上的開孔需求。

實務應用

CDMO 設施規劃清單：

  • ISO 5 區域：吸入式偵煙 + 閃燈警報（取代蜂鳴器） + 密封式通訊
  • ISO 7 區域：嵌入式消防偵測 + 密封喇叭膜 + 無排水設計
  • ISO 8 區域：標準偵煙（含防 VHP 誤報） + 電加熱排水陷阱 + 外圍捕蟲燈
  • 所有區域：嵌入式或齊平安裝、可清潔材質、與消毒劑相容

Section Summary / 本章重點回顧

  • General Requirements / 一般要求：All electrical equipment in aseptic areas must be cleanable, ledge-free, non-shedding, sealed, and compatible with sanitization agents. ISO 5 areas require hermetic sealing and absolute minimization of installed equipment / 無菌區域所有電氣設備必須可清潔、無凸緣、不脫落、密封、與消毒劑相容。ISO 5 區域要求氣密封和最小化安裝量
  • Control System Power / 控制系統電力：UPS is essential for digital control systems (BMS, SCADA, PLC) to ensure orderly shutdown and data integrity during power loss. Staggered restart programming prevents breaker trips / UPS 對數位控制系統不可或缺，確保斷電時有序關機與數據完整性。依序重啟程式防止跳閘
  • Power Distribution / 配電系統：DP monitoring power is critical — loss of pressure monitoring during outages directly impacts product quality assessment. Power quality (harmonics, phase balance) matters for precision equipment / 差壓監控的供電至關重要。電力品質（諧波、相位平衡）影響精密設備運作
  • Lighting / 照明：Minimum 500 lux at work areas. Flush-mount (recess) fixtures mandatory in ISO 5 to avoid airflow disruption. LED preferred for reduced maintenance. Consider photo stability impact on light-sensitive products / 工作區最低 500 lux。ISO 5 區域必須使用嵌入式燈具以避免干擾氣流。LED 首選以減少維護。須考慮光穩定性影響
  • Hazardous Environments / 防爆環境：ATEX classification is rare in sterile facilities due to high air changes, but failure mode analysis (HVAC loss) is required. Static dissipation and conductive floors may be needed / 無菌設施中防爆分類較罕見，但需進行失效模式分析。可能需要靜電消散和導電地板
  • Wiring / 配線：Minimize wall/ceiling penetrations, seal all conduits. Unsealed trunking is a hidden contamination reservoir. Mobile equipment cables must be kept off the floor / 最小化穿牆數量，密封所有管線。未密封線槽是隱藏的污染庫。移動設備電纜不可拖地
  • Door Interlocks / 門連鎖：Airlock interlocks maintain pressure cascades. Emergency override is mandatory for life safety. Electronic access control required for ISO 5 areas. Verify during commissioning/qualification / 氣閘互鎖維持壓差級聯。緊急覆寫功能為強制要求。ISO 5 區域需電子門禁。試車確效時需驗證
  • Miscellaneous / 雜項設備：Aspirated fire detection for ISO 5 (avoids in-room equipment). Sealed communication membranes. Insect traps outside cleanrooms. Bio-control heated traps for ISO 8 drains / ISO 5 使用吸入式偵煙。通訊系統加密封膜。捕蟲燈設於潔淨室外。ISO 8 排水使用電加熱生物控制陷阱
