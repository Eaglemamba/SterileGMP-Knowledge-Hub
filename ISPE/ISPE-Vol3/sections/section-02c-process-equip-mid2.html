3.2.7 Terminal Sterilization / 最終滅菌

Original Text — ISPE Baseline Guide

3.2.7 Terminal Sterilization

Terminal sterilization refers to a process within which the product, filled and sealed in its final primary package, is sterilized via moist heat, irradiation, or other suitable method. It is a well-accepted principle that sterile drugs should be manufactured using aseptic processing only when terminal sterilization is not feasible due to instability in the drug formulation.

Design and qualification of terminal sterilization processes is a complex topic. For additional information, refer to USP <1229.2>.

Terminal sterilization can be undertaken as a full overkill process (F0 ≥ 15), or as an adjunct process (F0 ≥ 8) for products that cannot tolerate full overkill exposure. The endpoint objective of both processes is the same — to produce product having a Probability of a Non-Sterile Unit (PNSU) of ≥ 1 in 106. Pre-sterilization bioburden is reduced using validated methods and routinely monitored, in order to achieve this level of sterility assurance for the adjunct process.

Design Factors for Terminal Sterilization

Several factors should be considered when designing a terminal sterilization process:

  • D-value of target organisms: Many products exhibit a protective effect on challenge organisms, increasing the required target F0. This is particularly important for adjunct processes, due to their sub-overkill lethality.
  • Range of F0 for the process: Container type and nominal size, fill volume, autoclave size, autoclave type, and product load pattern all have significant influence on the range of F0 delivered throughout the load. F0 is accumulated during heat up, cool down, and the soak interval at and above 121°C.
  • Maximum allowable pre-sterilization hold time: The time between completion of filling and start of sterilization should be defined, in order to ensure control of pre-sterilization bioburden. The allowable hold times directly impact staging area size requirements and material flow.

For products that are not heat-labile, stability samples may be subjected to single and double terminal sterilization cycles (worst case). Data is generated during stability testing to support additional processing as may be required.

教學解說 — 繁體中文

最終滅菌概述

最終滅菌（Terminal Sterilization）是將產品在已完成充填與密封的最終包裝狀態下進行滅菌的製程。常用方法包括濕熱（蒸汽滅菌鍋）、輻射照射及其他適當方法。

核心原則：最終滅菌優先

國際法規共識：只有當藥品配方無法承受最終滅菌條件（如蛋白質藥物熱不穩定）時，才應採用無菌製程（Aseptic Processing）。這反映了「能最終滅菌就不該用無菌製程」的基本邏輯。

F0 值與滅菌策略

F0 值代表在 121°C 下的等效滅菌時間：

  • 過度殺滅（Overkill）：F0 ≥ 15 — 不需了解產品中的生物負荷，純靠高 F0 保證滅菌
  • 輔助製程（Adjunct）：F0 ≥ 8 — 適用於不耐高溫產品，但需搭配嚴格的滅菌前生物負荷監控

兩者目標相同：達到 PNSU ≥ 10-6（每一百萬單位中不超過一個非無菌單位）。

比喻：全熟牛排 vs. 三分熟牛排

Overkill 像全熟牛排 — 不管肉的品質如何，高溫長時間一定熟透。Adjunct 像三分熟牛排 — 必須確認肉源品質優良（低生物負荷），才能用較短的加熱時間達到安全。

設計關鍵：D 值與 F0 分佈

產品溶液可能「保護」微生物（如高黏度或高蛋白含量），導致需要更高的 F0。此外，滅菌鍋中不同位置的 F0 會有差異 — 加熱與冷卻階段都會累積 F0，但速率因位置而異。製程設計必須確保「最冷點」也達到要求。

Facility Impact of Terminal Sterilization

Terminal sterilization also has a significant impact on facility layout/space requirements, process sequence, material flow and equipment flow. Factors for consideration include:

  • Products are often loaded into specialized trays and racks to promote good temperature uniformity, drainage during the cycle, and post-sterilization drying. After completion, they should be transferred into alternate trays or totes for inspection and secondary processing.
  • Terminal sterilization of syringes requires inversion during processing to avoid condensate accumulation in the syringe barrel, and may require de-nesting/re-nesting equipment.
  • Material flow and facility arrangement should be designed to segregate non-sterile and sterile material, such as via pass-through type autoclaves and segregated staging/drying areas.
  • Automated loading and unloading systems can be employed, especially for large volume containers or IV bags. Space requirements can be considerable.
  • Following sterilization, space should be provided for material draining and drying. HVAC equipment should be configured to control latent and sensible heat loads.
  • Container inspection for particulate matter, cosmetic defects, and container integrity should be performed after terminal sterilization, since the process may impact these attributes.
  • Utility requirements for large scale terminal sterilization operations are significant and should be carefully considered during facility design.

設施設計影響

最終滅菌對設施佈局有深遠影響，不僅是放一台滅菌鍋那麼簡單。

關鍵設施考量

  • 非無菌/無菌物料隔離：使用貫穿式（pass-through）滅菌鍋，物料從非無菌側進、無菌側出，確保清潔區隔
  • 專用托盤與回流：滅菌用托盤需回收到加工區重複使用，形成設備逆向物流
  • 注射器特殊處理：滅菌時需倒置以避免冷凝水累積在針筒內
  • 乾燥與暫存空間：滅菌後的產品需要排水和乾燥區，HVAC 系統需處理蒸汽帶來的顯熱與潛熱負載
  • 公用設施負荷：大規模滅菌操作的蒸汽、冷卻水、壓縮空氣需求極為可觀

CDMO 實務觀點

對代工廠而言，最終滅菌產品的設施需求遠大於純無菌充填線。需要額外的暫存區、專用托盤庫存、乾燥間及大容量公用設施。在產能規劃時，滅菌鍋的周轉時間（含裝載、滅菌、冷卻、卸載）往往是產線瓶頸。

Air Over-Pressure and Container Integrity

During the sterilization of liquid-filled containers, differential pressures between the interior of the containers and the sterilization chamber may potentially impact container integrity. Air over-pressure (air ballasting) is used to minimize the pressure differential between the container and the sterilizer to protect the integrity of the container, especially pre-filled syringes and plastic containers.

The amount of internal overpressure in the container is proportional to the internal temperature and does not remain constant throughout the process. The overpressure component should therefore be adjusted during the course of the cycle.

Autoclave Types

Among the sterilization methods available, moist heat autoclaving is the most widely used. There are several autoclave designs:

  • Air steam mix autoclaves: Clean steam is injected directly into the chamber along with sterile filtered air for overpressure control. Suitable means (fans or air ejector nozzles) are employed for good temperature uniformity. Steam must be of compendial quality. Usually used for small volume materials; load is drier upon unloading.
  • Superheated water shower type units: Water is heated/cooled indirectly using external heat exchangers. A recirculating pumping system sprays water over the product load via nozzle arrays for uniform heating and cooling. Water used as heating medium should be of known quality (e.g., distilled or USP purified water). Usually used for larger volume materials, achieving faster heating/cooling rates and better temperature uniformity.

空氣加壓與容器完整性

液體充填容器在高溫滅菌時，內部壓力會隨溫度升高而增加。如果滅菌鍋腔體壓力低於容器內壓，容器可能膨脹破裂或密封失效。因此使用空氣加壓（Air Ballasting）來平衡壓差。

比喻：蒸爐 vs. 水浴鍋

空氣蒸汽混合型像蒸爐 — 蒸汽直接包圍產品，適合小東西但大物件受熱不均。過熱水淋浴型像水浴鍋 — 水的熱傳導效率高，適合大容量產品，但卸載後會比較濕。

3.2.8 Inspection / 檢查

Original Text — ISPE Baseline Guide

3.2.8 Inspection

Products intended for parenteral administration in humans are subjected to various regulatory and compendial requirements for visual inspection. USP <790> establishes the expectation that each finished unit is inspected to ensure absence of particulate matter, as well as absence of any container or seal defect that could compromise sterility.

Requirements for final post-manufacture inspection vary depending on the product itself (e.g., lyophilized, liquid, suspension) as well as the primary package type.

The inspection process typically includes:

  • Foreign matter in solution
  • Fill volume
  • Ampoule and BFS container integrity
  • Vial cap/crimp and stopper (presence, color, visible defects)
  • Black spots at the seal of the ampoule
  • Cake appearance (for lyophilized products)

Inspection Process Design

The design of an inspection process should include which inspections to perform and how/when to perform them. A documented product risk profile/assessment should be developed.

In general, inspection operations can be implemented as:

  • In-process test (statistical sampling at defined intervals) during production
  • 100% in-line inspection (semi-automated or automated)
  • Finished product inspection only, 100%
  • Finished product inspection only, AQL basis
教學解說 — 繁體中文

檢查概述

所有供人體注射的產品都必須通過目視檢查（Visual Inspection），以確保無微粒污染（Particulate Matter）且容器密封完整。USP <790> 是目視檢查的核心法規依據。

常見檢查項目

  • 異物/微粒：溶液中的外來物質
  • 充填量：確認符合標示裝量
  • 容器完整性：安瓿密封、BFS 容器完整
  • 瓶蓋/壓蓋：膠塞存在性、顏色、壓蓋品質
  • 安瓿封口黑點：熔封不良的指標
  • 凍乾餅外觀：是否有塌餅（meltback）

檢查策略選擇

檢查可以在不同階段、以不同方式實施：

  • 製程中抽樣：統計取樣（成本低但覆蓋率有限）
  • 100% 線上檢查：即時全檢但影響產線效率
  • 成品 100% 全檢：離線全檢，需額外空間和人力
  • 成品 AQL 抽樣：依可接受品質限度抽樣

CDMO 實務觀點：檢查是產能瓶頸

在代工廠中，檢查往往是整條產線最大的瓶頸。充填機可能每分鐘產出 300-600 瓶，但手動檢查只能處理數十瓶/分鐘。自動化檢查設備的投資報酬率在高產量產品上非常顯著，但對多品項小批量的 CDMO 而言，設備轉換（changeover）時間也是關鍵考量。

Factors for Inspection Process Design

Several factors should be considered:

  • Capital cost of inspection equipment
  • Space requirements for inspection equipment (in-line versus decoupled)
  • Impact of product investigations on in-process material flows and reinspection requirements
  • Staffing requirements for automated/high tech equipment, especially for off-shift operations
  • Decreased overall line efficiency for in-line inspections
  • Increased material handling requirements for decoupled inspections
  • Use of PAT to reduce/eliminate inspection for certain attributes (e.g., fill weight)

The specific product and package type dictates which inspections are required. For example, some liquid products are susceptible to bubble formation during filling and require an off-line "relaxation time" or use of a pre-spin system prior to particulate inspection.

For high value products, additional inspection steps and PAT may be justified based on decreased product reject rates. For low value products, finished product inspection(s) may be more cost effective.

Emerging Inspection Technologies

  • X-ray analysis of lyophilized cakes for particulate matter
  • Near Infrared (NIR) analysis of lyophilized cakes for moisture content
  • Headspace Analysis (HSA) for products sealed under vacuum or inert gas

設計因素與成本效益

線上 vs. 離線檢查的取捨

  • 線上（In-line）：減少物料搬運，但降低產線整體效率（OEE）
  • 離線（Decoupled）：不影響產線速度，但需要暫存空間（WIP）和額外搬運人力

選擇取決於產品價值、批量大小及設施空間限制。

PAT（製程分析技術）可以即時監控某些屬性（如充填重量），減少或消除對這些項目的離線檢查需求。

新興檢查技術

  • X 射線分析：檢測凍乾餅中的不可見微粒
  • 近紅外光（NIR）：非破壞性測量凍乾餅水分含量
  • 頂空分析（HSA）：確認真空或惰性氣體密封的容器密封完整性（CCI）

這些技術需逐案評估是否納入檢查流程。

比喻：氣泡 vs. 微粒

某些液體產品在充填後會產生氣泡，這些氣泡在自動檢查時會被誤判為微粒。就像剛倒好的汽水 — 需要等氣泡消散（relaxation time）或先旋轉（pre-spin）讓氣泡浮出，才能準確檢測真正的異物。

3.2.8.1 Visual Inspection / 目視檢查

3.2.8.1 Visual Inspection

Many inspection criteria for products are visual; for example, "essentially free of visible particulate matter" or criteria based on product appearance/color or lyophilized cake appearance. Therefore, establishing a manual inspection baseline and challenge sets is important for training/qualification of human inspectors and for adjustment/qualification of automated equipment.

Manual inspection capability is also required when investigating production anomalies or high reject rates from automated equipment. A manual inspection capability should be developed and maintained.

Defect Library

Establishing and maintaining a defect library is required for construction and maintenance of challenge sets. Considerations include:

  • The library should consist of actual defects as well as defect photos or videos. Clear, written descriptions of defect types should be provided.
  • The library should include examples of each type of defect, with a focus on critical defects. Where possible, examples should be obtained from actual production rejects.
  • The quality of defects degrades over time due to repeated handling, so the library should be continuously refreshed with new reject material. This is particularly important for lyophilized products.

Factors Influencing Inspection

  • Human capabilities
  • Process and environmental conditions
  • Defect type and characteristics (particle size, color, shape, movement)
  • Product characteristics and package type

The Knapp methodology classifies containers based on their Probability of Rejection (PR):

  • Reject: 0.7071 < PR < 1.0
  • Gray Zone: 0.30 < PR < 0.7071
  • Accept: 0 < PR < 0.30

The security of rejecting "must-reject" containers is the Reject Zone Efficiency (RZE). Manual and automatic inspection processes should be qualified to ensure adequate removal of reject containers (high RZE) and acceptably low false reject rates.

目視檢查方法

許多檢查標準本質上是視覺性的 — 如「基本上無可見微粒」或凍乾餅外觀。因此建立人工檢查基線（Manual Inspection Baseline）和挑戰組（Challenge Sets）極為重要。

缺陷庫（Defect Library）

缺陷庫是檢查系統的基礎設施：

  • 包含實際缺陷樣品、照片/影片及書面描述
  • 重點收集關鍵缺陷（Critical Defects）的範例
  • 樣品會因反覆操作而劣化，需持續更新 — 尤其凍乾產品的缺陷樣品更易變質

重點：RZE 與假拒絕率的平衡

檢查系統需同時滿足兩個目標：(1) 足夠高的 RZE 確保真正缺陷品被剔除；(2) 足夠低的假拒絕率避免浪費合格品。可透過二次檢查（reinspection of 1x rejects）策略來降低假拒絕率，前提是合併後的 RZE 仍然達標。

Knapp 方法學：容器分類

拒絕區效率（RZE）衡量的是：必須被剔除的缺陷容器，實際被成功剔除的比例。

3.2.8.2 Operator Qualification / 操作人員資格

3.2.8.2 Operator Qualification

Operator qualification and training programs are required for manual and semi-automated inspection processes. Considerations include:

  • Inspectors should be qualified using a manual test set having a known proportion of defect containers. RZE (or POD) and false reject rates are calculated against acceptance criteria.
  • For new inspectors, multiple successful inspections are required for qualification.
  • Operators should be requalified annually, at a minimum.
  • Qualified shift times should be established.

Operators should be tested initially and at regular intervals for near vision acuity and color perception.

Operator fatigue is an important factor. Environmental conditions (ambient light, background noise, activity) and ergonomics should be optimized. Operators performing inspection should take routine breaks (e.g., once per hour) to reduce risk of eye fatigue.

檢查員資格與訓練

檢查員資格要求

  • 初始資格：使用已知缺陷比例的測試組進行多次成功檢查
  • 年度再確認：至少每年一次重新驗證
  • 視力測試：近視力敏銳度 + 色彩辨識能力（初始及定期）
  • 合格班次時間：建立每人每日可執行檢查的最長時間

疲勞管理

目視檢查極度依賴眼力，操作員疲勞是品質風險的主要來源。ISPE 建議：

  • 每小時強制休息一次
  • 優化環境照明、背景噪音及人體工學
  • 建立輪班機制，避免長時間連續檢查

比喻：航空管制員的輪班制度

目視檢查員的疲勞管理類似航空管制員 — 高度專注的工作不能連續太久。管制員每工作 1.5-2 小時就必須休息，目視檢查員同理。忽視疲勞管理等於接受檢查品質隨時間下降的風險。

3.2.8.3 Visual Inspection Methods and Equipment / 目視檢查方法與設備

Manual Visual Inspection (MVI)

Sealed, filled containers are inspected by qualified inspectors under controlled conditions. Critical Process Parameters (CPPs) for MVI include:

  • Light wavelength and intensity
  • Background and contrast
  • Inspection rate (pace)
  • Container handling and movement

Magnification can be employed; however, it can increase eye strain and may not improve overall detection rates. It is therefore not recommended for routine inspection.

Throughputs for MVI are low compared to semi-automated or automated equipment. For this reason, MVI is generally performed off-line.

Visual Inspection with Semi-Automatic Machines

A semi-automatic inspection machine transports and rotates the product while being inspected by an operator. Adjustable factors include: light, angle of product, angle of mirrors, background, and container rotation speed.

During inspection, the operator marks containers to be rejected; these are discarded automatically by the machine.

Throughput rates are higher than for manual inspection, but are often overestimated in the process design stage. Semi-automated systems can be designed for sequential or parallel inspection to increase throughput.

手動目視檢查 (MVI)

MVI 是最基礎的檢查方式：操作員在受控條件下逐瓶檢視。關鍵製程參數（CPP）包括光源波長/強度、背景對比、檢查速率及容器操作方式。

MVI 限制

  • 產出速度最低，通常離線進行
  • 放大鏡可用但不建議例行使用 — 增加眼睛疲勞且不一定提升檢出率
  • 仍是異常調查和自動設備高拒絕率時的必要能力

半自動檢查機

機器負責傳送與旋轉產品，操作員負責判斷。可調整光源、產品角度、鏡面角度、背景及旋轉速度來強化缺陷可見性。

比喻：壽司迴轉台 + 品管員

半自動檢查機就像壽司迴轉台 — 產品自動旋轉送到眼前，檢查員只需專心看（不用拿取和放回），大幅提升效率。但設計階段常高估實際產出速率，應以「最差情況產出率」進行產能分析。

Fully Automatic Inspection Machines

A fully automatic inspection machine consists of an automated material handling/transport system, outfitted with one or more camera stations and/or other instruments designed to inspect product for various defects.

Automated inspection systems may not be suitable for some container types, such as flexible containers. In these cases, a mix of automated, semi-automated, and manual operations may be employed.

Automated equipment is capable of high output rates and is suitable for in-line or off-line (decoupled) installations. In-line installations reduce material handling but impact line efficiencies. For off-line systems, material handling and WIP storage should be considered.

Examples of inspection capabilities include:

  • Particulates in solution
  • Fill level
  • Flip seal presence and color
  • Ampoule banding color
  • Crimp quality (absence of dents/wrinkles)
  • Cracks in tip, neck, shoulder, sidewall, or heel
  • Plunger placement (syringes and cartridges)
  • Needle shield presence
  • Lyophilized cake appearance

Improvements in image capture, processing rates, vision tools, and lighting systems allow capture and analysis of multiple images of each container in real time. Machines can capture and store large numbers of images; these video libraries are helpful during investigations and machine setup. Using video libraries, virtual inspection of container sets is possible, optimizing machine parameters while avoiding degradation of sample sets.

Inspection systems may have unique requirements impacting facility design, such as control of ambient lighting conditions or shielding for X-ray systems.

全自動檢查機

全自動系統使用自動化物料傳輸搭配一個或多個相機站及其他檢測儀器（如 X 射線、雷射頂空分析）。

自動化檢查能力一覽

  • 溶液中微粒
  • 充填液位
  • 翻蓋存在性與顏色
  • 安瓿環帶顏色
  • 壓蓋品質（無凹痕/皺褶）
  • 玻璃裂紋（瓶尖、頸、肩、壁、底）
  • 柱塞位置（注射器/卡匣）
  • 針頭護套存在性
  • 凍乾餅外觀

影像庫的價值

現代自動檢查機可擷取並儲存大量影像，形成「影像庫」。用途包括：

  • 調查異常拒絕率時回溯分析
  • 設備調機時使用影像進行「虛擬檢查」，避免反覆操作實體樣品導致劣化
  • 優化機器參數而不消耗挑戰組樣品

思考題：選擇檢查策略

3.2.9 Secondary Packaging / 二次包裝

Original Text — ISPE Baseline Guide

3.2.9 Secondary Packaging

The same general principles used for packaging of non-sterile products apply to sterile products. For further information, refer to the ISPE Good Practice Guide: Packaging, Labeling and Warehousing Facilities.

Note: Many regulatory markets are in the process of, or are considering, implementing product serialization requirements as an anti-counterfeiting measure. This involves application of a unique identifier code to the product (unit of use) during packaging that can be checked and verified throughout the distribution channel. Appropriate expertise should be sought during line design to accommodate future equipment and technology requirements in this area.

教學解說 — 繁體中文

二次包裝

無菌產品的二次包裝原則與一般產品相同，包括標示（Labeling）、裝盒、裝箱等。ISPE 另有專門的包裝、標示及倉儲設施指南可參考。

序列化（Serialization）趨勢

全球多個法規市場正在推動或已實施產品序列化要求：

  • 目的：防偽 — 每個單位產品都有唯一識別碼
  • 技術：在包裝過程中貼附唯一編碼（如 2D barcode），可在整個配送鏈中查驗
  • 設施影響：需要在包裝線上預留空間和介面，供序列化設備（印碼機、相機驗證系統、資料管理系統）使用

設計前瞻性

即使目前市場尚未強制要求序列化，新建或改建包裝線時應預留空間和公用設施介面，以便未來加裝序列化設備。改裝成本遠高於預留空間的成本。

CDMO 實務：序列化的挑戰

對 CDMO 而言，序列化帶來額外複雜度：不同客戶可能需要不同的序列化格式和標準（EU FMD、US DSCSA、中國追溯碼等）。包裝線需要靈活的軟體配置能力和快速切換能力。這是選擇包裝設備供應商時的關鍵評估項目。
