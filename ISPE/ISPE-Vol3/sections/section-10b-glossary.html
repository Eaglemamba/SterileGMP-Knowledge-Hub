13.1 Acronyms and Abbreviations

縮寫總覽與分類導讀

HVAC 與空氣處理相關術語

  • ACH / ACR — 換氣次數/換氣率：衡量潔淨室空氣品質的核心指標
  • AHU — 空調箱：潔淨室空氣處理的核心設備
  • ASHRAE — 美國暖通空調工程師學會：HVAC 設計標準的主要來源
  • HEPA — 高效微粒空氣過濾器：潔淨室的關鍵過濾元件
  • HVAC — 暖通空調系統：設施設計的核心系統
  • MERV — 最低效率報告值：過濾器效率分級
  • UAF / UFH — 單向氣流/單向氣流罩（層流罩）
  • ULPA — 超低穿透空氣過濾器
  • VAV — 可變風量系統

屏障系統與隔離技術

  • RABS — 限制進入屏障系統：開放式設計，依賴氣流溢出保護
  • RTP — 快速轉移埠：隔離器進出料的關鍵介面
  • VHP — 氣相過氧化氫：隔離器去污的首選方法
  • BFS — 吹灌封：先進無菌製程技術

品質與法規相關

  • cGMP — 現行良好生產規範
  • CPP / CQA — 關鍵製程參數/關鍵品質屬性
  • QbD — 品質源於設計：現代藥品開發核心理念
  • ICH — 國際協和會議：全球藥品法規協調機構
  • PIC/S — 藥品稽查合作組織
  • EM / EMS — 環境監測/環境監測系統
  • CFU — 菌落形成單位：微生物計數的標準單位

製程與公用設備

  • CIP / SIP — 就地清洗/就地滅菌
  • WFI — 注射用水：無菌製造最高等級的水
  • TOC — 總有機碳：水質監測關鍵參數
  • PAT — 製程分析技術
  • FIT — 過濾器完整性測試
  • CCIT — 容器密封完整性測試

易混淆縮寫提醒

  • DOP vs. DEHS vs. PAO — 三者都是 HEPA 過濾器完整性測試用的氣溶膠介質。DOP（鄰苯二甲酸二辛酯）因健康疑慮已被 DEHS 或 PAO 取代，但業界仍慣稱「DOP 測試」
  • DP — 壓差（Differential Pressure），非「藥品」(Drug Product)。在設施設計文件中，DP 幾乎都指壓差
  • RSR — 房間側可更換（HEPA 過濾器安裝方式），非 RSF（殘餘密封力）
  • CNC vs. UC — CNC（受控但未分級）有過濾通風，UC（未分級）為一般支援區域

13.2 Definitions: A – B

Acceptance Criteria

Measurable terms under which a test result will be considered acceptable.

Action Level

A requirement or condition set by the user, which, when exceeded, requires immediate intervention, including the investigation of cause and corrective action.

Air Change Rate (ACR)

The number of times the total air volume of a defined space is replaced in a given unit of time. This is computed by dividing the total volume of the subject space (in cubic feet) into the total volume of air exhausted from (or supplied to) the space per unit of time.

Airlock

Intermediate room or area that is normally ventilated and used to minimize the transfer of airborne contamination from one area to another. A room or space designed to act as a means of transfer between areas of different air classification or quality.

Alert Point

Used in determining when a parameter is drifting toward the extremes of the operating range.

Ampoule

A heat sealed all glass or all plastic container for sterile, injectable pharmaceutical products.

As-Built (ISO 14644-1)

Condition where the cleanroom or clean zone is complete with all services connected and functioning, but with no production equipment, materials or personnel present.

Aseptic

Free from disease-producing microorganisms. (PDA TR 22)

Aseptic Core

(See: Aseptic Processing Area)

Aseptic Process Simulation

(See: Media Fill)

Aseptic Processing (PDA TR 22)

Handling sterile materials in a controlled environment, in which the air supply, materials, equipment, and personnel are regulated to control microbial and particulate contamination to acceptable levels.

Aseptic Processing Area

Area in which the product is formulated, filled into containers, and sealed. Also known as the aseptic core.

At Rest (ISO 14644-1)

Condition where the cleanroom or clean zone is complete with equipment installed and operating in a manner agreed upon, but with no personnel present.

Autoclave

An apparatus into which moist heat (steam) under pressure is introduced to sterilize or decontaminate materials placed within (e.g., filter assemblies, glassware, etc.).

Automated System

Any facility system or piece of equipment that is PLC or computer controlled.

Background Environment

The environment that surrounds a critical area.

Barrier System

A system of physical partitions that affords ISO 5/Grade A protection by partially separating its interior from the surrounding environment utilizing airflow.

Bidirectional (Traffic)

Traffic pattern such that materials and personnel may enter and leave through an area.

Bioburden

The concentration of microbial matter per unit volume. Microbial matter includes viruses, bacteria, yeast, mold, and parts thereof.

定義 A – B：中文解說

潔淨室狀態三部曲 (ISO 14644-1)

理解潔淨室的三種測試狀態是設施確效的基礎：

  • As-Built（竣工態） — 設施完工、服務已連接且運作，但無生產設備、物料或人員。這是驗收建築工程時的測試狀態
  • At Rest（靜態） — 設備已安裝並運轉，但無操作人員。用於確認 HVAC 系統能力
  • In Operation（動態） — 正常生產狀態，人員在場操作。這是日常環境監測的測試條件

CDMO 角度：客戶稽核常要求提供這三種狀態的粒子數據，必須清楚區分並保存完整記錄。

無菌核心區相關術語群

  • 無菌（Aseptic） — 不含致病微生物
  • 無菌製程（Aseptic Processing） — 在受控環境中處理無菌物料，控制空氣、物料、設備與人員以維持可接受的微生物和微粒汙染水準
  • 無菌核心區（Aseptic Processing Area / Aseptic Core） — 產品調配、充填、封裝的區域
  • 無菌製程模擬（Aseptic Process Simulation） — 即培養基模擬充填（Media Fill），用培養基取代產品以評估製程無菌保證能力

Alert Point vs. Action Level — 常見混淆

  • Alert Point（警戒點） — 參數正在偏向操作範圍的極端值。觸發時需要關注和趨勢分析，但不一定要立即停線
  • Action Level（行動限度） — 超過時必須立即介入，包括調查原因和採取矯正措施

實務提醒：環境監測計畫中，Alert 和 Action 限度的設定依據必須有數據支持（通常基於歷史趨勢數據），不能隨意訂定。

屏障系統 (Barrier System)

屏障系統是利用物理隔板提供 ISO 5/Grade A 保護的系統，透過氣流將其內部與周圍環境部分隔離。

關鍵字：「部分隔離」(partially separating) — 這是 RABS 與隔離器 (Isolator) 的根本區別。RABS 依賴氣流溢出 (air overspill)，而非完全密封。

其他重要 A-B 術語

  • 接受標準（Acceptance Criteria） — 判定測試結果是否可接受的可量測條件
  • 安瓿（Ampoule） — 全玻璃或全塑膠的熱封容器，用於無菌注射藥品
  • 高壓滅菌釜（Autoclave） — 以濕熱（蒸汽）加壓滅菌的設備
  • 背景環境（Background Environment） — 圍繞關鍵區的環境（如 ISO 7/Grade B 區圍繞 ISO 5/Grade A 區）
  • 生物負荷（Bioburden） — 每單位體積的微生物濃度，包括病毒、細菌、酵母菌、黴菌及其碎片
  • 雙向動線（Bidirectional Traffic） — 人員和物料可以從同一區域進出的動線模式

Definitions: C

Calibration

A comparison of a measurement standard or instrument of known accuracy to detect, correlate, report, or eliminate by adjustment, any variation in the accuracy of the unknown standard or instrument.

Campaigning

(See: Temporal Separation)

Classified Space

An area with airborne viable and non-viable particle contamination controlled within preset limits. A cleanroom designated by ISO Standard 14644-1 volume units ("in operation") or European Community (EC) Grades A, B, C, D ("at rest" and "in operation"). For pharmaceutical manufacture, a classified space implies ongoing environmental monitoring.

Clean Area

An area where particulate and microbial levels are specified (e.g., a filling room – ISO 7/Grade B).

Cleaning

Action of removing and dissolving or dispersing soiling from a surface. Cleaning may be effected by one or more of the following means: physico-chemical (detergent action); chemical (e.g., sodium hydroxide); biochemical (e.g., enzymes); physical (e.g., shear forces caused by brushing or hosing). Cleaning efficiency is also dependent on the length of time and temperature of application.

Cleanroom (ISO 14644-1)

Room in which the concentration of airborne particles is controlled and which is constructed and used in a manner to minimize the introduction, generation, and retention of particles inside the room, and in which other relevant parameters, e.g., temperature, humidity, and pressure, are controlled as necessary.

Closed Process

A process condition when the product, materials, critical components or container/closure surfaces are contained and separated from the immediate process environment within a closed/sealed process equipment or enclosure.

Colony Forming Unit (CFU)

A measure of the number of bacteria present in the environment or on the surfaces of an aseptic processing room; measured as part of qualification and ongoing monitoring.

Commissioning

The documented process, verifying that equipment and systems are installed according to specifications, placing the equipment and systems into active service, and verifying its proper operation.

Compendial

Official; purported to comply with USP, EP, or JP.

Compounding

The bringing together into a homogenous mix of active ingredients, excipient, and solvent components.

Concurrent Processing

Two or more products being processed at the same time.

Contamination

The undesired introduction of impurities of a chemical or microbiological nature, or of foreign matter, into or onto a raw material, intermediate, or API during production, sampling, packaging or repackaging, storage or transport.

Controlled Not Classified (CNC)

An area without airborne particle limits, but with filtered ventilation.

Critical Area (FDA 2004)

An area designed to maintain sterility of sterile materials. Sterilized product, containers, closures, and equipment may be exposed in critical areas. Also known as critical zone.

Critical Process Parameter (CPP)

A process parameter whose variability has an impact on a critical quality attribute and therefore should be monitored or controlled to ensure the process produces desired quality.

Critical Process Step

For sterile products, this normally is an activity where product or product contact parts are exposed to the surrounding environment.

Critical Quality Attribute (CQA)

A physical, chemical, biological, or microbiological property or characteristic that should be within an appropriate limit, range, or distribution to ensure product quality.

Cross-Contamination

Contamination of a starting material, an intermediate product, or a finished product with another starting material or product during production.

定義 C：中文解說

空間分級系統 — 最重要的設施設計基礎

  • Classified Space（分級空間） — 空氣中活性和非活性粒子汙染控制在預設限度內的區域。ISO 14644-1 標準或 EU GMP Annex 1 Grade A/B/C/D。重點：分級空間意味著必須持續進行環境監測
  • Clean Area（潔淨區） — 指定了微粒和微生物水準的區域（如充填室 ISO 7/Grade B）
  • Cleanroom（潔淨室） — ISO 14644-1 定義：控制空氣中微粒濃度的房間，其建造和使用方式可最大限度減少微粒的引入、產生和滯留
  • CNC — 受控但未分級 — 沒有空氣微粒限度但有過濾通風。常見於更衣室外側、走廊等過渡區域

Open Process vs. Closed Process — 關鍵區別

封閉製程（Closed Process）：產品、物料或關鍵組件被密封在封閉設備中，與環境隔離。封閉製程可以在較低等級的環境中進行。

對比：開放製程（Open Process）在後續段落中定義 — 產品暴露於環境中，必須在相應的高等級環境下操作。

CDMO 營運影響：能夠論證製程為「封閉」是降低設施分級要求（從而降低營運成本）的重要策略。

品質設計三角：CPP、CQA、Critical Process Step

  • CQA（關鍵品質屬性） — 確保產品品質所需的物理、化學、生物或微生物特性
  • CPP（關鍵製程參數） — 其變異會影響 CQA 的製程參數，需要監控或控制
  • Critical Process Step（關鍵製程步驟） — 對無菌產品而言，通常是產品或產品接觸面暴露於周圍環境的步驟

三者的邏輯鏈：識別 CQA → 確定影響 CQA 的 CPP → 找出涉及暴露的 Critical Process Step → 設計相應的設施保護措施。

其他重要 C 術語

  • 校正（Calibration） — 將已知精度的標準與待測儀器比較，消除精度偏差。校正是確效的基礎工作
  • 試運轉（Commissioning） — 設備和系統安裝後的文件化驗證過程。注意：Commissioning 不等同於 Qualification，但兩者密切相關（參見 ISPE Vol.5）
  • 調配（Compounding） — 將活性成分、賦形劑和溶劑混合成均質藥液
  • 藥典（Compendial） — 符合 USP（美國藥典）、EP（歐洲藥典）或 JP（日本藥典）的官方標準
  • 交叉汙染（Cross-Contamination） — 不同原料或產品之間的相互汙染。CDMO 多產品設施必須特別關注
  • 同時生產（Concurrent Processing） — 同一時間處理兩種以上產品。需要嚴格的隔離和防交叉汙染措施

Definitions: D – F

Decontamination (FDA 2004)

A process that eliminates viable bioburden via use of sporicidal chemical agents.

Depyrogenation

Removal or destruction of endotoxins. (See: Dry Heat Sterilization)

Design Limit

The specified range, or accuracy of a controlled variable, used by the designer to determine the performance requirements of an engineered system.

Differential Pressure (DP)

Between adjoining rooms or zones, measured in Pascals (Pa) (1" wg = 254 Pa).

Disinfection

Removal, destruction, or deactivation of microorganisms on objects or surfaces. (See: Sanitization)

Disposables

Pre-sterilized products, equipment, and packaging designed to be used once (single-use) or a few times, depending on specific circumstances, and discarded.

Documented

The parameter value (or evidence that the value is within control limits) is recorded at some predefined frequency for future reference.

Dry Heat Sterilization

Sterilization utilizing a heating oven or continuous tunnel (gas or electric heated) as opposed to steam sterilization in an autoclave usually used for glassware and metal parts. In depyrogenation, temperatures of 250°C (482°F) result in sterilization and the inactivation of endotoxin present on the surface of the equipment.

Endotoxin

Cell wall debris (lipopolysaccharide) from Gram-negative bacteria. (See: Pyrogen)

Environmental Process Limits

Environmental limits that, if exceeded, may affect product quality adversely.

Excipient

An inactive ingredient used in the formulation of a drug product.

Extractables

Chemical substances that can be removed from polymeric materials using appropriate solvents (e.g., polar and non-polar).

Flow

Architectural terms for material or personnel traffic pattern in the facility.

Formulation

1. (noun) The chemical and physical composition of a drug product. 2. (verb) The act of compounding a drug product.

Functionality

Suitability for the intended purpose.

定義 D – F：中文解說

Disinfection vs. Sanitization vs. Decontamination — 三者有何不同？

  • 消毒（Disinfection） — 去除、破壞或滅活物體或表面上的微生物。較廣泛的概念
  • 清潔消毒（Sanitization） — 去汙的一部分，將活微生物降至已定義的可接受水準。通常使用化學劑或熱
  • 去汙（Decontamination） — 使用殺孢子劑消除活性生物負荷。最為徹底，適用於隔離器等高要求場景

層級關係：Decontamination（最嚴格）> Disinfection > Sanitization。選擇何種方法取決於目標微生物和環境分級。

滅菌與去熱原相關術語

  • 去熱原（Depyrogenation） — 去除或破壞內毒素。通常需要 250°C 乾熱處理
  • 乾熱滅菌（Dry Heat Sterilization） — 使用加熱烤箱或連續隧道進行滅菌，適用於玻璃器皿和金屬部件。250°C 可同時達到滅菌和去熱原的效果
  • 內毒素（Endotoxin） — 革蘭氏陰性菌的細胞壁碎片（脂多醣），可引起發熱反應
  • 致熱原（Pyrogen） — 能引起體溫升高的物質，通常指細菌內毒素

壓差 (DP) 與設計限度

  • 壓差（Differential Pressure） — 相鄰房間或區域之間的壓力差，以帕斯卡 (Pa) 計量。1 英寸水柱 = 254 Pa。壓差梯度是維持潔淨室分級的關鍵手段
  • 設計限度（Design Limit） — 設計師用於確定工程系統性能要求的受控變量範圍或精度
  • 環境製程限度（Environmental Process Limits） — 超過時可能對產品品質產生不良影響的環境限度

其他 D-F 術語

  • 拋棄式耗材（Disposables） — 預滅菌的一次性產品、設備和包材。即 Single-Use Systems（SUS），是現代 CDMO 降低交叉汙染風險的重要工具
  • 可萃取物（Extractables） — 使用溶劑可從高分子材料中提取的化學物質。與 Leachables（溶出物）構成 E&L 研究的兩個面向
  • 賦形劑（Excipient） — 藥品配方中的非活性成分
  • 動線（Flow） — 設施中物料或人員的交通動線模式，是建築設計用語
  • 配方（Formulation） — 藥品的化學和物理組成（名詞）；或調配藥品的行為（動詞）

Definitions: G – L

Gowning

Protective garments and the act of donning protective garments.

High Efficiency Particulate Air (HEPA) Filter

A filter with an efficiency in excess of 99.97% for 0.3 µm particles.

Humidifier

A device for adding moisture to room air.

Hydrophilic

Having a strong affinity for water; attracting, dissolving in, or absorbing water; readily absorbing moisture; having strong polar groups that readily interact with water. Its opposite is hydrophobic.

Hysteresis

The difference between the readings obtained when a given value of the measured variable is approached from opposite directions.

In Operation (or In-Use)

Room condition in which processing is being performed with operators present.

In-Process Control (IPC)

Checks performed during production to monitor and, if appropriate, to adjust the process to ensure that the intermediate or API (drug substance) conforms to its specifications and/or other defined quality criteria.

Isolator

A decontaminated unit meeting ISO 5/Grade A conditions that provides uncompromised, continuous, isolation of its interior from the surrounding environment. Isolators can be "open" or "closed".

Leachables

Typically, a subset of extractables; chemical substances that migrate into the drug product from process equipment or its container under normal conditions of use and/or storage.

Local Protection

Measures, such as hoods providing HEPA filtered air or other appropriate devices, procedures, or equipment design features, to protect the product from potential environmental contaminants.

Lyophilizer

A freeze dryer.

Lyophilization

The creation of a solid from a liquid by means of freezing, sublimation, and desorption.

定義 G – L：中文解說

隔離器 (Isolator) vs. RABS vs. 局部保護 (Local Protection)

這三個術語代表無菌保護的三種不同程度：

  • 隔離器（Isolator） — 經去汙的 ISO 5/Grade A 單元，提供「不妥協的、連續的」隔離。可以是開放式或封閉式。隔離器是最高等級的屏障技術
  • RABS（限制進入屏障系統） — 提供封閉但非密閉的環境，利用剛性壁和氣流溢出隔離內部與外部。保護程度次於隔離器
  • 局部保護（Local Protection） — HEPA 過濾空氣罩或其他適當裝置，保護產品免受環境汙染物影響。最基本的保護手段

保護等級：Isolator > RABS > Local Protection (LP/GAAS)。

CDMO 決策：隔離器前期投資高但可降低背景環境要求（ISO 7 或 ISO 8 即可），RABS 仍需 ISO 7/Grade B 背景。選擇需考量產品類型、批量和客戶要求。

HEPA 過濾器 — 潔淨室的守門員

高效微粒空氣（HEPA）過濾器：對 0.3 µm 粒子的過濾效率超過 99.97%。

關鍵知識點：

  • 0.3 µm 是 HEPA 過濾器效率最低的粒子尺寸（Most Penetrating Particle Size, MPPS）
  • 對比 ULPA：超低穿透空氣過濾器效率更高（99.999% 對 0.12 µm），用於特殊高要求場景
  • RSR（Room Side Replaceable）安裝方式允許從潔淨室側更換過濾器，避免技術夾層汙染風險

Extractables vs. Leachables — E&L 研究核心區別

  • 可萃取物（Extractables） — 使用強溶劑從高分子材料中萃取的化學物質。代表「最壞情況」的潛在遷移物質
  • 溶出物（Leachables） — Extractables 的子集。在正常使用/儲存條件下，從設備或容器遷移至藥品中的物質

關係：Extractables 研究先行（篩查），Leachables 研究隨後（實際條件驗證）。CDMO 必須為每個客戶產品進行 E&L 評估。

其他 G-L 術語

  • 更衣（Gowning） — 防護服裝及穿著防護服裝的動作。更衣程序是人員汙染控制的核心環節
  • 親水性（Hydrophilic） — 對水有強親和力。其反義詞為疏水性（Hydrophobic）。影響過濾器選擇和材料相容性
  • 遲滯（Hysteresis） — 從相反方向接近某測量值時讀數的差異。校正時需要考量的儀器特性
  • 冷凍乾燥機（Lyophilizer） — 即凍乾機。凍乾（Lyophilization）是將液體通過冷凍、昇華和解吸附轉化為固體的過程
  • 製程中控制（IPC） — 生產期間進行的檢查，監控並適時調整製程以確保產品符合規格

Definitions: M – O

Maintainability

The ease with which maintenance can be performed.

Media Fill

Method used to evaluate the efficacy of an aseptic process by substituting microbiological media for the normally processed product. Also known as Process Simulation Test or Aseptic Process Simulation.

Medical Device

A diagnostic or therapeutic article that does not achieve any of its principal intended purpose through chemical action within or on the body. Such devices include diagnostic test kits, crutches, electrodes, pacemakers, arterial grafts, intraocular lenses, and orthopedic pins or other orthopedic equipment.

Monitoring (ISO 14644-2)

Observations made by measurement in accordance with a defined method and plan to provide evidence of the performance of an installation. This information may be used to detect trends in operational state and to provide process support.

Non-Viable

Opposite of viable, not alive.

Normal Operating Condition

Values of a parameter that are normally observed while a process is operating. The normal operating condition should be within the alert and action limits.

Open Process

A process condition when the product, materials, or container/closure surfaces are exposed to the immediate process environment at a stage/time when such exposure could influence the quality or purity of the product.

Operational ("In Operation") (ISO 14644-1)

Agreed condition where the cleanroom or clean zone is functioning in the specified manner, with equipment operating and with the specified number of personnel present.

Ozone Depleting Substance (ODS)

Chemical substance, usually consisting of some combination of chlorine, fluorine, or bromine plus carbon, such as chlorofluorocarbons (CFC) and hydrochlorofluorocarbons (HCFC).

Overseal

Capping and crimping.

定義 M – O：中文解說

培養基充填試驗 (Media Fill) — CDMO 的核心能力指標

培養基充填（Media Fill）是使用微生物培養基替代產品來評估無菌製程效能的方法，又稱製程模擬試驗（Process Simulation Test）或無菌製程模擬（Aseptic Process Simulation）。

為何 CDMO 必須精通此術語：

  • Media Fill 是證明無菌保證能力的法規要求
  • 客戶稽核幾乎必問 Media Fill 的頻率、批量、合格標準
  • EU GMP Annex 1 (2022) 對 Media Fill 有更嚴格的要求
  • 詳細內容可參考 PDA TR22（本知識庫已收錄）

Open Process vs. Closed Process — 對照理解

  • 開放製程（Open Process） — 產品、物料或容器/密封表面暴露於周圍環境中，且此暴露可能影響產品品質或純度
  • 封閉製程（Closed Process） — 前面已定義：產品在封閉設備中與環境隔離

設施設計影響：開放製程步驟的環境等級要求通常比封閉製程高 1-2 個等級。例如，開放充填需要 ISO 5/Grade A，而封閉系統中的調配可在較低等級下進行。

監測 (Monitoring) — ISO 14644-2 的精確定義

監測是按照既定方法和計畫進行的測量觀察，以提供設施性能的證據。

關鍵要素：

  • 必須有「定義的方法和計畫」（defined method and plan）
  • 目的是提供「安裝性能的證據」
  • 可用於檢測操作狀態的趨勢
  • 與「驗證」(Verification) 和「確效」(Validation) 有不同的法規含義

其他 M-O 術語

  • 可維護性（Maintainability） — 執行維護的容易程度。設施設計應考量：設備是否易於拆卸清潔？HEPA 是否易於更換？
  • 正常操作條件（Normal Operating Condition） — 製程運作時通常觀察到的參數值，應在 Alert 和 Action 限度內
  • 非活性的（Non-Viable） — 非活的，Viable 的反義詞。環境監測分為 viable（活性微粒）和 non-viable（非活性微粒）兩類
  • 加蓋壓鋁蓋（Overseal） — 即 capping and crimping，西林瓶封裝的最後步驟
  • 臭氧消耗物質（ODS） — 含氯氟碳或溴碳的化學物質，環保法規限制使用

Definitions: P – Q

Particle Count

Airborne particle count of both viable (living) organisms and non-viable (inert) particles. Measured in particles per cubic foot (multiply by ~35.3 to obtain particles per cubic meter).

Particulate

Usually a solid particle large enough to be removed by filtration.

Parison

The hollow melted plastic tube extruded from the die head of a blow molding machine. The parison is expanded within the mold by air pressure to form a container.

Permeability

The ability of a body to pass a fluid under pressure.

Physical Separation

The separation of materials, spaces, or operations by means of physical barriers to prevent their mixing or overlap. Also known as Spatial Separation.

Potent

A substance that is active in relatively low doses or concentrations.

Pre-filter (HVAC)

Air filter placed ahead of a more efficient air filter to reduce the loading and extend the life of the higher efficiency filter.

Procedural Separation

The separation of materials, spaces, or operations by means of operational controls to prevent their mixing or overlap.

Process Contact Surface (ASME BPE)

A surface under design operation conditions that is in contact with, or has the potential to be in contact with, raw materials, in-process materials, APIs, clean utilities (e.g., WFI, CIP, pure steam, process gases), or components (e.g., stoppers) and where there is a potential for the surface to affect product safety, quality, identity, strength, or purity.

Process Gases

Gases which can affect product quality.

Process Support Systems

Systems that do not contact product; they are generally engineering systems.

Process Systems

Systems that may contact the drug substance or could otherwise directly impact product quality.

Process Validation

A documented program that provides a high degree of assurance that a specific process will consistently produce a result meeting pre-determined acceptance criteria.

Product Contact Surface

A process contact surface that is in contact with, or has the potential to be in contact with, a product where the product is defined by owner/user.

Pure Steam (USP)

Water that has been heated above 100°C (212°F) and vaporized in a manner that prevents source water entrainment. It contains no added substance. The level of steam saturation or dryness, and the amount of non-condensable gases are to be determined by the pure steam application.

Purified Water

Water for the preparation of medicinal products, other than those that require the use of water which is sterile and/or apyrogenic. Prepared by distillation, by ion exchange, or by any other suitable method.

Pyrogen

An agent capable of inducing an increase in body temperature; usually refers to fever caused by bacterial endotoxins.

Q8, Q9, Q10

ICH guidance documents dealing with pharmaceutical development, quality risk management, and pharmaceutical quality systems, respectively.

Qualification

Action of proving and documenting that equipment or ancillary systems are properly installed, work correctly, and actually lead to the expected results. Qualification is part of validation, but the individual qualification steps alone do not constitute process validation.

Quality Assurance (QA)

The activity of, or group responsible for, ensuring that the facility and systems meet GMP requirements.

Quality by Design (QbD)

A systematic approach to development that begins with predefined objectives and emphasizes product and process understanding based on sound science and Quality Risk Management.

Quality Risk Management

A systematic process for the assessment, control, communication, and review of risks to the quality of the drug (medicinal) product across the product lifecycle.

定義 P – Q：中文解說

隔離策略：Physical vs. Procedural vs. Temporal Separation

ISPE Vol.3 定義了三種防止交叉汙染的隔離策略：

  • 物理隔離（Physical/Spatial Separation） — 通過物理屏障隔離物料、空間或操作，防止混合或重疊。最可靠的方法
  • 程序隔離（Procedural Separation） — 通過操作控制手段隔離物料、空間或操作。依賴 SOP 執行
  • 時間隔離（Temporal Separation = Campaigning） — 確保兩種物料不在同一時間存在於同一空間

可靠性排序：Physical > Temporal > Procedural。CDMO 多產品設施通常結合三種策略使用。

接觸面分類 — Process Contact vs. Product Contact

  • 製程接觸面（Process Contact Surface） — ASME BPE 定義：在設計操作條件下，與原料、中間品、API、潔淨公用設備（如 WFI、CIP、純蒸汽、製程氣體）或組件（如膠塞）接觸或可能接觸的表面
  • 產品接觸面（Product Contact Surface） — Process Contact Surface 的子集，與「產品」（由業主/使用者定義）接觸或可能接觸的表面

區分意義：產品接觸面有更嚴格的材質、表面粗糙度和清潔驗證要求。

水系統層級 — Pure Steam, WFI, Purified Water

  • 純蒸汽（Pure Steam） — 水加熱至 100°C 以上並以防止源水夾帶的方式汽化。用於蒸汽與產品或製備物接觸的場合
  • 注射用水（WFI） — 通過蒸餾或反滲透純化，符合細菌內毒素限度（<0.25 EU/ml）。無菌製造的最高水質要求
  • 純化水（Purified Water） — 用於製備藥品（不包括需要無菌和/或無熱原水的藥品）

品質等級：Pure Steam > WFI > Purified Water。詳見 ISPE Baseline Vol.4 Water & Steam Systems。

品質體系 — ICH Q8/Q9/Q10 與 QbD

  • Q8 — 藥品開發（Pharmaceutical Development）
  • Q9 — 品質風險管理（Quality Risk Management）
  • Q10 — 藥品品質體系（Pharmaceutical Quality Systems）
  • QbD（品質源於設計） — 從預定義目標開始，強調基於科學和風險管理的產品與製程理解
  • 確效（Qualification） — 證明設備正確安裝、正常運作且產生預期結果的文件化行為。注意：Qualification 是 Validation 的一部分，但單獨的確效步驟不構成製程驗證

Qualification vs. Validation — 精確區分

許多人混用這兩個術語，但 ISPE 的定義非常明確：

  • Qualification — 針對「設備和輔助系統」，證明安裝正確、運作正常、結果符合預期。包括 IQ/OQ/PQ
  • Validation（製程驗證） — 針對「製程」，提供高度保證特定製程能持續產出符合預定接受標準的結果

簡記：Qualification 是確認「設備能力」，Validation 是確認「製程能力」。

Definitions: R – S

Recovery Time

The time after an upset in a room's HVAC environmental parameters for the room to return to "normal" conditions, such as a return to acceptable humidity levels after a room wash down. This occurs within a certain number of air changes after the upset source is removed, minimally six to ten, depending on the severity of the upset, the quality of the air supply, and the degree of mixing of room air.

Restricted Access Barrier System (RABS)

An aseptic processing system that provides an enclosed, but not closed, environment meeting ISO 5/Grade A conditions utilizing a rigid wall enclosure and air overspill to separate its interior from the surrounding environment.

Relative Humidity (RH)

A measure of the water vapor content of room air, expressed as a percentage.

Risk Assessment

A systematic process of organizing information to support a risk decision to be made within a risk management process. It consists of the identification of hazards and the analysis and evaluation of risks associated with exposure to those hazards.

Sanitization

That part of decontamination that reduces viable microorganisms to a defined acceptance level; normally achieved by using a chemical agent or heat to reduce microbial levels.

Sporicidal Agent

An agent that destroys bacterial and fungal spores.

Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)

Instructions that specify how an activity is to be accomplished.

Sterile

Absence of life; usually refers to absence of viable microorganisms.

Sterilization

The act or process (physical or chemical) that destroys or eliminates all forms of life (e.g., microorganisms); despite being stated as an absolute, the action of sterilization usually is stated in terms of probability.

Sterilizing Filter

A filter that, when challenged with the microorganism Brevundimonas diminuta, at a minimum concentration of 107 organisms per cm2 of filter surface, produces a sterile effluent.

定義 R – S：中文解說

RABS — 限制進入屏障系統的精確定義

RABS：一種無菌製程系統，提供「封閉但非密閉」(enclosed, but not closed) 的環境，符合 ISO 5/Grade A 條件，利用剛性壁和氣流溢出 (air overspill) 將內部與周圍環境隔離。

關鍵措辭解析：

  • "enclosed, but not closed" — 這是 RABS 與 Isolator 的核心區別。RABS 有物理圍護但不是氣密的
  • "air overspill" — RABS 的保護機制：正壓氣流從內部溢出，防止外部空氣侵入
  • "rigid wall enclosure" — 使用剛性壁（非柔性隔簾），是 RABS 區別於普通潔淨室的特徵

詳細比較可參考本知識庫 PDA PtC-12（RABS 專題）和 PDA PtC-Isolators（隔離器專題）。

恢復時間 (Recovery Time) — HVAC 設計的關鍵指標

恢復時間：房間 HVAC 環境參數受到擾動後恢復至正常條件所需的時間。

  • 最少需要 6-10 次換氣才能恢復（取決於擾動嚴重程度、送風品質和空氣混合程度）
  • EU GMP Annex 1 要求 Grade B 區域的恢復時間應在 15-20 分鐘內回到靜態標準
  • 恢復時間測試是潔淨室確效的必要項目

滅菌相關術語群

  • 無菌（Sterile） — 無生命存在；通常指無活性微生物。注意：儘管表述為絕對概念，實際上以機率表示
  • 滅菌（Sterilization） — 破壞或消除所有生命形式的過程（物理或化學）。通常以 PNSU（非無菌單位機率）表示，如 10-6
  • 除菌過濾器（Sterilizing Filter） — 在以 Brevundimonas diminuta（最小挑戰微生物）最少 107/cm2 濃度挑戰時，產生無菌流出液的過濾器。詳見 PDA TR26
  • 殺孢子劑（Sporicidal Agent） — 能破壞細菌和真菌孢子的化學劑。孢子是最耐受的微生物形態
  • 清潔消毒（Sanitization） — 去汙的一部分，將活微生物降至定義的可接受水準

其他 R-S 術語

  • 相對濕度（RH） — 室內空氣中水蒸氣含量，以百分比表示。HVAC 控制參數之一
  • 風險評估（Risk Assessment） — 組織資訊以支持風險管理決策的系統化過程。包括危害識別、風險分析和風險評價
  • 標準操作程序（SOP） — 規定如何完成某項活動的指示文件

Definitions: T – W

Temporal Separation

The separation of products or process ingredients such that two materials do not exist in the same space at the same time. Also known as Campaigning.

Terminal Filter

HVAC air filtration located at the entry point of air supply to the room (usually at the ceiling).

Terminal Sterilization

The process applied to product sealed in its final container that transforms a non-sterile product into a sterile one.

Toxic

A substance which is harmful.

Unclassified Area

Support area peripheral to manufacturing (e.g., warehouse, office).

Unidirectional Airflow (UAF) (ISO 14644-4)

Controlled airflow through the entire cross-section of a clean zone, with a steady velocity and approximately parallel streamlines. This type of airflow results in a directed transport of particles from the clean zone.

User Requirements Specification (URS)

Generally, the first in a series of specification documents. It provides a high-level description of the user's expectation of the project scope, with emphasis on product parameters and process performance parameters.

Validation

(See: Process Validation)

Verification

The act of reviewing, inspecting, testing, checking, auditing, or otherwise establishing and documenting whether items, processes, services, or documents conform to specified requirements.

Vial

A final container for a parenteral or diagnostic product. Sealed with a rubber closure and overseal. Generally required to be class I borosilicate glass.

Viable

Living.

Volatility

A measure of how quickly a substance forms vapors at ordinary temperatures. The more volatile the substance is, the faster it evaporates, and the higher the concentrations of vapor (gas) in the air.

Water for Injection (WFI) (USP)

Water purified by distillation or by reverse osmosis, it contains no added substance, and it meets the purity requirements of purified water. Although not intended to be sterile, it meets a test for a limit of bacterial endotoxin (less than 0.25 USP Endotoxin Units/ml).

定義 T – W：中文解說

最終滅菌 vs. 無菌製程 — 兩大無菌策略

  • 最終滅菌（Terminal Sterilization） — 將已密封在最終容器中的非無菌產品轉化為無菌產品的過程。通常使用高壓蒸汽或輻射
  • 無菌製程（Aseptic Processing） — 在受控環境中處理已滅菌物料，全程維持無菌狀態

選擇依據：若產品可耐受最終滅菌條件（熱、輻射），應優先採用最終滅菌，因其提供更高的無菌保證水準（SAL 10-6）。許多生物製劑無法耐受，只能使用無菌製程。

CDMO 角度：大多數 CDMO 客戶的產品為生物製劑，需要無菌充填而非最終滅菌。

單向氣流 (UAF) — 潔淨室空氣動力學的核心

單向氣流（Unidirectional Airflow）：通過潔淨區整個橫截面的受控氣流，具有穩定的速度和近似平行的流線。此類型的氣流能將粒子從潔淨區定向輸送出去。

  • ISO 5/Grade A 區域必須使用單向氣流
  • 典型速度：0.36-0.54 m/s（依據 EU GMP Annex 1）
  • 「層流」(Laminar Flow) 一詞在技術上不精確，正式術語應為 UAF
  • Terminal Filter（末端過濾器）安裝於送風入口處（通常在天花板），是實現 UAF 的硬體基礎

容器與包材術語

  • 西林瓶（Vial） — 注射或診斷產品的最終容器，以橡膠塞封和鋁蓋（Overseal）密封。通常要求為第一類硼矽酸鹽玻璃。詳見 PDA TR87（玻璃瓶處理）和 PDA TR43（容器缺陷分類）
  • Parison — BFS（吹灌封）製程中從模頭擠出的中空熔融塑料管，由氣壓在模具中膨脹成容器

其他 T-W 術語

  • 時間隔離（Temporal Separation / Campaigning） — 確保兩種物料不同時存在於同一空間。CDMO 多產品設施的常用策略
  • 未分級區域（Unclassified Area） — 製造區外圍的支援區域（如倉庫、辦公室）
  • URS（使用者需求規格） — 規格文件系列的第一份，描述使用者對專案範圍的期望，強調產品參數和製程性能參數
  • 驗證（Verification） — 審查、檢驗、測試、檢查、稽核或以其他方式確認項目是否符合規定要求
  • WFI（注射用水） — 通過蒸餾或反滲透純化，細菌內毒素 <0.25 EU/ml
  • 揮發性（Volatility） — 衡量物質在常溫下形成蒸氣的速度，影響清潔劑和消毒劑的選擇
