14.4 Scenario 3 — Apparently Unacceptable (but WAS) | 表面上不可接受（但其實可行）
Original Text (English)

In this example, the owner wishes to introduce a sex hormone into the facility. Sex hormones have been under increased scrutiny because of the risk of effects on fertility and birth defects. The owner has an excellent history of compliance with the agencies that cover the territories in which the products are sold.

A steering committee was set up of the stakeholders including management, legal, regulatory, and quality, operations, validation and cleaning specialist, engineering and Environment, Health, and Safety (EHS), with input from toxicology.

The first task of the committee was to conduct a risk analysis, beginning with the logic diagram (see Section 14.6) followed by the use of FMEA tools. All stakeholders took part in these exercises.

As soon as the committee had assembled sufficient data, they sought informal discussion with the regulatory agencies and discussed the proposal in concept. Supporting scientific data was made available and a draft of the QRMP document was tabled.

Following the logic diagram, segregation was possibly required for this product because it is being supplied to a country that has historically required segregation for sex hormones. An FMEA analysis was then undertaken with a team of stakeholders, this technique was chosen because it addresses a wide range of ICH Q9 topics, including:

  • Risk identification
  • Risk ranking (using the RPN scores)
  • Exposure control assessment
  • Risk analysis

After the FMEA review, the final version of the QRMP was produced to show how ICH Q9 had been applied to minimize or prevent risk to product and operators.

繁體中文解說

為何「表面上不可接受」？

性荷爾蒙（sex hormone）在全球多個監管地區歷史上被要求使用專屬廠房（dedicated facility）。初步看來，將其引入現有多產品設施似乎明顯違規。然而，本場景的關鍵教訓是：透過嚴謹的風險評估流程，可以科學地論證共用設施是可行的。

情境要點

  • 新引入物質：性荷爾蒙 Agent H（極低 ADE = 0.001 mg/day）
  • 挑戰：部分國家法規歷史上要求專屬設施
  • 策略：早期與監管機構非正式溝通 + FMEA + QRMP
  • 結論：透過適當管控措施，可在共用設施製造

比喻：法規要求 vs. 科學論證

就像駕駛人在不確定某條路段是否允許通行前，先主動詢問交通主管單位，而非直接行駛或放棄。提前與主管機關溝通、展示科學數據，是合規策略的核心。

監管溝通的重要性

委員會在擁有足夠數據後，立即主動與監管機關進行非正式討論，並提交 QRMP 草稿。這種「先溝通、再行動」的策略，降低了後期審查風險，也展現了廠商的透明度與責任感。

14.4 Scenario 3 — QRMP Overview | 品質風險管理計畫概述
Original Text (English)

Quality Risk Management Plan (QRMP) for Technology Transfer and Commercialization of Sex Hormone Agent H

Overview

This document summarizes the QRMP for XYZ Pharma at the Kingswood, CT site. The plan embraces the principles of ICH Q9 and uses risk management tools to support manufacturing strategies designed to minimize risks to product and operators.

The proposal is to introduce a sex hormone Agent H into the facility. The existing compounds all have QRMPs in place.

A formalized risk management approach was applied to the manufacturing facility. This involved a holistic assessment that identified the potential hazards and risks to product and operators for all products handled in the facility to ensure that appropriate controls were in place to manufacture these products safely.

A cornerstone of the QRMP is the establishment of ADE values for the active ingredients handled in the manufacturing facility. The ADE values serve as the starting point for the rest of the risk assessment process as described by ICH Q9.

繁體中文解說

QRMP 的作用

品質風險管理計畫（QRMP）是一份整合性文件，將 ICH Q9 框架系統性地套用於特定設施或產品引入。它不只是風險評估報告，更是一份行動計畫，說明：

  • 識別了哪些危害
  • 採用了哪些評估方法
  • 實施了哪些控制措施
  • 如何監控與審查

ADE 數值差異的意義

從表格可以清楚看出 Agent H（性荷爾蒙）的 ADE 遠低於其他化合物：

  • Antacid A：50 mg/day（相對安全）
  • Anti-hypertensive B：0.5 mg/day
  • Agent H：0.001 mg/day（最低，高效價）
  • NSAID D：40 mg/day

ADE = 0.001 mg/day = 1 µg/day，代表設備表面殘留量必須控制在極低水平。這是此情境「表面上不可接受」的核心原因。

ADE（可接受每日暴露量）定義

ADE 是每日接觸劑量的上限，低於此值預期不會對最敏感族群（包括患者、作業員）產生不良反應，且涵蓋終身暴露。它由合格毒理學家基於毒理數據推導，並包含適當的安全係數。

14.4 Scenario 3 — Facility Description & Controls | 設施描述與管控措施
Original Text (English)

Facility Description

The facility under review is a single corridor facility with access from a GMP gowning area for personnel and a material airlock to a warehouse, in which a pharmacy and sampling area is located. Each of the unit processes is located off the common corridor with both MALs (Material Airlocks) and PALs (Personnel Airlocks); both are single chamber and are pressurized to create a pressure cascade between the processing area and the corridor.

A suite of rooms are provided for dirty side wash, clean side dry, and separate clean storage of equipment. The area is served by a single Air Handling Unit (AHU) with a positive pressure corridor. Air is supplied on a once through basis (no recirculation). Room boundary redundant filtration is provided. Up to two compounds are processed at one time.

Segregation Philosophy (Post-FMEA)

  • The sex hormone API processes were closed. Surrogate testing showed that all other exposures provided an acceptable level of risk and plausible pathways had sufficient redundancy to be protective of other products.
  • Additional gowning introduced: operators wear disposable coveralls, bootees, and hair nets in sex hormone processing rooms. This gowning is removed at the room boundary.
  • Access to process rooms controlled by electronic verification; only the correct materials and personnel are allowed to enter.
  • Sex hormone handling equipment subjected to a closed in-place clean before leaving the process room.
  • All walls, floors, and ceilings are of hard washable coved construction.

Process and Equipment

  • Sampling of incoming API in an isolator with RTP (Rapid Transfer Port) to QA lab
  • Weighing in isolator — closed transfer bin with SBV (Split Butterfly Valve)
  • High shear granulation and wet milling in isolator; heel retention requires manual scraping
  • FBD discharge via SBV; vacuum discharge to in-line cone mill → bin via SBV
  • Bin blend; lubricant via transfer valve; PAT for blend uniformity
  • Compression via SBV-connected bin; coating (data shows no risk of exposure)
  • Bottle or blister packing

HVAC (Figure 14.10)

Pressure cascade: flow always away from corridor and operator zones toward rear wall. Supply through HEPA filters, exhaust via roughing and HEPA filters. MALs and PALs create lock chambers with door operation delay (purge before door release). System is once through — no recirculation risk. Hanging vinyl strip screens at room entrances.

繁體中文解說

單廊式設施（Single Corridor Facility）佈局

設施以單一走廊為主軸，各製程室由 MAL（物料氣閘）和 PAL（人員氣閘）隔離。此設計的優點：

  • 壓力階梯（pressure cascade）可控制空氣流向
  • 氣閘的「淨化延遲」（dwell period）防止門開時的瞬間污染
  • 走廊對製程室保持正壓，確保污染不會從走廊流入

封閉製程（Closed Processing）的核心邏輯

性荷爾蒙 ADE 僅 1 µg/day，若使用開放製程，微量揚塵即可能超標。解決方案是透過工程控制：

  • 隔離器（Isolator）：取樣、稱重在隔離環境進行
  • SBV（裂蝶閥）：密封物料轉移，防止揚塵
  • RTP（快速轉移口）：樣品安全移轉至 QA 實驗室
  • 就地封閉清潔（CIP）：設備離開製程室前先清潔

比喻：SBV 的作用

Split Butterfly Valve（裂蝶閥）像一個「對接連接器」——兩半分別安裝在物料桶與設備接口，對接時同時打開，分離時各自密封，全程粉末不接觸外界空氣，大幅降低揚塵風險。

HVAC 設計的多重防護

HVAC 系統採用「直流式」（once-through，無再循環），這意味著：

  • 污染物不會被再循環風帶回其他區域
  • 排氣通過 HEPA 過濾（粗效 + 高效，兩段保護）
  • 氣閘淨化確保開門時的污染不進入走廊

此設計使得「空氣傳播途徑」的風險透過多重屏障降至可接受水平。

14.4 Scenario 3 — Risk Assessment | 風險評估
Original Text (English)

Risk Identification

A risk assessment process was used to derive criteria to support decision making and control of risks to product and workers. The risk assessment involved an evaluation of all data relevant to support hazard identification, assessment of potency (dose-response) and severity, exposure assessment, and characterization of risk. Risk controls were implemented in a manner commensurate with the risk.

A review of all available data on each API was completed to identify all possible adverse effects. The critical effect (first adverse effect at the low end of the dose-response curve) was identified. The ADE value was derived by dividing the no-effect level for the critical endpoint by appropriate adjustment (uncertainty or safety) factors or to extrapolate to an acceptable level of cancer risk. ADE was used to derive product contact surface cleaning limits.

Establishment of ADE for the Sex Hormone (Agent H)

An ADE value of 1 µg/day was established for sex hormone Agent H. The rationale is summarized in the quality document "Risk Assessment for Sex Hormones." The ADE was based on the low clinical dose and incorporates appropriate adjustment factors to address:

  • Interindividual variability
  • Extrapolation to a no-effect level
  • Differences in bioavailability
  • Residual uncertainties regarding potential developmental effects

Risk Analysis — Plausible Pathways

Mix-up:

  • Bar code reading control of all equipment and materials where they pass into processing rooms
  • Separation of clean and dirty equipment by room; tagging of clean and dirty equipment

Retention (Product Contact Surfaces):

  • Swab monitoring against health-based limits; visually clean inspection
  • Routine monitoring shows consistent performance well below health-based limit (Figure 14.11)
  • Cleaning procedures include disassembly; dedicated blending bin

Mechanical Transfer:

  • Critical transfers are closed
  • All equipment and materials wiped down or bagged before leaving process room
  • Tack mats at room boundaries; outer gowning removed at boundaries

Airborne Transfer:

  • All processes in rooms protected by airlocks with interlocked doors and dwell period
  • Corridor positive pressure to process room; redundant HEPA filtration (99.997% × 2 passes)
  • For cross-contamination to occur, product must: escape process → pass through airlocks against low-velocity flow → travel corridor → enter another airlock → enter process room
  • Gowning protocols and access restriction prevent unauthorized entry
繁體中文解說

四大交叉污染途徑

  • 混淆（Mix-up）：錯誤物料進入製程室（人為錯誤）
  • 殘留（Retention）：前批物料殘留於設備表面
  • 機械轉移（Mechanical Transfer）：殘留物附著於人員、設備、物料表面帶出
  • 空氣傳播（Airborne Transfer）：微粒透過空氣系統傳播

空氣傳播的「多道障礙」論證

ISPE 原文詳細列出空氣傳播必須跨越的每一個障礙：

  1. 逃離製程（escape the process）
  2. 逆低速氣流通過氣閘進入走廊
  3. 在走廊中傳播
  4. 再次逆低速氣流進入另一氣閘
  5. 進入另一製程室及製程

每一步都有物理屏障阻攔，整條路徑的機率極低。這種「多重障礙論證」（multiple barrier argument）是現代 GMP 風險評估的核心邏輯。

ADE 到清潔限度的推導

ADE（1 µg/day）是起點，再結合：

  • 接受患者的最大每日劑量
  • 設備產品接觸面積
  • 批次大小

計算出設備表面最大允許殘留量（µg/cm²），作為清潔驗證的接受標準。

Surrogate Testing（替代物測試）

對於潛在有害物質（如性荷爾蒙），不能使用真實 API 進行清潔驗證的暴露測試。替代物（surrogate）應具有相似的物理化學特性（溶解度、黏附性），但毒性較低，可安全操作。

14.4 Scenario 3 — Cleaning Review, FMEA & Risk Control | 清潔審查、FMEA 及風險控制
Original Text (English)

Cleaning Review — Process Capability

The existing swab sample database was statistically evaluated against the new product's ADE. The swab data for all cleaning processes were found to have high Process Performance Indices (Ppk) with undetectable levels of Defects per Million Opportunities, with the exception of the blender.

The Ppk for the blender was 0.34 with potential for 14,994 Defects per Million Opportunities. This indicated the current cleaning process for the blender is not satisfactory to safely clean the new product.

A Design of Experiment (DoE) was performed to improve the blender cleaning process. Cleaning agent concentration, cleaning time, cleaning temperature, and rinse time were evaluated. The new data indicate the blender can be effectively cleaned to safe levels for the new product.

  • Ppk after DoE: 2.01
  • Defects per Million Opportunities: 251

Because these data indicate a small possibility of product residue, the blender will require a visual inspection and monitoring after each cleaning. To minimize regulatory impact, it was decided to dedicate the blending bin.

Risk Control

It was concluded that sex hormone Agent H could be introduced into the multiproduct manufacturing facility without causing a risk to patients. The control measures adopted were based on a belief that technique and skill-based procedures have a tendency to fail. The controls implemented are of a procedural, mechanical, and electronic nature and are designed to fail safe. Implemented controls include:

  • Electronic access and inventory control — entry and exit from any room permitted only for materials and personnel required at that time. Secondary systems ensure it cannot be bypassed.
  • Critical processes were closed and tested to ensure they afforded both operator and product protection robustly.
  • Automated cleaning introduced to minimize variability from manual cleaning. Small parts still manually cleaned with updated procedures to reduce errors.
  • Air flows and pressures were reversed to maintain security in the event of an upset. Redundant HEPA filtration protects the rest of the facility.
  • Control system effectiveness was verified with quantitative data.
  • Hierarchy of controls closely followed to ensure selection of appropriate technology.
  • Systems designed to alarm when out of range and default to a safe condition.

Risk Communication

A stakeholder team comprising toxicology, formulations, development, operations, quality, regulatory, EHS, and senior management was set up. Key stakeholder members from regulatory, quality, operations, and management were present during FMEA sessions. A web-based bulletin board was set up for collaborative document sharing.

Risk Review

The Quality Risk Plan is reviewed every six months. Stakeholders are rotated so that new members join for each review. Assessments from operators, a log of excursive events, and operability of the system are reviewed. An incentive program solicits input from operators — suggestions leading to risk reduction or quality improvement are implemented and evaluated continuously.

繁體中文解說

Ppk（製程效能指標）解讀

Ppk 衡量製程整體效能（包含製程平均值偏移）：

  • Ppk < 1.0：製程不穩定，超規風險高
  • Ppk = 0.34（改善前）：約 15,000 ppm 超標機率，不可接受
  • Ppk = 2.01（DoE 後）：約 251 ppm，可接受（需加強監控）
  • 工業標準：清潔驗證通常要求 Ppk ≥ 1.33

Scenario 3 的關鍵轉折點

不同於 Scenario 2（混摻機無法達到要求，最終放棄引入），Scenario 3 中：

  • DoE 成功改善清潔製程（Ppk 0.34 → 2.01）
  • 混摻桶（blending bin）改為專屬（dedicated），消除最高風險環節
  • 其他所有管控措施共同論證風險可接受

這正是「表面上不可接受，但實際上可行」的核心：工程措施加上科學數據，成功翻轉了初始的悲觀判斷。

管控層級（Hierarchy of Controls）

ISPE 強調選擇管控措施時應遵循優先順序（由高至低）：

  1. 消除（Elimination）：移除危害源
  2. 替代（Substitution）：以低危害替代
  3. 工程控制（Engineering Controls）：隔離器、密閉轉移
  4. 行政管控（Administrative Controls）：程序、訓練
  5. 個人防護（PPE）：防護服

本情境中，封閉製程（工程控制）是核心，程序和電子管制作為輔助。

「Fail-Safe」設計原則

ISPE 特別強調管控措施應設計為「fail safe」（失效安全）：當系統出現異常，應自動進入安全狀態（例如警報、鎖閉），而非依賴人員反應。電子門禁、HEPA 壓差警報均符合此原則。

六個月滾動審查的實踐意義

風險評估不是「做一次就完成」。定期審查、輪換委員（引入新視角）、結合操作人員的回饋，確保風險計畫與現實製程保持同步，這是 ICH Q9 持續改善精神的體現。

14.5 Scenario 4 — Existing Facility with Large Product Portfolio | 現有設施、大型產品組合
Original Text (English)

An existing facility is currently manufacturing 32 products using 10 different APIs. The products and APIs are shown in Table 14.5.

The processes used to manufacture the products are:

  • Process 1: Sample, weigh, mill, granulate, mill, dry, mill, blend, compression, and pack (10 steps)
  • Process 2: Sample, weigh, mill, granulate, mill, dry, mill, blend, compression, coat, and pack (11 steps)
  • Process 3: Sample, weigh, mill, granulate, mill, dry, mill, blend, mill, blend, compression, mill, blend, compression, coat, and pack (16 steps)

All processes are fairly open (i.e., there are no containment devices or engineering controls used).

繁體中文解說

Scenario 4 的複雜性

這是四個情境中規模最大、複雜度最高的。關鍵特點：

  • 32 個產品、10 種 API — 清潔矩陣維度極大（10×10 = 100 種組合）
  • 完全開放製程（無工程控制）— 交叉污染風險相對高
  • 矩陣法清潔驗證 — 以最嚴格組合作為整體標準
  • 初始分析顯示不符合 EU GMP — 需要系統性重新審查

比喻：矩陣法清潔驗證

想像 10 種 API 兩兩排列，就像一個 10×10 的棋盤。矩陣法的概念是：不需要驗證每一格（100 種組合），而是找出最嚴苛的那一格（最低清潔限度），以此作為整體驗證標準——只要最難的一格通過，其餘自動滿足。

清潔矩陣法（Matrix Approach）邏輯

原始矩陣：所有組合中最低限度 = 0.1 µg/cm²（Anti-hypertensive1 → Anti-hypertensive2）。

問題：EU GMP 第 3.6 節要求檢測方法能可靠偵測此限度，但原始 LOD 均高於 0.1 µg/cm²，不符合要求。

解決步驟：

  1. 改善分析方法（見 Table 14.7）
  2. 新 LOD 仍無法偵測 0.1 µg/cm²（Anti-hypertensive1 新 LOD = 0.05）
  3. 將此組合（Anti-hypertensive1 → Anti-hypertensive2）從矩陣中「拉出」，改為每次化學分析確認
  4. 新矩陣整體限度提升至 2.0 µg/cm²，新 LOD 足以偵測

EU GMP Section 3.6 的要求

EU GMP 第 3.6 節規定，若毒理學評估所推導的殘留限度無法被已驗證的分析方法可靠偵測，則相關產品不得在共用設施製造。這是 Scenario 4 初始不合規的法規依據。

繁體中文解說

風險評估的起點：法規符合性檢查

邏輯圖的第一個問題就是：「是否有法規要求使用專屬設施？」在 Scenario 4 中，初始答案幾乎是「是」，因為檢測方法無法偵測最嚴格的清潔限度。這驅動了整個改善行動。

分析方法改善的策略意義

提升 LOD（偵測下限）等同於提升對清潔有效性的「信心」。當 LOD 優於清潔限度，才能可靠地宣稱設備「清潔至符合標準」。若 LOD 高於清潔限度，則結果「合格」只代表「在方法偵測範圍內未發現問題」，科學意義不足。

「拉出矩陣」的實務操作

Anti-hypertensive1 → Anti-hypertensive2 被從矩陣拉出後，每次此序列發生時，需要：

  • 在 Anti-hypertensive2 生產前執行棉棒取樣（swab sampling）
  • 以化學分析確認殘留 < 0.1 µg/cm²
  • 合格後才能確認設備清潔並放行

這是一種「排除後個別處理」（case-by-case）的管控策略，既滿足法規要求，又不需要完全改變矩陣法。

歷史清潔數據的解讀

30 次清潔中有 1 次失敗（3.3%），根本原因是「未遵照程序」（human error），而非清潔製程本身失效。這是重要區別——製程能力本身沒問題，問題在行為。CAPA 選擇加強訓練（而非改變製程），具有針對性。

最差情況選擇的邏輯

Opioid 被選為最差情況（worst case），而非 Anti-hypertensive1（ADE 更低 = 25 vs 50 µg/day）。原因：

  • Anti-hypertensive1 批次數少（36/年）、批量小（7 kg）

風險 = 危害（ADE 低）× 暴露（頻率高、接觸面積大）。單純以 ADE 排名並不足夠。

「最差情況」與清潔驗證的「最差情況」不同

原文特別說明：用於交叉污染風險評估的最差情況（opioid）不等同於清潔驗證的最差情況。

  • 交叉污染 worst case：考量危害性 + 製程暴露頻率 + 接觸面積
  • 清潔驗證 worst case：通常選擇最難清潔的物質（cleanability rank 最高）

「涵蓋所有製程步驟」的意義

選擇 Opioid（Process 2，10 步）作為風險評估對象，並非因為它是最危險的，而是因為它的製程涵蓋設施中所有製程步驟。一次評估即可代表所有產品的製程節點，避免重複工作。

繁體中文解說

RPN（風險優先係數）計算與閾值

RPN = 嚴重度（S）× 發生率（O）× 偵測性（D）

  • RPN < 125：可接受風險，無需立即行動
  • 125 ≤ RPN ≤ 343：應實施風險降低措施（可繼續生產）
  • RPN > 343：不可接受風險，須停止生產直到降低風險

高 RPN 項目分析

RPN = 700（最高）：出現兩次，均為「設備未清潔（視覺檢查不足）」

  • 嚴重度 10（患者暴露）× 發生率 7（視覺常常無法發現）× 偵測性 10（無化學分析）= 700
  • 核心問題：僅靠視覺檢查無法可靠確認設備清潔到 0.1 µg/cm² 水平

RPN = 490 和 350：機械轉移問題（著裝未更換、設備帶殘留）

關鍵發現：最高風險來自「制度性缺陷」（視覺清潔驗證不足、著裝程序不完善），而非設施設計。

魚骨圖（Fishbone Analysis）的用途

魚骨圖（Ishikawa diagram）是 FMEA 的前置工具，用來系統性地腦力激盪四種交叉污染模式的所有可能原因（人員、方法、設備、環境、材料、量測）。完成魚骨圖後，再進行正式的 FMEA 表格分析，確保不遺漏任何失效模式。

「偵測性 = 10」的意義

偵測性評分為 10 代表「幾乎無法偵測」——現有管控完全無法發現此問題。在「Equipment not clean / Visual not adequate」這一行，視覺檢查無法偵測微量殘留，故 D = 10，直接讓 RPN 爆錶。這說明了為何化學分析（swab testing）是不可缺少的清潔驗證手段。

繁體中文解說

四大「必須停工」項目的實務意涵

  1. 走廊暫存（staging in corridor）：材料和設備在走廊中混合暫放，極易導致不同 API 的物料混淆。改善：禁止走廊暫存，所有物料直接進出製程室。
  2. 視覺清潔驗證不足：對 ADE 低的 API（如 Anti-hypertensive1），肉眼無法確認清潔至 0.1 µg/cm²。改善：引入棉棒取樣化學分析。
  3. 更換 API 時未更換著裝：操作人員從一個 API 製程區移至另一個時，若不更換防護服，會機械性地攜帶殘留物。改善：制定強制換裝程序。
  4. 設備殘留帶出製程室：設備移出前未充分清潔，導致殘留物散播至走廊和其他製程室。改善：設備離室前必須清潔驗收。

風險溝通的三個層次

  • 即時通報：不可接受風險 → 立即通知高管
  • 定期更新：每週更新直到風險降至可接受水平
  • 系統性管理：CAPA 管理所有改善行動；Change Control 確保評估持續有效

觸發重新審查的四種情況

風險評估不是靜態文件，以下任何情況均需重新評估：

  • 產品組合變動（新增/移除）
  • 設施、設備或製程變更
  • 產品特性變化（MDD、ADE 修訂）
  • 定期年度審查（最低頻率）

這與 ICH Q10「持續改善」精神一致，也是法規（EU GMP、FDA）對品質系統的基本要求。

在實務中，邏輯圖不是線性步驟清單，而是一個迭代式決策框架：

  • 每個問題的答案可能觸發新的調查（如改善分析方法）
  • 管控措施實施後需要重新評估（如 DoE 後重新計算 Ppk）
  • 最終結論需要所有路徑均通過，才能宣告風險可接受

法規要求的「二元篩選」

邏輯圖的最初兩個節點構成「法規篩選門檻」：

  1. 是否有明確法規要求專屬設施？若是，直接需要專屬設施，無需進一步分析。
  2. 分析方法能否偵測清潔限度？若否，EU GMP 3.6 不允許共用設施（直到方法改善）。

只有通過這兩個前提，才能進入風險分析階段。這也是 Scenario 4 初始「不合規」的根本原因，並驅動了整個分析方法改善工作。

邏輯圖的設計哲學

此邏輯圖體現了 ICH Q9 的核心精神：風險管理是一個動態過程，而非一次性評估。每個問題節點都允許「若現在不可行，是否可以改善後再試？」的路徑，避免了過度保守（直接放棄）或過於冒進（忽視風險）兩個極端。
