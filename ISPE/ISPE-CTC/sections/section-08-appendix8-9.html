About This Reference List  |  關於本參考清單

The ISPE CTC Guide cites 32 references spanning international regulatory guidance, pharmacopeial standards, industry guidelines, national standards, and equipment testing norms. Together they define the regulatory environment within which any CTC mapping and monitoring programme must operate.

For a CDMO operating in a multi-market environment (US, EU, Asia-Pacific), familiarity with these documents is not optional — inspectors from MHRA, FDA, and WHO routinely ask whether your qualification protocols align with the cited standards.

Key Regulatory Pillars

  • WHO TRS 961 Annex 9 — Global baseline for temperature-sensitive product storage & transport
  • EU GDP Guidelines 2013 — Binding EU distribution requirement for temperature control
  • USP <659> — Pharmacopeial storage temperature definitions
  • ASTM E2500 — Science/risk-based verification framework
  • 21 CFR Part 11 — Electronic records/signatures for data loggers & BMS

中文解說  |  Commentary

ISPE CTC 指南的 32 篇參考文獻構成了一個完整的法規生態系統。對 CDMO 而言，這份清單不只是書目，更是稽查準備的優先閱讀清單。

為什麼參考文獻很重要？

當稽查員問「你的溫度標準依據是什麼？」時，正確的回答應該引用具體文件——例如「依據 USP <659> 定義，2–8°C 儲存符合 Cold 分類」，而不是「依據公司 SOP」。

WHO TRS 961 Annex 9 和 EU GDP 2013 是全球冷鏈最被廣泛引用的文件，幾乎所有市場的監管機關都認可這兩份文件作為基準。

類比說明

這 32 篇參考文獻就像蓋房子時的建築法規、材料規範和施工準則——每一份都有其具體的適用範圍。ISPE CTC 指南的角色是把這些規範整合成一套可操作的實務框架。

最重要的縮寫群組

認證四步： IQ → OQ → PQ（+ FAT/SAT）— 認識這四個縮寫的順序和意義是 CTC 認證的基礎知識。

監控核心： BMS、RTD、MU、CPP、CQA — 這五個縮寫涵蓋了監控系統設計的核心概念。

法規框架： GDP、URS、SOP、SME — 這四個縮寫貫穿整份指南的品保框架。

容易混淆的縮寫

  • BAS vs. BMS： BAS（樓宇自動化系統）是廣義概念；BMS（樓宇管理系統）是藥廠習慣用語，兩者常互換使用。重點是：任何用於記錄 GMP 數據的系統都必須符合 21 CFR Part 11 / Annex 11。
  • RTD vs. 熱電偶： RTD（Pt100 鉑電阻）精度約 ±0.1–0.3°C；熱電偶精度較差（±0.5–2°C）。GMP 藥廠溫度監控優先選用 RTD。
  • CPP vs. CQA： CQA 是產品的品質屬性（如效價穩定性）；CPP 是影響 CQA 的製程參數（如溫度）。兩者是因果關係。
  • MU vs. Accuracy： MU（測量不確定度）是統計概念，包含感測器誤差、校正誤差和環境影響的綜合評估；Accuracy（準確度）只是感測器本身的規格。MU 才是 guard-banding 的計算依據。

FAT 與 SAT 的差別

FAT（Factory Acceptance Test）在製造商工廠進行——就像在汽車工廠驗收新車，確認基本功能正常。

SAT（Site Acceptance Test）在使用者場地進行——就像把新車開到自己的停車場後再做一次確認，確認在實際環境下仍然正常運作。

在 CTC 認證中，FAT 驗證設備出廠規格，SAT 驗證安裝後的現場表現，兩者缺一不可。

CDMO 實務應用

在撰寫 URS 時，建議將縮寫清單附在文件末尾作為定義附錄。這樣不僅方便閱讀，也能在稽查時展示文件完整性。

建議 CDMO 建立一份統一的 GMP 術語縮寫表（包含 CTC、HVAC、EMS 等系統），供所有 SOP 和認證文件共同引用，確保一致性。

Guard-Banding Concept  |  保護區間概念

Guard-banding is the practice of tightening acceptance criteria to account for Measurement Uncertainty (MU). If USP requires 2–8°C for Cold storage, and MU = ±0.3°C, then the guard-banded acceptance criteria become: 2.3°C ≤ T ≤ 7.7°C.

中文：保護區間（Guard-banding）是將驗收標準縮緊以涵蓋測量不確定度（MU）的做法。若 USP 要求 2–8°C，MU = ±0.3°C，則收緊後的驗收標準為 2.3–7.7°C。這樣即使感測器有最大誤差，仍能確保產品實際溫度在規格範圍內。

ISO/IEC Guide 98-3 (GUM) — 即 Guide to the Expression of Uncertainty in Measurement — 是計算 MU 的國際標準方法，也是 ISPE CTC 指南中 guard-banding 計算的理論依據。

Complete Guide Summary  |  指南完整總覽

ISPE Good Practice Guide: Controlled Temperature Chamber Mapping and Monitoring (2016)

Guide Overview  |  指南概述

The ISPE Good Practice Guide: Controlled Temperature Chamber Mapping and Monitoring (2016) provides a comprehensive, risk-based framework for the qualification, mapping, monitoring, and lifecycle management of temperature-controlled chambers used in pharmaceutical manufacturing, storage, and distribution.

The guide addresses the full range of chamber types — from bench-top stability chambers to warehouse-scale cold rooms — and spans the entire equipment lifecycle from URS development through ongoing periodic review.

It represents the pharmaceutical industry's consensus position on how to demonstrate that a controlled temperature environment reliably maintains product within specified temperature limits, accounting for real-world variables such as door openings, load configurations, power failures, and seasonal ambient changes.

Who This Guide Serves

  • QA/Validation Engineers — Protocol design, acceptance criteria, report review
  • Engineering/Facilities — URS, FAT, SAT, commissioning, alarm system design
  • Operations/Warehouse — Ongoing monitoring, excursion response SOPs
  • Regulatory Affairs — Inspection readiness, regulatory alignment
  • Senior Management — Governance, risk-based decision making

指南核心思想

ISPE CTC 指南的核心哲學是風險為基礎的生命週期管理——從設計之初就考慮最差情況，以科學和數據支持認證決策，並建立持續監控機制以維持認證狀態。

指南的結構遵循「計畫 → 設計 → 認證 → 運行 → 審查」的生命週期邏輯，每個階段都有明確的文件要求和品保輸出。

整體指南的結構比喻

這份指南就像一本「受控溫度環境管理的完全手冊」——從「我需要什麼樣的冰箱」（URS）到「如何確認它正常工作」（IQ/OQ/PQ），再到「如何確保它長期保持正常」（監控與定期審查）。

對 CDMO 而言，掌握這份指南等同於掌握了客戶稽查（Client Audit）和監管稽查（Regulatory Inspection）時所有冷鏈相關問題的標準答案框架。

2016 版的重要意義

2016 版是 ISPE CTC 指南的第一個完整版本（基於 2012 年的 Concept Paper 擴展而來），整合了 EU GDP 2013、ASTM E2500、WHO TRS 961 等最新法規要求，是目前行業最權威的 CTC 認證參考文件之一。

Top 10 Must-Know Principles  |  十大必懂核心原則

The essential knowledge framework for CTC mapping and monitoring — applicable from first qualification to lifecycle management

Principles 1–5

1. URS Drives Everything

Every qualification decision — acceptance criteria, sensor placement, alarm setpoints, test conditions — must trace back to the User Requirements Specification. If a requirement is not in the URS, it cannot be a qualification failure. If it is in the URS, it must be tested. The URS is the bridge between business/product needs and engineering/QA execution.

中文：所有認證決策都必須追溯到 URS。URS 是需求與執行之間的橋梁，沒有寫入 URS 的要求不能成為認證失敗的依據；寫入 URS 的要求必須被測試驗證。

2. Worst-Case Location Is Non-Negotiable

Mapping studies must identify the worst-case locations (hottest and coldest points) under all relevant conditions: empty chamber, loaded chamber, door-open conditions, and seasonal extremes. The monitoring sensor must be placed at the worst-case location(s) identified in mapping — not at the most convenient or accessible point.

中文：溫度映射必須識別最差位置（最熱和最冷點），並在空腔體、滿載、開門等所有相關條件下進行。監控感測器必須放置在映射確認的最差位置——而不是最方便的位置。

3. Measurement Uncertainty Must Be Accounted For

Every temperature measurement has uncertainty. MU must be calculated for the complete measurement chain (sensor + cable + data acquisition + calibration) and applied to acceptance criteria via guard-banding. Ignoring MU means your qualification data may not actually demonstrate that the product temperature was within specification.

中文：每次溫度測量都有不確定度。MU 必須針對完整測量鏈（感測器+線纜+數據採集+校正）計算，並通過保護區間應用於驗收標準。忽略 MU 意味著您的認證數據可能無法真正證明產品溫度在規格範圍內。

4. Alert ≠ Alarm — Both Are Required

Alert levels are early warning signals that allow corrective action before product is at risk. Alarm levels indicate the temperature has reached a point where product quality may be compromised. Setting only one level (as many companies do) is insufficient. The gap between alert and alarm must be scientifically justified based on MU, chamber response time, and product stability data.

中文：警示值（Alert）是提前預警，讓操作人員在產品受威脅前採取行動；警報值（Alarm）表示溫度已達到可能影響產品品質的程度。僅設置一個層次是不夠的，兩者之間的間距必須基於 MU、腔體響應時間和產品穩定性數據科學地論證。

5. FAT + SAT + IQ + OQ + PQ Is the Minimum

A complete qualification lifecycle requires all five stages. FAT validates the equipment meets specification at the vendor site. SAT confirms it still meets specification after shipping and installation. IQ verifies correct installation. OQ verifies correct operation (empty, worst-case). PQ verifies performance under actual or simulated operating conditions (loaded). Skipping any stage creates qualification gaps that may be cited during inspections.

中文：完整的認證生命週期需要五個階段：FAT（廠驗）→ SAT（現場驗收）→ IQ（安裝認證）→ OQ（運行認證）→ PQ（性能認證）。跳過任何一個階段都會產生認證空白，可能在稽查中被指出。

Principles 6–10

6. Load Configuration Changes Invalidate Mapping

Temperature distribution in a loaded chamber is fundamentally different from an empty chamber. PQ mapping must be conducted with a representative product load. Significant changes to load configuration (e.g. changing from 50% to 100% capacity, changing packaging format, adding new shelving) should trigger an assessment of whether requalification is required.

中文：滿載腔體的溫度分布與空腔體根本不同。PQ 映射必須在代表性產品負載下進行。裝載配置的重大變更（如容量從 50% 改為 100%、更換包裝格式、增加層架）應觸發是否需要重新認證的評估。

7. Monitoring System Data Must Be 21 CFR Part 11 / Annex 11 Compliant

All electronic temperature monitoring data used for GMP decisions (product release, deviation investigation, batch records) must meet data integrity requirements: audit trails, access controls, backup procedures, and system validation. This applies to BMS, data loggers, and any standalone monitoring software. Non-compliant data may be considered unreliable during FDA or MHRA inspections.

中文：所有用於 GMP 決策的電子溫度監控數據（產品放行、偏差調查、批次記錄）必須符合數據完整性要求：稽查跟蹤、訪問控制、備份程序和系統驗證。不合規的數據在 FDA 或 MHRA 稽查中可能被認為不可靠。

8. Excursions Require MKT Assessment Before Product Disposition

Every temperature excursion must be investigated, documented, and assessed for product impact before making a release or rejection decision. MKT calculation is the scientific basis for excursion impact assessment — it accounts for the non-linear relationship between temperature and degradation rate. A short excursion to a moderately elevated temperature may not require rejection if MKT remains within specification.

中文：每次溫度偏差都必須在做出放行或拒收決定之前進行調查、記錄和產品影響評估。MKT 計算是偏差影響評估的科學依據——它考慮了溫度與降解速率之間的非線性關係。如果 MKT 仍在規格範圍內，短暫的輕微超溫可能不需要拒收。

9. Periodic Review Maintains Qualification State

Qualification is not a one-time event — it is a lifecycle programme. Annual (or frequency-based) periodic reviews must verify: no unreported changes, calibration compliance, alarm response performance, trend analysis of temperature deviations, and continuing fitness for purpose. The result of a periodic review should be a formal documented conclusion: "qualification status maintained" or "requalification required."

中文：認證不是一次性事件——它是生命週期計畫。年度定期審查必須驗證：無未報告的變更、校正合規性、警報響應性能、溫度偏差趨勢分析和持續的適用性。定期審查的結果應該是正式文件化的結論：「認證狀態維持」或「需要重新認證」。

10. Risk-Based Approach Is Not an Excuse to Do Less

The guide's risk-based framework allows scientific justification for qualification scope decisions — fewer sensors, shorter study duration, reduced testing frequency for low-risk chambers. However, this flexibility requires documented risk assessment (FMEA or equivalent) with explicit rationale. "Risk-based" without documented risk assessment is simply "doing less" — which will not survive inspection scrutiny.

中文：風險為基礎的方法允許對認證範圍決策（更少感測器、更短研究時間、低風險腔體的較少測試）進行科學論證。但這種靈活性需要有文件化的風險評估（FMEA 或等效方法）和明確的理由。沒有文件化風險評估的「風險基礎」只是「做得更少」——這在稽查審查中無法站得住腳。

CDMO Cold Storage Complete Qualification Checklist  |  CDMO 冷藏儲存完整認證查核表

A practical lifecycle checklist covering all stages from URS through periodic review — aligned with ISPE CTC Guide 2016

How to Use This Checklist  |  如何使用本查核表

This checklist covers the complete lifecycle of a CTC qualification programme at a CDMO. Use it for: (1) new equipment qualification planning, (2) inspection readiness gap assessment, (3) periodic review completeness check, (4) change control impact assessment. Items marked Critical are routinely checked by regulatory inspectors.

查核表使用說明

本查核表涵蓋 CDMO 冷藏設備認證的完整生命週期。使用場景：(1) 新設備認證計畫、(2) 稽查準備差距評估、(3) 定期審查完整性檢查、(4) 變更控制影響評估。標記 Critical 的項目是監管稽查員例行檢查的重點。

Phase 1: User Requirements Specification (URS) & Qualification Planning  |  階段一：URS 與認證計畫

  • Critical URS developed and approved by QA, Engineering, Operations, and Product Management before equipment procurement
  • Critical URS defines: temperature range, allowable excursions, storage capacity, alarm setpoint requirements, power backup (UPS), security access, data integrity requirements
  • High URS references applicable standards: USP <659>, EU GDP 2013, 21 CFR Part 211, product-specific storage requirements
  • High FMEA or equivalent risk assessment performed to identify Critical Aspects and highest-risk failure modes
  • High Qualification Plan (QP) approved defining scope, schedule, responsibilities, and acceptance criteria methodology
  • Standard Requirements Traceability Matrix (RTM) created linking URS requirements to IQ/OQ/PQ test cases
  • Standard Measurement Uncertainty (MU) budget established for mapping sensors and monitoring sensors
  • Standard Guard-banding methodology documented and approved (showing how MU tightens acceptance criteria)

Phase 2: Factory Acceptance Test (FAT) & Site Acceptance Test (SAT)  |  階段二：廠驗與現場驗收

  • Critical FAT protocol approved and executed at vendor site before equipment shipment
  • Critical FAT verifies: temperature performance within specification, alarm functionality, controller operation, documentation package (manuals, calibration certificates, drawings)
  • High FAT includes temperature uniformity test (empty chamber) to confirm factory performance matches URS
  • High All FAT deviations documented and resolved (or accepted with risk assessment) before shipment approval
  • High SAT protocol executed after installation at CDMO site, before IQ initiation
  • Standard SAT confirms equipment arrived undamaged and performs as per FAT results in the CDMO environment
  • Standard SAT includes initial alarm test and basic temperature uniformity spot check

Phase 3: Installation Qualification (IQ)  |  階段三：安裝認證

  • Critical IQ Protocol approved before execution; includes all test cases traced to URS requirements
  • Critical Equipment identity verified: serial numbers, model numbers, specifications match URS and purchase order
  • Critical Calibration certificates verified for all installed instruments (temperature sensors, RH sensors, pressure gauges)
  • High Installation drawings (P&IDs, electrical diagrams) match as-built configuration
  • High Utility connections verified (electrical supply, drainage, network for BMS)
  • High Safety systems verified: over-temperature protection, low-temperature alarm, door alarm, power failure alarm
  • High UPS / backup power system tested and confirmed operational
  • Standard Spare parts list documented; critical spares procured and available
  • Standard SOPs for operation, cleaning, and emergency response referenced in IQ
  • Standard IQ Report completed and approved by QA before OQ initiation

Phase 4: Operational Qualification (OQ) — Mapping Studies  |  階段四：運行認證與溫度映射

  • Critical OQ Protocol approved; mapping sensor number and placement justified (minimum per chamber size and type)
  • Critical Mapping sensors calibrated to traceable standard within 12 months; calibration certificates current
  • Critical Empty chamber mapping completed at setpoint temperature — minimum study duration met (typically ≥72 hours; ≥ 3× thermal equilibrium time)
  • Critical Worst-case conditions tested: maximum door-opening frequency, maximum ambient temperature (summer conditions), minimum ambient temperature (winter conditions) where applicable
  • Critical Guard-banded acceptance criteria met: all mapped locations within (setpoint ± tolerance − MU) throughout study
  • High Worst-case locations identified from empty chamber mapping data (hottest and coldest points)
  • High Alarm and alert setpoints tested and confirmed functional at correct thresholds
  • High Power failure simulation: CTC maintains temperature within spec for minimum required duration on backup power
  • High Data logger performance verified: sampling interval, memory capacity, alarm transmission tested
  • Standard Statistical analysis completed: min, max, mean, standard deviation for each sensor location reported
  • Standard OQ Report completed and approved by QA before PQ initiation

Phase 5: Performance Qualification (PQ) — Loaded Conditions  |  階段五：性能認證（滿載條件）

  • Critical PQ Protocol approved; representative product load configuration documented and justified
  • Critical Loaded chamber mapping completed with product load ≥ minimum expected operating capacity
  • Critical Worst-case load scenario tested (maximum product mass, densest packing, worst air circulation configuration)
  • Critical All acceptance criteria (guard-banded) met under loaded conditions
  • High Worst-case locations confirmed or updated based on loaded mapping results
  • High Monitoring sensor placement confirmed/adjusted based on PQ worst-case location data
  • High Product temperature response time tested: how long does product take to warm up if door is left open or power fails
  • Standard PQ Report completed and approved by QA; qualification status formally declared
  • Standard Post-qualification SOPs updated: storage procedures, loading limits, alarm response, excursion management

Phase 6: Ongoing Monitoring & Continuous Qualification State  |  階段六：持續監控與認證狀態維持

  • Critical Continuous temperature monitoring system operational and recording at specified interval (typically ≤5 min)
  • Critical Monitoring sensors calibrated on schedule (typically every 6–12 months); calibration SOP followed
  • Critical All temperature excursions documented in deviation system; investigated and closed within SOP timeline
  • Critical MKT calculated for all excursions; product impact assessment documented before release or rejection decision
  • High Alarm response SOP in place: defined escalation, response time, corrective action steps
  • High BMS/monitoring system subject to periodic access review (who can acknowledge alarms, modify setpoints)
  • High Backup/contingency plan documented: what to do when CTC fails (alternative storage, emergency contacts, product transfer SOP)
  • Standard Temperature trend reports generated and reviewed quarterly (or per SOP frequency)
  • Standard Calibration drift analysis performed at each calibration cycle; out-of-tolerance instruments trigger retrospective review

Phase 7: Periodic Review & Requalification Triggers  |  階段七：定期審查與重新認證觸發

  • Critical Annual (or defined-frequency) periodic review completed and documented
  • Critical Periodic review covers: deviation history, calibration compliance, maintenance records, change history, trend analysis
  • Critical Formal conclusion documented: "qualification status maintained" or "requalification required"
  • High Requalification triggered by: major repair, relocation, refrigerant change, control system replacement, persistent alarm trends, or failed periodic review
  • High Change control programme covers all modifications to CTC system (equipment, SOPs, alarm setpoints, load configuration)
  • High All changes assessed for impact on qualification status before implementation
  • Standard SME (Subject Matter Expert) review obtained for all qualification-impacting changes
  • Standard Requalification scope risk-assessed: full or partial remapping based on nature and extent of change

Multi-Market Compliance Strategy  |  多市場合規策略

For CDMOs serving multiple markets, a single qualification programme aligned with the most stringent applicable requirements typically satisfies all markets simultaneously. The EU GDP 2013 + WHO TRS 961 combination covers most global market expectations. Adding 21 CFR Part 11 compliance for the monitoring system addresses FDA requirements. USP <659> acceptance criteria align with both EU and US pharmacopeial expectations.

推薦策略：以 EU GDP 2013 + WHO TRS 961 + USP <659> + 21 CFR Part 11 為設計基準，一套認證程序即可滿足歐盟、美國、WHO 預認證市場的要求。

Question 1 — Mapping Methodology & Worst-Case Design  |  問題一：映射方法論與最差情況設計

Scenario: Your CDMO operates a 200 m² cold room (2–8°C) that stores biopharmaceutical drug products and biological starting materials. The room has been in use for 3 years with a monitoring system but has never been formally mapped. An EU GDP inspector is scheduled in 6 months. You are the Validation Manager tasked with designing and executing the first comprehensive temperature mapping study.

情境：您的 CDMO 有一個 200 m² 的冷藏室（2–8°C），用於儲存生物製劑藥品和生物起始材料。該冷藏室使用 3 年，有監控系統但從未進行過正式的溫度映射。EU GDP 稽查員將在 6 個月後到訪。作為驗證經理，您負責設計和執行首次全面性溫度映射研究。

  1. Sensor Planning: Using ISPE CTC guidance, determine the minimum number of mapping sensors required for a 200 m² walk-in cold room. Where would you place them, and how would you justify your placement rationale? What are the mandatory locations that must always be included?
    感測器規劃：使用 ISPE CTC 指南確定 200 m² 步入式冷藏室所需的最少映射感測器數量。您如何放置它們並論證放置原理？哪些是必須包含的強制性位置？
  2. Worst-Case Conditions: List all worst-case test conditions that must be included in your OQ mapping protocol. For each condition, explain what makes it a worst case and how you would simulate it in the 200 m² cold room.
    最差情況條件：列出必須包含在 OQ 映射協議中的所有最差情況測試條件。對於每種情況，解釋什麼使其成為最差情況，以及如何在 200 m² 冷藏室中模擬它。
  3. Measurement Uncertainty: Your mapping data logger has a specified accuracy of ±0.3°C. Describe how you would calculate the total Measurement Uncertainty (MU) for the mapping system, and how you would apply guard-banding to the 2–8°C acceptance criteria. Show the mathematical relationship between MU and guard-banded limits.
    測量不確定度：您的映射數據記錄器具有 ±0.3°C 的規定精度。描述如何計算映射系統的總測量不確定度（MU），以及如何將保護區間應用於 2–8°C 驗收標準。展示 MU 與保護區間限制之間的數學關係。
  4. Inspection Readiness: The EU GDP inspector specifically asks: "Show me your temperature mapping data and explain how you identified the worst-case monitoring sensor location." Walk through the complete chain of evidence you would present, from the mapping protocol to the monitoring sensor placement justification.
    稽查準備：EU GDP 稽查員具體詢問：「給我看您的溫度映射數據，並解釋您如何確定最差情況監控感測器位置。」請說明您將呈現的完整證據鏈，從映射協議到監控感測器放置論證。

Model Answer Framework  |  參考答案框架

  • Sensors: ISPE recommends ≥1 sensor per 10 m³ volume (or minimum per chamber type). For 200 m² × ~3m height ≈ 600 m³: minimum ~15–20 mapping sensors. Mandatory locations: near evaporator, near door(s), corners at 3 heights (top/middle/bottom), centre of room, near air curtain return. Total grid typically 3D with horizontal and vertical coverage.
  • Worst-case conditions: (1) Empty chamber at setpoint; (2) Maximum door-opening frequency (simulated operational use); (3) Maximum ambient temperature (summer — highest expected external temperature); (4) Minimum ambient temperature (winter — risk of freezing near walls); (5) Power failure simulation; (6) Loaded chamber at maximum product density (PQ stage).
  • MU calculation: Total MU = √(u_sensor² + u_calibration² + u_resolution² + u_drift²). If sensor accuracy = ±0.3°C, apply expanded uncertainty U = k × MU (k=2 for 95% confidence). Guard-banded limits = [2 + U] to [8 − U] = [2.3°C to 7.7°C] if U = 0.3°C.
  • Evidence chain: Approved mapping protocol → raw data from all sensor locations → worst-case analysis report identifying hottest/coldest locations → monitoring sensor placement justification document → current calibration certificate for monitoring sensor → ongoing monitoring SOP referencing worst-case location.

Question 2 — Temperature Excursion Investigation & MKT Assessment  |  問題二：溫度偏差調查與 MKT 評估

Scenario: At 02:30 on a Monday morning, the BMS generates an alarm for Cold Room B (2–8°C, storing 500 units of a monoclonal antibody drug product with a shelf life of 18 months). The monitoring sensor reads 11.2°C. The on-call technician arrives at 03:15 and confirms the door seal gasket has failed. The room is restored to 5°C by 04:45. Review of the continuous monitoring data shows:

  • 02:00 — 7.8°C (last in-spec reading)
  • 02:30 — 9.1°C (first alarm reading)
  • 03:00 — 10.8°C
  • 03:15 — 11.2°C (peak)
  • 03:45 — 10.1°C (technician repairs seal, room starts cooling)
  • 04:45 — 5.3°C (room restored to specification)

情境：週一凌晨 2:30，BMS 對冷藏室 B（2–8°C，儲存 500 單位單株抗體藥品，保存期限 18 個月）發出警報。監控感測器顯示 11.2°C。值班技術人員在 3:15 到達並確認門封橡膠墊失效。冷藏室在 4:45 恢復至 5°C。連續監控數據顯示上述溫度記錄。

  1. Immediate Response: What immediate actions should the on-call technician take upon arriving and confirming the excursion? What documentation must be initiated within the first hour? Which SOPs are activated?
    即時響應：值班技術人員到達並確認偏差後應立即採取哪些行動？在第一個小時內必須啟動哪些文件？啟動哪些 SOP？
  2. MKT Calculation: Using the temperature-time data above and the Arrhenius equation framework described in the ISPE guide, outline how you would calculate the Mean Kinetic Temperature for this excursion. What product stability information would you need to complete the impact assessment? If the product's approved storage condition allows up to 8°C MKT, would this batch likely be acceptable?
    MKT 計算：使用上述溫度-時間數據和 ISPE 指南中描述的 Arrhenius 方程框架，概述如何計算此次偏差的平均動能溫度。完成影響評估需要哪些產品穩定性信息？如果產品的批准儲存條件允許 MKT 高達 8°C，這批產品是否可能被接受？
  3. Root Cause & CAPA: What is the most likely root cause of this excursion? Design a CAPA with at least 3 corrective actions and 2 preventive actions. How would you assess the effectiveness of each CAPA action?
    根本原因與 CAPA：此次偏差最可能的根本原因是什麼？設計一個至少包含 3 個糾正措施和 2 個預防措施的 CAPA。如何評估每個 CAPA 措施的有效性？
  4. Regulatory Reporting: Under EU GDP 2013, is this excursion reportable to any regulatory authority? Under what circumstances would a temperature excursion trigger a regulatory notification or Product Quality Review update? What would you include in the client notification for the 500 units stored in this cold room?
    法規報告：根據 EU GDP 2013，此次偏差是否需要向任何監管機關報告？在什麼情況下溫度偏差會觸發法規通知或產品品質回顧更新？您會在對儲存在此冷藏室的 500 單位的客戶通知中包含哪些內容？

Key Concepts to Address  |  解答關鍵概念

  • Excursion duration: ~2h45min from first out-of-spec to restoration — this is the critical window for impact assessment
  • MKT framework: Short duration at relatively low excursion temperature (11.2°C max) — MKT calculation likely shows cumulative thermal load well within specification. The Arrhenius equation weights higher temperatures exponentially, so a 2.75-hour excursion to 11°C contributes relatively little to total MKT over the product's storage lifetime
  • Root cause: Door seal gasket failure — mechanical failure of containment. Secondary failure: alarm response time (02:30 alarm → 03:15 arrival = 45 min delay)
  • CAPA examples: Corrective: immediate door gasket replacement; review of all cold room door seals in facility; retrospective review of alarm response times. Preventive: add door seal inspection to monthly PM checklist; revise on-call alarm response SOP to require acknowledgement within 15 minutes; install redundant door seal integrity sensor
  • EU GDP reporting: Not typically reportable to authority for isolated facility equipment failure with product retained in quarantine — but must be documented in the quality system, investigated, CAPAed, and included in GDP periodic review and Annual Product Quality Review (APQR)

Question 3 — Guide Integration & Lifecycle Strategy for a New CDMO CTC Programme  |  問題三：新 CDMO CTC 計畫的指南整合與生命週期策略

Scenario: You have just been appointed as the Head of Validation at a growing aseptic fill-finish CDMO. The company currently has: (1) 2 stability chambers (25°C/60%RH and 40°C/75%RH), (2) 1 refrigerator (2–8°C, 500L), (3) 1 -20°C freezer (200L), and (4) 1 walk-in cold room (2–8°C, 50 m²).   1. Prioritisation Strategy: Using a risk-based approach (referencing ICH Q9 and ISPE CTC guidance), in what order would you prioritise the qualification of the 5 CTCs listed above?   2. Reference & Standards Alignment: From Appendix 8 of the ISPE CTC Guide, identify which 6 references are most critical for your qualification programme. For each, explain specifically how it applies to at least one of the 5 CTCs in your facility.
    參考文獻與標準對應：從 ISPE CTC 指南的附錄 8 中，識別對您的認證計畫最關鍵的 6 個參考文獻。對每個文獻，具體說明它如何適用於您設施中 5 個 CTC 中的至少一個。
  3. Glossary Application: Five ISPE CTC Guide terms will be central to the qualification protocols you write. For each of the following terms, write one sentence showing how it applies to your specific facility context: (a) URS, (b) Worst-Case Location, (c) MKT, (d) Guard-Banding, (e) Periodic Review.
    術語應用：五個 ISPE CTC 指南術語將是您撰寫認證協議的核心。對以下每個術語，寫一句話說明它如何適用於您的具體設施環境：(a) URS、(b) 最差位置、(c) MKT、(d) 保護區間、(e) 定期審查。
  4. Programme Architecture: Design the complete document architecture for your CTC qualification programme. List all the SOPs, protocols, reports, and ongoing monitoring procedures you would need to create. Organise them by lifecycle phase (URS → Qualification → Operations → Periodic Review). Prioritisation Framework  |  優先化框架參考答案

  • Priority 1 — Walk-in cold room (2–8°C, 50 m²): Highest volume storage; stores drug products; highest regulatory exposure (EU GDP); largest risk to product quality if unqualified. Estimated timeline: URS + IQ/OQ = 3 months; PQ = additional 1 month.
  • Priority 2 — Refrigerator (2–8°C, 500L): Second cold storage asset; may store drug products or reference standards; same regulatory requirements as cold room but simpler qualification. Timeline: 2 months total.
  • Priority 3 — Stability chambers (25°C/60%RH and 40°C/75%RH): Critical for ICH Q1A stability studies; supports product registration; temperature + humidity dual parameter qualification. Timeline: 2 months each (can run in parallel).
  • Priority 4 — Freezer (-20°C, 200L): Important but typically lower volume; low immediate regulatory pressure if used for non-critical materials. Timeline: 2 months.
  • Overall programme: ~10–12 months to complete all 5 CTCs with QA review and report approval cycles. Budget for external mapping sensor rental (calibrated equipment) and potential consultant support for gap assessment.

Short Answer Keys  |  簡答參考

  • Q4.1: Cold = ≤8°C (refrigerator: 2–8°C); CCT = maintained at 2–8°C but allows 0–15°C excursions if MKT ≤8°C. CCT is more relevant for distribution scenarios (loading docks, shipping legs) where brief excursions are unavoidable but product integrity can still be demonstrated via MKT.
  • Q4.2: The controller sensor is placed for equipment control accuracy (near the evaporator or return air), not to represent the worst product temperature. If the monitoring sensor is co-located with the controller, it will show the best-case temperature rather than the temperature the product actually experiences. This could mask excursions at the worst-case location where product is stored.
  • Q4.3: The tighter criteria reflect application of Measurement Uncertainty (MU) guard-banding per ISO/IEC Guide 98-3 (GUM). Our mapping sensors have a combined MU of ±0.5°C. To ensure that any measurement within our acceptance window guarantees the true product temperature is within USP limits, we apply guard-banding: if MU = ±0.5°C, acceptance criteria = [2 + 0.5] to [8 − 0.5] = 2.5°C to 7.5°C.
  • Q4.4: Requalification triggers: (1) Major equipment repair (e.g. compressor replacement); (2) Relocation of the CTC; (3) Significant change to load configuration or storage capacity; (4) Replacement of control or monitoring system; (5) Recurring or unresolved temperature excursions; (6) Failed periodic review; (7) Significant facility modification affecting ambient temperature or air circulation; (8) Change of product type with different temperature sensitivity stored in the chamber.
