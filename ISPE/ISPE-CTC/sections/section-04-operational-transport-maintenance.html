Section 10.1 — Challenging the Area with Operational Activities | 以操作活動挑戰受測區域

ISPE Source Text

10.1 Challenging the Area with Operational Activities

The area being mapped should be challenged with specific known operational activities during the thermal mapping process. These activities should simulate normal processes and functions that would occur for routine operations, such as opening interior doors, opening exterior doors (Receiving Dock Doors, Shipping Dock Doors), running of equipment (e.g., thermo-formers, stretch wrappers, forklifts, conveyors) etc. where there is a potential impact to the conditions.

By performing these functions and operating equipment during the execution of the mapping studies, cause and effect relationships can be identified for the thermal profile.

Operational activities to simulate during mapping include:

  • Opening interior and exterior doors (dock doors, personnel doors)
  • Running warehouse equipment: forklifts, conveyors, stretch wrappers
  • Simulating loading and unloading cycles
  • Normal personnel traffic through the space

Note on Cause and Effect

The primary purpose of operational challenges is to capture the actual thermal profile experienced during real production — not an idealized empty-room baseline. A mapping study that omits these activities may underestimate temperature variability and produce specifications that cannot be met during routine operation.

中文解析 & CDMO應用

操作挑戰與熱圖譜映射

核心概念：操作活動挑戰

熱映射研究不只是在空置、靜態的設備中放置感測器。ISPE要求在模擬真實生產條件下執行映射，以暴露潛在的溫度變化因子。

這等同於在「最接近實際使用場景」的條件下驗證設備性能。

CDMO共用冷藏室裝載風險場景

在CDMO環境中，冷藏室通常同時存放來自多個客戶的產品，裝載風險更為複雜：

  • 場景1：多客戶同時存取 — 不同班次的操作員頻繁進出，若無vinyl curtain，每次開門都會造成熱侵入
  • 場景2：大批量裝載進入 — 大量托盤同時裝入可能暫時阻擋冷風循環，形成局部熱點
  • 場景3：叉車進入造成氣流擾動 — 叉車引擎散熱 + 門口開啟同時發生，放大溫度衝擊
  • 場景4：非工作時間的最低頻率開門 — 凌晨班次進出頻率不同，代表最差情況(worst case)未必在白天

提示：思考「驗證的場景」與「實際操作的場景」之間的差距（validation gap）對產品安全性的影響。

Section 10.2 — Open Door Recovery Test | 開門恢復測試

ISPE Source Text

10.2 Open Door Recovery Test

An example of a typical operational activity is to examine recovery time for the internal chamber temperature after opening the access door and simulating loading/unloading. It is recommended to determine the time that the chamber door may remain fully open before the monitoring sensors are no longer within the acceptable range.

This test can also help to determine how quickly the monitoring sensor recovers and returns to the normal operating temperature range.

Recommended Test Conditions

  • Use a minimum (simulated) load or no load — worst case with no thermal mass
  • Locate temperature sensors as recommended for thermal mapping, with special attention to sensors nearest the door
  • Set data acquisition rate to 1-minute intervals due to uncertainty in test duration

Acceptance Criteria

Generally, there are no specific acceptance criteria for this test unless a recovery time is specified in the unit manufacturer's specifications. The key output is a data-supported understanding of the chamber's tolerance for door-open events.

Special Case: Walk-in with Vinyl Curtains

For larger walk-in CTCs fitted with vinyl curtains, the units may be able to maintain internal conditions indefinitely, as the impact of passing through curtains is short-term and negligible. Vinyl curtains should be well-fitted, rest on the unit walls, and sit just clear of the floor.

中文解析 & CDMO應用

開門恢復測試詳解

為何選擇「最小/無負載」作為最差情況？

熱質量(thermal mass)越大，設備抵抗溫度波動的能力越強。若測試時使用空倉（最小熱質量），任何熱侵入都會被快速放大，反映最差情況。

這與工廠驗收測試(FAT/SAT)的邏輯一致：在最容易失敗的條件下驗證，通過才代表真正的品質保證。

開門緩衝時間 (Buffering Time) — 操作意義

開門恢復測試的關鍵輸出是開門允許時間上限，這個數字必須轉化為：

  • SOP中明確規定的「最大開門時間」
  • 作業員訓練的考核項目
  • CAPA追蹤的偏差觸發條件

如果SOP只寫「盡速關門」而沒有具體時間限制，等同於沒有控制。

Section 10.3 — Power Outage / Thermal Degradation Test | 停電/熱降解測試

ISPE Source Text

10.3 Power Outage / Thermal Degradation Test

Another type of test that may be helpful from a business continuity, disaster recovery, and operational perspective is a simulated Power Outage Test. This type of test would measure the amount of time the Controlled Temperature Chamber remained within the desired temperature range once electrical power was turned off.

Note: although it is expected the Controlled Temperature Chamber will have redundant and backup power supplies, the results from this test would provide data to define how quickly materials need to be moved to another location if the backup power systems failed. Doors to the storage chamber should remain closed during this test.

Recommended Test Setup

  • Use a minimum (simulated) load or no load in the CTC (worst case)
  • Perform as commissioning activity referenced in OQ before the storage unit is put into use, if possible
  • Locate temperature sensors as recommended for thermal mapping
  • Data acquisition rate: 1-minute intervals

Additional Operational Value

Once power is restored, the amount of time required to return to the appropriate required temperature can be of value for disaster recovery planning — specifically, the elapsed time from when power is restored until the last temperature sensor returns to the acceptable range.

Note: This test was previously included in the USP, but has subsequently been removed.

Sections 10.4–10.5 — Calibration Strategies, Access Control & Dehumidification | 校準策略、進出管制與除濕

ISPE Source Text

Calibration and Calibration Strategies

There are benefits to be derived from a considered calibration strategy:

  • Refrigeration system instruments: Many can only be calibrated when the system is emptied for major maintenance — therefore, they are calibrated following manufacturer recommendations
  • Control sensor: Can be verified at a single point, as the control functions are usually based on whether the system is above or below the operating set point
  • Monitoring sensors: Should be calibrated across the operating range, as they provide the data to support quality determinations in support of product quality

Access Control

Areas where product is stored should have a system to prevent unauthorized access. This system can be part of a site keycard system, or use a lock and key with procedural controls which identify who holds, and is entitled to use, the key.

Dehumidification

Dehumidification should be considered for a cold room or freezer. The life of the equipment can be extended where there are fewer defrost cycles required (reduced expansion/contraction). Dehumidification can also reduce the risk of ice forming on a freezer floor, which presents a safety hazard.

For large distribution facilities it may be worth considering the layout, and providing access to a walk-in freezer through a cold room, in order to reduce moisture in the freezer.

Equipment Durability

When specifying a new facility, it is worthwhile defining the system design life — the expected operating lifespan without significant maintenance. High quality refrigeration systems can have an expected life of 12 to 15 years. Standby systems are usually installed for an installation holding material of a significant commercial value.

中文解析 & CDMO應用

校準策略與進出管制深度解析

三層校準策略框架

關鍵原則：監測感測器的校準標準高於控制感測器，因為監測數據是產品放行的依據。

校準召回追蹤系統 (Calibration Recall System)

一個有效的校準召回系統應包含：

  • 儀器清冊(Asset Register)：每個感測器均有唯一ID、位置、校準週期、上次校準日期
  • 到期提醒：提前30天發送警示給校準工程師和QA
  • 逾期鎖定：超過校準有效期的感測器自動標記，其數據不得用於放行決策
  • 回溯評估：感測器超過校準期間若有偏差，需評估期間所有產品的影響
  • 替換驗證：校準後感測器重新安裝需確認讀值與同位置其他感測器的一致性

進出管制的GMP意義

進出管制不只是安全問題，也是GMP合規問題。未授權進入可能導致：

  • 產品被未記錄的方式取出（影響庫存完整性）
  • 不當操作造成溫度偏差（門被長時間打開）
  • 訪客接觸未貼標籤產品（交叉污染風險）

FDA 21 CFR Part 211.68 和 EU GMP Annex 15均要求儲存區域有適當的進出控制。

Sections 11.1 — Active Shipping Containers: Unit Load Devices (ULD) | 主動式運輸容器：單位裝載設備

ISPE Source Text

11.1 Active Shipping Containers: Unit Load Devices

Currently there are two types of Unit Load Devices (ULD) systems suitable for the shipping of temperature sensitive product, designed for use in aircraft. They are powered to either cool or heat the air within the container.

System 1 (TCCU) Key Features

  • Maintains 5°C (41°F) for 72 hours at 30°C (86°F) ambient
  • Handles brief ambient excursions from -40°C to +60°C
  • GPS/GSM module for real-time location and temperature transmission
  • Two modes: recharge mode (connected to AC) and transport mode (24VDC battery)
  • Interior designed for 40×48 inch pallet, up to 500 kg product

System 2 Key Features

  • Maintains 5°C for 30 hours at -10°C to +30°C ambient
  • NiMH batteries; three cooling compressors with heaters
  • Supports US and EURO pallet sizes, up to 953 kg
  • LED alarm indicators: system on (green), charge (blue), alarm (red), alert (yellow)
  • Alert triggers: battery <30%, inside temp out of spec, ambient temp out of spec, compressor failure

11.1.3 Qualification Approaches

Two possible approaches for active shipping containers:

  • Unit Qualification: Map each individual unit; confirm satisfactory operation; qualify service supplier for maintenance and calibration quality systems
  • Supplier Assessment: (1) Qualify the manufacturer (design + factory testing); (2) Qualify service supplier (maintenance + calibration quality systems). Usually paired with an independent temperature logger per shipment.

Regardless of approach, loading and unloading methods must be qualified and procedurally defined. When unloading, shipment should be placed into quarantine until data logger confirms conditions were maintained. If out-of-conditions, a Non-Conformance must be raised and the implications investigated.

主動式ULD運輸容器資格認證

單機資格 vs. 供應商評估：選擇邏輯

  • 選單機資格：當ULD由同一家供應商長期租用，數量固定，可針對每台設備建立獨立的資格文件。適合高價值/高風險產品。
  • 選供應商評估：當ULD數量龐大或頻繁更換，個別映射不切實際。透過審核製造商設計和服務商維護品質，輔以隨貨數據記錄器作為補充保證。
  • 關鍵補充措施：無論採哪種方法，每次運輸都應附帶獨立的溫度記錄器，這是最後一道確認機制。

冷鏈交接點 (Hand-off Points) 風險

從設施到最終目的地的冷鏈中，最脆弱的環節通常是交接點：

  • 設施倉庫 → 快遞取貨：產品在裝卸區暴露時間
  • 機場地面操作：從貨倉到飛機貨艙的轉移時間
  • 轉機點：兩段航班之間的等待時間
  • 目的地機場 → 客戶設施：最後一哩配送

每個交接點都應有明確的溫度確認程序和責任分工協議(Quality Agreement)。

Sections 11.2–11.5 — Temperature Controlled Trailers: Design, Insulation & Validation | 溫控拖車：設計、隔熱與驗證

ISPE Source Text

11.2 Temperature Controlled Trailers

Temperature controlled over-the-road trailers can be used to move significant amounts of temperature sensitive pharmaceutical product. Trailers have a different set of environmental and usage profiles when compared to in situ custom-built CTCs:

  • Loading and unloading operations
  • Defrost cycles
  • Loading patterns

Moisture, as a degradation element, is much more prevalent in trailers due to the operational profile — continuous running for several days, followed by periods of downtime when the trailer is empty. Rigorous inspection of each trailer should be performed prior to loading.

11.3 Trailer Design

Product loaded into the trailer should be equilibrated to the appropriate temperature before loading. During transit, as heat or cold penetrates the walls, roof, and floor, circulating airflows capture the environmental heat or cold and condition it to the desired temperature in the HVAC unit on the front of the trailer.

Depending on the geographical area, manufacturers build and test trailers to meet standards of one of two organizations:

  • North America: American Trucking Association (ATA) Technology & Maintenance Council (TMC)
  • Europe: ATP Agreement on International Carriage of Perishable Foodstuffs

TMC / ATP Classification Table

11.4 Insulation Degradation

Polyurethane foam insulation gradually loses its blowing agent gas (CO₂ and other gases) over time, which is replaced with air and water vapor. This transition occurs slowly over years and is normal. The temperature control unit is sized to assure a useful life of 15 years with timely maintenance.

11.5 Trailer Validation Approach

The validation exercise is focused on loading patterns and assuring that proper airflow can be achieved under conditions of heat and cold. Loading practices that block airflow impede heat addition/removal and result in product temperatures that may not be controlled within the required range. A written protocol should document temperatures nearest the product during seasonal extremes.

中文解析 & CDMO應用

溫控拖車驗證策略與CTC比較

比喻：為什麼F級拖車成功也意味著DF級會成功？

ISPE指出，在F級拖車上成功完成的驗證研究，也意味著DF級(深冷凍)拖車同樣適用。

這就像汽車安全測試：如果一輛配備標準安全設備的車通過了碰撞測試，那麼裝備更多安全功能的高配版車款自然也能通過 — 因為F級是市場主流且絕緣能力更低，通過代表更高標準的DF也能達標。

隔熱衰退的實務意義

聚氨酯泡沫的隔熱性能會隨時間下降（發泡劑氣體逸散 → 空氣和水蒸氣取代），這意味著：

  • 新拖車的初始性能 ≠ 使用5–10年後的性能
  • 驗證研究應考慮設備年齡和使用歷史
  • 定期熱掃描(thermal scanning)可早期發現隔熱劣化
  • 質量協議應要求承運商定期提供拖車維護記錄

Section 11.6 — Trailer Loading Patterns & Inspectional Criteria | 拖車裝載模式與檢查標準

ISPE Source Text

11.6 Trailer Loading Patterns

Loading patterns in a well maintained truck are the key determinant in assuring even product temperature within the trailer. Key requirements:

  • Product should be loaded on pallets that allow free airflow the length of the trailer
  • Product should not be in contact with the walls of the trailer
  • Double stacking of refrigerated goods should be carefully considered, as airflow distribution in the rear of the trailer may not be sufficient to maintain uniform temperature under extreme conditions
  • Product should be equilibrated to the appropriate temperature before loading

11.6.1 Inspectional Criteria

The Quality Agreement between the carrier and the company should define the shared responsibilities of inspection. The following items should be inspected for each use:

  • Air distribution chute intact
  • Air supply, air returns and floor drains clear of debris
  • Repairs to interior and exterior surfaces (damage from forklifts etc.)
  • Door seals intact
  • General cleanliness (visual inspection)
中文解析 & CDMO應用

裝載模式對溫度均一性的關鍵影響

裝載模式的三大原則

  • 原則1 — 氣流暢通：托盤之間必須保留足夠間距讓冷空氣循環，阻擋氣流等同於阻擋溫度調節能力
  • 原則2 — 不接觸側壁：拖車側壁是熱交換最強的區域（尤其夏季），接觸側壁的產品最容易超出溫度範圍
  • 原則3 — 預冷後才裝載：裝載未冷卻至目標溫度的產品，等同於在拖車內引入一個熱源，增加冷機負擔

每次使用前的拖車檢查清單

  • 空氣分配導管(air distribution chute)完整無損
  • 送風口、回風口、地板排水孔無堵塞
  • 內外側壁無叉車造成的穿孔或凹陷
  • 門封條(door seals)完整，無裂縫或破損
  • 整體清潔度符合要求（無異物、無前次貨物殘留）
  • 冷機組(reefer unit)正常運轉，設定點正確
  • 拖車內部溫度已達到目標範圍
  • TMC/ATP認證標牌可見且在有效期內

Sections 12.1–12.4 — Coils, Condensate, Functional Testing & Leak Testing | 盤管、冷凝管、功能測試與洩漏測試

ISPE Source Text

Chapter 12 Maintenance — Overview

The supplier of the refrigeration equipment should provide general recommendations for the maintenance of the equipment. These recommendations may not recognize the high value of the stored goods in this application. The potential impact on the maintenance program should be assessed.

12.1 Cleaning of the Coils

The coils (unit cooler and air cooled condenser) should be cleaned regularly and checked for any damage to ensure that performance levels are maintained. Damaged or distorted fins should be straightened with a suitable comb. This inspection should also ensure that there is no corrosion of the fins.

Regular inspection should also confirm that the defrost cycle is effective (where applicable), especially if the cycle is adjusted to account for seasonal variation.

General Cleaning: Dry cleaning is preferred, in order to minimize risk of contamination. Regular inspection is recommended to ensure that there is no unseen microbial/fungicidal growth.

12.2 Check the Condensate Pipework

The condensate pipework should be checked to ensure that it is not damaged and that it has the correct fall and flows freely. Trace heating or insulation also should be checked. Periodic verification during operation is also recommended; this should ensure that the pipe supports are adequate and have not loosened.

12.3 Functional Testing of Unit Coolers / Defrost Elements

There is usually no feedback from the solenoid valve on the unit coolers, so the only way of checking their correct operation is to carry out, periodically, a functional check to verify the performance of each unit cooler.

12.4 Leak Testing

  • Periodically check pipe runs and equipment for leaks using electronic test devices
  • Check refrigerant fluid level and pressure against system specification — early detection may help avoid system failure
  • Keep a record of refrigerant used for the system (may be mandatory depending on region)
  • Equipment rooms may be fitted with leakage detectors/alarms — these should be calibrated and checked regularly
  • End Users should be aware of local regulatory requirements
中文解析 & CDMO應用

盤管、冷凝管與洩漏測試詳解

關鍵洞察：製造商建議 ≠ GMP要求

ISPE明確指出，設備製造商的維護建議通常不考慮高價值藥品儲存的特殊需求。這意味著：

  • 製造商建議可能是基於食品或一般工業應用
  • GMP環境下的維護頻率和標準必須由使用方（CDMO）自行評估和設定
  • 維護計畫必須考慮「若此設備失效，對產品品質的影響是什麼？」

這正是風險基礎的PM計畫的基礎：設備關鍵性 → 決定維護頻率和深度。

盤管清潔的微生物風險

ISPE推薦乾式清潔(dry cleaning)而非濕式清潔，原因是：

  • 濕式清潔引入水分，可能在盤管翼片(fins)上形成生物膜(biofilm)滋生環境
  • 冷藏室的低溫並非無菌環境 — 某些嗜冷菌(psychrotrophic bacteria)和黴菌在2–8°C下仍可緩慢生長
  • 微生物污染的盤管可能通過氣流將孢子傳播至儲存產品的環境

冷媒洩漏記錄的法規要求

許多地區對冷媒使用和洩漏有強制性記錄要求：

  • 歐盟 F-Gas Regulation (EU 517/2014)：含氟冷媒超過一定量需定期洩漏檢查並記錄
  • EPA Section 608 (美國)：ODS冷媒洩漏超過閾值需修復並記錄
  • GMP視角：冷媒洩漏可能污染產品，且洩漏事件可能影響設備效能，進而影響儲存溫度

冷媒記錄(refrigerant log)應成為PM文件系統的一部分。

練習思考

Q：您的冷藏設備上次盤管清潔是什麼時候？清潔記錄是否包含了「清潔前/後目視檢查結果」以及清潔方法？如果不是乾式清潔，是否評估過微生物污染風險？

Sections 12.5–12.6 — Thermal Scanning & System Alarms | 熱掃描與系統警報

ISPE Source Text

12.5 Thermal Scanning

The panels, doors, door seals, and sealing of utility penetrations can degrade over time (foamed panels may be better in this respect than panels filled with mineral wool). A thermal scan of the installation will allow any degradation to be seen and appropriate action taken.

As a minimum, the penetrations should be visually inspected to ensure that they are sound with no leakage/ingress of condensation. For conduit penetrations, the internal sealing also should be checked to ensure that condensation is not leaking inside the conduit.

12.6 System Alarms

The entire alarm system should be periodically tested by direct or simulated temperature change against the specified alarm set points and proper functionality of audible and visual alarms at local and/or remote alarm stations (e.g., emails, phone calls, or text based alerts) should be confirmed and documented.

Regardless of the root cause, product losses from cold storage-related failures can be prevented with the proper design and operation of the alarm system, and proper End User response to an alarm event. Periodic testing of the alarm system should help to ensure that it will work when needed.

The system maintenance procedures can be used to provide a confirmation that the system performance remains satisfactory, supporting a reduced requirement for remapping where appropriate. The data from the monitoring system should be reviewed periodically, to look for any negative performance trends.

中文解析 & CDMO應用

熱掃描、警報系統與重新映射觸發條件

熱掃描(Thermal Scanning) — 非破壞性隔熱檢測

紅外熱成像(Infrared Thermography)可在不拆除板材的情況下，透過溫度差異識別隔熱劣化區域：

  • 正常隔熱：表面溫度均勻，無明顯熱橋
  • 隔熱劣化：面板某區域出現「熱點」，代表冷橋或隔熱材料受損
  • 門封條洩漏：門框周圍出現溫度梯度異常
  • 管道穿牆處：若密封不良，凝結水會沿管道滲入

建議：熱掃描應在設備滿載運行時進行，內外溫差最大，最容易發現問題。

警報測試的GMP要求

「定期測試警報」不只是技術要求，更是GMP合規要求。一個從未被測試的警報系統，在真正需要時可能完全失效。

  • 測試方式：直接溫度變化（模擬真實偏差）或受控模擬（注入測試信號）
  • 測試範圍：本地聲光警報 + 遠端通報（Email/SMS/Phone）
  • 文件要求：測試結果必須記錄，包含測試時間、觸發條件、通報到達時間
  • Response time目標：從警報觸發到有人確認，應有明確的期望時間

維護數據 → 減少重新映射需求

ISPE提供了一個重要的生命週期管理原則：如果系統維護記錄良好，且監測數據顯示性能穩定，可以減少重新熱映射的頻率。

這需要SME（主題專家）定期審查：

  • 監測數據趨勢 vs. 資格認證時的基準數據
  • 警報歷史記錄（是否出現新的偏差模式？）
  • 維護歷史（是否有影響性能的重大維修？）
  • 設備年齡和隔熱衰退評估

相反地，若出現以下情況，必須觸發重新映射：重大維修更換（壓縮機、冷凝器）、設備搬遷、性能數據顯示明顯趨勢偏移。

ISPE Source Text

PM Procedures by Equipment Type

CRT Storage Area (Warehouse)

  1. Inspect building envelope — no visible degradation of materials of construction or seals/curtains that would impact ingress of unconditioned air
  2. Review system alarm history since last maintenance (temperature monitoring system, HVAC temperature/humidity, airflow volume sensors) — look for negative performance trends
  3. Visually check HVAC system balance — verify balancing dampers remain in their as-commissioned/marked settings
  4. Check and clean heating/cooling coils if necessary
  5. Check control system records — confirm control valves are not operating at 100% (indicating performance limits or utility system issues)

Walk-in Refrigerators / Freezers

  1. Inspect system envelope — no visible degradation, door/window seals, door curtains, or air leakage paths (moisture or ice buildup)
  2. Review system alarm history (temperature monitoring + refrigeration system alarms) — note observations in maintenance record
  3. Check refrigeration system set point being maintained (e.g., compressor suction pressure)
  4. Review maintenance history to confirm:
    • Coils have been cleaned if needed
    • Fan operation has been checked
    • Operation of unit cooler control valves and thermal expansion valves checked
    • Defrost settings and operation verified (if applicable)

Standalone Refrigerators and Freezers

Same four-point checklist as walk-in units (envelope inspection, alarm history review, set point verification, maintenance history review for coils / fans / valves / defrost).

Performance Review by SME (Alternative to Full PM)

Where a system has multiple monitoring locations (control sensor + independent temperature monitor at hot/cold spots), the preventive maintenance is not required to confirm satisfactory unit operation in every cycle. Instead:

A review of current system performance data against the as-Qualified data by an SME can be used to confirm consistent system performance. This review should assess the thermal performance of unit insulation and the conditioning system, allowing the SME to determine if there has been a significant change in performance that may require maintenance activities, or if the system is still performing acceptably.

Chapters 10–12 Integrated Learning | 第10–12章整合學習

Key Messages from Chapters 10–12

Chapter 10 Core Takeaways

  • Operational realism: Mapping studies must include real operational activities — door openings, equipment operation, loading simulation — to be valid
  • Open door recovery: Test under empty (worst-case) conditions; 1-minute data acquisition; outputs inform SOP time limits
  • Power outage: Empty-chamber degradation data = the clock that governs your emergency response window
  • Calibration differentiation: Monitoring sensors need full-range multi-point calibration; control sensors only need single-point verification
  • Access control: Unauthorized access is a GMP deficiency, not just a security issue

Chapter 11 Core Takeaways

  • ULD qualification: Two approaches — individual unit mapping or supplier assessment system. Either way, an independent in-shipment logger is essential
  • Trailer as a CTC: Trailers are a mobile subclass of CTCs, with additional challenges from moisture, loading patterns, and road vibration
  • TMC/ATP standards: Industry standards provide a "family" qualification basis — an F-rated trailer study covers DF as well
  • Airflow is everything: Loading patterns that block airflow are the leading cause of temperature non-uniformity in trailers
  • Pre-shipment inspection: Every trailer, every time — document it per the Quality Agreement

Chapter 12 Core Takeaways

  • Manufacturer ≠ GMP: Equipment supplier maintenance recommendations do not account for high-value pharmaceutical storage — you must set your own risk-based standards
  • Dry clean coils: Moisture introduced during wet cleaning creates microbial growth risk in cold environments
  • Alarm testing is non-negotiable: Periodic testing of the full alarm pathway (local + remote) is a GMP requirement, not optional
  • Good maintenance can reduce re-mapping: Consistent PM + SME trend review can justify extended intervals between full thermal mapping studies
  • Major repairs trigger re-qualification: Compressor replacement, unit cooler change, controller replacement — all require re-assessment
