Chapter 12: Change Management — 變更管理

12.1 Introduction — 導論 | Pages 89–90

📖 Original Text (English)

12.1 Introduction

Change management processes should be established and applied throughout the lifecycle of manufacturing systems. The risk-based application of SME-led change management is one of the biggest challenges in the implementation of QRM-based C&Q as it applies the principles of ICH Q9. This Guide presents an approach which involves two modes of change management:

  • Engineering Change Management (ECM)
  • Quality Change Control (QCC)

Project change management that covers the elements of project scope, cost, and schedule is outside the scope of this Guide.

A system is considered to be changed when it is modified, altered, added to, removed, or improved in a way that makes its functions, physical features, or performance different from what they were prior to the change.

Replacing an existing part of a system with an identical one (like-for-like) may not be considered as a change. Organizations should define and follow a robust assessment process for classifying changes as like-for-like.

12.1.1 Purpose and Benefits

Change management is a documented program that:

  • Defines what is subject to change
  • Determines the risk for product impact caused by the change
  • Establishes a process for initiation, review, and approval of the request for change
  • Defines when the Quality Unit should be involved
  • Establishes a process for evaluation of the actual impact of the change

Change management is needed for the following reasons:

  • Ensures control of the impact on the current state of the system
  • Provides a basis for peer (SME) review and approval
  • Is a regulatory requirement (e.g., 21 CFR 211.100 and EU GMP Volume 4 §5.23)
  • Is a good business practice

Change management processes should not be (or perceived to be) an obstruction to fostering innovation and continual improvement. Efficient use of change management adds value by delivering technically sound solutions in an efficient manner.

🔧 核心概念：兩種變更管理模式

ISPE Vol.5 將變更管理分為兩種截然不同的機制，對應C&Q生命週期的不同階段：

  • ECM（工程變更管理）：適用於系統尚未被QA接受與放行之前，由工程或運營單位主導，重點在於控制設計與施工過程中的技術變更。
  • QCC（品質變更管制）：適用於系統已完成資格確認並正式投入生產之後，由QA主導，任何變更均需事前取得QA預先核准。

🏗 生活比喻：蓋房子 vs. 住房子之後

想像蓋一棟房子：

  • 施工階段（ECM）：牆壁位置、管線走向可以由工程師決定修改，只要記錄在施工圖並通知相關人員即可。
  • 入住後（QCC）：要拆牆或改電線，必須先向管理委員會（=QA）提出申請，獲准後才能動工，完工後還要驗收。

C&Q 中的「入住」時刻就是 QA 接受與放行（Acceptance and Release）。

⚠ 實務要點：Like-for-Like 的判定

同類替換（like-for-like）可能不構成「變更」，但這不是免費通行證。組織必須建立明確的評估程序，包含：

  • 零件型號、規格、製造商是否完全相同
  • 性能特性（如流量、壓力）是否無任何差異
  • 若有任何不同，即使微小，也應視為變更啟動評估

FDA 查廠常見缺失之一：企業聲稱 like-for-like，但缺乏書面評估記錄。

🏭 CDMO 實務應用

在 CDMO 環境中，ECM 與 QCC 的界線格外重要：

  • 新廠設施建造期間：使用 ECM，由工程部門主導，CDE 相關變更須通知QA
  • PQ 執行前後：即使系統還在試運行，一旦QA已正式放行，任何設計修改就必須走 QCC
  • 客戶導入新產品期間：若需修改現有已確認設備的參數或硬體，均屬 QCC 範疇

12.2 Engineering Change Management (ECM) — 工程變更管理

Pages 91–92

📖 Original Text (English)

ECM is a key component of the Engineering Quality Process. It provides the primary control mechanism and is fundamental in supporting the execution of regulatory/quality change control for CQAs. Appropriate SMEs, process owners, and the Quality Unit (where CAs/CDEs are impacted) should be involved in the evaluation, review, and approval.

There are three primary areas of application for ECM:

  • Provide lifecycle management for systems supporting both GMP and non-GMP regulated processes during operation
  • Provide the primary change management mechanism during a project phase including work intended to support C&Q (e.g., during DR/DQ, fabrication, FAT/SAT, and verification)
  • Manage and document technical changes for non-critical aspects of qualified systems

The review and approval method for the ECM system should enable effective and rapid change management that is sufficiently flexible to adapt to:

  • The stage of the project and related regulatory impact
  • The impact on manufacturing, maintenance, and system lifecycle

The level of documentation of the change should be commensurate with the level of risk related to the change.

12.2.1 Scope and Sequence of ECM

International GMPs require that changes that have the potential to impact product quality and patient safety be handled within the PQS (Pharmaceutical Quality System). This drives the need to establish a defined endpoint for application of ECM to GMP and non-GMP changes within the project lifecycle; from this point forward, full application of QCC to CAs/CDEs commences.

From a regulatory perspective, it is only after the acceptance and release of a system (for execution of PQ/Process Validation, if applicable, or for manufacturing for commercial use) that changes to systems with CAs/CDEs should go through QA-managed QCC.

During the operational phase of a system, ECM should continue to be applied to non-critical aspects of the system. Each change should be evaluated on whether CAs/CDEs are affected.

🧩 名詞解釋：ECM 的三大應用領域

ECM 並非只存在於建造期間，它在三個情境下均發揮作用：

  1. GMP 與非GMP系統運營期間的生命週期管理：廠房設備的長期維護更改
  2. C&Q 專案期間的主要變更機制：從 DR/DQ 到 FAT/SAT 都適用

📊 風險相稱原則

「文件程度應與變更風險相稱」是 ISPE 的一貫哲學。

低風險變更（非GMP管道）：簡單的工程記錄即可

中風險變更（GMP但非CDE）：需完整ECM記錄，通知相關SME

高風險變更（涉及CDE/CA）：ECM + QA核准，或直接走QCC

這與 ICH Q9 的風險管理原則一致——不應對所有變更一律採用最繁瑣的程序。

⚠ 常見混淆：ECM + QA核准 vs. QCC

許多實務人員容易混淆這兩種模式：

  • ECM + QA核准：適用於 放行前，QA 在工程框架內核准變更，不需要「預先批准」的正式變更單
  • QCC：適用於 放行後，正式品質管理系統（PQS）內的變更控制，必須在實施前取得QA預先批准

核心區別在於：時間點（放行前/後）和誰主導（工程/QA）。

🏭 CDMO 決策樹：ECM vs. QCC

遇到變更請求時，CDMO 工程師可依此判斷：

  1. 系統已被QA正式放行了嗎？→ 是 → 走 QCC
  2. 變更影響到 CA 或 CDE 嗎？→ 是 → 需QA核准（ECM或QCC視放行狀態而定）
  3. 變更只涉及非關鍵面向，且有System Risk Assessment支持嗎？→ 是 → ECM 即可，不需QA
  4. 以上皆否 → 與QA共同評估

12.3 Change Management during C&Q Projects — C&Q 期間的變更管理

Pages 92–93

📖 Original Text (English)

Before new systems are used for commercial or clinical operations, there is no risk to the patient associated with changes to design or function during the C&Q or project lifecycle. There is therefore no regulatory requirement for Quality Unit approval prior to implementation of a change.

There is, however, potential risk to the successful delivery of product and process user requirements and the risk control strategies determined as part of the approved System Risk Assessment. The Quality Unit should approve, prior to verification testing, any changes to the design or intended function or acceptance criteria associated with CAs/CDEs (including any new CAs/CDEs driven by changes in design).

In addition to maintaining the intended design after DR/DQ, ECM also plays a role in ensuring the integrity of data and evidence generated during C&Q. Fixes and repairs during C&Q have the potential to affect and invalidate testing and other evidence previously generated and compiled against the original configuration.

Test results from commissioning that do not meet acceptance criteria require:

  • Documented description of the incident
  • Description of corrective action(s) taken
  • Retest or re-verification of results following the correction
  • Assessment of the impact of the corrective action on any test results or other evidence already collected, and determination of any associated retesting requirements

A note should be made in the C&Q record and action taken to correct the item to meet the acceptance criteria. If the acceptance criteria cannot be met after corrective actions have been made, an SME should evaluate the issue and determine if it can be accepted as-is. A note should be made in the commissioning record to indicate why the item is acceptable as-is.

For more information on handling of discrepancies, refer to Section 7.4.

💬 中文解說 Commentary

🚦 重要原則：放行前不需QA預先核准

ISPE 明確指出：在系統尚未用於商業或臨床生產之前，設計或功能的改變 不會對病患造成直接風險，因此法規上並不要求 QA 在變更實施前預先核准。

這個原則打破了許多人的直覺。但這不代表可以隨意更改——ECM 仍然要求記錄所有變更，且涉及 CDE 的變更必須在驗證測試前取得 QA 核准。

⚠ 數據完整性警示：修正後的重測義務

C&Q 期間的修復工作（fix & repair）可能使已完成的測試數據失效。例如：

  • 儀器校正偏差被修正後，之前的測量結果需重新評估
  • 管線重新焊接後，之前的壓力測試可能需重做
  • 軟體邏輯修改後，相關的 IQ/OQ 測試步驟需重新確認

因此，ECM 記錄中必須包含「此次修正對現有測試數據的影響評估」，這是常被忽略的環節。

🔧 「As-Is 接受」的正確做法

當問題無法修正時（如設計限制），「接受現狀（accept as-is）」是允許的，但必須：

  1. 由有資格的 SME 評估可接受性
  2. 在試車記錄中明確說明為何可接受
  3. 評估是否影響 CDE/CQA（若有，則需QA認可）

空白的接受理由或僅寫「OK」是不合規的記錄。

12.4 Change Management during Operation — 運營期間的變更管理

Page 93

📖 Original Text (English)

After acceptance and release and after the system is placed into production use, any changes to the qualified system has the potential to affect product quality and patient safety. GMP regulations usually require Quality Unit pre-approval (approval prior to non-emergency implementation) of changes with the potential to affect product quality. When using a risk-based approach, QA-managed QCC should be applied when the change impacts CAs/CDEs.

After acceptance and release, there are scenarios that allow for application of ECM without Quality Unit involvement. Organizations should establish procedures that state the specific requirements for use of ECM within the organization or facility. Routine operations that may allow for application of ECM without Quality Unit involvement include, for example:

  • Changes to non-GMP aspects of systems
  • Changes to non-critical aspects of systems (as established by the System Risk Assessment)
  • Certain categories of changes, such as like-for-like, as documented in the completed verification and approved acceptance and release documentation

When QCC is used to manage changes, a log or tracking system should be used to maintain change histories for systems with CAs/CDEs. IT tools providing this functionality may be commercially available. A complete record of all changes to qualified systems should be able to be retrieved on demand.

💬 中文解說 Commentary

🏭 運營期間的變更管理框架

系統正式投入生產後，變更管理進入最嚴格的階段：

  • 影響 CDE/CA 的變更：必須走 QCC，QA 必須在實施前預先核准
  • 非關鍵面向的變更：可繼續使用 ECM，但需有 System Risk Assessment 支持
  • 緊急情況：GMP 通常允許緊急實施，但事後必須補正式核准程序

⚠ 法規要求：21 CFR 211.100 & EU GMP §5.23

美國 FDA（21 CFR 211.100）和歐盟 GMP（Volume 4, §5.23）均明確要求：

  • 書面程序（SOP）必須由有資格人員審核和核准
  • 任何偏離已核准程序的行為必須調查
  • 變更控制必須評估對已確認狀態的影響

這是為何 QCC 在放行後成為強制要求的法規基礎。

💻 變更追蹤系統的要求

QCC 要求維護完整的變更歷史，現代製藥企業通常使用以下工具：

  • 電子化變更控制系統（如 Veeva Vault QualityDocs、MasterControl）
  • 系統應能提供隨時調閱（on demand）的完整變更記錄
  • 稽核追蹤（audit trail）功能確保每筆變更均有時間戳記和核准人員記錄

FDA 查廠時，審查員可能要求即時調閱特定設備的完整變更歷史，因此系統的查詢功能至關重要。

12.5 Engineering Change Management Process — ECM 流程詳述

Pages 94–95

📖 Original Text (English)

ECM is defined as a process by which qualified representative(s) reviews proposed changes for its impact, approves or denies the requests, and manages and tracks their implementation. ECM is a good engineering practice to effectively manage project execution.

An ECM process may contain the following attributes:

  • Recorded name of the originator and date
  • Description of the change, including the affected system or area and intended purpose
  • Assessment of the potential impact of the change on:
    • Quality requirements
    • Project schedule
    • Project budget (relative to the latest approved budget)
    • System scope, design, or performance requirements (including safety, operability, reliability, construction, commissioning, operations, and maintenance)
    • Other systems
    • Engineering documentation
    • Qualification documents (including system classification)
  • Notification of the proposed change to stakeholders
  • Record of the approval or denial of the proposed change
  • Tracking through completion

ECM Application by Project Phase:

Project Conceptual Phase: There needs to be a clear definition of which documents are controlled by QA and where QA is only involved on request. Key product and process knowledge documents (such as the URS, pilot scale process reports, System Risk Assessment, and VMP) provide the basis for basic design and the requirements for CQAs and CPPs. Changes to these key documents should be addressed using ECM with Quality Unit approval.

Design Phase: Change management should be applied in alignment with the principles of GEP, with approval by SMEs. The mechanics of proposing, approving, implementing, and tracking changes should be simple since the design phase is very fluid. At the completion of the design phase, DR/DQ is completed. At this point, ECM is applied to GEP changes, and ECM with Quality Unit approval is applied to changes in CAs/CDEs.

Construction, C&Q Phase: For large projects, the site and project are primarily under the control of a contract organization. Changes should be implemented using the contract organization's change management policy with input by the owner. During construction, many changes are driven by the reality of the installed environment versus the design intent. Typical changes include:

  • Field inspections and testing performance — Controlled by ECM; may require retesting post-change
  • Departures from specification:
    • Readily corrected (e.g., pipe labels): Correct, reinspect, and document
    • Corrected later (or impacting other inspections/tests): Use discrepancy/punch list or change management
    • Cannot correct: Needs evaluation by the technical reviewer
  • Deviations — Only occur during performance testing when a product/process user requirement is not met. Steps:
    • Documentation and evaluation by Quality and Manufacturing Units
    • Determination of an assignable cause
    • Corrective action: definition, approval, implementation, test, confirmation, and documentation

Correction of deviations should be addressed in the following preferred order:

  1. Design change
  2. Installation change
  3. Operational change

Non-correctable deviations may require an SOP or other procedural change (procedural control) to compensate for the shortcoming.

💬 中文解說 Commentary

📋 ECM 表單的必要元素

一份完整的 ECM 變更申請記錄應包含以下內容，這也是稽核員查閱時的核查清單：

  • 發起人姓名與日期（可歸責性 Attributability）
  • 變更描述：影響的系統、區域、目的
  • 多維度影響評估：品質、進度、預算、其他系統、文件
  • 利益相關者通知記錄
  • 核准/拒絕記錄
  • 完成追蹤：確認已按核准內容實施

⚠ 偏差優先處理順序：設計 > 安裝 > 程序

ISPE 明確規定偏差改正的優先順序：

  1. 設計變更（最優先）：從根本解決問題，修改設計或規格
  2. 安裝變更：修改物理安裝方式，如管線位置、設備位置
  3. 操作程序變更（最後手段）：用 SOP 彌補設計/安裝的不足

這個優先順序反映了一個重要理念：依賴操作人員執行特殊程序來彌補硬體缺陷，比修改硬體本身風險更高。

🏭 CDMO 施工期間的 ECM 實例

典型的CDMO建廠 ECM 情境：

  • 管線路徑調整（非CDE）→ ECM，工程師核准，更新P&ID
  • WFI 分配管線設計壓力修改（可能涉及CDE）→ ECM + QA核准，更新URS
  • 無菌灌裝機 HEPA 過濾器規格由H13改H14（CDE）→ ECM + QA核准，評估對OQ的影響
  • 天花板石膏板牆面更換材質（非GMP）→ ECM，無需通知QA

13.1 Introduction & 13.1.1–13.1.2 | Pages 97–98

📖 Original Text (English)

13.1 Introduction

The goal of Good Documentation Practice (GDocP) is to create clear, concise documents that a knowledgeable peer may review without questions. Good Documentation Practice supports data integrity. Documents should be:

  • Attributable
  • Legible
  • Contemporaneous
  • Original
  • Accurate

These five attributes form the acronym ALCOA.

13.1.1 Scope and Purpose

This chapter applies to all C&Q documentation, including test documents and reports. In this chapter, GEP documents are considered to be the engineering records used to support commissioning, and GMP records are considered to be the documentation supporting qualification.

Table 13.1: Differences Between Commissioning vs. Qualification Testing and Documentation

13.1.2 Requirements for Vendor Documentation to Support Qualification

When the decision is made to use vendor documentation to support system qualification of CAs/CDEs, the justification should be documented and approved by SMEs and the Quality Unit. Considerations should include:

  • Vendor assessment
  • Acceptable vendor quality system
  • Vendor technical capability
  • Vendor application of GEP such that information obtained will be accurate and suitable for the purpose of qualification
💬 中文解說 Commentary

📑 ALCOA 原則詳解 — 數據完整性的基石

ALCOA 是全球製藥法規（FDA、EMA、WHO）要求的數據完整性核心框架：

  • A – Attributable（可歸責）：清楚記錄誰在何時執行了什麼操作。每筆記錄都必須有識別資訊（簽名/姓名縮寫 + 日期）。
  • L – Legible（清晰易讀）：記錄必須永久且清晰可讀。潦草、褪色、被遮蓋的記錄均不合規。
  • C – Contemporaneous（當時記錄）：在操作執行的當下立即記錄，不允許事後補記（backdating）。
  • O – Original（原始資料）：保留第一手記錄；若需謄抄，原始資料必須保留並標註。
  • A – Accurate（準確）：數據必須真實反映實際結果，不得捏造或篡改。

⚠ ALCOA+ 的延伸要求

現代法規（特別是 FDA 2018年數據完整性指南）在 ALCOA 基礎上加入四個「+」：

  • Complete（完整）：不得選擇性記錄；所有測試（包含不合格）都必須記錄
  • Consistent（一致）：記錄方式應跨人員、跨時間保持一致
  • Enduring（永久性）：記錄必須以不易損壞的方式保存（如不用鉛筆、可擦鋼筆）
  • Available（可取得）：稽核時可即時提供給主管機關審閱

🧪 GEP vs. GMP 文件的關鍵差異

一個容易理解的比較：

  • GEP（試車文件）：像工程師的工作筆記——可以反覆試驗，記錄最終結果；工程判斷足夠
  • GMP（確認文件）：像法庭證據——必須按計畫執行，逐步記錄，任何偏差都要記錄和說明

這個區別決定了試車報告能否被當作確認文件使用——必須達到 GMP 文件標準才行。

🏭 廠商文件用於確認的風險

許多 CDMO 希望使用設備廠商的 FAT/SAT 文件替代自己的 IQ/OQ，ISPE 允許此做法，但前提是：

  • 廠商已通過評估（Vendor Qualification/Audit）
  • 廠商的 QMS 達到可接受標準
  • 廠商文件已按 GDocP 要求執行
  • 使用理由已由 SME 和 QA 書面核准

若廠商文件品質不足，引用它可能使整個確認包的可信度受損。

📖 Original Text (English)

Testing and verification documentation are usually written in the local language. Summary documents are generally in English to facilitate regulatory review.

The use of forms/templates is encouraged to promote consistency. Forms/templates can be approved once when created and then utilized multiple times without pre-approval if the relevant content is not changed. Where this approach is used, there should be a procedure to ensure that the form/template used for data entry is current at the time of use.

The test procedures should be clear and unambiguous such that the results are repeatable and reproducible:

  • Repeatable: The same result would be achieved if the same procedure is repeated by the same trained and qualified personnel.
  • Reproducible: The same result would be achieved by different trained and qualified personnel following the same procedure.

The test document should indicate the order that the cases are to be executed. Acceptance criteria needs to be established and it should be clear that the tests demonstrate that the acceptance criteria have been met.

💬 中文解說 Commentary

📝 範本（Template）管理的最佳實踐

使用已核准的標準範本執行測試，是提升效率的聰明做法。關鍵要求：

  • 範本核准一次，多次使用，無需每次重新核准
  • 但必須確認使用時範本為最新版本（即文件管控有效）
  • SME 必須確認範本涵蓋該特定系統的所有必要測試——若不足，必須增補

🔄 可重複性 vs. 可再現性

這兩個概念源自量測系統分析（MSA）：

  • Repeatability（可重複性）：同一人、同一設備、同一條件，重複執行得到相同結果。測試的「自身一致性」。
  • Reproducibility（可再現性）：不同人、不同地點、不同設備，執行相同程序得到相同結果。測試的「跨人員一致性」。

若測試程序寫得模糊，不同人執行可能得到不同結果，確認的可信度就會下降。

⚠ 語言選擇與法規審查

執行文件可以用本地語言（如中文、日文）撰寫，但總結報告建議以英文撰寫，原因是：

  • FDA/EMA 查廠時審查員通常只能閱讀英文
  • 英文摘要讓跨國監管機構無需翻譯即可評估
  • CDMO 為多國客戶服務時，英文摘要減少溝通障礙

13.3 Document Execution — 文件執行規範

13.3.1 Test Execution Basics & 13.3.2 Good Test Execution Documentation | Pages 99–102

📖 Original Text (English)

13.3.1 Test Execution Basics

13.3.1.1 Executor Qualification and Signature Log

Individuals who enter information in a test document (i.e., executors) must possess the education, training, experience, or any combination thereof to perform their work and record their printed name, signature, and initials in a signature log. An executor cannot review or approve his/her own work.

13.3.1.2 Contemporaneous Records

Documents should be completed as work is performed. Results should be recorded at the time of activity. Backdating is not permitted.

The steps within a test case should be executed in order unless specified otherwise. Provide an explanation for any step or task performed out of sequence.

If a test script takes more than one calendar day to execute, indicate where testing stopped and initial and date this entry at the appropriate test step.

13.3.1.3 Transcribed Data

It is permissible to transcribe data from one form to another (e.g., printed tabular summary of manual entries or manually transcribing single entries from computer generated printouts). Transcribed data should be signed and dated. The date of the actual data should be included. Transcribed data should be checked by a second person. If data is transcribed, the raw data should be included — labeled, initialed and dated.

13.3.1.4 Printouts

Test steps should indicate when printouts are to be used. Printouts should be attached to the test plan/report appendix or contained in the appropriate reference file.

Where the output is from a thermal printer (e.g., for a particle counter), a photocopy should be used, signed and dated as a true copy by the individual responsible for the test. A footnote should be annotated in the test document to reference the location of the original.

13.3.1.5 Supporting Documentation

Attach or reference supporting documentation as necessary. Original data sheets and checklists should be available for review until the transcribed document has been accepted and approved. Refer to organization requirements or relevant regional regulations regarding any specific documentation requirements prior to destroying supporting documentation for GMP records/documents.

13.3.1.6 Proper Handling of Documents

It is understood and accepted that testing documents will experience some wear and tear typical of field executed documentation. However, care should be taken to protect documents from spills, excessive folding, or other physical damage.

13.3.1.7 Document Changes

Changes to approved documents require approval by representatives of the original approving groups.

13.3.2 Good Test Execution Documentation

The typical requirements for recording a GEP document should include:

  • Use permanent, non-fading, non-smearing ink.
    • Blue or black ink is preferred.
    • If colors are used for redline drawings, the definition of color meaning must be clear.
    • GEP field documentation recorded in pencil: a photocopy should be used, signed and dated as a true copy.
  • Ditto marks (") or arrows (↓) to indicate duplicate entries are not permitted.
  • Writing over an existing entry is not permitted (see Section 13.5 for corrections).
  • No scribbling on the record. When adding notes, use unique alpha/numeric identifiers to trace each entry to the corresponding footnote.
  • If a signature log is maintained, initials are acceptable in lieu of signatures everywhere except for document approvals.
  • Initial and date any handwritten changes or comments. If the reason for the change is not clear, provide an explanatory note.
  • Initial and date each page with an entry at least once.
  • Date format needs to be unambiguous. DD-MMM-YYYY is preferred (e.g., 30-MAY-2019).
  • Time format: use 24-hour clock (e.g., 1425) or am/pm (e.g., 2:25 pm).
  • For significant figures:
    • Use a zero before a decimal point if there is no other digit.
    • Record all figures as shown on the measuring instrument or readout.
    • Ensure calculations indicate the correct unit of measure and use the correct number of significant digits.
    • For final reported data, round to the number of places that agrees with the stated specification or limit.
  • For quantitative results, record the number. Examples of unacceptable entries:
    • "Pass", "Fail", "As Expected", "Same", "OK", "Yes", Checkmark "✓"
  • Record the observed system response from the test step execution in the results column. Use unambiguous terminology; it is not necessary to record verbatim the expected results.
  • Line out, initial, and date all blank spaces. Be careful to distinguish "N/A" (Not Applicable) and "N/AV" (Not Available). "N/A" should only be used prior to approval to execute.
  • Where entire sections, columns, rows, or whole pages are not utilized, cross out with a single diagonal line; initial and date.
  • Data superseded by a subsequent test or inspection may be deleted. The final record should be annotated to indicate if a repair, engineering change, or correction has been made.
  • Test reports should include a summary of the results including a conclusion that the system is or is not suitable for the intended purpose.
  • The completed record should be able to be copied/scanned legibly.

👥 執行者資格與簽名記錄

執行者（Executor）必須具備相應的教育背景、培訓和經驗。關鍵要求：

  • 每位執行者必須在簽名記錄表（Signature Log）中登記：印刷全名、正式簽名、縮寫（Initials）
  • 自我審核原則：任何人不得審核或核准自己的工作——這是 GDocP 的基本原則，也是利益衝突的防範機制

⏰ 當時記錄原則 — FDA 最常引用的缺失

「事後補記」（backdating）是 FDA 483 觀察和 Warning Letter 中最常見的 GDocP 缺失之一。

合規要求：測試步驟執行時立即記錄，不論是好是壞的結果。

常見違規情境：

  • 測試當天忘記記錄，隔天才填寫
  • 先記錄在便利貼，之後再謄抄到正式文件
  • 批次記錄在一天結束時集中填寫，而非操作當下填寫

電子記錄系統的稽核追蹤（Audit Trail）可以顯示記錄時間，使事後補記容易被發現。

📄 謄抄數據（Transcribed Data）的要求

當數據需要從原始記錄謄抄到另一份文件時：

  1. 謄抄者必須簽名並標注日期
  2. 包含原始數據的採集日期（不是謄抄日期）
  3. 必須由第二人核查謄抄準確性
  4. 原始數據必須保留，標記清楚並附上姓名縮寫和日期

🖨 熱感紙打印件的特殊處理

粒子計數器、天平等儀器常使用熱感紙（thermal paper），熱感紙容易褪色，違反「Legible」和「Enduring」原則。

正確做法：立即複印（photocopy），複印件由執行人員簽名並標注「True Copy」和日期，原件注明存放位置。

✏ 13.3.2 書面記錄的核心要求匯總

墨水要求：永久性、不褪色、不暈開；建議藍色或黑色；禁用鉛筆（除非立即複印存檔）

日期格式：DD-MMM-YYYY（如 30-MAY-2019）最佳，避免歧義（01/02/03 可能是1月2日或2月1日）

數量結果禁止寫：Pass / Fail / OK / Yes / ✓ — 必須記錄實際測量數值

空白格處理：必須劃線並簽名縮寫+日期，或標注 N/A，不得留空

文件標識：每頁至少簽名縮寫+日期一次

📈 N/A vs. N/AV 的區別

這個細節常被忽略但很重要：

  • N/A（Not Applicable，不適用）：只能在文件核准執行之前標注，表示這個測試項目根本不適用於該系統。一旦文件已核准開始執行，事後再標 N/A 通常需要解釋說明。
  • N/AV（Not Available，無法取得）：表示在執行時該項目無法評估，通常需要偏差記錄和說明。

🏭 CDMO GDocP 訓練重點

CDMO 在操作員 GDocP 培訓中最應強調的行為準則：

  • 「執行即記錄」文化——不接受「先做後記」
  • 記錄實際觀察值，不得記錄「意見」（Pass/OK）
  • 空白必須處理（N/A + 縮寫 + 日期）
  • 任何修改要可追溯（見13.5節）
  • 不得使用修正液（Whiteout）

13.5 Corrections — 文件更正規範

Pages 102–103

📖 Original Text (English)

Mistakes and errors happen. Do not erase, overwrite, or corrupt original recorded data. Correction fluid, correction tape, pencil, and erasable ink should be avoided. Data should not be altered, obliterated, erased, or overwritten.

Corrections are acceptable when:

  • Correcting test procedures or instructions that do not change the intent of the step
  • Correcting typographical errors
  • Correcting test/sampling instructions
  • Correcting test data with no impact on interpretation

Corrections can be handled in the following manner:

  • Mark a single line through the error

💬 中文解說 Commentary

✊ GDocP 更正的黃金法則 — 單線劃除

這是 GDocP 中最基本也最常被違反的規定：

23.5 24.2 [縮寫] [日期]

正確步驟：

  1. 用單一橫線劃過錯誤內容（原始數據仍可辨識）
  2. 在旁邊寫上正確值
  3. 簽上縮寫（Initials）和更正日期
  4. 若原因不明顯，加上唯一參照碼並在同頁附上說明

絕對禁止：修正液（Whiteout）、修正帶、覆蓋書寫、橡皮擦

🤔 為什麼原始數據必須可見？

「原始數據仍可辨識」這個要求背後是法規邏輯：

  • 若看不到原始數據，無法判斷更正是否合理
  • 若錯誤被完全遮蓋，看起來像資料造假
  • 稽核員需要能追蹤「原始紀錄 → 更正原因 → 最終值」的完整鏈條

使用修正液讓原始數據不可見，即使更正值是正確的，也可能被認定為違反數據完整性。

🏭 CDMO 實際情境：他人代為更正

當原始記錄者已離職或無法聯繫時，需要他人代為更正，必須：

  • 確認此情況符合公司/場地 SOP 的規定
  • 更正者必須說明為何由本人代為更正
  • 記錄更正者的縮寫和日期（而非原始記錄者）
  • 通常需要額外的說明文件或偏差記錄支持

這種情況在長期 C&Q 專案中並不少見，特別是使用臨時工程人員或承包商的情況下。

📝 練習思考：以下哪些更正方式合規？

  • 用白色修正液蓋住錯誤，然後在上面寫正確值 → 不合規（原始數據無法辨識）
  • 用雙線劃過錯誤的完整數字，旁邊寫正確值並簽名縮寫和日期 → 合規
  • 只劃掉錯誤數字中的一個位數，保留其他位數 → 不合規（應劃掉完整數值）
  • 劃掉錯誤值後添加更正值，但沒有日期 → 不合規（缺少日期，違反 Attributable）
  • 原因不明顯的更正，添加腳注說明更正理由 → 合規

這個彈性設計讓 CDMO 的 QA 部門可以將稽核資源集中在真正有品質影響的項目上，提高效率。

  • 適用法規的特定保存期限必須查明並遵守（各國法規不同）
  • 美國 FDA：GMP 記錄通常需保存至少2年（藥品有效期後），或更長
  • 歐盟 EMA / PICS：類似要求，但具體依產品類型而定

📦 21 CFR Part 11 與電子記錄

若使用電子系統儲存和管理確認文件，美國法規 21 CFR Part 11（電子記錄與電子簽名）即適用，要求：

  • 稽核追蹤（Audit Trail）：自動記錄誰在何時建立、修改、刪除記錄
  • 電子簽名：必須與簽署人唯一連結，不可否認（non-repudiation）
  • 系統確認：EDMS 本身需要通過 GAMP 5 等框架進行 CSV（電腦系統確認）
  • 數據保護：防止未授權修改，電子記錄的完整性必須受到保護

歐盟對應法規為 EU Annex 11（電腦化系統）。

🚨 災難復原程序

文件損毀（如火災、水災、電腦崩潰）時的應對程序：

  1. 優先嘗試復原原始文件（如從備份系統、其他副本）
  2. 若無法復原，可接受原始文件的副本作為正式記錄
  3. 副本必須由管理層（Management）核准，確認其為有效替代
  4. 建立事件記錄，說明原始文件損失的原因和過程

預防勝於治療——C&Q 執行期間即應規劃文件儲存策略，最小化損失風險。

🏭 CDMO 文件保存的實務建議

  • 多重備份：重要文件（如確認包）建議保有：原件 + 數位掃描件（EDMS）+ 異地備份
  • 保存期限管理：建立文件銷毀排程，確保在監管保存期限結束前不得銷毀 GMP 記錄
  • 客戶交付物管理：CDMO 為客戶執行的確認工作，文件的所有權和保存責任需在合約中明確約定
  • 查廠準備：確保查廠時能在30分鐘內提供任意指定的歷史確認文件——這是衡量文件管理體系效率的實際指標

Chapter 12：變更管理兩大支柱

  • ECM（工程變更管理）適用於系統放行前的C&Q及建造期間，由工程/運營主導，CDE相關變更須QA核准但不需預先批准；非關鍵面向在放行後亦可繼續使用ECM。
  • QCC（品質變更管制）適用於系統放行後的所有CDE/CA相關變更，由QA主導，所有變更必須在實施前取得QA預先核准；法規依據為21 CFR 211.100及EU GMP §5.23。
  • 偏差修正優先順序：設計變更 > 安裝變更 > 程序變更，依賴SOP彌補硬體缺陷是最後手段。

Chapter 13：GDocP 六大核心原則

  • ALCOA+（可歸責、清晰易讀、當時記錄、原始資料、準確 + 完整、一致、永久性、可取得）是文件管理的基石，直接對應 FDA 數據完整性要求。
  • 當時記錄（Contemporaneous）是最常被違反的原則之一；事後補記（backdating）是FDA 483最常引用的缺失。
  • 更正規範：單線劃除（原始數據仍可辨識）+ 正確值 + 縮寫 + 日期；絕對禁止修正液、塗改、覆蓋書寫。
  • 試車文件（GEP）可用於支持確認（GMP），前提是達到GMP文件標準（事先確認廠商文件品質）。
  • 電子記錄須符合21 CFR Part 11（美國）或EU Annex 11（歐盟），確認EDMS系統並維護稽核追蹤。
  • 文件儲存需確保隨時可取得；C&Q期間即應建立防損失計畫，並在災難發生時有復原程序。
