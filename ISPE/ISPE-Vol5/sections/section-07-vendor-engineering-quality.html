Chapter 10: Vendor Assessment for C&Q Documentation Purposes

10.1 Introduction & Purpose/Benefits & Principles — Pages 79–80

🌎 Original Text — English

10.1 Introduction

This chapter describes vendor assessments and controls for work where vendors follow their own procedures to perform testing and produce documentation that may be used to support system qualification. When vendor tests are used to directly support qualification where appropriate, the result is reduced on-site testing and reduced cost and time in the schedule without impacting quality. In addition, the SMEs on the project team can contribute to improved product quality by focusing their attention on other higher risk quality critical activities.

Vendor assessments (for C&Q documentation purposes) do not include the vendor selection and/or commercial assessment, though the results may impact those assessments. Vendor assessments for C&Q documentation purposes may be integrated into this broader vendor selection and qualification process or comprise a standalone activity for C&Q planning and execution purposes.

Regulatory expectations for vendor assessments are provided in Chapter 15 (Appendix 1). For detailed information on assessing vendors of automated systems, refer to ISPE GAMP® 5.

Note: It is not necessary to perform vendor assessments, as described in this Guide, for vendors that provide qualification services (sometimes referred to as secondary services) when those vendors are contracted to follow the regulated owner organization procedures and practices or applicable specialist standards, such as the US National Environmental Balancing Bureau (NEBB).

10.1.1 Purpose and Benefits

The objective of a vendor assessment is to establish confidence in the vendor and the vendor quality systems to perform testing, manage changes, and provide documentation that is accurate, complete, and unambiguous, with results matching what was expected. Vendor verification documentation assessments are highly recommended when documents may be used to support qualification. Purchaser and vendor cooperation is key to successfully achieving this goal.

Vendor assessments can minimize business/project risk associated with vendor performance, including project cost and schedule risk, by performing testing and completing documentation deliverables required to support verification of CAs (qualification) right the first time.

The use of vendor documentation can prevent repeating work that was appropriately performed and documented and can help to achieve GMP compliance at a reduced cost and in less time. Vendors are the experts in their particular systems and the use of vendor testing and documentation should be encouraged. As an example, consider verification of product contact materials. Accepting a certificate of conformity from a quality vendor can be an acceptable, low-risk approach for material verification; checking 100% of the related heat certificates would therefore be unnecessary.

10.1.2 Principles/Concepts

It is good engineering practice to assess vendor's capability for testing and documenting the systems they provide for conformance to requirements and specifications, based on the criticality of the system. The decision and justification to use vendor documentation to support the verification of CAs and associated CDEs should be based on the intended purpose of the manufacturing system and the suitability of the vendor quality systems. The decision and rationale should be documented in the C&Q Plan and approved by SMEs and the Quality Unit.

The Quality Unit and Engineering/Technical SME should agree on the type (e.g., checklist, test plan, execution report) and quality (Good Documentation Practices) of the deliverables. The Quality Unit should review the vendor assessment and confirm the extent to which the vendor's documentation package may be used to support qualification and verify suitability for the intended purpose. This review and confirmation should be conducted and documented as early as possible.

Quality Unit oversight in the use of vendor verification documentation is a risk-based decision, with consideration of risk to product quality. For example, there is less product quality risk in documenting pipe boroscoping or passivation activities as compared to an SAT for a sterile filling isolator.

解談 中文解析與學習重點

📚 核心概念：廠商評估 (Vendor Assessment) 是什麼？

本章所指的「廠商評估」，目的非常具體：確認廠商自行產生的測試記錄與文件是否可直接用來支援設備/系統的 Qualification（資格確認）。

其核心問題是：「這個廠商的文件品質，足以讓我們不需要在現場再次執行全套測試嗎？」

關鍵字翻譯：

  • Vendor Assessment（廠商評估）= 評估廠商產出 C&Q 支援文件的能力
  • Vendor Quality System（廠商品質系統）= 廠商的 QMS
  • Certificate of Conformity / CoC（符合性證明）= 廠商出具的材質/規格合格聲明書

⚠️ 重要區別：這不是供應商稽核！

很多人容易混淆兩件事：

  • C&Q 文件目的廠商評估（本章主題）：評估廠商能否產出符合 GDocP 的 C&Q 文件（FAT/SAT 報告、測試記錄等），讓業主不必重複做測試。
  • 商業供應商資格確認（Vendor Qualification / Supplier Audit）：評估原料藥、輔料、包材供應商的 GMP 合規性，確保材料品質。這是完全不同的目的！

本章明確說明：C&Q 廠商評估「不包含」商業廠商選擇評估，即使兩者結果可以相互參考。

💡 比喻：CoC 就像「產地證明」

試想你向一家信譽良好的不銹鋼管材廠商採購 316L 管材。如果你已評估過該廠商的 QMS 是可靠的，你可以接受他們的 CoC（符合性證明）來驗證材質，而不需要自己送 100% 的管材去做熱處理憑證查核（Heat Certificate Check）。

這就是「廠商是自己系統的專家」的精神——讓專家負責自己的工作，業主聚焦在真正的高風險項目上。

🔎 原則/概念重點整理

是否接受廠商文件的決策依據：

  • 製造系統的預期用途（intended purpose）
  • 廠商品質系統的適用性（suitability of vendor QMS）
  • 決策理由必須記錄於 C&Q Plan，由 SME 及 QU 核准

QU 監督程度是風險導向的：

  • 低風險：管道內窺鏡檢查 (Boroscoping)、鈍化 (Passivation) 文件
  • 高風險：無菌充填隔離器的 SAT（Site Acceptance Testing）

風險越高，QU 的介入程度越深——這是貫穿整個 ISPE Vol.5 的核心邏輯。

📋 豁免情況

如果廠商提供的是「次要服務」（例如 HVAC 平衡廠商 NEBB），且合約要求其依照業主程序或適用的專業標準執行，則不需要按本章要求進行廠商評估。

10.2 Communication of Requirements, Scope, and Deliverables

Effective communication framework with vendors — Page 80

🌎 Original Text — English

The project team should ensure that the fundamental project requirements are communicated effectively to all parties, both internal and external. In addition to the overall project requirements, specific items that should be communicated to vendors involved in the project should include, at a minimum:

  • Specific scope of work, including requirements and specifications as appropriate
  • Specific deliverables, including testing and documentation, that are in the scope of work
  • Quality assurance requirements for the work and deliverables
  • Project controls which will apply
  • Schedule of deliverables, including documentation deliverables and lead times for review and update

Depending on the size of the project, these items may be contained in a single document or detailed in several project plans, procedures, or specifications. Confirmation should be obtained that this communication has been understood; provisions should be made in the schedule for dialogue and/or meetings to provide clarification and confirm comprehension by the vendor.

From a project risk perspective and to help to ensure that quality is built in at the outset, arrangements should be made for supervision, inspections, and/or surveillance for the scope of work and associated deliverables across the lifecycle of their development.

💡 CDMO 採購情境類比

假設你的 CDMO 正在採購一台新的無菌充填機。在簽約前，你需要在採購規格書（URS）中清楚載明：

  • 廠商需提供哪些 FAT/SAT 測試文件
  • 文件格式需符合公司的 GDocP 要求
  • 變更通知程序（ECM）
  • 文件交付截止日期及審查周期

這樣在廠商製造期間就有明確依據，避免交機時發現文件格式不符合要求，被迫現場補做測試。

⚠️ 實務提醒：確認「理解」不等於「已收到」

本節特別要求要確認廠商理解（Confirm Comprehension），而不只是「發送文件給廠商」。應安排說明會或書面回覆確認，並在專案時程中預留對話時間。這在跨國設備採購中尤為重要。

10.3 Vendor Assessment Factors

11 evaluation factors + supply chain consideration — Pages 80–81

🌎 Original Text — English

As part of the vendor assessment for verification documentation purposes, the owner organization should assess the robustness of the vendor's Quality Management System (QMS). This provides insight into the strengths and weaknesses of the vendor capability and provides an opportunity to align or, if necessary, impose procedures or controls.

When performing a vendor assessment, the following factors should be evaluated:

  • Degree of novelty of the system provided by the vendor
  • Training and experience of the staff who will conduct the testing
  • Facilities and equipment available for the testing
  • Instrument calibration program
  • Vendor quality oversight
  • Engineering quality system
  • Technical capabilities
  • Procedures for material control, material processing, and incoming goods inspection
  • Documentation standards (Good Documentation Practice)
  • Software development and testing standards (testing, procedures)
  • Fabrication facilities

Consideration should also be given to assessing the supply chain and whether a vendor has suitable procedures in place for assessing their own vendors (e.g., for welding, piping, automation hardware and software) and subcontractors.

As discussed in Section 10.1, a vendor assessment for verification documentation purposes is not a vendor qualification/audit. It is different than a product-focused vendor assessment for raw materials, packaging components, Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (APIs), etc.

Refer to Chapter 27 (Appendix 13) for an example vendor assessment for verification documentation.

10.4 Vendor Assessment Strategy

4 approaches including waiver — Page 81

🌎 Original Text — English

A risk-based approach should be taken in determining the project-specific vendor assessment strategy.

There are several approaches for completing vendor assessments; examples include:

  • Use of existing vendor assessment from within the owner organization
  • On-site self-assessment by the vendor with a standardized vendor assessment template or questionnaire provided by the owner organization
  • On-site assessment by a technical SME or assessment team from the owner organization
  • Waiver of assessment

The waiver of any project-generated formal assessment may be granted based on a vendor's current qualification status, use history, previous audit, lack of criticality of vendor supplied information, or other documented and Quality Unit approved justification. The decision to reassess a vendor that has already been assessed should be based on risk, using the same factors that triggered the initial assessment.

The decisions and rationale for the approach taken can be documented and approved through the mechanism of the C&Q Plan or as a standalone activity. This may be part of an owner organization's PQS procedures and routine practices.

解談 中文解析與學習重點

⚠️ 何時可以申請豁免 (Waiver)？

豁免評估必須具備以下任一合理理由，並經過 QU 核准記錄於 C&Q Plan：

  • 廠商目前的資格確認狀態仍有效
  • 有良好的使用歷史紀錄（過去多次合作均無問題）
  • 有近期稽核記錄（如 ISO 9001 第三方稽核）
  • 廠商提供的資訊缺乏關鍵性（對品質影響風險低）
  • 其他經 QU 核准的有據理由

重新評估的決策同樣基於風險——如果廠商狀況未改變，且初次評估觸發因素依舊成立，則不必重新評估。

💡 思考練習：你的 CDMO 應選哪種策略？

情境 1：你們長期合作 5 年的自動化控制廠商，上次評估在 2 年前，近期沒有重大系統或人員變化。→ 可考慮沿用既有評估或豁免。

情境 2：新廠商首次提供無菌充填隔離器，全新設計，無合作歷史。→ 必須考慮SME 現場評估，且不宜豁免。

情境 3：廠商提供辦公室空調系統（非 GMP 區域），對產品無影響。→ 可考慮豁免或自我評估。

10.5 Use of Vendors Unfamiliar with GMP

Additional controls for non-GMP environment vendors — Page 82

🌎 Original Text — English

The use of vendor assessments, quality assurance plans, and/or vendor quality plans provide a means either to accept or reject a vendor, or to identify where additional resources should be applied to ensure success. Certain vendors may not operate within a GMP environment for most of their products and services to customers. A QMS may be an unfamiliar or informal practice. The vendor needs to be informed that they are supplying goods or services to a pharmaceutical manufacturer.

Deficiencies should be recognized early and appropriate support measures established to help to deliver the necessary requirements. Where deficiencies are identified, additional controls and resources should be applied as early as possible. The vendor needs to support this approach and their agreement should be obtained. Additional control measures, designed to mitigate the risks presented, may include:

  • Vendor training and guidance
  • Additional review and approval
  • Additional auditing of the vendor
  • Attending/witnessing critical phases of the design, fabrication, and testing at the vendor site
  • Support by third-party service providers
  • Additional owner testing to compensate for being unable to rely on vendor generated test documentation

當廠商不熟悉 GMP 環境時，業主需要採取補強措施。以下是六種工具，可單獨或組合使用：

  • ✅ 業主自行補測：當廠商文件不可信賴時，業主在現場額外執行測試

💡 實務情境：機械工廠 vs. 製藥設備廠

許多優秀的機械加工廠（如特殊管件、客製化零件廠商）可能只有 ISO 9001 認證，對 GMP、21 CFR Part 11、電子簽名等概念完全陌生。

這類廠商仍可被使用，但業主需要：

  • 明確告知他們是在為製藥廠供應
  • 提供文件格式範本和 GDocP 說明
  • 安排業主 QA 在關鍵製造階段現場見證
  • 若文件仍不可靠，業主需自行在安裝後補做測試

關鍵原則：缺陷要早發現、早處理，越到後期（如 SAT 時）才發現問題，補救成本越高。

Chapter 11: Engineering Quality Process

11.1 Introduction & Rationale — Pages 83–84

🌎 Original Text — English

11.1 Introduction

When the C&Q process is considered as a part of an organization-wide PQS, the result is an achievement of both GMP and GEP concepts. A separate quality system is not required for C&Q; instead, it can be part of the overall PQS or a subset of the PQS. For the purposes of this Guide, the term Engineering Quality Process is used to describe the process within the PQS that covers C&Q. This Guide recognizes that this function is referred to differently across organizations which have their own structure and assignment of roles and responsibilities. This chapter intends to focus on the concepts that should be addressed for this function.

The overall organization PQS program provides the quality practices for processes in order to consistently deliver quality products. The Engineering Quality Process, as a subset process of the PQS, addresses the quality practices for the C&Q process and is intended to support the overall PQS program.

Both the PQS and Engineering Quality Process need to emphasize quality (i.e., the product or service meeting the specified requirements) versus compliance (i.e., meeting current procedures or organization requirements). Good quality practices and methods through the PQS produce robust reliable systems and ensures compliance. The Engineering Quality Process needs to be established as part of the PQS; the Engineering Quality Process can then be used for developing sustainable, consistent, cost-effective, and practical engineering processes for project and operational lifecycle management.

11.1.1 Rationale

Current regulatory authority and ICH guidances emphasize a focus on building quality into the product and not by inspection. ICH Q10 augments the defined GMPs by describing specific quality system elements and management responsibilities that should be in place to achieve three main objectives; it states:

"Implementation of the Q10 model should result in achievement of three main objectives which complement or enhance regional GMP requirements:
  ▶ Achieve Product Realisation [Section 1.5.1]
  ▶ Establish and Maintain a State of Control [Section 1.5.2]
  ▶ Facilitate Continual Improvement [Section 1.5.3]"

The FDA 2006 Guidance for Industry: Quality Systems Approach to Pharmaceutical CGMP Regulations states the following as the goal of the guidance:

"This guidance describes a comprehensive quality systems model, which, if implemented, will allow manufacturers to support and sustain robust, modern quality systems that are consistent with CGMP regulations."

In alignment with ICH Q8, the FDA guidance states:

"The overarching philosophy articulated in both the CGMP regulations and in robust modern quality systems is: Quality should be built into the product, and testing alone cannot be relied on to ensure product quality."

Another useful resource in understanding Engineering Quality Process as a philosophy is the work of Dr. W. Edwards Deming. Deming believed in improving the process and in doing so using process measures to guide improvement efforts. In his book, Deming states:

"Conformance to specifications may be achieved in several ways:
1. By careful inspection, sorting the bad from the good. Dependence on inspection is hazardous and costly.
2. By work on the production process to shrink variation about the nominal value."

The approach presented in this Guide embraces regulatory guidance and Deming's principle that demonstration of conformance to specifications can be achieved by developing a process, which addresses causes and concerns for variability and reduces reliance on inspection. Thus, the focus of the Quality Unit should be to ensure adherence to a process that identifies CAs/CDEs and confirms that they have been satisfactorily tested.

解談 中文解析與學習重點

📚 核心概念：Engineering Quality Process 的定位

製藥品質系統 (PQS) 是組織層級的品質框架，涵蓋所有生產、品質、工程活動。

工程品質流程 (Engineering Quality Process) 是 PQS 的子集，專門負責 C&Q 過程中的品質實踐。

製藥品質系統 (PQS)
↓
工程品質流程 (Engineering Quality Process)
↓
C&Q 程序、工具、模板

重要區分：

  • 品質 (Quality) = 產品/服務符合規格要求（Specified Requirements）
  • 合規 (Compliance) = 符合現行程序或組織要求（Procedures）

品質良好的流程自然帶來合規；但僅僅合規未必代表品質。ISPE 強調要追求品質本質，而非只追求形式上的合規。

🌟 ICH Q10 三大目標

  • ① 產品實現 (Product Realisation)：建立能穩定生產符合規格產品的流程與系統
  • ② 建立並維持控制狀態 (State of Control)：確保系統持續在受控狀態運作
  • ③ 促進持續改善 (Continual Improvement)：透過變更管理和 CAPA 機制持續優化

這三個目標與 C&Q 的核心使命完全一致：確認系統達到預期目的（Product Realisation），並維持在受控狀態（State of Control），並在生命週期中持續改善。

💡 戴明原則 (Deming's Principle) 的深層意義

戴明博士（W. Edwards Deming）是現代品質管理之父，他的核心主張是：

「不要靠篩選找出壞品，要改善流程讓壞品不產生。」

這個哲學對 C&Q 的啟示是：

  • 舊思維：設備上線後做大量 IQ/OQ/PQ 測試，測完才知道有沒有問題
  • 新思維（ISPE Vol.5）：在設計、採購、安裝每個環節就建入品質要求，讓關鍵屬性 (CA) 在最合適的節點被驗證，而不是都堆到最後的 PQ 去

這正是 ISPE Vol.5 推動 Risk-Based C&Q 的哲學基礎。

⚠️ FDA 2006 質量系統指南的重要性

FDA 2006 年發布的「Quality Systems Approach to Pharmaceutical CGMP Regulations」建立了六系統檢查模型（將在 11.2 節詳述），並明確說明「品質應建入產品，不能單靠測試來保證品質」。這是現代 GMP 的核心哲學，也是 ISPE Vol.5 整個方法論的基礎。

11.2

11.2 Application

FDA 6-System Inspection Model + Engineering Quality Process objectives — Pages 84–86

🌎 Original Text — English

The Engineering Quality Process should include aspects that support the C&Q effort, such as maintenance and calibration, change management program, critical utilities, and the procedures for the C&Q process.

The approach described in this section is based on the six-system inspection model described in the FDA 2006 Guidance, whereby an organization's overall quality system provides the foundation for the five manufacturing systems that function within it. The six systems are as follows:

  • Quality system
  • Production system
  • Facilities and equipment system
  • Laboratory control system
  • Materials system
  • Packaging and labeling system

Product and process development/knowledge may be added to this list or may be included as part of the production system.

As part of the overall PQS, the Engineering Quality Process plays a significant role in providing the practices and controls within the facilities and equipment system.

The application of an Engineering Quality Process also supports other requirements, such as for HSE and efficiency. Understanding this broader context and embracing Engineering Quality Process as a philosophy, rather than simply a collection of tools and templates, supports the design of a sound C&Q program that delivers systems suitable for the intended purpose using cost-effective solutions.

An Engineering Quality Process can help to transform the way in which organizations currently operate and to reinforce the efficient implementation of a science and risk-based approach to facility start-up. The Engineering Quality Process has additional objectives, including to:

  • Provide a lean framework to foster innovation, encourage (rather than obstruct) continual improvement, and facilitate the effective, cost-efficient, and focused application of time and resources
  • Provide the framework for linking the CAs to the CDEs and the mechanism to capture the traceability
  • Enable science and risk-based decisions for C&Q testing, based on internally and externally generated knowledge rather than just completion of generic forms
  • Optimize lifecycle activities through smart management and maintenance of process and engineering knowledge
  • Acknowledge the importance of personnel development by facilitating an SME-led environment which accredits or trains those individuals that are responsible for executing these practices

To establish an Engineering Quality Process, there needs to be a means for:

  • Implementing the Engineering Quality Process by senior management, which requires engineering and Quality Unit sponsorship
  • Implementing a proposed C&Q process (transforming current practice to a desired practice) with stakeholders engaged
  • Identifying stakeholders
  • Defining roles and responsibilities
  • Identifying where the organization or site is in terms of implementation of the new C&Q process and the pathway for implementation
  • Establishing what the stakeholders can contribute to the proposed process
  • Measuring and maintaining progress, suitability, and effectiveness
  • Informing and getting input from stakeholders on the proposed C&Q process

When properly applied, the Engineering Quality Process achieves the following:

  • Delivers systems that are suitable for the intended purpose
  • Provides a systematic engineering structure to define, develop, and implement efficient streamlined engineering processes
  • Enables the assurance of quality and compliance with corporate policies, external regulations, and industry standards
  • Reflects the scope and extent of the C&Q program, and provides the associated tools and templates
  • Incorporates technical expertise and best practices

Once the Engineering Quality Process is established, the relevant internal and external engineering organizations should be appropriately trained on the Engineering Quality Process program and associated tools (including GEP).

In addition, the Engineering Quality Process should be sufficiently flexible in its operation to obtain the benefits of standardized work practices by providing a toolbox of templates and forms without imposing unnecessary duplication where information is available in alternative formats and of an acceptable quality. The Engineering Quality Process should both facilitate and encourage the use of knowledge generated in a previous stage/step which is relevant to the C&Q activity currently being undertaken.

解談 中文解析與學習重點

💡 比喻：工具箱 vs. 思維框架

本節特別強調，Engineering Quality Process 不應被視為「一堆模板和表格的集合」，而是一種思維框架（Philosophy）。

類比：ISO 9001 不只是一份程序書清單，它是一種品質管理哲學。同樣地，Engineering Quality Process 是一套讓工程師和 QA 共同以「品質建入」為目標協作的思維方式。

當工程師理解「為什麼要做 CA 識別」，而不只是「要填哪張表」，整個 C&Q 程式的品質才能真正提升。

⚠️ Engineering Quality Process 的 5 個額外目標

  • 精實框架（Lean Framework）— 促進創新，減少不必要的工作
  • CA↔CDE 追溯機制 — 確保關鍵屬性與關鍵設計要素的可追溯性
  • 科學與風險導向決策 — 基於知識而非僅填寫通用表格
  • 生命週期知識管理 — 優化工程知識的維護與傳承
  • SME 主導環境 — 重視人員發展，培訓具備執行能力的 SME

🏢 CDMO 應用：如何建立 Engineering Quality Process 計畫

對於快速成長的 CDMO，建立 Engineering Quality Process 的實務步驟：

  • Step 1：高層背書 — 需要工程部門和 QA 的高層共同推動，列入公司品質目標
  • Step 2：現狀評估 — 識別目前 C&Q 實踐與理想狀態的差距（Gap Analysis）
  • Step 3：利害關係人識別 — 工程、製造、研發、QA、HSE 都是利害關係人
  • Step 4：角色與職責定義 — 明確誰負責 C&Q Plan、誰核准文件（見 11.4 節）
  • Step 5：建立文件體系 — 政策 → 標準 → SOP → 模板/協議書（見 11.3 節）
  • Step 6：訓練與執行 — 對所有相關人員進行 Engineering Quality Process 訓練
  • Step 7：度量與持續改善 — 建立指標追蹤進度，並透過管理審查持續優化

📋 FDA 六系統稽查模型 (6-System FDA Inspection Model)

FDA 2006 年指南建立了一個以「品質系統」為基礎，支撐其他五個製造系統的框架：

Engineering Quality Process 主要作用於設施與設備系統，但其品質實踐方法影響所有系統。

11.3 Deliverables — Document Hierarchy

Policies → Standards → Procedures → Executable Documents — Pages 86–87

🌎 Original Text — English

The main deliverable from a quality system is to ensure that systems are operating as intended as captured in documentation. A document hierarchy within the Engineering Quality Process helps to identify where the requirements/expectations are derived. Figure 11.1 illustrates a typical document hierarchy for a quality system.

Figure 11.1: Typical Document Hierarchy for Quality System

On the top of the hierarchy are policies/directives, which are the high-level documents that capture the expectations of the organization. The expectations for C&Q may be that the systems used to manufacture products have been qualified to be suitable for the intended purpose. These expectations may be part of an overall validation policy or detailed in an independent policy.

The next level is standards, which identify the minimum deliverables that are required for implementation of the program. The intent is to avoid different parts of the organization interpreting the intent of the policies/directives differently. For C&Q, the deliverable may be a Qualification Summary Report. Standards should:

  • Reference any specific relevant regulatory authority guidance
  • Be used to implement a harmonized approach throughout the organization — this should be an organization-wide model with a process for sites to learn from each other and practice a wider continual improvement path
  • Limit special conditions — the standard should not make allowances for individual site preferences or commitments, but should include a process to grant exceptions after proper review and approval

The next level is procedures, which may establish steps to be followed for the implementation of standards. Procedures may be corporate or site-specific documents. Procedures may be used for multiple sites but should avoid the term "global" since not all sites in the organization may follow them. Examples of expected procedures are for:

  • URS
  • System Risk Assessment (identification of CAs/CDEs)
  • DR/DQ
  • Protocols/Plans
  • Engineering Change Management
  • Reports

The bottom level of the hierarchy is executable documents, which are the plans, test scripts, reports, etc. that document compliance to the established requirements.

In summary, the Engineering Quality Process should:

  • Define how oversight by the Quality Unit on the program or project will be accomplished
  • Define approvers of documentation — the Quality Unit does not need to approve every document
  • Establish the linkage to the QRM process for C&Q (System Risk Assessment as described in Chapter 4)
  • Address integrated C&Q efforts — e.g., integrate the C&Q process so that one set of testing and documentation can satisfy C&Q requirements
  • Address what can be done as part of start-up and how much shakedown is done before documentation starts
  • Identify the level of testing and expectations for documentation — this should be commensurate with the risk to product quality
  • Define the vendor selection process — based on the information available, decide on the level of oversight and use of vendors and their documentation
  • Provide a means for measuring progress and maintenance in the C&Q program — it should establish a metric control plan to measure progress
  • Ensure continual improvement — senior management should be responsible for the quality system governance through management review

📋 文件層級 (Document Hierarchy) 圖解

Level 1：政策/指令 (Policies/Directives)
↓ 定義期望目標
Level 2：標準 (Standards)
↓ 定義最低交付要求
Level 3：程序 (Procedures / SOPs)
↓ 定義執行步驟
Level 4：可執行文件 (Executable Documents)

可執行文件 = 測試計畫、測試腳本、執行報告、協議書等

💡 比喻：建築規範 vs. 施工圖

就像建築業中：

  • 政策 = 建築法規（你必須蓋一棟安全的建築）
  • 標準 = 結構設計規範（承重牆最少要多厚）
  • 程序 = 施工作業程序（如何澆鑄混凝土）
  • 可執行文件 = 具體的施工圖與檢驗記錄

層級越高越抽象，越低越具體。每個層級都從上一層獲得方向，不能跳層解釋或自行詮釋。

⚠️ 標準的重要要求：避免例外氾濫

標準應「限制特殊條件」（Limit Special Conditions）——不應讓個別廠區基於偏好做出例外。若需要例外，必須有正式的審查和核准機制。

這對於多廠區的大型 CDMO 特別重要：不同廠區不應自行詮釋驗證標準，應採用統一的組織標準，並透過「廠區互學習」機制持續改善。

⚠️ QU 不需要核准所有文件

Engineering Quality Process 要明確定義「哪些文件需要 QU 核准」。QU 不需要核准每一份文件——這會造成資源浪費和瓶頸。文件核准要求應與風險等級相對應（參考 Section 6.4 核准矩陣）。

11.4 Roles and Responsibilities

5 functional groups in Engineering Quality Process — Page 88

🌎 Original Text — English

The roles and responsibilities for the Engineering Quality Process should be defined. The functions that contribute to the Engineering Quality Process are:

  • Engineering: Align engineering systems to support the Engineering Quality Process
  • Manufacturing: (representative(s) of the group that will use the system to manufacture products) Ensure the Engineering Quality Process will be able to deliver a system suitable for the intended purpose
  • Technical Support/Research: Provide technical guidance for product and process requirements and critical parameter ranges based on Basis of Design studies during product development
  • Quality Unit: Ensure the Engineering Quality Process fits within the QMS and agree with the provisions of the Engineering Quality Process
  • HSE: Ensure the Engineering Quality Process addresses personnel safety and impact to the environment and includes provisions to meet local, state, and federal requirements
解談 中文解析與學習重點

💡 跨部門協作是成功關鍵

Engineering Quality Process 的成功，需要上述五個功能的真正協作，而非各自為政。

常見失敗模式：

  • 工程部門不讓 QU 早期介入（導致設計問題遲至 OQ 才發現）
  • 製造部門未參與 CA 識別（導致確認的系統不符合實際使用需求）
  • 技術支援未提供關鍵參數範圍（導致 OQ 測試條件設定錯誤）
  • HSE 被排除在外（導致設備設計安全缺陷）

成功模式：在 C&Q Plan 撰寫階段就召集所有五個功能共同定義範疇、角色、交付文件。

⚠️ Technical Support/Research 的特殊角色

技術支援/研發部門負責提供「設計基礎研究」（Basis of Design Studies）中確定的關鍵參數範圍。這是 CA/CDE 識別的核心輸入來源。若研發人員未參與 C&Q 過程，驗證測試的合格標準很可能缺乏科學依據。

對 CDMO 而言，這尤其重要：客戶提供的目標產品概況（TPP）和製程設計空間，必須被轉化為設備資格確認的驗收標準。
