9.1

Introduction — 簡介

Regulatory requirement to maintain qualified state | 維護確效狀態的法規要求

Original Text (English) — pp. 69

There is a regulatory requirement to maintain qualified systems in a validated state, i.e., the qualification documents remain valid and the system remains suitable for the intended purpose (refer to Chapter 15 for the regulatory basis). Quality systems are in place to maintain that status and should be subject to internal audit to ensure robustness. These quality systems are:

  • Change control
  • Maintenance
  • Calibration
  • Deviation

It is regulatory expectation that there is also a periodic review process to ensure that systems can be considered qualified (refer to Chapter 15 for the regulatory basis).

This chapter describes an approach for periodic review where the frequency of review or requalification is established based on risk, the current level of process understanding and process performance, and regulatory requirements.

中文解說與學習重點

確效狀態 (Validated / Qualified State) 的法規要求

法規（ICH Q7、EU GMP Annex 15、FDA Process Validation Guidance）明確要求：確效工作不是一次性事件，而是需要持續維護。一旦系統完成確效並取得資格後，須透過品質系統確保其持續適用。

ISPE 指出四個維護確效狀態的品質系統：

  • 變更管制 (Change Control)：任何對設備、系統、製程的變更都必須評估對確效狀態的影響
  • 維護 (Maintenance)：預防性與矯正性維護，確保設備功能正常
  • 校正 (Calibration)：量測設備的定期校正，確保數據可信
  • 偏差 (Deviation)：偏差調查揭露系統問題並採取矯正措施

重點提示：定期審查 vs. 再確效

定期審查 (Periodic Review)是確認系統仍維持在確效狀態的評估活動；而再確效 (Requalification)是當發現問題後需要重新執行確效測試的活動。定期審查的目的是「確認不需要再確效」或「識別需要再確效的觸發點」。

9.1.1

Rationale — 目的與理由

Regulatory expectations & science/risk-based approach | 法規期望與科學風險導向方法

Original Text (English) — pp. 69–71

Certain systems have regulatory expectations to confirm ongoing system performance through periodic verification; such systems include sterilizing autoclaves, depyrogenation tunnels/ovens, and equipment with a Sterilize-in-Place (SIP) functionality (e.g., freeze dryer). Table 9.1 summarizes examples of periodic verification frequencies for systems with regulatory expectations.

Table 9.1: Examples of Periodic Verification Frequencies for Systems with Regulatory Expectations

1 For additional information, refer to ISPE Baseline Guide: Sterile Product Manufacturing Facilities. 2 Industry practice for ISO 8/Grade C is annual. 3 If the SIP process is continuously monitored, it is usually not revalidated. Local requirements should be verified.

For other types of systems, organizations have traditionally used an annual detailed review of the change history, maintenance, and operational records to provide this confirmation. However, this approach has typically provided a small return when compared to the considerable investment to conduct the review process. In addition, there is concern that, due to the scale of the effort involved, there is a significant risk that important performance signals may be missed.

This Guide proposes applying a science and risk-based approach to periodic review:

  • For a critical utilities system (such as HVAC, clean compressed air, pharmaceutical water and steam systems), routine monitoring includes:
    • Regular reviews of the results
    • Regular reporting of system performance trends, preventative maintenance and calibration metrics
    • Regular check of qualification drawings (typically biannually) and of system tagging (typically annually)

    If routine monitoring is performed, then no additional review is considered necessary. The monitoring confirms system performance and the drawing verification confirms that documentation is up-to-date.

  • For other systems (e.g., manufacturing systems), the relative potential risk to product quality for different system types is evaluated and the frequency of review is defined based on the risk.

This proposed approach is a triage process that looks for any unusual performance signals at a high level. If none are observed, then the review is complete. If there are signals observed, then a deeper review is conducted that may result in detailed investigation and testing.

Table 9.1 解讀：哪些系統有明確法規要求？

  • 無菌充填線：每半年媒體充填 (APS)（FDA 無菌指南 [23]）

關鍵說明：SIP 若連續監控中，通常不需要再驗證——這正是 ISPE 「監測取代審查」邏輯的具體例子。

比喻：傳統年度審查 = 年底一次性大掃除

傳統方式是每年花大量人力把一年內所有紀錄翻出來審一遍，就像年底大掃除。問題是：

  • 工作量龐大，審查人員容易疲勞，反而漏掉真正重要的異常訊號
  • 大部分系統運作良好，年度審查不產生附加價值——低回報、高投入

ISPE 的科學/風險導向方法像是「設置警報器」：平時持續監控，有警報才深入調查。效率更高、同樣符合法規。

Cat 0 的核心邏輯：持續監測 = 定期審查

WFI、純化水、HVAC 等關鍵公用設施已經有嚴格的日常監測計畫（TOC、電導度、微生物、粒子計數等）。ISPE 認為：這些持續監測本身就是最好的定期審查。因此這些系統被列為 Cat 0，不需要額外的正式定期審查。

這個邏輯相當重要——它讓組織可以將資源集中在真正需要審查的高風險系統上。

9.1.2

Principles / Concepts — 原則與概念

Mature quality systems & 8 performance metrics | 成熟品質系統與8項績效指標

Original Text (English) — pp. 71–72

This periodic review approach is most efficient if the organization/site has mature quality systems and the associated quality system performance metrics. These metrics could include:

  • Monitoring of maintenance to ensure scheduled activities are not delayed beyond the permitted calendar window
    • Monitoring of breakdown/unplanned maintenance activities for frequency and recurrence of specific issues
  • Monitoring of calibration to ensure any scheduled checks/activities are not delayed beyond the permitted calendar window
  • Robust change control system with categorization of changes
  • Robust deviation system with categorization of deviations
  • Robust review of computer system audit trails and security at procedurally controlled intervals including:
    • Confirming users are not using generic names or shared accounts
    • Ensuring that there are no redundant users or obsolete users, e.g. for staff who have changed roles or left the organization (i.e., good user access management)
    • Reviewing the audit trail of administrator actions, ensuring a quality system record justifies each action, and listing the audit trails reviewed
    • Reviewing the audit trail of other user category actions, ensuring a quality system record justifies the actions (if applicable), and listing the audit trails reviewed
    • Reviewing the audit trail for changes to critical configuration settings identified in the data integrity assessment, ensuring a quality system record justifies the changes, and listing the audit trails reviewed
    • Verifying that audit trail reviews were performed as dictated in the system administrator SOP and operational SOP
    • Making sure the Quality Unit reviews the audit trail as verifier
    • Verifying backup and archiving of GMP critical data

The use of these performance metrics can minimize the amount of effort required to obtain the information for the periodic review.

Where these mature quality systems are not in place, the same principles can be used, but the process may not be as efficient.

8 大品質系統績效指標

ISPE 列出定期審查最有效率的前提條件是擁有成熟的品質系統，以下 8 項指標是核心衡量點：

  • 維護計畫完成率（排程維護是否準時執行）
  • 非計畫性維護 / 故障維護的頻率與重複性
  • 校正計畫完成率（是否在允許時窗內完成）
  • 變更管制的類別與數量分布
  • 偏差的類別與數量（根本原因是否指向設備）
  • 電腦系統稽核追蹤審查（詳見右側專欄）
  • 備份與歸檔驗證（GMP 關鍵數據）
  • 品質系統整體內稽結果

電腦稽核追蹤審查 (Computer Audit Trail Review) — 8 個必查項目

這是 Section 9.1.2 中最常被忽略的部分，FDA 21 CFR Part 11 及 EU GMP Annex 11 均有相關要求，以下 8 點必須逐項審查：

  1. 通用帳號/共用帳號：確認無人使用 "admin" 或共用帳號登入
  2. 使用者存取管理：已離職或轉職員工是否已停用帳號（用戶存取管理 UAM）
  3. 管理員操作稽核追蹤：每筆管理員操作是否均有品質系統紀錄佐證
  4. 一般用戶操作稽核追蹤：如適用，每筆操作是否均有紀錄佐證
  5. 關鍵組態設定變更：來自數據完整性評估的關鍵設定，任何變更均須有品質系統記錄支持
  6. 稽核追蹤審查執行確認：系統管理員 SOP 與操作 SOP 規定的審查是否確實執行
  7. 品質部門審查者確認：品質部門是否作為驗證者審查了稽核追蹤
  8. 備份與歸檔：GMP 關鍵數據的備份與歸檔已驗證

CDMO 實務思考：稽核追蹤審查的挑戰

許多 CDMO 擁有多個電腦化系統（LIMS、MES、SCADA、EBR 等），每個系統都需要執行上述 8 項審查。常見缺失包括：

  • 沒有 SOP 定義審查頻率（「定期」沒有明確定義）
  • IT 部門執行審查但品質部門未作為驗證者
  • 共用帳號問題未被視為 GMP 違規

建議：建立電腦化系統清單，每個系統指定責任人，定義審查頻率並記入 SOP。

9.2

Periodic Review Approach & Prerequisites

Two-phase overview & required inputs | 兩階段架構與先決條件

Original Text (English) — pp. 72–73

The periodic review approach proposed in this Guide consists of two phases:

  • Phase 1: System Categorization and Schedule Assignment
  • Phase 2: Periodic Review Execution
    • Initial Assessment (Tier A)
    • Second Level Assessment (Tier B)
    • Third Level Assessment (Tier C)

It is strongly recommended that the period review process be led by an individual who has a broad range of experience.

The Tier B and Tier C reviews should be conducted by a small team where necessary. This small team should consist of individuals who are knowledgeable in the system operating, maintenance, and calibration history; these are typically the system owner, maintenance, and a Quality Unit representative. The review should be approved, at a minimum, by the system owner and the Quality Unit.

9.2.1 Prerequisites

The required prerequisites for the periodic review process include:

  • List of validated manufacturing systems
  • Copy of relevant current regulations (i.e., those for the markets the facility supplies)
  • For each system:
    • As-built or as-is record drawing (qualification record drawing)
    • Change system metrics or system change history
    • Maintenance and calibration system metrics or system maintenance and calibration history
    • Deviation system metrics or deviations related to the system performance
    • System validation/qualification documents
    • Risk assessment (if available)
中文解說與學習重點

兩階段架構總覽

Phase 1（系統分類）：決定哪些系統需要審查、多久審查一次，是整個計畫的「前置規劃」。

Phase 2（執行）：實際執行審查，採用 A→B→C 三層篩選流程。大多數系統在 Tier A 即可完成（若無異常訊號），只有真正有問題的系統才需要深入到 Tier B 或 Tier C。

先決條件的實務意義

ISPE 列出的先決條件看似基本，卻常常是 CDMO 的痛點：

  • 確效系統清單：是否有完整且最新的清單？系統退役時是否及時移除？
  • 現行法規版本：是否追蹤各目標市場（FDA/EMA/PMDA）的最新版法規？
  • As-built 圖面：圖面是否隨每次變更維護更新？這是最常見的缺失之一。
  • 風險評估：若有，可以大幅加速分類決策；若無，分類仍可進行但較費時。

比喻：多專科醫療團隊

ISPE 建議 Tier B/C 審查由小型跨功能團隊（系統負責人、維護、品質）共同執行，就像醫院處理複雜病例時會召集多科會診。不同背景的人看到不同角度的問題，比單人審查更全面，同時也控制規模。

系統分類 (System Categorization) 的核心邏輯

分類的判斷依據是對產品品質的潛在影響程度，而製程的位置（早期 vs 後期）是重要判斷因子：

  • 早期製程（發酵、粗純化）：後續有多道製程和測試作為篩網，設備問題較不易直接影響最終產品 → 較低風險
  • 後期製程（Fill/Finish、滅菌）：直接影響最終產品品質，且後面無緩衝 → 較高風險

四個分類說明

  • Cat 0 無需正式定期審查：已有持續監測或每次使用均有完整性測試（如無菌過濾的完整性測試）。WFI、HVAC、壓縮空氣、包裝系統均屬此類。
  • Cat 1 遵照法規規定頻率：如滅菌釜依 EU GMP Annex 1 每年再驗證，無需在此基礎上再加定期審查。
  • Cat 2 每2年審查：Fill/Finish 充填系統、層析純化設備等——重要但有監測作為安全網。
  • Cat 3 每3年審查：錠劑塗裝機、錠片壓製機、發酵槽等複雜系統。注意：頻率低≠不重要，而是因為系統有足夠的製程監控作為早期預警。

常見誤解：Cat 3 頻率最低 = 最不重要？

發酵槽 (Bioreactor) 被列為 Cat 3（每3年），並非因為它不重要，而是因為製程本身有大量連續監控（pH、DO、溫度、攪拌等），這些監控數據本身就是持續的性能確認。因此不需要像高風險的 Fill/Finish (Cat 2) 那樣頻繁地正式定期審查。

CDMO 應用：如何建立系統分類清單

CDMO 實施步驟：

  1. 建立所有已確效設備/系統的完整清單（連結到確效文件 IQ/OQ/PQ）
  2. 對每個系統進行分類（Cat 0–3），記錄分類依據（SME 評估意見）
  3. 根據 Table 9.3 設定審查排程，將 Cat 2 和 Cat 3 系統排入多年計畫
  4. Cat 1 系統追蹤法規規定的年度再驗證時程，不另行定期審查
  5. 定期（至少每兩年）複審分類是否仍適當

比喻：汽車保養 vs. 航空器大修

Cat 0 如同汽車的油表警示燈——持續監測，有問題才進廠。Cat 1 如同飛機的法定大修（MRO）——法規規定固定週期，不可商量。Cat 2/3 則像高鐵的定期維護——依複雜程度設定不同頻率，在排定時間進行全面評估。

9.4

Phase 2: Periodic Review Execution — Overview

Three-tier triage across four quality systems | 三層篩選流程與四大品質系統

Original Text (English) — pp. 75–76

Using the frequency schedules established in Phase 1, the periodic reviews can be performed using a three-tier approach which involves reviewing the following four quality systems:

  • GMP compliance expectations
  • Change control system
  • Maintenance/calibration system
  • Deviation system

The three-tier approach is summarized as follows:

  • An initial assessment (Tier A) is done at a high level. If this initial assessment shows any signs that the system is not operating consistently, then the assessment progresses to the next level; if not, then the review is considered complete.
  • The second level assessment (Tier B) is more detailed. If this assessment shows that there are any issues, then the assessment progresses to the next level. If this assessment concludes that the system is still performing satisfactorily, then the review is complete.
  • The third level assessment (Tier C) involves defining and completing the actions required to determine the system to be suitable for the intended purpose, with the necessary supporting documentation updated as necessary.

The assessment period is the time period since the previous periodic review or since the original qualification.

Note: When a periodic review program is initially introduced, it is typical to cover the systems in each schedule class within the associated timeframe. For example, for systems with a two-year review period, the initial review of all systems in that schedule class can be completed in the first two years.

中文解說與學習重點

三層篩選流程：核心創新

這是 ISPE 定期審查方法的最核心創新。傳統做法是對所有系統執行同樣深度的年度審查；ISPE 的三層篩選則是：

A
初步評估 (Tier A)：高層次查詢，大多數系統在此完成
↓ 若發現異常訊號（Signal）
B
深入評估 (Tier B)：SME 評估，若無疑慮即完成
↓ 若對確效狀態有疑慮
C
矯正行動 (Tier C)：制定行動計畫，開立不符合紀錄

關鍵點：沒有訊號 = 審查完成，不需要為了「看起來做了很多工作」而進行無謂的深入審查。

四大品質系統 × 三個層級 = 12 個評估模塊

這個矩陣結構讓審查工作模組化：每個品質系統獨立評估，發現訊號的才升級到下一層，未發現訊號的即完成。例如：Tier A 查完四個系統，發現只有「偏差系統」有訊號，則只有偏差系統升級到 Tier B 評估，其餘三個系統的審查已完成。

比喻：急診分診 (Triage) 機制

ISPE 直接使用了 "triage process" 這個詞。急診分診的邏輯是：不是所有病人都需要立即手術，而是依嚴重程度分配醫療資源。同樣地，定期審查不是所有系統都需要深度調查，而是有訊號才深入，無訊號即完成，確保資源用在真正需要的地方。

「評估期間」的重要性

評估期間 (Assessment Period) = 自上次定期審查結束（或原始確效完成）至今的時間段。所有查詢均針對此期間的紀錄，確保審查的全面性且不遺漏過渡期的問題。

當首次導入定期審查計畫時，ISPE 允許在類別規定的時間框架內分批完成（例如 Cat 2 系統可在前兩年內陸續完成首次審查），不需要立刻全部完成。

9.4.1

Initial Assessment — Tier A

High-level signal detection across four quality systems | A 層：高層次異常訊號偵測

Original Text (English) — pp. 76–77

To perform the initial assessment, the records from the four quality systems are reviewed to identify any concerns related to the qualified state of the system being assessed.

GMP Compliance Expectations (Tier A)

If there is an organization/site quality system that monitors changes in regulatory requirements/expectations and proactively addresses changes within the organization/site, then this category does not need to be reviewed as part of periodic review.

If this system is not in place, then the Quality Unit should:

  • Review changes in the relevant regulatory regulations and guidances for the assessment period, with the current scope of qualification reviewed to ensure it meets any changes in the requirements where necessary.
  • Review related audit observations and determine if there are any changes in regulatory expectations.

Change Control System (Tier A)

For the system being assessed:

  • Perform a query in the change control system database for the assessment period
  • Review the query results and determine if there have been any reportable change requests with qualification impact that can affect the system quality attributes

Note: Change records that have been determined to be low risk, and thus are not reportable to regulatory agencies, do not need to be reviewed as part of the periodic review process.

Maintenance/Calibration System (Tier A)

For the system being assessed:

  • Perform a query in the maintenance management system for the assessment period
  • Determine if there were repeated corrective maintenance and/or calibration records

Deviation System (Tier A)

For the system being assessed:

  • Perform a query of the deviations for the assessment period
  • Review the query results for deviations with potential product quality impact where the root cause has been attributed to the system being assessed

Note: Deviations that have already been determined to have little or no risk to product quality do not need to be reviewed as part of the periodic review process.

After this initial assessment of each quality system:

  • If any concerns (regarding the potential impact to the qualified state) within a quality system are identified, then a second level assessment needs to be performed. Note that the scope of the second level assessment is limited to the quality system(s) of concern.
  • If no concerns are identified, then the assessment for that quality system is complete.
中文解說與學習重點

Tier A：四大品質系統的查詢重點

Tier A 是純粹的查詢與篩選，不需要深度分析，目的是找到「訊號 (Signal)」：

  • GMP 合規：若組織已有主動追蹤法規變化的系統，此項可免查。若無，則需檢視評估期間是否有法規修訂影響目前的確效範疇。
  • 變更管制：查詢評估期間是否有需申報且影響確效的變更。低風險/不可申報的變更不需要審查。
  • 維護/校正：查詢是否有重複的矯正性維護 (Corrective Maintenance)——這是設備退化的早期警訊。
  • 偏差：查詢根本原因指向設備的偏差，且有產品品質影響。已評估為低風險的偏差不需要審查。

關鍵術語：矯正性維護 vs. 預防性維護

  • 預防性維護 (Preventive Maintenance, PM)：依預定排程執行的例行維護，如定期更換耗材、潤滑保養。PM 按時完成是品質系統成熟的指標。
  • 矯正性維護 (Corrective Maintenance, CM)：設備故障後才執行的維修。重複發生的 CM（Repeated CM）是 Tier A 審查的觸發訊號——表示設備可能有系統性退化問題，需要進一步評估是否影響確效狀態。

GMP 合規評估的條件邏輯（重要！）

ISPE 允許組織用「主動法規追蹤系統」取代 Tier A 的 GMP 合規評估。這意味著：若公司已建立定期追蹤 FDA/EMA/各地衛生主管機關法規更新並採取行動的程序，則這個品質系統的 Tier A 評估可以免除。這是 ISPE 避免重複工作的實用設計。

CDMO 實務：建立 Tier A 查詢模板

建議為每個系統建立標準化查詢模板，內容包含：

  • 系統名稱與識別碼
  • 評估期間（起始日期 ~ 當前日期）
  • 各品質系統查詢結果（有/無訊號）
  • 若有訊號：列出訊號描述，決定是否升級至 Tier B
  • 審查者簽名與日期

大多數系統在 Tier A 應可在 15–30 分鐘內完成查詢（假設品質系統已良好維護且可快速查詢）。

9.4.2

Second Level Assessment — Tier B

Deeper SME review of flagged quality systems | B 層：SME 深入評估有訊號的品質系統

Original Text (English) — pp. 77

The second level assessment should only be performed on each quality system category that was determined during the initial assessment to require further assessment. All second level assessment activities should be performed by representatives (SMEs) from the same departments as the initial assessment team.

GMP Compliance Expectations (Tier B)

Using the information from the initial assessment:

  • Evaluate the impact of the regulatory changes identified in the initial assessment
  • In the judgment of the assessment team, determine if the changes present concerns about the qualified state of the system

Change Control System (Tier B)

Using the information from the initial assessment:

  • Evaluate the changes identified in the initial assessment
  • In the judgment of the assessment team, determine if the number and/or type of changes identified present concerns about the qualified state of the system

Maintenance/Calibration System (Tier B)

Using the information from the initial assessment:

  • Review the maintenance and calibration records identified in the initial assessment (including corrective maintenance)
  • In the judgment of the assessment team, determine if the records present concerns about the qualified state of the system

Deviation System (Tier B)

Using the information from the initial assessment:

  • Review the deviations with potential product impact identified in the initial assessment
  • In the judgment of the assessment team, determine if the deviations indicate that the system performance is not consistent and thus may not be performing in a qualified state
中文解說與學習重點

Tier B：從「有訊號」到「有疑慮」的判斷

Tier B 的核心問題是：「這個訊號是否影響系統的確效狀態？」

Tier A 只是確認「有訊號存在」，而 Tier B 需要 SME 團隊做出品質判斷：

  • 變更的數量/類型是否顯示系統已超出確效範疇？
  • 重複的矯正性維護是否代表設備已無法穩定執行關鍵功能？
  • 偏差的發生是否呈現系統性趨勢，顯示系統不再處於確效狀態？

Tier B 結論分兩種：無疑慮→完成 或 有疑慮→升級至 Tier C。

Tier B 的「範圍限制」原則

重要：Tier B 只針對 Tier A 中被標記的品質系統 執行。若 Tier A 評估四個品質系統，只有「維護/校正系統」發現訊號，則 Tier B 只需評估維護/校正，不需要重新評估其他三個。

這個設計確保審查工作與風險對應——有問題才深入，有效控制總工作量。

比喻：醫療診斷的逐步深入

Tier A 是抽血檢查報告（快速篩選），Tier B 是看診時醫生詳細問診與理學檢查（深入評估），Tier C 是安排進一步檢查與治療（矯正行動）。醫生（SME）在 Tier B 做出臨床判斷：這個異常是否需要進一步處置？

Tier B 評估的文件要求

Tier B 需要形成書面評估記錄，記錄：

  • 升級原因（來自 Tier A 的哪個訊號）
  • SME 團隊成員（系統負責人、維護代表、品質部門）
  • 評估依據（具體審查的紀錄編號或數據）
  • 結論：有無確效狀態疑慮
  • 若無疑慮：記錄審查完成，系統仍處於確效狀態
  • 若有疑慮：升級至 Tier C，啟動矯正行動
9.4.3

Third Level Assessment — Tier C

Defining actions to restore qualified state | C 層：制定矯正行動恢復確效狀態

Original Text (English) — pp. 77–78

The third level assessment involves defining and completing the actions required to determine the system to be suitable for the intended purpose, with the necessary supporting documentation updated as necessary.

At this level:

  • Actions are defined to restore or confirm the qualified state of the system
  • A non-conformance record is generated to capture and track the required actions
  • Actions may include requalification testing, documentation updates, or other corrective measures as determined by the assessment team
  • All supporting documentation must be updated to reflect the current state of the system

Refer to Chapter 25 (Appendix 11) for an example periodic review form. Refer to Chapter 26 (Appendix 12) for information regarding periodic review of Controlled Temperature Chambers (CTCs).

中文解說與學習重點

Tier C：從「疑慮」到「行動」的閉環

Tier C 是品質系統的矯正循環，確保發現的問題得到正式追蹤與解決：

  • 開立不符合紀錄 (Non-Conformance Record)：正式記錄問題，確保可追蹤
  • 定義矯正行動：可能包括再確效測試、文件更新、設備修復等
  • 更新文件：確效文件、圖面、SOP 必須反映系統現狀
  • 追蹤完成狀態：透過 CAPA 系統確認行動完成並有效

Tier C 可能觸發的行動類型

依據問題嚴重程度，Tier C 可能觸發：

  • 文件更新：更新 as-built 圖面、SOPs、確效計畫
  • 補充確效測試：針對特定功能執行補充 OQ 或 PQ 測試
  • 完整再確效 (Requalification)：若系統已顯著變更或性能嚴重偏離
  • 設備更換或升級：若矯正性維護已無法解決根本問題
  • 製程調整：若問題來自製程參數而非設備本身

CDMO 應用：Tier C 與 CAPA 系統的整合

建議將 Tier C 的矯正行動直接整合入公司的 CAPA (Corrective and Preventive Action) 系統：

  • 以「定期審查」作為 CAPA 觸發來源類別
  • 在 CAPA 中記錄根本原因分析（如偏差/維護趨勢）
  • 設定行動完成期限與責任人
  • CAPA 關閉前需有效性評估（Effectiveness Check）
  • 關閉後更新定期審查記錄，確認系統已恢復確效狀態

章節總結 Section Summary — Chapter 9: Periodic Review

本章介紹了 ISPE 科學與風險導向的定期審查方法，主要架構如下：

  • 法規基礎：法規要求維護確效狀態，四大品質系統（變更管制、維護、校正、偏差）加上定期審查共同支撐這個要求。
  • 關鍵創新 — Cat 0 的邏輯：WFI、HVAC 等關鍵公用設施已有持續監測計畫，持續監測本身即為最佳定期審查，因此 Cat 0 系統無需正式定期審查。
  • 系統分類 (Phase 1)：依產品品質影響風險分為 Cat 0–3；Cat 2 每2年、Cat 3 每3年執行定期審查；Cat 1 遵照法規規定的固定頻率（如滅菌釜年度再驗證）。
  • 三層篩選流程 (Phase 2)：Tier A → B → C 的漸進深入設計，大多數系統在 Tier A 即完成審查（無訊號），只有真正有問題的系統才需要深入調查，大幅提升效率。
  • 電腦稽核追蹤審查：Section 9.1.2 列出的 8 個必查項目（含使用者存取管理、管理員操作追蹤、備份驗證等）是數據完整性合規的重要組成，常被忽略。
  • 先決條件：完整的確效系統清單、最新法規版本、as-built 圖面、品質系統歷史紀錄是執行本方法的基礎。

對 CDMO 的啟示：導入此方法的優先事項是 (1) 建立系統分類清單 (2) 確保品質系統可快速查詢 (3) 建立電腦稽核追蹤 SOP (4) 培訓跨功能審查團隊。初次導入可依 Cat 2/3 的時間框架分批完成所有系統的首次審查。
