6.1

Introduction

Why planning is essential — C&Q as a managed engineering activity from URS to acceptance

Original Text (English)

6.1 Introduction

C&Q is a planned, documented, and managed engineering activity that starts with the establishment of the URS and continues to start-up and acceptance and release of systems. A well-planned program is essential and leads to an efficient, "right the first time", and cost-effective C&Q effort; the result is the delivery of systems that operate in a safe manner, meet established design requirements and stakeholder expectations, and are suitable for the intended purpose.

Planning provides the ties between process understanding and system design; it focuses qualification efforts to enhance the likelihood that the system will deliver quality products while maintaining a controlled operating state. It also acts as a means for communication between members of the multidisciplinary project team.

This chapter focuses on the formal C&Q Plan. There are other plans that can be generated, such as overall project plans or cleanliness plans, which have great value but are not covered in this chapter. Refer to Chapter 21 (Appendix 7) for a listing of supporting plans and general concepts for each.

Regulatory expectations for C&Q planning are described in Chapter 15 (Appendix 1).

中文解說 Commentary

C&Q 的本質：有計畫的工程活動

ISPE 強調 C&Q（Commissioning and Qualification，委試與確效）不是一個「做完測試就結束」的程序，而是一個有計畫、有文件、有管理的工程活動，從 URS 寫好的那一天起，一直延伸到系統正式接收釋放為止。

這個視角很重要：它把 C&Q 定位為工程專案的一部份，而不是質量部門在最後補做的文書工作。

比喻：蓋房子的施工計畫

C&Q Planning 就像建設公司在動工前要先做的「施工計畫書」。

你不能邊做邊想：「今天澆混凝土，明天再決定要不要驗收」。每一道工序都要排程、明確誰負責、何時完成、驗收標準是什麼。

C&Q Plan 就是那份施工計畫書，確保整個團隊（工程、品保、生產、廠商）都照同一份劇本走。

注意：本章聚焦正式 C&Q Plan

書中提醒讀者，還有很多其他計畫（例如清潔計畫、整體專案計畫），但本章只討論正式的 C&Q Plan。其他計畫請參考 Chapter 21（Appendix 7）。

6.1.1

Purpose and Benefits

Integrated schedule, critical path method, cross-team communication, and stakeholder expectations

Original Text (English)

6.1.1 Purpose and Benefits

A well-conceived and executed C&Q Plan can greatly facilitate a timely and cost-effective qualification effort. It is essential to have excellent communication, planning, and coordination between the Quality Unit, vendors, operations, automation, engineering, construction, commissioning, and qualification personnel, to name a few, to deliver a project on time, within budget, and meeting all expectations. A C&Q plan facilitates this communication.

An important secondary goal of the C&Q Plan is to foster an interdisciplinary team approach to C&Q. Well-planned project logistics are crucial to the success of every project. A well-coordinated effort leads to a high quality, effective, and efficient C&Q project.

A single, integrated, overall schedule should be developed with input from the entire project team. This schedule should cover all systems (utilizing defined system boundaries as discussed in Section 3.2) and phases of the projects lifecycle from construction to Process Validation/PPQ completion. This schedule should be updated and issued regularly. These updates should not drive a C&Q Plan revision, but the C&Q Plan should identify how the schedule updates will be captured and communicated.

An integrated schedule, using a critical path method, can increase the awareness of dependent activities and provide a clear understanding of the critical path. Including all project team members in the schedule development will engage the team members and foster better planning. All team members should comprehend the schedule format. Ordering long delivery time items early can help timelines, but consideration should be made to ensure the process requirements for those items are understood and included in the requisition. Understanding when to engage support for C&Q helps to support the success of the project.

A well-planned effort can ensure that the user, business, regulatory, and patient expectations are met. All efforts to ensure the system is suitable for the intended purpose (e.g., start-up, balancing, trial runs, establishing maintenance programs) should be included in the planning phase since this is where the formality of the documentation can be established. These expectations include:

  • Systems are delivered on time and within budget
  • Systems meet approved user requirements and are suitable for the intended purpose
  • Stakeholder needs, including speed to market, are met
  • Products produced from the systems meet defined quality standards
中文解說 Commentary

C&Q Plan 的三大效益

  • 跨部門溝通橋樑：讓品保、廠商、生產、自動化、工程、施工等不同團隊，有共同遵循的計畫文件
  • 跨學科團隊整合：打破部門壁壘，建立一個整合性的 C&Q 專案團隊
  • 利害關係人期望管理：準時交付、符合預算、符合預期用途、確保產品品質

關鍵術語：Critical Path Method（關鍵路徑法）

CPM 是專案管理中常用的排程技術。它找出專案中最長的依賴活動鏈，這條鏈上任何一個活動的延遲，都會直接導致整個專案延遲。

在 C&Q 中的意義：例如，HVAC 調試必須在無菌充填區 IQ 之前完成；IQ 完成才能做 OQ；OQ 完成才能做 PQ。這條鏈就是關鍵路徑。提早辨識它，才能及早採購長交期設備、安排人力資源。

比喻：排程失控的後果

想像一條填充線安裝專案，業主希望 10 月開始 PQ，但沒有人提前排好關鍵路徑：結果 8 月才發現水系統的 EP（認可廠商評估）還沒做，設備還沒到廠，OQ 就不能開始。

Critical Path Method 的目的，就是讓這種「到最後才發現問題」的狀況，在計畫階段就被發現和處理。

重點：排程更新 ≠ 計畫修訂

ISPE 特別指出：定期更新排程是正常的，但排程更新不應觸發 C&Q Plan 的版本修訂。C&Q Plan 應說明排程更新如何被紀錄與傳達，而非每次更新排程就重新審批計畫。這是很實用的操作建議。

6.2

C&Q Planning Approach

Level of planning based on project size, complexity, and risk — when a separate C&Q Plan is needed

Original Text (English)

6.2 C&Q Planning Approach

The amount of planning, documentation, reporting, and level of detail of the C&Q Plan should be based on expectations of the organization/site, size and complexity of the system or project, and the potential impact on product quality and patient safety.

A C&Q Plan should not repeat details that are in other documents but needs to capture the organization of those documents in order to facilitate an efficient project execution.

A separate C&Q Plan is not always necessary. Aspects of C&Q planning can be included with other documents, such as a validation plan or change control. For example, the simple change of adding a small system would not need a separate C&Q Plan; the requirements could be incorporated into a change control plan. More complex systems would benefit from a separate C&Q Plan, for example, the installation of a filling line with terminal sterilization and secondary packaging or the doubling in capacity of a manufacturing site including additional utilities to support the expansion.

中文解說 Commentary

計畫深度應與專案規模成比例

ISPE 採取風險比例原則（risk-proportionate approach）：計畫的詳盡程度取決於：

  • 組織/廠址的期望（公司內規）
  • 系統或專案的大小與複雜度
  • 對產品品質和病患安全的潛在影響

什麼情況需要獨立的 C&Q Plan？

不需要獨立計畫（納入變更管制即可）：
新增一台小型輔助設備（例如，更換一台蠕動泵）

需要獨立 C&Q Plan：

  • 新安裝含最終滅菌和二次包裝的充填線
  • 廠址產能倍增，含公用系統擴建（WFI、純蒸汽、潔淨空調）

判斷標準：專案越複雜、影響越大，越需要一份獨立的正式計畫文件來統籌管理。

C&Q Plan 不應重複其他文件

計畫的功能是「組織文件架構」，而非「複製文件內容」。它告訴讀者「哪個文件在哪裡、誰負責、什麼時候要完成」，而不是把 URS 或 SRA 的內容再抄一遍。

6.2.1

Timing of C&Q Plan

The approval sequence rule: C&Q Plan approved BEFORE test instructions, test instructions approved BEFORE execution

Original Text (English)

6.2.1 Timing of C&Q Plan

In an overall scheme, the creation of the C&Q Plan falls in the process map as shown in Figure 1.1 (Chapter 1).

It is important to note that C&Q planning begins prior to the creation of any formal written plans. C&Q planning starts during the planning and design phase of a project and continues through project implementation. A documented strategy may be developed as a precursor to a C&Q Plan, to help align team members with the process and personnel who will be responsible for C&Q.

A C&Q Plan should begin early in a project in the concept or early basic engineering phase.

The C&Q Plan needs to be approved before the respective testing documents (i.e., test instructions) are approved, and such test instructions need to be approved before test execution starts to support qualification. This is recommended for commissioning plans and test instructions.

The C&Q Plan should address how existing test documentation and test results from commissioning (including FAT, SAT, etc.) are referenced in qualification documents, as appropriate.

中文解說 Commentary

審批時序規則（業界最常被違反的規定之一）

ISPE 明確規定了三個必須依序進行的審批步驟：

  1. C&Q Plan 審批完成（計畫核准）
  2. 測試指令（Test Instructions）審批完成（IQ/OQ/PQ Protocol 核准）
  3. 開始執行測試

這個序列不能顛倒，更不能跳過。

為什麼業界常違反這個規則？

在實際 CDMO 或設備安裝專案中，常見情況：

  • 設備到廠了，工程師急著「先跑一下 IQ」，Protocol 還在品保審批中
  • C&Q Plan 還沒最終核准，但委試工程師已開始 FAT
  • 為了趕上 Launch 時程，OQ 執行完才補做 Protocol 審批

這些行為在法規稽查時，都可能被認定為程序不合規（Protocol not approved prior to execution）。一旦被 FDA 483 或 Warning Letter 點名，補救成本遠大於當初多等幾天審批的時間。

計畫應從概念階段或基本工程初期開始

C&Q 計畫不是在設備到廠前幾週才開始寫的文件。ISPE 建議在概念階段（Concept Phase）或基本工程初期（Early Basic Engineering）就開始規劃。

這是因為：URS 的品質要求、系統分類結果、SRA 的 CDE 辨識，都必須在計畫階段就確定，才能定義測試的範疇與接受標準。

6.2.2

Lifecycle of C&Q Plan

Kept up-to-date through the project; minor departures documented in Qualification Summary Report

Original Text (English)

6.2.2 Lifecycle of C&Q Plan

The C&Q Plan is not finalized until after the risk management process has reached a point to capture the impact of product quality decisions or of establishing the CDEs.

C&Q Plans are expected to be kept up-to-date as changes to the project are made. Revisions of the C&Q Plan may be necessary as new information is learned or as modifications to the design are made, e.g., changes in scope (additions or removals), timing, or identification of additional CAs/CDEs.

Approval is recommended for these early plans and for subsequent changes made by the revision process; this ensures agreement at various stages of the C&Q approach. Changes to the C&Q Plan should be reviewed and approved by the original approvers (or their designate) and a revision history should be maintained of significant changes to the Plan.

It should be noted that revisions may not always be necessary. Minor departures from the C&Q Plan may be documented in the Qualification Summary Report with appropriate justification and rationale, as deemed appropriate by the project team.

At the end of a project, a written document capturing the completion and closure of the project should be created. See Chapter 8 for more details regarding acceptance and release.

Changes to the C&Q Plan should be handled under change management (as discussed in Chapter 12) to ensure that as changes are implemented, the impact to C&Q efforts is addressed.

中文解說 Commentary

C&Q Plan 是活文件（Living Document）

C&Q Plan 不是寫完就鎖起來的文件。隨著專案進展，當設計變更、範疇調整、新的 CDE 被辨識時，計畫需要相應更新。

版本修訂原則：

  • 有重大變更 → 啟動正式版本修訂，由原核准者（或其代理人）審批
  • 輕微偏離 → 可記錄在 Qualification Summary Report（確效總結報告）中，加上理由說明，不一定要重改計畫

關鍵術語：Qualification Summary Report（確效總結報告）

確效總結報告是在整個確效活動完成後，彙整所有測試結果、偏差處理、執行摘要的最終報告文件。

ISPE 允許將輕微偏離 C&Q Plan 的情況記錄在這份報告中（加上說明），而不需要每次都修訂 C&Q Plan。這是一個務實的彈性規定。

計畫變更須走變更管理

C&Q Plan 的任何修訂都應透過變更管理（Change Management，Chapter 12）處理，以確保變更對 C&Q 工作的影響被適當評估和記錄。

這防止了「私下改計畫卻沒通知品保」的情形發生。

6.2.3

Elements to be Addressed

Scope of testing, document source identification, coordination with overall project execution plan

Original Text (English)

6.2.3 Elements to be Addressed

This section is intended to be a discussion of the elements that need to be addressed as part of C&Q planning, not the documents where they are captured.

6.2.3.1 Scope of Testing

System classification (as described in Chapter 3) identifies which systems require qualification. The System Risk Assessment (as described in Chapter 4) identifies which elements in those systems require qualification. The URS (as described in Chapter 2) and System Risk Assessment establish the scope of testing for qualification. The equipment specifications establish the scope of testing for commissioning.

The testing approach is based on the most efficient method to test the system and should be captured in the C&Q Plan. If systems are tested separately, the C&Q Plan should include a final test verifying that all systems are able to work together prior to acceptance and release. If PPQ is included in a separate plan, the linkage to the execution of that critical phase should be captured.

6.2.3.2 Identification of Document Source

Engineering firms, vendors, contractors, quality, engineering, and owners provide the documentation necessary for design, construction, commissioning, qualification, operation, and technical support of the systems. It is important to identify the documentation source early in the process so the impact on the testing of the system is understood and thus the quality of the documentation understood. This understanding will allow for utilization of the documentation to support qualification.

For example, the decision should be made during planning if any FAT, construction, SAT, or commissioning testing results and documentation will be used to satisfy the requirements to declare the system qualified or suitable for the intended purpose. This decision is based on vendor assessments for C&Q documentation purposes (as described in Chapter 10) and the criticality of the testing. It is often desirable to minimize the amount of retesting performed.

The C&Q Plan may also address the expectations of the documentation. It should be noted that pristine documentation, i.e., documentation that has minimal errors or corrections, is not essential but the documentation needs to be easy to follow and understand.

6.2.3.3 Coordination with Overall Project Execution Plan

Another important aspect is working with the project manager to ensure that the planning of C&Q activities, whether a separate plan or part of another plan, fits seamlessly into the overall Project Execution Plan. C&Q activities should be properly reflected in the Project Execution Plan at an appropriate level of detail to be able to identify interdependencies between C&Q as well as to other project activities. Commissioning is normally occurring throughout construction and needs to occur when it is appropriate; e.g., commissioning the agitation system cannot be completed until it is energized.

Having these activities in the Project Execution Plan supports:

  • Coordination of test activities among C&Q to prepare for FAT or SAT events
  • Ability to schedule sequence of testing for multiple systems (e.g., electrical supply before energizing a system)
  • Schedule review cycles for qualification documents with Quality Unit representatives
中文解說 Commentary

測試範疇的決定邏輯

測試範疇（Scope of Testing）由以下文件決定：

  • 系統分類（System Classification） → 決定哪些系統需要確效（直接影響 vs 非直接影響）
  • 系統風險評估（System Risk Assessment, SRA） → 決定系統中哪些元素需要確效（CDE 的辨識）
  • URS + SRA → 共同建立確效測試的範疇
  • 設備規格 → 決定委試（Commissioning）的測試範疇

文件來源決策（Document Source）

規劃階段必須決定：FAT、SAT、或委試文件是否可以用來支援確效結論？

這個決定的依據是：

  • 廠商評估（Vendor Assessment）的結果 → 廠商文件品質是否足夠？
  • 測試的關鍵性 → 是否涉及 CDE？

目的是減少重複測試（minimize retesting）。如果 FAT 已有充分記錄，就不需要到廠再做一次相同測試。

文件不需要完美無缺

ISPE 明確指出：「Pristine documentation is NOT essential.」 文件允許有更正記錄，重點是易於閱讀和理解。這是一個務實的立場，避免因過度追求「無更正文件」而浪費資源，或更糟糕地：將更正直接消除而非正式記錄。

與整體 Project Execution Plan（PEP）的協調

C&Q 活動必須整合進整體 PEP（專案執行計畫），這樣才能：

  • 識別 C&Q 活動與建設、採購、操作之間的相依性（interdependencies）
  • 合理排序多系統的測試順序（先供電，才能啟動）
  • 為品保文件審查排出時間窗口（QA review cycle）

CDMO 實務上，這意味著 C&Q 計畫工程師需要參加 PEP 的排程會議，而不是單獨在品保部門內部討論。

6.3

C&Q Plan

Typical contents of a C&Q Plan — the 8 key elements

Original Text (English)

6.3 C&Q Plan

The C&Q Plan typically encompasses:

  • Description of the scope of the plan (identification of the systems included)
  • Description of the C&Q execution strategy
  • C&Q related documentation requirements for each identified system (e.g., URS, FAT, SAT, Installation/Operational Testing, PQ, SOPs, etc.)
  • Roles and responsibilities, e.g., approval matrix (see Section 6.4 for example)
  • Reference necessary C&Q document templates (e.g., test instructions, test scripts, reports, discrepancy form)
  • Planned inspections and tests
    • Documentation requirements for the inspection and test results
    • Reference procedures for inspections and tests to be performed
    • Identification of SMEs for review of the inspection and test results
    • For tests to be used to support qualification, include the acceptance criteria to be met for all CDEs (Note: The plan may defer to individual test instructions)

The specific structure and content of the C&Q Plan is typically driven by organization/site requirements.

中文解說 Commentary

C&Q Plan 的典型內容架構

一份完整的 C&Q Plan 應包含以下主要元素：

  1. 計畫範疇（系統清單）
  2. 執行策略與方法
  3. 各系統的文件需求清單（URS、FAT、SAT、IQ/OQ/PQ、SOP）
  4. 角色與責任（含核准矩陣）
  5. 文件範本參考
  6. 計畫中的檢查與測試（含驗收標準、負責 SME、測試程序參考）

CDMO 應用：新充填線 C&Q Plan 範例架構

以 CDMO 承接新無菌充填線安裝專案為例，C&Q Plan 可能包含：

  • 範疇：充填機、凍乾機、去熱原烘箱、CIP/SIP 系統、隔離器（直接影響）；HVAC、純水系統、壓縮空氣（直接影響）；冷卻水（非直接影響）
  • 策略：凍乾機採 FAT（在廠商場地）+ SAT + IQ/OQ/PQ；充填機採廠商 FAT + 現場 IQ/OQ/PQ
  • 文件矩陣：列出每個系統的文件清單、負責部門、預計完成日期
  • 核准矩陣：依 Table 6.1 格式，明確每份文件的事前/事後核准者

CDE 的接受標準可延伸到個別測試指令

計畫中對 CDE 測試的接受標準，可以「轉引」（defer）至個別的測試指令（test instructions）中定義，不一定要在 C&Q Plan 中一一列明。這給了實際操作上的彈性。

6.3.1

Scope of the Effort

Both direct and not-direct impact systems; PQ for utilities; cycle development considerations

Original Text (English)

6.3.1 Scope of the Effort

The scope captures all systems that are covered within the scope of the project. This includes both direct impact and not direct impact systems (as described in Chapter 3). More details are provided below.

6.3.1.1 Additional Scope Considerations

PQ activities may be included in a C&Q Plan for pharmaceutical grade utilities (e.g., WFI, Purified Water), support systems (e.g., autoclaves, depyrogenation ovens, washers), environments (e.g., non-environmental monitoring in clean rooms), and non-product PQ (e.g., media fills for filling lines). Individual organizations/sites need to decide if these are in qualification or a separate validation program.

Cycle development for areas such as sterilization and cleaning also need to be considered. Cycle development can identify factors that require qualification or that modify the qualification. These are often executed after the equipment qualification effort. It is recommended to not perform the final qualification testing until after cycle development since changes identified by cycle development can be captured in the Engineering Change Management system (see Chapter 12). Individual organizations/sites need to decide if cycle development is part of the C&Q effort and capture the decision in the C&Q Plan.

中文解說 Commentary

範疇包含直接與非直接影響系統

C&Q Plan 的範疇不能只列直接影響（Direct Impact）系統。非直接影響（Not Direct Impact）的系統（如冷卻水、普通壓縮空氣）雖然不需要正式確效，但仍在 C&Q 計畫的管理範圍內，需要委試（Commissioning）。

PQ 活動可納入 C&Q Plan

以下項目的 PQ（Performance Qualification，性能確效）可以（但不強制）納入 C&Q Plan 管理：

  • 藥品級公用系統：WFI（注射用水）、純水（PW）
  • 支援系統：高壓滅菌器、去熱原烘箱、清洗機
  • 環境：無菌室的環境確認（非環境監控）
  • 非產品 PQ：無菌模擬充填（Media Fill）

各廠址需自行決定這些是否納入 C&Q 計畫，或另立獨立驗證計畫（Validation Program）。

周期開發應在最終確效測試之前完成

ISPE 特別建議：不要在 Cycle Development（周期開發）完成之前就進行最終確效測試。因為周期開發（如滅菌周期、清潔周期）可能發現需要修改設計的問題。若先做確效再改設計，就必須重做確效，成本很高。

正確順序：Cycle Development → 確認無設計變更 → 最終 IQ/OQ/PQ

6.3.2

Strategy and Approach

Commissioned-only vs commissioned+qualified; vendor documentation criteria; testing dependencies

Original Text (English)

6.3.2 Strategy and Approach

The C&Q Plan needs to define C&Q strategy and approach to aid in overall project planning. The C&Q Plan captures whether systems will be commissioned only or commissioned and qualified. The system classification (as described in Chapter 3) and System Risk Assessment (as described in Chapter 4) have identified the CPPs and CDEs that need to be supported by the C&Q effort.

The rigor of testing depends on the user requirements, CDEs, and system characteristics (e.g., packaged system, modular, or installed system) as well as experiences with the vendor.

An aspect that needs to be captured in the C&Q Plan is the identification of the documentation source that will be utilized to satisfy the C&Q requirements. Appropriate prerequisites, means, and checks should be described in the plan to ensure reliable results, e.g., the pre-approval of vendor/manufacturer documents by an SME. If vendor-provided documentation or testing is to be accepted to support qualification, the criteria for determining if it can be utilized needs to be established in the Plan. Refer to Chapter 10 for details regarding vendor assessments for C&Q documentation purposes.

The C&Q Plan should also capture if individual pieces of equipment or systems will be tested separately because they are delivered and installed at different times by different vendors or if testing will be executed once all components are installed. Dependencies within the scope of work (e.g., if one system needs to be tested before another system is tested) should also be included.

中文解說 Commentary

策略決策：只做委試，還是委試＋確效？

C&Q Plan 必須明確定義每個系統的策略：

  • Commissioned Only（僅委試）：適用於非直接影響系統（如冷卻水、普通電力）
  • Commissioned + Qualified（委試＋確效）：適用於直接影響系統（如無菌充填線、WFI 系統）

決定依據：系統分類（Chapter 3）＋ SRA 的 CDE 辨識結果。

確效嚴格度取決於多項因素

測試的嚴格程度（rigor of testing）受以下因素影響：

  • 使用者需求（URS 的要求）
  • CDEs 的數量和關鍵性
  • 系統特性（套裝系統 vs 模組化 vs 現場安裝）
  • 與廠商的過往合作經驗（有認可的廠商，文件可更多採用）

廠商文件使用標準：提前在計畫中定義

如果計畫採用廠商文件（如 FAT 報告、廠商 IQ）來支援確效結論，必須在計畫中預先定義使用標準，例如：

  • 廠商文件須由 SME 事前審批（pre-approval）
  • 廠商評估報告（Vendor Assessment）需達到什麼標準才能採用其文件
  • 接受哪種程度的廠商資質（Audit 結果、ISO 認證等）

這些標準必須在計畫中寫明，而非事後補救。

測試依賴性（Dependencies）必須明確記錄

例如：HVAC 系統的 OQ 必須在無菌室壓差和換氣次數確認之後，充填線的 IQ 才能開始。這種依賴關係必須在 C&Q Plan 中明確記載，以支援正確的測試排序。

6.3.3

Documentation

Document matrix, electronic storage, 14-item tracking list

Original Text (English)

6.3.3 Documentation

The C&Q Plan should capture document planning and control as well as execution of testing. The C&Q Plan should reference appropriate document control and content procedures to satisfy engineering, commissioning, qualification, operations, and maintenance requirements. Utilizing electronic storage of documents is recommended as it allows for searching and easier retrieval. If electronic storage will be utilized, ensure the vendors and contracting companies are aware and can support the delivery of their documentation in that means.

The Plan should define responsibilities for document preparation, collection, review and approval, and how the documents will be organized. This Guide recommends that a document matrix be considered as a means of planning and communicating these responsibilities. Documents also need to be tracked to ensure they are completed when they are needed.

Documents that are typically tracked include:

  • URS
  • System Risk Assessments
  • Drawings
  • Equipment specifications including any automation specifications (e.g., functional specifications)
  • Vendor correspondence and transmittals
  • Requests for information
  • Submittals (e.g., shop drawings, manufacturers data sheets, user manuals)
  • Required procedures (e.g., equipment operating procedures, maintenance)
  • Change management (see Chapter 12)
  • Schedules
  • Open items / punch list
  • Commissioning/qualification execution documents
  • Discrepancies
  • Turnover packages
  • Project reports
中文解說 Commentary

關鍵術語：Document Matrix（文件矩陣）

Document Matrix（文件矩陣）是一份表格，列出專案中所有需要的文件，以及每份文件的：

  • 負責編寫部門/人員
  • 審批者（含事前/事後）
  • 預計完成日期
  • 文件狀態追蹤

它是 C&Q Plan 中最實用的工具之一，讓專案經理能一眼掌握所有文件的進度。

關鍵術語：Turnover Package（移交文件包）

Turnover Package（TOP）是施工或安裝完成後，由承包商/廠商移交給業主的文件集合，通常包含：

  • As-built 竣工圖
  • 材質証書（Material Certificates）
  • 焊接記錄（Weld Records）
  • 設備手冊（User Manuals）
  • 校正記錄（Calibration Records）
  • 測試報告（FAT/SAT Reports）

TOP 是 IQ（安裝確效）的重要輸入文件，必須在確效開始前完成並通過審查。

15 類需追蹤的文件

ISPE 列出的追蹤清單涵蓋了整個 C&Q 生命週期的文件類型，從設計前的 URS 到最後的 Project Reports，確保沒有任何文件被遺漏。

特別值得注意的是清單中包含了：

  • Punch List（待辦清單）：施工中發現的缺失項目
  • Discrepancies（偏差）：測試中的偏差記錄
  • Turnover Packages（移交文件包）：正式的系統移交文件

建議採用電子文件儲存

ISPE 建議使用電子儲存（如 EDMS，電子文件管理系統）。但要注意：若使用電子系統，必須提前告知所有廠商和承包商，確保他們能以電子格式提交文件。

實務上，在 CDMO 的大型建設專案中，光是追蹤和管理廠商文件，就需要一個專職的文件控制工程師（Document Control Engineer）。

6.3.4

C&Q Plan Contents

System overview, installation phase, operational phase, discrepancy handling

Original Text (English)

6.3.4 C&Q Plan Contents

The C&Q Plan should define the systems that will be commissioned and qualified based on the system or area boundaries. The plan should include a description of systems including their means of automation. The system overview should be a high-level process narrative, including system boundaries, based on operational/functional requirements. The C&Q team representatives should provide input in defining system boundaries (which can be in a system description, marked on P&IDs, or highlighted on system drawings) in the early project phases to develop an executable plan. It is often possible to gather much of the system overview information from other project documentation.

The C&Q Plan should address the level of testing for each system. This may range from detailed descriptions to references to standard tests. An alternative is to provide only high-level descriptions in the C&Q Plan and leave the detailed descriptions to execution documents. The selected approach should increase the level of efficiency and effectiveness. The plan should also include project schedules by system (as defined by the system boundaries) that capture the dependencies between systems.

Commissioning is an important aspect that requires planning, documentation, and managed resources. Commissioning encompasses testing from FAT, testing during construction, SAT, installation testing, and much of the start-up activities of a project lifecycle including those that will be used to assess the qualification of a direct impact system.

Testing during commissioning should be used to support qualification. If commissioning documentation will be used to support qualification, it is critical to involve the leader of the qualification effort and the Quality Unit in the planning and coordination of commissioning activities and deliverables. A periodic commissioning planning and status meeting should be established.

The C&Q Plan needs to include C&Q steps. There are two typical phases:

  • Installation: Documentation supporting installation is successfully completed. This may include FAT and SAT documentation as well as site commissioning for systems built at the site.
  • Operational: Documentation supporting the functionality and performance of the system after the system has been mechanically completed. This may include testing performed in FAT and SAT.

The plan should address the steps to follow when test results do not meet the expected results or acceptance criteria (discrepancy). There should be a predefined methodology for how discrepancies should be handled (see Section 7.4).

中文解說 Commentary

系統概述（System Overview）的作用

系統概述是一個高層次的系統描述，說明系統的功能、邊界（System Boundary）、及自動化方式。

它可以標示在：

  • 文字說明中
  • P&ID（管道與儀表圖）上劃定範圍
  • 系統圖紙上標示邊界

早期定義系統邊界，是確保後續 IQ/OQ/PQ 不會「有漏網之魚」或「測試範疇過大」的關鍵。

兩個典型測試階段

階段 英文 對應確效活動 包含文件
安裝階段 Installation Phase IQ（安裝確效） FAT、SAT、現場安裝委試
運行階段 Operational Phase OQ（運行確效） 功能測試、性能測試（含部分 FAT/SAT）

委試文件用於確效的必要條件

若委試文件要用來支援確效結論，ISPE 強調：

  • 確效負責人和品質部門必須從計畫階段就參與委試活動的規劃與協調
  • 需要定期召開委試計畫與進度會議

這是因為委試文件的品質（格式、接受標準、審批程序）必須符合確效要求，而這只能在事前約定，不能事後補救。

差異處理程序（Discrepancy Handling）

C&Q Plan 必須預先定義：當測試結果不符合期望或接受標準時，應遵循的處理步驟。

這個「差異處理程序（Discrepancy Handling Procedure）」必須在執行前就寫好（predefined），而不是等到出現偏差才臨時決定怎麼做。詳見 Section 7.4。

中文解說 Commentary

關鍵術語：Pre-Approver vs Post-Approver

事前審批者（Pre-Approver）：在文件執行或發放之前完成審批。確保計畫合理、標準正確、執行前已獲授權。

事後審批者（Post-Approver）：在文件執行完成後審批。確認結果符合要求、偏差已處理、可以接受結論。

最嚴格的文件（如確效測試文件）同時需要事前和事後審批，代表「執行前批准計畫，執行後批准結果」。

核准矩陣的三個角色

  • Quality Unit（品質部門）：負責確保法規符合性和品質標準
  • Production User（生產用戶）：確保系統符合操作需求
  • Technical1（技術）：包含工程、製程、SME（學科專家）；同一學科只需一個簽名

Table 6.1 重要觀察

1. 確效測試文件最嚴格：IQ/OQ/PQ 文件同時需要所有三方（QA、User、Technical）的事前和事後審批，顯示其最高的法規重要性。

2. C&Q Plan 只需事前審批：計畫文件只有「事前」審批，完成後不需要再次審批（因為計畫本身是用來指導後續工作的，不是「結果文件」）。

3. 追溯矩陣不需要正式審批：Traceability Matrix 的核准欄位全部是 "Not applicable"，因為它是技術部門內部使用的追蹤工具。

4. 品保對 URS 的審批有條件性（注2）：若品保已批准過 URS 的來源文件（如品質需求文件），則 URS 本身可不需品保二次審批。

關鍵術語：Traceability Matrix（追溯矩陣）

追溯矩陣是一份文件，將每個 URS 需求項目，追溯到對應的設計文件、風險評估、測試項目，和測試結果。

它回答了「我的每一個使用者需求，是否都有對應的測試驗證？」這個問題。Table 6.1 顯示它不需要正式的 Pre/Post-Approval，由 Technical 負責製作即可。

CDMO 實務：如何使用核准矩陣

對 CDMO 的品保部門而言，Table 6.1 提供了一個最低限度的參考架構。實際操作時：

  • 公司 SOP 可以比 Table 6.1 要求更嚴格（增加更多審批者），但不應比它寬鬆
  • 稽查時，稽查員可能要求展示核准矩陣，並驗證實際簽名記錄是否符合
  • 若某文件缺少 Pre-Approval 就已執行，屬於重大程序違規（Protocol executed without prior approval）
  • 建議在新專案啟動時，以 Table 6.1 為基礎，製作公司特定的核准矩陣

本章重點摘要 (Chapter Summary)

  • C&Q Plan 是專案的核心治理文件，定義範疇、策略、文件要求、角色責任，和測試方法。
  • 審批時序規則：C&Q Plan 核准 → 測試指令核准 → 開始執行。此順序在業界常被違反，但稽查時後果嚴重。
  • 計畫深度應與風險成比例：簡單變更納入變更管制即可；複雜系統（填充線、公用系統擴建）需要獨立的正式 C&Q Plan。
  • C&Q Plan 是活文件：隨專案進展更新；輕微偏離可記錄在 Qualification Summary Report 中，不必每次修訂計畫。
  • Document Matrix（文件矩陣）是管理所有文件進度的最有效工具。
  • Turnover Package（移交文件包）是 IQ 的重要輸入，必須在確效開始前完成。
  • Table 6.1 核准矩陣明確定義了每份文件的事前/事後審批者，是 CDMO 建立公司特定核准程序的最佳參考範本。
  • 確效測試文件是唯一同時需要所有三方（QA、User、Technical）事前和事後審批的文件。
