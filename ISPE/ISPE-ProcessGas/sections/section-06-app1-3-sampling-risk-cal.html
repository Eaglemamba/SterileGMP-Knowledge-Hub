原文內容 Original Text

ISPE Process Gases

10 Appendix 1 – Example of the Development of a Sampling Strategy Based on a Risk Assessment

10.1 System Description

  • Compressed air is generated to provide instrument and product contact air to a number of buildings on site.
  • Two oil free compressors supply air though a compressor mounted air cooler and separator through a set of intermediate filters (Filters A and B). These filters are nominally rated at 0.01 ppm oil aerosol, 0.01 µm particulate.
  • The air then goes through a dryer rated at -40°F (-40°C) atmospheric Pressure Dewpoint (PDP), and then final filters (Filters C and D) rated at 0.01 µm, to feed the receiver.
  • Air is then distributed to the buildings, with additional Point Of Use (POU) filters where required (i.e., where air will contact product, product contact surfaces, or product primary containers).
  • The dryer is fitted with a high pressure dew point alarm that is fed to the Building Management System (BMS), together with a system status and system general alarm signal.
  • The system air pressure is monitored by the BMS for engineering purposes (through a transducer located in the receiver).

10.2 Control Strategy

The compressor control panels are interconnected to provide an automatic control system designed to maintain a constant discharge pressure, and swap the duty compressor on a time schedule basis.

Each dryer has its own independent control system and pressure dewpoint monitor.

10.2.1 Distribution Pipe Work Specifications

The company pipework specifications for compressed air are:

  • Up to 4″ diameter – copper and brass materials of construction with soldered joints.

10.2.2 Important Concepts

The air supplied by the system is -40°F (-40°C) atmospheric PDP. As shown in Table 10.1 this may be converted to a relative humidity in the distribution system, assuming that the pipework is typically operating circa 70°F (21°C), and the nominal pressure is 6 bar (87 PSIG):

Note: Calculation included for other PDPs for information.

RH = 100 × (Actual Vapor Pressure / Saturation Vapor Pressure)
Saturation vapor pressure at 70°F (21°C) = 25 mb

The quantity of water necessary to support microbiological growth is usually expressed as water activity (aw). For gases, water activity is related to relative humidity (RH = aw × 100%) and also dewpoint. Molds and yeasts do not grow where the aw is less than 0.6 and bacteria do not grow where the aw is less than 0.85 (Scott et al., 2001).

Compressed Air Quality Attributes:

  • Moisture content
  • Hydrocarbon levels
  • Particle count (viable and non-viable)

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

產品接觸氣體 (Product Contact Gas): 直接與產品、產品接觸表面、或初級包材接觸的氣體。這類氣體的品質屬性直接影響產品安全，因此受到最嚴格的控制與監測。

壓力露點 (Pressure Dewpoint, PDP): 在系統操作壓力下，空氣中水蒸氣開始凝結的溫度。-40°C PDP 代表空氣極乾燥——這是製藥氣體系統最常見的規格要求。

POU 過濾器 (Point Of Use Filter): 安裝在氣體最終使用點的過濾器，為直接接觸產品的氣體提供最後一道屏障。

比喻說明 Analogy

這個壓縮空氣系統就像一座城市的自來水網路：水廠（壓縮機）產出原水，經過初步處理（中間過濾器 A&B）、精密過濾（乾燥機＋最終過濾器 C&D）後進入配水網（管路），最後在每個用水點（POU）還有一道淨水器。城市中的醫院（產品接觸點）比家庭（非接觸點）需要更高品質的水，因此在醫院入口額外裝設更精密的過濾——這正是 POU 過濾器的概念。

重點提示 Key Notes

水活性（aw）是微生物生長的門檻： 黴菌/酵母菌 aw < 0.6 不生長；細菌 aw < 0.85 不生長。在 -40°C PDP、6 bar 下，系統約在 5% RH（aw = 0.05），遠低於任何微生物生長門檻。這就是為何露點控制可同步管理微生物風險的關鍵科學依據。

露點從 -40°C 升至 0°C，在 6 bar 下 RH 從 4.8% 跳至 146.4%——這意味著乾燥機一旦失效，系統迅速進入微生物可能生長的範圍。

附錄一 — 10.3 濕度含量 (Moisture Content)

原文內容 Original Text

ISPE Process Gases

10.3 Moisture Content

The moisture contained in outside air is compressed with the air in the compressor. As the air is compressed the temperature will rise, increasing the moisture carrying content despite the smaller volume.

As the air cools after compression, moisture will separate (condense) from the air as it goes through the compressor after-cooler, where it drains (usually from a skid mounted post compressor filter – shown as a pre-filter/cyclonic separator in the schematic). The air will then go through the intermediate filters (filter A and B) into the dryer.

The dryer will reduce the air moisture content of the air until it reaches a condition of at least -40°F (-40°C) dewpoint. This is monitored by a pressure dewpoint instrument in the compressed air dryer discharge by a PDP instrument.

Note that the dewpoint is measured with the air at atmospheric pressure – a small amount of air is taken from the outlet, and passed over the measuring sensor. A decrease in the PDP, e.g., from -41°F to -38°F (-41°C to -39°C) (i.e., increase in the moisture content) will give an alarm that is given visually and audibly locally, and repeated to the building management system.

The system pipework is all metallic, thus providing a very robust moisture barrier, separating the compressed air from the atmosphere. There is, therefore, very little opportunity for moisture to be taken up by the air during distribution.

The system integrity can be confirmed initially by checking the moisture content in the air after the dryer, then at the outlet furthest from the dryer (i.e., the one with the longest length of pipework); therefore, testing the air that has passed though the longest pipe run with greatest surface area, providing the maximum opportunity for moisture ingress.

"As this aspect of the system performance will not change through the life of the pipe system, routine monitoring is not considered to be required. The PDP monitor, however, will be considered a critical instrument used to confirm continuously the condition of the compressed air supplied to the system."

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Index Run（最長管程）： 指從壓縮機到末端出口的最長管路。選此點取樣的邏輯是「最惡情況原則」——若最長路徑的氣體品質合格，所有較短路徑必然也合格。這是一種高效率、科學化的取樣策略，避免盲目在每個出口取樣。

持續監測 vs. 週期取樣： PDP 監測儀是持續的（continuous），而人工取樣是週期性的（periodic）。對於關鍵參數（如露點），應優先配置持續監測，並連接 BMS 報警——這比定期派人取樣更可靠。

比喻說明 Analogy

想像你負責管理一棟大樓的熱水供應系統。只要進水溫度（熱水器出口）合格，加上最遠端的浴室（最長管程）出水也合格，就能推論整棟大樓的熱水品質都達標——你不需要在每間浴室都裝溫度計。這正是「Index Run」策略的精髓：用最惡情況點代表整個系統。

重點提示 Key Notes

金屬管路 = 天然濕度屏障： 銅/不鏽鋼管路本身就是極好的水分阻隔材料，幾乎不會讓外部濕度滲入。因此，一旦初始驗證確認管路完整性，濕度的日常監測可以依賴儀器而非人工取樣。

修改系統需重新驗證： 「Modification of the system would require appropriate testing.」管路修改或延伸後，必須重新驗證 Index Run。這是系統變更管控（Change Control）的重要觸發點，在 CDMO 環境中尤其容易被工程人員忽視。

原文內容 Original Text

ISPE Process Gases

10.4 Hydrocarbons/Oil

Hydrocarbon particulate and vapor contained in the outside air may be slightly reduced by the compressor air inlet filter – a coarse filter designed primarily to protect the equipment from damage. The remaining particles/vapor will then be compressed with the air. Depending on the type of hydrocarbon, it will remain as a vapor or form particulate; typically, the particulate size range is in the range 0.2 to 0.3 µm. The intermediate filters (Filters A and B) will remove particulate down to 0.01 µm; therefore, removing any hydrocarbon particulate, down to 0.01 mg/m³.

Another potential source of hydrocarbon contamination is the pipework, with the associated jointing process used. The engineering specifications call for degreased material, with any threaded joints in the system made without a hydrocarbon based lubricant.

Note that the final filters (Filters C and D) are not rated for oil vapor removal.

Initial testing will be pre- and post- the intermediate and from the point of use on the index run (longest pipe run) of each floor of the building served by the system.

The initial test will determine:

  • The challenge to Filters A and B (if the local environment is reasonable, the air delivered from the compressor may meet the specification with no further treatment)
  • The output air quality from the system intermediate filters (Filters A and B)
  • Any measurable contamination from the compressed air distribution pipework

10.5 Viable and Non-Viable Particulate

Coarse particulate in the outside air will be filtered out through the compressor air inlet filter. Particulate will be removed by the coalescing intermediate filters (Filters A and B) and by the commercial grade 1 µm final system filters (Filters C and D). Product protection (in this example, the product is an oral solid dose) would be provided by local 0.45 µm filters installed at each outlet where air will contact product, product contact surfaces, or product primary containers.

The initial test strategy is proposed to demonstrate that the system filtration is adequate to provide air meeting the specifications at the generation system, and that the distribution system is clean, i.e., free of particulate.

The ongoing monitoring proposed is intended to demonstrate that the system can reliably provide air meeting the specifications from the generation system.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

聚合過濾器 (Coalescing Filter): 設計用來捕捉油霧（oil aerosol）的特殊過濾器。油霧液滴在過濾介質上聚合成較大液滴後，受重力滴落排出。這類過濾器同時去除微粒和液態油污。

烴類來源的雙重性： 本節揭示烴類污染有兩個來源——(1) 外部環境空氣中的烴類，(2) 管路施工中殘留的潤滑劑或溶劑。初始取樣必須同時針對這兩個來源設計測試點。

重點提示 Key Notes

最終過濾器無法去除油蒸氣： Filters C & D 是微粒過濾，不是油蒸氣過濾器。油蒸氣（vapor phase）只有中間過濾器 A&B 能有效去除。若 A&B 失效，油蒸氣可能穿透至 POU——這是設計驗證和持續監測需覆蓋的關鍵場景。

0.45 µm POU 過濾器： 這是口服固體劑型（OSD）的典型規格，能截留大多數微生物。對無菌產品而言，POU 可能需要更高規格（0.2 µm 除菌級）。

比喻說明 Analogy

想像食品加工廠的通風系統：入口的粗濾網（壓縮機進氣過濾器）擋住樹葉和大塵粒，但細菌和廚房油煙（類比油霧）需要更精密的 HEPA 過濾器（類比 Filters A&B）。而空調出風口的最終過濾器（類比 Filters C&D）能再過濾微粒，但無法處理已氣化的油蒸氣——因此初期的管路清潔度（脫脂施工）至關重要。

實務應用 CDMO Application

在 CDMO 承接新客戶或新產品時，工程師常被問：「現有壓縮空氣系統能否直接用於這個產品？」正確答案需要：確認 POU 過濾器規格是否符合該產品的接觸要求、查看最近一次 index run 的烴類和微粒測試結果、確認管路材質和施工記錄。這些資訊應整合進設備資格檔（URS/DQ）和系統監測檔案。

ISPE Process Gases

10.6 Microbial Levels

Monitoring for viable particles will be treated in the same manner as non-viable, as the risk of proliferation within the receiver and distribution system is controlled by the low humidity (PDP) air within the system.

Assuming that the system will operate at -40°F (-40°C) DP, and at 90 psi (6 bar) with an ambient temperature of 70°F (21°C), the air will be at approximately 5% RH, i.e., an aw of 0.05.

The initial test strategy is proposed to demonstrate that the system filtration is adequate to provide air meeting the specifications at the generation system, and that the distribution system is free from microbial contamination.

The ongoing monitoring proposed is intended to demonstrate that the system can reliably provide air meeting the specifications from the generation system.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

露點作為微生物控制機制： 這是本節最重要的洞察。當系統保持在 -40°C PDP（aw = 0.05）時，微生物既無法在氣體中存活，也無法在管壁上增殖——因此微生物監測的設計邏輯與非活性微粒完全相同。這代表「維持 PDP 合規」等同於「維持微生物控制」，兩者是連動的。

重點提示 Key Notes

氣體微生物監測常被忽視： 許多工廠的氣體資格確認（Gas Qualification）只做非活性微粒，未做活性微粒（cfu/ft³）測試。ISPE 明確要求活性微粒監測——這在查廠時是容易被稽核員指出的缺漏。

驗收標準： 最終過濾器後 ≤ 0.03 cfu/ft³；POU 也是 ≤ 0.03 cfu/ft³。這個數字需在 SOP 和監測計畫中明確文件化。

比喻說明 Analogy

露點控制微生物，就像沙漠氣候天然抑制細菌：撒哈拉沙漠的低濕度讓細菌幾乎無法在露天環境存活，即使沙塵中帶有少量孢子，也無法增殖。壓縮空氣系統通過將 RH 壓至 5%，人工創造了一個「沙漠環境」，從源頭切斷微生物生長的必要條件。

「3天」的意義： 連續或非連續3天的測試，目的是捕捉典型操作變異。一天的快照（snapshot）可能是最好或最差的情況，3天數據才能建立合理的基線（baseline）。這個邏輯適用於所有 PQ 驗收測試，不限於氣體系統。

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

基於證據的監測頻率調整： ISPE 的方法論不是固定頻率，而是「連續3次過濾器更換週期中各測一次，然後根據結果重新評估」。這是 ICH Q9 品質風險管理（Quality Risk Management, QRM）在監測頻率設計上的直接應用——讓數據說話，不是盲目遵從固定排程。

修改後重新測試的觸發邏輯： 管路修改（extension, repair, material change）是系統完整性的潛在破壞點。ISPE 明確要求修改後重新執行 Index Run 測試——這應進入 Change Control 程序的必檢清單。

重點提示 Key Notes

烴類策略的「有無挑戰」分叉： 這是一個罕見但重要的差異化設計：若初始測試顯示環境烴類本底值低（無挑戰），每年一次即可；若本底值高（有挑戰），需更密集地在過濾器壽命中點和更換前後各測一次。這種設計充分體現了「基於風險的監測頻率」。

POU 過濾器的保護角色： 系統過濾器（C&D）失效或降效時，POU 過濾器仍提供最後防線。這也是為什麼系統層面的監測可以使用較寬鬆的頻率，而不需要每次生產都取樣。

監測頻率決策框架

初始狀態判斷：
  ┌─ 無烴類挑戰 → 年度監測（環境年變化追蹤）
  └─ 有烴類挑戰 → 每次過濾器更換前/中/後各測一次
                    → 連續3次後重新評估頻率

微粒與微生物：
  → 每次過濾器更換的中點、更換前各測一次
  → 連續3次後重新評估

所有 CQA：
  → 系統修改後必須重新執行 Index Run 測試

  2. 工廠決定將某樓層壓縮空氣管路延伸到新增生產間，需要執行哪些重新測試？哪些可以不測？

ISPE Process Gases

11 Appendix 2 – Risk Assessment Examples

From an audit/patient safety perspective the key system performance monitoring data could be kept in a system monitoring file to facilitate routine review of performance and analysis of any trends.

The contents of this file may include, as applicable:

  • A system as built record drawing (P&ID showing test points)
  • Critical filter test data
  • Particle monitoring results
  • Hydrocarbon test results
  • Microbial test results
  • Gas identity
  • Moisture content
"The 'package' of data produced should be reviewed to ensure that the initial performance 'baseline,' established during the performance verification is maintained and performance deterioration is absent."

There are a number of approaches to the system risk assessment. One approach is:

Step 1 – Define Quality Attributes / CPPs

The quality attributes/CPPs for the gas required at the POU are defined and documented together with the supporting rationale, e.g.:

  • The compressed air is supplied to an ISPE Grade 8 area. The specified particle count and microbial limits applied to the free air delivered are the same as the environment being supplied.
  • Moisture content is considered a critical factor as: (a) it may influence the microbial performance of the gas distribution system; (b) the product is moisture sensitive.
  • The hydrocarbon limits are defined as 0.5 mg/m³ in the organization quality standards for this application, due to risk of taste/color influence on the product coating.
  • Note that pressure is not considered a critical parameter as far as the system is concerned; if it is critical for the correct function of the system supplied by the gas, then that system will incorporate a pressure monitoring device.

Step 2 – Define How CQAs/CPPs are Monitored

  • The particle count is routinely taken at the supply point to the distribution system – reference SP1, and at the point of use with the furthest run of distribution pipe work – reference POU28.
  • The microbial levels are routinely taken at SP1 and at POU28.
  • The system PDP is monitored by a calibrated instrument on the dryer discharge, with a hard wired connection to the dryer discharge valve operated from the PDP instrument alarm.

Step 3 – Define How CQAs/CPPs Are Achieved & Equipment Risks

  • The system particle count is controlled by the system filters – standard commercial units within the distribution system's boundary, changed following a PM procedure on a time base.
    • The system is performance verified by testing at SP1 and at POU28 – i.e., with the maximum pipe work length.
    • These two points will be routinely monitored to confirm satisfactory system performance.
  • The system microbial levels are controlled by the system filters and also influenced by the Pressure Dewpoint (moisture content) of the air.
    • Performance verified by testing at SP1 and at POU28.
    • These two points will be routinely monitored.
  • The system hydrocarbon content is controlled by the system filters. The compressor is an oil free unit, so the challenge is due to atmospheric pollution and any potential contamination due to materials or construction techniques.
    • Initial installation performance verified by testing at compressor discharge, SP1, and POU28.
    • Challenge routinely checked by monitoring the hydrocarbon content at the discharge from the compressor.
  • The system Pressure Dewpoint (PDP) is controlled by the dryer. The dewpoint is continuously monitored by a calibrated instrument on the dryer discharge, with a hard wired connection to the dryer discharge valve operated from the PDP instrument alarm.

The equipment to be verified and maintained under quality change control is therefore the PDP monitor on the dryer, the distribution system including the final filters and pipe work up to the points of use, i.e., the components in the system that the risk assessment has identified as risk controls, and detection mechanisms.

Other aspects of the system are controlled through good engineering practices.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

系統監測檔案 (System Monitoring File)： 這是稽核時的核心文件包。P&ID（管道儀表圖）標示測試點、過濾器測試數據、所有 CQA 的歷史監測數據，應整合成一份可追溯的「系統健康紀錄」。這不僅是品質要求，也是工程管理的最佳實踐。

三步驟風險評估法： (1) 定義 CQA/CPP → (2) 定義監測方式 → (3) 定義如何達成並識別設備風險。這是一個結構化的、由需求到設計到監測的全鏈條方法，與 ICH Q9 的 FMEA 精神一致。

比喻說明 Analogy

這個三步驟風險評估就像醫師診斷病人：Step 1 是確認病人的關鍵生命指標（血壓、體溫、血氧）；Step 2 是決定用哪些儀器持續監測（心電圖、血氧夾、定時量體溫）；Step 3 是確認如何維持這些指標正常（用藥、手術、輸液）並識別可能失效的設備（電池、校準問題）。只有進行變更管控（Change Control）的設備才是系統的「關鍵設備」，其餘靠良好工程實踐管理即可。

重點提示 Key Notes

壓力不是關鍵參數——這個判斷需要理由： 「壓力非關鍵」的結論不是拍腦袋，而是基於「各使用點有自己的壓力監測設備，氣體系統本身無法精確控制每個 POU 的壓力」的工程邏輯。這個判斷和理由都必須文件化。

Quality Change Control 的邊界： 只有被風險評估識別為「風險控制」或「偵測機制」的設備（PDP 監測儀、系統過濾器、管路）需要納入品質變更管控；其他設備（如壓縮機的一般機械部件）可歸類為工程管理。這個區分在資源有限的 CDMO 環境中具有重要的實務意義。

實務應用 CDMO Application

CDMO 在接受客戶稽查時，最常被問的問題之一是：「你們怎麼知道這個氣體系統受控？」正確回答需要展示：(1) 有 P&ID 標示取樣點；(2) 有已完成的 PQ 測試數據；(3) 有持續監測的 SOP 和趨勢分析；(4) 有 Change Control 程序涵蓋關鍵設備修改。這份系統監測檔案就是最好的回應工具。

ISPE Process Gases

12 Appendix 3 – Calibration Strategies

It is considered good practice to review the instrumentation on a process gas system to determine the relative importance of the instruments, considering the system requirements.

Doing this helps to enable users to categorize the instruments into a hierarchy:

  • Product critical
  • Process or system critical
  • Safety or environmental critical
  • Non-critical or for information only

For further information refer to the ISPE GAMP Good Practice Guide: A Risk-Based Approach to Calibration Management (Reference 21, Appendix 8).

Once the instruments are categorized so that their function is clearly defined, an appropriate calibration strategy can be developed and applied.

System Description (Calibration Example)

The site compressed air is fed through a breathing air purifier then distributed to the use points through a medical grade copper pipe work. Each user point is fitted with a 0.3 micron filter, pressure regulator, and gauge connected to a female quick release coupling.

The users are all breathing air hoods – each hood is fitted with a male quick release coupling, and a variable area flow meter.

The breathing air unit is fitted with a number of alarms, including low inlet pressure, or high inlet pressure. These will be regularly checked as part of the equipment planned maintenance program.

Commissioning and Calibration Proposals

1. Air Purifier Discharge Flow Meter — Purchased as a commercial un-calibrated device; this is considered non-critical to be used for engineering information purposes only – flow will be checked at the outlets each time the hood is set up for use per the operating procedure.

The flow meter will be checked to ensure the specified unit has been installed correctly during Installation Verification. Routine calibration is not required. However, the air purifier periodic maintenance will include a visual inspection for operation and damage; the unit will be replaced if it is found to be damaged or non-operational.

2. Purifier Outlet Pressure Gauge — Purchased as a commercial un-calibrated device; this is considered non-critical to be used for engineering information purposes only – flow should be checked at the outlets periodically. Routine calibration is not required. The air purifier planned maintenance will include a visual inspection for operation and damage; the unit will be replaced if it is found to be damaged or non-operational.

3. Low Pressure Switch on Air Purifier Discharge — This is considered safety critical and will be calibrated annually, with the associated alarm functionally checked.

4. Outlet Pressure Gauges — Purchased as commercial un-calibrated devices considered non-critical to be used for engineering information purposes only – flow will be checked at the outlets each time the breathing air hood is used and each time the hood is set up for use per the operating procedure.

The pressure gauges will be checked to ensure the specified unit has been installed correctly during Installation Verification. Routine calibration is not required. However, the gauge will be verified annually (calibrated at a single point) at around the midpoint setting 40 to 50 psi required for the hood supply to ensure that it is not damaged and is consistent to facilitate its use as a reference when setting the flow to the hood. The working range for the hood is 25 to 70 psig.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

儀器分類層次 (Instrument Hierarchy)：

  • 產品關鍵 (Product Critical): 直接影響產品品質屬性，如 PDP 監測儀（影響濕度 CQA）。需最嚴格的校準計畫。
  • 製程/系統關鍵 (Process/System Critical): 影響系統正常運作，但不直接影響產品 CQA。如自動排水閥的感測器。
  • 安全/環境關鍵 (Safety/Environmental Critical): 不影響產品品質，但影響人員安全或環境合規。如低壓報警開關。
  • 非關鍵/資訊用 (Non-Critical): 僅供工程參考，不影響任何關鍵判斷。大多數現場顯示錶盤。

比喻說明 Analogy

這個校準層次就像汽車儀表板的重要性分級：速度表（關鍵，影響安全）需要定期驗證；引擎水溫表（系統關鍵，影響車輛壽命）需要定期確認；車廂溫度顯示（非關鍵，舒適性資訊）壞了換掉就好，不需要校準。氣體系統中，PDP 監測儀是「速度表」，非關鍵流量計是「車廂溫度顯示」，各自有不同的維護策略。

重點提示 Key Notes

「非關鍵」不代表不需管理： 非關鍵儀器雖不需定期校準，但仍需要：(1) 安裝驗證（確認安裝正確）；(2) 定期目視檢查（是否損壞）；(3) 損壞時替換。這些活動應列入預防性維護（PM）計畫，而非完全放任。

安全關鍵 = 每年校準 + 功能測試： 低壓開關是保護使用者（穿戴呼吸面罩的人員）的安全設備，一旦失效可能導致缺氧。因此即使它不影響產品 CQA，也必須每年校準並附加功能測試（alarm test）。這是法規和工廠安全管理的雙重要求。

「單點驗證」vs. 「完整校準」： 非關鍵的出口壓力計每年只做「中點單點確認」（40-50 psi 的中間值），而非完整的多點校準曲線。這是務實的資源分配——用最少的工作量確認儀器沒有明顯損壞，而非追求完整精度。

儀器類別      校準要求         頻率        失效後果

製程關鍵      多點校準         定期（如年度）   系統失控/停機
安全關鍵      完整校準+功能測試 年度           人員傷亡/環境

              (+可選擇單點驗證)

實務應用 CDMO Application

CDMO 在建立氣體系統校準計畫（Calibration Plan）時，最常見的錯誤是「一刀切」——所有儀器都套用相同頻率和方法。ISPE 的分層策略讓你可以：把有限的校準資源集中在產品關鍵和安全關鍵儀器上；對非關鍵儀器降低校準頻率（但不取消 PM 目視）；在稽查時用清晰的分類邏輯說明你的決策——這比「我們每6個月校準所有儀器」更有說服力，因為它展示了風險思維。

本節重點回顧 Section Summary

  • 取樣策略源於風險評估： 並非每個出口都需取樣——「Index Run」（最長管程）策略用最惡情況點代表整個系統，是科學且高效的設計方法。
  • 露點控制是微生物管控的根本： -40°C PDP 將系統 RH 壓至約 5%（aw = 0.05），遠低於任何微生物（細菌、黴菌）的生長下限，使微生物監測可以沿用非活性微粒的邏輯。
  • 初始測試建立基線；持續監測確認維持： PQ 測試覆蓋所有 CQA（濕度、烴類、總微粒、活性微粒）；持續監測頻率基於過濾器更換週期動態調整，而非固定時間表。
  • ISPE 三步驟風險評估： (1) 定義 CQA/CPP → (2) 定義監測方式 → (3) 定義如何達成及識別設備風險。只有被識別為風險控制或偵測機制的設備才納入品質變更管控。
  • 儀器校準分四層： 產品關鍵 > 製程關鍵 > 安全關鍵 > 非關鍵。分層策略讓有限資源集中在真正關鍵的儀器，非關鍵儀器以 PM 目視和替換管理，而非盲目套用全量校準。
  • CDMO 應用： 氣體系統 SOP 需同時滿足品質（CQA 監測）和工程（PM、變更管控）需求；系統監測檔案（P&ID + 所有測試數據）是稽查的核心文件包。
