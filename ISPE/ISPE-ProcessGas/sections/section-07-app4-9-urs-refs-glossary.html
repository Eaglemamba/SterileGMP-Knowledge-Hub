導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 — URS 是什麼？

User Requirement Specification (URS, 使用者需求規格書) 是整個資格確認（Qualification）生命週期的起點文件。它用業務語言描述「系統必須做到什麼」，而不涉及如何設計或製造。

URS 的重要性在於：它直接連結到 DQ（設計資格）、IQ（安裝資格）、OQ（運行資格）的驗收標準。沒有明確的 URS，後續的 IQ/OQ 測試就失去了判斷基準。

重點提示 — 15 個 URS 項目的分類策略

這15個需求項目中，哪些是 CQA（關鍵品質屬性），哪些是 CPP（關鍵製程參數），哪些只是商業需求？這個分類決定了資格確認的深度：

  • CQA 等級（1–6）：純度、水分、顆粒、微生物限度、壓力、流量 — 直接影響產品品質，需完整 IQ/OQ/PQ
  • 商業需求（7–15）：操作時間、維護、警報、供應商選擇 — 影響營運，但不直接影響產品品質

規格確認時，只有 CQA/CPP 相關的項目才需要包含在 PQ 測試計畫中。

比喻說明 — URS 就像建築許可前的需求書

建築師開始畫圖之前，業主必須先說清楚：需要幾層樓、要容納多少人、有沒有殘障設施需求。URS 就是這份「業主需求書」。工程師後來設計的圖面（DS）、安裝的結果（IQ）、測試的運行（OQ）都必須回溯到這份需求書來確認是否符合。

實務應用 — CDMO 填寫 URS 的常見痛點

在 CDMO 環境中，氣體系統通常服務多個客戶的多種產品。URS 中的「6.0 純度需求」與「4.0 顆粒/微生物限度」往往需要取最嚴格的客戶要求作為系統規格，否則未來接新客戶時必須重做 OQ/PQ。建議在 URS 審查時納入市場部與業務部門，提前預估未來5年可能引入的產品類型。

14.1 Minimize Leaks

It is very difficult to completely eliminate leaks; designs aim to minimize leaks. Pressure testing after construction and an ongoing leak detection program may be used to minimize these potentially expensive losses from a process gas system.

When defining the required process gas quality, it may be necessary to consider the air quality of the room the lines will pass through or rooms where the gas may be vented after use. The gas quality should meet or exceed the air quality of the room.

Pressurized gases are expensive; losses from quite small leaks can be considerable, as shown in Table 14.1.

14.2 Pressure/Flow Controllers

System controllers, such as pressure/flow controllers, can improve the performance of systems. Their primary function is to stabilize system pressure separate from and more precisely than compressor controls. A pressure/flow controller does not directly control a compressor and is generally not included as part of a compressor package. A pressure/flow controller is a device that serves to separate the supply side of a compressor system from the demand side.

The type of control system specified for a given system is largely determined by the type of compressor being used and the facility's process demand profile. If a system has a single compressor with a very steady demand, a simple control system may be appropriate. A complex system with multiple compressors and widely varying demand will require a more sophisticated control strategy. This is the situation when the generation of compressed air emanates from the same source for both process and instrument air applications in a pharmaceutical manufacturing environment.

The pressure/flow controller can also be used as a back pressure flow device capable of maintaining pressure on critical applications during temporary interruptions in supply.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 — 為何洩漏是 GMP 問題而非僅是成本問題

在一般工業理解中，氣體洩漏主要是金錢損失。但在製藥環境中，洩漏有更嚴重的 GMP 含義：

  • 氮氣覆蓋系統（N₂ blanketing）：若氮氣管線有微小洩漏點，環境空氣（含 O₂、水分、微生物）可能在壓力波動時逆滲進入系統，破壞惰性保護
  • 壓縮空氣系統：洩漏點可能成為外部污染物進入點，尤其當系統停機時（正壓不足）
  • 無菌充填用氣體：任何顆粒或微生物的侵入都可能導致批次廢棄

這解釋了為何 ISPE 要求「壓力測試後施工」並建立持續的洩漏偵測計畫。

重點提示 — IQ 中的洩漏測試要求

洩漏測試（Leak Test）是安裝資格（IQ）中的必要測試項目之一。常用方法：

  • 壓力衰減法（Pressure Decay）：封閉系統後觀察一定時間內的壓力降落，計算洩漏率
  • 肥皂泡法（Bubble Test）：適用於接頭、閥門的局部洩漏點檢測
  • 氦氣示蹤法（Helium Tracer）：高靈敏度洩漏偵測，適用於超高純度氣體系統

IQ 協議中應明確列出可接受的洩漏率規格（如 <0.1% 每小時）。

比喻說明 — Pressure/Flow Controller 的角色

把壓縮機想像成城市供水塔，各廠區設備是用水戶。沒有壓力/流量控制器時，一台大型設備突然用水，整個廠區水壓都會下降。Pressure/Flow Controller 就像各棟大樓的恆壓閥：確保供應側（水塔）的壓力波動不傳遞到需求側（各用水點）。在製藥無菌充填中，壓力穩定性直接影響灌裝精度，這個隔離功能至關重要。

Appendix 6 — Nitrogen Gas Generation Systems 氮氣產生系統

原文內容 Original Text

15. Appendix 6 – Nitrogen Gas Generation Systems

Nitrogen generation facilities operate based on one of three operating principles:

  1. membrane
  2. adsorption
  3. cryogenic

Membrane Process

The membrane process generates nitrogen purities ranging from 95% up to 99.5%. A unit would consist of an air compressor, filters, pre-heater (if required) membrane cartridges, piping, valves, and the control system. The process can be described as:

  • Compressed dry air enters the cartridge on one end.
  • Oxygen, carbon dioxide, and residual humidity permeate faster than nitrogen through the membrane wall, thus enabling a side venting of those gases to the atmosphere.
  • On the other cartridge end, the nitrogen exits and, depending upon the application, leaves the unit direct to the user's process or to a suitably dimensioned buffer tank.

The output of a membrane cartridge is dependent upon three factors:

  1. the pressure of the feed air
  2. the temperature of the feed air
  3. the required purity level of the nitrogen

For example, if temperature increases, then selectivity decreases, but capacity increases. If pressure increases, selectivity will increase. Pressure and temperature are adapted to the product specification by designing the air compressor and the pre-heater. System capacities can be changed by varying the number of cartridges in the unit.

Adsorption Process (PSA)

The adsorption process generates nitrogen purities ranging from 98% up to 99.99% or even higher. Each unit contains two molecular-sieve adsorbers together with the necessary air compressor, piping, valves, and control system. For adsorptive extraction of nitrogen from air, Carbon Molecular Sieves (CMS) are used as the adsorption agents. Under pressurized conditions, these adsorb oxygen, water vapors, and carbon dioxide, letting the nitrogen pass through the orifices of the sieve. To regenerate the molecular sieve after it has become loaded with oxygen, it is necessary to blow it back down to atmospheric pressure and purge it.

Production and regeneration phases are performed alternately at regular intervals. While one adsorber is in the production phase, adsorbing oxygen into the sieve under pressure and allowing the nitrogen to pass through, the other is regenerated by evacuating the pressure to the atmosphere. The performance of a PSA unit depends on the ambient temperature. The required purity and product pressure have an influence on the air demand and, of course, on the energy consumption.

Cryogenic Process

The cryogenic process generates nitrogen purities from 1 to 100 ppm oxygen. The cryogenic process separates air by means of rectification, which utilizes the different evaporation temperatures of the components of the air. A cryogenic plant mainly consists of a warm end (with an air compressor and a molecular sieve station) and a cold part (coldbox) with the rectification column where the actual air separation process takes place.

Inlet filters remove dust and other physical impurities from the air before it enters the air compressor, where it is compressed to the required process pressure. It is then precooled in the after-cooler or in a refrigeration drier. After passing through a moisture separator, the air enters one of two molecular adsorbers, where impurities (such as water, carbon dioxide, most hydrocarbons etc.) are removed. The process air is cooled down to almost liquefaction temperature in the main heat exchanger in counter-flow with the cold separation products and then fed into the bottom of the rectification column. There, the feed air is rectified. The pure nitrogen fraction is withdrawn at the top of the column, heated up in counter-flow in the main heat exchanger, and then fed into the product line. Cooling is supplied by Liquid Nitrogen (LIN) from the back-up system or is generated using an expansion turbine.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 — 三種氮氣產生技術比較

這三種技術在純度、成本、規模上有根本性差異，選型決策對製藥廠的長期運營影響極大：

  • 膜分離（Membrane）：最簡單，純度 95–99.5%，適合低純度需求（惰性保護、一般覆蓋）
  • PSA（Pressure Swing Adsorption）吸附法：純度 98–99.99%，中高用量現場產生的主流選擇
  • 低溫液化（Cryogenic）：純度最高（1–100 ppm O₂），適合超高純度需求，資本成本極高

重點提示 — PSA 氮氣的關鍵限制（選型決策點）

這是本附錄中最重要的工程決策點：

PSA 典型最高純度 = 99.5% N₂ = 最低 0.5% O₂ = 5,000 ppm O₂

如果應用需要 O₂ < 10 ppm（例如：高活性 API 的無氧包裝、鋰電池製造、某些生物製品的惰性覆蓋），PSA 系統根本無法達到規格。必須選擇液態氮（LIN）供應或低溫產生系統。

錯誤的技術選型在 URS 審查時最容易被忽略，卻在 OQ 測試時才發現，造成大量重工成本。

選型比較矩陣

技術         純度上限        O₂ 殘留        適用場景
膜分離       99.5% N₂       0.5% O₂       一般惰性保護
PSA          99.99% N₂      100 ppm O₂    中高純度現場產氮
低溫液化     99.9999% N₂    1 ppm O₂      超高純度需求

經濟臨界點（PSA vs 液態 N₂）：
用量 > ~2,000 m³/月 → PSA 現場產生通常更經濟
用量 < ~500 m³/月  → 液態 N₂ 外購+儲槽通常更經濟

比喻說明 — PSA 的雙塔輪替原理

PSA 的兩個吸附塔就像兩位接力跑步的選手：一位在跑步（生產氮氣、吸附氧氣），另一位在喘氣恢復（降壓再生、清除吸附的氧氣）。這個交替讓系統能連續輸出氮氣，不像單塔系統需要停機再生。分子篩就是跑步選手的體能——會疲勞（被 O₂ 佔滿），但能週期性恢復。

實務應用 — CDMO 無菌充填線的氮氣選型

在無菌充填 CDMO 中，氮氣用途通常分為兩類：

  • 產品直接接觸（product-contact）：瓶頭空間充氮、溶液覆蓋 → 通常需要藥典級純度，要求 O₂ <10–100 ppm → 必須用液態 N₂ 或低溫系統
  • 非直接接觸（non-product-contact）：設備覆蓋、安全惰化 → PSA 系統即可滿足

因此許多 CDMO 同時設置 PSA 系統（供非直接接觸用）+ 液態 N₂ 儲槽（供直接接觸用），以平衡成本與純度需求。

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 — 空氣組成對製藥氣體的意義

Table 16.1 告訴我們壓縮空氣的原料組成。對製藥而言，關鍵的污染物組分是：

  • O₂（20.99%）：PSA 氮氣系統要清除的主要對象；對易氧化藥品（脂質體、蛋白質）是降解風險
  • CO₂（0.03%）：藥典要求壓縮空氣中 CO₂ <500 ppm；高 CO₂ 可能改變注射劑 pH
  • Argon（0.933%）：化學惰性，通常不在控制指標中，但高純度氮氣中氬氣會被計入殘留量

重點提示 — 露點（Dew Point）是壓縮空氣品質的關鍵指標

Dew Point（露點）是壓縮空氣中水分含量的最重要指標。ISO 8573-1 以壓力露點（Pressure Dew Point, PDP）表示氣體乾燥程度：

  • ISO Class 1–2：PDP ≤ -70°C（超乾，用於敏感儀器氣體）
  • ISO Class 4：PDP ≤ +3°C（一般製程用途）
  • ISO Class 6：PDP ≤ +10°C（非關鍵應用）

Table 16.5 的絕對濕度資料直接連結到露點計算：溫度越高，空氣可攜帶的水分越多，冷凝風險越大。

核心概念解析 — 水活性（aW）與微生物控制

水活性（Water Activity, aW）是食品科學概念，但同樣適用於製藥氣體系統的微生物風險評估。aW 代表基質中自由水的比例（0=完全乾燥，1=純水）。

Table 16.6 的關鍵洞察：當 aW < 0.50 時，所有微生物均無法增殖。這解釋了為何乾燥的壓縮空氣（露點 <-20°C，對應的水分活性遠低於 0.5）本身不是微生物生長的良好環境——但管線中的冷凝水積聚點是高風險區域。

比喻說明 — 露點就像浴室鏡子起霧

洗熱水澡後，浴室鏡子起霧（凝結），因為空氣中的水分超過了鏡面溫度對應的飽和量。壓縮空氣管線中也一樣：若管線某段溫度低於氣體的露點，水分就會凝結在管壁上，形成積水——這既是管線腐蝕的根源，也是微生物的「綠洲」。乾燥器的任務就是讓露點遠低於管線可能達到的最低溫度。

核心概念解析 — 氣體系統品質的標準體系

製藥氣體系統的品質規格由多層標準體系共同定義，理解各層的優先順序至關重要：

  • 第一層：法規要求 — 21 CFR 210/211（美國）、EU Annex 1（歐洲）、ICH Q7/Q9 — 不可低於
  • 第二層：藥典規格 — USP、PhEur、JP 氣體專論 — 直接引用在 CoA 和批記錄
  • 第三層：工業標準 — ISO 8573-1（壓縮空氣純度等級）、ANSI/ISA（儀器空氣）— 設計依據
  • 第四層：指引文件 — ISPE、PDA、CGA — 最佳實踐，不具法律強制力

重點提示 — ISO 8573-1:2010 是關鍵設計標準

ISO 8573-1:2010（壓縮空氣純度等級）是設計壓縮空氣系統品質規格的核心參考標準，以三個指標分類：

  • 顆粒等級（Particle Class）：Class 1 = 最嚴格（≥0.1 µm <20,000/m³）
  • 水分等級（Humidity/Water Class）：以壓力露點（PDP）定義，Class 1 = PDP ≤ -70°C
  • 油脂等級（Oil Class）：Class 1 = 油含量 ≤ 0.01 mg/m³

藥典中的醫用空氣（Medical Air）要求通常對應 ISO 8573-1 Class 1–2。在 URS 中引用 ISO 8573-1 等級是定義純度規格的標準做法。

比喻說明 — 藥典氣體專論的角色

藥典氣體專論（如 USP <Medical Air>、PhEur <Nitrogen>）就像食品標準的「國家標準成分表」：它定義了每種氣體「達到藥用等級」的最低要求。製藥廠在 CoA 中引用這些專論，監管機構據此判斷氣體是否符合用於製造藥品的資格。超出藥典規格的自主限度（internal specification）可以更嚴格，但不能更寬鬆。

18.2 Definitions

Active Pharmaceutical Ingredient (API)
Any substance or mixture of substances intended to be used in the manufacture of a drug (medicinal) product and that, when used in the production of a drug, becomes an active ingredient of the drug product. Such substances are intended to furnish pharmacological activity or other direct effect in the diagnosis, cure, mitigation, treatment, or prevention of disease, or to affect the structure and function of the body. (ICH Q7)

Blanketing
Blanketing is the term used to describe putting a layer of gas, typically nitrogen into the space in a vessel above the product generally with the intention of preventing oxygen in the air contacting and degrading the product, though it may also be used to maintain a low oxygen atmosphere to reduce risks of explosion.

Critical Aspects
Are typically functions, features, abilities, and performance characteristics necessary for the manufacturing process and systems to ensure consistent product quality and patient safety. They should be identified and documented based on scientific product and process understanding. (ASTM E2500-07)

Critical Process Parameter (CPP)
A process parameter whose variability has an impact on the critical quality attribute and therefore should be monitored or controlled to ensure the process produces the desired quality. (ICH Q8(R2) II 4.0)

Critical Quality Attribute (CQA)
A physical, chemical, biological, or microbiological property or characteristic that should be within an appropriate limit, range, or distribution to ensure the desired product quality. CQAs are generally associated with the drug substance, excipients, intermediates (in process materials) and drug product. (ICH Q8(R2) II 2.2)

Dewpoint
The dewpoint is the temperature to which a given parcel of humid air must be cooled, at constant barometric pressure, for water vapor to condense into water. The condensed water is called dew. The dewpoint is a saturation temperature.

GxP Compliance
Meeting all applicable pharmaceutical and associated life-science regulatory requirements.

Subject Matter Expert (SME)
Those individuals with specific expertise in a particular area or field. SME responsibilities include planning and defining verification strategies, defining acceptance criteria, selection of appropriate test methods, execution of verification tests, and reviewing results. (ASTM E2500-07)

Supply Air
Conditioned air supplied mechanically to the space (room), measured here in cubic feet per minute (CFM). (Divide by 0.6 to get cubic meters per hour CMH.)

System Owner
The person ultimately responsible for the system performance. This person may be the head of the functional unit or department using that system, or maintaining the system although the role should be based on specific knowledge of the process rather than position in the organization. The system owner is responsible for ensuring that the system and its operation is in compliance and fit for intended use with applicable Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) throughout its useful life.

Process Gases
Process gases are defined as gases which can affect product quality.

Turnover Package (TOP)
A collection of pertinent design, construction, vendor, and operational documentation. This collection of documentation is used for the qualification and process validation activity, as well as reference and single source information for the life of any particular system, process, or piece of equipment. Note: This term is often used interchangeably with Engineering Turnover Package (ETOP).

Non-Liquefied Gases
Non-liquefied gases are elements, or combination of elements, that have boiling points lower than -130°F (-90°C). Examples include: nitrogen, oxygen, argon. When specific elements become liquefied at extremely low temperatures they are referred to as "cryogenic liquids."

Liquefied Gases
Liquefied gases are those elements or compounds that become liquid in their containers at ambient temperatures and at specified pressures from 25 psig (172 kPa) to 1500 psig (10,340 kPa). These gases have boiling points in the range of -130°F (-90°C) to 68°F (20°C).

Supercritical Fluid Extraction (SFE)
Supercritical Fluid Extraction (SFE) refers to a process in which a Supercritical Fluid (SF) is used as a solvent. At critical temperatures and pressures, SFs possess the properties of both the liquid and gas. The most widely used SF gas is carbon dioxide (CO₂).

核心概念解析 — 術語表的實際用途

本術語表不只是字典。在氣體系統資格確認計畫中，它有三個實際功能：

  • 一致性保證：確保 URS、DS、IQ、OQ、PQ 全套文件使用相同術語定義，避免稽查時被質疑
  • 跨部門溝通：工程、QA、生產的人員對 CPP、CQA、PDP 有共同的理解基礎
  • 監管對齊：所有定義都來自 ICH Q8/Q9、ASTM E2500 等官方文件，直接引用出處

重點提示 — 氣體系統資格確認必知的10個關鍵術語

  • PDP（Pressure Dewpoint）：壓力露點 — 壓縮空氣乾燥品質的主要量測指標
  • POU（Point of Use）：使用點 — 取樣和驗收規格應在 POU 驗證，而非供應端
  • Blanketing（覆蓋）：氮氣保護的技術術語，區別於「purging（吹掃）」
  • TVHC（Total Volatile Hydrocarbon）：壓縮空氣中油脂污染的量測項目
  • ETOP/TOP（交接包）：系統完工後移交 QA 用的全套文件集
  • FAT（工廠驗收測試）：在供應商處完成的預安裝資格確認測試
  • LEL（爆炸下限）：可燃氣體（O₂、H₂）安全設計的關鍵參數
  • PRV（壓力調節閥）：系統中的壓力控制元件，需在 IQ 中確認設定值
  • CQA/CPP：決定哪些氣體參數需要 PQ 驗證的分類依據
  • GEP（良好工程實踐）：低風險設施/設備的設計基準，不需要完整資格確認

比喻說明 — System Owner 與 Process Owner 的分工

System Owner（系統擁有者）就像大樓的管理處主任，負責確保電梯、空調、消防系統正常運行、符合法規。Process Owner（製程擁有者）就像大樓的租戶，負責在這個空間內的生產活動。當氣體系統發生問題時，System Owner 處理基礎設施合規；Process Owner 處理對批次的影響評估——兩者的 CAPA 責任劃分應在 SOP 中明確定義。

核心概念解析 — Blanketing vs. Purging vs. Pressurizing

這三個氮氣使用模式容易混淆，但目的和操作參數不同：

  • Blanketing（覆蓋）：在液面或產品上方維持惰性氣層，防止 O₂/水分接觸。通常是靜態或低流量持續補充模式
  • Purging（吹掃）：主動用氮氣流驅替容器或管線中的空氣，在使用前或清潔後進行。需要較大流量
  • Pressurizing（加壓）：利用氮氣建立正壓，防止外部氣體進入（如密封反應器）或推動液體傳輸

URS 中需要明確區分應用模式，因為它們對應不同的純度規格和流量計算。

單位換算快查 — 製藥氣體常用換算

體積流量：
1 SCFM = 28.32 L/min = 1.699 m³/hr

壓力：
1 psig = 0.0689 bar(g) = 6.895 kPa(g)
1 bar = 14.504 psig

露點換算（近似）：
PDP -70°C ≈ 水分含量 ~2.5 ppm(v)
PDP -40°C ≈ 水分含量 ~128 ppm(v)
PDP  -20°C ≈ 水分含量 ~935 ppm(v)
PDP   +3°C ≈ ISO 8573-1 Class 4 水分等級

CFM → m³/hr：÷ 0.589
m³/hr → CFM：× 0.589
