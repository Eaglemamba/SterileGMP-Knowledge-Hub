概述與試驗方法總覽 Introduction & Methods Overview

原文內容 Original Text

The test for bacterial endotoxins (BET) is used to detect or quantify endotoxins from gram-negative bacteria using amoebocyte lysate from the horseshoe crab (Limulus polyphemus or Tachypleus tridentatus). There are 3 techniques for this test:

  • the gel-clot technique, which is based on gel formation;
  • the turbidimetric technique, based on the development of turbidity after cleavage of an endogenous substrate;
  • the chromogenic technique, based on the development of colour after cleavage of a synthetic peptide-chromogen complex.

The following 6 methods are described in the present chapter:

Proceed by any of the 6 methods for the test. In the event of doubt or dispute, the final decision is made based upon method A unless otherwise indicated in the monograph.

The test is carried out in a manner that avoids endotoxin contamination.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

為什麼用馬蹄蟹（Horseshoe Crab）？

鱟（Horseshoe Crab）的血液（淡藍色，含銅而非鐵）對革蘭氏陰性菌的內毒素極度敏感——遇到內毒素後，Amoebocyte（阿米巴狀血細胞）內的酶聯反應會啟動凝血。這是 4 億年演化出的先天免疫機制。

LAL vs TAL：

  • Limulus polyphemus（美洲鱟）→ LAL（Limulus Amebocyte Lysate）
  • Tachypleus tridentatus（中國鱟/三棘鱟）→ TAL（Tachypleus Amebocyte Lysate）

兩者均被 Ph.Eur. 接受，差異在於地域來源和供應鏈。

方法 A 的仲裁地位：任何方法均可用於日常試驗，但遇到糾紛時以 Method A（凝膠法限度試驗）為準——因為 Method A 是最早建立的方法，法規解讀最清晰。

比喻說明 Analogy

鱟試劑就像「天然的警報系統」——革蘭氏陰性菌的內毒素（LPS，脂多醣）就是觸發警報的「信號分子」。一旦接觸，凝血反應（gel formation）就是警報響起。現代的光度定量法（C/D/E/F）相當於把警報訊號數位化，可以量化而非只是「有/無」。

重點提示 Key Notes

β-葡聚糖（β-Glucan）干擾問題：原版 LAL 試劑也會對某些 β-葡聚糖（如真菌細胞壁成分、部分靜脈輸液輔料）產生反應，造成假陽性。解決方案：使用「G factor-free LAL」（去除 G 因子的鱟試劑），專一偵測內毒素，不受 β-葡聚糖干擾。這在含 β-葡聚糖的藥品（如某些生物製劑輔料）測試時尤其重要。

1. 儀器設備 Apparatus

原文內容 Original Text

Depyrogenate all glassware and other heat-stable apparatus in a hot-air oven using a validated process. A commonly used minimum time and temperature is 30 min at 250 °C. If employing plastic apparatus, such as microplates and pipette tips for automatic pipetters, use apparatus shown to be free of detectable endotoxin and which does not interfere in the test.

NOTE: in this chapter, the term 'tube' includes all types of receptacles, for example microplate wells.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

去熱原（Depyrogenation）的必要性：若試驗器具本身含有內毒素，會干擾試驗結果（假陽性）。玻璃器具用乾熱（250°C × 30 min）可確實降解內毒素（3-log reduction per USP/Ph.Eur. 標準）。

塑膠耗材：microplate、移液吸頭無法耐受 250°C，必須購買已驗證為「Endotoxin-free」的認證產品（如 Pyrogen-free 規格移液管，通常 COA 上標示 <0.005 EU/mL）。

重點提示 Key Notes

乾熱去熱原的法規根據：250°C × 30 min 是 Ph.Eur. 和 USP 均認可的「minimum time-temperature」，對應 Fh ≥ 30 min（equivalent to ≥ 3-log reduction of endotoxin）。若用其他時間-溫度組合，需要驗證。在 FDA 查廠中，「depyrogenation cycle validation」是 Hot Air Tunnel/烘箱的必備文件。

2. 試劑與測試溶液 Reagents & Test Solutions

原文內容 Original Text

(1) Amoebocyte lysate
Amoebocyte lysate is a lyophilised product obtained from amoebocyte lysate from the horseshoe crab (Limulus polyphemus or Tachypleus tridentatus). This reagent refers only to a product manufactured in accordance with the regulations of the competent authority.

NOTE: amoebocyte lysate reacts with some β-glucans in addition to endotoxins. Amoebocyte lysate preparations which do not react with glucans are available (G factor-free preparations).

(2) Lysate solution
Dissolve amoebocyte lysate in water for BET or in a buffer, as recommended by the lysate manufacturer, by gentle stirring. Store the reconstituted lysate, refrigerated or frozen, as indicated by the manufacturer.

(3) Water for BET (water for bacterial endotoxins test)
Water for injections R or water produced by other procedures that shows no reaction with the lysate employed at the detection limit of the reagent.

3. Preparation of the Standard Endotoxin Stock Solution
The standard endotoxin stock solution is prepared from an endotoxin reference standard that has been calibrated against the International Standard, for example endotoxin standard BRP.
Endotoxin is expressed in International Units (IU). The equivalence in IU of the International Standard is stated by the World Health Organization.
NOTE: one International Unit (IU) of endotoxin is equal to one Endotoxin Unit (EU).

4. Preparation of Standard Endotoxin Solutions
After vigorously mixing the standard endotoxin stock solution, prepare appropriate serial dilutions using water for BET. Use the solutions as soon as possible to avoid loss of activity by adsorption.

5. Preparation of Test Solutions
Prepare the test solutions by dissolving or diluting active substances or medicinal products using water for BET. Adjust the pH of the test solution so that the pH of the mixture of the lysate and test solution falls within the pH range specified by the lysate manufacturer, usually 6.0 to 8.0. Buffers must be validated to be free of detectable endotoxin and interfering factors.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

試劑層次架構（3 個關鍵試劑）：

  • LAL 試劑（Amoebocyte lysate）：冷凍乾燥品，需用 BET 用水復溶，依廠商指示儲存（通常 -20°C 或 2–8°C）。
  • 標準內毒素（Standard Endotoxin BRP）：由歐洲藥品品質管理局（EDQM）標定，1 IU = 1 EU，用於每次試驗的陽性對照和靈敏度確認。
  • BET 用水：WFI 規格，需驗證在試劑偵測極限下無反應——這是整個試驗系統的「空白背景」。

IU vs EU：Ph.Eur. 用 IU（International Unit），USP 傳統用 EU（Endotoxin Unit）——兩者等值（1 IU = 1 EU），只是命名不同。

重點提示 Key Notes

標準內毒素稀釋的吸附損失問題：內毒素分子帶負電，容易吸附在玻璃器具或塑膠表面上。稀釋後「越稀越容易損失」，因此：

  • 稀釋後應「盡快使用（as soon as possible）」
  • 每次稀釋前需「充分震盪混合（vigorous mixing）」
  • 建議使用 Pyrogen-free 認證的低吸附移液管

pH 6.0–8.0 的重要性：LAL 反應的酶活性對 pH 敏感。pH 偏低或偏高會抑制反應，導致假陰性。含酸性緩衝劑的藥品（如某些維生素 C 注射液 pH ≈ 5.5）需調整 pH 後再進行 BET。

實務應用 Practical Application

CDMO QC 實驗室在進行新產品的 BET 方法建立時，「BET 用水的驗證」是第一步——用預計使用的 WFI 批次測試是否在 LAL 最低偵測極限下無反應。這個驗證結果需記錄並保留供稽核使用，換用新批 WFI 或新的水系統後需重新確認。

6. 最大有效稀釋倍數 Maximum Valid Dilution (MVD)

原文內容 Original Text

The Maximum Valid Dilution (MVD) is the maximum allowable dilution of a sample at which the endotoxin limit can be determined. Determine the MVD using the following formula:

MVD = (Endotoxin limit × Concentration of test solution) / λ

Endotoxin limit: the endotoxin limit for active substances administered parenterally, defined on the basis of dose, is equal to:

Endotoxin limit (K) = K / M
  • K = threshold pyrogenic dose of endotoxin per kilogram of body mass
  • M = maximum recommended bolus dose of product per kilogram of body mass

When the product is to be injected at frequent intervals or infused continuously, M is the maximum total dose administered in a single hour period.

The endotoxin limit for active substances administered parenterally is specified in units such as IU/mL, IU/mg, IU/Unit of biological activity, etc., in monographs.

Concentration of test solution:

  • mg/mL if the endotoxin limit is specified by mass (IU/mg)
  • Units/mL if the endotoxin limit is specified by unit of biological activity (IU/Unit)
  • mL/mL if the endotoxin limit is specified by volume (IU/mL)

λ = the labelled lysate sensitivity in the gel-clot technique (IU/mL) or the lowest concentration used in the standard curve of the turbidimetric or chromogenic techniques.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

MVD 的意義：MVD 是試驗設計的「安全邊界」——稀釋可以消除干擾因素，但稀釋太多就無法判斷是否符合內毒素限度。MVD 告訴你：在不超過這個稀釋倍數的前提下，結果是有意義的。

公式中三個關鍵變數：

  • Endotoxin limit（限度值）：由藥典各論或 K/M 公式決定，固定值
  • Concentration：待測溶液的實際濃度（mg/mL 或 mL/mL 等）
  • λ（Lambda）：LAL 試劑靈敏度，廠商標示值；靈敏度越高（λ 越小），MVD 越大

K/M 公式（限度值計算）：

  • K = 5 IU/kg（靜脈注射的閾值熱原量，Ph.Eur. 規定）
  • M = 最大單次（bolus）劑量（mg/kg 或 mL/kg），若持續輸注則取 1 小時最大總量
  • 限度值 = K/M，單位為 IU/mg 或 IU/mL

公式與計算 MVD Calculation Examples

【情境 1：小容量注射液（Aminoglycosides）】
  產品：20 mg/mL Gentamicin 注射液
  最大劑量：5 mg/kg/hour（IV infusion）
  K = 5 IU/kg
  M = 5 mg/kg → Endotoxin limit = K/M = 1 IU/mg
  Concentration = 20 mg/mL
  λ = 0.25 IU/mL（LAL 試劑靈敏度）

  MVD = (1 IU/mg × 20 mg/mL) / 0.25 IU/mL = 80

  → 可稀釋至多 80 倍進行測試

【情境 2：靈敏度對 MVD 的影響】
  相同產品，換用 λ = 0.125 IU/mL 的 LAL：
  MVD = (1 × 20) / 0.125 = 160
  → 更敏感的 LAL → 更大 MVD → 更容易消除干擾

【K = 5 IU/kg 的來源】
  由兔子熱原試驗（Rabbit Pyrogen Test）推導而來，
  0.2 EU/kg 為最低致熱量，
  乘以 25（安全係數）= 5 IU/kg
  此值被 ICH、Ph.Eur.、USP 一致採用

重點提示 Key Notes

MVD 是上限，不是目標稀釋倍數：許多人誤解「用 MVD 倍數稀釋」，但正確做法是「在 MVD 範圍內，找到能消除干擾且結果有效的最低稀釋倍數」。稀釋越多，靈敏度越低，越容易漏掉低量內毒素。

「Infusion 產品」的 M 計算：持續輸注或高頻率給藥（如每 4 小時一次），M 取「1 小時總量」，避免低估給藥量導致內毒素限度寬鬆設定。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 一支 100 mL 的 0.9% NaCl 注射液，最大輸注速率為 500 mL/hour，K = 5 IU/kg，體重 70 kg，λ = 0.25 IU/mL。計算其內毒素限度與 MVD。
  2. 為什麼「BET 用水」需要在 LAL 試劑的「偵測極限下無反應」？若 BET 用水本身含有微量內毒素，對試驗結果有何影響？
  3. 某注射用原料藥的藥典各論規定內毒素限度為 0.5 IU/mg，若 LAL 靈敏度 λ = 0.25 IU/mL，測試濃度 2 mg/mL，計算 MVD。若干擾因素測試失敗，有哪些方法可以嘗試消除干擾？
