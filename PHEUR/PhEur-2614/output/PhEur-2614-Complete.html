Section 1: Introduction, Reagents, Endotoxin Limit & MVD

概述、試劑、內毒素限度與MVD計算 | p1-p2

European Pharmacopoeia 11.0 — Chapter 2.6.14: Bacterial Endotoxins | p1–p2

本章學習目標

  • 理解細菌內毒素試驗（BET）的生物學原理：馬蹄蟹（Horseshoe Crab）鱟試劑與革蘭氏陰性菌內毒素的凝血反應機制
  • 掌握 6 種試驗方法（A–F）的分類架構：凝膠法（Gel-Clot）與光度定量法（Photometric）的技術特點與仲裁地位
  • 熟悉試驗所需試劑：LAL 試劑（Amoebocyte lysate）、標準內毒素（Standard endotoxin BRP）、BET 用水的規格要求
  • 能夠正確計算 MVD（Maximum Valid Dilution）及理解其在試驗設計中的核心作用
概述與試驗方法總覽 Introduction & Methods Overview

原文內容 Original Text

The test for bacterial endotoxins (BET) is used to detect or quantify endotoxins from gram-negative bacteria using amoebocyte lysate from the horseshoe crab (Limulus polyphemus or Tachypleus tridentatus). There are 3 techniques for this test:

  • the gel-clot technique, which is based on gel formation;
  • the turbidimetric technique, based on the development of turbidity after cleavage of an endogenous substrate;
  • the chromogenic technique, based on the development of colour after cleavage of a synthetic peptide-chromogen complex.

The following 6 methods are described in the present chapter:

Proceed by any of the 6 methods for the test. In the event of doubt or dispute, the final decision is made based upon method A unless otherwise indicated in the monograph.

The test is carried out in a manner that avoids endotoxin contamination.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

為什麼用馬蹄蟹（Horseshoe Crab）？

鱟（Horseshoe Crab）的血液（淡藍色，含銅而非鐵）對革蘭氏陰性菌的內毒素極度敏感——遇到內毒素後，Amoebocyte（阿米巴狀血細胞）內的酶聯反應會啟動凝血。這是 4 億年演化出的先天免疫機制。

LAL vs TAL：

  • Limulus polyphemus（美洲鱟）→ LAL（Limulus Amebocyte Lysate）
  • Tachypleus tridentatus（中國鱟/三棘鱟）→ TAL（Tachypleus Amebocyte Lysate）

兩者均被 Ph.Eur. 接受，差異在於地域來源和供應鏈。

方法 A 的仲裁地位：任何方法均可用於日常試驗，但遇到糾紛時以 Method A（凝膠法限度試驗）為準——因為 Method A 是最早建立的方法，法規解讀最清晰。

比喻說明 Analogy

鱟試劑就像「天然的警報系統」——革蘭氏陰性菌的內毒素（LPS，脂多醣）就是觸發警報的「信號分子」。一旦接觸，凝血反應（gel formation）就是警報響起。現代的光度定量法（C/D/E/F）相當於把警報訊號數位化，可以量化而非只是「有/無」。

重點提示 Key Notes

β-葡聚糖（β-Glucan）干擾問題：原版 LAL 試劑也會對某些 β-葡聚糖（如真菌細胞壁成分、部分靜脈輸液輔料）產生反應，造成假陽性。解決方案：使用「G factor-free LAL」（去除 G 因子的鱟試劑），專一偵測內毒素，不受 β-葡聚糖干擾。這在含 β-葡聚糖的藥品（如某些生物製劑輔料）測試時尤其重要。

1. 儀器設備 Apparatus

原文內容 Original Text

Depyrogenate all glassware and other heat-stable apparatus in a hot-air oven using a validated process. A commonly used minimum time and temperature is 30 min at 250 °C. If employing plastic apparatus, such as microplates and pipette tips for automatic pipetters, use apparatus shown to be free of detectable endotoxin and which does not interfere in the test.

NOTE: in this chapter, the term 'tube' includes all types of receptacles, for example microplate wells.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

去熱原（Depyrogenation）的必要性：若試驗器具本身含有內毒素，會干擾試驗結果（假陽性）。玻璃器具用乾熱（250°C × 30 min）可確實降解內毒素（3-log reduction per USP/Ph.Eur. 標準）。

塑膠耗材：microplate、移液吸頭無法耐受 250°C，必須購買已驗證為「Endotoxin-free」的認證產品（如 Pyrogen-free 規格移液管，通常 COA 上標示 <0.005 EU/mL）。

重點提示 Key Notes

乾熱去熱原的法規根據：250°C × 30 min 是 Ph.Eur. 和 USP 均認可的「minimum time-temperature」，對應 Fh ≥ 30 min（equivalent to ≥ 3-log reduction of endotoxin）。若用其他時間-溫度組合，需要驗證。在 FDA 查廠中，「depyrogenation cycle validation」是 Hot Air Tunnel/烘箱的必備文件。

2. 試劑與測試溶液 Reagents & Test Solutions

原文內容 Original Text

(1) Amoebocyte lysate
Amoebocyte lysate is a lyophilised product obtained from amoebocyte lysate from the horseshoe crab (Limulus polyphemus or Tachypleus tridentatus). This reagent refers only to a product manufactured in accordance with the regulations of the competent authority.

NOTE: amoebocyte lysate reacts with some β-glucans in addition to endotoxins. Amoebocyte lysate preparations which do not react with glucans are available (G factor-free preparations).

(2) Lysate solution
Dissolve amoebocyte lysate in water for BET or in a buffer, as recommended by the lysate manufacturer, by gentle stirring. Store the reconstituted lysate, refrigerated or frozen, as indicated by the manufacturer.

(3) Water for BET (water for bacterial endotoxins test)
Water for injections R or water produced by other procedures that shows no reaction with the lysate employed at the detection limit of the reagent.

3. Preparation of the Standard Endotoxin Stock Solution
The standard endotoxin stock solution is prepared from an endotoxin reference standard that has been calibrated against the International Standard, for example endotoxin standard BRP.
Endotoxin is expressed in International Units (IU). The equivalence in IU of the International Standard is stated by the World Health Organization.
NOTE: one International Unit (IU) of endotoxin is equal to one Endotoxin Unit (EU).

4. Preparation of Standard Endotoxin Solutions
After vigorously mixing the standard endotoxin stock solution, prepare appropriate serial dilutions using water for BET. Use the solutions as soon as possible to avoid loss of activity by adsorption.

5. Preparation of Test Solutions
Prepare the test solutions by dissolving or diluting active substances or medicinal products using water for BET. Adjust the pH of the test solution so that the pH of the mixture of the lysate and test solution falls within the pH range specified by the lysate manufacturer, usually 6.0 to 8.0. Buffers must be validated to be free of detectable endotoxin and interfering factors.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

試劑層次架構（3 個關鍵試劑）：

  • LAL 試劑（Amoebocyte lysate）：冷凍乾燥品，需用 BET 用水復溶，依廠商指示儲存（通常 -20°C 或 2–8°C）。
  • 標準內毒素（Standard Endotoxin BRP）：由歐洲藥品品質管理局（EDQM）標定，1 IU = 1 EU，用於每次試驗的陽性對照和靈敏度確認。
  • BET 用水：WFI 規格，需驗證在試劑偵測極限下無反應——這是整個試驗系統的「空白背景」。

IU vs EU：Ph.Eur. 用 IU（International Unit），USP 傳統用 EU（Endotoxin Unit）——兩者等值（1 IU = 1 EU），只是命名不同。

重點提示 Key Notes

標準內毒素稀釋的吸附損失問題：內毒素分子帶負電，容易吸附在玻璃器具或塑膠表面上。稀釋後「越稀越容易損失」，因此：

  • 稀釋後應「盡快使用（as soon as possible）」
  • 每次稀釋前需「充分震盪混合（vigorous mixing）」
  • 建議使用 Pyrogen-free 認證的低吸附移液管

pH 6.0–8.0 的重要性：LAL 反應的酶活性對 pH 敏感。pH 偏低或偏高會抑制反應，導致假陰性。含酸性緩衝劑的藥品（如某些維生素 C 注射液 pH ≈ 5.5）需調整 pH 後再進行 BET。

實務應用 Practical Application

CDMO QC 實驗室在進行新產品的 BET 方法建立時，「BET 用水的驗證」是第一步——用預計使用的 WFI 批次測試是否在 LAL 最低偵測極限下無反應。這個驗證結果需記錄並保留供稽核使用，換用新批 WFI 或新的水系統後需重新確認。

6. 最大有效稀釋倍數 Maximum Valid Dilution (MVD)

原文內容 Original Text

The Maximum Valid Dilution (MVD) is the maximum allowable dilution of a sample at which the endotoxin limit can be determined. Determine the MVD using the following formula:

MVD = (Endotoxin limit × Concentration of test solution) / λ

Endotoxin limit: the endotoxin limit for active substances administered parenterally, defined on the basis of dose, is equal to:

Endotoxin limit (K) = K / M
  • K = threshold pyrogenic dose of endotoxin per kilogram of body mass
  • M = maximum recommended bolus dose of product per kilogram of body mass

When the product is to be injected at frequent intervals or infused continuously, M is the maximum total dose administered in a single hour period.

The endotoxin limit for active substances administered parenterally is specified in units such as IU/mL, IU/mg, IU/Unit of biological activity, etc., in monographs.

Concentration of test solution:

  • mg/mL if the endotoxin limit is specified by mass (IU/mg)
  • Units/mL if the endotoxin limit is specified by unit of biological activity (IU/Unit)
  • mL/mL if the endotoxin limit is specified by volume (IU/mL)

λ = the labelled lysate sensitivity in the gel-clot technique (IU/mL) or the lowest concentration used in the standard curve of the turbidimetric or chromogenic techniques.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

MVD 的意義：MVD 是試驗設計的「安全邊界」——稀釋可以消除干擾因素，但稀釋太多就無法判斷是否符合內毒素限度。MVD 告訴你：在不超過這個稀釋倍數的前提下，結果是有意義的。

公式中三個關鍵變數：

  • Endotoxin limit（限度值）：由藥典各論或 K/M 公式決定，固定值
  • Concentration：待測溶液的實際濃度（mg/mL 或 mL/mL 等）
  • λ（Lambda）：LAL 試劑靈敏度，廠商標示值；靈敏度越高（λ 越小），MVD 越大

K/M 公式（限度值計算）：

  • K = 5 IU/kg（靜脈注射的閾值熱原量，Ph.Eur. 規定）
  • M = 最大單次（bolus）劑量（mg/kg 或 mL/kg），若持續輸注則取 1 小時最大總量
  • 限度值 = K/M，單位為 IU/mg 或 IU/mL

公式與計算 MVD Calculation Examples

【情境 1：小容量注射液（Aminoglycosides）】
  產品：20 mg/mL Gentamicin 注射液
  最大劑量：5 mg/kg/hour（IV infusion）
  K = 5 IU/kg
  M = 5 mg/kg → Endotoxin limit = K/M = 1 IU/mg
  Concentration = 20 mg/mL
  λ = 0.25 IU/mL（LAL 試劑靈敏度）

  MVD = (1 IU/mg × 20 mg/mL) / 0.25 IU/mL = 80

  → 可稀釋至多 80 倍進行測試

【情境 2：靈敏度對 MVD 的影響】
  相同產品，換用 λ = 0.125 IU/mL 的 LAL：
  MVD = (1 × 20) / 0.125 = 160
  → 更敏感的 LAL → 更大 MVD → 更容易消除干擾

【K = 5 IU/kg 的來源】
  由兔子熱原試驗（Rabbit Pyrogen Test）推導而來，
  0.2 EU/kg 為最低致熱量，
  乘以 25（安全係數）= 5 IU/kg
  此值被 ICH、Ph.Eur.、USP 一致採用

重點提示 Key Notes

MVD 是上限，不是目標稀釋倍數：許多人誤解「用 MVD 倍數稀釋」，但正確做法是「在 MVD 範圍內，找到能消除干擾且結果有效的最低稀釋倍數」。稀釋越多，靈敏度越低，越容易漏掉低量內毒素。

「Infusion 產品」的 M 計算：持續輸注或高頻率給藥（如每 4 小時一次），M 取「1 小時總量」，避免低估給藥量導致內毒素限度寬鬆設定。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 一支 100 mL 的 0.9% NaCl 注射液，最大輸注速率為 500 mL/hour，K = 5 IU/kg，體重 70 kg，λ = 0.25 IU/mL。計算其內毒素限度與 MVD。
  2. 為什麼「BET 用水」需要在 LAL 試劑的「偵測極限下無反應」？若 BET 用水本身含有微量內毒素，對試驗結果有何影響？
  3. 某注射用原料藥的藥典各論規定內毒素限度為 0.5 IU/mg，若 LAL 靈敏度 λ = 0.25 IU/mL，測試濃度 2 mg/mL，計算 MVD。若干擾因素測試失敗，有哪些方法可以嘗試消除干擾？

7. 凝膠法技術概述與靈敏度確認 Gel-Clot Technique & Lysate Sensitivity Confirmation

原文內容 Original Text

The gel-clot technique allows detection or quantification of endotoxins and is based on clotting of the lysate in the presence of endotoxins. The minimum concentration of endotoxins required to cause the lysate to clot under standard conditions is the labelled lysate sensitivity.

1. PREPARATORY TESTING
(i) Confirmation of the labelled lysate sensitivity

Confirm in 4 replicates the labelled sensitivity λ (expressed in IU/mL) of the lysate solution prior to use in the test. Confirmation is required when a new lot of lysate is used or when test conditions change.

Prepare standard solutions of at least 4 concentrations equivalent to 2λ, λ, 0.5λ and 0.25λ by diluting the standard endotoxin stock solution with water for BET.

Mix a volume of the lysate solution with an equal volume of 1 of the standard solutions (such as 0.1 mL aliquots) in each tube. Incubate the reaction mixture for a constant period according to the lysate manufacturer's recommendations (usually at 37 ± 1 °C for 60 ± 2 min), avoiding vibration.

Test the integrity of the gel: take each tube directly from the incubator and invert it through approximately 180° in one smooth motion. If a firm gel has formed that remains in place upon inversion, record the result as positive. A result is negative if an intact gel is not formed.

The test is considered valid when the lowest concentration of the standard solutions shows a negative result in all replicate tests.

The end-point is the lowest concentration in the series of decreasing concentrations of standard endotoxin that clots the lysate.

Determine the geometric mean end-point concentration:
Geometric mean = antilog(Σf / f) where Σf = sum of log₁₀ end-point concentrations, f = number of replicates.

The geometric mean end-point concentration is the measured sensitivity of the lysate solution (IU/mL). If this is not less than 0.5λ and not more than 2λ, the labelled sensitivity is confirmed.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

凝膠形成（Gel Formation）的機制：

內毒素（LPS）→ 啟動 LAL 中的 Factor C → 激活 Factor B → 激活凝固酶（Clotting enzyme）→ 凝固蛋白原（Coagulogen）裂解 → 不溶性凝膠（Coagulin gel）形成。

靈敏度確認的統計邏輯：

  • 4 稀釋度（2λ / λ / 0.5λ / 0.25λ）× 4 重複 = 16 管
  • 計算每組 4 管的幾何平均終點濃度
  • 合格標準：幾何平均值在 0.5λ 到 2λ 之間（即標示靈敏度的 ±1 倍對數範圍）

比喻說明 Analogy

靈敏度確認就像在使用溫度計前先「校正」——你知道試劑盒標示 λ = 0.25 IU/mL，但每批試劑可能略有差異。做一次靈敏度確認，就像用冰水（0°C）和沸水（100°C）確認溫度計是否還在正確範圍內。

「180°倒管」的判讀非常直觀——凝膠像果凍一樣固態就是陽性，沒有形成或呈流動狀就是陰性。

重點提示 Key Notes

絕對禁止振動（Avoid vibration）：培養期間的任何振動（路過的推車、離心機的震動）都可能破壞已形成的凝膠，導致假陰性。BET 試驗區通常設在避免振動的位置，或使用防振桌。

靈敏度確認的時機：每次使用新批次 LAL 試劑（New lot）都必須重新確認。這項要求常被 QC 人員忽略——換批次 LAL 就是一個重大「條件變更」。

幾何平均 vs 算術平均：使用幾何平均是因為稀釋系列為對數等比（×2）——對數空間中的算術平均等同原始空間中的幾何平均，更能反映稀釋系列的中心點。

公式與計算 Geometric Mean Calculation

靈敏度確認計算範例（λ = 0.25 IU/mL）：

4 組稀釋系列終點：
  Series 1: 0.25 IU/mL (= λ, 陽性最低濃度)
  Series 2: 0.125 IU/mL (= 0.5λ)
  Series 3: 0.25 IU/mL
  Series 4: 0.25 IU/mL

Σ log₁₀ = log(0.25) + log(0.125) + log(0.25) + log(0.25)
         = (-0.602) + (-0.903) + (-0.602) + (-0.602)
         = -2.709
f = 4
Mean = -2.709 / 4 = -0.677
Geometric mean = 10^(-0.677) = 0.210 IU/mL

判定：0.5λ = 0.125 ≤ 0.210 ≤ 2λ = 0.50 ✓
→ 靈敏度確認通過
7.1 干擾因素測試 Test for Interfering Factors (Table 2.6.14-1)

原文內容 Original Text

(ii) Test for interfering factors

Prepare solutions A, B, C and D as shown in Table 2.6.14.-1, and use the test solutions at a dilution less than the MVD, not containing any detectable endotoxins.

Table 2.6.14.-1

Legend:

  • Solution A = test solution, free of detectable endotoxins
  • Solution B = test for interference (test solution + known endotoxin)
  • Solution C = positive control (water for BET + known endotoxin)
  • Solution D = negative control (water for BET alone)

The test is considered valid when all replicates of solutions A and D show no reaction and the result of solution C confirms the labelled lysate sensitivity.

If the sensitivity of the lysate determined with solution B is not less than 0.5λ and not greater than 2λ, the test solution does not contain interfering factors.

If the preparation interferes at a dilution less than the MVD, repeat the test using a greater dilution (not exceeding MVD), or use a more sensitive lysate. Interference may also be overcome by filtration, neutralisation, dialysis or heat treatment.

To verify treatment effectiveness, repeat the test with endotoxin-spiked treated solution — confirming elimination without loss of endotoxins.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

四溶液的設計邏輯（ABCD 框架）：

  • A（待測品）：確認待測品本身不含可偵測的內毒素（基準）
  • B（待測品 + 已知內毒素）：核心——在產品基質中加入已知量內毒素，測試是否能正確偵測→判斷有無干擾
  • C（BET用水 + 已知內毒素）：對照——確認試劑本身靈敏度正常（消除變數）
  • D（BET用水，無內毒素）：陰性對照——確認 BET 用水和試劑均無背景訊號

判讀標準的設計意圖：B 的幾何平均在 0.5λ–2λ → 產品基質沒有讓 LAL 反應失真（既未抑制也未增強超出允許範圍）。

重點提示 Key Notes

「50%–200% recovery」的非正式說法：B 的結果在 0.5λ 到 2λ 之間，等於說「加進去的內毒素有 50%–200% 被回收」。低於 50%（抑制）→ 產品有抑制 LAL 反應的成分；高於 200%（增強）→ 產品或其稀釋液本身含有增強反應的物質（如某些去污劑）。

干擾排除策略優先順序（CDMO 實際操作）：

  1. 進一步稀釋（最簡單，只要不超過 MVD）
  2. 換用靈敏度更高的 LAL（λ 更小 → MVD 更大 → 可稀釋更多）
  3. 過濾、中和、透析、熱處理（需要驗證治療本身不損失內毒素）

實務應用 Practical Application

含有大量蛋白質的生物製劑（如抗體注射液）常見干擾問題。蛋白質可能通過以下機制干擾 BET：

  • 蛋白質結合內毒素（LPS binding protein）→ 隱藏內毒素 → 假陰性
  • 蛋白質沉澱在管底 → 遮蔽凝膠形成判讀

解決方案：常用 1:10 稀釋 + pH 調整，或換用 rFC（重組 Factor C）試劑，其對蛋白質基質的耐受性更強，且不受 β-葡聚糖干擾。rFC 方法也被 Ph.Eur. chapter 2.6.32 涵蓋（但 chapter 2.6.14 本身不包含 rFC）。

7.2 Method A — 限度試驗 Limit Test (Table 2.6.14-2)

原文內容 Original Text

2. LIMIT TEST (METHOD A)

(i) Procedure
Prepare solutions A, B, C and D as shown in Table 2.6.14.-2.

Prepare solution A and solution B (positive product control) using a dilution not greater than the MVD. Solutions B and C (positive controls) contain the standard endotoxin at a concentration corresponding to twice the labelled lysate sensitivity (2λ). Solution D (negative control) consists of water for BET.

(ii) Interpretation

The test is considered valid when both replicates of solutions B and C are positive and those of D are negative.

  • When a negative result is found for both replicates of solution A → the preparation complies.
  • When a positive result is found for both replicates of solution A → the preparation does not comply.
  • When a discordant result (one positive, one negative) is found for solution A → repeat the test.

In the repeat test:

  • Both A replicates negative → complies
  • One or both A replicates positive → does not comply

If the preparation does not comply at a dilution less than the MVD, the test may be repeated using a greater dilution (not exceeding MVD).

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Method A 的本質：定性（Pass/Fail）試驗

Method A 只回答「產品中的內毒素是否超過限度值？」——以稀釋到恰好等於 λ（= 限度值/MVD 換算後的濃度）時是否出現凝膠作為判定。

簡化版 4-tube 設計：與干擾因素測試相比，Method A 更精簡：

  • A×2：待測品（Pass/Fail 的主角）
  • B×2：待測品中加 2λ 內毒素（確認試驗基質中 LAL 仍有反應）
  • C×2：BET 用水中加 2λ（純試劑對照）
  • D×2：BET 用水（陰性對照）

重點提示 Key Notes

「矛盾結果（Discordant）」的處理：A 管一陽一陰時必須重複試驗。這個規定防止單次試驗誤差影響判定。重複試驗中「只要有一管陽性」就算不符合——重複試驗的標準更嚴格，因為第一次已有疑慮。

為什麼正控（B）用 2λ 而不是 λ？使用 2λ（是靈敏度的 2 倍）確保正對照試驗中出現「確定陽性」結果，即使試劑活性略有波動也不會誤判為陰性。

CDMO 常見陷阱：在批放行記錄中，BET 試驗結果報告「Negative（≤ endotoxin limit）」即代表通過，但 FDA 查廠員常要求提供：原始 LAL 試驗記錄（批號/靈敏度/稀釋計算）、B/C/D 管的驗證記錄。這些不能只保留「最終判定」。

比喻說明 Analogy

Method A 就像懷孕試劑（Pregnancy Test）——只有「陽性/陰性」兩種結果，快速易讀。雖然簡單，但在法規糾紛中最具公信力（仲裁地位），就像法庭上更信任陪審團的一致判決而非複雜的統計分析。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 一批靜脈注射液 BET 試驗：A 管結果「一陽一陰」，B/C 均陽，D 陰。下一步如何處理？重複試驗的合格標準是什麼？
  2. 若 B 管兩管均陰性，試驗是否有效？這代表什麼問題？應如何處理？
  3. Method A 與 Method B 的結果都可以用於放行，但為何 Method A 是仲裁方法？
7.3 Method B — 定量試驗 Quantitative Test (Table 2.6.14-3)

原文內容 Original Text

3. QUANTITATIVE TEST (METHOD B)

(i) Procedure
The test quantifies bacterial endotoxins in the test solution by titration to an end-point. Prepare solutions A, B, C and D as shown in Table 2.6.14.-3.

(ii) Calculation and interpretation

The test is considered valid when the following 3 conditions are met:

  1. both replicates of solution D (negative control) are negative
  2. both replicates of solution B (positive product control) are positive
  3. the geometric mean end-point concentration of solution C is in the range of 0.5λ to 2λ

To determine the endotoxin concentration of solution A, calculate the end-point concentration for each replicate by multiplying each end-point dilution factor by λ.

The endotoxin concentration in the test solution is the end-point concentration of the replicates. If the test is conducted with a diluted test solution, calculate the concentration in the original solution by multiplying the result by the dilution factor.

If none of the dilutions of the test solution is positive → report as less than λ (or less than the lowest dilution factor × λ).

If all dilutions are positive → report as equal to or greater than the largest dilution factor × λ.

The preparation meets the requirements if the endotoxin concentration in both replicates is less than that specified in the monograph.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Method B vs Method A 的根本差異：

  • Method A：只問「超不超過限度？」→ 單一稀釋倍數（≤ MVD）→ 一個 Pass/Fail 結果
  • Method B：問「到底有多少內毒素？」→ 連續稀釋（1→2→4→8）→ 找到「臨界稀釋倍數（End-point）」→ 計算濃度

終點稀釋計算（End-point Titration）原理：

解稀釋系列：1倍、2倍、4倍、8倍。找到「最後還能形成凝膠的稀釋倍數」（End-point）。End-point concentration = End-point dilution factor × λ。

公式與計算 Method B End-point Calculation

計算範例：
  λ = 0.25 IU/mL
  測試溶液先稀釋 10 倍後進行 Method B

  4 管 A（稀釋倍數 1, 2, 4, 8 倍）：
    1倍 → 陽性（有凝膠）
    2倍 → 陽性
    4倍 → 陰性  ← End-point 在第 3 管之後
    8倍 → 陰性

  End-point = 第 4 管（最後陽性）稀釋倍數 = 2
  End-point concentration = 2 × λ = 2 × 0.25 = 0.5 IU/mL

  原溶液中內毒素含量 = 0.5 × 10（稀釋因子）= 5 IU/mL

若「1倍也是陰性」（全部陰性）：
  報告 < λ = < 0.25 IU/mL（在偵測極限之下）

若「8倍仍陽性」（全部陽性）：
  報告 ≥ 8 × λ = ≥ 2 IU/mL（超出稀釋範圍，結果截尾）

重點提示 Key Notes

3 個有效性條件的意義：

  • (a) D 管陰性 → 排除背景汙染
  • (b) B 管陽性 → 確認基質中 LAL 仍能反應（與干擾因素測試 B 管相同概念）
  • (c) C 管幾何平均在 0.5λ–2λ → 試劑靈敏度正常

Method B 的用途：常用於需要「追蹤內毒素來源」（root cause investigation）或「原料藥內毒素含量資料建立」的場景，而非日常批放行（Method A 更簡單快速）。

實務應用 Practical Application

CDMO 通常對成品使用 Method A（Pass/Fail），但對原料藥（API）供應商評估或偏差調查時使用 Method B（定量），以確認：

  • 原料的批間內毒素含量變異範圍
  • 製程中哪個步驟去除了多少內毒素（對超濾/層析步驟的驗證）
  • 偏差調查中，追蹤可疑批次的內毒素來源與含量

本節重點回顧 Section Summary

  • 靈敏度確認（每次新批 LAL）：4 濃度（2λ/λ/0.5λ/0.25λ）× 4 重複，幾何平均需在 0.5λ–2λ，37±1°C，60±2 min，避免振動。
  • 干擾因素測試（ABCD 四溶液框架）：B（待測品+已知內毒素）的幾何平均需在 0.5λ–2λ；干擾排除依序：稀釋→換高靈敏度試劑→特殊處理（需驗證不損失內毒素）。
  • Method A（限度法）：4 管設計（A×2/B×2/C×2/D×2），Pass/Fail 判定；矛盾結果需重複試驗，重複後任一陽性即不符合。
  • Method B（定量法）：連續稀釋找終點，End-point conc. = End-point dilution factor × λ，3 個有效性條件（D陰/B陽/C在0.5λ–2λ）；Method A 仍為仲裁方法。

原文內容 Original Text

8. PHOTOMETRIC QUANTITATIVE TECHNIQUES (METHODS C, D, E AND F)

1. TURBIDIMETRIC TECHNIQUE (METHODS C AND F)

This technique is a photometric test to measure the increase in turbidity. Based on the test principle employed, this technique may be classified as:

  • The end-point-turbidimetric test (Method F): based on the quantitative relationship between the endotoxin concentration and the turbidity (absorbance or transmission) of the reaction mixture at the end of an incubation period.
  • The kinetic-turbidimetric test (Method C): measures either the time (onset time) needed for the reaction mixture to reach a predetermined absorbance or transmission, or the rate of turbidity development.

The test is carried out at the incubation temperature recommended by the lysate manufacturer (usually 37 ± 1 °C).

2. CHROMOGENIC TECHNIQUE (METHODS D AND E)

This technique is used to measure the chromophore released from a suitable chromogenic peptide by the reaction of endotoxins with the lysate.

  • The end-point-chromogenic test (Method E): based on the quantitative relationship between the endotoxin concentration and the quantity of chromophore released at the end of an incubation period.
  • The kinetic-chromogenic test (Method D): measures either the time (onset time) needed for the reaction mixture to reach a predetermined absorbance, or the rate of colour development.

The test is carried out at the incubation temperature recommended by the lysate manufacturer (usually 37 ± 1 °C).

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

6 種方法的分類矩陣：

信號機制比較：

  • 濁度法（C/F）：凝固蛋白原（Coagulogen）凝結 → 溶液變混濁 → 吸光度↑
  • 顯色法（D/E）：LAL 中的凝固酶裂解 pNA 色原（p-Nitroaniline）→ 釋放黃色色素 → 405 nm 吸光度↑

比喻說明 Analogy

End-point vs Kinetic：就像測量跑者速度的兩種方式：

  • End-point（終點法）：等 60 分鐘後看跑了多遠（測距離）
  • Kinetic（動態法）：計算跑到 50 公尺用了多少秒（測時間），或計算速度

Kinetic 法的優勢：更早獲得結果（不需等到培養結束），且對低濃度樣品更靈敏（因為反應時間的差異比終點濃度更容易偵測）。

重點提示 Key Notes

為什麼光度法越來越常用？

  • 全自動化（Automated microplate reader）→ 高通量、資料完整性更佳
  • 量化能力強 → 可監測內毒素趨勢，不只是 Pass/Fail
  • Methods C/D 的動態法：標準曲線範圍寬（可涵蓋 2–3 個 log₁₀），適合濃度變異大的樣品
  • Method E/F 的終點法：操作較簡單，但標準曲線範圍窄（通常 1 個 log₁₀）
8.3 光度法前置測試 Preparatory Testing for Photometric Techniques (Table 2.6.14-4)

原文內容 Original Text

3. PREPARATORY TESTING

Preparatory tests are conducted to show that the criteria for the standard curve are satisfied and that the test solution does not interfere with the test. Validation of the test method is required when any changes are made to the experimental conditions.

(i) Assurance of criteria for the standard curve

Using the standard endotoxin solution, prepare at least 3 endotoxin concentrations within the range indicated by the lysate manufacturer to generate the standard curve. Perform the test using at least 3 replicates of each standard endotoxin solution.

If the desired range is greater than 2 log₁₀ in the kinetic methods, additional standards must be included to bracket each log₁₀ increase in the range.

The absolute value of the correlation coefficient |r| must be greater than or equal to 0.980, for the range of endotoxin concentrations set up.

(ii) Test for interfering factors

Select an endotoxin concentration at or near the middle of the standard curve.

Prepare solutions A, B, C and D as shown in Table 2.6.14.-4.

The test is considered valid when:

  • |r| of standard curve from solution C ≥ 0.980
  • Solution D ≤ blank value limit or < endotoxin detection limit

Calculate the mean recovery of the added endotoxin by subtracting the mean concentration in solution A from that in solution B.

The test solution is considered free of interfering factors if the measured concentration of endotoxin added to the test solution is within 50–200 per cent of the known added endotoxin concentration (after subtraction of background from solution A).

When the endotoxin recovery is out of the specified range, the test solution is considered to contain interfering factors. Repeat the test using a greater dilution (not exceeding MVD), or use validated treatment (filtration, neutralisation, dialysis or heat treatment).

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

|r| ≥ 0.980 的標準曲線要求：

|r| 是 Pearson 相關係數的絕對值，反映內毒素濃度與光度信號之間的線性相關程度。

  • |r| = 1.000 → 完美線性
  • |r| ≥ 0.980 → 允許的最低線性度（對應 R² ≥ 0.960）
  • |r| < 0.980 → 試驗無效，需重新建立標準曲線

光度法與凝膠法干擾測試的對應關係：

本質上完全相同，只是表達方式不同。凝膠法用「幾何平均靈敏度範圍」，光度法用「回收率百分比」。

重點提示 Key Notes

動態法（C/D）的「每 log₁₀ 需加點」要求：若標準曲線範圍超過 2 個 log₁₀（如 0.005–5 IU/mL = 3 個 log₁₀），則每增加一個 log₁₀ 都要增加標準點密度。這確保在寬範圍曲線中，各濃度區間的線性度都被驗證，不允許用少數點外推。

「Middle of standard curve」的干擾加標設計：在曲線中間點加標，比在最高或最低點更能反映真實的干擾情況，避免邊緣效應影響判讀。

實務應用 Practical Application

CDMO 在使用全自動 BET 分析儀（如 Charles River Endosafe-nexgen-MCS）時，設備內建標準曲線管理和 |r| 計算，系統自動判定有效性。但這不免除 QC 人員理解原理的責任——FDA 稽核時常問「為什麼|r|值是這個？如何確保標準曲線仍有效？」

8.4 光度法試驗程序與結果判讀 Test Procedure & Interpretation

原文內容 Original Text

4. TEST

(i) Procedure
Follow the procedure described in 3. Preparatory testing, (ii) Test for interfering factors.

(ii) Calculation
Calculate the endotoxin concentration of each replicate of solution A using the standard curve generated by the positive control solution C.

The test is considered valid when the following 3 requirements are met:

  1. the results obtained with solution C comply with the requirements for validation defined under 3. Preparatory testing, (i) — Assurance of criteria for the standard curve (|r| ≥ 0.980);
  2. the endotoxin recovery, calculated from the endotoxin concentration found in solution B after subtracting the endotoxin concentration found in solution A, is within the range of 50–200 per cent;
  3. the result obtained with solution D (negative control) does not exceed the limit of the blank value required, or it is less than the endotoxin detection limit of the lysate reagent employed.

(iii) Interpretation
The preparation being examined complies with the test if the mean endotoxin concentration of the replicates of solution A, after correction for dilution and concentration, is less than the endotoxin limit for the product.

Guidelines on the test for bacterial endotoxins are given in general chapter 5.1.10.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

光度法試驗 3 個有效性條件：

  1. C（標準曲線）|r| ≥ 0.980：確認本次試驗的標準曲線有效——這是「量尺本身是否準確」
  2. B（正對照）50–200% recovery：確認待測基質不干擾 LAL 反應——這是「量尺用在這個樣品上是否還準確」
  3. D（陰性對照）≤ blank limit：確認背景沒有被汙染——這是「試驗環境是否乾淨」

計算結果修正（Correction for dilution and concentration）：光度法直接讀出稀釋後的內毒素濃度，需要乘以稀釋因子（DF）還原到原始樣品濃度，再與藥典限度比較。

公式與計算 Final Result Calculation

光度法最終結果計算流程：

1. 從標準曲線讀出 Solution A 的內毒素濃度
   (兩個重複各算一次，取平均)
   例：Solution A 讀數 = 0.12 IU/mL（稀釋 10 倍後）

2. 修正稀釋因子
   原始樣品濃度 = 0.12 × 10 = 1.2 IU/mL

3. 若限度以 IU/mg 表示，換算
   樣品濃度 = 20 mg/mL
   報告值 = 1.2 IU/mL ÷ 20 mg/mL = 0.06 IU/mg

4. 比較限度
   限度 = 0.5 IU/mg
   0.06 IU/mg < 0.5 IU/mg → PASS ✓

Recovery 計算（有效性確認）：
  Solution A 讀數：0.12 IU/mL
  Solution B 讀數：0.38 IU/mL（已加 0.25 IU/mL 中間點）
  Recovery = (0.38 - 0.12) / 0.25 × 100% = 104% ✓
  104% 在 50–200% 範圍內 → 無干擾

重點提示 Key Notes

光度法與凝膠法的互補關係（在 CDMO 中的選擇依據）：

  • 批放行（日常）：兩種均可。自動化程度要求高的選光度法（Methods C/D/E/F）；資源有限或低批量選凝膠法（Method A）。
  • 法規糾紛：Method A 為仲裁方法，光度法結果有爭議時需用 Method A 確認。
  • 生物製劑（蛋白質/抗體）：光度法通常需要更精細的干擾測試；部分品種 rFC 法（Ph.Eur. 2.6.32）更適合。
  • 水系統監測（WFI/Purified Water）：光度法（自動化）適合高頻監測。

實務應用 Practical Application

CDMO 在客戶品質協議（Quality Agreement）中，關於 BET 方法的常見條款：

  • 「凡有爭議，依 Method A 判定」——引用 Ph.Eur. 2.6.14 仲裁條款
  • 「方法適用性測試記錄由委托方保留，每次變更試驗條件須重新執行」
  • 「光度法試驗記錄須包含：標準曲線 |r| 值、B 溶液回收率、D 空白值」

這三項在 FDA 483 或 Warning Letter 中多次被引用為 BET 記錄不完整的觀察點。

本節重點回顧 Section Summary

  • 光度法分為濁度法（C/F）和顯色法（D/E）；動態法（C/D）測「onset time」，終點法（E/F）測「培養後信號值」；動態法範圍更寬但需更多標準點。
