Test C — To Determine Whether Containers Have Been Surface-Treated (Etching Test)

If there are uncertainties whether a container has been surface-treated, and/or to distinguish between type I and type II glass containers, test C is used in addition to test A. Alternatively, tests A and B may be used. Test C may be carried out either on unused samples or on samples previously used in test A.

Vials and Bottles

Rinse the containers twice with water R, fill to the brimful point with a mixture of 1 volume of hydrofluoric acid R and 9 volumes of hydrochloric acid R and allow to stand for 10 min. Empty the containers and rinse carefully 5 times with water R. Immediately before the test, rinse once again with water R. Submit the containers thus prepared to the same autoclaving and determination procedure as described in Test A.

If the results are considerably higher than those obtained from the original surfaces (by about a factor of 5 to 10), the samples have been surface-treated.

Ampoules, Cartridges and Syringes

NOTE: ampoules, cartridges and syringes made from glass tubing are not normally subjected to internal surface treatment because their high chemical resistance is dependent upon the chemical composition of the glass as a material. Apply the test method as described above. If the ampoules are not surface-treated, the new values are slightly lower than those obtained in previous tests.

Distinction between Type I and Type II — Table 3.2.1.-4

Type IType II
Values from Test C are closely similar to those found in Test A for type I containers. Values from Test C greatly exceed those found in Test A and are similar to but not larger than those for type III containers.

Test C — 蝕刻試驗（鑑別表面處理）

Test C的設計原理

Type II玻璃的高耐水解性來自「內表面處理層」（通常是硫磺化處理，形成薄薄的SiO₂富集層）。Test C先用HF/HCl混酸（1:9比例）蝕刻10分鐘，破壞這個表面處理層，然後重新做Test A。如果蝕刻後Test A結果大幅升高（5–10倍）→ 說明原本的高耐水解性依賴表面處理 → Type II。如果蝕刻後Test A結果變化不大 → 說明耐水解性來自玻璃本身組成 → Type I。

Table 3.2.1-4判讀邏輯
情況Test A結果Test C結果（蝕刻後）判定
Type I容器低（符合Type I/II限度）與Test A相近（微幅變化）→ Type I
Type II容器低（符合Type I/II限度）大幅升高（5–10倍），接近Type III限度→ Type II
安瓿/注射器管材的特別說明

安瓿、注射器和卡匣通常由玻璃管（glass tubing）製成，通常不進行內表面處理——因為其高化學耐性來自玻璃管本身的組成（Type I材質）。因此Test C蝕刻後，結果應略低於原Test A（因部分表面被蝕除，接觸面積微減）。若出現大幅升高，則是異常情況需進一步調查。

Test C的實際應用
  • 供應商審核：收到新批次玻璃瓶時，若供應商CoA顯示Type II但有疑問，可運行Test C確認是否真的有表面處理。
  • 表面處理失效調查：若穩定性測試中發現異常脫層或pH偏移，可用Test C評估表面處理層是否完整。
  • 試驗樣品再利用：允許使用做過Test A的容器做Test C，節省樣品量，特別適合小容量稀有容器。

Arsenic Test

The test applies to glass containers for aqueous parenteral preparations.

Method: Hydride generation atomic absorption spectrometry (2.2.23, Method I).

Test solution: Use the extraction solution obtained from containers of types I and II, after autoclaving at 121 °C for 1 h as described under Test A. Transfer 10.0 mL to a 100 mL volumetric flask. Add 10 mL of hydrochloric acid R and 5 mL of a 200 g/L solution of potassium iodide R. Heat on a water-bath at 80 °C for 20 min, allow to cool and dilute to 100.0 mL with water R.

Reference solutions: arsenic standard solution (1 ppm As) R. Apply same acid/KI treatment. Concentration range: typically 0.005–0.015 ppm of As.

Wavelength: 193.7 nm. Atomisation device: air-acetylene flame. Source: hollow-cathode lamp.

Limit: maximum 0.1 ppm of As.

Educational Commentary — Traditional Chinese

砷（Arsenic）試驗

為何需要砷試驗？

鈉鈣矽酸鹽玻璃在高溫熔製過程中，可能引入微量砷作為澄清劑（fining agent）——砷氧化物（As₂O₃）在高溫下可加速玻璃氣泡的消除。雖然現代製造已大量減少此用途，但仍有潛在風險。注射劑直接進入血液循環，砷的毒性（類金屬，致癌物）需要嚴格控制。

砷試驗參數彙整

適用對象：含水注射用玻璃容器（Type I 和 II）
萃取液來源：Test A已高壓滅菌萃取液（121°C × 1 h）
分析方法：氫化物生成原子吸收光譜法（HG-AAS）
波長：193.7 nm
參考液範圍：0.005–0.015 ppm As
限度：≤ 0.1 ppm As
氫化物生成AAS原理

標準AAS對砷的靈敏度有限。「氫化物生成法（Hydride Generation）」先用KI將As(V)還原為As(III)，再以硼氫化鈉（NaBH₄）反應生成砷化氫氣體（AsH₃），導入AAS石英管中原子化偵測。此法可將偵測靈敏度提升1000倍以上，達到ppb（µg/L）級別，因此能有效偵測微量砷釋出。

萃取液複用的設計

砷試驗的試驗液直接來自Test A的萃取液——同一次高壓滅菌萃取，既可做鹼性離子滴定（耐水解性），又可做砷分析（取10.0 mL），有效節省時間和容器用量，也確保兩個試驗的萃取條件一致性。

Spectral Transmission for Coloured Glass Containers

Equipment: A UV-Vis spectrophotometer equipped with a photodiode detector or photomultiplier tube coupled with an integrating sphere.

Preparation of Specimen

Break the glass container or cut it with a circular saw fitted with a wet abrasive wheel. Select sections representative of the wall thickness. If the specimen is too small, mask the uncovered portion with opaque paper or tape. Wash, dry and wipe with a lens tissue. Mount with wax, avoiding fingerprints.

Method

Place the specimen with its cylindrical axis parallel to the slit and the light beam perpendicular to the surface (minimise reflection losses). Measure the transmission of the specimen with reference to air in the spectral region of 290–450 nm, continuously or at intervals of 20 nm.

Limits

For preparations not for parenteral administration: spectral transmission does not exceed 10 per cent at any wavelength in 290–450 nm, irrespective of type and capacity.

For preparations for parenteral administration: see Table 3.2.1.-5.

有色玻璃容器光譜透射率

光譜透射率試驗的目的

有色玻璃（通常為棕色/琥珀色）用於保護光敏感藥品（如某些維生素、多肽類藥物）。光譜透射率試驗確認玻璃在290–450 nm（UV-近可見光範圍）的光阻擋效果——傳輸率越低，防光性越好。290 nm是藥品光降解的典型起始波長，450 nm是近可見光邊界。

Table 3.2.1-5限度設計邏輯
  • 火焰密封容器（Flame-sealed）的限度寬鬆於有密封件容器（With closures）：密封件（stopper/cap）本身可能允許微量光線透過，因此容器玻璃本身需要更嚴格的防光性補償。
  • 容量越大，限度越嚴（傳輸率越低）：大容量容器（如50 mL）壁厚較薄，玻璃層厚度不足以在每平方公分上提供足夠的光阻擋，因此需要更深的著色才能達標。小容量容器因相對壁厚較厚，允許略高的透射率。
樣品製備的關鍵點

測試玻璃片需「代表壁厚」——不同厚度的截面光透射率不同。使用積分球（integrating sphere）是為了收集被玻璃散射的光，確保量測的是真正的透射率（而非僅直射光）。手指指紋（油脂）會在UV波段吸收光，所以用鏡頭紙擦拭後再測量。

CDMO注意事項
  • 玻璃瓶採購時應要求供應商提供批次光譜透射率CoA，對應至相應的Table 3.2.1-5限度。
  • 若產品標籤（label）覆蓋玻璃表面積過大，可能影響目視檢查——需在設計上確保留出足夠的無標籤區域進行可見異物目視（參考Ph.Eur. 2.9.20要求）。
  • 出口EU市場的注射劑需要玻璃供應商提供符合Ph.Eur. 3.2.1的測試報告，包含Type分類確認和光譜透射率數據。

Annex — Test for Surface Hydrolytic Resistance by Flame Spectrometry

The surface hydrolytic resistance of glass of types I and II may be determined by analysis of the leaching solution by flame spectrometry. A number of elements that, when present as oxides in glass, contribute to the alkalinity of the solution, are determined and used to express an alkali equivalent.

The spectrometric method has the advantage of allowing the use of a much smaller sample of extract so that it can be applied to small individual containers. This enables an evaluation of the uniformity of the containers in a given batch where this is critical.

The results of this measurement are not equivalent to those of titrimetry and the 2 methods cannot be considered interchangeable. The titrimetric method is the reference method of the Pharmacopoeia; the spectrometric method may be used in justified and authorised cases.

Elements determined: sodium oxide (Na₂O), potassium oxide (K₂O), calcium oxide (CaO). Spectrochemical buffer: caesium chloride solution (80 g/L CsCl in 0.01 M HCl). Concentrations reported as alkali equivalent in µg/mL.

Cs緩衝液（Spectrochemical buffer）的作用

火焰光譜法中，鉀（K）和鈣（Ca）的測量容易受其他元素（如Na）的「電離干擾（ionization interference）」影響。加入大量Cs⁺（鉮離子）可飽和火焰中的電離空間，使K和Ca保持原子態（不被電離）而能被準確量測。這是火焰AAS/AES中「釋放劑（releasing agent）」的標準作法。
