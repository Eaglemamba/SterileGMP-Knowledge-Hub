本章學習目標

  • 理解無菌試驗（Sterility Test）的適用範圍及其結果的正確解讀方式
  • 掌握流體硫乙醇酸鹽培養基（FTM）與大豆酪蛋白消化物培養基（SCDM）的組成、製備及儲存條件
  • 了解培養基的適用性驗證：無菌性測試及生長促進測試（Growth Promotion Test）
  • 能夠識別 Table 2.6.1-1 中六種標準試驗微生物的菌種編號及其在 FTM/SCDM 的分配邏輯
2.6.1 概述 Overview

原文內容 Original Text

The test is applied to substances, preparations or articles which, according to the Pharmacopoeia, are required to be sterile. However, a satisfactory result only indicates that no contaminating micro-organism has been found in the sample examined in the conditions of the test.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

無菌試驗（Sterility Test）：依藥典規定，凡需符合「無菌」要求的製品（注射劑、眼用製劑、外科縫線等）均須通過本試驗。

重要限制：試驗結果為「通過」僅代表在本次抽樣條件下未發現微生物汙染，並不等於整批產品絕對無菌。這是破壞性末端測試的統計本質。

重點提示 Key Notes

藥典明確聲明：satisfactory result 只代表「在該檢測條件下未發現汙染」，這一句話是法規理解的關鍵。它強調無菌保證（Sterility Assurance）來自整個製程驗證，而非單靠末端無菌試驗。

這也是為何 ICH Q9 / PIC/S Annex 1 強調「滅菌確效 + 無菌製程確效」比成品無菌試驗更重要。

比喻說明 Analogy

就像餐廳從一鍋湯中取出一匙品嚐（Sterility Test），若這匙湯沒問題不代表整鍋沒問題。真正的品質保證來自廚房衛生管理（製程控制），而非只靠試喝。

防止微生物汙染的預防措施 Precautions Against Microbial Contamination

原文內容 Original Text

The test for sterility is carried out under aseptic conditions. In order to achieve such conditions, the test environment has to be adapted to the way in which the sterility test is performed. The precautions taken to avoid contamination are such that they do not affect any micro-organisms which are to be revealed in the test. The working conditions in which the tests are performed are monitored regularly by appropriate sampling of the working area and by carrying out appropriate controls.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

無菌條件（Aseptic Conditions）：無菌試驗須在無菌環境下操作，通常在 Grade A（ISO Class 5）隔離器（Isolator）或 RABS 內進行，避免假陽性（False Positive）。

關鍵矛盾：預防汙染的措施不得影響試驗本身對微生物的偵測能力——這是「預防不汙染」與「允許目標菌生長」兩個目標的平衡。

重點提示 Key Notes

「定期監控工作區域」意味著環境監測（Environmental Monitoring, EM）是無菌試驗的配套要求。若 EM 記錄出現異常，可成為判定試驗無效（Invalid Test）的依據（詳見本章 Interpretation 段落）。

實務應用 Practical Application

CDMO 在執行客戶產品的無菌試驗時，QC 實驗室需要文件證明：隔離器 / RABS 的 HEPA 完整性測試、EM 趨勢資料、操作人員的無菌技術訓練記錄。這些是稽核中最常被查核的配套文件。

培養基與培養溫度 Culture Media and Incubation Temperatures

原文內容 Original Text

Media for the test may be prepared as described below, or equivalent commercial media may be used provided that they comply with the growth promotion test.

The following culture media have been found to be suitable for the test for sterility. Fluid thioglycollate medium is primarily intended for the culture of anaerobic bacteria; however, it will also detect aerobic bacteria. Soya-bean casein digest medium is suitable for the culture of both fungi and aerobic bacteria.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

兩種培養基的分工：

  • FTM（Fluid Thioglycollate Medium，流體硫乙醇酸鹽培養基）：主要偵測厭氧菌（Anaerobes），但亦可偵測需氧菌。培養溫度 30–35°C。
  • SCDM（Soya-bean Casein Digest Medium，大豆酪蛋白消化物培養基）：偵測需氧菌與真菌（Fungi）。培養溫度 20–25°C。

商業培養基可接受，前提是通過生長促進測試。

比喻說明 Analogy

FTM 像一個「缺氧房間」——只有厭氧菌才能在裡面茁壯生長，但需氧菌也不會完全死亡；SCDM 像「正常辦公室環境」——需氧菌和真菌都能輕鬆繁殖。兩個房間各司其職，涵蓋所有可能的汙染源。

FTM 配方 Fluid Thioglycollate Medium — Composition

原文內容 Original Text

Fluid thioglycollate medium

pH after sterilisation: 7.1 ± 0.2

Mix the L-cystine, agar, sodium chloride, glucose, water-soluble yeast extract and pancreatic digest of casein with the water R and heat until solution is effected. Dissolve the sodium thioglycollate or thioglycollic acid in the solution and, if necessary, add 1 M sodium hydroxide so that, after sterilisation, the solution will have a pH of 7.1 ± 0.2.

Sterilise using a validated process. Store at a temperature between 2 °C and 25 °C in a sterile, airtight container.

If more than the upper one-third of the medium has acquired a pink colour, the medium may be restored once by heating the containers in a water-bath or in free-flowing steam until the pink colour disappears and cooling quickly.

Incubation temperature: 30–35 °C

For products containing a mercurial preservative that cannot be tested by the membrane-filtration method, fluid thioglycollate medium incubated at 20–25 °C may be used instead of soya-bean casein digest medium provided that it has been validated.

An alternative thioglycollate medium (without agar and resazurin) may be used where prescribed or justified and authorised. Incubate at 30–35 °C under anaerobic conditions.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

關鍵成分解析：

  • Sodium thioglycollate（硫乙醇酸鈉）：還原劑，消耗溶解氧，創造厭氧環境，使厭氧菌能生長。
  • Agar（瓊脂）：微量添加（0.75 g/L），增加培養基黏度以限制氧氣擴散，不形成固體培養基。
  • Resazurin（刃天青）：氧化還原指示劑。無氧時為無色/淡粉紅；有氧時轉為粉紅色。粉紅超過上 1/3 代表培養基已過度氧化。
  • L-Cystine：含硫胺基酸，協助維持還原環境。

重點提示 Key Notes

Resazurin 顏色的實務意義：若 FTM 上層 1/3 以上變粉紅，可以加熱恢復一次（Restore Once）。這個「只能恢復一次」的規定非常重要——在 FDA 查廠中，多次恢復同一批培養基會被記為 observation。

含汞防腐劑產品：含 Thiomersal（硫柳汞）的產品因無法用膜過濾法，改用 FTM 在 20–25°C 培養來代替 SCDM，必須做方法確效。

比喻說明 Analogy

Resazurin 就像培養基的「缺氧燈」——燈滅（無色）代表還原環境良好；燈亮（粉紅）代表氧氣已入侵，培養基可能失效。就像冰箱的溫度指示燈，顏色告訴你品質還在不在線。

公式與計算 pH Control

目標 pH（滅菌後）：7.1 ± 0.2
調節方式：加入 1 M NaOH 調整滅菌前 pH，
         因滅菌過程中 pH 會略有變化，
         需預先補償。

實驗室注意點：
- 每批次培養基需記錄滅菌後 pH
- pH 超出範圍 → 該批培養基不合格，
  不可用於產品無菌試驗
SCDM 配方 Soya-bean Casein Digest Medium — Composition

原文內容 Original Text

Soya-bean casein digest medium

pH after sterilisation: 7.3 ± 0.2

Dissolve the solids in water R, warming slightly to effect solution. Cool the solution to room temperature. Add 1 M sodium hydroxide, if necessary, so that after sterilisation the solution will have a pH of 7.3 ± 0.2. Filter, if necessary, to clarify, distribute into suitable vessels and sterilise using a validated process.

Store at a temperature between 2 °C and 25 °C in a sterile well-closed container, unless it is intended for immediate use.

Incubation temperature: 20–25 °C

The media used comply with the following tests, carried out before or in parallel with the test on the product to be examined.

Sterility. Incubate portions of the media for 14 days. No growth of micro-organisms occurs.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

FTM vs SCDM 比較：

  • SCDM 不含還原劑，pH 略高（7.3 vs 7.1），用於需氧環境。
  • SCDM 較 FTM 含更多氮源（Pancreatic digest of casein 17 g），更利於真菌生長。
  • Dipotassium hydrogen phosphate 提供緩衝能力，穩定培養基 pH。
  • 培養溫度較低（20–25°C）有利於真菌（Fungi）繁殖。

重點提示 Key Notes

培養基的無菌性確認（Media Sterility）：每批培養基在使用前，需取一部分在 14 天培養觀察，確認無微生物生長。這是培養基品管的最低要求，任何商業培養基也不例外。

培養基無菌性測試失敗 → 整批報廢，相關產品測試結果亦需重新評估。

實務應用 Practical Application

CDMO QC 實驗室通常直接購買商業化培養基（如 Sigma-Aldrich、BD 等廠牌），自製培養基耗時且風險高。但購買後仍須執行生長促進測試（Growth Promotion Test），並留存 COA 與批次記錄，供稽核查閱。

生長促進測試 Growth Promotion Test of Aerobes, Anaerobes and Fungi

原文內容 Original Text

Growth promotion test of aerobes, anaerobes and fungi. Test each batch of ready-prepared medium and each batch of medium prepared either from dehydrated medium or from ingredients. Suitable strains of micro-organisms are indicated in Table 2.6.1.-1.

Inoculate portions of fluid thioglycollate medium with a small number (not more than 100 CFU) of the following micro-organisms, using a separate portion of medium for each species: Clostridium sporogenes, Pseudomonas aeruginosa, Staphylococcus aureus.

Inoculate portions of soya-bean casein digest medium with a small number (not more than 100 CFU) of the following micro-organisms: Aspergillus brasiliensis, Bacillus subtilis, Candida albicans. Incubate for not more than 3 days in the case of bacteria and not more than 5 days in the case of fungi.

Table 2.6.1.-1. Strains of the test micro-organisms suitable for use in the growth promotion test and the method suitability test.

Seed lot culture maintenance techniques (seed-lot systems) are used so that the viable micro-organisms used for inoculation are not more than 5 passages removed from the original master seed-lot.

The media are suitable if a clearly visible growth of the micro-organisms occurs.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

六種標準菌株的分配邏輯：

  • FTM（30–35°C）：
    • Clostridium sporogenes（厭氧菌代表）
    • Pseudomonas aeruginosa（革蘭氏陰性需氧菌）
    • Staphylococcus aureus（革蘭氏陽性需氧菌）
  • SCDM（20–25°C）：
    • Aspergillus brasiliensis（黴菌代表）
    • Bacillus subtilis（芽孢桿菌，孢子形成菌）
    • Candida albicans（酵母菌代表）

100 CFU 上限：接種量「不多於 100 CFU」確保是低接種量條件，模擬真實汙染情境，避免過量接種掩蓋培養基不良表現。

重點提示 Key Notes

傳代次數限制（≤5 passages）：菌株連續傳代可能導致基因型或表現型漂移，影響其作為陽性對照的有效性。Seed-lot 系統（主種子批/工作種子批）是確保菌株穩定性的標準做法，亦是 USP 與 Ph.Eur. 共同要求。

判定標準：「清晰可見的生長」（clearly visible growth）才算通過，模糊或微弱生長不予接受。

比喻說明 Analogy

生長促進測試就像餐廳每天開門前測試廚房爐具——不是真的煮客人的菜，而是先點火確認爐具正常運作。這些標準菌株是「校正用的標準菜」，確認培養基這個「爐具」真的能讓微生物生長。

公式與計算 Timing Reference

生長促進測試培養時間：
  - 細菌（Bacteria）：≤ 3 天
  - 真菌（Fungi）：≤ 5 天

對照組（Positive control）：
  - 同一試驗同時執行
  - 不含待測產品的同批培養基

判定：
  ✓ Clearly visible growth → 培養基合格
  ✗ No visible growth → 培養基不合格，停用

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 為什麼 Clostridium sporogenes 被分配給 FTM 而不是 SCDM？它的生物學特性為何讓它成為厭氧菌的代表？
  2. 若一批商業培養基的 Growth Promotion Test 顯示 Aspergillus brasiliensis 生長「不明顯」，您的下一步行動是什麼？
  3. 為什麼傳代次數限制在 5 次以內？超過 5 次可能發生什麼變化？

本節重點回顧 Section Summary

  • 無菌試驗結果陽性只代表「未發現汙染」，不代表批次絕對無菌；真正的無菌保證來自製程驗證。
  • FTM（厭氧為主，30–35°C）與 SCDM（需氧菌+真菌，20–25°C）各有分工，六種標準菌株依培養基特性分配。
  • 每批培養基使用前須通過：(1) 無菌性測試（14天無生長）；(2) 生長促進測試（各標準菌株清晰可見生長，細菌≤3天/真菌≤5天）。
  • 標準菌株傳代不得超過 5 次（seed-lot system），以維持菌株特性穩定。

方法適用性測試 Method Suitability Test

原文內容 Original Text

Carry out a test as described below under Test for sterility of the product to be examined using exactly the same methods except for the following modifications.

Membrane filtration. After transferring the contents of the container or containers to be tested to the membrane add an inoculum of a small number of viable micro-organisms (not more than 100 CFU) to the final portion of sterile diluent used to rinse the filter.

Direct inoculation. After transferring the content of the container or containers to be tested to the culture medium add an inoculum of a small number of viable micro-organisms (not more than 100 CFU) to the medium.

In both cases use the same micro-organisms as those described above under Growth promotion test of aerobes, anaerobes and fungi. Perform a growth promotion test as a positive control. Incubate all the containers containing medium for not more than 5 days.

If clearly visible growth of micro-organisms is obtained after the incubation, visually comparable to that in the control vessel without product, either the product possesses no antimicrobial activity under the conditions of the test or such activity has been satisfactorily eliminated. The test for sterility may then be carried out without further modification.

If clearly visible growth is not obtained in the presence of the product to be tested, the product possesses antimicrobial activity that has not been satisfactorily eliminated. Modify the conditions in order to eliminate the antimicrobial activity and repeat the method suitability test.

This method suitability test is performed:

  1. when the test for sterility has to be carried out on a new product;
  2. whenever there is a change in the experimental conditions of the test.

The method suitability test may be performed simultaneously with the test for sterility of the product to be examined.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

方法適用性測試（Method Suitability Test）的目的：確認待測產品本身不會抑制微生物生長，避免因「假陰性（False Negative）」而誤判通過無菌試驗。

核心邏輯：在正常無菌試驗條件下，人為加入已知菌量（≤100 CFU）：

  • 若標準菌正常生長 → 產品無抑菌活性，方法有效
  • 若標準菌無法生長 → 產品有抑菌活性，需調整（如加中和劑、稀釋、或增加沖洗次數）

重點提示 Key Notes

觸發時機（務必記熟）：

  • (a) 新產品第一次做無菌試驗時
  • (b) 試驗條件發生任何變更時（換培養基批次不算「條件變更」，但換膜型號算）

注意：方法適用性測試可與正式無菌試驗同時進行——這在生產時程緊迫時很重要，不必等方法確認後才能做正式試驗。

實務應用 Practical Application

含抗生素的注射劑（如 Vancomycin）或含防腐劑（如 Benzalkonium chloride）的眼藥水，通常需要特殊處理：

  • 膜過濾法：增加沖洗次數（但不超過 5 × 100 mL）或加入中和劑（如 Polysorbate 80）
  • 直接接種法：加入適量中和劑（如 Lecithin + Polysorbate）

CDMO 在接受新客戶產品時，方法適用性測試記錄是品質協議（Quality Agreement）中必備的交付文件。

比喻說明 Analogy

方法適用性測試就像在測試麥克風之前，先對著麥克風說話確認聲音有傳出來——如果你加了產品之後標準菌消失，等於麥克風失靈了，怎麼能信任後續的試驗結果？

膜過濾法 Membrane Filtration

原文內容 Original Text

The test may be carried out using the technique of membrane filtration or by direct inoculation of the culture media with the product to be examined. The technique of membrane filtration is used whenever the nature of the product permits, that is, for filterable aqueous preparations, for alcoholic or oily preparations and for preparations miscible with or soluble in aqueous or oily solvents provided these solvents do not have an antimicrobial effect in the conditions of the test.

Use membrane filters having a nominal pore size not greater than 0.45 μm whose effectiveness to retain micro-organisms has been established. Cellulose nitrate filters, for example, are used for aqueous, oily and weakly alcoholic solutions and cellulose acetate filters, for example, for strongly alcoholic solutions.

The filtration apparatus and membrane are sterilised by appropriate means. The apparatus is designed so that the solution to be examined can be introduced and filtered under aseptic conditions.

Aqueous solutions. If appropriate, transfer a small quantity of a suitable, sterile diluent such as a 1 g/L neutral solution of meat or casein peptone pH 7.1 ± 0.2 onto the membrane and filter. Transfer the contents of the container(s) to the membrane(s). Filter immediately. If the product has antimicrobial properties, wash the membrane not less than 3 times by filtering through it each time the volume of the chosen sterile diluent. Do not exceed a washing cycle of 5 times 100 mL per filter.

Transfer the whole membrane to the culture medium or cut it aseptically into 2 equal parts and transfer one half to each of 2 suitable media. Incubate the media for not less than 14 days.

Soluble solids. Use for each medium not less than the quantity prescribed in Table 2.6.1.-2. Dissolve in a suitable solvent and proceed as for aqueous solutions.

Oils and oily solutions. Oils and oily solutions of sufficiently low viscosity may be filtered without dilution through a dry membrane. Viscous oils may be diluted with isopropyl myristate (shown not to have antimicrobial activity). Wash the membrane at least 3 times by filtering about 100 mL of a suitable sterile solution containing a suitable emulsifying agent, for example polysorbate 80 at a concentration of 10 g/L. Incubate at the same temperatures and times.

Ointments and creams. Dilute to 1 per cent in isopropyl myristate, heating if necessary to not more than 40 °C (in exceptional cases, not more than 44 °C). Filter as rapidly as possible and proceed as described for oils.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

膜過濾法的優先性：凡產品性質允許（可過濾），應優先選用膜過濾法，因為它能將大體積樣品濃縮於膜上，偵測靈敏度更高，也可透過沖洗去除產品中的抑菌成分。

0.45 μm 濾膜：標準孔徑，已被驗證能截留細菌。對於黴菌孢子（Fungal spores）一般也有效，但需要特別確認。

膜材質選擇：

  • Cellulose nitrate（硝化纖維素）→ 水性、油性、弱酒精性
  • Cellulose acetate（醋酸纖維素）→ 強酒精性（如含 70% 以上乙醇）

重點提示 Key Notes

最多沖洗 5 × 100 mL 規定的意涵：即使方法適用性測試顯示這樣的沖洗量無法完全消除抑菌活性，也不允許繼續增加沖洗量——必須改用其他方法（如加中和劑、換直接接種法）。此限制防止因過度稀釋而偽造「無菌」結果。

培養 14 天（不得少於）：注射劑、眼用製劑等藥典規定品種須培養至少 14 天。這是為了確保生長緩慢的微生物（如某些厭氧菌）也有足夠時間被偵測。

油性製劑特殊注意：Isopropyl myristate 本身無抑菌活性（需驗證），Polysorbate 80（10 g/L）用於乳化，使油相均勻分散過膜。

對 CDMO 注射劑生產來說，QC 實驗室通常備有膜過濾無菌測試儀（Closed Membrane Filtration System），如 Millipore Steritest 或 Sartorius Microsart。這類密閉系統的優點：

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

直接接種法的適用情境：當產品無法過濾時（如高黏度半固體、含不可濾顆粒的懸浮劑），必須用直接接種法。

10% 體積限制的邏輯：若加入太多產品，培養基本身的「緩衝能力」（培養基體積稀釋抑菌物）就無法發揮作用。10% 限制確保培養基主體不被產品過度改變。

濃縮培養基（Concentrated Medium）：當需要加入大量產品（如整管大容量注射劑），可使用 2× 或 5× 濃縮培養基，使最終混合液達到正常濃度。

重點提示 Key Notes

厭氧菌 FTM 的特殊處理：含油性產品每天需輕搖（避免油分層影響菌的接觸），但 FTM 用於偵測厭氧菌時「盡量不搖晃」——因為搖晃會引入氧氣，破壞厭氧環境，導致 Clostridium 無法生長（假陰性）。

這一矛盾需要特殊判斷：如果產品是油性的，且同時需要 FTM 偵測厭氧菌，SOP 應明確規定搖晃方式。

比喻說明 Analogy

直接接種法像是直接把「嫌疑人」（產品）帶進「偵訊室」（培養基），10% 的限制確保「偵訊室」的條件不會被嫌疑人本身的特性（抑菌活性）所破壞。若嫌疑人太強大，需先「解除武裝」（中和劑）再進入。

結果觀察與判讀 Observation and Interpretation of Results

原文內容 Original Text

At intervals during the incubation period and at its conclusion, examine the media for macroscopic evidence of microbial growth. If the material being tested renders the medium turbid so that the presence or absence of microbial growth cannot be readily determined by visual examination, 14 days after the beginning of incubation transfer portions (each not less than 1 mL) of the medium to fresh vessels of the same medium and then incubate the original and transfer vessels for not less than 4 days.

If no evidence of microbial growth is found, the product to be examined complies with the test for sterility.

If evidence of microbial growth is found the product does not comply with the test for sterility, unless it can be clearly demonstrated that the test was invalid for causes unrelated to the product. The test may be considered invalid only if one or more of the following conditions are fulfilled:

  1. the data of the microbiological monitoring of the sterility testing facility show a fault;
  2. a review of the testing procedure used during the test in question reveals a fault;
  3. microbial growth is found in the negative controls;
  4. after determination of the identity of the micro-organisms isolated from the test, the growth of this species or these species may be ascribed unequivocally to faults with respect to the material and/or the technique used in conducting the sterility test procedure.

If the test is declared to be invalid it is repeated with the same number of units as in the original test.

If no evidence of microbial growth is found in the repeat test the product examined complies with the test for sterility. If microbial growth is found in the repeat test the product examined does not comply with the test for sterility.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

試驗無效（Invalid Test）的四個法定條件：

  • (a) 環境監測異常：EM 顯示測試設施的潔淨度不合格（如隔離器內 EM 有菌計數超標）
  • (b) 操作程序有誤：事後審查發現操作步驟有偏差（如未按 SOP 執行沖洗步驟）
  • (c) 陰性對照組有生長：空白對照培養基出現微生物生長，直接否定試驗有效性
  • (d) 鑑定後確認是操作汙染：從試驗中鑑定出的菌種，能明確追溯至人員手部/試驗器具，而非來自產品

重點提示 Key Notes

「無效」不等於「通過」：試驗無效只是允許重複試驗，不等於產品自動放行。重複試驗（Same number of units）再次發現生長 → 確定不符合無菌要求。

「產品使培養基混濁」的特殊處理：若產品本身（如脂肪乳劑、蛋白質製劑）使培養基外觀模糊，14 天後取 ≥1 mL 轉入新培養基再觀察 4 天——增加「轉管培養（Subculture）」步驟。

查廠重點：FDA / EMA 稽核員最關注「無效試驗」的調查報告——理由是否充分、調查是否及時、是否有 CAPA？每一次「無效」都應有完整的偏差調查記錄。

實務應用 Practical Application

CDMO 在一批凍乾注射劑無菌試驗出現初始陽性（Initial Positive）時，標準處置流程：

  1. 立即通知 QA，啟動偏差調查
  2. 收集 EM 數據、操作人員訓練記錄、當天環境條件
  3. 對陽性培養基進行微生物鑑定（16S rRNA 定序或 MALDI-TOF）
  4. 評估是否符合上述四條無效條件
  5. 若確認無效 → 重複試驗（含客戶通知）
  6. 若無法確認無效 → 批次不放行，啟動市場評估（如有上市批次）

比喻說明 Analogy

四條無效條件就像足球比賽的「判例回放（VAR）」——只有在有確切證據顯示裁判（試驗環境）或球員（操作者）出錯時，才能推翻判決重賽。不能因為不喜歡結果就要求重賽，必須有根據。
