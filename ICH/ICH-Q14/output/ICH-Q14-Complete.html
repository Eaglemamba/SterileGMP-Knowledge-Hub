ICH Q14 in the ICH Architecture Preamble

ICH Q14 (Analytical Procedure Development, 2023) is a companion to ICH Q2(R2) (Validation of Analytical Procedures). Q14 addresses the development phase and lifecycle management of analytical procedures; Q2 covers the validation confirmation step.

Key links in the ICH quality system:

  • ICH Q8(R2): Pharmaceutical Development — systematic QbD approach applied to analytical procedure development
  • ICH Q9(R1): Quality Risk Management — risk assessment tools for identifying and ranking analytical procedure parameters
  • ICH Q10: Pharmaceutical Quality System — knowledge management, PQS framework for lifecycle oversight
  • ICH Q12: Lifecycle Management tools: Established Conditions (ECs), PACMP, PLCM Document
  • ICH M4Q (CTD): Format for submitting analytical procedure development information (3.2.S.4.2, 3.2.P.5.2, etc.)

Q14 brings the Quality by Design (QbD) philosophy — previously applied to pharmaceutical development and manufacturing — formally into the domain of analytical science.

Q14 的歷史意義

Q14 是 ICH Q-Series 中第一個專門針對「分析方法開發」的指引。在此之前，分析方法的開發沒有像製程開發（Q8/Q11）那樣系統性的 ICH 指引。Q14 正式將 QbD 的精神延伸到分析科學領域，使分析方法的開發、驗證、上市後管理形成完整的知識體系。

§1.1 Objective p1

This guideline describes science and risk-based approaches for developing and maintaining analytical procedures suitable for the evaluation of the quality of drug substances and drug products.

The systematic approach suggested in ICH Q8 together with principles of ICH Q9 can also be applied to the development and lifecycle management of analytical procedures. When developing an analytical procedure, a minimal (also known as traditional) approach or elements of an enhanced approach can be applied.

Furthermore, the guideline describes additional considerations for the development of multivariate analytical procedures and for real time release testing (RTRT).

This guideline complements ICH Q2 (Validation of Analytical Procedures). Using the tools described in ICH Q12, the guideline describes principles to support change management of analytical procedures based on risk management, comprehensive understanding of the analytical procedure and adherence to predefined criteria for performance characteristics.

The guideline also describes submission of analytical procedure development and related lifecycle information in the CTD format (ICH M4Q).

Key Benefit of the Enhanced Approach

The enhanced approach provides: (1) better assurance of analytical procedure performance; (2) the basis for an analytical procedure control strategy; and (3) the opportunity for more efficient regulatory approaches to post-approval changes.

§1.1 目的

Q14 的五大核心目的
  1. 科學與風險為基礎的框架：涵蓋最低限度（minimal）與強化（enhanced）兩種開發方法
  2. 分析方法生命週期管理：開發 → 驗證 → 上市 → 上市後持續管理的完整鏈條
  3. 多變量分析方法的額外考量：NIR 模型、化學計量學（chemometrics）等
  4. RTRT 的額外考量：即時放行試驗的開發與驗證框架
  5. CTD 申報框架：如何在 CTD（3.2.S.4.2, 3.2.P.5.2 等）中呈現分析方法開發資訊
Enhanced Approach 的三大商業利益

選擇 enhanced approach 的核心動機在於上市後的長期效益：

  1. 更好的性能保證：充分理解參數對性能的影響，降低方法失效風險
  2. 分析方法控制策略的基礎：系統性開發知識支撐更科學的 ECs 設定
  3. 更有效率的法規途徑：PAR/MODR 內的變更可能無需監管申報，節省時間和資源

§1.2 Scope p1

This guideline applies to analytical procedures used for release and stability testing of commercial drug substances and products, hereafter referred to as 'products'.

The guideline can also be applied to other analytical procedures used as part of the control strategy (ICH Q10 Pharmaceutical Quality System) following a risk-based approach.

The scientific principles described in this guideline can be applied in a phase-appropriate manner to analytical procedures used during clinical development.

§1.2 適用範圍

適用範圍三個層次
「Phase-Appropriate」的實務含義

Q14 的原則可在臨床開發期間以「階段適當」的方式靈活應用：

  • 早期臨床（Phase I/II）：可採用較簡化的開發，重點確認方法可用
  • 晚期臨床（Phase III）：方法應朝向商業版本靠攏，逐步完善開發包
  • 商業上市（NDA/BLA/MAA）：完整遵循 Q14 + Q2(R2)，提供完整的開發和驗證資料

§2 General Considerations for Analytical Procedures p1–2

The goal of development is to obtain an analytical procedure fit for the intended purpose — to measure an attribute or attributes of the material with the needed specificity/selectivity, accuracy, precision over the reportable range. Details of performance characteristics are described in ICH Q2.

Platform Analytical Procedures

In certain cases, an analytical procedure can be applied to multiple products with little or no modification of measurement conditions. For a new application of such platform analytical procedures, subsequent development can be abbreviated, and certain validation tests can be omitted based on a science- and risk-based justification.

Data Reuse Across Development and Validation

Data gained during development studies — for example, robustness data from a Design of Experiments (DoE) study — could be used as part of the validation data for the related analytical procedure performance characteristics. Studies do not necessarily need to be repeated.

§2 分析方法的一般考量

分析方法開發的最終目標

分析方法開發的目標是獲得「適合預定用途（fit for the intended purpose）」的分析方法，在可報告範圍（reportable range）內具備：

  • 專一性／選擇性（Specificity/Selectivity）：可區分目標分析物與干擾物
  • 準確度（Accuracy）：測量結果與真值的接近程度
  • 精密度（Precision）：重複測量結果的一致性

（詳細性能特性定義見 ICH Q2(R2)）

平台分析方法（Platform Analytical Procedure）— 適用條件與效益

定義：可用於多個產品、無需大幅修改的分析方法（例如：通用型 HPLC 方法、蛋白質含量 UV 法）。

當使用平台方法時，可縮減的工作：

  • 後續開發研究可縮減（abbreviated），不需從頭重做
  • 部分驗證測試可省略（omitted），需有科學/風險依據支持
  • 開發階段的稳健性 DoE 數據可直接作為驗證數據，不需重複實驗

重要前提：省略或縮減必須有充分的科學和風險依據，並在申請文件中說明理由。

§2.1 Minimal versus Enhanced Approaches p2

Minimum Required Elements (Minimal Approach)

Analytical procedure development should include the following elements as a minimum:

  1. Identifying the attributes of the product which need to be tested
  2. Selecting an appropriate technology and related instruments or suitable apparatus
  3. Conducting studies to evaluate analytical procedure performance characteristics such as specificity, accuracy and precision over the reportable range (including calibration model, lower and/or higher range limits) and robustness
  4. Documenting the analytical procedure including the analytical procedure control strategy

Additional Elements for the Enhanced Approach

The enhanced approach offers a systematic way of developing and refining knowledge of an analytical procedure. The anticipated performance criteria should be documented in an Analytical Target Profile (ATP). In addition to the minimum elements, an enhanced approach may include:

  • Conducting risk assessment and evaluating prior knowledge to identify analytical procedure parameters that can impact performance
  • Conducting uni- or multi-variate experiments and/or modelling to explore ranges and interactions between identified parameters
  • Defining an analytical procedure control strategy including set-points and/or ranges — these could include Proven Acceptable Ranges (PARs) and/or Method Operable Design Regions (MODRs)

Applying elements of the enhanced approach can lead to better understanding of the impact of parameters on performance, and more flexibility for lifecycle management: wider operating ranges and a more appropriate set of Established Conditions (ECs) with associated reporting categories.

§2.1 最低限度方法 vs. 強化方法

兩種開發方法的系統性比較
類比：開車時儀表板的重要性

最低限度方法（Minimal approach）就像：把車開到終點，確認沒有拋錨 — 只知道「能跑」，但不知道引擎溫度、油量、轉速如何變化，一旦出問題只能停車（方法失效）。

強化方法（Enhanced approach）就像：設計了完整的儀表板，實時顯示所有關鍵參數（引擎溫度、油量、轉速等）。不僅知道車能跑，更知道在什麼條件範圍內可以安全駕駛（MODR），哪些參數超出範圍會影響性能。

結論：有了儀表板，不僅更安全，也更容易向主管機關說明「為什麼這項調整不影響性能」，從而降低申報類別。

§2.2 The Analytical Procedure Lifecycle p2–3

Figure 1🔍 depicts elements of the analytical procedure lifecycle as related to the product lifecycle. Analytical procedure development and change management approaches are described in this guideline, whereas analytical procedure validation is described in ICH Q2.

Depending on the intended purpose of the analytical procedure and the development approach taken, the order and extent of each lifecycle element could vary, and several elements could occur simultaneously.

Key lifecycle elements (from Figure 1🔍):

  • Identify measurement needs — ATP definition, informed by QTPP and CQA identification
  • Analytical procedure development — technology selection, parameter investigation, robustness studies
  • Validation (ICH Q2) — confirmation of fitness for purpose against ATP criteria
  • Routine use and ongoing monitoring — system suitability testing (SST), trending, continual improvement
  • Change management — updates driven by continual improvement, process changes, or new knowledge

§2.2 分析方法生命週期

分析方法生命週期五階段
分析方法生命週期 vs. 製程生命週期的平行對應

Q14 的分析方法生命週期架構與製程開發的生命週期完全平行，使用相同的 ICH Q8/Q9/Q10/Q12 工具箱：

  • 製程：QTPP → CQA/CPP 識別 → 製程開發（Q8/Q11）→ 製程驗證（PV）→ 商業生產 → 上市後變更管理（Q12）
  • 分析方法：QTPP+CQA → ATP 定義 → 方法開發（Q14）→ 方法驗證（Q2）→ 例行使用與監控（Q10）→ 上市後變更管理（Q12+Q14）
開發與驗證的界線模糊 — 效率機會

Q14 明確指出：生命週期各元素的「順序和程度」可以變化，多個元素可以同時發生。

實務含義：開發研究（如穩健性 DoE）的數據可直接用於驗證，不需重複做相同實驗。這消除了傳統上「開發」和「驗證」之間的硬性界線，提高效率並降低成本。

Review: Key Benefits of the Enhanced Approach p2

Section 1.1 and Section 2.1 together establish the rationale for investing in the enhanced approach. The guideline explicitly states that the enhanced approach provides:

  • Better assurance of analytical procedure performance
  • The basis for an analytical procedure control strategy
  • The opportunity for more efficient regulatory approaches to post-approval changes

Applying enhanced approach elements can lead to:

  • Better understanding of the impact of analytical procedure parameters on performance
  • Wider operating ranges (PAR/MODR) that can be moved within without regulatory filing
  • A more appropriate set of ECs with associated lower reporting categories for post-approval changes

實習演練：Enhanced Approach 的核心優勢

考題

根據 ICH Q14 §1.1 和 §2.1，選擇 Enhanced approach 而非 Minimal approach 進行分析方法開發有哪些關鍵優勢？請說明其邏輯鏈。

解答

Enhanced approach 的核心優勢遵循以下邏輯鏈：

  1. 更深入的程序理解（Better procedure understanding）
    — 通過風險評估 + DoE，明確哪些參數影響性能、影響程度為何
  2. 更寬的操作範圍（Wider operating ranges）
    — 建立 PAR 和/或 MODR，在已知可接受範圍內操作時無需申報
  3. 更有效率的變更管理（More efficient change management）
    — 在 PAR/MODR 內的調整不屬於 EC 變更，可在 PQS 內部處理
  4. 更低的申報類別（Lower reporting categories）
    — 充分理解的 ECs 可獲得更低申報類別（從 Prior Approval 降為 Notification 或內部管控）

一句話總結：在開發階段多投入 → 上市後少報告、少審查、少成本。

延伸思考

為什麼 Enhanced approach 可以使用「更少且更精確的 ECs」，而 Minimal approach 的 ECs 通常更多？

解答

Minimal approach 對參數影響的理解有限，為確保安全，必須將更多參數列為 ECs（固定設定點），以控制潛在風險。

Enhanced approach 通過系統性研究（DoE、風險評估）充分理解了哪些參數「真正」影響性能，哪些不影響。因此可以：
(1) 對不影響性能的參數不設 ECs；
(2) 對有影響的參數設定 PAR/MODR 而非固定點，在更寬的範圍內操作而不需申報。

§3 Analytical Target Profile (ATP) pp 6–8

Product and process understanding (ICH Q8 and ICH Q11) leads to the identification of critical quality attributes (CQAs) requiring analytical measurement for control, which may be included in the quality target product profile (QTPP). Measurement needs can be captured in an analytical target profile (ATP), which forms the basis for development of the analytical procedure.

An ATP consists of:

  • A description of the intended purpose of the analytical procedure
  • Appropriate details on the product attributes to be measured
  • Relevant performance characteristics with associated performance criteria
  • Measurement requirements for one or more quality attributes

The ATP drives the choice of analytical technology. Multiple available analytical techniques may meet the performance criteria. Consideration of the operating environment (e.g., at-line, in-line or off-line) should be included in the technology selection.

Once a technology has been selected, the ATP serves as a foundation to derive the analytical procedure attributes and performance criteria for analytical procedure validation (ICH Q2).

Formal documentation and submission of an ATP is optional but can facilitate regulatory communication irrespective of the chosen development approach.

The ATP is maintained over the lifecycle and is used as a basis for lifecycle management to ensure that the existing, revised or new analytical procedure remains fit for the intended purpose.

Illustrative examples of ATPs are provided in Annex A.

§3 分析目標輪廓（ATP）

概念辨析：QTPP → CQA → ATP — 品質規劃三層階層

Q14 建立了一套前瞻性品質規劃的三層階層結構，每一層都服務於下一層：

邏輯鏈：QTPP 定義產品要什麼 → CQA 識別哪些屬性需要量測 → ATP 定義量測那些 CQA 的分析方法需達到什麼標準

類比：ATP 是分析方法的「職務說明書（Job Description）」

在設計一個分析方法（「僱用一個員工」）之前，必須先定義這個方法需要做什麼、達到什麼標準。

ATP 就是這份「職務說明書」：

  • 職位目的 → 分析方法的預期用途（例：定量放行規格中的特定雜質）
  • 工作內容 → 待測產品屬性的細節（例：手性雜質 A，濃度範圍 0–0.2%）
  • 績效標準 → 性能特性與對應標準（例：準確度 ±10%；精密度 RSD ≤ 5%）

先有 ATP（職務說明書），才選技術（面試候選人），才開發方法（訓練員工），才驗證（試用期考核）。

重要澄清：ATP 提交是「可選的（optional）」

Q14 明確指出：ATP 的正式文件化和提交是可選的，並非法規強制要求。

無論是否提交，ATP 作為內部開發工具都有價值，有助於團隊對分析方法需求達成共識。

§3 ATP: Technology Selection & Lifecycle Role pp 7–8

The ATP drives the choice of analytical technology. Multiple available analytical techniques may meet the performance criteria defined in the ATP. The choice of technique should consider which technology best meets the performance criteria and the operating environment (e.g., at-line, in-line or off-line).

Once a technology has been selected, the ATP serves as a foundation to:

  • Derive the analytical procedure attributes and associated performance criteria
  • Establish the basis for analytical procedure validation (ICH Q2)

The ATP is maintained over the lifecycle. It is used as a basis for lifecycle management — ensuring that any existing, revised or new analytical procedure remains fit for the intended purpose.

Illustrative examples of ATPs are provided in Annex A of ICH Q14.

§3 ATP 驅動技術選擇與生命週期管理

ATP 驅動的分析方法開發序列流程

Q14 描述了一個以 ATP 為起點的線性序列：

QTPP + CQA 識別
↓
ATP 建立
[定義：預期目的 / 待測屬性 / 性能標準]
↓
技術選擇（Technology Selection）
[問：哪種技術能滿足 ATP 的性能標準？考量操作環境]
↓

[風險評估 → 參數研究（DoE）→ PAR/MODR 確立]
↓

[ATP 性能標準 → 驗證接受標準；開發數據可用於驗證]

↓

關鍵特性：ATP 在整個生命週期中持續存在，每次評估修改後的方法是否仍「適合預定用途」時，ATP 都是判斷基準。

技術選擇時的操作環境考量

ATP 不只定義分析性能標準，還應考量操作環境對技術選擇的影響：

操作環境需求（如需要 in-line 即時量測）會直接限制可選的分析技術，因此 ATP 中應明確說明操作環境需求。

§4.1 Knowledge Management pp 8–9

As with product and manufacturing process development, knowledge management (ICH Q10) plays a critical role in analytical procedure development and during the lifecycle of the analytical procedure.

Prior knowledge is explicitly or implicitly used for informing decisions during analytical procedure development and lifecycle management. Prior knowledge can be:

  • Internal: a company's proprietary development and analytical experience
  • External: scientific and technical publications, or established scientific principles

Prior product knowledge plays an important role in identifying suitable analytical techniques. Knowledge of best practices, state-of-the-art technologies and regulatory expectations contribute to the selection of the most suitable technology for a given purpose.

Existing platform analytical procedures (e.g., protein content measurement by UV spectroscopy) can be leveraged to evaluate the attributes of a specific product without conducting additional procedure development.

As additional information is obtained, knowledge related to analytical procedures should be actively managed throughout the product lifecycle.

§4.1 知識管理

分析方法生命週期中的知識管理：內部 vs. 外部先驗知識

ICH Q10 的知識管理原則被正式引入分析方法開發領域。先驗知識（Prior Knowledge）是加速開發、降低風險的核心資產：

平台分析方法（Platform Analytical Procedure）的效益：對於可應用於多種產品的成熟方法（如 UV 法測定蛋白質含量），可直接利用現有先驗知識，省略重複的基礎開發，部分驗證測試亦可基於科學/風險理由省略。

「主動管理（actively managed）」的實踐意涵

Q14 要求知識需在整個產品生命週期中「主動管理」，不只是被動儲存：

  • 累積：開發數據、驗證結果、例行監控數據均納入知識庫
  • 分析：定期分析趨勢，識別方法性能漂移的早期信號
  • 應用：將新知識應用於風險評估更新（§4.2）和變更決策
  • 傳承：透過 PQS（ICH Q10）確保知識不因人員異動而流失

知識管理與風險管理（§4.2）是相互支撐的：先驗知識為風險評估提供輸入，風險評估結果又成為新的知識被管理。

§4.2 Risk Management pp 9–11

The use of quality risk management (QRM) (ICH Q9) is encouraged to aid in the development of a robust analytical procedure to reduce risk of poor performance and reporting incorrect results.

Risk assessment is typically performed early in analytical procedure development and is updated as more information becomes available. Risk assessment can be formal or informal and can be supported by prior knowledge.

Risk assessment tools as described in ICH Q9 Annex 1 can be used to:

  1. Identify analytical procedure parameters (factors and operational steps) with potential impact on its performance — e.g., Ishikawa diagram (Annex A Figure 2🔍)
  2. Assess the potential impact of analytical procedure parameters on the analytical procedure performance
  3. Identify and prioritise analytical procedure parameters to be investigated experimentally
  4. Inform the need and the extent of ongoing monitoring as part of risk review

Risk communication should be used to support continual improvement of the analytical procedure performance throughout its lifecycle.

The outcome of quality risk management should be documented in the relevant parts of the applicant's pharmaceutical quality system (PQS) (ICH Q10).

§4.2 風險管理

QRM 在分析方法開發中的四大應用

ICH Q9 的 QRM 工具（Ishikawa 圖、FMEA 等）被明確應用於分析方法開發的四個方向：

風險評估的時機：「開發早期執行，隨資訊更新」

Q14 明確強調風險評估應在開發早期（early in development）執行，並隨著更多信息的獲得持續更新：

  • 開發早期：基於先驗知識（§4.1）進行初始風險評估 — 此時數據有限，可採非正式方式（informal）
  • 開發過程中：實驗數據（DoE、穩健性研究）累積後更新風險評估，調整高/低風險分類
  • 驗證前：更新後的風險評估確認哪些性能特性需要驗證、驗證的嚴格程度
  • 上市後：例行監控數據（趨勢、OOS/OOT 調查）觸發風險評估更新

風險評估可以是正式（formal）或非正式（informal）的，關鍵是有記錄、有邏輯，並與先驗知識相連結。結果必須記錄於 PQS（ICH Q10）中。

Section Summary: ATP and QRM Integration pp 6–11

The ATP and QRM tools together form the foundation for systematic analytical procedure development under ICH Q14. The ATP captures measurement needs prospectively; QRM ensures that development resources are focused on the parameters and characteristics that matter most.

Key relationships:

  • ATP is informed by QTPP and CQA identification
  • ATP drives technology selection and defines validation acceptance criteria
  • Prior knowledge (§4.1) informs both ATP definition and initial risk assessment
  • Risk assessment (§4.2) prioritises experimental investigation of procedure parameters
  • ATP serves as the benchmark for lifecycle management decisions
  • QRM outcomes are documented in PQS (ICH Q10)

The degree of formality applied to both ATP documentation and risk assessment can be scaled to the complexity of the analytical procedure and the development approach chosen (minimal vs. enhanced).

解答

1. ATP 的定義：

ATP（Analytical Target Profile，分析目標輪廓）是對一項分析量測的前瞻性描述，說明此分析方法需達到的預期目的和性能特性標準。它是在實際開發分析方法之前建立的「量測需求規格書」，類似製程開發中的 QTPP 概念。

2. ATP 的核心元素（Q14 §3）：

  • 預期目的（intended purpose）：此分析方法用於量測什麼？用於哪個決策（放行、穩定性、製程監控）？
  • 待測產品屬性（product attributes to be measured）：具體的品質屬性、樣品基質類型、預期濃度或含量範圍
  • 性能特性與對應標準（performance characteristics with criteria）：例如選擇性、準確度目標值、精密度（RSD）上限、偵測限要求等
  • 量測要求（measurement requirements）：操作環境（off-line / at-line / in-line）、通量需求等

3. ATP 提交時機與強制性：

  • 建議提交時機：採用 Enhanced approach 開發；希望與審評機構在驗證接受標準上預先達成共識；作為 PACMP（預先核准變更方案）的基礎文件
  • 提交優點：有助於法規溝通，無論採用哪種開發方法均可提交；可減少審評問題；明確驗證接受標準的合理性依據
  • 不提交亦可：但仍需在驗證報告中說明性能標準的科學合理性，且 ATP 可作為內部開發工具使用

§5.1 Robustness pp 12–13

Robustness is a measure of the capacity of an analytical procedure to meet the expected performance criteria during normal use when subjected to deliberate variations of analytical procedure parameters.

Robustness evaluation should consider the duration of analysis, including the stability of sample preparations and reagents.

Prior knowledge and risk assessment inform the selection of parameters to investigate. Parameters likely to influence procedure performance over the intended period of use should be studied.

For most analytical procedures, robustness evaluation is conducted during development and does not need to be repeated during validation (per ICH Q2). Data from validation studies (e.g., intermediate precision) can complement robustness evaluation.

For procedures with inherent high parameter variability (e.g., those requiring biological reagents), wider parameter ranges may need to be investigated.

Robustness of multivariate procedures may require additional considerations (see Chapter 8).

The outcome of robustness evaluation should be documented and reflected in the analytical procedure control strategy.

§5.1 穩健性（Robustness）

核心定義：什麼是「穩健性」？

穩健性衡量的是：當分析程序參數發生刻意的小幅變動時，分析程序仍能符合預期性能標準的能力。

重點在於「deliberate variations」——這是主動設計的實驗，不是意外誤差。目的是找出程序的邊界，確保在正常使用條件下，微小偏差不會導致失敗。

評估時機：穩健性測試應在開發階段進行，不需要在驗證時重複（ICH Q2規範）。驗證階段的中間精密度數據可作為補充。

參數選擇依據：

  • 先驗知識（Prior knowledge）
  • 風險評估（Risk assessment）
  • 預期使用期間內最可能影響性能的參數
特別注意：生物試劑的高可變性

使用生物試劑（如抗體、酶）的分析程序，由於批次間固有的高可變性，需要調查更寬的參數範圍。這在生物製藥的效價分析（potency assay）中尤為重要。

穩健性評估結果必須記錄，並反映在分析程序控制策略中——這是§6的直接輸入。

§5.2 Analytical Procedure Parameter Ranges pp 13–14

Experiments to investigate parameter ranges generate additional knowledge about procedure performance. Analytical procedure attributes and associated criteria could be derived from the ATP.

Univariate Examination

Examining a single parameter in isolation establishes a Proven Acceptable Range (PAR) for the analytical procedure — the range within which a parameter can be varied while the procedure continues to meet its performance criteria.

Enhanced Approach (Multivariate)

Ranges and interactions are investigated in multivariate experiments (DoE). Risk assessment and prior knowledge identify parameters, attributes, and ranges for investigation. Categorical variables (e.g., different instruments, different analysts) can also be included in the experimental design.

Outcomes from development studies — understanding of relationships between AP parameters (inputs) and analytical responses (outputs). Based on results:

  • Fixed set-points defined for some parameters
  • PARs defined for individual parameters that can vary within an acceptable range
  • MODRs (Method Operable Design Regions) for combined ranges of two or more parameters within which the AP is shown fit for intended purpose

Key regulatory rule: Moving within an approved PAR or MODR does NOT require regulatory notification.

AP validation for PAR/MODR is required only for performance characteristics not already covered by development data. The part of a PAR or MODR intended for routine use (typically the intended operational conditions or set point) must be covered by validation data.

For future changes within a PAR or MODR: an assessment of the need and extent of additional validation tests is required. A validation strategy (e.g., as part of a validation protocol) can define the necessary scope.

§6 Analytical Procedure Control Strategy pp 14–16

The AP control strategy ensures that the analytical procedure is fit for its intended purpose during routine use throughout its lifecycle. It consists of controls derived from the current understanding of the AP, including development data, risk assessment, robustness evaluation, and prior knowledge.

The AP control strategy should be defined before validation and confirmed after validation is finalised.

AP Control Strategy Includes:

  • AP parameters that need to be controlled
  • System Suitability Test (SST) — an integral part of the analytical procedure

The AP should describe steps necessary to perform each analytical test: sample, reference materials, reagents, sample/control preparations, apparatus use, calibration curve generation, number of replicates, and formulae for calculating reportable results. The level of detail should enable a skilled analyst to perform the analysis.

System Suitability Test (SST)

The SST depends on the type and intent of the AP. It is typically conducted with one or more predefined materials (positive/negative controls). The SST verifies selected AP attributes, with acceptance criteria based on AP performance criteria. Components are selected using risk assessment, knowledge, and development data.

The SST verifies that the measurement system and analytical operations are fit for intended purpose. Validity of results depends on the outcome of the SST.

In the enhanced approach, well-designed SST parameters and criteria represent an important aspect of risk mitigation.

For multivariate APs: data quality is verified using suitable software tools.

§6 分析程序控制策略

AP 控制策略的四大組成要素

時序要求：控制策略應在驗證前定義，並在驗證完成後確認——這確保了驗證設計與控制策略目標一致。

類比：SST 就像飛行前的「起飛前檢查清單」

每次飛機起飛前，機長必須完成一份標準的起飛前檢查清單——引擎狀態、燃油、儀表等——確認所有系統正常運作，才能允許起飛。

SST 的邏輯完全相同：每次分析運行前，用預定義的材料（如系統適用性標準品）驗證儀器、試劑和操作系統是否正常運作，才能讓正式樣品的結果具有效力。

關鍵：結果有效性取決於 SST 是否通過。如同飛機不得跳過起飛前檢查，分析員不得在 SST 未通過的情況下報告結果。

Sample Suitability Assessment p 15

Often used for biologics: a sample or sample preparation is considered suitable if the measurement response satisfies predefined acceptance criteria. Along with SST, this is a prerequisite for the validity of the result.

Sample suitability assessment may include:

  • Assessment of the similarity of response between the reference material and the test sample
  • Assessment of interfering signals
  • For multivariate APs: a data quality check using software to verify the sample fits within the model space

Ongoing Monitoring

Ongoing monitoring is recommended to look for trends. It facilitates lifecycle management and enables proactive intervention before the AP performance degrades.

持續監控的重要性

Q14 推薦（非強制）持續監控，目的是：

  • 偵測趨勢（如儀器性能緩慢退化）
  • 支持生命週期管理

這是分析程序的「預防維護」，而非等待失敗後再補救。

§6.1 Established Conditions (ECs) for Analytical Procedures pp 16–17

In line with ICH Q12, applicants may propose Established Conditions (ECs) for an analytical procedure. ECs are identified using risk assessment, prior knowledge, and uni-/multi-variate experimentation.

Minimal vs Enhanced Approach to ECs

With a minimal approach: the number of ECs may be extensive, with fixed parameters and set points described in detail.

With an enhanced approach: increased understanding facilitates identification of an appropriate, reduced set of ECs focused on AP performance (e.g., acceptable ranges) when justified by AP understanding and risk management.

What ECs Could Consist of:

  • Performance characteristics and associated criteria (e.g., as described in the ATP)
  • Analytical procedure principle (physicochemical basis or specific technology)
  • SST and sample suitability assessment criteria
  • Set points and/or ranges for one or more AP parameters

Parameters that need control, or where the need for control cannot reasonably be excluded, should be identified as ECs. If SST or performance criteria demonstrate that a parameter is under control, that parameter may not need to be defined as an EC (or may be assigned a lower reporting category).

ECs and reporting categories are proposed by the applicant and assessed by regulatory authorities.

Important: Use of the enhanced approach should NOT lead to a less detailed description of the AP in the regulatory submission. Module 3 AP descriptions must still be suitably detailed, including ECs and supportive information.

§6.1 分析程序的既定條件（ECs）

概念連結：ICH Q12 的 EC 概念如何應用於分析程序？

ICH Q12 引入的既定條件（Established Conditions, ECs）概念原本針對製造工藝——EC 是法規批准文件中被納入監管的條件，變更EC需走法規申報程序。

Q14 將這個框架擴展到分析程序領域：

  • AP 的哪些參數或條件需要作為 EC？
  • EC 的變更需要什麼層級的申報？
  • 申請人如何設計 EC 集合，使日常操作靈活性最大化？
最小化方法 vs 增強方法 — EC 策略對比
常見誤解：增強方法 ≠ 減少申報文件細節

許多人誤以為「增強方法」意味著可以在法規申報中提交更少的資訊。這是錯誤的。

Q14 明確指出：增強方法不應導致 Module 3 中分析程序描述的細節減少。差別在於細節的性質：

  • 最小化方法：固定參數清單
  • 增強方法：性能標準 + 可接受範圍 + 支持性開發數據

兩者都需要充分的細節，只是描述的框架不同。

橋接研究（Bridging Study）的概念也適用於方法的場地轉移（site transfer）。即使方法本身不變，轉移到不同實驗室也需要評估。

§7 ATP as Basis for PACMP p20–21

The ATP could also form the basis of a PACMP. A PACMP allows changes (e.g., a technology change) to be reported at a lower reporting category than would otherwise apply, provided that pre-defined requirements are met.

Risk assessment outcomes submitted to regulatory authorities when ECs are proposed help justify reporting categories. Risk is reduced by defining relevant performance criteria as ECs.

Risk reduction is possible when sufficient understanding is available (Table 1) to design future bridging studies (Table 2).

ATP 作為 PACMP 的基礎

PACMP 應用於分析方法的實際效益

什麼是 PACMP？ Post-Approval Change Management Protocol — 一種預先與法規機關約定的「未來變更路徑圖」。當特定預定條件達成時，可按照較低申報類別進行變更。

ATP 如何成為 PACMP 的基礎？

  1. ATP 定義了方法必須達到的性能標準（如：準確度 95–105%，精密度 RSD ≤ 5%）
  2. 在 PACMP 中約定：若未來新方法（如 LC-MS/MS）滿足 ATP 要求 + 完成橋接研究，則可按 Notification 申報，而非 Prior Approval
  3. 法規機關審批 PACMP → 未來執行變更時直接按計劃操作，效率大幅提升

實際案例場景：

  • 公司預計未來 3 年內可能將效力測定從細胞測定更換為受體結合測定
  • 在申請時即建立 PACMP：定義驗收標準（ATP）、橋接研究計劃、可接受的可比性標準
  • 未來執行時：按 PACMP 計劃完成研究 → 以 Notification 申報，而非等候 Prior Approval（可節省 12–18 個月）

§7 製品/製程變更與全新分析方法的引入

製品或製程變更如何影響分析方法

製品或製程的任何重大變更都可能觸發分析方法的重新評估：

ATP 若已建立，應首先評估現有 ATP 是否仍然適用；若不適用，需更新 ATP，然後評估現有分析方法是否仍能滿足更新後的 ATP。

提出全新分析方法時的三步驟
  1. 全面風險評估：評估新方法引入的風險（技術複雜性、CQA 關鍵性、與原方法的差異程度）
  2. 建立新的方法控制策略：確認新方法的 ECs、SST 標準、PAR/MODR（若適用）
  3. 申報溝通中論證 ECs：在變更申請中提供充分的科學依據，說明新方法的 ECs 選擇與申報類別的合理性
PQS 在生命週期管理中的角色

PQS（藥品品質系統）是所有分析方法變更的「記憶體」：

  • 需要申報的變更：同時在 PQS 和法規申請文件中記錄
  • 不需要申報的變更（如 PAR/MODR 範圍內的調整）：僅在 PQS 中記錄，但記錄必須完整（包括變更原因、評估結果、批准記錄）
  • ICH Q12 合規性：PQS 的變更管理流程需符合 ICH Q12 建議，不能是單純的文件記錄，需有完整的變更評估和批准流程
綜合應用問題

某公司計劃將其原料藥（Drug Substance）的 HPLC 含量分析方法更換為 LC-MS/MS 方法（分析方法原理改變）。請問：(1) 需要進行哪些橋接研究？(2) 此變更通常適用哪種申報類別？(3) 如果公司希望降低申報類別，有哪些可能的工具？

解答

(1) 橋接研究要求（Table 2，情境 1 — 改變分析方法原理）：

  1. 全面驗證（Full Validation）：新 LC-MS/MS 方法需按 ICH Q2 進行全面驗證，包括專一性、線性、範圍、準確度、精密度、偵測限/定量限（若適用）、穩健性
  2. 代表性樣品的比較分析：需使用代表性批次樣品（包括邊緣批次、壓力降解樣品）同時以新舊兩種方法分析，確認結果一致性
  3. 參考材料比較：參考標準品在兩種方法下的分析結果需具有可比性
  4. 區分能力確認：新方法對於可接受（合格）vs. 不可接受（不合格）樣品的區分能力，必須與原 HPLC 方法相當

(2) 申報類別：通常為 Prior Approval（前置核准），因為：

  • 分析方法原理改變（HPLC-UV → LC-MS/MS）= 高程度變更
  • 原料藥含量分析 = 關鍵 CQA，直接關係到安全性和療效
  • 根據 Figure 2🔍 的風險評估邏輯：高 CQA 關鍵性 + 高變更程度 → Prior Approval

(3) 降低申報類別的工具：

  • PACMP（首選）：若在最初申請時已建立 PACMP，預先定義 LC-MS/MS 的驗收標準和橋接研究計劃，可以按 Notification 申報
  • ECs + 充分先驗知識：若公司有豐富的 LC-MS/MS 平台知識，且現有 HPLC 方法的 ATP 和 ECs 已完善定義，可在申報中提供全面的風險評估以支持較低申報類別
  • 限制：即使有上述工具，法規機關仍有最終決定權，中等風險的公司提案可能被要求升級為 Prior Approval

§8 Development of Multivariate Analytical Procedures p11–14

Multivariate analytical procedures are those where a result is determined through a multivariate calibration model utilising more than one input variable. The considerations here are for models using latent variables that are mathematically related to directly measured variables.

Sample and Sample Population

Multivariate models link measured model variables with values obtained from a validated reference analytical procedure or from reference samples. Care should be taken to ensure that uncertainty in the reference analytical procedure is sufficiently low in relation to the intended performance of the multivariate analytical procedure.

The sample population should encompass the sources of variability likely to occur during manufacturing and analysis: raw material quality, manufacturing process variability, storage conditions, sample preparation and testing. Development laboratory and pilot scale samples are often utilised to provide variability. Inclusion of commercial scale samples is recommended to capture variability related to specific equipment and/or processing conditions.

Data Transformation

The selection of the data transformation method(s) can be driven by the type of data, instrument or sample, the intended purpose of the model and/or prior knowledge. Any transformation should be documented and justified.

Variable Selection

Variable selection is performed during model development (e.g., wavelength range selection in spectroscopic applications). It depends on the measurement principle, application and other factors, and should be justified.

Robustness and Latent Variables

Model robustness should be built into the model by including relevant sources of variability. A critical factor is the number of latent variables — too many can result in model overfitting, potentially resulting in decreased robustness and a need for more frequent model updates.

Recalibration and Model Maintenance

Tracking calibration model performance is an important part of ongoing monitoring. Diagnostic tools include:

  • Examination of residuals (e.g., x-residuals or F-probability)
  • Outlier diagnostics (e.g., Hotelling's T-squared or Mahalanobis distance)

Continued performance should be confirmed on a periodic and event-driven basis by comparison of model predictions with known values of reference samples. Examples of events that could trigger confirmatory testing include new known process variability, unexpected process events or scheduled instrument maintenance.

Monitoring can trigger model rebuilding (recalibration) as part of continual improvement. When updating the model, new data may need to be included and old non-relevant data may be removed.

§8 多變量分析方法的開發

多變量分析方法的本質

多變量分析方法：通過使用多於一個輸入變量的校正模型來確定結果的分析方法。

典型範例：NIR（近紅外）光譜分析、拉曼光譜分析、多波長 UV 分析

核心原理：測量多個頻率/波長的吸光度（多個輸入變量）→ 通過數學模型（PLS、PCR 等） → 預測目標品質屬性（輸出）

樣品選擇策略：代表性的關鍵

多變量模型的性能上限由校正集（calibration set）的樣品多樣性決定。樣品選擇的三個層次：

潛在變量數目（Latent Variables）：多但不過

潛在變量數目是多變量模型開發中最關鍵的決策之一：

  • 太少：模型欠擬合（under-fitting），預測精度不足
  • 太多：模型過擬合（over-fitting），記住了噪音而非真實信號 → 穩健性下降，需更頻繁重新校正
  • 最佳數目：在校正集和驗證集上均有良好預測性能的最小數目

Q14 要求提供最終選擇的潛在變量數目的合理性說明，並可使用軟體提供的診斷圖（如 cross-validation plots）支持。

模型維護的觸發條件（事件驅動 + 定期）

模型性能確認需在以下時機進行：

  • 定期（periodic）：按預定頻率使用參考樣品比較模型預測值與已知值
  • 事件驅動（event-driven）：
    • 新的已知製程變異（如原料供應商更換）
    • 意外製程事件
    • 排定的儀器維護（可能影響光源、光路）
模型生命週期三階段（圖3）

ICH Q14 圖3 描述了多變量模型的生命週期：

  1. 模型建立：樣品選擇 → 數據採集 → 校正 → 內部測試 → 驗證（獨立數據）→ 維護計畫
  2. 例行使用：每次量測使用異常值診斷 → 定期/事件驅動確認
  3. 模型維護：發現不可接受性能 → 模型重新評估 → 可能需要重新開發或修改維護計畫

這是一個迭代的生命週期循環，允許模型隨著更多製程知識的積累而持續優化。

§9 Development of Analytical Procedures for RTRT p14–15

Real Time Release Testing (RTRT) is the ability to evaluate and ensure the quality of in-process and/or final product based on process data, which typically include a valid combination of measured material attributes and process controls (ICH Q8). RTRT measurements work in conjunction with all elements of the product control strategy.

RTRT can be based on an appropriate combination of one or more process measurements and/or material attributes to provide a value for one or more CQAs and should be specific for those CQAs. The relationship between the RTRT approach and the CQAs, as well as acceptance criteria, should be fully justified.

An RTRT analytical procedure should be validated as recommended in ICH Q2 and it should be demonstrated that the process measurements have appropriate specificity for the targeted quality attribute.

Sampling and Sample Interface

The measurement point(s) should be chosen to be representative of the entire material being processed with the sample duration or amount appropriately chosen (e.g., relative to a unit dose). The sample interface should remain consistent over the duration of manufacturing and should be robust to expected processing and environmental variations.

Product Specification

The RTRT approach should be included in the product specification (ICH Q6A, ICH Q6B). If the dossier includes a registered alternative control strategy to RTRT (e.g., traditional end-product testing when process analytics are unavailable), the related analytical procedures and when they would be applied should also be included in the product specifications.

Quantitative RTRT results should be expressed in the same units as those for traditional testing.

§9 即時放行試驗（RTRT）分析方法的開發

RTRT 的核心定義

RTRT（Real Time Release Testing）（來自 ICH Q8）：

基於製程數據（通常包括測量的物料屬性和製程控制的有效組合）來評估和確保中間製程和/或最終產品品質的能力。

關鍵特點：

  • RTRT 不是獨立的，必須與整個產品控制策略的所有元素協同工作
  • RTRT 可應用於 DS（藥物物質）、中間體（intermediates）和 DP（藥品）
  • 必須對目標 CQAs 具有充分的專一性
RTRT 的驗證要求

RTRT 分析方法必須依照 ICH Q2 進行驗證（與傳統方法相同的要求），並且需要額外證明：

  • 製程量測對目標品質屬性具有適當的專一性
  • 量測點能代表整個被處理物料（不是局部取樣）
  • 樣品介面在整個製造週期保持一致，對製程和環境變化穩健
RTRT 需納入產品規格的要求

依 ICH Q6A/Q6B，RTRT 方法需納入產品規格，包含：

  • 分析方法的參考（reference to the analytical procedure）
  • 相關的驗收標準
  • 定量結果的表達單位（必須與傳統試驗相同）

替代控制策略的要求：若申請資料包含 RTRT 的「已登記替代控制策略」（如 PAT 設備不可用時的傳統試驗），相關分析方法和適用情況也必須在規格中說明。

RTRT 在 CM（連續製造）中的應用

RTRT 在 CM 中特別重要（見 ICH Q13 §4.2）：

  • CM 的連續物料流使傳統的批次終點測試難以應用
  • NIR 等線上/在線方法可在製造過程中持續監控 CQAs（如含量均一性、水分）
  • RTRT 方法的開發和驗證需遵循 Q14 §8（多變量）+ §9（RTRT）的雙重要求
整合思考問題

一個錠劑製造商想用壓片機進料框架的 NIR 方法取代傳統的成品含量均一性測試（content uniformity, CU）。此方法需滿足哪些開發和驗證要求？

解答

此 NIR 方法屬於「多變量分析方法用於 RTRT」，需滿足 §8 和 §9 的雙重要求：

開發要求（§8 多變量）：

  • 樣品選擇：涵蓋商業規模製造的所有預期含量變異（90–110% label claim）
  • 參考方法：使用驗證的 HPLC 含量方法確定校正樣品的真實值
  • 模型開發：確定最佳潛在變量數目；定義波長範圍；建立維護計畫

RTRT 特殊要求（§9）：

  • 量測點代表性：NIR 探頭需代表整個批次的物料，非只是局部
  • 專一性驗證：方法需對含量（vs. 顏色、水分等干擾）具有充分選擇性
  • 樣品介面穩健性：探頭在整個製造週期（包括長時間 CM 運行）保持一致
  • 納入規格：在 DP 規格中列出 NIR RTRT 方法和驗收標準（與傳統測試相同單位）
  • 備援策略：PAT 故障時的傳統終點測試方案需在規格中說明

§11 Key Glossary Terms p16–22

§11 詞彙精要：Q14 vs. Q2(R2) 的術語對照

Q14 新引入術語 vs. Q2(R2) 既有術語
重要術語區分：AP Attribute vs. Performance Characteristic

AP Attribute（分析方法屬性）：技術特有（technology-specific）
例：HPLC 的「解析度 Rs」只有在色譜分析中才有意義。

Performance Characteristic（性能特性）：技術無關（technology-independent）
例：「準確度（accuracy）」對 HPLC、NIR、GC 等所有方法都適用。

→ 這個區分讓 ATP 可以用技術無關的語言定義要求（性能特性），而 SST 用技術特有的指標（AP attributes）來驗證在具體技術上的達成情況。

Q14 綜合總結問題

一家公司採用 Enhanced approach 開發了一個 HPLC 有關物質分析方法，並建立了 PAR 和 MODR，提交了 ATP 和 ECs。上市後，因儀器升級需要更換 HPLC 系統（相同方法原理，新廠牌儀器）。請描述完整的變更管理路徑。

解答

1. 變更識別和風險評估：

  • 屬於「同一原理內的變更」（Table 2）
  • 評估新儀器對原已批准的 PAR/MODR 的影響
  • 評估已批准的 SST 是否仍能在新儀器上有效偵測性能問題

2. 確認申報類別（依原提交的 EC 申報類別）：

  • 若「儀器廠牌」不是 EC → 此變更不需法規溝通，在 PQS 管理
  • 若「儀器廠牌」是 EC → 依原定申報類別（可能為通知）

3. 橋接研究（依 Table 2，同一原理內的變更）：

  • 驗證 SST 在新儀器上的性能（系統精密度等）
  • 比較分析（代表性樣品在新舊系統上的結果比較）
  • 確認原 PAR/MODR 在新儀器上仍然有效

4. 記錄和後續：

  • 在 PQS 記錄此變更（無論是否需要申報）
  • 更新分析方法描述，反映新儀器
