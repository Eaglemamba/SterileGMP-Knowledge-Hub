Section 3 Overview｜持續改善製程績效與產品品質——概述

原文內容 Original Text

3. CONTINUAL IMPROVEMENT OF PROCESS PERFORMANCE AND PRODUCT QUALITY

This section describes the lifecycle stage goals and the four specific pharmaceutical quality system elements that augment regional requirements to achieve the ICH Q10 objectives, as defined in Section 1.5. It does not restate all regional GMP requirements.

The four PQS elements are:

  • Process performance and product quality monitoring system;
  • Corrective action and preventive action (CAPA) system;
  • Change management system;
  • Management review of process performance and product quality.

These elements should be applied in a manner that is appropriate and proportionate to each of the product lifecycle stages, recognising the differences among, and the different goals of, each stage. Throughout the product lifecycle, companies are encouraged to evaluate opportunities for innovative approaches to improve product quality.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：Q10 的「額外貢獻」是什麼？

Section 3 開宗明義說：這一節描述的是「augment regional requirements（補充地區性 GMP 要求）」的內容，不是重複 GMP。換言之，若你只遵守 GMP，你已有某些這些要素的雛形；Q10 要求你把它們系統化、結構化、並貫穿整個產品生命週期。

四大要素的關係是：PPQMS（監測）→ CAPA（應對）→ Change Management（改變）→ Management Review（總覽），形成一個閉環的持續改善機制。

3.1 Lifecycle Stage Goals｜各生命週期階段目標

原文內容 Original Text

3.1 Lifecycle Stage Goals

The goals of each product lifecycle stage are described below.

3.1.1 Pharmaceutical Development

The goal of pharmaceutical development activities is to design a product and its manufacturing process to consistently deliver the intended performance and meet the needs of patients and healthcare professionals, and regulatory authorities and internal customers' requirements. Approaches to pharmaceutical development are described in ICH Q8. The results of exploratory and clinical development studies, while outside the scope of this guidance, are inputs to pharmaceutical development.

3.1.2 Technology Transfer

The goal of technology transfer activities is to transfer product and process knowledge between development and manufacturing, and within or between manufacturing sites to achieve product realisation. This knowledge forms the basis for the manufacturing process, control strategy, process validation approach and ongoing continual improvement.

3.1.3 Commercial Manufacturing

The goals of manufacturing activities include achieving product realisation, establishing and maintaining a state of control and facilitating continual improvement. The pharmaceutical quality system should assure that the desired product quality is routinely met, suitable process performance is achieved, the set of controls are appropriate, improvement opportunities are identified and evaluated, and the body of knowledge is continually expanded.

3.1.4 Product Discontinuation

The goal of product discontinuation activities is to manage the terminal stage of the product lifecycle effectively. For product discontinuation, a pre-defined approach should be used to manage activities such as retention of documentation and samples and continued product assessment (e.g., complaint handling and stability) and reporting in accordance with regulatory requirements.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：生命週期的四個階段

  • Pharmaceutical Development（藥品開發）：產品和製程的設計階段。Q10 與 Q8 銜接——Q8 提供具體的開發方法論（QbD、Design Space），Q10 將這個階段納入整體 PQS 框架。
  • Technology Transfer（技術轉移）：知識從開發端傳遞到製造端，或在製造廠之間傳遞。注意：技轉的成功與否，直接影響後續 Process Validation 和商業化品質。
  • Commercial Manufacturing（商業製造）：五個關鍵詞——product realisation（實現）、state of control（管控狀態）、continual improvement（持續改善）、improvement opportunities identified（識別改善機會）、body of knowledge expanded（知識體系擴展）。這五點是商業製造階段 PQS 的核心任務。
  • Product Discontinuation（產品停產）：常被忽視的階段。Q10 要求必須有預先定義的停產方法（pre-defined approach），涵蓋文件/樣本留存、持續客訴追蹤和穩定性評估。

比喻說明 Analogy — 生命週期如同建築的四個階段

開發 = 建築師繪製設計圖並做結構測試（Q8 確保設計穩健）

技術轉移 = 施工隊接收設計圖並進行試蓋（確保設計意圖能被實現）

商業製造 = 大樓投入使用，需要持續維護、監控電力/水管/電梯狀態（state of control），並持續尋找優化空間

停產 = 大樓拆除前的資料存檔、住戶安置、後續追蹤（確保沒有遺留問題影響居民安全）

停產不是「結束」，是「有序收尾」——市場上仍有存貨，患者仍在使用，品質責任未消失。

重點提示：技術轉移的 PQS 意涵

Q10 強調技術轉移傳遞的是「knowledge（知識）」，不只是 SOP。傳統技轉只給製程參數表；Q10 精神的技轉還要傳遞：

  • 為什麼選這個參數範圍（背後的科學理解）
  • 關鍵屬性（CQA）與製程參數（CPP）的關係
  • 已知的製程風險和應對策略
  • 先前的失敗案例和學習（lessons learned）

這些知識若沒有系統性傳遞，後繼製造廠只能「照本宣科」而缺乏真正理解，一旦製程出現偏差將難以排查根本原因。

3.2.1 Process Performance and Product Quality Monitoring System (PPQMS)｜製程績效與產品品質監測系統

原文內容 Original Text

3.2.1 Process Performance and Product Quality Monitoring System

Pharmaceutical companies should plan and execute a system for the monitoring of process performance and product quality to ensure a state of control is maintained. An effective monitoring system provides assurance of the continued capability of processes and controls to produce a product of desired quality and to identify areas for continual improvement.

The process performance and product quality monitoring system should:

  1. Use quality risk management to establish the control strategy. This can include parameters and attributes related to drug substance and drug product materials and components, facility and equipment operating conditions, in-process controls, finished product specifications, and the associated methods and frequency of monitoring and control. The control strategy should facilitate timely feedback/feedforward and appropriate corrective action and preventive action;
  2. Provide the tools for measurement and analysis of parameters and attributes identified in the control strategy (e.g., data management and statistical tools);
  3. Analyse parameters and attributes identified in the control strategy to verify continued operation within a state of control;
  4. Identify sources of variation affecting process performance and product quality for potential continual improvement activities to reduce or control variation;
  5. Include feedback on product quality from both internal and external sources, e.g., complaints, product rejections, non-conformances, recalls, deviations, audits and regulatory inspections and findings;
  6. Provide knowledge to enhance process understanding, enrich the design space (where established), and enable innovative approaches to process validation.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

比喻說明：PPQMS 是製程的「健康監控儀器」

想像一個病人戴著連續血壓計和血糖監測器。每隔幾分鐘測一次數值，數值在正常範圍內（state of control）就繼續；若開始偏高（趨勢警報），醫生介入調整（feedforward），還沒病發但已預先行動；若數值突然異常（out of control），立即告警啟動 CAPA。

PPQMS 對製程就是這樣的角色：

  • 血壓/血糖 = CQA 和 CPP（關鍵品質屬性和製程參數）
  • 正常範圍 = state of control（管控狀態）
  • 趨勢預警 = feedforward（前饋控制）
  • 突發異常 = trigger for CAPA（觸發矯正預防行動）
  • 長期健康報告 = Management Review（管理審查）

核心概念：Control Strategy（控制策略）

Q10 Glossary 定義：「A planned set of controls, derived from current product and process understanding, that assures process performance and product quality.」

控制策略的組成要素（a 項）：

  • 原料藥和製劑原料與組分的參數和屬性
  • 設施和設備操作條件
  • 製程中控制（IPC, In-Process Controls）
  • 成品規格
  • 各項監測的方法和頻率

關鍵：控制策略必須「facilitate timely feedback/feedforward」——即時回饋和前饋，不是事後補救，而是主動預測和管理。

重點提示：(f) 項的法規戰略意義

PPQMS 的 (f) 項說：監測系統產生的知識可以「enrich the design space（豐富設計空間），並支持創新的製程確效方法」。

這意味著商業製造階段積累的真實批次數據，可以用來：

  • 擴大或調整原有的 Design Space（需要法規申請）
  • 支持 Continued Process Verification（持續製程查驗）的數據包
  • 為 Real-Time Release Testing (RTRT) 提供歷史支撐

這是 Q10 PPQMS 與 FDA 製程確效 Stage 3（Continued Process Verification）的直接連接點。

CDMO 實務應用 Practical Application

CDMO 通常同時生產數十個客戶的產品。PPQMS 的挑戰在於：如何對不同複雜程度的產品設計比例適當的監測系統？

  • 高風險產品（如凍乾注射劑）：每批詳細 CPP 趨勢分析、SPC 管制圖、定期 Cpk 計算
  • 中風險產品（如口服固體製劑）：關鍵步驟 IPC 趨勢 + 每季批次回顧
  • 低風險產品：年度統計分析即可

比例原則（proportionate approach）是 Q10 的精神——並非所有產品都要同等深度監測，用 QRM 決定監測強度。

3.2.2 Corrective Action and Preventive Action (CAPA) System｜矯正措施與預防措施系統

原文內容 Original Text

3.2.2 Corrective Action and Preventive Action (CAPA) System

The pharmaceutical company should have a system for implementing corrective actions and preventive actions resulting from the investigation of complaints, product rejections, non-conformances, recalls, deviations, audits, regulatory inspections and findings, and trends from process performance and product quality monitoring.

A structured approach to the investigation process should be used with the objective of determining the root cause. The level of effort, formality, and documentation of the investigation should be commensurate with the level of risk, in line with ICH Q9.

CAPA methodology should result in product and process improvements and enhanced product and process understanding.

重點提示：CAPA 最常見的缺失（稽核發現）

  • 根本原因分析停於表象：「操作員疏忽」不是根本原因，為什麼系統允許疏忽發生才是。
  • CA 與 PA 混用或缺失 PA：很多公司只做 CA（防止再發），忽略用趨勢數據主動識別類似風險並做 PA。
  • 有效性驗證（Effectiveness Check）未執行：Q10 明確要求「effectiveness of the actions should be evaluated」——沒有驗證等同 CAPA 未關閉。
  • CAPA 觸發條件太窄：Q10 的觸發清單包含 complaints、rejections、non-conformances、recalls、deviations、audits、inspections 以及趨勢——趨勢觸發 CAPA（proactive CAPA）是大多數公司的弱點。

PPQMS → CAPA → Change Mgmt 的互動關係

三個系統是緊密互聯的，不能孤立運作：

  • PPQMS 發現製程偏移趨勢 → 觸發 CAPA 調查
  • CAPA 的根本原因導致製程需要修改 → 觸發 Change Management
  • 所有三個系統的結果 → 彙整至 Management Review
  • Management Review 的決策 → 反饋指導三個系統的優先序

這個循環正是 Q10 的 PDCA（Plan-Do-Check-Act）在品質管理系統層面的具體體現。

深度解析：Correction vs. Corrective Action vs. Preventive Action

這是製藥界最常混淆的三個概念，明確區分是 CAPA 系統的基礎：

ISO 9000:2005 定義備注：「Corrective action is taken to prevent recurrence whereas preventive action is taken to prevent occurrence.」這一句話是考試和稽核中最常被引用的區分依據。

3.2.3 Change Management System｜變更管理系統

原文內容 Original Text

3.2.3 Change Management System

Innovation, continual improvement, the outputs of process performance and product quality monitoring and CAPA drive change. In order to evaluate, approve and implement these changes properly, a company should have an effective change management system.

There is generally a difference in formality of change management processes prior to the initial regulatory submission and after submission, where changes to the regulatory filing might be required under regional requirements.

The change management system ensures continual improvement is undertaken in a timely and effective manner. It should provide a high degree of assurance there are no unintended consequences of the change.

The change management system should include the following, as appropriate for the stage of the lifecycle:

  1. Quality risk management should be utilised to evaluate proposed changes. The level of effort and formality of the evaluation should be commensurate with the level of risk;
  2. Proposed changes should be evaluated relative to the marketing authorisation, including design space, where established, and/or current product and process understanding. There should be an assessment to determine whether a change to the regulatory filing is required under regional requirements. As stated in ICH Q8, working within the design space is not considered a change (from a regulatory filing perspective). However, from a pharmaceutical quality system standpoint, all changes should be evaluated by a company's change management system;
  3. Proposed changes should be evaluated by expert teams contributing the appropriate expertise and knowledge from relevant areas (e.g., Pharmaceutical Development, Manufacturing, Quality, Regulatory Affairs and Medical), to ensure the change is technically justified. Prospective evaluation criteria for a proposed change should be set;
  4. After implementation, an evaluation of the change should be undertaken to confirm the change objectives were achieved and that there was no deleterious impact on product quality.

重點提示：「Prospective Evaluation Criteria」的重要性

(c) 項要求在實施前就設定「前瞻性評估標準」。這意味著：

  • 在變更實施前，就要預先定義「我如何知道這個變更成功了？」
  • 避免事後用「確認偏誤（confirmation bias）」解讀結果
  • 常見指標：規格測試通過率、製程能力指數（Cpk）、溶出曲線相似度等

這個要求與 FDA 製程確效指引（2011）中的 Stage 2 Protocol 精神一致——驗收標準要事先設定，不能看了數據再訂。

CDMO 實務應用 Practical Application

CDMO 面臨的特殊挑戰：同一個變更（如設備更換）可能同時影響多個客戶的產品，每個客戶對應不同地區的法規要求。

  • 共用設備更換：需同時評估所有使用該設備的產品，對每個產品分別做風險評估，向各自法規機關按要求申報
  • 廠址變更：涉及 Site Transfer，通常是 FDA Major Supplement + EMA Type II variation，時間和費用都是重大成本
  • Design Space 帶來的效率：有 Design Space 的客戶產品，在 Design Space 內的設備參數調整不需通知法規，節省大量時間和費用——這是 CDMO 協助客戶建立 Design Space 的長期回報

3.2.4 Management Review of Process Performance and Product Quality｜製程績效與產品品質的管理審查

原文內容 Original Text

3.2.4 Management Review of Process Performance and Product Quality

Management review should provide assurance that process performance and product quality are managed over the lifecycle. Depending on the size and complexity of the company, management review can be a series of reviews at various levels of management and should include a timely and effective communication and escalation process to raise appropriate quality issues to senior levels of management for review.

(a) The management review system should include:

  1. The results of regulatory inspections and findings, audits and other assessments, and commitments made to regulatory authorities;
  2. Periodic quality reviews, that can include:
    1. Measures of customer satisfaction such as product quality complaints and recalls;
    2. Conclusions of process performance and product quality monitoring;
    3. The effectiveness of process and product changes including those arising from corrective action and preventive actions.
  3. Any follow-up actions from previous management reviews.

(b) The management review system should identify appropriate actions, such as:

  1. Improvements to manufacturing processes and products;
  2. Provision, training and/or realignment of resources;
  3. Capture and dissemination of knowledge.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：Management Review 是四大要素的「整合總結」

Management Review 在結構上是前三個要素（PPQMS、CAPA、Change Management）的資訊匯集點：

  • PPQMS 的監測結論 → 進入 Management Review 的 (a)(2)(ii)
  • CAPA 的有效性評估 → 進入 Management Review 的 (a)(2)(iii)
  • Change Management 的結果 → 進入 Management Review 的 (a)(2)(iii)
  • 法規檢查發現 + 稽核結果 → 進入 Management Review 的 (a)(1)

Q10 強調「timely and effective communication and escalation process」——管理審查不是年度一次性形式，而是一個持續運行的升級機制。

CDMO 實務應用：APR/PQR 就是 Management Review 的具體落地

Annual Product Review (APR) / Product Quality Review (PQR)——這是全球 GMP 要求的年度產品品質報告，也是 Q10 Management Review（3.2.4）+ PQS Management Review（4.1）在實務中最直接的體現。

一份完整的 APR/PQR 應涵蓋（對應 Q10 審查內容）：

  • 批次合格率統計（← PPQMS 結論）
  • OOS/偏差趨勢分析（← PPQMS + CAPA）
  • CAPA 完成狀態與有效性（← CAPA 系統）
  • 年度變更清單與影響評估（← Change Management）
  • 穩定性數據趨勢（← PPQMS）
  • 客訴和召回數據（← 外部回饋）
  • 法規檢查發現和承諾事項狀態（← 3.2.4(a)(1)）
  • 供應商品質績效（← PPQMS 擴展）
  • 下一年度改善行動計畫（← 3.2.4(b)）

對 CDMO 而言，高品質的 APR/PQR 是向客戶展示品質管理能力的重要界面，也是客戶自身向法規機關展示製造受控的依據。

重點提示：「各層級管理審查」的設計

Q10 允許 Management Review 是「a series of reviews at various levels」——大公司通常設計多層次審查：

  • 操作層（每批/每週）：班組長審查批次記錄、IPC 數據
  • 部門層（每月）：QA 月報、偏差/CAPA 狀態
  • 廠區層（每季）：產品品質趨勢、重大事件回顧
  • 公司層（每年）：APR/PQR 彙整、高層管理決策（資源配置、策略調整）

關鍵：每一層的審查結果，必須有「escalation pathway」——確保重要品質問題能及時上達決策層，這正是 Q10 Section 4 的核心精神。

Section 4: Continual Improvement of the PQS｜品質系統本身的持續改善

原文內容 Original Text

4. CONTINUAL IMPROVEMENT OF THE PHARMACEUTICAL QUALITY SYSTEM

This section describes activities that should be conducted to manage and continually improve the pharmaceutical quality system.

4.1 Management Review of the Pharmaceutical Quality System

Management should have a formal process for reviewing the pharmaceutical quality system on a periodic basis. The review should include:

  1. Measurement of achievement of pharmaceutical quality system objectives;
  2. Assessment of performance indicators that can be used to monitor the effectiveness of processes within the pharmaceutical quality system, such as:
    1. Complaint, deviation, CAPA and change management processes;
    2. Feedback on outsourced activities;
    3. Self-assessment processes including risk assessments, trending, and audits;
    4. External assessments such as regulatory inspections and findings and customer audits.

4.2 Monitoring of Internal and External Factors Impacting the Pharmaceutical Quality System

Factors monitored by management can include:

  1. Emerging regulations, guidance and quality issues that can impact the Pharmaceutical Quality System;
  2. Innovations that might enhance the pharmaceutical quality system;
  3. Changes in business environment and objectives;
  4. Changes in product ownership.

4.3 Outcomes of Management Review and Monitoring

The outcome of management review of the pharmaceutical quality system and monitoring of internal and external factors can include:

  1. Improvements to the pharmaceutical quality system and related processes;
  2. Allocation or reallocation of resources and/or personnel training;
  3. Revisions to quality policy and quality objectives;
  4. Documentation and timely and effective communication of the results of the management review and actions, including escalation of appropriate issues to senior management.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：Section 3 vs. Section 4 的區別

兩個 Section 都涉及「Management Review」，但層次不同：

  • Section 3.2.4：審查個別「產品」的製程績效和品質狀態（product-level review）——這批產品的 PPQMS 結果如何、CAPA 有效嗎、變更有沒有達到預期？
  • Section 4.1：審查整個「PQS 系統」本身的健全性（system-level review）——CAPA 系統運作是否有效率？Change Management 流程是否能處理各種複雜度的變更？我們的整體品質政策是否仍然適當？

這是「改善產品品質」與「改善品質系統本身」的差異——Section 4 是 PQS 的 meta-layer（元層次）。

比喻說明：PQS 的「年度體檢」

Section 3.2.4 = 監測每個病人的血壓/血糖（產品層面的健康狀態）

Section 4.1 = 評估整個醫院的醫療系統是否運作良好（系統層面）：

  • 醫生（品質人員）的人力夠嗎？
  • 診斷流程（調查和 CAPA）是否有效率？
  • 醫院設備（監測工具）是否跟上最新技術？
  • 有沒有新的法規（新藥法/新 GMP 指引）要求醫院改變流程？

把醫院換成製藥廠，問題完全對應——Section 4 就是對「品質管理系統這個醫院」做年度體檢。

重點提示：4.2 外部因素監控的現實意義

Q10 要求主動監控「外部因素」，對製藥公司的具體意涵：

  • (a) Emerging regulations：FDA 發布新的 Process Validation Guidance（如 2011 年版）、EMA 新增 GMP Annex 修訂——品質系統必須響應更新
  • (b) Innovations：ICH Q12（Life Cycle Management）通過後，需評估 Change Management 系統是否能利用 PAC（Post-Approval Change Management Protocol）帶來的新彈性
  • (c) Business environment changes：公司被收購、新市場進入、產品組合擴大——PQS 的資源配置和流程設計需要跟著調整
  • (d) Product ownership changes：產品授權外移或轉讓給 CDMO——需要特別的 PQS 介面管理

CDMO 實務應用：APR 作為系統性改善工具

在 CDMO 情境下，Section 4 的 PQS 管理審查通常透過兩個機制實現：

  • Site Quality Management Review（廠區品質管理審查）：半年或每年一次，由廠長/品質長主持，審查全廠的 CAPA 完成率、偏差趨勢、稽核發現、法規 commitment 狀態、資源充足性
  • Quality System Metrics Dashboard（品質系統指標儀表板）：將 4.1(b) 要求的 performance indicators 視覺化，讓管理層每月都能看到 PQS 整體健康狀態

對客戶而言，他們有權利要求 CDMO 提供廠區層面的 Quality System Review 結果摘要，作為 oversight（監督）的一部分——Q10 Section 2.7（委外活動管理）與此直接相關。

情境練習 Scenario-Based Practice Questions

  1. PPQMS 觸發：你是 CDMO 的品質工程師，發現過去 6 個月某一注射劑的溶液可見異物不合格率從 0.1% 緩慢上升至 0.8%（仍在規格內，但趨勢持續）。根據 Q10 PPQMS 的精神，你應該採取什麼行動？這是否需要啟動 CAPA？
  2. CAPA 區分：一批口服錠劑因片重超出規格被拒絕。初步原因是加料斗感測器積粉導致讀值失準。請分別說明這個事件的 Correction、Corrective Action 和 Preventive Action 各應包含什麼內容？
  3. Change Management 雙軌：你的客戶有一個已批准的 Design Space，規定混合時間 15–45 分鐘。生產部希望將混合時間從 30 分鐘縮短至 20 分鐘以提高產能。(a) 這個變更需要通知法規機關嗎？(b) 需要通過 Change Management 系統嗎？(c) 如果客戶沒有 Design Space，答案有何不同？
  4. Management Review 設計：你是一家中型 CDMO 的 QA Director，需要設計 Q10 合規的 Management Review 架構。請建議三層次的審查結構，並說明每層應審查哪些內容、頻率為何、以及如何確保問題能有效升級到高層管理？

本章重點回顧 Section Summary

  • 生命週期四階段：開發 → 技術轉移 → 商業製造 → 停產；Q10 要求四大 PQS 要素在每個階段以適當且相稱的方式運作
  • PPQMS：以 QRM 建立控制策略，持續監測製程績效和產品品質，識別變異來源，維持管控狀態（state of control）並提供持續改善的知識基礎
  • CAPA：三層概念區分——Correction（修正本次事件）、Corrective Action（消除再發原因）、Preventive Action（消除潛在發生原因）；調查深度與風險等級相稱；必須驗證有效性
  • Change Management：法規雙軌——Design Space 內的變更不需法規申報，但仍需公司內部 Change Management；Design Space 外或影響核准文件的變更需依地區要求申報；實施後必須驗證目標達成
  • Management Review（3.2.4）：整合 PPQMS、CAPA、Change Management 結果的產品層面審查，APR/PQR 是其最直接的實務落地
  • Section 4 — PQS 持續改善：PQS 系統本身也需要被審查和改善（系統層面的 PDCA）；監控外部因素（新法規、科技進步、業務變化）確保 PQS 持續適切有效
  • 四要素的互聯性：PPQMS → CAPA → Change Management → Management Review 形成一個完整的閉環——這個閉環，就是 Q10 相較於基本 GMP 的核心附加價值
