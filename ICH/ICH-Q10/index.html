執行摘要 Executive Summary

ICH Q10 是製藥產業質量管理的「憲法」，定義了一套貫穿產品全生命週期的 Pharmaceutical Quality System (PQS, 藥品質量系統) 模型。它整合了 ISO 質量管理概念、GMP 法規要求，並與 Q8（藥品開發）和 Q9（質量風險管理）形成完整的「質量三部曲」。

三大核心目標

Product Realisation（產品實現）、State of Control（控制狀態）、Continual Improvement（持續改進）- 這三個目標構成 PQS 的基本價值主張。

四大 PQS 要素

Process Performance Monitoring（製程績效監控）、CAPA（矯正預防措施）、Change Management（變更管理）、Management Review（管理審查）- 支撐質量系統運作的四根支柱。

兩大賦能工具

Knowledge Management（知識管理）和 Quality Risk Management（質量風險管理）- 貫穿全生命週期的關鍵使能因素，連結 Q8 和 Q9 的核心精神。

四階段生命週期

Pharmaceutical Development（藥品開發）→ Technology Transfer（技術轉移）→ Commercial Manufacturing（商業製造）→ Product Discontinuation（產品停產）- PQS 必須適當且成比例地應用於每個階段。

學習目標 Learning Objectives

1
理解 PQS 模型與 GMP、ISO 9001 的關係，以及 Q8/Q9/Q10「質量三部曲」如何整合運作
2
掌握三大 ICH Q10 目標的實務意涵：Product Realisation、State of Control、Continual Improvement
3
學會應用四大 PQS 要素於產品生命週期各階段，建立有效的質量管理機制
4
理解 Knowledge Management 和 Quality Risk Management 作為 Enablers 的角色
5
認識 Management Responsibility 在建立與維護 PQS 中的關鍵作用
6
了解 ICH Q10 如何為科學與風險導向的法規策略創造機會（Annex 1）

1. Pharmaceutical Quality System - 藥品質量系統
📋 原文 Original Text

1.1 Introduction

This document establishes a new ICH tripartite guideline describing a model for an effective quality management system for the pharmaceutical industry, referred to as the Pharmaceutical Quality System.

ICH Q10 describes one comprehensive model for an effective pharmaceutical quality system that is based on International Standards Organisation (ISO) quality concepts, includes applicable Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP) regulations and complements ICH Q8 "Pharmaceutical Development" and ICH Q9 "Quality Risk Management".

ICH Q10 is a model for a pharmaceutical quality system that can be implemented throughout the different stages of a product lifecycle. Much of the content of ICH Q10 applicable to manufacturing sites is currently specified by regional GMP requirements. ICH Q10 is not intended to create any new expectations beyond current regulatory requirements. Consequently, the content of ICH Q10 that is additional to current regional GMP requirements is optional.

ICH Q10 demonstrates industry and regulatory authorities' support of an effective pharmaceutical quality system to enhance the quality and availability of medicines around the world in the interest of public health. Implementation of ICH Q10 throughout the product lifecycle should facilitate innovation and continual improvement and strengthen the link between pharmaceutical development and manufacturing activities.

💡 解析 Commentary
📚 核心術語解析
  • Pharmaceutical Quality System (PQS, 藥品質量系統)：指導和控制製藥公司質量相關活動的管理系統。這不只是品管部門的事，而是整個公司的運作框架。
  • Product Lifecycle（產品生命週期）：從藥品開發到停產的完整旅程，包括開發、技術轉移、商業製造、產品停產四個階段。
🎯 比喻說明

把 Q10 想成一間高級餐廳的「經營管理系統」：

  • GMP 是廚房的衛生標準和烹飪 SOP - 告訴你「怎麼做菜才合格」
  • Q8 是食譜研發過程 - 研究如何設計出好吃又能穩定製作的菜色
  • Q9 是風險評估 - 分析哪些食材容易出問題、哪些步驟最關鍵
  • Q10 則是整間餐廳的管理哲學 - 從食材採購、廚師培訓、客訴處理到持續精進，確保每一道菜都能維持水準
⚠️ 重點提示

關鍵句："ICH Q10 is not intended to create any new expectations beyond current regulatory requirements"

這句話非常重要！Q10 是「增強」而非「取代」GMP。對於已經符合 GMP 的工廠，Q10 額外的內容是可選的（optional），但實施它能帶來更好的質量保證和法規優勢。

🔗 CMC 連結

作為 CMC 專業人員，你已經熟悉 GMP 的各種要求。Q10 幫助你理解：

  • 為什麼要做這些 GMP 活動（不只是「法規要求」）
  • 如何將開發知識（Q8）有效轉移到製造
  • 如何用風險思維（Q9）優先處理問題
  • 如何建立讓產品質量持續進步的機制

1.2 Scope

This guideline applies to the systems supporting the development and manufacture of pharmaceutical drug substances (i.e., API) and drug products, including biotechnology and biological products, throughout the product lifecycle.

The elements of ICH Q10 should be applied in a manner that is appropriate and proportionate to each of the product lifecycle stages, recognising the differences among, and the different goals of each stage.

For the purposes of this guideline, the product lifecycle includes the following technical activities for new and existing products:

Pharmaceutical Development:

  • Drug substance development
  • Formulation development (including container/closure system)
  • Manufacture of investigational products
  • Delivery system development (where relevant)
  • Manufacturing process development and scale-up
  • Analytical method development

Technology Transfer:

  • New product transfers during Development through Manufacturing
  • Transfers within or between manufacturing and testing sites for marketed products

Commercial Manufacturing:

  • Acquisition and control of materials
  • Provision of facilities, utilities, and equipment
  • Production (including packaging and labelling)
  • Quality control and assurance
  • Release; Storage; Distribution (excluding wholesaler activities)

Product Discontinuation:

  • Retention of documentation
  • Sample retention
  • Continued product assessment and reporting
💡 解析 Commentary
📚 核心術語解析
  • Drug Substance (API, 原料藥)：具有藥理活性的化學物質，是藥品的「有效成分」
  • Drug Product (製劑)：最終給病人使用的藥品形式（錠劑、注射液等）
  • Appropriate and Proportionate（適當且成比例）：Q10 的核心哲學 - 不是一體適用，而是根據風險和階段調整力度
🎯 比喻說明：產品生命週期就像人的一生
  • Pharmaceutical Development = 懷孕期 + 嬰幼兒期：探索、學習、建立基礎能力
  • Technology Transfer = 青少年轉大人：將所學應用到真實世界，但還需要指導
  • Commercial Manufacturing = 職場生涯：穩定產出、持續精進、處理各種挑戰
  • Product Discontinuation = 退休安排：妥善交接、保存記錄、善後責任
🔗 CMC 連結

對應到你熟悉的 CTD Module 3：

  • Drug Substance Development → 3.2.S.2 (Manufacture), 3.2.S.4 (Control)
  • Drug Product Development → 3.2.P.2 (Pharmaceutical Development)
  • Manufacturing Process → 3.2.P.3 (Manufacture)
  • 這些 CTD 章節描述的「是什麼」，Q10 補充說明「該怎麼管理」
⚠️ 重點提示

"Appropriate and proportionate" 是 Q10 最重要的設計原則：

  • 早期開發階段：文件要求較寬鬆，鼓勵探索創新
  • 技術轉移階段：開始建立正式程序，但保持彈性
  • 商業製造階段：完整的 GMP 合規要求
  • 停產階段：維持必要的文件和樣品保存

1.3 Relationship of ICH Q10 to Regional GMP Requirements, ISO Standards and ICH Q7

Regional GMP requirements, the ICH Q7 Guideline, "Good Manufacturing Practice Guide for Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients", and ISO quality management system guidelines form the foundation for ICH Q10. To meet the objectives described below, ICH Q10 augments GMPs by describing specific quality system elements and management responsibilities. ICH Q10 provides a harmonised model for a pharmaceutical quality system throughout the lifecycle of a product and is intended to be used together with regional GMP requirements.

The regional GMPs do not explicitly address all stages of the product lifecycle (e.g., Development). The quality system elements and management responsibilities described in this guideline are intended to encourage the use of science and risk based approaches at each lifecycle stage, thereby promoting continual improvement across the entire product lifecycle.

💡 解析 Commentary
🎯 比喻說明：建築法規的層次

想像蓋房子的法規體系：

  • 建築法規（GMP）：必須遵守的最低標準 - 結構安全、消防設施
  • ISO 9001：品質管理通用框架 - 適用於任何產業
  • ICH Q7：API 專用的 GMP - 像是「化學工廠專用建築規範」
  • ICH Q10：整體營運管理哲學 - 像是「如何經營一間永續的建設公司」
⚠️ 重點提示

Science and Risk Based Approaches（科學與風險導向方法）是「質量三部曲」的共同精神：

  • Q8：用科學理解產品和製程（Design Space 概念）
  • Q9：用風險思維決定優先順序和控制力度
  • Q10：將科學和風險思維融入日常管理決策

1.4 Relationship of ICH Q10 to Regulatory Approaches

Regulatory approaches for a specific product or manufacturing facility should be commensurate with the level of product and process understanding, the results of quality risk management, and the effectiveness of the pharmaceutical quality system.

When implemented, the effectiveness of the pharmaceutical quality system can normally be evaluated during a regulatory inspection at the manufacturing site. Potential opportunities to enhance science and risk based regulatory approaches are identified in Annex 1. Regulatory processes will be determined by region.

💡 解析 Commentary
🔗 CMC 連結：法規優勢

這段話暗示了一個重要訊息：如果你能證明有良好的 PQS，可能獲得法規「優待」

  • 更少的查廠次數（risk-based inspection）
  • 更快的變更審批（post-approval changes）
  • 更靈活的驗證策略（process validation approaches）
  • 即時放行測試的可能性（real-time release testing）

這就像是「優良駕駛可以降低保險費」的概念。

⚠️ 重點提示

Annex 1 是非常重要的附錄，它列出了四種情境下可能獲得的法規優勢。這是 Q10 的「胡蘿蔔」- 提供誘因讓業界主動提升質量系統。

1.5 ICH Q10 Objectives

Implementation of the Q10 model should result in achievement of three main objectives which complement or enhance regional GMP requirements.

1.5.1 Achieve Product Realisation

To establish, implement and maintain a system that allows the delivery of products with the quality attributes appropriate to meet the needs of patients, health care professionals, regulatory authorities (including compliance with approved regulatory filings) and other internal and external customers.

1.5.2 Establish and Maintain a State of Control

To develop and use effective monitoring and control systems for process performance and product quality, thereby providing assurance of continued suitability and capability of processes. Quality risk management can be useful in identifying the monitoring and control systems.

1.5.3 Facilitate Continual Improvement

To identify and implement appropriate product quality improvements, process improvements, variability reduction, innovations and pharmaceutical quality system enhancements, thereby increasing the ability to fulfil quality needs consistently. Quality risk management can be useful for identifying and prioritising areas for continual improvement.

💡 解析 Commentary
📚 三大目標深度解析
  • Product Realisation（產品實現）：確保每一批產品都符合預期品質。這是「做出對的產品」。
  • State of Control（控制狀態）：確保製程持續穩定運作。這是「讓製程可預測、可控制」。
  • Continual Improvement（持續改進）：不斷精進，減少變異。這是「讓明天比今天更好」。
🎯 比喻說明：開車的三個境界
  • Product Realisation = 能夠安全抵達目的地（基本功能達成）
  • State of Control = 知道車子的狀況、掌控路況（隨時知道發生什麼事）
  • Continual Improvement = 持續優化駕駛技術、保養車輛（越開越順、越省油）
🔗 CMC 連結

這三個目標如何體現在你的日常工作：

  • Product Realisation：Batch Record 審核、CoA 放行、規格制定
  • State of Control：CPV（持續製程驗證）、Trend Analysis、環境監控
  • Continual Improvement：APR/PQR、CAPA 有效性評估、製程優化專案

1.6 Enablers: Knowledge Management and Quality Risk Management

Use of knowledge management and quality risk management will enable a company to implement ICH Q10 effectively and successfully. These enablers will facilitate achievement of the objectives described in Section 1.5 above by providing the means for science and risk based decisions related to product quality.

1.6.1 Knowledge Management

Product and process knowledge should be managed from development through the commercial life of the product up to and including product discontinuation. For example, development activities using scientific approaches provide knowledge for product and process understanding. Knowledge management is a systematic approach to acquiring, analysing, storing and disseminating information related to products, manufacturing processes and components.

Sources of knowledge include, but are not limited to prior knowledge (public domain or internally documented); pharmaceutical development studies; technology transfer activities; process validation studies over the product lifecycle; manufacturing experience; innovation; continual improvement; and change management activities.

1.6.2 Quality Risk Management

Quality risk management is integral to an effective pharmaceutical quality system. It can provide a proactive approach to identifying, scientifically evaluating and controlling potential risks to quality. It facilitates continual improvement of process performance and product quality throughout the product lifecycle. ICH Q9 provides principles and examples of tools for quality risk management that can be applied to different aspects of pharmaceutical quality.

💡 解析 Commentary
📚 核心術語解析
  • Knowledge Management (KM, 知識管理)：系統性地收集、分析、儲存和傳播產品與製程相關知識
  • Quality Risk Management (QRM, 質量風險管理)：識別、評估和控制可能影響品質的風險
  • Enabler（賦能工具）：不是目標本身，而是達成目標的手段
🎯 比喻說明：飛行員的兩個好幫手

想像你是飛行員：

  • Knowledge Management = 飛行手冊 + 經驗傳承：記錄每次飛行的學習、分享最佳實務、建立知識庫讓新人能快速上手
  • Quality Risk Management = 飛行前檢查 + 風險評估：起飛前檢查天氣、機械狀況，飛行中監控異常，根據風險調整航線

兩者結合，才能安全且有效率地完成每趟飛行。

🔗 CMC 連結

Knowledge Management 實務應用：

  • 技術轉移時的「Knowledge Transfer Package」
  • 製程開發報告（Process Development Report）的撰寫與傳承
  • 偏差調查時參考歷史資料（Prior Knowledge）
  • APR/PQR 中的趨勢分析累積知識

Quality Risk Management 連結 Q9：

  • FMEA 用於製程風險評估
  • Risk Ranking 決定變更審核層級
  • Risk-based 的驗證範圍決定
⚠️ 重點提示

KM 和 QRM 是「雙引擎」：

  • KM 提供「我們知道什麼」→ 決策的知識基礎
  • QRM 提供「我們該擔心什麼」→ 決策的優先順序
  • 兩者結合 = 科學且風險導向的決策

1.7 Design and Content Considerations

(a) The design, organisation and documentation of the pharmaceutical quality system should be well structured and clear to facilitate common understanding and consistent application.

(b) The elements of ICH Q10 should be applied in a manner that is appropriate and proportionate to each of the product lifecycle stages, recognising the different goals and knowledge available for each stage.

(c) The size and complexity of the company's activities should be taken into consideration when developing a new pharmaceutical quality system or modifying an existing one. The design of the pharmaceutical quality system should incorporate appropriate risk management principles. While some aspects of the pharmaceutical quality system can be company-wide and others site-specific, the effectiveness of the pharmaceutical quality system is normally demonstrated at the site level.

(d) The pharmaceutical quality system should include appropriate processes, resources and responsibilities to provide assurance of the quality of outsourced activities and purchased materials as described in Section 2.7.

(e) Management responsibilities, as described in Section 2, should be identified within the pharmaceutical quality system.

(f) The pharmaceutical quality system should include the following elements, as described in Section 3: process performance and product quality monitoring, corrective and preventive action, change management and management review.

(g) Performance indicators, as described in Section 4, should be identified and used to monitor the effectiveness of processes within the pharmaceutical quality system.

1.8 Quality Manual

A Quality Manual or equivalent documentation approach should be established and should contain the description of the pharmaceutical quality system. The description should include:

(a) The quality policy (see Section 2);

(b) The scope of the pharmaceutical quality system;

(c) Identification of the pharmaceutical quality system processes, as well as their sequences, linkages and interdependencies. Process maps and flow charts can be useful tools to facilitate depicting pharmaceutical quality system processes in a visual manner;

(d) Management responsibilities within the pharmaceutical quality system (see Section 2).

💡 解析 Commentary
📚 核心術語解析
  • Quality Manual（品質手冊）：描述整個 PQS 架構的最高層級文件，相當於公司質量管理的「憲法」
  • Performance Indicators（績效指標）：用來量化評估 PQS 有效性的可測量數值，也稱為 KPIs 或 Metrics
🎯 比喻說明：公司組織章程

Quality Manual 就像公司的「組織章程」或「員工手冊總綱」：

  • 說明公司的質量願景和政策（我們追求什麼）
  • 定義各部門的權責（誰負責什麼）
  • 描述主要流程如何互動（事情怎麼運作）
  • 不需要寫得像 SOP 那麼細節，但要讓人看懂整體架構
🔗 CMC 連結

常見的 Performance Indicators（績效指標）：

  • Batch Success Rate（批次合格率）
  • Right First Time（一次做對率）
  • Deviation Rate（偏差率）
  • CAPA On-time Closure Rate（CAPA 準時結案率）
  • Change Control Cycle Time（變更控制週期時間）
  • Complaint Rate（客訴率）
  • OOS Rate（超標率）
⚠️ 重點提示

關於「Appropriate and Proportionate」的實務意涵：

  • 小公司不需要和大藥廠一樣複雜的系統
  • 新創公司和成熟公司的 PQS 成熟度可以不同
  • 不同產品線可以有不同程度的控制（根據風險）
  • 關鍵是要能證明你的選擇是合理的（Justification）
2. Management Responsibility - 管理責任
📋 原文 Original Text

Leadership is essential to establish and maintain a company-wide commitment to quality and for the performance of the pharmaceutical quality system.

2.1 Management Commitment

(a) Senior management has the ultimate responsibility to ensure an effective pharmaceutical quality system is in place to achieve the quality objectives, and that roles, responsibilities, and authorities are defined, communicated and implemented throughout the company.

(b) Management should:

(1) Participate in the design, implementation, monitoring and maintenance of an effective pharmaceutical quality system;

(2) Demonstrate strong and visible support for the pharmaceutical quality system and ensure its implementation throughout their organisation;

(3) Ensure a timely and effective communication and escalation process exists to raise quality issues to the appropriate levels of management;

(4) Define individual and collective roles, responsibilities, authorities and inter-relationships of all organisational units related to the pharmaceutical quality system. Ensure these interactions are communicated and understood at all levels of the organisation. An independent quality unit/structure with authority to fulfil certain pharmaceutical quality system responsibilities is required by regional regulations;

(5) Conduct management reviews of process performance and product quality and of the pharmaceutical quality system;

(6) Advocate continual improvement;

(7) Commit appropriate resources.

💡 解析 Commentary
📚 核心術語解析
  • Senior Management（高階管理層）：有權力和責任動員公司/工廠資源的最高層級人員
  • Escalation Process（升級流程）：當問題超出基層能處理範圍時，向上呈報的機制
  • Independent Quality Unit（獨立品質單位）：QA/QC 部門，必須獨立於生產運作，有權力阻止不合格品放行
🎯 比喻說明：餐廳老闆 vs. 廚房經理

在高級餐廳中：

  • 老闆（Senior Management）不需要親自炒菜，但必須：設定餐廳定位、核准菜單、提供足夠人力和設備、定期品嚐確認品質、處理重大客訴
  • 主廚（Quality Unit）有權力：拒絕使用不合格食材、不讓品質不佳的菜出餐、即使老闆催促也能堅持標準
  • 關鍵是老闆要支持主廚的決定，而不是為了省成本或趕時間而妥協品質
⚠️ 重點提示

"Strong and visible support" 是關鍵詞：

  • 不是口頭說說，而是要看得到的行動
  • 管理層參與質量會議、審核質量報告
  • 質量問題優先於生產壓力
  • 提供足夠的資源（人力、設備、培訓預算）

稽查員會問：「高層有多投入質量管理？」

🔗 CMC 連結

常見的管理承諾問題（稽查時會被問到）：

  • 「最近的 Management Review Meeting 是什麼時候？」
  • 「當生產和品質有衝突時，如何決策？」
  • 「QA 有權力在必要時停止生產嗎？」
  • 「質量目標如何連結到員工績效評估？」

2.2 Quality Policy

(a) Senior management should establish a quality policy that describes the overall intentions and direction of the company related to quality.

(b) The quality policy should include an expectation to comply with applicable regulatory requirements and should facilitate continual improvement of the pharmaceutical quality system.

(c) The quality policy should be communicated to and understood by personnel at all levels in the company.

(d) The quality policy should be reviewed periodically for continuing effectiveness.

2.3 Quality Planning

(a) Senior management should ensure the quality objectives needed to implement the quality policy are defined and communicated.

(b) Quality objectives should be supported by all relevant levels of the company.

(c) Quality objectives should align with the company's strategies and be consistent with the quality policy.

(d) Management should provide the appropriate resources and training to achieve the quality objectives.

(e) Performance indicators that measure progress against quality objectives should be established, monitored, communicated regularly and acted upon as appropriate as described in Section 4.1 of this document.

💡 解析 Commentary
📚 核心術語解析
  • Quality Policy（質量政策）：高層正式表達的質量總方向和意圖，通常是一段簡潔的聲明
  • Quality Objectives（質量目標）：將質量政策轉化為可衡量活動的具體目標
🎯 比喻說明：公司的願景 vs. 年度目標
  • Quality Policy = 願景聲明：「我們致力於提供病人安全、有效、高品質的藥品」
  • Quality Objectives = 年度目標：「今年批次合格率提升至 99%」、「CAPA 結案週期縮短 20%」、「零重大偏差」
  • Policy 是長期不變的方向，Objectives 是具體可量化的里程碑
🔗 CMC 連結

Quality Objectives 範例（可作為 APR/PQR 的追蹤項目）：

  • Batch Rejection Rate < 1%
  • Critical/Major Deviation Rate < 5%
  • Customer Complaint Response within 48 hours
  • CAPA On-time Closure Rate > 95%
  • Training Compliance Rate = 100%
  • Right First Time (RFT) > 98%

2.4 Resource Management

(a) Management should determine and provide adequate and appropriate resources (human, financial, materials, facilities and equipment) to implement and maintain the pharmaceutical quality system and continually improve its effectiveness.

(b) Management should ensure that resources are appropriately applied to a specific product, process or site.

2.5 Internal Communication

(a) Management should ensure appropriate communication processes are established and implemented within the organisation.

(b) Communications processes should ensure the flow of appropriate information between all levels of the company.

(c) Communication processes should ensure the appropriate and timely escalation of certain product quality and pharmaceutical quality system issues.

2.6 Management Review

(a) Senior management should be responsible for pharmaceutical quality system governance through management review to ensure its continuing suitability and effectiveness.

(b) Management should assess the conclusions of periodic reviews of process performance and product quality and of the pharmaceutical quality system, as described in Sections 3 and 4.

💡 解析 Commentary
🎯 比喻說明：企業的健康檢查

Management Review 就像人的年度健康檢查：

  • 定期進行（通常每季或每年）
  • 檢視各項「健康指標」（Performance Indicators）
  • 發現問題要有改善計劃
  • 記錄檢查結果和後續行動
  • 高層要親自「看報告」，不能只交給秘書
⚠️ 重點提示

Escalation（升級呈報）的關鍵時機：

  • OOS 無法確定原因時
  • 偏差可能影響多批次或多產品時
  • 客訴涉及病人安全時
  • 問題超出 SOP 範圍時
  • 需要跨部門資源協調時

好的升級機制 = 問題不會「卡」在基層，高層能及時掌握重要訊息

🔗 CMC 連結

Resource Management 的實務考量：

  • QC 實驗室儀器足夠嗎？有備援嗎？
  • QA 人員與生產量的配比合理嗎？
  • 員工培訓預算充足嗎？
  • IT 系統能支援電子批次記錄嗎？

2.7 Management of Outsourced Activities and Purchased Materials

The pharmaceutical quality system, including the management responsibilities described in this section, extends to the control and review of any outsourced activities and quality of purchased materials. The pharmaceutical company is ultimately responsible to ensure processes are in place to assure the control of outsourced activities and quality of purchased materials. These processes should incorporate quality risk management and include:

(a) Assessing prior to outsourcing operations or selecting material suppliers, the suitability and competence of the other party to carry out the activity or provide the material using a defined supply chain (e.g., audits, material evaluations, qualification);

(b) Defining the responsibilities and communication processes for quality-related activities of the involved parties. For outsourced activities, this should be included in a written agreement between the contract giver and contract acceptor;

(c) Monitoring and review of the performance of the contract acceptor or the quality of the material from the provider, and the identification and implementation of any needed improvements;

(d) Monitoring incoming ingredients and materials to ensure they are from approved sources using the agreed supply chain.

2.8 Management of Change in Product Ownership

When product ownership changes, (e.g., through acquisitions) management should consider the complexity of this and ensure:

(a) The ongoing responsibilities are defined for each company involved;

(b) The necessary information is transferred.

💡 解析 Commentary
📚 核心術語解析
  • Outsourced Activities（外包活動）：委託外部執行的活動，如 CMO 製造、CRO 分析測試
  • Contract Giver（委託方）：發出委託的藥廠，對產品品質負最終責任
  • Contract Acceptor（受託方）：接受委託執行工作的外部公司
  • Quality Agreement（品質協議）：雙方約定品質責任分工的正式文件
🎯 比喻說明：餐廳外包食材採購

即使餐廳請供應商送魚，如果魚有問題讓客人食物中毒：

  • 客人會找餐廳負責，不會找供應商
  • 所以餐廳必須：事先評估供應商資格、明確約定品質標準、驗收時檢查品質、定期稽核供應商
  • 製藥業也一樣：外包不能外包責任
🔗 CMC 連結

供應商/外包管理的關鍵文件：

  • Quality Agreement：定義雙方品質責任（誰做什麼、如何通報偏差、變更如何處理）
  • Supplier Audit Report：現場稽核供應商的 GMP 合規性
  • Supplier Qualification：資格認定程序和結果
  • Annual Supplier Review：每年評估供應商績效
⚠️ 重點提示

產品所有權轉移（M&A 併購情境）：

  • 藥品被收購時，所有的知識和文件都要完整移交
  • 過渡期間的責任歸屬要明確
  • 原廠的技術人員支援安排
  • 法規變更通報義務的交接
3. Continual Improvement of Process Performance and Product Quality - 製程績效與產品質量的持續改進
📋 原文 Original Text

3.1 Lifecycle Stage Goals

3.1.1 Pharmaceutical Development: The goal is to design a product and its manufacturing process to consistently deliver the intended performance.

3.1.2 Technology Transfer: The goal is to transfer product and process knowledge between development and manufacturing to achieve product realisation.

3.1.3 Commercial Manufacturing: The goals include achieving product realisation, establishing and maintaining a state of control and facilitating continual improvement.

3.1.4 Product Discontinuation: The goal is to manage the terminal stage effectively, including retention of documentation and samples and continued product assessment.

3.2 Pharmaceutical Quality System Elements

The four elements that augment regional GMP requirements are:

  • Process performance and product quality monitoring system
  • Corrective action and preventive action (CAPA) system
  • Change management system
  • Management review of process performance and product quality
💡 解析 Commentary
📚 四大 PQS 要素
  • Monitoring：「眼睛」- 監控製程和品質，及早發現問題
  • CAPA：「手」- 矯正問題並預防再發
  • Change Management：「腦」- 有系統地管理變更
  • Management Review：「指揮中心」- 高層審視整體表現
🎯 比喻：產品生命週期就像人的一生
  • Development = 懷孕期 + 嬰幼兒期：探索、學習
  • Technology Transfer = 青少年轉大人：將所學應用到真實世界
  • Commercial Manufacturing = 職場生涯：穩定產出、持續精進
  • Discontinuation = 退休安排：妥善交接、保存記錄

3.2.1 Process Performance and Product Quality Monitoring System

The system should use quality risk management to establish the control strategy, provide tools for measurement and analysis, analyse parameters to verify continued operation within a state of control, identify sources of variation, include feedback from internal and external sources (complaints, deviations, recalls), and provide knowledge to enhance process understanding.

3.2.2 Corrective Action and Preventive Action (CAPA) System

A structured approach to investigation should be used to determine the root cause. The level of effort should be commensurate with risk (ICH Q9). CAPA methodology should result in product and process improvements.

💡 解析 Commentary
🔗 CMC 連結：監控工具
  • CPV：Stage 3 製程驗證，持續監控 CPP 和 CQA
  • Trend Analysis：SPC 圖表追蹤關鍵參數
  • APR/PQR：每年彙整回顧產品品質數據
  • Stability Monitoring：上市後持續穩定性監測
⚠️ CA vs PA 的區別
  • Corrective Action：消除已發現問題的原因，防止再發
  • Preventive Action：消除潛在問題的原因，防止發生

3.2.3 Change Management System

The system should: (a) Use quality risk management to evaluate proposed changes; (b) Evaluate changes relative to the marketing authorisation and design space; (c) Involve expert teams from relevant areas; (d) Evaluate effectiveness after implementation.

As stated in ICH Q8, working within the design space is not considered a change from a regulatory filing perspective. However, all changes should be evaluated by the company's change management system.

3.2.4 Management Review of Process Performance and Product Quality

The review should include: results of regulatory inspections and audits; periodic quality reviews (customer satisfaction, monitoring conclusions, effectiveness of changes); and follow-up actions from previous reviews.

💡 解析 Commentary
🎯 比喻：裝修房子的許可
  • 不需許可（Design Space 內）：換窗簾、移家具
  • 需報備（Minor Change）：換熱水器
  • 需許可（Major Change）：拆牆改管線

關鍵：即使不需外部許可，內部也要有記錄和評估！

🔗 CMC 連結：FDA 變更分類
  • PAS：重大變更，需 FDA 批准後才能實施
  • CBE-30：中度變更，通報後 30 天可實施
  • CBE-0：低風險變更，實施同時通報
  • Annual Report：最低風險，年度彙總
4. Continual Improvement of the Pharmaceutical Quality System - PQS 的持續改進
📋 原文 Original Text

4.1 Management Review of the PQS

Management should have a formal process for reviewing the pharmaceutical quality system on a periodic basis. The review should include:

(a) Measurement of achievement of pharmaceutical quality system objectives;

(b) Assessment of performance indicators such as: complaint, deviation, CAPA and change management processes; feedback on outsourced activities; self-assessment processes including risk assessments, trending, and audits; external assessments such as regulatory inspections and customer audits.

4.2 Monitoring of Internal and External Factors

Factors monitored by management can include: emerging regulations, guidance and quality issues; innovations that might enhance the PQS; changes in business environment and objectives; changes in product ownership.

4.3 Outcomes of Management Review and Monitoring

The outcomes can include: improvements to the PQS and related processes; allocation or reallocation of resources and/or personnel training; revisions to quality policy and quality objectives; documentation and timely communication of the results and escalation of appropriate issues to senior management.

  • 發現問題要有改善計劃

練習題與解答 Practice Questions
📝 練習題 1：PQS 三大目標

問題：ICH Q10 定義了三大目標。請說明「State of Control」與「Product Realisation」的差異，並舉例說明一個工廠可能達成 Product Realisation 但尚未達到 State of Control 的情境。

顯示解答

差異：

  • Product Realisation：每批產品都符合品質規格，能交付給病人使用
  • State of Control：製程持續穩定且可預測，變異在控制範圍內，沒有趨勢性偏移

情境範例：

一間工廠過去 12 個月生產了 50 批產品，每批都通過品質測試（Product Realisation 達成）。但是：

  • Assay 結果有明顯的向下趨勢（從 99% 逐漸降到 96%）
  • 頻繁出現需要調整的偏差
  • 不同批次之間的溶離度變異很大

這種情況下，雖然每批都「過關」，但製程不穩定、不可預測，隨時可能失控。這就是「有 Product Realisation 但沒有 State of Control」。

📝 練習題 2：Knowledge Management 與 Quality Risk Management

問題：ICH Q10 將 Knowledge Management (KM) 和 Quality Risk Management (QRM) 定義為「Enablers」。請解釋為什麼這兩個工具對於實現 Continual Improvement 目標特別重要？

顯示解答

Knowledge Management 的角色：

  • 累積和傳承製程知識，避免重蹈覆轍
  • 將偏差調查的學習轉化為可用的改進依據
  • 讓新的改進建立在已有的理解之上
  • Example：透過 KM，工程師可以知道過去為什麼某個參數不能超過特定值

Quality Risk Management 的角色：

  • 識別哪些改進最有價值（風險最高的地方）
  • 優先分配有限的資源到最需要的地方
  • 評估改進方案可能帶來的新風險
  • Example：透過 QRM，決定先改善失效率最高的設備，而不是平均分配資源

兩者結合：KM 告訴我們「我們知道什麼」，QRM 告訴我們「我們該擔心什麼」。結合起來才能做出「科學且風險導向」的改進決策。

📝 練習題 3：Annex 1 的法規策略應用

問題：根據 ICH Q10 Annex 1，一間同時展現「有效的 PQS」和「深入的產品/製程理解」的公司，可以獲得哪些潛在的法規優勢？請列舉三項並說明其商業價值。

顯示解答

三項潛在法規優勢及商業價值：

  1. Risk-based Regulatory Inspections（風險導向稽查）
    • 優勢：稽查頻率降低、稽查時間縮短
    • 商業價值：減少稽查準備成本、降低生產中斷風險、減少人力投入
  2. Optimised Post-approval Change Processes（優化的上市後變更流程）
    • 優勢：某些變更可以用較簡化的程序通報
    • 商業價值：加速製程改進實施、縮短從決策到執行的時間、更快回應市場需求
  3. Real-time Release Testing（即時放行測試）
    • 優勢：用 PAT 或線上監測取代傳統 QC 測試
    • 商業價值：縮短放行時間（從數天到數小時）、減少 QC 實驗室負擔、更快供應市場

📌 關鍵要點總結 Key Takeaways

  • Q10 是框架不是清單：它提供一個整合性的質量管理模型，而不是額外的 GMP 檢查項目
  • 三大目標：Product Realisation（做出好產品）、State of Control（製程可控）、Continual Improvement（持續變好）
  • 四大要素：Monitoring、CAPA、Change Management、Management Review - 這四根支柱支撐整個 PQS
  • 兩大 Enablers：Knowledge Management 和 Quality Risk Management 是達成目標的關鍵工具
  • 生命週期思維：PQS 要「適當且成比例地」應用於開發、技術轉移、商業製造、產品停產各階段
  • 管理責任：高層的承諾和參與是 PQS 成功的基礎，不能只交給 QA 部門
  • 法規優勢：Annex 1 明確指出做好 Q10 可以獲得的法規「紅利」
  • Q8/Q9/Q10 整合：這三份指南是「質量三部曲」，要一起理解和應用
