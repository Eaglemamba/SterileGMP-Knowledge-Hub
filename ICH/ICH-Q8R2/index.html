1. Introduction 緒論
📄 Original Text 原文

1.1 Objective of the Guideline

This guideline describes the suggested contents for the 3.2.P.2 (Pharmaceutical Development) section of a regulatory submission in the ICH M4 Common Technical Document (CTD) format.

The Pharmaceutical Development section provides an opportunity to present the knowledge gained through the application of scientific approaches and quality risk management to the development of a product and its manufacturing process.

The Pharmaceutical Development section is intended to provide a comprehensive understanding of the product and manufacturing process for reviewers and inspectors.

The guideline also indicates areas where the demonstration of greater understanding of pharmaceutical and manufacturing sciences can create a basis for flexible regulatory approaches. The degree of regulatory flexibility is predicated on the level of relevant scientific knowledge provided.

1.2 Scope

This guideline is intended to provide guidance on the contents of Section 3.2.P.2 for drug products as defined in the scope of Module 3 of the Common Technical Document (ICH guideline M4).

💡 Commentary 解析
🎯 比喻說明

想像你要向餐廳投資人說明你的新菜色。P.2 就像是「研發日誌」，記錄你如何選擇食材、嘗試不同烹調方法、最後確定食譜的過程。投資人（監管機構）看的不只是最終配方，更想了解你「為什麼這樣做」。

📖 核心概念
  • CTD 3.2.P.2: Common Technical Document 的藥品開發章節，全球統一送審格式
  • Quality Risk Management (QRM): 系統性評估、控制、審查風險的方法（詳見 ICH Q9）
  • Regulatory Flexibility: 監管機構給予的操作空間，取決於展現的科學理解程度
⚠️ 重點提示

核心價值主張：展現越多科學理解 → 獲得越多法規彈性。這是「知識換取自由」的交易。

🏭 CMC連結
  • Design Space 獲批後，範圍內製程調整不需重新申報
  • 減少變更申請 = 減少法規延遲 = 更快的持續改進
  • Real-time Release Testing 可取代部分成品放行測試
2. Pharmaceutical Development 核心原則
📄 Original Text 原文

The aim of pharmaceutical development is to design a quality product and its manufacturing process to consistently deliver the intended performance of the product.

It is important to recognize that quality cannot be tested into products; i.e., quality should be built in by design.

Design space is proposed by the applicant and is subject to regulatory assessment and approval. Working within the design space is not considered as a change. Movement out of the design space is considered to be a change and would normally initiate a regulatory post approval change process.

Enhanced Approach Benefits

Opportunities exist to develop more flexible regulatory approaches:

  • risk-based regulatory decisions (reviews and inspections)
  • manufacturing process improvements within approved design space without further regulatory review
  • reduction of post-approval submissions
  • real-time quality control, leading to reduction of end-product release testing
💡 Commentary 解析

"Quality cannot be tested into products; quality should be built in by design."

— ICH Q8 核心哲學

🎯 比喻說明

蓋房子：

  • ❌ 先隨便蓋，完工後做耐震測試，不過就拆掉重來
  • ✓ 先計算結構、選對材料、設計抗震系統，確保每棟房子都耐震

傳統製藥像前者，QbD 是後者。

📖 Design Space 定義

Design Space (設計空間): 經證實能確保品質的多維度參數組合範圍。

  • 範圍內操作 = 不算變更 = 自由調整
  • 超出範圍 = 需要變更申請 = 時間成本
🏭 四大法規彈性案例
  • Risk-based inspections: FDA 對 QbD 產品更聚焦製程理解
  • 製程改進免申報: 乾燥時間從 8hr 優化至 6hr，在 Design Space 內直接實施
  • 減少變更申請: 批量放大、設備更換可能免除申報
  • RTRT: 用 NIR 線上分析取代離線測試，從 2 週縮短至即時放行
2.1 Components of the Drug Product 藥品組成
📄 Original Text 原文

2.1.1 Drug Substance 原料藥

The physicochemical and biological properties of the drug substance that can influence the performance of the drug product should be identified. Examples include: solubility, water content, particle size, crystal properties, biological activity, and permeability.

These properties could be inter-related and might need to be considered in combination.

The compatibility of the drug substance with excipients should be evaluated.

2.1.2 Excipients 賦形劑

The excipients chosen, their concentration, and characteristics that can influence drug product performance should be discussed relative to the respective function of each excipient.

This should include all substances used in manufacture, whether they appear in the finished product or not (e.g., processing aids).

💡 Commentary 解析
🎯 比喻說明
  • 原料藥 = 主食材：牛肉的部位、切法、熟成度都影響口感
  • 賦形劑 = 配料調味料：各有功能，選錯會毀掉整道菜
  • 相容性 = 食材搭配：有些食材相剋（如牛奶+檸檬會凝結）
📖 核心概念
  • Physicochemical Properties: 溶解度、含水量、粒徑、晶型等
  • Inter-related: 粒徑影響溶解度，晶型影響穩定性，不能孤立看待
  • Processing Aids: 用於製程但不出現在成品的物質
  • Compatibility: 混合後不發生降解、變色或物理變化
🏭 CMC連結
  • 原料藥粒徑控制：上游粉碎參數直接影響下游混合均勻度和溶離
  • 賦形劑功能驗證：崩散劑需在加速條件下確認貨架期內仍有效
  • 相容性時機：早期開發就要做，不是 Phase III 才發現問題
2.2 Drug Product 藥品開發
📄 Original Text 原文

2.2.1 Formulation Development

A summary describing development of the formulation, including identification of attributes critical to quality. Information from formal experimental designs can be useful in identifying critical or interacting variables.

The summary should highlight the evolution of the formulation design from initial concept to final design.

Any changes between proposed commercial formulation and clinical batches should be clearly described with rationale.

2.2.2 Overages

Use of an overage to compensate for degradation is discouraged. Any overages should be justified considering safety and efficacy.

2.2.3 Physicochemical Properties

Studies could include development of a test for respirable fraction of inhaled product, or selection of dissolution vs. disintegration testing.

💡 Commentary 解析
🎯 食譜演化史

好的餐廳會記錄食譜的演變：V1太鹹減鹽、V2口感太硬增加燉煮時間、V3太辣調整辣椒量、V4定案標準化。監管機構想看到這個「思考過程」。

⚠️ Overage 的風險
  • 初期含量超標 → 安全性疑慮
  • 降解產物累積 → 雜質超標
  • 掩蓋根本問題 → 配方不穩定

唯一可接受：補償製程損失（如篩分損耗），非補償降解。

📐 DOE 設計範例
2³ Full Factorial Design:
因子 A: 黏合劑濃度 (2%, 5%)
因子 B: 造粒水量 (15%, 25%)
因子 C: 混合時間 (5 min, 15 min)

8 runs 實驗矩陣 → 分析主效應 & 交互作用
→ 找出 CQA 驅動因子
2.3 Manufacturing Process Development 製程開發
📄 Original Text 原文

Process development studies should provide the basis for process validation, continuous process verification, and process control requirements.

The programme should identify any critical process parameters that should be monitored or controlled.

Significant differences between manufacturing processes for pivotal clinical trials and the commercial process should be discussed.

An assessment of the ability of the process to reliably produce product of intended quality (process robustness) can be provided.

💡 Commentary 解析
📖 核心概念
  • Process Validation: 證明製程能持續生產符合規格產品
  • CPV (Continuous Process Verification): QbD 替代驗證方式，持續監控取代三批驗證
  • CPP (Critical Process Parameter): 變異會影響 CQA 的製程參數
  • Process Robustness: 製程耐受原料/設備/環境變異的能力
🏭 Scale-up 考量
  • 批量差異：100 kg → 300 kg
  • 設備差異：實驗室 vs 生產設備
  • 廠址差異：R&D 設施 vs 商業工廠

所有差異需科學評估並說明風險控制措施。

📐 Scale-up 計算
攪拌槽放大 (Tip Speed 恆定):
Tip Speed = π × D × N

小規模: D₁=0.2m, N₁=200rpm → 125.7 m/min
大規模: D₂=0.6m (3x放大)
        N₂ = 125.7/(π×0.6) = 66.7 rpm

結論: 放大3倍時轉速需降至1/3
2.4-2.6 Container Closure, Microbiological Attributes, Compatibility
📄 Original Text 原文

2.4 Container Closure System

Choice and rationale for selection should discuss: suitability for storage and transportation, integrity of container and closure, possible interaction between product and container, protection from moisture and light.

2.5 Microbiological Attributes

Rationale for performing or not performing microbial limits testing, selection and effectiveness of preservative systems, container closure integrity for sterile products.

2.6 Compatibility

Compatibility with reconstitution diluents and admixture prior to administration should be addressed.

💡 Commentary 解析
🎯 食物保存容器
  • 紅酒用深色玻璃（避光）
  • 可樂用鋁罐（阻氣）
  • 嬰兒食品用玻璃（安全）
  • 酸性果汁不能用金屬容器（會溶出金屬離子）
📖 核心概念
  • CCI (Container Closure Integrity): 防止微生物/氣體/水分進入的能力
  • E&L (Extractables/Leachables): 從容器萃取/遷移到產品的物質
  • PET: Preservative Effectiveness Test，證明防腐劑有效
🏭 包材選擇決策
  • 光敏感 → 棕色玻璃/鋁箔
  • 濕敏感 → 鋁塑泡殼
  • 注射劑 → 低溶出玻璃
  • 老年人 → 易開啟設計
Part I: 3. Glossary 術語表
📄 Key Definitions

Continuous Process Verification: An alternative approach to process validation in which manufacturing process performance is continuously monitored and evaluated.

Design Space: The multidimensional combination and interaction of input variables and process parameters that have been demonstrated to provide assurance of quality.

Formal Experimental Design: A structured method for determining the relationship between factors affecting a process and its output. Also known as "Design of Experiments".

Process Analytical Technology (PAT): A system for designing, analyzing, and controlling manufacturing through timely measurements during processing.

Process Robustness: Ability of a process to tolerate variability of materials and changes without negative impact on quality.

Quality: The suitability of either a drug substance or drug product for its intended use.

Part II Annex: Quality by Design (QbD) 品質源於設計
📄 Original Text 原文

This annex describes the principles of quality by design (QbD).

An applicant might choose either an empirical approach or a more systematic approach to product development.

A more systematic approach (QbD) can include:

  • incorporation of prior knowledge
  • results of studies using design of experiments
  • use of quality risk management
  • use of knowledge management throughout the lifecycle

The degree of regulatory flexibility is predicated on the level of relevant scientific knowledge provided.

💡 Commentary 解析
🎯 兩種開發策略

經驗法：像阿嬤做菜，「鹽巴抓一把」靠經驗累積，難以複製。

系統法 (QbD)：像現代主廚，精確秤量、記錄參數，任何人按食譜都能做出一樣的菜。

QbD 系統性開發框架

1 QTPP - 定義目標產品品質概況
↓
2 CQAs - 識別關鍵品質屬性
↓
3 Risk Assessment - 風險評估連結 CPPs
↓
4 Design Space - 建立設計空間
↓
5 Control Strategy - 制定控制策略
↓
6 Lifecycle Management - 持續改進
🏭 QbD 投資回報
  • 減少批次失敗：理解製程 = 預防問題
  • 加速技術轉移：知識文件化 = 更快建立新廠
  • 靈活供應鏈：Design Space 允許應對原料變化

某大藥廠統計：QbD 產品批次合格率從 85% 提升至 98%。

2.1 Quality Target Product Profile (QTPP) 目標產品品質概況
📄 Original Text 原文

The quality target product profile forms the basis of design for the development of the product.

Considerations could include:

  • Intended use in clinical setting, route of administration, dosage form, delivery systems
  • Dosage strength(s)
  • Container closure system
  • Therapeutic moiety release or delivery and attributes affecting pharmacokinetic characteristics
  • Drug product quality criteria (sterility, purity, stability, drug release)
💡 Commentary 解析
🎯 設計車子的目標

QTPP：「需要一台能載 5 人、市區使用、油耗低於 8L/100km、售價 $25,000 以下的車」

這不是說引擎要幾 CC，而是從使用者需求出發指導設計決策。

📖 QTPP 範例 - 口服緩釋錠
QTPP 元素目標理由
劑型緩釋錠減少服藥頻率
給藥途徑口服方便性
規格100/200 mg劑量彈性
藥物釋放12hr 持續釋放穩定血中濃度
穩定性24 個月商業化需求
⚠️ QTPP vs Specification
  • QTPP：「應該達成什麼」→ 開發初期指導方向
  • Specification：「必須符合什麼」→ 最終成品合格標準
2.2 Critical Quality Attributes (CQAs) 關鍵品質屬性
📄 Original Text 原文

A CQA is a physical, chemical, biological, or microbiological property or characteristic that should be within an appropriate limit, range, or distribution to ensure the desired product quality.

CQAs of solid oral dosage forms typically affect: product purity, strength, drug release, stability.

For drug substances, CQAs can include properties (e.g., particle size distribution, bulk density) that affect drug product CQAs.

Quality risk management can be used to prioritize the list of potential CQAs.

💡 Commentary 解析
🎯 汽車的 CQA
  • 煞車距離：必須在 X 公尺內停下（安全）→ CQA
  • 油耗：必須低於 Y L/100km（效能）→ CQA
  • 車身顏色：不影響安全/效能 → 非 CQA
📖 CQA 識別範例
品質屬性CQA?理由
含量 (Assay)是直接影響療效
溶離度是影響生體可用率
含量均一度是確保每錠劑量一致
雜質是影響安全性
硬度可能若影響溶離則為 CQA
外觀通常否不影響安全/療效
⚠️ CQA 識別是動態過程
  • 初期：基於 QTPP 和先前知識列出「潛在 CQA」
  • 開發中：透過實驗驗證哪些真正影響品質
  • 後期：精簡至真正的 CQA

過度定義會增加不必要的控制成本！

2.3 Risk Assessment 風險評估
📄 Original Text 原文

Risk assessment is a science-based process that can aid in identifying which material attributes and process parameters potentially have an effect on product CQAs.

Risk assessment tools can rank parameters based on: probability, severity, detectability.

The initial list can be modified through: design of experiments, mechanistic models, studies leading to mechanistic understanding.

💡 Commentary 解析
🎯 餐廳食安風險評估
  • 原料風險：海鮮不新鮮（高嚴重度）vs 蔬菜不新鮮（中嚴重度）
  • 製程風險：生熟食交叉污染（高機率）
  • 優先順序：先解決「高嚴重度 + 高機率」
📐 FMEA 計算
RPN = Severity × Occurrence × Detection

範例 - 造粒製程:
┌─────────────────┬───┬───┬───┬─────┐
│ 潛在失效模式     │ S │ O │ D │ RPN │
├─────────────────┼───┼───┼───┼─────┤
│ 水分過高        │ 8 │ 4 │ 3 │ 96  │
│ 混合不均勻      │ 9 │ 3 │ 5 │ 135 │ ← 優先
│ 篩網堵塞        │ 5 │ 2 │ 2 │ 20  │
└─────────────────┴───┴───┴───┴─────┘

RPN > 100 需立即改善
📖 常用風險工具
  • Ishikawa (魚骨圖): 識別所有可能影響 CQA 的因子
  • FMEA: 用 RPN 排序風險
  • Risk Matrix: 嚴重度 × 發生率二維矩陣
2.4 Design Space 設計空間
📄 Original Text 原文

A design space can be described in terms of: ranges of material attributes and process parameters, complex mathematical relationships, time dependent functions, multivariate models.

If applicable to multiple scales, describe in terms of scale-independent parameters (e.g., shear rate rather than agitation rate).

A combination of proven acceptable ranges does not constitute a design space.

Determining edge of failure can be helpful but is not essential.

💡 Commentary 解析
🎯 烘焙的「安全操作區」
  • PAR：「溫度 180-200°C」和「時間 15-20min」分別測試過可以
  • Design Space：知道「180°C/20min」「200°C/15min」等組合都能烤出好蛋糕

差異：Design Space 理解參數「交互作用」。

⚠️ Design Space ≠ PAR 組合

PAR 的笛卡兒乘積會產生「角落區域」，這些角落未經驗證可能導致失敗。

📐 Design Space 表達方式
方式一：參數範圍 (無交互作用)
溫度: 60-80°C
時間: 4-6 hr

方式二：數學關係式 (有交互作用)
溶離度 = 75 + 2.5×A - 1.8×B + 0.9×A×B
邊界條件: 溶離度 ≥ 80%

方式三：時間函數 (冷凍乾燥)
階段1: -40°C, 4hr (預凍)
階段2: -20→10°C, 0.5°C/hr (主乾燥)
階段3: 25°C, 6hr (二次乾燥)
🏭 Scale-Independent Parameters
Scale-DependentScale-Independent
攪拌轉速 (RPM)剪切速率 (s⁻¹) 或 Tip Speed
噴霧流量 (mL/min)噴霧速率/床面積
加熱時間 (min)升溫速率 (°C/min)
2.5 Control Strategy 控制策略
📄 Original Text 原文

A control strategy ensures that a product of required quality will be produced consistently.

Understanding sources of variability can provide opportunity to shift controls upstream and minimise end product testing.

A control strategy can include:

  • Control of input material attributes
  • Product specification(s)
  • Controls for unit operations
  • In-process or real-time release testing in lieu of end-product testing
  • Monitoring program for multivariate prediction models
💡 Commentary 解析
🎯 品質管控演化
  • 傳統（救火）：產品做完才檢驗，發現問題報廢處理
  • 進階（防火）：在製程中監控，發現偏差立即調整
  • QbD（設計防火）：從源頭理解變異，控制輸入確保輸出
📖 控制策略層次
層次控制點範例
Level 1輸入材料原料藥粒徑規格
Level 2製程參數造粒水量、混合時間
Level 3中間體顆粒水分、粒徑分佈
Level 4成品溶離度、含量測試

QbD 目標：將控制往上游移動，減少 Level 4 依賴。

🏭 Real-Time Release Testing (RTRT)
  • 傳統放行：抽樣 → 實驗室測試 → 等結果 → 放行（2週）
  • RTRT：製程數據 + PAT → 即時判定 → 生產完即放行

範例：用 NIR 預測含量取代 HPLC 測試。

2.6 Product Lifecycle Management 產品生命週期管理
📄 Original Text 原文

Throughout the product lifecycle, companies have opportunities to evaluate innovative approaches to improve product quality (see ICH Q10).

Process performance can be monitored to ensure it is working as anticipated. This monitoring could include trend analysis of the manufacturing process.

For design spaces using mathematical models, periodic model maintenance could be useful.

Expansion, reduction or redefinition of the design space could be desired upon gaining additional process knowledge. Change of design space is subject to regional requirements.

💡 Commentary 解析
🎯 餐廳持續改進

餐廳開業後不會停止改進：

  • 收集客戶反饋 → 調整食譜
  • 發現新食材 → 擴展菜單
  • 員工經驗累積 → 優化流程

藥品製造也一樣，上市後持續學習和改進。

🏭 生命週期活動
  • 監控：SPC 圖表追蹤 CPP 和 CQA 趨勢
  • 分析：OOS/OOT 調查，找出根本原因
  • 改進：製程優化，擴展 Design Space
  • 維護：模型更新，驗證持續有效
⚠️ Design Space 變更
  • 範圍內操作：不算變更，無需申報
  • 擴展 Design Space：需要申報（補充數據）
  • 縮小 Design Space：通常較簡單的通知
Appendix 1: Minimal vs Enhanced Approaches 最小化 vs 增強方法比較
📄 Q8(R2) Appendix 1 對照表
Aspect 面向 Minimal 最小化方法 Enhanced QbD 增強方法
Overall Development Mainly empirical; one variable at a time Systematic; multivariate experiments; design space established
Manufacturing Process Fixed; validation based on initial batches Adjustable within design space; continuous process verification
Process Controls In-process tests for go/no go; off-line analysis PAT tools with feed forward/feedback controls
Product Specifications Primary means of control; based on batch data Part of overall control strategy; based on desired performance
Control Strategy Controlled by end product testing Risk-based; quality controls shifted upstream; RTRT possible
Lifecycle Management Reactive (problem solving) Preventive; continual improvement facilitated
💡 Commentary 解析
🎯 比喻說明

最小化方法 = 傳統工廠：

  • 按固定 SOP 生產
  • 產品做完檢驗
  • 出問題才改善

QbD 方法 = 智慧工廠：

  • 理解製程，彈性調整
  • 即時監控，預防問題
  • 持續學習，主動改進
🏭 選擇哪種方法？

考量因素：

  • 產品複雜度：生物製劑、緩釋製劑更需要 QbD
  • 產品生命週期：預期長銷的產品值得投資 QbD
  • 公司能力：需要統計、PAT、風險管理能力
  • 法規期望：各區域對 QbD 的接受程度

實務建議：可以混合使用，關鍵製程用 QbD，簡單製程用傳統方法。

練習題 Practice Questions
📝 Questions 問題
練習題 1：QTPP vs CQA

情境：你正在開發一個口服速釋錠劑，含有水溶性原料藥 50mg。

問題：

  • (a) 列出此產品可能的 3 個 QTPP 元素
  • (b) 識別 3 個潛在的 CQA
  • (c) 說明 QTPP 和 CQA 的差異
練習題 2：Design Space

情境：造粒製程有兩個關鍵參數：造粒水量 (15-25%) 和混合時間 (5-15 min)。DOE 實驗顯示溶離度 = 70 + 0.8×水量 - 0.5×時間，溶離規格 ≥80%。

問題：

  • (a) 這是 Design Space 還是 Proven Acceptable Range？為什麼？
  • (b) 如果造粒水量設為 20%，混合時間的可接受範圍是多少？
  • (c) 如果你在 Design Space 內將水量從 20% 改為 22%，需要申報變更嗎？
練習題 3：Control Strategy

情境：你發現原料藥粒徑 (D50) 與成品溶離度高度相關。目前的控制策略是對每批成品進行溶離測試。

問題：

  • (a) 根據 QbD 原則，你會如何改進控制策略？
  • (b) 這樣做的好處是什麼？
  • (c) 需要什麼數據來支持這個變更？
💡 Answers 參考答案
答案 1：QTPP vs CQA

(a) QTPP 元素：

  • 劑型：速釋錠劑
  • 規格：50 mg
  • 藥物釋放：30 分鐘內 ≥85% 溶離

(b) 潛在 CQA：

  • 含量 (Assay)：直接影響療效
  • 溶離度：影響生體可用率
  • 含量均一度：確保每錠劑量一致

(c) 差異：QTPP 是從患者需求出發的設計目標（方向）；CQA 是確保產品品質的可測量屬性（指標）。

答案 2：Design Space

(a) 這是 Design Space，因為：

  • 有數學關係式描述參數交互作用
  • 理解參數對 CQA 的影響機制
  • 不只是單變數範圍的組合

(b) 溶離 = 70 + 0.8×20 - 0.5×時間 ≥ 80

86 - 0.5×時間 ≥ 80 → 時間 ≤ 12 min

所以混合時間範圍是 5-12 min。

(c) 不需要申報，因為在 Design Space 內的操作不算變更。

答案 3：Control Strategy

(a) 改進策略：

  • 將控制點上移：在原料藥入廠時控制 D50
  • 建立 D50 與溶離的相關性模型
  • 可能實施 RTRT，用 D50 預測溶離

(b) 好處：

  • 早期發現問題，避免生產報廢
  • 減少成品放行時間
  • 降低測試成本

(c) 支持數據：

  • D50 與溶離的相關性研究（多批數據）
  • 模型驗證數據
  • D50 測試方法驗證

📌 Key Takeaways 重點摘要

  • 核心哲學：Quality cannot be tested into products; quality should be built in by design.
  • QbD 六步驟：QTPP → CQA → Risk Assessment → Design Space → Control Strategy → Lifecycle Management
  • Design Space：經驗證的多維度參數範圍，範圍內操作不算變更
  • Control Strategy：將控制點上移，從源頭確保品質，減少成品測試依賴
  • 法規回報：展現更多科學理解 = 獲得更多法規彈性
  • 相關指南：ICH Q9 (Quality Risk Management), ICH Q10 (Pharmaceutical Quality System), ICH Q11 (Development and Manufacture of Drug Substances), ICH Q12 (Lifecycle Management)
