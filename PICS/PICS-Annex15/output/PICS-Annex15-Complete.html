Principle & General

原則與一般規定 — Lifecycle approach & risk-based qualification

Principle

This Annex describes the principles of qualification and validation which are applicable to the facilities, equipment, utilities and processes used for the manufacture of medicinal products and may also be used as supplementary optional guidance for active substances without introduction of additional requirements to Part II.

It is a GMP requirement that manufacturers control the critical aspects of their particular operations through qualification and validation over the life cycle of the product and process. Any planned changes to the facilities, equipment, utilities and processes, which may affect the quality of the product, should be formally documented and the impact on the validated status or control strategy assessed.

Computerised systems used for the manufacture of medicinal products should also be validated according to the requirements of Annex 11. The relevant concepts and guidance presented in ICH Q8, Q9, Q10 and Q11 should also be taken into account.

General

A quality risk management approach should be applied throughout the lifecycle of a medicinal product. As part of a quality risk management system, decisions on the scope and extent of qualification and validation should be based on a justified and documented risk assessment of the facilities, equipment, utilities and processes. Retrospective validation is no longer considered an acceptable approach.

Data supporting qualification and/or validation studies which were obtained from sources outside of the manufacturers own programmes may be used provided that this approach has been justified and that there is adequate assurance that controls were in place throughout the acquisition of such data.

原則與適用範圍解析

適用對象

本附錄適用於所有用於藥品製造的廠房、設備、公用系統（水、蒸汽、空氣等）及製程。亦可作為原料藥（Part II）的補充指引，但不增加額外要求。

回顧性確效已不被接受

2023年版明確廢除回顧性確效（Retrospective Validation）。所有製程必須採用前瞻性或持續性確效方法。這是與舊版的重要差異。

比喻：建築藍圖的邏輯

確效就如蓋房子：先畫設計圖（URS/DQ）→ 驗收建材安裝（IQ）→ 測試所有系統運作（OQ）→ 確認住人後功能正常（PQ）。每一步都有文件存證，品質從設計端就被建入，而非最後才驗收。

電腦化系統同樣需依 Annex 11 規定進行確效，並應參考 ICH Q8（藥品開發）、Q9（品質風險管理）、Q10（藥品品質系統）、Q11（原料藥開發）。

若引用外部機構（如供應商、CRO）的確效數據，需有充分文件證明數據的完整性與管控措施在資料取得期間均有效執行。

1. Organising and Planning for Qualification and Validation

組織與規劃 — Validation Master Plan (VMP)

1.1 – 1.8 Planning Requirements

1.1 All qualification and validation activities should be planned and take the life cycle of facilities, equipment, utilities, process and product into consideration.

1.2 Qualification and validation activities should only be performed by suitably trained personnel who follow approved procedures.

1.3 Qualification/validation personnel should report as defined in the pharmaceutical quality system although this may not necessarily be to a quality management or a quality assurance function. However, there should be appropriate quality oversight over the whole validation life cycle.

1.4 The key elements of the site qualification and validation programme should be clearly defined and documented in a validation master plan (VMP) or equivalent document.

1.5 The VMP or equivalent document should define the qualification/validation system and include or reference information on at least the following:

  1. Qualification and Validation policy;
  2. The organisational structure including roles and responsibilities;
  3. Summary of facilities, equipment, systems, processes and their Q&V status;
  4. Change control and deviation management for qualification and validation;
  5. Guidance on developing acceptance criteria;
  6. References to existing documents;
  7. The qualification and validation strategy, including requalification, where applicable.

1.6 For large and complex projects, planning takes on added importance and separate validation plans may enhance clarity.

1.7 A quality risk management approach should be used for qualification and validation activities. Risk assessments should be repeated, as required, in light of increased knowledge and understanding from any changes.

1.8 Appropriate checks should be incorporated into qualification and validation work to ensure the integrity of all data obtained.

VMP 規劃要點解析

驗證主計畫（VMP）的功能

VMP 是整個廠址確效體系的「總綱文件」，等同於確效的憲法。它不需要逐一描述每個設備，而是定義系統、政策、責任與策略的框架。具體確效細節在個別確效計畫（VP）中展開。

人員資格與品質監督

確效人員不一定要隸屬於 QA 部門（如工程部門可主導設備 IQ/OQ），但整個確效生命週期必須有適當的品質監督（quality oversight）。這允許彈性的組織設計，同時維持 GMP 要求。

VMP vs. VP 的層次關係

VMP = 城市規劃藍圖（定義道路系統、分區原則）；VP（個別驗證計畫）= 個別建築的施工圖。VMP 讓稽核員可以快速了解廠址整體確效狀態，VP 則提供每個設備/製程的技術細節。

VMP 必要內容（§1.5）說明
確效政策廠址整體確效策略聲明
組織架構與責任誰負責哪類確效活動
設施/設備/製程現況廠址確效狀態總表
變更控制與偏差管理確效活動中的偏差處理程序
驗收標準制定指引AC 設定的科學依據方法
現有文件參考SOPs、協議等的索引
確效策略（含再確效）生命週期維護計畫

數據完整性（§1.8）：確效過程中的所有數據獲取均需有適當的管控機制，確保 ALCOA+ 原則（可歸因、清晰、同步、原始、準確）的落實。

2. Documentation, Including VMP

文件管理，包含 VMP — Protocols, deviations & reports

2.1 – 2.10 Documentation Requirements

2.1 Good documentation practices are important to support knowledge management throughout the product lifecycle.

2.2 All documents generated during qualification and validation should be approved and authorised by appropriate personnel as defined in the pharmaceutical quality system.

2.3 The inter-relationship between documents in complex validation projects should be clearly defined.

2.4 Validation protocols should be prepared which defines the critical systems, attributes and parameters and the associated acceptance criteria.

2.5 Qualification documents may be combined together, where appropriate, e.g. installation qualification (IQ) and operational qualification (OQ).

2.6 Where validation protocols and other documentation are supplied by a third party providing validation services, appropriate personnel at the manufacturing site should confirm suitability and compliance with internal procedures before approval. Vendor protocols may be supplemented by additional documentation/test protocols before use.

2.7 Any significant changes to the approved protocol during execution, e.g. acceptance criteria, operating parameters etc., should be documented as a deviation and be scientifically justified.

2.8 Results which fail to meet the pre-defined acceptance criteria should be recorded as a deviation, and be fully investigated according to local procedures. Any implications for the validation should be discussed in the report.

2.9 The review and conclusions of the validation should be reported and the results obtained summarised against the acceptance criteria. Any subsequent changes to acceptance criteria should be scientifically justified and a final recommendation made as to the outcome of the validation.

2.10 A formal release for the next stage in the qualification and validation process should be authorised by the relevant responsible personnel either as part of the validation report approval or as a separate summary document. Conditional approval to proceed to the next qualification stage can be given where certain acceptance criteria or deviations have not been fully addressed and there is a documented assessment that there is no significant impact on the next activity.

確效文件管理解析

確效文件的三層結構

  • 計畫（Protocol）：確效前制定，定義 CPP、CQA、驗收標準、測試步驟
  • 執行記錄：測試期間填寫的原始數據與觀察
  • 報告（Report）：總結所有結果，對照 AC 得出結論，含異常分析

供應商協議使用注意事項（§2.6）

廠商提供的確效協議可以使用，但製造商必須先審查其適用性並確認符合內部程序。不能直接簽署執行而未審閱內容。必要時可補充額外測試協議。

條件性放行（§2.10）Conditional Approval

這是重要的靈活性條款。當部分 AC 未完全達成或偏差尚未完全結案時，可以有條件地進入下一確效階段，前提是：

  • 有書面的風險評估，說明未解決事項對下一階段無重大影響
  • 條件必須明確記錄在報告或獨立摘要文件中
  • 需由負責人員正式授權

偏差 vs. 計畫變更的區別

偏差（§2.7-2.8）：執行過程中發現的不預期事件（測試失敗、AC 未達成）或對已核准協議的重大修改。必須調查根本原因並評估對確效結論的影響。

計畫變更：協議核准前的修訂，走正常文件審查流程，不需要開偏差。

IQ+OQ 合併（§2.5）：對於簡單設備，可將 IQ 和 OQ 合併為一份文件（IOQ），減少文件負擔，同時仍需涵蓋兩個階段的所有測試要求。

3. Qualification Stages for Equipment, Facilities, Utilities and Systems

確效階段：URS → DQ → FAT/SAT → IQ → OQ → PQ

確效階段總覽

§3.1 指出確效活動應考慮從使用者需求規格（URS）到設備生命週期結束的所有階段。以下各子節說明每個階段的主要內容與建議測試項目（實際階段可依個別專案情況調整）。

3.2 User Requirements Specification (URS)

The specification for equipment, facilities, utilities or systems should be defined in a URS and/or a functional specification. The essential elements of quality need to be built in at this stage and any GMP risks mitigated to an acceptable level. The URS should be a point of reference throughout the validation life cycle.

3.3 Design Qualification (DQ)

The next element in the qualification of equipment, facilities, utilities, or systems is DQ where the compliance of the design with GMP should be demonstrated and documented. The requirements of the user requirements specification should be verified during the design qualification.

3.4 – 3.7 Factory Acceptance Testing (FAT) / Site Acceptance Testing (SAT)

3.4 Equipment, especially if incorporating novel or complex technology, may be evaluated, if applicable, at the vendor prior to delivery.

3.5 Prior to installation, equipment should be confirmed to comply with the URS/functional specification at the vendor site, if applicable.

3.6 Where appropriate and justified, documentation review and some tests could be performed at the FAT or other stages without the need to repeat on site at IQ/OQ if it can be shown that the functionality is not affected by the transport and installation.

3.7 FAT may be supplemented by the execution of a SAT following the receipt of equipment at the manufacturing site.

URS、DQ、FAT/SAT 解析

URS — 品質的源頭

URS 是確效生命週期中最重要的文件之一。它定義了設備/系統必須做到什麼，而不是如何做到。URS 應在採購前完成，並作為後續所有確效活動的參照基準（point of reference）。

  • 功能需求（能力、產量、精度）
  • GMP 合規性要求（材質、清潔性、GAMP 軟體類別）
  • 安全性要求
  • 環境需求（溫濕度、潔淨等級）

FAT vs. SAT 的差異

FAT（出廠驗收）：在供應商廠內執行，確認設備符合 URS 設計規格。設備尚未安裝於生產環境。

SAT（到廠驗收）：設備送達製造廠後執行，確認運輸過程未損壞且廠內整合正常。

§3.6 允許在 FAT 中完成的測試不需要在 IQ/OQ 中重複，前提是能證明功能不受運輸與安裝影響。這是重要的效率優化條款。

3.8 – 3.9 Installation Qualification (IQ)

3.8 IQ should be performed on equipment, facilities, utilities, or systems.

3.9 IQ should include, but is not limited to the following:

  1. Verification of the correct installation of components, instrumentation, equipment, pipe work and services against the engineering drawings and specifications;
  2. Verification of the correct installation against pre-defined criteria;
  3. Collection and collation of supplier operating and working instructions and maintenance requirements;
  4. Calibration of instrumentation;
  5. Verification of the materials of construction.

3.10 – 3.12 Operational Qualification (OQ)

3.10 OQ normally follows IQ but depending on the complexity of the equipment, it may be performed as a combined Installation/Operation Qualification (IOQ).

3.11 OQ should include but is not limited to the following:

  1. Tests that have been developed from the knowledge of processes, systems and equipment to ensure the system is operating as designed;
  2. Tests to confirm upper and lower operating limits, and/or "worst case" conditions.

3.12 The completion of a successful OQ should allow the finalisation of standard operating and cleaning procedures, operator training and preventative maintenance requirements.

3.13 – 3.14 Performance Qualification (PQ)

3.13 PQ should normally follow the successful completion of IQ and OQ. However, it may in some cases be appropriate to perform it in conjunction with OQ or Process Validation.

3.14 PQ should include, but is not limited to the following:

  1. Tests, using production materials, qualified substitutes or simulated product proven to have equivalent behaviour under normal operating conditions with worst case batch sizes. The frequency of sampling used to confirm process control should be justified;
  2. Tests should cover the operating range of the intended process, unless documented evidence from the development phases confirming the operational ranges is available.

IQ / OQ / PQ 詳解

階段核心問題使用材料
IQ是否正確安裝？工程圖、規格書、校準記錄
OQ是否在設計範圍內運作？最壞情況測試條件、上下限
PQ是否能穩定產出合格產品？實際生產材料或等效替代品

IQ 重點：校準與材質確認

IQ 不僅確認「裝好了」，還需收集所有供應商的操作/維護文件，並完成儀器校準。材質確認（Materials of Construction）對無菌製造特別重要——接觸表面必須符合 316L 不鏽鋼等規格。

OQ 重點：最壞情況測試

OQ 必須測試操作範圍的上限與下限（worst case conditions），而非只在正常中間值測試。例如：滅菌器 OQ 需在最小裝載量（最難冷凝）和最大裝載量（最難穿透）兩個條件下測試。

PQ 完成後解鎖的能力

成功的 OQ 完成後，才能正式確定 SOPs、清潔程序和操作員訓練課程（§3.12）。PQ 是使用實際生產材料或有科學依據的替代品進行，確認設備在真實使用條件下的表現。

比喻：汽車交付的類比

IQ = 交車前確認所有零件安裝正確、里程表已歸零；OQ = 在封閉跑道上測試各種速度、轉彎、剎車極限；PQ = 在真實路況下帶著乘客跑完特定里程，確認舒適、油耗、穩定性均符合承諾規格。

4. Re-qualification

再確效 — Periodic evaluation to maintain state of control

4.1 – 4.2 Re-qualification Requirements

4.1 Equipment, facilities, utilities and systems should be evaluated at an appropriate frequency to confirm that they remain in a state of control.

4.2 Where re-qualification is necessary and performed at a specific time period, the period should be justified and the criteria for evaluation defined. Furthermore, the possibility of small changes over time should be assessed.

再確效解析

再確效的觸發方式

再確效可以由以下情況觸發：

  • 週期性評估：依風險評估定義的固定週期（如每年、每3年）
  • 事件驅動：維修、改造、重大偏差、設備移動後
  • 持續性監控數據：趨勢異常時觸發

§4.2 重點：漸進性變化的評估

長期使用的設備可能因磨損、老化等因素發生細微累積性變化。再確效週期的設計需考量此類「小量長期變化」（small changes over time），週期設定需有風險評估支撐。

再確效範圍可以是完整的 IQ/OQ/PQ 重新執行，也可以是針對特定關鍵參數的簡化評估，視風險評估結果而定。

5. Process Validation

製程確效 — Traditional, Continuous, Hybrid approaches & Ongoing Verification

5.1 – 5.15 General Principles

5.1 The requirements and principles outlined in this section are applicable to the manufacture of all pharmaceutical dosage forms. They cover the initial validation of new processes, subsequent validation of modified processes, site transfers and ongoing process verification.

5.3 Manufacturing processes may be developed using a traditional approach or a continuous verification approach. However, irrespective of the approach used, processes must be shown to be robust and ensure consistent product quality before any product is released to the market. Retrospective validation is no longer an acceptable approach.

5.4 Process validation of new products should cover all intended marketed strengths and sites of manufacture. Bracketing could be justified for new products based on extensive process knowledge.

5.5 For process validation of products transferred between sites, the number of validation batches could be reduced by the use of a bracketing approach. Existing product knowledge, including the content of the previous validation, should be available.

5.7 Process validation should establish whether all quality attributes and process parameters, which are considered important for ensuring the validated state and acceptable product quality, can be consistently met by the process.

5.8 Normally batches manufactured for process validation should be the same size as the intended commercial scale batches.

5.9 Equipment, facilities, utilities and systems used for process validation should be qualified. Test methods should be validated for their intended use.

5.11 Production personnel are expected to be involved in the manufacture of validation batches to facilitate product understanding.

5.12 The suppliers of critical starting and packaging materials should be qualified prior to the manufacture of validation batches.

5.14 Where validation batches are released to the market this should be pre-defined. The conditions under which they are produced should fully comply with GMP, with the validation acceptance criteria.

製程確效一般原則解析

適用範圍廣泛

§5.1 明確涵蓋：新製程初始確效、修改後製程的再確效、廠址移轉（site transfer）確效、以及持續性製程查驗。幾乎涉及藥品生命週期的所有階段。

批次規模要求（§5.8）

確效批次通常應與商業規模批次相同大小。使用非商業規模批次需要額外科學依據。這確保確效結果真正代表商業生產條件。

括弧法（Bracketing）的應用

Bracketing 是一種科學化減少批次數量的策略：只驗證極端情況（最高/最低規格），中間規格視為已被涵蓋。例如：驗證 100mg 和 500mg 片劑，中間的 200mg 和 300mg 視為已確效。需要充足的科學依據和知識基礎。

確效批次的先決條件

在執行製程確效批次前，需確認：

  • 設備/系統已完成 IQ/OQ/PQ（§5.9）
  • 分析方法已確效（§5.9）
  • 關鍵起始物料和包材供應商已完成資格確認（§5.12）
  • 生產人員完成訓練（§5.11）

5.16 – 5.17 Concurrent Validation

5.16 In exceptional circumstances, where there is a strong benefit-risk ratio for the patient, it may be acceptable not to complete a validation programme before routine production starts and concurrent validation could be used. However, the decision to carry out concurrent validation must be justified, documented in the VMP for visibility and approved by authorised personnel.

5.17 Where a concurrent validation approach has been adopted, there should be sufficient data to support a conclusion that any given batch of product is uniform and meets the defined acceptance criteria. The results and conclusion should be formally documented and available to the Authorised Person prior to certification of the batch.

同步確效解析

同步確效的嚴格限制

Concurrent Validation（同步確效）是例外，不是常規。觸發條件：

  • 對病患有強烈的效益-風險比（例如孤兒藥、緊急藥物短缺）
  • 必須記錄在 VMP 中，透明可查
  • 需獲得授權人員批准

每批產品在放行前，授權人（Authorised Person, AP）必須獲得足夠數據，確認該批次均勻且符合 AC。

5.18 – 5.22 Traditional Process Validation

5.18 In the traditional approach, a number of batches of the finished product are manufactured under routine conditions to confirm reproducibility.

5.19 The number of batches manufactured and the number of samples taken should be based on quality risk management principles, allow the normal range of variation and trends to be established and provide sufficient data for evaluation.

5.20 Without prejudice to 5.19, it is generally considered acceptable that a minimum of three consecutive batches manufactured under routine conditions could constitute a validation of the process. An alternative number of batches may be justified.

5.21 A process validation protocol should be prepared which defines the critical process parameters (CPP), critical quality attributes (CQA) and the associated acceptance criteria which should be based on development data or documented process knowledge.

5.22 Process validation protocols should include, but are not limited to the following:

  1. A short description of the process and a reference to the Master Batch Record;
  2. Functions and responsibilities;
  3. Summary of the CQAs to be investigated;
  4. Summary of CPPs and their associated limits;
  5. Summary of other (non-critical) attributes and parameters to be monitored;
  6. List of equipment/facilities and calibration status;
  7. List of analytical methods and method validation;
  8. Proposed in-process controls with acceptance criteria;
  9. Additional testing with acceptance criteria;
  10. Sampling plan and rationale;
  11. Methods for recording and evaluating results;
  12. Process for release and certification of batches (if applicable).

傳統製程確效解析

「最少3批」的正確理解

§5.20 規定「一般可接受最少3批連續批次」，但這是最低門檻，而非最終答案。每個廠商必須基於品質風險管理，自行決定並論証需要多少批次才能展現足夠的保證。若製程複雜性高、理解程度低，3批可能不夠。

製程確效協議的 12 項必要內容（§5.22）

這是稽核常見查核點。協議必須在執行確效批次前完成核准。特別注意：

  • CQA 和 CPP 必須有開發數據或文件化的製程知識支撐
  • 取樣計畫需要科學依據（sampling plan with rationale）
  • 非關鍵參數（non-critical parameters）也需要監控計畫

CPP vs. CQA 的區別

CQA（關鍵品質屬性）：產品的輸出特性，如含量均勻度、溶出度、無菌性（定義「好產品」的標準）

CPP（關鍵製程參數）：製程的輸入變數，如混合時間、溫度、壓縮力（影響 CQA 的開關）

製程確效就是證明：在所定義的 CPP 範圍內操作，能穩定產出符合所有 CQA 的產品。

5.23 – 5.27 Continuous Process Verification & Hybrid Approach

5.23 For products developed by a quality by design approach, where it has been scientifically established during development that the established control strategy provides a high degree of assurance of product quality, then continuous process verification can be used as an alternative to traditional process validation.

5.24 The method by which the process will be verified should be defined. There should be a science based control strategy for the required attributes for incoming materials, CQAs and CPPs to confirm product realisation. Process Analytical Technology and multivariate statistical process control may be used as tools.

5.25 The general principles laid down in 5.1 – 5.14 above still apply.

5.26 A hybrid of the traditional approach and continuous process verification could be used where there is a substantial amount of product and process knowledge and understanding gained from manufacturing experience and historical batch data.

5.27 This approach may also be used for any validation activities after changes or during ongoing process verification even though the product was initially validated using a traditional approach.

5.28 – 5.32 Ongoing Process Verification during Lifecycle

5.28 Paragraphs 5.28-5.32 are applicable to all three approaches to process validation mentioned above.

5.29 Manufacturers should monitor product quality to ensure that a state of control is maintained throughout the product lifecycle with the relevant process trends evaluated.

5.30 The extent and frequency of ongoing process verification should be reviewed periodically. At any point throughout the product lifecycle, it may be appropriate to modify the requirements.

5.31 Ongoing process verification should be conducted under an approved protocol or equivalent documents and a corresponding report should be prepared. Statistical tools should be used, where appropriate, to support conclusions with regard to the variability and capability of a given process.

5.32 Ongoing process verification should be used throughout the product lifecycle to support the validated status of the product as documented in the Product Quality Review. Incremental changes over time should also be considered.

持續性確效、混合法與 OPV 解析

三種製程確效方法比較

方法適用情境關鍵工具
Traditional多數藥品；製程知識有限時≥3批連續確效批次
Continuous (CPV)QbD 開發；control strategy 科學成熟PAT、多變量 SPC
Hybrid有豐富歷史數據；製程理解深入兩種方法的組合

持續性製程查驗（OPV）— 確效不是終點

OPV（§5.28-5.32）是製程確效的生命週期維護機制。適用於所有三種確效方法。關鍵要求：

  • 有核准的協議和報告
  • 使用統計工具（製程能力指數 Cpk、SPC 管制圖）
  • 與產品品質回顧（PQR）整合
  • 考慮累積性漸進變化（incremental changes over time）

比喻：OPV 如同年度體檢

製程確效（初始）= 新車出廠前的全面品質檢驗（確認符合規格）；OPV = 車輛每年的例行保養檢查（確認仍在良好狀態）。兩者都不可缺少：前者建立基準，後者維持狀態。

PAT 在持續性確效中的角色

製程分析技術（Process Analytical Technology, PAT）允許即時監控 CQA，取代終點測試。例如：NIR（近紅外線光譜）即時監測混合均勻度，可替代傳統的取樣分析。PAT 是 CPV 方法的關鍵使能技術。

6. Verification of Transportation

運輸確效 — Route definition, risk assessment & environmental monitoring

6.1 – 6.4 Transportation Verification Requirements

6.1 Finished medicinal products, investigational medicinal products, bulk product and samples should be transported from manufacturing sites in accordance with the conditions defined in the marketing authorisation, the approved label, product specification file or as justified by the manufacturer.

6.2 It is recognised that verification of transportation may be challenging due to the variable factors involved however, transportation routes should be clearly defined. Seasonal and other variations should also be considered during verification of transport.

6.3 A risk assessment should be performed to consider the impact of variables in the transportation process other than those conditions which are continuously controlled or monitored, e.g. delays during transportation, failure of monitoring devices, topping up liquid nitrogen, product susceptibility and any other relevant factors.

6.4 Due to the variable conditions expected during transportation, continuous monitoring and recording of any critical environmental conditions to which the product may be subjected should be performed, unless otherwise justified.

運輸確效解析

運輸確效的挑戰性

運輸確效比一般設備確效更複雜，因為涉及大量不可控因素：天氣、交通延誤、不同運輸商、季節變化。§6.2 明確承認此挑戰，但要求路線必須明確定義。

風險評估的必要項目（§6.3）

運輸風險評估需考慮：

  • 運輸延誤（delays）的溫度影響
  • 溫度監測設備失效
  • 液態氮補充（低溫產品）
  • 產品對溫度/振動/光的敏感性
  • 季節性溫度變化（夏/冬最壞情況）

§6.4 持續監測：除非有充分科學依據，否則運輸全程必須對關鍵環境條件（溫度、濕度等）進行持續監測和記錄。數據記錄器（data logger）是標準工具。

7. Validation of Packaging

包裝確效 — Primary & secondary packaging equipment qualification

7.1 – 7.2 Packaging Validation Requirements

7.1 Variation in equipment processing parameters especially during primary packaging may have a significant impact on the integrity and correct functioning of the pack, e.g. blister strips, sachets and sterile components; therefore primary and secondary packaging equipment for finished and bulk products should be qualified.

7.2 Qualification of the equipment used for primary packing should be carried out at the minimum and maximum operating ranges defined for the critical process parameters such as temperature, machine speed and sealing pressure or for any other factors.

包裝確效解析

初級包裝確效的重要性

初級包裝（primary packaging，直接接觸產品）的完整性直接影響產品品質和患者安全。鋁塑泡罩（blister）的密封強度、小袋（sachet）的完整性、無菌包裝的完整性——任何密封失效都可能導致污染或降解。

最小-最大操作範圍測試（§7.2）

與 OQ 的「最壞情況」概念一致：包裝確效需在關鍵製程參數（溫度、機器速度、封口壓力）的最低和最高設定值下進行，確認整個操作範圍內的包裝完整性均符合要求。

次級包裝（secondary packaging，如外盒）也需確效，但側重點不同：主要確認標籤正確性、條碼識別、產品防護功能。

8. Qualification of Utilities

公用系統確效 — Steam, water, air, gases

8.1 – 8.3 Utilities Qualification Requirements

8.1 The quality of steam, water, air, other gases etc. should be confirmed following installation using the qualification steps described in section 3 above.

8.2 The period and extent of qualification should reflect any seasonal variations, if applicable, and the intended use of the utility.

8.3 A risk assessment should be carried out where there may be direct contact with the product, e.g. heating, ventilation and air-conditioning (HVAC) systems, or indirect contact such as through heat exchangers to mitigate any risks of failure.

公用系統確效解析

公用系統的 IQ/OQ/PQ 應用

§8.1 指明公用系統的確效使用第3節描述的相同架構（URS → DQ → IQ → OQ → PQ）。常見公用系統：

  • PW/WFI：注射用水/純化水（最嚴格）
  • Pure Steam：純蒸汽（直接接觸產品）
  • Compressed Air：壓縮空氣（油分/微粒/微生物）
  • HVAC：暖通空調（環境控制）
  • Nitrogen/CO₂：惰性氣體（保護填充）

季節性變化（§8.2）

某些公用系統的性能受季節影響：夏季冷卻水溫度升高影響 WFI 系統；冬季外氣條件影響 HVAC 除濕能力。確效計畫需涵蓋最嚴苛的季節條件，或說明全年均已測試。

直接/間接接觸的風險評估（§8.3）

直接接觸：HVAC（空氣直接接觸開放產品）→ 最高風險，需確認微粒和微生物符合潔淨等級要求

間接接觸：熱交換器（冷卻水通過金屬壁加熱/冷卻產品）→ 需確認無滲漏風險

9. Validation of Test Methods

測試方法確效 — Detection limits, microbial methods & recovery

9.1 – 9.3 Test Method Validation Requirements

9.1 All analytical test methods used in qualification, validation or cleaning exercises should be validated with an appropriate detection and quantification limit, where necessary, as defined in Chapter 6 of the PIC/S GMP guide Part I.

9.2 Where microbial testing of product is carried out, the method should be validated to confirm that the product does not influence the recovery of microorganisms.

9.3 Where microbial testing of surfaces in clean rooms is carried out, validation should be performed on the test method to confirm that sanitising agents do not influence the recovery of microorganisms.

測試方法確效解析

方法確效的基準：PIC/S GMP Part I Chapter 6

所有用於確效/清潔確效的分析方法，需依據 Part I 第6章（品質控制）進行確效，需確立：

  • 偵測極限（LOD）：最小可偵測量
  • 定量極限（LOQ）：可準確定量的最小量
  • 線性、專一性、準確性、精密性、耐用性

微生物方法的特殊要求（§9.2-9.3）

產品抑制測試（§9.2）：某些藥品成分（防腐劑、抗菌成分）可能抑制微生物生長，導致無菌測試假陰性。需通過「適用性測試」確認產品基質不影響回收率。

消毒劑干擾（§9.3）：環境監測時，表面殘留的消毒劑可能抑制接觸平板（contact plate）上的微生物生長。需確認取樣方法能有效中和殘留消毒劑（使用中和劑如卵磷脂+吐溫）。

10. Cleaning Validation

清潔確效 — Residue limits, sampling, worst case & hold times

10.1 – 10.8 Core Requirements

10.1 Cleaning validation should be performed in order to confirm the effectiveness of any cleaning procedure for all product contact equipment. Simulating agents may be used with appropriate scientific justification. Where similar types of equipment are grouped together, a justification of the specific equipment selected for cleaning validation is expected.

10.2 A visual check for cleanliness is an important part of the acceptance criteria for cleaning validation. It is not generally acceptable for this criterion alone to be used. Repeated cleaning and retesting until acceptable residue results are obtained is not considered an acceptable approach.

10.3 It is recognised that a cleaning validation programme may take some time to complete and validation with verification after each batch may be required for some products e.g. investigational medicinal products.

10.4 Validation should consider the level of automation in the cleaning process. Where an automatic process is used, the specified normal operating range of the utilities and equipment should be validated.

10.5 For all cleaning processes an assessment should be performed to determine the variable factors which influence cleaning effectiveness and performance, e.g. operators, the level of detail in procedures such as rinsing times etc. If variable factors have been identified, the worst case situations should be used as the basis for cleaning validation studies.

10.6 Limits for the carryover of product residues should be based on a toxicological evaluation. The justification for the selected limits should be documented in a risk assessment which includes all the supporting references. Limits should be established for the removal of any cleaning agents used. Acceptance criteria should consider the potential cumulative effect of multiple items of equipment in the process equipment train.

10.6.1 Therapeutic macromolecules and peptides are known to degrade and denature when exposed to pH extremes and/or heat, and may become pharmacologically inactive. A toxicological evaluation may therefore not be applicable in these circumstances.

10.6.2 If it is not feasible to test for specific product residues, other representative parameters may be selected, e.g. total organic carbon (TOC) and conductivity.

10.7 The risk presented by microbial and endotoxin contamination should be considered during the development of cleaning validation protocols.

10.8 The influence of the time between manufacture and cleaning and the time between cleaning and use should be taken into account to define dirty and clean hold times for the cleaning process.

清潔確效核心要求解析

HBEL/ADE 為殘留限度設定依據（§10.6）

產品殘留限度（carryover limits）必須基於毒理學評估（toxicological evaluation），即：

  • ADE（Acceptable Daily Exposure）：可接受日暴露量
  • HBEL（Health-Based Exposure Limit）：基於健康的暴露限值（EU EMA 指南用語）
  • PDE（Permitted Daily Exposure）：ICH Q3C 用語，同概念

傳統的 1/1000 劑量法（1/1000 dose criterion）已不符合現行要求，必須採用 HBEL/ADE。

§10.6.1 大分子/多肽的特例

治療性蛋白質和多肽（如單株抗體、胰島素）在 pH 極端或高溫下會降解失活，因此傳統毒理學計算（基於藥理活性）可能不適用。此情況下，可採用替代評估方法，如過敏原性評估或更保守的一般蛋白質殘留限度。

目視檢查不夠（§10.2）

目視清潔度檢查雖然是 AC 的一部分，但不能作為清潔確效的唯一驗收標準。必須搭配化學殘留分析（HPLC/UV/TOC）和/或微生物限度測試。

重要禁止條款：「反覆清潔和重新測試直至達到可接受殘留結果」的做法是不可接受的。清潔確效必須基於預先定義的清潔程序，而非靠重複嘗試達標。

比喻：TOC 作為替代指標

當產品殘留難以直接測量（如複雜天然產物或混合物），TOC（總有機碳）是常用替代指標：所有有機殘留都含碳，TOC 提供非專一性的「有機污染總量」指標。但需確認 TOC 方法對目標殘留的靈敏度。

10.9 – 10.15 Advanced Cleaning Validation Topics

10.9 Where campaign manufacture is carried out, the impact on the ease of cleaning at the end of the campaign should be considered and the maximum length of a campaign (in time and/or number of batches) should be the basis for cleaning validation exercises.

10.10 Where a worst case product approach is used as a cleaning validation model, a scientific rationale should be provided for the selection of the worst case product and the impact of new products to the site assessed. Criteria for determining the worst case may include solubility, cleanability, toxicity, and potency.

10.11 Cleaning validation protocols should specify or reference the locations to be sampled, the rationale for the selection of these locations and define the acceptance criteria.

10.12 Sampling should be carried out by swabbing and/or rinsing or by other means depending on the production equipment. The sampling materials and method should not influence the result. Recovery should be shown to be possible from all product contact materials sampled in the equipment with all the sampling methods used.

10.13 The cleaning procedure should be performed an appropriate number of times based on a risk assessment and meet the acceptance criteria in order to prove that the cleaning method is validated.

10.14 Where a cleaning process is ineffective or is not appropriate for some equipment, dedicated equipment or other appropriate measures should be used for each product as indicated in chapters 3 and 5 of the PIC/S GMP Guide.

10.15 Where manual cleaning of equipment is performed, it is especially important that the effectiveness of the manual process should be confirmed at a justified frequency.

進階清潔確效主題解析

最壞情況產品選擇（§10.10）

Worst Case Product 策略允許用一個（或幾個）代表性產品的清潔確效，涵蓋多個類似設備或製程。選擇最壞情況產品的標準：

  • 低溶解度（難以沖洗清除）
  • 低清潔性（如油脂類物質）
  • 高毒性/高效力（允許殘留量極低）
  • 高劑量（分母大，限度相對低）

髒保留時間與潔淨保留時間（§10.8）

Dirty Hold Time（DHT）：從最後一批生產結束到開始清潔的最長允許時間。需確認產品殘留在此期間不會形成更難清潔的狀態（如乾燥結垢）。

Clean Hold Time（CHT）：清潔完成後到設備被使用前的最長允許存放時間。需確認清潔後的設備不會在存放期間被微生物污染。

取樣回收率確認（§10.12）

無論使用棉棒取樣（swab）還是沖洗取樣（rinse），都必須確認取樣方法的回收率（recovery），即：從設備表面材質中能回收多少比例的殘留物。殘留限度計算必須校正回收率，例如回收率 70%，則分析得出的殘留量需除以 0.7 得到真實殘留量。

活動批次（Campaign）清潔的挑戰（§10.9）

連續生產同一產品多批次（campaign manufacture）後，殘留物可能更難清潔（長時間接觸、乾燥累積）。清潔確效應基於活動的最大長度（最多批次數或最長時間），以確保在最難清潔的情況下仍能達到清潔標準。

11. Change Control

變更管控 — Knowledge management & validation impact assessment

11.1 – 11.7 Change Control Requirements

11.1 The control of change is an important part of knowledge management and should be handled within the pharmaceutical quality system.

11.2 Written procedures should be in place to describe the actions to be taken if a planned change is proposed to a starting material, product component, process, equipment, premises, product range, method of production or testing, batch size, design space or any other change during the lifecycle that may affect product quality or reproducibility.

11.3 Where design space is used, the impact on changes to the design space should be considered against the registered design space within the marketing authorisation and the need for any regulatory actions assessed.

11.4 Quality risk management should be used to evaluate planned changes to determine the potential impact on product quality, pharmaceutical quality systems, documentation, validation, regulatory status, calibration, maintenance and on any other system to avoid unintended consequences and to plan for any necessary process validation, verification or requalification efforts.

11.5 Changes should be authorised and approved by the responsible persons or relevant functional personnel in accordance with the pharmaceutical quality system.

11.6 Supporting data, e.g. copies of documents, should be reviewed to confirm that the impact of the change has been demonstrated prior to final approval.

11.7 Following implementation, and where appropriate, an evaluation of the effectiveness of change should be carried out to confirm that the change has been successful.

變更管控與確效的整合

變更管控的廣泛觸發範圍（§11.2）

以下任何變更都可能觸發確效影響評估：

  • 起始物料或包材（供應商、規格）
  • 製程（參數、設備、廠房）
  • 批次規模
  • 設計空間（Design Space）
  • 生產或測試方法
  • 任何影響產品品質或重現性的變更

設計空間變更的雙軌評估（§11.3）

若使用了 Design Space（ICH Q8），變更需進行兩層評估：

  1. 科學評估：變更是否超出已確立的設計空間？
  2. 法規評估：是否超出已向法規機構登記/批准的設計空間？

在已批准的設計空間內移動不算法規層面的變更，但若超出則需申報。

變更有效性評估（§11.7）

變更實施後，需評估變更是否達到預期效果。例如：若設備升級是為了改善清潔效果，則需確認實際清潔殘留數據確實改善。這形成 PDCA（計畫-執行-確認-行動）的閉環管理。

12. Glossary

術語表 — Official definitions for key qualification & validation terms

術語表使用說明

本節定義了 Annex 15 中使用但在 PIC/S GMP 指南其他部分未定義的術語。標有 (ICH Q8)、(ICH Q9)、(ICH Q10) 的定義直接來源於對應 ICH 指南，確保與國際法規標準保持一致。

Bracketing Approach（括弧法）

科學與風險基礎的確效方法：僅測試某些預定設計因子（如規格、批次大小、包裝規格）的極端值，假設任何中間值均由極端值的確效所代表。

Change Control（變更管控）

由相關部門合格代表審查可能影響設施、系統、設備或製程確效狀態的擬議或實際變更的正式系統。目的是確定是否需要採取行動，以確保系統維持在確效狀態並加以記錄。

Cleaning Validation（清潔確效）

有文件記錄的証據，証明已核准的清潔程序可重複性地將設備中前一產品或清潔劑的殘留量減少至科學設定的最大允許攜帶量以下。

Cleaning Verification（清潔查驗）

在每批/每活動批次後通過化學分析收集証據，以証明前一產品或清潔劑的殘留量已減少至科學設定的最大允許攜帶量以下。（注意：不同於清潔「確效」）

Concurrent Validation（同步確效）

在例外情況下，基於患者重大受益的理由，在確效批次商業化的同時執行確效協議的確效。

Continuous Process Verification（持續性製程查驗）

傳統製程確效的替代方法，持續監測和評估製造製程性能。(ICH Q8)

Control Strategy（管控策略）

來自目前產品和製程理解、確保製程性能和產品品質的計畫性管控集合。管控可包括起始物料和藥品材料與組件的相關參數和屬性、設施設備操作條件、製程中管控、成品規格以及相關監測與管控方法和頻率。(ICH Q10)

Critical Process Parameter — CPP（關鍵製程參數）

其可變性對關鍵品質屬性有影響，因此應被監測或管控以確保製程產生所需品質的製程參數。(ICH Q8)

Critical Quality Attribute — CQA（關鍵品質屬性）

應在核准的限度、範圍或分佈內，以確保所需產品品質的物理、化學、生物或微生物性質或特性。(ICH Q8)

Design Qualification — DQ（設計確效）

有文件記錄的驗證，証明設施、系統和設備的擬議設計適合預定目的。

Design Space（設計空間）

已被証明能提供品質保證的輸入變數（如物料屬性）和製程參數的多維組合與交互作用。在設計空間內的操作不被視為變更；移出設計空間則被視為變更，通常會啟動法規批准後變更程序。(ICH Q8)

Installation Qualification — IQ（安裝確效）

有文件記錄的驗證，証明已安裝或修改的設施、系統和設備符合核准的設計和製造商的建議。

Knowledge Management（知識管理）

獲取、分析、儲存和傳播資訊的系統方法。(ICH Q10)

Lifecycle（生命週期）

產品、設備或設施從初始開發或使用到停止使用的所有階段。

Ongoing Process Verification — OPV（持續性製程查驗）

記錄製程在商業生產期間維持在管控狀態的証據（亦稱 continued process verification）。

Operational Qualification — OQ（操作確效）

有文件記錄的驗證，証明已安裝或修改的設施、系統和設備在預期操作範圍內如設計般運作。

Performance Qualification — PQ（性能確效）

有文件記錄的驗證，証明系統和設備能根據核准的製程方法和產品規格有效且可重複地執行。

Process Validation（製程確效）

有文件記錄的証據，証明在已確立的參數內操作的製程，能有效且可重複地生產符合其預定規格和品質屬性的藥品。

Quality by Design — QbD（品質源自設計）

從預定義目標出發，強調以健全的科學和品質風險管理為基礎的產品和製程理解與製程管控的系統方法。

Quality Risk Management（品質風險管理）

跨生命週期評估、管控、溝通和審查品質風險的系統化過程。(ICH Q9)

State of Control（管控狀態）

一組管控措施持續提供可接受製程性能和產品品質保證的條件。

User Requirements Specification — URS（使用者需求規格）

創建符合系統預定目的之可行設計所必要且足夠的業主、使用者和工程需求集合。

Worst Case（最壞情況）

在標準操作程序範圍內，包含上下處理限度和情況的條件或一組條件，與理想條件相比，最有可能導致產品或製程失敗的情況。此類條件不一定會實際引起產品或製程失敗。
