學習目標 Learning Objectives

  • 理解 ATMP 的定義範疇與 PIC/S Annex 2A 的適用對象，區分 Part A（通則）與 Part B（特定類型）的架構差異
  • 掌握 ATMP 製造中的品質風險管理 (QRM) 框架，包括 Contamination Control Strategy (CCS) 的建立邏輯與優先級排序
  • 熟悉廠房設計關鍵決策點：封閉系統分級允許條件、正負壓策略、隔離製造區 (segregated area) 的判斷依據

Scope — 範疇

Scope

The methods employed in the manufacture of Advanced Therapy Medicinal Products (ATMPs) are a critical factor in shaping the appropriate regulatory control. ATMPs can be defined therefore largely by reference to their method of manufacture.

For example, for gene therapy ATMPs, genetic modifications can be obtained through a variety of methods (e.g. viral & non-viral vectors, mRNA, ex vivo and in vivo genome-editing tools). The genetically modified cells can be of human origin (autologous or allogeneic) or of animal origin (xenogeneic cells), either primary or established cell lines. In a medicinal product, the genetically modified cells or gene therapy products can be presented alone or combined with medical devices.

This annex provides additional and specific guidance on the full range of ATMPs (as defined in the glossary) and the active substances that are used in their manufacture. This annex applies both to investigational ATMPs and market-authorised ATMPs. It can also be applied to ATMP manufacturing in hospital settings and for compassionate use programs, where authorised by national law.

This annex is divided into two main parts:

  • Part A: supplementary guidance and alternative provisions on the manufacture of ATMPs, from control over seed lots and cell banks through to finishing activities and testing.
  • Part B: further guidance on selected types of ATMPs and its substances.

範疇解析

ATMP 三大類型快速識別

  • 基因治療產品 (Gene Therapy ATMP)：透過病毒/非病毒載體、mRNA、基因編輯工具引入基因修飾
  • 細胞治療產品 (Somatic Cell Therapy ATMP)：人類細胞（自體/異體）經培養後回輸
  • 組織工程產品 (Tissue Engineered ATMP)：含有工程化細胞或組織，旨在再生/修復/替代組織

製造方法決定監管路徑

ATMP 的特殊之處在於「製造方法」本身就是產品定義的核心。CAR-T 的「從病人取血 → 病毒轉導 → 回輸」這個製程，決定了它必須遵循什麼法規。

CDMO 接案時第一個問題應是：「這個產品的製造方法讓它落在哪個 ATMP 分類？」分類決定法規義務。

適用範圍重要邊界

Annex 2A 同時適用於：臨床試驗用 ATMP（investigational）和上市後商業 ATMP（market-authorised）。在國家法規允許下，也可延伸至醫院自製和慈悲用藥場景。台灣 CDMO 為全球藥廠代工時，需確認適用 PIC/S 會員國的具體國家法規解讀。

Application of This Annex — 本附錄的適用方式

Application of This Annex

This annex, along with several other annexes of the Guide to GMP, provides guidance which supplements that in Part I: Basic Requirements for Medicinal Products and in Part II: Basic Requirements for active pharmaceutical ingredients of the PIC/S GMP Guide. This annex is not a stand-alone document and should be applied in conjunction with PIC/S GMP guidelines and annexes.

Where due to the nature of the product or technical necessities, specific guidance is provided in this annex, compliance with this annex is expected and takes precedence over other sections in the PIC/S GMP Guide unless there are good reasons for not doing so with documented sound scientific rationale applied using QRM principles.

In certain cases, other national laws may be applicable to the starting materials for ATMPs. For example:

  • (a) Tissues and cells used as starting materials of ATMPs may be subject to other national legislation that cover donation, procurement, testing, processing, preservation, storage and distribution.
  • (b) For blood or blood components used as starting materials for ATMPs, national legislation may provide the technical requirements for the selection of donors and the collection and testing of blood and blood components.

The manufacturing process for ATMPs is product-specific and different design approaches are possible. The appropriate application of GMP should be described, justified in the Clinical Trial Application (CTA) or Marketing Authorisation (MA), and in accordance with national law.

The manufacture and control of genetically modified organisms also needs to comply with other local, national or regional requirements. Appropriate containment should be established and maintained in facilities where any genetically modified organism is handled.

適用邏輯解析

法規層次優先順序

當 Annex 2A 對 ATMP 有特定規定時，優先於 PIC/S GMP Part I / Part II 的一般要求。但如有充分科學依據（以 QRM 原則支持並有文件記錄），可允許偏離。這給了 CDMO 合理的彈性空間，但代價是需要強健的風險評估文件。

起始原料的雙重法規義務

ATMP 起始原料（特別是人類組織/細胞、血液成分）除 GMP 外，還受捐贈、採購、測試等人體組織法規管轄。台灣 CDMO 如果涉及使用從海外引進的人類細胞，需確認進口許可及台灣《人體器官移植條例》相關規範。

GMP 與生物安全的雙軌合規

處理基因改造生物體（GMO）時，必須同時滿足 GMP 要求和生物安全等級（Biological Safety Level, BSL）要求。這兩者可能在某些設計上產生衝突（例如負壓艙對無菌保障的影響），Annex 2A 的 Section 3.7 對此有具體規定。

CDMO 決策點

接受 ATMP 委託製造前，合規評估清單應包含：(1) 確認產品分類 (2) 識別國家層級的起始原料法規義務 (3) 確認廠區現有 BSL 等級是否足夠 (4) 在 CTA/MA 文件中充分說明 GMP 適用策略。

Principle — 製造原則

Principle

The manufacture of ATMPs involves certain specific considerations arising from the nature of the products and the processes. The ways in which biological medicinal products are manufactured, controlled and administered make some particular precautions necessary.

Since materials and processing conditions used in manufacturing processes are designed to provide conditions for the growth of specific cells and microorganisms, this provides an opportunity for extraneous microbial contaminants (e.g. bacteria, fungi) to grow. In addition, some products may be limited in their ability to withstand a wide range of purification techniques, particularly those designed to inactivate or remove adventitious viral contaminants.

The design of the processes, equipment, facilities, utilities, the conditions of preparation and addition of buffers and reagents, sampling and training of the operators are key considerations to minimise such contamination events (i.e. engineering and technical controls). In addition, manufacturing processes need to be well designed and controlled so as not to add further variability to the product.

For biological materials that cannot be sterilized (e.g. by filtration), processing must be conducted aseptically to minimise the introduction of contaminants.

ATMPs require a combination of unique biological methods and standard physico-chemical assays for their Quality Control (QC). For many cell-based products, there is variability introduced through the starting materials that cannot be overcome by the manufacturing process or In-Process Controls (IPCs).

Adequate control of the starting and raw materials, well defined characterisation of the ATMP active substance and ATMP drug product release testing form the crucial part of the QC. Controls should take into consideration the intrinsic variability of the biological material needed for ATMP manufacturing. A robust manufacturing process is therefore crucial and in-process controls take on a particular importance in the manufacture of biological active substances and medicinal products.

ATMP 製造核心挑戰

ATMP 與傳統小分子的根本差異

傳統小分子藥品：原料藥結構明確，可用多種分析方法表徵，最終可滅菌。

ATMP：(1) 活細胞是「活的工廠」，培養條件同時適合雜菌生長；(2) 許多 ATMP 無法最終滅菌，必須無菌製造；(3) 起始原料（病患細胞）的生物學變異性無法被製程完全消除，這是 ATMP QC 最大的挑戰。

IPC 的特殊地位

傳統藥品中，製程中控制 (IPC) 是輔助工具；ATMP 中，IPC 往往是唯一能及時干預的手段。因為最終放行測試可能在產品交付前才能完成（特別是自體 ATMPs）。CDMO 必須在 IPC 設計上投入更多資源，而非依賴最終測試把關。

工程控制優先於程序控制

Annex 2A 強調「engineering and technical controls」——這意味著設計封閉系統、隔離器、適當的廠房氣流設計，比依賴人員操作規程更重要。這對 CDMO 資本支出決策有重要意義：投資硬體設施通常比訓練人員程序更能降低長期風險。

ATMP 污染風險公式

污染風險 = f(培養條件暴露時間 × 開放面積 × 操作複雜度)
                                 ÷ (工程控制強度 × 人員訓練水準)

降低風險的首要策略是縮短分子，即縮短暴露時間、縮小開放面積，並增強工程控制。

Chapter 1 — Pharmaceutical Quality System (品質管理系統)

Pharmaceutical Quality System & Quality Risk Management

1.1 ATMPs are not sold or supplied before an Authorised Person has certified that each production batch has been produced and controlled in accordance with the requirements of the CTA, MA and any other regulations relevant to the production, control and release of medicinal products as applicable. Special provisions apply for the supply of products that have a two-step release process (described in Section 6.14) or such that do not meet release specifications where there is no alternative treatment available (described in Sections 6.11 to 6.13).

(Replaces PIC/S GMP Guide Part I Section 1.4, xv)

1.2 GMP applies to the lifecycle stages from the manufacture of investigational ATMP, technology transfer, and commercial manufacturing through to product discontinuation. The biological processes may display inherent variability, so that the range and nature of by-products may be variable. As a result, Quality Risk Management (QRM) principles as detailed in Annex 20 are particularly important for this class of medicinal products and should be used to develop their control strategy across all stages of development and manufacturing steps to minimise variability and to reduce the opportunity for contamination and cross-contamination.

(Replaces PIC/S GMP Guide Part I Section 1.2)

品質管理系統重點

授權放行人 (Authorised Person) 的 ATMP 特殊義務

傳統藥品的 QP（歐洲）/ AP（台灣）放行是基於批次記錄和規格符合性。ATMP 增加了兩個特殊場景：

  • 兩步驟放行 (two-step release)：如 autologous CAR-T，在交付醫院時還需要補充最終品質確認
  • 不符規格緊急放行：當病患無其他治療選項時，不符規格產品也可能在 Competent Authority 批准下例外供應——這是 ATMP 獨有的人道主義彈性

QRM 在 ATMP 生命週期的全程應用

1.2 條明確要求 QRM 從臨床試驗製造貫穿至商業化、技術轉移直至產品停產。這意味著 CDMO 在承接技術轉移時，不能只接收 SOP，還需接收原研發廠的完整風險評估記錄，並在新製造地重新驗證風險控制措施的有效性。

生物製程的固有變異性管理

「biological processes may display inherent variability」——這句話的實踐意義是：ATMP 的 Critical Quality Attributes (CQAs) 和 Critical Process Parameters (CPPs) 設定必須容納合理的生物學變異範圍，而不是像小分子那樣追求極窄公差。CDMO 的製程驗證策略需要相應調整。

Chapter 2 — Personnel (人員)

Personnel Requirements

2.1 The health status of personnel should be taken into consideration for product safety. Personnel (including those concerned with cleaning, maintenance or quality control) employed in areas where ATMP active substances and products are manufactured and tested should receive training, and periodic retraining, specific to the products manufactured and to the duties assigned to them, including any specific safety measures to protect product, personnel and the environment.

2.2 Any changes in the health status of personnel, which could adversely affect the quality of the product, should prevent work in the production area. Health monitoring of staff should be commensurate with the risk; medical advice should be sought for personnel involved with hazardous organisms. General consideration should be given to Occupational Health & Safety (OH&S) for personnel involved with hazardous substances as required by national law.

2.3 Every person entering the manufacturing areas should wear clean protective garments appropriate to the operations to be carried out.

Where required to minimise the opportunity for cross-contamination, restrictions on the movement of all personnel (including QC, maintenance and cleaning personnel) should be controlled based on QRM principles.

In general, personnel should not pass from areas of exposure to live micro-organisms, genetically modified organisms, toxins or animals to areas where other products, inactivated products or different organisms are handled. If such route is unavoidable, a Contamination Control Strategy (CCS) based on QRM principles should be applied.

(Replaces PIC/S GMP Guide Part I Section 2.18)

ATMP 人員管理特點

雙向保護概念

ATMP 人員管理是雙向的：

  • 保護產品免於人員污染：人員健康狀態（如病毒感染）可能污染細胞培養基
  • 保護人員免於產品危害：病毒載體、基因改造生物體對人員構成潛在生物安全風險

這在傳統無菌製造中不存在——操作工作站人員通常只需保護產品，不需要特別的自身生物防護。

人員流動是污染控制的關鍵變數

2.3 條的核心要求：接觸「活微生物、GMO、毒素、動物」的人員，不得直接進入其他產品區域。這在多產品 CDMO 廠房中是高風險設計點。如果人員動線不可避免地交叉，必須有基於 QRM 的 CCS（污染控制策略）文件支撐。

實務設計建議

多產品 ATMP CDMO 的人員管理最佳實務：(1) 在平面設計時就規劃單向動線；(2) 建立「區域專屬人員」制度，避免人員在高風險區與低風險區之間流動；(3) 在 OH&S 計畫中納入定期血清學監測（如涉及特定病毒載體）。

稽核常見查核點

稽核員在人員章節通常查核：培訓記錄是否涵蓋 ATMP 特定安全措施？健康監測頻率是否與風險等級相符？更衣程序是否區分不同產品區域？人員動線圖是否與 CCS 文件一致？

Chapter 3 — Premises: Production Areas (廠房：生產區域)

Production Areas

3.1 Cross-contamination should be prevented for all products by appropriate design and operation of manufacturing facilities. The measures to prevent cross-contamination should be commensurate with the risks to product quality. QRM principles should be used to assess and control the risks.

Depending on the level of risk presented by some ATMPs, it may be necessary to dedicate premises and equipment for manufacturing and/or packaging operations to control the risk. Segregated production areas should be used for the manufacture of ATMPs presenting a risk that cannot be adequately controlled by operational and/or technical measures.

(Replaces PIC/S GMP Guide Part I Section 3.6)

3.2 Concurrent production of two or more different ATMPs/batches in the same area might be permitted where justified under QRM principles. For example:

  • (a) More than one closed isolator in the same room at the same time is acceptable, provided appropriate mitigation measures are taken.
  • (b) When more than one isolator is used to process different viral vectors in the same room, there should be 100% air exhaustion (no recirculation). In case of concurrent production of viral vectors, closed, separate and unidirectional waste handling is required.
  • (c) More than one biosafety cabinet (BSC) in the same room is only acceptable if effective technical and organisational measures are implemented to separate the activities.
  • (d) Multiple closed systems in the same area are permitted if their closed state can be demonstrated.

3.3 The measures and procedures necessary for containment (i.e. for environment and operator safety) should not conflict with those for product quality.

3.4 Special precautions should be taken for manufacturing activities involving infectious viral vectors (e.g. oncolytic viruses, replication competent vectors) that should be segregated based on a documented CCS and QRM principles. Simultaneous incubation and/or storage of replication competent vectors/products, or infected materials/products, with other materials/products is not acceptable.

廠房設計核心決策

隔離層級決策框架

廠房隔離要求由風險等級決定，由低到高：

  • Level 1：操作/技術控制即可（如 closed isolators 共用空間）
  • Level 2：需要 segregated production area（隔離生產區）
  • Level 3：需要 dedicated facility（專用廠房）

判斷依據：QRM 結果 + CCS 文件 + 國家法規要求。

3.2(b) 的病毒載體同區生產紅線

多種病毒載體同區同時生產時，100% 新鮮風（無循環）是硬性要求，這對 HVAC 系統是重大設計挑戰。台灣 CDMO 如計劃在同一廠房處理多種病毒（如 AAV、慢病毒），必須在建廠初期就規劃獨立的排氣系統，而非後期改造。

3.4 的絕對禁止：複製型載體的同時儲存/孵育

「replication competent vectors」（有複製能力的載體）與其他物料不得同時孵育或儲存，這是無條件禁止（"not acceptable"），沒有 QRM 豁免空間。設計儲存系統時必須確保物理隔離。

生物安全 vs. 無菌保障的矛盾

3.3 條提醒：生物安全（保護人員和環境）和產品品質（無菌保障）有時會相互衝突。例如，為了生物安全使用負壓艙，可能影響潔淨室的正壓維持。這個矛盾必須在 CCS 中明確處理，不能只顧其一。

Chapter 3 — Premises: Environmental Control & Air Classification (環境控制與潔淨度分級)

Environmental Control and Air Classification

3.5 Air handling units should be designed, constructed and maintained to minimise the risk of cross-contamination between different manufacturing areas and may need to be specific for an area. Consideration, based on QRM principles, should be given to the use of single pass air systems.

3.6 If materials (such as culture media and buffers) have to be measured or weighed during the production process, small stocks may be kept in the production area for a specified duration based on defined criteria.

(Replaces PIC/S GMP Guide Part I Section 3.13)

3.7 Positive pressure areas should be used to process sterile products, but negative pressure in specific areas at the point of exposure of pathogens is acceptable for containment reasons. Where negative pressure areas or BSCs are used for aseptic processing, they should be surrounded by a positive pressure clean zone of appropriate Grade. These pressure cascades should be clearly defined and continuously monitored with appropriate alarm settings as defined by Annex 1.

3.8 Air vent filters that are directly linked to the sterility of the product should be hydrophobic, monitored during use and validated for their scheduled life span with integrity testing at appropriate intervals. For short shelf life ATMPs, where microbiological quality tests are not available at the time of batch release prior to medical product administration, the filter integrity should be considered in the CCS as an additional risk factor.

3.9 Drainage systems must be designed so that effluents can be effectively neutralised or decontaminated to minimise the risk of cross-contamination. They must comply with national law to minimize the risk of contamination of the external environment according to the risk associated with the biohazardous nature of waste materials.

(Replaces PIC/S GMP Guide Part I Section 3.11)

3.10 The degree of environmental control should be adapted to the product and the production step, bearing in mind the potential level of contamination of the starting materials and the risks to the product. The microbiological environmental monitoring programme should be supplemented by methods to detect the presence of specific microorganisms (e.g. host organism, yeasts, moulds, anaerobes) where indicated by QRM principles.

3.11 Where processes are not closed and there is exposure of the product to the immediate room environment without a subsequent microbial inactivation process, appropriate environmental conditions should be applied. For aseptic manipulations, parameters in line with Annex 1 (i.e. Grade A with Grade B background) should be applied.

3.12 Only in exceptional circumstances when an appropriate manufacturing environment is not available, a less stringent environment than that specified in Section 3.11 may be acceptable for processes that are not closed where approved by the Competent Authority and in accordance with CTA or MA. However, this option should be considered exceptional and applicable only if the product is intended to treat a life-threatening condition where no alternative therapeutic options exist.

3.13 For closed systems, a lower classified area than Grade A in background Grade B might be acceptable based on the outcome of a QRM assessment:

  • (a) Processing inside single use sterile disposable kits, or using closed, automated manufacturing platform or incubation in closed flasks, bags or fermenters in Grade C may be acceptable if adequate control measures are implemented.
  • (b) If the closed system can be shown to remain integral throughout the entire usage, a background of Grade D might be acceptable.

3.14 In exceptional circumstances, it is permissible to perform a manufacturing step in premises not under direct control of the ATMP manufacturer or MAH (including placing equipment in hospital wards or theatre) where approved by the Competent Authority. Responsibilities of each party should be defined in written technical agreements.

環境控制策略解析

ATMP 潔淨度分級決策樹

開放製程（open process）：無後續微生物滅活步驟

  • 無菌操作 → Grade A + Grade B 背景（與 Annex 1 一致）
  • 例外：無其他治療選項的威脅生命疾病 → 可向主管機關申請豁免

封閉製程（closed process）：QRM 評估決定

  • 封閉但定期開啟 → Grade C 可接受
  • 全程保持密封完整性 → Grade D 可能可接受

3.8 短效期 ATMP 的過濾器風險

這是 ATMP 特有的難題：autologous CAR-T 等產品可能在無菌測試結果出爐前就已注射給病患。此時，「通氣過濾器是否完整」變成唯一可在放行前確認的間接無菌指標。CCS 必須包含此項風險的緩解措施，例如：每批次前後均進行完整性測試、強化操作員訓練、設定更嚴格的壓差監測閾值。

3.9 廢水處理是監管稽查熱點

生物危害廢水（如含病毒的培養廢液）的排放系統設計，必須同時滿足 GMP 和環境保護法規。台灣若設立 ATMP CDMO，需提前諮詢環保署和農委會關於 GMO 廢水的特殊排放標準。

3.14 「院區製造」的合規架構

某些 ATMP（特別是個人化療法）可能需要在醫院手術室完成最後一個製造步驟（如細胞回輸前的最終準備）。此時設備雖在醫院，但必須：(1) 主管機關事先批准；(2) 製造商與醫院之間有書面技術協議；(3) 在醫院中的操作維持驗證狀態。這對 CDMO 的品質合約管理能力是重大挑戰。

廠房設計稽查準備要點

稽查員在廠房章節重點查核：(1) 壓差設計圖及連續監測記錄；(2) 單程通風（single pass）的設計依據文件；(3) 封閉系統的完整性驗證記錄；(4) 負壓艙（如使用）如何確保無菌保障不被影響；(5) 廢水消毒系統的驗證報告。

Chapter 3 — Equipment (設備)

Equipment Requirements

3.15 Production equipment should not present any hazard to the products. The parts of the production equipment that come into contact with the product must not be reactive, additive or absorptive to such an extent that it will affect the quality of the product.

If single use systems (SUS) (i.e. disposable systems) are used, the manufacturer should take into account and verify the impact on the product from extractables, leachables, insoluble particulate and insoluble matter derived from such systems. Annex 1 regarding provisions for single use systems should be considered.

(Replaces PIC/S GMP Guide Part I Section 3.39)

3.16 Where required to minimise the risk of cross-contamination, restrictions on the movement of equipment should be applied. In general, equipment should not be moved from high-risk areas to other areas, or between high-risk areas (e.g. equipment used for the handling of cells from infected donors or the handling of oncolytic viruses). Where relocation is unavoidable, the risk should be assessed in line with QRM principles, mitigated and monitored.

3.17 The design of equipment used during handling of live organisms and cells, including those for sampling, should be considered to prevent any contamination during processing.

3.18 Primary containment should be designed and periodically tested to ensure the prevention of escape of biological agents into the immediate working environment.

3.19 Electronic systems used to support manufacturing must be qualified in accordance with Annex 11 and 15. Any analytical testing performed on materials not used in manufacturing but that support bioinformatics informing the manufacturing process (e.g. patient gene sequencing) should be validated. Such analytical equipment is expected to be qualified prior to use.

設備管理特殊要求

SUS (Single Use Systems) 的 E&L 評估義務

一次性使用系統 (SUS) 在 ATMP 中極為普遍（生物反應器袋、管路、過濾器），因為它消除了清洗驗證需求並降低交叉污染風險。但 3.15 條要求評估：

  • Extractables（可萃取物）：在溶劑/強烈條件下可能從材料遷移的化學物質
  • Leachables（可浸出物）：在實際使用條件下遷移至產品的物質
  • 不溶性微粒：可能對細胞治療產品造成毒性

3.18 一次性圍阻設備的定期測試義務

Primary containment（初級圍阻）包括手套箱、生物安全艙的密封系統。「定期測試」這個要求意味著需要建立測試頻率的 SOP，並保存記錄作為稽查文件。頻率應基於 QRM 評估，考慮設備使用強度和生物風險等級。

3.19 病患基因定序的設備資格

個人化 ATMP（如 autologous gene therapy）的製造通常從病患基因定序開始，以決定治療策略。3.19 條要求：這類「支持製造決策的生物資訊分析」設備和軟體也需要驗證和資格確認，即使這些設備本身不直接接觸藥品。這對 CDMO 的數位系統驗證範疇有重要意義。

設備稽查準備清單

ATMP 設備稽查關鍵文件：(1) SUS 供應商的 E&L 研究報告（含 AET 評估）；(2) SUS 完整性測試記錄（使用前後）；(3) Primary containment 定期測試記錄；(4) 生物資訊系統的 Annex 11 資格確認文件；(5) 設備移動風險評估記錄（如有）。

Chapter 4 — Documentation: Specifications & Traceability (文件管理：規格與可追溯性)

Documentation Requirements

Specifications

4.1 Specifications for ATMP starting and raw materials may need additional documentation on the source, origin, distribution chain, method of manufacture, and controls applied, to assure an appropriate level of control and oversight including their microbiological quality.

4.2 Some products may require specific definition of what materials constitute a batch. For autologous and donor-matched situations, the manufactured product should be viewed as a batch.

Traceability

4.3 Where human cells or tissues are used, full traceability is required from starting and raw materials, including all substances coming into contact with the cells or tissues through to confirmation of the receipt of the products at the point of use whilst maintaining the privacy of individuals and confidentiality of health-related information, according to national legislation.

4.4 For starting materials of human origin, the identification of the supplier and the anatomical environment from which the cells/tissues/virus originates (or, as appropriate, the identification of the cell-line, master cell bank, seed lot) should also be described.

4.5 A system that enables the bidirectional tracking of cells/tissues contained in ATMPs from the point of donation, through manufacturing, to the delivery of the finished product to the recipient should be created. This system can be manual or automated. It should be used throughout the manufacturing lifecycle to include clinical trial and commercial batches.

4.6 Traceability records should be kept as an auditable document and unequivocally linked to the relevant batch record. The storage system should ensure that traceability data allow for easy access, in case of an adverse reaction from the patient.

4.7 Traceability records for cellular and tissue-based products and for any personalized ATMP must be retained 30 years after the expiry date of the product unless otherwise specified in the MA/CTA or national law. Particular care should be taken to maintain the traceability of products for special use cases, such as donor-matched cells.

4.8 When xenogeneic cells are used as starting materials for ATMPs, information permitting the identification of the donor animal should be kept for 30 years unless otherwise specified in the MA/CTA or national legislation.

文件管理重點解析

「一病人一批次」的批次定義革命

傳統藥品的批次定義是「同一次製造過程中均一的一批材料」。ATMP 的 4.2 條要求：對於 autologous（自體）和 donor-matched（配對捐贈者）的產品，「那個病人的那次製造就是一個批次」。這從根本上改變了：

  • 批次記錄的設計（每個病患一份完整記錄）
  • 批次放行的工作量（批次可能多達每週數十個）
  • LIMS 系統的設計需求

雙向追溯 (bidirectional tracking) 的系統需求

4.5 條要求的雙向追溯意味著：

  • 正向追溯：從捐贈者 → 製造 → 病患（發現供應鏈問題時追蹤影響範圍）
  • 反向追溯：從病患不良反應 → 回溯製造批次 → 回到捐贈者（不良事件調查）

這要求資料系統在整個製造生命週期中保持完整的資料鏈，且在不良反應發生時能「快速」取用。

30年保存規則的實務挑戰

30年的追溯記錄保存是個系統挑戰：(1) 電子系統的長期資料完整性（軟體升級、格式遷移）；(2) 保存成本；(3) 個人資料保護法（GDPR 等）與強制保存義務的衝突。台灣 CDMO 需在 QMS 中建立專門的長期記錄保存政策，並評估資料主權與法規義務的平衡。

異種移植細胞（xenogeneic cells）的額外義務

使用豬或其他動物細胞作為起始原料時，不只要追蹤人類捐贈者，還要能識別供體動物（donor animal）。這觸及動物福利記錄、SPF（specific pathogen free）農場認證等額外的供應鏈文件需求。

CDMO 文件系統設計建議

為 ATMP 設計文件管理系統時需考慮：(1) 是否採用電子批次記錄系統（eBR）以應對高批次頻率；(2) 追溯資料庫需能存取個人健康資訊但同時加密保護；(3) 建立 30年資料生命週期管理計畫（包含媒體遷移策略）；(4) 設計系統能在不良反應發生後的 24 小時內提供完整追溯鏈。

Part A 第一部分總結 — Key Takeaways for CDMO Operations

  • 風險驅動架構：Annex 2A 全篇以 QRM 為核心，廠房分級、人員管理、設備選用、文件要求都基於風險評估，而非固定規則。CDMO 必須建立強健的 QRM 流程，並保存決策依據文件。
  • 封閉系統是 ATMP 的關鍵競爭力：3.13 條允許封閉系統在 Grade C 甚至 Grade D 背景下操作，意味著高度自動化的封閉製程可以降低廠房建設成本，同時提高污染控制能力——這是 ATMP CDMO 資本投資的重要考量方向。
  • CCS 文件是稽查核心：污染控制策略 (CCS) 在本文件中多次出現，是連結廠房設計、人員管理、製程控制的中樞文件。台灣 CDMO 應優先建立完整且有科學依據的 CCS。
  • 自體產品的批次管理革命：每個病患即為一個批次，要求 QMS 在人力、系統、流程上做好批次量劇增的準備，特別是 AP 放行能量和追溯系統的擴展性。
