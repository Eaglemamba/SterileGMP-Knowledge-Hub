Section 7: Personnel

第七節：人員
PIC/S Annex 1 (2022) — Manufacture of Sterile Medicinal Products | p18 - p20

本節學習目標

  • 理解人員是無菌製程中首要污染源，掌握「最少人數原則」及其對無菌保證的重要性
  • 熟悉各潔淨等級（Grade A/B vs C vs D）的更衣要求差異，以及無菌著裝確效 (Aseptic Gowning Qualification) 的程序與頻率
  • 瞭解人員健康管理、排除標準（脫落生物體 Shedding Organisms）與進入受限區域的規定
  • 掌握人員在潔淨室的行為規範、服裝管理（清洗驗證、最大使用次數）及活性微生物監測要求

本節核心原則 Core Principle of This Section

人員是無菌製程中最大的污染威脅。即使在最先進的隔離器 (Isolator) 技術下，人員仍透過操作、干預、設計決策等間接方式影響無菌保證。在 RABS（限制進出屏障系統）和開放式充填操作中，人員更是直接的污染風險來源。Section 7 的所有要求都圍繞這一核心原則展開：如何通過訓練、更衣、健康管理和行為規範，將人員污染風險最小化。

7.1–7.3 人員資格與訓練 Personnel Qualification & Training

原文內容 Original Text

7.1 The manufacturer should ensure that there are sufficient appropriate personnel, suitably qualified, trained and experienced in the manufacture and testing of sterile products, and any of the specific manufacturing technologies used in the site's manufacturing operations, to ensure compliance with GMP applicable to the manufacture and handling of sterile products.

7.2 Only the minimum number of personnel required should be present in cleanrooms. The maximum number of operators in cleanrooms should be determined, documented and considered during activities such as initial qualification and APS, so as not to compromise sterility assurance.

7.3 All personnel including those performing cleaning, maintenance, monitoring and those that access cleanrooms should receive regular training, gowning qualification and assessment in disciplines relevant to the correct manufacture of sterile products. This training should include the basic elements of microbiology and hygiene, with a specific focus on cleanroom practices, contamination control, aseptic techniques and the protection of sterile products (for those operators entering the grade B cleanrooms and/or intervening into grade A) and the potential safety implications to the patient if the product is not sterile. The level of training should be based on the criticality of the function and area in which the personnel are working.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

最少人數原則 (Minimum Personnel Principle)：每增加一個人，就增加一個污染源。人體每分鐘脫落約 10 萬個微粒，即使穿著無菌服裝，也無法完全阻隔。因此，Grade A/B 區的人員數量必須經過正式風險評估，在 APS（無菌製程模擬）時確認，並以文件規定最大人數。

訓練的三個層次：

  • 基礎層：微生物學基礎、個人衛生（所有接觸潔淨室人員）
  • 中階層：潔淨室行為、污染控制、更衣確效（Grade C/D 人員）
  • 高階層：無菌技術、APS 參與、患者安全意識（Grade A/B 人員）

比喻說明 Analogy

潔淨室的人員管理就像「手術室的外科團隊管理」——手術室只允許必要的外科醫生、麻醉師和護士進入，每個人都有嚴格的無菌培訓和認證，而且每個人進入都是一個風險決策。不允許額外人員「旁觀」，因為每個人都是潛在的污染源。PIC/S 7.2 的邏輯完全相同：潔淨室不是開放參觀的場所，每個進入者的資格與必要性都必須被審核。

重點提示 Key Notes

訓練必須告知操作員「若產品不無菌，對患者的潛在安全影響」——這是 PIC/S 2022 版新增強調的要點。理由是：當操作員理解自己的每一個動作可能影響患者生死，才能真正建立「無菌意識 (Aseptic Mindset)」，而不只是機械地遵從程序。

清潔人員、維護人員、環境監測人員也必須接受相同的訓練和更衣確效——他們雖然不直接操作產品，但進入潔淨室本身就是污染風險。這一點在稽核中常被忽略。

7.4–7.6 無菌著裝確效與取消資格 Aseptic Gowning Qualification & Disqualification

原文內容 Original Text

7.4 The personnel accessing grade A and B areas should be trained for aseptic gowning and aseptic behaviours. Compliance with aseptic gowning procedures should be confirmed by assessment and periodic reassessment at least annually, and should involve both visual and microbial assessment (using monitoring locations such as gloved fingers, forearms, chest and hood (facemask / forehead). See paragraph 9.30 for the expected limits). The unsupervised access to the grade A and grade B areas where aseptic operations are or will be conducted should be restricted to appropriately qualified personnel, who have passed the gowning assessment and have participated in a successful APS.

7.5 Unqualified personnel should not enter grade B cleanrooms or grade A in operation. If needed in exceptional cases, manufacturers should establish written procedures outlining the process by which unqualified personnel are brought into the grade B and A areas. An authorized person from the manufacturer should supervise the unqualified personnel during their activities and should assess the impact of these activities on the cleanliness of the area. Access by these persons should be assessed and recorded in accordance with the PQS.

7.6 There should be systems in place for the disqualification of personnel from working in or given unsupervised entry into cleanrooms that is based on aspects including ongoing assessment and/or identification of an adverse trend from the personnel monitoring programme and/or after being implicated in a failed APS. Once disqualified, retraining and requalification should be completed before permitting the operator to have any further involvement in aseptic practices. For operators entering grade B cleanrooms or performing intervention into grade A, this requalification should include consideration of participation in a successful APS.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

無菌著裝確效 (Aseptic Gowning Qualification)：評估操作員是否能正確穿著無菌服裝而不污染自身。評估包含兩個面向：

  • 目視評估 (Visual Assessment)：觀察更衣程序是否符合 SOP（順序、手套消毒時機、是否觸碰外表面）
  • 微生物評估 (Microbial Assessment)：更衣完成後，在戴手套的手指、前臂、胸部、頭套等位置取樣，確認微生物計數符合 9.30 節規定的限度

進入 Grade A/B 的雙重資格要求：必須同時滿足「通過更衣評估」AND「參與並通過 APS」兩個條件，才能獲准無監督進入。兩者缺一不可。

比喻說明 Analogy

更衣確效就像「外科手術刷手的資格認證」——不是每個醫學院學生都能自動進入手術室，必須通過手術刷手的正確性評估（目視）和培養結果確認（微生物）。一旦發現刷手不合格（或術後培養異常），必須重新訓練後才能再次進入手術室。APS（無菌製程模擬）的要求類似外科住院醫師的「第一次獨立手術」——必須在監督下成功完成後，才能獲得獨立執刀資格。

重點提示 Key Notes

取消資格 (Disqualification) 制度的三個觸發條件：

  • 持續評估中發現問題（如目視評估多次不通過）
  • 人員監測計畫中發現不良趨勢（如個人更衣後微生物計數持續偏高）
  • 被牽涉於失敗的 APS（即使不是主要操作者，參與本身也可能觸發重新評估）

取消資格後必須完成再訓練 AND 再確效。對於 Grade B/A 人員，再確效需考慮參與一次成功的 APS——這意味著重新獲得資格的過程可能需要數週。CDMO 必須確保有足夠的「備援合格人員」，以避免生產瓶頸。

更衣確效頻率要求

更衣確效週期：
  Grade A/B 人員：至少每年一次 (annually)
  新進人員：初始確效 → 通過後才能獨立進入
  失敗或 APS 失敗後：立即取消資格 → 再訓練 → 再確效

更衣微生物評估取樣位置（7.4 指定）：
  - 戴手套的手指 (gloved fingers)
  - 前臂 (forearms)
  - 胸部 (chest)
  - 頭套/面罩/前額 (hood/facemask/forehead)

參考限度：見 PIC/S Annex 1 第 9.30 節
（表格規定 Grade A/B 更衣後人員監測限度）

7.7–7.9 健康管理與個人衛生 Health Monitoring & Personal Hygiene

原文內容 Original Text

7.7 High standards of personal hygiene and cleanliness are essential to prevent excessive shedding or increased risk of introduction of microbial contamination. Personnel involved in the manufacture of sterile products should be instructed to report any specific health conditions or ailments which may cause the shedding of abnormal numbers or types of contaminants and therefore preclude cleanroom access. Health conditions and actions to be taken with regard to personnel who could be introducing an undue microbial hazard should be provided by the designated competent person and described in procedures.

7.8 Personnel who have been engaged in the processing of human or animal tissue materials or of cultures of micro-organisms, other than those used in the current manufacturing process, or any activities that may have a negative impact to quality (e.g. microbial contamination), should not enter clean areas unless clearly defined and effective decontamination and entry procedures have been followed and documented.

7.9 Wristwatches, make-up, jewellery, other personal items such as mobile phones and any other non-essential items should not be allowed in clean areas. Electronic devices used in cleanrooms, e.g. mobile phones and tablets, that are supplied by the manufacturer solely for use in the cleanrooms, may be acceptable if suitably designed to permit cleaning and disinfection commensurate with the grade in which they are used. The use and disinfection of such equipment should be included in the CCS.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

脫落生物體 (Shedding Organisms)：人體持續脫落皮膚細胞、毛髮、呼吸道分泌物等，這些攜帶微生物的顆粒是潔淨室中微生物污染的主要來源。以下健康狀況會顯著增加脫落量：

  • 皮膚疾病（濕疹、牛皮癬、傷口）
  • 上呼吸道感染（感冒、鼻竇炎）
  • 腸胃道感染（沙門氏菌、諾羅病毒）
  • 近期接觸動物組織或微生物培養物

員工有義務「主動回報」上述狀況——這必須成為企業文化的一部分，而非員工擔心請假被扣薪而隱瞞。

比喻說明 Analogy

個人健康管理就像「廚師的食品安全健康規定」——患有腸胃道感染的廚師不能上崗，因為他的手、呼吸、甚至汗水都可能污染食物。製藥廠的無菌操作員也一樣：感冒的操作員打個噴嚏，哪怕戴著口罩，噴出的氣溶膠微粒仍可能穿越防護，污染臨近的開口容器。這不是苛求，是對使用者安全的基本保障。

重點提示 Key Notes

7.8 的跨污染風險：在研發中心或多功能廠房中，同一名操作員可能上午在微生物實驗室培養菌株，下午進入無菌充填區——這是絕對禁止的。必須有明確的「去污染與進入程序 (decontamination and entry procedures)」，且必須文件記錄。

7.9 廠商供應的電子設備：智慧型手機、平板電腦在現代製造中越來越普遍，但必須滿足：(1) 廠商供應（非個人設備）；(2) 設計允許清潔消毒；(3) 納入 CCS。個人手機是絕對禁止的，因為無法確保消毒的有效性。

實務應用 Practical Application

在 CDMO 人員管理實務中，建立一個「健康回報系統」至關重要，同時需要配套的支持措施：例如患病員工有支薪病假（避免因擔心收入而帶病上班），以及明確的回報流程（向誰回報、回報後如何安排）。此外，對於接觸動物組織或細菌培養物的研發人員，需要建立「工作隔離時間表」，規定在接觸後需要等待多長時間（或完成哪些去污程序）才能進入潔淨區。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 一名操作員早晨在 QC 實驗室進行了沙門氏菌標準品的操作，下午被分配去 Grade C 區協助清潔工作。你作為班組長應如何處理？
  2. 管理層考慮在 Grade B 更衣室安裝廠商提供的平板電腦，用於即時查閱 SOP。這需要在 CCS 中增加哪些內容？
7.10–7.13 各等級更衣要求 Gowning Requirements by Cleanroom Grade

原文內容 Original Text

7.10 Cleanroom gowning and hand washing should follow a written procedure designed to minimize contamination of cleanroom clothing and/or the transfer of contaminants to the clean areas.

7.11 The clothing and its quality should be appropriate for the process and the grade of the working area. It should be worn in such a way as to protect the product from contamination. When the type of clothing chosen needs to provide the operator protection from the product, it should not compromise the protection of the product from contamination. Garments should be visually checked for cleanliness and integrity immediately prior to and after gowning. Gown integrity should also be checked upon exit. For sterilised garments and eye coverings, particular attention should be taken to ensure they have been subject to the sterilisation process, are within their specified hold time and that the packaging is visually inspected to ensure it is integral before use. Reusable garments (including eye coverings) should be replaced if damage is identified, or at a set frequency that is determined during qualification studies. The qualification of garments should consider any necessary garment testing requirements, including damage to garments that may not be identified by visual inspection alone.

7.12 Clothing should be chosen to limit shedding due to operators' movement.

7.13 A description of typical clothing required for each cleanliness grade is given below:

  • i. Grade B (including access / interventions into grade A): appropriate garments that are dedicated for use under a sterilised suit should be worn before gowning. Appropriately sterilised, non-powdered, rubber or plastic gloves should be worn while donning the sterilised garments. Sterile headgear should enclose all hair (including facial hair) and where separate from the rest of the gown, it should be tucked into the neck of the sterile suit. A sterile facemask and sterile eye coverings (e.g. goggles) should be worn to cover and enclose all facial skin and prevent the shedding of droplets and particles. Appropriate sterilised footwear (e.g. over-boots) should be worn. Trouser legs should be tucked inside the footwear. Garment sleeves should be tucked into a second pair of sterile gloves worn over the pair worn while donning the gown. The protective clothing should minimize shedding of fibres or particles and retain particles shed by the body. The particle shedding and the particle retention efficiencies of the garments should be assessed during the garment qualification. Garments should be packed and folded in such a way as to allow operators to don the gown without contacting the outer surface of the garment and to prevent the garment from touching the floor.
  • ii. Grade C: Hair, beards and moustaches should be covered. A single or two-piece trouser suit gathered at the wrists and with high neck and appropriately disinfected shoes or overshoes should be worn. They should minimize the shedding of fibres and particles.
  • iii. Grade D: Hair, beards and moustaches should be covered. A general protective suit and appropriately disinfected shoes or overshoes should be worn. Appropriate measures should be taken to avoid any ingress of contaminants from outside the clean area.
  • iv. Additional gowning including gloves and facemask may be required in grade C and D areas when performing activities considered to be a contamination risk as defined by the CCS.

核心概念解析 Key Concepts — 各等級更衣要求對照

四個等級的更衣要求從嚴格到基本遞減：

等級關鍵更衣要求手套要求
Grade B
（含進入 A 區）		全身無菌連身衣、頭套（含面部毛髮）、口罩、護目鏡、無菌靴套；褲腳塞入靴套，袖子塞入雙層手套雙層無菌手套（穿衣時＋穿衣後再加一副）
Grade C覆蓋頭髮鬍鬚、單/雙件連體褲（袖口收緊、高領）、消毒鞋或鞋套依 CCS 需要時加戴

比喻說明 Analogy

Grade B 的全套無菌更衣就像「太空衣」——每一個縫隙都必須密封，任何暴露的皮膚都是潛在的污染入口。護目鏡不只是眼部保護——它封閉了臉部皮膚暴露的最後一個區域（眼睛周圍）。雙層手套的設計也很精妙：穿衣時戴第一層（用來觸碰已消毒的無菌衣包裝），穿衣完成後再戴第二層（確保接觸產品環境的手套是在完全無菌條件下穿戴的）。每一個設計細節都是為了阻斷污染路徑。

重點提示 Key Notes

服裝目視檢查（7.11）的三個時間點：

  • 穿著前：檢查清潔度和完整性（確保包裝未破損、服裝無破洞）
  • 穿著後：確認穿著正確（所有縫隙封閉、袖口收進手套）
  • 脫除時：再次檢查完整性（損壞的服裝可能代表穿著過程中的完整性失效，需調查是否影響當班的無菌操作）

服裝保質期 (Hold Time) 管理：已滅菌服裝有有效期限（由確效研究決定）。使用前必須確認服裝仍在保質期內，過期未使用的服裝必須退回重新滅菌。這在日常管理中容易被疏漏，是稽核常見發現項目。

實務應用 Practical Application

服裝確效 (Garment Qualification) 是 CDMO 廠房啟用前的重要項目，通常包含：粒子脫落測試（ISO 9073 或等效）、粒子截留測試、清洗耐用性測試（評估最大清洗次數後的性能）。CDMO 選擇服裝供應商時，除了價格，還必須考慮供應商是否能提供完整的確效支持資料（Certificate of Compliance、粒子測試報告）。若服裝品質不達標，每一次更衣都是一次潛在的污染事件。

7.14–7.18 更衣室管理、服裝清洗與無菌行為 Gowning Rooms, Laundry & Aseptic Behaviour

原文內容 Original Text

7.14 Cleanroom gowning should be performed in change rooms of an appropriate cleanliness grade to ensure gown cleanliness is maintained. Outdoor clothing including socks (other than personal underwear) should not be brought into changing rooms leading directly to grade B and C areas. Single or two-piece facility trouser suits, covering the full length of the arms and the legs, and facility socks covering the feet, should be worn before entry to change rooms for grades B and C. Facility suits and socks should not present a risk of contamination to the gowning area or processes.

7.15 Every operator entering grade B or A areas should gown into clean, sterilised protective garments (including eye coverings and masks) of an appropriate size at each entry. The maximum period for which the sterilised gown may be worn before replacement during a shift should be defined as part of the garment qualification.

7.16 Gloves should be regularly disinfected during operations. Garments and gloves should be changed immediately if they become damaged and present any risk of product contamination.

7.17 Reusable clean area clothing should be cleaned in a laundry facility adequately segregated from production operations, using a qualified process ensuring that the clothing is not damaged and/or contaminated by fibres or particles during the repeated laundry process. After washing and before packing, garments should be visually inspected for damage and visual cleanliness. The garment management processes should be evaluated and determined as part of the garment qualification programme and should include a maximum number of laundry and sterilisation cycles.

7.18 Activities in clean areas that are not critical to the production processes should be kept to a minimum, especially when aseptic operations are in progress. Movement of personnel should be slow, controlled and methodical to avoid excessive shedding of particles and organisms due to over-vigorous activity. Operators performing aseptic operations should adhere to aseptic technique at all times to prevent changes in air currents that may introduce air of lower quality into the critical zone. Movement adjacent to the critical zone should be restricted and the obstruction of the path of the unidirectional (first air) airflow should be avoided. A review of airflow visualisation studies should be considered as part of the training programme.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

更衣室的等級鏈 (Cascade of Gowning Rooms)：更衣必須在一個「足夠潔淨等級的更衣室」進行。若在污染的環境中穿無菌衣，無菌衣的外表面立刻被環境微粒污染。典型的更衣鏈：

  • 室外服 → Grade D 更衣室（換廠服）
  • 廠服 → Grade C 更衣室（換 Grade C 作業服）
  • Grade C 作業服 → Grade B 更衣室（換無菌全套服裝）

服裝最大穿著時間：由確效研究決定，通常為一個班次（8-12小時）或更短。超過最大穿著時間必須更換，這是防止服裝內部積累微生物的重要管控點。

比喻說明 Analogy

無菌操作的行為規範就像「茶道的動作美學」——每個動作都要緩慢、有意識、有控制。快速移動會產生湍流，打亂 Grade A 區精心設計的單向氣流，讓來自低潔淨區的空氣「捲入」關鍵區域。這不只是規矩，而是有明確的氣流物理學基礎：氣流可視化研究 (Airflow Visualisation Study) 用煙霧試驗 (smoke study) 實際展示人員動作如何影響氣流路徑——這正是 7.18 要求將氣流可視化研究納入培訓計畫的原因。

重點提示 Key Notes

手套定期消毒（7.16）：手套的定期消毒（通常每 30 分鐘或每次觸碰非無菌表面後）是無菌操作的基本要求，必須在 SOP 中明確規定頻率和方式（IPA 噴霧或浸泡）。

服裝清洗管理的四個合規要點（7.17）：

  • 清洗設施必須與生產操作充分隔離（防止交叉污染）
  • 清洗程序必須經過確效（確保不損壞服裝纖維、不引入污染）
  • 清洗後目視檢查（損壞的服裝需丟棄）
  • 規定最大清洗與滅菌次數（超過限制的服裝必須報廢）

第一氣流 (First Air) 概念：從 HEPA 過濾器出來的最純淨氣流，稱為「First Air」。任何物體（人員、設備、手臂）橫越 First Air 的路徑，都可能在關鍵區域產生微渦流，讓攜帶微生物的氣溶膠進入。操作員必須學會「讓 First Air 不受阻礙地到達臨界表面」。

服裝生命週期管理框架

服裝確效程序（典型）：
  初始確效：
    - 粒子脫落測試（新品）
    - 粒子截留測試（新品）
  使用週期確效：
    - 清洗 N 次後重複粒子測試
    - 滅菌 M 次後重複粒子測試
    - 視覺完整性評估

管理限制（由確效決定）：
  最大清洗次數 = N 次（超過即報廢）
  最大滅菌次數 = M 次（超過即報廢）
  最大連續穿著時間 = T 小時（超過即更換）
  滅菌後最大保質期 = X 週（超過即重滅菌）

CDMO 服裝管理挑戰：
  多人共用服裝 → 需個人編號追蹤使用次數
  客戶稽核時服裝清洗記錄常被查核

實務應用 Practical Application

在 CDMO 實務中，氣流可視化研究 (Smoke Study) 的結果應納入操作員培訓計畫，讓操作員直觀看到自己的「錯誤動作」如何影響 Grade A 氣流。許多 CDMO 會在培訓影片中加入煙霧試驗片段：操作員快速橫越充填區時，Grade A 的 HEPA 氣流立刻被打亂，煙霧（代表污染微粒）被捲入容器開口上方——這比任何文字說明都更有說服力，能有效建立操作員的「無菌意識」。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 一個班次進行到一半，Grade B 充填間的操作員意識到自己的無菌手套外層破了一個小孔。應立即採取哪些步驟？這對正在充填的這一批次有何影響？
  2. 你的廠房 Grade B 服裝清洗合約由外部供應商負責。請列出在供應商稽核時你應查核的關鍵文件和程序。
  3. 為什麼操作員不能在充填操作進行時，橫越充填機前方的 Grade A 區域取物？從氣流物理學角度解釋這個禁止行為的理由。

本節重點回顧 Section Summary

  • 人員是無菌製程首要污染源。Grade A/B 區必須維持最少人數，並在 APS 時確認並文件化最大人數上限。所有接觸潔淨室的人員（含清潔、維護、監測人員）均須定期訓練，並了解無菌失敗對患者安全的影響。
  • Grade A/B 人員必須通過無菌著裝確效（目視+微生物評估），且至少每年重新評估；需同時參與成功的 APS，才能獲准無監督進入。出現不良趨勢或牽涉 APS 失敗者，應立即取消資格，完成再訓練與再確效後方可恢復。
  • 健康管理：操作員有義務回報可能增加脫落風險的健康狀況；接觸過微生物培養物或動物組織者須完成文件化去污程序；個人手錶、飾物、個人手機禁止進入潔淨區。
  • 各等級更衣要求遞增：Grade B 須全套無菌服裝（護目鏡、口罩、雙層手套、無菌靴套）；Grade C 為高領袖口收緊連體服；Grade D 為一般防護服。服裝須視覺檢查、遵守保質期，定期清洗（確效程序），並設定最大清洗與滅菌次數。手套需定期消毒，操作時動作應緩慢有序，避免阻擋 Grade A 的單向氣流（First Air）。
