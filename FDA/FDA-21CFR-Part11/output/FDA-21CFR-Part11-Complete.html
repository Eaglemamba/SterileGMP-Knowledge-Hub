0.1 Regulatory Background: Why Part 11 Exists

原文內容 Original Text

21 CFR Part 11 (Electronic Records; Electronic Signatures) was promulgated by FDA in 1997 (62 FR 13430). It establishes criteria under which the agency considers electronic records and electronic signatures to be trustworthy, reliable, and generally equivalent to paper records and handwritten signatures executed on paper.

Prior to Part 11, FDA regulations required paper records and handwritten signatures for most manufacturing, laboratory, and quality activities. As computerized systems became ubiquitous in pharmaceutical manufacturing during the 1990s, a regulatory framework was needed to:

  • Define when electronic records are acceptable alternatives to paper
  • Establish minimum standards for system security and data integrity
  • Ensure electronic signatures carry the same legal weight as handwritten signatures
  • Give FDA inspection authority over computer systems maintaining regulated records
"The agency considers electronic records, electronic signatures, and handwritten signatures executed to electronic records to be trustworthy, reliable, and generally equivalent to paper records and handwritten signatures executed on paper."
— 21 CFR §11.1(a)

Scope trigger: Part 11 applies to records in electronic form that are created, modified, maintained, archived, retrieved, or transmitted under any requirements set forth in agency regulations. The key phrase is "under any requirements" — Part 11 only applies where another FDA regulation already requires the record to exist.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

1997 年的背景： 1990 年代，製藥業電腦化浪潮洶湧。工廠裡的 SCADA 系統、LIMS、MES 開始大量取代紙本記錄，但 FDA 法規（如 21 CFR 211.188 批次記錄）仍寫「書面記錄」和「手寫簽名」。這個落差造成法規不確定性——電子記錄到底算不算合規？Part 11 就是填補這個空白的法規。

等效性原則 (Equivalence Principle)： Part 11 不是要求用電子方式，而是說：如果你選擇用電子方式，符合 Part 11 的電子記錄就等同於紙本記錄。這是一個「許可」框架，不是「強制」框架。

比喻說明 Analogy

把 Part 11 比作「電子合約的公證標準」：你要簽合約，可以手寫在紙上，也可以用電子簽名平台（如 DocuSign）。電子方式不是強制的，但如果你選擇電子方式，必須符合一定的安全標準（身份驗證、防竄改、時間戳記），才具備法律效力。Part 11 就是 FDA 版的這套標準。

重點提示 Key Notes

★ Part 11 適用範圍的關鍵字：「under any requirements set forth in agency regulations」——必須是其他 FDA 法規要求留存的記錄，Part 11 才管。

★ 電子化傳輸的紙本記錄不適用：若公司保存紙本批次記錄為官方記錄，只是掃描成 PDF 用電子郵件傳送，Part 11 不適用於該 PDF（§11.1(b) 明文排除「紙本記錄的電子傳輸」）。

Key predicate rules for pharmaceutical manufacturers:

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Predicate Rule（前提法規）判斷樹：

問題 1：有另一條 FDA 法規要求這筆記錄存在嗎？→ 否 → Part 11 不適用（即使記錄是電子的）

問題 2：這筆記錄是電子形式建立/維護的（而非紙本掃描）？→ 否 → Part 11 不適用

問題 3：公司選擇電子記錄作為官方記錄？→ 否 → Part 11 可能不完全適用（2003 指引裁量空間）

全部是 → Part 11 適用

比喻說明 Analogy

Predicate Rule 就像「主法」，Part 11 是「配套技術規範」。主法（如 21 CFR 211.188）說「你要有批次記錄」；Part 11 說「如果你用電子方式留這批次記錄，必須做到以下這些」。沒有主法要求，Part 11 無從附著。

CDMO 實務重點 CDMO Practical Notes

Amaran Biotech 實例： 假設 Amaran Biotech 為客戶執行無菌充填，使用以下系統：

  • eBR 系統（Veeva Vault MES）：predicate rule = §211.188（批次記錄）→ Part 11 適用
  • LIMS（LabWare）：predicate rule = §211.194（實驗室記錄）→ Part 11 適用
  • SCADA（Emerson DeltaV）：predicate rule = §211.68（電腦化設備數據）→ Part 11 適用
  • BMS（溫濕度監控）：predicate rule = §211.68 + FDA Aseptic Guidance EM data → Part 11 適用
  • HR 系統（員工薪資）：無 FDA predicate rule → Part 11 不適用
0.3 FDA 2003 Guidance: Narrowed Enforcement Scope

原文內容 Original Text

In August 2003, FDA issued guidance "21 CFR Part 11; Electronic Records; Electronic Signatures — Scope and Application" which significantly narrowed enforcement. Key provisions:

1. Enforcement Discretion for Legacy Systems: FDA exercises enforcement discretion for requirements §11.10(a) (validation), §11.10(b) (readable copies), §11.10(c) (protection), §11.10(d) (access), §11.10(e) (audit trails), §11.10(f) (operational checks), §11.10(g) (authority checks), §11.10(h) (device checks), §11.10(k) (system documentation), and §11.30 for legacy systems when: (a) the system was operational before August 20, 1997, AND (b) the system uses paper as the official record (electronic records supplement paper).

2. Validation Exception: FDA does not intend to take regulatory action based solely on §11.10(a) validation deficiencies for legacy systems that otherwise comply.

3. Copies Requirement: FDA clarified §11.10(b) — companies must be able to provide complete and accurate copies of records in a form that FDA can use during inspections. The format does not need to be specified in advance, but must be accessible.

4. What Part 11 Does NOT Require: Part 11 does NOT mandate:

  • Use of electronic records (paper is always an option)
  • Use of electronic signatures
  • Any specific technology solution
  • FDA pre-approval of systems before use

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

2003 指引的歷史背景： 1997 年 Part 11 發布後，業界反應是「太嚴苛了」。特別是對在 1997 年前就已運作的老舊系統（legacy systems），要求完全合規會造成巨大財務和運作負擔。FDA 在 2003 年發出指引，說：「我們理解，對於遺留系統，我們不會只因為技術上不符合某些條款就採取法規行動。」但這是「執法裁量 (enforcement discretion)」，不是「免除義務 (exemption from regulation)」。

重點提示 Key Notes

★ Legacy System 認定條件（兩者都要）： (1) 1997 年 8 月 20 日前就已在使用；AND (2) 以紙本為官方記錄，電子記錄只是補充。現代製藥廠幾乎很少有真正的「遺留系統」——大多數系統已更新換代，或已完全電子化，不符合「以紙本為官方記錄」的條件。

★ Part 11「不強制」的重要性： 如果 FDA 查廠時，你的電子記錄系統不符合 Part 11，FDA 不能只憑「違反 Part 11」就開 483。必須是違反了底層的 predicate rule（如批次記錄不完整 = §211.188 違規）。但 Part 11 違規通常暗示數據完整性問題，可能升級為 Warning Letter。

本章重點總結

  • Part 11 不強制電子化： 只規定「如果選擇電子記錄/電子簽名，必須符合這些標準」。紙本永遠是合規選項。

1.1 §11.1 — Scope

原文內容 Original Text

§ 11.1(a) — The regulations in this part set forth the criteria under which the agency considers electronic records, electronic signatures, and handwritten signatures executed to electronic records to be trustworthy, reliable, and generally equivalent to paper records and handwritten signatures executed on paper.

§ 11.1(b) — This part applies to records in electronic form that are created, modified, maintained, archived, retrieved, or transmitted, under any requirements set forth in agency regulations. This part also applies to electronic records submitted to the agency under requirements of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act and the Public Health Service Act, even if such records are not specifically identified in agency regulations. However, this part does not apply to paper records that are, or have been, transmitted by electronic means.

§ 11.1(c) — Where electronic signatures and their associated electronic records meet the requirements of this part, the agency will consider the electronic signatures to be equivalent to full handwritten signatures, initials, and other general signings as required by agency regulations, unless specifically excepted by regulation(s) effective on or after August 20, 1997.

§ 11.1(d) — Electronic records that meet the requirements of this part may be used in lieu of paper records, in accordance with §11.2, unless paper records are specifically required.

§ 11.1(e) — Computer systems (including hardware and software), controls, and attendant documentation maintained under this part shall be readily available for, and subject to, FDA inspection.

"This part does not apply to paper records that are, or have been, transmitted by electronic means."
— 21 CFR §11.1(b), key exclusion clause

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

§11.1(b) 的兩個觸發條件：

條件 1：記錄是電子形式（非紙本），且符合以下任一操作：建立、修改、維護、存檔、檢索、傳輸。

條件 2：這個電子記錄是「在 FDA 法規要求下」（predicate rule）才觸發。

排除條款（重要！）： 「紙本記錄的電子傳輸」不適用 Part 11。例：紙本批次記錄掃描成 PDF 寄給品保部門 → 那個 PDF 不是「電子記錄」，只是紙本的電子副本，Part 11 不適用。

§11.1(e) 查廠意涵 Inspection Implications

§11.1(e) 賦予 FDA 查廠官員檢查 Part 11 系統的完整權力——包括硬體、軟體、系統文件（驗證文件、SOP、存取控制清單）。這意味著 FDA 查廠不只看生產記錄，也可以要求：

  • 系統驗證報告（IQ/OQ/PQ）
  • 使用者帳號清單及權限設置
  • 稽核追蹤記錄
  • 系統 SOP 和管理員手冊
  • 備份和災難復原測試記錄

1.2 §11.2 — Implementation

原文內容 Original Text

§ 11.2(a) — Records maintained but not submitted to FDA:
Persons may use electronic records in lieu of paper records or electronic signatures in lieu of traditional signatures, in whole or in part, provided that the requirements of this part are met.

§ 11.2(b) — Records submitted to FDA:
Persons may use electronic records in lieu of paper records or electronic signatures in lieu of traditional signatures, in whole or in part, provided that:

  • (1) The requirements of this part are met; and
  • (2) The document or parts of a document to be submitted have been identified in public docket No. 92S-0251 as being the type of submission the agency accepts in electronic form.

This docket will identify specifically what types of documents or parts of documents are acceptable for electronic submission. The agency will periodically publish in the Federal Register a list of the types of documents it will accept electronically.

Practical impact: Most internal manufacturing records (eBR, LIMS) fall under §11.2(a) — maintained but not submitted to FDA. Records submitted electronically to FDA (e.g., eCTD submissions, eDMFs) must also meet the electronic submission criteria.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

§11.2 的兩種記錄類別：

類別 A — 內部維護記錄（不提交 FDA）： eBR、LIMS、SCADA 數據。僅需符合 Part 11 本身的要求。這是 CDMO 最常遇到的情況。

類別 B — 電子提交 FDA 的記錄： eCTD（電子通用技術文件）、電子 IND、電子 NDA/BLA。除符合 Part 11 外，還需確認 FDA 接受電子提交的特定記錄類型（docket 92S-0251 清單）。

對大多數製造型 CDMO 而言，日常操作記錄屬於類別 A。客戶的法規提交材料屬於類別 B（通常由客戶和法規事務部門處理，不是生產端的事）。

比喻說明 Analogy

§11.2(a) 就像你的「內部財務記帳」——只要符合公司內部規定和稅法，怎麼記都可以。§11.2(b) 就像「向國稅局申報的稅表」——除了要合規，還要用國稅局規定的格式和申報管道。

1.3 §11.3 — Definitions

原文內容 Original Text

§11.3(c) Biometric: A method of verifying an individual's identity based on measurement of the individual's physical feature(s) or repeatable action(s) where those features and/or actions are both unique to that individual and measurable.

§11.3(d) Closed system: An environment in which system access is controlled by persons who are responsible for the content of electronic records that are on the system.

§11.3(e) Digital signature: An electronic signature based upon cryptographic methods of originator authentication, computed by using a set of rules and a set of parameters such that the identity of the signer and the integrity of the data can be verified.

§11.3(f) Electronic record: Any combination of text, graphics, data, audio, pictorial, or other information representation in digital form that is created, modified, maintained, archived, retrieved, or distributed by a computer system.

§11.3(g) Electronic signature: A computer data compilation of any symbol or series of symbols executed, adopted, or authorized by an individual to be the legally binding equivalent of the individual's handwritten signature.

§11.3(h) Handwritten signature: The scripted name or legal mark of an individual handwritten by that individual and executed or adopted with the present intention to authenticate a writing in an electronic form. The act of signing with a writing or marking instrument such as a pen or stylus is preserved. The scripted name or legal mark, while conventionally applied to paper, may also be applied to other devices that capture the name or mark.

§11.3(i) Open system: An environment in which system access is not controlled by persons who are responsible for the content of electronic records that are on the system.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

Closed vs. Open System 判定 Key Concepts

判定關鍵：WHO controls access？

封閉系統 (Closed System) = 負責電子記錄內容的人（通常是公司的 IT/品保）控制系統存取。

開放系統 (Open System) = 存取由「對記錄內容不負責的他方」控制。

情境系統類型理由
公司自管的內部 DCS（Emerson DeltaV），IT 部門管理帳號封閉 ✓公司控制存取
AWS 上的雲端 LIMS，Amazon 管理底層基礎設施開放 ✓雲端供應商控制存取
公司管理的 VPN 內部網路 EDMS封閉 ✓公司控制 VPN 和帳號
透過公共電信網路傳輸的記錄開放 ✓傳輸途徑不受公司控制
第三方 CRO 的雲端試驗數據平台開放 ✓CRO 無法完全控制雲端存取

三種「簽名」類型辨析

1. Electronic Signature（電子簽名）： 最廣義。任何電腦資料組合（符號序列），被個人執行/採用/授權作為其手寫簽名的法律等效物。包含 ID+密碼組合、數位憑證、生物特徵等。

2. Digital Signature（數位簽名）： 電子簽名的子集。基於密碼學方法（非對稱加密、PKI）。既能驗證簽名者身份，也能驗證文件完整性（任何竄改都會使簽名無效）。Part 11 在開放系統（§11.30）要求使用。

3. Handwritten Signature Executed to Electronic Record（手寫簽名執行至電子記錄）： 用手寫板在電子設備上手寫的簽名。保留了手寫簽名的筆跡，但媒介是電子設備。Part 11 也規範這類簽名（當簽到電子記錄上時）。

Biometric 定義的實務意涵

§11.3(c) 對 biometric 的定義強調兩個條件：（1）對個人是唯一的；（2）可量測的。

符合條件的生物特徵：指紋、視網膜/虹膜掃描、臉部識別、聲紋、筆跡動態（不只是結果，而是簽名的速度/壓力/筆順）。

不符合條件（或爭議性）：僅有靜態掃描的簽名圖像（可複製，不唯一）。

生物特徵電子簽名適用 §11.200(b)，不需要雙重認證元素，但必須確保「只有真正的持有人才能使用」。

1.4 Closed vs. Open System — Practical Implications

原文內容 Original Text

The classification of a system as closed or open determines the applicable controls under Part 11:

RequirementClosed System (§11.10)Open System (§11.30)
All 11 controls of §11.10RequiredRequired (baseline)
Document encryptionNot explicitly requiredRequired (appropriate methods)
Digital signature standardsNot requiredRequired (when additional security needed)

FDA's rationale: In a closed system, physical and administrative access controls protect records. In an open system (accessible via public networks), records can be intercepted during transmission — encryption protects confidentiality, and digital signatures protect integrity.

The access control test: Ask "who controls the user accounts for this system?" If the answer is the same organization responsible for the records (the pharmaceutical company), it is typically a closed system. If a third party (cloud provider, ISP, external IT vendor) controls access, it is an open system.

Gray areas in practice: Many modern cloud-based systems blur the line. A cloud LIMS where the pharmaceutical company manages all user accounts (even though hosted externally) may be argued as a closed system if the company has administrative control over who can access the system. However, the transmission layer (internet) introduces open system elements. FDA has not issued definitive guidance on Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) systems — risk-based assessment is recommended.

比喻說明 Analogy

封閉系統： 就像你公司的內部保險庫——公司控制鑰匙和進出名單。即使保險庫在外面的建築物裡，只要你控制誰能進去，就是「封閉」的。

  • 開放系統需要額外控制： 在 §11.10 的 11 項控制基礎上，還需加密和數位簽名標準（§11.30）——保護傳輸過程中的機密性和完整性。

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

三個核心目標 (Authenticity + Integrity + Confidentiality)：

真實性 (Authenticity)： 確認記錄確實是聲稱建立者所建立的。電子簽名的身份驗證保障。

完整性 (Integrity)： 確認記錄自建立後未被竄改。稽核追蹤（§11.10e）和簽名連結（§11.70）保障。

機密性 (Confidentiality)： 「適當時」才需要——並非所有記錄都需要保密。GMP 記錄通常不需要加密（封閉系統內），但含有商業機密或個人資料的記錄可能需要。

FDA 483 高風險控制項目

11 項控制中，FDA 查廠時最常觀察到缺失的（排名）：

1st: (e) 稽核追蹤——未啟用、不完整、可被修改

2nd: (d) 存取控制——共用帳號、離職員工帳號未停用

3rd: (a) 驗證——系統未驗證或驗證不足

4th: (j) 書面政策——無 SOP 定義電子簽名問責制

5th: (i) 訓練——系統訓練記錄缺失或不完整

比喻說明 Analogy

把 §11.10 的 11 項控制比作一棟安全建築的防護層：

(a) 驗證 = 建築結構安全檢驗（基礎）；(d) 存取 = 門禁卡系統；(e) 稽核追蹤 = 24 小時 CCTV 監控；(f) 操作順序 = 工作流程鎖定（不能跳步驟）；(g) 權限 = 不同區域不同授權；(j) 政策 = 員工行為準則手冊

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

為什麼需要驗證？ §11.10(a) 要求系統能「辨識無效或被更改的記錄 (discern invalid or altered records)」——這意味著系統本身必須有足夠的可靠性，才能保證其中的記錄是可信的。一個未驗證的系統可能：(1) 計算錯誤而不報錯；(2) 允許記錄被修改而不留痕跡；(3) 在特定條件下失效，產生錯誤資料。

Part 11 驗證重點： Part 11 特定的驗證測試腳本必須測試：

  • 稽核追蹤功能是否啟用且不可關閉
  • 存取控制是否按角色正確限制
  • 電子簽名顯示姓名、時間、意義
  • 記錄匯出是否完整、準確
  • 備份恢復功能是否正常

FDA 2003 指引：遺留系統驗證例外

FDA 在 2003 年指引中指出，對於 1997 年 8 月前已在使用、且以紙本為官方記錄的遺留系統，FDA 對 §11.10(a) 驗證要求行使「執法裁量 (enforcement discretion)」。但這個例外適用範圍非常窄——現代 CDMO 系統幾乎不可能符合這兩個條件。

2.3 §11.10(e) — Audit Trails: The Most Critical Part 11 Requirement

原文內容 Original Text

§11.10(e): Use of secure, computer-generated, time-stamped audit trails to independently record the date and time of operator entries and actions that create, modify, or delete electronic records. Record changes shall not obscure previously recorded information. Such audit trail documentation shall be retained for a period at least as long as that required for the subject electronic records and shall be available for agency review and copying.

The 8 Critical Elements of a §11.10(e) Compliant Audit Trail:

  1. SECURE: Cannot be turned off, modified, or deleted by users. Only documented administrators with authorization can adjust settings, and such changes are themselves logged.
  2. COMPUTER-GENERATED: Automatically created by the system — NOT manually entered by users. This is the fundamental distinction from paper logbooks.
  3. TIME-STAMPED: Each entry records date and time, synchronized to a reliable time source (NTP). Time zone documentation required for multi-site systems.
  4. INDEPENDENTLY RECORDING: Audit trail is a separate log, not part of the editable data record. Cannot be in the same file as the record that can be edited.
  5. CREATE/MODIFY/DELETE: ALL three operations must be captured. The audit trail must record every original entry, every change (old value → new value, reason, user, timestamp), and every deletion.
  6. NOT OBSCURING: Changes must show BOTH old and new values. Overwriting old data without capturing original = non-compliant.
  7. RETAINED: Retained for at least as long as the associated electronic record. If a batch record is retained 3 years, its audit trail is retained 3 years.
  8. AVAILABLE FOR FDA REVIEW: FDA inspectors must be able to access and copy audit trails during inspections. Must be exportable in a readable format.
"Record changes shall not obscure previously recorded information."
— 21 CFR §11.10(e), critical anti-falsification provision

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

稽核追蹤最常見 FDA 483 缺失

1. 稽核追蹤未啟用： 「Audit trail functionality was not enabled on [system].」系統有稽核追蹤功能，但被管理員或廠商預設關閉。

2. 只記錄新值，不記錄舊值： 「The audit trail does not capture the original value prior to change.」系統只顯示修改後的值，無法追蹤原始數據。

3. 未記錄刪除操作： 「Deleted records are not captured in the audit trail.」有人刪除了記錄，但稽核追蹤沒有記錄。

4. 缺少修改原因： 「Reason for change not required when modifying electronic records.」系統允許無理由修改。

5. 稽核追蹤可被使用者修改： 「Non-administrative users have the ability to modify audit trail entries.」

6. 稽核追蹤未定期審查： 「No procedure for periodic review of audit trail records as part of quality oversight.」

比喻說明 Analogy

稽核追蹤就像銀行的「交易歷史記錄」。你的銀行帳戶每一筆存款、提款、轉帳都有自動記錄（computer-generated）、時間戳記（time-stamped），你無法修改或刪除這些記錄（secure）。如果有爭議，銀行可以提供完整的交易歷史（available for review）。GMP 電子記錄的稽核追蹤就是這個邏輯——只不過管的是製藥操作，而非金融交易。

稽核追蹤查廠演練

場景： FDA 查廠官員要求查看批次 #AMB-2026-0411 的 eBR 稽核追蹤。

你應該能展示：

  1. 每個輸入欄位的原始值（由誰輸入，何時輸入）
  2. 所有修改記錄（舊值、新值、修改者、修改時間、修改原因）
  3. 所有電子簽名記錄（簽名者姓名、時間、簽名意義）
  4. 任何嘗試但失敗的操作（拒絕訪問記錄）
  5. 管理員操作記錄（系統設定變更）

你應該能匯出這些記錄為 PDF 或 CSV，供查廠官員帶走複製。

2.4 §11.10(d) & (g) — Access Controls and Authority Checks

原文內容 Original Text

§11.10(d) — Limiting system access:
Limiting system access to authorized individuals.

This requires:

  • Individual user accounts (no shared/generic accounts)
  • Role-based access control (RBAC) matching job function and training
  • Periodic user access reviews
  • Timely deprovisioning when personnel change roles or leave
  • Physical access controls for workstations

§11.10(g) — Authority checks:
Use of authority checks to ensure that only authorized individuals can use the system, electronically sign a record, access the operation or computer system input or output device, alter a record, or perform the operation at issue.

Authority checks go beyond access (§11.10d) to specifically govern:

  • WHO can perform electronic signatures (validated signing authority)
  • WHO can approve specific records (role-based approval authority)
  • WHO can modify approved records (typically only QA with documented justification)
  • Separation of duties (operator cannot approve their own work)

Shared accounts — fundamental incompatibility with Part 11:
If two people share an ID/password, the signature is no longer unique to one individual (violating §11.100a) and actions cannot be attributed (violating §11.50 and ALCOA Attributable principle).

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

存取控制 vs 權限控制的差別

§11.10(d) 存取控制： 「你能不能進入這個系統？」的問題。就像大樓門禁——你有沒有門卡。

§11.10(g) 權限控制： 「進入系統後，你被允許做哪些操作？」的問題。就像大樓裡不同樓層需要不同的授權等級——普通員工可以去辦公室，但不能進伺服器機房、不能進財務室。

GMP 系統中的典型權限分層：

角色允許操作禁止操作
操作員 (Operator)輸入數據、查看記錄修改已完成步驟、批准記錄
QC 分析師輸入測試結果、查看規格修改規格、批准批次
QA 管理員批准批次記錄、授權偏差輸入生產數據（職責分離）
系統管理員帳號管理、系統設定直接修改 GMP 記錄內容

共用帳號（Shared Account）是系統性問題

當 FDA 查廠發現共用帳號時，這不只是 Part 11 §11.10(d) 問題，而是觸發多個合規問題的多米諾骨牌：

→ §11.100(a)：電子簽名不是個人唯一的

→ §11.50：簽名無法識別真實簽名者（違反「印刷姓名」要求）

→ ALCOA Attributable：記錄無法歸屬到個人

→ 21 CFR 211.68：操作人員責任不明

FDA 通常將共用帳號觀察提升為「資料完整性缺陷」，可能導致 Warning Letter。

2.5 §11.10(b)(c)(f)(h)(i)(j)(k) — Remaining Controls

原文內容 Original Text

§11.10(b) — Readable copies: The ability to generate accurate and complete copies of records in both human readable and electronic form suitable for inspection, review, and copying by the agency.

§11.10(c) — Record protection: Protection of records to enable their accurate and ready retrieval throughout the records retention period. Protection from: unauthorized alteration/deletion, data corruption, disaster loss, storage media degradation.

§11.10(f) — Operational system checks: Enforce permitted sequencing of steps and events, as appropriate. Example: in an eBR, an operator cannot sign "dispensing complete" before "weight check passed."

SOPs must define what constitutes an electronic signature, prohibited actions (sharing credentials), and consequences for misuse.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

§11.10(b) 可讀副本的查廠意涵

FDA 查廠時，查廠官員有權要求「一份批次記錄的完整、準確副本」帶走。§11.10(b) 要求你的系統能夠：

  • 即時匯出完整批次記錄（包含所有欄位、圖表、附件）
  • 以 FDA 能閱讀的格式（PDF、紙本列印）提供
  • 確保匯出副本與系統內原始記錄完全一致（不遺漏任何稽核追蹤條目）

常見問題：系統匯出的 PDF 缺少稽核追蹤頁，或格式無法完整顯示所有欄位。這些都會被 FDA 標記為 §11.10(b) 缺失。

§11.10(c) 記錄保護與備份策略

記錄保護需要涵蓋整個「保留期間 (retention period)」。保留期限由 predicate rule 設定：

  • 藥品批次記錄（§211.180）：效期後至少 1 年，最低 3 年
  • 生物製品批次記錄：效期後 10 年或更長
  • 驗證文件：系統使用期間 + 退役後若干年

保護措施：定期備份（每日增量 + 每週完整）、異地備份（災難恢復）、備份測試（每季驗證恢復能力）、儲存媒介管理（預防磁碟老化）。

§11.10(f) 操作順序控制 — eBR 的關鍵功能

在電子批次記錄中，操作順序控制確保製程步驟按正確順序執行。這是電子系統相比紙本的重要優勢：紙本批次記錄可以被操作員提前簽名，但 eBR 系統可以技術上強制「步驟 A 完成且簽名，才能開啟步驟 B」。這直接防止了回填簽名（backdating）問題。

原文內容 Original Text

§11.30 Controls for open systems:
Persons who use open systems to create, modify, maintain, or transmit electronic records shall employ procedures and controls designed to ensure the authenticity, integrity, and, as appropriate, the confidentiality of electronic records from the point of their creation to the point of their receipt.

Such procedures and controls shall include those identified in §11.10, as appropriate, and additional measures such as:

  • Document encryption, using appropriate encryption methods
  • Use of appropriate digital signature standards to ensure, as appropriate, record authenticity, integrity, and confidentiality

Why open systems need more:

  • In a closed system, physical and administrative controls protect records — the data "stays inside"
  • In an open system, records traverse public networks — interception is possible
  • Encryption protects confidentiality: even if intercepted, data cannot be read
  • Digital signatures protect integrity: any modification invalidates the signature, alerting recipients

Practical example: A CRO submitting clinical data to a pharma sponsor via a cloud platform. The CRO cannot control access to the AWS infrastructure — this is an open system. Data submission must use TLS encryption (in transit) and SHA-256 digital signatures to meet §11.30.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

開放系統額外控制的技術實施

文件加密 (Document Encryption)：

傳輸中加密（in transit）：TLS 1.2+ / HTTPS。瀏覽器的「掛鎖圖示」即表示 TLS 加密。所有 SaaS GMP 系統應強制使用 HTTPS。

靜態加密（at rest）：AES-256 對儲存的記錄加密。當資料庫被未授權存取時，無法讀取加密內容。

數位簽名標準 (Digital Signature Standards)：

FDA 認可的標準包括 FIPS 140-2 驗證的密碼模組、RSA/ECDSA 簽名算法。數位簽名將特定的簽名者公鑰和文件內容綁定——任何對文件的修改都會使簽名驗證失敗，提供技術上的完整性保障。

§11.30 vs §11.10：不是替代，是疊加

許多人誤以為開放系統只需要「加密和數位簽名」而不需要 §11.10 的其他控制。這是錯誤的。§11.30 的原文是：「shall include those identified in §11.10, as appropriate, and additional measures」——即 §11.10 的 11 項控制仍然適用，§11.30 再加上加密和數位簽名。

3.1 §11.50 — Signature Manifestations: Three Mandatory Elements

原文內容 Original Text

§11.50(a): Signed electronic records shall contain information associated with the signing that clearly indicates all of the following:

(1) The printed name of the signer;
The full human-readable name of the signer — not just a user ID, employee number, or initials. The name must be legible and unambiguous.

(2) The date and time when the signature was executed;
The exact timestamp when the signature was applied — NOT the date the record was created. This timestamp must be automatically generated by the system (computer-generated), not manually entered by the signer.

(3) The meaning (such as review, approval, responsibility, or authorship) associated with the signature.
The purpose of the signature must be explicitly stated. Examples:

  • "Reviewed by" — QC analyst reviewing test data
  • "Approved by" — QA manager approving batch record for release
  • "Authored by" — operator entering process data
  • "Verified by" — second-person verification step
  • "Accountability signature" — accepting responsibility for a critical operation

§11.50(b): The items identified in paragraphs (a)(1), (a)(2), and (a)(3) of this section shall be subject to the same controls as for electronic records and shall be included as part of any human readable form of the electronic record (such as electronic display or printout).

"The meaning (such as review, approval, responsibility, or authorship) associated with the signature."
— 21 CFR §11.50(a)(3), the often-neglected third element

Common deficiencies observed by FDA:

  • System displays only user ID (not printed name) in audit trail or on record
  • Date/time is manually entered by the signer (not computer-generated)
  • Meaning is generic ("signed") rather than specific ("approved for release")
  • Signature manifestation not included in printed/exported copies of records
  • Time zone not documented for systems used across multiple sites/time zones

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

三元素缺一不可： §11.50 的三個元素必須「全部同時出現」在簽名相關的記錄中，才符合要求。

元素 1 — 印刷姓名（Printed Name）： 「印刷」不是指必須從印表機輸出，而是指「清晰可辨的完整姓名字串」，對應手寫簽名的「可辨識性」。「John Doe」合規；「JD」或「jd01」不合規。

元素 2 — 自動時間戳記： 這個時間戳記必須由系統產生，不能是使用者手動輸入的日期欄位。如果系統允許使用者修改簽名時間，就違反了「computer-generated」的要求（§11.10e 同理）。

元素 3 — 簽名意義（最常被忽略）： 許多系統只顯示「Signed by: John Doe at 2026-04-11 09:23」，沒有說明「為什麼簽」。缺少這個元素使記錄無法判斷責任歸屬——「我簽名了」和「我批准了這個決定」是完全不同的法律含義。

比喻說明 Analogy

想像一份法院判決書。它必須包含：(1) 法官的全名（不是法院代碼）；(2) 判決日期和時間（由書記官記錄，不是法官自填）；(3) 判決性質（「駁回上訴」vs「判定有罪」vs「批准移轉管轄」）。三者缺一，判決的法律效力就不完整。§11.50 的電子簽名三要素邏輯完全相同。

欄位來源範例顯示

3.2 §11.70 — Signature/Record Linking: Anti-Falsification

原文內容 Original Text

§11.70: Electronic signatures and handwritten signatures executed to electronic records shall be linked to their respective electronic records to ensure that the signatures cannot be excised, copied, or otherwise transferred to falsify an electronic record by ordinary means.

The problem §11.70 solves — "Signature Cut-and-Paste" Fraud:
Without linking, a valid electronic signature from a legitimate record could be extracted and inserted into a falsified record. For example:

  • Extract QA Director's approval signature from a passed batch record
  • Insert it into a failed batch record that was changed to show passing results
  • Result: falsified batch record appears to have QA Director's approval

Technical implementations that satisfy §11.70:

  • Cryptographic binding: The signature is computed over the record content (digital signature/hash). Any modification to the record invalidates the signature — tampering is immediately detectable.
  • Database linking with record version control: The signature record contains an immutable foreign key reference to the specific version of the electronic record (identified by a hash or unique version ID). If the record changes, the signature no longer matches the current version.
  • Record locking: Once signed, the record is frozen — no edits allowed without going through a formal amendment workflow that requires re-signature, creating a new audit trail entry.

FDA inspection test: Inspectors may ask: "If I print this batch record, can the signature block be cut and pasted into another document?" The system design must make this technically impossible or immediately detectable.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

§11.70 的反偽造邏輯： 如果電子簽名只是一個「圖像」或「文字字串」附在記錄旁邊，任何有基本電腦技能的人都可以：複製這個圖像/字串 → 貼到另一份文件 → 得到一份看似有授權的偽造文件。

§11.70 要求「連結 (linked)」，意思是簽名必須在技術層面上與特定版本的記錄內容「綁定」，使得「簽名 + 記錄 A」的組合在技術上無法被轉移成「簽名 + 記錄 B」的有效組合。

比喻說明 Analogy

傳統的蠟封（wax seal）是一個好比喻：蠟封覆蓋信件開口，封蠟上有持有人的印章。如果你嘗試開封再重新封蠟，蠟封必然破損，無法恢復原狀，偽造一目了然。數位簽名的哈希值就是現代的蠟封——記錄內容改變了，哈希值就不對，「封蠟破損」立即可見。

§11.70 在查廠中的評估方法

FDA 查廠官員評估 §11.70 合規的常見方法：

方法 1： 要求列印一份批次記錄，然後問：「如果我把這個印刷版本的簽名掃描後，貼入另一份文件，系統能否偵測到？」——答案應該是「不需要偵測，因為印刷版本只是人眼可讀的副本；系統內的簽名綁定機制使電子記錄中的簽名無法被移植。」

方法 2： 嘗試修改一份已簽名的記錄，看系統是否阻止或強制重新簽名。

方法 3： 查看系統的技術文件，確認簽名機制（哈希值、版本鎖定、資料庫連結）的設計。

3.3 §11.100 — General Requirements: Uniqueness, Identity Verification & FDA Certification

原文內容 Original Text

§11.100(a) — Uniqueness:
Each electronic signature shall be unique to one individual and shall not be reused by, or reassigned to, anyone else.

This uniqueness requirement means:

  • No shared accounts (two people sharing one username/password)
  • No reassignment of identifiers (when an employee leaves, their user ID cannot be given to a new employee)
  • Even after deactivation, the same ID cannot be reused for a different person

§11.100(b) — Identity Verification:
Before an organization establishes, assigns, certifies, or otherwise sanctions an individual's electronic signature, or any element of such electronic signature, the organization shall verify the identity of the individual.

Acceptable identity verification methods:

  • Government-issued photo ID review during HR onboarding
  • In-person verification by HR or supervisor
  • Biometric enrollment process

§11.100(c) — FDA Certification Letter (the "Part 11 letter"):
Persons using electronic signatures shall, prior to or at the time of such use, certify to the agency in writing that the electronic signatures in their system are intended to be the legally binding equivalent of traditional handwritten signatures.

Upon agency request, such certification shall be submitted in paper form with a traditional handwritten signature to the docket established by the agency for such purposes (Docket No. 92S-0251).

This is a company-level certification, not per-system or per-user. One letter per company covers all systems and all users. The letter should reference the FDA docket number and state the company's intent that all electronic signatures used in FDA-regulated activities are intended to be legally binding equivalents of handwritten signatures.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

§11.100(a) 唯一性的實務影響

離職員工帳號管理： 當員工離職時，其帳號必須停用（不是刪除）。停用後的帳號不能分配給新員工，即使員工 ID 號碼格式相同。原因：稽核追蹤中已有大量與這個帳號關聯的記錄；如果帳號被重新分配，將來無法確定歷史操作是哪個真實的人所執行。

正確做法： 停用帳號 → 保留帳號（但設為「非活躍」狀態）→ 新員工建立全新帳號（即使有相似的姓名或工號前綴）

§11.100(c) FDA 認證信函詳解

這封信是什麼： 這是一份公司向 FDA 申報的聲明，說「我們公司使用電子簽名，而且我們理解並確認這些電子簽名在法律上等同於手寫簽名。」

什麼時候寄： 「prior to or at the time of such use」——在第一次使用電子簽名之前，或使用時寄出。實務上，在 Part 11 系統正式啟用前寄出。

寄給誰： FDA Office of Regional Operations (ORO)，參考 Docket No. 92S-0251。

格式： 紙本（不是電子提交！），含公司官員的手寫簽名（諷刺的是：宣告電子簽名有效的信，本身必須用手寫簽名）。

一封信涵蓋整個公司： 如果 Amaran Biotech 有三個系統（eBR、LIMS、SCADA），只需要寄一封信，不需要每個系統一封。

3.4 §11.200 — Electronic Signature Components and Controls

原文內容 Original Text

§11.200(a) — Non-biometric electronic signatures:
Electronic signatures that are not based upon biometrics shall:

(1) Employ at least two distinct identification components such as an identification code and password.

When BOTH components are required:

  • When used by an individual other than in the context of continuous or serial use: Must use BOTH identification components (ID code + password)
  • At the start of every non-continuous session (new login, after session timeout): Must use BOTH components

When ONE component is sufficient:

  • When an individual uses a series of signings during a single, continuous period of controlled system access: Subsequent signings need only employ ONE component (e.g., just re-entering the password)

(2) Be used only by their genuine owners; and

(3) Be administered and executed to ensure that attempted use of an individual's electronic signature by anyone other than its genuine owner requires collaboration of two or more individuals.

"When used by an individual other than in the context of continuous or serial use, each electronic signing shall employ both identification components."
— 21 CFR §11.200(a), the two-component rule

§11.200(b) — Biometric electronic signatures:
Electronic signatures based upon biometrics shall be designed to ensure that they cannot be used by anyone other than their genuine owners.

Biometric signatures (fingerprint, retinal scan, facial recognition, voice recognition) do not require the two-component rule because the biometric IS inherently individual and cannot be transferred. The system design must ensure the biometric cannot be spoofed.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

連續作業 vs 非連續作業判斷

單一連續作業 (Single continuous period)： 操作員登入系統（使用帳號 + 密碼），在同一個工作階段中完成多個簽名動作——只需要在後續簽名時再輸入一次密碼（一個元件即可）。

非連續情況（重新登入）： 以下情況必須重新使用兩個認證元件：

  • 工作階段超時（session timeout）自動登出後
  • 操作員主動登出後再登入
  • 從不同電腦或裝置登入
  • 帳號被鎖定後重新解鎖

實務上的常見設計： 初次登入 = 帳號 + 密碼（兩個元件）；同一工作階段內的簽名 = 只需輸入密碼（一個元件）；工作階段超時（通常 15-30 分鐘無操作）= 再次要求帳號 + 密碼。

§11.200(a)(3) 的反共謀設計要求

這條要求說：如果有人試圖使用別人的電子簽名，需要「兩個或兩個以上的人合謀」才能成功。這是一個技術上的防護設計要求：系統設計應確保，單靠一個未授權的人，無法在不被偵測的情況下使用他人的電子簽名。

這就是為什麼帳號 + 密碼的組合在 Part 11 中被稱為「兩個元件 (two distinct identification components)」——如果要偷用別人的簽名，需要同時知道帳號「AND」密碼，必須要有兩方的資訊，不能只靠一個人單獨獲得全部資訊（理論上）。

比喻說明 Analogy

§11.200 的連續作業規則就像銀行金庫的「雙鑰匙」制度：第一次開門，行長（帳號）+ 主管（密碼）兩把鑰匙都要用。一旦金庫開著，在同一個工作日內，只需要一把主管鑰匙就可以繼續存取。但如果金庫關上了（工作階段結束），下次要再開，必須再次使用兩把鑰匙。

3.5 §11.300 — Controls for Identification Codes/Passwords

原文內容 Original Text

Persons who use electronic signatures based upon use of identification codes in combination with passwords shall employ controls to ensure their security and integrity. Such controls shall include:

(a) Maintaining uniqueness:
Maintaining the uniqueness of each combined identification code and password, such that no two individuals have the same combination of identification code and password.

(b) Periodic review and revision:
Ensuring that identification code and password issuances are periodically checked, recalled, or revised (e.g., to cover such events as password aging, with appropriate token revisions as necessary).

(c) Loss management:
Following loss management procedures to electronically de-authorize lost, stolen, missing, or otherwise potentially compromised tokens, cards, and other devices that bear or generate identification code or password information, and to issue temporary or permanent replacements using suitable, rigorous controls.

(d) Transaction safeguards:
Use of transaction safeguards to prevent unauthorized use of passwords and/or identification codes, and to detect and report in an immediate and urgent manner any attempts at their unauthorized use to the system security unit, and, as appropriate, to organizational management.

(e) Initial and periodic device testing:
Initial and periodic testing of devices, such as tokens or cards, that bear or generate identification code or password information to ensure that they function properly and have not been altered in an unauthorized manner.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

§11.300 五個控制要素的實施框架

(a) 唯一性： 系統在技術上不允許兩個用戶設置相同的帳號+密碼組合。大多數現代系統已自動實現（帳號唯一，密碼哈希儲存防止碰撞）。但需要 SOP 明確禁止重用舊帳號名稱。

(b) 定期更新： 密碼管理政策應包含：

  • 密碼有效期（建議：60-90 天）
  • 密碼複雜度要求（長度≥8、大小寫、數字、特殊字元）
  • 密碼歷史記錄（禁止使用最近 10-24 個密碼）
  • 初次登入強制更改（由 IT 設定的初始密碼）

(c) 遺失管理（最關鍵的 SOP）：

  • 立即報告程序（員工發現憑證遺失/被盜立即通報）
  • 立即停用帳號（不是等查明，而是即刻）
  • 重新身份驗證後發放新憑證
  • 事件記錄和調查

(d) 交易保護： 技術實施：

  • 連續N次（通常3-5次）登入失敗自動鎖定帳號
  • 鎖定事件即時通知系統安全人員（可用電子郵件或系統警報）
  • 所有認證嘗試（成功和失敗）均記錄於稽核追蹤

(e) 裝置測試： 適用於硬體令牌（如 RSA SecurID、智慧卡、USB 金鑰）：初次發放前測試 + 定期測試是否正常運作 + 檢查是否有篡改跡象。

FDA 查廠密碼管控常見觀察

常見 FDA 483 觀察：

  • 「系統管理員密碼未定期更換」
  • 「初始密碼未強制使用者在首次登入時更改」
  • 「無書面程序定義憑證遺失/被盜的報告和處置流程」
  • 「登入失敗未觸發帳號鎖定」
  • 「系統允許使用者設定簡單密碼（如 '12345'）」

注意： §11.300 並未明文規定「密碼需 90 天更換」——但「periodically checked, recalled, or revised」的要求使定期更新密碼成為業界標準做法，FDA 查廠時會期待看到書面的密碼政策。

本章重點總結

  • §11.50 三元素缺一不可： 電子簽名必須自動顯示（1）完整印刷姓名、（2）系統產生的時間戳記、（3）明確的簽名意義（審查/批准/撰寫/驗證）。「簽名意義」是最常被忽略的元素，也是 FDA 查廠的關注點。
  • §11.70 簽名連結防偽造： 簽名必須技術上綁定至特定版本的記錄，使「簽名剪貼」偽造在技術上不可能或即刻可偵測。密碼學哈希值和記錄鎖定是主流實施方式。
  • §11.100(c) FDA 認證信函是公司級義務： 首次使用電子簽名前，公司需寄紙本信函至 FDA（Docket No. 92S-0251），聲明電子簽名是手寫簽名的法律等效物。一封信覆蓋整個公司的所有系統。
  • §11.200 雙元件認證： 非生物特徵簽名需要帳號+密碼兩個元件。連續工作階段的後續簽名可用單元件（僅密碼）；每次新工作階段（包括超時後）必須重新使用雙元件。§11.300 進一步要求完整的密碼管控框架（唯一性、定期更新、遺失處理、交易保護、裝置測試）。
