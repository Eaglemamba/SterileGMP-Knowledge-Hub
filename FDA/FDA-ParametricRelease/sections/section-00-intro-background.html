FDA Guidance — Parametric Release of Terminally Sterilized Drug Products

Section I & II：導論與背景

I. Introduction 導論

原文內容 Original Text

This guidance provides recommendations to applicants on information to include in support of parametric release for sterile products terminally sterilized by moist heat when submitting a new drug application (NDA), abbreviated new drug application (ANDA), new animal drug application (NADA), abbreviated new animal drug application (ANADA), biologics license application (BLA), or supplement or other postmarketing report.

Currently, FDA requires that sterile products meet certain sterility requirements before release to the market. In many cases, the requirements for batch release are fulfilled by conducting a sterility test on finished units drawn from the batch. Parametric release is defined as a sterility assurance release program where demonstrated control of the sterilization process enables a firm to use defined critical process controls, in lieu of the sterility test, to fulfill the intent of 21 CFR 211.165(a), and 211.167(a). Under this strategy, market release of terminally sterilized products can be based upon meeting the defined sterilization parameters and not on performing an approved sterility test. Meeting the requirements of the parametric release process can provide greater assurance that a batch meets the sterility requirement than can be achieved with a sterility test of finished units drawn from the batch.

"Parametric release is defined as a sterility assurance release program where demonstrated control of the sterilization process enables a firm to use defined critical process controls, in lieu of the sterility test."

This guidance does not provide information on procedures, studies, or data concerning efficacy and qualification/validation of moist heat sterilization processes. This guidance also does not provide information on sterility assurance validation programs. However, you may find information relating to such topics in the Agency's guidance for industry on Submission of Documentation for Sterilization Process Validation in Applications for Human and Veterinary Drug Products. Current Good Manufacturing Practices (CGMP) requirements for process validation are found at 21 CFR 211.100 and, for sterile products in particular, at 21 CFR 211.113(b). Adherence to CGMPs is required for all marketed products.

The principles in the guidance may also be applicable to products sterilized by other terminal sterilization processes, such as radiation sterilization, which may be suitable for parametric release. For these types of applications, we recommend the applicant discuss with the review division whether applying the guidance would be appropriate.

FDA's guidance documents, including this guidance, do not establish legally enforceable responsibilities. Instead, guidances describe the Agency's current thinking on a topic and should be viewed only as recommendations, unless specific regulatory or statutory requirements are cited. The use of the word should in Agency guidances means that something is suggested or recommended, but not required.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Parametric Release (參數放行)：以經過驗證的滅菌製程「關鍵製程參數」(Critical Process Parameters, CPPs) 的達成結果作為批次放行依據，取代傳統的成品無菌試驗。核心邏輯是：如果你能「證明製程已正確執行」，就比「從成品中抽樣檢測」更能保證無菌性。

Terminal Sterilization (最終滅菌)：藥品在最終容器中（已完成充填與密封後）進行滅菌處理。與 Aseptic Filling (無菌充填) 的根本差異在於：最終滅菌有一個可量化的殺菌步驟，而無菌充填依賴整個製程鏈的無菌環境維護。

21 CFR 211.165(a) 與 211.167(a)：這兩條法規分別規定藥品放行前須符合品質標準、以及無菌藥品須進行無菌試驗。參數放行的法規依據就是用「符合已驗證的製程參數」來「滿足這兩條法規的意旨」(fulfill the intent)——注意 FDA 用的是 "fulfill the intent"，而非 "exempt from"。

比喻說明 Analogy

想像你經營一家食品工廠，必須確保每批罐頭都是無菌的。傳統做法是：每批隨機取幾罐打開檢查有沒有細菌（無菌試驗）。問題是，你只開了 20 罐，這批有 100,000 罐——這像在一座大型圖書館隨機翻 20 本書，就宣稱「整座圖書館沒有錯字」。

參數放行的做法是：你不開罐頭，而是確保滅菌鍋在 121°C 以上維持了至少 15 分鐘，所有溫度探針都記錄合格，化學指示劑變色正常。因為你已經科學驗證過「這個條件下所有微生物都會被殺死」，所以製程參數的達成本身就是最強的無菌證據。

重點提示 Key Notes

適用範圍限定：本 Guidance 僅針對「濕熱最終滅菌」(Moist Heat Terminal Sterilization)。對於輻射滅菌 (Radiation Sterilization) 等其他最終滅菌方式，FDA 建議申請者先與審查部門討論是否適用本指引的原則。

不涉及驗證細節：本 Guidance 不涵蓋滅菌製程的驗證程序、研究或數據——這些內容在 FDA 另一份指引《Submission of Documentation for Sterilization Process Validation》中。CDMO 在建立參數放行系統時，兩份指引必須同時參考。

法律定位："Does not establish legally enforceable responsibilities"——這是所有 FDA Guidance 的標準但書。但在查廠實務中，偏離 Guidance 建議必須有科學理由和文件支持 **(PDA TR30, Section 1.2)**。

實務應用 Practical Application

CDMO 情境：當客戶委託製造的注射劑採用最終滅菌製程時，CDMO 可評估是否申請參數放行。優勢包括：(1) 無菌試驗耗時 14 天，參數放行可顯著縮短放行時間；(2) 消除因無菌試驗偽陽性導致的批次調查風險；(3) 提供比無菌試驗更高的無菌保證水準。但前提是 CDMO 必須有成熟的滅菌製程驗證體系和完善的製程參數監控系統。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 參數放行與傳統無菌試驗放行相比，為何能提供「更高的無菌保證」？請從統計抽樣的局限性角度說明。
  2. 21 CFR 211.165(a) 和 211.167(a) 分別規定什麼？參數放行如何「滿足其意旨」而非「豁免」這些要求？
  3. 如果你的 CDMO 同時有濕熱滅菌和輻射滅菌產品線，申請參數放行時的策略會有何不同？
II. Background 背景

原文內容 Original Text

Sterility testing by cultivation of finished units drawn from the batch is limited in its ability to detect contamination because of the following: (1) the small number of samples required for testing, which restricts the ability to capture those microorganisms dispersed in a large volume, and (2) the limited ability of the prescribed culture media to stimulate growth of all potential microorganisms. Typically, these tests will detect only major errors in the manufacturing process that result in contamination of a large number of product units. However, data derived from in-process controls of a validated terminal sterilization process can provide more accurate information regarding product sterility because the probability of product bioburden surviving the sterilization process in any single unit of a product can be calculated to be less than one in a million.

"The probability of product bioburden surviving the sterilization process in any single unit of a product can be calculated to be less than one in a million."

Parametric release allows manufacturers to replace sterility testing of samples drawn from the finished product as a release criterion with acceptance criteria for the control of identified process parameters. These parameters, called critical parameters, are critical to a successful sterilization process and are based on an in-depth knowledge of the process, the product, the effects of the sterilization process on the product itself, and any microorganisms that become associated with the product during the manufacturing process. Parametric release of the batch is then based on documented evidence of the control of critical parameters, removing the need to test samples drawn from the finished product.

A sterilization load monitor, either in the form of a physical, chemical (ANSI 2008), or biological indicator, is included with each load to satisfy the requirement for a laboratory test. In addition, the sterilization load monitor is always considered a critical process parameter. A successful load monitor result, the meeting of the acceptance criteria of the critical parameters, and having a well validated sterility assurance program demonstrate that there is a state of control of the manufacturing process for the product. The load monitor(s) should be placed in appropriate positions to indicate that the load was exposed to a sterilization process which was measured and recorded for conformance with defined criteria for parametric release. This position(s) is determined based on the evaluation of development and qualification data. The location and number of monitors should be described and justified in the application. Alternative procedures for demonstrating that a load or part of a load was exposed to a sterilization process should be discussed with the review division(s) prior to submitting a plan for parametric release.

FDA conducts scientific evaluation of the parametric release program as part of a cooperative effort among our review staff, compliance staff, and field investigators.

FDA has accepted the practice of parametric release for drug products terminally sterilized by moist heat since 1985. Parametric release, described in the International Conference on Harmonisation (ICH) Q6A (ICH 2000), is endorsed by regulatory and/or pharmaceutical manufacturing groups in the US (PDA 1999, USP 2009), EU (PIC/S 2007, EMEA 2001), and Japan (Sasaki 2002).

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

無菌試驗的統計限制：FDA 在此明確指出傳統無菌試驗的兩大根本缺陷。假設一批產品有 10,000 瓶，依 USP <71> 抽取 20 瓶進行無菌試驗。即使有 1% 的污染率（100 瓶被污染），20 瓶全部合格的機率仍高達約 82%。換言之，傳統無菌試驗只能偵測到「大量污染」，對「低水準污染」幾乎無能為力。

10⁻⁶ SAL (Sterility Assurance Level, 無菌保證水準)："One in a million" 即 SAL = 10⁻⁶，意為每個單位產品的非無菌機率不超過百萬分之一。這是最終滅菌產品的國際公認標準 **(PIC/S Annex 1 2022, Section 8.1)**。

Sterilization Load Monitor (滅菌負載監控器)：可以是物理指示器（如溫度記錄器）、化學指示器（如 Class 5 整合指示器）或生物指示器（如含有 Geobacillus stearothermophilus 的芽孢條）。FDA 明確指出：load monitor 本身「永遠被視為一個 Critical Process Parameter」。

比喻說明 Analogy

無菌試驗就像「在一座城市中隨機敲 20 戶人家的門，確認沒有竊賊」。如果這座城市有 10,000 戶，你敲的 20 戶剛好都沒問題，不代表整座城市是安全的。

參數放行則像「在每個入口安裝監視器，並記錄城門在整夜都保持關閉且上鎖」。你不需要逐戶檢查，因為你已經驗證了整個防禦系統的有效性。Load Monitor 就是放在城裡最薄弱位置的「哨兵」，確認最不利條件下也達到了安全標準。

重點提示 Key Notes

Load Monitor 的選型與位置：FDA 建議 load monitor 放置在「appropriate positions」，此位置由開發和確認數據決定。實務上，這意味著必須先完成 Heat Distribution (熱分佈) 和 Heat Penetration (熱穿透) 研究，找出滅菌鍋中的 cold spot (冷點)，再將 load monitor 放置在最不利位置 **(PDA TR30, Section 4.3)**。

化學指示器分級：FDA 引用 ANSI/AAMI/ISO 15882:2008，推薦使用 Class 5 Integrating Indicator（整合指示器），因為其反應與生物指示器高度相關。Class 3 (Specific Test Indicator) 僅在「特定情況」下可接受——例如當 overkill cycle 已充分驗證且 F₀ 值遠超最低要求時。

國際共識：FDA 自 1985 年起接受參數放行。ICH Q6A、PDA TR30 (1999)、USP <1222> (2009)、PIC/S (2007)、EMEA (2001)、日本 (2002) 均已背書——這是一個成熟的、有全球法規共識的做法 **(USP <1222>, Section 1)**。

公式與計算 Formula

Sterility Assurance Level (SAL) 計算概念：

SAL = 10⁻⁶ (標準要求)

對於 Overkill Approach：
  F₀ ≥ 12 分鐘 (通常目標 15-20 分鐘)
  假設 Bioburden = 10⁶ CFU，D₁₂₁ = 1 min
  → 滅菌後存活機率 = 10⁶ × 10⁻¹²  = 10⁻⁶ ✓

對於 Bioburden-Based Approach：
  根據實際 bioburden 數據和微生物耐熱性計算
  所需 F₀ 值，確保達到 SAL ≤ 10⁻⁶

實務應用 Practical Application

CDMO 滅菌負載監控器選型決策：

對於 CDMO 而言，load monitor 的選型直接影響參數放行的可行性：

  • Biological Indicator (BI, 生物指示器)：最直接的微生物殺滅證據，但需 48-72 小時培養判讀，可能抵消參數放行的時間優勢
  • Chemical Indicator (CI, 化學指示器)：結果立即可讀，推薦 Class 5 整合指示器。對多產品線 CDMO 最實用，可實現「當日放行」
  • Physical Indicator (溫度探針)：提供連續的 F₀ 數據記錄，通常作為主要監控手段配合 CI/BI 使用

多數 CDMO 採用「物理監控（溫度探針 + F₀ 計算）+ Class 5 化學指示器」的組合方案，兼顧科學嚴謹性與放行效率。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. FDA 指出無菌試驗有兩大局限性。請分別說明這兩個局限性，並計算：若一批 10,000 瓶產品中有 50 瓶被污染（0.5%），依 USP <71> 抽 20 瓶全部合格的機率約為多少？
  2. 為何 FDA 將 sterilization load monitor「永遠視為 Critical Process Parameter」？如果 load monitor 結果失敗但所有物理參數（溫度、壓力、時間）都合格，該批次能否放行？
  3. 比較 Overkill Approach 和 Bioburden-Based Approach 的 F₀ 要求差異。在什麼產品特性下，CDMO 可能需要選擇 bioburden-based approach？**(參考 PDA TR30, Section 3)**
  4. FDA 引用了 ANSI/AAMI/ISO 15882:2008 關於化學指示器的分級。Class 5 Integrating Indicator 與 Class 3 Specific Test Indicator 的關鍵差異是什麼？為何 FDA 推薦 Class 5？

本節重點回顧 Section Summary

  • 參數放行的定義：以已驗證滅菌製程的關鍵參數達成作為批次放行依據，取代傳統無菌試驗，滿足 21 CFR 211.165(a) 和 211.167(a) 的意旨
  • 無菌試驗的局限：受限於(1)小樣本抽樣的統計弱點、(2)培養基對所有微生物生長刺激能力有限，僅能偵測大規模污染事件
  • SAL 10⁻⁶：經驗證的最終滅菌製程可將每個產品單位的非無菌機率控制在百萬分之一以下，遠優於無菌試驗能達到的檢測靈敏度
  • Load Monitor 是 CPP：無論是物理、化學或生物指示器，load monitor 永遠被視為關鍵製程參數，其位置須基於熱分佈/熱穿透研究數據來決定
  • 全球共識：FDA (1985)、ICH Q6A (2000)、PDA TR30 (1999)、USP <1222> (2009)、PIC/S (2007)、EMEA (2001) 均支持參數放行，是成熟且國際認可的做法
