本章學習目標

  • 說明無菌性檢驗 (Sterility Test) 的統計侷限性，並解釋為何不能僅靠它作為批次放行的唯一保證

  • 分析介入操作 (Interventions) 和停線事件 (Line Stoppages) 的記錄要求，及其對無菌性的影響
Section XI — Sterility Testing 無菌性檢驗（概覽）

原文內容 Original Text

21 CFR 211.167(a): For each batch of drug product purporting to be sterile and/or pyrogen-free, there shall be appropriate laboratory testing to determine conformance to such requirements. The test procedures shall be in writing and shall be followed.
21 CFR 211.165(e): The accuracy, sensitivity, specificity, and reproducibility of test methods employed by the firm shall be established and documented.
21 CFR 211.160(b): Laboratory controls shall include the establishment of scientifically sound and appropriate specifications, standards, sampling plans, and test procedures designed to assure that components, drug product containers, closures, in-process materials, labeling, and drug products conform to appropriate standards of identity, strength, quality, and purity.

Certain aspects of sterility testing are of particular importance, including control of the testing environment, understanding the test limitations, and investigating manufacturing systems following a positive test.

The testing laboratory environment should employ facilities and controls comparable to those used for aseptic filling operations. Poor or deficient sterility test facilities or controls can result in test failure. If production facilities and controls are significantly better than those for sterility testing, the danger exists of mistakenly attributing a positive sterility test result to a faulty laboratory even when the product tested could have, in fact, been nonsterile. Therefore, a manufacturing deficiency may go undetected.

The use of isolators for sterility testing minimizes the chance of a false positive test result.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：無菌性檢驗的三大關鍵面向

FDA 指出，無菌性檢驗有三個特別重要的面向，缺一不可：

  • 測試環境的管控——實驗室本身要有達到無菌操作等級的環境控制
  • 理解測試的侷限性——統計上，這個測試並不是高靈敏度的篩選工具
  • 陽性結果後的系統調查——不能只看實驗室，必須追溯製程

這三點對 CDMO 的 QC 實驗室和 QA 部門都有直接影響，在客戶稽核時也常被問及。

警示：實驗室品質不能低於生產環境

FDA 的邏輯非常清楚：如果生產區的環控比實驗室更嚴格，當陽性結果出現時， 廠商可能錯誤地把責任推給實驗室，卻對真正不無菌的產品放行， 造成製程缺失被掩蓋。

實務建議：QC 微生物實驗室應維持至少 ISO 5（A/B 級）的操作條件， 並定期進行環境監測，且數據須可追溯。

CDMO COO 應用：隔離器 (Isolator) 投資的業務邏輯

FDA 明確推薦使用隔離器進行無菌性檢驗以降低假陽性機率。 對 CDMO 而言，採用隔離器不只是技術升級，更是商業風險管理： 減少因假陽性導致的批次報廢、重測成本與交期延誤， 同時在客戶稽核時展現先進的實驗室管控能力，提升競爭差異化。

Section XI-A — Microbiological Laboratory Controls 微生物實驗室管控

原文內容 Original Text

A Sterility testing methods are required to be accurate and reproducible, in accordance with 211.194 and 211.165. USP <71> "Sterility Tests" is the principal source used for sterility testing methods, including information on test procedures and media.

As a part of methods validation, appropriate microbiological challenge testing will demonstrate reproducibility of the method to reliably recover representative microorganisms. If growth is inhibited, modifications (e.g., increased dilution, additional membrane filter washes, addition of inactivating agents) to the test method should be implemented to optimize recovery.

Ultimately, methods validation studies should demonstrate that the method does not provide an opportunity for false negatives.

It is essential that the media used to perform sterility testing be rendered sterile and demonstrated as growth promoting. Personnel performing sterility testing should be qualified and trained for the task. A written program should be in place to maintain updated training of personnel and confirm acceptable sterility testing practices.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：方法驗證的三個關鍵證明

無菌性檢驗的方法驗證必須能夠回答三個問題：

  • 促生長性 (Growth Promotion)：培養基本身能支持目標微生物生長嗎？（培養基每批次都需確認）
  • 抑菌效果排除 (Bacteriostasis/Fungistasis Test)：產品本身是否抑制微生物生長？若有，修改方法（稀釋、沖洗、加中和劑）
  • 無假陰性 (No False Negatives)：方法不能因產品抑制或操作問題錯過真正的污染

類比：方法驗證就像「偵探訓練」

想像你要訓練一位偵探去抓壞人（微生物）。你必須先確認： 這位偵探的工具（培養基）能正常運作嗎？ 現場有沒有干擾偵探辦案的阻礙（產品的抑制效果）？ 訓練測試中，偵探是否每次都能找到藏起來的壞人（挑戰微生物）？ 如果偵探在訓練中也找不到，就不能派他去執行真正的任務。

CDMO 實驗室合規要點

常見稽核缺失：培養基促生長記錄不完整、人員訓練記錄缺少驗證環節、 或方法驗證報告未涵蓋抑菌性測試。 對於含抗菌成分的產品（如含防腐劑的多劑量製劑）， 抑菌性測試尤其重要，是客戶技術轉移清單中必須確認的項目。

Section XI-B — Sampling and Incubation 取樣與培育

原文內容 Original Text

B Sterility tests are limited in their ability to detect contamination because of the small sample size typically used. For example, as described by USP, statistical evaluations indicate that the sterility test sampling plan "only enables the detection of contamination in a lot in which 10% of the units are contaminated about nine times out of ten in making the test."

Statistical Illustration: If a 10,000-unit lot with a 0.1% contamination level was sterility tested using 20 units, there is a 98% chance that the batch would pass the test. This means a lot with 10 contaminated units out of 10,000 would almost certainly be released.

It is important that the samples represent the entire batch and processing conditions. Samples should be taken:

  • at the beginning, middle, and end of the aseptic processing operation
  • in conjunction with processing interventions or excursions

Because of the limited sensitivity of the test, any positive result is considered a serious CGMP issue that should be thoroughly investigated.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

重要警示：無菌性檢驗不是高靈敏度的測試

這個統計數字令人震驚，但卻是業界必須正視的事實： 一批有 10 瓶污染的 10,000 瓶批次， 用 20 個樣品進行無菌性檢驗，有 98% 的機率會通過。

這正是為什麼 FDA 強調：無菌保證不能只靠最終的無菌性檢驗， 而必須建立在整個製程的系統管控上——亦即 「Sterility Assurance by Process」 而非 「Sterility Assurance by Testing」。

代表性取樣的策略意義

FDA 要求樣品必須代表整批製程條件，特別是：

  • 批次頭、中、尾：涵蓋不同時間點的環境暴露與設備狀態
  • 介入操作後：人員介入是最高污染風險時段，應特別標記追蹤

對 CDMO 而言，取樣計畫必須在批次製造規程 (BPR) 中明確記錄， 包含取樣時機的 rationale，以應對客戶及 FDA 的文件審查。

CDMO COO 啟示：陽性即嚴肅事件

任何無菌陽性結果都是嚴重的 CGMP 問題，不能以「可能是實驗室污染」 為理由輕易帶過。即使最後判定為實驗室錯誤， 整個調查過程與決策邏輯都必須有文件支持。 對 CDMO 而言，此類事件若處理不當，可能影響客戶對整個委託生產平台的信心。

原文內容 Original Text

C Care should be taken in the performance of the sterility test to preclude any activity that allows for possible sample contamination. When microbial growth is observed, the lot should be considered nonsterile and an investigation conducted. An initial positive test would be invalid only in an instance in which microbial growth can be unequivocally ascribed to laboratory error.

Only if conclusive and documented evidence clearly shows that the contamination occurred as part of testing should a new test be performed. When available evidence is inconclusive, batches should be rejected as not conforming to sterility requirements.

After considering all relevant factors concerning the manufacture of the product and testing of the samples, the comprehensive written investigation should include specific conclusions and identify corrective actions. The investigation's persuasive evidence of the origin of the contamination should be based on at least the following:

  1. Identification (speciation) of the organism in the sterility test
  2. Record of laboratory tests and deviations
  3. Monitoring of production area environment
  4. Monitoring Personnel
  5. Product Presterilization Bioburden
  6. Production record review
  7. Manufacturing history

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

黃金原則：有生長就是陽性，不能「自動無效化」

FDA 的立場非常強硬：只要測試中出現微生物生長， 該批次即被視為不無菌，調查必須立即啟動。 唯一可以使初次測試無效的情況，是有明確且有文件支持的 實驗室錯誤證據。

常見錯誤做法（稽核紅旗）：調查結論寫「可能是實驗室污染」但缺乏具體證據， 然後直接進行重測。FDA 視此為程序不符合，可能導致 Warning Letter。

七大調查要素的邏輯架構

這七個要素不是隨機列舉，而是系統性地涵蓋了污染可能的所有來源：

  • 要素 1（菌種鑑定）：確認是哪種菌，才能比對環境資料庫找出來源
  • 要素 2（實驗室偏差）：排除或確認實驗室為污染源
  • 要素 3（生產環境監測）：比對生產日的環境數據趨勢
  • 要素 4（人員監測）：操作人員的微生物監測資料
  • 要素 5（滅菌前生物負荷）：原料或中間體的微生物起始量
  • 要素 6（批次記錄）：製程中是否有異常事件或偏差
  • 要素 7（製造歷程）：歷史數據、過去偏差、製程變更記錄
調查七大要素詳解 Investigation Elements — Detail

要素 1 & 2 原文 Elements 1 & 2

1. Identification (speciation) of the organism

Sterility test isolates should be identified to the species level. Microbiological monitoring data should be reviewed to determine if the organism is also found in laboratory and production environments, personnel, or product bioburden. Advanced identification methods (e.g., nucleic-acid based) are valuable for investigational purposes. When comparing results from environmental monitoring and sterility positives, both identifications should be performed using the same methodology.

2. Record of laboratory tests and deviations

Review of laboratory deviation and investigation findings can help to eliminate or implicate the laboratory as the source of contamination. For example, if the organism is seldom found in the laboratory environment, product contamination is more likely than laboratory error. If the organism is found in laboratory and production environments, it can still indicate product contamination.

The proper handling of deviations is an essential aspect of laboratory control. When a deviation occurs during sterility testing, it should be documented, investigated, and remedied. If any deviation is considered to have compromised the integrity of the sterility test, the test should be invalidated immediately without incubation.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

要素 1：菌種鑑定的技術深度

僅報告「革蘭氏陽性球菌」是不夠的，FDA 要求鑑定至種 (Species) 層級。 原因在於：唯有到種層級，才能與環境監測資料庫進行比對， 確認同一菌種是否存在於生產環境、實驗室或特定人員身上。

現代分子生物學方法（如 16S rRNA 定序）比傳統生化鑑定更精確， FDA 特別提到核酸定序在調查中的價值。 重要：環監資料與無菌陽性的鑑定方法必須一致，否則比對無效。

要素 2：偏差記錄的「立即無效化」規則

若測試過程中的偏差已危害測試的完整性（例如：隔離器洩漏、 培養基受污染），必須立即無效化，不可繼續培育。 這代表無效化的時間點是「發現偏差當下」，而非事後討論。

稽核紅旗：若廠商在培育結束後才討論是否無效化， FDA 會質疑該決定是受測試結果影響（如果無生長就不會提無效化）， 而非基於真正的偏差理由——此模式本身即為 CGMP 違規。

CDMO COO 應用：環境監測資料庫的戰略價值

維護一個完整的、可查詢菌種歷史記錄的微生物資料庫， 是無菌陽性調查的核心競爭力。 CDMO 在服務多個客戶時，若能快速從資料庫中找出特定菌種的歷史出現點， 調查效率與說服力都會大幅提升，也更能保護客戶批次免於不必要的拒絕。

要素 3 & 4 原文 Elements 3 & 4

3. Monitoring of production area environment

Trend analysis of microorganisms in the critical and immediately adjacent areas is especially helpful in determining the source of contamination in a sterility failure investigation. Consideration of environmental microbial data should not be limited to results of monitoring the production environment for the lot, day, or shift associated with the suspect lot. For example, results showing little or no recovery of microorganisms can be misleading, especially when preceded or followed by a finding of an adverse trend or atypically high microbial counts. It is therefore important to look at both short- and long-term environmental trend analyses.

4. Monitoring Personnel

The review of data and associated trends from daily monitoring of personnel can provide important information indicating a route of contamination. The adequacy of personnel practices and training also merit significant review and consideration.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

要素 3：趨勢分析的時間維度

FDA 特別警告：僅看「批次生產當天」的環境監測結果是不夠的， 甚至可能誤導調查方向。正確做法是同時檢視：

  • 短期趨勢：生產前後數天的環監數據
  • 長期趨勢：該填充線過去數月的環監走向，是否有上升趨勢

「當天數據正常」並不代表製程無問題——若前後幾天有超標記錄， 這個「正常」數據反而可能是異常中的偶然。

要素 4：人員是最高風險污染源

無菌製程中，人員是最主要的污染來源（人體每分鐘會散發數千個微粒）。 FDA 要求調查時必須審查：人員的每日手套、更衣監測數據， 以及人員訓練記錄是否符合要求。

特別注意：若調查批次的填充日有臨時或新人員參與操作， 這一點必須在調查報告中明確評估。

要素 5–7 原文 Elements 5–7

5. Product Presterilization Bioburden

We recommend review of trends in product bioburden and consideration of whether adverse bioburden trends have occurred.

6. Production record review

Complete batch and production control records should be reviewed to detect any signs of failures or anomalies that could have a bearing on product sterility. For example, the investigation should include elements such as:

  • Events that could have impacted on the critical zone
  • Batch and trending data that indicate whether utility and/or support systems are functioning properly — e.g., records of air quality monitoring for filling lines could show a time at which there was improper air balance or an unusually high particle count
  • Whether construction or maintenance activities could have had an adverse impact

7. Manufacturing history

The manufacturing history of a product or similar products should be reviewed as part of the investigation. Past deviations, problems, or changes (e.g., process, components, equipment) are among the factors that can provide an indication of the origin of the problem.

Additional FDA Guidance on Lab Trends

A sterility positive result can be viewed as indicative of production or laboratory problems, and the entire manufacturing process should be comprehensively investigated since such problems often can extend beyond a single batch. Where a laboratory has a good track record with respect to errors, this history can lower suspicion of the lab. However, where a laboratory has a poor track record, firms should not assume that the contamination is automatically more attributable to the laboratory and consequently overlook a genuine production problem. Accordingly, it is essential that all sterility positives be thoroughly investigated.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

要素 5：滅菌前生物負荷的趨勢意義

無菌製程是「無法透過最終滅菌挽救」的製程， 因此滅菌前的微生物負荷控制至關重要。 若調查發現生物負荷有上升趨勢，代表原料或製程中間體的管控可能已出現問題， 需追溯原物料供應商、水系統、或製程設備的清潔狀態。

要素 6：批次記錄中的「隱藏線索」

FDA 點名三類特別重要的記錄：

  • 關鍵區域事件：靠近暴露產品的任何活動（包含機器調整、容器掉落等）
  • 支援系統記錄：HVAC 氣流、微粒計數、WFI TOC 等——這些「周邊」數據往往是污染的早期預警信號
  • 施工與維修：即使在鄰近區域進行的工程也可能影響無菌區的微粒或氣流

要素 7 類比：製造歷程是「犯罪現場時間軸」

警察調查謀殺案時不只看案發當天——他們會建立完整的時間軸， 找出所有可能相關的事件和關係人。 製造歷程審查的邏輯完全一樣：近期設備變更、製程偏差、 或原物料供應商改變，都可能是問題的根源。 FDA 特別強調「相似產品」也要納入調查範圍——因為同一填充線、 同一操作人員、同一環境可能影響多個品項。

CDMO COO 總結：調查報告的商業意涵

一份高品質的無菌陽性調查報告，不只是法規合規的要求， 更是 CDMO 對客戶的信任合約。 當客戶的產品在你的廠房出現無菌陽性，他們首先要看的是： 調查是否完整、客觀、有科學支撐，且 CAPA 是否實際有效。 調查報告的品質直接反映 CDMO 的品質文化與技術能力。

Section XII — Batch Record Review: Process Control Documentation 批次記錄審查：製程管控文件

原文內容 Original Text

21 CFR 211.100(b): Written production and process control procedures shall be followed in the execution of the various production and process control functions and shall be documented at the time of performance. Any deviation from the written procedures shall be recorded and justified.
21 CFR 211.192: All drug product production and control records, including those for packaging and labeling, shall be reviewed and approved by the quality control unit to determine compliance with all established, approved written procedures before a batch is released or distributed. Any unexplained discrepancy or the failure of a batch or any of its components to meet any of its specifications shall be thoroughly investigated, whether or not the batch has already been distributed. The investigation shall extend to other batches of the same drug product and other drug products that may have been associated with the specific failure or discrepancy.

Manufacturers should build process and environmental control activities into their aseptic processing operation. It is critical that these activities be maintained and strictly implemented on a daily basis.

The requirement for review of all batch records and data for conformance with written procedures, operating parameters, and product specifications prior to arriving at the final release decision calls for an overall review of process and system performance for that given cycle of manufacture. All in-process and laboratory control results must be included with the batch record documentation in accordance with section 211.188.

Review of environmental and personnel monitoring data, as well as other data relating to acceptability of output from support systems (e.g., HEPA/HVAC, WFI, steam generator) and proper functioning of equipment (e.g., batch alarms report; integrity of various filters) are considered essential elements of the batch release decision.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：批次放行 = 整個製造生態系統的評估

FDA 對批次放行的定義不只是「產品符合規格」， 而是對整個製造循環（Cycle of Manufacture）的全面評估。 放行審查必須涵蓋：

  • 製程管控記錄：在線數據是否符合製程規格
  • 環境監測數據：生產期間環控是否維持在可接受範圍
  • 人員監測數據：操作人員的更衣及動態微粒監測
  • 支援系統記錄：HVAC、WFI、蒸汽產生器的運行數據
  • 設備功能確認：批次警報記錄、過濾器完整性測試結果
  • 實驗室管控結果：所有中間體及最終產品檢驗數據

「每日嚴格執行」的合規文化意涵

FDA 使用「daily basis（每日）」這個字眼具有深意。 製程管控不是稽核前才執行的活動，而是每一個生產班次的基本要求。 這對 CDMO 的班組長 (Shift Supervisor) 和 QA 現場代表 (QA Floor Rep) 有直接的行為要求：每天完成、每天確認、每天記錄。

CDMO COO 應用：批次記錄的數位化策略

傳統紙本批次記錄難以支持快速的多品項切換。 導入電子批次記錄系統 (eBRS/EBR) 可以： 自動整合環監、支援系統、實驗室數據， 減少人工匯整錯誤，並讓 QA 放行審查更高效。 這也是客戶在技術轉移評估中越來越常要求的 CDMO 能力指標。

Section XII — Interventions & Line Stoppages 介入操作與停線記錄

原文內容 Original Text

While interventions and/or stoppages are normally recorded in the batch record, the manner of documenting these occurrences varies. In particular, line stoppages and any unplanned interventions should be sufficiently documented in batch records with the associated time and duration of the event.

In addition to lengthened dwell time of sterile product elements in the critical area, an extensive intervention can increase contamination risk. Sterility failures have often been attributed to atypical or extensive interventions that have occurred as a response to an undesirable event during the aseptic process.

Written procedures describing the need for line clearances in the event of certain interventions, such as machine adjustments and any repairs, should be established. Such interventions should be documented with more detail than minor events. Interventions that result in substantial activity near exposed product or container closures or that last beyond a reasonable exposure time should, where appropriate, result in a local or full line clearance.

Any disruption in power supply, however momentary, that could affect product quality is a manufacturing deviation and must be included in batch records (211.100, 211.192).

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

高風險事件：非計畫性介入是最常見的無菌失敗根因

FDA 明確指出：許多無菌失敗案例的根本原因， 是非計畫性、長時間的介入操作（例如機器故障時的人工調整）。 這類操作往往在緊急狀況下倉促執行，無菌技術更容易出現漏洞。

批次記錄的記載要求：

  • 介入類型（計畫性 vs. 非計畫性）
  • 發生時間與持續時間
  • 操作人員身份
  • 是否觸發停線清場 (Line Clearance)

停線清場 (Line Clearance) 的觸發標準

FDA 要求廠商建立書面程序，明確定義哪些介入事件需要觸發局部或全線清場。 觸發情境應包含（但不限於）：

  • 暴露產品附近的大型機械維修
  • 超過合理暴露時間的連續介入
  • 任何危及關鍵區域無菌性的事件

對 CDMO 而言，這項程序的設計品質，在技術轉移審查時會被客戶直接評估。

電力中斷：零容忍的批次記錄要求

任何可能影響產品品質的電力中斷——即使只是瞬間—— 都必須記載於批次記錄並作為製造偏差處理。 影響範圍可能包含：HVAC 氣流中斷、填充泵停止、 層流罩風扇停轉、環境監測設備暫停等。 這些都需要在偏差評估中逐一確認影響程度。

CDMO COO 實務建議：介入分級管理制度

建議建立介入操作分級制度（例如 Level 1 / 2 / 3）， 依嚴重程度規定不同的記錄詳細度、QA 通報要求及清場判斷標準。 此制度能幫助操作人員在現場快速判斷， 同時確保批次記錄的完整性能通過 FDA 及客戶稽核的檢視， 也是多品項 CDMO 環境下實現標準化管控的有效工具。

綜合思考練習 Integrated Practice Questions

  1. 一批 5,000 瓶的注射劑進行無菌性檢驗（取 20 瓶），結果 1 瓶出現菌落生長。你是 QA 主管，接下來 48 小時的決策步驟為何？請說明你會在什麼條件下允許重測，又在什麼條件下必須拒絕批次。
  2. 調查報告顯示，陽性菌株為 Bacillus subtilis，而該菌在過去 6 個月的生產環境監測資料庫中從未出現，但在實驗室區域曾出現兩次。這對你的結論有何影響？若比對方法不一致（生產用 API 20E，實驗室用 16S 定序），你如何處理？
  3. 填充班次進行中，因瓶子堵塞而停機 25 分鐘，操作員在 ISO 5 區進行了徒手排除，未告知 QA。事後在批次記錄中只記載「短暫停機，已恢復生產」。作為稽核員，你會提出哪些 483 觀察？
  4. 客戶要求你的 CDMO 提供最近 12 個月的無菌陽性調查 KPI 報告，包含：發生率、判定來源（實驗室 vs. 製程）、CAPA 完成率。請設計一個滿足 FDA 期望且對客戶有說服力的報告架構。
