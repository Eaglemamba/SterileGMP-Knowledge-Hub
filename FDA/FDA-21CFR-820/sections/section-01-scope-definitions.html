Section 1: §820.1–820.3 — Scope, Applicability & Definitions

§820.1–820.3：適用範圍與定義
21 CFR Part 820 — QMSR | FDA | §§ 820.1–820.3 | eCFR current as of April 2026
QMSR 過渡說明 / QMSR Transition Note: The FDA Quality Management System Regulation (QMSR) final rule was published February 2, 2024, effective February 2, 2026. QMSR aligns Part 820 with ISO 13485:2016. This section covers the foundational regulatory text and definitions as they inform current practice, with explicit QMSR amendment notes where terminology has changed.

本章學習目標 Learning Objectives

  • 準確判斷 §820.1 適用範圍：哪些實體和活動在 Part 820 管轄之下，哪些器材類別享有豁免或減免要求
  • 掌握 §820.3 全部關鍵定義，特別是「Manufacturer」的廣義定義——涵蓋合約滅菌商、重新標示商等非傳統製造角色
  • 清晰區分 DHF（設計歷程檔案）、DHR（器材歷史記錄）、DMR（器材主記錄）三種文件的內容、目的與負責人
  • 理解組合產品（Combination Product）在何種條件下適用 Part 820，以及與 21 CFR Part 211 的交叉合規義務
  • 掌握 QMSR 最終規則（2024）對 Part 820 定義架構的影響，理解如何對應 ISO 13485:2016 術語
1.1 §820.1 — Scope（適用範圍）

原文內容 Original Text

§820.1(a) — General

(1) Current good manufacturing practice (CGMP) requirements are set forth in this quality system regulation. The requirements in this part govern the methods used in, and the facilities and controls used for, the design, manufacture, packaging, labeling, storage, installation, and servicing of all finished devices intended for human use. The requirements in this part are intended to ensure that finished devices will be safe and effective and otherwise in compliance with the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (the act). This part establishes basic requirements applicable to manufacturers of finished medical devices.

(2) If a manufacturer engages in only some operations subject to the requirements in this part, and not in others, that manufacturer need only comply with those requirements applicable to the operations in which it is engaged.

(3) With respect to class I devices, design controls apply only to those devices listed in §820.30(a)(2). This regulation does not apply to manufacturers of components or parts of finished devices, but such manufacturers are encouraged to use appropriate provisions of this regulation as guidance. Manufacturers of human blood and blood components are not subject to this part, but are subject to part 606 of this chapter.

§820.1(b) — Responsibility of manufacturers
Each manufacturer shall be responsible for establishing and maintaining an adequate quality system with the organizational structure, responsibilities, procedures, processes, and adequate resources for assuring that devices will be safe and effective and otherwise in compliance with this part.

§820.1(c) — Applicability
The quality system regulation applies to any finished device manufacturer who imports, exports, repackages, or relabels a device. A manufacturer of a finished medical device must comply with this part regardless of where the device is manufactured.

§820.1(d) — Foreign manufacturers
If a manufacturer who offers devices for import into the United States refuses to permit or allow the completion of a Food and Drug Administration inspection of the foreign facility for the purpose of determining compliance with this part, it shall appear for purposes of section 801(a) of the act, that the methods used in, and the facilities and controls used for, the design, manufacture, packaging, labeling, storage, installation, or servicing of any devices produced at such facility that are offered for import into the United States do not conform to the requirements of section 520(f) of the act and this part and that the devices manufactured at that facility are adulterated.

§820.1(e) — Exemptions or variances
Any person who wishes to petition for an exemption or variance from any device quality system requirement is subject to the requirements of section 520(f)(2) of the act. Petitions for an exemption or variance shall be submitted according to the procedures set forth in §10.30 of this chapter, the FDA's administrative procedures. Guidance documents on the exemption procedures are available from CDRH.

"Each manufacturer shall be responsible for establishing and maintaining an adequate quality system…for assuring that devices will be safe and effective."
— 21 CFR §820.1(b)

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

§820.1 適用範圍四個核心要點

1. 適用對象：「Finished Device」的製造商
Part 820 的管轄對象是「已完成品（finished device）」的製造商。零件/組件製造商（component manufacturers）不被強制適用，但 FDA 鼓勵其參考 Part 820 作為指引。這是很多人誤解的地方——若你只製造 O-ring 或電子晶片，Part 820 對你不具強制效力。

2. 地理適用範圍：在美國銷售即適用
§820.1(c) 明確指出：無論製造地在哪，只要器材在美國上市，製造商就必須符合 Part 820。這意味著台灣、中國、歐洲的醫療器材製造商，只要出口至美國，都在 FDA 的 QMSR 管轄範圍內。

3. Class I 設計管制豁免
第 I 類器材通常享有設計管制（§820.30）的豁免，但有例外——§820.30(a)(2) 列有特定第 I 類器材仍需設計管制（如某些滅菌器、心臟節律器測試儀等）。第 II 和第 III 類器材均需完整設計管制。

4. 「任何製造活動」原則
§820.1(a)(2) 規定：若製造商只參與部分製造活動，則只需符合相關活動的要求。例如，若某公司只做最終包裝，不做製造，則只需符合包裝和標示相關的 Part 820 條款。

常見陷阱：哪些人算「Manufacturer」？

Part 820 對「Manufacturer」的定義出乎意料地廣，以下角色都可能被納入：

  • Specification Developer（規格開發商）： 即使自己不生產，只要設計器材規格並外包生產，就是 Part 820 的「Manufacturer」
  • Remanufacturer（再製造商）： 對原廠器材進行重大修改或修復，使其效能規格改變者
  • Contract Sterilizer（合約滅菌商）： 只負責滅菌的供應商，也可能被視為 Part 820 的適用對象
  • Relabeler（重新標示商）： 以自有品牌重新標示他人製造之器材者

詳見 §820.3(o) 定義說明。

QMSR 更新說明（2024 最終規則）

在 QMSR 最終規則下，§820.1 被修訂為透過「incorporation by reference」將 ISO 13485:2016 納入美國法規框架。實務意義：

  • ISO 13485:2016 認證廠商更容易展示 Part 820 合規
  • FDA 查廠官員將對照 ISO 13485 要求進行評估
  • §820.1 的適用範圍邏輯基本不變，但定義架構轉移至 ISO 13485

練習思考 Practice Questions

  2. 某合約研究機構（CRO）負責替器材公司進行最終功能測試並簽發放行，但不參與製造。CRO 是否受 Part 820 約束？
  3. §820.1(a)(2) 的「部分活動原則」對一個只做滅菌服務的合約滅菌商意味著什麼？它至少需要符合 Part 820 的哪些章節？
1.2 §820.3 — Key Definitions（關鍵定義：Part 1）

原文內容 Original Text

§820.3(a) — Act
Act means the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act, as amended (secs. 201–903, 52 Stat. 1040 et seq., as amended (21 U.S.C. 321–394)). All definitions in section 201 of the act shall apply.

§820.3(b) — Complaint
Complaint means any written, electronic, or oral communication that alleges deficiencies related to the identity, quality, durability, reliability, safety, effectiveness, or performance of a device after it is released for distribution.

§820.3(d) — Component
Component means any raw material, substance, piece, part, software, firmware, labeling, or assembly which is intended to be included as part of the finished, packaged, and labeled device.

§820.3(f) — Design history file (DHF)
Design history file means a compilation of records which describes the design history of a finished device.

§820.3(g) — Design input
Design input means the physical and performance requirements of a device that are used as a basis for device design.

§820.3(h) — Design output
Design output means the results of a design effort at each design phase and at the end of the total design effort. The finished design output is the basis for the device master record. Total finished design outputs consist of the device, its packaging and labeling, and the device master record.

§820.3(i) — Design review
Design review means a documented, comprehensive, systematic examination of a design to evaluate the adequacy of the design requirements, to evaluate the capability of the design to meet these requirements, and to identify problems.

§820.3(j) — Device history record (DHR)
Device history record means a compilation of records containing the production history of a finished device.

§820.3(k) — Device master record (DMR)
Device master record means a compilation of records containing the procedures and specifications for a finished device.

§820.3(l) — Establish
Establish means define, document (in writing or electronically), and implement.

§820.3(m) — Finished device
Finished device means any device or accessory to any device that is suitable for use or capable of functioning, whether or not it is packaged or sterilized.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

「Complaint」定義的法規意涵

§820.3(b) 的投訴定義有三個關鍵要件：

  1. 書面、電子或口頭——口頭抱怨也算投訴！電話投訴必須書面記錄
  2. 聲稱「缺陷（deficiencies）」——用戶的主觀不滿不一定是投訴，必須涉及器材本身的問題
  3. 「放行後（after released for distribution）」——這是時間門檻，製造過程中的不合格不算投訴，而屬 CAPA 管理範疇

陷阱：若有人打電話說「操作說明書不清楚」，這算 Complaint 嗎？如果說明書缺陷可能影響器材的安全使用，則是。

「Component」定義的廣義性

§820.3(d) 的 Component 定義包括：軟體（software）和韌體（firmware）——這與藥品 GMP 的「成分」概念不同。一個醫療軟體 App 如果是醫療器材的組成部分，就是「component」，必須受設計管制。

QMSR 注意：ISO 13485 使用「product」而非「component」，但概念範疇類似。

「Establish」= 定義 + 文件化 + 實施

§820.3(l) 的「Establish」定義非常重要——凡是法規要求「establish」某程序，就必須同時做到：

  • Define（定義）： 明確規定要做什麼
  • Document（文件化）： 寫下來（紙本或電子）
  • Implement（實施）： 實際執行

只有 SOP 但未實施 = 未「Establish」。FDA 查廠時，僅出示文件是不夠的，必須證明實際執行。

比喻說明：DMR vs DHR vs DHF — 餐廳比喻

用餐廳來理解三種記錄的差異：

  • DMR（器材主記錄）= 食譜書： 規定這道菜「應該」如何製作——食材規格、烹調程序、品質標準。是永久性的規範文件。
  • DHR（器材歷史記錄）= 每日出菜記錄： 記錄「實際上」今天這批菜是怎麼做的——用了哪些食材（lot number）、誰做的、什麼時間、品管結果。每批次都有一份。
  • DHF（設計歷程檔案）= 菜品研發檔案： 記錄這道菜「如何被設計出來的」——從最初構想、試作記錄、改良版本，到最終定案的過程。研發完成後通常封存。
1.3 §820.3 — Key Definitions（關鍵定義：Part 2）

原文內容 Original Text

§820.3(o) — Manufacturer
Manufacturer means any person who designs, manufactures, fabricates, assembles, or processes a finished device. Manufacturer includes but is not limited to those who perform the functions of contract sterilization, installation, relabeling, remanufacturing, repacking, or specification development, and initial distributors of foreign entities performing these functions.

§820.3(n) — Management
Management means executive management at the highest organizational level of a manufacturer, or at the level within a manufacturer that has been given the authority and responsibility for ensuring that quality system requirements are effectively established and maintained.

§820.3(p) — Manufacturing material
Manufacturing material means any material or substance used in or used to facilitate the manufacturing process, a concomitant constituent, or a byproduct constituent that is not intended by the manufacturer to be included in the finished device.

§820.3(q) — Nonconformity
Nonconformity means the nonfulfillment of a specified requirement.

§820.3(r) — Product
Product means components, manufacturing materials, in-process devices, finished devices, and returned devices.

§820.3(s) — Quality
Quality means the totality of features and characteristics that bear on the ability of a device to satisfy fitness-for-use, including safety and performance.

§820.3(t) — Quality audit
Quality audit means a systematic, independent examination of a manufacturer's quality system that is performed at defined intervals and at sufficient frequency to determine whether both quality system activities and the results of such activities comply with quality system procedures, that these procedures are implemented effectively, and that these procedures are suitable to achieve quality system objectives.

§820.3(u) — Quality policy
Quality policy means the overall intentions and direction of an organization with respect to quality, as established by management.

§820.3(v) — Quality system
Quality system means the organizational structure, responsibilities, procedures, processes, and resources for implementing quality management.

§820.3(w) — Remanufacturer
Remanufacturer means any person who processes, conditions, renovates, repackages, restores, or any other act done to a finished device that significantly changes the finished device's performance or safety specifications, or intended use.

§820.3(y) — Risk
Risk means the potential for harm [cross-reference: ISO 14971 — Medical devices — Application of risk management to medical devices].

§820.3(z) — Specification
Specification means any requirement with which a product, process, service, or other activity must conform.

§820.3(aa) — Validation
Validation means confirmation by examination and provision of objective evidence that the particular requirements for a specific intended use can be consistently fulfilled.

§820.3(aa)(1) — Process validation: establishing by objective evidence that a process consistently produces a result or product meeting its predetermined specifications.

§820.3(aa)(2) — Design validation: establishing by objective evidence that device specifications conform with user needs and intended use(s).

§820.3(z)(1) — Verification
Verification means confirmation by examination and provision of objective evidence that specified requirements have been fulfilled.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

重要陷阱：「Manufacturer」包含合約滅菌商與重新標示商

§820.3(o) 對「Manufacturer」的定義是 Part 820 最重要的陷阱之一。以下角色都被明確列為「Manufacturer」：

  • Contract Sterilizer（合約滅菌商）： 只接受委託進行 EO/輻射/蒸汽滅菌，不參與其他製造步驟，仍受 Part 820 約束
  • Relabeler（重新標示商）： 以自有品牌重新貼標，FDA 認為這是「製造活動」
  • Specification Developer（規格開發商）： 僅撰寫產品規格、外包所有製造，仍是「Manufacturer」，必須建立 QMS
  • Initial Distributor of Foreign Entities（外國實體的初始分銷商）： 在美國代理進口的分銷商，若外國製造商執行以上活動，美國端的初始分銷商也可能被視為製造商

實務含意： 若貴公司只負責合約滅菌，仍需建立至少涵蓋製程管制、記錄保存、CAPA 的 QMS。

Validation vs. Verification：法規區別

這是 FDA 查廠中最常混淆的兩個概念：

中文查驗 / 驗證確效 / 驗證確認

關鍵邏輯： Verification 確認「做對了」；Validation 確認「做的是對的東西」。

Risk：與 ISO 14971 的連結

§820.3(y) 將「Risk」定義為「潛在傷害（potential for harm）」，並交叉參照 ISO 14971（醫療器材風險管理）。這是 Part 820 唯一直接引用 ISO 標準的定義。

ISO 14971 完整定義： Risk = Probability of harm × Severity of that harm

QMSR 重要性： 在 QMSR 框架下，風險管理（ISO 14971）的整合更加深入。製造商必須在設計管制、CAPA、供應商管理等各環節展示基於風險的決策。

Quality vs. Quality System：兩個不同定義

Quality（品質）§820.3(s)： 以「適用性（fitness-for-use）」為核心，包含安全性和性能。這是「品質」的目標定義。

Quality System（品質系統）§820.3(v)： 是「組織架構 + 責任 + 程序 + 製程 + 資源」的總體，是實現品質的手段。

QMSR 將「Quality System」更名為「Quality Management System（QMS）」以對應 ISO 13485 術語，但概念架構相同。

1.4 DHF / DHR / DMR — 三大文件比較

原文內容 Original Text

The regulatory basis for the three core device documentation systems derives from §820.3:

  • Design History File (DHF) — §820.3(f): "A compilation of records which describes the design history of a finished device." [Maintained per §820.30(j)]
  • Device Master Record (DMR) — §820.3(k): "A compilation of records containing the procedures and specifications for a finished device." [Maintained per §820.181]
  • Device History Record (DHR) — §820.3(j): "A compilation of records containing the production history of a finished device." [Maintained per §820.184]

§820.181 — Device master record: Each manufacturer shall maintain device master records (DMR's). Each manufacturer shall ensure that each DMR is prepared and approved in accordance with §820.40. The DMR for each type of device shall include, or refer to the location of, the following information:

  • (a) Device specifications including appropriate drawings, composition, formulation, component specifications, and software specifications;
  • (b) Production process specifications including the appropriate equipment specifications, production methods, production procedures, and production environment specifications;
  • (c) Quality assurance procedures and specifications including acceptance criteria and the quality assurance equipment to be used;
  • (d) Packaging and labeling specifications, including methods and processes used; and
  • (e) Installation, maintenance, and servicing procedures and methods.

§820.184 — Device history record: Each manufacturer shall maintain device history records (DHR's). Each manufacturer shall establish and maintain procedures to ensure that DHR's for each batch, lot, or unit are maintained to demonstrate that the device is manufactured in accordance with the DMR and the requirements of this part. The DHR shall include, or refer to the location of, the following information:

  • (a) The dates of manufacture;
  • (b) The quantity manufactured;
  • (c) The quantity released for distribution;
  • (d) The acceptance records which demonstrate the device is manufactured in accordance with the DMR;
  • (e) The primary identification label and labeling used for each production unit; and
  • (f) Any unique device identifier (UDI) or universal product code (UPC), and any other device identification(s) and control number(s) used.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

最容易混淆的三對關係

DHF vs DMR：

DHF 是「過去式」——設計決策的歷程記錄，包含所有被否定的方案、設計審查會議紀錄、中間版本。DMR 是「現在式」——目前有效的製造規格，是控制性文件，需要版本管控和批准。

DHF 回答「為什麼這樣設計」；DMR 回答「應該怎麼製造」。

DMR vs DHR：

DMR 是「規定」；DHR 是「執行記錄」。DHR 必須能證明「實際製造符合 DMR 規定」。FDA 查廠時，DHR 不能引用一個 DMR 版本，但實際製造卻使用不同版本——這是嚴重缺陷。

DHF vs DHR：

DHF 在設計階段建立，原則上設計鎖定後不再增長（僅設計變更時更新）。DHR 在每次生產時新建，持續增加。

FDA 483 常見 DHF/DMR/DHR 缺陷

  • DHF 缺陷： 設計驗證記錄不完整；設計審查未有獨立人員參與；設計輸入未能可測量（vague requirements）
  • DMR 缺陷： 規格未更新以反映實際製程；交叉引用文件無法追溯；軟體規格缺失
  • DHR 缺陷： 批次記錄不完整；未記錄所有接受/拒絕決策；批次與 DMR 版本不對應

QMSR / ISO 13485 術語對照

QMSR 最終規則後，FDA 鼓勵使用 ISO 13485 術語，但傳統 Part 820 術語仍廣泛使用：

Part 820 傳統術語ISO 13485 對應術語
Design History File (DHF)Design and Development File
Device Master Record (DMR)Device Definition Documents
Device History Record (DHR)Production Records / Traceability Records
Quality System (QS)Quality Management System (QMS)
Corrective and Preventive Action (CAPA)Corrective Action + Preventive Action（分開）

業界通常繼續使用 DHF/DMR/DHR 等縮寫，FDA 查廠時兩種術語皆可接受，但文件系統內部應保持一致性。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. FDA 查廠官員要求查看你的 DHF。DHF 裡應該包含哪五類最重要的文件？若你的器材在設計過程中有三次重大設計變更，這些變更的記錄應在 DHF 的哪個部分？
  2. 某批次的 DHR 顯示，該批次使用了一個組件，其批次號碼無法在 DMR 核准的供應商清單中找到。這代表什麼問題？應如何啟動什麼流程？
  3. 若你公司的 DMR 更新了第三版（Rev C），但倉庫中仍有用 Rev A 製造的成品，這些成品的 DHR 應記錄哪個版本？這算不算不合格？
1.5 組合產品（Combination Products）— 適用範圍與定義

原文內容 Original Text

Definition — 21 CFR §3.2(e): Combination Product

Combination product means a product comprised of two or more regulated components, i.e., drug/device, biologic/device, drug/biologic, or drug/device/biologic, that are physically, chemically, or otherwise combined or mixed and produced as a single entity; or two or more separate products packaged together in a single package or as a unit and comprised of drug and device products, device and biological products, or biological and drug products; or a drug, device, or biological product packaged separately that according to its investigational plan or proposed labeling is intended for use only with an approved individually specified drug, device, or biological product where both are required to achieve the intended use, indication, or effect; or any investigational drug, device, or biological product packaged separately that according to its proposed labeling is for use only with another individually specified investigational drug, device, or biological product where both are required to achieve the intended use, indication, or effect.

21 CFR Part 4 — CGMP Obligations for Combination Products

§4.4(b) — Combination products with a device primary mode of action:
If the primary mode of action (PMOA) of a combination product is that of a device, the manufacturer must comply with 21 CFR Part 820 (device CGMP). In addition, if the combination product contains a drug constituent part, manufacturers must also comply with certain drug CGMP requirements under 21 CFR Part 211 as specified in §4.4(b).

§4.4(a) — Combination products with a drug primary mode of action:
If the primary mode of action is that of a drug, the manufacturer must comply with 21 CFR Parts 210 and 211 (drug CGMP). In addition, certain device quality system requirements from Part 820 apply, specifically the design controls, corrective and preventive action (CAPA), and complaint handling requirements.

Primary Mode of Action (PMOA) Determination:
FDA's Office of Combination Products (OCP) makes PMOA determinations. The PMOA is the single mode of action that provides the most important therapeutic action. When in doubt, manufacturers may submit a Request for Designation (RFD) to OCP under 21 CFR Part 3, Subpart B.

Cross-cutting Part 820 requirements that apply regardless of PMOA:

  • §820.30 — Design controls (when PMOA is drug or biologic)
  • §820.100 — Corrective and preventive action
  • §820.198 — Complaint files
  • §820.22 — Quality audit

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

什麼時候是「Device」？Part 820 適用的判斷邏輯

產品是否受 Part 820 管轄，取決於以下判斷鏈：

步驟 1： 是否為「Medical Device」（FD&C Act §201(h) 定義）？若是，繼續。

步驟 2： 是否為「Finished Device」（§820.3(m)）？若是，Part 820 全面適用。

步驟 3（組合產品）： 若含有藥品或生物製品成分，確認 PMOA：

  • PMOA = Device → 主要適用 Part 820，補充適用 Part 211
  • PMOA = Drug/Biologic → 主要適用 Part 211，補充適用 Part 820 部分條款

常見組合產品例子：預填充注射器（Device + Drug）、藥物洗脫支架（Device + Drug）、胰島素自動注射筆（Device + Drug）、CAR-T 細胞治療（Biologic + Device）。

CDMO 特別注意：組合產品的雙重合規義務

對合約製造廠（CDMO）而言，若客戶的產品是組合產品，需特別確認：

  • 客戶是否已取得 OCP 的 PMOA 決定？應要求客戶提供 RFD 結果
  • 若 PMOA 為 Drug，你的設施是否同時符合 Part 211 和 Part 820 相關條款（設計管制、CAPA、投訴）？
  • Part 820 + Part 211 雙重查廠：FDA 可能派遣 CDRH 和 CDER 兩個中心的查廠官員聯合查廠
  • 強化的批次記錄要求：需同時滿足 DHR（器材角度）和批次記錄（Batch Record，藥品角度）的資訊要求

Part 820 vs Part 211：關鍵差異對比

比較項目21 CFR Part 820（器材）21 CFR Part 211（藥品）
核心哲學設計管制 + 系統方法批次管控 + 測試確認

實務應用：CDMO 合約充填完成廠的 Part 820 義務

情境： 一家 CDMO 在台灣為客戶的藥物洗脫支架（drug-eluting stent，DES）提供藥物塗層和最終包裝服務。客戶已確認 DES 的 PMOA 為 Device（金屬支架提供主要治療功能）。

此 CDMO 在 Part 820 下的最低義務：

  • §820.1(a)(2) 部分活動原則： 只需符合其參與活動（塗層、包裝）的相關 Part 820 條款
  • DMR 管理： 必須持有或可存取與其工作相關的 DMR 章節（塗層規格、包裝規格）
  • DHR 建立： 必須為每批塗層/包裝操作建立 DHR，記錄：操作日期、批量、操作人員、使用的藥物批次號、塗層厚度量測記錄、包裝完整性測試記錄
  • 製程管制（§820.70）： 塗層製程必須有書面 SOP，並具備符合 DMR 規格的製程確效
  • 設備管制（§820.72）： 所有塗層和量測設備需有校驗計畫
  • 供應商管理（§820.50）： 藥物塗層材料的供應商必須在核准供應商清單中，並有品質協議
  • 投訴管理（§820.198）： 若 CDMO 收到任何關於其塗層工作的投訴，必須依 Part 820 的投訴處理程序處理

此 CDMO 同時需要的 Part 211 要求（因產品含藥品成分）：

  • 塗層藥物的組件測試和放行（§211.84）
  • 無菌製程管制（若塗層製程在無菌環境中）

實務建議： 此類 CDMO 通常建立一套「整合 QMS」，同時符合 ISO 13485（器材）和 ISO 9001 + GMP（藥品），並在品質協議中明確劃分客戶與 CDMO 的各自義務。

1.6 CAPA 相關定義：Corrective Action vs. Preventive Action

原文內容 Original Text

Corrective action (矯正措施):
Action taken to eliminate the cause of an existing nonconformity, defect, or other undesirable situation in order to prevent recurrence. [Referenced in §820.100(a); ISO 13485 §8.5.2]

Preventive action (預防措施):
Action taken to eliminate the cause of a potential nonconformity, defect, or other undesirable potential situation in order to prevent occurrence. [Referenced in §820.100(a); ISO 13485 §8.5.3]

§820.100(a) — CAPA Procedures: Each manufacturer shall establish and maintain procedures for implementing corrective and preventive action. The procedures shall include requirements for:

  • (1) Analyzing processes, work operations, concessions, quality audit reports, quality records, service records, complaints, returned product, and other sources of quality data to identify existing and potential causes of nonconforming product, or other quality problems;
  • (2) Investigating the cause of nonconformities relating to product, processes, and the quality system;
  • (3) Identifying the action(s) needed to correct and prevent recurrence of nonconforming product and other quality problems;
  • (4) Verifying or validating the corrective and preventive action to ensure that such action is effective and does not adversely affect the finished device;
  • (5) Implementing and recording changes in methods and procedures needed to correct and prevent identified quality problems;
  • (6) Ensuring that information related to quality problems or nonconforming product is disseminated to those directly responsible for assuring the quality of such product or the prevention of such problems; and
  • (7) Submitting relevant information on identified quality problems, as well as corrective and preventive actions, for management review.
"The CAPA system is the backbone of the quality system. Failure to maintain a CAPA system that adequately identifies and addresses systemic quality issues is one of the most common FDA 483 observations."
— FDA CDRH, Device CGMP Manual

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

Corrective Action vs. Preventive Action：核心差異

項目Corrective Action（矯正措施）Preventive Action（預防措施）
觸發條件問題「已發生」問題「尚未發生」但有風險
目的防止「再發（recurrence）」防止「首次發生（occurrence）」
數據來源不合格品、投訴、審核缺陷趨勢分析、風險評估、製程數據
ISO 13485§8.5.2§8.5.3
例子三批產品超出滅菌確效範圍 → 重新確效環境監控趨勢上升 → 預防性清潔程序更新

重要： 不要把「矯正（Correction）」和「矯正措施（Corrective Action）」混淆！「Correction」只是處理眼前的不合格品（例如報廢）；「Corrective Action」是消除根本原因（Root Cause）。

QMSR / ISO 13485 CAPA 架構變化

在 QMSR 框架（對應 ISO 13485）下，CAPA 的處理方式有一個重要的概念轉變：

  • ISO 13485 §8.5 將矯正措施和預防措施分開管理（不同於某些 QMS 將兩者合并）
  • ISO 13485 更強調「風險比例性（risk-proportionate response）」——低風險的 CA 可以有簡化的程序，高風險需要正式 CAPA
  • CAPA 結果必須回饋到管理審查（Management Review）

實務建議：建立「CAPA 分類矩陣」，依風險等級決定是否需要正式 CAPA vs. 快速矯正（Quick Fix）。

練習思考 Practice Questions

本章重點總結 Section Summary

  • §820.1 適用範圍： Part 820 適用於所有在美銷售的「Finished Device」製造商，無論製造地點。「Manufacturer」的定義極廣，包括合約滅菌商、規格開發商、重新標示商等——即使不直接製造，也可能受 Part 820 約束。第 I 類器材享有設計管制豁免（§820.30），但仍受其他 Part 820 條款約束。
  • 三大文件核心區別： DHF（設計歷程）= 「我們如何設計」；DMR（器材主記錄）= 「應如何製造」；DHR（器材歷史記錄）= 「實際上如何製造」。DHR 必須能與 DMR 版本對應，是 FDA 查廠最常檢查的項目之一。
  • 關鍵定義陷阱： Validation ≠ Verification。Validation 確認「做的是對的東西（符合使用者需求）」；Verification 確認「正確地做了（符合設計輸入規格）」。兩者都必須有客觀證據支持。
  • 組合產品雙重合規： 當 PMOA 為 Device 時，Part 820 為主要 CGMP，同時需補充 Part 211 的部分要求。CDMO 承接組合產品業務前，必須確認 OCP 的 PMOA 認定，並在品質協議中明確雙方義務。
  • QMSR 2024 過渡重點： Part 820 自 2026 年 2 月起與 ISO 13485:2016 整合對齊。傳統術語（DHF/DMR/DHR）在業界持續通用，但 FDA 查廠將更多參照 ISO 13485 的要求框架進行評估。建議廠商維護 Part 820 傳統術語與 ISO 13485 術語的對照清單。
