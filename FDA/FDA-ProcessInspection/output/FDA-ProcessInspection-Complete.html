📘 計畫識別解讀 — 查廠官員的「工作手冊」

什麼是 CPGM（Compliance Program Guidance Manual）？

CPGM 是 FDA 查廠官員（Investigator/Inspector）在執行查廠任務時所依循的內部工作指引。它不是對藥廠的直接規範，而是告訴 FDA 人員「要查什麼、怎麼查、怎麼報告」。

本計畫 7356.002A 是無菌藥品製程查廠的專屬 CPGM，搭配通用查廠計畫 7356.002 使用。

💡 比喻：CPGM 就像查廠官員的「標準作業程序（SOP）」

正如藥廠的 SOP 告訴員工如何操作設備，CPGM 告訴 FDA Investigator 如何系統性地評估藥廠是否符合 GMP。兩者的邏輯完全相同——程序標準化、結果可比較、文件可稽核。

⚠️ 重要：查廠代碼的意義

  • 56002A（Full Inspection）：完整查廠，覆蓋全部系統
  • 56002I（Abbreviated Inspection）：縮短查廠，選定系統查核
  • OAI（Official Action Indicated）：缺失嚴重，需採取正式監管行動
  • EIR（Establishment Inspection Report）：每次查廠後的正式書面記錄

關鍵術語

  • PAC：Program Assignment Code，計畫分配代碼，用於 FACTS 系統記錄查廠活動
  • FACTS：FDA 的查廠記錄資料庫系統
  • MARCS-CMS：FDA 監管行動管理系統
  • ADE：Adverse Drug Experience，藥物不良事件

Part I — Background 計畫背景

PART I — BACKGROUND

This program covers the manufacture and testing of all sterile drug products, including drugs that are:

  • Sterilized by filtration or other means and aseptically processed
  • Terminally sterilized

Products Covered

  • Sterile bulk drugs
  • Ophthalmic drugs
  • Otic dosage forms
  • Small volume parenterals (SVS) — small molecule and licensed biological therapeutic drug products
  • Large volume parenterals (LVP)
  • Any other drug products required to be sterile or labeled as sterile

Exclusions

  • Center for Biologics Evaluation and Research (CBER) regulated products
  • Veterinary drug products

The guidance information in this program is tailored to sterile manufacturing operations and should be used in conjunction with the Compliance Program for Drug Manufacturing Inspections (CP 7356.002).

Reference to 2004 Aseptic Processing Guidance

In 2004, FDA published the Guidance for Industry: Sterile Drug Products Produced by Aseptic Processing — Current Good Manufacturing Practice (referred to throughout as "FDA's 2004 Aseptic Processing Guidance").

"The Guidance for Industry does not establish mandatory requirements and should not be referred to as the justification for an inspectional observation. Justification for inspectional observations originates from the CGMP regulations, 21 CFR Parts 210 and 211."

Manufacturers who follow the 2004 Aseptic Processing Guidance are generally considered compliant with CGMP regulations. However, alternate approaches may be used if such approaches satisfy the requirements of 21 CFR Parts 210 and 211.

📘 背景解讀 — 適用範圍與法規層級

本計畫的雙重定位

本 CPGM 7356.002A 是無菌製程查廠的專門補充，必須與母計畫 CP 7356.002（一般藥品製造查廠）搭配使用。查廠時，兩份文件同時適用。

兩大製程的法規意義

FDA 認為無菌加工的污染風險高於最終滅菌，因此對其有更嚴格的查廠要求。

⚠️ 指引 vs 法規：關鍵區別

2004 Aseptic Processing Guidance（指引）不是法規強制要求。

查廠缺失的法律依據必須來自 21 CFR Part 210 或 21 CFR Part 211（法規強制）。

實務意涵：藥廠可以偏離指引，但必須用替代方法達到同等效果，且必須能向查廠官員說明替代方法如何符合 CFR 要求。

💡 CBER 為何排除在外？

CBER（生物製品評估與研究中心）負責監管疫苗、血液製品、基因療法等。這類產品有其專屬的查廠計畫。本 CPGM 7356.002A 僅涵蓋 CDER（藥物評估與研究中心）監管的無菌藥品，包括小分子藥物和授權生物治療藥品（licensed biologic therapeutics）。

🏭 對藥廠的實務啟示

  • 了解 FDA Investigator 的查廠邏輯，有助於 internal audit 的設計
  • 查廠官員的觀察依據是 21 CFR，不是 Guidance——藥廠答辯時同樣要引用 CFR
  • CBER 產品（如疫苗）適用不同的查廠計畫，不在本文件範圍

Part II — Implementation 執行架構

2.1 OBJECTIVES

The primary objective of this program is to provide guidance for conducting inspections of manufacturers of sterile bulk and sterile finished dosage drug products to determine compliance with the Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act and the CGMPs, Title 21, CFR Parts 210 and 211.

Other Objectives

  • Obtain information on operations impacting on sterility, to identify areas for improvement and correction.
  • Evaluate current good manufacturing practices in the sterile drug industry.
  • Initiate appropriate action against manufacturers observed to be out of compliance.

📘 目標解讀 — 查廠的三重目的

查廠目標的優先層次

  1. 合規評估：主要目標——確認廠商是否符合 21 CFR 210/211
  2. 資訊蒐集：識別可改善的製程缺失（不一定構成違規）
  3. 法規行動：對不合規廠商啟動正式監管措施（Warning Letter、進口警示等）

💡 查廠不只是「找缺失」

FDA 查廠的目的包含了解產業現況以制定政策，不僅僅是懲罰不合規廠商。這也是為什麼查廠官員即使在合規廠商也會詳細記錄製程——這些資訊有助於 FDA 了解產業趨勢。

2.2 PROGRAM MANAGEMENT INSTRUCTIONS

A. STRATEGY — (1) Inspection of Systems

Inspections should be made and reported using the system definitions in this compliance program. Focusing on systems increases inspection efficiency because the systems are often applicable to multiple profile classes.

Selection of system(s) for coverage is made by the District Office based on:

  • The firm's specific operation
  • Previous coverage
  • Compliance history
  • Other priorities determined by the District Office

The inspection should normally result in a determination of acceptability or non-acceptability for all profile classes. Coverage of a system should be sufficiently detailed so that the conclusion applies to all profile classes.

The determination that a system is adequately controlled for one profile class can be extended to another profile class even if not specifically reviewed.

A. STRATEGY — (2) Inspectional Scope

Inspections are performed as either:

  • Full Inspections (56002A)
  • Abbreviated Inspections (56002I)

Using the systems strategy outlined in CP 7356.002.

B. INSPECTION PLANNING

Implement this program when sterile drug manufacturers are inspected as part of a regular statutory inspection. CDER identifies firms for inspection based on a risk-based prioritization model aligned with annual performance goals.

Consider using a team approach when appropriate. Team members should:

  • Be very familiar with FDA's 2004 Guidance for Industry: Sterile Drug Products Produced by Aseptic Processing
  • Be well qualified in sterile product manufacturing (parenteral drug manufacture, aseptic technique, sterilization methods)
  • Include microbiologists with experience in sterility testing, endotoxin testing, and microbial controls

📘 策略解讀 — 系統式查廠的邏輯

六大系統查廠框架（Six Systems Framework）

FDA 採用系統式查廠（Systems-Based Inspection）而非逐項清單式查廠。本 CPGM 7356.002A 涵蓋以下六大系統（詳見 Part III）：

  1. 品質系統 Quality System（3.5）
  2. 廠房與設備系統 Facilities & Equipment（3.6）
  3. 物料系統 Materials System（3.7）
  4. 生產系統 Production System（3.8）
  5. 包裝與標籤系統 Packaging & Labeling（3.9）
  6. 實驗室控制系統 Laboratory Control System（3.10）

💡 系統式查廠的優勢

傳統逐項清單查廠容易流於表面；系統式查廠要求查廠官員從源頭理解整個系統的控制狀態。例如：查品質系統時，會評估 CAPA、偏差管理、變更管制是否相互連結，而不只是確認文件是否存在。

⚠️ 縮短查廠（Abbreviated Inspection）不等於寬鬆

Abbreviated Inspection 並非「走走看看」，而是針對特定系統進行深度查核。District Office 根據廠商合規歷史決定查核哪些系統——若有既往 OAI 記錄，缺失系統必然被重點查核。

風險優先查廠模型（Risk-Based Prioritization）

CDER 使用風險評分模型決定查廠優先順序，評估因素包括：

  • 查廠間隔時間（越久未查，風險越高）
  • 既往缺失記錄（OAI 歷史）
  • 產品對患者的重要性（essential medicines）
  • 製程複雜度（無菌加工 > 最終滅菌）

🏭 藥廠準備要點

  • 熟悉六大系統框架，按此架構設計 mock inspection
  • 查廠團隊應有微生物學家——確保廠內微生物相關系統（EM、APS、無菌測試）有專人管理
  • CDER 的風險模型偏向高頻查廠無菌製程，需保持持續性合規，而非「查前突擊準備」

📘 Profile Class 解讀 — 產品代碼與查廠差異

Profile Class 的重要性

Profile Class 代碼決定查廠的重點方向。製程類型不同，查廠要求也不同：

💡 生物製劑（SVS）的特殊性

蛋白質藥物（如抗體、胰島素）無法承受熱滅菌，必須採過濾除菌（0.22 μm 過濾）加上無菌加工。這也是生物製劑工廠必然面對更複雜無菌查廠要求的原因。

⚠️ 凍乾產品（SVL）的雙重挑戰

SVL 產品（凍乾注射劑）需要通過兩個高風險步驟：

  1. 無菌填充到小瓶（Aseptic Filling）
  2. 凍乾機內的塞瓶操作（In-Lyophilizer Stoppering）

任何一個步驟出現問題都可能導致整批產品無菌性失敗，是 FDA 查廠時的高風險焦點。

  • 查廠官員會根據 Profile Class 調整查廠重點，廠方應按此規劃 quality system 文件

3.1 Types of Sterilization 滅菌種類

3.1 TYPES OF STERILIZATION

There are two broad methods to produce a sterile drug product:

  • Terminal Sterilization
  • Aseptic Processing of sterilized unit components

Terminal sterilization should be utilized when the product and container/closure system are able to withstand the terminal sterilization process.

A. Terminal Sterilization

The terminal sterilization process usually involves filling and sealing product containers under high quality environmental conditions designed to minimize microbial and particulate contamination. In most cases, the product, container, and closure have low bioburden, but are not sterile at the time of filling. The product is then subjected to a sterilization process in its final container.

Methods of terminal sterilization:

  • Moist Heat Sterilization
  • Irradiation
  • Ethylene Oxide (typically for assembled components/kits)

Types of sterilization cycles:

  1. Overkill method:
    • Generally used for heat stable materials
    • Designed to provide significant sterility assurance regardless of bioburden number and resistance
    • Results in greater heat/exposure input to the product
  2. Bioburden Based cycle:
    • Requires studies to determine number and resistance of microorganisms in product and incoming components
    • Cycle developed to destroy microbial load without degrading product
    • Requires routine bioburden monitoring of batches and ongoing knowledge of heat resistance of organisms

Reference: PDA Technical Report No. 1 (Revised 2007) — Validation of Moist Heat Sterilization Processes.

B. Aseptic Processing

Aseptic processing presents a higher risk of microbial contamination than terminal sterilization. In an aseptic filling process, the drug product, containers, and closures are sterilized separately and then brought together under an extremely high quality environmental condition.

Aseptic processing involves more variables than terminal sterilization. Any manual or mechanical manipulation of the sterilized drug, containers, or closures poses the risk of microbial contamination.

Technologies reducing human intervention in critical areas:

  • RABS (Restricted Access Barrier Systems)
  • Blow-Fill-Seal systems
  • Isolators — completely separate the aseptic filling line from the external environment
"For terminal sterilized products that are aseptically filled prior to terminal sterilization, less rigorous aseptic controls can be considered."

📘 滅菌種類解讀 — FDA 查廠的風險優先邏輯

💡 FDA 的查廠重點選擇邏輯

若廠商同時有多條生產線，查廠官員優先選擇：

  • 無菌加工線中最多人工干預的那條
  • 最終滅菌產品中使用生物負荷循環（較敏感）的那個

這體現了 FDA 的核心原則：選最高風險的情境作為評估基準。

⚠️ 人為因素是無菌查廠重點

人員是傳統無菌加工線中最大的污染來源，尤其當操作人員須進入 Grade A（ISO 5 / Class 100）區域時。

RABS、Isolator 技術的核心價值就是物理隔絕人員與無菌區域，大幅降低人為污染風險——這也是為何使用這些技術的廠商可能符合縮短查廠（Abbreviated Inspection）條件。

關鍵技術術語

  • RABS：Restricted Access Barrier System，限制進入屏障系統——半封閉，仍允許有限干預
  • Isolator：完全封閉的隔離器系統——操作人員透過手套箱操作，無直接接觸
  • Blow-Fill-Seal (BFS)：吹瓶-填充-封口一體化系統，全程在封閉環境完成
  • Overkill：過殺法——通常目標 SAL ≤ 10⁻⁶，且 F₀ ≥ 12 min
  • Bioburden：生物負荷——產品及組件滅菌前的微生物數量

🏭 對藥廠的實務啟示

  • 若產品兼具最終滅菌可行性，應優先採用最終滅菌（FDA 明確表示此為優先選擇）
  • 採用 Bioburden-Based 循環的廠商必須有完善的生物負荷監控程序，查廠時必然被重點審查
  • 導入 Isolator 或封閉 RABS 是取得 Abbreviated Inspection 資格的重要前提之一

📘 報告規範解讀 — 藥廠如何理解 EIR

EIR（Establishment Inspection Report）的結構邏輯

EIR 按六大系統組織，意味著查廠官員的觀察與記錄都以系統為單位。FDA-483（觀察表）是查廠現場發現缺失的初步文件，EIR 是事後整理的完整書面記錄。

重要：即使沒有發出 FDA-483，查廠官員仍需提交 EIR——這份文件是 FDA 未來查廠決策的重要依據。

⚠️ EIR 的公開性

EIR 可透過 FOIA（資訊自由法）申請取得，是業界進行競爭情報分析的重要來源。廠商應定期閱讀同業的 EIR，了解 FDA 的查廠趨勢與關注焦點。

3.3 Inspection Approaches 查廠方法

3.3 INSPECTION APPROACHES

Related Compliance Programs:

  • CP 7356.002C — Radioactive Drugs (supplementary for radioactive sterile drugs)
  • CP 7356.002P — PET Drugs (specific for Positron Emission Tomography drugs; this program does NOT apply)
  • CP 7356.002M — Licensed Biological Therapeutic Drug Products (use in conjunction)

Full Inspections (PAC 56002A)

Provide a comprehensive evaluation of CGMP compliance. Normally includes at least four systems, one of which must be the Quality System. For sterile drugs, must also include Facilities & Equipment and Production Systems.

Full Inspections are conducted:

  • Initial inspection of a drug firm
  • First inspection after a Warning Letter, regulatory action, or significant FDA-483 findings
  • When Abbreviated Inspection reveals significant deficiencies
  • Significant changes in firm's operations since last inspection
  • History of recurring violations, fluctuating compliance
  • Information from samples, complaints, Field Alerts, recalls questions product quality

Abbreviated Inspections (PAC 56002I)

May be appropriate if BOTH conditions are satisfied:

  1. The firm has implemented a formal risk management program including:
    • Modern separation and automation approach (isolators, closed RABS)
    • Upstream bioburden controls
    • Robust quality system responsive to potential hazards
    • Formal lifecycle quality risk management aligned with ICH Q9
  2. The firm has sustained acceptable compliance history AND one of:
    • Produces terminally sterilized products using robust sterilization methods; OR
    • Has implemented robust risk management with daily assurance through design and control
    AND at inspection start:
    • Review of media fills, sterility testing, recalls, complaints reveals no sterility failure of distributed batches
    • Record of satisfactory CGMP compliance (e.g., two consecutive NAI or ≤ one VAI), with no Class 1 recalls

Abbreviated Inspection — Critical Elements by System

Facilities and Equipment:

  • Cleaning and disinfection
  • Facility/equipment layout and air handling for preventing viable and non-viable contamination
  • Material flow
  • Quality control of classified areas (air pressure balance, HEPA filtration)
  • Trending data supporting adequacy of clean room quality
  • Documented investigation into discrepancy

Materials:

  • Microbial and endotoxin control of incoming materials and components
  • Quality of water supply, maintenance, qualification
  • Operation of water and process gas systems
  • Documented investigations into OOS, deviations, discrepancies

Production:

  • Observation of operator behavior and aseptic techniques during manufacturing
  • Production line operations and interventions
  • Personnel training in aseptic techniques
  • Major production line repair or maintenance issues
  • Risk assessment on microbial and endotoxin controls, including hold times of critical steps
  • Validation of sterilization of equipment, container-closures and supplies
  • Media fills design and results
  • Documented investigations into deviations, discrepancies, and OOS results

Laboratory:

  • Investigations into OOS, deviations, discrepancies
  • Test methods and controls, including adherence to validated methods
  • Training and qualifications of laboratory personnel
  • Trending of water system test results
  • Systems for recovery, identification and trending of environmental monitoring isolates

📘 查廠方法解讀 — Full vs Abbreviated 策略

Full Inspection 的三個必查系統

無菌藥品 Full Inspection 在 CP 7356.002 要求「至少四系統」的基礎上，強制要求包含：

  1. 品質系統（Quality System）— 所有查廠必查
  2. 廠房設備系統（Facilities & Equipment）— 無菌製程特有強制
  3. 生產系統（Production System）— 無菌製程特有強制

加上其他至少一個系統，共四個或以上。

Abbreviated Inspection 資格的雙重門檻

必須同時滿足兩個條件：

條件一（製程設計）：

  • ✅ 使用 Isolator 或封閉 RABS
  • ✅ 有上游生物負荷控制
  • ✅ 品質系統對潛在風險有回應能力
  • ✅ 符合 ICH Q9 的生命週期風險管理

條件二（合規歷史）：

  • ✅ 連續兩次 NAI 或不超過一次 VAI
  • ✅ 無 Class 1 recall
  • ✅ 無已上市批次的無菌失敗記錄
  • ✅（最終滅菌廠商優先符合）

💡 Full vs Abbreviated 的本質差異

Abbreviated Inspection 不是「查少一點」，而是針對無菌保障關鍵指標的精準深查。兩者的差異：

⚠️ NAI / VAI / OAI 的定義

  • NAI（No Action Indicated）：無缺失，無需行動——最佳結果
  • VAI（Voluntary Action Indicated）：有缺失，廠商自行改善——可接受
  • OAI（Official Action Indicated）：重大缺失，需 FDA 正式行動——最差結果，下次必為 Full Inspection

Abbreviated Inspection 各系統的重點提示

縮短查廠的重點落在微生物控制數據：

  • 廠房設備：HEPA 完整性、氣壓差趨勢、消毒效果
  • 物料：進廠物料的內毒素（ET）與微生物限度測試
  • 生產：APS（Media Fill）結果、操作人員無菌技術現場觀察
  • 實驗室：EM 菌株鑑別與趨勢分析、OOS 調查品質

🏭 取得 Abbreviated Inspection 資格的策略路徑

  1. 技術升級：評估導入 Isolator 或封閉 RABS 的可行性（長期投資，降低查廠強度）
  2. 合規記錄維持：確保連續兩次 NAI 或最多一次 VAI（持續性合規，非突擊準備）
  3. ICH Q9 對齊：建立正式的生命週期風險管理計畫，包含 Risk Assessment 文件化
  4. APS 設計強化：Media Fill 計畫需覆蓋最壞情境（Worst Case），結果需完全通過
  5. 零 Class 1 Recall：強化上市後監控（Post-Market Surveillance），早期發現問題

📌 本節重點摘要 Section Summary — 3.1–3.3

  • 製程優先順序：FDA 明確偏好最終滅菌（風險較低），若產品可耐受應優先採用
  • Overkill vs Bioburden：Bioburden-Based 循環需更嚴格的生物負荷監控，查廠焦點更複雜
  • 人員污染：無菌加工中人員是最大污染源；Isolator/RABS 技術可有效降低風險並符合縮短查廠條件
  • Full Inspection：無菌廠必查品質系統 + 廠房設備 + 生產系統（至少三系統，加上其他至少一個）
  • Abbreviated Inspection 雙重門檻：（1）先進技術 + ICH Q9 風險管理；（2）持續良好合規紀錄（≥2 NAI 或 ≤1 VAI，無 Class 1 recall，無無菌失敗）
  • 縮短查廠重點：媒體填充（APS）結果、EM 趨勢、OOS 調查品質、人員技術現場觀察
  • EIR 按系統組織：了解系統式查廠邏輯有助於規劃 mock inspection 和 internal audit

3.5 Quality System 品質系統

3.5 QUALITY SYSTEM

The inspection of the quality system is two-phased:

  1. Phase 1: Evaluate whether the Quality Unit has fulfilled responsibility regarding review and approval of procedures and assured their suitability for use.
  2. Phase 2: Assess the data collected by the firm to identify potential quality problems — involves large amounts of data linking to other inspectional systems.

The comprehensive review of data by the firm is essential to assuring products are produced with a high degree of sterility assurance. Data summaries and trend reports maintained by the Quality Unit should be reviewed during every inspection. During a routine CGMP inspection, this review can help determine which option (Full or Abbreviated) to select.

Records Pertaining to Quality — Four Categories:

1. Product Quality Indicators:

  • Periodic product evaluations, complaints, adverse events, investigations
  • Field Alert Reports (FARs)
  • Product retain evaluations, rejected lots, stability, returned goods
  • Indicators of possible contamination: hazy/cloudy product, foreign particulates in injectables, cracked/leaky containers

2. Discrepancy and Failure Investigations:

  • All initial positive sterility tests and endotoxin failures — regardless of final disposition
  • All media fill failures — regardless of final disposition
  • Unexpected results or trends
  • All failures during validation or revalidation of sterilization/depyrogenation processes
  • All investigations involving media fills/process simulations
  • EM (microbial/viable and particle/non-viable) results exceeding alert or action levels
  • Process deviations or equipment malfunctions of critical equipment (sterilizers, lyophilizers)
  • OOS results for assay, impurities, particulate matter, or reconstitution time
  • Product rejects

3. Trend Reports / Quality Indicator Summaries:

  • For aseptic processing: summary of all media fills since last inspection
  • Environmental monitoring trend data (microbial and particle counts)
  • Personnel monitoring trend data
  • Summary of water system test results

4. Change Controls (since last inspection):

  • Sterilizers, lyophilizers, depyrogenation equipment
  • Aseptic processing line
  • Clean steam generator, process gas system
  • WFI and/or Purified Water system
  • Air handling systems (HVAC)
  • Automated building management system

📘 品質系統查廠解讀 — 數據驅動的兩階段評估

兩階段評估的深層邏輯

FDA 近年查廠更強調 Phase 2——有 SOP 但沒有有效的數據分析系統，仍屬重大缺失。

⚠️ 最關鍵原則：「無論最終判定如何」都要調查

FDA 明確要求：所有初次陽性無菌試驗（initial positive sterility test）、內毒素失敗和培養基模擬試驗失敗（media fill failure）必須調查記錄，無論最終批次是否放行。

這意味著即使實驗室最終判定為實驗室錯誤（Lab Error）並放行批次，該初次失敗仍需完整調查文件化。查廠時，未記錄的初次失敗是嚴重缺失。

💡 品質系統查廠的「偵探邏輯」

FDA 查廠官員用品質系統數據來決定接下來要深查哪個生產系統。若 EM 趨勢顯示 Grade B 區域有持續超過 Alert Level 的情況，查廠官員就會深查廠房設備系統和生產系統。這是數據驅動的查廠策略。

🏭 品質系統查廠準備要點

  • 準備完整的 Media Fill 摘要：包含上次查廠以來所有 APS 的批次、設計、結果
  • EM 趨勢報告：依區域、菌種分類，顯示趨勢線，說明 Alert/Action Level 設定依據
  • 初次失敗調查清單：所有初次陽性無菌試驗記錄，包含調查結論和處置
  • 關鍵設備變更清單：Sterilizer、WFI、HVAC 的所有變更及驗證文件
  • 水系統趨勢報告：WFI、PW 的 TOC、電導度、微生物計數趨勢

3.6 Facilities and Equipment System 廠房與設備系統

3.6 FACILITIES AND EQUIPMENT SYSTEM

The principle objective is to provide suitable protection of product. Evaluation is two-part:

  1. Review and evaluate the firm's rationale for and adequacy of facility and equipment design
  2. Evaluate the data relevant to the state of control of facility and equipment

Investigators should also look for visible deficiencies: cleanliness, equipment deterioration (warping, corrosion), inaccessible surfaces, unqualified changes to critical equipment.

A. Facilities — Clean Room Design

  • Evaluate design and layout (personnel/material flow, cleanroom design)
  • Specifications for air filtration, air classification, pressure differentials, temperature, humidity must be appropriate and risk-based
  • Review certification and qualification data: air flow pattern studies, HEPA filter integrity, air velocity, non-viable particle counts, pressure differentials, temperature/humidity
  • Evaluate airflow pattern (smoke studies) under dynamic conditions to verify unidirectional airflow in critical areas

Ongoing monitoring requirements:

  • Special attention to construction near cleanrooms (fungal spores risk)
  • Non-viable particle monitoring during operations at highest-risk sites
  • Pressure differentials, temperature, humidity monitored continuously during routine production
  • Continuous monitoring systems alarmed to alert operators of excursions
  • Excursions from acceptable ranges investigated for product impact
  • Periodic HEPA filter recertification (integrity testing + air velocity checks)

Sanitization/disinfection:

  • Review suitability, efficacy, and limitations of disinfecting agents
  • Review data establishing expiry of disinfection solutions
  • For multi-use facilities: evaluate changeover procedures and cleaning to prevent cross-contamination

📘 廠房系統解讀 — 動態確認是核心

「設計合理性 + 控制數據」的雙層評估

FDA 不只看「廠房設計是否符合規格」，更要看「設計是否有風險科學依據」，以及「日常運行數據是否顯示系統處於受控狀態」。

⚠️ 動態條件下的煙霧測試（Smoke Study）

FDA 要求煙霧測試必須在動態條件（Dynamic Conditions）下進行——即有人員、設備運作、正常操作的情況下，而非靜態空調房間。

靜態條件的煙霧測試不足以代表實際操作時的氣流狀況。動態測試的目的是確認：在最壞操作情境下，Grade A 區域的單向氣流是否仍能有效保護暴露產品。

💡 廠房施工期間的特別注意

靠近潔淨室的施工活動（甚至翻牆、鑽孔）可能釋放大量真菌孢子。FDA 要求：施工結束後，必須通過適當的環境監控和 Media Fill 確認潔淨室恢復到可接受狀態，才能恢復生產。

3.6B Equipment — Key Equipment Inspection Points 設備查廠重點

B. Equipment — Specific Considerations

(e) Sterilizers（高壓滅菌器 / 乾熱設備）

  • Review IQ, OQ, PQ of autoclaves, dry heat ovens/tunnels, SIP systems, chemical sterilization (H₂O₂, peracetic acid)
  • Physical examination of equipment; review engineering specs / DQ
  • Calibration, maintenance, drainage, measuring devices (temperature sensors, pressure gauges)
  • Unplanned and preventative maintenance records reviewed
  • Repeat sterilizations due to cycle failures indicate serious problems — impact on product quality must be evaluated
  • Computer-controlled systems (PLC, SCADA) may require 21 CFR Part 11 compliance assessment

(f) Lyophilizer（凍乾機）

  • Partially sealed vials are exposed from filling until stoppering — must be transported and loaded into lyophilizers under Class 100 (ISO 5) protection
  • Investigators should observe transport of vials and loading
  • Key areas: sterilization validation of the lyophilization chamber between uses, current sterilization controls, leak testing of chamber, integrity testing of air/gas filters, calibration of temperature and pressure controllers

(g) Isolators（隔離器）

  • Evaluate design and control elements: pressure differential, glove integrity, transfer port protection
  • Transfer of containers, closures, supplies into isolator must be carefully controlled
  • Current decontamination methods (vaporized H₂O₂, steam H₂O₂, peracetic acid) capable of surface sterilization but lack penetrating capability of steam
  • Decontamination validation must demonstrate a minimum 6-log reduction of the biological indicator (BI)
  • BI placement must consider worst-case locations and obstructed surfaces
  • Product contact surfaces inside the isolator must be sterilized to render free of microorganisms (minimum 6-log BI reduction)

(h) RABS（限制進入屏障系統）

  • Rigid wall enclosure providing full physical separation of filling line from operators
  • Inside surfaces disinfected with sporicidal agent (not automated decontamination cycle as in isolators)
  • Two types: Open RABS (doors may open in pre-defined circumstances) vs Closed RABS (doors never opened during operation)
  • Surrounding cleanroom typically Class 10,000 (ISO 7)

RABS inspection checklist:

  • Gloves and gauntlets are sterile when installed; disinfected or changed at appropriate intervals
  • Written procedures for open-door interventions; all interventions documented in batch records and followed by disinfection
  • RABS entry documented with line clearance in batch records
  • All fluid pathways and product contact parts sterilized prior to each batch
  • Transfer system prevents exposure of sterile surfaces to less clean environments
  • Non-product contact surfaces undergo thorough sporicidal disinfection before each batch — effectiveness validated and routinely evaluated by EM program

(i) Blow-Fill-Seal (BFS)

  • Automated: containers formed, filled, sealed in continuous operation
  • Typical uses: sterile ophthalmic and respiratory care products
  • Air quality at exposure points must meet Class 100 (ISO 5) microbiological level
  • Advanced BFS equipment may locate in Class 100,000 (ISO 8); otherwise Class 10,000 (ISO 7)

BFS inspection checklist:

  • HEPA-filtered or sterile air used during all exposure steps (parison formation, molding, filling)
  • Monitoring and PM programs for utility integrity (cooling water, heating) — leaks can contaminate product
  • SIP system for product line: validated cycle, condensate properly drained, line protected between sterilization and use
  • Personnel properly gowned and trained for classified environment

(j) Reactor / Centrifuge / Dryer / Mill (Sterile Bulk API)

  • Equipment and all transfer piping sterilized prior to processing (SIP with clean steam or chemical sterilant)
  • Must remain integral (no leaks) and sterile throughout entire manufacturing process
  • Any open operation (e.g., adding seed crystals) requires Class 100 (ISO 5) air protection and carefully designed aseptic technique

(2) Container/Closure Processing Equipment

  • Depyrogenation equipment: dry heat oven and/or depyrogenation tunnel; stoppers may be depyrogenated by WFI washing dilution

(3) Support Utilities

  • Water System: Review WFI generation equipment and distribution loop (tanks, lines, isometric diagrams, vent filters, PM schedules)
  • HVAC: Refer to Section IV of FDA's 2004 Aseptic Processing Guidance

📘 設備查廠解讀 — 各設備的核心風險

Sterilizer — 重複滅菌是危險信號

FDA 特別指出：因循環失敗而重複滅菌（repeat sterilization）可能表示設備有嚴重問題。查廠官員會：

  • 審查設備日誌中的循環失敗記錄
  • 評估重複滅菌對產品品質的影響
  • 確認相關調查和 CAPA

藥廠應建立循環失敗 = 偏差調查的自動觸發機制。

⚠️ Lyophilizer — 最危險的轉移步驟

凍乾瓶（半塞狀態）從填充線轉移到凍乾機這段時間是風險最高的暴露期。FDA 查廠官員會實際觀察這個操作，確認：

  • 轉移過程全程在 ISO 5 保護下
  • 凍乾機裝載作業的人員技術和 SOP 執行
  • 凍乾機腔室在兩批之間的滅菌驗證

Isolator 去污驗證要求（6-log BI）

要求：隔離器去污程序必須達到最低 6-log（百萬倍）生物指示劑（BI）殺滅率。

挑戰點：H₂O₂ 蒸氣等表面殺菌劑無法穿透被遮蔽或保護的表面（如螺絲縫隙、管道連接處）。驗證時必須在最壞情況位置放置 BI。

實務要求：

  • 量化測量裝置（如近紅外光）或化學指示劑確認最壞位置
  • 產品接觸表面需滅菌至無任何微生物（不僅僅是去污）

⚠️ Open RABS 的陷阱：開門即失去 RABS 定義

FDA 明確指出：若門在填充作業中例行性開啟，該系統不再被視為 RABS（因為不再有效限制進入關鍵區域）。每次開門干預都必須：

  • 有書面程序定義允許的干預情境
  • 記錄於批次記錄
  • 干預後執行消毒

BFS — 空氣品質的直接關係

研究顯示 BFS 污染單位數與周圍空氣微生物濃度有直接相關。因此：

  • 產品暴露步驟（parison 形成、模具成型、填充）必須有 ISO 5 空氣品質
  • 先進 BFS 設備可在 ISO 8 環境（降低廠房等級要求，節省成本）
  • 產品供給管線通常採用 SIP（蒸氣就地滅菌），必須驗證且冷凝水需正確排放

🏭 廠房設備系統查廠準備清單

  • 潔淨室認證記錄：最近一次 HEPA 完整性測試、非活性粒子計數、壓差驗證
  • 煙霧測試記錄：確認在動態條件下執行；保存影像記錄
  • 消毒驗證：消毒劑有效期數據、輪替消毒劑的 VPHP/孢子殺菌劑效果驗證
  • Isolator/RABS 去污記錄：6-log BI 驗證資料、每批去污程序記錄
  • 凍乾機裝載 SOP：確認有 ISO 5 保護記錄，操作人員訓練記錄
  • 設備日誌：所有循環失敗記錄及調查文件
  • 水系統等距圖（Isometric Drawing）：需為最新版本

📌 本節重點摘要 Section Summary — 3.4–3.6

  • 品質系統兩階段：Phase 1（程序審查）+ Phase 2（數據趨勢評估）——後者是現代查廠的核心
  • 必查記錄四類：產品投訴/ADE → 偏差/失敗調查 → EM/Media Fill 趨勢 → 關鍵設備變更控制
  • 初次失敗必查：無菌試驗初次陽性、Media Fill 失敗、內毒素失敗，無論最終判定均需調查文件
  • 廠房雙層評估：設計合理性（有科學依據） + 控制數據（日常監控有效）
  • 動態煙霧測試：必須在生產操作條件下執行，靜態不足
  • Isolator 6-log：去污驗證需達生物指示劑 6-log 殺滅率；注意穿透盲點
  • RABS 開門原則：例行開門 = 失去 RABS 定義；每次干預必須記錄、消毒
  • Lyophilizer 轉移：半塞瓶轉移至凍乾機全程需 ISO 5 保護；查廠官員可能觀察操作
  • BFS 空氣品質：所有暴露步驟需 ISO 5 空氣；空氣微生物濃度與污染率直接相關

  • 了解 100% 目視檢查（Visual Inspection）的查廠關注焦點與人員資格要求
  • 識別參數放行（Parametric Release）的適用條件與法規依據

3.7 Materials System 物料系統

3.7 MATERIALS SYSTEM

In sterile operations, the quality attributes of each material (ingredients, WFI, containers, closures) have a bearing on the critical attributes of the finished product. Review procedures for receipt, handling, sampling, testing, approval and storage of manufacturing materials. Emphasis on incoming materials represented to be sterile and/or pyrogen free.

(1) Water Systems — WFI

WFI is an ingredient in many sterile drugs and also used for depyrogenation of equipment and stoppers, and in cleaning. Purified water can be used for some sterile non-injectable solutions.

  • Observe and understand generation and distribution systems
  • Evaluate "As Built" diagrams; inspect for leaks, pipe slopes, dead legs, non-sanitary fittings
  • Evaluate how microbial alert and action levels are established
  • Evaluate sampling sites, procedures, frequencies and tests
  • Review PM and calibration of critical instruments
  • Review raw data to verify all above is completed per procedure
  • Review and observe routine in-line monitoring (TOC and conductivity)
  • Review trend data for chemistry, microbiological and endotoxin tests
  • Review investigation of results at or over alert and action levels

(2) Process Gas

  • Gases used in aseptic operations or downstream of sterilization must be filtered through a sterilizing grade filter
  • Evaluate integrity testing of hydrophobic filters
  • Controls over final filtration and filter integrity testing
  • Nitrogen gas overlay for oxygen-sensitive products: gas used as component in finished product

(3) Pre-washed / Ready-to-Sterilize Closures

Many SVP manufacturers purchase stoppers that are ready-to-sterilize (pyrogen-free). Even if no washing/depyrogenation is done in-house, the manufacturer is still responsible for assuring acceptable quality.

  • Pyrogen requirements must be in stopper specifications
  • If not testing each incoming lot, must establish supplier reliability via vendor qualification + periodic testing

Reference: 21 CFR 211.94(c) — containers/closures must be processed to remove pyrogenic substances where indicated.

(4) Microbiological and Endotoxin Testing of Components

21 CFR 211.84(d): Each lot liable to microbial contamination shall be subjected to microbiological tests before use.

  • Evaluate the firm's system for determining if micro/endotoxin testing is required
  • Review rationale for setting acceptance criteria
  • Verify materials meet test criteria; if not, investigations and corrective actions implemented

(5) Verification of Containers and Closures

Physical and chemical characteristics can be critical to sterility and stability. Many containers/closures look alike but have different materials or surface treatments (silicone on stoppers, ammonium sulfate on Type I glass).

  • Evaluate procedures for assuring correct materials, correct dimensions (critical to container-closure integrity), free of critical defects

(6) Container / Closure Integrity (CCI)

Integrity of the CCS is critical to assuring units remain sterile through shipment, storage and use.

  • Verify all incoming container-closure components meet specifications including all appropriate dimensions
  • Determine studies adequately simulate stress conditions of sterilization process, handling and storage
  • Verify units tested in validation are appropriate (for terminally sterilized products: exposed to maximum sterilization cycles)
  • Sensitivity of the test is specified
  • Container-closure integrity demonstrated during validation and as part of the stability program (in lieu of sterility testing) over shelf life

📘 物料系統解讀 — 無菌保障從物料開始

⚠️ 採購進廠的預洗塞——藥廠仍需負責

即使向廠商購買「Ready-to-Sterilize」（預清洗無熱原）的橡皮塞，藥廠仍須：

  1. 在規格書中納入熱原要求
  2. 若不每批次自行測試，須通過廠商資格確認（Vendor Qualification）建立對廠商測試結果的信任基礎
  3. 定期進行驗收測試以確認廠商持續合規

這是查廠時常被忽視的責任歸屬問題——委外不等於免責。

CCI 取代無菌測試的法規依據

FDA 允許在產品穩定性計畫中以容器密封完整性測試（CCI）取代無菌測試。前提：

  • CCI 方法需在驗證研究中確認靈敏度
  • 測試需涵蓋最大滅菌循環次數的壓力條件
  • 需覆蓋整個產品儲存期

查廠時 FDA 會確認：驗證設計是否真正模擬了最壞情況（包含滅菌、運輸、儲存應力）。

💡 製程氣體的「隱形風險」

氮氣覆蓋（Nitrogen Overlay）是氧敏感產品的保護措施，但若氮氣未通過除菌過濾，反而成為微生物污染途徑。查廠官員會確認：所有在滅菌下游或無菌操作中接觸產品的氣體，是否通過疏水性除菌過濾（0.22 μm），並有完整性測試記錄。

🏭 物料系統查廠準備要點

  • WFI 等距圖：確保為最新版本，標示所有採樣點、死角長度、坡度
  • WFI/PW 趨勢報告：TOC、電導度、微生物、內毒素，含 Alert/Action Level 及超標調查
  • 廠商資格確認：預洗塞廠商的 QA Agreement 和定期驗收測試記錄
  • CCI 驗證資料：方法靈敏度、最壞情況設計、穩定性 CCI 數據
  • 製程氣體過濾器：完整性測試記錄，包含每批使用後的測試

3.8 Production System 生產系統

3.8 PRODUCTION SYSTEM — Overview

Production practices and conditions can have a direct and significant adverse impact on drug sterility assurance. The Production System should be included in all Full Inspections. Coverage of critical elements should be part of all inspections (Full and Abbreviated) of aseptically processed sterile drug manufacturers.

Critical observations during inspection:

  • Adequacy of aseptic technique
  • Personnel behavior and practices in the cleanroom
  • Movement of people and materials before and during aseptic operation
  • Robustness of production process design
  • Disinfection practices

Higher-risk operations requiring real-time observation:

  • Set-up of filling lines (especially powder filling lines and lines requiring multiple aseptic assemblies or non-SIP product pathways)
  • Cleaning and disinfection of line and room — all difficult-to-access surfaces
  • Protection of critical contact surfaces throughout operations and post-sterilization
  • Aseptic technique during operations, including handling of equipment jams and stoppages
  • Personnel flow and material flow in relation to microbial control
  • Filling operations: gowning technique, gown integrity, adherence to SOPs, nature and frequency of interventions
  • Atypical interventions associated with unplanned events (e.g., operator changes filling pump mid-operation)
  • Extra manipulations for assembly of sterile filtration apparatus not SIP-sterilized
  • Handling of partially stoppered vials in lyophilization — all steps under Class 100
  • Environmental monitoring — observation of actual monitoring operations and sample site rationale
  • Proper stopper seating and capping performed under unidirectional flow

📘 生產系統解讀 — 現場觀察是核心

為什麼「現場觀察」不可取代？

FDA 強調生產系統查廠必須包含製程的即時觀察（Real-Time Observation）。原因：

  • 許多無菌操作缺失只在「現場進行時」才看得到（如操作人員跨越無菌區、設備卡住時的不當處置）
  • SOP 寫得完美，但實際執行可能偏離
  • 文件查核只能看到「應該怎麼做」，觀察才能看到「實際怎麼做」

⚠️ 最高風險操作：干預（Interventions）

FDA 查廠官員會特別關注非計畫性干預（如設備卡塞、操作員臨時更換零件）。這類事件：

  • 必須記錄在批次記錄中
  • 應在 Media Fill 中模擬（作為 Worst Case Interventions）
  • 需要評估對無菌性的影響

觀察到操作員在未記錄的情況下進行干預，是重大缺失。

💡 凍乾產品的「全程 ISO 5 原則」

凍乾產品從填充、半塞、轉移到凍乾機裝載、凍乾循環中的塞瓶，整個過程半塞瓶的無菌產品持續暴露於環境。FDA 要求全程均在 Class 100 (ISO 5) 條件下——這意味著轉移走廊、凍乾機裝載區域都需要 ISO 5 保護。

3.8 Production — Critical Operations 關鍵操作查廠

(1) Media Fills / Process Simulations（培養基模擬試驗）

Media fills validate aseptic processing operations. All media fills should closely simulate manufacturing operations, incorporating worst-case activities and conditions as well as operator interventions.

  • Manually intensive operations: media fill size should equal or approach commercial production lot size and duration
  • Isolator processes: lower number of units proportional to overall operation is acceptable (lower risk due to lack of direct human intervention)
  • Semi-annually for each processing line; activities and interventions representative of each shift must be included
  • With more than one shift: may require more than one media fill per line every 6 months
  • Verify accountability of all filled units (filled vs. incubated)
  • All discarded units must have reasonable and assignable cause for rejection
  • Cracked and leaking units after incubation: investigated, counted, and all rejected units properly justified
  • Units examined after incubation: if not by microbiologist, must be overseen by QU and operators properly trained by microbiologist

(2) Sterile Filtration（除菌過濾）

  • Verify filters used in production are identical to those used in validation studies (i.e., those submitted in drug applications)
  • Verify actual operating parameters and allowable extremes are covered in validation studies
  • Validation of filter sterilization performed for all products — pay special attention to legacy products
  • Observe filter integrity testing to verify procedures are followed
  • Review investigations of any integrity test failures

(3) Sterilization and Depyrogenation of Containers, Closures and Equipment

  • Review validation/revalidation of sterilization and depyrogenation processes
  • Check if firm verifies validated parameters (loading patterns, cycle parameters) are met for each load
  • Rubber stopper depyrogenation by WFI washing: validation must demonstrate 3-log reduction of bacterial endotoxin
  • When firm performs own siliconization: silicon level after wash must be validated to meet predetermined acceptance criteria
  • Clean steam used for stopper sterilization must be assayed for endotoxin
  • Evaluate firm's understanding of process bioburden and adequacy of validated hold times for critical steps
  • Increased bioburden can lead to drug product degradation and contribute impurities (including endotoxin)

(4) Lyophilization

  • Review validation of lyophilization cycles for selected products
  • Verify all critical cycle parameters confirmed for each lot
  • EM performed routinely in areas of lyophilizer loading and unloading
  • Personnel monitoring conducted on operators performing loading/unloading
  • Observe transport and loading of partially stoppered vials — must be under Class 100 (ISO 5) conditions with proper aseptic technique

(5) Sealing of Vials

  • A vial is not sealed until aluminum overseal is crimped in place over the rubber stopper
  • If stoppered vials exit aseptic processing zone prior to capping, verify proper safeguards: HEPA filtered air protection and qualified in-line detectors that reject vials with improperly seated stoppers

(6) Terminal Sterilization

  • Determine type of sterilization cycles (bioburden-based or overkill)
  • Review validation/revalidation/periodic evaluation of terminal sterilization cycles
  • Verify parameters and loading patterns in production match those used in validation
  • Determine minimum acceptable cycle allowed in SOP vs. validated cycle (using BI)
  • Determine how sterilization cycles are documented, monitored, and reviewed
  • Review deviations or atypical data from sterilization operations

(7) Parametric Release of Terminally Sterilized Drug Product

Defined as a sterility assurance release based on demonstrated control of the sterilization process — uses critical process control data in lieu of the sterility test to fulfill 21 CFR 211.167(a).

  • Allowed only for products terminally sterilized by heat
  • Must be identified in the appropriate regulatory filing as the release method
  • Must have an approved application
  • If encountered: verify parametric release method has been submitted and approved in the appropriate drug application
  • Verify conditions in FDA's Compliance Policy Guide Section 490.200 are met

(8) 100% Visual Inspection of Injectable Products

  • Verify written procedures define defects to be removed and actions if critical defects exceed pre-determined level
  • Significant defect categories identified; each batch results compared to established action levels
  • Evaluate appropriateness and rationale for pre-determined action levels
  • Evaluate investigation into cause of rejects (cracks, visible particulates, foreign matter)
  • Observe the inspection process; challenge manual inspection rates through observation
  • Evaluate personnel qualification and requalification including use of reference samples
  • Manual inspection: employees trained and qualified under actual or simulated production conditions
  • Automated/semi-automated: equipment qualified and software/settings validated for all product types (clear vials, amber vials, colored solutions, suspensions)

📘 關鍵操作解讀

Media Fill 頻率與規模的要求

核心原則：Media Fill 設計必須真實反映最壞情況操作，包含所有代表性干預。

⚠️ Legacy Products 的除菌過濾驗證盲點

FDA 特別點名要注意舊產品（Legacy Products）——這些是早期未提交藥物申請的老產品，可能從未進行正式的除菌過濾驗證。

查廠時，FDA 會確認：

  • 實際使用的過濾器型號與驗證研究（及 NDA/ANDA 申請）完全一致
  • 操作參數上下限均在驗證範圍內
  • 舊產品是否有補充驗證記錄

除熱原驗證：3-log vs 6-log

注意：除熱原（Depyrogenation）驗證針對內毒素；Isolator 去污驗證針對微生物（BI）——兩者標準和對象不同。

參數放行（Parametric Release）的特殊地位

參數放行是用滅菌製程控制數據取代最終無菌測試作為放行依據。條件：

  • 僅限熱滅菌的最終滅菌產品（不適用無菌加工）
  • 必須在藥物申請中獲得 FDA 批准
  • 須符合 FDA CPG Section 490.200 的所有條件

查廠時，未經批准即進行參數放行是嚴重缺失。

⚠️ 100% 目視檢查的查廠重點

FDA 對目視檢查的要求不只是「有做」，而是：

  • 人員資格：有使用參考樣品（Reference Samples）進行資格確認
  • 速率挑戰：查廠官員可能現場挑戰（challenge）目視檢查速率——確認操作員在實際速率下仍能識別缺陷
  • 自動化設備：對每種容器類型（透明瓶、琥珀瓶、有色溶液、懸浮劑）均需完成設備確認
  • Action Level：缺陷數量達到 Action Level 時的處置程序必須書面化

💡 Clean Steam 的內毒素測試

Clean Steam（潔淨蒸氣）用於橡皮塞蒸氣滅菌，FDA 要求潔淨蒸氣本身必須測試內毒素。理由：若潔淨蒸氣含有內毒素，在滅菌過程中可能污染已清洗的橡皮塞，造成最終產品超過內毒素限度。這是容易被忽略的上游控制點。

🏭 生產系統查廠準備要點

  • Media Fill 匯總：上次查廠以來所有 APS 批次記錄（按線別、班次），含干預記錄和結果
  • 過濾器驗證矩陣：所有產品 × 過濾器型號，確認一致性；Legacy Product 需特別確認
  • 除熱原驗證報告：3-log 內毒素減少確認，含重新驗證觸發條件
  • 凍乾機裝載記錄：含環境監控和人員監控數據，確認 ISO 5 保護的文件記錄
  • 目視檢查 SOP 和人員資格記錄：含 Reference Set、速率設定依據
  • Clean Steam 內毒素測試記錄：確認有定期測試計畫和記錄
  • 批次記錄中的干預記錄：所有計畫外干預均已記錄並評估

III.9 Packaging & Labeling System ── 包裝與標籤系統

3.9 Packaging & Labeling System

Container/Closure Integrity During Packaging Operations

Investigators should evaluate whether the firm controls container/closure integrity throughout packaging operations. Packaging operations can potentially compromise the seal integrity established during filling operations if not properly controlled.

  • Examine packaging line controls to ensure seals are not disturbed
  • Review in-process checks performed on sealed containers during packaging
  • Assess whether packaging line speeds and pressures are validated
  • Check if damaged containers are identified and rejected before labeling

Unlabeled Product Controls

Proper controls over unlabeled product are essential to prevent mislabeling errors. The investigator should verify:

  • Segregation of unlabeled product from other products
  • Reconciliation procedures for labels issued vs. used vs. destroyed
  • 100% examination of labeled product for correct labels
  • Controls to prevent label mix-ups between different product strengths or lot numbers

Temperature-Sensitive Products

For products requiring refrigerated storage (2–8°C) or frozen storage:

  • Verify temperature monitoring during packaging operations
  • Assess whether product is returned to proper storage between packaging steps
  • Review cumulative temperature excursion data and impact assessments
  • Confirm shipping container validation covers expected temperature ranges

Reject Tracking and Accountability

A robust reject system ensures non-conforming product does not reach commerce:

  • Documented reconciliation of product units entering and exiting packaging
  • Clear identification and segregation of rejected units
  • Investigation of reject rates exceeding historical norms
  • Review of reject trends across multiple lots

🏭 包裝與標籤系統的查廠重點

📦 包裝操作中的容器/密封完整性

灌裝操作所建立的密封完整性（CCI）可能因後續包裝操作而受損，因此 FDA 查廠員必須評估：

  • 包裝線的管制措施是否能保護已密封容器
  • 包裝過程中是否對密封容器進行過程中檢查（in-process check）
  • 包裝線速度與壓力是否經過驗證
  • 損壞容器是否在貼標前即被剔除

⚠️ 未貼標產品的管制

標籤混淆（label mix-up）是 FDA 歷年來查察發現的常見問題之一。關鍵管制措施：

  • 分隔儲存：未貼標產品與其他產品須實體隔離
  • 標籤對帳：發出數量 = 使用量 + 銷毀量（差異須解釋）
  • 貼標後 100% 外觀確認：品項、規格、批號須正確

🌡️ 溫控產品的特殊要求

冷藏（2–8°C）或冷凍無菌品在包裝作業期間同樣須維持溫控：

  • 包裝區或包裝機台的溫度監測記錄
  • 各包裝步驟間產品是否返回適當儲存
  • 溫度偏差（temperature excursion）的累計評估報告
  • 運輸容器的驗證（需涵蓋最極端氣候條件）

💡 實務類比：包裝就像最後一關守門員

無菌灌裝的密封就像「建構了保護罩」，而包裝操作若缺乏管制，等於把這個保護罩暴露在各種物理衝擊中。FDA 的邏輯是：驗證不只在灌裝，後續每一個可能影響 CCI 的操作步驟都需要受控。

III.10 Laboratory Control System ── 實驗室管制系統

3.10 Laboratory Control System

Sterility Testing (USP <71>)

The sterility test is an important part of a firm's overall quality system for sterile products. Key areas of review:

  • Growth promotion testing: All media lots must be tested for growth promotion, using appropriate microorganism strains (Clostridium sporogenes for thioglycolate; Candida albicans and Aspergillus brasiliensis for FTM/SCDM)
  • Method validation (bacteriostasis/fungistasis): Firms must validate that the product does not inhibit microbial growth — method suitability testing must be performed for each product
  • Sample size: Per USP <71> requirements based on batch size
  • Incubation conditions: 14-day incubation at 30–35°C (Fluid Thioglycollate Medium) and 20–25°C (Soybean-Casein Digest Medium)
  • Environmental controls in sterility testing laboratory: ISO 5 (Class 100) conditions required; isolator systems strongly preferred

Endotoxin/Pyrogen Testing (USP <85>)

  • Bacterial Endotoxins Test (BET/LAL): Limulus Amebocyte Lysate test
  • Inhibition/Enhancement (I/E) testing required to confirm product compatibility
  • Maximum Valid Dilution (MVD) calculation must be documented
  • Positive Product Controls (PPC) required in each test
  • Endotoxin limits: typically K/M (0.5 EU/mL for intrathecal; 5 EU/kg/hr for IV)

Environmental Monitoring (EM) Program

A comprehensive EM program must cover all critical and controlled areas:

  • All shifts monitored: EM must not be limited to daytime only
  • Alert and Action Levels: Defined separately for each grade; action level triggers investigation
  • Monitoring methods:
    • Active air sampling (e.g., SAS impactor, centrifugal sampler)
    • Settle plates (passive air)
    • Contact plates (RODAC) for surfaces
    • Swabs for irregular surfaces
  • Trending: Data must be trended over time; adverse trends require investigation even if individual results are below action levels
  • Sample site rotation: All critical and support areas must be included in the rotation

Personnel Monitoring

  • Glove monitoring: at the end of every aseptic manufacturing session (daily or more frequently)
  • Gown monitoring: at defined frequency (typically monthly or more frequently for aseptic areas)
  • Limits: based on ISO grade and contamination risk — Grade A personnel typically 0 CFU/glove
  • Training documentation: personnel must demonstrate sterile gowning competency
  • Re-gowning qualifications: periodic re-qualification of gowning technique required

Disinfectant Efficacy Validation

  • Multiple disinfectants used in rotation (typically 2–3 different agents to prevent resistance)
  • Efficacy testing performed on representative material coupons (actual cleanroom surface materials)
  • Sporicide validation: must demonstrate efficacy against spore-forming organisms
  • Challenge organisms: must include environmental isolates recovered from the facility
  • Contact time validation: must use actual contact times used in operations

Microorganism Identification

Identification programs are critical for trend analysis and contamination investigation:

  • All isolates from critical areas (ISO 5 / Grade A) must be identified to species level
  • Identification required for:
    • Positive sterility test results
    • Positive media fill results
    • EM exceedances at alert/action level
  • Methods: MALDI-TOF, 16S rRNA sequencing, API strips, or validated commercial systems
  • Library updates required when new environmental isolates are encountered

Microbiological Media Preparation

  • Media preparation records must document formulation, sterilization, and growth promotion testing
  • Dehydrated media: manufacturer's certificate of analysis, but firm must perform growth promotion
  • Media fill media: TSB (Tryptic Soy Broth) — same as used in sterility testing

Biological Indicators (BI) Program

  • BI storage per manufacturer's requirements (usually 2–8°C)
  • D-value verification upon receipt and periodically during shelf life
  • Positive controls run with each sterilization validation cycle
  • Species-specific BIs: Geobacillus stearothermophilus (moist heat), Bacillus atrophaeus (dry heat/EO)

Incubator Calibration and Maintenance

  • Temperature mapping of incubators required (qualification)
  • Continuous or frequent temperature monitoring with alarm systems
  • Routine calibration of temperature probes
  • Contingency procedures for incubator failures

Microbiologist Training

  • Sterility testing personnel must be trained in aseptic technique
  • Periodic competency assessments
  • Training on organism identification methods

OOS Investigations — Sterility Test Failures

An initial positive sterility test result is a serious event under CGMP. The standard is strict:

  • A sterility test may be invalidated ONLY if there is clear, documented evidence of laboratory error
  • Examples of valid invalidation: documented instrument malfunction, confirmed procedural deviation, documented operator error
  • Growth alone (without clear cause) is NOT sufficient for invalidation
  • Results suggesting product or process failure must trigger a thorough investigation including review of: manufacturing records, EM data, personnel monitoring, media fill history, and CCI data
  • FDA is very skeptical of firms that routinely invalidate positive sterility tests

🔬 實驗室管制系統的查廠要點

🧫 無菌測試（USP <71>）關鍵要素

  • 培養基促生長測試：每批培養基均需驗證，使用特定菌株（硫醇甘露酸鹽培養基用 Cl. sporogenes；TSB 用 C. albicans + A. brasiliensis）
  • 方法適用性測試（bacteriostasis / fungistasis）：每一產品都需驗證產品不會抑制微生物生長
  • 孵育條件：30–35°C（FTM）+ 20–25°C（SCDM），各 14 天
  • 環境要求：測試環境需達 ISO 5 (Class 100)；建議使用隔離艙（isolator）

🦠 內毒素測試（USP <85>）

  • LAL 鱟試劑法（Limulus Amebocyte Lysate）為主要方法
  • 干擾測試（I/E testing）：確認產品與 LAL 試劑相容
  • MVD 計算：最大有效稀釋倍數，需有文件化計算依據
  • 限量：通常 0.5 EU/mL（鞘內注射）；5 EU/kg/hr（靜脈注射）

⚠️ 環境監測（EM）程序的常見缺失

  • 只監測白班：FDA 要求所有班次均需監測
  • 未設定 Alert / Action Level：兩者均需依 ISO 等級分別定義
  • 趨勢分析缺失：即使單次未超標，趨勢惡化也需調查
  • 取樣點輪換不足：應涵蓋所有關鍵區域與支援區域

📊 人員監測要求（Personnel Monitoring）

  • 手套監測頻率：每次無菌作業結束時（daily 或更頻繁）
  • 更衣監測頻率：定期（無菌區通常每月或更頻繁）
  • 限量：ISO Grade A 手套通常要求 0 CFU
  • 更衣資格確認：定期重新確認人員更衣技術

🧴 消毒劑效力驗證要點

  • 多種消毒劑輪換：通常 2–3 種，防止抗藥性（包含一種殺芽孢劑 sporicide）
  • 材質試片測試：需使用廠房實際表面材料（不能只用 SS 片）
  • 環境分離菌株：挑戰菌須包含廠內分離的環境菌
  • 接觸時間：必須與實際操作接觸時間一致

🔍 微生物鑑定程序

  • 關鍵區域（ISO 5 / Grade A）所有分離菌均需鑑定至菌種層級
  • 必須鑑定的情形：陽性無菌測試、Media Fill 陽性、EM 超標（Alert/Action Level）
  • 常用方法：MALDI-TOF 質譜儀、16S rRNA 定序

🚨 OOS 無菌測試結果：FDA 的嚴格立場

初次陽性無菌測試結果是 CGMP 下的重大事件。FDA 的立場非常明確：

  • 無效化（invalidation）只有在有清楚文件化的實驗室錯誤時才被接受
  • 「有菌生長但找不到原因」= 不可無效化
  • 有效的無效化理由：文件化的儀器故障、確認的程序偏差、記錄在案的操作員錯誤
  • 若調查指向產品或製程問題，必須全面調查，包含：製造記錄、EM 數據、人員監測、Media Fill 歷史、CCI 數據

FDA 對頻繁無效化陽性無菌測試的廠商持高度懷疑態度。

💡 類比：實驗室是最後一道安全網

如果說無菌製程是「建造保險箱」，那實驗室就是「測試保險箱是否真的鎖好」。因此 FDA 要求：測試本身要可靠（培養基要能生長）、測試環境要無菌（ISO 5）、結果要被認真對待（不能輕易無效化）。

III.11 Sampling ── 取樣

3.11 Sampling

Aseptic Sample Collection

Investigators should collect samples using aseptic technique to avoid contamination of the sample that could produce a false positive result. Sample collection should:

  • Use sterile sampling equipment (forceps, bags, gloves)
  • Follow aseptic technique throughout sample handling
  • Maintain cold chain for temperature-sensitive products
  • Document chain of custody for all samples

Sample Quantities

Standard investigational sample quantities for sterile products:

  • Sterility testing (USP <71>): 48 units minimum (double the normal USP requirement of 10–20 units, to allow the FDA lab and firm to conduct independent testing)
  • Endotoxin (USP <85>): 20 units
  • Particulate matter (USP <788>): as needed
  • Potency/identity: per product specifications

Documentary Samples

In addition to physical product samples, investigators should collect documentary samples including:

  • Batch production records (certified copies)
  • Laboratory testing records
  • Environmental monitoring data
  • Deviation and investigation reports
  • Validation protocols and reports
  • Stability data
  • Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)

Reserve/Retain Samples

  • 21 CFR 211.170 requires retention samples for each lot
  • Sterile products must be stored under conditions consistent with product labeling
  • Retention period: 1 year after expiration date for prescription products

📋 取樣管理的查廠重點

🧤 無菌取樣技術

查廠員取樣如果操作不當，可能造成「假陽性」結果，讓好產品被判定為不合格。因此 FDA 查廠員本身也需要遵循無菌技術：

  • 使用滅菌的取樣器材
  • 溫控產品全程冷鏈維持
  • 完整的 chain of custody 文件記錄

📁 文件樣品（Documentary Samples）

除了實體產品，FDA 查廠員還會帶走大量文件的認證複本：

  • 批次生產紀錄（certified copy）
  • 實驗室測試紀錄
  • 環境監測數據
  • 偏差與調查報告
  • 驗證協議與報告
  • 穩定性數據、SOP

這些文件構成 FDA 事後評估的重要依據。

依 21 CFR 211.170：

  • 每批產品均需留樣

FDA District Laboratory Assignments

Investigational samples collected by FDA investigators are submitted to the appropriate FDA laboratory based on geographic district. The laboratory assignments for sterile drug process inspections are:

Analytical Methods — Key USP Chapters

  • USP <71> Sterility Tests: Primary sterility testing method for finished sterile products; both direct inoculation and membrane filtration methods; 14-day incubation

🗺️ FDA 地區實驗室分配

注意：中部區（CER）也送往 NRL，而非設立於中部的實驗室。

📋 三大核心 USP 分析法

  • USP <71> 無菌測試：直接接種法或薄膜過濾法；30–35°C / 20–25°C 各 14 天
  • USP <85> 細菌內毒素測試：LAL 法（凝膠、濁度、顯色）；每次測試需含 Positive Product Control（PPC）
  • USP <788> 注射劑微粒：光阻法（首選）；LVI（≥100 mL）和 SVI（<100 mL）限量不同

💡 為什麼取 48 單位做無菌測試？

USP <71> 規定的最低測試數量通常是 10–20 單位（依批量大小）。FDA 要求 48 單位是因為：

  • FDA 實驗室需要進行獨立測試
  • 廠商也可以同時進行自己的測試（雙份確認）
  • 萬一需要複驗，有足夠的備份樣品

這體現了 FDA 「但信任、要驗證」的原則：不依賴廠商的測試結果，而是獨立驗證。

Part V: Regulatory/Administrative Strategy ── 法規行政策略

Part V: Regulatory/Administrative Strategy

The following types of CGMP deficiencies may warrant regulatory or administrative action. This list is intended as guidance for investigators and is not exhaustive. The severity and pattern of deficiencies, along with the firm's compliance history, will inform the type and scope of action taken.

16 Categories of CGMP Deficiencies Warranting Action

  1. Contamination: Evidence of microbial contamination of sterile drug product, including positive sterility tests (with valid reason for batch distribution) or positive media fills
  2. Specification Failure: Release of product that fails to meet established specifications (identity, strength, quality, or purity)
  3. Distribution of Non-Conforming Product: Distribution of product known to fail specifications or otherwise not conforming to CGMP
  4. Sterilization Validation Failure: Failure to validate terminal sterilization cycles using parametric methods (e.g., F₀ calculation), or use of sterilization cycles without adequate validation
  5. Media Fill Validation Failure: Inadequate aseptic process simulation (media fill) program — insufficient frequency, inadequate design, failure to simulate worst-case conditions, or unresolved positive units
  6. Investigation Failures: Failure to adequately investigate out-of-specification (OOS) results, including positive sterility tests, media fill positives, or EM exceedances; or failure to implement effective corrective actions
  7. Facility/Equipment Inadequacy: Facilities or equipment inadequate to ensure sterility — including HVAC failures, inadequate cleanroom design, or equipment that cannot be adequately cleaned and sanitized
  8. Disinfection Program Failure: Inadequate disinfection/sanitization program, including failure to validate disinfectant efficacy or failure to use sporicides in cleanroom environments
  9. WFI System Failure: Water for Injection system non-compliance — including action level exceedances, inadequate trending, inadequate maintenance, or failure to demonstrate endotoxin removal efficacy
  10. Poor Employee Practices: Documented aseptic technique failures by personnel — including poor gowning practices, improper interventions in aseptic areas, or failure to maintain aseptic conditions
  11. Inadequate Training: Failure to adequately train personnel in aseptic technique, CGMP, or specific job functions; including failure to document training or assess competency
  12. 100% Inspection Failure: Failure to perform adequate 100% visual inspection of sterile products for visible particulates, container defects, or other quality attributes
  13. Batch Record Incompleteness: Incomplete or inaccurate batch production records; failure to document critical in-process controls; or records that cannot be used to reconstruct the manufacturing process
  14. Inadequate Test Methodology: Use of test methods that are not validated, not suitable for their intended purpose, or not in compliance with compendial requirements (e.g., USP <71>, USP <85>)
  15. Inadequate Environmental Monitoring Program: EM program that does not cover all critical areas, all shifts, does not have defined alert/action levels, or fails to trend data over time
  16. Inadequate Personnel Monitoring: Personnel monitoring program that is insufficient in frequency, does not cover critical area personnel, or does not include both glove and gown monitoring

⚖️ 法規行政策略的查廠觀點

🚨 16 類重大缺失的分類邏輯

FDA 列出這 16 類缺失，是幫助查廠員判斷「哪些問題嚴重到需要啟動法規行政行動」。可以分成幾大群組理解：

缺失 1–3：產品直接品質問題
污染、規格失敗、分銷不合格品 ── 消費者已或可能已接觸到問題產品
缺失 4–5：關鍵驗證失敗
末端滅菌驗證、Media Fill 驗證 ── 製程的「無菌保證基礎」無法建立
缺失 6：調查系統崩壞
OOS / 陽性測試結果無有效調查或 CAPA ── 問題沒被發現或被掩蓋
缺失 7–9：基礎設施問題
廠房設備不足、消毒程序失效、WFI 系統問題 ── 製造環境本身有問題
缺失 10–11：人員問題
無菌操作不良、訓練不足 ── 人為因素是無菌失敗的主要來源
缺失 12–13：操作與記錄問題
目視檢驗不足、批次記錄不完整 ── 無法保證每批品質
缺失 14–16：監測與測試系統問題
測試方法不適當、EM 程序不足、人員監測不足 ── 偵測系統失靈

💡 Warning Letter 的行政本質

CPGM 7356.002A 明確指出：Warning Letter 是一種行政建議行動（advisory action），而非最終懲處。它傳遞的訊息是：「FDA 已識別問題，請改正，否則將採取更強力措施。」

對於嚴重的違規（如已有汙染產品流出市場），FDA 可以跳過 Warning Letter 直接採取法律行動（Recall、Seizure、Injunction）。

Part VII: Center Responsibilities ── 中心職責

Part VII: Center Responsibilities

CDER Oversight Role

The Center for Drug Evaluation and Research (CDER) provides oversight of sterile drug process inspections through two primary offices:

  • Office of Pharmaceutical Quality (OPQ): Provides technical expertise for complex manufacturing processes; reviews inspection findings for significant pharmaceutical quality issues; participates in regulatory action decisions for significant CGMP violations
  • Office of Surveillance (OS): Coordinates surveillance inspection planning; reviews and acts on inspection reports for potential regulatory action; liaises with ORA on CGMP enforcement

Warning Letter as Advisory Action

The Warning Letter is explicitly characterized in this CPGM as an advisory action — it advises the firm of significant violations and requests corrective action within a specified timeframe (typically 15 working days). Key points:

  • Warning Letters are posted publicly on the FDA website
  • Firms must respond with specific corrective action plans and timelines
  • Failure to respond adequately may trigger escalated regulatory action
  • For serious violations (e.g., contaminated product already in commerce), FDA may proceed directly to Seizure, Injunction, or Recall without first issuing a Warning Letter

Drug Shortage Notification and Balance

CDER explicitly acknowledges the tension between CGMP enforcement and drug availability. The CPGM states that CDER will:

  • Consider drug availability implications when making regulatory action decisions for products in shortage or with limited alternative sources
  • Work with firms to identify ways to maintain supply while addressing CGMP violations
  • This does not mean CGMP violations are overlooked — rather, CDER coordinates the scope and timing of actions
  • Manufacturers are encouraged to proactively notify FDA of supply disruptions at: DrugShortages@CDER.fda.gov

CDER's Role in Inspection Support

  • Provides science-based technical guidance to ORA investigators upon request
  • Reviews inspection reports (EIR — Establishment Inspection Report)
  • Issues NAI / VAI / OAI classifications in coordination with ORA
  • Supports foreign inspection program for sterile drug manufacturers

🏛️ CDER 中心職責的管理架構

⚠️ Warning Letter 的本質：行政建議，非最終懲處

很多人誤解 Warning Letter 是 FDA 最嚴厲的行動，實際上它只是行政建議的起點：

  • Warning Letter = FDA 通知廠商「我們發現重大問題，請在 15 個工作日內回應」
  • 公開於 FDA 網站，具相當大的市場/聲譽壓力
  • 若問題嚴重（如已有汙染品上市），可直接進行查扣（Seizure）、禁制令（Injunction）或召回（Recall）

⚖️ 品質合規 vs. 藥品可及性的平衡

CPGM 罕見地承認 FDA 在執法上面臨「雙重目標」：

  • 確保 CGMP 合規：維護公眾用藥安全
  • 維持藥品供應：避免因強制行動造成藥品短缺，尤其是無替代品的必要用藥

這不意味著違規會被放過，而是 CDER 會協調行動的範圍與時機，讓廠商有機會在持續供應下進行改正。

💡 ORA vs. CDER 的分工

簡單類比：ORA 是警察（執行查廠、收集證據）；CDER 是檢察官+法官（評估證據、決定行動）。兩者密切合作，但職責分工明確。最終法規行動的決定通常需要 CDER（特別是 OPQ/OS）的參與。

Attachment A: Inspection Questions by Topic ── 查廠問題清單（依主題）

Attachment A Overview

Attachment A provides structured inspection question sets organized by 8 major topics. These questions serve as a guide for investigators during on-site inspections of sterile drug manufacturing facilities. They are not exhaustive but represent key areas of inquiry.

Topic 1: Moist Heat Sterilization

  • Sterilizer validation (heat distribution, heat penetration studies)
  • F₀ calculation methodology and target values
  • Biological indicator (BI) placement and challenge
  • Load configuration control (maximum and minimum load validated)
  • Cycle monitoring (temperature, pressure, time recording)
  • Preventive maintenance and calibration of sterilizer
  • Steam quality (superheat, non-condensable gases, Bowie-Dick test for porous loads)

Topic 2: Dry Heat Sterilization / Depyrogenation

  • Oven validation (heat distribution, penetration, uniformity)
  • Depyrogenation validation: ≥3-log reduction of endotoxin using Geobacillus stearothermophilus or equivalent endotoxin challenge
  • Temperature mapping: all positions in oven at minimum and maximum load
  • Cycle parameters: typical 250°C / ≥30 min for depyrogenation
  • Belt conveyor speed validation for tunnel depyrogenation ovens

Topic 3: Aseptic Filling

  • HVAC system qualification (air flow patterns, smoke studies, pressure differentials)
  • Personnel gowning qualification and re-qualification
  • Aseptic technique during interventions
  • Media fill program: frequency, design, number of units, incubation, positives
  • Environmental monitoring during filling: settle plates, active air, personnel
  • Line clearance procedures

Topic 4: Lyophilization

  • Lyophilizer qualification and sterilization-in-place (SIP) validation
  • Cycle development: primary drying, secondary drying, collapse temperature
  • Chamber load configuration consistency
  • Stopper seating/crimping during and after lyophilization
  • Residual moisture testing (Karl Fischer or loss on drying)
  • Headspace gas analysis for oxygen-sensitive products

Topic 5: Isolator Barrier Technology

  • Isolator design: unidirectional airflow in Grade A zone, pressure differentials
  • Decontamination cycle validation: ≥6-log reduction of resistant spore BIs (Geobacillus stearothermophilus or equivalent)
  • Cycle development: VHP/H₂O₂ concentration, contact time, aeration
  • Glove integrity testing: routine leak testing of gloves and half-suits
  • Transfer port control: RTP (Rapid Transfer Port) design and use
  • EM requirements: reduced but still required; focus on isolator exit points

Topic 6: Environmental Monitoring — Non-viable (Particles)

  • Particle counter placement and qualification
  • Alert and action levels per ISO grade (ISO 5/7/8 equivalents)
  • At-rest vs. in-operation classification
  • Frequency and locations of non-viable particle monitoring
  • Actions taken when limits are exceeded

Topic 7: Environmental Monitoring — Viable (Microbiology)

  • Viable EM program design: sampling locations, frequencies, methods
  • All shifts monitored: day, evening, weekend
  • Alert and action levels: separate for each ISO grade and monitoring method
  • Personnel monitoring: gloves (every session), gown (periodic)
  • Microorganism identification: all critical area isolates and exceedances
  • Trending and investigation of adverse trends
  • Disinfectant efficacy validation and rotation program

Topic 8: Biological Indicators

  • BI specifications: species, population, D-value, Z-value
  • BI storage conditions and shelf life management
  • D-value verification: incoming testing and periodic re-testing
  • Positive control use in sterilization validation runs
  • Incubation conditions for BI recovery: temperature, duration, reading
  • Contamination prevention when handling positive BIs

📝 Attachment A 八大主題的查廠要點

🔥 主題1：濕熱滅菌（Moist Heat Sterilization）
  • F₀ 計算方法與目標值是否有驗證基礎
  • BI 放置位置是否代表最冷點（cold spot）
  • 最大與最小裝載量均需驗證
  • 蒸汽品質：過熱蒸汽、不凝性氣體的控制
🌡️ 主題2：乾熱滅菌/去熱原（Dry Heat / Depyrogenation）
  • 關鍵驗證指標：≥3-log 內毒素減少（通常 250°C / ≥30 分鐘）
  • 溫度映射：所有位置均需達標
  • 隧道型去熱原爐：傳送帶速度需驗證
💉 主題3：無菌灌裝（Aseptic Filling）
  • HVAC 確認（氣流型態、煙霧測試、壓差）
  • Media Fill：頻率（每半年/每班次）、設計、陽性單位處理
  • 人員無菌技術：干預操作最關鍵
❄️ 主題4：凍乾（Lyophilization）
  • 凍乾機 SIP（就地滅菌）驗證
  • 凍乾循環開發：一次乾燥、二次乾燥、崩解溫度
  • 殘留水分測試（Karl Fischer）
  • 塞子就位與軋蓋完整性確認
🛡️ 主題5：隔離艙技術（Isolator）
  • 去汙驗證：≥6-log BI 減少（G. stearothermophilus 或同等）
  • VHP 濃度、接觸時間、通風（aeration）
  • 手套完整性：日常洩漏測試（routine leak test）
  • 快速轉移口（RTP）使用管制
🌫️ 主題6：非活性粒子環境監測
  • 粒子計數器擺放位置與確認
  • Alert/Action Level 依 ISO 等級分別定義
  • 靜態（at-rest）vs 動態（in-operation）分類結果
🦠 主題7：活性微生物環境監測與人員監測
  • 全班次監測（含假日班）
  • 各等級各方法分別設定 Alert/Action Level
  • 關鍵區域所有菌株需鑑定至菌種
  • 趨勢分析：未超標但趨勢惡化 = 需調查
  • 消毒劑輪換計劃與效力驗證
🧫 主題8：生物指示劑（Biological Indicators）
  • BI 規格：菌種、菌數、D 值、Z 值
  • D 值驗證：到貨時及定期複驗
  • 陽性對照（positive control）必須包含在每次驗證運行中
  • 孵育條件：溫度、時間、判讀標準
