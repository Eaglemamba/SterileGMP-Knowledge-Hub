3.4 System Inspection Coverage 系統查廠覆蓋範圍

3.4 SYSTEM INSPECTION COVERAGE

Compliance Program 7356.002, Drug Manufacturing Inspections, lists the areas that should be covered when inspecting each of the six systems. This program (7356.002A) provides additional guidance, by system, for areas of specific concern for sterile drug products.

Attachment A is a list of questions intended to be an aid in conducting inspections. The answers do not have to be reported in the EIR unless they are relevant. Coverage includes:

  • Moist heat sterilization
  • Dry heat sterilization / depyrogenation
  • Aseptic filling
  • Lyophilization
  • Isolators
  • Environmental monitoring
  • Biological indicators

兩層指引的關係

CP 7356.002（母計畫）提供通用六系統查廠架構；CP 7356.002A（本文件）在此基礎上提供無菌製程特有的補充要求。兩份文件必須搭配使用——母計畫設定基線，本文件提高無菌製程標準。

💡 Attachment A 的使用邏輯

Attachment A 的問題清單是查廠輔助工具，不是強制清單。查廠官員可選擇性使用；答案不需一定記錄在 EIR 中，除非有缺失。藥廠可研讀這份清單來了解 FDA 的查廠思維，用於 mock inspection 設計。

3.5 Quality System 品質系統

3.5 QUALITY SYSTEM

The inspection of the quality system is two-phased:

  1. Phase 1: Evaluate whether the Quality Unit has fulfilled responsibility regarding review and approval of procedures and assured their suitability for use.
  2. Phase 2: Assess the data collected by the firm to identify potential quality problems — involves large amounts of data linking to other inspectional systems.

The comprehensive review of data by the firm is essential to assuring products are produced with a high degree of sterility assurance. Data summaries and trend reports maintained by the Quality Unit should be reviewed during every inspection. During a routine CGMP inspection, this review can help determine which option (Full or Abbreviated) to select.

Records Pertaining to Quality — Four Categories:

1. Product Quality Indicators:

  • Periodic product evaluations, complaints, adverse events, investigations
  • Field Alert Reports (FARs)
  • Product retain evaluations, rejected lots, stability, returned goods
  • Indicators of possible contamination: hazy/cloudy product, foreign particulates in injectables, cracked/leaky containers

2. Discrepancy and Failure Investigations:

  • All initial positive sterility tests and endotoxin failures — regardless of final disposition
  • All media fill failures — regardless of final disposition
  • Unexpected results or trends
  • All failures during validation or revalidation of sterilization/depyrogenation processes
  • All investigations involving media fills/process simulations
  • EM (microbial/viable and particle/non-viable) results exceeding alert or action levels
  • Process deviations or equipment malfunctions of critical equipment (sterilizers, lyophilizers)
  • OOS results for assay, impurities, particulate matter, or reconstitution time
  • Product rejects

3. Trend Reports / Quality Indicator Summaries:

  • For aseptic processing: summary of all media fills since last inspection
  • Environmental monitoring trend data (microbial and particle counts)
  • Personnel monitoring trend data
  • Summary of water system test results

4. Change Controls (since last inspection):

  • Sterilizers, lyophilizers, depyrogenation equipment
  • Aseptic processing line
  • Clean steam generator, process gas system
  • WFI and/or Purified Water system
  • Air handling systems (HVAC)
  • Automated building management system

📘 品質系統查廠解讀 — 數據驅動的兩階段評估

⚠️ 最關鍵原則：「無論最終判定如何」都要調查

FDA 明確要求：所有初次陽性無菌試驗（initial positive sterility test）、內毒素失敗和培養基模擬試驗失敗（media fill failure）必須調查記錄，無論最終批次是否放行。

這意味著即使實驗室最終判定為實驗室錯誤（Lab Error）並放行批次，該初次失敗仍需完整調查文件化。查廠時，未記錄的初次失敗是嚴重缺失。

💡 品質系統查廠的「偵探邏輯」

FDA 查廠官員用品質系統數據來決定接下來要深查哪個生產系統。若 EM 趨勢顯示 Grade B 區域有持續超過 Alert Level 的情況，查廠官員就會深查廠房設備系統和生產系統。這是數據驅動的查廠策略。

🏭 品質系統查廠準備要點

  • 準備完整的 Media Fill 摘要：包含上次查廠以來所有 APS 的批次、設計、結果
  • EM 趨勢報告：依區域、菌種分類，顯示趨勢線，說明 Alert/Action Level 設定依據
  • 初次失敗調查清單：所有初次陽性無菌試驗記錄，包含調查結論和處置
  • 關鍵設備變更清單：Sterilizer、WFI、HVAC 的所有變更及驗證文件
  • 水系統趨勢報告：WFI、PW 的 TOC、電導度、微生物計數趨勢

3.6 Facilities and Equipment System 廠房與設備系統

3.6 FACILITIES AND EQUIPMENT SYSTEM

The principle objective is to provide suitable protection of product. Evaluation is two-part:

  1. Review and evaluate the firm's rationale for and adequacy of facility and equipment design
  2. Evaluate the data relevant to the state of control of facility and equipment

Investigators should also look for visible deficiencies: cleanliness, equipment deterioration (warping, corrosion), inaccessible surfaces, unqualified changes to critical equipment.

A. Facilities — Clean Room Design

  • Evaluate design and layout (personnel/material flow, cleanroom design)
  • Specifications for air filtration, air classification, pressure differentials, temperature, humidity must be appropriate and risk-based
  • Review certification and qualification data: air flow pattern studies, HEPA filter integrity, air velocity, non-viable particle counts, pressure differentials, temperature/humidity
  • Evaluate airflow pattern (smoke studies) under dynamic conditions to verify unidirectional airflow in critical areas

Ongoing monitoring requirements:

  • Special attention to construction near cleanrooms (fungal spores risk)
  • Non-viable particle monitoring during operations at highest-risk sites
  • Pressure differentials, temperature, humidity monitored continuously during routine production
  • Continuous monitoring systems alarmed to alert operators of excursions
  • Excursions from acceptable ranges investigated for product impact
  • Periodic HEPA filter recertification (integrity testing + air velocity checks)

Sanitization/disinfection:

  • Review suitability, efficacy, and limitations of disinfecting agents
  • Review data establishing expiry of disinfection solutions
  • For multi-use facilities: evaluate changeover procedures and cleaning to prevent cross-contamination

📘 廠房系統解讀 — 動態確認是核心

⚠️ 動態條件下的煙霧測試（Smoke Study）

FDA 要求煙霧測試必須在動態條件（Dynamic Conditions）下進行——即有人員、設備運作、正常操作的情況下，而非靜態空調房間。

靜態條件的煙霧測試不足以代表實際操作時的氣流狀況。動態測試的目的是確認：在最壞操作情境下，Grade A 區域的單向氣流是否仍能有效保護暴露產品。

💡 廠房施工期間的特別注意

靠近潔淨室的施工活動（甚至翻牆、鑽孔）可能釋放大量真菌孢子。FDA 要求：施工結束後，必須通過適當的環境監控和 Media Fill 確認潔淨室恢復到可接受狀態，才能恢復生產。

「設計合理性 + 控制數據」的雙層評估

FDA 不只看「廠房設計是否符合規格」，更要看「設計是否有風險科學依據」，以及「日常運行數據是否顯示系統處於受控狀態」。

評估層面查廠官員看什麼
設計合理性壓差設計、氣流分類、潔淨室佈局的科學依據
控制狀態數據壓差趨勢、HEPA 完整性測試記錄、EM 趨勢
可見缺失生鏽、變形、難以清潔的死角

3.6B Equipment — Key Equipment Inspection Points 設備查廠重點

B. Equipment — Specific Considerations

(e) Sterilizers（高壓滅菌器 / 乾熱設備）

  • Review IQ, OQ, PQ of autoclaves, dry heat ovens/tunnels, SIP systems, chemical sterilization (H₂O₂, peracetic acid)
  • Physical examination of equipment; review engineering specs / DQ
  • Calibration, maintenance, drainage, measuring devices (temperature sensors, pressure gauges)
  • Unplanned and preventative maintenance records reviewed
  • Repeat sterilizations due to cycle failures indicate serious problems — impact on product quality must be evaluated
  • Computer-controlled systems (PLC, SCADA) may require 21 CFR Part 11 compliance assessment

(f) Lyophilizer（凍乾機）

  • Partially sealed vials are exposed from filling until stoppering — must be transported and loaded into lyophilizers under Class 100 (ISO 5) protection
  • Investigators should observe transport of vials and loading
  • Key areas: sterilization validation of the lyophilization chamber between uses, current sterilization controls, leak testing of chamber, integrity testing of air/gas filters, calibration of temperature and pressure controllers

(g) Isolators（隔離器）

  • Evaluate design and control elements: pressure differential, glove integrity, transfer port protection
  • Transfer of containers, closures, supplies into isolator must be carefully controlled
  • Current decontamination methods (vaporized H₂O₂, steam H₂O₂, peracetic acid) capable of surface sterilization but lack penetrating capability of steam
  • Decontamination validation must demonstrate a minimum 6-log reduction of the biological indicator (BI)
  • BI placement must consider worst-case locations and obstructed surfaces
  • Product contact surfaces inside the isolator must be sterilized to render free of microorganisms (minimum 6-log BI reduction)

(h) RABS（限制進入屏障系統）

  • Rigid wall enclosure providing full physical separation of filling line from operators
  • Inside surfaces disinfected with sporicidal agent (not automated decontamination cycle as in isolators)
  • Two types: Open RABS (doors may open in pre-defined circumstances) vs Closed RABS (doors never opened during operation)
  • Surrounding cleanroom typically Class 10,000 (ISO 7)

RABS inspection checklist:

  • Gloves and gauntlets are sterile when installed; disinfected or changed at appropriate intervals
  • Written procedures for open-door interventions; all interventions documented in batch records and followed by disinfection
  • RABS entry documented with line clearance in batch records
  • All fluid pathways and product contact parts sterilized prior to each batch
  • Transfer system prevents exposure of sterile surfaces to less clean environments
  • Non-product contact surfaces undergo thorough sporicidal disinfection before each batch — effectiveness validated and routinely evaluated by EM program

(i) Blow-Fill-Seal (BFS)

  • Automated: containers formed, filled, sealed in continuous operation
  • Typical uses: sterile ophthalmic and respiratory care products
  • Air quality at exposure points must meet Class 100 (ISO 5) microbiological level
  • Advanced BFS equipment may locate in Class 100,000 (ISO 8); otherwise Class 10,000 (ISO 7)

BFS inspection checklist:

  • HEPA-filtered or sterile air used during all exposure steps (parison formation, molding, filling)
  • Monitoring and PM programs for utility integrity (cooling water, heating) — leaks can contaminate product
  • SIP system for product line: validated cycle, condensate properly drained, line protected between sterilization and use
  • Personnel properly gowned and trained for classified environment

(j) Reactor / Centrifuge / Dryer / Mill (Sterile Bulk API)

  • Equipment and all transfer piping sterilized prior to processing (SIP with clean steam or chemical sterilant)
  • Must remain integral (no leaks) and sterile throughout entire manufacturing process
  • Any open operation (e.g., adding seed crystals) requires Class 100 (ISO 5) air protection and carefully designed aseptic technique

(2) Container/Closure Processing Equipment

  • Depyrogenation equipment: dry heat oven and/or depyrogenation tunnel; stoppers may be depyrogenated by WFI washing dilution

(3) Support Utilities

  • Water System: Review WFI generation equipment and distribution loop (tanks, lines, isometric diagrams, vent filters, PM schedules)
  • HVAC: Refer to Section IV of FDA's 2004 Aseptic Processing Guidance

📘 設備查廠解讀 — 各設備的核心風險

Sterilizer — 重複滅菌是危險信號

FDA 特別指出：因循環失敗而重複滅菌（repeat sterilization）可能表示設備有嚴重問題。查廠官員會：

  • 審查設備日誌中的循環失敗記錄
  • 評估重複滅菌對產品品質的影響
  • 確認相關調查和 CAPA

藥廠應建立循環失敗 = 偏差調查的自動觸發機制。

⚠️ Lyophilizer — 最危險的轉移步驟

凍乾瓶（半塞狀態）從填充線轉移到凍乾機這段時間是風險最高的暴露期。FDA 查廠官員會實際觀察這個操作，確認：

  • 轉移過程全程在 ISO 5 保護下
  • 凍乾機裝載作業的人員技術和 SOP 執行
  • 凍乾機腔室在兩批之間的滅菌驗證

Isolator 去污驗證要求（6-log BI）

要求：隔離器去污程序必須達到最低 6-log（百萬倍）生物指示劑（BI）殺滅率。

挑戰點：H₂O₂ 蒸氣等表面殺菌劑無法穿透被遮蔽或保護的表面（如螺絲縫隙、管道連接處）。驗證時必須在最壞情況位置放置 BI。

實務要求：

  • 量化測量裝置（如近紅外光）或化學指示劑確認最壞位置
  • 產品接觸表面需滅菌至無任何微生物（不僅僅是去污）

⚠️ Open RABS 的陷阱：開門即失去 RABS 定義

FDA 明確指出：若門在填充作業中例行性開啟，該系統不再被視為 RABS（因為不再有效限制進入關鍵區域）。每次開門干預都必須：

  • 有書面程序定義允許的干預情境
  • 記錄於批次記錄
  • 干預後執行消毒

BFS — 空氣品質的直接關係

研究顯示 BFS 污染單位數與周圍空氣微生物濃度有直接相關。因此：

  • 產品暴露步驟（parison 形成、模具成型、填充）必須有 ISO 5 空氣品質
  • 先進 BFS 設備可在 ISO 8 環境（降低廠房等級要求，節省成本）
  • 產品供給管線通常採用 SIP（蒸氣就地滅菌），必須驗證且冷凝水需正確排放

🏭 廠房設備系統查廠準備清單

  • 潔淨室認證記錄：最近一次 HEPA 完整性測試、非活性粒子計數、壓差驗證
  • 煙霧測試記錄：確認在動態條件下執行；保存影像記錄
  • 消毒驗證：消毒劑有效期數據、輪替消毒劑的 VPHP/孢子殺菌劑效果驗證
  • Isolator/RABS 去污記錄：6-log BI 驗證資料、每批去污程序記錄
  • 凍乾機裝載 SOP：確認有 ISO 5 保護記錄，操作人員訓練記錄
  • 設備日誌：所有循環失敗記錄及調查文件
  • 水系統等距圖（Isometric Drawing）：需為最新版本

📌 本節重點摘要 Section Summary — 3.4–3.6

  • 品質系統兩階段：Phase 1（程序審查）+ Phase 2（數據趨勢評估）——後者是現代查廠的核心
  • 必查記錄四類：產品投訴/ADE → 偏差/失敗調查 → EM/Media Fill 趨勢 → 關鍵設備變更控制
  • 初次失敗必查：無菌試驗初次陽性、Media Fill 失敗、內毒素失敗，無論最終判定均需調查文件
  • 廠房雙層評估：設計合理性（有科學依據） + 控制數據（日常監控有效）
  • 動態煙霧測試：必須在生產操作條件下執行，靜態不足
  • Isolator 6-log：去污驗證需達生物指示劑 6-log 殺滅率；注意穿透盲點
  • RABS 開門原則：例行開門 = 失去 RABS 定義；每次干預必須記錄、消毒
  • Lyophilizer 轉移：半塞瓶轉移至凍乾機全程需 ISO 5 保護；查廠官員可能觀察操作
  • BFS 空氣品質：所有暴露步驟需 ISO 5 空氣；空氣微生物濃度與污染率直接相關
