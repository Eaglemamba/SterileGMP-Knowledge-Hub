3.1 Types of Sterilization 滅菌種類

3.1 TYPES OF STERILIZATION

There are two broad methods to produce a sterile drug product:

  • Terminal Sterilization
  • Aseptic Processing of sterilized unit components

Terminal sterilization should be utilized when the product and container/closure system are able to withstand the terminal sterilization process.

A. Terminal Sterilization

The terminal sterilization process usually involves filling and sealing product containers under high quality environmental conditions designed to minimize microbial and particulate contamination. In most cases, the product, container, and closure have low bioburden, but are not sterile at the time of filling. The product is then subjected to a sterilization process in its final container.

Methods of terminal sterilization:

  • Moist Heat Sterilization
  • Irradiation
  • Ethylene Oxide (typically for assembled components/kits)

Types of sterilization cycles:

  1. Overkill method:
    • Generally used for heat stable materials
    • Designed to provide significant sterility assurance regardless of bioburden number and resistance
    • Results in greater heat/exposure input to the product
  2. Bioburden Based cycle:
    • Requires studies to determine number and resistance of microorganisms in product and incoming components
    • Cycle developed to destroy microbial load without degrading product
    • Requires routine bioburden monitoring of batches and ongoing knowledge of heat resistance of organisms

Reference: PDA Technical Report No. 1 (Revised 2007) — Validation of Moist Heat Sterilization Processes.

B. Aseptic Processing

Aseptic processing presents a higher risk of microbial contamination than terminal sterilization. In an aseptic filling process, the drug product, containers, and closures are sterilized separately and then brought together under an extremely high quality environmental condition.

Aseptic processing involves more variables than terminal sterilization. Any manual or mechanical manipulation of the sterilized drug, containers, or closures poses the risk of microbial contamination.

Technologies reducing human intervention in critical areas:

  • RABS (Restricted Access Barrier Systems)
  • Blow-Fill-Seal systems
  • Isolators — completely separate the aseptic filling line from the external environment
"For terminal sterilized products that are aseptically filled prior to terminal sterilization, less rigorous aseptic controls can be considered."

💡 FDA 的查廠重點選擇邏輯

若廠商同時有多條生產線，查廠官員優先選擇：

  • 無菌加工線中最多人工干預的那條
  • 最終滅菌產品中使用生物負荷循環（較敏感）的那個

這體現了 FDA 的核心原則：選最高風險的情境作為評估基準。

⚠️ 人為因素是無菌查廠重點

人員是傳統無菌加工線中最大的污染來源，尤其當操作人員須進入 Grade A（ISO 5 / Class 100）區域時。

RABS、Isolator 技術的核心價值就是物理隔絕人員與無菌區域，大幅降低人為污染風險——這也是為何使用這些技術的廠商可能符合縮短查廠（Abbreviated Inspection）條件。

關鍵技術術語

  • RABS：Restricted Access Barrier System，限制進入屏障系統——半封閉，仍允許有限干預
  • Isolator：完全封閉的隔離器系統——操作人員透過手套箱操作，無直接接觸
  • Blow-Fill-Seal (BFS)：吹瓶-填充-封口一體化系統，全程在封閉環境完成
  • Overkill：過殺法——通常目標 SAL ≤ 10⁻⁶，且 F₀ ≥ 12 min
  • Bioburden：生物負荷——產品及組件滅菌前的微生物數量

🏭 對藥廠的實務啟示

  • 若產品兼具最終滅菌可行性，應優先採用最終滅菌（FDA 明確表示此為優先選擇）
  • 採用 Bioburden-Based 循環的廠商必須有完善的生物負荷監控程序，查廠時必然被重點審查
  • 導入 Isolator 或封閉 RABS 是取得 Abbreviated Inspection 資格的重要前提之一

3.2 Reporting 報告規範

3.2 REPORTING

Follow instructions in the current editions of the IOM (Investigations Operations Manual) and Guide to International Inspection and Travel in preparing the inspection report.

  • Use Turbo EIR for all reports, both domestic and international, even if a FDA-483 was not issued or was issued outside of Turbo EIR.
  • The "Manufacturing / Design Operations" section of the report should be organized by the systems described in this program that were covered during the inspection.
  • Briefly summarize review of each covered system in accordance with the key elements outlined in this Compliance Program.
  • Add more details and supporting evidence for the systems found to be deficient.

📘 報告規範解讀 — 藥廠如何理解 EIR

EIR（Establishment Inspection Report）的結構邏輯

EIR 按六大系統組織，意味著查廠官員的觀察與記錄都以系統為單位。FDA-483（觀察表）是查廠現場發現缺失的初步文件，EIR 是事後整理的完整書面記錄。

重要：即使沒有發出 FDA-483，查廠官員仍需提交 EIR——這份文件是 FDA 未來查廠決策的重要依據。

⚠️ EIR 的公開性

EIR 可透過 FOIA（資訊自由法）申請取得，是業界進行競爭情報分析的重要來源。廠商應定期閱讀同業的 EIR，了解 FDA 的查廠趨勢與關注焦點。

3.3 Inspection Approaches 查廠方法

3.3 INSPECTION APPROACHES

Related Compliance Programs:

  • CP 7356.002C — Radioactive Drugs (supplementary for radioactive sterile drugs)
  • CP 7356.002P — PET Drugs (specific for Positron Emission Tomography drugs; this program does NOT apply)
  • CP 7356.002M — Licensed Biological Therapeutic Drug Products (use in conjunction)

Full Inspections (PAC 56002A)

Provide a comprehensive evaluation of CGMP compliance. Normally includes at least four systems, one of which must be the Quality System. For sterile drugs, must also include Facilities & Equipment and Production Systems.

Full Inspections are conducted:

  • Initial inspection of a drug firm
  • First inspection after a Warning Letter, regulatory action, or significant FDA-483 findings
  • When Abbreviated Inspection reveals significant deficiencies
  • Significant changes in firm's operations since last inspection
  • History of recurring violations, fluctuating compliance
  • Information from samples, complaints, Field Alerts, recalls questions product quality

Abbreviated Inspections (PAC 56002I)

May be appropriate if BOTH conditions are satisfied:

  1. The firm has implemented a formal risk management program including:
    • Modern separation and automation approach (isolators, closed RABS)
    • Upstream bioburden controls
    • Robust quality system responsive to potential hazards
    • Formal lifecycle quality risk management aligned with ICH Q9
  2. The firm has sustained acceptable compliance history AND one of:
    • Produces terminally sterilized products using robust sterilization methods; OR
    • Has implemented robust risk management with daily assurance through design and control
    AND at inspection start:
    • Review of media fills, sterility testing, recalls, complaints reveals no sterility failure of distributed batches
    • Record of satisfactory CGMP compliance (e.g., two consecutive NAI or ≤ one VAI), with no Class 1 recalls

Abbreviated Inspection — Critical Elements by System

Facilities and Equipment:

  • Cleaning and disinfection
  • Facility/equipment layout and air handling for preventing viable and non-viable contamination
  • Material flow
  • Quality control of classified areas (air pressure balance, HEPA filtration)
  • Trending data supporting adequacy of clean room quality
  • Documented investigation into discrepancy

Materials:

  • Microbial and endotoxin control of incoming materials and components
  • Quality of water supply, maintenance, qualification
  • Operation of water and process gas systems
  • Documented investigations into OOS, deviations, discrepancies

Production:

  • Observation of operator behavior and aseptic techniques during manufacturing
  • Production line operations and interventions
  • Personnel training in aseptic techniques
  • Major production line repair or maintenance issues
  • Risk assessment on microbial and endotoxin controls, including hold times of critical steps
  • Validation of sterilization of equipment, container-closures and supplies
  • Media fills design and results
  • Documented investigations into deviations, discrepancies, and OOS results

Laboratory:

  • Investigations into OOS, deviations, discrepancies
  • Test methods and controls, including adherence to validated methods
  • Training and qualifications of laboratory personnel
  • Trending of water system test results
  • Systems for recovery, identification and trending of environmental monitoring isolates

📘 查廠方法解讀 — Full vs Abbreviated 策略

Full Inspection 的三個必查系統

無菌藥品 Full Inspection 在 CP 7356.002 要求「至少四系統」的基礎上，強制要求包含：

  1. 品質系統（Quality System）— 所有查廠必查
  2. 廠房設備系統（Facilities & Equipment）— 無菌製程特有強制
  3. 生產系統（Production System）— 無菌製程特有強制

加上其他至少一個系統，共四個或以上。

Abbreviated Inspection 資格的雙重門檻

必須同時滿足兩個條件：

條件一（製程設計）：

  • ✅ 使用 Isolator 或封閉 RABS
  • ✅ 有上游生物負荷控制
  • ✅ 品質系統對潛在風險有回應能力
  • ✅ 符合 ICH Q9 的生命週期風險管理

條件二（合規歷史）：

  • ✅ 連續兩次 NAI 或不超過一次 VAI
  • ✅ 無 Class 1 recall
  • ✅ 無已上市批次的無菌失敗記錄
  • ✅（最終滅菌廠商優先符合）

💡 Full vs Abbreviated 的本質差異

Abbreviated Inspection 不是「查少一點」，而是針對無菌保障關鍵指標的精準深查。兩者的差異：

Full InspectionAbbreviated Inspection
系統數≥ 4 系統品質系統以外各系統的關鍵要點
適用廠商新廠、問題廠、重大變更持續合規、先進技術廠商
主要焦點全面 GMP 合規微生物控制與無菌保障

⚠️ NAI / VAI / OAI 的定義

  • NAI（No Action Indicated）：無缺失，無需行動——最佳結果
  • VAI（Voluntary Action Indicated）：有缺失，廠商自行改善——可接受
  • OAI（Official Action Indicated）：重大缺失，需 FDA 正式行動——最差結果，下次必為 Full Inspection

Abbreviated Inspection 各系統的重點提示

縮短查廠的重點落在微生物控制數據：

  • 廠房設備：HEPA 完整性、氣壓差趨勢、消毒效果
  • 物料：進廠物料的內毒素（ET）與微生物限度測試
  • 生產：APS（Media Fill）結果、操作人員無菌技術現場觀察
  • 實驗室：EM 菌株鑑別與趨勢分析、OOS 調查品質

🏭 取得 Abbreviated Inspection 資格的策略路徑

  1. 技術升級：評估導入 Isolator 或封閉 RABS 的可行性（長期投資，降低查廠強度）
  2. 合規記錄維持：確保連續兩次 NAI 或最多一次 VAI（持續性合規，非突擊準備）
  3. ICH Q9 對齊：建立正式的生命週期風險管理計畫，包含 Risk Assessment 文件化
  4. APS 設計強化：Media Fill 計畫需覆蓋最壞情境（Worst Case），結果需完全通過
  5. 零 Class 1 Recall：強化上市後監控（Post-Market Surveillance），早期發現問題

📌 本節重點摘要 Section Summary — 3.1–3.3

  • 製程優先順序：FDA 明確偏好最終滅菌（風險較低），若產品可耐受應優先採用
  • Overkill vs Bioburden：Bioburden-Based 循環需更嚴格的生物負荷監控，查廠焦點更複雜
  • 人員污染：無菌加工中人員是最大污染源；Isolator/RABS 技術可有效降低風險並符合縮短查廠條件
  • Full Inspection：無菌廠必查品質系統 + 廠房設備 + 生產系統（至少三系統，加上其他至少一個）
  • Abbreviated Inspection 雙重門檻：（1）先進技術 + ICH Q9 風險管理；（2）持續良好合規紀錄（≥2 NAI 或 ≤1 VAI，無 Class 1 recall，無無菌失敗）
  • 縮短查廠重點：媒體填充（APS）結果、EM 趨勢、OOS 調查品質、人員技術現場觀察
  • EIR 按系統組織：了解系統式查廠邏輯有助於規劃 mock inspection 和 internal audit
