3.7 Materials System 物料系統

3.7 MATERIALS SYSTEM

In sterile operations, the quality attributes of each material (ingredients, WFI, containers, closures) have a bearing on the critical attributes of the finished product. Review procedures for receipt, handling, sampling, testing, approval and storage of manufacturing materials. Emphasis on incoming materials represented to be sterile and/or pyrogen free.

(1) Water Systems — WFI

WFI is an ingredient in many sterile drugs and also used for depyrogenation of equipment and stoppers, and in cleaning. Purified water can be used for some sterile non-injectable solutions.

  • Observe and understand generation and distribution systems
  • Evaluate "As Built" diagrams; inspect for leaks, pipe slopes, dead legs, non-sanitary fittings
  • Evaluate how microbial alert and action levels are established
  • Evaluate sampling sites, procedures, frequencies and tests
  • Review PM and calibration of critical instruments
  • Review raw data to verify all above is completed per procedure
  • Review and observe routine in-line monitoring (TOC and conductivity)
  • Review trend data for chemistry, microbiological and endotoxin tests
  • Review investigation of results at or over alert and action levels

(2) Process Gas

  • Gases used in aseptic operations or downstream of sterilization must be filtered through a sterilizing grade filter
  • Evaluate integrity testing of hydrophobic filters
  • Controls over final filtration and filter integrity testing
  • Nitrogen gas overlay for oxygen-sensitive products: gas used as component in finished product

(3) Pre-washed / Ready-to-Sterilize Closures

Many SVP manufacturers purchase stoppers that are ready-to-sterilize (pyrogen-free). Even if no washing/depyrogenation is done in-house, the manufacturer is still responsible for assuring acceptable quality.

  • Pyrogen requirements must be in stopper specifications
  • If not testing each incoming lot, must establish supplier reliability via vendor qualification + periodic testing

Reference: 21 CFR 211.94(c) — containers/closures must be processed to remove pyrogenic substances where indicated.

(4) Microbiological and Endotoxin Testing of Components

21 CFR 211.84(d): Each lot liable to microbial contamination shall be subjected to microbiological tests before use.

  • Evaluate the firm's system for determining if micro/endotoxin testing is required
  • Review rationale for setting acceptance criteria
  • Verify materials meet test criteria; if not, investigations and corrective actions implemented

(5) Verification of Containers and Closures

Physical and chemical characteristics can be critical to sterility and stability. Many containers/closures look alike but have different materials or surface treatments (silicone on stoppers, ammonium sulfate on Type I glass).

  • Evaluate procedures for assuring correct materials, correct dimensions (critical to container-closure integrity), free of critical defects

(6) Container / Closure Integrity (CCI)

Integrity of the CCS is critical to assuring units remain sterile through shipment, storage and use.

  • Verify all incoming container-closure components meet specifications including all appropriate dimensions
  • Determine studies adequately simulate stress conditions of sterilization process, handling and storage
  • Verify units tested in validation are appropriate (for terminally sterilized products: exposed to maximum sterilization cycles)
  • Sensitivity of the test is specified
  • Container-closure integrity demonstrated during validation and as part of the stability program (in lieu of sterility testing) over shelf life

⚠️ 採購進廠的預洗塞——藥廠仍需負責

即使向廠商購買「Ready-to-Sterilize」（預清洗無熱原）的橡皮塞，藥廠仍須：

  1. 在規格書中納入熱原要求
  2. 若不每批次自行測試，須通過廠商資格確認（Vendor Qualification）建立對廠商測試結果的信任基礎
  3. 定期進行驗收測試以確認廠商持續合規

這是查廠時常被忽視的責任歸屬問題——委外不等於免責。

CCI 取代無菌測試的法規依據

FDA 允許在產品穩定性計畫中以容器密封完整性測試（CCI）取代無菌測試。前提：

  • CCI 方法需在驗證研究中確認靈敏度
  • 測試需涵蓋最大滅菌循環次數的壓力條件
  • 需覆蓋整個產品儲存期

查廠時 FDA 會確認：驗證設計是否真正模擬了最壞情況（包含滅菌、運輸、儲存應力）。

💡 製程氣體的「隱形風險」

氮氣覆蓋（Nitrogen Overlay）是氧敏感產品的保護措施，但若氮氣未通過除菌過濾，反而成為微生物污染途徑。查廠官員會確認：所有在滅菌下游或無菌操作中接觸產品的氣體，是否通過疏水性除菌過濾（0.22 μm），並有完整性測試記錄。

🏭 物料系統查廠準備要點

  • WFI 等距圖：確保為最新版本，標示所有採樣點、死角長度、坡度
  • WFI/PW 趨勢報告：TOC、電導度、微生物、內毒素，含 Alert/Action Level 及超標調查
  • 廠商資格確認：預洗塞廠商的 QA Agreement 和定期驗收測試記錄
  • CCI 驗證資料：方法靈敏度、最壞情況設計、穩定性 CCI 數據
  • 製程氣體過濾器：完整性測試記錄，包含每批使用後的測試

3.8 Production System 生產系統

3.8 PRODUCTION SYSTEM — Overview

Production practices and conditions can have a direct and significant adverse impact on drug sterility assurance. The Production System should be included in all Full Inspections. Coverage of critical elements should be part of all inspections (Full and Abbreviated) of aseptically processed sterile drug manufacturers.

Critical observations during inspection:

  • Adequacy of aseptic technique
  • Personnel behavior and practices in the cleanroom
  • Movement of people and materials before and during aseptic operation
  • Robustness of production process design
  • Disinfection practices

Higher-risk operations requiring real-time observation:

  • Set-up of filling lines (especially powder filling lines and lines requiring multiple aseptic assemblies or non-SIP product pathways)
  • Cleaning and disinfection of line and room — all difficult-to-access surfaces
  • Protection of critical contact surfaces throughout operations and post-sterilization
  • Aseptic technique during operations, including handling of equipment jams and stoppages
  • Personnel flow and material flow in relation to microbial control
  • Filling operations: gowning technique, gown integrity, adherence to SOPs, nature and frequency of interventions
  • Atypical interventions associated with unplanned events (e.g., operator changes filling pump mid-operation)
  • Extra manipulations for assembly of sterile filtration apparatus not SIP-sterilized
  • Handling of partially stoppered vials in lyophilization — all steps under Class 100
  • Environmental monitoring — observation of actual monitoring operations and sample site rationale
  • Proper stopper seating and capping performed under unidirectional flow

📘 生產系統解讀 — 現場觀察是核心

為什麼「現場觀察」不可取代？

FDA 強調生產系統查廠必須包含製程的即時觀察（Real-Time Observation）。原因：

  • 許多無菌操作缺失只在「現場進行時」才看得到（如操作人員跨越無菌區、設備卡住時的不當處置）
  • SOP 寫得完美，但實際執行可能偏離
  • 文件查核只能看到「應該怎麼做」，觀察才能看到「實際怎麼做」

⚠️ 最高風險操作：干預（Interventions）

FDA 查廠官員會特別關注非計畫性干預（如設備卡塞、操作員臨時更換零件）。這類事件：

  • 必須記錄在批次記錄中
  • 應在 Media Fill 中模擬（作為 Worst Case Interventions）
  • 需要評估對無菌性的影響

觀察到操作員在未記錄的情況下進行干預，是重大缺失。

💡 凍乾產品的「全程 ISO 5 原則」

凍乾產品從填充、半塞、轉移到凍乾機裝載、凍乾循環中的塞瓶，整個過程半塞瓶的無菌產品持續暴露於環境。FDA 要求全程均在 Class 100 (ISO 5) 條件下——這意味著轉移走廊、凍乾機裝載區域都需要 ISO 5 保護。

3.8 Production — Critical Operations 關鍵操作查廠

(1) Media Fills / Process Simulations（培養基模擬試驗）

Media fills validate aseptic processing operations. All media fills should closely simulate manufacturing operations, incorporating worst-case activities and conditions as well as operator interventions.

  • Manually intensive operations: media fill size should equal or approach commercial production lot size and duration
  • Isolator processes: lower number of units proportional to overall operation is acceptable (lower risk due to lack of direct human intervention)
  • Semi-annually for each processing line; activities and interventions representative of each shift must be included
  • With more than one shift: may require more than one media fill per line every 6 months
  • Verify accountability of all filled units (filled vs. incubated)
  • All discarded units must have reasonable and assignable cause for rejection
  • Cracked and leaking units after incubation: investigated, counted, and all rejected units properly justified
  • Units examined after incubation: if not by microbiologist, must be overseen by QU and operators properly trained by microbiologist

(2) Sterile Filtration（除菌過濾）

  • Verify filters used in production are identical to those used in validation studies (i.e., those submitted in drug applications)
  • Verify actual operating parameters and allowable extremes are covered in validation studies
  • Validation of filter sterilization performed for all products — pay special attention to legacy products
  • Observe filter integrity testing to verify procedures are followed
  • Review investigations of any integrity test failures

(3) Sterilization and Depyrogenation of Containers, Closures and Equipment

  • Review validation/revalidation of sterilization and depyrogenation processes
  • Check if firm verifies validated parameters (loading patterns, cycle parameters) are met for each load
  • Rubber stopper depyrogenation by WFI washing: validation must demonstrate 3-log reduction of bacterial endotoxin
  • When firm performs own siliconization: silicon level after wash must be validated to meet predetermined acceptance criteria
  • Clean steam used for stopper sterilization must be assayed for endotoxin
  • Evaluate firm's understanding of process bioburden and adequacy of validated hold times for critical steps
  • Increased bioburden can lead to drug product degradation and contribute impurities (including endotoxin)

(4) Lyophilization

  • Review validation of lyophilization cycles for selected products
  • Verify all critical cycle parameters confirmed for each lot
  • EM performed routinely in areas of lyophilizer loading and unloading
  • Personnel monitoring conducted on operators performing loading/unloading
  • Observe transport and loading of partially stoppered vials — must be under Class 100 (ISO 5) conditions with proper aseptic technique

(5) Sealing of Vials

  • A vial is not sealed until aluminum overseal is crimped in place over the rubber stopper
  • If stoppered vials exit aseptic processing zone prior to capping, verify proper safeguards: HEPA filtered air protection and qualified in-line detectors that reject vials with improperly seated stoppers

(6) Terminal Sterilization

  • Determine type of sterilization cycles (bioburden-based or overkill)
  • Review validation/revalidation/periodic evaluation of terminal sterilization cycles
  • Verify parameters and loading patterns in production match those used in validation
  • Determine minimum acceptable cycle allowed in SOP vs. validated cycle (using BI)
  • Determine how sterilization cycles are documented, monitored, and reviewed
  • Review deviations or atypical data from sterilization operations

(7) Parametric Release of Terminally Sterilized Drug Product

Defined as a sterility assurance release based on demonstrated control of the sterilization process — uses critical process control data in lieu of the sterility test to fulfill 21 CFR 211.167(a).

  • Allowed only for products terminally sterilized by heat
  • Must be identified in the appropriate regulatory filing as the release method
  • Must have an approved application
  • If encountered: verify parametric release method has been submitted and approved in the appropriate drug application
  • Verify conditions in FDA's Compliance Policy Guide Section 490.200 are met

(8) 100% Visual Inspection of Injectable Products

  • Verify written procedures define defects to be removed and actions if critical defects exceed pre-determined level
  • Significant defect categories identified; each batch results compared to established action levels
  • Evaluate appropriateness and rationale for pre-determined action levels
  • Evaluate investigation into cause of rejects (cracks, visible particulates, foreign matter)
  • Observe the inspection process; challenge manual inspection rates through observation
  • Evaluate personnel qualification and requalification including use of reference samples
  • Manual inspection: employees trained and qualified under actual or simulated production conditions
  • Automated/semi-automated: equipment qualified and software/settings validated for all product types (clear vials, amber vials, colored solutions, suspensions)

📘 關鍵操作解讀

Media Fill 頻率與規模的要求

操作類型Media Fill 規模頻率
傳統人工無菌操作≥ 商業批次規模（單位數、持續時間）每線每班次每半年
Isolator 操作可小於商業批次（比例性降低）每半年
多班次操作各班次的干預活動均須涵蓋可能需每線每班次 >1 次/半年

核心原則：Media Fill 設計必須真實反映最壞情況操作，包含所有代表性干預。

⚠️ Legacy Products 的除菌過濾驗證盲點

FDA 特別點名要注意舊產品（Legacy Products）——這些是早期未提交藥物申請的老產品，可能從未進行正式的除菌過濾驗證。

查廠時，FDA 會確認：

  • 實際使用的過濾器型號與驗證研究（及 NDA/ANDA 申請）完全一致
  • 操作參數上下限均在驗證範圍內
  • 舊產品是否有補充驗證記錄

除熱原驗證：3-log vs 6-log

操作要求依據
橡皮塞 WFI 洗滌除熱原內毒素 3-log 減少FDA CPGM 7356.002A
乾熱除熱原隧道內毒素 3-log 減少一般行業標準
Isolator 去污（滅菌）生物指示劑 6-log 減少FDA CPGM 7356.002A (Section 3.6)

注意：除熱原（Depyrogenation）驗證針對內毒素；Isolator 去污驗證針對微生物（BI）——兩者標準和對象不同。

參數放行（Parametric Release）的特殊地位

參數放行是用滅菌製程控制數據取代最終無菌測試作為放行依據。條件：

  • 僅限熱滅菌的最終滅菌產品（不適用無菌加工）
  • 必須在藥物申請中獲得 FDA 批准
  • 須符合 FDA CPG Section 490.200 的所有條件

查廠時，未經批准即進行參數放行是嚴重缺失。

⚠️ 100% 目視檢查的查廠重點

FDA 對目視檢查的要求不只是「有做」，而是：

  • 人員資格：有使用參考樣品（Reference Samples）進行資格確認
  • 速率挑戰：查廠官員可能現場挑戰（challenge）目視檢查速率——確認操作員在實際速率下仍能識別缺陷
  • 自動化設備：對每種容器類型（透明瓶、琥珀瓶、有色溶液、懸浮劑）均需完成設備確認
  • Action Level：缺陷數量達到 Action Level 時的處置程序必須書面化

💡 Clean Steam 的內毒素測試

Clean Steam（潔淨蒸氣）用於橡皮塞蒸氣滅菌，FDA 要求潔淨蒸氣本身必須測試內毒素。理由：若潔淨蒸氣含有內毒素，在滅菌過程中可能污染已清洗的橡皮塞，造成最終產品超過內毒素限度。這是容易被忽略的上游控制點。

🏭 生產系統查廠準備要點

  • Media Fill 匯總：上次查廠以來所有 APS 批次記錄（按線別、班次），含干預記錄和結果
  • 過濾器驗證矩陣：所有產品 × 過濾器型號，確認一致性；Legacy Product 需特別確認
  • 除熱原驗證報告：3-log 內毒素減少確認，含重新驗證觸發條件
  • 凍乾機裝載記錄：含環境監控和人員監控數據，確認 ISO 5 保護的文件記錄
  • 目視檢查 SOP 和人員資格記錄：含 Reference Set、速率設定依據
  • Clean Steam 內毒素測試記錄：確認有定期測試計畫和記錄
  • 批次記錄中的干預記錄：所有計畫外干預均已記錄並評估

📌 本節重點摘要 Section Summary — 3.7–3.8

  • WFI 六大查廠重點：死角、坡度、Alert/Action Level 設定依據、採樣計畫代表性、即時 TOC/電導度監控、趨勢分析與超標調查
  • CCI 取代無菌測試：需驗證 CCI 方法靈敏度，並在穩定性測試中持續確認
  • 進廠密封件責任：採購預洗塞不免除藥廠責任——需廠商資格確認和定期驗收測試
  • 媒體填充頻率：每線每班次每半年；人工操作需近商業批次規模；Isolator 可比例降低
  • Legacy Products：除菌過濾驗證需確認與申請文件中過濾器型號完全一致
  • 除熱原 3-log：WFI 洗滌橡皮塞需達內毒素 3-log 減少（vs Isolator 去污 6-log 微生物）
  • Clean Steam 測試：用於塞子滅菌的潔淨蒸氣必須測試內毒素
  • 參數放行：僅限熱滅菌最終滅菌產品；必須有 FDA 批准的藥物申請
  • 目視檢查挑戰：查廠官員可現場挑戰檢查速率；人員資格需含參考樣品確認
  • 現場觀察不可替代：生產系統查廠核心是即時觀察，文件查核不足以評估操作合規性
