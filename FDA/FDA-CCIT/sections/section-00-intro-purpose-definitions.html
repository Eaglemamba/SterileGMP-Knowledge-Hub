核心概念解析 Key Concepts

FDA Guidance (FDA 指引文件): FDA Guidance 屬於「非約束性建議」(Nonbinding Recommendations)，不具法律強制力，但代表 FDA 目前對該議題的思考方向。在實務中，偏離 Guidance 建議需要有充分的科學理由支持。

Alternative Approach (替代方法): FDA 明確允許使用替代方法，前提是該方法符合適用法規的要求。這是風險導向法規架構的核心精神。

FDA Guidance 就像「行車建議路線」——導航推薦你走高速公路，但你可以選擇走省道，只要你能安全到達目的地即可。然而，如果你選擇了不同路線卻出了事，你需要解釋為何不按建議走。

I. Purpose and Scope 目的與範疇

原文內容 Original Text

This guidance document provides recommendations to you, manufacturers, for using methods other than sterility testing to confirm container and closure system integrity as a part of the stability protocol for sterile biological products, human and animal drugs, and medical devices. This guidance document finalizes the draft guidance of the same title dated January 1998 (January 28, 1998, 63 Federal Register (FR) 4272).

Manufacturers of drugs and biologics purporting to be sterile must test each batch or lot, as the case may be, to ensure that the product in question conforms to sterility requirements. 21 CFR 211.167(a); 21 CFR 610.12. Such drugs and biologics are also subject to stability testing requirements. 21 CFR 211.166. The stability testing requirements include maintaining a written testing program designed to assess stability characteristics. Manufacturers of medical devices must validate processes, including sterilization for a device purporting to be sterile. 21 CFR 820.75. Stability testing should be part of the design validation of such devices. In vitro diagnostic products for human use are required to be labeled with stability information. 21 CFR 809.10. For products labeled as sterile, we consider sterility to be a stability characteristic.

The purpose of stability testing is to provide evidence on how the quality of a substance or product varies with time under the influence of a variety of environmental factors such as temperature, humidity, and light, which enables you to establish or modify recommended storage conditions, retest periods, and shelf life or dating period, as the case may be. This guidance document applies only to the replacement of the sterility test with an appropriate container and closure system integrity test in the stability written testing program (referred to in this guidance as the "stability protocol"), recommending an alternative to sterility testing for supporting the continued capability of containers to maintain sterility. The guidance document does not apply to sterility testing methods for product sterility testing prior to release, as container and closure system integrity tests cannot demonstrate a product's initial sterility.

This guidance document provides information that we recommend you consider when you propose using alternative methods to sterility testing to confirm the integrity of a container and closure system throughout the product's shelf life or dating period. The recommendations in this guidance document apply to both pre- and post-approval stability protocols for sterile biological products, human and animal drugs, including investigational and bulk drugs. For medical devices, the recommendations in this guidance document apply to stability protocols for those devices labeled as sterile.

If you currently perform sterility testing as a stability-indicating test as part of a stability protocol, you may continue to do so. If your product is approved for an alternative to sterility testing as a component of your stability protocol, this document is not intended to recommend additional testing requirements.

FDA's guidance documents, including this guidance, do not establish legally enforceable responsibilities. Instead, guidances describe the FDA's current thinking on a topic and should be viewed only as recommendations, unless specific regulatory or statutory requirements are cited. The use of the word should in FDA's guidances means that something is suggested or recommended, but not required.

1 This guidance document was prepared by an intercenter working group with representatives from the Center for Biologics Evaluation and Research, Center for Devices and Radiological Health, Center for Drug Evaluation and Research, and the Center for Veterinary Medicine.

2 "Dating period" being the term used for biologics, as defined at 21 CFR 600.3(l), and "shelf life" being the term used for other drugs.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Container and Closure System Integrity (容器密封系統完整性): 指容器與密封件組成的系統，具備防止微生物侵入、維持產品無菌狀態的能力。這是 CCI (Container Closure Integrity) 的核心概念，後續在 USP <1207> 系列中被全面系統化。

Stability Protocol (安定性計畫): 依據 21 CFR 211.166，製造商須維護書面安定性測試計畫。本指引僅針對此計畫中的無菌試驗部分，建議以 CCIT 替代。

Sterility as a Stability Characteristic (無菌性作為安定性特性): FDA 明確將無菌性定位為安定性特性之一，意味著安定性計畫必須包含確認產品在效期內持續維持無菌的措施。

比喻說明 Analogy

想像你把食物放進真空密封袋保存在冰箱裡。安定性測試就像定期檢查食物是否變質（溫度、外觀、氣味），而「無菌性作為安定性特性」意味著你也必須定期確認密封袋沒有破損或漏氣。與其每次都打開袋子取樣培養（sterility test——一旦打開就破壞了密封），不如用儀器檢測密封袋的完整性（CCIT）更合理。

重點提示 Key Notes

關鍵限制——不適用於放行無菌試驗：本 Guidance 明確指出 CCIT 不能取代產品放行前的無菌試驗（Release Sterility Testing, USP <71>）。原因是 CCIT 只能證明「容器沒有洩漏」，無法證明「產品本身是無菌的」。放行無菌試驗驗證的是製程本身是否成功產出無菌產品。

適用範圍廣泛：涵蓋生物製品、人用藥品、動物用藥品、醫療器械，以及核准前與核准後的安定性計畫，包括 IND (Investigational New Drug) 階段的試驗用藥。

實務應用 Practical Application

CDMO 安定性計畫設計：對於無菌充填代工廠而言，本指引意味著安定性計畫中每個時間點（如 3M, 6M, 9M, 12M, 18M, 24M, 36M）原本需要執行的 Sterility Test 可以改為 CCIT。這帶來多重好處：

  • 節省樣品：Sterility Test 是破壞性測試，每次需耗用 20 個以上單位；CCIT 若採用非破壞性方法，測試後樣品可繼續用於其他安定性項目
  • 縮短時程：Sterility Test 至少需要 14 天培養期；CCIT 可在數分鐘至數小時內完成
  • 降低偽陽性風險：Sterility Test 的偽陽性（操作污染）是業界長期痛點，CCIT 排除了此變數

  2. 一家 CDMO 同時代工生物製品與小分子藥品，本指引是否兩者皆適用？法規依據為何？

II. Introduction 導論

原文內容 Original Text

Products labeled as sterile are expected to be free from viable microbial contamination throughout the product's entire shelf life or dating period. For products labeled as sterile, we consider sterility to be a stability characteristic. As a result, the stability protocol should include confirmation of continuing sterility throughout the product's shelf life or dating period. The minimum sterility testing generally performed as a component of the stability protocol for sterile products is at the initial time point (release) and final testing interval (i.e., expiration). Additional testing is often performed at appropriate intervals within this time period (e.g., annually). However, as discussed below sterility tests for the purpose of demonstrating continuing sterility have limitations, with respect to the method's reliability, accuracy, and the conclusions that may be derived from the results. Because of the limitations of sterility tests described below, sterility tests are not recommended as a component of a stability program for confirming the continued sterility throughout a product's shelf life or dating period. Alternative methods may be more reliable in confirming the integrity of the container and closure system as a component of the stability protocol for sterile products.

This guidance document does not suggest specific test methods and acceptance criteria (except for references to USP methods), nor does it provide comprehensive lists of tests. You should determine these details based on good scientific principles for each specific container and closure system taking into consideration particular product formulations and, where applicable, routes of administration.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Continuing Sterility (持續無菌性): 產品自放行至效期結束的整個期間，必須維持無菌狀態。這不是一次性的證明，而是持續性的保證。安定性計畫的無菌相關測試就是為了提供此保證。

Sterility Test 的局限性 (Limitations): FDA 在此處預告了後續 Section IV 將詳述的內容——Sterility Test 在可靠性、準確性和結論推導上存在根本性限制。這是推動 CCIT 替代的科學基礎。

比喻說明 Analogy

傳統的安定性無菌試驗就像「定期開門檢查冰箱裡的食物有沒有壞掉」——每次開門都有讓外部細菌進入的風險（偽陽性），而且你只能在打開的那一刻看到狀況。CCIT 則像是在冰箱門上裝了溫度感測器和密封監測器，不需要開門就能確認密封狀態是否完好。

重點提示 Key Notes

測試頻率基線：FDA 指出安定性計畫中 Sterility Test 的最低要求是在「初始時間點（放行）」和「最終測試區間（效期）」執行，中間通常每年測試一次。這設定了 CCIT 替代後應維持的最低頻率基準。

方法不做具體指定：FDA 刻意不指定具體的 CCIT 方法或接受標準，將此責任留給製造商依科學原則決定。這與 PIC/S Annex 1 (2022) Section 8.21 的理念一致——「容器密封完整性應使用經驗證且適當靈敏的方法確認」**(PIC/S Annex 1 2022, Section 8.21)**。

USP <1207> 的角色：雖然 FDA 在 2008 年發布此 Guidance 時 USP <1207> 尚在發展中（2016 年正式發布），但現今 USP <1207> 已成為選擇和驗證 CCIT 方法的主要技術參考，將容器密封完整性測試方法分為確定性方法 (Deterministic Methods) 與概率性方法 (Probabilistic Methods)。

III. Definitions 定義

原文內容 Original Text

The definitions presented here are for the purposes of this guidance only.

A container and closure system refers to the entirety of packaging components that together contain and protect the product.

A packaging component means any single part of a container and closure system. Typical components are containers (e.g., ampules, vials, bottles), container liners, closures (e.g., screw caps, stoppers), closure liners, stopper overseals, container inner seals, administration ports (e.g., on large-volume parenterals), overwraps, administration accessories, and container labels.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 Key Concepts

Container and Closure System (容器密封系統): 不僅僅是「瓶子加蓋子」，而是所有包裝組件的總和。FDA 使用「entirety of packaging components」一詞，強調 CCI 必須考慮系統整體，而非單一組件。

Packaging Component (包裝組件): 容器密封系統中的任何單一部件。FDA 列舉了完整的範例清單：

  • 容器 (Containers): Ampules (安瓿)、Vials (西林瓶)、Bottles (瓶)
  • 密封件 (Closures): Screw Caps (螺旋蓋)、Stoppers (膠塞)
  • 輔助組件: Container Liners (內襯)、Closure Liners (蓋墊)、Stopper Overseals (鋁蓋/翻蓋)、Administration Ports (給藥口)、Overwraps (外包裝)、Container Labels (標籤)

比喻說明 Analogy

Container and Closure System 就像「餐廳的餐具系統」——不只是碗和蓋子，還包括保鮮膜、密封條、外包裝盒等所有保護食物的組件。你不會只檢查碗有沒有裂，而忽略保鮮膜有沒有破洞。同理，CCIT 必須評估整個系統的完整性，而非僅測試單一組件。

重點提示 Key Notes

定義的實務意義：此定義直接影響 CCIT 方法的選擇。例如：

  • Vial system (西林瓶系統): 包含 Glass Vial + Rubber Stopper + Aluminum Crimp Seal。CCIT 必須能檢測所有三個組件交界面的洩漏
  • Pre-filled Syringe (預充填注射器): 包含 Glass Barrel + Plunger Stopper + Tip Cap/Needle Shield。每個接合面都是潛在洩漏路徑
  • Bag system (軟袋系統): 包含 Film Body + Port + Overwrap。焊接處和 Port 接合處是關鍵測試點

PDA Technical Report No. 27 (Pharmaceutical Package Integrity) 對各類容器系統的 CCI 測試方法有更詳細的技術指引 **(PDA TR27)**。

實務應用 Practical Application

CDMO 容器密封系統清單管理：作為 CDMO，你可能為不同客戶使用多種容器密封系統。建議建立一份完整的容器密封系統矩陣，包含：

  • 每種產品使用的容器密封系統組件清單
  • 各系統對應的已驗證 CCIT 方法
  • 測試參數和接受標準
  • Bracketing Matrix (分群矩陣) 的適用性評估——相同容器類型的產品是否可以共用驗證數據（參見 Section VII 及 ICH Q1D）

本節重點回顧 Section Summary

  • 核心主張：FDA 建議在安定性計畫中以 CCIT 取代 Sterility Test，因為後者存在可靠性、準確性和結論推導上的根本限制
  • 適用範疇：涵蓋生物製品、人用/動物用藥品、醫療器械的核准前與核准後安定性計畫，但不適用於放行無菌試驗
  • 法規依據：21 CFR 211.166 (安定性測試)、21 CFR 211.167 (放行測試)、21 CFR 610.12 (生物製品無菌試驗) 構成法規框架
  • 定義澄清：Container and Closure System 是所有包裝組件的整體，CCIT 必須評估整個系統而非單一組件
  • 方法靈活性：FDA 不指定具體方法，製造商依科學原則選擇適合其容器系統的 CCIT 方法（現可參考 USP <1207> 分類架構）
  • CDMO 啟示：CCIT 替代可節省安定性樣品、縮短測試時程、降低偽陽性風險，是安定性計畫優化的重要契機
