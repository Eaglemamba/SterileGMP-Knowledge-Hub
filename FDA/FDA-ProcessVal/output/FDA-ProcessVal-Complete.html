I

Introduction — What This Guidance Represents

導論 — 本指引的定位與性質

FDA Guidance Nature / 本指引的法律性質

This guidance represents the Food and Drug Administration's (FDA's) current thinking on this topic. It does not create or confer any rights for or on any person and does not operate to bind FDA or the public. You can use an alternative approach if the approach satisfies the requirements of the applicable statutes and regulations.

本文件代表FDA對本主題的現行思維，不具法律強制效力，但反映FDA的期望。若採用替代方法且能符合相關法規要求，亦可被接受。

Original Text (FDA, January 2011)

This guidance outlines the general principles and approaches that FDA considers appropriate elements of process validation for the manufacture of human and animal drug and biological products, including active pharmaceutical ingredients (APIs or drug substances), collectively referred to in this guidance as drugs or products. This guidance incorporates principles and approaches that all manufacturers can use to validate manufacturing processes.

This guidance aligns process validation activities with a product lifecycle concept and with existing FDA guidance, including the FDA/International Conference on Harmonisation (ICH) guidances for industry, Q8(R2) Pharmaceutical Development, Q9 Quality Risk Management, and Q10 Pharmaceutical Quality System. Although this guidance does not repeat the concepts and principles explained in those guidances, FDA encourages the use of modern pharmaceutical development concepts, quality risk management, and quality systems at all stages of the manufacturing process lifecycle.

The lifecycle concept links product and process development, qualification of the commercial manufacturing process, and maintenance of the process in a state of control during routine commercial production. This guidance supports process improvement and innovation through sound science.

Guidance Scope — What Is Covered

  • Human drugs
  • Veterinary drugs
  • Biological and biotechnology products
  • Finished products and active pharmaceutical ingredients (APIs or drug substances)
  • The drug constituent of a combination (drug and medical device) product

What Is NOT Covered

  • Type A medicated articles and medicated feed
  • Medical devices (separate guidance applies)
  • Dietary supplements
  • Human tissues intended for transplantation under section 361 of the PHS Act
  • Automated process control systems (computer hardware/software validation — addressed separately)
  • Regulatory submission content requirements (refer to appropriate Center guidance)
Key Statement: "FDA encourages the use of modern pharmaceutical development concepts, quality risk management, and quality systems at all stages of the manufacturing process lifecycle."

中文解說  |  本指引的核心定位

什麼是「現行思維」(Current Thinking)？

FDA指引文件在法律上屬於「非約束性建議」(nonbinding recommendations)，但在法規實務中，FDA查廠官員會以此作為判斷標準。簡單說：不照做可以，但你必須有充分的科學理由。對CDMO而言，這類指引實際上等同於強制要求。

為何要對齊ICH Q8/Q9/Q10？

2011年之前，FDA與ICH的驗證思維存在落差。2011版指引的重要性在於將製程驗證「嵌入」到ICH的品質系統框架中：

  • ICH Q8(R2)：製藥開發 — 設計空間、PAT
  • ICH Q9：品質風險管理 — 識別、評估、控制風險
  • ICH Q10：藥品品質系統 — 持續改善、流程卓越

三者合力支撐「生命週期驗證」(lifecycle validation)概念。

比喻說明：驗證就像蓋房子

Stage 1（設計）= 建築師繪製設計圖，確立施工規範。
Stage 2（確效）= 建商實際施工並驗收，確認房子符合設計。
Stage 3（持續）= 屋主定期維護、監測結構，確保房子長期安全。

1987年的舊思維只重視「驗收」(Stage 2)，卻忽略了設計品質與長期監測。

適用範圍的重要邊界

本指引不涵蓋電腦化系統驗證(CSV)、醫療器材製程驗證、或膳食補充品。但它明確適用於：

  • 無菌注射劑（小瓶、預充式注射器）
  • 原料藥(API)製程
  • 生物製劑（含抗體、疫苗）

對於無菌充填CDMO而言，本指引是最核心的法規文件之一。

Background — Why FDA Updated the 1987 Guidance

背景沿革 — 為何FDA要更新1987年指引

Original Text

In the Federal Register of May 11, 1987 (52 FR 17638), FDA issued a notice announcing the availability of a guidance entitled Guideline on General Principles of Process Validation (the 1987 guidance). Since then, we have obtained additional experience through our regulatory oversight that allows us to update our recommendations to industry on this topic.

This revised guidance conveys FDA's current thinking on process validation and is consistent with basic principles first introduced in the 1987 guidance. The revised guidance also provides recommendations that reflect some of the goals of FDA's initiative entitled "Pharmaceutical CGMPs for the 21st Century — A Risk-Based Approach," particularly with regard to the use of technological advances in pharmaceutical manufacturing, as well as implementation of modern risk management and quality system tools and concepts.

Key Statement: "This revised guidance replaces the 1987 guidance."

FDA has the authority and responsibility to inspect and evaluate process validation performed by manufacturers. The CGMP regulations for validating pharmaceutical (drug) manufacturing require that drug products be produced with a high degree of assurance of meeting all the attributes they are intended to possess.

Regulatory Authority Citations

  • 21 CFR 211.100(a) — Written procedures; deviations. Requires written procedures for production and process controls, and documentation that instructions were followed.
  • 21 CFR 211.110(a) — Sampling and testing of in-process materials and drug products. Requires control procedures to monitor output and validate performance of manufacturing processes that may be responsible for causing variability.

中文解說  |  1987→2011年的思維轉變

1987年舊指引的局限

1987年版的製程驗證指引將驗證視為一個「時間點事件」(point-in-time event)：在商業生產開始前，做3批次的驗證批次(PPQ runs)，然後就算完成。這個思維的問題在於：

  • 驗證完成後，製程是否仍維持受控狀態？沒有持續監測機制
  • 原料供應商改變、設備老化、人員更替 — 這些變化對製程的衝擊未被追蹤
  • 過度依賴批次放行檢測，而非過程理解

21世紀cGMP計畫的影響

FDA在2002年啟動「21世紀cGMP：風險導向方法」(Pharmaceutical CGMPs for the 21st Century — A Risk-Based Approach)計畫，這是2011年指引的直接前身。關鍵變化：

  • 舊思維：製程驗證 = 合規義務，做完就放
  • 新思維：製程驗證 = 過程知識積累，持續改善
  • 工具面：引入PAT（過程分析技術）、設計空間、QbD

21 CFR 211.100(a) vs 211.110(a) — 實務差異

211.100(a) 管的是「有沒有書面程序」— 重點在文件化。查廠時，FDA會要求看SOP、批次製造紀錄(BMR)。

211.110(a) 管的是「有沒有監控製程變異」— 重點在科學證據。查廠時，FDA會要求看IPT(In-Process Testing)數據、統計製程管制(SPC)圖表、OOS調查。

CDMO的稽核準備應將這兩條規範分開準備應對策略。

Process Validation and Drug Quality

製程驗證與藥品品質 — 為何不能靠終端檢測

Original Text

Effective process validation contributes significantly to assuring drug quality. The basic principle of quality assurance is that a drug should be produced that is fit for its intended use. This principle incorporates the understanding that the following conditions exist:

  • Quality, safety, and efficacy are designed or built into the product.
  • Quality cannot be adequately assured merely by in-process and finished-product inspection or testing.
  • Each step of a manufacturing process is controlled to assure that the finished product meets all quality attributes including specifications.
Fundamental Principle: "Quality cannot be adequately assured merely by in-process and finished-product inspection or testing."

This statement represents a foundational shift in pharmaceutical quality philosophy — moving from testing quality in to building quality in.

Three Pillars of Quality Assurance

中文解說  |  品質不是「檢驗」出來的

核心哲學轉變：Testing vs. Building

這三個原則代表製藥業最重要的品質哲學轉變：

  • 舊思維（Testing-in）：生產完成後，大量取樣檢測，合格就放行 — 就像用考試成績來「測量」學習成效
  • 新思維（Building-in）：品質在設計階段就被建入，製程受控，每批次都是合格品 — 就像「設計好的教學系統讓每個學生都能學會」

對無菌注射劑而言，這特別重要，因為無菌性(sterility)無法靠破壞性取樣全面確認。

為何「終端檢測」有根本性局限？

以10,000支注射劑批次為例：

  • USP無菌檢測通常抽樣20支 — 覆蓋率僅0.2%
  • 若污染率為0.1%（即10支），抽樣可能完全漏掉
  • 內毒素(endotoxin)分布不均，局部超標可能被稀釋掉
  • 微量雜質(sub-visible particles)計數抽樣具統計不確定性

因此，「受控的製程」比「大量的檢測」提供更強的品質保證。

「Fit for Intended Use」的法規意義

「適合其預定用途」是GMP的核心定義，源自ICH Q6A/Q6B。在實務中，這意味著：

  • 臨床患者能獲得一致劑量的藥品
  • 無菌製劑對免疫缺陷患者不引入感染風險
  • 外觀、性狀符合患者可接受性
  • 穩定性符合整個效期的使用需求

餐廳比喻

想像一家餐廳：

  • 舊做法：廚師隨意烹飪，服務生在上菜前每道菜都試味道，不合格就扔掉 — 浪費大、漏網率高
  • 新做法：標準化食譜+訓練有素的廚師+標準化備料流程+溫控設備 — 每道菜從一開始就是合格的

製程驗證就是確保「餐廳系統」（而非「試吃員」）保證食品安全。

練習思考 Practice Questions

  1. 請用具體數字說明「終端檢測無法保證品質」的局限性。以一個10,000支無菌注射劑批次為例，假設污染率為0.05%，統計上取樣20支的「漏網」概率是多少？
  2. 「Quality is built in」的原則如何體現在您廠區的某一個具體製程步驟？請舉例說明。
  3. 若客戶要求CDMO在批次放行前增加更多終端檢測項目，您會如何用本指引的邏輯回應？
B

Approach to Process Validation — Definition & Three-Stage Lifecycle

製程驗證方法論 — 定義與三階段生命週期

FDA Official Definition of Process Validation / 製程驗證官方定義

"Process validation is defined as the collection and evaluation of data, from the process design stage through commercial production, which establishes scientific evidence that a process is capable of consistently delivering quality product."

「製程驗證定義為：從製程設計階段到商業生產過程中，資料的收集與評估，以建立科學證據，證明製程能夠一致地生產出品質合格的產品。」

Three-Stage Lifecycle Model

Process validation involves a series of activities taking place over the lifecycle of the product and process. This guidance describes process validation activities in three stages:

  • Stage 1 — Process Design: The commercial manufacturing process is defined during this stage based on knowledge gained through development and scale-up activities.
  • Stage 2 — Process Qualification: During this stage, the process design is evaluated to determine if the process is capable of reproducible commercial manufacturing.
  • Stage 3 — Continued Process Verification: Ongoing assurance is gained during routine production that the process remains in a state of control.

This guidance describes activities typical of each stage, but in practice, some activities might occur in multiple stages.

Key Requirement: "Before any batch from the process is commercially distributed for use by consumers, a manufacturer should have gained a high degree of assurance in the performance of the manufacturing process such that it will consistently produce APIs and drug products meeting those attributes relating to identity, strength, quality, purity, and potency."

The assurance should be obtained from objective information and data from laboratory-, pilot-, and/or commercial-scale studies. Information and data should demonstrate that the commercial manufacturing process is capable of consistently producing acceptable quality products within commercial manufacturing conditions.

中文解說  |  定義逐字拆解

定義的五個關鍵詞

  1. Collection and evaluation of data（收集與評估資料）— 強調科學數據，非主觀判斷
  2. From process design stage（從製程設計階段起）— 驗證從開發就開始，不是生產前才開始
  3. Through commercial production（貫穿商業生產）— 上市後仍持續
  4. Scientific evidence（科學證據）— 可量化、可重現、有統計支撐
  5. Capable of consistently delivering（能夠一致地提供）— 強調「一致性」，單批成功不算驗證

Identity, Strength, Quality, Purity, Potency

「ISQPP」五個品質屬性來自FD&C Act第501條，是判斷藥品是否達標的法定標準：

  • Identity（鑑別）：確認是聲稱的活性成分
  • Strength（效價/含量）：主成分含量符合標示
  • Quality（品質）：整體品質符合預定標準
  • Purity（純度）：無不可接受的雜質
  • Potency（效力）：具有適當的治療活性（生物製劑尤為重要）

「Some activities might occur in multiple stages」的實務意義

FDA承認三個階段在實務中有重疊。例如：

  • 製程設計(Stage 1)的Scale-up數據可能同時作為Stage 2的支撐證據
  • Stage 2的PPQ批次數據可為Stage 3的基準建立提供依據
  • Stage 3發現的變異可能觸發回到Stage 1的製程重新設計

這是「生命週期」概念的精髓——不是單向線性流程，而是持續回饋的循環。

Stage 1 — Process Design: Key Activities

  • Define Critical Quality Attributes (CQAs)
  • Identify Critical Process Parameters (CPPs) and their ranges
  • Conduct risk assessment to identify variability sources
  • Perform Design of Experiments (DoE) studies at lab and/or pilot scale
  • Establish the design space (if applicable, per ICH Q8)
  • Develop process understanding through development studies
  • Scale-up activities — from lab to pilot to commercial scale
  • Establish control strategy

Stage 2 — Process Qualification: Key Activities

  • Qualification of equipment, utilities, facilities (IQ/OQ)
  • Process Performance Qualification (PPQ) — commercial-scale runs
  • Demonstrate reproducibility across multiple batches
  • Evaluate worst-case conditions (if applicable)
  • Confirm all CQAs are met consistently
  • Generate data sufficient to support commercial distribution

Stage 3 — Continued Process Verification: Key Activities

  • Ongoing statistical monitoring of process performance
  • Annual Product Review (APR) / Product Quality Review (PQR)
  • Statistical Process Control (SPC) charts for CPPs and CQAs
  • Investigation of adverse trends before they become OOS
  • Maintain validated state through change control
  • Evaluate impact of material, equipment, process changes

中文解說  |  三階段實務對照

Stage 1 在CDMO的實務

對CDMO而言，Stage 1通常由客戶（藥廠研發部門）主導，CDMO承接時已有：

  • 製程描述與開發報告
  • 初步CQA清單
  • 小試/中試數據

CDMO的責任是對這些知識進行技術轉移評估，確認在商業設備/設施條件下可重現。這個評估本身就是Stage 1知識的一部分。

Stage 2 — PPQ批次數的科學判斷

2011年指引最大的突破之一：廢除「必須3批」的硬性規定，改為「科學判斷」。影響PPQ批次數的因素：

  • 製程複雜度與變異程度
  • 批次間的一致性統計分析
  • 先前的開發數據充分性
  • 關鍵步驟的風險等級

實務上，仍以3批為起點，但高風險製程可能需要更多，低複雜度/已有充分先例的製程可能較少。

Stage 3 — 從「義務」到「競爭優勢」

Stage 3（持續製程驗證）是許多公司最薄弱的環節，也是FDA近年查廠重點。優秀的Stage 3系統應包括：

  • 即時製程監控儀表板
  • SPC管制圖（Shewhart, CUSUM）
  • 製程能力指數(Cpk)定期評估
  • 趨勢分析觸發調查機制

對CDMO而言，出色的Stage 3系統是吸引高端客戶的差異化優勢。

生命週期的「回饋迴路」

三個階段不是單向流動的，而是有回饋機制：

  • Stage 3發現產品趨勢異常 → 觸發CAPA → 可能需要回到Stage 1重新評估CPP
  • Stage 2的PPQ數據 → 建立Stage 3的統計控制基準
  • Stage 1的設計空間 → 定義Stage 3的監控範圍與警戒限

這個回饋機制確保「驗證知識」不會在商業生產開始後就停止積累。

Understanding Variation — The Scientific Foundation

理解變異 — 製程驗證的科學基礎

Original Text

A successful validation program depends upon information and knowledge from product and process development. This knowledge and understanding is the basis for establishing an approach to control of the manufacturing process that results in products with the desired quality attributes. Manufacturers should:

  • Understand the sources of variation
  • Detect the presence and degree of variation
  • Understand the impact of variation on the process and ultimately on product attributes
  • Control the variation in a manner commensurate with the risk it represents to the process and product

Each manufacturer should judge whether it has gained sufficient understanding to provide a high degree of assurance in its manufacturing process to justify commercial distribution of the drug product.

Key Concept: Variation control must be "commensurate with the risk it represents" — this is the direct integration of ICH Q9 risk management principles into process validation.

中文解說  |  變異管理四步驟

四步驟變異管理框架

FDA要求的四步驟，對應到實際工具：

  • 1. 理解變異來源 → Ishikawa魚骨圖、FMEA風險評估、DoE實驗設計
  • 2. 偵測變異的存在與程度 → IPT（過程中測試）、PAT（過程分析技術）、SPC管制圖
  • 3. 理解變異對產品的衝擊 → 多變量分析、製程能力分析、相關性研究
  • 4. 按風險等級控制變異 → 基於ICH Q9的控制策略、製程規格、警戒限與行動限

「Commensurate with Risk」的精髓

這句話代表FDA接受風險比例原則：不是每個變異來源都需要相同程度的控制。例如：

  • 高風險：直接影響無菌性的過濾步驟 → 100%完整性測試
  • 中風險：影響含量均勻度的混合時間 → 統計抽樣監控
  • 低風險：標籤外觀印刷位置偏差 → 定期目視抽查

資源有限時，這個原則幫助您把驗證心力放在最重要的地方。

「High Degree of Assurance」如何量化？

本指引沒有定義「高度保證」的數字標準，但業界通常接受：

  • 製程能力：Cpk ≥ 1.33（長期能力），Ppk ≥ 1.33（PPQ批次）
  • 拒絕率：PPQ批次OOS率 = 0，或統計上可接受
  • 批間一致性：關鍵屬性的批間CV值在預定範圍內
  • 趨勢穩定性：SPC管制圖無非隨機模式

最終由製造商的品質系統和科學判斷決定「足夠的保證」。

變異管理：天氣預報的比喻

製程中的變異就像天氣變化：

  • 理解來源= 氣象學家研究氣壓、濕度、洋流
  • 偵測程度= 溫度計、雨量計、氣象雷達
  • 理解衝擊= 預測台風路徑對農業的損失
  • 按風險控制= 台風警報（高風險）vs 陰天提醒（低風險）

沒有人期望完全消除天氣變化，但好的氣象系統能讓我們「在受控範圍內」應對變化。

CDMO是「持有驗證責任」的製造商

在FDA的眼中，CDMO是製造商(manufacturer)，對其設施和製程的驗證狀態負有直接責任。客戶（持牌人/MAH）同樣有責任確保其委外製造符合法規，但不能完全將責任轉嫁給CDMO。

實務上，CDMO通常：

  • 擁有設施、設備的驗證文件（Stage 2 IQ/OQ）
  • 與客戶共同擁有/管理製程驗證文件（PPQ, Stage 3）
  • 透過品質協議(QA)明確劃分雙方責任

技術轉移是Stage 1的核心

對CDMO而言，技術轉移(Technology Transfer)本質上就是Stage 1的「製程設計知識獲取」過程。關鍵的轉移元素：

  • 製程描述與流程圖(PFD)
  • CQA/CPP清單與規格
  • 控制策略文件(Control Strategy)
  • 分析方法與驗證報告
  • 批次紀錄(BMR)模板
  • 材料平衡與批次計算

技術轉移評估報告(TTR)是Stage 1完成的關鍵交付物。

多客戶廠區的矩陣驗證策略

無菌充填CDMO通常採用矩陣法(Matrix/Bracketing)進行設備確效，避免對每個產品重複進行全套IQ/OQ。關鍵原則：

  • 選擇「最差情況」產品代表整個產品組
  • 容量範圍(fill volume range)矩陣設計
  • 容器類型(vial, PFS, cartridge)分別驗證
  • 充填速度範圍的邊界驗證

新客戶產品加入時，需評估是否在已驗證矩陣範圍內，若超出則觸發補充驗證。

CDMO與客戶的驗證責任：共管帳戶

可將CDMO-客戶驗證責任比喻為銀行的「共管帳戶」：

  • 雙方都需要「簽名」才能進行重大操作（如製程變更）
  • CDMO管理「帳戶基礎設施」（設備、設施、環境）
  • 客戶提供「存款知識」（開發數據、品質標準）
  • 品質協議是「帳戶合約」

任何一方的疏失都可能危及整個帳戶（批次/產品的合規狀態）。

Statutory Requirements for Process Validation

製程驗證的法定要求  |  FDCA § 501(a)(2)(B) & cGMP Framework

Original Text — FDA Guidance (January 2011)

Process validation for drugs (finished pharmaceuticals and components) is a legally enforceable requirement under section 501(a)(2)(B) of the Act (21 U.S.C. 351(a)(2)(B)), which states the following:

A drug . . . shall be deemed to be adulterated . . . if . . . the methods used in, or the facilities or controls used for, its manufacture, processing, packing, or holding do not conform to or are not operated or administered in conformity with current good manufacturing practice to assure that such drug meets the requirements of this Act as to safety and has the identity and strength, and meets the quality and purity characteristics, which it purports or is represented to possess.

FDA regulations describing current good manufacturing practice (CGMP) for finished pharmaceuticals are provided in 21 CFR parts 210 and 211.

The CGMP regulations require that manufacturing processes be designed and controlled to assure that in-process materials and the finished product meet predetermined quality requirements and do so consistently and reliably. Process validation is required, in both general and specific terms, by the CGMP regulations in parts 210 and 211.

§ 211.100(a) — "There shall be written procedures for production and process control designed to assure that the drug products have the identity, strength, quality, and purity they purport or are represented to possess."

This regulation requires manufacturers to design a process, including operations and controls, which results in a product meeting these attributes.

中文解說 & 實務應用

法律層級：為何製程驗證不可選擇？

許多人誤以為製程驗證只是「最佳實務」。事實上，FDCA 第 501(a)(2)(B) 條直接規定：若製造方法不符合 cGMP，藥品即被視為摻假品（adulterated）。這意味著：

  • 不符合 cGMP = 產品可被強制召回或扣押
  • FDA 可在無法院命令下發布警告信或拒絕進口
  • 在 CDMO 環境，合約若要求符合 cGMP，驗證義務自動適用

比喻：餐廳衛生法規 vs. 製藥 cGMP

就如同衛生主管機關規定餐廳必須控制食品溫度，而不只是「希望」廚師留意，FDA 的法律框架同樣要求製造商以系統化方式證明製程產出符合品質要求——不是靠運氣，而是靠設計。製程驗證就是這個「系統化證明」的機制。

CDMO 實務重點：法規責任歸屬

在委託製造場景中，法規責任仍由持有 NDA/ANDA 的藥廠（MAH）承擔，但 CDMO 作為實際製造方，必須提供完整的製程驗證數據。查廠時 FDA 會直接稽核 CDMO 設施，任何缺失都可能導致客戶產品無法出貨。

引導：考量 FDA 管轄範圍、數據可移植性、以及 21 CFR 211 對「製造商」的定義。

§100

21 CFR § 211.100(a) — Production and Process Controls

生產與製程管制  |  書面程序設計的法定根基

Regulatory Text & Context

The foundation for process validation is provided in § 211.100(a), which states:

"There shall be written procedures for production and process control designed to assure that the drug products have the identity, strength, quality, and purity they purport or are represented to possess..."

This regulation requires manufacturers to design a process, including operations and controls, which results in a product meeting these attributes.

The CGMP regulations require that manufacturing processes be:

  • Designed to meet predetermined quality requirements
  • Controlled to meet these requirements
  • Operated consistently and reliably

Process validation is required in both general and specific terms by the CGMP regulations in parts 210 and 211. Focusing exclusively on qualification efforts without also understanding the manufacturing process and associated variations may not lead to adequate assurance of quality.

Key principle: Manufacturers must design a process that reliably delivers the intended quality — not merely test whether a batch passes.

中文解說：「設計」vs.「測試」的根本差異

§211.100(a) 的核心哲學

這條法規強調的不是「最終測試合格」，而是製程設計本身必須確保品質。這是 FDA 從舊式「測試品質進產品（test quality in）」轉向「設計品質入產品（build quality in）」的法規基礎。

  • 舊思維：生產完成 → 測試 → 如果通過就放行
  • 新思維（FDA 要求）：了解製程 → 設計管制策略 → 製程本身確保品質 → 測試作為確認

比喻：橋梁結構工程

一座橋樑不是靠「每天用重車壓一次、沒倒就算安全」來驗證品質。工程師透過材料科學、載重計算與設計規範，確保橋梁在設計階段就具備足夠強度。藥品製程驗證的邏輯相同：理解製程，設計控制，然後以驗證數據確認設計是否有效。

無菌充填的實務意涵

在無菌充填操作中，§211.100(a) 意味著：灌裝速度、氮氣覆蓋、橡皮塞壓合力等參數不是隨意設定——每個書面程序都應有科學依據，能連結到產品的品質屬性（如無菌保證水準、密封完整性）。

思考練習 2

Q：您的工廠正在撰寫一個新針劑充填製程的批次製造記錄（BMR）。依據 §211.100(a)，哪些類型的製程參數應納入書面程序，且必須說明設定依據？

引導：考量 identity（含量）、strength（效價）、quality（外觀/pH/不溶性微粒）、purity（雜質）各自對應的製程步驟。

思考練習 3

Q：一位生產主管說：「我們上個月生產的 20 批都通過 QC 放行，這樣就算製程已驗證了嗎？」請用 §211.100(a) 的精神回應這個說法。

§110

21 CFR § 211.110 — In-Process Sampling & Specifications

製程中取樣與規格  |  管制變異的法規工具

Original Text — §211.110(a) & (b)

§ 211.110(a) — Sampling and testing of in-process materials and drug products requires that control procedures ". . . be established to monitor the output and to validate the performance of those manufacturing processes that may be responsible for causing variability in the characteristics of in-process material and the drug product."

Under this regulation, even well-designed processes must include in-process control procedures to assure final product quality.

In addition, the CGMP regulations regarding sampling set forth a number of requirements for validation:

  • Samples must represent the batch under analysis (§ 211.160(b)(3))
  • The sampling plan must result in statistical confidence (§ 211.165(c) and (d))
  • The batch must meet its predetermined specifications (§ 211.165(a))
§ 211.110(b) — Two principles for in-process specifications:
  1. In-process specifications "shall be consistent with drug product final specifications."
  2. In-process specifications "shall be derived from previous acceptable process average and process variability estimates where possible and determined by the application of suitable statistical procedures where appropriate."

This requirement establishes the need for manufacturers to analyze process performance and control batch-to-batch variability.

中文解說：製程中管制的雙重目的

§211.110(a)：為何「好設計」仍需製程中管制？

即使製程設計完善，原物料批次差異、設備磨損、人員操作差異都可能引入變異。FDA 明確要求製程中管制程序必須能：

  • 監測輸出（monitor output）：即時偵測偏差趨勢，不等到最終放行才發現
  • 驗證效能（validate performance）：持續確認製程運作在驗證條件內

對無菌充填而言，這涵蓋環境監控、容器完整性測試、裝量檢查等在線管制。

§211.110(b)：兩個原則的實務意義

原則一（與最終規格一致）：若最終產品 pH 規格為 6.8–7.2，製程中 pH 規格不得設為 6.5–7.5，否則中間品即使通過也可能在後續步驟漂移超出最終規格。

原則二（由統計推導）：製程中規格不能憑感覺設定，必須基於歷史製程平均值與變異估計。這直接呼應 Stage 3 持續製程查驗（CPV）所需的統計分析能力。

比喻：飛機飛行中的儀器監控

飛行前的地面檢查（類比驗證前的設計確認）固然重要，但飛行中駕駛必須持續監控高度、速度、引擎參數（類比製程中管制）。若只有起飛前的檢查而沒有飛行中監控，任何在途中出現的偏差都無法即時發現。§211.110(a) 要求的就是這個「飛行中監控」機制。

無菌充填 CDMO 具體應用

以凍乾注射劑為例，§211.110 在實務中的對應項目：

  • 充填重量：每隔 N 支抽查，規格需與最終含量規格統計一致
  • 凍乾終點：殘存水分的製程中測量（Karl Fischer），規格由歷史批次變異推導
  • 班次取樣計劃：需有統計依據，不能只抽「方便取樣」的位置

§180

21 CFR § 211.180(e) — Periodic Review & Process Maintenance

定期審查與製程維護  |  Stage 3 持續查驗的法規根基

Regulatory Basis for Continuous Process Monitoring

§ 211.180(e) requires that information and data about product quality and manufacturing experience be periodically reviewed to determine whether any changes to the established process are warranted.

Ongoing feedback about product quality and process performance is an essential feature of process maintenance.

In addition, the CGMP regulations also describe facilities and equipment requirements that are integral to process validation:

§ 211.42 — Facilities must be of suitable size, construction, and location to facilitate proper operations.
§ 211.63 — Equipment must be of appropriate design, adequate size, and suitably located to facilitate operations for its intended use.
§ 211.68 — Automated, mechanical, and electronic equipment must be calibrated, inspected, or checked according to a written program designed to assure proper performance.

Summary: The CGMP regulations require that manufacturing processes be designed and controlled to assure that in-process materials and the finished product meet predetermined quality requirements and do so consistently and reliably.

中文解說：製程驗證的「後半段」義務

§211.180(e) 與 Stage 3 CPV 的連結

這條法規是 FDA 2011 年指引中「Stage 3：持續製程查驗（Continued Process Verification, CPV）」的法規根據。它要求：

  • 定期（periodically）——不是只在有問題時才審查
  • 審查品質數據與製造經驗——包括 OOS/OOT 趨勢、客訴、批次拒絕率
  • 判斷是否需要變更——主動識別改善機會，而非被動等待問題

比喻：汽車定期保養 vs. 等待拋錨

§211.180(e) 的精神就像定期保養手冊：汽車製造商要求每 10,000 公里更換機油，不是因為引擎已經壞了，而是為了維持設計效能。製藥廠商同樣需要定期「健康檢查」其製程，確認它仍在驗證狀態（state of control）內運作。

設備相關條文（§211.42/63/68）的驗證意涵

這三條法規構成設備確認（IQ/OQ/PQ）的法規基礎：

  • §211.42 → 廠房設計 / 空調系統確認（HVAC qualification）
  • §211.63 → 設備設計確認（DQ）與安裝確認（IQ）
  • §211.68 → 校驗計劃（calibration program）/ 儀器確認（OQ）

在無菌充填環境，這三條法規同時支撐了潔淨室認證、充填機確認、以及所有量測儀器的校驗要求。

II

General Considerations for Process Validation

製程驗證的一般考量  |  科學理解、QbD 原則與風險導向方法

FDA's Foundational Framework

In all stages of the product lifecycle, good project management and good archiving that capture scientific knowledge will make the process validation program more effective and efficient.

The following practices should ensure uniform collection and assessment of information about the process and enhance the accessibility of such information later in the product lifecycle:

  • An integrated team approach to process validation that includes expertise from a variety of disciplines (process engineering, industrial pharmacy, analytical chemistry, microbiology, statistics, manufacturing, and quality assurance). Project plans, along with the full support of senior management, are essential elements for success.
  • Throughout the product lifecycle, various studies can be initiated to discover, observe, correlate, or confirm information about the product and process. All studies should be planned and conducted according to sound scientific principles, appropriately documented, and approved in accordance with established procedure appropriate for the stage of the lifecycle.
"Focusing exclusively on qualification efforts without also understanding the manufacturing process and associated variations may not lead to adequate assurance of quality."

After establishing and confirming the process, manufacturers must maintain the process in a state of control over the life of the process, even as materials, equipment, production environment, personnel, and manufacturing procedures change.

中文解說：從「批次思維」到「生命週期思維」

核心訊息：理解 > 確認（Understand > Qualify）

FDA 的這段文字是整個 2011 年指引的哲學核心：單純做確認（qualification）而不理解製程，不足以保證品質。

這對業界的衝擊在於：

  • 傳統做法：按固定格式完成 IQ/OQ/PQ，收到報告 = 驗證完成
  • FDA 期望：在驗證前就充分理解製程，知道哪些變數影響品質、影響程度如何

「所有材料、設備、環境、人員、程序都可能改變」

這句話特別重要：FDA 明確說明，即使原材料供應商更換、設備老化更新、操作員流動，製造商都有責任維持製程在管制狀態（state of control）。這是持續製程驗證（CPV）存在的根本原因，也是 CDMO 在技術轉移時必須建立完整製程理解的理由。

跨職能整合團隊 & 科學知識管理  |  製程驗證的組織基礎

FDA Requirements for Team Structure & Documentation

FDA recommends an integrated team approach to process validation that draws on multiple disciplines. The disciplines listed include:

  • Process engineering
  • Industrial pharmacy
  • Analytical chemistry
  • Microbiology
  • Statistics
  • Manufacturing (operations)
  • Quality assurance

Project plans, along with the full support of senior management, are essential elements for success.

中文解說：為何需要多學科整合？

比喻：航空器適航認證

一架新型飛機的適航認證需要機械工程師、氣動工程師、航電工程師、飛行員、管制官員共同參與，沒有任何單一職能可以獨立完成。製程驗證的跨職能要求出自同樣邏輯：任何單一部門都無法全面評估一個複雜製藥製程的所有風險面向。

CDMO 挑戰：高離職率與知識管理

FDA 強調「良好文件歸檔」的背後，是對一個現實的認知：人員流動（尤其在 CDMO 環境）會造成製程知識流失。當年做驗證的人離職後，接手者必須能從文件中重建足夠的製程理解。這使得「知識管理系統」成為製程驗證的隱形基礎建設。

Risk

Criticality as a Continuum: Risk-Based Thinking

關鍵性為連續譜：風險導向思維  |  ICH Q9 與 QbD 的實踐

FDA's Risk-Proportionate Approach

The terms attribute(s) and parameter(s) are not categorized with respect to criticality in this guidance. With a lifecycle approach to process validation that employs risk-based decision making throughout that lifecycle, the perception of criticality as a continuum rather than a binary state is more useful.

All attributes and parameters should be evaluated in terms of their:

  • Roles in the process
  • Impact on the product or in-process material
  • Reevaluated as new information becomes available

The degree of control over those attributes or parameters should be commensurate with their risk to the process and process output. A higher degree of control is appropriate for attributes or parameters that pose a higher risk.

"The Agency recognizes that terminology usage can vary and expects that each manufacturer will communicate the meaning and intent of its terminology and categorization to the Agency."

Many products are single-source or involve complicated manufacturing processes. Homogeneity within a batch and consistency between batches are goals of process validation activities. Validation offers assurance that a process is reasonably protected against sources of variability that could affect production output, cause supply problems, and negatively affect public health.

中文解說：打破「關鍵/非關鍵」的二元思維

「連續譜」思維的實務意義

傳統做法傾向於將製程參數分類為「關鍵（critical）」或「非關鍵（non-critical）」兩類，然後對「關鍵」項目施以嚴格管制，「非關鍵」項目則幾乎不管。FDA 在此明確說：這種二元分類是不夠用的。

更有用的框架是：

  • 每個參數都有一個影響分數（對 CQA 的衝擊）
  • 管制嚴格度與影響分數成比例
  • 隨著新數據累積，影響分數可以動態更新
CPP 關鍵製程參數
→
製程步驟 單元操作
→
CQA 關鍵品質屬性

CPP 的偏離 → 透過製程機制 → 影響 CQA 的符合性

比喻：飛機引擎的風險管理

引擎螺絲和客艙扶手螺絲都是「螺絲」，但風險等級天壤之別。沒有工程師會說「螺絲是關鍵零件，所以每顆螺絲都用同等級的質量管制」——而是按照失效後果嚴重性分配管制資源。製程參數的關鍵性評估邏輯完全相同。

「批內均一性 + 批間一致性」雙重目標

FDA 明確指出這兩者是製程驗證的目標：

  • 批內均一性（Homogeneity within a batch）：同一批內每個劑量單位含量、效力、外觀一致。對混合製程、充填製程尤為關鍵。
  • 批間一致性（Consistency between batches）：不同批次在相同條件下生產，性能表現一致。這是「再現性（reproducibility）」的法規要求。

QbD

QbD Principles, Design Space & Understanding Variability

品質源於設計原則、設計空間與變異性理解  |  ICH Q8/Q9/Q10 框架整合

Scientific Understanding as Validation Foundation

FDA's 2011 guidance aligns with the Quality by Design (QbD) framework established under ICH Q8/Q9/Q10. The core principles embedded in Sections I-II include:

  • Design of Experiments (DOE): Systematic studies to understand how process variables interact and impact product quality attributes.
  • Risk Analysis: Formal tools (FMEA, fishbone diagrams, risk ranking) to identify and prioritize sources of variability before validation begins.
  • Prior Knowledge: Platform knowledge, development data, clinical manufacturing data, and literature all inform the validation strategy.
  • Design Space: The multidimensional combination of input variables and process parameters that have been demonstrated to provide assurance of quality. Operating within the design space is not considered a change.
  • Control Strategy: The planned set of controls derived from current product and process understanding that ensures process performance and product quality.
Key insight from the guidance: "Process understanding is the foundation for reducing validation burden." Manufacturers who deeply understand their processes need fewer confirmatory experiments because they can predict how the process will behave under variation.

Manufacturers should use ongoing programs to collect and analyze product and process data to evaluate the state of control of the process. These programs may identify process or product problems or opportunities for process improvements.

Manufacturers of legacy products can take advantage of the knowledge gained from the original process development and qualification work as well as manufacturing experience to continually improve their processes. Implementation of these recommendations for legacy products and processes would likely begin with Stage 3 activities.

中文解說：QbD 如何降低驗證負擔？

QbD 框架核心三要素

  • 目標產品品質概況 (QTPP)：定義產品必須達到的品質特徵（用途、劑型、給藥途徑、效價等）
  • 關鍵品質屬性 (CQA)：從 QTPP 推導出必須管控的產品屬性（如含量均勻度、無菌性、可見微粒）
  • 關鍵製程參數 (CPP)：對 CQA 有顯著影響且必須加以監控/管制的製程參數
QTPP — 目標產品品質概況
↓
CQA — 關鍵品質屬性
↓
CPP — 關鍵製程參數 + 管制策略

「設計空間」與驗證的關係

ICH Q8 定義的設計空間（Design Space）是 QbD 的核心工具。當製造商能夠：

  1. 透過 DOE 證明在設計空間內操作均能滿足 CQA
  2. 建立穩健的製程設計空間

則在設計空間內的操作調整不視為法規意義上的製程變更（change），毋需補充驗證。這就是「理解製程 = 減少驗證負擔」的具體機制。

Legacy 產品的特別考量

許多已商業化的老產品（legacy products）當初並非以 QbD 框架開發，驗證文件可能只有三批 PPQ 報告，缺乏深層的製程理解文件。FDA 建議這類產品從 Stage 3（CPV）開始著手，逐步補充製程知識，而不是要求重做全部 Stage 1-2 活動。對 CDMO 而言，接手此類產品需評估現有文件的知識密度，判斷是否有足夠基礎支持後續製程管理。

比喻：地圖 vs. 衛星導航

傳統的三批 PPQ 驗證就像用一張舊地圖導航：你知道一條路，只要按照那條路走就能到目的地，但若路封閉了，你不知道替代路徑。QbD 框架建立的是衛星導航系統：你理解整個道路網絡（設計空間），因此任何合理的路徑調整都不需要重新「驗證整個旅程」。

識別方法 & 風險評估工具

如何識別 CQA？

  1. 從 QTPP 出發，列出所有可能的品質屬性
  2. 評估每個屬性若超出規格，對患者安全/療效的影響嚴重性
  3. 嚴重性高的屬性 = CQA（需監控且有明確規格）
  4. 例如：無菌注射劑的「無菌性」嚴重性 = 最高 → 必為 CQA

如何識別 CPP？

  1. 列出所有製程參數（每個單元操作的輸入變數）
  2. 透過 DOE 或科學推理，評估每個參數對各 CQA 的影響
  3. 影響顯著 = CPP；影響可忽略 = 一般製程參數（需記錄但管控強度較低）
  4. 管控強度與風險等級成比例（風險連續譜原則）

常用風險評估工具

  • Ishikawa 魚骨圖：識別所有潛在影響因素
  • FMEA（失效模式與效應分析）：量化風險優先指數（RPN）
  • 因果矩陣（C&E Matrix）：系統評估參數-屬性關聯性

An Important Nuance: Not All CPP Excursions Reject a Batch

It is important to distinguish between a CPP excursion and a CQA failure. A CPP out of range does not automatically mean the batch fails — it triggers a risk assessment to determine whether the out-of-range CPP actually impacted any CQA. This is where thorough process understanding (Stage 1 data) becomes critical: manufacturers who understand the quantitative relationship between CPP and CQA can make science-based batch disposition decisions.

一個重要區分：CPP 偏差 ≠ 批次拒絕

CPP 超出操作範圍不等於自動拒批。它觸發的是偏差調查 + 影響評估：該 CPP 的偏差是否實際影響了 CQA？這個評估需要充分的製程理解數據作基礎（Stage 1 研究的 CPP-CQA 關係曲線）。這正是「製程理解降低驗證負擔」的另一個維度：深度理解製程，讓批次拋棄決策更有科學依據，而非保守地拒絕一切有偏差的批次。

Stage 1: Process Design — Overview | 第一階段：製程設計概覽

FDA source text pp.11 | Defining the commercial manufacturing process

B. Stage 1 — Process Design (FDA Original Text)

"Process design is the activity of defining the commercial manufacturing process that will be reflected in planned master production and control records. The goal of this stage is to design a process suitable for routine commercial manufacturing that can consistently deliver a product that meets its quality attributes."

Stage 1 is where manufacturers make fundamental decisions about how a product will be manufactured at commercial scale. The outputs of Stage 1 are codified in the master production and control records (MPCRs) — the living documents that govern every commercial batch.

The two major sub-sections within Stage 1 are:

  1. Building and Capturing Process Knowledge and Understanding — the science-based learning phase
  2. Establishing a Strategy for Process Control — converting knowledge into a defensible control approach

Early process design experiments do not need to be performed under full CGMP conditions, but must follow sound scientific methods and good documentation practices, consistent with ICH Q10 Pharmaceutical Quality System.

中文解析：第一階段定義與地位

什麼是第一階段？

Stage 1（製程設計）是整個製程確效生命週期的「知識建構期」。在這個階段，您定義商業化生產製程——決定用哪些參數、哪個範圍、怎麼控制。

比喻：Stage 1 就像蓋房子前的「建築設計圖」階段。你在這裡決定結構、材料、承重規格，後續的施工（Stage 2）和居住監測（Stage 3）都依此進行。

三階段生命週期快速回顧

  • Stage 1 — 製程設計：建立製程知識與管控策略
  • Stage 2 — 製程確效：在商業規模證明製程可重現
  • Stage 3 — 持續製程驗證：上市後持續監控製程狀態

FDA的核心邏輯：Stage 1做得越扎實，Stage 2需要的批次數越少，Stage 3的問題也越少。

CGMP要求的例外情況

早期製程設計實驗（如實驗室小試）不需完整CGMP條件，但有一個重要例外：

病毒清除研究（Viral Clearance）和雜質清除研究（Impurity Clearance）即使在小規模進行，也必須有品質單位（QU）的監督，確保方法的科學性和結論的可信度。

原因：這類研究直接推算商業規模的產品品質，馬虎不得。

Building and Capturing Process Knowledge | 建立與記錄製程知識

FDA source text pp.11–12 | Sources of knowledge, variability, and documentation

1. Building and Capturing Process Knowledge and Understanding

Product development activities provide key inputs to the process design stage, such as the intended dosage form, the quality attributes, and a general manufacturing pathway. Process information available from product development activities can be leveraged in the process design stage.

The functionality and limitations of commercial manufacturing equipment should be considered in the process design, as well as predicted contributions to variability posed by:

  • Different component lots
  • Production operators
  • Environmental conditions
  • Measurement systems in the production setting

However, the full spectrum of input variability typical of commercial production is not generally known at this stage. Laboratory or pilot-scale models designed to be representative of the commercial process can be used to estimate variability.

It is essential that activities and studies resulting in process understanding be documented. Documentation should reflect the basis for decisions made about the process. For example, manufacturers should document the variables studied for a unit operation and the rationale for those variables identified as significant.

中文解析：製程知識的來源與變異管理

製程知識的四大來源

  • 開發數據（Development Data）：配方開發、劑型研究、初步可行性試驗的結果
  • 文獻資料（Literature）：學術論文、監管指引、技術標準
  • 先前經驗（Prior Experience）：公司內部相似產品的製造歷史、平台製程
  • 製造歷史（Manufacturing History）：既有批次記錄、偏差調查、CAPA結果

四大變異來源（Variability Sources）

FDA特別點名這四個商業生產中的主要變異來源：

  • 不同原料批次（Component Lots）：例如API粒徑批次間差異
  • 操作員（Operators）：不同人員的操作差異、培訓一致性
  • 環境條件（Environmental Conditions）：溫濕度、空調系統季節性變化
  • 量測系統（Measurement Systems）：儀器校正、分析方法精密度

關鍵認知：Stage 1時，商業規模的完整變異範圍通常還不完全清楚——這是正常的，用實驗室或試產規模去估計。

CDMO實務：知識轉入的挑戰

在CDMO環境下，製程知識通常從委託方（Sponsor/Client）轉入。這帶來特有挑戰：

  • 委託方的開發數據可能不完整或格式不一
  • 委託方的「先前經驗」可能是商業機密，不完全分享
  • CDMO自己的設備（如灌封機型號）可能與原開發設備不同
  • 環境條件（如廠房等級、空調設計）也可能有差異

因此，CDMO的Stage 1必須重新評估這些差異對製程的影響，不能直接沿用委託方的舊數據。

DOE Studies and Risk Analysis Tools | 實驗設計與風險分析工具

FDA source text pp.11–12 | Revealing relationships, multivariate interactions, and establishing ranges

Design of Experiment (DOE) in Process Design

"Design of Experiment (DOE) studies can help develop process knowledge by revealing relationships, including multivariate interactions, between the variable inputs (e.g., component characteristics or process parameters) and the resulting outputs (e.g., in-process material, intermediates, or the final product)."

Risk analysis tools can be used to screen potential variables for DOE studies to minimize the total number of experiments conducted while maximizing knowledge gained.

The results of DOE studies can provide justification for establishing ranges of:

  • Incoming component quality
  • Equipment parameters
  • In-process material quality attributes
"FDA does not generally expect manufacturers to develop and test the process until it fails."

Other activities such as laboratory or pilot-scale experiments also assist in evaluation of certain conditions and prediction of commercial process performance. Computer-based or virtual simulations of unit operations can provide process understanding and help avoid problems at commercial scale.

It is important to understand the degree to which models represent the commercial process, including any differences that might exist, as this may impact the relevance of information derived from the models.

中文解析：DOE方法論與製程理解

DOE的核心價值

DOE（實驗設計，Design of Experiment）不是盲目試誤，而是用統計方法同時研究多個因素的影響和交互作用。

比較：

  • 傳統OFAT（One Factor at a Time）：每次只改一個變數，看不出交互作用，需要大量實驗
  • DOE：同時改變多個變數，用最少實驗次數找出最重要的因素及其組合效應

DOE在無菌製程中的應用示例

以無菌灌封為例，DOE可以同時研究：

  • 灌封速度（Fill speed）× 針頭間距（Needle spacing）
  • 溶液溫度（Solution temp）× 灌封重量（Fill weight）
  • 氮氣沖洗壓力（N₂ purge pressure）× 頂空氧含量（Headspace O₂）

輸出（Output）可以是：灌封重量準確性、顆粒計數、頂空含氧量——即CQA。

透過DOE，找出哪些CPP對CQA有顯著影響（主效應），以及哪些組合有交互作用（交互效應）。

風險工具 → DOE的篩選步驟

先用風險工具（如FMEA、魚骨圖、風險矩陣）列出所有潛在因素，再篩選進入DOE，可大幅減少實驗次數：

典型流程：

列出所有因素
Ishikawa / Brainstorming → FMEA/風險評分
篩出高風險因素 → 設計DOE
高風險因素入選 → 分析結果
確定CPP

「不需要做到失敗」的FDA立場

FDA明確表示：不期望廠商去找製程的「崩潰邊界」（failure boundary）。

實務意涵：您需要的是建立足夠的操作空間讓正常變異有緩衝，而不是找出「最極限能接受的條件」。監管官員看的是您的理解是否足以合理設定控制範圍，不是您跑了多少個失敗批次。

比喻：高速公路行駛

想像開車在高速公路：

  • PAR = 法定速限範圍（例如：60–120 km/h），在這個範圍內行駛都是合法的、被證明安全的
  • NOR = 您公司的車隊管理規定（例如：80–100 km/h），這是日常正常操作區間，給突發狀況留有緩衝

平常開車（商業生產）在NOR（80-100）。偶爾因超車需要到NOR以外但還在PAR以內（如110km/h）？需要評估，但不一定違規。超出PAR（超過120km/h）？那就是真正的問題了。

設計空間（Design Space）

ICH Q8(R2) 定義的設計空間是比PAR更高層次的概念：

  • 設計空間 = 多個CPP和物料屬性的多維組合空間，在此空間內操作可保證產品品質
  • PAR = 單一參數的可接受範圍（一維）
  • 設計空間需要考慮參數間的交互作用（多維）

在設計空間內移動（如調整某個CPP）不視為變更（Change），不需要監管提交審批。超出設計空間則需要Post-Approval Change（PAC）。

NOR設定的實務建議

NOR應設在PAR的中間偏窄，確保：

  • 正常生產變異（儀器精度、操作員差異）不會超出NOR
  • NOR超出到PAR邊界有足夠緩衝空間
  • NOR到PAR邊界的距離 ≥ 製程正常變異的3倍標準差（作為經驗法則）

如果NOR設得太靠近PAR邊界，生產中偶爾的正常波動就會觸發偏差——導致不必要的調查負擔。

Process Design Space and Operating Space | 製程設計空間與操作空間

Multidimensional combinations of inputs — from ICH Q8(R2) to FDA 2011 Guidance

Design Space Concept in Process Design

The process design stage is the appropriate time to establish the design space — the multidimensional combination and interaction of input variables (material attributes and process parameters) that have been demonstrated to provide assurance of quality.

While FDA does not mandate design space for all products, Stage 1 data provides the scientific foundation if a sponsor chooses to pursue this regulatory pathway. The practical benefit is regulatory flexibility: movement within the design space does not constitute a change and does not require prior regulatory approval.

Key distinctions in the design space hierarchy:

  • Knowledge Space: The full range explored during development (including failure modes)
  • Design Space: The multidimensional subspace where product quality is assured (ICH Q8)
  • Normal Operating Range (NOR): The tighter range used for routine commercial production control

Moving outside the design space requires a regulatory submission. Moving within it does not. This creates a direct financial and operational incentive to conduct thorough Stage 1 studies — more design space means more manufacturing flexibility.

CMA（Critical Material Attribute）的概念

就像製程有CPP，原料也有關鍵物料屬性（CMA）。CMA是指原料的特性，其變動會直接影響製程效能或產品CQA。

CMA的管控思路：

  • 在收貨時（IQC）設定供應商規格，確保符合CMA範圍
  • 在Stage 1中，用DOE研究CMA的可接受範圍
  • 批次間原料變異→ 是商業生產變異的主要來源之一

CDMO場景：原料批次變異的挑戰

CDMO通常不控制原料採購（由Sponsor決定供應商），但必須管控原料批次間的變異對製程的影響：

  • 問題：Sponsor更換API供應商，新批次粒徑分佈更寬
  • 影響：溶解速率改變 → 含量均一性CQA可能受影響
  • Stage 1的防護：如果在Stage 1中已研究了API粒徑的CMA範圍（PAR），可以判斷新供應商的粒徑是否在已研究範圍內，避免盲目放行

生物製劑的額外挑戰

生物製劑（如mAb、重組蛋白）的CMA比小分子藥物複雜得多：

  • 蛋白質的高階結構（二級、三級、四級）難以完全表徵
  • 糖基化模式（Glycosylation pattern）可能因細胞培養條件輕微改變
  • FDA因此對生物製劑的「不能高度表徵（cannot be highly characterized）」情況，要求更嚴格的製程內監控

這也是FDA Guidance特別提到的第二種需要特別關注控制策略的情境。

Scale-Up Considerations | 製程放大考量

From lab/pilot scale to commercial manufacturing — what changes and what doesn't

Scale-Up: From Lab and Pilot to Commercial Scale

Laboratory or pilot-scale models designed to be representative of the commercial process can be used to estimate variability. However, several critical differences must be accounted for when scaling up:

  • Equipment functionality and limitations: Commercial equipment capabilities may differ from lab/pilot equipment
  • Surface area-to-volume ratios: Often change significantly at scale
  • Heat and mass transfer characteristics: Mixing dynamics change at larger volumes
  • Environmental contribution to variability: Commercial environments have greater inherent variability than controlled labs
  • Operator variability: Multiple operators, shifts, and training levels at commercial scale vs. expert lab personnel

Platform processes and prior knowledge can accelerate Stage 1 development. If a manufacturer has successfully validated a similar process previously, that knowledge can be used to justify reduced experimentation — provided the differences between the platform and the new process are documented and addressed.

Computer-based or virtual simulations of certain unit operations can provide process understanding and help avoid problems at commercial scale, but the degree to which models represent the commercial process must be understood.

平台製程（Platform Process）的善用

CDMO的核心競爭優勢之一，就是「平台製程知識」：

  • 例如：CDMO已有10個mAb產品的無菌灌封歷史，知道在同廠牌灌封機上，灌封速度CPP的PAR通常在哪個範圍

  • FDA允許「已知製程的縮減實驗」，但要求廠商說明差異性評估（comparability assessment）

  • 板架均一性（Shelf uniformity）：邊緣與中心的溫度差異在大型凍乾機中更顯著
  • 裝載量效應（Loading effect）：全載vs.半載，升華速率不同
  • 設備間轉移（Equipment transfer）：實驗型凍乾機 → 商業凍乾機，參數不能直接套用

ICH Q8附錄提到：設計空間可以跨越不同規模，但需要通過縮放參數（scale-dependent parameters）的研究來支持。

Establishing a Strategy for Process Control | 建立製程管控策略

FDA source text p.12 | Translating process knowledge into a defensible control approach

2. Establishing a Strategy for Process Control

"Process knowledge and understanding is the basis for establishing an approach to process control for each unit operation and the process overall. Strategies for process control can be designed to reduce input variation, adjust for input variation during manufacturing (and so reduce its impact on the output), or combine both approaches."

Process controls address variability to assure quality of the product. Controls can consist of material analysis and equipment monitoring at significant processing points (§ 211.110(c)).

FDA expects controls to include both examination of material quality and equipment monitoring. Special attention to control the process through operational limits and in-process monitoring is essential in two scenarios:

  1. When the product attribute is not readily measurable due to limitations of sampling or detectability (e.g., viral clearance or microbial contamination)
  2. When intermediates and products cannot be highly characterized and well-defined quality attributes cannot be identified

More advanced strategies, which may involve the use of Process Analytical Technology (PAT), can include timely analysis and control loops to adjust the processing conditions so that the output remains constant.

These controls are established in the master production and control records (§ 211.186(a) and (b)(9)).

中文解析：三層控制策略

管控策略的兩大方向

FDA明確說明，製程管控策略可以從兩個方向入手（或結合）：

  • 方向1：降低輸入變異（Reduce input variation）
    例如：對原料設定嚴格的IQC規格，確保進料CMA的一致性；在過濾前過濾賦形劑以降低雜質；使用自動化配製減少操作員差異。
  • 方向2：即時調整製程（Adjust for input variation during manufacturing）
    例如：使用PAT（製程分析技術）即時監測配製溶液的濃度，自動調整攪拌時間；在灌封中使用閉環控制系統自動補正灌封重量偏差。

兩種特殊情境下的加強管控

情境1：產品屬性難以量測

無菌性（sterility）是最典型的例子——不能每支都做無菌測試（破壞性且不具代表性），所以要靠：

  • 嚴格的環境管控（EM監測）
  • 過濾器完整性測試
  • 製程模擬（媒體填充/APS）

情境2：中間體無法完全表徵

生物製劑中間體（如細胞收穫液、純化中間體）的高階結構難以完全量測，必須依靠嚴格的製程參數管控（如pH、溫度、時間）作為品質的代理指標（surrogate）。

PAT在無菌製造的應用潛力

PAT（製程分析技術）是更先進的管控策略，在無菌製造中的應用包括：

  • 在線濃度監測（NIR/Raman）：即時確認配製濃度，不需等離線HPLC結果
  • 在線粒徑監測：配製過程中確認API粒徑分佈
  • 100%視覺檢查自動化：高速相機+AI影像判斷，比人工更可靠
  • 灌封重量閉環控制：自動補正，減少人工調整頻率

PAT的導入通常在Stage 1確定，Stage 2驗證其可靠性——這也是Stage 1投資的高回報領域。

  2. PAT的導入（如在線NIR）通常需要大量投資。CDMO應如何向客戶說明PAT的監管價值（不只是效率），以爭取客戶接受這個成本？
  3. 「降低輸入變異」vs.「即時調整製程」——兩者在監管文件（如品質計畫、控制策略報告）中有不同的呈現方式。您如何在Stage 1報告中清楚區分並記錄？

Documentation — Process Design Report | 文件記錄：製程設計報告

Capturing the basis for decisions — what to document and why it matters

Documentation Requirements in Stage 1

"It is essential that activities and studies resulting in process understanding be documented. Documentation should reflect the basis for decisions made about the process. For example, manufacturers should document the variables studied for a unit operation and the rationale for those variables identified as significant."

Good documentation at Stage 1 serves multiple functions:

  • Provides the scientific basis for CPP identification and range-setting
  • Enables regulatory reviewers to understand the design logic
  • Feeds into Stage 2 (PPQ) protocol design — what to test and why
  • Enables Stage 3 (CPV) monitoring plan design — which parameters to track
  • Supports post-approval change management — knowing what was studied before
  • Facilitates technology transfer (to CDMO or new site) by capturing the institutional knowledge

The Process Design Report (PDR) or Development Report is the primary output document of Stage 1. It typically includes:

  • Process flow diagram and unit operation descriptions
  • CPP and CMA identification rationale (with supporting studies)
  • DOE study summaries and key findings
  • NOR and PAR ranges with scientific justification
  • Control strategy rationale
  • Scale-up assessment
  • Risk assessment summaries

Technology Transfer | 技術轉移

Transferring Stage 1 knowledge from development to manufacturing — CDMO context

Knowledge Transfer to Manufacturing

Stage 1 culminates in a formal technology transfer from development to manufacturing. This transfer is more than a document handover — it is the migration of all accumulated process understanding that enables manufacturing personnel to produce the product correctly and consistently.

Key elements of a complete technology transfer package include:

  • Process Design Report (PDR) — complete Stage 1 documentation
  • Draft Master Batch Record (MBR) — reflecting all Stage 1 learnings
  • CPP/CMA list with ranges — NOR and PAR for each parameter
  • Control strategy document — in-process controls, monitoring plan
  • Analytical method transfers — all test methods used in Stage 1
  • Reference standards and materials — API samples, standards
  • Training records — knowledge transfer to manufacturing operators
  • Equipment qualification status — IQ/OQ/PQ of commercial equipment
  • Site comparability assessment — if transferring between facilities

The effectiveness of technology transfer directly determines the success of Stage 2 Process Performance Qualification (PPQ). Gaps in Stage 1 knowledge become failures in Stage 2 PPQ batches.

  • 分析方法未轉移確認：Sponsor的HPLC方法在CDMO的儀器上未驗證，造成數據不可比
  • 工程批數量不足：未完整確認製程在CDMO條件下的可重現性，直接進入PPQ
  • 人員培訓不足：CDMO操作人員未充分理解Stage 1決策背後的科學原理，只是機械性按SOP操作

技術轉移失敗的真實代價

Stage 2的PPQ批次失敗代價極高：

  • 一批商業規模失敗批次可能損失數十萬至數百萬美元的原料和製造成本
  • PPQ失敗會延遲NDA/BLA申請，推遲產品上市1–2年
  • FDA可能對頻繁PPQ失敗展開合規稽查

結論：在Stage 1（技術轉移的工程批）投資足夠時間和資源，是CDMO最有效的風險管理。

思考練習 11

  1. 一個CDMO接受技術轉移，Sponsor提供的PDR顯示凍乾CPP（板架降溫速率）已有完整DOE數據支持，PAR為 -0.5至-1.5°C/min。但CDMO的凍乾機與Sponsor的型號不同。您如何評估原有PAR是否適用於CDMO的設備？

  3. CDMO在完成工程批後，更新了MBR中的兩個CPP範圍（比Sponsor原始PDR更窄）。這個更新是否需要Sponsor確認？是否需要法規更新（post-approval change）？判斷依據是什麼？

Integrating Stage 1 principles into CDMO operations — from intake to PPQ readiness

Stage 1 in the CDMO Lifecycle

For a CDMO, Stage 1 encompasses both the knowledge received through technology transfer and the additional studies conducted at the CDMO facility to validate applicability to the CDMO's specific equipment, environment, and processes.

The goal is the same as FDA's expectation: arrive at Stage 2 with sufficient process understanding to design a meaningful PPQ protocol, execute it successfully, and establish an ongoing Stage 3 monitoring program that detects drift before it becomes a failure.

Stage 1 Readiness Checklist for PPQ (Stage 2):

  • All CPPs and CMAs are identified and documented with scientific rationale
  • NOR and PAR established for all CPPs (with data)
  • Control strategy defined for all unit operations
  • Master Batch Record finalized
  • All analytical methods transferred and qualified
  • Commercial-scale equipment qualified (IQ/OQ/PQ complete)
  • Personnel trained and qualified
  • Engineering run data reviewed and approved
  • PPQ protocol drafted and approved (includes sampling plan, acceptance criteria)

Stage 2 Overview — 第二階段概覽

What is Stage 2? — 第二階段是什麼？

Stage 2 is Process Qualification (PQ) — the stage where the process design developed in Stage 1 is evaluated to determine whether it is capable of reproducible commercial manufacture. This is the commercial-scale proof-of-concept. No amount of lab-scale or pilot data can substitute for demonstrating control at full commercial scale.

第二階段是製程確效（Process Qualification）——在此階段評估第一階段所設計的製程，判斷是否能夠實現可重複的商業規模生產。這是商業規模的概念驗證。任何實驗室或中試規模的數據，都無法取代在完整商業規模下展示製程控制能力。

FDA Source Text — 原文

C. Stage 2 — Process Qualification

During the process qualification (PQ) stage of process validation, the process design is evaluated to determine if it is capable of reproducible commercial manufacture. This stage has two elements: (1) design of the facility and qualification of the equipment and utilities and (2) process performance qualification (PPQ). During Stage 2, CGMP-compliant procedures must be followed. Successful completion of Stage 2 is necessary before commercial distribution.

Products manufactured during this stage, if acceptable, can be released for distribution.

  2. 製程效能確效（PPQ）——核心工作，整合所有已確效的廠房、設備、人員、材料，在商業規模下運行整個製程

IV-A: Design of Facility and Qualification of Utilities and Equipment — 廠房設計與設備/公用系統確效
FDA Source Text — 原文

1. Design of a Facility and Qualification of Utilities and Equipment

Proper design of a manufacturing facility is required under part 211, subpart C, of the CGMP regulations on Buildings and Facilities. It is essential that activities performed to assure proper facility design and commissioning precede PPQ. Here, the term qualification refers to activities undertaken to demonstrate that utilities and equipment are suitable for their intended use and perform properly. These activities necessarily precede manufacturing products at the commercial scale.

Qualification of utilities and equipment generally includes the following activities:

  • Selecting utilities and equipment construction materials, operating principles, and performance characteristics based on whether they are appropriate for their specific uses.
  • Verifying that utility systems and equipment are built and installed in compliance with the design specifications (e.g., built as designed with proper materials, capacity, and functions, and properly connected and calibrated).
  • Verifying that utility systems and equipment operate in accordance with the process requirements in all anticipated operating ranges. This should include challenging the equipment or system functions while under load comparable to that expected during routine production. It should also include the performance of interventions, stoppage, and start-up as is expected during routine production. Operating ranges should be shown capable of being held as long as would be necessary during routine production.
中文解析 — Commentary

廠房設計與設備確效的三層邏輯

「確效」（Qualification）的定義

FDA 在此明確定義：確效 = 「證明設備和公用系統適合其預期用途並正常運作」的一系列活動。這不是形式上的文件填寫，而是必須有客觀證據支撐的系統性活動。

無菌充填廠的實際應用

在無菌充填廠（Aseptic Fill-Finish CDMO），設備確效對象包括：

  • 充填線設備：充填泵（蠕動/時間壓力式）、加塞機、壓蓋機
  • 公用系統（Utilities）：HVAC（ISO 5/7/8 潔淨室）、注射用水（WFI）、壓縮空氣、氮氣覆蓋系統
  • 滅菌設備：高壓滅菌釜（Autoclave）、隧道式乾熱滅菌箱（Depyrogenation Tunnel）
  • 隔離系統：RABS / 隔離器（Isolator）

Qualification Plan Requirements

Qualification of utilities and equipment can be covered under individual plans or as part of an overall project plan. The plan should consider the requirements of use and can incorporate risk management to prioritize certain activities and to identify a level of effort in both the performance and documentation of qualification activities. The plan should identify the following items:

  1. the studies or tests to use,
  2. the criteria appropriate to assess outcomes,
  3. the timing of qualification activities,
  4. the responsibilities of relevant departments and the quality unit, and
  5. the procedures for documenting and approving the qualification.

IV-B: Process Performance Qualification (PPQ) — 製程效能確效概論
FDA Source Text — 原文

2. Process Performance Qualification

The process performance qualification (PPQ) is the second element of Stage 2, process qualification. The PPQ combines the actual facility, utilities, equipment (each now qualified), and the trained personnel with the commercial manufacturing process, control procedures, and components to produce commercial batches. A successful PPQ will confirm the process design and demonstrate that the commercial manufacturing process performs as expected.

Success at this stage signals an important milestone in the product lifecycle. A manufacturer must successfully complete PPQ before commencing commercial distribution of the drug product. The decision to begin commercial distribution should be supported by data from commercial-scale batches. Data from laboratory and pilot studies can provide additional assurance that the commercial manufacturing process performs as expected.

The approach to PPQ should be based on sound science and the manufacturer's overall level of product and process understanding and demonstrable control. The cumulative data from all relevant studies (e.g., designed experiments; laboratory, pilot, and commercial batches) should be used to establish the manufacturing conditions in the PPQ. To understand the commercial process sufficiently, the manufacturer will need to consider the effects of scale. However, it is not typically necessary to explore the entire operating range at commercial scale if assurance can be provided by process design data. Previous credible experience with sufficiently similar products and processes can also be helpful. In addition, we strongly recommend firms employ objective measures (e.g., statistical metrics) wherever feasible and meaningful to achieve adequate assurance.

中文解析 — Commentary

PPQ 的本質：整合驗證

PPQ 是什麼的整合？

PPQ 將以下所有元素整合在一起，在商業規模下運行：

  • 已確效的廠房（Qualified Facility）
  • 已確效的公用系統（Qualified Utilities）
  • 已確效的設備（Qualified Equipment）
  • 訓練合格的人員（Trained Personnel）
  • 商業製造製程（Commercial Manufacturing Process）
  • 製程控制程序（Control Procedures）
  • 商業原料/組件（Commercial Components）

這就是為什麼 PPQ 必須在設備確效之後——你無法用未確效的設備來確效製程。

核心立場：商業規模數據才算數

FDA 明確指出：「開始商業配銷的決定，必須有商業規模批次數據的支持。」實驗室和中試數據只能提供「額外保證」，不能作為主要依據。

這直接回應了業界常見的誤解：3 批 10 公升的實驗批次數據，不能代替商業規模的 PPQ。

規模效應（Scale Effects）

FDA 要求製造商考慮「規模效應」（effects of scale）。在無菌充填廠中，這特別重要：

  • 配製槽攪拌：從 50L 放大到 500L，混合動力學完全不同
  • 過濾時間：商業規模的過濾面積和壓差與小試完全不同
  • 充填速度：高速充填下的充填量均勻性與低速小批次不同
  • 環境暴露時間（LST/BIER）：商業批次的最長暴露時間可能遠長於小批次

比喻：廚師做菜 vs. 餐廳量產

一位廚師在廚房試做了 3 份完美的餐點（Stage 1 開發）。但餐廳開業前，必須證明同樣的食譜在商業廚房、由訓練過的廚師、用商業規模的設備，能持續產出同樣品質——這就是 PPQ。你不能說「試做時很好所以一定沒問題」。

思考練習

  1. PPQ 整合了七個元素。哪個元素最容易被忽略？在無菌充填廠中，「訓練合格的人員」對 PPQ 結果有什麼影響？
  2. FDA 說「不必在商業規模探索整個操作範圍，如果設計數據能提供保證」——這個條件在實際操作中如何判斷？
  3. 「以往對類似產品/製程的可信賴經驗也有幫助」——在 CDMO 環境中，如何將平台製程（Platform Process）的歷史數據用於支持新產品的 PPQ？
IV-C: PPQ Protocol Requirements — PPQ 方案要素
FDA Source Text — 原文

3. PPQ Protocol

A written protocol that specifies the manufacturing conditions, controls, testing, and expected outcomes is essential for this stage of process validation. We recommend that the protocol discuss the following elements:

  • The manufacturing conditions, including operating parameters, processing limits, and component (raw material) inputs.
  • The data to be collected and when and how it will be evaluated.
  • Tests to be performed (in-process, release, characterization) and acceptance criteria for each significant processing step.
  • The sampling plan, including sampling points, number of samples, and the frequency of sampling for each unit operation and attribute. The number of samples should be adequate to provide sufficient statistical confidence of quality both within a batch and between batches. The confidence level selected can be based on risk analysis as it relates to the particular attribute under examination. Sampling during this stage should be more extensive than is typical during routine production.
  • Criteria and process performance indicators that allow for a science- and risk-based decision about the ability of the process to consistently produce quality products. The criteria should include:
    • A description of the statistical methods to be used in analyzing all collected data (e.g., statistical metrics defining both intra-batch and inter-batch variability).
    • Provision for addressing deviations from expected conditions and handling of nonconforming data. Data should not be excluded from further consideration in terms of PPQ without a documented, science-based justification.
  • Design of facilities and the qualification of utilities and equipment, personnel training and qualification, and verification of material sources (components and container/closures), if not previously accomplished.
  • Status of the validation of analytical methods used in measuring the process, in-process materials, and the product.
  • Review and approval of the protocol by appropriate departments and the quality unit.
中文解析 — PPQ 方案要素深度解析

PPQ 方案：書面承諾的力量

為什麼書面方案如此重要？

PPQ 方案是「事前承諾」（Pre-specified Commitment）。你必須在執行前就定義好：做什麼測試、收什麼數據、合格標準是什麼。這防止了「做完再決定標準」（Cherry-picking）的問題。FDA 稽核時會比對方案與實際執行是否一致。

PPQ 方案要素清單（8 項）

  • 製造條件：操作參數、加工限制、原料投入規格
  • 數據收集計畫：收集什麼、何時收、如何評估
  • 測試項目與接受標準：製程中測試、放行測試、特性鑑定測試
  • 取樣計畫：取樣點、樣品數量、頻率（比例行生產更密集）
  • 製程效能指標：科學/風險決策依據，含統計方法
  • 廠房/設備確效狀態確認（若尚未完成）
  • 分析方法確效狀態確認
  • 方案的部門審查與 QU 核准記錄

數據排除的嚴格要求

FDA 特別強調：「PPQ 數據不得在未有有文件記錄的、以科學為基礎的正當理由的情況下排除於進一步考量之外。」這意味著你不能因為某個數據點「看起來像異常值」就輕易捨棄——必須有完整的 OOS/OOT 調查支撐。

無菌充填廠 PPQ 方案範例要素

  • 操作參數：充填速度（例：300 瓶/分）、充填量（例：10.0 mL ± 0.2 mL）、加塞力（例：20-40 N）、壓蓋扭矩
  • 製程中測試：每 30 分鐘充填量抽查、沉降碟 EM、浮游菌採樣、容器完整性（CCIT 先期測試）
  • 放行測試：無菌（§71.3a/直接法）、可見異物（100% 燈檢）、pH、效價、CCIT
  • 接受標準：所有批次符合成品規格；無菌培養基模擬（APS）生長單元 = 0；每批製程能力指數 Ppk ≥ 1.33

思考練習

  1. PPQ 方案必須包含「分析方法確效狀態確認」。如果某個分析方法在 PPQ 執行時還沒有完成確效，對 PPQ 的合規性有何影響？
  2. 方案中要求「QU 核准」——如果 PPQ 過程中發現需要修改一個操作參數，必須怎麼做？
  3. 數據不得無正當理由排除。假設某批次中有一個充填量數據明顯偏低，如何合規地處理這個數據點？
Enhanced Sampling Rationale — 強化取樣的理由
FDA Source Text — Sampling Requirements

Enhanced Sampling in PPQ

The sampling plan, including sampling points, number of samples, and the frequency of sampling for each unit operation and attribute. The number of samples should be adequate to provide sufficient statistical confidence of quality both within a batch and between batches. The confidence level selected can be based on risk analysis as it relates to the particular attribute under examination. Sampling during this stage should be more extensive than is typical during routine production.

In most cases, PPQ will have a higher level of sampling, additional testing, and greater scrutiny of process performance than would be typical of routine commercial production. The level of monitoring and testing should be sufficient to confirm uniform product quality throughout the batch. The increased level of scrutiny, testing, and sampling should continue through the process verification stage as appropriate, to establish levels and frequency of routine sampling and monitoring for the particular product and process.

Considerations for the duration of the heightened sampling and monitoring period could include, but are not limited to, volume of production, process complexity, level of process understanding, and experience with similar products and processes.

中文解析 — 為什麼 PPQ 需要比例行生產更多樣品？

強化取樣的根本理由

核心邏輯：你在學習，不是在確認

例行生產的取樣計畫，是建立在「製程已被證明穩定」的前提下設計的。PPQ 的前提恰恰相反：你還不知道這個商業規模製程是否穩定——你正在蒐集足夠的數據來証明它穩定。因此需要更多樣品。

三個統計理由

  1. 批內均勻性（Intra-batch Uniformity）：需要足夠多的樣品，才能偵測到批次內部的變異（例如充填線起始/中段/結尾的充填量差異）
  2. 批間變異（Inter-batch Variability）：需要跨多批次的數據，才能估計批間變異並建立容忍區間
  3. 統計信心水準（Statistical Confidence Level）：樣品數越多，對製程能力的估計越精確，信心區間越窄

比喻：氣象預報的道理

例行天氣預報用 3 個氣象站的數據就夠（因為已有數十年數據建立了模型）。但如果你要第一次為某個新城市建立天氣預報模型，你需要在各個位置、各個時段密集布點測量——這就是 PPQ 的強化取樣邏輯。

強化取樣可基於風險分析調整

FDA 說「信心水準可基於風險分析」——這表示不同屬性的取樣強度可以不同：

  • 高風險屬性（例：無菌性、可見異物）→ 最高取樣密度
  • 中風險屬性（例：充填量、pH）→ 標準加密取樣
  • 低風險屬性（例：外觀、標籤）→ 適度強化即可

無菌充填廠：PPQ 強化取樣舉例

以一個 10,000 瓶的 PPQ 批次為例：

  • 充填量：例行每 30 分鐘查 10 瓶 → PPQ 每 15 分鐘查 20 瓶（覆蓋充填線前/中/後）
  • 沉降碟：例行灌裝時間的 50% → PPQ 全程（100%）擺放沉降碟
  • CCIT：例行每批抽取 n=30 → PPQ 每批抽取 n=100
  • 可見異物：100% 燈檢（兩者相同，因本就是全數檢驗）

思考練習

  1. 你的 PPQ 方案規定每 15 分鐘抽查充填量 20 瓶。執行中發現這個頻率造成大量操作干擾，想改為每 30 分鐘查 10 瓶。這個改變需要什麼程序？
  2. 強化取樣何時可以停止？FDA 說「應持續到適當的製程確認階段」——在你的公司，這個決定如何做出？
  3. 決定強化取樣持續時間的四個考量因素（生產量、製程複雜度、理解程度、類似產品經驗），在無菌充填廠中哪個最難量化？為什麼？

中文解析 — 統計工具深度說明

PPQ 的兩個核心統計工具

工具 1：容忍區間（Tolerance Interval, TI）

定義：在一定置信度（例如 95%）下，能夠覆蓋至少某一比例（例如 99%）的製程輸出的區間。

用途：用來確認製程產出的大部分產品（不只是樣品）都落在規格限制內。

與規格的差別：規格告訴你「可接受範圍」；容忍區間告訴你「實際製程分布有多少落在這個範圍內」。

解讀範例：如果 95/99 容忍區間（95% 置信度，覆蓋 99% 的產品）為 [9.8 mL, 10.2 mL]，而規格是 9.5–10.5 mL，則製程通過。若容忍區間超出規格，製程能力不足。

工具 2：製程效能指數（Ppk）

定義：Ppk = min[(USL - X̄) / 3σ, (X̄ - LSL) / 3σ]，其中 σ 為樣品整體標準差（含批間變異）

與 Cpk 的差別：Cpk 使用製程內部變異（組內）；Ppk 使用整體變異（含批間），更保守，更適合 PPQ 的「長期能力估計」。

FDA 建議標準：業界一般要求 PPQ 階段 Ppk ≥ 1.33（對應 Sigma Level ≈ 4），或依風險和產品屬性設定更嚴格的標準。

無菌充填廠應用：充填量 Ppk、容器密封完整性（CCIT 測試結果）、pH 等關鍵質量屬性（CQA）都應計算 Ppk。

批內 vs 批間變異的分析

FDA 明確要求統計方法必須定義「批內（intra-batch）和批間（inter-batch）變異」：

  • 批內：同一批次內不同時間點/位置的變異（例如充填量的批內漂移）
  • 批間：不同批次之間的均值/標準差差異（例如每批充填量平均值是否一致）

兩者都必須在規格內且可控，製程才算真正穩定。

思考練習

  1. 你的 PPQ 批次充填量數據計算得 Ppk = 1.18，規格為 9.5–10.5 mL，均值 10.0 mL，Ppk 未達 1.33。這是否代表 PPQ 失敗？應如何決策？
  2. 容忍區間（TI）和 Ppk 都可以衡量製程能力，但它們各自強調不同方面。各舉一個情境說明何時用 TI 更合適，何時用 Ppk 更合適。
  3. FDA 說「僅建議（strongly recommend）」使用統計指標——這代表不用也合規嗎？從稽核的角度，不使用統計方法有什麼風險？
PPQ Protocol Execution — PPQ 方案執行要求
FDA Source Text — Execution Requirements

4. PPQ Protocol Execution and Report

Execution of the PPQ protocol should not begin until the protocol has been reviewed and approved by all appropriate departments, including the quality unit. Any departures from the protocol must be made according to established procedure or provisions in the protocol. Such departures must be justified and approved by all appropriate departments and the quality unit before implementation (§ 211.100).

中文解析 — 執行的合規要求

PPQ 執行的三大合規要求

要求 1：先核准，再執行

PPQ 方案必須在所有相關部門（含 QU）審查核准後，才能開始執行。這不是走過場的簽名——是正式的書面核准。執行前開始意味著違規（§211.100）。

要求 2：偏離必須事先核准

如果執行中需要偏離方案，必須依照既定程序或方案中的規定處理，並在實施前獲得所有相關部門和 QU 的批准。「先做再說」在 PPQ 是不被允許的。

要求 3：真實商業條件下執行

PPQ 批次必須：

  • 遵循商業製造製程和例行程序（不能用特殊程序）
  • 由日常操作該製程的人員執行（不能用「最優秀的員工」操作）
  • 在正常操作條件下進行（HVAC、WFI、原料、環境均為正常狀態）

常見錯誤：PPQ 用特殊安排

有些公司 PPQ 期間用最資深的員工操作、特別清潔設備、選用最佳批次原料——這些都違背了「正常條件」的要求。PPQ 結果應代表平均水準下的製程能力，而非最優狀況。如果只有在特殊安排下才能通過，這個製程本質上是不穩定的。

比喻：駕照路考的道理

PPQ 就像駕照路考。不能讓最頂尖的賽車手去代考（不是日常操作人員），不能用特改的賽道（不是正常條件）。考官要看的是一般駕駛員在正常道路上的正常水準。

思考練習

  1. PPQ 執行中，操作員發現充填針有輕微堵塞，需要比方案規定更換充填針的時間提前 2 小時。依 FDA 指引，此時應如何處理？
  2. 「由日常操作該製程的人員執行」——在 CDMO 環境中，如果 PPQ 剛好在某位關鍵操作員請假時進行，是否應該延後 PPQ？如何在方案中預先規定這種情況？
  3. 正常操作條件要求使用「商業原料」。如果 PPQ 使用的某個原料批次後來發現有品質問題，這對 PPQ 結論有何影響？
PPQ Report Requirements — PPQ 報告要求
FDA Source Text — Report Requirements

PPQ Report

A report documenting and assessing adherence to the written PPQ protocol should be prepared in a timely manner after the completion of the protocol. This report should:

  • Discuss and cross-reference all aspects of the protocol.
  • Summarize data collected and analyze the data, as specified by the protocol.
  • Summarize and discuss all manufacturing nonconformances such as deviations, atypical events, out-of-specification results, and relate these to the conclusions about the state of control of the process.
  • Describe corrective actions taken or needed.
  • State the conclusion(s) regarding whether the data analyzed supports the conclusion that the process is in a state of control or not. If not, the report should indicate what corrective actions are needed and the timeline for such actions.
  • Include review and approval by all appropriate departments and the quality unit.
中文解析 — PPQ 報告的六大要素

PPQ 報告：最終的書面結論

PPQ 報告結構清單（6 項）

  • 方案交叉對照：逐一對照方案要素，確認執行狀況
  • 數據彙總與分析：依方案要求的統計方法分析所有數據
  • 所有不符合項彙總：偏差、異常事件、OOS 結果，並與製程控制結論連結
  • 矯正措施描述：已採取或需採取的 CAPA
  • 明確結論：製程處於受控狀態，或不在受控狀態（含後續行動計畫）
  • 相關部門及 QU 的審查與核准

關鍵：結論必須明確，不能模糊

PPQ 報告的結論只有兩種：

  1. 製程處於受控狀態（可開始商業配銷）
  2. 製程不在受控狀態（必須說明需要的矯正措施和時程）

含糊的結論（例如「大部分結果符合要求」）不被接受。

不符合項必須全部記錄並關聯結論

FDA 要求：所有偏差、異常事件、OOS 結果都必須：

  • 在報告中被完整彙總（不能選擇性呈現）
  • 與製程控制狀態結論明確連結（例如：這個偏差是否影響製程受控狀態的判斷）

這與 OOS 調查的要求一致：數據不能未經科學正當理由就被忽視。

PPQ 報告「及時準備」的實際意義

FDA 說報告應「在方案完成後及時準備」。業界一般理解為：

  • 初稿：批次完成後 30 天內
  • QU 核准版：60 天內
  • 批次放行必須等 PPQ 報告核准（至少要有初步結論）

在 CDMO 環境中，客戶通常急於知道 PPQ 結果，這造成快速完成報告的壓力，但不應為此犧牲報告的完整性。

思考練習

  1. PPQ 報告顯示製程有 2 個輕微偏差（已 CAPA）和 1 個 OOS（已調查，判定為實驗室錯誤）。報告的結論應如何撰寫？
  2. 「及時準備」——如果 PPQ 報告尚未完成 QU 核准，是否可以放行 PPQ 批次？FDA 的立場是什麼？
  3. PPQ 報告需要「所有相關部門」核准。在你的組織中，哪些部門應該在 PPQ 報告上簽名？為什麼每個部門都有責任？
PAT: Process Analytical Technology — 製程分析技術的 PPQ 方法
FDA Source Text — PAT Approach

PPQ for PAT-Enabled Processes

A manufacturing process that uses PAT may warrant a different PPQ approach. PAT processes are designed to measure in real time the attributes of an in-process material and then adjust the process in a timely control loop so the process maintains the desired quality of the output material.

The process design stage and the process qualification stage should focus on the measurement system and control loop for the measured attribute. Regardless, the goal of validating any manufacturing process is the same: to establish scientific evidence that the process is reproducible and will consistently deliver quality products.

In the case of a strategy using PAT, the approach to process qualification will differ from that used in other process designs. Further information on PAT processes can be found in FDA's guidance for industry on PAT — A Framework for Innovative Pharmaceutical Development, Manufacturing, and Quality Assurance.

中文解析 — PAT 製程的不同 PPQ 邏輯

PAT 製程的根本差異

什麼是 PAT（製程分析技術）？

PAT 是一種即時製程控制策略：在製程運行過程中，連續測量關鍵物料屬性（CMA），並根據即時測量結果主動調整製程參數，使製程輸出始終維持在目標品質水準。

常見範例：近紅外光譜（NIR）監測混合均勻度 → 達到均勻度標準時自動停止混合（終點控制，而非固定時間）

PAT 製程 PPQ 的焦點轉移

傳統製程 PPQ 重點：確認固定製程條件下的輸出穩定性

PAT 製程 PPQ 重點：確認測量系統和控制閉環（Control Loop）的可靠性

具體而言：

  • NIR 模型是否準確？（量測系統確效）
  • 控制邏輯是否正確響應？（控制迴路測試）
  • 在異常情況下的響應是否符合預期？（故障模式測試）

PAT 在無菌充填廠的潛在應用

  • 充填量即時監控：重量感測器（In-line weighing）即時偵測每瓶充填量，異常時自動停線
  • 配製濃度監控：UV/Vis 或 NIR 即時監測 API 濃度，達到目標後停止配製
  • 容器完整性即時偵測：高壓放電（HVLD）100% 偵測密封完整性

目前無菌充填廠的 PAT 應用仍在逐步推廣，但趨勢明確。

不變的核心目標

無論使用傳統 PPQ 還是 PAT 方法，目標都一樣：「建立科學證據，證明製程具有重現性並能持續提供優質產品。」PAT 只是改變了如何達到這個目標的方法，而不是改變目標本身。

思考練習

  1. 傳統 PPQ 和 PAT-based PPQ 的取樣策略有什麼根本差別？為什麼 PAT 製程可以用不同的取樣方式？
  2. 如果你的充填線導入了 In-line 充填量即時監控（PAT），PPQ 方案中如何規定測量系統確效的要求？
  3. PAT 允許製程即時自我調整——這是否與「固定製程條件」的傳統 PPQ 概念相矛盾？如何在法規框架下理解這個差異？
What Happens When PPQ Fails — PPQ 失敗時怎麼辦？
Regulatory Basis — 法規基礎

FDA's Position on PPQ Failure

From the guidance:

"If not [in a state of control], the report should indicate what corrective actions are needed and the timeline for such actions."

And from the execution requirements:

"A manufacturer must successfully complete PPQ before commencing commercial distribution of the drug product."

The guidance also states that departures from the protocol must be justified and approved before implementation (§ 211.100). Nonconforming data cannot be excluded without documented, science-based justification.

Regarding concurrent release: products manufactured during Stage 2, if acceptable, can be released for distribution — but this is contingent on meeting specifications AND demonstrating process control.

中文解析 — PPQ 失敗的合規後果與應對

PPQ 失敗的定義與後果

何謂「PPQ 失敗」？

PPQ 失敗不只是「某瓶不合格」——它是指 PPQ 報告的結論無法支持「製程處於受控狀態」的判斷。這可能發生在：

  • 多個批次的 Ppk < 1.33（系統性能力不足）
  • 批間變異過大（製程不具重現性）
  • 有未解決的 OOS 且根本原因指向製程設計缺陷
  • 關鍵偏差（Critical Deviation）發生且無法證明不影響產品品質

PPQ 失敗的法規後果

  • 不得商業配銷：PPQ 未成功完成，不能開始商業配銷
  • 已生產批次的處置：若 PPQ 批次因不符合規格而無法放行，必須依 §211.192 進行全面調查
  • 重新 PPQ：進行必要的製程改善後，必須重新執行 PPQ（或部分 PPQ）
  • FDA 通報：若 NDA/ANDA 已申報，製程重大變更可能需要補充申請

PPQ 失敗後的標準處理流程

  1. 偏差調查：識別所有不符合項，進行根本原因分析（RCA）
  2. 範圍界定：確認失敗是局部性的（操作失誤）還是系統性的（製程設計缺陷）
  3. CAPA 制定：制定有時程的矯正預防措施
  4. 製程改善：依 CAPA 修改製程設計或控制策略（可能需要回到 Stage 1）
  5. 重新 PPQ：再次執行完整或部分 PPQ，並撰寫新報告
  6. 法規評估：評估是否需要更新申請文件

CDMO 視角：PPQ 失敗的商業影響

在 CDMO 環境中，PPQ 失敗的影響遠超技術層面：客戶的上市時程延遲、緊急採購成本、客戶關係緊張、可能的合約違約條款——這些都是真實的商業後果。這正是為什麼 Stage 1 的製程設計品質如此關鍵：做好前端設計，才能減少 Stage 2 失敗的風險。

比喻：橋梁結構測試

PPQ 就像橋梁開放前的最終結構測試。如果測試失敗，橋不能通車（不能商業配銷）。你需要找出哪裡有結構問題（根本原因），修復它，然後重新測試。你不能說「算了，先通車再說，希望不會塌」——這在藥品生產中就是危及患者安全。

思考練習

  1. PPQ 三批次中，第二批次有一個 OOS（充填量偏低），調查後確認是操作員充填針安裝錯誤（人員錯誤，非製程設計問題），已 CAPA。PPQ 是否可以繼續？結論如何撰寫？
  2. PPQ 後發現批間 Ppk = 1.12（目標 1.33），但每批批內均勻性良好。這代表什麼？根本原因最可能在哪裡？
  3. 客戶要求「縮短 PPQ 批次數量，只做 2 批」以節省成本。作為 CDMO 品保主管，你如何回應這個要求？你會引用 FDA 指引的哪個部分？

中文解析 — 同步放行的條件與風險

什麼是同步放行（Concurrent Release）？

定義

同步放行是指在 PPQ 尚未完全完成（例如跨批次分析和報告尚在進行）的情況下，將符合規格的個別 PPQ 批次放行到市場。FDA 在 Stage 2 允許此做法，但有前提條件。

同步放行的條件

  • 該批次必須符合所有成品規格（放行測試通過）
  • 製造商對製程有足夠的理解，能夠預期批次品質
  • 須有充分的科學和風險評估支持此決策
  • Stage 3（持續製程確認）的計畫必須已就位

注意：Stage 3 有更完整的說明

FDA 在 Stage 2 僅簡短提及同步放行的可行性。完整的同步放行策略、條件和風險評估要求，在 Stage 3（持續製程確認）部分有更詳盡的說明。在 CDMO 工作時，通常需要與客戶的法規事務部門共同評估是否採用同步放行策略。

Risk-Based Justification for Enhanced Sampling — 強化取樣的風險基礎正當理由
Risk-Based Framework — FDA Expectation

Risk-Based Approach to PPQ Sampling

FDA states: "The confidence level selected can be based on risk analysis as it relates to the particular attribute under examination."

This means the extent of enhanced sampling should be justified by:

  • The criticality of the quality attribute (CQA)
  • The inherent process variability observed in development
  • The degree of process understanding (QTPP linkage)
  • Prior process history with similar products/processes
  • The potential patient safety impact of process failures

Higher risk attributes demand more samples, more frequent sampling, and more stringent acceptance criteria in the PPQ sampling plan.

風險分層取樣的實際操作

文件化正當理由的重要性

Key Message — 核心訊息

Stage 2 is the commercial-scale proof-of-concept — you cannot fake your way through it with small-scale data.

Three small-scale batches of excellent data do not replace one rigorously executed commercial-scale PPQ. The FDA's position is unambiguous: the decision to begin commercial distribution must be supported by commercial-scale batch data. Lab and pilot data are supplementary, not substitutes.

Enhanced sampling is not bureaucratic excess — it is how you build the statistical foundation that justifies reducing scrutiny in Stage 3. You are investing in evidence now to earn the right to operate with standard monitoring later.

第二階段是商業規模的概念驗證——你無法用小規模數據蒙混過關。

三批優秀的小規模數據，無法取代一次嚴格執行的商業規模 PPQ。FDA 的立場毫不含糊：開始商業配銷的決定，必須有商業規模批次數據的支持。實驗室和中試數據是補充，不是替代。

強化取樣不是官僚主義的繁文縟節——它是你建立統計基礎的方式，而這個基礎正是你在 Stage 3 降低監控強度的正當理由。你現在投資於證據，才能換來未來以標準監控水準運作的權利。

Where We Are in the Lifecycle

FDA's 2011 Process Validation Guidance establishes a three-stage lifecycle approach. Stages 1 and 2 build knowledge and demonstrate initial process capability. Stage 3 is where that capability is sustained and confirmed across the full commercial production horizon.

Unlike traditional "three-batch validation," the lifecycle model recognizes that validation is never truly finished — it is an ongoing commitment to process understanding.

"The goal of the third validation stage is continual assurance that the process remains in a state of control (the validated state) during commercial manufacture."
— FDA Process Validation Guidance, 2011, p.14

生命週期定位 — 中文解析

三階段生命週期邏輯

  • 第一階段（製程設計）：了解製程，識別關鍵製程參數（CPP）與關鍵品質屬性（CQA）
  • 第二階段（製程確認 PPQ）：在商業規模下執行，確認製程能重複生產符合規格的產品
  • 第三階段（CPV）：商業生產過程中持續監控，確認製程維持受控狀態

比喻：駕照考試 vs. 日常駕駛

第二階段 PPQ 如同考取駕照（通過測驗，取得資格）。第三階段 CPV 則是每天上路行駛——真正的安全記錄來自於持續的行為，而非一次考試。

傳統「三批次驗證」的思維等同於：只要考一次試，就永遠不需要再證明自己還會開車。FDA 2011 指引明確拒絕了這種思維。

重點：為什麼 CPV 是閉合迴路的關鍵？

Stage 1+2 的資料量有限（通常只有 3–10 批次）。真正代表商業製程變異性的資料，只有在 Stage 3 大量批次生產後才能獲得。CPV 讓驗證從「靜態證明」轉為「動態保證」。

D. Stage 3 — Continued Process Verification

The goal of the third validation stage is continual assurance that the process remains in a state of control (the validated state) during commercial manufacture. A system or systems for detecting unplanned departures from the process as designed is essential to accomplish this goal.

Adherence to the CGMP requirements, specifically, the collection and evaluation of information and data about the performance of the process, will allow detection of undesired process variability. Evaluating the performance of the process identifies problems and determines whether action must be taken to correct, anticipate, and prevent problems so that the process remains in control (§ 211.180(e)).

An ongoing program to collect and analyze product and process data that relate to product quality must be established (§ 211.180(e)). The data collected should include relevant process trends and quality of incoming materials or components, in-process material, and finished products. The data should be statistically trended and reviewed by trained personnel. The information collected should verify that the quality attributes are being appropriately controlled throughout the process.

"We recommend that a statistician or person with adequate training in statistical process control techniques develop the data collection plan and statistical methods and procedures used in measuring and evaluating process stability and process capability."

第三階段定義 — 中文解析

核心定義：CPV（Continued Process Verification）

持續製程查驗是指在商業生產期間，透過系統性的資料收集、統計分析與趨勢監控，持續確認製程保持在已驗證的受控狀態。

  • 目標：偵測非預期的製程偏離
  • 手段：統計方法 + 訓練人員審核
  • 法規依據：21 CFR § 211.180(e)
  • 資料範圍：原料、中間品、成品、製程參數

法規連結：§ 211.180(e)

此條款要求製造商建立書面程序，定期評估製造記錄，以確認批次始終符合既定標準。CPV 計畫是滿足此要求的主要機制。

FDA 明確建議由統計學家或具備充分 SPC 訓練的人員來設計資料收集計畫——這意味著 CPV 不是 QA 任意決定抽幾個點的事，而是需要統計學設計。

比喻：血壓監測 vs. 健康體檢

PPQ（第二階段）如同一次完整的健康體檢——深入但不頻繁。CPV 則像每天量血壓——不那麼深入，但持續追蹤趨勢，能在問題惡化前發出警告。

只做體檢不量血壓，你可能在兩次體檢之間悄悄發展出高血壓危機。製程也是如此。

思考練習

  1. FDA 建議由「統計學家或受過充分 SPC 訓練的人員」設計資料收集計畫。在您的組織中，誰最可能負責這項工作？這個責任目前是否明確？
  2. § 211.180(e) 要求定期評估製造記錄。「定期」在您公司的 SOP 中如何定義？月度？季度？批次基礎？
  3. 如果一個 CDMO 同時生產 15 種產品，每種產品都需要獨立的 CPV 計畫，這在資源上有何挑戰？如何優先排序？

Data Collection Program Requirements

We recommend continued monitoring and sampling of process parameters and quality attributes at the level established during the process qualification stage until sufficient data are available to generate significant variability estimates. These estimates can provide the basis for establishing levels and frequency of routine sampling and monitoring for the particular product and process.

Monitoring can then be adjusted to a statistically appropriate and representative level. Process variability should be periodically assessed and monitoring adjusted accordingly.

Procedures should describe how trending and calculations are to be performed and should guard against:

  • Overreaction to individual events (false positive signals leading to unnecessary investigation)
  • Failure to detect unintended process variability (missing real signals)

Production data should be collected to evaluate process stability and capability. The quality unit should review this information. If properly carried out, these efforts can identify variability in the process and/or signal potential process improvements.

Variation can also be detected by timely assessment of:

  • Defect complaints
  • Out-of-specification findings
  • Process deviation reports
  • Process yield variations
  • Batch records
  • Incoming raw material records
  • Adverse event reports

資料收集計畫 — 中文解析

取樣計畫設計原則

FDA 建議的取樣策略分為兩個階段：

  • 早期商業化階段：維持 PPQ 階段的取樣頻率與數量，持續收集直到有足夠資料估計製程變異性
  • 穩定後階段：根據統計分析結果，調整至「統計上合適且具代表性」的常規水準

關鍵字：statistically appropriate and representative — 不是越多越好，也不是越少越省事，而是根據資料說話。

雙向錯誤：過度反應 vs. 遲鈍

正確設計的 SPC 計畫（加上明確的管制界限規則）可以平衡這兩種錯誤。

資料來源全景圖

CPV 不只是看檢驗數據，還涵蓋：

  • 製程參數（溫度、壓力、速度、填充量等）
  • 中間品檢測結果
  • 成品放行數據
  • 原物料 CoA 與放行趨勢
  • 客訴與不良反應報告
  • 批次良率變異
  • 偏差與 CAPA 記錄

Block 4 | 第四區塊 — SPC Tools: Cpk vs. Ppk | 統計製程管制工具

Statistical Process Control — Key Indices

FDA references ASTM E2281-03 "Standard Practice for Process and Measurement Capability Indices" as a useful resource for measuring and evaluating process stability and process capability.

Two primary indices used in pharmaceutical CPV:

Process Capability Index (Cpk) — measures how well a process performs relative to specifications, assuming the process is in statistical control (within-subgroup variation only).

Process Performance Index (Ppk) — measures actual observed process performance including all sources of variation (total variation: within + between subgroups).

General industry benchmarks:

  • Cpk / Ppk ≥ 1.33 → capable process (generally acceptable)
  • Cpk / Ppk ≥ 1.67 → highly capable (Six Sigma target)
  • Cpk / Ppk < 1.00 → process not capable, action required
  • Cpk / Ppk 1.00–1.33 → marginal, enhanced monitoring needed

Cpk vs. Ppk 深度解析

Cpk 公式

Cpk = min[ (USL - X̄) / (3σ_within),
           (X̄ - LSL) / (3σ_within) ]

σ_within = R̄ / d2  (from control chart)

Example:
  USL = 505 mg, LSL = 495 mg, X̄ = 501 mg
  σ_within = 1.2 mg

  CPU = (505 - 501) / (3 × 1.2) = 4/3.6 = 1.11
  CPL = (501 - 495) / (3 × 1.2) = 6/3.6 = 1.67
  Cpk = min(1.11, 1.67) = 1.11

Ppk 公式

Ppk = min[ (USL - X̄) / (3σ_total),
           (X̄ - LSL) / (3σ_total) ]

σ_total = S  (overall standard deviation)

Key difference:
  σ_within uses R̄/d2 (only random short-term noise)
  σ_total uses all data variation (includes shifts,
  drifts, lot-to-lot, operator effects, seasonal, etc.)

If Ppk << Cpk → large between-batch variation exists
  → investigate batch-to-batch sources

比喻：短跑 vs. 馬拉松

Cpk 像是短跑成績——在最佳狀態、最短時間內的表現。Ppk 像是全年所有比賽的平均成績——包含了天氣、體力狀態、場地等所有實際條件的影響。

對於法規申請（NDA/BLA），FDA 更關注 Ppk，因為它反映的是真實商業生產的全貌。

Block 5 | 第五區塊 — Control Charts: Xbar-R & Other Tools | 管制圖與 SPC 工具

Statistical Tools for CPV

Many tools and techniques, some statistical and others more qualitative, can be used to detect variation, characterize it, and determine the root cause. FDA recommends that the manufacturer use quantitative, statistical methods whenever appropriate and feasible.

Scrutiny of intra-batch as well as inter-batch variation is part of a comprehensive continued process verification program under § 211.180(e).

Key statistical tools used in pharmaceutical CPV:

  • Xbar-R charts — monitor process mean and range over time
  • Individual-MR (I-MR) charts — for individual measurements (n=1)
  • P-charts / U-charts — for attribute data (defect rates)
  • CUSUM charts — cumulative sum, sensitive to small shifts
  • EWMA charts — exponentially weighted moving average
  • Histogram + Normality tests — assess distribution shape
  • Box plots — compare variation across batches/lines/operators
  • Scatter plots / Regression — explore CPP-CQA relationships

Control charts detect signals via Western Electric rules (or similar):

  • 1 point beyond 3σ (UCL/LCL)
  • 2 of 3 consecutive points beyond 2σ
  • 4 of 5 consecutive points beyond 1σ
  • 8 consecutive points on one side of centerline (run rule)
  • Trends: 6+ consecutive points moving in one direction

管制圖詳解 — 中文解析

Xbar-R Chart 結構示意

Xbar Chart (均值圖):
  UCL_X = X̄̄ + A2 × R̄     ← 上管制界限
  CL_X  = X̄̄               ← 中心線（總均值）
  LCL_X = X̄̄ - A2 × R̄     ← 下管制界限

R Chart (全距圖):
  UCL_R = D4 × R̄           ← 上管制界限
  CL_R  = R̄                ← 中心線（平均全距）
  LCL_R = D3 × R̄           ← 下管制界限（n≤6時=0）

常用係數 (n=5):
  A2 = 0.577, D3 = 0, D4 = 2.114

圖示 (填充重量, mg):
  UCL_X ─────────────────── 502.8
        ●   ●           ●
  CL_X  ─── ─── ─── ─── ─── 501.0
              ●   ●
  LCL_X ─────────────────── 499.2

  批次:    1   2   3   4   5

Detecting and Characterizing Variation

Good process design and development should anticipate significant sources of variability and establish appropriate detection, control, and/or mitigation strategies, as well as appropriate alert and action limits.

However, a process is likely to encounter sources of variation that were not previously detected or to which the process was not previously exposed. Many tools and techniques, some statistical and others more qualitative, can be used to detect variation, characterize it, and determine the root cause.

Production line operators and quality unit staff should be encouraged to provide feedback on process performance. FDA recommends that the quality unit meet periodically with production staff to evaluate data, discuss possible trends or undesirable process variation, and coordinate any correction or follow-up actions by production.

Sources of Variation to Monitor

  • Intra-batch variation (within a single lot)
  • Inter-batch variation (lot-to-lot)
  • Seasonal variation (temperature, humidity effects)
  • Shift-to-shift / operator-to-operator variation
  • Equipment drift (wear, calibration drift)
  • Incoming material variability (supplier lots)
  • Line clearance and setup variation
  • Environmental monitoring trends (viable, non-viable)

Block 8 | 第八區塊 — OOT vs. OOS Investigation | 超趨勢 vs. 超規格調查

Out-of-Trend vs. Out-of-Specification

Variation can be detected by the timely assessment of defect complaints, out-of-specification findings, process deviation reports, process yield variations, batch records, incoming raw material records, and adverse event reports.

FDA distinguishes between two types of concerning findings in CPV data:

Out-of-Trend (OOT): A result or series of results that deviate from expected statistical behavior — exceeding control chart limits or trend analysis thresholds — even if still within specification limits. OOT signals a potential change in process behavior before it becomes a quality problem.

Out-of-Specification (OOS): A result that falls outside established specification limits. OOS requires a formal investigation per 21 CFR § 211.192 and FDA's OOS guidance (2006).

The key principle: OOT investigations should catch problems before they become OOS events. A robust CPV program uses OOT as a leading indicator system.

OOT vs. OOS 決策矩陣

OOT → OOS 決策流程

步驟 1：偵測 OOT 訊號（超出管制界限或觸發趨勢規則）

步驟 2：啟動 OOT 調查——是否為計算錯誤或儀器問題？

步驟 3：確認 OOT 屬實——執行根本原因分析

步驟 4：判斷是否已達規格界限或有升級 OOS 風險

步驟 5：若升級為 OOS，啟動 § 211.192 正式調查

步驟 6：實施 CAPA，持續監控改善效果

比喻：體溫計 vs. 急診

OOT 如同體溫達到 37.5°C（輕微偏高）——需要關注、多喝水、休息，但還不用去急診。OOS 如同體溫達到 40°C——必須立即就醫，這是緊急事件。

好的 CPV 系統在你「還只是 37.5°C」時就介入，防止惡化到 40°C。這就是為什麼 OOT 系統的設計至關重要。

OOT 定義的挑戰

OOT 沒有像 OOS 那樣有明確的法規定義，各公司需要在 SOP 中自行定義觸發標準。常見定義方式：

  • 任何超出 UCL/LCL 的點
  • 連續 N 個點在中心線同側（N 由 SPC 規則決定，常用 8）
  • 連續 M 個點持續上升或下降（常用 6）
  • 穩定性資料的線性回歸斜率超過設定閾值
  • Cpk 低於預設警戒值（如 <1.33）

Block 11 | 第十一區塊 — Concurrent Release of PPQ Batches | PPQ 批次同期放行

V. Concurrent Release of PPQ Batches

In most cases, the PPQ study needs to be completed successfully and a high degree of assurance in the process achieved before commercial distribution of a product. In special situations, the PPQ protocol can be designed to release a PPQ batch for distribution before complete execution of the protocol steps and activities, i.e., concurrent release. FDA expects that concurrent release will be used rarely.

Concurrent release might be appropriate for:

  • Processes used infrequently for various reasons
  • Drugs for which there is limited demand (e.g., orphan drugs, minor use and minor species veterinary drugs)
  • Drugs with short half lives (e.g., radiopharmaceuticals, including positron emission tomography drugs)
  • Drugs that are medically necessary and are being manufactured in coordination with the Agency to alleviate a short supply
"Conclusions about a commercial manufacturing process can only be made after the PPQ protocol is fully executed and the data are fully evaluated."

If Stage 2 qualification is not successful, additional design studies and qualification may be necessary. The new product and process understanding obtained from the unsuccessful qualification study(ies) can have negative implications if any lot was already distributed.

同期放行 — 中文解析

同期放行（Concurrent Release）的定義

同期放行是指在 PPQ 協議尚未完全執行完畢的情況下，允許 PPQ 批次進行商業配送。這是常規做法的例外，而非規範。

FDA 明確表示：「FDA 預期同期放行將被少用（rarely）。」這個 "rarely" 是一個強烈的法規信號。

同期放行的風險警示

FDA 明確指出：如果 Stage 2 確認未成功（即 PPQ 失敗），而部分批次已在同期放行下配送到市場，後果嚴重——因為這意味著可能已放行了在不可重複製程下生產的產品。

這是同期放行最大的法規風險：一旦 PPQ 最終結論是「製程未確認」，所有已同期放行的批次都面臨回收（Recall）的可能性。

比喻：試映會 vs. 正式上映

同期放行就像電影還在剪輯中就讓觀眾進場——理論上可行，但如果剪輯完成後發現劇情根本不通順，觀眾的體驗就已經受損了，而且退票很困難。常規做法是先完成整部電影（完整 PPQ），確認品質後才開始售票（商業放行）。

Block 12 | 第十二區塊 — Concurrent Release: Conditions & Safeguards | 同期放行的條件與防護措施

Requirements for Concurrent Release

Circumstances and rationale for concurrent release should be fully described in the PPQ protocol. Even when process performance assessment based on the PPQ protocol is still outstanding, any lot released concurrently must comply with:

  • All CGMPs
  • Regulatory approval requirements
  • PPQ protocol lot release criteria

Lot release under a PPQ protocol is based upon meeting confidence levels appropriate for each quality attribute of the drug.

When warranted and used, concurrent release should be accompanied by:

  • A system for careful oversight of the distributed batch
  • Rapid customer feedback mechanisms
  • Customer complaints and defect reports rapidly assessed to determine root cause

Concurrently released lots must also be assessed in light of any negative PPQ study finding or conclusions and appropriate corrective action must be taken (§§ 211.100(a), 211.180(e), and 211.192).

FDA recommends that each batch in a concurrent release program be evaluated for inclusion in the stability program. Stability test data should be promptly evaluated to ensure rapid detection and correction of any problems.

同期放行的完整要求 — 中文解析

同期放行的 6 大必要條件

  • 1. 預先規劃：同期放行的理由和情境必須在 PPQ 協議中事先完整描述（不能事後補說明）
  • 2. 全面 CGMP 合規：即使在協議尚未完成的情況下，每批次必須完全符合 CGMP 要求
  • 3. 批次放行標準：需達到 PPQ 協議規定的放行標準（通常比常規放行更嚴格的統計置信度）
  • 4. 上市後監控系統：需要建立快速回饋機制，包含客訴、不良報告的快速評估
  • 5. 穩定性計畫納入：每個同期放行批次都應納入穩定性計畫，且需迅速評估穩定性資料
  • 6. PPQ 負面結論的回應：若最終 PPQ 結論為失敗，必須對已放行批次採取適當糾正措施

「置信水準（Confidence Level）」在 PPQ 放行中的應用

常規商業放行通常基於規格合規（Yes/No）。PPQ 同期放行要求更高的統計保證，例如：

  • 需達到 95% 置信水準，95% 的個體將在規格內（95/95 容忍區間）
  • 或達到 Ppk ≥ 1.33 的特定標準
  • 或多個關鍵屬性的加權統計評估

具體標準需在 PPQ 協議中事先定義，不能在評估後才決定。

上市後快速監控系統

同期放行必須伴隨強化的上市後監控：

  • 設立專門的客訴快速回應流程（縮短正常回應時間）
  • 建立配銷追蹤系統（知道每批次在哪裡）
  • 與 FDA 保持溝通（特別是供應短缺情境下的協調）
  • 穩定性資料即時審查（不能等到年度審查）
  • 任何 PPQ 負面發現立即評估對已放行批次的影響

思考練習

  1. 您的公司生產一種孤兒藥，全球僅有約 200 名患者。您正在執行 PPQ（3 批次計畫），第一批 PPQ 資料看起來很好。客戶要求立即供貨，因為目前斷貨。您如何評估是否適合同期放行？需要準備哪些文件？
  2. F-18 PET 顯影劑的半衰期是 110 分鐘。請解釋為什麼傳統 PPQ 完成後再放行對這類產品完全不可行，以及同期放行在此情境的特殊性。
  3. 一個同期放行批次已配送出去，隨後第二批 PPQ 的無菌測試 OOS 調查顯示培養基模擬試驗存在系統性問題。您需要採取什麼行動？涉及哪些法規條款？
Block 13 | 第十三區塊 — CDMO Aseptic Fill-Finish: CPV Implementation | CDMO 無菌灌裝 CPV 實施

Stage 3 in a CDMO Context

Contract Development and Manufacturing Organizations (CDMOs) face unique challenges in implementing Stage 3 Continued Process Verification. Unlike integrated pharmaceutical companies, CDMOs must manage CPV programs across multiple client products, often with different regulatory filing requirements, specification systems, and client oversight expectations.

Key considerations for CDMO CPV implementation:

  • Client-specific CPV plan requirements (sponsor's statistical standards may differ)
  • Data ownership and access (client vs. CDMO data systems)
  • Multi-product facility shared equipment trend data
  • Regulatory filing coordination (who files PAS/CBE for process changes?)
  • PQR/APR compilation responsibility (CDMO provides data, client files)
  • Stage 3 monitoring during tech transfer from another manufacturer
  • Enhanced CPV requirements for aseptic processes (EM trends, MFT, CCIT)

CDMO 無菌灌裝的 CPV 實施策略

無菌灌裝 CPV 特有監控項目

  • 環境監測（EM）趨勢：浮游菌、沉降菌、表面微生物、非活性微粒——月度趨勢圖，識別季節性或設施退化
  • 培養基模擬試驗（MFT/Media Fill）：每次執行結果趨勢，長期合格率監控
  • 容器封閉完整性（CCIT）：高壓滅菌後殘留氧氣趨勢、染料侵入測試結果
  • 可見異物（VPI）：每批次異物比率趨勢，依異物類型分類統計
  • 無菌測試（SAL）：雖為百分之百通過/失敗，仍需趨勢監控（即使零陽性，也需確認測試方法的持續有效性）
  • 殘留溶媒：適用於使用有機溶劑的配方（如脂質奈米粒子）

CDMO CPV 計畫的優先順序建議

高優先順序（必須在首批商業化生產前建立）：

  • 填充量 CPV（Xbar-R chart，Cpk/Ppk 月度計算）
  • EM 趨勢計畫（月度趨勢報告，OOT 定義）
  • 批次良率趨勢

中優先順序（首年內建立）：

  • 關鍵設備參數趨勢（填充速度、蒸汽壓力、凍乾曲線）
  • 原料 CoA 關鍵參數趨勢
  • CCIT 趨勢

持續優化：基於早期 CPV 資料結果，識別最重要的變異來源並優先監控。

思考練習

  1. 您的 CDMO 正在技術轉移一個已在原廠生產了 3 年的生物製品。原廠有完整的 CPV 歷史資料。技術轉移完成後，您需要從 Stage 1 重新開始，還是可以利用原廠的 CPV 資料？需要哪些額外確認活動？
  2. 您的 CDMO 同時服務 Client A（美國 FDA 申請）和 Client B（EMA 申請）。兩個客戶對 CPV 的統計方法要求不同。您如何在同一個設施、同一條生產線上管理這兩套要求？
  3. EM 趨勢顯示過去 6 個月 Grade B 區域的浮游菌數量緩慢上升（從平均 0.1 CFU/m³ 升至 0.8 CFU/m³，規格 <1 CFU/m³）。這屬於 OOT 嗎？應採取什麼行動？如何決定是否暫停生產？

VI. Documentation | 第六章：文件要求

Documentation — The Foundation of Validation

Documentation at each stage of the process validation lifecycle is essential for effective communication in complex, lengthy, and multidisciplinary projects. Documentation is important so that knowledge gained about a product and process is accessible and comprehensible to others involved in each stage of the lifecycle.

Information transparency and accessibility are fundamental tenets of the scientific method. They are also essential to enabling organizational units responsible and accountable for the process to make informed, science-based decisions that ultimately support the release of a product to commerce.

"Information transparency and accessibility are fundamental tenets of the scientific method."
— FDA Guidance, January 2011

為何文件是驗證的核心

FDA將文件視為驗證本身的一部分，而非僅是行政工作。其背後邏輯是：沒有文件記錄的工作，在法規上等同於沒有做過。

核心原則

製程驗證跨越多年、多部門、多人員。文件是確保：

  • 知識傳承 — 開發知識不因人員異動而流失
  • 科學透明性 — 任何人都能理解決策依據
  • 法規可稽核性 — FDA查廠時可追溯完整驗證歷程
  • 商業放行依據 — QA能做出有根據的放行決定

比喻：文件如同法庭證據

驗證就像一場法庭案件——你的製程是「被告」，FDA是「法官」。無論你的製程實際上多穩定，若無文件佐證，你就是「無罪推定」的另一面：沒有證據，沒有勝訴。

CGMP Documentation Requirements by Stage

The degree and type of documentation required by CGMP vary during the validation lifecycle. Documentation requirements are greatest during Stage 2, process qualification, and Stage 3, continued process verification. Studies during these stages must conform to CGMPs and must be approved by the quality unit in accordance with the regulations (see §§ 211.22 and 211.100).

Viral and impurity clearance studies, even when performed at small scale, also require quality unit oversight.

Key Regulatory Citations

  • 21 CFR § 211.22 — Responsibilities of the Quality Control Unit
  • 21 CFR § 211.100 — Written procedures; deviations
  • 21 CFR § 211.186 — Master production and control records

各階段CGMP文件要求強度

Stage 1 — 製程設計（Process Design）

文件要求相對彈性。主要產出：

  • 商業製造Master Batch Record (§ 211.186)
  • 製程流程圖（Process Flow Diagrams）
  • 各規模的製程比較文件
  • 支持程序（Supporting Procedures）

Stage 2 — 製程確認（Process Qualification）

文件要求最高，全面符合CGMP：

  • PPQ Protocol（需QA事前批准）
  • 批次製造記錄
  • 偏差報告
  • PPQ Final Report（需QA批准）

Stage 3 — 持續製程驗證（CPV）

文件要求持續且系統性：

  • CPV計畫
  • 定期統計趨勢報告
  • 製程能力指數（Cpk）記錄
  • CAPA記錄（如有觸發）

重要提醒：病毒/雜質清除研究

即使在小規模進行的病毒清除或雜質清除研究，同樣需要Quality Unit的監管與文件核准——不能因為是實驗室規模就降低文件標準。

Process Flow Diagrams — A Key Stage 1 Output

CGMP documents for commercial manufacturing (i.e., the initial commercial master batch production and control record (§ 211.186) and supporting procedures) are key outputs of Stage 1, process design.

We recommend that firms diagram the process flow for the full-scale process. Process flow diagrams should describe:

  • Each unit operation and its placement in the overall process
  • Monitoring and control points
  • Component inputs and other processing material inputs (e.g., processing aids)
  • Expected outputs (i.e., in-process materials and finished product)

It is also useful to generate and preserve process flow diagrams of the various scales as the process design progresses to facilitate comparison and decision making about their comparability.

製程流程圖：Stage 1的核心輸出文件

製程流程圖不只是「示意圖」，它是FDA期待看到的科學決策工具，展示從小試到商業規模的可比性評估。

完整製程流程圖應包含

  • 單元操作（Unit Operations） — 每個步驟的順序與位置
  • 監控點（Monitoring Points） — 在線、離線量測點
  • 控制點（Control Points） — CPP觸發的控制行動
  • 物料輸入 — 原料、輔料、加工助劑
  • 中間體輸出 — 中間產品規格
  • 成品輸出 — 最終CQA要求

比喻：縮放比例地圖

保留各規模（1L → 50L → 500L → 商業批）的流程圖，就像保留不同比例尺的地圖——放大看細節，縮小看格局，兩者都需要，才能向FDA說明「放大規模的可比性」。

CDMO實務應用

在無菌充填作業中，製程流程圖應涵蓋：配液 → 過濾（0.22µm） → 充填 → 壓蓋 → 滅菌（若適用），並標註每個步驟的環境監控點（EMPs）與在製品檢測點（IPCs）。

VII. Analytical Methodology | 第七章：分析方法學

Analytical Methods in Process Validation

Process knowledge depends on accurate and precise measuring techniques used to test and examine the quality of drug components, in-process materials, and finished products.

Validated analytical methods are not necessarily required during product- and process-development activities or when used in characterization studies. Nevertheless, analytical methods should be scientifically sound (e.g., specific, sensitive, and accurate) and provide results that are reliable.

There should be assurance of proper equipment function for laboratory experiments. Procedures for analytical method and equipment maintenance, documentation practices, and calibration practices supporting process-development efforts should be documented or described.

New analytical technology and modifications to existing technology are continually being developed and can be used to characterize the process or the product. Use of these methods is particularly appropriate when they reduce risk by providing greater understanding or control of product quality.

However, analytical methods supporting commercial batch release must follow CGMPs in parts 210 and 211. Clinical supply production should follow the CGMPs appropriate for the particular phase of clinical studies.

分析方法學：開發 vs. 商業放行的不同標準

FDA在此明確區分兩種情境下的分析方法要求，這是很多公司容易混淆的地方。

關鍵原則：PPQ前方法須到位

雖然FDA說開發階段不強制要求完整方法驗證，但實務上：進入PPQ（Stage 2）之前，所有商業放行方法必須已完成驗證。否則PPQ數據的可信度將受質疑，導致整個驗證工作失去意義。

新興分析技術（PAT）

FDA鼓勵採用新興分析技術（如過程分析技術PAT），特別是當這些技術能：

  • 提升對製程或產品的理解
  • 降低品質風險
  • 提供更即時的製程控制

參見：FDA PAT Guidance (September 2004)

CDMO實務：在製品vs.放行檢測

  • 在製品檢測（IPC）：pH、滲透壓、過濾壓差——開發階段可用研究級方法
  • 成品放行：效力、含量均一性、無菌——必須已完成USP/ICH驗證

Validation Document Hierarchy | 驗證文件層級架構

FDA Expected Document Structure

While the guidance does not mandate specific document titles, the following hierarchy represents FDA's expected documentation framework for a complete process validation lifecycle:

Validation Master Plan (VMP) | 驗證總計畫
↓
Stage 1: Process Development Report | 製程開發報告
↓
Stage 2: PPQ Protocol + PPQ Report | 製程確認規程與報告

各層級文件說明

驗證總計畫（VMP）

為整個驗證生命週期設定框架。包含：驗證範圍、方法論、組織職責、時程。通常是呈交藥證申請（NDA/BLA）的核心文件之一。

Stage 1：製程開發報告

記錄所有導向商業製程設計的知識，包括：DoE研究結果、CQA/CPP識別、設計空間定義、規模放大評估。這是Stage 2的科學基礎。

Stage 2：PPQ規程 + PPQ報告

PPQ規程：事前定義驗收標準、批次數量、統計方法，需QA批准後才能執行。
PPQ報告：記錄所有PPQ批次結果，結論是否符合驗收標準，需QA批准。

Stage 3：CPV計畫 + 定期報告

CPV計畫：定義監控指標、頻率、統計方法、觸發CAPA的條件。
定期報告：通常每季或半年一份，呈現製程能力趨勢，為持續改善提供依據。

Capability of a process

Releasing for distribution a lot of finished product, manufactured following a qualification protocol, that meets the lot release criteria established in the protocol, but before the entire study protocol has been executed.

術語深度解析

製程能力（Process Capability）

統計意涵：常以Cpk（製程能力指數）表示。Cpk ≥ 1.33 通常被視為可接受的商業製造基準。在CPV報告中，Cpk是判斷製程是否持續在管制狀態的核心指標。

商業製造製程（Commercial Manufacturing Process）

關鍵排除項：臨床試驗批（IND material）不算商業製造製程。這意味著臨床批的驗證要求可以低於商業批，但進入NDA/BLA申請後，必須提交商業製造製程的完整驗證數據。

同步放行（Concurrent Release）

這是一個需要謹慎使用的機制。允許在PPQ尚未全部完成前放行某些批次，但條件是：該批次已符合預定的放行標準，且放行決定基於科學依據。不建議作為常規操作。

持續製程驗證（CPV）

CPV = Stage 3的正式名稱。它不是一次性活動，而是永久性監控體系。只要產品還在商業生產，CPV就必須持續運行。這也是FDA 2011年新架構的最大改變之一——驗證不再「完成」，而是持續進行。

績效指標（Performance Indicators）

在CPV框架下，典型績效指標包括：批次良率、OOS率、偏差率、Cpk趨勢。這些指標需在CPV計畫中預先定義，並定期在周期報告中呈現。

製程設計 vs. 製程確認 vs. 製程驗證

三個易混淆的術語：

  • 製程設計 = Stage 1，定義製程
  • 製程確認 = Stage 2，確認製程
  • 製程驗證 = 三個Stage的總和，是完整概念

管制狀態（State of Control）

這是CPV的最終目標。它不只是「產品合格」，而是「製程持續可預測」。SPC（統計製程管制）圖中，數據點在管制界限內且無特殊趨勢，才代表真正的管制狀態。

Key Message | 核心訊息

Documentation IS Validation

"If it wasn't documented, it wasn't done."
— GMP Industry Principle

The FDA's 2011 Process Validation Guidance makes clear that documentation is not merely administrative overhead — it is the substance of validation itself. For regulatory purposes:

  • An undocumented validation does not exist. No matter how scientifically sound the work, if it cannot be demonstrated through records, it has no regulatory standing.
  • Documentation enables accountability. The Quality Unit's ability to make science-based release decisions depends entirely on complete, accurate, and accessible records.
  • Documentation enables learning. The lifecycle approach to process validation requires that knowledge is captured and transferred — documentation is the only mechanism for doing so across time, teams, and regulatory submissions.
  • Documentation protects patients. The entire purpose of CGMP documentation is to create an auditable trail that demonstrates a manufacturer's commitment to consistent product quality — ultimately protecting patient safety.

This principle applies at every stage: the development knowledge captured in Stage 1 documents becomes the scientific justification for Stage 2 PPQ design; the PPQ evidence becomes the foundation for Stage 3 CPV; and the ongoing CPV records become the evidence presented in regulatory submissions and inspections.

文件即驗證的本質

「沒有記錄，就是沒有做過。」

FDA 2011年製程驗證指引的核心訊息之一：文件不是行政負擔，文件就是驗證本身。從法規角度看：

沒有文件 = 驗證不存在

無論製程實際上有多穩定、科學工作有多嚴謹，若無法以記錄證明，在法規上等同於未執行。FDA查廠時，稽查員看的是文件，不是你的口頭描述。

Electronic Records & 21 CFR Part 11 | 電子記錄與法規要求

21 CFR Part 11 — Core Requirements

When validation records are maintained electronically, 21 CFR Part 11 applies. Key requirements include:

  • Audit Trail — All record creation, modification, and deletion must be time-stamped and attributed to the individual who made the change.
  • Electronic Signatures — Must be legally binding, attributable to a unique individual, and cannot be repudiated.
  • System Validation (CSV) — The computerized system used to maintain records must itself be validated (Computer System Validation).
  • Access Controls — Role-based permissions prevent unauthorized modification.
  • Record Integrity — Data must be protected from alteration or loss over the required retention period.
  • Backup and Recovery — Validated backup procedures and tested recovery capability.

The FDA's expectation is that electronic records provide at least the same level of reliability and trustworthiness as paper records they replace.
