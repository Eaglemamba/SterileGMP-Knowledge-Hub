VI. Documentation | 第六章：文件要求

Documentation — The Foundation of Validation

Documentation at each stage of the process validation lifecycle is essential for effective communication in complex, lengthy, and multidisciplinary projects. Documentation is important so that knowledge gained about a product and process is accessible and comprehensible to others involved in each stage of the lifecycle.

Information transparency and accessibility are fundamental tenets of the scientific method. They are also essential to enabling organizational units responsible and accountable for the process to make informed, science-based decisions that ultimately support the release of a product to commerce.

"Information transparency and accessibility are fundamental tenets of the scientific method."
— FDA Guidance, January 2011

為何文件是驗證的核心

FDA將文件視為驗證本身的一部分，而非僅是行政工作。其背後邏輯是：沒有文件記錄的工作，在法規上等同於沒有做過。

核心原則

製程驗證跨越多年、多部門、多人員。文件是確保：

  • 知識傳承 — 開發知識不因人員異動而流失
  • 科學透明性 — 任何人都能理解決策依據
  • 法規可稽核性 — FDA查廠時可追溯完整驗證歷程
  • 商業放行依據 — QA能做出有根據的放行決定

比喻：文件如同法庭證據

驗證就像一場法庭案件——你的製程是「被告」，FDA是「法官」。無論你的製程實際上多穩定，若無文件佐證，你就是「無罪推定」的另一面：沒有證據，沒有勝訴。

CGMP Documentation Requirements by Stage

The degree and type of documentation required by CGMP vary during the validation lifecycle. Documentation requirements are greatest during Stage 2, process qualification, and Stage 3, continued process verification. Studies during these stages must conform to CGMPs and must be approved by the quality unit in accordance with the regulations (see §§ 211.22 and 211.100).

Viral and impurity clearance studies, even when performed at small scale, also require quality unit oversight.

Key Regulatory Citations

  • 21 CFR § 211.22 — Responsibilities of the Quality Control Unit
  • 21 CFR § 211.100 — Written procedures; deviations
  • 21 CFR § 211.186 — Master production and control records

各階段CGMP文件要求強度

Stage 1 — 製程設計（Process Design）

文件要求相對彈性。主要產出：

  • 商業製造Master Batch Record (§ 211.186)
  • 製程流程圖（Process Flow Diagrams）
  • 各規模的製程比較文件
  • 支持程序（Supporting Procedures）

Stage 2 — 製程確認（Process Qualification）

文件要求最高，全面符合CGMP：

  • PPQ Protocol（需QA事前批准）
  • 批次製造記錄
  • 偏差報告
  • PPQ Final Report（需QA批准）

Stage 3 — 持續製程驗證（CPV）

文件要求持續且系統性：

  • CPV計畫
  • 定期統計趨勢報告
  • 製程能力指數（Cpk）記錄
  • CAPA記錄（如有觸發）

重要提醒：病毒/雜質清除研究

即使在小規模進行的病毒清除或雜質清除研究，同樣需要Quality Unit的監管與文件核准——不能因為是實驗室規模就降低文件標準。

Process Flow Diagrams — A Key Stage 1 Output

CGMP documents for commercial manufacturing (i.e., the initial commercial master batch production and control record (§ 211.186) and supporting procedures) are key outputs of Stage 1, process design.

We recommend that firms diagram the process flow for the full-scale process. Process flow diagrams should describe:

  • Each unit operation and its placement in the overall process
  • Monitoring and control points
  • Component inputs and other processing material inputs (e.g., processing aids)
  • Expected outputs (i.e., in-process materials and finished product)

It is also useful to generate and preserve process flow diagrams of the various scales as the process design progresses to facilitate comparison and decision making about their comparability.

製程流程圖：Stage 1的核心輸出文件

製程流程圖不只是「示意圖」，它是FDA期待看到的科學決策工具，展示從小試到商業規模的可比性評估。

完整製程流程圖應包含

  • 單元操作（Unit Operations） — 每個步驟的順序與位置
  • 監控點（Monitoring Points） — 在線、離線量測點
  • 控制點（Control Points） — CPP觸發的控制行動
  • 物料輸入 — 原料、輔料、加工助劑
  • 中間體輸出 — 中間產品規格
  • 成品輸出 — 最終CQA要求

比喻：縮放比例地圖

保留各規模（1L → 50L → 500L → 商業批）的流程圖，就像保留不同比例尺的地圖——放大看細節，縮小看格局，兩者都需要，才能向FDA說明「放大規模的可比性」。

CDMO實務應用

在無菌充填作業中，製程流程圖應涵蓋：配液 → 過濾（0.22µm） → 充填 → 壓蓋 → 滅菌（若適用），並標註每個步驟的環境監控點（EMPs）與在製品檢測點（IPCs）。

VII. Analytical Methodology | 第七章：分析方法學

Analytical Methods in Process Validation

Process knowledge depends on accurate and precise measuring techniques used to test and examine the quality of drug components, in-process materials, and finished products.

Validated analytical methods are not necessarily required during product- and process-development activities or when used in characterization studies. Nevertheless, analytical methods should be scientifically sound (e.g., specific, sensitive, and accurate) and provide results that are reliable.

There should be assurance of proper equipment function for laboratory experiments. Procedures for analytical method and equipment maintenance, documentation practices, and calibration practices supporting process-development efforts should be documented or described.

New analytical technology and modifications to existing technology are continually being developed and can be used to characterize the process or the product. Use of these methods is particularly appropriate when they reduce risk by providing greater understanding or control of product quality.

However, analytical methods supporting commercial batch release must follow CGMPs in parts 210 and 211. Clinical supply production should follow the CGMPs appropriate for the particular phase of clinical studies.

分析方法學：開發 vs. 商業放行的不同標準

FDA在此明確區分兩種情境下的分析方法要求，這是很多公司容易混淆的地方。

情境 方法驗證要求 最低標準
Stage 1開發 / 特性研究 不強制要求完整驗證 科學合理、可靠、有紀錄
PPQ批次（商業前） 方法需在PPQ前完成驗證 符合USP <1225>或ICH Q2
商業批放行 完整CGMP驗證必要 21 CFR parts 210 & 211
臨床供應批 依臨床試驗階段適用CGMP 隨Phase遞增

關鍵原則：PPQ前方法須到位

雖然FDA說開發階段不強制要求完整方法驗證，但實務上：進入PPQ（Stage 2）之前，所有商業放行方法必須已完成驗證。否則PPQ數據的可信度將受質疑，導致整個驗證工作失去意義。

新興分析技術（PAT）

FDA鼓勵採用新興分析技術（如過程分析技術PAT），特別是當這些技術能：

  • 提升對製程或產品的理解
  • 降低品質風險
  • 提供更即時的製程控制

參見：FDA PAT Guidance (September 2004)

CDMO實務：在製品vs.放行檢測

  • 在製品檢測（IPC）：pH、滲透壓、過濾壓差——開發階段可用研究級方法
  • 成品放行：效力、含量均一性、無菌——必須已完成USP/ICH驗證

Validation Document Hierarchy | 驗證文件層級架構

FDA Expected Document Structure

While the guidance does not mandate specific document titles, the following hierarchy represents FDA's expected documentation framework for a complete process validation lifecycle:

Validation Master Plan (VMP) | 驗證總計畫
↓
Stage 1: Process Development Report | 製程開發報告
↓
Stage 2: PPQ Protocol + PPQ Report | 製程確認規程與報告
↓
Stage 3: CPV Plan + Periodic Reports | 持續製程驗證計畫與定期報告
↓
Supporting Records: Batch Records, Deviation Reports, OOS Reports, Lab Notebooks

Each level feeds into the next — the VMP sets the framework, Stage reports provide evidence, and supporting records are the raw data.

各層級文件說明

驗證總計畫（VMP）

為整個驗證生命週期設定框架。包含：驗證範圍、方法論、組織職責、時程。通常是呈交藥證申請（NDA/BLA）的核心文件之一。

Stage 1：製程開發報告

記錄所有導向商業製程設計的知識，包括：DoE研究結果、CQA/CPP識別、設計空間定義、規模放大評估。這是Stage 2的科學基礎。

Stage 2：PPQ規程 + PPQ報告

PPQ規程：事前定義驗收標準、批次數量、統計方法，需QA批准後才能執行。
PPQ報告：記錄所有PPQ批次結果，結論是否符合驗收標準，需QA批准。

Stage 3：CPV計畫 + 定期報告

CPV計畫：定義監控指標、頻率、統計方法、觸發CAPA的條件。
定期報告：通常每季或半年一份，呈現製程能力趨勢，為持續改善提供依據。

CDMO Documentation Checklist | CDMO文件查檢表

Pre-PPQ Documentation Readiness

Before executing any PPQ batch, the following documents must be in place and approved by the Quality Unit:

  • Process Development Report / Technology Transfer Summary completed
  • Master Batch Record (MBR) approved and issued
  • PPQ Protocol reviewed and approved by QA
  • All analytical methods validated (per USP <1225> or ICH Q2)
  • Equipment qualification (IQ/OQ/PQ) completed for all critical equipment
  • Cleaning validation completed (or cleaning equivalency study)
  • Process Flow Diagram reviewed and current
  • Risk assessment (FMEA or equivalent) documented
  • Acceptance criteria pre-defined and statistically justified
  • Sampling plan documented

PPQ執行中與完成後

執行中（During PPQ）：

  • 批次製造記錄（BPR）實時填寫，無事後補填
  • 所有偏差即時記錄並開立偏差報告
  • OOS結果依21 CFR § 211.192調查
  • 環境監測數據完整記錄
  • 在製品檢測結果記錄於BPR
  • 電子記錄符合21 CFR Part 11要求（若適用）

完成後（Post-PPQ）：

  • PPQ Final Report（含統計分析）完成並由QA批准
  • 所有批次放行前QA審核完成
  • 與VMP的符合性確認
  • CPV計畫啟動，Stage 3正式開始
  • 任何變更啟動變更控制（Change Control）

21 CFR Part 11 電子記錄注意事項

若使用電子系統記錄PPQ數據，必須確保：稽核追蹤（Audit Trail）功能啟用、電子簽章符合要求、系統驗證（CSV）已完成。

Glossary — Original Text | 術語表原文

FDA Glossary (as published)

Capability of a process

Ability of a process to produce a product that will fulfill the requirements of that product. The concept of process capability can also be defined in statistical terms. (ISO 9000:2005)

Commercial manufacturing process

The manufacturing process resulting in commercial product (i.e., drug that is marketed, distributed, and sold or intended to be sold). For the purposes of this guidance, the term commercial manufacturing process does not include clinical trial or treatment IND material.

Concurrent release

Releasing for distribution a lot of finished product, manufactured following a qualification protocol, that meets the lot release criteria established in the protocol, but before the entire study protocol has been executed.

Continued process verification

Assuring that during routine production the process remains in a state of control.

Performance indicators

Measurable values used to quantify quality objectives to reflect the performance of an organization, process or system, also known as performance metrics in some regions. (ICH Q10)

Process design

Defining the commercial manufacturing process based on knowledge gained through development and scale-up activities.

Process qualification

Confirming that the manufacturing process as designed is capable of reproducible commercial manufacturing.

Process validation

The collection and evaluation of data, from the process design stage through commercial production, which establishes scientific evidence that a process is capable of consistently delivering quality products.

Quality

The degree to which a set of inherent properties of a product, system, or process fulfils requirements. (ICH Q9)

State of control

A condition in which the set of controls consistently provides assurance of continued process performance and product quality. (ICH Q10)

術語深度解析

製程能力（Process Capability）

統計意涵：常以Cpk（製程能力指數）表示。Cpk ≥ 1.33 通常被視為可接受的商業製造基準。在CPV報告中，Cpk是判斷製程是否持續在管制狀態的核心指標。

商業製造製程（Commercial Manufacturing Process）

關鍵排除項：臨床試驗批（IND material）不算商業製造製程。這意味著臨床批的驗證要求可以低於商業批，但進入NDA/BLA申請後，必須提交商業製造製程的完整驗證數據。

同步放行（Concurrent Release）

這是一個需要謹慎使用的機制。允許在PPQ尚未全部完成前放行某些批次，但條件是：該批次已符合預定的放行標準，且放行決定基於科學依據。不建議作為常規操作。

持續製程驗證（CPV）

CPV = Stage 3的正式名稱。它不是一次性活動，而是永久性監控體系。只要產品還在商業生產，CPV就必須持續運行。這也是FDA 2011年新架構的最大改變之一——驗證不再「完成」，而是持續進行。

績效指標（Performance Indicators）

在CPV框架下，典型績效指標包括：批次良率、OOS率、偏差率、Cpk趨勢。這些指標需在CPV計畫中預先定義，並定期在周期報告中呈現。

製程設計 vs. 製程確認 vs. 製程驗證

三個易混淆的術語：

  • 製程設計 = Stage 1，定義製程
  • 製程確認 = Stage 2，確認製程
  • 製程驗證 = 三個Stage的總和，是完整概念

管制狀態（State of Control）

這是CPV的最終目標。它不只是「產品合格」，而是「製程持續可預測」。SPC（統計製程管制）圖中，數據點在管制界限內且無特殊趨勢，才代表真正的管制狀態。

Bilingual Glossary — Full Reference Table | 雙語術語完整參考表

All key terms from the FDA guidance and the broader process validation context. 包含FDA指引中的正式術語及製程驗證廣義背景下的核心詞彙。

# English Term 中文術語 Definition | 定義 Commentary | 解析與應用
1 Process Validation 製程驗證 Collection and evaluation of data from process design through commercial production establishing scientific evidence that a process consistently delivers quality products. 強調「整個生命週期」的數據收集——不只是PPQ批次，而是從設計到商業生產的連續過程。
2 Process Design 製程設計 Defining the commercial manufacturing process based on knowledge gained through development and scale-up activities. Stage 1的正式名稱。重點是「定義」——確定CPP、CQA、設計空間，並記錄於製程開發報告中。
3 Process Qualification (PPQ) 製程確認 Confirming that the manufacturing process as designed is capable of reproducible commercial manufacturing. Stage 2的正式名稱。「確認」意味著Stage 1定義的製程，在商業規模下能「可重現地」生產出符合規格的產品。
4 Continued Process Verification (CPV) 持續製程驗證 Assuring that during routine production the process remains in a state of control. Stage 3的正式名稱。CPV是永久性的——只要產品還在生產，CPV就不能停止。這是2011年指引的重大創新。
5 Critical Quality Attribute (CQA) 關鍵品質屬性 A physical, chemical, biological, or microbiological property or characteristic that should be within an appropriate limit, range, or distribution to ensure the desired product quality. CQA是產品品質的「DNA」。例如：無菌注射劑的CQAs包含無菌性、效力、可見異物、pH。所有驗證活動都以確保CQA為核心。
6 Critical Process Parameter (CPP) 關鍵製程參數 A process parameter whose variability has an impact on a critical quality attribute and therefore should be monitored or controlled to ensure the process produces the desired quality. CPP是影響CQA的製程「開關」。例如：滅菌溫度、過濾壓力。PPQ期間，CPP必須在已驗證範圍內操作並記錄。
7 Design Space 設計空間 The multidimensional combination and interaction of input variables (e.g., material attributes) and process parameters that have been demonstrated to provide assurance of quality. (ICH Q8) 在設計空間內操作，不視為變更。超出設計空間，才需要啟動變更控制向FDA申報。這給了製藥公司更大的製程操作彈性。
8 Normal Operating Range (NOR) 正常操作範圍 The range established around a setpoint within which a process parameter is controlled during normal operations. NOR ⊂ PAR ⊂ Design Space。NOR是日常操作的「巡航高度」，PAR是設計空間內的「安全飛行範圍」。PPQ批次需在NOR內操作。
9 Proven Acceptable Range (PAR) 已驗證可接受範圍 A characterized range for a process parameter for which operation within this range, while keeping other parameters constant, will result in producing a material meeting relevant quality criteria. PAR通過DoE研究確立，代表製程參數可以變動的邊界。申報至藥證中，移動操作條件至PAR外才需要補充申請。
10 State of Control 管制狀態 A condition in which the set of controls consistently provides assurance of continued process performance and product quality. (ICH Q10) State of Control是CPV的終極目標，也是FDA查廠時評估的核心問題：「你的製程今天還在管制中嗎？」SPC圖是最直接的證明工具。
11 Concurrent Release 同步放行 Releasing a lot of finished product manufactured following a qualification protocol that meets lot release criteria before the entire study protocol has been executed. 同步放行是例外機制，非常規作法。適用情境：孤兒藥或罕見病藥品供應緊迫時。需要QA特別審核並記錄決策依據。
12 Performance Indicators 績效指標 Measurable values used to quantify quality objectives to reflect the performance of an organization, process or system. (ICH Q10) CPV報告的主體內容。常見KPIs：批次良率 ≥ 98%、OOS率 ≤ 0.5%、Cpk ≥ 1.33。需在CPV計畫中預先定義目標值。
13 Process Capability 製程能力 Ability of a process to produce a product that will fulfill the requirements. Can be defined statistically (e.g., Cpk). (ISO 9000:2005) Cpk = min[(USL-μ)/3σ, (μ-LSL)/3σ]。Cpk ≥ 1.33 = 4σ品質水準，一般為製藥業最低可接受標準；Cpk ≥ 1.67 = 5σ，為較嚴格要求。
14 Quality 品質 The degree to which a set of inherent properties of a product, system, or process fulfils requirements. (ICH Q9) FDA採用ICH Q9對品質的定義，強調「符合要求的程度」而非絕對的完美。這個定義支撐了QRM（品質風險管理）的框架。
15 Commercial Manufacturing Process 商業製造製程 The manufacturing process resulting in commercial product. Does not include clinical trial or treatment IND material. 這個定義劃清了臨床批與商業批的界線。只有商業製造製程才需要完整的三階段製程驗證——臨床批適用不同（但遞增）的CGMP要求。
16 Validation Master Plan (VMP) 驗證總計畫 A document that describes the overall philosophy, intentions, and approach to be used to establish performance adequacy. Defines the scope, responsibilities, and schedule for all validation activities. VMP是驗證的「憲法」。FDA雖未強制要求VMP的特定格式，但通常在藥品申請中需提交。CDMO應在接受技術轉移後立即制定VMP。
17 PPQ Protocol 製程確認規程 A written document that specifies the manufacturing conditions, controls, testing, and expected outcomes for PPQ execution. Must be approved by the Quality Unit before execution. PPQ Protocol是Stage 2的核心文件。必須在執行前由QA批准，且必須預先定義驗收標準（pre-defined acceptance criteria）——不能在看到數據後才定標準。
18 Annual Product Review (APR) 年度產品品質回顧 A systematic review of product quality data performed annually to verify consistency of the process and identify product or process improvements. Required by 21 CFR § 211.180(e). APR是Stage 3 CPV框架的重要組成部分。它整合全年的批次數據、OOS、偏差、變更，評估製程是否持續穩定。法規要求每年完成。

Key Message | 核心訊息

Documentation IS Validation

"If it wasn't documented, it wasn't done."
— GMP Industry Principle

The FDA's 2011 Process Validation Guidance makes clear that documentation is not merely administrative overhead — it is the substance of validation itself. For regulatory purposes:

  • An undocumented validation does not exist. No matter how scientifically sound the work, if it cannot be demonstrated through records, it has no regulatory standing.
  • Documentation enables accountability. The Quality Unit's ability to make science-based release decisions depends entirely on complete, accurate, and accessible records.
  • Documentation enables learning. The lifecycle approach to process validation requires that knowledge is captured and transferred — documentation is the only mechanism for doing so across time, teams, and regulatory submissions.
  • Documentation protects patients. The entire purpose of CGMP documentation is to create an auditable trail that demonstrates a manufacturer's commitment to consistent product quality — ultimately protecting patient safety.

This principle applies at every stage: the development knowledge captured in Stage 1 documents becomes the scientific justification for Stage 2 PPQ design; the PPQ evidence becomes the foundation for Stage 3 CPV; and the ongoing CPV records become the evidence presented in regulatory submissions and inspections.

文件即驗證的本質

「沒有記錄，就是沒有做過。」
— GMP產業通行原則

FDA 2011年製程驗證指引的核心訊息之一：文件不是行政負擔，文件就是驗證本身。從法規角度看：

沒有文件 = 驗證不存在

無論製程實際上有多穩定、科學工作有多嚴謹，若無法以記錄證明，在法規上等同於未執行。FDA查廠時，稽查員看的是文件，不是你的口頭描述。

三階段文件的邏輯鏈

  • Stage 1文件（開發知識）→ 成為Stage 2 PPQ設計的科學依據
  • Stage 2文件（PPQ證據）→ 成為Stage 3 CPV的基準線
  • Stage 3文件（CPV記錄）→ 成為下一次藥證申請、變更申請的數據來源

三個階段的文件形成閉環，缺少任何一環，整個驗證架構就不完整。

CDMO的文件文化建立

作為CDMO，服務多個客戶的驗證專案，文件管理尤為重要：

  • 建立標準化文件模板（減少人為差異）
  • 確保文件系統符合21 CFR Part 11（電子記錄）
  • 技術轉移時明確文件所有權（客戶 vs. CDMO）
  • 定期文件培訓（防止「for training only」心態）
  • GMP ready at all times——文件隨時可接受FDA稽查

思考練習 | Reflection

如果明天FDA稽查員出現在你的設施，要求查看最近一個PPQ批次的完整文件包，你能在30分鐘內提供以下所有文件嗎？

  • QA事前批准的PPQ Protocol
  • 完整的批次製造記錄
  • 所有在製品檢測數據
  • 偏差報告（如有）
  • 分析方法驗證報告
  • 統計分析結果
  • QA批准的PPQ Final Report

如果答案是「不確定」，那就是你的文件管理系統需要改進的地方。

Electronic Records & 21 CFR Part 11 | 電子記錄與法規要求

21 CFR Part 11 — Core Requirements

When validation records are maintained electronically, 21 CFR Part 11 applies. Key requirements include:

  • Audit Trail — All record creation, modification, and deletion must be time-stamped and attributed to the individual who made the change.
  • Electronic Signatures — Must be legally binding, attributable to a unique individual, and cannot be repudiated.
  • System Validation (CSV) — The computerized system used to maintain records must itself be validated (Computer System Validation).
  • Access Controls — Role-based permissions prevent unauthorized modification.
  • Record Integrity — Data must be protected from alteration or loss over the required retention period.
  • Backup and Recovery — Validated backup procedures and tested recovery capability.

The FDA's expectation is that electronic records provide at least the same level of reliability and trustworthiness as paper records they replace.

電子記錄的CDMO實務重點

CSV（電腦系統驗證）

用於保存PPQ/CPV數據的任何電子系統（LIMS、MES、EBR）都必須完成CSV。驗證範疇包括：

  • 使用者需求規格（URS）
  • 功能規格（FS）
  • IQ / OQ / PQ（或GAMP 5等效框架）
  • 稽核追蹤驗證

資料完整性（Data Integrity）

FDA近年查廠中，資料完整性違規是最常見的483觀察事項之一。ALCOA+ 原則必須貫穿所有記錄系統：

  • Attributable — 可歸屬
  • Legible — 清晰可讀
  • Contemporaneous — 即時記錄
  • Original — 原始數據
  • Accurate — 精確
  • + Complete, Consistent, Enduring, Available
