I

Statutory Requirements for Process Validation

製程驗證的法定要求  |  FDCA § 501(a)(2)(B) & cGMP Framework

Original Text — FDA Guidance (January 2011)

Process validation for drugs (finished pharmaceuticals and components) is a legally enforceable requirement under section 501(a)(2)(B) of the Act (21 U.S.C. 351(a)(2)(B)), which states the following:

A drug . . . shall be deemed to be adulterated . . . if . . . the methods used in, or the facilities or controls used for, its manufacture, processing, packing, or holding do not conform to or are not operated or administered in conformity with current good manufacturing practice to assure that such drug meets the requirements of this Act as to safety and has the identity and strength, and meets the quality and purity characteristics, which it purports or is represented to possess.

FDA regulations describing current good manufacturing practice (CGMP) for finished pharmaceuticals are provided in 21 CFR parts 210 and 211.

The CGMP regulations require that manufacturing processes be designed and controlled to assure that in-process materials and the finished product meet predetermined quality requirements and do so consistently and reliably. Process validation is required, in both general and specific terms, by the CGMP regulations in parts 210 and 211.

§ 211.100(a) — "There shall be written procedures for production and process control designed to assure that the drug products have the identity, strength, quality, and purity they purport or are represented to possess."

This regulation requires manufacturers to design a process, including operations and controls, which results in a product meeting these attributes.

中文解說 & 實務應用

法律層級：為何製程驗證不可選擇？

許多人誤以為製程驗證只是「最佳實務」。事實上，FDCA 第 501(a)(2)(B) 條直接規定：若製造方法不符合 cGMP，藥品即被視為摻假品（adulterated）。這意味著：

  • 不符合 cGMP = 產品可被強制召回或扣押
  • FDA 可在無法院命令下發布警告信或拒絕進口
  • 在 CDMO 環境，合約若要求符合 cGMP，驗證義務自動適用

比喻：餐廳衛生法規 vs. 製藥 cGMP

就如同衛生主管機關規定餐廳必須控制食品溫度，而不只是「希望」廚師留意，FDA 的法律框架同樣要求製造商以系統化方式證明製程產出符合品質要求——不是靠運氣，而是靠設計。製程驗證就是這個「系統化證明」的機制。

CDMO 實務重點：法規責任歸屬

在委託製造場景中，法規責任仍由持有 NDA/ANDA 的藥廠（MAH）承擔，但 CDMO 作為實際製造方，必須提供完整的製程驗證數據。查廠時 FDA 會直接稽核 CDMO 設施，任何缺失都可能導致客戶產品無法出貨。

思考練習 1

Q：若一家 CDMO 接到新客戶的技術轉移案，對方聲稱「此製程已在歐盟經過驗證」，是否可以直接省略 FDA 要求的製程驗證？

引導：考量 FDA 管轄範圍、數據可移植性、以及 21 CFR 211 對「製造商」的定義。

§100

21 CFR § 211.100(a) — Production and Process Controls

生產與製程管制  |  書面程序設計的法定根基

Regulatory Text & Context

The foundation for process validation is provided in § 211.100(a), which states:

"There shall be written procedures for production and process control designed to assure that the drug products have the identity, strength, quality, and purity they purport or are represented to possess..."

This regulation requires manufacturers to design a process, including operations and controls, which results in a product meeting these attributes.

The CGMP regulations require that manufacturing processes be:

  • Designed to meet predetermined quality requirements
  • Controlled to meet these requirements
  • Operated consistently and reliably

Process validation is required in both general and specific terms by the CGMP regulations in parts 210 and 211. Focusing exclusively on qualification efforts without also understanding the manufacturing process and associated variations may not lead to adequate assurance of quality.

Key principle: Manufacturers must design a process that reliably delivers the intended quality — not merely test whether a batch passes.

中文解說：「設計」vs.「測試」的根本差異

§211.100(a) 的核心哲學

這條法規強調的不是「最終測試合格」，而是製程設計本身必須確保品質。這是 FDA 從舊式「測試品質進產品（test quality in）」轉向「設計品質入產品（build quality in）」的法規基礎。

  • 舊思維：生產完成 → 測試 → 如果通過就放行
  • 新思維（FDA 要求）：了解製程 → 設計管制策略 → 製程本身確保品質 → 測試作為確認

比喻：橋梁結構工程

一座橋樑不是靠「每天用重車壓一次、沒倒就算安全」來驗證品質。工程師透過材料科學、載重計算與設計規範，確保橋梁在設計階段就具備足夠強度。藥品製程驗證的邏輯相同：理解製程，設計控制，然後以驗證數據確認設計是否有效。

無菌充填的實務意涵

在無菌充填操作中，§211.100(a) 意味著：灌裝速度、氮氣覆蓋、橡皮塞壓合力等參數不是隨意設定——每個書面程序都應有科學依據，能連結到產品的品質屬性（如無菌保證水準、密封完整性）。

思考練習 2

Q：您的工廠正在撰寫一個新針劑充填製程的批次製造記錄（BMR）。依據 §211.100(a)，哪些類型的製程參數應納入書面程序，且必須說明設定依據？

引導：考量 identity（含量）、strength（效價）、quality（外觀/pH/不溶性微粒）、purity（雜質）各自對應的製程步驟。

思考練習 3

Q：一位生產主管說：「我們上個月生產的 20 批都通過 QC 放行，這樣就算製程已驗證了嗎？」請用 §211.100(a) 的精神回應這個說法。

§110

21 CFR § 211.110 — In-Process Sampling & Specifications

製程中取樣與規格  |  管制變異的法規工具

Original Text — §211.110(a) & (b)

§ 211.110(a) — Sampling and testing of in-process materials and drug products requires that control procedures ". . . be established to monitor the output and to validate the performance of those manufacturing processes that may be responsible for causing variability in the characteristics of in-process material and the drug product."

Under this regulation, even well-designed processes must include in-process control procedures to assure final product quality.

In addition, the CGMP regulations regarding sampling set forth a number of requirements for validation:

  • Samples must represent the batch under analysis (§ 211.160(b)(3))
  • The sampling plan must result in statistical confidence (§ 211.165(c) and (d))
  • The batch must meet its predetermined specifications (§ 211.165(a))
§ 211.110(b) — Two principles for in-process specifications:
  1. In-process specifications "shall be consistent with drug product final specifications."
  2. In-process specifications "shall be derived from previous acceptable process average and process variability estimates where possible and determined by the application of suitable statistical procedures where appropriate."

This requirement establishes the need for manufacturers to analyze process performance and control batch-to-batch variability.

中文解說：製程中管制的雙重目的

§211.110(a)：為何「好設計」仍需製程中管制？

即使製程設計完善，原物料批次差異、設備磨損、人員操作差異都可能引入變異。FDA 明確要求製程中管制程序必須能：

  • 監測輸出（monitor output）：即時偵測偏差趨勢，不等到最終放行才發現
  • 驗證效能（validate performance）：持續確認製程運作在驗證條件內

對無菌充填而言，這涵蓋環境監控、容器完整性測試、裝量檢查等在線管制。

§211.110(b)：兩個原則的實務意義

原則一（與最終規格一致）：若最終產品 pH 規格為 6.8–7.2，製程中 pH 規格不得設為 6.5–7.5，否則中間品即使通過也可能在後續步驟漂移超出最終規格。

原則二（由統計推導）：製程中規格不能憑感覺設定，必須基於歷史製程平均值與變異估計。這直接呼應 Stage 3 持續製程查驗（CPV）所需的統計分析能力。

比喻：飛機飛行中的儀器監控

飛行前的地面檢查（類比驗證前的設計確認）固然重要，但飛行中駕駛必須持續監控高度、速度、引擎參數（類比製程中管制）。若只有起飛前的檢查而沒有飛行中監控，任何在途中出現的偏差都無法即時發現。§211.110(a) 要求的就是這個「飛行中監控」機制。

無菌充填 CDMO 具體應用

以凍乾注射劑為例，§211.110 在實務中的對應項目：

  • 充填重量：每隔 N 支抽查，規格需與最終含量規格統計一致
  • 凍乾終點：殘存水分的製程中測量（Karl Fischer），規格由歷史批次變異推導
  • 班次取樣計劃：需有統計依據，不能只抽「方便取樣」的位置

思考練習 4

Q：您的工廠正在設定一個新蛋白質製劑充填製程的製程中 pH 規格。最終產品規格為 pH 6.5–7.0，您有 15 批歷史數據，充填後 pH 平均為 6.74，標準差為 0.08。依 §211.110(b) 原則二，您會如何推導合理的製程中 pH 規格？需要哪些統計工具？

思考練習 5

Q：FDA 查廠官員問：「您的製程中取樣計劃如何符合 §211.165(c) 的統計信心要求？」如果您的回答是「我們每批抽取 5 個樣品，這是行業慣例」，這樣的答覆是否充分？請說明理由。

§180

21 CFR § 211.180(e) — Periodic Review & Process Maintenance

定期審查與製程維護  |  Stage 3 持續查驗的法規根基

Regulatory Basis for Continuous Process Monitoring

§ 211.180(e) requires that information and data about product quality and manufacturing experience be periodically reviewed to determine whether any changes to the established process are warranted.

Ongoing feedback about product quality and process performance is an essential feature of process maintenance.

In addition, the CGMP regulations also describe facilities and equipment requirements that are integral to process validation:

§ 211.42 — Facilities must be of suitable size, construction, and location to facilitate proper operations.
§ 211.63 — Equipment must be of appropriate design, adequate size, and suitably located to facilitate operations for its intended use.
§ 211.68 — Automated, mechanical, and electronic equipment must be calibrated, inspected, or checked according to a written program designed to assure proper performance.

Summary: The CGMP regulations require that manufacturing processes be designed and controlled to assure that in-process materials and the finished product meet predetermined quality requirements and do so consistently and reliably.

中文解說：製程驗證的「後半段」義務

§211.180(e) 與 Stage 3 CPV 的連結

這條法規是 FDA 2011 年指引中「Stage 3：持續製程查驗（Continued Process Verification, CPV）」的法規根據。它要求：

  • 定期（periodically）——不是只在有問題時才審查
  • 審查品質數據與製造經驗——包括 OOS/OOT 趨勢、客訴、批次拒絕率
  • 判斷是否需要變更——主動識別改善機會，而非被動等待問題

比喻：汽車定期保養 vs. 等待拋錨

§211.180(e) 的精神就像定期保養手冊：汽車製造商要求每 10,000 公里更換機油，不是因為引擎已經壞了，而是為了維持設計效能。製藥廠商同樣需要定期「健康檢查」其製程，確認它仍在驗證狀態（state of control）內運作。

設備相關條文（§211.42/63/68）的驗證意涵

這三條法規構成設備確認（IQ/OQ/PQ）的法規基礎：

  • §211.42 → 廠房設計 / 空調系統確認（HVAC qualification）
  • §211.63 → 設備設計確認（DQ）與安裝確認（IQ）
  • §211.68 → 校驗計劃（calibration program）/ 儀器確認（OQ）

在無菌充填環境，這三條法規同時支撐了潔淨室認證、充填機確認、以及所有量測儀器的校驗要求。

思考練習 6

Q：您的工廠每季進行產品品質審查（PQR）。一項已商業化五年的凍乾產品，近兩季的凍乾週期時間平均增加了 4%，但所有批次均符合規格放行。依 §211.180(e)，這個趨勢是否需要採取行動？請描述您的決策流程。

II

General Considerations for Process Validation

製程驗證的一般考量  |  科學理解、QbD 原則與風險導向方法

FDA's Foundational Framework

In all stages of the product lifecycle, good project management and good archiving that capture scientific knowledge will make the process validation program more effective and efficient.

The following practices should ensure uniform collection and assessment of information about the process and enhance the accessibility of such information later in the product lifecycle:

  • An integrated team approach to process validation that includes expertise from a variety of disciplines (process engineering, industrial pharmacy, analytical chemistry, microbiology, statistics, manufacturing, and quality assurance). Project plans, along with the full support of senior management, are essential elements for success.
  • Throughout the product lifecycle, various studies can be initiated to discover, observe, correlate, or confirm information about the product and process. All studies should be planned and conducted according to sound scientific principles, appropriately documented, and approved in accordance with established procedure appropriate for the stage of the lifecycle.
"Focusing exclusively on qualification efforts without also understanding the manufacturing process and associated variations may not lead to adequate assurance of quality."

After establishing and confirming the process, manufacturers must maintain the process in a state of control over the life of the process, even as materials, equipment, production environment, personnel, and manufacturing procedures change.

中文解說：從「批次思維」到「生命週期思維」

核心訊息：理解 > 確認（Understand > Qualify）

FDA 的這段文字是整個 2011 年指引的哲學核心：單純做確認（qualification）而不理解製程，不足以保證品質。

這對業界的衝擊在於：

  • 傳統做法：按固定格式完成 IQ/OQ/PQ，收到報告 = 驗證完成
  • FDA 期望：在驗證前就充分理解製程，知道哪些變數影響品質、影響程度如何

「所有材料、設備、環境、人員、程序都可能改變」

這句話特別重要：FDA 明確說明，即使原材料供應商更換、設備老化更新、操作員流動，製造商都有責任維持製程在管制狀態（state of control）。這是持續製程驗證（CPV）存在的根本原因，也是 CDMO 在技術轉移時必須建立完整製程理解的理由。

思考練習 7

Q：您的 CDMO 接手一個已商業化三年的產品，原廠提供的驗證報告顯示三批 PPQ 均通過。但您的工廠設備型號不同，原料供應商也不同。依 FDA 的「生命週期」觀點，這份驗證報告對您的新廠有多大效力？您需要補充哪些工作？

Team

Integrated Team Approach & Scientific Knowledge Management

跨職能整合團隊 & 科學知識管理  |  製程驗證的組織基礎

FDA Requirements for Team Structure & Documentation

FDA recommends an integrated team approach to process validation that draws on multiple disciplines. The disciplines listed include:

  • Process engineering
  • Industrial pharmacy
  • Analytical chemistry
  • Microbiology
  • Statistics
  • Manufacturing (operations)
  • Quality assurance

Project plans, along with the full support of senior management, are essential elements for success.

Throughout the product lifecycle, studies should be:

  • Planned and conducted according to sound scientific principles
  • Appropriately documented
  • Approved in accordance with established procedures for the relevant lifecycle stage

The terms attribute(s) (quality, product, component) and parameter(s) (process, operating, equipment) are not categorized with respect to criticality in this guidance. With a lifecycle approach employing risk-based decision making, the perception of criticality as a continuum rather than a binary state is more useful.

中文解說：為何需要多學科整合？

比喻：航空器適航認證

一架新型飛機的適航認證需要機械工程師、氣動工程師、航電工程師、飛行員、管制官員共同參與，沒有任何單一職能可以獨立完成。製程驗證的跨職能要求出自同樣邏輯：任何單一部門都無法全面評估一個複雜製藥製程的所有風險面向。

CDMO 挑戰：高離職率與知識管理

FDA 強調「良好文件歸檔」的背後，是對一個現實的認知：人員流動（尤其在 CDMO 環境）會造成製程知識流失。當年做驗證的人離職後，接手者必須能從文件中重建足夠的製程理解。這使得「知識管理系統」成為製程驗證的隱形基礎建設。

Risk

Criticality as a Continuum: Risk-Based Thinking

關鍵性為連續譜：風險導向思維  |  ICH Q9 與 QbD 的實踐

FDA's Risk-Proportionate Approach

The terms attribute(s) and parameter(s) are not categorized with respect to criticality in this guidance. With a lifecycle approach to process validation that employs risk-based decision making throughout that lifecycle, the perception of criticality as a continuum rather than a binary state is more useful.

All attributes and parameters should be evaluated in terms of their:

  • Roles in the process
  • Impact on the product or in-process material
  • Reevaluated as new information becomes available

The degree of control over those attributes or parameters should be commensurate with their risk to the process and process output. A higher degree of control is appropriate for attributes or parameters that pose a higher risk.

"The Agency recognizes that terminology usage can vary and expects that each manufacturer will communicate the meaning and intent of its terminology and categorization to the Agency."

Many products are single-source or involve complicated manufacturing processes. Homogeneity within a batch and consistency between batches are goals of process validation activities. Validation offers assurance that a process is reasonably protected against sources of variability that could affect production output, cause supply problems, and negatively affect public health.

中文解說：打破「關鍵/非關鍵」的二元思維

「連續譜」思維的實務意義

傳統做法傾向於將製程參數分類為「關鍵（critical）」或「非關鍵（non-critical）」兩類，然後對「關鍵」項目施以嚴格管制，「非關鍵」項目則幾乎不管。FDA 在此明確說：這種二元分類是不夠用的。

更有用的框架是：

  • 每個參數都有一個影響分數（對 CQA 的衝擊）
  • 管制嚴格度與影響分數成比例
  • 隨著新數據累積，影響分數可以動態更新
CPP 關鍵製程參數
→
製程步驟 單元操作
→
CQA 關鍵品質屬性

CPP 的偏離 → 透過製程機制 → 影響 CQA 的符合性

比喻：飛機引擎的風險管理

引擎螺絲和客艙扶手螺絲都是「螺絲」，但風險等級天壤之別。沒有工程師會說「螺絲是關鍵零件，所以每顆螺絲都用同等級的質量管制」——而是按照失效後果嚴重性分配管制資源。製程參數的關鍵性評估邏輯完全相同。

「批內均一性 + 批間一致性」雙重目標

FDA 明確指出這兩者是製程驗證的目標：

  • 批內均一性（Homogeneity within a batch）：同一批內每個劑量單位含量、效力、外觀一致。對混合製程、充填製程尤為關鍵。
  • 批間一致性（Consistency between batches）：不同批次在相同條件下生產，性能表現一致。這是「再現性（reproducibility）」的法規要求。

QbD Principles, Design Space & Understanding Variability

品質源於設計原則、設計空間與變異性理解  |  ICH Q8/Q9/Q10 框架整合

Scientific Understanding as Validation Foundation

FDA's 2011 guidance aligns with the Quality by Design (QbD) framework established under ICH Q8/Q9/Q10. The core principles embedded in Sections I-II include:

  • Design of Experiments (DOE): Systematic studies to understand how process variables interact and impact product quality attributes.
  • Risk Analysis: Formal tools (FMEA, fishbone diagrams, risk ranking) to identify and prioritize sources of variability before validation begins.
  • Prior Knowledge: Platform knowledge, development data, clinical manufacturing data, and literature all inform the validation strategy.
  • Design Space: The multidimensional combination of input variables and process parameters that have been demonstrated to provide assurance of quality. Operating within the design space is not considered a change.
  • Control Strategy: The planned set of controls derived from current product and process understanding that ensures process performance and product quality.
Key insight from the guidance: "Process understanding is the foundation for reducing validation burden." Manufacturers who deeply understand their processes need fewer confirmatory experiments because they can predict how the process will behave under variation.

Manufacturers should use ongoing programs to collect and analyze product and process data to evaluate the state of control of the process. These programs may identify process or product problems or opportunities for process improvements.

Manufacturers of legacy products can take advantage of the knowledge gained from the original process development and qualification work as well as manufacturing experience to continually improve their processes. Implementation of these recommendations for legacy products and processes would likely begin with Stage 3 activities.

QbD 框架核心三要素

  • 目標產品品質概況 (QTPP)：定義產品必須達到的品質特徵（用途、劑型、給藥途徑、效價等）
  • 關鍵品質屬性 (CQA)：從 QTPP 推導出必須管控的產品屬性（如含量均勻度、無菌性、可見微粒）
  • 關鍵製程參數 (CPP)：對 CQA 有顯著影響且必須加以監控/管制的製程參數

「設計空間」與驗證的關係

ICH Q8 定義的設計空間（Design Space）是 QbD 的核心工具。當製造商能夠：

  1. 透過 DOE 證明在設計空間內操作均能滿足 CQA
  2. 建立穩健的製程設計空間

則在設計空間內的操作調整不視為法規意義上的製程變更（change），毋需補充驗證。這就是「理解製程 = 減少驗證負擔」的具體機制。

Legacy 產品的特別考量

許多已商業化的老產品（legacy products）當初並非以 QbD 框架開發，驗證文件可能只有三批 PPQ 報告，缺乏深層的製程理解文件。FDA 建議這類產品從 Stage 3（CPV）開始著手，逐步補充製程知識，而不是要求重做全部 Stage 1-2 活動。對 CDMO 而言，接手此類產品需評估現有文件的知識密度，判斷是否有足夠基礎支持後續製程管理。

比喻：地圖 vs. 衛星導航

傳統的三批 PPQ 驗證就像用一張舊地圖導航：你知道一條路，只要按照那條路走就能到目的地，但若路封閉了，你不知道替代路徑。QbD 框架建立的是衛星導航系統：你理解整個道路網絡（設計空間），因此任何合理的路徑調整都不需要重新「驗證整個旅程」。

識別方法 & 風險評估工具

如何識別 CQA？

  1. 從 QTPP 出發，列出所有可能的品質屬性
  2. 評估每個屬性若超出規格，對患者安全/療效的影響嚴重性
  3. 嚴重性高的屬性 = CQA（需監控且有明確規格）
  4. 例如：無菌注射劑的「無菌性」嚴重性 = 最高 → 必為 CQA

如何識別 CPP？

  1. 列出所有製程參數（每個單元操作的輸入變數）
  2. 透過 DOE 或科學推理，評估每個參數對各 CQA 的影響
  3. 影響顯著 = CPP；影響可忽略 = 一般製程參數（需記錄但管控強度較低）
  4. 管控強度與風險等級成比例（風險連續譜原則）

An Important Nuance: Not All CPP Excursions Reject a Batch

It is important to distinguish between a CPP excursion and a CQA failure. A CPP out of range does not automatically mean the batch fails — it triggers a risk assessment to determine whether the out-of-range CPP actually impacted any CQA. This is where thorough process understanding (Stage 1 data) becomes critical: manufacturers who understand the quantitative relationship between CPP and CQA can make science-based batch disposition decisions.

一個重要區分：CPP 偏差 ≠ 批次拒絕

CPP 超出操作範圍不等於自動拒批。它觸發的是偏差調查 + 影響評估：該 CPP 的偏差是否實際影響了 CQA？這個評估需要充分的製程理解數據作基礎（Stage 1 研究的 CPP-CQA 關係曲線）。這正是「製程理解降低驗證負擔」的另一個維度：深度理解製程，讓批次拋棄決策更有科學依據，而非保守地拒絕一切有偏差的批次。

Core Message / 核心訊息

Process understanding is the foundation for reducing validation burden.

製程理解是降低驗證負擔的根本基礎。

FDA does not expect manufacturers to simply run three batches and call it validated. The Agency expects manufacturers to truly understand their processes — how each parameter affects quality, where variability originates, and how the process can be controlled to consistently deliver the intended quality. Validation then becomes the formal confirmation of that understanding, not a substitute for it.

FDA 的期望不是「跑三批通過就算驗證」，而是要製造商真正理解製程——每個參數如何影響品質、變異從何而來、如何管制以持續交付目標品質。驗證是這種理解的正式確認，而非替代品。

How Process Understanding Reduces Validation Burden

  • Fewer PPQ batches needed: When Stage 1 DOE data fully characterizes the design space, Stage 2 PPQ becomes confirmatory rather than exploratory.
  • Design space flexibility: Manufacturers operating within a proven design space can make adjustments without full revalidation.
  • Science-based batch disposition: CPP excursions can be assessed against known CPP-CQA relationships rather than defaulting to rejection.
  • Efficient CPV programs: Understanding which parameters matter most enables targeted, efficient ongoing monitoring.

給 CDMO 從業者的結語

Section I-II 對 CDMO 的三個核心意涵

  1. 法規義務是不可談判的底線：FDCA 501(a)(2)(B) 和 21 CFR 210/211 構成的法規框架，讓製程驗證在美國市場是法律要求，不是選擇。CDMO 無法以「這是客戶的責任」規避。
  2. 理解製程是競爭力，不只是合規：真正理解製程的 CDMO 可以在技術問題出現時做出更快的科學判斷，減少批次失敗，降低給客戶帶來的延遲風險。這是可以差異化的核心能力。
  3. 知識管理是基礎建設：FDA 強調「良好文件歸檔」的背後，是對製程知識傳承的重視。在人員流動率高的 CDMO 環境，知識管理系統（如 SOP 品質、研究報告可追溯性）的完善程度，直接影響製程驗證的長期有效性。

最後一個比喻：醫師與處方

一位好醫師不會因為「上次給這個病人開這個藥有效」就不再診斷直接開同樣的處方——他會理解疾病機制、藥物作用原理，並根據患者當前狀況判斷。FDA 對製造商的期望類似：不是「複製上次的驗證方案」，而是真正理解製程，讓每次的製造決策都有科學依據。
