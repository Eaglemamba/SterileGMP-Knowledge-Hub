0.1 What Is an IND and Why Does It Exist?

原文內容 Original Text

Statutory Basis: Section 505(i) of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FD&C Act) prohibits the interstate shipment of any drug intended for investigational use in humans unless the sponsor has an effective Investigational New Drug Application on file with FDA. This is the foundational legal hook that makes Part 312 operative.

Purpose of the IND: The IND exists to give FDA the opportunity to review safety data and study protocols before clinical trials begin. Its twin purposes are:

  1. Ensuring adequate preclinical data exists to support the proposed human studies (safety threshold), and
  2. Ensuring adequate human subject protections are in place (ethical threshold).

How the IND becomes effective: An IND is not approved by FDA — it becomes effective automatically 30 calendar days after FDA receives a complete submission, unless FDA places the study on clinical hold within that window. A clinical hold is an order from FDA to delay or suspend a proposed or ongoing clinical investigation.

"A sponsor may begin a clinical investigation 30 days after FDA receives the IND, or on earlier notification by FDA that the investigation may begin." — 21 CFR §312.40(b)

Two principal IND types:

  • Commercial IND: Sponsor is a pharmaceutical or biotechnology company; the ultimate goal is submission of a New Drug Application (NDA) or Biologics License Application (BLA). Full IND with complete CMC, pharmacology, and clinical protocol sections required.
  • Research (Investigator) IND: Sponsor-investigator is typically an academic physician or institution; no commercial intent. FDA applies somewhat reduced expectations, but the IND must still meet Part 312 requirements.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 — IND vs NDA：申請 ≠ 批准

IND（試驗中新藥申請） 是一份「我想要開始做人體試驗」的通知書，不是 FDA 的批准文件。IND 的生效方式是「沉默同意」——FDA 在 30 天內未發出臨床擱置，即視為有效。

NDA / BLA（新藥申請 / 生物製品許可申請） 才是真正的「上市批准」——需要 FDA 審查全部 Phase 1–3 的安全性與有效性資料後，主動核發批准函。

簡單對照：IND = 向城市申請「動工許可」（開工前等 30 天看是否被叫停）；NDA/BLA = 最終的「使用執照」（需要驗收合格才能核發）。

比喻說明 — IND 是「建築開工許可」

想像你要蓋一棟醫院（開發一個新藥）：

  • IND 就是你向城市規劃局（FDA）提交「施工申請書」。你繳交設計圖（CMC 資料）、土地安全報告（動物毒理學）和施工計畫（臨床方案）。
  • 城市局有 30 天 審查你的計畫。如果他們認為施工會危及周邊居民（受試者安全疑慮），他們會發出「停工令」（clinical hold）。
  • 如果 30 天過了什麼都沒說，你就可以開工（開始人體試驗）。
  • NDA/BLA 是最終的「建築驗收合格書」——蓋完整棟醫院、全部驗收通過後才能正式開業（上市銷售）。

關鍵差異：開工不需要城市局「批准」，只需要他們「不反對」。上市才需要他們正式核發許可。

常見誤解澄清 Common Misconception

誤解：「IND 就是 FDA 批准可以做人體試驗」

正確觀念： IND 生效是 FDA 的「不干預」，而非「主動批准」。差異在於：若 FDA 審查發現安全疑慮，他們可以在 30 天內發出臨床擱置（clinical hold）而不必主動核發任何許可文件。

這意味著：CDMO 若看到客戶說「IND 已批准」，需要核實說法是否準確——正確說法應為「IND 已生效 (IND is effective)」。

0.2 §312.1 — Scope: When Is an IND Required?

原文內容 Original Text

§312.1(a) General scope: Part 312 applies to any clinical investigation of a drug that is subject to section 505(i) of the FD&C Act. An IND is required when a drug is:

  1. Not approved for marketing in the United States (i.e., not the subject of an effective NDA or BLA), AND
  2. Intended for use in a clinical investigation involving human subjects, AND
  3. Intended to be shipped in interstate commerce to conduct the investigation.

IND is required for:

  • Phase 1, 2, and 3 clinical trials for new chemical entities (NCEs) and new biological entities
  • Bioequivalence (BE) studies for certain products (see §312.2(b)(1) exemption below)
  • New indication studies for already-marketed drugs — if the drug crosses state lines in the context of an unapproved use, and the study is not covered by the marketed product labeling
  • New route of administration, new population, new dose range studies for approved drugs when these parameters go beyond the approved label

Key geographic trigger — "interstate commerce": The IND requirement is activated when investigational drug crosses state lines. Purely intrastate investigations with locally manufactured drug may theoretically be exempt, though this is increasingly rare in practice.

"A sponsor shall submit an IND to FDA if the sponsor intends to conduct a clinical investigation with an investigational new drug that is subject to §312.2." — §312.20(a)

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 — 三要件同時成立才需要 IND

IND 要求的觸發需要三個條件同時成立：

  • 條件 1： 藥品在美國尚未獲批上市（或使用超出核准範圍）
  • 條件 2： 用於人體臨床試驗
  • 條件 3： 跨州運輸

只要其中一個條件不成立，就可能不需要 IND（但仍須評估其他豁免條件）。

CDMO 實務應用 — 哪些情況 CDMO 會碰到 IND 相關藥品？

  • Phase 1 臨床批次製造： 客戶提供 IND 號碼，要求 CDMO 製造首批人體試驗用藥品（藥品跨州送到臨床試驗機構 → IND 必要）
  • 已上市藥品的新適應症試驗： 客戶持有核准 NDA，但現在要用同一藥品做癌症新適應症 → 超出核准範圍 → 需要新的 IND
  • 生物相似藥研發： 生物相似藥在申請 BLA 前，也可能需要 IND 來進行臨床比對試驗

重點提示 — 「州際商業」觸發條件

實務上，幾乎所有多中心臨床試驗都會跨州（藥品從製造場地運到各臨床試驗機構），因此「跨州」條件幾乎必然成立。

CDMO 工廠在加州製造藥品，送到紐約的臨床試驗中心 → 跨州 → IND 適用。就算製造廠和試驗中心在同一州，FDA 的規管範圍也涵蓋一切影響州際商業的藥品，實際上很難完全規避 IND 要求。

0.3 §312.2 — Exemptions from IND Requirement

原文內容 Original Text

§312.2 lists specific categories of investigations that are exempt from the IND requirement. Understanding these exemptions is critical — both to avoid unnecessary IND filings and to avoid the error of not filing when one is required.

Exemption 1 — Lawful marketing exemption (§312.2(a)): A drug that is already lawfully marketed in the United States is not subject to IND requirements when used for clinical investigations that:

  • Are not intended to support approval of a new indication or significant labeling change,
  • Are not intended to support a significant change in the advertising for the product,
  • Do not involve a route of administration, dose, or use in a patient population that would significantly increase the risks associated with the product, and
  • Are conducted in compliance with the requirements for review by an Institutional Review Board (§56) and the requirements for informed consent (§50).

Exemption 2 — Bioavailability/bioequivalence (BA/BE) exemption (§312.2(b)(1)): A clinical investigation that is intended to show that a drug product is bioavailable or bioequivalent is exempt from the IND requirement provided all of the following conditions are met:

  • The drug product is not radioactively labeled,
  • The investigation is not intended to support approval of a new indication or significant labeling change,
  • The investigation is not intended to evaluate a new drug product,
  • The investigation involves a single dose of a marketed formulation, or the investigation involves the comparison of two marketed formulations, and
  • The drug product under investigation is otherwise lawfully marketed in the United States.

Exemption 3 — Foreign clinical investigations (§312.2(d)): Investigations conducted outside of the United States are not required to comply with the IND regulations, provided the data from those investigations will not be submitted to support a U.S. marketing application. If foreign data is intended to support a U.S. NDA or BLA, those studies must meet FDA standards (even if no U.S. IND was filed — the foreign data must still be conducted per ICH E6 GCP and the results must be interpretable by FDA).

Exemption 4 — Previously marketed drugs in certain Phase 1 contexts: FDA has historically applied reduced scrutiny for lawfully marketed drugs studied in Phase 1 settings where the dose, route, and patient population are within the approved label. However, this does not eliminate all reporting obligations — IRB oversight and informed consent still apply.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 — 豁免 ≠ 無監管

豁免 IND 要求並不代表研究完全不受監管。所有豁免項目仍必須遵守：

  • IRB（機構審查委員會）審查義務（21 CFR Part 56）
  • 知情同意要求（21 CFR Part 50）
  • 適用的 CGMP 要求（Parts 210/211）

豁免只是「不需要向 FDA 提交 IND 文件」，不是「免除一切法規義務」。

比喻說明 — 豁免的邏輯：已有「信用紀錄」的藥品

把 IND 申請比作「信用審查」：一個全新藥品（NCE）沒有任何安全紀錄，FDA 必須從頭審查，就像銀行對新客戶做嚴格信用調查。

已上市藥品（lawfully marketed drug）已有多年的安全紀錄，如果你只是用它做標準的 BE 研究或常規臨床使用，FDA 允許「跳過信用審查」——因為風險輪廓已充分了解。

但一旦你把這個「信用良好」的藥品用於全新適應症、全新劑量或高風險族群，風險輪廓改變，就必須重新申請 IND。

練習思考 — 需要 IND 還是豁免？

判斷以下情境是否需要 IND：

  1. 藥廠對已核准的降壓藥品做 BA 研究，比較原廠錠劑與新配方膠囊（同劑量、同適應症）。
    → 可能適用 §312.2(b)(1) BE 豁免
  2. 研究者在美國大學研究已核准的化療藥品用於一種罕見新癌症，跨州送樣品。
    → 新適應症 + 跨州 = 需要 IND
  3. 歐洲進行的 Phase 2 試驗，數據將用於支持美國 NDA 申請。
    → 不強制需要美國 IND，但數據必須符合 FDA 標準與 ICH E6 GCP

重點提示 — BA/BE 豁免的實務意涵

BA/BE 豁免（§312.2(b)(1)）是學名藥產業最常用的豁免條款。CDMO 承接 ANDA（學名藥申請）相關的 BE 研究批次製造時，客戶通常不需要 IND——但 CDMO 仍需：

  • 確認豁免條件全部成立（非放射性標記、非新適應症等）
  • 以符合 CGMP 的方式製造 BE 研究批次
  • 保留完整的製造和放行紀錄
0.4 IND Phases — Key Definitions & CMC Implications

原文內容 Original Text

21 CFR §312.21 defines the three phases of clinical investigation. Phase definitions are critical for CDMOs because they directly determine the level of CMC (Chemistry, Manufacturing, and Controls) documentation and GMP compliance expected by FDA.

Phase 1 (§312.21(a)) — First-in-Human Safety Studies:

  • Typically 20–80 subjects, usually healthy volunteers (or patients in oncology)
  • Primary objective: safety, tolerability, pharmacokinetics (PK), pharmacodynamics (PD)
  • Secondary objectives: maximum tolerated dose, dose escalation parameters
  • CMC requirements: relatively lower — FDA recognizes manufacturing processes are still evolving; a preliminary process description, basic specifications, and analytical testing are sufficient
  • GMP standard: "Phase-appropriate GMP" — full commercial CGMP not required, but risk-based controls must be in place

Phase 2 (§312.21(b)) — Exploratory Efficacy and Dose Ranging:

  • Typically 100–300 patients with the target disease or condition
  • Primary objective: exploratory efficacy evidence, optimal dose range identification
  • CMC requirements: intermediate — process must be more defined, with validated analytical methods and tighter specifications; stability data program underway
  • GMP standard: increased GMP rigor; process consistency becomes critical as commercial-scale manufacturing planning begins

Phase 3 (§312.21(c)) — Confirmatory Efficacy and Safety:

  • Typically 300–3,000+ patients across multiple sites; large-scale, randomized controlled trials
  • Primary objective: confirmatory demonstration of efficacy and safety supporting marketing approval
  • CMC requirements: full commercial-scale GMP required; the CMC section of the IND must reflect the commercial manufacturing intent; all critical quality attributes (CQAs) must be defined; process validation (or process performance qualification) must be underway or completed
  • GMP standard: essentially equivalent to commercial CGMP (21 CFR Parts 210/211 or Part 600 for biologics)
"The CMC section should contain sufficient information about the drug substance and drug product to assure the proper identification, quality, purity, and strength of the investigational drug." — FDA IND Guidance (21 CFR §312.23(a)(7))

核心概念解析 — Phase 對 CDMO 的意涵：CMC 要求的逐步遞增

Phase CMC 成熟度要求 GMP 標準 CDMO 典型工作
Phase 1 初步製程描述、基本規格 Phase-appropriate（風險管控） 初期批次製造、分析方法開發
Phase 2 製程定義更明確、驗證分析方法、穩定性計畫 強化 GMP 一致性 製程優化、批次放大、穩定性研究
Phase 3 商業規模製程、完整 CQA 定義、製程驗證 完整商業 CGMP 商業規模批次製造、製程確效、法規批（PPQ）

比喻說明 — Phase 1/2/3 的製程成熟度就像「建房三階段」

  • Phase 1 = 打地基： 你只需要讓基礎安全，不需要裝潢細節。製程可以不完美，但不能有安全疑慮。
  • Phase 2 = 蓋主體結構： 牆壁要筆直，門窗要合規格，開始需要建築師圖說（製程規格文件）。
  • Phase 3 = 完工準備入住： 所有細節必須與最終核准的建築圖（商業製程）完全一致，因為之後不能隨意改動——改動需要向主管機關申報（manufacturing change supplement）。

重點提示 — Phase-Appropriate GMP

「Phase-appropriate GMP」是 FDA 的彈性原則——但彈性有邊界：

  • Phase 1 不需要完整的製程驗證，但需要有風險基礎的品質管制措施（如無菌填充的無菌保證、原料身份確認）
  • FDA 在 Phase 1 稽查時，焦點在「藥品是否安全用於人體」，而非追求完美的 CGMP 合規性
  • 任何 Phase 的藥品，都必須做到：正確標示（labeling）、無菌性保證（如適用）、無交叉污染（cross-contamination prevention）
0.5 Why Part 312 Matters for CDMOs

原文內容 Original Text

Under 21 CFR Part 312, the sponsor is the entity that assumes responsibility for the IND — typically the pharmaceutical or biotechnology company. When a sponsor contracts out manufacturing to a CDMO, the sponsor retains IND ownership and responsibility, but the CDMO assumes direct operational obligations for anything it manufactures under that IND.

CDMO's key obligations under Part 312:

  1. Manufacture per IND specifications: The CDMO must manufacture the drug substance or drug product exactly as described in the IND CMC section. Any deviation from IND specifications is a regulatory event that could affect the validity of clinical data.
  2. Maintain records sufficient for FDA inspection: All batch records, raw material records, environmental monitoring records, and deviation records must be maintained and available for FDA review. FDA may inspect the CDMO during the IND phase even before commercial approval.
  3. Notify sponsor of deviations that might affect safety or efficacy: If the CDMO identifies an out-of-specification (OOS) result, a manufacturing deviation, or an equipment failure that could affect the quality of a clinical batch, the sponsor must be notified immediately. The sponsor is responsible for evaluating whether the deviation triggers a reporting obligation to FDA (e.g., IND safety report under §312.32).
  4. Support sponsor's IND amendment obligations: If the CDMO implements a manufacturing change (e.g., equipment change, site change, process modification), the sponsor must file a CMC amendment to the IND. The CDMO must proactively inform the sponsor of any such changes.
  5. Comply with phase-appropriate GMP: The level of GMP compliance expected by FDA scales with the clinical phase. CDMO must understand where its client's program is and apply the appropriate controls.

Failure scenarios for CDMOs:

  • Manufacturing deviation → clinical hold: A significant deviation (e.g., sterility failure, wrong excipient) can cause FDA to place the entire clinical program on clinical hold until the issue is resolved.
  • OOS result → batch rejection: An OOS result on a clinical batch means the batch cannot be used; if it was already administered to subjects, a safety evaluation is required.
  • Process change not filed → potential protocol violation: If the CDMO changes the manufacturing process without notifying the sponsor, and the sponsor fails to file an IND amendment, clinical data from those batches may be unacceptable to FDA.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念解析 — Sponsor vs CDMO 的責任分工

Part 312 的法律責任架構如下：

  • Sponsor（贊助廠商）：持有 IND、對 FDA 負責、承擔所有申報義務（IND amendment、安全報告等）
  • CDMO（合約製造商）：依據 IND CMC 規格製造、對 Sponsor 負責、維持 GMP 合規性

CDMO 雖然不直接向 FDA 申報 IND，但 FDA 稽查時可以直接到 CDMO 工廠稽查製造紀錄。CDMO 的合規狀況直接影響 Sponsor IND 的有效性。

比喻：Sponsor 是「建案開發商」，CDMO 是「建築承包商」。開發商對業主（FDA）負責，但如果承包商偷工減料，建案一樣會被勒令停工。

CDMO 實務情境 — 客戶要求製造 Phase 1 批次，CDMO 應如何應對？

情境： 一家生技公司（Sponsor）請 CDMO 製造其 Phase 1 臨床試驗用小分子藥品，該客戶擁有有效的 IND（IND 號碼 XXXXX）。

CDMO 應立即確認的項目：

  1. 取得 IND CMC 章節副本：確認製造方法、規格、分析程序與 IND 描述一致
  2. 品質協議（Quality Agreement）：明確訂定偏差通報時限（通常 24 小時內通知 Sponsor）、製程變更預先通知義務、FDA 稽查時的通知機制
  3. 製造紀錄設計：確保批次記錄足以支持 FDA 稽查，且與 IND 描述的製程步驟對應
  4. Phase-appropriate GMP 落差分析：評估現有 GMP 體系是否足以支持 Phase 1 要求（通常是可以的，但需確認無菌製程、原料管制等關鍵領域）
  5. 放行程序：確認批次放行標準與 IND 規格一致；CDMO 通常負責 manufacturing release（工廠放行），Sponsor 負責 IND batch disposition（IND 批次最終處置）

練習思考 — 偏差情境判斷

CDMO 在製造 Phase 2 臨床批次時發現下列情況，請判斷每種情況的處置優先順序：

  1. 無菌充填環境監控（EM）有一個接觸碟（contact plate）超出警戒限（alert limit）但在行動限（action limit）以下。
    → 依偏差處理 SOP，立即記錄；通知 Sponsor 評估是否影響 IND 報告
  2. 已放行並運送給臨床機構的批次，事後發現有效期限標示錯誤（少標了 3 個月）。
    → 高度緊急：可能需要召回（product recall），Sponsor 必須立即評估 FDA 通報義務
  3. CDMO 計畫將充填線設備從 A 型灌裝機更換為 B 型，但尚未更新批次記錄模板。
    → 設備變更必須事先通知 Sponsor；Sponsor 評估是否需要提交 IND CMC 修正案（prior approval supplement or annual report）

重點提示 — Clinical Hold 的 CDMO 影響

FDA 的 clinical hold（臨床擱置） 是 IND 監管中最嚴重的處置——FDA 可命令暫停或延遲已規劃或進行中的臨床試驗。

對 CDMO 的實際衝擊：

  • 正在製造中的批次可能被要求暫停放行
  • 已製造完成的批次可能無法繼續出貨給臨床機構
  • CDMO 可能需要協助 Sponsor 準備 FDA 要求的 clinical hold response（通常有 30 天期限）
  • 整個臨床計畫的延誤成本可達每週數百萬美元

這也是為什麼 CDMO 的 GMP 合規性和快速偏差通報機制，對於藥物開發程序的整體時程至關重要。

本節重點回顧 Section Summary

  • IND 的本質： 依據 FD&C Act §505(i) 設立，是啟動人體試驗的「通知申請」而非「批准文件」——提交後 30 天自動生效，除非 FDA 發出臨床擱置（clinical hold）
  • IND 觸發三要件： 藥品未獲上市批准 + 用於人體試驗 + 跨州運輸；三者同時成立方須申請 IND
  • 關鍵豁免： 已上市藥品用於同適應症同族群的臨床觀察（§312.2(a) lawful marketing exemption）；符合條件的 BA/BE 研究（§312.2(b)(1)）；豁免 IND 但 IRB 審查與知情同意義務不變
  • Phase 遞進： Phase 1（20–80 人，安全性，phase-appropriate GMP）→ Phase 2（100–300 人，探索療效）→ Phase 3（300–3000+ 人，完整商業 CGMP，製程驗證）；CMC 要求隨 Phase 逐步升級

1.1 §312.3 — Key Definitions 關鍵定義

Regulatory Text & Definitions

§312.3 — Definitions and interpretations
The following definitions apply to this part:

Sponsor — A person who takes responsibility for and initiates a clinical investigation. The sponsor may be an individual or pharmaceutical company, governmental agency, academic institution, private organization, or other organization. The sponsor does not actually conduct the investigation unless the sponsor is also an investigator.

Investigator — An individual who actually conducts a clinical investigation (i.e., under whose immediate direction the drug is administered or dispensed to a subject). In the event an investigation is conducted by a team of individuals, the investigator is the responsible leader of the team.

Sponsor-Investigator — An individual who both initiates and conducts an investigation, and under whose immediate direction the investigational drug is administered or dispensed. The term does not include any person other than an individual. The requirements applicable to a sponsor-investigator under this part include both those applicable to an investigator and a sponsor.

Clinical investigation — Any experiment in which a drug is administered or dispensed to, or used involving, one or more human subjects. For the purposes of this part, the term "clinical investigation" means any such use of a drug except for the use of a marketed drug in the course of medical practice.

Phase of investigation — The FDA recognizes three phases (Phase 1, 2, 3) of clinical investigation of a drug in humans, as described in §312.21. Phases are defined by purpose, not by sequence; some drugs may proceed from Phase 1 directly to Phase 3.

IND safety report — An expedited report, submitted to FDA and all participating investigators, of an unexpected serious adverse drug reaction or findings from other studies that suggest a significant risk to human subjects.

Protocol — A document that describes the objectives, design, methodology, statistical considerations, and organization of a clinical trial. The protocol also typically describes the conditions under which the trial is to be performed and managed.

Reasonable basis to conclude — The evidentiary standard applied when determining whether a drug is exempt from IND requirements. Sponsors must have a reasonable basis (supported by published literature or prior experience) to conclude the drug meets applicable exemption criteria.

中文解析 Commentary

核心概念：Sponsor vs. Investigator 責任分工

Sponsor（贊助者）是 IND 的持有人，對 FDA 負有最終的法規責任，包括：IND 的提交與維護、嚴重不良事件的快速通報（15 天報告）、監測臨床試驗的整體安全性、以及確保所有製造商（包含 CDMO）符合 GMP 要求。

Investigator（研究者）是實際執行試驗的現場醫師或科學家，對受試者安全與試驗方案遵循負責，需向 Sponsor 報告不良事件，並由 Sponsor 彙整後通報 FDA。

關鍵分工邏輯：Sponsor 持有 IND、對 FDA 負責；Investigator 執行研究、對受試者負責。兩者的責任鏈在整個藥物開發過程中必須清晰且有文件記錄。

Sponsor-Investigator 的特殊地位

學術界的研究者醫師（investigator-physician）常見此角色——例如大學醫院的教授同時提交 IND 並親自執行臨床試驗。此人必須同時履行 Sponsor 義務（IND 維護、安全通報、CMC 責任）和 Investigator 義務（受試者知情同意、方案遵循）。

注意：§312.3 明確規定 Sponsor-Investigator 只能是「個人」（individual），公司法人不能擔任此角色。

Protocol 的法規意涵

Protocol 不只是科學文件——它是 IND 申請的核心組成部分（§312.23(a)(6)）。FDA 審查 Protocol 以確認：研究設計是否合理、受試者選擇標準是否適當、劑量遞增計畫是否安全、終點是否可測量。Protocol 的任何重大修改（protocol amendment）均需事先提交 FDA。

IND Safety Report 的時效要求

依 §312.32：

  • 15 個日曆日：未預期嚴重不良反應（unexpected serious adverse reaction）
  • 7 個日曆日：危及生命的未預期嚴重不良反應，需先口頭/電話通報，再於 15 日內補書面報告
  • 年度報告：IND 每年須提交進度報告（§312.33）

中文解析 Commentary

四種 IND 類型的實務選擇邏輯

Commercial IND 是製藥公司主流路徑：從 Phase 1 開始建立完整的開發文件，目標是取得 NDA/BLA 上市許可。每個臨床階段都會更新 IND，CMC 要求隨階段逐步提升。

Research IND（非商業型）：學術研究者常用此類型，例如大學醫院的 PI 測試既有藥物的新適應症，或探索性機制研究。FDA 對此類 IND 的 CMC 要求相對寬鬆，但安全性要求完全相同。

Treatment IND：適用於已有療效信號、但尚未獲批准的藥物。例如某罕見癌症新藥在 Phase 2 顯示顯著效益，FDA 可允許在等待 NDA 審查期間，讓無其他選擇的重症患者提前使用。

Emergency Use IND：最快速的路徑，FDA 可在電話中授權。典型情境：某患者感染罕見病原體，唯一有效藥物仍在開發中，主治醫師致電 FDA 尋求緊急授權。

比喻說明 Analogy — 駕照分類

四種 IND 類型如同不同類別的駕照：

  • Commercial IND = 職業大客車駕照：全套訓練、嚴格考核，目標是商業載客（上市銷售）
  • Research IND = 普通小客車駕照：自用研究，不商業化，要求較寬
  • Treatment IND = 特殊許可證：在正式執照核發前，允許在特定條件下執業
  • Emergency Use IND = 緊急豁免：現場緊急情況下，警察可臨時授權無照者駕車急救，事後補辦文件

重要提示：CDMO 與 IND 類型的關係

無論哪種 IND 類型，委外製造（CDMO）對 Sponsor 的 IND 沒有直接法規義務。CDMO 的責任是：依據 Sponsor 的規格和 GMP 法規製造藥品。但 Sponsor 有義務確保其 CDMO 符合 GMP——FDA 查廠時，若發現 CDMO 的 GMP 問題，FDA 可對 IND 發出臨床暫停（Clinical Hold）。

1.3 §312.10 — Waivers 豁免申請

§312.10 — Waivers

(a) General. A sponsor may request FDA to waive any requirement of this part. A waiver request may be submitted either in an IND or in a separate document.

Grounds for waiver — sponsor must demonstrate one of the following:

  1. The alternative proposed satisfies the purpose of the requirement being waived, or
  2. The waiver is in the interest of the public health.

(b) Procedure. A waiver request is required to contain at minimum:

  • An identification of the specific requirement that is subject of the waiver request
  • A reference to the applicable part of the regulations
  • A statement of why the waiver is sought
  • Documentation or information supporting the waiver

FDA action: FDA may grant a waiver if the waiver request demonstrates adequate grounds. A waiver may be conditional and may require compensatory measures. FDA's grant of waiver must be documented in the IND file.

Practical examples of waiver requests:

  • Waiver of a specific stability time point when sufficient data from accelerated studies are available
  • Waiver of the annual IND report for a phase-completed study where a new IND submission would be filed
  • Waiver of specific CMC requirements when an orphan drug is manufactured in extremely small quantities

中文解析 Commentary

豁免申請的策略性使用

§312.10 豁免機制是 FDA 法規彈性的重要體現。當標準要求對特定情況不適用或不合理時，Sponsor 可主動請求豁免，而無需違規後再辯解。

兩個法定豁免理由：

  • 替代方案可達相同目的：例如，某要求是為了確保藥品穩定性，若 Sponsor 能提供更完整的加速穩定性數據，FDA 可接受此替代方案並豁免特定測試時間點要求。
  • 符合公共衛生利益：例如，對極罕見疾病的孤兒藥，強制要求大規模工藝驗證在科學上和實務上均不可行，FDA 可基於公共衛生利益豁免。

豁免申請的實務要點

主動申請，而非事後辯護：豁免必須在違反要求之前申請並獲批准。FDA 不接受「我已經做了一個不符合要求的替代方案，請事後豁免」的邏輯。

文件完整性：豁免獲批後，相關文件必須保存在 IND 文件中。FDA 查廠或審查時，稽查員可能要求查閱原始豁免申請及 FDA 的批准函。

條件性豁免：FDA 常見的豁免批准是附條件的——例如「豁免某穩定性時間點，但需在 Phase 2 開始前提供額外數據」。條件必須完全落實。

  1. 一家 Sponsor 正在開發治療極罕見遺傳疾病的基因療法（全球患者不足 200 人）。該藥物無法以商業規模製造。Sponsor 可以依 §312.10 以哪個理由申請豁免商業規模工藝驗證要求？需提供哪些支持文件？
  2. 若 FDA 批准了一項條件性豁免，但 Sponsor 未能在要求的時間點前履行條件，FDA 可採取什麼行動？
1.4 §§312.20–312.22 — Submitting an IND & Phase Definitions 提交 IND 與臨床階段定義

§312.20 — Requirement for an IND

A sponsor shall submit an IND to FDA if the sponsor intends to conduct a clinical investigation with an investigational new drug. A sponsor need not submit a separate IND for each clinical investigation, as long as the investigations are described within the same IND.

Who may submit: Any person — including pharmaceutical companies, academic institutions, individual physicians, and government agencies — who intends to conduct a clinical investigation may submit an IND. The IND becomes effective 30 days after FDA receives it, unless FDA notifies the sponsor that the investigation is subject to a clinical hold.

§312.21 — Phases of an Investigation

(a) Phase 1. Phase 1 includes the initial introduction of an investigational new drug into humans. Phase 1 studies are typically closely monitored and may be conducted in patients or normal volunteer subjects. These studies are designed to determine the metabolism and pharmacologic actions of the drug in humans, the side effects associated with increasing doses, and, if possible, to gain early evidence on effectiveness. During Phase 1, sufficient information about the drug's pharmacokinetics and pharmacological effects should be obtained to permit the design of well-controlled, scientifically valid Phase 2 studies. The total number of subjects and patients included in Phase 1 studies varies with the drug, but is generally in the range of 20 to 80.

(b) Phase 2. Phase 2 includes the controlled clinical studies conducted to evaluate the effectiveness of the drug for a particular indication or indications in patients with the disease or condition under study and to determine the common short-term side effects and risks associated with the drug. Phase 2 studies are typically well controlled, closely monitored, and conducted in a relatively small number of patients, usually involving no more than several hundred patients.

(c) Phase 3. Phase 3 studies are expanded controlled and uncontrolled trials. They are performed after preliminary evidence suggesting effectiveness of the drug has been obtained, and are intended to gather the additional information about effectiveness and safety that is needed to evaluate the overall benefit-risk relationship of the drug and to provide an adequate basis for physician labeling. Phase 3 studies usually include from several hundred to several thousand subjects.

§312.22 — General Principles of the IND Submission

FDA's primary objectives in reviewing an IND are, in all phases of the investigation, to assure the safety and rights of subjects, and, in Phase 2 and 3, to help assure that the quality of the scientific evaluation of drugs is adequate to permit an evaluation of the drug's effectiveness and safety. Therefore, although FDA will review the IND to determine whether the proposed studies represent an undue risk to subjects, FDA will not, except in extraordinary circumstances, impose requirements for specific kinds of investigations or clinical protocols beyond those necessary to assure the safety of the subjects.

Key principle: For Phase 1, the sponsor must provide evidence that the drug is reasonably safe for the proposed investigation. The amount of information necessary to make this determination depends on the novelty of the drug, the extent to which it has been studied previously, the known or suspected risks, and the developmental phase of the drug.

中文解析 Commentary

IND 的 30 日靜默期機制

§312.20 規定的 IND 生效機制是「提交後 30 天自動生效」，除非 FDA 在此期間發出 Clinical Hold（臨床暫停）。這意味著：

  • FDA 不需要明確批准 IND——靜默即代表默許
  • 若 FDA 有疑慮，必須在 30 天內主動採取行動
  • 實務上，FDA 常在 30 天內以信件或電話告知審查意見，即使不發出 Clinical Hold，也會提出要求

此機制的設計是為了避免 FDA 積壓審查造成的開發延遲，同時保留安全把關的機制。

比喻說明 — 三個臨床階段如同建造房屋

三個臨床階段的邏輯，恰似建造一棟房屋的三個驗收階段：

  • Phase 1 = 地基安全檢查：建商在開始蓋牆之前，先請結構工程師確認地基承重是否安全、有無潛在下陷風險。這個階段的問題是：「這個地基能安全支撐嗎？」類比藥物開發：先確認藥物在人體的安全性、找出最大耐受劑量，才能繼續開發。
  • Phase 2 = 結構審查：地基確認後，開始建造結構體，此時要確認「這個設計對本棟建築的用途是否有效？」類比藥物開發：在有疾病的患者中驗證療效信號，確認劑量範圍合理。
  • Phase 3 = 完整使用執照核發前審查：房屋全部完工後，各主管機關進行全面驗收——消防、電氣、結構、環境，確認可以安全交屋使用。類比藥物開發：大規模、多中心試驗確認整體效益/風險，為 NDA/BLA 提供充分證據。

Phase 1 的靈活性：患者 vs. 健康受試者

§312.21(a) 允許 Phase 1 在「patients or normal volunteer subjects」中進行。這個選擇不是任意的：

  • 腫瘤藥物：通常在癌症患者中進行 Phase 1，因為毒性較大，對健康受試者倫理上不可接受
  • 抗感染藥：可在健康受試者中進行 Phase 1 PK 研究，再在患者中進行 Phase 2
  • 生物製劑/基因療法：通常從患者開始，因疾病特異性機制在正常人中無法評估

§312.22 的核心哲學：安全為先，不過度管控設計

FDA 的立場是：Phase 1 只要求「reasonably safe for proposed investigation」——這是一個風險導向的最低門檻，而不是要求 Phase 1 就必須達到商業級別的科學嚴謹性。

這反映了美國法規的核心邏輯：不阻礙創新，但確保基本安全。FDA 不會要求 Phase 1 就設計完美的隨機對照試驗，但會確保受試者不暴露於不合理的風險中。

1.5 Phase-Appropriate GMP for CDMOs 委外製造商的階段性 GMP 要求

Phase-Appropriate CGMP Framework

FDA's 2008 guidance document "CGMP for Phase 1 Investigational Drugs" established the foundational framework for phase-appropriate manufacturing standards. This guidance clarified that full commercial CGMP compliance is not required for Phase 1, but certain minimum CGMP elements are always mandatory regardless of phase.

FDA 2008 Guidance — Key Points for Phase 1:

  • Full identity, strength, purity, and quality testing is required for Phase 1 drug substances and drug products
  • Written procedures (SOPs) are required; formal validation is not required for Phase 1
  • All personnel must be qualified; training records must be maintained
  • Equipment must be suitable and qualified for its intended use; full IQ/OQ/PQ is not required
  • Batch records with full traceability must be maintained
  • Sterility testing and relevant safety tests (e.g., endotoxin, particulates for injectables) are always required
  • Process validation is not required for Phase 1, but process development data must support GMP manufacture

中文解析 Commentary

FDA 2008 Phase 1 CGMP 指引的核心貢獻

在 2008 年指引發布之前，業界對 Phase 1 GMP 要求存在大量不確定性——有人認為 Phase 1 幾乎不需要 GMP，有人認為必須完全符合商業級別 GMP。FDA 的 2008 年指引明確了「Phase-Appropriate CGMP」的概念：

  • 不可豁免的底線：品質屬性（identity/strength/purity/quality）的測試 + 安全性測試（無菌性、內毒素）+ 批次記錄可追溯性
  • 可彈性處理的部分：製程驗證、設備正式資格驗證（IQ/OQ/PQ）、穩定性全套研究
  • 核心邏輯：Phase 1 的主要風險是「給受試者不安全的藥物」，而不是「製造效率不足」，因此 GMP 聚焦於安全屬性的保障。

重要提示：Sponsor 持有 IND，無論有多少 CDMO 參與

無論一個 IND 涉及幾家 CDMO 或合作機構，IND 始終由 Sponsor 持有，Sponsor 對 FDA 負最終責任。

這意味著：

  • Sponsor 必須對每一家 CDMO 的 GMP 狀態進行稽核和確認
  • 若 CDMO A 負責原料藥、CDMO B 負責製劑、CDMO C 負責無菌充填，三家均需符合各自階段的 GMP 要求
  • FDA 可對任何一家 CDMO 發起查廠，若發現嚴重 GMP 缺失，可對 Sponsor 的 IND 發出 Clinical Hold，即使 Sponsor 本身無問題
  • Sponsor 在 IND 中需記錄所有製造地點（§312.23(a)(7)）

應用情境 Application — CDMO 接到 Phase 1 無菌注射劑訂單

情境：一家 CDMO 接到一家生技公司的訂單，要求製造用於 Phase 1 FIH（First-in-Human）臨床試驗的無菌注射劑（lyophilized vial）。Sponsor 提供了完整的 IND 號碼和製造規格書。

CDMO 最低 GMP 文件要求：

  • 批次製造記錄（BMR）：完整批次記錄，包含每個製造步驟、設備編號、環境監控數據、操作員簽名、偏差記錄
  • 品質協議（Quality Agreement）：Sponsor 與 CDMO 之間必須有書面品質協議，明確責任分工
  • 放行測試報告：無菌性（Sterility）、內毒素（Endotoxin / LAL）、可見異物（Visual inspection）、微粒（Particulate matter per USP <788>）、效價（Potency/Assay）、外觀（Appearance）、復原時間（Reconstitution time）、pH、水分含量（Moisture / Karl Fischer）
  • 冷凍乾燥週期記錄：溫度/壓力記錄與已驗證或已確認的冷凍乾燥曲線對比
  • 容器密封完整性（CCI）：根據製程設計提供 CCI 測試或驗證數據
  • 稽核軌跡：所有偏差的 CAPA 記錄；廢批處理記錄

不需要（Phase 1 階段）：製程工藝驗證（PPQ）、完整的三批驗證、設備全套 IQ/OQ/PQ（設備資格確認文件即可）

情境分類練習 Practice — 識別 IND 場景

請判斷以下各情境對應的 IND 類型與 GMP 要求層級：

  1. 哈佛醫學院某教授正在研究已上市的二甲雙胍（Metformin）用於治療早期阿茲海默症的機制，擬招募 40 位患者進行小型試驗。
    （提示：考量是否為商業化意圖）
  2. 某大型跨國藥廠的抗體藥物即將進行 FIH Phase 1 試驗，由 CDMO 製造臨床用藥，最終目標是提交 BLA。
    （提示：CMC 要求深度？）
  3. 一位重症罕見病患者（全球已知案例不足 10 例）的主治醫師，正在向 FDA 申請使用一種仍在早期開發中的實驗性藥物進行搶救性治療。
    （提示：時間緊迫性如何影響 IND 類型選擇？）
  4. 某新藥已完成 Phase 3 試驗並提交 NDA，正在等待 FDA 審查。此時，一批患有相同疾病且無其他治療選項的重症患者希望提前使用此藥。
    （提示：哪種 IND 類型專為此情境設計？）
章節摘要 Section Summary

Key Regulatory Framework Points

  • §312.3 Definitions: Sponsor holds the IND and bears ultimate FDA responsibility; Investigator conducts the trial; Sponsor-Investigator combines both roles in a single individual (must be an individual, not a company).
  • IND Types: Four types — Commercial (NDA/BLA intent), Research/Non-commercial (academic, no NDA intent), Treatment (pre-approval access for seriously ill), Emergency Use (single-patient crisis). Each type carries different CMC depth expectations.
  • §312.10 Waivers: Any IND requirement may be waived by written request to FDA. Grounds: alternative satisfies requirement's purpose, or waiver is in public health interest. Must be pre-approved and documented in IND file.
  • §312.20 IND Submission: IND becomes effective 30 days after FDA receipt unless a Clinical Hold is issued. FDA does not "approve" an IND — silence is acquiescence.
  • §312.21 Phase Definitions: Phase 1 (20–80 subjects, safety/PK, patients or healthy volunteers); Phase 2 (hundreds, controlled, effectiveness + safety); Phase 3 (hundreds to thousands, expanded, NDA/BLA basis).
  • §312.22 General Principles: Phase 1 requires reasonable safety evidence; FDA will not impose unnecessary design requirements beyond subject safety protection.
  • Phase-Appropriate CGMP: FDA 2008 guidance establishes that full commercial CGMP is not required for Phase 1, but critical safety testing (sterility, endotoxin, identity, potency) and batch record traceability are always mandatory.

重點回顧 Key Takeaways

三個最重要的 CDMO 實務要點

  1. IND 是 Sponsor 的，不是 CDMO 的：CDMO 的責任是按規格製造並符合 GMP，但 IND 的法規所有權和 FDA 責任永遠在 Sponsor 身上。Sponsor 必須將所有製造地點登記在 IND 的 CMC 部分中。
  2. Phase 1 不免除安全性測試：即使是 Phase-Appropriate CGMP，無菌注射劑的無菌性、內毒素、微粒測試不可省略，無論臨床階段為何。
  3. GMP 要求隨階段升級：CDMO 應在合約和品質協議中明確規定不同臨床階段的 GMP 要求，並確保在 Phase 2 → Phase 3 過渡時，提前啟動製程驗證和規模放大工作。

常見查廠觀察缺失（FDA Warning Letter 案例模式）

  • CDMO 未向 Sponsor 及時通報製程偏差，導致 Sponsor 的 IND 安全報告出現遺漏
  • Phase 1 批次記錄不完整，缺乏環境監控數據或操作員簽名
  • Sponsor 未對 CDMO 進行定期 GMP 稽核，被 FDA 認定為「不充分的供應商監管」
  • 從 Phase 2 至 Phase 3 規模放大後，未重新評估製程對品質屬性的影響，直接引用 Phase 2 數據

2.1 Overall IND Format — §312.23(a) IND 申請整體格式

The 10 Elements of a Complete IND Application

§312.23(a) specifies that an IND application shall contain the following elements, submitted as separate sections:

  1. (a)(1) Cover sheet — FDA Form 1571, identifying the sponsor, drug substance, and phase of investigation.
  2. (a)(2) Table of contents — Index of all submitted sections.
  3. (a)(3) Introductory statement and general investigational plan — Brief narrative description of the drug, development rationale, and the scope of the planned clinical investigation.
  4. (a)(4) Investigator's brochure (IB) — Summary of all prior human and animal data; updated as new information becomes available.
  5. (a)(5) Clinical protocol(s) — Detailed study design, dosing regimen, inclusion/exclusion criteria, safety monitoring plan.
  6. (a)(6) Chemistry, Manufacturing, and Controls (CMC) — Element 7 in the CFR numbering; the primary CDMO deliverable. Full description of the drug substance and drug product manufacturing, specifications, controls, and stability.
  7. (a)(7) Pharmacology and toxicology information — Nonclinical pharmacodynamics, ADME, toxicology data supporting safe first-in-human exposure.
  8. (a)(8) Previous human experience — Summary of prior clinical experience, if any (foreign or domestic).
  9. (a)(9) Additional information — Special considerations: drug dependence potential, radioactive drugs, pediatric studies.
  10. (a)(10) Relevant information — Any other information that FDA requests or sponsor considers pertinent.
§312.23(a) — "An original IND application is required to contain the information set forth in paragraphs (a)(1) through (a)(10) of this section."

FDA reviewers in the Division of Pharmaceutical Review (or equivalent) focus primary scientific scrutiny on Element 6 (CMC) and Element 7 (Pharmacology/Tox) when evaluating whether a 30-day clinical hold should be issued.

中文解析 Commentary

核心概念：CMC 章節是 CDMO 的主戰場

10 個 IND 元素中，絕大多數由 Sponsor 的臨床、藥理、毒理團隊負責。CMC 章節（§312.23(a)(7)，即第 6 個內容元素）是 CDMO 對 IND 的核心貢獻，直接決定：

  • FDA 是否對製造安全性有顧慮而發 Clinical Hold（30 天暫停令）
  • 試驗藥物批次能否符合放行標準供臨床使用
  • 製造變更是否需要事先通知 FDA

CDMO 通常以 CMC Disclosure Package 的形式提供資料，由 Sponsor 整合進 IND 申請書中。CDMO 的資料正確性與完整性由 Sponsor 對 FDA 負最終責任，但 CDMO 的實際製造行為（文件、記錄、製程）直接面對 FDA 查廠。

IND 數字混淆提醒

§312.23(a) 法規條文將 CMC 稱為第 7 個子項目 (a)(7)，但在 IND 申請書的慣例順序中，它通常被稱為「第 6 個元素」（因為 Cover Sheet 和 TOC 是行政格式，有時不計入實質科學內容計數）。考試或審查情境中，認識 §312.23(a)(7) 這個 CFR 引用號碼最為關鍵。

30 天 Clinical Hold 與 CMC 的關係

FDA 收到 IND 申請後有 30 天審查期。若 CMC 章節資料不足，FDA 可依 §312.42 發出 Clinical Hold，禁止臨床試驗啟動。常見 CMC 觸發原因：

  • 無菌保證方法描述不清
  • 雜質超標或缺乏毒理學評估
  • 穩定性資料不足以支撐試驗期間的使用期效
  • 容器相容性資料缺失（尤其蛋白質藥物）
2.2 Drug Substance CMC Requirements — §312.23(a)(7)(i) 原料藥 CMC 要求

§312.23(a)(7)(i) — Drug Substance Information

The CMC section for the drug substance (active pharmaceutical ingredient, API) must include the following seven elements:

1. Description
A description of the drug substance including its physical, chemical, or biological characteristics; structural formula where applicable; and the source of the substance. For biologics, describe the cell line, expression system, or fermentation organism.

2. Manufacturer
The name, address, and U.S. Drug Establishment Registration number (DEA registration, if applicable) of the drug substance manufacturer. If multiple manufacturers or suppliers are used, all must be listed.

3. Method of Preparation
A description of the synthesis, fermentation, extraction, isolation, or purification procedure with sufficient detail to assess the consistency and reproducibility of the process. Key intermediate steps, reagents, solvents, and catalysts should be identified. The level of detail scales with clinical phase.

4. Analytical Controls
A list of the tests performed, reference to analytical methods, and acceptance criteria for: identity, purity, potency (where applicable), and assay. Methods must be described or referenced to pharmacopeial methods. Residual solvents (ICH Q3C), elemental impurities (ICH Q3D), genotoxic impurities (ICH M7), and process-related impurities should be addressed.

5. Reference Standards
A description of the reference standard used in testing. For Phase 1, an in-house primary reference standard with characterization data is acceptable. By Phase 3, the reference standard should be fully characterized and traceable to a compendial or primary standard.

6. Container/Closure System
A description of the container/closure system used for bulk drug substance storage (e.g., HDPE drum, glass vial, stainless steel container). Compatibility of the container with the drug substance should be addressed, particularly for light-sensitive or moisture-sensitive materials.

7. Stability
Stability data sufficient to support the use of the drug substance during the planned clinical trial period. This should include accelerated stability data and real-time data to the extent available. An initial proposed shelf life or re-test date must be stated. The stability protocol should specify storage conditions, test intervals, and testing parameters.

中文解析 Commentary

核心概念：Drug Substance vs. Drug Product 的界線

Drug Substance（原料藥 / API）是藥物的活性成分本身，尚未製成最終劑型。例如：

  • 小分子藥：合成後、精製後的化學原體（bulk API）
  • 蛋白質藥：純化後的 Bulk Drug Substance（BDS），儲存於 -80°C 凍存袋
  • mRNA 藥：製備後的 mRNA 原液（未包覆 LNP 前）

Drug Product（製劑 / 成品）是加入賦形劑、經製程（無菌灌裝、凍乾等）後的最終臨床使用劑型。兩者的 CMC 提交分屬不同子節，對應不同的製造場地與製程控制要求。

比喻：原料藥的 CMC 就像食材規格書

原料藥的 CMC 就像餐廳對食材供應商要求的「食材驗收規格書」：必須說明來源（哪個農場/合成廠）、製備方式（有機耕種/合成路徑）、品質指標（農藥殘留/雜質限度）、儲存方式（冷鏈/防潮容器）。Phase 1 時，規格書可以粗略一些（食材還在試用期）；Phase 3 時，每一個項目都必須有充分數據支撐，如同商業供應合約。

常見的 FDA 483 觸發點：雜質控制

藥物雜質控制是 CMC 審查的高風險區域。CDMO 需特別注意：

  • 基因毒性雜質（ICH M7）：需進行 DEREK/Sarah 計算毒性評估；Phase 1 可採用 TTC（Threshold of Toxicological Concern）方法設限
  • 殘留溶劑（ICH Q3C）：必須列出所有合成步驟使用的溶劑，並與 Class 1/2/3 限度比對
  • 元素雜質（ICH Q3D）：需提供風險評估報告，說明哪些元素可能引入（催化劑殘留、設備浸出）

參考標準的實務建議

Phase 1 時，CDMO 通常以一批經完整表徵的 API 批次作為 Working Reference Standard，需提供：鑑別（NMR、IR、MS）、純度（HPLC）、含量、及外觀數據。此標準應附有 COA 並經 QA 正式批准。Phase 3 前，應考慮是否需要建立 Primary Reference Standard 並存入長期穩定保存計畫。

2.3 Drug Product CMC Requirements — §312.23(a)(7)(ii) 製劑 CMC 要求

§312.23(a)(7)(ii) — Drug Product Information

The CMC section for the drug product (finished dosage form) must address the following elements:

Composition
A quantitative listing of all components in the formulation, including the drug substance and all excipients. Each excipient must be listed with its grade (e.g., USP, NF, Ph.Eur.), quantity per unit dose, and function (e.g., tonicity agent, pH adjuster, cryoprotectant, surfactant, preservative). The function of each excipient must be justified from a formulation science standpoint.

Manufacturer
Name and address of the drug product manufacturer (fill/finish site), and the packager/labeler if different. All contract manufacturing sites, including testing laboratories, should be identified.

Method of Manufacture
For sterile injectables, the manufacturing description must specify:

  • Sterilization method: terminal sterilization (preferred per FDA/EMA guidance) or aseptic processing; rationale for method selection
  • Process overview: compounding, filtration (0.22 µm membrane filter, pre-filter), filling, and lyophilization (if applicable)
  • Sterilizing filtration: filter validation (extractables, integrity test, bacterial challenge)
  • Critical process parameters (CPPs): bioburden at each step, hold times, temperature controls

Controls
Full release specifications with acceptance criteria for all tests performed on the finished drug product. For sterile injectables, this must include:

  • Appearance, pH, osmolality/tonicity
  • Assay (potency/concentration), related substances (degradation products)
  • Sterility (USP <71>)
  • Endotoxin (LAL test, USP <85>)
  • Particulate matter (USP <788> for large-volume parenterals; USP <789> for small-volume parenterals)
  • Container closure integrity (CCI)
  • Protein-specific: sub-visible particles (MFI or RMM), aggregation (SEC-HPLC), potency assay

Container/Closure System
Description of the primary packaging components (e.g., Type I glass vial, bromobutyl stopper, aluminum crimp seal) including supplier, grade, and compatibility data. Reference to USP <661> and USP <1660> for glass containers; stopper extractables data should be available by Phase 2–3.

Sterility-Specific Additional Requirements

  • Media fills: Results of most recent Aseptic Process Simulation (APS); 0 contaminated units expected per FDA guidance (≤3 per 10,000 units per EU Annex 1)
  • Environmental monitoring (EM): Classification of fill/finish area (ISO Class 5 critical zone); viable and non-viable EM results during clinical batch manufacture
  • Endotoxin (LAL): Method validation for product-specific inhibition/enhancement; limit calculation
  • Particulate matter: Light obscuration (HIAC) and microscopic methods per USP <788>/<789>
  • Container closure integrity (CCI): Description of method (vacuum decay, laser headspace, dye ingress) and acceptance criteria

核心概念：製劑 CMC 的雙層控制邏輯

製劑 CMC 涵蓋兩個層次的控制：

  • 製程中控制（In-Process Controls, IPC）：填充重量、pH 確認、溶液澄清度——在製造過程中即時監控，防止問題品到達最終放行階段
  • 最終放行規格（Release Specifications）：批次放行前執行的全套測試，結果需在 COA 上呈現並由 QP/QA 簽署放行

對 FDA 而言，兩層控制都必須在 CMC 章節中描述。Phase 1 時 IPC 可較簡略，但無菌保證相關的測試（無菌試驗、內毒素、微粒）從一開始就必須完整執行，沒有「Phase-Appropriate 豁免」。

末端滅菌 vs. 非同步無菌製程的法規選擇

FDA 的立場（1987 Guidance + 2004 Aseptic Processing Guidance）：末端滅菌（Terminal Sterilization）是首選，因為無菌保證水準（SAL = 10⁻⁶）高於非同步無菌製程。若藥物無法耐受末端滅菌（熱敏感、輻射敏感），才使用非同步無菌製程（Aseptic Processing）。在 CMC 章節中，必須明確說明採用哪種方法，並提供合理性說明。

FDA 483 高風險：Media Fill 資料

FDA 查廠時，Media Fill（培養基模擬灌裝）結果是最常被要求的文件之一。CMC 章節應包含最近一次 Media Fill 的摘要（日期、批量、結果、EM 結果）。若 CDMO 的 Media Fill 紀錄與 IND 中描述的設施不一致，可能觸發 Data Integrity 警告或 Warning Letter。

USP <788> vs. <789> 的區別

兩者都是微粒測試，但應用範圍不同：

  • USP <788> Particulate Matter in Injections：適用於大容量注射劑（LVP, ≥100 mL）及非靜脈注射品；限度較寬
  • USP <789> Particulate Matter in Ophthalmic Solutions：適用於眼用注射劑；限度更嚴格
  • 小容量注射劑（SVP, <100 mL）適用 USP <788> 中的 SVP 限度標準

蛋白質製劑另需依 USP <787>（亞可見微粒）或 MFI（流動影像）評估蛋白質聚集體。

2.4 Phase-Appropriate CMC Depth 臨床階段適應性 CMC 深度

Phase-Appropriate CMC Requirements

FDA's "Guidance for Industry: INDs for Phase 2 and Phase 3 Studies — Chemistry, Manufacturing, and Controls Information" (2003) and related guidance documents establish the principle that CMC requirements should be commensurate with the phase of investigation and associated patient risk. The key regulatory basis is §312.23(a)(7)(iv):

§312.23(a)(7)(iv) — "Although in each phase of the investigation sufficient information is required to assure the proper identification, quality, purity, and strength of the investigational drug, the amount of information needed to make that assurance will vary with the phase of the investigation, the proposed duration of the investigation, the dosage form, and the amount of information otherwise available."

比喻：CMC 是從家庭食譜到工廠配方的演進

Phase 1 = 粗略的家庭食譜：知道主要食材和大致步驟，能做出一道菜（安全的藥）就夠了。不需要克數精確到小數點、不需要通過 ISO 認證的廚房。

Phase 2 = 開始標準化的小餐廳食譜：每次做出來的口感要一致，開始記錄時間/溫度/比例。客人更多了，品質控制變得重要。

Phase 3 = 工業化的標準作業程序（SOP）：每個步驟都有驗證數據，廚師換了也能做出完全一樣的產品。這是 NDA/BLA 申請的前哨站——FDA 會來「查廚房」（PAI）。

關鍵：Phase-Appropriate 不代表 Phase 1 可以品質差，而是某些文件深度（驗證報告、統計設計）可以與階段相稱。無菌保證、患者安全相關測試從第一天就要全面執行。

Phase 1, Single-dose, Healthy volunteers, Low dose
  → Minimum viable CMC: outline synthesis + preliminary specs + accelerated stability

Phase 3, Chronic dosing, Paediatric patients, High dose
  → Full commercial CMC: validated process + full ICH specs + complete stability program

風險越高（慢性給藥、脆弱族群、高劑量），CMC 文件的深度要求越接近商業申請水準。FDA 審查官在評估 CMC 充分性時，會同時考量臨床方案的風險設計（Phase × Risk 矩陣）。

CDMO 的實務注意：Phase 1 GMP 的灰色地帶

FDA 2008 指引《CGMP for Phase 1 Investigational Drugs》明確指出，Phase 1 的 GMP 要求可「適當調整」，但以下事項沒有例外：

  • 完整批次記錄（Batch Production Records）
  • 完整放行測試（包含無菌、內毒素、含量）
  • QA 的書面批次放行授權
  • OOS（規格外結果）調查程序
  • SOP 文件體系

Phase 1 可簡化的是：廠房驗證深度、分析方法的全面驗證程度、年度產品回顧的正式程度。

2.5 IND Amendments for CMC Changes IND CMC 修訂申報類型

Types of IND Amendments

Once an IND is in effect, any changes to the CMC section must be submitted through the appropriate amendment mechanism. The classification of the amendment determines the required timing and whether FDA approval is needed before implementation.

Protocol Amendment — §312.30
Required when a change affects the safety of subjects or represents a significant alteration to the study design. For CMC, this applies when manufacturing changes could affect the drug's safety profile (e.g., change in sterilization method, formulation change affecting tolerability).

  • Timing: Submit before implementation; sponsor must wait 30 days for FDA review (or until FDA notifies sponsor that review is complete)
  • 30-day rule: Clinical investigation may not proceed until 30 days after FDA receipt, unless FDA notifies earlier

Information Amendment — §312.31
Used for CMC changes that do not affect the safety of subjects but represent significant new information about the drug substance or drug product. This is the most common amendment type for routine CMC updates.

  • Examples: Change in drug substance supplier (same API quality), specification tightening, new stability data, method changes
  • Timing: Submit within 30 days of obtaining the information; may implement before FDA review

Major Manufacturing Changes
The following changes require advance FDA review and must be submitted as either a Protocol or Information Amendment before implementation:

  • Change in manufacturing site (drug substance or drug product)
  • Significant change in manufacturing process (e.g., new synthesis route, new sterilization approach)
  • Change in specification that widens acceptance criteria
  • Change in container/closure system

Annual Report — §312.33
Minor CMC changes that do not individually warrant a separate amendment may be accumulated and reported in the annual IND report, submitted within 60 days of the anniversary date of the IND effective date. Includes:

  • Updated stability data (new time points)
  • Minor specification refinements based on accumulated batch data
  • New manufacturing lots manufactured under unchanged process
  • Reference standard updates

核心概念：變更分類決策樹

面對任何 CMC 變更，CDMO 和 Sponsor 應依以下邏輯分類：

  1. 此變更是否影響患者安全？（安全性直接相關）→ 是：Protocol Amendment（等 30 天）
  2. 此變更是重大的新 CMC 資訊，但不影響安全？ → 是：Information Amendment（30 天內提交，可先執行）
  3. 此變更是否為小幅改進，不影響品質或安全？ → 是：Annual Report 彙整

分類模糊時，應諮詢 FDA（Pre-IND meeting 或 Type B meeting）以避免在沒有取得 FDA 認可的情況下執行重大變更。

情境應用：CDMO 場地變更的申報義務

場景：Sponsor 的 Phase 2 試驗進行中，決定將製劑灌裝場地從 CDMO-A（美國）遷移至 CDMO-B（新加坡）。

需要什麼？

  • 製造場地變更屬於重大變更 → 以 Information Amendment 提交（假設製程不變，安全無疑慮）
  • 若製程同時有調整（如更換滅菌方式）→ 可能需要 Protocol Amendment
  • CDMO-B 需提供：廠址資訊（名稱、地址、FEI 號）、設施描述、近期 Media Fill 紀錄摘要、EM 資料
  • CDMO-B 必須在 FDA Drug Establishment Registration 完成登記

風險：若未提交 Amendment 即開始在 CDMO-B 製造，臨床批次等同於在未核准場地生產，可觸發 §312.70 臨床試驗終止。

年度報告 vs. 立即申報的界線

許多 CDMO/Sponsor 的常見錯誤：將「應立即提交的 Information Amendment」誤歸為「可等年度報告」的小變更。原則：任何可能影響批次間一致性的製程調整，都不應等到年度報告才通知 FDA。穩妥做法是建立內部 CMC Change Control 機制，每個變更先評估申報類型，再執行。

2.6 Sterile Injectables: Additional CMC Requirements 無菌注射劑：額外 CMC 要求

Additional CMC Requirements for Sterile Injectables

Sterile injectable drug products carry the highest patient safety risk of any dosage form, as there is no barrier between the drug and systemic circulation. FDA therefore imposes additional CMC data requirements beyond those for oral dosage forms.

Endotoxin (Bacterial) Limit
The endotoxin limit for parenteral drug products is established using the K/M formula:

  • K = Endotoxin threshold = 5 EU/kg/hr for intravenous administration (FDA/USP <85> default)
  • M = Maximum human dose (in mL or mg per kg per hour)
  • Endotoxin Limit (EL) = K ÷ M
  • For intrathecal products: K = 0.2 EU/kg/hr (more restrictive)

The LAL (Limulus Amebocyte Lysate) test must be validated for the specific product matrix to confirm absence of interference. Gel-clot, turbidimetric, and chromogenic methods are all acceptable.

Container Closure Integrity (CCI)
The CMC section must describe the container closure integrity testing method and acceptance criteria. Deterministic methods (vacuum decay, laser headspace analysis, high voltage leak detection) are preferred over probabilistic methods (dye ingress) for risk-based CCI assurance. The CCI program must cover: initial CCI validation, incoming container inspection, and post-process CCI verification.

Aseptic Processing
If aseptic processing is used, the CMC section should include:

  • Media fill (APS) summary results: number of units filled, number contaminated, lot numbers and dates
  • Environmental monitoring (EM) classification: ISO Class 5 for open product exposure; ISO Class 7 background; ISO Class 8 support areas
  • Personnel gowning qualification summary

Extractables and Leachables (E&L)
For Phase 1: preliminary extractables data from container/closure and single-use systems is acceptable; a formal E&L risk assessment should document rationale for not yet having full leachables data.
For Phase 2–3: leachables data from actual drug product stored under stability conditions must be generated. Toxicological risk assessment (per PQRI guidance) must support any detected leachables above the Safety Concern Threshold (SCT = 0.15 µg/day).

公式：內毒素限度計算 K/M

內毒素限度（EL）= K ÷ M

K = 閾值常數（靜脈注射 = 5 EU/kg/hr）
M = 最大給藥量（每公斤體重每小時最大劑量，單位 mL/kg/hr 或 mg/kg/hr）

範例計算：
  藥品：蛋白質注射劑，IV，最大劑量 10 mL/hr，體重 70 kg
  M = 10 mL ÷ 70 kg ÷ 1 hr ≈ 0.143 mL/kg/hr
  EL = 5 EU/kg/hr ÷ 0.143 mL/kg/hr ≈ 35 EU/mL

若藥品標示濃度為 1 mg/mL，則規格為 ≤35 EU/mg（或 ≤35 EU/mL）

注意：鞘內（intrathecal）給藥 K = 0.2 EU/kg/hr，限度更嚴

CDMO 需確保 LAL 試驗已針對該藥品基質進行干擾測試（inhibition/enhancement test），且每批次 LAL 試驗記錄完整歸檔。

無菌製程模擬（Media Fill）的合規要求

FDA 2004 Aseptic Processing Guidance 及 EU GMP Annex 1（2022）均要求：

  • 每個無菌灌裝線每 6 個月執行一次 Media Fill
  • 每次模擬批量應代表正常商業批量，最少 5,000 單位（針對低污染率評估）
  • 合格標準：0 污染單位為目標；超過 0.1% 污染率視為一律不合格，需完整調查
  • Media Fill 失敗是高風險事件，可能觸發 FDA Warning Letter

在 IND CMC 中，需提供至少一次近期 Media Fill 的摘要資訊（非完整報告），包括日期、規模、結果。

E&L：Phase 1 的合規最低門檻

很多小型生技公司在 Phase 1 IND 時提交不完整的 E&L 資料，FDA 通常接受以下 Phase 1 最低要求：

  • 提供容器/封口的供應商 Extractables 研究報告摘要（通常由容器供應商提供）
  • 書面風險評估：說明為何現階段 Leachables 資料尚不完整，以及何時計畫完成
  • 確認使用材料符合 USP <661> / USP <87> / ISO 10993 等相關生物相容性標準

Phase 3 前若仍無法提供 Leachables 資料，FDA 可能在 Pre-NDA meeting 中要求補充，成為 NDA 申請的瓶頸。

2.7 FDA Right to Inspect Manufacturing Sites FDA 對製造場地的查廠權利

FDA Inspection Authority for IND Manufacturing Sites

Under §312.68 and the general authority of the Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FD&C Act §704), FDA has the right to inspect all facilities involved in the manufacture, processing, packing, and testing of investigational drugs listed in an IND application.

Which sites are subject to inspection?
Any manufacturing site listed in the IND CMC section is potentially subject to FDA inspection, including:

  • Drug substance synthesis or fermentation facilities
  • Drug product fill/finish facilities (CDMOs)
  • Contract testing laboratories (analytical testing, stability)
  • Packaging and labeling sites

CDMO Obligations During an IND

  • Record maintenance: All batch production records, deviation reports, OOS investigations, change control records, and validation documentation must be maintained and accessible for FDA review
  • Allow inspection: Refusal to permit an FDA inspection constitutes grounds for clinical hold (§312.44) or disqualification of the facility
  • Report manufacturing deviations: Critical deviations (particularly those affecting sterility or potency of released clinical batches) must be communicated to the sponsor promptly; the sponsor is obligated to assess whether an IND amendment or safety report is required

Pre-Approval Inspection (PAI)
For Phase 3 sites, FDA may conduct a PAI concurrent with NDA/BLA review. CDMOs used during Phase 3 clinical trials are high priority targets for PAI. A PAI failure can result in a Complete Response Letter (CRL) even if the clinical data are compelling.

  • Inspection: All sites listed in the IND CMC section are subject to FDA inspection; CDMOs must maintain records, allow access, and communicate deviations to the sponsor.

3.1 §312.30 — Protocol Amendments 協議修改申請

Original Text & Regulatory Requirements

§ 312.30 Protocol amendments.

A sponsor shall submit a protocol amendment describing any change in a Phase 1 protocol that significantly affects the safety of subjects. A sponsor shall submit a protocol amendment describing any change in a Phase 2 or 3 protocol that significantly affects the safety of subjects, the scope of the investigation, or the scientific quality of the study. Examples of changes requiring a protocol amendment include:

  • Any increase in drug dosage or duration of exposure of individual research subjects beyond that in the current protocol
  • Any significant increase in the number of research subjects
  • The addition of a new test article
  • The addition of a new clinical investigator or research site
  • A significant change in the study design (e.g., adding or dropping control groups)

New Protocol Amendments

A sponsor who adds a new protocol after the IND goes into effect shall submit the new protocol to FDA as a protocol amendment. The sponsor may begin the clinical investigation on the date the amendment is submitted to FDA unless the sponsor has received a clinical hold or is conducting a Phase 1 investigation, in which case FDA's 30-day review period applies before enrollment begins.

Three Categories of Protocol Amendments

  1. New Protocol Amendment: Adding an entirely new clinical study (e.g., adding Phase 2 study to an existing Phase 1 IND). The new protocol must be submitted before any subjects are enrolled in the new study.
  2. Protocol Change Amendment: Modifying an existing protocol — changing dose, duration of exposure, patient population, endpoints, or study design elements that affect subject safety or scientific quality.
  3. New Investigator Amendment: Adding a new investigator or clinical site to the study. Must include a signed Form FDA 1572 (Statement of Investigator) and curriculum vitae.

When FDA Prior Approval (30-Day Wait) is Required:

  • Any change that represents a significant increase in risk to subjects
  • Adding a new indication to the IND
  • Phase 1 protocols: sponsor must wait 30 days after submitting the protocol amendment before initiating the study, unless FDA notifies that study may proceed earlier

Notify Within 30 Days (Information Amendment):

  • Minor changes that do not significantly affect subject safety, scope, or scientific quality
  • Administrative changes (e.g., sponsor contact information update)
  • Changes already reflected in other amendments submitted concurrently
"A sponsor shall submit a protocol amendment describing any change in a Phase 2 or 3 protocol that significantly affects the safety of subjects, the scope of the investigation, or the scientific quality of the study."
— 21 CFR §312.30(b)

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

三類協議修改的核心邏輯 Three Types of Protocol Amendments

§312.30 的核心問題是：「這個變更是否會影響受試者安全、研究範疇或科學品質？」 如果是，就需要提交協議修改。

實務上可以用三個問題快速篩選：

  1. 是全新研究？ → 新協議修改（附完整協議文本 + FDA Form 1572）
  2. 是更改現有協議中的劑量/時間/族群/設計？ → 協議變更修改（附修訂協議及變更說明）
  3. 只是加入新研究員/機構？ → 新研究員修改（附 FDA Form 1572 + CV）

這三類都歸屬於「Protocol Amendments」，不同的只是內容和附件要求。

Phase 1 vs Phase 2/3 的規則差異

§312.30 對不同研究期別設有不同的啟動規則：

  • Phase 1：提交新協議後需等待 30 天（FDA 默示批准期），除非 FDA 提前通知可進行。這是因為 Phase 1 本身就是首次人體研究，安全不確定性最高。
  • Phase 2/3：可在提交修改的同日開始，除非收到 Clinical Hold 通知或 FDA 要求等待。

實務警示：許多申辦人誤以為所有協議修改都需等待 30 天。事實上只有 Phase 1 的新協議和顯著增加風險的變更才適用 30 天等待期。

新研究員修改 — CDMO 場景 New Investigator Amendment in CDMO Context

當多中心臨床試驗新增研究機構時，申辦人必須在受試者入組前提交新研究員修改。每位新研究員都需要：

  • 已簽署的 FDA Form 1572（Statement of Investigator，研究員承諾書）
  • 研究員的 CV（確認資格與相關經驗）
  • 機構審查委員會（IRB）批准文件

CDMO 角色提醒：合約製造商不是研究員，不需簽署 1572。但 CDMO 若涉及臨床試驗物料製造，必須確保其設施資訊已被納入 IND 的 CMC 部分，並在設施或方法有重大變更時知會申辦人。

練習思考 Quick Classification

判斷以下情境屬於哪類協議修改，是否需等待 FDA 批准：

  1. Phase 1 IND 下，申辦人想新增一個 Phase 2 劑量確認研究（新適應症，同一藥物）
  2. 現有 Phase 3 研究中，更改主要終點的評估時間從 6 個月延長到 9 個月（原因：觀察到治療效果出現較慢）
  3. Phase 2 研究新增一個澳洲的臨床試驗機構，由具有豐富專科經驗的研究員主持

參考答案在右欄下方

答案： (1) 新協議修改，需等 30 天（Phase 1 規則）。(2) 協議變更修改，可立即執行（Phase 3），但需提交說明文件。(3) 新研究員修改，不需等待，但入組前必須已提交。

3.2 §312.31 — Information Amendments 資訊修改申請

Original Text & Regulatory Requirements

§ 312.31 Information amendments.

A sponsor shall report to FDA, as a matter of routine communication, information relevant to the investigation that is not a protocol amendment, IND safety report, or annual report. Such information shall be submitted in the form of an information amendment whenever:

  • The sponsor acquires new nonclinical data that could affect the safety of subjects
  • The sponsor acquires new information regarding the chemistry, manufacturing, and controls (CMC) section
  • There is a change in the responsible medical officer or sponsor contact
  • There are significant changes in the sponsor's organizational structure

Timing: Information amendments shall be submitted within 30 days of the sponsor's acquiring the relevant information. If the information is directly related to an ongoing study's safety, submission shall be made as soon as practicable.

CMC Changes Requiring Information Amendments:

  • Change in manufacturing site for drug substance or drug product
  • Adoption of a new analytical method that materially changes release specifications
  • Change in synthesis route for the API (could affect impurity profile)
  • Change in container/closure system
  • Change in sterilization method or cycle parameters (for sterile products)
  • New stability data indicating shortened shelf life or significant degradation
  • Out-of-specification results for clinical batches already administered to subjects

What is NOT an Information Amendment:

  • Protocol changes (those go as Protocol Amendments under §312.30)
  • Safety events meeting SUSAR criteria (those go as IND Safety Reports under §312.32)
  • Annual summary information (that goes in Annual Report under §312.33)

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

資訊修改的定位：「其他所有重要資訊的容納槽」

§312.31 可以理解為 IND 申請體系的「通用收件槽」——凡是重要且新獲得的資訊，若不屬於協議修改、安全報告或年度報告的範疇，就透過資訊修改遞交給 FDA。

資訊修改在實務上最常出現在以下兩大情境：

  • CMC 更新：製造商變更、方法變更、設施變更 — 任何可能影響臨床試驗物料品質的製造/分析變更都需要資訊修改
  • 非臨床新數據：動物實驗新發現（毒性、藥理）可能影響臨床計畫的安全評估

CMC 資訊修改 — CDMO 最直接相關的法規義務

對合約製造機構（CDMO）而言，§312.31 是最直接相關的條款之一，因為 CMC 變更往往發生在製造端：

  • 製造場址變更：例如原廠 A 生產，改由關係企業或合約工廠 B 生產 → 申辦人需提交資訊修改，更新 IND 的 CMC 章節
  • 分析方法變更：採用新的 HPLC 方法替代舊方法進行效價測試 → 需提交資訊修改並附新方法驗證摘要
  • 滅菌製程變更：修改無菌過濾條件或最終滅菌循環參數 → 需提交資訊修改附驗證數據

核心原則：CDMO 不是 IND 持有人，不能直接遞交資訊修改。但 CDMO 有義務及時通知申辦人任何影響臨床物料品質的製造或測試變更，使申辦人能在 30 天期限內遞交資訊修改。

常見 FDA 483：CMC 變更未提交資訊修改

FDA 查廠官員在審查 IND 文件與內部記錄時，會特別核查以下問題：

  • 製造記錄顯示批次生產場址已更換，但 FDA 系統中的 IND 資料仍顯示原場址 → 典型 483
  • 分析方法變更後的批次已用於臨床試驗，但申辦人未向 FDA 報告方法變更 → 嚴重的資訊申報缺失
  • 穩定性試驗新數據顯示貨架期縮短，但 IND 中的有效期仍為原始值 → 可能導致已過期試驗藥品繼續使用於試驗

後果：這類疏失可能導致 FDA 對整個臨床計畫的數據完整性提出質疑，甚至觸發 Clinical Hold。

比喻：資訊修改如同建築許可的「變更通知」

想像建築師獲得了某棟樓的建築許可（類比 IND 批准）。開工後若材料、工法有任何重大變更，都必須通知審查機關（類比 FDA）。資訊修改就像這種「施工變更通知」——不改變整體設計藍圖（不是協議修改），也不是安全事故（不是安全報告），但仍是主管機關需要知道的重要變更。未通知便擅自更改，可能導致整個建築許可被撤銷（類比 IND Clinical Hold）。

3.3 §312.32 — IND Safety Reports（重點章節）IND 安全報告

Original Text & Regulatory Requirements

§ 312.32 IND safety reports.

(a) Definitions.

  • Adverse event (AE): Any untoward medical occurrence associated with the use of a drug in humans, whether or not considered drug related.
  • Serious adverse event (SAE): Any adverse event that results in death, is life-threatening, requires inpatient hospitalization or prolongation of existing hospitalization, results in persistent or significant disability or incapacity, is a congenital anomaly/birth defect, or is an important medical event that may not be immediately life-threatening but may jeopardize the patient and may require medical or surgical intervention to prevent one of the listed outcomes.
  • Unexpected adverse drug reaction: An adverse drug reaction, the nature or severity of which is not consistent with the applicable product information (e.g., Investigator's Brochure for an unapproved product or, for an approved product, the package insert).
  • Suspected adverse drug reaction (SADR): Any adverse event for which there is a reasonable possibility that the drug caused the adverse event.

SUSAR = Serious + Unexpected + Suspected Adverse Reaction

Reporting Obligations:

(b)(1) 7-Day Expedited Report: A sponsor shall notify FDA and all participating investigators in a written IND safety report of any suspected adverse drug reaction that is both serious and unexpected and that is fatal or life-threatening. The 7-day clock begins on the day the sponsor first receives information indicating that the SUSAR is fatal or life-threatening.

(b)(2) 15-Day Expedited Report: A sponsor shall notify FDA and all participating investigators in a written IND safety report of any adverse experience associated with the use of the drug that is both serious and unexpected and that is not fatal or life-threatening. The report shall be submitted within 15 calendar days after the sponsor's initial receipt of information that a reportable adverse experience has occurred.

(b)(3) Follow-up Reports: A sponsor shall also notify FDA of any findings from animal or in vitro studies that suggest a significant risk for human subjects including reports of mutagenicity, teratogenicity, or carcinogenicity. Such findings shall be reported within 15 calendar days after the sponsor receives information suggesting significant risk.

(b)(4) Annual Report Safety Summary: A sponsor shall submit a safety update in the annual report (§312.33) describing the sponsor's approach to monitoring for serious and unexpected adverse experiences and a summary of such experiences occurring during the study.

Content of Safety Reports:

  • A narrative description of the event including relevant medical history
  • Description of adverse event: onset, duration, severity, outcome
  • Drug information: dose, route of administration, start/stop dates, batch/lot number
  • Assessment of the relationship between the drug and the adverse event (causality assessment)
  • Relevant concomitant medications and medical conditions
  • Proposed actions to be taken: dose modification, protocol amendment, or clinical hold request

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

SUSAR 定義拆解 — 三個條件缺一不可

SUSAR（Serious Unexpected Suspected Adverse Reaction，嚴重且非預期的疑似藥物不良反應）必須同時滿足三個條件：

  1. Serious（嚴重性）：死亡、危及生命、需住院、永久殘疾、先天畸形、或重要醫療事件（任一符合即可）
  2. Unexpected（非預期性）：該事件的性質或嚴重程度，在研究員手冊（Investigator's Brochure）中未被列為已知不良反應，或雖有列出但實際發生的嚴重程度比已知描述更嚴重
  3. Suspected（疑似因果關係）：有合理可能性認為是試驗藥品導致，而非完全由受試者的基礎疾病或其他因素所致

只有同時滿足三個條件，才需提交快速安全報告。若不嚴重（非 SAE）、已預期、或因果關係可排除，則不屬於 SUSAR，只需在年度報告中彙整。

「未預期（Unexpected）」的判斷基準詳解

「未預期」是最容易引起爭議的判斷要素。FDA 的立場是：

  • 判斷基準文件：研究員手冊（Investigator's Brochure, IB）中記載的不良反應資料。若藥物已上市，則以核准仿單（Package Insert / Prescribing Information）為準。
  • 若 IB 中描述某不良反應為「輕度頭痛（mild headache）」，而受試者出現「嚴重且需住院的頭痛」，則即使「頭痛」這個事件已在 IB 中列出，但因嚴重程度超出 IB 描述，仍視為「未預期」。
  • 當研究員手冊更新後，新的安全資訊被納入 IB，原本「未預期」的反應會轉變為「已預期」，未來類似事件就不再需要快速報告。這是申辦人更新 IB 的重要動機之一。

比喻：安全報告如同航空業的強制事件報告

航空業有一套強制事故通報制度：無論飛行員或航空公司是否「有過錯」，某些安全事件（如機械故障、鳥擊、跑道侵入）都必須在規定時間內向監管機關通報。這套制度的目的是系統性安全改善，而非追責。

IND 安全報告的邏輯完全一致：FDA 不是要追究申辦人責任，而是需要即時獲得安全信號，以便在必要時採取保護受試者的監管行動（如發出 Clinical Hold）。因此，申辦人不能因為「認為事件與藥物無關」就延遲或省略報告——若符合 SUSAR 定義，就必須報告，同時在報告中說明因果關係評估意見。

CDMO 在安全報告中的角色與邊界

這是 CDMO 從業人員最需要理解的邊界問題：

角色 責任
申辦人（Sponsor） IND 持有人，唯一有法規義務向 FDA 提交 IND 安全報告的實體
CDMO 不是 IND 持有人，不直接向 FDA 遞交安全報告。但必須立即通知申辦人任何可能影響患者安全的製造問題（OOS 結果、偏差、污染等）
研究員（Investigator） 負責向申辦人報告不良事件；申辦人匯總後向 FDA 報告

CDMO 的核心義務：提供所有製造相關資訊（批號、配方、製程、OOS 調查結果）以支持申辦人的安全評估。CDMO 隱瞞或延遲通報製造問題，可能使申辦人錯過法定報告期限，是嚴重的合規風險。

致命 FDA 483：申辦人未遞交 7 天報告

§312.32 是 FDA 臨床調查查廠中被引用頻率最高的條款之一。典型的 483 觀察項目包括：

  • 「Failure to submit 7-day IND safety report for a fatal SUSAR」
    情境：申辦人收到受試者死亡報告，判斷為「預期反應（expected）」而未提交 7 天報告。FDA 查廠時發現死亡事件的性質實際上超出 IB 描述，應屬「未預期」，構成應報告的 SUSAR。
  • 「Failure to submit IND safety report within 15 calendar days」
    情境：申辦人收到嚴重不良事件報告後，花時間進行內部評估，導致超過 15 天期限才提交報告。
  • 「Safety reports lack required causality assessment or lot number information」
    情境：提交了安全報告但遺漏批次號碼，或因果關係評估僅一句話未提供科學依據。

後果等級：7 天報告缺失是 FDA 最嚴格對待的違規之一，可能直接觸發 Clinical Hold（臨床暫停令），影響整個開發計畫。

3.4 §312.33 — Annual Reports 年度報告

Original Text & Regulatory Requirements

§ 312.33 Annual reports.

A sponsor shall within 60 days of the anniversary date that the IND went into effect, submit a brief report of the progress of the investigation that includes:

(a) Individual study information: For each clinical study in the IND, a brief summary of:

  • Title of study and study number
  • Protocol phase (Phase 1, 2, or 3)
  • Study objective and patient population studied
  • Total number of subjects planned and enrolled
  • Most frequent and most serious adverse experiences by body system
  • Summary of all IND safety reports submitted during the year
  • Current status of each study: ongoing, suspended, completed, or discontinued

(b) Summary of current status of the IND including the following information:

  • Summary of the overall status: list of studies, number of subjects enrolled, current study phase
  • Description of the general investigational plan for the coming year
  • A summary of the protocol changes made during the year and the reason for each change

(c) A brief summary of significant foreign marketing developments:

  • If the drug has been marketed in a foreign country, provide a brief summary of significant foreign marketing developments, including foreign regulatory authority actions, foreign adverse events, and differences in labeling from US labeling

(d) Chemistry, Manufacturing, and Controls (CMC) updates:

  • Any changes in the manufacturing or testing information for the drug substance or drug product that were implemented during the past year
  • Current specifications for the drug substance and drug product
  • Stability updates: summary of any new stability data generated during the year

(e) Microbiological information (for antimicrobial drugs):

  • Summary of microbiological data, including in vitro susceptibility data and data related to development of bacterial resistance

Deadline: Annual reports are due within 60 calendar days of the anniversary of the IND effective date. There is no grace period. If the IND became effective on April 14, the annual report is due by June 13 of each subsequent year.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

年度報告的六大必備項目

§312.33 年度報告的框架可以記憶為「六大模組」：

  1. 個別研究資訊：每個臨床試驗的狀態、入組數、不良事件摘要
  2. IND 整體狀態與未來計畫：過去一年的進展 + 下一年的研究計畫
  3. 協議修改摘要：全年提交的所有協議修改及其原因
  4. 境外市場發展：若藥物已在其他國家上市，需報告境外監管動態
  5. CMC 更新：全年製造或測試方法的任何變更，加上最新規格與穩定性數據
  6. 微生物學資料（抗感染藥物）：體外敏感性數據及抗藥性發展資訊

年度報告不是快速安全報告——它是一份全年回顧文件，不能取代 7 天或 15 天的即時報告義務。

年度報告與 IND 生效日 — 時鐘計算

§312.33 的截止日是 IND 生效日週年後 60 天。IND 生效日不是 FDA 批准日，而是 FDA 收到 IND 申請後的第 31 天（若未發出臨床暫停令）：

  • IND 收件日：例如 2026-04-14
  • IND 生效日：2026-05-14（若無 Clinical Hold）
  • 第一份年度報告截止：2027-07-13（IND 生效後 1 年 + 60 天）

申辦人必須在內部系統中追蹤 IND 生效日，建立提醒機制，確保不逾期。FDA 的年度報告追蹤系統會主動核查遞交合規性。

CMC 章節年度更新 — 與 CDMO 的協作義務

年度報告中的 CMC 更新章節，是 CDMO 與申辦人全年溝通成果的彙整。需要包括：

  • 本年度所有已提交的資訊修改（§312.31）的摘要
  • 生產場址、製程、測試方法的任何變更
  • 批次製造記錄中的任何重要偏差及調查結果
  • 最新的藥品或藥物原料穩定性數據，包括任何超趨勢或 OOS 結果

如果 CDMO 在全年未曾將重要製造資訊通知申辦人，申辦人在年度報告截止前可能才發現資訊缺口，導致要麼遞交不完整的年度報告，要麼匆忙提交大量補充資訊修改，兩者都是合規風險。

年度報告的常見疏失

  • 遺漏已終止研究：某研究雖已停止招募，但仍有受試者在觀察期中，相關不良事件仍需在年度報告中列出
  • 安全事件摘要不完整：只列出當年快速報告的 SUSAR，遺漏非 SUSAR 但仍重要的不良事件趨勢
  • CMC 更新遺漏製造場址變更：若已在 §312.31 提交資訊修改，年度報告中仍需摘要說明，但不能以此為由省略年度 CMC 彙整
  • 未來計畫描述過於模糊：FDA 用年度報告評估臨床開發計畫的合理性；「視情況而定」此類描述可能引發 FDA 詢問函

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

比較表的記憶框架

三種報告機制的核心差異可以用一句話記憶：

  • 7 天：「有人在死/要死了 → 立即報告」
  • 15 天：「嚴重但非立即死亡的新發現 → 兩週內報告」
  • 年度報告：「一年來所有安全資訊的總帳 → 週年後 60 天內交卷」

另一個記憶重點：7 天和 15 天報告都需要同時通知 FDA + 所有研究員，而年度報告只送 FDA。這是因為快速安全報告的目的之一是讓前線研究員即時知道潛在安全風險，以保護目前正在入組中的受試者。

SUSAR 三要件的灰色地帶

實務中最常發生爭議的情境：

  • 「嚴重」vs「非嚴重」的邊界：「重要醫療事件（important medical event）」的認定具有主觀性。例如：嚴重低血糖需要靜脈注射葡萄糖但未住院 — 通常認定為「嚴重」，因為若未及時干預可能危及生命。
  • 「未預期」的更新時機：當申辦人決定更新 IB 以反映新認識到的不良反應時，更新後的 IB 使原先「未預期」的反應變成「預期」。但 IB 更新本身也必須通知 FDA（透過協議修改或資訊修改）。
  • 「疑似因果關係」的判斷分歧：研究員填報「可能無關（Unrelated）」，但申辦人醫學審查後認為無法排除因果關係 → 申辦人應按照「疑似」處理，提交報告，並在報告中說明兩方評估意見的差異。FDA 的立場是：有疑問時，報告優先。

Practice: Classify the Report Type

練習一：報告類型分類 Classify the Report Type

請為以下每個情境分類：屬於 7 天報告、15 天報告，還是只需在年度報告中提及？

  1. Phase 2 研究中，一位受試者在注射試驗藥品後第 3 天因急性肝衰竭住院，需要緊急肝臟移植評估。研究員評估為「可能相關（probably related）」。肝毒性在 IB 中僅列為「輕度、可逆性 ALT 上升」，從未提及急性肝衰竭。
  2. Phase 1 試驗中，一位受試者出現 Grade 2 噁心（需要藥物處置但未住院）。IB 中已明確列出噁心為常見不良反應（Grade 1-3）。
  3. 動物毒理研究新數據顯示高劑量組大鼠出現肝臟腺瘤，而目前 Phase 2 試驗的人體劑量為動物致癌劑量的 1/5。
  4. Phase 3 研究中，一位 73 歲女性受試者在第 8 週治療後死亡，死因初步判斷為心臟病發作（MI），已知她有高血壓和糖尿病病史。研究員認為與藥物「不太可能相關（unlikely related）」。IB 中無心血管事件記載。

練習一答案解析

  1. 15 天報告（非 7 天，因「住院」是嚴重，但「需移植評估」尚非「危及生命（life-threatening）已確認」——若情況惡化至危及生命則升級為 7 天）。SERIOUS ✓（住院）+ UNEXPECTED ✓（急性肝衰竭超出 IB）+ SUSPECTED ✓（可能相關）= SUSAR → 15-day report
  2. 年度報告。Grade 2 噁心：非 SAE（未住院、非危及生命）。SERIOUS ✗ → 不是 SUSAR，無需快速報告。
  3. 15 天報告（動物致癌性發現 = 「significant risk for human subjects」，按 §312.32(b)(3) 提交 15 天報告）
  4. 15 天報告（可能有爭議，但保守立場適用）。死亡 = SERIOUS ✓。心臟病發作未在 IB 列出 = UNEXPECTED ✓。「Unlikely related」≠「不可能相關」——FDA 立場：只要不能完全排除因果關係，就仍屬 SUSPECTED ✓。有疑問時，報告優先。

Application: CDMO Endotoxin OOS Scenario

CDMO 應用情境：內毒素 OOS — 從製造偏差到 IND 安全報告的完整流程

情境背景：

某 CDMO 正在為一家生物技術公司（申辦人）製造 Phase 2 臨床試驗用靜脈注射製劑。QC 部門在例行穩定性測試中發現：已放行並配送至臨床試驗機構的 3 個批次（批號：CT-2026-011, 012, 013），其中內毒素含量超出規格上限 2 倍（OOS 結果）。這 3 個批次已對共計 47 名受試者給藥，約有 12 名受試者仍在使用中。

問題：

  1. CDMO QC 部門發現 OOS 結果後，應立即採取哪些行動？
  2. CDMO 應向申辦人提供哪些具體資訊？
  3. 申辦人收到通知後，應進行哪些評估以決定是否需要提交 IND 安全報告？
  4. 若決定需要提交安全報告，適用 7 天還是 15 天報告？報告內容應包括哪些要素？
  5. 除安全報告外，申辦人是否還需提交其他類型的修改申請？

CDMO 情境完整解析

Step 1 — CDMO 立即行動（OOS 確認後 24 小時內）：

  • 啟動 OOS 調查程序，確認結果是否為「真 OOS」（排除實驗誤差）
  • 立即通知申辦人（電話 + 書面確認），說明：哪些批次、OOS 數值、已給藥批次的配送/回收狀態
  • 隔離所有相關批次（若仍有未使用存貨）
  • 同步向 QA 通報，啟動偏差調查，保存所有原始記錄

Step 2 — CDMO 提供給申辦人的資訊清單：

  • OOS 批次的批號、製造日期、配送日期、配送地點
  • 具體 OOS 測試結果（測試日期、測試方法、規格上下限、實測值）
  • 初步根因評估（製程、原料、測試方法、設備問題）
  • 已給藥受試者數量（如可知）
  • 相關批次製造記錄（Batch Record）摘要
  • 任何類似歷史偏差記錄（趨勢分析）

Step 3 — 申辦人安全評估（決定是否觸發 IND 安全報告）：

  • 內毒素超標 2 倍的臨床風險評估：靜脈注射製劑中內毒素可直接引起發熱反應（Pyrogenic Reaction）、嚴重時引起膿毒性休克（Septic Shock）
  • 查詢 47 名受試者中是否有不明原因的發熱反應、寒顫、低血壓報告，特別是給藥後 1-4 小時內的不良事件
  • 評估「unexpected」：內毒素相關的系統性發炎反應是否已列於 IB？通常未針對製造污染導致的內毒素反應進行說明

Step 4 — 報告類型判定：

  • 若調查發現任何受試者出現發熱性反應或更嚴重事件（住院、低血壓）且與給藥時間相關：→ 15 天 IND 安全報告（SAE + Unexpected + Suspected）
  • 若調查發現受試者出現危及生命的嚴重膿毒症反應：→ 7 天 IND 安全報告
  • 即使目前無症狀：→ 申辦人應至少提交資訊修改（§312.31）報告 CMC OOS 問題，並評估是否需要修改入組/給藥計畫（可能需要協議修改）

Step 5 — 其他必要申請：

  • §312.31 資訊修改：OOS 製造發現 → CMC 資訊修改（30 天內）
  • §312.30 協議修改：若決定停用受影響批次並修改試驗程序，需提交協議修改
  • 考慮主動向 FDA 申請討論（Pre-Meeting Request）評估是否需要 Clinical Hold
  • 通知所有研究員（主動聯繫各中心，確認受試者狀況）

關鍵教訓： CDMO 的職責在 Step 1 和 Step 2。申辦人的職責從 Step 3 開始。兩者的溝通速度和資訊完整性，直接決定了申辦人能否在法定期限內完成安全評估並遞交報告。

4.1 §312.50–312.53 — Sponsor Obligations to Investigators

Regulatory Text & Analysis

§312.50 — General Sponsor Responsibilities

Sponsors are responsible for selecting qualified investigators and providing each investigator with the information needed to conduct an investigation properly, for ensuring proper monitoring of the investigation(s), for ensuring that the investigation(s) is conducted in accordance with the general investigational plan and protocols contained in the IND, for maintaining an effective IND with respect to the investigations, and for ensuring that FDA and all participating investigators are promptly informed of significant new adverse effects or risks with respect to the drug.

§312.51 — Transfer of Obligations to a Contract Research Organization (CRO)

A sponsor may transfer responsibility for any or all of the sponsor's obligations under this part to a contract research organization. Any such transfer shall be described in writing. If not all obligations are transferred, the writing is required to describe each obligation being assumed by the CRO. A CRO that assumes any obligation of a sponsor shall comply with the specific regulations applicable to that obligation and shall be subject to the same regulatory action as a sponsor for failure to comply with any obligation assumed under this part. The sponsor retains ultimate responsibility for all transferred obligations that the CRO fails to fulfill.

§312.52 — Use of Contract Research Organizations

If a sponsor uses a CRO, the sponsor shall transfer in writing each obligation being assumed by the CRO. The FDA may inspect the CRO to determine whether it is in compliance with the regulations applicable to the obligations assumed and whether it is performing the obligations that were transferred to it.

"A contract research organization that assumes any obligation of a sponsor shall comply with the specific regulations applicable to that obligation and shall be subject to the same regulatory action as a sponsor for failure to comply."
— 21 CFR §312.52(b)

§312.53 — Selecting Investigators and Monitors

Before permitting an investigator to begin participation in an investigation, the sponsor shall obtain the following:

  • A signed Form FDA 1572 — Statement of Investigator — from each investigator
  • A curriculum vitae or other statement of qualifications of the investigator showing the education, training, and experience that qualifies the investigator as an expert in the clinical investigation of the drug for the use under investigation
  • A protocol describing the clinical study to be conducted by the investigator
  • Financial disclosure information if required under 21 CFR Part 54

The sponsor must also select a monitor who is qualified by training and experience to monitor the progress of the investigation.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：申辦者義務的五大支柱

§312.50 定義了申辦者對研究者的五大核心義務，可以用首字母記憶：SIPME

  • S — Select（選擇）： 選擇具備資格的研究者
  • I — Inform（告知）： 提供 Investigator's Brochure（IB）及所有必要資訊
  • P — Protocol compliance（協議合規）： 確保研究依照 IND 協議進行
  • M — Monitor（監測）： 持續監測研究進展
  • E — Emergent safety（緊急安全）： 立即通知 FDA 及所有研究者重大新增安全疑慮

這五大義務不能「授權給他人就免責」——即使轉移給 CRO，申辦者仍承擔最終責任。

比喻：總承包商與分包商模型

申辦者就像總承包商（General Contractor），承接了 FDA（業主）委託的「臨床試驗大樓工程」。CRO、研究者、CDMO 都是分包商（Subcontractors）。

關鍵法律邏輯：

  • 總承包商可以把鋼筋工程外包給分包商（§312.52 轉移義務）
  • 但如果鋼筋不合格，業主（FDA）找的是總承包商，不是分包商
  • FDA 也有權直接查驗分包商工地（CRO 查廠權）
  • 分包商若不遵規，和總承包商受到同等監管處分（CRO 同等監管標準）

這個邏輯直接解釋了為何申辦者要謹慎選擇 CRO 和 CDMO，並在品質協議中設置充分的管控機制。

CRO 義務轉移必須是書面的——口頭無效

§312.51 的「書面（in writing）」要求不是行政形式：口頭協議將義務轉移給 CRO 是無效的。FDA 查廠時若找不到書面轉移協議，申辦者對 CRO 執行的所有義務仍然負責。

實務提醒：CRO 合約必須包含「義務轉移矩陣（obligation transfer matrix）」，明確列出哪些 Part 312 義務由 CRO 承擔。若只有一般性的「CRO 同意提供服務」條款，不符合 §312.51 的書面要求。

4.2 Form FDA 1572 — Statement of Investigator

Form FDA 1572 — Required Elements

Form FDA 1572 is the formal commitment document signed by each clinical investigator before participating in an IND study. It is not merely administrative paperwork — it is a legal agreement between the investigator and the federal government. Providing false information on Form FDA 1572 may constitute a federal crime under 18 U.S.C. §1001.

Required information fields on FDA 1572:

  • Name and address of the investigator
  • Name and address of the research facility where the investigation will be conducted
  • Names of sub-investigators who will be assisting the investigator in the study
  • Name and address of the Institutional Review Board (IRB) responsible for reviewing and approving the study
  • Names of other IRBs that will review the study at each additional site
  • Reference to the current protocol and investigator's brochure

Investigator commitments upon signing FDA 1572:

  1. Conduct the study in accordance with the signed protocol and any amendments
  2. Personally conduct or supervise the described investigation
  3. Inform patients or subjects that the drugs are being used for investigational purposes
  4. Ensure requirements relating to obtaining informed consent (21 CFR Part 50) are met
  5. Report to the sponsor adverse experiences (AEs) that occur in the course of the investigation
  6. Read and understand the information in the Investigator's Brochure (IB), including potential risks and side effects of the drug
  7. Ensure that all associates, colleagues, and employees assisting in the investigation are informed about their obligations in meeting the above commitments
  8. Maintain adequate and accurate records per §312.62 and make them available for inspection
  9. Ensure that the IRB complies with 21 CFR Part 56 requirements
  10. Comply with all other requirements regarding obligations of clinical investigators under 21 CFR Part 312

Record retention obligation from FDA 1572 / §312.62:

An investigator shall retain records required to be maintained per §312.62 for a period of 2 years following the date a marketing application is approved for the drug for the indication for which it is being investigated; or, if no application is to be filed or if the application is not approved for such indication, until 2 years after the investigation is discontinued and FDA is notified.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：FDA 1572 的法律地位

FDA 1572 不是普通表格——它具有聯邦法律效力。關鍵點：

  • 聯邦聲明（Federal Statement）： 簽署意味著向聯邦政府做出正式聲明；蓄意提供虛假資訊可依聯邦刑法 18 U.S.C. §1001 起訴
  • IRB 委任： 1572 上填寫的 IRB 就是 FDA 認定有責任審查本研究的機構——更換 IRB 需要申辦者知情並通知 FDA
  • Sub-investigator 義務連帶： 主要研究者（PI）對所有 1572 上列名的 sub-investigator 的行為負責
  • IB 閱讀義務： 簽署 1572 代表研究者承諾已閱讀並理解 IB——若後來發生研究者不了解藥品已知風險的情況，1572 會成為重要的法律依據

藥品處置記錄要求 — 每批次每受試者追蹤

藥品處置記錄必須追蹤（§312.62(a)）：

對每個批次（Lot）：
  ├── 收到數量（Received from sponsor）
  ├── 收到日期（Date received）
  ├── 批號（Lot number）
  └── 使用 / 返還 / 銷毀明細：
        ├── 分發給哪位受試者（Subject ID）
        ├── 每次給藥量（Dose per administration）
        ├── 給藥日期（Administration dates）
        ├── 剩餘量（Unused quantity）
        ├── 返還申辦者數量（Returned to sponsor）
        └── 銷毀記錄（Destroyed: method, date, witness）

每筆記錄必須可以追溯到：
  原始批號 → 受試者 → 給藥日期 → 結果（不良事件 / 療效）

為何如此重要： 若臨床試驗中出現嚴重不良事件（SAE），FDA 及申辦者必須能追蹤「哪個批次的藥品給了哪個受試者」。記錄不全是臨床試驗中最常見的 Warning Letter 缺失之一。

記錄保存期限的雙重觸發機制

記錄保存期限從兩個事件中較晚發生者起算 2 年：

  • 事件 A： 該藥品的 NDA/BLA 核准日
  • 事件 B： 研究終止且 FDA 收到通知的日期

實務困難：研究者無法知道「NDA 何時獲批」。因此，申辦者有義務通知研究者 NDA 核准日期，以便研究者從該日起計算 2 年保存期限。品質協議通常包含「申辦者在 NDA 核准後 30 天內通知 CDMO」的條款。

4.3 §312.60–312.62 — Investigator Responsibilities

Investigator Obligations: Conduct, Control & Records

§312.60 — General Responsibilities of Investigators

An investigator is responsible for ensuring that an investigation is conducted according to the signed investigator statement, the investigational plan, and applicable regulations; for protecting the rights, safety, and welfare of subjects under the investigator's care; and for the control of drugs under investigation. An investigator shall, under 21 CFR Part 50, obtain the informed consent of each human subject to whom the drug is administered, except as provided in §50.23 or §50.24. Additional specific responsibilities of clinical investigators are set forth in Parts 50 and 56.

§312.61 — Control of the Investigational Drug

An investigator shall administer the drug only to subjects under the investigator's personal supervision or under the supervision of a sub-investigator responsible to the investigator. The investigator shall not supply the investigational drug to any person not authorized under this part to receive it.

Critical implications of §312.61:

  • The investigational drug cannot be shared between study sites unless properly authorized
  • The investigator cannot give the drug to a physician not listed as sub-investigator on Form FDA 1572
  • The drug cannot be used for "compassionate use" or "expanded access" outside the IND framework without separate authorization
  • Drug accountability records must be maintained to demonstrate compliance with this section

§312.62 — Investigator Recordkeeping and Record Retention

(a) Disposition of drug: An investigator is required to maintain adequate records of the disposition of the drug, including dates, quantity, and use by subjects. If the investigation is terminated, suspended, discontinued, or completed, the investigator shall return the unused supplies of the drug to the sponsor, or otherwise provide for disposition of the unused supplies of the drug in accordance with the sponsor's instructions.

(b) Case histories: An investigator is required to prepare and maintain adequate and accurate case histories that record all observations and other data pertinent to the investigation on each individual administered the investigational drug or employed as a control in the investigation. Case histories include the case report forms and supporting data including, for example, signed and dated consent forms and medical records including, for example, progress notes of the physician, the individual's hospital chart(s), and the nurses' notes. The case history for each individual shall document that informed consent was obtained prior to participation in the study.

(c) Record retention: An investigator shall retain records required to be maintained under this part for a period of 2 years following the date a marketing application is approved for the drug for the indication for which it is being investigated; or, if no application is to be filed or if the application is not approved for such indication, until 2 years after the investigation is discontinued and FDA is notified.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：研究者 vs. 申辦者 vs. CDMO 責任分工

Part 312 Subpart D 中，三個角色的責任分工如下：

  • 申辦者（Sponsor）： 持有 IND、對整個試驗負最終責任、提供藥品給研究者
  • 研究者（Investigator）： 在臨床現場執行試驗、管理受試者、維護藥品控制記錄
  • CDMO： 製造臨床試驗用藥、維護生產批次記錄、提供 CMC 數據給申辦者的 IND

三個角色各自有獨立的 FDA 查廠風險：FDA 可以分別查驗申辦者、研究者和 CDMO，發現的缺失可能成為臨床暫停的依據。

比喻：藥品控制如同管制藥品物流

§312.61 的藥品控制要求與管制藥品（Controlled Substances）的物流管理邏輯非常相似：

  • 每個環節都要記錄： 就像嗎啡從藥局到病房每一步都要登記，臨床試驗用藥從 CDMO 到申辦者到研究者到受試者，每一步都要有帳
  • 不能轉讓： 研究者不能把藥品給其他醫師使用，正如藥局不能把管制藥品轉給鄰近診所
  • 剩餘量要歸還或銷毀： 試驗結束後，未使用的藥品必須依申辦者指示返還或銷毀，並保留證明

這個比喻幫助理解為何記錄要精確到「批號 × 受試者 × 每次給藥」的粒度。

Case History vs. CRF — 兩者都是 §312.62(b) 要求

§312.62(b) 明確指出「case histories」包括兩個層次的文件：

  • 原始醫療記錄（Source Documents）： 醫師進展記錄（progress notes）、住院病歷（hospital chart）、護理記錄（nurses' notes）——這些是「原始資料」
  • 臨床試驗記錄表（CRF, Case Report Form）： 從原始記錄中摘錄的標準化試驗數據表格

資料完整性重點： FDA 查廠時，若 CRF 數據與原始醫療記錄不一致，這是重大的 GCP 違規（data integrity violation）。兩者必須能相互核實（source data verification, SDV）。

4.4 GMP Requirements for Investigational Drugs — The CDMO Perspective

CGMP for Investigational Drugs: Phase-Dependent Standards

§312.60 states that investigators are responsible for ensuring investigational drugs comply with "applicable current good manufacturing practice." This cross-reference triggers the full GMP framework — but the applicable standard varies by phase.

FDA Guidance: CGMP for Phase 1 Investigational Drugs (2008)

FDA published guidance clarifying minimum CGMP for Phase 1 investigational drugs. Key minimum requirements:

  • Written procedures for all critical manufacturing operations — batch record templates, SOPs for weighing, mixing, filling, sterilization, and labeling
  • Identity, strength, purity, and quality testing — confirmation that the drug manufactured is the drug intended, at the correct potency, without unacceptable impurities
  • Equipment maintenance and calibration records — traceable to measurements, dated, reviewed
  • Batch records with full traceability — who did what, when, with what equipment, using which materials, at what process parameters
  • Complete investigation of failures — out-of-specification (OOS) results, deviations, unexpected observations must be formally investigated and documented
  • Personnel training records — evidence that all personnel performing critical operations have been trained on applicable procedures

Phase 2 and Phase 3 — Full 21 CFR Parts 210/211 Compliance Required

For Phase 2 and Phase 3 clinical trials, the full requirements of 21 CFR Parts 210 and 211 apply. This means all provisions governing facilities, equipment qualification, component testing, in-process controls, laboratory controls, production records, and batch release apply to the clinical trial material. CDMOs manufacturing Phase 2/3 material must comply with the same GMP standards as commercial drug manufacturing.

Special Case: Sterile Phase 1 Injectables

Even for Phase 1, sterile injectable products require full aseptic processing controls. The rationale is straightforward: a non-sterile injection can cause immediate, serious, life-threatening infection in a human subject. Phase 1 injectables must therefore demonstrate:

  • Aseptic processing controls documented and validated (or justified for Phase 1 where formal validation may not yet be complete)
  • Media fills demonstrating aseptic technique capability
  • Environmental monitoring program for cleanroom and RABS/isolator
  • Sterility testing per appropriate pharmacopoeial method
  • Container-closure integrity testing
  • Personnel aseptic technique qualification

FDA's 2004 Aseptic Processing Guidance and the PICS Annex 1 (2022) both apply to sterile Phase 1 investigational products manufactured in commercial sterile manufacturing facilities.

導師解析 Tutorial Commentary

核心概念：為何 Phase 1 GMP 要求「最低但不是沒有」？

2008 FDA 指引的核心邏輯：Phase 1 試驗的主要目的是確認安全性和藥物動力學，而非療效。這表示：

  • 受試者數量少（通常 10–30 人），但每位都是高風險志願者
  • 藥品品質直接影響 Phase 1 試驗的安全信號解讀——若藥品受到微生物污染或純度不足，安全信號可能是假陽性（污染物引起），或掩蓋藥品本身的真實毒性
  • 因此，「最低 GMP」不等於「沒有 GMP」：它等於確保藥品品質的最少必要管控

常見錯誤認知：小批量 = 不需要嚴格 GMP。正確認知：小批量 + 人體首次使用 = 每個批次的品質管控更加重要，因為沒有大批量數據平滑統計誤差。

無菌注射劑 Phase 1 的 GMP 底線不可妥協

無論是 Phase 1 還是 Phase 2/3，無菌注射劑的非無菌（non-sterility）後果是立即且嚴重的：

  • 靜脈注射受到細菌污染的藥品可在數小時內引起敗血症（sepsis）
  • 歷史案例：2012 年美國新英格蘭複合藥局（NECC）事件，受污染的硬膜外類固醇注射劑導致 64 人死亡、750+ 人感染真菌性腦膜炎
  • Phase 1 安全試驗的悲劇反諷：若因 GMP 不足導致受試者感染，整個臨床計畫和安全數據庫都將受到質疑

CDMO 實務： 客戶常以「只是 Phase 1，規格可以寬鬆」為由要求降低無菌管控標準——這是錯誤且危險的要求。CDMO 應堅持無菌製程標準不因臨床階段而妥協。

4.5 CDMO Obligations Under Part 312 (Indirect)

CDMO's Position in the Part 312 Framework

CDMOs are not directly named as regulated entities in 21 CFR Part 312 — the IND holder (sponsor) is the primary regulated party. However, CDMOs are operationally bound by Part 312 through multiple indirect mechanisms that create real regulatory exposure:

1. Quality Agreements Referencing Part 312 Obligations

Under FDA's 2013 guidance "Contract Manufacturing Arrangements for Drugs: Quality Agreements," CDMOs and their sponsor clients must establish written quality agreements that allocate specific GMP and regulatory obligations. Best-practice quality agreements for clinical-stage products will reference §312.62 (record retention), §312.65 (inspections), and §312.50 (sponsor obligations that flow to CDMO manufacturing operations). If a quality agreement is absent or inadequate, FDA may hold the sponsor responsible for CDMO failures — which in turn drives sponsor commercial pressure on the CDMO.

2. FDA Inspection Rights at Manufacturing Sites

21 CFR §312.65 grants FDA the authority to inspect any facility where the investigation drug is manufactured or stored. Sponsors are required under §312.65 to ensure that FDA inspectors have access to all manufacturing records, equipment, and personnel. FDA does not need advance notice to inspect a CDMO manufacturing clinical investigational drugs. CDMOs must maintain manufacturing batch records, equipment maintenance records, personnel training records, and environmental monitoring data in a form that can be immediately presented to FDA inspectors.

3. IND CMC Section — CDMO Must Be Listed

The Chemistry, Manufacturing, and Controls (CMC) section of an IND must identify all manufacturing sites involved in the production of the investigational drug. This means:

  • CDMO name, address, and registration number must appear in the IND CMC section
  • CDMO manufacturing processes, equipment, and controls must be described in the CMC section
  • Any change to the CDMO or manufacturing process may require an IND amendment
  • If the CDMO has not been inspected by FDA and FDA determines an inspection is necessary, FDA may place the IND on clinical hold pending inspection

4. CDMO Manufacturing Records Support Sponsor's IND

The sponsor's annual IND report (§312.33) must include a summary of manufacturing changes and a description of the current manufacturing process. CDMOs must provide updated CMC data to the sponsor for each annual report. If CDMO records are incomplete or manufacturing processes have changed without IND notification, the sponsor's annual report may be inaccurate — an IND violation for which the sponsor bears responsibility.

核心概念：CDMO 的「間接但真實」的 Part 312 義務

Part 312 不直接點名 CDMO，但 CDMO 在臨床試驗供應鏈中的四個綁定機制：

  1. 品質協議（Quality Agreement）： 申辦者透過合約將 Part 312 義務下傳至 CDMO
  2. FDA 查廠權（Inspection Rights）： FDA 可直接查廠，不需通過申辦者
  3. IND CMC 揭露（CMC Disclosure）： CDMO 被點名在 IND 中，資訊不符即違規
  4. 生產記錄支持（Record Support）： CDMO 記錄是申辦者 IND 報告的基礎資料

商業影響： CDMO 若發生重大 GMP 缺失，不只影響自身的 FDA 查廠結果——還可能觸發申辦者的 IND 臨床暫停（clinical hold），影響所有使用該 CDMO 製造藥品的臨床計畫。

比喻：CDMO 是 IND「不具名但可見」的夥伴

想像 IND 是一場婚禮的主辦合約，申辦者是新人（IND 持有者），CDMO 是婚宴場地（製造設施）：

  • 合約上的名字是新人，但婚禮的成敗很大程度取決於場地的品質
  • 如果場地發生食安問題（GMP 違規），整個婚禮（臨床計畫）都可能停擺
  • 衛生局（FDA）可以直接查驗場地，不需要事先告知新人
  • 場地名稱必須在婚禮申報文件（IND CMC）中公開揭露

結論：CDMO 雖不是「婚姻當事人」，但一旦出事，後果由所有人共同承擔。

製造偏差通知流程 — CDMO 的義務

當 CDMO 發生製造偏差時，Part 312 框架下的通知義務如下：

  1. CDMO 內部調查： 偏差發生 → 隔離批次 → 啟動偏差調查（通常 24–72 小時內）
  2. 通知申辦者： 依品質協議規定的時限通知申辦者（通常重大偏差 24 小時內）
  3. 申辦者評估： 申辦者評估偏差是否需要 IND 修正（IND amendment）或安全報告
  4. IND 修正決定： 若偏差影響藥品安全性或 CMC 資料，申辦者必須提交 IND 修正
  5. 監管機構通報： 若偏差可能影響受試者安全，申辦者依 §312.32 向 FDA 提交安全報告

重要： CDMO 通知申辦者，申辦者決定是否通知 FDA——CDMO 不直接向 FDA 報告（除非 FDA 直接查廠時問及）。這個單向流程是 Part 312 責任結構的核心。

4.6 Clinical Hold (§312.42) — The CDMO Business Risk

Clinical Hold: Authority, Grounds & Resolution

§312.42 gives FDA the authority to issue a clinical hold — an order to the sponsor to delay a proposed clinical investigation or to suspend an ongoing investigation. A clinical hold is one of FDA's most powerful enforcement tools because it immediately halts human studies.

Grounds for a Clinical Hold

FDA may place an IND on clinical hold if it finds that:

  • Unreasonable risk: Human subjects would be exposed to an unreasonable and significant risk of illness or injury based on available data
  • Unqualified investigator: The clinical investigator named in the IND is not qualified by reason of his/her scientific training and experience to conduct the investigation described in the IND
  • False or misleading materials: The IND contains an untrue statement of a material fact or omits material information required by Part 312
  • Insufficient CMC information: The IND does not contain sufficient information required under §312.23 to assess the risks to subjects of the proposed studies
  • Manufacturing deficiencies: If FDA discovers that conditions in the manufacturing process, testing, or controls create a basis for concluding the drug cannot be made with appropriate purity, quality, strength, or identity

Scope of Clinical Hold

A clinical hold may be placed on all studies in the IND, or on specific studies within the IND. FDA may suspend all clinical investigations of a drug if manufacturing deficiencies affect the product across all studies.

Clinical Hold Resolution

To have a clinical hold removed, the sponsor must:

  1. Receive a written communication from FDA specifying the basis for the clinical hold
  2. Prepare a complete response addressing all issues identified by FDA
  3. Submit the complete response to FDA

FDA must respond within 30 calendar days of receipt of the complete response. If FDA does not respond within 30 days and the complete response adequately addresses the deficiencies, the sponsor may resume the investigation (for Phase 2/3) after notifying FDA. FDA's response may be a "complete response letter" lifting the hold, or a request for additional information.

Manufacturing-Based Clinical Hold Scenario

FDA inspects a sterile drug manufacturing facility (CDMO) and observes the following during an FDA Form 483 inspection of an aseptic processing line:

  • Three consecutive media fills have failed (growth observed in simulation units)
  • Environmental monitoring shows persistent Gram-positive organisms at Grade A fill zone
  • Aseptic gown qualification program shows expired qualifications for two filling operators
  • Investigation of a prior EM excursion was inadequately documented with no root cause identified

Based on these findings, FDA may conclude that drugs manufactured at this site cannot be assessed as safe for human administration and may issue a clinical hold on all IND studies using drug product manufactured at this CDMO site — across all sponsor clients' programs.

Chapter Summary — Section 4 本章重點摘要

Key Regulatory Framework Summary

§312.50–312.53: The Sponsor–Investigator–CRO Triad

The sponsor holds ultimate responsibility for all aspects of the IND investigation. Obligations may be transferred in writing to CROs, but the sponsor remains liable for CRO failures. Form FDA 1572 is the binding commitment document that ties each investigator to the regulatory framework — it functions as a signed federal statement with potential criminal liability for false information.

§312.60–312.62: Investigator Operational Obligations

Investigators must control the investigational drug strictly — no unauthorized transfer or use outside the IND framework. Drug disposition records must track every unit from lot receipt to subject administration to return or disposal, with traceability to specific lot numbers, subjects, and dates. Case histories (source documents and CRFs) must be maintained with source data verifiability. All records must be retained for 2 years after marketing approval or 2 years after investigation discontinuation plus FDA notification — whichever is later.

GMP Standards: Phase-Dependent, Safety-Absolute

Phase 1 requires minimum CGMP (written procedures, identity/strength/purity testing, traceable batch records, failure investigation, training records). Phase 2/3 requires full 21 CFR Parts 210/211. Sterile injectables require full aseptic processing controls regardless of phase — there is no reduced standard for sterility because the safety consequences of a non-sterile injection are immediate and potentially fatal.

CDMO Obligations: Indirect but Consequential

CDMOs are bound through four mechanisms: quality agreements, FDA inspection rights, IND CMC disclosure requirements, and manufacturing record support for the sponsor's IND. The responsibility matrix shows CDMOs own batch records, GMP execution, validation, and environmental monitoring — while sponsors own IND filing, safety reporting decisions, investigator management, and CMC section compilation.

Clinical Hold: The Highest-Stakes Outcome

FDA may issue a clinical hold based on manufacturing deficiencies at a CDMO. A manufacturing-based clinical hold can suspend all clinical programs using product from that CDMO — across all sponsor clients. The 30-day FDA response requirement applies to the sponsor's complete response, not to the underlying CDMO investigation, which may take months. CDMOs must understand that their GMP compliance directly protects their clients' clinical programs and their own commercial viability.

研究者必須嚴格控制臨床試驗用藥——不得在 IND 框架外轉讓或使用。藥品處置記錄必須追蹤從批次收受到受試者給藥再到返還或銷毀的每個單位，可追溯至具體批號、受試者和日期。所有記錄必須保存至 NDA 核准後 2 年，或試驗終止且 FDA 通知後 2 年——以較晚者為準。

GMP 標準：隨臨床階段調整，安全性底線不妥協

Phase 1 要求最低 CGMP（書面程序、鑑別/強度/純度測試、可追溯批次記錄、失敗調查、訓練記錄）。Phase 2/3 要求完整的 21 CFR Parts 210/211。無論臨床階段，無菌注射劑都需要完整的無菌製程管控——因為非無菌注射的安全後果是立即且可能致命的。

CDMO 義務：間接但後果重大

CDMO 透過四個機制被綁定：品質協議、FDA 查廠權、IND CMC 揭露要求，以及為申辦者 IND 提供製造記錄支持。責任矩陣顯示 CDMO 擁有批次記錄、GMP 執行、驗證和環境監測——申辦者擁有 IND 提交、安全報告決策、研究者管理和 CMC 部分編寫。

臨床暫停：最高風險結果

FDA 可基於 CDMO 的製造缺失發出臨床暫停。製造原因的臨床暫停可能暫停使用該 CDMO 製造藥品的所有臨床計畫——跨越所有申辦者客戶。FDA 的 30 天回應要求適用於申辦者的完整回應，而不是 CDMO 的底層調查（後者可能需要數個月）。CDMO 必須理解：其 GMP 合規直接保護客戶的臨床計畫和自身的商業存活能力。
